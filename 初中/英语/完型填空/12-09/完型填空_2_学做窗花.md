# 中考英语完形填空：学做窗花

## 完形填空原文

Window flowers, or chuanghua, are a type of traditional Chinese paper cutting used to decorate windows during festivals. Last Saturday, my grandmother taught me how to make them. She said that when she was young, every family in the village would put up window flowers to ___1___ the New Year.

Grandmother prepared thin red paper, a pair of small sharp scissors, and a pencil. She said red paper is used because red is a lucky ___2___ in Chinese culture. It represents happiness and good fortune.

First, she folded a piece of red paper several times into a small triangle. Then she drew a simple pattern on it with a pencil. The pattern had flowers, leaves, and a little bird. I watched her hands move ___3___ and steadily—she had been doing this for over fifty years.

Then Grandmother picked up the scissors and began to cut. Her cuts were smooth and ___4___. Within minutes, she unfolded the paper and a beautiful symmetrical design appeared. I clapped my hands in amazement. It looked like ___5___!

Now it was my turn. Grandmother drew a simpler pattern for me—just a basic flower shape. I folded the paper and tried to cut along the lines. But my hands were not ___6___ enough, and I accidentally cut off one of the petals. I felt disappointed.

Grandmother smiled and said, "Don't worry. Even I used to make ___7___ when I was learning. The key is to practice more." She gave me another piece of paper, and I tried again. This time, I cut more slowly and carefully. When I unfolded it, the flower was not perfect, but it was much ___8___ than my first try.

We spent the whole afternoon making window flowers. By the end, I had made five pieces that I was ___9___ of. We put them on the windows, and they looked beautiful with the sunlight shining through.

Grandmother said, "Window flowers carry our wishes for a happy life. Every cut tells a ___10___." I realized that this simple art form holds deep meaning and I want to keep learning.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：家家户户贴窗花来迎接新年。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"迎接/欢迎"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"离开"。welcome the New Year 意为"迎接新年"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：红色在中国文化中是幸运的颜色。A 项意为"数字"；B 项意为"动物"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"形状"。red is a lucky color。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看她的手熟练而稳定地移动。A 项意为"紧张地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"生气地"；D 项意为"熟练地"。五十多年的经验让手法 skillfully。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的剪裁流畅而精确。A 项意为"粗糙的"；B 项意为"杂乱的"；C 项意为"精确的"；D 项意为"慢的"。smooth and precise 形容剪纸高手。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来像魔法一样。A 项意为"无"；B 项意为"魔法"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"浪费"。looked like magic 表示惊叹。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手不够稳，不小心剪掉了一个花瓣。A 项意为"脏的"；B 项意为"大的"；C 项意为"稳的"；D 项意为"冷的"。剪纸需要手 steady。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学习时也会犯错。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"借口"。make mistakes 意为"犯错"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比第一次好多了。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更小"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更难"。much better than my first try。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做了五个让我自豪的窗花。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"惭愧的"。be proud of 意为"为……自豪"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一刀都在讲述一个故事。A 项意为"谎言"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"秘密"。Every cut tells a story 意为"每一剪都有故事"。故选 B。
