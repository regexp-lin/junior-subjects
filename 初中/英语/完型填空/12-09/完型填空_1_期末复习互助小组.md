# 中考英语完形填空：期末复习互助小组

## 完形填空原文

The final exams were coming in two weeks. Many students in our class felt stressed, especially about math and physics. Our class monitor had a great idea: why not form study groups so we could ___1___ each other?

Our teacher agreed and helped us organize. She divided the class into six groups of seven or eight students. Each group had at least one student who was ___2___ in math and one who was good at English. This way, everyone could learn from each other's strengths.

My group met every afternoon after school in the library. We made a study ___3___ for the two weeks. On Monday and Wednesday, we focused on math. On Tuesday and Thursday, we worked on English. Friday was for other subjects.

During our first meeting, Zhang Hua explained a difficult math formula to us. He was very ___4___ at explaining things step by step. I had struggled with that formula for days, but after his explanation, I finally understood it. I felt so ___5___ that I wanted to shout "Yes!" in the library.

In return, I helped the group with English grammar. I made a simple chart showing the ___6___ between the present perfect tense and the simple past tense. Several classmates said my chart was very helpful and asked if they could take photos of it.

As the days went by, our group became more and more ___7___. We not only studied together but also encouraged each other. When someone felt tired or hopeless, the others would say, "Don't worry. We'll get through this ___8___."

On the day before the exam, we had a final review session. Everyone felt more ___9___ than before. Zhang Hua said, "No matter what the results are, I'm glad we studied together. It made the hard work much more enjoyable."

After the exams, most of us did better than expected. Our teacher said our study group was a great ___10___. I believe that when we help others, we also help ourselves.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：组建学习小组互相帮助。A 项意为"欺骗"；B 项意为"打架"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"测试"。help each other 意为"互相帮助"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每组至少有一个数学强的同学。A 项意为"弱的"；B 项意为"差的"；C 项意为"慢的"；D 项意为"强的"。strong in math 意为"数学很好"。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们制定了两周的学习计划。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"歌曲"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"故事"。study plan 意为"学习计划"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他很擅长一步步地解释。A 项意为"差的"；B 项意为"好的"；C 项意为"害羞的"；D 项意为"懒的"。good at explaining。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：终于理解了，我感到如释重负。A 项意为"伤心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"如释重负的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。struggled 多日后终于懂了会 relieved。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我做了一个图表展示现在完成时和一般过去时的区别。A 项意为"相似之处"；B 项意为"不同之处"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"价格"。比较两种时态的 differences。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们小组越来越团结。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"团结的"；C 项意为"疲惫的"；D 项意为"安静的"。互助学习后更 united。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们会一起度过难关。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"分开"；C 项意为"一起"；D 项意为"离开"。get through this together。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：考前大家都比之前更自信了。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"困惑的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"自信的"。复习充分后 more confident。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说我们的学习小组非常成功。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"成功"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"事故"。成绩提高说明是 a great success。故选 B。
