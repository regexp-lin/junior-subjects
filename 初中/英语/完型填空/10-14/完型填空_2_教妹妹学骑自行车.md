# 中考英语完形填空：教妹妹学骑自行车

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my six-year-old sister Lily asked me to teach her how to ride a bicycle. She had been watching other kids ride in the park and wanted to ___1___ them.

I took out my old bicycle and adjusted the seat to make it low enough for her. Then I brought her to the open area near our apartment. Lily looked at the bicycle with both excitement and ___2___.

"Don't worry," I said. "I'll hold the back of the bicycle. You just need to keep your ___3___ on the handlebars and pedal forward." She nodded and climbed on.

At first, the bicycle wobbled badly. Lily screamed every time it ___4___ to one side. I held on tightly and ran beside her. My arms got tired quickly, but I didn't want to ___5___ her down.

After about twenty minutes, Lily started to find her balance. The bicycle became ___6___ to control. I slowly loosened my grip without telling her. She rode forward for about five meters on her own before she realized I had let go.

"I'm doing it! I'm doing it!" she shouted ___7___. But as soon as she looked back at me, she lost her balance and fell off. I rushed over to check on her. Luckily, she only had a small scratch on her knee.

I thought she would cry and refuse to continue. But to my ___8___, she stood up, brushed the dust off her clothes, and said, "Let me try again." I was really proud of her courage.

By the end of the afternoon, Lily could ride the bicycle by herself for a short ___9___. She was so happy that she rode around the park three times. Our parents came down to watch and clapped for her.

Teaching Lily to ride taught me something too: patience and encouragement can help someone ___10___ any challenge.

2. A. anger &emsp;&emsp; B. pride &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. boredom
3. A. eyes &emsp;&emsp; B. feet &emsp;&emsp; C. hands &emsp;&emsp; D. head
4. A. leaned &emsp;&emsp; B. jumped &emsp;&emsp; C. flew &emsp;&emsp; D. broke
5. A. calm &emsp;&emsp; B. let &emsp;&emsp; C. turn &emsp;&emsp; D. put
6. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. heavier &emsp;&emsp; D. slower
7. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. sadly &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
8. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. surprise &emsp;&emsp; C. regret &emsp;&emsp; D. shame
9. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. distance &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson
10. A. create &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. cause &emsp;&emsp; D. overcome

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她一直看着其他孩子骑车，也想加入他们。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"加入"；D 项意为"离开"。join them 意为"加入他们"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Lily 带着兴奋和恐惧看着自行车。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"无聊"。第一次骑车既兴奋又 fear。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你只需要把手放在车把上，向前蹬。A 项意为"眼睛"；B 项意为"脚"；C 项意为"手"；D 项意为"头"。hands on the handlebars 意为"手握车把"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次自行车向一边倾斜，Lily 就尖叫。A 项意为"倾斜"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"飞"；D 项意为"坏"。leaned to one side 意为"向一边倾斜"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不想让她失望。A 项意为"使冷静"；B 项 let down 意为"使失望"；C 项意为"转向"；D 项意为"放"。let her down 意为"让她失望"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自行车变得更容易控制了。A 项意为"更难"；B 项意为"更容易"；C 项意为"更重"；D 项意为"更慢"。找到平衡后 easier to control。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她开心地喊道。A 项意为"生气地"；B 项意为"悲伤地"；C 项意为"开心地"；D 项意为"安静地"。学会骑车 shouted happily。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但令我惊讶的是，她站了起来。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"惊讶"；C 项意为"后悔"；D 项意为"羞耻"。to my surprise 意为"令我惊讶的是"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Lily 能自己骑一小段距离了。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"距离"；D 项意为"课"。ride for a short distance 意为"骑一小段距离"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：耐心和鼓励能帮助一个人克服任何挑战。A 项意为"创造"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"引起"；D 项意为"克服"。overcome any challenge 意为"克服任何挑战"。故选 D。
