# 中考英语完形填空：宠物店的相遇

## 完形填空原文

Every Saturday morning, I walk past a pet shop on my way to the library. I never planned to go in — I was ___1___ of dogs after being bitten as a child. But one rainy day, I had to step inside to escape the storm.

The shop was warm and quiet. In one corner, I saw a small dog with soft brown fur. It was sleeping in its basket. The owner said, "That's Buddy. He's been here for three months. No one wants him because he's too ___2___ — he doesn't run or play much."

I looked at Buddy. His eyes were gentle. Somehow, I wasn't afraid. I knelt down and slowly reached out my hand. Buddy opened his eyes, sniffed my fingers, and then gently licked them. My heart ___3___.

"The vet says he's healthy," the owner went on. "He's just shy. He needs someone patient."

I thought about my lonely weekends — studying alone, eating alone. Maybe we ___4___ each other. That afternoon, I went home and talked to my parents. To my surprise, they agreed. "If you're ready to take care of him," my mom said, "we'll support you."

A week later, Buddy came home with us. The first few days were difficult. He hid under the sofa and refused to eat. I sat on the floor for hours, ___5___ to him in a soft voice. I didn't rush him. Day by day, he began to trust me.

One evening, Buddy walked over and rested his head on my knee. It was the first time he had ___6___ me on his own. I almost cried. That small act meant everything.

Now Buddy follows me everywhere. He waits by the door when I come home from school. We take ___9___ together every evening. My fear of dogs is ___7___. Buddy didn't just find a home — he helped me overcome a fear I had carried for years.

Sometimes the best things in life come when we least ___8___ them. A rainy day, a shy dog, and a moment of ___10___ can change everything.

## 选项

1. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. sure
2. A. active &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. big &emsp;&emsp; D. old
3. A. broke &emsp;&emsp; B. melted &emsp;&emsp; C. stopped &emsp;&emsp; D. raced
4. A. needed &emsp;&emsp; B. hurt &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. left
5. A. shouting &emsp;&emsp; B. talking &emsp;&emsp; C. singing &emsp;&emsp; D. laughing
6. A. thanked &emsp;&emsp; B. followed &emsp;&emsp; C. approached &emsp;&emsp; D. attacked
7. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. gone &emsp;&emsp; C. growing &emsp;&emsp; D. same
8. A. expect &emsp;&emsp; B. want &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. understand
9. A. walks &emsp;&emsp; B. buses &emsp;&emsp; C. exams &emsp;&emsp; D. photos
10. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. anger

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小时候被咬过后我一直___狗。A 项意为"自豪"；B 项意为"害怕"；C 项意为"疲惫"；D 项意为"确信"。"be afraid of"（害怕）与"being bitten as a child"（小时候被咬）呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没人要他因为他太___——不爱跑也不爱玩。A 项意为"活跃"；B 项意为"安静"；C 项意为"大"；D 项意为"老"。"quiet"与"doesn't run or play much"呼应，形容狗性格内向。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的心___了。A 项意为"碎"；B 项意为"融化了"；C 项意为"停"；D 项意为"狂跳"。"heart melted"（心融化了）是固定表达，形容被温柔举动感动。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许我们互相___。A 项意为"需要"；B 项意为"伤害"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"离开"。"need each other"（彼此需要）与作者孤独、Buddy 无人领养形成共鸣。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在地板上好几个小时，用轻柔的声音对他___。A 项意为"喊叫"；B 项意为"说话"；C 项意为"唱歌"；D 项意为"笑"。"talking to him in a soft voice"（轻声对他说话）体现耐心建立信任。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是他第一次主动___我。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"跟随"；C 项意为"靠近"；D 项意为"攻击"。"approached"（接近）与"rested his head on my knee"（把头靠在我膝盖上）对应，体现主动亲近。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对狗的恐惧已经___了。A 项意为"真实的"；B 项意为"消失了"；C 项意为"增长"；D 项意为"相同"。"gone"表示恐惧已消除，与"overcome a fear"呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时最好的事情在我们最不___的时候出现。A 项意为"预料"；B 项意为"想要"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"理解"。"least expect"（最意想不到时）是常用搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们每天晚上一起___。A 项意为"散步"；B 项意为"公交"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"照片"。"take walks"（散步）是遛狗的常用表达。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个雨天、一只害羞的狗和一刻___能改变一切。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"愤怒"。"courage"（勇气）与作者克服恐惧、主动接触狗的主题呼应。故选 B。
