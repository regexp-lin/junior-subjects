# 中考英语完形填空：天台的日落

## 完形填空原文

Our school building has six floors, and on the top there is a small rooftop. Students are not usually ___1___ to go up there, but one day after a difficult exam, I found the door unlocked. I climbed the stairs and stepped outside.

The view took my breath away. The sun was setting over the city. The sky turned from blue to orange, then to soft pink. For the first time in weeks, I felt ___2___. All my worries about the exam seemed to fade away.

I sat down on the warm concrete and watched. A bird flew across the sky. Somewhere below, I could hear the ___3___ of cars and people, but up here it felt like a different world. I thought about the pressure I had been under — staying up late, worrying about grades, forgetting to eat properly. Was it all really so important?

That moment on the rooftop changed something in me. I realized that life is not only about exams and scores. There are ___4___ things — like this sunset — that no test can measure. Beauty, peace, and the simple joy of being alive.

The next day, I told my best friend about the rooftop. We started going there together whenever we needed a break. Sometimes we talked. Sometimes we sat in ___5___, just watching the sky. It became our secret place.

One evening, we saw our math teacher, Mr. Chen, standing by the railing. He smiled when he saw us. "I come here too," he said. "Even teachers need to ___6___ sometimes." We laughed. It was comforting to know that adults felt the same way.

Now I visit the rooftop at least once a week. I've learned that it's okay to step away from the rush of daily life. Taking a moment to ___7___ the world around us can recharge our energy and clear our minds.

Find your own rooftop — a place where you can breathe, think, and remember what truly ___8___. When you feel ___9___, remember that sometimes the best ___10___ we can give ourselves is permission to slow down.

## 选项

1. A. allowed &emsp;&emsp; B. asked &emsp;&emsp; C. forced &emsp;&emsp; D. encouraged
2. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. peaceful &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
3. A. sound &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. taste &emsp;&emsp; D. touch
4. A. fewer &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. worse
5. A. surprise &emsp;&emsp; B. silence &emsp;&emsp; C. excitement &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
6. A. work &emsp;&emsp; B. rest &emsp;&emsp; C. teach &emsp;&emsp; D. run
7. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. notice &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
8. A. costs &emsp;&emsp; B. matters &emsp;&emsp; C. happens &emsp;&emsp; D. ends
9. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. overwhelmed &emsp;&emsp; C. hungry &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
10. A. advice &emsp;&emsp; B. gift &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. rule

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 被动语态与动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生通常不被___去那里。A 项意为"允许"；B 项意为"要求"；C 项意为"强迫"；D 项意为"鼓励"。"be allowed to do"（被允许做）是常用搭配，说明天台一般不对学生开放。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：几周以来第一次我感到___。A 项意为"紧张"；B 项意为"平静"；C 项意为"生气"；D 项意为"孤独"。"peaceful"与"worries faded away"（忧虑消散）呼应，观日落带来内心平静。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能听到汽车和人们的___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"味道"；D 项意为"触感"。城市中可"听到"的是"声音"（sound）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：还有___东西——比如这场日落——是考试无法衡量的。A 项意为"更少"；B 项意为"更多"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更糟"。"more things"指除成绩外还有更多重要的东西，与"no test can measure"呼应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们___地坐着，只是看天。A 项意为"惊讶"；B 项意为"沉默"；C 项意为"兴奋"；D 项意为"悲伤"。"sat in silence"（静默地坐着）是固定搭配，无需言语的默契。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师也需要不时___。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"休息"；C 项意为"教学"；D 项意为"跑步"。"rest"与"need to"搭配，老师也需要放松休息。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：花一点时间___周围的世界可以给我们充电、清空思绪。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"注意/欣赏"；D 项意为"避免"。"notice"在此有"留心观察、欣赏"之意，与观景主题契合。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：记住什么才是真正___的。A 项意为"花费"；B 项意为"重要"；C 项意为"发生"；D 项意为"结束"。"what truly matters"（什么才真正重要）是常用表达。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当你感到___时，记住……。A 项意为"疲惫"；B 项意为"不堪重负"；C 项意为"饥饿"；D 项意为"寒冷"。"overwhelmed"（不堪重负）与前文"step away from the rush"呼应，指压力大、喘不过气时。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们能给自己最好的___就是允许自己放慢脚步。A 项意为"建议"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"教训"；D 项意为"规则"。"the best gift we can give ourselves"（我们能给自己的最好礼物）是地道表达。故选 B。
