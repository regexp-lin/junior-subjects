# 中考英语完形填空：美术课的调色板

## 完形填空原文

In our art class, each student has a small wooden palette for mixing colors. At first, I thought it was just a ___1___ tool. But as I started to paint, I found that the palette could create magic. When I mixed blue and yellow, I got green. When I added a little red, the color ___2___ warmer. I was amazed by how many colors could come from just a few basic ones.

Our art teacher, Mr. Li, always told us, "Don't be afraid to try. Every great painting ___3___ with a single color on the palette." I remember one rainy afternoon. I wanted to draw the sky after the rain, but I didn't know which colors to use. Mr. Li stood beside me and said, "Look at the sky. What do you see?" I looked ___4___ the window and said, "Gray, but also a little blue." He smiled and said, "Then mix them on your palette. Trust your eyes."

From that day on, I began to see colors differently. The world became more ___5___. I noticed the golden light in the morning, the soft pink of the evening sky, and even the different greens of the leaves. My palette was no longer just a tool—it became my way of ___6___ the world.

Now when I feel stressed before exams, I sometimes sit down with my palette and paint. The quiet time helps me ___7___. I have learned that life is like mixing colors. Sometimes we get the exact color we want; sometimes we get something ___8___, but still beautiful. The important thing is to keep trying and never ___9___ the joy of creating something with our own hands.

I will always thank that small palette in our art class. It taught me that creativity is not about being perfect, but about being ___10___ to explore and discover.

2. A. felt &emsp;&emsp; B. turned &emsp;&emsp; C. stayed &emsp;&emsp; D. kept
3. A. starts &emsp;&emsp; B. ends &emsp;&emsp; C. stops &emsp;&emsp; D. fails

7. A. relax &emsp;&emsp; B. worry &emsp;&emsp; C. rush &emsp;&emsp; D. argue
8. A. wrong &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. dark &emsp;&emsp; D. light
9. A. lose &emsp;&emsp; B. find &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. hide

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我认为它只是一个___工具。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"沉重的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"坏的"。调色板是简单的绘画工具，与后文"create magic"形成对比，突出其不凡之处。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我加一点红色时，颜色___更暖了。A 项意为"感到"；B 项意为"变成、变得"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"继续"。形容颜色变化用"turn"，"turned warmer"表示色调变暖。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一幅伟大的画作都从调色板上的单一颜色___。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"结束"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"失败"。"starts with"意为"从……开始"，强调创作始于简单的一笔。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___窗外看去，说道……。A 项意为"在"；B 项意为"透过"；C 项意为"在……上方"；D 项意为"在……后面"。"look through the window"为固定搭配，意为"透过窗户看"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：世界变得越发___。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"空的"。学会观察色彩后，眼中的世界更丰富、美丽。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的调色板不再只是工具——它成为我___世界的方式。A 项意为"理解"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"错过"。"understanding"与"way of"搭配，表示通过色彩理解、认识世界。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这段安静的时间 helps me ___。A 项意为"放松"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"匆忙"；D 项意为"争论"。绘画带来的安静时光能帮助减压放松，与"stressed before exams"呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们得到意想不到的颜色，但仍然美丽。A 项意为"错误的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"深的"；D 项意为"浅的"。"different"指与预期不同但未必不好，与"still beautiful"形成转折。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是不断尝试，永远不要___亲手创造的乐趣。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"找到"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"隐藏"。"lose"与"never"连用，表示"不要失去"，强调保持创造力。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：创造力不在于完美，而在于___去探索和发现。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"疲惫的"；C 项意为"愿意的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。"willing to"意为"愿意做"，与"explore and discover"搭配恰当。故选 C。
