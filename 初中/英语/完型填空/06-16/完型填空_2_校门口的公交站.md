# 中考英语完形填空：校门口的公交站

## 完形填空原文

There is a bus stop right outside our school gate. Every afternoon after school, many students wait there for the bus. I used to think it was just a place to stand and wait. But over time, I have discovered that this small bus stop holds many ___1___ memories for me.

Last year, I had a difficult time with my math. I often felt ___2___ and did not want to talk to anyone. One rainy evening, I stood alone at the bus stop when my classmate Xiaoming came over. He didn't ask about my grades. Instead, he shared his own story of ___3___ math problems when he was in Grade 7. "You will get through it," he said. "I believe in you." Those simple words made me feel much better.

Since then, the bus stop has become a special place where we ___4___ our day. We talk about homework, sports, and sometimes our dreams for the future. When the bus arrives, we get on together and find seats side by side. The ___5___ ride home feels shorter when we have friends to talk to.

I have also met students from other classes at the bus stop. We may not know each other's names at first, but a smile or a "Hello" can ___6___ a new friendship. One boy I met there is now one of my best friends. We both love reading, and we often ___7___ books at the bus stop while waiting.

The bus stop has taught me something important: the best moments in life often happen in ordinary places. We don't need to go somewhere special to find warmth and ___8___. Sometimes, all we need is to slow down, look around, and be ___9___ to the people around us. I hope that wherever life takes me, I will always remember to ___10___ those simple moments of connection.

## 选项

1. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. sweet &emsp;&emsp; C. old &emsp;&emsp; D. bad
2. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
3. A. avoiding &emsp;&emsp; B. solving &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. forgetting
4. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
5. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. short &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. tiring
6. A. break &emsp;&emsp; B. end &emsp;&emsp; C. start &emsp;&emsp; D. lose
7. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. exchange &emsp;&emsp; D. throw
8. A. danger &emsp;&emsp; B. trouble &emsp;&emsp; C. silence &emsp;&emsp; D. friendship
9. A. kind &emsp;&emsp; B. rude &emsp;&emsp; C. blind &emsp;&emsp; D. deaf
10. A. value &emsp;&emsp; B. waste &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. ignore

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但随着时间的推移，我发现这个小小的公交站为我储存了许多___回忆。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"甜蜜的、美好的"；C 项意为"旧的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。"sweet memories"与后文友谊、互助等内容相呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我常常感到___，不想和任何人说话。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"担心的、焦虑的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。数学困难时期心情低落，"worried"符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相反，他分享了自己七年级时___数学问题的经历。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"解决"；C 项意为"制造"；D 项意为"忘记"。"solving math problems"指克服数学难题，与"difficult time with math"呼应。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从此，公交站成了一个我们___一天（收获）的特殊地方。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"忘记"。"share our day"意为分享一天的经历，与后文对话内容一致。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有朋友聊天时，___的回家路程感觉更短了。A 项意为"长的"；B 项意为"短的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"累的"。"long ride"与"feels shorter"形成对比，强调陪伴让时间过得快。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个微笑或一声"你好"就能___一段新的友谊。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"结束"；C 项意为"开始"；D 项意为"失去"。"start a friendship"为常见搭配，表示开启友谊。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都喜欢阅读，常常在公交站等车时___书籍。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"交换"；D 项意为"扔"。"exchange books"指互相借阅或交换读物，符合学生之间分享阅读的语境。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不需要去特别的地方才能找到温暖和___。A 项意为"危险"；B 项意为"麻烦"；C 项意为"寂静"；D 项意为"友谊"。"friendship"与"warmth"并列，概括前文所写的人际温情。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时，我们只需要放慢脚步，环顾四周，对身边的人___。A 项意为"友善的"；B 项意为"粗鲁的"；C 项意为"盲的"；D 项意为"聋的"。"be kind to"意为"对……友善"，与前文"smile""Hello"相呼应。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望无论人生把我带到哪里，我都能永远记得去___那些简单的连接时刻。A 项意为"珍惜"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"忽略"。"value"意为重视、珍惜，与"remember"形成递进。故选 A。
