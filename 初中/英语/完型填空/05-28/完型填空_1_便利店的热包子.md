# 中考英语完形填空：便利店的热包子

## 完形填空原文

Every morning on my way to school, I pass by a small convenience store. The owner, Uncle Wang, stands at the door and greets customers with a warm smile. His store opens at 5:30 a.m. to sell fresh steamed buns to workers who start their day early.

One cold winter morning, I forgot to bring lunch money. I stood outside the store, feeling ___1___. I had no idea how I would get through the day.

"Hey, young friend!" Uncle Wang called out. "You look cold. Come in and warm up."

I shook my head. "I... I don't have any money today."

He laughed kindly. "Who said you need money? A warm place is ___2___ for everyone." He handed me a steamed bun. "Eat this. You need energy for school."

"I can't take it without paying," I said.

"You can ___3___ me tomorrow," Uncle Wang said. "I trust you. Besides, when I was young, many people helped me. Now it's my turn to help others."

That steamed bun was the warmest thing I had ever tasted. It wasn't just the heat — it was the ___4___ behind it. From that day on, I always said hello to Uncle Wang when I passed by. Sometimes I would buy an extra bun and leave it for the next person who might need it, just like Uncle Wang had done for me.

One day, I saw a homeless man sitting near the store. He looked hungry. I bought two buns and gave one to him. He thanked me with tears in his eyes. Uncle Wang saw what happened and gave me a thumbs-up. "Kindness is like a ___5___. It spreads from one person to another," he said.

Years have passed. I still visit Uncle Wang's store whenever I can. His small act of kindness changed how I see the world. In a busy city where everyone seems to ___6___ past each other, a warm bun and a friendly smile can make all the difference. We don't need to do ___7___ things to help. Sometimes a simple gesture is enough to ___8___ someone's day — or even their life.

Uncle Wang never asked for anything in return. He just wanted to pass on the warmth he had once ___9___. And that's exactly what I'm trying to do too. In a world that often feels cold, we can all be someone's warm steamed bun — a small ___10___ that says, "I see you. I care. You matter."

## 选项

1. A. warm &emsp;&emsp; B. hungry &emsp;&emsp; C. helpless &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. free &emsp;&emsp; C. nice &emsp;&emsp; D. safe
3. A. pay &emsp;&emsp; B. thank &emsp;&emsp; C. visit &emsp;&emsp; D. help
4. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. kindness &emsp;&emsp; D. heat
5. A. fire &emsp;&emsp; B. seed &emsp;&emsp; C. chain &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
6. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. walk &emsp;&emsp; C. hurry &emsp;&emsp; D. rush
7. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. small &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. hard
8. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. save &emsp;&emsp; C. brighten &emsp;&emsp; D. fill
9. A. received &emsp;&emsp; B. given &emsp;&emsp; C. made &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
10. A. word &emsp;&emsp; B. sign &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. message

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我站在店外，感觉___。A 项意为"暖和"；B 项意为"饿"；C 项意为"无助"；D 项意为"累"。"forgot to bring lunch money"（忘了带午饭钱）导致不知如何熬过一天，"helpless"（无助）最准确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：暖和的地方对每个人都是___的。A 项意为"开放的"；B 项意为"免费的"；C 项意为"好的"；D 项意为"安全的"。"free for everyone"（对 everyone 免费开放）与后文免费给包子相呼应。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你明天可以___我。A 项意为"付钱给"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"拜访"；D 项意为"帮助"。"pay me"指明天再来付钱，表示信任。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不只是热度，更是它背后的___。A 项意为"味道"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"善意"；D 项意为"热量"。与"the heat"对比，强调" kindness"（善意）更令人温暖。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：善意就像一条___，从一个人传到另一个人。A 项意为"火"；B 项意为"种子"；C 项意为"链条"；D 项意为"礼物"。"chain"（链条）与"spreads from one person to another"（从一人传向另一人）相呼应，表示善意的传递。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在一个人人都似乎彼此___而过的忙碌城市里。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"走"；C 项意为"匆忙"；D 项意为"冲"。"hurry past"（匆忙经过）描述都市中人们行色匆匆、彼此疏离。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不需要做___的事来帮忙。A 项意为"大的"；B 项意为"小的"；C 项意为"容易的"；D 项意为"难的"。"don't need to do big things"（不必做大事）与"a simple gesture is enough"（一个简单的举动就够）呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时一个简单的举动就足以___某人一天。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"拯救"；C 项意为"照亮、使开心"；D 项意为"填满"。"brighten someone's day"（点亮某人一天）是固定表达。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他只是想传递他曾___过的温暖。A 项意为"收到"；B 项意为"给予"；C 项意为"制造"；D 项意为"失去"。"received"（收到）与"when I was young, many people helped me"呼应，表示他曾被帮助。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都可以成为某人温热包子——一个传递"我看到你，我在意，你很重要"的小小___。A 项意为"词"；B 项意为"标志"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"信息"。"message"（信息）概括后面引号中的含义。故选 D。
