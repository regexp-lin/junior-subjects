# 中考英语完形填空：山间徒步

## 完形填空原文

During the National Day holiday, my family decided to go hiking in the mountains near our city. We got up early in the morning and drove for about an hour to reach the ___1___ of the mountain. The air was fresh and cool, and we could hear birds singing in the trees. It was a ___2___ day for hiking.

We started our walk along a small path. My younger brother was full of energy and ran ahead. My father told him to slow down and stay ___3___ us so we wouldn't get lost. After walking for half an hour, we reached a clear stream. The water was so clean that we could see small fish swimming in it. We sat by the stream for a while and had some snacks. My mother had ___4___ sandwiches and fruit for us. They tasted especially good in the fresh mountain air.

Then we continued climbing. The path became steeper and more ___5___. I began to feel tired, but my father encouraged me. He said, "We are almost at the top. Don't give up!" His words gave me strength. Finally, we ___6___ the top. The view was amazing! We could see the whole city below us. I took out my phone and took many photos to ___7___ the beautiful moment. My brother said we looked like real explorers.

On our way back, the sun was setting. The sky turned golden and red. We walked slowly and talked about what we had seen. My father said that hiking helped us stay ___8___ and enjoy nature. He hoped we could come back again next year. That day in the mountains was one of the best ___9___ of my life. I learned that with patience and effort, we can reach our goals. The mountain taught me that the journey itself is as important as the ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. top &emsp;&emsp; B. middle &emsp;&emsp; C. foot &emsp;&emsp; D. side
2. A. bad &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. rainy &emsp;&emsp; D. windy
3. A. far from &emsp;&emsp; B. next to &emsp;&emsp; C. behind &emsp;&emsp; D. ahead of
4. A. bought &emsp;&emsp; B. cooked &emsp;&emsp; C. prepared &emsp;&emsp; D. ordered
5. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. wide &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. short
6. A. left &emsp;&emsp; B. missed &emsp;&emsp; C. reached &emsp;&emsp; D. passed
7. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
8. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. healthy &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
9. A. lessons &emsp;&emsp; B. dreams &emsp;&emsp; C. memories &emsp;&emsp; D. plans
10. A. start &emsp;&emsp; B. danger &emsp;&emsp; C. destination &emsp;&emsp; D. problem

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们早上早早起床，开了一个小时车到达山___。A 项意为"顶"；B 项意为"中间"；C 项意为"脚、山脚"；D 项意为"侧面"。通常驱车到达的是山脚，再开始登山，"foot of the mountain"意为"山脚"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是徒步的___日子。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"完美的"；C 项意为"下雨的"；D 项意为"有风的"。前文描述空气清新、鸟鸣，适合徒步，"perfect"符合积极语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲让他慢下来，___我们待着，这样才不会走丢。A 项意为"远离"；B 项意为"紧挨"；C 项意为"在……后面"；D 项意为"在……前面"。弟弟跑在前面，父亲希望他跟在我们后面不要乱跑，"behind"符合安全要求。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈为我们___了三明治和水果。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"煮"；C 项意为"准备"；D 项意为"点餐"。"prepare food"意为"准备食物"，可涵盖购买、制作、打包等，最通用。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小路变得越发陡峭，更加___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"宽的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"短的"。与"steeper"并列，说明路更难走，"difficult"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后，我们___了山顶。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"错过"；C 项意为"到达"；D 项意为"经过"。"reach the top"意为"到达山顶"，与后文看到美景一致。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我拿出手机拍了很多照片来___这美好时刻。A 项意为"记住、留念"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"隐藏"。"remember the moment"通过拍照记录，意为"记住这一刻"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲说徒步帮助我们保持___，享受大自然。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"健康的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"懒的"。"stay healthy"与徒步、运动的益处相符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：山里度过的这一天是我生命中最好的___之一。A 项意为"教训"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"回忆"；D 项意为"计划"。"one of the best memories"意为"最美好的回忆之一"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大山教会我，旅程本身和___一样重要。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"危险"；C 项意为"目的地"；D 项意为"问题"。"the journey"与"the destination"常成对出现，强调过程与结果同等重要。故选 C。
