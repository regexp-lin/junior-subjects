# 中考英语完形填空：手工制作

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our art teacher asked us to make something by hand for a class project. I had no ___1___ what to create. My friend Lily suggested making a paper lantern. She said it was not too difficult and the result looked ___2___. I decided to give it a try.

We went to a craft store to buy colored paper, scissors, glue, and a small LED light. At first, I thought I could finish it quickly, but I was ___3___. Folding the paper in the right way took me a long time. I made several mistakes and had to start over. Lily was patient and showed me the correct ___4___ step by step. With her help, I slowly got better.

After two hours of work, my lantern was finally done. It was not perfect, but I felt very ___5___ when I looked at it. I had made something with my own hands! On the day we showed our projects, our teacher said my lantern was creative. She encouraged us to ___6___ making things by hand because it helps develop our imagination and keeps our hands busy in a good way.

From this experience, I learned that doing something ___7___ takes time and practice. I used to think handmade things were easy. Now I understand that every craft needs ___8___ and care. I have started to enjoy making small gifts for my family. Last week I made a simple card for my mother's birthday. She was so ___9___ that she put it on her desk. Making things by hand brings joy not only to the maker but also to those who ___10___ the gifts.

1. A. idea &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. way
2. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
3. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. wrong &emsp;&emsp; C. lazy &emsp;&emsp; D. brave
4. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. way &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. question
5. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
6. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. hate
7. A. well &emsp;&emsp; B. fast &emsp;&emsp; C. late &emsp;&emsp; D. early
8. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. speed
9. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. happy
10. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. receive &emsp;&emsp; C. sell &emsp;&emsp; D. lose

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___要做什么。A 项意为"想法、主意"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"方式"。"have no idea"为固定搭配，意为"不知道、没想法"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说不太难，成品看起来很___。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"贵的"。推荐做纸灯笼时，通常强调成品好看，"beautiful"符合积极语境。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我以为能很快完成，但我___了。A 项意为"对的"；B 项意为"错的"；C 项意为"懒的"；D 项意为"勇敢的"。后文说折叠纸张花了很多时间，说明原先的估计错了。"wrong"符合"我错了"的表达。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Lily 很有耐心，一步一步地教我做事的正确___。A 项意为"答案"；B 项意为"方法、方式"；C 项意为"理由"；D 项意为"问题"。"the correct way"意为"正确的方法"，教做手工时指操作方式。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看着它感到非常___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。亲手完成作品后通常感到自豪，"proud"符合成就感。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她鼓励我们___亲手做东西。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"讨厌"。"keep doing"意为"继续做某事"，与"encourage"鼓励的语义一致。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了把事情做得___需要时间和练习。A 项意为"好"；B 项意为"快"；C 项意为"晚"；D 项意为"早"。"do something well"意为"把某事做好"，与"takes time and practice"呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我明白每件手工作品都需要___和用心。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"速度"。前文提到多次失败重做，突出需要耐心，"patience"与"care"并列合理。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她非常___，把它放在桌上。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"开心的"。收到孩子亲手做的礼物，母亲会感到高兴，"happy"最自然。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：亲手制作不仅给制作者带来快乐，也给___礼物的人带来快乐。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"收到"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"失去"。"receive the gifts"意为"收到礼物"，与"those who"指收到礼物的人一致。故选 B。
