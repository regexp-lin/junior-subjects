# 中考英语完形填空：班级文化墙

## 完形填空原文

Our head teacher announced that each class would create a culture wall in the hallway. The theme was "Our Dreams." She said it was a chance to show our class spirit and ___1___. Everyone was excited about the project.

We held a class meeting to plan the wall. Some students suggested drawing pictures, while others wanted to write poems or essays. After a long discussion, we decided to combine all these ideas into one ___2___ design. We would have a big tree in the centre, with each leaf carrying a student's dream.

I was put in charge of the art team. We had five days to complete the wall. On the first day, we bought coloured paper, markers, glue and scissors. Two students who were good at ___3___ drew a large tree trunk and branches on a big piece of brown paper. It looked very realistic.

Then each student wrote their dream on a leaf-shaped piece of paper. Some dreams were big — "I want to be a doctor" or "I want to travel the world." Others were more ___4___ — "I want to get an A in maths" or "I want to learn to cook." Every dream was valuable, no matter how big or small.

We ___5___ the leaves onto the branches one by one. The tree slowly came to life, full of green, yellow and orange leaves. Around the tree, we added photos of our class activities and short quotes about following your dreams. One student wrote a beautiful sentence: "A dream does not become reality through ___6___; it takes determination and hard work."

The most challenging part was making everything look ___7___. Some letters were too big while others were too small. Some photos were crooked. We had to redo several parts. But nobody complained — everyone wanted the wall to be perfect.

When the wall was finished, we stepped back and admired our work. It was ___8___. The tree was full of dreams, and the whole wall was colourful and inspiring. Our head teacher took photos and shared them in the parents' group chat. Many parents left positive ___9___.

The headmaster chose our wall as the best in the school. We won a small prize, but the real reward was the ___10___ we felt. Working together on the culture wall brought our class closer than ever.

## 选项

1. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. weakness &emsp;&emsp; C. creativity &emsp;&emsp; D. loneliness
2. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. unified &emsp;&emsp; C. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; D. separate
3. A. singing &emsp;&emsp; B. cooking &emsp;&emsp; C. drawing &emsp;&emsp; D. dancing
4. A. impossible &emsp;&emsp; B. modest &emsp;&emsp; C. silly &emsp;&emsp; D. useless
5. A. threw &emsp;&emsp; B. stuck &emsp;&emsp; C. hid &emsp;&emsp; D. dropped
6. A. magic &emsp;&emsp; B. luck &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. sleep
7. A. messy &emsp;&emsp; B. neat &emsp;&emsp; C. dark &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
8. A. disappointing &emsp;&emsp; B. frightening &emsp;&emsp; C. stunning &emsp;&emsp; D. confusing
9. A. complaints &emsp;&emsp; B. comments &emsp;&emsp; C. warnings &emsp;&emsp; D. bills
10. A. guilt &emsp;&emsp; B. regret &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. pride

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是展示我们班级精神和___的机会。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"弱点"；C 项意为"创造力"；D 项意为"孤独"。制作文化墙体现创造力，"creativity"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定把所有想法合并成一个___的设计。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"统一的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"分开的"。把画画、写诗等想法融为一体，"unified"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两名___好的同学画了树干和树枝。A 项意为"唱歌"；B 项意为"烹饪"；C 项意为"画画"；D 项意为"跳舞"。画树干需要画画技能，"drawing"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：其他梦想更___。A 项意为"不可能的"；B 项意为"谦虚的/朴素的"；C 项意为"愚蠢的"；D 项意为"无用的"。与前面"big"的梦想对比，"modest"表示朴实的小目标。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把树叶一片片___到树枝上。A 项意为"扔"；B 项意为"粘贴"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"掉落"。用胶水粘贴纸质树叶，"stuck"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：梦想不会通过___变成现实。A 项意为"魔法"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"睡觉"。梦想不能靠魔法实现，需要决心和努力，"magic"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最难的是让一切看起来___。A 项意为"杂乱的"；B 项意为"整齐的"；C 项意为"黑暗的"；D 项意为"空的"。后文提到字太大太小、照片歪斜需要重做，说明追求整齐，"neat"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它___极了。A 项意为"令人失望的"；B 项意为"令人害怕的"；C 项意为"令人惊艳的"；D 项意为"令人困惑的"。完成后很惊艳，"stunning"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多家长留下了积极的___。A 项意为"投诉"；B 项意为"评论"；C 项意为"警告"；D 项意为"账单"。在群里留下正面评论，"comments"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：真正的奖励是我们感受到的___。A 项意为"内疚"；B 项意为"遗憾"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"骄傲"。合作完成作品并获奖感到自豪，"pride"正确。故选 D。
