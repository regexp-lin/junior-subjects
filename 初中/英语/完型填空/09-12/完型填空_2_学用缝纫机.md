# 中考英语完形填空：学用缝纫机

## 完形填空原文

During the labor education class last week, we learned how to use a sewing machine. I had never used one before and felt a little ___1___. Our teacher, Mrs. Wang, first showed us the basic parts of the machine and explained how they worked. She said that sewing was a useful skill that could ___2___ us in many ways in life.

We started with a simple task—sewing two pieces of cloth together in a straight line. I threaded the needle carefully and put the cloth under the presser foot. When I stepped on the pedal, the machine began to run. At first, my line was not ___3___ at all. It went up and down like a wavy road. Mrs. Wang came over and told me to go slowly and keep my eyes on the cloth. "Practice makes ___4___," she said with a smile.

I tried again. This time I moved the cloth more steadily. The line became straighter. After finishing ten lines, I felt more ___5___. Mrs. Wang said we could try to make a simple handkerchief. We each chose a piece of colorful fabric and practiced sewing the edges. I chose blue cloth because it was my ___6___ color. It took me about twenty minutes to finish. When I held my handmade handkerchief, I felt a sense of ___7___.

Some of my classmates had difficulties too. Xiao Mei's thread broke twice, and she had to rethread the machine. But she didn't give up. We helped each other and shared our ___8___. By the end of the class, everyone had made something. Mrs. Wang said we had done a good job and encouraged us to practice at home if we had a sewing machine.

That afternoon, I showed my handkerchief to my mother. She was surprised and happy. She said she would teach me to sew buttons and fix clothes. I realized that learning practical skills could make us more ___9___. We don't need to ___10___ on others for every small thing. Sometimes we can solve problems by ourselves.

2. A. hurt&emsp;&emsp; B. help&emsp;&emsp; C. stop&emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
3. A. straight&emsp;&emsp; B. long&emsp;&emsp; C. short&emsp;&emsp; D. thick
4. A. perfect&emsp;&emsp; B. wrong&emsp;&emsp; C. different&emsp;&emsp; D. same
5. A. worried&emsp;&emsp; B. confused&emsp;&emsp; C. confident&emsp;&emsp; D. tired
6. A. worst&emsp;&emsp; B. best&emsp;&emsp; C. favorite&emsp;&emsp; D. only
7. A. sadness&emsp;&emsp; B. anger&emsp;&emsp; C. pride&emsp;&emsp; D. fear
8. A. secrets&emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes&emsp;&emsp; C. tips&emsp;&emsp; D. problems
9. A. dependent&emsp;&emsp; B. independent&emsp;&emsp; C. different&emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
10. A. wait&emsp;&emsp; B. depend&emsp;&emsp; C. call&emsp;&emsp; D. look

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从未用过缝纫机，感到有点紧张。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。第一次使用新设备时 nervous 符合常理，故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说缝纫是一项有用的技能，能在生活中多方面帮助我们。A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"麻烦"。useful skill 与 help 搭配，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我的线一点也不直。A 项意为"直的"；B 项意为"长的"；C 项意为"短的"；D 项意为"粗的"。后文"wavy road"和"straighter"说明一开始不 straight，故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定谚语
**【解析】** 本句意为：熟能生巧，她微笑着说。A 项意为"完美的"；B 项意为"错误的"；C 项意为"不同的"；D 项意为"相同的"。Practice makes perfect 是固定谚语，意为"熟能生巧"，故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：缝完十道线后，我感到更自信了。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"困惑的"；C 项意为"自信的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。练习后 line became straighter，自然更 confident，故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了蓝布，因为那是我最喜欢的颜色。A 项意为"最差的"；B 项意为"最好的"；C 项意为"最喜欢的"；D 项意为"唯一的"。favorite color 意为"最喜欢的颜色"，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我拿着自己做的 handkerchief 时，我感到一种自豪感。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"骄傲、自豪"；D 项意为"恐惧"。a sense of pride 意为"自豪感"，故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相帮助，分享小技巧。A 项意为"秘密"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"技巧、诀窍"；D 项意为"问题"。share tips 表示分享经验技巧，符合语境，故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到学习实用技能能让我们更独立。A 项意为"依赖的"；B 项意为"独立的"；C 项意为"不同的"；D 项意为"困难的"。与后文"don't need to depend on others"呼应，选 independent，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不必事事依赖他人。A 项 wait 常与 for 搭配；B 项 depend 与 on 搭配意为"依赖"；C 项意为"打电话"；D 项意为"看"。depend on others 意为"依赖他人"，故选 B。
