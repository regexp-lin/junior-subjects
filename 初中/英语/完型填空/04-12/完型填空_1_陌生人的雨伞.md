# 中考英语完形填空：陌生人的雨伞

## 完形填空原文

It was a rainy afternoon when I walked out of the bookstore. I had bought a new novel and couldn't wait to read it. But the sky had turned dark and heavy rain was falling. I stood under a small roof, ___1___ what to do. I had no umbrella and my house was a twenty-minute walk away.

A woman in her fifties came out of the bookstore and opened her umbrella. She looked at me and said, "It's going to rain for a while. Would you like to ___2___ my umbrella? I live just around the corner."

I was surprised. "But how will you get home?" I asked.

She smiled. "I'll be fine. I can run. You have a long ___3___ ahead. Take it."

Before I could refuse, she had put the umbrella in my hand and walked into the rain. I ___4___ her go, feeling both grateful and a little guilty. I didn't even know her name.

I walked home slowly under the umbrella. The rain beat against it, but I stayed dry. When I arrived, I asked my mother how I could ___5___ the umbrella to the woman. "She said she lives around the corner from the bookstore," I explained.

My mother thought for a moment. "There are many ___6___ in that area. But you could leave the umbrella at the bookstore and ask them to keep it for someone who describes it. Or — did she mention anything else?"

"She said she often goes there to read," I said.

The next day, I went back to the bookstore. The owner, an old man, nodded when I described the woman. "I know her. She comes here every Saturday. I'll ___7___ her the umbrella when she returns."

A week later, I visited the bookstore again. The owner smiled and said, "She got her umbrella. She also asked me to give you this." He handed me a small ___8___. Inside was a note: "Kindness is like a seed. When you plant it, it grows."

I never met that woman again. But her words stayed with me. Since then, I have tried to help ___9___ when I can — lending a pen, carrying heavy bags, or simply smiling at someone who looks sad. I learned that a small act of kindness can ___10___ someone's day, and it costs nothing.

## 选项

1. A. wondering &emsp;&emsp; B. discussing &emsp;&emsp; C. deciding &emsp;&emsp; D. remembering
2. A. borrow &emsp;&emsp; B. share &emsp;&emsp; C. buy &emsp;&emsp; D. return
3. A. way &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. road &emsp;&emsp; D. trip
4. A. watched &emsp;&emsp; B. helped &emsp;&emsp; C. followed &emsp;&emsp; D. called
5. A. send &emsp;&emsp; B. give &emsp;&emsp; C. bring &emsp;&emsp; D. return
6. A. schools &emsp;&emsp; B. houses &emsp;&emsp; C. shops &emsp;&emsp; D. parks
7. A. sell &emsp;&emsp; B. pass &emsp;&emsp; C. lend &emsp;&emsp; D. show
8. A. book &emsp;&emsp; B. letter &emsp;&emsp; C. bag &emsp;&emsp; D. card
9. A. others &emsp;&emsp; B. myself &emsp;&emsp; C. her &emsp;&emsp; D. everyone
10. A. waste &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. break &emsp;&emsp; D. save

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我站在小屋檐下，___该怎么做。A 项意为"想知道"；B 项意为"讨论"；C 项意为"决定"；D 项意为"记得"。没带伞又下大雨，作者在思考、犹豫，"wondering"（wondering what to do）表示"在考虑/想知道该怎么办"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你愿意___我的伞吗？A 项意为"借"；B 项意为"共用"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"归还"。女士主动提出把伞给作者用，作者之后会归还，所以是"借"（borrow）给作者。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你前面还有很长的___。A 项意为"路/路程"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"道路"；D 项意为"旅行"。"a long way ahead"是固定表达，意为"前面还有很长的路要走"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___她离开。A 项意为"观看/注视"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"打电话"。作者没有追上或阻拦，只是"看着"（watched）那位女士走进雨中离开。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我问妈妈怎样才能把伞___给那位女士。A 项意为"寄送"；B 项意为"给"；C 项意为"带来"；D 项意为"归还"。伞是借来的，需要"归还"（return）给原主人。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那片区域有很多___。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"房子/住宅"；C 项意为"商店"；D 项意为"公园"。妈妈说"around the corner"附近有很多"房子"（houses），难以确定是哪一家，所以不好直接上门还伞。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她再来时我会把伞___给她。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"传递/转交"；C 项意为"借"；D 项意为"展示"。店主答应在女士来书店时把伞"转交"（pass）给她。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他递给我一张小___。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"信"；C 项意为"包"；D 项意为"卡片"。下文说里面有一张"note"（便条），这种小纸片通常装在"卡片"（card）里。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那以后，我尽力在力所能及时帮助___。A 项意为"其他人"；B 项意为"我自己"；C 项意为"她"；D 项意为"每个人"。作者被善举感动，开始帮助"别人"（others），延续善意。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小小的善举可以___某人的一天。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"打破"；D 项意为"拯救"。"change someone's day"意为"改变某人的一天"，指让这一天变得更好。故选 B。
