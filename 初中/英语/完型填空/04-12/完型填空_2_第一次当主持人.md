# 中考英语完形填空：第一次当主持人

## 完形填空原文

Our school English Festival was two weeks away. Each class had to prepare a short play. Our teacher Ms. Wang asked for a volunteer to be the host. Nobody raised their hand. Hosting meant speaking in front of the whole school — more than six hundred students and teachers. The idea made my heart ___1___.

I had never been good at speaking in public. Last year, I had to give a one-minute speech in class. My hands shook and I forgot half of my ___2___. I did not want to repeat that experience.

But that night, I couldn't sleep. I kept thinking about the host position. What if I ___3___? What if this was my chance to improve?

The next morning, I went to Ms. Wang's office. "I'd like to try being the host," I said. My voice was so quiet that she asked me to ___4___ myself.

She smiled. "I'm glad you volunteered. Let's start practicing tomorrow."

For the next two weeks, I practiced every day. I read the script aloud in my room, in front of a mirror, and then in the empty classroom. Ms. Wang gave me tips: speak slowly, look at the audience, and ___5___ when I made a small mistake. "Nobody is perfect," she said. "The audience wants you to succeed."

The night before the festival, I was too nervous to eat. My father sat down with me and said, "Remember when you first learned to ride a bike? You fell many times. But you never gave up. Tomorrow is just another ___6___ to try."

His words helped. The next afternoon, I stood on the stage. The lights were bright. I could see hundreds of faces. I took a deep breath and began to speak.

My voice was steady. I introduced each class's performance one by one. When I ___7___ a word, I paused, smiled, and went on. The audience didn't laugh at me. They listened.

When the festival ended, several students came up to me. "You did great!" one of them said. Another said, "I could never do that. You were so ___8___."

I wasn't brave at the start. I was scared. But I learned something ___9___: courage doesn't mean you have no fear. It means you do the right thing even when you're ___10___. That afternoon on the stage, I took a small step. But for me, it was a big one.

1. A. jump &emsp;&emsp; B. beat &emsp;&emsp; C. rest &emsp;&emsp; D. break
2. A. lines &emsp;&emsp; B. books &emsp;&emsp; C. notes &emsp;&emsp; D. ideas
3. A. failed &emsp;&emsp; B. tried &emsp;&emsp; C. refused &emsp;&emsp; D. left
4. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. believe &emsp;&emsp; C. repeat &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoy
5. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. continue &emsp;&emsp; D. cry
6. A. way &emsp;&emsp; B. chance &emsp;&emsp; C. day &emsp;&emsp; D. place
7. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. wrote &emsp;&emsp; C. changed &emsp;&emsp; D. heard
8. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. brave &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. quiet
9. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
10. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. happy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个想法让我的心___。A 项意为"跳"；B 项意为"敲击"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"打破"。"make one's heart jump"意为"让某人心跳加速/心里一紧"，形容紧张害怕。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我忘了大半的___。A 项意为"台词/句子"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"笔记"；D 项意为"想法"。演讲时忘记的是准备好的"台词"（lines），即要说的内容。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我___会怎样？A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"离开"。作者在考虑要不要"试试"（tried）当主持人，这是改变的开始。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的声音太小了，她让我___自己。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"相信"；C 项意为"重复"；D 项意为"享受"。声音太小听不清，老师让作者"再说一遍"（repeat yourself）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：犯小错误时要___。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"继续"；D 项意为"哭"。老师建议犯小错时不要停，要"继续"（continue）说下去。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：明天只是又一个___去尝试。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项意为"日子"；D 项意为"地方"。爸爸鼓励说这只是又一个"机会"（chance）去尝试，像学骑车一样。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我___一个词时，我停顿了一下，微微一笑，然后继续。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"听到"。主持时忘词是常有的，"忘记"（forgot）某个词后停顿、微笑再继续。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你太___了。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"安静的"。同学夸作者敢在众人面前主持，说他很"勇敢"（brave）。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学到了一些___的东西。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。关于勇气与恐惧的道理是"重要的"（important）人生领悟。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：勇气意味着即使在你___的时候也做正确的事。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"高兴的"。勇敢不是不害怕，而是"害怕"（afraid）时依然行动。故选 C。
