# 中考英语完形填空：拍毕业照

## 完形填空原文

It was a sunny morning in June. Our class was going to take our graduation photos — the last group photo we would take as junior high school students. We had been looking ___1___ to this day for a long time.

In the morning, we put on our white shirts and dark blue uniforms. The girls fixed their hair and the boys made sure their collars were straight. We wanted to look our ___2___ for this special moment.

The photographer had set up his equipment on the playground. He asked us to stand in rows according to our ___3___. The taller students stood at the back and the shorter ones at the front. I stood in the third row, right next to my best friend.

"Everyone, smile! Say cheese!" the photographer shouted. We all smiled widely. But then someone made a funny face and we all ___4___ laughing. The photographer had to ask us to be serious. We tried again, and this time we managed to keep serious faces — though it was really difficult!

After the formal photo, we had some free time for casual photos. We took photos with our favourite teachers, with our close friends, and with our class flag. The atmosphere was full of ___5___ and a little sadness. We knew that soon we would go to different high schools and might not see each other so often.

Our headteacher made a short speech. She said that these three years had been the most ___6___ time of her teaching career. She encouraged us to work hard in high school and to never forget our roots. Many of us had ___7___ in our eyes when she finished.

When I look at that graduation photo now, I can see every face — the ones I studied with, the ones I laughed with, and the ones I grew up with. That photo ___8___ our friendship and our youth forever. It reminds me that no matter where we go, we will always be part of this big family.

I will ___9___ this photo carefully. It is not just a picture — it is a treasure that holds our best memories of junior high school. Years from now, when I look at it again, I will still remember the sunshine, the smiles, and the ___10___ we shared on that special day.

## 选项

1. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. for &emsp;&emsp; C. forward &emsp;&emsp; D. up
2. A. worst &emsp;&emsp; B. best &emsp;&emsp; C. oldest &emsp;&emsp; D. youngest
3. A. age &emsp;&emsp; B. height &emsp;&emsp; C. weight &emsp;&emsp; D. grade
4. A. stopped &emsp;&emsp; B. started &emsp;&emsp; C. kept &emsp;&emsp; D. burst
5. A. anger &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
6. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. unforgettable &emsp;&emsp; D. ordinary
7. A. smiles &emsp;&emsp; B. tears &emsp;&emsp; C. sweat &emsp;&emsp; D. blood
8. A. forgets &emsp;&emsp; B. loses &emsp;&emsp; C. captures &emsp;&emsp; D. destroys
9. A. throw &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. sell &emsp;&emsp; D. waste
10. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. anger &emsp;&emsp; C. warmth &emsp;&emsp; D. coldness

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们期盼这一天很久了。A 项意为"在……之后"；B 项意为"为了"；C 项意为"向前"；D 项意为"向上"。look forward to 意为"期待；盼望"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们想在这个特殊时刻展现出最好的自己。A 项意为"最坏的"；B 项意为"最好的"；C 项意为"最老的"；D 项意为"最年轻的"。look one's best 意为"展现最佳状态"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：摄影师让我们按身高排成几排。A 项意为"年龄"；B 项意为"身高"；C 项意为"体重"；D 项意为"年级"。后文"高个在后矮个在前"对应 height。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：有人做了个鬼脸，我们都大笑起来。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"爆发"。burst out laughing 意为"突然大笑"。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：气氛充满欢乐，也带有一点悲伤。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"欢乐"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"惊讶"。毕业拍照时欢乐与惜别并存，joy 正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说这三年是她教学生涯中最难忘的时光。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"难忘的"；D 项意为"普通的"。老师对学生的情感用 unforgettable。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她讲完时，我们许多人眼里含泪。A 项意为"笑容"；B 项意为"泪水"；C 项意为"汗水"；D 项意为"血"。感人的演讲引发泪水，tears 正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那张照片永远定格了我们的友谊和青春。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"捕捉；定格"；D 项意为"破坏"。照片 captures 瞬间，captures 正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会好好保存这张照片。A 项意为"扔掉"；B 项意为"保存"；C 项意为"卖掉"；D 项意为"浪费"。珍贵照片要保存，keep 正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：多年后我再看到它，依然会记得那天的阳光、笑容和我们共享的温暖。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"温暖"；D 项意为"寒冷"。毕业美好回忆用 warmth。故选 C。
