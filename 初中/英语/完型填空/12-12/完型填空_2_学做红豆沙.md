# 中考英语完形填空：学做红豆沙

## 完形填空原文

On a cold winter afternoon, my grandma asked if I wanted to learn how to make red bean paste (红豆沙). I was very ___1___ because I loved eating it in sweet soup and pastries.

Grandma first showed me how to wash the red beans and ___2___ them in water for at least four hours. "This step is very important," she said. "If we skip it, the beans will be hard to cook." I ___3___ carefully and wrote down everything in my notebook.

The next day, we put the beans in a pot and added plenty of water. We cooked them over low heat for about two hours. I had to ___4___ the pot from time to time to prevent it from burning. The smell was wonderful—sweet and warm.

When the beans became soft, we mashed them with a spoon and added sugar. Grandma said we should add the sugar ___5___, tasting as we went, because everyone has different preferences. I followed her ___6___ and the paste turned out just right.

Making red bean paste was ___7___ than I had expected. It needed patience and care. But when I tasted the final product, I felt a great sense of ___8___. Grandma smiled and said, "Now you can make it for your family."

I'm grateful for this ___9___ with my grandma. She not only taught me a recipe but also passed on her love for traditional food. I hope to make red bean paste for my friends someday and ___10___ this warmth with them.

## 选项

1. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. put &emsp;&emsp; D. soak
3. A. listened &emsp;&emsp; B. spoke &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote
4. A. move &emsp;&emsp; B. wash &emsp;&emsp; C. stir &emsp;&emsp; D. cover
5. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
6. A. advice &emsp;&emsp; B. example &emsp;&emsp; C. order &emsp;&emsp; D. rule
7. A. easier &emsp;&emsp; B. harder &emsp;&emsp; C. simpler &emsp;&emsp; D. faster
8. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. pride &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. worry
9. A. trip &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. party
10. A. share &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. forget

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常兴奋，因为我爱吃甜品和糕点里的红豆沙。A 项意为"惊讶的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"累的"。喜爱红豆沙的人听到要学做会感到 excited。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶先教我如何洗红豆，然后把它们浸泡在水中至少四小时。A 项 keep 需接介词；B 项 leave 表"留下"；C 项 put 表"放置"；D 项 soak 意为"浸泡"。做豆沙前需要 soak（浸泡）红豆。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我认真聆听，把一切都记在笔记本上。A 项 listened 与 carefully 搭配表"认真听"；B 项意为"说"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"写"。学习时是 listen（听）奶奶讲解。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我时不时要搅拌锅以防糊底。A 项意为"移动"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"搅拌"；D 项意为"盖住"。煮豆时 prevent burning 需要 stir（搅拌）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说我们应该慢慢加糖，边加边尝，因为每个人口味不同。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"慢慢地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。tasting as we went 暗示要慢慢加，慢慢尝。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我听从了她的建议，红豆沙做得刚刚好。A 项 advice 意为"建议"；B 项 example 意为"例子"；C 项 order 意为"命令"；D 项 rule 意为"规则"。follow one's advice 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：做红豆沙比我预想的更难。A 项意为"更容易"；B 项意为"更难"；C 项意为"更简单"；D 项意为"更快"。后文说 needed patience and care，说明比预期 harder。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我尝到最终成品时，我感到巨大的自豪感。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"自豪"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"担心"。亲手做成红豆沙应有 pride（自豪感）。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感谢和奶奶的这次学习经历。A 项意为"旅行"；B 项 lesson 可表"一课、一次学习"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"派对"。学做红豆沙是一次 lesson（学习经历）。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望将来能为朋友做红豆沙，与他们分享这份温暖。A 项 share 意为"分享"；B 项 keep 意为"保持"；C 项 hide 意为"隐藏"；D 项 forget 意为"忘记"。与朋友 share（分享）温暖最恰当。故选 A。
