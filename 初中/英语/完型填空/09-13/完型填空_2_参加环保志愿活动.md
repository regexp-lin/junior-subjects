# 中考英语完形填空：参加环保志愿活动

## 完形填空原文

On Sunday morning, I joined a volunteer activity to protect the environment. We met at the gate of the city park at 8 o'clock. There were about thirty students from different schools, all wearing green vests with the ___1___ "Protect Our Earth".

Our task was to pick up litter in the park. At first, I thought it would be easy, but I was ___2___. There was garbage everywhere: plastic bottles, paper bags, and even used masks. We worked in pairs and filled our bags ___3___ within an hour.

While picking up trash, I ___4___ a small bird that seemed to be hurt. It couldn't fly properly. I quickly ran to our team leader and told her about it. She called the animal rescue center, and they said they would come soon. I felt ___5___ that we could help the little creature.

During a short break, we sat under a big tree and had some water. One of the organizers gave us a ___6___ about why we should reduce plastic use. She said that plastic waste harms wildlife and pollutes our oceans. Her words made me ___7___ more about environmental protection.

Before we left, we took a group photo. Everyone was tired but had a big ___8___ on their faces. I realized that doing something meaningful for our planet made us happy. From that day on, I decided to ___9___ less plastic and encourage my family to do the same.

Small actions can make a big ___10___. If everyone does their part, we can create a cleaner and greener world together.

## 选项

1. A. words &emsp;&emsp; B. letters &emsp;&emsp; C. signs &emsp;&emsp; D. symbols
2. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. wrong &emsp;&emsp; C. correct &emsp;&emsp; D. sure
3. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
4. A. found &emsp;&emsp; B. looked &emsp;&emsp; C. watched &emsp;&emsp; D. searched
5. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. sad &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
6. A. talk &emsp;&emsp; B. message &emsp;&emsp; C. letter &emsp;&emsp; D. notice
7. A. think &emsp;&emsp; B. care &emsp;&emsp; C. know &emsp;&emsp; D. believe
8. A. tear &emsp;&emsp; B. smile &emsp;&emsp; C. noise &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
9. A. use &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. waste &emsp;&emsp; D. throw
10. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. chance &emsp;&emsp; C. choice &emsp;&emsp; D. challenge

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都穿着印有"保护我们的地球"字样的绿色背心。A 项意为"字；词"；B 项意为"字母；信"；C 项意为"标志；迹象"；D 项意为"符号"。背心上印的是文字内容，用 words。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我以为会很容易，但我错了。A 项意为"正确的"；B 项意为"错误的"；C 项意为"正确的"；D 项意为"确定的"。I was wrong 表示"我错了"，与"以为容易"形成对比。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们两人一组工作，一小时内袋子就很快装满了。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"缓慢地"；C 项意为"开心地"；D 项意为"难过地"。垃圾多，填满袋子速度快，quickly 合适。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：捡垃圾时，我发现一只似乎受伤的小鸟。A 项意为"发现"；B 项意为"看"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"搜查"。find 表示偶然发现，符合语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：能帮助这个小生命，我感到骄傲。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"难过的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。帮助受伤动物后感到自豪是正面情感。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位组织者给我们做了一次关于为什么要减少塑料使用的演讲。A 项意为"演讲；谈话"；B 项意为"信息"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"通知"。give sb a talk 为固定搭配，意为"给某人做演讲"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话让我更加关心环境保护。A 项意为"思考"；B 项意为"关心；在意"；C 项意为"知道"；D 项意为"相信"。make sb care more 表示"使某人更关心"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都很累，但脸上都挂着大大的笑容。A 项意为"眼泪"；B 项意为"微笑"；C 项意为"噪音"；D 项意为"惊讶"。做有意义的事后感到开心，smile 符合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那天起，我决定少用塑料，并鼓励家人也这样做。A 项意为"使用"；B 项意为"购买"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"扔掉"。reduce plastic use 对应 use less plastic。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的行动能带来大的改变。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"挑战"。make a big change 为常见搭配，意为"产生重大改变"。故选 A。
