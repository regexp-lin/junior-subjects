# 中考英语完形填空：学校科学实验课

## 完形填空原文

Last Thursday afternoon, we had an exciting science class in the lab. Our teacher, Mr. Wang, asked us to do an ___1___ about how plants grow. We divided into groups of four and each group got a small pot with soil and some bean seeds.

First, we put the seeds into the soil and ___2___ them with water. Then we placed the pots near the window where they could get enough sunlight. Mr. Wang told us to ___3___ our plants every day and write down what we saw in our notebooks.

During the next two weeks, I ___4___ watched my beans grow. At first, nothing happened. I felt a little ___5___. But on the fifth day, I noticed a small green shoot coming out of the soil! I was so excited that I ___6___ called my parents to tell them the good news.

Our teacher said that plants need water, sunlight and air to grow. He also ___7___ us that we should be patient because nature has its own pace. Finally, when all the plants had grown well, we had a small ___8___ in class. Each group shared their observations and we learned a lot from each other.

This experiment ___9___ me that learning by doing is much more interesting than just reading from books. I really ___10___ the hands-on experience and I hope we can have more such activities in the future.

## 选项

1. A. experiment &emsp;&emsp; B. examination &emsp;&emsp; C. experience &emsp;&emsp; D. excuse
2. A. covered &emsp;&emsp; B. watered &emsp;&emsp; C. filled &emsp;&emsp; D. washed
3. A. take care of &emsp;&emsp; B. take part in &emsp;&emsp; C. take place &emsp;&emsp; D. take off
4. A. carefully &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. suddenly
5. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. disappointed &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
6. A. even &emsp;&emsp; B. still &emsp;&emsp; C. already &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
7. A. reminded &emsp;&emsp; B. refused &emsp;&emsp; C. realized &emsp;&emsp; D. reported
8. A. discussion &emsp;&emsp; B. decision &emsp;&emsp; C. direction &emsp;&emsp; D. distance
9. A. taught &emsp;&emsp; B. told &emsp;&emsp; C. offered &emsp;&emsp; D. gave
10. A. enjoyed &emsp;&emsp; B. finished &emsp;&emsp; C. missed &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师让我们做一个关于植物如何生长的实验。A 项意为"实验"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"经历；经验"；D 项意为"借口"。根据上下文"do an..."和"how plants grow"，应填"实验"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我们把种子放进土壤里并用水浇灌。A 项意为"覆盖"；B 项意为"浇水"；C 项意为"装满"；D 项意为"洗"。种子需要水才能生长，watering 符合植物生长的逻辑。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师让我们每天照顾植物并在笔记本上记录所见。A 项意为"照顾"；B 项意为"参加"；C 项意为"发生"；D 项意为"起飞；脱下"。照料植物用 take care of。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在接下来的两周里，我仔细观察豆子的生长。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"突然地"。观察植物生长需要仔细，carefully 最合适。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初什么都没有发生，我感到有点失望。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"失望的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。几天没动静，感到失望符合语境。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太兴奋了，甚至打电话告诉父母这个好消息。A 项意为"甚至"；B 项意为"仍然"；C 项意为"已经"；D 项意为"几乎不"。even 表递进，强调兴奋程度。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他还提醒我们应对大自然有耐心，因为它有自己的节奏。A 项意为"提醒"；B 项意为"拒绝"；C 项意为"意识到"；D 项意为"报告"。remind sb that 意为"提醒某人……"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后，当所有植物都长得好时，我们在课堂上进行了小型讨论。A 项意为"讨论"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"方向"；D 项意为"距离"。share observations 对应"讨论"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个实验告诉我，动手学习比仅仅读书有趣得多。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"告诉"；C 项意为"提供"；D 项意为"给"。tell sb that 为常见搭配，表示"告诉某人……"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我真的很享受这种动手实践的经历，希望未来能有更多这样的活动。A 项意为"享受"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"想念；错过"；D 项意为"忘记"。enjoy 与 positive experience 搭配。故选 A。
