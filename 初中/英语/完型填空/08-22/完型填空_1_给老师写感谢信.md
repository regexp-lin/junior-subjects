# 中考英语完形填空：给老师写感谢信

## 完形填空原文

Last week was Teacher's Day. I wanted to do something special for my English teacher, Mr Zhang. He has helped me a lot since I entered junior high. I decided to write him a thank-you letter.

I sat down at my desk and took out a piece of nice paper. At first, I did not know how to ___1___. There were so many things I wanted to say. I thought about the times when Mr Zhang had stayed after class to explain grammar to me. I remembered how he had encouraged me when I failed my first English exam. His words had given me ___2___ to try again.

I started writing. "Dear Mr Zhang," I wrote. "Thank you for being my teacher. You have taught me not only English but also how to learn. You said that making mistakes is part of learning. Those words ___3___ me a lot."

I continued to write about the activities in his class. We often did role-plays and group discussions. Mr Zhang made the lessons interesting. He never made us feel ___4___ when we gave wrong answers. Instead, he would say, "Good try! Now let's see how we can improve."

I also wrote about my progress. At the beginning of the year, I was afraid to speak English in class. Now I can answer questions and even give short ___5___. This change would not have happened without Mr Zhang's patience and support.

When I finished the letter, I read it again to check for spelling mistakes. I wanted the letter to be ___6___. I put it in a nice envelope and drew a small heart on the corner. The next morning, I went to the teachers' office and gave the letter to Mr Zhang before class.

He opened it and read it with a smile. Then he looked at me and said, "Thank you. This means a lot to me. Keep up the good work." I felt warm inside. Sometimes we forget to ___7___ the people who help us. A simple thank-you can make a big difference.

I have kept a copy of the letter in my diary. When I feel discouraged, I read it and remember that there are people who ___8___ in me. I hope to become a teacher like Mr Zhang one day — someone who inspires students and helps them grow.

Writing the thank-you letter taught me an important ___9___: we should never take kindness for granted. When someone helps us, we should express our gratitude. A few minutes of writing can bring ___10___ to both the giver and the receiver.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我不知道如何___。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"忘记"。"how to start"意为"如何下笔/如何开始"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话给了我再次尝试的___。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"悲伤"。"courage to try again"意为"再次尝试的勇气"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那些话___我很多。A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"使困惑"；D 项意为"使无聊"。"helped me a lot"意为"对我帮助很大"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他从不让我们在答错时感到___。A 项意为"骄傲"；B 项意为"愚蠢/难堪"；C 项意为"自信"；D 项意为"兴奋"。"feel stupid"指答错时的尴尬感。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我甚至能做个简短的___。A 项意为"信"；B 项意为"演讲"；C 项意为"歌"；D 项意为"故事"。"give short speeches"意为"做简短发言"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望这封信___。A 项意为"长"；B 项意为"短"；C 项意为"完美"；D 项意为"无聊"。检查拼写是为求完美，"perfect"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们会忘记___帮助我们的人。A 项意为"责备"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"忽略"。"thank the people"意为"感谢那些人"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我记住有人___我。A 项意为"嘲笑"；B 项意为"相信"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"担心"。"believe in me"意为"相信/信任我"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写感谢信教会我重要的___。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"道理/课"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"运动"。"important lesson"意为"重要的道理"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：几分钟的书写能为给予者和接受者都带来___。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"愤怒"。"bring joy"意为"带来快乐"。故选 B。
