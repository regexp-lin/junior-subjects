# 中考英语完形填空：学骑平衡车

## 完形填空原文

My neighbour's son has a hoverboard. I had seen him glide around the community on it and always wanted to try. Last month, my parents bought me one as a reward for my good grades. I was so ___1___ that I could not wait to start.

The hoverboard has two wheels and is powered by a battery. You stand on it with both feet and lean forward or backward to move. It sounds simple, but it is not. When I first stepped on it, I lost my ___2___ immediately and had to jump off. My father held my arm and said, "Take it easy. Nobody gets it on the first try."

I tried again. I stood still first and got used to the feeling under my feet. Then I leaned forward very slowly. The board moved! I was so surprised that I leaned back too quickly and almost fell. My father caught me. "Small movements," he reminded me. "Don't make ___3___ changes."

I practised in our living room for the first few days. There was more space and fewer things to bump into. Gradually, I learned to control the speed by adjusting how much I leaned. Going straight was easier than turning. To turn, I had to twist my feet slightly. At first, my turns were too sharp and I would ___4___ off the board.

After a week of practice, I could ride around the room without falling. My father said I was ready to try outdoors. We went to the pavement in front of our building. It was wider and smoother than the floor at home. I felt more ___5___ riding there.

I started to ride around the community. Some of my classmates saw me and wanted to try. I let them have a go, but I warned them to be careful. Not everyone could ___6___ it as quickly as I did. One friend fell on his first try and said his arm hurt a little. Safety always comes first.

My mother set some rules: I must wear a helmet, I cannot ride on busy roads, and I must not ride when it is dark. I agreed. These rules are there to ___7___ us. I do not want to get hurt or hurt others.

Riding the hoverboard has become part of my daily life. I sometimes ride it to the convenience store to buy snacks. It is faster than walking and more fun than taking the bus. My grandparents were amazed when they saw me. They said that when they were young, they had never seen such a ___8___.

I am grateful to my parents for this gift. Learning to ride has taught me that new skills need patience. I could not master it in one day, but with ___9___ effort, I got there. Now I can help other beginners too. It feels good to share what I have learned and see others succeed. That is the real ___10___ of learning.

1. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. way &emsp;&emsp; B. balance &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. courage
3. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. sudden &emsp;&emsp; C. slow &emsp;&emsp; D. gentle
4. A. step &emsp;&emsp; B. jump &emsp;&emsp; C. fall &emsp;&emsp; D. run
5. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. confident &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
6. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. learn &emsp;&emsp; C. borrow &emsp;&emsp; D. sell
7. A. protect &emsp;&emsp; B. punish &emsp;&emsp; C. bother &emsp;&emsp; D. limit
8. A. toy &emsp;&emsp; B. tool &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. vehicle
9. A. no &emsp;&emsp; B. repeated &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. single
10. A. difficulty &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. cost &emsp;&emsp; D. speed

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，迫不及待要开始。A 项意为"难过"；B 项意为"兴奋"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"疲倦"。收到礼物后兴奋，"excited"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我立刻失去了___，只能跳下来。A 项意为"方向"；B 项意为"平衡"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"勇气"。"lost balance"意为"失去平衡"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要做___变化。A 项意为"小的"；B 项意为"突然的"；C 项意为"慢的"；D 项意为"轻柔的"。"sudden changes"指突然大幅度动作易摔倒。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初转弯太急，我会___踏板。A 项意为"踩"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"摔"；D 项意为"跑"。"fall off"意为"从……上摔下"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在那里骑我觉得更___。A 项意为"紧张"；B 项意为"自信"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"无聊"。户外路面更好，更有信心，"confident"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不是每个人都能像我一样快___它。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"学会"；C 项意为"借"；D 项意为"卖"。"learn it"意为"学会骑"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些规则是为了___我们。A 项意为"保护"；B 项意为"惩罚"；C 项意为"打扰"；D 项意为"限制"。"protect us"意为"保护我们"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们年轻时从没见过这样的___。A 项意为"玩具"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"交通工具"。"vehicle"指平衡车这类代步工具。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但经过___努力，我做到了。A 项意为"没有"；B 项意为"反复的"；C 项意为"很少"；D 项意为"单一的"。"repeated effort"意为"持续/反复努力"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那才是学习的真正___。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"成本"；D 项意为"速度"。"the real joy of learning"意为"学习的真正乐趣"。故选 B。
