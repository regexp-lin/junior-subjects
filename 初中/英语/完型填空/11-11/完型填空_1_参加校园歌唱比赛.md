# 中考英语完形填空：参加校园歌唱比赛

## 完形填空原文

The school singing competition was held last month. I had always loved singing, so I ___1___ to take part. I chose an English song that I had practiced many times. My music teacher said my voice was suitable for it.

The day before the competition, I felt very nervous. I practiced the song again and again until I could sing it without ___2___ the words. My best friend Zhang Wei came to encourage me. He said, "Just be yourself. The audience will ___3___ your passion." His words made me feel better.

On the stage, the lights were bright. I took a deep breath and began to sing. For the first few lines, my voice was a little ___4___, but soon I relaxed. I forgot about the judges and the crowd. I just focused on the music and the meaning of the song. When I finished, the hall was quiet for a moment. Then people started to ___5___. I felt so happy that I smiled from ear to ear.

I didn't win first prize, but I got the third place. More ___6___, I overcame my fear of performing in front of many people. Before this, I would feel shy when speaking in class. Now I am much more ___7___. The competition taught me that courage grows when we step out of our comfort zone. I will continue to sing and maybe ___8___ in more activities. I believe that every experience, win or ___9___, helps us grow. I am grateful for this ___10___ and for all the support from my teachers and friends.

## 选项

1. A. refused &emsp;&emsp; B. decided &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. failed
2. A. remembering &emsp;&emsp; B. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; C. writing &emsp;&emsp; D. changing
3. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. see &emsp;&emsp; C. hear &emsp;&emsp; D. touch
4. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. clear &emsp;&emsp; C. shaky &emsp;&emsp; D. soft
5. A. cry &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. clap &emsp;&emsp; D. speak
6. A. importantly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
7. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. shy &emsp;&emsp; C. confident &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
8. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. hold &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
9. A. win &emsp;&emsp; B. lose &emsp;&emsp; C. success &emsp;&emsp; D. prize
10. A. idea &emsp;&emsp; B. chance &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直喜欢唱歌，所以决定参加。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"失败"。decided to take part 意为"决定参加"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我反复练习直到能不忘词地唱完。A 项意为"记得"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"改变"。without forgetting 意为"不会忘词"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：观众会感受到你的热情。A 项意为"感受"；B 项意为"看到"；C 项意为"听到"；D 项意为"触摸"。feel your passion 意为"感受你的热情"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：前几句我的声音有点发抖。A 项意为"响的"；B 项意为"清晰的"；C 项意为"颤抖的"；D 项意为"柔和的"。紧张时声音 shaky。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后人们开始鼓掌。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"鼓掌"；D 项意为"说话"。表演后观众 clap。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：更重要的是，我克服了在众人面前表演的恐惧。A 项意为"重要地"；B 项意为"仔细地"；C 项意为"慢地"；D 项意为"快地"。more importantly 意为"更重要的是"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在自信多了。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"害羞的"；C 项意为"自信的"；D 项意为"担心的"。克服恐惧后变得 confident。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会继续唱歌，也许参加更多活动。A 项意为"拿"；B 项意为"参加"；C 项意为"举办"；D 项意为"错过"。join in activities 意为"参加活动"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信每次经历，无论输赢，都帮助我们成长。A 项意为"赢"；B 项意为"输"；C 项意为"成功"；D 项意为"奖品"。win or lose 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感激这次机会以及老师朋友的帮助。A 项意为"想法"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"功课"。grateful for the chance 意为"感激机会"。故选 B。
