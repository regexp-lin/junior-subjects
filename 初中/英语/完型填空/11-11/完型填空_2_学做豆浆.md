# 中考英语完形填空：学做豆浆

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my grandmother taught me how to make soybean milk at home. She said homemade soy milk was healthier and ___1___ than the one from shops. I was curious and wanted to learn.

First, we needed to soak the soybeans in water. Grandma said we should leave them for at least six hours, or ___2___ overnight. The beans would become soft and easier to break. In the morning, the beans had grown bigger. I poured out the water and rinsed them ___3___.

Next, we put the beans and some fresh water into a blender. We turned it on and let it run for about two minutes. The beans were broken into a white liquid. Grandma said we could add a little sugar if we liked it ___4___, but she preferred the original taste. I added just a little.

Then came the important step: we had to ___5___ the liquid. We used a piece of cloth to separate the soy milk from the bean residue. I poured the mixture slowly and squeezed the cloth gently. The smooth milk ran through, and the thick residue stayed in the cloth. We could use the residue to make other ___6___ if we wanted.

Finally, we boiled the soy milk in a pot. Grandma warned that we must boil it thoroughly, or it might not be safe to ___7___. We stirred it from time to time to avoid burning. When it was ready, the whole kitchen smelled ___8___. I poured a cup for myself. It was warm and tasty. Making soy milk was not difficult at all. I think I will make it again. Maybe I can try adding different flavors next time, such as red dates or peanuts. It's ___9___ to learn these life skills from our elders. They have so much ___10___ to pass on to us.

1. A. cheaper &emsp;&emsp; B. dearer &emsp;&emsp; C. sweeter &emsp;&emsp; D. bitter
2. A. better &emsp;&emsp; B. worse &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. earlier
3. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
4. A. salty &emsp;&emsp; B. sweet &emsp;&emsp; C. sour &emsp;&emsp; D. hot
5. A. drink &emsp;&emsp; B. filter &emsp;&emsp; C. mix &emsp;&emsp; D. pour
6. A. drinks &emsp;&emsp; B. dishes &emsp;&emsp; C. tools &emsp;&emsp; D. gifts
7. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. drink &emsp;&emsp; D. keep
8. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; D. weak
9. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. useless

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：自制豆浆比店里买的更健康更便宜。A 项意为"更便宜的"；B 项意为"更贵的"；C 项意为"更甜的"；D 项意为"更苦的"。homemade 通常更便宜。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：至少要泡六小时，或者泡一夜更好。A 项意为"更好"；B 项意为"更糟"；C 项意为"更晚"；D 项意为"更早"。or better overnight 意为"或者泡一夜更好"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我倒掉水，仔细冲洗豆子。A 项意为"慢地"；B 项意为"快地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。冲洗要 carefully。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果喜欢甜一点可以加点糖。A 项意为"咸的"；B 项意为"甜的"；C 项意为"酸的"；D 项意为"热的"。加糖是喜欢 sweet。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键一步是过滤液体。A 项意为"喝"；B 项意为"过滤"；C 项意为"混合"；D 项意为"倒"。分离豆浆和豆渣用 filter。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：豆渣可以用来做其他菜肴。A 项意为"饮料"；B 项意为"菜肴"；C 项意为"工具"；D 项意为"礼物"。豆渣可做菜，用 dishes。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须煮透，否则喝起来可能不安全。A 项意为"煮"；B 项意为"闻"；C 项意为"喝"；D 项意为"保存"。豆浆要 safe to drink。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：煮好时整个厨房闻起来很香。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"美妙的"；D 项意为"弱的"。豆浆香气用 wonderful。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：向长辈学习这些生活技能很有意义。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"无用的"。学习技能是 interesting。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：长辈有很多智慧传承给我们。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"智慧"；D 项意为"食物"。pass on 传承 wisdom。故选 C。
