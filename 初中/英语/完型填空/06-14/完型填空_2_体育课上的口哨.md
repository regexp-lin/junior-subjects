# 中考英语完形填空：体育课上的口哨

## 完形填空原文

Our PE teacher Mr. Wang always carries a small silver whistle. When he blows it, the whole class ___1___ and listens. That sound has become the signal of every PE lesson — it means "Stop and pay attention." For three years, I have heard that whistle hundreds of times, but I never thought it would ___2___ me one day.

It happened during the 800-meter run last month. I was the slowest in my class and always felt ___3___ when we had running practice. That day, when the race started, I fell behind quickly. Soon I was the only one still running on the track. I wanted to stop and give up.

Just then, I heard the whistle. But this time it was different — Mr. Wang was blowing it in a ___4___, rhythmic way. He was running beside me, keeping the same speed. "Keep going! Don't ___5___!" he shouted. The whistle seemed to match my footsteps, encouraging me to move forward. With his company, I ___6___ the finish line at last. I was last, but I had never felt prouder.

After that, Mr. Wang often used the whistle in a special way to encourage slower students. A short blow meant "You can do it." Two quick blows meant "Almost there." The whistle was no longer just a tool for ___7___. It became a language of care and support.

Now when I hear the whistle, I remember that day. I learned that the real ___8___ of PE is not about winning, but about trying our best and never giving up. Mr. Wang's whistle taught me that everyone has their own ___9___, and what matters is that we keep running toward it. The whistle will always remind me to be ___10___ to myself and to others who are still on their way.

## 选项

1. A. cries &emsp;&emsp; B. stops &emsp;&emsp; C. laughs &emsp;&emsp; D. sleeps
2. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. hit &emsp;&emsp; C. follow &emsp;&emsp; D. find
3. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. embarrassed &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
4. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. soft &emsp;&emsp; C. steady &emsp;&emsp; D. silent
5. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. laugh &emsp;&emsp; C. give up &emsp;&emsp; D. look back
6. A. missed &emsp;&emsp; B. crossed &emsp;&emsp; C. drew &emsp;&emsp; D. watched
7. A. fun &emsp;&emsp; B. order &emsp;&emsp; C. health &emsp;&emsp; D. safety
8. A. rule &emsp;&emsp; B. spirit &emsp;&emsp; C. score &emsp;&emsp; D. skill
9. A. dream &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. exam &emsp;&emsp; D. team
10. A. strict &emsp;&emsp; B. kind &emsp;&emsp; C. honest &emsp;&emsp; D. brave

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当他吹响口哨时，全班___并聆听。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"笑"；D 项意为"睡觉"。"stops"与后文"Stop and pay attention"相呼应，表示口哨声让同学停下并注意。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：三年来我听过那口哨声数百次，但从没想过有一天它会___我。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"击中"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"找到"。"help"与后文老师陪跑、鼓励的情节相呼应，表示口哨在关键时刻帮助了"我"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我是班里跑得最慢的，跑步行进时总感到___。A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"尴尬的"；D 项意为"放松的"。"embarrassed"与"the slowest"相呼应，表示因落后而不好意思。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但这次不同——王老师以一种___、有节奏的方式吹着口哨。A 项意为"响亮的"；B 项意为"轻柔的"；C 项意为"平稳的、有节奏的"；D 项意为"安静的"。"steady"与"rhythmic"并列，表示口哨声稳定而有节奏，与前文"match my footsteps"相呼应。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："坚持！别___！"他喊道。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"笑"；C 项意为"放弃"；D 项意为"回头看"。"give up"与"Keep going"形成对比，鼓励坚持到底，与前文"I wanted to stop and give up"相呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在他的陪伴下，我最终___了终点线。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"越过、穿过"；C 项意为"画"；D 项意为"观看"。"crossed the finish line"为固定搭配，表示冲过终点。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：口哨不再只是维持___的工具。A 项意为"乐趣"；B 项意为"秩序"；C 项意为"健康"；D 项意为"安全"。"order"与开头"Stop and pay attention"相呼应，表示口哨原本用于维持课堂秩序。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了体育真正的___不是赢，而是尽力而为、永不放弃。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"精神"；C 项意为"分数"；D 项意为"技能"。"spirit"与"not about winning, but about trying our best"相呼应，强调体育精神。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师的口哨教会我，每个人都有自己的___，重要的是我们不断朝它奔跑。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"队伍"。"dream"与"keep running toward it"相呼应，表示每个人都有目标与梦想。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：口哨会一直提醒我，对自己和仍在路上的他人要___。A 项意为"严格"；B 项意为"友善"；C 项意为"诚实"；D 项意为"勇敢"。"kind"与"care and support"相呼应，表示像老师一样给予他人鼓励与善意。故选 B。
