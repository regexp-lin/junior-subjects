# 中考英语完形填空：走廊里的饮水机

## 完形填空原文

At the end of our school hallway stands a simple drinking fountain. It has been there since I entered this school three years ago. Every day, hundreds of students ___1___ by it — some stop for a quick drink, others just walk past. To most people, it is nothing ___2___. But to me, it holds special memories.

In Grade 7, I was shy and had few friends. One noon, I was waiting in line at the fountain when a girl behind me said, "Your water bottle is really cute!" I turned around, ___3___. It was the first time someone had talked to me so friendly. We started chatting and soon became good friends. That small ___4___ at the fountain changed my school life.

Since then, the fountain has become a place where I meet my friends. We often gather there between classes, drinking water and sharing stories. Sometimes we ___5___ about our homework, sometimes we laugh about funny things that happened in class. The fountain has witnessed our friendship growing ___6___.

On hot summer days, the cool water from the fountain feels like a small gift. On busy exam days, a short break there helps me ___7___ and think clearly. The fountain never asks for anything. It just stands there, ___8___ water to whoever needs it.

I will graduate soon and leave this school. But I will always remember the fountain in the hallway. It taught me that ___9___ things in life often bring the greatest joy. A kind word, a cool drink, or a moment with friends — these are what make our days ___10___. Thank you, little fountain.

## 选项

1. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. pass &emsp;&emsp; C. jump &emsp;&emsp; D. sit
2. A. important &emsp;&emsp; B. expensive &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. new
3. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
4. A. talk &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. moment &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
5. A. worry &emsp;&emsp; B. complain &emsp;&emsp; C. think &emsp;&emsp; D. dream
6. A. stronger &emsp;&emsp; B. longer &emsp;&emsp; C. faster &emsp;&emsp; D. earlier
7. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. play &emsp;&emsp; D. eat
8. A. selling &emsp;&emsp; B. offering &emsp;&emsp; C. borrowing &emsp;&emsp; D. keeping
9. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
10. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. bright &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. busy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天，数百名学生从它旁边___。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"经过"；C 项意为"跳"；D 项意为"坐"。"pass by"意为"经过"，与后文"some stop for a quick drink, others just walk past"相呼应，表示学生日常经过饮水机。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对大多数人来说，它没什么___。A 项意为"重要的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"新的"。"nothing special"为常用表达，意为"没什么特别的"，与后文"But to me, it holds special memories"形成对比。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我转过身，感到___。A 项意为"惊讶的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。"surprised"与"It was the first time someone had talked to me so friendly"相呼应，表示害羞的作者第一次被主动搭话时的惊讶。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：饮水机旁那个小小的___改变了我的校园生活。A 项意为"谈话"；B 项意为"课程"；C 项意为"瞬间、时刻"；D 项意为"礼物"。"moment"指相遇的那一刻，与"that small"搭配，强调一个短暂的瞬间带来的深远影响。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们___作业，有时笑着谈论课上发生的趣事。A 项意为"担忧、发愁"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"思考"；D 项意为"梦想"。"worry about"意为"为……担忧"，与"homework"搭配，表示朋友间聊作业烦恼，贴近学生生活。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：饮水机见证了我们的友谊日渐___。A 项意为"更牢固"；B 项意为"更长久"；C 项意为"更快"；D 项意为"更早"。"growing stronger"表示友谊日益深厚，与"witnessed our friendship"相呼应。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：考试日忙碌时，在那里短暂休息能帮我___并理清思路。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"玩耍"；D 项意为"吃东西"。"rest"与"a short break"相呼应，表示稍作休息恢复状态。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：饮水机从不索取什么，只是站在那里，给需要的人___水。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"提供"；C 项意为"借"；D 项意为"保存"。"offering water to whoever needs it"表示主动提供，体现饮水机的默默奉献。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会我，生活中___的事物往往带来最大的快乐。A 项意为"大的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。"simple"与后文"A kind word, a cool drink"相呼应，强调平凡事物中的幸福感。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：正是这些让我们的日子变得___。A 项意为"漫长的"；B 项意为"明亮的、美好的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。"bright"在此处比喻日子充满温暖与希望，与前文"greatest joy"相呼应。故选 B。
