# 中考英语完形填空：爷爷的木工房

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather had a small workshop behind our house. It was full of wooden tools, sawdust, and the smell of fresh wood. When I was little, I was not allowed to go in — Grandpa said the tools were too ___1___ for a child. But I always stood at the door and watched him work.

As I grew older, Grandpa started teaching me. "Woodworking is not just about making things," he said. "It's about patience. You can't ___2___ a good piece. You have to wait for it."

I learned slowly. At first I only sanded wood — running my hand over the surface again and again until it felt smooth. It was boring, but Grandpa never hurried me. "Every master was once a ___3___," he would say.

When I was thirteen, Grandpa let me make my first real project: a small wooden box. I measured, sawed, and sanded for two weeks. When I finished, the corners weren't perfect. I felt ___4___. "Grandpa, it's not good enough."

He picked up the box and turned it over in his hands. "It's your first work. It's supposed to have marks. Those marks tell a story — your story. Perfection comes with time. What matters now is that you ___5___."

Years later, Grandpa's hands became unsteady. He could no longer hold the saw. But he still sat in the workshop every afternoon, ___6___ me while I worked. "Your turns are getting smoother," he would say. "You're learning."

I understood then. The workshop was never just about wood. It was about ___7___ — Grandfather and grandson, one generation passing knowledge to the next. The tools, the smell, the patient hours — they were all part of a language that didn't need words.

When Grandpa passed away last year, I inherited his workshop. I keep his old tools on the wall. Sometimes when I'm sanding a piece of wood, I still hear his ___8___: "Take your time. A good piece can't be rushed."

That wooden box I made at thirteen still sits on my desk. Its imperfect corners remind me that growth is a ___9___, not a finish line. And every time I walk into the workshop, I feel Grandpa's presence. He's not gone — he lives on in every cut, every sand, every lesson about patience and ___10___ that he left behind.

1. A. heavy &emsp;&emsp; B. sharp &emsp;&emsp; C. old &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
2. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. rush &emsp;&emsp; C. find &emsp;&emsp; D. break
3. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. beginner &emsp;&emsp; C. expert &emsp;&emsp; D. child
4. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. disappointed &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. excited
5. A. tried &emsp;&emsp; B. failed &emsp;&emsp; C. won &emsp;&emsp; D. quit
6. A. watching &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. teaching &emsp;&emsp; D. calling
7. A. love &emsp;&emsp; B. work &emsp;&emsp; C. connection &emsp;&emsp; D. memory
8. A. laugh &emsp;&emsp; B. voice &emsp;&emsp; C. song &emsp;&emsp; D. story
9. A. race &emsp;&emsp; B. game &emsp;&emsp; C. journey &emsp;&emsp; D. test
10. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. skill &emsp;&emsp; C. care &emsp;&emsp; D. luck

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷说工具对小孩来说太___了。A 项意为"重"；B 项意为"锋利"；C 项意为"旧"；D 项意为"贵"。"sharp"（锋利）与木工工具（锯子、凿子等）特性相符，小孩易受伤。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你不能___一件好作品。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"催促、赶制"；C 项意为"找到"；D 项意为"弄坏"。"rush"（急于完成）与"have to wait for it"（必须等待）形成对比，强调耐心。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个大师都曾是___。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"初学者"；C 项意为"专家"；D 项意为"孩子"。"beginner"（初学者）与"master"（大师）形成对比，鼓励从基础做起。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：角落不完美，我感到___。A 项意为"骄傲"；B 项意为"失望"；C 项意为"惊讶"；D 项意为"兴奋"。"disappointed"与"it's not good enough"相呼应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在重要的是你___了。A 项意为"尝试了"；B 项意为"失败了"；C 项意为"赢了"；D 项意为"放弃了"。"tried"强调过程比结果重要，完成第一次作品本身就是进步。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他每天下午仍坐在工房里，___我工作。A 项意为"观看"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"教导"；D 项意为"叫"。"watching"与"could no longer hold the saw"相呼应，爷爷已无力动手，只能在一旁看着。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：工房从来不只是木头，更是关于___。A 项意为"爱"；B 项意为"工作"；C 项意为"连结"；D 项意为"记忆"。"connection"（连结）概括祖孙间技艺与感情的传承。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仿佛仍能听见他的___。A 项意为"笑声"；B 项意为"声音、话语"；C 项意为"歌"；D 项意为"故事"。"voice"（声音）与后文引用的"Take your time..."相呼应。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不完美的角落提醒我，成长是一场___，不是终点。A 项意为"赛跑"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"旅程"；D 项意为"测试"。"journey"（旅程）与"not a finish line"相呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他留下的关于耐心和___的每一课。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"技巧"；C 项意为"用心、认真"；D 项意为"运气"。"care"（用心）与"patience"（耐心）并列，是爷爷传授的做事态度。故选 C。
