# 中考英语完形填空：课间广播的歌声

## 完形填空原文

Our school plays music during the ten-minute break between classes. Most students don't pay much attention — they're too busy talking, running to the bathroom, or finishing homework. I used to be the same. The music was just background ___1___, something that filled the empty air.

Then one morning everything changed. I was walking through the hallway, feeling stressed about a math test I had just failed. My head was down. I didn't want to talk to anyone. ___2___ a familiar song came through the speakers. It was a song my mother used to sing when I was little — a soft, gentle melody that always made me feel safe.

I stopped walking. I stood still in the middle of the busy hallway. For those few minutes, I wasn't thinking about the test or my grades. I was ___3___ back to my childhood, to rainy afternoons when Mom would sing while I played with my toys. The music wrapped around me like a warm blanket.

When the song ended, I realized I was crying. Not because I was sad, but because I had ___4___ how much I needed that moment. In our rush to get through each day, we often forget to feel. We forget that a simple song can ___5___ us to a different place — a memory, a feeling, a person we miss.

From that day on, I started to really listen to the break-time music. Some days it was classical. Some days it was pop. Each song had its own ___6___. I learned to use those ten minutes as a small gift to myself — a chance to breathe, to pause, to remember that life is more than grades and tests.

I even started a "song of the day" notebook. I wrote down which song played and how it made me feel. My friends thought I was ___7___. But when one of them had a bad day, I played her a song from my notebook. She sat quietly and listened. "I never knew the break music could actually mean something," she said.

Music is everywhere. We hear it in elevators, in shops, on our phones. But how often do we really ___8___ it? That morning in the hallway taught me something: the best things in life are often free and right in front of us. We just need to ___9___ down and notice. A song on the school speakers, a melody from the past — these small things can heal us, remind us, and bring us back to ourselves when we feel ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：音乐只是背景___。A 项意为"噪音"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"人声"；D 项意为"音乐"。"background sound"（背景声音）指被忽视的、填充环境的声响。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___熟悉的歌声从喇叭里传出。A 项意为"所以"；B 项意为"但是"；C 项意为"然后"；D 项意为"或者"。"Then"表示时间顺序，描述下一刻发生的事。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被___回了童年。A 项意为"带走"；B 项意为"带来"；C 项意为"送"；D 项意为"携带"。"be taken back to"（被带回到）是常用搭配，表示思绪被拉回过去。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为我___了自己多需要那一刻。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记起"；C 项意为"理解"；D 项意为"相信"。"remembered"（记起）与"forget to feel"形成对比，突然意识到自己需要那样的抚慰。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们忘记了简单的歌可以把我们___到不同地方。A 项意为"送"；B 项意为"带来"；C 项意为"带"；D 项意为"引导"。"take sb to"（把某人带到）与"a memory, a feeling"相呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每首歌都有它自己的___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"意义"；C 项意为"节奏"；D 项意为"歌手"。"meaning"（意义）与前文"could actually mean something"相呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：朋友觉得我很___。A 项意为"勇敢"；B 项意为"奇怪"；C 项意为"聪明"；D 项意为"善良"。"strange"（奇怪）与记"song of the day"笔记本的做法相呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们有多久真的___它了？A 项意为"听见"；B 项意为"倾听"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"播放"。"listen to"强调主动、用心地听，与"hear"（被动听到）形成对比。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只需要___下来去留意。A 项意为"坐"；B 项意为"放慢"；C 项意为"平静"；D 项意为"安静"。"slow down"（放慢脚步）与"notice"相呼应，指放慢节奏去感受。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们感到___时，这些小事物能治愈我们。A 项意为"迷失的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"忙的"；D 项意为"快乐的"。"lost"（迷失）与"bring us back to ourselves"相呼应，指在压力和焦虑中迷失自我。故选 A。
