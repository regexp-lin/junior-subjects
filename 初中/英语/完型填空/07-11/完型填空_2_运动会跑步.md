# 中考英语完形填空：运动会跑步

## 完形填空原文

Our school sports day was in October. I had signed up for the 800-metre run. To be honest, I was not a very good runner. I ran slowly and often got out of ___1___ quickly. But my PE teacher said everyone should try at least once. So I decided to ___2___ it a try.

Two weeks before the race, I started to practise. Every afternoon after school, I ran around the playground. At first, I could only run two laps before I had to stop. My legs felt heavy and my heart beat fast. I wanted to ___3___, but I thought of my teacher's words and kept going. Gradually, I could run a little further each day.

On the day of the race, I stood at the starting line with the other runners. My hands were ___4___. "Ready? Go!" the teacher shouted. We all started running. I tried to keep a steady pace and not run too fast at the beginning. Some runners passed me in the first minute. I felt a little discouraged, but I told myself to focus on my own race.

By the second lap, I was tired. My breath came in short ___5___. But I remembered my training. I had run this far many times. I could do it. I kept my eyes on the track ahead and kept moving. One by one, I passed a few runners who had started too fast and were now slowing down.

When I saw the finish line, I gathered all my ___6___ and ran as fast as I could. The crowd was cheering. I crossed the line and bent over, hands on my knees, breathing hard. I did not win. I came in fifth. But when I looked up, I saw my classmates running towards me with big smiles. "You did it! You finished!" they shouted.

That moment, I did not care about the ___7___. What mattered was that I had tried my best and never stopped. The PE teacher patted my shoulder and said, "Well done. The real victory is not always coming first. It is about overcoming yourself." I will always ___8___ that race. It taught me that with determination and practice, we can achieve more than we think. We do not have to be the best to feel ___9___. Running that race changed my ___10___ about sports. It is not only for the fastest — it is for everyone who is willing to try. We just need to do our best.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我跑得慢，经常很快___。A 项意为"呼吸"；B 项意为"心智"；C 项意为"视线"；D 项意为"控制"。"get out of breath"意为"上气不接下气"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定___一次。A 项意为"给"；B 项意为"拿"；C 项意为"做"；D 项意为"有"。"give it a try"意为"试一试"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想___，但想起老师的话就坚持了。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"放弃"；D 项意为"获胜"。累的时候想放弃，"quit"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手___。A 项意为"温暖"；B 项意为"干净"；C 项意为"出汗的"；D 项意为"受伤的"。赛前紧张会手心出汗，"sweaty"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：呼吸变得短而急促。A 项意为"话"；B 项意为"步伐"；C 项意为"喘息"；D 项意为"笑声"。"short gasps"意为"急促的喘息"，描述跑步时的呼吸。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我集中全部___冲向终点。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"力气"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"金钱"。"gather strength"意为"积蓄力量、拼尽全力"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻我不在意___。A 项意为"天气"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"结果、名次"；D 项意为"人群"。没得第一但不介意名次，"result"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会永远___那场比赛。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"重复"；D 项意为"观看"。难忘的经历用"remember"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不必得第一也能感到___。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"难过的"；D 项意为"担心的"。尽力而为就会有自豪感，"proud"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那场比赛改变了我对体育的___。A 项意为"想法、看法"；B 项意为"身体"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"规则"。"changed my mind about"意为"改变了对……的看法"。故选 A。
