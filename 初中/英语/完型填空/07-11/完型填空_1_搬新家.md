# 中考英语完形填空：搬新家

## 完形填空原文

Last month, my family moved to a new house. It was in a different part of the city, closer to my school. I had lived in our old home for twelve years, so I felt ___1___ about leaving. I would miss the big tree in the garden where I used to play, and the neighbours who always said hello.

On the moving day, we got up early. The movers came with a truck and carried all our ___2___ out of the old house. I helped pack my books and toys into boxes. Mum said we had to be careful with the fragile things. I wrapped my favourite mug in newspaper and put it in a box marked "Handle with ___3___."

The new house was bigger and had more rooms. My bedroom had a large window that faced the garden. I could see a small pond and some trees from my window. "You can put your desk here by the window," Dad said. "The natural ___4___ will be good for your eyes when you study." I liked that idea.

The first few nights in the new house felt strange. I lay in bed and heard unfamiliar sounds. The floor creaked sometimes. I could not ___5___ as well as before. Mum said it was normal. "It takes time to feel at home in a new place," she said. "Give yourself a week or two."

After two weeks, I began to ___6___ the new house. I made friends with the boy next door. We played basketball in his garden. I also found a new route to school — it was only a ten-minute walk. The old house was a forty-minute bus ride away. What a ___7___!

One weekend, we invited our old neighbours to visit. They came and had dinner with us. We showed them around the new house. They said it was lovely. I realised that home is not just a ___8___. It is the people we love and the memories we carry with us. We can make new memories in the new house, but we will always ___9___ the old ones. Moving taught me that change can be good, and we can learn to adapt and find ___10___ wherever we go.

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. sad &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
2. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. furniture &emsp;&emsp; C. clothes &emsp;&emsp; D. gifts
3. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. speed &emsp;&emsp; C. force &emsp;&emsp; D. warmth
4. A. light &emsp;&emsp; B. dark &emsp;&emsp; C. wind &emsp;&emsp; D. heat
5. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. study &emsp;&emsp; D. play
6. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. dislike &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
7. A. problem &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. mistake &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
8. A. school &emsp;&emsp; B. building &emsp;&emsp; C. room &emsp;&emsp; D. garden
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. change
10. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. happiness &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. fear

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：住了十二年的老房子，要离开我感到___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"难过的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。离开熟悉的家会不舍，"sad"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：搬家工人用卡车把我们的___搬出老房子。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"家具、物品"；C 项意为"衣服"；D 项意为"礼物"。搬家主要搬家具和 belongings，"furniture"可泛指家当。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：盒子写上"小心___"。A 项意为"小心、注意"；B 项意为"速度"；C 项意为"力气"；D 项意为"温暖"。"Handle with care"意为"小心轻放"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自然___对学习时的眼睛有好处。A 项意为"光线"；B 项意为"黑暗"；C 项意为"风"；D 项意为"热"。靠窗学习利用自然光，"light"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不像以前那样___得好。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"睡"；C 项意为"学习"；D 项意为"玩"。新环境不熟悉、有陌生声响会影响睡眠，"sleep"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两周后我开始___新家。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"不喜欢"；C 项意为"享受、喜欢"；D 项意为"忘记"。适应后开始喜欢新家，"enjoy"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：以前要坐四十分钟公交，现在是十分钟步行。真是___！A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"区别、改进"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"惊喜"。"What a difference!"意为"差别真大/好多了"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：家不仅仅是___。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"建筑、房子"；C 项意为"房间"；D 项意为"花园"。家不只是一栋建筑，更是人和回忆。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们永远会___老房子的回忆。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"改变"。珍视旧回忆用"remember"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们能学会适应，无论去哪里都能找到___。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"幸福"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"恐惧"。积极结尾，在新地方找到幸福，"happiness"正确。故选 B。
