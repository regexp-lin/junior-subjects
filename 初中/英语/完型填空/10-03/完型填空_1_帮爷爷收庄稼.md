# 中考英语完形填空：帮爷爷收庄稼

## 完形填空原文

During the National Day holiday, my family drove to the countryside to visit my grandparents. Grandpa told us that it was harvest season and he needed some ___1___ in the field.

The next morning, I woke up very early. The air in the countryside was fresh and cool. After a simple breakfast, Grandpa took me to the corn field. I had never seen so many tall corn plants before. They stood in neat ___2___ like soldiers.

Grandpa showed me how to pick the corn. "Hold the ear of corn firmly, twist it, and pull it down," he said. It looked ___3___, but when I tried, it was harder than I thought. My hands were not strong enough, and the first ear took me a long time. Grandpa laughed and said, "You'll get ___4___ at it with practice."

After about an hour, my arms were sore and my back hurt. I wanted to ___5___, but I looked at Grandpa — he was still working steadily without any complaint. He was already seventy years old. I felt ashamed and decided to keep going.

By noon, we had filled several large bags with corn. Grandpa looked at the bags and smiled with ___6___. "This has been a good year," he said. We carried the bags back home together. Grandma had already prepared a big lunch for us.

During lunch, Grandpa told me about how ___7___ farming used to be. "When I was young, we did everything by hand. There were no machines," he said. "Now things are much easier, but farmers still work very hard." His words made me realize how much effort goes into the food on our ___8___.

That afternoon, I helped Grandma dry some corn in the yard. The golden corn shining under the sun was a beautiful ___9___. I took some photos and sent them to my classmates. They were all jealous and said they wanted to come next time.

This trip taught me to ___10___ the hard work of farmers. Every grain of rice and every piece of food comes from their sweat. I will never waste food again.

## 选项

1. A. advice &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
2. A. circles &emsp;&emsp; B. rows &emsp;&emsp; C. groups &emsp;&emsp; D. piles
3. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; D. impossible
4. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. slower &emsp;&emsp; D. sadder
5. A. start &emsp;&emsp; B. continue &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. run
6. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. anger &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. satisfaction
7. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. fun &emsp;&emsp; D. short
8. A. table &emsp;&emsp; B. desk &emsp;&emsp; C. bed &emsp;&emsp; D. shelf
9. A. sound &emsp;&emsp; B. sight &emsp;&emsp; C. taste &emsp;&emsp; D. touch
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. doubt &emsp;&emsp; C. respect &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷告诉我们这是收获季节，他需要一些帮忙。A 项意为"建议"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"休息"。need some help 意为"需要帮忙"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：玉米整齐地排成行，像士兵一样。A 项意为"圆圈"；B 项意为"行、排"；C 项意为"组"；D 项意为"堆"。庄稼排成 rows。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来很容易，但当我尝试时比想象的要难。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"不可能的"。与后文 harder 形成转折。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：你练习后会做得更好。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更慢"；D 项意为"更悲伤"。get better at 意为"变得更擅长"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想停下来，但看到爷爷还在稳稳地干活。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"停下"；D 项意为"跑"。胳膊酸背痛，想 stop。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷看着装满的袋子，满意地笑了。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"满意"。丰收后 smiled with satisfaction。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷告诉我过去种地是多么辛苦。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"辛苦的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"短的"。后文提到全靠手工，说明很 hard。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话让我意识到我们餐桌上的食物要付出多少努力。A 项意为"桌子"；B 项意为"书桌"；C 项意为"床"；D 项意为"架子"。food on our table 意为"餐桌上的食物"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：金灿灿的玉米在阳光下闪闪发光，是一幅美丽的景象。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"景象"；C 项意为"味道"；D 项意为"触觉"。视觉上的美用 sight。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次旅行教会我尊重农民的辛勤劳动。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"怀疑"；C 项意为"尊重"；D 项意为"避免"。respect the hard work 意为"尊重辛勤劳动"。故选 C。
