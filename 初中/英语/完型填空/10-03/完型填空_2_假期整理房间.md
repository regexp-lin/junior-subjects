# 中考英语完形填空：假期整理房间

## 完形填空原文

On the third day of the holiday, my mother looked at my messy room and shook her head. "You really need to ___1___ your room," she said. "It looks like a disaster zone." I looked around and had to agree. Books, clothes, and toys were everywhere.

I started with my desk. There were piles of old papers, broken pens, and empty snack bags. I ___2___ the useless things into a big garbage bag and organized the rest neatly. I found some old exam papers from last year and realized how much I had ___3___ since then.

Next, I moved to my bookshelf. I took out all the books and sorted them by subject. I put my English books together, my math books together, and my science books together. I also found a novel that my friend had ___4___ me two months ago. I had totally forgotten about it. I made a note to return it to her.

The hardest part was my closet. I had so many clothes that the door could ___5___ close. I decided to pick out the ones I no longer wore. Some were too small for me — I had grown a lot this year. Mum said we could ___6___ them to younger children in the community. I thought that was a great idea.

After three hours of hard work, my room looked completely ___7___. The floor was clean, the desk was tidy, and everything was in its proper place. I opened the window to let fresh air in. Standing there, I felt a wonderful sense of ___8___.

Mum came in and was pleasantly surprised. "I can't believe this is the same room!" she said. Dad also came to look and gave me a thumbs up. Their praise made me feel ___9___.

I learned something important that day. A clean and organized space can make you feel calm and focused. From now on, I will spend a few minutes every day to keep my room ___10___. It's a small habit, but it can make a big difference in life.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你真的需要打扫你的房间。A 项意为"刷漆"；B 项意为"打扫"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"离开"。messy room 需要 clean。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把没用的东西扔进大垃圾袋。A 项意为"扔"；B 项意为"放"；C 项意为"寄"；D 项意为"推"。threw into garbage bag 意为"扔进垃圾袋"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现去年的旧试卷，意识到自己进步了多少。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"提高"；D 项意为"忘记"。看到旧试卷感慨 improved。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还找到一本两个月前朋友借给我的小说。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"借（给）"；C 项意为"教"；D 项意为"展示"。后文要 return，说明是朋友 lent 给我的。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：衣服太多了，门几乎关不上。A 项意为"容易地"；B 项意为"几乎不"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"总是"。衣服多到门 hardly close。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说我们可以把它们捐给社区里的小孩子。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"捐赠"；D 项意为"藏"。donate to younger children 意为"捐给小孩子"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：三个小时的辛苦后，房间看起来完全不同了。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"暗的"；C 项意为"不同的"；D 项意为"小的"。整理前后 completely different。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到一种美妙的成就感。A 项意为"幽默"；B 项意为"危险"；C 项意为"成就"；D 项意为"内疚"。完成整理后的 a sense of achievement。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的表扬让我感到高兴。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"高兴的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"疲倦的"。受到 praise 感到 happy。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从现在起，我每天花几分钟保持房间整洁。A 项意为"凌乱的"；B 项意为"嘈杂的"；C 项意为"空的"；D 项意为"整洁的"。keep my room neat 意为"保持房间整洁"。故选 D。
