# 中考英语完形填空：第一次参加校外研学旅行

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our class went on a study trip to a science museum in another city. It was my first time joining an off-campus educational tour. I was both excited and a little ___1___. We had to get up very early and take a bus for two hours to get there.

When we arrived, the museum guide welcomed us and gave us a brief ___2___ of the museum. She said we would visit four halls—Space, Ocean, Technology, and Future. Each hall had interactive exhibits. We could touch the screens, push buttons, and even experience a simulated earthquake. It was much more ___3___ than just reading from books.

My favorite part was the Space Hall. I put on a VR headset and "walked" on the surface of the moon. It felt so real that I could ___4___ believe it was not true. I also saw a huge model of the solar system. The planets moved slowly around the sun. The guide explained the distance between the Earth and the sun. The numbers were so large that they were ___5___ to imagine.

At noon, we had lunch in the museum cafeteria. The teacher reminded us to stay in groups and not to ___6___ away from the team. After lunch, we had free time to explore. I bought some postcards and a small robot toy at the gift shop as souvenirs. I would give the postcards to my parents and ___7___ the robot for my younger brother.

On the way back, we shared our experiences on the bus. Everyone agreed that it was a memorable trip. We had learned so much outside the classroom. The teacher said our school would organize more study trips in the ___8___. I cannot wait for the next one. This trip made me realize that learning can happen ___9___—not only in the classroom but also in the world around us. All of ___10___ felt grateful for this wonderful experience.

## 选项

1. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. introduction &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. book &emsp;&emsp; D. letter
3. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
4. A. always &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. often &emsp;&emsp; D. sometimes
5. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. slow
6. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. walk &emsp;&emsp; C. go &emsp;&emsp; D. fly
7. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. keep &emsp;&emsp; D. bring
8. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. future &emsp;&emsp; C. morning &emsp;&emsp; D. evening
9. A. somewhere &emsp;&emsp; B. nowhere &emsp;&emsp; C. anywhere &emsp;&emsp; D. everywhere
10. A. us &emsp;&emsp; B. you &emsp;&emsp; C. them &emsp;&emsp; D. it

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：既兴奋又有点紧张。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项 nervous 意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"生气的"。第一次参加用 nervous。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：给我们简单介绍了博物馆。A 项 introduction 意为"介绍"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"信"。give a brief introduction 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比光看书有趣得多。A 项意为"难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项 interesting 意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"贵的"。互动展品更有趣。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：真实得几乎不敢相信是假的。A 项意为"总是"；B 项 hardly 意为"几乎不"；C 项意为"经常"；D 项意为"有时"。could hardly believe 表难以相信。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：数字大得难以想象。A 项意为"容易"；B 项 hard 意为"困难"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"慢"。hard to imagine 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：提醒我们不要离队。A 项 run away 意为"逃跑"；B 项 walk 不搭配 away；C 项 go away 意为"离开"；D 项 fly away 意为"飞走"。go away from team 离队。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把机器人留给弟弟。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项 keep for 意为"留给"；D 项意为"带来"。礼物留给弟弟用 keep for。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校未来会组织更多研学活动。A 项意为"过去"；B 项 in the future 意为"未来"；C 项意为"早上"；D 项意为"晚上"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 代词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学习可以随时随地发生。A 项意为"某处"；B 项意为"无处"；C 项 anywhere 多用于否定疑问；D 项 everywhere 意为"到处"。强调处处可学用 everywhere。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们所有人都对这次经历心怀感激。A 项 us 用于 all of us；B 项意为"你们"；C 项意为"他们"；D 项意为"它"。all of us 指我们大家。故选 A。
