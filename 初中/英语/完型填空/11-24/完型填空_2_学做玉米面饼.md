# 中考英语完形填空：学做玉米面饼

## 完形填空原文

My grandmother comes from the north of China, where people often eat corn pancakes. Last Sunday she decided to teach me how to make them. I had never tried corn flour before and was curious about the ___1___.

Grandma said we needed corn flour, wheat flour, warm water, and a pinch of salt. She mixed them in a large bowl. The ratio of corn to wheat flour was important—too much corn would make the pancakes ___2___. I helped her stir the mixture until it became a soft dough. Then we let it rest for about fifteen minutes. Grandma said this step could not be ___3___ because the dough needed time to absorb the water.

Next, we divided the dough into small balls and flattened each one with our hands. Grandma showed me how to make them round and of equal ___4___. I tried my best but my first few were a bit uneven. She smiled and said I would get better with practice. We heated a flat pan and brushed a thin layer of oil on it. When the pan was hot enough, we placed the pancakes on it. We had to ___5___ them over when one side turned golden brown.

The smell of the cooking pancakes filled the kitchen. I could not wait to taste them. When they were done, Grandma put them on a plate. I took a bite—the outside was slightly crispy and the inside was soft. They tasted ___6___ than I had expected. Grandma said we could eat them with savory dishes or spread some sauce on top. I had two pancakes for lunch and felt full and ___7___.

Making corn pancakes with Grandma was a meaningful experience. She told me this was a traditional food from her hometown. Learning to make it helped me understand her ___8___ and the food culture of northern China. I will try to make these pancakes again and maybe ___9___ the recipe with my friends. Traditional food connects us to our roots and brings ___10___ to our table.

## 选项

1. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. price &emsp;&emsp; C. color &emsp;&emsp; D. size
2. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. delicious
3. A. skipped &emsp;&emsp; B. done &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoyed &emsp;&emsp; D. finished
4. A. weight &emsp;&emsp; B. shape &emsp;&emsp; C. thickness &emsp;&emsp; D. color
5. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. put &emsp;&emsp; C. take &emsp;&emsp; D. throw
6. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. less &emsp;&emsp; D. more
7. A. hungry &emsp;&emsp; B. satisfied &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
8. A. childhood &emsp;&emsp; B. hobby &emsp;&emsp; C. job &emsp;&emsp; D. plan
9. A. sell &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
10. A. worry &emsp;&emsp; B. sadness &emsp;&emsp; C. warmth &emsp;&emsp; D. fear

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从没试过玉米面，好奇它的味道。A 项 taste 意为"味道"；B 项意为"价格"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"尺寸"。食物用 taste。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：玉米太多会导致饼过硬。A 项意为"软的"；B 项 hard 意为"硬的"；C 项意为"甜的"；D 项意为"美味的"。比例不对会变 hard。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这步不能跳过。A 项 skip 意为"跳过"；B 项意为"做"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"完成"。不能省去的步骤用 cannot be skipped。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做成圆形且厚度均匀。A 项意为"重量"；B 项意为"形状"；C 项 thickness 意为"厚度"；D 项意为"颜色"。做饼皮用 equal thickness。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一面金黄后要翻面。A 项 turn over 意为"翻面"；B 项意为"放"；C 项意为"拿"；D 项意为"扔"。煎饼翻面用 turn over。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道比预期更好。A 项意为"更差"；B 项 better 意为"更好"；C 项意为"更少"；D 项意为"更多"。超预期用 better than expected。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：吃得又饱又满足。A 项意为"饿的"；B 项 satisfied 意为"满足的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"生气的"。吃得满意用 satisfied。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮我理解奶奶的童年和北方饮食文化。A 项 childhood 意为"童年"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"计划"。传统食物与 hometown 关联 childhood。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许会和朋友们分享食谱。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"买"；C 项 share 意为"分享"；D 项意为"藏"。分享食谱用 share。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：传统美食连接我们的根，为餐桌带来温暖。A 项意为"担忧"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项 warmth 意为"温暖"；D 项意为"恐惧"。家庭美食用 brings warmth。故选 C。
