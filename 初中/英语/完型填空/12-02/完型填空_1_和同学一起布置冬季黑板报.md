# 中考英语完形填空：和同学一起布置冬季黑板报

## 完形填空原文

Every month, our class needs to design a new blackboard newspaper. This time, the theme was "Winter Wonderland." Our teacher asked me and three classmates to ___1___ the task together.

We held a short meeting after school to discuss our plan. Wang Lei suggested drawing a big snowman in the center. Zhang Mei wanted to add some winter poems. I thought we could include some fun facts about ___2___. Everyone had different ideas, but we listened to each other and finally agreed on a design that included all our suggestions.

The next day, we started working. Wang Lei was ___3___ at drawing, so he sketched the snowman and some pine trees. Zhang Mei wrote a beautiful poem in both Chinese and English with her neat handwriting. I was responsible for the fun facts section, where I wrote about why no two snowflakes are ___4___. Li Hua decorated the borders with colorful chalk.

It took us two afternoons to ___5___ the blackboard newspaper. During the process, we faced some problems. At one point, Wang Lei accidentally erased part of Zhang Mei's poem. She was a little ___6___ at first, but Wang Lei said sorry right away. Then they worked together to rewrite the poem, and it looked even ___7___ than before.

When our classmates saw the finished blackboard newspaper on Monday morning, they were all impressed. Our teacher gave us a big ___8___ and said it was the best one this semester. She also said that the secret to a great blackboard newspaper is not just artistic skill, but ___9___ among team members.

I felt proud of our work. This experience taught me that cooperation can turn ordinary ideas into something ___10___. I look forward to working with my classmates again.

1. A. avoid &emsp;&emsp; B. complete &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. refuse
2. A. summer &emsp;&emsp; B. spring &emsp;&emsp; C. winter &emsp;&emsp; D. autumn
3. A. poor &emsp;&emsp; B. good &emsp;&emsp; C. slow &emsp;&emsp; D. weak
4. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. big &emsp;&emsp; C. alike &emsp;&emsp; D. old
5. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. break &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. begin
6. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. upset &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
7. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. smaller &emsp;&emsp; D. darker
8. A. task &emsp;&emsp; B. punishment &emsp;&emsp; C. thumbs-up &emsp;&emsp; D. question
9. A. competition &emsp;&emsp; B. argument &emsp;&emsp; C. cooperation &emsp;&emsp; D. silence
10. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. ordinary &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. wonderful

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我和三位同学一起完成这个任务。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"拒绝"。complete the task 意为"完成任务"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得可以加入一些关于冬天的有趣知识。A 项意为"夏天"；B 项意为"春天"；C 项意为"冬天"；D 项意为"秋天"。主题是 Winter Wonderland，故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王磊擅长画画，所以他来画雪人和松树。A 项意为"差的"；B 项意为"好的"；C 项意为"慢的"；D 项意为"弱的"。be good at 意为"擅长"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写了为什么没有两片雪花是一样的。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"大的"；C 项意为"相似的"；D 项意为"老的"。no two snowflakes are alike 意为"没有两片雪花相同"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们花了两个下午完成了黑板报。A 项意为"完成"；B 项意为"打破"；C 项意为"丢失"；D 项意为"开始"。finish the blackboard newspaper。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她一开始有点不高兴。A 项意为"高兴的"；B 项意为"沮丧的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。作品被误擦会感到 upset。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重写的诗看起来比之前更好。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更小"；D 项意为"更暗"。even better than before 意为"比之前更好"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师给了我们一个大大的赞。A 项意为"任务"；B 项意为"惩罚"；C 项意为"赞/竖起大拇指"；D 项意为"问题"。gave us a big thumbs-up 表示表扬。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好黑板报的秘诀不仅是艺术技巧，还有团队成员之间的合作。A 项意为"竞争"；B 项意为"争论"；C 项意为"合作"；D 项意为"沉默"。cooperation 呼应全文主题。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：合作可以把普通的想法变成了不起的东西。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"精彩的"。ordinary 与 wonderful 形成对比。故选 D。
