# 中考英语完形填空：学做姜汁红糖水

## 完形填空原文

On a cold December evening, I came home from school feeling very cold. My grandmother was in the kitchen making something that smelled warm and sweet. She told me she was making ginger and brown sugar water—a traditional Chinese ___1___ to keep warm in winter.

I asked if I could learn how to make it. Grandmother smiled and said, "Of course!" First, she told me to wash a piece of fresh ginger and cut it into thin ___2___. She reminded me to be careful with the knife. Then I put the ginger slices into a small pot and added two cups of water.

Next, Grandmother told me to turn on the stove and ___3___ the water slowly. While waiting, she showed me how to break the brown sugar into small pieces. She said brown sugar is better for your ___4___ than white sugar, especially in winter. After the water started to boil, I added the brown sugar and stirred it gently with a spoon.

The kitchen was filled with a sweet and spicy smell. Grandmother said we needed to let it cook for about ten more ___5___. During the wait, she told me that when she was young, her mother used to make this drink for the whole family on cold nights. It was a way to show ___6___ and care.

Finally, the ginger brown sugar water was ready. I poured it into two cups and gave one to Grandmother. We sat together and drank it ___7___. The warm liquid went down my throat and I could feel my body getting warm from the ___8___. It was simple but very comforting.

Grandmother said, "The best food is not always the most expensive. Sometimes, the ___9___ things bring the most warmth." I agreed. Making ginger brown sugar water was easy, but it carried the love of ___10___. I will remember this recipe and this warm evening forever.

7. A. sadly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：姜汁红糖水是一种传统的中式饮品。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"饮品"；C 项意为"玩具"；D 项意为"运动"。ginger and brown sugar water 是一种 drink。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把生姜切成薄片。A 项意为"薄片"；B 项意为"球"；C 项意为"方块"；D 项意为"条"。cut into thin slices 意为"切成薄片"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：开火慢慢加热水。A 项意为"冷却"；B 项意为"冷冻"；C 项意为"加热"；D 项意为"倒"。heat the water 意为"加热水"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：红糖比白糖对健康更好。A 项意为"眼睛"；B 项意为"健康"；C 项意为"体重"；D 项意为"牙齿"。better for your health 意为"对健康更好"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：还需要再煮大约十分钟。A 项意为"小时"；B 项意为"天"；C 项意为"分钟"；D 项意为"秒"。煮饮品需要 ten more minutes。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是表达爱和关怀的方式。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"爱"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"骄傲"。为家人做饮品是表达 love。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们坐在一起慢慢喝。A 项意为"伤心地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。热饮要 slowly 品味。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：能感觉到身体从内部变暖。A 项意为"外面"；B 项意为"顶部"；C 项意为"内部"；D 项意为"后面"。warm from the inside 意为"从内部变暖"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最简单的东西带来最多的温暖。A 项意为"最难的"；B 项意为"最简单的"；C 项意为"最奇怪的"；D 项意为"最冷的"。与 most expensive 对应，the simplest things。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它承载着一代代人的爱。A 项意为"邻居"；B 项意为"陌生人"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"世代"。奶奶传承给我的食谱体现 love of generations。故选 D。
