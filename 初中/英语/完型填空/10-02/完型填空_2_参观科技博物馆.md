# 中考英语完形填空：参观科技博物馆

## 完形填空原文

On October 2nd, my best friend Li Hua and I visited the city science museum. We had been looking ___1___ to this trip for a long time.

When we arrived, the museum was already crowded with visitors. The building was huge and modern. We first went to the Space Hall. There were models of rockets and satellites hanging from the ceiling. A big screen showed a video about how astronauts live and work in space. I was ___2___ by the fact that astronauts have to exercise for two hours every day to keep their muscles strong.

Next, we entered the Robot Exhibition. A small robot greeted us at the door and said, "Welcome!" Li Hua was so surprised that he almost ___3___ his phone. The robot could dance, answer questions, and even play chess. We watched it play against a visitor and the robot ___4___ easily. "Robots are getting smarter and smarter," Li Hua said.

Then we went to the Science Experiment Area. We were ___5___ to try some experiments ourselves. I made a simple circuit that lit up a small bulb. Li Hua built a model bridge and tested how much ___6___ it could hold. It was exciting to learn science by doing things with our own hands.

The most impressive part was the 4D cinema. We watched a short film about deep-sea creatures. The seats moved, wind blew on our faces, and water sprayed lightly on us. It felt like we were really ___7___ in the ocean. Both of us screamed with excitement.

Before leaving, we stopped at the gift shop. I bought a small telescope and Li Hua chose a book about ___8___. We both agreed that this was one of the best days of our holiday.

On the way home, we talked about what we had seen and learned. Li Hua said he wanted to become a scientist when he grew ___9___. I told him I was interested in space exploration. We both believed that science and technology would make the world a ___10___ place.

## 选项

1. A. back &emsp;&emsp; B. forward &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down
2. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. amazed &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointed
3. A. sold &emsp;&emsp; B. broke &emsp;&emsp; C. dropped &emsp;&emsp; D. hid
4. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. cried &emsp;&emsp; C. won &emsp;&emsp; D. left
5. A. told &emsp;&emsp; B. refused &emsp;&emsp; C. warned &emsp;&emsp; D. allowed
6. A. water &emsp;&emsp; B. weight &emsp;&emsp; C. light &emsp;&emsp; D. heat
7. A. flying &emsp;&emsp; B. swimming &emsp;&emsp; C. running &emsp;&emsp; D. climbing
8. A. cooking &emsp;&emsp; B. sports &emsp;&emsp; C. inventions &emsp;&emsp; D. music
9. A. old &emsp;&emsp; B. up &emsp;&emsp; C. tall &emsp;&emsp; D. strong
10. A. darker &emsp;&emsp; B. worse &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. smaller

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们盼望这次旅行很久了。A 项 look back 意为"回顾"；B 项 look forward to 意为"期待"；C 项 look up 意为"查找"；D 项 look down 意为"向下看"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：宇航员每天要锻炼两小时以保持肌肉强壮，这让我很惊讶。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"失望的"。新奇事实让人 amazed。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李华太惊讶了，差点掉了手机。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"打破"；C 项意为"掉落"；D 项意为"藏"。惊讶到差点 dropped his phone。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们看机器人跟一位游客下棋，机器人轻松赢了。A 项意为"输"；B 项意为"哭"；C 项意为"赢"；D 项意为"离开"。won easily 表示"轻松获胜"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们被允许自己尝试一些实验。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"拒绝"；C 项意为"警告"；D 项意为"允许"。be allowed to 意为"被允许"。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李华搭了一个桥模型，测试它能承受多少重量。A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"重量"；C 项意为"光"；D 项意为"热"。测试桥的承重用 weight。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感觉像我们真的在海洋里游泳。A 项意为"飞"；B 项意为"游泳"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"爬"。deep-sea 深海，所以是 swimming。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李华选了一本关于发明的书。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"运动"；C 项意为"发明"；D 项意为"音乐"。在科技馆买关于 inventions 的书最合理。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：李华说他长大后想成为科学家。A 项 grew old 意为"变老"；B 项 grew up 意为"长大"；C 项 grew tall 意为"长高"；D 项 grew strong 意为"变强壮"。when he grew up 意为"长大后"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都相信科技会让世界变成一个更好的地方。A 项意为"更暗的"；B 项意为"更差的"；C 项意为"更好的"；D 项意为"更小的"。科技让世界 better。故选 C。
