# 中考英语完形填空：假期学做饭

## 完形填空原文

During the National Day holiday, I decided to learn to cook. I had always wanted to make a meal for my family, but I never had enough ___1___ during school days.

On the morning of October 2nd, I got up early and went to the kitchen. My mother was surprised to see me there. "What are you doing?" she asked. I told her I wanted to learn to ___2___ tomato and egg stir-fry. She smiled and said she would teach me step by step.

First, she showed me how to crack the eggs into a bowl. I was too ___3___ and broke the first egg onto the table. Mum laughed and said, "Don't worry. Everyone makes mistakes at the beginning." I tried again, and this time I did it ___4___.

Next, I washed the tomatoes and cut them into pieces. Mum reminded me to be careful with the knife. I held it ___5___ and cut slowly. Then she asked me to heat the oil in the pan. When the oil was hot, I poured the eggs in. They sizzled and turned golden quickly. I stirred them with a spatula and felt like a real ___6___.

After that, I added the tomatoes and some salt, sugar, and soy sauce. The kitchen was filled with a wonderful ___7___. A few minutes later, the dish was ready. I put it on a plate and carried it to the dining table.

My father took the first bite. "Not bad at all!" he said with a big smile. My little brother liked it too and asked for a ___8___ serving. I felt so happy and proud. All the effort was worth it.

Mum said cooking was an important life ___9___. She told me that knowing how to cook would help me in the future. I agreed. From that day on, I made a plan to learn one new dish every week.

Learning to cook taught me that trying new things is not as ___10___ as it seems. With patience and practice, anyone can do it.

6. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. doctor &emsp;&emsp; C. driver &emsp;&emsp; D. chef
7. A. smell &emsp;&emsp; B. noise &emsp;&emsp; C. color &emsp;&emsp; D. view
8. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. second &emsp;&emsp; C. last &emsp;&emsp; D. small
9. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. skill &emsp;&emsp; C. test &emsp;&emsp; D. dream

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直想给家人做顿饭，但上学时没有足够的时间。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"空间"。上学期间缺少 time。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我告诉她我想学做番茄炒蛋。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"做（饭）"；D 项意为"种植"。learn to cook 意为"学做饭"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太紧张了，把第一个蛋打到了桌子上。A 项意为"仔细的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"耐心的"；D 项意为"懒的"。第一次做所以 nervous。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我再试了一次，这次做对了。A 项意为"错误地"；B 项意为"慢慢地"；C 项意为"正确地"；D 项意为"糟糕地"。与第一次失败形成对比，这次 correctly。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我紧紧握住刀，慢慢地切。A 项意为"松松地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"紧紧地"；D 项意为"轻轻地"。切菜要 tightly 握住刀。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得自己像一个真正的厨师。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"医生"；C 项意为"司机"；D 项意为"厨师"。做饭时像 chef。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房里弥漫着美妙的香味。A 项意为"气味"；B 项意为"噪音"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"风景"。做菜散发 smell。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 序数词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：弟弟也喜欢，还要了第二份。A 项意为"第一"；B 项意为"第二"；C 项意为"最后"；D 项意为"小的"。吃完一份还要一份即 second serving。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说做饭是一项重要的生活技能。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"技能"；C 项意为"测试"；D 项意为"梦想"。life skill 意为"生活技能"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学做饭让我明白尝试新事物并没有看起来那么难。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。not as difficult as it seems 意为"没有想象中那么难"。故选 C。
