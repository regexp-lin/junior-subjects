# 中考英语完形填空：海边捡贝壳的女孩

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, my family went to the seaside for a week. I had expected to spend the days swimming and playing games. But on the first morning, I woke up early and found myself walking alone on the beach.

The sand was soft under my feet. The waves made a gentle sound. I felt ___1___ for the first time in months. School had been stressful. I had been worrying about exams and grades. Here, there was nothing to worry about.

I noticed a girl about my age. She was bending down, picking up shells one by one. She put some in a bag and left others on the sand. I walked over and asked, "What are you looking for?"

She smiled. "Not looking for anything ___2___. I just like to see what the sea brings each day. Every morning is different. Yesterday I found a purple shell. Today, who knows?"

Her answer surprised me. I had always thought you needed a ___3___ when you did something. But she was just enjoying the moment. We walked together for a while. She showed me how to tell if a shell was still alive, and which ones were rare. I had never known the beach could be so interesting.

"We spend too much time ___4___ for results," she said. "Sometimes the best part is the journey — the walking, the looking, the wondering what we might find."

I thought about her words all day. She was right. I had been so focused on getting good grades that I had forgotten to enjoy learning. I had been so worried about the future that I had ___5___ the present.

The next morning, I joined her again. We didn't find any ___6___ shells, but we talked about our dreams, our schools, and our favorite books. It was one of the best conversations I had ever had. No phones, no plans — just two girls and the endless sea.

When we said goodbye at the end of the week, she gave me a small shell. "A ___7___ from the sea," she said. "Remember: sometimes the simplest moments are the ones we remember forever."

I still keep that shell on my desk. It reminds me that not everything in life needs a purpose. Some things are ___8___ just because they make us happy. The girl on the beach taught me to slow down and notice the small ___9___ — the color of a shell, the sound of waves, the warmth of a conversation. In a world that always asks "What's the point?", she showed me that the point can simply be to ___10___ — fully, gently, and with an open heart.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：几个月来我第一次感到___。A 项意为"紧张"；B 项意为"平和"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"累"。"peaceful"与"soft sand"、"gentle sound"、"nothing to worry about"相呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不是特地___找什么。A 项意为"特别的"；B 项意为"贵的"；C 项意为"有用的"；D 项意为"漂亮的"。"not looking for anything special"与随性享受当下相呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我总以为做事需要有___。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"理由"；C 项意为"目标"；D 项意为"想法"。"goal"与"you needed a goal when you did something"相呼应。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们花了太多时间___结果。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"问"；C 项意为"寻找"；D 项意为"希望"。"looking for results"（追求结果）与"the best part is the journey"形成对比。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太担心未来，以至于___了当下。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"忽视"。"ignored the present"与前文"worried about the future"呼应。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们没找到任何___的贝壳。A 项意为"特别的"；B 项意为"大的"；C 项意为"稀有的"；D 项意为"贵的"。"special"与"but we talked"形成转折。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她给了我一个小贝壳："来自大海的___"。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"信息"；C 项意为"记忆"；D 项意为"珍宝"。"gift from the sea"既指贝壳也指这段经历。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些东西___仅仅因为它们让我们快乐。A 项意为"重要的"；B 项意为"有意义的"；C 项意为"宝贵的"；D 项意为"值得的"。"valuable"与"make us happy"搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教会我留意那些小小的___。A 项意为"东西"；B 项意为"贝壳"；C 项意为"乐趣"；D 项意为"时刻"。"joys"与后文"the color of a shell, the sound of waves"相呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要义可以仅仅是___——全心全意、温和地、敞开心扉地。A 项意为"生活、活着"；B 项意为"停留"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"等待"。"live"与"fully, gently, and with an open heart"搭配，强调活在当下。故选 A。
