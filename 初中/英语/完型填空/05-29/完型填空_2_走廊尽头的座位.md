# 中考英语完形填空：走廊尽头的座位

## 完形填空原文

When I transferred to a new school in Grade 8, I felt lost. The other students had known each other for years. They had their own groups. I ate lunch alone and sat by myself during breaks. The seat at the end of the corridor became my ___1___ — a quiet place where nobody would notice me.

One afternoon, I was reading there when I heard a voice. "Mind if I sit here?"

I looked up. It was a boy from my class. I had seen him before but never ___2___ to him. He always sat in the front and seemed confident. Why would he want to sit with me?

"Sure," I said, moving my bag.

He sat down and pulled out a sandwich. "I noticed you're always alone. I used to be the new kid too. It's not easy."

His words ___3___ me. I had thought nobody cared. We started talking. He told me about his first week at this school — how he had felt like an outsider until someone invited him to join a study group. "A small act of ___4___ can change everything," he said.

From that day on, we often met at the corridor seat. Sometimes we studied together. Sometimes we just talked. He introduced me to his friends. Slowly, I began to feel like I ___5___.

One day, I asked him why he had chosen to talk to me. "You looked like you needed a friend," he said. "And I remembered what it felt like to need one."

His answer made me realize something important. We often think we're alone in our ___6___, but we're not. Many people have been through similar experiences. The boy didn't do anything extraordinary — he just reached out. But that simple act ___7___ my whole school year.

Now I'm in Grade 9. I still sit at the corridor seat sometimes, but not because I'm lonely. I sit there to watch for other students who might need a friend. When I see someone sitting alone, I remember the boy who ___8___ me. I walk over and say, "Mind if I sit here?"

Kindness doesn't need to be loud or perfect. Sometimes it's just a seat shared in a quiet corridor, a few words of ___9___, or a simple question that says, "I see you. You're not invisible." The boy at the corridor seat taught me that. And now it's my turn to pass it on — one ___10___ at a time, one seat at a time.

## 选项

1. A. hiding place &emsp;&emsp; B. study room &emsp;&emsp; C. favorite spot &emsp;&emsp; D. secret corner
2. A. listened &emsp;&emsp; B. talked &emsp;&emsp; C. waved &emsp;&emsp; D. smiled
3. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. amused
4. A. courage &emsp;&emsp; B. kindness &emsp;&emsp; C. honesty &emsp;&emsp; D. patience
5. A. fitted in &emsp;&emsp; B. stood out &emsp;&emsp; C. gave up &emsp;&emsp; D. fell behind
6. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. struggle &emsp;&emsp; D. success
7. A. saved &emsp;&emsp; B. changed &emsp;&emsp; C. ended &emsp;&emsp; D. started
8. A. helped &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. followed &emsp;&emsp; D. invited
9. A. advice &emsp;&emsp; B. comfort &emsp;&emsp; C. praise &emsp;&emsp; D. warning
10. A. word &emsp;&emsp; B. step &emsp;&emsp; C. smile &emsp;&emsp; D. hand

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：走廊尽头的座位成了我的___。A 项意为"藏身之处"；B 项意为"书房"；C 项意为"最喜欢的地方"；D 项意为"秘密角落"。"a quiet place where nobody would notice me"（没人会注意我的安静地方）说明是"躲藏"之所，"hiding place"最贴切。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我见过他但从没___过他。A 项意为"听"；B 项意为"说话"；C 项意为"挥手"；D 项意为"微笑"。"talk to"（与……说话）表示未曾交流。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话___了我。A 项意为"使惊讶"；B 项意为"使担心"；C 项意为"使兴奋"；D 项意为"使娱乐"。"surprised"与"had thought nobody cared"（原以为没人关心）呼应，有人主动搭话让"我"意外。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小的___之举能改变一切。A 项意为"勇气"；B 项意为"善意"；C 项意为"诚实"；D 项意为"耐心"。"kindness"（善意）与"someone invited him to join"相呼应，指被邀请加入学习小组的善意。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：慢慢地，我开始觉得自己___了。A 项意为"融入"；B 项意为"突出"；C 项意为"放弃"；D 项意为"落后"。"fitted in"（融入）与"introduced me to his friends"相呼应，指逐渐融入集体。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们常以为自己的___是独有的，其实不是。A 项意为"欢乐"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"挣扎、困境"；D 项意为"成功"。"struggle"与"need a friend"、"felt like an outsider"相呼应。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但那简单的举动___了我整个学年。A 项意为"拯救"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"结束"；D 项意为"开始"。"changed"概括了从孤独到有朋友、从 outsider 到融入的变化。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想到那个___我的男孩。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"教导"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"邀请"。"helped"概括男孩主动搭话、介绍朋友等帮助。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时只是几句___的话。A 项意为"建议"；B 项意为"安慰"；C 项意为"赞扬"；D 项意为"警告"。"comfort"（安慰）与"I see you. You're not invisible"相呼应。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在轮到我把善意传递下去——一次一个___。A 项意为"词"；B 项意为"脚步"；C 项意为"微笑"；D 项意为"手"。"one smile at a time"（一次一个微笑）与"one seat at a time"并列，指用微笑和座位传递善意。故选 C。
