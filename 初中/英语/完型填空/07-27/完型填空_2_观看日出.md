# 中考英语完形填空：观看日出

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, my father suggested that we get up early to watch the sunrise. I thought it would be boring at first, but I agreed to go with him. We planned to climb a small hill near our town.

We set off at four o'clock in the morning. It was still dark and the air was cool. The path up the hill was not easy to ___1___. My father held a flashlight and we walked slowly. I could hear birds starting to sing in the trees. "They wake up early too," my father said with a smile.

When we reached the top, the sky was beginning to get ___2___. At first there was just a little light in the east. Then, slowly, the light grew stronger. The clouds turned from grey to pink and then to orange. I had never seen anything so ___3___. I felt as if I were watching a beautiful painting come to life.

Suddenly, the sun appeared at the edge of the sky. It was a small red ball at first, and it rose higher and higher. Within a few minutes, the whole sky was bright. The sun was no longer red — it became golden and ___4___. I could not stop looking at it.

My father put his hand on my shoulder and said, "Every new day is a gift. We should be ___5___ for what we have." I nodded. Standing there on the hill, I felt peaceful and full of hope. The worries I had about school and exams seemed to ___6___ away.

We stayed for about half an hour before going back down. On our way home, my father told me that he used to watch the sunrise with his own father when he was young. "Some things never change," he said. "The sun still rises every morning, and it is still beautiful." I understood what he meant. Some simple ___7___ in life are the most precious.

That morning taught me to slow down and enjoy the beauty around me. We often rush through our days and forget to ___8___ the small things. A sunrise, a walk with family, or a moment of quiet — these are the things that make life meaningful.

I have watched the sunrise many times since then. Each time I ___9___ something new. But that first time with my father will always be special. It was the day I learned that the best things in life are often free — we just need to open our eyes and ___10___ them.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：上山的路不容易___。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"找到"；C 项意为"建造"；D 项意为"穿过"。"find"在此指"辨认、找到"山路，天未亮时路径难辨。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们到达山顶时，天空开始变得___。A 项意为"黑暗的"；B 项意为"晴朗的/清晰的"；C 项意为"多云的"；D 项意为"冷的"。日出前夕天色渐亮，"clear"表示变亮、变清晰。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未见过如此___的景象。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。日出景象令人赞叹，"beautiful"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：太阳不再发红——它变得金黄而___。A 项意为"微弱的"；B 项意为"柔和的"；C 项意为"明亮的"；D 项意为"冷的"。日出后的太阳光芒明亮，"bright"贴切。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们应对所拥有的心怀___。A 项意为"准备好的"；B 项意为"抱歉的"；C 项意为"感激的"；D 项意为"担心的"。"be thankful for"意为"对……心怀感激"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：对学校和考试的担忧似乎都___了。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"飞"；C 项意为"走"；D 项意为"拿"。"go away"为固定搭配，意为"消失"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生命中一些简单的___是最珍贵的。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"时刻"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"计划"。"moments"指日出、与家人同行等片段。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们常常匆匆度过每一天，忘记___小事。A 项意为"注意"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"隐藏"。"notice"表示留意、关注生活中的小美好。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次我都会___新的事物。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"发现"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"教"。"discover"表示有所发现、有所感悟。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只需睁大眼睛并___它们。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"触摸"；C 项意为"接受/感受"；D 项意为"记住"。"receive"此处指用心接受、感受生活中的美好。故选 C。
