# 中考英语完形填空：暑假打工体验

## 完形填空原文

This summer, I got my first part-time job at a small bookshop near my home. I wanted to earn some money to buy a new bicycle. The owner, Mr. Wang, was a kind man who agreed to ___1___ me for the holiday.

On my first day, I was very nervous. Mr. Wang showed me around and taught me how to help customers find books. I had to learn to ___2___ the books on the shelves and keep the shop tidy. At first, I made many mistakes. I put the wrong books in the wrong places and sometimes gave customers the wrong change. But Mr. Wang never got angry. He said, "Everyone needs time to learn. Just do your ___3___."

The job was not easy. I had to stand for many hours and my feet often hurt. Some customers were polite and said "thank you," but others were in a hurry and did not say much. I learned to stay ___4___ no matter what. A smile always helps.

After two weeks, I began to feel more confident. I could find any book quickly and I knew how to use the cash register. One day, a little girl came in looking for a storybook. She could not ___5___ which one to choose. I helped her pick one that was suitable for her age. When she left with a big smile, I felt really good. That was the best ___6___ of my job.

By the end of the summer, I had saved enough money for my bicycle. But I got something even more valuable — I learned what it means to work. I understand now that money does not come ___7___. My parents work hard every day to support our family. I will appreciate their efforts more from now on.

I plan to work at the bookshop again next summer. Mr. Wang said he would be happy to have me back. Working there was a ___8___ experience that I will never forget. It taught me responsibility, patience, and the joy of helping others.

If you have a chance to try a part-time job, I would ___9___ you to do it. You will learn a lot about yourself and the world. Just remember: every job, no matter how small, is worth doing ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老板王先生同意在假期___我。A 项意为"邀请"；B 项意为"雇用"；C 项意为"感谢"；D 项意为"跟随"。"hire"表示招聘、雇用员工，符合打工语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我必须学会在书架上___书籍并保持店铺整洁。A 项意为"阅读"；B 项意为"整理/摆放"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"写"。"arrange"指按顺序或规则摆放书籍。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都需要时间学习，尽力做好你的___。A 项意为"最好的"；B 项意为"作业"；C 项意为"方式"；D 项意为"工作"。"do one's best"为固定搭配，意为"尽力而为"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了无论遇到什么都要保持___。A 项意为"安静的"；B 项意为"耐心的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"生气的"。面对不同顾客需保持耐心和礼貌，"patient"最恰当。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她无法___该选哪一本。A 项意为"决定"；B 项意为"相信"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"记得"。"decide which one to choose"意为"决定选哪一本"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是工作中最好的___。A 项意为"部分"；B 项意为"时刻"；C 项意为"原因"；D 项意为"价格"。"the best moment"指最令人满意的瞬间。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我现在明白钱不是___得来的。A 项意为"早"；B 项意为"晚"；C 项意为"容易地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。"does not come easily"意为"得来不易"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在那里工作是一次___的经历，我永远不会忘记。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"可怕的"。"special"强调难忘、有意义。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果有机会尝试兼职，我___你去试试。A 项意为"允许"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"命令"；D 项意为"强迫"。"advise sb. to do"意为"建议某人做某事"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：记住：无论多小的工作，都值得___去做。A 项意为"糟糕地"；B 项意为"认真地/好好地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"秘密地"。"worth doing well"表示值得认真对待。故选 B。
