# 中考英语完形填空：博物馆的惊喜

## 完形填空原文

Last spring, our class took a trip to the city museum. I had never been there before and didn't expect much — I thought it would be ___1___ and boring, just a lot of old things in glass cases.

When we arrived, a friendly guide greeted us. "Today we'll explore something ___2___," she said with a smile. "I want you to find one thing that speaks to your heart."

I walked through the halls without much interest until I stopped in front of a small painting. It showed a boy sitting by a ___3___, reading a book. The sunlight fell on his face. Something about it felt familiar — maybe because I used to read by the river near my grandparents' house when I was younger.

I stood there for a long time. The guide noticed and came over. "This painting is over a hundred years old," she said. "The artist was only sixteen when he painted it. He ___4___ that reading could change a young person's life."

Her words surprised me. A teenager, like me, had created something that still moved people after a century. It made me think: what could I create if I tried?

Before leaving, I bought a postcard of the painting. I ___5___ it on my desk at home. Whenever I feel tired of studying, I look at it and remember that day.

The museum trip changed my ___6___ about "boring" places. Old things can hold powerful stories. They connect us to people who lived long ago but had dreams just like ours.

I've visited the museum two more times since then. Each visit, I discover something ___8___. Last time, I even ___7___ my little sister to go with me. She loved it too.

Now I understand why people say museums are treasure houses. The real treasure isn't the objects ___9___ — it's the ___10___ and inspiration they give us.

## 选项

1. A. noisy &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. dusty &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
2. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. special &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
3. A. tree &emsp;&emsp; B. river &emsp;&emsp; C. mountain &emsp;&emsp; D. bridge
4. A. believed &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. doubted &emsp;&emsp; D. refused
5. A. hid &emsp;&emsp; B. put &emsp;&emsp; C. threw &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
6. A. idea &emsp;&emsp; B. plan &emsp;&emsp; C. question &emsp;&emsp; D. habit
7. A. invited &emsp;&emsp; B. ordered &emsp;&emsp; C. forced &emsp;&emsp; D. allowed
8. A. old &emsp;&emsp; B. new &emsp;&emsp; C. big &emsp;&emsp; D. small
9. A. themselves &emsp;&emsp; B. itself &emsp;&emsp; C. herself &emsp;&emsp; D. himself
10. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. feelings &emsp;&emsp; D. courage

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以为会___且无聊。A 项意为"吵闹的"；B 项意为"安静的"；C 项意为"布满灰尘的"；D 项意为"空的"。博物馆给"从未去过"的人第一印象常是古老、陈旧，"dusty"（布满灰尘的）符合对老地方 stereotypical 想象。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今天我们要探索一些___的东西。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。向导说"find one thing that speaks to your heart"（找一件触动你心的事物），强调体验的"特别"（special）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：画中男孩坐在___旁读书。A 项意为"树"；B 项意为"河"；C 项意为"山"；D 项意为"桥"。后文提到"I used to read by the river near my grandparents' house"（我曾在外婆家附近的河边读书），前后呼应。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他___阅读能改变年轻人的生活。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"拒绝"。"believed"与"reading could change"搭配，表示画家的信念。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把明信片___在家里的书桌上。A 项意为"藏起来"；B 项意为"放"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"丢失"。"put it on my desk"（放在书桌上）是常见搭配，表示摆放留念。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：博物馆之行改变了我对"无聊"地方的___。A 项意为"想法/看法"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"习惯"。"change one's idea about"（改变某人对……的看法）是常用表达。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：上次我甚至___妹妹一起去。A 项意为"邀请"；B 项意为"命令"；C 项意为"强迫"；D 项意为"允许"。"invited sb. to go"（邀请某人去）符合兄妹同行的情境。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次参观，我都会发现一些___的东西。A 项意为"旧的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"大的"；D 项意为"小的"。"discover something new"（发现新事物）是常用表达，每次都有新收获。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 反身代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：真正的宝藏不是物品___。A 项意为"本身"（themselves）；B 项意为"它自己"（itself）；C 项意为"她自己"；D 项意为"他自己"。"objects"为复数，用反身代词"themselves"指代，固定搭配"the objects themselves"表示"物品本身"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：而是它们带给我们的___和灵感。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"情感/感受"；D 项意为"勇气"。"feelings"与"inspiration"（灵感）并列，指艺术引发的情感共鸣。故选 C。
