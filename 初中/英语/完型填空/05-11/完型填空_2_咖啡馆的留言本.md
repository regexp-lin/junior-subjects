# 中考英语完形填空：咖啡馆的留言本

## 完形填空原文

There is a small café near my school. I often go there after class to do my homework. One afternoon, I noticed a thick notebook on the corner table. A sign above it said: "Please ___1___ your story."

I opened it and began to read. Each page was filled with short notes — some were about happy moments, some about ___2___. A student had written about passing an exam. A mother had written a letter to her daughter. An old man had drawn a simple picture of a sunset.

I was deeply ___3___. These strangers had shared their hearts in this little book. I wanted to add something too, but I didn't know what to write.

That night, I thought about my week. I had argued with my best friend over something silly. We hadn't ___4___ for three days. I felt sad and also a little stupid — the argument wasn't worth losing a friend.

The next day, I returned to the café. I took a pen and wrote: "To my friend: I'm sorry. I ___5___ our friendship more than being right. — From someone who misses you."

I didn't sign my name. I wasn't ready to show it to my friend yet. But writing it down made me feel ___6___. Sometimes we need to find the courage to say things, even if we say them first to ourselves.

A week later, I went back to the café and checked the notebook. Someone had written below my note: "If you're the one who wrote the message above — your friend probably ___7___ you too. Don't wait too long."

That message gave me the courage I needed. I called my friend that evening. We talked for an hour and made up. She said she had been waiting for me to reach out.

Now I visit the café regularly. I still write in the notebook sometimes. It has become a place where people support each other ___8___ they have never met. A simple notebook connected me not only to my friend but also to the kindness of strangers.

I learned that words have power. When we ___9___ our feelings, we open the door for understanding. The café has a ___10___ atmosphere that makes everyone feel at home. And sometimes, a simple notebook can change everything.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：请___你的故事。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"隐藏"；C 项意为"出售"；D 项意为"扔"。留言本的作用是让人们"分享"（share）自己的故事，与下文人们写下的内容呼应。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有的关于快乐时刻，有的关于___。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"天气"。与"happy moments"形成对比，"sadness"（悲伤）体现留言内容的多样性。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我深受___。A 项意为"感动"；B 项意为"无聊"；C 项意为"生气"；D 项意为"劳累"。"be deeply moved"（深受感动）是固定搭配，陌生人的分享触动了作者。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们三天没有___了。A 项意为"说话"；B 项意为"玩耍"；C 项意为"学习"；D 项意为"旅行"。"hadn't spoken"（没说过话）指吵架后互不理睬的状态。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___我们的友谊胜过做对。A 项意为"需要"；B 项意为"珍惜"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"失去"。"value...more than"（珍惜……胜过）表达对友谊的重视，道歉时强调更在乎友情而非对错。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写下来让我感觉___。A 项意为"更糟"；B 项意为"更孤独"；C 项意为"更轻松"；D 项意为"更忙"。倾诉心声后感到"lighter"（更轻松），符合情绪释放后的心理感受。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你的朋友可能也___你。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"责备"。"misses you"（想念你）与上文"From someone who misses you"呼应，鼓励作者主动和好。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人们互相支持，___他们从未见过面。A 项意为"尽管"；B 项意为"因为"；C 项意为"当……时"；D 项意为"如果"。"although"表让步，强调"尽管素不相识，依然互相支持"的温暖。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们___自己的感受时，就打开了理解之门。A 项意为"保持"；B 项意为"表达"；C 项意为"控制"；D 项意为"改变"。"express our feelings"（表达感受）与"open the door for understanding"逻辑相连。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：咖啡馆有一种___的氛围，让每个人感到宾至如归。A 项意为"寒冷的"；B 项意为"宁静的"；C 项意为"吵闹的"；D 项意为"黑暗的"。"peaceful atmosphere"（宁静的氛围）与"feel at home"相契合，形容咖啡馆的温馨。故选 B。
