# 中考英语完形填空：晨跑时的秒表

## 完形填空原文

Our school holds a morning run every Tuesday and Thursday. Students gather on the playground at 6:30 a.m. Before we start, our PE teacher holds a stopwatch in his hand. He uses it to record our running ___1___. At first, I didn't like morning runs. I felt tired and often wanted to give up. But the stopwatch changed my view.

One day, the teacher told us that he would write down each student's time. "Try to beat your own record next time," he said. That ___2___ made me think. I began to run a little faster each week. When I saw my time improve from five minutes to four minutes and thirty seconds, I felt very ___3___. The stopwatch was no longer something to fear. It became a tool that helped me see my progress.

Through morning runs, I learned that progress needs ___4___. We cannot become better in one day. But if we keep trying, we will see small changes. The stopwatch shows us the truth—every second counts. It also teaches us to set goals and work ___5___ them. Now I look forward to Tuesday and Thursday mornings. I want to see if I can run faster than before.

My classmates and I often ___6___ each other before we run. "Come on! You can do it!" we say. This encouragement helps us when we feel tired. The stopwatch reminds us that we are not running alone. We are all part of a team. Even when we cross the finish line at different times, we ___7___ the same goal—to become healthier and stronger.

I keep a small notebook to write down my running times. Looking at the numbers, I can see how I have ___8___ over the months. The stopwatch has taught me an important lesson: success is not about being the fastest, but about being better than you were ___9___. I am grateful for the morning runs and the stopwatch. They have given me both a strong body and a valuable ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. way &emsp;&emsp; D. reason
2. A. idea &emsp;&emsp; B. question &emsp;&emsp; C. joke &emsp;&emsp; D. word
3. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
4. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. luck &emsp;&emsp; C. help &emsp;&emsp; D. time
5. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. toward &emsp;&emsp; D. against
6. A. thank &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. encourage &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
7. A. share &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
8. A. improved &emsp;&emsp; B. stayed &emsp;&emsp; C. failed &emsp;&emsp; D. stopped
9. A. tomorrow &emsp;&emsp; B. yesterday &emsp;&emsp; C. today &emsp;&emsp; D. ago
10. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. prize &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. game

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他用秒表记录我们的跑步___。A 项意为"地点"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"方式"；D 项意为"理由"。"time"与"stopwatch"直接相关，秒表用于计时。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那句___让我思考。A 项意为"想法"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"话"。"word"与前文"Try to beat your own record"呼应，指老师说的话。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当看到时间从五分钟进步到四分三十秒时，我感到非常___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"proud"与"time improve"相呼应，表示进步带来的成就感。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：通过晨跑，我学会了进步需要___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"时间"。"time"与后文"cannot become better in one day"、"keep trying"相呼应，强调持之以恒。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它也教会我们设定目标并___目标努力。A 项意为"为了"；B 项意为"与"；C 项意为"朝、向"；D 项意为"反对"。"work toward"为固定搭配，意为"朝着……努力"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和同学在跑步前常常互相___。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"鼓励"；D 项意为"跟随"。"encourage"与后文"Come on! You can do it!"相呼应。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使我们以不同时间冲过终点，我们___同一个目标。A 项意为"共有、分享"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"错过"。"share"与"the same goal"搭配，表示共同追求。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看这些数字，我能看到这几个月来我是如何___的。A 项意为"进步"；B 项意为"保持"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"停止"。"improved"与"running times"、"better than before"相呼应。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 词义辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：成功不是跑得最快，而是比___的自己更好。A 项意为"明天"；B 项意为"昨天"；C 项意为"今天"；D 项意为"以前"。"yesterday"指过去的自己，与"better than"形成对比。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：晨跑和秒表给了我强健的身体和一个宝贵的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"教训、道理"；D 项意为"游戏"。"lesson"指前文"progress needs time"、"better than yesterday"等人生启示。故选 C。
