# 中考英语完形填空：值日生的扫帚

## 完形填空原文

Every week, our class has a duty group to clean the classroom. Last month, it was my turn to be the duty leader. Every afternoon after school, I had to get the broom from the corner and sweep the floor. At first, I thought it was a ___1___ job. I just wanted to finish it quickly and go home. But one day, something changed my mind.

That day, our head teacher stood by the door and watched us clean. When I finished sweeping, she came over and said, "You did a good job. The floor looks ___2___ now." Her words made me feel proud. From then on, I began to ___3___ the duty work. I not only swept the floor clean but also helped my classmates put the chairs in order. We became a good team and finished the cleaning ___4___.

Through the duty work, I learned that every job is important. The broom in my hand may look simple, but it helps keep our classroom tidy. A clean classroom makes us feel comfortable and helps us study better. I also learned to work with others. When we ___5___ the work together, everyone feels happy. Now I am no longer ___6___ about being a duty student. Instead, I feel it is my responsibility to do it well.

My parents often tell me that small things can make a big ___7___. I think the broom in the corner is a good example. It reminds me that we should not look down upon any job. Every role in our class has its own ___8___. Whether we are cleaning the floor or writing on the blackboard, we are all helping to make our class better. I am ___9___ that I had the chance to learn this lesson. The broom has taught me more than how to sweep—it has taught me about responsibility and ___10___.

2. A. dirty &emsp;&emsp; B. clean &emsp;&emsp; C. wet &emsp;&emsp; D. dark
3. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid

6. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
7. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. decision &emsp;&emsp; C. difference &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
8. A. price &emsp;&emsp; B. value &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. way
9. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. sure &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. glad

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我觉得这是一份___的工作。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"令人兴奋的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"重要的"。"boring"与后文"just wanted to finish it quickly"相呼应，表示一开始认为值日枯燥无趣。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你做得很好，地板现在看起来很___。A 项意为"脏的"；B 项意为"干净的"；C 项意为"湿的"；D 项意为"暗的"。"clean"与"sweep the floor"相呼应，表示扫地后的结果。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那时起，我开始___值日工作。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"避免"。"enjoy"与"made me feel proud"相呼应，表示态度从敷衍转为积极。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们成了一个好团队，___完成了打扫。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"早早地"；C 项意为"独自地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。"early"与"became a good team"呼应，表示 teamwork 带来效率提升。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们一起___工作时，每个人都感到开心。A 项意为"分担、分享"；B 项意为"发现"；C 项意为"节省"；D 项意为"保持"。"share"与"work with others"呼应，指分工合作。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我不再___当值日生了。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"害怕的、不情愿的"。"afraid"与前文态度转变形成对比，不再排斥值日。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母常说小事能产生很大的___。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"影响、不同"；D 项意为"问题"。"make a big difference"为固定搭配，意为"产生很大影响"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班每个角色都有自己的___。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"价值"；C 项意为"理由"；D 项意为"方式"。"value"与"every role"呼应，表示每份工作都有意义。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___有机会学到这一课。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"确定的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"高兴的"。"glad"与"learned this lesson"相呼应，表达感恩心态。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：扫帚教会我的不仅是扫地，还有责任和___。A 项意为"友谊"；B 项意为"知识"；C 项意为"团队合作"；D 项意为"运气"。"teamwork"与"work with others"、"share the work"相呼应，总结全文主题。故选 C。
