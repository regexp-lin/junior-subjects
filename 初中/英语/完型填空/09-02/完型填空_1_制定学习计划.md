# 中考英语完形填空：制定学习计划

## 完形填空原文

At the beginning of the new term, our teacher asked us to make a study plan. She said a good plan could help us use our time more ___1___ and achieve better results. I thought it was a great idea, so I sat down that evening to write mine.

First, I looked at my timetable. I had classes from Monday to Friday, and weekends were ___2___. I decided to divide my free time into study time and rest time. I knew that studying too much without breaks would make me tired, and I would not be able to ___3___ well.

For each subject, I set a specific goal. In maths, I wanted to finish all the exercises in the workbook. In English, I planned to memorise twenty new words every week and read one short article every day. For Chinese, I would practise writing one essay each week. These goals were not too easy or too ___4___ — they were just right for me.

I also made rules for myself. No playing mobile games during study time. No ___5___ homework until the last minute. And I had to review what I had learned in class on the same day. My father said that reviewing on time helps move knowledge from short-term memory to ___6___ memory. I believed him because he was a teacher himself.

The first week was the hardest. I was not used to following a strict schedule. Sometimes I wanted to watch TV or chat with friends online. But I ___7___ myself that the plan was for my own good. Slowly, studying according to the plan became a habit.

After one month, I could already see the ___8___. My test scores improved in almost every subject. I also felt less stressed because I did not have to rush to finish homework at the last minute. My teacher praised me in front of the class, which made me feel very ___9___.

Now I understand why planning is so important. It is like a map that guides you on a long journey. Without a map, you might get lost. With a good plan, you can reach your ___10___ step by step. I will keep following my study plan and adjust it when needed.

## 选项

1. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. wisely &emsp;&emsp; C. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
2. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. free &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. tiring
3. A. sleep &emsp;&emsp; B. eat &emsp;&emsp; C. concentrate &emsp;&emsp; D. exercise
4. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. common
5. A. copying &emsp;&emsp; B. leaving &emsp;&emsp; C. checking &emsp;&emsp; D. collecting
6. A. bad &emsp;&emsp; B. false &emsp;&emsp; C. long-term &emsp;&emsp; D. weak
7. A. reminded &emsp;&emsp; B. thanked &emsp;&emsp; C. forgave &emsp;&emsp; D. entertained
8. A. problems &emsp;&emsp; B. results &emsp;&emsp; C. dangers &emsp;&emsp; D. excuses
9. A. ashamed &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
10. A. destination &emsp;&emsp; B. station &emsp;&emsp; C. office &emsp;&emsp; D. airport

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好的计划能帮助我们更___地利用时间。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"明智地"；C 项意为"粗心地"；D 项意为"安静地"。合理利用时间应是明智地，"wisely"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：周末是___的。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"空闲的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"累人的"。周一到周五上课，周末空闲，"free"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不休息地学习太多会让我疲惫，无法好好___。A 项意为"睡觉"；B 项意为"吃"；C 项意为"集中注意力"；D 项意为"锻炼"。学习疲劳影响专注力，"concentrate"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些目标不太容易也不太___。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"普通的"。与"easy"对应的反义词是"difficult"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不把作业___到最后一刻。A 项意为"抄写"；B 项意为"留下/拖延"；C 项意为"检查"；D 项意为"收集"。"leaving homework until the last minute"意为"把作业拖到最后"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：及时复习能把知识从短期记忆转移到___记忆。A 项意为"坏的"；B 项意为"虚假的"；C 项意为"长期的"；D 项意为"弱的"。与"short-term"对应的是"long-term"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我___自己这个计划对自己有好处。A 项意为"提醒"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"原谅"；D 项意为"娱乐"。自我提醒坚持计划，"reminded"正确。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个月后，我已经能看到___了。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"成果"；C 项意为"危险"；D 项意为"借口"。坚持计划后看到成效，"results"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师在全班面前表扬了我，这让我感到非常___。A 项意为"羞愧的"；B 项意为"担忧的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"自豪的"。被表扬感到自豪，"proud"正确。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有了好的计划，你可以一步步到达___。A 项意为"目的地"；B 项意为"车站"；C 项意为"办公室"；D 项意为"机场"。前文用旅行做比喻，"destination"意为"目的地/目标"。故选 A。
