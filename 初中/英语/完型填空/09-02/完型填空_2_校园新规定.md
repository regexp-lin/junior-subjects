# 中考英语完形填空：校园新规定

## 完形填空原文

This September, our school introduced several new rules. The headmaster announced them during the opening ceremony. Some students were excited, while others felt ___1___. I was one of those who needed time to get used to the changes.

The first new rule was about mobile phones. Students were no longer allowed to bring phones to school. If a phone was found, it would be kept by the teacher until parents came to ___2___ it. Many students complained about this rule. They said they needed phones to contact their parents. But the headmaster explained that phones were a major distraction in class. Students spent too much time on social media and games ___3___ of studying.

The second rule was about the school uniform. We now had to wear our uniforms every day, not just on Mondays. At first, I thought this was ___4___. I liked wearing my own clothes because they showed my personality. But later I realised that uniforms saved time in the morning. I no longer had to spend ten minutes deciding what to ___5___.

The third rule encouraged students to exercise more. Every class had to do a thirty-minute group exercise after the last lesson. We could choose from running, skipping rope or playing basketball. I chose basketball and found it was a great way to ___6___ after a long day of study. My body felt healthier, and I could sleep better at night.

The most popular new rule was about the library. It would now stay open until 8 p.m. on weekdays, giving students more time to read and study. The school also added hundreds of new books, including many popular science and story books. I was ___7___ because I loved reading.

After a few weeks, most students accepted the new rules. We found that they were not meant to ___8___ our freedom, but to help us develop good habits. The phone rule helped us focus in class. The uniform rule taught us simplicity. The exercise rule improved our ___9___. And the library rule gave us access to more knowledge.

I learned that change can be uncomfortable at first, but it often leads to ___10___. Sometimes the rules we dislike the most are the ones that help us the most.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些学生很兴奋，而另一些感到___。A 项意为"高兴的"；B 项意为"担忧的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。与excited形成对比，新规定让一些人担心，"worried"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手机会被老师保管，直到家长来___。A 项意为"修理"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"领取"；D 项意为"藏"。家长来学校领回手机，"collect"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生花太多时间在社交媒体和游戏上___学习。A 项意为"因为"；B 项意为"代替"；C 项意为"前面"；D 项意为"外面"。"instead of"意为"而不是"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我觉得这___。A 项意为"精彩的"；B 项意为"必要的"；C 项意为"不公平的"；D 项意为"明智的"。喜欢穿自己衣服的学生觉得天天穿校服不公平，"unfair"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不再需要花十分钟决定___什么。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"穿"；D 项意为"读"。讨论的是穿衣服的问题，"wear"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现这是学习一整天后___的好方式。A 项意为"放弃"；B 项意为"冷静下来"；C 项意为"炫耀"；D 项意为"放松"。运动帮助放松身心，"relax"正确。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___因为我喜欢阅读。A 项意为"失望的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"困惑的"。爱读书的人听到图书馆延长开放时间会高兴，"delighted"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些规定不是为了___我们的自由。A 项意为"保护"；B 项意为"增加"；C 项意为"限制"；D 项意为"享受"。规定的目的不是限制自由而是帮助养成好习惯，"limit"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：运动规则改善了我们的___。A 项意为"成绩"；B 项意为"健康"；C 项意为"财富"；D 项意为"外貌"。运动改善健康，"health"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：改变起初可能不舒服，但往往通向___。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"危险"；C 项意为"成长"；D 项意为"失去"。文章积极总结，改变带来成长，"growth"正确。故选 C。
