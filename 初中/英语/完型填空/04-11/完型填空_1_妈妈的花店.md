# 中考英语完形填空：妈妈的花店

## 完形填空原文

My mom owned a small flower shop on a quiet street. The shop was not big, but it was full of colors and sweet ___1___. I often helped her after school, wrapping bouquets and watering the plants.

Business was never great. A large flower chain store opened across the street last year, and many of our old customers started shopping there ___2___. The chain store had lower prices, more varieties, and even a delivery service. Mom's little shop couldn't compete.

One evening, I found Mom sitting behind the counter, looking ___3___. "Maybe I should close the shop," she said quietly. "Nobody comes here anymore."

I didn't know what to say. I loved the shop. It felt like a second ___4___ to me. But I could see how hard things had become.

The next day at school, I told my friend Xiao Mei about the problem. She thought for a moment and said, "What if your mom does something the big store ___5___? Something personal and special?"

That gave me an idea. I went home and suggested to Mom that she start teaching flower-arranging classes on weekends. "You're the best at making bouquets," I said. "Why not ___6___ that skill with others?"

Mom was unsure at first, but she agreed to try. We made posters and put them up around the neighborhood. The first class had only four people. But they loved it. Mom taught them how to pick the right flowers, match ___7___, and create arrangements for different occasions.

Word spread quickly. By the second month, every weekend class was ___8___. Some students even started ordering custom bouquets from Mom's shop instead of buying from the chain store.

Slowly, business improved. The shop was lively again, filled with laughter and the sound of people learning something new. Mom smiled more than she had in ___9___.

"You know what saved this shop?" she said to me one evening. "It wasn't a big idea or a lot of money. It was the thing that the big store doesn't have — a personal ___10___."

## 选项

1. A. sounds &emsp;&emsp; B. smells &emsp;&emsp; C. tastes &emsp;&emsp; D. feelings
2. A. already &emsp;&emsp; B. instead &emsp;&emsp; C. again &emsp;&emsp; D. together
3. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
4. A. home &emsp;&emsp; B. school &emsp;&emsp; C. chance &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
5. A. has &emsp;&emsp; B. can't &emsp;&emsp; C. needs &emsp;&emsp; D. wants
6. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. share &emsp;&emsp; C. sell &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
7. A. prices &emsp;&emsp; B. sizes &emsp;&emsp; C. colors &emsp;&emsp; D. names
8. A. cancelled &emsp;&emsp; B. empty &emsp;&emsp; C. free &emsp;&emsp; D. full
9. A. days &emsp;&emsp; B. months &emsp;&emsp; C. hours &emsp;&emsp; D. minutes
10. A. touch &emsp;&emsp; B. record &emsp;&emsp; C. price &emsp;&emsp; D. rule

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：店不大，但充满了色彩和甜美的___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"味道"；D 项意为"感觉"。花店里自然充满了花的香"气味"（smells），与 colors 并列描述花店的美好氛围。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多老顾客开始___去那里购物。A 项意为"已经"；B 项意为"代替/反而"；C 项意为"再一次"；D 项意为"一起"。顾客不来妈妈的店了，"反而"（instead）去了对面的连锁店，表示转向另一个选择。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现妈妈坐在柜台后面，看起来很___。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"担忧的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。生意不好，考虑关店，妈妈自然很"担忧"（worried）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它对我来说就像第二个___。A 项意为"家"；B 项意为"学校"；C 项意为"机会"；D 项意为"选择"。她经常放学后来帮忙，对花店有很深的感情，花店就像第二个"家"（home）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："如果你妈妈做一些大店___做到的事呢？"A 项意为"有"；B 项意为"不能"；C 项意为"需要"；D 项意为"想要"。朋友建议找差异化优势，做大店"不能"（can't）做到的事——个人化的、特别的服务。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："为什么不把这个技能___给其他人？"A 项意为"藏起来"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"忘记"。建议妈妈开花艺课，把插花技巧"分享"（share）给别人。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈教她们如何挑选花材、搭配___，以及为不同场合设计插花。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"尺寸"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"名字"。插花时最重要的搭配之一就是"颜色"（colors）的协调。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二个月开始，每周末的课都___了。A 项意为"取消的"；B 项意为"空的"；C 项意为"免费的"；D 项意为"满的"。口碑传播后越来越多人来学，课程"满员"（full），说明非常受欢迎。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈比___以来笑得更多了。A 项意为"天"；B 项意为"月"；C 项意为"小时"；D 项意为"分钟"。花店困难了很长时间，现在重新变好，她比好几个"月"（months）以来都更开心。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它是大店没有的——一种个人的___。A 项意为"关怀/温度"；B 项意为"记录"；C 项意为"价格"；D 项意为"规则"。"personal touch"意为"个人特色/人情味"，是一个固定搭配，指小店特有的人情温暖和个性化服务。故选 A。
