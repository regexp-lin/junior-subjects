# 中考英语完形填空：学校心理健康讲座

## 完形填空原文

Last Tuesday afternoon, our school held a mental health lecture in the school hall. A psychologist from the city came to ___1___ with us about how to deal with stress and emotions. All students from Grade 8 and 9 were invited to ___2___.

The psychologist, Mr. Chen, started by asking us a question: "Do you ever feel worried or ___3___ before exams?" Many hands went up. He smiled and said that it was completely normal. Then he taught us some simple ___4___ to relax, such as taking deep breaths and listening to soft music.

He also talked about the importance of ___5___. "When you have problems, don't keep them to yourself," he said. "Talk to your parents, teachers or friends. Sharing your feelings can make you feel much ___6___." His words really made me think about my own life.

During the Q&A session, I ___7___ up my hand and asked how to handle peer pressure. Mr. Chen gave me helpful advice. He said we should learn to say "no" when necessary and always stay true to ___8___. After the lecture, I felt more confident about facing challenges.

The school will hold such lectures ___9___ every term from now on. I believe this will help us grow both academically and ___10___. Mental health is as important as physical health!

## 选项

1. A. share&emsp;&emsp; B. discuss&emsp;&emsp; C. argue&emsp;&emsp; D. compete
2. A. attend&emsp;&emsp; B. leave&emsp;&emsp; C. miss&emsp;&emsp; D. refuse
3. A. proud&emsp;&emsp; B. nervous&emsp;&emsp; C. happy&emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
4. A. ways&emsp;&emsp; B. problems&emsp;&emsp; C. ideas&emsp;&emsp; D. games
5. A. silence&emsp;&emsp; B. communication&emsp;&emsp; C. competition&emsp;&emsp; D. isolation
6. A. worse&emsp;&emsp; B. better&emsp;&emsp; C. busier&emsp;&emsp; D. lonelier
7. A. put&emsp;&emsp; B. gave&emsp;&emsp; C. took&emsp;&emsp; D. raised
8. A. ourselves&emsp;&emsp; B. themselves&emsp;&emsp; C. yourselves&emsp;&emsp; D. itself
9. A. nearly&emsp;&emsp; B. almost&emsp;&emsp; C. once&emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
10. A. physically&emsp;&emsp; B. mentally&emsp;&emsp; C. quickly&emsp;&emsp; D. slowly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：市里的一位心理专家来和我们讨论如何应对压力和情绪。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"讨论"，discuss with 为固定搭配；C 项意为"争论"；D 项意为"竞争"。讲座是与学生讨论话题。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：八九年级的所有学生都被邀请参加。A 项意为"参加；出席"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"拒绝"。attend 表示参加活动。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：考试前你们有没有感到担忧或紧张？A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"放松的"。"worried or nervous"考前常见的负面情绪。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后他教给我们一些简单的放松方法。A 项意为"方法；方式"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"游戏"。"ways to relax"放松的方法。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他还谈到了沟通的重要性。A 项意为"沉默"；B 项意为"交流；沟通"；C 项意为"竞争"；D 项意为"孤立"。后文提到"talk to"和"sharing"，应是沟通。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：倾诉你的感受能让你感觉好很多。A 项意为"更糟"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更忙"；D 项意为"更孤独"。分享感受通常会让人感觉更好。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：在问答环节，我举手提问。A 项 put up 可表"举起"但常用 raise one's hand；B、C 项不搭配；D 项"raise up one's hand"即"raised my hand"意为"举手"。raise one's hand 为固定搭配。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 反身代词
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说我们应在必要时学会说"不"，并始终忠于自己。A 项意为"我们自己"；B 项意为"他们自己"；C 项意为"你们自己"；D 项意为"它自己"。主语为 we，用 ourselves。stay true to oneself 忠于自己。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校从现在起每学期都会举办一次这样的讲座。A 项意为"几乎"；B 项意为"几乎"；C 项意为"一次"，once every term 每学期一次；D 项意为"几乎不"。"once every term"为固定表达。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信这能帮助我们在学业和心理上都能成长。A 项意为"身体上"；B 项意为"心理上；精神上"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"缓慢地"。与 mental health 对应，用 mentally。故选 B。
