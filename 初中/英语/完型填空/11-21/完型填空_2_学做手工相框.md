# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工相框

## 完形填空原文

During the weekend craft class, our teacher taught us how to make a photo frame by hand. She said it would be a perfect gift for family members. I was excited because I had a photo of my family that I wanted to ___1___ in a special way.

First, we chose a piece of thick cardboard as the base. We measured and cut it into a rectangle. The teacher said the size should be ___2___ larger than our photo so there would be space for the frame border. Then we cut a smaller rectangle in the middle for the photo to show through. This part required patience—I had to cut ___3___ and straight.

Next, we decorated the frame. I used colorful paper, dried flowers, and some small shells I had collected at the beach. I glued them onto the border and made a unique design. My classmate Linda chose a simpler style—she only used paint. Both ___4___ looked beautiful in different ways.

When the glue was dry, I carefully placed my family photo behind the frame and fixed it with tape. I added a stand at the back so the frame could ___5___ on the desk. Finally, I wrapped it in pretty paper and gave it to my mom on her birthday. She was so ___6___ that her eyes became wet. She said it was the best gift she had ever received.

Making this photo frame was not ___7___, but it taught me that handmade gifts can mean more than store-bought ones. The time and love we put into our work make it valuable. I plan to make more frames for our grandparents. I want them to know how ___8___ they are to our family. Small handmade gifts can truly show our ___9___ and bring people closer together. I also want to ___10___ this skill with my friends so they can make meaningful gifts too.

## 选项

1. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. display &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. throw
2. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. a bit &emsp;&emsp; C. very &emsp;&emsp; D. so
3. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. badly
4. A. designs &emsp;&emsp; B. photos &emsp;&emsp; C. teachers &emsp;&emsp; D. boxes
5. A. hang &emsp;&emsp; B. stand &emsp;&emsp; C. lie &emsp;&emsp; D. sit
6. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. moved &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
7. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. late
8. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. young &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. love &emsp;&emsp; D. luck
10. A. sell &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. forget

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有张全家福想用特别的方式展示。A 项意为"保存"；B 项意为"展示"；C 项意为"藏起来"；D 项意为"扔掉"。做相框是为了 display 照片。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尺寸应比照片稍大一点以便留出边框空间。A 项 much 表程度大；B 项 a bit 意为"稍微"；C 项 very 表程度；D 项 so 表如此。留边框用 a bit larger。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这部分需要耐心——我必须慢慢剪、剪直。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"糟糕地"。精细手工用 cut slowly。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两种设计以不同方式看起来都很美。A 项意为"设计"；B 项意为"照片"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"盒子"。指代两人的相框风格用 designs。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在背面加了支架，这样相框可以立在桌上。A 项意为"悬挂"；B 项 stand 意为"站立/立放"；C 项意为"躺"；D 项意为"坐"。加 stand 是为了 stand on desk。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她非常感动，眼眶都湿了。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"感动的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。收到心意礼物用 moved。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做这个相框并不容易。A 项意为"难的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"快的"；D 项意为"晚的"。not easy 表示有难度，与后文"教会我"呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想让他们知道他们对我们家有多重要。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"年轻的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。表达对长辈的珍视用 important。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小手工作品能真切表达我们的爱，让人更亲近。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"爱"；D 项意为"运气"。礼物表达的是 love。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也想和朋友分享这个技能，让他们也能做有意义的礼物。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"买"；C 项 share 意为"分享"；D 项意为"忘记"。分享技能用 share。故选 C。
