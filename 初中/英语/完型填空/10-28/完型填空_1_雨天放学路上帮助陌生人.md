# 中考英语完形填空：雨天放学路上帮助陌生人

## 完形填空原文

It was a rainy afternoon. School was over and I was walking home with my umbrella. The rain was getting ___1___, so I walked faster. Then I saw an old woman standing under a tree near the bus stop. She was wet and looked ___2___. She had no umbrella with her.

I walked over and asked, "Are you OK? Do you need help?" She said her umbrella was broken and she couldn't ___3___ a taxi in the rain. "My home is not far. Would you like to share my umbrella?" I asked. She smiled and said ___4___, "Thank you, young lady. You are very kind."

We walked together slowly. I held the umbrella high so that we ___5___ could stay dry. The old woman told me she was going to visit her daughter. She said her daughter lived in a building ___6___ the end of this street. I felt happy to help her.

When we reached her daughter's building, she thanked me again. "You ___7___ my day," she said. "Not many young people would stop to help a stranger in the rain." I said it was nothing and ___8___ her good luck. Then I continued my way home.

That day I learned something important. A small act of kindness can ___9___ a big difference to someone's day. I didn't do anything special, but the old woman's smile made me feel warm inside. From then on, I have tried to help others whenever I ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. lighter &emsp;&emsp; B. heavier &emsp;&emsp; C. slower &emsp;&emsp; D. clearer
2. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. hungry &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
3. A. find &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. drive &emsp;&emsp; D. park
4. A. sadly &emsp;&emsp; B. thankfully &emsp;&emsp; C. proudly &emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
5. A. all &emsp;&emsp; B. both &emsp;&emsp; C. either &emsp;&emsp; D. neither
6. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. in &emsp;&emsp; C. by &emsp;&emsp; D. at
7. A. made &emsp;&emsp; B. saved &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. wasted
8. A. hoped &emsp;&emsp; B. expected &emsp;&emsp; C. wanted &emsp;&emsp; D. wished
9. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. get &emsp;&emsp; C. make &emsp;&emsp; D. give
10. A. can &emsp;&emsp; B. may &emsp;&emsp; C. must &emsp;&emsp; D. need

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：雨越下越大，所以我走得更快了。A 项意为"更轻的"；B 项 heavier 意为"更重的"，rain gets heavier 表示"雨越下越大"；C 项意为"更慢的"；D 项意为"更清晰的"。雨势变大用 heavier。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她浑身湿透，看起来担忧/着急。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"饥饿的"；C 项意为"担忧的"；D 项意为"放松的"。淋雨又没伞，老人会 look worried。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的伞坏了，雨天打不到出租车。A 项 find 意为"找到"，find a taxi 表示"打到出租车"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"驾驶"；D 项意为"停放"。打车用 find a taxi。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她感激地说："谢谢你，小姑娘，你真好。"A 项意为"难过地"；B 项 thankfully 意为"感激地"；C 项意为"骄傲地"；D 项意为"生气地"。接受帮助时表示感谢用 thankfully。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把伞举高，这样我们俩都能保持干爽。A 项 all 用于三者及以上；B 项 both 意为"两者都"，指我和老人两人；C 项 either 意为"两者之一"；D 项 neither 意为"两者都不"。两人共伞用 both。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说女儿住在这条街尽头的一栋楼里。A 项 on 一般表表面；B 项 in 表在内部；C 项 by 表"在……旁边"；D 项 at the end of 意为"在……的尽头"，固定搭配。at the end of the street 正确。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说："你让我的今天变得美好。"A 项 made 意为"使"，make one's day 表示"让某人开心/让某人的一天变美好"；B 项意为"救"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"浪费"。make sb's day 为常见表达。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我说没什么，并祝她好运。A 项 hope 后接从句，不接 sb+名词；B 项意为"期望"；C 项意为"想要"；D 项 wish 意为"祝愿"，wish sb good luck 表示"祝某人好运"。wish sb good luck 为正确搭配。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小小的善意举动就能给他人带来很大影响。A 项 take 一般不用于此搭配；B 项 get 需与 from 搭配；C 项 make a difference 意为"产生影响、有所作为"，固定短语；D 项 give 与 to 搭配表"给"。make a difference to 正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 情态动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那以后， whenever I can（每当我能做到时）我就尽力帮助别人。A 项 can 意为"能够"， whenever I can 表示"只要我能"；B 项 may 表可能性；C 项 must 表必须；D 项 need 表需要。 whenever I can 是常用表达。故选 A。
