# 中考英语完形填空：和同学去植物园写生

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my classmates and I went to the botanical garden for a drawing activity. Our art teacher had organized the trip. We met at the school gate at 8:00 a.m. and took a bus together. Everyone was excited because it was our ___1___ time to draw outdoors.

The botanical garden was full of beautiful flowers and green plants. We walked around to find a good place to sit. Finally, we ___2___ a quiet spot near a small pond. The willow trees around it looked like a green curtain. "This is perfect!" said Li Ming.

We took out our sketchbooks and pencils. The teacher told us to ___3___ what we saw and to use our imagination. I decided to draw the pond with lotus flowers. At first, my drawing didn't look good. I felt a bit ___4___ and wanted to start again. But the teacher said, "Keep trying. Every artist makes mistakes."

After about an hour, my picture was almost done. I added some butterflies and birds to make it more ___5___. When we finished, we showed our drawings to each other. Wang Fang had drawn a huge sunflower. It looked so ___6___ that we all praised her.

Before we left, the teacher took a group photo of us with our sketchbooks. She said we would ___7___ this day for a long time. I think she was right. Drawing in nature was much more fun than drawing in the classroom. The fresh air and beautiful views ___8___ us feel relaxed and creative.

On the bus back to school, we talked about our drawings and laughed a lot. I realized that spending time with friends and doing what we love could bring us great ___9___. I hope we can have more ___10___ like this in the future!

## 选项

1. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. second &emsp;&emsp; C. third &emsp;&emsp; D. last
2. A. chose &emsp;&emsp; B. built &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaned &emsp;&emsp; D. passed
3. A. copy &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. draw &emsp;&emsp; D. describe
4. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. happy &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointed
5. A. serious &emsp;&emsp; B. lively &emsp;&emsp; C. dark &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
6. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. real &emsp;&emsp; C. ugly &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
7. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. lose &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. remember
8. A. let &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. kept &emsp;&emsp; D. helped
9. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. questions &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
10. A. lessons &emsp;&emsp; B. exams &emsp;&emsp; C. games &emsp;&emsp; D. activities

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 序数词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都很兴奋，因为这是我们第一次户外写生。A 项 first 意为"第一"；B 项意为"第二"；C 项意为"第三"；D 项意为"最后"。根据 excited 和 it was our...time，首次户外写生用 first。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们四处走动找合适位置，最后选了一个靠近小池塘的安静地点。A 项 chose 意为"选择"；B 项意为"建造"；C 项意为"打扫"；D 项意为"经过"。find a spot 后选地点用 chose。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们画出所看到的事物，并发挥想象力。A 项意为"复制"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项 draw 意为"画"；D 项意为"描述"。写生活动用 draw what we saw。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初画得不好，我有点失望想重画。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"开心的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项 disappointed 意为"失望的"。画不好想重画，说明感到 disappointed。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我加上蝴蝶和鸟，让画面更生动。A 项意为"严肃的"；B 项 lively 意为"活泼的、生动的"；C 项意为"暗的"；D 项意为"简单的"。加蝴蝶、鸟会让画更 lively。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王芳画了一朵大向日葵，看起来太逼真了，我们都夸她。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项 real 意为"真实的、逼真的"；C 项意为"丑的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。画得好被夸用 look real。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说我们会长久记住这一天。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"想念、错过"；D 项 remember 意为"记住"。难忘的回忆用 remember。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 使役动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：清新的空气和美景让我们感到放松又有创造力。A 项 let 后接省略 to 的不定式；B 项 made 用于 make sb feel... 表示"使某人感到"；C 项 kept 表持续；D 项 help 后接 feel 也可，但 make sb feel 更常用表"使……感到"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到和朋友在一起做喜欢的事能带来很大快乐。A 项 joy 意为"快乐"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"麻烦"。bring us great joy 表示"带给我们很大快乐"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望将来能有更多这样的活动。A 项意为"课、教训"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项 activities 意为"活动"。写生属于 activity，希望有更多类似活动。故选 D。
