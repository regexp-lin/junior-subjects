# 中考英语完形填空：学种小麦草

## 完形填空原文

In our biology class this week, the teacher gave each of us a small pot and some wheat seeds. She asked us to grow wheatgrass at home and ___1___ its growth every day. I thought it would be a fun project.

When I got home, I put some soil into the pot and spread the seeds ___2___ on top. Then I covered them with a thin layer of soil and watered them gently. My mother helped me find a sunny ___3___ near the window to place the pot.

For the first two days, nothing happened. I was a little ___4___ and wondered if I had done something wrong. But my mother told me to be patient. She said seeds need time to sprout.

On the third morning, I was surprised to see tiny green ___5___ pushing through the soil. They were very small, but I could already tell they were alive and growing. I felt so ___6___!

Every day after school, the first thing I did was check my wheatgrass. I watered it carefully and made sure it got enough ___7___. By the end of the week, the grass had grown about five centimeters tall. It looked fresh and green.

I took some photos and brought my pot to school. When I compared mine with my classmates' pots, I noticed that everyone's grass looked ___8___. Some were taller, some were shorter, and some were a deeper green. The teacher said the differences were because of different growing ___9___ like light, water, and temperature.

This project taught me to be patient and observant. Watching a tiny seed grow into a green plant is truly ___10___. I plan to keep growing different plants at home.

7. A. sleep &emsp;&emsp; B. sunlight &emsp;&emsp; C. space &emsp;&emsp; D. noise
8. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. bad &emsp;&emsp; D. old
9. A. conditions &emsp;&emsp; B. seasons &emsp;&emsp; C. prices &emsp;&emsp; D. stories

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们种麦草并每天记录它的生长。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"记录"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"忘记"。record its growth 意为"记录生长情况"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把种子均匀地撒在土上。A 项意为"厚厚地"；B 项意为"均匀地"；C 项意为"重重地"；D 项意为"凌乱地"。种子要 spread evenly。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈帮我在窗边找了个阳光充足的位置。A 项意为"地点/位置"；B 项意为"房间"；C 项意为"地板"；D 项意为"花园"。a sunny spot 意为"一个阳光充足的位置"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：头两天什么也没发生，我有点失望。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"失望的"；D 项意为"危险的"。没看到变化会 disappointed。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我惊喜地看到嫩芽从土里冒出来。A 项意为"花"；B 项意为"叶子"；C 项意为"嫩芽"；D 项意为"果实"。刚发芽是 shoots。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到非常高兴。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"抱歉的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"高兴的"。种子发芽让人 happy。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仔细浇水并确保它得到足够的阳光。A 项意为"睡眠"；B 项意为"阳光"；C 项意为"空间"；D 项意为"噪音"。植物需要 sunlight。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现每个人的草看起来都不一样。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"旧的"。后文描述高矮不一，说明 different。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：差异是因为不同的生长条件。A 项意为"条件"；B 项意为"季节"；C 项意为"价格"；D 项意为"故事"。growing conditions 意为"生长条件"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看着一粒小种子长成绿色植物真的很奇妙。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"累人的"；C 项意为"惊奇的/奇妙的"；D 项意为"令人困惑的"。种子成长是 amazing 的过程。故选 C。
