# 中考英语完形填空：班级篮球友谊赛

## 完形填空原文

Last Thursday, our class had a basketball friendly match against Class Two. I was chosen as one of the ___1___, and I felt both proud and nervous.

Before the match, our PE teacher helped us warm up. He told us to work as a team and not to play ___2___. He said that passing the ball was just as important as shooting. We all nodded and promised to do our best.

The match began at 4:00 p.m. Class Two was very ___3___. Their tallest player kept scoring from close range. By half-time, we were behind by six points. Some of my teammates looked ___4___, but our captain said, "Don't give up! We still have the second half."

In the second half, we changed our ___5___. We started passing the ball more quickly and running into open spaces. I received a pass from my teammate and made a three-point shot. The crowd ___6___ loudly. That goal gave us a lot of confidence.

With two minutes left, the score was tied. I got the ball near the basket and tried to shoot, but a player from Class Two ___7___ me. The ball went to my teammate Wang Hao, who jumped up and scored the winning point! We all ___8___ with joy.

After the match, we shook hands with Class Two. They congratulated us, and we thanked them for a great game. Our teacher said he was ___9___ of our teamwork and spirit.

This match taught me that success comes from working together. Even when things seem ___10___, we should never stop trying.

## 选项

1. A. winners &emsp;&emsp; B. players &emsp;&emsp; C. teachers &emsp;&emsp; D. judges
2. A. fairly &emsp;&emsp; B. alone &emsp;&emsp; C. safely &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
3. A. weak &emsp;&emsp; B. lazy &emsp;&emsp; C. strong &emsp;&emsp; D. shy
4. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. bored

6. A. cried &emsp;&emsp; B. cheered &emsp;&emsp; C. complained &emsp;&emsp; D. whispered
7. A. helped &emsp;&emsp; B. caught &emsp;&emsp; C. blocked &emsp;&emsp; D. invited
8. A. jumped &emsp;&emsp; B. slept &emsp;&emsp; C. sat &emsp;&emsp; D. fell
9. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. sure
10. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. funny &emsp;&emsp; C. safe &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被选为球员之一。A 项意为"获胜者"；B 项意为"球员"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"裁判"。被选去上场比赛是 players。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师告诉我们要团队合作，不要单打独斗。A 项意为"公平地"；B 项意为"独自"；C 项意为"安全地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。强调团队不能 play alone。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：二班非常强。A 项意为"弱的"；B 项意为"懒的"；C 项意为"强的"；D 项意为"害羞的"。对手持续得分说明 strong。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些队友看起来很担心。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。落后六分会 worried。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下半场我们改变了策略。A 项意为"衣服"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"策略"；D 项意为"座位"。changed strategy 意为"改变策略"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：观众大声欢呼。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"欢呼"；C 项意为"抱怨"；D 项意为"低语"。进球后 cheered loudly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：二班的球员挡住了我。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"抓住"；C 项意为"挡住"；D 项意为"邀请"。blocked 意为"防守/挡住"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都高兴地跳了起来。A 项意为"跳"；B 项意为"睡"；C 项意为"坐"；D 项意为"跌倒"。jumped with joy 意为"欢呼雀跃"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说他为我们的团队合作感到骄傲。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"确定的"。proud of 意为"以……为傲"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使事情看起来很困难，我们也不应该停止尝试。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"安全的"；D 项意为"困难的"。落后时局面 difficult。故选 D。
