# 中考英语完形填空：校园垃圾分类

## 完形填空原文

Our school has started a recycling program to protect the environment. At first, many students did not think it was ___1___. They threw all kinds of rubbish into the same bin because it seemed easier. But our teacher, Mr. Wang, never ___2___ trying to change our habits. He believed that young people could lead the way in caring for our planet.

He placed different colored bins around the school — blue for paper, green for plastic, and yellow for metal. He also gave us a ___3___ about why recycling matters. "Every bottle you recycle can be ___4___ into something new," he said. His words made us think about the impact of our daily choices.

Gradually, students began to ___5___ the rules. We started to separate our waste carefully before throwing it away. Some students even became "recycling monitors" to help others. I ___6___ to be one of them. It was my job to check the bins each day and make sure everything was in the right place.

At the end of the first month, we had a surprising ___7___. Our school had recycled over 500 kg of waste! The principal was so pleased that he ___8___ our class with a certificate at the school assembly. We felt proud of what we had achieved together.

Now recycling has become a ___9___ part of our daily life. We not only do it at school but also at home with our families. Our small actions can make a big ___10___ to the Earth. Let's all work together for a greener future!

## 选项

1. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. interesting
2. A. gave up &emsp;&emsp; B. stopped &emsp;&emsp; C. kept &emsp;&emsp; D. finished
3. A. talk &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. speech &emsp;&emsp; D. story
4. A. made &emsp;&emsp; B. turned &emsp;&emsp; C. changed &emsp;&emsp; D. become
5. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. obey &emsp;&emsp; C. understand &emsp;&emsp; D. learn
6. A. wanted &emsp;&emsp; B. hoped &emsp;&emsp; C. volunteered &emsp;&emsp; D. decided
7. A. news &emsp;&emsp; B. result &emsp;&emsp; C. number &emsp;&emsp; D. change
8. A. gave &emsp;&emsp; B. presented &emsp;&emsp; C. offered &emsp;&emsp; D. sent
9. A. normal &emsp;&emsp; B. natural &emsp;&emsp; C. usual &emsp;&emsp; D. regular
10. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. help &emsp;&emsp; D. effect

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，许多学生认为这并不___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。"important"与后文"never gave up trying"形成对比，学生最初不重视，老师却坚持改变习惯。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但王老师从未___努力改变我们的习惯。A 项"gave up"意为"放弃"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"完成"。"never gave up"为常用表达，意为"从未放弃"，与老师坚持教育相呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他还给我们做了一场关于回收重要性的___。A 项意为"谈话、讲座"；B 项意为"课"；C 项意为"演讲"；D 项意为"故事"。"give a talk"为固定搭配，意为"做讲座、发言"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你回收的每个瓶子都可以___成新的东西。A 项"made"通常与"into"搭配；B 项"turned into"意为"变成、转化为"；C 项"changed into"也可表转化；D 项"become"不与"into"搭配。"be turned into"强调废旧物品被转化再利用。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，学生们开始___这些规则。A 项意为"遵守"；B 项意为"服从"；C 项意为"理解"；D 项意为"学习"。"follow the rules"为常用搭配，意为"遵守规则"，与垃圾分类情境相符。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___成为其中一员。A 项意为"想要"；B 项意为"希望"；C 项意为"自愿"；D 项意为"决定"。"volunteered"与"recycling monitors"相呼应，表示主动报名担任环保监督员。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一个月结束时，我们有了令人惊讶的___。A 项意为"新闻"；B 项意为"结果"；C 项意为"数字"；D 项意为"变化"。"result"与后文"recycled over 500 kg"相呼应，指回收工作的成果。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长非常高兴，___我们班一张证书。A 项意为"给"；B 项意为"颁发、授予"；C 项意为"提供"；D 项意为"发送"。"present sb with sth"为正式表达，意为"向某人颁发某物"，适用于颁奖场合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如今，回收已成为我们日常生活中___的一部分。A 项意为"正常的"；B 项意为"自然的"；C 项意为"通常的"；D 项意为"定期的"。"natural part"强调回收已融入生活、成为自然而然的习惯。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的小小行动能为地球带来很大___。A 项意为"变化"；B 项"make a difference"意为"产生影响、有所作为"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"效果"。"make a big difference"为固定搭配，强调个人行动的重要性。故选 B。
