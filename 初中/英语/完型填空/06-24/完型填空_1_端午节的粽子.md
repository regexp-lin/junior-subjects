# 中考英语完形填空：端午节的粽子

## 完形填空原文

The Dragon Boat Festival is one of the most important traditional festivals in China. It falls on the fifth day of the fifth month of the lunar calendar. People celebrate it in many ways, and each family has its own customs.

One of the most ___1___ traditions is making zongzi. Zongzi is a kind of sticky rice dumpling wrapped in bamboo leaves. It can be sweet or salty, with different fillings like red beans or meat. Every year before the festival, my grandmother would ___2___ me how to make them. At first, I found it difficult to fold the leaves correctly, but with practice, I gradually ___3___ the skill. Now I can make zongzi by myself.

The story behind the festival is about Qu Yuan, a famous poet who loved his ___4___. When his country was in danger, he jumped into a river. People threw zongzi into the water to ___5___ fish from eating his body. Today, we still make zongzi to remember him and his spirit.

During the festival, dragon boat races are also ___6___ in many cities. Teams row their boats to the beat of drums. The ___7___ is exciting to watch. Last year, I went to see a race with my family. We cheered for our ___8___ team and felt very proud when they won first place.

The Dragon Boat Festival teaches us to remember our ___9___ and traditions. It brings families together and helps us ___10___ our cultural heritage. I love this festival very much, and I hope to pass these traditions on to the next generation.

## 选项

1. A. popular &emsp;&emsp; B. common &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. famous
2. A. tell &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. show &emsp;&emsp; D. give
3. A. learned &emsp;&emsp; B. improved &emsp;&emsp; C. mastered &emsp;&emsp; D. developed
4. A. family &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. country &emsp;&emsp; D. people
5. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. prevent &emsp;&emsp; D. keep
6. A. made &emsp;&emsp; B. held &emsp;&emsp; C. done &emsp;&emsp; D. played
7. A. sport &emsp;&emsp; B. game &emsp;&emsp; C. race &emsp;&emsp; D. competition
8. A. favorite &emsp;&emsp; B. local &emsp;&emsp; C. own &emsp;&emsp; D. best
9. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. history &emsp;&emsp; C. culture &emsp;&emsp; D. heroes
10. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. protect &emsp;&emsp; C. value &emsp;&emsp; D. keep

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最___的传统之一就是包粽子。A 项意为"流行的、受欢迎的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"著名的"。"popular"强调端午节包粽子这一习俗广受欢迎、流传广泛。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每年节前，奶奶都会___我如何包粽子。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"展示"；D 项意为"给"。"teach sb how to do sth"为固定搭配，意为"教某人如何做某事"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我觉得很难，但经过练习，我逐渐___了这门技艺。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"提高"；C 项意为"掌握"；D 项意为"发展"。"mastered"与"with practice"相呼应，表示熟练掌握技能。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：屈原是一位热爱___的著名诗人。A 项意为"家人"；B 项意为"朋友"；C 项意为"国家"；D 项意为"人们"。"country"与屈原爱国诗人的历史人物形象相符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人们把粽子扔进水中，以___鱼吃他的身体。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"阻止"；C 项意为"预防"；D 项意为"保持"。"stop sb from doing sth"为固定搭配，意为"阻止某人做某事"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：节日期间，也会___龙舟赛。A 项意为"制作"；B 项意为"举行、举办"；C 项意为"做"；D 项意为"玩"。"hold"与"race"搭配，意为"举办比赛"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这场___看起来令人兴奋。A 项意为"运动"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"赛跑、比赛"；D 项意为"竞赛"。"race"与前文"dragon boat races"相呼应，指龙舟赛本身。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们为我们___的队伍加油。A 项意为"最喜欢的"；B 项意为"当地的"；C 项意为"自己的"；D 项意为"最好的"。"local team"与观看龙舟赛的地域情境相呼应，指本地的队伍。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：端午节教导我们铭记我们的___和传统。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"历史"；C 项意为"文化"；D 项意为"英雄"。"history"与"remember"、与屈原故事相呼应，强调铭记历史。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它把家人聚在一起，帮助我们___我们的文化遗产。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"保护"；C 项意为"珍视、重视"；D 项意为"保持"。"value"强调重视和珍惜文化传统，与"cultural heritage"搭配恰当。故选 C。
