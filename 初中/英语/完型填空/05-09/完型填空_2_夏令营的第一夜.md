# 中考英语完形填空：夏令营的第一夜

## 完形填空原文

I had never been away from home for more than one night. So when my parents signed me up for a week-long summer camp, I was ___1___. What if I didn't like the food? What if the other kids were mean? What if I couldn't fall asleep?

The bus ride to the camp took three hours. I sat by the window, clutching my backpack and staring at the passing trees. The girl sitting next to me was called Xiao Wen. She tried to start a conversation, but I was too ___2___ to talk.

When we arrived, the camp looked nothing like I had imagined. It was in the mountains, surrounded by tall pine trees. The air smelled fresh, and I could hear a stream ___3___ somewhere nearby. For a moment, I forgot about being scared.

Our cabins were small but ___4___. Each one had four bunk beds. I got the top bunk. When I climbed up, my bag fell and landed on the head of the girl below me. It was Xiao Wen again. She burst out laughing, and this time, I laughed too.

After dinner, the camp leader organized a bonfire. We sat in a circle and introduced ourselves. When my ___5___ came, my voice shook. "Hi, I'm Li Fang. I'm from Guangzhou. I've never been to a camp before." To my surprise, several other kids said the same thing. I wasn't the only ___6___ one.

That night, as I lay in my bunk staring at the wooden ceiling, I couldn't sleep. The forest sounds were strange and ___7___ — owls hooting, branches creaking, and something rustling in the bushes outside.

"Are you awake?" whispered Xiao Wen from below.

"Yes," I whispered back.

"Me too. I'm a little scared."

"Me too," I admitted.

We talked in whispers for almost an hour — about school, about our families, about what we wanted to be when we grew up. ___8___ I knew it, my eyes were getting heavy.

I woke up to sunlight streaming through the window and birds singing outside. Xiao Wen was already up, sitting on her bed and ___9___.

"Good morning, roommate," she said cheerfully.

I smiled. The night had seemed so long and frightening, but here in the morning light, everything felt ___10___. And I realized that having someone to share your fears with makes them only half as big.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当父母帮我报了一周夏令营，我很___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。后面连续三个"What if"的担忧，说明"我"很"紧张"（nervous）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，没法说话。A 项意为"忙的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"害羞的"。第一次远离家，心里充满担忧，太"焦虑"（worried）所以不想说话。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能听到一条溪流在附近___。A 项意为"流淌"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"变干"；D 项意为"上升"。溪流在山间"流淌"（running），能听到水声。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小木屋小但___。A 项意为"黑暗的"；B 项意为"舒适的"；C 项意为"空的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。虽然小但"温馨舒适"（cozy），有上下铺。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我的___时，我声音发抖。A 项意为"公交车"；B 项意为"轮次"；C 项意为"小屋"；D 项意为"愿望"。大家轮流自我介绍，"my turn"（轮到我）。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不是唯一一个___的。A 项意为"新来的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。好几个孩子也说是第一次来夏令营，"我"不是唯一的"新手"（new one）。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：森林的声音很奇怪且___。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"不熟悉的"；D 项意为"温柔的"。猫头鹰叫声、树枝吱嘎声对城市孩子来说很"陌生"（unfamiliar）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___我意识到，我的眼皮已经变重了。A 项意为"之后"；B 项意为"在……之前"；C 项意为"自从"；D 项意为"直到"。"Before I knew it"（不知不觉间）是固定表达，聊着聊着就困了。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小文已经起来了，坐在床上___。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"微笑"；D 项意为"画画"。早上醒来，室友开心地"微笑"（smiling）着打招呼。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在晨光中，一切感觉都___了。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"一样的"；C 项意为"更糟的"；D 项意为"错的"。昨晚漫长恐惧，但早晨一切感觉"不同了"（different），变得美好起来。故选 A。
