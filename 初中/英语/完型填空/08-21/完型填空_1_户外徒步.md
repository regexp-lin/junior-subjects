# 中考英语完形填空：户外徒步

## 完形填空原文

During the National Day holiday, my family went hiking in the mountains. We chose a trail that was suitable for beginners. The path was well-marked, and we had a map to ___1___ us.

We left home early in the morning. The drive to the mountain took about two hours. When we arrived, the parking lot was already full of cars. Many families had the same ___2___ as us — to enjoy the fresh air and exercise.

The trail started at the foot of the mountain. Tall trees lined the path, and we could hear birds singing. My younger brother ran ahead excitedly. My father reminded him to stay close because the path could be ___3___ in some places.

We walked for about an hour before we reached a rest area. There was a wooden bench where we could sit and drink water. My mother had prepared sandwiches and fruit for our lunch. We ate a little and then continued our ___4___.

The middle part of the trail was steeper. We had to climb over rocks and tree roots. I held my brother's hand to help him. My legs began to feel tired, but I did not want to give up. "We are almost at the top," my father encouraged us.

When we finally reached the summit, the view was amazing. We could see the whole city below us. The sky was clear and blue. We took many photos to ___5___ the moment. My brother jumped up and down with joy.

We rested at the top for thirty minutes. Then we started the descent. Going downhill was easier on our legs but required more ___6___. We had to step carefully to avoid slipping on loose stones.

On our way down, we met an elderly couple who were climbing up. They moved slowly but steadily. We greeted them and stepped aside to let them pass. The man smiled and said, "Enjoy your hike. The view at the top is worth the ___7___."

When we got back to the car, we were tired but satisfied. My brother fell asleep on the drive home. I thought about what the man had said. He was right — the effort we had put in made the beautiful view even more ___8___.

My father said we would try a longer trail next time. I am looking forward to it. Hiking has become one of our favourite family ___9___. It is a great way to spend time together, stay healthy, and ___10___ the beauty of nature.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小路有清晰标记，我们有地图来___我们。A 项意为"跟随"；B 项意为"引导"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"忘记"。"guide us"意为"为我们引路"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多家庭和我们有同样的___。A 项意为"想法"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"答案"。"the same idea"指同样的出游目的。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为小路有些地方可能很___。A 项意为"宽的"；B 项意为"平坦的"；C 项意为"窄的"；D 项意为"直的"。山路窄需跟紧孩子，"narrow"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们吃了点东西，然后继续___。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"旅程"；C 项意为"餐食"；D 项意为"交谈"。"continue our journey"意为"继续行程"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们拍了很多照片来___这一刻。A 项意为"节省"；B 项意为"捕捉"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"错过"。"capture the moment"意为"定格这一刻"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下山对腿轻松些，但需要更___。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"力量"；C 项意为"小心"；D 项意为"勇气"。下山需谨慎防滑，"care"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：山顶的景色值得付出的___。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"努力"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"食物"。"worth the effort"意为"值得这番付出"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的付出让美景更加___。A 项意为"普通"；B 项意为"特别"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"糟糕"。付出后的收获更显珍贵，"special"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：徒步已成为我们最喜爱的家庭___之一。A 项意为"家务"；B 项意为"规则"；C 项意为"活动"；D 项意为"责任"。"family activities"意为"家庭活动"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：是共度时光、保持健康、___自然之美的好方式。A 项意为"忽略"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"破坏"。"enjoy the beauty"意为"欣赏/享受自然之美"。故选 C。
