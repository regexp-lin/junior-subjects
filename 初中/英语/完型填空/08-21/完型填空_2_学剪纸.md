# 中考英语完形填空：学剪纸

## 完形填空原文

My grandmother is good at paper-cutting. She can cut beautiful patterns of flowers, animals, and Chinese characters. Last week, she offered to teach me this traditional skill. I was excited to learn.

Grandmother said that paper-cutting required ___1___ and a steady hand. "You cannot rush," she told me. "Each cut must be careful and precise." She gave me a pair of small scissors and some red paper. In Chinese culture, red paper is often used for paper-cutting because it symbolises good ___2___.

We started with a simple shape — a circle. I folded the paper in half, then in half again. Grandmother showed me where to cut. I tried to follow her instructions, but my first circle looked more like a square with rounded corners. She laughed kindly and said, "Practice makes perfect."

After a few more tries, I managed to cut a proper circle. Grandmother praised me and said we could move on to something more ___3___. She taught me how to cut a "double happiness" character. This character is often used at weddings. We had to fold the paper in a special way and cut along the lines she had drawn.

I found the character much harder than the circle. My scissors kept slipping, and I accidentally cut through the wrong part. The paper tore. I felt ___4___, but Grandmother said it was normal. "Even experienced cutters make mistakes. The important thing is to learn from them."

I took a new piece of paper and tried again. This time, I cut more slowly. I paid attention to every small movement. When I finished, I unfolded the paper. There was the "double happiness" character! It was not perfect, but it was recognisable. I was so ___5___ that I ran to show my parents.

Grandmother said that paper-cutting has a long history in China. It dates back more than two thousand years. In the past, people used it to decorate windows and doors during festivals. Today, it is still a popular ___6___ art form.

She gave me a book with many paper-cutting patterns. "Practise these at home," she said. "When you come back next week, we can try more difficult designs." I thanked her and promised to ___7___.

That night, I practised cutting a butterfly. It took me almost an hour. The wings were tricky because they had many small details. But when I finally finished, the butterfly looked lovely. I stuck it on my bedroom window. The sunlight shone through the cuts, creating beautiful ___8___ on the wall.

Learning paper-cutting has made me appreciate Chinese traditional culture more. Every cut connects me to the past. I hope to master this skill and pass it on to others someday. Traditional arts should not be ___9___. They are a precious part of our heritage that we must ___10___ and protect.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说剪纸需要___和稳定的手法。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"力量"；D 项意为"运气"。"patience"与"不能着急"呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为红色象征着好___。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"天气"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"消息"。"good luck"意为"好运"，红色寓意吉祥。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我们可以尝试更___的东西。A 项意为"简单"；B 项意为"困难"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"简单"。"more difficult"与从圆圈进阶到双喜字相符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：纸撕了，我感到___。A 项意为"骄傲"；B 项意为"挫败"；C 项意为"兴奋"；D 项意为"放松"。剪坏后的沮丧，"frustrated"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，跑去向父母展示。A 项意为"疲倦"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"无聊"。"proud"表示完成作品后的自豪。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：至今仍是流行的___艺术形式。A 项意为"现代"；B 项意为"传统"；C 项意为"外国"；D 项意为"数字"。"traditional art form"意为"传统艺术形式"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感谢她并答应会___。A 项意为"练习"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"拒绝"。"promised to practise"意为"答应会练习"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阳光透过剪痕，在墙上投下美丽的___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"影子/光影"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"味道"。"shadows"指光影效果。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：传统艺术不应被___。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"练习"；D 项意为"教授"。"should not be forgotten"意为"不应被遗忘"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须___和保护它。A 项意为"忽略"；B 项意为"珍视"；C 项意为"破坏"；D 项意为"售卖"。"value and protect"意为"珍视并保护"。故选 B。
