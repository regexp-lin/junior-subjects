# 中考英语完形填空：骑自行车环湖

## 完形填空原文

Every Sunday morning, my father and I go cycling around the lake near our home. It has become our favourite weekend activity. The lake is about five kilometres around, and it takes us about forty minutes to complete one ___1___.

We usually leave home at six o'clock, when the air is still cool and fresh. My father says the morning is the best time for exercise because the park is quiet and ___2___. We ride side by side along the path, sometimes talking, sometimes just enjoying the view.

The path around the lake is flat and well-maintained. Tall trees line both sides, providing shade on hot days. We often see people jogging, walking their dogs, or ___3___ tai chi by the water. Everyone greets each other with a smile or a nod.

Last week, we saw something special. A group of ducks were swimming near the shore. We stopped our bikes to watch them. The mother duck led her ducklings in a straight line. They looked so ___4___ that we could not help taking some photos.

My father taught me how to ride a bike when I was six. At first, I was afraid of falling. He held the back of my seat and ran beside me until I could balance ___5___. Now cycling has become a skill I use every day. I ride to school, to the library, and of course, around the lake with my father.

Cycling is not only good for our health but also a great way to spend ___6___ together. During our rides, my father tells me stories from his childhood. He says he used to cycle to school every day, rain or shine. Those stories help me understand him better.

Sometimes we take a break halfway. We sit on a bench, drink some water, and watch the ducks and boats on the ___7___. My father often says that we should appreciate the simple pleasures in life. A quiet morning by the lake is one of them.

I hope we can continue this tradition for many years. When I grow up, I want to bring my own children here and share the same ___8___ with them. Perhaps they will also learn to love cycling and the beauty of ___9___. For now, I will ___10___ enjoying these Sunday mornings with my father.

## 选项

1. A. game &emsp;&emsp; B. circle &emsp;&emsp; C. race &emsp;&emsp; D. trip
2. A. crowded &emsp;&emsp; B. peaceful &emsp;&emsp; C. noisy &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty
3. A. doing &emsp;&emsp; B. making &emsp;&emsp; C. playing &emsp;&emsp; D. watching
4. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. cute &emsp;&emsp; C. scary &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
5. A. myself &emsp;&emsp; B. himself &emsp;&emsp; C. themselves &emsp;&emsp; D. ourselves
6. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. space
7. A. road &emsp;&emsp; B. lake &emsp;&emsp; C. hill &emsp;&emsp; D. bridge
8. A. secret &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. experience &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson
9. A. nature &emsp;&emsp; B. city &emsp;&emsp; C. school &emsp;&emsp; D. home
10. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. finish

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：环湖一圈大约五公里，我们完成一___大约需要四十分钟。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"一圈"；C 项意为"比赛"；D 项意为"旅行"。"complete one circle"意为"完成一圈"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：早晨是锻炼的最佳时间，因为公园安静且___。A 项意为"拥挤的"；B 项意为"宁静的"；C 项意为"喧闹的"；D 项意为"脏的"。早晨公园宁静祥和，"peaceful"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们常看到人们慢跑、遛狗或在湖边___太极。A 项意为"做"；B 项意为"制作"；C 项意为"玩"；D 项意为"观看"。"do tai chi"为固定搭配，意为"打太极"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们看起来太___了，我们忍不住拍了照。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"可爱的"；C 项意为"可怕的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。小鸭子可爱才让人想拍照，"cute"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他扶着我的车座跟跑，直到我能自己保持平衡。A 项意为"我自己"；B 项意为"他自己"；C 项意为"他们自己"；D 项意为"我们自己"。"balance myself"指"自己保持平衡"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：骑车不仅有益健康，也是一起度过___的好方式。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时光"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"空间"。"spend time together"意为"共度时光"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们坐在长椅上，喝水，看___上的鸭子和船只。A 项意为"道路"；B 项意为"湖"；C 项意为"山"；D 项意为"桥"。上下文是环湖骑行，鸭子船只在湖上，"lake"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想带自己的孩子来这里，与他们分享同样的___。A 项意为"秘密"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"经历"；D 项意为"课程"。传承同样的骑行和亲近自然的经历，"experience"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许他们也会学会热爱骑行和大自然之美。A 项意为"自然"；B 项意为"城市"；C 项意为"学校"；D 项意为"家"。"the beauty of nature"意为"自然之美"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：眼下，我会___享受这些与父亲共度的周日早晨。A 项意为"继续"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"完成"。"keep doing"意为"继续做某事"。故选 A。
