# 中考英语完形填空：去书店买书

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday afternoon, I went to the new bookstore in the shopping mall with my best friend Lin Hui. We both love reading, and we had been ___1___ to visit this bookstore since it opened last month.

The bookstore was on the third floor. As soon as we entered, we were amazed by the number of books. There were shelves from floor to ceiling, filled with books of every ___2___. The smell of new books made us feel excited.

We decided to look for books in different sections. Lin Hui wanted to find a science fiction novel, while I was looking for a book about history. We agreed to meet at the checkout counter in one ___3___.

I walked slowly along the history section. There were so many interesting titles that I did not know which one to ___4___. Finally, I picked up a book about ancient China. I read the introduction on the back cover. It seemed perfect for my summer reading project.

When I looked at my watch, forty minutes had already passed. I hurried to the checkout counter. Lin Hui was already there, waiting with two books in her hands. "I found a great science fiction story and a collection of short ___5___," she said. "I could not decide between them, so I bought both."

We went to the coffee corner inside the bookstore. It had comfortable chairs and a quiet atmosphere. We ordered two cups of hot chocolate and ___6___ our new books. Lin Hui read the first chapter of her novel aloud. I listened and could already imagine the exciting story.

Before we left, we each got a membership card. The staff said that members could get a 10% ___7___ on all books. "We should come here every month," Lin Hui said. I agreed. A bookstore is much better than staring at a phone screen all day.

I spent sixty yuan on my history book. My parents had given me some pocket money for the summer, and I thought buying books was a good way to ___8___ it. Reading not only brings knowledge but also helps us relax after a busy day at school.

When I got home, I put the new book on my bedside table. I planned to read at least twenty ___9___ every night before sleep. My mother was happy to see my new book. She said she was proud that I chose to spend my time in a useful ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一直___自它上月开业以来去参观这家书店。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"忘记"。"had been planning"意为"一直计划着"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：书架上摆满了各种___的书。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"尺寸"；C 项意为"主题/科目"；D 项意为"价格"。"every subject"指各类题材的书籍。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们约定一___后在收银台见面。A 项意为"天"；B 项意为"周"；C 项意为"小时"；D 项意为"分钟"。逛书店通常约定一小时后见面，"hour"合理。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有那么多有趣的书名，我不知道选哪一本。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"选择"；C 项意为"借"；D 项意为"归还"。"which one to choose"意为"选哪一本"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我买了一本很棒的科幻故事和一本短篇___集。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"歌曲"；C 项意为"电影"；D 项意为"游戏"。"short stories"意为"短篇小说"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们点了两杯热巧克力，___了新书。A 项意为"关闭"；B 项意为"打开"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"扔"。"opened our new books"意为"翻开新书"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：店员说会员购书可享10%的___。A 项意为"折扣"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"建议"。"get a discount"意为"享受折扣"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得买书是___零花钱的好方式。A 项意为"节省"；B 项意为"使用"；C 项意为"丢失"；D 项意为"浪费"。"use it"意为"使用它（零花钱）"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我打算每晚睡前至少读二十___。A 项意为"词"；B 项意为"页"；C 项意为"本"；D 项意为"小时"。"twenty pages"表示读二十页，量合理。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她为我选择以有用的方式度过时间而感到骄傲。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"东西"。"in a useful way"意为"以有益的方式"。故选 A。
