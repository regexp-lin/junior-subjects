# 中考英语完形填空：食堂阿姨的南瓜粥

## 完形填空原文

Last winter, I caught a bad cold and stayed in the dorm for three days. I had no appetite and could hardly eat anything. On the fourth morning, I finally went to the canteen. I stood in line, feeling weak and ___1___. When my turn came, I just pointed at a bowl of rice porridge. The canteen auntie looked at me and said, "You look pale. Are you feeling better?"

I nodded. She then took a smaller bowl and filled it with something different — warm pumpkin porridge. "This is ___2___ for your stomach," she said. "No charge. Eat it slowly." I was surprised. I hadn't asked for it. I didn't even know her name. But she had noticed that I was ill and had cared enough to ___3___ something special.

I sat down and took a spoonful. The porridge was sweet and soft. With each bite, I felt warmer — not just in my body, but in my heart. That simple bowl of pumpkin porridge ___4___ me that kindness doesn't need to be loud or grand. Sometimes it's just a warm bowl of porridge from someone who pays attention.

Since then, I've learned to pay attention too. When a classmate seems ___5___, I ask if they need help. When someone drops their books, I pick them up. Small acts of kindness may seem ___6___, but they can mean a lot to someone who is having a hard day.

I still go to that canteen. The pumpkin porridge is now a regular dish. Every time I see it, I remember that winter morning — the steam rising from the bowl, the auntie's gentle smile, and the ___7___ that a stranger can make you feel at home. I hope one day I can ___8___ that warmth to others, just as she did for me.

The canteen auntie doesn't know my name either. We never had a long conversation. But she taught me something important: in a busy world, taking a moment to notice and care can ___9___ a big difference. A bowl of pumpkin porridge is just a bowl of porridge. But given with kindness, it becomes a ___10___ of comfort that stays with you for a long time.

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. hungry &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. bad &emsp;&emsp; C. ready &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
3. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. cook &emsp;&emsp; C. offer &emsp;&emsp; D. order
4. A. told &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. reminded &emsp;&emsp; D. allowed
5. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. upset &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
6. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. small &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
7. A. idea &emsp;&emsp; B. feeling &emsp;&emsp; C. plan &emsp;&emsp; D. thought
8. A. pass &emsp;&emsp; B. bring &emsp;&emsp; C. send &emsp;&emsp; D. carry
9. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. get &emsp;&emsp; D. have
10. A. sign &emsp;&emsp; B. piece &emsp;&emsp; C. part &emsp;&emsp; D. gift

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到虚弱且___。A 项意为"兴奋"；B 项意为"饥饿"；C 项意为"疲惫"；D 项意为"生气"。"tired"与"feeling weak"、"had a bad cold"、"could hardly eat"相呼应，病后体虚。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这对你的胃___。A 项意为"好"；B 项意为"坏"；C 项意为"准备好"；D 项意为"糟糕"。"good for"（对……有益）是常用搭配，南瓜粥对生病的人肠胃好。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她足够关心而___了特别的东西。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"烹饪"；C 项意为"提供"；D 项意为"点餐"。"offer"（主动提供）与"had noticed"、"cared enough"相呼应，阿姨主动送上。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那简单的一碗南瓜粥___我，善意不必大声或隆重。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"教会"；C 项意为"提醒"；D 项意为"允许"。"taught"与"learned to pay attention"相呼应，强调从中获得领悟。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当同学看起来___时，我问是否需要帮助。A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"难过/沮丧"；C 项意为"忙碌"；D 项意为"骄傲"。"upset"与"need help"相呼应，需要关心的是情绪不好的人。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的善意举动可能看起来很___。A 项意为"容易"；B 项意为"小"；C 项意为"奇怪"；D 项意为"困难"。"small"与"Small acts of kindness"、"can mean a lot"形成对比。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陌生人让你___有家的感觉。A 项意为"想法"；B 项意为"感觉"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"想法"。"feeling"与"make you feel at home"相呼应，指那种被关心的感觉。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望有一天能把那份温暖___给他人。A 项意为"传递"；B 项意为"带来"；C 项意为"发送"；D 项意为"携带"。"pass...to"（把……传给）与"just as she did for me"相呼应，延续善意。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：花一点时间去注意和关心可以___很大的不同。A 项意为"制造"；B 项意为"拿"；C 项意为"得到"；D 项意为"有"。"make a difference"（产生影响）是固定搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但带着善意给出时，它就成了一份能长久陪伴的安慰___。A 项意为"标志"；B 项意为"一块"；C 项意为"一部分"；D 项意为"礼物"。"gift"（礼物）与"given with kindness"、"comfort"相呼应。故选 D。
