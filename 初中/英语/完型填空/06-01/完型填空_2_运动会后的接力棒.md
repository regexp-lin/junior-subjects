# 中考英语完形填空：运动会后的接力棒

## 完形填空原文

Our school sports meet was last month. I was in the relay race team. We had trained for weeks. We believed we would win. But during the race, I dropped the baton. Our team came in last. I felt so ashamed that I didn't want to ___1___ anyone.

After the race, I sat alone on the steps. I held the baton in my hand. It felt heavy — not because it was, but because of the ___2___ I had let down. I was about to leave when my teammate Tom came over. He sat down beside me without saying a word. Then he said, "We ___3___ as a team. We lose as a team too. Nobody runs alone in a relay."

His words surprised me. I had expected him to be angry. Instead, he was ___4___. "Next year we'll train harder," he said. "And you'll run the last leg again. We believe in you."

That baton — the one I had dropped — became something different to me. It was no longer a sign of ___5___. It became a reminder that my teammates had chosen to stand by me. They didn't blame me. They chose to ___6___ together for the next race.

I kept the baton. I put it on my desk. Every time I felt like giving up on something difficult, I looked at it. It reminded me that one ___7___ doesn't define you. What matters is whether you stand up again. It also reminded me that we need people who ___8___ us when we fall — people who don't walk away but stay and help us get back up.

Now I understand that a relay race is not just about speed. It's about trust. When you pass the baton, you're saying, "I trust you to run with this." When someone drops it, the real test is: do we ___9___ each other enough to try again?

That baton still sits on my desk. I don't need to look at it often anymore. But I will never forget the lesson it taught me: in life, we all drop the baton sometimes. The ___10___ is not to run away. It's to pick it up, pass it on, and keep running — together.

## 选项

1. A. see &emsp;&emsp; B. meet &emsp;&emsp; C. talk to &emsp;&emsp; D. look at
2. A. teachers &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. team &emsp;&emsp; D. family
3. A. win &emsp;&emsp; B. run &emsp;&emsp; C. play &emsp;&emsp; D. train
4. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. calm &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. nervous
5. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. victory &emsp;&emsp; C. failure &emsp;&emsp; D. courage
6. A. practice &emsp;&emsp; B. compete &emsp;&emsp; C. fight &emsp;&emsp; D. prepare
7. A. race &emsp;&emsp; B. mistake &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. moment
8. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. support &emsp;&emsp; C. teach &emsp;&emsp; D. watch
9. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. need &emsp;&emsp; C. trust &emsp;&emsp; D. know
10. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. way &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. question

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太羞愧了，不想___任何人。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"面对/遇见"；C 项意为"和……说话"；D 项意为"看"。"meet"在此处意为"面对"，与"felt so ashamed"相呼应，不想面对队友。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为___让我失望了。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"朋友"；C 项意为"队伍"；D 项意为"家人"。"team"与"relay race team"、"let down"相呼应，接力是团队项目。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们作为队伍___。A 项意为"赢"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"玩"；D 项意为"训练"。"win"与"lose"形成对比，输赢都是团队的事。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相反，他很___。A 项意为"伤心"；B 项意为"平静"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"紧张"。"calm"与"expected him to be angry"形成对比，队友没有发火而是冷静安慰。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它不再是___的标志。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"胜利"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"勇气"。"failure"与"dropped the baton"、"came in last"相呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们选择一起___下一场比赛。A 项意为"练习"；B 项意为"竞争"；C 项意为"战斗"；D 项意为"准备"。"prepare for"（为……做准备）与"Next year we'll train harder"相呼应。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个___不能定义你。A 项意为"比赛"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"时刻"。"mistake"（失误）与"dropped the baton"相呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们需要在我们跌倒时___我们的人。A 项意为"跟随"；B 项意为"支持"；C 项意为"教导"；D 项意为"观看"。"support"与"stand by me"、"don't walk away but stay"相呼应。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们是否足够___彼此愿意再试一次？A 项意为"喜欢"；B 项意为"需要"；C 项意为"信任"；D 项意为"了解"。"trust"与"I trust you to run with this"相呼应，接力赛核心是信任。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___不是逃跑。A 项意为"答案"；B 项意为"方式"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"问题"。"answer"（答案）与"the lesson"相呼应，面对失误的正确做法是什么。故选 A。
