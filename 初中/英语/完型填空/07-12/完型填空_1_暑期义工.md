# 中考英语完形填空：暑期义工

## 完形填空原文

This summer, I joined a volunteer programme at the community centre. Every Tuesday and Thursday, I would go there to help. My main job was to help look after the children while their parents attended classes. I had never done this kind of work before, so I felt a little ___1___ at first.

On my first day, I met a girl named Xiaomei. She was about six years old and very shy. She did not want to leave her mother's side. I sat down next to her and asked if she liked to draw. She nodded. I gave her some paper and crayons. ___2___, she began to draw a picture of her family. As she drew, she became more relaxed and even started to talk to me. I felt happy that I could help her feel at ease.

Some days the work was tiring. There were many children and they all wanted attention. I had to read stories, play games, and help them with simple activities. But when I saw their smiling faces, all my ___3___ seemed to disappear. One boy named Tao Tao always made me laugh. He would pretend to be a superhero and run around the room. The children loved him.

One afternoon, a little girl fell and hurt her knee. She started to cry. I ___4___ her to the office and found a bandage. I cleaned the small cut and put the bandage on. "You are very brave," I told her. She stopped crying and gave me a small smile. That moment, I understood that being a volunteer was not just about having fun. It was about being there when someone needed ___5___.

By the end of the summer, I had made many new friends — both the children and the other volunteers. On my last day, Xiaomei gave me a drawing she had made. It showed the two of us holding hands. "Thank you for playing with me," she said. I was deeply ___6___. I realised that even small acts of kindness can make a big ___7___. I hope to continue volunteering in the future. Helping others has become something I truly ___8___. That summer taught me the real ___9___ of giving. I will always ___10___ those warm moments with the children.

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. Finally &emsp;&emsp; B. Sadly &emsp;&emsp; C. Suddenly &emsp;&emsp; D. Quickly
3. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. worry &emsp;&emsp; C. tiredness &emsp;&emsp; D. hunger
4. A. sent &emsp;&emsp; B. carried &emsp;&emsp; C. led &emsp;&emsp; D. drove
5. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. food
6. A. moved &emsp;&emsp; B. surprised &emsp;&emsp; C. scared &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
7. A. problem &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. mistake &emsp;&emsp; D. change
8. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. finish
9. A. cost &emsp;&emsp; B. meaning &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. place
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. expect

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从没做过这种工作，起初感到有点___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"生气的"。第一次做义工会紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___，她开始画全家福。A 项意为"最终"；B 项意为"悲伤地"；C 项意为"突然"；D 项意为"很快地"。从害羞到愿意画画，是逐渐放松后的结果，"Finally"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到他们笑脸时，我的___似乎都消失了。A 项意为"快乐"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"疲惫"；D 项意为"饥饿"。前文说工作累，看到笑容后疲惫感消失，"tiredness"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___她去办公室找创可贴。A 项意为"送"；B 项意为"抱"；C 项意为"引领"；D 项意为"开车送"。"led her"意为"带她去"，小孩能走，用"lead"最合适。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：义工是在别人需要___时出现。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"金钱"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"食物"。义工提供帮助，"help"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：收到小美的画，我深受___。A 项意为"感动的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"担心的"。"deeply moved"意为"深受感动"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的善举能产生很大___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"影响"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"改变"。"make a difference"意为"产生影响"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他人成了我真正___的事。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"享受"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"完成"。乐意继续做义工，是享受帮助他人，"enjoy"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个夏天让我懂得了给予的真正___。A 项意为"代价"；B 项意为"意义"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"地方"。"the meaning of giving"意为"给予的意义"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会永远___与孩子们相处的温暖时光。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"期待"。难忘的回忆用"remember"正确。故选 B。
