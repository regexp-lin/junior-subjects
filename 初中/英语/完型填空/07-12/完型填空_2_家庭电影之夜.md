# 中考英语完形填空：家庭电影之夜

## 完形填空原文

Every Friday night, our family has a special tradition — we watch a movie together. We take turns choosing the film. Last week it was my ___1___. I chose an animated film that Mum said was too childish, but Dad said we should watch whatever ___2___ me happy. So we did.

We make popcorn and bring out some snacks. Mum always prepares a big bowl of fruit. "We need to eat healthily even when we watch movies," she says. We sit on the sofa with the lights off. Dad dims the lamp and the room becomes cosy. The film begins.

During the movie, we laugh together at the funny parts. Sometimes my younger brother asks questions about what will happen next. "Shh, just ___3___," I tell him. He always gets too ___4___ and wants to know the ending right away. "You will find out." Halfway through, Mum passes around the popcorn. We share everything. It feels warm to be together like this.

After the film, we always talk about it. Dad asks us what we liked most. My brother says he liked the ___5___ character who could fly. I say I liked the music. Mum says she liked the message — that family is important. We all have different opinions, and that is fine. Talking about the film together is sometimes more ___6___ than watching it.

I look forward to Friday nights. In our busy lives, we often do things alone — homework, mobile phones, each in our own room. But on movie night, we are together. No phones, no distractions. Just us and a good story. These moments have become my favourite ___7___ of the week. I hope we never stop this tradition. Family movie night reminds me that the best things in life are often the simplest — being with the people we ___8___, sharing laughter and stories. That is worth more than any film. I have learned that ___9___ time with family is one of life's greatest ___10___.

3. A. listen &emsp;&emsp; B. watch &emsp;&emsp; C. run &emsp;&emsp; D. speak
4. A. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
5. A. main &emsp;&emsp; B. brave &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
6. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. fun &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
7. A. day &emsp;&emsp; B. part &emsp;&emsp; C. subject &emsp;&emsp; D. game
8. A. thank &emsp;&emsp; B. love &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. miss

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周轮到我的___。A 项意为"轮次"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"主意"；D 项意为"电影"。"it was my turn"意为"轮到我了"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说该看___我开心的电影。A 项意为"使"；B 项意为"让"；C 项意为"有"；D 项意为"帮助"。"whatever made me happy"意为" whatever 让我开心的"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要___，待会就知道了。A 项意为"听"；B 项意为"看"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"说"。看电影时要弟弟安静地看，"watch"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他老是太___，想马上知道结局。A 项意为"困的"；B 项意为"兴奋的、好奇的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"累的"。急着想知道结局说明兴奋好奇，"excited"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：弟弟说他喜欢会飞的那个___角色。A 项意为"主要的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"滑稽的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。主角会飞较常见，"main character"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一起讨论电影有时比看更___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。讨论带来乐趣，"fun"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些时光成了一周里我最喜欢的___。A 项意为"日子"；B 项意为"部分"；C 项意为"科目"；D 项意为"游戏"。"favourite part of the week"意为"一周中最喜欢的部分"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与我们___的人在一起。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"爱"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"想念"。"the people we love"意为"我们爱的人"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我懂得了与家人___时光是人生最大的___之一。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"度过"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"节省"。"spending time"意为"度过时光"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与家人相处的时光是人生最大的___之一。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"礼物、恩赐"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"规则"。"greatest gifts"意为"最珍贵的礼物"。故选 B。
