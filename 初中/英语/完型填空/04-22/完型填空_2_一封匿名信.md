# 中考英语完形填空：一封匿名信

## 完形填空原文

After the mid-term exam, I got my worst grade ever in math. I felt like a complete ___1___. Everyone around me seemed to be doing well, while I was falling further and further behind.

I didn't tell anyone how I felt. I just put on a fake smile and pretended everything was ___2___. But inside, I was drowning in self-doubt.

On Thursday morning, I opened my desk and found a small piece of folded paper. It wasn't signed. In neat handwriting, it said: "Don't be so hard on yourself. One bad test doesn't ___3___ who you are. I believe in you."

I read it five times. Who wrote this? I looked around the classroom, but nobody was paying ___4___ to me. The note was simple, but it made my heavy heart feel just a little lighter.

The next day, there was another note: "Did you know that Einstein failed his college entrance exam? Failure is not the opposite of success — it's part of the ___5___ to success."

I smiled for the first time in days.

Every morning for a whole week, I found a new note in my desk. Each one had a different message of ___6___. One said, "You're stronger than you think." Another said, "Progress is more important than perfection."

By Friday, I felt completely ___7___. I started studying math again, asking questions in class, and even solving problems I used to skip. The notes had given me something I had lost — confidence.

I never found out who wrote those notes. I ___8___ it was my best friend Xiao Mei, because the handwriting looked similar to hers. But when I asked, she just smiled and said, "Does it really matter who wrote them? What matters is that they ___9___."

She was right. It didn't matter. What mattered was that someone in this world cared enough to write a few kind words to a ___10___ who was struggling. And those words changed everything.

## 选项

1. A. hero &emsp;&emsp; B. failure &emsp;&emsp; C. stranger &emsp;&emsp; D. winner
2. A. wrong &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. over &emsp;&emsp; D. fine
3. A. define &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. remember &emsp;&emsp; D. tell
4. A. attention &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. respect &emsp;&emsp; D. time
5. A. end &emsp;&emsp; B. journey &emsp;&emsp; C. dream &emsp;&emsp; D. plan
6. A. warning &emsp;&emsp; B. encouragement &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
7. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. different &emsp;&emsp; D. confused
8. A. feared &emsp;&emsp; B. doubted &emsp;&emsp; C. suspected &emsp;&emsp; D. denied
9. A. failed &emsp;&emsp; B. stopped &emsp;&emsp; C. helped &emsp;&emsp; D. hurt
10. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. parent &emsp;&emsp; C. classmate &emsp;&emsp; D. neighbor

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得自己完全是个___。A 项意为"英雄"；B 项意为"失败者"；C 项意为"陌生人"；D 项意为"赢家"。考了最差的成绩，自我感觉是个"失败者"（failure）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我只是假装一切都___。A 项意为"错的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"结束了"；D 项意为"好的"。他不想让别人知道自己难过，所以装作一切"都好"（fine）。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一次考砸不能___你是谁。A 项意为"定义"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"记住"；D 项意为"告诉"。匿名信的意思是一次考试成绩不能"定义"（define）一个人的全部价值。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：但没人在___我。A 项意为"注意"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"尊重"；D 项意为"时间"。"pay attention to"（注意）是固定搭配，他环顾四周想找出写纸条的人，但没人在看他。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：失败不是成功的反面——它是通往成功的___的一部分。A 项意为"结尾"；B 项意为"旅程"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"计划"。失败是通往成功的"旅程"（journey）中的一部分，体现了成长过程中失败的必然性。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一张都有不同的___信息。A 项意为"警告"；B 项意为"鼓励"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"悲伤"。"你比你想的更强大""进步比完美更重要"，这些都是"鼓励"（encouragement）的话语。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到周五，我感觉完全___了。A 项意为"疲倦的"；B 项意为"迷失的"；C 项意为"不一样的"；D 项意为"困惑的"。收到一周的鼓励纸条后，他重拾信心，重新开始努力学习，感觉自己变得"不一样"（different）了——从消沉变得积极。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___是我最好的朋友小梅写的。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"怀疑"；C 项意为"猜测/怀疑是"；D 项意为"否认"。笔迹跟小梅的很像，所以他"怀疑/猜测"（suspected）是她写的。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："重要的是它们___了你。"A 项意为"失败了"；B 项意为"停止了"；C 项意为"帮助了"；D 项意为"伤害了"。那些纸条让他重获信心、重新振作，它们"帮助"（helped）了他。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有人关心一个正在挣扎的___。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"父母"；C 项意为"同学"；D 项意为"邻居"。事情发生在教室里，纸条放在课桌里，写纸条的人和收到纸条的人都是同学，所以是关心一位正在困难中的"同学"（classmate）。故选 C。
