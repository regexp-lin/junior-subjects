# 中考英语完形填空：校门口的早餐摊

## 完形填空原文

Every morning, a small breakfast cart stood right outside our school gate. The owner was a middle-aged woman everyone called Aunt Fang. She sold steamed buns, soy milk, and egg pancakes. Her food was ___1___ but delicious.

Most students grabbed breakfast from her before the morning bell. Aunt Fang knew almost every student by ___2___. "The usual?" she would ask with a big smile, and before you could answer, your favorite food was already in her hands.

I had been buying breakfast from Aunt Fang since seventh grade. Over the years, I noticed something ___3___: she always gave extra food to a thin boy named Xiao Wei. His egg pancake was always bigger, and she would quietly slip an extra bun into his bag.

One day, I asked her about it. "Xiao Wei's family doesn't have much ___4___," she said softly. "His mom works two jobs, and he often comes to school without eating. I can't let a child go hungry."

"But doesn't that mean you ___5___ money?" I asked.

Aunt Fang laughed. "A little. But one extra bun won't make me poor. And seeing that boy eat a full breakfast makes me ___6___ than any money could."

This winter, something happened that showed how much Aunt Fang meant to all of us. She got sick and couldn't come for a whole week. The spot outside the school gate was ___7___. Students looked lost — not just because they missed the food, but because they missed her warm smile and cheerful voice.

When Aunt Fang finally came back, something ___8___ was waiting for her. The students had collected money and bought her a thick winter coat and a new pair of gloves. A card was attached: "Dear Aunt Fang, thank you for feeding us every morning. Please stay ___9___ and warm."

Aunt Fang read the card and tears ran down her face. "You kids," she whispered, shaking her head. But she was smiling.

That morning, I realized that kindness is like her soy milk — warm, simple, and it goes both ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. cheap &emsp;&emsp; C. rare &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
2. A. face &emsp;&emsp; B. name &emsp;&emsp; C. voice &emsp;&emsp; D. age
3. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. dangerous
4. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. food &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. space
5. A. save &emsp;&emsp; B. make &emsp;&emsp; C. borrow &emsp;&emsp; D. lose
6. A. sadder &emsp;&emsp; B. richer &emsp;&emsp; C. weaker &emsp;&emsp; D. lonelier
7. A. crowded &emsp;&emsp; B. noisy &emsp;&emsp; C. empty &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty
8. A. scary &emsp;&emsp; B. surprising &emsp;&emsp; C. confusing &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointing
9. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. healthy &emsp;&emsp; D. young
10. A. sides &emsp;&emsp; B. ways &emsp;&emsp; C. ends &emsp;&emsp; D. parts

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的食物___但美味。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"稀有的"；D 项意为"冷的"。路边早餐摊的食物自然是"便宜的"（cheap），but 转折表示虽然便宜但好吃。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：方阿姨几乎能叫出每个学生的___。A 项意为"脸"；B 项意为"名字"；C 项意为"声音"；D 项意为"年龄"。"know someone by name"（能叫出某人的名字）是固定表达，说明她跟学生们很熟。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我注意到一件___的事。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"危险的"。她悄悄多给小伟食物，这个细节不同寻常，是"特别的"（special）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："小伟的家里没有很多___。"A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"食物"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"空间"。妈妈打两份工，经济困难，没有多少"钱"（money）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："但那不是意味着你要___钱吗？"A 项意为"省"；B 项意为"赚"；C 项意为"借"；D 项意为"亏损"。多给食物不收钱，就是在"亏"（lose）钱。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到那个男孩吃上饱饭让我觉得比任何钱都___。A 项意为"更难过"；B 项意为"更富足"；C 项意为"更虚弱"；D 项意为"更孤独"。虽然少赚了一点钱，但精神上感觉更"富足"（richer），体现了精神层面的满足。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校门口那个位置是___的。A 项意为"拥挤的"；B 项意为"嘈杂的"；C 项意为"空的"；D 项意为"脏的"。方阿姨生病不来了，摊位是"空的"（empty），学生们不习惯。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有一件___的事在等着她。A 项意为"可怕的"；B 项意为"令人惊喜的"；C 项意为"令人困惑的"；D 项意为"令人失望的"。学生们集资买了冬衣和手套给她，这是一份"令人惊喜的"（surprising）回馈。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："请保持___和温暖。"A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"安静的"；C 项意为"健康的"；D 项意为"年轻的"。方阿姨刚生了病，学生们祝她保持"健康"（healthy），"stay healthy"是常见祝福语。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定表达
**【解析】** 本句意为：善意就像她的豆浆——温暖、简单，而且是双向的（goes both ___）。A 项意为"面"；B 项意为"方向"；C 项意为"末端"；D 项意为"部分"。"goes both ways"（双向的）是固定表达，意思是善意是相互的：方阿姨关照学生，学生也回馈方阿姨。故选 B。
