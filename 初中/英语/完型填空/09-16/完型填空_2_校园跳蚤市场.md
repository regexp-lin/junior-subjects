# 中考英语完形填空：校园跳蚤市场

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a flea market last Friday afternoon. The activity aimed to teach us about recycling and the ___1___ of saving money. Each class set up a stall in the playground.

My classmates and I brought things we no longer needed: books, toys, stationery and some clothes. We ___2___ them neatly on our table and wrote price tags. I was responsible for selling, so I had to talk to customers and explain the ___3___ of our items.

At first, few people came to our stall. I felt a little ___4___. Then I had an idea — I held up a sign that said "Buy one get one free for books!" Soon, more students came over. A girl bought three storybooks and said she would ___5___ them with her younger sister. I was glad that our books would have a new home.

The atmosphere became livelier. Students were walking around, ___6___ for good deals. Some were bargaining politely. Our teacher walked by and said we had done a good job. She also bought a small plant pot from us. By the end of the afternoon, we had ___7___ almost all our items. We felt very satisfied.

The money we earned would be donated to a charity that helps poor children. Knowing that our small efforts could ___8___ others made us feel meaningful. This flea market was not just about buying and selling — it was about sharing, caring and making a ___9___.

I hope our school will hold more such activities. They help us learn practical skills and understand that we can contribute to society even as students. Everyone has something to ___10___, and every little bit counts.

2. A. laid &emsp;&emsp; B. threw &emsp;&emsp; C. hid &emsp;&emsp; D. dropped
3. A. size &emsp;&emsp; B. quality &emsp;&emsp; C. color &emsp;&emsp; D. price
4. A. confident &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
5. A. share &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. sell &emsp;&emsp; D. throw
6. A. waiting &emsp;&emsp; B. looking &emsp;&emsp; C. asking &emsp;&emsp; D. paying
7. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. sold &emsp;&emsp; C. bought &emsp;&emsp; D. kept
8. A. hurt &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. catch
9. A. mistake &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. decision &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
10. A. receive &emsp;&emsp; B. offer &emsp;&emsp; C. need &emsp;&emsp; D. want

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：活动旨在让我们了解循环利用和节约用钱的重要性。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"重要性"；C 项意为"理由"；D 项意为"方法"。the importance of 意为"……的重要性"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把它们整齐地摆在桌上，并写好价签。A 项意为"放置；摆"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"掉落"。lay out 或 lay...on the table 表示"摆放"。 laid 是 lay 的过去式。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我负责销售，所以要向顾客介绍我们商品的质量。A 项意为"尺寸"；B 项意为"质量"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"价格"。向顾客推销时介绍商品 quality 常见。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初很少有人来我们摊位，我感到有点紧张。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"放松的"。客人少时担心销量，nervous 合适。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个女孩买了三本故事书，说要和妹妹一起分享。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"保留"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"扔"。share with 意为"与……分享"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生们四处走动，寻找划算的商品。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"寻找"；C 项意为"询问"；D 项意为"支付"。look for 意为"寻找"，good deals 指"好买卖"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到下午结束时，我们几乎卖完了所有商品。A 项意为"丢失"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"保留"。sell items 意为"售出商品"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：知道我们的小小努力能帮助他人，让我们感到很有意义。A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"阻止"；D 项意为"抓住"。捐助慈善，help others 正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这场跳蚤市场不仅是买卖，更是关于分享、关爱和做出改变。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"不同；改变"；C 项意为"决定"；D 项意为"选择"。make a difference 为固定搭配，意为"产生影响；做出改变"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都能有所贡献，每一份力量都算数。A 项意为"接收"；B 项意为"提供；贡献"；C 项意为"需要"；D 项意为"想要"。have something to offer 意为"有可贡献之处"。故选 B。
