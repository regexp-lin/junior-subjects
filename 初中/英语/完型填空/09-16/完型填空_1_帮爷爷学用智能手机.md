# 中考英语完形填空：帮爷爷学用智能手机

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather got a new smartphone as a birthday gift, but he didn't know how to use it. He asked me to ___1___ him. I was happy to help because I wanted to spend more time with him.

We started with the basic functions. First, I taught him how to ___2___ the phone and answer calls. He practiced many times and gradually got familiar with it. Then we moved on to WeChat. I showed him how to send voice messages and make video calls. When he ___3___ saw his old friend on the screen, he was so excited that his hands were shaking.

Sometimes Grandfather would forget the steps and ask me the ___4___ questions again. I never got impatient. I would explain slowly and ___5___ him to try again. I also wrote down the steps in a notebook so he could check when I was not around.

One day, Grandfather successfully made a video call to my aunt by himself. He was so ___6___ that he called me immediately to share the good news. I felt warm inside — teaching him was not just about the phone, it was about ___7___ our bond.

Through this experience, I learned that elderly people need our patience and ___8___. They may learn slowly, but they never stop trying. I also realized that technology can ___9___ people together, no matter how far apart they live.

Now Grandfather sends me voice messages every day. I think the best gift we can give our grandparents is our ___10___ and time.

## 选项

1. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. tell &emsp;&emsp; C. ask &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
2. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. answer &emsp;&emsp; C. receive &emsp;&emsp; D. pick
3. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. once &emsp;&emsp; C. finally &emsp;&emsp; D. usually
4. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. same &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. easy
5. A. encourage &emsp;&emsp; B. force &emsp;&emsp; C. allow &emsp;&emsp; D. invite
6. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
7. A. breaking &emsp;&emsp; B. building &emsp;&emsp; C. losing &emsp;&emsp; D. finding
8. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. gifts &emsp;&emsp; D. praise
9. A. bring &emsp;&emsp; B. send &emsp;&emsp; C. take &emsp;&emsp; D. put
10. A. present &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. advice &emsp;&emsp; D. knowledge

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他请我教他（如何使用）。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"告诉"；C 项意为"问"；D 项意为"跟随"。爷爷不会用手机，需要"教"，teach 正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我教他如何接听电话。A 项意为"打开"；B 项意为"接听"；C 项意为"接收"；D 项意为"拿起"。answer the phone/calls 为固定搭配，意为"接电话"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当他终于能在屏幕上看到老朋友时，他激动得手都在抖。A 项意为"首先"；B 项意为"曾经；一旦"；C 项意为"终于"；D 项意为"通常"。finally 强调经过学习后成功做到。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时爷爷会忘记步骤，反复问我同样的问题。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"同样的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"容易的"。the same questions 表示"同样的问题"，符合健忘的情境。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会慢慢解释并鼓励他再试一次。A 项意为"鼓励"；B 项意为"强迫"；C 项意为"允许"；D 项意为"邀请"。对老人耐心教导用 encourage。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他非常骄傲，立刻打电话告诉我这个好消息。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"疲倦的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。独立完成视频通话后感到自豪，proud 恰当。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教他不仅仅关乎手机，更是关于增进我们的感情。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"建立；增进"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"找到"。build bond 意为"增进感情/纽带"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：通过这次经历，我学到老年人需要我们的耐心和帮助。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"表扬"。patience and help 并列，表示陪伴与支持。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也意识到科技能把人们联结在一起，无论他们住得多远。A 项意为"带来"；B 项意为"发送"；C 项意为"带去"；D 项意为"放"。bring people together 为固定搭配，意为"把人们团聚在一起"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我认为我们能给祖辈最好的礼物是我们的耐心和时间。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"建议"；D 项意为"知识"。前文强调 patience，此处与 time 并列呼应。故选 B。
