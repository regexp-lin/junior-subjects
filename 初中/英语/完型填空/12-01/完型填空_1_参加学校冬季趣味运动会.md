# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校冬季趣味运动会

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school held a winter fun sports meeting. It was different from the usual sports day because all the events were designed to be ___1___ and creative. Our class was very excited and started preparing a week before.

The first event was the three-legged race. I was paired with my best friend Li Ming. We had to tie our legs together and ___2___ to the finish line. At first, we kept falling down because we could not match our steps. But after a few tries, we found a good ___3___ and moved much faster. In the end, we finished third.

The second event was the balloon relay. Each team member had to carry a balloon between their knees and walk to the next person ___4___ dropping it. It looked easy, but it was actually very hard. Many students dropped their balloons and had to ___5___ over. Our team worked together and cheered for each other loudly.

The most interesting event was the tug-of-war. Our class ___6___ against Class Two, who were the champions last year. Everyone pulled the rope as hard as they could. Our faces turned red and our hands hurt, but nobody wanted to ___7___. In the last moment, we made a big effort and won the match. The whole class jumped up and down with joy.

After the sports meeting, our headteacher praised us for our teamwork and ___8___. She said that winning or losing was not the most important thing. What truly mattered was that we ___9___ the process and supported each other. I agree with her. This winter sports meeting taught me that when people work together with the same ___10___, nothing is impossible.

6. A. talked &emsp;&emsp; B. played &emsp;&emsp; C. competed &emsp;&emsp; D. agreed
7. A. give up &emsp;&emsp; B. show off &emsp;&emsp; C. come back &emsp;&emsp; D. sit down

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：所有项目都设计得有趣且有创意。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"危险的"。趣味运动会强调 fun。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们要绑腿跑到终点线。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"游泳"；D 项意为"飞"。三人两足比赛需要 run to the finish line。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尝试几次后找到了好的节奏。A 项意为"想法"；B 项意为"节奏"；C 项意为"借口"；D 项意为"原因"。配合步伐需要找到 rhythm。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用膝盖夹着气球走到下一个人那里，不能掉落。A 项意为"有"；B 项意为"通过"；C 项意为"不"；D 项意为"关于"。without dropping 表示不掉落。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多同学掉了气球不得不重新开始。A 项 start over 意为"重新开始"；B 项意为"思考"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"转"。start over 符合语境。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班与二班比赛。A 项意为"交谈"；B 项意为"玩"；C 项意为"竞争"；D 项意为"同意"。competed against 意为"与……竞争"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有人想要放弃。A 项意为"放弃"；B 项意为"炫耀"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"坐下"。拔河比赛中坚持不 give up。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任表扬我们的团队合作和精神。A 项意为"沉默"；B 项意为"精神"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"速度"。teamwork and spirit 搭配合理。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：真正重要的是我们享受过程并互相支持。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"讨厌"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"错过"。enjoyed the process 意为"享受过程"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当人们朝着同一个目标共同努力时，没有什么是不可能的。A 项意为"目标"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"疑问"；D 项意为"习惯"。the same goal 表示共同目标。故选 A。
