# 中考英语完形填空：学做雪花剪纸

## 完形填空原文

Paper cutting is a traditional Chinese art. Last weekend, our art teacher taught us how to make snowflake paper cuts. December had just begun, and everyone wanted to ___1___ the classroom for winter.

First, the teacher gave each of us a square piece of white paper. She showed us how to fold it into a triangle shape, and then fold it ___2___ two more times. The folding part was not difficult, but we had to be neat. If the edges were not ___3___, the snowflake would not look good.

Next came the cutting part. The teacher drew some simple patterns on the board and asked us to ___4___ our own designs on the folded paper. I drew small curves and triangles along the edges. Then I carefully cut along the lines with scissors. I had to be very ___5___ because one wrong cut could ruin the whole piece.

When I unfolded my paper, I was amazed. A beautiful six-pointed snowflake ___6___ in my hands. Every student's snowflake was different—some had stars, some had circles, and some had heart shapes. The teacher said that was the ___7___ of paper cutting: no two pieces are exactly the same.

We hung our snowflakes on the windows with tape. When the sunlight came ___8___, the white snowflakes looked like real ones falling from the sky. The whole classroom became warm and beautiful. Some students even made extra snowflakes to give to their ___9___.

I learned that paper cutting needs both patience and creativity. A simple piece of paper can become a work of art if you put your ___10___ into it. I want to learn more paper-cutting skills and make decorations for every season.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家想装饰教室迎接冬天。A 项意为"清洁"；B 项意为"装饰"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"涂色"。decorate the classroom 意为"装饰教室"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：再对折两次。A 项意为"再"；B 项意为"一次"；C 项意为"从不"；D 项意为"很少"。fold it again two more times 表示再折两次。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果边缘不整齐，雪花就不好看。A 项意为"彩色的"；B 项意为"笔直的/整齐的"；C 项意为"厚的"；D 项意为"宽的"。折纸边缘要 straight。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要求我们在折好的纸上画出自己的图案。A 项意为"画"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"抄"。draw designs 意为"画图案"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须非常小心，因为一刀剪错就会毁掉整张纸。A 项意为"快速的"；B 项意为"小心的"；C 项意为"勇敢的"；D 项意为"大声的"。剪纸需要 careful。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：展开后一片美丽的六角雪花出现在手中。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"破裂"；C 项意为"出现"；D 项意为"掉落"。appeared in my hands 表示呈现在手中。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这就是剪纸的美——没有两张是完全相同的。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"美"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"危险"。独一无二体现 beauty。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阳光透过窗户照进来。A 项 come through 意为"透过"；B 项意为"出去"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"越过"。阳光透过窗户用 through。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些同学多做了几个雪花送给父母。A 项意为"父母"；B 项意为"敌人"；C 项意为"陌生人"；D 项意为"医生"。送手工作品给 parents 合理。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要用心，一张简单的纸也能变成艺术品。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"心"；C 项意为"包"；D 项意为"脚"。put your heart into it 意为"用心投入"。故选 B。
