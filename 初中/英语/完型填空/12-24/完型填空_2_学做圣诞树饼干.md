# 中考英语完形填空：学做圣诞树饼干

## 完形填空原文

Christmas was coming and I wanted to do something special. My aunt taught me how to make Christmas tree cookies. I was very ___1___ to learn because they looked so beautiful in the pictures she showed me.

First, we prepared the dough. We mixed flour, butter, sugar and a little ___2___. I helped roll out the dough until it was smooth. Then we used tree-shaped cookie cutters to cut out the ___3___. It was fun to see the green trees appear one by one.

After that, we baked the cookies in the oven for about fifteen minutes. While they were baking, we prepared the icing. We mixed powdered sugar with a few drops of water and green food coloring. The icing looked like ___4___ snow on a tree.

When the cookies cooled down, we decorated them. I used a small brush to spread the green icing on each cookie. Then I added some small candy balls to make them look like Christmas ___5___. My aunt said I did a great job and the cookies looked ___6___.

We put the cookies on a plate and took a photo. Later, we shared them with our neighbors. Everyone said they were ___7___ and asked for the recipe. I felt so proud that I had made something so ___8___ by myself.

Making Christmas tree cookies taught me that holiday joy can come from small things. The best part was not just eating them, but ___9___ time with my aunt and sharing the results with others. I hope to make them again next ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
2. A. salt &emsp;&emsp; B. milk &emsp;&emsp; C. oil &emsp;&emsp; D. juice
3. A. shapes &emsp;&emsp; B. sizes &emsp;&emsp; C. colors &emsp;&emsp; D. flavors
4. A. dirty &emsp;&emsp; B. fresh &emsp;&emsp; C. dark &emsp;&emsp; D. thick
5. A. lights &emsp;&emsp; B. gifts &emsp;&emsp; C. trees &emsp;&emsp; D. stars
6. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. lovely &emsp;&emsp; D. common
7. A. delicious &emsp;&emsp; B. ugly &emsp;&emsp; C. cheap &emsp;&emsp; D. hard
8. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
9. A. losing &emsp;&emsp; B. wasting &emsp;&emsp; C. spending &emsp;&emsp; D. saving
10. A. week &emsp;&emsp; B. month &emsp;&emsp; C. year &emsp;&emsp; D. holiday

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常兴奋想学，因为她给我看的图片太漂亮了。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"担心的"。看到漂亮的饼干想学习，应是 excited。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们混合面粉、黄油、糖和一点盐。A 项意为"盐"；B 项意为"牛奶"；C 项意为"油"；D 项意为"果汁"。做饼干面团常用 flour, butter, sugar, salt 搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们用圣诞树形状的模具切出形状。A 项意为"形状"；B 项意为"尺寸"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"口味"。cookie cutter 切出的是 shapes。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：糖霜看起来像树上的新鲜雪。A 项意为"脏的"；B 项意为"新鲜的"；C 项意为"深的"；D 项意为"厚的"。green food coloring 加白糖霜，像 fresh snow。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我加了些小糖球，让它们看起来像圣诞灯饰。A 项意为"灯"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"树"；D 项意为"星星"。candy balls 装饰在树上常像 Christmas lights。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阿姨说我做得很好，饼干看起来很可爱。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"可怕的"；C 项意为"可爱的"；D 项意为"普通的"。did a great job 对应正面评价，lovely 合适。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都说很好吃，还要了食谱。A 项意为"美味的"；B 项意为"丑的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"硬的"。要食谱说明觉得好吃，delicious 符合。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到很自豪，能亲手做出这么特别的东西。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。自豪源于做出 special 的东西。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最好的部分不仅是吃，而是和阿姨共度时光并与他人分享成果。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"度过"；D 项意为"节省"。spend time with sb 意为"与某人共度时光"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望明年还能再做。A 项意为"周"；B 项意为"月"；C 项意为"年"；D 项意为"假期"。圣诞节每年一次，next year 最自然。故选 C。
