# 中考英语完形填空：科技馆的探秘之旅

## 完形填空原文

During the spring holiday, our school organized a trip to the science museum. I had never been there before, so I was full of ___1___. When we entered the huge building, I was amazed by all the interesting ___2___ around me.

The first exhibit we visited was about space. We could touch a real piece of meteorite and learn about the ___3___. A guide explained how astronauts live and work in space. I listened carefully and asked many questions. The guide smiled and said I had a ___4___ mind.

Then we moved to the robotics section. There were robots that could dance, play chess, and even ___5___ simple conversations. I tried to control one with a remote, but at first I couldn't make it move correctly. After several ___6___, I finally succeeded. My classmates cheered for me.

Before we left, we watched a 3D film about the deep sea. The creatures and the underwater world were so ___7___ that I felt as if I were really diving. When the film ended, I made a ___8___ to myself: I would work hard and maybe one day become a scientist to explore the unknown world.

That day at the museum, I learned that science is not only in textbooks. It is ___9___ us, waiting to be discovered. Every question we ask brings us ___10___ to the truth.

## 选项

1. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. excitement &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. worry
2. A. people &emsp;&emsp; B. exhibits &emsp;&emsp; C. books &emsp;&emsp; D. pictures
3. A. moon &emsp;&emsp; B. stars &emsp;&emsp; C. universe &emsp;&emsp; D. sun
4. A. curious &emsp;&emsp; B. empty &emsp;&emsp; C. closed &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
5. A. have &emsp;&emsp; B. make &emsp;&emsp; C. hold &emsp;&emsp; D. take
6. A. ideas &emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; C. tries &emsp;&emsp; D. plans
7. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. real &emsp;&emsp; C. small &emsp;&emsp; D. dark
8. A. promise &emsp;&emsp; B. wish &emsp;&emsp; C. decision &emsp;&emsp; D. call
9. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. beside &emsp;&emsp; C. around &emsp;&emsp; D. under
10. A. farther &emsp;&emsp; B. closer &emsp;&emsp; C. slower &emsp;&emsp; D. faster

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从未去过那里，所以充满了兴奋。excitement 意为"兴奋"，与从未去过的新鲜感相符。A项"恐惧"；B项"兴奋"；C项"悲伤"；D项"担忧"。excitement 符合春游科技馆的期待心情。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我进入那座巨大的建筑时，周围所有有趣的展品令我惊叹。exhibits 意为"展品"。A项"人们"；B项"展品"；C项"书籍"；D项"图片"。exhibits 与博物馆语境一致。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们可以触摸一块真正的陨石，了解宇宙。universe 意为"宇宙"，与太空展区主题一致。A项"月亮"；B项"星星"；C项"宇宙"；D项"太阳"。universe 涵盖太空知识的整体概念。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：讲解员微笑着说我有一颗好奇的心。curious 意为"好奇的"，与前文"问了许多问题"呼应。A项"好奇的"；B项"空的"；C项"封闭的"；D项"懒惰的"。curious mind 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些机器人能跳舞、下棋，甚至进行简单的对话。have a conversation 为固定搭配。A项"有"；B项"制造"；C项"举行"；D项"拿"。have conversations 表示进行对话。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过几次尝试后，我终于成功了。try 作名词意为"尝试"。A项"想法"；B项"错误"；C项"尝试"；D项"计划"。several tries 与 finally succeeded 形成对比。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些生物和水下世界如此逼真，让我仿佛真的在潜水。real 意为"真实的、逼真的"。A项"无聊的"；B项"逼真的"；C项"小的"；D项"黑暗的"。so real that 表示真实感强。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：电影结束时，我向自己许下了一个承诺。make a promise 为固定搭配。A项"承诺"；B项"愿望"；C项"决定"；D项"电话"。promise to oneself 表示自我承诺。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：科学就在我们身边，等待被发现。around 意为"在……周围"。A项"在……后面"；B项"在……旁边"；C项"在……周围"；D项"在……下面"。around us 表示无处不在。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们提出的每个问题都让我们更接近真理。closer 意为"更接近"。A项"更远"；B项"更接近"；C项"更慢"；D项"更快"。bring us closer to 为常见搭配。故选 B。
