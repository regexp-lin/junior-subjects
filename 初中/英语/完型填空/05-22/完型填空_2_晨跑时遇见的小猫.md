# 中考英语完形填空：晨跑时遇见的小猫

## 完形填空原文

Every morning, I go jogging in the park near my home. One day, as I was running along the path, I heard a weak ___1___ coming from behind some bushes. I stopped and went to look. There I found a small cat. It was thin and dirty, and one of its legs seemed ___2___.

I didn't know what to do. I had never had a pet before. But I couldn't just ___3___ it there. I gently picked up the cat and brought it home. My mother was surprised when she saw it. "We need to take it to the ___4___," she said.

The vet told us that the cat was about two months old and had a minor leg injury. With proper care, it would ___5___ soon. My mother agreed to let me keep the cat until we found its owner. We put up "Found Cat" notices in the neighborhood, but nobody ___6___.

Weeks passed. The cat, which I named Miao Miao, grew stronger and more playful. It would wait for me at the door every morning and follow me around the house. Gradually, our family grew ___7___ to the little creature. When no one came to claim it, we decided to adopt it officially.

That morning run ___8___ my life. It taught me that sometimes we meet those who need help when we least expect it. A small act of kindness can ___9___ a big difference. Now Miao Miao is not just a pet—she is a ___10___ of our family.

## 选项

1. A. song &emsp;&emsp; B. sound &emsp;&emsp; C. music &emsp;&emsp; D. voice
2. A. broken &emsp;&emsp; B. strong &emsp;&emsp; C. healthy &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
3. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. put &emsp;&emsp; C. take &emsp;&emsp; D. bring
4. A. hospital &emsp;&emsp; B. vet &emsp;&emsp; C. school &emsp;&emsp; D. market
5. A. recover &emsp;&emsp; B. disappear &emsp;&emsp; C. fall &emsp;&emsp; D. grow
6. A. called &emsp;&emsp; B. replied &emsp;&emsp; C. wrote &emsp;&emsp; D. came
7. A. used &emsp;&emsp; B. attached &emsp;&emsp; C. similar &emsp;&emsp; D. close
8. A. saved &emsp;&emsp; B. changed &emsp;&emsp; C. improved &emsp;&emsp; D. ended
9. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. get &emsp;&emsp; D. have
10. A. guest &emsp;&emsp; B. friend &emsp;&emsp; C. member &emsp;&emsp; D. helper

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我听到从灌木丛后面传来微弱的声音。voice 常用于动物或人发出的声音。A项"歌曲"；B项"声音"（泛指）；C项"音乐"；D项"嗓音、声音"。小猫的叫声用 voice 更贴切。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它又瘦又脏，一条腿似乎受伤了。broken 意为"折断的、受伤的"。A项"受伤的/折断的"；B项"强壮的"；C项"健康的"；D项"干净的"。后文提到 leg injury，与 broken 呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我不能就这样把它留在那里。leave 意为"留下、遗弃"。A项"离开、留下"；B项"放"；C项"带"；D项"带来"。leave it there 表示置之不理。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们需要带它去看兽医。vet 是 veterinarian 的简写，意为"兽医"。A项"医院"；B项"兽医"；C项"学校"；D项"市场"。受伤的动物应送兽医处。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过妥善照料，它很快就会康复。recover 意为"康复、恢复"。A项"康复"；B项"消失"；C项"摔倒"；D项"成长"。recover from injury 为常用搭配。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在社区张贴了"寻猫启事"，但没有人回应。reply 意为"回复、回应"。A项"打电话"；B项"回复"；C项"写信"；D项"来"。nobody replied 表示无人认领。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词/分词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，我们全家都喜爱上了这个小家伙。grow attached to 意为"对……产生感情、喜爱"。A项"习惯的"；B项"依恋的"；C项"相似的"；D项"亲密的"。attached 强调情感联结。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次晨跑改变了我的生活。change 意为"改变"。A项"拯救"；B项"改变"；C项"改善"；D项"结束"。changed my life 概括全文影响。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小小的善举可以产生很大的影响。make a difference 为固定搭配。A项"制造、产生"；B项"拿"；C项"得到"；D项"有"。make a big difference 意为"产生很大影响"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在喵喵不仅是宠物，她是我们家庭的一员。member 意为"成员"。A项"客人"；B项"朋友"；C项"成员"；D项"助手"。member of the family 表示家人。故选 C。
