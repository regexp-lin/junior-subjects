# 中考英语完形填空：学包饺子

## 完形填空原文

During the Spring Festival, my grandmother taught me how to make dumplings. She has been making them for over fifty years. "Making dumplings together is an important family tradition," she said. "When you grow up, you will ___1___ this skill to your children too."

First, we prepared the filling. We used minced pork, Chinese cabbage, ginger, and green onions. Grandma said the key was to mix everything well and add the right amount of salt and soy sauce. I helped her chop the vegetables. My cuts were not as ___2___ as hers, but she said that practice would make me better.

Then we made the dough. Grandma mixed flour and water until it became a smooth ball. She showed me how to roll the dough into a long snake shape, then cut it into small pieces. I took a piece and tried to roll it into a round wrapper with a rolling pin. My first few wrappers were too thick in the ___3___ and too thin at the edges. Grandma laughed and said, "That's normal for beginners. The centre should be slightly thicker to hold the filling."

The hardest part was folding the dumpling. I put some filling in the centre of the wrapper, then folded it in half and ___4___ the edges together. My first dumplings looked messy. Some had too much filling and burst open when we cooked them. Grandma said, "Don't put too much. It's better to start with less and add more as you get better."

We made over a hundred dumplings. Grandma boiled half of them and fried the other half. When they were ready, the whole family sat down to eat. My father said my dumplings tasted ___5___, even though they looked a bit odd. I felt proud. Grandma put one of my best-looking dumplings on top of the plate. "We always ___6___ the first one to the kitchen god," she explained. "It's an old custom."

Making dumplings with Grandma that day was a special ___7___. I learned not only a skill but also the meaning of family traditions. Food brings people together. When we cook and eat together, we share more than a meal — we share love and ___8___. I hope to make dumplings with my own family one day and pass on the recipes and stories. As Grandma said, some things are too important to ___9___. They connect the past and the future, and they keep our culture ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你长大后也会把这个技能___给孩子们。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"传承"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"隐藏"。"pass on"意为"传承"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我切的没有她的___。A 项意为"锋利的"；B 项意为"均匀的"；C 项意为"快的"；D 项意为"大的"。切菜要均匀，"even"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我前几张皮中间太厚，边缘太薄。A 项意为"中间"；B 项意为"角"；C 项意为"边缘"；D 项意为"边"。"middle"与"edges"相对。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对折后把边缘___在一起。A 项意为"拉"；B 项意为"捏/压"；C 项意为"剪"；D 项意为"扔"。包饺子需捏合边缘，"pressed"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说我包的饺子味道___。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"很好的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"苦的"。父亲鼓励说好吃，"wonderful"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们总是把第一个___灶神。A 项意为"供奉"；B 项意为"给"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"送"。"offer to"意为"供奉给"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天和奶奶包饺子是特别的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"工作"。"memory"意为"回忆"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们分享的不仅是食物，还有爱和___。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"书"。家庭聚餐分享爱和回忆，"memories"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些东西太重要不能___。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"烹饪"。传统不能失传，"lose"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们让我们的文化保持___。A 项意为"秘密的"；B 项意为"鲜活的"；C 项意为"昂贵的"；D 项意为"困难的"。"keep alive"意为"延续下去"。故选 B。
