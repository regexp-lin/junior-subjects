# 中考英语完形填空：参加剪纸兴趣班

## 完形填空原文

Last term, our school started a paper-cutting club. I had always admired Chinese paper-cutting art and decided to join. The teacher was a kind grandmother who had been cutting paper for forty years. She said, "Paper-cutting is not difficult. What you need is a pair of scissors, some red paper, and a little ___1___."

In the first lesson, we learned to cut simple shapes: circles, hearts, and flowers. I thought it would be easy, but my hands did not listen to me. My circles looked like ovals, and my flowers had uneven petals. The teacher smiled and said, "Don't ___2___. Everyone starts this way. Your hands will learn with practice."

We folded the paper before cutting. Folding helped us make ___3___ patterns. A piece of paper folded twice could make a four-petaled flower; folded more times, it could make a snowflake. The teacher showed us how to draw the outline first with a pencil, then cut along the lines. "Never cut without drawing first," she said. "One wrong cut can ___4___ the whole piece."

After a month, I could cut simple patterns quite well. The teacher then taught us the traditional "double happiness" character often used at weddings. It was more complex. I had to cut very carefully around the thin lines. My first attempt broke in two places. I felt ___5___, but the teacher said, "Paper is cheap. Try again." She was right. On my third try, I made a complete "double happiness" that my parents proudly displayed on our wall.

Before the Spring Festival, our club held an exhibition. We displayed our best work in the school hall. Many students and parents came to see. I had made a paper-cutting of a rabbit for the Year of the Rabbit. Someone asked if they could ___6___ it. I felt so honoured. The teacher said that paper-cutting was a gift that could be shared. "When you give your work to others, you share not just paper but culture and ___7___," she said.

The paper-cutting club has become my favourite after-school activity. I find it relaxing to focus on the scissors and paper. In this busy world, we need activities that help us ___8___. Paper-cutting does that. I also learned to appreciate traditional art more. Our ancestors created beautiful things with simple tools. We should not let these ___9___ disappear. I will keep practising and perhaps one day teach others too. As the teacher said, the best way to learn is to ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你需要的是一把剪刀、一些红纸和一点___。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"速度"。剪纸需要耐心，"patience"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要___。A 项意为"担心"；B 项意为"笑"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"哭"。"Don't worry"意为"别担心"，老师鼓励学生。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：折叠帮助我们做出___图案。A 项意为"相似的"；B 项意为"对称的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。对折产生对称图形，"symmetric"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一刀剪错可能___整幅作品。A 项意为"修复"；B 项意为"改善"；C 项意为"毁掉"；D 项意为"保存"。"ruin"意为"毁坏"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一次剪坏了，我感到___。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"失望的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"放松的"。剪坏会失望，"disappointed"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有人问能否___它。A 项意为"购买"；B 项意为"借"；C 项意为"复制"；D 项意为"拿"。有人想购买作品，"buy"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你分享的不仅是纸还有文化和___。A 项意为"纸"；B 项意为"温暖"；C 项意为"剪刀"；D 项意为"时间"。赠送传递温暖，"warmth"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们需要能帮助我们___的活动。A 项意为"匆忙"；B 项意为"放松"；C 项意为"竞争"；D 项意为"争论"。剪纸让人放松，"relax"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不应让这些___消失。A 项意为"技艺"；B 项意为"纸"；C 项意为"剪刀"；D 项意为"书"。传统技艺不能失传，"skills"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说过，最好的学习方式就是___。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"藏"。"教是最好的学"，"teach"正确。故选 A。
