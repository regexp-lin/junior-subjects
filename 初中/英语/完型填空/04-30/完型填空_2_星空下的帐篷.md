# 中考英语完形填空：星空下的帐篷

## 完形填空原文

My dad promised to take me camping for years, but he was always too ___1___ with work. "Next time," he would say. "Next time" never came — until last weekend.

On Saturday morning, Dad put a tent, sleeping bags, and a small cooler into the car. We drove for two hours to a lake in the mountains. The air was fresh, and the water was so ___2___ that we could see the stones at the bottom.

Setting up the tent was harder than we thought. Neither of us had done it before. We read the instructions three times, but the tent still looked like a sad, droopy balloon. We ___3___ for almost an hour before it finally stood up straight.

"Not bad for beginners," Dad said, wiping sweat from his forehead. I laughed. We were both terrible at camping, but it didn't ___4___.

In the afternoon, we tried fishing. Dad was surprisingly ___5___ — he caught nothing for two hours. I, on the other hand, caught a small fish on my first try. Dad's mouth dropped open. "Beginner's luck!" he said. I just grinned.

For dinner, we cooked the fish over a small fire. It was a little burned on the outside and a little raw on the ___6___, but we ate every bite. Everything tasted better outdoors.

As the sun set, the sky turned from orange to purple to ___7___. Then, one by one, the stars appeared. I had never seen so many stars in my life. In the city, the lights were too bright to see them. But here, the sky looked like someone had scattered a million tiny diamonds across a black cloth.

"Dad, this is amazing," I whispered.

Dad lay beside me on the grass, his hands behind his head. "I'm sorry it took me so long to do this," he said ___8___. "I should have made time earlier."

I leaned against his shoulder. "It doesn't matter when," I said. "What matters is that we're here ___9___."

We stayed up late that night, talking about everything — school, his childhood, our favorite movies. I realized that I hadn't talked to my dad like this in a very long ___10___. Sometimes, all you need is a tent, a sky full of stars, and someone you love beside you.

1. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. busy &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. deep &emsp;&emsp; C. clear &emsp;&emsp; D. wide
3. A. slept &emsp;&emsp; B. waited &emsp;&emsp; C. argued &emsp;&emsp; D. tried
4. A. happen &emsp;&emsp; B. matter &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. work
5. A. lucky &emsp;&emsp; B. skillful &emsp;&emsp; C. unlucky &emsp;&emsp; D. excited

7. A. white &emsp;&emsp; B. dark &emsp;&emsp; C. red &emsp;&emsp; D. green
8. A. proudly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. softly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
9. A. still &emsp;&emsp; B. alone &emsp;&emsp; C. now &emsp;&emsp; D. again
10. A. distance &emsp;&emsp; B. way &emsp;&emsp; C. story &emsp;&emsp; D. time

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸答应带我去露营好多年了，但他总是工作太___。A 项意为"仔细的"；B 项意为"忙碌的"；C 项意为"快乐的"；D 项意为"生气的"。爸爸一直说"下次"但从不兑现，因为他总是太"忙"（busy）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：水___得可以看到底部的石头。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"深的"；C 项意为"清澈的"；D 项意为"宽的"。能看到水底的石头，说明水非常"清澈"（clear）。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们___了将近一个小时，帐篷才终于立起来。A 项意为"睡觉"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"争论"；D 项意为"尝试"。搭帐篷不顺利，他们"尝试"（tried）了很久才成功。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们露营都很菜，但这没___。A 项意为"发生"；B 项意为"要紧"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"工作"。"It didn't matter"（没关系）是固定表达，表示虽然做不好但重要的是享受过程。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸出奇地___——他两个小时一条鱼都没钓到。A 项意为"幸运的"；B 项意为"熟练的"；C 项意为"不幸运的"；D 项意为"兴奋的"。两小时什么都没钓到，而"我"一次就钓到了，形成对比，爸爸很"不走运"（unlucky）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：外面有点焦，___有点生。A 项意为"顶部"；B 项意为"外面"；C 项意为"里面"；D 项意为"底部"。前文说"outside"（外面）有点焦，与之对应的是"inside"（里面）有点生，说明烤鱼技术不成熟。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天空从橙色变为紫色，再变为___。A 项意为"白色"；B 项意为"黑暗的"；C 项意为"红色"；D 项意为"绿色"。日落后天色从明变暗，最终变"黑"（dark），然后才能看到星星。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："对不起，我花了这么久才做这件事，"他___说。A 项意为"骄傲地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"轻声地"；D 项意为"大声地"。爸爸在表达歉意和遗憾，语气自然是"轻声地"（softly），流露出温柔和内疚。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："重要的是我们___在这里。"A 项意为"仍然"；B 项意为"独自"；C 项意为"现在"；D 项意为"再次"。不管以前错过了多少次，重要的是"现在"（now）他们在一起。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到我已经很长___没有这样跟爸爸聊天了。A 项意为"距离"；B 项意为"路"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"时间"。"a very long time"（很长时间）是固定表达，说明平时太忙、交流太少。故选 D。
