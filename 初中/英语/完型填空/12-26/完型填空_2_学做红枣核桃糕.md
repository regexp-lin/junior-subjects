# 中考英语完形填空：学做红枣核桃糕

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, I learned to make red date walnut cake from my grandmother. It's a traditional Chinese snack that has been ___1___ in our family for generations.

Grandma told me that the ___2___ step was to prepare the ingredients. We needed red dates, walnuts, glutinous rice flour, and some sugar. She showed me how to ___3___ the dates and remove the pits carefully. Though it took time, Grandma said that being ___4___ was the key to making delicious food.

After preparing the ingredients, we mixed them together. Grandma poured the flour into a big bowl and added hot water ___5___. "We need to knead the dough until it becomes smooth," she explained. I tried my best, but my hands soon felt ___6___. Grandma smiled and said, "Don't give up. Practice makes perfect."

Then we shaped the dough into small cakes and put walnut pieces on top. Grandma placed them in a steamer and we waited for about twenty ___7___. The sweet smell filled the kitchen, and I could not wait to taste our creation.

When the cakes were ready, we sat down and enjoyed them together. The taste was ___8___ — sweet, soft, and full of the love that Grandma had put into teaching me. I realized that learning to cook was not just about the food itself, but also about ___9___ skills and traditions from our elders.

From that day on, I have decided to learn ___10___ traditional recipes from Grandma. I want to keep our family's food culture alive.

## 选项

1. A. forgotten&emsp;&emsp; B. passed down&emsp;&emsp; C. sold&emsp;&emsp; D. invented
2. A. last&emsp;&emsp; B. first&emsp;&emsp; C. same&emsp;&emsp; D. wrong
3. A. wash&emsp;&emsp; B. buy&emsp;&emsp; C. eat&emsp;&emsp; D. throw
4. A. lazy&emsp;&emsp; B. patient&emsp;&emsp; C. angry&emsp;&emsp; D. proud
5. A. slowly&emsp;&emsp; B. quickly&emsp;&emsp; C. loudly&emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
6. A. warm&emsp;&emsp; B. tired&emsp;&emsp; C. cold&emsp;&emsp; D. comfortable
7. A. seconds&emsp;&emsp; B. hours&emsp;&emsp; C. minutes&emsp;&emsp; D. days
8. A. terrible&emsp;&emsp; B. ordinary&emsp;&emsp; C. wonderful&emsp;&emsp; D. strange
9. A. forgetting&emsp;&emsp; B. losing&emsp;&emsp; C. learning&emsp;&emsp; D. avoiding
10. A. fewer&emsp;&emsp; B. less&emsp;&emsp; C. more&emsp;&emsp; D. many

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我们家世代相传的传统中式点心。A 项意为"被遗忘"；B 项意为"被传承/流传下来"；C 项意为"被卖掉"；D 项意为"被发明"。pass down 与 for generations 搭配，表示代代相传，故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶告诉我第一步是准备食材。A 项意为"最后的"；B 项意为"第一的"；C 项意为"相同的"；D 项意为"错误的"。做食物通常从准备食材开始，the first step 符合逻辑，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我怎么清洗红枣并小心地去核。A 项意为"清洗"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"吃"；D 项意为"扔"。做食物前需清洗食材，remove the pits 表示去核，与 wash 搭配，故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然费时，但奶奶说耐心是做出美味食物的关键。A 项意为"懒惰的"；B 项意为"有耐心的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。It took time 暗示需要耐心，故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶把面粉倒进大碗里，慢慢地加入热水。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。和面时需慢慢加水以便控制水量，故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽力了，但双手很快就感到疲劳。A 项意为"温暖的"；B 项意为"疲劳的"；C 项意为"冷的"；D 项意为"舒适的"。揉面是体力活，hands feel tired 合理，故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（时间）
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶把它们放进蒸锅，我们等了大约二十分钟。A 项意为"秒"；B 项意为"小时"；C 项意为"分钟"；D 项意为"天"。蒸糕点通常需要十几到二十分钟，twenty minutes 符合实际，故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道好极了——香甜、柔软，饱含奶奶教我时投入的爱。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"极好的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。sweet, soft, full of love 描述正面，wonderful 与之呼应，故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到学做饭不仅仅是食物本身，也是从长辈那里学习技能和传统。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"学习"；D 项意为"避免"。learning to cook 与 learning skills 形成呼应，故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那天起，我决定向奶奶学习更多传统食谱。A 项意为"更少的"（修饰可数名词）；B 项意为"更少的"（修饰不可数名词）；C 项意为"更多的"；D 项意为"许多"（无比较含义）。recipes 为可数名词，learn more 表示继续学习更多，故选 C。
