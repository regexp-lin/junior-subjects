# 中考英语完形填空：和同学一起练习英语短剧

## 完形填空原文

Our English teacher told us that we would put on a short play for the school English festival. My classmate Li Ming and I were asked to work ___1___ on a funny story about two friends. We were both excited but a little nervous because we had never acted in English before.

Every afternoon after class, we met in the empty classroom to ___2___. First, we read the script many times until we could remember our lines. Li Ming played the role of a lazy cat and I played a busy bee. We had to speak our lines clearly and use different ___3___ to show our feelings. Sometimes we laughed at our own mistakes, but we never gave up.

Our teacher often came to ___4___ us and gave useful advice. She said we should practice in front of a mirror to improve our expressions. We followed her suggestion and found it really helped. The more we practiced, the more ___5___ we became.

On the day of the performance, we put on our simple costumes and waited behind the stage. When it was our turn, we walked onto the stage and bowed to the audience. I took a deep breath and said my first line. To our surprise, everything went well. The audience laughed at the right moments and clapped loudly when we finished. We felt so ___6___ and proud of ourselves.

This experience taught me that teamwork is important. When we ___7___ each other, we can achieve things we never thought possible. I will never forget this wonderful memory and the ___8___ we had together. I hope we can have more ___9___ like this in the ___10___.

3. A. voices &emsp;&emsp; B. books &emsp;&emsp; C. pens &emsp;&emsp; D. chairs
4. A. watch &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. punish &emsp;&emsp; D. forget

6. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. happy
7. A. fight &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. blame &emsp;&emsp; D. ignore
8. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. homework &emsp;&emsp; D. test
9. A. exams &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. chances &emsp;&emsp; D. mistakes

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李明和我被要求一起合作一个关于两个朋友的有趣故事。A 项意为"单独"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"快速地"。合作演短剧用 work together。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天下午放学后，我们在空教室里见面排练。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"玩"；C 项意为"练习"；D 项意为"争吵"。排练短剧用 practice。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们得清晰地说出台词，用不同的声音表达情感。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"钢笔"；D 项意为"椅子"。表演用不同嗓音表达感情。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师常来观看我们排练并给出有用建议。A 项意为"观看"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"惩罚"；D 项意为"忘记"。老师来看排练是 watch。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：练习越多，我们越自信。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"自信的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。the more...the more confident 表示越练越有信心。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们感到非常开心并为自己骄傲。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"开心的"。演出成功后的感受是 happy。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们互相帮助时，能完成意想不到的事。A 项意为"打架"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"责备"；D 项意为"忽视"。团队合作强调 help each other。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记这段美好回忆和我们一起度过的快乐时光。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"作业"；D 项意为"考试"。排练回忆用 fun。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望将来有更多这样的机会。A 项意为"考试"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"机会"；D 项意为"错误"。期待更多类似机会用 chances。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望未来有更多这样的机会。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"未来"；C 项意为"早上"；D 项意为"晚上"。in the future 意为"在未来"。故选 B。
