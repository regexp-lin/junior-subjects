# 中考英语完形填空：校园秋季美食节

## 完形填空原文

Our school held an Autumn Food Festival last Friday. It was one of the most ___1___ events of the year. Every class had a food stand. We sold different kinds of snacks and drinks. My class decided to make pumpkin pies because pumpkins are ___2___ in autumn.

Three days before the festival, we started to prepare. We bought flour, sugar, eggs and pumpkins from the market. On Friday morning, we ___3___ early to set up our stand. Some students put up a sign that said "Best Pumpkin Pies". Others arranged the tables and chairs. I was in charge of ___4___ the money.

At 10 o'clock, the festival began. Many students and teachers came to visit. Our pies sold quickly! We were so busy that we could ___5___ take a rest. By noon, we had sold out all our pies. Everyone said they ___6___ our pies very much.

The principal walked around and tried food from each class. When he came to our stand, he smiled and said, "Well done! You've done a ___7___ job." We felt very proud. After the festival, we counted the money. We had made over 500 yuan. We decided to ___8___ the money to buy books for our class library.

That day we learned that working together could bring great ___9___. We also learned how to make pumpkin pies. It was an ___10___ experience that I will never forget. I hope our school will hold more activities like this next year.

## 选项

1. A. popular &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
2. A. cheap &emsp;&emsp; B. fresh &emsp;&emsp; C. expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. small
3. A. woke up &emsp;&emsp; B. stayed up &emsp;&emsp; C. gave up &emsp;&emsp; D. turned on
4. A. losing &emsp;&emsp; B. wasting &emsp;&emsp; C. borrowing &emsp;&emsp; D. collecting
5. A. hardly &emsp;&emsp; B. easily &emsp;&emsp; C. nearly &emsp;&emsp; D. always
6. A. enjoyed &emsp;&emsp; B. hated &emsp;&emsp; C. refused &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
7. A. bad &emsp;&emsp; B. wrong &emsp;&emsp; C. good &emsp;&emsp; D. poor
8. A. spend &emsp;&emsp; B. save &emsp;&emsp; C. use &emsp;&emsp; D. keep
9. A. problems &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. pain &emsp;&emsp; D. worry
10. A. ordinary &emsp;&emsp; B. special &emsp;&emsp; C. same &emsp;&emsp; D. simple

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是今年最受欢迎的活动之一。A 项意为"受欢迎的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。美食节是学生喜爱的校园活动，popular 符合语境。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为南瓜在秋天很新鲜。A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"新鲜的"；C 项意为"贵的"；D 项意为"小的"。秋季是南瓜丰收季节，fresh 表示当季新鲜。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：周五早上我们早早起床布置摊位。A 项意为"醒来、起床"；B 项意为"熬夜"；C 项意为"放弃"；D 项意为"打开"。woke up early 指早起准备活动。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我负责收钱。A 项意为"丢失"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"借"；D 项意为"收集、收取"。卖东西时收钱用 collect the money。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们太忙了，几乎没法休息。A 项意为"几乎不"；B 项意为"容易地"；C 项意为"几乎、将近"；D 项意为"总是"。could hardly take a rest 表示忙得几乎没有休息时间。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都说非常喜欢我们的派。A 项意为"享受、喜欢"；B 项意为"讨厌"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"忘记"。enjoy something very much 表示非常喜欢。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们做得很好。A 项意为"坏的"；B 项意为"错误的"；C 项意为"好的"；D 项意为"差的"。principal 表扬学生，do a good job 为常用表达。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定用这笔钱给班级图书馆买书。A 项意为"花费"；B 项意为"节省"；C 项意为"使用"；D 项意为"保持"。use the money to do something 表示用钱做某事。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学到了合作能带来巨大的快乐。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"痛苦"；D 项意为"担忧"。合作完成活动带来的是 joy。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是一次我永远不会忘记的特殊经历。A 项意为"普通的"；B 项意为"特殊的"；C 项意为"相同的"；D 项意为"简单的"。值得铭记的经历用 special 形容。故选 B。
