# 中考英语完形填空：帮妹妹辅导功课

## 完形填空原文

My younger sister is in primary school. She often has difficulty with her maths homework. Last month, my mother asked me to help her. "You are good at maths," she said. "Can you spend some time ___1___ your sister?" I agreed, though I was not sure if I could be a good teacher.

The first evening, my sister showed me her maths book. The topic was fractions. I had learned it years ago, but I had to ___2___ the rules before I could explain them. I found my old notebooks and reviewed quickly. Then I sat down with my sister and started to teach.

I tried to explain in a simple way. Instead of using difficult words, I used examples from daily life. For example, I said, "If you cut an apple into four equal parts and eat one, you have eaten one ___3___." My sister nodded and seemed to understand. But when I gave her a similar problem, she made mistakes again. I felt a little ___4___, but I told myself to be patient.

I learned that my sister needed more practice. I made some extra exercises for her and checked her answers every day. When she got a problem ___5___, I would praise her. When she was wrong, I would explain the steps again slowly. Gradually, her test scores improved. She got 85 points on the last maths test, up from 70 before. I was so happy for her.

Helping my sister also helped me. Going over primary school maths ___6___ my own knowledge. I understood the topics more deeply when I had to teach them. My sister and I also became ___7___. We used to argue about silly things, but now we work together and respect each other more.

My mother said that teaching is the best way to learn. I think she is ___8___. When you explain something to others, you have to organise your thoughts clearly. That process makes you learn better. I will ___9___ helping my sister until she no longer needs it. Maybe one day she will help someone else too. That is how knowledge gets ___10___.

7. A. stranger &emsp;&emsp; B. closer &emsp;&emsp; C. older &emsp;&emsp; D. busier

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你能花点时间___你妹妹吗？A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"看"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"打电话"。帮助功课即教导，"teaching"正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我得先___规则才能解释。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记起"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"写"。多年前学的需先回忆，"remember"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你把苹果切成四等份吃一份，你就吃了四___之一。A 项意为"半"；B 项意为"四分之一"；C 项意为"整个"；D 项意为"块"。"one quarter"意为四分之一。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹又做错时我有点___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"失望的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。教了还错会失望，"disappointed"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当她做___一道题时，我会表扬她。A 项意为"错的"；B 项意为"对的"；C 项意为"难的"；D 项意为"简单的"。做对才表扬，"right"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：复习小学数学___了我自己的知识。A 项意为"减少"；B 项意为"刷新/巩固"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"浪费"。温故知新，"refreshed"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和妹妹也变得更___了。A 项意为"更陌生"；B 项意为"更亲密"；C 项意为"更老"；D 项意为"更忙"。一起学习增进感情，"closer"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得她说的___。A 项意为"错的"；B 项意为"对的"；C 项意为"晚的"；D 项意为"早的"。认可母亲观点，"right"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会___帮助妹妹直到她不再需要。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"完成"。"continue"符合坚持辅导之意。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：知识就是这样被___的。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"失去"。教与学即知识传递分享，"shared"正确。故选 B。
