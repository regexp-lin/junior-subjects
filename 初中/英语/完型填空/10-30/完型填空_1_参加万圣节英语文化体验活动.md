# 中考英语完形填空：参加万圣节英语文化体验活动

## 完形填空原文

Last week, our school organized a Halloween English culture activity. I had never ___1___ such an event before, so I was very curious about it.

On that afternoon, the English teachers decorated our classroom with pumpkins, spider webs, and orange balloons. They told us the ___2___ for Halloween and why people dress up in costumes. It was interesting to learn that Halloween comes from an old Western tradition.

Then we played some games. One of them was "Trick or Treat" in English. We had to knock on the "door" and ___3___ "Trick or treat!" in a loud voice. At first, I felt a little shy, but when my deskmate encouraged me, I ___4___ to try. It was actually quite exciting!

After the games, we made paper masks. I chose to make a cat face. I drew two big eyes and cut them out ___5___. When I put on my mask, everyone said it looked funny. We took many photos together.

Before we left, our teacher gave each of us some candy. She said it was a Halloween ___6___. I felt happy not only because of the candy but also because I had learned something new about English culture. I found that learning English could be ___7___ when we connected it with real-life activities.

This experience made me want to ___8___ more about Western festivals. I hope our school will hold more activities like this in the ___9___. It was a wonderful way to practice English and have ___10___ at the same time.

## 选项

1. A. joined &emsp;&emsp; B. missed &emsp;&emsp; C. seen &emsp;&emsp; D. heard
2. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. rule
3. A. speak &emsp;&emsp; B. say &emsp;&emsp; C. tell &emsp;&emsp; D. talk
4. A. agreed &emsp;&emsp; B. decided &emsp;&emsp; C. seemed &emsp;&emsp; D. happened
5. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
6. A. habit &emsp;&emsp; B. gift &emsp;&emsp; C. prize &emsp;&emsp; D. task
7. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. fun
8. A. know &emsp;&emsp; B. learn &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. write
9. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. future &emsp;&emsp; C. present &emsp;&emsp; D. end
10. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. work

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未参加过这样的活动，所以非常好奇。A 项意为"参加"；B 项意为"错过"；C 项意为"看见"；D 项意为"听说"。从未 joined 过万圣节活动。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们给我们讲了万圣节的原因以及人们为什么穿 costumes。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"课"；C 项意为"原因"；D 项意为"规则"。the reason for Halloween 指庆祝万圣节的原因。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（say/speak/tell/talk）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们得敲门并大声说"Trick or treat!"。A 项意为"说（语言）"；B 项意为"说（内容）"；C 项意为"告诉"；D 项意为"谈论"。say 后接具体说的话。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初有点害羞，但同桌鼓励我时，我同意试一试。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"似乎"；D 项意为"发生"。agreed to try 意为"同意尝试"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我画了两只大眼睛并慢慢地剪出来。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"仔细地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。初学者剪纸时往往 slowly 操作。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说这是万圣节礼物。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"奖品"；D 项意为"任务"。糖果作为 Halloween gift。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现把英语和实际活动结合时，学英语可以很有趣。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"容易的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。结合实际活动让学习 fun。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次经历让我想阅读了解更多西方节日。A 项意为"知道"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"写"。read more about 意为"阅读更多关于"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望学校将来能举办更多这样的活动。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"将来"；C 项意为"现在"；D 项意为"结束"。in the future 意为"将来"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是练习英语和同时享受乐趣的好方式。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"工作"。have fun 意为"玩得开心"。故选 B。
