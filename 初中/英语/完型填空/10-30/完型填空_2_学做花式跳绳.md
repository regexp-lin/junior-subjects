# 中考英语完形填空：学做花式跳绳

## 完形填空原文

During the last sports meeting, I saw some students doing fancy rope skipping. They jumped so well that I couldn't help watching. I ___1___ for a moment, then decided to learn it myself.

The next day, I bought a skipping rope and started to practice. At first, I could only do basic jumps. Every time I tried to do a ___2___ jump, I would trip over the rope and fall. My legs often hurt, and I felt like giving up. But I remembered the cool moves I had seen, so I ___3___ practicing.

My PE teacher noticed my interest and offered to help me. She said, "Fancy rope skipping needs good timing. You must ___4___ when to jump and when to swing the rope." She showed me step by step. First, I learned to swing the rope with one hand. Then I practiced jumping with both feet together. ___5___ , I could do a few simple tricks.

Week by week, I made progress. I learned to cross my arms and jump, and then I learned to jump on one foot. Each small success made me feel ___6___. One afternoon, I finally did a double jump! I was so happy that I ___7___ to tell my parents when I got home.

Now I can do many fancy moves. Rope skipping has become my favorite ___8___ after class. It not only keeps me healthy but also brings me joy. I have even joined the school rope skipping team. We will take part in a competition next month. I am a little nervous, but I believe that with ___9___ practice, we can do well.

If you want to learn something new, don't be afraid of ___10___. Keep trying and you will succeed one day.

## 选项

1. A. refused &emsp;&emsp; B. decided &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. hesitated
2. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. special &emsp;&emsp; C. normal &emsp;&emsp; D. usual
3. A. finished &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoyed &emsp;&emsp; C. kept &emsp;&emsp; D. minded
4. A. know &emsp;&emsp; B. guess &emsp;&emsp; C. wonder &emsp;&emsp; D. imagine
5. A. Suddenly &emsp;&emsp; B. Finally &emsp;&emsp; C. Quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. Gradually
6. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
7. A. waited &emsp;&emsp; B. remembered &emsp;&emsp; C. couldn't wait &emsp;&emsp; D. had to
8. A. subject &emsp;&emsp; B. activity &emsp;&emsp; C. hobby &emsp;&emsp; D. job
9. A. less &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. few &emsp;&emsp; D. little
10. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. failure &emsp;&emsp; D. time

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我犹豫了片刻，然后决定去学。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"犹豫"。hesitate for a moment 意为"犹豫片刻"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次尝试做特殊跳跃时，都会被绳子绊倒。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"特殊的"；C 项意为"正常的"；D 项意为"通常的"。fancy 对应 special jump。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我想起看到的酷动作，所以继续练习。A 项意为"完成"；B 项意为"享受"；C 项意为"保持/继续"；D 项意为"介意"。kept practicing 意为"继续练习"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你必须知道何时跳、何时摇绳。A 项意为"知道"；B 项意为"猜测"；C 项意为"想知道"；D 项意为"想象"。know when 表掌握时机。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，我能做几个简单的花式了。A 项意为"突然"；B 项意为"最终"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"渐渐地"。Gradually 表示经过逐步练习后掌握技巧。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一点小进步都让我感到自豪。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。取得进步后感到 proud。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太开心了，回到家迫不及待地想告诉父母。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"迫不及待"；D 项意为"不得不"。couldn't wait to tell 表急切分享。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：跳绳已成为课后我最喜欢的爱好。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"爱好"；D 项意为"工作"。favorite hobby 意为"最喜欢的爱好"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信通过更多练习，我们能表现好。A 项意为"更少"；B 项意为"更多"；C 项意为"很少"；D 项意为"一点"。with more practice 表多加练习。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你想学新事物，不要害怕失败。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"时间"。don't be afraid of failure 意为"不要怕失败"。故选 C。
