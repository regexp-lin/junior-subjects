# 中考英语完形填空：学习急救知识

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school invited a nurse from the local hospital to teach us first aid. She said that everyone should learn basic first aid because you never know when you might need it. I was ___1___ to learn something that could help save lives.

The nurse started with how to help someone who is choking. She used a dummy to demonstrate. "First, stand behind the person and make a fist with one hand. Place it above their belly button, then use your other hand to pull ___2___ quickly," she said. We took turns practising on the dummy. My first try was too gentle. The nurse said we needed to use more force to be effective.

Next, we learned about cuts and bleeding. The nurse said that for most small cuts, we should ___3___ the wound with a clean cloth and apply pressure for several minutes. "Never use your bare hands if you can avoid it," she warned. "Use gloves or a clean towel to protect yourself from germs." We also learned that we should raise the injured part above the heart if possible, as this helps ___4___ the blood flow.

We also learned CPR for someone whose heart has stopped. The nurse said that in such an emergency, every second ___5___. We should call for help first, then start chest compressions. She showed us the correct position and rhythm. "Push hard and fast, about 100 to 120 times per minute," she said. It was tiring to practise, but the nurse said that doing CPR correctly could double or triple the person's chance of ___6___.

At the end of the class, the nurse gave us a booklet with first aid tips. She said, "I hope you never need to use these skills, but if you do, you will be ___7___ that you learned them." I put the booklet in my school bag. My mother was glad when I told her about the class. She said our family should have a first aid kit at home. I offered to help her ___8___ one.

Learning first aid made me feel more responsible. I used to think that helping in emergencies was only for doctors and nurses. Now I know that ordinary people can make a real ___9___ if they act quickly and correctly. I will review the booklet from time to time to keep the knowledge ___10___ in my mind. You never know when you might need it.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___学习能救人命的东西。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"渴望的"；C 项意为"懒惰的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。"eager to learn"意为"渴望学习"，符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用另一只手快速向___拉。A 项意为"向前"；B 项意为"向后"；C 项意为"向上"；D 项意为"向下"。海姆立克法需向上用力，"upward"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对于大多数小伤口，我们应用干净布___伤口并按压几分钟。A 项意为"覆盖/捂住"；B 项意为"打开"；C 项意为"清洁"；D 项意为"触摸"。止血需捂住伤口加压，"cover"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：抬高受伤部位有助于___血流。A 项意为"增加"；B 项意为"减少"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为" warming"。抬高伤肢可减少出血，"reduce"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在这种紧急情况下，每一秒都___。A 项意为"重要"；B 项意为"花费"；C 项意为"经过"；D 项意为"节省"。"every second matters"意为"分秒必争"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：正确做CPR可使人生存___提高两到三倍。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"生存"；C 项意为"康复"；D 项意为"幸福"。"chance of survival"意为"生存机会"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果需要用到，你会___学过这些技能。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"抱歉的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"生气的"。掌握急救技能会用上时会庆幸，"glad"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我主动帮妈妈___一个急救箱。A 项意为"购买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"制作"；D 项意为"借"。准备家用急救箱通常需要购买，"buy"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：普通人若快速正确地行动能产生真正的___。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"噪音"；C 项意为"影响"；D 项意为"问题"。"make a real difference"意为"产生真正影响"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会不时复习手册以保持知识在脑海中的___。A 项意为"新鲜的"；B 项意为"旧的"；C 项意为"秘密的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。"keep fresh"意为"保持清晰/不忘"。故选 A。
