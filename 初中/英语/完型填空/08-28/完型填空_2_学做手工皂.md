# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工皂

## 完形填空原文

My aunt loves making things by hand. Last summer she taught me how to make soap at home. I had never thought that soap could be made ___1___ a kitchen. She said we only needed a few simple ingredients: oils, lye, and water.

First, we had to be very careful. Lye can burn the skin, so we wore gloves and safety goggles. My aunt measured the lye and added it slowly to cold water. She told me never to add water to lye, only lye to water. "The order ___2___," she said. I watched carefully and took notes.

We used olive oil and coconut oil for our soap. My aunt said different oils give different benefits. Olive oil is gentle for the skin, and coconut oil makes the soap bubbly. We heated the oils in a pot until they were warm. Then we ___3___ the lye water into the oils and stirred slowly. The mixture had to be stirred for about twenty minutes until it became thick like pudding. My arm was tired, but I did not want to stop.

Next, we added some lavender essential oil for a nice ___4___. We could have added dried flowers or herbs too, but we kept it simple for the first time. We poured the mixture into a mould and covered it with a towel. The soap needed to "rest" for 24 to 48 hours before we could take it out.

Two days later, we removed the soap from the mould. It was soft and needed to ___5___ for another four to six weeks. My aunt said this waiting period was called "curing." During this time, the soap became harder and milder. Fresh soap right after making can be too strong for the skin.

When the soap was finally ready, I gave some to my friends. They all said it felt ___6___ on their skin and smelled wonderful. I felt proud that I had made something useful with my own hands. Making soap also made me think about what we buy. Many products in shops have chemicals we cannot ___7___. Making our own lets us choose natural ingredients.

I have made several batches of soap since then. Each time I try different scents: lemon, mint, or rose. My aunt says I am getting better. She says the ___8___ to good soap is patience — patience when stirring, patience when waiting. I agree. Good things take time. Maybe one day I will make soap as gifts for the whole ___9___. For now, I enjoy the quiet satisfaction of creating something ___10___ from simple materials.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未想过肥皂能在厨房___制作。A 项意为"在……里"；B 项意为"在……外"；C 项意为"在……下"；D 项意为"在……后"。"in a kitchen"意为"在厨房里"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：顺序___。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"重要"；C 项意为"花费"；D 项意为"改变"。"The order matters"意为"顺序很重要"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把碱液水___到油里。A 项意为"倒入"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"滴落"；D 项意为"藏"。"poured"意为"倒入"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们加了薰衣草精油以获得宜人的___。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"香味"；C 项意为"形状"；D 项意为"尺寸"。精油增添香味，"smell"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：皂体还软，需要再___四到六周。A 项意为"晾干/固化"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"冷却"；D 项意为"加热"。皂的熟成 curing 即晾干固化，"dry"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们都说在皮肤上感觉___，闻起来很棒。A 项意为"粗糙"；B 项意为"柔软"；C 项意为"硬"；D 项意为"冷"。手工皂触感柔和，"soft"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多产品含有我们无法___的化学成分。A 项意为"发音"；B 项意为"理解"；C 项意为"记住"；D 项意为"看见"。化学名词难懂，"understand"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
