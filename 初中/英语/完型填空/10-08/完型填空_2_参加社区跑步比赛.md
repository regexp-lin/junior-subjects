# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区跑步比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, our community held a running competition. I had been practicing for two weeks, and I was ___1___ to take part in it.

The race started at eight in the morning. About fifty runners gathered at the starting line. When the whistle blew, everyone ___2___ forward. I ran as fast as I could, but many people passed me. I began to feel tired after the first kilometer.

"My legs hurt. Should I ___3___?" I thought. Then I remembered my father's words: "Never give up. Finish what you start." I took a deep breath and kept running.

Along the way, some volunteers handed out bottles of water. I drank a little and ___4___ running. The sun was getting hotter, and sweat ran down my face. But I could see the finish line in the distance.

When I finally crossed the finish line, I was out of breath. I didn't win a prize — the ___5___ three runners got medals. But I didn't care. I had completed the 3-kilometer race, and that made me feel ___6___.

My mother was waiting at the finish line with a bottle of water and a towel. She said, "I'm ___7___ of you! You didn't stop even when it was hard." Her words made me smile.

The community organized this event to encourage people to ___8___ and stay healthy. Many families joined in — fathers, mothers, and even grandparents. It was a wonderful ___9___ to spend a Sunday morning.

I have decided to run every weekend from now on. Maybe next year I can run ___10___ and win a medal!

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. ready
2. A. walked &emsp;&emsp; B. jumped &emsp;&emsp; C. rushed &emsp;&emsp; D. stepped
3. A. continue &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. rest &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
4. A. stopped &emsp;&emsp; B. kept &emsp;&emsp; C. finished &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoyed
5. A. last &emsp;&emsp; B. next &emsp;&emsp; C. first &emsp;&emsp; D. only
6. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. sorry &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
7. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. ashamed
8. A. sleep &emsp;&emsp; B. eat &emsp;&emsp; C. exercise &emsp;&emsp; D. study
9. A. way &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. question &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
10. A. slower &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. earlier

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我练了两周，准备好参加了。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"准备好的"。be ready to 意为"准备好做某事"。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：哨声一响，大家都冲了出去。A 项意为"走"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"冲、奔"；D 项意为"迈步"。rushed forward 形容起跑时的动作。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我腿疼，要不要停下？A 项意为"继续"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"等待"。与后文"Never give up"形成对比，此处是考虑是否 stop。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我喝了一点水，继续跑。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"保持、继续"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"享受"。keep doing 意为"继续做某事"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 序数词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：前三名选手获得了奖牌。A 项意为"最后的"；B 项意为"下一个"；C 项意为"第一的"；D 项意为"唯一的"。the first three 意为"前三名"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我完成了3公里赛跑，这让我感到很自豪。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"抱歉的"；D 项意为"生气的"。feel proud 意为"感到自豪"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说你真棒！再难你都没停。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"羞愧的"。be proud of 意为"以……为荣"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：社区组织活动是为了鼓励人们锻炼、保持健康。A 项意为"睡觉"；B 项意为"吃"；C 项意为"锻炼"；D 项意为"学习"。encourage to exercise 符合跑步比赛的主题。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是度过周日早晨的好方式。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"错误"。a way to spend 意为"度过……的方式"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许明年我能跑得更快，赢得奖牌。A 项意为"更慢"；B 项意为"更快"；C 项意为"更晚"；D 项意为"更早"。run faster 才能 win a medal。故选 B。
