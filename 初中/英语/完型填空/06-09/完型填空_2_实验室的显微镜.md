# 中考英语完形填空：实验室的显微镜

## 完形填空原文

The science lab was my favourite place at school. I loved going there every Friday afternoon for our biology class. What made me most excited was the microscope in the corner. Through it, we could see a whole new world that our eyes could not ___1___ alone.

Our teacher, Mr. Chen, taught us how to use the microscope. First, we had to ___2___ a small piece of onion skin on a glass slide. Then we added a drop of water and put another piece of glass on top. "Be careful," Mr. Chen said. "Don't press too ___3___, or you will break it." We all nodded and did our best.

When I looked through the microscope for the first time, I was ___4___. I could see tiny cells clearly! They looked like small boxes side by side. I had read about them in books, but seeing them with my own eyes was completely ___5___. "Science is not just in books," Mr. Chen said. "It is all around us, waiting to be discovered."

One day, my classmate Xiao Mei forgot to bring her slide. She looked ___6___. I offered to share mine with her. "We can take turns," I said. She smiled and thanked me. Working together made the lesson more ___7___. We discussed what we saw and learned from each other.

That microscope opened my eyes to the wonders of science. It taught me to be ___8___ and to look at things more carefully. Now I understand why scientists love their work—there is always something new to ___9___. I hope one day I can have my own microscope and ___10___ exploring the small world around us.

## 选项

1. A. see &emsp;&emsp; B. find &emsp;&emsp; C. watch &emsp;&emsp; D. notice
2. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. put &emsp;&emsp; C. lay &emsp;&emsp; D. set
3. A. high &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. heavy
4. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. amazed &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
5. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. easy
6. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. upset &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
7. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. short &emsp;&emsp; C. fun &emsp;&emsp; D. hard
8. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. patient &emsp;&emsp; C. brave &emsp;&emsp; D. strict
9. A. learn &emsp;&emsp; B. read &emsp;&emsp; C. write &emsp;&emsp; D. remember
10. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. finish &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：透过它，我们能看见肉眼单独无法___的全新世界。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"找到"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"注意到"。"see alone"指仅凭肉眼看见，显微镜帮助我们看到肉眼看不到的微观世界。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我们得把一小片洋葱皮___在玻璃载玻片上。A 项意为"放置"（多指较正式）；B 项意为"放、搁"；C 项意为"铺设"；D 项意为"设置"。"put...on a slide"为常用搭配，意为"把……放在载玻片上"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要太用力___按，否则会弄碎。A 项意为"高"；B 项意为"用力地、狠狠地"；C 项意为"快地"；D 项意为"重地"。"press too hard"指按压过猛，与"break"相呼应。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我第一次透过显微镜看时，我___了。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"惊叹的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。第一次看到细胞时的惊奇用"amazed"最恰当。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在书上看过和亲眼所见完全是___的。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"容易的"。"completely different"强调亲眼观察与读书的体验截然不同。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小梅忘了带载玻片，她看起来___。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"沮丧的、难过的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。忘带材料自然感到沮丧，"upset"恰当。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一起合作让这节课更加___。A 项意为"长的"；B 项意为"短的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"困难的"。"more fun"指合作让课堂更有趣，与"Working together"及"discussed"相呼应。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会我要___，更仔细地观察事物。A 项意为"迅速的"；B 项意为"有耐心的"；C 项意为"勇敢的"；D 项意为"严格的"。"be patient"与"look at things more carefully"相呼应，科学观察需要耐心。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：总有一些新的东西可以___。A 项意为"学习、了解"；B 项意为"阅读"；C 项意为"写作"；D 项意为"记忆"。"something new to learn"为常见表达，意为"有待学习的新事物"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望有一天能有自己的显微镜，___探索身边的小世界。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"避免"。"keep exploring"为固定搭配，意为"持续探索"，与"continue"同义。故选 B。
