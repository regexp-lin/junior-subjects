# 中考英语完形填空：课间操的领操员

## 完形填空原文

Every morning at 9:30, the music would start and all students would run to the playground. It was time for our morning exercises. Standing in front of everyone was Zhang Lei, our class monitor. He was the one who ___1___ the exercises and we all followed him.

At first, Zhang Lei was very nervous. He often made small ___2___ when he moved. But he never gave up. He practised at home every evening so that he could do better the next day. His hard work ___3___ us all. Slowly, he became more confident and his movements became smooth.

One cold winter morning, Zhang Lei had a bad cold. The teacher asked him to rest, but he ___4___ to come. "The class needs me," he said. "If I don't show up, who will lead?" He stood in the front as usual, though his voice was a bit weak. We were all ___5___ by his sense of responsibility.

Through leading the morning exercises, Zhang Lei learned an important ___6___: being a leader means more than just standing in the front. It means setting a good example and never letting others down. He also made many friends because students often came to him for ___7___. He was always patient and willing to help.

Now Zhang Lei has graduated, but we still remember him. Every time we hear the morning exercise music, we think of the boy who stood in the front with a ___8___ on his face. He taught us that with confidence and practice, anyone can ___9___ a role in making things better. The playground and that memory will always ___10___ special to us.

1. A. led &emsp;&emsp; B. watched &emsp;&emsp; C. joined &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoyed
2. A. changes &emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; C. choices &emsp;&emsp; D. plans
3. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. touched &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
4. A. agreed &emsp;&emsp; B. refused &emsp;&emsp; C. decided &emsp;&emsp; D. failed
5. A. moved &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. interested
6. A. rule &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. idea &emsp;&emsp; D. story
7. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. advice
8. A. smile &emsp;&emsp; B. tear &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
9. A. play &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. make &emsp;&emsp; D. get
10. A. feel &emsp;&emsp; B. become &emsp;&emsp; C. stay &emsp;&emsp; D. remain

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他是___课间操的人，我们都跟着他做。A 项意为"带领、引导"；B 项意为"观看"；C 项意为"加入"；D 项意为"享受"。"led the exercises"指领操，与后文"we all followed him"相呼应。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他做动作时经常犯小的___。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"计划"。"made small mistakes"指犯小错误，与"nervous"及"never gave up"语境相符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的努力___了我们所有人。A 项意为"使惊讶"；B 项意为"感动"；C 项意为"使担忧"；D 项意为"使无聊"。"touched us"指他的坚持感动了大家。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让他休息，但他___要来。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"拒绝"；C 项意为"决定"；D 项意为"失败"。"decided to come"指他决定坚持到场，体现责任感。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（被动语态）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都___于他的责任感。A 项意为"感动"；B 项意为"兴奋"；C 项意为"放松"；D 项意为"感兴趣"。"were moved by"意为"被……感动"，与"sense of responsibility"相呼应。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：通过领操，张磊学到了一个重要的___。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"教训、启示"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"故事"。"learned an important lesson"为常见搭配，意为"学到重要的一课"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们经常来找他寻求___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"建议"。"come to sb. for advice"意为"向某人请教、征求意见"。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们想起那个站在最前面、脸上带着___的男孩。A 项意为"微笑"；B 项意为"眼泪"；C 项意为"担忧"；D 项意为"惊讶"。"a smile on his face"与积极阳光的领操员形象相符。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他让我们明白，只要有信心和练习，任何人都能___角色让事情变好。A 项意为"扮演"；B 项意为"承担"；C 项意为"制造"；D 项意为"得到"。"play a role"为固定搭配，意为"扮演角色、发挥作用"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：操场和那段记忆将永远对我们来说___特别。A 项意为"感觉"；B 项意为"变得"；C 项意为"停留"；D 项意为"保持、继续"。"remain special"意为"保持特别"，常见于"remain + adj."结构。故选 D。
