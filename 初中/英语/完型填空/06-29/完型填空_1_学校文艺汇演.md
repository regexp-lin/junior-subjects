# 中考英语完形填空：学校文艺汇演

## 完形填空原文

The school art show was the most exciting event of our semester. When our teacher asked who wanted to take part, I raised my hand ___1___ thinking. I loved singing and had always dreamed of standing on the stage. However, when the day came closer, I became more and more ___2___. What if I forgot the words? What if people laughed at me?

My best friend Tina saw that I was worried. She told me that everyone feels nervous before a performance. "Just practice more and ___3___ in yourself," she said. Her words made me feel better. I practiced my song every day after school. Sometimes I sang in front of the mirror to see how I looked. I also asked my family to listen and give me ___4___.

The night before the show, I could hardly sleep. I kept ___5___ about the next day. When I got to school, I saw many students in colorful costumes. The hall was full of parents and teachers. My hands were shaking when it was my ___6___ to go on stage. I took a deep breath and walked out. The bright lights made me blink, but then I started to sing. To my surprise, I remembered every word. The audience was quiet and listened carefully. When I finished, everyone clapped loudly. I felt so ___7___ that I smiled from ear to ear.

That experience taught me an important lesson: fear is natural, but we should not let it ___8___ us from trying. If we prepare well and believe in ourselves, we can do better than we think. Now I look forward to next year's art show. I might try something new, like playing a musical ___9___ or joining a dance group. The stage is not as scary as it seems. It is a place where we can ___10___ our talents and share our joy with others.

2. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
3. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. look &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
4. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. advice &emsp;&emsp; C. presents &emsp;&emsp; D. food
5. A. talking &emsp;&emsp; B. thinking &emsp;&emsp; C. reading &emsp;&emsp; D. writing
6. A. way &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. idea &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
7. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
8. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. save &emsp;&emsp; C. bring &emsp;&emsp; D. send
9. A. book &emsp;&emsp; B. game &emsp;&emsp; C. instrument &emsp;&emsp; D. sport
10. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. show &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. forget

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：当老师问谁想参加时，我___思考就举起了手。A 项意为"没有"；B 项意为"带着"；C 项意为"为了"；D 项意为"关于"。"without thinking"为固定搭配，意为"不假思索地"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但随着日子临近，我越来越___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"放松的"。后文提到担心忘词、被嘲笑，可见是紧张，"nervous"符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：多练习，并___自己。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"等待"。"believe in yourself"意为"相信自己"，是常用鼓励语。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我请家人听我唱并给我___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"食物"。"give advice"意为"给予建议"，排练时请家人提意见最合理。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直在___第二天。A 项意为"谈论"；B 项意为"想着"；C 项意为"阅读"；D 项意为"写"。"keep thinking about"意为"不停地想"，失眠时反复想明天的事。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我上台的___时，我的手在发抖。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"时间、时候"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"问题"。"one's time to do"意为"轮到某人做某事"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表演成功后我感到非常___，笑得合不拢嘴。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"生气的"。成功演出后自然感到自豪，"proud"符合成就感。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：恐惧是自然的，但我们不应让它___我们尝试。A 项意为"阻止"；B 项意为"拯救"；C 项意为"带来"；D 项意为"寄送"。"stop sb. from doing"意为"阻止某人做某事"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我可能尝试新的东西，比如演奏某种___或加入舞蹈队。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"乐器"；D 项意为"运动"。"musical instrument"意为"乐器"，与演奏呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：舞台是我们可以___才华并与他人分享快乐的地方。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"展示"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"忘记"。"show one's talents"意为"展示才华"，符合舞台表演场景。故选 B。
