# 中考英语完形填空：学做热可可

## 完形填空原文

Winter is the perfect season for a cup of hot cocoa. Last Saturday, my older sister taught me how to make it at home. She said it was much ___1___ than the instant cocoa you buy in stores.

First, she asked me to get the ingredients: cocoa powder, milk, sugar, and a little vanilla extract. She said the ___2___ of a good cup of hot cocoa is using real cocoa powder instead of the pre-mixed kind. I measured two tablespoons of cocoa powder and one tablespoon of sugar, and put them into a small pot.

Then my sister told me to add a little milk to the pot and stir the mixture until it became a smooth ___3___. This step was important because it prevented lumps from forming. After that, I slowly poured in the rest of the milk while ___4___ stirring. My sister said we should heat it on low fire and never let it boil, or the milk would taste ___5___.

While the cocoa was warming up, my sister showed me a special trick. She added a tiny drop of vanilla extract to the pot. "This makes it taste extra ___6___," she explained. The whole kitchen began to smell like a chocolate shop.

When the cocoa was hot enough, I poured it into two big mugs. My sister topped hers with some small marshmallows. I tried the cocoa without anything on top first—it was rich, creamy, and not too ___7___. Then I added marshmallows to mine too, and watched them slowly ___8___ on the surface.

We sat by the window and enjoyed our hot cocoa together. Outside, the wind was blowing hard, but inside we felt warm and ___9___. My sister said that making hot cocoa is one of the simplest pleasures of winter.

I think she is right. Sometimes ___10___ does not come from big events but from small, warm moments like this.

3. A. ice &emsp;&emsp; B. stone &emsp;&emsp; C. paste &emsp;&emsp; D. paper

6. A. sour &emsp;&emsp; B. bitter &emsp;&emsp; C. salty &emsp;&emsp; D. special
7. A. sweet &emsp;&emsp; B. hot &emsp;&emsp; C. thick &emsp;&emsp; D. light
8. A. sink &emsp;&emsp; B. melt &emsp;&emsp; C. break &emsp;&emsp; D. jump
9. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. scared &emsp;&emsp; C. cozy &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
10. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. happiness &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. pressure

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自制热可可比店里买的速溶可可好喝得多。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好吃"；C 项意为"更贵"；D 项意为"更难"。自制比速溶 tastier。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一杯好的热可可的秘诀是用真正的可可粉。A 项意为"秘诀"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"危险"；D 项意为"规则"。the secret of 意为"……的秘诀"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：搅拌直到变成光滑的糊状。A 项意为"冰"；B 项意为"石头"；C 项意为"糊状物"；D 项意为"纸"。可可粉加少量牛奶搅拌成 smooth paste。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一边倒入牛奶一边继续搅拌。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"保持/继续"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。while keeping stirring 意为"继续搅拌"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：牛奶煮沸了会变味。A 项意为"甜的"；B 项意为"新鲜的"；C 项意为"不好的"；D 项意为"冷的"。牛奶煮沸会 taste bad。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这会让味道特别好。A 项意为"酸的"；B 项意为"苦的"；C 项意为"咸的"；D 项意为"特别的"。加香草精使味道 extra special。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道浓郁、顺滑、不太甜。A 项意为"甜的"；B 项意为"热的"；C 项意为"浓的"；D 项意为"淡的"。not too sweet 表示甜度适中。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看着棉花糖在表面慢慢融化。A 项意为"沉下去"；B 项意为"融化"；C 项意为"打破"；D 项意为"跳"。棉花糖在热饮上会 melt。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：里面我们感觉温暖又舒适。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"舒适的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。warm and cozy 是冬天室内的感觉。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时幸福不是来自大事件，而是来自这样小而温暖的时刻。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"幸福"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"压力"。小幸福对应 happiness。故选 B。
