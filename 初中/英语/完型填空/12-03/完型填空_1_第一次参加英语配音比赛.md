# 中考英语完形填空：第一次参加英语配音比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held an English dubbing competition. Students could choose a movie scene and dub it in English. I had never joined such a competition before, but my English teacher ___1___ me to give it a try.

I chose a scene from the movie "Finding Nemo" because I loved the story. In the scene, the father fish Marlin is looking for his son Nemo and meets a funny fish named Dory. I decided to play the role of Marlin. My partner Liu Wei played Dory. We had to match our voices with the ___2___ of the characters on screen.

We practiced every day after school for two weeks. At first, my pronunciation was not ___3___ enough, and I could not keep up with the speed of the dialogue. Liu Wei was much better than me because she watched English movies every weekend. She ___4___ helped me correct my pronunciation and taught me how to express different emotions with my voice.

On the day of the competition, I was very ___5___. My hands were shaking when I held the microphone. But when the movie scene started playing, I forgot about my fear and focused on my lines. Liu Wei and I worked perfectly together. The ___6___ laughed at the funny parts and clapped when we finished.

We did not win first place, but we got the "Best Team Spirit" award. I was still very ___7___ because we had tried our best. The judge said our performance was natural and full of ___8___.

This competition changed my attitude toward English. Before, I thought English was just about tests and grades. Now I know it can also be a way to have ___9___ and express myself. I plan to join more English activities in the future and keep ___10___ my speaking skills.

## 选项

1. A. warned &emsp;&emsp; B. encouraged &emsp;&emsp; C. stopped &emsp;&emsp; D. punished
2. A. movements &emsp;&emsp; B. colors &emsp;&emsp; C. sizes &emsp;&emsp; D. weights
3. A. bad &emsp;&emsp; B. standard &emsp;&emsp; C. ugly &emsp;&emsp; D. low
4. A. patiently &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. lazily &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
5. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. hungry
6. A. teachers &emsp;&emsp; B. judges &emsp;&emsp; C. audience &emsp;&emsp; D. parents
7. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. scared
8. A. silence &emsp;&emsp; B. energy &emsp;&emsp; C. danger &emsp;&emsp; D. pressure
9. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. pain &emsp;&emsp; D. stress
10. A. wasting &emsp;&emsp; B. losing &emsp;&emsp; C. hiding &emsp;&emsp; D. improving

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：英语老师鼓励我试一试。A 项意为"警告"；B 项意为"鼓励"；C 项意为"阻止"；D 项意为"惩罚"。老师 encouraged 学生尝试。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：声音要和屏幕上角色的动作相匹配。A 项意为"动作"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"大小"；D 项意为"重量"。配音需配合 movements（口型动作）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始发音不够标准。A 项意为"差的"；B 项意为"标准的"；C 项意为"丑的"；D 项意为"低的"。pronunciation not standard enough。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她耐心地帮我纠正发音。A 项意为"耐心地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"懒惰地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。帮助朋友用 patiently。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛那天我非常紧张。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"饿的"。第一次比赛会 nervous。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：观众在搞笑部分笑了，在我们结束时鼓掌。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"评委"；C 项意为"观众"；D 项意为"父母"。audience 指全体观众。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仍然很高兴，因为我们尽力了。A 项意为"伤心的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。获得奖项、尽力了会 happy。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委说我们的表演自然且充满活力。A 项意为"沉默"；B 项意为"活力"；C 项意为"危险"；D 项意为"压力"。表演好用 full of energy。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：英语也可以是一种获得乐趣和表达自我的方式。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"痛苦"；D 项意为"压力"。have fun 意为"获得乐趣"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：继续提高口语技能。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"丢失"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"提高"。keep improving 意为"不断提高"。故选 D。
