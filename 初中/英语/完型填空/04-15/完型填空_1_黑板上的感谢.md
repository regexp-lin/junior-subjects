# 中考英语完形填空：黑板上的感谢

## 完形填空原文

Mr. Zhang was our Chinese teacher for three years. He was strict but fair. He never gave up on any student, no matter how ___1___ they were in the subject. I was one of the students who struggled. I found Chinese boring and often failed the exams. Mr. Zhang noticed. He started to stay after class to help me. He didn't scold me. Instead, he asked what I found difficult and worked with me step by step.

Slowly, my grades improved. By the end of Grade 9, I had gone from the bottom of the class to the top ten. I wanted to ___2___ Mr. Zhang, but I was too shy to say it out loud.

Our last day of junior high was coming. Many students were planning to give gifts to their teachers. I didn't have much money for an expensive present. I thought about what I could do.

That night, I had an idea. I would write something on the blackboard — a message for Mr. Zhang. The next morning, I came to school very ___3___. The classroom was empty. I picked up a piece of chalk and wrote in big letters: "Thank you, Mr. Zhang. You believed in me when I didn't believe in myself."

I didn't sign my name. I wanted it to be a ___4___ from all of us who had been helped by him. Then I left quietly.

When the bell rang and students came in, someone saw the words and called out, "Look at the blackboard!" Everyone turned to look. Mr. Zhang walked in. He ___5___ at the blackboard for a long time. I saw his eyes turn red. He cleared his throat and said, "I don't know who wrote this. But thank you. This is the best gift I've ever ___6___."

He didn't erase the words. They stayed on the blackboard the whole day. Other students started to add their own messages. "Thank you for your patience." "You made Chinese interesting." "We will miss you." By the afternoon, the blackboard was full of gratitude.

At the end of the day, Mr. Zhang took a photo of the blackboard. "I will keep this ___7___," he said. "It reminds me why I chose to be a teacher."

That experience taught me something important. Saying thank you doesn't need to be grand or expensive. Sometimes, a few honest words can mean more than any ___8___. And the best time to express gratitude is ___9___ — before it's too late. I'm glad I wrote those words. I'm glad Mr. Zhang knew how much he had ___10___ to our lives.

1. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. bad &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. slow
2. A. thank &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. visit &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
3. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. early &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
4. A. letter &emsp;&emsp; B. gift &emsp;&emsp; C. message &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson
5. A. looked &emsp;&emsp; B. wrote &emsp;&emsp; C. pointed &emsp;&emsp; D. ran
6. A. bought &emsp;&emsp; B. received &emsp;&emsp; C. sent &emsp;&emsp; D. made
7. A. forever &emsp;&emsp; B. away &emsp;&emsp; C. back &emsp;&emsp; D. here
8. A. pen &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. book
9. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. now &emsp;&emsp; D. tomorrow
10. A. meant &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. cost &emsp;&emsp; D. taken

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不管学生在这门课上多么___，他从不放弃。A 项意为"好"；B 项意为"差"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"慢"。张老师不放弃任何学生，包括成绩"差"（bad）的。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想___张老师，但不好意思说出口。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"拜访"；D 项意为"跟随"。成绩提高后想"感谢"（thank）老师。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天早上，我很___来到学校。A 项意为"晚"；B 项意为"早"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"慢"。要趁没人时在黑板上写字，需要"提早"（early）到校。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想让它代表所有受过他帮助的人的___。A 项意为"信"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"心意/留言"；D 项意为"课"。"message"可表示共同的心意或留言。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他对着黑板___了很久。A 项意为"看"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"指"；D 项意为"跑"。老师看到留言后长时间"看着"（looked at）黑板。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我___过的最好的礼物。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"收到"；C 项意为"送"；D 项意为"做"。老师作为收礼物的人，用"received"（收到）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会___保留这张照片。A 项意为"永远"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"这里"。"keep this forever"表示会一直珍藏。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时几句真心话比任何___都更有意义。A 项意为"笔"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"书"。与"grand or expensive"对应，指的是贵重"礼物"（gift）。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表达感激的最佳时机是___。A 项意为"晚"；B 项意为"从不"；C 项意为"现在"；D 项意为"明天"。与"before it's too late"呼应，强调要"趁现在"（now）表达感谢。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很高兴张老师知道他对我们的生活___了多少。A 项意为"意味着/重要"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"花费"；D 项意为"拿走"。"meant"可表示"对…很重要/意义重大"。故选 A。
