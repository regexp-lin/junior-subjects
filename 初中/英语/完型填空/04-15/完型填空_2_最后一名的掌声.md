# 中考英语完形填空：最后一名的掌声

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a 1,500-meter race every year. I was never a good runner. I was short and got tired easily. But our teacher said everyone had to take part. "It's not about winning," she said. "It's about ___1___ the race."

On the day of the race, I stood at the starting line with fifty other students. The whistle blew. Everyone rushed forward. I ran as fast as I could, but I was soon left ___2___. By the second lap, most runners were far ahead. I was breathing hard. My legs felt heavy. I wanted to ___3___, but I kept going.

When I entered the third lap, I noticed something. The students who had already finished were standing along the track. They weren't laughing. They were ___4___. "Come on! You can do it!" someone shouted. More voices joined in. "Keep going!" "Almost there!"

Their cheers gave me strength. I ___5___ my pace. Step by step, I moved forward. My body was tired, but my heart felt lighter.

Finally, I crossed the finish line. I was last. Dead last. But the moment I crossed, something unexpected happened. The crowd — students, teachers, even the principal — started to clap. The applause was ___6___ than for the first-place winner. People were smiling at me. Someone patted my shoulder. "Well done," a teacher said. "You didn't give up. That's what matters."

I was confused at first. I had lost. Why were they cheering? Then I understood. They weren't cheering for my ___7___. They were cheering for my effort. For not giving up when every step was painful. For finishing what I had started.

That day changed how I saw competition. Winning isn't everything. Sometimes, the ___8___ respect goes to those who try their best, even when they know they might not win. The last runner who crosses the line with determination can inspire others as much as the first one who breaks the ___9___.

I still don't enjoy running. But I run anyway. Because I remember that afternoon — the cheers, the applause, and the lesson I learned: it's not about being first. It's about ___10___ your best and never stopping until you reach the end.

## 选项

1. A. watching &emsp;&emsp; B. finishing &emsp;&emsp; C. winning &emsp;&emsp; D. starting
2. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. ahead &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. together
3. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. win &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
4. A. cheering &emsp;&emsp; B. crying &emsp;&emsp; C. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; D. leaving
5. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. slowed &emsp;&emsp; C. increased &emsp;&emsp; D. kept
6. A. softer &emsp;&emsp; B. louder &emsp;&emsp; C. longer &emsp;&emsp; D. shorter
7. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. place &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. prize
8. A. least &emsp;&emsp; B. most &emsp;&emsp; C. same &emsp;&emsp; D. different
9. A. rule &emsp;&emsp; B. record &emsp;&emsp; C. line &emsp;&emsp; D. law
10. A. being &emsp;&emsp; B. trying &emsp;&emsp; C. doing &emsp;&emsp; D. making

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的不是获胜，而是___比赛。A 项意为"观看"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"赢得"；D 项意为"开始"。老师强调参与和坚持，关键是"完成"（finishing）比赛。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很快就被甩在___了。A 项意为"后面"；B 项意为"前面"；C 项意为"独自"；D 项意为"一起"。"left behind"意为"被落在后面"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想___，但我继续跑。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"赢"；D 项意为"休息"。 legs heavy 时想"停下来"（stop），但坚持继续。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们不是在笑，而是在___。A 项意为"加油/欢呼"；B 项意为"哭"；C 项意为"睡"；D 项意为"离开"。后文有 "Come on! You can do it!"，说明在"加油"（cheering）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的加油声给了我力量，我___了步伐。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"放慢"；C 项意为"加快"；D 项意为"保持"。受到鼓励后更有力气，"加快"（increased）了脚步。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：掌声比给第一名的还要___。A 项意为"更轻"；B 项意为"更响"；C 项意为"更长"；D 项意为"更短"。大家为最后一名的坚持喝彩，掌声"更响亮"（louder）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们不是在为我的___欢呼。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"名次"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"奖品"。作者是最后一名，大家不是为"名次"（place）欢呼。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时，___的尊重会给那些竭尽全力的人。A 项意为"最少的"；B 项意为"最多的"；C 项意为"同样的"；D 项意为"不同的"。"the most respect"表示最大的尊重，给坚持到底的人。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：就像第一个打破___的人一样激励别人。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"纪录"；C 项意为"线"；D 项意为"法律"。"break the record"意为"打破纪录"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是尽___全力，永不止步直到终点。A 项意为"是"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"做"；D 项意为"制造"。"try your best"意为"竭尽全力"。故选 B。
