# 中考英语完形填空：爷爷的旧怀表

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather had an old pocket watch. He had carried it for over fifty years. The silver cover was worn and the hands moved slowly, but Grandpa ___1___ it. He said it reminded him of his father, who had given it to him when he turned eighteen.

When I was a child, I loved to sit on Grandpa's knee and watch him take out the watch. He would open the cover, show me the numbers, and tell me stories about the past. "Time goes by ___2___,", he often said. "Use it well."

Last spring, Grandpa fell ill. He stayed in the hospital for two weeks. When he came home, he was weak. One afternoon, he called me to his room. "I want to give you something," he said. He placed the old watch in my ___3___. "Take good care of it. And remember — every minute counts."

I didn't know what to say. I was honored, but also sad. It felt like he was saying goodbye.

Grandpa passed away that summer. For a long time, I couldn't bring myself to look at the watch. It made me ___4___ him too much. I kept it in a drawer, wrapped in a soft cloth.

Then came my first big exam in junior high — the mid-term test. I was nervous. The night before, I couldn't sleep. I thought about Grandpa's words: "Every minute counts." I took out the watch. I held it in my hand and imagined Grandpa ___5___ me to do my best. Somehow, I felt calmer.

The next day, I put the watch in my pocket during the exam. When I felt worried, I touched it. It was like having Grandpa beside me. I finished the exam with more ___6___ than I had expected.

Since then, the watch has become my lucky charm. But more importantly, it reminds me of Grandpa's lesson: time is precious. We can't ___7___ it back. So I try to waste less time on my phone and spend more time reading, learning, and being with my family.

On Grandpa's birthday every year, I wind the watch and let it run for a while. Though it no longer keeps perfect time, it still ___8___ me of the love that has passed from one generation to another. Some things — like love and wisdom — never grow old. They are ___9___ from the past and carried into the future. That is the real ___10___ Grandpa left me.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表壳磨损、走得慢，但爷爷___它。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"珍视"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"丢失"。爷爷戴了五十多年，虽旧却依然珍惜，"treasured"（珍视）贴合文意。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：时光___流逝。A 项意为"快"；B 项意为"慢"；C 项意为"晚"；D 项意为"早"。爷爷教导珍惜时间，常用"time goes by fast"（时光飞逝）来提醒。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他把旧表放在我的___。A 项意为"口袋"；B 项意为"手"；C 项意为"包"；D 项意为"桌子"。把表递给孩子时，应放在"手"（hand）里。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它让我太___他了。A 项意为"想念"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"打电话"。"make me miss him"意为看见怀表就会"想念"（miss）爷爷。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想象爷爷在___我尽力。A 项意为"问"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"告诉"；D 项意为"鼓励"。"encourage sb to do"表示"鼓励某人做某事"，与考前打气的情景相符。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我完成考试时比预想的更有___。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"信心"；D 项意为"运气"。怀表带来镇定，考试完成得更有"信心"（confidence）。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们无法把它___来。A 项意为"带回"；B 项意为"拿走"；C 项意为"送"；D 项意为"得到"。"bring back"意为"使…回来/挽回"，时间流逝后无法"挽回"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：它仍然___我那份代代相传的爱。A 项意为"警告"；B 项意为"提醒"；C 项意为"告诉"；D 项意为"问"。"remind sb of"为固定搭配，意为"使某人想起"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们从过去___下来，传向未来。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"扔掉"；C 项意为"传递"；D 项意为"藏"。"pass from...to"表示"从…传到…"，爱与智慧被"传承"（passed）。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这才是爷爷留给我的真正___。A 项意为"手表"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"教训/教诲"。作者强调不仅是怀表，更是珍惜时间、传承爱的"教诲"（lesson）。故选 D。
