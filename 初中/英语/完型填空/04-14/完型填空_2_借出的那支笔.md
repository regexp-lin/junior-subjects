# 中考英语完形填空：借出的那支笔

## 完形填空原文

It was the first day of the final exam. I had prepared everything: my ID card, my eraser, and two extra pens. But when I opened my pencil case, I found that both pens were out of ink. I had forgotten to ___1___ them.

I felt my face turn pale. The exam would start in five minutes. I rushed to the nearest classroom to borrow a pen. Most students were already seated and busy with their own things. I asked a boy I didn't know. "Excuse me, do you have an ___2___ pen I could borrow?"

He looked at his pencil case. He had only one pen. But he handed it to me without hesitating. "Here. Use it. I have a pencil as a backup."

"Are you sure? What if you need it?"

"I'll be ___3___," he said. "Good luck."

I thanked him and ran back to my classroom. During the exam, I used his pen. It wrote smoothly. I tried my best to answer every question. When the exam ___4___, I looked for the boy to return the pen. But I couldn't find him. He must have left quickly. I didn't even know his name or which class he was in.

I asked around, but nobody seemed to know who he was. I kept the pen in my bag, hoping to ___5___ him someday. A week passed. Two weeks passed. I started to worry. What if I never found him? I had his pen, and he had lent it to a complete ___6___.

One afternoon, I was walking in the hallway when I saw him. He was talking to a teacher. I ran over. "Hey! I've been looking for you! Here's your pen. Thank you so much for lending it to me."

He looked surprised, then smiled. "You remembered. I thought you might have forgotten. It's just a pen."

"It's not just a pen," I said. "You helped me when I was in trouble. I'll never forget that."

We became friends after that. His name was Chen Hao. He said he had lent pens to many people during exams. "Some returned them, some didn't," he said. "But I ___7___ lend when someone needs help. A pen isn't worth much. Trust is."

His words stayed with me. Now I always carry an extra pen. When someone ___8___ one before an exam, I'm happy to help. I've also learned that small acts of kindness can ___9___ into something bigger. That borrowed pen didn't just help me pass an exam. It led me to a new friend and taught me a valuable lesson about trust and ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我忘了___它们。A 项意为"找到"；B 项意为"用"；C 项意为"灌墨/补充"；D 项意为"买"。两支笔都没墨水，是忘了"灌墨"（refill）或换芯。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你有没有___的笔可以借我？A 项意为"旧的"；B 项意为"多余的"；C 项意为"新的"；D 项意为"便宜的"。"extra"表示"多余的/备用的"，借笔时常用。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会___的。A 项意为"没问题"；B 项意为"抱歉"；C 项意为"错的"；D 项意为"累的"。"I'll be fine"表示自己没问题，让作者放心用。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：考试___后，我去找男孩还笔。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"结束"；C 项意为"来"；D 项意为"停止"。"when the exam ended"表示考试"结束"后还笔。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把笔放在包里，希望有一天能___他。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"遇见"；D 项意为"打电话"。"meet"表示能再遇到他并还笔。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他把笔借给了一个完全陌生的人。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"学生"；C 项意为"陌生人"；D 项意为"朋友"。"complete stranger"意为"完全不认识的人"，强调信任。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但有人需要帮助时，我___会借。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"总是"；D 项意为"有时"。"always"体现他乐于助人的习惯。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当有人在考试前___笔时，我很乐意帮忙。A 项意为"丢失"；B 项意为"需要"；C 项意为"带"；D 项意为"找到"。"needs one"表示需要（一支笔）。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：小的善举可以___成更大的东西。A 项意为"转变"；B 项意为"生长"；C 项意为"落下"；D 项意为"得到"。"turn into"意为"变成"，善意可以化为更大的影响。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教会我关于信任和___的宝贵一课。A 项意为"考试"；B 项意为"笔"；C 项意为"善意"；D 项意为"运气"。与"trust"并列，应是"kindness"（善意）。故选 C。
