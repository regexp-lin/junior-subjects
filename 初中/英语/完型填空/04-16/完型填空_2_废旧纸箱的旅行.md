# 中考英语完形填空：废旧纸箱的旅行

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held a recycling project. Each class had to collect used cardboard boxes and turn them into something useful. My class decided to make a small library corner.

We gathered boxes from our homes and the school cafeteria. At first, the boxes looked old and ___1___. Nobody believed we could make something beautiful from them.

Our teacher, Mr. Chen, showed us how to cut, fold, and paint the boxes. "Every ___2___ thing can become useful again if we use our imagination," he said. We worked in groups. Some students cut the boxes into shelves. Others painted them with bright colors and simple patterns.

I was in charge of painting. I chose blue and green — the colors of the sky and grass. As I painted, I started to ___3___ the boxes differently. They weren't just trash anymore. They were becoming something that could hold books and bring joy to our classroom.

After three days of hard work, our library corner was ready. We placed it near the window where there was plenty of ___4___. The shelves held over two hundred books that students had brought from home. Everyone was proud of what we had created.

The best part came later. A first-grade student walked into our classroom, saw the colorful shelves, and said with a wide smile, "Wow! Did you really make this from ___5___ boxes? That's amazing!" Her words made us feel that all our effort was worth it.

Our library corner became the most popular spot in the classroom. During breaks, students would ___6___ around it, choosing books to read. Even students who didn't like reading before started to borrow books. The old boxes had brought us something special — a love for reading.

I learned an important lesson from this project. We often throw away things without thinking. But with a little creativity and ___7___, we can give them a new life. The cardboard boxes had completed a wonderful journey — from trash to treasure.

Now when I see empty boxes or other "waste" at home, I always ask myself: "Can I use this for something ___8___?" Sometimes the answer is yes. A small change in our habits can make a big difference to our planet.

The recycling project didn't just change our classroom. It changed the way we ___9___ things. Nothing is truly worthless if we're willing to see its hidden value. That's the message those old cardboard boxes taught us — and it's a lesson we'll ___10___ for a long time.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，这些箱子看起来又旧又___。A 项意为"干净的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"丑的"；D 项意为"重的"。废旧纸箱又旧又"丑"（ugly），所以大家不相信能做出漂亮的东西。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词/过去分词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："每一个___的东西，只要我们发挥想象，都能再次变得有用。"A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"无用的"；D 项意为"坏的"。老师强调看似"无用"（useless）的废弃品可再利用，与后文"再次变得有用"形成对比。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我涂色时，我开始___这些箱子不一样了。A 项意为"看待/看见"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"找到"。"see...differently"表示"用不同的眼光看待"，从前当垃圾，现在看到了新可能。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把它放在窗边，那里有充足的___。A 项意为"风"；B 项意为"光线"；C 项意为"雨"；D 项意为"雪"。书架放在窗边是为了有足够"光线"（light）便于阅读。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："你们真的是用___箱子做的吗？太厉害了！"A 项意为"新的"；B 项意为"大的"；C 项意为"旧的"；D 项意为"小的"。项目主题是回收废旧纸箱，所以强调的是"旧"（old）箱子。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：课间，同学们会___在周围，挑选要读的书。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"站"；C 项意为"坐"；D 项意为"聚集"。"gather around"意为"聚集在……周围"，形容大家围在书架旁选书。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但只要我们有一点创意和___，就能赋予它们新的生命。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"努力"；D 项意为"运气"。变废为宝需要创意和"努力"（effort），creativity 与 effort 常一起使用。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："我能用这个做点___的事吗？"A 项意为"别的"；B 项意为"有用的"；C 项意为"不同的"；D 项意为"相同的"。"something useful"意为"有用处的东西"，符合变废为宝的主题。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它改变了我们___事物的方式。A 项意为"思考/看待"；B 项意为"寻找"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"依赖"。"think about"表示"思考、看待"，项目让人重新"看待"废弃物的价值。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是废旧纸箱教给我们的道理——也是我们会长久___的一课。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"失去"。有意义的道理会让人"铭记"（remember）很久。故选 A。
