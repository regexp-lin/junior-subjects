# 中考英语完形填空：捡到的钱包

## 完形填空原文

On my way home from school last Tuesday, I found a brown wallet lying on the sidewalk. I ___1___ it up and opened it. Inside were about 500 yuan, a bus card, and an ID card with a photo of an elderly woman.

I didn't know what to do at first. My friend suggested I keep the money and throw the wallet away. "Nobody will ever ___2___," he said. But something in my heart told me that was wrong.

I decided to take the wallet to the nearest police station. The officer there was kind. He ___3___ the ID card and said he would try to find the owner. "You did the right thing," he told me with a smile.

Two days later, my mother received a phone call. It was from the woman who had lost the wallet. She wanted to meet me and say thank you in person.

We met at a small café. The woman, Mrs. Wang, was over sixty years old. She held my hands with ___4___ and said, "Thank you, young man. That money was for my granddaughter's birthday gift. You have a kind heart."

She insisted on giving me 50 yuan as a reward. I refused at first, but she said, "Please ___5___ it. It's not for the money you returned — it's for the honesty you showed."

That day I learned something important. Being honest is not always easy. Sometimes it ___6___ us to go against what others suggest. But doing the right thing always makes us feel good about ourselves.

Mrs. Wang and I became friends. She sometimes sends me messages to ask about my studies. Her ___7___ reminds me that small acts of kindness can create lasting connections.

I still walk past that sidewalk sometimes. Every time I do, I remember the wallet and the choice I made. I'm ___8___ I chose to do the right thing. That decision changed not only Mrs. Wang's day but also the way I see myself.

Honesty is like a seed. When you plant it, it grows into something beautiful. The wallet I found that day was ___9___, but the lesson it taught me was priceless. I hope that whenever you face a similar situation, you will make the same choice — to be honest and ___10___ others as you would want to be treated.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它___起来并打开。A 项意为"捡起"；B 项意为"看"；C 项意为"翻转"；D 项意为"放"。钱包在地上，需要先"捡起"（pick up）才能打开。pick up 为固定短语，表示"拾起"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："没人会___的。"A 项意为"知道"；B 项意为"在乎"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"注意到"。朋友建议私留钱，意思是失主"不会知道"（know）是谁捡的。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他___了身份证，说会试着找到失主。A 项意为"查看/核对"；B 项意为"画"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"丢失"。警察接到失物后会"查看"（check）证件信息以便联系失主。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她___着握住我的手。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"惊讶"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"眼泪"。老妇人感激时通常会眼含"泪水"（tears），"with tears"表示激动落泪。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："请___下。这不是为你还回的钱——是为你所展现的诚实。"A 项意为"接受"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"扔"。她坚持给报酬，希望"我""收下"（take）以表心意。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时它___我们去做与他人建议相反的事。A 项意为"要求"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"教"；D 项意为"允许"。"ask sb to do"表示"要求某人做某事"，坚守诚实有时需要"要求"（asks）我们顶住外界压力。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的___提醒我，小小的善意可以建立持久的联系。A 项意为"善意"；B 项意为"钱包"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"电话"。老妇人发消息关心"我"的学习，体现的是"善意"（kindness）。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___我选择做了正确的事。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"抱歉的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"担忧的"。做了正确决定后，内心应当是"高兴"（glad）的。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天我捡到的钱包是___，但它教给我的道理是无价的。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"有用的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"特别的"。与后文"无价"形成对比，钱包本身只是"普通的"（ordinary）物品。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：诚实待人，像希望被___那样去___他人。A 项意为"对待"；B 项意为"看"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"相信"。对应"treat others as you would want to be treated"（己所不欲勿施于人），空处应为"对待"（treat）。故选 A。
