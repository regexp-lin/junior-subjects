# 中考英语完形填空：校园菜园

## 完形填空原文

Our school started a vegetable garden project last spring. The headmaster said it would help us learn about nature and ___1___. Every class was given a small piece of land to grow vegetables.

At first, many students were not ___2___ in the project. We thought gardening was boring and dirty. But our science teacher, Mr Wang, was very patient. He showed us how to prepare the soil and plant seeds. He said, "Taking care of plants is like taking care of a friend. You need to give them water, sunlight, and ___3___."

My class decided to grow tomatoes. We dug the soil, planted the seeds, and watered them every day. For the first two weeks, ___4___ happened. We just saw brown soil. Some of my classmates started to lose hope. "Maybe we did something wrong," they said. But Mr Wang told us to be ___5___ and wait.

Then one morning, tiny green shoots appeared from the soil. We were so excited that we ran to tell the whole school. Over the next few weeks, the shoots grew into small plants with green leaves. We learned to pull out the weeds and ___6___ the plants from insects. It was hard work, but we enjoyed watching our tomatoes grow bigger each day.

Two months later, the tomatoes turned red. It was time to ___7___ them. We carefully picked every tomato and washed them clean. Our class had the most tomatoes among all the classes, and we felt very ___8___ of ourselves.

We used some tomatoes to make a salad for lunch. It was the most delicious salad I had ever ___9___. Maybe it was because we grew the tomatoes with our own hands.

The garden project taught us more than just gardening. It taught us patience, teamwork, and the joy of seeing the ___10___ of our hard work.

## 选项

1. A. music &emsp;&emsp; B. teamwork &emsp;&emsp; C. history &emsp;&emsp; D. painting
2. A. interested &emsp;&emsp; B. successful &emsp;&emsp; C. careful &emsp;&emsp; D. different
3. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. space &emsp;&emsp; C. attention &emsp;&emsp; D. freedom
4. A. nothing &emsp;&emsp; B. something &emsp;&emsp; C. anything &emsp;&emsp; D. everything
5. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. honest &emsp;&emsp; C. patient &emsp;&emsp; D. polite
6. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. protect &emsp;&emsp; C. feed &emsp;&emsp; D. separate
7. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. count &emsp;&emsp; D. harvest
8. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. sure
9. A. seen &emsp;&emsp; B. bought &emsp;&emsp; C. tasted &emsp;&emsp; D. made
10. A. results &emsp;&emsp; B. secrets &emsp;&emsp; C. stories &emsp;&emsp; D. problems

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长说这个项目能帮助我们了解大自然和___。A 项意为"音乐"；B 项意为"团队合作"；C 项意为"历史"；D 项意为"绘画"。菜园项目需要大家协作完成，后文也提到 teamwork，"teamwork"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始很多学生对这个项目不___。A 项意为"感兴趣的"；B 项意为"成功的"；C 项意为"仔细的"；D 项意为"不同的"。"be interested in"是固定搭配，意为"对……感兴趣"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你需要给它们水、阳光和___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"空间"；C 项意为"关注、关心"；D 项意为"自由"。照顾植物就像照顾朋友，需要关注和关心，"attention"最合理。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：前两周，___都没有发生。A 项意为"什么都没有"；B 项意为"某些东西"；C 项意为"任何东西"；D 项意为"所有东西"。播种后两周没有变化，只看到泥土，"nothing happened"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师让我们要___并等待。A 项意为"勇敢的"；B 项意为"诚实的"；C 项意为"耐心的"；D 项意为"有礼貌的"。植物生长需要时间，老师让大家耐心等待，"patient"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学会了拔草和___植物免受虫害。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"保护"；C 项意为"喂养"；D 项意为"分开"。"protect...from"意为"保护……免受……"，此处指保护植物免受虫害。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：番茄变红了，到了___它们的时候了。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"数"；D 项意为"收获"。番茄成熟了就可以收获了，"harvest"正确。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班的番茄最多，我们感到非常___。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"疲惫的"；D 项意为"确定的"。"feel proud of"意为"为……感到自豪"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我___过的最好吃的沙拉。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"品尝"；D 项意为"制作"。评价食物好吃用"tasted"（品尝过的），最恰当。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会了我们耐心、团队合作和看到辛勤劳动___的喜悦。A 项意为"成果"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"问题"。"the results of hard work"意为"劳动的成果"。故选 A。
