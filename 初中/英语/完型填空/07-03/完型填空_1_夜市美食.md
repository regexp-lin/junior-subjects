# 中考英语完形填空：夜市美食

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, my cousin visited us from another city. To welcome him, my parents took us to the night market near our home. It was his first time visiting a night market, and he was very ___1___ about everything.

The market was crowded and colourful. There were hundreds of stalls on both sides of the street, selling food, clothes, toys, and many other things. The ___2___ of grilled meat and fried noodles filled the air. My cousin kept pointing at different stalls and asking, "What's that? Can we try it?"

We stopped at a stall that sold stinky tofu. My cousin took one look at it and ___3___ his nose. "It smells terrible!" he said. I laughed and told him, "It smells bad, but it ___4___ really good. Just give it a try." He took a small bite and his eyes went wide. "Wow, you're right!" he said. From then on, he was willing to try ___5___ new.

Next, we had some barbecued squid on sticks. The seller was very ___6___ and chatted with us while cooking. He told us he had been running this stall for over twenty years. "I started when I was young," he said. "Now my children help me." I could see the pride in his eyes.

After eating, we played some games at a stall. My cousin tried to throw rings onto bottles to win a toy. He ___7___ the first three times, but on his last try, the ring landed perfectly. He won a small stuffed bear and was so ___8___ that he danced around.

On the way home, my cousin said, "This is the most fun I've had in a long time." I felt ___9___ that he enjoyed our city. The night market is not just about food and shopping. It is a place full of life, laughter, and warm ___10___ between people.

## 选项

1. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. curious &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. colour &emsp;&emsp; B. sound &emsp;&emsp; C. smell &emsp;&emsp; D. shape
3. A. covered &emsp;&emsp; B. touched &emsp;&emsp; C. blew &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaned
4. A. looks &emsp;&emsp; B. sounds &emsp;&emsp; C. feels &emsp;&emsp; D. tastes
5. A. nothing &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. everything &emsp;&emsp; D. something
6. A. serious &emsp;&emsp; B. friendly &emsp;&emsp; C. strict &emsp;&emsp; D. shy
7. A. won &emsp;&emsp; B. succeeded &emsp;&emsp; C. missed &emsp;&emsp; D. stopped
8. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. scared &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
9. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. glad &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
10. A. competitions &emsp;&emsp; B. arguments &emsp;&emsp; C. connections &emsp;&emsp; D. instructions

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是他第一次逛夜市，他对一切都非常___。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"好奇的"；D 项意为"生气的"。第一次来夜市，对新鲜事物充满好奇，"curious"最贴切。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烤肉和炒面的___弥漫在空气中。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"形状"。美食的香味充满空气，"smell"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我表弟看了一眼就___鼻子。A 项意为"捂住"；B 项意为"触摸"；C 项意为"吹"；D 项意为"清洁"。闻到臭豆腐的味道捂鼻子，"covered his nose"是常见表达。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（感官动词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它闻起来不好，但___起来真的很好。A 项意为"看起来"；B 项意为"听起来"；C 项意为"摸起来"；D 项意为"尝起来"。与前面的"smells"（闻起来）对比，"tastes"（尝起来）形成转折。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那以后，他愿意尝试___新的东西。A 项意为"什么都不"；B 项意为"任何东西"；C 项意为"所有东西"；D 项意为"某些东西"。"something new"意为"新的东西"，表示愿意尝试新事物。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：卖家非常___，边做边和我们聊天。A 项意为"严肃的"；B 项意为"友善的"；C 项意为"严格的"；D 项意为"害羞的"。边做边聊天说明人很友善、健谈，"friendly"最合适。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他前三次都___了，但最后一次成功了。A 项意为"赢了"；B 项意为"成功了"；C 项意为"没投中"；D 项意为"停止了"。前三次没投中，最后一次才成功，"missed"意为"没中"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他赢了一个小玩偶，___得跳起舞来。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。赢了奖品高兴地跳舞，"happy"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___他喜欢我们的城市。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"高兴的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。表弟玩得开心，自己感到高兴，"glad"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：夜市是一个充满生活、欢笑和人与人之间温暖___的地方。A 项意为"比赛"；B 项意为"争论"；C 项意为"联系、连接"；D 项意为"说明"。人们之间的温暖互动用"connections"表达。故选 C。
