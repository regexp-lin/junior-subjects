# 中考英语完形填空：升旗台的护旗手

## 完形填空原文

Every Monday morning, our school holds a flag-raising ceremony. Three students stand in front of the flagpole as flag guards. Last term, I was chosen to be one of them. I felt both honored and ___1___.

The day before the ceremony, we practised for two hours. The teacher told us that every step and every move must be exact. "You represent our school," she said. I tried my best to ___2___ every detail. My arms ached, but I didn't complain. I knew that the other two guards were ___3___ just as hard.

On Monday morning, I put on my uniform and stood ready. My heart was beating fast. When the national anthem started, we raised the flag together. I focused on my ___4___ and tried not to make any mistakes. The flag went up slowly, and when it reached the top, I felt a wave of pride.

After the ceremony, many classmates said we did a good job. But what I remembered most was not the praise. It was the ___5___ of standing there with hundreds of students singing together. That moment made me understand what it means to belong to something bigger than ___6___.

Being a flag guard taught me about responsibility. We had to arrive early, practise carefully, and work as a ___7___. No one could do it alone. If one of us made a mistake, the whole ceremony would be affected. This ___8___ me that in life, we often need to depend on others and others depend on us.

I will never forget those Monday mornings. The red flag against the blue sky, the solemn music, and the ___9___ on everyone's faces — they remind me that some moments in life are meant to be ___10___ in our hearts and remembered.

## 选项

1. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
2. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. ignore
3. A. playing &emsp;&emsp; B. resting &emsp;&emsp; C. working &emsp;&emsp; D. waiting
4. A. music &emsp;&emsp; B. steps &emsp;&emsp; C. friends &emsp;&emsp; D. uniform
5. A. sound &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. taste &emsp;&emsp; D. touch
6. A. yourself &emsp;&emsp; B. myself &emsp;&emsp; C. herself &emsp;&emsp; D. itself
7. A. class &emsp;&emsp; B. team &emsp;&emsp; C. family &emsp;&emsp; D. group
8. A. reminded &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. asked &emsp;&emsp; D. allowed
9. A. smiles &emsp;&emsp; B. tears &emsp;&emsp; C. worries &emsp;&emsp; D. surprises
10. A. shared &emsp;&emsp; B. kept &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. hidden

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到既荣幸又___。A 项意为"紧张"；B 项意为"放松"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"懒惰"。"nervous"与"honored"形成对比，被选为护旗手既光荣又紧张。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽全力___每个细节。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"忽视"。"remember every detail"与"every step and every move must be exact"相呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道另外两名护旗手也在同样努力地___。A 项意为"玩耍"；B 项意为"休息"；C 项意为"努力"；D 项意为"等待"。"working just as hard"与"tried my best"相呼应，强调共同努力。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我专注于我的___，尽量不出错。A 项意为"音乐"；B 项意为"步骤、动作"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"制服"。"my steps"与"every step and every move must be exact"相呼应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我最难忘的是站在那里与数百名学生一起歌唱的___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"味道"；D 项意为"触觉"。"the sound of...singing together"与"national anthem"、"singing"相呼应。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻让我理解属于比___更大的事物意味着什么。A 项意为"你自己"；B 项意为"我自己"；C 项意为"她自己"；D 项意为"它自己"。"bigger than myself"为常见表达，指超越个人的集体。主语是"I"，故用 myself。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须早到、认真练习，并作为___一起合作。A 项意为"班级"；B 项意为"团队"；C 项意为"家庭"；D 项意为"小组、群体"。"work as a group"与"No one could do it alone"相呼应，强调集体协作。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这___我，在生活中我们常常需要依靠他人，他人也依靠我们。A 项意为"提醒"；B 项意为"教会"；C 项意为"问"；D 项意为"允许"。"taught me"与"Being a flag guard taught me"相呼应，强调从中获得的人生启示。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：红旗映蓝天、庄重的音乐、以及每个人脸上的___——它们提醒我生命中有些时刻值得被珍藏和铭记。A 项意为"微笑"；B 项意为"眼泪"；C 项意为"担忧"；D 项意为"惊讶"。"smiles"与庄重而积极的升旗仪式氛围相符。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生命中有些时刻值得被___在心并铭记。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"珍藏、保持"；C 项意为"留下"；D 项意为"隐藏"。"kept in our hearts"意为"珍藏在心中"，与"remembered"相呼应。故选 B。
