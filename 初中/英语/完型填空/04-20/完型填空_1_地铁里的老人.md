# 中考英语完形填空：地铁里的老人

## 完形填空原文

That morning, I was in a hurry to get to school. The subway was crowded as usual. I pushed through the people and found a seat. Just as I sat down, I noticed an old man standing nearby. He held a heavy bag in one hand and ___1___ to a pole with the other. His face looked tired.

I knew I should offer my seat. But I was so ___2___. I had stayed up late studying the night before. "Someone else will do it," I thought, and I looked down at my phone.

A few minutes passed. The old man was still standing. Nobody seemed to ___3___ him. I felt uncomfortable. Then I remembered what my grandma once said: "A small kind act can make a big ___4___."

I stood up and touched the old man's arm. "Please take my seat," I said.

He looked surprised, then smiled. "Thank you, young man. That's very ___5___ of you."

As he sat down, I saw relief on his face. He put his bag on the floor and said, "I'm going to visit my granddaughter. She's in hospital. I bring her food every day."

His words made me feel both sad and proud. I was glad I had made the right ___6___.

The rest of the journey, I stood by the door. It wasn't easy, but I didn't mind. When I got off the train, the old man waved at me and said, "Good luck with your ___7___!"

That simple moment stayed with me all day. I realized that being kind doesn't ___8___ much time or effort. Sometimes, it just means giving up something small — like a seat — for someone who ___9___ it more.

Since then, I have tried to be more careful about the people around me. On buses and subways, I often look for those who might need a seat. A little kindness can ___10___ someone's day, and it makes me feel better too.

## 选项

1. A. held &emsp;&emsp; B. pointed &emsp;&emsp; C. tied &emsp;&emsp; D. moved
2. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
3. A. notice &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. know &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
4. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. dream &emsp;&emsp; C. difference &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
5. A. clever &emsp;&emsp; B. kind &emsp;&emsp; C. brave &emsp;&emsp; D. strict
6. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. question &emsp;&emsp; C. choice &emsp;&emsp; D. way
7. A. work &emsp;&emsp; B. study &emsp;&emsp; C. life &emsp;&emsp; D. family
8. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. cost &emsp;&emsp; C. spend &emsp;&emsp; D. pay
9. A. wants &emsp;&emsp; B. needs &emsp;&emsp; C. likes &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoys
10. A. waste &emsp;&emsp; B. break &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. lose

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他一只手拿着沉重的包，另一只手___着扶手。A 项意为"抓住/握着"；B 项意为"指向"；C 项意为"系/绑"；D 项意为"移动"。在地铁里站不稳时，人们会"抓住"（held）扶手保持平衡。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我太___了。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"疲惫的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。前文说昨晚熬夜学习，所以作者很"疲惫"（tired），这也是他起初不想让座的理由。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：似乎没有人___他。A 项意为"注意到"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"认识"；D 项意为"跟随"。其他乘客没有"注意到"（notice）老人的困境，所以没人让座。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小小的善举能带来很大的___。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"改变/不同"；D 项意为"错误"。奶奶的话是"make a big difference"（产生很大影响），是常见表达。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你真是太___了。A 项意为"聪明的"；B 项意为"善良的"；C 项意为"勇敢的"；D 项意为"严格的"。老人感谢作者让座，夸他"善良"（kind）。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很高兴做出了正确的___。A 项意为"回答"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"方式"。作者在让座与不让座之间做了"选择"（choice），最终选择让座。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：祝你的___顺利！A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"生活"；D 项意为"家庭"。作者背着书包去上学，老人祝他"学习"（study）顺利，符合情境。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：善良并不___太多时间或精力。A 项意为"花费"；B 项意为"价值/花费"；C 项意为"花费"；D 项意为"支付"。"take time/effort"为固定搭配，主语多为it或行为，表示"需要/花费"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时，只是把一些小东西——比如座位——让给更___它的人。A 项意为"想要"；B 项意为"需要"；C 项意为"喜欢"；D 项意为"享受"。让座给老人是因为他们更"需要"（needs）座位，强调必要性。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一点善意可以___某人的一天。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"打破"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"失去"。"change someone's day"意为"改变某人的一天"，指让这一天变得更好。故选 C。
