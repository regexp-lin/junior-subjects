# 中考英语完形填空：第一次骑自行车上学

## 完形填空原文

I turned thirteen last month, and my parents gave me a new bicycle as a birthday gift. I had learned to ride when I was ten, but I had never cycled to school. The road was busy, and my parents always said it was too ___1___.

But I really wanted to try. I asked my dad again and again. Finally, he agreed, but only if he could ride ___2___ me on the first day.

That morning, I got up early. I checked the tires, the brakes, and my helmet. Everything was ready. Dad and I set off together. The sun was just rising, and the air felt ___3___.

At first, I was nervous. Cars passed by quickly. My hands gripped the handlebars tightly. Dad rode beside me and said, "Take it easy. Stay on the right side. You'll be ___4___."

His words helped me relax. After ten minutes, I began to feel more confident. I enjoyed the wind in my face and the freedom of the ride.

We reached the school gate. Dad smiled and said, "You did it! Tomorrow you can try ___5___."

The next day, I rode alone. It was a little scary at first, but I remembered Dad's advice. I followed the traffic rules and arrived at school safely. My classmates were ___6___ when they heard I had cycled by myself.

"Wow, that's cool!" said Li Ming. "I wish I could do that too."

From then on, I cycled to school every day when the weather was fine. It became part of my ___7___. I felt healthier and more energetic. I also learned that trying new things might seem ___8___ at first, but with courage and practice, we can do more than we think.

My parents were proud of me. Dad said, "You've grown up. You're not a little kid anymore." That made me feel good. Riding to school was more than just a way to get there — it was a step toward ___9___.

Now I always tell my younger friends: don't be afraid to try. The first time might be hard, but every expert was once a ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; C. far &emsp;&emsp; D. early
2. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. behind &emsp;&emsp; D. without
3. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. hot &emsp;&emsp; C. fresh &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
4. A. fine &emsp;&emsp; B. wrong &emsp;&emsp; C. late &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
5. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. again &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. fast
6. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
7. A. habit &emsp;&emsp; B. hobby &emsp;&emsp; C. plan &emsp;&emsp; D. dream
8. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
9. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. adulthood &emsp;&emsp; C. friendship &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness
10. A. winner &emsp;&emsp; B. teacher &emsp;&emsp; C. beginner &emsp;&emsp; D. leader

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母总是说这太___了。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"远的"；D 项意为"早的"。道路繁忙，父母担心孩子骑车安全，认为"危险"（dangerous）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：前提是他第一天能___我一起骑。A 项意为"为了"；B 项意为"和……一起"；C 项意为"在……后面"；D 项意为"没有"。爸爸答应陪同，所以要"和"（with）作者一起骑。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：空气感觉很___。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"热的"；C 项意为"清新的"；D 项意为"干燥的"。清晨太阳刚升起，空气通常"清新"（fresh），适合骑车。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：放轻松，靠右骑，你会___的。A 项意为"没事的/可以的"；B 项意为"错的"；C 项意为"晚的"；D 项意为"迷路的"。爸爸在安慰作者，说他"没问题"（fine）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：明天你可以___试试。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"再次"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"快速地"。第一天爸爸陪同，第二天让他"独自"（alone）骑车。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们听说我独自骑车来时都很___。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。李明的"Wow, that's cool!"表明大家很"惊讶"（surprised）和佩服。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这成了我___的一部分。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"梦想"。每天骑车上学成为一种日常"习惯"（habit）。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尝试新事物一开始可能看起来很___。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。与后文"with courage and practice"呼应，强调起初"困难"（difficult）。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是迈向___的一步。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"成年"；C 项意为"友谊"；D 项意为"幸福"。前文爸爸说"You've grown up"，强调这是成长的标志，迈向"成年"（adulthood）。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个专家都曾是个___。A 项意为"赢家"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"初学者"；D 项意为"领导者"。常言道"Every expert was once a beginner"（每个专家都曾是新手），鼓励勇敢尝试。故选 C。
