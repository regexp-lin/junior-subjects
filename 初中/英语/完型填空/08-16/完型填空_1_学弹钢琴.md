# 中考英语完形填空：学弹钢琴

## 完形填空原文

Last month, my parents bought me a keyboard as a birthday gift. I had always wanted to learn how to play the piano. When the big box arrived, I could not wait to ___1___ it and see my new instrument.

My mother found a piano teacher in our community. Her name was Mrs Chen, and she had been teaching for over twenty years. On my first lesson, she taught me how to sit correctly and place my fingers on the keys. "Relax your shoulders," she said. "Playing the piano should feel natural, not ___2___."

At first, I thought it would be easy. But when I tried to play a simple tune, my fingers would not move the ___3___ I wanted. The notes sounded wrong, and I felt frustrated. Mrs Chen smiled and said, "Everyone starts this way. You need to ___4___ every day. Even ten minutes of practice can make a difference."

So I practised every afternoon after school. I started with easy exercises — just pressing one key at a time. Slowly, I learned to play "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star." When I finally played it without ___5___, I was so proud that I called my grandmother to listen over the phone.

As weeks passed, I began to enjoy the ___6___ of practice. The piano was no longer a difficult task but a way to express my feelings. When I felt sad, I played slower pieces. When I was happy, I played faster ones.

Last weekend, Mrs Chen organised a small ___7___ for her students. I was nervous because I would play in front of others. But when I sat down at the piano and began to play, I forgot my nerves. The music carried me away. After I finished, everyone clapped. My parents had big ___8___ on their faces.

Learning the piano has taught me that nothing comes without effort. I used to give up easily when things were hard. Now I understand that ___9___ is the key to success. I will keep practising and hope to play more beautiful ___10___ in the future.

1. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. close &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
2. A. comfortable &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
3. A. way &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. place &emsp;&emsp; D. reason

6. A. difficulty &emsp;&emsp; B. process &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. question
7. A. party &emsp;&emsp; B. concert &emsp;&emsp; C. meeting &emsp;&emsp; D. trip
8. A. tears &emsp;&emsp; B. smiles &emsp;&emsp; C. worries &emsp;&emsp; D. fears
9. A. talent &emsp;&emsp; B. luck &emsp;&emsp; C. patience &emsp;&emsp; D. money
10. A. piano &emsp;&emsp; B. teacher &emsp;&emsp; C. music &emsp;&emsp; D. keyboard

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我等不及要___它，看看我的新乐器。A 项意为"打开"；B 项意为"关闭"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"清洁"。收到礼物后迫不及待想打开盒子，"open"正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：弹钢琴应该感觉自然，而不是___。A 项意为"舒服的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"疲倦的"；D 项意为"奇怪的/不自然的"。与 natural 相对，表示不应感觉别扭，"strange"正确。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手指不能按我想要的___移动。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"地点"；D 项意为"原因"。"the way I wanted"意为"我想要的方式"，"way"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你需要每天___。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"倾听"。学习乐器需要每天练习，"practise"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我终于能毫无___地弹奏时。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"音乐"；D 项意为"休息"。"without mistakes"意为"没有错误地/完美地弹奏"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始享受练习的___。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"过程"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"问题"。"the process of practice"意为"练习的过程"，享受过程。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师为她学生们组织了一场小型___。A 项意为"聚会"；B 项意为"音乐会"；C 项意为"会议"；D 项意为"旅行"。钢琴学生上台演奏应是音乐会，"concert"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我父母脸上露出大大的___。A 项意为"眼泪"；B 项意为"笑容"；C 项意为"担忧"；D 项意为"恐惧"。孩子演奏成功父母高兴，"smiles"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我现在理解___是成功的关键。A 项意为"天赋"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"耐心"；D 项意为"金钱"。学钢琴需要坚持不懈，耐心是关键词，"patience"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望将来能弹奏更美妙的___。A 项意为"钢琴"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"音乐"；D 项意为"键盘"。"play music"意为"演奏音乐"，"music"正确。故选 C。
