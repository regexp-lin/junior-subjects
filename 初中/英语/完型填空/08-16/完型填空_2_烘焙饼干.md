# 中考英语完形填空：烘焙饼干

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my cousin Amy came to visit us. She is two years older than me and loves cooking. She suggested that we bake chocolate chip cookies together. I had never baked before, but I was ___1___ to try.

First, we gathered all the ingredients: flour, butter, sugar, eggs, and chocolate chips. Amy showed me how to measure everything correctly. "Baking is like science," she said. "You must follow the ___2___ carefully, or the cookies will not turn out right."

We mixed the butter and sugar in a large bowl. Then we added the eggs one at a time. I stirred the mixture while Amy added the flour. The dough became thick and sticky. "Now we need to add the chocolate chips and ___3___ everything well," Amy said.

We used two spoons to place small balls of dough on the baking tray. Amy said we should leave some space between each cookie because they would ___4___ in the oven. I placed them too close at first, and Amy laughed. "They will stick together if we leave them like that!"

We put the tray into the preheated oven and set the timer for twelve minutes. While we waited, we washed the bowls and spoons. The kitchen soon smelled ___5___. I could not wait to taste the cookies.

When the timer rang, we took the tray out. The cookies looked golden and perfect. But Amy said we must let them cool for a few minutes. "If you eat them too soon, they will be too soft and might ___6___ you," she warned.

Finally, we could try them. The first bite was amazing — crispy on the outside and soft inside, with sweet chocolate in every bite. My little brother ran into the kitchen when he ___7___ the smell. He ate three cookies in a row and asked for more.

Amy said she would teach me how to bake banana bread next time. I am ___8___ that baking is not as hard as I thought. With a good recipe and a little patience, anyone can make delicious treats at home.

I wrapped some cookies in a nice box to ___9___ to my grandparents. They called the next day and said the cookies were the best they had ever tasted. That made me feel so ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未烘焙过，但我___尝试。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"疲倦的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。第一次尝试新事物感到兴奋，"excited"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你必须仔细遵守___，否则饼干会做不好。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"食谱"；C 项意为"图片"；D 项意为"故事"。"follow the recipe"意为"按照食谱操作"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我们需要加入巧克力豆并___均匀。A 项意为"混合"；B 项意为"分开"；C 项意为"扔掉"；D 项意为"弄碎"。"mix everything well"意为"把所有材料混合均匀"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个饼干之间要留些空间，因为它们会在烤箱里___。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"收缩"；C 项意为"膨胀"；D 项意为"烤焦"。饼干烘烤时会膨胀变大，"expand"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房很快闻起来___。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美妙的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"酸的"。烤饼干的香气宜人，"wonderful"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果太早吃，它们会太软，可能会___你。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"烫伤/弄伤"；C 项意为"取悦"；D 项意为"使惊讶"。刚出炉的饼干烫嘴可能烫伤，"hurt"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：弟弟闻___香味跑进厨房。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"听到"；C 项意为"感觉"；D 项意为"闻到"。"smell the smell"意为"闻到气味"。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___烘焙没有我想的那么难。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"高兴的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"担心的"。"glad"表示高兴、欣慰。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把一些饼干装进漂亮的盒子里___给祖父母。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"借"；C 项意为"送"；D 项意为"展示"。"give to"意为"送给"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那让我感到非常___。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"孤单的"；D 项意为"困倦的"。得到夸奖感到骄傲，"proud"正确。故选 B。
