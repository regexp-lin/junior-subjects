# 中考英语完形填空：学做炸年糕

## 完形填空原文

During the Spring Festival, my grandmother decided to teach me how to make fried rice cakes. I was very ___1___ because I had never tried cooking before.

First, we needed to prepare the ___2___. Grandma cut the rice cake into thin pieces. Then she told me to heat some oil in a pan. "Wait until the oil is hot," she said. "But don't let it get too ___3___, or the cakes will burn."

When the oil was ready, I carefully put the rice cake pieces into the pan. They made a ___4___ sound. I was a little scared, but Grandma smiled and said, "That's normal. Just turn them over when one side turns golden."

I used a spatula to ___5___ the pieces. Some of them stuck together at first. Grandma helped me separate them. "Be patient," she said. "Cooking takes ___6___."

After a few minutes, the rice cakes became crispy and golden. We added some salt and a little sugar. Grandma said the secret was to add the seasoning at the right ___7___. Finally, we put the fried rice cakes on a plate. They looked delicious and ___8___ good.

I took a bite. It was even better than I had ___9___. "Grandma, you're the best cook!" I said. She laughed and patted my head. "Next time, you can try to make it by ___10___. Practice makes perfect."

## 选项

1. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
2. A. ingredients &emsp;&emsp; B. tools &emsp;&emsp; C. dishes &emsp;&emsp; D. recipes
3. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. hot &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. cool
4. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. loud &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. soft
5. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. cut &emsp;&emsp; C. turn &emsp;&emsp; D. wash
6. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. help
7. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. way &emsp;&emsp; C. moment &emsp;&emsp; D. reason
8. A. smelled &emsp;&emsp; B. felt &emsp;&emsp; C. sounded &emsp;&emsp; D. looked
9. A. hoped &emsp;&emsp; B. expected &emsp;&emsp; C. believed &emsp;&emsp; D. imagined
10. A. yourself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. myself &emsp;&emsp; D. ourselves

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常兴奋，因为我以前从未尝试过做饭。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"担心的"。第一次学做菜，心情应是"兴奋的"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我们需要准备食材。A 项意为"食材"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"盘子"；D 项意为"食谱"。下文提到切年糕、热油等，都是准备食材的步骤。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但不要让它太热，否则年糕会烧焦。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"热的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"凉的"。油太热会导致食物烧焦，与 or the cakes will burn 对应。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们发出很大的声响。A 项意为"安静的"；B 项意为"响亮的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"轻柔的"。油炸食物入锅时会发出滋滋响声，a loud sound 符合常识。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用铲子翻动年糕片。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"切"；C 项意为"翻动"；D 项意为"洗"。后文 "turn them over when one side turns golden" 提示应选"翻动"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说，做饭需要时间。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"帮助"。"Be patient" 暗示需要等待，与 "takes time" 搭配。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说秘诀是在正确的时机加调料。A 项意为"地方"；B 项意为"方式"；C 项意为"时刻、时机"；D 项意为"原因"。at the right moment 意为"在恰当的时机"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们看起来很好吃，闻起来也很香。A 项意为"闻起来"；B 项意为"摸起来"；C 项意为"听起来"；D 项意为"看起来"。食物"闻起来香"用 smell good，且前文已有 looked。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比我想象的还要好吃。A 项意为"希望"；B 项意为"预料、期望"；C 项意为"相信"；D 项意为"想象"。expect 可表示对味道的预期，常用于比较级结构。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 反身代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次你可以自己试着做。A 项意为"你自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"我自己"；D 项意为"我们自己"。奶奶对"我"说话，应鼓励"你"独立完成，用 by yourself。故选 A。
