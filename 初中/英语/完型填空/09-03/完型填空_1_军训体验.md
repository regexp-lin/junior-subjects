# 中考英语完形填空：军训体验

## 完形填空原文

Every September, our school holds a week-long military training for new students. This year, it was my turn. I had heard many stories about it — some said it was ___1___, while others said it was a valuable experience. I decided to keep an open mind.

On the first morning, we gathered on the playground at six o'clock. Our instructor was a young soldier named Captain Zhang. He stood straight like a tree and spoke in a loud, clear ___2___. "From today, you are no longer ordinary students. You are soldiers," he said. We all stood a little taller after hearing his words.

The training began with basic moves: standing at attention, turning left and right, and marching. These seemed simple, but doing them ___3___ for a long time was exhausting. The sun was burning hot, and sweat ran down our faces. Some students felt dizzy and had to sit down. I wanted to give up too, but I looked around and saw my classmates still ___4___. I told myself to hold on.

The afternoons were slightly easier. We learned to fold our quilts into neat squares, which the soldiers called "tofu blocks." My first attempt looked more like a ___5___ than a block. But after practising many times, I finally got it right. Captain Zhang nodded with approval, and I felt a sense of achievement.

The most memorable part was the night march on the fourth day. We walked five kilometres in the dark with only the stars above us. Nobody spoke. The only ___6___ was our footsteps on the ground. I felt tired, but also strangely peaceful. Walking together in silence created a strong ___7___ among us.

By the end of the week, we were different people. We could stand perfectly still for thirty minutes. We could march in perfect formation. More importantly, we had learned the value of ___8___. When one person made a mistake, the whole team had to start over. We learned to support and encourage each other.

At the closing ceremony, Captain Zhang gave a short speech. "You have all done well," he said. "Remember what you learned here: when things get tough, don't ___9___." Many students, including me, had tears in our eyes.

Military training was one of the most ___10___ experiences of my school life. It taught me discipline, teamwork and perseverance. I will carry these lessons with me forever.

## 选项

1. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxing &emsp;&emsp; D. funny
2. A. voice &emsp;&emsp; B. letter &emsp;&emsp; C. song &emsp;&emsp; D. story
3. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. secretly &emsp;&emsp; C. correctly &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
4. A. resting &emsp;&emsp; B. leaving &emsp;&emsp; C. trying &emsp;&emsp; D. sleeping
5. A. ball &emsp;&emsp; B. pillow &emsp;&emsp; C. hat &emsp;&emsp; D. cloud
6. A. light &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. sound &emsp;&emsp; D. colour
7. A. wall &emsp;&emsp; B. bond &emsp;&emsp; C. bridge &emsp;&emsp; D. fence
8. A. competition &emsp;&emsp; B. independence &emsp;&emsp; C. teamwork &emsp;&emsp; D. privacy
9. A. stand up &emsp;&emsp; B. give up &emsp;&emsp; C. grow up &emsp;&emsp; D. look up
10. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. disappointing &emsp;&emsp; C. confusing &emsp;&emsp; D. meaningful

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有人说军训很___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"可怕的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。与后面说"valuable experience"形成对比，有人觉得很辛苦可怕，"terrible"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他用洪亮清晰的___说话。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"信"；C 项意为"歌"；D 项意为"故事"。教官说话的声音，"voice"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：长时间___地做这些动作很累人。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"秘密地"；C 项意为"正确地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。军训要求动作标准正确，"correctly"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到同学们仍在___。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"努力/坚持"；D 项意为"睡觉"。看到同学还在坚持给了自己动力，"trying"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我第一次叠的看起来更像一朵___而不是方块。A 项意为"球"；B 项意为"枕头"；C 项意为"帽子"；D 项意为"云朵"。被子叠得蓬松不成形，像云朵，"cloud"正确。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：唯一的___是脚步声。A 项意为"光"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"声音"；D 项意为"颜色"。没人说话，只有脚步声，"sound"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一起沉默行走在我们之间建立了一种强烈的___。A 项意为"墙"；B 项意为"纽带/联系"；C 项意为"桥"；D 项意为"栅栏"。共同经历增进了同学间的感情纽带，"bond"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学到了___的价值。A 项意为"竞争"；B 项意为"独立"；C 项意为"团队合作"；D 项意为"隐私"。一人犯错全队重来，这体现团队合作，"teamwork"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：困难时不要___。A 项意为"站起来"；B 项意为"放弃"；C 项意为"长大"；D 项意为"向上看"。教官鼓励不要放弃，"give up"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：军训是我学校生活中最___的经历之一。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"令人失望的"；C 项意为"令人困惑的"；D 项意为"有意义的"。总结军训的深远意义，"meaningful"正确。故选 D。
