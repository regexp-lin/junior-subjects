# 中考英语完形填空：认识新同桌

## 完形填空原文

When the new term started, our teacher rearranged the seating. I was placed next to a girl I had never spoken to before. Her name was Wang Mei. She was quiet and always kept to ___1___. I wondered if we would get along.

On the first day, I tried to start a conversation. "Hi, I'm Li Hua. Nice to meet you," I said. She looked up from her book and gave me a small ___2___. "Nice to meet you too," she replied softly. Then she went back to reading. I felt a little disappointed, but I did not give up.

Over the next few days, I noticed that Wang Mei was very ___3___ in class. She took careful notes and always answered the teacher's questions correctly. However, she rarely talked to anyone during breaks. She would sit at her desk, either reading or drawing in a small notebook.

One afternoon, I accidentally saw her drawings. They were ___4___ — beautiful sketches of animals, flowers and landscapes. "Wow, these are amazing!" I said. Her face turned red, but I could tell she was pleased. "Thank you. I've been drawing since I was little," she said. That was the longest sentence she had said to me.

From that day on, we began to talk more. I learned that Wang Mei was not unfriendly — she was just ___5___. She found it hard to start conversations with people she did not know well. But once she felt comfortable, she was actually very warm and ___6___.

We started helping each other with our studies. She was excellent at maths and science, while I was better at English and Chinese. We would ___7___ our knowledge during breaks and study time. This arrangement benefited both of us, and our grades improved.

Wang Mei also taught me to draw. I was terrible at first — my cat looked like a potato. She laughed and said, "Everyone starts somewhere. Just keep ___8___." With her patient guidance, my drawings slowly got better. Drawing became a hobby I truly enjoyed.

By the end of the month, Wang Mei and I had become close friends. I realised that first ___9___ can be misleading. Someone who seems cold and distant might actually be kind and talented. You just need to take the time to get to know them.

Our teacher once said, "A good deskmate is a treasure." I completely ___10___ with her. I am lucky to have Wang Mei as my deskmate and friend.

## 选项

1. A. himself &emsp;&emsp; B. themselves &emsp;&emsp; C. herself &emsp;&emsp; D. myself
2. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. smile &emsp;&emsp; C. note &emsp;&emsp; D. test
3. A. lazy &emsp;&emsp; B. careless &emsp;&emsp; C. noisy &emsp;&emsp; D. hardworking
4. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
5. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. shy &emsp;&emsp; C. rude &emsp;&emsp; D. selfish
6. A. humorous &emsp;&emsp; B. strict &emsp;&emsp; C. cold &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
7. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. waste &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
8. A. waiting &emsp;&emsp; B. complaining &emsp;&emsp; C. copying &emsp;&emsp; D. practising
9. A. impressions &emsp;&emsp; B. lessons &emsp;&emsp; C. choices &emsp;&emsp; D. exams
10. A. argued &emsp;&emsp; B. agreed &emsp;&emsp; C. compared &emsp;&emsp; D. competed

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 反身代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她总是独来独往。A 项意为"他自己"；B 项意为"他们自己"；C 项意为"她自己"；D 项意为"我自己"。主语是she，"kept to herself"意为"独处"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她从书中抬头给了我一个小小的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"微笑"；C 项意为"便条"；D 项意为"测试"。礼貌地微笑回应，"smile"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王梅在课堂上非常___。A 项意为"懒惰的"；B 项意为"粗心的"；C 项意为"吵闹的"；D 项意为"勤奋的"。认真做笔记、正确回答问题，"hardworking"正确。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们___——精美的动物、花卉和风景素描。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"精彩的"；D 项意为"简单的"。后文赞叹"amazing"，"wonderful"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她不是不友好——她只是___。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"害羞的"；C 项意为"粗鲁的"；D 项意为"自私的"。不善于与陌生人交流是害羞，"shy"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一旦她感到舒适，她其实很温暖而且___。A 项意为"幽默的"；B 项意为"严格的"；C 项意为"冷漠的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。与warm并列的积极形容词，"humorous"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们会在课间和学习时间___知识。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"忘记"。互相帮助学习即分享知识，"share"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都有起步阶段，继续___就好。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"抄写"；D 项意为"练习"。画得不好需要多练习，"practising"正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一___可能具有误导性。A 项意为"印象"；B 项意为"课程"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"考试"。"first impressions"意为"第一印象"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我完全___她的话。A 项意为"争论"；B 项意为"同意"；C 项意为"比较"；D 项意为"竞争"。"agree with"意为"同意"。故选 B。
