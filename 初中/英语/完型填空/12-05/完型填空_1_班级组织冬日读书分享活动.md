# 中考英语完形填空：班级组织冬日读书分享活动

## 完形填空原文

Our Chinese teacher believes that reading is like a warm fire in winter—it gives light and warmth to our minds. So she organized a "Winter Reading Sharing" event for our class last Thursday afternoon.

Each student had to choose a ___1___ they had read recently and share it with the class. We had one week to prepare. I chose "The Old Man and the Sea" because the story deeply ___2___ me. I prepared a short speech and made a poster with some key quotes from the book.

When the event began, the first student to share was Li Na. She talked about a science fiction novel about space travel. Her presentation was so ___3___ that everyone listened carefully. She even brought a model spaceship she had made herself to show us.

Then it was my turn. I walked to the front of the classroom with my poster. I told my classmates about the old fisherman Santiago and his ___4___ to catch a big fish. I explained that the book taught me to never give up, even when things seem ___5___. Some classmates said they wanted to read the book after hearing my sharing.

The most surprising presentation came from Zhang Wei. He shared a book of Chinese poems. Most of us thought poetry was ___6___, but Zhang Wei read the poems with such feeling that we were all moved. He showed us that poems can express deep emotions in just a few ___7___.

After everyone finished, our teacher asked us to ___8___ for the best presentation. Li Na won first place, but we all felt like winners. Our teacher said, "Books open doors to different ___9___. The more you read, the wider your view becomes."

This event made me realize that every book has something valuable to ___10___. I have already started making a reading list for the winter holiday.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个同学要选一本最近读过的书分享。A 项意为"歌曲"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"电影"。读书分享活动选 book。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个故事深深触动了我。A 项意为"使无聊"；B 项意为"伤害"；C 项意为"触动"；D 项意为"使困惑"。deeply touched me 意为"深深打动了我"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的展示非常生动，大家都认真听。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"生动的"。vivid 使人认真倾听。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我讲述了老渔夫Santiago捕大鱼的奋斗。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"奋斗/挣扎"；C 项意为"能力"；D 项意为"恐惧"。《老人与海》核心是 struggle。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使事情看起来没有希望也不放弃。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"无望的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。never give up even when hopeless。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大多数人觉得诗歌很无聊。A 项意为"令人兴奋的"；B 项意为"枯燥的"；C 项意为"有用的"；D 项意为"现代的"。与后文转折 but 对应，之前认为 dull。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：诗歌能用寥寥几个字表达深刻情感。A 项意为"页"；B 项意为"字/词"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"图片"。诗歌特点是 a few words 表达情感。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们投票选出最佳展示。A 项意为"支付"；B 项意为"投票"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"打架"。vote for 意为"投票支持"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：书籍打开了通向不同世界的大门。A 项意为"世界"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"危险"；D 项意为"考试"。Books open doors to different worlds。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每本书都有宝贵的东西可以提供。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"隐藏"；C 项意为"提供"；D 项意为"毁坏"。has something valuable to offer。故选 C。
