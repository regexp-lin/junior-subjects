# 中考英语完形填空：学做毛线手套

## 完形填空原文

As December arrived, the weather became colder and colder. My grandmother noticed that my hands were always red and cold when I came home from school. She said, "Let me teach you how to ___1___ a pair of woolen gloves. Handmade gloves are warmer than the ones from the store."

I had never knitted anything before, so I was a bit ___2___. Grandmother said it was not as hard as I thought. She took me to a craft shop and we chose a ball of soft blue yarn. She also bought a pair of knitting needles for beginners—they were ___3___ than the normal ones, which made them easier to hold.

Back at home, Grandmother sat beside me and showed me the basic stitches. The first stitch was called the "knit stitch." She moved the needles slowly so I could ___4___ every step. I tried to copy her movements, but my fingers felt clumsy and the yarn kept getting ___5___.

After an hour of practice, I could finally do the knit stitch without making mistakes. Grandmother said, "Good! Now let me teach you the purl stitch. With these two stitches, you can make ___6___ anything." I practiced the purl stitch for another hour until my fingers were sore.

Over the next five days, I spent thirty minutes each evening knitting. Slowly, a small piece of fabric ___7___ under my needles. Grandmother helped me shape it into a glove. When I finished the first one, it was not perfect—one finger was a little longer than the ___8___. But Grandmother said it was wonderful for a first try.

It took me three more days to finish the second glove. When I wore the pair to school, my hands stayed warm all day. My classmates were ___9___ when I told them I made the gloves myself. Some of them even asked me to teach them.

Knitting taught me that learning a new skill takes time and ___10___. But the reward is worth every minute of effort.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我教你织一双毛线手套。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"借"；C 项意为"织"；D 项意为"扔"。knit a pair of woolen gloves 意为"织毛线手套"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没织过东西，所以有点担心。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"生气的"。第一次做会 worried。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：初学者用的织针比普通的粗，更容易握。A 项意为"更细的"；B 项意为"更短的"；C 项意为"更粗的"；D 项意为"更锋利的"。初学者用 thicker 针方便操作。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她慢慢演示，这样我能跟上每一步。A 项意为"跳过"；B 项意为"跟随"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"忘记"。follow every step 意为"跟上每一步"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：毛线老是缠在一起。A 项意为"干净的"；B 项意为"缠绕的"；C 项意为"干的"；D 项意为"笔直的"。初学者织毛线会 tangled（缠绕）。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：会了这两种针法，几乎什么都能织。A 项意为"几乎不"；B 项意为"从不"；C 项意为"几乎"；D 项意为"很少"。almost anything 意为"几乎任何东西"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一小块织物在我的针下慢慢长大。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"长大/增长"；C 项意为"破裂"；D 项意为"掉落"。织物逐渐 grew。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有一个手指比其他的长一点。A 项意为"其他的"；B 项意为"袜子"；C 项意为"手臂"；D 项意为"针"。one finger longer than the others。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们知道我自己做的手套时很惊讶。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"伤心的"。自制手套让人 surprised。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学习新技能需要时间和耐心。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"沉默"；D 项意为"耐心"。time and patience 是学技能的关键。故选 D。
