# 中考英语完形填空：学做月饼

## 完形填空原文

Before the Mid-Autumn Festival this year, our school organised a special activity — making mooncakes by hand. The cooking teacher, Mrs. Huang, would teach us the traditional method. I had never made mooncakes before and was very ___1___ to try.

On the day of the activity, we gathered in the school kitchen. Mrs. Huang had prepared all the ingredients on the tables: flour, sugar, oil, and various ___2___ such as red bean paste, lotus seed paste and salted egg yolks. She also brought out wooden moulds with beautiful patterns carved on them.

Mrs. Huang first demonstrated the process step by step. She mixed the flour with sugar and oil to make the dough. "The dough must be soft but not sticky," she explained. "If you add too much oil, it will fall ___3___. If you add too little, it will be too dry." I watched carefully and took mental notes.

Next came the filling. She took a small ball of red bean paste, placed it in the centre of a flattened piece of dough, and carefully ___4___ the dough around it. "Make sure there are no holes, or the filling will leak out during baking," she warned. Then she pressed the ball into the mould and gently tapped it out. A perfectly shaped mooncake appeared, with a ___5___ flower pattern on top.

Now it was our turn. I rolled up my sleeves and started working. The dough felt strange in my hands — smooth and slightly oily. My first attempt was a disaster. The dough was too thick on one side and too ___6___ on the other. When I pressed it into the mould, the pattern was uneven. Mrs. Huang came over and said, "Don't worry. It takes practice. Try to ___7___ the dough evenly."

I tried again, this time more carefully. My second mooncake was much better, though still not as good as Mrs. Huang's. By my fourth attempt, I had gotten the hang of it. I made mooncakes with different fillings and even ___8___ to make one with a salted egg yolk in the centre. That was the hardest type because the yolk was fragile and easy to break.

After we finished, Mrs. Huang baked all the mooncakes in the oven. The ___9___ smell filled the entire building. When the mooncakes were done, they looked golden and professional. I could hardly believe I had made them myself.

I took my mooncakes home and shared them with my family. My grandmother said they tasted just like the ones she used to make when she was young. That was the best ___10___ I could have received.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没做过月饼，非常___想试试。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"伤心的"；D 项意为"生气的"。第一次尝试新事物感到兴奋，"excited"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：各种___如红豆沙、莲蓉和咸蛋黄。A 项意为"饮料"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"馅料"；D 项意为"盘子"。红豆沙、莲蓉、蛋黄都是月饼的馅料，"fillings"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：油放太多，面团会___。A 项意为"散开"；B 项意为"睡着"；C 项意为"落后"；D 项意为"翻倒"。"fall apart"意为"散架/碎裂"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小心地把面皮___在馅料周围。A 项意为"切"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"包裹"；D 项意为"烧"。用面皮包裹馅料，"wrapped"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：上面有___的花纹。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"可爱的/美丽的"；C 项意为"暗的"；D 项意为"破碎的"。模具压出的花纹漂亮，"lovely"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一边太厚另一边太___。A 项意为"厚的"；B 项意为"薄的"；C 项意为"长的"；D 项意为"宽的"。与thick对应的是thin，"thin"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：试着把面团___均匀。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"展开/铺平"；C 项意为"冷冻"；D 项意为"涂"。把面团均匀展开，"spread"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我甚至___做了一个有咸蛋黄的。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"成功做到"。"managed to"意为"成功做到"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___的香味弥漫了整栋楼。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美妙的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"酸的"。烘焙月饼的香味美好，"wonderful"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是我能收到的最好的___。A 项意为"惩罚"；B 项意为"警告"；C 项意为"称赞/赞美"；D 项意为"问题"。奶奶的评价是最好的赞美，"compliment"正确。故选 C。
