# 中考英语完形填空：帮助转学生

## 完形填空原文

A new student joined our class last week. His name was Zhang Wei, and he had just moved to our city from a small town in Yunnan. He looked nervous and did not talk to anyone on his first day. I could tell he was feeling ___1___.

During the lunch break, I noticed Zhang Wei sitting alone in the corner of the canteen. He was eating quietly and staring at his plate. I remembered how I had felt when I transferred to a new school two years ago — scared, lonely and ___2___. I decided to go over and talk to him.

"Hi, I'm Li Hua. Would you like to sit with us?" I asked, pointing to my group of friends. He looked ___3___ for a moment, then smiled and said, "Sure, thank you." He picked up his tray and followed me to our table.

My friends were welcoming. They asked Zhang Wei about his hometown, his hobbies and his favourite subjects. He told us that he loved nature and had often gone hiking in the ___4___ near his old home. "The scenery there is amazing," he said, his eyes lighting up. "I miss it sometimes." We could hear a touch of homesickness in his voice.

Over the next few days, I tried to help Zhang Wei ___5___ to his new school. I showed him where the library, the sports hall and the teachers' offices were. I also introduced him to other classmates. Some of them became his friends quickly, especially those who shared his interest in outdoor ___6___.

The biggest challenge for Zhang Wei was the coursework. The teaching pace here was faster than at his old school, and he struggled with maths and English. I offered to study with him after school. We would meet in the library and go through the difficult problems together. He was a fast ___7___ and caught up within a few weeks.

One day, Zhang Wei brought a bag of dried wild mushrooms from Yunnan. "My mother sent these from home," he said. "They are a ___8___ in our town. Please take some as a thank-you gift." I was touched by his thoughtfulness.

By the end of the month, Zhang Wei had become a popular member of our class. He was no longer the quiet boy sitting alone. He participated actively in class discussions and even ___9___ for the school basketball team.

Helping Zhang Wei made me realise that a small act of kindness can make a huge ___10___ in someone's life. All it takes is a warm smile and a simple "Would you like to join us?"

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. lucky
2. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. unsure &emsp;&emsp; C. patient &emsp;&emsp; D. grateful
3. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointed
4. A. deserts &emsp;&emsp; B. oceans &emsp;&emsp; C. mountains &emsp;&emsp; D. cities
5. A. object &emsp;&emsp; B. adapt &emsp;&emsp; C. return &emsp;&emsp; D. compare
6. A. activities &emsp;&emsp; B. furniture &emsp;&emsp; C. clothing &emsp;&emsp; D. homework
7. A. runner &emsp;&emsp; B. speaker &emsp;&emsp; C. learner &emsp;&emsp; D. driver
8. A. secret &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. mistake &emsp;&emsp; D. speciality
9. A. looked out &emsp;&emsp; B. tried out &emsp;&emsp; C. turned out &emsp;&emsp; D. found out
10. A. mess &emsp;&emsp; B. noise &emsp;&emsp; C. difference &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能看出他感到___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"迷茫的/不知所措的"；D 项意为"幸运的"。新环境中不和任何人说话，感到迷茫，"lost"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：害怕、孤独和___。A 项意为"勇敢的"；B 项意为"不确定的"；C 项意为"耐心的"；D 项意为"感激的"。转学初期对一切不确定，"unsure"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他看起来___了一会儿，然后微笑着说。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"失望的"。没想到有人主动邀请，感到惊讶，"surprised"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他经常在老家附近的___徒步。A 项意为"沙漠"；B 项意为"海洋"；C 项意为"山"；D 项意为"城市"。云南多山，适合在山中徒步，"mountains"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试着帮助张伟___新学校。A 项意为"反对"；B 项意为"适应"；C 项意为"返回"；D 项意为"比较"。"adapt to"意为"适应"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尤其是那些喜欢户外___的人。A 项意为"活动"；B 项意为"家具"；C 项意为"衣服"；D 项意为"作业"。前文提到张伟喜欢自然和徒步，"activities"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他是一个快速的___。A 项意为"跑步者"；B 项意为"演讲者"；C 项意为"学习者"；D 项意为"司机"。几周内就跟上了学习进度，学得快，"learner"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我们那里的___。A 项意为"秘密"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"特产"。云南野生菌是当地特产，"speciality"正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他甚至___了校篮球队。A 项意为"小心/注意"；B 项意为"参加选拔"；C 项意为"结果是"；D 项意为"发现"。"tried out for"意为"参加...的选拔"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小小的善举可以在某人生命中产生巨大的___。A 项意为"混乱"；B 项意为"噪音"；C 项意为"不同/影响"；D 项意为"错误"。"make a difference"意为"产生影响"。故选 C。
