# 中考英语完形填空：雨天的足球赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school football team had been preparing for the district competition for two months. When the big day finally came, the weather was ___1___. Dark clouds covered the sky and rain began to fall just before the match started. Some of my teammates looked worried, but our coach said, "Rain or shine, we play."

The pitch was slippery and the ball was hard to ___2___. In the first ten minutes, I slipped twice and nearly fell. The other team was strong and fast. They scored the first goal in the fifteenth minute. Our spirits dropped, and during the break, nobody spoke.

Coach Wang gathered us in a circle. "Listen," he said, "the rain is the same for both teams. The team that ___3___ better to the conditions will win." His words were simple but powerful. We nodded and ran back onto the field with renewed energy.

In the second half, we changed our ___4___. Instead of trying long passes, we kept the ball close and made short, quick passes. This worked much better on the wet ground. Slowly, we began to control the game. In the fifty-fifth minute, my friend Xiao Chen got the ball near the goal and kicked it hard. The goalkeeper ___5___, but the ball went in. We were tied at 1-1!

The crowd cheered loudly. I could see my parents in the stands, clapping and shouting. Their support gave us extra ___6___. We pushed forward even harder. With only five minutes left, I received a pass on the right side. I looked up and saw a gap between two defenders. Without thinking, I kicked the ball with all my strength. It curved through the air and flew into the ___7___ corner of the goal. 2-1!

The final whistle blew moments later. We had won! My teammates rushed towards me, and we fell into a muddy pile, laughing and crying at the same time. Coach Wang ran over and hugged us all. "I am so ___8___ of you," he said.

That rainy afternoon taught me an important lesson. Difficult conditions do not decide the result — your ___9___ does. We could have given up when the weather turned bad and we fell behind. But we chose to fight. And in the end, we proved that hard work and teamwork can ___10___ any challenge.

## 选项

1. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. warm &emsp;&emsp; C. awful &emsp;&emsp; D. sunny
2. A. find &emsp;&emsp; B. control &emsp;&emsp; C. carry &emsp;&emsp; D. throw
3. A. adapts &emsp;&emsp; B. listens &emsp;&emsp; C. talks &emsp;&emsp; D. points
4. A. uniform &emsp;&emsp; B. captain &emsp;&emsp; C. strategy &emsp;&emsp; D. schedule
5. A. scored &emsp;&emsp; B. dived &emsp;&emsp; C. laughed &emsp;&emsp; D. agreed
6. A. homework &emsp;&emsp; B. pressure &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. motivation
7. A. top &emsp;&emsp; B. bottom &emsp;&emsp; C. middle &emsp;&emsp; D. outside
8. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. ashamed
9. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. size &emsp;&emsp; C. age &emsp;&emsp; D. attitude
10. A. avoid &emsp;&emsp; B. create &emsp;&emsp; C. overcome &emsp;&emsp; D. ignore

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到了比赛当天，天气很___。A 项意为"完美的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"糟糕的"；D 项意为"晴朗的"。后文说乌云密布、下雨，天气很糟糕，"awful"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：球场很滑，球很难___。A 项意为"找到"；B 项意为"控制"；C 项意为"搬运"；D 项意为"扔"。湿滑的场地上球难以控制，"control"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：更好地___条件的队伍会赢。A 项意为"适应"；B 项意为"听"；C 项意为"说"；D 项意为"指"。适应雨天条件的球队能获胜，"adapts"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下半场我们改变了___。A 项意为"制服"；B 项意为"队长"；C 项意为"策略"；D 项意为"日程"。从长传改为短传配合是改变战术策略，"strategy"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：守门员___了，但球还是进了。A 项意为"得分"；B 项意为"扑救/飞扑"；C 项意为"笑"；D 项意为"同意"。守门员飞扑扑救但没挡住，"dived"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的支持给了我们额外的___。A 项意为"作业"；B 项意为"压力"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"动力"。父母的加油助威给予了动力，"motivation"正确。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：球划过弧线飞入球门的___角。A 项意为"顶部"；B 项意为"底部"；C 项意为"中间"；D 项意为"外面"。弧线球飞入球门上角是精彩进球的经典描写，"top"正确。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太为你们___了。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"羞愧的"。"proud of"意为"为……骄傲"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：困难的条件不能决定结果——你的___才能。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"大小"；C 项意为"年龄"；D 项意为"态度"。面对困难的态度决定成败，"attitude"正确。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：努力和团队合作能___任何挑战。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"创造"；C 项意为"克服"；D 项意为"忽视"。"overcome challenges"意为"克服挑战"。故选 C。
