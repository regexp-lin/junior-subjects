# 中考英语完形填空：学做早餐

## 完形填空原文

My parents both work early in the morning, so they usually leave home before I wake up. For years, my breakfast was just bread and milk from the fridge. One day, I decided to learn to cook a ___1___ breakfast for myself.

I started with something simple — fried eggs. I watched a short video online and thought it looked easy. I heated the pan, added some oil, and cracked an egg into it. But the oil was too hot, and the egg started to spit and pop. I jumped back in ___2___ and almost dropped the spatula. The egg turned out burnt on the bottom and raw on top. It was terrible.

I did not give up. The next morning, I tried again. This time, I kept the heat ___3___ and waited patiently. The egg cooked evenly and looked much better. I added a pinch of salt and ate it with toast. It was not perfect, but it tasted good enough to make me smile.

Over the following weeks, I learned to make more dishes. I could make porridge, boiled eggs, and even simple sandwiches with vegetables and cheese. I also learned to make pancakes from a ___4___ my grandmother gave me. She told me the secret was to mix the batter until it was smooth, with no lumps.

Making breakfast every morning became a ___5___. I set my alarm fifteen minutes earlier so I would have enough time. At first, it was hard to get out of bed, but soon I got used to it. I found that eating a hot, home-made breakfast made me feel more ___6___ throughout the morning.

My mother was surprised and pleased when she found out. "I did not know you could cook!" she said. She bought me a small cookbook for beginners. I looked through it and ___7___ several recipes I wanted to try, including omelettes and fruit salad.

Cooking breakfast also taught me to be more ___8___. I had to plan what ingredients to buy, manage my time in the morning, and clean up after myself. These might seem like small things, but they helped me become more independent.

Now, I sometimes make breakfast for the whole family on weekends. My father always says my pancakes are the best. I know he is being kind, but his words still give me a warm ___9___ inside.

Learning to cook was one of the most useful things I did during middle school. It gave me a life skill that I will ___10___ forever. And it all started with one burnt egg.

3. A. high &emsp;&emsp; B. low &emsp;&emsp; C. off &emsp;&emsp; D. fast
4. A. recipe &emsp;&emsp; B. ticket &emsp;&emsp; C. letter &emsp;&emsp; D. photo
5. A. joke &emsp;&emsp; B. habit &emsp;&emsp; C. dream &emsp;&emsp; D. secret

7. A. threw away &emsp;&emsp; B. gave up &emsp;&emsp; C. turned down &emsp;&emsp; D. marked down
8. A. careless &emsp;&emsp; B. responsible &emsp;&emsp; C. popular &emsp;&emsp; D. humorous
9. A. pain &emsp;&emsp; B. feeling &emsp;&emsp; C. doubt &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
10. A. waste &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. question &emsp;&emsp; D. regret

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定学做一份___的早餐。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"像样的/正式的"；C 项意为"免费的"；D 项意为"快速的"。之前只吃面包牛奶，现在想做一份像样的早餐，"proper"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我吓得向后跳。A 项意为"惊讶"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"恐惧/惊吓"；D 项意为"羞耻"。油溅起来很吓人，"in fright"意为"惊恐地"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次我把火保持___。A 项意为"高"；B 项意为"低"；C 项意为"关"；D 项意为"快"。第一次火太大导致煎糊，这次用小火慢煎，"low"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了用奶奶给我的一份___做煎饼。A 项意为"食谱"；B 项意为"票"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"照片"。做煎饼需要食谱指导，"recipe"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天早上做早餐成了一种___。A 项意为"笑话"；B 项意为"习惯"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"秘密"。每天坚持做变成了习惯，"habit"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：吃热的自制早餐让我整个上午感觉更___。A 项意为"疲倦的"；B 项意为"困的"；C 项意为"精力充沛的"；D 项意为"饥饿的"。营养早餐让人精力充沛，"energetic"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我翻阅了它并___了几个想试的食谱。A 项意为"扔掉"；B 项意为"放弃"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"标记下来"。看到想做的食谱标记下来，"marked down"正确。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做早餐也教会了我更___。A 项意为"粗心的"；B 项意为"有责任心的"；C 项意为"受欢迎的"；D 项意为"幽默的"。后文提到计划食材、管理时间、收拾厨房，这些都是责任心的体现，"responsible"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话让我心里有一种温暖的___。A 项意为"疼痛"；B 项意为"感觉"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"恐惧"。爸爸的夸奖带来温暖的感觉，"feeling"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它给了我一项我将永远___的生活技能。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"保持/保留"；C 项意为"质疑"；D 项意为"后悔"。学到的技能终身受益，"keep"正确。故选 B。
