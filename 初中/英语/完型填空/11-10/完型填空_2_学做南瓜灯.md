# 中考英语完形填空：学做南瓜灯

## 完形填空原文

Halloween was coming. My cousin from America taught me how to make a pumpkin lantern. I had seen ___1___ in movies but never made one myself. I was eager to try.

First, we went to the market and bought a round pumpkin. My cousin said we should choose one that was ___2___ enough to stand steadily. We also needed a knife, a spoon, and a candle. Back home, we cut off the top of the pumpkin and used the spoon to ___3___ out the inside. The pumpkin flesh was soft and easy to remove. My hands got a bit sticky, but it was fun.

Next, we drew a face on the pumpkin with a pencil. We could make it scary or funny—it was our ___4___. I drew two triangle eyes and a smiling mouth with sharp teeth. Then we carefully cut along the lines with the knife. My cousin warned me to be ___5___ so that I wouldn't cut my fingers. It took us about half an hour to finish the carving.

After that, we put a small candle inside and lit it. When we turned ___6___ the lights, the pumpkin lantern glowed in the dark. It looked amazing! I placed it by the window. My little sister saw it and said it was the ___7___ pumpkin she had ever seen. Making the lantern was not as ___8___ as I had thought. With a little patience and creativity, anyone can do it. I plan to make another one next year. Maybe I can try a different ___9___ to make it even more special. This traditional activity made me understand more about Western culture. I think it's ___10___ to learn about different festivals around the world.

## 选项

1. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. one &emsp;&emsp; D. some
2. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. big &emsp;&emsp; C. flat &emsp;&emsp; D. round
3. A. dig &emsp;&emsp; B. pull &emsp;&emsp; C. take &emsp;&emsp; D. scoop
4. A. job &emsp;&emsp; B. turn &emsp;&emsp; C. choice &emsp;&emsp; D. chance
5. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. slow &emsp;&emsp; C. careful &emsp;&emsp; D. brave
6. A. off &emsp;&emsp; B. on &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down
7. A. ugliest &emsp;&emsp; B. smallest &emsp;&emsp; C. coolest &emsp;&emsp; D. oldest
8. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. long
9. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. size &emsp;&emsp; C. design &emsp;&emsp; D. material
10. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. useless &emsp;&emsp; C. important &emsp;&emsp; D. strange

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在电影里见过南瓜灯，但从没亲手做过。A 项意为"它"；B 项意为"它们"；C 项意为"一个"；D 项意为"一些"。指代前文 pumpkin lanterns，用 them。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要选一个够平的南瓜才能放稳。A 项意为"小的"；B 项意为"大的"；C 项意为"平的"；D 项意为"圆的"。stand steadily 需要底部 flat。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用勺子把里面挖空。A 项意为"挖"；B 项意为"拉"；C 项意为"拿"；D 项意为"舀"。scoop out 意为"挖出、舀出"。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：可以做成恐怖或搞笑的——这是我们的选择。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"轮流"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"机会"。it was our choice 意为"由我们决定"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表哥提醒我要小心，别切到手指。A 项意为"快的"；B 项意为"慢的"；C 项意为"小心的"；D 项意为"勇敢的"。用刀需 be careful。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们关掉灯后，南瓜灯在黑暗中发光。A 项意为"关"；B 项意为"开"；C 项意为"向上"；D 项意为"向下"。turn off the lights 意为"关灯"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹说这是她见过最酷的南瓜。A 项意为"最丑的"；B 项意为"最小的"；C 项意为"最酷的"；D 项意为"最老的"。赞美用 coolest。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做南瓜灯没有想象中那么难。A 项意为"容易"；B 项意为"困难"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"长"。not as hard as I thought 意为"没想象中难"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许可以试试不同的设计让它更特别。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"大小"；C 项意为"设计"；D 项意为"材料"。different design 意为"不同设计"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：了解世界各地的不同节日很重要。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无用的"；C 项意为"重要的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。学习文化用 important。故选 C。
