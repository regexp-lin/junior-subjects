# 中考英语完形填空：和同学策划主题班会

## 完形填空原文

Our class was going to hold a theme class meeting about "Thanksgiving". The head teacher asked several of us to ___1___ it together. I was excited because I had never organized such an activity before.

We had our first meeting last Monday. Five students sat around a table and shared ___2___. Someone suggested we could invite our parents to watch. Another thought we should make a short play. We wrote down all ideas and then voted. ___3___, we decided to have three parts: a speech, a group dance, and a thank-you card making session.

I was in charge of the speech. I needed to write something that could express our ___4___ to teachers and parents. I spent two evenings on it. When I read it to my group, they said it was moving. Lin Mei would lead the dance. She chose a song ___5___ "Grateful Heart". We practiced after school every day. Sometimes we argued about the steps, but we always found a way to ___6___ in the end.

On the day of the class meeting, everything went ___7___. Parents came and sat at the back. When we showed our thank-you cards, many parents had tears in their eyes. Our head teacher said we had done a wonderful job. This experience taught me that teamwork can make difficult tasks much ___8___. I also learned to appreciate the people around me more. I am already looking ___9___ to our next theme class meeting. I wonder what topic we will ___10___ next time.

3. A. First &emsp;&emsp; B. Finally &emsp;&emsp; C. Luckily &emsp;&emsp; D. However
4. A. thanks &emsp;&emsp; B. wishes &emsp;&emsp; C. dreams &emsp;&emsp; D. secrets
5. A. called &emsp;&emsp; B. named &emsp;&emsp; C. known &emsp;&emsp; D. known as
6. A. agree &emsp;&emsp; B. fight &emsp;&emsp; C. wait &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
7. A. wrong &emsp;&emsp; B. badly &emsp;&emsp; C. well &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任请我们几个人一起策划。A 项意为"计划；策划"；B 项意为"加入"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"离开"。plan 意为"策划、计划"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：五个同学围坐一桌分享想法。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"书"。策划会议分享 ideas。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最后我们决定分为三部分。A 项意为"首先"；B 项意为"最后"；C 项意为"幸运地"；D 项意为"然而"。讨论后得出结论用 Finally。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我需要写能表达对老师和家长感激之情的内容。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"祝愿"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"秘密"。感恩主题用 thanks。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她选了一首叫《感恩的心》的歌。A 项意为"叫；称作"；B 项意为"命名"；C 项意为"知道"；D 项意为"以……著称"。a song called 意为"一首名叫……的歌"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们为舞步争吵，但总能找到方法达成一致。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"打架"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"离开"。find a way to agree 意为"达成一致"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班会那天一切都进展顺利。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"糟糕地"；C 项意为"好"；D 项意为"慢地"。go well 意为"顺利进行"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：团队合作能让困难的任务变得简单得多。A 项意为"更难"；B 项意为"更容易"；C 项意为"更长"；D 项意为"更短"。teamwork 使任务 easier。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我已经期待下一次主题班会了。A 项意为"向上"；B 项意为"为了"；C 项意为"向前"；D 项意为"在"。look forward to 意为"期待"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不知下次我们会选什么主题。A 项意为"选择"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"记得"。choose topic 意为"选择主题"。故选 A。
