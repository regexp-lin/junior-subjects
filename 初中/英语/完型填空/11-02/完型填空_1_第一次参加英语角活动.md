# 中考英语完形填空：第一次参加英语角活动

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday afternoon, I went to the English Corner in our school for the first time. I had always wanted to ___1___ it, but I was too shy to speak English in front of others.

When I arrived, there were already about twenty students standing in small ___2___. They were talking about their favorite movies. A senior student came to me and said, "Welcome! Don't be nervous. Just enjoy ___3___ English."

At first, I just listened. Some students spoke English very ___4___, and I admired them a lot. Then a girl asked me, "What's your favorite movie?" I thought for a moment and ___5___, "I like The Lion King." She smiled and asked me why. I told her it was because the story taught me to be ___6___.

After that, I felt more comfortable. I started to talk with other students about music and sports. I made some ___7___ during the activity. We exchanged phone numbers and planned to practice English together.

The English Corner lasted for about an hour. When it was over, I didn't want to ___8___. I realized that speaking English is not as scary as I had imagined. The most important thing is to open your ___9___ and try.

On my way home, I felt very happy. I decided to go to the English Corner every week. I believe that with more practice, my spoken English will get ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. build &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. start &emsp;&emsp; D. watch
2. A. groups &emsp;&emsp; B. lines &emsp;&emsp; C. classes &emsp;&emsp; D. teams
3. A. writing &emsp;&emsp; B. reading &emsp;&emsp; C. speaking &emsp;&emsp; D. listening
4. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. fluently
5. A. asked &emsp;&emsp; B. answered &emsp;&emsp; C. shouted &emsp;&emsp; D. repeated
6. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. polite &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. quiet
7. A. plans &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; D. choices
8. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. move &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
9. A. eyes &emsp;&emsp; B. mind &emsp;&emsp; C. mouth &emsp;&emsp; D. heart
10. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. louder &emsp;&emsp; D. lower

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直想加入英语角，但太害羞了。A 项意为"建造"；B 项意为"加入"；C 项意为"开始"；D 项意为"观看"。join 意为"加入（活动/组织）"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大约二十个学生站成小组在讨论。A 项意为"小组"；B 项意为"队列"；C 项意为"班级"；D 项意为"团队"。in small groups 意为"分成小组"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：别紧张，享受说英语的过程。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"读"；C 项意为"说"；D 项意为"听"。英语角的核心是 speaking English。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一些同学英语说得非常流利，我很佩服。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"安静地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"流利地"。spoke fluently 令人佩服。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想了一下回答说。A 项意为"问"；B 项意为"回答"；C 项意为"喊"；D 项意为"重复"。对方提问后 answered。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为这个故事教会我要勇敢。A 项意为"勇敢的"；B 项意为"有礼貌的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"安静的"。《狮子王》的主题是 brave。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：活动中我交了一些朋友。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"朋友"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"选择"。交换号码说明 made friends。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：结束时我不想离开。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"等待"。意犹未尽不想 leave。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最重要的是张开嘴巴去尝试。A 项意为"眼睛"；B 项意为"头脑"；C 项意为"嘴巴"；D 项意为"心"。口语需要 open your mouth。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信多练习后口语会变得更好。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更大声"；D 项意为"更低"。get better 意为"变得更好"。故选 B。
