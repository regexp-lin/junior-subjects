# 中考英语完形填空：第一次独立购物

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my mother asked me to go to the supermarket alone for the first time. She gave me a shopping list and two hundred yuan. "You are old enough to do this," she said. I felt both excited and a little ___1___.

The supermarket was about ten minutes' walk from our home. I had been there many times with my mother, but going alone felt different. I took a basket and started to look for the items on the list. The first thing was milk. I found the dairy section easily. But there were so many ___2___ — full-fat, low-fat, skim, and different brands. I did not know which to choose. I remembered my mother usually bought the one with a blue label. I found it and put two bottles in my basket.

Next was rice. The bags were heavy. I asked a staff member for help. She kindly showed me where the smaller bags were. I chose a five-kilogram bag that I could ___3___ home. For the vegetables, I picked fresh tomatoes and cucumbers. I had learned from my mother to check if they were firm and had no soft spots.

At the checkout, I placed my items on the conveyor belt. The cashier scanned them and said, "That will be 156 yuan." I took out my money and counted it carefully. I had just enough, with a little ___4___ left. I put the change in my wallet and packed the groceries into bags.

When I got home, my mother was pleased. She checked the items and said I had bought everything correctly. "You even chose the right milk!" she said. I felt a sense of ___5___. I had completed my first solo shopping trip successfully.

That experience taught me important life skills. I learned to compare prices and make ___6___. I also learned that it is okay to ask for help when needed. The staff at the supermarket were friendly and willing to assist. Most importantly, I learned to manage money. I did not spend on unnecessary things. I ___7___ to the list and came home with the right amount of change.

Now I often help with the shopping. Sometimes I go alone, and sometimes I go with my mother. Each time I become more ___8___. Shopping alone is a small step, but it is part of growing up. My mother says that learning to do things by myself will help me when I ___9___ home for university or work. I believe she is right. We all need to learn to be ___10___ and take care of ourselves.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我既兴奋又有点___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。第一次独自购物会紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但有这么多___。A 项意为"选择"；B 项意为"价格"；C 项意为"尺寸"；D 项意为"颜色"。各种牛奶可选，"choices"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了一个五公斤的袋子能___回家。A 项意为"推"；B 项意为"搬/提"；C 项意为"开车"；D 项意为"扔"。"carry"意为"搬运"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：钱刚好够，还剩一点___。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"找零"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"水"。"change"意为"找零"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到一种___。A 项意为"骄傲"；B 项意为"遗憾"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"恐惧"。成功完成第一次购物感到骄傲，"pride"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了比较价格并做___。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"选择"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"计划"。"make choices"意为"做选择"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___清单，带回了正确的找零。A 项意为"添加"；B 项意为"坚持"；C 项意为"归还"；D 项意为"忘记"。"stuck to"意为"坚持遵守"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次我都变得更___。A 项意为"依赖的"；B 项意为"独立的"；C 项意为"害羞的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。独自购物让人更独立，"independent"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我___家去上大学或工作时会有帮助。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"待"；C 项意为"建造"；D 项意为"参观"。"leave home"意为"离家"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都需要学会___并照顾自己。A 项意为"依赖的"；B 项意为"独立的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"自由的"。"independent"与前文呼应。故选 B。
