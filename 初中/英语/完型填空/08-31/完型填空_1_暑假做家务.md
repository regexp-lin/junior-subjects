# 中考英语完形填空：暑假做家务

## 完形填空原文

This summer, my parents were both busy with work. They came home late every day and looked tired. I decided to help with the housework. At first I thought it would be easy. I was ___1___.

Every morning I made my bed and cleaned my room. Then I would sweep the floor and take out the rubbish. On weekends, I helped with the laundry. I had to separate the white clothes from the coloured ones. My mother said mixing them could ___2___ the colours. I also learned to use the washing machine. It was not as difficult as I had imagined.

The hardest task was cooking. My parents usually ate lunch at the office, so I only needed to prepare dinner. I started with simple dishes like tomato and egg, and fried rice. Sometimes I burned the food or added too much salt. But my parents never ___3___. They always said "Thank you" and ate everything. Their support encouraged me to keep trying.

Doing housework taught me to appreciate my parents more. I had never realised how much work they did every day. Now I understand why they are sometimes too ___4___ to play with me. They are not lazy — they are exhausted. I also learned that a clean and tidy home does not happen by itself. Someone has to put in the ___5___.

One day my mother came home with flowers. She said, "These are for you. Thank you for being such a helpful child." I put them in a vase and felt warm inside. It was nice to be ___6___. My father said that when I grow up and have my own family, I would be glad I learned these skills. I think he is right. Being able to take care of myself and my home is an important part of ___7___.

I will continue to help with housework even when school starts. I can do some tasks before or after homework. A little help each day can make a big ___8___. My parents have given me so much. The least I can do is share the load. As the saying goes, "Many hands make ___9___ work." When we all contribute, our home becomes a happier ___10___.

2. A. protect &emsp;&emsp; B. improve &emsp;&emsp; C. fade &emsp;&emsp; D. keep
3. A. praised &emsp;&emsp; B. complained &emsp;&emsp; C. thanked &emsp;&emsp; D. smiled

7. A. childhood &emsp;&emsp; B. adulthood &emsp;&emsp; C. school &emsp;&emsp; D. work

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我觉得会很容易，我___了。A 项意为"对的"；B 项意为"错了"；C 项意为"确定的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。后文显示家务并不简单，之前想错了，"wrong"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：混洗可能会___颜色。A 项意为"保护"；B 项意为"改善"；C 项意为"使褪色"；D 项意为"保持"。混洗会导致串色褪色，"fade"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但父母从未___。A 项意为"表扬"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"感谢"；D 项意为"微笑"。"never complained"意为"从不抱怨"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我明白为什么他们有时太___没空陪我玩。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"渴望的"。"too tired"意为"太累了"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为： someone has to put in the ___。A 项意为"努力"；B 项意为"金钱"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"运气"。"put in the effort"意为"付出努力"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：被___感觉真好。A 项意为"奖励/认可"；B 项意为"惩罚"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"忽视"。收到鲜花是认可奖励，"rewarded"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：是___的重要部分。A 项意为"童年"；B 项意为"成年"；C 项意为"学校"；D 项意为"工作"。长大后成家需此能力，"adulthood"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天一点帮助能产生很大___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"影响"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"噪音"。"make a big difference"意为"产生很大影响"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与谚语
**【解析】** 本句意为：众人拾柴火焰高。A 项意为"轻的"；B 项意为"重的"；C 项意为"艰难的"；D 项意为"容易的"。"Many hands make light work"为固定谚语。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的家会成为更幸福的___。A 项意为"地方"；B 项意为"房间"；C 项意为"建筑物"；D 项意为"街道"。"place"可指家/场所。故选 A。
