# 中考英语完形填空：周末逛农贸市场

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my mom asked me to go to the farmers' market with her. I had never been there before, so I was quite ___1___. We left home early in the morning and arrived at the market around 7:30.

The market was much ___2___ than I had expected. There were many stalls selling fresh vegetables, fruits, eggs and even live fish. The air was filled with the smell of flowers and the ___3___ of people bargaining. I saw an old farmer selling tomatoes. His tomatoes looked bright red and very ___4___. Mom bought two kilos and said they were cheaper than those in the supermarket.

We walked from one stall to another. Mom taught me how to ___5___ good vegetables. She said we should choose those that look fresh and have no dark spots. I tried to help her carry the shopping bags, but they became heavier and heavier. My arms started to ___6___, but I didn't complain. I knew Mom needed my help.

Before we left, we stopped at a flower stall. Mom ___7___ a bunch of sunflowers for our living room. The seller wrapped them in newspaper and gave us a big smile. On our way home, Mom said that buying from the farmers' market not only supports local farmers but also helps us eat ___8___. I agreed with her completely.

That morning I learned something ___9___ — the farmers' market is not just a place to buy food, it's a place where we can feel the warmth of life and the joy of ___10___ with our community.

## 选项

1. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
2. A. smaller &emsp;&emsp; B. bigger &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaner &emsp;&emsp; D. quieter
3. A. sound &emsp;&emsp; B. noise &emsp;&emsp; C. voice &emsp;&emsp; D. music
4. A. cheap &emsp;&emsp; B. expensive &emsp;&emsp; C. healthy &emsp;&emsp; D. delicious
5. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. choose &emsp;&emsp; C. wash &emsp;&emsp; D. grow
6. A. hurt &emsp;&emsp; B. ache &emsp;&emsp; C. break &emsp;&emsp; D. fall
7. A. picked &emsp;&emsp; B. picked up &emsp;&emsp; C. took &emsp;&emsp; D. brought
8. A. less &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. worse
9. A. important &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
10. A. working &emsp;&emsp; B. playing &emsp;&emsp; C. connecting &emsp;&emsp; D. fighting

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未去过那里，所以相当兴奋。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"劳累的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。第一次去新地方，感到兴奋符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：市场比我预想的要大得多。A 项意为"更小的"；B 项意为"更大的"；C 项意为"更干净的"；D 项意为"更安静的"。后文描述摊位多、商品丰富，bigger 符合。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：空气中弥漫着花香和人们讨价还价的声音。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"噪音"；C 项意为"嗓音"；D 项意为"音乐"。bargaining 产生的是一般性声音，sound 较中性。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的西红柿看起来鲜红，非常好吃。A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"贵的"；C 项意为"健康的"；D 项意为"美味的"。bright red 描述外观，好吃是从外观推断的品质。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈教我如何挑选好蔬菜。A 项意为"烹调"；B 项意为"选择；挑选"；C 项意为"洗"；D 项意为"种植"。挑选蔬菜用 choose。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的胳膊开始酸痛，但我没有抱怨。A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"疼痛；酸痛"；C 项意为"折断"；D 项意为"落下"。拎重物后胳膊酸痛用 ache。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词及短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈为客厅挑了一束向日葵。A 项意为"挑选"；B 项意为"捡起"；C 项意为"拿"；D 项意为"带来"。pick 可表示"挑选"，pick a bunch 意为"挑选一束"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从农贸市场购买不仅能支持当地农民，还能让我们吃得更健康。A 项意为"更少"；B 项意为"更多"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更差"。eat better 指吃得更新鲜、更健康。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天早上我学到了一些重要的东西。A 项意为"重要的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。后文总结人生感悟，important 合适。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：农贸市场不仅是买食物的地方，还是我们能感受生活温暖和与社区联结快乐的地方。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"玩耍"；C 项意为"联结；联系"；D 项意为"争斗"。connecting with community 意为"与社区建立联系"。故选 C。
