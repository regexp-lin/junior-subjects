# 中考英语完形填空：制作手抄报

## 完形填空原文

Our class was going to hold a hand-copy newspaper competition. The theme was "My Favorite Book". I decided to make one ___1___ my best friend Li Ming. We met after school to plan our work.

First, we needed to choose a book to introduce. We had a long ___2___ and finally picked "The Little Prince". It was a book that had touched both of our hearts. Then we divided the work: I would write the main ___3___ and draw some pictures, while Li Ming would design the layout and add the border.

I spent two evenings writing the article. I had to ___4___ my words carefully because the space on the newspaper was limited. Li Ming was good at art, so his part looked really ___5___. He used different colors and made the title stand out.

On the day before the deadline, we ___6___ our work together. At first, something looked a bit wrong — the pictures and words didn't match well. We didn't give up. We moved things around and made a few ___7___. After three hours of hard work, our hand-copy newspaper was finally complete.

When we handed it in, our teacher said we had done a ___8___ job. She especially liked our creative ideas. A week later, we heard the good news — we won the first prize! We were so happy that we ___9___ hands and jumped for joy.

This experience taught me that teamwork makes difficult tasks easier. When we ___10___ our strengths, we can achieve amazing things together.

## 选项

1. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. from
2. A. discussion &emsp;&emsp; B. decision &emsp;&emsp; C. direction &emsp;&emsp; D. design
3. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. article &emsp;&emsp; C. letter &emsp;&emsp; D. diary
4. A. choose &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. correct &emsp;&emsp; D. copy
5. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. simple &emsp;&emsp; D. messy
6. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. put up &emsp;&emsp; C. put on &emsp;&emsp; D. put down
7. A. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; B. changes &emsp;&emsp; C. choices &emsp;&emsp; D. suggestions
8. A. bad &emsp;&emsp; B. poor &emsp;&emsp; C. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
9. A. shook &emsp;&emsp; B. waved &emsp;&emsp; C. held &emsp;&emsp; D. raised
10. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. share &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. lose

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定和最好的朋友李明一起制作一份。A 项意为"为"；B 项意为"和……一起"；C 项意为"关于"；D 项意为"从"。make one with sb 表示"与某人一起做"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们进行了长时间的讨论，最终选定了《小王子》。A 项意为"讨论"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"方向"；D 项意为"设计"。have a discussion 为固定搭配，讨论后做出选择。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我负责写主要内容并画一些图。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"文章"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"日记"。手抄报上的文字内容通常称为 article。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我必须仔细选词，因为报纸上的空间有限。A 项意为"选择"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"改正"；D 项意为"复制"。choose one's words 表示"斟酌用词"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李明擅长艺术，所以他负责的部分看起来真的很美观。A 项意为"丑陋的"；B 项意为"美丽的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"凌乱的"。good at art 对应 beautiful。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：截止日期前一天，我们把各自的工作整合在一起。A 项 put 与 together 连用表示"整合"；B 项意为"张贴"；C 项意为"穿上；上演"；D 项意为"放下"。put our work together 表示"把各部分拼在一起"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们调整了布局，做了几处修改。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"改变；修改"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"建议"。move things around 后做 changes 合理。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说你做得很好。A 项意为"坏的"；B 项意为"差的"；C 项意为"极好的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。老师表扬用 wonderful/job 搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们太开心了，握手并高兴得跳起来。A 项意为"握（手）"；B 项意为"挥动"；C 项意为"握住"；D 项意为"举起"。shake hands 为固定搭配，意为"握手"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们分享彼此的长处时，我们能一起做成了不起的事。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"失去"。share strengths 与 teamwork 呼应。故选 B。
