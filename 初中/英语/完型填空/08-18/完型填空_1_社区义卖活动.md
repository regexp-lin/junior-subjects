# 中考英语完形填空：社区义卖活动

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, our community held a charity sale to raise money for children in poor villages. Many families took part in the event. My mother and I got up early to prepare our stall.

We gathered things we no longer needed: old books, toys, clothes that were too small for me, and some handmade crafts. My mother also baked some cookies to ___1___. "Everything we sell will go to help those children," she said.

The sale started at nine o'clock in the community square. There were about thirty stalls, each with different items. Some sold second-hand clothes, some sold toys and games, and some sold home-made food. The atmosphere was lively and ___2___.

Our stall was near the entrance. A lot of people stopped to look at our items. A little girl bought a teddy bear that I had loved when I was younger. She hugged it tightly and said thank you. I felt ___3___ that my old toy would make another child happy.

Around noon, an elderly couple came to our stall. They bought several books and two boxes of cookies. The man said they wanted to support the good ___4___. Before they left, they gave us an extra fifty yuan as a donation. We were deeply moved.

By two o'clock in the afternoon, we had sold almost everything. We counted the money together. We had collected over eight hundred yuan from our stall ___5___. Together with other stalls, the event raised more than twenty thousand yuan for the charity.

I learned something important that day. Helping others does not always mean giving a lot of money. We could give things we no longer need, or we could give our ___6___. The cookies my mother baked took time and effort. The toys and books we donated had once been precious to us. Every small act of kindness ___7___.

Before we went home, the organiser thanked all the volunteers. She said that the success of the event depended on everyone's ___8___. I felt proud to be part of it. I hope we can have more events like this in our community.

My mother said we would take part in the next charity sale too. I have already started to collect things to ___9___ next time. I also want to learn how to bake so that I can contribute something delicious. Helping others makes me feel that I am doing something ___10___ with my life.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈还烤了些饼干来___。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"藏"。义卖活动中烤饼干是为了出售，"sell"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：气氛活跃且___。A 项意为"安静的"；B 项意为"友善的"；C 项意为"悲伤的"；D 项意为"严肃的"。社区义卖气氛亲切友好，"friendly"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的旧玩具能让另一个孩子开心，我感到___。A 项意为"抱歉"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"生气"；D 项意为"无聊"。捐赠旧物带来满足感，"proud"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老先生说他们想支持这个好___。A 项意为"事业"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"计划"。"support the good cause"意为"支持这项善举"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：仅我们自己的摊位就筹集了超过八百元。A 项意为"单独/仅"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"分开"；D 项意为"在后面"。"alone"强调单独我们摊位就筹到这么多。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们也可以付出我们的___。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"空间"。后文说烤饼干花费时间精力，"time"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一份小小的善意都___。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"重要"；C 项意为"消失"；D 项意为"伤害"。"matters"意为"有意义/重要"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：活动的成功取决于每个人的___。A 项意为"批评"；B 项意为"支持"；C 项意为"沉默"；D 项意为"抱怨"。"everyone's support"意为"大家的支持"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我已开始收集东西下次___。A 项意为"保留"；B 项意为"捐赠"；C 项意为"打破"；D 项意为"买"。为下次义卖准备捐赠物品，"donate"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他人让我觉得自己在做___的事。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"meaningful"表示有意义。故选 C。
