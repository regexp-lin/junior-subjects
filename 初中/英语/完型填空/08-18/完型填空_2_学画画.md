# 中考英语完形填空：学画画

## 完形填空原文

This summer, I joined an art class at the community centre. I had always enjoyed drawing, but I had never had proper lessons. The teacher, Mr Wang, was a kind man who had been teaching art for fifteen years.

On the first day, Mr Wang said that anyone could learn to draw. "Drawing is not about talent," he told us. "It's about observation and ___1___. You need to look carefully at what you want to draw, and then practise again and again."

We started with simple shapes: circles, squares, and triangles. I thought it would be boring, but Mr Wang made it interesting. He said that everything in the world could be broken down into these basic ___2___. "Even a tree is just many circles and lines," he explained.

After two weeks, we began to draw still life. Mr Wang brought fruits, vases, and flowers to the classroom. We placed them on a table and drew what we saw. My first attempt looked nothing like the real ___3___. The apple I drew was too round, and the banana was crooked. Mr Wang smiled and said, "Don't worry. Every artist started like this."

I practised at home every day. I drew the cup on my desk, the books on my shelf, and the view from my window. Slowly, my drawings began to improve. I learned to use different shades of pencil to show light and ___4___. I learned that the background was as important as the main subject.

One afternoon, we went to the park to draw outdoors. I chose to draw an old tree by the lake. I sat on the grass and spent two hours ___5___ on my sketch. When I showed it to Mr Wang, he said, "You have caught the spirit of the tree. That's what good art is about—not copying exactly, but showing how you ___6___ it."

The art class ended last week. I was a bit sad, but Mr Wang said we could come back anytime for advice. He gave each of us a small sketchbook as a ___7___. "Keep drawing," he said. "Draw every day, even if it's just for ten minutes."

I have filled half of my sketchbook already. I draw during breaks at school, on the bus, and before I go to sleep. My classmates often ask me to draw something for them. I feel ___8___ when they smile at my drawings.

My parents have hung some of my drawings on the living room wall. Every time I look at them, I remember Mr Wang's words: "Art is a way to ___9___ your feelings. There is no right or wrong. Just be honest with what you see and feel."

I want to continue learning. Maybe one day I will be able to draw portraits or paint with watercolours. For now, I am happy to ___10___ the world around me through my pencil.

9. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. express &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. change

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：画画不在于天赋，而在于观察和___。A 项意为"天赋"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"金钱"。与"观察"并列，强调练习的重要性，"practice"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：世上一切都可以分解为这些基本___。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"形状"；C 项意为"尺寸"；D 项意为"价格"。前文提到圆形、方形、三角形，是基本形状，"shapes"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的第一次尝试和真实的___一点也不像。A 项意为"东西"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"人"；D 项意为"时间"。静物写生画的是实物，"thing"泛指实物。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会用不同深浅的铅笔表现光影和___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"阴影"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"味道"。"light and shadow"为固定搭配，意为"明暗"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在草地上花了两小时___我的素描。A 项意为"玩"；B 项意为"工作/创作"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"说话"。"working on"意为"致力于/埋头于"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好艺术不是精确复制，而是表现你如何___它。A 项意为"听到"；B 项意为"感受"；C 项意为"品尝"；D 项意为"闻到"。艺术表达个人感受，"feel"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他送给我们每人一本小素描本作为___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"账单"；D 项意为"信"。"as a gift"意为"作为礼物"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们常常让我给他们画点东西，他们对着我的画微笑时我感到___。A 项意为"疲倦"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"高兴"；D 项意为"无聊"。得到认可感到开心，"pleased"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：艺术是___情感的一种方式。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"表达"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"改变"。"express your feelings"意为"表达你的感受"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：眼下，我很高兴用铅笔___周围的世界。A 项意为"描绘"；B 项意为"忽略"；C 项意为"破坏"；D 项意为"记住"。"describe the world"意为"描绘世界"。故选 A。
