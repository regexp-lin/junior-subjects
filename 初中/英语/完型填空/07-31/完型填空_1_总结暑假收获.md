# 中考英语完形填空：总结暑假收获

## 完形填空原文

The summer holiday has come to an end. As I sit at my desk and look back at the past two months, I feel that it was a meaningful and ___1___ holiday. I would like to share what I have learned and gained.

First of all, I improved my cooking skills. I learned to make simple dishes like fried rice and vegetable soup. At the beginning, I was ___2___ that I would burn the food or make it taste bad. But with practice, I became more confident. Now I can help my parents prepare meals when they are busy. This makes me feel proud and useful.

Secondly, I read more books than usual. I finished five books — two novels, one science book, and two books about history. Reading opened my ___3___ to new ideas and different worlds. I also learned to take notes while reading, which helped me remember and understand the content better. I plan to keep this habit during the school year.

Thirdly, I spent quality time with my family. We went on a short trip to the countryside. We visited my grandparents and helped them with some work in the garden. My grandfather taught me how to grow vegetables. He said that working with the earth can ___4___ you feel calm and connected to nature. I found that he was right.

Of course, I also did my homework and reviewed what I had learned last term. I did not want to ___5___ everything during the holiday. A little study each day helped me stay fresh and ready for the new term. I believe that small steps every day can lead to big progress.

The holiday was not perfect. There were days when I felt lazy and did nothing useful. But I have learned that it is okay to rest sometimes. We cannot be productive every single moment. What matters is that we try our best and do not give up on our ___6___.

As I prepare for the new term, I feel both excited and a little nervous. New subjects, new challenges, and new opportunities are waiting. I will take what I have learned this summer and use it to become a better ___7___. I will work hard, stay curious, and never stop learning.

To all my classmates: I hope you also had a good holiday. Let us share our stories when we meet. And let us support each other in the year ___8___. Together we can achieve more.

The summer of 2024 will always hold a special place in my heart. It was a time of growth, discovery, and gratitude. I am thankful for every experience, every lesson, and every person who made this holiday ___9___. Here is to a new beginning and a wonderful year ___10___!

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到这是一个有意义且___的假期。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"有成效的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。"productive"表示充实、有收获。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我___会把食物烧焦或做得难吃。A 项意为"确信的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。"worried that"意为"担心会……"，初学者常见心理。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：阅读为我___打开了新的想法和不同世界的大门。A 项意为"门"；B 项意为"心智/思维"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"房间"。"open one's mind"为固定搭配，意为"开阔眼界、打开思维"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与土地打交道能___你感到平静并与自然连接。A 项意为"使"；B 项意为"让"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"教"。"make sb. feel"意为"使某人感到"，"make"后接省略 to 的不定式。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不想在假期里___一切。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"学习"；D 项意为"教"。"forget everything"指把所学忘光，复习可避免遗忘。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是我们尽力而为，不放弃我们的___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"目标"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"玩具"。"goals"与"try our best"、"give up"呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我将运用暑期所学，成为更好的___。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"家长"；C 项意为"学生"；D 项意为"工人"。作者为初中生，新学期目标是更好学习，"student"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词/介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我们在___的一年里互相支持。A 项意为"以前"；B 项意为"前方/未来"；C 项意为"后面"；D 项意为"过去"。"the year ahead"意为"即将到来的一年"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感谢每一位让这个假期变得___的人。A 项意为"长的"；B 项意为"短的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"special"表示难忘、有意义。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词/介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：为新开始和___美好的一年干杯！A 项意为"后面"；B 项意为"以前"；C 项意为"前方"；D 项意为"过去"。"a wonderful year ahead"与"new beginning"呼应，祝愿未来一年。故选 C。
