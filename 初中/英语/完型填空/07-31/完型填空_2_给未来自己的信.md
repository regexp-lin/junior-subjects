# 中考英语完形填空：给未来自己的信

## 完形填空原文

Dear Future Me,

I am writing this letter to you on the last day of the summer holiday. I am 14 years old now, a junior high student. I wonder what you will be like when you read this. Will you still ___1___ the person I am today? I hope so, but I also hope you have grown in many ways.

Right now, I have many dreams. I want to do well in my exams and get into a good high school. I want to learn to play the guitar. I want to travel to different places and see the world. I hope you have achieved at least ___2___ of these dreams. And if you have not, I hope you are still trying. Remember: it is never too late to pursue what you love.

I also want to remind you of some important things. First, never forget the people who have helped you. Your parents, teachers, and friends have given you so much. When you have the chance, ___3___ them. A simple "thank you" can mean a lot. Second, do not be afraid of failure. Every successful person has failed many times. What matters is that you learn from your mistakes and keep ___4___. Third, take care of your health. Exercise, eat well, and get enough sleep. Your body will thank you when you are older.

I am a bit nervous about the future. There are so many things I do not know. But I am also ___5___. I believe that with hard work and a positive attitude, you can overcome any difficulty. Trust yourself. You are stronger than you think.

When you read this letter, you might ___6___ some of the things I am worrying about today. You might laugh at how small those problems seem now. That is okay. It means you have grown. But please do not forget the feelings you had when you were 14. Those feelings — hope, fear, curiosity, and excitement — are what make us human. They connect you to the person who wrote this letter.

I hope you are happy. I hope you have found work that you love and people who love you. I hope you have not given up on your ___7___. Most importantly, I hope you are still kind — to yourself and to others. The world needs more kindness.

If there is one thing I want you to remember, it is this: you are ___8___ of great things. Do not let fear hold you back. Do not let others tell you what you cannot do. Believe in yourself, work hard, and never stop learning. The future is in your hands.

I will put this letter in a safe place and open it in five years. By then, I will be 19. I wonder what ___9___ will have brought. Whatever happens, I promise to stay true to myself. And I hope you — future me — have done the same.

Good luck, future me. I am rooting for you. Let us make our dreams come ___10___.

With love,
Your 14-year-old self

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当你读这封信时，你还会___今天的我吗？A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"遇见"。"remember"表示是否还记得当年的自己。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望你至少实现了这些梦想中的___。A 项意为"一些"；B 项意为"没有"；C 项意为"全部"；D 项意为"很少"。"at least some"表示至少完成其中一部分。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有机会时，要___他们。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"责备"；C 项意为"忽视"；D 项意为"离开"。"thank them"与前文"people who have helped you"呼应。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是从错误中学习并继续___。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"抱怨"。"keep trying"意为"坚持不懈"，与"learn from mistakes"连贯。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我也___。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"充满希望的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"hopeful"与"nervous"形成转折，表达对未来的信心。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可能会___我今天担心的一些事。A 项意为"记得"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"解决"；D 项意为"创造"。"forget"表示时过境迁，当年的烦恼已不重要。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望你没有放弃你的___。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"游戏"。"dreams"与全文关于梦想的主题一致。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你有能力成就伟大的事。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"有能力的"；D 项意为"充满的"。"capable of"意为"能够做……"，表达自我激励。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不知道___会带来什么。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"帮助"。"what time will have brought"指五年间会发生的变化。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我们一起让梦想___。A 项意为"回来"；B 项意为"成真"；C 项意为"出来"；D 项意为"进来"。"make dreams come true"为固定搭配，意为"实现梦想"。故选 B。
