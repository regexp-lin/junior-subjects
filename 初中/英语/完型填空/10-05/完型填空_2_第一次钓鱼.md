# 中考英语完形填空：第一次钓鱼

## 完形填空原文

On October 5th, my father took me fishing at a lake near our town. It was my ___1___ time fishing, and I was very excited.

We arrived at the lake early in the morning. The surface of the water was calm and smooth, like a ___2___. A few other fishermen were already sitting by the lake with their fishing rods. They looked very patient and focused.

Father taught me how to put the bait on the hook. I was a little ___3___ to touch the worm at first, but Father showed me it was harmless. Then he showed me how to cast the line into the water. My first try went wrong — the hook landed right at my feet! Father laughed and helped me try ___4___.

After casting the line successfully, we sat down and waited. And waited. And waited some more. Nothing happened for a long time. I started to feel ___5___. "Can we go home?" I asked. Father shook his head. "Fishing teaches you patience," he said. "Good things come to those who ___6___."

About an hour later, I suddenly felt a pull on my line. "I think I've got one!" I shouted. Father rushed over and told me to pull the rod up ___7___. I pulled with all my strength. After a short struggle, a silver fish appeared above the water. It was not very big, but I was overjoyed.

Father helped me take the fish off the hook and put it in a bucket. I stared at it, feeling a great sense of ___8___. I had caught my first fish! The other fishermen nearby smiled and congratulated me.

We stayed at the lake until noon and I caught two more fish. Father caught five. On the way home, I held the bucket ___9___ as if it contained treasure.

That evening, Mother cooked the fish for dinner. It tasted absolutely delicious — probably because I had caught it ___10___. Fishing turned out to be much more fun than I had expected.

## 选项

1. A. last &emsp;&emsp; B. second &emsp;&emsp; C. first &emsp;&emsp; D. only
2. A. mirror &emsp;&emsp; B. blanket &emsp;&emsp; C. road &emsp;&emsp; D. wall
3. A. eager &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. ready &emsp;&emsp; D. happy
4. A. later &emsp;&emsp; B. again &emsp;&emsp; C. harder &emsp;&emsp; D. alone
5. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. hungry &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
6. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. complain &emsp;&emsp; D. rush
7. A. gently &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. suddenly
8. A. loss &emsp;&emsp; B. humor &emsp;&emsp; C. achievement &emsp;&emsp; D. danger
9. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. sadly &emsp;&emsp; D. proudly
10. A. myself &emsp;&emsp; B. himself &emsp;&emsp; C. itself &emsp;&emsp; D. herself

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 序数词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我第一次钓鱼，我非常兴奋。A 项意为"最后的"；B 项意为"第二"；C 项意为"第一"；D 项意为"唯一的"。标题和后文都表明 first time。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：水面平静光滑，像一面镜子。A 项意为"镜子"；B 项意为"毯子"；C 项意为"路"；D 项意为"墙"。calm and smooth like a mirror 是常见比喻。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始我有点害怕碰虫子。A 项意为"渴望的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"准备好的"；D 项意为"开心的"。第一次碰虫子会 afraid。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸帮我再试一次。A 项意为"后来"；B 项意为"再次"；C 项意为"更努力"；D 项意为"独自"。第一次失败后 try again。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始觉得无聊。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"饿的"；D 项意为"冷的"。等了很久没动静，感到 bored。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好事属于那些等待的人。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"抱怨"；D 项意为"匆忙"。Good things come to those who wait 是谚语。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸告诉我小心地把鱼竿拉起来。A 项意为"温柔地"；B 项意为"慢慢地"；C 项意为"小心地"；D 项意为"突然地"。钓鱼拉竿要 carefully 以免脱钩。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到巨大的成就感。A 项意为"损失"；B 项意为"幽默"；C 项意为"成就"；D 项意为"危险"。第一次钓到鱼的 a sense of achievement。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家路上我自豪地拿着水桶，好像里面装着宝藏。A 项意为"生气地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"悲伤地"；D 项意为"自豪地"。钓到鱼很 proudly。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：可能因为是我自己钓的，所以特别好吃。A 项意为"我自己"；B 项意为"他自己"；C 项意为"它自己"；D 项意为"她自己"。主语是 I，所以用 myself。故选 A。
