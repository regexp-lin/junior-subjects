# 中考英语完形填空：制作手工贺卡送朋友

## 完形填空原文

My best friend Xiao Mei's birthday was coming. I wanted to give her something special — not just a bought gift, but something made with my own hands. I decided to make a handmade card for her.

I went to a craft store and bought some colourful paper, glue, scissors, and a few other ___1___. I had never made a card before, so I searched online for some ideas. I found a beautiful design with folded paper flowers and decided to try it.

That weekend, I sat at my desk and started working. First, I cut the paper into small squares and folded each one into a petal shape. It took me a long time because I had to be very ___2___ — one wrong fold and the flower would look messy. Then I glued the petals together to form a flower. I made three flowers in different colours: pink, yellow and blue.

Next, I folded a piece of red cardstock in half to make the card base. I arranged the paper flowers on the front and stuck them down. I also drew a small butterfly ___3___ the flowers. At the top, I wrote "Happy Birthday, Xiao Mei!" in my best handwriting.

Inside the card, I wrote a personal message. I told her how grateful I was for her friendship and how much she meant to me. I ___4___ several times before I finally got the words right. I wanted every word to be perfect.

On the day of her birthday, I gave her the card. When she opened it, her eyes lit up. "You made this yourself? It's so ___5___!" she exclaimed. She hugged me tightly and said it was the best birthday gift she had ever received. Her ___6___ made all my effort worthwhile.

Making that card taught me something important. A handmade gift may not cost much money, but it carries our heart and time. It shows the receiver that we ___7___ about them. Sometimes the best gifts are not the most expensive ones, but the ones made with love.

I have made more handmade cards since then — for Teachers' Day, for Mother's Day, and for friends who moved away. Each card is ___8___, and each one carries my sincere wishes. I enjoy the process of creating something with my hands. It brings me a sense of ___9___ and joy.

If you want to show someone you care, try making a card for them. You might be ___10___ at how much they appreciate it.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我去工艺品店买了彩纸、胶水、剪刀和其他材料。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"材料"；C 项意为"书籍"；D 项意为"衣服"。做手工需要 materials。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：花了很长时间，因为我必须非常仔细——折错一步花就不好看。A 项意为"快速的"；B 项意为"粗心的"；C 项意为"仔细的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。手工需要细心，careful 正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还在花中间画了一只小蝴蝶。A 项意为"在……下面"；B 项意为"在……后面"；C 项意为"在……中间"；D 项意为"在……上面"。蝴蝶在花丛中，among 正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我重写了好几次才把文字写好。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"失败"；C 项意为"重写"；D 项意为"忘记"。想写完美所以多次 rewrote。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你自己做的？太美了！A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"美丽的"；C 项意为"丑的"；D 项意为"普通的"。收到手工礼物时赞美用 beautiful。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的反应让我觉得所有努力都值得。A 项意为"沉默"；B 项意为"反应"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"拒绝"。朋友高兴的表情和拥抱是 reaction。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：它向收礼人表明我们关心他们。A 项意为"知道"；B 项意为"关心"；C 项意为"想"；D 项意为"担心"。care about 意为"关心；在乎"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每张卡都是独特的，每张都承载着我真诚的祝愿。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"无用的"；D 项意为"独特的"。手工制品各不相同，unique 正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：创作的过程带给我成就感和快乐。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"骄傲；成就感"；C 项意为"羞愧"；D 项意为"恐惧"。sense of pride 意为"成就感"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可能会惊讶于他们有多喜欢它。A 项意为"失望的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。be surprised at 意为"对……感到惊讶"。故选 B。
