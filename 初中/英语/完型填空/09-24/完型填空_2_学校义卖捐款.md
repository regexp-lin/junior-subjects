# 中考英语完形填空：学校义卖捐款

## 完形填空原文

Our school organized a charity sale last month to raise money for children in poor mountainous areas. The event was planned by our student union and supported by all the teachers. Every class was asked to set up a stall and sell things.

Our class decided to sell handmade crafts and used books. We spent a week ___1___ items. Some students made paper flowers and bookmarks. Others brought books they had read and didn't need anymore. I contributed a few storybooks and a toy that I had ___2___ when I was younger.

On the day of the sale, the playground was full of stalls and people. Each stall had a sign showing its class number. Our stall was decorated with colourful balloons and posters. We had ___3___ everything neatly on the table. When the sale started, many students and teachers came to look.

"Come and have a look! All for a good cause!" we shouted. A teacher bought three paper flowers. A younger student picked a storybook and paid with his pocket money. We felt very ___4___ when we made each sale, because we knew the money would go to children who needed it.

By noon, we had sold most of our items. Our class had raised over 500 yuan. The headteacher made a speech. She said that our school had raised a total of 15,000 yuan this year. The money would be used to buy books and school supplies for children in a village school in the west of China.

She also thanked everyone for their ___5___ and kindness. "Every small act of kindness matters," she said. "When we help others, we also make ourselves happier."

This charity sale was a meaningful ___6___. It taught me that we don't need to be rich to help others. We can contribute in our own way — by giving our time, our skills, or things we no longer need. What ___7___ is that we have a willing heart.

I hope our school will hold more such activities in the future. I will definitely take part again. Helping others gives me a warm feeling ___8___. It reminds me that we are all connected and that we can make the world a better place together.

If you have a chance to join a charity activity, don't ___9___ it. You might find that giving is more rewarding than receiving. The smiles of those we help are the best ___10___ we can get.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们花了一周时间收集物品。A 项意为"购买"；B 项意为"收集"；C 项意为"扔掉"；D 项意为"丢失"。义卖前需要收集要卖的物品，collecting 正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我贡献了几本故事书和一个我小时候已经穿不下的玩具。A 项意为"卖掉"；B 项意为"长大后穿不下/用不上"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"弄坏"。outgrow 意为"长大后不再适合"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把所有东西整齐地摆在桌上。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"摆放；排列"；C 项意为"破坏"；D 项意为"浪费"。义卖需要整齐摆放商品，arranged 正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每做成一笔买卖我们都感到骄傲，因为我们知道这些钱会捐给需要的孩子。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。为善事筹款感到自豪，proud 正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她还感谢大家的慷慨和善意。A 项意为"懒惰"；B 项意为"慷慨"；C 项意为"自私"；D 项意为"粗鲁"。义卖捐款体现 generosity。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次义卖是一次有意义的经历。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"经历"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"比赛"。参与活动获得 experience。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是我们有一颗愿意付出的心。A 项意为"重要"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"花费"。what matters 意为"重要的是"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他人让我内心感到温暖。A 项意为"外面"；B 项意为"里面"；C 项意为"前面"；D 项意为"后面"。内心的温暖用 inside。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你有机会参加慈善活动，不要错过。A 项意为"接受"；B 项意为"错过"；C 项意为"抓住"；D 项意为"找到"。don't miss it 意为"不要错过"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们帮助的人的笑容是我们能得到的最好回报。A 项意为"惩罚"；B 项意为"回报"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"困难"。帮助他人得到的回报，reward 正确。故选 B。
