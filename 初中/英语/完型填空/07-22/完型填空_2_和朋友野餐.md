# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友野餐

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my friends and I planned a picnic in the park. We decided to meet at the gate at nine in the morning. Everyone brought something to ___1___ — I brought sandwiches, Li Ming brought fruit, and Wang Fang brought drinks and snacks. Together we had more than enough food.

We found a nice spot under a big tree. The ___2___ was soft and green, perfect for sitting on. We spread out our blanket and laid all the food in the middle. Before we started eating, we took lots of ___3___. We wanted to remember this happy day.

As we ate, we talked and laughed. We shared stories about our summer holiday. Li Ming told us about his trip to the mountains. Wang Fang said she had learned to swim. I talked about the books I had read. Time ___4___ so quickly when you are having fun. Suddenly it was already noon.

After lunch, we played some games. We ran races and played catch. A little dog from a nearby family came over and ___5___ with us. It was so cute that we could not stop playing with it. The owner smiled and said we could come back anytime.

When it was time to leave, we made sure to ___6___ all our rubbish. "Leave the park as clean as we found it," Wang Fang said. We packed everything and said goodbye. I felt a bit ___7___ because the day had been so wonderful and I did not want it to end.

On my way home, I thought about how lucky I am to have such good friends. We do not need expensive things to have fun — just good company and a beautiful day. I hope we can have another picnic ___8___. Maybe next time we can invite more friends and make it even ___9___.

That picnic was one of the best ___10___ of my summer. It reminded me that the simplest moments often bring the greatest joy.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每人带些东西来___。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"烹饪"；D 项意为"种植"。野餐时大家带食物一起分享，"share"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___柔软翠绿，适合坐下。A 项意为"路"；B 项意为"草地"；C 项意为"石头"；D 项意为"沙子"。树下野餐通常坐在草地上，"grass"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们拍了 lots of ___。A 项意为"笔记"；B 项意为"照片"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"礼物"。"took photos"意为"拍照"，"photos"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当你玩得开心时，时间___得真快。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"飞逝"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"返回"。"Time flies"意为"时光飞逝"，是常用表达。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一只小狗过来和我们___。A 项意为"睡觉"；B 项意为"吃"；C 项意为"玩耍"；D 项意为"哭"。后文"could not stop playing with it"说明在玩耍，"played"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们确保___所有垃圾。A 项意为"收集/收拾"；B 项意为"烧"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"忘记"。野餐后收拾垃圾，"collect"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点___因为这一天太美好，不想结束。A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"难过"；C 项意为"生气"；D 项意为"无聊"。美好时光结束时会感到不舍、难过，"sad"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望我们能___再野餐一次。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"很快"；C 项意为"独自"；D 项意为"晚"。"soon"表示不久后再聚，符合期待。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：邀请更多朋友，办得更加___。A 项意为"更小"；B 项意为"更安静"；C 项意为"更大"；D 项意为"更慢"。人多了规模更大，"bigger"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次野餐是我暑假最美好的___之一。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"考试"。美好经历成为回忆，"memories"正确。故选 B。
