# 中考英语完形填空：学做泡菜

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, Grandma came to visit us. She said she would teach me how to make pickles. I was very ___1___ because I had never tried it before and wanted to learn something new.

First, Grandma and I went to the market to buy fresh vegetables. We bought some cabbage, carrots and a few other ___2___. Grandma said the vegetables must be clean and fresh. When we got home, we washed everything ___3___ under the tap.

Then Grandma taught me the steps. We cut the cabbage into small pieces and put them in a big ___4___. We added some salt and mixed everything with our hands. Grandma said we had to wait for about half an hour to let the salt work. During that time, we ___5___ the carrots into thin strips.

After that, we prepared the pickle sauce. Grandma mixed vinegar, sugar, and a little garlic together. The ___6___ smelled a little strong, but Grandma said it would make the pickles taste delicious. We poured the sauce over the vegetables and pressed them down. Finally, we put a lid on the jar and placed it in the ___7___.

Grandma told me we had to wait for two or three days before eating. I was a bit ___8___ because I wanted to taste it right away! But when the day came, I opened the jar and tried a piece. The pickle was crunchy and a little sour. I ___9___ it very much. Grandma smiled and said, "Now you can make pickles by yourself. The more you practice, the ___10___ you will become."

## 选项

1. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. curious
2. A. fruits &emsp;&emsp; B. ingredients &emsp;&emsp; C. drinks &emsp;&emsp; D. snacks
3. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
4. A. plate &emsp;&emsp; B. bowl &emsp;&emsp; C. cup &emsp;&emsp; D. bottle
5. A. turned &emsp;&emsp; B. cut &emsp;&emsp; C. threw &emsp;&emsp; D. dropped
6. A. smell &emsp;&emsp; B. taste &emsp;&emsp; C. color &emsp;&emsp; D. sound
7. A. kitchen &emsp;&emsp; B. fridge &emsp;&emsp; C. room &emsp;&emsp; D. garden
8. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. patient &emsp;&emsp; C. impatient &emsp;&emsp; D. sad
9. A. hated &emsp;&emsp; B. disliked &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoyed &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
10. A. progress &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. better

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很好奇，因为我从未尝试过，想学点新东西。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"担忧的"；D 项意为"好奇的"。"想学新东西"与 curious（好奇）最契合。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们买了一些卷心菜、胡萝卜和其他食材。A 项意为"水果"；B 项意为"食材；配料"；C 项意为"饮料"；D 项意为"零食"。做泡菜的其他原料用 ingredients。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家后，我们在水龙头下仔细清洗了所有东西。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。清洗食材应 carefully，确保干净。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把卷心菜切成小块，放进一个大碗里。A 项意为"盘子"；B 项意为"碗"；C 项意为"杯子"；D 项意为"瓶子"。放蔬菜混合腌制用 bowl 合适。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在那段时间里，我们把胡萝卜切成细丝。A 项意为"翻转"；B 项意为"切"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"掉落"。cut...into strips 意为"切成条/丝"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道闻起来有点冲，但奶奶说会让泡菜很好吃。A 项意为"气味"；B 项意为"味道"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"声音"。smelled 与 smell 搭配，指气味。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们盖上盖子，把罐子放进冰箱。A 项意为"厨房"；B 项意为"冰箱"；C 项意为"房间"；D 项意为"花园"。泡菜需冷藏保鲜，放 fridge 正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点不耐烦，因为想马上就尝。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"有耐心的"；C 项意为"不耐烦的"；D 项意为"难过的"。想立刻品尝却要等几天，用 impatient。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：泡菜又脆又有点酸，我非常喜欢。A 项意为"讨厌"；B 项意为"不喜欢"；C 项意为"喜欢"；D 项意为"忘记"。enjoy 与 liked 同义，表示喜爱。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 比较级及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说："练习越多，你就会越熟练。"A 项意为"进步"；B 项意为"完美"；C 项意为"容易"；D 项意为"更好"。The more...the better 为固定句型，the better you will become 意为"你会变得越好"。故选 D。
