# 中考英语完形填空：学做纸艺玫瑰花

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday afternoon, my cousin came to visit and brought some red and green paper. "Let me teach you how to make paper roses," she said. I had seen paper roses in shops but never made one ___1___. I was curious and said yes right away.

First, we cut the red paper into small squares. Each square would become one petal of the rose. My cousin ___2___ me to fold each square in a special way. At the beginning, my folds were not neat and I wanted to give up. "Don't ___3___," she said. "Practice makes perfect." I tried again and again, and my petals became better.

Next, we rolled the folded paper to create the petal shape. Then we glued the petals together one by one. The rose started to ___4___ shape. We needed about eight petals for one flower. I chose different shades of red to make my rose look more natural.

After the petals were done, we made the stem. We ___5___ a green paper into a thin stick and glued it to the bottom of the flower. We also cut out two green leaves and stuck them on the stem. The paper rose was ___6___ finished!

I placed my rose in a small vase on the desk. It looked almost like a real rose from a distance. My mother was ___7___ when she saw it. "You made this?" she asked. I felt proud and nodded.

Making paper roses ___8___ patience and careful hands. You cannot rush it. But when you see the beautiful result, all the effort seems ___9___. Now I want to learn to make more flowers — maybe sunflowers or lilies next time. Paper crafts can bring so much ___10___ into our daily life!

## 选项

1. A. myself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. himself &emsp;&emsp; D. yourself
2. A. allowed &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. invited &emsp;&emsp; D. ordered
3. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. worry &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
4. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. lose &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. break
5. A. turned &emsp;&emsp; B. rolled &emsp;&emsp; C. cut &emsp;&emsp; D. threw
6. A. finally &emsp;&emsp; B. nearly &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. seldom
7. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. surprised &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
8. A. needs &emsp;&emsp; B. spends &emsp;&emsp; C. saves &emsp;&emsp; D. wastes
9. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. wrong &emsp;&emsp; C. worth it &emsp;&emsp; D. impossible
10. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. sadness &emsp;&emsp; C. joy &emsp;&emsp; D. difficulty

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 反身代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在商店见过纸玫瑰，但从没自己做过。A 项意为"我自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"他自己"；D 项意为"你自己"。主语是 I，做纸玫瑰用 myself。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐教我如何用一种特殊方式折叠每个方块。A 项意为"允许"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"邀请"；D 项意为"命令"。teach sb to do 表示"教某人做某事"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我折得不好想放弃，表姐说"别走/别放弃，熟能生巧"。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"离开"。想放弃时她说 Don't leave，意为别放弃。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把花瓣一片片粘在一起，玫瑰开始成形。A 项 take shape 意为"成形、成型"；B 项 lose shape 意为"变形"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"打破"。制作过程中逐渐 take shape。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把绿色纸卷成一根细棍，粘在花的底部。A 项意为"转动、变成"；B 项意为"卷、卷起"；C 项意为"剪"；D 项意为"扔"。制作茎用 roll paper into a stick。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：粘好叶子和茎后，纸玫瑰终于完成了。A 项意为"终于、最后"；B 项意为"几乎"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"很少"。完成制作过程用 finally。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈看到时很惊讶，问"这是你做的？"A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。看到孩子做出逼真作品会 be surprised。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做纸玫瑰需要耐心和细心的双手。A 项 need 意为"需要"；B 项 spend 主语为人；C 项意为"节省"；D 项意为"浪费"。做某事需要某物用 needs。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当你看到漂亮的成品时，所有的努力似乎都值得。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"错的"；C 项意为"值得的"；D 项意为"不可能的"。看到好结果，努力 worth it。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：纸艺能给我们的日常生活带来很多快乐。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"快乐"；D 项意为"困难"。制作手工带来 joy。故选 C。
