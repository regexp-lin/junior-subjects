# 中考英语完形填空：和家人去海边度周末

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my family decided to spend the weekend at the beach. We woke up early and ___1___ the car with our bags, towels, and a big umbrella. The drive took about two hours. During the trip, my younger brother and I sang songs together. We were all in high ___2___.

When we arrived, the sun was shining brightly and the sea looked very ___3___. Dad found a good place near the water and set up the umbrella. Mum put our things on the sand. My brother ran straight toward the waves. "Wait for me!" I shouted and ___4___ after him.

We played in the water for a long time. Sometimes the waves were big and we jumped over them. Sometimes we sat on the sand and built a small castle. Dad took many ___5___ of us with his camera. He said we looked so happy.

At noon, we had a picnic under the umbrella. Mum had prepared sandwiches, fruit, and some cold drinks. Everything tasted ___6___ because we were hungry after playing. After lunch, we rested for a while. Mum told us an interesting story about the ocean. We listened ___7___ and asked her many questions.

Before we left, we collected all our rubbish and put it in the bin. "We should ___8___ the beach clean," Dad said. "So more people can enjoy it." We all agreed.

On the way home, my brother fell asleep in the car. He was too ___9___. I looked out of the window and thought about the wonderful day. Spending time with family at the beach was the best ___10___ of my weekend. I hope we can go there again soon!

## 选项

1. A. filled &emsp;&emsp; B. drove &emsp;&emsp; C. repaired &emsp;&emsp; D. washed
2. A. spirits &emsp;&emsp; B. buildings &emsp;&emsp; C. mountains &emsp;&emsp; D. prices
3. A. dark &emsp;&emsp; B. blue &emsp;&emsp; C. dirty &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
4. A. walked &emsp;&emsp; B. rode &emsp;&emsp; C. flew &emsp;&emsp; D. followed
5. A. pictures &emsp;&emsp; B. books &emsp;&emsp; C. letters &emsp;&emsp; D. messages
6. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. delicious &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. sour
7. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
8. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. make &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
9. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
10. A. part &emsp;&emsp; B. idea &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. subject

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们早早起床，把包、毛巾和大阳伞装进车里。A 项意为"填满、装满"；B 项意为"驾驶"；C 项意为"修理"；D 项意为"清洗"。fill the car with 表示"把……装进车里"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：旅途上我和弟弟一起唱歌，我们兴致都很高。A 项 in high spirits 意为"兴致高昂、情绪高涨"；B 项意为"建筑物"；C 项意为"山"；D 项意为"价格"。B、C、D 均不与 in high 搭配表情绪。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：太阳明媚，大海看起来非常蓝。A 项意为"黑暗的"；B 项意为"蓝色的"；C 项意为"脏的"；D 项意为"冷的"。晴朗阳光下海水通常呈蓝色。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：弟弟冲向海浪，我喊道"等等我"，跟在他后面追去。A 项意为"走"；B 项意为"骑"；C 项意为"飞"；D 项意为"跟随、追随"。follow after sb 表示"跟在某人后面"。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸用相机给我们拍了很多照片。A 项 take pictures 意为"拍照"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"消息"。用相机自然是拍 pictures。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：玩累了之后很饿，所以什么都觉得好吃。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美味的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"酸的"。玩饿后食物会 tasted delicious。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们认真地听妈妈讲关于大海的有趣故事，并问她很多问题。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"认真地"；D 项意为"大声地"。认真听才会 ask many questions。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说我们应该保持海滩的清洁，这样更多人才能享受它。A 项 keep...clean 意为"保持……清洁"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项 make...clean 可表打扫，但 keep 强调持续保持；D 项意为"忘记"。环保语境用 keep clean。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家的路上弟弟在车里睡着了，他太累了。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"疲惫的"；D 项意为"生气的"。玩一天后 fell asleep 因为 tired。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：和家人去海边度周末是我周末最好的部分。A 项 the best part 意为"最好的部分"；B 项意为"主意"；C 项意为"理由"；D 项意为"科目"。描述周末最好的部分用 part。故选 A。
