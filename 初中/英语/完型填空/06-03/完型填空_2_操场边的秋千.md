# 中考英语完形填空：操场边的秋千

## 完形填空原文

There is an old swing near our school playground. The chains are a little rusty, but it still works. During break time, many students ___1___ to play on it. I used to be one of them. But when I entered Grade 9, I became too busy. I had no time for the swing. I walked past it every day but never ___2___.

One afternoon, I was feeling stressed about a math test. I had studied for hours but still couldn't understand some problems. I needed a ___3___. I walked to the playground and sat on the swing. I didn't swing high — I just sat there gently. The wind blew on my face. I looked at the sky. Slowly, I began to feel ___4___.

That day I realized something. The swing was still there, waiting. It didn't care if I was a Grade 7 kid or a Grade 9 student. It didn't ___5___ if I had good grades or bad grades. It was just there, quiet and patient. Sometimes we need such a place — a place where we can ___6___ our worries and just be ourselves.

Now I visit the swing when I feel tired or worried. I don't swing as high as I did when I was younger. But that's fine. The swing teaches me that it's okay to ___7___. We don't always have to run fast or work hard. Sometimes slowing down helps us see things more clearly.

The swing has been there for many years. Maybe it will still be there when I ___8___ school. I hope younger students will find it too — a simple place where they can rest, think, and remember that life is not only about exams. It's also about small ___9___ like sitting on an old swing and feeling the wind.

I am grateful for that old swing. In a world full of homework and stress, it offers me a moment of ___10___. That is more valuable than I once thought.

## 选项

1. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. hope &emsp;&emsp; D. decide
2. A. stopped &emsp;&emsp; B. tried &emsp;&emsp; C. sat &emsp;&emsp; D. played
3. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. break &emsp;&emsp; C. help &emsp;&emsp; D. change
4. A. better &emsp;&emsp; B. worse &emsp;&emsp; C. busier &emsp;&emsp; D. weaker
5. A. matter &emsp;&emsp; B. know &emsp;&emsp; C. ask &emsp;&emsp; D. tell
6. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. keep
7. A. relax &emsp;&emsp; B. hurry &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. compete
8. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. enter &emsp;&emsp; C. love &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
9. A. dreams &emsp;&emsp; B. joys &emsp;&emsp; C. ideas &emsp;&emsp; D. plans
10. A. peace &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. pride

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多学生___在上面玩。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"喜欢"；C 项意为"希望"；D 项意为"决定"。"like to play"（喜欢玩）与"During break time"相呼应，课间喜欢荡秋千。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我每天经过但从没___。A 项意为"停下来"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"坐"；D 项意为"玩"。"never stopped"与"had no time for the swing"相呼应，匆匆路过不曾停留。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我需要一个___。A 项意为"休息"（名词，指歇息）；B 项意为"休息、暂停"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"改变"。"a break"与"feeling stressed"、"studied for hours"相呼应，需要短暂休息。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：慢慢地，我开始感到___。A 项意为"更好"；B 项意为"更糟"；C 项意为"更忙"；D 项意为"更弱"。"feel better"与"sit there gently"、"wind blew"相呼应，放松后心情好转。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它不___我成绩好坏。A 项意为"在意、重要"；B 项意为"知道"；C 项意为"问"；D 项意为"告诉"。"doesn't matter"（不重要）与"didn't care"相呼应，秋千不在乎这些。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个我们可以___烦恼、做自己的地方。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"保持"。"forget our worries"与"just be ourselves"相呼应，暂时放下烦恼。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：秋千教会我，___是可以的。A 项意为"放松"；B 项意为"匆忙"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"竞争"。"relax"与"slowing down"、"don't always have to run fast"相呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许当我___学校时它还在。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"进入"；C 项意为"喜爱"；D 项意为"想念"。"leave school"（毕业离校）与时间线相呼应。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生活也关乎像坐在旧秋千上感受微风这样的小___。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"计划"。"joys"与"sitting on an old swing and feeling the wind"相呼应，指生活中的小确幸。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在充满作业和压力的世界里，它给我一刻___。A 项意为"宁静、平和"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"骄傲"。"peace"与"rest"、"slowing down"相呼应，秋千带来内心的安宁。故选 A。
