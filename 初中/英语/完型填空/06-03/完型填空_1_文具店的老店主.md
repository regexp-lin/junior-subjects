# 中考英语完形填空：文具店的老店主

## 完形填空原文

There is a small stationery shop near our school. An old man runs it. He has white hair and always wears a kind smile. Every time I go there to buy pens or notebooks, he ___1___ me by my name. "Back again, Xiao Ming?" he says. I feel ___2___ when I hear that. It's nice to be remembered.

Last month, I lost my wallet on the way to school. I had no money for lunch. I walked past the shop and the old man ___3___ me. "You look worried today," he said. I told him what happened. Without saying much, he handed me twenty yuan. "Take it. You can ___4___ me back tomorrow." I was surprised. He trusted me even though we didn't know each other well. The next day, I returned the money. He just nodded and said, "I knew you would ___5___."

That day I learned something important. Trust is not about papers or promises. It's about choosing to ___6___ in people. The old man's shop is small. The shelves are old. But his kindness makes it a ___7___ place. Many students like to visit it, not only for school things, but also for the warm feeling.

I once asked him why he remembered so many students' names. He smiled and said, "Every student is ___8___. When I remember your name, you feel that you matter." Those words stayed with me. Now I try to remember people's names too. It's a small thing, but it can make someone's day ___9___.

The old man will probably keep his shop for many more years. I hope I can still visit him when I ___10___ from high school. That little shop and the old man's smile — they are part of my best memories of middle school.

## 选项

1. A. calls &emsp;&emsp; B. knows &emsp;&emsp; C. meets &emsp;&emsp; D. finds
2. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. warm &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
3. A. saw &emsp;&emsp; B. stopped &emsp;&emsp; C. helped &emsp;&emsp; D. followed
4. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. pay &emsp;&emsp; C. bring &emsp;&emsp; D. send
5. A. come &emsp;&emsp; B. stay &emsp;&emsp; C. wait &emsp;&emsp; D. agree
6. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. think &emsp;&emsp; C. look &emsp;&emsp; D. depend
7. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. busy &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
8. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. young &emsp;&emsp; C. same &emsp;&emsp; D. clever
9. A. longer &emsp;&emsp; B. brighter &emsp;&emsp; C. shorter &emsp;&emsp; D. earlier
10. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. return &emsp;&emsp; C. graduate &emsp;&emsp; D. move

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次我去那里买笔或笔记本，他都用我的名字___我。A 项意为"称呼、叫"；B 项意为"认识"；C 项意为"遇见"；D 项意为"找到"。"calls me by my name"（用名字称呼我）与后文"Back again, Xiao Ming?"相呼应。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：听到那句话我感到___。A 项意为"难过"；B 项意为"温暖"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"疲惫"。"warm"与"It's nice to be remembered"相呼应，被记住名字让人感到温暖。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老人___了我。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"拦住、叫住"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"跟随"。"stopped me"与"You look worried today"相呼应，老人主动叫住并关心。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你明天可以___还我。A 项意为"给"；B 项意为"付、偿还"；C 项意为"带来"；D 项意为"寄送"。"pay me back"（还钱给我）为固定搭配，与借钱情节相呼应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我就知道你会___。A 项意为"来"；B 项意为"留下"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"同意"。"would come"与"returned the money"相呼应，指会来还钱。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为： trust 是关于选择___人们。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"认为"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"依靠"。"believe in people"（相信人）与 trust 的主题相呼应。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但他的善良使它成为一个___的地方。A 项意为"安静的"；B 项意为"忙碌的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"干净的"。"special"与" kindness"、"warm feeling"相呼应，强调特别。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个学生都是___。A 项意为"不同的、独一无二的"；B 项意为"年轻的"；C 项意为"相同的"；D 项意为"聪明的"。"different"与"remember your name"、"you matter"相呼应，每个学生都与众不同。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它能让某人的一天更加___。A 项意为"更长"；B 项意为"更明亮、更开心"；C 项意为"更短"；D 项意为"更早"。"brighter"与"make someone's day"相呼应，指让人心情更好。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望当我从高中___后还能去拜访他。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"返回"；C 项意为"毕业"；D 项意为"搬走"。"graduate from high school"（从高中毕业）与时间线相呼应。故选 C。
