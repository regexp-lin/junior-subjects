# 中考英语完形填空：养小仓鼠

## 完形填空原文

Two months ago, my parents agreed to let me keep a hamster. I had wanted a pet for a long time, and a hamster seemed perfect — small, cute, and easy to care for. I named my new friend Xiao Bai because of his white fur.

Xiao Bai lives in a clear plastic cage with a wheel, a small house, and a water bottle. Every morning, I ___1___ his water and give him fresh food. Hamsters need a special mix of seeds and grains. I also give him small pieces of vegetables like carrots and cucumbers. He ___2___ them quickly with his tiny paws.

At first, Xiao Bai was shy. He would hide in his house whenever I came close. I read online that hamsters need time to get used to their owners. So I spoke to him softly every day and offered him treats from my hand. After about two weeks, he began to ___3___ me. Now he even climbs onto my hand when I put it in the cage.

Hamsters are nocturnal animals. They sleep during the day and become ___4___ at night. Sometimes I hear Xiao Bai running on his wheel at midnight. The sound used to bother me, but now I find it quite comforting. It reminds me that my little friend is happy and healthy.

Cleaning the cage is an important task. I do it every week. I take Xiao Bai out and put him in a safe box. Then I remove the old bedding, wash the cage, and put in fresh wood shavings. Xiao Bai always explores his clean cage with ___5___ when I put him back.

My little brother loves Xiao Bai too. He always wants to hold him. I taught my brother to be gentle because hamsters can get ___6___ easily. We must support their whole body when we hold them, and never squeeze them.

Last week, Xiao Bai escaped from his cage. The door was not closed properly. We searched the whole house and finally found him under the sofa, eating a piece of cracker. I was so ___7___ that I had found him. From then on, I always double-check the cage door.

Keeping a pet has taught me responsibility. I cannot forget to feed Xiao Bai or clean his cage. My parents said that taking care of a living thing would help me grow up. I think they are ___8___. Xiao Bai depends on me, and I must not let him down.

I hope Xiao Bai will stay with us for a long time. Hamsters usually live for about two to three years. I will make sure his life is happy and ___9___. Maybe when I grow up, I will still remember my first pet and the ___10___ he brought to our family.

3. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. trust &emsp;&emsp; C. bite &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
4. A. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; B. hungry &emsp;&emsp; C. active &emsp;&emsp; D. tired

7. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. relieved &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
8. A. wrong &emsp;&emsp; B. right &emsp;&emsp; C. late &emsp;&emsp; D. early
9. A. short &emsp;&emsp; B. comfortable &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天早晨，我___它的水并给它新鲜食物。A 项意为"更换"；B 项意为"喝"；C 项意为"洒"；D 项意为"浪费"。"change his water"意为"换水"，保持饮水新鲜。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它用小爪子___得很快。A 项意为"扔"；B 项意为"拿"；C 项意为"吃"；D 项意为"掉"。"eats them quickly"指仓鼠快速吃蔬菜。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大约两周后，它开始___我。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"信任"；C 项意为"咬"；D 项意为"躲避"。"trust"表示熟悉后开始信任主人。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们白天睡觉，晚上变得___。A 项意为"困倦的"；B 项意为"饥饿的"；C 项意为"活跃的"；D 项意为"疲倦的"。夜行性动物夜间活跃，"active"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小白回到干净笼子时总是带着___探索。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"好奇"；D 项意为"恐惧"。"curiosity"表示仓鼠对新环境的好奇。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：仓鼠很容易___。A 项意为"兴奋"；B 项意为"受惊吓"；C 项意为"强壮"；D 项意为"懒惰"。"get scared easily"意为"容易受惊吓"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：找到它我真___。A 项意为"难过"；B 项意为"生气"；C 项意为"如释重负"；D 项意为"无聊"。"relieved"表示找到宠物后的放心。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得他们说得___。A 项意为"错的"；B 项意为"对的"；C 项意为"晚的"；D 项意为"早的"。"they are right"表示认同父母的话。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会确保它过得快乐而___。A 项意为"短暂的"；B 项意为"舒适的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"孤单的"。"comfortable"指生活舒适。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许长大后我仍会记得我的第一只宠物以及它给我们家带来的___。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"担忧"；D 项意为"愤怒"。宠物带来欢乐，"joy"正确。故选 B。
