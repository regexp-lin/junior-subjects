# 中考英语完形填空：学滑冰

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, my cousin invited me to go ice skating. I had never tried it before, and I was both excited and a little nervous. The skating rink was in a large shopping mall, and it was packed with people enjoying the ice.

We rented a pair of skates. I put them on and stood up carefully. The blades felt strange under my feet. I could barely stand, let alone ___1___. My cousin, who had been skating for years, held my arm and led me onto the ice.

"Relax," she said. "Bend your knees a little and keep your balance. Don't look down at your feet — look ___2___." I tried to follow her advice, but my legs were shaking. I held onto the side rail and moved slowly along the edge.

After a while, I felt a bit more confident. I let go of the rail and took a few steps. Immediately, my feet slipped, and I fell on the ice. It was cold and hurt a little, but I was not ___3___. My cousin helped me up and said, "Everybody falls when they first learn. The important thing is to get up and try again."

I got up and tried again. And again. By the end of the first hour, I had fallen at least ten times. But each time I fell, I learned something. I learned to shift my weight, to move my feet in a "V" shape, and to use my arms for ___4___.

Gradually, I could skate a short distance without falling. The feeling of gliding on the ice was amazing. It was like ___5___, but on a smooth, cold surface. I could feel the wind on my face as I moved forward.

My cousin taught me how to stop. "Turn your feet sideways and press the blades into the ice," she said. I tried it, and it worked! Stopping was almost as hard as starting, but I finally ___6___ it.

When it was time to leave, I did not want to go. I had so much fun! My cousin said we could come back next week. I could not wait to ___7___ again.

The next day, my legs were sore. My mother said it was normal after trying a new sport. She suggested that I rest and drink plenty of water. I did, and by the third day, I felt much better.

Learning to ice skate taught me that new skills take time. I could not expect to be perfect on the first day. But with ___8___ and practice, I could improve. This lesson applies to many things in life — studying, sports, or even making friends.

I plan to go skating every week this winter. Maybe by the end of the season, I will be able to skate backwards or even do a simple spin. For now, I am happy to glide forward and enjoy the ___9___ of the ice beneath my feet. Skating has become one of my favourite winter ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我几乎站不住，更不用说___了。A 项意为"坐"；B 项意为"滑冰"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"跳"。在溜冰场语境下，"skate"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要低头看脚——向___看。A 项意为"后面"；B 项意为"前方"；C 项意为"上"；D 项意为"下"。"look ahead"意为"向前看"，有助于保持平衡。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冰很冷，有点疼，但我并不___。A 项意为"受伤"；B 项意为"受伤"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"尴尬"。摔倒后继续尝试，说明没有吓到，"scared"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学会了用双臂保持___。A 项意为"平衡"；B 项意为"速度"；C 项意为"力量"；D 项意为"温暖"。滑冰时手臂帮助保持平衡，"balance"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在冰上滑行的感觉就像___一样。A 项意为"走路"；B 项意为"飞翔"；C 项意为"游泳"；D 项意为"骑车"。滑冰轻盈感常被比作飞翔，"flying"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：停下来几乎和起步一样难，但我终于___了。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"掌握"；D 项意为"避免"。"mastered it"意为"学会了"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我迫不及待想再次___。A 项意为"练习"；B 项意为"休息"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"睡觉"。"practise again"意为"再练一次"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但只要有___和练习，我就能进步。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"天赋"。"patience and practice"意为"耐心与练习"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我满意地向前滑行，享受脚下冰面的___。A 项意为"寒冷"；B 项意为"光滑"；C 项意为"坚硬"；D 项意为"黑暗"。"smoothness"形容冰面平滑的滑感。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：滑冰已成为我最喜欢的冬季___之一。A 项意为"家务"；B 项意为"科目"；C 项意为"活动"；D 项意为"问题"。"winter activities"意为"冬季活动"。故选 C。
