# 中考英语完形填空：社区游泳池

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, a new swimming pool opened in our community. My friends and I were very ___1___ because we finally had a cool place to go during the hot days.

On the first day, we arrived early. The pool was bright and clean. A lifeguard stood by the water and explained the ___2___ to everyone: no running, no pushing, and no diving in the shallow end. I thought the rules were reasonable because they kept everyone safe.

I had learned to swim when I was eight, but I was not very good at it. I could only do the breaststroke slowly. My friend Li Wei, however, swam like a fish. He moved through the water so ___3___ that I felt a little jealous. I asked him to teach me, and he happily agreed.

Li Wei told me that the key to swimming faster was proper breathing. "You must breathe in through your mouth and breathe out through your ___4___ underwater," he said. I practised this many times. At first, I kept swallowing water and coughing. But Li Wei was very ___5___. He stayed beside me and corrected my movements again and again.

After about a week, I could feel the ___6___. My strokes became smoother and I did not get tired as quickly as before. I even tried the freestyle stroke, which was much harder but also more exciting. Li Wei said my progress was impressive.

The swimming pool also brought the community closer together. Every evening, families would gather there. Parents watched their children play while chatting with ___7___. Old people did gentle exercises in the warm pool. It became a place where everyone could relax and enjoy.

By the end of August, I could swim two full lengths without ___8___. I was proud of myself. More importantly, I learned that with the right guidance and enough practice, anyone can ___9___ a new skill.

I am thankful for the community pool. It gave me not only a healthy hobby but also a deeper ___10___ with my friends and neighbours. I cannot wait for next summer.

## 选项

1. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. rules &emsp;&emsp; B. stories &emsp;&emsp; C. lessons &emsp;&emsp; D. prices
3. A. carefully &emsp;&emsp; B. noisily &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
4. A. mouth &emsp;&emsp; B. nose &emsp;&emsp; C. eyes &emsp;&emsp; D. ears
5. A. strict &emsp;&emsp; B. patient &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. quiet
6. A. pressure &emsp;&emsp; B. danger &emsp;&emsp; C. difference &emsp;&emsp; D. pain
7. A. strangers &emsp;&emsp; B. teachers &emsp;&emsp; C. neighbours &emsp;&emsp; D. classmates
8. A. thinking &emsp;&emsp; B. stopping &emsp;&emsp; C. moving &emsp;&emsp; D. breathing
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. master &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. drop
10. A. argument &emsp;&emsp; B. competition &emsp;&emsp; C. connection &emsp;&emsp; D. difference

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和朋友们非常___，因为终于有了一个在炎热天气里可以去的清凉地方。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"生气的"。新游泳池开放让人兴奋，"excited"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：救生员向大家解释了___：不准跑、不准推人、不准在浅水区跳水。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"课程"；D 项意为"价格"。后文列举的是安全规定，"rules"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词/形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他在水里游得那么___，让我有点嫉妒。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"吵闹地"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"慢"。前文说李伟游泳像鱼一样，"fast"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（常识判断）
**【解析】** 本句意为：在水下要用___呼气。A 项意为"嘴"；B 项意为"鼻子"；C 项意为"眼睛"；D 项意为"耳朵"。游泳时嘴吸气、鼻子在水下呼气是正确的换气方法，"nose"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但李伟非常___。A 项意为"严格的"；B 项意为"耐心的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"安静的"。他一直在旁边反复纠正动作，体现了耐心，"patient"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大约一周后，我能感觉到___。A 项意为"压力"；B 项意为"危险"；C 项意为"不同/变化"；D 项意为"疼痛"。后文说动作更顺畅、不那么累了，说明有了变化，"difference"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：家长们看孩子玩水，同时和___聊天。A 项意为"陌生人"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"邻居"；D 项意为"同学"。社区游泳池里的家庭，互相是邻居关系，"neighbours"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能不___地游完两个全程。A 项意为"思考"；B 项意为"停下"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"呼吸"。体力进步后可以不间断地游完全程，"stopping"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要有正确的指导和足够的练习，任何人都能___一项新技能。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"掌握"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"放下"。"master a skill"意为"掌握技能"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它不仅给了我一个健康的爱好，还给了我与朋友和邻居之间更深的___。A 项意为"争吵"；B 项意为"竞赛"；C 项意为"联系"；D 项意为"不同"。"connection"表示人际之间的联系和纽带。故选 C。
