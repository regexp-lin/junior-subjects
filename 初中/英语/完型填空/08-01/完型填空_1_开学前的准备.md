# 中考英语完形填空：开学前的准备

## 完形填空原文

The summer holiday was nearly over. In just one week, I would go back to school. My mother reminded me to start getting ready. "You cannot leave everything to the ___1___ minute," she said.

First, I checked my school bag. It was old and one of the straps was broken. My father took me to a shop and we picked a new one together. It was dark blue with a strong zip. I liked it very much because it had several ___2___ where I could put my books, pens, and water bottle separately.

Next, I went through my notebooks and textbooks from last term. Some of the notebooks were only half used, so I decided to ___3___ using them instead of buying new ones. This was a good way to save money and reduce waste. My mother was pleased with my decision.

Then I made a study plan for the new term. I wrote down the subjects I found ___4___ last year — maths and physics — and decided to spend more time on them. I also planned to preview each lesson before class so that I could follow the teacher more ___5___. I stuck the plan on the wall above my desk where I could see it every day.

My mother also suggested that I adjust my sleeping schedule. During the holiday, I often stayed up late and woke up after nine o'clock. But school starts at seven thirty, so I needed to go to bed ___6___ and get up early. The first few days were hard because my body was used to the holiday routine, but I managed to change step by step.

On the last day of the holiday, I laid out my school uniform, packed my bag, and sharpened my pencils. Everything was ___7___. I felt a mix of nervousness and excitement. A new term means new knowledge, new challenges, and new chances to ___8___.

Looking back, I am glad that I prepared early. Starting school without any rush made me feel calm and ___9___. I think good preparation is half the success. If you plan ahead, you will be ready for ___10___ comes your way.

## 选项

1. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. last &emsp;&emsp; C. next &emsp;&emsp; D. right
2. A. colours &emsp;&emsp; B. pockets &emsp;&emsp; C. buttons &emsp;&emsp; D. handles
3. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. continue &emsp;&emsp; C. start &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoy
4. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. easily &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
6. A. later &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. longer &emsp;&emsp; D. faster
7. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. broken &emsp;&emsp; C. ready &emsp;&emsp; D. wrong
8. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. play &emsp;&emsp; C. improve &emsp;&emsp; D. complain
9. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. confused &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. confident
10. A. whoever &emsp;&emsp; B. whatever &emsp;&emsp; C. wherever &emsp;&emsp; D. whenever

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你不能把所有事情都留到___一刻。A 项意为"第一的"；B 项意为"最后的"；C 项意为"下一个的"；D 项意为"正确的"。"the last minute"为固定搭配，意为"最后一刻"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它有好几个___，我可以分别放书、笔和水瓶。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"口袋/夹层"；C 项意为"纽扣"；D 项意为"把手"。书包里分隔放物品的是"pockets"（夹层）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些笔记本只用了一半，所以我决定___使用它们，而不是买新的。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"开始"；D 项意为"享受"。笔记本还没用完，继续用可以省钱，"continue"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写下了去年觉得___的科目——数学和物理。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。后文说要花更多时间在这些科目上，说明这些科目有难度，"difficult"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我计划课前预习，这样上课时能更___地跟上老师。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"容易地"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"大声地"。预习的目的是让上课更轻松，"easily"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我需要___上床睡觉并早起。A 项意为"更晚"；B 项意为"更早"；C 项意为"更长"；D 项意为"更快"。假期晚睡晚起，开学需要早睡早起，"earlier"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一切都___了。A 项意为"丢失的"；B 项意为"坏掉的"；C 项意为"准备好的"；D 项意为"错误的"。前文描述了各种准备工作，最后一切就绪，"ready"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新学期意味着新知识、新挑战和___的新机会。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"玩耍"；C 项意为"提高"；D 项意为"抱怨"。新学期是进步的机会，"improve"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有匆忙地开学让我感到平静和___。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"困惑的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"自信的"。充分准备带来的是自信，"confident"正确。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 复合代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你提前计划，你就能准备好面对___到来的事。A 项意为"无论谁"；B 项意为"无论什么"；C 项意为"无论哪里"；D 项意为"无论何时"。"whatever comes your way"意为"无论遇到什么"。故选 B。
