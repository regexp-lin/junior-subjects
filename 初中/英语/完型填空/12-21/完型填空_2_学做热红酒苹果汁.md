# 中考英语完形填空：学做热红酒苹果汁

## 完形填空原文

Last winter, my mother taught me how to make a warm drink called "hot spiced apple juice." It tastes a bit like mulled wine but has no ___1___. It's perfect for cold winter days. I was curious and wanted to learn.

First, we needed some ___2___: fresh apple juice, cinnamon sticks, cloves, orange slices, and a little honey. Mom went to the supermarket and bought everything we needed. When she came back, she ___3___ all the ingredients on the kitchen table.

"Now, watch carefully," Mom said. She poured the apple juice into a pot and turned on the stove. Then she added the cinnamon and cloves. "We must not ___4___ the heat too high," she warned. "Or the juice will lose its fresh taste." I nodded and remembered her words.

Soon the kitchen was filled with a wonderful ___5___. It smelled like Christmas! I could not help taking a deep breath. "Can I try it now?" I asked. Mom smiled and said we had to ___6___ for another five minutes. She wanted to make sure all the flavors mixed well.

When it was ready, Mom poured the hot juice into two beautiful cups. She added a slice of orange on top of each one. I took a small ___7___ and it was amazing! The drink was sweet, warm, and full of spice. I felt so warm inside.

From that day on, I often make this drink for my family when the weather gets cold. My younger brother loves it too. Making this special drink has become one of our favorite ___8___ to spend time together. I'm ___9___ that Mom took the time to teach me. It's not just a drink—it's a way to show ___10___ to the people we care about.

3. A. hid &emsp;&emsp; B. threw &emsp;&emsp; C. laid &emsp;&emsp; D. broke

5. A. noise &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. color &emsp;&emsp; D. taste
6. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
7. A. bite &emsp;&emsp; B. sip &emsp;&emsp; C. piece &emsp;&emsp; D. spoon
8. A. ways &emsp;&emsp; B. places &emsp;&emsp; C. times &emsp;&emsp; D. reasons
9. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
10. A. love &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它尝起来有点像热红酒，但不含酒精。A 项意为"糖"；B 项意为"盐"；C 项意为"酒精"；D 项意为"水"。前文提到像 mulled wine（热红酒），但适合学生的是无酒精版本。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我们需要一些原料。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"原料、配料"；C 项意为"食谱"；D 项意为"菜肴"。后文列举苹果汁、肉桂等，均为食材原料。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她回来后，把所有的原料摆放在厨房桌上。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"放置、摆"；D 项意为"打破"。lay 可表示"摆放"，lay out/laid 符合语境。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一定不能把火开得太大。A 项意为"转动、调"；B 项意为"保持"；C 项意为"拿"；D 项意为"放"。turn the heat high 表示把火调大。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很快厨房里弥漫着美妙的香味。A 项意为"噪音"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"味道"。后文 It smelled like Christmas 与之呼应。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说我们还得再等五分钟。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"休息"。wait for 表示等待，让味道充分融合。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我抿了一小口，太棒了！A 项意为"咬"；B 项意为"啜饮、小口"；C 项意为"块、片"；D 项意为"勺子"。喝饮料用 sip（小口喝）。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：制作这种特别的饮料已成为我们最喜欢的共度时光的方式之一。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"次数"；D 项意为"原因"。one of our favorite ways 表示最喜欢的方式。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很自豪妈妈花时间教我。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"自豪的、骄傲的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。proud 表示因妈妈愿意花时间教自己而感到自豪与欣慰，符合感恩的语境。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这不仅仅是饮料——它是一种向我们在乎的人表达爱的方式。A 项意为"爱"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"悲伤"。show love 表达关爱，符合温情主题。故选 A。
