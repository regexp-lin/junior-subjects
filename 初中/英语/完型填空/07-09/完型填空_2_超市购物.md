# 中考英语完形填空：超市购物

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, Mum asked me to help her with the shopping. "You are old enough now," she said. "Let us go to the supermarket together." I had been to the supermarket many times before, but I had never paid much ___1___ to how Mum chose things. This time I would learn.

We took a shopping cart and walked through the aisles. Mum had a list in her hand. "First, we need milk, bread and eggs," she said. I found the milk section and picked up a bottle. "Wait," Mum said. "Check the ___2___ date. We should choose one that will last longer." I looked at the dates and chose a bottle that would stay fresh for another week.

When we passed the fruit section, Mum showed me how to pick good apples. "Choose ones that are firm and have a nice ___3___," she said. "Avoid ones with bruises or soft spots." I had never thought that shopping needed so much care. Mum said that small choices could make a big ___4___ to our health and how much money we spent.

At the vegetable counter, Mum taught me another lesson. "Sometimes the vegetables that look a little imperfect are ___5___ and just as good," she said. "We do not need to waste food just because it does not look perfect." I nodded. That made sense.

When we finished, we had a full cart. Mum let me push it to the checkout. The cashier scanned our items and told us the total. Mum handed me her wallet and said, "You pay today." My hands were a little ___6___ as I counted the money. It was the first time I had paid for our family shopping. Mum smiled and said, "You did a good ___7___."

On the way home, I thought about what I had learned. Shopping was not just about buying things. It was about making smart ___8___, comparing prices, and choosing what was best for our family. I felt that I had grown up a little that day. Maybe next time I could help Mum plan the ___9___ before we went. I was starting to understand what it meant to be ___10___ in a family.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未太多___妈妈如何选东西。A 项意为"注意力"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"拜访"。"pay attention to"意为"注意、关注"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看一下___日期。A 项意为"出生"；B 项意为"保质期、到期"；C 项意为"假期"；D 项意为"会议"。选牛奶要看保质期，"expiry date"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：选结实的、___好的苹果。A 项意为"大小"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"价格"；D 项意为"气味"。苹果颜色可反映新鲜度，"colour"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：小选择能对我们的健康和花费产生很大___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"影响"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"改变"。"make a difference"意为"产生影响"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来不太完美的蔬菜有时更___，一样好吃。A 项意为"便宜"；B 项意为"重"；C 项意为"甜"；D 项意为"大"。品相稍差的蔬果通常更便宜，"cheaper"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我数钱时手有点___。A 项意为"凉的"；B 项意为"热的"；C 项意为"稳的"；D 项意为"抖的"。第一次付款紧张手抖，"shaky"正确。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你___做得很好。A 项意为"工作、任务"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"旅行"。"did a good job"意为"做得很好"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：购物是关于做出明智的___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"选择"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"计划"。购物需要做选择，"choices"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许下次我能在去之前帮妈妈规划___。A 项意为"菜单"；B 项意为"清单"；C 项意为"地图"；D 项意为"书"。购物前做购物清单，"list"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始理解在家庭中___意味着什么。A 项意为"有帮助的"；B 项意为"著名的"；C 项意为"富有的"；D 项意为"年轻的"。帮家里购物是参与家庭、成为有用的人，"helpful"正确。故选 A。
