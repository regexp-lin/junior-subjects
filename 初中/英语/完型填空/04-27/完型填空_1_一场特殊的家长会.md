# 中考英语完形填空：一场特殊的家长会

## 完形填空原文

Last term, our school held a different kind of parent-teacher meeting. Instead of talking about grades, the teachers asked parents and students to sit together and ___1___ each other a letter.

"Write what you've never had the courage to say," our head teacher, Ms. Chen, said. The classroom fell silent. I looked at my mother. She was looking at me too. We both had tears in our eyes before we even started ___2___.

I wrote: "Mom, I know you work hard for our family. Sometimes I'm too tired to help with housework. I'm sorry. I love you." My hand was shaking as I ___3___ the paper to her. She read it and hugged me tightly. "I'm sorry too," she said. "I sometimes push you too hard because I want the best for you. I forget you're still a child who needs rest."

Mom's letter to me said: "You are my pride. I don't say it often, but I see how hard you try. Even when you fail, you never give up. I'm ___4___ of you." I had never heard her say those words. I always thought she only cared about my scores.

Around the classroom, I saw other families having similar moments. A father who was always busy finally told his son, "I'm sorry I missed your basketball games." A girl read to her mother, "Thank you for ___5___ me when I was sad." There were hugs, tears, and smiles everywhere.

Ms. Chen said at the end, "Today we didn't talk about grades or rankings. We talked about something more ___6___ — love and understanding. Sometimes we forget to express our feelings. This meeting is a reminder that our families need our words, not just our actions."

That parent-teacher meeting changed the way I communicate with my mother. We now have a "weekly chat" every Sunday. We talk about not only school but also our feelings. Our ___7___ has become much closer.

I learned that love needs to be spoken. Don't assume your family knows how you feel. ___8___ them. Tell them you appreciate them. A simple "thank you" or "I love you" can ___9___ a relationship.

I still keep the letters from that day in my drawer. Whenever I feel distant from my family, I read them again. They remind me of that special afternoon when we finally ___10___ our hearts to each other.

7. A. relationship &emsp;&emsp; B. homework &emsp;&emsp; C. argument &emsp;&emsp; D. secret
8. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. ignore &emsp;&emsp; C. tell &emsp;&emsp; D. blame
9. A. break &emsp;&emsp; B. end &emsp;&emsp; C. warm &emsp;&emsp; D. lose

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师请家长和学生坐在一起，互相___一封信。A 项意为"寄"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"给"。"give each other a letter"表示互相"给"对方一封信，强调当面交流。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们甚至还没开始___，眼里就都有了泪水。A 项意为"说"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"听"；D 项意为"想"。家长会任务是写信，所以"开始写"（writing）前就已感动。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我手抖着把纸___给她。A 项意为"递"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"丢"；D 项意为"藏"。"hand"表示亲手递过去，体现郑重。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："我为你感到___。"A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"累"；C 项意为"自豪"；D 项意为"羞愧"。"be proud of"为固定搭配，妈妈表达对孩子的骄傲。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："谢谢你在难过时___我。"A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"帮"；C 项意为"支持"；D 项意为"推"。"support"强调情感上的支撑和陪伴。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今天我们谈的是更___的东西——爱与理解。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。与分数相比，情感交流更"重要"（important）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的___变得更亲密了。A 项意为"关系"；B 项意为"作业"；C 项意为"争吵"；D 项意为"秘密"。"relationship"指母子之间的关系。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要以为家人知道你的感受。___他们。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"忽视"；C 项意为"告诉"；D 项意为"责备"。"Tell them"表示要把感受说出来。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一句简单的"谢谢"或"我爱你"能___一段关系。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"结束"；C 项意为"温暖"；D 项意为"失去"。"warm"作动词，表示让关系更温暖、更亲密。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们让我想起那个特别的下午，我们终于向彼此___了心。A 项意为"打开"；B 项意为"关闭"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"保护"。"open our hearts"表示敞开心扉、坦诚相待。故选 A。
