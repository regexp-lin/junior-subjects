# 中考英语完形填空：集市上的陶艺

## 完形填空原文

"Would you like to try?" the old man asked with a kind smile. I ___2___ my head. "I'll probably ruin it," I said. "There's no such thing as a mistake in art," he replied. "Every piece is unique." His words encouraged me. I sat down and put my hands on the clay.

At first, the clay didn't obey me. My bowl looked crooked and uneven. I felt ___3___. "Don't give up," the old man said. "Feel the clay. Let your hands learn." I took a deep breath and tried again. This time, I moved more slowly. I paid attention to the shape. Little by little, something like a cup began to ___4___.

"Not bad for a first try," the old man said. He showed me how to smooth the edges and add a simple pattern. Under his guidance, my clumsy cup became something I could be ___5___ of. "Take it home," he said. "It will remind you that beautiful things take time and patience."

I carried my clay cup home carefully. I put it on my desk. Every time I look at it, I remember the old man's words: "Every piece is unique." My cup is not perfect — it has a small crack and the shape is a bit ___6___. But I made it with my own hands. That makes it special.

I later learned that the old man had been making pottery for over fifty years. He came to the market every year, not to sell many pieces, but to ___7___ the craft to young people. "Traditional skills will disappear if no one learns them," he said. "I want to pass them on before it's too late."

That day at the market, I learned something beyond pottery. I learned that perfection is not the only goal. I also learned that some people spend their lives ___9___ knowledge to the next generation. They are the quiet heroes of our culture.

I still have that clay cup. It sits on my desk, a ___10___ of a lesson I learned on a spring day: beauty can be imperfect, and the best teachers are often the kindest ones.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位老人在小桌前___陶罐和花瓶。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"制作"；D 项意为"打碎"。后文说"hands moved...A lump of clay turned into a bowl"，是现场"制作"（making）陶器。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___了摇头。A 项意为"点头"；B 项意为"摇"；C 项意为"举起"；D 项意为"放低"。后文"I'll probably ruin it"表示不敢尝试，所以是"摇头"（shook）拒绝。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的碗歪歪扭扭、不平整。我感到___。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"放松的"；C 项意为"沮丧的"；D 项意为"兴奋的"。做得不好会让人"沮丧"（discouraged）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一点一点地，像杯子一样的东西开始___。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"落下"；C 项意为"出现"；D 项意为"破碎"。"appear"表示在手中渐渐成型。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在他指导下，我笨拙的杯子变成了我可以为之___的东西。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"累"；C 项意为"自豪"；D 项意为"羞愧"。"be proud of"表示对自己作品感到骄傲。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：形状有点___。A 项意为"完美"；B 项意为"圆"；C 项意为"不均匀"；D 项意为"干净"。与前文" crooked and uneven"呼应，首次尝试形状仍"不均匀"（uneven）。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他每年到集市，不是为了多卖，而是为了向年轻人___手艺。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"传授"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"卖"。"teach the craft"表示传承技艺。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：亲手创造某物的___——尝试、失败、进步——才是重要的。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"过程"；C 项意为"结果"；D 项意为"代价"。"process"强调制作过程中的体验。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些人一生都在把知识___给下一代。A 项意为"保存"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"传递"；D 项意为"发现"。"pass...to"为固定搭配，表示"传承"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它放在我桌上，是春天那天所学道理的___。A 项意为"记忆"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"提醒物"；D 项意为"奖品"。"reminder"指让人想起某事的具体物品。故选 C。
