# 中考英语完形填空：十一月总结与十二月展望

## 完形填空原文

November is almost over. Our headteacher asked us to write a short summary of the past month and our plans for December. I sat down and thought about what had happened.

In November, I joined the school English drama club and made many new friends. I also improved my math scores ___1___ working with a study partner. However, I sometimes stayed up too late and felt tired in the morning. I need to manage my time ___2___ next month.

For December, I have several goals. First, I want to finish reading two English storybooks before the winter break. Second, I plan to exercise ___3___ at least three times a week to stay healthy. Third, I will help my little brother with his homework every weekend. These goals may seem small, but they will help me become a ___4___ person.

Our headteacher said that reflection helps us grow. When we look back at what we have done, we can see our progress and ___5___. When we look forward, we can set clear goals and work toward them. I will keep a simple diary in December to record my daily ___6___.

I am excited about the coming month. December brings the promise of a new beginning. I believe that with ___7___ and effort, I can achieve my goals. Let's welcome December with a positive attitude and make every day ___8___. The end of November is a good time to say ___9___ to the past and hello to the future. I am ready for the ___10___ and challenges that December will bring.

## 选项

1. A. by &emsp;&emsp; B. from &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. without
2. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. less &emsp;&emsp; D. more
3. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. regularly &emsp;&emsp; D. seldom
4. A. weaker &emsp;&emsp; B. busier &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. lazier
5. A. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. achievements &emsp;&emsp; D. fears
6. A. meals &emsp;&emsp; B. activities &emsp;&emsp; C. games &emsp;&emsp; D. mistakes
7. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. laziness
8. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. meaningless &emsp;&emsp; C. count &emsp;&emsp; D. matter
9. A. hello &emsp;&emsp; B. goodbye &emsp;&emsp; C. sorry &emsp;&emsp; D. thanks
10. A. dangers &emsp;&emsp; B. opportunities &emsp;&emsp; C. failures &emsp;&emsp; D. fears

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：通过和学伴一起学习，数学成绩提高了。A 项 by 表示"通过……方式"；B 项意为"从"；C 项意为"与"；D 项意为"没有"。by working 表示通过……方式。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下个月需要更好地管理时间。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更少"；D 项意为"更多"。改进用 manage better。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：计划每周至少定期锻炼三次。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"缓慢地"；C 项意为"定期地"；D 项意为"很少"。规律运动用 regularly。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些目标会帮助我成为更好的人。A 项意为"更弱的"；B 项意为"更忙的"；C 项意为"更好的"；D 项意为"更懒的"。成长目标用 better person。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回顾时能看到进步和成就。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"成就"；D 项意为"恐惧"。progress 与 achievements 对应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：十二月会简单记录每日活动。A 项意为"餐食"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"错误"。日记记录 daily activities。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相信有耐心和努力就能实现目标。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"懒惰"。达成目标需 patience。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：以积极心态迎接十二月，让每一天都有价值。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"无意义的"；C 项 make every day count 意为"让每天都有价值"；D 项 matter 也可，但 count 更常用。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：十一月末是告别过去、迎接未来的好时机。A 项意为"你好"；B 项 say goodbye 意为"告别"；C 项意为"抱歉"；D 项意为"感谢"。与 hello to the future 对应用 goodbye。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：已准备好迎接十二月带来的机遇与挑战。A 项意为"危险"；B 项意为"机遇"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"恐惧"。积极展望用 opportunities。故选 B。
