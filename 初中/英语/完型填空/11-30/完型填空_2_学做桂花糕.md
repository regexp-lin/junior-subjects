# 中考英语完形填空：学做桂花糕

## 完形填空原文

Osmanthus cake is a traditional Chinese dessert that smells wonderful. Last Sunday, my mother taught me how to make it. She said the best time to make osmanthus cake is in autumn when the flowers are in bloom. I was ___1___ to learn because I had always loved its sweet and fragrant taste.

First, we collected fresh osmanthus flowers. Mother said we had to wash them ___2___ and remove any stems or leaves. Then we mixed rice flour, sugar, and a little water to make a soft dough. We added the osmanthus flowers to the mixture. The kitchen soon smelled ___3___—like a sweet autumn garden.

Next, we poured the mixture into a steaming tray. Mother showed me how to smooth the surface with a spoon. We had to be ___4___ so that the cake would cook evenly. Then we put the tray into the steamer and waited for about twenty minutes. The waiting was the hardest part—I kept peeking to see if it was ready.

When the cake was done, we cut it into small squares. The cake was soft, sticky, and had a beautiful golden color with flower pieces inside. I took a bite and the osmanthus flavor filled my mouth. It was ___5___ than any store-bought cake. Mother said we could pack some and give them to our ___6___.

Making osmanthus cake taught me that traditional foods connect us with the seasons and our culture. I plan to make it ___7___ next autumn when the flowers bloom. This sweet memory will always stay in my heart. My grandmother used to make this cake when she was ___8___, and now I am learning to carry on the tradition. Every bite reminds me of family, love, and the ___9___ of simple homemade food. I hope to pass this ___10___ to the next generation one day.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很兴奋能学，因为一直喜欢它香甜的味道。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"累的"。学习喜爱食物用 excited。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说必须仔细清洗，去掉花梗和叶子。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"仔细地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"缓慢地"。清洗食材需 carefully。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房很快飘出美妙的香气。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美妙的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"冷的"。桂花香用 wonderful。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：得小心操作才能均匀蒸熟。A 项意为"粗心的"；B 项意为"小心的"；C 项意为"懒惰的"；D 项意为"快的"。均匀蒸制需 careful。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比店里买的好吃多了。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更酸"；D 项意为"更便宜"。 homemade 比 store-bought better。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：可以打包一些送给邻居。A 项意为"敌人"；B 项意为"邻居"；C 项意为"陌生人"；D 项意为"老师"。分享美食给 neighbors。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：打算明年秋天桂花盛开时再做。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"再"；C 项意为"很少"；D 项意为"一次"。make it again 表示再做。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶年轻时经常做这种糕点。A 项意为"年轻的"；B 项意为"年老的"；C 项意为"生病的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。过去做糕点用 when young。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一口都让我想起家人、爱和家常美食的美好。A 项意为"成本"；B 项意为"味道"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"美好"。家庭温暖与 beauty 呼应。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望有一天能把这门技艺传给下一代。A 项意为"考试"；B 项意为"技能"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"礼物"。传统手艺是 skill。故选 B。
