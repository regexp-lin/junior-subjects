# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工香包

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my aunt came to visit us. She is good at making handmade sachets. When I saw the beautiful sachets she had made, I asked her to ___1___ me how to make one. She agreed with a smile.

First, we needed to prepare some ___2___: cloth, thread, dried flowers, and a little cotton. My aunt said we could use any color of cloth we liked. I chose a piece of red cloth because it looked warm and traditional.

We cut the cloth into two small squares of the same ___3___. Then we sewed three sides together, leaving one side open. I had never sewn before, so my stitches were not very neat. My aunt was patient and told me that ___4___ makes perfect. I tried again and again until I did it better.

Next, we filled the small bag with dried flowers and a little cotton. The flowers had a sweet ___5___. My aunt said that sachets could keep the smell for a long time. We could put them in our drawers or hang them in our rooms.

After filling, we ___6___ the last side. My sachet was finally ready! It wasn't as perfect as my aunt's, but I was ___7___ of it. I gave it to my mother as a gift. She was so happy that she put it in her handbag right away.

Making sachets is a traditional Chinese ___8___. Through this activity, I learned not only a skill but also something about our culture. I hope to make more sachets for my friends and family. Maybe I can ___9___ different shapes and colors next time. It was a meaningful and ___10___ afternoon.

2. A. tools &emsp;&emsp; B. materials &emsp;&emsp; C. gifts &emsp;&emsp; D. books
3. A. length &emsp;&emsp; B. size &emsp;&emsp; C. width &emsp;&emsp; D. height
4. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. practice &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. help
5. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. sound &emsp;&emsp; D. color
6. A. opened &emsp;&emsp; B. closed &emsp;&emsp; C. cut &emsp;&emsp; D. touched
7. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. shy
8. A. sport &emsp;&emsp; B. subject &emsp;&emsp; C. craft &emsp;&emsp; D. game
9. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. sell &emsp;&emsp; D. find
10. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. tiring &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoyable &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到她做的漂亮香包，请她教我怎么做。A 项意为"展示"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"告诉"；D 项意为"给"。teach sb. how to do 意为"教某人如何做"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先我们要准备一些材料。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"材料"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"书"。布、线、干花等是 materials。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把布剪成两片相同大小的小方块。A 项意为"长度"；B 项意为"大小"；C 项意为"宽度"；D 项意为"高度"。of the same size 意为"同样大小"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与习语
**【解析】** 本句意为：阿姨耐心地说熟能生巧。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"帮助"。Practice makes perfect 为固定搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：干花有香甜的气味。A 项意为"味道"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"声音"；D 项意为"颜色"。sweet 修饰 smell 表香气。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：填满后，我们缝上最后一边。A 项意为"打开"；B 项意为"关上/合上"；C 项意为"剪"；D 项意为"触摸"。缝上开口处用 closed。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然不如阿姨做的完美，但我为自己的作品感到自豪。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"害羞的"。be proud of 意为"以……为傲"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做香包是中国的传统手艺。A 项意为"运动"；B 项意为"科目"；C 项意为"手艺/工艺"；D 项意为"游戏"。手工香包属于 traditional craft。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许下次可以尝试不同的形状和颜色。A 项意为"尝试"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"找到"。try different shapes 意为"尝试不同形状"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是一个有意义且令人愉快的下午。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"累人的"；C 项意为"令人愉快的"；D 项意为"困难的"。与 meaningful 并列用 enjoyable。故选 C。
