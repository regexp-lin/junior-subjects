# 中考英语完形填空：十月总结与十一月展望

## 完形填空原文

October is almost over. Tonight, I sat down at my desk and thought about what I had ___1___ this month. I wanted to make a summary before November comes.

In October, I joined the school reading club. I ___2___ three books and shared my thoughts with my classmates. Reading has become an important part of my daily life. I also took part in the school sports meeting. Though I didn't win any medals, I tried my best and learned the ___3___ of teamwork.

However, I had some problems too. I was sometimes ___4___ for morning classes because I stayed up late doing homework. My math teacher said I needed to manage my time better. I agree. In November, I will go to bed ___5___ and get up earlier.

For the new month, I have made some plans. First, I want to improve my English. I will listen to English songs and watch short English videos every day. Second, I hope to ___6___ more exercise. I plan to run in the school playground for 15 minutes after school. Third, I will help my mother with housework at least once a week. She works hard and I should do my ___7___.

I wrote all these plans in my diary. I believe that if I stick to them, I will become a ___8___ person. The end of October is not an end—it is a new beginning. Let's welcome November with a ___9___ heart and work towards our goals!

What about you? Have you thought about your October summary and November ___10___?

## 选项

1. A. done &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. broken
2. A. wrote &emsp;&emsp; B. bought &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. borrowed
3. A. habit &emsp;&emsp; B. importance &emsp;&emsp; C. way &emsp;&emsp; D. reason
4. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. late &emsp;&emsp; C. quick &emsp;&emsp; D. slow
5. A. later &emsp;&emsp; B. earlier &emsp;&emsp; C. sooner &emsp;&emsp; D. faster
6. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. have &emsp;&emsp; D. do
7. A. homework &emsp;&emsp; B. part &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. room
8. A. weaker &emsp;&emsp; B. worse &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. slower
9. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. full &emsp;&emsp; C. heavy &emsp;&emsp; D. warm
10. A. memories &emsp;&emsp; B. plans &emsp;&emsp; C. problems &emsp;&emsp; D. mistakes

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在书桌前思考这个月我做了什么。A 项意为"做"；B 项意为"丢失"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"打碎"。总结十月 what I had done。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我读了三本书并和同学分享想法。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"借"。读书俱乐部里 read books。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尽管没获奖牌，但我尽力了，并学到了团队合作的重要性。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项意为"重要性"；C 项意为"方式"；D 项意为"原因"。learn the importance of 意为"学到……的重要性"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我因为熬夜做作业而早上课迟到。A 项意为"早"；B 项意为"晚/迟"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"慢"。be late for 意为"迟到"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：十一月我要更早睡觉、更早起床。A 项意为"更晚"；B 项意为"更早"；C 项意为"更快"；D 项意为"更快"。改善迟到需 go to bed earlier。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望多做运动。A 项意为"制造"；B 项意为"带走"；C 项意为"有"；D 项意为"做"。do exercise 意为"做运动"。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈工作辛苦，我应该尽自己的一份力。A 项意为"作业"；B 项意为"部分"；C 项意为"最好"；D 项意为"房间"。do one's part 意为"尽自己的职责"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信坚持计划会让自己变得更好。A 项意为"更弱"；B 项意为"更糟"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更慢"。坚持计划会 become better。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我们怀着温暖的心迎接十一月。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"满的"；C 项意为"重的"；D 项意为"温暖的"。warm heart 表积极心态。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你想过十月的总结和十一月的展望了吗？A 项意为"回忆"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"错误"。November plans 与 summary 对应。故选 B。
