# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校义卖活动

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school held a charity sale to raise money for children in poor areas. I was ___1___ to take part in it with my classmates. We decided to sell some handmade items that we had made during art class.

Early in the morning, we set up our stall on the playground. Many students came to look at our things. A girl ___2___ a small painting and asked about its price. I felt a little nervous when I told her it was fifteen yuan. To my surprise, she said it was a good price and bought it ___3___. Her smile made me feel confident.

As the morning went on, more and more people ___4___ our stall. My friend Wang Ming was good at persuading others. He helped us sell several bookmarks and key chains. By noon, we had already ___5___ over two hundred yuan. We were so excited!

In the afternoon, a teacher came to our stall and picked up a handmade bracelet. She said she wanted to buy it for her daughter. We gave her a small ___6___ because she was so kind. She thanked us and wished us success.

When the sale ended, we counted the money together. Our class had raised 580 yuan in total! Our headteacher said we did a great ___7___. This experience taught me that working together can make a real ___8___. I hope the money will ___9___ those children in need. I look forward to joining more charity activities in the ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. pointed at &emsp;&emsp; B. looked for &emsp;&emsp; C. threw away &emsp;&emsp; D. gave up
3. A. immediately &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. nearly
4. A. left &emsp;&emsp; B. visited &emsp;&emsp; C. built &emsp;&emsp; D. repaired
5. A. spent &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. borrowed &emsp;&emsp; D. collected
6. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. discount &emsp;&emsp; C. prize &emsp;&emsp; D. bill
7. A. difference &emsp;&emsp; B. job &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
8. A. difference &emsp;&emsp; B. mistake &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
9. A. hurt &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
10. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. future &emsp;&emsp; C. morning &emsp;&emsp; D. evening

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很兴奋能参加义卖活动。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。参加慈善义卖应该是 excited。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个女孩指着一幅小画询问价格。A 项意为"指向"；B 项意为"寻找"；C 项意为"扔掉"；D 项意为"放弃"。女孩要买画，应是 pointed at。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她立即购买了。A 项意为"立即"；B 项意为"缓慢地"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"几乎"。女孩认可价格后 immediately 购买。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：越来越多的人来参观我们的摊位。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"参观/光顾"；C 项意为"建造"；D 项意为"修理"。visiting our stall 表示来买东西。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到中午我们已经募集了超过两百元。A 项意为"花费"；B 项意为"丢失"；C 项意为"借"；D 项意为"募集/收集"。raise money 对应 collected。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们给了她一点折扣因为她很友善。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"折扣"；C 项意为"奖品"；D 项意为"账单"。给老师优惠用 discount。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任说我们做得很好。A 项意为"区别"；B 项意为"工作/事情"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"选择"。do a great job 意为"做得很好"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个经历让我明白齐心协力能带来真正的改变。A 项意为"区别/改变"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"选择"。make a difference 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望这些钱能帮助那些需要帮助的孩子。A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。慈善筹款目的是 help 贫困儿童。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我期待未来参加更多慈善活动。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"未来"；C 项意为"早上"；D 项意为"晚上"。in the future 意为"在未来"。故选 B。
