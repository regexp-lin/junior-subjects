# 中考英语完形填空：圣诞节了解西方文化

## 完形填空原文

Christmas is one of the most important ___1___ in Western countries. Last December, our English teacher organized a special class to help us learn about this festival.

She told us that Christmas ___2___ on December 25th. People celebrate it to remember the birth of Jesus Christ. In many families, they put up a Christmas tree and ___3___ it with lights and ornaments. Children often hang stockings by the fireplace, hoping that Santa Claus will ___4___ them gifts.

We also learned about some Christmas traditions. For example, people send Christmas cards to ___5___ their best wishes to friends and family. Many families have a big dinner together on Christmas Eve. Turkey and Christmas pudding are ___6___ food on that day.

Our teacher encouraged us to understand different cultures. She said that learning about Western festivals can help us ___7___ our horizons and make friends with people from around the world. I found it very ___8___ and decided to share what I learned with my parents.

Through this lesson, I came to ___9___ that culture exchange is important. We should respect and learn from each other's traditions. That is the true ___10___ of festivals—bringing people together.

## 选项

1. A. games &emsp;&emsp; B. festivals &emsp;&emsp; C. subjects &emsp;&emsp; D. sports
2. A. falls &emsp;&emsp; B. keeps &emsp;&emsp; C. stays &emsp;&emsp; D. holds
3. A. cover &emsp;&emsp; B. fill &emsp;&emsp; C. decorate &emsp;&emsp; D. draw
4. A. bring &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. carry &emsp;&emsp; D. fetch
5. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. give &emsp;&emsp; C. express &emsp;&emsp; D. pass
6. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. popular &emsp;&emsp; C. terrible &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
7. A. widen &emsp;&emsp; B. raise &emsp;&emsp; C. open &emsp;&emsp; D. build
8. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
9. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. realize &emsp;&emsp; C. imagine &emsp;&emsp; D. notice
10. A. rule &emsp;&emsp; B. reason &emsp;&emsp; C. spirit &emsp;&emsp; D. result

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：圣诞节是西方国家最重要的节日之一。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"节日"；C 项意为"科目"；D 项意为"运动"。Christmas 是节日，与 festivals 搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：圣诞节在12月25日。A 项意为"落在、适逢"；B 项意为"保持"；C 项意为"停留"；D 项意为"举行"。"fall on" 表示日期"落在某一天"，为固定搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人们用彩灯和装饰品装饰圣诞树。A 项意为"覆盖"；B 项意为"装满"；C 项意为"装饰"；D 项意为"画"。decorate...with 表示"用……装饰"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望圣诞老人会给他们带来礼物。A 项意为"带来"；B 项意为"拿走"；C 项意为"搬运"；D 项意为"取来"。Santa Claus brings gifts 符合圣诞老人送礼物的情景。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人们寄圣诞卡片来表达对亲友的美好祝愿。A 项意为"展示"；B 项意为"给予"；C 项意为"表达"；D 项意为"传递"。express one's wishes 表示"表达祝愿"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：火鸡和圣诞布丁是那天受欢迎的食物。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"受欢迎的"；C 项意为"糟糕的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。传统圣诞食物应描述为"受欢迎的"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：了解西方节日可以帮助我们开阔眼界。A 项意为"拓宽"；B 项意为"提高"；C 项意为"打开"；D 项意为"建造"。"widen one's horizons" 为固定搭配，意为"开阔眼界"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得非常有趣，决定和父母分享所学。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"简单的"。学习新文化应感到"有趣"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：通过这节课，我意识到文化交流很重要。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"意识到"；C 项意为"想象"；D 项意为"注意到"。"come to realize" 表示"逐渐意识到"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那就是节日的真正意义——把人们团结在一起。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"原因"；C 项意为"精神、意义"；D 项意为"结果"。the spirit of festivals 表示"节日的意义"。故选 C。
