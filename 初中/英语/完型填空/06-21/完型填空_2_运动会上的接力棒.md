# 中考英语完形填空：运动会上的接力棒

## 完形填空原文

The school sports day is one of the most exciting events of the year. My favorite event is the relay race. Four runners in each team pass a baton from one to another. The baton is a small stick, but it means something big for our team. It carries our hope and ___1___.

Last year, I was the third runner. I stood in my lane and waited for my teammate. When I saw him running toward me, my heart ___2___ fast. I stretched out my hand to receive the baton. The handover must be smooth. If we drop it, we might lose the race. I caught it firmly and started to run as ___3___ as I could.

Running with the baton in my hand, I felt the weight of responsibility. My teammates had done their best. Now it was my ___4___ to run faster. I passed the baton to the last runner, who sprinted to the finish line. We didn't win first place, but we tried our best. The baton had connected all four of us. We learned that ___5___ matters more than winning.

This year, I will run again. I have been practicing the handover with my teammates. We need to ___6___ the right moment and the right speed. Sometimes we make mistakes, but we never give up. We help each other improve. The baton has taught us to work as a ___7___.

The relay race is different from other races. You cannot win alone. You need to trust your teammates and pass the baton well. The baton is a ___8___ of teamwork. When I hold it, I feel that I am part of something bigger than myself. I am running not just for me, but for my whole team.

I will never forget the feeling of passing the baton. It is a moment of connection and trust. The baton may be small, but it ___9___ a powerful message: we are together. I hope our team can run better this year and create new ___10___ together.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接力棒承载着我们的希望和___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"骄傲"。"pride"与"hope"并列，表示团队的希望与荣耀。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到队友朝我跑来时，我的心___得很快。A 项意为"跳、击打"；B 项意为"移动"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"停留"。"heart beat fast"为固定表达，指心跳加速、紧张激动。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我稳稳接住接力棒，尽可能___地跑。A 项意为"慢"；B 项意为"远"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"晚"。"as fast as I could"与接力赛情境相符，要全力冲刺。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：队友已尽力，现在轮到我来跑了。A 项意为"轮次、轮到"；B 项意为"主意"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"问题"。"it was my turn"为常用表达，意为"轮到我了"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们明白了___比获胜更重要。A 项意为"练习"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"努力"。"effort"与"tried our best"相呼应，强调尽力而为的意义。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们要___正确的时机与速度。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"抓住"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"选择"。"catch"与"right moment"搭配，指把握交接时机。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接力棒教会我们作为___协作。A 项意为"班级"；B 项意为"团队"；C 项意为"小组"；D 项意为"家庭"。"team"与接力赛、teammates 相呼应，强调团队精神。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接力棒是团队协作的___。A 项意为"图画"；B 项意为"象征、标志"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"尺寸"。"sign"与"teamwork"搭配，指团队精神的象征。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接力棒虽小，却___着有力的信息：我们在一起。A 项意为"传递、承载"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"说"；D 项意为"买"。"carries"与"message"搭配，carry a message 意为"传递信息"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望今年我们队能跑得更好，一起创造新的___。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"比赛"；C 项意为"回忆"；D 项意为"奖品"。"memories"与"never forget"、"create together"相呼应，指共同创造的美好回忆。故选 C。
