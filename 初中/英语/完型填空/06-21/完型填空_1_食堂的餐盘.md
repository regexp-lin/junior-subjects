# 中考英语完形填空：食堂的餐盘

## 完形填空原文

Every day at lunchtime, we go to the school canteen. There, we each get a tray to hold our food. The trays are simple and plastic, but they play an important ___1___ in our school life. When I hold my tray, I walk carefully to avoid spilling. I have learned to ___2___ my steps and keep my balance.

The canteen workers put different dishes on our trays. We can choose what we like. Some students always take vegetables because they want to stay ___3___. Others prefer meat and rice. I think it's good to have a balanced meal. The tray helps us carry everything at once. Without it, we would need to make many ___4___ to get our food.

After we finish eating, we take our trays to the collection area. We should put them in the right place. This is a small thing, but it shows our ___5___ to keep the canteen clean. When everyone does this, the workers have less work to do. I feel happy when I see a tidy canteen.

Sometimes I sit with my friends and we share the same table. We put our trays side by side and talk while eating. The tray becomes a ___6___ that brings us together. We discuss homework, share stories, and laugh together. These lunch breaks are some of my favorite ___7___ at school.

The canteen tray has taught me something simple but important. It reminds me that small things can be useful. It also reminds me to be ___8___ and take care of shared things. When I graduate, I will remember the trays, the food, and the happy moments with my classmates. The tray is not just for food—it carries our ___9___ and friendship. I am ___10___ for these ordinary days at the canteen.

1. A. role &emsp;&emsp; B. place &emsp;&emsp; C. way &emsp;&emsp; D. time
2. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. count &emsp;&emsp; C. control &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
3. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. healthy &emsp;&emsp; C. tall &emsp;&emsp; D. clever
4. A. plans &emsp;&emsp; B. decisions &emsp;&emsp; C. calls &emsp;&emsp; D. trips
5. A. wish &emsp;&emsp; B. habit &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. promise
6. A. tool &emsp;&emsp; B. table &emsp;&emsp; C. bridge &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
7. A. subjects &emsp;&emsp; B. lessons &emsp;&emsp; C. moments &emsp;&emsp; D. grades
8. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. brave &emsp;&emsp; C. strict &emsp;&emsp; D. busy
9. A. books &emsp;&emsp; B. memories &emsp;&emsp; C. bags &emsp;&emsp; D. dreams
10. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. thankful

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：餐盘虽简单，却在校园生活中扮演重要的___。A 项意为"角色"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"方式"；D 项意为"时间"。"play an important role"为固定搭配，意为"扮演重要角色"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了___步伐、保持平衡。A 项意为"放慢"；B 项意为"数"；C 项意为"控制"；D 项意为"跟随"。"control my steps"与"keep my balance"相呼应，指控制步伐以防洒出。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有同学常选蔬菜，因为想保持___。A 项意为"强壮的"；B 项意为"健康的"；C 项意为"高的"；D 项意为"聪明的"。"healthy"与"vegetables"相呼应，多吃蔬菜保持健康。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有餐盘，我们得往返多次才能取完食物。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"电话"；D 项意为"往返、趟"。"make many trips"指多次往返取餐。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这件小事体现我们保持食堂整洁的___。A 项意为"愿望"；B 项意为"习惯"；C 项意为"理由"；D 项意为"承诺"。"habit"与"put them in the right place"相呼应，指养成归位的好习惯。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：餐盘成为把我们聚在一起的___。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"桌子"；C 项意为"桥梁"；D 项意为"礼物"。"bridge"在此喻指连接、纽带，bring us together 表示把我们联系在一起。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些午休时光是学校里我最喜欢的___之一。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"课程"；C 项意为"时刻"；D 项意为"成绩"。"moments"与"lunch breaks"、"sit with friends"相呼应，指美好时光。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它提醒我要___，爱护公物。A 项意为"细心的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"严格的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。"careful"与"take care of shared things"相呼应。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：餐盘承载的不仅是食物，还有我们的___和友谊。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"包"；D 项意为"梦想"。"memories"与"remember"、"happy moments"相呼应。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___在食堂度过的这些平凡日子。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"感激的"。"thankful"与"ordinary days"、"happy moments"相呼应，表达感恩。故选 D。
