# 中考英语完形填空：学做寿司

## 完形填空原文

My cousin from Japan came to visit us during the summer holiday. She offered to teach me how to make sushi. I had eaten sushi in restaurants but had never tried to make it myself. I was ___1___ to learn.

First, we prepared the ingredients: rice, seaweed, vinegar, sugar, and some fresh vegetables. My cousin said the rice was very important. It had to be cooked in a ___2___ way and mixed with vinegar and sugar while it was still warm. I watched her carefully as she showed me each step.

The hardest part was spreading the rice on the seaweed. I had to wet my hands to prevent the rice from sticking. I placed a thin layer of rice on the seaweed sheet, but I made it too thick in some places and too thin in others. My cousin smiled and said, "Don't worry. It takes ___3___. You will get better with practice." She showed me again how to spread the rice evenly.

Then we added the filling. We used cucumber, carrot, and egg. I cut the vegetables into long thin strips. My cousin said the shape and size ___4___ a lot for the final look of the sushi. I tried to cut them as evenly as possible, but my strips were a bit uneven. "That's okay for the first time," my cousin said kindly.

The next step was rolling. I placed the filling in the middle of the rice and slowly rolled the seaweed sheet. I had to roll it ___5___ so that it would not fall apart. The first roll was loose and looked messy. The second one was a little better. By the third try, I managed to make a roll that looked almost ___6___.

We cut the rolls into small pieces. My cousin used a sharp knife and dipped it in water between cuts. I tried to do the same. The pieces were not as neat as hers, but they were acceptable. We arranged them on a plate and ___7___ them with some soy sauce and wasabi.

When we sat down to eat, I felt proud. The sushi I had made did not taste as good as the restaurant's, but eating it was ___8___ because I had created something with my own hands. My cousin said, "Making food for others is a way to show love. When you cook for your family, you are giving them something special." I will remember her words and try to ___9___ more homemade meals for my family. Cooking can be a way to express our care and ___10___.

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. eager &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. wrong &emsp;&emsp; D. fast
3. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. luck &emsp;&emsp; C. practice &emsp;&emsp; D. tools
4. A. mattered &emsp;&emsp; B. cost &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. saved
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. tightly &emsp;&emsp; C. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
6. A. professional &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
7. A. covered &emsp;&emsp; B. served &emsp;&emsp; C. filled &emsp;&emsp; D. mixed
8. A. delicious &emsp;&emsp; B. disappointing &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. wasteful
9. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. prepare &emsp;&emsp; C. waste &emsp;&emsp; D. forget

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___学。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"渴望的"；C 项意为"疲倦的"；D 项意为"生气的"。"eager to learn"意为"渴望学习"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须用___方式煮。A 项意为"特殊的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"错误的"；D 项意为"快速的"。寿司米有专门煮法，"special"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这需要___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"练习"；D 项意为"工具"。做好需要多练，"practice"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：形状和大小对寿司最终外观___很大。A 项意为"重要"；B 项意为"花费"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"节省"。"matter a lot"意为"很重要"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须___卷才不会散。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"紧实地"；C 项意为"粗心地"；D 项意为"大声地"。"roll tightly"使卷紧实。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第三卷看起来几乎___。A 项意为"专业的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"空的"。越做越好，接近专业水准，"professional"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：摆盘并___酱油和芥末。A 项意为"覆盖"；B 项意为"配/侍奉"；C 项意为"填满"；D 项意为"混合"。"serve with"意为"配上……食用"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：味道不如餐厅的，但吃的时候很___，因为是亲手做的。A 项意为"美味的"；B 项意为"令人失望的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"浪费的"。亲手做的有特殊意义，"special"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会多给家人___家常菜。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"准备"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"忘记"。"prepare meals"意为"做饭"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烹饪可表达我们的关怀和___。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"爱"；D 项意为"后悔"。做饭给家人是表达爱的方式，"love"正确。故选 C。
