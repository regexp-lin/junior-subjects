# 中考英语完形填空：参观科技馆

## 完形填空原文

Our class went on a trip to the Science Museum last month. We were all excited because we had studied many science topics in our textbooks, and now we could see them come to ___1___.

The museum was a large modern building. When we entered, we saw a huge model of the solar system hanging from the ceiling. The planets moved slowly around the sun. I had learned about this in class, but seeing it with my own eyes was much more ___2___.

Our guide took us to the first floor, which was about human inventions. We saw old telephones, the first computers, and many other machines. I was ___3___ by how technology had changed over the years. The first computer was as big as a room, but now we hold tiny smartphones in our hands.

On the second floor, there was an experiment area. We could touch and try some of the exhibits. I tried a machine that showed how electricity works. I turned a handle, and a small light bulb lit up. It was amazing to see the ___4___ between my action and the result. My classmate Lin Tao tried a balance board. He had to stand in the right position to keep the board level. He kept failing at first, but after several ___5___, he succeeded. We all cheered for him.

The most interesting part was the robot section. A robot could solve a Rubik's cube in less than a minute. It moved so fast that our eyes could barely ___6___ it. Another robot could draw pictures. We gave it a photo of our teacher, and it drew a cartoon version. We laughed when we saw the ___7___.

Before we left, we watched a short film about space exploration. It showed how astronauts live and work on the space station. The film made me ___8___ about the future. Maybe one day I could work in science and help discover new things about our world.

The trip to the Science Museum was both fun and educational. I learned that science is not just in books — it is all around us. I hope to visit again and ___9___ more. The museum often has new exhibitions, so each visit can bring new ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. life &emsp;&emsp; B. school &emsp;&emsp; C. mind &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
2. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. impressive &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
3. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. amazed &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
4. A. distance &emsp;&emsp; B. connection &emsp;&emsp; C. difference &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
5. A. lessons &emsp;&emsp; B. attempts &emsp;&emsp; C. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; D. excuses
6. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. touch &emsp;&emsp; C. hear &emsp;&emsp; D. smell
7. A. photo &emsp;&emsp; B. result &emsp;&emsp; C. robot &emsp;&emsp; D. teacher
8. A. dream &emsp;&emsp; B. worry &emsp;&emsp; C. complain &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
9. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. explore &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
10. A. difficulties &emsp;&emsp; B. surprises &emsp;&emsp; C. problems &emsp;&emsp; D. fears

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在可以看到它们在现实中___。A 项意为"生活/实现"；B 项意为"学校"；C 项意为"思维"；D 项意为"休息"。"come to life"意为"活灵活现、变得真实"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：亲眼所见更加___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"令人印象深刻的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。实物展示比课本更震撼，"impressive"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对科技多年来的变化感到___。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"疲倦的"；C 项意为"惊叹的"；D 项意为"生气的"。"amazed"表示惊讶赞叹。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到我的动作和结果之间的___很有趣。A 项意为"距离"；B 项意为"联系"；C 项意为"差别"；D 项意为"问题"。"connection"指因果关系。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：几次___后他成功了。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"借口"。"after several attempts"意为"几次尝试之后"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：动得太快眼睛几乎跟不上。A 项意为"跟随"；B 项意为"触摸"；C 项意为"听"；D 项意为"闻"。"follow"在此表示眼睛跟不上速度。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到___我们笑了。A 项意为"照片"；B 项意为"结果"；C 项意为"机器人"；D 项意为"老师"。看到机器人画的漫画效果，"result"指画画的结果。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：影片让我对未来___。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"抱怨"；D 项意为"忘记"。"dream about"意为"憧憬"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望再去___更多。A 项意为"读"；B 项意为"探索"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"离开"。"explore"意为探索、参观。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次参观都能带来新的___。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"惊喜"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"恐惧"。"new surprises"意为"新惊喜"。故选 B。
