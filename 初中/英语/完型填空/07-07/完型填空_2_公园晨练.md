# 中考英语完形填空：公园晨练

## 完形填空原文

My father has a habit of going to the park every morning for exercise. This summer, he asked me to ___1___ him. At first, I did not want to go. Getting up early during the holiday sounded terrible. But Dad said it would be good for my health, so I agreed to ___2___ it.

The first morning, my alarm went off at five thirty. I dragged myself out of bed and followed Dad to the park. When we arrived, I was surprised to see so many people already there. Some old people were doing tai chi under the trees. A group of women were dancing to ___3___ music. Several men were jogging along the paths.

Dad and I started with a slow jog around the lake. The air was fresh and cool, and the lake reflected the morning sunlight like a ___4___. I had never noticed how beautiful the park was at this hour. After jogging, Dad taught me some simple stretching exercises. "Stretching helps your body stay ___5___ and prevents injuries," he explained.

Over the next few days, I began to enjoy our morning routine. I felt more ___6___ during the day and slept better at night. I also made some new friends at the park. An old gentleman named Mr Zhao taught me how to play badminton. He was over seventy years old but still very ___7___. He could hit the shuttlecock so fast that I could barely return it.

One morning, it started to rain. I thought we would stay home, but Dad put on his raincoat and said, "A little rain cannot ___8___ us." We went to the park anyway and jogged in the light rain. It was a completely different ___9___ — the sound of raindrops on leaves, the smell of wet earth, and the cool breeze on our faces. It was actually wonderful.

By the end of summer, morning exercise had become a part of my life. I ___10___ that a simple habit can change you in ways you never expect.

## 选项

1. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. watch &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
2. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. refuse &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. finish
3. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. lively &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
4. A. mirror &emsp;&emsp; B. cloud &emsp;&emsp; C. painting &emsp;&emsp; D. curtain
5. A. heavy &emsp;&emsp; B. thin &emsp;&emsp; C. flexible &emsp;&emsp; D. weak
6. A. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; B. lazy &emsp;&emsp; C. energetic &emsp;&emsp; D. anxious
7. A. slow &emsp;&emsp; B. fit &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. rich
8. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. follow &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. find
9. A. season &emsp;&emsp; B. experience &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. subject
10. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. doubted &emsp;&emsp; C. imagined &emsp;&emsp; D. realised

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年暑假，他让我___他。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"加入"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"离开"。"join him"意为"加入他/和他一起去"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说对健康有好处，我同意___一下。A 项意为"尝试"；B 项意为"拒绝"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"完成"。虽然不情愿但同意试试，"try it"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一群女士随着___的音乐跳舞。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"活泼的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。晨练跳舞配的是轻快活泼的音乐，"lively"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（比喻）
**【解析】** 本句意为：湖面映着晨光，像一面___。A 项意为"镜子"；B 项意为"云"；C 项意为"画"；D 项意为"窗帘"。湖面反射阳光如同镜面，"mirror"是常见比喻。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：拉伸帮助身体保持___并防止受伤。A 项意为"沉重的"；B 项意为"瘦的"；C 项意为"柔韧的"；D 项意为"虚弱的"。拉伸使身体柔韧，"flexible"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：白天我变得更___。A 项意为"困倦的"；B 项意为"懒惰的"；C 项意为"精力充沛的"；D 项意为"焦虑的"。晨练后白天精神好，"energetic"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他七十多岁了但身体仍然很___。A 项意为"慢的"；B 项意为"健康的、健壮的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"富有的"。老人还能打羽毛球说明很健壮，"fit"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一点雨___不了我们。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"跟随"；C 项意为"阻止"；D 项意为"找到"。小雨不能阻止锻炼的决心，"stop"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是一种完全不同的___。A 项意为"季节"；B 项意为"体验"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"科目"。雨中晨练是独特的体验，"experience"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___一个简单的习惯可以用你意想不到的方式改变你。A 项意为"担心"；B 项意为"怀疑"；C 项意为"想象"；D 项意为"意识到"。通过经历领悟到的道理，"realised"正确。故选 D。
