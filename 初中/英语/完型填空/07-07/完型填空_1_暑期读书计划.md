# 中考英语完形填空：暑期读书计划

## 完形填空原文

At the start of summer, our English teacher gave us a reading challenge. She asked each student to read at least five books during the holiday and write a short ___1___ for each one. Some students thought it was too much work, but I was actually quite happy about it. I love reading.

I went to the city library the next day and ___2___ six books. I chose different types: two novels, one science book, one history book, one collection of poems, and one comic book. My mum looked at the pile on my desk and laughed. "Are you sure you can ___3___ all of these in two months?" she asked. I smiled and said, "Of course!"

I started with a novel called "The Secret Garden." The story was about a lonely girl who discovered a hidden garden and brought it back to ___4___. The more I read, the more I wanted to know what would happen next. I finished the whole book in just three days. Then I wrote my review, sharing what I liked most about the ___5___ and what lessons I learned from it.

The science book was a bit more ___6___. Some of the words were new to me and I had to use a dictionary. But the pictures and experiments inside were fascinating. I learned about how ___7___ work and why the sky is blue. It opened a whole new world for me.

By the end of July, I had finished all six books. I even added two more to my list because I could not ___8___ reading. My mum said reading had changed me. "You are more thoughtful and you express yourself much better now," she said.

When school started again, I handed in my reviews with great ___9___. The teacher read them and said she was very pleased. I realised that reading is not just homework — it is a ___10___ that can be enjoyed for a lifetime.

## 选项

1. A. letter &emsp;&emsp; B. review &emsp;&emsp; C. notice &emsp;&emsp; D. diary
2. A. bought &emsp;&emsp; B. sold &emsp;&emsp; C. borrowed &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote
3. A. carry &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. understand &emsp;&emsp; D. copy
4. A. life &emsp;&emsp; B. school &emsp;&emsp; C. work &emsp;&emsp; D. sleep
5. A. garden &emsp;&emsp; B. picture &emsp;&emsp; C. story &emsp;&emsp; D. library
6. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; C. challenging &emsp;&emsp; D. popular
7. A. computers &emsp;&emsp; B. rainbows &emsp;&emsp; C. cars &emsp;&emsp; D. clocks
8. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. start &emsp;&emsp; C. mind &emsp;&emsp; D. keep
9. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. pride &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
10. A. subject &emsp;&emsp; B. task &emsp;&emsp; C. habit &emsp;&emsp; D. test

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每读完一本书写一篇简短的___。A 项意为"信"；B 项意为"书评"；C 项意为"通知"；D 项意为"日记"。读完书后写的是书评，"review"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天我去市图书馆___了六本书。A 项意为"买了"；B 项意为"卖了"；C 项意为"借了"；D 项意为"写了"。去图书馆是借书，"borrowed"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你确定两个月内能___这些书吗？A 项意为"搬运"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"理解"；D 项意为"抄写"。两个月读完六本书，"finish"意为"读完"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个孤独的女孩发现了一座隐秘花园，把它恢复了___。A 项意为"生命、活力"；B 项意为"学校"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"睡眠"。"bring back to life"意为"使恢复生机"，是固定搭配。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我分享了这个___中我最喜欢的部分和学到的道理。A 项意为"花园"；B 项意为"图画"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"图书馆"。写书评分享的是故事内容，"story"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：科学类的书有点更___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"有挑战性的"；D 项意为"流行的"。后文提到有生词要查字典，说明有难度，"challenging"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我了解了___是如何形成的以及天空为什么是蓝色的。A 项意为"电脑"；B 项意为"彩虹"；C 项意为"汽车"；D 项意为"时钟"。与"天空为什么是蓝色"并列的自然现象，"彩虹如何形成"最合适。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我甚至又多读了两本，因为我停不下来。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"介意"；D 项意为"保持"。"could not stop reading"意为"停不下来"，表达热爱阅读。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我带着极大的___交上了书评。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"自豪"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"悲伤"。完成了挑战，骄傲地交作业，"pride"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阅读不只是作业，它是一种可以终身享受的___。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"任务"；C 项意为"习惯"；D 项意为"考试"。阅读是终身受益的好习惯，"habit"正确。故选 C。
