# 中考英语完形填空：写毛笔字

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, my grandfather invited me to learn Chinese calligraphy. He has been practising calligraphy for over fifty years and his writing is truly ___1___. I had always admired his work but never tried it myself.

On the first day, Grandpa prepared everything we needed: a brush, ink, rice paper, and a felt mat. He first taught me how to ___2___ the brush correctly. "The way you hold the brush decides how your characters will look," he said. I tried to copy his hand position, but it felt ___3___ because I was used to holding a ballpoint pen.

Grandpa asked me to start with simple strokes — horizontal lines and vertical lines. It sounded easy, but when I put the brush on the paper, my lines were shaky and ___4___. Grandpa did not criticise me. Instead, he said, "Every master was once a beginner. Just keep ___5___."

Day after day, I practised the basic strokes. Slowly, my lines became straighter and smoother. After two weeks, Grandpa taught me to write whole ___6___. The first character I learned was "永" (yǒng), which means "forever." He told me this character contains all the basic strokes, so it is the best one to start with.

Writing calligraphy requires great ___7___. You have to control your breathing, focus your mind, and move the brush steadily. If you rush, the characters will look messy. I found that when I wrote calmly, the results were much ___8___.

By the end of summer, I could write several characters quite well. Grandpa said he was ___9___ of my progress. He hung my best piece on the wall beside his own work. Seeing my writing next to his made me feel very honoured.

Learning calligraphy was more than just learning to write beautifully. It taught me discipline, patience, and the importance of ___10___ to improve. I plan to keep practising, just like Grandpa has done for fifty years.

1. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
2. A. hold &emsp;&emsp; B. make &emsp;&emsp; C. wash &emsp;&emsp; D. break
3. A. natural &emsp;&emsp; B. comfortable &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. easy

6. A. sentences &emsp;&emsp; B. characters &emsp;&emsp; C. stories &emsp;&emsp; D. poems
7. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. strength &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. concentration
8. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. fewer &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. smaller
9. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. ashamed

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他练了五十多年书法，字写得真的很___。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"漂亮的"；C 项意为"搞笑的"；D 项意为"简单的"。五十年功力，书法作品很漂亮，"beautiful"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他先教我如何正确地___毛笔。A 项意为"握住"；B 项意为"制作"；C 项意为"清洗"；D 项意为"打断"。学书法首先学握笔姿势，"hold"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感觉很___因为我习惯了握圆珠笔。A 项意为"自然的"；B 项意为"舒适的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"容易的"。握毛笔和握圆珠笔不同，感觉不习惯、奇怪，"strange"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的线条颤抖且___。A 项意为"粗的"；B 项意为"直的"；C 项意为"不均匀的"；D 项意为"彩色的"。初学者线条不稳、粗细不均，"uneven"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个大师都曾是初学者，只要坚持___。A 项意为"说话"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"练习"；D 项意为"阅读"。学书法要坚持练习，"practising"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两周后爷爷教我写整个___。A 项意为"句子"；B 项意为"汉字"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"诗"。从笔画进阶到写完整的汉字，"characters"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写书法需要极大的___。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"力量"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"专注力"。后文提到要控制呼吸、集中精神，需要专注，"concentration"正确。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我静心书写时，效果___得多。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更少"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更小"。心静则字好，"better"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷说他为我的进步感到___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"羞愧的"。"proud of"意为"为……自豪"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会了我纪律、耐心和___提高的重要性。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"假装"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"坚持"。坚持不断提高，"persisting"正确。故选 D。
