# 中考英语完形填空：参加科学展

## 完形填空原文

Last week, our school held a science fair. Students could show their own science projects. I had been working on a project about plants and light for a month. I wanted to ___1___ how different colors of light affect plant growth.

I set up a small experiment at home. I put three bean plants under red, blue, and green light. I watered them every day and wrote down what I ___2___. After four weeks, the plant under blue light grew the fastest. The one under green light grew the slowest. I was excited about my findings and decided to share them at the fair.

On the day of the fair, I brought my plants and my notes to school. Many students and teachers came to look at the projects. At first, I felt a little ___3___. What if nobody liked my project? But when a teacher stopped at my table and asked me questions, I slowly became more confident. I explained my experiment step by step. She smiled and said my work was very ___4___.

During the fair, I also walked around to see other projects. One student had made a simple robot. Another had built a model of the solar system. I was ___5___ by how creative everyone was. We all had different ideas, but we all shared the same love for science.

At the end of the day, the teachers chose the best projects. I did not win first prize, but I got a certificate for "Best Observation." I was very happy. For me, the most important thing was not winning but ___6___ something new and sharing it with others.

The science fair taught me that learning is not just about books and exams. It is about asking questions, doing experiments, and ___7___ your own answers. Science is everywhere around us — we just need to be curious.

I plan to take part in the science fair again next year. I want to try a new project, perhaps something about ___8___. My teacher said she would help me find a good topic. I am already looking forward to it.

If you have a chance to join a science fair, do not ___9___ it. It is a great way to learn, make friends, and discover what you are interested in. Remember: every scientist started as a curious ___10___ who was not afraid to ask "why."

6. A. buying &emsp;&emsp; B. learning &emsp;&emsp; C. winning &emsp;&emsp; D. losing
7. A. copying &emsp;&emsp; B. finding &emsp;&emsp; C. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; D. accepting
8. A. music &emsp;&emsp; B. sports &emsp;&emsp; C. robots &emsp;&emsp; D. games
9. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; C. finish &emsp;&emsp; D. understand
10. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. child &emsp;&emsp; C. parent &emsp;&emsp; D. doctor

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想___不同颜色的光如何影响植物生长。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"知道"；C 项意为"查明/发现"；D 项意为"相信"。"find out"强调通过实验或调查得出结果，符合科学探究语境。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我每天浇水并记录我___的内容。A 项意为"想"；B 项意为"看见/观察到"；C 项意为"听到"；D 项意为"读"。实验需记录观察到的现象，"saw"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我感到有点___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。在众人面前展示项目会紧张，"nervous"恰当。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她微笑着说我的工作非常___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"错误的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。老师肯定学生的项目，"interesting"表示认可。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对大家的创造性感到___。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"累的"。"surprised by"表示对他人创意感到惊喜。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对我而言最重要的是___新事物并与他人分享。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"赢"；D 项意为"输"。强调过程而非结果，"learning"符合科学精神。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学习是提问、做实验并___自己的答案。A 项意为"抄袭"；B 项意为"发现"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"接受"。"finding your own answers"意为主动探索、得出结论。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想尝试新项目，也许是关于___的。A 项意为"音乐"；B 项意为"运动"；C 项意为"机器人"；D 项意为"游戏"。前文提到有同学做了机器人，此处举例呼应，"robots"合适。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果有机会参加科学展，不要___它。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"享受"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"理解"。"do not miss it"意为"不要错过这个机会"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每位科学家最初都是敢于问"为什么"的好奇___。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"孩子"；C 项意为"家长"；D 项意为"医生"。"curious child"强调保持童真和好奇心。故选 B。
