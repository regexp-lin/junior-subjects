# 中考英语完形填空：班级的板报墙

## 完形填空原文

At the back of our classroom, there is a big blackboard. We call it the class bulletin board. Every month, different groups of students design and draw something new on it. Last month, it was my group's turn. At first, we didn't know what to ___1___. We had many ideas but couldn't decide. Then our teacher said, "Why not show something about spring?" We all agreed.

We spent three afternoons working on it. Someone drew flowers and trees. Someone wrote short poems. I was in charge of the ___2___. I tried to make them bright and beautiful. It was not easy, but we worked as a team. When we finished, the board looked colorful and full of life. Our classmates came to look and said it was ___3___. We felt very happy.

The bulletin board is more than just a decoration. It brings our class together. When we work on it, we learn to ___4___ ideas and listen to each other. We also learn that good work takes time. We cannot finish everything in one day. We need to plan, ___5___, and improve step by step.

Every time I look at the bulletin board, I remember the ___6___ we spent while creating it. We laughed when someone made a mistake. We helped each other when someone felt tired. These moments made our friendship stronger. The board has become a ___7___ of our class spirit. It shows that we can create something beautiful when we work together.

I think every class needs a place like this. A place where students can ___8___ their talents and share their thoughts. The bulletin board gives us this chance. It reminds us that we are not just studying for exams—we are also learning to express ourselves and work with others. I am ___9___ to be part of this. The bulletin board has taught me that teamwork and creativity can make ordinary things ___10___.

7. A. sign &emsp;&emsp; B. result &emsp;&emsp; C. part &emsp;&emsp; D. size
8. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. lose &emsp;&emsp; C. test &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
9. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. sorry &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. tired

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我们不知道___什么。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"选择"；C 项意为"找"；D 项意为"保持"。"choose"与"many ideas but couldn't decide"相呼应，表示在多个方案中做选择。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我负责___部分。A 项意为"图画"；B 项意为"标题"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"故事"。"colors"与"make them bright and beautiful"相呼应，指板报的配色与色彩表现。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们来看后说板报很___。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"精彩的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"wonderful"与"colorful and full of life"、"felt very happy"相呼应，表示赞美。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做板报时我们学会___想法、倾听彼此。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"隐藏"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"改变"。"share"与"listen to each other"相呼应，指团队协作。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们需要规划、___并逐步改进。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"离开"。"try"与"plan"、"improve"形成连贯的做事步骤。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次看到板报我就想起创作时共度的___。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"作业"；D 项意为"时刻"。"moments"与"we spent"、"laughed"、"helped each other"相呼应，指共同创作的美好时刻。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：板报已成为我们班级精神的___。A 项意为"标志、象征"；B 项意为"结果"；C 项意为"部分"；D 项意为"尺寸"。"sign"与"class spirit"搭配，表示象征。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生可以___才能、分享想法的场所。A 项意为"展示"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"测试"；D 项意为"隐藏"。"show"与"talents"、"share their thoughts"相呼应。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：能参与其中我很___。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"抱歉的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"累的"。"proud"与"part of this"相呼应，表达参与创作的成就感。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：团队合作与创意能让寻常事物变得___。A 项意为"有用的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"重的"。"special"与"ordinary things"形成对比，升华主题。故选 B。
