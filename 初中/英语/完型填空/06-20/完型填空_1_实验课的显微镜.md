# 中考英语完形填空：实验课的显微镜

## 完形填空原文

In our science class, we often do experiments. Last week, our teacher brought a microscope to the lab. It was the first time I had seen one so close. The teacher said we would use it to look at tiny things that our eyes can ___1___ see. I was very excited.

We put a drop of water under the lens. When I looked through the microscope, I was ___2___. There were so many small living things in just one drop of water! I never knew that such a small world existed. The teacher told us that these tiny creatures are everywhere around us. We just need the right ___3___ to find them.

From that day on, I became more interested in science. I started to ask more questions in class and read science books after school. The microscope taught me an important ___4___: there is always more to discover if we look carefully. Things that seem simple may hide amazing secrets.

I also learned that science needs patience. Sometimes we had to ___5___ the lens many times before we could see clearly. My classmates and I helped each other when we had problems. We shared what we saw and discussed our ___6___. Learning became more fun when we worked together.

Now I understand why our teacher loves the microscope. It opens a new ___7___ for us. It shows us that the world is full of wonder. I hope one day I can have my own microscope and ___8___ exploring the tiny world. Maybe I will become a scientist when I grow up. The microscope has given me a big ___9___ and made me more curious about the world. I am ___10___ for this wonderful lesson.

## 选项

1. A. usually &emsp;&emsp; B. normally &emsp;&emsp; C. easily &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
2. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
3. A. tool &emsp;&emsp; B. book &emsp;&emsp; C. teacher &emsp;&emsp; D. time
4. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. joke &emsp;&emsp; D. rule
5. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. move &emsp;&emsp; C. adjust &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
6. A. answers &emsp;&emsp; B. results &emsp;&emsp; C. ideas &emsp;&emsp; D. plans
7. A. door &emsp;&emsp; B. window &emsp;&emsp; C. road &emsp;&emsp; D. way
8. A. try &emsp;&emsp; B. continue &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. begin
9. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. dream &emsp;&emsp; C. hobby &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
10. A. ready &emsp;&emsp; B. famous &emsp;&emsp; C. thankful &emsp;&emsp; D. sorry

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说要我们用显微镜来看肉眼___看到的小东西。A 项意为"通常"；B 项意为"正常地"；C 项意为"容易地"；D 项意为"几乎不"。"can hardly see"与"tiny things"相呼应，肉眼几乎看不见微小事物，需借助显微镜。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我透过显微镜看时，我___了。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"累的"。"surprised"与"saw so many small living things"相呼应，表达发现微观世界的惊讶。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只需要正确的___来发现它们。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"时间"。"tool"指显微镜，与"right"搭配表示找到合适的工具。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：显微镜教会我一个重要的___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"道理、教训"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"规则"。"lesson"与"taught"相呼应，指人生启示。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们得多次___镜片才能看清。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"移动"；C 项意为"调整"；D 项意为"清洁"。"adjust"与"before we could see clearly"呼应，指调焦对焦。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们分享所见，讨论彼此的___。A 项意为"答案"；B 项意为"结果"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"计划"。"ideas"与"shared what we saw"、"discussed"相呼应，指观察后的想法。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它为我们打开了一扇新的___。A 项意为"门"；B 项意为"窗"；C 项意为"路"；D 项意为"方式"。"open a new door"为常用表达，喻指开启新视野。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望有一天我能有自己的显微镜并___探索微观世界。A 项意为"尝试"；B 项意为"继续"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"开始"。"begin"与"have my own microscope"呼应，开始属于自己的探索之旅。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：显微镜给了我一个大的___，让我对世界更加好奇。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"爱好"；D 项意为"问题"。"dream"与"become a scientist"相呼应。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对这堂精彩的课很___。A 项意为"准备好的"；B 项意为"著名的"；C 项意为"感激的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。"thankful"与"wonderful lesson"相呼应，表达感恩。故选 C。
