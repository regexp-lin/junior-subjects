# 中考英语完形填空：班级英语演讲比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held an English speech contest. My English teacher asked if anyone would like to ___1___. I was nervous, but I decided to try. I wanted to challenge myself and improve my spoken English.

I spent two weeks ___2___ my speech. I chose the topic "My Dream" and wrote it in English. Every evening, I practiced in front of the mirror. At first, I spoke too ___3___, and my voice shook. But day by day, I became more confident.

On the day of the contest, the hall was full of students and teachers. When my ___4___ came, I walked onto the stage. My hands were shaking, but I took a deep breath and began to speak. I tried to remember every word I had practiced. To my surprise, I didn't feel as nervous as before. I even ___5___ at the audience when I talked about my dream of becoming a teacher.

When I finished, everyone clapped. I felt so ___6___ that I had done my best. A few days later, the results came out. I won the second prize! I couldn't ___7___ it when my teacher told me the news. My parents were also very proud of me.

This experience taught me an important ___8___: If we dare to try, we can achieve more than we think. The speech contest was not just about winning. It was about growing and becoming ___9___ than before. I hope to join more activities like this in the ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. join &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. refuse &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
2. A. preparing &emsp;&emsp; B. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; C. copying &emsp;&emsp; D. finishing
3. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. softly &emsp;&emsp; D. clearly
4. A. chance &emsp;&emsp; B. turn &emsp;&emsp; C. idea &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
5. A. shouted &emsp;&emsp; B. smiled &emsp;&emsp; C. cried &emsp;&emsp; D. waved
6. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
7. A. understand &emsp;&emsp; B. believe &emsp;&emsp; C. accept &emsp;&emsp; D. remember
8. A. lesson &emsp;&emsp; B. exam &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. party
9. A. weaker &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. braver &emsp;&emsp; D. quieter
10. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. future &emsp;&emsp; C. present &emsp;&emsp; D. moment

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的英语老师问是否有人愿意参加。A 项意为"参加"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"避免"。参加比赛用 join。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我花了两周时间准备演讲稿。A 项意为"准备"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"抄袭"；D 项意为"完成"。spend time doing 后接 preparing 表准备。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我说话声音太小，而且发抖。A 项意为"大声地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"轻柔地/小声地"；D 项意为"清楚地"。紧张时 voice shook 说明说得太小声。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我的时候，我走上舞台。A 项意为"机会"；B 项意为"轮次"；C 项意为"主意"；D 项意为"选择"。when one's turn comes 意为"轮到某人时"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我甚至在谈论成为老师的梦想时对观众微笑。A 项意为"喊叫"；B 项意为"微笑"；C 项意为"哭泣"；D 项意为"挥手"。演讲时自然微笑，smile at 意为"对……微笑"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到很自豪，因为已经尽了全力。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"自豪的"。做完演讲、观众鼓掌后感到 proud。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当老师告诉我这个消息时，我简直不敢相信。A 项意为"理解"；B 项意为"相信"；C 项意为"接受"；D 项意为"记得"。couldn't believe it 意为"不敢相信"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次经历教会了我重要的一课。A 项意为"课/教训"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"聚会"。teach sb. a lesson 意为"给某人上一课"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是关于成长，变得更勇敢。A 项意为"更弱"；B 项意为"更慢"；C 项意为"更勇敢"；D 项意为"更安静"。敢于尝试的经历让人更 braver。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望将来参加更多这样的活动。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"将来"；C 项意为"现在"；D 项意为"片刻"。in the future 意为"将来"。故选 B。
