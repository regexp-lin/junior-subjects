# 中考英语完形填空：放学后的自行车

## 完形填空原文

My bicycle has been with me since I entered middle school. Every afternoon when the last class ___1___, I walk to the parking area and find my blue bike. Riding home through the streets has become the favorite part of my day.

The ___2___ from school to my home is not very long—about twenty minutes. But during those twenty minutes, I feel completely free. The wind blows through my hair, and I can see the sky changing from blue to orange as the sun ___3___. Sometimes I pass by the old tree on Green Street. I have watched its leaves turn from green to yellow, then fall, and then grow again. The bicycle has helped me ___4___ the seasons.

One rainy afternoon, my bike had a flat tire. I didn't know what to do. A senior student from our school stopped and asked if I needed ___5___. He helped me push the bike to a repair shop nearby. I was grateful and asked for his name. He just smiled and said, "We're all ___6___ here. Help each other." From that day on, I have tried to help others when I can, just like he did.

My bicycle has also brought me closer to my father. On weekends, we often ride together to the park. We talk about school, life, and sometimes nothing ___7___ at all. Those peaceful rides have created our best father-son moments. I realize that the best conversations don't always happen at the dinner table—they can happen anywhere, ___8___ on a simple bike ride.

I know that one day I will outgrow this bicycle and get a new one. But I will never ___9___ the feeling of freedom it gave me, or the lessons about kindness and family that I learned along the way. My bicycle is not just a ___10___ of transportation—it is a part of my growth story.

## 选项

1. A. starts &emsp;&emsp; B. begins &emsp;&emsp; C. ends &emsp;&emsp; D. opens
2. A. road &emsp;&emsp; B. way &emsp;&emsp; C. ride &emsp;&emsp; D. trip
3. A. rises &emsp;&emsp; B. sets &emsp;&emsp; C. shines &emsp;&emsp; D. hides
4. A. notice &emsp;&emsp; B. miss &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. ignore
5. A. advice &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. time
6. A. strangers &emsp;&emsp; B. classmates &emsp;&emsp; C. friends &emsp;&emsp; D. neighbors
7. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. serious &emsp;&emsp; D. new
8. A. even &emsp;&emsp; B. only &emsp;&emsp; C. still &emsp;&emsp; D. yet
9. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. find
10. A. tool &emsp;&emsp; B. toy &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. way

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天下午当最后一节课___时，我走向停车区找到我的蓝色自行车。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"结束"；D 项意为"打开"。"when the last class ends"意为放学时，符合放学骑车的语境。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从学校到我家的___不长——大约二十分钟。A 项意为"道路"；B 项意为"路、方式"；C 项意为"骑行、骑程"；D 项意为"旅行"。"ride"与骑车主题呼应，指骑行路程。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：风拂过发丝，我能看到天空随着太阳___从蓝色变为橙色。A 项意为"升起"；B 项意为"落下"；C 项意为"照耀"；D 项意为"躲藏"。"as the sun sets"指傍晚日落时分，天空呈橙色的景象。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自行车帮助我___四季变化。A 项意为"注意、观察到"；B 项意为"错过"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"忽略"。"notice"与后文树叶颜色变化的细致描写相呼应。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位高年级学长停下来问我是否需要___。A 项意为"建议"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"时间"。"need help"与后文"帮助推车到修车铺"相呼应。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他只是笑笑说："我们都是___。互相帮助。"A 项意为"陌生人"；B 项意为"同学"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"邻居"。"classmates"指同校学生，强调校园互助精神。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们聊学校、生活，有时什么都不___。A 项意为"特别的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"严肃的"；D 项意为"新的"。"nothing special"为固定表达，意为"没什么特别的"，强调轻松随意的陪伴。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最好的对话不总发生在餐桌旁——它们可以发生在任何地方，___在简单的骑行中。A 项意为"甚至"；B 项意为"仅仅"；C 项意为"仍然"；D 项意为"还"。"even"表递进，强调连骑车时也能有深入交流。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我永远不会___它给予我的自由感。A 项意为"记得"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"找到"。"never forget"意为永远不忘，与"will never"表达珍惜记忆。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的自行车不仅仅是___——它是我成长故事的一部分。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"玩具"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"方式"。"a way of transportation"意为交通方式，与前文"not just"形成转折，突出其情感价值。故选 D。
