# 中考英语完形填空：音乐课的钢琴

## 完形填空原文

There is an old piano in the corner of our music room. When I first saw it, I thought it was too ___1___ and old to make beautiful sounds. But our music teacher, Ms. Wang, changed my mind. She sat down and played a simple song. The notes filled the room, and I felt something ___2___ inside my heart.

Ms. Wang said that anyone could learn to play if they practiced with ___3___. She encouraged me to try. At first, my fingers felt stiff and I hit the ___4___ keys many times. I wanted to give up. But Ms. Wang would sit beside me and say, "Every musician ___5___ this. Keep trying." Her words gave me the courage to continue.

Week after week, I practiced. The piano was no longer a strange ___6___. It became my friend. When I felt happy, I would play cheerful songs. When I felt sad, I would play soft music to ___7___ myself. Music became my way of expressing feelings that were hard to put into words.

One day, our class had a small performance. I was so nervous that my hands were shaking. But when I sat at the piano, I took a deep breath and began to play. To my ___8___, the music flowed naturally. My classmates clapped when I finished. At that moment, I understood that the best gift we can give to others is to share what we have learned and ___9___ with them.

Now the piano in our music room reminds me that nothing is impossible if we are ___10___ to learn and never give up. That old piano has taught me more than music—it has taught me about life.

## 选项

1. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. heavy &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty
2. A. break &emsp;&emsp; B. move &emsp;&emsp; C. freeze &emsp;&emsp; D. fall
3. A. patience &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. help
4. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. wrong &emsp;&emsp; C. white &emsp;&emsp; D. black
5. A. avoids &emsp;&emsp; B. finishes &emsp;&emsp; C. passes &emsp;&emsp; D. forgets
6. A. machine &emsp;&emsp; B. object &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. toy
7. A. calm &emsp;&emsp; B. wake &emsp;&emsp; C. push &emsp;&emsp; D. stop
8. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. surprise &emsp;&emsp; C. regret &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
9. A. compete &emsp;&emsp; B. argue &emsp;&emsp; C. grow &emsp;&emsp; D. share
10. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. ready &emsp;&emsp; C. sorry &emsp;&emsp; D. late

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我看到它时，觉得它太___太旧，发不出美妙的声音。A 项意为"大的"；B 项意为"安静的"；C 项意为"重的"；D 项意为"脏的"。"dirty and old"形成并列，描写破旧钢琴的外观，与后文"changed my mind"形成对比。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：音符充满房间，我心中感到有什么东西___了。A 项意为"破碎"；B 项意为"动、触动"；C 项意为"冻结"；D 项意为"落下"。"something move inside"表示内心被触动，符合音乐带来的情感共鸣。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说任何人只要带着___练习，都能学会弹奏。A 项意为"耐心"；B 项意为"金钱"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"帮助"。"patience"与学琴需要持之以恒相呼应。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初手指僵硬，我经常按到___琴键。A 项意为"对的"；B 项意为"错的"；C 项意为"白的"；D 项意为"黑的"。"wrong keys"指弹错音，与"wanted to give up"呼应初学时的困难。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每位音乐家都会___这个阶段。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"经历、度过"；D 项意为"忘记"。"pass through"意为经历，与"Keep trying"鼓励坚持相呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：钢琴不再是一个陌生的___。A 项意为"机器"；B 项意为"物品、东西"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"玩具"。"object"泛指物体，与前文"strange"及后文"became my friend"形成对比。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我感到难过时，我会弹奏轻柔的音乐来___自己。A 项意为"使平静"；B 项意为"叫醒"；C 项意为"推"；D 项意为"停止"。"calm oneself"意为让自己平静下来，符合音乐抚慰情绪的语境。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：令我___的是，音乐自然流淌而出。A 项意为"快乐"；B 项意为"惊讶"；C 项意为"遗憾"；D 项意为"恐惧"。"To my surprise"为固定短语，表示出乎意料，与"so nervous"形成转折。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们能给予他人最好的礼物，就是分享所学并与他们___。A 项意为"竞争"；B 项意为"争论"；C 项意为"成长"；D 项意为"分享"。"share"与"share what we have learned"呼应，强调分享的美好。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要我们___学习且永不放弃，没有什么是不可能的。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"准备好的、愿意的"；C 项意为"抱歉的"；D 项意为"迟的"。"ready to learn"意为乐于学习，与"never give up"共同表达积极态度。故选 B。
