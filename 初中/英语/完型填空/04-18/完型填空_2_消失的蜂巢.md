# 中考英语完形填空：消失的蜂巢

## 完形填空原文

Last spring, a beehive appeared in the old oak tree behind our school. At first, some students were ___1___ and wanted it removed. But our biology teacher, Ms. Lin, said we should leave it alone.

"Bees are important," she explained. "They help plants grow by carrying pollen from flower to flower. Without bees, many of the ___2___ we eat every day would disappear."

Ms. Lin made the beehive part of our science class. We observed the bees from a safe distance, recording their behavior in notebooks. I was amazed at how ___3___ they worked — flying back and forth, never stopping, each bee knowing exactly what to do.

One morning in May, a student ran into the classroom shouting, "The bees are ___4___!" We rushed outside and saw that the beehive was empty. Not a single bee remained.

Ms. Lin looked worried. She explained that bees were disappearing all over the world. "Scientists call it Colony Collapse Disorder," she said. "Nobody knows exactly ___5___ it happens, but pollution, pesticides, and loss of flowers are all possible reasons."

We felt sad. The oak tree looked ___6___ without the busy little bees flying around it.

"Can we do anything to help?" I asked.

Ms. Lin smiled. "Actually, yes. We can plant bee-friendly flowers in our school garden. Bees need flowers for ___7___, and if we give them a reason to come back, maybe they will."

Our class spent the next two weeks planting lavender, sunflowers, and wildflowers around the school. We also put up a small sign: "Bee Garden — Please Don't Pick the Flowers."

By early June, we started hearing a familiar buzzing sound. A few bees appeared among the flowers, then more and more. They didn't return to the old hive, but they built a ___8___ one in a tree nearby.

We cheered. It was a small victory, but it ___9___ us something big: even small actions can make a real difference when we work together to ___10___ the natural world.

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. scared &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. pleased
2. A. meat &emsp;&emsp; B. fruits &emsp;&emsp; C. fish &emsp;&emsp; D. bread
3. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. lazily &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. badly
4. A. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; B. fighting &emsp;&emsp; C. growing &emsp;&emsp; D. gone
5. A. why &emsp;&emsp; B. when &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. who
6. A. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; D. tall
7. A. water &emsp;&emsp; B. shelter &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
8. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. broken &emsp;&emsp; C. new &emsp;&emsp; D. bigger
9. A. cost &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. lent &emsp;&emsp; D. sold
10. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. destroy &emsp;&emsp; C. study &emsp;&emsp; D. protect

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，一些学生很___，想把它移走。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"高兴的"。蜜蜂会蜇人，学生们害怕所以想移走蜂巢，"scared"（害怕的）最符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有蜜蜂，我们每天吃的很多___都会消失。A 项意为"肉"；B 项意为"水果"；C 项意为"鱼"；D 项意为"面包"。蜜蜂通过授粉帮助植物生长，主要影响的是"水果"（fruits）等农作物的产量。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我惊讶于它们工作得多么___。A 项意为"缓慢地"；B 项意为"懒散地"；C 项意为"努力地/辛苦地"；D 项意为"糟糕地"。蜜蜂来来回回、从不停歇，说明它们工作非常"努力"（hard）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："蜜蜂___了！"A 项意为"在睡觉"；B 项意为"在打架"；C 项意为"在生长"；D 项意为"不见了"。蜂巢空了，一只蜜蜂都没有，"gone"（消失了/不见了）最准确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 疑问词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有人确切知道___会发生。A 项意为"为什么"；B 项意为"何时"；C 项意为"哪里"；D 项意为"谁"。科学家对蜜蜂大规模消失的原因不完全清楚，不知道"为什么"（why）会发生。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有了忙碌的小蜜蜂飞来飞去，那棵橡树看起来很___。A 项意为"美丽的"；B 项意为"孤独的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"高的"。蜜蜂不在了，大树失去了往日的热闹，显得"孤独"（lonely）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蜜蜂需要花朵获取___。A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"庇护"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"休息"。蜜蜂从花朵中采集花蜜和花粉作为"食物"（food）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们没有回到旧蜂巢，但在附近的一棵树上建了一个___的。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"破碎的"；C 项意为"新的"；D 项意为"更大的"。蜜蜂没回旧巢，而是另建了一个"新"（new）蜂巢。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但它___了我们一些重大的道理。A 项意为"花费"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"借出"；D 项意为"卖"。这次经历"教会"（taught）了同学们一个重要的道理。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们齐心协力___自然界时，即使小小的行动也能产生真正的影响。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"毁坏"；C 项意为"研究"；D 项意为"保护"。全文主旨是关爱蜜蜂、种植花朵，这些都是"保护"（protect）自然的行为。故选 D。
