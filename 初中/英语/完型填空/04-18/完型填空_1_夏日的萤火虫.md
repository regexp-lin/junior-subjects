# 中考英语完形填空：夏日的萤火虫

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, I spent a week at my grandparents' house in the countryside. The first night, Grandpa said to me, "Would you like to see something ___1___? Follow me."

We walked along a small path behind the house. The sky was dark, and the air was warm. Crickets sang in the grass. Then, as we turned a corner, I saw them — hundreds of tiny lights ___2___ in the darkness, like stars that had fallen to the earth.

"Fireflies!" I whispered. I had read about them in books but had never seen them in real life. In the city where I live, there are too many streetlights. Fireflies cannot ___3___ there anymore.

Grandpa and I sat down on the grass. He told me that when he was a boy, fireflies were everywhere in summer. "We used to catch them in jars and watch them glow," he said. "But we always ___4___ them free before going to bed. They belong to the night."

I watched the fireflies dance. Each one carried its own small light, blinking on and off. Sometimes they flew ___5___, as if playing a game. Other times they stayed still, resting on leaves. The sight was so beautiful that I forgot about my phone and all my worries.

"Fireflies only live for about two weeks as adults," Grandpa said. "They spend most of their lives as larvae, hiding in the soil. When they finally grow wings, they have just a short time to shine."

His words made me think. Maybe we are all a bit like fireflies. We wait and prepare for a long time, and when our moment comes, we must ___6___ it to shine. The fireflies didn't waste their light. They used every night to glow and dance.

Before we went back, I caught one firefly gently in my hands. I watched it blink — a tiny miracle of nature. Then I opened my hands and let it go. It flew back into the darkness, ___7___ its light with the others.

That night I lay in bed, thinking about what I had seen. The fireflies had taught me something: even the smallest light can ___8___ the darkness. We don't need to be big or powerful to make a difference. We just need to glow with what we have.

I hope to visit Grandpa again next summer. I want to see the fireflies once more — those tiny messengers of joy that turn ordinary summer nights into something magical. And I want to remember that life, like a firefly's light, is short but ___9___. We should use it well.

That week in the countryside changed me. I learned to notice the small wonders around us — the fireflies in the field, the stars in the sky, the smell of grass after rain. Beauty is everywhere if we only take the time to ___10___ it.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："你想看一些___的东西吗？"A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"可怕的"。爷爷要带孙子看萤火虫，用"特别的"（special）更合适，表达惊喜与期待。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：成百上千的小光点在黑暗中___。A 项意为"舞蹈/飞舞"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"落下"；D 项意为"躲藏"。萤火虫在夜色中"飞舞"（dancing），与后文"watch the fireflies dance"呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在城市里萤火虫无法再___了。A 项意为"生活/生存"；B 项意为"飞"；C 项意为"停留"；D 项意为"生长"。光污染使萤火虫无法在城市"生存"（live）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："但我们总是在睡觉前把它们___。"A 项 send free 不常见；B 项"扔"；C 项"释放"；D 项 give free 意为"免费给"。"set...free"意为"释放"，把萤火虫放归自然。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时它们___地飞着，好像在玩游戏。A 项意为"单独"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"慢"。"flew together"表示萤火虫成群"一起"飞舞，与"playing a game"呼应。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们的时刻到来时，我们必须___它去发光。A 项意为"选择"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"利用"；D 项意为"节省"。"use the moment to shine"意为"抓住机会发光"，与后文"萤火虫不浪费它们的光"对应。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它飞回黑暗中，与其他萤火虫___它的光。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"展示"；D 项意为"藏"。"sharing its light with the others"表示回归群体，"分享"光芒。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使最微小的光也能___黑暗。A 项意为"充满"；B 项意为"打破/驱散"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"忘记"。"break the darkness"意为"驱散黑暗"，小小的光也能照亮。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生命像萤火虫的光，短暂却___。A 项意为"黑暗的"；B 项意为"长的"；C 项意为"明亮的"；D 项意为"冷的"。与萤火虫发光呼应，生命虽短却"明亮"（bright）有价值。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：美无处不在，只要我们愿意花时间去___它。A 项意为"发现"；B 项意为"创造"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"错过"。"find"表示主动去"发现"身边的美。故选 A。
