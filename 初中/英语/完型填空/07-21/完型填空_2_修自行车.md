# 中考英语完形填空：修自行车

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday morning, I found that my bicycle had a flat tyre. I was going to meet my friends at the park, so I had to fix it. I had never repaired a bike before, but my dad had shown me the ___1___ once. I decided to try it myself.

First, I turned the bike upside down and found the right tools in the garage. I needed a pump, a spanner, and a patch kit. Dad always said that having the correct ___2___ makes any job easier. I removed the wheel and took off the tyre. Finding the hole was the tricky part — I had to put the inner tube in water and watch for ___3___. Bubbles told me where the hole was.

I cleaned the area around the hole, put on the patch, and waited for it to dry. Then I put everything back together. When I ___4___ the tyre with the pump, I felt a bit nervous. Would it hold the air? I tested it — no more leaks! I was so ___5___ that I had done it by myself.

When I arrived at the park, my friends asked why I was late. I told them about the flat tyre and how I fixed it. "You're like a ___6___!" one of them said. I laughed and said anyone can learn if they are willing to try.

That experience taught me an important lesson — we should not ___7___ when we face problems. Sometimes the solution is simpler than we think. We just need to stay calm and take one step at a time. My dad says that learning to fix things helps us become more ___8___.

From now on, I want to learn more skills like this. Maybe I can help ___9___ when their bikes have problems too. It feels good to be useful and to solve problems with your own hands. That is the ___10___ of learning.

## 选项

1. A. steps &emsp;&emsp; B. rules &emsp;&emsp; C. stories &emsp;&emsp; D. games
2. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. tools &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. space
3. A. fish &emsp;&emsp; B. bubbles &emsp;&emsp; C. stones &emsp;&emsp; D. leaves
4. A. filled &emsp;&emsp; B. emptied &emsp;&emsp; C. washed &emsp;&emsp; D. painted
5. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
6. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. doctor &emsp;&emsp; C. engineer &emsp;&emsp; D. driver
7. A. give up &emsp;&emsp; B. wake up &emsp;&emsp; C. grow up &emsp;&emsp; D. hurry up
8. A. independent &emsp;&emsp; B. lazy &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. shy
9. A. parents &emsp;&emsp; B. teachers &emsp;&emsp; C. others &emsp;&emsp; D. pets
10. A. start &emsp;&emsp; B. end &emsp;&emsp; C. joy &emsp;&emsp; D. problem

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸曾给我展示过___一次。A 项意为"步骤"；B 项意为"规则"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"游戏"。修车是教步骤，"steps"正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有正确的___能让任何工作更容易。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"空间"。前文提到 pump、spanner 等，"tools"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须把内胎放进水里观察___。A 项意为"鱼"；B 项意为"气泡"；C 项意为"石头"；D 项意为"叶子"。找漏气处时，漏气会产生气泡，"bubbles"正确。后文也提到"Bubbles told me"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我用打气筒___轮胎时。A 项意为"充满/充气"；B 项意为"倒空"；C 项意为"洗"；D 项意为"刷漆"。修好漏气后要充气，"filled"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我为自己完成而感到非常___。A 项意为"抱歉"；B 项意为"自豪"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"无聊"。独立完成任务应有成就感，"proud"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你像个___！A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"医生"；C 项意为"工程师"；D 项意为"司机"。会修车被夸像工程师，"engineer"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：面对问题时我们不应该___。A 项意为"放弃"；B 项意为"醒来"；C 项意为"长大"；D 项意为"赶快"。"give up"意为"放弃"，符合"解决问题"的主题。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学会修理东西帮助我们变得更___。A 项意为"独立的"；B 项意为"懒惰的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"害羞的"。能自己动手修东西体现独立，"independent"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词/名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许我能在他们的车出问题时帮助___。A 项意为"父母"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"他人"；D 项意为"宠物"。帮助朋友等同龄人用"others"概括。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那就是学习的___。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"结束"；C 项意为"乐趣"；D 项意为"问题"。"feels good to be useful"强调学习的乐趣和意义，"joy"正确。故选 C。
