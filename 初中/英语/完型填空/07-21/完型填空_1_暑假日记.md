# 中考英语完形填空：暑假日记

## 完形填空原文

At the beginning of summer, my mum gave me a notebook. "Write something in it every day," she said. "It will help you remember your holiday." I was not sure at first, but I decided to give it a ___1___.

The first week, I only wrote short sentences like "Went to the park today" or "Had noodles for lunch." It felt a bit ___2___. Then one afternoon, I had a really fun day with my cousins. We played basketball, had ice cream, and laughed a lot. When I sat down to write that evening, the words just ___3___ out. I filled two pages without realising it.

From then on, I began to enjoy writing my diary. I wrote about the small things that made me happy — a beautiful sunset, a kind word from a stranger, or a ___4___ movie I watched. I also wrote about the difficult moments, like when I argued with my friend. Putting my feelings on paper somehow made me feel ___5___.

One day, I looked back at my diary from the first week. I was surprised to see how much I had ___6___ in just a month. The things that once worried me no longer seemed so big. I could see my own growth, and that made me feel proud.

My mum was right. A diary is not just a record of events — it is a friend that ___7___ to you without judging. It helps us understand ourselves better. Now I keep the notebook by my bed and write before I sleep. It has become part of my ___8___.

I plan to keep this habit even when school starts again. Maybe when I am ___9___, I can read these pages and remember the happy days of my youth. What a wonderful ___10___ that will be!

3. A. ran &emsp;&emsp; B. flew &emsp;&emsp; C. pulled &emsp;&emsp; D. poured
4. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. funny &emsp;&emsp; C. old &emsp;&emsp; D. cheap

6. A. changed &emsp;&emsp; B. failed &emsp;&emsp; C. rested &emsp;&emsp; D. travelled
7. A. listens &emsp;&emsp; B. shouts &emsp;&emsp; C. laughs &emsp;&emsp; D. runs
8. A. homework &emsp;&emsp; B. routine &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. sport
9. A. young &emsp;&emsp; B. busy &emsp;&emsp; C. old &emsp;&emsp; D. ill

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定___一下。A 项意为"尝试"；B 项意为"递"；C 项意为"打电话"；D 项意为"休息"。"give it a try"意为"试一试"，是固定搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感觉有点___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"令人兴奋的"；D 项意为"有用的"。只写短句、流水账会显得无聊，"boring"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：话语就这样___出来。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"飞"；C 项意为"拉"；D 项意为"倾泻"。"pour out"意为"倾泻而出"，形容写作顺畅。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一部我看过的___电影。A 项意为"长的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"旧的"；D 项意为"便宜的"。让人开心的事包括有趣的电影，"funny"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把感受写在纸上 somehow 让我感觉___。A 项意为"更糟"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更生气"；D 项意为"更悲伤"。写日记宣泄情绪后会感觉好一些，"better"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我惊讶地发现自己在仅仅一个月内___了多少。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"失败"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"旅行"。回顾日记看到自己的成长变化，"changed"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它是一个___你而不评判的朋友。A 项意为"倾听"；B 项意为"喊叫"；C 项意为"笑"；D 项意为"跑"。日记像朋友一样倾听心声，"listens"正确。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它已成为我___的一部分。A 项意为"作业"；B 项意为"日常惯例"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"运动"。睡前写日记成为固定习惯，"routine"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许当我___时，可以翻看这些页回忆青春。A 项意为"年轻的"；B 项意为"忙碌的"；C 项意为"年老的"；D 项意为"生病的"。将来年老时回顾，"old"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那将是多么美妙的___！A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"旅行"；C 项意为"测试"；D 项意为"回忆"。"read these pages and remember"指向回忆，"memory"正确。故选 D。
