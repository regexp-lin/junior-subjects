# 中考英语完形填空：奶奶的菜谱

## 完形填空原文

When I was little, my grandmother was the best cook in our family. Every Spring Festival, she would make dozens of dishes, and the whole house was filled with wonderful ___1___. My favorite was her sweet red bean soup. It tasted like nothing else in the world.

Last year, Grandma got sick and had to stay in the hospital. She couldn't cook anymore. The Spring Festival dinner that year was ___2___ — we ordered food from a restaurant. It was fine, but everyone ___3___ Grandma's cooking.

One afternoon, I visited Grandma in the hospital. She looked thin and weak, but her eyes were still ___4___. "I worry that my recipes will be lost," she told me. "Nobody in the family knows how to make them."

I held her hand and made a ___5___: "I'll learn, Grandma. Teach me everything."

Over the next few weeks, Grandma told me her recipes from her hospital bed. She couldn't show me how to cook, so she described every step with great ___6___. "The key to the red bean soup," she said, "is to add a tiny bit of orange peel. That's the ___7___."

I went home and practiced. My first few tries were terrible. The beans were too hard, the soup was too sweet, and I even ___8___ a pot. But I kept trying. Each time, I called Grandma and told her what went wrong. She would laugh and say, "That's exactly the same mistake I made when I was your ___9___."

After many attempts, I finally made a bowl of red bean soup that tasted just like Grandma's. I brought it to the hospital. Grandma took one sip, closed her eyes, and smiled. "Perfect," she whispered.

Grandma is much better now. This Spring Festival, I stood in the kitchen and cooked for the whole family. Grandma sat in her chair, watching me with ___10___ eyes. The recipes were safe — not just on paper, but in my hands and in my heart.

## 选项

1. A. sounds &emsp;&emsp; B. colors &emsp;&emsp; C. smells &emsp;&emsp; D. lights
2. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. exciting &emsp;&emsp; D. noisy
3. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. missed &emsp;&emsp; C. hated &emsp;&emsp; D. changed
4. A. bright &emsp;&emsp; B. closed &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
5. A. joke &emsp;&emsp; B. wish &emsp;&emsp; C. mistake &emsp;&emsp; D. promise
6. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. care &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
7. A. problem &emsp;&emsp; B. secret &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. answer
8. A. sold &emsp;&emsp; B. hid &emsp;&emsp; C. burned &emsp;&emsp; D. bought
9. A. age &emsp;&emsp; B. size &emsp;&emsp; C. place &emsp;&emsp; D. name
10. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. sad &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：整个屋子充满了美妙的___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"灯光"。奶奶做了很多菜，房子里自然充满了美妙的"气味"（smells），"be filled with smells"描述做饭时满屋飘香的场景。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那年的春节晚餐是___的——我们从餐厅订了外卖。A 项意为"特别的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"令人兴奋的"；D 项意为"嘈杂的"。以前奶奶亲手做饭，现在改为餐厅订餐，所以是"不同的"（different）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但每个人都___奶奶做的饭。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"讨厌"；D 项意为"改变"。餐厅的饭虽然还行，但大家都"想念"（missed）奶奶的手艺。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她看起来又瘦又虚弱，但她的眼睛仍然是___的。A 项意为"明亮的"；B 项意为"闭着的"；C 项意为"疲倦的"；D 项意为"干燥的"。虽然身体虚弱，但精神依然好，眼睛依然"明亮"（bright），用 but 转折，表达她内心的坚强。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我握着她的手，做了一个___。A 项意为"笑话"；B 项意为"愿望"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"承诺"。后面说"我会学的，奶奶。教我所有的"，这是一个"承诺"（promise）。"make a promise"是固定搭配。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她非常___地描述了每一个步骤。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"细心/关怀"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"恐惧"。奶奶不能亲自示范，所以她用语言非常"细心"（care）地描述每个步骤，"with great care"意为非常仔细地。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："关键是加一点点橙皮。这就是___。"A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"秘诀"；C 项意为"原因"；D 项意为"答案"。奶奶在传授独门技巧，加橙皮是让红豆汤特别好吃的"秘诀"（secret）。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我甚至___了一个锅。A 项意为"卖掉"；B 项意为"藏起"；C 项意为"烧焦/烧坏"；D 项意为"买"。初学做饭，豆太硬、汤太甜，甚至把锅"烧坏"（burned）了，描述初学者的狼狈。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："那跟我在你这个___时犯的错一模一样。"A 项意为"年龄"；B 项意为"大小"；C 项意为"地方"；D 项意为"名字"。奶奶说自己年轻时也犯过同样的错，"at your age"（在你这个年龄）是常见表达。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶坐在椅子上，用___的眼神看着我。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"伤心的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"困倦的"。孙子学会了她的手艺，代替她为全家做饭，奶奶内心充满"自豪"（proud）。故选 C。
