# 中考英语完形填空：班级图书角

## 完形填空原文

There was an empty corner in our classroom that nobody used. It was dusty and piled with broken chairs. One day, our class monitor, Li Fang, had an ___1___. "Why don't we turn this corner into a reading space?" she suggested.

Most students didn't think it was ___2___. "Nobody reads during break time," said one boy. "We'd rather play on our phones." But Li Fang didn't give up. She made a plan and showed it to our head teacher, Ms. Chen, who happily ___3___ it.

The next Saturday, Li Fang came to school with cleaning tools. Only three students showed up to ___4___ — me and two others. We spent the whole morning cleaning the corner, painting the wall light blue, and putting up a small wooden shelf.

"We still need books," Li Fang said. She wrote a ___5___ and posted it on the class notice board: "Donate a book, share a world." At first, only a few books appeared on the shelf — mostly old textbooks nobody wanted.

Then something unexpected happened. Ms. Chen brought ten brand-new novels from her own collection. When other students saw their teacher's ___6___, they started bringing books too. Within a week, the shelf was full of stories, science books, and comic books.

The reading corner slowly changed our class. During break time, instead of staring at phones, some students started picking up books. They would sit on the colorful cushions Li Fang had brought and ___7___ quietly. Conversations changed too — students began talking about interesting books instead of just games and gossip.

The biggest ___8___ was Zhang Peng, a boy who had never read a single book for fun. One day, I saw him sitting in the corner, completely lost in a detective novel. He didn't even hear the bell ___9___.

At the end of the term, our class won the school's "Best Reading Class" award. Li Fang stood on stage, smiling, but she said the ___10___ belonged to everyone. "I just cleaned a corner," she said. "You filled it with life."

1. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. idea &emsp;&emsp; C. accident &emsp;&emsp; D. excuse
2. A. fair &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. possible &emsp;&emsp; D. funny
3. A. refused &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. supported &emsp;&emsp; D. copied

7. A. sleep &emsp;&emsp; B. argue &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. draw
8. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. mistake &emsp;&emsp; D. chance
9. A. ring &emsp;&emsp; B. break &emsp;&emsp; C. move &emsp;&emsp; D. stop

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班长李芳有了一个___。A 项意为"答案"；B 项意为"主意"；C 项意为"事故"；D 项意为"借口"。后面她提出建议把角落改成阅读空间，这是一个"主意"（idea）。"have an idea"是固定搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大多数同学不觉得这是___的。A 项意为"公平的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"可能的/可行的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。同学们觉得课间没人看书，所以认为这个想法不"可行"（possible），"不可能成功"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师高兴地___了这个计划。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"支持"；D 项意为"复制"。"happily"说明陈老师很开心，是"支持"（supported）了这个计划。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只有三个同学来___。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"帮忙"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"吃饭"。李芳带着清洁工具来学校，需要人来"帮忙"（help）一起打扫和布置。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她写了一个___贴在班级通知栏上。A 项意为"信"；B 项意为"日记"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"告示/标语"。后面写着"捐一本书，分享一个世界"，这是一条宣传"标语/告示"（sign），贴在通知栏上号召大家捐书。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当其他同学看到老师的___后，也开始带书来了。A 项意为"支持"；B 项意为"担忧"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"笑话"。陈老师带头捐了十本新书，这是以实际行动"支持"（support），学生们被老师的行为感染，也纷纷效仿。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们坐在李芳带来的彩色坐垫上安静地___。A 项意为"睡觉"；B 项意为"争论"；C 项意为"阅读"；D 项意为"画画"。这是阅读角，学生们在课间开始"阅读"（read），不再只看手机。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最大的___是张鹏。A 项意为"变化/改变"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"机会"。张鹏原本从不读书，现在却沉迷于侦探小说，他是变化最大的人，是最大的"改变"（change）。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他甚至没听到铃声___。A 项意为"响"；B 项意为"打破"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"停止"。他看书太专注了，连上课铃"响"（ring）了都没听到。"hear the bell ring"（听到铃响）是常见表达。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但她说___属于每一个人。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"责任"；D 项意为"功劳"。班级获奖了，李芳站在台上却说"功劳"（credit）属于大家，体现了她的谦虚和团队精神。故选 D。
