# 中考英语完形填空：学做糯米糍

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, I decided to learn how to make glutinous rice balls (糯米糍) with my grandmother. She has been making them for over forty years and is very ___1___ at it.

First, we mixed glutinous rice flour with water to make the dough. I added too much water at ___2___ and the dough became too sticky. Grandma laughed and said, "Add a little more flour. You'll get it ___3___." I tried again and this time the dough was just right.

___4___ we made the dough into small balls and put red bean paste inside. Grandma's hands moved ___5___ while mine were slow and clumsy. "Practice makes perfect," she told me. I ___6___ her words and kept trying.

After we finished making all the balls, we steamed them for about fifteen ___7___. When the rice balls were ready, they looked soft and delicious. I took a bite—it was ___8___ sweet! Grandma was happy to see me enjoying the food.

Making糯米糍 with Grandma was not only fun but also meaningful. I learned that traditional skills need to be ___9___ from one generation to the next. I hope I can ___10___ this recipe to my children someday.

## 选项

1. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. best
2. A. last &emsp;&emsp; B. first &emsp;&emsp; C. once &emsp;&emsp; D. then
3. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. right &emsp;&emsp; D. wrong
4. A. So &emsp;&emsp; B. But &emsp;&emsp; C. Then &emsp;&emsp; D. Or
5. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
6. A. remembered &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. refused &emsp;&emsp; D. missed
7. A. seconds &emsp;&emsp; B. hours &emsp;&emsp; C. minutes &emsp;&emsp; D. days
8. A. too &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. so &emsp;&emsp; D. much
9. A. given &emsp;&emsp; B. passed &emsp;&emsp; C. sold &emsp;&emsp; D. bought
10. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. tell &emsp;&emsp; C. send &emsp;&emsp; D. lend

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她已经做了四十多年，非常擅长。A 项意为"好的"，be good at 为固定搭配；B 项 well 作副词表"好地"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"最好"。be good at 意为"擅长"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我起初加了太多水，面团变得太粘。A 项 at last 意为"最后"；B 项 at first 意为"起初"；C 项 at once 意为"立刻"；D 项 at then 不是固定搭配。根据"too much water"和"tried again"可知是起初。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词/形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说"你会做好的"。A 项意为"快地"；B 项意为"慢地"；C 项 get it right 意为"做对、做好"；D 项 get it wrong 意为"做错"。奶奶在鼓励，应为"做好"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们把面团做成小球，包入红豆沙。A 项意为"所以"；B 项意为"但是"；C 项意为"然后"；D 项意为"或者"。描述制作步骤的先后顺序。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶的手动得很快，而我的又慢又笨拙。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"安静地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"大声地"。与"slow"形成对比。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我记住了她的话，继续努力。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"错过"。后文"kept trying"说明听从了奶奶的 advice。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们蒸了大约十五分钟。A 项意为"秒"；B 项意为"小时"；C 项意为"分钟"；D 项意为"天"。糯米糍蒸制时间通常为十几分钟。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：咬一口——非常甜！A 项 too 表"太"，常含消极意味；B 项 very 意为"非常"；C 项 so 需搭配 that；D 项 much 修饰不可数名词。表达"很甜"用 very。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：传统技艺需要一代代传承。A 项意为"给予"；B 项 pass...from...to... 意为"传递、传承"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"买"。传承技艺用 pass。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望将来能把这道食谱教给我的孩子。A 项 teach 意为"教"；B 项 tell 意为"告诉"；C 项 send 意为"送"；D 项 lend 意为"借"。传授食谱用 teach。故选 A。
