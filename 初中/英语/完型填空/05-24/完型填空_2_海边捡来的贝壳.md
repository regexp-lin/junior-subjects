# 中考英语完形填空：海边捡来的贝壳

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, my family went to the seaside for a vacation. I had never seen the sea before, so when I first __1___ the blue water and endless horizon, I was completely amazed.

Every morning, I would wake up early and walk along the beach. I loved to ___2___ for shells that the waves had washed onto the shore. Some were tiny and white, like little pieces of the moon. Others were larger, with beautiful patterns and ___3___. I would pick them up one by one and put them in my pocket.

One day, I met an old fisherman who was sitting by his boat. He watched me collecting shells and smiled. "Do you know that each shell has a ___4___?" he asked. "It was once home to a sea creature. When the creature grew, it left and the shell stayed behind." His words made me look at the shells ___5___. They were no longer just pretty objects—they were stories of life.

I brought my shell collection home and ___6___ them in a glass jar. I placed it on my desk where I could see it every day. Whenever I felt tired or stressed from studying, I would look at those shells and remember the ___7___ of the sea, the warm sand under my feet, and the old fisherman's wisdom.

That trip taught me to find beauty in small things. The sea gave us not only ___8___ and waves, but also tiny treasures that carried memories. Sometimes the most valuable things in life are not expensive—they are the ___9___ we collect along the journey. Those shells still remind me of a summer when I learned to ___10___ the simple joys of life.

## 选项

1. A. touched &emsp;&emsp; B. saw &emsp;&emsp; C. tasted &emsp;&emsp; D. heard
2. A. search &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. ask &emsp;&emsp; D. pay
3. A. colors &emsp;&emsp; B. sizes &emsp;&emsp; C. shapes &emsp;&emsp; D. smells
4. A. price &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. owner &emsp;&emsp; D. secret
5. A. differently &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
6. A. hid &emsp;&emsp; B. displayed &emsp;&emsp; C. threw &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
7. A. depth &emsp;&emsp; B. sound &emsp;&emsp; C. silence &emsp;&emsp; D. power
8. A. fish &emsp;&emsp; B. sand &emsp;&emsp; C. wind &emsp;&emsp; D. salt
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. memories &emsp;&emsp; C. shells &emsp;&emsp; D. gifts
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. avoid &emsp;&emsp; C. appreciate &emsp;&emsp; D. miss

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我第一次看到碧蓝的海水和无尽的地平线时，我完全被震撼了。see 意为"看到"。A项"触摸"；B项"看到"；C项"品尝"；D项"听到"。saw 与 blue water, horizon 的视觉描写一致。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我喜欢搜寻被海浪冲上岸的贝壳。search for 意为"搜寻、寻找"。A项"搜寻"；B项"等待"；C项"询问"；D项"支付"。search for shells 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些更大，有着美丽的纹路和颜色。colors 与 patterns 并列，描述贝壳外观。A项"颜色"；B项"尺寸"；C项"形状"；D项"气味"。patterns and colors 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你知道每个贝壳都有一个故事吗？story 与后文"they were stories of life"呼应。A项"价格"；B项"故事"；C项"主人"；D项"秘密"。have a story 表示有来历。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话让我用不同的眼光看待这些贝壳。differently 意为"不同地"。A项"不同地"；B项"粗心地"；C项"迅速地"；D项"悲伤地"。look at differently 表示换一种视角。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把收集的贝壳带回家，陈列在一个玻璃罐里。display 意为"陈列、展示"。A项"隐藏"；B项"陈列"；C项"扔掉"；D项"丢失"。displayed them 与后文 on my desk 一致。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会看着那些贝壳，回忆起大海的声音。sound 意为"声音"。A项"深度"；B项"声音"；C项"寂静"；D项"力量"。sound of the sea 为常见搭配。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大海带给我们的不仅是风和浪，还有承载回忆的微小宝藏。wind and waves 为常用搭配，意为"风浪"。A项"鱼"；B项"沙子"；C项"风"；D项"盐"。wind 与 waves 搭配自然。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时人生中最珍贵的不是昂贵的物品，而是旅途中收集的回忆。memories 意为"回忆"，与全文怀旧主题一致。A项"金钱"；B项"回忆"；C项"贝壳"；D项"礼物"。memories we collect 点明主题。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那些贝壳仍让我想起那个学会欣赏生活简单快乐的夏天。appreciate 意为"欣赏、感激"。A项"忘记"；B项"避免"；C项"欣赏"；D项"想念"。appreciate the simple joys 为常见搭配。故选 C。
