# 中考英语完形填空：午休时的教室风波

## 完形填空原文

It was a quiet noon. Most students were taking a nap at their desks. I was about to fall asleep when I heard a loud ___1___. I looked up and saw that the blackboard had fallen to the ground. Pieces of chalk were scattered everywhere.

The noise woke everyone up. For a moment, we all sat in ___2___, not knowing what to do. Then our monitor stood up and said, "Let's clean it up together." She began to pick up the chalk, and one by one, we all ___3___ in. Some students moved the broken blackboard to the corner. Others swept the floor. In less than ten minutes, the classroom was ___4___ again.

Our teacher came in later and was surprised to see everything in order. "Who did this?" she asked. We looked at each other and smiled. "We all did it," our monitor replied. The teacher's face ___5___ with pride. "I'm proud of you," she said. "You didn't wait for others to solve the problem. You took ___6___ and worked as a team."

That afternoon, I learned something important. When ___7___ happens, complaining doesn't help. What matters is taking action. Everyone has the ___8___ to make a difference. And when we work together, we can ___9___ any difficulty. That small incident became a ___10___ moment for our class—we discovered that we were stronger when we stood together.

## 选项

1. A. music &emsp;&emsp; B. noise &emsp;&emsp; C. voice &emsp;&emsp; D. song
2. A. excitement &emsp;&emsp; B. silence &emsp;&emsp; C. happiness &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
3. A. ran &emsp;&emsp; B. joined &emsp;&emsp; C. went &emsp;&emsp; D. came
4. A. dirty &emsp;&emsp; B. clean &emsp;&emsp; C. broken &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
5. A. turned &emsp;&emsp; B. filled &emsp;&emsp; C. lit up &emsp;&emsp; D. went pale
6. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. turns &emsp;&emsp; C. action &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
7. A. something good &emsp;&emsp; B. nothing &emsp;&emsp; C. something bad &emsp;&emsp; D. everything
8. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. duty &emsp;&emsp; C. power &emsp;&emsp; D. idea
9. A. meet &emsp;&emsp; B. face &emsp;&emsp; C. overcome &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
10. A. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; B. teaching &emsp;&emsp; C. losing &emsp;&emsp; D. winning

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我正要睡着时听到一声巨响。noise 意为"噪音、声响"。A项"音乐"；B项"声响"；C项"嗓音"；D项"歌曲"。loud noise 与黑板掉落相符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有一刻，我们都静坐着，不知如何是好。silence 意为"寂静、沉默"。A项"兴奋"；B项"沉默"；C项"快乐"；D项"麻烦"。in silence 表示沉默无语。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一个接一个地加入进来。join in 意为"加入、参与"。A项"跑"；B项"加入"；C项"去"；D项"来"。joined in 与 cleaning 呼应。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不到十分钟，教室又变干净了。clean 意为"干净的"。A项"脏的"；B项"干净的"；C项"坏的"；D项"空的"。clean 与 scattered chalk 形成对比。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师的脸上露出自豪的光芒。lit up 意为"容光焕发、亮起来"。A项"转身"；B项"充满"；C项"容光焕发"；D项"变苍白"。face lit up with pride 为常见表达。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们主动采取行动，团队协作。take action 意为"采取行动"。A项"时间"；B项"轮流"；C项"行动"；D项"休息"。take action 为固定搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当不好的事情发生时，抱怨没有用。something bad 指意外、麻烦。A项"好事"；B项"没什么"；C项"坏事"；D项"一切"。与 complaining 语境一致。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都有做出改变的力量。power 意为"力量、能力"。A项"权利"；B项"职责"；C项"力量"；D项"想法"。power to make a difference 为常见搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们团结合作时，我们能克服任何困难。overcome 意为"克服"。A项"遇见"；B项"面对"；C项"克服"；D项"避免"。overcome difficulty 为固定搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那件小事成了我们班级的一堂生动的课。teaching 作形容词意为"有教育意义的"。A项"遗忘的"；B项"有教导意义的"；C项"失去的"；D项"胜利的"。teaching moment 意为"教育契机"。故选 B。
