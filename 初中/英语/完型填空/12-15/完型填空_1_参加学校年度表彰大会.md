# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校年度表彰大会

## 完形填空原文

It was a sunny afternoon. The school auditorium was ___1___ with students and teachers. Everyone was excited because it was our annual awards ceremony. I sat ___2___ my classmates, feeling nervous but also proud.

The principal gave an opening speech. He encouraged us to keep ___3___ hard and never give up on our dreams. Then the awards were announced. When I heard my name called for "Excellent Student of the Year," I couldn't ___4___ my ears. I walked to the stage with a pounding heart.

___5___ I received the certificate, I felt a wave of happiness. Looking at the audience, I saw my parents smiling at me. They had taken time off work to come. Their ___6___ meant more to me than the award itself.

After the ceremony, many friends came to ___7___ me. We took photos together and shared our joy. My English teacher said, "You deserve it. You've made great ___8___ this year." Her words encouraged me to do even better.

That day became an unforgettable ___9___ in my life. It taught me that hard work pays off and that the support of family and teachers is ___10___. I will always remember this special moment.

## 选项

1. A. empty &emsp;&emsp; B. filled &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. dark
2. A. against &emsp;&emsp; B. among &emsp;&emsp; C. beyond &emsp;&emsp; D. except
3. A. working &emsp;&emsp; B. playing &emsp;&emsp; C. resting &emsp;&emsp; D. waiting
4. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. touch &emsp;&emsp; C. cover &emsp;&emsp; D. wash
5. A. Unless &emsp;&emsp; B. Though &emsp;&emsp; C. When &emsp;&emsp; D. Until
6. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. presence &emsp;&emsp; C. advice &emsp;&emsp; D. money
7. A. congratulate &emsp;&emsp; B. thank &emsp;&emsp; C. greet &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
8. A. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; B. progress &emsp;&emsp; C. noise &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
9. A. lesson &emsp;&emsp; B. memory &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. test
10. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. useless &emsp;&emsp; C. valuable &emsp;&emsp; D. common

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校礼堂里挤满了学生和老师。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"被填满的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"黑暗的"。be filled with 意为"挤满、充满"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和同学们坐在一起。A 项意为"反对"；B 项意为"在……之中"；C 项意为"超过"；D 项意为"除了"。sit among one's classmates 意为"坐在同学中间"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他鼓励我们继续努力学习。A 项意为"工作/努力"；B 项意为"玩耍"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"等待"。keep working hard 意为"继续努力"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不敢相信自己的耳朵。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"触摸"；C 项意为"遮盖"；D 项意为"洗"。couldn't believe one's ears 意为"不敢相信自己的耳朵"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我接过证书时，感到一阵幸福。A 项意为"除非"；B 项意为"尽管"；C 项意为"当……时"；D 项意为"直到"。When 引导时间状语从句。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的到场对我来说比奖项本身更重要。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"到场/存在"；C 项意为"建议"；D 项意为"钱"。Their presence 指父母到场支持。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多朋友前来祝贺我。A 项意为"祝贺"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"问候"；D 项意为"跟随"。congratulate sb 意为"祝贺某人"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你今年取得了很大进步。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"进步"；C 项意为"噪音"；D 项意为"麻烦"。made great progress 意为"取得很大进步"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天成为我生命中难忘的记忆。A 项意为"课/教训"；B 项意为"记忆"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"测试"。unforgettable memory 意为"难忘的记忆"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：家人和老师的支持是宝贵的。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"无用的"；C 项意为"宝贵的"；D 项意为"普通的"。valuable 修饰 support 最贴切。故选 C。
