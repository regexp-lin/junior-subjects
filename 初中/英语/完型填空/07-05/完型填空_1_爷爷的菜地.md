# 中考英语完形填空：爷爷的菜地

## 完形填空原文

Every summer holiday, I go to visit my grandparents in the countryside. The thing I enjoy most is spending time in Grandpa's vegetable garden. It is not very big, but it is always full of ___1___ — tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, and many kinds of greens.

Grandpa has been growing vegetables for over forty years. He gets up at five o'clock every morning to work in the garden. I once asked him, "Don't you feel ___2___? You do the same thing every day." He laughed and said, "How can I be tired? Watching things grow makes me happy."

This summer, Grandpa decided to teach me how to plant watermelons. He said watermelons need a lot of ___3___ and water. First, we dug small holes in the soil and placed the seeds inside. Then we covered them gently with earth and watered them. Grandpa told me I had to be ___4___ because watermelons take a long time to grow.

Every morning, I went to the garden to ___5___ the plants. I watered them and pulled out the weeds around them. After about three weeks, small yellow flowers appeared. Grandpa said, "That's a good ___6___. The flowers will turn into watermelons soon." I could hardly wait.

One month later, I saw small green watermelons hanging on the plants. I was so ___7___ that I wanted to pick one right away. But Grandpa stopped me. "They are not ready yet," he said. "You need to wait until they become bigger and the skin turns ___8___."

I waited for another two weeks. Finally, Grandpa said it was time. I picked the biggest watermelon and cut it open. The inside was bright red and very sweet. It was the best watermelon I had ever eaten. I was ___9___ of what I had grown.

Grandpa smiled and said, "You see, good things come to those who ___10___." I will always remember this lesson from his garden.

## 选项

1. A. animals &emsp;&emsp; B. flowers &emsp;&emsp; C. vegetables &emsp;&emsp; D. fruits
2. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. scared &emsp;&emsp; D. hungry
3. A. sunlight &emsp;&emsp; B. wind &emsp;&emsp; C. rain &emsp;&emsp; D. snow
4. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. brave &emsp;&emsp; C. patient &emsp;&emsp; D. honest
5. A. count &emsp;&emsp; B. check &emsp;&emsp; C. move &emsp;&emsp; D. sell
6. A. sign &emsp;&emsp; B. joke &emsp;&emsp; C. story &emsp;&emsp; D. plan
7. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. excited
8. A. darker &emsp;&emsp; B. lighter &emsp;&emsp; C. thinner &emsp;&emsp; D. softer
9. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. sure &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
10. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. pay &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. ask

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：菜地不大，但总是满是___——番茄、黄瓜、辣椒和各种绿叶菜。A 项意为"动物"；B 项意为"花"；C 项意为"蔬菜"；D 项意为"水果"。后面列举的都是蔬菜，"vegetables"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你不觉得___吗？你每天做同样的事情。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"累的、厌倦的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"饿的"。每天重复劳动，问是否觉得厌倦，"tired"有"厌倦"之意。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：西瓜需要大量___和水。A 项意为"阳光"；B 项意为"风"；C 项意为"雨"；D 项意为"雪"。西瓜生长需要充足的阳光和水分，"sunlight"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷说我必须要___因为西瓜需要很长时间才能长大。A 项意为"小心的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"耐心的"；D 项意为"诚实的"。生长时间长需要耐心等待，"patient"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天早上我去菜地___植物。A 项意为"数"；B 项意为"查看"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"卖"。每天去看看植物长势，浇水除草，"check"意为"查看"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是个好___，花很快就会变成西瓜。A 项意为"迹象"；B 项意为"玩笑"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"计划"。开花是西瓜将要结果的好征兆，"sign"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，想马上摘一个。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"兴奋的"。看到果实长出来，兴奋得想立刻采摘，"excited"正确。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：你要等到它们变大，皮变得更___。A 项意为"更深色的"；B 项意为"更浅色的"；C 项意为"更薄的"；D 项意为"更软的"。西瓜成熟时外皮颜色会变深，"darker"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我为自己种的东西感到___。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"确定的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"累的"。"proud of"意为"为……感到自豪"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与谚语
**【解析】** 本句意为：好东西属于那些___的人。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"付出"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"请求"。"Good things come to those who wait"是英语谚语，意为"好事多磨"。故选 A。
