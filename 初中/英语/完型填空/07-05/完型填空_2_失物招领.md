# 中考英语完形填空：失物招领

## 完形填空原文

Last week, something happened that taught me the real meaning of honesty. I was walking to school when I noticed a wallet lying on the ground near the school gate. I picked it up and looked ___1___. There was quite a lot of money inside, along with an ID card and some bank cards.

For a moment, I did not know what to do. A voice in my head said, "Nobody saw you. You could just ___2___ the money." But another voice quickly said, "That's wrong. The person who lost it must be very worried." I knew the second voice was ___3___.

I looked at the ID card and saw the name "Li Ming" on it. I did not know anyone by that name. I decided to take the wallet to the school's lost-and-found ___4___. When I handed it to the teacher on duty, she praised me and said I did the right thing.

During the lunch break, a man came to the school. He looked anxious and ___5___. He told the gate guard that he had lost his wallet somewhere near the school that morning. The guard took him to the office, and the teacher ___6___ the wallet to him. The man opened it, checked everything, and his face was full of relief.

"Thank you so much! I've been looking for it all morning," he said. "My daughter's tuition money is in it. I was so ___7___ that I might have lost it forever." When the teacher told him that a student had found and returned it, the man wanted to give me some money as a ___8___. But I said no. "It was the right thing to do," I said.

The man shook my hand and thanked me again. After he left, my classmates said they were ___9___ of me. I felt happy, not because of any reward, but because I had made an honest ___10___. That feeling was worth more than any amount of money.

## 选项

1. A. outside &emsp;&emsp; B. inside &emsp;&emsp; C. around &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
2. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. throw &emsp;&emsp; C. count &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
3. A. wrong &emsp;&emsp; B. loud &emsp;&emsp; C. right &emsp;&emsp; D. funny
4. A. office &emsp;&emsp; B. shop &emsp;&emsp; C. garden &emsp;&emsp; D. playground
5. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; C. calm &emsp;&emsp; D. upset
6. A. sold &emsp;&emsp; B. showed &emsp;&emsp; C. returned &emsp;&emsp; D. lent
7. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
8. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. reward &emsp;&emsp; C. ticket &emsp;&emsp; D. souvenir
9. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. sure
10. A. promise &emsp;&emsp; B. choice &emsp;&emsp; C. wish &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我捡起钱包看了看___。A 项意为"外面"；B 项意为"里面"；C 项意为"周围"；D 项意为"后面"。捡到钱包后打开看里面的东西，"looked inside"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没人看到你，你可以___这些钱。A 项意为"留着"；B 项意为"扔掉"；C 项意为"数"；D 项意为"藏起来"。内心邪念想把钱据为己有，"keep the money"最合理。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道第二个声音是___的。A 项意为"错误的"；B 项意为"大声的"；C 项意为"正确的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。第二个声音说应该归还，这是正确的做法，"right"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定把钱包送到学校的失物招领___。A 项意为"办公室"；B 项意为"商店"；C 项意为"花园"；D 项意为"操场"。失物招领通常设在办公室，"office"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他看起来焦虑而且___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"困倦的"；C 项意为"冷静的"；D 项意为"沮丧的、不安的"。丢了钱包又焦虑又不安，"upset"与"anxious"并列表达。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师把钱包___给他。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"展示"；C 项意为"归还"；D 项意为"借出"。把失物还给失主，"returned"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，觉得可能永远找不回来了。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。担心钱包找不回来，"afraid"表示恐惧和担忧。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个人想给我一些钱作为___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"酬谢"；C 项意为"票"；D 项意为"纪念品"。为感谢拾金不昧而给的钱是酬谢，"reward"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们说他们为我感到___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"确定的"。"proud of"意为"为……自豪"，同学为"我"的诚实感到骄傲。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为我做了一个诚实的___。A 项意为"承诺"；B 项意为"选择"；C 项意为"愿望"；D 项意为"错误"。面对诱惑选择归还钱包，是一个诚实的选择，"choice"正确。故选 B。
