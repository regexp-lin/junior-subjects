# 中考英语完形填空：一次迷路经历

## 完形填空原文

Last month, I went to a new shopping center with my cousin. It was a very big place with many shops and restaurants. We walked around for a long time and had a lot of fun. But when we decided to go home, we could not find the way out.

We looked for the exit but everything seemed ___1___. Every corner looked the same. I started to feel worried. My cousin tried to stay calm and said, "Don't ___2___. We will find the way." But I could see that she was a bit nervous too.

We walked for another ten minutes but still could not find the exit. I suggested that we ___3___ someone for help. So we went up to a kind-looking lady and asked her the way. She smiled and pointed to a sign above us. "You need to go straight, then turn left at the second corner," she said.

We followed her directions and finally saw the main door. I felt so ___4___ that we were going to get out! But when we reached the door, we realized we had come in from the other side. Our bikes were still somewhere else. We had to ask another person for ___5___, and this time a security guard helped us find the parking area.

That day taught me an important lesson. When you are lost, the best thing to do is to ask for help. There is nothing ___6___ about admitting that you need assistance. Many people are willing to help if you just ask. I used to think that asking for help made me look weak, but now I understand that it is actually a sign of ___7___.

I also learned to pay more attention to my surroundings. Now when I go to a new place, I always look for landmarks or signs that can help me find my way back. It is a good habit to ___8___.

My cousin and I still laugh about that day sometimes. What started as a fun trip turned into a small adventure. We did not let the experience ___9___ us. Instead, we learned from it and became better at finding our way in unfamiliar places.

If you ever get lost, remember: stay calm, ask for help, and believe that you will find your way. Every ___10___ can teach you something useful.

1. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. familiar &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. beautiful
2. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. cry &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. leave

7. A. weakness &emsp;&emsp; B. strength &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. pride
8. A. break &emsp;&emsp; B. develop &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
9. A. hurt &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. save &emsp;&emsp; D. change
10. A. journey &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. experience

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们找出口，但一切看起来都很___。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"熟悉的"；C 项意为"陌生的"；D 项意为"美丽的"。迷路时对周围环境感到陌生，"strange"表示不熟悉、难以辨认。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：别___，我们会找到路的。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"哭"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"离开"。"Don't worry"为常见安慰语，表示"不要担心"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我建议我们___某人帮忙。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"请求/问"；D 项意为"跟随"。"ask sb. for help"意为"向某人寻求帮助"，迷路时应主动求助。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：想到快要出去了，我感到很___。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。找到出路后心情放松，"relaxed"最恰当。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不得不向另一个人请求___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"指引/方向"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"食物"。找停车处需要问路，"ask for directions"意为"问路"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：承认你需要帮助没有什么___的。A 项意为"错误的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"简单的"。"There is nothing wrong about..."意为"……没有什么不对的"，寻求帮助不是丢人的事。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我认为求助实际上是___的表现。A 项意为"弱点"；B 项意为"力量/勇气"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"骄傲"。敢于承认需要帮助并主动求助是一种勇气和智慧，"strength"贴切。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是一个值得___的好习惯。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"培养"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。"develop a habit"意为"养成习惯"，留心周围环境是好习惯。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们没有让这段经历___我们。A 项意为"伤害/打击"；B 项意为"阻止"；C 项意为"拯救"；D 项意为"改变"。"hurt"在此指"挫伤信心或留下阴影"，不因迷路而消极。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一次___都能教会你一些有用的东西。A 项意为"旅程"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"经历"。"experience"泛指各种经历，与"get lost"呼应，强调从经历中学习。故选 D。
