# 中考英语完形填空：周末骑行

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my father suggested that we go cycling together. He said it would be a good way to exercise and enjoy the autumn weather. I ___1___ agreed because I had been sitting at my desk studying all week and needed some fresh air.

We set off early on Saturday morning. My father had planned a route along the river. The road was flat and smooth, ___2___ for cycling. The morning air was cool, and the sun was just rising above the trees. Birds were singing, and the river sparkled in the golden light. It was a beautiful ___3___.

At first, we rode side by side and chatted. My father told me about his childhood, when he used to cycle to school every day. "It was ten kilometres each way," he said. "In winter, the road was icy and I had to ride very ___4___. But I never complained." I admired his determination.

After about an hour, we reached a small village. There was a market selling fresh fruits and vegetables. We stopped to rest and bought some oranges. The oranges were ___5___ and sweet — much better than the ones in the supermarket. An old farmer told us they had been picked that very morning.

We continued riding after the break. The road became hillier, and I started to feel tired. My legs ached and I was breathing ___6___. I wanted to stop, but my father encouraged me. "Just a little further. There's a wonderful view at the top of the hill," he said. I trusted him and kept pedalling.

When we finally reached the top, I understood why he had pushed me. Below us was a vast field of sunflowers, stretching as far as the eye could see. The bright yellow flowers swayed gently in the ___7___. It was one of the most spectacular sights I had ever seen. I took out my phone and snapped several photos.

We sat on a bench and drank water while admiring the view. My father put his arm around my shoulder and said, "Life is like cycling. Sometimes the road is easy, and sometimes it goes uphill. But if you keep going, you will always find something ___8___ waiting for you." His words stuck in my mind.

On the way home, the wind was at our backs, and riding was much ___9___. We arrived home tired but happy. My mother had prepared a warm lunch for us. As I ate, I thought about what a perfect day it had been.

That cycling trip taught me two things: first, spending time with family is ___10___; second, the best rewards often come after the hardest efforts.

1. A. unwillingly &emsp;&emsp; B. sadly &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
2. A. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. narrow &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
3. A. dream &emsp;&emsp; B. scene &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. memory
4. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. bravely
5. A. sour &emsp;&emsp; B. bitter &emsp;&emsp; C. juicy &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
6. A. easily &emsp;&emsp; B. heavily &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. softly
7. A. rain &emsp;&emsp; B. snow &emsp;&emsp; C. breeze &emsp;&emsp; D. storm
8. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
9. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. slower &emsp;&emsp; D. worse
10. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. priceless &emsp;&emsp; C. hopeless &emsp;&emsp; D. careless

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___地同意了。A 项意为"不情愿地"；B 项意为"伤心地"；C 项意为"高兴地"；D 项意为"生气地"。学了一周需要新鲜空气，自然高兴同意，"happily"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：路平坦光滑，___骑行。A 项意为"危险的"；B 项意为"完美的"；C 项意为"狭窄的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。平坦光滑的路适合骑车，"perfect"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是一幅美丽的___。A 项意为"梦"；B 项意为"景色"；C 项意为"课程"；D 项意为"记忆"。描述眼前所见的自然景色，"scene"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冬天路上结冰，我不得不骑得很___。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"小心地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"勇敢地"。结冰的路需要小心骑行，"carefully"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：橙子___又甜。A 项意为"酸的"；B 项意为"苦的"；C 项意为"多汁的"；D 项意为"干的"。新鲜橙子多汁甜蜜，"juicy"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始喘得很___。A 项意为"容易地"；B 项意为"沉重地"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"轻轻地"。爬坡累了呼吸沉重，"heavily"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：黄色的花朵在___中轻轻摇曳。A 项意为"雨"；B 项意为"雪"；C 项意为"微风"；D 项意为"暴风雨"。花朵轻轻摇曳是微风，"breeze"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：坚持下去，总有___的事在等你。A 项意为"可怕的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。爬坡后看到美景，坚持有美好的回报，"beautiful"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：风从背后吹来，骑行___多了。A 项意为"更难的"；B 项意为"更容易的"；C 项意为"更慢的"；D 项意为"更糟的"。顺风骑行更轻松，"easier"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与家人共度时光是___的。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"无价的"；C 项意为"无望的"；D 项意为"粗心的"。家庭时光无价，"priceless"正确。故选 B。
