# 中考英语完形填空：中秋节团圆

## 完形填空原文

The Mid-Autumn Festival is one of the most important traditional holidays in China. It falls on the fifteenth day of the eighth lunar month, when the moon is at its ___1___ and brightest. For our family, it is a time for reunion and celebration.

This year, the festival fell on a Saturday, so my whole family could be ___2___. My grandparents came from the countryside, and my uncle's family drove from another city. The house was full of laughter and chatter. I had not seen my grandparents for three months, and I was overjoyed to ___3___ them again.

My grandmother spent the whole afternoon cooking a big dinner. She made many traditional dishes — braised fish for good luck, dumplings for reunion, and sweet rice balls for ___4___. I helped her wash the vegetables and set the table. The kitchen was filled with wonderful smells that made my mouth water.

When dinner was ready, we all sat around the big round table. In Chinese culture, a round table ___5___ togetherness and completeness. My grandfather raised his glass and said, "May our family always be healthy and together." Everyone clapped and repeated his words. It was a warm and touching moment.

After dinner, we went to the balcony to admire the moon. It was a clear night, and the moon looked like a huge silver plate hanging in the sky. My grandmother told us the legend of Chang'e, the moon goddess. I had heard the story many times, but I never got ___6___ of it. There was something magical about hearing it under the bright moonlight.

Of course, we also ate mooncakes. My mother had bought several boxes with different ___7___ — red bean, lotus seed paste, egg yolk, and my favourite, pineapple. I bit into a pineapple mooncake and smiled. It was sweet but not too heavy.

The best part of the evening was when we played a game. My uncle suggested that each person should recite a poem or say a sentence about the ___8___. My grandfather recited a famous poem by Su Shi: "May we all be blessed with longevity, though we are miles apart, we can share the beauty of the moon." His deep voice made the words sound even more ___9___.

As the night grew late, I looked at my family around me and felt truly grateful. In our busy modern lives, it is rare for everyone to be together. The Mid-Autumn Festival reminds us of what matters most — not money, not success, but the ___10___ of those we love.

## 选项

1. A. smallest &emsp;&emsp; B. fullest &emsp;&emsp; C. thinnest &emsp;&emsp; D. darkest
2. A. apart &emsp;&emsp; B. away &emsp;&emsp; C. together &emsp;&emsp; D. alone
3. A. call &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. hug
4. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. anger &emsp;&emsp; C. harmony &emsp;&emsp; D. loneliness
5. A. prevents &emsp;&emsp; B. represents &emsp;&emsp; C. replaces &emsp;&emsp; D. reduces
6. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. fond
7. A. shapes &emsp;&emsp; B. fillings &emsp;&emsp; C. colours &emsp;&emsp; D. prices
8. A. sun &emsp;&emsp; B. moon &emsp;&emsp; C. stars &emsp;&emsp; D. clouds
9. A. frightening &emsp;&emsp; B. confusing &emsp;&emsp; C. moving &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
10. A. company &emsp;&emsp; B. advice &emsp;&emsp; C. wealth &emsp;&emsp; D. power

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：月亮最___最亮的时候。A 项意为"最小的"；B 项意为"最满的"；C 项意为"最薄的"；D 项意为"最暗的"。农历八月十五月圆之夜，"fullest"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：全家人都可以___。A 项意为"分开"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"在一起"；D 项意为"独自"。周末全家可以团聚，"together"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很开心能再次___他们。A 项意为"打电话"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"拥抱"。三个月未见的祖父母，见面会拥抱，"hug"正确。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤圆代表___。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"和谐/和美"；D 项意为"孤独"。汤圆象征团圆和美，"harmony"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在中国文化中，圆桌___团圆和圆满。A 项意为"阻止"；B 项意为"代表/象征"；C 项意为"替代"；D 项意为"减少"。圆桌象征团圆，"represents"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我听了很多次但从未___它。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"厌倦"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"喜欢"。"get tired of"意为"对...厌倦"，never got tired of意为"百听不厌"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈买了好几盒不同___的月饼。A 项意为"形状"；B 项意为"馅料"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"价格"。后文列举红豆、莲蓉等都是馅料，"fillings"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每人要背一首诗或说一句关于___的话。A 项意为"太阳"；B 项意为"月亮"；C 项意为"星星"；D 项意为"云"。中秋赏月，话题围绕月亮，"moon"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他低沉的声音让这些词听起来更___。A 项意为"可怕的"；B 项意为"令人困惑的"；C 项意为"感人的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。深沉的声音朗诵经典诗词很感人，"moving"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最重要的是亲人的___。A 项意为"陪伴"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"财富"；D 项意为"权力"。中秋节的核心是家人的陪伴，"company"正确。故选 A。
