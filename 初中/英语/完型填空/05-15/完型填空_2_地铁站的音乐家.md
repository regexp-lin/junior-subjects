# 中考英语完形填空：地铁站的音乐家

## 完形填空原文

Every morning on my way to school, I pass through the underground station. Usually I'm in a hurry — checking my phone, worried about being late. But one Monday, I heard something that made me ___1___.

A man was playing the violin in the corner. His eyes were closed. The music filled the tunnel. People rushed past him, but he didn't seem to ___2___. He was lost in his own world. I stood still and listened. For three whole minutes, I forgot about my math test. I forgot about the crowded train. I only heard the music.

When he finished, I walked over and dropped some coins into his case. "Thank you," he said. "That was ___3___. You're the first person who actually stopped to listen today." I felt a little sad. So many people had walked by without hearing a note.

"That's okay," he said, as if reading my mind. "Music isn't for everyone at every moment. But for those who need it, it's there. That's why I play — not for the money, but for the ___4___ who might need a moment of beauty in their busy day."

His words changed how I saw street performers. They weren't just asking for money. They were ___5___ a gift — the gift of art, freely given. Whether we accepted it or not was up to us.

Since then, I try to slow down when I pass through the station. Sometimes I stay for one song. Sometimes I just pause for a few seconds. That small ___6___ makes a difference. It reminds me that life isn't only about getting from place A to place B. There's beauty along the way, if we're willing to notice it.

Last week, I saw a young girl standing near the violinist, listening with her eyes closed. She looked peaceful. I thought about what the musician had said — "for those who need it, it's there." Maybe that girl was having a ___7___ day. Maybe the music helped. We never know how a simple melody might ___8___ someone's morning.

So the next time you're rushing through a station or a street, take a moment. Listen. You might discover something that changes your day. Art is everywhere, waiting for those who are ___9___ enough to hear it. And sometimes, the most beautiful moments come when we least ___10___ them.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我听到让我___的东西。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"停下"；C 项意为"睡"；D 项意为"喊"。"made me stop"与后文"stood still and listened"呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人们匆匆而过，但他似乎不___。A 项意为"在意"；B 项意为"注意到"；C 项意为"演奏"；D 项意为"休息"。"didn't seem to care"（似乎不在意）形容沉浸于音乐。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那太___了。A 项意为"无聊"；B 项意为"美妙"；C 项意为"奇怪"；D 项意为"糟糕"。"wonderful"表达感谢与赞赏，你是第一个停下倾听的人。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：为了那些可能需要的___。A 项意为"少数"；B 项意为"许多"；C 项意为"那些人"；D 项意为"没有人"。"the ones who"（那些……的人）是常用结构。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们是在___一份礼物。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"提供"；C 项意为"保留"；D 项意为"扔"。"offering a gift"（提供礼物）（提供礼物）与"freely given"呼应。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个小小的___带来了改变。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"习惯"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"问题"。"That small habit"（那个小习惯）指放慢脚步、倾听音乐。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许那女孩经历了___的一天。A 项意为"幸运"；B 项意为"困难"；C 项意为"漫长"；D 项意为"下雨"。"difficult day"与"for those who need it"对应，音乐抚慰困境中的人。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：简单的旋律可能___某人的早晨。A 项意为"毁掉"；B 项意为"照亮"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"浪费"。"brighten someone's morning"（照亮某人的早晨）形容音乐带来美好。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：艺术无处不在，等待那些足够___去聆听的人。A 项意为"年轻"；B 项意为"年长"；C 项意为"安静"；D 项意为"勇敢"。"quiet enough to hear"（足够静心去听）与匆忙经过形成对比。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最美好的时刻往往在我们最不___的时候出现。A 项意为"想要"；B 项意为"预料"；C 项意为"需要"；D 项意为"错过"。"least expect"（最意想不到时）是常用搭配。故选 B。
