# 中考英语完形填空：废弃车站的涂鸦

## 完形填空原文

Behind our neighborhood there is an old train station. It has been ___1___ for ten years — no trains stop there anymore. Most people think it's just an ugly, empty building. But my art teacher, Ms. Chen, saw something different.

One Saturday, she took our class there. "Look at these walls," she said. Over the years, local artists had painted murals on the concrete. There were flowers, animals, and scenes from old stories. "Art can ___2___ forgotten places," Ms. Chen explained. "It gives them new life."

I had never thought about art that way. I always thought art belonged in museums. But here, in this ___3___ place, art was everywhere. It was free for anyone to see. No tickets, no rules — just beauty for everyone.

Ms. Chen gave us a task: add something positive to one wall. We could paint whatever we wanted, as long as it brought ___4___. I was nervous at first. What if I made a mistake? What if people didn't like it?

"Art doesn't have to be perfect," Ms. Chen said. "It just has to be honest. Paint what you ___5___."

I thought about my grandmother's garden — the sunflowers she grew every summer. I started painting. My hands were shaking, but I kept going. When I finished, I stepped back and looked. It wasn't perfect, but it was ___6___. It was mine.

Other students painted their memories too — a favorite mountain, a childhood pet, a dream they had. By the end of the day, the gray wall had become a ___7___ of colors and stories. Strangers who passed by stopped to look. Some smiled. One man said, "This place used to be so sad. Now it feels alive."

That day taught me that we don't need permission to create. We don't need expensive tools or a famous gallery. We just need the ___8___ to pick up a brush and add something beautiful to the world. Even a forgotten station can become a gallery. Even a blank wall can ___9___ a story.

Art belongs everywhere — not just in museums, but in the streets, in old buildings, and in the hearts of everyone who dares to ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. open &emsp;&emsp; B. closed &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. built
2. A. destroy &emsp;&emsp; B. transform &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. ignore
3. A. modern &emsp;&emsp; B. abandoned &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. noisy
4. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
5. A. see &emsp;&emsp; B. feel &emsp;&emsp; C. hear &emsp;&emsp; D. taste
6. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. real &emsp;&emsp; C. fake &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
7. A. piece &emsp;&emsp; B. sea &emsp;&emsp; C. box &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
8. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. talent
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. tell &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. lose
10. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. watch &emsp;&emsp; C. create &emsp;&emsp; D. complain

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它已经___十年了。A 项意为"开放"；B 项意为"关闭"；C 项意为"忙碌"；D 项意为"建造"。"closed"与"no trains stop there anymore"呼应，车站已废弃。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：艺术可以___被遗忘的地方。A 项意为"毁坏"；B 项意为"改变/改造"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"忽视"。"transform"（改造）与"gives them new life"呼应。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在这个___的地方，艺术无处不在。A 项意为"现代"；B 项意为"被废弃的"；C 项意为"干净"；D 项意为"吵闹"。"abandoned"与"old train station"、"no trains"一致。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要它能带来___。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"愤怒"。"bring joy"（带来快乐）与"something positive"对应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：画你___的。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"感受"；C 项意为"听见"；D 项意为"品尝"。"paint what you feel"（画你所感）强调真诚表达。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不完美，但是___。A 项意为"丑"；B 项意为"真实的"；C 项意为"假的"；D 项意为"简单"。"real"与"honest"、"mine"呼应，强调真诚。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：灰墙变成了一片___的色彩和故事。A 项意为"一块"；B 项意为"一片"；C 项意为"一盒"；D 项意为"礼物"。"a sea of"（一片、大量）是常用表达。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只需要___拿起画笔。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"天赋"。"courage"与"dare to create"呼应，勇于尝试。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：空墙也能___一个故事。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"讲述"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"失去"。"tell a story"（讲述故事）与涂鸦主题相符。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在每个敢于___的人心中。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"观看"；C 项意为"创作"；D 项意为"抱怨"。"dare to create"（敢于创作）总结全文主题。故选 C。
