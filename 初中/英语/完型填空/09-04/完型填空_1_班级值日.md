# 中考英语完形填空：班级值日

## 完形填空原文

In our school, every class takes turns to be on duty for a week. This week it was our class's turn. The teacher divided us into groups and gave each group a different ___1___. My group was responsible for keeping the playground clean.

On Monday morning, we arrived at school thirty minutes early. The playground was covered with fallen leaves and some rubbish. We grabbed brooms, dustpans and rubbish bags, and started working. It was a ___2___ job than I had expected. The playground was huge, and the wind kept blowing leaves around. Every time we swept one area clean, more leaves would fall.

"This is endless!" my classmate Xiao Ming complained. I understood his frustration, but I said, "Let's work ___3___ instead of complaining. If we each focus on one section, we'll finish faster." So we divided the playground into four parts, and each person took one. This strategy worked well, and we ___4___ the task in just forty minutes.

During the week, we also had to check whether other students were following school rules. We reminded students not to run in the hallways, not to ___5___ food in the classroom, and not to litter. Some students listened politely, but a few younger ones ignored us. I learned that it takes ___6___ to be a good leader — you must be firm but respectful.

The hardest part was Wednesday afternoon. It rained heavily, and the hallway floors became wet and ___7___. We placed warning signs and mopped the floors several times. A first-year student almost fell, but I caught her arm just in time. She thanked me, and I felt glad that we were there to ___8___.

By Friday, our duty week was over. The headmaster praised our class during the morning assembly. She said our playground had never been so clean. Hearing this, I felt that all our hard work had been ___9___. Even Xiao Ming, who had complained on the first day, smiled with pride.

Being on duty taught me that every small task matters. Keeping the school clean and safe is everyone's ___10___. When we all do our part, our school becomes a better place for everyone.

## 选项

1. A. subject &emsp;&emsp; B. task &emsp;&emsp; C. hobby &emsp;&emsp; D. dream
2. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. easier &emsp;&emsp; C. funnier &emsp;&emsp; D. safer
3. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. separately &emsp;&emsp; C. together &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
4. A. avoided &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. copied &emsp;&emsp; D. completed
5. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. eat &emsp;&emsp; C. grow &emsp;&emsp; D. sell
6. A. patience &emsp;&emsp; B. silence &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. luck
7. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. warm &emsp;&emsp; C. slippery &emsp;&emsp; D. soft
8. A. play &emsp;&emsp; B. study &emsp;&emsp; C. help &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
9. A. wasted &emsp;&emsp; B. worthwhile &emsp;&emsp; C. forgotten &emsp;&emsp; D. unnecessary
10. A. responsibility &emsp;&emsp; B. interest &emsp;&emsp; C. talent &emsp;&emsp; D. weakness

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师把我们分成小组，给每组分配了不同的___。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"任务"；C 项意为"爱好"；D 项意为"梦想"。值日分配的是任务，"task"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这份工作比我预期的___。A 项意为"更难的"；B 项意为"更容易的"；C 项意为"更有趣的"；D 项意为"更安全的"。后文说操场很大、风吹落叶，比想象中困难，"harder"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我们___工作，而不是抱怨。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"分别地"；C 项意为"一起"；D 项意为"大声地"。鼓励一起合作，"together"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们仅用四十分钟就___了任务。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"抄写"；D 项意为"完成"。策略奏效，快速完成了任务，"completed"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们提醒学生不要在教室里___食物。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"吃"；C 项意为"种植"；D 项意为"卖"。学校规定不能在教室吃东西，"eat"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做好领导者需要___。A 项意为"耐心"；B 项意为"沉默"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"运气"。面对不听话的学生需要耐心，"patience"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：走廊地板变得湿滑___。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"滑的"；D 项意为"柔软的"。雨天地面湿滑，"slippery"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很高兴我们在那里能够___。A 项意为"玩"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"离开"。及时扶住了差点摔倒的同学，感到能帮上忙很好，"help"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得我们所有的辛苦都是___的。A 项意为"浪费的"；B 项意为"值得的"；C 项意为"被遗忘的"；D 项意为"不必要的"。获得表扬说明付出有价值，"worthwhile"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：保持学校清洁和安全是每个人的___。A 项意为"责任"；B 项意为"兴趣"；C 项意为"才能"；D 项意为"弱点"。维护校园环境是大家的责任，"responsibility"正确。故选 A。
