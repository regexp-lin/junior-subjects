# 中考英语完形填空：第一次英语课演讲

## 完形填空原文

Last week, our English teacher Ms. Liu announced that each student had to give a three-minute speech in class. When I heard this, my heart started beating ___1___. Public speaking was my biggest fear.

Ms. Liu gave us three days to prepare. She said we could talk about any topic we liked, as long as it was in English. I spent a long time thinking about what to say. ___2___, I decided to talk about my favourite book, "Charlotte's Web." I had read it many times and knew the story well.

I wrote my speech on a piece of paper and practised reading it aloud in my room. At first, my voice was shaky and I kept ___3___ words. But after practising for two hours, I could say the whole speech smoothly. My mother listened to me and gave some ___4___. She suggested I make more eye contact and speak more slowly.

The day of the speech arrived. I was the fifth student to present. As I watched my classmates go before me, some were confident and fluent, while others were as nervous as I was. When my turn came, I walked to the front of the class on ___5___ legs.

I took a deep breath and began. "Today I want to share a book that changed my life..." My voice trembled at first, but as I talked about the story, I gradually ___6___. I described how Charlotte, a small spider, saved her friend Wilbur through her wisdom and love. I could see my classmates listening ___7___. Some even nodded when I talked about the theme of friendship.

When I finished, there was a moment of silence. Then the class burst into applause. Ms. Liu smiled and said, "Well done! Your love for the book really ___8___ through." I returned to my seat feeling as if I were walking on air.

That experience taught me something important. The things we fear the most are often not as ___9___ as we imagine. We just need to prepare well and take the first step. Now I actually look forward to the next speech. I have already started thinking about what topic to ___10___.

1. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. fast &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
2. A. Sadly &emsp;&emsp; B. Suddenly &emsp;&emsp; C. Finally &emsp;&emsp; D. Luckily
3. A. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; B. spelling &emsp;&emsp; C. writing &emsp;&emsp; D. counting
4. A. presents &emsp;&emsp; B. excuses &emsp;&emsp; C. advice &emsp;&emsp; D. warnings
5. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. shaky &emsp;&emsp; C. long &emsp;&emsp; D. broken
6. A. calmed down &emsp;&emsp; B. gave up &emsp;&emsp; C. broke down &emsp;&emsp; D. fell down
7. A. impatiently &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. unwillingly &emsp;&emsp; D. attentively
8. A. broke &emsp;&emsp; B. came &emsp;&emsp; C. fell &emsp;&emsp; D. went
9. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. scary &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
10. A. avoid &emsp;&emsp; B. choose &emsp;&emsp; C. hate &emsp;&emsp; D. forget

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的心开始跳得___。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"轻柔地"；D 项意为"安静地"。紧张时心跳加速，"fast"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___，我决定谈我最爱的书。A 项意为"遗憾地"；B 项意为"突然"；C 项意为"最终"；D 项意为"幸运地"。花了很长时间思考后做出决定，"Finally"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的声音发抖，而且不断___单词。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"拼写"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"数"。背诵时忘词，"forgetting"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈听了我的演讲并给了一些___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"借口"；C 项意为"建议"；D 项意为"警告"。后文提到她建议多眼神交流、说慢点，是给建议，"advice"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用___的腿走到教室前面。A 项意为"强壮的"；B 项意为"颤抖的"；C 项意为"长的"；D 项意为"断的"。紧张导致腿发抖，"shaky"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：随着我讲述故事，我逐渐___。A 项意为"冷静下来"；B 项意为"放弃"；C 项意为"崩溃"；D 项意为"摔倒"。开始紧张后逐渐平静，"calmed down"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能看到同学们在___地听。A 项意为"不耐烦地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"不情愿地"；D 项意为"专注地"。同学们认真听讲，"attentively"正确。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你对书的热爱真的___出来了。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"来"；C 项意为"落下"；D 项意为"去"。"came through"意为"(感情)表达出来/传达出来"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们最害怕的事情往往不像想象中那么___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"可怕的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。克服恐惧后发现没那么可怕，"scary"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我已经开始想___什么话题了。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"选择"；C 项意为"讨厌"；D 项意为"忘记"。期待下次演讲，开始选题目，"choose"正确。故选 B。
