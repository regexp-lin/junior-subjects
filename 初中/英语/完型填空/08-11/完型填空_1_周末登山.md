# 中考英语完形填空：周末登山

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my family went hiking on Green Mountain. It was a clear autumn day, perfect for outdoor activities. We packed some water, snacks, and a camera, and set off early in the morning.

The path started gently. We walked through a small forest where birds were singing. The air was fresh and ___1___. My younger sister ran ahead excitedly. My father reminded her to slow down and save her energy for the steep parts later.

About half an hour later, the path became steeper. My legs started to feel ___2___. I wanted to stop and rest, but my mother said, "We're almost halfway. Keep going!" I took a few deep breaths and continued. Step by step, we climbed higher. When I looked back, I could see the whole town below. The view was ___3___.

We reached the top at noon. There was a small pavilion where we could sit and rest. We ate our sandwiches and drank the water we had brought. The wind blew gently, and we felt ___4___. My father pointed to the distant hills and said, "When you climb a mountain, you learn that the best views come after the hardest climb."

On our way down, my sister found a butterfly with colourful wings. She was so ___5___ that she tried to catch it, but it flew away. "That's okay," my mother said. "Some beautiful things are meant to be admired from a distance, not caught." My sister nodded, though she still looked a bit sad.

When we got home, we were all tired but satisfied. I had learned an important ___6___: challenges make us stronger. The steep path had been difficult, but reaching the top had made it all worthwhile. I also learned to appreciate nature more. The mountain had given us fresh air, beautiful views, and precious family time.

I hope we can go hiking again soon. Next time, I will bring a notebook to ___7___ the plants and animals we see. Maybe I can start a small nature diary. Hiking is not just exercise — it is a way to ___8___ with the natural world and with the people we love. I will ___9___ my parents to plan our next trip. There are many other mountains nearby waiting for us to ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：空气清新而___。A 项意为"脏的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"凉爽的"；D 项意为"干燥的"。秋天山林空气凉爽宜人，"cool"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的腿开始感到___。A 项意为"强壮的"；B 项意为"疲倦的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"饥饿的"。爬陡坡会腿酸，"tired"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：景色___。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"令人惊叹的"；D 项意为"多云的"。登高俯瞰城镇景色壮观，"amazing"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：微风轻拂，我们感到___。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"平静的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。山顶休息身心平静，"peaceful"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她太___了，试图抓住它。A 项意为"好奇的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"担心的"。对蝴蝶好奇想捕捉，"curious"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学到了重要的___。A 项意为"道理/教训"；B 项意为"学科"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"游戏"。"lesson"意为人生道理。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次我要带笔记本___看到的动植物。A 项意为"记录"；B 项意为"画"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"藏"。记录观察内容，"record"正确。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：是与自然世界和我们所爱的人___的方式。A 项意为"竞争"；B 项意为"争论"；C 项意为"连结"；D 项意为"比较"。"connect with"意为"与……建立联系"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会___父母计划下次出行。A 项意为"允许"；B 项意为"请求"；C 项意为"禁止"；D 项意为"忘记"。"ask sb to do"意为"请某人做某事"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：附近还有很多山等我们去___。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"探索/发现"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"破坏"。"discover"意为发现、探索。故选 B。
