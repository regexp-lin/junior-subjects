# 中考英语完形填空：第一次洗碗

## 完形填空原文

Last week, my mother was not feeling well. She had a headache and needed to rest. So I offered to help with the housework. "Let me wash the dishes," I said. My mother looked ___1___ and agreed.

I had never washed dishes before. I thought it would be easy — just add soap and rinse with water. But when I stood in front of the sink, I realised it was not that ___2___. There were plates, bowls, cups, and chopsticks. The oil on the plates was sticky and hard to remove. I had to scrub each item carefully.

I filled the sink with warm water and added dish soap. Bubbles formed quickly. I put the first plate into the water and started rubbing. The oil ___3___ slowly. I had to use more force than I had expected. My hands soon became wrinkled from the warm water. I wondered how my mother did this every day without ___4___.

After washing about half of the dishes, I felt my back starting to ache. I had been standing for twenty minutes. I wanted to ___5___ and rest, but I told myself to finish the job. My mother was resting, and I did not want to disappoint her. I took a deep breath and continued.

Finally, I rinsed all the dishes with clean water and placed them on the drying rack. When I looked at the pile of clean dishes, I felt a sense of ___6___. I had completed something I had never done before. I went to tell my mother. She smiled and said, "Thank you, dear. You did a great job. I'm proud of you." Her words made me feel even better.

From that day on, I began to help with the dishes more often. I realised that housework is not easy and that my mother ___7___ a lot every day. I should not take her work for granted. Now I try to share the housework and make her life a little easier. A small action can show our ___8___ and gratitude to our parents. I will ___9___ helping at home. After all, we are part of the family and should ___10___ our share of the work.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈看起来___，同意了。A 项意为"惊讶的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"紧张的"。孩子主动帮忙洗碗让妈妈惊喜，"surprised"正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到没那么___。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"重要的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。原以为简单实际不然，"simple"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：油___得很慢。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"增加"；C 项意为"到达"；D 项意为"返回"。洗掉油污即油污消失，"disappeared"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈每天做这些从不___。A 项意为"抱怨"；B 项意为"笑"；C 项意为"唱歌"；D 项意为"睡觉"。对比自己觉得累，妈妈从不抱怨，"complaining"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想___休息。A 项意为"继续"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为" hurry"；D 项意为"等待"。累时想停下休息，"stop"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有一种___感。A 项意为"羞愧"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"悲伤"。完成从未做过的事感到自豪，"pride"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈每天___很多。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"做"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。"does a lot"意为"做很多事"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小举动能表达我们对父母的___和感恩。A 项意为"怀疑"；B 项意为"爱"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"后悔"。帮做家务表达爱与感激，"love"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会___在家帮忙。A 项意为"结束"；B 项意为"保持/继续"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"讨厌"。"keep doing"意为"继续做"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们应___自己那份工作。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"分担"；C 项意为"忽视"；D 项意为"浪费"。"share one's share"意为"承担自己该做的部分"。故选 B。
