# 中考英语完形填空：端午节

## 完形填空原文

The Dragon Boat Festival is one of the most important traditional festivals in China. It ___1___ on the fifth day of the fifth month of the lunar calendar. This year, my family decided to celebrate it in a special way.

Early in the morning, my grandmother started to make zongzi. She had prepared the ___2___ the night before: rice, red beans, pork, and bamboo leaves. I sat beside her and watched carefully. She showed me how to fold the leaves into a cone shape and fill it with rice. "The secret is to tie them ___3___ so the rice won't fall out when we cook them," she said.

While the zongzi were cooking, my father told us the story of Qu Yuan. He was a great poet and minister who loved his ___4___. When his country was in danger, he was very sad. He jumped into the river to show his love for his country. People threw zongzi into the river to ___5___ the fish from eating his body. That is why we eat zongzi on this day.

In the afternoon, we went to the river to watch the dragon boat race. Many teams were ready to compete. The boats were long and ___6___, with dragon heads at the front. When the race started, the rowers pulled their oars together and the boats moved fast. The crowd cheered loudly. Our city's team won the race! We were all very ___7___.

When we got home, Grandmother's zongzi were ready. They smelled wonderful. I took a bite and the taste was even better than last year. "You are getting better at making zongzi, Grandma!" I said. She smiled and said, "Traditional festivals help us ___8___ our culture. I hope you will pass these customs to your children one day."

That day I learned that festivals are not just about good food. They help us remember our ___9___ and bring families together. I will never forget this special Dragon Boat Festival ___10___ with my family.

8. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. understand &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. lose
9. A. plans &emsp;&emsp; B. history &emsp;&emsp; C. hobbies &emsp;&emsp; D. dreams

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：端午节___在农历五月初五。A 项意为"发生"；B 项意为"适逢、落在"；C 项意为"出现"；D 项意为"停留"。"fall on"表示节日在某日，"falls"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她前一晚就准备好了___。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"原料、食材"；C 项意为"菜肴"；D 项意为"礼物"。后文列举米、红豆、猪肉等，是包粽子的原料，"ingredients"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键是要___捆好，煮的时候米才不会掉出来。A 项意为"松地"；B 项意为"慢地"；C 项意为"紧地"；D 项意为"静悄悄地"。包粽子要捆紧，"tightly"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他是热爱___的伟大诗人和大臣。A 项意为"家庭"；B 项意为"国家"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"学生"。屈原爱国，"country"与后文跳江殉国呼应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人们把粽子扔进河里，___鱼吃他的身体。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"拯救"；C 项意为"阻止"；D 项意为"保护"。"keep...from"意为"阻止...做"，固定搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：龙舟又长又___，船头有龙首。A 项意为"窄的"；B 项意为"宽的"；C 项意为"短的"；D 项意为"重的"。龙舟细长，划起来快，"narrow"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们市队赢了，大家都很___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"紧张的"。赢得比赛自然兴奋，"excited"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：传统节日帮助我们___自己的文化。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"理解"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"失去"。节日传承文化，让人理解传统，"understand"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：节日帮助我们铭记___，让家人团聚。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"历史"；C 项意为"爱好"；D 项意为"梦想"。传统节日承载历史记忆，"history"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记和家人一起___的这个端午节。A 项意为"度过"；B 项意为"支付"；C 项意为"花费（时间）"；D 项意为"花费（金钱）"。"spend time with family"意为"与家人共度时光"。故选 A。
