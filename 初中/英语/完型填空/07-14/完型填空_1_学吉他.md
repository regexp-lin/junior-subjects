# 中考英语完形填空：学吉他

## 完形填空原文

This summer I decided to learn to play the guitar. My cousin had been playing for a year and her music always sounded so ___1___. I wanted to be like her. My parents bought me a guitar as a birthday gift and found a teacher for me.

The first lesson was harder than I ___2___. My fingers hurt when I pressed the strings. The teacher said, "This is normal. Every beginner feels the same. You need to ___3___ and practise every day." I nodded and tried my best to remember the basic chords.

Week after week, I practised for at least half an hour every day. At first my fingers often made the wrong ___4___ and the music sounded terrible. But little by little, I could play a simple song. The first time I played "Happy Birthday" for my mum, she was so ___5___ that she gave me a big hug.

One day my teacher said, "You have made great progress. Would you like to play at the school concert next month?" I was nervous but excited. I practised the song ___6___ times until I could play it without mistakes. When the day came, I stood on the stage with my guitar. My hands were shaking at first, but when the music started, I ___7___ myself and played well.

After the concert, many classmates said they wanted to learn the guitar too. I felt proud and happy. Learning the guitar taught me that ___8___ is the key to success. Nothing is easy at the beginning, but if you keep trying, you will ___9___ your dreams. Now the guitar has become my best friend. When I feel tired or sad, I pick it up and play a few songs. Music always ___10___ my mood.

## 选项

1. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. loud &emsp;&emsp; D. slow
2. A. expected &emsp;&emsp; B. hoped &emsp;&emsp; C. knew &emsp;&emsp; D. saw
3. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. practise
4. A. sounds &emsp;&emsp; B. songs &emsp;&emsp; C. words &emsp;&emsp; D. chords
5. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
6. A. many &emsp;&emsp; B. few &emsp;&emsp; C. little &emsp;&emsp; D. much
7. A. helped &emsp;&emsp; B. taught &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
8. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. practice &emsp;&emsp; D. money
9. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. achieve &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. explain
10. A. changes &emsp;&emsp; B. improves &emsp;&emsp; C. loses &emsp;&emsp; D. keeps

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表姐弹的音乐听起来很___。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"美妙的"；C 项意为"大声的"；D 项意为"慢的"。表达羡慕表姐弹得好听，"beautiful"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一节课比我___的还要难。A 项意为"预期的"；B 项意为"希望"；C 项意为"知道"；D 项意为"看见"。"harder than expected"意为"比预期的难"，常用搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你需要___，每天练习。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"练习"。老师强调要坚持练习，"practise"与后文"practise every day"呼应。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的手指常按错，发出错误的___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"歌曲"；C 项意为"词"；D 项意为"和弦"。弹错产生错误的音，"sounds"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈非常___，给了我一个拥抱。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。孩子学会弹生日歌，母亲感到骄傲，"proud"最贴切。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把这首歌练习了___遍。A 项意为"许多"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"少量"；D 项意为"大量"。"many times"表示反复练习，体现努力。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（含反身代词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：音乐开始后，我___下来，弹得很好。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"放松"；D 项意为"忘记"。"relaxed myself"意为"让自己放松"，克服紧张。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学吉他让我明白了___是成功的关键。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"练习"；D 项意为"金钱"。全文强调反复练习，"practice"是核心主题。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要坚持努力，你就会___梦想。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"实现"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"解释"。"achieve your dreams"意为"实现梦想"，常用表达。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：音乐总能___我的心情。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"改善"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"保持"。弹琴缓解疲惫和难过，"improves"表示使心情变好。故选 B。
