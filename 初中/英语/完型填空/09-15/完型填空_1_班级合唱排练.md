# 中考英语完形填空：班级合唱排练

## 完形填空原文

Our class was going to take part in the school choir competition. We had only two weeks to prepare, so we needed to practice ___1___. Every afternoon after class, we would go to the music room and sing together.

Our music teacher, Miss Chen, chose a song ___2___ "We Are the World". It was a classic song about unity and hope. At first, some of us couldn't sing in tune. Miss Chen was very ___3___ and taught us note by note. She said that singing was not only about the voice but also about feeling the music with our ___4___.

We practiced the same song again and again. Sometimes I felt ___5___, but when I saw everyone working hard, I encouraged myself to keep going. One boy in our class had a beautiful voice. He often helped those who had ___6___ with high notes. We learned from each other and improved day by day.

Two days before the competition, we had a dress rehearsal. We stood in rows according to our ___7___. Miss Chen reminded us to smile and make eye contact with the audience. "Remember," she said, "you are not just singing for yourselves, but for everyone who ___8___ you."

On the competition day, we performed our best. When the results were announced, our class won second place! We hugged each other and some of us had ___9___ in our eyes. It was not just a competition — it was a journey of growth and friendship. I will never ___10___ those wonderful days we spent together.

## 选项

1. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. easily
2. A. called &emsp;&emsp; B. calling &emsp;&emsp; C. to call &emsp;&emsp; D. call
3. A. strict &emsp;&emsp; B. patient &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. nervous
4. A. hands &emsp;&emsp; B. ears &emsp;&emsp; C. hearts &emsp;&emsp; D. eyes
5. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
6. A. fun &emsp;&emsp; B. interest &emsp;&emsp; C. difficulty &emsp;&emsp; D. time
7. A. ages &emsp;&emsp; B. names &emsp;&emsp; C. heights &emsp;&emsp; D. grades
8. A. watches &emsp;&emsp; B. listens &emsp;&emsp; C. hears &emsp;&emsp; D. sees
9. A. smiles &emsp;&emsp; B. tears &emsp;&emsp; C. sweat &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只有两周时间准备，所以需要努力练习。A 项意为"缓慢地"；B 项意为"仔细地"；C 项意为"努力地"；D 项意为"容易地"。practice hard 表示"努力练习"，符合时间紧迫的语境。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 非谓语动词/过去分词作定语
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师选了一首名为《天下一家》的歌。A 项 called 作后置定语，意为"被称为"；B 项为现在分词；C 项为不定式；D 项为动词原形。a song called "..." 为常见结构。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初有人唱不准调，陈老师很有耐心，一个音一个音地教我们。A 项意为"严格的"；B 项意为"耐心的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"紧张的"。教唱不准的学生需要耐心。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说唱歌不仅是嗓音的事，还要用心感受音乐。A 项意为"手"；B 项意为"耳朵"；C 项意为"心"；D 项意为"眼睛"。feel with our hearts 表示"用心感受"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我感到疲倦，但看到大家努力时，我鼓励自己坚持下去。A 项意为"疲倦的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"放松的"。反复练习同一首歌会累，tired 符合。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他经常帮助那些唱高音有困难的同学。A 项意为"乐趣"；B 项意为"兴趣"；C 项意为"困难"；D 项意为"时间"。have difficulty with 为固定搭配，意为"在……方面有困难"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们按身高排成几列站着。A 项意为"年龄"；B 项意为"名字"；C 项意为"身高"；D 项意为"年级"。合唱队形通常按身高排列。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：记住，你们不仅是为自己唱，也是为每一位观看你们的人唱。A 项意为"观看"；B 项意为"听"；C 项意为"听见"；D 项意为"看见"。watch 指专注地看表演。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相拥抱，有些人眼含热泪。A 项意为"微笑"；B 项意为"眼泪"；C 项意为"汗水"；D 项意为"恐惧"。获奖后的激动情绪用 tears。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记我们一起度过的那些美好时光。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"享受"。never forget 表示"永不忘怀"，强调难忘。故选 A。
