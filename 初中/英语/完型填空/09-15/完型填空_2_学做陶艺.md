# 中考英语完形填空：学做陶艺

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, I joined a pottery class at the community center. I had always been ___1___ in making things with my hands, so I was looking forward to it.

On the first day, the teacher, Mr. Liu, showed us how to use the pottery wheel. He put a lump of clay in the center and ___2___ it with water. Then he stepped on the pedal and the wheel began to spin. His hands moved gently, and soon a beautiful vase took ___3___. It looked so easy! But when I tried it myself, the clay went everywhere. I made a ___4___ of my first piece.

Mr. Liu smiled and said, "Don't worry. Everyone makes mistakes at first. The ___5___ is to keep trying." I took his advice and practiced every day. Slowly, I learned to control the speed and use the right amount of ___6___. By the end of the week, I could make a simple cup.

The best part was when we painted our works. I chose blue and white colors, which are ___7___ in traditional Chinese pottery. I carefully drew some flowers on my cup. When I finished, I felt a sense of ___8___ — I had created something with my own hands!

Two weeks later, we got our fired pottery back. My cup was not perfect, but it was special to me. I gave it to my grandmother as a gift. She said it was the ___9___ gift she had ever received because it was made with love.

This experience made me understand that creating something takes time and patience. Nothing valuable comes ___10___. We need to learn step by step and never give up.

## 选项

1. A. interested &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
2. A. mixed &emsp;&emsp; B. covered &emsp;&emsp; C. wet &emsp;&emsp; D. filled
3. A. shape &emsp;&emsp; B. place &emsp;&emsp; C. part &emsp;&emsp; D. care
4. A. mess &emsp;&emsp; B. success &emsp;&emsp; C. progress &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
5. A. rule &emsp;&emsp; B. key &emsp;&emsp; C. way &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
6. A. water &emsp;&emsp; B. sand &emsp;&emsp; C. paint &emsp;&emsp; D. soil
7. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. common &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. rare
8. A. pride &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
9. A. cheapest &emsp;&emsp; B. best &emsp;&emsp; C. biggest &emsp;&emsp; D. oldest
10. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. early &emsp;&emsp; C. easily &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直对手工制作感兴趣，所以很期待。A 项意为"感兴趣的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。be interested in 为固定搭配，意为"对……感兴趣"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他把一块黏土放在中心，用水湿润。A 项意为"混合"；B 项意为"覆盖；弄湿"；C 项意为"湿的"（形容词）；D 项意为"装满"。cover with water 可表示用水湿润黏土表面，陶艺中常用此方式保持黏土柔软。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：很快一个美丽的花瓶成形了。A 项意为"形状"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"部分"；D 项意为"照料"。take shape 为固定短语，意为"成形"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的第一件作品弄得一团糟。A 项意为"混乱"；B 项意为"成功"；C 项意为"进步"；D 项意为"选择"。make a mess of 为固定搭配，意为"把……弄糟"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键是要不断尝试。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"关键"；C 项意为"方法"；D 项意为"问题"。the key is to... 为常见句型，意为"关键在于……"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，我学会了控制速度和使用适量的水。A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"沙子"；C 项意为"颜料"；D 项意为"泥土"。陶艺中用水调节黏土软硬度，water 正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了蓝白两色，这在中国传统陶艺中很常见。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"常见的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"罕见的"。青花瓷为传统特色，common 说明普遍使用。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：完成后，我感到一种自豪感——我用自己的双手创造了东西！A 项意为"自豪"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"愤怒"。a sense of pride 意为"自豪感"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说这是她收到过的最好的礼物，因为是用爱心做的。A 项意为"最便宜的"；B 项意为"最好的"；C 项意为"最大的"；D 项意为"最老的"。奶奶表达喜爱用 the best gift。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：有价值的东西不会轻易得来。A 项意为"晚"；B 项意为"早"；C 项意为"容易地"；D 项意为"快速"。nothing comes easily 意为"没有什么是轻易得来的"。故选 C。
