# 中考英语完形填空：敬老院的歌声

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, our class went to a nursing home to ___1___ the elderly. We had prepared some songs and small gifts. When we arrived, we saw many old people sitting in the garden, some in wheelchairs and others on benches. Their eyes lit up when they ___2___ us.

We began by singing some old songs that our teacher had ___3___ for us. At first, we sang softly, feeling a little shy. But when we saw the smiles on their faces, we became more ___4___. One grandmother even stood up and ___5___ her hands to the music. Soon, everyone was clapping together. The ___6___ in the air was warm and joyful.

After the performance, we sat down to chat with the elderly. An old man told us stories about his youth. He said that our visit had made him feel young again. His words made us ___7___ that a simple act of kindness could mean so much.

Before we left, we gave each person a handmade card. One grandmother held my hand and said, "Thank you for ___8___ your time with us. You've brought sunshine into our lives." I felt tears in my eyes. That day, I learned that ___9___ others doesn't always require grand actions. Sometimes, a song and a smile are ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. visit &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. thank &emsp;&emsp; D. call
2. A. met &emsp;&emsp; B. saw &emsp;&emsp; C. heard &emsp;&emsp; D. found
3. A. written &emsp;&emsp; B. chosen &emsp;&emsp; C. bought &emsp;&emsp; D. played
4. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. confident &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. serious
5. A. raised &emsp;&emsp; B. washed &emsp;&emsp; C. held &emsp;&emsp; D. shook
6. A. weather &emsp;&emsp; B. atmosphere &emsp;&emsp; C. noise &emsp;&emsp; D. light
7. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. realize &emsp;&emsp; C. imagine &emsp;&emsp; D. remember
8. A. wasting &emsp;&emsp; B. spending &emsp;&emsp; C. saving &emsp;&emsp; D. losing
9. A. helping &emsp;&emsp; B. teaching &emsp;&emsp; C. pleasing &emsp;&emsp; D. understanding
10. A. enough &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. possible &emsp;&emsp; D. necessary

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周六，我们班去敬老院探望老人。visit 意为"探望、参观"，visit the elderly 为常见搭配。A项"探望"；B项"帮助"；C项"感谢"；D项"打电话"。visit 最符合敬老院探访的语境。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当他们看到我们时，他们的眼睛亮了起来。see 表示"看到"，与 eyes lit up 呼应。A项"遇见"；B项"看到"；C项"听见"；D项"发现"。saw 强调视觉感知，与眼睛发亮的描写一致。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们首先唱了一些老师为我们挑选的老歌。choose 意为"挑选、选择"。A项"写"；B项"挑选"；C项"买"；D项"演奏"。chosen for us 表示老师事先选好的歌。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们看到老人们脸上的笑容时，我们变得更加自信。confident 意为"自信的"。A项"紧张的"；B项"自信的"；C项"累的"；D项"严肃的"。从 shy 到 confident 是情感的积极转变。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位奶奶甚至站起来，随着音乐举起双手。raise one's hands 意为"举起双手"。A项"举起"；B项"洗"；C项"握住"；D项"摇"。raised her hands to the music 表示随音乐举手。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：空气中的氛围温暖而欢乐。atmosphere 意为"氛围、气氛"。A项"天气"；B项"氛围"；C项"噪音"；D项"光线"。atmosphere 描写的是整体感受。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话让我们意识到，一个简单的善举可以如此意义重大。realize 意为"意识到"。A项"忘记"；B项"意识到"；C项"想象"；D项"记得"。realize 表示领悟、认知。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感谢你花时间陪伴我们。spend time with sb. 为固定搭配，意为"与某人共度时光"。A项"浪费"；B项"花费"；C项"节省"；D项"失去"。spending 表付出时间。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天我学到，帮助他人并不总是需要宏大的行动。help 意为"帮助"，与全文志愿服务主题一致。A项"帮助"；B项"教导"；C项"取悦"；D项"理解"。helping others 贯穿全文。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时，一首歌和一个微笑就足够了。enough 意为"足够的"。A项"足够的"；B项"困难的"；C项"可能的"；D项"必要的"。are enough 表示"就足够了"，点明主题。故选 A。
