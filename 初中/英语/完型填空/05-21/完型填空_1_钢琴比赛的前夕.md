# 中考英语完形填空：钢琴比赛的前夕

## 完形填空原文

The night before the piano competition, I sat alone in my room, my fingers ___1___ over the keys. I had practiced for months, but now I could hardly remember a single ___2___. My hands were shaking, and my mind was full of ___3___. What if I made a mistake in front of hundreds of people? What if I let my teacher down?

Just then, my mother came in with a cup of ___4___ tea. She sat down beside me and said nothing for a long time. Finally, she began to tell me a story about her own ___5___ when she was young. She had once been too nervous to speak in class, but she learned that everyone feels afraid sometimes. "The ___6___ is not to avoid fear," she said, "but to move forward despite it."

Her words ___7___ me. I took a deep breath and placed my hands on the piano again. This time, I played slowly, ___8___ each note. The music filled the room, and gradually, my nervousness started to ___9___. I realized that I had done my best. Whatever happened the next day, I would be ___10___ of myself for trying.

The next morning, I walked onto the stage with a calm heart. I played my piece from beginning to end. Though I didn't win first prize, I won something better—the courage to face my fears.

## 选项

1. A. moving &emsp;&emsp; B. trembling &emsp;&emsp; C. resting &emsp;&emsp; D. dancing
2. A. song &emsp;&emsp; B. note &emsp;&emsp; C. piece &emsp;&emsp; D. key
3. A. hope &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. doubt &emsp;&emsp; D. excitement
4. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. warm &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. bitter
5. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. failure &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. dream
6. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. question &emsp;&emsp; C. secret &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
7. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. encouraged &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. confused
8. A. missing &emsp;&emsp; B. feeling &emsp;&emsp; C. hearing &emsp;&emsp; D. forgetting
9. A. grow &emsp;&emsp; B. disappear &emsp;&emsp; C. return &emsp;&emsp; D. appear
10. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. ashamed

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛前夕，我独自坐在房间里，手指在琴键上颤抖。根据后文"My hands were shaking"可知作者非常紧张，手指应是颤抖的。A项"移动"；B项"颤抖"；C项"休息"；D项"跳舞"。trembling 与 shaking 语义呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我练习了几个月，但现在连一个曲子都想不起来。piece 可指乐曲、曲子。A项"歌曲"；B项"音符"；C项"乐曲、曲子"；D项"琴键"。根据语境，作者说的是整首曲子，piece 最合适。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的脑子里充满了疑虑。后文"What if I made a mistake..."正是 doubts 的具体内容。A项"希望"；B项"喜悦"；C项"疑虑、怀疑"；D项"兴奋"。doubt 符合紧张时的心理。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：母亲端着一杯温热的茶走了进来。warm tea 表示温暖的茶，体现母亲的关怀。A项"冷的"；B项"温暖的"；C项"甜的"；D项"苦的"。warm 与母亲安抚孩子的温情语境相符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她开始讲述自己年轻时也曾感到的恐惧。后文"too nervous to speak"说明是恐惧的经历。A项"成功"；B项"失败"；C项"恐惧"；D项"梦想"。fear 与 nervous 呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：秘诀不是逃避恐惧，而是尽管恐惧也要前进。secret 表示"秘诀、秘密"，指应对恐惧的方法。A项"答案"；B项"问题"；C项"秘诀"；D项"问题"。the secret is...为常见搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话鼓励了我。encourage 意为"鼓励"，符合母亲话语带来的正面影响。A项"使惊讶"；B项"鼓励"；C项"使担忧"；D项"使困惑"。encouraged 与后文作者重拾信心相符。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次我慢慢弹奏，感受每一个音符。feel 意为"感受、体会"。A项"错过"；B项"感受"；C项"听见"；D项"忘记"。feeling each note 表示用心体会每个音符。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，我的紧张开始消失。disappear 意为"消失"。A项"增长"；B项"消失"；C项"回来"；D项"出现"。nervousness started to disappear 表示紧张情绪消退。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无论第二天发生什么，我都会为自己的努力感到自豪。be proud of 为固定搭配，意为"以……为荣"。A项"累的"；B项"害怕的"；C项"自豪的"；D项"羞愧的"。proud 与积极成长的主题一致。故选 C。
