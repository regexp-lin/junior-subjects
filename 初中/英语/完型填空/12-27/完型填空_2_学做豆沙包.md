# 中考英语完形填空：学做豆沙包

## 完形填空原文

Red bean paste buns, or dousha bao, are a popular traditional Chinese snack. Last weekend, my grandmother taught me how to make them. She said she had been making these buns since she was a ___1___ girl.

First, we prepared the red bean paste filling. Grandmother had soaked the red beans overnight. She boiled them until they were very soft, then mashed them with a spoon and added sugar. The paste had to be smooth and not too ___2___. I helped stir the mixture over low heat until the extra water dried up.

Next, we made the dough. Grandmother mixed flour, yeast, sugar, and warm water in a big bowl. She told me to ___3___ the dough with my hands for about fifteen minutes. "The more you knead, the softer the buns will be," she said. My arms felt tired, but I did not ___4___.

After the dough had risen for an hour, we divided it into small round pieces. Grandmother showed me how to flatten each piece with my palm and put a spoonful of red bean paste in the ___5___. Then she carefully folded the dough around the filling and pinched it shut at the top. Her buns were round and beautiful, while mine were a bit ___6___.

We placed the buns in a steamer and cooked them for fifteen minutes. When the lid was ___7___, clouds of steam came out. The buns had become big and white, and they smelled amazing. I took one and bit into it—the soft dough and sweet filling were a ___8___ match.

Grandmother smiled and said, "You did a great job for your first time. ___9___ makes perfect." I felt proud of myself. I plan to make dousha bao for my family during the Spring Festival. Nothing says "home" like food made with ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她从小就在做这种包子。A 项意为"高的"；B 项意为"年轻的"；C 项意为"富有的"；D 项意为"著名的"。since she was a young girl 意为"从小"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：豆沙要细腻，不能太湿。A 项意为"甜的"；B 项意为"干的"；C 项意为"湿的"；D 项意为"冷的"。豆沙需要炒干水分，not too wet。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用手揉面团大约十五分钟。A 项意为"切"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"揉"；D 项意为"扔"。knead the dough 意为"揉面"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：胳膊很累但没有停下来。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"停下"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"笑"。did not stop 表示坚持。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在中间放一勺豆沙馅。A 项意为"角落"；B 项意为"中间"；C 项意为"边缘"；D 项意为"底部"。包子馅放在面皮 center。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的包子又圆又漂亮，而我的有点丑。A 项意为"完美的"；B 项意为"丑的"；C 项意为"扁的"；D 项意为"小的"。初学者做的 a bit ugly，与 round and beautiful 对比。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（过去分词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：揭开盖子时蒸汽冒出来。A 项意为"关闭的"；B 项意为"破碎的"；C 项意为"拿开的"；D 项意为"清洁的"。lid was removed 意为"盖子被掀开"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：柔软的面皮和甜甜的馅料是完美搭配。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"差的"；C 项意为"完美的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。a perfect match 意为"完美搭配"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：熟能生巧。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"速度"。Practice makes perfect 是固定表达。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有什么比用爱做的食物更有家的味道。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"爱"；D 项意为"沉默"。made with love 意为"用爱做的"。故选 C。
