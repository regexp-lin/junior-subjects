# 中考英语完形填空：第一次主持学校升旗仪式

## 完形填空原文

Last Monday was a special day for me. I was chosen to host the flag-raising ceremony for the first time. I had always watched other students do it and ___1___ what it would be like to stand there. Now my dream had come true.

I woke up very early that morning. I put on my neatest uniform and practiced my script one more ___2___. When I arrived at school, the playground was still quiet. I stood behind the stage and felt my hands getting cold. My teacher came over and said, "Just be ___3___. You've prepared well." Her words helped me calm down.

The bell rang and students began to gather. I walked onto the stage with the national flag team. My heart was beating so fast that I could ___4___ hear it. I took a deep breath and started to speak. "Dear teachers and students, good morning. The flag-raising ceremony will now begin."

Everything went ___5___. The flag was raised slowly while we sang the national anthem. I looked at the red flag rising against the blue sky and felt a deep sense of pride. When the ceremony ended, I announced, "The ceremony is over. Thank you." The students clapped and I felt a wave of ___6___.

After the ceremony, my headteacher patted me on the shoulder and said I had done a great job. She told me that hosting such an event was an honor and a ___7___. I learned that with enough preparation and courage, we can overcome our fears. This experience will always ___8___ in my memory. I am grateful for the opportunity and hope to serve our school in more ways in the ___9___. Every challenge we face helps us grow and become ___10___ people.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直想知道站在那里会是什么感觉。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"想知道"；C 项意为"知道"；D 项意为"同意"。憧憬时用 wondered。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把稿子再练一遍。A 项 one more time 意为"再一遍"；B 项意为"方式"；C 项意为"天"；D 项意为"年"。practice one more time 为常用表达。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：保持冷静，你准备得很充分。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"冷静的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。老师安慰用 be calm。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：心跳得太快几乎能听见。A 项意为"几乎不"；B 项意为"几乎"；C 项意为"从不"；D 项意为"很少"。心跳剧烈用 almost hear。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一切进展顺利。A 项 wrong 为形容词；B 项意为"糟糕地"；C 项意为"顺利地"；D 项意为"缓慢地"。went smoothly 意为"顺利进行"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到一阵如释重负。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"解脱/ relief"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"恐惧"。完成紧张任务后感到 relief。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：主持这样的活动是荣誉也是责任。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"责任"；D 项意为"错误"。honor and responsibility 为常见搭配。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这段经历将永远留在我记忆中。A 项意为"停留"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"消失"；D 项意为"忘记"。stay in memory 意为"铭记"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望未来能以更多方式为学校服务。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"未来"；C 项意为"早上"；D 项意为"晚上"。in the future 意为"在未来"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个挑战都帮助我们成长，成为更强大的人。A 项意为"更弱的"；B 项意为"更年轻的"；C 项意为"更强大的"；D 项意为"更年长的"。克服挑战使人 stronger。故选 C。
