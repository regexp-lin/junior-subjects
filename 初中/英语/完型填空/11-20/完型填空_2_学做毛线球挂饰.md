# 中考英语完形填空：学做毛线球挂饰

## 完形填空原文

During the art festival last month, I learned how to make wool ball ornaments from our craft teacher. She said we could use them to decorate our rooms or give them as gifts. I thought it would be fun, so I decided to give it a ___1___.

First, we needed two cardboard circles with a hole in the middle. We placed them together and started wrapping yarn around ___2___. The teacher said the more yarn we wrapped, the fuller the wool ball would be. I chose bright red and white yarn to make a candy cane style ball. Wrapping took a long time—I had to go round and round ___3___ the hole was completely filled.

When I thought it was thick enough, I cut the yarn and tied it. Then came the ___4___ part. I had to cut the yarn along the edge between the two cardboard circles. I had to be very careful, or the ball would fall apart. My hands were trembling a little, but I ___5___ slowly and steadily.

Finally, I tied a string through the middle to hold all the yarn together. I removed the cardboard and fluffed up the ball. It looked so soft and cute! I made three more—a blue one, a green one, and a yellow one. I hung them on my bedroom window. When the wind blows, they swing gently and look ___6___.

My grandma saw my wool balls and said they reminded her of her childhood. She said she used to make ___7___ things when she was young. I felt happy that my handiwork could bring back her sweet ___8___. Making these small ornaments taught me that simple crafts can bring joy to ourselves and others. I plan to make some for the coming New Year and hang them on our ___9___ tree. Maybe I can also teach my little sister how to make them. Sharing skills with family members can make our ___10___ warmer.

## 选项

1. A. hand &emsp;&emsp; B. try &emsp;&emsp; C. way &emsp;&emsp; D. look
2. A. them &emsp;&emsp; B. it &emsp;&emsp; C. him &emsp;&emsp; D. her
3. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. until &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. because
4. A. easiest &emsp;&emsp; B. hardest &emsp;&emsp; C. funniest &emsp;&emsp; D. simplest
5. A. ran &emsp;&emsp; B. walked &emsp;&emsp; C. worked &emsp;&emsp; D. played
6. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. lovely &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
7. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. same &emsp;&emsp; C. similar &emsp;&emsp; D. modern
8. A. dreams &emsp;&emsp; B. memories &emsp;&emsp; C. plans &emsp;&emsp; D. ideas
9. A. Christmas &emsp;&emsp; B. birthday &emsp;&emsp; C. school &emsp;&emsp; D. family
10. A. house &emsp;&emsp; B. room &emsp;&emsp; C. home &emsp;&emsp; D. school

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定试一试。A 项 give a hand 意为"帮忙"；B 项 give it a try 意为"试一试"；C 项 give a way 意为"让路"；D 项 give a look 意为"看一眼"。学习新技能用 give it a try。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把毛线缠在它们上面。A 项意为"它们"；B 项意为"它"；C 项意为"他"；D 项意为"她"。指代 two cardboard circles 用 them。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要一圈圈缠直到洞被完全填满。A 项意为"当……时"；B 项意为"直到"；C 项意为"如果"；D 项意为"因为"。go round until filled 表示持续到填满。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接下来是最难的部分。A 项意为"最容易的"；B 项意为"最难的"；C 项意为"最有趣的"；D 项意为"最简单的"。要小心剪否则会散，是 hardest part。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我缓慢而稳定地操作。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"走"；C 项意为"工作/操作"；D 项意为"玩"。做手工用 worked。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：随风轻轻摇摆看起来很可爱。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"可爱的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。毛线球装饰用 lovely。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说年轻时也做过类似的东西。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"相同的"；C 项意为"相似的"；D 项意为"现代的"。勾起回忆说明是 similar 手工。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手工作品能唤起她甜蜜的回忆。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"想法"。remind of childhood 对应 memories。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：打算新年做一些挂在圣诞树上。A 项意为"圣诞节"；B 项意为"生日"；C 项意为"学校"；D 项意为"家庭"。挂饰与 Christmas tree 搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与家人分享技能能让我们的家更温暖。A 项意为"房屋"；B 项意为"房间"；C 项意为"家"；D 项意为"学校"。家庭氛围用 home，强调情感。故选 C。
