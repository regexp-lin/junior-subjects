# 中考英语完形填空：乡村生活

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, I spent two weeks at my grandparents' house in the countryside. It was a completely different ___1___ from the busy city where I live. Instead of tall buildings and noisy traffic, I saw green fields, blue sky, and friendly faces everywhere.

Every morning, I woke up to the sound of birds singing. My grandfather took me to the farm to ___2___ him with the daily work. I learned to feed the chickens and water the vegetables. At first, I thought it would be boring, but I soon found that farm work could be quite ___3___. I felt proud when I picked my first tomato from the garden.

In the afternoon, my grandmother taught me how to make bread. The smell of freshly baked bread ___4___ the whole house. We sat under a big tree and enjoyed our bread with homemade jam. My grandmother told me stories about her childhood. She said life was simpler in the past, but people were ___5___ and helped each other more.

One evening, we went to the village square where the neighbors had gathered. Someone played the guitar and others sang along. I had never experienced such a warm and ___6___ atmosphere before. The villagers did not need expensive things to be happy; they found joy in simple moments together.

When it was time to leave, I felt a little ___7___. The countryside had taught me important lessons: to enjoy the simple things in life, to work with my hands, and to ___8___ the people around me. I hope I can ___9___ back soon and spend more time in that peaceful place. The memory of those two weeks will always ___10___ in my heart.

## 选项

1. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. experience &emsp;&emsp; C. life &emsp;&emsp; D. world
2. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. watch &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
3. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. fun &emsp;&emsp; D. easy
4. A. filled &emsp;&emsp; B. covered &emsp;&emsp; C. entered &emsp;&emsp; D. passed
5. A. kinder &emsp;&emsp; B. richer &emsp;&emsp; C. stronger &emsp;&emsp; D. cleverer
6. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. friendly &emsp;&emsp; C. serious &emsp;&emsp; D. strict
7. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
8. A. thank &emsp;&emsp; B. miss &emsp;&emsp; C. respect &emsp;&emsp; D. care about
9. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. walk &emsp;&emsp; C. come &emsp;&emsp; D. go
10. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. live &emsp;&emsp; C. remain &emsp;&emsp; D. keep

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那完全是一种与我所住的繁忙城市不同的___。A 项意为"地方"；B 项意为"经历、体验"；C 项意为"生活"；D 项意为"世界"。"experience"强调乡村生活带给"我"的整体感受与城市生活的对比。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷带我去农场___他做日常农活。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"加入"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"跟随"。"help sb with sth"为固定搭配，意为"帮助某人做某事"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我觉得会很无聊，但很快就发现农活其实挺___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"容易的"。"fun"与"boring"形成对比，表示农活并非无聊而是有趣。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：刚出炉的面包香气___了整个房子。A 项意为"充满、填满"；B 项意为"覆盖"；C 项意为"进入"；D 项意为"经过"。"fill"与气味扩散的情境相搭配，"fill the house"意为"充满整个房子"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说过去生活更简单，但人们更___，彼此帮助更多。A 项意为"友善的"；B 项意为"富有的"；C 项意为"强壮的"；D 项意为"聪明的"。"kinder"与"helped each other more"相呼应，强调人与人之间的善意。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没体验过如此温暖而___的氛围。A 项意为"安静的"；B 项意为"友善的"；C 项意为"严肃的"；D 项意为"严厉的"。"friendly"与"warm"、村民聚会的场景相呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到了离开的时候，我感到有点___。A 项意为"伤心的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"sad"符合与乡村分别、不舍的情感。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：乡村教会我要享受生活中的简单事物，亲手劳作，以及___身边的人。A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"尊重"；D 项意为"关心"。"care about"意为"关心、在乎"，与乡村邻里互助的主题一致。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望能很快回来。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"走"；C 项意为"来"；D 项意为"去"。"come back"为固定搭配，意为"回来、再来"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那两周的记忆将永远___在我心中。A 项意为"停留、留存"；B 项意为"居住、生活"；C 项意为"保持、留下"；D 项意为"保持"。"stay in one's heart"为常用表达，意为"留在某人心中"。故选 A。
