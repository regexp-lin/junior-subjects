# 中考英语完形填空：宠物故事

## 完形填空原文

When I was ten years old, I got my first pet — a little dog named Lucky. My parents gave him to me as a birthday ___1___. At first, I was excited but also a little worried. I had never taken care of an animal before. Would I be able to do it well?

Lucky was a golden retriever with soft fur. He was very ___2___ and always wanted to play. Every day after school, I would take him for a walk in the park. At first, he often pulled on the leash and ran in different directions. With time and ___3___, however, he learned to walk calmly beside me. I felt proud of our progress together.

One night, something strange happened. I woke up at midnight and heard Lucky barking loudly. I got out of bed to see what was wrong. To my ___4___, I found that there was a small fire in the kitchen! A candle had fallen over. Lucky had smelled the smoke and tried to warn us. Thanks to him, we put out the fire quickly and no one was hurt.

From that day on, I realized that Lucky was not just a pet — he was a real ___5___. He had saved our family. I became more responsible and took better care of him. I made sure he had enough food and water, and I brushed his fur every week. Our ___6___ grew stronger as we spent more time together.

When Lucky grew old, he could not run as fast as before. But he still liked to sit by my side when I did my homework. His gentle eyes seemed to say that he was ___7___ for the years we had shared. Last year, Lucky passed away peacefully. I was very sad, but I will never forget the ___8___ he brought into my life. He taught me about loyalty, love, and the joy of having a furry friend. I believe that one day I will have another dog, and I will give him the same ___9___ and love that Lucky received. That is the best way to ___10___ my dear Lucky.

## 选项

1. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. cake &emsp;&emsp; C. party &emsp;&emsp; D. card
2. A. quiet &emsp;&emsp; B. lazy &emsp;&emsp; C. active &emsp;&emsp; D. shy
3. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. help
4. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. surprise &emsp;&emsp; C. pleasure &emsp;&emsp; D. happiness
5. A. helper &emsp;&emsp; B. hero &emsp;&emsp; C. teacher &emsp;&emsp; D. friend
6. A. friendship &emsp;&emsp; B. relation &emsp;&emsp; C. feeling &emsp;&emsp; D. trust
7. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. thankful &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
8. A. hope &emsp;&emsp; B. luck &emsp;&emsp; C. happiness &emsp;&emsp; D. success
9. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. care &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. room
10. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. thank &emsp;&emsp; D. honor

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母把它作为生日___送给了我。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"蛋糕"；C 项意为"聚会"；D 项意为"卡片"。"as a birthday gift"为常用表达，意为"作为生日礼物"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它非常___，总想玩。A 项意为"安静的"；B 项意为"懒惰的"；C 项意为"活泼的、好动的"；D 项意为"害羞的"。"active"与"always wanted to play"相呼应，形容小狗活泼好动。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：随着时间和___，它学会了安静地走在我身旁。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"帮助"。"patience"与驯养宠物需要耐心教导的情境相符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：令我___的是，我发现厨房着了一小团火。A 项意为"喜悦"；B 项意为"惊讶"；C 项意为"愉快"；D 项意为"幸福"。"to one's surprise"为固定搭配，意为"令某人惊讶的是"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那以后我意识到，Lucky 不仅是宠物，更是真正的___。A 项意为"助手"；B 项意为"英雄"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"朋友"。"hero"与后文"saved our family"相呼应，强调其救命之恩。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：随着我们相处时间增多，我们的___变得更深厚。A 项意为"友谊"；B 项意为"关系"；C 项意为"感情"；D 项意为"信任"。"friendship"与主人和宠物之间的情感纽带最为贴切。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它温柔的眼神似乎在说，对我们共度的岁月它很___。A 项意为"抱歉"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"感激"；D 项意为"害怕"。"thankful"与共度时光、心怀感激的情感相符。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记它带给我的___。A 项意为"希望"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"幸福、快乐"；D 项意为"成功"。"happiness"与后文"joy of having a furry friend"相呼应。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会给予它和 Lucky 同样的___与爱。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"照顾、关怀"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"空间"。"care"与"love"并列，与前文"took better care of him"相呼应。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是___我亲爱的 Lucky 的最好方式。A 项意为"想念"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"感谢"；D 项意为"纪念、尊敬"。"honor"意为"以行动纪念、表达敬意"，与"最好的方式"相呼应。故选 D。
