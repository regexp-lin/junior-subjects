# 中考英语完形填空：修自行车摊的老师傅

## 完形填空原文

There is a bicycle repair stall at the corner of our street. An old craftsman has been running it for over thirty years. His hands are rough from years of work, but his skills are excellent. When my bike had a flat tire last month, I took it to him. He ___1___ the problem in seconds and started to fix it. "This will only take a few minutes," he said. I sat on a small stool and watched. His movements were slow but ___2___. Every turn of the wrench was exact.

While he worked, we talked. I asked him why he still did this job at his age. He smiled and said, "I ___3___ it. When I see a bike run well again, I feel happy." He didn't charge me much — just five yuan for the repair. I wanted to give him more, but he ___4___ his hand. "A fair price is enough," he said. I left with a working bike and a ___5___ respect for the old man.

A week later, I passed by his stall again. A child's bike was broken. The child looked worried. The old man was fixing it carefully. I heard him say, "Don't worry. Your bike will be ___6___ soon." The child's face lit up. That moment I understood — the old man didn't just fix bikes. He fixed people's ___7___ too. His patience and kindness made everyone feel better.

Now I always say hello when I pass his stall. Sometimes I stop to chat. He tells me stories about the old days when bicycles were the main ___8___ of transport. Those stories are interesting. I learn that every job has its own value. The old craftsman may not be rich, but he has something more — the ___9___ of doing what he loves and helping others. In a world that moves so fast, his small stall reminds us that some things never ___10___. Good work, honest people, and a warm heart — they are always there.

## 选项

1. A. found &emsp;&emsp; B. saw &emsp;&emsp; C. understood &emsp;&emsp; D. checked
2. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
3. A. need &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; C. finish &emsp;&emsp; D. mind
4. A. raised &emsp;&emsp; B. waved &emsp;&emsp; C. shook &emsp;&emsp; D. held
5. A. deep &emsp;&emsp; B. high &emsp;&emsp; C. new &emsp;&emsp; D. true
6. A. fine &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. nice &emsp;&emsp; D. good
7. A. worries &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. minds &emsp;&emsp; D. feelings
8. A. way &emsp;&emsp; B. tool &emsp;&emsp; C. means &emsp;&emsp; D. kind
9. A. hope &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. dream &emsp;&emsp; D. chance
10. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. pass

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他几秒钟就___了问题，开始修理。A 项意为"发现、找出"；B 项意为"看见"；C 项意为"理解"；D 项意为"检查"。"found the problem"与"flat tire"相呼应，指快速诊断出问题。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的动作缓慢但___。A 项意为"快"；B 项意为"仔细的"；C 项意为"容易"；D 项意为"简单"。"careful"与"slow"、"Every turn of the wrench was exact"相呼应，慢而精准。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___这份工作。A 项意为"需要"；B 项意为"享受"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"介意"。"enjoy"与"feel happy"、"see a bike run well again"相呼应，热爱这份工作。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但他___了手（拒绝）。A 项意为"举起"；B 项意为"挥动"；C 项意为"摇"；D 项意为"握住"。"shook his hand"在此语境中表示摆手拒绝，不肯多收钱。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我带着修好的车和___的敬意离开了。A 项意为"深深的"；B 项意为"高的"；C 项意为"新的"；D 项意为"真实的"。"deep respect"为常用搭配，指由衷的敬意。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你的自行车很快就会___了。A 项意为"好的、完好的"；B 项意为"健康、良好"；C 项意为"好的、漂亮的"；D 项意为"好的"。"be fine"指修好、恢复正常，与"fix"相呼应。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他不只修车，也修人们的___。A 项意为"忧虑"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"头脑"；D 项意为"感受"。"worries"与"child looked worried"、"feel better"相呼应，安慰人心的忧虑。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那时自行车是主要的交通___。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"手段、方式"；D 项意为"种类"。"means of transport"为固定搭配，意为"交通方式"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他拥有更重要的东西——做热爱之事并帮助他人的___。A 项意为"希望"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"机会"。"joy"与"doing what he loves and helping others"相呼应，指内心的喜悦。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在飞速发展的世界里，他的小摊位提醒我们有些东西永远不会___。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"过去"。"never change"与"Good work, honest people, and a warm heart — they are always there"相呼应，美好的品质永恒不变。故选 A。
