# 中考英语完形填空：帮助新来的交换生适应校园

## 完形填空原文

Last week, an exchange student named Tom arrived at our school from Australia. He would stay for one month to ___1___ Chinese culture and improve his Chinese. Our teacher asked me to be his buddy and help him get used to school life.

On the first day, Tom looked a little ___2___. Everything was new to him—the teaching style, the food in the canteen, and even the way students greeted each other. I showed him around the campus and introduced him to my friends. He was very friendly and tried to ___3___ everyone in Chinese, although his pronunciation was sometimes funny.

The biggest challenge for Tom was the food. He was not used to eating with ___4___. I taught him how to hold them correctly, and he practiced again and again. By the third day, he could pick up noodles without dropping them. He was so proud of himself that he took a photo and sent it to his ___5___ back in Australia.

In class, Tom sometimes could not ___6___ what the teacher said because the lessons were in Chinese. I sat next to him and wrote simple English notes to help him follow along. He told me that he really appreciated my ___7___.

During the second week, Tom became more confident. He even raised his hand to answer a question in Chinese class. Although his answer was not perfect, everyone ___8___ for him. After class, he said to me, "Thank you for making me feel at home. You are the ___9___ buddy I could ask for."

Having Tom at our school was a wonderful experience. I not only helped him learn about China, but I also learned about Australian culture. This experience taught me that ___10___ between different cultures makes the world more colorful.

## 选项

1. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. experience &emsp;&emsp; C. refuse &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
2. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
3. A. greet &emsp;&emsp; B. fight &emsp;&emsp; C. test &emsp;&emsp; D. trick
4. A. forks &emsp;&emsp; B. spoons &emsp;&emsp; C. chopsticks &emsp;&emsp; D. knives
5. A. teachers &emsp;&emsp; B. family &emsp;&emsp; C. doctors &emsp;&emsp; D. neighbors
6. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. understand &emsp;&emsp; D. repeat
7. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. joke &emsp;&emsp; C. help &emsp;&emsp; D. silence
8. A. cried &emsp;&emsp; B. cheered &emsp;&emsp; C. shouted &emsp;&emsp; D. complained
9. A. worst &emsp;&emsp; B. best &emsp;&emsp; C. busiest &emsp;&emsp; D. oldest
10. A. exchange &emsp;&emsp; B. competition &emsp;&emsp; C. argument &emsp;&emsp; D. distance

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他会在这里待一个月来体验中国文化。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"体验"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"避免"。exchange student 来 experience Chinese culture。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一天 Tom 看起来有点迷茫。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"迷茫的"；D 项意为"懒的"。新环境让人感到 lost。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他很友好，尝试用中文跟每个人打招呼。A 项意为"打招呼"；B 项意为"打架"；C 项意为"测试"；D 项意为"欺骗"。greet everyone 意为"向大家问好"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他不习惯用筷子吃饭。A 项意为"叉子"；B 项意为"勺子"；C 项意为"筷子"；D 项意为"刀"。中国人用 chopsticks 吃饭，外国人不习惯。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他拍了照片发给在澳大利亚的家人。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"家人"；C 项意为"医生"；D 项意为"邻居"。分享生活成就给 family。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Tom 有时听不懂老师说的话。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"理解"；D 项意为"重复"。中文课程他无法 understand。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他非常感谢我的帮助。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"笑话"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"沉默"。appreciated my help 意为"感激我的帮助"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然答案不完美，但大家都为他欢呼。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"欢呼"；C 项意为"大喊"；D 项意为"抱怨"。同学为他鼓励，用 cheered。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你是我能遇到的最好的搭档。A 项意为"最差的"；B 项意为"最好的"；C 项意为"最忙的"；D 项意为"最老的"。the best buddy 表示感谢。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不同文化之间的交流让世界更加多彩。A 项意为"交流"；B 项意为"竞争"；C 项意为"争论"；D 项意为"距离"。cultural exchange 意为"文化交流"。故选 A。
