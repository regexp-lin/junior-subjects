# 中考英语完形填空：暑假支教

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, I joined a volunteering programme and went to a small village to teach. It was my first time being a "teacher". I was nervous but also excited. The village was in the mountains. The road was long and winding. When we finally arrived, I saw a small school with only three classrooms. The children were waiting for us at the gate. Their eyes were ___1___ and full of curiosity.

I was asked to teach English. The students had few learning materials. There was no projector or computer. We had to use a blackboard and chalk. But the children were very eager to learn. They listened ___2___ and raised their hands to answer questions. One boy named Xiao Qiang never missed a class. He walked an hour every day to get to school. "I want to learn English so I can see the world when I grow up," he told me.

The village had no library. The children had never seen so many books. When we brought some storybooks, they were overjoyed. They gathered around and looked at the pictures with ___3___. I read stories to them every afternoon. Their faces glowed when they heard about faraway places and interesting characters. I realised that education can open ___4___ for these children. It can help them dream bigger.

Life in the village was simple. We ate simple meals and slept in a small dormitory. There was no air conditioning. At night we could see the stars clearly. It was ___5___ from the city, but I had never felt so peaceful. The children's smiles and their "Thank you, teacher" made every day meaningful.

When the programme ended, we had to say goodbye. The children gave us handmade cards and flowers. Some of them cried. Xiao Qiang said, "Teacher, will you come back?" I promised I would ___6___ to visit. I gave him my email address so we could stay in touch.

That experience changed me. I used to take my education for granted. Now I understand how ___7___ it is. I also learned that helping others does not require much. Sometimes a little time and care can make a big ___8___. I plan to join the programme again next summer. I want to see how the children are doing. I want to tell them that they can achieve their dreams if they keep ___9___. The children in that village taught me as much as I taught them. They taught me about gratitude, hope, and the simple joy of ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的眼睛___，充满好奇。A 项意为"闭着的"；B 项意为"明亮的"；C 项意为"疲惫的"；D 项意为"生气的"。期待老师到来时眼神明亮，"bright"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们___地听讲，举手回答问题。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"吵闹地"；C 项意为"认真地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。渴望学习故专注听讲，"carefully"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们围在一起，带着___看图片。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"惊奇"；D 项意为"愤怒"。首次见到这么多书，充满好奇与惊叹，"wonder"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（比喻）
**【解析】** 本句意为：教育能为这些孩子打开___。A 项意为"窗户"；B 项意为"门"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"房间"。"open doors"比喻创造机会，常用表达。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：和城里很___，但从未感到如此平静。A 项意为"远"；B 项意为"近"；C 项意为"不同"；D 项意为"相似"。"different from"表示与城市形成对比。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我答应会___再来看他们。A 项意为"努力/争取"；B 项意为"同意"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"拒绝"。"try to visit"表示尽量再来。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我明白教育有多___。A 项意为"容易"；B 项意为"无聊"；C 项意为"重要"；D 项意为"便宜"。支教后认识到教育的重要性，"important"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时一点时间和关心就能产生很大___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"不同/影响"；D 项意为"麻烦"。"make a difference"为固定搭配。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要坚持___，就能实现梦想。A 项意为"玩耍"；B 项意为"努力"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"等待"。"keep trying"意为"不断努力"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们让我体会到感恩、希望和___的单纯快乐。A 项意为"教学"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"旅行"。山村孩子渴望学习，"learning"的快乐点题。故选 B。
