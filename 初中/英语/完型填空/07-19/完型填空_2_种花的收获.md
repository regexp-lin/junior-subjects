# 中考英语完形填空：种花的收获

## 完形填空原文

This spring, my mum gave me a few sunflower seeds and said, "Why don't you try growing them? It might be a fun ___1___." I had never grown anything before, but I thought it would be interesting to try.

I found a large pot, filled it with soil, and planted the seeds about two centimetres deep. Then I watered them and put the pot on the balcony where there was plenty of ___2___. Mum told me to water them every morning but not too much, or the seeds would rot.

For the first week, I checked the pot every day, but there was no ___3___. I started to worry that I had done something wrong. "Maybe the seeds are bad," I said to Mum. She shook her head and said, "Just be patient. Plants have their own ___4___."

On the ninth day, I saw a tiny green shoot pushing through the soil. I was so ___5___ that I called everyone in the family to come and look. Over the following weeks, the shoot grew taller and taller. It grew new leaves almost every day. I was amazed at how ___6___ plants could grow.

By July, my sunflower was nearly as ___7___ as me! Its thick stem stood straight, and at the top, a large yellow flower opened up. It always turned to face the sun, which is why sunflowers got their ___8___. Every morning, I would sit beside it and read. It felt like having a quiet friend.

One day, a neighbour saw my sunflower and said, "What a beautiful plant! You must have taken very good ___9___ of it." I felt proud. Growing this sunflower taught me that small efforts every day can lead to wonderful ___10___. I plan to plant even more flowers next spring.

## 选项

1. A. experiment &emsp;&emsp; B. competition &emsp;&emsp; C. subject &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
2. A. rain &emsp;&emsp; B. wind &emsp;&emsp; C. sunlight &emsp;&emsp; D. space
3. A. danger &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. noise &emsp;&emsp; D. colour
4. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. language &emsp;&emsp; C. place &emsp;&emsp; D. schedule
5. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. frightened
6. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. fast &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
7. A. old &emsp;&emsp; B. heavy &emsp;&emsp; C. tall &emsp;&emsp; D. wide
8. A. colour &emsp;&emsp; B. name &emsp;&emsp; C. shape &emsp;&emsp; D. smell
9. A. notice &emsp;&emsp; B. care &emsp;&emsp; C. photos &emsp;&emsp; D. notes
10. A. results &emsp;&emsp; B. surprises &emsp;&emsp; C. memories &emsp;&emsp; D. stories

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这可能是一个有趣的___。A 项意为"实验、尝试"；B 项意为"比赛"；C 项意为"科目"；D 项意为"问题"。第一次种花是一种新的尝试/实验，"experiment"正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把花盆放在阳台上，那里有充足的___。A 项意为"雨"；B 项意为"风"；C 项意为"阳光"；D 项意为"空间"。向日葵需要充足的阳光，"sunlight"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一周我每天查看花盆，但没有任何___。A 项意为"危险"；B 项意为"变化"；C 项意为"噪音"；D 项意为"颜色"。种下种子后没看到任何变化，"change"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：植物有自己的___。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"语言"；C 项意为"地方"；D 项意为"时间表"。植物有自己的生长节奏/时间表，"schedule"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，叫全家人都来看。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。看到种子发芽非常兴奋，"excited"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我惊讶于植物能长得多___。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。每天都长新叶子，生长速度很快，"fast"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到七月，我的向日葵几乎和我一样___了。A 项意为"老的"；B 项意为"重的"；C 项意为"高的"；D 项意为"宽的"。向日葵长得和人差不多高，"tall"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它总是朝向太阳，这就是向日葵得到___的原因。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"名字"；C 项意为"形状"；D 项意为"气味"。sunflower（向日葵）的名字来源于它朝向太阳，"name"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你一定照顾得非常好。A 项意为"通知"；B 项意为"照料"；C 项意为"照片"；D 项意为"笔记"。"take good care of"是固定搭配，意为"好好照顾"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天的小努力可以带来美好的___。A 项意为"成果"；B 项意为"惊喜"；C 项意为"记忆"；D 项意为"故事"。日积月累的努力产生好成果，"results"正确。故选 A。
