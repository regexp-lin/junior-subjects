# 中考英语完形填空：学做书签

## 完形填空原文

In our art class last week, our teacher Mrs. Chen taught us how to make bookmarks. She said that a handmade bookmark could make reading more ___1___. I was excited to try because I love reading and often use a piece of paper to mark my place.

First, we needed some materials: coloured cardboard, scissors, glue, and some decorative items like ribbons and stickers. Mrs. Chen showed us several ___2___ of bookmarks — some were simple rectangles, others were shaped like animals or flowers. I decided to make one shaped like a leaf, because autumn had just begun and leaves were falling ___3___ the trees.

I cut the cardboard into a leaf shape carefully. It was not easy — my first cut was uneven and I had to start again. "Don't ___4___. Take your time," Mrs. Chen said gently. Her words encouraged me. On my second try, the shape looked much better.

Then I decorated the bookmark. I drew some veins on the leaf with a green pen and added a small ribbon at the top. I wanted to write a favourite ___5___ on it. I chose "Books are bridges to new worlds." When I finished, I felt proud of my work. It wasn't perfect, but it was ___6___ because I had made it myself.

Mrs. Chen said we could make more bookmarks as gifts. I made two more — one for my best friend and one for my mother. My mother loves reading too. When I gave her the bookmark, she smiled and said she would use it ___7___ when she read her favourite novels.

Making bookmarks has become one of my hobbies now. Whenever I have free time, I like to create new designs. I have made bookmarks of different shapes and colours. Some of my classmates have asked me to ___8___ them how to make bookmarks. I feel happy to share my skills with others.

A simple bookmark can bring joy to both the maker and the receiver. If you enjoy reading, why not try making your own bookmark? It's easy, fun, and you will have a ___9___ tool to use every time you open a book. Who knows? You might ___10___ a new hobby that you love!

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工书签能让阅读更有趣。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"令人愉快的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。书签可增添阅读乐趣，enjoyable 正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师向我们展示了多种书签设计。A 项意为"尺寸"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"设计"；D 项意为"价格"。后文提到不同形状，designs 正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：秋天到了，叶子从树上落下。A 项意为"从"；B 项意为"进入"；C 项意为"在……下面"；D 项意为"在……后面"。fall from 意为"从……落下"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要着急，慢慢来。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"匆忙"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"忘记"。don't rush 与 take your time 呼应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想在上面写一句喜欢的格言。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"诗歌"；C 项意为"引语、格言"；D 项意为"歌曲"。Books are bridges... 是 quote。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然不完美，但因为是自己做的所以很特别。A 项意为"特别的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"难看的"。亲手制作使书签 special。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说读书时会一直用它。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"有时"；D 项意为"总是"。收到礼物会 always 使用。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有同学请我教他们做书签。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"教授"；C 项意为"借给"；D 项意为"卖掉"。teach sb how to do 意为"教某人如何做"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次打开书都会有属于自己的工具。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"个人的、自己的"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"重的"。自己做的书签是 personal。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：说不定你会发现一个喜欢的新爱好。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"发现"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。discover a hobby 意为"发现一种爱好"。故选 B。
