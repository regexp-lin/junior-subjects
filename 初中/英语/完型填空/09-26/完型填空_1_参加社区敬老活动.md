# 中考英语完形填空：参加社区敬老活动

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, our school organised a volunteer activity to visit the elderly in the community. I joined the activity with some of my classmates. We prepared some gifts and performances to ___1___ the elderly residents.

When we arrived at the community centre, many elderly people were already waiting for us. They smiled when they saw us. We first helped them clean their rooms and water the plants. Grandma Li, an 80-year-old lady, said she lived alone and often felt ___2___. Our visit made her day much brighter.

After the cleaning, we put on a small performance. Some students sang songs, others danced, and I ___3___ a poem. The elderly clapped their hands happily. Grandma Wang, who used to be a music teacher, even sang a folk song with us. Her voice was still clear and beautiful. We were all ___4___ by her spirit.

Then we sat down and chatted with them. They told us stories about the old days — how life was ___5___ when they were young, and how the city had changed over the years. We listened carefully. I realised that the elderly had so much wisdom to share. Spending time with them was not only helpful to them but also ___6___ for us.

Before we left, we promised to ___7___ again. Grandma Li held my hand and said, "Thank you for coming. You are like sunshine on a cloudy day." Her words touched my heart. I understood that a little ___8___ could mean so much to someone who feels lonely.

The activity taught me an important lesson: we should respect and care for the elderly. They have given so much to our society. It is our ___9___ to make them feel loved and valued. I will take part in more such activities in the future and ___10___ my friends to join me.

1. A. surprise &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. please &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
2. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. healthy
3. A. wrote &emsp;&emsp; B. read &emsp;&emsp; C. recited &emsp;&emsp; D. copied
4. A. moved &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
5. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. modern &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
6. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. harmful &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
7. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. visit &emsp;&emsp; C. call &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
8. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. kindness &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
9. A. choice &emsp;&emsp; B. duty &emsp;&emsp; C. plan &emsp;&emsp; D. hope
10. A. allow &emsp;&emsp; B. encourage &emsp;&emsp; C. force &emsp;&emsp; D. invite

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们准备了礼物和节目来取悦老人家。A 项意为"使惊讶"；B 项意为"教授"；C 项意为"使高兴、取悦"；D 项意为"跟随"。准备礼物和表演的目的是 please（让……高兴）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李奶奶说她独居，经常感到孤独。A 项意为"高兴的"；B 项意为"孤独的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"健康的"。独居老人常感到 lonely。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我朗诵了一首诗。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"读"；C 项意为"背诵、朗诵"；D 项意为"抄写"。在表演中朗诵诗歌用 recited。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都被她的精神感动了。A 项意为"感动的"；B 项意为"疲劳的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。老教师仍保持活力，令人 moved。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们讲述过去的日子——年轻时生活是多么艰难。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"现代的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。老一辈年轻时生活 often difficult。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陪伴他们不仅对他们有帮助，对我们来说也很有意义。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"有害的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。志愿活动对志愿者 also meaningful。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：离开前，我们答应会再来探望。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"探望、拜访"；C 项意为"打电话"；D 项意为"等待"。promise to visit again 意为"答应再来探望"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了，一点善意对感到孤独的人来说意义重大。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"善意"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"礼物"。此处强调善意 kindness。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们有责任让他们感到被爱和重视。A 项意为"选择"；B 项意为"责任"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"希望"。it is our duty to 意为"我们有责任……"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会鼓励我的朋友们加入我。A 项意为"允许"；B 项意为"鼓励"；C 项意为"强迫"；D 项意为"邀请"。encourage sb to do 意为"鼓励某人做某事"。故选 B。
