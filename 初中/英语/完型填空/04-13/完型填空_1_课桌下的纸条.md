# 中考英语完形填空：课桌下的纸条

## 完形填空原文

I was having a bad week. I had failed the math exam, argued with my best friend, and felt like nobody cared about me. Every morning I woke up ___1___ to go to school. I sat at my desk with my head down, not wanting to talk to anyone.

On Wednesday, I found a small note under my desk. It said: "You are braver than you think. Have a good day!" There was no name. I looked around, but nobody was looking at me. I put the note in my pencil case and ___2___ about it all morning. Who had written it? Why?

The next day, there was another note. "Your smile is beautiful. Share it with the world." Again, no name. I started to feel a little ___3___. Someone had noticed me. Someone cared.

I decided to find out who it was. I came to school early on Friday and hid behind the door. A few minutes later, I saw Lin Na — a quiet girl who sat in the back row — ___4___ something under my desk. She quickly returned to her seat before anyone else arrived.

I was surprised. Lin Na and I had barely spoken. We were in the same class for two years, but I had never really gotten to ___5___ her. She was always reading or drawing alone.

At break time, I walked over to her. "Thank you for the notes," I said. "They helped me a lot."

Her face turned red. "How did you... I didn't mean for you to find out. I just... you looked sad. I wanted to do something."

"That was very ___6___ of you," I said. "Can we be friends?"

She smiled. "I'd like that."

From that day on, Lin Na and I became good friends. I learned that she loved painting and had a pet cat. She learned that I enjoyed music. We started eating lunch together and ___7___ our secrets. I also began to write small notes for other students who looked down. I left them on desks or in lockers, without signing my name.

One day, our teacher asked us to write an essay about "A person who helped me." I wrote about Lin Na. When I read it aloud in class, she hugged me and said, "You helped me too. Before you talked to me, I felt ___8___. Now I have a friend."

A simple note can ___9___ a lot. It doesn't need to be long or perfect. Sometimes, a few kind words are enough to change someone's day — or even their life. I will never ___10___ what Lin Na did for me in that difficult week.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天早上醒来都___去上学。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"不愿意"；C 项意为"准备好的"；D 项意为"高兴的"。那周心情很差，"不愿意"（unwilling）去学校，符合低落情绪。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把纸条放进铅笔盒，整个早上都在___这件事。A 项意为"想"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"谈论"；D 项意为"知道"。"thought about"意为"思考/琢磨"谁写的、为什么写。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始觉得有点___了。A 项意为"更糟"；B 项意为"更冷"；C 项意为"更温暖"；D 项意为"更生气"。有人关心自己，心里会感到"更温暖"（warmer）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到林娜把某样东西___在我的课桌下。A 项意为"画"；B 项意为"放置"；C 项意为"发现"；D 项意为"拿"。她在悄悄放纸条，"放置"（placing）符合动作。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们同班两年，但我从未真正___过她。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"喜欢"；C 项意为"了解"；D 项意为"遇见"。"get to know"是固定搭配，意为"逐渐了解/认识"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你这样做非常___。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"善良的"；C 项意为"聪明的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。写鼓励纸条关心别人是"善良的"（kind）行为。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们开始一起吃午饭，并___彼此的秘密。A 项意为"保持"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"写"。好朋友之间会"分享"（sharing）秘密。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在你跟我说话之前，我感觉___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"孤独的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"勇敢的"。林娜以前总是一个人，所以感到"孤独"（lonely）。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一张简单的纸条可以___很多。A 项意为"意味着/有意义"；B 项意为"花费"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"拿"。"mean a lot"是固定表达，意为"意义重大/很重要"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会___林娜在那艰难的一周里为我做的事。A 项意为"理解"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"记得"；D 项意为"想念"。"never forget"表示会永远铭记那份善意。故选 B。
