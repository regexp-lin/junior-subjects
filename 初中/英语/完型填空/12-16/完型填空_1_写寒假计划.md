# 中考英语完形填空：写寒假计划

## 完形填空原文

Winter vacation is coming! I'm excited because I have a lot of free time. Last night, I sat down and wrote a ___1___ for my winter holiday. I want to make good use of every day.

First, I plan to ___2___ up early and do some exercise in the morning. Running helps me stay healthy and gives me energy for the whole day. I will also read for at least one hour ___3___. Reading is my favorite hobby and I have many interesting books waiting for me.

___4___, I need to finish my homework. My teacher said we should complete it before the Spring Festival. I will do a little each day so that I won't feel too ___5___. Of course, I will spend time with my family too. We plan to visit my grandparents in the countryside and enjoy the traditional food together.

I also want to learn something new. Maybe I can learn to cook a simple dish or pick up a new ___6___ like painting. My best friend suggested that we could study together sometimes. That sounds like a good idea!

I know that ___7___ a plan is easier than following it. So I will try my best to stick to my schedule. I believe that with a clear plan, my winter vacation will be both ___8___ and meaningful. ___9___ I follow my plan well, I am sure I will have a wonderful time. Let me ___10___ from tomorrow!

## 选项

1. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. letter &emsp;&emsp; C. story &emsp;&emsp; D. diary
2. A. get &emsp;&emsp; B. wake &emsp;&emsp; C. stand &emsp;&emsp; D. stay
3. A. every day &emsp;&emsp; B. every night &emsp;&emsp; C. some day &emsp;&emsp; D. one day
4. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Besides &emsp;&emsp; C. Instead &emsp;&emsp; D. Although
5. A. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. excited
6. A. sport &emsp;&emsp; B. subject &emsp;&emsp; C. skill &emsp;&emsp; D. game
7. A. thinking &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. making &emsp;&emsp; D. remembering
8. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. long &emsp;&emsp; C. short &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoyable
9. A. If &emsp;&emsp; B. Unless &emsp;&emsp; C. Because &emsp;&emsp; D. Though
10. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. start &emsp;&emsp; D. wait

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨晚，我坐下来为寒假写了一个计划。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"信"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"日记"。根据上文"winter vacation is coming"以及后文"make good use of every day"，此处应填"计划"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我计划早起并在早上锻炼。A 项 get up 意为"起床"；B 项 wake up 意为"醒来"；C 项 stand up 意为"站起来"；D 项 stay up 意为"熬夜"。与"do some exercise in the morning"搭配，应选"起床"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我每天也会至少读一个小时的书。A 项意为"每天"；B 项意为"每晚"；C 项意为"某天"；D 项意为"有一天"。根据"at least one hour"和计划性描述，应为每天坚持。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：此外，我需要完成作业。A 项意为"然而"；B 项意为"此外、另外"；C 项意为"相反"；D 项意为"虽然"。此处表示并列递进，补充另一项计划。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会每天做一点，这样就不会感到太累。A 项意为"放松的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"疲惫的"；D 项意为"兴奋的"。每天做一点作业是为了避免一次性做太多感到疲惫。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许我可以学做一道简单的菜，或者学习一项新技能比如绘画。A 项意为"运动"；B 项意为"科目"；C 项意为"技能"；D 项意为"游戏"。cook 和 painting 都属于技能。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道制定计划比执行计划容易。A 项意为"思考"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"制定"；D 项意为"记住"。make a plan 是固定搭配，意为"制定计划"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信有了清晰的计划，我的寒假将既愉快又有意义。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"长的"；C 项意为"短的"；D 项意为"令人愉快的"。与 meaningful 并列，表示假期质量。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我很好地执行计划，我相信我会度过美好的时光。A 项意为"如果"；B 项意为"除非"；C 项意为"因为"；D 项意为"虽然"。此处表示条件关系。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我从明天开始吧！A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"开始"；D 项意为"等待"。start from 意为"从……开始"，与计划开头呼应。故选 C。
