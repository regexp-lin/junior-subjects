# 中考英语完形填空：雪地里的脚印

## 完形填空原文

It snowed heavily last January. By morning, the whole city was covered in a thick white blanket. Schools were closed, and most people stayed ___1___.

I was excited at first. No school! I could sleep in, play games, and eat snacks all day. But by noon, I was already ___2___. There was nothing to do.

I looked out the window and saw something strange. In the fresh snow covering our yard, there was a line of small footprints leading from the street to our back door. They were too small to be ___3___ — they looked like they belonged to an animal.

I put on my coat and boots and followed the footprints. They led me around the side of the house to the old wooden shed in the backyard. The door was slightly ___4___. I pushed it open and looked inside.

There, huddled in the corner on a pile of old newspapers, was a small grey cat. It was thin, dirty, and ___5___. When it saw me, it didn't run. It just looked at me with big green eyes, as if asking for help.

I ran back inside and told my mother. She brought out a bowl of warm milk and some leftover chicken. The cat ate ___6___ — it must have been starving for days.

"Can we keep it?" I asked.

My mother hesitated. "We'll take care of it for now," she said. "But we should try to find its ___7___."

We put up notices around the neighborhood and posted online, but nobody came forward. After two weeks, Mom finally said the words I had been ___8___ to hear: "I guess it's ours now."

I named the cat Snowball, because she came to us in a snowstorm. She quickly became part of the family. She slept on my bed, sat on my lap while I did homework, and always ___9___ me at the door when I came home from school.

That snow day changed my life in a way I never expected. I had been bored and lonely, and the snow ___10___ me a friend. Sometimes, the best things find you when you're not even looking.

## 选项

1. A. outside &emsp;&emsp; B. awake &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. indoors
2. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
3. A. animal &emsp;&emsp; B. children's &emsp;&emsp; C. human &emsp;&emsp; D. bird
4. A. broken &emsp;&emsp; B. locked &emsp;&emsp; C. open &emsp;&emsp; D. painted
5. A. shaking &emsp;&emsp; B. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; C. playing &emsp;&emsp; D. hiding
6. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. politely &emsp;&emsp; C. noisily &emsp;&emsp; D. hungrily
7. A. name &emsp;&emsp; B. owner &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. home
8. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. sorry &emsp;&emsp; C. waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. ready
9. A. met &emsp;&emsp; B. left &emsp;&emsp; C. followed &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
10. A. told &emsp;&emsp; B. brought &emsp;&emsp; C. cost &emsp;&emsp; D. taught

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大多数人待在___。A 项意为"外面"；B 项意为"醒着"；C 项意为"独自"；D 项意为"室内"。大雪封城，学校停课，人们待在"室内"（indoors）。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到中午我已经___了。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"生气的"。后文说"什么都没得做"，说明开始觉得"无聊"（bored）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：脚印太小了，不像___的。A 项意为"动物的"；B 项意为"小孩的"；C 项意为"人的"；D 项意为"鸟的"。脚印不像"人"（human）的，看起来像动物留下的，后面证实是猫。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：门微微___着。A 项意为"坏的"；B 项意为"锁着的"；C 项意为"开着的"；D 项意为"被漆的"。猫钻进了棚子，门微微"开着"（open），所以才有脚印通向这里。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（现在分词作表语）
**【解析】** 本句意为：它又瘦又脏，还在___。A 项意为"发抖"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"玩耍"；D 项意为"躲藏"。大雪天，流浪猫又冷又饿，在"发抖"（shaking）。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：猫___地吃了起来。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"礼貌地"；C 项意为"吵闹地"；D 项意为"饥饿地"。后面说它一定饿了好几天了，所以吃得"饥饿地"（hungrily）。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我们应该试着找到它的___。A 项意为"名字"；B 项意为"主人"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"家"。妈妈说先照顾着但要找找它的"主人"（owner），看是不是有人丢了猫。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈终于说了我一直___的话。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"抱歉"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"准备好"。"我"一直"等着"（waiting）听妈妈说可以养这只猫。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次我放学回家，它总是在门口___我。A 项意为"迎接"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"忘记"。猫在门口"迎接"（met）主人回家，体现亲密的关系。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：雪___给了我一个朋友。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"带来"；C 项意为"花费"；D 项意为"教"。大雪"带来了"（brought）一只猫，也带来了一个朋友。故选 B。
