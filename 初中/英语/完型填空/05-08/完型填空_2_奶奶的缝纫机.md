# 中考英语完形填空：奶奶的缝纫机

## 完形填空原文

In the corner of my grandmother's bedroom stood an old sewing machine. It was black, heavy, and made a rhythmic clicking sound whenever she used it. As a child, I loved watching her ___1___ — her fingers moved so quickly that they seemed to dance across the fabric.

Grandma could make almost anything: dresses, curtains, pillow covers, and even ___2___ for my stuffed animals. "A good pair of hands can turn old cloth into gold," she used to say.

When I was ten, I ripped my favorite jacket during a basketball game. A long tear went right through the sleeve. I was ___3___ — it was the jacket my mother had bought me for my birthday.

"Bring it to me," Grandma said. She examined the tear and nodded. "I can ___4___ this."

She sat down at the sewing machine and worked for almost an hour. When she was done, she handed me the jacket. The tear was completely ___5___. Instead of simply closing the hole, she had sewn a small star pattern over it in bright yellow thread. It looked even better than before.

"Why didn't you just close the hole?" I asked.

She smiled. "Because a scar doesn't have to be ugly. If you ___6___ it with care, it can become something beautiful."

I wore that jacket for two more years. When classmates asked about the star, I always told them the ___7___ proudly.

Years later, Grandma's hands became too stiff to sew. The sewing machine sat ___8___ in the corner, collecting dust. One day, I asked her to teach me. She looked surprised but happy. "You really want to learn?" she asked.

"Of course," I said. "Someone needs to keep the machine ___9___."

She taught me the basics — how to thread the needle, how to control the speed, how to sew a straight line. I wasn't good at first. My lines were crooked and my stitches were uneven. But Grandma just laughed and said, "That's exactly how I started."

Now, whenever something tears, I don't throw it away. I sit down at the old sewing machine, close my eyes for a moment, and ___10___ of Grandma. Then I start to sew — turning scars into stars.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我喜欢看她___。A 项意为"做饭"；B 项意为"画画"；C 项意为"缝纫"；D 项意为"写"。前文说到缝纫机，奶奶在"缝纫"（sew），手指在布料上飞舞。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：甚至给我的毛绒玩具做___。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"玩具"；C 项意为"鞋子"；D 项意为"衣服"。用缝纫机给玩偶做"衣服"（clothes），与前面的dresses等并列。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___——那是妈妈生日买给我的。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"难过的"；C 项意为"惊讶的"；D 项意为"自豪的"。最爱的生日礼物被撕破了，自然很"难过"（upset）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能___这个。A 项意为"修理"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"藏"。奶奶检查了裂口，说自己能"修好"（fix）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：裂口完全___了。A 项意为"新的"；B 项意为"干净的"；C 项意为"消失了的"；D 项意为"开着的"。修好后裂口完全"消失了"（gone），看不出来了。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你用心___它，它能变成美丽的东西。A 项意为"对待"；B 项意为"剪"；C 项意为"留"；D 项意为"覆盖"。奶奶说伤疤如果用心"对待"（treat），可以变成美丽的点缀。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学问起星星时，我总是自豪地讲这个___。A 项意为"笑话"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"规则"。向同学分享奶奶修补夹克的"故事"（story）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：缝纫机___地待在角落里积灰。A 项意为"安静地"；B 项意为"安全地"；C 项意为"吵闹地"；D 项意为"骄傲地"。奶奶不再用了，缝纫机"安静地"（quietly）闲置在角落。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：得有人让这台机器继续___。A 项意为"干净的"；B 项意为"安静的"；C 项意为"运转的"；D 项意为"新的"。"keep...running"（让……持续运转），"我"要让缝纫机不被闲置。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：闭上眼，___奶奶。A 项意为"梦见"；B 项意为"听到"；C 项意为"想"；D 项意为"谈论"。"think of"（想起）是固定搭配，每次缝纫时都会"想起"奶奶。故选 C。
