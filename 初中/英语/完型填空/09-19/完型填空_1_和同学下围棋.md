# 中考英语完形填空：和同学下围棋

## 完形填空原文

Weiqi (Go) is an ancient Chinese board game. Last month, our school started a Weiqi club and I decided to join. At the first meeting, I met Li Ming, who had been playing for three years. He offered to ___1___ me.

"The rules seem complicated, but you'll understand ___2___ you practice more," Li Ming said. He taught me how to place the black and white stones on the board. At the beginning, I always made ___3___ and lost quickly. Li Ming never laughed at me. Instead, he encouraged me to think ___4___ before each move.

As weeks went by, I became more interested. I began to see that Weiqi was not just a game — it was about strategy and patience. Every game was different, and I learned something ___5___ each time. One afternoon, I finally won a game! I was so ___6___ that I couldn't stop smiling.

Now Weiqi has become my favourite after-school activity. Playing with Li Ming has not only improved my ___7___ but also strengthened our friendship. We often ___8___ about life while playing. I have learned that in Weiqi, as in life, we need to be careful and never give ___9___ easily. I'm looking forward to ___10___ the school Weiqi competition next month!

## 选项

1. A. teach&emsp;&emsp; B. beat&emsp;&emsp; C. watch&emsp;&emsp; D. follow
2. A. if&emsp;&emsp; B. until&emsp;&emsp; C. unless&emsp;&emsp; D. when
3. A. mistakes&emsp;&emsp; B. progress&emsp;&emsp; C. friends&emsp;&emsp; D. plans
4. A. carefully&emsp;&emsp; B. quickly&emsp;&emsp; C. loudly&emsp;&emsp; D. happily
5. A. old&emsp;&emsp; B. new&emsp;&emsp; C. bad&emsp;&emsp; D. same
6. A. tired&emsp;&emsp; B. worried&emsp;&emsp; C. excited&emsp;&emsp; D. bored
7. A. scores&emsp;&emsp; B. skills&emsp;&emsp; C. homework&emsp;&emsp; D. health
8. A. argue&emsp;&emsp; B. chat&emsp;&emsp; C. fight&emsp;&emsp; D. complain
9. A. in&emsp;&emsp; B. up&emsp;&emsp; C. off&emsp;&emsp; D. out
10. A. joining&emsp;&emsp; B. joining in&emsp;&emsp; C. taking part in&emsp;&emsp; D. attending

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他主动提出教我。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"打败"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"跟随"。offer to do 后接 teach，表示愿意教。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：规则看起来复杂，但只要你多练习就会明白。A 项 if 意为"如果；只要"；B 项 until 意为"直到"；C 项 unless 意为"除非"；D 项 when 意为"当……时"。"if you practice more"表条件。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我总是出错，很快就输了。A 项意为"错误"，make mistakes 为固定搭配；B 项意为"进步"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"计划"。"make mistakes"犯错误。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相反，他鼓励我每走一步之前都要仔细思考。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"开心地"。下棋需仔细思考。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每局都不同，每次我都能学到新东西。A 项意为"老的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"相同的"。learn something new 学到新东西。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太兴奋了，忍不住微笑。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。赢棋后应感到兴奋。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：和李明一起下棋不仅提高了我的技术，也增进了我们的友谊。A 项意为"分数"；B 项意为"技能；技术"；C 项意为"作业"；D 项意为"健康"。下棋提高的是棋艺技能。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们下棋时经常聊天。A 项意为"争论"；B 项意为"聊天"；C 项意为"打架"；D 项意为"抱怨"。chat about 聊关于……的话题。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了，下棋如人生，我们需要小心，不要轻易放弃。A 项 give in 意为"屈服"；B 项 give up 意为"放弃"；C 项 give off 意为"发出"；D 项 give out 意为"分发"。"never give up easily"不要轻易放弃。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我期待参加下个月的学校围棋比赛！A 项 join 意为"加入（组织）"；B 项 join in 意为"参加（活动）"；C 项 take part in 意为"参加（活动）"；D 项 attend 意为"出席"。参加比赛用 take part in 或 join in，且 look forward to 后接动名词。故选 C。
