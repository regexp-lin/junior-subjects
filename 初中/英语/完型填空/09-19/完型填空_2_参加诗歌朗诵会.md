# 中考英语完形填空：参加诗歌朗诵会

## 完形填空原文

Our school's Poetry Recitation Contest was held last Friday. I had never taken part in such an event before, so I felt both nervous and ___1___. I chose a famous Chinese poem about spring and practiced reciting it every day for two weeks.

On the day of the contest, I wore my best clothes and went to the school hall ___2___. When my name was called, I walked onto the stage. My hands were shaking a little, but I took a deep ___3___ and began to recite. I tried to put my feelings into every word and express the beauty of the poem.

To my surprise, the audience listened ___4___ and gave me a warm applause when I finished. I won the second prize! The judge said my recitation was full of ___5___ and that I had shown a good understanding of the poem. I was so happy that I could ___6___ believe it.

Looking back, I realize that the key to success was ___7___. Without daily practice, I would not have done so well. My Chinese teacher also told me that reading poetry can help us appreciate the ___8___ of language. From now on, I plan to read more poems in my ___9___ time. Maybe next year I will try to win the first ___10___!

## 选项

1. A. bored&emsp;&emsp; B. excited&emsp;&emsp; C. angry&emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. early&emsp;&emsp; B. late&emsp;&emsp; C. slowly&emsp;&emsp; D. alone
3. A. sleep&emsp;&emsp; B. breath&emsp;&emsp; C. rest&emsp;&emsp; D. look
4. A. carelessly&emsp;&emsp; B. loudly&emsp;&emsp; C. quietly&emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
5. A. fear&emsp;&emsp; B. sadness&emsp;&emsp; C. emotion&emsp;&emsp; D. boredom
6. A. almost&emsp;&emsp; B. hardly&emsp;&emsp; C. nearly&emsp;&emsp; D. quite
7. A. luck&emsp;&emsp; B. practice&emsp;&emsp; C. talent&emsp;&emsp; D. money
8. A. difficulty&emsp;&emsp; B. length&emsp;&emsp; C. beauty&emsp;&emsp; D. speed
9. A. busy&emsp;&emsp; B. free&emsp;&emsp; C. working&emsp;&emsp; D. sleeping
10. A. place&emsp;&emsp; B. prize&emsp;&emsp; C. seat&emsp;&emsp; D. chance

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未参加过这样的活动，所以感到既紧张又兴奋。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"累的"。nervous 与 excited 常一起形容参赛心情。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛当天，我穿上最好的衣服，早早来到学校礼堂。A 项意为"早"；B 项意为"晚"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"独自"。重要活动通常会提前到场。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我深吸一口气，开始朗诵。A 项意为"睡觉"；B 项意为"呼吸"，take a deep breath 深吸一口气；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"看"。"take a deep breath"为固定短语。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：令我惊讶的是，观众静静地听着，并在结束时给了我热烈的掌声。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"快速地"。听朗诵时应安静。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委说我的朗诵充满情感，对诗歌理解得很好。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"情感"；D 项意为"无聊"。"full of emotion"充满感情，为褒义。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我高兴得几乎不敢相信。A 项 almost 可与 believe 连用但语气不同；B 项 could hardly believe 意为"几乎不敢相信"；C 项 nearly 类似 almost；D 项 quite 不用于此。"could hardly believe"为常用表达。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回顾过去，我意识到成功的关键是练习。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"天赋"；D 项意为"金钱"。后文"Without daily practice"与之呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的语文老师还告诉我，读诗能帮助我们欣赏语言之美。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"长度"；C 项意为"美"；D 项意为"速度"。"appreciate the beauty of"欣赏……之美。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从现在起，我计划在空闲时间多读诗。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"空闲的"，free time 闲暇时间；C 项意为"工作的"；D 项意为"睡觉的"。"in one's free time"在某人空闲时。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许明年我会争取赢得一等奖！A 项意为"名次；地方"；B 项意为"奖赏；奖项"；C 项意为"座位"；D 项意为"机会"。与"second prize"对应，first prize 一等奖。故选 B。
