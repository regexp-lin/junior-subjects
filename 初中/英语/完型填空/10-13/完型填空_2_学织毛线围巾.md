# 中考英语完形填空：学织毛线围巾

## 完形填空原文

Last winter, my grandmother taught me how to knit a wool scarf. At first, I thought it would be very ___1___, but I was wrong. When I held the knitting needles for the first time, my fingers felt clumsy and I kept making mistakes. Grandma smiled and said, "Don't worry. Everyone starts ___2___ when they first learn."

She showed me the basic steps. First, I had to cast on the stitches — that means putting the wool onto the needles. I tried many times before I got it ___3___. Then came the main part: knitting row after row. The movements seemed simple, but doing them correctly took a lot of practice. My grandmother sat ___4___ me to guide my hands and helped me whenever I was stuck.

As the days went by, I became more skilled. The scarf grew longer and longer. I chose soft grey wool because it would ___5___ well with my winter coat. Sometimes I made a mistake and had to undo several rows. Grandma never got angry. She said that making mistakes was part of learning and that patience was ___6___.

After about two weeks, my scarf was finally finished. When I wrapped it around my neck for the first time, I felt so ___7___. It was not perfect — you could still see a few small holes where I had dropped stitches — but it was made by my own hands. I gave it to my mother as a birthday gift. She was very ___8___ and said it was the best present she had ever received.

Now I am knitting a second scarf for my father. I have learned that learning a new skill takes time and effort, but the ___9___ to try is worth it. If you want to try knitting, do not be afraid to start. Just like my grandmother said, "The first step is always the ___10___."

## 选项

1. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
2. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. bravely &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
3. A. right &emsp;&emsp; B. wrong &emsp;&emsp; C. back &emsp;&emsp; D. away
4. A. against &emsp;&emsp; B. beside &emsp;&emsp; C. behind &emsp;&emsp; D. above
5. A. fight &emsp;&emsp; B. agree &emsp;&emsp; C. match &emsp;&emsp; D. compare
6. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. silly &emsp;&emsp; D. rare
7. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. nervous
8. A. moved &emsp;&emsp; B. surprised &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
9. A. cost &emsp;&emsp; B. result &emsp;&emsp; C. choice &emsp;&emsp; D. chance
10. A. longest &emsp;&emsp; B. hardest &emsp;&emsp; C. easiest &emsp;&emsp; D. shortest

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我以为会非常简单，但我错了。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。与后文"手指笨拙、常出错"形成转折，起初以为 easy。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：祖母笑着说："别担心，每个人初学时都会这样小心翼翼的。"A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"勇敢地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。初学新技能时需 carefully（小心谨慎）。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试了很多次才做对。A 项意为"正确的"；B 项意为"错误的"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"离开"。get it right 为固定搭配，意为"做对、弄对"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：祖母坐在我身后指导我的手，每当我遇到困难时就帮助我。A 项意为"反对、靠着"；B 项意为"在……旁边"；C 项意为"在……后面"；D 项意为"在……上方"。教织毛衣时常 sit behind 学生以便从身后手把手指导。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了柔软的灰色毛线，因为它和我的冬季外套很相配。A 项意为"战斗"；B 项意为"同意"；C 项意为"匹配、相配"；D 项意为"比较"。match well with 意为"与……相配"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：祖母说犯错是学习的一部分，耐心很重要。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"愚蠢的"；D 项意为"罕见的"。学习手艺需要耐心，patience is important。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我第一次把围巾围在脖子上时，我感到非常自豪。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"紧张的"。亲手完成作品感到 proud。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：母亲非常感动，说这是她收到过的最好的礼物。A 项意为"感动的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"放松的"。收到孩子亲手做的礼物，母亲会 be moved。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了学新技能需要时间和努力，但选择去尝试是值得的。A 项意为"花费、成本"；B 项意为"结果"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"机会"。the choice to try 意为"选择去尝试"，强调主动尝试的价值。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：祖母说："第一步总是最难的。"A 项意为"最长的"；B 项意为"最难的"；C 项意为"最容易的"；D 项意为"最短的"。开始新事物时第一步最难，the first step is the hardest 为常用表达。故选 B。
