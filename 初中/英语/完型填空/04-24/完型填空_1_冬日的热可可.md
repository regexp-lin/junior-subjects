# 中考英语完形填空：冬日的热可可

## 完形填空原文

It was a cold December afternoon. I walked home from school with my hands in my pockets, trying to ___1___ warm. The wind was blowing hard, and I could see my breath in the air.

As I passed by a small café, I noticed an elderly man sitting alone by the window. He had a cup in front of him and looked a little ___2___. Something made me stop and go inside.

"One hot chocolate, please," I said to the waiter. I paid for two cups ___3___ I only wanted one. "Could you give the extra one to that gentleman by the window?" I asked quietly.

The waiter smiled and nodded. A few minutes later, I sat at a table near the door, drinking my hot chocolate. It warmed my hands and my heart.

The old man looked up when the waiter brought him the second cup. He seemed ___4___. He turned around and saw me. I gave him a small wave. He walked over slowly and sat down beside me.

"Thank you, young friend," he said. "My daughter used to bring me hot chocolate on cold days. She moved to another city last year. I've felt ___5___ ever since."

We talked for almost an hour. He told me about his life, his work as a teacher, and his love for reading. I told him about my school and my dreams. When it was time to leave, he ___6___ my shoulder and said, "You've made my day. Kindness costs nothing but means everything."

Since that day, I have often ___7___ that café in winter. Sometimes I buy an extra cup for a stranger. The warmth of hot chocolate on a cold day can ___8___ more than just a drink — it can connect hearts.

That December afternoon taught me something ___9___: a small act of kindness can bring warmth to someone's day. You don't need to do something ___10___ to make a difference. Sometimes, a cup of hot chocolate is enough.

## 选项

1. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. feel &emsp;&emsp; C. keep &emsp;&emsp; D. become
2. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
3. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. though &emsp;&emsp; C. if &emsp;&emsp; D. when
4. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
5. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. busy &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. lucky
6. A. touched &emsp;&emsp; B. hit &emsp;&emsp; C. pulled &emsp;&emsp; D. pushed
7. A. passed &emsp;&emsp; B. visited &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. missed
8. A. mean &emsp;&emsp; B. give &emsp;&emsp; C. bring &emsp;&emsp; D. take
9. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
10. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. small &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. easy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我手插在口袋里步行回家，试图___暖和。A 项意为"保持"；B 项意为"感觉"；C 项意为"保持"（keep 后需加形容词）；D 项意为"变得"。"stay warm"为固定搭配，表示"保持暖和"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他面前有一杯饮料，看起来有点___。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"孤独的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。后文提到他女儿搬走了，他一直感到孤独，此处独自坐在窗边也暗示"孤独"（lonely）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我付了两杯的钱___我只要一杯。A 项意为"因为"；B 项意为"虽然"；C 项意为"如果"；D 项意为"当……时"。"though"表转折，意为"虽然只要一杯，却付了两杯的钱"，符合为老人多买一杯的情节。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：服务员给他送上第二杯时，他看起来___。A 项意为"惊讶的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"疲倦的"。陌生人 unexpectedly 送他一杯热可可，他感到"惊讶"（surprised）是自然的。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她去年搬去了另一个城市。从那以后我一直感到___。A 项意为"坚强的"；B 项意为"忙碌的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"幸运的"。女儿搬走后，老人独居，会感到"孤独"（lonely），与前文"lonely"相呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：该离开时，他___我的肩膀说……。A 项意为"触碰/轻拍"；B 项意为"打"；C 项意为"拉"；D 项意为"推"。"touch one's shoulder"表示友好地轻拍肩膀，表达感激和鼓励。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自从那天起，我冬天经常___那家咖啡馆。A 项意为"经过"；B 项意为"拜访/光顾"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"想念"。后文说有时会给陌生人买一杯，说明他常"光顾"（visited）那家店。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冬日里一杯热可可的温暖，能___的不仅仅是饮料——它能连接人心。A 项意为"意味着"；B 项意为"给"；C 项意为"带来"；D 项意为"拿"。"mean more than"表示"意味着比……更多"，强调其象征意义。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个十二月的下午教给了我一些___的道理。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"a small act of kindness"说明道理并不复杂，是"简单的"（simple）却深刻的。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你不需要做___的事就能带来改变。A 项意为"大的"；B 项意为"小的"；C 项意为"难的"；D 项意为"容易的"。"something big"与后文"a cup of hot chocolate is enough"形成对比，强调微小善举也能产生意义。故选 A。
