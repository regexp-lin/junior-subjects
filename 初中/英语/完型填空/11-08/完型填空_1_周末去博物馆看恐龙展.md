# 中考英语完形填空：周末去博物馆看恐龙展

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my father took me to the Natural History Museum to see a dinosaur exhibition. I had been looking ___1___ to this trip for a long time because I am a big fan of dinosaurs.

When we entered the exhibition hall, I was immediately ___2___ by the huge skeleton of a Tyrannosaurus Rex. It was about twelve meters long and five meters tall. I couldn't believe that such giant creatures once ___3___ on Earth.

There were many other dinosaur models as well. Some were plant-eaters with long necks, and others were small but ___4___ hunters. Each model had a sign that explained the dinosaur's name, size, and eating habits. I read every sign ___5___ and took lots of notes.

One area had an interactive screen where visitors could see what dinosaurs might have looked like when they were ___6___. I touched the screen and watched a Triceratops walk through a forest. It felt like I was traveling back in ___7___!

My favorite part was the fossil digging experience. We used small brushes to carefully uncover ___8___ dinosaur bones hidden in sand. I found a small fossil and the guide said I could take a photo with it. I was thrilled!

Before we left, I bought a small dinosaur model from the gift shop. My father said this visit was a great way to learn about ___9___. He was right. I learned so much more here than from books alone.

I hope to visit more museums in the future. They are like windows that ___10___ us to see the wonderful world beyond our daily lives.

## 选项

1. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. forward &emsp;&emsp; C. down &emsp;&emsp; D. back
2. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. troubled &emsp;&emsp; C. amazed &emsp;&emsp; D. hurt
3. A. flew &emsp;&emsp; B. lived &emsp;&emsp; C. hid &emsp;&emsp; D. swam
4. A. lazy &emsp;&emsp; B. slow &emsp;&emsp; C. gentle &emsp;&emsp; D. fierce
5. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
6. A. alive &emsp;&emsp; B. asleep &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. away
7. A. space &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. line &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
8. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. fake &emsp;&emsp; C. broken &emsp;&emsp; D. modern
9. A. art &emsp;&emsp; B. music &emsp;&emsp; C. history &emsp;&emsp; D. math
10. A. force &emsp;&emsp; B. allow &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. warn

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我期待这次旅行很久了。A 项 look up 意为"查找"；B 项 look forward to 意为"期待"；C 项 look down 意为"向下看"；D 项 look back 意为"回顾"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我立刻被霸王龙的巨大骨架震撼了。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"困扰的"；C 项意为"惊叹的"；D 项意为"受伤的"。巨大骨架让人 amazed。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我无法相信这么巨大的生物曾经在地球上生活过。A 项意为"飞"；B 项意为"生活"；C 项意为"躲藏"；D 项意为"游泳"。lived on Earth 意为"在地球上生活"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：其他有些体型小但很凶猛的猎手。A 项意为"懒的"；B 项意为"慢的"；C 项意为"温和的"；D 项意为"凶猛的"。小型肉食恐龙是 fierce hunters。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仔细地阅读每个标牌并记了很多笔记。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"伤心地"。认真学习用 carefully。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：可以看到恐龙活着时可能的样子。A 项意为"活着的"；B 项意为"睡着的"；C 项意为"独自的"；D 项意为"离开的"。when they were alive 意为"它们活着的时候"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感觉像在时间旅行。A 项意为"空间"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"线"；D 项意为"麻烦"。traveling back in time 意为"穿越时空"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用小刷子小心地挖出藏在沙子里的真恐龙骨骼。A 项意为"真的"；B 项意为"假的"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"现代的"。化石发掘体验中的 real bones。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说这次参观是学习历史的好方式。A 项意为"艺术"；B 项意为"音乐"；C 项意为"历史"；D 项意为"数学"。恐龙展属于 history。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：博物馆像窗户一样让我们看到日常之外的精彩世界。A 项意为"强迫"；B 项意为"允许"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"警告"。allow us to see 意为"让我们看到"。故选 B。
