# 中考英语完形填空：晚自习后的路灯

## 完形填空原文

Wang Fang lived in a small village. Her school was in the town, about five kilometres away. Every evening after the last class, she had to walk home alone. The road was dark, and she often felt ___1___. But there was one thing that made her feel better—the street lamps along the way.

Those old lamps would ___2___ at exactly 6:30 every evening. When Wang Fang saw their warm light, she knew she was safe. The light helped her see the road clearly and kept away her ___3___. She walked under the lamps, step by step, and thought about what she had learned that day. Sometimes she would ___4___ a song to herself. The journey home became a quiet time for her to think.

One night, Wang Fang noticed that one lamp was not working. The next day, she told the village head about it. "Thank you for letting us know," he said. "We will ___5___ it as soon as possible." Two days later, the lamp was fixed. Wang Fang felt happy that she could help. She learned that speaking up could make a ___6___.

In winter, the nights were longer. Wang Fang often walked home in the cold wind. But when she saw those lights ahead, she felt warm inside. The lamps were like friends waiting for her. They ___7___ her that she was not alone. Many students like her walked this road every day. The lamps had been there for years, guiding one generation after another.

Years later, Wang Fang became a teacher. She often told her students about those lamps. "Never be afraid of the dark," she said. "There is always light somewhere. And remember, you can also be a ___8___ for others." The story of the lamps taught her that small acts of kindness could light up someone's way. She hoped her students would always look for the light and ___9___ become the light for others. The road and those street lamps would forever ___10___ in her memory.

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
2. A. turn on &emsp;&emsp; B. turn off &emsp;&emsp; C. turn down &emsp;&emsp; D. turn up
3. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. sadness &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
4. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. sing &emsp;&emsp; C. write &emsp;&emsp; D. tell
5. A. check &emsp;&emsp; B. build &emsp;&emsp; C. repair &emsp;&emsp; D. buy
6. A. choice &emsp;&emsp; B. plan &emsp;&emsp; C. difference &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
7. A. told &emsp;&emsp; B. reminded &emsp;&emsp; C. asked &emsp;&emsp; D. promised
8. A. lamp &emsp;&emsp; B. star &emsp;&emsp; C. sun &emsp;&emsp; D. fire
9. A. sometimes &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. already &emsp;&emsp; D. perhaps
10. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. remain &emsp;&emsp; D. keep

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这条路很暗，她常常感到___。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。夜晚独行在黑暗的路上，自然感到害怕。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那些旧路灯每晚六点半准时___。A 项意为"打开、亮起"；B 项意为"关闭"；C 项意为"调低"；D 项意为"调高"。"turn on"指灯亮起来，与后文"warm light"相呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：灯光帮她看清道路，驱散了她的___。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"恐惧"。"kept away her fear"与前文"felt afraid"相呼应，灯光驱散恐惧。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（动宾搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时她会自己___一首歌。A 项意为"读"；B 项意为"唱"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"讲"。"sing a song to oneself"为常见搭配，意为"轻声哼唱"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们会尽快___它。A 项意为"检查"；B 项意为"建造"；C 项意为"修理"；D 项意为"购买"。路灯不亮，需要"修理"（repair）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：她明白了说出来能让情况有所___。A 项意为"选择"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"不同、改变"；D 项意为"错误"。"make a difference"为固定搭配，意为"产生影响、有所改变"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们___她，她并不孤单。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"提醒"；C 项意为"问"；D 项意为"承诺"。"remind sb. that..."意为"提醒某人……"，路灯的存在让她意识到有人守护。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（比喻用法）
**【解析】** 本句意为：记住，你也可以成为别人的___。A 项意为"灯"；B 项意为"星星"；C 项意为"太阳"；D 项意为"火"。与前文"light"及"lamps"主题呼应，鼓励学生也成为照亮他人的"灯"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她希望学生永远寻找光明，___成为照亮他人的光。A 项意为"有时"；B 项意为"从不"；C 项意为"已经"；D 项意为"也许、或许"。"perhaps become the light"表达美好的祝愿与期待。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那条路和那些路灯将永远___在她的记忆里。A 项意为"停留"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"留存、保留"；D 项意为"保持"。"remain in one's memory"意为"留在记忆中"，为常见表达。故选 C。
