# 中考英语完形填空：收音机的声音

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather's radio was old and worn. The buttons were loose, and the sound sometimes came out fuzzy. But Grandpa would never ___1___ it. He had bought it forty years ago with his first month's salary. To him, it was more than a machine — it was a ___2___ of his youth.

Every evening after dinner, Grandpa would sit in his armchair, turn on the radio, and listen to the news or music. I used to think it was ___3___. "Why not use your phone or watch TV?" I asked him once.

He smiled and said, "When I was young, the radio was our only window to the world. I listened to it with my whole family. Those were happy ___4___."

One day, the radio stopped working. Grandpa tried to fix it, but he couldn't. He looked so sad. I had never seen him like that before.

I decided to ___5___ him. I went online and searched for someone who could repair old radios. After a few days, I found an old repairman in the next town. I took the radio to him.

"It's a classic," the repairman said. "I'll do my best." A week later, he called. The radio was ready.

I brought it home and gave it to Grandpa. When he turned it on and heard the familiar ___6___, his eyes lit up. "You fixed it! Thank you, my dear."

From that day on, I started to understand. The radio was not just about sound. It was about ___7___ — memories of the past, and a connection between Grandpa and his family. Sometimes we hold on to old things not because they are useful, but because they ___8___ something important to us.

Now I sometimes sit with Grandpa and listen to the radio too. We don't talk much, but we share the same moment. In a world full of screens and noise, that simple ___9___ feels strangely peaceful.

Grandpa says the best gift I ever gave him was not the repaired radio, but the ___10___ that I finally understood why it mattered.

## 选项

1. A. sell &emsp;&emsp; B. throw &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
2. A. picture &emsp;&emsp; B. memory &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. tool
3. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. modern &emsp;&emsp; C. useful &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
4. A. days &emsp;&emsp; B. hours &emsp;&emsp; C. minutes &emsp;&emsp; D. years
5. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. call &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
6. A. music &emsp;&emsp; B. voice &emsp;&emsp; C. noise &emsp;&emsp; D. sound
7. A. feeling &emsp;&emsp; B. meaning &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. result
8. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. carry &emsp;&emsp; C. bring &emsp;&emsp; D. keep
9. A. habit &emsp;&emsp; B. activity &emsp;&emsp; C. moment &emsp;&emsp; D. story
10. A. fact &emsp;&emsp; B. idea &emsp;&emsp; C. hope &emsp;&emsp; D. truth

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但爷爷从不___它。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"扔掉"；C 项意为"丢失"；D 项意为"忘记"。"would never throw it"表示爷爷非常珍惜，绝不"扔掉"（throw）这台收音机。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对他来说，这不仅仅是一台机器——更是他青春的___。A 项意为"图片"；B 项意为"记忆"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"工具"。爷爷用第一个月工资买的，代表过去的时光，是青春的"记忆"（memory）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前觉得这很___。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"现代的"；C 项意为"有用的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。作者不理解爷爷为何不用手机或电视，觉得听收音机"奇怪"（strange）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是一段快乐的___。A 项意为"日子/时光"；B 项意为"小时"；C 项意为"分钟"；D 项意为"年"。"Those were happy days"是常用表达，指"那是一段快乐的日子"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定___他。A 项意为"跟随"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"打电话"；D 项意为"等待"。爷爷的收音机坏了很难过，作者想"帮助"（help）他修好。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当他打开收音机听见熟悉的___时。A 项意为"音乐"；B 项意为"嗓音"；C 项意为"噪音"；D 项意为"声音"。泛指收音机发出的"声音"（sound），比 music 更全面。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：收音机不仅仅是关于声音，而是关于___。A 项意为"感觉"；B 项意为"意义"；C 项意为"原因"；D 项意为"结果"。后文解释了 memories、connection，强调其"意义"（meaning）。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们保留旧东西，不是因为它们有用，而是因为它们为我们___了某些重要的东西。A 项意为"记得"；B 项意为"承载/携带"；C 项意为"带来"；D 项意为"保持"。"carry"有"承载（情感、记忆）"之义，指物品承载了重要回忆。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在充满屏幕和噪音的世界里，那个简单的___感觉 strangely peaceful。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"时刻"；D 项意为"故事"。指祖孙一起听收音机的"时刻"（moment）很安宁。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最好的礼物不是修好的收音机，而是我终于理解它为何重要的这个___。A 项意为"事实"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"希望"；D 项意为"真相"。"the fact that..."是常见句型，表示"……这一事实"。故选 A。
