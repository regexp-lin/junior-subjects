# 中考英语完形填空：失而复得的手表

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather gave me a watch on my twelfth birthday. It was not an expensive watch — just a simple silver one with a leather strap. But to me, it was the most ___1___ thing I owned, because Grandpa had worn it for twenty years before passing it to me.

One afternoon, I took the watch off during P.E. class and put it in my bag. When the class was over, I reached into my bag and felt my heart ___2___. The watch was gone.

I searched everywhere — under the bench, around the track, even in the trash can. ___3___. I felt like crying. How could I lose something so important?

I told our P.E. teacher, Mr. Chen. He announced it to the whole class: "If anyone has seen a silver watch, please return it. No questions ___4___."

Nobody came forward. I went home feeling terrible. That night, I couldn't sleep. I kept thinking about Grandpa's face when he gave me the watch. "Take good care of it," he had said with a ___5___ smile. And I had failed.

The next morning, something ___6___ was sitting on my desk when I walked into the classroom. It was a small box wrapped in notebook paper. Inside was my watch, along with a handwritten note: "I'm sorry I took it. I thought it was cool and I wanted to try it on. I didn't mean to keep it. Please ___7___ me."

There was no name on the note. I looked around the room, but nobody looked guilty. I held the watch tightly and felt a wave of ___8___ wash over me.

During lunch break, I stood up in front of the class. "I got my watch back," I said. "I don't know who returned it, but I want to say thank you. It takes courage to ___9___ a mistake. I'm not angry. I'm just grateful."

The classroom was quiet. Somewhere in the back row, I saw a boy's eyes turn ___10___. He blinked quickly and looked down. I didn't say anything more. Some things are better left unspoken.

## 选项

1. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. precious &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. common
2. A. sing &emsp;&emsp; B. dance &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. jump
3. A. Something &emsp;&emsp; B. Everything &emsp;&emsp; C. Nothing &emsp;&emsp; D. Anything
4. A. asked &emsp;&emsp; B. answered &emsp;&emsp; C. given &emsp;&emsp; D. needed
5. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. warm &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. weak
6. A. heavy &emsp;&emsp; B. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; C. unexpected &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
7. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. teach &emsp;&emsp; D. forgive
8. A. anger &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. relief &emsp;&emsp; D. pride
9. A. repeat &emsp;&emsp; B. avoid &emsp;&emsp; C. admit &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoy
10. A. red &emsp;&emsp; B. bright &emsp;&emsp; C. cold &emsp;&emsp; D. dry

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但对我来说，它是我拥有的最___的东西。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"珍贵的"；C 项意为"漂亮的"；D 项意为"普通的"。前文说手表不贵，但因为是爷爷戴了20年才传给他的，所以它是"珍贵的"（precious），有感情价值。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我伸手进包里，感到心都___了。A 项意为"唱歌"；B 项意为"跳舞"；C 项意为"停了"；D 项意为"跳"。发现手表不见了，心一下子"停了"（stop），"heart stopped"形容极度震惊和恐慌。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：什么都___。A 项意为"某些东西"；B 项意为"所有东西"；C 项意为"什么都没有"；D 项意为"任何东西"。到处找了但什么都"没找到"（Nothing），一个独立句表示绝望。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："如果任何人看到了一块银色手表，请归还它。不会追___任何问题。"A 项意为"问的"；B 项意为"回答的"；C 项意为"给的"；D 项意为"需要的"。"No questions asked"（不追问原因）是固定表达，意思是归还即可，不会追究。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他脸上带着___的微笑把手表给了我。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"虚弱的"。爷爷把珍爱的手表传给孙子，自然是带着"温暖的"（warm）微笑。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二天早上，我走进教室时桌上放着一件___的东西。A 项意为"重的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"意想不到的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。前一天还以为找不回来了，突然出现在桌上，是"意想不到的"（unexpected）。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："我不是故意要留着的。请___我。"A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"教"；D 项意为"原谅"。拿了别人的东西并道歉归还，请求对方"原谅"（forgive）。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我紧紧握着手表，感到一阵___席卷而来。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"宽慰"；D 项意为"骄傲"。失而复得，紧张和担忧消散，取而代之的是"如释重负"（relief）的感觉。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："___一个错误需要勇气。"A 项意为"重复"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"承认"；D 项意为"享受"。匿名归还并附上道歉信，这是"承认"（admit）错误的行为，需要勇气。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到后排一个男孩的眼睛变___了。A 项意为"红的"；B 项意为"明亮的"；C 项意为"冷的"；D 项意为"干的"。听到"我"不生气并且表示感谢，那个拿了手表的男孩感动得眼眶发"红"（red），快要哭了。故选 A。
