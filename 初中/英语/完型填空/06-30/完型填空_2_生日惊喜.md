# 中考英语完形填空：生日惊喜

## 完形填空原文

My birthday was last Sunday. I thought it would be a normal day because my parents had to work and my best friends were busy. I planned to stay at home and watch some movies. Little did I know that my family and friends had prepared a big ___1___ for me.

That morning, my mum said she needed to go to the supermarket and asked me to wait at home. I felt a bit ___2___, but I agreed. An hour later, the doorbell rang. I opened the door and was shocked. There stood my classmates with a beautiful birthday cake! They shouted "Surprise!" and I could not believe my ___3___. They had been planning this for a whole week. My friend Emma had created a secret group chat to organize everything. They had even invited my cousin, who I had not seen for months.

We went into the living room. The room was decorated with balloons and colourful streamers. On the table there was a pile of ___4___ wrapped in pretty paper. I was so touched that my eyes got wet. I never thought so many people cared about me. We played games, sang songs, and had a lot of fun. When it was time to cut the cake, everyone sang "Happy Birthday" to me. I made a wish and blew out the ___5___. I wished that we could always stay good friends.

The best part was when my dad came home early from work. He had ___6___ to get leave so he could join us. He brought me a new basketball, which I had wanted for a long time. I hugged him and said thank you. My mum had made my favourite dishes for dinner. We all sat around the table and enjoyed the ___7___ together. It was the happiest birthday I had ever had.

That day taught me something important. Sometimes we feel ___8___, but we are often loved more than we realise. Our family and friends may not always say it, but they show their love through actions. I will never ___9___ this special surprise. From now on, I want to do the same for others—to make people feel loved and ___10___. That is the best gift we can give.

8. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. alone &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. happy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一点都不知道家人和朋友为我准备了一个大___。A 项意为"晚餐"；B 项意为"惊喜"；C 项意为"会议"；D 项意为"课程"。后文多次出现"surprise"，且为生日惊喜派对，"surprise"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点___，但我同意了。A 项意为"饿的"；B 项意为"孤单的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。以为生日没人陪，独自在家会感到孤单，"lonely"符合。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我简直不敢相信自己的___。A 项意为"耳朵"；B 项意为"鼻子"；C 项意为"眼睛"；D 项意为"嘴巴"。"cannot believe one's eyes"为固定搭配，意为"不敢相信自己所看到的"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：桌上有一堆用漂亮纸包装的___。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"衣服"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"花"。生日派对上桌上通常是礼物，"presents"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与文化常识
**【解析】** 本句意为：我许了愿，吹灭了___。A 项意为"蜡烛"；B 项意为"灯"；C 项意为"火"；D 项意为"火柴"。生日蛋糕上吹灭的是蜡烛，"candles"为固定搭配。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他___请到假以便能参加我们的派对。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"设法做到"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"决定"。"managed to"意为"成功地（做到）"，强调克服困难请到假。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们围坐桌边，一起享用___。A 项意为"蛋糕"；B 项意为"饭菜、餐食"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"音乐"。" favourite dishes"指妈妈做的菜，大家一起吃的是晚餐，"meal"合适。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们感到___，但我们得到的爱往往比我们意识到的要多。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"孤单的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"开心的"。与后文"被爱"形成对比，之前以为没人陪会感到"孤单"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会___这次特别的惊喜。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"记住"；D 项意为"离开"。"never forget"意为"永不忘怀"，表示铭记于心。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想让别人感到被爱和___。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"富有的"；D 项意为"著名的"。"feel special"意为"感到自己很特别、被重视"，与"loved"并列，表达被关爱。故选 B。
