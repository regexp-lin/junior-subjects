# 中考英语完形填空：博物馆参观

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my class went on a trip to the city museum. It was my first time ___1___ a museum, and I was very excited. When we arrived, a friendly guide welcomed us and said she would show us around. The museum had many different halls. We started with the history hall, where we saw ancient tools and old coins. The guide told us interesting ___2___ about how people lived hundreds of years ago. I had never ___3___ that history could be so fascinating.

Then we walked to the science hall. There were many hands-on exhibits. We could touch some of them and try small experiments. I tried to make a simple circuit, and when the small bulb lit up, I felt like a real ___4___. My classmate Lucy and I worked together on a puzzle about the solar system. We learned that the Earth ___5___ around the Sun. It was much more fun than reading about it in our textbooks.

After a short break, we went to the art hall. The paintings were beautiful, but I did not understand all of them. Our teacher said that art does not always need to be understood. "Just ___6___ it and see what you feel," she said. So I stood in front of a colorful painting and tried to feel what the artist wanted to express. To my surprise, I found myself enjoying it.

Before we left, we bought some postcards in the gift shop as ___7___. On the bus back to school, we talked about what we had seen. Everyone agreed that the trip was wonderful. I hope we can visit more museums in the future. Museums are great ___8___ to learn new things and open our minds. I will always ___9___ this special day. It made me realize that learning can be fun when we go out and explore the world ___10___ ourselves.

## 选项

1. A. visiting &emsp;&emsp; B. building &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; D. selling
2. A. jokes &emsp;&emsp; B. stories &emsp;&emsp; C. questions &emsp;&emsp; D. answers
3. A. imagined &emsp;&emsp; B. remembered &emsp;&emsp; C. forgotten &emsp;&emsp; D. understood
4. A. doctor &emsp;&emsp; B. teacher &emsp;&emsp; C. scientist &emsp;&emsp; D. artist
5. A. runs &emsp;&emsp; B. jumps &emsp;&emsp; C. moves &emsp;&emsp; D. travels
6. A. draw &emsp;&emsp; B. copy &emsp;&emsp; C. watch &emsp;&emsp; D. hear
7. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. souvenirs &emsp;&emsp; C. clothes &emsp;&emsp; D. books
8. A. rooms &emsp;&emsp; B. places &emsp;&emsp; C. times &emsp;&emsp; D. ways
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
10. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. from

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我第一次___博物馆，我非常兴奋。A 项意为"参观"；B 项意为"建造"；C 项意为"打扫"；D 项意为"出售"。"visiting a museum"意为"参观博物馆"，符合班级出行场景。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：导游给我们讲了关于古人生活的有趣的___。A 项意为"笑话"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"答案"。讲解历史时讲述的是故事或趣闻，"stories"符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未___历史可以如此迷人。A 项意为"想象过"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"理解"。首次发现历史有趣，表示之前未曾想象过，"imagined"最恰当。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当小灯泡亮起时，我觉得自己像个真正的___。A 项意为"医生"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"科学家"；D 项意为"艺术家"。做电路实验成功，有科学家的成就感，"scientist"符合。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与科学常识
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学到地球___太阳运转。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"绕行、运行"。"travel around"意为"围绕……运行"，描述地球绕太阳公转。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：就___它，看看你有什么感受。A 项意为"画"；B 项意为"模仿"；C 项意为"观看、欣赏"；D 项意为"听"。欣赏画作用"watch"或"look at"，此处 watch 有"用心观看"之意。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：离开前，我们在礼品店买了一些明信片作为___。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"纪念品"；C 项意为"衣服"；D 项意为"书"。博物馆买的明信片是纪念品，"souvenirs"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：博物馆是___新事物、开阔眼界的好___。A 项意为"房间"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"次数"；D 项意为"方式"。博物馆是"地方"，"place to learn"意为"学习的好地方"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会永远___这个特别的日子。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"离开"。难忘的经历用"remember"表示铭记。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们走出门去___自己探索世界时，学习可以是有趣的。A 项意为"和"；B 项意为"由、靠"；C 项意为"为"；D 项意为"从"。"by ourselves"意为"亲自、亲身"，强调亲身体验探索。故选 B。
