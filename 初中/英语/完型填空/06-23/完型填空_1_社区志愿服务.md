# 中考英语完形填空：社区志愿服务

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, I joined a community volunteer program. Every Saturday morning, I went to the community center to help elderly people. At first, I was not sure if I could do the job ___1___. I had never worked with old people before. But the leader of the program said, "Just be ___2___ and patient. They will like you."

My main task was to read newspapers for Mr. Wang, who has poor eyesight. He is over eighty years old but still loves to know what is happening in the world. When I read to him, he would sometimes ___3___ and ask me questions. I learned a lot from our conversations. He told me stories about his youth and taught me to ___4___ every day. I began to look forward to our meetings.

One day, Mr. Wang said to me, "Young people like you give us ___5___. You make us feel that we are not alone." His words made me realize that even small acts of kindness can ___6___ a big difference. I felt proud of what I was doing. The experience also helped me ___7___ my own grandparents more. I started to visit them more often and listen to their stories.

By the end of the summer, I had made many new friends at the community center. The volunteer work did not bring me money, but it brought me something much more ___8___—happiness and a sense of purpose. I have decided to continue volunteering in the future. Helping others has become an important ___9___ of my life. I hope more young people will join in and ___10___ their time to help those in need. Together, we can make our community a warmer place.

## 选项

1. A. badly &emsp;&emsp; B. well &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
2. A. strict &emsp;&emsp; B. kind &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. serious
3. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
4. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. play &emsp;&emsp; C. learn &emsp;&emsp; D. work
5. A. hope &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. gifts
6. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. get &emsp;&emsp; D. find
7. A. understand &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
8. A. useful &emsp;&emsp; B. valuable &emsp;&emsp; C. cheap &emsp;&emsp; D. easy
9. A. moment &emsp;&emsp; B. place &emsp;&emsp; C. part &emsp;&emsp; D. idea
10. A. save &emsp;&emsp; B. give &emsp;&emsp; C. keep &emsp;&emsp; D. lose

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始，我不确定自己能否把这项工作做好。A 项意为"糟糕地"；B 项意为"好"；C 项意为"缓慢地"；D 项意为"快速地"。"do the job well"表示"把工作做好"，与"not sure"相呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：项目负责人说："只要___和有耐心，他们会喜欢你的。"A 项意为"严格的"；B 项意为"友善的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"严肃的"。"kind"与志愿服务、与老人相处的情境相呼应。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我给他读报时，他有时会___并向我提问。A 项意为"停下来"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"等待"。"stop"与"ask me questions"相呼应，表示读报时停下来交流。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他告诉我他年轻时的故事，并教我每天都要___。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"玩"；C 项意为"学习"；D 项意为"工作"。"learn"与"taught me"、"conversations"相呼应，老人常勉励晚辈好学。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王先生说："像你们这样的年轻人给我们带来___。"A 项意为"希望"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"礼物"。"hope"与"feel that we are not alone"相呼应，志愿服务给老人带来希望与陪伴感。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话让我意识到，即使微小的善举也能产生很大___。A 项"make a difference"意为"产生影响、有所不同"；B 项"take"；C 项"get"；D 项"find"均无此固定搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这段经历也帮助我更加___自己的祖父母。A 项意为"理解"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"想念"。"understand"与"started to visit them more often and listen"相呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：志愿工作没有给我带来金钱，但带来了更有___的东西。A 项意为"有用的"；B 项意为"宝贵的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"容易的"。"valuable"与"much more"、与"happiness and a sense of purpose"相呼应。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他人已成为我生活中重要的___。A 项意为"时刻"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"部分"；D 项意为"想法"。"part of my life"为常用表达，意为"生活的一部分"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望更多年轻人能加入进来，___时间去帮助需要的人。A 项意为"节省"；B 项意为"奉献、给予"；C 项意为"保留"；D 项意为"失去"。"give their time"与志愿服务相呼应，表示奉献时间。故选 B。
