# 中考英语完形填空：火车上的意外友谊

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, I took a train to visit my grandparents. The journey was long—about eight hours. I was traveling alone for the first time, and I felt a little ___1___ at the beginning. I brought a book to read, but soon I found it hard to ___2___ on the words. The train was crowded and noisy.

Then a girl about my age sat down next to me. She smiled and said hello. I smiled back. We started to talk. She was also going to visit her family in another city. We discovered that we both loved the same kind of music and the same books. Time ___3___ so fast when we were talking. Before I knew it, we had been friends for hours. She shared her snacks with me, and I shared mine with her. It felt like we had ___4___ each other for years.

When the train stopped at a station, we both got off to buy some water. There was a long ___5___ at the small shop. We waited together and continued our conversation. She told me about her dream of becoming a writer. I told her about my interest in painting. We encouraged each other to ___6___ our dreams. Her positive attitude made me feel much better about my trip.

At the end of the journey, we exchanged our phone numbers. We promised to keep in ___7___. Now, months later, we still send messages to each other every week. That train ride taught me an important ___8___: friendship can appear when we least expect it. We just need to be ___9___ to others and open our hearts. A stranger today might become a ___10___ friend tomorrow. I am thankful for that wonderful journey and the friend I met along the way.

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. depend &emsp;&emsp; B. focus &emsp;&emsp; C. try &emsp;&emsp; D. hold
3. A. ran &emsp;&emsp; B. walked &emsp;&emsp; C. flew &emsp;&emsp; D. stopped
4. A. helped &emsp;&emsp; B. met &emsp;&emsp; C. known &emsp;&emsp; D. missed
5. A. line &emsp;&emsp; B. road &emsp;&emsp; C. bridge &emsp;&emsp; D. table
6. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. follow &emsp;&emsp; D. believe
7. A. touch &emsp;&emsp; B. mind &emsp;&emsp; C. order &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
8. A. subject &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. game
9. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. polite &emsp;&emsp; C. similar &emsp;&emsp; D. clever
10. A. new &emsp;&emsp; B. old &emsp;&emsp; C. close &emsp;&emsp; D. same

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我第一次独自出行，一开始感到有点___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"孤单的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。"lonely"与"traveling alone for the first time"相呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我带了本书读，但很快就发现很难___在文字上。A 项意为"依靠"；B 项意为"集中注意力"；C 项意为"尝试"；D 项意为"握住"。"focus on"为固定搭配，意为"集中注意力于"，与"crowded and noisy"相呼应。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们聊天时，时间___得如此快。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"走"；C 项意为"飞"；D 项意为"停止"。"Time flies"为固定表达，意为"时光飞逝"，形容时间过得快。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感觉就像我们已经___对方很多年了。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"遇见"；C 项意为"认识"；D 项意为"想念"。"known each other for years"表示"相识多年"，形容一见如故。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小店前排了很长的___。A 项意为"队伍"；B 项意为"道路"；C 项意为"桥"；D 项意为"桌子"。"long line"指排队等候的队伍，与"waited"相呼应。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相鼓励要___自己的梦想。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"追随、追求"；D 项意为"相信"。"follow our dreams"为常用表达，意为"追求梦想"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们约定保持___。A 项"keep in touch"意为"保持联系"；B 项"keep in mind"意为"牢记"；C 项"in order"意为"井然有序"；D 项"in trouble"意为"陷入困境"。与"send messages every week"相呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次火车之旅教会我一个重要的___。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"教训、道理"；D 项意为"游戏"。"lesson"在此指人生道理，与"taught me"相呼应。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只需要对他人___，敞开心扉。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"有礼貌的、友善的"；C 项意为"相似的"；D 项意为"聪明的"。"polite"与"open our hearts"相呼应，表示友善待人。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今天的陌生人可能成为明天的___朋友。A 项意为"新的"；B 项意为"老的"；C 项意为"亲密的"；D 项意为"同样的"。"close friend"意为"亲密的朋友"，与"stranger"形成对比。故选 C。
