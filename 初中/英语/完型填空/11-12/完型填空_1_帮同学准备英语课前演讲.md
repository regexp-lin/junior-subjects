# 中考英语完形填空：帮同学准备英语课前演讲

## 完形填空原文

My classmate Wang Lin was nervous about the English speech before class. Every week, one student has to give a short speech in front of the class. It was his ___1___ next Tuesday. He asked me to help him prepare because my English was better.

We met after school in the library. First, I helped him choose a ___2___. He liked sports, so we decided on "My Favorite Soccer Player". Then we wrote an outline together. I told him to keep the speech short—about two or three minutes. He shouldn't try to ___3___ too much in such limited time.

Next, we practiced the pronunciation of difficult words. Wang Lin had trouble with "professional" and "championship". I read them aloud and he repeated after me. We did this many ___4___ until he could say them correctly. I also suggested he use some body language to make his speech more ___5___. For example, he could use hand gestures when talking about scores.

We rehearsed the whole speech several times. Each time, I gave him some ___6___. At first, he spoke too fast. I told him to slow down and take a breath between sentences. He improved a lot. By the third practice, he was much more ___7___.

On Tuesday, Wang Lin gave his speech. He did a great job! The teacher praised him and the classmates applauded. After class, he came to thank me. He said, "Without your help, I wouldn't have ___8___ it. Thank you so much!" I felt happy to help. Helping others also helps ___9___ learn. When we teach, we understand things better. I hope more classmates will come to me when they need ___10___. It's a pleasure to share what I know.

## 选项

1. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. chance &emsp;&emsp; D. day
2. A. book &emsp;&emsp; B. topic &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. movie
3. A. cover &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. remember &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
4. A. ways &emsp;&emsp; B. times &emsp;&emsp; C. ideas &emsp;&emsp; D. days
5. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. lively &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
6. A. praise &emsp;&emsp; B. advice &emsp;&emsp; C. gifts &emsp;&emsp; D. questions
7. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. shy &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
8. A. made &emsp;&emsp; B. done &emsp;&emsp; C. got &emsp;&emsp; D. passed
9. A. myself &emsp;&emsp; B. himself &emsp;&emsp; C. ourselves &emsp;&emsp; D. yourselves
10. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. answers

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下周二轮到他了。A 项意为"轮流"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"机会"；D 项意为"日子"。It was his turn 意为"轮到他了"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我帮他选了一个主题。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"主题"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"电影"。演讲需选 topic。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在这么短时间内不应该试图涵盖太多内容。A 项意为"覆盖；涵盖"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"记得"；D 项意为"错过"。cover too much 意为"讲太多"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们重复了很多次直到他说对。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"次数"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"天"。many times 意为"多次"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我建议他用手势让演讲更生动。A 项意为"长的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"生动的"；D 项意为"困难的"。body language 使演讲 lively。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次练习我都给他一些建议。A 项意为"表扬"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"问题"。give advice 意为"给建议"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：练到第三次时他放松多了。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"害羞的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。练习后变得 relaxed。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有你的帮助我不可能成功。A 项意为"做；成功"；B 项意为"做"；C 项意为"得到"；D 项意为"通过"。make it 意为"成功；做到"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助别人也能帮助自己学习。A 项意为"我自己"；B 项意为"他自己"；C 项意为"我们自己"；D 项意为"你们自己"。主语是 I，用 myself。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望更多同学需要帮助时来找我。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"答案"。need help 意为"需要帮助"。故选 B。
