# 中考英语完形填空：学做彩色蜡烛

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, I joined a candle-making class at the community center. I had always thought candles were just for giving ___1___, but this class showed me they could also be beautiful works of art.

The teacher prepared all the materials for us: wax blocks, wicks, dyes in different colors, fragrance oils, and glass containers. She said making candles is not ___2___ at all if you follow the steps carefully.

First, we broke the wax blocks into small pieces and put them into a pot. The teacher heated the pot slowly until the wax ___3___. She told us never to heat it too fast, or it might be dangerous. Safety is always the most ___4___ thing.

When the wax became liquid, we added the dye. I chose blue and purple because I wanted my candle to look like the night ___5___. I stirred the wax gently until the colors mixed well. Then I added a few drops of lavender fragrance oil. The sweet smell made everyone ___6___.

Next, we placed a wick in the center of our glass container. The teacher helped us hold it straight with two chopsticks. Then we ___7___ poured the colored wax into the container. I added the blue wax first, waited a few minutes, and then added the purple wax on top. This created a beautiful two-layer ___8___.

We had to wait about an hour for the candles to cool down and become solid. While waiting, the teacher showed us photos of candles in different ___9___: stars, flowers, and even animals. I want to try making those next time.

When I took my candle home, my mother was surprised by how beautiful it was. She said it would be a perfect ___10___ for her reading table. I was very proud of my work.

3. A. froze &emsp;&emsp; B. melted &emsp;&emsp; C. burned &emsp;&emsp; D. dried
4. A. funny &emsp;&emsp; B. common &emsp;&emsp; C. important &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
5. A. sky &emsp;&emsp; B. sea &emsp;&emsp; C. forest &emsp;&emsp; D. garden
6. A. cry &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. smile
7. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
8. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. effect &emsp;&emsp; C. sound &emsp;&emsp; D. weight
9. A. sizes &emsp;&emsp; B. prices &emsp;&emsp; C. shapes &emsp;&emsp; D. ages
10. A. toy &emsp;&emsp; B. tool &emsp;&emsp; C. decoration &emsp;&emsp; D. snack

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直以为蜡烛只是用来照明的。A 项意为"光"；B 项意为"热"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"水"。蜡烛最基本的功能是 giving light。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做蜡烛一点也不难，只要按步骤来。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。not difficult at all 意为"一点也不难"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师慢慢加热直到蜡块融化。A 项意为"结冰"；B 项意为"融化"；C 项意为"燃烧"；D 项意为"变干"。蜡受热会 melted。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：安全始终是最重要的事。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"重要的"；D 项意为"简单的"。the most important 意为"最重要的"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了蓝色和紫色因为想让蜡烛看起来像夜空。A 项意为"天空"；B 项意为"海"；C 项意为"森林"；D 项意为"花园"。蓝紫色像 night sky。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：甜美的香味让大家都笑了。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"睡"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"微笑"。好闻的香味让人 smile。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们小心地把彩色蜡液倒入容器。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"小心地"；D 项意为"大声地"。热蜡液需要 carefully 操作。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这样形成了漂亮的双层效果。A 项意为"味道"；B 项意为"效果"；C 项意为"声音"；D 项意为"重量"。two-layer effect 意为"双层效果"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师展示了不同形状的蜡烛照片。A 项意为"大小"；B 项意为"价格"；C 项意为"形状"；D 项意为"年龄"。星星、花、动物都是不同 shapes。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说它会是阅读桌上完美的装饰品。A 项意为"玩具"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"装饰品"；D 项意为"零食"。漂亮蜡烛做 decoration。故选 C。
