# 中考英语完形填空：课间十分钟

## 完形填空原文

The ten-minute break between classes is important for every student. It gives us time to rest our minds and prepare for the next lesson. Different students spend the break in different ___1___. Some go to the bathroom or get a drink of water. Others walk around the corridor or chat with friends in the hallway.

My favorite thing to do during the break is to look out of the window. Our classroom is on the third floor, and we have a good ___2___ of the playground. I like watching students playing basketball or doing exercises. Sometimes I see teachers walking and talking. The fresh air from the open window makes me feel ___3___.

Last week, something interesting happened. My deskmate Xiao Ming found a bird with a hurt wing on the windowsill. We were both ___4___ and didn't know what to do. We quickly went to our biology teacher for help. She told us to put the bird in a soft box and keep it warm. We followed her ___5___ and the little bird seemed to feel better. Later, the teacher took it to an animal rescue center.

That experience made me realize that the break is not only for resting. We can also use it to ___6___ our surroundings and help others when needed. Now I pay more attention to what happens around me. Yesterday I helped a first-year student find his ___7___ when he looked lost in the corridor. He thanked me with a big smile. Small acts of kindness can make a big ___8___.

Our headteacher often reminds us to use the break ___9___. She says we shouldn't run in the hallway or play too wildly. Safety always comes first. I think a good break helps us study better in the next class. So let's make the ___10___ of every ten-minute break!

## 选项

1. A. ways&emsp;&emsp; B. places&emsp;&emsp; C. times&emsp;&emsp; D. things
2. A. view&emsp;&emsp; B. picture&emsp;&emsp; C. photo&emsp;&emsp; D. map
3. A. tired&emsp;&emsp; B. sleepy&emsp;&emsp; C. refreshed&emsp;&emsp; D. hungry
4. A. excited&emsp;&emsp; B. worried&emsp;&emsp; C. bored&emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
5. A. advice&emsp;&emsp; B. orders&emsp;&emsp; C. rules&emsp;&emsp; D. suggestions
6. A. forget&emsp;&emsp; B. avoid&emsp;&emsp; C. observe&emsp;&emsp; D. leave
7. A. book&emsp;&emsp; B. bag&emsp;&emsp; C. classroom&emsp;&emsp; D. friend
8. A. mistake&emsp;&emsp; B. problem&emsp;&emsp; C. difference&emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
9. A. wisely&emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly&emsp;&emsp; C. slowly&emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
10. A. worst&emsp;&emsp; B. best&emsp;&emsp; C. least&emsp;&emsp; D. most

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：不同的学生以不同的方式度过课间。A 项意为"方式、方法"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"事情"。in different ways 意为"以不同方式"，故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的教室在三楼，可以很好地看到操场。A 项意为"视野、景色"；B 项意为"图片"；C 项意为"照片"；D 项意为"地图"。have a good view of 意为"能很好地看到"，故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从打开的窗户吹进来的新鲜空气让我感到神清气爽。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"困倦的"；C 项意为"恢复精神的"；D 项意为"饥饿的"。fresh air 让人 refreshed，故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都很担心，不知道该怎么办。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"放松的"。发现受伤的小鸟自然会 worried，故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们听从她的建议，小鸟似乎好多了。A 项意为"建议"；B 项意为"命令"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"建议"（可数）。follow one's advice 是固定搭配，故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们也可以利用课间观察周围的环境，在需要时帮助他人。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"观察"；D 项意为"离开"。observe surroundings 意为"观察周围"，符合语境，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：昨天我帮助一位初一学生在走廊里找到他的教室，当时他看起来很迷茫。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"包"；C 项意为"教室"；D 项意为"朋友"。looked lost 说明找不到教室，故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的善举能产生很大的影响。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"区别、影响"；D 项意为"麻烦"。make a big difference 意为"产生很大影响"，故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任经常提醒我们要明智地利用课间。A 项意为"明智地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"缓慢地"；D 项意为"快速地"。use wisely 与后文 safety 呼应，故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：所以让我们充分利用每一个十分钟的课间吧！A 项 worst 意为"最差"；B 项 best 意为"最好"；C 项 least 意为"最少"；D 项 most 意为"最多"。make the most of 意为"充分利用"，故选 D。
