# 中考英语完形填空：自行车旅行

## 完形填空原文

This summer, my dad suggested that we go on a bicycle trip together. I was both excited and a little ___1___. I had never ridden my bike for more than an hour before, and Dad said this trip would take the whole day.

We got up very early on Saturday morning. Mum prepared some sandwiches and bottles of water for us. We put everything into our backpacks and ___2___ off at six o'clock. The morning air was cool and fresh. Birds were singing in the trees along the road. I felt full of ___3___.

After riding for about two hours, we reached a small village. An old farmer was working in his field. He waved at us and shouted, "Good morning! Where are you going?" We told him we were heading to the lake. He smiled and said, "It's a ___4___ place. You'll love it."

By noon, the sun was very strong and I started to feel tired. My legs were sore and I wanted to ___5___. Dad noticed my struggle and said, "Let's take a break under that big tree." We sat down, drank some water, and ate our sandwiches. After the rest, I felt much ___6___.

We continued riding and finally arrived at the lake in the early afternoon. The water was clear and blue, surrounded by green hills. It was more beautiful than I had ___7___. We sat by the lake and enjoyed the peaceful view. Dad said, "Do you see? The best things in life are worth the ___8___."

On the way back, I did not feel as tired as before. Perhaps it was because the beautiful scenery had given me new strength. When we got home, Mum asked us how the trip was. I said, "It was ___9___! I want to do it again." Dad laughed and said, "That's my boy."

That bicycle trip taught me that sometimes you need to push through difficulties to ___10___ something truly wonderful.

## 选项

1. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
2. A. took &emsp;&emsp; B. set &emsp;&emsp; C. turned &emsp;&emsp; D. put
3. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. energy &emsp;&emsp; C. doubt &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
4. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; C. lovely &emsp;&emsp; D. busy
5. A. give up &emsp;&emsp; B. speed up &emsp;&emsp; C. grow up &emsp;&emsp; D. stay up
6. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. heavier &emsp;&emsp; D. slower
7. A. forgotten &emsp;&emsp; B. expected &emsp;&emsp; C. drawn &emsp;&emsp; D. refused
8. A. effort &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. risk
9. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. amazing &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. normal
10. A. lose &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. reach &emsp;&emsp; D. describe

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我既兴奋又有点___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。从未骑过这么远，对全天旅途有些担心，"worried"与"excited"形成并列情感。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们六点___出发了。A 项"took off"意为"起飞"；B 项"set off"意为"出发"；C 项"turned off"意为"关闭"；D 项"put off"意为"推迟"。骑车出门用"set off"表示出发。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到充满___。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"精力"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"悲伤"。清晨空气清新，充满活力，"full of energy"意为"精力充沛"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是个___的地方，你们会喜欢的。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"可爱的、美好的"；D 项意为"繁忙的"。农民推荐湖边风景，说那里很美好，"lovely"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的腿很酸，我想___。A 项意为"放弃"；B 项意为"加速"；C 项意为"长大"；D 项意为"熬夜"。又累又疼想放弃继续骑行，"give up"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：休息之后，我感觉___多了。A 项意为"更差的"；B 项意为"更好的"；C 项意为"更重的"；D 项意为"更慢的"。休息喝水吃东西后恢复体力，"felt much better"意为"感觉好多了"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：湖泊比我___的还要美丽。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"期待、预想"；C 项意为"画"；D 项意为"拒绝"。实际风景超出预期，"more beautiful than I had expected"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生活中最好的东西值得你付出___。A 项意为"努力"；B 项意为"金钱"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"风险"。"worth the effort"意为"值得付出努力"，呼应骑行辛苦但风景值得。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：太___了！我还想再来一次。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"令人惊叹的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"普通的"。旅途美好，还想再来，"amazing"表达惊喜赞叹。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时你需要克服困难才能___真正美好的东西。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"到达、达到"；D 项意为"描述"。克服困难后才能获得美好事物，"reach something wonderful"意为"到达/获得美好的事物"。故选 C。
