# 中考英语完形填空：新同桌

## 完形填空原文

At the beginning of the new term, our teacher rearranged the seats. I was a little unhappy because I had to ___1___ my old deskmate, Lily. We had been sitting together for a whole year and became very good friends. Now my new deskmate was a boy called Zhang Wei.

Zhang Wei was very quiet. He hardly talked to anyone in class. During the first few days, we barely ___2___ a word. I thought he was unfriendly and did not want to be bothered. I even complained to Lily, "I wish I could sit with you again. My new deskmate is so ___3___."

One day, I forgot to bring my pencil case. I was worried because we had a maths test that morning. Without saying anything, Zhang Wei ___4___ pushed a pen towards me. I looked at him in surprise and said thank you. He just nodded slightly and went back to his work. That small action changed my ___5___ of him.

I decided to try to know him better. During lunch break, I asked him what he liked to do after school. His face ___6___ up and he said, "I like drawing!" He took out a notebook full of beautiful sketches. There were drawings of animals, buildings, and cartoon characters. I was amazed. "These are ___7___!" I said.

From that day on, we started talking more. I found out that Zhang Wei was not unfriendly at all. He was just shy and did not know how to start a ___8___. Once he felt comfortable, he was actually very funny and kind. He taught me how to draw simple cartoons, and I helped him with his English.

By the end of the term, Zhang Wei and I had become great friends. I learned an important ___9___: you should never judge a person too quickly. Sometimes the quietest people have the most ___10___ hearts.

## 选项

1. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. meet &emsp;&emsp; C. help &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
2. A. heard &emsp;&emsp; B. wrote &emsp;&emsp; C. exchanged &emsp;&emsp; D. remembered
3. A. noisy &emsp;&emsp; B. cold &emsp;&emsp; C. lazy &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
4. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. nervously &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
5. A. opinion &emsp;&emsp; B. memory &emsp;&emsp; C. dream &emsp;&emsp; D. habit
6. A. lit &emsp;&emsp; B. gave &emsp;&emsp; C. shut &emsp;&emsp; D. blew
7. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. incredible &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
8. A. game &emsp;&emsp; B. project &emsp;&emsp; C. conversation &emsp;&emsp; D. competition
9. A. skill &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. word &emsp;&emsp; D. song
10. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. broken &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. empty

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点不开心，因为我不得不___我的旧同桌 Lily。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"遇见"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"跟随"。换座位意味着要离开原来的同桌，"leave"正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：头几天我们几乎没有___一句话。A 项意为"听到"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"交流、交换"；D 项意为"记住"。"exchange a word"意为"交谈、说话"，指两人之间几乎没有交流。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的新同桌太___了。A 项意为"吵闹的"；B 项意为"冷淡的"；C 项意为"懒惰的"；D 项意为"粗心的"。张伟不爱说话，"我"觉得他冷漠不友好，"cold"最合适。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：张伟什么也没说，___把一支笔推向我。A 项意为"生气地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"紧张地"；D 项意为"安静地"。他性格安静，默默地帮助"我"，"quietly"与前文他的性格一致。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个小举动改变了我对他的___。A 项意为"看法"；B 项意为"记忆"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"习惯"。从觉得他冷漠到改变看法，"opinion"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的脸___了起来。A 项意为"亮起"；B 项意为"给"；C 项意为"关闭"；D 项意为"吹"。"face lit up"是固定搭配，意为"脸上放光、容光焕发"，表示提到喜好时很开心。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些画太___了！A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"令人难以置信的好"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。看到精美画作发出赞叹，"incredible"表示赞叹不已。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他只是害羞，不知道如何开始一段___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"项目"；C 项意为"对话"；D 项意为"比赛"。"start a conversation"意为"发起对话"，解释他不爱说话的原因。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学到了一个重要的___。A 项意为"技能"；B 项意为"教训、道理"；C 项意为"单词"；D 项意为"歌曲"。通过经历领悟到的道理，"lesson"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最安静的人有时候拥有最___的心。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"破碎的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"空的"。安静的人内心善良美好，"beautiful hearts"意为"美丽的心灵"。故选 C。
