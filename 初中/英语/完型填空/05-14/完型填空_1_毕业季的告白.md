# 中考英语完形填空：毕业季的告白

## 完形填空原文

Graduation was only two weeks away. I had known Li Ming for three years — we sat next to each other in class, shared snacks at break time, and helped each other with homework. But I had never told him how much his friendship meant to me.

One afternoon, I found a note on my desk. It said: "Meet me at the old oak tree after school. — L.M." My heart ___1___. Was this what I thought it was?

I arrived early and waited. When Li Ming appeared, he looked nervous. "I have something to tell you," he said. "Before we all go to different high schools, I didn't want to ___2___ any regrets."

I held my breath. "You've been my best friend since seventh grade," he continued. "You made me laugh when I was sad. You ___3___ me when I failed the math test. I just wanted to say thank you. Thank you for being you."

I was so moved that I couldn't speak for a moment. Then I said, "I feel the same way. I was actually planning to tell you the same thing. I was ___4___ you wouldn't want to hear it."

We both laughed. The tension melted away. We sat under the tree and talked for over an hour — about our memories, our dreams, and our hopes for the future. It was one of the best ___5___ we had ever had.

"What if we go to different schools and lose touch?" Li Ming asked suddenly.

"Then we'll make sure we don't," I said. "We can text every week. We can meet during holidays. Friendship doesn't ___6___ just because we're in different places."

He smiled. "I'm glad I wrote that note."

"So am I," I said. "Some things are too important to leave unsaid. We almost ___7___ our chance to say them."

On graduation day, we took a photo together under the old oak tree. We promised to stay in touch. That conversation taught me something valuable: don't wait for the "right moment" to express your ___8___. The right moment might be now. And the people who matter deserve to know how much they ___9___.

If you have something to say to someone, say it. Don't let the chance ___10___ by.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的心___了。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"狂跳"；C 项意为"碎"；D 项意为"休息"。"heart raced"（心跳加速）形容紧张期待。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不想___任何遗憾。A 项意为"有"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"避免"。"have regrets"（留下遗憾）与"before we go"呼应，不想带着未说的话毕业。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我数学考砸时你___了我。A 项意为"表扬"；B 项意为"鼓励"；C 项意为"惩罚"；D 项意为"责备"。"encouraged"（鼓励）与"when I failed"搭配，体现友情支持。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___你不想听。A 项意为"确信"；B 项意为"害怕"；C 项意为"高兴"；D 项意为"自豪"。"was afraid"（担心）解释为何迟迟未开口。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我们有过的最好的___之一。A 项意为"考试"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"对话"；D 项意为"争论"。"conversations"与"talked for over an hour"呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：友谊不会仅仅因为我们在不同地方就___。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"增长"；C 项意为"开始"；D 项意为"继续"。"disappear"（消失）与"stay in touch"形成对比。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们差点___了说出来的机会。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"抓住"；C 项意为"创造"；D 项意为"浪费"。"missed our chance"（错过机会）强调及时表达的重要性。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要等待"对的时刻"才表达你的___。A 项意为"观点"；B 项意为"感受"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"秘密"。"express your feelings"（表达感受）与全文主题一致。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的人 deserve to know how much they ___。A 项意为"需要"；B 项意为"重要"；C 项意为"知道"；D 项意为"忘记"。"how much they matter"（他们有多重要）体现真诚表达。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：别让机会___。A 项意为"溜走"；B 项意为"来"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"飞"。"don't let the chance go by"（别让机会溜走）是常用表达。故选 A。
