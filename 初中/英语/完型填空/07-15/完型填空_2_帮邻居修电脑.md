# 中考英语完形填空：帮邻居修电脑

## 完形填空原文

Mr Wang is our neighbour. He is over sixty years old and lives alone. One afternoon, I saw him standing at his door, looking ___1___. I went over and asked what was wrong.

"My computer won't turn on," he said. "I need to video call my daughter who lives abroad. She is the only family I have." I could see that he was worried. I told him I would try to help. I had learned some computer ___2___ from my father, who works in IT.

I went into Mr Wang's house and looked at his computer. First I checked if it was plugged in. It was. Then I pressed the power button but nothing happened. I thought the ___3___ might be the problem. I unplugged it and plugged it in again. Still nothing.

I looked around and found a power strip. Maybe the switch was off. I pressed it and then tried the computer again. This time, the screen ___4___! Mr Wang's face lit up. "You are amazing!" he said. I felt proud but I told him it was just a small problem.

We sat down and I helped him open the video call software. He had forgotten his ___5___. I helped him reset it step by step. When his daughter's face appeared on the screen, Mr Wang was so happy that his eyes became red. "Thank you, thank you," he said to me. "Without your help, I would not have been able to ___6___ her today."

Before I left, I wrote down some simple ___7___ for him. "If the computer doesn't work, first check the power. Then try restarting it." I also told him he could call me anytime if he had problems. He smiled and said, "Young people like you make our community a better ___8___."

That evening, Mr Wang came to our door with a bag of fruit. "This is a small thank-you gift," he said. My parents were pleased. "Helping others is a good ___9___," my father said. I agreed. It feels great to use what I have learned to help someone in need. A small act of ___10___ can make a big difference to someone's day.

## 选项

1. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
2. A. games &emsp;&emsp; B. skills &emsp;&emsp; C. books &emsp;&emsp; D. songs
3. A. screen &emsp;&emsp; B. keyboard &emsp;&emsp; C. connection &emsp;&emsp; D. mouse
4. A. broke &emsp;&emsp; B. turned on &emsp;&emsp; C. went off &emsp;&emsp; D. fell down
5. A. name &emsp;&emsp; B. address &emsp;&emsp; C. password &emsp;&emsp; D. number
6. A. visit &emsp;&emsp; B. meet &emsp;&emsp; C. call &emsp;&emsp; D. see
7. A. letters &emsp;&emsp; B. notes &emsp;&emsp; C. stories &emsp;&emsp; D. messages
8. A. school &emsp;&emsp; B. place &emsp;&emsp; C. city &emsp;&emsp; D. country
9. A. job &emsp;&emsp; B. habit &emsp;&emsp; C. hobby &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson
10. A. kindness &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. patience &emsp;&emsp; D. wisdom

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看见他站在门口，看起来很___。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"担忧的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。电脑坏了联系不上女儿，自然担忧，"worried"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我跟做IT的爸爸学过一些电脑___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"技能"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"歌"。修电脑需要的是技能，"skills"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得问题可能在___。A 项意为"屏幕"；B 项意为"键盘"；C 项意为"连接/电源"；D 项意为"鼠标"。后文拔插电源线，说明怀疑电源连接，"connection"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词词组辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次屏幕___了！A 项意为"坏了"；B 项意为"亮了/开机"；C 项意为"熄了"；D 项意为"掉下来"。成功开机，"turned on"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他忘了___。A 项意为"名字"；B 项意为"地址"；C 项意为"密码"；D 项意为"号码"。视频通话软件需要密码登录，"password"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有你的帮助，我今天就___不到她了。A 项意为"拜访"；B 项意为"见面"；C 项意为"打电话"；D 项意为"见到"。"see"在此指视频中见到，最贴切。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我给他写了一些简单的___。A 项意为"信"；B 项意为"笔记/说明"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"消息"。写下操作步骤，"notes"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：像你这样的年轻人让我们的社区变得更___。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"城市"；D 项意为"国家"。"a better place"意为"更好的地方"，常用表达。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他人是一个好___。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"习惯"；C 项意为"爱好"；D 项意为"功课"。助人为乐应成为习惯，"habit"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的___之举能大大改变他人一天。A 项意为"善意"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"耐心"；D 项意为"智慧"。帮邻居修电脑是善意行为，"kindness"正确。故选 A。
