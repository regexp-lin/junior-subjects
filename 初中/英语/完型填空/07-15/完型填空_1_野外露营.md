# 中考英语完形填空：野外露营

## 完形填空原文

For my birthday this year, my parents gave me a special gift — a camping trip to the mountains. I had always wanted to sleep outdoors under the stars, and finally my ___1___ was coming true.

We drove for about three hours before we reached the campsite. It was a flat area near a stream, surrounded by tall trees. Dad and I set up the tent while Mum ___2___ the cooking equipment. It took us nearly an hour because we were not experienced. The tent kept falling down, which made us laugh.

After the tent was finally ___3___, we went exploring. We walked along a narrow path through the forest. I saw many different birds and even a small squirrel jumping between branches. Mum pointed at some wildflowers and said, "Look how ___4___ nature is. You can never see this in the city."

As the sun went down, we made a campfire. Dad showed me how to start a fire ___5___ using dry leaves and small sticks. We cooked sausages over the fire and ate them with bread. The food tasted extra good because we were eating outdoors. After dinner, we roasted marshmallows and told ___6___ around the fire. Dad told a funny story about his own childhood camping trip, and we all laughed until our stomachs hurt.

The best moment came when we lay on the grass and looked up. The sky was full of ___7___. I had never seen so many before. In the city, the lights are too bright to see them clearly. Here, every star seemed close enough to ___8___. I even saw a shooting star and quickly made a wish.

That night, I fell asleep listening to the gentle sound of the stream. When I woke up the next morning, the air was cool and ___9___. I decided that camping was one of the best experiences I had ever had. Nature has a special power to make us feel ___10___ and free.

## 选项

1. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. dream &emsp;&emsp; C. joke &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
2. A. prepared &emsp;&emsp; B. returned &emsp;&emsp; C. repaired &emsp;&emsp; D. sold
3. A. broken &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. ready &emsp;&emsp; D. closed
4. A. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; B. amazing &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. noisy
5. A. safely &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. lazily &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
6. A. lies &emsp;&emsp; B. secrets &emsp;&emsp; C. stories &emsp;&emsp; D. jokes
7. A. clouds &emsp;&emsp; B. stars &emsp;&emsp; C. birds &emsp;&emsp; D. planes
8. A. touch &emsp;&emsp; B. count &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
9. A. dirty &emsp;&emsp; B. heavy &emsp;&emsp; C. hot &emsp;&emsp; D. fresh
10. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. calm &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的___终于要实现了。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"玩笑"；D 项意为"问题"。"dream was coming true"是固定搭配，意为"梦想成真"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈___烹饪设备。A 项意为"准备"；B 项意为"返回"；C 项意为"修理"；D 项意为"卖"。搭帐篷的同时妈妈准备做饭的工具，"prepared"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帐篷终于___了之后，我们去探险。A 项意为"坏掉的"；B 项意为"丢失的"；C 项意为"准备好的"；D 项意为"关闭的"。帐篷搭好了，"ready"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看看大自然多___。A 项意为"危险的"；B 项意为"令人惊叹的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"吵闹的"。赞叹大自然的美，"amazing"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸教我如何___地用干树叶和小树枝生火。A 项意为"安全地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"懒散地"；D 项意为"悲伤地"。教孩子生火强调安全操作，"safely"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们围着篝火讲___。A 项意为"谎言"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"笑话"。后文说爸爸讲了一个有趣的故事，"stories"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天空满是___。A 项意为"云"；B 项意为"星星"；C 项意为"鸟"；D 项意为"飞机"。后文提到看流星、在城市灯光太亮看不到，指的是星星。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每颗星星似乎近到可以___。A 项意为"触摸"；B 项意为"数"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"隐藏"。星星看起来很近，仿佛伸手可及，"touch"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：早晨空气凉爽而___。A 项意为"脏的"；B 项意为"沉重的"；C 项意为"炎热的"；D 项意为"清新的"。山间清晨空气清新，"fresh"正确。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大自然有一种特别的力量让我们感到___和自由。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"平静的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。大自然让人内心平静，"calm"与"free"并列。故选 B。
