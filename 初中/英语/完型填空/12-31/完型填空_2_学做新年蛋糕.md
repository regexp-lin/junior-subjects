# 中考英语完形填空：学做新年蛋糕

## 完形填空原文

New Year's Eve is a special day. This year, I decided to learn how to make a New Year cake with my mother. She said it would bring good luck for the coming year.

The first ___1___ was to gather all the ingredients. We needed flour, eggs, sugar, milk, and some butter. Mom showed me how to mix them in the correct ___2___. "Start with the flour and eggs, then add the rest," she said. I followed her instructions carefully.

Next, we poured the mixture into a cake pan and put it in the oven. Mom set the temperature and told me we had to ___3___ for about forty minutes. I sat nearby and watched through the oven door. The kitchen slowly filled with a ___4___ smell. I could not wait to taste it.

When the cake was ready, Mom took it out and let it cool. Then we decorated it together with cream and fruit. I wrote "Happy New Year" on top with red frosting. It looked ___5___! Mom took a photo and said she would send it to our family group chat.

We cut the cake and shared it with our family. Everyone said it was delicious. I felt ___6___ of what I had made. Making the cake was not just about cooking — it was about spending ___7___ time with Mom and learning something new.

From that day on, I want to learn more ___8___ from my mother. I hope to make a New Year cake again next year, and maybe try other dishes too. Cooking can bring ___9___ to our family, and I want to be a part of that. The New Year cake has become a special ___10___ for me.

## 选项

1. A. step &emsp;&emsp; B. day &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. idea
2. A. room &emsp;&emsp; B. order &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. place
3. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. run &emsp;&emsp; C. cook &emsp;&emsp; D. eat
4. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. sour
5. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
6. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
7. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. free &emsp;&emsp; C. quality &emsp;&emsp; D. short
8. A. games &emsp;&emsp; B. recipes &emsp;&emsp; C. songs &emsp;&emsp; D. stories
9. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
10. A. hobby &emsp;&emsp; B. memory &emsp;&emsp; C. job &emsp;&emsp; D. gift

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一步是准备所有食材。A 项 step 表示"步骤"；B 项意为"一天"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"主意"。the first step 为做事的常见表达，故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈教我怎么按正确顺序混合它们。A 项意为"房间"；B 项 order 表示"顺序"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"地点"。in the correct order 表示按正确顺序，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要等大约四十分钟。A 项 wait 表示"等待"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"煮"；D 项意为"吃"。烤蛋糕需等待，wait for 为固定搭配，故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房渐渐充满香甜的气味。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"甜的"；D 项意为"酸的"。蛋糕烤制时通常有甜香，sweet smell 符合语境，故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来棒极了！A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"极好的"；D 项意为"简单的"。装饰好的蛋糕用 wonderful 形容，故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对自己做的东西感到自豪。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项 proud 表示"自豪的"；D 项意为"生气的"。feel proud of 表示对……感到自豪，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做蛋糕不仅是做饭，更是和妈妈共度有质量的时光。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"自由的"；C 项 quality time 表示"优质时光"；D 项意为"短的"。quality time 为常用搭配，故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那以后想向妈妈学更多食谱。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项 recipes 表示"食谱"；C 项意为"歌曲"；D 项意为"故事"。学做菜用 learn recipes，故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烹饪能为家庭带来快乐。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项 joy 表示"快乐"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"恐惧"。cooking bring joy 为积极表达，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新年蛋糕对我来说成了一段特别的回忆。A 项意为"爱好"；B 项 memory 表示"回忆"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"礼物"。special memory 表示美好记忆，符合语境，故选 B。
