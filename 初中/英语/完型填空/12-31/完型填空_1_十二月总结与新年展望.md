# 中考英语完形填空：十二月总结与新年展望

## 完形填空原文

December 31st is the last day of the year. My teacher asked us to write a summary of December and our hopes for the New Year. I sat at my desk and began to ___1___.

In December, I took part in the school English song competition and won second place. I also helped organize our class New Year party. However, I sometimes ___2___ too much time on my phone. I hope to use my time more wisely in the coming year.

For the New Year, I have clear goals. First, I want to improve my English by ___3___ to more English songs and watching one English movie each month. Second, I plan to run in the school sports meet in spring. Third, I will try to read at least one book every two weeks. These goals may seem ___4___, but they will help me grow step by step.

My teacher said that looking back helps us understand ourselves. When we think about what we have done, we can see both our progress and our ___5___. When we look ahead, we can set goals and work toward them. I will keep a simple journal in the New Year to record my ___6___ and thoughts.

I am excited about 2025. The New Year brings new chances. I believe that with hard work and ___7___, I can reach my goals. Let's welcome the New Year with a smiling face and make every day ___8___. The last day of December is a good time to say ___9___ to the old year and hello to the new one. I am ready for the joys and ___10___ that the New Year will bring.

## 选项

1. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. read &emsp;&emsp; C. write &emsp;&emsp; D. sleep
2. A. spent &emsp;&emsp; B. saved &emsp;&emsp; C. found &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
3. A. listening &emsp;&emsp; B. hearing &emsp;&emsp; C. speaking &emsp;&emsp; D. singing
4. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. large &emsp;&emsp; D. small
5. A. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; B. secrets &emsp;&emsp; C. dreams &emsp;&emsp; D. fears
6. A. meals &emsp;&emsp; B. hobbies &emsp;&emsp; C. activities &emsp;&emsp; D. games
7. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. laziness
8. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. meaningless &emsp;&emsp; C. count &emsp;&emsp; D. matter
9. A. hello &emsp;&emsp; B. goodbye &emsp;&emsp; C. sorry &emsp;&emsp; D. thanks
10. A. dangers &emsp;&emsp; B. challenges &emsp;&emsp; C. failures &emsp;&emsp; D. fears

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在书桌前开始写。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"读"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"睡觉"。write a summary 表明要写总结，故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我有时在手机上花了太多时间。A 项 spent 表示"花费（时间）"；B 项意为"节省"；C 项意为"发现"；D 项意为"失去"。spend time on 为固定搭配，故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：想通过多听英文歌和提高英语。A 项 listening 表示"听"；B 项 hearing 表示"听见"；C 项意为"说"；D 项意为"唱"。improve English by listening 为常用表达，listen to 为固定搭配，故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些目标看似很小。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"大的"；D 项意为"小的"。后文 step by step 暗示目标不大但有用，small 与之呼应，故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回顾时能看到进步和错误。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"恐惧"。both...and 连接 progress 与 mistakes，反思需看到两方面，故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新年会简单记录每日活动和想法。A 项意为"餐食"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"活动"；D 项意为"游戏"。日记记录 daily activities and thoughts，故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相信通过努力和耐心能实现目标。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"懒惰"。达成目标需 patience 与 hard work，故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：以积极心态迎接新年，让每一天都有意义。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"无意义的"；C 项 make every day count 意为"让每天都有价值"；D 项 matter 语法上也可，但 count 更常用。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：十二月最后一天是告别旧年、迎接新年的好时机。A 项意为"你好"；B 项 say goodbye 意为"告别"；C 项意为"抱歉"；D 项意为"感谢"。与 hello to the new one 对应用 goodbye，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：已准备好迎接新年带来的喜悦与挑战。A 项意为"危险"；B 项意为"挑战"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"恐惧"。joys and challenges 为新年的常见表达，故选 B。
