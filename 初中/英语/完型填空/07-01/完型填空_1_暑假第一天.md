# 中考英语完形填空：暑假第一天

## 完形填空原文

July 1st was the first day of my summer holiday. I woke up very early because I was too ___1___ to sleep. I had been waiting for this day for a long time. No more homework, no more exams — just weeks of freedom ahead.

After breakfast, my mum asked me about my ___2___ for the summer. I told her I wanted to learn to swim. She smiled and said she had already signed me up for swimming lessons at the community pool. I was so happy that I gave her a big ___3___.

In the afternoon, my best friend Tom came over to my house. We sat in the garden and made a list of things we wanted to do during the holiday. We wanted to ride our bikes to the lake, visit the new science museum, and ___4___ a lemonade stand to earn some pocket money. The list grew longer and longer. "This is going to be the best summer ___5___!" Tom said with a big smile.

Later that evening, my whole family had a barbecue in the backyard. My dad was in charge of the grill. He cooked sausages and corn. My little sister helped to ___6___ the table. We sat outside, eating and laughing under the stars. The night air was warm and I felt truly ___7___.

Before going to bed, I wrote in my diary. I wanted to ___8___ every special moment of this summer. I knew that time would pass quickly, so I decided to make the most of each day. I did not want to ___9___ any chances to try new things and create happy memories.

As I turned off the light, I thought to myself, "What a great ___10___ to start the holiday!" I closed my eyes and fell asleep with a smile on my face.

## 选项

1. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. scared
2. A. dreams &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. plans &emsp;&emsp; D. grades
3. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. hug &emsp;&emsp; C. surprise &emsp;&emsp; D. chance
4. A. set up &emsp;&emsp; B. put away &emsp;&emsp; C. pick up &emsp;&emsp; D. give up
5. A. ever &emsp;&emsp; B. already &emsp;&emsp; C. still &emsp;&emsp; D. never
6. A. clean &emsp;&emsp; B. set &emsp;&emsp; C. move &emsp;&emsp; D. draw
7. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. polite
8. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. record &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. share
9. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. have &emsp;&emsp; D. get
10. A. reason &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. way &emsp;&emsp; D. place

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我起得很早，因为我太___了睡不着。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。暑假第一天期待已久，因为兴奋而早起，"excited"最合适。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈问我暑假的___。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"成绩"。暑假刚开始，妈妈询问的是假期安排和计划，"plans"最恰当。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太高兴了，给了她一个大大的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"拥抱"；C 项意为"惊喜"；D 项意为"机会"。因为高兴而拥抱妈妈，"gave her a big hug"是常见搭配。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们想___一个柠檬水摊来赚零花钱。A 项意为"搭建、开设"；B 项意为"收起来"；C 项意为"捡起"；D 项意为"放弃"。"set up a stand"意为"摆一个摊位"，符合语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这将是___最棒的夏天！A 项意为"曾经、有史以来"；B 项意为"已经"；C 项意为"仍然"；D 项意为"从不"。"the best summer ever"意为"有史以来最好的夏天"，是常见的口语表达。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的小妹妹帮忙___桌子。A 项意为"打扫"；B 项意为"摆放"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"画"。"set the table"是固定搭配，意为"摆餐具、布置餐桌"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：夜晚的空气温暖，我感到真正的___。A 项意为"勇敢的"；B 项意为"放松的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"有礼貌的"。假期第一天，和家人在星空下吃烧烤，心情应是放松愉悦的，"relaxed"最贴切。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想___这个夏天每一个特别的时刻。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记录"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"分享"。前文提到写日记，目的是记录，"record"符合。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不想___任何尝试新事物和创造美好回忆的机会。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"拿"；C 项意为"有"；D 项意为"得到"。"miss chances"意为"错过机会"，是常见搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：多么棒的一个___来开始假期呀！A 项意为"原因"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"方式"；D 项意为"地方"。"What a great way to start"意为"多棒的开始方式"，是地道表达。故选 C。
