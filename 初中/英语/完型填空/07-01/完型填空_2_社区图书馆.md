# 中考英语完形填空：社区图书馆

## 完形填空原文

There is a small library in our community. It is not as big as the city library, but it is a very ___1___ place for everyone in the neighbourhood. My grandmother often takes me there on weekends.

The library has three rooms. The first room is for children. It has colourful bookshelves, soft cushions on the floor, and a reading corner with a big bear toy. Little kids love to sit there and ___2___ picture books. The second room is the main reading room for adults. It is always very quiet because people go there to study or read newspapers. The third room is a ___3___ room where the library holds different events, such as story-telling hours and book clubs.

Last Saturday, something special happened. The library invited a local writer to give a ___4___. His name was Mr Chen. He wrote children's stories and was quite famous in our city. When he arrived, the room was already full of people. He told us how he ___5___ writing stories. "When I was a boy, I loved reading," he said. "One day, I thought, 'Why not write my own stories?' So I picked up a pen and started." His words were simple but very ___6___.

After his talk, we could ask him questions. A girl asked him what the most ___7___ part of writing was. He said, "Starting is easy. The hard part is not giving up when things get difficult." I thought about this carefully. His advice could be used not only in writing but also in ___8___ — in studying, in sports, in anything we do.

Before we left, Mr Chen signed books for us. I ___9___ got his signature on my favourite story book. It was a wonderful afternoon. The library is more than just a place to borrow books. It is a place where people come together, learn new things, and feel ___10___ to a community.

## 选项

1. A. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; B. special &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
2. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; D. collect
3. A. meeting &emsp;&emsp; B. waiting &emsp;&emsp; C. dining &emsp;&emsp; D. living
4. A. concert &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. talk &emsp;&emsp; D. test
5. A. stopped &emsp;&emsp; B. avoided &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. started
6. A. funny &emsp;&emsp; B. inspiring &emsp;&emsp; C. confusing &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
7. A. interesting &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. popular &emsp;&emsp; D. common
8. A. everything &emsp;&emsp; B. nothing &emsp;&emsp; C. something &emsp;&emsp; D. anything
9. A. hardly &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. proudly &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
10. A. close &emsp;&emsp; B. similar &emsp;&emsp; C. polite &emsp;&emsp; D. connected

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它不如市图书馆大，但对社区每个人来说是一个非常___的地方。A 项意为"危险的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。社区图书馆虽小但对居民意义非凡，"special"最合适。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小朋友们喜欢坐在那儿___图画书。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"欣赏、享受"；D 项意为"收集"。在阅读角看图画书，"enjoy picture books"意为"享受图画书"，符合语境。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第三个房间是___室，图书馆在那里举办不同活动。A 项意为"会议"；B 项意为"等候"；C 项意为"用餐"；D 项意为"起居"。举办故事会和读书会等活动的房间是"活动/会议室"，"meeting room"最合适。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书馆邀请了一位本地作家来做一场___。A 项意为"音乐会"；B 项意为"课"；C 项意为"讲座、演讲"；D 项意为"考试"。作家来分享经历，是一场"talk"（讲座）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他告诉我们他是如何___写故事的。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"开始"。后文他说"我拿起笔开始写"，是讲述创作的起点，"started"正确。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话很简单但非常___。A 项意为"搞笑的"；B 项意为"鼓舞人心的"；C 项意为"令人困惑的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。作家鼓励大家坚持创作，话语虽简单却有激励作用，"inspiring"最恰当。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个女孩问他写作中最___的部分是什么。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"流行的"；D 项意为"常见的"。后文回答"困难的部分是不放弃"，与问题中"最困难的"呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的建议不仅适用于写作，也适用于___——学习、运动和我们做的一切事情。A 项意为"所有事情"；B 项意为"什么都没有"；C 项意为"某些事情"；D 项意为"任何事情"。后文列举了学习、运动等多方面，说明适用于"一切事情"，"everything"最全面。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___地拿到了他在我最喜欢的故事书上的签名。A 项意为"几乎不"；B 项意为"从不"；C 项意为"自豪地"；D 项意为"悲伤地"。得到喜欢的作家签名，心情是自豪开心的，"proudly"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书馆是人们聚在一起、学习新事物、感到与社区___的地方。A 项意为"亲密的"；B 项意为"相似的"；C 项意为"有礼貌的"；D 项意为"有联系的"。"feel connected to"意为"感到与……相连、有归属感"，符合社区情感。故选 D。
