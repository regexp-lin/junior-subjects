# 中考英语完形填空：修自行车的爷爷

## 完形填空原文

For as long as anyone could remember, an old man named Grandpa Wang had set up his small bicycle repair stand at the corner of our street. He was a quiet man with kind eyes and hands covered in dark oil ___1___. Every day from morning to evening, he fixed flat tires, adjusted brakes, and tightened loose chains.

My friends and I ___2___ rode our bikes past his stand on our way to school. We never paid much attention to him. He was just part of the street, like the old tree or the mailbox. But one day, my bike chain broke right in front of him. I ___3___ off and felt anxious because I had no money with me.

Grandpa Wang saw me and smiled. "Bring it here, young man," he said. I hesitated, but he ___4___ my bike gently and started working on it. Within five minutes, the chain was fixed. I touched my empty pockets and felt ___5___. "I'm sorry, Grandpa Wang. I don't have any money to pay you today," I mumbled.

He laughed a warm, deep laugh. "Don't worry about it," he said, wiping his hands on a rag. "Just do well in school. That's enough ___6___ for an old man like me." 

I thanked him and rode away, feeling deeply moved. A few weeks later, the neighborhood committee announced that they were going to clear the street corner, which meant Grandpa Wang had to ___7___. When people heard this, something amazing happened. Neighbors started pouring in with their bikes — even ones that didn't need ___8___. Some brought him hot tea, others brought homemade food.

We all suddenly realized how much we had taken his quiet presence for ___9___. He wasn't just a repairman; he was the unspoken guardian of our neighborhood's daily trips. On his last day, I went to say goodbye and gave him a drawing of his stand. He looked at it for a long time, his eyes ___10___ with tears. "This," he said softly, "is the best payment I could ever receive."

1. A. spots &emsp;&emsp; B. paintings &emsp;&emsp; C. marks &emsp;&emsp; D. stars
2. A. seldom &emsp;&emsp; B. often &emsp;&emsp; C. never &emsp;&emsp; D. suddenly
3. A. fell &emsp;&emsp; B. took &emsp;&emsp; C. jumped &emsp;&emsp; D. turned
4. A. washed &emsp;&emsp; B. accepted &emsp;&emsp; C. sold &emsp;&emsp; D. painted
5. A. relieved &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. embarrassed &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
6. A. payment &emsp;&emsp; B. punishment &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. change
7. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. complain &emsp;&emsp; C. stay &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
8. A. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; B. fixing &emsp;&emsp; C. returning &emsp;&emsp; D. locking
9. A. true &emsp;&emsp; B. real &emsp;&emsp; C. granted &emsp;&emsp; D. sure
10. A. shining &emsp;&emsp; B. covering &emsp;&emsp; C. flying &emsp;&emsp; D. sleeping

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他有一双覆满黑色油___的手。A 项意为"斑点"；B 项意为"绘画"；C 项意为"痕迹/污迹"；D 项意为"星星"。修自行车常年和机油打交道，手上自然沾满了洗不掉的深色油"污渍/痕迹"（oil marks）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和我的朋友们在上学路上___骑车经过他的摊位。A 项意为"很少"；B 项意为"经常"；C 项意为"从不"；D 项意为"突然"。因为是"on our way to school"（在上学的路上），所以路线固定，自然是"经常"经过那里。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___下车，感到很焦虑，因为我没带钱。A 项意为"落下"；B 项意为"带走"；C 项意为"跳（下）"；D 项意为"转弯"。链条突然断了，骑车的人第一反应是赶紧"跳下"车（jumped off）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有些犹豫，他却温柔地___了我的车，开始修理。A 项意为"洗"；B 项意为"接受/接过"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"涂漆"。"I hesitated"（我犹豫）是因为没带钱，但王爷爷不管这些，直接"接过"了车开始修（accepted my bike）。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我摸了摸空空的口袋，感到很___。A 项意为"如释重负的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"尴尬的/难堪的"；D 项意为"生气的"。修好了车要付钱的时候却发现没带钱，自然会感到"尴尬"（embarrassed）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好好学习，这对我这样的老人来说就是足够的___了。A 项意为"报酬/付款"；B 项意为"惩罚"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"零钱"。前文作者为无法"pay"（付钱）而道歉，爷爷安慰他说好好学习就是最好的"报酬/付款"（payment）。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这意味着王爷爷必须得___了。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"留下"；D 项意为"离开"。前文说街道办要清理街角（clear the street corner），那么占道摆摊的王爷爷自然就必须得"离开"（leave）。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：邻居们涌来推着自行车——甚至是不需要___的。A 项意为"清洗"；B 项意为"修理"；C 项意为"归还"；D 项意为"锁"。大家知道王爷爷要走了，纷纷推车来光顾，甚至有些车根本不需要"修理"（fixing），以此来表达不舍和支持。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 词组辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们突然意识到，我们是多么把他安静的存在视为___。A 项意为"真实的"；B 项意为"真的"；C 项意为"理所当然"；D 项意为"确信的"。"take something for granted"（把……视为理所当然）是固定搭配。王爷爷一直默默在街角，大家习以为常却忽视了他的价值。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词兼现在分词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他看了很久，眼睛里___着泪光。A 项意为"闪烁/闪亮"；B 项意为"覆盖"；C 项意为"飞翔"；D 项意为"睡觉"。收到"我"送他的画，他非常感动，眼里有泪水当然是"闪烁"着泪光（eyes shining with tears）。故选 A。
