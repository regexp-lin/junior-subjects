# 中考英语完形填空：一次失败的演出

## 完形填空原文

The school talent show was the most awaited event of the year. Liu Peng and his two best friends had formed a band and practiced for two months to perform a rock song. They called themselves "The Dreamers."

When the day finally arrived, the auditorium was full of students and teachers. Liu Peng, as the lead singer and guitarist, felt a mix of excitement and ___1___. He had visualized this moment a hundred times — the stage lights, the cheering crowd, and the perfect music.

Finally, it was their turn. They walked onto the stage like rock stars. The crowd clapped ___2___. Liu Peng plugged in his guitar, struck the first powerful chord, and ready to sing. But instead of music, a loud, sharp ___3___ came from the speakers. It sounded like an angry animal.

Everyone covered their ___4___. Liu Peng froze in shock. His bandmates looked at him, confused. The sound engineer backstage quickly waved his hands, signaling that the amplifier had completely ___5___ down. Without the amplifier, an electric guitar makes almost no sound.

For a moment, there was terrible silence in the hall. Liu Peng felt his face turning entirely red. He wanted to drop his guitar and run away from the ___6___. It was a complete disaster. Two months of hard work, only to end in silence before it even began.

Then, someone in the front row started clapping slowly. It was Mr. Gao, their history teacher. Soon, a few more students joined in. Then the whole auditorium was clapping, not for the music, but for their ___7___.

"Sing it anyway!" someone shouted from the back.

Liu Peng looked at his friends. They nodded. He took a deep breath, stepped away from the broken microphone, and strummed his unamplified guitar as hard as he could. Then, he started to sing at the top of his ___8___. His friends joined in on the drums and bass.

It wasn't a perfect rock concert, and the music was barely loud enough to fill the room. But the raw energy and passion were undeniable. The audience started clapping ___9___ with the beat. 

When they finished, the crowd gave them a standing ovation. As they walked off the stage, Liu Peng realized something important: Success isn't about everything going perfectly; it's about not giving up when things go ___10___.

1. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. nervousness &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
2. A. blindly &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. wildly &emsp;&emsp; D. coldly
3. A. song &emsp;&emsp; B. word &emsp;&emsp; C. bell &emsp;&emsp; D. noise
4. A. eyes &emsp;&emsp; B. ears &emsp;&emsp; C. mouths &emsp;&emsp; D. faces
5. A. broken &emsp;&emsp; B. turned &emsp;&emsp; C. sat &emsp;&emsp; D. written
6. A. classroom &emsp;&emsp; B. playground &emsp;&emsp; C. library &emsp;&emsp; D. stage

8. A. lungs &emsp;&emsp; B. hands &emsp;&emsp; C. feet &emsp;&emsp; D. mind
9. A. against &emsp;&emsp; B. along &emsp;&emsp; C. deeply &emsp;&emsp; D. outside

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：作为主唱和吉他手，刘鹏感到夹杂着兴奋和___的心情。A 项意为"喜悦"；B 项意为"紧张"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"愤怒"。登台演出前除了兴奋，最常见的情绪自然是"紧张"（nervousness）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人群___地鼓掌。A 项意为"盲目地"；B 项意为"安静地"；C 项意为"狂热地"；D 项意为"冷冰冰地"。前文说"The school talent show was the most awaited event"（学校才艺秀是最受期待的活动），当他们像摇滚明星一样上场时，大家期待值拉满，所以是"狂热地/热情地"（wildly）鼓掌。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但音箱里传来的不是音乐，而是一声巨大尖锐的___。A 项意为"歌曲"；B 项意为"单词"；C 项意为"铃声"；D 项意为"噪音"。演出出了故障，吉他发出的声音像生气的动物，当然是刺耳的"噪音"（noise）。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：所有人都捂住了他们的___。A 项意为"眼睛"；B 项意为"耳朵"；C 项意为"嘴巴"；D 项意为"脸"。因为扩音器发出了极其尖锐刺耳的噪音，听众第一反应肯定是捂住"耳朵"（ears）。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：扩音设备完全___了。A 项 break down 意为"出故障/坏掉"；B 项 turn down 意为"拒绝/调小"；C 项 sit down 意为"坐下"；D 项 write down 意为"写下"。设备发不出声音了，说明是设备"坏掉/发生故障"了（broken down）。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他真想扔下吉他逃离___。A 项意为"教室"；B 项意为"操场"；C 项意为"图书馆"；D 项意为"舞台"。此时他们正在演出，出现这种堪称灾难的故障，他觉得丢脸，想逃离的地方自然是"舞台"（stage）。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：全场开始鼓掌，不是为了音乐，而是为了他们的___。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"美丽"。乐队遇到严重故障却仍然坚持站在台上，观众的掌声是为了鼓励他们面临尴尬时的"勇气"（courage）。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他开始扯着___唱歌。A 项意为"肺"；B 项意为"手"；C 项意为"脚"；D 项意为"大脑"。"at the top of one's lungs"（扯着嗓子/尽最大声）是固定搭配。因为麦克风和扩音器坏了，他只能用尽全力大声吼叫着演唱。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：观众开始___节拍拍手。A 项 against 意为"反对"；B 项 along (with) 意为"伴随/一起"；C 项 deeply 意为"深深地"；D 项 outside 意为"在外面"。"clap along with the beat"（跟着节拍一起拍手）是音乐演唱会中观众参与的常见互动。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定词组
**【解析】** 本句意为：成功不是一切都完美；而是当事情出___时永不放弃。A 项意为"对的"；B 项意为"错的"；C 项意为"快的"；D 项意为"慢的"。"go wrong"（出差错/不顺）是固定搭配。演出设备坏了就是一种典型的"go wrong"，但他们坚持完成了演出，因此得出了这样的感悟。故选 B。
