# 中考英语完形填空：读书分享

## 完形填空原文

Every Friday afternoon, our class holds a "Book Sharing" activity. Each student takes turns to introduce a book they have read. Last week, it was my ___1___. I had chosen a book about a boy who discovered a magical garden. I was a bit ___2___ at first because I had never spoken in front of the whole class before. But when I started talking about the story, I forgot my nervousness. I described how the boy found the garden and made friends with the animals there. My classmates listened ___3___ and asked me many questions. It felt great to share something I loved with others.

The activity has changed the way I ___4___ reading. Before, I used to read quickly and seldom thought about the stories deeply. Now I pay more attention to the characters and their feelings. When I know I will share a book, I read it more ___5___. I take notes and prepare what I want to say. This habit has made my reading experience much richer.

What I enjoy most is hearing other students' ___6___. Everyone has different tastes. Some like adventure stories; others prefer science books or biographies. Through their sharing, I have discovered many good books that I might never have picked up by myself. Our teacher says that sharing books is like ___7___ a window to different worlds. I agree. Every book opens a new ___8___ of imagination and knowledge.

I hope our "Book Sharing" activity will ___9___ for a long time. It has brought our class closer together. We learn from each other and grow through the stories we share. Reading is no longer just a personal hobby — it has become a way for us to ___10___ and connect with one another.

## 选项

1. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. turn &emsp;&emsp; C. day &emsp;&emsp; D. chance
2. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
3. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
4. A. think of &emsp;&emsp; B. look at &emsp;&emsp; C. talk about &emsp;&emsp; D. depend on
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. fast &emsp;&emsp; C. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
6. A. opinions &emsp;&emsp; B. suggestions &emsp;&emsp; C. answers &emsp;&emsp; D. reports
7. A. opening &emsp;&emsp; B. closing &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaning &emsp;&emsp; D. painting
8. A. room &emsp;&emsp; B. door &emsp;&emsp; C. world &emsp;&emsp; D. idea
9. A. last &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. keep &emsp;&emsp; D. continue
10. A. compete &emsp;&emsp; B. discuss &emsp;&emsp; C. communicate &emsp;&emsp; D. argue

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：上周轮到我___。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"轮次"；C 项意为"日子"；D 项意为"机会"。"it was my turn"为固定搭配，意为"轮到我"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我有点___，因为我从未在全班面前讲过话。A 项意为"高兴的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。后文"forgot my nervousness"与前文呼应，"nervous"符合首次上台的情境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们___地听，并问了我很多问题。A 项意为"大声地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"认真地"；D 项意为"安静地"。"carefully"与"asked many questions"相呼应，表示认真倾听、积极互动。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个活动改变了我对阅读的___方式。A 项意为"看待、思考"；B 项意为"看"；C 项意为"谈论"；D 项意为"依赖"。"think of"意为"对……的看法、思考方式"，与"changed"搭配表示改变对阅读的态度。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我知道要分享一本书时，我会更___地读它。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快的"；C 项意为"粗心地"；D 项意为"快速地"。后文"take notes and prepare"说明阅读更仔细、深入，"slowly"强调精读。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我最喜欢的是听其他同学的___。A 项意为"观点、见解"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"答案"；D 项意为"报告"。"opinions"与"different tastes"、每个人分享不同书籍相呼应，表示各自的看法与喜好。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师常说，分享书籍就像___一扇通往不同世界的窗。A 项意为"打开"；B 项意为"关上"；C 项意为"清洁"；D 项意为"绘画"。"opening a window"为常用比喻，意为"打开视野、开拓眼界"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每本书都打开一个充满想象和知识的新___。A 项意为"房间"；B 项意为"门"；C 项意为"世界"；D 项意为"想法"。"world"与"different worlds"、前文比喻相呼应，强调书籍带来新世界。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望我们的"读书分享"活动能___很久。A 项意为"持续"（多作不及物）；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"继续"。"continue"与"for a long time"搭配，意为"持续进行"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阅读不再只是个人爱好，它已成为我们___和彼此连接的方式。A 项意为"竞争"；B 项意为"讨论"；C 项意为"交流"；D 项意为"争论"。"communicate"与"connect with one another"相呼应，强调交流与互动。故选 C。
