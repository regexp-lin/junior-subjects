# 中考英语完形填空：养小仓鼠

## 完形填空原文

For my fourteenth birthday, my parents gave me a hamster. I had been asking for a pet for a long time. They chose a hamster because it is small and easy to ___1___. I named her Cookie because of her golden brown colour.

At first, Cookie was shy and hid in her nest whenever I came near. I learned that hamsters are nocturnal — they sleep during the day and are ___2___ at night. So I started to play with her in the evening. I would put my hand slowly into the cage and wait for her to come out. After a week, she began to climb onto my hand. Her tiny paws tickled my palm. I felt so happy that she ___3___ me.

Taking care of Cookie taught me responsibility. I had to clean her cage every week and refill her water bottle every day. Her favourite food was sunflower seeds and fresh vegetables. I gave her a small piece of carrot or cucumber every other day. I also made sure she had enough ___4___ to run. Hamsters need exercise, so I bought her a running wheel. She would run on it for hours at night. The wheel sometimes made a little noise, but I got used to it.

One morning I noticed that Cookie had stored a lot of food in her cheeks. Her face looked very ___5___. I was worried and searched online. I learned that hamsters have special pouches in their cheeks to carry food. They often store food to eat later. It was completely normal! I felt relieved.

Cookie has been with me for six months now. She recognises my voice and comes to the cage door when I call her name. Sometimes I let her run around in a safe area in my room. I have to watch her carefully because she is ___6___ and can squeeze through small gaps. She has become an important part of our family. My little brother loves to watch her too.

Having a pet is a big ___7___. I cannot go on long trips without making arrangements for Cookie. But the joy she brings is worth it. When I feel stressed about homework, watching Cookie run on her wheel or nibble on a treat helps me relax. She has taught me about patience, care, and the simple ___8___ of having a little friend. I hope Cookie will stay healthy and ___9___ for a long time. Every pet owner should remember: our animals ___10___ on us for everything. We must treat them with love and responsibility.

## 选项

1. A. catch &emsp;&emsp; B. care for &emsp;&emsp; C. find &emsp;&emsp; D. sell
2. A. sleepy &emsp;&emsp; B. active &emsp;&emsp; C. hungry &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
3. A. trusted &emsp;&emsp; B. bit &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
4. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. water &emsp;&emsp; C. space &emsp;&emsp; D. light
5. A. thin &emsp;&emsp; B. fat &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty

7. A. game &emsp;&emsp; B. gift &emsp;&emsp; C. commitment &emsp;&emsp; D. hobby
8. A. difficulty &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. cost
9. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. sad &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. hungry

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们选仓鼠因为它小而且容易___。A 项意为"抓"；B 项意为"照料"；C 项意为"找"；D 项意为"卖"。"easy to care for"意为"容易照料"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们白天睡觉，晚上___。A 项意为"困的"；B 项意为"活跃的"；C 项意为"饿的"；D 项意为"生气的"。夜行性动物晚上活跃，"active"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她开始爬上我的手，我很高兴她___我。A 项意为"信任"；B 项意为"咬"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"忘记"。愿意爬上手说明信任，"trusted"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我确保她有足够的___跑步。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"水"；C 项意为"空间"；D 项意为"光"。仓鼠需要运动空间，"space"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把食物存在颊囊里，脸看起来非常___。A 项意为"瘦的"；B 项意为"鼓的/胖的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"脏的"。颊囊存食后鼓胀，"fat"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她很___能钻进小缝隙。A 项意为"慢的"；B 项意为"小的"；C 项意为"重的"；D 项意为"懒的"。仓鼠体小能钻缝，"tiny"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：养宠物是很大的___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"承诺/责任"；D 项意为"爱好"。养宠物意味着长期责任，"commitment"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教会我耐心、关怀和拥有小伙伴的简单___。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"成本"。宠物带来快乐，"joy"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望Cookie能健康___地活很久。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"悲伤的"；C 项意为"快乐的"；D 项意为"饿的"。"happy"祝愿宠物快乐生活。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：宠物的一切都___我们。A 项意为"依靠"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"工作"。"depend on"意为"依赖"。故选 A。
