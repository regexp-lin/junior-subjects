# 中考英语完形填空：参加英语角

## 完形填空原文

Every Saturday afternoon, there is an English Corner in the city square. Anyone who wants to practise speaking English can come. I had heard about it many times but never had the ___1___ to go. Last week, my English teacher encouraged me to try. She said, "The only way to improve speaking is to speak."

When I arrived, about twenty people were already there. Some were students like me, and some were office workers. A friendly man in his thirties came over and said, "Hi! What's your name?" I was so ___2___ that I could hardly speak. My heart was beating fast. But I took a deep breath and said, "My name is Zhang Wei. I'm a junior high student."

We started with simple topics: the weather, our hobbies, and our favourite foods. I made many mistakes. Sometimes I used the wrong tense; sometimes I could not find the right ___3___. But nobody laughed at me. They all nodded and encouraged me to continue. One lady even said, "Your English is better than you think. Don't be afraid to make ___4___."

As I relaxed, I found it easier to ___5___. I talked about my summer holiday and my favourite basketball team. A university student asked me questions about my school life. We chatted for almost half an hour. I realised that I knew more English than I thought — I just needed the chance to ___6___ it.

When it was time to leave, the organiser gave me a small card. It said, "See you next Saturday!" I have been back every week since then. My ___7___ has improved a lot. I used to be afraid of English class, but now I even raise my hand to answer questions. My teacher noticed the change and praised me.

English Corner taught me that language is for ___8___. We learn grammar and vocabulary not just to pass exams, but to connect with people. The important thing is to keep trying. I have made several friends at English Corner. We sometimes meet for coffee and practise English together. I am ___9___ that I finally took that first step. I will continue to join the English Corner and hope to help ___10___ like me who are afraid to speak.

## 选项

1. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. idea
2. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
3. A. book &emsp;&emsp; B. word &emsp;&emsp; C. teacher &emsp;&emsp; D. answer
4. A. friends &emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; C. plans &emsp;&emsp; D. notes
5. A. listen &emsp;&emsp; B. write &emsp;&emsp; C. speak &emsp;&emsp; D. read
6. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. use &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. hide
7. A. writing &emsp;&emsp; B. confidence &emsp;&emsp; C. grammar &emsp;&emsp; D. vocabulary
8. A. communication &emsp;&emsp; B. translation &emsp;&emsp; C. examination &emsp;&emsp; D. memorisation
9. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. glad &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
10. A. others &emsp;&emsp; B. teachers &emsp;&emsp; C. parents &emsp;&emsp; D. enemies

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我多次听说但一直没有___去。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"主意"。不敢去参加需要勇气，"courage"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，几乎说不出话。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。第一次参加紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时找不到正确的___。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"词"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"答案"。表达时找词，"word"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要害怕犯___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"笔记"。"make mistakes"意为"犯错误"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：放松后我发现更容易___了。A 项意为"听"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"说"；D 项意为"读"。英语角练习口语，"speak"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我只需要有机会___它。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"使用"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"隐藏"。使用所学英语，"use"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的___提高了很多。A 项意为"写作"；B 项意为"自信"；C 项意为"语法"；D 项意为"词汇"。敢于举手回答问题体现自信提升，"confidence"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：语言是用来___的。A 项意为"交流"；B 项意为"翻译"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"记忆"。语言的首要目的是交流，"communication"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___终于迈出了第一步。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"高兴的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"疲劳的"。迈出第一步感到高兴，"glad"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望帮助像我一样不敢说的___。A 项意为"其他人"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"父母"；D 项意为"敌人"。"others"泛指其他人。故选 A。
