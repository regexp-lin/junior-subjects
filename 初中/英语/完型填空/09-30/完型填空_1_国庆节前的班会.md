# 中考英语完形填空：国庆节前的班会

## 完形填空原文

The day before National Day, our class held a special class meeting. Our head teacher, Miss Zhang, said she wanted to talk about the meaning of National Day and what we could do as students to show our ___1___ for our country.

The meeting started with a short video about the history of China. We learned about the difficult journey of our nation and how our ancestors had ___2___ for a better life. Many heroes had sacrificed everything for our country's independence and prosperity. Watching the video, I felt a strong sense of pride.

Then Miss Zhang asked us to share our thoughts. "What does our country mean to you?" she asked. One classmate said that China meant home — a place where we belonged. Another said it meant ___3___ — the rich culture and long history that we should be proud of. I said that our country meant hope — the chance to grow and achieve our dreams in a peaceful and developing nation.

Miss Zhang smiled and said we had all given wonderful answers. She then told us that loving our country didn't always mean doing something big. We could start with small things: studying hard, being ___4___ to others, following rules, and protecting the environment. "Every good thing you do makes our country a little ___5___," she said.

We also discussed our plans for the National Day holiday. Some students would travel with their families. Others would stay at home and rest. I planned to visit a museum to learn more about our country's history. Miss Zhang said that was a good idea and ___6___ us to make good use of the holiday.

At the end of the meeting, we sang the national anthem together. Standing there, singing with my classmates, I felt a deep connection to our country. The ___7___ made me think about the responsibility we all share as Chinese citizens.

That class meeting was meaningful. It reminded me that we should never take our peaceful life for ___8___. We should be grateful and work hard to contribute to our country's future. As students, the best way to love our country is to study well and become useful ___9___ of society when we grow up. I will ___10___ this lesson in mind and do my part for our country.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她想谈谈国庆的意义以及我们作为学生如何表达对祖国的热爱。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"热爱"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"愤怒"。show love for country 意为"表达对祖国的热爱"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们了解了民族的艰难历程，先辈们如何为美好生活而奋斗。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"奋斗"；C 项意为"玩耍"；D 项意为"休息"。fought for 意为"为……而奋斗"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：另一位同学说祖国意味着文化——我们应为之骄傲的丰富文化和悠久历史。A 项意为"文化"；B 项意为"时尚"；C 项意为"运动"；D 项意为"食物"。后文 rich culture 提示选 culture。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们可以从小事做起：努力学习、善待他人、遵守规则、保护环境。A 项意为"粗鲁的"；B 项意为"善良的"；C 项意为"严格的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。being kind to others 意为"善待他人"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每件好事都让我们的国家更好一点。A 项意为"更糟"；B 项意为"更穷"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更小"。好事让国家 better。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：张老师说这是个好主意，鼓励我们好好利用假期。A 项意为"允许"；B 项意为"鼓励"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"阻止"。encourage sb to do 意为"鼓励某人做"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻让我思考作为中国公民我们共同承担的责任。A 项意为"瞬间、时刻"；B 项意为"会议"；C 项意为"歌曲"；D 项意为"视频"。唱歌时的 moment。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：它提醒我们不应把和平生活视为理所当然。A 项意为"免费"；B 项意为"理所当然"；C 项意为"乐趣"；D 项意为"容易"。take ... for granted 意为"视……为理所当然"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：作为学生，爱国的最好方式是好好学习，长大后成为社会的有用成员。A 项意为"领导者"；B 项意为"成员"；C 项意为"访客"；D 项意为"陌生人"。members of society 意为"社会成员"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会牢记这节课，为国家尽自己的一份力。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"保持"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"错过"。keep ... in mind 意为"牢记"。故选 B。
