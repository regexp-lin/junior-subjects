# 中考英语完形填空：九月总结与展望

## 完形填空原文

September has come to an end. It is the last day of the month, and I am sitting at my desk, thinking about what I have experienced and learned during the past four weeks.

September was a busy and ___1___ month for me. School started at the beginning of the month. I met my new classmates and teachers. At first, I felt a little nervous because everything was new. But soon I made friends and began to enjoy school life again. I joined the English club and the basketball team. Both have brought me a lot of ___2___.

In terms of study, I made some progress. My maths improved a bit, thanks to my teacher's patient explanations. However, I still need to work harder on English. I sometimes forget new vocabulary and make grammar ___3___. I have decided to spend more time reading English every day from October. "Practice makes perfect," as the saying goes.

I also took part in several meaningful activities. I visited the elderly in the community, helped my mother tidy up at home, and took part in the school art performance. These experiences taught me the ___4___ of helping others and staying active. I learned that life is not only about exams and grades — it is also about being a good person and ___5___ to the community.

Looking back at September, I have both successes and regrets. I am proud that I tried new things and didn't give up when things were difficult. But I also ___6___ that I wasted some time on games and social media. I could have used that time for reading or exercise.

Looking forward to October, I have set some goals. First, I want to improve my English by reading at least one short passage every day. Second, I want to keep ___7___ basketball to stay healthy. Third, I want to help at least one person each week, whether it is a classmate, a family member, or a stranger. Small acts of kindness can make a big ___8___.

September is over, but a new month is beginning. I hope that in October I can do better and become a ___9___ version of myself. Every day is a new chance to learn and grow. I will try my best to make the most of ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. tiring &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
2. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
3. A. progress &emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; C. plans &emsp;&emsp; D. friends
4. A. difficulty &emsp;&emsp; B. importance &emsp;&emsp; C. cost &emsp;&emsp; D. speed
5. A. replying &emsp;&emsp; B. contributing &emsp;&emsp; C. comparing &emsp;&emsp; D. returning
6. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. regret &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoy
7. A. watching &emsp;&emsp; B. playing &emsp;&emsp; C. avoiding &emsp;&emsp; D. buying
8. A. decision &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
9. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. slower
10. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. her &emsp;&emsp; D. him

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：九月对我来说是忙碌而有意义的一个月。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"疲劳的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"空的"。后文提到收获，meaningful 正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两个社团都给我带来了很多快乐。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"恐惧"。参加社团带来 joy。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有时会忘记新词汇，犯语法错误。A 项意为"进步"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"朋友"。make mistakes 意为"犯错误"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些经历教会了我帮助他人和保持积极的重要性。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"重要性"；C 项意为"成本"；D 项意为"速度"。the importance of 意为"……的重要性"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生活不仅是考试和分数，也是做个好人、为社会做贡献。A 项意为"回复"；B 项意为"贡献"；C 项意为"比较"；D 项意为"归还"。contributing to the community 意为"为社会做贡献"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我也后悔在一些游戏和社交媒体上浪费了时间。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"后悔"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"享受"。浪费时间会 regret。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想继续打篮球保持健康。A 项意为"观看"；B 项意为"打（球）"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"买"。keep playing 意为"继续打"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的善举能产生很大影响。A 项意为"决定"；B 项意为"不同、影响"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"错误"。make a big difference 意为"产生很大影响"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望十月我能做得更好，成为更好的自己。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更快"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更慢"。a better version 意为"更好的版本"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会尽力充分利用它（每一天）。A 项意为"它"；B 项意为"它们"；C 项意为"她"；D 项意为"他"。指代 every day，用 it。故选 A。
