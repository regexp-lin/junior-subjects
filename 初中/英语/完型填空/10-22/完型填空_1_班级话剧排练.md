# 中考英语完形填空：班级话剧排练

## 完形填空原文

Our school drama festival was only two weeks away. Our class chose to perform a short play called "The Lost Key." I was ___1___ to play the main character. We had our first rehearsal in the school hall last Wednesday.

At the beginning, nothing went well. Some students forgot their ___2___ and others spoke too fast. Our teacher, Ms. Chen, asked us to read the script again and again. "Don't rush," she said. "Take your ___3___ and speak clearly." We practiced the same scene many times until everyone got it right.

I had a lot of lines to remember. Every night before sleeping, I would ___4___ them in my bedroom. My mom sometimes helped me by reading the other parts. One day I made a big mistake on stage — I said the wrong line at the wrong moment. Everyone laughed, but I felt so ___5___. Ms. Chen comforted me, "Mistakes help us learn. Try again."

The day before the show, we had a full dress rehearsal. We wore our costumes and used real ___6___. This time everything went smoothly. Ms. Chen smiled and said, "You've made great progress. I'm ___7___ of all of you."

On the performance day, my heart was beating fast. But when the music started, I ___8___ all my nervousness and became the character. The audience cheered when we finished. We didn't get the first prize, but we learned that teamwork and hard work ___9___ more than winning. That experience of preparing for the play together will always ___10___ in my memory.

1. A. chosen &emsp;&emsp; B. allowed &emsp;&emsp; C. refused &emsp;&emsp; D. invited
2. A. lines &emsp;&emsp; B. books &emsp;&emsp; C. bags &emsp;&emsp; D. clothes
3. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. chance &emsp;&emsp; D. way
4. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. copy &emsp;&emsp; C. check &emsp;&emsp; D. repeat
5. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. embarrassed &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. excited
6. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. tools &emsp;&emsp; C. props &emsp;&emsp; D. gifts
7. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. sorry
8. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. remembered &emsp;&emsp; C. found &emsp;&emsp; D. kept
9. A. cost &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. need &emsp;&emsp; D. mean
10. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. pass &emsp;&emsp; C. stay &emsp;&emsp; D. lose

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被选中扮演主要角色。A 项意为"被选中"；B 项意为"被允许"；C 项意为"被拒绝"；D 项意为"被邀请"。句中需表达"被选来演"之意，chosen 符合语境。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些学生忘记了台词。A 项意为"台词"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"包"；D 项意为"衣服"。话剧排练中忘记的是 lines（台词）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：别着急，慢慢来，说清楚。A 项意为"轮流"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"机会"；D 项意为"方式"。take your time 为固定搭配，意为"慢慢来、别着急"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每晚睡前，我都在卧室反复背诵台词。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"抄写"；C 项意为"检查"；D 项意为"重复；背诵"。记台词用 repeat 表示反复练习、背诵。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都笑了，我感到很尴尬。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"尴尬的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"兴奋的"。台上说错台词、被人笑，应感到 embarrassed。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们穿着戏服，使用真正的道具。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"道具"；D 项意为"礼物"。话剧表演中的 props 指舞台道具。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师微笑着说："你们进步很大，我为你们所有人感到骄傲。"A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"疲惫的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。be proud of 意为"以……为傲"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：音乐一响起，我忘记了所有紧张，融入了角色。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"发现"；D 项意为"保持"。forget one's nervousness 指放下紧张、投入表演。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学到团队合作和努力比获胜更重要。A 项意为"花费"；B 项意为"需要（时间）"；C 项意为"需要"；D 项意为"意味着；更重要"。mean more than 意为"比……更重要"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一起准备话剧的经历将永远留在我记忆中。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"度过"；C 项意为"停留；留存"；D 项意为"失去"。stay in one's memory 意为"留在记忆中"。故选 C。
