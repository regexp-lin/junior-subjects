# 中考英语完形填空：停电的夜晚

## 完形填空原文

It was a hot night in July. I was doing my homework when suddenly the lights went ___1___. The whole house became dark. I heard Mum call from the living room, "The power is out!" I felt a little scared in the darkness.

Dad found some candles and lit them. The soft candlelight filled the room. It was ___2___ from the bright electric lights we were used to. "Let us sit together and tell stories," Dad suggested. We all gathered around the small table. Mum told us a story about her ___3___ when she was a child. She said they did not have computers or mobile phones. Children played outside and read books for fun.

My younger brother asked, "What did you do when the ___4___ went out at night?" Grandma smiled and said they used oil lamps. "Sometimes we would just go to bed early and ___5___ about the stars." I had never thought about that. We were so busy with our phones and tablets that we almost forgot how beautiful a simple night could be.

Dad brought out his guitar. We sang songs together by the candlelight. The ___6___ was warm and happy. I realised we had not spent an evening like this for a long time. Usually everyone was in a different room, each looking at their own screen.

When the power came back two hours later, we were almost ___7___ to see the lights turn on. We had enjoyed the quiet time together so much. That night I learned something ___8___: sometimes we need to slow down and enjoy the simple things. A power cut might seem like bad luck, but it gave our family a ___9___ to be together without any distractions. I will always ___10___ that special evening.

## 选项

1. A. on &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. up &emsp;&emsp; D. down
2. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. similar &emsp;&emsp; D. bright
3. A. school &emsp;&emsp; B. childhood &emsp;&emsp; C. work &emsp;&emsp; D. family
4. A. water &emsp;&emsp; B. power &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. gas
5. A. talk &emsp;&emsp; B. dream &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. write
6. A. room &emsp;&emsp; B. weather &emsp;&emsp; C. atmosphere &emsp;&emsp; D. candle
7. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. sorry &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
8. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
9. A. reason &emsp;&emsp; B. chance &emsp;&emsp; C. plan &emsp;&emsp; D. wish
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. expect

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：灯突然___了。A 项意为"打开"；B 项意为"熄灭"；C 项意为"调亮"；D 项意为"落下"。"went out"意为"熄灭"，与停电语境相符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这与我们习惯的明亮电灯___。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"相似的"；D 项意为"明亮的"。蜡烛光与电灯光不同，"different"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈讲了她小时候的___故事。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"童年"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"家庭"。后文描述没有电脑手机、在外面玩，是童年生活，"childhood"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：晚上___停了你们怎么办？A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"电"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"煤气"。与本文停电主题一致，"power"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们就早睡，___星星。A 项意为"谈论"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"阅读"；D 项意为"写"。"talk about"意为"谈论"，聊星星是合理的睡前活动。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___温暖而快乐。A 项意为"房间"；B 项意为"天气"；C 项意为"气氛"；D 项意为"蜡烛"。描述家庭聚会的氛围，"atmosphere"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：来电时我们几乎___灯亮起来。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"遗憾的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。"sorry to see"意为"不舍得看到"，享受黑暗中的时光后对来电略带遗憾。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那晚我学到了___的东西。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"困难的"。领悟到放慢脚步的道理是重要收获，"important"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：停电给了我们家人一个___聚在一起、不被干扰。A 项意为"理由"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"愿望"。"chance to"意为"……的机会"，停电带来团聚机会。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会永远___那个特别的夜晚。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"期待"。难忘的回忆用"remember"正确。故选 B。
