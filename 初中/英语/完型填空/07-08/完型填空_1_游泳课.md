# 中考英语完形填空：游泳课

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, Mum signed me up for swimming lessons at the community pool. I had never learned to swim before, and I felt a little ___1___ about it. On the first day, I stood by the pool and watched other children jump into the water. They looked so confident. I wished I could be like them.

Our teacher, Mr Chen, was very patient. He told us that everyone learns at a different ___2___, and there was nothing to be afraid of. First, he taught us how to hold our breath underwater. "Take a deep breath, then put your face in the water and count to five," he said. I tried it and was surprised to find it was not as ___3___ as I had thought.

The next step was learning to float on our backs. I lay on the water and looked up at the blue sky. For a moment, I felt like I was flying. But when I tried to kick my legs, I went ___4___ and got some water in my mouth. Mr Chen laughed and said, "That happens to everyone. Try again."

By the third week, I could swim a few metres. My arms and legs started to work ___5___. The feeling of moving through the water was wonderful. I no longer felt afraid. ___6___, I looked forward to each lesson.

One day, Mum came to watch. She sat by the pool and smiled when she saw me swim. "I am so ___7___ of you," she said. Those words made me feel even better. Now I go to the pool with my friends every weekend. Swimming has become one of my favourite ___8___. I learned that with practice and courage, we can overcome our fears and discover new things we ___9___. The pool is no longer a place of fear but a place of ___10___ and joy.

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
2. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. speed &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. size
3. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. useful &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. important
4. A. down &emsp;&emsp; B. up &emsp;&emsp; C. away &emsp;&emsp; D. back
5. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. together &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
6. A. Still &emsp;&emsp; B. Instead &emsp;&emsp; C. However &emsp;&emsp; D. Besides
7. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. sorry
8. A. subjects &emsp;&emsp; B. hobbies &emsp;&emsp; C. games &emsp;&emsp; D. tests
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. miss &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; D. hate
10. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从没学过游泳，对此感到有点___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"放松的"。第一次学游泳通常会紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人学习的___不同。A 项意为"地方"；B 项意为"速度"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"大小"。"at a different speed"意为"以不同速度"，指学习快慢不同。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现没有想象中那么___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"有用的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"重要的"。后文说"was surprised"表明比预期简单，即没想象中难。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我蹬腿时，身体就___了，喝了一口水。A 项意为"下沉"；B 项意为"上升"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"回来"。初学者蹬腿不当会下沉呛水，"went down"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手臂和腿开始___配合工作。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。"work together"意为"协调配合"，游泳需要手脚协调。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___，我期待每一堂课。A 项意为"仍然"；B 项意为"相反"；C 项意为"然而"；D 项意为"此外"。"Instead"表示转折，从害怕变为期待。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说你让我感到很___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。"be proud of"意为"以……为自豪"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：游泳成了我最喜欢的___之一。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"考试"。游泳是爱好，"hobbies"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们能克服恐惧，发现我们___的新事物。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"讨厌"。发现能享受的新事物，表达收获乐趣。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：泳池不再是恐惧之地，而是___和快乐的地方。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"悲伤"。与"joy"并列，泳池带来乐趣，"fun"正确。故选 B。
