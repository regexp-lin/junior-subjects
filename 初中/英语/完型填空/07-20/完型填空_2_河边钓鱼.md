# 中考英语完形填空：河边钓鱼

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my dad took me fishing by the river. We got up early and drove for about half an hour to reach a quiet ___1___ outside the city. The weather was perfect — the sun was shining and there was a gentle breeze.

Dad set up our fishing rods and taught me how to put the bait on the hook. "Be ___2___," he said. "The fish can smell us if we move too much." I sat still on the small stool and waited. At first, I felt a bit impatient. Nothing seemed to be happening. But Dad said fishing teaches us to wait and ___3___.

After nearly an hour, I felt a pull on my line! My heart beat fast. I carefully pulled the rod up and saw a silver fish ___4___ in the sunlight. "Well done!" Dad cheered. I was so excited that I could not stop smiling. We put the fish in our bucket and ___5___ fishing.

We caught three more fish that morning. Dad said we would take them home and Mum would cook them for dinner. On our way back, we passed by a beautiful field of ___6___. The yellow flowers danced in the wind. I told Dad that I wanted to come again next weekend.

Fishing has made me understand that good things come to those who wait. We cannot ___7___ success in a hurry. Sometimes we need to slow down and enjoy the moment. Dad also said that spending time in ___8___ helps us feel calm and peaceful.

I used to think fishing was boring, but now I find it really ___9___. It is not just about catching fish — it is about being with family and learning from nature. I hope we can have more ___10___ like this together.

## 选项

1. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. room &emsp;&emsp; C. street &emsp;&emsp; D. building
2. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. loud &emsp;&emsp; D. busy
3. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. hope &emsp;&emsp; C. shout &emsp;&emsp; D. sleep
4. A. swimming &emsp;&emsp; B. shining &emsp;&emsp; C. falling &emsp;&emsp; D. hiding
5. A. stopped &emsp;&emsp; B. started &emsp;&emsp; C. continued &emsp;&emsp; D. finished
6. A. trees &emsp;&emsp; B. grass &emsp;&emsp; C. flowers &emsp;&emsp; D. vegetables
7. A. get &emsp;&emsp; B. lose &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
8. A. nature &emsp;&emsp; B. school &emsp;&emsp; C. city &emsp;&emsp; D. home
9. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. terrible &emsp;&emsp; D. dangerous
10. A. games &emsp;&emsp; B. lessons &emsp;&emsp; C. trips &emsp;&emsp; D. tests

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们开车约半小时到达城外的安静___。A 项意为"地方"；B 项意为"房间"；C 项意为"街道"；D 项意为"建筑"。河边钓鱼的地点用"place"最合适。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要___。鱼如果动静太大能闻到我们的气味。A 项意为"快的"；B 项意为"安静的"；C 项意为"大声的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。钓鱼需要保持安静避免惊动鱼，"quiet"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：钓鱼教我们等待和___。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"希望"；C 项意为"喊"；D 项意为"睡"。钓鱼需要耐心等待和心怀希望，"hope"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到一条银色的鱼在阳光下___。A 项意为"游泳"；B 项意为"闪耀"；C 项意为"下落"；D 项意为"躲藏"。鱼在阳光下闪闪发光，"shining"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把鱼放进桶里，___钓鱼。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"继续"；D 项意为"完成"。钓到鱼后继续钓，"continued"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们经过一片美丽的___地。A 项意为"树"；B 项意为"草"；C 项意为"花"；D 项意为"蔬菜"。后文"黄花在风中舞动"指花朵，"flowers"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不能匆忙___成功。A 项意为"得到"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"错过"。"get success in a hurry"意为"急于求成"，符合文意。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在大___中度过时光帮助我们感到平静。A 项意为"自然"；B 项意为"学校"；C 项意为"城市"；D 项意为"家"。河边钓鱼是在大自然中，"nature"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我现在觉得它真的很___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"糟糕的"；D 项意为"危险的"。从"boring"转变为正面感受，"interesting"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望我们能多有这样的___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"课程"；C 项意为"旅行/郊游"；D 项意为"考试"。河边钓鱼是一次出游，"trips"正确。故选 C。
