# 中考英语完形填空：学做水果拼盘

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, Mom said we would have some guests for dinner. She asked if I could help make a fruit platter. I had never made one before, but I was ___1___ to learn.

Mom took me to the fruit market. We bought apples, oranges, grapes, strawberries, and a small watermelon. The colorful fruits looked so ___2___ that I couldn't wait to start.

Back home, Mom showed me how to wash and dry each fruit first. "Clean hands and clean fruits are very ___3___," she said. Then we cut the watermelon into small triangles and arranged them around the edge of a large plate. This would be the ___4___ of our design.

Next, I peeled the oranges and divided them into segments. I placed them in a circle in the middle. The strawberries were already clean, so I just cut off their green tops and put them ___5___ the oranges and the watermelon. The red strawberries made the platter look much more beautiful.

I was careful not to ___6___ the fruits too close together. Mom said leaving a little space between them made the platter look more professional. I added some grapes here and there for a final touch.

When I finished, the platter looked like a colorful work of art. Mom took a photo of it and said she was ___7___ of me. The guests loved it too. They said it was both delicious and beautiful.

Making the fruit platter taught me that cooking is not just about taste—it's also about making food look ___8___. A little creativity can turn ordinary things into something ___9___. I will try to make more fruit platters in the future and maybe ___10___ some new designs of my own.

2. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. fresh &emsp;&emsp; C. heavy &emsp;&emsp; D. cheap
3. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. useless
4. A. center &emsp;&emsp; B. base &emsp;&emsp; C. top &emsp;&emsp; D. side
5. A. under &emsp;&emsp; B. beside &emsp;&emsp; C. above &emsp;&emsp; D. between
6. A. push &emsp;&emsp; B. throw &emsp;&emsp; C. drop &emsp;&emsp; D. mix
7. A. ashamed &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
8. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. cheap &emsp;&emsp; C. attractive &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
9. A. ordinary &emsp;&emsp; B. special &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
10. A. copy &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. create &emsp;&emsp; D. borrow

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从来没做过，但很愿意学。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"愿意的"；D 项意为"生气的"。be willing to learn 意为乐于学习。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：五颜六色的水果看起来很新鲜。A 项意为"贵的"；B 项意为"新鲜的"；C 项意为"重的"；D 项意为"便宜的"。水果市场买的新鲜水果用 fresh。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：干净的手和干净的水果很重要。A 项意为"难的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"没用的"。食品安全中清洁很重要。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：西瓜片沿大盘边缘摆好，这将成为我们造型的基础。A 项意为"中心"；B 项意为"基础"；C 项意为"顶部"；D 项意为"侧面"。base of design 意为设计的基础。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把草莓放在橘子和西瓜之间。A 项意为"在……下面"；B 项意为"在……旁边"；C 项意为"在……上面"；D 项意为"在……之间"。between A and B 表示在两者之间。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我注意不让水果挤得太紧。A 项意为"推、挤"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"掉落"；D 项意为"混合"。push...close together 意为挤在一起。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈拍了张照，说她为我骄傲。A 项意为"羞愧的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"担心的"。be proud of 意为以……为傲。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做饭不只是味道，还要让食物看起来吸引人。A 项意为"快的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"吸引人的"；D 项意为"简单的"。look attractive 意为看起来诱人。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一点创意就能把普通的东西变得特别。A 项意为"普通的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。turn...into something special 意为变成特别的东西。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：将来要多做水果拼盘，也许还能自己创造一些新造型。A 项意为"复制"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"创造"；D 项意为"借"。create designs 意为创造设计。故选 C。
