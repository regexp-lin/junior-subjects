# 中考英语完形填空：参加校园辩论赛选拔

## 完形填空原文

Our school was going to hold a debate competition next month, and they needed to ___1___ students for the team. When I saw the notice on the bulletin board, I felt both excited and nervous.

I had always enjoyed ___2___ different topics with my friends, but speaking in front of a large audience was something new to me. I decided to give it a try anyway. After school, I went to the speech room where the selection was held.

There were about thirty students waiting. The teacher explained the rules: each of us would have three minutes to present our ___3___ on the topic "Should students use smartphones at school?" I had thought about this topic before, so I wasn't completely unprepared.

When my turn came, I took a deep breath and walked to the front. I ___4___ that smartphones could help us look up information, but they might also distract us from listening in class. I tried to speak clearly and make eye contact with the judges.

To my surprise, I was chosen! The teacher said my arguments were logical and I stayed ___5___ during my speech. I was so happy that I could hardly believe it.

Now I need to practice more with my new teammates. We will meet every Tuesday after school to ___6___ our debating skills. I hope we can do well in the competition and bring ___7___ to our school.

Looking back, I'm glad I didn't ___8___ to join. Sometimes we need to step out of our comfort zone to discover our ___9___. If you have a dream, don't be afraid to try. You might ___10___ yourself!

## 选项

1. A. choose &emsp;&emsp; B. invite &emsp;&emsp; C. send &emsp;&emsp; D. call
2. A. discussing &emsp;&emsp; B. watching &emsp;&emsp; C. reading &emsp;&emsp; D. writing
3. A. ideas &emsp;&emsp; B. questions &emsp;&emsp; C. answers &emsp;&emsp; D. stories
4. A. refused &emsp;&emsp; B. argued &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. wondered
5. A. calm &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
6. A. improve &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. teach
7. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. honor &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
8. A. promise &emsp;&emsp; B. decide &emsp;&emsp; C. fail &emsp;&emsp; D. hesitate
9. A. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. talents &emsp;&emsp; D. fears
10. A. hurt &emsp;&emsp; B. surprise &emsp;&emsp; C. disappoint &emsp;&emsp; D. punish

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校需要选拔学生参加辩论队。A 项意为"选择、选拔"；B 项意为"邀请"；C 项意为"送"；D 项意为"打电话"。choose 与选拔语境相符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直喜欢和朋友讨论各种话题。A 项意为"讨论"；B 项意为"观看"；C 项意为"阅读"；D 项意为"写作"。discuss topics 意为讨论话题。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每人有三分钟就"学生在校是否可用手机"这个主题陈述自己的观点。A 项意为"观点、想法"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"答案"；D 项意为"故事"。辩论中陈述 ideas/观点。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我论证说手机能帮助查资料，但也可能分散课堂注意力。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"论证、主张"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"想知道"。辩论中 argue 表示论证观点。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师夸我论证有逻辑，演讲时保持镇定。A 项意为"镇定的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。stay calm 表示保持冷静。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们每周二放学后见面，提高辩论技能。A 项意为"提高"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"教"。improve skills 意为提升技能。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望能在比赛中取得好成绩，为学校争光。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"荣誉"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"恐惧"。bring honor 意为带来荣誉。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回想起来，庆幸当初没有犹豫参加。A 项意为"承诺"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"犹豫"。hesitate to join 意为犹豫是否参加。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们要走出舒适区才能发现自己的才能。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"才能"；D 项意为"恐惧"。discover talents 意为发现才能。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有梦想就勇敢尝试，你可能会惊艳到自己。A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"使惊讶"；C 项意为"使失望"；D 项意为"惩罚"。surprise yourself 意为令自己惊喜。故选 B。
