# 中考英语完形填空：无声的钢琴课

## 完形填空原文

My neighbor, Mr. Sun, was a piano teacher. Every afternoon, beautiful music floated from his apartment into ours. I ___1___ dreamed of learning to play like that.

One day, I gathered my courage and knocked on his door. "Mr. Sun, could you teach me piano?" I asked.

He looked at me kindly. "Do your parents ___2___?"

I hesitated. My parents didn't have much money, and piano lessons were expensive. "I... I can't pay you," I admitted, looking down.

To my surprise, he smiled. "I didn't ask about money. I asked if your parents agree. Bring me their ___3___, and we'll start next week."

And so began my piano lessons. Mr. Sun was strict but ___4___. He never raised his voice, even when I played the same wrong note for the tenth time. "Music is not about being perfect," he would say. "It's about expressing what's inside you."

I practiced every day on a paper keyboard my father drew for me. It made no ___5___, of course, but I could practice my finger movements. Mr. Sun thought it was the most creative solution he had ever seen.

After six months, I could play several simple pieces. Mr. Sun entered me into a school music ___6___. I was terrified. The other students had expensive pianos at home and had been playing for years.

On the day of the competition, my hands were cold and my heart was beating ___7___. When my turn came, I sat down, closed my eyes, and imagined I was back in Mr. Sun's living room. Then I began to play.

I didn't win first place. I came in third. But when I finished, the audience clapped loudly, and Mr. Sun, sitting in the front row, had tears in his ___8___.

After the competition, I asked him why he had agreed to teach me for free. He was quiet for a moment, then said, "When I was young, someone did the same for me. I was a poor boy who loved music. A kind teacher gave me free lessons and ___9___ my life. I promised myself that one day, I would do the same for someone else."

I understood then that kindness is not something you keep. It's something you ___10___. And when you pass it on, it grows.

## 选项

1. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. hardly &emsp;&emsp; C. always &emsp;&emsp; D. seldom
2. A. know &emsp;&emsp; B. agree &emsp;&emsp; C. pay &emsp;&emsp; D. care
3. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. phone &emsp;&emsp; C. permission &emsp;&emsp; D. address

6. A. class &emsp;&emsp; B. club &emsp;&emsp; C. competition &emsp;&emsp; D. concert
7. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. softly &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
8. A. hands &emsp;&emsp; B. pockets &emsp;&emsp; C. heart &emsp;&emsp; D. eyes
9. A. saved &emsp;&emsp; B. changed &emsp;&emsp; C. ended &emsp;&emsp; D. controlled
10. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. pass &emsp;&emsp; D. use

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___梦想着能像那样弹琴。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"几乎不"；C 项意为"总是"；D 项意为"很少"。每天听到美妙的琴声，"我""总是"（always）梦想自己也能弹。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你的父母___吗？A 项意为"知道"；B 项意为"同意"；C 项意为"付钱"；D 项意为"在意"。孙老师没问钱的事，而是问父母是否"同意"（agree）让孩子学琴。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把他们的___带来。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"电话"；C 项意为"许可"；D 项意为"地址"。对应前面的"if your parents agree"，需要的是父母的"许可"（permission）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：孙老师严格但___。A 项意为"严格的"；B 项意为"耐心的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。与strict形成转折，即使弹错十次也不发火，说明他很"耐心"（patient）。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：纸键盘当然不发出___。A 项意为"意义"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"差别"；D 项意为"进步"。纸做的键盘没有真琴，自然没有"声音"（sound），但可以练习指法。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：孙老师让我参加学校音乐___。A 项意为"班级"；B 项意为"俱乐部"；C 项意为"比赛"；D 项意为"音乐会"。后文多次提到competition、won、first place，说明是音乐"比赛"（competition）。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的心跳得很___。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"轻轻地"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"安静地"。比赛时紧张，心跳"快"（fast）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：孙老师___里有泪水。A 项意为"手"；B 项意为"口袋"；C 项意为"心"；D 项意为"眼睛"。"tears in one's eyes"（眼含泪水）是固定搭配，孙老师被学生的表现感动得热泪盈眶。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位好心的老师给了我免费课程，___了我的人生。A 项意为"拯救"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"结束"；D 项意为"控制"。免费的钢琴课让一个穷孩子走上了音乐之路，"改变了"（changed）他的人生。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：善良不是你留着的东西，而是你___的东西。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"传递"；D 项意为"使用"。"pass on"（传递下去），善良要传递给他人才会发扬光大。故选 C。
