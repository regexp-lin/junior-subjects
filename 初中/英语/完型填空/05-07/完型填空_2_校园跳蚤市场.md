# 中考英语完形填空：校园跳蚤市场

## 完形填空原文

Every June, our school holds a flea market. Students can sell old books, toys, clothes, and anything else they no longer ___1___. The money raised goes to a local charity.

This year, I decided to sell some of my old things. I gathered a box of picture books, a few board games, and a pair of roller skates I had outgrown. My mother also added a set of cooking pots she never used.

On the day of the market, each class set up a table in the playground. The ___2___ was lively and colorful. Some students made posters. Others shouted prices like real shopkeepers. It felt more like a festival than a school event.

At first, business was ___3___. People walked by our table without stopping. I started to feel discouraged. Then Li Na, my teammate, had an idea. "Let's offer a special deal," she said. "Buy two items and get one ___4___."

The deal worked like magic. Within an hour, we had sold most of our books and all of the board games. A first-grader bought my roller skates and immediately put them on, skating ___5___ around the playground with a huge grin on his face.

But the cooking pots were a harder sell. Nobody seemed ___6___ in them. Then an old woman walked by. She stopped, picked up a pot, and examined it carefully. "How much?" she asked.

"Twenty yuan," I said.

She shook her head. "Too much for an old woman like me." She started to walk away.

I looked at the pots. My mother hadn't used them in years. They were just taking up ___7___ at home. "Wait," I called out. "You can have them for five yuan."

Her face broke into a wide smile. "You're a ___8___ child," she said, handing me a five-yuan note.

By the end of the day, our class had raised over 800 yuan. We were the ___9___ earners in the whole school. But the best part wasn't the money. It was seeing the joy on people's faces when they found something they loved at a price they could afford.

I went home that evening with an empty box and a full ___10___. Sometimes, letting go of old things makes room for something better — like the warm feeling of helping others.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生可以卖掉不再___的旧东西。A 项意为"需要"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"制作"；D 项意为"知道"。跳蚤市场卖的是不再"需要"（need）的旧物。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___热闹而多彩。A 项意为"教室"；B 项意为"图书馆"；C 项意为"场景"；D 项意为"办公室"。描述市场现场的"场景"（scene）热闹多彩。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始生意很___。A 项意为"好的"；B 项意为"慢的/冷清的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。人们走过不停留，"我"开始泄气，说明生意"冷清"（slow）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：买两件东西___送一件。A 项意为"免费的"；B 项意为"回"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"远"。"buy two get one free"（买二送一）是常见促销方式。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个一年级小朋友马上穿上，___地在操场上滑。A 项意为"伤心地"；B 项意为"小心地"；C 项意为"开心地"；D 项意为"安静地"。配合"huge grin on his face"（脸上大大的笑容），小朋友"开心地"（happily）滑行。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：没人似乎对它们感___。A 项意为"有经验的"；B 项意为"迷路的"；C 项意为"成功的"；D 项意为"感兴趣的"。"be interested in"（对……感兴趣）是固定搭配，锅子没人感兴趣。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们只是在家里占___。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"空间"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"精力"。"take up space"（占空间）是固定搭配，锅子在家闲置占地方。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你是个___的孩子。A 项意为"聪明的"；B 项意为"善良的"；C 项意为"勇敢的"；D 项意为"安静的"。降价卖给老人是善意的行为，老人夸孩子"善良"（kind）。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班是全校收入最___的。A 项意为"最低的"；B 项意为"最快的"；C 项意为"最高的"；D 项意为"最差的"。"top earners"（收入最高者），800多元是全校最多的。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我带着空箱子和满满的___回家。A 项意为"盒子"；B 项意为"心"；C 项意为"口袋"；D 项意为"胃"。"a full heart"（满心欢喜）与"an empty box"形成对比，物品卖完了但内心充盈。故选 B。
