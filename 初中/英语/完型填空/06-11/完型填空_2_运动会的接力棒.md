# 中考英语完形填空：运动会的接力棒

## 完形填空原文

The relay race was the most exciting event at our school sports meet. Four runners in each team would pass a baton from one to another. I was the second runner in our class team. The day before the race, we practised ___1___ in the playground. Our PE teacher told us that the most important thing was the handover. "The baton must never ___2___," he said. "If you drop it, you lose precious seconds."

On the day of the race, I stood in my position, watching our first runner come towards me. My hands were sweaty, and my heart was ___3___ fast. When I felt the baton in my hand, I ran as fast as I could. The wind rushed past my ears. I could hear my classmates ___4___ loudly. I focused only on reaching the next runner and passing the baton smoothly.

The moment I passed it to our third runner, I felt a sudden ___5___. I had done my part. Now it was up to my teammates to carry on. I stood by the track, watching them run. When our fourth runner crossed the ___6___ line first, we all jumped and cheered. We had won because we had worked together. No single runner could do it alone — we needed every ___7___ of the race.

That relay baton taught me something important. In life, we often have to pass things on — knowledge, hope, or responsibility. We receive from those before us and give to those ___8___ us. The baton is just a simple stick, but it stands for something bigger: the idea that we are all ___9___ in the same race that never really ends.

I still keep a photo of our team holding the baton together. Whenever I look at it, I remember that success is not only about running fast. It is about trusting others and being ___10___ when they trust you.

## 选项

1. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. together &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
2. A. break &emsp;&emsp; B. fall &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. move
3. A. beating &emsp;&emsp; B. jumping &emsp;&emsp; C. turning &emsp;&emsp; D. running
4. A. singing &emsp;&emsp; B. shouting &emsp;&emsp; C. talking &emsp;&emsp; D. laughing
5. A. pain &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. joy &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
6. A. start &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. middle &emsp;&emsp; D. safety
7. A. step &emsp;&emsp; B. person &emsp;&emsp; C. second &emsp;&emsp; D. moment
8. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. beside &emsp;&emsp; C. behind &emsp;&emsp; D. after
9. A. running &emsp;&emsp; B. winning &emsp;&emsp; C. competing &emsp;&emsp; D. training
10. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. honest &emsp;&emsp; C. careful &emsp;&emsp; D. worthy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛前一天，我们在操场上___练习。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"快速"；D 项意为"缓慢"。"practised together"与接力赛需要四人配合的特性相呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：体育老师说："接力棒绝不能___。"A 项意为"断裂"；B 项意为"掉落"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"移动"。"must never fall"与后文"If you drop it"相呼应，强调交接时不能脱手。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手在出汗，心脏___得很快。A 项意为"跳动"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"转动"；D 项意为"跑"。"heart was beating fast"为固定搭配，表示紧张心跳加速。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能听到同学们在 loudly ___。A 项意为"唱歌"；B 项意为"呐喊"；C 项意为"说话"；D 项意为"笑"。"shouting loudly"与比赛时加油助威的场景相呼应。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把接力棒交给第三棒的那一刻，我感到一阵突如其来的___。A 项意为"疼痛"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"喜悦"；D 项意为"惊讶"。"joy"与完成自己任务后的如释重负和满足感相呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们的第四棒首先冲过___线时，我们都跳起来欢呼。A 项意为"起点"；B 项意为"终点"；C 项意为"中间"；D 项意为"安全"。"finish line"为固定搭配，指跑道终点线。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有一个跑步者能独自完成——我们需要比赛的每一___。A 项意为"棒、环节"；B 项意为"人"；C 项意为"秒"；D 项意为"时刻"。"every step"指接力的每一棒、每一环节，与"four runners"、"pass the baton"相呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们从前人那里接收，再传递给___我们的人。A 项意为"在……之前"；B 项意为"在……旁边"；C 项意为"在……后面"；D 项意为"在……之后"。"those after us"指我们之后的接棒者，与"those before us"形成对照。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接力棒只是一根简单的棒子，但它代表更重要的东西：我们都在___同一场永无终点的赛跑。A 项意为"奔跑"；B 项意为"获胜"；C 项意为"参与竞争"；D 项意为"训练"。"competing in the same race"与接力赛及人生传承的主题相呼应，强调大家共同参与。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：成功不仅仅是跑得快，还关乎信任他人，以及在他们信任你时变得___。A 项意为"勇敢"；B 项意为"诚实"；C 项意为"小心"；D 项意为"值得信赖的"。"worthy"此处意为"值得被信任的"，与"trusting others"、"when they trust you"相呼应。故选 D。
