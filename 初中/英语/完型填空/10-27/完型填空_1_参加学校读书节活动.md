# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校读书节活动

## 完形填空原文

Our school holds a Reading Festival every year in March. This year, I decided to ___1___ part in it. The festival would last three days. On the first day, there was an opening ceremony in the school hall. Our headteacher gave a speech and encouraged us to read ___2___.

I signed up for the "Book Sharing" activity. Each student had to introduce a favorite book in front of the class. I chose a book about adventure. At first, I felt very ___3___ when I stood on the stage. But when I looked at my classmates' friendly faces, I became calm. I ___4___ my book clearly and everyone listened quietly. When I finished, they clapped loudly. That made me very happy.

There was also a book fair in the playground. Many publishers came to sell books at special ___5___. I bought two storybooks and a magazine. My best friend found an interesting science book. We sat under a tree and ___6___ reading our new books together. Time passed so quickly!

On the last day, the school announced the winners of the reading competition. I didn't win a prize, but I wasn't ___7___. The most important thing was that I had discovered the joy of reading. I also made some new friends who love books as much as I do. I will ___8___ join the Reading Festival next year. Reading really opens up a whole new ___9___ for us. As the saying goes, "A book is like a garden that you can carry in your ___10___."

## 选项

1. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. make &emsp;&emsp; D. get
2. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. much &emsp;&emsp; C. many &emsp;&emsp; D. most
3. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
4. A. spoke &emsp;&emsp; B. wrote &emsp;&emsp; C. drew &emsp;&emsp; D. sang
5. A. places &emsp;&emsp; B. prices &emsp;&emsp; C. plans &emsp;&emsp; D. ways
6. A. started &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoyed &emsp;&emsp; C. finished &emsp;&emsp; D. stopped
7. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. glad &emsp;&emsp; C. sad &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy
8. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. seldom &emsp;&emsp; C. sometimes &emsp;&emsp; D. certainly
9. A. room &emsp;&emsp; B. world &emsp;&emsp; C. box &emsp;&emsp; D. bag
10. A. hand &emsp;&emsp; B. pocket &emsp;&emsp; C. desk &emsp;&emsp; D. schoolbag

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：今年我决定参加读书节活动。A 项 take part in 意为"参加"；B 项 join 意为"加入"，通常接组织或人；C 项意为"制作"；D 项 get 无 get part in 的搭配。参加活动用 take part in。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长演讲并鼓励我们多读书。A 项 read more 意为"读更多书"；B 项 much 修饰不可数名词，read much 较少用；C 项 many 修饰可数名词；D 项 most 表最高级。encourage to read 后接 more 表"读得更多"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初站在台上我感到很紧张。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"放松的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。初次上台演讲通常是 nervous，后文提到看同学后 became calm 可印证。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我清晰地向大家介绍了我的书，大家都安静地听。A 项意为"说、讲述"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"画"；D 项意为"唱"。在台上介绍书用 speak about / introduce。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多出版社来以特价卖书。A 项意为"地方"；B 项意为"价格"，at special prices 表"以特价"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"方法"。书展卖书用 special prices。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们坐在树下一起愉快地读新买的书。A 项意为"开始"；B 项 enjoyed 后接 doing，enjoy reading 意为"享受阅读"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"停止"。语境是享受读书时光，用 enjoyed。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我没获奖，但我不难过。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"高兴的"；C 项意为"难过的"；D 项意为"困倦的"。没获奖通常会 sad，但文中说"最重要的发现了阅读的快乐"，所以 wasn't sad。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明年一定会再参加读书节。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"有时"；D 项意为"一定、肯定"。表达积极意愿用 certainly。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阅读真的为我们打开了全新的世界。A 项意为"房间"；B 项 whole new world 意为"全新世界"；C 项意为"盒子"；D 项意为"袋子"。open up a new world 为常见表达。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：正如俗话所说，"书如花园，可揣于 pocket"。A 项 in your hand 表"在手中"；B 项 carry in your pocket 意为"揣在兜里携带"，与谚语相符；C、D 项语义不符。谚语 "A book is a garden carried in the pocket" 用 pocket。故选 B。
