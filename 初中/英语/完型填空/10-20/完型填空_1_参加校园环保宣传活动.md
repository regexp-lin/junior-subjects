# 中考英语完形填空：参加校园环保宣传活动

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held an environmental protection activity. I was excited to ___1___ in it. We wanted to tell everyone how important it is to save water and electricity, and to recycle waste.

The activity started on Monday morning. We put up posters around the school with the ___2___ "Go Green, Save the Earth". Many students stopped to look at them. Some even took photos. At noon, we set up a small ___3___ in the school square. We gave out leaflets and asked students to sign their names if they agreed to protect the environment.

I was a little ___4___ at first because I had to talk to strangers. But when a junior student came to me and said, "I will tell my family about this," I felt encouraged. Soon I became more confident. I ___5___ more than fifty students that afternoon.

Our teacher said we did a good job. She also told us that small ___6___ in our daily life can help a lot. For example, we can turn off the lights when we leave a room, and we can use both sides of the paper. I decided to ___7___ these habits from that day on.

The activity ___8___ for a week. On the last day, we had a short meeting. Our headmaster gave a speech and thanked all of us. He said that our school would hold such activities ___9___ every year. I was proud of what we had done. Protecting the environment is everyone's duty, and I hope more students will ___10___ us next time.

## 选项

1. A. take part &emsp;&emsp; B. take care &emsp;&emsp; C. take place &emsp;&emsp; D. take off
2. A. sentence &emsp;&emsp; B. message &emsp;&emsp; C. word &emsp;&emsp; D. phrase
3. A. shop &emsp;&emsp; B. desk &emsp;&emsp; C. booth &emsp;&emsp; D. room
4. A. shy &emsp;&emsp; B. brave &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. calm
5. A. met &emsp;&emsp; B. helped &emsp;&emsp; C. invited &emsp;&emsp; D. taught
6. A. changes &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. ideas &emsp;&emsp; D. plans
7. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. break &emsp;&emsp; C. form &emsp;&emsp; D. lose
8. A. lasted &emsp;&emsp; B. started &emsp;&emsp; C. began &emsp;&emsp; D. ended
9. A. still &emsp;&emsp; B. already &emsp;&emsp; C. once &emsp;&emsp; D. again
10. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. thank &emsp;&emsp; D. visit

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很兴奋能参加其中。A 项意为"参加"；B 项意为"照顾"；C 项意为"发生"；D 项意为"起飞"。take part in 为固定搭配，意为"参加"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在校园各处张贴了写有"绿色出行，拯救地球"的宣传海报。A 项意为"句子"；B 项意为"信息；标语"；C 项意为"词"；D 项意为"短语"。海报上的标语内容用 message 最恰当。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：中午我们在学校广场搭了一个小摊位。A 项意为"商店"；B 项意为"书桌"；C 项意为"摊位"；D 项意为"房间"。活动中发传单、签名的场所用 booth（摊位）最合适。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我有点害羞，因为我要和陌生人说话。A 项意为"害羞的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"平静的"。与陌生人交谈会害羞，shy 符合语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天下午我邀请了五十多名学生（签名）。A 项意为"遇见"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"邀请"；D 项意为"教"。宣传活动是邀请同学签名承诺，invited 最贴切。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说我们日常生活中的小改变就能起到很大帮助。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"计划"。后文举例关灯、双面用纸等，都是 changes。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定从那天起养成这些习惯。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"打破"；C 项意为"形成、养成"；D 项意为"失去"。form habits 为固定搭配，意为"养成习惯"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：活动持续了一周。A 项意为"持续"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"开始"；D 项意为"结束"。last for 表示"持续"，与"一周"搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长说我们学校每年都会再次举办这样的活动。A 项意为"仍然"；B 项意为"已经"；C 项意为"一次"；D 项意为"又；再"。hold such activities again 表示"再次举办"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望下次有更多学生会加入我们。A 项意为"跟随"；B 项意为"加入"；C 项意为"感谢"；D 项意为"参观"。希望更多人参与活动，join us 意为"加入我们"。故选 B。
