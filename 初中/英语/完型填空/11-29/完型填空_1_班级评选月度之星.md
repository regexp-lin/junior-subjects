# 中考英语完形填空：班级评选月度之星

## 完形填空原文

Every month, our class holds a "Star of the Month" selection. Students vote for the classmate who has improved the most or helped others the best. This month, I was ___1___ to be chosen. I couldn't believe it when our headteacher announced my name.

Last month, I had helped organize the class library. I spent several afternoons sorting books and making a simple catalog. I also helped two classmates with their English homework when they asked. I didn't do these things to win any ___2___—I just wanted to be useful. So when I was selected, I felt surprised and honored.

On the day of the award, our headteacher gave me a certificate and a small gift. She said that small acts of kindness could make a big difference. My classmates clapped and some came to congratulate me. I said thank you to ___3___ and promised to keep doing my best.

This experience made me realize that our efforts are ___4___ by others. When we help without expecting anything in return, we often receive more than we give. I will continue to contribute to our class in my own way. Being the Star of the Month is not just an honor—it is also a ___5___ to do better. I hope more students will work hard and have the ___6___ to be recognized one day. The certificate will ___7___ on my bedroom wall as a reminder. Kindness and hard work always ___8___ in the end. I am ___9___ to my classmates for their support. Let's all try to be the best ___10___ of ourselves.

1. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. lucky &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. prize &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. exam &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
3. A. herself &emsp;&emsp; B. himself &emsp;&emsp; C. everyone &emsp;&emsp; D. nobody
4. A. forgotten &emsp;&emsp; B. noticed &emsp;&emsp; C. ignored &emsp;&emsp; D. refused
5. A. game &emsp;&emsp; B. responsibility &emsp;&emsp; C. joke &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
6. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. chance &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. place
7. A. fall &emsp;&emsp; B. hang &emsp;&emsp; C. disappear &emsp;&emsp; D. break
8. A. fail &emsp;&emsp; B. pay off &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
9. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. grateful &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
10. A. version &emsp;&emsp; B. copy &emsp;&emsp; C. part &emsp;&emsp; D. piece

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个月我很幸运被选中。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"幸运的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"累的"。被选为月度之星用 lucky。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做这些不是为了得奖。A 项意为"奖励"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"错误"。帮助他人不为 prize。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我向大家致谢。A 项意为"她自己"；B 项意为"他自己"；C 项意为"每个人"；D 项意为"没人"。感谢所有同学用 everyone。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的付出会被他人注意到。A 项意为"被忘记"；B 项意为"被注意到"；C 项意为"被忽视"；D 项意为"被拒绝"。努力被看见用 noticed。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不仅是荣誉，也是做得更好的责任。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"责任"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"休息"。荣誉带来 responsibility。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望更多同学有机会被认可。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"地点"。have the chance 表示有机会。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：证书会挂在卧室墙上作为提醒。A 项意为"落下"；B 项意为"悬挂"；C 项意为"消失"；D 项意为"打破"。证书挂墙上用 hang。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：善良和努力终有回报。A 项意为"失败"；B 项 pay off 意为"有回报"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"离开"。付出 pay off。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感谢同学们的支持。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"感激的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。be grateful to 表示感谢。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我们都努力成为最好的自己。A 项 version 意为"版本"，the best version of ourselves 指"最好的自己"；B 项意为"复制"；C 项意为"部分"；D 项意为"碎片"。故选 A。
