# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工皮革钥匙扣

## 完形填空原文

My uncle is good at leather craft. Last weekend, he taught me how to make a leather keychain. I had always admired his handmade belts and bags, so I was eager to learn. He said we would start with something ___1___—a simple keychain.

First, we chose a piece of soft leather. Uncle said the leather should be neither too thick nor too thin. We drew a small shape on the leather—I chose a leaf. Then we cut it out carefully with a sharp knife. I had to be very ___2___ not to cut my fingers. The cutting took me a long time because I wanted every edge to be smooth.

Next, we punched a hole at the top for the key ring. We used a special tool to make the hole. Uncle showed me how to burn the edges with a small torch to prevent the leather from ___3___. The smell of burned leather was strong but not unpleasant. After that, we oiled the leather to make it softer and to protect it.

Finally, we attached a metal ring to the keychain. I held my first handmade keychain in my hand. It wasn't perfect, but it was ___4___ special because I had made it myself. Uncle said practice would make me better. I gave the keychain to my dad as a gift. He was very happy and said he would ___5___ it every day.

Making the keychain taught me that handmade things have more meaning than factory-made ones. I plan to make ___6___ and give them to friends and family. Each piece carries our love and care. My uncle said the skills would ___7___ useful in the future. I might even try making a small leather ___8___ next time. Handicrafts connect us with traditions and make our lives more ___9___. I will ___10___ this meaningful lesson forever.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从简单的东西开始——一个简单的钥匙扣。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。入门从 simple 开始。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：得非常小心，不要割伤手指。A 项意为"粗心的"；B 项意为"小心的"；C 项意为"懒惰的"；D 项意为"快的"。用刀需 careful。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烧边防止皮革褪色。A 项意为"生长"；B 项意为"褪色"；C 项意为"断裂"；D 项意为"出现"。皮革边缘易 fading。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽不完美，但真的很特别，因为是亲手做的。A 项意为"仍然"；B 项意为"已经"；C 项意为"真正地"；D 项意为"从不"。强调 special 用 really。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说会天天使用。A 项意为"丢失"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"使用"；D 项意为"扔掉"。钥匙扣 daily use。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词/代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：打算多做几个送给朋友和家人。A 项意为"更少"；B 项意为"更多"；C 项意为"更少"；D 项意为"没有"。送多人需 make more。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔说这些技能将来会很有用。pove useful 意为"证明有用"；A 项 become 需接形容词；B 项 stay 意为"保持"；C 项 seem 需加 to be。prove useful 为常见搭配。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次也许试试做一个小皮夹。A 项意为"钱包"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"手机"；D 项意为"电脑"。皮革 craft 可做 wallet。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工艺让生活更有意义。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"空的"。传统手艺使生活 meaningful。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会永远记得这有意义的一课。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"想念"。美好经历用 remember。故选 B。
