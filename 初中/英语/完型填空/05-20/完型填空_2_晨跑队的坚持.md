# 中考英语完形填空：晨跑队的坚持

## 完形填空原文

Every morning at 6:30, a group of students meets at the school gate. We call ourselves the "Morning Running Team." We run around the playground for 20 minutes before class. It sounds ___1___, but staying consistent is not easy.

When I first joined, I could only run two laps. I would be out of ___2___ and want to stop. My legs felt heavy, and my heart was beating fast. "Don't give up. You'll get stronger," said our team leader, Zhang Wei. He had been running for a year and could easily ___3___ five laps. I decided to follow his example.

Week by week, I ran a little more. By the third month, I could run four laps without ___4___. I felt proud of my progress. But then winter came. The cold wind made it hard to get out of bed. Many students ___5___ the team. Only a few of us kept running. On the coldest mornings, we would encourage each other: "If we can run today, we can do ___6___."

Spring came, and our hard work paid off. In the school sports meeting, our running team won the relay race. The principal praised us for our ___7___. "You didn't run for medals. You ran for yourselves," he said. "That's the real ___8___ of sports."

Now I understand that ___9___ is the key to improvement. Whether it's running, studying, or learning a skill, we need to keep trying. Every small step brings us closer to our ___10___. The morning run taught me that the best time to start is now.

## 选项

1. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
2. A. breath &emsp;&emsp; B. mind &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. way
3. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. start &emsp;&emsp; C. watch &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
4. A. stopping &emsp;&emsp; B. trying &emsp;&emsp; C. thinking &emsp;&emsp; D. waiting
5. A. joined &emsp;&emsp; B. left &emsp;&emsp; C. led &emsp;&emsp; D. found
6. A. nothing &emsp;&emsp; B. anything &emsp;&emsp; C. something &emsp;&emsp; D. everything
7. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. talent &emsp;&emsp; C. persistence &emsp;&emsp; D. luck
8. A. rule &emsp;&emsp; B. spirit &emsp;&emsp; C. result &emsp;&emsp; D. reason
9. A. talent &emsp;&emsp; B. persistence &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. help
10. A. school &emsp;&emsp; B. team &emsp;&emsp; C. goals &emsp;&emsp; D. friends

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：听起来容易，但坚持下去并不简单。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。It sounds easy 与后文 staying consistent is not easy 形成对比。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会喘不上气，想要停下来。A 项意为"呼吸"；B 项意为"头脑"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"方式"。out of breath 为固定搭配，意为"上气不接下气"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他已经跑了一年，能轻松跑完五圈。A 项意为"完成"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"错过"。finish five laps 意为"跑完五圈"，与作者只能跑两圈形成对比。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到第三个月，我可以不间断地跑四圈。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"思考"；D 项意为"等待"。without stopping 意为"不停止"，体现耐力进步。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多学生离开了队伍。A 项意为"加入"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"领导"；D 项意为"找到"。left the team 与 Only a few kept running 形成对比，冬天寒冷使很多人放弃。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我们今天能跑，我们就能做到任何事。A 项意为"没有"；B 项意为"任何事"；C 项意为"某事"；D 项意为"每件事"。we can do anything 意为"我们能做任何事"，是常见的励志表达。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长赞扬了我们的坚持。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"天赋"；C 项意为"坚持"；D 项意为"运气"。praised us for our persistence 与全文"坚持晨跑、不放弃"主题一致。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那就是体育真正的精神。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"精神"；C 项意为"结果"；D 项意为"原因"。the real spirit of sports 意为"体育的真正精神"，指为自我而非奖牌而坚持。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我明白了坚持是进步的关键。A 项意为"天赋"；B 项意为"坚持"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"帮助"。persistence 与全文主题一致，是核心关键词。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一步都让我们离目标更近。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"队伍"；C 项意为"目标"；D 项意为"朋友"。closer to our goals 意为"离目标更近"，为常用励志表达。故选 C。
