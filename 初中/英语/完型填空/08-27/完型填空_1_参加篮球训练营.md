# 中考英语完形填空：参加篮球训练营

## 完形填空原文

This summer I joined a basketball training camp. I had always loved watching basketball games on TV, but I had never received professional training. The camp lasted two weeks and was held at our school sports centre.

On the first day, the coach asked us to run five laps around the court as a warm-up. I was out of breath ___1___ the third lap. The coach said, "Basketball needs strong legs and good stamina. You need to build your fitness first." I realised that playing basketball was ___2___ than I thought.

Every morning we practised basic skills: dribbling, passing, and shooting. The coach corrected our posture and movements. "Keep your knees slightly bent," he said. "And ___3___ your eyes on the ball." I used to look at the ground when I dribbled, but the coach said we should look ahead to see our teammates and the basket.

We also learned teamwork. In a real game, you cannot win ___4___. We did many passing exercises. The coach said that a good pass was sometimes more important than a good shot. I learned to pass the ball at the right ___5___ so my teammate could catch it easily.

By the second week, we started to play practice matches. I was assigned to guard an older boy who was much taller than me. At first I felt ___6___, but the coach said, "Use your speed. Don't try to block him directly." I learned to stay close and use quick moves. We lost that match, but I felt I had improved.

On the last day, the camp held a friendly tournament. Our team won two games and lost one. I scored my first basket in a real match! The coach gave each of us a certificate. He said, "Remember, ___7___ makes perfect. Keep practising and you will get better."

The camp was hard work, but I enjoyed every moment. I made new friends and learned to love the sport even more. Now I play basketball at least three times a week. I hope to ___8___ the school team next year. Basketball has taught me that success comes from effort and never giving ___9___. I will always ___10___ the lessons I learned at the camp.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第三圈___我就喘不上气了。A 项意为"在……之后"；B 项意为"在……之前"；C 项意为"在……期间"；D 项意为"直到"。表示第三圈前就喘气，强调体能差，"before"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现打篮球比我想的___。A 项意为"更容易"；B 项意为"更难"；C 项意为"更简单"；D 项意为"更短"。需体能和技巧，比想象难，"harder"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：___你的眼睛盯着球。A 项意为"放"；B 项意为"保持"；C 项意为"拿"；D 项意为"握"。"keep your eyes on"意为"眼睛盯着"，"keep"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：真正比赛里你不能___赢。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"快速"；D 项意为"慢慢"。"cannot win alone"强调团队合作。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了在正确的___传球。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"时刻"；C 项意为"地方"；D 项意为"方式"。"at the right time"强调时机恰当。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我感到___。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"绝望的/没希望的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。面对更高大的对手感到难以应对，"hopeless"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与谚语
**【解析】** 本句意为：记住，___铸就完美。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"天赋"；D 项意为"金钱"。"Practice makes perfect"为固定谚语。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望明年能___校队。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"加入"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"教练"。"join"意为"加入"，符合语境。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：成功来自努力和永不___。A 项"give in"投降；B 项"give up"放弃；C 项"give out"分发；D 项"give off"发出。永不放弃，"up"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会永远___在训练营学到的教训。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"忽视"；D 项意为"想念"。"remember"意为"牢记"，符合语境。故选 B。
