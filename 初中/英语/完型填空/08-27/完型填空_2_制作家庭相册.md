# 中考英语完形填空：制作家庭相册

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, I found a box of old family photos in the attic. They were from when I was a baby and when my parents were young. Many photos were faded and some had turned yellow. I decided to make a family album to ___1___ these memories.

First, I bought a large album with thick pages and some special photo corners. I ___2___ the photos by year and by event. Photos from my first birthday went in one section, and our family trip to the seaside went in another. It took me two whole days to sort them all out.

I also wrote short captions under each photo. For example, under a photo of me learning to walk, I wrote: "My first steps, age one." Under a photo of my grandparents, I wrote: "Grandma and Grandpa's fiftieth anniversary." Writing these ___3___ helped me learn more about my family history. My father told me stories about his childhood as we looked at the old pictures together.

Some photos were torn at the corners. I used tape to ___4___ them carefully. I could not make them look perfect, but at least they would not get worse. I placed each photo gently in the album, making sure not to bend or damage them.

When the album was finished, I showed it to my family. My mother had tears in her eyes. She said, "Thank you for doing this. These photos are so ___5___ to us." My younger brother enjoyed looking at pictures of himself as a toddler. He could not believe he was ever that small!

I made a second album for my grandparents. I chose the best photos of our family gatherings and sent it to them. They called to say how much they ___6___ it. Grandpa said he would look at it every day. That made me feel warm inside.

Making the album taught me that memories are worth ___7___. In this digital age, we take many photos with our phones but often forget to look at them. Printing photos and putting them in an album helps us ___8___ and share our stories. I plan to add new photos to the album every year. One day, I will pass it on to my own children and tell them the stories ___9___ each picture. That will keep our family history ___10___ for generations.

9. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. about &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. except

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定制作家庭相册来___这些回忆。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"保存"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"隐藏"。制作相册旨在保存记忆，"preserve"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我按年份和事件___照片。A 项意为"混合"；B 项意为"整理"；C 项意为"扔掉"；D 项意为"出售"。"organised"意为"整理归类"，符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写这些___让我更了解家族史。A 项意为"信"；B 项意为"说明文字"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"笔记"。前文提到"short captions"，此处指说明文字，"captions"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用胶带小心地___它们。A 项意为"剪"；B 项意为"修复"；C 项意为"上色"；D 项意为"复制"。破损照片需修复，"repair"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些照片对我们来说非常___。A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"珍贵的"；C 项意为"新的"；D 项意为"普通的"。家庭回忆很珍贵，"valuable"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们打电话说非常___它。A 项意为"不喜欢"；B 项意为"喜爱"；C 项意为"忽视"；D 项意为"归还"。祖父母很喜欢相册，"loved"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：制作相册教会我回忆值得___。A 项意为"扔掉"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"保存"；D 项意为"删除"。"worth keeping"意为"值得保存"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把照片洗出来放进相册有助于我们___和分享故事。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"想象"；D 项意为"发明"。相册帮助记录和回忆，"remember"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：告诉她们每张照片___的故事。A 项意为"在……后面"；B 项意为"关于"；C 项意为"没有"；D 项意为"除了"。"stories about each picture"意为"关于每张照片的故事"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这将使家族历史世代___。A 项意为"秘密的"；B 项意为"鲜活的/延续的"；C 项意为"沉默的"；D 项意为"空的"。"keep alive"意为"让……延续下去"。故选 B。
