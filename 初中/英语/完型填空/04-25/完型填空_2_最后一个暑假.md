# 中考英语完形填空：最后一个暑假

## 完形填空原文

This was my last summer before senior high school. I knew everything would ___1___ after the new term started. New school, new friends, new challenges. I wanted to make this summer special.

My best friend, Chen Yu, had the same idea. "Let's do something we'll remember forever," he said one morning. We made a list: climb the small mountain behind our town, learn to cook three dishes, read five books, and ___2___ our grandmothers at least once a week.

The mountain climb was harder than we expected. Halfway up, I wanted to ___3___. Chen Yu said, "We've come this far. Let's finish what we started." His words encouraged me. When we reached the top, the view was amazing. We sat there for an hour, ___4___ nothing, just enjoying the moment.

Visiting our grandmothers became the best part of the week. My grandmother lived alone. She was always ___5___ to see me. She taught me how to make her famous dumplings. "You'll need this when you grow up," she said with a smile. I realized I had been too busy with homework to spend time with her. I felt a little ___6___.

By the end of the summer, we had completed our list. But the real ___7___ was not the list itself. It was the time we spent together, the talks we had, and the memories we created. Chen Yu and I promised to stay friends no matter how far apart we would be.

The last day of summer, we rode our bikes to the river. We threw small stones into the water and watched the ripples spread. "Some friendships are like those ripples," Chen Yu said. "They never really ___8___." I knew what he meant. True friendship lasts.

Now I'm in senior high. Things have changed, but Chen Yu and I still meet when we can. That summer taught me to ___9___ the present. Don't wait for the "right time" to do what matters. The best time is now.

That was my last summer as a junior high student. I will never ___10___ it.

## 选项

1. A. improve &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. disappear &emsp;&emsp; D. return
2. A. call &emsp;&emsp; B. visit &emsp;&emsp; C. thank &emsp;&emsp; D. help
3. A. stay &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. rest &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
4. A. saying &emsp;&emsp; B. doing &emsp;&emsp; C. thinking &emsp;&emsp; D. planning
5. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. sorry
6. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. guilty &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
7. A. goal &emsp;&emsp; B. prize &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. treasure
8. A. grow &emsp;&emsp; B. last &emsp;&emsp; C. end &emsp;&emsp; D. fade
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. cherish &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. understand &emsp;&emsp; D. repeat

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道新学期开始后一切都会___。A 项意为"改善"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"消失"；D 项意为"回来"。升入高中意味着新环境、新同学，生活会"改变"（change）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每周至少___奶奶一次。A 项意为"打电话"；B 项意为"看望"；C 项意为"感谢"；D 项意为"帮助"。后文提到去奶奶家学包饺子，说明是"拜访"（visit）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爬了一半，我想___。A 项意为"留下"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"离开"。爬山很累时想放弃，即想"停下"（stop）不爬了。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在那儿坐了一小时，___什么都不做，只是享受当下。A 项意为"说"；B 项意为"做"；C 项意为"想"；D 项意为"计划"。"saying nothing"表示不说话、安静地待着，与"just enjoying"呼应。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她见到我总很___。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"担忧的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。奶奶见到孙辈来探望会"开心"（happy）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到自己过去太忙于作业，没时间陪她。我有点___。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"内疚的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"放松的"。忽视家人会让人感到"内疚"（guilty）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但真正的___不是清单本身。A 项意为"目标"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"珍宝"。一起度过的时光、彼此的对话和回忆才是真正的"珍宝"（treasure）。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："有些友谊就像那些涟漪，它们从不真正___。"A 项意为"生长"；B 项意为"持续"；C 项意为"结束"；D 项意为"褪色"。"never really end"表示友谊不会真正"结束"（end），与前文"lasts"呼应。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个夏天教会我___当下。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"珍惜"；D 项意为"想念"。珍惜当下是"cherish the present"的常见表达，cherish 意为"珍惜"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会___它。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"理解"；D 项意为"重复"。"never forget"表示这段回忆将永远铭记。故选 A。
