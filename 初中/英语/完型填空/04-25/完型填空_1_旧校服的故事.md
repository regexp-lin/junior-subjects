# 中考英语完形填空：旧校服的故事

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school asked students to donate old uniforms for children in mountain villages. I found my old school jacket in the back of my closet. It was too ___1___ for me now, but the memories it held were still fresh.

I remembered the first day I wore it. I was so nervous that I buttoned it wrong. My desk mate, Xiao Lin, noticed and helped me fix it ___2___ the teacher saw. We became good friends after that.

There was a small ink stain on the sleeve. It happened during a music class when we were learning to write music notes. I was so ___3___ that I knocked over the ink bottle. The stain never came out, but every time I saw it, I smiled.

I put the jacket in a bag and brought it to school. At the donation point, I saw many other students with their old uniforms. A teacher was ___4___ them. "These will mean a lot to the children," she said. "For some families, a school uniform is a luxury."

Her words made me think ___5___. I had always taken my uniforms for granted. I never thought about children who couldn't afford one. Perhaps my old jacket would help a boy or girl feel they ___6___ in at school.

I wrote a short note and put it in the pocket: "This jacket brought me good luck. I hope it brings you the same." I didn't know who would receive it, but I hoped they would feel a little warmth from a stranger.

A few weeks later, the school received thank-you letters from the village school. One letter was from a girl named Xiaomei. She wrote, "I got a jacket with an ink stain on the sleeve. There was a note in the pocket. Thank you for making me feel that someone ___7___." My heart warmed when I read it.

Sometimes we don't realize how much a small thing can ___8___. An old jacket that we no longer need might be a treasure to someone else. I learned that day that giving is not about how much we give, but about the ___9___ behind it.

My old school jacket is ___10___ now, but its story continues in a small village far away. And that makes me happy.

1. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. big &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. new
2. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. until
3. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. careless &emsp;&emsp; C. patient &emsp;&emsp; D. brave

7. A. cares &emsp;&emsp; B. forgets &emsp;&emsp; C. leaves &emsp;&emsp; D. loses
8. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. cost &emsp;&emsp; C. mean &emsp;&emsp; D. spend
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. heart &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
10. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. gone &emsp;&emsp; C. broken &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在对我来说太___了。A 项意为"小的"；B 项意为"大的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"新的"。校服放在衣柜后面，说明是旧的，现在穿不下是因为长大了，衣服变"小"（small）了。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小林注意到并帮我在老师发现___修正好了。A 项意为"在……之前"；B 项意为"在……之后"；C 项意为"当……时"；D 项意为"直到"。"before the teacher saw"表示抢在老师看见之前改好，避免尴尬。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我当时太___了，打翻了墨水瓶。A 项意为"小心的"；B 项意为"粗心的"；C 项意为"耐心的"；D 项意为"勇敢的"。打翻墨水导致袖口有污渍，是因为"粗心"（careless）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位老师正在___它们。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"收集"；D 项意为"洗"。捐赠点老师负责"收集"（collecting）学生们捐的校服。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话让我___思考。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"仔细地"；C 项意为"开心地"；D 项意为"伤心地"。意识到山区孩子可能买不起校服，会让人"认真/仔细"（carefully）反思。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：或许我的旧外套能帮一个男孩或女孩感到他们能在学校___。A 项意为"适合"；B 项意为"属于"；C 项意为"停留"；D 项意为"玩"。"fit in"为固定搭配，表示"融入、合群"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：谢谢你让我感到有人___。A 项意为"在乎"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"失去"。口袋里的字条让收到衣服的孩子感到陌生人也在"关心"（cares）她。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们意识不到一件小事能___多少。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"花费"；C 项意为"意味着"；D 项意为"花费（时间）"。"how much...can mean"表示"能意味着多少"，强调小事的价值。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：给予不在于我们给了多少，而在于背后的___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"心意"；D 项意为"礼物"。与"how much"对比，强调"心意"（heart）比数量重要。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的旧校服现在___了，但它的故事在远方的小村庄继续。A 项意为"丢失的"；B 项意为"不在了/已送出"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"脏的"。"gone"表示已捐出、不在身边，但故事在延续。故选 B。
