# 中考英语完形填空：班级合唱团排练

## 完形填空原文

Our school would hold a choir competition in one month. Our class decided to take part in it. Every Tuesday and Thursday after school, we went to the hall to ___1___. At first, it was not easy. Some of us sang too loudly while others sang too ___2___. Our voices didn't sound good together.

Mr. Wang, our music teacher, told us that a choir was like a team. "Everyone must ___3___ to the same rhythm," he said. "Listen to each other, not just yourself." We practiced the song many times. Little by little, we began to sing in ___4___. It was wonderful to hear our voices blend together.

One girl named Lily had a very sweet voice. She often sang the solo part. The rest of us sang the chorus. We had to remember when to come in and when to ___5___. Sometimes we made mistakes and had to start again. But no one complained. We all knew that practice makes ___6___.

The day before the competition, we had a final rehearsal. We wore our white shirts and dark trousers, just like the real performance. Mr. Wang said we were ___7___ than before. "Have confidence in yourselves," he encouraged us. "You've worked so hard. Just enjoy the ___8___ on stage."

When the competition day came, we stood on the stage and sang our hearts out. The audience clapped loudly when we finished. We didn't win first place, but we were still very ___9___. We had learned to work as one and to support each other. That was the real ___10___ of being in a choir. I will always remember those rehearsal days with my classmates.

## 选项

1. A. play &emsp;&emsp; B. practice &emsp;&emsp; C. rest &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
2. A. softly &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. clearly
3. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. jump &emsp;&emsp; C. keep &emsp;&emsp; D. listen
4. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. silence &emsp;&emsp; C. harmony &emsp;&emsp; D. peace
5. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. start &emsp;&emsp; C. sit &emsp;&emsp; D. stand
6. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. good
7. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. taller &emsp;&emsp; D. shorter
8. A. moment &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. exam
9. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
10. A. reason &emsp;&emsp; B. result &emsp;&emsp; C. meaning &emsp;&emsp; D. way

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每周二、周四放学后，我们去大厅练习。A 项意为"玩"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"等待"。合唱团去大厅是为了排练，practice 符合。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些人唱得太大声，有些人唱得太轻。A 项意为"轻柔地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"清晰地"。与 too loudly 形成对比，too softly 表示"太轻、太柔"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都必须跟上同样的节奏。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"听"。keep to 为固定搭配，意为"遵守、跟上"。keep to the same rhythm 指跟上同一节奏。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：渐渐地，我们开始和谐地唱在一起。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"安静"；C 项意为"和谐"；D 项意为"和平"。sing in harmony 指"和谐地合唱"，与合唱团主题一致。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们得记住什么时候进、什么时候停。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"坐"；D 项意为"站"。when to come in 与 when to stop 对应，表示进与停的时机。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：但没人抱怨，因为我们都知道熟能生巧。A 项意为"完美的"；B 项意为"更好的"；C 项意为"最好的"；D 项意为"好的"。practice makes perfect 为固定谚语，意为"熟能生巧"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师说我们比以前好了。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更高"；D 项意为"更矮"。经过练习，表现进步用 better。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在舞台上尽情享受那一刻吧。A 项意为"时刻"；B 项意为"课程"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"考试"。enjoy the moment 指享受表演的当下。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们没有得第一名，但仍然非常自豪。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。付出努力并学会合作，即使未夺冠也可感到自豪。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这才是参加合唱团的真谛所在。A 项意为"理由"；B 项意为"结果"；C 项意为"意义"；D 项意为"方式"。the real way of being in a choir 指参加合唱团的真正方式或本质体验。故选 D。
