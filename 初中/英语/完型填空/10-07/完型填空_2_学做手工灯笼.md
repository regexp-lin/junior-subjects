# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工灯笼

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, our art teacher taught us how to make paper lanterns. It was my first time trying this traditional craft, and I was both ___1___ and a little nervous.

First, we needed to prepare the materials: colored paper, scissors, glue, and a string. The teacher showed us step by step. She took a piece of red paper and ___2___ it in half. Then she cut many thin lines along the folded edge, but she was careful not to cut all the way through. "This is the most important part," she said. "If you cut too deep, the paper will ___3___ apart."

I followed her instructions carefully. My hands were not very steady at first, and I made a small ___4___. But the teacher said it was okay and encouraged me to try again. I took another piece of paper and did it more slowly. This time it worked!

Next, we rolled the cut paper into a cylinder shape and ___5___ the two ends together with glue. The paper lantern began to take shape. I chose a yellow string to hang it with. When I held it up, the lantern looked ___6___ — like a real one you would see during the Lantern Festival.

Our teacher said that making lanterns by hand had been a ___7___ in China for hundreds of years. In the past, people made them to bring good luck. Today, we still make them for festivals and for fun.

I brought my lantern home and hung it above my desk. When I turned on the light, the ___8___ from inside made the lantern glow softly. I felt proud of what I had made with my own hands. My little sister saw it and asked me to teach her. I was happy to ___9___ my new skill with her.

Making things by hand takes time and patience, but the ___10___ of creating something beautiful is worth it. I can't wait to try making more crafts!

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我第一次尝试这项传统手艺，我既兴奋又有点紧张。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"生气的"。与 nervous 形成对比，第一次尝试会 excited。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她拿起一张红纸，对折。A 项意为"扔"；B 项意为"折叠"；C 项意为"撕"；D 项意为"掉落"。折纸第一步是 fold 纸。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你剪得太深，纸会散开。A 项 come apart 意为"分开"；B 项 fall apart 意为"散开、破裂"；C 项 run 一般不接 apart；D 项 take apart 意为"拆开"。纸剪太深会 fall apart。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的手不太稳，我犯了个小错。A 项意为"决定"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"选择"。与 try again 呼应，made a mistake。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把剪好的纸卷成圆筒形，用胶水把两端粘在一起。A 项意为"连接"；B 项意为"分开"；C 项意为"比较"；D 项意为"分割"。做灯笼需 connect the ends。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我举起来时，灯笼看起来很漂亮。A 项意为"丑陋的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"漂亮的"；D 项意为"普通的"。与 like a real one 呼应，做成功应 beautiful。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说过，手工做灯笼在中国已有数百年传统。A 项意为"课"；B 项意为"传统"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"运动"。for hundreds of years 说明是 tradition。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我把灯打开时，里面的光让灯笼柔柔地亮起来。A 项意为"烟"；B 项意为"风"；C 项意为"光"；D 项意为"热"。灯发出的光使灯笼 glow。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹看到了，让我教她。我很乐意和她分享我的新技能。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"拒绝"。教别人即 share the skill。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工制作需要时间和耐心，但创造美好事物的快乐是值得的。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"成本"；C 项意为"快乐"；D 项意为"危险"。creating something beautiful 带来 joy。故选 C。
