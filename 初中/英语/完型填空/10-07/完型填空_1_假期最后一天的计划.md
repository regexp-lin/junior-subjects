# 中考英语完形填空：假期最后一天的计划

## 完形填空原文

Tomorrow will be the last day of my winter holiday. School starts the day after. I want to make full use of this special day and have a good ___1___ before I go back to class.

In the morning, I plan to wake up early and go for a jog in the park. Exercise helps me feel ___2___ and ready for the new term. After that, I will have a big breakfast with my family. Mother said she would make my favorite pancakes.

In the afternoon, I have two things to do. First, I need to finish ___3___ my schoolbag and check that all my books and stationery are ready. I don't want to forget anything on the first day. ___4___, I will meet my best friend Li Ming at the library. We want to borrow some new books together and chat about our holiday.

In the evening, our family will have dinner together. Father suggested we go out to a restaurant to ___5___ the end of the holiday. I think that's a great idea. After dinner, I will set my alarm clock and go to bed ___6___ so that I can get enough sleep for the first school day.

I know some of my classmates feel ___7___ when the holiday is about to end. But I try to think ___8___ — the new term means new lessons, new friends, and new chances to learn. I am actually looking forward to seeing my teachers and classmates again.

This last day of the holiday will be both relaxing and ___9___. I hope everything goes well and I can start the new term with a smile on my ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. game &emsp;&emsp; C. meal &emsp;&emsp; D. trip
2. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. fresh &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
3. A. packing &emsp;&emsp; B. opening &emsp;&emsp; C. buying &emsp;&emsp; D. losing
4. A. Besides &emsp;&emsp; B. However &emsp;&emsp; C. Instead &emsp;&emsp; D. Second
5. A. celebrate &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
6. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. early &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
7. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. sad &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
8. A. badly &emsp;&emsp; B. differently &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. clearly
9. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. useless &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
10. A. hands &emsp;&emsp; B. back &emsp;&emsp; C. face &emsp;&emsp; D. legs

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想充分利用这特别的一天，在回学校上课前好好休息一下。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"一餐"；D 项意为"旅行"。假期最后一天希望 have a good rest。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：运动帮助我感到精神焕发，为新学期做好准备。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"精神焕发的"；D 项意为"孤单的"。运动后 feel fresh 意为感觉精神。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我需要收拾好书包，检查所有书本和文具是否齐全。A 项意为"收拾、打包"；B 项意为"打开"；C 项意为"购买"；D 项意为"丢失"。finish packing schoolbag 意为"收拾好书包"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 连接词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：其次，我会在图书馆和好朋友李明见面。A 项意为"此外"；B 项意为"然而"；C 项意为"相反"；D 项意为"第二、其次"。与 First 对应，用 Second。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸建议我们出去下馆子庆祝假期结束。A 项意为"庆祝"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"想念、错过"。celebrate the end of the holiday 意为"庆祝假期结束"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：晚饭后，我会设好闹钟，早点睡觉。A 项意为"晚"；B 项意为"早"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。为保证第二天精神，需 go to bed early。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道有些同学在假期即将结束时感到难过。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"难过的"；D 项意为"放松的"。假期结束通常令人感到难过。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我努力换个角度思考。A 项意为"糟糕地"；B 项意为"不同地"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"清楚地"。think differently 意为"换个角度想"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：假期的最后一天既放松又有意义。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"无用的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"困难的"。与 relaxing 并列，meaningful 符合积极含义。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望一切顺利，能面带微笑开始新学期。A 项意为"手"；B 项意为"背"；C 项意为"脸"；D 项意为"腿"。a smile on one's face 意为"脸上带着微笑"。故选 C。
