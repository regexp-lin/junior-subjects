# 中考英语完形填空：爷爷的钓鱼竿

## 完形填空原文

Every summer, twelve-year-old Chen Yu visited his grandfather in the countryside. His grandfather was a quiet old man who loved fishing. He had an old bamboo fishing rod that he had used for over thirty ___1___.

One morning, Grandpa woke Chen Yu up very early. "Let's go fishing," he said with a warm smile. Chen Yu rubbed his sleepy eyes but ___2___ got out of bed. They walked along a narrow path to a small river behind the village.

Grandpa showed Chen Yu how to hold the rod and throw the line into the water. "Fishing needs ___3___," Grandpa said. "You can't rush it." Chen Yu nodded, but after twenty minutes of waiting, he started to feel ___4___. Nothing was biting. He wanted to give up and go home to play video games.

"Grandpa, this is so boring. There are no fish here," Chen Yu ___5___.

Grandpa laughed softly. "The fish are there. They're just not ready yet. In life, many good things take time. If you leave now, you'll never know what you ___6___."

Chen Yu looked at his grandfather's calm face and decided to stay. He sat quietly, watching the water and ___7___ to the birds singing in the trees. Slowly, he began to feel peaceful.

Suddenly, the line moved! Chen Yu's heart jumped. Grandpa helped him pull the rod carefully. A beautiful silver fish came out of the ___8___. It was not very big, but Chen Yu was so excited that he shouted with joy.

"See?" Grandpa said, putting his hand on Chen Yu's shoulder. "Good things come to those who ___9___."

That evening, they cooked the fish together. It was the most delicious meal Chen Yu had ever had — not because of the fish itself, but because of the ___10___ he shared with his grandfather that day.

## 选项

1. A. days &emsp;&emsp; B. weeks &emsp;&emsp; C. months &emsp;&emsp; D. years
2. A. sadly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. still &emsp;&emsp; D. never
3. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. speed
4. A. hungry &emsp;&emsp; B. scared &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
5. A. agreed &emsp;&emsp; B. explained &emsp;&emsp; C. complained &emsp;&emsp; D. promised
6. A. missed &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. broke &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
7. A. talking &emsp;&emsp; B. listening &emsp;&emsp; C. running &emsp;&emsp; D. pointing
8. A. sky &emsp;&emsp; B. ground &emsp;&emsp; C. water &emsp;&emsp; D. tree
9. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. fight &emsp;&emsp; D. pay
10. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. secret &emsp;&emsp; D. memory

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他有一根用了三十多___的旧竹制钓鱼竿。A 项意为"天"；B 项意为"周"；C 项意为"月"；D 项意为"年"。一根钓鱼竿用了三十多天/周/月都不足以称之为"旧"，用了三十多"年"（years）才合理，也体现了爷爷对这根鱼竿的珍视。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈宇揉了揉睡意朦胧的眼睛，但___起了床。A 项意为"伤心地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"仍然/还是"；D 项意为"从不"。虽然很困，但"还是"（still）起了床，说明他愿意陪爷爷去钓鱼。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："钓鱼需要___，"爷爷说。"你不能急。"A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"速度"。后面紧接着说"You can't rush it"（不能急），与"耐心"（patience）完美对应。这也是全文的中心主旨。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等了二十分钟后，他开始感到___。A 项意为"饿的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"冷的"。没有鱼上钩，等了很久，后文他说"this is so boring"，因此他是感到"无聊"（bored）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："爷爷，太无聊了。这里没有鱼，"陈宇___道。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"解释"；C 项意为"抱怨"；D 项意为"承诺"。他说太无聊了、没有鱼，这是在表达不满，是"抱怨"（complained）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："如果你现在离开，你永远不会知道你___了什么。"A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"打破"；D 项意为"丢失"。爷爷在劝孙子坚持，如果放弃离开，就会"错过"（missed）好的结果。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他安静地坐着，看着水面，___着树上鸟儿的歌唱。A 项意为"说话"；B 项意为"听"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"指"。他静静地坐在河边，除了看水面，还在"倾听"（listening）鸟鸣声，营造出宁静的氛围。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一条漂亮的银色鱼从___中出来。A 项意为"天空"；B 项意为"地面"；C 项意为"水"；D 项意为"树"。他们在河边钓鱼，鱼自然是从"水"（water）里钓出来的。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与谚语理解
**【解析】** 本句意为："看到了吗？"爷爷说。"好事属于那些___的人。"A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"战斗"；D 项意为"付钱"。这是英语谚语 "Good things come to those who wait"（好事降临于有耐心等待的人），呼应了全文"耐心"的主题。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不是因为鱼本身，而是因为他那天与爷爷分享的___。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"秘密"；D 项意为"回忆"。那顿饭之所以美味，不是因为鱼，而是因为那天和爷爷一起钓鱼、做饭的美好"回忆"（memory）。故选 D。
