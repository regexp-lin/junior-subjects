# 中考英语完形填空：停电夜的蜡烛

## 完形填空原文

It was a Friday evening. I was doing my homework when suddenly all the lights went out. The whole neighborhood was in ___1___. My mom brought out candles and placed them on the table.

"We haven't had a power outage in years," she said. "Maybe it's a good chance to talk."

At first I thought it would be boring. Without the TV or computer, what could we do? But Mom had a different ___2___. She started telling stories about her childhood — how she and her sisters used to play hide-and-seek in the dark, and how her father would tell ghost stories by candlelight.

I had never heard these stories before. Mom was always busy. We usually ate dinner in ___3___ while watching our phones. That night, with only candlelight, everything felt different. We could see each other's faces clearly. We could hear each other without any ___4___.

Dad joined us too. He told us about his first job and how he met Mom. I learned that they had known each other since high school. "We used to write letters," Mom said with a smile. "No phones, no internet. Just pen and paper."

I realized how much I had been ___5___. I thought I knew my parents, but I didn't. I knew their rules and their worries, but not their stories. That candlelit night ___6___ me that the best moments sometimes happen when we slow down and really listen.

When the power came back two hours later, I felt a little ___7___. The bright lights made everything ordinary again. But I had learned something important: we don't always need more — sometimes we need less. Less noise, less speed, less distraction. A simple ___8___ can bring a family together in a way that nothing else can.

The next evening, I suggested we have a "no-screen hour" before dinner. Mom and Dad agreed. We didn't need a power outage to ___9___ that. We could choose to create those moments ourselves. Sometimes the darkness doesn't hide things — it helps us see what truly ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. light &emsp;&emsp; B. silence &emsp;&emsp; C. darkness &emsp;&emsp; D. peace
2. A. idea &emsp;&emsp; B. plan &emsp;&emsp; C. way &emsp;&emsp; D. mind
3. A. happiness &emsp;&emsp; B. sadness &emsp;&emsp; C. silence &emsp;&emsp; D. excitement
4. A. music &emsp;&emsp; B. noise &emsp;&emsp; C. voice &emsp;&emsp; D. sound
5. A. missing &emsp;&emsp; B. losing &emsp;&emsp; C. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; D. avoiding
6. A. told &emsp;&emsp; B. showed &emsp;&emsp; C. taught &emsp;&emsp; D. asked
7. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. pleased &emsp;&emsp; C. surprised &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointed
8. A. candle &emsp;&emsp; B. meal &emsp;&emsp; C. moment &emsp;&emsp; D. story
9. A. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; B. discover &emsp;&emsp; C. remember &emsp;&emsp; D. achieve
10. A. matters &emsp;&emsp; B. works &emsp;&emsp; C. happens &emsp;&emsp; D. appears

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：整个小区陷入___。A 项意为"光亮"；B 项意为"寂静"；C 项意为"黑暗"；D 项意为"和平"。停电后应是一片"黑暗"（darkness）。"in darkness"意为在黑暗中。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但妈妈有不一样的___。A 项意为"想法"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"方式"；D 项意为"头脑"。"have a different idea"（有不同的想法）表示妈妈对停电之夜的看法与"我"不同。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们通常一边看手机一边___地吃饭。A 项意为"快乐"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"沉默"；D 项意为"兴奋"。"in silence"（沉默地）表示各自看手机、很少交流的状态。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们能听见彼此，没有任何___。A 项意为"音乐"；B 项意为"噪音"；C 项意为"声音"；D 项意为"声响"。"without any noise"（没有任何噪音）指没有电视、手机等的干扰。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到自己一直___了多少。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。"missing"指错过了许多父母的故事和相处时光。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个烛光之夜___了我。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"展示"；C 项意为"教会"；D 项意为"问"。"taught"（教会）与后文"the best moments...when we slow down"相呼应，是一次领悟。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：来电后我有点___。A 项意为"兴奋"；B 项意为"高兴"；C 项意为"惊讶"；D 项意为"失望"。"disappointed"（失望）与"bright lights made everything ordinary again"相呼应，享受过烛光夜后反而舍不得结束。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一支简单的___能以别的东西做不到的方式把一家人聚在一起。A 项意为"蜡烛"；B 项意为"饭菜"；C 项意为"时刻"；D 项意为"故事"。全文围绕停电夜与蜡烛展开，强调烛光的特殊作用。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不需要停电也能___那样（的时光）。A 项意为"享受"；B 项意为"发现"；C 项意为"记住"；D 项意为"实现、达到"。"achieve that"指主动创造出那样的温馨时刻。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时黑暗不是遮蔽，而是帮助我们看清什么真正___。A 项意为"重要"；B 项意为"起作用"；C 项意为"发生"；D 项意为"出现"。"what truly matters"（什么才真正重要）是常用表达，指家人相处、真心倾听。故选 A。
