# 中考英语完形填空：画展上的无名画

## 完形填空原文

Our school art club held a painting exhibition in the hall. Students could display their works. I had painted a landscape of the mountains near my hometown, but I didn't sign my name. I was too ___1___ to put my name on it — what if people didn't like it?

On the opening day, I walked around and looked at other students' paintings. They were ___2___ — colorful, creative, and full of life. My painting seemed plain in comparison. I stood in a corner and watched people pass by. Most of them didn't stop at my work.

Then I noticed an old man standing in front of my painting for a long time. He had gray hair and wore simple clothes. He ___3___ his head and said to the teacher beside him, "This one is special. The artist understands the mountains. You can feel the peace in it."

My heart beat faster. The teacher asked, "Do you know who painted it? There's no name."

The old man smiled. "It doesn't ___4___. True art speaks for itself. The person who painted this has a gift. I hope they never hide it."

I wanted to step forward and say, "It's me!" But my legs wouldn't move. I was afraid. What if he changed his mind when he saw me? What if I ___5___ him?

The old man left. I ran after him and finally caught up at the gate. "Sir," I said, breathing hard, "I painted that picture. Thank you for your kind words."

He looked at me with warm eyes. "Young friend, never be afraid to ___6___ your work. Your painting has its own voice. Let it be heard."

That day I learned an important lesson. We often ___7___ ourselves too much. We worry about what others think and hide our talents. But the world needs to see what we can do. Our creations, like that mountain landscape, have value — not because everyone likes them, but because they come from our ___8___.

Back at the exhibition, I found a pen and wrote my name in the corner of my painting. It was a small step, but it ___9___ a big change in how I saw myself. I was no longer the shy girl who hid in the corner. I was an artist, ready to share her ___10___ with the world.

2. A. awful &emsp;&emsp; B. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. strange

9. A. made &emsp;&emsp; B. meant &emsp;&emsp; C. brought &emsp;&emsp; D. showed
10. A. dreams &emsp;&emsp; B. gifts &emsp;&emsp; C. secrets &emsp;&emsp; D. worries

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，不敢把名字写在上面。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"害羞的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"勇敢的"。"shy"与后文"what if people didn't like it"（别人不喜欢怎么办）相呼应，表现缺乏自信。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们很___。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"精彩的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。"colorful, creative, and full of life"（色彩丰富、有创意、充满生机）说明其他作品很"精彩"（wonderful）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他___了点头，对旁边的老师说。A 项意为"摇"；B 项意为"点"；C 项意为"转"；D 项意为"低下"。"nod one's head"（点头）表示赞同或认可，老人认可这幅画。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：那不___。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"要紧"；C 项意为"知道"；D 项意为"在意"。"it doesn't matter"（无所谓、不重要）是固定搭配，老人说不必知道作者是谁。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果他见到我，我会___他怎么办？A 项意为"感谢"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"使失望"；D 项意为"使高兴"。"disappoint"（使失望）与前文"changed his mind"（改变想法）呼应，担心本人不如画作让人满意。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：永远不要害怕___你的作品。A 项意为"抄袭"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"隐藏"。老人鼓励"我"大胆"分享"（share）作品，与后文"Let it be heard"相呼应。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们常常太___自己了。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"信任"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"爱"。"doubt ourselves"（怀疑自己）与"worry about what others think"（在乎别人看法）相呼应。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为它们来自我们的___。A 项意为"手"；B 项意为"心"；C 项意为"头"；D 项意为"眼"。"come from our hearts"（发自内心）表示作品承载真情，有独特价值。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这只是小小的一步，但它___了巨大的改变。A 项意为"制造"；B 项意为"意味着"；C 项意为"带来"；D 项意为"展示"。"meant"（意味着）表示署名这一步象征着对自我认知的大转变。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我是一名艺术家，准备好与世界分享她的___。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"才华、礼物"；C 项意为"秘密"；D 项意为"担忧"。"gifts"（天赋、才华）与前文"has a gift"呼应，指艺术才能。故选 B。
