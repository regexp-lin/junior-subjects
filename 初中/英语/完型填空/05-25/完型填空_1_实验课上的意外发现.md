# 中考英语完形填空：实验课上的意外发现

## 完形填空原文

In our science class last week, we did an experiment about plants. Each group was given a bean seed and a small pot. We were told to plant the seed, water it every day, and ___1___ its growth. I thought it would be boring, but I was wrong.

My partner and I ___2___ our seed carefully. We put it in the soil, gave it enough water, and placed the pot by the window where it could get sunlight. For the first few days, nothing seemed to happen. I almost ___3___ that we had done something wrong.

Then one morning, I noticed a tiny green ___4___ pushing through the soil. I was so excited that I called my partner over. We watched it every day. The stem grew taller, and small leaves began to ___5___. It was amazing to see how a small seed could turn into a living plant.

Our teacher said that every scientist had to learn ___6___. "You cannot hurry nature," she told us. "Some things need time to grow." Her words stayed with me. I realized that learning was like growing a plant. We needed to be ___7___ and keep trying, even when we didn't see results immediately.

That simple experiment taught me more than any textbook. I learned to ___8___ the small changes in life. I learned that ___9___ and care could bring about beautiful results. Most importantly, I learned that the best discoveries often come from ___10___ the ordinary things around us with curiosity.

9. A. hurry &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. worry
10. A. changing &emsp;&emsp; B. throwing &emsp;&emsp; C. observing &emsp;&emsp; D. destroying

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们被告知要种下种子，每天浇水，并记录它的生长情况。record 意为"记录"。A项"记录"；B项"忘记"；C项"停止"；D项"错过"。record growth 为实验常用表达。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和搭档认真地种下了我们的种子。plant 意为"种植"。A项"吃"；B项"种植"；C项"丢失"；D项"找到"。planted 与 experiment about plants 一致。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我几乎怀疑我们哪里做错了。doubt 意为"怀疑"。A项"希望"；B项"怀疑"；C项"相信"；D项"同意"。doubted 与 nothing seemed to happen 呼应。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我注意到一根细小的绿色茎从土壤中钻出。stem 意为"茎"，豆芽最初长出的是茎。A项"花"；B项"叶"；C项"茎"；D项"根"。stem 与后文 The stem grew taller 呼应。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：茎越长越高，小小的叶子开始出现。appear 意为"出现"。A项"掉落"；B项"出现"；C项"枯萎"；D项"消失"。appear 描述植物生长过程。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说得每个科学家都要学会耐心。learn patience 意为"学会耐心"。A项"快速地"（副词）；B项"耐心"；C项"运气"；D项"速度"。patience 与后文 cannot hurry nature 呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们需要有耐心并坚持尝试，即使不能立即看到结果。patient 意为"有耐心的"。A项"有耐心的"；B项"懒惰的"；C项"生气的"；D项"紧张的"。be patient 与 learn patience 呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了关注生活中的小变化。notice 意为"注意到、关注"。A项"忽略"；B项"注意到"；C项"避免"；D项"忘记"。notice the small changes 与实验观察一致。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了耐心和用心能带来美好的结果。patience 与 care 并列。A项"匆忙"；B项"耐心"；C项"恐惧"；D项"担忧"。patience and care 呼应前文。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最重要的是，我学到最好的发现往往来自以好奇心观察身边的平凡事物。observe 意为"观察"。A项"改变"；B项"扔掉"；C项"观察"；D项"破坏"。observing...with curiosity 与科学发现主题一致。故选 C。
