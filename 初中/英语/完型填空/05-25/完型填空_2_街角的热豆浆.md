# 中考英语完形填空：街角的热豆浆

## 完形填空原文

There is a small shop at the corner of our street. Every morning, an elderly couple sells fresh soy milk there. I pass by the shop on my way to school, but I had never stopped to buy anything until one cold winter day.

That morning, I left home in a ___1___ and forgot to eat breakfast. By the time I reached the street corner, I was shaking with cold and hunger. The old lady noticed me and called out, "Young one, come and have something ___2___." I walked over, a little shy. She poured a cup of hot soy milk and handed it to me. " ___3___ it. It's on the house today."

I took the cup with both hands. The warmth spread through my fingers and reached my heart. That simple cup of soy milk ___4___ more than any breakfast I had ever had. I thanked her again and again. She just smiled and said, "When you grow up, remember to show ___5___ to others who need it."

Since then, I have become a ___6___ customer. Every time I pass by, I buy a cup of soy milk. But more importantly, I have never forgotten her words. Last month, when I saw a lost child crying at the bus station, I ___7___ to help him find his mother. The kindness I received that winter morning was like a seed. It has grown in my heart and ___8___ me to pass it on.

That small shop at the corner doesn't sell expensive things. But it sells something far more valuable—___9___ and warmth. In our busy lives, we often forget the power of a simple act of kindness. A cup of hot soy milk on a cold morning can ___10___ someone's whole day. I will always be grateful for that winter morning and the lesson it taught me.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天早上我匆匆出门，忘了吃早饭。in a hurry 意为"匆忙地"。A项"匆忙"；B项"公交车"；C项"汽车"；D项"路"。in a hurry 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老太太招呼我："孩子，来喝点热的。"hot 与后文 hot soy milk 及 cold winter 呼应。A项"甜的"；B项"热的"；C项"冷的"；D项"苦的"。something hot 在寒冷冬日最合适。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：喝吧，今天免费。Drink 为祈使句，直接叫对方喝。A项"买"；B项"喝"；C项"拿"；D项"做"。Drink it 与 handing the cup 一致。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那杯简单的豆浆对我意义重大，胜过任何一顿早餐。mean 意为"意味着、对……有意义"。A项"花费"；B项"意味、有意义"；C项"支付"；D项"出售"。meant more than 表示更有价值。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等你长大了，记得对那些需要帮助的人施以善意。kindness 意为"善意、善良"。A项"善意"；B项"金钱"；C项"礼物"；D项"东西"。show kindness to 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自那以后，我成了常客。regular 意为"经常的、固定的"。A项"新的"；B项"经常的"；C项"富有的"；D项"年轻的"。regular customer 意为"老主顾"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我看到一个迷路的孩子在公交站哭时，我停下来帮他找妈妈。stop to do 意为"停下来去做某事"。A项"拒绝"；B项"忘记"；C项"停下"；D项"希望"。stopped to help 表示主动相助。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它在我心中生根，鼓励我将其传递下去。encourage 意为"鼓励"。A项"允许"；B项"鼓励"；C项"强迫"；D项"警告"。encouraged me to pass it on 表示善意传递。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但它卖的 something far more valuable—善意与温暖。kindness 与 warmth 并列。A项"食物"；B项"善意"；C项"牛奶"；D项"金钱"。kindness and warmth 点明主题。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：寒冷早晨的一杯热豆浆能改变某人整整一天。change 意为"改变"。A项"打破"；B项"浪费"；C项"改变"；D项"拯救"。change someone's whole day 表示带来积极影响。故选 C。
