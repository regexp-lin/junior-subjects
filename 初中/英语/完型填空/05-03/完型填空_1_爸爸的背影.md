# 中考英语完形填空：爸爸的背影

## 完形填空原文

Every morning, my father walks me to the bus stop. It's only a five-minute walk, but he has done it every single day since I started middle school. "I'm not a little kid anymore," I once ___1___, but he just smiled and said, "Humor me."

My father is a quiet man. He doesn't say much, and he rarely shows his ___2___. While other dads hug their children or say "I love you," mine simply nods and says, "Study hard." For a long time, I thought he didn't care.

One rainy Tuesday, I forgot my umbrella. By the time the bus arrived, I was completely ___3___. When I got home that evening, Dad was sitting at the table, looking guilty. "I'm sorry," he said. "I should have ___4___ you to bring it."

"It's fine, Dad," I said. "It's just rain."

But the next morning, I found a small umbrella in my school bag. There was a note stuck to it: "Just in case." The handwriting was shaky and ___5___, and I realized it had taken him a lot of effort to write those three words in English.

That small act made me ___6___ all the other things he had done quietly. Every night, he set his alarm for 5:30 a.m. to make me breakfast. Every Sunday, he ___7___ my shoes until they shone. When I was sick, he stayed up all night checking my temperature.

I began to watch him more ___8___ at the bus stop each morning. After I got on the bus, he didn't leave right away. He stood there, watching the bus drive away, his hands in his pockets, until it disappeared around the corner. Then he would ___9___ turn and walk home alone.

One morning, from the bus window, I saw his hair was greyer than I remembered. His shoulders were slightly bent. He looked older and ___10___. My eyes filled with tears.

That afternoon, when he met me at the bus stop, I did something I hadn't done in years. I gave him a hug. He stiffened for a moment, then slowly put his arms around me. Neither of us said a word. We didn't need to.

1. A. complained &emsp;&emsp; B. agreed &emsp;&emsp; C. explained &emsp;&emsp; D. suggested
2. A. talent &emsp;&emsp; B. feelings &emsp;&emsp; C. plans &emsp;&emsp; D. secrets
3. A. wet &emsp;&emsp; B. cold &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. late
4. A. taught &emsp;&emsp; B. reminded &emsp;&emsp; C. allowed &emsp;&emsp; D. helped
5. A. neat &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. unclear &emsp;&emsp; D. large
6. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. notice &emsp;&emsp; C. doubt &emsp;&emsp; D. copy
7. A. washed &emsp;&emsp; B. polished &emsp;&emsp; C. bought &emsp;&emsp; D. chose
8. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. angrily &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. happily
9. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. suddenly
10. A. taller &emsp;&emsp; B. stronger &emsp;&emsp; C. smaller &emsp;&emsp; D. heavier

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不再是小孩了，我曾经___。A 项意为"抱怨"；B 项意为"同意"；C 项意为"解释"；D 项意为"建议"。"我"嫌爸爸还送自己到车站，带着不满的语气"抱怨"（complained）。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他很少表达自己的___。A 项意为"天赋"；B 项意为"感情"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"秘密"。后文对比别的爸爸会拥抱、说"我爱你"，而"我"爸爸很少表露"感情"（feelings）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等公交到的时候，我完全___了。A 项意为"湿透的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"迷路的"；D 项意为"迟到的"。下雨天忘带伞，自然是淋得"湿透了"（wet）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我应该___你带伞的。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"提醒"；C 项意为"允许"；D 项意为"帮助"。爸爸自责没有"提醒"（reminded）孩子带伞，体现他的细心和关爱。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：字迹颤抖且___。A 项意为"整洁的"；B 项意为"仔细的"；C 项意为"不清楚的"；D 项意为"大的"。后文说爸爸写那三个英文单词花了很大力气，说明他不擅长英文，字迹"不太清楚"（unclear）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个小小的举动让我___了他默默做的其他事情。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"注意到"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"模仿"。从小纸条开始，"我"开始"留意到"（notice）爸爸一直默默付出的种种。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个周日，他把我的鞋___得发亮。A 项意为"洗"；B 项意为"擦亮"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"选择"。"until they shone"（直到发亮），"擦亮"（polished）鞋子使其发亮。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始每天早上更___地观察他。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"高兴地"。开始关注爸爸的一举一动，"仔细地"（carefully）观察他在车站的行为。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后他会___转身独自走回家。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"高兴地"；D 项意为"突然地"。目送公交车消失后，爸爸"慢慢地"（slowly）转身，流露出不舍和寂寞。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：他看起来更老了，也更___了。A 项意为"更高"；B 项意为"更强壮"；C 项意为"更小/更瘦弱"；D 项意为"更重"。前文说他头发更白了、肩膀微驼，整个人看起来更"瘦小"（smaller），体现岁月的痕迹。故选 C。
