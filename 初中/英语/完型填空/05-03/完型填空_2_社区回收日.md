# 中考英语完形填空：社区回收日

## 完形填空原文

Last spring, our school organized a community recycling day. Each class had to ___1___ recyclable materials from the neighborhood and sort them at school. My class was given the streets closest to the school.

At first, nobody was ___2___ about the project. "It's just picking up trash," said my classmate Wang Lei. "What's the point?" Most of us felt the same way. We would rather have stayed in the classroom.

On Saturday morning, we split into groups and went door to door. I knocked on Mrs. Zhao's door first. She was an elderly woman who lived ___3___. When I explained what we were doing, her face lit up. "Wait here," she said, and came back with three bags full of old newspapers and plastic bottles. "I've been ___4___ to get rid of these for months," she said. "Thank you so much!"

Her reaction surprised me. I had expected people to be ___5___ at us for disturbing their weekend. Instead, most neighbors were happy to help. Some even invited us in for water and snacks.

By noon, we had collected so much material that we needed to make several ___6___ back to school. Old clothes, glass bottles, cardboard boxes, and electronic waste filled the classroom. Sorting everything took the whole afternoon.

During the sorting, I learned things I never knew. For example, different types of plastic need to be recycled ___7___. Batteries must be handled with special care because they contain harmful chemicals.

Our teacher told us that the materials we collected would be sent to recycling factories. The ___8___ from selling them would be donated to a children's charity.

At the end of the day, I was tired but ___9___. We had helped clean up the neighborhood, learned about recycling, and raised money for children in need — all in one day.

Wang Lei, who had complained the loudest that morning, said on the way home, "Actually, that was pretty ___10___. Can we do it again next month?"

## 选项

1. A. produce &emsp;&emsp; B. collect &emsp;&emsp; C. buy &emsp;&emsp; D. throw
2. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. serious &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. curious
3. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. nearby &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. safely
4. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. planning &emsp;&emsp; C. waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. learning
5. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. surprised &emsp;&emsp; C. pleased &emsp;&emsp; D. amused
6. A. calls &emsp;&emsp; B. trips &emsp;&emsp; C. turns &emsp;&emsp; D. plans
7. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. separately &emsp;&emsp; C. freely &emsp;&emsp; D. easily
8. A. news &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. experience &emsp;&emsp; D. advice
9. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
10. A. tiring &emsp;&emsp; B. cool &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. strange

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个班要从社区___可回收材料。A 项意为"生产"；B 项意为"收集"；C 项意为"购买"；D 项意为"扔"。回收日的任务是"收集"（collect）可回收物品。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始没人对这个项目感到___。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"严肃的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"好奇的"。后文说"就是捡垃圾"，大家宁愿待在教室，说明没人感到"兴奋"（excited）。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她是一位___生活的老太太。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"附近"；C 项意为"快乐地"；D 项意为"安全地"。一个人住的老人积攒了很多废品处理不掉，"独居"（alone）更符合语境。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___处理这些东西已经好几个月了。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"学习"。老人独居，一直"等着"（waiting）有人帮忙处理这些回收物，表达了感激。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我本以为人们会因为我们打扰了周末而___我们。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"逗乐的"。周末上门打扰，"我"预计邻居会"生气"（angry），但结果恰恰相反。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们需要跑好几___回学校。A 项意为"电话"；B 项意为"趟"；C 项意为"轮次"；D 项意为"计划"。"make several trips"（跑好几趟）是固定表达，说明收集的东西太多需要多次运送。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不同类型的塑料需要___回收。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"分开地"；C 项意为"自由地"；D 项意为"容易地"。不同塑料材质不同，需要"分开"（separately）处理回收。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：卖它们得到的___会捐给儿童慈善机构。A 项意为"新闻"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"经验"；D 项意为"建议"。回收物品卖掉获得"钱"（money），再用来做慈善。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一天结束时我很累但是很___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"自豪的"。虽然辛苦，但做了有意义的事，所以感到"自豪"（proud）。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：其实那还挺___的。A 项意为"累人的"；B 项意为"酷的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。之前最不情愿的王磊现在觉得活动很"酷"（cool），还想下个月再做，态度完全转变。故选 B。
