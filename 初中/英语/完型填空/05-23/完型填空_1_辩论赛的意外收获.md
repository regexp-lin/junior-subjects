# 中考英语完形填空：辩论赛的意外收获

## 完形填空原文

I never thought I would join a debate competition. I was always the quiet one in class, ___1___ to speak up. But when our teacher asked for volunteers, something pushed me to raise my hand.

Our team's topic was "Should students use smartphones at school?" I was assigned to argue for the ___2___ side. At first, I had no idea what to say. I spent hours in the library, ___3___ for information. To my surprise, I found many interesting facts and examples.

The competition day came. When I stood on the stage, my heart was beating fast. I looked at my teammates, and they nodded at me with ___4___. I took a deep breath and began to speak. My voice was shaky at first, but it grew ___5___ as I went on. I even made the audience laugh with a funny example.

When the results were announced, we didn't win first place. But the judge came to me and said, "You have great ___6___. Keep practicing, and you will go far." Those words meant more to me than any prize.

That experience taught me an important ___7___: we never know what we can do until we try. My fear of speaking had held me back for years. But once I stepped out of my ___8___ zone, I discovered a whole new world. Now I'm not afraid to ___9___ my opinions anymore. Sometimes, the greatest victory is not winning the competition, but ___10___ yourself.

## 选项

1. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. ready &emsp;&emsp; D. willing
2. A. wrong &emsp;&emsp; B. same &emsp;&emsp; C. opposite &emsp;&emsp; D. other
3. A. waiting &emsp;&emsp; B. searching &emsp;&emsp; C. asking &emsp;&emsp; D. paying
4. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. doubt &emsp;&emsp; C. encouragement &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
5. A. weaker &emsp;&emsp; B. softer &emsp;&emsp; C. stronger &emsp;&emsp; D. lower
6. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. humor &emsp;&emsp; C. potential &emsp;&emsp; D. money
7. A. lesson &emsp;&emsp; B. subject &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. story
8. A. comfort &emsp;&emsp; B. danger &emsp;&emsp; C. safe &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
9. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
10. A. beating &emsp;&emsp; B. knowing &emsp;&emsp; C. winning &emsp;&emsp; D. helping

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在班里总是最安静的那个，害怕发言。afraid 意为"害怕的"。A项"开心的"；B项"害怕的"；C项"准备好的"；D项"愿意的"。afraid to speak up 与前文 quiet 呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被分配去论证正方/反方。opposite 意为"相反的"，辩论中一方支持一方反对。A项"错误的"；B项"相同的"；C项"相反的"；D项"其他的"。opposite side 指对方观点。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在图书馆花了几个小时搜索资料。search for 意为"搜索、寻找"。A项"等待"；B项"搜索"；C项"询问"；D项"支付"。searching for information 为常见搭配。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们充满鼓励地向我点头。encouragement 意为"鼓励"。A项"恐惧"；B项"怀疑"；C项"鼓励"；D项"愤怒"。with encouragement 表示 supportive。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的声音在发抖，但越说越有力。stronger 意为"更有力的"。A项"更弱"；B项"更轻"；C项"更有力"；D项"更低"。grew stronger 与 shaky 形成对比。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你有很大的潜力。potential 意为"潜力"。A项"运气"；B项"幽默"；C项"潜力"；D项"钱"。great potential 为常见表扬用语。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次经历教会了我重要的一课。lesson 意为"课程、教训"。A项"一课、教训"；B项"学科"；C项"问题"；D项"故事"。important lesson 为常用搭配。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：但当我走出舒适区后。comfort zone 意为"舒适区"。A项"舒适"；B项"危险"；C项"安全"；D项"休息"。step out of comfort zone 为固定表达。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我不再害怕表达自己的观点了。share 意为"分享、表达"。A项"隐藏"；B项"改变"；C项"分享、表达"；D项"忘记"。share one's opinions 意为"表达观点"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时，最大的胜利不是赢得比赛，而是战胜自己。beat 意为"战胜、打败"，beat yourself 指超越自我。A项"战胜"；B项"了解"；C项"赢得"；D项"帮助"。beating yourself 与 winning the competition 形成对比。故选 A。
