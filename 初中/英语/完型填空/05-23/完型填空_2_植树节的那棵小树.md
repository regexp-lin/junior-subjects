# 中考英语完形填空：植树节的那棵小树

## 完形填空原文

March 12th is Tree Planting Day in China. This year, our class decided to plant trees in a park. Early that morning, we met at the school gate with shovels and ___1___. The weather was perfect—sunny and warm.

When we arrived at the park, a worker showed us how to dig the right ___2___ for each tree. "The hole should be deep enough for the roots," he said. I had never planted a tree before, so I listened ___3___. My partner and I chose a young maple tree. We dug together, taking turns when our arms got tired.

After we placed the tree in the hole, we filled it with soil and ___4___ it with water. The worker told us to put a wooden stick next to the tree to support it while it grew. We also wrote our ___5___ on a small card and hung it on the stick. "May you grow tall and strong," I wrote.

Every weekend after that, I would go to the park to ___6___ my tree. I watered it, pulled out weeds around it, and watched it grow. At first, the leaves were small and few. But as the weeks passed, they became ___7___ and greener.

One year later, the little tree had grown much taller. Standing beside it, I felt a sense of ___8___. I had helped create something that would live for many years. Planting a tree is like planting hope. It ___9___ us that small actions today can lead to great changes tomorrow. That tree will always remind me of the day I learned to care for something ___10___ myself.

## 选项

1. A. books &emsp;&emsp; B. seeds &emsp;&emsp; C. water &emsp;&emsp; D. food
2. A. hole &emsp;&emsp; B. place &emsp;&emsp; C. ground &emsp;&emsp; D. space
3. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. nervously &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
4. A. covered &emsp;&emsp; B. watered &emsp;&emsp; C. washed &emsp;&emsp; D. filled
5. A. names &emsp;&emsp; B. wishes &emsp;&emsp; C. numbers &emsp;&emsp; D. addresses
6. A. visit &emsp;&emsp; B. find &emsp;&emsp; C. cut &emsp;&emsp; D. move
7. A. fewer &emsp;&emsp; B. smaller &emsp;&emsp; C. more &emsp;&emsp; D. drier
8. A. pride &emsp;&emsp; B. sadness &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
9. A. teaches &emsp;&emsp; B. asks &emsp;&emsp; C. tells &emsp;&emsp; D. reminds
10. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. beyond &emsp;&emsp; C. about &emsp;&emsp; D. except

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天一早，我们带着铲子和水在学校门口集合。water 在此可指浇水用的水或容器。A项"书"；B项"种子"；C项"水"；D项"食物"。植树需要水来浇灌。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位工人教我们如何为每棵树挖合适的坑。hole 意为"坑、洞"。A项"坑"；B项"地方"；C项"地面"；D项"空间"。dig the hole 为植树固定搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没种过树，所以认真听着。carefully 意为"认真地"。A项"粗心地"；B项"紧张地"；C项"认真地"；D项"快速地"。listened carefully 体现学习态度。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把土填进去并浇水。water 作动词意为"浇水"。A项"覆盖"；B项"浇水"；C项"洗"；D项"填满"。watered it 与植树步骤一致。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把自己的祝愿写在小卡片上。wishes 意为"祝愿、愿望"。A项"名字"；B项"祝愿"；C项"数字"；D项"地址"。后文"May you grow tall and strong"即为 wish。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：之后的每个周末，我都会去公园探望我的树。visit 意为"探望、参观"。A项"探望"；B项"寻找"；C项"砍"；D项"移动"。visit my tree 表示定期看望。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但随着几周过去，叶子变得更多更绿了。more 与 greener 并列，表示生长趋势。A项"更少"；B项"更小"；C项"更多"；D项"更干燥"。more and greener 描述树苗成长。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：站在它旁边，我感到一种自豪感。pride 意为"自豪"。A项"自豪"；B项"悲伤"；C项"担忧"；D项"恐惧"。sense of pride 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它提醒我们，今天的小行动可以带来明天的大改变。remind 意为"提醒"。A项"教导"；B项"询问"；C项"告诉"；D项"提醒"。remind sb. that 为常见搭配。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那棵树总会让我想起学会关心自己以外事物的那一天。beyond 意为"超越、在……之外"。A项"为了"；B项"超越"；C项"关于"；D项"除了"。beyond myself 表示超越自我、关心他人/他物。故选 B。
