# 中考英语完形填空：学打羽毛球

## 完形填空原文

My friend Wang Fang is good at badminton. She invited me to play with her every weekend. I agreed, though I had ___1___ played badminton before. I thought it would be easy — you just hit the shuttlecock, right?

How wrong I was! At the first lesson, I could hardly hit the shuttlecock. It went in every ___2___ except the right one. Wang Fang was very patient. She taught me how to hold the racket correctly and how to ___3___ my eyes on the shuttlecock. "Don't move your body too much. Use your ___4___, not your whole arm," she said.

I practiced hard. Every time I ___5___ a shot, I felt a little more confident. Slowly, I learned to serve and return the shuttlecock. One day, we had a real match. I lost, of course, but I ___6___ 15 points! Wang Fang said that was great progress for a beginner.

Now I really ___7___ playing badminton. It is not only good exercise but also helps me relax after studying. I have made a plan to practice at ___8___ three times a week. I believe with more practice, I will get better. Maybe one day I can even ___9___ Wang Fang in a match! That is my ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. ever&emsp;&emsp; B. never&emsp;&emsp; C. always&emsp;&emsp; D. often
2. A. direction&emsp;&emsp; B. place&emsp;&emsp; C. way&emsp;&emsp; D. time
3. A. keep&emsp;&emsp; B. put&emsp;&emsp; C. take&emsp;&emsp; D. get
4. A. leg&emsp;&emsp; B. hand&emsp;&emsp; C. head&emsp;&emsp; D. wrist
5. A. missed&emsp;&emsp; B. caught&emsp;&emsp; C. made&emsp;&emsp; D. tried
6. A. got&emsp;&emsp; B. lost&emsp;&emsp; C. gave&emsp;&emsp; D. took
7. A. hate&emsp;&emsp; B. enjoy&emsp;&emsp; C. finish&emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
8. A. least&emsp;&emsp; B. most&emsp;&emsp; C. first&emsp;&emsp; D. last
9. A. help&emsp;&emsp; B. beat&emsp;&emsp; C. meet&emsp;&emsp; D. teach
10. A. problem&emsp;&emsp; B. dream&emsp;&emsp; C. mistake&emsp;&emsp; D. question

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我答应了，尽管我之前从未打过羽毛球。A 项 ever 常用于疑问或肯定句；B 项 never 意为"从未"；C 项 always 意为"总是"；D 项 often 意为"经常"。"had never played"从未打过。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：羽毛球飞向各个方向，就是不对。A 项意为"方向"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"方式"；D 项意为"时间"。"every direction"各个方向。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我怎么正确握拍，怎么盯住球。A 项 keep one's eyes on 意为"注视；盯住"；B 项 put 不常用于此搭配；C 项 take 不用于此搭配；D 项 get 不用于此搭配。"keep one's eyes on"为固定短语。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：身体不要动太多，用手腕，而不是整个手臂。A 项意为"腿"；B 项意为"手"；C 项意为"头"；D 项意为"手腕"。打羽毛球用手腕发力是技术要点。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次成功击球，我就更有信心一点。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"抓住"；C 项 make a shot 意为"击球；投篮"；D 项意为"尝试"。"make a shot"击球成功。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我当然输了，但我得了15分！A 项 got 意为"得到"，got 15 points 得了15分；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"给"；D 项意为"拿"。"got points"得分。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我真的很喜欢打羽毛球。A 项意为"讨厌"；B 项意为"喜欢；享受"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"避免"。"enjoy doing"喜欢做某事。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我制定了计划，每周至少练习三次。A 项 at least 意为"至少"；B 项 at most 意为"至多"；C 项 at first 意为"起初"；D 项 at last 意为"最后"。"at least"至少。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许有一天我甚至能在比赛中打败王芳！A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"打败"；C 项意为"遇见"；D 项意为"教"。"beat sb in a match"在比赛中打败某人。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是我的梦想。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"问题"。"That is my dream"那是我的梦想，与上文"也许能打败她"呼应。故选 B。
