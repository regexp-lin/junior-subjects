# 中考英语完形填空：秋游野炊

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, our class went on an autumn outing to a park by the lake. The main activity was a picnic where we would cook our own lunch. We were divided into several groups, and each group had to ___1___ food and cooking tools.

My group chose to make fried rice and vegetable soup. Li Hua was responsible ___2___ lighting the fire, while I cut the vegetables. At first, we had some ___3___ starting the fire because the wood was a bit wet. But after several tries, we finally succeeded. The fire made our faces warm in the cool autumn ___4___.

When the food was ready, we sat together on the grass and enjoyed our meal. Everyone agreed that the food tasted ___5___ than we had expected — perhaps because we made it ourselves! After lunch, we played some team games. I had ___6___ had so much fun with my classmates.

The teacher said that this kind of activity could help us learn to work ___7___ and solve problems together. I think she was right. We not only enjoyed the beautiful autumn ___8___ but also learned the importance of cooperation. I hope we can have ___9___ outing like this next semester. It was really an ___10___ experience!

## 选项

1. A. prepare&emsp;&emsp; B. buy&emsp;&emsp; C. eat&emsp;&emsp; D. share
2. A. for&emsp;&emsp; B. of&emsp;&emsp; C. with&emsp;&emsp; D. to
3. A. fun&emsp;&emsp; B. trouble&emsp;&emsp; C. time&emsp;&emsp; D. success
4. A. weather&emsp;&emsp; B. air&emsp;&emsp; C. wind&emsp;&emsp; D. temperature
5. A. worse&emsp;&emsp; B. better&emsp;&emsp; C. terrible&emsp;&emsp; D. bad
6. A. ever&emsp;&emsp; B. never&emsp;&emsp; C. always&emsp;&emsp; D. sometimes
7. A. alone&emsp;&emsp; B. together&emsp;&emsp; C. quickly&emsp;&emsp; D. silently
8. A. scenery&emsp;&emsp; B. sky&emsp;&emsp; C. tree&emsp;&emsp; D. flower
9. A. other&emsp;&emsp; B. another&emsp;&emsp; C. the other&emsp;&emsp; D. others
10. A. boring&emsp;&emsp; B. ordinary&emsp;&emsp; C. unforgettable&emsp;&emsp; D. terrible

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每组需要准备食物和炊具。A 项意为"准备"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"吃"；D 项意为"分享"。"prepare food"准备食物，符合野炊情境。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：李华负责生火。A 项 be responsible for 意为"对……负责"，为固定搭配；B 项 of 不与此搭配；C 项 with 不与此搭配；D 项 to 不与此搭配。responsible for 为固定短语。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我们生火时遇到了一些困难。A 项意为"乐趣"；B 项 have trouble (in) doing 意为"做……有困难"；C 项 have a good time 意为"玩得开心"；D 项意为"成功"。"have trouble doing"为固定表达。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：火焰在凉爽的秋风中让我们的脸暖和起来。A 项意为"天气"；B 项意为"空气"，in the cool autumn air 在凉爽的秋日中；C 项意为"风"；D 项意为"温度"。autumn air 秋天的空气/氛围。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都觉得食物比预期更好吃。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"糟糕的"；D 项意为"坏的"。than we had expected 提示用比较级，且后文说"因为是自己做的"应为更好。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未和同学玩得这么开心过。A 项 ever 常用于疑问或比较；B 项 never 与 had 连用，never had so much fun 从未如此开心；C 项 always 意为"总是"；D 项 sometimes 意为"有时"。"have never had so much fun"为强调表达。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说这类活动能帮助我们学会合作、一起解决问题。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"沉默地"。"work together"合作，与"solve problems together"呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不仅欣赏了美丽的秋景，还学会了合作的重要性。A 项意为"风景"；B 项意为"天空"；C 项意为"树"；D 项意为"花"。"autumn scenery"秋景。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望下学期还能有一次这样的郊游。A 项 other 后需接名词；B 项 another 意为"又一个；另一次"；C 项 the other 指两者中另一个；D 项 others 意为"其他人/物"。"another outing"又一次郊游。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这真是一次难忘的经历！A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"难忘的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。positive 结尾，应为难忘的。故选 C。
