# 中考英语完形填空：学织围巾

## 完形填空原文

When winter came, I wanted to make a scarf for my grandmother. She always takes care of me, and I wished to give her something ___1___. I had never knitted before, so I asked my mother to teach me.

Mother brought out two knitting needles and a ball of wool. She showed me the basic ___2___: cast on, knit, and cast off. At first, my hands moved slowly and I made many ___3___. The wool sometimes fell from my needles. I felt a little frustrated, but my mother encouraged me. She said, "Don't worry. ___4___ needs practice. You will get better."

I practiced every day after school. Little by little, my stitches became more even. After two weeks, I had knitted a long strip. It was time to ___5___ off and finish the scarf. I was so happy when I saw the finished scarf. Although it was not perfect, it was made with my ___6___ love.

On Grandma's birthday, I gave her the scarf. When she put it around her neck, her eyes became wet. She hugged me and said, "This is the most beautiful ___7___ I have ever received. Thank you, my dear." I learned that handmade gifts can ___8___ much more than bought ones. They carry our hearts in them. Now I am thinking about knitting a hat for my father. I believe he will ___9___ it too. Knitting has become one of my favorite ways to ___10___ my love for my family.

## 选项

1. A. cheap &emsp;&emsp; B. special &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
2. A. steps &emsp;&emsp; B. ideas &emsp;&emsp; C. rules &emsp;&emsp; D. stories
3. A. plans &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; D. choices
4. A. Something &emsp;&emsp; B. Nothing &emsp;&emsp; C. Everything &emsp;&emsp; D. Anything
5. A. take &emsp;&emsp; B. cast &emsp;&emsp; C. get &emsp;&emsp; D. turn
6. A. whole &emsp;&emsp; B. true &emsp;&emsp; C. real &emsp;&emsp; D. full
7. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. scarf &emsp;&emsp; C. cloth &emsp;&emsp; D. thing
8. A. cost &emsp;&emsp; B. mean &emsp;&emsp; C. spend &emsp;&emsp; D. pay
9. A. like &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
10. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. hide &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. keep

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想送她一份特别的礼物。A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"简单的"。亲手制作表达心意，用 special。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我基本步骤：起针、织、收针。A 项意为"步骤"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"故事"。cast on, knit, cast off 是编织的 steps。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我动作很慢，犯了很多错误。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"朋友"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"选择"。初学者容易 make mistakes。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：凡事都需要练习。A 项意为"某事"；B 项意为"没什么"；C 项意为"一切"；D 项意为"任何事"。Everything needs practice 意为"熟能生巧"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：该收针完成围巾了。A 项意为"拿走"；B 项意为"投；收"；C 项意为"得到"；D 项意为"转动"。cast off 在编织中意为"收针"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽不完美，但凝聚了我全心全意的爱。A 项意为"完整的；全部的"；B 项意为"真的"；C 项意为"真实的"；D 项意为"满的"。whole love 意为"全心全意的爱"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我这辈子收到的最美的礼物。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"围巾"；C 项意为"布"；D 项意为"东西"。奶奶在夸礼物，用 gift。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工礼物比买来的更有意义。A 项意为"花费"；B 项意为"意味着"；C 项意为"花费"；D 项意为"支付"。mean more 意为"意义更大、更有内涵"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信爸爸也会喜欢。A 项意为"喜欢"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"忘记"。收到礼物用 like。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：编织成为我向家人表达爱的方式之一。A 项意为"展示；表达"；B 项意为"隐藏"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"保持"。show one's love 意为"表达爱意"。故选 A。
