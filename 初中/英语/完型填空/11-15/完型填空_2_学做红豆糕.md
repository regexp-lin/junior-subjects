# 中考英语完形填空：学做红豆糕

## 完形填空原文

My aunt is good at making traditional Chinese snacks. Last week, she came to visit and offered to teach me how to make red bean cakes. I had eaten them many times but never tried to make them ___1___.

First, we needed to prepare the red bean paste. Aunt soaked the red beans in water overnight. The next morning, she boiled them until they became soft. Then she mashed them and mixed in some sugar. The paste looked smooth and had a ___2___ sweet smell.

Next came the sticky rice flour. We mixed it with a little water to form a soft dough. Aunt said we shouldn't add too much water, or the dough would become too ___3___ to handle. I kneaded the dough carefully until it felt smooth.

Then we divided the dough into small balls. We flattened each ball, put a spoonful of red bean paste in the center, and wrapped the dough around it. We had to make sure the paste didn't ___4___ out. Aunt showed me how to pinch the edges together neatly.

After that, we pressed each ball into a round, flat shape. Aunt had a wooden mold with a flower pattern. We put the cake in the mold and pressed it down. When we ___5___ it out, there was a pretty design on top.

We steamed the cakes for about fifteen minutes. When we opened the lid, the kitchen was filled with a wonderful ___6___. The cakes looked shiny and smelled delicious. I couldn't wait to try one.

Aunt said we should let them cool a bit first. When I took my first bite, the outside was soft and chewy, and the inside was sweet and smooth. It tasted even better than the ones from the bakery. I felt ___7___ of myself for making something so tasty.

Aunt wrapped some cakes for me to take home. I shared them with my parents and classmates. Everyone said they were delicious. Making red bean cakes is not very ___8___, but it requires patience. I hope to learn more traditional recipes from Aunt. Food made with love always tastes the best, and the ___9___ of learning from family is something I will always ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. myself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. ourselves &emsp;&emsp; D. itself
2. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. pleasant &emsp;&emsp; D. strong
3. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. soft &emsp;&emsp; C. dry &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
4. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. pour &emsp;&emsp; C. leak &emsp;&emsp; D. fall
5. A. pulled &emsp;&emsp; B. pushed &emsp;&emsp; C. knocked &emsp;&emsp; D. turned
6. A. smoke &emsp;&emsp; B. smell &emsp;&emsp; C. sound &emsp;&emsp; D. sight
7. A. ashamed &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
8. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. cheap
9. A. chance &emsp;&emsp; B. experience &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. question
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. finish

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：吃过很多次但从没自己做过。A 项 myself 意为"我自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"我们自己"；D 项意为"它自己"。主语是 I，故用 myself。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：豆沙看起来细腻，有一股宜人的甜香。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"令人愉快的"；D 项意为"强烈的"。甜香用 pleasant。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：水加太多面团会太软不好操作。A 项意为"硬的"；B 项意为"软的"；C 项意为"干的"；D 项意为"冷的"。too soft to handle 意为太软难以操作。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要确保豆沙不会漏出来。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"倾倒"；C 项意为"渗出、漏"；D 项意为"掉落"。leak out 意为漏出。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从模具里取出来，上面就有漂亮的花纹。A 项 pull out 意为"取出"；B 项意为"推"；C 项意为"敲"；D 项意为"翻转"。从模具取出用 pull out。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房里弥漫着好闻的香味。A 项意为"烟"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"声音"；D 项意为"景象"。wonderful smell 意为香味。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做出这么好吃的东西，我感到很骄傲。A 项意为"羞愧的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。feel proud of 意为感到自豪。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做红豆糕不算很难，但需要耐心。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项 difficult 意为"困难的"；C 项意为"贵的"；D 项意为"便宜的"。not very difficult 与 requires patience 对应。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：向家人学习的经历会永远铭记。A 项意为"机会"；B 项意为"经历"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"问题"。the experience of 意为……的经历。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：向家人学习的经历会永远铭记。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"完成"。will always remember 意为永远铭记。故选 B。
