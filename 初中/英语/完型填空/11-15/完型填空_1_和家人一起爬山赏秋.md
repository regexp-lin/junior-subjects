# 中考英语完形填空：和家人一起爬山赏秋

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my parents suggested that we go hiking in the mountains to enjoy the autumn scenery. I was a bit lazy at first, but I agreed when they said we would have a picnic at the top.

We started early in the morning. The air was cool and ___1___, and the sky was clear blue. As we walked up the mountain path, I noticed that the leaves had started to change color. Some were yellow, some were orange, and a few were already deep red. It was like a ___2___ painting.

My little sister ran ahead, full of energy. Dad warned her to slow down because the path could be slippery. Mom walked beside me and pointed out different plants. She knew the names of many trees and flowers. I was ___3___ that she knew so much about nature.

About halfway up, we took a short break. Mom brought out some water and snacks. We sat on a big rock and looked down at the valley below. The view was ___4___. I could see our whole city in the distance, and the river looked like a silver ribbon winding through the hills.

After we reached the top, we spread a blanket on the grass and had our picnic. We had sandwiches, fruit, and some homemade cookies. Everything tasted ___5___ better in the fresh mountain air. Dad told jokes and we all laughed. It was one of the best days I had spent with my family.

On our way down, the sun was setting. The mountains were bathed in golden light, and the colorful leaves seemed to ___6___ even brighter. I took many photos with my phone. I want to keep these ___7___ forever.

That day reminded me that we don't need to travel far to find beauty. Sometimes the most wonderful things are right ___8___ us—we just need to open our eyes and appreciate them. I hope we can do this again next autumn. Spending ___9___ with family is truly the best gift. Maybe next time I will ___10___ my best friend to join us too.

## 选项

1. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. fresh &emsp;&emsp; C. hot &emsp;&emsp; D. dirty
2. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. living &emsp;&emsp; D. dark
3. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
4. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. amazing &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. narrow
5. A. less &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. few
6. A. grow &emsp;&emsp; B. seem &emsp;&emsp; C. turn &emsp;&emsp; D. feel
7. A. thoughts &emsp;&emsp; B. memories &emsp;&emsp; C. plans &emsp;&emsp; D. secrets
8. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. above &emsp;&emsp; C. around &emsp;&emsp; D. under
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. energy &emsp;&emsp; D. space
10. A. invite &emsp;&emsp; B. force &emsp;&emsp; C. allow &emsp;&emsp; D. order

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：空气凉爽清新。A 项意为"干燥的"；B 项意为"清新的"；C 项意为"热的"；D 项意为"脏的"。清晨山间空气用 fresh。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：像一幅活生生的画。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项 living 意为"生动的、活生生的"；D 项意为"黑暗的"。a living painting 形容秋景生动。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对妈妈懂这么多大自然知识感到惊讶。A 项意为"惊讶的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"生气的"。be surprised that 表示惊讶。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：景色令人惊叹。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"令人惊叹的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"狭窄的"。山巅俯瞰用 amazing。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在山间清新的空气中，一切都好吃多了。A 项 less 意为"更少"；B 项 more 需与 better 搭配；C 项 much 修饰比较级；D 项 few 修饰可数名词。much better 意为好得多。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：彩叶在夕阳下仿佛更亮了。A 项意为"生长"；B 项意为"似乎"；C 项意为"变成"；D 项意为"感觉"。seem to 表示似乎。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想永远保留这些回忆。A 项意为"想法"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"秘密"。keep memories 意为珍藏回忆。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最美的东西往往就在我们身边。A 项意为"在……后面"；B 项意为"在……上面"；C 项 right around 意为"就在周围"；D 项意为"在……下面"。right around us 意为就在身边。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与家人共度的时光是最好的礼物。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"精力"；D 项意为"空间"。spending time with 意为与……共度时光。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次也许可以邀请好朋友一起来。A 项意为"邀请"；B 项意为"强迫"；C 项意为"允许"；D 项意为"命令"。invite sb to join 意为邀请某人参加。故选 A。
