# 中考英语完形填空：暑假最后的惊喜

## 完形填空原文

The summer holiday was almost over. School would start in three days. I had spent most of the holiday doing homework, reading books, and helping at home. I felt a little ___1___ that the holiday was ending. I would miss the long days and the freedom.

On the last Friday of the holiday, my parents told me they had a surprise for me. "Pack your bag," Mum said. "We are going on a short trip tomorrow morning." I was curious but they would not tell me where we were ___2___. I tried to guess all evening but could not figure it out.

The next morning, we got into the car and drove for about two hours. When we finally stopped, I looked out of the window and could not ___3___ my eyes. We were at the seaside! I had always wanted to see the ocean, but we had never had the time to go. My parents had saved this as a special gift for me.

The beach was beautiful. The sand was soft and warm. The waves came and went, making a gentle sound. I took off my shoes and ran towards the water. The sea was cool and ___4___. I had never felt so happy. My parents sat on the sand and watched me play. I could see the joy on their faces too.

We spent the whole day there. I swam in the sea, built sand castles, and collected shells. For lunch, we had a picnic on the beach. My father told me that he and Mum had been ___5___ this trip for weeks. "We wanted to give you a wonderful memory before school starts," he said. I was so moved that I hugged them both.

That evening, we watched the sunset together. The sky turned orange and pink. It was the most beautiful ___6___ I had ever seen. I took many photos with my phone. I wanted to keep this moment forever.

On our way home, I thought about the summer. It had been a good one — I had learned to cook, made new friends, and read many books. But this last trip was the best ___7___ of all. It was not just about the beach or the sea. It was about my parents' love and the effort they made to make me happy.

I will never forget that day. Whenever I feel tired or stressed during the school year, I will think of the ocean, the sunset, and my parents' smiles. Some ___8___ are worth keeping in our hearts forever.

Thank you, Mum and Dad, for the surprise. Thank you for always thinking of me. I promise to work hard at school and make you ___9___. And when I grow up, I hope to give you surprises too — not just things, but time, love, and ___10___ memories together.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：假期即将结束，我感到有点___。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"难过的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。假期快结束会不舍，"sad"恰当。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们不肯告诉我我们要___哪里。A 项意为"居住"；B 项意为"工作"；C 项意为"去"；D 项意为"停留"。"where we were going"意为"我们要去的地方"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我望向窗外，简直不敢___自己的眼睛。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"睁开"；C 项意为"闭上"；D 项意为"遮住"。"could not believe one's eyes"为固定搭配，意为"不敢相信所见"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：海水凉爽而___。A 项意为"脏的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"深的"；D 项意为"咸的"。海水有咸味是常识，"salty"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲说他和妈妈已经___这次旅行好几周了。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"错过"。"planning"表示提前筹划惊喜旅行。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我见过的最美的___。A 项意为"电影"；B 项意为"画面/景象"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"音乐"。"picture"指眼前的日落景象。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但这最后一次旅行是所有中最好的___。A 项意为"日子"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"游戏"。父母安排的惊喜像一份礼物，"gift"最恰当。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：某些___值得永远珍藏在心中。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"时刻"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"考试"。海滩、日落、父母笑容等美好瞬间，"moments"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发誓会努力学习，让你们___。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"难过的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"累的"。"make you proud"意为"让你们骄傲"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望也能给你们惊喜——不仅是物质，还有时间、爱与___的共同回忆。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"短暂的"；C 项意为"廉价的"；D 项意为"美好的"。"wonderful memories"表示美好难忘的回忆。故选 D。
