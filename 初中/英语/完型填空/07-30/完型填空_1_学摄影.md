# 中考英语完形填空：学摄影

## 完形填空原文

My uncle is a photographer. He takes pictures for newspapers and magazines. This summer, he offered to teach me how to use a camera. I was very ___1___ because I had always wanted to learn photography.

On the first day, Uncle gave me an old camera and showed me the basics. "The most important thing is not the camera," he said. "It is the person behind it. You need to learn to ___2___ — really see what is around you." I nodded, but I did not quite understand what he meant then.

We went to the park to practice. Uncle told me to look for interesting things: the way light falls on leaves, the smile on a child's face, or a bird resting on a branch. At first, I just pointed my camera everywhere and took many photos. But when I looked at them later, most of them were ___3___. "You are taking too many pictures without thinking," Uncle said. "Slow down. Choose your subject carefully."

I tried again. This time I stood still and looked around for a long time. I noticed an old man sitting on a bench, feeding pigeons. The afternoon sun made his face look warm and kind. I lifted my camera, waited for the right moment, and pressed the button. When I showed Uncle the photo, he smiled. "Now you are ___4___," he said.

Over the next few weeks, we went to different places — the market, the river, and even my school. I learned about light, angles, and composition. I also learned that the best photos often capture moments that will never happen again. A baby's first laugh, a friend's surprise, or a sunset over the lake — these ___5___ are precious.

One day, I took a picture of my grandmother in her garden. She was watering her flowers and did not know I was there. The photo caught her natural smile. When I gave her a printed copy, she was so happy that she put it in a frame. That was when I understood what Uncle had said. Photography is not just about ___6___ pictures. It is about keeping beautiful moments and sharing them with the people we love.

I still have a lot to learn, but I enjoy every moment with my camera. Uncle says that everyone can learn the technical part, but what makes a good photographer is a ___7___ heart and curious eyes. I will keep practicing and one day I hope to take photos that can touch people's ___8___, just like Uncle's photos do.

If you are interested in photography, do not wait for a perfect camera. Start with what you have — even your phone. The best camera is the one you have with you. Remember: the world is full of beauty. We just need to slow down and ___9___ it. Every moment can become a ___10___ memory if we learn to see it with our hearts.

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
2. A. run &emsp;&emsp; B. listen &emsp;&emsp; C. look &emsp;&emsp; D. speak
3. A. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; B. ordinary &emsp;&emsp; C. expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. clear
4. A. learning &emsp;&emsp; B. playing &emsp;&emsp; C. waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. winning
5. A. cameras &emsp;&emsp; B. moments &emsp;&emsp; C. places &emsp;&emsp; D. lessons
6. A. drawing &emsp;&emsp; B. taking &emsp;&emsp; C. buying &emsp;&emsp; D. copying
7. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. kind &emsp;&emsp; C. weak &emsp;&emsp; D. busy
8. A. hands &emsp;&emsp; B. hearts &emsp;&emsp; C. eyes &emsp;&emsp; D. ears
9. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. notice &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid
10. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. long &emsp;&emsp; C. forgotten &emsp;&emsp; D. lasting

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常___，因为我一直想学摄影。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"担心的"。"excited"表示愿望实现时的兴奋心情。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你需要学会___——真正看清周围的一切。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"听"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"说"。后文"really see"与之呼应，"look"强调用心观察。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：后来看照片时，大多数都很___。A 项意为"精彩的"；B 项意为"普通的/平淡的"；C 项意为"昂贵的"；D 项意为"清晰的"。随便乱拍缺乏思考和构图，效果平平，"ordinary"恰当。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说："现在你在___了。"A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"玩"；C 项意为"等"；D 项意为"赢"。掌握了观察和捕捉时机，说明在进步，"learning"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些___是珍贵的。A 项意为"相机"；B 项意为"时刻"；C 项意为"地方"；D 项意为"课程"。前文列举婴儿的笑、朋友的惊喜、日落等，都是瞬间，"moments"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：摄影不仅是___照片。A 项意为"画"；B 项意为"拍"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"复制"。"take pictures"为固定搭配，且下文强调记录与分享，不止于"拍"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：造就优秀摄影师的是___的心和好奇的眼睛。A 项意为"冷漠的"；B 项意为"善良的/温情的"；C 项意为"脆弱的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。"kind heart"指对世界充满善意与关注。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望拍出能触动人们___的照片。A 项意为"手"；B 项意为"心"；C 项意为"眼睛"；D 项意为"耳朵"。"touch people's hearts"意为"触动人心"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只需放慢脚步并___它。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"注意"；D 项意为"避免"。"notice"表示留意、发现生活中的美。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：若用心去看，每个瞬间都能成为___的回忆。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"长的"；C 项意为"被遗忘的"；D 项意为"持久的"。"lasting memory"意为"持久的记忆"。故选 D。
