# 中考英语完形填空：学做花生糖

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my mother suggested we make peanut candy at home. It was a traditional snack that her grandmother used to make. I was curious and agreed to ___1___ right away.

First, we needed to prepare the peanuts. Mother poured them into a frying pan and ___2___ them over low heat. She said we had to stir them often so they wouldn't burn. After about ten minutes, the peanuts turned golden and smelled ___3___. Then we removed the skins and broke them into smaller pieces.

Next, we made the sugar syrup. Mother poured sugar and a little water into a pot. As the mixture heated, it began to ___4___ and turn brown. "We need to be careful here," she said. "The syrup must reach the right temperature." I watched closely as she stirred it with a wooden spoon.

When the syrup was ready, we quickly mixed in the peanut pieces. Mother poured the mixture onto a greased tray and I helped spread it ___5___. We had to work fast before it hardened. Then we used a knife to cut it into small squares while it was still warm.

After it cooled completely, we tried our peanut candy. It was crispy outside and sweet inside. The ___6___ of roasted peanuts filled my mouth. "It's even better than the ones from the store!" I said. Mother smiled and said that homemade food always tasted ___7___ because we put our love into it.

Making peanut candy together was a wonderful ___8___. I learned that some of the best things in life come from spending time with family and ___9___ old traditions. I hope we can make it again during the Spring Festival and ___10___ it with our relatives.

## 选项

1. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. try &emsp;&emsp; D. wait
2. A. roasted &emsp;&emsp; B. washed &emsp;&emsp; C. boiled &emsp;&emsp; D. froze
3. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
4. A. melt &emsp;&emsp; B. freeze &emsp;&emsp; C. dry &emsp;&emsp; D. burn
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. evenly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
6. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. color &emsp;&emsp; C. smell &emsp;&emsp; D. sound
7. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. similar &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. common
8. A. game &emsp;&emsp; B. experience &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
9. A. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; B. breaking &emsp;&emsp; C. keeping &emsp;&emsp; D. losing
10. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. throw

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很好奇，立刻同意尝试。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"尝试"；D 项意为"等待"。agree to try 表示同意试着做。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈把花生倒进平底锅，用小火烘烤。A 项意为"烘烤"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"煮"；D 项意为"冷冻"。做花生糖需烘烤花生，roast 符合。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大约十分钟后，花生变成金黄色，闻起来很香。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"极好的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。烤好的花生香味用 wonderful。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：随着混合料加热，糖开始融化并变成棕色。A 项意为"融化"；B 项意为"冷冻"；C 项意为"变干"；D 项意为"燃烧"。糖加热会 melt。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈把混合物倒在抹了油的托盘上，我帮着均匀摊开。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"均匀地"；D 项意为"大声地"。spread evenly 表示摊匀。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烤花生的味道充满口腔。A 项意为"味道"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"声音"。品尝时 fill my mouth 的是 taste。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说 homemade 食物总是更好吃，因为我们投入了爱。A 项意为"更糟"；B 项意为"相似的"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"普通的"。put love into it 对应 taste better。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一起做花生糖是一次很好的经历。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"经历"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"错误"。做糖果的过程是 experience。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生活中最好的东西来自与家人共度时光和传承古老传统。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"打破"；C 项意为"保持、传承"；D 项意为"失去"。keep traditions 表示传承传统。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望春节时能再做，并与亲戚分享。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"扔"。春节与亲人 share 食物是习俗。故选 C。
