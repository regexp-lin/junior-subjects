# 中考英语完形填空：第一次坐飞机

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, my family went to Beijing by plane. It was my first time on an airplane. I had always wanted to fly, but when the day finally came, I felt a little ___1___. My father held my hand and said, "Don't worry. Flying is very safe."

At the airport, we checked in our bags and went through security. The staff were kind and everything went smoothly. When we walked onto the plane, I was surprised by how ___2___ it was inside. There were rows of seats and a long aisle in the middle. We found our seats and put our bags in the overhead compartment.

Before take-off, a flight attendant showed us the safety instructions. She told us how to use the oxygen mask and where the ___3___ exits were. I listened carefully even though I hoped we would never need them. Then the plane started to move. It taxied slowly to the runway. My heart was beating fast.

When the plane began to ___4___, I pressed my back against the seat. I could feel the engines getting louder. The ground moved away quickly and soon we were high above the clouds. Looking out of the window, I saw the city become smaller and smaller. Then we entered the clouds and everything turned white. It was ___5___!

A few minutes later, we were above the clouds. The sky was bright blue and the sun was shining. The clouds below looked like a soft white blanket. I had never seen anything so beautiful. My nervousness ___6___ and I began to enjoy the flight.

The flight attendant brought us drinks and a small meal. I chose orange juice. It was a short flight — only about two hours. When the pilot said we would ___7___ soon, I looked out of the window again. I could see the mountains and buildings of Beijing. The plane landed smoothly. I was a little sad that the flight was over. I had enjoyed every ___8___.

That trip made me love travelling. I hope to take more plane trips in the future. The world is so big and there is so much to see. My first flight was an ___9___ I will never forget. It taught me that trying new things can open up a whole new world. Next time I fly, I will not be ___10___ at all. I will just look forward to the adventure.

8. A. hour &emsp;&emsp; B. minute &emsp;&emsp; C. second &emsp;&emsp; D. day
9. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. experience &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. dream

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天终于到了，我有点___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。第一次坐飞机会紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：走进飞机，我惊讶于里面有多___。A 项意为"小"；B 项意为"大"；C 项意为"暗"；D 项意为"吵"。首次进机舱会觉得宽敞，"big"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她告诉我们应急面罩用法和___出口在哪。A 项意为"后方"；B 项意为"前方"；C 项意为"紧急"；D 项意为"侧面"。"emergency exit"意为"紧急出口"，安全须知常用语。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词词组辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当飞机开始___时，我紧贴椅背。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"降落"；C 项意为"起飞"；D 项意为"转向"。起飞时紧张，"take off"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冲进云层后一片白，太___了！A 项意为"可怕的"；B 项意为"令人惊叹的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"困难的"。首次体验飞行，"amazing"表达惊喜。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的紧张___了，开始享受飞行。A 项意为"增长"；B 项意为"消失"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"停留"。适应后紧张感消失，"disappeared"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：飞行员说我们很快就要___了。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"到达"；C 项意为"降落"；D 项意为"出发"。"land"专指飞机降落，与下文"landed smoothly"呼应。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我享受每一___。A 项意为"小时"；B 项意为"分钟"；C 项意为"秒"；D 项意为"天"。强调全程每一刻都享受，"every second"最贴切。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一次飞行是一次我永远不会忘记的___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"经历"；C 项意为"课"；D 项意为"梦"。亲身飞行是一次经历，"experience"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次飞行我一点都不会___了。A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"难过"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"生气"。与开头"nervous"呼应，不再害怕，"afraid"正确。故选 C。
