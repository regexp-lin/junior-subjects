# 中考英语完形填空：制作生日贺卡

## 完形填空原文

Next week is my mum's birthday. I wanted to give her something special this year. Instead of ___1___ a gift from a shop, I decided to make a birthday card by hand. I believed that a handmade card would mean more to her than anything I could buy.

I went to the stationery shop after school and bought some coloured paper, glitter, stickers, and a set of markers. The shop owner asked me what I was making. When I told her, she smiled and said, "Your mum is ___2___ to have a child like you."

Back at home, I spread everything out on my desk and started working. First, I folded a piece of pink paper in half. Pink is Mum's ___3___ colour. On the front, I drew a big heart and decorated it with glitter. Inside, I wanted to write something that would make her feel ___4___. I thought for a long time.

Finally, I wrote: "Dear Mum, thank you for always being there for me. You work so hard every day, but you never ___5___. You are the strongest and most loving person I know. I promise to study hard and make you proud. Happy Birthday! Love, your child."

I also glued some small flower stickers around the message to make it look ___6___. Then I put the card in an envelope and hid it in my schoolbag. I could hardly ___7___ to see her face when she opened it.

On her birthday morning, I woke up early and placed the card on the dining table next to her coffee cup. When Mum came down and read the card, her eyes filled with ___8___. She hugged me tightly and said, "This is the best gift I have ever received."

I learned that the most ___9___ gifts don't have to cost a lot of money. What truly matters is the thought and love we put into them. A simple card can carry the ___10___ message in the world.

## 选项

1. A. making &emsp;&emsp; B. stealing &emsp;&emsp; C. buying &emsp;&emsp; D. borrowing
2. A. lucky &emsp;&emsp; B. sorry &emsp;&emsp; C. afraid &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
3. A. least &emsp;&emsp; B. favourite &emsp;&emsp; C. only &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
4. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. guilty &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
5. A. agree &emsp;&emsp; B. complain &emsp;&emsp; C. laugh &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
6. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. dark &emsp;&emsp; C. pretty &emsp;&emsp; D. plain
7. A. afford &emsp;&emsp; B. decide &emsp;&emsp; C. wait &emsp;&emsp; D. remember
8. A. anger &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. tears &emsp;&emsp; D. doubt
9. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. common &emsp;&emsp; C. valuable &emsp;&emsp; D. ordinary
10. A. shortest &emsp;&emsp; B. deepest &emsp;&emsp; C. funniest &emsp;&emsp; D. strangest

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定不从商店___礼物，而是亲手做一张贺卡。A 项意为"制作"；B 项意为"偷"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"借"。与"handmade"对比的是商店购买，"buying"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你妈妈真___有你这样的孩子。A 项意为"幸运的"；B 项意为"抱歉的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"生气的"。店主夸奖妈妈有这样孝顺的孩子很幸运，"lucky"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：粉色是妈妈最___的颜色。A 项意为"最少的"；B 项意为"最喜爱的"；C 项意为"唯一的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。特意选粉色是因为妈妈最喜欢，"favourite"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想写一些让她感到___的话。A 项意为"特别的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"内疚的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。生日贺卡希望让妈妈觉得自己很特别，"special"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你每天工作那么辛苦，但从不___。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"笑"；D 项意为"休息"。赞美妈妈辛苦却从不抱怨，"complain"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还贴了一些小花贴纸让它看起来更___。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"暗的"；C 项意为"漂亮的"；D 项意为"朴素的"。装饰贺卡使其更漂亮，"pretty"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我迫不及___想看她打开时的表情。A 项意为"负担得起"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"记住"。"could hardly wait"意为"迫不及待"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈读了贺卡，眼里充满了___。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"泪水"；D 项意为"怀疑"。被感动得流泪，"tears"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最___的礼物不一定花很多钱。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"有价值的"；D 项意为"平凡的"。最有价值的礼物不一定贵，"valuable"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（最高级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：一张简单的卡片可以承载世界上最___的心意。A 项意为"最短的"；B 项意为"最深的"；C 项意为"最有趣的"；D 项意为"最奇怪的"。卡片承载最深的情意，"deepest"正确。故选 B。
