# 中考英语完形填空：补课回家的夜路

## 完形填空原文

I have tutoring classes every Tuesday and Thursday evening. The class ends at 9 p.m. By the time I get out, the streets are quiet and dark. I used to feel ___1___ walking home alone. The shadows under the streetlamps made me imagine all kinds of scary things.

One night, I was halfway home when I heard footsteps behind me. My heart beat fast. I didn't dare to ___2___. Then someone called my name. It was my father. He had been waiting at the tutoring center and had followed me at a distance.

"I didn't want you to feel like a child," he said. "But I also couldn't let you walk alone in the dark. So I walked behind you. You didn't need to see me — you just needed to know you weren't ___3___."

I was surprised and moved. I had thought I was brave enough to handle the night walk. But Dad had been there all along, ___4___ from a distance. He had never said anything. He had never made me feel helpless. He had simply made sure I was safe.

"That's what parents do," Dad said with a smile. "We don't always need to be in front. Sometimes the best way to ___5___ is to walk behind — close enough to catch you if you fall, far enough to let you feel independent."

From that night on, I knew Dad would be somewhere on my way home. Sometimes I caught a ___6___ of him in the corner of my eye. Sometimes I didn't see him at all. But I always felt calm. I wasn't afraid of the dark anymore. I wasn't afraid of being alone. Because I knew that love doesn't always ___7___. Sometimes it follows quietly, like a shadow that never leaves.

When I got accepted into my dream high school, Dad said, "You did it yourself. I just walked behind." But I knew the ___8___. I had never truly walked alone. Every step of that dark road, he had been there. His silent presence gave me the ___9___ to keep going — not because he carried me, but because he made me believe I could carry myself.

Now I'm older. I don't need Dad to walk behind me anymore. But I will never forget those Tuesday and Thursday nights — the quiet streets, the distant footsteps, and the love that ___10___ without asking for anything in return. That's the kind of love that shapes us: steady, silent, and always there when we need it most.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我过去一个人走回家感到___。A 项意为"兴奋"；B 项意为"害怕"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"累"。"afraid"与"quiet and dark"、"shadows"、"imagine scary things"相呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不敢___。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"回头看"；C 项意为"停"；D 项意为"喊"。"look back"（回头看）与听到身后脚步声时的恐惧反应相符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你只需要知道你并不___。A 项意为"迷路"；B 项意为"独自"；C 项意为"迟到"；D 项意为"错"。"alone"与父亲暗中跟随相呼应，强调有他在旁。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸一直在那里，从远处___着我。A 项意为"看着"；B 项意为"跟着"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"等待"。"watching"（注视、守护）与"at a distance"搭配，强调默默守护。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时最好的___方式就是在后面走。A 项意为"保护"；B 项意为"教导"；C 项意为"爱"；D 项意为"引导"。"protect"与"catch you if you fall"相呼应。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我眼角能___到他。A 项意为"看"；B 项意为"景色"；C 项意为"一瞥"；D 项意为"视野"。"catch a glimpse of"（瞥见）是固定搭配。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为我知道爱不总要___在前。A 项意为"说"；B 项意为"展示"；C 项意为"带领"；D 项意为"出现"。"lead"与"walk behind"形成对比，爱有时默默跟随。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我知道___。A 项意为"原因"；B 项意为"真相"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"秘密"。"truth"指"我从未真正独自走过"这一事实。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他无声的陪伴给了我继续前行的___。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"希望"；C 项意为"勇气"；D 项意为"力量"。"courage"（勇气）与"keep going"、"believe I could carry myself"相呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那种从不求回报的___的爱。A 项意为"给予"；B 项意为"停留"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"成长"。"gives"与"without asking for anything in return"相呼应，指无私付出。故选 A。
