# 中考英语完形填空：志愿者日的红马甲

## 完形填空原文

Our school holds a Community Service Day every spring. Students wear red vests and help out in the neighborhood — cleaning parks, visiting the elderly, or teaching children to read. I had never joined before. I thought it was for "good students" who had nothing better to do. I was wrong.

Last year, my teacher signed me up. "Try it once," she said. "You might ___1___ something."

I was assigned to a nursing home. My task was to read newspapers to an old man named Mr. Lin. He was in his eighties and could no longer see well. At first I felt awkward. I didn't know what to ___2___. But when I started reading, something changed. Mr. Lin leaned forward, his face full of interest. He asked me to slow down so he could imagine every scene. "Young friend," he said, "your voice brings the world back to me."

His words made me feel ___3___. I had never thought that something so simple — reading aloud — could matter so much. For two hours, I read about sports, weather, and a story about a lost dog that was found. Mr. Lin laughed at the happy ending. "I used to have a dog," he said. "Thank you for this afternoon. You've given me more than a newspaper. You've given me ___4___."

When I left the nursing home, I felt different. The red vest no longer felt like a costume. It felt like a ___5___ — a promise to help, to care, to show up. I had gone there thinking I would "do a favor." I left knowing I had received more than I had given.

That night, I wrote in my diary: "Today I learned that serving others doesn't make you less. It makes you ___6___. The more you give, the more you understand what really matters."

Since then, I've joined every Community Service Day. I've read to Mr. Lin many times. We've become friends. He tells me stories about his youth, and I tell him about school. The red vest hangs in my closet now, not just for one day a year. I wear it whenever I ___7___ — at the library, at the animal shelter, anywhere someone might need a helping hand.

Some people think volunteering is about ___8___ time. They're wrong. It's about finding meaning. That red vest taught me that we are all connected. Your smile can brighten someone's day. Your voice can bring the world to someone who can't see it. Your presence can make someone feel less ___9___.

We don't need to be superheroes to make a difference. We just need to ___10___ — in a red vest or not — and say, "I'm here. How can I help?" That's the spirit of Community Service Day. And that's the spirit we can carry every day.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可能会___到什么。A 项意为"学到"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"错过"。"learn"与后文"Today I learned"相呼应，老师鼓励尝试可能会有所收获。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不知道该___什么。A 项意为"说"；B 项意为"做"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"穿"。"what to say"（该说什么）与"felt awkward"（感到尴尬）相呼应，初次见面不知如何开口。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话让我感到___。A 项意为"奇怪"；B 项意为"有用"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"紧张"。"proud"（骄傲）与"could matter so much"相呼应，朗读能带来价值让"我"感到有价值。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你给我的不止是报纸，你给了我___。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"陪伴"；C 项意为"新闻"；D 项意为"希望"。"company"（陪伴）与独居老人需要人交流相呼应，朗读带来的是陪伴感。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它感觉像一个___——帮助、关心、出现的承诺。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"制服"；C 项意为"象征"；D 项意为"记忆"。"symbol"（象征）与前文"no longer felt like a costume"对比，红马甲成为志愿精神的象征。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：服务他人不会让你变少，而是让你___。A 项意为"更多"；B 项意为"更少"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更弱"。"more"与"doesn't make you less"形成对比，付出反而让自我更丰富。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每当我___时就穿着它。A 项意为"做志愿者"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"旅行"。"volunteer"与全文主题一致，红马甲在志愿活动时穿。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有人认为志愿活动是关于___时间。A 项意为"节省"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"花费"；D 项意为"失去"。"wasting"与"They're wrong. It's about finding meaning"形成对比，反对把志愿当成浪费时间。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你的存在能让某人感觉不那么___。A 项意为"快乐"；B 项意为"忙碌"；C 项意为"孤独"；D 项意为"累"。"lonely"（孤独）与"Your presence"（你的在场）相呼应，陪伴能减少孤独感。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只需要___——无论穿不穿红马甲——然后说"我在这儿，我能帮忙吗？"A 项意为"出现"；B 项意为"成长"；C 项意为"大声说"；D 项意为"赶快"。"show up"（到场、出现）与"I'm here. How can I help?"相呼应，指出现并主动提供帮助。故选 A。
