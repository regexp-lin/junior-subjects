# 中考英语完形填空：学做陶艺花瓶

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, I went to an art centre with my mother to learn pottery. I had always wanted to make something with my own hands. The teacher, Ms. Chen, welcomed us and showed us around the ___1___. There were many beautiful vases and cups on the shelves.

"Today we will make a small vase," Ms. Chen said. She gave each of us a lump of clay. It felt cold and ___2___ in my hands. "First, centre the clay on the wheel," she told us. "Then use your hands to shape it." I turned on the wheel and put my hands on the clay. But it didn't look like a vase — it looked ___3___ a bowl!

Ms. Chen came over and helped me. "Don't push too hard," she said. "Be gentle. Let the clay tell you what it wants to become." I tried again. This time I moved my hands slowly and carefully. The clay began to rise and take ___4___. I was so excited that I almost forgot to keep my hands wet. "The clay must stay wet, or it will crack," Ms. Chen reminded us.

After we finished shaping, we had to wait for the vases to ___5___. That took two days. When we came back, we painted our vases with different colours. I chose blue and white, like the sky and clouds. My mother chose red and gold. "Every vase is ___6___, just like every person," Ms. Chen said.

When I took my vase home, I put it on my desk. It wasn't perfect — one side was a little ___7___ than the other. But I loved it because I had made it myself. Making pottery taught me that we don't need to be ___8___ at something the first time. What matters is that we try and keep ___9___. Next month, I want to make a cup. I'm sure I'll do ___10___ with more practice.

## 选项

1. A. library &emsp;&emsp; B. classroom &emsp;&emsp; C. studio &emsp;&emsp; D. office
2. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. hot &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
3. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. at &emsp;&emsp; D. with
4. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. shape &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. place
5. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. cool &emsp;&emsp; C. warm &emsp;&emsp; D. wet
6. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. cheap
7. A. taller &emsp;&emsp; B. shorter &emsp;&emsp; C. thicker &emsp;&emsp; D. thinner
8. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. bad &emsp;&emsp; D. ready
9. A. trying &emsp;&emsp; B. crying &emsp;&emsp; C. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; D. waiting
10. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. less &emsp;&emsp; D. more

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师欢迎我们，带我们参观了工作室。A 项意为"图书馆"；B 项意为"教室"；C 项意为"工作室"；D 项意为"办公室"。陶艺学习场所通常称为 studio（工作室）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：泥土摸起来冰凉且柔软。A 项意为"柔软的"；B 项意为"硬的"；C 项意为"热的"；D 项意为"干的"。陶土（clay）未干时质地柔软，可塑，soft 符合。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：但看起来不像花瓶——看起来像碗。A 项意为"为了"；B 项意为"像"；C 项意为"在"；D 项意为"和……一起"。look like 为固定搭配，意为"看起来像"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：泥土开始隆起并成形。A 项意为"小心"；B 项意为"形状"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"地方"。take shape 为固定搭配，意为"成形、初具规模"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：塑形完成后，我们要等花瓶晾干。A 项意为"变干"；B 项意为"变凉"；C 项意为"变暖"；D 项意为"变湿"。陶艺制作中塑形后需晾干再上色，dry 符合。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个花瓶都是不同的，就像每个人一样。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"昂贵的"；D 项意为"便宜的"。手工制作各有特色，different 与"每个人"呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：一边比另一边稍厚一些。A 项意为"更高"；B 项意为"更矮"；C 项意为"更厚"；D 项意为"更薄"。形容花瓶壁厚薄不均用 thicker。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做陶艺让我明白，我们第一次不需要做得完美。A 项意为"好的"；B 项意为"完美的"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"准备好的"。be perfect at 意为"在某方面完美"，与"第一次"形成对比。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是我们尝试并坚持下去。A 项意为"尝试"；B 项意为"哭泣"；C 项意为"睡觉"；D 项意为"等待"。keep trying 为常见搭配，意为"不断尝试、坚持"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信多练习会做得更好。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更少"；D 项意为"更多"。do better 意为"做得更好"，与 more practice 呼应。故选 B。
