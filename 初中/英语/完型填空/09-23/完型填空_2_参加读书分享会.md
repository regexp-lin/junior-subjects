# 中考英语完形填空：参加读书分享会

## 完形填空原文

Our school library holds a reading sharing activity every month. Last week, I was chosen to represent our class and share my favourite book. I felt both nervous and ___1___, because I had never spoken in front of so many people before.

The book I chose was Charlotte's Web, a story about friendship between a pig and a spider. I had read it three times and I loved it. To prepare for the sharing, I made some notes about the main characters and the ___2___ of the story. I also marked my favourite paragraphs to read aloud.

On the day of the activity, about fifty students and several teachers gathered in the library. When my name was called, I walked to the front with my book in hand. My legs were shaking, but I took a deep breath and began to speak.

I introduced the book and explained why I liked it. "This book teaches us that true friendship can happen between anyone, even a pig and a spider," I said. "It also shows us the ___3___ of life — that we should appreciate every moment." Then I read a short passage. My voice was a bit ___4___ at first, but as I got into the story, I became more relaxed.

After I finished, several students raised their hands to ask questions. One girl asked, "What part of the book moved you the most?" I thought for a moment and said, "When Charlotte died. She gave everything for her friend. That made me ___5___ the meaning of sacrifice." The audience nodded. I felt that they understood what I meant.

Our librarian, Mrs. Chen, came up and praised my sharing. "You did a great job. Your passion for reading really ___6___ through," she said. She gave me a bookmark as a small gift. I was so happy that I could hardly believe it.

That experience encouraged me to read more and share more. I have ___7___ joined the school's reading club. Now I meet with other book lovers every Friday to discuss what we've read. We learn from each other and ___8___ our love of books.

Reading opens our minds and brings us to new worlds. Sharing our reading with others makes the experience even more ___9___. I would recommend every student to take part in such activities. You might discover that you ___10___ speaking about books more than you thought!

8. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. share &emsp;&emsp; C. lose &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
9. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. meaningless &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
10. A. hate &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. fear

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到既紧张又兴奋，因为我从未在这么多人面前讲过话。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。被选中分享时的复杂心情，excited 与 nervous 搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我做了关于主要角色和故事情节的笔记。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"情节"；C 项意为"封面"；D 项意为"作者"。分享书籍需要介绍 plot。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它也向我们展示生命的价值——我们应该珍惜每一刻。A 项意为"长度"；B 项意为"价值"；C 项意为"困难"；D 项意为"速度"。appreciate every moment 对应 value of life。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的声音有点发抖，但进入故事后我就放松多了。A 项意为"大声的"；B 项意为"清晰的"；C 项意为"颤抖的"；D 项意为"甜美的"。紧张时声音发抖，shaky 正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那让我理解了牺牲的含义。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"理解"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"忽视"。感人的情节让人理解牺牲，understand 正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：你对阅读的热爱真的体现出来了。A 项意为"来"；B 项意为"去"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"走"。come through 意为"表现出来；被理解"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那次经历鼓舞了我，我已经加入了学校读书俱乐部。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"自从"；D 项意为"已经"。鼓励后的积极结果，already 表示已完成。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相学习，分享对书籍的热爱。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"忘记"。读书会的目的就是分享，share 正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与他人分享阅读让这种体验更加有意义。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"无意义的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"困难的"。分享使体验更有意义，meaningful 正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可能会发现你比想象中更喜欢谈论书籍。A 项意为"憎恨"；B 项意为"享受；喜欢"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"害怕"。推荐参与时用 enjoy。故选 B。
