# 中考英语完形填空：学骑独轮车

## 完形填空原文

During the summer, I saw a boy riding a unicycle in the park. He moved smoothly and gracefully, as if he were dancing on one wheel. I was deeply ___1___ and decided that I wanted to learn too.

I told my father about my idea, and he was supportive. He found a second-hand unicycle online and bought it for me. When it arrived, I was thrilled. But as soon as I tried to sit on it, I realised how ___2___ it would be. The unicycle had no handlebars — there was nothing to hold onto. I fell off within two seconds.

My father held my arm and helped me balance. "Take it slowly," he said. "Learning to ride a unicycle takes time and ___3___." I nodded and climbed back on. That first afternoon, I must have fallen at least fifty times. My arms and legs were covered with bruises. But I refused to ___4___.

Every evening after homework, I practised in our neighbourhood. At first, I needed to hold the wall for support. I would push off, pedal a few times and then ___5___ off. Gradually, I was able to ride a little further each day. After one week, I could go about ten metres without any support.

The hardest part was learning to turn. On a bicycle, you can steer with the handlebars. But on a unicycle, you have to shift your ___6___ and twist your hips. It felt very unnatural at first. I kept falling whenever I tried to change direction. A kind old man who watched me practise every day said, "Don't think too much. Let your body feel the ___7___."

After three weeks, something amazing happened. I suddenly found my balance. Riding the unicycle felt as ___8___ as walking. I could go straight, turn left and right, and even ride backwards a little. My father recorded a video of me and sent it to our relatives. They were all very impressed.

Some of my classmates asked me to teach them. I agreed and found that teaching was almost as ___9___ as learning. Watching their excited faces when they managed a few pedals made me very happy.

Learning to ride a unicycle taught me that nothing is impossible if you are willing to put in the effort. Every expert was once a beginner. The key is to keep trying, even when you ___10___ down.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常___，决定也要学。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"担忧的"；C 项意为"惊叹的"；D 项意为"困惑的"。看到精彩表演感到惊叹，"amazed"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到这会多么___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"安全的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"困难的"。两秒就摔下来，说明很难，"difficult"正确。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学骑独轮车需要时间和___。A 项意为"耐心"；B 项意为"沉默"；C 项意为"金钱"；D 项意为"速度"。学习需要耐心，"patience"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我拒绝___。A 项意为"出现"；B 项意为"放弃"；C 项意为"长大"；D 项意为"快点"。摔了很多次但不放弃，"give up"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蹬几下然后___下来。A 项意为"拿"；B 项意为"摔/掉"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"飞"。初学者骑几下就会掉下来，"fall"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你必须转移你的___并扭动臀部。A 项意为"重心/体重"；B 项意为"书包"；C 项意为"鞋子"；D 项意为"眼睛"。骑独轮车转弯需要转移重心，"weight"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让你的身体感受___。A 项意为"温度"；B 项意为"压力"；C 项意为"平衡"；D 项意为"颜色"。骑独轮车的关键是感受平衡，"balance"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：骑独轮车感觉和走路一样___。A 项意为"危险的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"自然的"。熟练后骑车像走路一样自然，"natural"正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教别人几乎和学习一样___。A 项意为"累人的"；B 项意为"有收获的/令人满足的"；C 项意为"恼人的"；D 项意为"令人害怕的"。看到别人学会感到满足，"rewarding"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键是即使___也要继续尝试。A 项意为"坐"；B 项意为"摔倒"；C 项意为"冷静"；D 项意为"打破"。"fall down"意为"摔倒"，与前文不断摔倒呼应。故选 B。
