# 中考英语完形填空：帮邻居遛狗

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday morning, Mrs. Chen from next door knocked on our door. She had to go to the hospital to see her sick mother and needed ___1___ to look after her dog, Doudou. I volunteered to help because I had always wanted to have a dog of my own.

I took Doudou for a walk in the park. At first, he was a bit nervous and pulled on the leash. I spoke to him ___2___ and patted his head. Soon he became calm and started to enjoy the walk. We walked along the path, and Doudou stopped to smell the flowers and trees. It was a ___3___ experience for me.

When we got to a big lawn, I decided to let Doudou run freely for a while. I ___4___ the leash and he ran around happily. Other dog owners were also there with their pets. Doudou made friends with a small white dog. They played ___5___ for about fifteen minutes. I felt so happy watching them.

On our way back, Doudou suddenly stopped. He was looking at something on the ground. I went closer and found a lost wallet. I picked it up and saw an ID card inside. I ___6___ to take it to the police station. The police officer thanked me and said he would try to find the owner. Helping others made me feel ___7___.

When Mrs. Chen returned in the evening, she was very grateful. She gave me a box of cookies as a thank-you ___8___. I told her that I enjoyed spending time with Doudou and would be glad to help again. From this experience, I learned that helping neighbors could not only make them happy but also ___9___ our own lives. Sometimes the best things in life come from simple ___10___ of kindness.

1. A. someone&emsp;&emsp; B. anyone&emsp;&emsp; C. nobody&emsp;&emsp; D. everyone
2. A. loudly&emsp;&emsp; B. gently&emsp;&emsp; C. quickly&emsp;&emsp; D. angrily
3. A. terrible&emsp;&emsp; B. wonderful&emsp;&emsp; C. boring&emsp;&emsp; D. tiring
4. A. held&emsp;&emsp; B. dropped&emsp;&emsp; C. released&emsp;&emsp; D. threw
5. A. alone&emsp;&emsp; B. together&emsp;&emsp; C. silently&emsp;&emsp; D. sadly

7. A. tired&emsp;&emsp; B. proud&emsp;&emsp; C. worried&emsp;&emsp; D. bored
8. A. gift&emsp;&emsp; B. message&emsp;&emsp; C. letter&emsp;&emsp; D. call
9. A. change&emsp;&emsp; B. waste&emsp;&emsp; C. forget&emsp;&emsp; D. ruin
10. A. acts&emsp;&emsp; B. ideas&emsp;&emsp; C. plans&emsp;&emsp; D. words

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她需要有人帮忙照看她的狗豆豆。A 项意为"某人"；B 项意为"任何人"；C 项意为"没有人"；D 项意为"每个人"。need someone to do 表示"需要某人做某事"，故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我轻声对他说话，拍拍他的头。A 项意为"大声地"；B 项意为"轻柔地、温和地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"生气地"。让紧张的狗平静下来应 gentle，故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这对我而言是一次美妙的经历。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美妙的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"令人疲惫的"。全文基调积极，wonderful 最合适，故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我松开牵绳，它开心地跑来跑去。A 项意为"抓住"；B 项意为"掉落"；C 项意为"松开、释放"；D 项意为"扔"。let him run freely 对应 release the leash，故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们一起玩了大约十五分钟。A 项意为"单独"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"沉默地"；D 项意为"伤心地"。两只狗一起玩用 together，故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定把它送到派出所。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"失败"。decide to do 表示做出决定，符合拾金不昧的情节，故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他人让我感到骄傲。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。帮助他人后的正向感受用 proud，故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她给了我一盒饼干作为感谢的礼物。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"信息"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"电话"。as a thank-you gift 是常用表达，故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助邻居不仅能让他们开心，也能丰富我们自己的生活。A 项意为"改变、丰富"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"毁坏"。change our lives 在此为积极含义，故选 A。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时生活中最美好的事来自简单的善举。A 项意为"行为、举动"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"话语"。acts of kindness 意为"善举"，故选 A。
