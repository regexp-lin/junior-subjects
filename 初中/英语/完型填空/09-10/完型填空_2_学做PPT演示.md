# 中考英语完形填空：学做PPT演示

## 完形填空原文

Our history teacher asked each group to make a PPT presentation about ancient Chinese inventions. I was in a group with three classmates. We decided to present ___1___ the Four Great Inventions of ancient China. First, we searched for information on the Internet and found many useful materials. Then we had a meeting to ___2___ how to divide the work.

I was responsible for making the slides. At first, I didn't know much about PowerPoint. I spent a whole afternoon ___3___ how to use it. I learned to add pictures, change fonts, and create simple animations. It was not easy at the beginning, but I kept trying. My father gave me some ___4___ when I had problems. He said, "Don't give up. You can learn anything if you put your mind to it."

After two days of hard work, our PPT was finally ___5___. We had 15 slides with clear titles and beautiful images. Our group leader rehearsed the presentation several times. We also prepared some questions that our classmates might ask. Everyone felt a bit nervous ___6___ the day of the presentation, but we encouraged each other.

When it was our turn, we stood in front of the class. I controlled the slides while my teammates spoke. We introduced the compass, gunpowder, papermaking, and printing. The teacher nodded with a smile and our classmates listened ___7___. After we finished, the whole class gave us warm applause. The teacher said our presentation was well-organized and ___8___.

From this experience, I learned that teamwork is important. I also discovered that learning new skills could open ___9___ doors for me. Now I use PPT often when I need to share my ideas. Making that first presentation gave me confidence to try ___10___ new things in the future.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定演示关于中国古代四大发明。A 项 in 表示"在……里"；B 项 on 表示"关于"；C 项 at 表示"在"；D 项 for 表示"为了"。present on 意为"就……进行演示"，故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后我们开会讨论如何分工。A 项意为"讨论"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。have a meeting to discuss 符合语境，故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我花了一整个下午学习如何使用它。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"学"；C 项意为"玩"；D 项意为"等"。spend time doing sth，且"didn't know much"说明需要 learning，故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我遇到问题时，爸爸给了我一些建议。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"礼物"。give advice 是固定搭配，后文"Don't give up"也是建议，故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过两天的努力，我们的PPT终于准备好了。A 项意为"坏的、破损的"；B 项意为"丢失的"；C 项意为"准备好的"；D 项意为"空的"。be ready 表示完成准备，故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在展示当天，每个人都有点紧张。A 项 on 用于具体某一天；B 项 at 用于时刻；C 项 in 用于较长时间；D 项 by 表示"到……为止"。on the day 是固定表达，故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们认真地听着。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"认真地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"安静地"。listen carefully 表示专注听讲，符合语境，故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说我们的演示组织得很好，很有创意。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"有创意的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"错误的"。与 well-organized 并列的积极评价用 creative，故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析及固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也发现学习新技能能为我打开新的大门。A 项意为"旧的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"相同的"；D 项意为"关闭的"。open new doors 意为"开启新可能"，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：制作第一次演示给了我信心，让我在未来敢于尝试其他新事物。A 项 other 后接复数名词；B 项 another 接单数；C 项 others 单独使用；D 项 the other 指两者中另一方。try other new things 正确，故选 A。
