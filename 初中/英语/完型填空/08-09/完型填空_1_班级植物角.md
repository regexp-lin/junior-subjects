# 中考英语完形填空：班级植物角

## 完形填空原文

Our class decided to create a plant corner near the window. Everyone was excited about the idea. We wanted to make our classroom more lively and ___1___. So we brought different kinds of plants from home.

Li Ming brought a small cactus. He said it didn't need much water and was easy to ___2___. Wang Fang brought a pot of green beans that she had grown from seeds. Zhang Wei brought a spider plant that his grandmother had given him. I brought a jade plant that my mother helped me choose at the flower market.

We placed all the plants on a wooden shelf by the window. Each plant had a small ___3___ with its name and who was taking care of it. We took turns watering the plants every week. We also learned to remove dead leaves and check if the soil was dry.

At first, I was a bit ___4___. I had never taken care of plants before. Would I water them too much or too little? My teacher said, "Just observe them. If the leaves look yellow, you might be watering too much. If they look wilted, they might need more water." I started paying more attention to the plants, and soon I could tell when they needed care.

One morning, we found that Wang Fang's bean plant had grown a white flower. We were all ___5___! She had nurtured it from a tiny seed, and now it was blooming. It made us realise that with patience and care, small things can grow into something beautiful.

The plant corner became our favourite ___6___ in the classroom. During breaks, we would gather around to watch the plants grow. Sometimes we even talked to them, which might sound funny, but we believed it helped them grow better!

Our teacher said the plant corner taught us responsibility. We had to remember to water, to observe, and to ___7___ when something was wrong. These skills would be useful in our daily lives too.

Now our classroom feels more like a small garden. The plants bring us ___8___ and remind us of the beauty of nature. I am proud of our class plant corner, and I will ___9___ to take good care of my jade plant. Maybe one day it will ___10___ into a bigger, stronger plant, just like us growing up.

1. A. noisy &emsp;&emsp; B. bright &emsp;&emsp; C. crowded &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
2. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. find &emsp;&emsp; C. choose &emsp;&emsp; D. care for
3. A. book &emsp;&emsp; B. sign &emsp;&emsp; C. picture &emsp;&emsp; D. gift

8. A. peace &emsp;&emsp; B. trouble &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. doubt
9. A. fail &emsp;&emsp; B. try &emsp;&emsp; C. continue &emsp;&emsp; D. stop
10. A. fall &emsp;&emsp; B. grow &emsp;&emsp; C. break &emsp;&emsp; D. disappear

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们想让教室更生机勃勃、更___。A 项意为"吵闹的"；B 项意为"明亮的/充满生机的"；C 项意为"拥挤的"；D 项意为"空的"。植物角让教室更明亮有生气，"bright"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说仙人掌不需要太多水，很容易___。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"找到"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"照料"。"care for"意为"照料"，符合养植物的语境。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每盆植物都有一个小___，写着名字和照料者。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"标牌"；C 项意为"图片"；D 项意为"礼物"。标牌用于标注植物信息，"sign"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我有点___。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"生气的"。从未养过植物会担心养不好，"worried"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都很___！A 项意为"疲倦的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"紧张的"。小豆子开花了令人惊喜，"surprised"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：植物角成了教室里我们最喜欢的___。A 项意为"角落"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"学科"；D 项意为"爱好"。此处指最喜欢去的场所，"place"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们要记住浇水、观察，在出问题时___。A 项意为"注意到"；B 项意为"忽视"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"隐藏"。"notice when something was wrong"意为"注意到问题"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：植物带给我们___，提醒我们自然之美。A 项意为"平静/安宁"；B 项意为"麻烦"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"怀疑"。植物带来平和感，"peace"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会___好好照料我的玉树。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"继续"；D 项意为"停止"。"continue to do"意为"继续做某事"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许有一天它会___成更大更壮的植物。A 项意为"落下"；B 项意为"生长"；C 项意为"打破"；D 项意为"消失"。"grow into"意为"长成"。故选 B。
