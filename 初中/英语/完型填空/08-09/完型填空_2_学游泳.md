# 中考英语完形填空：学游泳

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, my parents signed me up for swimming lessons at the community sports centre. I had always been afraid of water, so I was very ___1___ about the first lesson.

On the first day, our coach Mr Chen asked us to sit by the pool and watch him demonstrate. He showed us how to hold our breath underwater and how to move our arms and legs. "Swimming is like riding a bicycle," he said. "Once you learn it, you never forget. But you have to overcome your ___2___ first."

We started with the easiest skill: floating on our backs. I lay down in the shallow water and tried to relax. At first, my body wanted to sink. I was so scared that I ___3___ out of the water immediately. Mr Chen came over and said, "Trust the water. Breathe slowly. Let your body feel the water holding you." I tried again, and this time I managed to float for a few seconds. It felt like a small ___4___.

The next few lessons, we practised kicking our legs and moving our arms. We wore float boards to help us stay on the surface. Every time I made a little progress, Mr Chen would give me a thumbs up. His ___5___ made me want to try harder.

By the fourth lesson, I could swim a short distance without the float board. I was so proud of myself! But then I made a mistake — I breathed at the wrong time and swallowed some water. I started coughing and felt ___6___. I thought about giving up. But Mr Chen said, "Everyone swallows water when they learn. It's part of the process. Don't let one small problem ___7___ you."

I took a short rest and went back into the pool. With more practice, I gradually improved. By the end of the two-week course, I could swim across the pool. My parents came to watch the final test and cheered when I ___8___ the other side.

Learning to swim taught me an important lesson: fear can be overcome with patience and practice. What seemed impossible at first became ___9___ after I took the first step. Now I enjoy swimming, and I often go to the pool with my friends. The water that once frightened me has become a place of ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
2. A. tiredness &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. hunger &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
3. A. swam &emsp;&emsp; B. jumped &emsp;&emsp; C. floated &emsp;&emsp; D. walked
4. A. failure &emsp;&emsp; B. victory &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. accident
5. A. criticism &emsp;&emsp; B. encouragement &emsp;&emsp; C. silence &emsp;&emsp; D. doubt
6. A. comfortable &emsp;&emsp; B. embarrassed &emsp;&emsp; C. peaceful &emsp;&emsp; D. confident
7. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. follow &emsp;&emsp; D. save
8. A. left &emsp;&emsp; B. reached &emsp;&emsp; C. passed &emsp;&emsp; D. missed
9. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. possible &emsp;&emsp; C. stranger &emsp;&emsp; D. duller
10. A. danger &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. anger

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直怕水，所以对第一节课非常___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。怕水的人上游泳课会紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但你得先克服你的___。A 项意为"疲倦"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"饥饿"；D 项意为"寒冷"。学游泳需克服对水的恐惧，"fear"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太害怕了，立刻从水中___出来。A 项意为"游泳"；B 项意为"跳"；C 项意为"漂浮"；D 项意为"走"。"jump out of the water"意为"从水中跳出来"，因害怕而急忙出水。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感觉像是一个小小的___。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"胜利"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"意外"。能漂浮几秒钟是小进步、小胜利，"victory"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的___让我更想努力。A 项意为"批评"；B 项意为"鼓励"；C 项意为"沉默"；D 项意为"怀疑"。"give a thumbs up"是鼓励，"encouragement"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始咳嗽，感到___。A 项意为"舒适的"；B 项意为"尴尬的"；C 项意为"平静的"；D 项意为"自信的"。呛水后感到尴尬难堪，"embarrassed"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：别让一个小问题___你。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"阻止"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"拯救"。"stop sb"意为"阻止某人（继续）"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母来看结业测试，当我___对岸时欢呼起来。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"到达"；C 项意为"经过"；D 项意为"错过"。"reach the other side"意为"游到对岸"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初看似不可能的事，在我迈出第一步后变得___。A 项意为"更难的"；B 项意为"可能的"；C 项意为"更奇怪的"；D 项意为"更枯燥的"。"possible"与"impossible"形成对比。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：曾经让我害怕的水变成了___的地方。A 项意为"危险"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"愤怒"。学会游泳后享受水中乐趣，"fun"正确。故选 B。
