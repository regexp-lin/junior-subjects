# 中考英语完形填空：开学第一天

## 完形填空原文

September 1st was a special day — it was the first day of the new school year. I woke up early and felt a mix of excitement and worry. Everything seemed ___1___ because I was entering a new grade with new teachers.

After breakfast, I put on my new school uniform and checked my bag one more time. I had prepared all my textbooks, notebooks and pens the night before. My mother smiled and said, "You look ___2___. Don't worry, you'll have a great day." Her words made me feel a little better.

When I arrived at school, the playground was full of students. Some were chatting happily, while others looked just as ___3___ as I was. I saw my best friend Lily near the school gate. She waved at me and ran over. "I'm so glad to see you!" she said. Having a friend nearby made me feel much more ___4___.

Our new classroom was on the third floor. It was bigger and brighter than last year's. The desks were arranged in neat rows. I chose a seat by the window so I could enjoy the ___5___ of the trees outside. Soon, our new head teacher walked in. She introduced herself as Ms. Wang and told us about the rules for the new term.

Ms. Wang seemed strict but ___6___. She said, "This year will be challenging, but I believe every one of you can succeed if you work hard." Her encouragement gave me ___7___. I decided to set higher goals for myself this year.

During the break, I met several new classmates. One boy named Tom had just moved to our city from Shanghai. He seemed shy at first, but we soon found that we both loved basketball. We agreed to ___8___ together after school. Making new friends was easier than I had expected.

By the end of the day, I no longer felt worried. Instead, I was looking forward to the days ahead. The new school year was full of ___9___. I wrote in my diary that night: "Today was a good start. I will try my best to make this year ___10___."

1. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. simple

3. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
4. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. confused &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
5. A. colour &emsp;&emsp; B. view &emsp;&emsp; C. smell &emsp;&emsp; D. sound
6. A. impatient &emsp;&emsp; B. careless &emsp;&emsp; C. kind &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
7. A. pressure &emsp;&emsp; B. trouble &emsp;&emsp; C. confidence &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
8. A. study &emsp;&emsp; B. practise &emsp;&emsp; C. cook &emsp;&emsp; D. sing
9. A. problems &emsp;&emsp; B. dangers &emsp;&emsp; C. possibilities &emsp;&emsp; D. complaints

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一切似乎都很___，因为我要进入新年级。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"陌生的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"简单的"。新年级、新老师，一切感觉陌生，"strange"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你看起来___了。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"准备好的"；C 项意为"生病的"；D 项意为"老的"。穿好校服、检查书包后准备就绪，"ready"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：其他人看起来和我一样___。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。开学第一天大家都紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有朋友在身边让我感觉___多了。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"放松的"；C 项意为"困惑的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。见到好朋友会放松，"relaxed"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选了窗边的座位，这样可以欣赏外面树木的___。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"风景"；C 项意为"气味"；D 项意为"声音"。窗外看到的是景色，"view"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师看起来严格但___。A 项意为"不耐烦的"；B 项意为"粗心的"；C 项意为"和善的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。"strict but kind"表示严格但和善，转折关系。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的鼓励给了我___。A 项意为"压力"；B 项意为"麻烦"；C 项意为"信心"；D 项意为"悲伤"。老师鼓励带来信心，"confidence"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们约好放学后一起___。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"做饭"；D 项意为"唱歌"。前文提到都爱篮球，放学后一起练习，"practise"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新学年充满了___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"危险"；C 项意为"可能性"；D 项意为"抱怨"。积极展望新学年，"possibilities"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会尽力让这一年变得___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"难忘的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"短暂的"。努力让学年精彩难忘，"unforgettable"正确。故选 B。
