# 中考英语完形填空：新班主任

## 完形填空原文

When the new term started, we learned that we would have a new head teacher. Everyone was curious. Our old teacher, Mr. Li, had been with us for two years and we all ___1___ him. We wondered what the new teacher would be like.

On the first day, a young woman walked into our classroom. She wore a simple dress and carried a big bag of books. "Good morning, everyone. I'm Ms. Chen, your new head teacher," she said with a warm ___2___. Her voice was soft but clear.

Ms. Chen did something ___3___ on her first day. Instead of talking about rules, she asked each of us to write down three things we hoped to achieve this year. She collected our papers and read some of them aloud. "I want to improve my English," one student wrote. "I want to make more friends," wrote ___4___. Ms. Chen nodded and said, "I will do my best to help each of you reach your goals."

In the following weeks, we found that Ms. Chen was different from other teachers. She never shouted at us when we made mistakes. ___5___, she would patiently explain where we went wrong and how to do better. She also organised many interesting activities, such as English speech contests, science experiments and art exhibitions. These activities made our school life more ___6___.

One thing I admired most about Ms. Chen was her fairness. She treated every student ___7___, whether they were top students or struggled with their studies. She spent extra time helping those who fell behind. She often said, "Every student has potential. Sometimes they just need someone to ___8___ in them."

Gradually, our class changed. Students who used to be quiet began to raise their hands in class. Those who often argued learned to ___9___ with each other. Our test scores improved too. By the end of the first month, Ms. Chen had won the hearts of the whole class.

I am grateful to have Ms. Chen as our teacher. She taught me that a good teacher does not just teach knowledge — a good teacher inspires students to become ___10___ versions of themselves.

7. A. coldly &emsp;&emsp; B. differently &emsp;&emsp; C. equally &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李老师带了我们两年，我们都___他。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"不喜欢"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"责怪"。老师离开，学生想念，"missed"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她带着温暖的___说。A 项意为"微笑"；B 项意为"哭泣"；C 项意为"喊叫"；D 项意为"手势"。新老师友善地微笑着自我介绍，"smile"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：陈老师第一天做了一件___的事。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"不寻常的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。不讲规则而让学生写目标，做法与众不同，"unusual"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___写道"我想交更多朋友"。A 项意为"其他的"（形容词）；B 项意为"另一个"（泛指）；C 项意为"其他人"（复数）；D 项意为"另一个"（特指两者中的另一个）。此处泛指另一位同学，"another"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连接副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___，她会耐心解释。A 项意为"然而"；B 项意为"相反/取而代之"；C 项意为"此外"；D 项意为"因此"。前文说她不会大喊，取而代之的是耐心解释，"Instead"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些活动使我们的学校生活更加___。A 项意为"有压力的"；B 项意为"丰富多彩的"；C 项意为"痛苦的"；D 项意为"浪费的"。各种活动让生活丰富多彩，"colourful"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她___对待每个学生。A 项意为"冷淡地"；B 项意为"不同地"；C 项意为"平等地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。前文提到公平，"equally"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们只需要有人___他们。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"竞争"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"投资"。"believe in"意为"相信/信任"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经常吵架的同学学会了互相___。A 项意为"打架"；B 项意为"争论"；C 项意为"合作"；D 项意为"不同意"。与吵架形成对比，学会合作，"cooperate"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好老师激励学生成为___的自己。A 项意为"更差的"；B 项意为"更老的"；C 项意为"更弱的"；D 项意为"更好的"。老师帮助学生进步，"better"正确。故选 D。
