# 中考英语完形填空：最后的值日

## 完形填空原文

It was the last duty day of the term. Tomorrow we would have our graduation ceremony, and then summer break would begin. For three years, Li Na and I had been on duty together every Tuesday. This would be our ___1___ time.

We arrived at the classroom early. The room was quiet — no students, no noise. Just empty desks and the blackboard we had cleaned so many times ___2___. "It feels strange," Li Na said. "I've always complained about doing duty, but now I don't want it to end."

I knew exactly what she meant. We had often hurried through our duties, ___3___ to go home. But today we moved slowly. We swept the floor carefully. We wiped every desk. We watered the plants by the window. We wanted to do everything perfectly, as if doing it well would somehow make the moment last ___4___.

"When did we grow up?" Li Na asked suddenly. "It feels like yesterday we were nervous first-year students, afraid of making mistakes. Now we're about to leave."

I didn't have an answer. Time had passed so quickly. The classroom had witnessed our laughter, our tears, and our growth. Every scratch on the desk, every mark on the wall — they all told ___5___ of our three years here.

We finished our duties and stood at the door, looking back at the room. "Should we leave a note for the next class?" Li Na suggested. "Something to encourage them?"

We found a piece of paper and wrote: "Take care of this classroom. It will take care of you. — The last duty team of Class 3." We stuck it on the wall near the door. Maybe the new students would see it and feel the same ___6___ we had felt for this place.

As we walked out, Li Na took one last look. "Thank you," she said quietly. I wasn't sure if she was talking to me or to the classroom. Perhaps both. We had ___7___ this space together, and it had given us more than a place to study — it had given us memories and friendships that would last a lifetime.

The hall was empty. Our footsteps echoed. For the first time, I realized that leaving wasn't just about moving to a new school. It was about ___8___ a chapter of our lives and stepping into the next one with courage and hope.

I'll never forget that last duty day. It taught me that even the most ordinary tasks can become meaningful when we ___9___ them with our hearts. Sweeping the floor, wiping the desks — these small acts were our way of saying goodbye to a place we had loved.

Li Na and I promised to stay in touch. We might do duty in different schools next year, but we would always remember this day — the day we cleaned the classroom together for the last time and said goodbye to our ___10___ middle school life.

## 选项

1. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. last &emsp;&emsp; C. best &emsp;&emsp; D. worst
2. A. ago &emsp;&emsp; B. before &emsp;&emsp; C. later &emsp;&emsp; D. again
3. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. eager &emsp;&emsp; C. ready &emsp;&emsp; D. sorry
4. A. shorter &emsp;&emsp; B. longer &emsp;&emsp; C. faster &emsp;&emsp; D. slower
5. A. stories &emsp;&emsp; B. secrets &emsp;&emsp; C. lessons &emsp;&emsp; D. news
6. A. love &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
7. A. shared &emsp;&emsp; B. built &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaned &emsp;&emsp; D. left
8. A. closing &emsp;&emsp; B. opening &emsp;&emsp; C. reading &emsp;&emsp; D. writing
9. A. do &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. start &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
10. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. happy &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. short

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这将是我们___一起值日。A 项意为"第一次"；B 项意为"最后一次"；C 项意为"最好"；D 项意为"最差"。文章标题为"最后的值日"，且明天毕业，故为"最后一次"（last）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只有空桌子和我们曾清洁过很多次的黑板___。A 项意为"以前"；B 项意为"以前"；C 项意为"之后"；D 项意为"再次"。"before"在此表"以往、从前"，强调过去多次清洁的经历。ago 多与具体时间连用，before 可单独使用表"以往"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们曾经常匆忙完成值日，___回家。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"渴望"；C 项意为"准备好"；D 项意为"抱歉"。"eager to go home"表示"急着想回家"，与"hurried through"对应。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好像做好它就能 somehow 让这一刻持续得___。A 项意为"更短"；B 项意为"更长"；C 项意为"更快"；D 项意为"更慢"。不想分别，希望当下"更久"（longer）一些。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：桌上的每道划痕、墙上的每个印记——都在述说我们三年来的___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"教训"；D 项意为"新闻"。痕迹承载回忆，是"故事"（stories）。"tell stories of"意为"讲述……的故事"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许新生会看到它，感受到我们曾对这里怀有的那份___。A 项意为"爱"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"担忧"；D 项意为"愤怒"。对教室的情感是"爱"（love）。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们曾___这个空间，它也给予了我们比学习场所更多的东西。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"建造"；C 项意为"清洁"；D 项意为"离开"。"shared this space"表示一起使用、共同经历这个教室。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：离开不仅是去新学校，而是___人生的一个章节，怀着勇气和希望迈入下一章。A 项意为"结束/合上"；B 项意为"打开"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"写"。"closing a chapter"喻指结束一段经历。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们用真心___它们时，再平凡的任务也会变得有意义。A 项意为"做"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"开始"；D 项意为"忘记"。"do...with our hearts"意为"用心去做"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：告别我们___的初中生活。A 项意为"长的"；B 项意为"快乐的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"短的"。与"last"对应，强调值得怀念的"快乐"（happy）初中生活。short 语法可通但语义不如 happy 贴切。故选 B。
