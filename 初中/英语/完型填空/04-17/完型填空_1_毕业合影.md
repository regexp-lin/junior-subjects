# 中考英语完形填空：毕业合影

## 完形填空原文

The day of our graduation photo finally arrived. For three years, we had studied, laughed, and grown up together in this classroom. Now we were about to ___1___ it for the last time as a whole class.

Our teacher, Mrs. Li, asked us to stand in rows according to our height. I stood in the second row, right next to my best friend Xiao Ming. He had been quiet all morning. "Are you okay?" I whispered. He nodded but didn't ___2___. I knew he would miss our class as much as I would.

The photographer adjusted his camera and asked us to smile. "Say cheese!" he shouted. We all laughed and smiled — some ___3___, some with tears in their eyes. The click of the camera captured that moment forever.

Looking at the photo later, I noticed something special. In the front row sat our class monitor, who had always been strict but fair. Next to her was the quiet girl who loved reading. In the back stood the boys who had won the basketball ___4___ last month. Every face in that photo told a story of our three years together.

We had shared so much — stressful exam weeks, exciting sports days, and silly jokes during breaks. We had argued sometimes, but we had also ___5___ each other through difficult times. The photo was not just a picture. It was a memory of our friendship and growth.

That evening, I put the photo on my desk. I knew that in a few months we would go to different high schools. We might not see each other ___6___ as before. But whenever I looked at this photo, I would remember the good times we had shared.

Graduation means saying goodbye, but it also means carrying forward the ___7___ we have learned and the friendships we have built. The photo will always remind me that no matter where life takes us, we were once a family in Class 3.

Years from now, when we look at this photo again, we will smile. We will remember the young people we were and the dreams we had. And perhaps we will ___8___ one another that the best days of our lives included each other.

So here's to our class — to every face in that photo, to every story behind each smile. May we all find success and happiness in our ___9___ journeys. And may we never forget that we once stood together, shoulder to shoulder, as one ___10___ class.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我们将最后一次作为整个班级___它。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"参观"；C 项意为"打扫"；D 项意为"建造"。毕业照意味着即将毕业，"离开"（leave）教室和学校。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他点点头但没有___。A 项意为"移动"；B 项意为"说话"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"等待"。他情绪低落，只是点头，"没有说话"（speak）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都笑了——有些人笑得很___，有些人眼里含着泪。A 项意为"自豪地"；B 项意为"勇敢地"；C 项意为"灿烂地"；D 项意为"小心地"。"smile brightly"表示"灿烂地微笑"，与眼泪形成对比。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：后排站着上个月赢得篮球___的男生们。A 项意为"比赛（泛指）"；B 项意为"比赛/竞赛"；C 项意为"队伍"；D 项意为"练习"。指具体某次篮球"比赛"（match），"win the match"为常用搭配。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们有时会争吵，但我们也曾___彼此度过艰难时光。A 项意为"支持/帮助"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"观看"。"support each other through difficult times"意为"互相扶持度过困难时期"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们可能不会像以前那样___见面了。A 项意为"经常"；B 项意为"好"；C 项意为"多"；D 项意为"快"。毕业后各奔东西，见面不会"像以前那样频繁"（as often）。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：毕业意味着告别，也意味着带着我们学到的___和我们建立的友谊继续前行。A 项意为"技能"；B 项意为"教训/道理"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"成绩"。这里指人生"道理"（lessons），与友谊并列。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许我们会___彼此，生命中最美好的日子里有对方的陪伴。A 项意为"告诉"；B 项意为"提醒"；C 项意为"问"；D 项意为"承诺"。"remind one another"意为"互相提醒"，回忆时唤起彼此的回忆。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：愿我们都能在各自的___征途中找到成功与幸福。A 项意为"未来的"；B 项意为"过去的"；C 项意为"现在的"；D 项意为"近来的"。毕业祝福指向"未来"（future）的人生道路。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：愿我们永不忘记，我们曾肩并肩站在一起，是一个___的集体。A 项意为"成功的"；B 项意为"友善的"；C 项意为"团结的"；D 项意为"著名的"。"united"强调班级的团结一心，与"肩并肩"呼应。故选 C。
