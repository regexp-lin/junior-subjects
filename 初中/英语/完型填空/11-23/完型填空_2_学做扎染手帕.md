# 中考英语完形填空：学做扎染手帕

## 完形填空原文

In our art class last week, we learned tie-dye, a traditional craft. The teacher brought white cotton handkerchiefs and bottles of fabric dye. She said we could create our own patterns by folding and tying the cloth before ___1___ it. I had seen tie-dye clothes in shops but never tried to make ___2___ myself.

First, we laid the handkerchief flat on the table. The teacher showed us different folding methods—we could fold it into a triangle, a square, or just roll it. I chose to fold mine like a fan. Then we used rubber bands to tie the cloth tightly. The teacher said the tied parts would stay white because the dye could not ___3___ them. The rest would become colorful.

Next, we put on gloves and mixed the dye with water. I chose blue and purple—my favorite colors. We dipped the tied handkerchief into the dye. I had to wait for about twenty minutes. I was so ___4___ to see the result that I kept checking the time.

When the time was up, we took out the handkerchief and rinsed it with cold water until the water ran clear. Then we ___5___ cut the rubber bands and unfolded the cloth. I was amazed! The pattern was beautiful—circles and lines in blue and purple. No two handkerchiefs looked the ___6___. Each one was unique.

I gave my tie-dye handkerchief to my mom. She said it was lovely and would use it when she goes out. Making tie-dye taught me that we can create beautiful things with simple ___7___. I want to try tie-dyeing a T-shirt next time. Maybe I can make one for my dad as a birthday gift. This craft is fun and ___8___ to learn. Anyone can give it a try. I plan to ___9___ what I learned with my cousin so we can make tie-dye gifts together. Learning from a good ___10___ and practicing hands-on is the best way to master a skill.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：染色前通过折叠绑扎布料来创造图案。A 项意为"洗"；B 项 dye 意为"染色"；C 项意为"晾干"；D 项意为"穿"。扎染工艺用 before dyeing。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从没亲手做过一件。A 项 it 指特定物；B 项 them 指复数；C 项 one 泛指"一个"；D 项 those 指那些。make one 指做一件扎染品。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：绑住的部分会保持白色，因为染料无法到达。A 项 reach 意为"到达"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"覆盖"；D 项意为"找到"。染料进不去用 could not reach。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我十分期待结果，不停看时间。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项 excited 意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"担心的"。期待成果用 excited。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们小心地剪开橡皮筋并展开布料。A 项 carefully 意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"快速地"。避免弄坏作品用 carefully。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有两条手帕看起来一样。A 项 the same 意为"相同的"；B 项 different 表不同；C 项意为"美的"；D 项意为"丑的"。look the same 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用简单的工具就能创造美。A 项 tools 意为"工具"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"时间"。扎染用简单 tools。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这项手工艺有趣且易学。A 项意为"难的"；B 项 easy 意为"容易的"；C 项意为"贵的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。Anyone can try 说明 easy。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：打算和表妹分享所学，一起做扎染礼物。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"买"；C 项 share 意为"分享"；D 项意为"保留"。分享技能用 share。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：向好老师学习并动手实践是掌握技能的最佳方式。A 项 teacher 意为"老师"；B 项意为"学生"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"家长"。学习技艺用 teacher。故选 A。
