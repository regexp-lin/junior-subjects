# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校心理健康主题活动

## 完形填空原文

Last Thursday, our school held a Mental Health Day with various activities. The theme was "Care for Yourself, Care for Others." I joined the event with my classmates. We all thought it was important to learn how to ___1___ stress and stay positive.

In the morning, we attended a lecture in the school hall. A psychologist from the city hospital came to talk to us. She said that feeling stressed before exams was normal. The key was to find healthy ways to ___2___ with it. She suggested taking short walks, listening to music, or talking to friends. She also reminded us that getting enough sleep was ___3___. Many students nodded and wrote down her tips.

After the lecture, we took part in group activities on the playground. One activity was a "compliment circle." We stood in a circle and each person had to say something ___4___ about the person on their right. At first, I felt a little shy, but when I received my compliment, I smiled. It felt good to both give and receive kind words. We also did some relaxing exercises. The teacher asked us to close our eyes, take deep breaths, and imagine ourselves in a ___5___ place like a beach or a forest.

In the afternoon, we wrote our worries on small cards and put them into a "worry box." The teacher said we did not have to ___6___ everything in our hearts. Sharing could make us feel lighter. I wrote about my fear of the coming math test. After writing it down, I somehow felt a bit ___7___.

That day I learned that taking care of our mental health is as important as taking care of our bodies. We should not be afraid to ask for ___8___ when we need it. Our school will hold such activities every term. I am looking forward to the next one and will encourage more friends to ___9___. We should all learn to treat ourselves ___10___ and be kind to our minds.

## 选项

1. A. avoid &emsp;&emsp; B. manage &emsp;&emsp; C. create &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
2. A. deal &emsp;&emsp; B. play &emsp;&emsp; C. fight &emsp;&emsp; D. agree
3. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. necessary &emsp;&emsp; C. possible &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
4. A. bad &emsp;&emsp; B. kind &emsp;&emsp; C. true &emsp;&emsp; D. wrong
5. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. noisy &emsp;&emsp; C. peaceful &emsp;&emsp; D. crowded
6. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. share &emsp;&emsp; C. throw &emsp;&emsp; D. sell
7. A. heavier &emsp;&emsp; B. worse &emsp;&emsp; C. better &emsp;&emsp; D. angrier
8. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. time
9. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. refuse
10. A. badly &emsp;&emsp; B. kindly &emsp;&emsp; C. coldly &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学会如何应对压力、保持积极很重要。A 项意为"避免"；B 项 manage 意为"应对/管理"；C 项意为"制造"；D 项意为"忘记"。manage stress 为常见搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键是找到健康的方式来应对它。A 项 deal with 意为"应对"；B 项意为"玩"；C 项意为"打架"；D 项意为"同意"。deal with stress 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：充足的睡眠是必要的。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"必要的"；C 项意为"可能的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。健康建议用 necessary。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每人要说右边同学的好话。A 项意为"坏的"；B 项 kind 意为"善意/赞美"；C 项意为"真实的"；D 项意为"错误的"。compliment 赞美用 kind words。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：想象自己在海滩或森林等宁静之处。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"吵闹的"；C 项 peaceful 意为"宁静的"；D 项意为"拥挤的"。放松想象用 peaceful place。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不必把所有事都藏在心里。A 项 keep 意为"保留/埋藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"卖"。keep in heart 表藏在心里。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写下来后感觉好了一些。A 项意为"更沉重"；B 项意为"更糟"；C 项 better 意为"更好"；D 项意为"更生气"。宣泄后感觉 better。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：需要时不要害怕求助。A 项意为"钱"；B 项 ask for help 意为"求助"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"时间"。心理健康语境用 help。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：会鼓励更多朋友参加。A 项意为"离开"；B 项 join 意为"参加"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"拒绝"。join the activity 参加活动。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都要学会善待自己。A 项意为"糟糕地"；B 项 kindly 意为"善意地"；C 项意为"冷漠地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。心理健康用 treat ourselves kindly。故选 B。
