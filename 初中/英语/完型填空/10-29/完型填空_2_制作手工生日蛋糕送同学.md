# 中考英语完形填空：制作手工生日蛋糕送同学

## 完形填空原文

Next Saturday is Li Ming's birthday. He is my best friend at school. I wanted to give him a special gift — a cake made by ___1___. I had never made a cake before, so I asked my mother to teach me.

On Sunday morning, we started. First, we got the ingredients ___2___: flour, eggs, sugar, butter, and milk. Mum said we must measure everything carefully, or the cake would not taste good. I followed her instructions step by step.

We mixed the flour with the eggs and sugar in a big bowl. I ___3___ the mixture for about five minutes until it became smooth. Then we added butter and milk. The next step was to pour the mixture into a cake tin and put it in the oven. We had to ___4___ for forty minutes. The kitchen soon smelt wonderful!

When the cake was ready, we took it out and let it cool. Then came the fun part — decorating! I spread white cream on top and wrote "Happy Birthday, Li Ming" with chocolate. I also put some fresh strawberries around the ___5___. It looked really beautiful.

On Li Ming's birthday, I brought the cake to school. When he saw it, his eyes became wide. "You made this for me?" he asked in ___6___. I nodded and said, "Yes! I hope you like it." He smiled and gave me a big hug.

We shared the cake with our classmates at lunch. Everyone said it was ___7___. Li Ming said it was the best birthday gift he had ever received. Making the cake took me a whole morning, but seeing his happy face made all the hard work ___8___. I learned that a handmade gift can mean much more than something bought from a ___9___. It carries your care and effort. I will try to make more things with my own hands for the people I ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. himself &emsp;&emsp; B. myself &emsp;&emsp; C. herself &emsp;&emsp; D. themselves
2. A. ready &emsp;&emsp; B. away &emsp;&emsp; C. back &emsp;&emsp; D. out
3. A. shook &emsp;&emsp; B. stirred &emsp;&emsp; C. dropped &emsp;&emsp; D. moved
4. A. stand &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. sit &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
5. A. middle &emsp;&emsp; B. side &emsp;&emsp; C. edge &emsp;&emsp; D. top
6. A. surprise &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
7. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. delicious &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
8. A. worthless &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. worth it &emsp;&emsp; D. impossible
9. A. library &emsp;&emsp; B. hospital &emsp;&emsp; C. bank &emsp;&emsp; D. shop
10. A. hate &emsp;&emsp; B. know &emsp;&emsp; C. care about &emsp;&emsp; D. laugh at

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 反身代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想送他一份特别的礼物——我自己做的蛋糕。A 项意为"他自己"；B 项意为"我自己"；C 项意为"她自己"；D 项意为"他们自己"。主语是 I，亲手做的蛋糕用 made by myself。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：首先，我们准备好原料：面粉、鸡蛋、糖、黄油和牛奶。A 项 get...ready 意为"准备好"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"出去"。做蛋糕前要 get ingredients ready。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我搅拌混合物大约五分钟，直到它变得光滑。A 项意为"摇晃"；B 项意为"搅拌"；C 项意为"掉落"；D 项意为"移动"。做蛋糕时搅拌面糊用 stirred。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们得等四十分钟。A 项意为"站立"；B 项 wait 意为"等待"；C 项意为"坐"；D 项意为"休息"。蛋糕在烤箱里烘烤需要 wait。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还在蛋糕的边缘放了些新鲜草莓。A 项意为"中间"；B 项意为"侧面"；C 项 around the edge 意为"沿着边缘"；D 项意为"顶部"。装饰蛋糕时草莓可放 around the edge。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他惊讶地问"这是你为我做的？"A 项 in surprise 意为"惊讶地"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"悲伤"。收到亲手做的礼物会 in surprise。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家说蛋糕很好吃。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美味的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。分享蛋糕后大家说 delicious。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到他开心的表情，所有的辛苦都值得了。A 项意为"毫无价值的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项 worth it 意为"值得"；D 项意为"不可能的"。付出得到回报用 made it worth it。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工礼物比在商店买的东西更有意义。A 项意为"图书馆"；B 项意为"医院"；C 项意为"银行"；D 项意为"商店"。与 handmade 相对的是 from a shop。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会试着为自己在意的人亲手做更多东西。A 项意为"讨厌"；B 项意为"认识"；C 项 care about 意为"关心、在意"；D 项意为"嘲笑"。送礼物给 care about 的人。故选 C。
