# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校志愿者招募面试

## 完形填空原文

Last week, our school posted a notice about recruiting volunteers for the library. I have always loved reading and wanted to ___1___ something for the school. So I decided to sign up for the interview.

The interview was on Friday afternoon. I arrived at the meeting room ten minutes early. There were about twenty students waiting outside. I felt a bit ___2___ because I had never been to an interview before. I took a deep breath and told myself to stay calm.

Soon it was my ___3___. I walked in and saw two teachers sitting behind a desk. They smiled and asked me to sit down. The first question was, "Why do you want to be a volunteer?" I said I enjoyed helping others and could learn a lot from working in the library. They seemed pleased with my ___4___.

Then they asked about my free time. I said I had two free afternoons ___5___ week. They nodded and wrote something down. The last question was, "What will you do if a student makes too much noise in the library?" I thought for a moment and said I would ___6___ remind them quietly instead of shouting. The teachers smiled again.

After the interview, I waited for the result. Three days later, I got good news — I was ___7___. I was so happy! Now I will go to the library every Tuesday and Thursday to help ___8___ books and keep the reading area tidy. I believe this experience will help me grow and make new ___9___. If you also want to try, don't be afraid. Just be yourself and show your ___10___ to help!

## 选项

1. A. do &emsp;&emsp; B. get &emsp;&emsp; C. take &emsp;&emsp; D. make
2. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy
3. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. chance &emsp;&emsp; C. turn &emsp;&emsp; D. way
4. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. question &emsp;&emsp; C. idea &emsp;&emsp; D. problem
5. A. all &emsp;&emsp; B. both &emsp;&emsp; C. each &emsp;&emsp; D. every
6. A. politely &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. badly
7. A. refused &emsp;&emsp; B. chosen &emsp;&emsp; C. missed &emsp;&emsp; D. left
8. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. organize &emsp;&emsp; D. write
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
10. A. wish &emsp;&emsp; B. hope &emsp;&emsp; C. need &emsp;&emsp; D. plan

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直喜欢阅读，想为学校做点事情。A 项 do something 意为"做某事"；B 项意为"得到"；C 项意为"拿、取"；D 项意为"制作"。want to do something for the school 表示"想为学校做贡献"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点紧张，因为我从来没有参加过面试。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"放松的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"困倦的"。首次参加面试通常会感到 nervous。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很快轮到我了。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项 It's one's turn 意为"轮到某人"；D 项意为"方式"。排队等候用 it's my turn。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师们似乎对我的回答很满意。A 项意为"回答、答案"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"主意"；D 项意为"问题、麻烦"。回答面试问题后，对方对 answer 满意。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 限定词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我说每周有两个下午有空。A 项 all week 意为"整周"；B 项 both 指两者，不与 week 单数搭配；C 项 each week 意为"每周"；D 项 every week 意为"每周"。two free afternoons every week 为常见表达，every 强调定期、每逢。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会礼貌地轻声提醒他们，而不是大喊。A 项意为"礼貌地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"糟糕地"。instead of shouting 说明是 politely remind。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：三天后我收到好消息——我被选中了。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项 be chosen 意为"被选中"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"离开"。面试成功即被 chosen。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我将每周二和周四去图书馆帮忙整理书籍、保持阅读区整洁。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"整理、组织"；D 项意为"写"。图书馆志愿者 organize books。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我相信这段经历会帮助我成长并结交新朋友。A 项意为"钱"；B 项 make friends 意为"交朋友"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"麻烦"。志愿活动通常能 make new friends。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做你自己，展示你帮助他人的愿望。A 项 wish 意为"愿望"；B 项 hope 意为"希望"；C 项意为"需要"；D 项意为"计划"。show your wish to help 表示"展现帮助的意愿"。故选 A。
