# 中考英语完形填空：小区里的义工

## 完形填空原文

Every Saturday morning, a group of students from our community gather at the small garden near the gate. We are volunteers. Our job is to help clean the garden and ___1___ flowers for the elderly residents.

It all started when Mrs. Zhang, who lives in Building 3, fell and hurt her leg. She could no longer take care of her beloved garden. The flowers began to wither. Some neighbors ___2___ to help, but they were too busy. Then our science teacher, Mr. Wang, had an idea. "Why don't we form a student volunteer group?" he said.

At first, only five of us joined. We didn't know much about gardening, but we were willing to ___3___. Mr. Wang taught us how to water plants, remove weeds, and trim the branches. Week by week, the garden began to look better.

Mrs. Zhang sat on a bench and watched us work. Her eyes were full of gratitude. "You children have ___4___ hearts," she said. "This garden was my comfort when I was sad. Seeing it alive again makes me so happy." Her words made us feel that our work was meaningful.

More students joined our group. Now we have over twenty members. We not only take care of the garden but also help other ___5___ in the community. Some of us read to the blind Mr. Li. Others help carry groceries for Grandma Wang. Small acts, but they make a big difference.

One afternoon, a little girl from the community came to the garden. She said she wanted to be a volunteer ___6___. "My grandma said you make our community beautiful. I want to do that too," she said. We welcomed her warmly. It was wonderful to see that our actions had ___7___ others.

Being a volunteer has taught me many things. I learned that helping others doesn't require much — just time and a willing heart. I also learned that a community becomes ___8___ when people care for each other. We are not just neighbors; we are like a big family.

I will never forget the ___9___ on Mrs. Zhang's face when she saw her garden in bloom again. That smile was the best reward. If you have time, consider volunteering in your community. You might be ___10___ by how much joy it brings — not just to others, but to yourself.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的工作是帮忙打扫花园并___花。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"种植"；C 项意为"采摘"；D 项意为"画"。义工在花园里"种"（plant）花，与后文照料花园的情节一致。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些邻居___帮忙，但太忙了。A 项意为"提出"；B 项意为"拒绝"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"决定"。"offered to help"表示主动提出帮忙，但因为忙而无法做到。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不太懂园艺，但愿意___。A 项意为"玩"；B 项意为"学"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"离开"。不懂园艺却愿意"学习"（learn），引出王老师教大家的情节。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："你们这些孩子有___的心。"A 项意为"冰冷的"；B 项意为"善良的"；C 项意为"空的"；D 项意为"沉重的"。张奶奶感激学生帮忙，夸他们有"善良"（kind）的心。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不仅照料花园，还帮助社区里的其他___。A 项意为"工人"；B 项意为"学生"；C 项意为"老人"；D 项意为"孩子"。后文提到盲人李大爷、王奶奶，都是"老人"（elders）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说也想___当义工。A 项意为"也"（用于肯定句末）；B 项意为"也"（用于否定句）；C 项意为"也"（用于句中）；D 项意为"还"。"too"用于肯定句句末，表示"也想当义工"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很高兴看到我们的行动___了别人。A 项意为"激励"；B 项意为"阻止"；C 项意为"惊讶"；D 项意为"担心"。小女孩受感染也想做义工，说明我们的行为"激励"（inspired）了她。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当人们互相关心时，社区会变得___。A 项意为"更小"；B 项意为"更弱"；C 项意为"更强"；D 项意为"更安静"。"stronger"表示邻里互助让社区更有凝聚力。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记张奶奶看到花园重新开花时脸上的___。A 项意为"眼泪"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"笑容"；D 项意为"恐惧"。"smile"与后文"best reward"呼应，表示她的笑容是最好的回报。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可能会___于它带来的快乐——不仅给别人，也给自己。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"无聊"；C 项意为"惊讶"；D 项意为"疲倦"。"be surprised by"表示对志愿活动带来的快乐感到意外。故选 C。
