# 中考英语完形填空：漫画书里的梦想

## 完形填空原文

When I was in Grade 7, I found an old comic book in my father's drawer. The cover was torn, but the drawings inside were still ___1___. It was a story about a young artist who never gave up on his dream.

The main character, a boy named Xiao Ming, loved drawing. His parents wanted him to focus on ___2___ instead. "Art won't help you get a good job," they said. But Xiao Ming kept drawing in secret. He practiced every night by the light of a small lamp.

One day, a famous artist visited his school. He saw Xiao Ming's drawings and said, "You have a real ___3___. Don't let anyone tell you to stop." Those words changed Xiao Ming's life. He worked even harder and later became a successful illustrator.

I closed the comic book and looked at my own drawings on the wall. I had always loved art but was ___4___ to tell my parents. That night, I made a decision. I would follow my dream, just like Xiao Ming did.

I started to draw more seriously. I joined the school art club and entered a painting competition. I didn't ___5___ the first time, but I didn't give up. My art teacher said, "Every artist fails before they succeed. The important thing is to keep trying."

A year later, I won a prize in a city-wide competition. When I showed the ___6___ to my parents, they were surprised. "We didn't know you were so good," my mother said. "We're sorry we didn't support you earlier." From then on, they encouraged me to draw.

That old comic book is still on my shelf. Whenever I feel ___7___, I read it again. It reminds me that dreams are worth fighting for. Some people might not understand your passion at first, but if you believe in yourself and keep working, you can ___8___ your goals.

The comic was just a story, but it gave me courage to write my own. Everyone has a dream ___9___ inside them. Sometimes we need a little push to let it out. For me, that push came from a torn book in my father's drawer. I wonder if he ___10___ that it would inspire me one day.

## 选项

1. A. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; B. cheap &emsp;&emsp; C. useful &emsp;&emsp; D. heavy
2. A. music &emsp;&emsp; B. sports &emsp;&emsp; C. studies &emsp;&emsp; D. games
3. A. problem &emsp;&emsp; B. talent &emsp;&emsp; C. habit &emsp;&emsp; D. idea
4. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. ready &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
5. A. lose &emsp;&emsp; B. win &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. join
6. A. book &emsp;&emsp; B. prize &emsp;&emsp; C. painting &emsp;&emsp; D. photo
7. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. discouraged &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
8. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. reach &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
9. A. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; B. running &emsp;&emsp; C. hiding &emsp;&emsp; D. growing
10. A. knew &emsp;&emsp; B. hoped &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. agreed

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：封面破了，但里面的画仍然___。A 项意为"美丽的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"有用的"；D 项意为"重的"。旧漫画书的画依旧"好看"（beautiful），与"cover was torn"形成对比。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母希望他专注于___。A 项意为"音乐"；B 项意为"体育"；C 项意为"学业"；D 项意为"游戏"。父母常说"画画找不到好工作"，是希望孩子重视"学业"（studies）。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："你有真正的___。别让任何人叫你停下。"A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"天赋"；C 项意为"习惯"；D 项意为"想法"。画家看好小明的画，是夸他有"天赋"（talent）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直喜欢画画，但___告诉父母。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"准备好"；C 项意为"开心"；D 项意为"惊讶"。父母不支持画画，所以不敢"跟父母说"（afraid to tell）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一次我没有___，但没有放弃。A 项意为"输"；B 项意为"赢"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"参加"。"didn't win"表示第一次参赛没有获奖。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我把___给父母看时，他们很惊讶。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"奖"；C 项意为"画"；D 项意为"照片"。前文提到"won a prize"，此处是展示获奖的"奖"（prize）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每当感到___时，我会再读一遍。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"沮丧的"；D 项意为"生气的"。"discouraged"表示灰心、想放弃时，用漫画给自己打气。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相信自己并继续努力，你就能___目标。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"达到"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"离开"。"reach your goals"为固定搭配，表示"实现目标"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人心中都___着一个梦想。A 项意为"睡"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"生长"。"hiding inside"表示梦想藏在心里，等待被发掘。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我好奇他是否___有一天它会激励我。A 项意为"知道"；B 项意为"希望"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"同意"。"knew"表示父亲是否早知道这本书会影响孩子，带有怀念意味。故选 A。
