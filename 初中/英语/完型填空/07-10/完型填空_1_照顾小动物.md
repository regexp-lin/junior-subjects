# 中考英语完形填空：照顾小动物

## 完形填空原文

Our neighbour, Mrs Wang, went on a trip last month. She asked my family to take care of her cat, Xiao Bai, for two weeks. I was excited because I had always wanted to have a pet. Mum said it would be a good chance to learn about ___1___.

The first day, Xiao Bai hid under the sofa. She would not come out even when we put food and water near her. I felt a little ___2___. "Maybe she is scared because everything is new," Dad said. "We need to give her time and space." So we left her alone and spoke to her in a soft voice when we passed by.

By the third day, Xiao Bai began to come out. She walked slowly to her food bowl and ate a little. I sat on the floor a few metres away and did not move. ___3___, she came closer. She even let me stroke her head. Her fur was so soft! I was very happy.

Every morning I had to ___4___ her food and change her water. I also cleaned her litter box. At first I thought it was a lot of work, but I got used to it quickly. Mum said that taking care of a living thing taught us ___5___ — we had to think about its needs before our own.

One night, Xiao Bai did not eat her dinner. I was worried. I checked if she was hurt, but she looked fine. The next morning she was still not eating. I called Mrs Wang. She said Xiao Bai might be ___6___ her and feeling sad. She told me to spend more time playing with the cat. I tried that, and it worked. Xiao Bai began to eat again when I sat with her and talked to her.

When Mrs Wang came back, Xiao Bai ran to her at once. But before she left, she rubbed against my leg. It felt like she was saying thank you. I learned that animals have ___7___ too. They feel happy, scared and sad, just like us. Taking care of Xiao Bai made me understand that we should always be ___8___ to animals. I hope one day I can have a pet of my own and give it a happy ___9___. The two weeks with Xiao Bai was an experience I will never ___10___.

1. A. cooking &emsp;&emsp; B. responsibility &emsp;&emsp; C. games &emsp;&emsp; D. travel
2. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
3. A. Suddenly &emsp;&emsp; B. Slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. Quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. Finally
4. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. prepare &emsp;&emsp; C. sell &emsp;&emsp; D. share
5. A. stories &emsp;&emsp; B. responsibility &emsp;&emsp; C. tricks &emsp;&emsp; D. jokes
6. A. missing &emsp;&emsp; B. following &emsp;&emsp; C. calling &emsp;&emsp; D. helping
7. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. feelings &emsp;&emsp; C. homes &emsp;&emsp; D. toys
8. A. kind &emsp;&emsp; B. strict &emsp;&emsp; C. rude &emsp;&emsp; D. busy
9. A. cage &emsp;&emsp; B. home &emsp;&emsp; C. toy &emsp;&emsp; D. bowl
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. repeat &emsp;&emsp; D. finish

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说这是学习___的好机会。A 项意为"做饭"；B 项意为"责任"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"旅行"。照顾宠物可以学习责任心，"responsibility"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小猫躲着不出来，我感到有点___。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"生气的"。宠物不理人时会担心，"worried"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___，它靠近了。A 项意为"突然"；B 项意为"慢慢地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"最终"。猫适应新环境是渐进的，"Slowly"符合。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天早晨我得___它的食物并换水。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"准备"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"分享"。"prepare food"意为"准备食物"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：照顾生命教会我们___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"责任"；C 项意为"把戏"；D 项意为"笑话"。后文说要先考虑它的需求，体现责任，"responsibility"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：猫咪可能在___主人，感到难过。A 项意为"想念"；B 项意为"跟随"；C 项意为"叫"；D 项意为"帮助"。"missing her"意为"想念她"，与"feeling sad"呼应。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：动物也有___。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"情感"；C 项意为"家"；D 项意为"玩具"。后文说它们会开心、害怕、难过，"feelings"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们应该始终对动物___。A 项意为"友善的"；B 项意为"严格的"；C 项意为"粗鲁的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。"be kind to"意为"对……友善"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望有一天能有自己的宠物，给它一个快乐的___。A 项意为"笼子"；B 项意为"家"；C 项意为"玩具"；D 项意为"碗"。"give it a happy home"意为"给它一个幸福的家"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与小白在一起的两周是我永远不会___的经历。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"重复"；D 项意为"完成"。"never forget"意为"永不忘却"，强调难忘。故选 A。
