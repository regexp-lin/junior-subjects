# 中考英语完形填空：写给老师的信

## 完形填空原文

The last day of junior high was a mix of happiness and sadness. We were happy because summer holiday was beginning, but we were also sad to say goodbye to our teachers. That night, I decided to write a letter to my English teacher, Ms Li. She had helped me so much over the past three years.

I sat at my desk with a piece of paper and a pen. At first, I did not know what to ___1___. There were so many things I wanted to say. I remembered the first day when I was too shy to speak in class. Ms Li never laughed at me. Instead, she encouraged me to try. "Every ___2___ starts with a small step," she said.

I wrote about how her patience had made a difference. When I made mistakes in my writing, she would point them out gently and explain how to improve. She never made me feel ___3___. She made me believe that I could do better. I also wrote about the fun activities in her class — the role-plays, the group discussions, and the English songs we sang together. Learning English had never been so ___4___ before I met her.

In the letter, I thanked her for teaching me more than just grammar and vocabulary. She taught us to be confident, to help each other, and to never ___5___ up when things were difficult. "These lessons will stay with me for life," I wrote.

The next morning, I went to school early to give her the letter. When she read it, her eyes became ___6___. She gave me a warm hug and said, "Thank you. This is the best gift I have ever received." I was glad I had written the letter. Sometimes we forget to tell the people we ___7___ how much they mean to us. A simple letter can express our gratitude and make someone's day.

I learned that saying thank you is important. We should not wait for a special ___8___ to show our appreciation. A few words of thanks can bring warmth to both the giver and the receiver. I will always be ___9___ to Ms Li for everything she did for me. She is not just a teacher — she is someone who helped me become a better ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我不知道该___什么。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"画"。写信时不知写什么内容，"write"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一次___都从小步开始。A 项意为"旅程、历程"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"赛跑"；D 项意为"考试"。"every journey"比喻学习或人生历程，从小步开始。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她从未让我感到___。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"愚蠢的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"勇敢的"。耐心纠错而不打击学生，"never made me feel stupid"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：遇见她之前，学英语从未如此___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。角色扮演、讨论、唱歌让学习有趣，"interesting"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：遇到困难时不要___。A 项意为"查找"；B 项意为"放弃"；C 项意为"拿走"；D 项意为"放下"。"give up"意为"放弃"，鼓励坚持。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她读信时眼睛变得___。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"明亮的"；C 项意为"湿润的"；D 项意为"黑暗的"。"eyes became wet"意为"眼含泪水"，表示感动。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们忘了告诉我们所___的人他们对我们多重要。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"在乎、关心"；D 项意为"嘲笑"。"care about"意为"关心、在意"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不应等待特殊的___才表达感谢。A 项意为"日子"；B 项意为"场合"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"地方"。"special occasion"意为"特殊场合"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远会对李老师___她为我做的一切。A 项意为"相似的"；B 项意为"感激的"；C 项意为"亲近的"；D 项意为"严格的"。"be thankful to"意为"对……心存感激"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她帮助我成为更好的___。A 项意为"学生"；B 项意为"人"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"孩子"。老师育人，帮助学生成为更好的人，"person"最全面。故选 B。
