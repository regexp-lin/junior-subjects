# 中考英语完形填空：暴雨中的善意

## 完形填空原文

One summer afternoon, a sudden rainstorm hit our city. I was walking home from the bookshop without an umbrella. The rain was so ___1___ that I could hardly see the road ahead. I had to run to the nearest bus stop to find shelter.

When I got there, I was already soaking wet. There were several other people waiting under the small roof. An old lady was standing at the edge, and the rain was still falling on her ___2___. She looked cold and worried. I moved to one side so that she could stand in a drier spot. She smiled at me and said, "Thank you, young man. You are very ___3___."

We waited for about twenty minutes, but the rain did not ___4___. Some people started to get anxious. Then a man in a white car pulled over near the bus stop. He rolled down the window and asked, "Does anyone need a ___5___? I can take a few people." The old lady and two other women got into his car. Before she left, the old lady took something out of her bag. It was a small folding umbrella. She ___6___ it to me and said, "Take this. I don't need it now."

I was very touched by her ___7___. A stranger whom I had just met gave me something she could have kept for herself. I opened the umbrella and started walking home. The rain was still pouring, but I felt ___8___ inside.

When I got home, I told my mum what had happened. She said, "Kindness is like a circle. When you help someone, it often comes ___9___ to you." I thought about her words carefully. That afternoon, I learned an important lesson: a small act of kindness can make a big ___10___ in someone's day.

1. A. heavy &emsp;&emsp; B. light &emsp;&emsp; C. slow &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
2. A. hands &emsp;&emsp; B. face &emsp;&emsp; C. shoulders &emsp;&emsp; D. feet
3. A. clever &emsp;&emsp; B. funny &emsp;&emsp; C. kind &emsp;&emsp; D. quiet
4. A. start &emsp;&emsp; B. fall &emsp;&emsp; C. stop &emsp;&emsp; D. change
5. A. ride &emsp;&emsp; B. drink &emsp;&emsp; C. ticket &emsp;&emsp; D. photo
6. A. threw &emsp;&emsp; B. sold &emsp;&emsp; C. showed &emsp;&emsp; D. handed
7. A. beauty &emsp;&emsp; B. kindness &emsp;&emsp; C. humour &emsp;&emsp; D. bravery
8. A. warm &emsp;&emsp; B. cold &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
9. A. down &emsp;&emsp; B. out &emsp;&emsp; C. back &emsp;&emsp; D. up
10. A. promise &emsp;&emsp; B. mistake &emsp;&emsp; C. difference &emsp;&emsp; D. decision

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：雨太___了，我几乎看不见前方的路。A 项意为"大的"；B 项意为"轻的"；C 项意为"慢的"；D 项意为"干的"。暴雨导致视线模糊，"heavy rain"是常见搭配，意为"大雨"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位老太太站在边缘，雨仍然落在她的___上。A 项意为"手"；B 项意为"脸"；C 项意为"肩膀"；D 项意为"脚"。站在棚子边缘，雨水最先淋到肩膀，"shoulders"最合理。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：谢谢你，年轻人，你真___。A 项意为"聪明的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"善良的"；D 项意为"安静的"。让出位置给老人是善良的表现，"kind"最合适。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等了大约二十分钟，雨还没有___。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"落下"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"改变"。一直在等雨停，"the rain did not stop"意为"雨没有停"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有人需要搭___吗？A 项意为"搭车"；B 项意为"饮料"；C 项意为"票"；D 项意为"照片"。开车来问是否需要搭车，"need a ride"意为"需要搭个车"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把雨伞___给我。A 项意为"扔"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"展示"；D 项意为"递给"。老人把伞递给"我"，"handed"意为"递给"，动作温和自然。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被她的___深深感动。A 项意为"美丽"；B 项意为"善良"；C 项意为"幽默"；D 项意为"勇敢"。陌生老人送伞是善意的举动，"kindness"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：雨还在下，但我内心感到___。A 项意为"温暖的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"累的"。收到善意后心里感到温暖，与外面的大雨形成对比。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：当你帮助别人时，善意往往会___到你身边。A 项意为"向下"；B 项意为"向外"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"向上"。"come back to you"意为"回到你身边"，呼应"善意像一个圈"的比喻。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小小的善举可以在别人的一天中产生很大的___。A 项意为"承诺"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"不同、影响"；D 项意为"决定"。"make a big difference"是固定搭配，意为"产生很大影响"。故选 C。
