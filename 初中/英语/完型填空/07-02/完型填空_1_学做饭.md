# 中考英语完形填空：学做饭

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, I decided to learn how to cook. My parents were both busy with work, and I thought it would be nice if I could ___1___ dinner for the family.

I started with something simple — fried eggs. My mum showed me how to do it step by step. First, I put some oil in the pan and waited until it was ___2___. Then I broke an egg into the pan. It made a loud sound, and I jumped back in fear. My mum laughed and said, "Don't worry. Just be ___3___ and keep going." I tried again and this time the egg looked much better.

Day by day, I learned to cook more dishes. I could make tomato and egg soup, fried rice, and even dumplings. Of course, I also made many ___4___. Once I put too much salt in the soup and nobody could drink it. Another time I forgot to turn off the stove and the vegetables were completely ___5___. But I did not give up. Each failure taught me something new.

One evening, I decided to cook a full meal for my family as a ___6___. I spent the whole afternoon in the kitchen. I made three dishes and a soup. When my parents came home and saw the food on the table, they could not ___7___ their eyes. "Did you really make all of this?" my dad asked. I nodded proudly.

We sat down together and enjoyed the meal. My mum said the dishes were ___8___ and she was very proud of me. My dad said I was even better than him in the kitchen. I knew they were being kind, but their words made me feel ___9___.

Learning to cook was not easy, but it was worth it. Now I understand that cooking is not just about food — it is also a way to show ___10___ to the people you care about.

## 选项

1. A. order &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. prepare &emsp;&emsp; D. taste
2. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. hot &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
3. A. careful &emsp;&emsp; B. quick &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. honest
4. A. friends &emsp;&emsp; B. decisions &emsp;&emsp; C. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; D. promises
5. A. frozen &emsp;&emsp; B. burnt &emsp;&emsp; C. washed &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
6. A. joke &emsp;&emsp; B. surprise &emsp;&emsp; C. task &emsp;&emsp; D. test
7. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. open &emsp;&emsp; C. close &emsp;&emsp; D. cover
8. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. expensive &emsp;&emsp; C. simple &emsp;&emsp; D. delicious
9. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. warm &emsp;&emsp; C. shy &emsp;&emsp; D. sorry
10. A. love &emsp;&emsp; B. talent &emsp;&emsp; C. power &emsp;&emsp; D. courage

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我能为家人___晚餐就好了。A 项意为"点（餐）"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"准备"；D 项意为"品尝"。自己动手做饭给家人吃，"prepare dinner"意为"准备晚餐"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在锅里放了一些油，等到油___。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"热的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"干的"。煎蛋需要等油烧热，"hot"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：别担心，___一点继续做。A 项意为"小心的"；B 项意为"快速的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"诚实的"。煎蛋时被吓到，妈妈鼓励要小心继续，"careful"最合适。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当然，我也犯了很多___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"承诺"。后文提到放太多盐、忘关炉子等，都是做菜过程中的失误，"mistakes"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我忘记关炉子，蔬菜完全___了。A 项意为"冷冻的"；B 项意为"烧焦的"；C 项意为"洗过的"；D 项意为"丢失的"。忘关炉子导致菜烧焦，"burnt"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定做一顿完整的饭给家人作为___。A 项意为"玩笑"；B 项意为"惊喜"；C 项意为"任务"；D 项意为"测试"。趁父母不在家偷偷做好一桌菜，是给他们一个惊喜，"surprise"最恰当。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们不敢___自己的眼睛。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"睁开"；C 项意为"闭上"；D 项意为"遮住"。"could not believe their eyes"是固定搭配，意为"不敢相信自己所见"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说菜很___，她非常为我骄傲。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"美味的"。妈妈夸奖孩子做的菜好吃，"delicious"正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的话让我感到___。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"害羞的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。家人的夸奖让人心里温暖，"warm"最贴切。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做饭也是向你关心的人表达___的一种方式。A 项意为"爱"；B 项意为"天赋"；C 项意为"力量"；D 项意为"勇气"。为家人做饭是表达爱的方式，"show love"最合适。故选 A。
