# 中考英语完形填空：暑假支教日记

## 完形填空原文

This summer, I joined a volunteer teaching programme in a mountain village. For two weeks, a group of senior high students and I taught English, art, and music to the children there. It was one of the most ___1___ experiences of my life.

The village was far from the city. We took a bus for six hours and then walked for another hour along a narrow dirt road. When we finally arrived, the village head welcomed us warmly. The school was small — just three classrooms and a tiny playground. But the children's eyes were full of curiosity and ___2___.

I was responsible for teaching English to the younger group, aged seven to ten. Most of them had never had a real English lesson before. They could not even say "hello" ___3___. But they were eager to learn. On the first day, I taught them the alphabet and some simple greetings. They repeated after me with big smiles on their faces.

Teaching was not easy. I had no textbooks or projector — only a blackboard and coloured chalk. I had to think of creative ways to make the lessons ___4___. I used songs, games, and hand-drawn pictures. The children loved the games, especially "Simon Says." They would laugh and jump around the classroom, and I could see them learning without even ___5___ it.

The living conditions were basic. We slept in sleeping bags on the classroom floor and cooked our own meals. The water came from a well, and there was no internet. At first, I found it hard to ___6___ to life without my phone. But after a few days, I stopped thinking about it. I realised that face-to-face conversations and outdoor activities could be just as enjoyable.

What touched me the most was the children's ___7___. On our last day, they gave us handmade gifts — paper flowers, drawn portraits, and tiny wooden carvings. One girl handed me a bracelet made of red string and said, "Teacher, please come back next year." I had to hold back my ___8___.

The experience changed my view of the world. I used to take many things for granted — clean water, electricity, fast internet. Now I understand how ___9___ some families still are, and I feel a stronger desire to help.

I plan to volunteer again next summer. Teaching those children taught me something I could never learn from a textbook: that giving is more ___10___ than receiving.

## 选项

1. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. painful &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
2. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. excitement &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
3. A. correctly &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. politely
4. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. short &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. interesting
5. A. believing &emsp;&emsp; B. realising &emsp;&emsp; C. admitting &emsp;&emsp; D. refusing
6. A. object &emsp;&emsp; B. reply &emsp;&emsp; C. adjust &emsp;&emsp; D. return
7. A. wealth &emsp;&emsp; B. complaints &emsp;&emsp; C. gratitude &emsp;&emsp; D. silence
8. A. breath &emsp;&emsp; B. tears &emsp;&emsp; C. words &emsp;&emsp; D. laughter
9. A. rich &emsp;&emsp; B. lucky &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. poor
10. A. rewarding &emsp;&emsp; B. tiring &emsp;&emsp; C. confusing &emsp;&emsp; D. frightening

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我人生中最___的经历之一。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"痛苦的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。支教经历对人生有深远意义，"meaningful"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：孩子们的眼里充满了好奇和___。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"兴奋"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"悲伤"。看到新老师到来孩子们好奇又兴奋，"excitement"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们甚至不能___地说"你好"。A 项意为"正确地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"礼貌地"。没学过英语连打招呼都说不准确，"correctly"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我必须想创造性的方式让课堂变得___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"短的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。用歌曲、游戏和图画让课堂有趣，"interesting"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能看到他们在不知不觉中就学到了知识。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"意识到"；C 项意为"承认"；D 项意为"拒绝"。"without realising it"意为"不知不觉地"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我很难___没有手机的生活。A 项意为"反对"；B 项意为"回复"；C 项意为"适应"；D 项意为"返回"。"adjust to"意为"适应"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最让我感动的是孩子们的___。A 项意为"财富"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"感恩"；D 项意为"沉默"。孩子们送手工礼物表达感激之情，"gratitude"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不得不忍住___。A 项意为"呼吸"；B 项意为"眼泪"；C 项意为"话语"；D 项意为"笑声"。被感动得差点哭了，"hold back tears"意为"忍住眼泪"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我现在理解了一些家庭仍然多么___。A 项意为"富有的"；B 项意为"幸运的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"贫困的"。山村生活条件艰苦，体会到有些家庭的贫困，"poor"正确。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：给予比接受更___。A 项意为"有回报的/值得的"；B 项意为"疲倦的"；C 项意为"令人困惑的"；D 项意为"令人恐惧的"。"rewarding"表示付出后获得精神上的满足，给予比接受更有价值。故选 A。
