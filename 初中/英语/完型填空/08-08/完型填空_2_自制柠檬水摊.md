# 中考英语完形填空：自制柠檬水摊

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, my cousin Xiaoli and I came up with an idea: we would set up a lemonade stand in front of our house. We wanted to earn some pocket money and also have fun doing it ___1___.

We started by making a list of things we needed: lemons, sugar, water, ice, cups, and a small table. My aunt helped us buy the ingredients, but she made us ___2___ for them with our own savings. "If you want to run a business, you have to invest first," she said. I thought this was a good lesson.

The next morning, we got up early and squeezed thirty lemons. My hands hurt and the juice stung every tiny cut on my fingers. It was harder work than we had ___3___. But when we tasted the lemonade, we knew it was worth the effort — it was fresh, sweet, and perfectly cold.

We set up our stand at ten o'clock. Xiaoli made a big colourful sign that read "Fresh Lemonade — 3 yuan per cup." I arranged the cups neatly on the table and put a jar of ___4___ on the side. Then we waited.

The first hour was quiet. Only two people walked by, and neither of them stopped. I started to feel ___5___. Maybe nobody wanted lemonade. But then a group of children from the neighbourhood came over. They bought five cups and said it was delicious. Word spread quickly, and soon more people came.

By noon, we had sold twenty cups. Some customers even came back for a ___6___ cup. An old lady told us our lemonade reminded her of her childhood. A father bought cups for his whole family and left a generous tip. Each sale made us more confident and ___7___.

In the afternoon, the sun got very hot. More people wanted cold drinks, and our lemonade sold faster than ever. We ran out of ice twice and had to ask my aunt to buy more. By four o'clock, we had sold all fifty cups and earned over 150 yuan.

After paying back the cost of ingredients, we each had about sixty yuan of ___8___. It was not a huge amount, but we felt incredibly proud. We had earned it through our own hard work.

The lemonade stand taught me several things. First, starting something new always takes ___9___ — you have to try even when you are not sure it will work. Second, quality matters — people came back because our lemonade was genuinely good. Third, working with a partner makes everything more ___10___ and less stressful.

9. A. courage &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. permission &emsp;&emsp; D. luck

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们想赚零花钱，同时也想___玩得开心。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"秘密地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。两个人合伙做事，"together"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阿姨让我们用自己的积蓄___。A 项意为"做饭"；B 项意为"问"；C 项意为"付款"；D 项意为"等"。"pay for"意为"支付"，用自己的钱买原料。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这比我们___的要辛苦得多。A 项意为"喜欢"；B 项意为"预期"；C 项意为"记得"；D 项意为"选择"。实际比预期困难，"expected"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（语境推断）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把杯子整齐摆好，旁边放了一罐___。A 项意为"花"；B 项意为"冰"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"玩具"。卖冷饮旁边放一罐冰块，"ice"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始感到___。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"气馁的"；D 项意为"满意的"。第一个小时几乎没人买，让人有点灰心，"discouraged"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 序数词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些顾客甚至回来买___杯。A 项意为"第一"；B 项意为"最后"；C 项意为"碎的"；D 项意为"第二"。已经买过又回来再买一杯，"second"正确。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每卖出一杯都让我们更自信和___。A 项意为"疲倦的"；B 项意为"快乐的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"生气的"。生意好心情也好，"cheerful"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：扣除原料成本后，我们每人有大约六十元的___。A 项意为"零钱"；B 项意为"债务"；C 项意为"利润"；D 项意为"麻烦"。收入减去成本就是利润，"profit"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：开始做新事情总是需要___。A 项意为"勇气"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"许可"；D 项意为"运气"。后文说"即使不确定是否成功也要尝试"，这需要勇气，"courage"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：和伙伴合作让一切都更___，压力更小。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"愉快的"；D 项意为"危险的"。有伙伴合作更快乐，"enjoyable"正确。故选 C。
