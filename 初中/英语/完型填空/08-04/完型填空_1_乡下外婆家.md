# 中考英语完形填空：乡下外婆家

## 完形填空原文

Every summer, I spend a week at my grandmother's house in the countryside. It is a small village surrounded by green hills and rice fields. The air there is fresh and the nights are so ___1___ that you can hear the frogs singing.

This year, I arrived on a Monday morning. Grandma was already waiting at the bus stop with a warm smile. She had prepared my favourite dish — braised pork with potatoes. After lunch, I helped her ___2___ the dishes while she told me about the village news. Mr. Chen next door had a new grandson, and the old bridge over the river had been repaired.

The next day, Grandma took me to her vegetable garden. It was much bigger than I ___3___. There were tomatoes, cucumbers, beans, and several kinds of greens. She taught me how to tell when a tomato was ripe enough to pick. "When it turns deep red and feels a little ___4___, it is ready," she said. I picked a basketful and felt like a real farmer.

In the evenings, we sat in the courtyard and watched the stars. The sky in the countryside is very different from the one in the city. You can see ___5___ of stars, and sometimes even the Milky Way. Grandma told me that when she was young, there were no street lights, so the stars were the only light at night. I tried to ___6___ what that was like and found it both beautiful and a little scary.

One afternoon, it started to rain heavily. We could not go outside, so Grandma taught me to make rice dumplings. She was very ___7___ with her hands. She could wrap a perfect dumpling in seconds, while mine kept falling apart. She laughed and said, "Do not worry. Practice makes ___8___." By the end of the afternoon, my dumplings looked much better.

The week went by too quickly. When it was time to leave, I felt ___9___. I hugged Grandma tightly and promised to come back soon. On the bus home, I looked out of the window at the green hills growing smaller and smaller. I realised that Grandma's house was not just a place — it was where my happiest childhood ___10___ were made.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：夜晚非常___，你能听到青蛙在唱歌。A 项意为"吵闹的"；B 项意为"黑暗的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"冷的"。乡村夜晚安静才能听到蛙鸣，"quiet"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：午饭后我帮她___碗碟。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"买"；D 项意为"数"。饭后洗碗是日常家务，"wash"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：菜园比我___的大得多。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"预期"；C 项意为"想要"；D 项意为"不喜欢"。菜园超出了预期的大小，"expected"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（常识判断）
**【解析】** 本句意为：当番茄变成深红色且摸起来有点___时就可以摘了。A 项意为"硬的"；B 项意为"软的"；C 项意为"干的"；D 项意为"冷的"。成熟的番茄微微发软，"soft"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你能看到___颗星星。A 项意为"成千上万"；B 项意为"成对"；C 项意为"成群"；D 项意为"各种"。"thousands of"意为"成千上万的"，形容星星数量多。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试着___那是什么样子。A 项意为"解释"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"想象"；D 项意为"证明"。没有路灯的夜晚需要靠想象，"imagine"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的手非常___。A 项意为"仔细的"；B 项意为"灵巧的"；C 项意为"强大的"；D 项意为"快乐的"。外婆几秒钟就能包好一个粽子，说明手很灵巧，"skilful"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：熟能生___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"意义"；D 项意为"完美"。"Practice makes perfect"为英语谚语，意为"熟能生巧"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要离开时，我感到___。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"幸运的"。舍不得离开外婆家，感到难过，"sad"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它是我最幸福的童年___诞生的地方。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"决定"；D 项意为"错误"。外婆家承载着美好的童年回忆，"memories"正确。故选 B。
