# 中考英语完形填空：第一次做演讲

## 完形填空原文

Last term, our English teacher told us that everyone had to give a speech in front of the class. When I heard this, my heart sank. I was one of the ___1___ students in the class and hated speaking in public.

I had two weeks to prepare. The topic I chose was "My Favourite Season." I picked spring because it was easy to talk about — flowers, warm weather, and new beginnings. I wrote my speech carefully, making sure every sentence was ___2___ and easy to understand.

After writing it, I practised reading it aloud at home. At first, I spoke too fast and my voice was shaky. My mother sat in the living room and listened. "Slow down," she said, "and look up from your paper sometimes. ___3___ contact makes your speech more powerful." I followed her advice and practised again and again.

The night before the speech, I could hardly ___4___. I kept imagining all the things that could go wrong: What if I forgot my words? What if everyone laughed at me? I told my father about my fears. He said, "Everyone feels nervous before speaking in public. The ___5___ is that you have prepared well. Trust yourself."

The next morning, I walked into the classroom with my speech in my hand. My palms were sweating. When the teacher called my name, I stood up slowly and walked to the front. I took a deep ___6___ and began.

The first few sentences were the hardest. My voice trembled a little. But as I continued, I began to feel more comfortable. I remembered my mother's advice and looked at my classmates. Some of them were nodding and smiling, which gave me ___7___. By the end, I was speaking naturally, almost as if I were just talking to friends.

When I finished, the class clapped. My teacher said I did a good job and praised my clear pronunciation. I walked back to my seat feeling ___8___ than I had ever felt before.

That experience taught me something valuable: the things we fear most are often not as ___9___ as we imagine. If we prepare well and face our fears bravely, we can surprise ourselves. Now, I am not afraid of public speaking any more. In fact, I have even started to ___10___ it.

## 选项

1. A. tallest &emsp;&emsp; B. smartest &emsp;&emsp; C. shyest &emsp;&emsp; D. strongest
2. A. long &emsp;&emsp; B. clear &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. secret
3. A. Eye &emsp;&emsp; B. Hand &emsp;&emsp; C. Body &emsp;&emsp; D. Ear
4. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. move &emsp;&emsp; C. sleep &emsp;&emsp; D. think
5. A. pity &emsp;&emsp; B. truth &emsp;&emsp; C. problem &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
6. A. hole &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. breath &emsp;&emsp; D. thought
7. A. pressure &emsp;&emsp; B. trouble &emsp;&emsp; C. courage &emsp;&emsp; D. homework
8. A. sadder &emsp;&emsp; B. weaker &emsp;&emsp; C. angrier &emsp;&emsp; D. prouder
9. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. important
10. A. hate &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; D. avoid

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（最高级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我是班上最___的学生之一，讨厌在公众面前讲话。A 项意为"最高的"；B 项意为"最聪明的"；C 项意为"最害羞的"；D 项意为"最强壮的"。害怕公开演讲说明性格害羞，"shyest"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我确保每个句子都___且容易理解。A 项意为"长的"；B 项意为"清晰的"；C 项意为"搞笑的"；D 项意为"秘密的"。写演讲稿要清楚明了，"clear"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：___接触能让你的演讲更有力。A 项意为"眼睛"；B 项意为"手"；C 项意为"身体"；D 项意为"耳朵"。"Eye contact"（眼神交流）是演讲技巧的常用表达。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：演讲前一晚，我几乎___不着。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"动"；C 项意为"睡"；D 项意为"想"。紧张导致失眠，"sleep"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___是你已经准备充分了。A 项意为"可惜"；B 项意为"事实"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"惊喜"。"The truth is..."意为"事实是……"，爸爸在安慰他。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我深___了一下然后开始。A 项意为"洞"；B 项意为"觉"；C 项意为"呼吸"；D 项意为"想法"。"take a deep breath"为固定搭配，意为"深呼吸"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们有的点头微笑，这给了我___。A 项意为"压力"；B 项意为"麻烦"；C 项意为"勇气"；D 项意为"作业"。同学的正面反馈给予了信心和勇气，"courage"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我走回座位时比以前任何时候都感到更___。A 项意为"更悲伤"；B 项意为"更虚弱"；C 项意为"更生气"；D 项意为"更自豪"。成功完成演讲后感到自豪，"prouder"正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们最害怕的事往往并不像我们想象的那么___。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"可怕的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"重要的"。实际经历后发现没有想象中那么可怕，"terrible"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：事实上，我甚至开始___它了。A 项意为"憎恨"；B 项意为"害怕"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"避免"。从害怕到享受，态度完全转变，"enjoy"正确。故选 C。
