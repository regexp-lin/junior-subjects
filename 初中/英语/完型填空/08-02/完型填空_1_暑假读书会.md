# 中考英语完形填空：暑假读书会

## 完形填空原文

This summer, my best friend Wang Lin started a reading club in our neighbourhood. She put up a notice on the community board, inviting anyone who liked books to ___1___.

At first, only four of us came. We met every Wednesday afternoon at Wang Lin's home. Each week, one person would choose a book and ___2___ it to the group. Then we would all read it and discuss it the following week. It was a simple idea, but it worked very well.

The first book we read was "Charlotte's Web." I had read it before, but reading it again as a teenager gave me a completely ___3___ feeling. When I was younger, I only cared about the story. This time, I noticed the beautiful language and the deep message about friendship and sacrifice. Our discussion that week lasted for two hours because everyone had so much to ___4___.

As the weeks went by, more people heard about our club and wanted to join. By the end of July, we had twelve ___5___. Some were our classmates, and some were younger children from the neighbourhood. Wang Lin suggested that we let the younger ones pick easier books sometimes, so that everyone could participate equally. I thought this was a very ___6___ idea.

Reading together was different from reading alone. When I read by myself, I sometimes skipped parts I did not understand. But in the group, someone always had a good explanation. We helped each other understand ___7___ passages and learned new vocabulary together. I realised that sharing ideas could make reading much more enjoyable.

The reading club also improved my ___8___ skills. In the beginning, I was shy and did not like talking in front of others. But the friendly atmosphere encouraged me to express my thoughts. By August, I could share my opinions clearly and ___9___ without feeling nervous.

The summer reading club was one of the best experiences of my holiday. I read ten books in total and made several new friends. Wang Lin said she would continue the club during the school year, and I was the first to say ___10___. I believe that reading brings people together in a special way.

## 选项

1. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. join &emsp;&emsp; C. pay &emsp;&emsp; D. work
2. A. lend &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. recommend &emsp;&emsp; D. return
3. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. different
4. A. read &emsp;&emsp; B. write &emsp;&emsp; C. say &emsp;&emsp; D. copy
5. A. books &emsp;&emsp; B. members &emsp;&emsp; C. teachers &emsp;&emsp; D. families
6. A. silly &emsp;&emsp; B. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; C. thoughtful &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
7. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. short &emsp;&emsp; C. tricky &emsp;&emsp; D. funny
8. A. cooking &emsp;&emsp; B. writing &emsp;&emsp; C. speaking &emsp;&emsp; D. swimming
9. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. sadly &emsp;&emsp; C. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; D. confidently
10. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. no &emsp;&emsp; C. yes &emsp;&emsp; D. goodbye

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她邀请任何喜欢书的人来___。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"加入"；C 项意为"支付"；D 项意为"工作"。邀请加入读书会，"join"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每周一个人选一本书并向小组___。A 项意为"借出"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"推荐"；D 项意为"归还"。选书后向大家推荐，"recommend"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：作为青少年重读给了我完全___的感受。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"不同的"。小时候读和青少年时读感受不同，"different"正确。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都有很多要___的。A 项意为"读"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"说"；D 项意为"抄写"。讨论环节大家有很多话要说，"say"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到七月底我们有了十二个___。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"成员"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"家庭"。更多人加入，成员增加到十二人，"members"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得这是一个非常___的主意。A 项意为"愚蠢的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"体贴的/周到的"；D 项意为"困难的"。照顾到低龄读者体现了周到，"thoughtful"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相帮助理解___的段落。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"短的"；C 项意为"棘手的/难懂的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。独自阅读时会跳过不懂的部分，在小组里互相帮助理解难懂段落，"tricky"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：读书会也提高了我的___技能。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"写作"；C 项意为"口语/表达"；D 项意为"游泳"。后文提到从害羞不敢说话到能清楚表达想法，"speaking"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我能清楚且___地分享我的观点而不紧张。A 项意为"生气地"；B 项意为"悲伤地"；C 项意为"粗心地"；D 项意为"自信地"。从害羞到自信，"confidently"正确。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 语境理解
**【解析】** 本句意为：我是第一个说___的。A 项意为"抱歉"；B 项意为"不"；C 项意为"是"；D 项意为"再见"。王林提议继续办读书会，作者第一个表示同意，"yes"正确。故选 C。
