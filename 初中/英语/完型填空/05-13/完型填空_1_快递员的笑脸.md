# 中考英语完形填空：快递员的笑脸

## 完形填空原文

During the pandemic, I stayed at home for months. Online classes made me feel bored and disconnected from the world. The only person I saw every day was the delivery man — a middle-aged man who brought our food and packages.

At first, I didn't pay much ___1___ to him. I would open the door, take the package, and close the door quickly. One day, my mother wasn't home, and I had to sign for a parcel. The delivery man smiled and said, "Stay safe, young man. Things will get ___2___."

His simple words stayed with me. After that, I started noticing him more. He always wore a mask, but I could see the kindness in his eyes. Even on rainy days, he never seemed ___3___. He would wave at me through the window if he saw me studying.

I decided to do something for him. I made a thank-you card and left a bottle of water by the door. The next time he came, he was surprised. "For me?" he asked. I nodded. His face lit up. "Thank you," he said. "This ___4___ my day."

That small exchange changed something. I began to appreciate the people who worked behind the scenes — delivery workers, cleaners, supermarket staff. They kept our lives ___5___ when everything felt uncertain. Their work might seem ordinary, but it was essential.

I started writing thank-you notes to other workers too. The security guard at our building. The bus driver who waited when he saw me running. Simple ___6___ of gratitude can mean a lot to someone having a hard day.

My grandmother always said, "Kindness is a language that everyone understands." I never really understood that until I ___7___ it myself. A smile, a thank-you, or a bottle of water — these small acts create connections. They remind us that we're not alone.

Now, whenever I receive a delivery, I make sure to say thank you and smile back. The delivery man and I have become ___8___. He sometimes asks about my studies. I ask about his family. We're not just stranger and customer anymore — we're two people who chose to see the ___9___ in each other.

In difficult times, the people who keep showing up deserve our respect and ___10___.

1. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. attention &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. visit
2. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. harder &emsp;&emsp; D. stranger
3. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
4. A. made &emsp;&emsp; B. ruined &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. saved
5. A. moving &emsp;&emsp; B. stopping &emsp;&emsp; C. changing &emsp;&emsp; D. building
6. A. gifts &emsp;&emsp; B. words &emsp;&emsp; C. gestures &emsp;&emsp; D. money
7. A. heard &emsp;&emsp; B. experienced &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. imagined
8. A. enemies &emsp;&emsp; B. friends &emsp;&emsp; C. strangers &emsp;&emsp; D. competitors
9. A. weakness &emsp;&emsp; B. goodness &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. illness
10. A. criticism &emsp;&emsp; B. kindness &emsp;&emsp; C. silence &emsp;&emsp; D. distance

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我没怎么___他。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"注意"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"拜访"。"pay attention to"（注意、关注）是固定搭配。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：注意安全，年轻人。一切会___的。A 项意为"更糟"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更难"；D 项意为"更奇怪"。"get better"（变好）是疫情期间常用的安慰语。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使下雨天，他似乎也从不___。A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"疲惫"；C 项意为"生气"；D 项意为"无聊"。快递员风雨无阻仍保持笑容，"never seemed tired"体现敬业乐观。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这___了我的一天。A 项意为"使……圆满"；B 项意为"毁掉"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"拯救"。"made my day"（让我一天都开心）是常用表达。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们让我们的生活继续___。A 项意为"运转"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"建造"。"kept our lives moving"（让生活继续运转）形容维持社会正常运行。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：简单的感恩___对辛苦一天的人意义重大。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"话语"；C 项意为"举动/表示"；D 项意为"钱"。"gestures of gratitude"（感恩的表示）涵盖卡片、微笑等行为。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：直到我亲自___才真正理解。A 项意为"听说"；B 项意为"经历"；C 项意为"阅读"；D 项意为"想象"。"experienced it myself"（亲身经历）强调实践出真知。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：快递员和我成了___。A 项意为"敌人"；B 项意为"朋友"；C 项意为"陌生人"；D 项意为"竞争者"。"become friends"与下文互相问候、关心呼应。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们选择看见彼此身上的___。A 项意为"弱点"；B 项意为"善意/美好"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"疾病"。"see the goodness"（看到善意）体现相互尊重与温暖。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：坚守岗位的人值得我们的尊重和___。A 项意为"批评"；B 项意为"善意"；C 项意为"沉默"；D 项意为"距离"。"respect and kindness"（尊重与善意）与主题呼应。故选 B。
