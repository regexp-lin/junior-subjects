# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校天文观测活动

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday night, our school organized a special activity — an astronomy observation on the playground. Many students like me were ___1___ to join in. We brought our jackets because it could get cold at night.

At 8 p.m., we gathered on the playground. The sky was clear and we could see countless stars. Our science teacher, Mr. Wang, brought a ___2___ telescope. He told us we would look at the moon and some planets tonight. I had never looked through a telescope before, so I felt very excited.

We took turns to look. When it was my ___3___, I put my eye close to the telescope. Wow! The moon looked so big and bright! I could even see some dark ___4___ on its surface. Mr. Wang said they were craters formed a long time ago.

After looking at the moon, we turned the telescope to find Jupiter. It took us a few ___5___ to find it, but when we did, it was amazing. Jupiter looked like a bright spot with four small dots around it — those were its moons! Mr. Wang ___6___ us that Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system.

The activity ended at 10 p.m. On my way home, I couldn't stop thinking about what I had seen. The universe is so vast and ___7___. I decided to learn more about space. Maybe one day I could ___8___ a scientist and explore the stars. This unforgettable night will always ___9___ me of how wonderful science can be and how much there is still to ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
2. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. powerful &emsp;&emsp; C. cheap &emsp;&emsp; D. broken
3. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. turn &emsp;&emsp; C. chance &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
4. A. lines &emsp;&emsp; B. spots &emsp;&emsp; C. lights &emsp;&emsp; D. colors
5. A. hours &emsp;&emsp; B. days &emsp;&emsp; C. minutes &emsp;&emsp; D. weeks
6. A. asked &emsp;&emsp; B. told &emsp;&emsp; C. answered &emsp;&emsp; D. agreed
7. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. noisy
8. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. become &emsp;&emsp; C. grow &emsp;&emsp; D. get
9. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. remind &emsp;&emsp; D. think
10. A. discover &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. lose

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多像我一样的学生都很兴奋能参加。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"疲惫的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。参加天文观测活动，学生应是 excited（兴奋的）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师带来了一台强大的望远镜。A 项意为"强大的；高性能的"；B 项意为"小的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"坏的"。天文观测需要 powerful（高性能的）望远镜才能看清天体。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我的时候，我把眼睛凑近望远镜。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项意为"选择"；D 项意为"轮流"。take turns 后接 to do，轮到某人用 it's one's turn。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我甚至能看到月球表面的一些暗色斑点。A 项意为"线条"；B 项意为"光线"；C 项意为"斑点"；D 项意为"颜色"。月球表面的坑（craters）在望远镜中看起来像 dark spots。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们花了几分钟才找到木星。A 项意为"小时"；B 项意为"分钟"；C 项意为"天"；D 项意为"周"。用望远镜在天空中定位行星，通常只需几分钟。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王老师告诉我们木星是太阳系中最大的行星。A 项意为"询问"；B 项意为"回答"；C 项意为"同意"；D 项意为"告诉"。老师向学生介绍知识，用 tell sb. sth.。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：宇宙是如此浩瀚而美丽。A 项意为"美丽的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"嘈杂的"。vast 与 beautiful 常搭配形容宇宙的壮丽。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许有一天我能成为科学家，探索星辰。A 项意为"转动"；B 项意为"成长"；C 项意为"得到"；D 项意为"成为"。become a scientist 意为"成为科学家"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个难忘的夜晚将永远提醒我科学是多么奇妙。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"提醒"；D 项意为"想"。remind sb. of sth. 意为"使某人想起某事；提醒某人"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：科学是多么奇妙，还有多少东西有待发现。A 项意为"发现"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"失去"。there is still to discover 指"尚有未发现的事物"。故选 A。
