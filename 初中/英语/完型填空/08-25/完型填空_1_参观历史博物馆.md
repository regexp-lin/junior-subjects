# 中考英语完形填空：参观历史博物馆

## 完形填空原文

Our history teacher organised a class trip to the city museum last month. She said that seeing real objects from the past would help us understand our lessons ___1___. I had never been to a museum before, so I was looking forward to the visit.

The museum was a large building with white walls. When we walked in, the first thing I saw was a huge dinosaur skeleton. It was ___2___ than I had imagined. A guide welcomed us and explained that we would tour three halls: ancient China, traditional crafts, and modern history.

In the ancient China hall, we saw pottery, bronze ware, and old coins. Each object had a small card describing when and where it was ___3___. I was especially interested in a set of ancient bells. The guide said they were over two thousand years old. I could not ___4___ that such delicate things had survived for so long.

The traditional crafts hall showed how people used to make silk, paper, and pottery. There were videos showing the ___5___ process. I learned that making a piece of fine china could take many weeks. The craftsmen in the past were really patient and ___6___.

Before we left, we had half an hour of ___7___ time. My friends and I went to the gift shop. I bought a postcard with a picture of the museum to keep as a souvenir. Some classmates took photos in front of their favourite exhibits. Our teacher reminded us not to use flash, as it could ___8___ the old objects.

On the bus back to school, we talked about what we had seen. The museum visit made history feel more ___9___ to me. Reading about the past in books is one thing, but standing in front of real artefacts is quite another. I hope we can go on more such ___10___ in the future. Learning outside the classroom can be fun and memorable.

## 选项

1. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. faster &emsp;&emsp; D. slower
2. A. smaller &emsp;&emsp; B. taller &emsp;&emsp; C. lighter &emsp;&emsp; D. cheaper
3. A. sold &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. found &emsp;&emsp; D. given
4. A. understand &emsp;&emsp; B. believe &emsp;&emsp; C. expect &emsp;&emsp; D. remember
5. A. making &emsp;&emsp; B. buying &emsp;&emsp; C. selling &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaning
6. A. skilled &emsp;&emsp; B. lazy &emsp;&emsp; C. young &emsp;&emsp; D. rich
7. A. study &emsp;&emsp; B. rest &emsp;&emsp; C. free &emsp;&emsp; D. work
8. A. protect &emsp;&emsp; B. repair &emsp;&emsp; C. damage &emsp;&emsp; D. improve
9. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. real &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
10. A. games &emsp;&emsp; B. exams &emsp;&emsp; C. trips &emsp;&emsp; D. meetings

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到过去的真实物品能帮助我们用更___的方式理解课程。A 项意为"更差地"；B 项意为"更好地"；C 项意为"更快地"；D 项意为"更慢地"。参观博物馆有助于更好理解，"better"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它比我想象的___多了。A 项意为"更小"；B 项意为"更高"；C 项意为"更轻"；D 项意为"更便宜"。恐龙骨架通常令人惊讶地高大，"taller"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每件文物都有小卡片描述何时何地被___。A 项意为"出售"；B 项意为"制作"；C 项意为"发现"；D 项意为"给予"。博物馆展品标签通常注明出土地点，"found"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我无法___如此精美的东西能保存这么久。A 项意为"理解"；B 项意为"相信"；C 项意为"期待"；D 项意为"记住"。"could not believe"意为"难以置信"，"believe"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有视频展示___过程。A 项意为"制作"；B 项意为"购买"；C 项意为"出售"；D 项意为"清洁"。传统工艺厅展示制作工艺，"making"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：过去的工匠真的很有耐心而且___。A 项意为"技艺精湛的"；B 项意为"懒惰的"；C 项意为"年轻的"；D 项意为"富有的"。制作精美瓷器需要高超技艺，"skilled"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：离开前我们有半小时___时间。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"休息"；C 项意为"自由"；D 项意为"工作"。"free time"意为"自由活动时间"，"free"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师提醒我们不要用闪光灯，因为可能___古老文物。A 项意为"保护"；B 项意为"修复"；C 项意为"损坏"；D 项意为"改善"。闪光灯会损害文物，"damage"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：博物馆之旅让历史对我而言感觉更___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"真实的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。看到真实文物使历史更真实，"real"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望将来能参加更多这样的___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"旅行"；D 项意为"会议"。博物馆参观是外出旅行，"trips"正确。故选 C。
