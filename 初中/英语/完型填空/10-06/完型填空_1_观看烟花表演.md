# 中考英语完形填空：观看烟花表演

## 完形填空原文

On the evening of October 6th, our city held a fireworks show to celebrate the National Day holiday. My family and I went to the riverside to ___1___ it.

When we arrived, the riverbank was already packed with people. Children were running around with glowing sticks, and street vendors were selling snacks and drinks. The ___2___ was lively and festive.

At eight o'clock sharp, the first firework shot up into the dark sky. It exploded into a shower of golden sparks, like a giant flower ___3___ in the night. Everyone cheered and clapped their hands. My little brother jumped up and down with excitement.

The fireworks came in all shapes and colors — red, green, blue, purple, and gold. Some looked like stars, some looked like waterfalls, and some ___4___ hearts or smiley faces in the sky. Each one was more beautiful than the last. The whole sky was lit up as if it were daytime.

I took out my phone and tried to ___5___ some photos. But it was hard to capture the beauty of the moment. The fireworks moved too fast and my photos turned out blurry. Father said, "Sometimes it's better to just enjoy the moment with your ___6___ instead of through a screen." I put my phone away and looked up.

He was right. Watching the fireworks without any distraction was a completely different ___7___. I could feel the boom in my chest and see the colors reflected on the river surface. It was breathtaking.

The show lasted about thirty minutes. When the last firework ___8___ in the sky, the crowd burst into loud applause. People were smiling and taking group photos. The night air was filled with the smell of smoke and the sound of happy voices.

On the way home, Mother said that fireworks had been a tradition in China for ___9___ of years. They were first invented in ancient China and later spread to other countries. I felt proud to know that something so beautiful came from our culture.

That night, I lay in bed thinking about the colorful sky. It was a holiday evening I would always ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一家去河边观看烟花表演。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"取消"。去现场 watch 烟花。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：气氛热闹而喜庆。A 项意为"天气"；B 项意为"交通"；C 项意为"课"；D 项意为"气氛"。lively and festive 形容 atmosphere。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：像一朵巨大的花在夜空中绽放。A 项意为"死亡"；B 项意为"绽放"；C 项意为"掉落"；D 项意为"隐藏"。花 blooming 意为"绽放"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有的在天空中形成爱心或笑脸图案。A 项意为"形成"；B 项意为"擦掉"；C 项意为"打破"；D 项意为"失去"。formed hearts 意为"形成爱心"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我拿出手机想拍一些照片。A 项意为"删除"；B 项意为"打印"；C 项意为"拍"；D 项意为"画"。take photos 意为"拍照"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时候用眼睛享受当下比通过屏幕更好。A 项意为"手"；B 项意为"耳朵"；C 项意为"眼睛"；D 项意为"脚"。与 through a screen 对比，用 eyes 直接看。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不受干扰地看烟花是完全不同的体验。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"体验"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"疑问"。a different experience 意为"不同的体验"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当最后一朵烟花在天空中消散。A 项意为"出现"；B 项意为"消散"；C 项意为"生长"；D 项意为"停留"。烟花最后 faded 消散。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 数词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：烟花在中国已有数千年的传统。A 项意为"数十"；B 项意为"数百"；C 项意为"数千"；D 项意为"数百万"。thousands of years 意为"数千年"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是一个我会永远记住的假期夜晚。A 项意为"后悔"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"讨厌"；D 项意为"记住"。美好回忆会 always remember。故选 D。
