# 中考英语完形填空：给远方笔友写信

## 完形填空原文

I have a pen pal named Tom. He lives in London, England. We started writing to each other two years ago when our schools became ___1___ schools. Although we have never met in person, we have become close friends.

During the holiday, I decided to write Tom a long letter to tell him about my life in China. I sat at my desk, picked up a pen, and began to ___2___.

First, I told him about the National Day holiday and how Chinese people celebrate it. I described the flag-raising ceremony, the fireworks, and the family dinners. I also ___3___ some photos of our celebrations. I hoped he would find Chinese culture interesting.

Then I wrote about my daily life at school. I told him about our morning exercises, the class meetings, and the after-school clubs. Tom once said that Chinese students study much ___4___ than British students. I explained that although we have a lot of homework, we also have time for sports and hobbies.

I also asked Tom some questions. I was ___5___ about life in England. What do British students eat for lunch? Do they wear school uniforms? What do they do after school? I have always wanted to visit London and see Big Ben and the Thames River with my own eyes.

Writing the letter took me about an hour. When I finished, I read it again carefully to check for any ___6___. I wanted my English to be correct and natural. After making a few changes, I felt ___7___ with it.

I put the letter in an envelope, wrote Tom's ___8___ on the front, and stuck a stamp on it. The next day, I went to the post office and sent it. The lady there told me it would take about two weeks to arrive.

Now I am waiting ___9___ for Tom's reply. I wonder what he will tell me about his life. Writing letters may be slower than sending emails, but there is something ___10___ about receiving a handwritten letter from a friend far away.

6. A. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; B. secrets &emsp;&emsp; C. stamps &emsp;&emsp; D. photos
7. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. satisfied &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointed
8. A. age &emsp;&emsp; B. address &emsp;&emsp; C. name &emsp;&emsp; D. phone number
9. A. impatiently &emsp;&emsp; B. sadly &emsp;&emsp; C. angrily &emsp;&emsp; D. eagerly
10. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. special &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. boring

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两年前我们学校结为友好学校时开始通信。A 项意为"伙伴的"；B 项意为"竞争的"；C 项意为"邻居"；D 项意为"老的"。partner schools 意为"友好学校"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在桌前，拿起笔，开始写。A 项意为"读"；B 项意为"画"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"唱"。写信 began to write。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还附上了一些庆祝活动的照片。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"包含、附上"；C 项意为"丢失"；D 项意为"扔"。included some photos 意为"附上照片"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：Tom 曾说中国学生比英国学生学习更刻苦。A 项意为"更少"；B 项意为"更差"；C 项意为"更刻苦"；D 项意为"更慢"。study harder 意为"学习更刻苦"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对英国的生活很好奇。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"好奇的"。后文提出很多问题，说明 curious。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仔细重读一遍检查有没有错误。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"邮票"；D 项意为"照片"。check for mistakes 意为"检查错误"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：修改几处后我很满意。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"满意的"；D 项意为"失望的"。felt satisfied with 意为"对……满意"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把 Tom 的地址写在信封正面。A 项意为"年龄"；B 项意为"地址"；C 项意为"名字"；D 项意为"电话号码"。寄信要写 address。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在我正急切地等待 Tom 的回信。A 项意为"不耐烦地"；B 项意为"悲伤地"；C 项意为"生气地"；D 项意为"急切地"。期待回信用 eagerly。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：收到远方朋友的手写信有一种特别的感觉。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"特别的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。手写信比电邮更 special。故选 B。
