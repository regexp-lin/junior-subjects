# 中考英语完形填空：学做蛋炒饭

## 完形填空原文

My parents were both busy last Saturday, so I decided to cook lunch by myself. I chose to make egg fried rice because it seemed ___1___ enough for a beginner like me.

First, I needed to prepare the ingredients. I took two eggs, some leftover rice, a green onion, and a little salt from the kitchen. I also found some ham in the ___2___. I cut the ham and green onion into small pieces.

Then I cracked the eggs into a bowl and ___3___ them with chopsticks. My mother had told me before that beating the eggs well would make the rice taste better. I tried my best to mix them evenly.

Next, I turned on the stove and put some oil in the pan. When the oil was ___4___, I poured in the eggs. They started bubbling right away. I quickly stirred them with a spatula before they ___5___.

After that, I added the rice and kept stirring. I used the spatula to break up any big ___6___ of rice. Then I added the ham and green onion pieces. A wonderful smell filled the ___7___.

Finally, I added a little salt and stirred everything one more time. I turned off the stove and put the fried rice on a plate. It looked golden and smelled ___8___.

When my parents came home, they were surprised to see lunch on the table. They tasted it and said it was ___9___. I felt very proud of myself. Cooking is not as difficult as I thought. I want to learn more ___10___ and cook for my family more often.

1. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. dangerous

3. A. cooked &emsp;&emsp; B. washed &emsp;&emsp; C. beat &emsp;&emsp; D. threw
4. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. hot &emsp;&emsp; C. thick &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
5. A. burned &emsp;&emsp; B. froze &emsp;&emsp; C. fell &emsp;&emsp; D. melted
6. A. bags &emsp;&emsp; B. bowls &emsp;&emsp; C. lumps &emsp;&emsp; D. bottles
7. A. garden &emsp;&emsp; B. kitchen &emsp;&emsp; C. bedroom &emsp;&emsp; D. classroom
8. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. sour &emsp;&emsp; C. delicious &emsp;&emsp; D. bitter
9. A. awful &emsp;&emsp; B. salty &emsp;&emsp; C. raw &emsp;&emsp; D. great
10. A. songs &emsp;&emsp; B. recipes &emsp;&emsp; C. languages &emsp;&emsp; D. sports

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我选做蛋炒饭因为对新手来说足够简单。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"昂贵的"；D 项意为"危险的"。蛋炒饭对初学者 simple enough。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在冰箱里找到一些火腿。A 项意为"冰箱"；B 项意为"花园"；C 项意为"浴室"；D 项意为"卧室"。火腿存放在 fridge。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把鸡蛋打入碗中用筷子搅拌。A 项意为"煮"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"打/搅"；D 项意为"扔"。beat eggs 意为"打鸡蛋"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：油热后我倒入鸡蛋。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"热的"；C 项意为"厚的"；D 项意为"干净的"。油 hot 后才能炒。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：趁鸡蛋没烧焦前赶紧翻炒。A 项意为"烧焦"；B 项意为"冻住"；C 项意为"掉落"；D 项意为"融化"。before they burned 意为"趁没烧焦"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我用铲子把大块的饭打散。A 项意为"袋子"；B 项意为"碗"；C 项意为"块/团"；D 项意为"瓶子"。big lumps of rice 意为"大块的饭团"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：美妙的香味充满了厨房。A 项意为"花园"；B 项意为"厨房"；C 项意为"卧室"；D 项意为"教室"。炒饭的香味在 kitchen 里。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来金黄色闻起来很香。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"酸的"；C 项意为"美味的"；D 项意为"苦的"。smelled delicious 意为"闻起来香"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们尝了说很棒。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"咸的"；C 项意为"生的"；D 项意为"很棒的"。父母夸奖说 great。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想学更多食谱，更经常给家人做饭。A 项意为"歌曲"；B 项意为"食谱"；C 项意为"语言"；D 项意为"运动"。learn more recipes 意为"学更多食谱"。故选 B。
