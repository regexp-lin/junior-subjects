# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校跳绳比赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a rope-jumping competition last Wednesday. Every class had to send five students to ___1___ in the event. I was one of them.

I had been practicing for two weeks before the competition. At first, I could only jump about fifty times without ___2___. But after daily practice, I improved a lot and could jump over one hundred and fifty times.

On the day of the competition, the playground was full of ___3___. Students from different classes were warming up. Some were already jumping very fast. I felt a little pressure, but my friends encouraged me and said, "Just do your ___4___."

When it was my turn, I took a deep breath and started jumping. I tried to keep a ___5___ rhythm and not rush. The rope swung smoothly around me. I could hear my classmates counting for me: "...eighty-one, eighty-two..."

Suddenly, my foot caught the rope and I ___6___. I quickly picked up the rope and continued. My teacher had told me that stopping for too long would ___7___ more time. So I jumped as fast as I could until the whistle blew.

In the end, I jumped one hundred and thirty-two times. It wasn't my best ___8___, but I was still happy because I didn't give up after the mistake. Our class got third place overall, and we received a certificate.

After the competition, our PE teacher said that rope jumping is one of the ___9___ exercises for our health. It helps build strong legs and improves coordination. I decided to keep practicing every day, not just for competitions, but for my own ___10___.

3. A. energy &emsp;&emsp; B. water &emsp;&emsp; C. food &emsp;&emsp; D. silence
4. A. homework &emsp;&emsp; B. best &emsp;&emsp; C. job &emsp;&emsp; D. duty

7. A. save &emsp;&emsp; B. waste &emsp;&emsp; C. need &emsp;&emsp; D. give

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个班派五名学生参加比赛。A 项意为"躲藏"；B 项意为"参赛"；C 项意为"学习"；D 项意为"休息"。compete in 意为"参加比赛"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我只能不停跳约五十下。A 项意为"思考"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"停下"；D 项意为"吃"。without stopping 意为"不停地"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：操场上充满了活力。A 项意为"活力/能量"；B 项意为"水"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"安静"。比赛日 full of energy。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：朋友们鼓励我说"尽你所能"。A 项意为"作业"；B 项意为"最好"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"职责"。do your best 意为"尽力而为"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我试着保持稳定的节奏。A 项意为"快的"；B 项意为"稳定的"；C 项意为"慢的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。steady rhythm 意为"稳定的节奏"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：脚绊到绳子我被绊了一下。A 项意为"笑"；B 项意为"哭"；C 项意为"绊倒"；D 项意为"睡"。caught the rope 导致 tripped。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：停太久会浪费更多时间。A 项意为"节省"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"需要"；D 项意为"给"。waste time 意为"浪费时间"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然不是我的最好成绩。A 项意为"成绩/分数"；B 项意为"朋友"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"借口"。best score 意为"最好成绩"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词最高级
**【解析】** 本句意为：跳绳是对健康最好的运动之一。A 项意为"最差的"；B 项意为"最难的"；C 项意为"最好的"；D 项意为"最便宜的"。one of the best exercises。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不只为了比赛，也为了自己的健康。A 项意为"财富"；B 项意为"安全"；C 项意为"健康"；D 项意为"运气"。运动是为了 health。故选 C。
