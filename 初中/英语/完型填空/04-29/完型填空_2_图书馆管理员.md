# 中考英语完形填空：图书馆管理员

## 完形填空原文

Our school library had a strict rule: no talking, no eating, and all books must be returned on time. The person who made sure everyone followed these rules was Mrs. Xu, the librarian. She was a small woman with glasses and a very serious ___1___.

Most students were a little afraid of her. She never smiled. If you returned a book even one day late, she would give you a long lecture about ___2___. If you talked too loudly, she would appear behind you like a ghost and say "Shhh!" in a voice that could freeze water.

I used to think Mrs. Xu simply didn't like students. But one winter afternoon, I ___3___ my mind completely.

It was raining hard that day, and I went to the library to wait for my mom to pick me up. I sat in a corner, ___4___ and wet. I had forgotten my jacket, and the library's heating wasn't working.

Mrs. Xu walked over. I expected her to tell me to stop dripping water on the floor. ___5___, she placed a warm cup of tea on my desk and draped a thick blanket over my shoulders.

"Drink this," she said quietly. "Your mom called. She'll be thirty minutes ___6___."

I was so surprised that I couldn't speak. She turned to walk away, then stopped. "You know," she said without looking back, "I'm strict because I ___7___ about this library and every student who comes here. A good library is like a quiet garden — it needs someone to take care of it so that ___8___ can grow."

After that day, I started seeing Mrs. Xu differently. I noticed that she always put bookmarks in new books with a tiny handwritten ___9___ that said "Happy reading!" I noticed that she stayed late every Friday to organize the shelves so students would find what they needed on Monday.

Mrs. Xu retired last month. At her farewell party, the library was packed with students — past and present. We all stood and clapped. There were tears in her eyes, and for the first time, I saw her ___10___.

1. A. face &emsp;&emsp; B. voice &emsp;&emsp; C. body &emsp;&emsp; D. hair
2. A. friendship &emsp;&emsp; B. honesty &emsp;&emsp; C. responsibility &emsp;&emsp; D. creativity
3. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. changed &emsp;&emsp; C. kept &emsp;&emsp; D. made
4. A. shaking &emsp;&emsp; B. singing &emsp;&emsp; C. laughing &emsp;&emsp; D. sleeping
5. A. Therefore &emsp;&emsp; B. However &emsp;&emsp; C. Instead &emsp;&emsp; D. Besides
6. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. late &emsp;&emsp; C. ago &emsp;&emsp; D. away
7. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. worry &emsp;&emsp; C. know &emsp;&emsp; D. hear
8. A. trees &emsp;&emsp; B. flowers &emsp;&emsp; C. minds &emsp;&emsp; D. walls
9. A. name &emsp;&emsp; B. rule &emsp;&emsp; C. price &emsp;&emsp; D. note
10. A. cry &emsp;&emsp; B. leave &emsp;&emsp; C. smile &emsp;&emsp; D. shout

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她是一位戴着眼镜、___非常严肃的小个子女士。A 项意为"脸"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"身体"；D 项意为"头发"。描述一个人的外在印象，"脸"（face）上的严肃表情最直观，"a serious face"意为严肃的表情。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果你还书哪怕晚了一天，她都会给你上一堂关于___的长课。A 项意为"友谊"；B 项意为"诚实"；C 项意为"责任"；D 项意为"创造力"。还书是借书者的"责任"（responsibility），迟还就是不负责任，所以她会讲一番关于责任的道理。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但一个冬天的下午，我彻底___了想法。A 项意为"丢失"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"做出"。"changed my mind"（改变了想法）是固定搭配，表示"我"对徐老师的看法从此发生了转变。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（现在分词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在角落里，又___又湿。A 项意为"发抖"；B 项意为"唱歌"；C 项意为"笑"；D 项意为"睡觉"。下大雨又忘了带外套，暖气还坏了，又冷又湿自然会"发抖"（shaking）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连接副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以为她会叫我别把水滴在地板上。___，她在我桌上放了一杯热茶。A 项意为"因此"；B 项意为"然而"；C 项意为"相反"；D 项意为"此外"。预期她会责骂，实际上她给了温暖。"Instead"（相反）强调预期与实际完全相反。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："你妈妈打了电话。她会晚三十分钟。"A 项意为"早的"；B 项意为"晚的"；C 项意为"以前"；D 项意为"远"。下大雨路况不好，妈妈会"晚"（late）到三十分钟。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："我严格是因为我___这个图书馆和每一个来这里的学生。"A 项意为"关心"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"知道"；D 项意为"听说"。"care about"（关心）是固定搭配，她严格是因为"关心"，不是因为不喜欢学生。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它需要有人照料，这样___才能成长。A 项意为"树"；B 项意为"花"；C 项意为"心灵/思想"；D 项意为"墙"。她把图书馆比作安静的花园，需要有人照料的不是花草，而是学生的"心灵/思想"（minds），寓意图书馆滋养学生的精神成长。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她总是在新书里放书签，上面有一个小小的手写___，写着"祝阅读愉快！"A 项意为"名字"；B 项意为"规则"；C 项意为"价格"；D 项意为"便签/字条"。手写的祝福语写在一个小"便签"（note）上，夹在书签里。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她眼里含着泪水，我第一次看到她___。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"微笑"；D 项意为"喊叫"。全文反复强调她从不笑、很严肃，而在告别会上被学生们的爱感动，她第一次"微笑"（smile）了，形成全文的情感高潮。故选 C。
