# 中考英语完形填空：生日蛋糕的秘密

## 完形填空原文

It was the night before my fourteenth birthday. I was lying in bed, thinking about what gifts I might get. But I heard strange ___1___ coming from the kitchen — the sound of bowls clinking and a mixer running.

I tiptoed to the kitchen door and peeked inside. There was my dad, wearing Mom's pink apron, covered in flour. He was trying to bake a birthday cake. The kitchen was a complete ___2___ — egg shells on the floor, sugar spilled on the counter, and something that smelled slightly burned.

Dad was not a good cook. Mom usually made my birthday cake every year. But this year, Mom was away on a business trip and wouldn't be back until ___3___. I suddenly understood: Dad was trying to make the cake himself so I wouldn't feel sad on my birthday.

I watched him ___4___ for a while. He stared at his phone screen, reading a recipe, then poured something into a bowl, then checked the phone again. He cracked an egg, but half the shell fell into the ___5___. He picked out the tiny pieces one by one, very patiently.

I almost laughed, but then I felt something warm in my chest. Dad had never baked anything in his life. He was doing this ___6___ for me.

I went back to bed quietly, pretending I had seen ___7___.

The next morning, Dad woke me up with a big smile. "Happy birthday!" he said, holding a cake. It was the ugliest cake I had ever seen — the icing was uneven, the candles were ___8___, and one side was higher than the other.

"I know it's not ___9___," Dad said, scratching his head. "But I tried my best."

I took a bite. It was a little too sweet and slightly dry. But honestly, it was the most delicious cake I had ever ___10___. Because every bite was filled with love.

## 选项

1. A. noises &emsp;&emsp; B. voices &emsp;&emsp; C. songs &emsp;&emsp; D. stories
2. A. surprise &emsp;&emsp; B. success &emsp;&emsp; C. mess &emsp;&emsp; D. mystery
3. A. tonight &emsp;&emsp; B. yesterday &emsp;&emsp; C. now &emsp;&emsp; D. tomorrow
4. A. secretly &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. angrily &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
5. A. oven &emsp;&emsp; B. bowl &emsp;&emsp; C. cup &emsp;&emsp; D. plate
6. A. easily &emsp;&emsp; B. selfishly &emsp;&emsp; C. entirely &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
7. A. everything &emsp;&emsp; B. something &emsp;&emsp; C. anything &emsp;&emsp; D. nothing
8. A. straight &emsp;&emsp; B. crooked &emsp;&emsp; C. missing &emsp;&emsp; D. colorful
9. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. cheap &emsp;&emsp; C. perfect &emsp;&emsp; D. ready
10. A. made &emsp;&emsp; B. bought &emsp;&emsp; C. seen &emsp;&emsp; D. tasted

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我听到从厨房传来奇怪的___——碗碰撞和搅拌机运转的声音。A 项意为"噪音/声响"；B 项意为"人声"；C 项意为"歌曲"；D 项意为"故事"。厨房里锅碗瓢盆碰撞的声音是"噪音/声响"（noises），不是人说话的声音。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房完全是一片___。A 项意为"惊喜"；B 项意为"成功"；C 项意为"一团糟"；D 项意为"谜团"。蛋壳在地上、糖洒了一台面、还有烧焦的味道，厨房"一团糟"（mess）。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词/时间词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈出差了，要到___才回来。A 项意为"今晚"；B 项意为"昨天"；C 项意为"现在"；D 项意为"明天"。现在是生日前一晚，妈妈明天才回，赶不上做蛋糕，所以爸爸自己来。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___地看了他一会儿。A 项意为"偷偷地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"生气地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。"我"不想被发现，所以是"偷偷地"（secretly）在门口观察爸爸。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他打了一个鸡蛋，但一半的蛋壳掉进了___里。A 项意为"烤箱"；B 项意为"碗"；C 项意为"杯子"；D 项意为"盘子"。做蛋糕时把鸡蛋打进"碗"（bowl）里，蛋壳掉进碗里是初学者常犯的错误。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他这样做___是为了我。A 项意为"容易地"；B 项意为"自私地"；C 项意为"完全地"；D 项意为"快速地"。爸爸从不烘焙，却"完全"（entirely）是为了"我"的生日而尝试做蛋糕。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 不定代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我悄悄回到床上，假装什么都没___看到。A 项意为"一切"；B 项意为"某事"；C 项意为"任何事"；D 项意为"什么都没有"。"我"不想让爸爸知道被发现了，所以假装什么都"没"（nothing）看到。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：糖霜不均匀，蜡烛是___的。A 项意为"直的"；B 项意为"歪的"；C 项意为"缺少的"；D 项意为"多彩的"。描述蛋糕很丑，蜡烛插得"歪歪扭扭"（crooked），与糖霜不均匀、一边高一边低呼应。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："我知道它不___，"爸爸挠着头说。A 项意为"大的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"完美的"；D 项意为"准备好的"。爸爸知道蛋糕做得不好看，不"完美"（perfect），但他尽力了。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它是我___过的最美味的蛋糕。A 项意为"做"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"看到"；D 项意为"品尝"。虽然蛋糕太甜又偏干，但因为饱含爱意，它是"我"吃过（"品尝"tasted）的最好吃的蛋糕。故选 D。
