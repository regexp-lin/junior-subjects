# 中考英语完形填空：图书馆的老管理员

## 完形填空原文

There is a small library near my school. It does not look special from the outside — just an old building with a wooden door. But inside, it is full of ___1___ waiting to be discovered. And the person who makes the library truly special is Mr. Zhou, the old librarian.

Mr. Zhou has worked at the library for over thirty years. He knows where every book is, even without checking the computer. If you tell him what kind of story you like, he can ___2___ the perfect book for you in seconds. He once told me, "Books are like friends. I have lived with them for so long that I know each one by heart."

I started visiting the library in the fourth grade. At that time, I was not a good reader. I found most books ___3___ and could not focus for more than ten minutes. Mr. Zhou noticed this and did not push me. Instead, he handed me a thin picture book about a brave little dog. "Try this one," he said. "I think you will like it."

He was right. I ___4___ the book in one afternoon and immediately wanted more. Mr. Zhou then gave me another book, slightly longer and more challenging. Step by step, he guided me from picture books to chapter books, and finally to novels. He always chose books that matched my ___5___ perfectly — not too easy and not too hard.

Mr. Zhou also started a "reading corner" every Saturday morning. Children from the neighbourhood would sit on colourful cushions while he read stories aloud. He used different ___6___ for different characters, which made the stories come alive. Even the naughty kids sat quietly and listened.

Last month, I heard that Mr. Zhou was going to ___7___. He was already sixty-five. The news made me feel both happy and sad — happy for him because he deserved a rest, but sad because the library would not be the same ___8___ him.

On his last day, many people came to say goodbye. Former students, parents, and neighbours all brought flowers and thank-you cards. Mr. Zhou's eyes were red, but he smiled and said, "I am not leaving the books. I am just reading them at home now."

I gave him a card I had made myself. Inside, I wrote: "Thank you for teaching me to love reading. You did not just lend me books — you ___9___ my world."

Mr. Zhou is retired now, but his influence lives on. Every time I pick up a book, I think of him. He showed me that a good librarian does not just organise books — he connects people with ___10___ that can change their lives.

1. A. dangers &emsp;&emsp; B. treasures &emsp;&emsp; C. problems &emsp;&emsp; D. machines
2. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. hide &emsp;&emsp; C. find &emsp;&emsp; D. copy

7. A. travel &emsp;&emsp; B. retire &emsp;&emsp; C. move &emsp;&emsp; D. study
8. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. like &emsp;&emsp; C. with &emsp;&emsp; D. without
9. A. closed &emsp;&emsp; B. opened &emsp;&emsp; C. destroyed &emsp;&emsp; D. forgot
10. A. toys &emsp;&emsp; B. games &emsp;&emsp; C. stories &emsp;&emsp; D. rules

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：里面满是等待被发现的___。A 项意为"危险"；B 项意为"宝藏"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"机器"。图书馆里的书籍是知识的宝藏，"treasures"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他能在几秒钟内为你___到最合适的书。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"藏"；C 项意为"找到"；D 项意为"复制"。馆长熟悉所有书，能快速找到合适的，"find"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得大部分书都很___，无法集中注意力超过十分钟。A 项意为"令人兴奋的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"重的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。读不进去觉得无聊，"boring"正确。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一个下午就___了那本书，立刻想要更多。A 项意为"读完"；B 项意为"弄丢"；C 项意为"归还"；D 项意为"卖掉"。一个下午读完说明很喜欢，"finished"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他总是选择与我的___完美匹配的书——不太简单也不太难。A 项意为"年龄"；B 项意为"水平"；C 项意为"重量"；D 项意为"身高"。难度匹配阅读水平，"level"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他为不同角色用不同的___，使故事栩栩如生。A 项意为"衣服"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"语言"；D 项意为"座位"。朗读时为不同角色变换声音，"voices"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我听说周先生要___了。A 项意为"旅行"；B 项意为"退休"；C 项意为"搬家"；D 项意为"学习"。他已经六十五岁了，"retire"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：图书馆___他就不一样了。A 项意为"为了"；B 项意为"像"；C 项意为"有"；D 项意为"没有"。没有周先生图书馆将不再一样，"without"正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你没有只是借给我书——你___了我的世界。A 项意为"关闭"；B 项意为"打开"；C 项意为"摧毁"；D 项意为"忘记"。"opened my world"意为"打开了我的世界"，表示阅读拓宽了视野。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他把人们和能改变人生的___联系起来。A 项意为"玩具"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"规则"。图书馆员把读者与好的故事和书籍联系起来，"stories"正确。故选 C。
