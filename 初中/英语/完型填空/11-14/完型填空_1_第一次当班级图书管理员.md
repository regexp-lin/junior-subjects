# 中考英语完形填空：第一次当班级图书管理员

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our head teacher asked for volunteers to manage the class library. I raised my hand without ___1___. I love reading, and I thought this would be a great way to share books with my classmates.

The class library has about two hundred books. My main job is to ___2___ them in order and help classmates borrow and return books. Every Monday and Thursday afternoon, I sit at the little desk near the bookshelf and write down each borrower's name and the book title.

At first, I found it ___3___ to remember where each book should go. The books are organized by type: fiction, science, and history. I made a simple list and stuck it on the wall. Now I can find the right place for any book quickly.

What I enjoy most is ___4___ new books with classmates. When someone asks for a recommendation, I try to suggest something that matches their interests. Last week, Li Ming wanted an exciting story, so I ___5___ him a detective novel. He loved it and came back for another one the next day.

Sometimes books come back with small damages—a bent corner or a torn page. I have learned to ___6___ them carefully with tape. Our teacher said we should teach everyone to treat books with ___7___, so I put up a notice: "Love our books, and they will love you back!"

Being the class librarian has made me more responsible. I arrive early to open the library and stay late to make sure everything is in place. My classmates often thank me, and that makes me feel that my ___8___ is worthwhile. I hope our class library will grow and help more students ___9___ the joy of reading. After all, a good book is like a good friend—it can ___10___ you to new worlds and new ideas.

## 选项

1. A. thinking &emsp;&emsp; B. hesitating &emsp;&emsp; C. waiting &emsp;&emsp; D. asking
2. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. buy
3. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
4. A. sharing &emsp;&emsp; B. hiding &emsp;&emsp; C. losing &emsp;&emsp; D. copying
5. A. lent &emsp;&emsp; B. gave &emsp;&emsp; C. sold &emsp;&emsp; D. threw
6. A. repair &emsp;&emsp; B. replace &emsp;&emsp; C. remove &emsp;&emsp; D. reject
7. A. anger &emsp;&emsp; B. care &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. pride
8. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. effort &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. time
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. discover &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
10. A. push &emsp;&emsp; B. lead &emsp;&emsp; C. force &emsp;&emsp; D. send

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我毫不犹豫地举起了手。A 项意为"思考"；B 项意为"犹豫"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"询问"。without hesitating 意为毫不犹豫。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的主要工作是保持书籍有序，帮助同学借阅和归还。A 项 keep...in order 意为"保持……有序"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"买"。图书管理需 keep books in order。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我发现很难记住每本书该放哪里。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。find it hard to do 意为发现做某事很难。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我最享受的是和同学分享新书。A 项意为"分享"；B 项意为"藏"；C 项意为"丢失"；D 项意为"复制"。share books 与推荐书籍语境相符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我推荐了他一本侦探小说。A 项意为"借出"；B 项意为"给、推荐"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"扔"。suggest/give him a book 表示推荐。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了用胶带仔细修补它们。A 项意为"修理"；B 项意为"替换"；C 项意为"移除"；D 项意为"拒绝"。repair 表示修补损坏的书。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们应该教大家爱惜书籍。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"爱护"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"骄傲"。treat with care 意为小心对待。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：同学们的感谢让我觉得付出是值得的。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"努力、付出"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"时间"。effort is worthwhile 意为付出值得。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望班级图书角能帮助更多学生发现阅读的乐趣。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"发现"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"错过"。discover the joy 意为发现乐趣。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好书如好友，能带你领略新世界、新想法。A 项意为"推"；B 项意为"引领"；C 项意为"强迫"；D 项意为"送"。lead you to 意为引领你到。故选 B。
