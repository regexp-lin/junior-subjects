# 中考英语完形填空：学做布艺手提袋

## 完形填空原文

My grandma loves sewing. Last Sunday, she asked if I wanted to learn how to make a fabric tote bag. I said yes ___1___ I had always wanted to try making something with my own hands.

Grandma got out her sewing machine and some colorful fabric. She said we would need fabric for the outside and the inside, thread, a zipper, and two handles. It sounded like a lot, but she ___2___ me that it wouldn't be too difficult.

First, we measured and cut the fabric into the right size. Grandma taught me how to use pins to hold the pieces together before sewing. "The pins keep everything in ___3___ so your stitches stay straight," she explained.

Then we sewed the sides together. I had never used a sewing machine before, so Grandma guided my hands. I had to press the foot pedal ___4___ and move the fabric slowly. At first, my stitches were a bit wavy, but they got better with practice.

The hardest part was attaching the handles. We had to make sure they were the same length and sewed on ___5___. Grandma said symmetry was important—if one handle was shorter, the bag would hang crooked.

After about two hours, we finished! The bag had a nice blue pattern on the outside and a plain white lining inside. I couldn't ___6___ that I had made something so useful. I decided to use it as my school bag for carrying books and my water bottle.

Grandma said that handmade things have a special ___7___ because they carry the maker's love and effort. She was right. Every time I use my tote bag, I remember that afternoon with Grandma and feel ___8___. I want to learn more sewing skills and maybe make a pencil case or a small pillow next. With ___9___ and practice, I believe I can create many beautiful things. Grandma smiled and said she would teach me ___10___ I was ready to learn more.

## 选项

1. A. because &emsp;&emsp; B. although &emsp;&emsp; C. unless &emsp;&emsp; D. until
2. A. warned &emsp;&emsp; B. promised &emsp;&emsp; C. advised &emsp;&emsp; D. refused
3. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. danger
4. A. gently &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
5. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. differently &emsp;&emsp; C. equally &emsp;&emsp; D. suddenly
6. A. believe &emsp;&emsp; B. understand &emsp;&emsp; C. imagine &emsp;&emsp; D. expect
7. A. size &emsp;&emsp; B. color &emsp;&emsp; C. value &emsp;&emsp; D. price
8. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. warm &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. speed
10. A. whenever &emsp;&emsp; B. wherever &emsp;&emsp; C. whoever &emsp;&emsp; D. however

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我说好，因为一直想亲手做点东西。A 项意为"因为"；B 项意为"虽然"；C 项意为"除非"；D 项意为"直到"。表示原因用 because。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她安慰我说不会太难。A 项意为"警告"；B 项意为"承诺"；C 项意为"劝告、安慰"；D 项意为"拒绝"。advise 在此有宽慰之意。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：别针让布料保持在原位，这样缝线才能笔直。A 项 keep...in place 意为"固定、保持在原位"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"危险"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要轻踩踏板，慢慢移动布料。A 项意为"轻轻地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"几乎不"。缝纫需 gently 控制。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要确保两条提手长度相同，缝得一样牢固。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"不同地"；C 项意为"同样地"；D 项意为"突然地"。equally 与 symmetry 呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不敢相信自己做出了这么实用的东西。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"理解"；C 项意为"想象"；D 项意为"期待"。couldn't believe 表示难以置信。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：手工做的东西有一种特殊的价值。A 项意为"尺寸"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"价值"；D 项意为"价格"。special value 指情感价值。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次用这个袋子都会想起和奶奶在一起的下午，感到温暖。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"孤独的"；C 项意为"温暖的"；D 项意为"生气的"。feel warm 表示内心温暖。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要有耐心多多练习，相信能做出很多漂亮的东西。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"速度"。patience and practice 常搭配使用。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶笑着说只要我准备好学更多，她就会教。A 项 whenever 意为"每当、只要"；B 项意为"无论哪里"；C 项意为"无论谁"；D 项意为"然而"。whenever 表示任何时候。故选 A。
