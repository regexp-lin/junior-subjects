# 中考英语完形填空：邻居老爷爷的故事

## 完形填空原文

Mr. Wang is our next-door neighbor. He is over seventy years old, but he is always full of energy. Every morning, I see him watering the plants in his small garden. He has lived in this neighborhood for more than fifty years and knows almost everyone. When my family first moved here, he came to ___1___ us with a basket of fresh vegetables from his garden. His kindness made us feel welcome right away.

Mr. Wang loves to tell stories. He often ___2___ about his youth — how he worked on a farm, studied at night, and eventually became a teacher. His stories are not only interesting but also full of life lessons. He always says, "Hard work never ___3___ anyone." I have learned a lot from his words. Whenever I feel lazy or want to give up, I remember his stories and find the ___4___ to keep going.

Last month, Mr. Wang was ill and had to stay in hospital for a week. Many neighbors went to ___5___ him. When he came back home, we organized a small party to celebrate his recovery. He was so moved that his eyes became ___6___. He said we were like his family. From that day on, I have understood that a good neighbor is more than someone who lives nearby — they can be a true friend and a wise ___7___.

I feel lucky to have Mr. Wang as my neighbor. He has taught me the importance of being kind to others and never losing hope. I hope he will stay ___8___ and happy for many more years. Maybe one day, I can also become someone who ___9___ warmth and wisdom to the people around me, just like Mr. Wang does. That is the best way to ___10___ him for everything he has given us.

## 选项

1. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. welcome &emsp;&emsp; C. visit &emsp;&emsp; D. call
2. A. writes &emsp;&emsp; B. talks &emsp;&emsp; C. reads &emsp;&emsp; D. thinks
3. A. hurts &emsp;&emsp; B. fails &emsp;&emsp; C. stops &emsp;&emsp; D. leaves
4. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. reason &emsp;&emsp; C. strength &emsp;&emsp; D. way
5. A. see &emsp;&emsp; B. thank &emsp;&emsp; C. ask &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
6. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. wet &emsp;&emsp; C. big &emsp;&emsp; D. small
7. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. doctor &emsp;&emsp; C. guide &emsp;&emsp; D. neighbor
8. A. young &emsp;&emsp; B. healthy &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. rich
9. A. brings &emsp;&emsp; B. takes &emsp;&emsp; C. sends &emsp;&emsp; D. gives
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. miss &emsp;&emsp; C. thank &emsp;&emsp; D. remember

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们家刚搬来时，他带着一篮新鲜蔬菜来___我们。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"欢迎"；C 项意为"拜访"；D 项意为"打电话"。"welcome sb"意为"欢迎某人"，带着礼物来访即表示欢迎新邻居，符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他经常___他的青年时代。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"谈论"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"想"。"talk about"意为"谈论"，后文"tell stories"与"talk about"相呼应，都是口头讲述。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他总说："努力从不会___任何人。"A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"辜负、使失败"；C 项意为"阻止"；D 项意为"离开"。"Hard work never fails anyone"为常见谚语，意为"勤劳从不辜负人"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我记起他的故事，就找到了继续前进的___。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"理由"；C 项意为"力量、勇气"；D 项意为"方式"。"find the strength to keep going"意为"找到继续前行的力量"，与"想放弃"形成转折。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多邻居去医院___他。A 项意为"看望、探望"；B 项意为"感谢"；C 项意为"问"；D 项意为"跟随"。"see sb"在医院语境下常表示"探望某人"，与"visit"相似。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他非常感动，眼睛变得___。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"湿的"；C 项意为"大的"；D 项意为"小的"。"eyes became wet"意为"眼眶湿润"，是感动落泪的委婉说法。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好邻居不仅仅是住在附近的人——他们可以是真正的朋友和智慧的___。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"医生"；C 项意为"引路人、导师"；D 项意为"邻居"。"guide"可指人生路上的指引者，前文提到"life lessons"和"learned a lot"，故"wise guide"最贴切。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望他未来许多年都能保持___和快乐。A 项意为"年轻的"；B 项意为"健康的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"富有的"。"stay healthy"为常见祝福，与前文"ill"、"hospital"、"recovery"形成呼应。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许有一天，我也能成为给周围的人___温暖和智慧的人。A 项意为"带来"；B 项意为"带走"；C 项意为"发送"；D 项意为"给予"。"bring...to sb"意为"给某人带来……"，与"give"相近，但"bring"更强调将某物带给对方。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是对他给予我们一切最好的___方式。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"感谢"；D 项意为"记得"。"thank sb for sth"为固定搭配，意为"因某事感谢某人"。将温暖和智慧传递给他人，即是对老人最好的感谢。故选 C。
