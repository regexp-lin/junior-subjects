# 中考英语完形填空：学校秋季运动会

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held the autumn sports meeting. It was one of the most exciting ___1___ of the year. All the students looked forward to it.

On the morning of the sports meeting, the weather was fine. The sun was shining and a cool wind was blowing. We ___2___ at the playground before eight o'clock. Our class teacher gave us a short speech. She said, "No matter whether you win or lose, the most important thing is to do your best." Her words made us feel more ___3___.

I took part in the 800-meter run. When I stood at the starting line, my heart was beating ___4___. The moment the teacher said "Ready, go!", I ran as fast as I could. At first I was in the front, but after two laps I felt very tired. I wanted to ___5___, but I heard my classmates shouting, "Come on! You can do it!" Their encouragement gave me strength. I kept running and finally ___6___ the finishing line.

I didn't get first place, but I was still happy. Taking part in the sports meeting helped me understand that ___7___ is more important than winning. I also learned to never give up even when things get ___8___.

After the sports meeting, we took many photos together. Everyone had a ___9___ on their face. That day will always be a wonderful ___10___ in my school life.

## 选项

1. A. events &emsp;&emsp; B. lessons &emsp;&emsp; C. exams &emsp;&emsp; D. holidays
2. A. left &emsp;&emsp; B. gathered &emsp;&emsp; C. waited &emsp;&emsp; D. stayed
3. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. confident &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy
4. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
5. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. walk &emsp;&emsp; C. sit &emsp;&emsp; D. sleep
6. A. passed &emsp;&emsp; B. crossed &emsp;&emsp; C. reached &emsp;&emsp; D. found
7. A. trying &emsp;&emsp; B. winning &emsp;&emsp; C. running &emsp;&emsp; D. waiting
8. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. simple &emsp;&emsp; D. tough
9. A. tear &emsp;&emsp; B. smile &emsp;&emsp; C. mark &emsp;&emsp; D. wound
10. A. lesson &emsp;&emsp; B. gift &emsp;&emsp; C. memory &emsp;&emsp; D. story

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我们一年中最令人兴奋的活动之一。A 项意为"活动、赛事"；B 项意为"课程"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"假期"。运动会是 school event，故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们在八点前聚集在操场。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"聚集"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"停留"。gather at the playground 表示在操场聚集。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话让我们感到更有信心。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"自信的"；D 项意为"困倦的"。老师的鼓励让人 feel confident。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我站在起跑线上时，我的心跳得很快。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"安静地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。比赛前紧张时心跳 fast。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想停下来，但听到同学们在喊"加油"。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"走"；C 项意为"坐"；D 项意为"睡"。感到累时想 stop 跑步，与后文"kept running"形成对比。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我继续跑，最终冲过了终点线。A 项意为"经过、传递"；B 项意为"穿过、越过"；C 项意为"到达"；D 项意为"发现"。cross the finishing line 为固定搭配，意为"冲过终点线"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：参加运动会帮我明白了努力尝试比获胜更重要。A 项意为"尝试、努力"；B 项意为"获胜"；C 项意为"跑步"；D 项意为"等待"。强调 participation 和 trying，与 winning 对比。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也学会了即使事情变得艰难也不要放弃。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"艰难的"。things get tough 为常用搭配，意为"事情变难"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人的脸上都带着笑容。A 项意为"眼泪"；B 项意为"微笑"；C 项意为"标记"；D 项意为"伤口"。a smile on one's face 为常用表达。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那天将永远是我学校生活中美好的回忆。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"回忆"；D 项意为"故事"。a wonderful memory 意为"美好的回忆"。故选 C。
