# 中考英语完形填空：帮邻居照顾宠物猫

## 完形填空原文

Last week, my neighbor Mrs. Wang had to go on a business trip. She asked me to ___1___ her cat, Mimi, for five days. I was happy to help because I love animals.

Mrs. Wang told me that Mimi was a ___2___ cat. She liked to eat twice a day and needed fresh water every morning. I should also clean her litter box and play with her for a while each day. "Don't worry," Mrs. Wang said. "Mimi is ___3___ and she won't bite you."

On the first day, I went to Mrs. Wang's house with a key. When I opened the door, Mimi was ___4___ on the sofa. She looked at me and gave a soft "meow". I gave her food and water, and she ate ___5___. Everything went well.

The next day, I couldn't find Mimi anywhere. I looked under the bed, behind the curtains, and in every ___6___. I was worried. Just when I was about to call Mrs. Wang, Mimi jumped out of the wardrobe! She had been hiding there. I felt ___7___ and laughed. Cats really like to hide in quiet places.

During those five days, I visited Mimi every morning and evening. I learned how to take care of a pet. It was not always ___8___, but it taught me to be responsible. When Mrs. Wang came back, she thanked me and gave me a small gift. She said I did a ___9___ job. I was proud of myself. Maybe one day I will have a pet cat of my ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. feed &emsp;&emsp; C. watch &emsp;&emsp; D. take
2. A. wild &emsp;&emsp; B. lazy &emsp;&emsp; C. three-year-old &emsp;&emsp; D. hungry
3. A. strict &emsp;&emsp; B. friendly &emsp;&emsp; C. shy &emsp;&emsp; D. sad
4. A. running &emsp;&emsp; B. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; C. jumping &emsp;&emsp; D. eating
5. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. badly &emsp;&emsp; D. never
6. A. room &emsp;&emsp; B. corner &emsp;&emsp; C. box &emsp;&emsp; D. hole
7. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
8. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
9. A. good &emsp;&emsp; B. bad &emsp;&emsp; C. poor &emsp;&emsp; D. wrong
10. A. friend &emsp;&emsp; B. family &emsp;&emsp; C. own &emsp;&emsp; D. home

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她请我帮她照看她的猫咪咪，共五天。A 项意为"购买"；B 项意为"喂"；C 项意为"照看、看管"；D 项意为"带走"。watch/take care of one's pet 表示帮忙照看宠物。watch 在此意为"照看"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词/复合词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王太太告诉我咪咪是一只三岁的猫。A 项意为"野生的"；B 项意为"懒惰的"；C 项意为"三岁的"；D 项意为"饥饿的"。后文描述喂养习惯，three-year-old 补充猫的基本信息。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王太太说"别担心，咪咪很友好，不会咬你"。A 项意为"严格的"；B 项意为"友好的"；C 项意为"害羞的"；D 项意为"难过的"。friendly 与"won't bite"相符，让"我"放心。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我打开门时，咪咪正躺在沙发上。A 项意为"跑"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"跳"；D 项意为"吃"。躺在沙发上的猫通常是 sleeping 或 resting。sleeping on the sofa 最自然。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我给了它食物和水，它吃得很快。A 项意为"快地"；B 项意为"慢地"；C 项意为"糟糕地"；D 项意为"从不"。猫咪饿了会 eat quickly，表示一切顺利。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我找遍了床下、窗帘后和每个角落。A 项意为"房间"；B 项意为"角落"；C 项意为"盒子"；D 项意为"洞"。every corner 意为"每个角落"，与 under the bed、behind the curtains 并列。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到惊讶，笑了起来。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"疲惫的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。猫从衣柜里突然跳出来，令人 feel surprised。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：照顾宠物并不总是轻松的，但它教会我要有责任心。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"容易的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。not always easy 与后文 taught me to be responsible 呼应。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我做得很好。A 项意为"好的"；B 项意为"坏的"；C 项意为"差的"；D 项意为"错误的"。do a good job 为固定搭配，意为"干得好"。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词/形容词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许有一天我会拥有自己的宠物猫。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"家庭"；C 项意为"自己的"；D 项意为"家"。of my own 为固定短语，意为"属于自己的"。故选 C。
