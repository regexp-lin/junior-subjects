# 中考英语完形填空：雨后彩虹

## 完形填空原文

It had been raining all afternoon. I sat by the window and watched the drops fall on the glass. I felt a little bored and a bit ___1___. The sky was grey and the world outside looked dull. I had wanted to play basketball with my friends, but the rain had ruined our plans.

Then, around five o'clock, the rain began to stop. The clouds started to ___2___ and patches of blue sky appeared. I went outside and took a deep breath. The air smelled fresh — a mix of wet soil and grass. Everything looked cleaner and greener after the rain.

I looked up and there it was — a beautiful rainbow ___3___ across the sky. It had seven colours: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo and violet. I had seen rainbows in books and photos before, but seeing one in real life was different. It was so ___4___ that I stood there for a long time, unable to look away.

I ran back inside to get my phone and take a photo. When I came back, the rainbow was starting to fade. I managed to take one picture before it disappeared completely. I felt ___5___ that I had caught it. Rainbows do not last long. They remind us that beautiful things can appear when we least expect them, and that they will not stay forever. We need to ___6___ the moment.

That evening, I thought about what the rainbow had taught me. After the grey rain, something wonderful had appeared. It made me think about life. Sometimes we go through difficult ___7___ — like the rain. But if we wait patiently, something good may come. The rainbow gave me hope. I decided not to feel sad about the cancelled basketball game. Instead, I would look forward to the next sunny day. And I would remember: after every storm, there can be a ___8___. That simple ___9___ has stayed with me ever since. When things get difficult, I try to stay ___10___ and believe that better days will come.

1. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. happy &emsp;&emsp; C. sad &emsp;&emsp; D. calm

7. A. moments &emsp;&emsp; B. times &emsp;&emsp; C. places &emsp;&emsp; D. friends
8. A. rainbow &emsp;&emsp; B. storm &emsp;&emsp; C. cloud &emsp;&emsp; D. wind
9. A. lesson &emsp;&emsp; B. photo &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. book

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点无聊也有点___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"开心的"；C 项意为"难过的"；D 项意为"平静的"。下雨取消计划会让人失落，"sad"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：云开始___，露出一块块蓝天。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"出现"；C 项意为"变暗"；D 项意为"落下"。雨停后云散开，"disappear"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一道美丽的彩虹___在天际。A 项意为"飞"；B 项意为"延伸、伸展"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"落下"。"stretching across"意为"横跨、延伸"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它如此___，我久久驻足。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。彩虹美丽令人驻足，"beautiful"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___抓拍到了。A 项意为"遗憾的"；B 项意为"幸运的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"生气的"。"lucky"表示庆幸拍到了。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们要___那一刻。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"错过"；C 项意为"珍惜"；D 项意为"浪费"。"cherish the moment"意为"珍惜当下"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们会经历艰难的___。A 项意为"时刻"；B 项意为"时光、时期"；C 项意为"地方"；D 项意为"朋友"。"difficult times"意为"艰难时期"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每场风雨过后都可能出现___。A 项意为"彩虹"；B 项意为"暴风雨"；C 项意为"云"；D 项意为"风"。与主题呼应，"rainbow"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个简单的___一直留在我心中。A 项意为"道理、教训"；B 项意为"照片"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"书"。"lesson"指从彩虹中领悟的道理。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：困难时我尽量保持___，相信好日子会来。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"充满希望的"；D 项意为"累的"。"stay hopeful"意为"保持希望"。故选 C。
