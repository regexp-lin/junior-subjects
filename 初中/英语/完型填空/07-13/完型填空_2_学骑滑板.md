# 中考英语完形填空：学骑滑板

## 完形填空原文

My best friend Lin Kai got a new skateboard for his birthday. He asked me if I wanted to learn how to ride it. I had never tried skateboarding before, but I said yes. We went to the park where the ground was flat and ___1___.

Lin Kai showed me the basics first. "Put one foot on the board and push with the other," he said. "Keep your balance." It sounded easy, but when I tried, the board shot out from under my feet. I ___2___ on the ground. My hands stung a little. Lin Kai helped me up and said, "Everybody falls at first. Try again."

I got back on the board. This time I pushed more gently. The board moved forward. I felt a moment of excitement — I was actually moving! But then I lost my balance and fell again. My ___3___ was bruised, but I was not seriously hurt. Lin Kai said I was making progress. "Each fall teaches you something," he said. "You are learning how not to fall."

After many tries, I could finally ride for a few metres without falling. The feeling of the wind in my face was amazing. I felt so ___4___. I had done something I thought I could never do. Lin Kai cheered and gave me a high-five. "See? I knew you could do it!"

That afternoon we practised for two hours. By the end, I could turn a little and ride slowly around the park. I was still not as good as Lin Kai, but I had improved a lot. He said we could practise again next weekend. I could not ___5___.

On the way home, I thought about what I had learned. Learning something new is never easy. There will be falls and ___6___. But if we keep trying and never give up, we will get better. The skateboard had taught me that ___7___ is the key to success. Every expert was once a beginner. I could not wait to tell my parents about my new ___8___. Maybe they would let me get a skateboard of my own one day. For now, I was just happy to have a friend who was willing to ___9___ me and help me learn. That is what real friends do — they encourage us to try new things and celebrate our ___10___ with us.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们去了地面平坦且___的公园。A 项意为"粗糙的"；B 项意为"平滑的"；C 项意为"潮湿的"；D 项意为"黑暗的"。滑板需要平滑地面，"smooth"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：滑板从脚下溜走，我___在地。A 项意为"站"；B 项意为"坐"；C 项意为"摔"；D 项意为"跳"。失去平衡后摔倒，"fell"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的___青了一块。A 项意为"头"；B 项意为"膝盖"；C 项意为"手臂"；D 项意为"脚"。滑板摔倒常伤膝盖，"knee"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我做了以为自己做不到的事，感到非常___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"难过的"；D 项意为"生气的"。"proud"表示成就感。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他说下周末再练，我___。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"理解"；C 项意为"相信"；D 项意为"记住"。"could not wait"意为"等不及"，期待下次练习。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：会有摔倒和___。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"失败"；C 项意为"奖品"；D 项意为"游戏"。"falls and failures"意为"摔倒和失败"，与学习过程对应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：滑板教会我___是成功的关键。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"速度"；D 项意为"金钱"。通过反复练习学会滑板，"practice"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我等不及告诉父母我的新___。A 项意为"玩具"；B 项意为"技能"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"礼物"。学会滑板是新技能，"skill"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有朋友愿意___我、帮我学。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"打败"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"忘记"。"teach me"意为"教我"，朋友教滑板。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们鼓励我们尝试新事物，并与我们共同庆祝我们的___。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"成功"；D 项意为"错误"。"celebrate our successes"意为"庆祝我们的成功"。故选 C。
