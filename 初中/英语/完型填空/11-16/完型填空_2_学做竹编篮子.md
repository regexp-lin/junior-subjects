# 中考英语完形填空：学做竹编篮子

## 完形填空原文

During the summer holiday, I went to visit my grandparents in the countryside. Grandpa showed me his bamboo weaving skills. He had made many baskets, fans, and small boxes. I asked him to teach me, and he agreed with a smile.

First, Grandpa soaked thin bamboo strips in water. He said the strips needed to be ___1___ so they wouldn't break when we bent them. We waited for about an hour before we could start.

Grandpa showed me the basic weaving pattern. We would weave the strips over and under each other, like a checkerboard. "Hold the strips ___2___ but not too tight," he said. "If you pull too hard, the bamboo will snap."

I tried to follow his movements. At first, my fingers felt clumsy. The strips kept slipping out of place. Grandpa was very ___3___. He corrected my hands gently and said, "Don't rush. Bamboo weaving takes time to master."

We worked on a small rectangular base first. I had to count the strips carefully to make sure the pattern was ___4___. When the base was done, we bent the strips upward to form the sides. This was the trickiest part. I had to keep the angle ___5___ so the basket wouldn't lean to one side.

After several hours, we finished a small basket. It wasn't as perfect as Grandpa's, but it was mine. I felt a great sense of ___6___. Grandpa said that every craftsperson starts from somewhere, and practice makes perfect.

I took the basket home and use it to hold fruits on our kitchen table. Every time I look at it, I ___7___ that afternoon with Grandpa. Bamboo weaving is an old traditional skill. I hope more young people will learn it and keep this ___8___ alive.

Grandpa promised to teach me more patterns next time I visit. I want to try making a round basket or a small storage box. Traditional crafts connect us to our ___9___ and teach us the value of patience and care. I am grateful that Grandpa is willing to ___10___ his knowledge with me.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：竹条需要泡湿，弯曲时才不会断。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"湿的"；C 项意为"硬的"；D 项意为"粗糙的"。soaked 与 wet 呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要稳稳握住竹条，但不要太紧。A 项意为"松散地"；B 项意为"牢固地"；C 项意为"粗心地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。firmly but not too tight 意为握稳但不过紧。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷很有耐心。A 项意为"严格的"；B 项意为"有耐心的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"懒的"。教新手需要 patient。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要仔细数竹条，确保纹路整齐。A 项意为"错的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项 even 意为"均匀的、整齐的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。pattern even 表示纹路整齐。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要始终保持角度一致，篮子才不歪斜。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项 the same 意为"相同的"；C 项意为"尖锐的"；D 项意为"圆的"。keep the angle same 意为保持角度一致。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很有成就感。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"惊讶"。sense of pride 意为自豪感。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次看到篮子就会想起和爷爷在一起的那个下午。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"后悔"；D 项意为"想念"。remember 与 look at it 呼应。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望更多年轻人学习并传承这一传统。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项意为"传统"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"游戏"。keep tradition alive 意为传承传统。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：传统手工艺把我们与过去连接起来。A 项意为"未来"；B 项意为"现在"；C 项意为"过去"；D 项意为"思想"。traditional crafts 与 past 呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感激爷爷愿意与我分享他的技艺。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"失去"。share knowledge 意为分享知识。故选 B。
