# 中考英语完形填空：一张旧地图

## 完形填空原文

While cleaning the attic last weekend, I found a dusty old box behind the bookshelf. Inside was a hand-drawn map on yellowed paper. It showed our neighborhood, but everything looked ___1___ — the streets were narrower, there were no tall buildings, and a river ran through the middle of what is now Main Street.

"Grandpa, what's this?" I asked, bringing the map downstairs.

His eyes widened when he saw it. "I drew that when I was your age," he said, his voice filled with ___2___. "That's what this neighborhood looked like sixty years ago."

I was fascinated. "There was a river here?"

"Yes," he said, sitting down slowly. "We used to swim in it during summer. There were fish, frogs, and even turtles. The water was so ___3___ you could drink it."

I couldn't believe it. The same spot was now a busy road covered in concrete. "What happened to the river?" I asked.

"They filled it in when I was about twenty," Grandpa explained. "The city was growing, and they needed more ___4___ for roads and buildings. People thought it was progress."

He pointed to another spot on the map. "Here was a forest where we played every afternoon. And here was old Mr. Huang's farm — he grew the best watermelons in town." He ___5___ at the memory.

"Do you miss it?" I asked.

He was ___6___ for a moment. "Every day," he said quietly. "But you can't stop time. Things change. The important thing is to ___7___ what we have now and to learn from the past."

That conversation inspired me. For my geography project, I decided to compare Grandpa's map with a ___8___ one. I interviewed elderly neighbors, collected old photos, and created a before-and-after display.

My teacher loved it. She said it was the most meaningful project she had ever ___9___. "History isn't just in books," she told the class. "It's in our neighborhoods, our families, and even in our attics."

I hung Grandpa's map on my bedroom wall. Every time I look at it, I'm ___10___ that the places we love today will also change someday. And that makes me want to appreciate them even more.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但一切看起来都___。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"现代的"。后文描述街道更窄、没有高楼、有河流，和现在完全"不同"（different）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的声音充满了___。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"惊喜"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"骄傲"。看到自己年少时画的地图，爷爷既惊又喜，充满"惊喜"（surprise）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：水___到可以喝。A 项意为"深的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"宽的"。能直接饮用说明河水非常"干净"（clean）。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：城市在发展，需要更多___来建路和建筑。A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"空间"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"时间"。填河是为了腾出更多"空间"（space）用于城市建设。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他回忆起这些时___了。A 项意为"哭了"；B 项意为"担心了"；C 项意为"笑了"；D 项意为"喊了"。回忆老黄伯的西瓜是美好的童年记忆，爷爷"微笑了"（smiled）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他___了一会儿。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"沉默的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。被问到是否想念过去，爷爷"沉默"（quiet）了一会儿才回答，表达深沉的感情。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是___我们现在拥有的。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"保护"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"卖"。爷爷说时间不能倒流，要"珍惜/保护"（protect）现在拥有的。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定把爷爷的地图和一张___地图做对比。A 项意为"外国的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"现代的"；D 项意为"秘密的"。旧地图和"现代"（modern）地图对比，做before-and-after展示。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是她___过的最有意义的项目。A 项意为"买过"；B 项意为"画过"；C 项意为"写过"；D 项意为"见过"。老师评价这是她"见过"（seen）的最有意义的地理项目。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次看到它，我都被___我们今天喜欢的地方将来也会变。A 项意为"提醒"；B 项意为"担心"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"承诺"。旧地图"提醒"（reminded）"我"一切都在变化，所以更要珍惜当下。故选 A。
