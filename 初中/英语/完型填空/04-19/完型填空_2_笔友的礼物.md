# 中考英语完形填空：笔友的礼物

## 完形填空原文

Two years ago, I joined a pen pal program at school. I was matched with Emma, a girl from Australia. We started writing letters to each other. At first, our letters were short and simple — we talked about our schools, our hobbies, and the weather. ___1___, our friendship grew. We shared our dreams, our worries, and our favorite books.

Emma always drew small pictures at the bottom of her letters. Sometimes it was a kangaroo, sometimes a koala. I looked forward to every letter, wondering what ___2___ she would draw next.

Last month, Emma wrote that she was sending me a gift. "It's something from my country," she said. "I hope you'll like it." I was so ___3___. I had never received a gift from someone so far away.

The package arrived three weeks later. It was a flat box, not too heavy. I opened it carefully. Inside was a beautiful scarf with patterns of Australian flowers and a note. The note said: "Friendship knows no distance. Thank you for being my friend. — Emma"

I held the scarf and felt my eyes get wet. Emma and I had never met. We lived in different countries and spoke different ___4___ languages. Yet she had taken the time to choose a gift, pack it, and send it across the ocean. That meant more to me than the scarf itself.

I wore the scarf to school the next day. When my classmates asked where I got it, I told them about Emma. "You have a pen pal in Australia? That's so cool!" they said. I realized that the scarf was not just a piece of cloth. It was a ___5___ of a friendship that had crossed thousands of kilometers.

I decided to send Emma something in return. I thought about what would represent my country and my gratitude. I chose a Chinese knot — a traditional symbol of good luck and connection. I wrapped it carefully and wrote a long letter to ___6___ my thanks.

A few weeks later, Emma wrote back. She loved the knot and had hung it in her room. "Every time I see it," she wrote, "I remember that I have a friend on the other side of the world. It makes me feel less ___7___."

Her words made me understand something important. In a world that sometimes feels divided, small gestures like gifts and letters can build bridges. We don't need to meet in person to be ___8___. We just need to care and show that we do.

The scarf now sits on my shelf. I don't wear it every day — I want to keep it special. But whenever I feel lonely or doubtful about the world, I look at it and remember Emma's note: "Friendship knows no distance." It reminds me that kindness can travel ___9___ any border, and that a girl in Australia and a girl in China can be true friends.

I hope to meet Emma one day. Until then, we will keep writing. And I will always be ___10___ for the gift she sent — not because of what it was, but because of what it meant. It was a reminder that we are never alone as long as we reach out to others.

## 选项

1. A. Quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. Slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. Suddenly &emsp;&emsp; D. Finally
2. A. picture &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. color &emsp;&emsp; D. word
3. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
4. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. second &emsp;&emsp; C. foreign &emsp;&emsp; D. native
5. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. sign &emsp;&emsp; C. price &emsp;&emsp; D. choice
6. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. hide &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. receive
7. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. busy &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
8. A. friends &emsp;&emsp; B. neighbors &emsp;&emsp; C. classmates &emsp;&emsp; D. strangers
9. A. across &emsp;&emsp; B. behind &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. except
10. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. ready &emsp;&emsp; C. thankful &emsp;&emsp; D. famous

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___，我们的友谊加深了。A 项意为"很快"；B 项意为"逐渐地"；C 项意为"突然"；D 项意为"最终"。"Slowly"与"at first...short and simple"呼应，表示友谊是"慢慢"建立起来的。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我期待每一封信，好奇她下次会画什么___。A 项意为"图画"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"词"。前文说她常在信尾画小图，故为"图画"（picture）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"担忧的"；D 项意为"累的"。收到远方的礼物，心情是"兴奋"（excited）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们住在不同国家，说不同的___语言。A 项意为"第一"；B 项意为"第二"；C 项意为"外语"；D 项意为"母语"。"native language"意为"母语"，两人各自说自己的母语。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：围巾不仅是一块布，它是跨越数千公里友谊的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"象征/标志"；C 项意为"价格"；D 项意为"选择"。"sign"表示围巾是这段友谊的"象征"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仔细包装并写了一封长信来___我的感谢。A 项意为"表达/展示"；B 项意为"藏"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"收到"。"show one's thanks"意为"表达感谢"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："每次看到它，我就想起世界的另一端有个朋友，这让我感觉不那么___。"A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"忙碌"；C 项意为"孤独"；D 项意为"生气"。"less lonely"表示友谊减轻"孤独"（lonely）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不必见面也能成为___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"邻居"；C 项意为"同学"；D 项意为"陌生人"。全文讲笔友情谊，空处应为"朋友"（friends）。"be friends"为固定搭配。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：善意可以___任何边界传播。A 项意为"跨越"；B 项意为"在……后面"；C 项意为"没有"；D 项意为"除了"。"travel across"意为"跨越"，强调超越国界。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会永远___她送我的礼物。A 项意为"抱歉"；B 项意为"准备好"；C 项意为"感激"；D 项意为"著名"。"be thankful for"意为"对……心存感激"。故选 C。
