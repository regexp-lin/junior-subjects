# 中考英语完形填空：爸爸的出租车

## 完形填空原文

My father has been a taxi driver for fifteen years. He works from early morning until late at night. When I was younger, I felt a bit ___1___ when my classmates talked about their fathers' jobs. "My dad is a manager." "My dad works in an office." I never said much about my father's job.

One rainy evening, Dad came home later than usual. He looked tired. "There was an old lady at the train station," he said. "She had no umbrella and didn't know how to get home. I drove her for free." Mom smiled and said, "That's just like you."

I started to pay more ___2___ to the stories Dad told at dinner. He had helped a lost child find his parents. He had driven a sick woman to the hospital in the middle of the night. He had given free rides to students who couldn't afford the fare. My father didn't earn much, but he had a ___3___ heart.

One day, I went with Dad to work. I sat in the back and watched him drive. He was always polite to passengers. He helped an elderly man with his bags. He gave directions to a tourist who didn't speak Chinese well. When we stopped for lunch, Dad shared his food with a homeless man sitting near the restaurant.

"Dad," I asked, "why do you help so many people? You work so hard already."

He thought for a moment. "When I drive, I meet many people. Some are happy, some are sad. If I can ___4___ their day a little better, why not? A taxi is not just for going from one place to another. It can be a place where people feel a bit of ___5___ on a difficult day."

His words stayed with me. I began to understand that a job is not only about money or title. It's about how we ___6___ others. My father might drive a taxi, but he drives it with kindness and care. That makes him a great man in my eyes.

Last month, a woman came to our door. She said she was the mother of the child Dad had helped years ago. She had spent a long time ___7___ our address. She wanted to thank Dad in person and brought a small gift. Dad refused the gift but was clearly moved. "I just did what anyone would do," he said.

Now when my classmates ask about my father's job, I say with pride, "My dad drives a taxi. He helps people every day. I think he has the most ___8___ job in the world."

I have learned from my father that we can find meaning in any work if we do it with love. The taxi he drives is old and simple. But the warmth he brings to his passengers makes it something ___9___. It's not just a car — it's a place where strangers can feel a little less alone in a big city.

Thank you, Dad, for teaching me that the best way to succeed in life is not to climb the highest, but to lift others ___10___ as we go.

## 选项

1. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. ashamed &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
2. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. attention &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. care
3. A. kind &emsp;&emsp; B. weak &emsp;&emsp; C. brave &emsp;&emsp; D. clever
4. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. find &emsp;&emsp; D. spend
5. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. worry &emsp;&emsp; C. warmth &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
6. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. trust &emsp;&emsp; C. treat &emsp;&emsp; D. need
7. A. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; B. losing &emsp;&emsp; C. finding &emsp;&emsp; D. changing
8. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. interesting &emsp;&emsp; C. meaningful &emsp;&emsp; D. tiring
9. A. cheap &emsp;&emsp; B. new &emsp;&emsp; C. special &emsp;&emsp; D. fast
10. A. up &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. out &emsp;&emsp; D. back

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小时候，当同学谈起他们父亲的工作时，我有点___。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"羞耻的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。别人爸爸是经理、坐办公室，自己爸爸开出租车，起初会感到"不好意思"（ashamed）。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我开始更___地听爸爸晚饭时讲的故事。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"注意力"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"关心"。"pay attention to"为固定短语，意为"关注、留心"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸挣得不多，但有一颗___的心。A 项意为"善良的"；B 项意为"弱的"；C 项意为"勇敢的"；D 项意为"聪明的"。前文列举他帮助他人，故用"善良"（kind）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："如果我能让他们的日子___好一点，为什么不呢？"A 项意为"使"；B 项意为"保持"；C 项意为"发现"；D 项意为"花费"。"make...better"意为"使……变得更好"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：出租车可以是让人在艰难日子里感到一点___的地方。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"担忧"；C 项意为"温暖"；D 项意为"恐惧"。爸爸的善意带给乘客的是"温暖"（warmth）。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：工作不仅关乎金钱或头衔，还关乎我们如何___他人。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"信任"；C 项意为"对待"；D 项意为"需要"。"treat others"意为"对待他人"，与爸爸的善良行为对应。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她花了很长时间___我们的地址。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"找到"；D 项意为"改变"。"spend time finding"表示费心"找到"地址，专程来道谢。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："我觉得他有世界上最有___的工作。"A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"有趣的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"累人的"。儿子理解了爸爸帮助他人的价值，认为工作很"有意义"（meaningful）。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但他带给乘客的温暖让它变得___。A 项意为"便宜的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"快的"。车虽旧，因爸爸的善意而显得"特别"（special）。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词/介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人生最好的成功不是爬得最高，而是在前行的路上___他人一把。A 项意为"向上"；B 项意为"向下"；C 项意为"向外"；D 项意为"回去"。"lift others up"意为"帮助他人提升/振作"，为常用表达。故选 A。
