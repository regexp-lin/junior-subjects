# 中考英语完形填空：和朋友爬山

## 完形填空原文

On October 4th, my friends and I decided to climb Green Mountain. It was a sunny day, perfect for outdoor ___1___.

We met at the bus stop at seven in the morning and took a bus to the foot of the mountain. Looking up, the mountain seemed very ___2___. I wondered if I could make it to the top. But my friend Wang Lei patted me on the shoulder and said, "Don't worry. We'll do it together."

The first part of the path was wide and easy. We walked and chatted happily. The trees along the way were turning yellow and red. Autumn ___3___ were everywhere, making the mountain look like a colorful painting.

As we climbed higher, the path became steeper and ___4___. I started to feel tired. My legs were heavy and I was breathing hard. Some of my friends slowed down too. But Wang Lei kept encouraging us. "Just one more step at a time," he said. His words gave me ___5___.

After about two hours, we finally reached the top. The view was amazing! We could see the whole city below us. The buildings looked tiny, and the river looked like a silver ribbon. We all ___6___ with joy and took photos to remember this moment.

We sat on some rocks and shared the food we had brought — bread, fruit, and water. Everything tasted ___7___ than usual, maybe because we had worked so hard to get there. We laughed, joked, and enjoyed the fresh mountain air.

On the way down, I ___8___ slipped on some wet rocks. Luckily, Wang Lei caught my arm just in time. "Be careful," he said. I was grateful to have such a good friend. True friends are those who stay by your side when things get ___9___.

We arrived at the bottom of the mountain at around three o'clock. Although we were all exhausted, we felt proud of ourselves. This trip taught me that no matter how difficult the ___10___ is, as long as you don't give up and have good friends beside you, you can reach the top.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天气晴朗，非常适合户外活动。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"餐"。outdoor activities 意为"户外活动"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：仰望山峰，山看起来很高。A 项意为"低的"；B 项意为"小的"；C 项意为"高的"；D 项意为"平的"。从山脚仰望觉得 high。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：秋叶遍地，让山看起来像一幅五彩斑斓的画。A 项意为"树叶"；B 项意为"花"；C 项意为"水果"；D 项意为"鸟"。前文提到 yellow and red，秋天的 leaves。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：越往上爬，路越陡越窄。A 项意为"更宽"；B 项意为"更光滑"；C 项意为"更短"；D 项意为"更窄"。与前文 wide 对比，变 narrower。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话给了我力量。A 项意为"压力"；B 项意为"力量"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"悲伤"。鼓励的话给人 strength。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都开心地欢呼起来。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"欢呼"；C 项意为"抱怨"；D 项意为"低语"。到达山顶开心 cheered with joy。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：所有东西都比平时好吃。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更奇怪"；C 项意为"更好"；D 项意为"更轻"。辛苦后食物 tasted better。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下山时我差点滑倒在湿滑的石头上。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"总是"；C 项意为"很少"；D 项意为"几乎"。almost slipped 意为"差点滑倒"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：真正的朋友是在困难时陪在你身边的人。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。when things get tough 意为"当事情变得困难时"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无论旅程多么艰难，只要不放弃且有好友相伴，你就能到达顶峰。A 项意为"旅程"；B 项意为"课"；C 项意为"测试"；D 项意为"游戏"。整篇讲爬山 journey。故选 A。
