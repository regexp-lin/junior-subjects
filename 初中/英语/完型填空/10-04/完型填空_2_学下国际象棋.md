# 中考英语完形填空：学下国际象棋

## 完形填空原文

My uncle came to visit us during the National Day holiday. He is a chess lover and has won many competitions. On October 4th, he offered to ___1___ me how to play chess.

"Chess is not just a game," he said. "It teaches you to think carefully and plan ___2___." I was curious and excited to learn.

He started by showing me the board and the pieces. Each piece has a different ___3___ and can move in different ways. The king is the most important piece, but it can only move one step at a time. The queen is the most powerful — she can move in any direction. I thought that was very ___4___.

Then we started to play our first game. Uncle told me to think before every move. "Don't just react to what the other player does," he said. "Try to ___5___ what they might do next." This was hard for me because I was used to making quick decisions without much thought.

In our first game, I lost ___6___. Uncle captured almost all my pieces within ten minutes. I felt a little disappointed, but he said, "Losing is part of learning. The important thing is to understand ___7___ you lost."

We played three more games that afternoon. I lost all of them, but each time I lasted a little ___8___. In the last game, I even managed to capture Uncle's queen, which made me very proud. He said I was making fast progress.

That evening, Uncle gave me a chess book and suggested that I ___9___ some basic strategies. He also told me about an online chess platform where I could play with other beginners. I was excited to continue learning.

Chess has become my new hobby. It is ___10___ different from computer games — it requires real thinking and patience. I hope that one day I can play as well as my uncle.

## 选项

1. A. ask &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. allow &emsp;&emsp; D. pay
2. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. back &emsp;&emsp; C. ahead &emsp;&emsp; D. away
3. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. price &emsp;&emsp; C. name &emsp;&emsp; D. role
4. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. scary &emsp;&emsp; C. interesting &emsp;&emsp; D. sad
5. A. remember &emsp;&emsp; B. predict &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. copy
6. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
7. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. where &emsp;&emsp; C. why &emsp;&emsp; D. who
8. A. shorter &emsp;&emsp; B. worse &emsp;&emsp; C. faster &emsp;&emsp; D. longer
9. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. study &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. hate
10. A. hardly &emsp;&emsp; B. completely &emsp;&emsp; C. never &emsp;&emsp; D. almost

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他主动提出教我下棋。A 项意为"问"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"允许"；D 项意为"付钱"。teach sb how to 意为"教某人如何"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教你仔细思考并提前计划。A 项意为"在后面"；B 项意为"回来"；C 项意为"提前"；D 项意为"离开"。plan ahead 意为"提前计划"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个棋子有不同的角色，移动方式也不同。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"价格"；C 项意为"名字"；D 项意为"角色"。后文解释不同棋子的功能，即 role。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得这非常有趣。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"可怕的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"悲伤的"。学到新知识觉得 interesting。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：试着预测对方接下来可能做什么。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"预测"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"复制"。下棋要 predict 对手的动作。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一盘棋我输得很快。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"开心地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。十分钟内几乎全被吃掉，lost quickly。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的是理解你为什么输了。A 项意为"何时"；B 项意为"哪里"；C 项意为"为什么"；D 项意为"谁"。understand why you lost 意为"理解输的原因"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次我都坚持得更久一点。A 项意为"更短"；B 项意为"更差"；C 项意为"更快"；D 项意为"更长"。lasted a little longer 意为"坚持更久"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔给了我一本棋书，建议我学习一些基本策略。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"讨厌"。study some basic strategies 意为"学习基本策略"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：国际象棋与电脑游戏完全不同。A 项意为"几乎不"；B 项意为"完全地"；C 项意为"从不"；D 项意为"几乎"。completely different 意为"完全不同"。故选 B。
