# 中考英语完形填空：学做风筝

## 完形填空原文

One sunny afternoon during the summer holiday, my uncle came to visit us. He brought some bamboo sticks, colourful paper, and string. "Let me teach you how to make a kite," he said with a big smile. I was ___1___ because I had always wanted to fly a kite but never knew how to make one.

Uncle first showed me how to ___2___ the bamboo sticks into a cross shape. He used string to tie them tightly at the centre. "The frame must be strong," he explained, "or the kite will break when the wind blows." I watched carefully and tried to remember every step.

Next, we laid the frame on a large piece of red paper. Uncle drew a diamond shape around it and cut it out with scissors. Then he showed me how to ___3___ the paper onto the frame using glue. I helped him smooth out the wrinkles. It was harder than it looked because the paper was thin and tore ___4___.

After the body was done, Uncle cut long strips of green paper for the tail. "The tail helps the kite stay ___5___ in the air," he said. "Without it, the kite will spin and crash." We attached three strips to the bottom of the kite. It looked beautiful — red and green, just like a big butterfly.

Finally, we tied a long string to the centre of the frame. Uncle said the angle of the string was very ___6___. If it was too high, the kite would not catch the wind. If it was too low, it would dive into the ground.

We took our kite to the park near our home. The wind was gentle and perfect for flying. Uncle held the kite while I ran forward with the string. On my first try, the kite rose a little and then fell. I was ___7___, but Uncle encouraged me to try again. On the third attempt, the kite finally caught the wind and soared into the sky. I shouted with ___8___ as I watched it dance above the trees.

That evening, I hung the kite on my bedroom wall. Every time I looked at it, I remembered the fun afternoon with my uncle. Making things with your own hands gives you a special sense of ___9___. I decided to learn to make other things too — maybe a paper lantern or a wooden toy.

Uncle left the next day, but the kite stayed with me. It was more than just a toy. It was a ___10___ of the time we spent together and the joy of creating something from nothing.

9. A. humour &emsp;&emsp; B. achievement &emsp;&emsp; C. danger &emsp;&emsp; D. loss

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常___因为我一直想放风筝但不知道怎么做。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"激动的"；D 项意为"困惑的"。一直想做的事有机会学，自然很激动，"thrilled"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔先教我把竹条___成十字形。A 项意为"排列/摆放"；B 项意为"扔"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"收集"。将竹条摆成十字形，"arrange"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然后他教我用胶水把纸___到框架上。A 项意为"缝"；B 项意为"粘"；C 项意为"挂"；D 项意为"落下"。用胶水粘贴纸张，"stick"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：纸很薄，很___就撕破了。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"容易地"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"沉重地"。薄纸容易撕破，"easily"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尾巴帮助风筝在空中保持___。A 项意为"湿的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"稳定的"；D 项意为"安静的"。风筝尾巴的作用是保持平衡稳定，"stable"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：叔叔说线的角度非常___。A 项意为"普通的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"重要的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。后文解释角度太高或太低都不行，说明角度很关键，"important"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一次尝试风筝升起又落下，我很___。A 项意为"失望的"；B 项意为"满意的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。第一次失败当然失望，但叔叔鼓励再试，"disappointed"正确。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看着风筝在树上方飞舞，我___地大喊。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"喜悦"。风筝终于飞起来了，非常开心，"joy"正确。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用自己的双手做东西会给你一种特殊的___感。A 项意为"幽默"；B 项意为"成就"；C 项意为"危险"；D 项意为"失去"。亲手制作带来成就感，"achievement"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它不仅仅是一个玩具，它是我们共度时光和创造快乐的___。A 项意为"象征"；B 项意为"复制品"；C 项意为"图片"；D 项意为"问题"。风筝象征着美好回忆，"symbol"正确。故选 A。
