# 中考英语完形填空：夜市小吃

## 完形填空原文

Every Friday evening in August, my family went to the night market near the river. It was one of my favourite ___1___ during the summer holiday. The market was always crowded and full of life.

As soon as we arrived, wonderful smells from the food stalls filled the air. My mother loved the grilled corn, and my father always went straight to the lamb skewer stand. My little sister and I ___2___ had the same thing — cotton candy and bubble tea. We each had our own favourites, and nobody could change our minds.

One evening, I decided to try something ___3___. I noticed a stall selling stinky tofu. I had always thought it smelled terrible and refused to eat it. But that night, the stall owner was very friendly. He offered me a small piece to taste for free. I hesitated, then took a ___4___ bite. To my surprise, the taste was actually delicious — crispy on the outside and soft inside. I immediately bought a full portion.

My sister laughed at me. "You said you would never eat that!" she said. I smiled and replied, "Well, sometimes you have to try things before you ___5___ them." That night, I learned an important lesson: do not judge things based only on first impressions.

The night market was not just about food. There were also games, crafts, and small shops selling accessories and clothing. My mother liked to look at the handmade earrings and bracelets. My father enjoyed watching the sugar painting artist, who could create beautiful animal ___6___ using melted sugar. It was like magic.

My favourite part was the small stage at the centre of the market. Local musicians and performers put on ___7___ there every weekend. One night, a group of teenagers performed a traditional drum dance. The rhythm was powerful and exciting. Everyone stopped to watch, and the crowd clapped along with the ___8___.

As the summer went on, the night market became a place full of warm memories. It was where our family relaxed, laughed, and enjoyed being ___9___. I hope the night market will still be there next summer. If it is, I will definitely go back — and this time, I might try something even more ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. subjects &emsp;&emsp; B. activities &emsp;&emsp; C. problems &emsp;&emsp; D. lessons
2. A. never &emsp;&emsp; B. seldom &emsp;&emsp; C. always &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
3. A. new &emsp;&emsp; B. expensive &emsp;&emsp; C. safe &emsp;&emsp; D. familiar
4. A. big &emsp;&emsp; B. quick &emsp;&emsp; C. small &emsp;&emsp; D. long
5. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. cook &emsp;&emsp; C. judge &emsp;&emsp; D. sell
6. A. toys &emsp;&emsp; B. shapes &emsp;&emsp; C. cages &emsp;&emsp; D. houses
7. A. meals &emsp;&emsp; B. exams &emsp;&emsp; C. shows &emsp;&emsp; D. meetings
8. A. beat &emsp;&emsp; B. song &emsp;&emsp; C. speech &emsp;&emsp; D. picture
9. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. together &emsp;&emsp; C. apart &emsp;&emsp; D. away
10. A. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. adventurous

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是暑假期间我最喜欢的___之一。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"课程"。逛夜市是一种活动，"activities"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我和妹妹___吃同样的东西——棉花糖和奶茶。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"总是"；D 项意为"几乎不"。后文说"每个人有自己的最爱，没人能改变我们的想法"，说明她们总是选一样的，"always"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一天晚上，我决定试一些___的东西。A 项意为"新的"；B 项意为"贵的"；C 项意为"安全的"；D 项意为"熟悉的"。尝试之前没吃过的臭豆腐，是新的尝试，"new"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我犹豫了一下，然后___地咬了一口。A 项意为"大的"；B 项意为"快的"；C 项意为"小的"；D 项意为"长的"。之前对臭豆腐有抵触，所以先小心地小口尝试，"small"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时候你必须先尝试再___它们。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"做饭"；C 项意为"评判"；D 项意为"卖"。后文说"不要仅凭第一印象评判事物"，"judge"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：糖画艺人能用融化的糖创作出漂亮的动物___。A 项意为"玩具"；B 项意为"形状/造型"；C 项意为"笼子"；D 项意为"房子"。糖画做出的是动物造型，"shapes"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当地的音乐家和表演者每周末在那里___。A 项意为"饭菜"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"表演"；D 项意为"会议"。音乐家和表演者在舞台上进行表演，"shows"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人群随着___鼓掌。A 项意为"节拍"；B 项意为"歌曲"；C 项意为"演讲"；D 项意为"图片"。前文提到鼓舞表演，节奏强劲，人们随着鼓点节拍鼓掌，"beat"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我们家人放松、欢笑、享受___的地方。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"分开"；D 项意为"远离"。全家一起逛夜市，享受团聚时光，"together"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次，我可能会尝试更___的东西。A 项意为"危险的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"有冒险精神的"。从不敢吃臭豆腐到愿意尝试更大胆的食物，"adventurous"正确。故选 D。
