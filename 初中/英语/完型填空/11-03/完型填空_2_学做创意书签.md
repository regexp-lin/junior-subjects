# 中考英语完形填空：学做创意书签

## 完形填空原文

Reading is one of my favorite hobbies. I have many books, but I often lose my place because I don't have enough bookmarks. So last weekend, I decided to ___1___ my own creative bookmarks.

I found some colored cardboard, scissors, markers, and stickers at home. I also watched a short ___2___ online to get some ideas. The video showed many beautiful bookmarks in different shapes.

First, I cut a piece of yellow cardboard into a long rectangle. Then I drew a smiling sun at the top. I used orange and red markers to color it. It looked very ___3___. I wrote my favorite quote on the bookmark: "Every day is a new beginning."

For the second bookmark, I wanted to try something ___4___. I cut the cardboard into the shape of a cat. I drew two big eyes and a small nose. Then I added some whiskers with a thin black pen. This one was my favorite because it was so ___5___.

I made five bookmarks in total. Each one had a different ___6___ and a different color. I gave two of them to my best friend, and she was very surprised. She said they were even more beautiful than the ones in the ___7___.

My mother saw my bookmarks and ___8___ me. She said that making things with our own hands is a meaningful activity. It not only helps us be creative but also gives us a sense of ___9___.

Now I use my handmade bookmarks every day. They make reading even more ___10___. I plan to make more for my classmates as birthday gifts.

## 选项

1. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. borrow &emsp;&emsp; C. make &emsp;&emsp; D. find
2. A. movie &emsp;&emsp; B. video &emsp;&emsp; C. song &emsp;&emsp; D. story
3. A. dark &emsp;&emsp; B. cold &emsp;&emsp; C. bright &emsp;&emsp; D. heavy
4. A. easier &emsp;&emsp; B. cheaper &emsp;&emsp; C. smaller &emsp;&emsp; D. different
5. A. cute &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. common
6. A. price &emsp;&emsp; B. weight &emsp;&emsp; C. shape &emsp;&emsp; D. smell
7. A. school &emsp;&emsp; B. store &emsp;&emsp; C. library &emsp;&emsp; D. kitchen
8. A. stopped &emsp;&emsp; B. praised &emsp;&emsp; C. punished &emsp;&emsp; D. doubted
9. A. humor &emsp;&emsp; B. duty &emsp;&emsp; C. danger &emsp;&emsp; D. achievement
10. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoyable &emsp;&emsp; C. tiring &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定自己做创意书签。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"借"；C 项意为"做"；D 项意为"找"。make my own 意为"自己做"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在网上看了一段短视频来获取灵感。A 项意为"电影"；B 项意为"视频"；C 项意为"歌曲"；D 项意为"故事"。网上看教程用 video。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用橙色和红色涂色后看起来很鲜艳。A 项意为"暗的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"鲜艳的/明亮的"；D 项意为"重的"。暖色调看起来 bright。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二个书签我想尝试不一样的。A 项意为"更容易的"；B 项意为"更便宜的"；C 项意为"更小的"；D 项意为"不同的"。换一种风格所以 different。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个是我最喜欢的因为它太可爱了。A 项意为"可爱的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"普通的"。猫咪造型 cute。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个书签都有不同的形状和颜色。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"重量"；C 项意为"形状"；D 项意为"气味"。不同造型对应 shape。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说比商店里卖的还漂亮。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"商店"；C 项意为"图书馆"；D 项意为"厨房"。对比手工和 store 里买的。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈看到后表扬了我。A 项意为"阻止"；B 项意为"表扬"；C 项意为"惩罚"；D 项意为"怀疑"。妈妈认可后 praised。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：动手做东西不仅让我们有创意，还带来成就感。A 项意为"幽默"；B 项意为"责任"；C 项意为"危险"；D 项意为"成就"。a sense of achievement 意为"成就感"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们让阅读更加愉快。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"令人愉快的"；C 项意为"累人的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。漂亮书签让阅读 more enjoyable。故选 B。
