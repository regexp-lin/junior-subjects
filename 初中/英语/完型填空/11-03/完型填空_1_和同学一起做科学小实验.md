# 中考英语完形填空：和同学一起做科学小实验

## 完形填空原文

Last Wednesday, our science teacher asked us to do a simple experiment in groups. My group had four members. We needed to find out which materials could ___1___ electricity.

The teacher gave each group a battery, a small light bulb, and some wires. She also provided different ___2___: a metal spoon, a plastic ruler, a glass cup, a wooden stick, and an iron key. We had to connect each object to the circuit and see if the bulb would light up.

We started with the metal spoon. When we connected it, the bulb ___3___ right away. We were very excited. Then we tried the plastic ruler. Nothing happened. The bulb stayed ___4___. My classmate Li Wei wrote down the results in our notebook.

Next, we tested the glass cup and the wooden stick. ___5___ of them could make the bulb light up. Finally, we tried the iron key, and the bulb became bright again. We ___6___ that metals can conduct electricity, but plastic, glass, and wood cannot.

After the experiment, each group shared their ___7___ with the class. Our teacher was pleased and said we had done a wonderful job. She explained that understanding which materials conduct electricity is very ___8___ in daily life. It helps us stay safe around electrical things.

I really enjoyed this experiment. It was much more interesting than just ___9___ a textbook. I hope we can do more hands-on experiments like this. Learning by doing is the best ___10___ to understand science.

3. A. broke &emsp;&emsp; B. fell &emsp;&emsp; C. lit &emsp;&emsp; D. moved
4. A. bright &emsp;&emsp; B. dark &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. warm

6. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. doubted &emsp;&emsp; C. imagined &emsp;&emsp; D. discovered
7. A. results &emsp;&emsp; B. secrets &emsp;&emsp; C. wishes &emsp;&emsp; D. troubles
8. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. important &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
9. A. reading &emsp;&emsp; B. buying &emsp;&emsp; C. copying &emsp;&emsp; D. making

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们需要找出哪些材料能导电。A 项意为"产生"；B 项意为"传导"；C 项意为"节省"；D 项意为"浪费"。conduct electricity 意为"导电"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师还提供了不同的物品。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"物品"；C 项意为"机器"；D 项意为"食物"。勺子、尺子等是不同 objects。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：连接金属勺子后，灯泡立刻亮了。A 项意为"坏了"；B 项意为"掉了"；C 项意为"亮了"；D 项意为"动了"。lit up 意为"亮起来"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：什么也没发生，灯泡还是暗的。A 项意为"亮的"；B 项意为"暗的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"温暖的"。没通电所以 stayed dark。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两者都不能让灯泡亮起来。A 项意为"两者都"；B 项意为"全部"；C 项意为"两者都不"；D 项意为"两者中任一"。玻璃和木头都不导电，用 Neither。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们发现金属能导电。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"怀疑"；C 项意为"想象"；D 项意为"发现"。实验后 discovered 规律。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每组和全班分享实验结果。A 项意为"结果"；B 项意为"秘密"；C 项意为"愿望"；D 项意为"麻烦"。shared results 意为"分享结果"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：了解哪些材料导电在日常生活中非常重要。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"重要的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。关系到安全所以 important。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比单纯看课本有趣得多。A 项意为"阅读"；B 项意为"购买"；C 项意为"抄写"；D 项意为"制作"。对比动手和 reading a textbook。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：边做边学是理解科学最好的方式。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"方式"；C 项意为"地方"；D 项意为"原因"。the best way to 意为"最好的方式"。故选 B。
