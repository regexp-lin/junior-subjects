# 中考英语完形填空：给老师送贺卡

## 完形填空原文

Teacher's Day was coming. Our class decided to make greeting cards for our teachers. Each of us would write a message of thanks. I wanted to make my card special because my English teacher, Ms Wang, had helped me a lot over the past year.

I bought some coloured paper, glue, and a few stickers. I had never made a greeting card before, so I searched online for ideas. I found a simple design: fold the paper in half, cut out a heart shape on the front, and ___1___ a message inside. I decided to try it.

I spent an hour making the card. I drew some flowers and a sun on the cover. I wrote "Thank you, Ms Wang!" in large letters. Inside, I wrote a short ___2___ about how she had encouraged me when I failed an English test. She had said, "Mistakes help us learn. Don't give up." Those words had stayed with me and helped me improve. I also thanked her for making our lessons ___3___ with games and stories.

On Teacher's Day, we all handed our cards to our teachers before class. When I gave my card to Ms Wang, she smiled and said, "Thank you! May I open it now?" I nodded. She read it carefully, and I saw her eyes become a little ___4___. She said, "This means a lot to me. I'm so glad I could help you. Keep working hard!" I felt warm inside. A simple card could ___5___ so much emotion.

Later that day, I saw that Ms Wang had put our cards on her desk. She kept them there for the whole week. Every time I passed her office, I felt ___6___ to see my card among them. It reminded me that teachers care about their students and that our gratitude matters to them.

Making the card taught me something important. We should not wait for special occasions to express our thanks. A small act of ___7___ can make someone's day. I have decided to thank my parents and friends more often. Sometimes we forget to say "thank you" to the people who ___8___ us every day. Let us not take them for granted. A kind word or a small gift can ___9___ our appreciation and strengthen our relationships. Gratitude is a gift we can give to others, and it costs nothing but means ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在里面___一段话。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"写"；C 项意为"抄"；D 项意为"忘"。贺卡内页写祝福语，"write"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我写了一小段___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"笑话"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"游戏"。回忆老师鼓励的经历，"story"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感谢她让课堂通过游戏和故事变得___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"长的"。"interesting"表示有趣。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的眼睛变得有点___。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"湿润的"；C 项意为"明亮的"；D 项意为"冷的"。"eyes become wet"表示感动落泪。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一张简单贺卡能___如此多情感。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"传递/承载"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"浪费"。"carry"意为承载、传达。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到我的贺卡在其中我感到___。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。自己的心意被老师珍藏感到骄傲，"proud"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的___能让别人开心一天。A 项意为"善意"；B 项意为"粗鲁"；C 项意为"粗心"；D 项意为"懒惰"。"act of kindness"意为善举。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对那些每天___我们的人。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"讨厌"；D 项意为"离开"。应感谢帮助自己的人，"help"正确。故选 B。
