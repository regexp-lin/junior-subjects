# 中考英语完形填空：学做意大利面

## 完形填空原文

My mother loves Italian food. Last month, she suggested we learn to make spaghetti together. I had never cooked pasta before, so I was curious to ___1___.

We looked up a recipe online. It seemed simple: boil the pasta, make the sauce, and mix them together. We went to the supermarket and bought spaghetti, tomatoes, garlic, and some herbs. My mother said fresh ingredients were ___2___ for a good taste.

We started by boiling a large pot of water. When the water was bubbling, we added the spaghetti and a pinch of salt. The recipe said to cook it for about ten minutes. I kept checking the time, afraid of ___3___ it — too soft would be mushy, too hard would be difficult to chew. My mother taught me to throw a piece against the wall: if it sticks, it's ready. We laughed when I tried it and the pasta flew across the kitchen!

While the pasta was cooking, we made the tomato sauce. We chopped the tomatoes and garlic, heated some oil in a pan, and added them. The kitchen was soon filled with a wonderful ___4___. We added salt, pepper, and a little sugar. My mother said the sugar would balance the sourness of the tomatoes. I had never thought that cooking needed so much ___5___.

When the pasta was ready, we drained it and put it into the sauce. We mixed them gently and added some cheese on top. I could not wait to ___6___ it. We sat down at the table. The first bite was delicious! The pasta was just right — not too soft, not too hard. The sauce was rich and flavourful. My mother said, "Cooking together is more fun than cooking ___7___. And the food tastes better when we make it with love." I agreed completely.

That evening, I helped clear the table and wash the dishes. I realised that a good meal involved not just cooking but also cleaning up. Everyone in the family should ___8___ the work. My father said he would do the cooking next weekend and try a different dish. I look forward to learning more recipes. Cooking has become a new ___9___ for me. It is a practical skill that I will use for the rest of my life. And sharing a homemade meal with family is one of life's simple ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从没做过意面，所以好奇想___。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。"try"意为尝试。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新鲜食材对好口味很___。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"便宜的"；D 项意为"无用的"。"important"表示重要。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：担心___它。A 项意为"煮不够"；B 项意为"煮过头"；C 项意为"吃"；D 项意为"浪费"。后文说太软会糊、太硬难嚼，"overcooking"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房很快充满美妙的___。A 项意为"噪音"；B 项意为"香味"；C 项意为"冷"；D 项意为"黑暗"。做酱时飘香，"smell"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从没想过做饭需要这么多___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"学问/技巧"；D 项意为"运气"。加糖平衡酸味等是烹调知识，"knowledge"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：迫不及待想___。A 项意为"煮"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"品尝"；D 项意为"扔"。"taste"意为品尝。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一起做比___做更有趣。A 项意为"一起"；B 项意为"独自"；C 项意为"快速"；D 项意为"慢慢"。"alone"与"together"形成对比。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：家里每个人都应___这份活。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"分担"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"完成"。"share the work"意为分工。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做饭成了我的新___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"责任"。"hobby"表示爱好。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与家人共享家常饭是人生简单的___之一。A 项意为"烦恼"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"错误"。"joys"意为快乐、乐事。故选 B。
