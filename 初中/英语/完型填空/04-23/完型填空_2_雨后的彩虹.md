# 中考英语完形填空：雨后的彩虹

## 完形填空原文

I had failed the math exam. My parents were disappointed. My friends didn't say much. I felt like a loser. For several days, I stayed in my room and didn't want to talk to anyone. The world felt dark and ___1___.

One afternoon, a heavy rain started. I sat by the window and watched the drops hit the glass. The sky was gray. Everything looked sad, just like my mood.

The rain lasted for about an hour. Then it stopped. The clouds began to ___2___, and the sun came out. I looked up — and there it was. A rainbow stretched across the sky. Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet. So bright and so beautiful.

I ran outside. The air smelled fresh. The wet grass reflected the sunlight. I stood there, staring at the rainbow, and something inside me began to ___3___.

A rainbow appears only after rain. Without the storm, there would be no rainbow. Maybe my failure was like the rain. It was painful, but it could lead to something beautiful if I didn't ___4___.

I went back inside and took out my math book. I would try again. I would ask my teacher for help. I would practice more. The exam was over, but my ___5___ was not.

In the weeks that followed, I worked hard. I did extra exercises. I joined a study group. Slowly, my math improved. In the next exam, I got a much better ___6___. My parents were proud. I was proud too.

I often think about that rainbow. It taught me that hard times don't last forever. After every storm, there is a ___7___ of sunshine. We just need to wait, and to believe.

Now when I face difficulties, I remember that day. I tell myself: "This is my rain. My rainbow is coming." It helps me stay ___8___ and keep going.

Life is full of ups and downs. We cannot avoid the rain, but we can choose how we respond to it. We can hide in fear, or we can ___9___ the storm and look for the rainbow on the other side.

That rainbow in the sky was a gift. It didn't last long — maybe ten minutes. But the lesson it taught me will ___10___ forever.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：世界感觉阴暗而___。A 项意为"明亮的"；B 项意为"空洞的/虚无的"；C 项意为"充满希望的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。考试失败后心情低落，感觉世界"空洞"（empty）、没有希望。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：云开始___，太阳出来了。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"落下"；C 项意为"升起"；D 项意为"移动"。雨停后云散开、太阳出现，云逐渐"消失"（disappear）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我内心有什么东西开始___。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"破碎"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"受伤"。看到彩虹后心情好转，内心开始"改变"（change）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我不___，它可能带来美好的东西。A 项意为"尝试"；B 项意为"放弃"；C 项意为"等待"；D 项意为"担心"。与"lead to something beautiful"呼应，不"放弃"（give up）才能迎来转机。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：考试结束了，但我的___没有。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"旅程"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"书"。学习是一段持续的过程，"journey"指学习与成长的历程。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下一次考试，我获得了更好的___。A 项意为"成绩"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"机会"。考试相关用"成绩"（grade）。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每场风暴过后，都有一___阳光。A 项意为"滴"；B 项意为"一点"；C 项意为"缕/道"；D 项意为"片"。"a ray of sunshine"是固定表达，意为"一缕阳光/一丝希望"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它帮我保持___并继续前进。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"积极的"。面对困难时保持"积极"（positive）心态。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们可以恐惧地躲藏，也可以___风暴并寻找另一边的彩虹。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"害怕"；C 项意为"面对"；D 项意为"忘记"。与"hide in fear"对比，选择"面对"（face）困难。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但它教给我的道理将___永远。A 项意为"持续"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"停留"；D 项意为"去"。"last forever"意为"永远持续/永存"。故选 A。
