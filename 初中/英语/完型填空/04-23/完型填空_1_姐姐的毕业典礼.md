# 中考英语完形填空：姐姐的毕业典礼

## 完形填空原文

My elder sister graduated from high school last June. The whole family went to attend her graduation ceremony. It was a hot day, but the stadium was full of proud parents and excited students.

I sat in the crowd and watched. When my sister's name was called, she walked onto the stage. She received her diploma and shook the principal's hand. Her face was full of ___1___. I had never seen her look so happy.

I thought about all the years we had spent together. When I was little, she used to help me with my homework. When I was sad, she would ___2___ me. She had always been there for me. Now she was leaving for university in another city. I would ___3___ her very much.

During the ceremony, the headmaster gave a speech. He said, "Graduation is not an end. It is the beginning of a new ___4___. I hope you will always remember where you came from and the people who supported you."

His words made me think. My sister had worked hard for twelve years. She had stayed up late before exams. She had given up weekends to study. All her ___5___ had paid off.

After the ceremony, we took photos. My sister put her arm around my shoulder and said, "Your turn will come soon. Study hard, and don't give up when things get ___6___."

I nodded. "I will. And I'll come to your university to visit you."

She smiled. "I'd like that. We'll always be ___7___, no matter how far apart we are."

That day, I learned something important. Growing up means that people we love sometimes leave. But the love and memories we share never ___8___. We carry them with us wherever we go.

My sister left for university in September. I video-call her every weekend. She tells me about her new life, and I tell her about school. Distance has not changed our ___9___. We are still close.

Graduation is a time to celebrate, but it is also a time to say thank you. I am grateful for my sister — for her love, her patience, and the ___10___ she has given me. I will work hard so that one day I can make her proud too.

## 选项

1. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. anger &emsp;&emsp; C. joy &emsp;&emsp; D. worry
2. A. punish &emsp;&emsp; B. encourage &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. blame
3. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. need &emsp;&emsp; C. help &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
4. A. life &emsp;&emsp; B. job &emsp;&emsp; C. school &emsp;&emsp; D. journey
5. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. effort &emsp;&emsp; D. luck
6. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. fast
7. A. friends &emsp;&emsp; B. classmates &emsp;&emsp; C. family &emsp;&emsp; D. neighbors
8. A. appear &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. disappear &emsp;&emsp; D. improve
9. A. habit &emsp;&emsp; B. plan &emsp;&emsp; C. friendship &emsp;&emsp; D. hobby
10. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. example &emsp;&emsp; D. chance

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她脸上洋溢着___。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"喜悦"；D 项意为"担忧"。毕业典礼上拿到毕业证，自然是充满"喜悦"（joy）。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我难过时，她会___我。A 项意为"惩罚"；B 项意为"鼓励"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"责备"。姐姐关心弟弟，难过时会"鼓励"（encourage）他。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会非常___她。A 项意为"想念"；B 项意为"需要"；C 项意为"帮助"；D 项意为"跟随"。姐姐要去外地读大学，作者会"想念"（miss）她。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是新的___的开始。A 项意为"生活"；B 项意为"工作"；C 项意为"学校"；D 项意为"旅程"。"the beginning of a new journey"是常用表达，比喻人生新阶段。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她所有的___都有了回报。A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"努力"；D 项意为"运气"。前文描述熬夜、牺牲周末学习，"努力"（effort）与"paid off"搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当事情变得___时不要放弃。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"快的"。姐姐劝弟弟在困难时坚持，"hard"与"don't give up"呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无论相隔多远，我们永远是___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"同学"；C 项意为"家人"；D 项意为"邻居"。姐弟关系，强调"家人"（family）纽带。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我们分享的爱与回忆永远不会___。A 项意为"出现"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"消失"；D 项意为"改善"。亲情与回忆会一直留存，不会"消失"（disappear）。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：距离没有改变我们的___。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"情谊/友谊"；D 项意为"爱好"。姐弟虽分隔两地，但感情不变，"friendship"可泛指亲密关系。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感激她的爱、耐心以及她给我的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"课/教训"；C 项意为"榜样/示范"；D 项意为"机会"。姐姐的言传身教是"榜样"（example）。故选 C。
