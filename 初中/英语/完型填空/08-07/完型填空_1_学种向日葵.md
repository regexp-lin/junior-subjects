# 中考英语完形填空：学种向日葵

## 完形填空原文

This spring, our school started a gardening project. Each class was given a small plot of land behind the teaching building. Our class decided to grow sunflowers because they are bright, cheerful, and ___1___ to take care of.

Our biology teacher, Ms. Huang, taught us the basics. She said sunflowers need plenty of sunlight and water, but the soil should not be too ___2___ or the roots will rot. She gave each group a bag of seeds and showed us how to plant them about two centimetres deep.

I was in charge of watering our plot every morning. At first, nothing seemed to happen. I stared at the bare soil and wondered if the seeds were even alive. After about a week, tiny green shoots ___3___ through the surface. I was so excited that I called my classmates to come and see.

Over the following weeks, the shoots grew taller and stronger. We added some fertiliser and pulled out the weeds that competed with the sunflowers for ___4___. It was hard work, especially under the hot sun, but nobody complained. We all wanted our sunflowers to be the tallest in the school.

One morning, I noticed that some leaves had small holes in them. Ms. Huang examined them and said insects were eating the leaves. She taught us to make a natural spray using garlic and water instead of chemical pesticides. "We should protect the ___5___ while growing our plants," she said. I agreed completely.

By June, our sunflowers had grown over a metre tall. Then, one by one, big golden flowers began to ___6___. They were stunning. Each flower head was as large as a dinner plate, and they all faced the sun. Our classmates from other classes came to take photos with them.

The gardening project taught me more than just how to grow plants. It taught me ___7___. You cannot rush a seed to grow. You have to water it, care for it, and wait. Life is the same way — good things take time.

When summer came, the flowers produced seeds. We ___8___ them and saved some for next year's planting. We also roasted some and shared them as snacks. They tasted wonderful — probably because we had grown them ___9___.

I never thought I would enjoy gardening so much. It connected me with nature and gave me a sense of ___10___. I look forward to planting something new next spring.

## 选项

1. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. expensive &emsp;&emsp; C. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; D. easy
2. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. wet &emsp;&emsp; C. deep &emsp;&emsp; D. dark
3. A. pushed &emsp;&emsp; B. fell &emsp;&emsp; C. flew &emsp;&emsp; D. rolled
4. A. space &emsp;&emsp; B. nutrients &emsp;&emsp; C. shade &emsp;&emsp; D. beauty
5. A. building &emsp;&emsp; B. classroom &emsp;&emsp; C. environment &emsp;&emsp; D. playground
6. A. die &emsp;&emsp; B. close &emsp;&emsp; C. bloom &emsp;&emsp; D. break
7. A. courage &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. speed &emsp;&emsp; D. strength
8. A. ate &emsp;&emsp; B. harvested &emsp;&emsp; C. threw &emsp;&emsp; D. painted
9. A. ourselves &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. cheaply &emsp;&emsp; D. secretly
10. A. humour &emsp;&emsp; B. guilt &emsp;&emsp; C. purpose &emsp;&emsp; D. loss

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：向日葵明亮、欢快，而且___照料。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"容易的"。向日葵好种好养，"easy"正确。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：土壤不应该太___，否则根会腐烂。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"湿的"；C 项意为"深的"；D 项意为"暗的"。土壤太湿根会烂，"wet"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小绿芽___过土壤表面。A 项意为"推/钻"；B 项意为"掉落"；C 项意为"飞"；D 项意为"滚"。嫩芽从土里钻出来，"pushed through"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们拔掉与向日葵争夺___的杂草。A 项意为"空间"；B 项意为"养分"；C 项意为"阴凉"；D 项意为"美丽"。杂草和植物争夺土壤中的养分，"nutrients"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：种植物的同时也要保护___。A 项意为"建筑"；B 项意为"教室"；C 项意为"环境"；D 项意为"操场"。用天然喷雾代替化学农药是保护环境，"environment"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大大的金色花朵开始___。A 项意为"死"；B 项意为"关闭"；C 项意为"开放/绽放"；D 项意为"打破"。花朵盛开，"bloom"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会了我___。A 项意为"勇气"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"速度"；D 项意为"力量"。后文说"不能催促种子生长，好事需要时间"，这是耐心，"patience"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们___了种子，留了一些给明年种。A 项意为"吃了"；B 项意为"收获"；C 项意为"扔了"；D 项意为"画了"。花结籽后进行收获，"harvested"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们味道极好——可能因为是我们___种的。A 项意为"自己"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"便宜地"；D 项意为"秘密地"。自己亲手种的吃起来格外香，"ourselves"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它给了我一种___感。A 项意为"幽默"；B 项意为"内疚"；C 项意为"目标/意义"；D 项意为"失去"。"a sense of purpose"意为"一种使命感/意义感"。故选 C。
