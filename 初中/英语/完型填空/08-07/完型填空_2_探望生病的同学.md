# 中考英语完形填空：探望生病的同学

## 完形填空原文

My classmate Zhang Hao had been absent from school for a whole week. Our teacher told us that he had broken his leg in a bicycle accident and was resting at home. Everyone in the class felt ___1___ for him, so we decided to visit him on Saturday.

We collected some money and bought a basket of fruit, a box of chocolates, and a get-well card. Each of us wrote a short ___2___ on the card. I wrote, "Get well soon! We miss you in class." Li Mei drew a funny cartoon of our teacher on the card, which made everyone laugh.

On Saturday morning, five of us took the bus to Zhang Hao's home. His mother opened the door and looked ___3___ to see us. "Zhang Hao will be so happy," she said. "He has been feeling down lately."

We found Zhang Hao sitting on the sofa with his leg in a cast. He looked pale but smiled brightly when he saw us. "I thought you guys had forgotten about me!" he joked. We sat around him and started talking about everything that had happened at school during the ___4___.

We told him about the new English teacher, the basketball match between our class and Class Three, and the funny thing that happened in the chemistry lab. Zhang Hao listened carefully and laughed at all the right moments. I could see that our visit was already making him feel ___5___.

Then we gave him the gifts. His eyes lit up when he read the card. He was especially amused by Li Mei's cartoon. "This really looks like Mr. Wang!" he said, holding up the card for his mother to see. She laughed too and thanked us for being such good ___6___.

Before we left, Zhang Hao asked about the homework. We promised to bring him copies of the notes and help him ___7___ up when he returned. "You don't have to worry about falling behind," I said. "We will make sure you are ready."

On the way home, I thought about how a simple visit could mean so much to someone. Zhang Hao had been feeling lonely and bored at home. Our visit gave him something he really needed — the feeling that people ___8___ about him.

The next Monday, our teacher praised us for visiting Zhang Hao. She said, "Taking care of each other is what makes a class a ___9___." I agreed. After all, friendship is not just about having fun together. It is about being there for each other, especially during ___10___ times.

3. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. disappointed &emsp;&emsp; C. frightened &emsp;&emsp; D. bored

7. A. give &emsp;&emsp; B. catch &emsp;&emsp; C. grow &emsp;&emsp; D. wake
8. A. argued &emsp;&emsp; B. cared &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. wondered
9. A. family &emsp;&emsp; B. team &emsp;&emsp; C. company &emsp;&emsp; D. club

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：班里每个人都为他感到___。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"难过的/同情的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。"feel sorry for"意为"为……感到难过/同情"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们每个人在卡片上写了一条短___。A 项意为"文章"；B 项意为"留言"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"诗"。在慰问卡上写短留言，"message"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他妈妈开门后看到我们很___。A 项意为"惊喜的"；B 项意为"失望的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。没有提前通知的探望让她惊喜，"surprised"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们聊起了这___学校里发生的一切。A 项意为"假期"；B 项意为"周末"；C 项意为"周/星期"；D 项意为"年"。张浩缺席一周，我们讲这一周的事，"week"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的探望已经在让他感觉___了。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"困倦的"；D 项意为"紧张的"。同学的陪伴让他心情好转，"better"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她感谢我们做了这么好的___。A 项意为"学生"；B 项意为"邻居"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"孩子"。探望同学体现了好朋友的关心，"friends"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他___上进度。A 项意为"给"；B 项意为"赶上"；C 项意为"成长"；D 项意为"醒来"。"catch up"意为"赶上"，帮他补习落下的功课。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的探望给了他一种人们___他的感觉。A 项意为"争吵"；B 项意为"关心"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"想知道"。"care about"意为"关心"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：互相关心是让一个班级成为一个___的原因。A 项意为"家庭"；B 项意为"团队"；C 项意为"公司"；D 项意为"俱乐部"。班级像家一样互相关爱，"family"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：友谊是在彼此身边，尤其是在___的时刻。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"忙碌的"；C 项意为"艰难的"；D 项意为"空闲的"。困难时刻更显友谊珍贵，"tough"正确。故选 C。
