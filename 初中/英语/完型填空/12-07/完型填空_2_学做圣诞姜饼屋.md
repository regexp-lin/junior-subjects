# 中考英语完形填空：学做圣诞姜饼屋

## 完形填空原文

December is a time for festive activities. Our English teacher organized a cultural experience class and taught us how to make a gingerbread house. She said it is a ___1___ tradition in Western countries during the Christmas season.

First, she gave each group a set of pre-baked gingerbread pieces. There were walls, a roof, a door, and a chimney piece. She also prepared bowls of white icing, which would be used as the ___2___ to hold the pieces together. Each group received a bag of colorful candies for decoration.

My group started by building the walls. We spread icing along the ___3___ of each piece and pressed them together. We had to hold them still for a minute while the icing dried. This required ___4___ because if we let go too early, the walls would fall apart.

Next, we carefully placed the roof pieces on top. This was the trickiest part. The roof kept ___5___ off to one side. After several attempts, we finally got it to stay in place. We felt so relieved.

Then came the fun part—decoration. We used icing to draw windows and a door on the front wall. We pressed colorful candies onto the roof to look like ___6___. Someone in my group made a tiny snowman out of icing and placed it beside the front door. It looked adorable.

When all the groups finished, we put our gingerbread houses on a long table for ___7___. Each house was unique. Some were simple and neat, while others were covered in candies from top to bottom. Our teacher took photos and said she would share them with the whole school.

The best part was that we could ___8___ our gingerbread houses after the display. My group decided to share ours with other classmates. Everyone agreed that the gingerbread tasted ___9___ with the sweet icing and candies.

Making a gingerbread house was not just about food. It was about creativity, teamwork, and learning about another ___10___. I hope our school will have more activities like this.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在西方国家圣诞季这是一个流行的传统。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"流行的/受欢迎的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。a popular tradition。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：白色糖霜用作粘合剂把各部分粘在一起。A 项意为"油漆"；B 项意为"胶水"；C 项意为"水"；D 项意为"胶带"。icing 用作 glue 来粘合。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们沿着每块姜饼的边缘涂上糖霜。A 项意为"顶部"；B 项意为"中间"；C 项意为"边缘"；D 项意为"后面"。along the edges 意为"沿着边缘"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这需要耐心，因为太早松手墙会倒。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"运气"；C 项意为"耐心"；D 项意为"愤怒"。等待糖霜干需要 patience。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：屋顶老是滑到一边。A 项意为"滑动"；B 项意为"飞"；C 项意为"生长"；D 项意为"上升"。sliding off 意为"滑落"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把彩色糖果按在屋顶上看起来像瓦片。A 项意为"星星"；B 项意为"瓦片"；C 项意为"叶子"；D 项意为"云"。屋顶上的糖果像 tiles。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把姜饼屋放在长桌上展示。A 项意为"出售"；B 项意为"展示"；C 项意为"维修"；D 项意为"储存"。for display 意为"供展示"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：展示后我们可以吃掉姜饼屋。A 项意为"扔"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"吃"；D 项意为"藏"。姜饼屋是食物，可以 eat。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都说配上糖霜和糖果味道很棒。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"更差"；C 项意为"酸的"；D 项意为"棒的"。tasted great 意为"味道很好"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是关于创造力、团队合作和了解另一种文化。A 项意为"语言"；B 项意为"文化"；C 项意为"运动"；D 项意为"科目"。了解西方圣诞传统是 another culture。故选 B。
