# 中考英语完形填空：周末和爸爸去观鸟

## 完形填空原文

My father is a nature lover. Last Saturday morning, he woke me up early and said, "Let's go birdwatching at the lake!" I was still sleepy, but his excitement made me ___1___ to go.

We drove to a lake about thirty minutes from our home. It was a cold December morning, and the air was fresh. Father brought a pair of binoculars and a bird guidebook. He told me that winter is a great ___2___ for birdwatching because many migratory birds come to southern China to escape the cold weather in the north.

When we arrived at the lake, everything was quiet. Father whispered that we had to be ___3___ so we would not scare the birds away. We found a good spot behind some tall reeds and sat down on a small folding chair.

Soon, I saw a group of white birds flying across the water. Father looked through the binoculars and said ___4___, "Those are egrets! Look at how gracefully they fly." I took the binoculars and saw their long necks and beautiful white feathers. They were truly ___5___.

Over the next two hours, we spotted more than ten different kinds of birds. Father helped me identify each one using the guidebook. I learned to ___6___ a kingfisher by its bright blue color and a sparrow by its small round body. My favorite was a mandarin duck with colorful feathers that looked like a ___7___.

Father took many photos with his camera. He said he would print the best ones and help me make a bird observation ___8___. I thought that was a great idea.

On the way home, Father said, "Nature is the best classroom. It teaches us to observe, to be patient, and to ___9___ the beauty of life." I agreed. This birdwatching trip opened my eyes to the wonderful world of birds. I hope we can do this more ___10___ in the future.

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. unwilling &emsp;&emsp; C. eager &emsp;&emsp; D. sad
2. A. problem &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. subject &emsp;&emsp; D. place
3. A. loud &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. messy
4. A. angrily &emsp;&emsp; B. sadly &emsp;&emsp; C. excitedly &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
5. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. scary &emsp;&emsp; C. tiny &emsp;&emsp; D. stunning
6. A. recognize &emsp;&emsp; B. catch &emsp;&emsp; C. feed &emsp;&emsp; D. train
7. A. painting &emsp;&emsp; B. stone &emsp;&emsp; C. building &emsp;&emsp; D. cloud
8. A. diary &emsp;&emsp; B. test &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. race
9. A. destroy &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. appreciate &emsp;&emsp; D. waste
10. A. carefully &emsp;&emsp; B. often &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸的热情让我也渴望去。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"不情愿的"；C 项意为"渴望的"；D 项意为"伤心的"。被感染后 eager to go。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冬天是观鸟的好时机。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"时机"；C 项意为"科目"；D 项意为"地方"。a great time for birdwatching。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须安静才不会吓走鸟。A 项意为"大声的"；B 项意为"安静的"；C 项意为"快速的"；D 项意为"凌乱的"。观鸟需要 be quiet。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸兴奋地说是白鹭。A 项意为"生气地"；B 项意为"伤心地"；C 项意为"兴奋地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。发现鸟类用 excitedly。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们真的很惊艳。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"可怕的"；C 项意为"微小的"；D 项意为"惊艳的"。白鹭的优美姿态 truly stunning。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了通过颜色辨认翠鸟。A 项意为"辨认"；B 项意为"抓"；C 项意为"喂"；D 项意为"训练"。recognize a bird 意为"辨认鸟类"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：鸳鸯的彩色羽毛看起来像一幅画。A 项意为"画"；B 项意为"石头"；C 项意为"建筑"；D 项意为"云"。looked like a painting 形容色彩美丽。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮我做一本观鸟日记。A 项意为"日记"；B 项意为"测试"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"比赛"。bird observation diary 意为"观鸟日记"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大自然教我们观察、耐心和欣赏生命之美。A 项意为"破坏"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"欣赏"；D 项意为"浪费"。appreciate the beauty of life。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望以后能更经常这样做。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"经常地"；C 项意为"缓慢地"；D 项意为"大声地"。do this more often。故选 B。
