# 中考英语完形填空：学做纸风车

## 完形填空原文

When I was little, my grandfather often made paper pinwheels for me. I loved watching them spin in the ___1___. This weekend, I decided to learn how to make one myself.

I asked my grandfather to teach me. He was very ___2___ and took out some colored paper, a pair of scissors, a pin, and a thin stick. He said these were all the things we needed.

First, he cut a square piece of paper. Then he drew two lines from corner to corner, making an "X" in the ___3___. He cut along each line but stopped about two centimeters from the center. I watched every step ___4___.

Next, he folded every other corner to the center point. He told me to hold them ___5___ while he pushed a pin through all the layers and the center. Then he attached the pin to the top of the stick. When he blew on it, the pinwheel started spinning beautifully!

Now it was my ___6___. I chose a piece of blue paper with white clouds on it. I followed the same steps, but when I folded the corners, one of them ___7___. Grandfather helped me fix it with a small piece of tape. Finally, my pinwheel was complete.

I ran outside and held it up. The autumn wind made it spin very ___8___. I felt like I was a child again, running around the yard with my new toy.

Grandfather sat on the bench and watched me with a warm ___9___. He said, "Simple things can bring the greatest joy." I agreed. Making this pinwheel was not just a craft activity—it was a special ___10___ with my grandfather.

## 选项

1. A. rain &emsp;&emsp; B. wind &emsp;&emsp; C. snow &emsp;&emsp; D. sun
2. A. busy &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. glad &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
3. A. middle &emsp;&emsp; B. corner &emsp;&emsp; C. side &emsp;&emsp; D. back
4. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. closely &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
5. A. loosely &emsp;&emsp; B. tightly &emsp;&emsp; C. gently &emsp;&emsp; D. softly
6. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. idea &emsp;&emsp; D. wish
7. A. burned &emsp;&emsp; B. disappeared &emsp;&emsp; C. tore &emsp;&emsp; D. grew
8. A. heavily &emsp;&emsp; B. sadly &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. fast
9. A. voice &emsp;&emsp; B. smile &emsp;&emsp; C. touch &emsp;&emsp; D. word
10. A. memory &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. secret

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我喜欢看风车在风中旋转。A 项意为"雨"；B 项意为"风"；C 项意为"雪"；D 项意为"太阳"。风车靠 wind 转动。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷很高兴，拿出了一些彩纸。A 项意为"忙的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"生气的"。孙子要学手工爷爷 glad。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他从对角到对角画了两条线，在中间形成了"X"。A 项意为"中间"；B 项意为"角落"；C 项意为"边"；D 项意为"后面"。对角线交叉在 middle。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我仔细地看着每一个步骤。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"仔细地/近距离地"；D 项意为"大声地"。watched closely 意为"仔细观察"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他让我紧紧按住角。A 项意为"松地"；B 项意为"紧地"；C 项意为"轻柔地"；D 项意为"柔软地"。固定角需要 hold tightly。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：现在轮到我了。A 项意为"轮次"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"愿望"。my turn 意为"轮到我"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：折角时其中一个撕裂了。A 项意为"燃烧了"；B 项意为"消失了"；C 项意为"撕裂了"；D 项意为"长大了"。纸被弄坏用 tore。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：秋风让风车转得很快。A 项意为"重重地"；B 项意为"伤心地"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"快"。秋风强劲风车 spin fast。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷坐在长椅上带着温暖的微笑看我。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"微笑"；C 项意为"触碰"；D 项意为"话语"。warm smile 意为"温暖的微笑"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做风车不只是手工活动，更是和爷爷的一段特别回忆。A 项意为"回忆"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"教训"；D 项意为"秘密"。a special memory 意为"一段特别的回忆"。故选 A。
