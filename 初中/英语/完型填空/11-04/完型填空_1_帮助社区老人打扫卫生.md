# 中考英语完形填空：帮助社区老人打扫卫生

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, our class organized a volunteer activity. We went to a community center to help some elderly people ___1___ their rooms. I was happy to be part of this meaningful event.

When we arrived, an old lady named Grandma Wang greeted us warmly. She lived ___2___ because her children worked in another city. Her room was not very messy, but she couldn't reach the high places to clean them.

I helped Grandma Wang wipe the windows. My friend Li Ming swept the ___3___ while another classmate organized the books on the shelf. We worked together and finished everything in about one hour. The room looked much ___4___ than before.

After cleaning, Grandma Wang made us some tea and told us stories about her ___5___. She used to be a teacher at a primary school. She taught there for over thirty years. Her stories were very interesting, and we listened ___6___.

Before we left, Grandma Wang held my hand and said, "Thank you so much. Your visit makes me feel less ___7___." I was deeply moved by her words. I realized that sometimes older people don't just need a clean room—they also need ___8___.

On our way back to school, we talked about what we had experienced. Everyone agreed that helping others brings us true ___9___. I decided to visit the community center more often and spend time with the elderly people there.

Volunteering taught me an important ___10___: giving is even better than receiving.

## 选项

1. A. paint &emsp;&emsp; B. build &emsp;&emsp; C. clean &emsp;&emsp; D. lock
2. A. happily &emsp;&emsp; B. alone &emsp;&emsp; C. freely &emsp;&emsp; D. safely
3. A. floor &emsp;&emsp; B. desk &emsp;&emsp; C. wall &emsp;&emsp; D. roof
4. A. darker &emsp;&emsp; B. smaller &emsp;&emsp; C. dirtier &emsp;&emsp; D. brighter
5. A. dream &emsp;&emsp; B. past &emsp;&emsp; C. plan &emsp;&emsp; D. hobby
6. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. nervously &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
7. A. lonely &emsp;&emsp; B. hungry &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
8. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. company &emsp;&emsp; D. medicine
9. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. happiness &emsp;&emsp; C. surprise &emsp;&emsp; D. knowledge
10. A. lesson &emsp;&emsp; B. subject &emsp;&emsp; C. skill &emsp;&emsp; D. language

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们去社区帮老人打扫房间。A 项意为"粉刷"；B 项意为"建造"；C 项意为"打扫"；D 项意为"锁"。clean their rooms 意为"打扫房间"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她独自一人住，因为孩子在另一个城市工作。A 项意为"开心地"；B 项意为"独自"；C 项意为"自由地"；D 项意为"安全地"。子女不在身边所以 lived alone。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：李明在扫地。A 项意为"地板"；B 项意为"桌子"；C 项意为"墙壁"；D 项意为"屋顶"。sweep the floor 意为"扫地"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：房间看起来比之前明亮多了。A 项意为"更暗"；B 项意为"更小"；C 项意为"更脏"；D 项意为"更明亮"。打扫后 brighter。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王奶奶给我们讲了她过去的故事。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"过去"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"爱好"。讲以前的经历是 her past。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的故事很有趣，我们认真地听。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"紧张地"；C 项意为"认真地"；D 项意为"伤心地"。故事有趣所以 listened carefully。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们的来访让我感觉不那么孤独了。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"饥饿的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"生气的"。独居老人感到 less lonely。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老人不只需要干净的房间，还需要陪伴。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"陪伴"；D 项意为"药"。company 意为"陪伴"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助别人带给我们真正的幸福。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"幸福"；C 项意为"惊喜"；D 项意为"知识"。true happiness 意为"真正的幸福"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：志愿服务教会了我一个重要的道理。A 项意为"教训/道理"；B 项意为"科目"；C 项意为"技能"；D 项意为"语言"。taught me a lesson 意为"教会我一个道理"。故选 A。
