# 中考英语完形填空：秋天的校园

## 完形填空原文

Autumn is my favourite season, and our school looks especially beautiful during this time. The trees along the path turn from green to gold and red, creating a ___1___ picture. Every morning when I walk through the school gate, I feel as if I am entering a painting.

The air in autumn is fresh and cool. It is neither too hot nor too cold — just ___2___ for outdoor activities. Our PE teacher takes advantage of this weather and often holds classes on the playground instead of in the gym. We run, play football and do stretching exercises under the clear blue sky. Everyone ___3___ these classes because we can breathe fresh air while exercising.

One of the most beautiful spots on campus is the small garden behind the library. In autumn, the chrysanthemums bloom in various colours — yellow, white, purple and pink. The garden also has a few maple trees whose leaves turn bright ___4___. Many students like to sit there during breaks, reading or simply enjoying the scenery. It is a peaceful place that helps us ___5___ from the stress of studying.

Our school also organises an autumn harvest festival every October. Each class grows vegetables in a small plot of land behind the canteen. By autumn, the vegetables are ready to be ___6___. Last year, our class grew tomatoes and peppers. We were so proud when we cooked a big pot of tomato soup using our own tomatoes. The soup tasted extra ___7___ because we had grown the ingredients ourselves.

Autumn also brings some challenges. The weather can change quickly, and students sometimes catch ___8___ if they do not dress warmly enough. Our teacher always reminds us to bring a jacket and to drink plenty of water. She says staying healthy is just as important as studying hard.

As the days get shorter, the sunsets become more dramatic. Sometimes after school, I sit on a bench and watch the sky change from orange to purple. These quiet moments make me feel ___9___ and thankful. I think autumn teaches us an important lesson: change can be beautiful. The leaves fall, but they will grow back in spring. Nothing is ___10___ — there is always a new beginning.

## 选项

1. A. dark &emsp;&emsp; B. colourful &emsp;&emsp; C. pale &emsp;&emsp; D. plain
2. A. dangerous &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. perfect &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
3. A. cancels &emsp;&emsp; B. avoids &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoys &emsp;&emsp; D. misses
4. A. green &emsp;&emsp; B. blue &emsp;&emsp; C. black &emsp;&emsp; D. red
5. A. escape &emsp;&emsp; B. suffer &emsp;&emsp; C. learn &emsp;&emsp; D. benefit
6. A. planted &emsp;&emsp; B. harvested &emsp;&emsp; C. watered &emsp;&emsp; D. wasted
7. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. sour &emsp;&emsp; C. bitter &emsp;&emsp; D. delicious
8. A. colds &emsp;&emsp; B. buses &emsp;&emsp; C. fish &emsp;&emsp; D. trains
9. A. angry &emsp;&emsp; B. calm &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
10. A. permanent &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. important &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：树叶从绿变成金色和红色，形成了一幅___的画面。A 项意为"黑暗的"；B 项意为"色彩缤纷的"；C 项意为"苍白的"；D 项意为"朴素的"。多种颜色变化构成缤纷画面，"colourful"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不冷不热，___进行户外活动。A 项意为"危险的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"完美的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。适宜的天气最适合户外，"perfect"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都___这些课。A 项意为"取消"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"喜欢"；D 项意为"错过"。在户外呼吸新鲜空气做运动，大家喜欢，"enjoys"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（常识）
**【解析】** 本句意为：枫树叶子变成明亮的___色。A 项意为"绿"；B 项意为"蓝"；C 项意为"黑"；D 项意为"红"。秋天枫叶变红是常识，"red"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助我们从学习压力中___。A 项意为"逃离/暂时摆脱"；B 项意为"遭受"；C 项意为"学习"；D 项意为"受益"。"escape from stress"意为"从压力中解脱"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到了秋天，蔬菜可以被___了。A 项意为"种植"；B 项意为"收获"；C 项意为"浇水"；D 项意为"浪费"。秋天是收获的季节，"harvested"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：汤的味道格外___。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"酸的"；C 项意为"苦的"；D 项意为"美味的"。自己种的食材做的汤特别好吃，"delicious"正确。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生有时会___如果穿得不够暖。A 项意为"感冒"；B 项意为"公交车"；C 项意为"鱼"；D 项意为"火车"。"catch colds"意为"感冒"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些安静的时刻让我感到___和感恩。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"平静的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。安静地看日落感到平静，"calm"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有什么是___的——总有新的开始。A 项意为"永恒的/永久的"；B 项意为"美丽的"；C 项意为"重要的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。落叶春天会再长，说明没有什么是永恒不变的，"permanent"正确。故选 A。
