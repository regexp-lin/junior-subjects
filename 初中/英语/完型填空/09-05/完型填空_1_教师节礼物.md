# 中考英语完形填空：教师节礼物

## 完形填空原文

Teachers' Day was coming, and our class wanted to do something special for our teachers. We had a class meeting to discuss ___1___. Many ideas were suggested — buying flowers, writing cards, or performing a song. After a long discussion, we decided to prepare a surprise party.

We formed a planning committee of five students. I was chosen to be the ___2___ because I was good at organising things. We had only three days to prepare, so we needed to work quickly and efficiently.

First, we decorated the classroom. We bought colourful balloons and paper ribbons. Two students with good handwriting wrote "Happy Teachers' Day" on the blackboard in beautiful ___3___. We also made a big poster with photos of our class activities throughout the year. Looking at the poster, I felt warm inside — it recorded so many precious ___4___.

Next, we prepared performances. Some students practised a song called "You Raise Me Up." Others wrote a short play about a teacher who changed a student's life. I helped write a class letter to our head teacher, thanking her for everything she had done. It was not easy to put our ___5___ into words, but we tried our best.

On Teachers' Day morning, we asked our head teacher to come to the classroom five minutes early. When she opened the door, we all shouted, " ___6___ Teachers' Day!" She stood at the door, completely surprised. Her eyes became red, and I could see she was trying not to cry.

The performances went smoothly. When we sang the song, several students had tears in their eyes. Our head teacher was deeply ___7___. After the play, she said, "This is the best gift I have ever received. It is not about the decorations or the performances — it is about your hearts."

She then shared a story about her first year as a teacher. She said she had almost ___8___ teaching because it was so difficult. But a student's thank-you note had given her the courage to continue. "That is why I always tell you — a kind word can change someone's ___9___," she said.

That Teachers' Day was unforgettable. We learned that the best gifts are not expensive — they come from ___10___. A sincere "thank you" means more than any amount of money.

5. A. anger &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. doubt &emsp;&emsp; D. gratitude
6. A. Merry &emsp;&emsp; B. Happy &emsp;&emsp; C. Lucky &emsp;&emsp; D. Funny

9. A. name &emsp;&emsp; B. address &emsp;&emsp; C. age &emsp;&emsp; D. life

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们开班会讨论___。A 项意为"作业"；B 项意为"运动"；C 项意为"主意"；D 项意为"考试"。讨论为教师节做什么的各种想法，"ideas"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被选为___因为我擅长组织。A 项意为"负责人"；B 项意为"运动员"；C 项意为"司机"；D 项意为"歌手"。组织委员会的负责人，"leader"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两名字写得好的同学用漂亮的___在黑板上写了祝福语。A 项意为"数字"；B 项意为"字母"；C 项意为"图片"；D 项意为"地图"。写英文祝福语是写字母，"letters"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：海报记录了很多珍贵的___。A 项意为"记忆/回忆"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"秘密"。一年中的活动照片记录着美好回忆，"memories"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把我们的___用文字表达出来不容易。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"感激"。感谢老师所做的一切，表达的是感激之情，"gratitude"正确。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：___教师节！A 项意为"圣诞快乐"常用搭配；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"幸运的"；D 项意为"有趣的"。"Happy Teachers' Day"是固定表达。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的班主任深深地被___了。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"感动的"；C 项意为"困惑的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。学生的表演让老师感动，"touched"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说她几乎___了教学。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"享受"；C 项意为"放弃"；D 项意为"选择"。教学困难差点放弃，"quit"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一句善意的话可以改变一个人的___。A 项意为"名字"；B 项意为"地址"；C 项意为"年龄"；D 项意为"人生"。老师因学生的感谢信继续教书，改变了人生，"life"正确。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最好的礼物来自___。A 项意为"内心"；B 项意为"口袋"；C 项意为"商店"；D 项意为"办公室"。真诚的感谢来自内心，"the heart"正确。故选 A。
