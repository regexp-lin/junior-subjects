# 中考英语完形填空：屋顶上的秘密花园

## 完形填空原文

We lived on the fifth floor of an old apartment building. There was no yard, no garden, and no place for anything green to ___1___. That changed the summer my mother decided to grow vegetables on the rooftop.

"Are you serious?" I asked when she carried bags of soil up the narrow stairs. She just smiled and said, "Watch and ___2___."

She filled old buckets and wooden boxes with soil, then planted tomato seeds, pepper seeds, and a few herbs. I thought she was ___3___ her time. Nothing could grow up there — it was too hot, too windy, and too dry.

But Mom watered the plants every morning before work. She checked them every evening after dinner. She talked to them as if they were her ___4___. "Come on, little ones," she would whisper. "You can do it."

To my surprise, green shoots began to ___5___ after two weeks. By the end of July, tiny tomatoes hung from the stems, and the pepper plants stood tall and proud. The rooftop, once grey and ___6___, was now full of life and color.

Mom started sharing vegetables with our neighbors. Mrs. Wang on the third floor got tomatoes. Mr. Li next door received fresh peppers. ___7___, other families began bringing their own pots to the rooftop. Someone planted sunflowers. Someone else added strawberries.

By September, our rooftop had become a real ___8___. Neighbors who had never spoken to each other were now chatting among the plants, sharing gardening tips and recipes. The building felt warmer, not because of the sun, but because of the ___9___ between people.

One evening, as Mom and I sat on the rooftop watching the sunset behind the tomato plants, I said, "Mom, I can't believe all of this started with a few bags of soil."

She looked at me and said, "The best things in life start ___10___. You just have to believe and keep going."

## 选项

1. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. grow &emsp;&emsp; C. fall &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
2. A. learn &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. try
3. A. saving &emsp;&emsp; B. spending &emsp;&emsp; C. wasting &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoying
4. A. children &emsp;&emsp; B. students &emsp;&emsp; C. neighbors &emsp;&emsp; D. friends
5. A. die &emsp;&emsp; B. appear &emsp;&emsp; C. disappear &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
6. A. empty &emsp;&emsp; B. clean &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. crowded
7. A. Finally &emsp;&emsp; B. Suddenly &emsp;&emsp; C. Luckily &emsp;&emsp; D. Soon
8. A. garden &emsp;&emsp; B. market &emsp;&emsp; C. playground &emsp;&emsp; D. restaurant
9. A. difference &emsp;&emsp; B. distance &emsp;&emsp; C. connection &emsp;&emsp; D. competition
10. A. late &emsp;&emsp; B. small &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. easy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有院子、没有花园，没有地方让绿色的东西___。A 项意为"躲藏"；B 项意为"生长"；C 项意为"落下"；D 项意为"休息"。植物需要空间来"生长"（grow），这里描述住在公寓楼没有种植条件。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看着，然后___。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"尝试"。妈妈自信地说"看好了，学着点"（watch and learn），表示她会证明自己是对的。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得她在___时间。A 项意为"节省"；B 项意为"花费"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"享受"。"waste one's time"（浪费时间）是固定搭配，"我"认为屋顶条件恶劣，种菜是白费力气。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她跟植物说话，好像它们是她的___。A 项意为"孩子"；B 项意为"学生"；C 项意为"邻居"；D 项意为"朋友"。妈妈温柔地对植物说"小家伙们，你们可以的"，像对待自己的"孩子"（children）一样呵护。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两周后绿芽开始___了。A 项意为"死亡"；B 项意为"出现"；C 项意为"消失"；D 项意为"变干"。种子发芽，绿色嫩芽"出现"（appear）了，令"我"惊讶。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：屋顶曾经是灰色且___的。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"干净的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"拥挤的"。与现在"full of life and color"形成对比，之前屋顶是"空旷的"（empty），毫无生气。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___，其他家庭也开始把花盆搬上屋顶。A 项意为"最终"；B 项意为"突然"；C 项意为"幸运地"；D 项意为"不久"。受到妈妈的启发，"不久"（soon）邻居们也加入了，体现影响力的快速传播。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的屋顶变成了一个真正的___。A 项意为"花园"；B 项意为"市场"；C 项意为"操场"；D 项意为"餐厅"。各种植物种满了屋顶，标题也提到"秘密花园"，屋顶变成了一个"花园"（garden）。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：楼房感觉更温暖了，不是因为阳光，而是因为人与人之间的___。A 项意为"不同"；B 项意为"距离"；C 项意为"联系"；D 项意为"竞争"。邻居们从陌生到交流分享，人与人的"联系"（connection）让社区变得温暖。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生活中最好的事情都从___开始。A 项意为"晚"；B 项意为"小"；C 项意为"快"；D 项意为"容易"。妈妈的哲理是：最美好的事物都从"小"（small）处开始，几袋土就能创造一个花园。故选 B。
