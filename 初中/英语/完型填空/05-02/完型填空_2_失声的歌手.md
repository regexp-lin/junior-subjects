# 中考英语完形填空：失声的歌手

## 完形填空原文

Lin Yue had the best voice in our school. When she sang, everyone stopped to ___1___. Teachers said she had a gift. Students said she would be famous one day.

Then, one cold morning in November, Lin Yue woke up and couldn't speak. Her voice was completely ___2___. The doctor said she had damaged her vocal cords and needed to rest them for at least three months. No singing, no shouting — not even whispering.

For Lin Yue, this was like losing a part of herself. She had been chosen to sing at the school's New Year concert, the biggest ___3___ of the year. Now she had to give it up.

At first, she refused to come to school. She stayed in her room and ___4___ for days. Her mother told my mother that Lin Yue had stopped eating properly and barely slept.

Our class decided to do something. We made a large card with messages from every student. Some wrote jokes. Some drew pictures. I wrote: "Your voice is resting, not gone. It will come back ___5___ than ever."

When we brought the card to her house, Lin Yue opened the door with red eyes. She read every ___6___ carefully, and slowly, a small smile appeared on her face.

After that, Lin Yue returned to school. She couldn't talk, so she communicated by writing notes on a small whiteboard. At first, it was ___7___ for her. But soon, classmates started writing notes back to her, and it became a special way of chatting.

During those three months, Lin Yue discovered something ___8___. She began writing song lyrics. Without her voice, she turned to words on paper. She wrote about friendship, about silence, about waiting. Her lyrics were so beautiful that our music teacher used one of them in the concert.

When spring came, Lin Yue's voice ___9___. On the first day she could sing again, she performed her own song in front of the whole school. There was not a dry eye in the audience.

"Sometimes," she said into the microphone, "you have to ___10___ something to discover what really matters."

## 选项

1. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. listen &emsp;&emsp; C. practice &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
2. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. low &emsp;&emsp; C. gone &emsp;&emsp; D. changed
3. A. event &emsp;&emsp; B. exam &emsp;&emsp; C. holiday &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson
4. A. studied &emsp;&emsp; B. sang &emsp;&emsp; C. cried &emsp;&emsp; D. played
5. A. weaker &emsp;&emsp; B. quieter &emsp;&emsp; C. slower &emsp;&emsp; D. stronger
6. A. message &emsp;&emsp; B. book &emsp;&emsp; C. letter &emsp;&emsp; D. note
7. A. strange &emsp;&emsp; B. exciting &emsp;&emsp; C. boring &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxing
8. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. new &emsp;&emsp; C. familiar &emsp;&emsp; D. useless
9. A. broke &emsp;&emsp; B. dropped &emsp;&emsp; C. returned &emsp;&emsp; D. disappeared
10. A. lose &emsp;&emsp; B. choose &emsp;&emsp; C. remember &emsp;&emsp; D. share

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她唱歌时，每个人都停下来___。A 项意为"帮忙"；B 项意为"听"；C 项意为"练习"；D 项意为"离开"。她声音好听，大家停下来"听"（listen）她唱歌。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的声音完全___了。A 项意为"轻柔的"；B 项意为"低的"；C 项意为"消失了的"；D 项意为"改变了的"。完全说不出话来，声音"消失了"（gone），情况很严重。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校新年音乐会是一年中最大的___。A 项意为"活动"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"假期"；D 项意为"课程"。新年音乐会是学校最大的"活动"（event）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她待在房间里___了好几天。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"唱歌"；C 项意为"哭"；D 项意为"玩耍"。失去声音对歌手来说是巨大打击，她待在房间里"哭"（cried），后面还提到不好好吃饭、几乎不睡。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：你的声音在休息，不是消失了。它会比以前更___地回来。A 项意为"更弱"；B 项意为"更安静"；C 项意为"更慢"；D 项意为"更强"。鼓励她声音休息后会更"强"（stronger），给她信心。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她仔细读了每条___。A 项意为"留言"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"笔记"。卡片上写满了同学们的"留言"（message），包括笑话和画。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始对她来说很___。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"令人兴奋的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"放松的"。习惯用声音交流的人突然只能写字沟通，起初感觉很"奇怪"（strange），不适应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：林悦发现了某些___的东西。A 项意为"可怕的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"熟悉的"；D 项意为"无用的"。她发现了写歌词这个"新"（new）才能，是失声带来的意外收获。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：春天来了，林悦的声音___了。A 项意为"断裂"；B 项意为"下降"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"消失"。经过三个月休养，声音"回来了"（returned），与前文"it will come back"呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时候你必须___某些东西才能发现什么是真正重要的。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"选择"；C 项意为"记住"；D 项意为"分享"。她失去了声音却发现了写词的才华，"失去"（lose）才懂得珍惜，点明主题。故选 A。
