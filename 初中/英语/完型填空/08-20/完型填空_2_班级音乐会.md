# 中考英语完形填空：班级音乐会

## 完形填空原文

Our class decided to hold a mini concert before the winter holiday. Everyone was excited because we could show our talents and have fun together. We had three weeks to prepare.

I played the guitar, so I signed up to perform a song. My best friend Lin Tao played the piano. We decided to ___1___ a duet together. We practised every day after school in the music room. At first, our timing was off, but we kept trying until we could play in perfect harmony.

Other classmates had different talents. Zhang Mei could sing beautifully. Wang Lei could play the flute. Some girls prepared a group dance. We all had something to ___2___, and that made our concert special.

We also needed someone to be the host. Li Na volunteered because she was confident and had a clear voice. She wrote a short ___3___ for each performance. She would introduce the performer and the piece, making the audience feel welcome.

The day of the concert finally arrived. Our classroom was decorated with balloons and coloured paper. Parents and teachers were invited to watch. I was very ___4___ before my turn. My hands were cold and sweaty. But when Lin Tao and I walked onto the "stage" (the front of the classroom), the familiar music calmed me down.

We played our piece without any mistakes. The audience clapped loudly. I looked at my parents — they were smiling and taking photos. I felt a great sense of ___5___. All our practice had paid off.

The concert lasted for about an hour. There were songs, dances, and even a short magic show. The last performance was a choir — the whole class sang a song together. The ___6___ was warm and joyful. Everyone was smiling.

After the concert, our teacher said she was proud of us. "You not only showed your talents but also learned to work as a ___7___," she said. "Preparing for this concert required cooperation and patience."

We took a lot of photos and ate snacks together. It was a wonderful memory. I kept the programme sheet as a ___8___. On it were the names of all the performers and their pieces. Maybe years later, I will look at it and remember this special day.

That night, I wrote in my diary: "Today we held our class concert. I was nervous at first, but the moment I started playing, everything felt right. Music brings people ___9___. I am grateful for my classmates and this unforgettable ___10___."

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们决定一起___二重奏。A 项意为"表演"；B 项意为"听"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"唱"。"perform a duet"意为"表演二重奏"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都有可以___的东西。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"忘记"。"share"指展示、分享才艺。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她为每个节目写了简短的___。A 项意为"歌曲"；B 项意为"稿子/串词"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"故事"。"script"指主持人串词。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：轮到我们之前我非常___。A 项意为"自信"；B 项意为"紧张"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"疲倦"。"nervous"与后文手冷出汗相符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到巨大的___。A 项意为"担忧"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"悲伤"。表演成功后的自豪感，"pride"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___温暖而欢乐。A 项意为"天气"；B 项意为"气氛"；C 项意为"房间"；D 项意为"食物"。"atmosphere"指现场氛围。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们也学会了作为___合作。A 项意为"团队"；B 项意为"家庭"；C 项意为"小组"；D 项意为"班级"。"work as a team"意为"团队协作"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把节目单留作___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"纪念品"；C 项意为"作业"；D 项意为"奖品"。"as a souvenir"意为"作为纪念"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：音乐让人___。A 项意为"分开"；B 项意为"聚在一起"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"在后面"。"bring people together"意为"让人们团结/聚在一起"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感谢同学们和这段难忘的___。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"经历"；C 项意为"科目"；D 项意为"游戏"。"unforgettable experience"意为"难忘的经历"。故选 B。
