# 中考英语完形填空：参观博物馆

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our class went on a school trip to the city museum. We had been studying Chinese history, and our teacher said that seeing real artefacts would help us understand the past better. I was looking ___1___ to the visit.

The museum was a large building with tall columns at the entrance. Inside, it was cool and quiet. We were divided into small groups, and each group had a guide. Our guide was a young woman who spoke clearly and knew a lot about the ___2___.

The first room showed tools and pottery from thousands of years ago. I was amazed to see how people lived without modern technology. They made their own bowls, cups, and cooking pots from clay. The guide said that each piece told a ___3___ about the people who made it.

We moved to the second room, which displayed ancient coins and jewellery. There was a gold crown that once belonged to a queen. I pressed my face close to the glass to see the fine details. The guide warned us not to ___4___ the displays. "These objects are very old and fragile," she said.

In the third room, we saw weapons and armour from different dynasties. There were swords, spears, and shields. Our guide explained how soldiers protected themselves in battles. I had never thought about history in such a ___5___ way before. It was like stepping back in time.

We had a short break at noon. The museum had a cafe where we could buy drinks and snacks. I sat with my friends and discussed what we had seen. We all agreed that the trip was much more ___6___ than reading from a textbook.

After lunch, we visited the art section. There were traditional paintings of mountains, rivers, and flowers. The colours were beautiful, and the brushwork was delicate. Our teacher said that art reflects the culture and ___7___ of a time. I began to appreciate Chinese art more.

Before we left, we went to the gift shop. I bought a small replica of an ancient coin for my grandfather. He loves collecting such things. The shop also sold postcards, books, and other ___8___. I could have spent the whole afternoon there.

The bus ride back to school was quiet. Many of us were tired but happy. I thought about everything I had learned. Museums are like time machines — they take us to the past and help us ___9___ the present. I hope we can visit more ___10___ in the future.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___这次参观。A 项意为"向后"；B 项意为"向前"；C 项意为"向下"；D 项意为"向上"。"look forward to"意为"期待"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们的讲解员对___了解很多。A 项意为"建筑"；B 项意为"展品"；C 项意为"访客"；D 项意为"员工"。博物馆讲解员熟悉展品，"exhibits"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一件都讲述制作它的那个时代的___。A 项意为"笑话"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"谎言"；D 项意为"笑话"。文物承载历史故事，"story"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：讲解员提醒我们不要___展品。A 项意为"触摸"；B 项意为"清洁"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"画"。文物脆弱不能触摸，"touch"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从未以如此___的方式思考过历史。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"生动的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"简单的"。"vivid"表示生动形象。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都认为这次出行比读课本更___。A 项意为"累人"；B 项意为"有趣"；C 项意为"昂贵"；D 项意为"困难"。"interesting"符合实地参观的体验。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：艺术反映一个时代的文化和___。A 项意为"天气"；B 项意为"价值观"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"衣服"。"values"指价值观、审美。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：商店还卖明信片、书籍和其他___。A 项意为"财宝"；B 项意为"纪念品"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"家具"。"souvenirs"意为"纪念品"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们带我们回到过去，帮助我们___现在。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"理解"；C 项意为"忽略"；D 项意为"改变"。了解历史有助于理解当下，"understand"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望将来能参观更多___。A 项意为"博物馆"；B 项意为"历史"；C 项意为"旅行"；D 项意为"学校"。"visit more museums"意为"参观更多博物馆"。故选 A。
