# 中考英语完形填空：暴风雨中的篮球赛

## 完形填空原文

The basketball finals were scheduled for Friday afternoon. Our team had practiced for months, and we were ___1___ to win. Nothing could stop us — or so we thought.

At noon, dark clouds rolled across the sky. By the time the game was supposed to start, rain was pouring down so hard that we could barely see the other end of the outdoor ___2___. The referee considered canceling the game.

"We can't cancel!" shouted our captain, Liu Wei. "We've worked too hard for this." The other team's captain ___3___, and eventually the referee agreed to let us play.

The first half was terrible. The ball was slippery, the ground was muddy, and every pass went ___4___. We fell behind by twelve points. During halftime, some of my teammates wanted to give up. "There's no way we can win in this weather," said Chen Hao, shaking his wet hair.

Liu Wei stood up. "Listen," he said, his voice ___5___ but firm. "The rain is the same for both teams. If we give up now, we'll regret it forever. Let's just play our best and see what happens."

Something changed after that. Maybe it was Liu Wei's words, or maybe it was the thought of all those months of ___6___. Either way, we went back onto the court with a different attitude.

In the second half, we played like a completely different team. We stopped worrying about the rain and started ___7___ on the game. Shot by shot, we closed the gap. With two minutes left, the score was tied.

Then, in the final thirty seconds, I got the ball near the three-point line. My shoes were full of water. My hands were ___8___. But I jumped, aimed, and released the ball. It flew through the rain in a perfect arc and went straight through the hoop.

The whistle blew. We had won by three points.

Our team screamed and hugged each other in the rain. Nobody cared about being wet anymore. We had ___9___ something more important than a trophy — we had proved to ourselves that we could overcome anything.

That game taught me a ___10___ I'll never forget: the biggest obstacles are not outside — they're in your head. And the moment you decide not to quit, you've already half won.

9. A. collected &emsp;&emsp; B. received &emsp;&emsp; C. won &emsp;&emsp; D. discovered

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们练习了好几个月，___赢了。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"准备好的"；C 项意为"抱歉的"；D 项意为"幸运的"。"be ready to"（准备好做某事），经过数月训练，已经"准备好"要赢。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们几乎看不到室外___的另一端。A 项意为"教室"；B 项意为"球场"；C 项意为"泳池"；D 项意为"舞台"。篮球比赛在室外"球场"（court）进行。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对方队长也___了。A 项意为"拒绝"；B 项意为"离开"；C 项意为"同意"；D 项意为"笑"。两队队长都不想取消比赛，对方也"同意"（agreed）继续打。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个传球都___了。A 项意为"错的"；B 项意为"好的"；C 项意为"快的"；D 项意为"远的"。"go wrong"（出错）是固定表达，雨天球滑地滑，传球全部失误。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的声音___但坚定。A 项意为"大声的"；B 项意为"愤怒的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"虚弱的"。与"but firm"形成转折，声音虽"轻"（quiet）但很坚定，体现冷静的领导力。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：想到那几个月的___。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"训练"；C 项意为"思考"；D 项意为"休息"。前文提到"practiced for months"，这里回忆的是数月的"训练"（training）。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不再担心雨，开始___比赛。A 项意为"依赖"；B 项意为"专注"；C 项意为"尝试"；D 项意为"工作"。"focus on"（专注于）是固定搭配，不再受天气干扰，全力"专注"比赛。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手在___。A 项意为"温暖的"；B 项意为"干净的"；C 项意为"颤抖的"；D 项意为"强壮的"。鞋里灌满水，手又冷又紧张，正在"颤抖"（shaking），但仍然投出了关键一球。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们___了比奖杯更重要的东西。A 项意为"收集"；B 项意为"收到"；C 项意为"赢得"；D 项意为"发现"。通过拼搏"赢得"（won）的不仅是比赛，更是对自己的信心。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那场比赛教会了我一个永远不会忘记的___。A 项意为"教训/道理"；B 项意为"技能"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"故事"。"teach someone a lesson"（让某人学到一个道理），这里指人生感悟。故选 A。
