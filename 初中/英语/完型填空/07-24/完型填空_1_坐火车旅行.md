# 中考英语完形填空：坐火车旅行

## 完形填空原文

Last summer, my family took a train trip to visit my grandparents. It was my first long ___1___ by train. I was very excited when we arrived at the station. There were so many people carrying bags and suitcases.

We found our seats and put our luggage on the rack above. The train ___2___ slowly at first, then faster and faster. I looked out of the window and watched the scenery fly by. Green fields, small villages, and tall mountains passed before my eyes. It was like watching a moving ___3___.

After about an hour, I felt a bit hungry. My mum had packed some snacks — biscuits, fruit, and a few sandwiches. We shared them and had a small picnic on the train. The food tasted ___4___ perhaps because we were travelling together.

An old man sat next to us. He was going to the same city. He told us stories about his youth and how he used to travel by train when he was young. "Trains have changed a lot," he said. "But the ___5___ of travel remains the same — discovering new places and meeting new people."

The journey took six hours. When we finally ___6___ at my grandparents' city, it was already evening. My grandparents were waiting for us at the station. They hugged us tightly. I was tired but very happy.

That train trip taught me that the ___7___ is just as important as the destination. We should enjoy every moment of our journey, not just rush to the end. I also made a new ___8___ — the kind old man. He gave me his phone number and said I could call him if I ever visited his town again.

I hope to take more train trips in the future. There is something special about ___9___ by train — you can see the world slowly unfold outside your window. It is different from flying, where everything seems to pass in a blink. Trains let you ___10___ and breathe.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我第一次长途火车___。A 项意为"步行"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"旅程"；D 项意为"休息"。"long journey"意为"长途旅行"，正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：火车起初___得很慢，然后越来越快。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"启动"；C 项意为"坏了"；D 项意为"等待"。火车启动加速，"started"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：像看一部移动的___。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"电影"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"歌"。窗外景色掠过如电影画面，"film"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：食物尝起来很___，或许因为我们一起旅行。A 项意为"难吃"；B 项意为"美味"；C 项意为"奇怪"；D 项意为"冷"。与家人共享让食物更香，"delicious"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但旅行的___始终相同。A 项意为"费用"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"速度"；D 项意为"难度"。发现新地方、结识新人是旅行的乐趣，"joy"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们终于___爷爷奶奶的城市时。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"到达"；C 项意为"停留"；D 项意为"睡觉"。"arrived at"意为"到达"，正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___和目的地同样重要。A 项意为"票"；B 项意为"旅途"；C 项意为"火车"；D 项意为"包"。旅途过程与目的地同样重要，"journey"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我还结交了一个新___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"计划"。与老人相识成为朋友，"friend"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：火车___有特别之处。A 项意为"开车"；B 项意为"旅行"；C 项意为"跑步"；D 项意为"飞行"。"travelling by train"意为"乘火车旅行"，正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：火车让你可以___和呼吸。A 项意为"匆忙"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"放慢"；D 项意为"放弃"。与"flying"对比，火车让人放慢节奏，"slow down"正确。故选 C。
