# 中考英语完形填空：学下棋

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather loves playing Chinese chess. Every weekend when I visit him, he would ask, "Want to learn?" For a long time I said no — I thought it was a game for ___1___ people. But one day I felt bored and agreed to try.

Grandpa placed the board on the table and explained the basic ___2___. "Each piece moves in a different way," he said. "The horse jumps, the cannon needs a screen, and the elephant cannot cross the river." It sounded complicated at first. I made many mistakes and lost game after game. Grandpa never laughed at me. He just said, "Think before you ___3___."

Slowly, I began to understand the game. I learned to plan my moves ahead. Grandpa taught me that a good player does not only look at the next move — they think about the whole ___4___. "Chess is like life," he said. "Every choice has results. You must learn to accept both wins and ___5___."

After a few months, I could sometimes beat Grandpa. He would smile and say, "You have improved a lot!" That made me feel proud. But more importantly, I enjoyed the time we spent ___6___. We talked about school, life, and dreams while playing. The chess board became our special place to connect.

I have brought a small chess set to school. During breaks, my friends and I play quick games. Some of them are now learning too. We ___7___ each other and have fun together. Chess has become more than a game — it is a way to build ___8___.

Grandpa says that chess will stay with me for life. No matter how old I get, I can always find someone to play with. I hope to teach my own children or grandchildren one day, just like Grandpa taught me. That would be the best ___9___ I could pass on.

Learning chess has taught me patience, thinking, and the joy of ___10___ with someone you love. I am thankful for Grandpa's patience and for this wonderful game.

1. A. young &emsp;&emsp; B. old &emsp;&emsp; C. busy &emsp;&emsp; D. lazy
2. A. rules &emsp;&emsp; B. stories &emsp;&emsp; C. songs &emsp;&emsp; D. pictures
3. A. move &emsp;&emsp; B. sleep &emsp;&emsp; C. eat &emsp;&emsp; D. speak
4. A. game &emsp;&emsp; B. day &emsp;&emsp; C. book &emsp;&emsp; D. room
5. A. prizes &emsp;&emsp; B. losses &emsp;&emsp; C. gifts &emsp;&emsp; D. friends
6. A. alone &emsp;&emsp; B. together &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
7. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. fight &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. blame
8. A. houses &emsp;&emsp; B. friendship &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. rules
9. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. game &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. story

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以为那是___人的游戏。A 项意为"年轻的"；B 项意为"年老的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。象棋常被认为是老人活动，"old"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他解释了基本的___。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"歌"；D 项意为"图画"。下棋要讲规则，"rules"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在___之前要想好。A 项意为"走棋"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"吃"；D 项意为"说话"。下棋时"move"指走棋，正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要考虑整盘___。A 项意为"棋局"；B 项意为"日子"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"房间"。下棋考虑整盘局面，"game"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：要学会接受胜利和___。A 项意为"奖品"；B 项意为"失败"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"朋友"。与"wins"对应的是失败，"losses"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我享受我们一起___度过的时光。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"大声地"。"together"强调与爷爷共处的时光，正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们互相___，一起玩得开心。A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"打架"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"责备"。朋友之间互相教下棋，"teach"正确。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是建立___的方式。A 项意为"房子"；B 项意为"友谊"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"规则"。与朋友下棋增进友谊，"friendship"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那将是我能传承的最好___。A 项意为"礼物/传承"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"课程"；D 项意为"故事"。"pass on"传承的可以是技艺作为礼物，"gift"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与所爱之人___的乐趣。A 项意为"争论"；B 项意为"玩耍"；C 项意为"竞争"；D 项意为"工作"。与爷爷下棋是玩耍，"playing"正确。故选 B。
