# 中考英语完形填空：课间的毽子

## 完形填空原文

When the bell rings for the break, our classroom becomes noisy with laughter. Many classmates run to the playground, but I prefer to stay near the window. From there, I can see a group of students playing jianzi in the schoolyard. The colorful feather shuttlecock flies up and down in the air. It looks so ___1___ that I wish I could join them.

Last term, I never tried jianzi because I thought it was too ___2___ for me. I was afraid of failing in front of others. But one day, my friend Xiaoming came to me with a smile. "Come on, it's easy to learn," he said. "I will ___3___ you." So I walked to the playground with him. At first, I could only kick the shuttlecock once or twice before it ___4___. I felt a little disappointed. However, Xiaoming did not laugh at me. Instead, he showed me how to ___5___ my foot and keep balance. Day by day, I practiced during every break. Slowly, I could kick it five times, then ten times. The more I practiced, the more ___6___ I became.

Now jianzi is one of my favorite activities. It helps me relax after hard study. It also teaches me that we should never ___7___ ourselves before we try. Sometimes we think something is too difficult, but when we give it a ___8___, we may find it is not as hard as we imagined. The shuttlecock in the air reminds me that every small step can lead to great progress. I am ___9___ to my friend Xiaoming for his patience and kindness. Thanks to him, I have found both a new hobby and a valuable ___10___.

1. A. fun &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
2. A. simple &emsp;&emsp; B. useful &emsp;&emsp; C. hard &emsp;&emsp; D. popular
3. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. follow &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
4. A. flew &emsp;&emsp; B. rose &emsp;&emsp; C. fell &emsp;&emsp; D. stopped

6. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. confident &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
7. A. help &emsp;&emsp; B. trust &emsp;&emsp; C. thank &emsp;&emsp; D. judge
8. A. reason &emsp;&emsp; B. answer &emsp;&emsp; C. chance &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
9. A. similar &emsp;&emsp; B. polite &emsp;&emsp; C. thankful &emsp;&emsp; D. close
10. A. game &emsp;&emsp; B. prize &emsp;&emsp; C. toy &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：毽子在空中上下飞舞，看起来非常___，我真想加入他们。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。"fun"与"wish I could join them"相呼应，表示毽子看起来有趣而引人参与。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：上学期我从未尝试过毽子，因为我觉得对我来说太___了。A 项意为"简单的"；B 项意为"有用的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"流行的"。"hard"与后文"afraid of failing"形成因果，解释不敢尝试的原因。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小明笑着对我说："来吧，很容易学的，我会___你。"A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"跟随"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"忘记"。"teach"与"easy to learn"相呼应，表示朋友愿意教"我"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我只能踢一两次，毽子就___了。A 项意为"飞"；B 项意为"上升"；C 项意为"落下、掉下"；D 项意为"停止"。"fell"指毽子落地，与"only kick once or twice"呼应，说明初学时踢得不多。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他教我怎么___脚并保持平衡。A 项意为"移动"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"藏"。"move"指踢毽子的腿部动作，与"keep balance"共同描述技术要领。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：练习得越多，我就越___。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"自信的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。"confident"与进步过程相呼应，表示从"afraid of failing"到敢于尝试的成长。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它也教会我们，在尝试之前不应___自己。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"信任"；C 项意为"感谢"；D 项意为"评判、否定"。"judge"在此处意为过早下结论否定自己，与"before we try"形成对比。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们给它一个___时，也许会发现它并不像想象的那么难。A 项意为"理由"；B 项意为"答案"；C 项意为"机会、试试"；D 项意为"礼物"。"give it a chance"为常见搭配，意为"试一下"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对朋友小明的耐心和善良非常___。A 项意为"相似的"；B 项意为"礼貌的"；C 项意为"感激的"；D 项意为"亲近的"。"thankful"与"Thanks to him"相呼应，表达感恩。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：多亏了他，我既找到了新爱好，也收获了一个宝贵的___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"玩具"；D 项意为"教训、道理"。"lesson"指前文"never judge ourselves before we try"等人生态度，呼应"valuable"。故选 D。
