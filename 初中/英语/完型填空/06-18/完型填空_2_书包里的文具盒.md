# 中考英语完形填空：书包里的文具盒

## 完形填空原文

Every morning when I pack my schoolbag, I always put my pencil case in the front pocket. It is a blue box that my mother gave me on my first day of junior high. Though it is not expensive, it is very ___1___ to me. Inside there are pencils, erasers, a ruler and a few pens. These small things help me get through every school day.

One Monday, I forgot to bring my pencil case. I had to ___2___ pens from my classmates. I felt so sorry for troubling them. From that day on, I made a habit of checking my bag before leaving home. A small pencil case may seem ___3___, but it plays an important role in our daily study. Without it, we cannot take notes clearly or ___4___ our homework neatly. Good habits start from small things. Being organized helps us save time and avoid ___5___.

My pencil case also reminds me of my mother's love. She chose it carefully because she wanted me to study hard and take care of my ___6___. Every time I open it, I think of her words: "Use your tools well, and they will serve you well." I try to keep my pencils sharp and my erasers clean. I never throw them around carelessly. In this way, I show ___7___ for both the gifts and the person who gave them.

Some classmates like to buy new stationery every week. But I believe that what matters is not how new or expensive our things are, but how we ___8___ them. My old pencil case still works perfectly after two years. It has become a silent ___9___ that stays with me every day. When I grow up, I will keep it as a memory of my junior high years. It will always remind me of the simple ___10___ that small things in life can bring great meaning.

## 选项

1. A. special &emsp;&emsp; B. cheap &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. heavy
2. A. borrow &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. collect &emsp;&emsp; D. return
3. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. ordinary &emsp;&emsp; D. different
4. A. copy &emsp;&emsp; B. correct &emsp;&emsp; C. finish &emsp;&emsp; D. check
5. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; C. friends &emsp;&emsp; D. ideas
6. A. things &emsp;&emsp; B. books &emsp;&emsp; C. grades &emsp;&emsp; D. health
7. A. hope &emsp;&emsp; B. interest &emsp;&emsp; C. pity &emsp;&emsp; D. respect
8. A. lose &emsp;&emsp; B. use &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. share
9. A. teacher &emsp;&emsp; B. parent &emsp;&emsp; C. friend &emsp;&emsp; D. student
10. A. question &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. joke &emsp;&emsp; D. truth

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽然它不贵，但对我来说非常___。A 项意为"特别的、珍贵的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"重的"。"special"与"my mother gave me"及后文"reminds me of my mother's love"相呼应，强调情感价值。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我忘了带文具盒，不得不向同学___笔。A 项意为"借"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"收集"；D 项意为"归还"。"borrow"与"from my classmates"搭配，表示临时借用。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个小小的文具盒看起来可能很___，但它在日常学习中扮演重要角色。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"完美的"；C 项意为"普通的、平常的"；D 项意为"不同的"。"ordinary"与"plays an important role"形成转折，突出小物大用。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有它，我们无法清楚地记笔记，也无法工整地___作业。A 项意为"抄袭"；B 项意为"改正"；C 项意为"完成"；D 项意为"检查"。"finish"与"take notes"并列，均指日常学习任务。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有条理能帮助我们节省时间，避免___。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"错误、麻烦"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"想法"。"mistakes"与"being organized"形成对比，指因忘带文具等导致的失误。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她希望我好好学习，并照顾好我的___。A 项意为"物品、东西"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"成绩"；D 项意为"健康"。"things"在此泛指文具等学习用品，与"take care of"搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这样，我对礼物和送礼之人表达___。A 项意为"希望"；B 项意为"兴趣"；C 项意为"同情"；D 项意为"尊重、珍视"。"respect"与"never throw them around carelessly"及"take care of"相呼应。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：重要的不是东西多新多贵，而是我们如何___它们。A 项意为"丢失"；B 项意为"使用、对待"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"分享"。"use"在此含"使用、对待"之意，与"take care of"、"keep...sharp"等一致。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它已成为一个无声的___，每天陪伴着我。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"家长"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"学生"。"friend"将文具盒拟人化，与"stays with me every day"呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它会一直提醒我生活中那个简单的___：小事也能带来重大意义。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"道理、真相"。"truth"与"small things in life can bring great meaning"呼应，总结全文主旨。故选 D。
