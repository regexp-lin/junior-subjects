# 中考英语完形填空：教室里的地球仪

## 完形填空原文

In the corner of our geography classroom, there is a blue and green globe. It has been there for many years, and every student has ___1___ it at least once. When our teacher spins it, we can see all the countries and oceans turning slowly. It makes the world feel so small and ___2___.

I remember the first geography lesson when I was in Grade 7. Our teacher asked us to ___3___ the globe and find China. I put my finger on our country and then moved it across the Pacific Ocean to America. For the first time, I ___4___ how far away other countries were. Before that, they were just names in textbooks. The globe made them real.

When we study different cultures or climates, the teacher always points at the globe. "See, the ___5___ is here. That's why it's so hot." or "This country is on the other side of the world." The globe helps us ___6___ what we learn in books. It connects words with places.

Sometimes after class, I go to the globe and ___7___ it slowly. I imagine visiting the places under my fingers — the pyramids in Egypt, the Eiffel Tower in Paris, the Great Wall right here at home. The globe has ___8___ my curiosity about the world. It makes me want to learn more languages and one day travel to see these places with my own eyes.

Our teacher says the globe is a ___9___ of our classroom. No matter where we come from or what we dream of, we all share the same earth. When I look at it, I feel that we are all connected. The globe doesn't only teach geography — it teaches us to be ___10___ and open-minded about different people and cultures around the world.

2. A. dark &emsp;&emsp; B. close &emsp;&emsp; C. huge &emsp;&emsp; D. empty
3. A. hold &emsp;&emsp; B. draw &emsp;&emsp; C. fix &emsp;&emsp; D. repair
4. A. understood &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. wondered &emsp;&emsp; D. agreed
5. A. sun &emsp;&emsp; B. equator &emsp;&emsp; C. pole &emsp;&emsp; D. mountain

7. A. push &emsp;&emsp; B. spin &emsp;&emsp; C. throw &emsp;&emsp; D. drop
8. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. satisfied &emsp;&emsp; C. awakened &emsp;&emsp; D. changed
9. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. tool &emsp;&emsp; C. symbol &emsp;&emsp; D. prize
10. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. curious &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. serious

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它在那里已经很多年了，每个学生至少___过它一次。A 项意为"触摸"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"制作"；D 项意为"画"。"touched"与后文"put my finger on"、"under my fingers"相呼应，指触碰地球仪。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这让世界显得那么小又___。A 项意为"黑暗的"；B 项意为"亲近的、紧密的"；C 项意为"巨大的"；D 项意为"空的"。"close"与"small"并列，表示地球仪让遥远的国家显得触手可及，与前文"all the countries and oceans"相呼应。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师让我们___地球仪并找到中国。A 项意为"握住、扶着"；B 项意为"画"；C 项意为"固定"；D 项意为"修理"。"hold the globe"指扶着地球仪进行操作，与"find China"相呼应。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是第一次，我___其他国家有多远。A 项意为"理解、明白"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"想知道"；D 项意为"同意"。"understood"表示通过触摸地球仪，真正理解了距离概念，与"Before that, they were just names"形成对比。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（地理词汇）
**【解析】** 本句意为：看，___在这里，所以那里很热。A 项意为"太阳"；B 项意为"赤道"；C 项意为"极地"；D 项意为"山"。讲解气候时常用赤道（equator）解释热带地区高温成因，属于地理课常用词汇。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：地球仪帮助我们___书本上学到的知识。A 项意为"记住"；B 项意为"想象"；C 项意为"理解"；D 项意为"复制"。"understand"与"connects words with places"相呼应，表示将抽象知识与具体地理位置联系起来以便理解。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时课后我会去地球仪旁，慢慢___它。A 项意为"推"；B 项意为"转动"；C 项意为"扔"；D 项意为"丢下"。"spin"与开头"When our teacher spins it"相呼应，指转动地球仪。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：地球仪___了我对世界的好奇心。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"满足"；C 项意为"唤醒、激发"；D 项意为"改变"。"awakened"与"makes me want to learn more"相呼应，表示地球仪激发了探索世界的兴趣。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说地球仪是我们教室的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"工具"；C 项意为"象征"；D 项意为"奖品"。"symbol"与"we all share the same earth"相呼应，强调地球仪象征着人类共同家园。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会我们对待世界各地不同的人和文化要___和开放。A 项意为"骄傲"；B 项意为"好奇的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"严肃的"。"curious"与"open-minded"并列，与前文"curiosity about the world"相呼应，强调对多元文化的兴趣与接纳。故选 B。
