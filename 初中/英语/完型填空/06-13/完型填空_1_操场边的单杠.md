# 中考英语完形填空：操场边的单杠

## 完形填空原文

Beside our school playground stands a set of simple bars — we call them the "monkey bars". When I first saw them in Grade 7, they looked ___1___ and boring. I never thought I would spend so much time there.

My PE teacher noticed that I was weak in arm strength. He said, "Try the bars. Start with hanging, then try to pull yourself up." At first, I could only ___2___ for a few seconds. My arms hurt and I wanted to give up. But my teacher kept ___3___ me. "One more try today. You will get stronger." I followed his advice and came to the bars every day after school.

Week after week, I felt the change. From five seconds to ten, from ten to twenty. One day, I finally pulled myself up once! I was so ___4___ that I shouted. My classmates who were playing basketball nearby came over and clapped. That moment made me ___5___ that hard work pays off.

The bars have taught me more than physical strength. They taught me about ___6___. Every time I fail, I get up and try again. The bars don't judge; they just wait for me to come back. Now when I face difficulties in study or life, I think of those bars. I remember the feeling when I ___7___ my first pull-up, and I tell myself to keep trying.

The monkey bars are still there, a bit ___8___ from rain and sun. But they stand strong, just like the spirit they have given us. I hope every student can find their own "bars" in life — something that pushes them to become ___9___. Because in the end, the bar we climb is not made of metal. It is made of our own ___10___ and belief.

## 选项

1. A. tall &emsp;&emsp; B. old &emsp;&emsp; C. useful &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
2. A. rest &emsp;&emsp; B. hang &emsp;&emsp; C. stand &emsp;&emsp; D. sit
3. A. watching &emsp;&emsp; B. helping &emsp;&emsp; C. encouraging &emsp;&emsp; D. teaching
4. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
5. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. doubt &emsp;&emsp; C. believe &emsp;&emsp; D. wonder
6. A. teamwork &emsp;&emsp; B. patience &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. speed
7. A. missed &emsp;&emsp; B. achieved &emsp;&emsp; C. planned &emsp;&emsp; D. avoided
8. A. broken &emsp;&emsp; B. shiny &emsp;&emsp; C. rusty &emsp;&emsp; D. new
9. A. stronger &emsp;&emsp; B. taller &emsp;&emsp; C. faster &emsp;&emsp; D. richer
10. A. dreams &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. grades &emsp;&emsp; D. strength

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：七年级时第一次看到它们，它们看起来___又无聊。A 项意为"高的"；B 项意为"旧的"；C 项意为"有用的"；D 项意为"奇怪的、陌生的"。"strange"与"boring"并列，表示起初觉得单杠陌生无趣，与后文"never thought I would spend so much time there"形成对比。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初，我只能___几秒钟。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"悬挂"；C 项意为"站立"；D 项意为"坐"。前文提到"Start with hanging"，"hang"指吊在单杠上，符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我的老师一直___我。A 项意为"观看"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"鼓励"；D 项意为"教导"。"encouraging"与后文"One more try today. You will get stronger."相呼应，表示老师的鼓励话语。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有一天，我终于做了一次引体向上！我太___了，忍不住喊了出来。A 项意为"疲惫"；B 项意为"兴奋"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"害怕"。"excited"与"shouted"、"clapped"相呼应，表示达成目标后的激动心情。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻让我___努力会有回报。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"怀疑"；C 项意为"相信"；D 项意为"想知道"。"believe that"表示从亲身经历中领悟到努力的意义，与"hard work pays off"相呼应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：单杠教会我的不仅是体力，还有___。A 项意为"团队合作"；B 项意为"耐心"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"速度"。"patience"与后文"Every time I fail, I get up and try again"相呼应，强调坚持尝试的耐心。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我铭记着完成第一个引体向上时的感受。A 项意为"错过"；B 项意为"实现、完成"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"避免"。"achieved"与"my first pull-up"相呼应，表示达成目标。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：单杠还在那里，因风雨日晒而有些___。A 项意为"破损的"；B 项意为"闪亮的"；C 项意为"生锈的"；D 项意为"新的"。"rusty from rain and sun"表示历经风雨后表面生锈，与"stand strong"形成对比。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望每个学生都能在生活中找到自己的"单杠"——某种促使他们变得更___的东西。A 项意为"更强壮"；B 项意为"更高"；C 项意为"更快"；D 项意为"更富有"。"stronger"与全文主题（单杠、臂力、毅力）相呼应。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：因为我们攀爬的杠不是金属做的，而是由我们自己的___和信念铸成的。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"金钱"；C 项意为"成绩"；D 项意为"力量"。"strength"与"belief"并列，与前文"physical strength"、"get stronger"相呼应，强调内在力量。故选 D。
