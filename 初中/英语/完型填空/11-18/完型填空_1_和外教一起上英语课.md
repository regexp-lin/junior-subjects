# 中考英语完形填空：和外教一起上英语课

## 完形填空原文

This semester, our school invited a foreign teacher from America to teach us English. Her name is Mrs. Brown, and she has a ___1___ way of teaching. She often uses games and stories to make our lessons interesting.

Last Tuesday, we had a special English class with her. Instead of reading from the textbook, she asked us to ___2___ a short play in groups. My group chose to perform a story about a lost puppy. I was ___3___ to play the main character, and I had to speak all my lines in English.

At first, I felt very nervous. My hands were shaking and I ___4___ forget my lines. But Mrs. Brown smiled at me and said, "Don't worry. Just try your best." Her words gave me ___5___. I took a deep breath and started to speak. To my surprise, I remembered everything and spoke quite fluently.

After our performance, Mrs. Brown said we all did a wonderful job. She especially praised our ___6___ and encouraged us to speak more English outside the classroom. She told us that making mistakes is part of learning and we should never be ___7___ of trying.

That class made me love English even more. I now understand that learning a language is not just about ___8___ grammar rules—it's also about using it in real situations. I hope we can have more classes like this. I will ___9___ my best to improve my spoken English and one day I can communicate with people from different countries ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. same
2. A. write &emsp;&emsp; B. perform &emsp;&emsp; C. read &emsp;&emsp; D. copy
3. A. chosen &emsp;&emsp; B. allowed &emsp;&emsp; C. refused &emsp;&emsp; D. invited
4. A. feared &emsp;&emsp; B. hoped &emsp;&emsp; C. tried &emsp;&emsp; D. preferred
5. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. sadness
6. A. pronunciation &emsp;&emsp; B. spelling &emsp;&emsp; C. handwriting &emsp;&emsp; D. reading
7. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. sure &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
8. A. breaking &emsp;&emsp; B. remembering &emsp;&emsp; C. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; D. changing
9. A. make &emsp;&emsp; B. take &emsp;&emsp; C. try &emsp;&emsp; D. get
10. A. carefully &emsp;&emsp; B. nervously &emsp;&emsp; C. freely &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她有不一样的教学方式。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"相同的"。外教用游戏和故事，是 different 的方式。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她让我们分组表演短剧。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"表演"；C 项意为"读"；D 项意为"抄写"。表演戏剧用 perform。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被选为扮演主角。A 项意为"被选中"；B 项意为"被允许"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"被邀请"。play the main character 说明被 chosen。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我害怕会忘词。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"希望"；C 项意为"尝试"；D 项意为"更喜欢"。nervous 时 fear forgetting。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话给了我勇气。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"悲伤"。Don't worry 给的是 courage。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她特别表扬了我们的发音。A 项意为"发音"；B 项意为"拼写"；C 项意为"书写"；D 项意为"阅读"。英语口语课强调 pronunciation。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不应该害怕尝试。A 项意为"自豪的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"确定的"；D 项意为"疲惫的"。be afraid of 意为"害怕"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学语言不仅是记住语法规则。A 项意为"打破"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"改变"。学习语法需要 remembering。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会尽全力提高口语。A 项 make one's best 不存在；B 项 take one's best 不存在；C 项 try one's best 意为"尽全力"；D 项 get one's best 不存在。try my best 为固定搭配。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：将来能自如地与外国人交流。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"紧张地"；C 项意为"自由地/流利地"；D 项意为"缓慢地"。communicate freely 表示流畅交流。故选 C。
