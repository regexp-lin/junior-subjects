# 中考英语完形填空：学做纸浆画

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, I learned how to make paper pulp paintings at a community art center. It was a new experience for me, and I was ___1___ to try it.

The teacher first explained that we needed to tear old newspapers into small pieces and soak ___2___ in water overnight. The next day, we would mix the paper with glue to make a kind of pulp. I had never thought that waste paper could be ___3___ into something beautiful.

When I started my first painting, I chose to make a sunset over the sea. I mixed different colors into the pulp—red and orange for the sky, blue for the ocean. The most important thing was to apply the pulp ___4___ on the cardboard. If I put too much in one place, it would take a long time to dry.

I worked on my painting for about two hours. Sometimes the pulp didn't stick well and I had to ___5___ more glue. The teacher told me that would help it stick better. Finally, I finished my artwork. I had to wait for it to dry ___6___ I could take it home.

The next day, when I went to pick up my painting, I was surprised by how good it looked. The colors blended together ___7___, and the texture was interesting. My mom said it was creative and wanted to hang it in our living room. I felt very ___8___ of my work.

Making paper pulp paintings taught me that we can create something ___9___ from ordinary materials. I also learned that art doesn't always require expensive supplies—sometimes the best creations come from simple things. I plan to make more paper pulp paintings and maybe give ___10___ as gifts to my friends.

## 选项

1. A. afraid &emsp;&emsp; B. tired &emsp;&emsp; C. eager &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
2. A. them &emsp;&emsp; B. it &emsp;&emsp; C. us &emsp;&emsp; D. him
3. A. thrown &emsp;&emsp; B. turned &emsp;&emsp; C. put &emsp;&emsp; D. broken
4. A. thickly &emsp;&emsp; B. evenly &emsp;&emsp; C. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly
5. A. remove &emsp;&emsp; B. add &emsp;&emsp; C. change &emsp;&emsp; D. finish
6. A. before &emsp;&emsp; B. after &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. until
7. A. badly &emsp;&emsp; B. terribly &emsp;&emsp; C. nicely &emsp;&emsp; D. strangely
8. A. ashamed &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. nervous
9. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. cheap &emsp;&emsp; C. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; D. heavy
10. A. it &emsp;&emsp; B. them &emsp;&emsp; C. one &emsp;&emsp; D. some

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我渴望尝试。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"疲惫的"；C 项意为"渴望的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。学习新技能用 eager to try。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把旧报纸撕成小块后浸泡在水中过夜。A 项意为"它们"；B 项意为"它"；C 项意为"我们"；D 项意为"他"。指代 small pieces（复数）用 them。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：废纸可以变成美丽的东西。A 项意为"被扔掉"；B 项意为"被转变"；C 项意为"被放"；D 项意为"被打碎"。be turned into 意为"被转变为"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最重要的是要均匀地涂抹纸浆。A 项意为"厚厚地"；B 项意为"均匀地"；C 项意为"粗心地"；D 项意为"快速地"。涂抹要 evenly 才美观。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时纸浆粘不好，需要再添加胶水。A 项意为"移除"；B 项意为"添加"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"完成"。add more glue 意为"添加更多胶水"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须等它干透才能带回家。A 项意为"在……之前"；B 项意为"在……之后"；C 项意为"当……时"；D 项意为"直到"。take it home before it dries 逻辑错误；应为 wait...before 或 until。此处"等它干才能带回家"用 before 表示"在能带回家之前必须等它干"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：颜色融合得很好。A 项意为"糟糕地"；B 项意为"可怕地"；C 项意为"很好地"；D 项意为"奇怪地"。效果好用 nicely。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我对自己的作品感到很自豪。A 项意为"羞愧的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"紧张的"。作品被夸奖用 proud。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用普通材料也能创造出美丽的东西。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"美丽的"；D 项意为"重的"。艺术创作 produce 的是 beautiful 作品。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：多做纸浆画送给朋友当礼物。A 项 it 指单数；B 项 them 指复数；C 项 one 指一个；D 项 some 可指一些。more paintings 用 them 指代。故选 B。
