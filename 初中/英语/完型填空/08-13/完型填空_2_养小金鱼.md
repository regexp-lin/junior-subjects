# 中考英语完形填空：养小金鱼

## 完形填空原文

For my birthday, my father bought me a small fish tank with five goldfish. I was very happy and promised to take good care of them. But I soon learned that keeping fish was not as ___1___ as I had thought.

The fish tank needed to be cleaned regularly. I had to change the water once a week. The first time I did it, I poured tap water directly into the tank. My father stopped me and said, "Tap water contains chlorine. You need to ___2___ the water for a day before putting it in the tank. Otherwise, it might harm the fish." I had no idea that such a small ___3___ could make a big difference.

Feeding the fish was also important. My father said I should not give them too much food. "A little each time is enough," he said. "If you give them too much, the ___4___ food will dirty the water and make the fish sick." I used to think that more food meant more love, but I was wrong. I learned to feed them only a small pinch of food twice a day.

One morning, I noticed that one fish was swimming slowly and staying at the bottom of the tank. It did not ___5___ when I tapped on the glass. I was worried. I quickly searched online and found that the fish might be sick. I told my father, and we went to the pet shop. The shop owner said the fish might have too much ammonia in the water. He gave us some medicine and advised us to clean the tank more often. We followed his ___6___, and after a few days, the fish became active again. I was so relieved!

Taking care of the fish taught me responsibility. I had to remember to feed them, clean the tank, and watch for any signs of ___7___. Sometimes I wanted to skip the cleaning, but I knew the fish depended on me. I could not let them down.

Now my goldfish are healthy and swimming happily. When I look at them, I feel a sense of ___8___. They are not just pets — they are living creatures that need our care and attention. I have learned that every life deserves respect, no matter how small. I will continue to take good care of them and ___9___ what I have learned to others who want to keep fish. Responsible pet ownership is a ___10___ we should all understand.

## 选项

1. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. expensive &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
2. A. drink &emsp;&emsp; B. heat &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. colour
3. A. detail &emsp;&emsp; B. fish &emsp;&emsp; C. tank &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
4. A. fresh &emsp;&emsp; B. left &emsp;&emsp; C. cooked &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
5. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. swim &emsp;&emsp; C. respond &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
6. A. advice &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. fish &emsp;&emsp; D. water
7. A. hunger &emsp;&emsp; B. illness &emsp;&emsp; C. joy &emsp;&emsp; D. growth
8. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. pride &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
9. A. sell &emsp;&emsp; B. share &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
10. A. hobby &emsp;&emsp; B. lesson &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. joke

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：养鱼并不像想的那么___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"昂贵的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。原以为简单实际不然，"easy"与后文形成对比。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：需要把水___一天再倒入鱼缸。A 项意为"喝"；B 项意为"加热"；C 项意为"放置"；D 项意为"染色"。"leave the water"指静置让氯气挥发。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不知道这样的小___会有这么大影响。A 项意为"细节"；B 项意为"鱼"；C 项意为"鱼缸"；D 项意为"礼物"。静置水的细节很重要，"detail"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___的食物会弄脏水。A 项意为"新鲜的"；B 项意为"剩余的"；C 项意为"煮过的"；D 项意为"干净的"。吃不完的剩余饲料污染水质，"left"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我敲玻璃时它没有___。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"游泳"；C 项意为"反应"；D 项意为"休息"。"respond"意为有反应。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们按他的___做了。A 项意为"建议"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"鱼"；D 项意为"水"。"follow one's advice"意为听从建议。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：注意任何___的迹象。A 项意为"饥饿"；B 项意为"生病"；C 项意为"快乐"；D 项意为"生长"。鱼生病需要及时发现，"illness"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有一种___感。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"愤怒"。把鱼养好感到自豪，"pride"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会把学到的___给想养鱼的人。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"忘"。"share"意为分享经验。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：负责任养宠是我们都应懂的___。A 项意为"爱好"；B 项意为"道理"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"笑话"。"lesson"意为道理、教训。故选 B。
