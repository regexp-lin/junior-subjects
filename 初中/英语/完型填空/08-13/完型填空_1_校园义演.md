# 中考英语完形填空：校园义演

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school held a charity performance. The money raised would help buy books for children in poor areas. Many students signed up to take part. I joined the dance group with my friends.

We had been practising for three weeks. Our dance was to a pop song that we all liked. At first, we could not move in the same ___1___. Some of us were faster, some slower. Our teacher said we needed to practise until we became one team. We met every afternoon after class and danced until our legs ___2___. Gradually, we learned to match each other's movements.

On the day of the performance, I woke up feeling nervous. What if I made a mistake in front of the whole school? My mother said, "Just do your best. The audience will ___3___ your effort." Her words helped me relax a little.

The hall was full of students and teachers. When our turn came, we walked onto the stage. The music started, and we began to dance. I tried to focus on every move and not think about the audience. Halfway through, I almost ___4___ a step, but I quickly caught myself and continued. Nobody seemed to notice.

When the music ended, the audience clapped loudly. We bowed and walked off the stage. I felt a wave of relief and ___5___. We had done it! Our teacher gave us a thumbs up from the side of the stage.

There were many other performances — singing, drama, and magic tricks. A boy from Grade 8 played the piano beautifully. A girl recited a poem about friendship. Everyone had worked hard to make the show a ___6___.

At the end, the headmaster announced that we had raised over five thousand yuan. He thanked all the participants and said that our school was ___7___ of us. I felt proud to have contributed something. The performance was not just about raising money — it was about working together and sharing our talents with others. It taught me that when we ___8___ our time and effort for a good cause, we can make a real difference. I hope our school will ___9___ more events like this. Every student has something to ___10___, and such activities help us discover our own strengths.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最初我们___不一致。A 项意为"方向"；B 项意为"方式"；C 项意为"空间"；D 项意为"速度"。跳舞动作节奏不统一，"way"指方式。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：练到腿都___。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"疼"；C 项意为"动"；D 项意为"生长"。"ached"表示酸痛。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：观众会___你的努力。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"欣赏"；C 项意为"忽视"；D 项意为"拒绝"。"appreciate"意为欣赏、认可。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我差点___一步。A 项意为"错过/出错"；B 项意为"找到"；C 项意为"重复"；D 项意为"教"。"miss a step"意为漏跳或跳错一步。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到如释重负和___。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"愤怒"。表演成功感到骄傲，"pride"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家努力让演出成为___。A 项意为"失败"；B 项意为"成功"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"错误"。"a success"意为大获成功。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学校为我们感到___。A 项意为"害怕"；B 项意为"疲倦"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"无聊"。"be proud of"意为"以……为傲"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我们为善事___时间和精力。A 项意为"节省"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"奉献"；D 项意为"失去"。"give"表示付出、奉献。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望学校多___类似活动。A 项意为"取消"；B 项意为"组织"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。"organise"意为组织。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每位学生都有可以___的。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"失去"；D 项意为"忘记"。"share"指分享才艺。故选 B。
