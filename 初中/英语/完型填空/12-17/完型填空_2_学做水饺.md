# 中考英语完形填空：学做水饺

## 完形填空原文

Last winter vacation, my grandma came to stay with us. She said she would teach me how to make dumplings. I was very ___1___ because I had never made them before.

First, Grandma showed me how to make the dough. She mixed flour and water, then ___2___ the dough for several minutes. "The dough needs to be soft but not too sticky," she said. I tried to do the same, but my dough was too wet at first. Grandma helped me add some ___3___ flour, and it became better.

Next came the filling. We used pork, cabbage and some seasonings. Grandma taught me to chop the vegetables ___4___ and mix everything well. "The filling should taste good," she smiled. I could not ___5___ to try it!

The most interesting part was wrapping the dumplings. I placed some filling in the middle of a wrapper, then folded and pinched the edges. But my first few dumplings looked ___6___. Some were too fat, and some had holes. Grandma did not laugh at me. ___7___, she showed me again and again until I could do it well.

When the dumplings were cooked, we sat down and ate together. They tasted ___8___! I felt so proud of myself. Making dumplings is not easy, but it is a lot of ___9___. I will make dumplings for my family again. I hope Grandma will be ___10___ with me next time.

## 选项

1. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
2. A. cut &emsp;&emsp; B. cooked &emsp;&emsp; C. kneaded &emsp;&emsp; D. washed
3. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. most &emsp;&emsp; D. few
4. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. finely &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
5. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. afford &emsp;&emsp; D. refuse
6. A. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. perfect &emsp;&emsp; D. similar
7. A. However &emsp;&emsp; B. Instead &emsp;&emsp; C. Besides &emsp;&emsp; D. Also
8. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. delicious &emsp;&emsp; C. sour &emsp;&emsp; D. bitter
9. A. work &emsp;&emsp; B. fun &emsp;&emsp; C. trouble &emsp;&emsp; D. worry
10. A. strict &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. pleased &emsp;&emsp; D. sad

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我非常兴奋，因为我从来没做过饺子。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。第一次学做饺子，心情应是"兴奋"，故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她把面粉和水混合，然后揉面团揉了几分钟。A 项意为"切"；B 项意为"煮"；C 项意为"揉（面）"；D 项意为"洗"。制作面团需要"揉面"，knead 专指揉面团，故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词/比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶帮我多加了一些面粉，就好多了。面团太湿需要加面粉，more flour"更多面粉"符合语境。less 更少不合逻辑；most/few 语法不当，故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶教我蔬菜要切得很细，并且把所有东西搅拌均匀。A 项意为"快地"；B 项意为"细心地、切得细"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"慢地"。做饺子馅时蔬菜要切得细碎，finely 最合适，故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我迫不及待想尝一尝！cannot wait to do sth. 为固定搭配，意为"迫不及待做某事"。help/afford/refuse 在此搭配中语义不通，故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但我最初包的几个饺子看起来很奇怪。A 项意为"漂亮的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"完美的"；D 项意为"相似的"。后文说"有的太胖，有的有洞"，说明样子"奇怪"，故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词/衔接词
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶没有笑话我，而是反复给我示范直到我学会。Instead 表示"相反地、而是"，承接前文"没有笑话"与后文"反复示范"的对比。However 表转折但语气不同；Besides/Also 表递进，故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：饺子吃起来很好吃！A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美味的"；C 项意为"酸的"；D 项意为"苦的"。作者感到自豪，说明味道好，delicious 符合，故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做饺子不容易，但很有乐趣。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"乐趣"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"担忧"。a lot of fun 为常用搭配，表示"很有乐趣"，与前文 not easy 形成对比，故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望下次奶奶会对我满意。be pleased with 为固定搭配，意为"对……满意"。A 项 strict"严格"；B 项 angry"生气"；D 项 sad"难过"均不符合"期待下次再做"的积极语境，故选 C。
