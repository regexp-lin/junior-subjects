# 中考英语完形填空：晨跑途中的小狗

## 完形填空原文

I started morning running to prepare for the school sports meeting. Every day at 6 a.m., I would put on my sneakers and head to the park. It was ___1___ at first — I often wanted to give up and go back to bed.

One morning, as I was running along the path, I heard a weak sound. I stopped and found a small dog under a bench. It looked hungry and scared. I ___2___ down and touched its head gently. The dog didn't run away. Instead, it wagged its tail.

I had no food with me, so I ran home, got some bread and milk, and ___3___ back. The dog ate quickly. From that day on, it waited for me every morning at the same spot. We became friends. I would run first, and the dog would ___4___ me. Sometimes it ran ahead and looked back as if to say, "Hurry up!"

My morning runs were no longer boring. I had a reason to get up early. I even ___5___ faster because I didn't want to keep the dog waiting. A month later, I came second in the 800-meter race. My teacher asked, "What's your secret?" I smiled and thought of my little running ___6___.

One day, the dog wasn't there. I waited for twenty minutes. Finally, I saw a woman walking toward me with the dog on a leash. "Is this your dog?" she asked. "I found it lost in the street last week. I've been looking for its owner."

I explained how we had met. The woman said the dog had run away from its previous home because it was treated badly. She had decided to ___7___ it. "Would you like to visit him sometimes?" she asked.

"Of course!" I said. I was sad to say goodbye, but I was ___8___ that the dog had found a loving home. That little dog taught me something important: helping others can also help ourselves. Its friendship made me a ___9___ runner and a better person. Sometimes the best things in life come when we ___10___ expect them — like a stray dog on an early morning path.

## 选项

1. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. fun &emsp;&emsp; D. safe
2. A. lay &emsp;&emsp; B. sat &emsp;&emsp; C. bent &emsp;&emsp; D. fell
3. A. came &emsp;&emsp; B. went &emsp;&emsp; C. returned &emsp;&emsp; D. walked
4. A. follow &emsp;&emsp; B. lead &emsp;&emsp; C. watch &emsp;&emsp; D. help
5. A. walked &emsp;&emsp; B. moved &emsp;&emsp; C. grew &emsp;&emsp; D. ran
6. A. friend &emsp;&emsp; B. teacher &emsp;&emsp; C. partner &emsp;&emsp; D. pet
7. A. keep &emsp;&emsp; B. buy &emsp;&emsp; C. sell &emsp;&emsp; D. leave
8. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. glad &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
9. A. slower &emsp;&emsp; B. faster &emsp;&emsp; C. weaker &emsp;&emsp; D. lazier
10. A. always &emsp;&emsp; B. never &emsp;&emsp; C. seldom &emsp;&emsp; D. least

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始很___，我常常想放弃回去睡觉。A 项意为"容易"；B 项意为"困难"；C 项意为"有趣"；D 项意为"安全"。"hard"与后文"often wanted to give up"（常想放弃）相呼应。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我___下来轻轻摸了摸它的头。A 项意为"躺"；B 项意为"坐"；C 项意为"弯、俯身"；D 项意为"摔倒"。"bend down"（弯腰、蹲下）表示俯身去接触地上的小狗。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我跑回家拿了面包和牛奶，然后___来了。A 项意为"来"；B 项意为"去"；C 项意为"返回"；D 项意为"走"。"return"强调回到原来的地点，从家回到公园。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我先跑，小狗会___我。A 项意为"跟随"；B 项意为"带领"；C 项意为"观看"；D 项意为"帮助"。小狗跟在"我"身后一起跑，用"follow"（跟随）。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我甚至___得更快了。A 项意为"走"；B 项意为"移动"；C 项意为"成长"；D 项意为"跑"。语境是晨跑，且有"faster"修饰，应填"ran"（跑）。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想起了我的小跑步___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"伙伴"；D 项意为"宠物"。"running partner"（跑步伙伴）指一起跑步的搭档。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她决定___它。A 项意为"收留、养"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"离开"。"keep"此处意为"收养、养"，女士捡到流浪狗后决定收养。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___小狗找到了有爱的家。A 项意为"难过"；B 项意为"生气"；C 项意为"高兴"；D 项意为"担心"。"glad"与"found a loving home"相呼应。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它的陪伴让我成为更___的跑步者。A 项意为"更慢的"；B 项意为"更快的"；C 项意为"更弱的"；D 项意为"更懒的"。前文提到"came second"和"ran faster"，可知是"更快的"（faster）跑者。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：生活中最好的事往往在我们最不___的时候出现。A 项意为"总是"；B 项意为"从不"；C 项意为"很少"；D 项意为"最少"。"least expect"（最想不到）是固定搭配，与"意外惊喜"相呼应。故选 D。
