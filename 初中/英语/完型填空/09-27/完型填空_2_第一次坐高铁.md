# 中考英语完形填空：第一次坐高铁

## 完形填空原文

During the National Day holiday, my family planned to visit my uncle in another city. We decided to take the high-speed rail for the first time. I had only taken ordinary trains before, so I was very ___1___ about the experience.

We arrived at the railway station two hours before departure. The station was huge and modern. There were clear signs everywhere. We found our platform easily and waited for the train. When the white bullet train ___2___ into the station, I couldn't believe how smooth and quiet it was. It looked like a silver arrow.

We got on the train and found our seats. The carriage was clean and comfortable. The seats were much more ___3___ than those on ordinary trains. There was plenty of legroom. I put my bag on the overhead rack and sat down.

The train started to move. At first, I didn't even notice we were moving — it was so smooth! Soon we were ___4___ at over 300 kilometres per hour. I looked out of the window. The scenery flew by so fast that the trees and buildings became a blur. It was amazing and a little ___5___.

About an hour later, we arrived at our destination. The journey that used to take five hours by ordinary train now took only one hour. My father said, "The high-speed rail has ___6___ changed the way we travel in China. It's one of our country's great achievements."

During the trip, I also noticed that the train was very quiet. Passengers spoke softly or used earphones. Everyone tried to keep the ___7___ comfortable for others. I thought this was a good habit that we should all follow.

I enjoyed my first high-speed rail experience very much. It was fast, comfortable, and convenient. I hope to take the high-speed rail again when we travel. Perhaps next time we can go even ___8___ — to Beijing or Shanghai. The high-speed rail network in China connects so many cities. It has made our country feel much ___9___. I'm proud of our country's development and look forward to more ___10___ journeys in the future.

7. A. temperature &emsp;&emsp; B. environment &emsp;&emsp; C. price &emsp;&emsp; D. speed

9. A. larger &emsp;&emsp; B. smaller &emsp;&emsp; C. richer &emsp;&emsp; D. poorer

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前只坐过普通火车，所以对这次体验很好奇。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"好奇的"；C 项意为"疲劳的"；D 项意为"悲伤的"。第一次体验会 curious。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当白色动车驶入车站时，我不敢相信它如此平稳安静。A 项意为"冲"；B 项意为"滑"；C 项意为"驶入、拉"；D 项意为"驾驶"。火车 pull into station 意为"进站"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：座位比普通火车舒适得多。A 项意为"硬的"；B 项意为"宽的"；C 项意为"小的"；D 项意为"舒适的"。高铁座位更 comfortable。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很快我们就以每小时300多公里的速度行驶。A 项意为"走"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"旅行、行驶"；D 项意为"站"。travelling at ... speed 意为"以……速度行驶"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：景色飞逝令人惊叹，也有点可怕。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"可怕的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"容易的"。高速下景色模糊可能让人感到 scary。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：高铁完全改变了我们在中国的出行方式。A 项意为"缓慢地"；B 项意为"完全地"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"粗心地"。completely changed 意为"彻底改变"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都努力为他人保持舒适的环境。A 项意为"温度"；B 项意为"环境"；C 项意为"价格"；D 项意为"速度"。keep the environment comfortable 意为"保持环境舒适"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许下次我们可以去更远的地方——北京或上海。A 项意为"更慢"；B 项意为"更快"；C 项意为"更远"；D 项意为"更近"。go farther 意为"去更远"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：高铁网络让我们的国家感觉小了很多。A 项意为"更大"；B 项意为"更小"；C 项意为"更富有"；D 项意为"更贫穷"。交通便利使距离感缩短，国家feel smaller。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：期待未来有更多精彩的旅程。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"精彩的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。wonderful journeys 意为"精彩的旅程"。故选 C。
