# 中考英语完形填空：班级种植角

## 完形填空原文

Our class has a small planting corner by the window. It was our head teacher's idea. She said that taking care of plants could teach us responsibility and help us learn about ___1___. We all agreed and decided to start our own "mini garden".

Each group was given a small pot and some seeds. We could choose what to plant. My group chose to grow green beans because they grow fast and are easy to ___2___. We filled the pot with soil, planted the seeds, and watered them. Then we placed our pot in the sunny corner and waited.

For the first few days, nothing happened. Some classmates were ___3___ and thought the seeds might be dead. But our teacher told us to be patient. "Seeds need time to grow. Just keep watering them and give them enough ___4___," she said.

About a week later, tiny green shoots appeared! We were so excited. We took turns to water the plants every day. We also moved the pot a little so that the shoots could get sunlight from different ___5___. Slowly, the plants grew taller and grew leaves.

One day, we noticed that one plant was turning yellow. We asked our teacher for help. She said we might have given it too much water. "Plants need water, but they also need the soil to be ___6___ sometimes. Don't water them every hour!" She laughed. We followed her advice and the plant gradually recovered.

Now our planting corner is full of green plants. We have beans, sunflowers, and even a small tomato plant. Taking care of them has become part of our daily routine. We have learned that growth takes time and ___7___. We have also learned to work together — when someone is ___8___, others will help water the plants.

Our planting corner has made our classroom more lively and beautiful. More importantly, it has taught us to appreciate nature and the ___9___ of life. I hope our plants will keep growing and maybe one day we can ___10___ the vegetables we grow. That would be truly wonderful!

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：照顾植物能教我们责任感，帮助我们了解自然。A 项意为"数学"；B 项意为"历史"；C 项意为"自然"；D 项意为"音乐"。种植与 nature 相关。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们组选了种绿豆，因为它们长得快，容易照料。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"照顾"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"吃"。care for 意为"照料"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些同学担心种子可能死了。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。几天没发芽会 worried。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：继续浇水，给它们充足的阳光。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"阳光"；C 项意为"空间"；D 项意为"空气"。植物生长需要 sunlight。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们稍微移动花盆，让幼苗从不同角度获得阳光。A 项意为"形状"；B 项意为"角度"；C 项意为"尺寸"；D 项意为"颜色"。from different angles 意为"从不同角度"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：植物也需要土壤有时干一点。A 项意为"湿的"；B 项意为"干的"；C 项意为"脏的"；D 项意为"软的"。水太多时需让土壤 dry。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学到了成长需要时间和耐心。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"速度"；C 项意为"耐心"；D 项意为"钱"。与前文 be patient 呼应，patience 正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当有人忙的时候，其他人会帮忙浇水。A 项意为"忙碌的"；B 项意为"空闲的"；C 项意为"生病的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。互相帮助是当某人 busy 时。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会我们欣赏自然和生命之美。A 项意为"长度"；B 项意为"美"；C 项意为"结束"；D 项意为"困难"。the beauty of life 意为"生命之美"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望有一天我们能品尝自己种的蔬菜。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"品尝"；D 项意为"扔"。taste the vegetables 意为"品尝蔬菜"。故选 C。
