# 中考英语完形填空：做纸飞机比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last Thursday, our PE teacher organised a paper plane competition for our grade. He said it would be fun and would also teach us about the basic ___1___ of flight. We were all excited and could not wait to start.

First, we had to make our own paper planes. The teacher showed us different folding methods. Some planes were designed to fly far, others to fly for a long ___2___. I chose to make a plane that could fly straight and far. I folded the paper carefully, making sure each fold was sharp and even. A small mistake could ___3___ the plane's flight.

When we finished making our planes, we went to the playground. The weather was perfect — sunny with a gentle breeze. We lined up and took turns to throw our planes. The rule was simple: the plane that flew the ___4___ would win.

My first throw was not very good. The plane turned to the side and landed after only a few metres. I was a little ___5___. But I watched how other students threw their planes. Some threw with more force, others threw at a certain angle. I learned that the way you throw ___6___ a lot.

I adjusted my plane slightly and tried again. This time I threw it at a slight upward angle with a smooth motion. The plane flew straight and glided through the air. It landed about fifteen metres away! I was so happy. ___7___, it wasn't the longest flight — a boy from Class 3 won with twenty metres. But I had improved a lot from my first try.

The competition was not only about winning. We had a great time together. We laughed when some planes flew in ___8___ directions or made funny loops. The teacher said that sometimes the process of trying and learning was more valuable than the result.

I enjoyed the paper plane competition very much. It reminded me that we can have fun with ___9___ things. We don't always need expensive toys or equipment. A piece of paper, some creativity, and a willingness to try can bring us joy. I might even make paper planes at home and ___10___ with my younger brother. Who knows? We might hold our own family competition!

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这既能带来乐趣，也能教我们飞行的基本原理。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"原理"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"问题"。basic principles of flight 意为"飞行基本原理"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些飞机设计成飞得远，有些设计成飞得久。A 项意为"方式"；B 项意为"距离"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"高度"。fly for a long time 意为"飞行时间长"。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小错误会影响飞机的飞行。A 项意为"改善"；B 项意为"影响"；C 项意为"记录"；D 项意为"重复"。mistakes can affect 意为"错误会影响"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：规则很简单：飞得最远的赢。A 项意为"最快"；B 项意为"最高"；C 项意为"最远"；D 项意为"最慢"。纸飞机比赛通常比飞得远。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：飞机只飞了几米就落地，我有点失望。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"失望的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"放松的"。飞得不好会 disappointed。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学到投掷方式很重要。A 项意为"重要"；B 项意为"花费"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"节省"。the way matters a lot 意为"方式很重要"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：然而，那不是飞得最远的——三班一个男生以二十米获胜。A 项意为"此外"；B 项意为"然而"；C 项意为"因此"；D 项意为"反而"。表示转折用 However。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些飞机朝错误的方向飞或转圈时我们大笑。A 项意为"错误的"；B 项意为"相同的"；C 项意为"正确的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。wrong directions 意为"错误方向"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它提醒我们简单的东西也能带来乐趣。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"现代的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"困难的"。纸张是 simple thing。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我可能在家做纸飞机和弟弟一起玩。A 项意为"争论"；B 项意为"玩耍"；C 项意为"打架"；D 项意为"学习"。play with 意为"和……一起玩"。故选 B。
