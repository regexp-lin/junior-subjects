# 中考英语完形填空：参加数学竞赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, I took part in a city-wide mathematics competition. It was my first time participating in such an event. I had always enjoyed maths and was good at it, but I knew the competition would be ___1___. Many excellent students from different schools would be there.

I spent two weeks preparing for the competition. Every day after school, I would ___2___ extra maths problems. My maths teacher, Mr. Wang, gave me some past papers and taught me some problem-solving skills. He said the key was not to memorize formulas but to understand the ___3___ behind each problem.

On the day of the competition, I woke up early. I felt both excited and nervous. My mother made me a good breakfast and said, "Just do your best. The ___4___ is not the most important thing. What matters is that you try." Her words made me feel better.

The competition was held in a large hall. There were about two hundred students. When the paper was handed out, I took a deep breath and began. The first few questions were not too difficult. I solved them ___5___. But then I met a problem that I couldn't figure out. I spent a long time on it and almost gave up. Then I remembered Mr. Wang's advice: "When you're stuck, try to look at the problem from a ___6___ angle." I tried a different method and finally found the answer.

When the results came out a week later, I had won a second prize. I was very happy, though not completely ___7___. I knew I could have done better on that difficult problem if I had had more time. But my parents and teachers were proud of me. They said that taking part was more important than winning.

The competition taught me many things. I learned that preparation is important, and so is staying calm under ___8___. I also learned that there are always smarter people than us, and we should learn from them. Most importantly, I learned to enjoy the process of ___9___ challenges rather than only focusing on the result.

I will continue to work hard on maths and maybe take part in more competitions in the future. Each competition is a chance to ___10___ ourselves and grow.

## 选项

1. A. easy &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. challenging &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
2. A. copy &emsp;&emsp; B. practise &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
3. A. answer &emsp;&emsp; B. idea &emsp;&emsp; C. length &emsp;&emsp; D. number
4. A. score &emsp;&emsp; B. time &emsp;&emsp; C. place &emsp;&emsp; D. paper
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
6. A. same &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. wrong &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
7. A. surprised &emsp;&emsp; B. satisfied &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
8. A. pressure &emsp;&emsp; B. freedom &emsp;&emsp; C. silence &emsp;&emsp; D. sunshine
9. A. avoiding &emsp;&emsp; B. facing &emsp;&emsp; C. forgetting &emsp;&emsp; D. fearing
10. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. challenge &emsp;&emsp; C. please &emsp;&emsp; D. blame

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我知道竞赛会很有挑战性。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"有挑战性的"；D 项意为"简单的"。城市竞赛会 challenging。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天放学后我都会额外练习数学题。A 项意为"抄写"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"忘记"。prepare 需要 practise。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键不是死记公式，而是要理解每道题背后的思路。A 项意为"答案"；B 项意为"想法、思路"；C 项意为"长度"；D 项意为"数字"。understand the idea 意为"理解思路"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：分数不是最重要的，重要的是你努力了。A 项意为"分数"；B 项意为"时间"；C 项意为"名次"；D 项意为"试卷"。竞赛中 score 常被看重。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：前几题不太难，我很快做出来了。A 项意为"缓慢地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"紧张地"。不难的题可 quickly 解决。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：卡住时，试试从不同角度看待问题。A 项意为"相同的"；B 项意为"不同的"；C 项意为"错误的"；D 项意为"简单的"。from a different angle 意为"换一个角度"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很高兴， though 不完全满意。A 项意为"惊讶的"；B 项意为"满意的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"疲劳的"。觉得还能更好，故 not completely satisfied。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：准备重要，在压力下保持冷静也很重要。A 项意为"压力"；B 项意为"自由"；C 项意为"沉默"；D 项意为"阳光"。竞赛场景有 pressure。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最重要的是学会享受面对挑战的过程。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"面对"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"害怕"。face challenges 意为"面对挑战"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次竞赛都是挑战自我、成长的机会。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"挑战"；C 项意为"取悦"；D 项意为"责备"。challenge ourselves 意为"挑战自我"。故选 B。
