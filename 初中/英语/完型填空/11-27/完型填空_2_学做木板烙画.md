# 中考英语完形填空：学做木板烙画

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, I went to a craft workshop to learn pyrography—the art of drawing on wood with a hot tool. The instructor said this traditional skill has a long history in China. I was curious and wanted to try it by ___1___.

First, we chose a piece of smooth wood. The instructor said the wood must be dry and clean. We practiced on small pieces before working on our real project. The tool looked like a pen but had a hot tip. We had to be very ___2___ because it could burn our fingers if we touched it carelessly.

I decided to draw a simple bamboo pattern. I held the tool gently and moved it slowly across the wood. At first, my lines were ___3___ and uneven. The instructor encouraged me to practice more. She said the key was to control the temperature and the ___4___ of the pen.

After about an hour of practice, my skills improved. The lines became smoother and I could create different shades by changing the pressure. I felt ___5___ when I saw my bamboo drawing taking shape. When I finished, I carefully wiped the wood with a cloth to remove any dust. The final work looked quite nice—not perfect, but it was ___6___ my own creation.

I took my pyrography work home and put it on my desk. My parents said it was a unique art form and they had never seen ___7___ like it before. Making pyrography taught me that patience and practice can help us master new skills. I plan to make more pieces and maybe give one to my best friend as a ___8___. This traditional art connects the past with the ___9___, and I am glad to be part of keeping it ___10___.

1. A. himself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. myself &emsp;&emsp; D. yourself
2. A. careless &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. happy
3. A. straight &emsp;&emsp; B. smooth &emsp;&emsp; C. shaky &emsp;&emsp; D. perfect
4. A. color &emsp;&emsp; B. size &emsp;&emsp; C. speed &emsp;&emsp; D. price
5. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
6. A. still &emsp;&emsp; B. already &emsp;&emsp; C. never &emsp;&emsp; D. hardly
7. A. nothing &emsp;&emsp; B. something &emsp;&emsp; C. everything &emsp;&emsp; D. anything
8. A. exam &emsp;&emsp; B. gift &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
9. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. present &emsp;&emsp; C. future &emsp;&emsp; D. morning
10. A. alive &emsp;&emsp; B. dead &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. broken

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很好奇想亲自试一试。A 项意为"他自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"我自己"；D 项意为"你自己"。主语是 I，用 by myself 表示亲自。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：必须非常小心，因为如果不小心碰到会烫伤手指。A 项意为"粗心的"；B 项意为"小心的"；C 项意为"累的"；D 项意为"开心的"。使用高温工具需 careful。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的线条颤抖且不均匀。A 项意为"直的"；B 项意为"平滑的"；C 项意为"颤抖的"；D 项意为"完美的"。新手线条 shaky。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：关键在于控制温度和运笔速度。A 项意为"颜色"；B 项意为"大小"；C 项意为"速度"；D 项意为"价格"。烙画需控制 speed。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到竹子图案成型我感到骄傲。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"生气的"。作品成型用 proud。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽不完美，但已是自己的创作。A 项意为"仍然"；B 项意为"已经"；C 项意为"从不"；D 项意为"几乎不"。强调是自己的作品用 already。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母说从未见过这样的东西。A 项意为"没有东西"；B 项意为"某物"；C 项意为"一切"；D 项意为"任何东西"。never seen something like it 表示从未见过类似之物。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：打算多做几件，也许送一个给好朋友当礼物。A 项意为"考试"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"课程"；D 项意为"错误"。送朋友用 as a gift。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这项传统艺术连接过去与未来。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"现在"；C 项意为"未来"；D 项意为"早上"。past 与 future 对应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很高兴能为传承这项技艺出一份力。A 项意为"活着的/延续的"；B 项意为"死的"；C 项意为"丢失的"；D 项意为"破碎的"。keep...alive 意为"使……延续"。故选 A。
