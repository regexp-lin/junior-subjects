# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校冬季长跑活动

## 完形填空原文

Our school held a winter long-distance running event last Friday. The weather was cold but sunny, which was ___1___ for running. All students from Grade 7 to 9 were encouraged to take part. I signed up with several friends because we wanted to challenge ourselves.

The race was three kilometers long. At the starting line, we did some warm-up exercises. The PE teacher told us to run at our own ___2___ and not to push too hard at the beginning. When the whistle blew, hundreds of students began to run. I tried to keep a steady pace and breathe ___3___.

About halfway through, my legs started to feel tired. I wanted to stop, but I saw older students running past me and some younger ones still trying their best. Their ___4___ encouraged me to keep going. I told myself, "Don't give up. You can do it." Step by step, I got closer to the finish line.

When I finally crossed the line, I was out of breath but very happy. A volunteer handed me a bottle of water and a warm towel. My friend Zhang Wei finished just before me. We hugged each other and ___5___ our success. The headmaster gave a short speech and said that everyone who completed the race was a ___6___.

This activity taught me that perseverance is the key to success. No matter how difficult the road is, we should never ___7___ our dreams. I hope to join more sports events ___8___. Running in winter not only makes our bodies stronger but also builds our willpower. I will keep running and become a ___9___ person. Every step we take brings us ___10___ to our goals.

## 选项

1. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
2. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. height &emsp;&emsp; C. weight &emsp;&emsp; D. age
3. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. deeply &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
4. A. words &emsp;&emsp; B. spirit &emsp;&emsp; C. clothes &emsp;&emsp; D. food
5. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. celebrated &emsp;&emsp; C. regretted &emsp;&emsp; D. ignored
6. A. loser &emsp;&emsp; B. winner &emsp;&emsp; C. stranger &emsp;&emsp; D. teacher
7. A. achieve &emsp;&emsp; B. give up &emsp;&emsp; C. remember &emsp;&emsp; D. find
8. A. in the past &emsp;&emsp; B. in the future &emsp;&emsp; C. at night &emsp;&emsp; D. at noon
9. A. healthier &emsp;&emsp; B. busier &emsp;&emsp; C. lazier &emsp;&emsp; D. older
10. A. farther &emsp;&emsp; B. slower &emsp;&emsp; C. closer &emsp;&emsp; D. higher

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：天气寒冷但晴朗，非常适合跑步。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"完美的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。晴朗冷天适合长跑用 perfect。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：体育老师让我们按自己的速度跑，开头不要太猛。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"高度"；C 项意为"体重"；D 项意为"年龄"。at one's own speed 表示按自己的节奏。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我尽量保持稳定配速并深呼吸。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"缓慢地"；C 项意为"深深地"；D 项意为"紧张地"。呼吸用 breathe deeply。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的精神鼓舞我继续前进。A 项意为"话语"；B 项意为"精神"；C 项意为"衣服"；D 项意为"食物"。他人坚持的精神鼓舞自己用 spirit。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们拥抱彼此，庆祝成功。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"庆祝"；C 项意为"后悔"；D 项意为"忽视"。完成比赛后 celebrated 成功。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长说完成比赛的每个人都是赢家。A 项意为"失败者"；B 项意为"赢家"；C 项意为"陌生人"；D 项意为"老师"。坚持完赛即 winner。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无论道路多难，我们都不应放弃梦想。A 项意为"实现"；B 项 give up 意为"放弃"；C 项意为"记住"；D 项意为"找到"。永不放弃梦想用 never give up。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望未来参加更多体育活动。A 项意为"在过去"；B 项 in the future 意为"在未来"；C 项意为"在晚上"；D 项意为"在中午"。将来参加用 in the future。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会坚持跑步，成为更健康的人。A 项意为"更健康的"；B 项意为"更忙的"；C 项意为"更懒的"；D 项意为"更年长的"。运动使人 healthier。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一步都让我们离目标更近。A 项意为"更远"；B 项意为"更慢"；C 项意为"更近"；D 项意为"更高"。brings us closer to goals 意为"离目标更近"。故选 C。
