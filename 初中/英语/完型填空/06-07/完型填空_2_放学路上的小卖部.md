# 中考英语完形填空：放学路上的小卖部

## 完形填空原文

On the way home from school, there was a small shop that Liu Ming passed every day. The owner was an old man named Grandpa Wang. His shop sold snacks, drinks, and school things. It was not big, but it was always ___1___ and warm. Many students liked to stop there after class.

One rainy day, Liu Ming forgot to bring his umbrella. He stood under a tree near the shop, getting wet. Grandpa Wang saw him and ___2___ him to come in. "Come inside, boy. You'll catch a cold if you stay in the rain." Liu Ming went in and dried himself. Grandpa Wang even gave him a cup of hot water. Liu Ming felt very ___3___ inside.

From then on, Liu Ming often visited the shop. Sometimes he bought something small. Sometimes he just went in to say hello. Grandpa Wang was always ___4___ to chat when students dropped by. He would tell stories about the old days. Liu Ming liked to listen. He learned that Grandpa Wang had run the shop for over twenty years. Many students had grown up and ___5___ the neighborhood, but they still came back to visit him when they had time.

One afternoon, Liu Ming noticed that Grandpa Wang looked tired. "Are you okay?" he asked. Grandpa Wang smiled and said, "I'm fine. I'm just getting old." Liu Ming thought for a moment and said, "Let me help you. I can ___6___ the heavy boxes onto the shelves after school." Grandpa Wang's eyes lit up. "That would be wonderful, thank you!"

After that, Liu Ming helped at the shop twice a week. He learned how to ___7___ customers and keep the shop tidy. Grandpa Wang was very grateful. He said, "A kind heart is the best ___8___ a person can have. Never lose it." Liu Ming understood. Helping others could bring real happiness. The small shop had become more than a place to buy things. It was a place where ___9___ and care met. Liu Ming would always remember those afternoons and the smile on Grandpa Wang's face. Sometimes the simplest acts of ___10___ can change both the giver and the receiver.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：店面不大，但总是___且温暖。A 项意为"干净的"；B 项意为"明亮的"；C 项意为"清晰的"；D 项意为"安静的"。小卖部"clean and warm"形容整洁舒适，吸引学生。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：王爷爷看见他，___他进来。A 项意为"要求"；B 项意为"邀请"；C 项意为"告诉"；D 项意为"允许"。"invited him to come in"表示热情邀请避雨，体现善意。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：刘明内心感到非常___。A 项意为"感激的"；B 项意为"高兴的"；C 项意为"温暖的"；D 项意为"放松的"。"felt very warm inside"指被老人的善意触动，心中涌起暖意。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（短语搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生路过时，王爷爷总是___聊天。A 项意为"高兴的"；B 项意为"愿意的、准备好的"；C 项意为"确定的"；D 项意为"空闲的"。"free to chat"指有空、乐意与人交谈，体现老人和蔼可亲。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：许多学生长大了，___了这片街区。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"经过"。"left the neighborhood"指毕业后搬离，但有空仍回来探望。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：放学后我可以帮你把重箱子___到架子上。A 项意为"携带、搬运"；B 项意为"移动、搬动"；C 项意为"举起"；D 项意为"带来"。"lift...onto the shelves"指举起重物放上货架，符合小店场景。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他学会了如何___顾客并保持店铺整洁。A 项意为"招待、服务"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"见面"；D 项意为"欢迎"。"serve customers"为商店场景常用表达。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：善良的心是一个人能拥有的最好___。A 项意为"礼物、天赋"；B 项意为"习惯"；C 项意为"技能"；D 项意为"品质"。"quality"指人的品质、特质，与"善良的心"相呼应。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是___与关爱相遇的地方。A 项意为"善意"；B 项意为"友谊"；C 项意为"信任"；D 项意为"尊重"。"kindness and care"与全文助人、感恩主题一致。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时最简单的___行为能改变给予者和接受者双方。A 项意为"给予"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"关心"。"acts of helping"概括刘明帮老人搬箱子、打理店铺等善举。故选 B。
