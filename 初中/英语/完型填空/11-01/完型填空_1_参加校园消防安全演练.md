# 中考英语完形填空：参加校园消防安全演练

## 完形填空原文

Last Tuesday, our school held a fire safety drill. It was the first time I had taken part in such a ___1___, so I felt both nervous and excited.

Before the drill began, our teacher explained the ___2___ to us. She told us to cover our mouths with wet towels and walk quickly to the playground. She also reminded us not to ___3___ back to take anything from our desks. Safety always comes first.

When the alarm rang, everyone stood up and moved towards the door ___4___. Some students were a little scared, but we helped each other. I held my friend's hand and we went downstairs together. It was important to stay ___5___ and follow the teacher's instructions.

We arrived at the playground in less than three minutes. The headmaster said we did a ___6___ job. Then a fireman came and showed us how to use a fire extinguisher. He said that knowing how to ___7___ ourselves in a fire is very important.

After the drill, we went back to our classroom. Our teacher asked us to ___8___ what we had learned today. I wrote in my notebook that I should always know where the nearest exit is. I also learned that we should never use the lift during a fire.

This drill taught me a lot. I used to think fire drills were not ___9___. Now I understand they can save our lives. I hope everyone takes fire safety ___10___.

1. A. meeting &emsp;&emsp; B. drill &emsp;&emsp; C. party &emsp;&emsp; D. competition
2. A. rules &emsp;&emsp; B. stories &emsp;&emsp; C. jokes &emsp;&emsp; D. games
3. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. come &emsp;&emsp; C. go &emsp;&emsp; D. give
4. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. quickly &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
5. A. calm &emsp;&emsp; B. silent &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. awake
6. A. poor &emsp;&emsp; B. great &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
7. A. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; B. teach &emsp;&emsp; C. protect &emsp;&emsp; D. introduce
8. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. copy &emsp;&emsp; C. record &emsp;&emsp; D. change
9. A. useful &emsp;&emsp; B. difficult &emsp;&emsp; C. popular &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
10. A. easily &emsp;&emsp; B. seriously &emsp;&emsp; C. happily &emsp;&emsp; D. quickly

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我第一次参加这样的演练。A 项意为"会议"；B 项意为"演练"；C 项意为"派对"；D 项意为"比赛"。fire safety drill 对应 drill。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师给我们解释了规则。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"游戏"。演练前讲 rules（注意事项）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她提醒我们不要回去拿桌上的东西。A 项意为"看"；B 项意为"来"；C 项意为"去"；D 项意为"给"。go back 意为"回去"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都站起来快速地向门口移动。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"安静地"。紧急疏散时需 quickly。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：保持冷静并听从老师的指示很重要。A 项意为"冷静的"；B 项意为"沉默的"；C 项意为"独自的"；D 项意为"醒着的"。stay calm 意为"保持冷静"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：校长说我们做得很好。A 项意为"差的"；B 项意为"好的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"简单的"。三分钟到达是 great job。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：知道如何在火灾中保护自己非常重要。A 项意为"享受"；B 项意为"教"；C 项意为"保护"；D 项意为"介绍"。protect ourselves 意为"保护自己"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师要求我们记录今天学到的内容。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"抄写"；C 项意为"记录"；D 项意为"改变"。record what we learned 意为"记录所学"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我过去认为消防演练没什么用。A 项意为"有用的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"流行的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。后文说 now I understand 说明之前认为 not useful。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望每个人都认真对待消防安全。A 项意为"容易地"；B 项意为"认真地"；C 项意为"快乐地"；D 项意为"快速地"。take...seriously 意为"认真对待"。故选 B。
