# 中考英语完形填空：加入学校社团

## 完形填空原文

At the beginning of each school year, our school holds a club fair. Dozens of clubs set up tables in the playground and try to attract new ___1___. This year, I decided to join one.

I walked around the fair and looked at the different options. There was a drama club, a photography club, a robotics club, a chess club and many more. Each table had colourful posters and enthusiastic students explaining what their club did. It was hard to ___2___ because everything looked interesting.

My friend suggested the basketball club, but I was not tall enough to play well. Another friend recommended the art club, but I could barely draw a straight line. Then I noticed a small table in the corner with a sign that read "School Newspaper Club." A boy was sitting there ___3___, with no one else around.

I walked over and asked, "What do you do in this club?" His eyes lit up. "We write articles, take photos and design the school newspaper. We report on school events, interview teachers and students, and ___4___ share important news with the whole school." It sounded exciting. I had always loved writing, and this seemed like a great chance to ___5___ my skills.

I signed up on the spot. At the first meeting, I met the other members. There were only eight of us, but everyone was passionate about writing and storytelling. Our advisor, Mr. Zhao, told us that running a newspaper required teamwork, creativity and ___6___. "You must meet deadlines, check your facts and always be fair in your reporting," he said.

My first assignment was to write about the school sports day. I interviewed athletes, took notes and wrote a five-hundred-word article. It took me three drafts before Mr. Zhao was ___7___. "Good writing requires rewriting," he said. I was tired but proud when I saw my article ___8___ in the school newspaper.

Joining the newspaper club was one of the best decisions I have made. It taught me to observe the world more carefully and to ___9___ my thoughts clearly. I have also made great friends who share my love for writing. If you have not joined a club yet, I strongly ___10___ it. You never know what talents you might discover.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多社团摆摊吸引新___。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"成员"；C 项意为"父母"；D 项意为"访客"。社团招新吸引的是新成员，"members"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很难___因为一切看起来都很有趣。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"拒绝"；C 项意为"决定"；D 项意为"休息"。选择太多难以决定，"decide"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词/形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个男孩___坐在那里，周围没有其他人。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"生气地"；C 项意为"骄傲地"；D 项意为"高兴地"。没有其他人在旁边，他独自一人，"alone"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们___和全校分享重要新闻。A 项意为"秘密地"；B 项意为"很少"；C 项意为"基本上"；D 项意为"从不"。报纸的核心功能就是分享新闻，"basically"表示"总的来说"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这似乎是___我的技能的好机会。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"发展/提高"；D 项意为"忘记"。通过写作来提高技能，"develop"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：办报纸需要团队合作、创造力和___。A 项意为"财富"；B 项意为"纪律"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"沉默"。后文提到按时交稿、核实事实，这些都需要纪律，"discipline"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写了三稿后赵老师才___。A 项意为"满意的"；B 项意为"惊讶的"；C 项意为"担忧的"；D 项意为"困惑的"。多次修改直到老师满意，"satisfied"正确。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到我的文章被___在校报上时很自豪。A 项意为"消失"；B 项意为"失败"；C 项意为"印刷/刊登"；D 项意为"丢失"。文章被刊登在报纸上，"printed"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它教会我更清晰地___我的想法。A 项意为"表达"；B 项意为"忽视"；C 项意为"抄写"；D 项意为"隐藏"。写作帮助表达思想，"express"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我强烈___加入社团。A 项意为"怀疑"；B 项意为"害怕"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"推荐"。分享好的经验并推荐给别人，"recommend"正确。故选 D。
