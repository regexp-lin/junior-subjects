# 中考英语完形填空：学校图书馆

## 完形填空原文

Our school library is my favourite place on campus. It is a three-storey building with large windows that let in plenty of natural ___1___. Every time I step inside, I feel a sense of peace and excitement — peace because it is so quiet, and excitement because of all the books waiting to be read.

The library has over twenty thousand books, covering every subject you can imagine. The first floor is for textbooks and reference materials. Students go there when they need to look up information for their homework or ___2___. The second floor has novels, short stories and poetry collections. This is where I spend most of my time. The third floor has magazines, newspapers and a computer room where students can search for information ___3___.

Mrs. Li, our librarian, is a kind woman in her fifties. She knows almost every book in the library and can always recommend the ___4___ one for you. When I told her I liked adventure stories, she introduced me to "Treasure Island" and "Robinson Crusoe." Both books became my favourites. She also started a "Book of the Month" programme, where she displays one recommended book each month with a short ___5___.

Last year, I volunteered to help in the library after school. My job was to return books to the correct shelves and help other students ___6___ what they were looking for. It was not always easy — sometimes students gave me only a vague description, like "a book with a blue cover about animals." But I enjoyed the challenge. It felt like solving a ___7___.

Through volunteering, I discovered that many students did not visit the library regularly. Some said they were too ___8___ with homework. Others said they preferred watching videos to reading books. I found this sad because books offer something that videos cannot — they make you use your imagination and think ___9___.

To encourage more students to read, our library held a reading challenge last semester. Students who read at least five books in a month received a certificate and a small prize. The response was amazing — over two hundred students participated. Many of them told me afterwards that they had not realised how enjoyable reading could be.

I believe that a good library is the ___10___ of a school. It is where knowledge lives and where minds grow. I hope more students will discover the joy of reading, just as I did.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大窗户让充足的自然___照进来。A 项意为"水"；B 项意为"空气"；C 项意为"光"；D 项意为"气体"。窗户透进的是自然光，"light"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生在那里查找作业或___所需的信息。A 项意为"课题/项目"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"餐饭"；D 项意为"旅行"。学生查阅参考资料是为了做作业或课题，"projects"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生可以___搜索信息。A 项意为"离线"；B 项意为"在线"；C 项意为"在外面"；D 项意为"地下"。电脑室可以上网搜索，"online"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她总能推荐___的书。A 项意为"最便宜的"；B 项意为"最重的"；C 项意为"最差的"；D 项意为"合适的"。图书管理员推荐适合你的书，"right"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她展示推荐书并配上简短的___。A 项意为"书评"；B 项意为"测试"；C 项意为"账单"；D 项意为"票"。推荐书配书评，"review"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助其他学生___他们要找的书。A 项意为"丢失"；B 项意为"隐藏"；C 项意为"找到"；D 项意为"扔"。帮读者找书，"find"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这就像解一个___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"谜题"；C 项意为"犯罪"；D 项意为"打架"。根据模糊描述找书像解谜，"puzzle"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些学生说他们作业太___了。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"空闲的"；C 项意为"忙碌的"；D 项意为"熟悉的"。"busy with"意为"忙于"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：书能让你发挥想象力和___思考。A 项意为"更少"；B 项意为"深入地"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"大声地"。阅读促进深入思考，"deeply"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（比喻）
**【解析】** 本句意为：好的图书馆是学校的___。A 项意为"心脏"；B 项意为"大门"；C 项意为"屋顶"；D 项意为"墙"。图书馆是学校核心，"heart"比喻最重要的部分。故选 A。
