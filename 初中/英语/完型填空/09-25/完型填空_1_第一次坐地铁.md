# 中考英语完形填空：第一次坐地铁

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my mother took me to the city centre by subway for the first time. I had always travelled by bus or by car, so I was very curious about the underground train. We walked to the nearest station, which was about ten minutes from our home.

When we entered the station, I was surprised by how clean and modern it was. Bright lights lit up the platform. Many people were waiting in line. My mother bought two tickets from a machine. She said we could also use a transport card, which was more ___1___. I put the ticket in my pocket carefully.

We went down the escalator to the platform. I saw the train tracks and the electronic board showing when the next train would ___2___. "Stand behind the yellow line," my mother reminded me. "It's dangerous to get too close to the edge." I nodded and stepped back.

When the train arrived, the doors opened automatically. We got on and found two empty seats. The train was quite ___3___ — there were passengers reading, looking at their phones, or just sitting quietly. The train started to move. It was much faster than I had expected. Through the windows, I could see the dark tunnel and the dim lights passing by. It felt a little ___4___, but also exciting.

After three stops, we got off. We followed the signs to find the exit. My mother said that in big cities, the subway was the best way to avoid traffic jams. "During rush hour, cars move very slowly, but the subway is always ___5___," she explained.

We did some shopping and had lunch in the city centre. On our way back, we took the subway again. This time I felt more ___6___. I even helped an old lady find her way when she looked lost.

My first subway ride was a memorable experience. I learned that the subway is not only convenient but also environmentally ___7___, because it uses electricity instead of petrol. I hope that in the future, more cities will have subways so that people can travel more ___8___.

I'm looking forward to taking the subway again. Maybe next time I can ___9___ the route myself and travel alone. It would be a big step towards becoming more ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我们也可以用交通卡，那样更方便。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"方便的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。交通卡比单次购票方便，convenient 正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到电子屏显示下一班车什么时候到达。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"到达"；C 项意为"停止"；D 项意为"打破"。站台显示屏显示列车到达时间，arrive 正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：地铁里相当拥挤——有人在看书、看手机，或安静地坐着。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"拥挤的"；C 项意为"脏的"；D 项意为"旧的"。城市地铁通常 crowded。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：穿过黑暗的隧道有点可怕，但也很刺激。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"可怕的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"容易的"。首次在隧道中乘车可能感到 scary。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：高峰时段汽车很慢，但地铁总是很快。A 项意为"慢的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"快的"；D 项意为"危险的"。与汽车慢形成对比，地铁 fast。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次我感觉更自信了，甚至帮助一位迷路的老奶奶。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"自信的"；C 项意为"疲劳的"；D 项意为"悲伤的"。第二次乘坐更有信心，confident 正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：地铁不仅方便，而且环保，因为它用电而不是汽油。A 项意为"有害的"；B 项意为"友好的"；C 项意为"有用的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。environmentally friendly 意为"环保的"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望更多城市有地铁，这样人们出行更容易。A 项意为"缓慢地"；B 项意为"容易地"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"粗心地"。travel easily 意为"出行便利"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许下次我可以自己跟着路线走，独自出行。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记住"；C 项意为"跟随；沿着"；D 项意为"画"。follow the route 意为"按路线走"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这将是向更加独立迈进的一大步。A 项意为"依赖的"；B 项意为"独立的"；C 项意为"耐心的"；D 项意为"沉默的"。独自出行体现 independence。故选 B。
