# 中考英语完形填空：学编中国结

## 完形填空原文

During the Cultural Festival at our school, there was a workshop on making Chinese knots. Chinese knots are traditional decorations that represent good luck and happiness. I had always admired their beautiful ___1___, so I signed up for the workshop.

The teacher, Mrs. Zhang, had been making Chinese knots for over twenty years. She showed us different types of knots — some were simple, some were complex. For beginners, she said we would start with the basic "good luck" knot. She ___2___ each step slowly so that we could follow.

First, we needed a long red cord. Mrs. Zhang said that red was the traditional colour because it symbolizes joy and good fortune in Chinese culture. We folded the cord in a specific way and then pulled it through to form loops. My fingers felt clumsy at first. The cord kept slipping and my loops were ___3___. Mrs. Zhang came over and helped me adjust. "Don't pull too hard. Be gentle with the cord," she advised.

After many attempts, I finally made my first knot. It wasn't perfect, but I was proud of it. Mrs. Zhang smiled and said, "Practice makes ___4___. You'll get better with time." She encouraged us to take our knots home and practise more.

In the following weeks, I practised whenever I had free time. I made knots of different sizes. I gave one to my grandmother for her birthday. She was very ___5___ and hung it on the wall of her room. I also made a small one to hang on my school bag. Every time I saw it, I felt a connection to our traditional culture.

Making Chinese knots requires patience and concentration. You have to ___6___ on each step. If you rush, the knots will be messy. Mrs. Zhang had told us that in the past, people often made Chinese knots during the Spring Festival to bring good luck to their families. The craft has been ___7___ down from generation to generation.

Now I understand why traditional crafts are so valuable. They are not just beautiful objects — they carry the wisdom and culture of our ancestors. Learning to make Chinese knots has made me ___8___ our traditions more. I hope to learn more about Chinese traditional arts in the future.

If you have a chance to learn a traditional craft, I would strongly ___9___ you to try it. You might discover a new hobby and gain a deeper ___10___ of our culture at the same time.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直很欣赏它们漂亮的设计，所以报了名。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"设计"；C 项意为"尺寸"；D 项意为"材料"。中国结的美在于 design。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她慢慢解释每一步，这样我们才能跟上。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"解释"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"避免"。教手工需要 explained。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：绳子总是滑脱，我编的环不均匀。A 项意为"完美的"；B 项意为"不均匀的"；C 项意为"干净的"；D 项意为"牢固的"。初学者做得不整齐，uneven 正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定谚语
**【解析】** 本句意为：熟能生巧，假以时日你会越做越好。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"完美"；C 项意为"进步"；D 项意为"没有"。Practice makes perfect 意为"熟能生巧"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶非常高兴，把它挂在房间墙上。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。收到礼物用 pleased。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你必须专注于每一步。A 项意为"集中注意力"；B 项意为"依靠"；C 项意为"放弃"；D 项意为"转向"。focus on 意为"专注于"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：这项技艺代代相传。A 项意为"扔"；B 项意为"传递"；C 项意为"放"；D 项意为"拿"。pass down 意为"传承"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学编中国结让我更加欣赏我们的传统。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"欣赏"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"忽视"。学习传统工艺后更欣赏传统，admire 正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我强烈建议你试试。A 项意为"命令"；B 项意为"建议"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"阻止"。advise sb to do 意为"建议某人做"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你可能会发现新爱好，同时对我们的文化有更深入的理解。A 项意为"理解"；B 项意为"记忆"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"困难"。gain a deeper understanding 意为"获得更深的理解"。故选 A。
