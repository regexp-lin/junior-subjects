# 中考英语完形填空：国庆节升旗仪式

## 完形填空原文

October 1st is our National Day. Every year on this special day, our school holds a flag-raising ceremony to ___1___ the birthday of our country.

This morning, all the students and teachers gathered on the playground early. Everyone was wearing the school uniform and looked very ___2___. The national flag was bright red, shining under the morning sun. When the music started, the flag-bearers walked towards the flagpole with steady steps. We all stood straight and watched ___3___.

As the national anthem began to play, the flag rose slowly into the sky. I put my right hand over my heart and sang along with my classmates. At that moment, I felt very ___4___. I thought about all the people who had worked hard to build our country and make it strong.

After the ceremony, our principal gave a short ___5___. She told us that National Day was not just a holiday for fun. It was a day to remember history and think about our future. She said that as students, we should study hard and prepare ourselves to ___6___ our country one day.

Then our class went back to the classroom. Our head teacher asked us to write down one thing we could do for our country. Some students wrote "study harder." Others wrote "protect the ___7___" or "help people in need." I wrote "be a responsible citizen." I believe that if everyone does their part, our country will become even ___8___.

In the afternoon, my family had a big dinner together to celebrate the holiday. My grandparents told us stories about ___9___ life was like when they were young. Things were much harder back then. Hearing their stories made me feel grateful for the happy life we have today.

This National Day was ___10___ to me. It was not just a day off from school — it was a day that filled my heart with love for our country.

8. A. weaker &emsp;&emsp; B. smaller &emsp;&emsp; C. worse &emsp;&emsp; D. better
9. A. how &emsp;&emsp; B. what &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. who

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每年在这个特殊的日子，学校举行升旗仪式来庆祝祖国的生日。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"庆祝"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"改变"。celebrate the birthday 意为"庆祝生日"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都穿着校服，看起来非常严肃认真。A 项意为"疲倦的"；B 项意为"严肃的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"懒惰的"。升旗仪式场合应表现严肃。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都站得笔直，安静地观看。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"悲伤地"；C 项意为"生气地"；D 项意为"安静地"。升旗仪式要保持安静。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在那一刻，我感到非常自豪。A 项意为"孤独的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。后文提到想起建设国家的人们，感到骄傲。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：仪式结束后，校长做了简短的演讲。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"演讲"；C 项意为"信"；D 项意为"歌"。校长在仪式后 gave a speech。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：作为学生，我们应该努力学习，准备有一天为祖国服务。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"服务"；D 项意为"责备"。serve our country 意为"为国服务"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：其他人写了"保护环境"或"帮助需要帮助的人"。A 项意为"环境"；B 项意为"操场"；C 项意为"校服"；D 项意为"考试"。protect the environment 意为"保护环境"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果每个人都尽自己的责任，国家会变得更好。A 项意为"更弱"；B 项意为"更小"；C 项意为"更差"；D 项意为"更好"。做好自己的事让国家 better。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：祖父母告诉我们他们年轻时生活是什么样的。A 项意为"怎样"；B 项意为"什么"；C 项意为"哪里"；D 项意为"谁"。what life was like 意为"生活是什么样的"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这个国庆节对我来说很特别。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"特别的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。后文说不仅是放假，更是充满爱国之情的一天。故选 C。
