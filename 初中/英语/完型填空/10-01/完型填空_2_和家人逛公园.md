# 中考英语完形填空：和家人逛公园

## 完形填空原文

Today was the first day of the National Day holiday. The weather was perfect — sunny and cool. My parents decided to take our whole family to the city park for a ___1___ day out.

We arrived at the park around nine o'clock. There were already many people there. Families with children, old couples walking hand in hand, and groups of young people taking photos everywhere. The park was ___2___ with laughter and music.

We first walked along the lake. The water was clear and I could see small fish swimming in it. My little sister was so ___3___ that she wanted to jump in and catch them. My mother quickly held her hand and told her to just watch.

Then we found a nice spot under a big tree and spread out a blanket for a picnic. My father had prepared sandwiches, fruit, and juice. We sat together, eating and ___4___. My grandmother told us funny stories about her childhood. We all laughed so hard that our stomachs hurt.

After lunch, my father suggested we rent a small boat on the lake. I was a little ___5___ at first because I couldn't swim. But my father promised to keep me safe. Once we were on the water, I felt calm and relaxed. The gentle breeze blew across my ___6___, and I could see the colorful autumn leaves on both sides of the lake. It was so beautiful.

In the afternoon, we visited the flower garden. There were roses, sunflowers, and many other kinds of flowers. My sister picked up some fallen ___7___ and made a small crown for my grandmother. Grandma wore it on her head and smiled happily. That was the sweetest moment of the day.

Before we left, we took a family photo in front of the park gate. Everyone was smiling. Looking at the photo later, I realized how ___8___ it was to spend time with family.

On the way home, my sister fell ___9___ in the car. She must have been very tired after a whole day of fun. I looked out of the window at the sunset and felt thankful for such a wonderful day.

The National Day holiday had just begun, but I already had a beautiful ___10___ to treasure.

2. A. covered &emsp;&emsp; B. filled &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. troubled
3. A. scared &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. tired

5. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. scared &emsp;&emsp; D. happy
6. A. face &emsp;&emsp; B. book &emsp;&emsp; C. desk &emsp;&emsp; D. bag
7. A. stones &emsp;&emsp; B. leaves &emsp;&emsp; C. sticks &emsp;&emsp; D. petals
8. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. strange
9. A. ill &emsp;&emsp; B. down &emsp;&emsp; C. asleep &emsp;&emsp; D. behind
10. A. memory &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. lesson &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我父母决定带全家去城市公园度过美好的一天。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"孤独的"；C 项意为"美好的"；D 项意为"危险的"。天气好、全家出游应是 wonderful。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（被动语态）
**【解析】** 本句意为：公园里充满了笑声和音乐。A 项意为"覆盖"；B 项意为"充满"；C 项意为"无聊"；D 项意为"困扰"。be filled with 意为"充满"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我妹妹太兴奋了，想跳进去抓鱼。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"疲倦的"。小孩看到鱼想抓是 excited。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们坐在一起吃东西、聊天。A 项意为"争论"；B 项意为"打架"；C 项意为"哭泣"；D 项意为"聊天"。野餐时边吃边 chatting。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始我有点害怕，因为我不会游泳。A 项意为"勇敢的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"害怕的"；D 项意为"开心的"。不会游泳所以 scared。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：微风吹过我的脸庞。A 项意为"脸"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"桌子"；D 项意为"书包"。breeze blew across my face 意为"微风拂面"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹捡起一些掉落的花瓣，给奶奶做了一个小花冠。A 项意为"石头"；B 项意为"叶子"；C 项意为"树枝"；D 项意为"花瓣"。在花园里捡花瓣做花冠更合理。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我意识到和家人在一起是多么重要。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。全文表达家人相处的珍贵，important 正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家路上，妹妹在车上睡着了。A 项意为"生病"；B 项意为"摔倒"；C 项意为"睡着"；D 项意为"落后"。fall asleep 意为"睡着"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：假期才刚开始，但我已经有了一段美好的记忆值得珍藏。A 项意为"记忆"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"课程"；D 项意为"错误"。a beautiful memory to treasure 意为"值得珍藏的美好记忆"。故选 A。
