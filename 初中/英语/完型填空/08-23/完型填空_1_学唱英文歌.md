# 中考英语完形填空：学唱英文歌

## 完形填空原文

Our English teacher, Miss Li, suggested that we learn an English song to improve our pronunciation. She said that singing could help us remember words and practise our ___1___. The whole class thought it was a great idea.

We chose a popular song that was not too difficult. The lyrics were clear, and the melody was catchy. Miss Li played the song once, and we listened carefully. Then she wrote the lyrics on the blackboard. We read them ___2___ to make sure we understood every word.

The first step was to learn the melody. We hummed along without singing the words. It helped us get familiar with the tune. Some of us were a bit shy at first. We were afraid of singing ___3___ in front of others. But Miss Li said, "Everyone can sing. There is no right or wrong. Just enjoy the music."

We practised the chorus first because it was the easiest part. We sang it together again and again. Gradually, our voices began to blend. The feeling of singing in harmony was ___4___. I had never realised that group singing could sound so good.

Then we learned the verses. They had more words and required better pronunciation. Miss Li corrected us when we mispronounced words. "Pay attention to the 'th' sound," she said. "And the 'r' and 'l' are different in English." We practised those sounds ___5___ until we got them right.

After two weeks of practice, we could sing the whole song. Miss Li recorded us and played it back. We were surprised to hear how we sounded. It was not perfect, but we had improved a lot. We felt ___6___ of ourselves.

We performed the song at the school arts festival. Our parents came to watch. When we finished, the audience clapped warmly. My mother said she could understand most of the words. That was the best ___7___ we could get — our English was clear enough for others to understand.

Since then, I have developed a habit of learning English through songs. I listen to English songs when I do homework or travel by bus. I look up the lyrics if I do not understand them. Singing has become a fun way to ___8___ my English.

I recommend this ___10___ to my friends. Learning through music is more enjoyable than memorising word lists. The melodies help us remember the words, and the positive feelings make the experience ___9___. I hope more students will discover the joy of learning English through songs.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说唱歌能帮助我们记单词并练习___。A 项意为"语法"；B 项意为"发音"；C 项意为"拼写"；D 项意为"写作"。学唱英文歌主要练发音，"pronunciation"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们___朗读以确保理解每个词。A 项意为"大声地"；B 项意为"默读"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"慢慢地"。"read aloud"意为"大声朗读"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们害怕在大家面前唱___。A 项意为"大声地"；B 项意为"错"；C 项意为"轻轻地"；D 项意为"清晰地"。害羞是怕唱错，"wrongly"符合。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：和谐齐唱的感觉非常___。A 项意为"无聊"；B 项意为"令人惊叹"；C 项意为"累人"；D 项意为"令人担心"。"amazing"表示效果出众。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们___练习那些发音直到正确。A 项意为"马虎地"；B 项意为"分开地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"突然地"。"practise carefully"意为"认真练习"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们对自己感到___。A 项意为"羞愧"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"无聊"。"feel proud of"意为"为……感到骄傲"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是我们能得到的最好的___。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"奖品"；C 项意为"夸奖"；D 项意为"惩罚"。"compliment"指被听懂即是最好的肯定。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：唱歌已成为___英语的有趣方式。A 项意为"测试"；B 项意为"提高"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"忽略"。"improve my English"意为"提高英语"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：积极情绪让这段经历变得___。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"无意义"；C 项意为"难忘"；D 项意为"无聊"。"unforgettable"意为"难忘的"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我向朋友推荐这种___。A 项意为"歌曲"；B 项意为"课程"；C 项意为"音乐"；D 项意为"方法"。"recommend this method"意为"推荐这种方法"。故选 D。
