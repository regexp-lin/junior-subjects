# 中考英语完形填空：参加朗诵比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held an English recitation competition. I had always enjoyed reading poems and stories aloud, so I decided to take part. My English teacher helped me choose a suitable piece — a short poem about nature.

I practised every day for two weeks. I read the poem again and again until I could recite it from ___1___. I also paid attention to my intonation and pauses. A good recitation is not just about saying the words correctly; it is about expressing the ___2___ behind them.

The day of the competition arrived. I put on my best clothes and went to the school hall. There were about twenty participants. We drew lots to decide the order of performance. I got number twelve — right in the ___3___. I was neither first nor last, which made me feel a bit more relaxed.

As I watched the other participants, I learned from them. Some spoke too quickly; others were too quiet. I reminded myself to speak clearly and at a steady ___4___. When my turn came, I took a deep breath and walked onto the stage.

The lights were bright, and I could see the judges sitting in the front row. I felt a moment of ___5___, but then I remembered my teacher's words: "Imagine you are sharing the poem with a friend." I began to recite. My voice was steady, and I tried to put emotion into every line.

When I finished, the hall was silent for a second. Then the audience burst into applause. I felt a wave of ___6___. I had done my best, and that was what mattered. The judges wrote on their papers. I could not see their expressions, but I hoped for the best.

We had to wait until the end to hear the results. All the participants sat together. Some looked confident; others looked nervous. The host announced the names of the winners. When I heard my name for the third prize, I could not ___7___ my ears. I had won! I walked onto the stage to receive my certificate. My teacher was smiling in the audience.

The certificate is now on my bedroom wall. It reminds me that hard work pays off. I used to be shy about speaking in front of people. This competition helped me overcome my ___8___ and build confidence. I am grateful for the experience.

I will take part in the competition again next year. Next time, I might choose a longer piece or try a different style. Recitation has become one of my favourite ___9___. It combines language learning with artistic expression. I encourage my classmates to try it too. Everyone has a voice worth ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我反复朗读直到能___背诵。A 项意为"纸"；B 项意为"记忆"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"心"。"recite from memory"意为"脱稿背诵"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：朗诵不仅是正确发音，还要表达其中的___。A 项意为"词语"；B 项意为"情感"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"错误"。"the feelings behind them"指文字背后的情感。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我抽到12号——正好在___。A 项意为"开头"；B 项意为"中间"；C 项意为"结尾"；D 项意为"角落"。"in the middle"意为"在中间"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我提醒自己要清晰、稳定___地演讲。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"声响"；D 项意为"价格"。"at a steady speed"意为"以稳定语速"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到片刻的___。A 项意为"自信"；B 项意为"紧张"；C 项意为"喜悦"；D 项意为"骄傲"。上台时短暂紧张，"nervousness"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到一阵___。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"放松"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"恐惧"。完成朗诵后的放松，"relief"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我简直不敢相信自己的耳朵。A 项意为"相信"；B 项意为"关闭"；C 项意为"盖住"；D 项意为"清洁"。"could not believe my ears"为固定表达。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛帮我克服了___并建立信心。A 项意为"勇气"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"天赋"；D 项意为"运气"。"overcome my fear"意为"克服恐惧"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：朗诵已成为我最喜爱的___之一。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"家务"；D 项意为"游戏"。"favourite activities"意为"喜爱的活动"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都有值得___的声音。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"分享"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"失去"。"worth sharing"意为"值得分享"。故选 B。
