# 中考英语完形填空：和爷爷下象棋

## 完形填空原文

My grandfather loves playing Chinese chess. He has played it for more than sixty years. This summer, he offered to teach me. I had never played before, but I was ___1___ to learn.

Grandpa explained the rules slowly. Each piece moves in a different way. The "horse" moves in an L shape, and the "chariot" can move in a straight line. I tried to remember everything, but it was not easy. In our first game, I ___2___ in less than ten moves. Grandpa smiled and said, "That is normal. Everyone loses when they first learn."

I did not give up. I practiced with Grandpa every evening after dinner. At first, I only thought about my own moves. I wanted to attack quickly and ___3___ the game. But Grandpa taught me that chess is not about speed. "You must think about what your opponent will do next," he said. "Plan your moves carefully."

Slowly, I began to understand the game better. I learned to protect my important pieces and to ___4___ for chances. Sometimes the best move is to wait. Grandpa said that chess is like life — we need both courage and patience.

One afternoon, I finally won a game! I was so ___5___ that I could not believe it. Grandpa clapped his hands and said, "Well done! You are learning fast." I knew he had not played his best on purpose — he had let me win to encourage me. But I still felt proud.

Playing chess with Grandpa has become our special time together. We sit in the garden, drink tea, and talk while we play. He tells me stories about his youth and the lessons life has taught him. I have learned more from these ___6___ than from the chess itself.

Grandpa says that chess teaches us to think before we act. In life, we often have to make difficult ___7___. If we rush, we may make mistakes. But if we think carefully, we can find better solutions. I will remember this advice for the rest of my life.

I hope Grandpa and I can keep playing chess together for many years. Each game is a ___8___ to learn something new. And each moment with him is precious. Someday I want to teach my own children to play, just as Grandpa taught me.

If you have a grandparent or an older person in your family, try to spend time with them. They have so much ___9___ and love to share. You might be surprised by what you can learn from them. A game of chess, a walk in the park, or simply sitting and talking — these simple things can create ___10___ that last forever.

2. A. won &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. left &emsp;&emsp; D. started
3. A. finish &emsp;&emsp; B. watch &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. understand
4. A. look &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. ask &emsp;&emsp; D. run
5. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. bored

7. A. friends &emsp;&emsp; B. plans &emsp;&emsp; C. decisions &emsp;&emsp; D. mistakes
8. A. chance &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. reason &emsp;&emsp; D. secret
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. wisdom &emsp;&emsp; C. time &emsp;&emsp; D. strength

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没下过象棋，但___学。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"累的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"抱歉的"。"happy to learn"表示乐意、高兴去学。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一局我在十步之内就___了。A 项意为"赢"；B 项意为"输"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"开始"。初学者很快输掉很正常，"lost"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想快速进攻并___比赛。A 项意为"结束"；B 项意为"观看"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"理解"。"finish the game"指迅速赢得比赛。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我学会了保护重要棋子并___时机。A 项意为"看"；B 项意为"等"；C 项意为"问"；D 项意为"跑"。"wait for chances"意为"等待机会"，符合棋理。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太___了，简直不敢相信。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。首次赢棋会非常兴奋，"excited"恰当。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从这些___中学到的比从象棋本身更多。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"走法"；D 项意为"棋子"。前文提到爷爷讲青年时代的故事，"stories"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在生活中我们常要做艰难的___。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"计划"；C 项意为"决定"；D 项意为"错误"。"make decisions"意为"做决定"，与"think before we act"呼应。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一局都是___学新东西的机会。A 项意为"机会"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"原因"；D 项意为"秘密"。"a chance to learn"意为"一次学习的机会"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们有很多___和爱愿意分享。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"智慧"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"力量"。长辈分享人生经验，"wisdom"最贴切。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些简单的事能创造___永恒的___。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"回忆"；D 项意为"规则"。"memories that last forever"意为"永恒的回忆"。故选 C。
