# 中考英语完形填空：去动物园

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my family went to the city zoo. It was my little sister's birthday and she had always wanted to see real animals. We arrived early in the morning to avoid the crowds.

The first place we visited was the monkey house. The monkeys were very active. They jumped from one branch to another and made funny faces at the visitors. My sister ___1___ so hard that she could not stop. She said the monkeys looked like her cousins when they played. We all laughed.

Then we went to see the elephants. They were much ___2___ than I had imagined. One elephant was eating hay with its long trunk. A zookeeper stood nearby and told us that elephants can remember things for many years. "They are very smart animals," he said. I was surprised by how gentle they seemed.

We also saw lions, tigers, and bears. They were kept in large areas that looked like their natural ___3___. My father explained that modern zoos try to give animals a comfortable home. "Zoos help protect animals and teach people about them," he said. I thought that was a good ___4___.

At noon, we had lunch at a restaurant inside the zoo. After that, we went to the bird garden. There were many colorful birds — parrots, peacocks, and some I had never ___5___ before. One parrot could even say "hello"! My sister tried to talk to it and it answered. She was so happy.

Before we left, we visited the gift shop. My sister chose a stuffed panda as her birthday present. I bought a book about animal protection. I wanted to ___6___ more about how we can help protect wildlife.

That day at the zoo was wonderful. I learned that animals are not so different from us — they have families, they play, and they need care. We should ___7___ them and their home. Every animal has the right to live in peace.

I hope to visit the zoo again soon. Maybe next time we can see the new baby pandas that were born last month. My sister has already ___8___ that she wants to go back for her next birthday too. I think that is a great idea.

If you have a chance to visit a zoo, take the time to read the information about each animal. You will learn a lot. And remember: we share this planet with many other ___9___. It is our responsibility to treat them with kindness and respect. A trip to the zoo can be more than fun — it can be a lesson in ___10___ and love for all living things.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹笑得停不下来。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"笑"；C 项意为"喊"；D 项意为"睡"。猴子做鬼脸逗人笑，"laughed"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大象比我想象的___得多。A 项意为"更小"；B 项意为"更大"；C 项意为"更弱"；D 项意为"更瘦"。第一次见到真大象会觉得体型很大，"bigger"符合常识。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们被安置在看起来像自然___的大区域里。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"水"；C 项意为"家园"；D 项意为"朋友"。"natural home"指模拟自然栖息地。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我认为那是个好___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"想法/观念"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"故事"。父亲解释动物园的作用，"idea"表示对这种做法的看法。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些鸟我从未___过。A 项意为"看见"；B 项意为"听过"；C 项意为"吃过"；D 项意为"买过"。"had never seen"意为"从未见过"，指新奇的鸟类。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想___更多关于如何保护野生动物的知识。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"教"；D 项意为"忘记"。"learn more about"意为"进一步了解"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们应当___它们和它们的家园。A 项意为"捕捉"；B 项意为"尊重"；C 项意为"狩猎"；D 项意为"跟随"。"respect"表示尊重、保护动物及其栖息地。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妹妹已经___下次生日还要再来。A 项意为"希望"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"拒绝"。"decided"表示下定决心、做出选择。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们与许多其他___共享这个星球。A 项意为"植物"；B 项意为"地方"；C 项意为"东西"；D 项意为"生物"。"creatures"指动物等生物。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：动物园之旅不仅是乐趣——更是关于___与对所有生命的爱的课程。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"关爱"；D 项意为"仇恨"。"care"与"love"并列，表示关怀与爱护。故选 C。
