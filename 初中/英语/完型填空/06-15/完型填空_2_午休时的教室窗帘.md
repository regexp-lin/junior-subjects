# 中考英语完形填空：午休时的教室窗帘

## 完形填空原文

Every noon after lunch, our class has a break. The classroom becomes quiet, and someone always draws the curtains to ___1___ the bright sunlight. For thirty minutes, we rest our heads on the desks and try to sleep. To me, those curtains have become a ___2___ of peace and rest.

When I first came to this school, I could not fall asleep during the noon break. The light was too strong, and I felt ___3___. One day, my deskmate saw me covering my eyes with my hands. Without saying a word, she stood up and drew the curtains. The room became darker and softer. "Try again," she said. I closed my eyes and finally had a good rest. That small ___4___ made me realize that kindness often comes in simple ways.

Since then, I have learned to help others in the same way. When I arrive at the classroom early, I draw the curtains ___5___ so that everyone can rest comfortably. When someone is feeling tired, I offer to share my ___6___ or water. These small acts may seem ___7___, but they can make a big difference to someone's day.

The curtains also remind me of the importance of rest. We study hard every day, but our bodies and minds need time to ___8___. Just as the curtains block the light and create a peaceful space, we should also learn to create moments of calm in our busy lives. Rest is not lazy — it helps us do better later.

I will graduate soon. When I think of my junior high years, I will remember the quiet noon breaks, the soft light through the curtains, and the friends who ___9___ each other in small ways. These memories will stay with me forever, like the gentle touch of those classroom curtains on a warm afternoon. They taught me that the best things in life are often ___10___ and kind.

## 选项

1. A. catch &emsp;&emsp; B. block &emsp;&emsp; C. find &emsp;&emsp; D. follow
2. A. symbol &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. rule &emsp;&emsp; D. game
3. A. relaxed &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. uncomfortable &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
4. A. moment &emsp;&emsp; B. exam &emsp;&emsp; C. prize &emsp;&emsp; D. book
5. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
6. A. homework &emsp;&emsp; B. pencil &emsp;&emsp; C. jacket &emsp;&emsp; D. umbrella
7. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. small &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
8. A. work &emsp;&emsp; B. play &emsp;&emsp; C. run &emsp;&emsp; D. rest
9. A. fight &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. blame
10. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：总有人会拉上窗帘来___强烈的阳光。A 项意为"抓住"；B 项意为"遮挡、阻挡"；C 项意为"找到"；D 项意为"跟随"。拉窗帘的目的是"block"（遮挡）阳光，便于午休，符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对我来说，那些窗帘已成为安静与休息的___。A 项意为"象征"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"游戏"。"symbol"表示窗帘代表、象征着午休时的宁静，与"peace and rest"相呼应。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：光线太强，我感到___。A 项意为"放松的"；B 项意为"兴奋的"；C 项意为"不舒服的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。"uncomfortable"与"the light was too strong"相呼应，表示因强光难以入睡的不适。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个小小的___让我明白，善意往往以简单的方式呈现。A 项意为"瞬间、时刻"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"奖品"；D 项意为"书"。"moment"指同桌拉窗帘的那一举动，强调一个简单的善举带来的感悟。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我提前到教室时，我会___拉上窗帘，让每个人都能舒适地休息。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。"quickly"表示主动、及时地为同学准备午休环境，体现乐于助人。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当有人感到累时，我会主动分享我的___或水。A 项意为"作业"；B 项意为"铅笔"；C 项意为"外套"；D 项意为"雨伞"。"jacket"可与"water"并列，表示借衣服保暖或递水等小关怀，贴近学生生活。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些小小的举动可能看起来___，但它们能对他人的一天产生很大影响。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"微不足道的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。"small"与后文"make a big difference"形成对比，强调小事不小。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们每天努力学习，但身体和心灵需要时间___。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"玩耍"；C 项意为"跑步"；D 项意为"休息"。"rest"与"importance of rest"及"block the light and create a peaceful space"相呼应，强调休息的重要性。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我会记得那些以小小方式___彼此的朋友。A 项意为"打架"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"责怪"。"help"与全文"kindness"、"small acts"相呼应，指朋友间的互助与关怀。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们教会我，生活中最美好的事物往往___而温暖。A 项意为"艰难的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。"simple"与"kind"并列，呼应前文"kindness often comes in simple ways"，升华主题。故选 B。
