# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校大扫除迎接期末

## 完形填空原文

With the end of the semester approaching, our school organized a big cleaning day last Friday. Every class was responsible for cleaning their own classroom and a part of the school's public areas. Our class was ___1___ to clean the science laboratory.

Our class monitor divided us into four groups. My group was in charge of wiping the desks and washing the lab equipment. Another group swept and mopped the ___2___. The third group cleaned the windows, and the fourth group organized the bookshelves and cabinets.

At first, the lab looked messy. There were dust and stains everywhere because the lab had not been deeply cleaned for a long time. I put on my rubber gloves and started wiping the desks with a wet cloth. The stains were very ___3___ to remove, so I had to scrub hard. My arms felt tired after just fifteen minutes.

My partner Xiao Ming was washing the glass bottles and test tubes. He was very ___4___ because they were fragile and could break easily. He washed each one slowly and placed them neatly on the drying rack. I admired his patience.

After two hours of hard work, the lab was completely ___5___. The desks were shining, the floor was spotless, and all the equipment was arranged in ___6___. Our teacher came to check and said she could hardly believe it was the same room.

The cleaning day was tiring, but it gave us a sense of ___7___. When we looked at the clean lab, we all felt happy. Our teacher said that taking care of our learning environment is just as important as ___8___ for exams.

I think she is right. A clean and organized space helps us think more ___9___ and study more efficiently. I will try to keep my own desk clean every day from now on. Good habits start with small ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班被分配打扫科学实验室。A 项意为"惩罚"；B 项意为"分配"；C 项意为"强迫"；D 项意为"忘记"。was assigned to 意为"被分配去做"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：另一组扫地和拖地板。A 项意为"天花板"；B 项意为"墙壁"；C 项意为"地板"；D 项意为"屋顶"。swept and mopped the floor。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：污渍很难去除，所以我得使劲擦。A 项意为"容易的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"快速的"。hard to remove 意为"难以去除"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他非常小心，因为玻璃器皿易碎。A 项意为"粗心的"；B 项意为"小心的"；C 项意为"勇敢的"；D 项意为"懒的"。洗易碎品需要 careful。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两小时后实验室完全焕然一新。A 项意为"脏的"；B 项意为"焕然一新的"；C 项意为"破碎的"；D 项意为"空的"。completely transformed 意为"彻底改变"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：所有设备整齐有序地排列。A 项意为"危险"；B 项意为"秩序"；C 项意为"麻烦"；D 项意为"秘密"。arranged in order 意为"整齐排列"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大扫除很累，但给我们带来了成就感。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"失败"；C 项意为"成就"；D 项意为"遗憾"。a sense of achievement 意为"成就感"。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爱护学习环境和准备考试一样重要。A 项意为"准备"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"支付"；D 项意为"看"。preparing for exams 意为"备考"。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：整洁的空间帮助我们思考更清晰。A 项意为"缓慢地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"伤心地"；D 项意为"清晰地"。think more clearly。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好习惯从小行动开始。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"行动"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"礼物"。Good habits start with small actions。故选 B。
