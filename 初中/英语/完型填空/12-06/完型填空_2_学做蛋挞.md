# 中考英语完形填空：学做蛋挞

## 完形填空原文

Egg tarts are one of my favorite desserts. I always buy them from the bakery near our school. But last weekend, my aunt came to visit and taught me how to make them at ___1___.

My aunt is a great baker. She brought all the ingredients with her: pre-made tart shells, eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. She said the tart shells were the hardest part to make from scratch, so using store-bought ones was a good ___2___ for beginners.

First, we made the egg custard filling. My aunt cracked three eggs into a bowl and told me to ___3___ them gently. She said we should not beat them too hard, or the filling would have bubbles and not be smooth. Then we added warm milk, sugar, and a few drops of vanilla extract. I stirred the mixture until the sugar was completely ___4___.

Next, my aunt showed me an important trick. She poured the mixture through a fine strainer into another bowl. "This step removes any egg bits and makes the filling extra ___5___," she explained. I was surprised by how much attention to detail baking required.

Then we placed the tart shells on a baking tray. I carefully poured the custard into each shell, filling them about three-quarters ___6___. My aunt said if we filled them too much, the custard would overflow in the oven.

We put the tray into the preheated oven and waited for twenty minutes. The kitchen smelled ___7___. When the timer rang, I looked through the oven door and saw the tarts had turned golden on top with some beautiful brown spots. They looked just like the ones from the ___8___.

After they cooled down a little, I took a bite. The shell was flaky and crispy, and the custard was silky and ___9___. It was even better than the ones I usually bought. My aunt laughed and said, "See? Homemade food always has extra love in it."

I was so happy that I could make egg tarts by myself. I plan to make them again for my family during the winter ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：姑姑教我在家做蛋挞。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"家"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"公园"。at home 意为"在家"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用买来的挞皮对初学者是个好选择。A 项意为"选择"；B 项意为"借口"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"问题"。a good choice 意为"好选择"。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让我轻轻打散鸡蛋。A 项意为"打碎"；B 项意为"煮沸"；C 项意为"打/搅"；D 项意为"烧"。beat the eggs gently 意为"轻轻搅拌鸡蛋"。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（过去分词）
**【解析】** 本句意为：搅拌直到糖完全溶解。A 项意为"浪费的"；B 项意为"溶解的"；C 项意为"冷冻的"；D 项意为"保存的"。sugar was completely dissolved。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这一步去掉蛋液杂质，使馅料格外顺滑。A 项意为"浓的"；B 项意为"粗糙的"；C 项意为"顺滑的"；D 项意为"暗的"。过滤使 filling extra smooth。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：倒入蛋液大约填到四分之三满。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"满的"；C 项意为"开的"；D 项意为"关的"。three-quarters full 意为"四分之三满"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房闻起来很棒。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"美妙的"；D 项意为"冷的"。烘焙时香味 wonderful。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看起来和面包店买的一样。A 项意为"图书馆"；B 项意为"医院"；C 项意为"面包店"；D 项意为"书店"。开头提到 bakery near school。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：蛋液丝滑香甜。A 项意为"酸的"；B 项意为"苦的"；C 项意为"咸的"；D 项意为"甜的"。蛋挞馅 silky and sweet。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：打算寒假再给家人做蛋挞。A 项意为"假期"；B 项意为"考试"；C 项意为"测试"；D 项意为"课"。winter holiday 意为"寒假"。故选 A。
