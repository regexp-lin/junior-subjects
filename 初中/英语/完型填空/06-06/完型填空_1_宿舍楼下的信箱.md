# 中考英语完形填空：宿舍楼下的信箱

## 完形填空原文

Every afternoon after class, Li Ming would walk past the old mailbox at the bottom of his dormitory building. It was ___1___ and quiet, but something about it always made him stop. Last year, his grandma had sent him a letter. That was the first time he ___2___ received a real letter in his life. He still kept it in his desk drawer.

One day, Li Ming noticed a small girl standing by the mailbox. She looked ___3___ and was holding an envelope. "Can you help me?" she asked. "I want to send a letter to my parents, but I don't know ___4___ to do it." Li Ming smiled and showed her how to put the letter in and raise the red flag. The girl was very ___5___.

From that day on, Li Ming often saw students standing by the mailbox. Some were sending letters to their families. Others were waiting for letters from friends. The small mailbox had become a special ___6___ that connected hearts.

One rainy afternoon, Li Ming found a letter on the ground near the mailbox. It had fallen out. He ___7___ picked it up and put it back safely. When he looked at the envelope, he saw it was addressed to a boy in Class 3. The next day, Li Ming found that boy and gave him the letter. The boy's eyes lit up with ___8___. "Thank you! This is from my grandpa. I've been waiting for it for a long time!"

Now Li Ming understands. A simple mailbox can ___9___ much more than letters. It carries love, hope, and the warmth of home. Sometimes the smallest things can bring the ___10___ joy.

## 选项

1. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. simple &emsp;&emsp; C. old &emsp;&emsp; D. common
2. A. ever &emsp;&emsp; B. once &emsp;&emsp; C. never &emsp;&emsp; D. already
3. A. shy &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. lost
4. A. when &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. where &emsp;&emsp; D. why
5. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. thankful &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
6. A. place &emsp;&emsp; B. road &emsp;&emsp; C. bridge &emsp;&emsp; D. way
7. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. carefully &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. quietly
8. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. surprise &emsp;&emsp; C. hope &emsp;&emsp; D. thanks
9. A. carry &emsp;&emsp; B. bring &emsp;&emsp; C. take &emsp;&emsp; D. send
10. A. biggest &emsp;&emsp; B. deepest &emsp;&emsp; C. greatest &emsp;&emsp; D. sweetest

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：信箱又___又安静，但总有什么让它吸引李明停下脚步。A 项意为"小的"；B 项意为"简单的"；C 项意为"旧的"；D 项意为"普通的"。"simple and quiet"为常见搭配，形容信箱朴实低调。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是他生平___收到的一封真正的信。A 项意为"曾经"；B 项意为"一次"；C 项意为"从不"；D 项意为"已经"。"the first time he ever received"表示"他第一次曾经收到"，强调首次经历。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她看起来___，手里拿着一封信封。A 项意为"害羞的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"迷茫的、不知所措的"。"lost"在此意为不知所措，与后文"不知如何寄信"相呼应。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想给父母寄信，但不知道___寄。A 项意为"何时"；B 项意为"如何"；C 项意为"哪里"；D 项意为"为什么"。"how to do it"指操作方法，符合"不会寄信"的语境。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：女孩非常___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"感激的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"放松的"。学会如何寄信后，女孩感到兴奋开心。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（比喻用法）
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的信箱已成为连接心灵的特别___。A 项意为"地方"；B 项意为"道路"；C 项意为"桥梁"；D 项意为"方式"。"bridge that connected hearts"为比喻用法，指沟通情感的纽带。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他___捡起信，妥善放回。A 项意为"迅速地"；B 项意为"小心地"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"安静地"。对待他人的信应小心轻放，体现尊重。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：男孩的眼中闪烁着___。A 项意为"喜悦"；B 项意为"惊讶"；C 项意为"希望"；D 项意为"感谢"。"eyes lit up with joy"为常见表达，收到盼望已久的爷爷来信充满喜悦。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一个简单的信箱可以___的远不止信件。A 项意为"运送、传递"；B 项意为"带来"；C 项意为"拿走"；D 项意为"寄出"。"carry"与后文"It carries love, hope..."形成呼应。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时最小的东西却能带来___的快乐。A 项意为"最大的"；B 项意为"最深的"；C 项意为"最伟大的"；D 项意为"最甜蜜的"。"sweetest joy"形容温暖美好的快乐，与"最小的东西"形成对比。故选 D。
