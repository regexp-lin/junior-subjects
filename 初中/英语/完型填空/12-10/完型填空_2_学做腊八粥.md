# 中考英语完形填空：学做腊八粥

## 完形填空原文

Laba porridge is a traditional Chinese food eaten on the eighth day of the twelfth lunar month. My grandfather says it is one of the oldest winter traditions in China. Last year, he taught me how to ___1___ it from scratch.

The night before, Grandfather and I prepared the ingredients. We needed eight different kinds of grains and beans: rice, millet, red beans, green beans, peanuts, lotus seeds, dried dates, and longan. Grandfather said the number eight is considered ___2___ in Chinese culture, so using eight ingredients brings good fortune.

First, we soaked the beans and lotus seeds in water ___3___. Grandfather explained that soaking them makes them softer and easier to cook. "If you skip this step, the beans will be too hard even after hours of cooking," he said.

The next morning, we got up early. Grandfather put all the soaked ingredients into a big pot and added plenty of water. He turned on the stove and brought the water to a ___4___. Then he turned the heat down to low and let it simmer slowly.

While the porridge was cooking, Grandfather told me stories about Laba Festival. He said that long ago, people made Laba porridge to ___5___ their ancestors and pray for a good harvest. It was also a way for families to gather and share food during the cold winter.

After about two hours, the porridge became thick and ___6___. The kitchen smelled wonderful. Grandfather added some rock sugar to make it slightly sweet. He stirred it gently and said, "Good porridge needs time. You cannot ___7___ it."

We poured the porridge into bowls and sat down to eat. The first spoonful was warm and ___8___. Each bite had different textures—some soft, some chewy. It was much more interesting than regular porridge.

Grandfather smiled and said, "Laba porridge is not just food. It reminds us to be ___9___ for what we have and to share with others." I decided to make Laba porridge every year and carry on this beautiful ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爷爷教我从头开始做腊八粥。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"做"；D 项意为"扔"。make it from scratch 意为"从零开始做"。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：数字八在中国文化中被认为是幸运的。A 项意为"不幸的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"危险的"；D 项意为"幸运的"。八是 lucky number。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们将豆子和莲子泡在水里过夜。A 项意为"过夜"；B 项意为"立刻"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"大声地"。soaked overnight 意为"泡一夜"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把水烧开。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"沸腾"；C 项意为"冰冻"；D 项意为"结束"。brought the water to a boil 意为"把水烧开"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：人们做腊八粥来祭祀祖先。A 项意为"欺骗"；B 项意为"责备"；C 项意为"纪念/尊敬"；D 项意为"伤害"。honor their ancestors 意为"祭祀祖先"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：粥变得浓稠而顺滑。A 项意为"稀的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"空的"；D 项意为"浓稠顺滑的"。thick and creamy 形容好粥。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好粥需要时间，不能急。A 项意为"急/赶"；B 项意为"浪费"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"隐藏"。cannot rush it 意为"不能急"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一勺温暖而令人舒适。A 项意为"酸的"；B 项意为"苦的"；C 项意为"令人舒适的"；D 项意为"辣的"。warm and comforting 形容冬天的热粥。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：提醒我们感恩所拥有的。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"感恩的"；D 项意为"生气的"。be thankful for 意为"感恩"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：传承这个美好的传统。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"传统"；C 项意为"竞赛"；D 项意为"考试"。carry on this beautiful tradition。故选 B。
