# 中考英语完形填空：学校举办英语歌曲大赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school holds an English Song Contest every December. This year, my friend Xiao Li and I decided to enter as a duo. We both loved singing, but performing on stage was a different ___1___.

We spent a week choosing the right song. Xiao Li suggested a fast pop song, but I thought a slower ballad would be easier to sing well. After listening to many songs, we finally picked "You Raise Me Up" because the melody was beautiful and the ___2___ were meaningful.

We practiced every day during lunch break. At first, our voices did not blend well. Xiao Li sang too ___3___ and I sang too softly. Our music teacher heard us and offered some advice. She taught us how to control our volume and breathe ___4___ while singing. After a few days, our harmony improved a lot.

The night before the contest, I could not ___5___. I kept imagining myself forgetting the words on stage. I told Xiao Li about my worry, and she said, "Just focus on the music and forget about the audience. We have practiced so many times—we are ___6___."

On the day of the contest, the school hall was packed with students and teachers. When our turn came, I took a deep breath and walked onto the stage. The music started, and I began to sing. ___7___, my nervousness disappeared as I focused on the melody. Xiao Li and I looked at each other and smiled. Our voices filled the hall, and I could see some teachers nodding along.

When we finished, the audience gave us warm ___8___. We did not win first prize—that went to a group who performed a creative English rap. But we received the "Most Beautiful Harmony" award.

Walking off the stage, I felt incredibly ___9___. This experience taught me that courage is not the absence of fear, but the willingness to ___10___ even when you are afraid.

## 选项

1. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. challenge &emsp;&emsp; C. subject &emsp;&emsp; D. color
2. A. pictures &emsp;&emsp; B. lyrics &emsp;&emsp; C. numbers &emsp;&emsp; D. pages
3. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quietly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. badly
4. A. properly &emsp;&emsp; B. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; C. lazily &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
5. A. eat &emsp;&emsp; B. study &emsp;&emsp; C. sleep &emsp;&emsp; D. speak
6. A. lost &emsp;&emsp; B. ready &emsp;&emsp; C. wrong &emsp;&emsp; D. late
7. A. Sadly &emsp;&emsp; B. Surprisingly &emsp;&emsp; C. Unfortunately &emsp;&emsp; D. Angrily
8. A. silence &emsp;&emsp; B. complaints &emsp;&emsp; C. applause &emsp;&emsp; D. questions
9. A. disappointed &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
10. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. try &emsp;&emsp; C. quit &emsp;&emsp; D. cry

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在舞台上表演是另一个挑战。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"挑战"；C 项意为"科目"；D 项意为"颜色"。performing on stage is a different challenge。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：歌词很有意义。A 项意为"图片"；B 项意为"歌词"；C 项意为"数字"；D 项意为"页"。lyrics were meaningful 意为"歌词有意义"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小李唱得太大声，我唱得太轻。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"安静地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"糟糕地"。too loudly 与 too softly 对比。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：教我们唱歌时如何正确地呼吸。A 项意为"正确地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"懒惰地"；D 项意为"紧张地"。breathe properly 意为"正确呼吸"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛前一晚我睡不着。A 项意为"吃"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"睡觉"；D 项意为"说话"。紧张到 could not sleep。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们练了那么多次——我们准备好了。A 项意为"迷失的"；B 项意为"准备好的"；C 项意为"错的"；D 项意为"迟的"。we are ready 表示信心。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：令人惊讶的是，我的紧张感消失了。A 项意为"悲伤地"；B 项意为"令人惊讶地"；C 项意为"不幸地"；D 项意为"生气地"。Surprisingly 引出转折。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：观众给了我们热烈的掌声。A 项意为"沉默"；B 项意为"抱怨"；C 项意为"掌声"；D 项意为"问题"。warm applause 意为"热烈掌声"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：走下舞台，我感到无比自豪。A 项意为"失望的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"自豪的"。获奖后 incredibly proud。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：勇气不是没有恐惧，而是即使害怕也愿意尝试。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"放弃"；D 项意为"哭"。willingness to try 意为"愿意尝试"。故选 B。
