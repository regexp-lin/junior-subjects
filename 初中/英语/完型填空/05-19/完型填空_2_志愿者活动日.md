# 中考英语完形填空：志愿者活动日

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, our school organized a volunteer activity at a community center. We would help ___1___ people and clean the reading room. I had never done volunteer work before, so I was a little nervous.

When we arrived, an old man was sitting alone in the garden. I walked over and said hello. At first, he seemed ___2___, but when I asked about his family, he began to talk. He told me that his children worked in other cities and rarely ___3___ home. "I'm glad you came to talk to me," he said. "I feel less lonely now."

In the afternoon, we cleaned the reading room. We ___4___ the books, wiped the tables, and swept the floor. It was tiring work, but when we saw the clean and tidy room, we felt ___5___. A staff member said, "Thank you, students. Your help means a lot to us." Her words made us realize that small actions can make a big ___6___.

On the bus ride back to school, I thought about the day. I had thought volunteer work would be ___7___, but I was wrong. It was meaningful. Helping others made me feel useful. I learned that we don't need to do great things to ___8___ the world. We can start with small acts of ___9___. From that day on, I decided to take part in more volunteer ___10___ in the future.

## 选项

1. A. young &emsp;&emsp; B. sick &emsp;&emsp; C. elderly &emsp;&emsp; D. busy
2. A. shy &emsp;&emsp; B. happy &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
3. A. went &emsp;&emsp; B. came &emsp;&emsp; C. returned &emsp;&emsp; D. stayed
4. A. bought &emsp;&emsp; B. sold &emsp;&emsp; C. organized &emsp;&emsp; D. wrote
5. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
6. A. noise &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. money &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
7. A. interesting &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
8. A. build &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. create &emsp;&emsp; D. discover
9. A. courage &emsp;&emsp; B. kindness &emsp;&emsp; C. patience &emsp;&emsp; D. honesty
10. A. games &emsp;&emsp; B. activities &emsp;&emsp; C. classes &emsp;&emsp; D. trips

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们将帮助老人并打扫阅览室。A 项意为"年轻的"；B 项意为"生病的"；C 项意为"年老的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。community center 常见服务对象为 elderly people（老年人），与后文老人独坐呼应。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初他显得害羞。A 项意为"害羞的"；B 项意为"开心的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"疲倦的"。陌生人搭话时可能 shy，随后打开话题开始交谈。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的孩子们在其他城市工作，很少回家。A 项意为"去"；B 项意为"来"；C 项意为"回来"；D 项意为"停留"。return home 意为"回家"，子女在外工作，很少回乡。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们整理书籍、擦桌子、扫地。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"整理"；D 项意为"写"。organize the books 意为"整理书籍"，与 wipe、sweep 构成清洁工作。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但当我们看到整洁的房间时，我们感到骄傲。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。完成劳动后看到成果，feeling proud 符合情境。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的话让我们意识到小小的举动可以产生很大的影响。A 项意为"噪音"；B 项意为"不同；影响"；C 项意为"钱"；D 项意为"麻烦"。make a big difference 为固定搭配，意为"产生重大影响"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我曾以为志愿工作会很无聊，但我错了。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"容易的"；D 项意为"困难的"。had thought...would be boring 与后文 meaningful 形成对比。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我明白了我们不需要做大事就能改变世界。A 项意为"建造"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"创造"；D 项意为"发现"。change the world 为常用表达，与"小小善举"形成对比。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们可以从小小的善举开始。A 项意为"勇气"；B 项意为"善良"；C 项意为"耐心"；D 项意为"诚实"。acts of kindness 意为"善举"，与志愿活动主题一致。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从那天起，我决定今后参加更多志愿活动。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"课程"；D 项意为"旅行"。volunteer activities 意为"志愿活动"，与开头 volunteer activity 呼应。故选 B。
