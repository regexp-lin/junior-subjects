# 中考英语完形填空：雨天的公交站

## 完形填空原文

It was a rainy Friday afternoon. I had missed the school bus and had to wait at the bus stop. The rain was heavy. I stood under the small roof, but my clothes were still getting wet. I felt cold and ___1___.

Then I noticed an old lady standing a few steps away. She had no umbrella and was getting wet. Without thinking, I moved closer and said, "Please share the space with me. It's ___2___ here." She looked surprised, then smiled and came over. We stood together under the small roof, trying to stay dry.

We started to ___3___. She told me she was going to visit her grandson. I told her about my school and the test I had the next week. The rain kept falling, but we didn't mind. It felt good to talk. When the bus finally came, she said, "Thank you for your kindness. You ___4___ a young man with a warm heart."

Her words stayed with me. I had only shared a little space. But she had made me feel that my small act ___5___ something. That rainy afternoon taught me a lesson: kindness doesn't need to be big. Sometimes it's just making room for someone else when you have very little ___6___ yourself.

Since then, I have tried to look for chances to help. When I see someone ___7___ help on the street, I offer it. When a classmate has no pen, I lend one. These small things take little effort, but they can ___8___ someone's day.

The bus stop is still there. I pass it every day. Every time I see it, I remember that rainy afternoon and the old lady's smile. Rainy days used to make me feel ___9___. Now they remind me that even on difficult days, we can still choose to be kind. And that choice can turn a cold, wet afternoon into a warm ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. lonely &emsp;&emsp; D. sleepy
2. A. warm &emsp;&emsp; B. dry &emsp;&emsp; C. safe &emsp;&emsp; D. clean
3. A. laugh &emsp;&emsp; B. talk &emsp;&emsp; C. walk &emsp;&emsp; D. sing
4. A. seem &emsp;&emsp; B. grow &emsp;&emsp; C. become &emsp;&emsp; D. sound
5. A. meant &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. cost &emsp;&emsp; D. saved
6. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. with &emsp;&emsp; C. to &emsp;&emsp; D. by
7. A. needing &emsp;&emsp; B. giving &emsp;&emsp; C. getting &emsp;&emsp; D. finding
8. A. break &emsp;&emsp; B. change &emsp;&emsp; C. waste &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
9. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. sad &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
10. A. memory &emsp;&emsp; B. story &emsp;&emsp; C. evening &emsp;&emsp; D. moment

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到寒冷且___。A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"无聊"；C 项意为"孤单"；D 项意为"困倦"。"lonely"与"stood under the small roof"、"missed the school bus"相呼应，雨天独自等车感到孤独。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这儿比较___。A 项意为"暖和"；B 项意为"干燥"；C 项意为"安全"；D 项意为"干净"。"dry"与"trying to stay dry"、"my clothes were still getting wet"相呼应，棚顶下能避雨更干燥。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们开始___。A 项意为"笑"；B 项意为"交谈"；C 项意为"走"；D 项意为"唱"。"talk"与"She told me"、"I told her"相呼应，两人聊天。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你___一个内心温暖的年轻人。A 项意为"似乎"；B 项意为"成长"；C 项意为"成为"；D 项意为"听起来"。"become"与老人的评价相呼应，夸奖"你成长为/是一个善良的人"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但她让我觉得我小小的举动___了什么。A 项意为"意味着"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"花费"；D 项意为"挽救"。"meant"与"felt that my small act"相呼应，小小的善意有意义。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当你自己也几乎没有___时。A 项意为"为"；B 项意为"和"；C 项意为"向"；D 项意为"被"。"little for yourself"指自己拥有的空间或东西也不多，仍愿意分享。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我看到街上有人___帮助时。A 项意为"需要"；B 项意为"给予"；C 项意为"得到"；D 项意为"找到"。"needing help"与"offer it"相呼应，看到需要帮助的人就伸出援手。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但它们能够___某人的一天。A 项意为"破坏"；B 项意为"改变"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"忘记"。"change someone's day"（改变某人的一天）是常见表达，指让日子变得更好。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：雨天过去常让我感到___。A 项意为"兴奋"；B 项意为"难过"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"疲惫"。"tired"与"Now they remind me"形成对比，从前雨天令人疲惫厌倦，如今有了温暖回忆。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把寒冷潮湿的下午变成温暖的___。A 项意为"回忆"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"傍晚"；D 项意为"时刻"。"moment"（时刻）与"turn...into"相呼应，善意的选择能将糟糕的下午变为温暖的时刻。故选 D。
