# 中考英语完形填空：图书馆的旧书签

## 完形填空原文

Last Tuesday, I was studying in the school library when a small piece of paper fell out of the book I had just picked up. It was an old bookmark. On it, someone had written a few words in blue ink: "Keep reading. Stories change us." There was no name, only a date from five years ago.

I sat there, holding the bookmark, feeling ___1___. Who had left it? A student like me? A teacher? I would never know. But those simple words made me think. I had been feeling stressed about the exams. I had almost ___2___ reading for pleasure. That bookmark seemed to say: "Don't forget why you started."

I decided to keep the bookmark. I put it in my pencil case. Now, whenever I feel too tired to open a book, I look at it. It ___3___ me that reading is not just for exams. It's for joy, for new ideas, and for the moments when a story touches your heart.

I also started a small habit. When I finish a book that I really ___4___, I sometimes leave a short note for the next reader. I don't sign my name. I just hope that someday, someone like me will find it and feel the same ___5___ that I felt when I found that old bookmark.

One day I ___6___ to pass this bookmark to a younger student. That would be the best lesson I could give.

The library is quiet. Thousands of books stand on the shelves. But that one old bookmark showed me that books connect us — not just with writers, but with each other. We are all ___7___, sharing stories through time. A bookmark is small. But it can carry a big message: you are not alone in your ___8___.

I still have that bookmark. The blue ink has faded a little. The paper is soft from age. But the words are still ___9___. "Keep reading. Stories change us." I believe they will change me, one book at a time. That old bookmark is more than paper — it is a ___10___ of how stories connect us across the years.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我坐在那里，拿着书签，感到___。A 项意为"无聊"；B 项意为"好奇"；C 项意为"害怕"；D 项意为"孤独"。"curious"与"Who had left it?"相呼应，想知道是谁留下的。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我几乎___为快乐而阅读。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"完成"。"stopped"与"feeling stressed about the exams"相呼应，考试压力使人放弃休闲阅读。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它___我，阅读不只是为了考试。A 项意为"问"；B 项意为"告诉"；C 项意为"提醒"；D 项意为"警告"。"reminds"与"Don't forget why you started"相呼应，书签起提醒作用。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我读完一本我真正___的书时。A 项意为"喜欢"；B 项意为"喜爱"；C 项意为"需要"；D 项意为"买"。"love"与"really"相搭配，表示真心喜爱的书，语气更强。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感到同样的___，就像我找到旧书签时的感觉。A 项意为"担忧"；B 项意为"温暖"；C 项意为"惊讶"；D 项意为"恐惧"。"warmth"与"touches your heart"相呼应，指被触动的温暖感受。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：总有一天我___把这本书签传给更年轻的学弟学妹。A 项意为"希望"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"祝愿"。"plan to do"（计划做某事）与"One day"、"the best lesson I could give"相呼应，表示已做打算。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都是___，通过时间分享故事。A 项意为"读者"；B 项意为"作者"；C 项意为"老师"；D 项意为"学生"。"readers"与"books connect us"、"sharing stories"相呼应。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在你的___中你并不孤单。A 项意为"旅程"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"工作"；D 项意为"考试"。"journey"在此指阅读与成长的旅程，与"Stories change us"相呼应。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但文字仍然___。A 项意为"清晰"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"长"；D 项意为"简单明了"。"simple"与"blue ink has faded"形成对比，虽陈旧但含义依然简洁有力。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它是关于故事如何跨越岁月连接我们的___。A 项意为"标志"；B 项意为"礼物"；C 项意为"理由"；D 项意为"故事"。"story"（故事）与"how stories connect us across the years"相呼应，书签本身成为传承的故事。故选 D。
