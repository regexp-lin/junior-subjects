# 中考英语完形填空：帮低年级同学辅导功课

## 完形填空原文

Our school started a peer tutoring program this semester. Senior students like me help younger students with their homework. I was ___1___ to tutor two Grade 7 students in math every Tuesday afternoon. At first, I wasn't sure if I could do it well, but I decided to give it a try.

The first day, I met Xiao Hua and Li Na in the library. They both looked a little shy. I smiled and introduced ___2___. I asked them what topics they found difficult. They said word problems were the hardest. I understood because I had the same ___3___ when I was in Grade 7.

We started with a simple problem. I explained the steps slowly and drew a picture to help them understand. When they got the right answer, their faces lit up. I felt happy to see their ___4___. We worked through several problems together. Sometimes they made mistakes, but I never got angry. I just asked them to think again and find the error by ___5___.

After a few weeks, both students improved a lot. Xiao Hua could solve problems faster, and Li Na became more confident. Their math teacher thanked me and said I had done a wonderful ___6___. The two students also gave me a thank-you card. I was deeply moved.

This experience taught me that teaching others is also a way of learning. When we explain things to others, we understand them ___7___ ourselves. I hope more students will join this program. Helping others brings us joy and makes our school a ___8___ place. I will continue to tutor as long as I have ___9___. Every small act of kindness can make a big ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. asked &emsp;&emsp; B. refused &emsp;&emsp; C. allowed &emsp;&emsp; D. forced
2. A. himself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. myself &emsp;&emsp; D. themselves
3. A. problem &emsp;&emsp; B. answer &emsp;&emsp; C. gift &emsp;&emsp; D. book
4. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. anger &emsp;&emsp; C. joy &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
5. A. yourself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. themselves &emsp;&emsp; D. ourselves
6. A. mistake &emsp;&emsp; B. job &emsp;&emsp; C. exam &emsp;&emsp; D. game
7. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. less &emsp;&emsp; D. more
8. A. colder &emsp;&emsp; B. warmer &emsp;&emsp; C. bigger &emsp;&emsp; D. smaller
9. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. money &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
10. A. problem &emsp;&emsp; B. difference &emsp;&emsp; C. mistake &emsp;&emsp; D. noise

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我被安排每周二下午辅导两名初一学生数学。A 项意为"被要求"；B 项意为"拒绝"；C 项意为"允许"；D 项意为"强迫"。参与辅导项目用 was asked。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我微笑着介绍自己。A 项意为"他自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"我自己"；D 项意为"他们自己"。主语是 I，介绍自己用 myself。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我理解，因为初一时也有同样的问题。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"答案"；C 项意为"礼物"；D 项意为"书"。应用题难是 problem。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到他们的喜悦我很开心。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"愤怒"；C 项意为"喜悦"；D 项意为"恐惧"。做对题后的表情是 joy。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：让他们再想想，自己找出错误。A 项意为"你自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"他们自己"；D 项意为"我们自己"。指代 Xiao Hua and Li Na 用 themselves。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：数学老师说我做得非常好。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"工作"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"游戏"。do a wonderful job 意为"做得很好"。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：向他人讲解时，我们也更好地理解知识。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更少"；D 项意为"更多"。教即学，understand better。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助他人带来快乐，让校园更温暖。A 项意为"更冷"；B 项意为"更温暖"；C 项意为"更大"；D 项意为"更小"。互助营造 warmer 氛围。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：只要有时间就会继续辅导。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"金钱"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"愤怒"。as long as I have time 表示有时间就做。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每一个小小的善举都能带来很大改变。A 项意为"问题"；B 项 make a difference 意为"产生影响"；C 项意为"错误"；D 项意为"噪音"。善举 make a big difference。故选 B。
