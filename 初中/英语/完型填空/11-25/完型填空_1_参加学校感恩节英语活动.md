# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校感恩节英语活动

## 完形填空原文

Last Thursday was Thanksgiving Day. Our school held an English activity to celebrate it. The English teachers had prepared many interesting programs. I signed up for the speech contest—we had to give a short speech in English about what we were ___1___ for. I spent two days writing and practicing my speech.

On the morning of the activity, the school hall was decorated with autumn colors. There were paper pumpkins, dried corn, and leaves on the walls. The atmosphere was warm and ___2___. The headmaster gave an opening speech. He said Thanksgiving was a time to express gratitude and to ___3___ the people who helped us. His words reminded me of my teachers, parents, and friends.

The speech contest started at 9 o'clock. I was the fifth speaker. When I waited for my turn, I felt my heart beating ___4___. But when I stood on the stage and looked at the audience, I took a deep breath and began. I talked about my gratitude to my parents for their love, to my teachers for their patience, and to my friends for their support. I spoke for about three minutes. The judges gave me a ___5___ and I felt relieved.

After the contest, we had a "Thank You Card" activity. Each student wrote thank-you cards to people they wanted to thank. I wrote one to my mom and one to my English teacher. I tried to express my feelings ___6___ and honestly. Then we exchanged cards with our classmates. Reading the cards from others made me feel ___7___ and valued.

The activity ended at noon. I learned that Thanksgiving is not just about a holiday—it is about having a grateful heart. We should remember to thank the people around us every day, not ___8___ on special occasions. I will try to say "thank you" more often from now on. This experience made me understand that gratitude can make our lives ___9___ and our relationships stronger. We will keep this wonderful memory in ___10___ hearts forever.

## 选项

1. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. thankful &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. noisy &emsp;&emsp; C. festive &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
3. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. ignore &emsp;&emsp; D. blame
4. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. fast &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. gently
5. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. smile &emsp;&emsp; C. hug &emsp;&emsp; D. reply
6. A. clearly &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. carelessly
7. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. lonely &emsp;&emsp; C. warm &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
8. A. only &emsp;&emsp; B. always &emsp;&emsp; C. never &emsp;&emsp; D. sometimes
9. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. poorer &emsp;&emsp; C. richer &emsp;&emsp; D. worse
10. A. my &emsp;&emsp; B. your &emsp;&emsp; C. our &emsp;&emsp; D. their

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用英语简短讲述我们感激什么。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项 thankful 意为"感激的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"生气的"。感恩节主题用 thankful for。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：氛围温暖而喜庆。A 项意为"冷的"；B 项意为"吵闹的"；C 项 festive 意为"节日般的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。节日庆祝用 festive。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：铭记那些帮助过我们的人。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项 remember 意为"铭记"；C 项意为"忽视"；D 项意为"责备"。感恩语境用 remember。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等待时心跳得很快。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项 fast 意为"快地"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"轻柔地"。紧张时 heart beating fast。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委对我微笑，我松了口气。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项 smile 意为"微笑"；C 项意为"拥抱"；D 项意为"回复"。评委肯定用 gave me a smile。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尽量清晰真诚地表达感受。A 项 clearly 意为"清晰地"；B 项意为"慢慢地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"粗心地"。写感谢卡用 clearly。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：读别人的卡片让我感到温暖和被重视。A 项意为"悲伤的"；B 项意为"孤独的"；C 项 warm 意为"温暖的"；D 项意为"冷漠的"。被感谢时 feel warm。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不应仅在特殊场合才感谢。A 项 only 意为"仅仅"；B 项意为"总是"；C 项意为"从不"；D 项意为"有时"。not only on special occasions 为常见表达。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：感恩让生活更丰富、关系更牢固。A 项意为"更艰难"；B 项意为"更穷"；C 项 richer 意为"更丰富"；D 项意为"更糟"。感恩使生活 richer。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们永远把这段回忆珍藏在心中。A 项意为"我的"；B 项意为"你的"；C 项 our 意为"我们的"；D 项意为"他们的"。in our hearts 指我们大家心中。故选 C。
