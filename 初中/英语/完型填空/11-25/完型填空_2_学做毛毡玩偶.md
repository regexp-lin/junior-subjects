# 中考英语完形填空：学做毛毡玩偶

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, I joined a craft workshop and learned how to make felt dolls. Felt is a kind of soft fabric made from wool. The teacher said we could make small animals or characters. I decided to make a little cat. I had seen felt toys in stores but never thought I could make one ___1___.

First, we chose the colors of wool felt. I picked gray for the body and pink for the ears. We cut the felt into shapes according to the pattern. The teacher warned us to cut ___2___ so the edges would be neat. I had to cut two body shapes, four ear pieces, and a small tail. Then we sewed them together with a needle and thread. I am not good at sewing, so I worked very ___3___. The teacher came to help me when my stitches were too loose.

The most fun part was adding the details. We used small beads for the eyes and embroidered a mouth with red thread. I also added some whiskers. The cat began to look ___4___ and cute. I stuffed a little cotton inside to make it soft before sewing the last opening. Finally, I tied a small ribbon around its neck. My felt cat was ___5___!

I showed my creation to my family. My younger sister loved it so much that she asked me to make one for her too. I promised I would make a rabbit for her next time. Making the felt doll took me about three hours, but I enjoyed every ___6___ of it. The teacher said felt crafting required patience, but the result was always ___7___.

I learned that handmade toys have special value because they carry our time and love. I will keep practicing and try to make more ___8___ designs. Maybe I can give them as gifts to my friends on their birthdays. This craft has become one of my favorite ___9___. I hope to improve my skills and create even better works in the ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. yourself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. myself &emsp;&emsp; D. itself
2. A. carelessly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. loudly
3. A. fast &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. easily
4. A. ugly &emsp;&emsp; B. terrible &emsp;&emsp; C. lively &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
5. A. broken &emsp;&emsp; B. lost &emsp;&emsp; C. done &emsp;&emsp; D. sold
6. A. minute &emsp;&emsp; B. hour &emsp;&emsp; C. second &emsp;&emsp; D. moment
7. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. worthless &emsp;&emsp; C. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
8. A. similar &emsp;&emsp; B. different &emsp;&emsp; C. same &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
9. A. subjects &emsp;&emsp; B. hobbies &emsp;&emsp; C. exams &emsp;&emsp; D. games
10. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. future &emsp;&emsp; C. morning &emsp;&emsp; D. evening

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：从没想过能亲手做一个。A 项意为"你自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项 myself 意为"亲自"；D 项意为"它自己"。强调亲手制作用 myself。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：提醒要小心剪，边缘才整齐。A 项意为"粗心地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项 carefully 意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"大声地"。精细裁剪用 cut carefully。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：缝纫不好所以做得很慢。A 项意为"快地"；B 项 slowly 意为"慢慢地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"容易地"。不熟练需 work slowly。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：猫咪开始显得生动可爱。A 项意为"丑的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项 lively 意为"生动的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。加细节后用 lively。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：毛毡猫做好了！A 项意为"坏的"；B 项意为"丢失的"；C 项 done 意为"完成"；D 项意为"卖掉的"。完成作品用 was done。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：享受每一分钟。A 项 minute 意为"分钟"；B 项意为"小时"；C 项意为"秒"；D 项意为"时刻"。enjoy every minute 为常见搭配。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：成品总是很棒。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"无价值的"；C 项 wonderful 意为"精彩的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。耐心付出后结果 wonderful。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：会继续练习，尝试更多不同的设计。A 项意为"相似的"；B 项 different 意为"不同的"；C 项意为"相同的"；D 项意为"简单的"。更多创意用 different designs。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这已成为我最喜欢的爱好之一。A 项意为"科目"；B 项 hobbies 意为"爱好"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"游戏"。手工制作属 hobby。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望未来能提升技艺、创作更好的作品。A 项意为"过去"；B 项 in the future 意为"未来"；C 项意为"早上"；D 项意为"晚上"。展望用 future。故选 B。
