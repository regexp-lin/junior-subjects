# 中考英语完形填空：暑假夏令营

## 完形填空原文

This summer I joined a seven-day camp in the mountains. It was my first time away from home for so long. I felt both excited and a little ___1___ when my parents drove me to the camp gate on the first day.

There were about fifty students from different schools. We were divided into small groups. I was in Group Five with seven other students. Our group leader was a university student named Zhang Lei. He was kind and always made us ___2___. On the first evening, we sat around a campfire and introduced ourselves. I made three new friends that night.

Every day we had different activities. We went hiking, learned to put up tents, and cooked meals together. The most ___3___ part was the night hike. We walked through the forest with torches. It was dark and a bit scary, but we held hands and encouraged each other. When we reached the top of the hill and saw the stars, we all ___4___. It was the most beautiful sight I had ever seen.

I also learned to work as a team. In one activity, we had to cross a small river by stepping on stones. We had to ___5___ each other and move slowly. Our group was the first to finish. We felt so proud! The camp taught me that we are stronger when we work together.

On the last day, we had a closing ceremony. Each group performed a short show. Our group sang a song we had practised. I will never forget the ___6___ on everyone's faces when we finished. We hugged and promised to keep in touch.

I did not want to leave. The camp had become my second ___7___. I had learned new skills, made new friends, and discovered that I could do things I never thought I could. The experience made me more ___8___ and independent.

When I got home, I immediately wrote letters to my new friends. We still chat online now. I hope we can all go back to the camp next year. That week in the mountains was the best ___9___ of my summer. It changed me in ways I never expected.

I am already looking forward to next summer. Maybe I can become a group leader like Zhang Lei one day and help other kids have the same wonderful ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一天父母送我到营地时，我感到既兴奋又有点___。A 项意为"开心"；B 项意为"紧张"；C 项意为"生气"；D 项意为"无聊"。第一次离家这么久会紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他和善，总让我们___。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"笑"；C 项意为"睡"；D 项意为"争论"。领队让大家开心，"laugh"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最___的部分是夜行。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"困难的"；C 项意为"令人兴奋的"；D 项意为"容易的"。夜行森林很刺激，"exciting"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词/动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到星星时我们都___。A 项意为"坐下"；B 项意为"喊"；C 项意为"笑"；D 项意为"欢呼"。看到美景时欢呼，"cheered"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须互相___，慢慢移动。A 项意为"帮助"；B 项意为"推"；C 项意为"责备"；D 项意为"忘记"。过河需要互相帮助，"help"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记我们结束时每个人脸上的___。A 项意为"泪水"；B 项意为"笑容"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"恐惧"。表演成功后大家笑，"smiles"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：营地成了我的第二个___。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"家"；C 项意为"房间"；D 项意为"城市"。"second home"意为第二个家，表达对营地的感情。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经历让我更___和独立。A 项意为"害羞"；B 项意为"自信"；C 项意为"懒惰"；D 项意为"害怕"。夏令营锻炼后更自信，"confident"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：山中那一周是暑假最美好的___。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"周"；C 项意为"回忆"；D 项意为"游戏"。美好经历成为回忆，"memory"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：帮助其他孩子拥有同样美好的___。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"天气"；C 项意为"经历"；D 项意为"奖品"。传承夏令营的美好经历，"experience"正确。故选 C。
