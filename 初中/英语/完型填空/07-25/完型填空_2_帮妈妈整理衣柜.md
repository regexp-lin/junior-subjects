# 中考英语完形填空：帮妈妈整理衣柜

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, Mum asked me to help her tidy the wardrobe. I had nothing else to do, so I agreed. I did not expect it to be fun, but it turned out to be a special ___1___ with my mum.

We took everything out of the wardrobe and laid it on the bed. There were winter coats, summer dresses, and many things Mum had not worn for years. "Let us ___2___ what to keep and what to give away," Mum said. We went through each piece together. Some clothes brought back memories. Mum held up an old dress and said, "I wore this on my first date with your dad." Her eyes were ___3___.

I found a small box at the bottom. Inside were old photos and letters. Mum sat down and looked through them. She told me stories about her childhood and how she met Dad. I had never heard some of these ___4___ before. It was like discovering a secret part of my family.

We decided to give away three bags of clothes to people in need. Mum said that things we no longer use can still ___5___ others. "One person's old coat can be another person's warmth," she told me. I felt good knowing we were helping someone.

After four hours, the wardrobe was clean and ___6___. Everything had its place. Mum hugged me and said, "Thank you for your help. I could not have done it without you." I realised that sometimes the best way to show ___7___ is to spend time together and help with simple tasks.

That afternoon, I learned more about my mum than I had in years. I learned that she keeps old things because they hold ___8___. I learned that she cares about helping others. Most importantly, I learned that a boring job can become meaningful when you do it with someone you ___9___.

I will never complain about helping with housework again. It is not just work — it is a chance to be with family and learn their ___10___. I am grateful for that Saturday with Mum.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：结果成了和妈妈特别的___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"时光"；C 项意为"课程"；D 项意为"争吵"。"time with Mum"意为与妈妈共度的时光，正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们___哪些保留、哪些捐出。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"决定"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"卖"。整理时要决定取舍，"decide"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的眼睛___。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"明亮的/有神的"；C 项意为"闭着的"；D 项意为"生气的"。回忆美好往事时眼睛会亮，"bright"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以前从未听过一些这些___。A 项意为"歌"；B 项意为"故事"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"课程"。妈妈讲述的是往事故事，"stories"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们不再用的东西仍能___别人。A 项意为"伤害"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"打扰"；D 项意为"惊吓"。捐出旧衣帮助有需要的人，"help"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：衣柜变得干净且___。A 项意为"空的"；B 项意为"脏的"；C 项意为"整洁的"；D 项意为"满的"。整理后衣柜整洁，"tidy"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表达___的最好方式是一起度过时光。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"爱"；D 项意为"骄傲"。帮妈妈干活是表达爱，"love"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她保留旧物是因为它们承载___。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"秘密"。旧物承载回忆，"memories"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与你___的人一起做时，无聊的事会变得有意义。A 项意为"恨"；B 项意为"爱"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"责备"。与所爱的人一起做事，"love"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是与家人相处、了解他们___的机会。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"名字"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"习惯"。前文妈妈讲了童年故事等，了解家人的故事，"stories"正确。故选 C。
