# 中考英语完形填空：搬家那天

## 完形填空原文

The moving truck arrived at seven in the morning. My parents had been packing for weeks, but I had refused to ___1___. Putting things into boxes meant admitting that we were really leaving.

I had lived in this apartment since I was born. Every scratch on the wall, every stain on the carpet had a ___2___ behind it. The mark near the door was from the time I tried to ride my bicycle indoors. The crayon drawings on my bedroom wall were from when I was five.

"We need to go, Xiao Yu," my father said gently. "The new city will be ___3___ too, I promise."

I didn't answer. I walked through the empty rooms one last time. Without furniture, they looked much ___4___ than I remembered. The echoes of my footsteps bounced off the bare walls.

In my room, I found something under the windowsill — a small plastic dinosaur. It was the toy I had ___5___ everywhere when I was little. I thought I had lost it years ago. I put it in my pocket.

Downstairs, my best friend, Zhou Hao, was waiting by the truck. He handed me a small ___6___ wrapped in newspaper. "Open it later," he said, looking at the ground. His voice was shaking.

We hugged without speaking. I got into the car and looked back as we drove away. Zhou Hao stood on the sidewalk, waving until we turned the ___7___. Then he was gone.

The drive to the new city took six hours. I spent most of it staring out the window, watching the ___8___ scenery change from city to countryside to city again. My mother tried to start conversations, but I gave only short answers.

When we finally arrived at the new apartment, I went to my room and sat on the bare floor. I opened Zhou Hao's gift. Inside was a photo album — pictures of us from first grade to ninth grade. On the last page, he had written: "New places, same ___9___. Call me anytime."

I smiled for the first time that day. Home might be a new address now, but ___10___ like Zhou Hao — that was something no move could take away.

1. A. agree &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. speak
2. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. rule &emsp;&emsp; D. secret
3. A. small &emsp;&emsp; B. nice &emsp;&emsp; C. quiet &emsp;&emsp; D. old
4. A. darker &emsp;&emsp; B. warmer &emsp;&emsp; C. bigger &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaner
5. A. hidden &emsp;&emsp; B. carried &emsp;&emsp; C. broken &emsp;&emsp; D. thrown
6. A. box &emsp;&emsp; B. letter &emsp;&emsp; C. package &emsp;&emsp; D. bag
7. A. corner &emsp;&emsp; B. page &emsp;&emsp; C. light &emsp;&emsp; D. key
8. A. inside &emsp;&emsp; B. outside &emsp;&emsp; C. front &emsp;&emsp; D. back
9. A. family &emsp;&emsp; B. friendship &emsp;&emsp; C. dream &emsp;&emsp; D. address
10. A. trust &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. friendship

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：父母打包了好几周，但我拒绝___。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"帮忙"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"说话"。后文说把东西装进箱子就等于承认要搬走了，所以"我"拒绝"帮忙"（help）打包。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：墙上的每个划痕、地毯上的每个污渍后面都有一个___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"秘密"。后文举了骑自行车留下的印记、蜡笔画等例子，每个痕迹都有一个"故事"（story）。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新城市也会很___的。A 项意为"小的"；B 项意为"好的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"旧的"。爸爸安慰孩子新地方也会"好"（nice），不用太担心。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（比较级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：没了家具，房间看起来比记忆中___得多。A 项意为"更暗"；B 项意为"更暖"；C 项意为"更大"；D 项意为"更干净"。家具搬走后空荡荡的，房间显得比以前"更大"（bigger）。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我小时候到哪里都___的玩具。A 项意为"藏"；B 项意为"带"；C 项意为"弄坏"；D 项意为"扔"。小时候最喜欢的玩具走到哪"带"（carried）到哪。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他递给我一个用报纸包着的小___。A 项意为"盒子"；B 项意为"信"；C 项意为"包裹"；D 项意为"袋子"。用报纸"包着"的东西，用"包裹"（package）最合适，后面打开发现是相册。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：周浩站在人行道上挥手，直到我们转过___。A 项意为"拐角"；B 项意为"页"；C 项意为"灯"；D 项意为"钥匙"。"turn the corner"（转弯）是固定搭配，车子拐过街角后就看不到朋友了。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我大部分时间望着窗外，看___的风景变化。A 项意为"里面"；B 项意为"外面"；C 项意为"前面"；D 项意为"后面"。透过车窗看"外面"（outside）的景色从城市变乡村再变城市。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新地方，同样的___。A 项意为"家人"；B 项意为"友谊"；C 项意为"梦想"；D 项意为"地址"。地方变了但"友谊"（friendship）不变，这是周浩传达的核心信息。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但像周浩这样的___——是搬家带不走的。A 项意为"信任"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"友谊"。呼应全文主题，地址可以变但真正的"友谊"（friendship）永远不会因距离而消失。故选 D。
