# 中考英语完形填空：学做三明治

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday morning, my mother was busy with housework, so she asked me to make lunch for myself. I had never cooked before, but I decided to try making a sandwich. It seemed simple ___1___.

I opened the fridge and took out bread, eggs, vegetables, and butter. First, I heated the frying pan and broke two eggs into it. I had to be ___2___ not to break the yolks. Then I sprinkled a little salt on the eggs. When they were cooked, I put them on a plate.

Next, I spread some butter on two ___3___ of bread. I placed lettuce and tomato slices on one piece, then added the eggs. I put the other piece on top and pressed gently. The sandwich looked good, but when I took a bite, I found it was too ___4___. I had forgotten to add any flavouring!

I went back to the kitchen and tried again. This time I added a little mayonnaise and a pinch of salt. The second sandwich tasted much ___5___. My mother came in and smiled when she saw my creation. "Well done! You learned something new today," she said.

From that day on, I have made sandwiches several times. Each time I try different ___6___, like cheese or ham. I also learned that cooking needs practice. The first try might not be perfect, but if we keep ___7___, we will get better.

Making my own food gives me a sense of ___8___. I feel proud when I can prepare a simple meal. My little brother often asks me to make sandwiches for him too. Maybe one day I will learn to cook more ___9___ dishes, but for now, I am happy with my sandwiches. As the saying goes, "Practice makes ___10___."

## 选项

1. A. enough &emsp;&emsp; B. too &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. very
2. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. careful &emsp;&emsp; C. brave &emsp;&emsp; D. strong
3. A. bags &emsp;&emsp; B. pieces &emsp;&emsp; C. boxes &emsp;&emsp; D. kinds
4. A. sweet &emsp;&emsp; B. salty &emsp;&emsp; C. bitter &emsp;&emsp; D. plain
5. A. worse &emsp;&emsp; B. better &emsp;&emsp; C. stranger &emsp;&emsp; D. hotter
6. A. tools &emsp;&emsp; B. ingredients &emsp;&emsp; C. recipes &emsp;&emsp; D. methods
7. A. waiting &emsp;&emsp; B. trying &emsp;&emsp; C. dreaming &emsp;&emsp; D. worrying
8. A. achievement &emsp;&emsp; B. sadness &emsp;&emsp; C. fear &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
9. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. expensive &emsp;&emsp; C. common &emsp;&emsp; D. terrible
10. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. perfect &emsp;&emsp; C. friends &emsp;&emsp; D. mistakes

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做三明治似乎___简单。A 项意为"足够地"；B 项意为"太"；C 项意为"相当"；D 项意为"非常"。"simple enough"意为"足够简单"，符合"决定尝试"的语境。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不得不___不要弄破蛋黄。A 项意为"快速的"；B 项意为"小心的"；C 项意为"勇敢的"；D 项意为"强壮的"。打鸡蛋时要小心保持蛋黄完整，"careful"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在两___面包上涂了黄油。A 项意为"袋"；B 项意为"片/块"；C 项意为"盒"；D 项意为"种"。三明治用两片面包，"pieces"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：咬一口发现太___了。A 项意为"甜的"；B 项意为"咸的"；C 项意为"苦的"；D 项意为"清淡的/无味的"。后文说忘记加调味料，所以味道清淡，"plain"正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二个三明治味道___多了。A 项意为"更差"；B 项意为"更好"；C 项意为"更奇怪"；D 项意为"更辣"。加了蛋黄酱和盐后味道改善，"better"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每次我尝试不同的___，如奶酪或火腿。A 项意为"工具"；B 项意为"配料"；C 项意为"食谱"；D 项意为"方法"。奶酪、火腿是食材配料，"ingredients"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果我们继续___，我们会越来越好。A 项意为"等待"；B 项意为"尝试"；C 项意为"做梦"；D 项意为"担心"。坚持练习才会进步，"trying"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：自己做食物给我一种___感。A 项意为"成就"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"惊讶"。能自己做饭带来成就感，"achievement"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许有一天我会学做更___的菜。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"普通的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。从三明治到更复杂的菜，"difficult"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：正如谚语所说："熟能生___。" A 项意为"金钱"；B 项意为"完美的"；C 项意为"朋友"；D 项意为"错误"。"Practice makes perfect"为固定谚语，意为"熟能生巧"。故选 B。
