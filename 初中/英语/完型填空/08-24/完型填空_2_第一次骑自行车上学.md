# 中考英语完形填空：第一次骑自行车上学

## 完形填空原文

This term, my father bought me a new bicycle. He said I was old enough to ride to school by myself. I was both excited and a little ___1___. The school was about twenty minutes away by bike.

On the first morning, I got up early and had a quick breakfast. I checked the tyres and made sure the brakes worked properly. Safety was the most ___2___ thing, my father had told me. I waved goodbye to my mother and set off.

The road was busy with cars and other cyclists. I stayed on the right side and kept my eyes on the road ___3___. When I reached a busy crossing, I got off and pushed my bike across. My father had taught me to never take risks. I would rather be a few minutes ___4___ than have an accident.

About halfway to school, I saw my classmate Li Ming riding ahead. I called his name and he stopped to wait for me. We rode the ___5___ part of the way together. He had been cycling to school for a year and gave me some useful tips. "Avoid the main road during rush hour if you can," he said. "The small streets are quieter and safer."

When I arrived at school, I felt a great sense of ___6___. I had completed my first solo bike ride! I locked my bike in the school yard and ran to my classroom. My friends asked how my ride was. "It was wonderful!" I said. "I think I will ride every day from now on."

Cycling to school has many ___7___. It helps me wake up in the morning and saves bus fare. It is also good for the environment. Best of all, I feel more independent. I don't need to ___8___ on my parents to take me to school anymore.

Now, two months later, cycling has become a part of my daily routine. I even enjoy the ride when the weather is fine. The morning ___9___ and fresh air make me feel energetic for the whole day. My first ride taught me that trying something new can bring pleasant ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
2. A. interesting &emsp;&emsp; B. expensive &emsp;&emsp; C. important &emsp;&emsp; D. difficult
3. A. carefully &emsp;&emsp; B. slowly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. happily
4. A. early &emsp;&emsp; B. late &emsp;&emsp; C. fast &emsp;&emsp; D. far
5. A. first &emsp;&emsp; B. last &emsp;&emsp; C. same &emsp;&emsp; D. long
6. A. pride &emsp;&emsp; B. regret &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. shame
7. A. problems &emsp;&emsp; B. advantages &emsp;&emsp; C. questions &emsp;&emsp; D. disadvantages
8. A. depend &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. call &emsp;&emsp; D. look
9. A. sunshine &emsp;&emsp; B. rain &emsp;&emsp; C. snow &emsp;&emsp; D. wind
10. A. memories &emsp;&emsp; B. surprises &emsp;&emsp; C. challenges &emsp;&emsp; D. results

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我既兴奋又有点___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"疲劳的"。第一次独自骑车上学，紧张符合常理，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：安全是最___的事。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"昂贵的"；C 项意为"重要的"；D 项意为"困难的"。父亲强调安全最重要，"important"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我双眼___盯着路面。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"慢慢地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"开心地"。注意安全需仔细观察路况，"carefully"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我宁愿迟到几分钟也不愿发生事故。A 项意为"早的"；B 项意为"晚的"；C 项意为"快的"；D 项意为"远的"。"a few minutes late"意为"晚几分钟"，符合语境。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一起骑了___一段路。A 项意为"第一的"；B 项意为"最后的"；C 项意为"相同的"；D 项意为"长的"。在半路遇见，剩下的是后半程，"last"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：到达学校时我感到强烈的___感。A 项意为"骄傲"；B 项意为"遗憾"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"羞愧"。完成第一次独自骑行感到自豪，"pride"正确。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：骑车上学有很多___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"优点"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"缺点"。后文列举好处，应用"advantages"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不再需要___父母送我了。A 项意为"依靠"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"打电话"；D 项意为"看"。"depend on"意为"依靠"，符合语境。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：早晨的___和新鲜空气让我一整天精力充沛。A 项意为"阳光"；B 项意为"雨"；C 项意为"雪"；D 项意为"风"。"weather is fine"对应阳光，"sunshine"正确。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尝试新事物能带来愉快的___。A 项意为"回忆"；B 项意为"惊喜"；C 项意为"挑战"；D 项意为"结果"。第一次骑车上学带来的是惊喜体验，"surprises"正确。故选 B。
