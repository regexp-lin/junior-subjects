# 中考英语完形填空：社区义卖活动

## 完形填空原文

Our neighbourhood held a charity fair last Saturday. The money would be used to help poor children in the village schools. I decided to take part and ___1___ some of my old books, toys, and clothes to sell.

I set up a small table in front of my house early that morning. I arranged everything neatly and wrote price tags. My little brother wanted to help, so I let him be in charge of the toy section. He was very ___2___ and welcomed every customer with a big smile.

The first person to stop was an old lady. She picked up a storybook and asked, "How much is this?" I said three yuan. She paid and said, "My granddaughter will love it. Thank you for doing this good ___3___." Her words made me feel warm inside.

More and more people came. A mother bought several picture books for her children. A middle-aged man ___4___ a box of old stamps that my father had given me. He said he collected stamps and was happy to find something interesting. I was glad that my things could find new owners who would ___5___ them.

By noon, we had sold almost half of our items. We used the money to buy some drinks and shared them with other volunteers. Everyone was tired but ___6___. We were working together for a good cause.

In the afternoon, a little girl came with her mother. She looked at a stuffed bear for a long time. Her mother said they could not afford it. I saw the ___7___ in the girl's eyes. I walked over and said, "This bear is a gift for you. You can have it for free." The girl's face lit up with joy. Her mother thanked me again and again. That moment made the whole day ___8___ for me.

When the fair ended, we counted the money. Our neighbourhood had raised over two thousand yuan. The organiser said we would buy school bags and stationery for the village children. I felt proud to have ___9___ to this. Even a small action can make a ___10___ when we work together.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定参加并___一些旧书、玩具和衣服来卖。A 项意为"扔掉"；B 项意为"带来"；C 项意为"藏起来"；D 项意为"借"。义卖需要带来物品，"bring"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他非常___，用大大的笑容迎接每位顾客。A 项意为"害羞的"；B 项意为"诚实的"；C 项意为"精力充沛的"；D 项意为"安静的"。热情接待顾客需要活力，"energetic"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：谢谢你做这件好___。A 项意为"工作"；B 项意为"好事/善行"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"旅行"。"good deed"意为"善行"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一位中年男子___了一盒爸爸给我的旧邮票。A 项意为"归还"；B 项意为"挑选"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"丢失"。"choose"意为"选择购买"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的东西能找到会___它们的新主人。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"打破"；C 项意为"珍惜"；D 项意为"浪费"。东西被珍惜才体现价值，"value"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都很累但___。A 项意为"困倦的"；B 项意为"饥饿的"；C 项意为"开心的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。做善事虽累但内心快乐，"happy"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到女孩眼中的___。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"渴望/希望"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"怀疑"。想要熊但买不起，眼里是渴望，"hope"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那一刻让整一天对我来说变得___。A 项意为"更长"；B 项意为"特别"；C 项意为"更忙"；D 项意为"更容易"。赠熊的善举让这天有了特别意义，"special"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：能为这___我感到骄傲。A 项意为"回复"；B 项意为"贡献"；C 项意为"反对"；D 项意为"同意"。"contribute to"意为"为……做贡献"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：小小的行动汇聚起来也能产生___。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"改变/不同"；D 项意为"噪音"。"make a difference"意为"产生影响、有所作为"。故选 C。
