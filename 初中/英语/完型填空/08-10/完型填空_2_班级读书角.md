# 中考英语完形填空：班级读书角

## 完形填空原文

Our class started a reading corner this term. Our headteacher brought a bookshelf and asked each of us to ___1___ one or two books that we had read and enjoyed. Soon the shelf was full of storybooks, science books, and magazines.

I donated a copy of Robinson Crusoe. I had read it during the summer holiday and found it very ___2___. I hoped other classmates would enjoy it too. Wang Lei brought three detective stories. Li Na brought a book about space. Our teacher also added some classic novels that she thought we would like.

We made some simple ___3___. First, we could borrow one book at a time. Second, we had to return it within two weeks. Third, we should keep the books clean and not write or draw in them. Everyone agreed to follow these rules.

At first, only a few students went to the reading corner. Most of us were busy with homework and had little ___4___ for reading. But our teacher encouraged us to read for at least fifteen minutes every day. "Reading is not a task," she said. "It's a way to explore new worlds and ___5___ your imagination."

Gradually, more students became interested. During the morning break, some of us would sit by the bookshelf and read. We sometimes shared our thoughts about the books we had read. "Did you finish that mystery story?" "Yes! The ending was ___6___! I never expected it." These conversations made the reading corner a lively place.

One day, I noticed that a book was missing. It was the one about space that Li Na had brought. We searched everywhere but could not find it. I felt a bit ___7___. Would the reading corner fall apart if books started disappearing?

The next day, a boy from another class came to our room. He said he had accidentally taken the book home because he thought it was his. He ___8___ and returned it. We were relieved. The experience taught us that mistakes can happen, but being honest and returning things is the right thing to do.

Now the reading corner is one of the most popular places in our classroom. Many of us have discovered new ___9___ through the books we read. Some love science fiction, some prefer history, and some enjoy poetry. Reading has brought us closer together and made our class more ___10___.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任让每人___一两本读过且喜欢的书。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"买"；C 项意为"分享"；D 项意为"卖"。读书角靠大家分享书籍，"share"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：暑假读过觉得非常___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"令人兴奋的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。《鲁滨逊漂流记》情节刺激，"exciting"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们制定了一些简单的___。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"规则"；C 项意为"笑话"；D 项意为"礼物"。后文列出借阅规定，"rules"正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家作业多，___很少。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"空间"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"帮助"。忙于作业导致阅读时间少，"time"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：是探索新世界、___想象力的方式。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"限制"；C 项意为"发展"；D 项意为"忘记"。"develop imagination"意为"培养想象力"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：结局太___了！完全没想到。A 项意为"奇怪的"；B 项意为"令人惊讶的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。推理小说结局出人意料，"surprising"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我有点___。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"失望的"；C 项意为"放松的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。书丢了让人失望，"disappointed"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他___并把书还了回来。A 项意为"笑"；B 项意为"道歉"；C 项意为"争论"；D 项意为"成功"。误拿后应道歉并归还，"apologized"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多人在阅读中发现了新的___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"错误"。读书发现兴趣和爱好，"hobbies"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阅读让我们更亲近，班级更___。A 项意为"吵闹的"；B 项意为"团结的"；C 项意为"竞争的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。共同爱好增进团结，"united"正确。故选 B。
