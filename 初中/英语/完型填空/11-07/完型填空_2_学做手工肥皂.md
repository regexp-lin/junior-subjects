# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工肥皂

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, my mother and I attended a handmade soap workshop at the community center. I had always been curious about how soap was ___1___, so I was very happy to join this activity.

The instructor was a friendly lady who had been making soap for over ten years. She first introduced the basic ___2___: soap base, essential oils, dried flower petals, and silicone molds. She said that natural handmade soap is much better for our skin than factory-made ones.

We started by cutting the soap base into small ___3___. Then we put them into a glass bowl and heated it in a microwave for about one minute. The soap base slowly melted into a clear liquid. The instructor reminded us to be ___4___ because the liquid was very hot.

Next, we added a few drops of lavender essential oil. A lovely ___5___ immediately filled the room. Then we added some purple dried flower petals. The instructor said we could also add food coloring if we wanted our soap to be a special ___6___.

After mixing everything well, we poured the liquid into the molds. I chose a star-shaped mold and a heart-shaped one. We had to wait about thirty minutes for the soap to become ___7___.

When I took my soaps out of the molds, they looked like little works of art. The star-shaped one was my ___8___ because it had tiny flower petals inside that looked beautiful.

I brought the soaps home and put one in the bathroom. When I washed my hands with it, my skin felt very ___9___. I gave the heart-shaped one to my grandmother as a gift. She loved it and said it was the most ___10___ gift she had ever received.

## 选项

1. A. sold &emsp;&emsp; B. made &emsp;&emsp; C. used &emsp;&emsp; D. broken
2. A. rules &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. ingredients &emsp;&emsp; D. machines
3. A. pieces &emsp;&emsp; B. lines &emsp;&emsp; C. circles &emsp;&emsp; D. holes
4. A. quick &emsp;&emsp; B. quiet &emsp;&emsp; C. careful &emsp;&emsp; D. brave
5. A. taste &emsp;&emsp; B. sound &emsp;&emsp; C. sight &emsp;&emsp; D. smell
6. A. size &emsp;&emsp; B. color &emsp;&emsp; C. shape &emsp;&emsp; D. weight
7. A. liquid &emsp;&emsp; B. hot &emsp;&emsp; C. soft &emsp;&emsp; D. solid
8. A. favorite &emsp;&emsp; B. biggest &emsp;&emsp; C. cheapest &emsp;&emsp; D. heaviest
9. A. rough &emsp;&emsp; B. smooth &emsp;&emsp; C. dry &emsp;&emsp; D. cold
10. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. common &emsp;&emsp; C. thoughtful &emsp;&emsp; D. simple

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我一直好奇肥皂是怎么制作的。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"制作"；C 项意为"使用"；D 项意为"打碎"。how soap was made 意为"肥皂怎么做的"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她先介绍了基本原料。A 项意为"规则"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"原料/配料"；D 项意为"机器"。皂基、精油等是 ingredients。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把皂基切成小块。A 项意为"块"；B 项意为"线"；C 项意为"圆"；D 项意为"洞"。cut into small pieces 意为"切成小块"。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师提醒我们要小心因为液体很烫。A 项意为"快的"；B 项意为"安静的"；C 项意为"小心的"；D 项意为"勇敢的"。热液体需要 be careful。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一股可爱的香味立刻充满了房间。A 项意为"味道"；B 项意为"声音"；C 项意为"景象"；D 项意为"气味"。精油产生 lovely smell。故选 D。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果想让肥皂有特殊颜色可以加食用色素。A 项意为"大小"；B 项意为"颜色"；C 项意为"形状"；D 项意为"重量"。food coloring 用来改变 color。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：等大约三十分钟让肥皂变成固体。A 项意为"液体的"；B 项意为"热的"；C 项意为"软的"；D 项意为"固体的"。液体凝固变 solid。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：星星形状的是我最喜欢的。A 项意为"最喜欢的"；B 项意为"最大的"；C 项意为"最便宜的"；D 项意为"最重的"。因为里面有花瓣所以是 favorite。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用它洗手后皮肤感觉很光滑。A 项意为"粗糙的"；B 项意为"光滑的"；C 项意为"干的"；D 项意为"冷的"。天然肥皂洗后 smooth。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：奶奶说这是她收到过的最贴心的礼物。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"普通的"；C 项意为"贴心的/周到的"；D 项意为"简单的"。手工礼物是 thoughtful gift。故选 C。
