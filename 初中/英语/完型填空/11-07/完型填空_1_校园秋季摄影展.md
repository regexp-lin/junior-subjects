# 中考英语完形填空：校园秋季摄影展

## 完形填空原文

Our school organized an autumn photography exhibition last week. Students were invited to ___1___ photos about "The Beauty of Autumn." I love taking photos, so I was very excited about it.

During the past month, I had taken many autumn photos. I chose my three best ones to enter the exhibition. The first photo was of golden ___2___ falling from a big tree in the park. The second one showed a beautiful sunset behind the school building. The third was a close-up of morning dew on red maple leaves.

On the day of the exhibition, all the photos were displayed in the school hall. There were over one hundred photos from different students. I was ___3___ to see so many wonderful works. Some photos showed autumn fields, some showed fruit harvests, and others captured autumn animals.

Many teachers and students came to ___4___ the exhibition. They walked slowly and stopped in front of the photos they liked. I noticed that my sunset photo attracted a lot of ___5___. Several people said it was very beautiful.

At the end of the day, the judges announced the ___6___. My sunset photo won second prize! I was so surprised and happy. The first prize went to a student who took a photo of an old man feeding birds in an autumn ___7___.

Our art teacher said that photography helps us ___8___ the world more carefully. Sometimes we walk past beautiful things without noticing them. A camera can help us stop and ___9___ the moment.

This experience encouraged me to keep taking photos. I want to capture every ___10___ in life and share them with others.

2. A. snow &emsp;&emsp; B. flowers &emsp;&emsp; C. leaves &emsp;&emsp; D. stones
3. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. amazed &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
4. A. build &emsp;&emsp; B. close &emsp;&emsp; C. visit &emsp;&emsp; D. clean

6. A. prices &emsp;&emsp; B. rules &emsp;&emsp; C. results &emsp;&emsp; D. secrets
7. A. classroom &emsp;&emsp; B. park &emsp;&emsp; C. kitchen &emsp;&emsp; D. hospital
8. A. change &emsp;&emsp; B. observe &emsp;&emsp; C. protect &emsp;&emsp; D. imagine
9. A. waste &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoy &emsp;&emsp; D. miss
10. A. season &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. exam &emsp;&emsp; D. difficulty

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学生被邀请提交关于"秋之美"的照片。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"提交"；C 项意为"删除"；D 项意为"复制"。submit photos 意为"提交照片"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一张照片是金色的落叶。A 项意为"雪"；B 项意为"花"；C 项意为"叶子"；D 项意为"石头"。秋天 golden leaves 飘落。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到这么多精彩作品我很惊叹。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"惊叹的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。很多优秀作品让人 amazed。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很多师生来参观展览。A 项意为"建造"；B 项意为"关闭"；C 项意为"参观"；D 项意为"打扫"。visit the exhibition 意为"参观展览"。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的日落照片吸引了很多关注。A 项意为"关注/注意"；B 项意为"麻烦"；C 项意为"噪音"；D 项意为"危险"。attracted attention 意为"吸引注意力"。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：评委宣布了结果。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"规则"；C 项意为"结果"；D 项意为"秘密"。announced the results 意为"宣布结果"。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一等奖是一位同学拍的秋天公园里老人喂鸟的照片。A 项意为"教室"；B 项意为"公园"；C 项意为"厨房"；D 项意为"医院"。在 autumn park 喂鸟。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：摄影帮助我们更仔细地观察世界。A 项意为"改变"；B 项意为"观察"；C 项意为"保护"；D 项意为"想象"。observe the world 意为"观察世界"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：相机帮助我们停下来享受那个瞬间。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"错过"。enjoy the moment 意为"享受当下"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想捕捉生活中的每个季节。A 项意为"季节"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"困难"。capture every season 呼应秋季摄影主题。故选 A。
