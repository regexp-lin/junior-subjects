# 中考英语完形填空：学做姜饼人

## 完形填空原文

Last week, my aunt taught me how to make gingerbread men. She said it was a ___1___ tradition during Christmas in Western countries. I was curious and wanted to try it myself.

First, we mixed flour, butter, sugar, and some spices together. The dough had to be kneaded for at least ten minutes until it became ___2___. My arms were tired, but my aunt said this step was very important. Then we wrapped the dough in plastic and put it in the fridge for half an hour.

After that, we rolled out the dough on a flat surface. I used a gingerbread man cookie cutter to ___3___ the shapes. I made six figures and placed them carefully on a baking tray. My aunt said we should leave some ___4___ between each cookie so they wouldn't stick together when they expanded in the oven.

We baked them for about twelve minutes. The kitchen smelled ___5___. When the gingerbread men were ready, we took them out and let them cool down. Then came the fun part—decorating! I used icing and colored sugar to draw faces and buttons on ___6___. I made one with a big smile and another with a funny hat.

I gave two gingerbread men to my little cousin. She was so ___7___ that she wanted to learn how to make them too. My aunt was happy to see us enjoying ___8___. She said cooking together could bring families closer.

Making gingerbread men was not as ___9___ as I had thought. The key was to follow the steps carefully. I have written down the recipe and plan to make them again during the winter holiday. Maybe I can ___10___ them as gifts to my friends and teachers.

## 选项

1. A. new &emsp;&emsp; B. popular &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
2. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. dry &emsp;&emsp; D. wet
3. A. draw &emsp;&emsp; B. cut &emsp;&emsp; C. paint &emsp;&emsp; D. build
4. A. space &emsp;&emsp; B. water &emsp;&emsp; C. sugar &emsp;&emsp; D. glue
5. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. wonderful &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. awful
6. A. them &emsp;&emsp; B. it &emsp;&emsp; C. him &emsp;&emsp; D. her
7. A. sad &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
8. A. himself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. ourselves &emsp;&emsp; D. themselves
9. A. interesting &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. difficult &emsp;&emsp; D. simple
10. A. buy &emsp;&emsp; B. sell &emsp;&emsp; C. give &emsp;&emsp; D. throw

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是西方国家圣诞节的传统习俗。A 项意为"新的"；B 项意为"流行的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。Christmas tradition 用 popular。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：揉至少十分钟直到面团变软。A 项意为"柔软的"；B 项意为"硬的"；C 项意为"干的"；D 项意为"湿的"。kneaded dough 应变得 soft。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用姜饼人模具切出形状。A 项意为"画"；B 项意为"切"；C 项意为"画/涂"；D 项意为"建造"。cookie cutter 用来 cut 形状。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个饼干之间要留空隙。A 项意为"空间"；B 项意为"水"；C 项意为"糖"；D 项意为"胶水"。防止粘连需留 space。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：厨房闻起来很棒。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美妙的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"可怕的"。烘焙香气用 wonderful。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：用糖霜在它们上面画脸和纽扣。A 项意为"它们"；B 项意为"它"；C 项意为"他"；D 项意为"她"。指代多个姜饼人用 them。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：表妹非常兴奋也想学做。A 项意为"难过的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"担心的"。收到礼物后 excited。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：姑姑很高兴看到我们玩得开心。A 项意为"他自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"我们自己"；D 项意为"他们自己"。指代"我"和表妹用 ourselves。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：做姜饼人没有想象中那么难。A 项意为"有趣的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"困难的"；D 项意为"简单的"。not as difficult as 表示比预期简单。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：可以当作礼物送给朋友和老师。A 项意为"买"；B 项意为"卖"；C 项意为"送给"；D 项意为"扔掉"。give as gifts 意为"作为礼物赠送"。故选 C。
