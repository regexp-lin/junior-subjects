# 中考英语完形填空：参加班级读书分享会

## 完形填空原文

Yesterday afternoon, our class held a book sharing activity. Each of us had to introduce a book we had read and explain ___1___ we liked it. I was a little nervous because I'm not good at speaking in front of others.

I had chosen a book called "The Little Prince". I prepared for a whole week. I wrote down the main ideas and ___2___ my speech several times at home. When my turn came, I stood up and walked to the front of the classroom. My heart was beating ___3___.

I started to share my thoughts about the book. I told my classmates that the story taught me about friendship and responsibility. The more I spoke, the ___4___ I felt. My teacher nodded and smiled, which made me more confident. When I finished, everyone clapped. I was so relieved and happy.

Zhang Wei shared a science fiction novel. His ___5___ was very vivid and we all felt as if we were in the story. Li Mei talked about a history book. She had made a mind map to help us understand the timeline. I learned a lot from ___6___ presentations.

Our headteacher said that reading broadens our minds and sharing helps us learn from each other. She encouraged us to ___7___ this activity every month. I think it's a wonderful idea. Reading has become one of my favorite ___8___ now. I have already chosen the book for next month's sharing—a novel about a young inventor. I can't ___9___ to tell my classmates about it. This experience made me realize that sharing knowledge can be as ___10___ as gaining it.

## 选项

1. A. what &emsp;&emsp; B. why &emsp;&emsp; C. how &emsp;&emsp; D. when
2. A. practiced &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. wrote &emsp;&emsp; D. copied
3. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. gently
4. A. more nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. less nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. more tired &emsp;&emsp; D. less tired
5. A. writing &emsp;&emsp; B. description &emsp;&emsp; C. drawing &emsp;&emsp; D. question
6. A. their &emsp;&emsp; B. his &emsp;&emsp; C. her &emsp;&emsp; D. our
7. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. avoid &emsp;&emsp; C. continue &emsp;&emsp; D. finish
8. A. subjects &emsp;&emsp; B. hobbies &emsp;&emsp; C. games &emsp;&emsp; D. lessons
9. A. wait &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. decide &emsp;&emsp; D. agree
10. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. boring &emsp;&emsp; C. enjoyable &emsp;&emsp; D. tiring

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 连词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：介绍一本书并解释为什么喜欢它。A 项意为"什么"；B 项意为"为什么"；C 项意为"如何"；D 项意为"何时"。explain why 表示解释原因。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在家练习了好几遍演讲。A 项意为"练习"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"写"；D 项意为"抄写"。准备演讲需要 practiced。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的心跳得很快。A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"轻柔地"。紧张时心跳 quickly。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词比较级
**【解析】** 本句意为：说得越多，越不紧张。A 项意为"更紧张"；B 项意为"较不紧张"；C 项意为"更累"；D 项意为"较不累"。the more...the less nervous 表示渐入佳境。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的描述非常生动。A 项意为"写作"；B 项意为"描述"；C 项意为"图画"；D 项意为"问题"。share 时用 description 描述故事。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从他们的展示中学到很多。A 项意为"他们的"；B 项意为"他的"；C 项意为"她的"；D 项意为"我们的"。指代 Zhang Wei 和 Li Mei 多人用 their。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她鼓励我们每月继续办这个活动。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"避免"；C 项意为"继续"；D 项意为"完成"。鼓励持续办活动用 continue。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：阅读已成为我最喜欢的爱好之一。A 项意为"科目"；B 项意为"爱好"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"课程"。reading 是一种 hobby。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：迫不及待想跟同学分享。A 项 can't wait to 意为"迫不及待"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"决定"；D 项意为"同意"。can't wait to tell 为常用搭配。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：分享知识和获得知识一样令人愉悦。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"令人愉快的"；D 项意为"令人疲惫的"。sharing 与 gaining 一样 enjoyable。故选 C。
