# 中考英语完形填空：和同学一起排练课本剧

## 完形填空原文

Our English teacher asked us to prepare a short play based on a text from our textbook. My classmates and I decided to work together. We chose the story "The Gift of the Magi" and planned to perform it in front of the whole class. It was our first time putting on a ___1___ like this.

We held a meeting after school to divide the roles. Tom would play the husband and I would be the wife. Three other students would take care of the props and background music. Everyone was excited and had many ___2___. We wrote a simple script and practiced our lines every day during the break. At first, I felt shy and forgot my words ___3___. But with more practice, I became more confident.

On Wednesday afternoon, we had our first full rehearsal in the classroom. We moved the desks aside to make ___4___ for the stage. When we performed, our teacher watched carefully and gave us suggestions. She said we should speak more ___5___ so that the students at the back could hear us. She also suggested adding some gestures to make the characters more lively.

We practiced again and again. Sometimes we made mistakes and had to ___6___ from the beginning. But nobody complained. We all wanted to do our best for the performance. The day before the show, we checked all the props—the old watch, the wooden comb, and the long hair. Everything was ___7___.

Finally, the big day came. We stood on the "stage" and began our play. I was nervous at first, but when I looked at my classmates' encouraging faces, I relaxed. The performance went well and we received warm applause. Our teacher said we had done a ___8___ job and our teamwork was excellent. This experience taught me that with cooperation and hard work, we can achieve great things together. I will never ___9___ this wonderful memory of rehearsing with my friends. We were all proud of ___10___ achievement as a team, and we hope to perform again soon.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我们第一次上演这样的戏剧。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项 put on a play 意为"上演戏剧"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"旅行"。课本剧表演用 play。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大家都很兴奋，有很多想法。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"食物"。讨论排练时有 ideas。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我很害羞，经常忘词。A 项 easily 意为"容易地"；B 项意为"几乎不"；C 项意为"慢慢地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。初学易忘用 forgot easily。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把桌子挪到一边腾出舞台空间。A 项 make room 意为"腾出空间"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"时间"；D 项意为"帮助"。make room for 为固定搭配。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她说我们应该说得更大声，这样后排同学能听见。A 项意为"安静地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"柔和地"；D 项意为"快速地"。让后排听见需 speak loudly。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们出错，只好从头开始。A 项 start 意为"开始"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"完成"。start from the beginning 意为"从头开始"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一切就绪。A 项意为"丢失的"；B 项 ready 意为"准备好的"；C 项意为"坏的"；D 项意为"贵的"。演出前检查完毕用 ready。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说我们做得很好。A 项意为"坏的"；B 项意为"差的"；C 项意为"出色的"；D 项意为"糟糕的"。表扬用 done a great job。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记与朋友排练的美好回忆。A 项意为"记得"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"喜欢"。never forget 表达珍视。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们都为团队的成就感到自豪。A 项意为"我的"；B 项意为"你的"；C 项 our 意为"我们的"；D 项意为"他们的"。团队成就用 our achievement。故选 C。
