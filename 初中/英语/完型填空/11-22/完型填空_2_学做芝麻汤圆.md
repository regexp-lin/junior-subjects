# 中考英语完形填空：学做芝麻汤圆

## 完形填空原文

The Lantern Festival was coming, and my grandmother said she would teach me how to make sesame tangyuan. Tangyuan are round rice dumplings that stand for family reunion. I had eaten them many times but never made them ___1___. I was curious and could not wait to learn.

First, Grandma mixed glutinous rice flour with warm water to make the dough. She said the water must not be too hot or the dough would become ___2___. I helped her knead the dough until it was smooth and soft. Then we divided it into small pieces, each about the size of a walnut. We made a small hole in each piece with our thumbs and put the sesame filling inside. The filling was made of ground black sesame, sugar, and a little butter. It smelled ___3___.

The next step was to close the hole and roll the dough into a round ball. I had to be careful not to leave any cracks, or the filling would leak out when cooking. My first few tangyuan looked a bit ___4___, but Grandma said practice makes perfect. I kept trying and my skills improved. Soon I could make round and smooth tangyuan just like ___5___.

We boiled a pot of water and gently dropped the tangyuan in. When they floated to the surface, we added a little cold water and waited for them to float again. Grandma said this made them ___6___ soft and chewy. Finally, we served them in sweet ginger soup. The tangyuan looked white and shiny, and tasted wonderful.

Making tangyuan with Grandma was a special experience. She told me that in the past, families would get together to make tangyuan before the festival. It was not just about the food, but about the ___7___ of being together. I will make tangyuan with my family every Lantern Festival from now on. It is a wonderful ___8___ to pass down through the generations. I also want to ___9___ this skill with my younger cousins. Every ___10___ we spend together in the kitchen is precious.

## 选项

1. A. myself &emsp;&emsp; B. herself &emsp;&emsp; C. himself &emsp;&emsp; D. itself
2. A. soft &emsp;&emsp; B. hard &emsp;&emsp; C. sweet &emsp;&emsp; D. salty
3. A. terrible &emsp;&emsp; B. delicious &emsp;&emsp; C. strange &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
4. A. perfect &emsp;&emsp; B. beautiful &emsp;&emsp; C. ugly &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
5. A. she &emsp;&emsp; B. her &emsp;&emsp; C. hers &emsp;&emsp; D. herself
6. A. less &emsp;&emsp; B. more &emsp;&emsp; C. much &emsp;&emsp; D. very
7. A. joy &emsp;&emsp; B. sadness &emsp;&emsp; C. worry &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
8. A. habit &emsp;&emsp; B. tradition &emsp;&emsp; C. game &emsp;&emsp; D. sport
9. A. cook &emsp;&emsp; B. eat &emsp;&emsp; C. share &emsp;&emsp; D. buy
10. A. day &emsp;&emsp; B. year &emsp;&emsp; C. moment &emsp;&emsp; D. week

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：吃过很多次但从没亲手做过。A 项 myself 意为"亲自/自己"；B 项意为"她自己"；C 项意为"他自己"；D 项意为"它自己"。强调亲手做用 myself。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：水不能太烫，否则面团会变硬。A 项意为"软的"；B 项意为"硬的"；C 项意为"甜的"；D 项意为"咸的"。热水和面会导致 dough 变 hard。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：闻起来很香。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"美味的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。芝麻馅料用 smelled delicious。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我前几个汤圆看起来有点丑。A 项意为"完美的"；B 项意为"美丽的"；C 项意为"丑的"；D 项意为"贵的"。初学做得不好用 ugly，与后文 practice 呼应。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：很快就能像奶奶一样做出又圆又滑的汤圆。A 项为主格；B 项 her 作介词 like 宾语；C 项为名词性物主代词；D 项为反身代词。like her 指"像她那样"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词/比较级辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这样能让汤圆更软更有嚼劲。A 项意为"更少"；B 项 more 意为"更"；C 项 much 修饰比较级；D 项 very 不能接形容词原级。make...more soft 表更软。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不只是食物，更是团聚的喜悦。A 项意为"快乐"；B 项意为"悲伤"；C 项意为"担心"；D 项意为"恐惧"。家人团聚用 joy。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是值得世代传承的美好传统。A 项意为"习惯"；B 项 tradition 意为"传统"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"运动"。pass down 传承用 tradition。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也想和表弟表妹分享这个技能。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"吃"；C 项 share 意为"分享"；D 项意为"买"。传承技能用 share。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一起在厨房度过的每一刻都很珍贵。A 项意为"天"；B 项意为"年"；C 项 moment 意为"时刻"；D 项意为"周"。every moment 强调每个瞬间。故选 C。
