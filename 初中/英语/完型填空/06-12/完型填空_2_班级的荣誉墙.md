# 中考英语完形填空：班级的荣誉墙

## 完形填空原文

At the back of our classroom, there is a wall covered with certificates, photos and small trophies. We call it our "Honour Wall". Every time our class wins a competition or someone does something great, we add something new to it. The wall has become a ___1___ of our shared efforts and joy.

When I first entered this class in Grade 8, the wall was almost empty. Our head teacher said, "This wall is waiting for us. Let's ___2___ it up together with our achievements." Her words encouraged us. We worked hard in the school basketball match and won third place. The day we put that certificate on the wall, everyone clapped and smiled. We felt ___3___ of ourselves.

The wall is not only for big wins. Sometimes we add a thank-you note from another class, or a photo of us helping in the community. These small things ___4___ that success is not only about scores. It is also about kindness, teamwork and never giving up. When I feel tired or ___5___ with my studies, I look at the wall. It reminds me of all the good times we have had and gives me strength to ___6___ on when things get tough.

Last month, we added a new item — a certificate for "Best Class Spirit". We didn't win any sports event that term, but we helped each other through difficult times. The wall taught me that honour does not always come from ___7___. Sometimes it comes from how we treat each other and face challenges together.

I will ___8___ this class next year when I go to senior high school. But I know the Honour Wall will stay in my heart forever. It is more than a wall — it is a ___9___ in who we are and what we believe in. Wherever we go, we will carry that ___10___ with us.

## 选项

1. A. picture &emsp;&emsp; B. symbol &emsp;&emsp; C. part &emsp;&emsp; D. kind
2. A. build &emsp;&emsp; B. fill &emsp;&emsp; C. set &emsp;&emsp; D. find
3. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. sorry
4. A. show &emsp;&emsp; B. hope &emsp;&emsp; C. wonder &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
5. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised
6. A. go &emsp;&emsp; B. hold &emsp;&emsp; C. move &emsp;&emsp; D. call
7. A. winning &emsp;&emsp; B. learning &emsp;&emsp; C. playing &emsp;&emsp; D. waiting
8. A. leave &emsp;&emsp; B. miss &emsp;&emsp; C. join &emsp;&emsp; D. visit
9. A. story &emsp;&emsp; B. dream &emsp;&emsp; C. record &emsp;&emsp; D. lesson
10. A. wall &emsp;&emsp; B. spirit &emsp;&emsp; C. photo &emsp;&emsp; D. certificate

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这面墙已成为我们共同努力与欢乐的___。A 项意为"图画"；B 项意为"象征"；C 项意为"部分"；D 项意为"种类"。"symbol"与"of our shared efforts and joy"相呼应，强调荣誉墙代表班级的集体精神。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：班主任说："这面墙在等待我们，让我们用成就一起___它。"A 项意为"建造"；B 项意为"填满"；C 项意为"摆放"；D 项意为"找到"。"fill it up"为固定搭配，指用成就填满这面墙，与后文"add something new"相呼应。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们把证书挂上墙的那天，大家一起鼓掌微笑，我们为自己感到___。A 项意为"疲惫"；B 项意为"害怕"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"抱歉"。"proud of ourselves"与获得名次、大家鼓掌的场景相呼应。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这些小事___成功不仅仅是分数。A 项意为"表明、展示"；B 项意为"希望"；C 项意为"想知道"；D 项意为"忘记"。"show that"表示这些小事说明、体现了某种道理，与后文"success is not only about scores"相呼应。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我学习感到疲惫或___时，我会看看这面墙。A 项意为"兴奋"；B 项意为"担忧"；C 项意为"无聊、厌倦"；D 项意为"惊讶"。"bored with my studies"表示对学习感到厌倦，与"tired"并列，与后文"gives me strength"形成对照。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：它提醒我们经历过的美好时光，并在困难时给我力量继续___。A 项意为"前进"；B 项意为"坚持"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"呼叫"。"hold on"为固定搭配，意为"坚持、挺住"，与"when things get tough"相呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：荣誉墙教会我，荣誉并非总是来自___。A 项意为"获胜"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"玩耍"；D 项意为"等待"。"winning"与后文"we didn't win any sports event"形成对比，强调荣誉也可来自其他方面。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：明年上高中时，我将___这个班级。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"想念"；C 项意为"加入"；D 项意为"参观"。"leave this class"与"go to senior high school"相呼应，表示初中毕业离开。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它不仅仅是一面墙——它是关于我们是谁、我们相信什么的___。A 项意为"故事"；B 项意为"梦想"；C 项意为"记录"；D 项意为"一课"。"a lesson in"意为"关于……的一课、启示"，强调荣誉墙教会我们认识自我与信念。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无论我们走到哪里，都会带着那份___。A 项意为"墙"；B 项意为"精神"；C 项意为"照片"；D 项意为"证书"。"spirit"与全文主题"class spirit"、"what we believe in"相呼应，强调带走的是一种精神而非实物。故选 B。
