# 中考英语完形填空：学滑冰

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, my parents took me to an ice rink. I had never tried ice skating before. I was both excited and a little ___1___. What if I fell and hurt myself?

We rented a pair of ice skates. When I put them on and stood up, my legs felt shaky. The blades were very thin, and I could not ___2___ my balance. I held onto the wall and took small steps. Other skaters glided past me smoothly. I wished I could skate like them.

My father used to skate when he was young. He came to help me. "Bend your knees a little," he said. "Keep your weight forward. And don't look down — look ahead." I tried to ___3___ his advice. I let go of the wall and pushed one foot forward. For a moment, I was moving! But then I lost my balance and fell. My bottom hit the ice. It was cold and a bit ___4___, but I was not seriously hurt. I got up and tried again.

Falling became part of my learning. I fell many times that afternoon. Each time I fell, I learned something. I learned to keep my arms out for balance. I learned to bend my knees more. I learned not to be ___5___ of falling — it was the only way to improve.

By the end of the first hour, I could skate a short distance without holding the wall. I was still slow and unsteady, but I was making ___6___. My mother cheered from the side. "You're doing great!" she said. Her encouragement meant a lot to me.

We went back to the ice rink two more times that week. Each visit, I improved a little. On the third visit, I could skate around the rink once without falling. I felt so ___7___! My father said, "Remember how nervous you were on the first day? Now look at you. Practice really does make perfect."

Ice skating taught me that new skills take time to learn. We should not ___8___ ourselves when we make mistakes. Every expert was once a beginner. The important thing is to keep trying and never give up. I hope to improve my skating this winter and perhaps try ___9___ sports like skiing in the future. Learning something new is always ___10___, even when it is challenging at first.

## 选项

1. A. confident &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
2. A. lose &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. find &emsp;&emsp; D. forget
3. A. ignore &emsp;&emsp; B. follow &emsp;&emsp; C. forget &emsp;&emsp; D. refuse
4. A. comfortable &emsp;&emsp; B. painful &emsp;&emsp; C. warm &emsp;&emsp; D. smooth
5. A. proud &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. tired &emsp;&emsp; D. sure
6. A. mistakes &emsp;&emsp; B. progress &emsp;&emsp; C. noise &emsp;&emsp; D. trouble
7. A. disappointed &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. accomplished &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
8. A. praise &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. criticise &emsp;&emsp; D. trust
9. A. other &emsp;&emsp; B. same &emsp;&emsp; C. easy &emsp;&emsp; D. boring
10. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. worthwhile &emsp;&emsp; C. tiring &emsp;&emsp; D. dangerous

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：既兴奋又有点___。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"放松的"。第一次滑冰会紧张，"nervous"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：无法___平衡。A 项意为"失去"；B 项意为"保持"；C 项意为"找到"；D 项意为"忘记"。冰刀细难以保持平衡，"keep"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我努力___他的建议。A 项意为"忽视"；B 项意为"遵循"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"拒绝"。"follow advice"意为听从建议。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冰很冷，有点___。A 项意为"舒适的"；B 项意为"疼的"；C 项意为"温暖的"；D 项意为"光滑的"。摔在冰上会疼，"painful"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学会不___摔倒。A 项意为"骄傲的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"疲倦的"；D 项意为"确定的"。"not be afraid of falling"意为不怕摔。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我在取得___。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"进步"；C 项意为"噪音"；D 项意为"麻烦"。"make progress"意为取得进步。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我感到非常___！A 项意为"失望的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"有成就感的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。完成一圈不摔感到有成就感，"accomplished"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：犯错时不应___自己。A 项意为"表扬"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"苛责"；D 项意为"信任"。"criticise"意为批评、苛责。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许以后尝试___运动比如滑雪。A 项意为"其他的"；B 项意为"同样的"；C 项意为"简单的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"other sports"意为其他运动。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学新东西总是___的。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"值得的"；C 项意为"累人的"；D 项意为"危险的"。"worthwhile"意为值得的、有意义的。故选 B。
