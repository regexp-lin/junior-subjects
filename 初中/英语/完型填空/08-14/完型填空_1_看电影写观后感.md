# 中考英语完形填空：看电影写观后感

## 完形填空原文

Last weekend, our Chinese teacher asked us to watch a film and write a review. She said it would help us practise our writing and learn to express our opinions. I chose an animation film about a young girl who chased her dreams.

The film was shown at a small cinema near my home. I went there with my classmate Xiaohua. The cinema was not very ___1___, but the screen was big and the sound was clear. We bought some popcorn and found our seats. When the lights went down, I felt excited.

The story was touching. The girl faced many difficulties but never ___2___ her dream. She met a kind mentor who taught her to believe in herself. There were funny moments that made us laugh, and sad moments that made some audience members ___3___. I was deeply moved by the girl's courage and persistence.

When the film ended, I stayed in my seat for a while, thinking about what I had seen. The message of the film was clear: follow your heart and never give up. On our way home, Xiaohua and I ___4___ the characters and the plot. We had different favourite scenes, but we both agreed that it was a meaningful film.

That evening, I sat down to write my review. At first, I did not know where to ___5___. I had so many thoughts but could not organise them. So I wrote an outline first: the story, my favourite part, and what I had learned. This helped me structure my ideas.

I wrote about how the film had inspired me. In my daily life, I sometimes felt ___6___ when things became difficult. But the girl in the film showed me that challenges are part of growth. I also wrote about the importance of having people who support you. The mentor in the film played a key ___7___ in the girl's success.

The next day, I handed in my review. Our teacher read some of them aloud in class. When she read mine, I felt a bit shy but also ___8___. She said my review showed good thinking and clear expression. She encouraged us to watch more good films and write about them. "Films can open our minds and ___9___ us to see the world from different angles," she said.

I have started to watch films more carefully now. I pay attention to the story, the characters, and the music. After each film, I take a few notes. Writing reviews has become a ___10___ way for me to reflect on what I watch and to improve my writing skills at the same time.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：影院不太___。A 项意为"小的"；B 项意为"拥挤的"；C 项意为"现代的"；D 项意为"暗的"。后文说屏幕大音响好，说明设备不错，可能影院不够新，"modern"与后文形成对比。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：面对许多困难但从未___梦想。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"实现"；C 项意为"改变"；D 项意为"放弃"。"never gave up her dream"意为永不放弃。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：悲伤场景让一些观众___。A 项意为"笑"；B 项意为"唱"；C 项意为"哭"；D 项意为"睡"。悲伤情节让人落泪，"cry"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：回家路上我们___角色和剧情。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"讨论"；C 项意为"忽视"；D 项意为"抄袭"。"discussed"意为交流看法。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初不知从何___。A 项意为"去"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"跑"；D 项意为"停留"。"where to start"意为如何下笔。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：遇到困难时有时感到___。A 项意为"自信的"；B 项意为"气馁的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"骄傲的"。困难让人沮丧，"discouraged"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：导师在女孩成功中起关键___。A 项意为"游戏"；B 项意为"作用"；C 项意为"规则"；D 项意为"地方"。"play a key role"意为扮演重要角色。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有点害羞但也___。A 项意为"生气"；B 项意为"无聊"；C 项意为"骄傲"；D 项意为"害怕"。被表扬时感到骄傲，"proud"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：电影能开阔思维，___我们从不同角度看世界。A 项意为"强迫"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"命令"。"help sb to do"意为帮助某人做。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：写观后感成了___的方式。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无用的"；C 项意为"有益的"；D 项意为"危险的"。"helpful"表示有帮助。故选 C。
