# 中考英语完形填空：跳绳比赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held a rope-skipping competition. Each class had to choose five students to ___1___. I was surprised when my teacher chose me because I was not the best at skipping. But she said, "You work hard and never give up. That is what we need."

We had two weeks to practise. Every afternoon after school, we met on the playground. At first, I kept ___2___ my feet and could only skip about thirty times in a minute. I felt frustrated. But my teammates encouraged me. "You can do it!" they said. Their words gave me ___3___.

I practised every day. I learned to breathe properly and find the right rhythm. Little by little, I improved. By the end of the first week, I could skip sixty times. By the second week, I ___4___ eighty. I was so proud of myself!

The day of the competition finally came. We wore our sports uniforms and stood in line. When the whistle blew, we all started jumping. The rope hit the ground with a steady ___5___. I focused on my breathing and tried not to look at others. "One, two, three..." I counted in my mind.

When the competition ended, we had to wait for the results. My hands were ___6___ because I was so nervous. Then the headmaster announced the winners. Our class came second! We did not get first place, but we had tried our best. We ___7___ happily and hugged each other.

Looking back, I learned that success is not only about winning. It is about doing your best and growing through the ___8___. I am thankful for my teacher who believed in me and for my teammates who never stopped supporting me. Sometimes we need someone to give us a ___9___ and tell us we can do it.

Next year, I want to join the competition again. I will practise even harder and hope to ___10___ our class to first place!

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每班要选五名学生___。A 项意为"观看"；B 项意为"参加"；C 项意为"裁判"；D 项意为"离开"。参加比赛用"join"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我老是___脚。A 项意为"绊到/打到"；B 项意为"洗"；C 项意为"举起"；D 项意为"清洁"。"hit one's feet"指跳绳时绳打到脚，"hitting"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的话给了我___。A 项意为"力量"；B 项意为"钱"；C 项意为"食物"；D 项意为"时间"。鼓励的话语给人力量，"power"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第二周我___了八十个。A 项意为"达到"；B 项意为"失去"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"错过"。跳绳数量达到八十，"reached"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：绳子落地发出稳定的___。A 项意为"声音"；B 项意为"气味"；C 项意为"味道"；D 项意为"颜色"。绳子落地有声音，"sound"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的手在___，因为很紧张。A 项意为"温暖"；B 项意为"干的"；C 项意为"发抖"；D 项意为"脏的"。紧张时手会发抖，"shaking"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们高兴地___并拥抱。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"欢呼"；C 项意为"睡"；D 项意为"争论"。取得成绩后欢呼庆祝，"shouted"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在___中成长。A 项意为"过程"；B 项意为"结果"；C 项意为"奖品"；D 项意为"规则"。成长发生在过程之中，"process"正确。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我们需要有人给我们一个___。A 项意为"课程"；B 项意为"机会"；C 项意为"推动/鼓励"；D 项意为"礼物"。"give sb a push"意为"推一把/鼓励"，符合文意。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：希望___班级获得第一名。A 项意为"带来"；B 项意为"送"；C 项意为"跟随"；D 项意为"邀请"。"bring sb to first place"意为"带领某人获第一"，"bring"正确。故选 A。
