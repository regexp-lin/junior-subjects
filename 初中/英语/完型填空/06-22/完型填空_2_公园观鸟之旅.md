# 中考英语完形填空：公园观鸟之旅

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday, my father took me to the city park for bird watching. I had never done this before, so I was full of ___1___. We woke up early because birds are most active in the morning. My father brought a pair of binoculars and a small notebook. "We will ___2___ what we see," he said.

When we arrived at the park, it was still quiet. The sun was just rising, and the grass was wet with dew. We walked slowly along the path and listened ___3___. Suddenly, my father pointed to a tree. "Look! A sparrow!" he whispered. I raised the binoculars and saw a small brown bird. It was hopping from branch to branch. I felt so ___4___ that I could see it so clearly.

We saw many different birds that morning—robins, magpies, and even a woodpecker. My father knew the names of all of them. He had learned about birds when he was young. "Nature has so much to ___5___ us," he said. "We just need to slow down and look." I wrote down each bird in my notebook. The experience made me ___6___ that there was a whole world of life around us that I had never noticed before.

By noon, we had seen over ten kinds of birds. My legs were a little tired from walking, but I did not mind. The fresh air and the beauty of nature made me forget my ___7___. On our way home, my father and I talked about what we had seen. I realized that spending time with him in nature was ___8___ better than staying at home playing games. We promised to come back next month. I can't ___9___ for our next bird-watching trip. This adventure has given me a new hobby and a deeper ___10___ with my father.

## 选项

1. A. fear &emsp;&emsp; B. excitement &emsp;&emsp; C. sadness &emsp;&emsp; D. anger
2. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. record &emsp;&emsp; C. hide &emsp;&emsp; D. lose
3. A. loudly &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. slowly &emsp;&emsp; D. carefully
4. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. afraid
5. A. teach &emsp;&emsp; B. give &emsp;&emsp; C. take &emsp;&emsp; D. buy
6. A. wonder &emsp;&emsp; B. imagine &emsp;&emsp; C. understand &emsp;&emsp; D. guess
7. A. homework &emsp;&emsp; B. hunger &emsp;&emsp; C. tiredness &emsp;&emsp; D. pain
8. A. much &emsp;&emsp; B. very &emsp;&emsp; C. quite &emsp;&emsp; D. rather
9. A. ask &emsp;&emsp; B. wait &emsp;&emsp; C. hope &emsp;&emsp; D. prepare
10. A. agreement &emsp;&emsp; B. discussion &emsp;&emsp; C. competition &emsp;&emsp; D. connection

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没做过观鸟，所以充满___。A 项意为"恐惧"；B 项意为"兴奋"；C 项意为"悲伤"；D 项意为"愤怒"。"excitement"与"never done this before"相呼应，表达对新体验的期待。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说："我们会把看到的内容___下来。"A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记录"；C 项意为"隐藏"；D 项意为"丢失"。"record"与"notebook"、"wrote down"相呼应。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们沿小路慢慢走，___聆听。A 项意为"大声地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"缓慢地"；D 项意为"仔细地"。"carefully"与观察鸟类需要安静聆听相呼应。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：能用望远镜看得这么清楚，我感到很___。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"兴奋的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。"excited"与"see it so clearly"相呼应，表达发现鸟类的喜悦。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说："大自然有很多东西可以___我们。"A 项意为"教"；B 项意为"给予"；C 项意为"带走"；D 项意为"买"。"teach"与"learn"、"slow down and look"相呼应。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次经历让我___身边有一个从未注意过的生命世界。A 项意为"想知道"；B 项意为"想象"；C 项意为"理解、明白"；D 项意为"猜测"。"understand"与"never noticed before"相呼应，表示领悟。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：新鲜空气和大自然的美让我忘却___。A 项意为"作业"；B 项意为"饥饿"；C 项意为"疲惫"；D 项意为"疼痛"。"tiredness"与"legs were a little tired"相呼应。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：和爸爸在大自然中共度时光比在家玩游戏___好。A 项"much better"意为"好得多"；B 项"very"不能直接修饰比较级；C 项"quite"通常修饰原级；D 项"rather"可修饰比较级但不如much常用。修饰比较级用much。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我迫不及待___下一次观鸟之旅。A 项意为"问"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"希望"；D 项意为"准备"。"can't wait for"为固定搭配，意为"迫不及待"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次冒险给了我新爱好，也让我和爸爸有了更深的___。A 项意为"协议"；B 项意为"讨论"；C 项意为"竞争"；D 项意为"联系、纽带"。"connection"与"spending time with him"相呼应，指情感联结。故选 D。
