# 中考英语完形填空：周末的家庭晚餐

## 完形填空原文

Every Saturday evening, my family gathers in the kitchen to prepare dinner together. This has become our favorite ___1___ of the week. My father cuts the vegetables, my mother cooks the main dish, and I help set the table. We talk and laugh while we work. This time together makes me feel ___2___ and happy.

Last weekend, my mother taught me how to make tomato and egg soup. I had never tried cooking before, so I was a little ___3___. She showed me step by step. First, I had to wash the tomatoes carefully. Then I broke the eggs into a bowl. My first egg had some shell in it, and we both laughed. "Don't ___4___," my mother said. "Practice makes perfect."

When the soup was ready, we sat down at the table. The food smelled wonderful. My father said that our family dinner was more important than any restaurant. I ___5___ with him. Eating together helps us understand each other better. We share our day, our problems, and our dreams. My younger brother sometimes tells funny stories, and we all laugh until our stomachs hurt.

I have learned that a simple family dinner can ___6___ us closer. It is not about the food itself. It is about the time we spend together, the care we show, and the love we share. I will always ___7___ these warm evenings when I grow up. They are the best ___8___ that money cannot buy. I feel lucky to have such a loving family. Every Saturday, I look ___9___ to our dinner time. It reminds me that home is not just a place—it is where our hearts meet. I am ___10___ to be part of this tradition.

## 选项

1. A. moment &emsp;&emsp; B. part &emsp;&emsp; C. hour &emsp;&emsp; D. subject
2. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. warm &emsp;&emsp; C. alone &emsp;&emsp; D. busy
3. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. angry &emsp;&emsp; D. bored
4. A. stop &emsp;&emsp; B. run &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. worry
5. A. agreed &emsp;&emsp; B. argued &emsp;&emsp; C. played &emsp;&emsp; D. competed
6. A. bring &emsp;&emsp; B. send &emsp;&emsp; C. keep &emsp;&emsp; D. push
7. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. remember &emsp;&emsp; C. miss &emsp;&emsp; D. change
8. A. books &emsp;&emsp; B. clothes &emsp;&emsp; C. games &emsp;&emsp; D. gifts
9. A. for &emsp;&emsp; B. at &emsp;&emsp; C. forward &emsp;&emsp; D. up
10. A. sorry &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. glad &emsp;&emsp; D. surprised

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这已成为我们一周中最喜欢的___。A 项意为"时刻"；B 项意为"部分"；C 项意为"小时"；D 项意为"科目"。"favorite part of the week"指一周中最喜欢的部分/时光。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这段团聚时光让我感到___和快乐。A 项意为"疲惫的"；B 项意为"温暖的"；C 项意为"孤独的"；D 项意为"忙碌的"。"warm"与家人团聚的氛围相呼应，表达内心的温暖。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我从没试过做饭，所以有点___。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"骄傲的"；C 项意为"生气的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。"nervous"与"never tried cooking before"相呼应，第一次做某事会紧张。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说："别___，熟能生巧。"A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"担心"。"Don't worry"是常用安慰语，与"Practice makes perfect"相呼应。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸说家庭晚餐比任何餐厅都重要，我___他。A 项意为"同意"；B 项意为"争论"；C 项意为"玩"；D 项意为"竞争"。"agree with"为固定搭配，表示同意某人。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：简单的家庭晚餐能让我们___得更亲密。A 项意为"带来"；B 项意为"送去"；C 项意为"保持"；D 项意为"推"。"bring us closer"为常用搭配，意为"让我们更亲近"。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：长大后我会一直___这些温暖的夜晚。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"记得"；C 项意为"想念"；D 项意为"改变"。"remember"与"will always"相呼应，表达永久铭记。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们是金钱买不到的宝贵___。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"衣服"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"礼物"。"gifts"在此喻指珍贵收获，与"money cannot buy"相呼应。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每周六我都___晚餐时光。A 项"look for"意为"寻找"；B 项"look at"意为"看"；C 项"look forward to"意为"期待"；D 项"look up"意为"查阅"。"look forward to"表示期盼，与"every Saturday"相呼应。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很___能成为这个传统的一部分。A 项意为"抱歉的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"高兴的"；D 项意为"惊讶的"。"glad"与"part of this tradition"相呼应，表达感恩与喜悦。故选 C。
