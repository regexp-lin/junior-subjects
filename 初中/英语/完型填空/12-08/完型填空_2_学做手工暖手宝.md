# 中考英语完形填空：学做手工暖手宝

## 完形填空原文

Winter mornings can be very cold. My hands always feel frozen on the way to school. Last weekend, my craft teacher showed us how to make a simple hand warmer using fabric and rice. I thought it was a ___1___ and practical idea.

The materials were easy to find: a piece of soft cotton fabric, some dry rice, and needle and thread. The teacher said we could also add a few drops of lavender oil to make it smell ___2___.

First, we cut the fabric into two heart-shaped pieces of the same size. The teacher reminded us to leave a little extra space around the ___3___ for sewing. I chose a red piece of fabric because red is the color of warmth.

Then we placed the two pieces together with the nice sides facing ___4___. We sewed along the edge, leaving a small opening at the bottom. This step needed concentration because the stitches had to be small and even. I made a few ___5___ at first, but I slowly got better.

After sewing, we turned the fabric inside out through the opening. Now the nice pattern was on the ___6___. Next, I used a small funnel to pour rice into the heart-shaped pouch. I filled it about three-quarters full so it would still be soft and ___7___.

Finally, I sewed the opening shut by hand. The hand warmer was complete. To use it, I just needed to put it in the microwave for thirty ___8___, and it would stay warm for about an hour.

I made two hand warmers that day. I gave one to my mother as a gift. She was very ___9___ and said it was the most thoughtful present she had ever received. She put it in her coat pocket and used it on her morning walk.

Making this hand warmer taught me that the best gifts are not expensive ones, but the ones made with ___10___ and care. I plan to make more for my friends before the winter holiday.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得这是一个聪明又实用的主意。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"危险的"；C 项意为"聪明的"；D 项意为"无用的"。clever and practical 形容好主意。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：加几滴薰衣草精油使它闻起来好闻。A 项意为"糟糕的"；B 项意为"好的"；C 项意为"奇怪的"；D 项意为"酸的"。smell nice 意为"闻起来好"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：边缘要留出缝纫的余地。A 项意为"中心"；B 项意为"顶部"；C 项意为"边缘"；D 项意为"后面"。缝纫需在 edge 留余量。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：把两片布面朝里放在一起。A 项意为"朝内"；B 项意为"朝外"；C 项意为"朝上"；D 项意为"朝下"。缝纫时花面 facing inward，翻转后花面朝外。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：一开始犯了几个错误，但慢慢变好了。A 项意为"朋友"；B 项意为"错误"；C 项意为"借口"；D 项意为"计划"。初学缝纫会 make mistakes。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：翻过来后花纹在外面。A 项意为"里面"；B 项意为"底部"；C 项意为"外面"；D 项意为"侧面"。翻转后图案在 outside。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：填四分之三满，这样它仍然柔软且有弹性。A 项意为"硬的"；B 项意为"重的"；C 项意为"灵活的/有弹性的"；D 项意为"冷的"。soft and flexible 适合暖手宝。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：放微波炉里加热三十秒。A 项意为"小时"；B 项意为"分钟"；C 项意为"秒"；D 项意为"天"。微波加热暖手宝用 thirty seconds。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈非常感动。A 项意为"生气的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"感动的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。收到手工礼物会 touched。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：最好的礼物不是贵的，而是用爱和关怀做的。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"爱"；C 项意为"速度"；D 项意为"运气"。made with love and care。故选 B。
