# 中考英语完形填空：给山区小朋友寄冬衣

## 完形填空原文

Last week, our school started a charity drive called "Warm Winter, Warm Hearts." The goal was to collect winter clothes and send them to children in mountain areas who do not have enough warm clothes to ___1___ the cold season.

Our headteacher showed us some photos of the mountain school. The children there wore thin clothes even in December. Some of them did not even have proper ___2___. The photos made everyone feel sad, and we all wanted to help.

Each class set up a collection box. Students could bring old but clean jackets, sweaters, scarves, and gloves. I went home and told my parents about the ___3___. My mother helped me pick out three jackets that I had outgrown. She washed and ironed them carefully. My father also found a warm down vest that was still in good ___4___.

The next day, I brought the clothes to school. I was surprised to see how many items our classmates had already ___5___. The collection box was almost full. Some students even brought new clothes they had bought with their own pocket money. Their ___6___ moved everyone deeply.

Our class also decided to write personal letters to the children. I wrote a short letter saying, "Dear friend, I hope this jacket keeps you warm. ___7___ is temporary, but kindness lasts forever. Keep studying hard and never give up on your dreams." I folded the letter and put it in the jacket pocket.

After a week, all the boxes were packed and sent to the mountain school by a delivery service. Two weeks later, we received a thank-you video from the children. They were wearing our clothes and ___8___ happily at the camera. One little girl held up my letter and said, "Thank you, big brother. I feel very warm."

Watching that video, I felt a warm glow in my ___9___. Helping others is not about how much you give, but about the love you put into it. I learned that even small acts of ___10___ can make a big difference in someone's life.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：没有足够的暖衣来度过寒冷季节。A 项意为"享受"；B 项意为"度过/存活"；C 项意为"错过"；D 项意为"开始"。survive the cold season 意为"挺过寒冬"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些孩子甚至没有像样的鞋子。A 项意为"鞋子"；B 项意为"书"；C 项意为"玩具"；D 项意为"工具"。冬天穿着单薄且没有 proper shoes。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我告诉父母关于这个活动。A 项意为"考试"；B 项意为"活动"；C 项意为"事故"；D 项意为"会议"。charity drive 是一项 activity。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸找到一件状况还不错的羽绒背心。A 项意为"状况"；B 项意为"味道"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"价格"。in good condition 意为"状况良好"。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我惊讶于同学们已经捐了这么多东西。A 项意为"卖"；B 项意为"藏"；C 项意为"捐赠"；D 项意为"扔"。慈善活动中 donated。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们的慷慨深深打动了每个人。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"慷慨"；C 项意为"恐惧"；D 项意为"懒惰"。用自己零花钱买新衣捐出体现 generosity。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：冬天是暂时的，但善意是永恒的。A 项意为"冬天"；B 项意为"幸福"；C 项意为"友谊"；D 项意为"夏天"。给山区孩子寄冬衣，Winter is temporary 与 kindness 对比。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们穿着我们的衣服，开心地对着摄像头挥手。A 项意为"哭"；B 项意为"喊"；C 项意为"挥手"；D 项意为"打架"。waving happily 是感谢的表达。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：看到视频，我心里感到温暖。A 项意为"胃"；B 项意为"头"；C 项意为"手"；D 项意为"心"。a warm glow in my heart 意为"内心温暖"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：即使是小小的善举也能给别人的生活带来大的改变。A 项意为"勇敢"；B 项意为"善良"；C 项意为"沉默"；D 项意为"运气"。acts of kindness 意为"善举"。故选 B。
