# 中考英语完形填空：学校文艺汇演

## 完形填空原文

Last Friday, our school held an art performance to celebrate the upcoming National Day. The performance took place in the school hall. Students from different grades had ___1___ for weeks to show their talents.

I was part of our class's dance group. We had chosen a traditional Chinese dance. At first, some of us found the moves difficult. We often stepped on each other's feet and forgot the ___2___. But we never gave up. We practised after class every day. Our teacher, Miss Liu, encouraged us and said that ___3___ would bring success.

On the day of the performance, I felt very nervous. My heart was beating fast when we waited backstage. "Take a deep breath. You've prepared well. Just do your ___4___," my friend Xiaoming said. His words helped me calm down.

When our turn came, we walked onto the stage. The lights were bright and the audience was watching quietly. The music started and we began to dance. To my surprise, everything went ___5___. We remembered every move and danced in perfect harmony. When we finished, the audience clapped loudly. I felt so proud and happy.

There were many other wonderful performances too. Some students sang popular songs, others performed magic tricks. The most impressive one was a group of students who played traditional Chinese instruments. Their ___6___ was so beautiful that everyone listened in silence.

The principal gave a speech at the end. He said that the arts were an important part of education. Learning to perform could help us build ___7___ and teamwork. He encouraged us to develop our hobbies and discover our talents.

The art performance was a great success. It not only brought us joy but also helped us understand the ___8___ of art. I learned that hard work pays off and that performing in front of others can be a wonderful experience. I'm already looking forward to the next school ___9___. Maybe I'll try something new — perhaps singing or playing an instrument. The stage is a place where we can ___10___ ourselves and share our joy with others.

2. A. words &emsp;&emsp; B. music &emsp;&emsp; C. steps &emsp;&emsp; D. clothes
3. A. luck &emsp;&emsp; B. practice &emsp;&emsp; C. talent &emsp;&emsp; D. time
4. A. worst &emsp;&emsp; B. best &emsp;&emsp; C. least &emsp;&emsp; D. most
5. A. wrong &emsp;&emsp; B. badly &emsp;&emsp; C. smoothly &emsp;&emsp; D. slowly
6. A. voice &emsp;&emsp; B. music &emsp;&emsp; C. dance &emsp;&emsp; D. speech
7. A. wealth &emsp;&emsp; B. confidence &emsp;&emsp; C. speed &emsp;&emsp; D. height
8. A. price &emsp;&emsp; B. value &emsp;&emsp; C. cost &emsp;&emsp; D. size
9. A. exam &emsp;&emsp; B. meeting &emsp;&emsp; C. event &emsp;&emsp; D. trip

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不同年级的学生为展示才艺已准备了好几周。A 项意为"休息"；B 项意为"准备"；C 项意为"玩耍"；D 项意为"睡觉"。演出前需要 prepare。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们经常踩到彼此的脚，忘记了舞步。A 项意为"词语"；B 项意为"音乐"；C 项意为"步伐、舞步"；D 项意为"衣服"。舞蹈用 steps。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师鼓励我们说练习会带来成功。A 项意为"运气"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"天赋"；D 项意为"时间"。practice brings success 意为"熟能生巧"。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们准备得很好，尽力就好。A 项意为"最差"；B 项意为"最好"；C 项意为"最少"；D 项意为"最多"。do your best 意为"尽力"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：令我们惊讶的是，一切进行得很顺利。A 项意为"错误的"；B 项意为"糟糕地"；C 项意为"顺利地"；D 项意为"缓慢地"。went smoothly 意为"顺利进行"。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们演奏的音乐如此优美，大家静静聆听。A 项意为"嗓音"；B 项意为"音乐"；C 项意为"舞蹈"；D 项意为"演讲"。演奏乐器产生 music。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：学习表演能帮助我们建立自信和团队合作精神。A 项意为"财富"；B 项意为"自信"；C 项意为"速度"；D 项意为"身高"。表演能 build confidence。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：演出不仅带来快乐，还帮助我们理解艺术的价值。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"价值"；C 项意为"成本"；D 项意为"尺寸"。the value of art 意为"艺术的价值"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我期待着下一次学校活动。A 项意为"考试"；B 项意为"会议"；C 项意为"活动"；D 项意为"旅行"。school event 意为"学校活动"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：舞台是我们表达自我、与他人分享快乐的地方。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"表达"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"改变"。express ourselves 意为"表达自我"。故选 B。
