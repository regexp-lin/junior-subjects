# 中考英语完形填空：帮妈妈整理旧衣物

## 完形填空原文

Last Sunday afternoon, my mother asked me to help her tidy up the old clothes in the wardrobe. She said we had too many clothes that we no longer wore, and it was time to ___1___ out what to keep and what to give away.

I agreed and we started working together. Mom pulled out all the clothes from the wardrobe and put them on the bed. There were coats, sweaters, dresses, and many other items. Some of them were still in good ___2___, but we had outgrown them or they were out of style.

"These clothes can still be useful to someone else," my mother said. "We can donate them to people who need them." I thought it was a good idea. Helping others while tidying up our home made the work more ___3___.

We sorted the clothes into three piles: one to keep, one to donate, and one to throw away. The clothes that were torn or too worn could not be donated. We put them in a bag for recycling. My mother said that even old clothes could be ___4___ in some way, so we shouldn't just throw them in the rubbish.

While we were sorting, I found a small dress that I had worn when I was five. It had a cute pattern of little flowers. I showed it to my mother. She smiled and said, "You loved this dress. You wore it ___5___ when you were little." We decided to keep it as a memory. Some things are worth keeping not for use, but for the ___6___ they bring.

After two hours of work, we had finished. The wardrobe looked much neater. We had two big bags of clothes ready to donate. My mother would take them to a charity shop the next day. I felt proud that we had done something good. Donating clothes is a simple way to ___7___ others and protect the environment at the same time.

From this experience, I learned that we should not hold on to things we don't need. Sharing with others can bring ___8___ to both the giver and the receiver. I will help my mother with housework more often from now on. A tidy home and a helpful heart make life more ___9___. Next time we tidy up, I will ___10___ to find more things to share with those in need.

4. A. useless &emsp;&emsp; B. recycled &emsp;&emsp; C. lost &emsp;&emsp; D. broken

6. A. trouble &emsp;&emsp; B. memories &emsp;&emsp; C. problems &emsp;&emsp; D. costs
7. A. ignore &emsp;&emsp; B. help &emsp;&emsp; C. blame &emsp;&emsp; D. hurt
8. A. sadness &emsp;&emsp; B. joy &emsp;&emsp; C. anger &emsp;&emsp; D. fear
9. A. harder &emsp;&emsp; B. longer &emsp;&emsp; C. worse &emsp;&emsp; D. better

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：该整理一下哪些要留、哪些要捐出去了。A 项意为"看"；B 项意为"分类、整理"；C 项意为"卖"；D 项意为"洗"。sort out 意为"分类整理"。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些衣服状况还好，但我们穿不下了或过时了。A 项意为"价格"；B 项意为"状况"；C 项意为"颜色"；D 项意为"尺寸"。in good condition 意为"状况良好"。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在整理家的同时帮助他人，让工作更有意义。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"有意义的"；D 项意为"昂贵的"。捐赠让整理变得 meaningful。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（被动语态）
**【解析】** 本句意为：旧衣服也能以某种方式被回收利用。A 项意为"无用的"；B 项意为"回收"；C 项意为"丢失"；D 项意为"打破"。be recycled 意为"被回收"。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：你小时候经常穿这件。A 项意为"从不"；B 项意为"经常"；C 项意为"很少"；D 项意为"有时"。喜欢的东西会 often 穿。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有些东西值得保留不是因为有用，而是因为它们带来的回忆。A 项意为"麻烦"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"成本"。memories 与上文"作为记忆"呼应。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：捐赠衣物是帮助他人、同时保护环境的简单方式。A 项意为"忽视"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"责备"；D 项意为"伤害"。donate 能 help others。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：与他人分享能给给予者和接收者都带来快乐。A 项意为"悲伤"；B 项意为"快乐"；C 项意为"愤怒"；D 项意为"恐惧"。sharing brings joy。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：整洁的家和乐于助人的心让生活更美好。A 项意为"更艰难"；B 项意为"更长"；C 项意为"更糟"；D 项意为"更好"。make life better 意为"让生活更美好"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：下次整理时我会努力找更多东西与需要的人分享。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"努力、尝试"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"失败"。try to do 意为"努力做"。故选 B。
