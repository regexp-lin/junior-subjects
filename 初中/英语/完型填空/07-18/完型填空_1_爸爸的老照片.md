# 中考英语完形填空：爸爸的老照片

## 完形填空原文

One rainy afternoon, I was bored at home and decided to explore the attic. Among the old boxes and dusty furniture, I found a small album full of ___1___. They were my dad's old photos from when he was young.

I sat on the floor and looked through them one by one. In the first photo, Dad was about my ___2___. He was standing in front of a school gate with a big smile on his face. He looked so different from now — thin, with short hair and wearing a school uniform that seemed too big for him.

There were photos of Dad playing football with his friends, riding a bicycle on a country road, and sitting in a classroom ___3___ at the camera. In one photo, he was holding a trophy. I guessed he must have won some kind of ___4___. I had never known that Dad was good at sports.

The photo that caught my attention most was one of Dad standing beside a river with a fishing rod. He looked very ___5___ and relaxed. It reminded me of the times when Dad took me fishing. He always said fishing taught him to be ___6___, because you can never rush a fish.

When Dad came home, I showed him the album. His face ___7___ up with surprise and joy. "I haven't seen these in years!" he said. He sat down beside me and started telling me the ___8___ behind each photo. The football photo was from a match where his team won the school championship. The trophy was for a science competition.

We spent the whole evening talking and laughing. I felt like I was getting to know a completely ___9___ side of my dad — the boy he used to be. That rainy afternoon turned into one of the most memorable days of my summer, all because of a dusty old ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. letters &emsp;&emsp; B. photos &emsp;&emsp; C. books &emsp;&emsp; D. coins
2. A. age &emsp;&emsp; B. height &emsp;&emsp; C. weight &emsp;&emsp; D. size
3. A. pointing &emsp;&emsp; B. shouting &emsp;&emsp; C. smiling &emsp;&emsp; D. throwing
4. A. prize &emsp;&emsp; B. game &emsp;&emsp; C. competition &emsp;&emsp; D. gift
5. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. happy &emsp;&emsp; D. scared
6. A. brave &emsp;&emsp; B. honest &emsp;&emsp; C. patient &emsp;&emsp; D. polite
7. A. blew &emsp;&emsp; B. lit &emsp;&emsp; C. shut &emsp;&emsp; D. turned
8. A. secrets &emsp;&emsp; B. problems &emsp;&emsp; C. stories &emsp;&emsp; D. rules
9. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. new &emsp;&emsp; C. dark &emsp;&emsp; D. old
10. A. album &emsp;&emsp; B. toy &emsp;&emsp; C. map &emsp;&emsp; D. diary

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我发现了一个装满___的小相册。A 项意为"信"；B 项意为"照片"；C 项意为"书"；D 项意为"硬币"。相册里装的是照片，且后文说"old photos"，"photos"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：爸爸大约是我这个___。A 项意为"年龄"；B 项意为"身高"；C 项意为"体重"；D 项意为"尺码"。照片中爸爸和"我"年纪差不多大，"age"正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：坐在教室里对着相机___。A 项意为"指向"；B 项意为"喊叫"；C 项意为"微笑"；D 项意为"投掷"。拍照时对着镜头微笑，"smiling"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我猜他一定赢了某种___。A 项意为"奖品"；B 项意为"游戏"；C 项意为"比赛"；D 项意为"礼物"。拿着奖杯说明赢了比赛，"competition"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他看起来非常___和放松。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"快乐的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。河边钓鱼，心情愉悦放松，"happy"与"relaxed"并列。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：钓鱼教会他要___，因为你不能催一条鱼。A 项意为"勇敢的"；B 项意为"诚实的"；C 项意为"耐心的"；D 项意为"有礼貌的"。钓鱼需要耐心等待，"patient"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的脸___起来，充满惊喜和喜悦。A 项意为"吹"；B 项意为"亮起"；C 项意为"关闭"；D 项意为"转"。"face lit up"是固定搭配，意为"容光焕发"。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他开始给我讲每张照片背后的___。A 项意为"秘密"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"规则"。分享照片背后的故事和回忆，"stories"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得自己正在了解爸爸一个完全___的一面。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"新的"；C 项意为"黑暗的"；D 项意为"旧的"。之前不了解的一面是全新的，"new"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：全因为一本落满灰尘的旧___。A 项意为"相册"；B 项意为"玩具"；C 项意为"地图"；D 项意为"日记"。整篇文章围绕相册展开，"album"正确。故选 A。
