# 中考英语完形填空：参观消防站

## 完形填空原文

Last week, our class visited the local fire station. It was an educational trip that I had been looking forward to for a long time. Firefighters are heroes. They risk their lives to save people. I wanted to learn more about their ___1___.

When we arrived, Captain Li welcomed us. He was tall and strong, with a kind smile. "Welcome to our station," he said. "Today I will show you what we do and how we work." First, he took us to see the fire trucks. They were big and red, with long hoses and ladders. Captain Li explained that each truck carries different ___2___. Some have water, some have foam for oil fires, and some have tools for rescue missions.

We also saw the fire suits. They are very heavy and can protect firefighters from heat and smoke. "When we get a call, we must put on our suits and get on the truck within one ___3___," Captain Li said. "Every second counts when someone's life is in danger." I was amazed. One minute! I could not even put on my school uniform that fast.

Then we had a drill. The alarm rang and we had to "escape" from a smoky room. We crawled low because smoke rises. We covered our mouths with wet cloths. It was not real, but I still felt a bit ___4___. I understood why it is important to know these skills. In a real fire, staying calm can save your life.

Captain Li also taught us about fire prevention. "Most fires can be ___5___," he said. "Do not play with matches or lighters. Do not leave the kitchen when you are cooking. Check your electrical appliances regularly." He gave each of us a small booklet with fire safety tips. I decided to take it home and share it with my ___6___.

Before we left, we took a group photo with the firefighters. They let us sit in the fire truck and try on a helmet. I felt like a real firefighter for a moment! On the bus back to school, we talked about what we had learned. My friend said, "I want to be a firefighter when I grow up." I said, "Me too. They are so ___7___."

That visit changed the way I think about firefighters. I used to think they only put out fires. But they do much more — they rescue people from car accidents, help during floods, and even save ___8___ from trees. They are always ready to help. I will never forget that day. It taught me to respect those who ___9___ their lives for others. We should all learn from their courage and selflessness. The next time I see a fire truck, I will remember the firefighters we met and feel ___10___ that such brave people are there to protect us.

1. A. hobbies &emsp;&emsp; B. jobs &emsp;&emsp; C. games &emsp;&emsp; D. rules

3. A. hour &emsp;&emsp; B. day &emsp;&emsp; C. minute &emsp;&emsp; D. week
4. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. excited &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. happy
5. A. started &emsp;&emsp; B. prevented &emsp;&emsp; C. caused &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoyed
6. A. teachers &emsp;&emsp; B. classmates &emsp;&emsp; C. family &emsp;&emsp; D. friends
7. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. brave &emsp;&emsp; C. lazy &emsp;&emsp; D. strict
8. A. fish &emsp;&emsp; B. birds &emsp;&emsp; C. cats &emsp;&emsp; D. dogs
9. A. save &emsp;&emsp; B. risk &emsp;&emsp; C. waste &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoy

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想更多了解他们的___。A 项意为"爱好"；B 项意为"工作"；C 项意为"游戏"；D 项意为"规则"。消防员的工作内容，"jobs"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每辆消防车携带不同的___。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"装备"；C 项意为"衣服"；D 项意为"书"。水、泡沫、救援工具等为装备，"equipment"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：接到警报后必须在一___内穿好装备上车。A 项意为"小时"；B 项意为"天"；C 项意为"分钟"；D 项意为"周"。强调出警速度，"one minute"符合实际。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：虽是模拟，我仍感到有点___。A 项意为"无聊"；B 项意为"兴奋"；C 项意为"紧张"；D 项意为"开心"。模拟逃生会有紧张感，"nervous"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：大多数火灾是可以___的。A 项意为"引起"；B 项意为"预防"；C 项意为"造成"；D 项意为"享受"。防火教育重点在预防，"prevented"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我决定带回家和___分享。A 项意为"老师"；B 项意为"同学"；C 项意为"家人"；D 项意为"朋友"。防火手册通常先与家人分享，"family"正确。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他们太___了。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"勇敢的"；C 项意为"懒的"；D 项意为"严格的"。消防员舍己为人，"brave"正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：甚至把树上的___救下来。A 项意为"鱼"；B 项意为"鸟"；C 项意为"猫"；D 项意为"狗"。爬树求救常见于猫，"cats"正确。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：尊重那些为他人___生命的人。A 项意为"拯救"；B 项意为"冒……风险"；C 项意为"浪费"；D 项意为"享受"。"risk their lives"意为"冒生命危险"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：想到有如此勇敢的人保护我们，感到___。A 项意为"担心"；B 项意为"难过"；C 项意为"安心"；D 项意为"抱歉"。"feel safe"表示有安全感。故选 C。
