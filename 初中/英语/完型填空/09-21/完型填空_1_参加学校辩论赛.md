# 中考英语完形填空：参加学校辩论赛

## 完形填空原文

Last month, our school held an English debate competition. I was ___1___ to be chosen as a member of our class team. The topic was "Should students use mobile phones at school?" We were the negative side, meaning we had to argue that students should not use phones during school hours.

We spent two weeks preparing for the debate. Every afternoon after school, our team gathered in the empty classroom to ___2___ our arguments. Our English teacher, Miss Li, gave us a lot of help. She taught us how to organize our ideas and how to ___3___ our points clearly.

The day of the competition finally arrived. When I walked onto the stage, my heart was beating so fast that I could hardly ___4___. The other team spoke first. They gave many reasons to support their opinion. When it was our turn, my teammate stood up and presented our main arguments. I was responsible for the rebuttal part — I had to respond to what the opposite side had said.

I took a deep breath and stood up. I looked at the ___5___ and tried to speak slowly and clearly. To my surprise, my voice was steady and my words came out naturally. I pointed out that phones could distract students from their studies and that face-to-face communication was more ___6___ for building real friendships.

At the end, the judges announced the result. We won! I was so ___7___ that I could not believe it. Our team hugged each other and cheered. Miss Li came over with a big smile and said, "You did a wonderful job. I'm ___8___ of all of you."

This experience taught me that with enough preparation and ___9___, we can overcome our fears and achieve our goals. I now feel more confident when speaking in front of people. I hope to take ___10___ in more such activities in the future.

2. A. practice &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. avoid &emsp;&emsp; D. refuse
3. A. hide &emsp;&emsp; B. express &emsp;&emsp; C. doubt &emsp;&emsp; D. change

6. A. difficult &emsp;&emsp; B. important &emsp;&emsp; C. useless &emsp;&emsp; D. dangerous
7. A. worried &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. excited &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointed
8. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. afraid &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. ashamed
9. A. money &emsp;&emsp; B. courage &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. time
10. A. care &emsp;&emsp; B. part &emsp;&emsp; C. place &emsp;&emsp; D. pride

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很荣幸被选为我们班辩论队的一员。A 项意为"害怕的"；B 项意为"骄傲的；荣幸的"；C 项意为"疲劳的"；D 项意为"无聊的"。被选为队员是荣誉，proud 符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每天放学后，我们队在空教室里练习我们的论据。A 项意为"练习"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"拒绝"。准备辩论需要练习，practice 正确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她教我们如何组织想法、如何清晰地表达观点。A 项意为"隐藏"；B 项意为"表达"；C 项意为"怀疑"；D 项意为"改变"。辩论需要清晰表达观点，express 正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：走上舞台时，我的心跳得太快，几乎无法呼吸。A 项意为"思考"；B 项意为"呼吸"；C 项意为"移动"；D 项意为"睡觉"。heart beating fast 常与"紧张得喘不过气"相关，hardly breathe 为常见表达。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看看观众，尽量说得慢而清晰。A 项意为"观众"；B 项意为"老师"；C 项意为"队友"；D 项意为"评委"。在舞台上演讲时面对的是观众，audience 正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：面对面交流对于建立真正的友谊更重要。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"重要的"；C 项意为"无用的"；D 项意为"危险的"。建立友谊的重要性，important 符合论点。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们赢了！我太激动了，简直不敢相信。A 项意为"担心的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"激动的"；D 项意为"失望的"。获胜后感到兴奋，excited 正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：你们做得太棒了，我为你们所有人感到骄傲。A 项意为"疲劳的"；B 项意为"害怕的"；C 项意为"骄傲的"；D 项意为"羞愧的"。be proud of 意为"以……为骄傲"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这次经历告诉我，只要有足够的准备和勇气，我们就能克服恐惧、实现目标。A 项意为"钱"；B 项意为"勇气"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"时间"。克服恐惧需要勇气，courage 正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 固定短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我希望将来能参加更多这样的活动。A 项意为"照顾"；B 项意为"参加"；C 项意为"发生"；D 项意为"骄傲"。take part in 意为"参加"。故选 B。
