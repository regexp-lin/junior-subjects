# 中考英语完形填空：学做糖葫芦

## 完形填空原文

During the winter holiday, I visited my grandmother in the countryside. She is famous in our village for making sweet candied hawthorn, which we call "tanghulu" in Chinese. One afternoon, she asked if I wanted to ___1___ how to make it. I agreed immediately because I had always loved eating it.

Grandmother first washed the fresh hawthorn fruits and dried them with a clean cloth. "The fruits must be completely dry," she said, "or the sugar coating will not ___2___ well." Then she threaded the fruits onto bamboo sticks, usually five or six on each stick. She made it look so ___3___, but when I tried, my fruits kept falling off.

"Don't rush. Hold the stick firmly and push the fruit through the centre," she told me. After many ___4___, I finally managed to string a few sticks. Grandmother smiled and said I was learning quickly.

The next step was the most exciting — making the sugar syrup. She heated sugar and water in a pot over a low fire. "We must watch the colour ___5___," she said. "When it turns golden, it's ready. If we take it off too early, the coating will be too soft. If we wait too long, it will become too hard."

When the syrup was ready, Grandmother quickly dipped each stick of hawthorn into the hot sugar. The fruits were covered with a shiny, transparent ___6___. She placed them on a greased board to cool. In a few minutes, the sugar hardened and the tanghulu was done.

I tried making one by myself. I was so nervous that my hand ___7___. The sugar dripped unevenly and my tanghulu looked ugly. Grandmother encouraged me, "The first time is always the hardest. You'll do better next time."

That evening, we sat together and enjoyed the tanghulu we had made. The sweet and sour taste brought back many happy ___8___ of my childhood. Grandmother said that making tanghulu was not just about the food — it was about passing down traditions and spending ___9___ time with family.

I felt very grateful for that day. I had not only learned a new skill but also understood the ___10___ of family traditions. When I go back to the city, I will try to make tanghulu for my friends and share this wonderful experience with them.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她问我是否想学做糖葫芦。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"学习"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"拒绝"。祖母教做糖葫芦，learn 符合语境。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：果实必须完全干燥，否则糖衣粘不住。A 项意为"品尝"；B 项意为"粘住"；C 项意为"闻起来"；D 项意为"看起来"。糖衣需要附着在果实上，stick 正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她做起来看起来很容易，但我试的时候水果总是掉下来。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。与"我"的困难形成对比，easy 正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：经过多次尝试，我终于穿好几串。A 项意为"错误"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"想法"；D 项意为"尝试"。after many attempts 意为"经过多次尝试"。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们必须仔细观察糖浆的颜色。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"粗心地"；C 项意为"快速地"；D 项意为"缓慢地"。熬糖浆需要仔细观察火候，carefully 正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：果实被一层光亮透明的糖衣包裹。A 项意为"盖子"；B 项意为"涂层；外衣"；C 项意为"层"；D 项意为"片"。糖衣 coating，coat 为动词"涂覆"的名词用法，sugar coat 糖衣。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我太紧张了，手在发抖。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"颤抖"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"受伤"。紧张时手抖，shook 正确。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：酸甜的味道唤起了我童年的许多快乐回忆。A 项意为"梦想"；B 项意为"回忆"；C 项意为"计划"；D 项意为"秘密"。bring back memories 意为"唤起回忆"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 固定短语
**【解析】** 本句意为：做糖葫芦不仅是关于食物，更是关于传承传统和与家人共度美好时光。A 项意为"短的"；B 项意为"忙碌的"；C 项意为"高质量的；美好的"；D 项意为"空闲的"。quality time 意为"有意义的共处时光"。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我也明白了家庭传统的重要性。A 项意为"困难"；B 项意为"重要性"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"原因"。理解传统的重要性，importance 正确。故选 B。
