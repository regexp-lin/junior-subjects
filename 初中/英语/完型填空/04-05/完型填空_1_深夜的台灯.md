# 中考英语完形填空：深夜的台灯

## 完形填空原文

Liu Mei was in her last year of middle school. The final exam was only one month away, and she studied very hard every night. Her desk lamp was always the last light on in the whole ___1___.

Her father worked at a factory and came home late every day. He was not good with words, and he ___2___ said "I love you" or gave her hugs. Liu Mei sometimes felt that her father didn't care about her.

One night, Liu Mei was studying math at her desk. It was already past midnight. She was so ___3___ that she could hardly keep her eyes open. She decided to get a glass of water from the kitchen.

When she ___4___ her bedroom door, she was shocked. Her father was sitting on a small chair in the dark hallway, right outside her room. He had fallen asleep with his head leaning against the wall.

"Dad?" Liu Mei whispered. Her father ___5___ up immediately. "Oh, you're still awake? I was just... checking if the front door was locked," he said quickly, looking a little embarrassed.

But Liu Mei noticed a ___6___ beside him — a cup of warm milk and two biscuits on a small plate. They were for her. She suddenly ___7___ that every morning when she woke up, there was always a glass of milk on her desk. She had thought her mother made it, but her mother always went to bed before ten.

Liu Mei's eyes filled with ___8___. She finally realized that her father showed love not through words, but through small, quiet actions — waiting outside her door every night, making sure she was fine, and preparing milk for her without ever ___9___ it.

She took the milk and biscuits. "Thanks, Dad," she said softly. Her father smiled, and that simple smile was ___10___ a thousand words.

## 选项

1. A. school &emsp;&emsp; B. building &emsp;&emsp; C. street &emsp;&emsp; D. city
2. A. always &emsp;&emsp; B. often &emsp;&emsp; C. seldom &emsp;&emsp; D. usually
3. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. hungry &emsp;&emsp; C. nervous &emsp;&emsp; D. lonely
4. A. closed &emsp;&emsp; B. opened &emsp;&emsp; C. painted &emsp;&emsp; D. cleaned
5. A. gave &emsp;&emsp; B. picked &emsp;&emsp; C. woke &emsp;&emsp; D. stood
6. A. book &emsp;&emsp; B. bag &emsp;&emsp; C. tray &emsp;&emsp; D. phone
7. A. forgot &emsp;&emsp; B. realized &emsp;&emsp; C. imagined &emsp;&emsp; D. hoped
8. A. anger &emsp;&emsp; B. fear &emsp;&emsp; C. tears &emsp;&emsp; D. surprise
9. A. mentioning &emsp;&emsp; B. enjoying &emsp;&emsp; C. refusing &emsp;&emsp; D. finishing
10. A. louder than &emsp;&emsp; B. worth &emsp;&emsp; C. less than &emsp;&emsp; D. about

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的台灯总是整栋___里最后熄灭的灯。A 项意为"学校"；B 项意为"建筑/楼"；C 项意为"街道"；D 项意为"城市"。她在家里学习，台灯是整栋楼里最后亮着的灯，说明她学到很晚。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他不善言辞，___说"我爱你"或者拥抱她。A 项意为"总是"；B 项意为"经常"；C 项意为"很少"；D 项意为"通常"。前文说他不善言辞（not good with words），所以他"很少"（seldom）表达爱意，与后文他通过无声行动表达爱形成对比。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她太___了，几乎睁不开眼。A 项意为"疲倦的"；B 项意为"饥饿的"；C 项意为"紧张的"；D 项意为"孤独的"。已经过了午夜，学了很久，"几乎睁不开眼"说明她非常"疲倦"（tired）。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当她___卧室门时，她震惊了。A 项意为"关上"；B 项意为"打开"；C 项意为"粉刷"；D 项意为"打扫"。她要去厨房拿水，所以是"打开"（opened）卧室门走出去，然后才看到走廊里的父亲。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的父亲立刻___了。A 项意为"给"；B 项意为"捡起"；C 项意为"醒来"；D 项意为"站起"。父亲在走廊睡着了，被女儿的声音叫醒，"woke up"（醒来）是固定搭配。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：但刘梅注意到他旁边有一个___——一杯温牛奶和一个小盘子里放着两块饼干。A 项意为"书"；B 项意为"包"；C 项意为"托盘"；D 项意为"手机"。后面描述的是牛奶和饼干，放在一起自然需要一个"托盘"（tray）来承载。故选 C。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她突然___每天早上醒来时桌上总有一杯牛奶。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"意识到"；C 项意为"想象"；D 项意为"希望"。看到父亲准备的牛奶后，她恍然大悟，"意识到"（realized）原来每天桌上的牛奶都是父亲准备的。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：刘梅的眼里充满了___。A 项意为"愤怒"；B 项意为"恐惧"；C 项意为"泪水"；D 项意为"惊讶"。她被父亲默默的爱所感动，眼里充满了"泪水"（tears），"eyes filled with tears"是常见表达。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（动名词形式）
**【解析】** 本句意为：为她准备牛奶却从不___。A 项意为"提起/提及"；B 项意为"享受"；C 项意为"拒绝"；D 项意为"完成"。父亲默默做这些事却从不"提及"（mentioning），体现了他沉默但深沉的爱。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词/短语辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那个简单的微笑___一千句话。A 项意为"比……更响"；B 项意为"抵得上/值得"；C 项意为"少于"；D 项意为"大约"。"worth a thousand words"意为"抵得上千言万语"，父亲的微笑虽无声，但胜过千言万语。故选 B。
