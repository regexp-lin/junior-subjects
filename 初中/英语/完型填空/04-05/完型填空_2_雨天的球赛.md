# 中考英语完形填空：雨天的球赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school basketball team had been practicing for months. We finally made it to the city finals. Everyone was excited, but on the day of the big game, dark clouds covered the sky and heavy rain started to ___1___.

The game was held at an outdoor court. The referee said the match would go on as planned. Some of my teammates looked ___2___ — the wet ground was slippery, and it was hard to see clearly in the rain.

"We can't play in this weather!" Wang Hao, our tallest player, said. "We'll definitely ___3___."

Coach Zhang gathered us together. "Listen," he said calmly. "The rain falls on both teams ___4___. It's the same difficulty for everyone. The team that adjusts faster will win."

The game started. In the first half, we played terribly. The ball kept ___5___ out of our hands. The other team scored again and again. By halftime, we were behind by twelve points.

During the break, Coach Zhang didn't ___6___ at us. Instead, he said, "Forget the score. Focus on one thing: slow down. In the rain, you need to hold the ball tighter and pass more carefully."

We went back on the court with a new plan. We slowed our pace, made shorter passes, and stayed ___7___ to each other. Slowly, we started to catch up. With two minutes left, we were only behind by two points.

In the last thirty seconds, I got the ball. The rain was pouring down my face, and I could ___8___ see the basket. But I remembered what Coach said: slow down. I took a deep breath, aimed carefully, and threw the ball. It went in! The game was ___9___!

We didn't actually win — the game ended in a tie and we lost in overtime. But none of us felt sad. We had learned something more important than ___10___: when life gives you rain, don't complain. Just learn to play in it.

## 选项

1. A. fall &emsp;&emsp; B. stop &emsp;&emsp; C. shine &emsp;&emsp; D. blow
2. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. bored &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. proud
3. A. win &emsp;&emsp; B. lose &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. try
4. A. differently &emsp;&emsp; B. quickly &emsp;&emsp; C. equally &emsp;&emsp; D. heavily
5. A. jumping &emsp;&emsp; B. slipping &emsp;&emsp; C. flying &emsp;&emsp; D. rolling
6. A. laugh &emsp;&emsp; B. point &emsp;&emsp; C. look &emsp;&emsp; D. shout
7. A. close &emsp;&emsp; B. strange &emsp;&emsp; C. polite &emsp;&emsp; D. blind
8. A. clearly &emsp;&emsp; B. easily &emsp;&emsp; C. hardly &emsp;&emsp; D. always
9. A. over &emsp;&emsp; B. tied &emsp;&emsp; C. started &emsp;&emsp; D. cancelled
10. A. playing &emsp;&emsp; B. winning &emsp;&emsp; C. practicing &emsp;&emsp; D. resting

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：乌云遮住天空，大雨开始___。A 项意为"落下"；B 项意为"停止"；C 项意为"照耀"；D 项意为"吹"。"rain falls"（雨落下）是最自然的搭配。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我的一些队友看起来很___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"无聊的"；C 项意为"担忧的"；D 项意为"自豪的"。后面解释了原因：地面湿滑、看不清楚，这些不利条件让队友们感到"担忧"（worried）。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："这种天气我们没法打！我们肯定会___。"A 项意为"赢"；B 项意为"输"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"尝试"。王浩认为恶劣天气对己方不利，所以觉得肯定会"输"（lose）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："雨对两支队伍是___下的。"A 项意为"不同地"；B 项意为"快速地"；C 项意为"平等地"；D 项意为"猛烈地"。教练的意思是雨对双方都一样，困难是"平等的"（equally），关键在于谁能更快适应。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：球不断从我们手中___出去。A 项意为"跳"；B 项意为"滑落"；C 项意为"飞"；D 项意为"滚"。下雨天地面和手都是湿的，球会从手中"滑落"（slipping），与前文"slippery"呼应。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：中场休息时，张教练没有___我们。A 项意为"嘲笑"；B 项意为"指"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"大喊/吼"。球队落后12分表现很差，教练本可以大声训斥，但他没有"吼"（shout at）大家，而是冷静地给出建议，体现了教练的沉稳。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：保持彼此___。A 项意为"靠近的"；B 项意为"奇怪的"；C 项意为"礼貌的"；D 项意为"盲目的"。教练说要短传，所以队员之间要保持"靠近"（close），这样传球距离短，不容易失误。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：雨水倾泻在我脸上，我___看清篮筐。A 项意为"清楚地"；B 项意为"容易地"；C 项意为"几乎不"；D 项意为"总是"。大雨倾盆打在脸上，"几乎看不见"（could hardly see）篮筐，体现了投篮的困难程度。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：比赛___了！A 项意为"结束了"；B 项意为"打平了"；C 项意为"开始了"；D 项意为"取消了"。后文明确说"the game ended in a tie"（比赛以平局结束），投进这球后双方打平，比赛"打平"（tied）。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们学到了比___更重要的东西。A 项意为"打球"；B 项意为"赢"；C 项意为"练习"；D 项意为"休息"。虽然最后没有赢（加时赛输了），但他们学到了比"赢"（winning）更重要的道理——面对困难不要抱怨，而是学会适应。故选 B。
