# 中考英语完形填空：第一次当校园广播站主持人

## 完形填空原文

Last month, I was chosen to be a host for our school radio station. When I heard the news, I felt both excited and ___1___. I had never spoken in front of so many people before. The teacher told me that I would read the morning news and play some music for the whole school.

On my first day, I arrived at the radio room early. I ___2___ the script several times to make sure I would not make any mistakes. My hands were shaking when I picked up the microphone. "Relax," said the teacher. "Just speak clearly and ___3___."

The music started, and then it was my turn. I took a deep breath and began to read. At first, my voice sounded a bit ___4___, but soon I became more confident. I read the weather report, the school news, and a short story. When I finished, the teacher gave me a thumbs-up. I felt so ___5___ that I could not stop smiling.

After that day, I host the radio programme every Tuesday morning. I have learned a lot. Speaking clearly is important, but so is choosing the right ___6___. I try to pick songs that students enjoy and news that is useful. Sometimes I make small mistakes, but I have learned to ___7___ them and keep going.

Being a radio host has helped me become braver. I used to be shy when speaking in class, but now I can stand in front of others more ___8___. If you get a chance to try something new, do not be afraid. The first step might be difficult, but the ___9___ is wonderful. I am thankful for this ___10___ and I will do my best to make our school radio better.

1. A. happy &emsp;&emsp; B. nervous &emsp;&emsp; C. bored &emsp;&emsp; D. angry
2. A. wrote &emsp;&emsp; B. practised &emsp;&emsp; C. forgot &emsp;&emsp; D. copied
3. A. slowly &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. bravely
4. A. strong &emsp;&emsp; B. sweet &emsp;&emsp; C. weak &emsp;&emsp; D. deep
5. A. tired &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. worried &emsp;&emsp; D. sad
6. A. time &emsp;&emsp; B. content &emsp;&emsp; C. place &emsp;&emsp; D. reason
7. A. avoid &emsp;&emsp; B. correct &emsp;&emsp; C. ignore &emsp;&emsp; D. remember
8. A. carefully &emsp;&emsp; B. easily &emsp;&emsp; C. suddenly &emsp;&emsp; D. seriously
9. A. journey &emsp;&emsp; B. problem &emsp;&emsp; C. exam &emsp;&emsp; D. mistake
10. A. gift &emsp;&emsp; B. idea &emsp;&emsp; C. chance &emsp;&emsp; D. prize

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：当我听到这个消息时，我感到既兴奋又紧张。A 项意为"开心的"；B 项意为"紧张的"；C 项意为"无聊的"；D 项意为"生气的"。第一次当主持人，从未在这么多人面前说话，excited 和 nervous 常一起出现。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我反复练习稿子，确保不出错。A 项意为"写"；B 项意为"练习"；C 项意为"忘记"；D 项意为"抄写"。practise the script 意为"练习稿子"，符合上台前准备的情境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：老师说："放轻松，清晰地说话，语速慢一点。"A 项意为"慢慢地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"勇敢地"。广播主持需 speak clearly，语速不宜过快，slowly 更合适。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我的声音听起来有点微弱，但很快我就更有信心了。A 项意为"强壮的"；B 项意为"甜美的"；C 项意为"微弱的"；D 项意为"深沉的"。紧张的初学者声音往往 weak，与后文"soon became more confident"形成对比。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：完成后老师竖起大拇指，我感到非常自豪，忍不住微笑。A 项意为"累的"；B 项意为"自豪的"；C 项意为"担心的"；D 项意为"悲伤的"。顺利完成首次主持，得到肯定，应感到 proud。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：说得清楚很重要，但选择合适的内容也很重要。A 项意为"时间"；B 项意为"内容"；C 项意为"地方"；D 项意为"原因"。后文提到选歌和选新闻，都是在选 content。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时我会犯小错，但我学会了忽略它们并继续下去。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"纠正"；C 项意为"忽略"；D 项意为"记住"。ignore small mistakes and keep going 指不在小错误上纠结，继续主持。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：以前在课堂上发言会害羞，但现在我可以更轻松地站在他人面前。A 项意为"仔细地"；B 项意为"轻松地"；C 项意为"突然地"；D 项意为"严肃地"。stand in front of others more easily 表示"更容易、更自然地面对他人"。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：第一步可能很难，但这段旅程很美妙。A 项意为"旅程、过程"；B 项意为"问题"；C 项意为"考试"；D 项意为"错误"。the journey is wonderful 指整个成长过程很美好。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我很感激这次机会，我会尽力让校园广播更好。A 项意为"礼物"；B 项意为"想法"；C 项意为"机会"；D 项意为"奖品"。be thankful for this chance 表示对这次机会心怀感激。故选 C。
