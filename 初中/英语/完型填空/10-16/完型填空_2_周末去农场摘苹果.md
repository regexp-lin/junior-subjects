# 中考英语完形填空：周末去农场摘苹果

## 完形填空原文

Last Saturday, my parents took me to a farm to pick apples. It was a sunny autumn morning and the ___1___ was fresh. We drove for about an hour and finally reached the farm. As soon as we got out of the car, I could see rows of apple trees with red and golden fruit hanging from the branches.

The farmer gave each of us a basket and showed us how to pick apples ___2___. "Choose the ones that are ripe and pull them gently," he said. "Do not shake the branches, or the apples will ___3___." I followed his advice and soon my basket was half full.

Walking between the trees, I enjoyed the peace of the farm. Birds were singing and bees were ___4___ around the flowers. I picked a big red apple and bit into it. The taste was sweet and delicious — much better than the apples from the supermarket. My father said that fruit tastes better when you pick it ___5___ yourself.

After we finished picking, we went to the farmhouse to pay for our apples. The farmer's wife offered us some homemade apple juice. It was so ___6___ that we each had two glasses. Before we left, we also bought some eggs and vegetables. The farmer said they were all ___7___ that morning.

On our way home, I thought about what a wonderful day it had been. Picking apples is not only fun but also teaches us where our food ___8___. I hope we can come back next year. Maybe we can try picking ___9___ like strawberries or pumpkins. I will never ___10___ this special weekend with my family.

1. A. food &emsp;&emsp; B. water &emsp;&emsp; C. air &emsp;&emsp; D. light
2. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. correctly &emsp;&emsp; C. loudly &emsp;&emsp; D. sadly
3. A. grow &emsp;&emsp; B. fall &emsp;&emsp; C. rise &emsp;&emsp; D. stay
4. A. flying &emsp;&emsp; B. running &emsp;&emsp; C. jumping &emsp;&emsp; D. walking
5. A. with &emsp;&emsp; B. by &emsp;&emsp; C. for &emsp;&emsp; D. from
6. A. cold &emsp;&emsp; B. fresh &emsp;&emsp; C. bitter &emsp;&emsp; D. expensive
7. A. cooked &emsp;&emsp; B. collected &emsp;&emsp; C. cleaned &emsp;&emsp; D. sold
8. A. leaves &emsp;&emsp; B. keeps &emsp;&emsp; C. comes &emsp;&emsp; D. returns
9. A. others &emsp;&emsp; B. another &emsp;&emsp; C. the other &emsp;&emsp; D. other
10. A. forget &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. believe &emsp;&emsp; D. expect

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：那是一个晴朗的秋日早晨，空气清新。A 项意为"食物"；B 项意为"水"；C 项意为"空气"；D 项意为"光线"。形容秋日早晨清新常用 fresh air。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：农夫给我们每人一个篮子，并示范如何正确摘苹果。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"正确地"；C 项意为"大声地"；D 项意为"悲伤地"。农民示范的是正确方法，pick correctly 符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：不要摇晃树枝，否则苹果会掉落。A 项意为"生长"；B 项意为"掉落"；C 项意为"升起"；D 项意为"停留"。shake the branches 会导致苹果 fall down。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：鸟儿在歌唱，蜜蜂在花丛中飞舞。A 项意为"飞"；B 项意为"跑"；C 项意为"跳"；D 项意为"走"。蜜蜂在花间用 fly 描述。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：父亲说亲手摘的水果味道更好。A 项意为"和……一起"；B 项意为"通过"；C 项意为"为"；D 项意为"从"。pick it for yourself 意为"为自己采摘、亲手采摘"，强调亲身经历。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：农家自制的苹果汁非常新鲜，我们每人喝了两杯。A 项意为"凉的"；B 项意为"新鲜的"；C 项意为"苦的"；D 项意为"贵的"。homemade 的果汁 fresh 更贴切，突出农场特色。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：农夫说鸡蛋和蔬菜都是今早刚采收的。A 项意为"烹饪"；B 项意为"收集、采收"；C 项意为"清洗"；D 项意为"出售"。collected that morning 指当天早晨采摘/收集。故选 B。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析及固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：摘苹果不仅有趣，还让我们了解食物从何而来。A 项意为"离开"；B 项意为"保存"；C 项意为"来自"；D 项意为"回来"。where our food comes from 为固定表达，意为"食物从哪里来"。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 代词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：也许明年我们可以试试摘草莓或南瓜等其他水果。A 项意为"其他的"（需接复数）；B 项意为"另一个"；C 项意为"另一个"（两者中的另一）；D 项意为"其他的"。other things like... 表示"其他诸如……的东西"。故选 D。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我永远不会忘记这个与家人共度的特别周末。A 项意为"忘记"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"相信"；D 项意为"期待"。never forget 表示"永不忘怀"，表达对美好回忆的珍惜。故选 A。
