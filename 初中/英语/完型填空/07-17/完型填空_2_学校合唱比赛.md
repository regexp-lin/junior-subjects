# 中考英语完形填空：学校合唱比赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school choir competition was held last month. Every class had to perform a song. Our class chose "You Are My Sunshine" — a classic song that everyone knows. We had only two weeks to ___1___. Everyone was a bit nervous because we had never sung together as a class before.

Our music teacher, Mr Chen, helped us a lot. He divided us into different parts. Some students sang the main melody while others sang the harmony. At first it sounded ___2___. We could not keep the same rhythm. Mr Chen was patient. He said, "Listen to each other. Singing in a choir means we become one voice." We practised every lunch break and after school.

I was not a good singer. I was afraid I would ___3___ the performance. But my classmates encouraged me. "Just follow the person next to you," they said. "We are in this together." Their words made me feel better. I tried my best to blend my voice with the others. Slowly, we began to sound more ___4___.

On the day of the competition, we wore white shirts and dark trousers. We looked neat and united. Before we went on stage, we held hands and took a deep breath. "We can do this!" our monitor said. The ___5___ was full of students and teachers. When the music started, we all sang from our hearts. I forgot my nervousness. I only felt the joy of singing together.

We did not win first place, but we won the "Best Team Spirit" award. The judge said, "Your class showed the true meaning of unity. Your voices blended perfectly." We were all very ___6___. We had worked hard and it had paid off.

That experience taught me something important. Success is not always about being the best. Sometimes it is about doing something ___7___ with others. When we sing together, our small voices become one big, beautiful sound. That is the magic of a choir.

I still remember the moment we stood on stage. The lights were bright and the audience was quiet. We opened our mouths and the music ___8___. It was one of the best moments of my school life. I am grateful to my classmates for their patience and support. Together we created something we could never have achieved ___9___. That is what makes our class special. We are not just classmates. We are a ___10___ that can achieve great things when we work together.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们只有两周时间___。A 项意为"准备"；B 项意为"等待"；C 项意为"休息"；D 项意为"旅行"。比赛前需要准备，"prepare"正确。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初听起来很___。A 项意为"完美的"；B 项意为"糟糕的"；C 项意为"安静的"；D 项意为"大声的"。刚练时节奏不齐，声音杂乱，"terrible"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我怕会___演出。A 项意为"改进"；B 项意为"帮助"；C 项意为"搞砸"；D 项意为"观看"。唱得不好担心破坏整体，"ruin"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：慢慢地，我们的歌声变得更___。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"吵闹的"；C 项意为"和谐的"；D 项意为"弱的"。合唱追求和谐，"harmonious"正确。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___里坐满了学生和老师。A 项意为"礼堂"；B 项意为"教室"；C 项意为"办公室"；D 项意为"图书馆"。比赛通常在礼堂进行，"hall"正确。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：获得"最佳团队精神"奖，大家都很___。A 项意为"难过"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"生气"；D 项意为"无聊"。获奖后的骄傲，"proud"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：有时成功在于与他人___做某事。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"快速"；C 项意为"一起"；D 项意为"慢"。强调集体协作，"together"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们开口，音乐就___了。A 项意为"停止"；B 项意为"开始"；C 项意为"失败"；D 项意为"结束"。开唱即音乐响起，"started"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们一起创造了独自一人无法___完成的事。A 项意为"独自"；B 项意为"首先"；C 项意为"最后"；D 项意为"一起"。"achieve alone"与"together"形成对比。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们是一个___，团结能成就大事。A 项意为"小组"；B 项意为"团队"；C 项意为"班级"；D 项意为"家庭"。"team"强调协作精神，与"work together"呼应。故选 B。
