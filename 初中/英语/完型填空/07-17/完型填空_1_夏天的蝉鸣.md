# 中考英语完形填空：夏天的蝉鸣

## 完形填空原文

Every summer, the cicadas come. Their singing fills the trees and becomes the ___1___ of hot afternoons. When I was a child, I found the sound too loud and sometimes annoying. But now I have learned to love it. To me, it is the real sound of summer.

Cicadas spend most of their lives underground. They live there for several years, sometimes more than ten years. Then one day they come out, climb up a tree, and ___2___ their wings. For a few weeks, they sing as loud as they can. Then their short life above ground comes to an end. Knowing this, I feel that each cicada's song is special. They are ___3___ every moment they have.

Last summer, I was doing my homework by the window. The cicadas were singing outside. At first I could not ___4___. I closed the window to block the noise. But the room became too hot, so I had to open it again. Slowly, I began to listen. The sound was not just noise. It had a pattern — sometimes high, sometimes low, like a natural ___5___. I found it quite peaceful. My mind became calm and I finished my homework quickly.

One afternoon, I went for a walk in the park. I sat under a big tree and closed my eyes. The cicadas were everywhere. Their voices mixed with the wind in the leaves. I felt like I was in the middle of a summer ___6___. For the first time, I understood why some people love this sound. It connects us to nature. It reminds us that we are part of something bigger.

Summer will pass and the cicadas will ___7___. But their song will stay in my memory. When winter comes and everything is quiet, I will think of those hot afternoons. I will remember the sound that used to seem so ___8___. Now it brings me warm feelings. It tells me that summer will come again.

I hope that when I grow up, I will still have a place where I can hear the cicadas. Maybe I will take my children to listen to them one day. I will tell them the story of these small ___9___ and their short but wonderful life. The cicada's song is a gift. We should learn to ___10___ it before it is gone.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们的鸣声充满树木，成为炎热午后的一种___。A 项意为"寂静"；B 项意为"音乐"；C 项意为"问题"；D 项意为"秘密"。蝉鸣如夏日背景音乐，"music"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们爬上树，___翅膀。A 项意为"清洁"；B 项意为"展开"；C 项意为"藏"；D 项意为"弄破"。蝉出土后展翅，"spread their wings"正确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们在___每一刻。A 项意为"浪费"；B 项意为"节省"；C 项意为"享受"；D 项意为"失去"。生命短暂故珍惜当下，"enjoying"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：起初我无法___。A 项意为"集中注意力"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"说话"；D 项意为"听见"。蝉鸣干扰做作业，"concentrate"正确。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：像一首自然的___。A 项意为"机器"；B 项意为"歌"；C 项意为"铃"；D 项意为"鼓"。蝉鸣有高低起伏如歌，"song"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：仿佛置身于一场夏日___中。A 项意为"聚会"；B 项意为"音乐会"；C 项意为"比赛"；D 项意为"考试"。蝉鸣与风声交织如音乐会，"concert"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：夏天过去，蝉会___。A 项意为"出现"；B 项意为"歌唱"；C 项意为"消失"；D 项意为"生长"。夏天结束蝉消失，"disappear"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：曾经觉得那么___的声音。A 项意为"令人愉快的"；B 项意为"恼人的"；C 项意为"美的"；D 项意为"奇怪的"。与前文"annoying"呼应，"annoying"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：给孩子们讲这些小___的故事。A 项意为"鸟"；B 项意为"昆虫"；C 项意为"动物"；D 项意为"树"。蝉属于昆虫，"insects"正确。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们应该学会___它，趁它还在。A 项意为"避免"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"珍惜"；D 项意为"改变"。珍惜短暂的美好，"treasure"正确。故选 C。
