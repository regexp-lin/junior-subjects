# 中考英语完形填空：篮球赛

## 完形填空原文

Our school held its annual basketball competition last month. My class signed up immediately because we had some of the best ___1___ in the whole grade. We practised every afternoon after school, hoping to win the championship.

The first two games were easy. We won both of them by large scores. Everyone was ___2___ and we started to think we would definitely win the final. Our coach, Mr Liu, warned us, "Don't be too confident. The other team is very ___3___ too. You need to keep practising hard."

The final came on Friday afternoon. The whole school gathered in the gym to watch. Our opponent was Class Five, who had also won all their previous games. From the very beginning, we could tell this game would be ___4___. Both teams played well and the score was always very close.

In the last five minutes, Class Five was ahead by two points. I could see some of my teammates looking ___5___. But our captain, Wang Lei, shouted, "Come on! We can still do it! Don't give up!" His words gave us new ___6___.

With only one minute left, I got the ball near the three-point line. My heart was beating fast. I took a deep breath and ___7___ the ball towards the basket. Time seemed to slow down. The ball went up, hit the rim, bounced once, and then — it went in! The crowd went wild. We were now one point ___8___.

Class Five tried to score in the final seconds but failed. When the final whistle blew, our whole team jumped up and cheered. We had won! It was the ___9___ moment of my school life so far.

After the game, Mr Liu said to us, "You won because you never gave up, even when things looked bad. Remember, ___10___ is not just about talent. It is about teamwork and perseverance."

## 选项

1. A. students &emsp;&emsp; B. players &emsp;&emsp; C. singers &emsp;&emsp; D. painters
2. A. nervous &emsp;&emsp; B. confused &emsp;&emsp; C. confident &emsp;&emsp; D. disappointed
3. A. weak &emsp;&emsp; B. slow &emsp;&emsp; C. strong &emsp;&emsp; D. careless
4. A. boring &emsp;&emsp; B. tough &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. short
5. A. excited &emsp;&emsp; B. worried &emsp;&emsp; C. proud &emsp;&emsp; D. relaxed
6. A. problems &emsp;&emsp; B. energy &emsp;&emsp; C. pressure &emsp;&emsp; D. homework
7. A. threw &emsp;&emsp; B. caught &emsp;&emsp; C. kicked &emsp;&emsp; D. held
8. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. ahead &emsp;&emsp; C. away &emsp;&emsp; D. outside
9. A. saddest &emsp;&emsp; B. quietest &emsp;&emsp; C. proudest &emsp;&emsp; D. longest
10. A. success &emsp;&emsp; B. failure &emsp;&emsp; C. luck &emsp;&emsp; D. health

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们班有全年级最好的几位___。A 项意为"学生"；B 项意为"球员"；C 项意为"歌手"；D 项意为"画家"。篮球比赛报名是因为有好的球员，"players"正确。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：每个人都很___，我们开始觉得一定能赢得决赛。A 项意为"紧张的"；B 项意为"困惑的"；C 项意为"自信的"；D 项意为"失望的"。前两场大胜，大家变得自信，"confident"正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：对方也非常___。A 项意为"弱的"；B 项意为"慢的"；C 项意为"强的"；D 项意为"粗心的"。教练提醒不能轻敌，对手也很强，"strong"正确。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们看得出这场比赛会很___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"艰难的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"短的"。两支强队对决，比赛会很艰难激烈，"tough"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我看到一些队友看起来___。A 项意为"兴奋的"；B 项意为"担心的"；C 项意为"自豪的"；D 项意为"放松的"。落后两分，队友们开始担忧，"worried"正确。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他的话给了我们新的___。A 项意为"问题"；B 项意为"能量"；C 项意为"压力"；D 项意为"作业"。队长鼓励振奋了士气，给了新的力量，"energy"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我深吸一口气，___球投向篮筐。A 项意为"投、扔"；B 项意为"接住"；C 项意为"踢"；D 项意为"拿着"。篮球投篮用"threw"，意为"投出"。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 副词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们现在领先一分。A 项意为"落后"；B 项意为"领先"；C 项意为"远离"；D 项意为"外面"。三分球进了，从落后两分变成领先一分，"ahead"正确。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（最高级）
**【解析】** 本句意为：这是我学校生活中目前为止最___的时刻。A 项意为"最悲伤的"；B 项意为"最安静的"；C 项意为"最自豪的"；D 项意为"最长的"。赢得冠军是最值得自豪的时刻，"proudest"正确。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：___不仅在于天赋，更在于团队合作和坚持。A 项意为"成功"；B 项意为"失败"；C 项意为"运气"；D 项意为"健康"。教练总结成功的秘诀，"success"正确。故选 A。
