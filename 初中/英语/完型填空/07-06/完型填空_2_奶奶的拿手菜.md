# 中考英语完形填空：奶奶的拿手菜

## 完形填空原文

My grandmother makes the best braised pork in the world. At least, that is what everyone in my family ___1___. Every time we visit her, she makes this dish for us. The moment we walk through the door, the wonderful smell fills the whole house.

Grandma learned to cook from her own mother when she was just a little girl. In those days, life was not ___2___ and food was simple. But Grandma's mother always tried her best to make meals tasty. Grandma often says, "Good food does not need ___3___ ingredients. It needs love and patience."

Last Sunday, I asked Grandma to teach me her secret ___4___. She smiled and took me into the kitchen. "There's no real secret," she said. "The key is to cook it slowly over low heat for a long time." She showed me each step ___5___. We chose the best piece of pork, cut it into small pieces, and added soy sauce, sugar, ginger, and a little cooking wine.

Then came the hardest part — waiting. The pork had to cook slowly for almost two ___6___. I kept wanting to open the lid and check, but Grandma stopped me. "If you keep opening it, the heat will escape and the meat won't be ___7___ enough," she explained. So I sat in the living room and watched TV, though I kept looking at the kitchen clock.

Finally, the dish was ready. The pork was soft, shiny, and ___8___ coloured. I took a bite and closed my eyes. It was perfect. "You see," Grandma said, "the best things in life cannot be ___9___."

I wrote down every step in my notebook. One day, when I grow up, I want to make this dish for my own family and ___10___ this tradition to the next generation.

## 选项

1. A. doubts &emsp;&emsp; B. forgets &emsp;&emsp; C. believes &emsp;&emsp; D. denies
2. A. hard &emsp;&emsp; B. easy &emsp;&emsp; C. short &emsp;&emsp; D. long
3. A. expensive &emsp;&emsp; B. fresh &emsp;&emsp; C. foreign &emsp;&emsp; D. sweet
4. A. plan &emsp;&emsp; B. recipe &emsp;&emsp; C. story &emsp;&emsp; D. rule
5. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. secretly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
6. A. days &emsp;&emsp; B. hours &emsp;&emsp; C. minutes &emsp;&emsp; D. seconds
7. A. hot &emsp;&emsp; B. cold &emsp;&emsp; C. tender &emsp;&emsp; D. dry
8. A. lightly &emsp;&emsp; B. badly &emsp;&emsp; C. strangely &emsp;&emsp; D. beautifully
9. A. bought &emsp;&emsp; B. rushed &emsp;&emsp; C. copied &emsp;&emsp; D. wasted
10. A. explain &emsp;&emsp; B. return &emsp;&emsp; C. pass &emsp;&emsp; D. lend

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：至少，这是我们家每个人___的。A 项意为"怀疑"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"相信"；D 项意为"否认"。全家人都认为奶奶做的红烧肉最好吃，"believes"正确。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：在那个年代，生活并不___。A 项意为"困难的"；B 项意为"容易的"；C 项意为"短暂的"；D 项意为"漫长的"。过去生活不容易，"life was not easy"符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：好的食物不需要___的食材，需要的是爱和耐心。A 项意为"昂贵的"；B 项意为"新鲜的"；C 项意为"外国的"；D 项意为"甜的"。强调食材不需要多贵重，关键在于用心，"expensive"正确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我请奶奶教我她的秘密___。A 项意为"计划"；B 项意为"食谱"；C 项意为"故事"；D 项意为"规则"。学做菜是学食谱/做法，"recipe"正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她___地给我展示每一个步骤。A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"秘密地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"紧张地"。教做菜一步步仔细演示，"carefully"正确。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析（时间单位）
**【解析】** 本句意为：猪肉要慢慢炖将近两___。A 项意为"天"；B 项意为"小时"；C 项意为"分钟"；D 项意为"秒"。红烧肉需要小火慢炖约两小时，"hours"正确。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：如果一直掀盖子，热量散失，肉就不够___。A 项意为"热的"；B 项意为"冷的"；C 项意为"嫩的"；D 项意为"干的"。红烧肉要炖到肉软嫩，"tender"正确。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：猪肉柔软、有光泽、颜色___。A 项意为"浅浅地"；B 项意为"糟糕地"；C 项意为"奇怪地"；D 项意为"漂亮地"。红烧肉色泽诱人，"beautifully coloured"意为"颜色漂亮"。故选 D。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：生活中最好的东西不能被___。A 项意为"买到"；B 项意为"匆忙赶出"；C 项意为"复制"；D 项意为"浪费"。好东西需要慢慢等待，不能急躁赶工，"rushed"意为"仓促完成"。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：我想把这个传统___给下一代。A 项意为"解释"；B 项意为"返回"；C 项意为"传递"；D 项意为"借出"。"pass...to the next generation"意为"传给下一代"，是常见搭配。故选 C。
