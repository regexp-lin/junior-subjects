# 中考英语完形填空：旧相机的故事

## 完形填空原文

When Grandpa moved to a nursing home last spring, he gave me an old film camera. "Take good care of it," he said. "It has ___1___ more of the world than most people ever will."

The camera was heavy and scratched. It didn't look special at all. In a world of smartphones, nobody used film cameras anymore. I almost put it in a drawer and ___2___ about it.

But one Sunday afternoon, I was bored and decided to try it out. I loaded a roll of film — I had to watch an online video to learn ___3___ — and went to the park near my house.

Using the old camera was completely ___4___ from using a phone. I couldn't see the picture right away. I had only 36 shots on the roll, so I couldn't waste any. Before pressing the button, I had to think ___5___: Was this scene worth keeping?

I spent the whole afternoon taking just twelve photos. I photographed an old man feeding pigeons, a child chasing bubbles, and the golden sunlight falling ___6___ the leaves. Each time I pressed the button, I felt excited — like I was catching a moment that would never come back.

A week later, I picked up the developed photos from the shop. I spread them on my desk and was ___7___. They were not perfect — some were too dark, some were blurry — but every single one told a story. They felt more ___8___ than the hundreds of photos sitting in my phone.

I called Grandpa and told him about my experience. He laughed. "That camera taught me to slow down and really ___9___ at the world," he said. "Every photo cost money, so I had to choose carefully. And because I chose carefully, every photo became a treasure."

Now I take the old camera with me every weekend. My friends think I'm strange, but I don't mind. The camera has taught me that sometimes, having ___10___ makes you appreciate more.

## 选项

1. A. seen &emsp;&emsp; B. heard &emsp;&emsp; C. touched &emsp;&emsp; D. smelled
2. A. talked &emsp;&emsp; B. forgot &emsp;&emsp; C. cared &emsp;&emsp; D. worried
3. A. why &emsp;&emsp; B. how &emsp;&emsp; C. when &emsp;&emsp; D. where
4. A. different &emsp;&emsp; B. far &emsp;&emsp; C. safe &emsp;&emsp; D. free
5. A. quickly &emsp;&emsp; B. loudly &emsp;&emsp; C. carefully &emsp;&emsp; D. happily
6. A. behind &emsp;&emsp; B. through &emsp;&emsp; C. above &emsp;&emsp; D. under
7. A. bored &emsp;&emsp; B. angry &emsp;&emsp; C. amazed &emsp;&emsp; D. scared
8. A. real &emsp;&emsp; B. cheap &emsp;&emsp; C. funny &emsp;&emsp; D. common
9. A. point &emsp;&emsp; B. shout &emsp;&emsp; C. look &emsp;&emsp; D. laugh
10. A. more &emsp;&emsp; B. less &emsp;&emsp; C. enough &emsp;&emsp; D. nothing

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（现在完成时）
**【解析】** 本句意为："它比大多数人一辈子___的世界还要多。"A 项意为"看到"；B 项意为"听到"；C 项意为"触摸"；D 项意为"闻到"。相机是用来拍照记录世界的，"seen"（看到）最符合相机的功能。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我几乎把它放进抽屉里然后___了它。A 项意为"谈论"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"关心"；D 项意为"担忧"。在智能手机时代，旧胶片相机看起来没什么用，所以他差点把它塞进抽屉"忘掉"（forgot）。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 疑问词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我不得不看一个在线视频来学___装胶卷。A 项意为"为什么"；B 项意为"怎样"；C 项意为"何时"；D 项意为"哪里"。他不会用胶片相机，需要学习"怎么"（how）操作装胶卷。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析与固定搭配
**【解析】** 本句意为：使用旧相机和用手机完全___。A 项意为"不同的"；B 项意为"远的"；C 项意为"安全的"；D 项意为"免费的"。"be different from"（与……不同）是固定搭配。胶片相机不能即拍即看，与手机体验完全不同。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：按快门前，我必须___想：这个画面值得保留吗？A 项意为"快速地"；B 项意为"大声地"；C 项意为"仔细地"；D 项意为"开心地"。只有36张，不能浪费，所以每次按快门前都要"仔细"（carefully）考虑。故选 C。

**6. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：金色的阳光___树叶洒落。A 项意为"在……后面"；B 项意为"穿过"；C 项意为"在……上方"；D 项意为"在……下方"。阳光"穿过"（through）树叶的缝隙洒下来，是一个非常有画面感的场景。故选 B。

**7. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我把它们摊在桌上，感到很___。A 项意为"无聊的"；B 项意为"生气的"；C 项意为"惊叹的"；D 项意为"害怕的"。虽然照片不完美，但每一张都讲述一个故事，让他感到"惊叹"（amazed）。故选 C。

**8. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：它们感觉比手机里数百张照片更___。A 项意为"真实的"；B 项意为"便宜的"；C 项意为"有趣的"；D 项意为"普通的"。精心拍摄的少量照片比随手拍的海量照片更有"真实"（real）感，更有温度和故事。故选 A。

**9. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："那台相机教会我慢下来，真正___这个世界。"A 项意为"指"；B 项意为"喊"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"笑"。相机让人学会观察，"look at the world"（看这个世界）——用心去观察和发现美。故选 C。

**10. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 不定代词/副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：拥有更___反而让你更懂珍惜。A 项意为"更多"；B 项意为"更少"；C 项意为"足够"；D 项意为"没有"。胶卷只有36张，正因为机会"更少"（less），所以更加珍惜每一次按快门的机会，与全文"少即是多"的主题呼应。故选 B。
