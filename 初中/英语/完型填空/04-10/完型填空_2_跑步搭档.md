# 中考英语完形填空：跑步搭档

## 完形填空原文

I used to hate running. Every time our P.E. teacher asked us to run laps around the track, I would be the last one to ___1___. I was slow, out of breath, and embarrassed. Running felt like punishment.

Then, at the start of eighth grade, a new student named He Yang joined our class. He was tall, thin, and loved running. He ran every morning before school — rain or ___2___.

One day after P.E. class, He Yang came over to me. "Do you want to run with me tomorrow morning?" he asked. I thought he was ___3___. Why would the fastest runner in our class want to run with the slowest?

"I'm serious," he said, seeing my doubtful face. "Running is more fun with a ___4___."

The next morning, I dragged myself out of bed at six o'clock and met He Yang at the school gate. I expected him to sprint ahead and leave me behind. But ___5___, he matched my slow pace exactly.

"You don't have to wait for me," I said, feeling guilty.

"I'm not waiting," he said with a grin. "I'm running ___6___ you. There's a difference."

We ran together every morning after that. He Yang never complained about my ___7___. Instead, he taught me how to breathe properly, how to land on my feet the right way, and how to set small goals. "Don't think about the whole track," he said. "Just focus on reaching the next tree."

Slowly, I improved. One tree became two trees, then three. After two months, I could run a full lap without ___8___. After three months, I ran my first two laps without slowing down.

At the school sports day, I signed up for the 800-meter race — something I would never have ___9___ doing before. I didn't win. I came in fifth. But when I crossed the finish line, He Yang was there, cheering louder than anyone.

"You see?" he said, giving me a high five. "You were never slow. You just needed someone to run ___10___ you."

## 选项

1. A. start &emsp;&emsp; B. finish &emsp;&emsp; C. leave &emsp;&emsp; D. arrive
2. A. snow &emsp;&emsp; B. wind &emsp;&emsp; C. shine &emsp;&emsp; D. cloud
3. A. lying &emsp;&emsp; B. joking &emsp;&emsp; C. leaving &emsp;&emsp; D. dreaming
4. A. coach &emsp;&emsp; B. partner &emsp;&emsp; C. fan &emsp;&emsp; D. teacher
5. A. instead &emsp;&emsp; B. again &emsp;&emsp; C. also &emsp;&emsp; D. therefore
6. A. against &emsp;&emsp; B. behind &emsp;&emsp; C. without &emsp;&emsp; D. with
7. A. speed &emsp;&emsp; B. height &emsp;&emsp; C. weight &emsp;&emsp; D. grade
8. A. thinking &emsp;&emsp; B. stopping &emsp;&emsp; C. smiling &emsp;&emsp; D. talking
9. A. imagined &emsp;&emsp; B. finished &emsp;&emsp; C. avoided &emsp;&emsp; D. enjoyed
10. A. after &emsp;&emsp; B. past &emsp;&emsp; C. beside &emsp;&emsp; D. before

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我总是最后一个___的。A 项意为"开始"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"到达"。跑步时他是最慢的，所以总是最后一个"完成"（finish）跑圈。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：他每天早上跑步——无论下雨还是___。A 项意为"雪"；B 项意为"风"；C 项意为"晴天"；D 项意为"云"。"rain or shine"（无论风雨还是晴天）是固定表达，意为无论天气如何都坚持。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我觉得他在___。A 项意为"撒谎"；B 项意为"开玩笑"；C 项意为"离开"；D 项意为"做梦"。最快的跑者邀请最慢的一起跑，这太不可思议了，"我"觉得他在"开玩笑"（joking）。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："有个___跑步更有趣。"A 项意为"教练"；B 项意为"搭档"；C 项意为"粉丝"；D 项意为"老师"。一起跑步的人就是跑步"搭档"（partner），也呼应了文章标题。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我以为他会冲到前面把我甩在后面。但___，他完全配合我的慢节奏。A 项意为"相反"；B 项意为"再一次"；C 项意为"也"；D 项意为"因此"。预期与实际相反，用"instead"（相反）表示转折。故选 A。

**6. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："我不是在等你，"他笑着说。"我是在___你跑。这有区别。"A 项意为"对抗"；B 项意为"在……后面"；C 项意为"没有"；D 项意为"和……一起"。他强调的是陪伴而非等待，"run with you"（和你一起跑）体现了真正的伙伴关系。故选 D。

**7. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：贺阳从不抱怨我的___。A 项意为"速度"；B 项意为"身高"；C 项意为"体重"；D 项意为"成绩"。"我"跑得慢，但贺阳从不嫌弃"我"的"速度"（speed）慢。故选 A。

**8. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：两个月后，我可以跑完整整一圈而不___。A 项意为"思考"；B 项意为"停下来"；C 项意为"微笑"；D 项意为"说话"。以前跑不下来，现在可以一圈不"停"（stopping），这是很大的进步。故选 B。

**9. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我报名了800米——这是我以前从没___自己会做的事。A 项意为"想象"；B 项意为"完成"；C 项意为"避免"；D 项意为"享受"。以前那么讨厌跑步，根本"想象"（imagined）不到自己会报名参加800米比赛。故选 A。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 介词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为："你只是需要一个人跑在你___。"A 项意为"后面"；B 项意为"经过"；C 项意为"旁边"；D 项意为"前面"。贺阳一直陪在"我"身旁一起跑，不是在前面也不是在后面，而是在"旁边"（beside），代表并肩前行的友谊。故选 C。
