# 中考英语完形填空：毕业典礼上的花束

## 完形填空原文

It was our graduation day. The sun was shining brightly, and the school hall was filled with students in uniforms. We sat ___1___ with our families, waiting for the ceremony to begin. My heart was beating fast—I couldn't believe that three years had passed so quickly.

When my name was called, I walked onto the stage to receive my diploma. Suddenly, I saw my mother in the crowd. She was holding a ___2___ of flowers and waving at me. Her eyes were ___3___, but she was smiling. At that moment, I felt my own eyes become wet. I remembered all the times she had gotten up early to make breakfast, the nights she had sat beside me when I was ___4___, and the words of encouragement she had given me when I wanted to give up.

After the ceremony, I ran to my mother and hugged her tightly. "Thank you, Mom. I couldn't have done it without you," I said. She put the flowers in my hands and said, "I'm so ___5___ of you. These flowers are for your new beginning."

The bouquet had sunflowers, which always ___6___ toward the light. My mother said she chose them because she hoped I would always look for the bright side in life. I nodded and promised myself that I would ___7___ her words in my heart forever.

That afternoon, we took many photos together. My mother's smile in those photos is something I will always ___8___. The graduation bouquet didn't last long, but the love and memories it ___9___ will stay with me for the rest of my life. It was not just a gift of flowers—it was a gift of love and hope for the ___10___.

## 选项

1. A. closely &emsp;&emsp; B. together &emsp;&emsp; C. quietly &emsp;&emsp; D. nervously
2. A. piece &emsp;&emsp; B. bunch &emsp;&emsp; C. group &emsp;&emsp; D. set
3. A. dry &emsp;&emsp; B. bright &emsp;&emsp; C. red &emsp;&emsp; D. clear
4. A. studying &emsp;&emsp; B. sleeping &emsp;&emsp; C. ill &emsp;&emsp; D. tired
5. A. sure &emsp;&emsp; B. proud &emsp;&emsp; C. glad &emsp;&emsp; D. happy
6. A. turn &emsp;&emsp; B. point &emsp;&emsp; C. look &emsp;&emsp; D. face
7. A. put &emsp;&emsp; B. keep &emsp;&emsp; C. remember &emsp;&emsp; D. hold
8. A. miss &emsp;&emsp; B. forget &emsp;&emsp; C. treasure &emsp;&emsp; D. save
9. A. showed &emsp;&emsp; B. meant &emsp;&emsp; C. carried &emsp;&emsp; D. brought
10. A. past &emsp;&emsp; B. present &emsp;&emsp; C. future &emsp;&emsp; D. moment

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 副词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我们和家人们___坐着，等待典礼开始。A 项意为"紧密地"；B 项意为"一起"；C 项意为"安静地"；D 项意为"紧张地"。"sit nervously"指紧张地坐着，与后文"心跳很快"相呼应。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 量词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她捧着___花，向我挥手。A 项意为"一片、一块"；B 项意为"一束"；C 项意为"一群"；D 项意为"一套"。"a bunch of flowers"是固定搭配，指一束花。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：她的眼睛是___的，但她微笑着。A 项意为"干的"；B 项意为"明亮的"；C 项意为"红的"；D 项意为"清澈的"。后文"我自己的眼睛也湿润了"说明是感动落泪，"red eyes"指哭红的眼睛。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：我记得她在我___时整晚坐在我身边。A 项意为"学习"；B 项意为"睡觉"；C 项意为"生病"；D 项意为"疲倦"。"when I was tired"指疲倦时陪在身边，体现母亲的关怀与陪伴。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 形容词辨析（固定搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈说道："我真为你感到___。这些花是给你新的开始。"A 项意为"确定"；B 项意为"骄傲"；C 项意为"高兴"；D 项意为"快乐"。"be proud of"是固定搭配，意为"为……感到骄傲"。故选 B。

**6. 【答案】A**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：花束里有向日葵，它们总是___着光的方向。A 项意为"转向、朝向"；B 项意为"指向"；C 项意为"看"；D 项意为"面对"。"turn toward the light"描述向日葵向光生长的特点。故选 A。

**7. 【答案】D**
**【考点】** 动词辨析（动宾搭配）
**【解析】** 本句意为：我点头，暗下决心要将她的话永远___在心里。A 项意为"放"；B 项意为"保存、牢记"；C 项意为"记得"；D 项意为"铭记、牢记"。"hold her words in my heart"指把话铭记在心，与"keep"同义。故选 D。

**8. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：妈妈在照片中的笑容是我将永远___的。A 项意为"想念"；B 项意为"忘记"；C 项意为"珍视、珍藏"；D 项意为"保存"。"treasure"在此表示"珍视、视为宝贵"，比"remember"更有情感。故选 C。

**9. 【答案】B**
**【考点】** 动词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：毕业花束很快凋谢了，但它所___的爱与回忆将伴随我一生。A 项意为"展示"；B 项意为"意味着、代表"；C 项意为"承载"；D 项意为"带来"。"meant"指花束所代表的深意，与"love and memories"搭配自然。故选 B。

**10. 【答案】C**
**【考点】** 名词辨析
**【解析】** 本句意为：这不仅是花的礼物，更是对___的爱与希望的礼物。A 项意为"过去"；B 项意为"现在"；C 项意为"未来"；D 项意为"此刻"。毕业寓意新起点，"hope for the future"指对未来的希望，与前文"new beginning"呼应。故选 C。
