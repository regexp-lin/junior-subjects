# 同级比较全攻略——as...as 的正反操作与句型转换 (Equal Comparison)

> 日期：2026年08月03日 | 语法分类：句法结构 | 难度：中等

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 什么是同级比较？

同级比较表示"和……一样"，用 **as...as** 结构。它是比较级三大核心之一（原级/比较级/最高级），中考必考！

### 🎯 核心结构

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **as + 形容词/副词原级 + as** | 和……一样 | Tom is **as tall as** Jack. |
| **not as/so + 形容词/副词原级 + as** | 不如……/没有……那么 | Tom is **not as tall as** Jack. |

---

### ⚡ 三大核心用法

#### 用法一：as + 形容词 + as（两个 as 之间放原级！）

- She is **as beautiful as** her mother.（她和她妈妈一样漂亮）
- He runs **as fast as** his brother.（他跑得和他哥哥一样快）

#### 用法二：否定形式 not as/so...as = "不如"

- This book is **not as interesting as** that one.（这本书不如那本有趣）
- She doesn't sing **as/so well as** her sister.（她唱得不如她姐姐好）

#### 用法三：as...as 的"倍数"表达

- This river is **twice as wide as** that one.（这条河是那条的两倍宽）
- He earns **three times as much as** I do.（他赚的是我的三倍）

---

### 🔗 六大高频固定搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **as soon as possible** | 尽快 | Please reply **as soon as possible**. |
| **as...as possible** | 尽可能…… | Run **as fast as possible**. |
| **as...as + sb. + can** | 尽可能…… | Read **as many books as you can**. |
| **as well as** | 也；和……一样好 | She speaks French **as well as** English. |
| **as long as** | 只要 | You can go out **as long as** you finish homework. |
| **as far as** | 就……而言/远至 | **As far as** I know, he's honest. |

---

### 📍 句型转换（中考必考！）

| 原句 | 转换句 | 转换规则 |
|------|--------|----------|
| Tom is taller than Jack. | Jack is **not as/so tall as** Tom. | 比较级 → not as...as |
| Tom is not as tall as Jack. | Jack is **taller than** Tom. | not as...as → 比较级 |
| Tom is as tall as Jack. | Tom is **the same height as** Jack. | as...as → the same...as |

**核心转换公式**：
- A is 比较级 + than B = B is not as/so + 原级 + as A
- not as/so + 原级 + as = 比较级 + than（注意主语互换！）

---

### ⚠️ 易错警示

1. **as 和 as 之间只能放原级**：~~as taller as~~❌ → as tall as ✅
2. **否定可用 so 替换第一个 as**：not so tall as = not as tall as
3. **as...as 中间放形容词还是副词**：修饰名词用形容词，修饰动词用副词
4. **as many/much...as**：可数用 many，不可数用 much

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

1. This math problem is ________ that one.
   A. as difficult as    B. as more difficult as    C. so difficult as    D. as difficult than

2. She doesn't run ________ her brother.
   A. as faster as    B. as fast as    C. so fast than    D. as fast than

3. — Is Tom as ________ as Jack?
   — No, Tom is much shorter.
   A. taller    B. tallest    C. tall    D. more tall

4. Please finish the work ________.
   A. as quick as possible    B. as quickly as possible
   C. so quickly as possible    D. as quick as you can

5. This room is ________ that one. They are both 20 square meters.
   A. as big as    B. bigger than    C. not as big as    D. the biggest of

6. He has ________ books as I do.
   A. as much    B. as many    C. so much    D. many

7. This river is three times ________ that one.
   A. wider than    B. as wide as    C. the widest    D. so wide as

8. — Lucy speaks English well.
   — Lily speaks English ________ Lucy.
   A. as good as    B. as well as    C. better as    D. so well than

9. Jack is not ________ his father. He is much thinner.
   A. as strong as    B. stronger than    C. the strongest    D. so strong than

10. He tried to run as ________ as he could to catch the bus.
    A. fastly    B. faster    C. fast    D. fastest

11. The population of this city is not ________ that one.
    A. as large as    B. as many as    C. as much as    D. larger as

12. I'll come back ________.
    A. as soon as I will can    B. as soon as I can
    C. as soon as can    D. so soon as can I

13. ________ I know, she has gone abroad.
    A. As long as    B. As well as    C. As far as    D. As soon as

14. The red box is ________ the blue one.
    A. not so heavy as    B. not so heavier as
    C. not as heavy than    D. not so heavy than

15. You should read ________ English books ________ you can.
    A. as much; as    B. as many; as    C. so much; as    D. many; as

16. "He is as clever as his sister" means ________.
    A. He is cleverer than his sister.
    B. His sister is not so clever as he.
    C. He and his sister are both clever.
    D. His sister is cleverer than he.

17. Tom is taller than Jack. = Jack is ________ Tom.
    A. as tall as    B. not so tall as    C. taller than    D. the tallest of

18. She arrived ________ we expected.
    A. as early as    B. as earlier as    C. so early than    D. as earliest as

19. You can stay here ________ you keep quiet.
    A. as far as    B. as well as    C. as long as    D. as soon as

20. This coat costs ________ that one. They are the same price.
    A. twice as much as    B. as much as    C. not as much as    D. much more than

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (A)**
📌 as...as 中间放**原级** difficult。"这道数学题和那道一样难"。
❌ B 放了比较级 more difficult；D 用了 than（as...as 结构用 as 不用 than）。

---

**第2题 (B)**
📌 否定句中 not as fast as = "跑得不如……快"。两个 as 之间放**原级** fast。
❌ A 放了比较级 faster；C/D 用了 than，as...as 结构不搭配 than。

---

**第3题 (C)**
📌 as...as 中间只能用**原级** tall。疑问句中问"Tom 和 Jack 一样高吗？"
❌ A/D 是比较级；B 是最高级。as...as 永远接原级！

---

**第4题 (B)**
📌 finish 是动词，修饰动词用**副词** quickly。as quickly as possible = 尽可能快地完成。
❌ A quick 是形容词，修饰动词应用副词；D 用 as...as you can 结构，但 quick 不能修饰动词。

---

**第5题 (A)**
📌 "和……一样大"用 as big as。后面说"都是20平米"说明一样大。
❌ B 表示"更大"，与"一样"矛盾；C 表示"不如"，也矛盾。

---

**第6题 (B)**
📌 books 是可数名词复数，用 **as many...as**。不可数名词才用 much。
❌ A/C 用了 much，books 是可数名词。

---

**第7题 (B)**
📌 倍数表达：三倍用 **three times as wide as**。倍数放在 as...as 前面。
❌ A 虽然表达倍数也可以用比较级，但结构应该是 three times wider than，选项 A 不符合。

---

**第8题 (B)**
📌 speak 是动词，修饰动词用**副词** well。as well as = "和……一样好"。
❌ A good 是形容词，不能修饰动词 speaks。

---

**第9题 (A)**
📌 not as strong as = "不如……壮"。Jack 比父亲瘦，所以"不如父亲强壮"。
❌ D 用了 than，so...than 不是正确搭配，应该是 so...as 或 as...as。

---

**第10题 (C)**
📌 as...as he could 中间用**原级** fast。注意 fast 本身就是副词（不需要加 -ly）！
❌ A fastly 是错误形式（fast 的副词就是 fast）；B 是比较级。

---

**第11题 (A)**
📌 population（人口）用 large/small 形容，不用 many/much。"人口不如那个城市多"。
❌ B/C 用了 many/much 搭配 population，不地道。

---

**第12题 (B)**
📌 as soon as I can = "尽快"。as soon as 后面跟主语 + 情态动词。
❌ A will can 语法错误（两个情态动词不能连用）。

---

**第13题 (C)**
📌 as far as I know = "据我所知"，是固定搭配。
❌ A as long as = 只要；B as well as = 也/和；D as soon as = 一……就。

---

**第14题 (A)**
📌 not so heavy as = not as heavy as = "没有……那么重"。否定形式第一个 as 可以换成 so。
❌ B/C/D 都是错误搭配——比较级 heavier 不能放在 as...as 中；than 不能和 as 搭配。

---

**第15题 (B)**
📌 books 是可数名词，用 **as many...as** you can。"尽可能多读英语书"。
❌ A/C 用了 much，不能修饰可数名词复数。

---

**第16题 (C)**
📌 as clever as = "和……一样聪明"，意思是**两个人都聪明**且程度相同。
❌ A/D 表示一个比另一个聪明；B 表示姐姐不如他聪明——都不是"一样聪明"的意思。

---

**第17题 (B)**
📌 句型转换！Tom is taller than Jack = Jack is **not so/as tall as** Tom。比较级转原级否定，注意**主语互换**。
❌ A 表示一样高，与原句"更高"矛盾；C/D 意思和原句不等价。

---

**第18题 (A)**
📌 as early as = "和……一样早"。两个 as 之间用**原级** early。
❌ B earlier 是比较级，不能放在 as...as 中。

---

**第19题 (C)**
📌 as long as = "只要"。"只要你保持安静就可以待在这里"。
❌ A as far as 据……所知；B as well as 也；D as soon as 一……就。语义不符。

---

**第20题 (B)**
📌 后面说"价格一样"，所以用 as much as = "和……花一样多的钱"。
❌ A 两倍价格，与"一样"矛盾；C/D 表示不如或更多，也矛盾。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：as...as 的核心是**两个 as 之间只放原级**！否定用 not as/so...as，句型转换时记住**主语互换**。六大固定搭配（as soon as possible、as long as 等）是中考高频考点，必须背熟！💪
