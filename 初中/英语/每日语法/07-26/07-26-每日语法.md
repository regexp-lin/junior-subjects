## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

**第 1–10 题：基础巩固**

1. My sister is good ______ drawing. She won the art competition last month.
   - A. at   B. in   C. for   D. with

2. The students are looking forward ______ the summer holiday.
   - A. at   B. to   C. for   D. in

3. Don't worry ______ your exam. You've prepared well.
   - A. about   B. for   C. on   D. with

4. We should never ______ our dreams no matter how difficult it is.
   - A. give up   B. give in   C. give away   D. give back

5. The meeting will ______ next Monday. Please be there on time.
   - A. take place   B. take part   C. take care   D. take turns

6. I haven't ______ my pen pal for two years. I miss her so much.
   - A. heard from   B. heard of   C. listened to   D. depended on

7. The desk is made ______ wood. It looks very strong.
   - A. from   B. of   C. in   D. by

8. I have difficulty ______ the long article. Can you help me?
   - A. understand   B. understanding   C. to understand   D. understood

9. Jack didn't ______ at the party. Maybe he was too busy.
   - A. show up   B. pick up   C. set up   D. work out

10. By the ______, have you finished your homework?
    - A. road   B. way   C. time   D. fact

**第 11–20 题：能力提升**

11. Our success depends ______ how hard we work.
    - A. in   B. on   C. at   D. with

12. The bottle is full ______ water. You can drink it.
    - A. with   B. of   C. in   D. for

13. He arrived ______ Beijing Railway Station at 10 o'clock.
    - A. at   B. in   C. to   D. on

14. I'd like to have noodles ______ rice for lunch today.
    - A. instead of   B. instead   C. because of   D. as a result

15. Everyone makes ______. What matters is learning from them.
    - A. a decision   B. a mistake   C. progress   D. friends

16. The teacher is strict ______ us. But we all respect her.
    - A. to   B. for   C. with   D. at

17. We need to ______ the problem before it gets worse.
    - A. work out   B. run out   C. hand in   D. try on

18. I'm used to ______ early. It makes me feel energetic.
    - A. get up   B. getting up   C. got up   D. gets up

19. After ______, he is still our friend. We should forgive him.
    - A. all   B. that   C. this   D. it

20. Make ______ you turn off the lights when you leave.
    - A. sure   B. clear   C. up   D. out

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第 1 题** ✅ **A. at**  
**易错选项**：B. in  
**解析**：固定搭配 **be good at**（擅长）。后面跟名词或动名词，如 be good at English / swimming。❌ 没有 be good in 这种说法哦！

---

**第 2 题** ✅ **B. to**  
**易错选项**：C. for  
**解析**：**look forward to** 中的 to 是介词，后面必须接 **动名词 doing**！如 look forward to seeing you。很多同学会选 for，但英语里没有 look forward for，记牢：**to 是介词，不是不定式**！

---

**第 3 题** ✅ **A. about**  
**易错选项**：D. with  
**解析**：**worry about** = 担心某事/某人。worry 只和 about 搭配，没有 worry with。with 常和 agree、strict 等连用。

---

**第 4 题** ✅ **A. give up**  
**易错选项**：B. give in  
**解析**：**give up** = 放弃（梦想、习惯等）；**give in** = 屈服、让步。题干说 "never give up your dreams"，显然选 give up。其他：give away 赠送；give back 归还。

---

**第 5 题** ✅ **A. take place**  
**易错选项**：B. take part  
**解析**：**take place** = 发生（无被动语态，主语常是事件）；**take part in** = 参加。会议"发生"用 take place，人"参加"用 take part in。⚠️ take place 不能用被动！

---

**第 6 题** ✅ **A. heard from**  
**易错选项**：B. heard of  
**解析**：**hear from sb** = 收到某人的来信/消息；**hear of** = 听说过某人/某事。两年没"收到信"用 hear from；"听说过这个歌手"用 hear of。别搞混！

---

**第 7 题** ✅ **B. of**  
**易错选项**：A. from  
**解析**：**be made of** = 看得出原材料（木头→桌子）；**be made from** = 看不出原材料（木头→纸）。桌子能看出是木头做的，选 of！一个小技巧：能看出来用 of，看不出来用 from。

---

**第 8 题** ✅ **B. understanding**  
**易错选项**：A. understand  
**解析**：**have difficulty/trouble (in) doing sth** 是固定句型，in 可省略，但后面**必须是动名词**！同理：have fun doing, spend time doing。

---

**第 9 题** ✅ **A. show up**  
**易错选项**：B. pick up  
**解析**：**show up** = 出现、到场；**pick up** = 接人、捡起、学会。杰克没"出现"在派对，选 show up。pick up 是"去接人"，不是"出现"。

---

**第 10 题** ✅ **B. way**  
**易错选项**：C. time  
**解析**：**by the way** = 顺便说一下，是固定短语，way 不能换成 road、time 等。记口诀：**By the way，顺便提一嘴**！

---

**第 11 题** ✅ **B. on**  
**易错选项**：A. in  
**解析**：**depend on** = 依靠、取决于。depend 只和 on 搭配，没有 depend in。Success depends on hard work. 成功取决于努力。

---

**第 12 题** ✅ **B. of**  
**易错选项**：A. with  
**解析**：**be full of** = 充满。虽然中文说"装满"，但英语用 full **of**，不用 full with。相反，**fill with** 才是正确的：fill the cup with water。

---

**第 13 题** ✅ **A. at**  
**易错选项**：B. in  
**解析**：**arrive at** = 小地方（车站、机场、学校等）；**arrive in** = 大地方（城市、国家）。Beijing Railway Station 是具体地点，用 at！in 用于 Beijing（城市）。

---

**第 14 题** ✅ **A. instead of**  
**易错选项**：D. as a result  
**解析**：**instead of** = 而不是（表示替代）；**as a result** = 因此（表示结果）。句意是"吃面条而不是米饭"，表示选择替代，用 instead of。instead of 是介词短语，后接名词、代词或动名词。

---

**第 15 题** ✅ **B. a mistake**  
**易错选项**：C. progress  
**解析**：**make a mistake** = 犯错误；**make progress** = 取得进步。题干的 "learning from them" 暗示是"从错误中学习"，所以选 make a mistake。而且 make a mistake 常用复数形式 mistakes：Everyone makes mistakes.

---

**第 16 题** ✅ **C. with**  
**易错选项**：A. to  
**解析**：**be strict with sb** = 对某人严格。strict 只和 with 搭配！be strict with 对人，be strict about/in 对事：Our teacher is strict with us and strict about homework.

---

**第 17 题** ✅ **A. work out**  
**易错选项**：C. hand in  
**解析**：**work out** = 解决（问题）、算出、锻炼；**hand in** = 上交。我们需要"解决"问题，选 work out。hand in 是交作业、交卷子。

---

**第 18 题** ✅ **B. getting up**  
**易错选项**：A. get up  
**解析**：**be used to doing** = 习惯于做某事（to 是介词）；**used to do** = 过去常常做某事（已不做了）。我"习惯于"早起，to 后面必须接动名词 getting up！很多同学会混淆 used to do 和 be used to doing，切记：**be used to + doing**！

---

**第 19 题** ✅ **A. all**  
**易错选项**：B. that  
**解析**：**after all** = 毕竟、终究，是固定搭配。常用来表示"尽管……但毕竟……"的让步语气。Don't blame him—after all, he's just a child. 别怪他，毕竟他只是个孩子。

---

**第 20 题** ✅ **A. sure**  
**易错选项**：B. clear  
**解析**：**make sure (that)** = 确保，后接从句。Make sure you turn off the lights. 确保你关灯。make clear 也可以表示"澄清、说明"，但 make sure 才是"确保"的意思，用法不同。

---

💡 **今日小结**：固定搭配没有捷径，**多读、多背、多用**才是王道！做完形填空时，看到空格前后的关键词，马上联想对应的搭配，准确率会大大提升！加油，中考必胜！🎉
