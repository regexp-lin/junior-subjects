# 考场交际达人必备——Situational Communication（情景交际用语）

> 日期：2026年07月25日 | 语法分类：交际用语 | 难度：中等

---

## 一、知识点精讲

交际用语是中考**高频考点**，几乎年年必考！🎯 它考察的是在**真实情景**中该说什么、怎么应答。就像玩游戏要背技能表一样，把这些"台词"背熟，考场上就能秒杀！

### 📞 1. 打电话用语 (Making a Phone Call)

| 中文意思 | 英文表达 |
|---------|---------|
| 我找……可以吗？ | **May I speak to...?** / Is ... there? |
| 我是…… | **This is... speaking.** / This is ... (这里不能说 I am ...!) |
| 请稍等 | **Hold on, please.** / Hold the line, please. |
| 抱歉，他/她不在 | **I'm sorry, he/she is not in.** |
| 要我留个口信吗？ | Can I take a message? |
| 我就是 | **Speaking.** |

💡 **易错提醒**：打电话说"我是小明"要用 **This is Xiao Ming speaking**，绝不说 I am Xiao Ming！

---

### 🏥 2. 看病用语 (Seeing a Doctor)

| 中文意思 | 英文表达 |
|---------|---------|
| 你怎么了？ | **What's wrong with you?** / What's the matter with you? |
| 我头疼/肚子疼 | **I have a headache/stomachache.** |
| 你最好…… | **You'd better...** |
| 一天吃三次药 | **Take this medicine three times a day.** |
| 量体温 | take one's temperature |
| 多休息 | have a good rest |

---

### 🗺️ 3. 问路用语 (Asking the Way)

| 中文意思 | 英文表达 |
|---------|---------|
| 打扰一下，怎么去……？ | **Excuse me, how can I get to...?** |
| 沿着……走，在……左/右转 | **Go along... and turn left/right at...** |
| 步行大约……分钟 | **It's about... minutes' walk.** |
| 在……和……之间 | between... and... |
| 在……拐角处 | at the corner of... |

---

### 🛒 4. 购物用语 (Shopping)

| 中文意思 | 英文表达 |
|---------|---------|
| 需要帮忙吗？ | **Can I help you?** / May I help you? |
| 我想买…… | **I'd like...** / I want to buy... |
| 什么尺寸/颜色？ | **What size/color...?** |
| 多少钱？ | **How much is it?** |
| 我买了 | **I'll take it.** |

---

### 🎉 5. 邀请与建议 (Invitation & Suggestion)

| 类型 | 英文表达 |
|-----|---------|
| 邀请 | **Would you like to...?** (委婉客气) |
| 建议 | **Why not...?** / Why don't you...? |
| 建议 | **What/How about...?** (+ 动名词/名词) |
| 建议 | **Let's...** (咱们一起……) |
| 建议 | **You'd better...** (你最好……) |
| 建议 | **Shall we...?** (咱们……好吗？) |

📌 **重点**：**Would you like** 后接 **to do**；**What about / How about** 后接 **doing** 或名词！

---

### 😊 6. 应答套路 (Responses)

| 对方说 | 你应答 |
|-------|-------|
| Thanks. / Thank you. | **You're welcome.** / Not at all. / My pleasure. |
| Sorry. | **That's OK.** / Never mind. / It doesn't matter. |
| 赞美 (You're great!) | **Thank you.** (谦虚接受) |

---

### 📖 对话示例

**A: 打电话**

> **A:** Hello! May I speak to Tom?  
> **B:** Hold on, please. ... Sorry, he is not in. Can I take a message?  
> **A:** Yes. This is Mike speaking. Please tell him to call me back.

**B: 看病**

> **Doctor:** What's wrong with you?  
> **Patient:** I have a headache and a fever.  
> **Doctor:** You'd better take this medicine three times a day and have a good rest.

**C: 问路**

> **A:** Excuse me, how can I get to the post office?  
> **B:** Go along this street and turn right at the second crossing. It's about five minutes' walk.

**D: 购物**

> **Shop assistant:** Can I help you?  
> **Customer:** I'd like a T-shirt.  
> **Shop assistant:** What size do you wear?  
> **Customer:** Size M. How much is it?  
> **Shop assistant:** 88 yuan.  
> **Customer:** I'll take it.

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

1. — Hello! May I speak to Lucy?  
   — ________  
   A. Yes, I am.  
   B. Hold on, please.  
   C. Who are you?  
   D. I'm Lucy.

2. — Thank you for helping me with my English.  
   — ________  
   A. You're welcome.  
   B. Don't say that.  
   C. I'm sorry.  
   D. It's a pity.

3. — What's wrong with you? You look pale.  
   — ________  
   A. I'm fine.  
   B. I have a stomachache.  
   C. You're welcome.  
   D. Never mind.

4. — Excuse me, ________ the nearest bank?  
   — Go along this road and turn left at the second crossing.  
   A. how can I get to  
   B. where is  
   C. which is the way to  
   D. A and C are both OK

5. — Would you like ________ to the cinema with me?  
   — Sure, I'd love to.  
   A. go  
   B. to go  
   C. going  
   D. goes

6. — I'm sorry I'm late.  
   — ________  
   A. You're welcome.  
   B. That's OK.  
   C. Thank you.  
   D. Good idea.

7. — Can I help you?  
   — ________  
   A. Yes, I'd like a pair of shoes.  
   B. No, you can't.  
   C. I'm fine.  
   D. You're right.

8. — Why not ________ swimming this weekend?  
   — Good idea!  
   A. go  
   B. to go  
   C. going  
   D. goes

9. — Hello, may I speak to Mr. Wang?  
   — ________  
   A. Yes, I'm Mr. Wang.  
   B. This is Mr. Wang speaking.  
   C. I'm Mr. Wang.  
   D. Mr. Wang is me.

10. — How much ________ the jacket?  
    — 199 yuan.  
    A. is  
    B. are  
    C. do  
    D. does

11. — What about ________ a picnic in the park?  
    — That sounds great!  
    A. have  
    B. to have  
    C. having  
    D. has

12. — You'd better ________ too much junk food.  
    — OK, I will.  
    A. not eat  
    B. not to eat  
    C. don't eat  
    D. to not eat

13. — Shall we ________ football after school?  
    — Yes, let's.  
    A. play  
    B. to play  
    C. playing  
    D. played

14. — Excuse me, is there a bookstore near here?  
    — Yes. It's about ________ walk.  
    A. five minute  
    B. five minutes

15. — ________ — Not at all.  
    A. Thank you very much.  
    B. I'm sorry.  
    C. Excuse me.

16. — I have a bad cold. What should I do?  
    — You'd better ________ this medicine and ________ in bed.

    — ________  
    A. I'd like the blue one.  
    B. Size M, please.  
    C. It's 50 yuan.

18. — Let's go out for dinner, ________?  
    — Good idea!  
    A. will you  
    B. shall we  
    C. don't we  
    D. do we

19. — I'm sorry, Tom is not in. Can I take a message?  
    — ________. I'll call back later.  
    A. Yes, please  
    B. No, thanks  
    C. That's OK  
    D. You're welcome

20. — ________? — I'd like to buy a birthday gift for my sister.

    B. Can I help you  
    C. How much is it

## 三、答案与详解

|----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| C  | A  | A  | C  | A  | A  | B  | B  | B  | B  |

### 错因深度解析

**第1题** 选 **B** ✅  
打电话找人时，接电话的人若需要转接，会说 **Hold on, please**（请稍等）。A 答非所问；C 不礼貌；D 若接电话的是 Lucy 本人，应说 **This is Lucy speaking** 或 **Speaking**。

**第2题** 选 **A** ✅  
对方说 **Thank you**，标准应答是 **You're welcome**（不客气）。**Not at all** 也可以。B、C、D 均不符合感谢应答场景。

**第3题** 选 **B** ✅  
医生问 **What's wrong with you?**（你怎么了？），病人应描述症状，如 **I have a stomachache**（我肚子疼）。A 与"脸色苍白"矛盾；C、D 是道歉/感谢的应答。

**第4题** 选 **D** ✅  
**How can I get to...?** 和 **Which is the way to...?** 都表示"怎么去……"，都可用来问路。B 语法上虽可，但习惯上用 **how can I get to** 更常见。答案 D 说明 A、C 都对。

**第5题** 选 **B** ✅  
**Would you like** 后必须接 **to do**，所以选 **to go**。🎯 牢记：**Would you like to do** ≠ **What about doing**！

**第6题** 选 **B** ✅  
对方道歉 **I'm sorry**，应答用 **That's OK** / **Never mind** / **It doesn't matter**。A 是感谢的应答；C、D 不适用。

**第7题** 选 **A** ✅  
店员问 **Can I help you?**，顾客应说明需求，如 **I'd like a pair of shoes**。B 不礼貌；C、D 答非所问。

**第8题** 选 **A** ✅  
**Why not** 后接**动词原形**：Why not **go** swimming? 同理：**Why don't you go** swimming? 注意不用 to！

**第9题** 选 **B** ✅  
打电话时自报家门用 **This is ... speaking**，不能说 I am Mr. Wang。这是中考**必背**句型！📞

**第10题** 选 **A** ✅  
**How much is the jacket?**（这件夹克多少钱？）主语 the jacket 是单数，用 is。价格用 **How much** 提问。

**第11题** 选 **C** ✅  
**What about / How about** 后接 **动名词** 或名词：What about **having** a picnic? 不能接 to do 或动词原形！

**第12题** 选 **A** ✅  
**You'd better not do**（你最好别做……），not 直接加动词原形。不能用 not to eat 或 don't eat。

**第13题** 选 **A** ✅  
**Shall we** 后接**动词原形**：Shall we **play** football? 表示"咱们……好吗？"

**第14题** 选 **C** ✅  
"五分钟的步行"用 **five minutes' walk**（复数分钟 + 所有格 's）。B 缺所有格；A 单复数错误；D 若用 five-minute，需作定语：a five-minute walk，但这里 about 后接的是名词短语，常用 five minutes' walk。

**第15题** 选 **A** ✅  
**Not at all** 是对 **Thank you** 的应答，意为"不客气"。所以上一句应是感谢的话。选 A。

**第16题** 选 **A** ✅  
**You'd better do** 和 **You'd better stay**，两个都是动词原形。**had better** 后始终接动词原形。

**第17题** 选 **B** ✅  
问 **What size?**（什么尺寸？），答 Size M。A 是颜色；C 是价格；D 是决定购买，都不对。

**第18题** 选 **B** ✅  
**Let's...** 开头的反义疑问句，附加问句用 **shall we**？表示邀请对方一起做。这是固定搭配！

**第19题** 选 **B** ✅  
对方问 **Can I take a message?**（要留口信吗？），若不留口信、打算稍后再打，应说 **No, thanks**。A 是同意留口信。

**第20题** 选 **B** ✅  
顾客进店，店员常说 **Can I help you?**（需要帮忙吗？）。答句 **I'd like to buy...** 是典型购物用语，说明上一句是店员招呼语。

---

💪 **今日小结**：交际用语靠"背台词"！打电话、看病、问路、购物、邀请建议、感谢道歉……把每种场景的**高频句**记牢，考场不慌！加油！🚀
