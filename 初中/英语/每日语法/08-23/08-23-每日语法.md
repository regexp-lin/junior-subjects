# 📦 动词短语的拆装艺术——可分/不可分动词短语 (Separable vs Inseparable Phrasal Verbs)

> 日期：2026年08月23日 | 语法分类：动词短语 | 难度：中等

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 什么是动词短语（Phrasal Verb）？

**动词短语**由"**动词 + 副词/介词**"构成，表达一个完整的意义，往往不同于动词和介词各自的本义。例如：turn on（打开）、look after（照料）、put off（推迟）。动词短语是中考高频考点，其中**可分**与**不可分**的区分直接影响宾语的位置，是同学们最容易出错的地方。

---

### 🎯 可分动词短语（Separable Phrasal Verbs）

**可分动词短语**的宾语可以插在**动词和副词之间**，也可以放在短语后面。但当宾语是**代词**（it, them, him 等）时，**代词必须放在中间**，不能放在后面。

| 动词短语 | 名词宾语位置 | 代词宾语位置 | 例句 |
|----------|--------------|--------------|------|
| turn on | turn on the light / turn the light on | turn **it** on ✓ | Turn it on, please. |
| turn off | turn off the TV / turn the TV off | turn **it** off ✓ | Don't forget to turn it off. |
| put on | put on your coat / put your coat on | put **it** on ✓ | Put it on before you go out. |
| take off | take off your shoes / take your shoes off | take **them** off ✓ | Take them off at the door. |
| pick up | pick up the book / pick the book up | pick **it** up ✓ | I'll pick it up tomorrow. |
| put away | put away the toys / put the toys away | put **them** away ✓ | Put them away now. |
| wake up | wake up the child / wake the child up | wake **him** up ✓ | Don't wake him up. |
| throw away | throw away the trash / throw the trash away | throw **it** away ✓ | Just throw it away. |

**核心规则**：代词作宾语时，**必须放在动词和副词之间**。✗ turn on it → ✓ turn it on

---

### ⚠️ 错误 vs 正确对照表

| 错误 | 正确 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| turn on it | turn **it** on | 代词必须插中间 |
| put off them | put **them** off | 同上 |
| pick up it | pick **it** up | 同上 |
| take off it | take **it** off | 同上 |
| turn off them | turn **them** off | 同上 |

---

### 🎯 不可分动词短语（Inseparable Phrasal Verbs）

**不可分动词短语**的宾语**只能放在整个短语后面**，不能插在中间。无论名词还是代词，一律后置。

| 动词短语 | 宾语位置 | 例句 | 错误示例 |
|----------|----------|------|----------|
| look after | 只能后置 | look after the baby / look after him | look him after ✗ |
| look for | 只能后置 | look for my keys / look for them | look them for ✗ |
| listen to | 只能后置 | listen to music / listen to it | listen it to ✗ |
| arrive at | 只能后置 | arrive at the station | arrive the station at ✗ |
| wait for | 只能后置 | wait for the bus / wait for it | wait it for ✗ |
| depend on | 只能后置 | depend on your help | depend your help on ✗ |
| look at | 只能后置 | look at the picture / look at it | look it at ✗ |
| belong to | 只能后置 | belong to me | belong me to ✗ |

**记忆要点**：这类短语中的第二个词多为**介词**（to, for, at, on, after 等），介词后面必须紧跟宾语，所以不能拆分。

---

### 📋 如何判断可分还是不可分？

| 类型 | 第二个词 | 宾语位置 | 典型例词 |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| **可分** | 副词（up, down, on, off, away） | 可插中间；代词必插中间 | turn on, put on, pick up, take off |
| **不可分** | 介词（to, for, at, after, on） | 只能放后面 | look after, look for, listen to, wait for |

**简单判断**：若第二个词是 **up, off, on, down, away** 等副词，多为可分；若是 **to, for, at, after** 等介词，多为不可分。但需注意：**on, off** 作副词时可分，作介词时语义不同（如 rely on 不可分）。

---

### 📋 易错点速查表

| 易错点 | 正确 | 错误 |
|--------|------|------|
| 可分+代词 | turn it on, pick it up | turn on it, pick up it ✗ |
| 不可分+代词 | look after him, wait for it | look him after, wait it for ✗ |
| 混淆可分/不可分 | put it on (可分) | put on it ✗ |
| 混淆可分/不可分 | look for it (不可分) | look it for ✗ |

---

### 📋 常考动词短语分类汇总

| 可分（代词插中间） | 不可分（宾语只后置） |
|--------------------|----------------------|
| turn on/off | look after |
| put on | look for |
| take off | listen to |
| pick up | wait for |
| put away | arrive at |
| wake up | depend on |
| throw away | look at |
| turn down/up | belong to |
| give up (特殊：宾语可插中间) | take care of |

**give up**：可说 give up smoking 或 give it up，代词插中间。

❌ 易错点：选 A turn off it 违反"代词插中间"规则；选 C/D 缺宾语。

🎯 **今日总结**：**可分动词短语**（turn on, put on, pick up, take off 等）中，**代词作宾语必须插在动词与副词之间**（turn it on ✓, turn on it ✗）。**不可分动词短语**（look after, look for, listen to, wait for 等）中，**宾语只能放在短语后面**（look after him ✓, look him after ✗）。先判断短语可分还是不可分，再决定宾语位置！💪
