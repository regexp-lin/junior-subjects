# 强调句 It is/was...that/who 大揭秘 (Cleft Sentences: It is...that)

> 日期：2026年08月11日 | 语法分类：特殊句型 | 难度：较难

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 什么是强调句？

强调句（Cleft Sentence）用来**突出句子中的某一成分**，基本结构：

**It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who/whom + 其余部分**

| 强调对象 | 连接词 | 例句 |
|----------|--------|------|
| 人（主语/宾语） | that / who / whom | It **was Tom that/who** broke the window. |
| 物、时间、地点等 | that | It **was in 2020 that** I met him. |

---

### 🎯 强调句的"还原法"

**口诀**：去掉 It is/was 和 that/who，剩余部分能还原成完整句，就是强调句！

| 强调句 | 还原 | 强调部分 |
|--------|------|----------|
| It was **Tom** that I met yesterday. | I met Tom yesterday. | 宾语 Tom |
| It was **yesterday** that I met Tom. | I met Tom yesterday. | 时间状语 |
| It was **in the library** that I met Tom. | I met Tom in the library. | 地点状语 |

---

### 🎯 强调不同成分

| 原句 | 强调主语 | 强调宾语 | 强调时间状语 |
|------|----------|----------|--------------|
| Tom broke the window. | It **was Tom that/who** broke the window. | — | — |
| I met him in 2020. | It **was I that/who** met him in 2020. | It **was him that** I met in 2020. | It **was in 2020 that** I met him. |

**注意**：强调主语时，若被强调部分是"我"，用 It was **I** that...（正式）或 It was **me** that...（口语）。

---

### ⚡ 强调句的疑问形式

| 类型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般疑问 | Is/Was it + 被强调部分 + that...? | **Was it** Tom **that** broke the window? |
| 特殊疑问 | 疑问词 + is/was it + that...? | **Who was it that** broke the window? |

---

### 📍 强调句 vs 其他 It 句型（必考易混！）

| 句型 | 判断方法 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| **强调句** | 去掉 It is/was...that 可还原为完整句 | It was **Tom that** I saw. → I saw Tom. ✓ |
| **形式主语 it** | that 后是完整句，it 代替不定式/从句 | It is important **that we study hard**. |
| **强调句** | that 后缺成分（被强调部分在前面） | It was **Tom** that I saw ___. (缺宾语) |
| **定语从句** | that 在从句中作成分 | It is the book **that** I bought yesterday. |

**区分关键**：强调句的 that 后**缺少**被强调的那部分；形式主语的 that 从句**完整**。

---

### 📍 易错点汇总

| 易错点 | 正确用法 | 错误示例 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 时态一致 | 原句过去时，用 It **was**... | It is Tom that...（原句是昨天） |
| 人称代词的格 | 强调主语用 I/he；强调宾语用 me/him | It was **him** that broke...（强调主语应用 he） |
| that 不可省略 | 强调句中 that 一般不省略 | It was Tom ___ I saw.（缺 that） |
| 地点/时间状语 | 保留介词 in/on/at 等 | It was **in** 2020 that...（不能省 in） |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

1. It was in 2020 ________ I first met my best friend.
   A. when    B. which    C. that    D. where

2. It was Tom ________ broke the window yesterday.
   A. which    B. who    C. whom    D. whose

3. ________ was it that you saw at the party?
   A. Who    B. Whom    C. What    D. Which

4. It ________ at midnight that the fire broke out.
   A. is    B. was    C. has been    D. had been

5. Is it in the library ________ we will have the meeting?
   A. where    B. which    C. that    D. when

6. It was ________ that I gave the book to.
   A. he    B. him    C. his    D. her

7. It was not until 2020 ________ he graduated from university.
   A. when    B. which    C. that    D. then

8. It is the ability to learn ________ matters most.
   A. which    B. who    C. that    D. what

9. ________ was it that told you the news?
   A. Who    B. Whom    C. Which    D. What

10. It was in Beijing ________ the Olympic Games were held in 2008.
    A. where    B. which    C. that    D. when

11. It ________ my mother who/that gave me the present.
    A. is    B. was    C. has been    D. were

12. Was it yesterday ________ you lost your keys?
    A. when    B. which    C. that    D. where

13. It was ________ who helped me with my homework.
    A. him    B. he    C. his    D. they

14. It is not you ________ are wrong; it is I ________ am wrong.
    A. that; that    B. who; who    C. that; who    D. who; that

15. ________ was it that made you change your mind?
    A. What    B. Who    C. Which    D. Whom

16. It was because of the rain ________ the match was put off.
    A. when    B. which    C. that    D. why

17. It was in this room ________ he was born.
    A. where    B. which    C. that    D. when

18. It ________ the teacher that/who answered the question.
    A. is    B. was    C. has been    D. were

19. I don't know ________ that broke the glass.
    A. who it was    B. who was it    C. it was who    D. was it who

20. It was ________ that we had a heated discussion.
    A. yesterday    B. the classroom    C. this problem    D. All A, B, C can be emphasized

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (C)**
📌 强调句结构：It was + 被强调部分（in 2020）+ **that** + 其余。强调句只能用 that（或强调人时用 who/whom），不能用 when。
❌ A when 用于定语从句或状语从句；B which 用于定语从句；D where 用于地点。

---

**第2题 (B)**
📌 强调主语 Tom（人）→ 用 **who** 或 that。who 强调人更常见。
❌ A which 用于物；C whom 用于宾语；D whose 表所属。

---

**第3题 (A)**
📌 特殊疑问强调句：**Who** was it that you saw? 问"谁"，用 Who。
❌ B Whom 作宾语时用，但疑问句首习惯用 Who；C/D 不符句意。

---

**第4题 (B)**
📌 原句 the fire broke out 是过去时，强调句时态一致用 **was**。
❌ A is 表现在；C/D 完成时与强调句结构不符。

---

**第5题 (C)**
📌 强调地点状语 in the library，用 **that**。注意：强调句不用 where，用 that。
❌ A where 用于定语从句中先行词是地点时；B which 用于定语从句。

---

**第6题 (B)**
📌 强调"把书给了谁"→ 强调宾语（人），用 **him**。give sth to sb，强调 sb 用宾格。
❌ A he 是主格，强调宾语用宾格；C his 是物主代词；D 性别不符。

---

**第7题 (C)**
📌 Not until 强调句：It was **not until** 2020 **that** he graduated. 固定结构。
❌ A when 不用于强调句；B which 不用于此；D then 不是连接词。

---

**第8题 (C)**
📌 强调主语 the ability to learn，物/抽象概念用 **that**。
❌ A which 用于定语从句；B who 用于人；D what 引导名词性从句，不用于强调句。

---

**第9题 (A)**
📌 Who was it that told you? 强调"谁"告诉了你。
❌ B Whom 作宾语；C/D 不符"人"的疑问。

---

**第10题 (C)**
📌 强调地点状语 in Beijing，用 **that**。
❌ A where 用于定语从句，先行词是 place 时；B which 用于定语从句。

---

**第11题 (B)**
📌 gave 是过去时，强调句用 **was**。
❌ A is 表现在；C 完成时不符；D were 与 my mother 单数不一致。

---

**第12题 (C)**
📌 强调时间状语 yesterday，用 **that**。
❌ A when 不用于强调句；B which 用于定语从句。

---

**第13题 (B)**
📌 强调主语"谁帮了我"→ 用主格 **he**。who 前的被强调部分是主语，用主格。
❌ A him 是宾格；C his 是物主代词；D they 与 who 单复数不一致（若强调多人可用 they）。

---

**第14题 (B)**
📌 强调主语时，连接词用 **who**（人）。It is not you **who** are wrong; it is I **who** am wrong.
❌ A/C/D 中 that 强调人也可，但 who 更常见；且从句谓语要与被强调主语一致（you→are, I→am）。

---

**第15题 (A)**
📌 What was it that made you...? 强调"什么"让你改变主意。
❌ B Who 问人；C Which 需限定范围；D Whom 作宾语。

---

**第16题 (C)**
📌 强调原因状语 because of the rain，用 **that**。
❌ A when 不用于强调；B which 用于定语从句；D why 不用于强调句。

---

**第17题 (C)**
📌 强调地点状语 in this room，用 **that**。
❌ A where 用于定语从句，如 the room where he was born；此处是强调句。

---

**第18题 (B)**
📌 answered 是过去时，用 **was**。
❌ A is 表现在；C 完成时不符；D were 与 the teacher 单数不一致。

---

**第19题 (A)**
📌 宾语从句中的疑问强调句用陈述语序：I don't know **who it was that** broke the glass.
❌ B 疑问语序不能用于宾语从句；C/D 语序错误。

---

**第20题 (D)**
📌 强调句可强调时间（yesterday）、地点（the classroom）、宾语（this problem）等。三者均可作为被强调部分。
❌ 题目考查的是"哪些可被强调"，三者都符合强调句结构。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：强调句三板斧——**结构**（It is/was + 强调部分 + that/who + 其余）→ **还原法**（去掉 It...that 能还原即强调句）→ **连接词**（人用 who/that，其他一律 that）！牢记：强调句**不用 when/where/which**，只用 **that**（人可用 who）！💪
