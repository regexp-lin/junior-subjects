# 小 It 大显神通——从天气到强调句的万能用法 (The Word "It")

> 日期：2026年07月21日 | 语法分类：句法结构 | 难度：较难

---

## 一、知识点精讲

小 It 大概是英语里最"忙"的词了！它一会儿当形式主语，一会儿当形式宾语，还能强调、报时、说天气……今天咱们就把 It 的七十二般变化捋清楚！🔮

### 1️⃣ It 做形式主语——为"真主语"挡枪

当句子的真正主语是不定式、动名词或从句时，为了避免头重脚轻，我们派 It 上场当"替身"！

| 句型结构 | 用法说明 | 例句 |
|---------|---------|------|
| **It is + adj + (for sb) + to do sth** | for sb 修饰**动作**的难度、可能性等 | **It** is difficult **for him** to finish the task. 对他来说完成这项任务很难。 |
| **It is + adj + (of sb) + to do sth** | of sb 修饰**人品**，赞扬/批评某人 | **It** is kind **of you** to help me. 你真好，帮助了我。（赞美人） |
| **It is + adj/n + that 从句** | 从句做真正主语 | **It** is important **that** we protect the environment. 保护环境很重要。 |

💡 **for vs of 速记口诀**：**for** 说事情难不难，**of** 夸人好不好！形容词修饰人用 of（kind, foolish, clever），修饰事用 for（difficult, important, necessary）。

### 2️⃣ It 做形式宾语——"真宾语"在后头

当宾语是不定式或从句，后面又跟补语时，It 来当"占位符"：

| 句型结构 | 例句 |
|---------|------|
| sb find/think/make + **it** + adj + to do sth | I find **it** hard **to understand** this book. 我发现这本书很难懂。 |
| sb consider/feel + **it** + adj + that 从句 | She considers **it** lucky **that** she passed the exam. 她觉得通过考试很幸运。 |

### 3️⃣ It 表天气、时间、距离——"万能代词"

| 用法 | 例句 |
|-----|------|
| 天气 | **It's** sunny today. / **It** was raining yesterday. |
| 时间 | **It's** 3 o'clock. / **It** is Monday. |
| 距离 | **It's** 5 km from here to the station. |

### 4️⃣ 强调句——It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其余

把想强调的部分提前，用 **It is/was ... that/who** 框起来！注意：**去掉 It is/was ... that/who，句子依然完整**，这就是判断强调句的金标准！✨

| 原句 | 强调主语 | 强调状语 |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Tom met Mary in the park yesterday. | **It was Tom who** met Mary in the park yesterday. | **It was in the park that** Tom met Mary yesterday. |

### 5️⃣ 固定句型——It 的黄金搭档

| 句型 | 例句 |
|-----|------|
| It seems that... | **It seems that** he is angry. 他似乎生气了。 |
| It takes sb time to do | **It takes me** half an hour **to get** to school. 我花半小时到校。 |
| It is said that... | **It is said that** the movie is wonderful. 据说这部电影很精彩。 |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

1. ______ is important for us to learn a foreign language.
   A. This    B. That    C. It    D. There

2. I find ______ impossible to finish the work in one day.
   A. this    B. that    C. it    D. them

3. ______ was in 2020 that the pandemic began to spread widely.
   A. It    B. This    C. That    D. There

4. ______ is 8 kilometers from my home to the school.
   A. This    B. That    C. It    D. There

5. It is kind ______ you to lend me your bike.
   A. for    B. of    C. to    D. with

6. It is difficult ______ the little boy to solve this problem.
   A. for    B. of    C. to    D. with

7. ______ seems that nobody knows the answer.
   A. This    B. That    C. It    D. There

8. It ______ me two hours to finish my homework yesterday.
   A. spent    B. cost    C. took    D. paid

9. ______ is said that he will come back next week.
   A. This    B. That    C. It    D. There

10. It was Tom ______ broke the window.
    A. which    B. whom    C. who    D. whose

11. We think ______ necessary to save water.
    A. this    B. that    C. it    D. them

12. ______ is Monday today. Let's go to school!
    A. This    B. That    C. It    D. There

13. It is foolish ______ him to say such words.
    A. for    B. of    C. to    D. with

14. ______ was in the library that I found my lost book.
    A. It    B. This    C. That    D. There

15. It ______ sunny this afternoon. Shall we go for a walk?
    A. is    B. was    C. will be    D. has been

16. The teacher made ______ clear that we must hand in our homework on time.
    A. this    B. that    C. it    D. them

17. It is possible ______ anyone to make mistakes.
    A. for    B. of    C. to    D. with

18. ______ took us three days to climb the mountain.
    A. This    B. That    C. It    D. We

19. It is necessary ______ that we protect wild animals.
    A. for    B. of    C. /    D. to

20. ______ was his mother who encouraged him to try again.
    A. It    B. This    C. That    D. She

## 三、答案与详解

### 答案对照表

| 1-10 | 11-20 |
|------|-------|
| 1.C  2.C  3.A  4.C  5.B | 11.C  12.C  13.B  14.A  15.C |
| 6.A  7.C  8.C  9.C  10.C | 16.C  17.A  18.C  19.C  20.A |

---

### 错因深度解析

**第1题** ✅ **C**  
考查形式主语。真正主语是 to learn a foreign language，需要 It 做形式主语。**This/That/There** 都不能充当形式主语的"替身"，只有 It 可以！

**第2题** ✅ **C**  
考查形式宾语。find + it + adj + to do 是经典结构，it 占位，真正宾语是不定式。如果用 this/that，后面无法接形容词补语和不定式。

**第3题** ✅ **A**  
强调句！强调时间状语 in 2020。句型：It was + 被强调部分 + that + 其余。去掉 It was...that 后：The pandemic began to spread widely in 2020. 句子完整，证明是强调句。

**第4题** ✅ **C**  
It 表距离。It is + 距离 + from...to... 是固定用法，指"从某地到某地有多远"。

**第5题** ✅ **B**  
**of**！kind 是修饰"人"的品质——你这个人真好。口诀：夸人用 of（kind, clever, foolish, careless...）。

**第6题** ✅ **A**  
**for**！difficult 修饰的是"解决这道题"这件事的难度，不是评价小男孩的人品。修饰事用 for。

**第7题** ✅ **C**  
It seems that... 固定句型，表示"似乎……"。It 做形式主语，that 从句是真正主语。

**第8题** ✅ **C**  
It takes sb time to do sth —— 花某人多少时间做某事。**注意**：spend 的主语是人（I spent two hours...），take 的主语是 It（It took me two hours...）！

**第9题** ✅ **C**  
It is said that... 固定句型，"据说……"。类似还有 It is reported that... / It is known that...

**第10题** ✅ **C**  
强调句，强调主语 Tom。强调人时用 **who**（也可用 that），不用 which。which 只指物！

**第11题** ✅ **C**  
think + it + adj + to do，形式宾语结构。我们觉得节约用水是必要的——it 指代 to save water。

**第12题** ✅ **C**  
It 表时间/日期。It is Monday today. 和 It is 3 o'clock. 一样，都是 It 的"报时"功能。

**第13题** ✅ **B**  
**of**！foolish 形容的是"他"这个人——他真蠢，会说那种话。批评人的品格用 of。

**第14题** ✅ **A**  
强调句，强调地点状语 in the library。去掉框架：I found my lost book in the library. 完整！

**第15题** ✅ **C**  
It 表天气。this afternoon 是将来的时间，所以用将来时 **will be**。注意别看到 this 就选 This！

**第16题** ✅ **C**  
make + it + adj + that 从句。老师使"我们必须按时交作业"这一点变得清楚。it 做形式宾语。

**第17题** ✅ **A**  
**for**！possible 修饰的是"犯错"这件事的可能性，不涉及对"anyone"人品的评价。

**第18题** ✅ **C**  
It takes sb time to do sth。爬那座山花了我们三天时间。It 做形式主语。

**第19题** ✅ **C**  
It is necessary that... 句型。that 从句是真正主语，中间不需要加介词。**易错**：别被 for sb 搞混，这里没有 sb，直接 It is necessary that...

**第20题** ✅ **A**  
强调句，强调主语 his mother。强调人用 who 或 that。**D. She** 是干扰项——如果是普通句才用 She，这里是被强调句，必须用 It was...who...

---

🎉 **小结**：It 的七十二变，核心就是——**形式主语/宾语用 It，天气时间距离用 It，强调句框架用 It，固定句型记牢 It**！多练几遍，中考拿下！💪
