# ✨ 非谓语动词（二）——doing 的五大用法 (Gerunds & Present Participles: Five Uses of V-ing)

> 日期：2026年10月11日 | 语法分类：非谓语动词 | 难度：中等偏难

## 一、知识点精讲

### 1. 动名词 vs 现在分词

| 名称 | 词性 | 功能 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **动名词 (Gerund)** | 动词 + ing，起**名词**作用 | 作主语、宾语、表语 | **Swimming** is my hobby.（游泳是爱好） |
| **现在分词 (Present Participle)** | 动词 + ing，起**形容词/副词**作用 | 作定语、状语、宾补 | The **smiling** girl is my sister.（微笑的女孩） |

**形式相同，功能不同**：doing 既可作动名词，也可作现在分词，需根据句中位置和语法功能判断。

---

### 2. doing 的五大用法总览

| 用法 | 句法功能 | 动名词/分词 | 例句 |
|------|----------|-------------|------|
| **① 作主语** | 谓语前，表"做某事" | 动名词 | **Reading** books enriches our mind. |
| **② 作宾语** | 动词后，作宾语 | 动名词 | I enjoy **playing** basketball. |
| **③ 作表语** | 系动词后 | 动名词 | My job is **teaching** English. |
| **④ 作定语** | 修饰名词（前置/后置） | 分词或动名词 | a **sleeping** baby（熟睡的婴儿） |
| **⑤ 介词后** | 介词宾语 | 动名词 | I'm used to **getting** up early. |

---

### 3. ① 作主语——动名词作主语

| 句型 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| V-ing + 谓语 | **Getting up** early is good for health. |
| It is no use/ useless + doing | **It** is no use **crying** over spilt milk. |
| There is no + doing | **There** is no **telling** what will happen. |

**注意**：作主语时 doing 表抽象、习惯性动作；表一次性的具体动作更常见用不定式。

---

### 4. ② 作宾语——后接 doing 的常见动词（必须掌握）

| 动词/短语 | 例句 |
|-----------|------|
| enjoy | I enjoy **listening** to music. |
| finish | Have you finished **doing** your homework? |
| mind | Would you mind **opening** the window? |
| practice | She practices **speaking** English every day. |
| avoid | We should avoid **making** the same mistakes. |
| keep | He kept **working** until midnight. |
| suggest | She suggested **going** to the park. |
| consider | I'm considering **changing** my job. |
| admit | He admitted **breaking** the vase. |
| risk | Don't risk **losing** your money. |
| imagine | Can you imagine **living** without electricity? |
| delay | Don't delay **sending** the letter. |
| postpone | They postponed **holding** the meeting. |
| give up | He gave up **smoking** last year. |
| can't help | I can't help **laughing** at his joke. |
| feel like | I don't feel like **eating** anything. |
| be worth | The book is worth **reading**. |

**易混对比**：有的动词既可接 to do 又可接 doing，含义不同（如 stop to do 停下做某事 vs stop doing 停止做某事）。

---

### 5. ③ 作表语——动名词作表语

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 主语 + be + doing | Her hobby is **collecting** stamps. |
| 主语 + be + doing | My favorite sport is **swimming**. |

**与进行时区分**：表语时主语与 doing 是同一概念（爱好 = 集邮）；进行时是主语正在做某事（He is swimming. 他正在游泳）。

---

### 6. ④ 作定语——分词作定语

| 位置 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **前置** | 单个 doing 放名词前，表"正在…的" | a **boiling** river（沸腾的河流） |
| **前置** | 表功能/用途 | a **swimming** pool（游泳池） |
| **后置** | doing 短语放名词后 | the boy **standing** by the door（站在门旁的男孩） |

**前置定语**：boiling water（沸腾的水）、flying birds（飞鸟）、working people（劳动人民）

---

### 7. ⑤ 介词后接 doing——必考重点

| 短语/结构 | 例句 |
|-----------|------|
| look forward to | I look forward to **seeing** you soon. |
| be used to | He is used to **living** alone. |
| be busy | She is busy **preparing** for the exam. |
| spend... (in) doing | I spend two hours **doing** homework every day. |
| have difficulty (in) doing | He has difficulty **understanding** the text. |
| prevent/stop...from doing | Nothing can prevent me **from going**. |
| thank sb for doing | Thank you for **helping** me. |
| apologize for doing | I apologize for **being** late. |
| be interested in | She is interested in **learning** French. |
| succeed in | He succeeded in **passing** the exam. |
| be good at | Tom is good at **playing** the piano. |
| instead of | Let's walk instead of **driving**. |
| feel like | I feel like **having** a rest. |

**⚠️ 易错提醒**：look forward to、be used to、pay attention to 中的 **to 是介词**，后接 doing，不是不定式！

---

### 8. stop / remember / forget / try 的 to do vs doing 辨析

| 动词 | + to do | + doing |
|------|---------|---------|
| stop | stop to do 停下（手中事）去做另一事 | stop doing 停止正在做的事 |
| remember | remember to do 记得要去做（未做） | remember doing 记得做过（已做） |
| forget | forget to do 忘记要去做（未做） | forget doing 忘记做过（已做） |
| try | try to do 努力/试图做 | try doing 试着做（试验） |
| go on | go on to do 接着做另一件事 | go on doing 继续做同一件事 |

## 三、答案与详解

### 🔍 错因深度解析

---

**第1题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：动名词作主语。Reading English every morning 作句子的主语，谓语为 helps。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。不定式也可作主语，但表"每天早晨读英语"是习惯性动作，动名词更自然；且 help 作谓语时，动名词主语更常见。

---

**第2题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：enjoy 后接 doing。enjoy 为必须接动名词的动词之一。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。enjoy 后只能接 doing，不能接 to do。

---

**第3题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：mind 后接 doing。Would you mind opening...? 表"你介意开一下窗吗？"

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。mind 后接 doing；mind to do 为错误搭配。

---

**第4题** 答案：B

📌 **考点**：be used to doing 表"习惯于"。to 为介词，后接动名词。

❌ **易错点**：易选 A。be used to 中的 to 是介词，不是不定式符号，必须接 doing。区分：used to do（过去常常）、be used to doing（习惯于）。

---

**第5题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。the girl sitting under the tree = the girl who is sitting under the tree。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。sat 为过去式，不能作定语；sitting 为现在分词，表"正坐在树下的"。

---

**第6题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：spend...doing 固定搭配。spend + 时间 + (in) doing sth。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。spend 后接 doing，不接 to do；spend time to do 为错误表达。

---

**第7题** 答案：B

📌 **考点**：look forward to doing。to 为介词，后接动名词。

❌ **易错点**：易选 A。look forward to 中的 to 是介词，后必须接 doing，不能接 hear。

---

**第8题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：suggest 后接 doing。suggest going = suggest that we (should) go。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。suggest 后接 doing，不接 to do；suggest sb to do 为错误用法。

---

**第9题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：动名词作表语。My hobby is collecting stamps，表语与主语等价（爱好=集邮）。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。to collect 也可作表语，但"爱好是集邮"用动名词更自然，表习惯性、抽象的活动。

---

**第10题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：keep 后接 doing。keep doing 表"持续做某事"。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。keep 后接 doing，不接 to do。

---

**第11题** 答案：B

📌 **考点**：过去分词作定语表被动/完成状态。the lost child = the child who was lost（走失的孩子）。

❌ **易错点**：易选 A。losing 表主动进行；"走失的"孩子应是被动的、已完成的状态，用过去分词 lost。此题考查 -ed 与 -ing 定语辨析。

---

**第12题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：have difficulty (in) doing。介词 in 可省略，后接动名词。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。have difficulty 后接 (in) doing，不接 to do。

---

**第13题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：thank sb for doing。介词 for 后接动名词。

❌ **易错点**：易选 A。for 是介词，后接 doing；thank you for help 中 help 作名词可，但 thank you for helping 更完整。

---

**第14题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：practice 后接 doing。practice playing the piano 表"练习弹钢琴"。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。practice 后只能接 doing，不能接 to do。

---

**第15题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：avoid 后接 doing。avoid making mistakes 表"避免犯错误"。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。avoid 后接 doing，不接 to do。

---

**第16题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：stop doing 表"停止正在做的事"。句意：他看到老师时停止了说话。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。stop to talk 表"停下来去说话"；stop talking 表"停止说话"。根据语境"看到老师"应停止说话，选 stop talking。

---

**第17题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：feel like 后接 doing。feel like going out 表"想出去"。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。feel like 中的 like 为介词，后接 doing，不接 to do。

---

**第18题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：现在分词作后置定语。the boy standing by the window = the boy who is standing by the window。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。stood 为过去式；standing 为现在分词作定语，表"正站在窗边的男孩"。

---

**第19题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：be busy doing。be busy 后接动名词，表"忙于做某事"。

❌ **易错点**：易选 B。be busy 后接 doing，不接 to do。

---

**第20题** 答案：C

📌 **考点**：prevent...from doing。prevent us from achieving 表"阻止我们实现"。from 为介词，后接 doing。

❌ **易错点**：易选 D。prevent sb from doing 中 from 不能省略；prevent us achieving 不完整，需 from achieving。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：doing 的五大用法——① 作主语（Reading is fun）；② 作宾语（enjoy/finish/mind/practice/avoid/keep/suggest 等后接 doing）；③ 作表语（My hobby is collecting stamps）；④ 作定语（a sleeping baby / the boy standing there）；⑤ 介词后接 doing（look forward to / be used to / be busy / spend...doing / have difficulty doing / prevent...from doing）。**核心易错**：① look forward to、be used to、pay attention to 中的 to 是介词，后接 doing；② spend、have difficulty、be busy 等固定搭配后接 doing；③ stop/remember/forget + to do 与 + doing 含义不同；④ 作定语时 -ing 表主动/进行，-ed 表被动/完成；⑤ prevent...from doing 中 from 不能省略。
