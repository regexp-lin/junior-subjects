# would rather / had better / would like 三兄弟终极对决 (Preference Expressions)

> 日期：2026年08月05日 | 语法分类：句法结构 | 难度：中等

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 三个"想要/宁愿/最好"的表达

这三个短语都涉及"意愿"或"建议"，但语气、用法和搭配截然不同！

| 短语 | 含义 | 语气 | 后接动词形式 |
|------|------|------|-------------|
| **would like** | 想要 | 委婉客气 | to do |
| **would rather** | 宁愿/更愿意 | 主观偏好 | do（动词原形） |
| **had better** | 最好 | 建议/警告 | do（动词原形） |

---

### 🎯 逐个击破

#### ① would like = want 的委婉版

- **would like + to do**：想要做某事
- **would like + 名词**：想要某物
- **would like sb. to do**：想要某人做某事

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| would like to do | I **would like to** have a cup of tea. |
| would like + 名词 | I **would like** some water. |
| would like sb. to do | I **would like you to** come. |
| 缩写形式 | I**'d like to** go home. |

**疑问形式**：Would you like to...? / Would you like some...?

---

#### ② would rather = 宁愿（表示选择偏好）

- **would rather + 动词原形**（不加 to！）
- **would rather...than...**：宁愿……而不愿……
- 缩写形式：**'d rather**

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| would rather do | I **would rather stay** at home. |
| would rather do than do | I **would rather walk than** take a bus. |
| would rather not do | I **would rather not go** out today. |

⚠️ 注意：than 后面也是**动词原形**！

---

#### ③ had better = 最好（带有劝告/警告色彩）

- **had better + 动词原形**（不加 to！）
- 缩写形式：**'d better**
- 否定形式：**had better not + 动词原形**

| 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| had better do | You **had better hurry** up. |
| had better not do | You **had better not be** late. |
| 缩写 | You**'d better** go now. |

⚠️ 注意：had better 虽然含 had，但**不是过去时**，表示对现在或将来的建议！

---

### ⚡ 三者对比

| 对比项 | would like | would rather | had better |
|--------|-----------|--------------|------------|
| 后接动词 | **to do** | **do** | **do** |
| 语气 | 委婉/客气 | 主观偏好 | 劝告/警告 |
| 否定形式 | would not like to do | would rather not do | had better not do |
| 缩写 | 'd like | 'd rather | 'd better |
| 时态 | 不是过去时 | 不是过去时 | 不是过去时 |

---

### 📍 中考高频陷阱

| 陷阱 | 错误示范 | 正确写法 |
|------|----------|----------|
| would rather 后加 to | I'd rather ~~to stay~~. | I'd rather **stay**. |
| had better 后加 to | You'd better ~~to go~~. | You'd better **go**. |
| had better 否定位置错 | You'd ~~not better~~ go. | You'd **better not** go. |
| would rather...than 后加 to | I'd rather walk than ~~to ride~~. | I'd rather walk than **ride**. |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

1. — Would you like ________ some coffee?
   — Yes, please.
   A. have    B. having    C. to have    D. had

2. I would rather ________ at home than ________ shopping.
   A. stay; go    B. to stay; to go    C. stay; to go    D. staying; going

3. You'd better ________ late for school again.
   A. not to be    B. not be    C. don't be    D. to not be

4. — Would you like to go swimming with us?
   — ________.
   A. Yes, I would like    B. Yes, I'd like to
   C. Yes, I like    D. Yes, I would

5. He would rather ________ the bus than ________ a taxi.
   A. take; take    B. to take; to take    C. taking; taking    D. take; to take

6. You had better ________ more exercise to keep healthy.
   A. to take    B. take    C. taking    D. taken

7. I'd like you ________ my birthday party.
   A. come to    B. to come to    C. coming to    D. came to

8. She would rather not ________ out in the rain.
   A. to go    B. going    C. goes    D. go

9. You'd better ________ the window. It's very cold outside.
   A. not open    B. not to open    C. don't open    D. not opening

10. — What would you like ________?
    — I'd like some noodles.
    A. eat    B. eating    C. to eat    D. ate

11. He ________ stay up late. He has an exam tomorrow.
    A. had not better    B. had better not    C. had better don't    D. would not better

12. I'd rather ________ English than ________ math.
    A. study; study    B. to study; to study    C. studying; studying    D. study; to study

13. Would you like ________ juice or ________ milk?
    A. some; some    B. any; any    C. some; any    D. any; some

14. You'd better ________ to bed early tonight. You look tired.
    A. go    B. to go    C. going    D. went

15. She would like ________ a new dress for the party.
    A. buy    B. buying    C. bought    D. to buy

16. — Would you rather ________ by plane or by train?
    — I'd rather ________ by train. It's safer.
    A. travel; travel    B. to travel; travel    C. travel; to travel    D. to travel; to travel

17. He'd better not ________ so much junk food.
    A. eat    B. to eat    C. eating    D. eats

18. I'd like my father ________ me a new bike.
    A. buy    B. buying    C. to buy    D. bought

19. — Would you like to come to my house for dinner?
    — I'd like to, ________ I'm too busy today.
    A. and    B. so    C. but    D. or

20. They would rather ________ the problem by themselves than ________ the teacher for help.
    A. solve; ask    B. to solve; to ask    C. solve; to ask    D. solving; asking

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (C)**
📌 would like 后面接 **to do**。Would you like **to have** some coffee? "你想喝咖啡吗？"
❌ A/B/D 都不是 would like 的正确搭配。

---

**第2题 (A)**
📌 would rather do **than** do，两边都用**动词原形**。"宁愿待在家也不去购物"。
❌ B 加了 to；C than 后加了 to；D 用了 doing。

---

**第3题 (B)**
📌 had better 的否定是 **had better not + 动词原形**。"你最好别再迟到了"。
❌ A 加了 to；C 用了 don't（had better 不用 don't 否定）。

---

**第4题 (B)**
📌 回答 Would you like to...? 用 **Yes, I'd like to**（to 后面省略了重复的动词）。
❌ A 缺少 to；C 语义不对；D 不完整。

---

**第5题 (A)**
📌 would rather do **than** do，两边都用**动词原形** take。"宁愿坐公交也不打车"。
❌ B/C/D 形式都不对，would rather...than 后面永远是原形对原形。

---

**第6题 (B)**
📌 had better + **动词原形** take。"你最好多锻炼保持健康"。
❌ A 加了 to；C/D 形式不对。

---

**第7题 (B)**
📌 would like sb. **to do** sth. = "想要某人做某事"。"我想让你来参加我的生日派对"。
❌ A 缺少 to；C/D 形式不对。

---

**第8题 (D)**
📌 would rather not + **动词原形** go。"她宁愿不在雨中出去"。
❌ A 加了 to；B/C 形式不对。

---

**第9题 (A)**
📌 had better not + **动词原形** open。"你最好别开窗，外面很冷"。
❌ B 加了 to；C 用了 don't（错误否定方式）。

---

**第10题 (C)**
📌 would like + **to do**。What would you like **to eat**? "你想吃什么？"
❌ A/B/D 都不是 would like 的正确搭配。

---

**第11题 (B)**
📌 had better 的否定是 **had better not**，not 放在 better 后面。
❌ A not 放在了 had 后面（错误位置）；C 用了 don't。

---

**第12题 (A)**
📌 would rather do **than** do，两边动词原形。study...than study。
❌ B 加了 to；C 用了 doing；D 混合了 to do 和 do。

---

**第13题 (A)**
📌 Would you like...? 是表示**邀请/提供**的句型，用 **some** 而不是 any（这是 some 用于疑问句的特殊情况——表示邀请时用 some）。
❌ B/C/D 用了 any，提供饮食时用 some 更礼貌。

---

**第14题 (A)**
📌 had better + **动词原形** go。"你今晚最好早点睡"。
❌ B 加了 to，had better 后不需要 to。

---

**第15题 (D)**
📌 would like + **to do**。She would like **to buy** a new dress. "她想买一条新裙子"。
❌ A 缺少 to；B/C 形式不对。

---

**第16题 (A)**
📌 would rather + **动词原形**，问答两边都用原形 travel。
❌ B/C/D 加了 to，would rather 后面不要 to。

---

**第17题 (A)**
📌 had better not + **动词原形** eat。"他最好别吃太多垃圾食品"。
❌ B 加了 to；C/D 形式不对。

---

**第18题 (C)**
📌 would like sb. + **to do**。I'd like my father **to buy** me a new bike.
❌ A 缺少 to；B/D 形式不对。

---

**第19题 (C)**
📌 I'd like to 后面是转折 = "我想去，**但是**我太忙了"。转折用 **but**。
❌ A and 表并列；B so 表结果；D or 表选择——都不符合语境。

---

**第20题 (A)**
📌 would rather do **than** do，两边动词原形 solve 和 ask。"宁愿自己解决也不向老师求助"。
❌ B/C/D 形式不对，than 后面必须是动词原形。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：三兄弟最大的区别在**后接动词形式**——would like + **to do**，would rather + **do**，had better + **do**。记住 would rather 和 had better 后面**不加 to**，否定形式分别是 rather not do 和 better not do。考试看到 'd 缩写时，根据后面跟的是 to do 还是 do 来判断是哪个短语！💪
