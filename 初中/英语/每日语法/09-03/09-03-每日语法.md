# 🎭 百变 it——it 的用法大全 (The Many Uses of "It")

> 日期：2026年09月03日 | 语法分类：代词专题 | 难度：中等

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 it 不只是"它"

很多同学一看到 it 就翻译成"它"，但在英语里 it 的用法远不止这一个。中考中 it 是高频考点，常见用法包括：指代、形式主语、形式宾语、表时间/天气/距离等。

---

### 📋 it 的六大用法

| 用法 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **① 指代上文** | 代替前面提到的事物 | I bought a book. **It** is interesting. |
| **② 表天气** | 作主语表天气 | **It** is sunny today. |
| **③ 表时间** | 作主语表时间 | **It** is eight o'clock now. |
| **④ 表距离** | 作主语表距离 | **It** is five kilometers from here to school. |
| **⑤ 形式主语** | 代替真正的主语（to do / that 从句） | **It** is important **to study** hard. |
| **⑥ 形式宾语** | 代替真正的宾语（to do / that 从句） | I find **it** hard **to learn** math. |

---

### 📋 it 作形式主语——重点突破

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **It is + adj + to do** | It 形式主语，to do 真正主语 | **It** is easy **to answer** this question. |
| **It is + adj + for sb + to do** | for sb 表"对某人来说" | **It** is important **for** us **to protect** the environment. |
| **It is + adj + of sb + to do** | of sb 表"某人品质" | **It** is kind **of** you **to help** me. |
| **It is + n + to do / that...** | 名词作表语 | **It** is a pleasure **to work** with you. |
| **It is + 过去分词 + that...** | 被动结构 | **It** is reported **that** the meeting was a success. |
| **It seems/appears + that...** | 似乎…… | **It** seems **that** he is happy. |

**for 与 of 的区别口诀**：形容词描述"事情难易"用 for（difficult, easy, important, necessary）；描述"人的品质"用 of（kind, nice, clever, foolish, brave）。

---

### 📋 it 作形式宾语

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **find/think/make + it + adj + to do** | it 形式宾语 | I **find it** difficult **to finish** the work alone. |
| **think it + adj/n + that...** | it 代替 that 从句 | I **think it** necessary **that** we should start early. |

---

### 📋 it 与 one / that 的区别

| 代词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **it** | 指**同一个**东西 | I lost my pen. I can't find **it**.（就是那支笔） |
| **one** | 指**同类中的一个**（泛指） | I lost my pen. I need to buy **one**.（任意一支新笔） |
| **that** | 指同类但**有限定修饰**（特指） | The weather in Beijing is colder than **that** in Guangzhou. |

---

### ⚠️ 中考高频陷阱

| 陷阱 | 正解 |
|------|------|
| To learn English is important. | 可以，但中考更常用 **It** is important to learn English. |
| I find difficult to finish it. | I find **it** difficult to finish. |
| It is kind for you to help me. | It is kind **of** you to help me.（描述人的品质用 of） |
| I lost my pen. I need to buy it. | I need to buy **one**.（买的不是同一支） |
| The weather here is warmer than Beijing. | ...warmer than **that** in Beijing.（用 that 代替 weather） |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

*全面考查 it 的指代、形式主语、形式宾语、表天气时间距离、与 one/that 辨析。*

1. —What's the weather like today? —_____ is cloudy.
   A. That    B. This    C. It    D. One

2. _____ is important for us to learn English well.
   A. This    B. That    C. It    D. One

3. I find _____ hard to remember all the new words.
   A. that    B. this    C. it    D. one

4. _____ is very kind of you to carry the box for the old man.
   A. That    B. This    C. It    D. One

5. —What time is _____? —_____ is half past nine.
   A. it; It    B. it; That    C. that; It    D. this; It

6. I lost my watch yesterday. I must buy _____.
   A. it    B. that    C. one    D. this

7. The weather in Guangzhou is warmer than _____ in Harbin.
   A. it    B. one    C. this    D. that

8. _____ is about ten minutes' walk from my home to school.
   A. That    B. This    C. It    D. There

9. She thought _____ necessary to tell him the truth.
   A. this    B. that    C. it    D. one

10. _____ seems that he has known the news already.
    A. That    B. This    C. It    D. He

11. _____ is difficult for him to finish the work in two days.
    A. That    B. This    C. It    D. There

12. —Is this your pen? —No, _____ is in my bag.
    A. one    B. that    C. it    D. mine

13. _____ is foolish of them to believe his words.
    A. That    B. This    C. It    D. One

14. I think _____ no use crying over spilt milk.
    A. this    B. that    C. it    D. one

15. _____ rained heavily yesterday, so we stayed at home.
    A. That    B. This    C. It    D. There

16. We find _____ impossible to finish so much work in one hour.
    A. this    B. that    C. one    D. it

17. _____ is necessary for students to do some exercise every day.
    A. That    B. This    C. It    D. One

18. The population of China is larger than _____ of Japan.
    A. it    B. one    C. this    D. that

19. —Do you have a computer? —Yes, I have _____. _____ is very useful.
    A. it; It    B. one; One    C. one; It    D. it; One

20. _____ took us two hours to get to the top of the mountain.
    A. That    B. This    C. It    D. There

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (C)**
📌 考点：it 表天气。询问天气时用 it 作主语，回答也用 It is cloudy.
❌ 易错点：选 A/B That/This 不用于表天气的固定句型。

---

**第2题 (C)**
📌 考点：it 作形式主语。It is important for us to learn English well. 真正主语是 to learn English well。
❌ 易错点：选 A/B 不能作形式主语；形式主语只能用 it。

---

**第3题 (C)**
📌 考点：it 作形式宾语。find it hard to do sth，it 代替后面的 to remember all the new words。
❌ 易错点：选 A that 不能作形式宾语；find + it + adj + to do 是固定结构。

---

**第4题 (C)**
📌 考点：It is + adj + of sb + to do。kind 描述人的品质，用 of。It 是形式主语。
❌ 易错点：关键是识别 it 作形式主语的句型。

---

**第5题 (A)**
📌 考点：it 表时间。询问时间 What time is it? 回答 It is half past nine.
❌ 易错点：问答都用 it，不用 that/this。

---

**第6题 (C)**
📌 考点：it 与 one 辨析。手表丢了，买的是"同类的一块新手表"（泛指），用 one。
❌ 易错点：选 A it 指同一块手表，但丢了的不可能再买回来；买新的用 one。

---

**第7题 (D)**
📌 考点：that 代替同类名词避免重复。that 代替 the weather（特指哈尔滨的天气），用于比较句。
❌ 易错点：选 A it 指同一个；选 B one 泛指；比较句中代替不可数名词用 that。

---

**第8题 (C)**
📌 考点：it 表距离。It is + 距离 + from A to B 表"从A到B有多远"。
❌ 易错点：选 D There 不用于表距离的句型。

---

**第9题 (C)**
📌 考点：it 作形式宾语。think it necessary to do sth，it 代替 to tell him the truth。
❌ 易错点：think/find/make + it + adj + to do 是固定结构，it 不可省略。

---

**第10题 (C)**
📌 考点：It seems that... 表"似乎"。It 作形式主语，that 引导真正的主语从句。
❌ 易错点：选 D He seems 语法可以但结构不同，此处考 It seems that 句型。

---

**第11题 (C)**
📌 考点：It is + adj + for sb + to do。difficult 描述事情难度，用 for。It 形式主语。
❌ 易错点：形式主语只能用 it，不能用 that/this/there。

---

**第12题 (D)**
📌 考点：名词性物主代词。"我的（笔）在书包里"，mine = my pen，指同一支笔。
❌ 易错点：选 C it 指"这支笔"，但问的是"这是你的笔吗？不是"，说明不是同一支，应说"我的在包里"用 mine。

---

**第13题 (C)**
📌 考点：It is + adj + of sb + to do。foolish 描述人的愚蠢（品质），用 of。
❌ 易错点：描述人品质的形容词（kind, nice, clever, foolish, brave, careless）用 of。

---

**第14题 (C)**
📌 考点：It is no use doing sth 固定句型，表"做……没有用"。it 作形式主语。
❌ 易错点：这是固定表达，no use 后接 doing（动名词）。

---

**第15题 (C)**
📌 考点：it 表天气。描述下雨 It rained heavily。
❌ 易错点：天气、自然现象用 it 作主语，不用 that/there。

---

**第16题 (D)**
📌 考点：find it + adj + to do。it 作形式宾语，代替 to finish so much work in one hour。
❌ 易错点：find/think/make/consider + it + adj/n + to do，it 不可省略。

---

**第17题 (C)**
📌 考点：It is + adj + for sb + to do。necessary 描述事情性质，用 for。
❌ 易错点：形式主语固定用 it，不用 that/this。

---

**第18题 (D)**
📌 考点：that 代替同类名词。that 代替 the population，用于比较句避免重复。
❌ 易错点：比较句中代替前面的名词，可数单数用 one/the one，不可数或特指用 that。

---

**第19题 (C)**
📌 考点：one 和 it 综合辨析。"你有电脑吗？有，我有一台"——have one（泛指一台）。"它很有用"——It is useful（指代前面提到的那台）。
❌ 易错点：第一空买/拥有一台（泛指）用 one；第二空指代已拥有的（特指）用 it。

---

**第20题 (C)**
📌 考点：It takes sb + 时间 + to do sth。"花了我们两小时"，it 作形式主语。
❌ 易错点：It takes... to do... 是固定句型，形式主语只能用 it。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：it 的用法归纳——① **指代**上文提到的同一事物；② **表天气/时间/距离**作主语；③ **形式主语**：It is + adj + (for/of sb) + to do；④ **形式宾语**：find/think/make + it + adj + to do；⑤ **与 one 辨析**：it = 同一个，one = 同类另一个；⑥ **与 that 辨析**：比较句中代替不可数名词/特指名词用 that。记住：**形容词描述事情用 for，描述人品质用 of**！💪
