# 🔗 过去与现在的桥梁——完成时态专题 (Perfect Tenses)

> 日期：2026年09月06日 | 语法分类：时态（重难点） | 难度：困难

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 完成时态的核心思想

完成时态强调的是**动作对某个时间点的影响或结果**，不只是"做了"，而是"做了之后怎样了"。中考主要考**现在完成时**和**过去完成时**。

---

### 📋 现在完成时

| 要素 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **结构** | have/has + 过去分词(done) |
| **用法一** | 过去发生的动作对**现在**有影响/结果 |
| **用法二** | 过去开始持续到**现在**的动作或状态 |
| **标志词** | already, yet, just, ever, never, since, for, so far, recently, in the past few years |

**用法一（结果）**：
- I **have lost** my key.（钥匙丢了→现在找不到）
- He **has gone** to Beijing.（他去了北京→现在不在这里）

**用法二（持续）**：
- I **have lived** here **for** ten years.（住了十年→还在住）
- She **has studied** English **since** 2020.（从2020年开始学→还在学）

---

### 📋 have been to / have gone to / have been in 辨析

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **have been to** | 去过（已回来） | I **have been to** Beijing twice.（去过两次，现在回来了） |
| **have gone to** | 去了（未回来） | He **has gone to** Beijing.（去了北京，不在这里） |
| **have been in** | 待在（持续） | She **has been in** Beijing for three years.（在北京待了三年） |

---

### 📋 短暂动词 vs 延续动词

现在完成时与 for/since 连用时，动词必须用**延续性动词**。短暂动词需转换：

| 短暂动词 | 延续性替换 | 例句 |
|----------|-----------|------|
| buy | have | I have **had** this book for two years. |
| borrow | keep | He has **kept** the book since Monday. |
| open | be open | The shop has **been open** since 8 a.m. |
| close | be closed | The museum has **been closed** for a week. |
| begin/start | be on | The movie has **been on** for 20 minutes. |
| die | be dead | His grandpa has **been dead** for five years. |
| leave | be away (from) | He has **been away** from home for two months. |
| join | be in / be a member of | She has **been in** the club for a year. |
| come/arrive | be here/there | He has **been here** since yesterday. |
| finish | be over | The class has **been over** for ten minutes. |

---

### 📋 过去完成时

| 要素 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **结构** | had + 过去分词(done) |
| **含义** | "过去的过去"——在过去某个时间/动作之前已完成 |
| **标志词** | by the time, before, after, by + 过去时间, already (与过去时搭配) |

- **By the time** I got to the station, the train **had left**.（到站前，火车已走了）
- He told me that he **had finished** the work.（他告诉我他已完成了——完成在 told 之前）

---

### 📋 现在完成时 vs 一般过去时

| 对比 | 现在完成时 | 一般过去时 |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| **关注点** | 过去动作对**现在**的影响 | 过去发生的事实（与现在无关） |
| **标志词** | since, for, already, yet, ever | yesterday, last..., ago, in 2020 |
| **例句** | I **have read** the book.（读过了→知道内容） | I **read** the book yesterday.（昨天读的→描述过去） |

---

### ⚠️ 中考高频陷阱

| 陷阱 | 正解 |
|------|------|
| He has come here for two hours. | He has **been** here for two hours.（come 短暂→ be here 延续） |
| I have bought the bike since last year. | I have **had** the bike since last year.（buy→have） |
| He has gone to Beijing twice. | He has **been to** Beijing twice.（去过用 been to） |
| He died for five years. | He has **been dead** for five years.（die→be dead） |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

*全面考查现在完成时、过去完成时、短暂动词转延续动词、been to/gone to/been in 辨析。*

1. —Where is your father? —He _____ Shanghai. He will be back next week.
   A. has been to    B. has gone to    C. has been in    D. went to

2. I _____ this book since last month. It's very interesting.
   A. have bought    B. have had    C. bought    D. had

3. He _____ the army for five years. He likes his job.
   A. has joined    B. joined    C. has been in    D. was in

4. By the time I arrived at the cinema, the movie _____.
   A. began    B. has begun    C. had begun    D. begins

5. —Have you ever _____ to the Great Wall? —Yes, I _____ there last year.
   A. gone; went    B. been; went    C. been; have been    D. gone; have gone

6. The factory has been open _____ two years ago.
   A. for    B. since    C. in    D. before

7. He told me that he _____ the book already.
   A. has read    B. reads    C. had read    D. would read

8. —How long have you _____ here? —For about three years.
   A. come    B. arrived    C. been    D. got

9. She _____ in this school since she was six years old.
   A. studied    B. has studied    C. studies    D. was studying

10. The old man _____ for ten years. We all miss him.
    A. has died    B. died    C. has been dead    D. was dead

11. I _____ my homework already. Can I go out to play?
    A. finish    B. finished    C. have finished    D. had finished

12. —_____ you _____ your lunch yet? —Yes, I have.
    A. Did; have    B. Have; had    C. Do; have    D. Are; having

13. Mr. Wang _____ to work by bus. But now he rides a bike.
    A. is used to go    B. used to going    C. used to go    D. is used to going

14. The meeting _____ for half an hour when I got there.
    A. had begun    B. had been on    C. has begun    D. has been on

15. She has _____ been to Europe. She hopes to go there someday.
    A. already    B. ever    C. never    D. just

16. Great changes _____ in our city in the past ten years.
    A. took place    B. have taken place    C. were taken place    D. have been taken place

17. —Is Tom at home? —No, he _____ to the library.
    A. has been    B. has gone    C. went    D. goes

18. He _____ away from his hometown for twenty years. He really misses it.
    A. was    B. has been    C. went    D. has gone

19. Jack _____ the book to the library. He doesn't have it now.
    A. returned    B. has returned    C. returns    D. is returning

20. They _____ friends since they met in 2018.
    A. made    B. have made    C. have been    D. are

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (B)**
📌 考点：have gone to 表"去了（未回来）"。父亲不在→去了上海，下周才回来。
❌ 易错点：选 A has been to 表"去过已回来"，与"在哪里"的问题矛盾。

---

**第2题 (B)**
📌 考点：短暂动词转延续。since last month 表持续，buy 是短暂动词，需转为 have had。
❌ 易错点：选 A have bought 不能与 since 连用；buy 短暂→ have 延续。

---

**第3题 (C)**
📌 考点：短暂动词转延续。for five years 表持续，join 是短暂动词，转为 has been in。
❌ 易错点：选 A has joined 不能与 for 连用；join→be in / be a member of。

---

**第4题 (C)**
📌 考点：过去完成时。by the time I arrived（过去时间点），电影在到达之前已开始→ had begun。
❌ 易错点：选 A/B 未使用过去完成时；by the time + 过去时 → 主句用 had done。

---

**第5题 (B)**
📌 考点：have been to 表"去过"。ever 提示经历；last year 表过去确定时间用一般过去时 went。
❌ 易错点：选 A gone to 表"去了未回来"，与 Have you ever... 不符。

---

**第6题 (B)**
📌 考点：since + 过去时间点。since two years ago = for two years。since 后接时间点。
❌ 易错点：选 A for 后接时间段（for two years），不接 ago。

---

**第7题 (C)**
📌 考点：过去完成时。主句 told（过去时），从句"已读完"发生在 told 之前→ had read。
❌ 易错点：选 A has read 应随主句后退为 had read。

---

**第8题 (C)**
📌 考点：短暂动词转延续。How long 表持续，come/arrive 是短暂动词，需用 been（= be here）。
❌ 易错点：选 A/B/D come/arrived/got 都是短暂动词，不能与 How long 连用。

---

**第9题 (B)**
📌 考点：since + 过去时间。since she was six 表从过去持续到现在，用现在完成时 has studied。
❌ 易错点：选 A studied 一般过去时不能表持续到现在。

---

**第10题 (C)**
📌 考点：短暂动词转延续。for ten years 表持续，die 是短暂动词，需转为 has been dead。
❌ 易错点：选 A has died 不能与 for 连用；die→be dead。

---

**第11题 (C)**
📌 考点：现在完成时表结果。already 提示已完成，且结果是"现在可以出去玩了"，用 have finished。
❌ 易错点：选 D had finished 过去完成时需有过去参照时间，此处看现在结果用现在完成时。

---

**第12题 (B)**
📌 考点：现在完成时疑问句。yet 提示完成时，Have you had your lunch yet?
❌ 易错点：选 A Did you have 虽然也正确但 yet 更常与完成时搭配。

---

**第13题 (C)**
📌 考点：used to do 表"过去常常"。区分 used to do（过去常做）和 be used to doing（习惯于做）。
❌ 易错点：选 D is used to going 表"习惯于坐公交"，但句意"现在骑自行车了"说明过去是坐公交→ used to go。

---

**第14题 (B)**
📌 考点：过去完成时 + 延续动词。when I got there 为过去参照，会议在到达前已进行半小时。begin 短暂→ be on 延续→ had been on。
❌ 易错点：选 A had begun 不能与 for half an hour 连用。

---

**第15题 (C)**
📌 考点：never 用于完成时表"从不"。"她从未去过欧洲"用 has never been to。
❌ 易错点：选 A already 用于肯定句；选 B ever 用于疑问句。

---

**第16题 (B)**
📌 考点：in the past ten years 标志词→现在完成时。变化"已发生"用 have taken place。
❌ 易错点：选 A took place 一般过去时与 in the past ten years 不搭；take place 无被动语态，选 C/D 错误。

---

**第17题 (B)**
📌 考点：have gone to 表"去了（不在这里）"。Tom 不在家→去了图书馆（还没回来）。
❌ 易错点：选 A has been 表"去过已回来"，与"不在家"矛盾。

---

**第18题 (B)**
📌 考点：短暂动词转延续。for twenty years 表持续，leave 短暂→ be away，用 has been away。
❌ 易错点：选 D has gone 短暂动词不能与 for 连用。

---

**第19题 (B)**
📌 考点：现在完成时表结果。归还了书→结果是现在没有了。has returned 强调对现在的影响。
❌ 易错点：选 A returned 一般过去时只陈述事实，不强调"现在没有"的结果。

---

**第20题 (C)**
📌 考点：since + 过去时间 → 现在完成时。"自从2018年相识以来一直是朋友"，friends 为状态，用 have been。
❌ 易错点：选 B have made 不能与 since 持续搭配；make friends 是短暂动作，be friends 是持续状态。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：完成时态三大核心——① **现在完成时**：have/has done，标志词 since/for/already/yet/ever/never，强调对现在的影响或持续；② **过去完成时**：had done，表"过去的过去"，标志词 by the time/before + 过去时；③ **短暂→延续转换**是中考必考陷阱：buy→have, join→be in, die→be dead, leave→be away, begin→be on, borrow→keep。牢记 **have been to（去过）/ have gone to（去了）/ have been in（待在）**三者区别！💪
