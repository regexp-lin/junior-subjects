# 复合句变简单句——句式"瘦身"大作战 (Sentence Compression: Complex to Simple)

> 日期：2026年08月10日 | 语法分类：句法转换 | 难度：中等

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 什么是句式转换？

把**结构复杂的复合句**（含从句）转换成**结构简洁的简单句**，让句子更精练，是中考常考的句法能力。

| 转换类型 | 复合句 | 简单句 |
|----------|--------|--------|
| 定语从句→分词 | The boy who is reading is Tom. | The boy **reading** is Tom. |
| 状语从句→介词短语 | When he heard the news, he was happy. | **On hearing** the news, he was happy. |
| 宾语从句→不定式 | I hope that I can see you soon. | I hope **to see** you soon. |

---

### 🎯 转换方法详解

#### ① 定语从句 → 分词短语

| 从句类型 | 转换规则 | 例句 |
|----------|----------|------|
| **主动关系** | 去掉关系词+be，保留 V-ing | The man who is standing there is my father. → The man **standing there** is my father. |
| **被动关系** | 去掉关系词+be，保留 V-ed | The book which was written by him is popular. → The book **written by him** is popular. |

**注意**：定语从句的谓语是**进行时**或**一般时主动**→现在分词；是**被动语态**→过去分词。

---

#### ② 状语从句 → 介词短语/分词

| 状语类型 | 转换方法 | 例句 |
|----------|----------|------|
| 时间状语 | when/after + 主语+V → on/V-ing | When he arrived, he called me. → **On arriving**, he called me. |
| 原因状语 | because + 从句 → because of + 名词 | Because he was ill, he stayed home. → **Because of his illness**, he stayed home. |
| 条件状语 | if + 从句 → 介词短语 | If there is no water, we can't live. → **Without water**, we can't live. |

---

#### ③ 宾语从句 → 不定式/动名词

| 从句类型 | 转换方法 | 例句 |
|----------|----------|------|
| hope/wish + that 从句 | → 不定式 | I hope that I will pass the exam. → I hope **to pass** the exam. |
| suggest + that 从句 | → suggest + 动名词 | He suggested that we (should) leave. → He suggested **leaving**. |
| 疑问词 + to do | 简化宾语从句 | I don't know what I should do. → I don't know **what to do**. |

---

#### ④ 其他常见转换

| 复合句 | 简单句 |
|--------|--------|
| so...that 可以/不能 | too...to / enough to |
| The box is so heavy that I can't lift it. | The box is **too heavy to lift**. |
| 目的状语从句 | 不定式 |
| He came here so that he could see you. | He came here **to see you**. |

---

### ⚡ 转换要点速记

| 原结构 | 转换为 | 关键 |
|--------|--------|------|
| 定语从句（主动） | 现在分词 doing | 主语与动词主动关系 |
| 定语从句（被动） | 过去分词 done | 主语与动词被动关系 |
| when + 主谓 | on + doing | 主语一致可省略 |
| because + 从句 | because of + n. | 动词变名词 |
| so...that 否定 | too...to | 表"太……而不能" |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

1. The girl who is singing on the stage is my sister.
   Which has the same meaning?
   A. The girl sang on the stage is my sister.
   B. The girl singing on the stage is my sister.
   C. The girl to sing on the stage is my sister.
   D. The girl sings on the stage is my sister.

2. The problem that was discussed at the meeting is very important.
   Which is the correct simple form?
   A. The problem discussing at the meeting is very important.
   B. The problem discussed at the meeting is very important.
   C. The problem to discuss at the meeting is very important.
   D. The problem discuss at the meeting is very important.

3. When he heard the news, he was very excited.
   Which has the same meaning?
   A. Heard the news, he was very excited.
   B. On hearing the news, he was very excited.
   C. To hear the news, he was very excited.
   D. Hearing the news, he was very excited. (需注意逻辑主语)

4. Because she was ill, she didn't come to school.
   Which is the correct simple form?
   A. Because of ill, she didn't come to school.
   B. Because of her illness, she didn't come to school.
   C. For her ill, she didn't come to school.
   D. Due to she was ill, she didn't come to school.

5. I hope that I can see you again soon.
   Which has the same meaning?
   A. I hope seeing you again soon.
   B. I hope to see you again soon.
   C. I hope see you again soon.
   D. I hope for seeing you again soon.

6. The man who is talking to the teacher is our headmaster.
   Which is the correct simple form?
   A. The man talked to the teacher is our headmaster.
   B. The man talking to the teacher is our headmaster.
   C. The man to talk to the teacher is our headmaster.
   D. The man talks to the teacher is our headmaster.

7. The house that was built last year is for sale.
   Which has the same meaning?
   A. The house building last year is for sale.
   B. The house built last year is for sale.
   C. The house to build last year is for sale.
   D. The house build last year is for sale.

8. He came here so that he could help us.
   Which is the correct simple form?
   A. He came here for helping us.
   B. He came here to help us.
   C. He came here so as helping us.
   D. He came here for to help us.

9. The book that was written by Lu Xun is famous.
   Which has the same meaning?
   A. The book writing by Lu Xun is famous.
   B. The book written by Lu Xun is famous.
   C. The book to write by Lu Xun is famous.
   D. The book wrote by Lu Xun is famous.

10. The box is so heavy that I can't carry it.
    Which has the same meaning?
    A. The box is too heavy to carry it.
    B. The box is too heavy to carry.
    C. The box is very heavy that I can't carry.
    D. The box is heavy enough to carry.

11. I don't know what I should do next.
    Which is the correct simple form?
    A. I don't know what to do next.
    B. I don't know what do next.
    C. I don't know how to do next.
    D. I don't know to do what next.

12. The boy who is running in the playground is Tom.
    Which has the same meaning?
    A. The boy ran in the playground is Tom.
    B. The boy running in the playground is Tom.
    C. The boy to run in the playground is Tom.
    D. The boy run in the playground is Tom.

13. If there is no sunlight, plants cannot grow.
    Which is the correct simple form?
    A. If no sunlight, plants cannot grow.
    B. Without sunlight, plants cannot grow.
    C. With no sunlight, plants cannot grow.
    D. Not having sunlight, plants cannot grow.

14. She suggested that we (should) start early.
    Which has the same meaning?
    A. She suggested to start early.
    B. She suggested starting early.
    C. She suggested start early.
    D. She suggested for starting early.

15. The window that was broken by the boy has been repaired.
    Which is the correct simple form?
    A. The window breaking by the boy has been repaired.

    C. The window to break by the boy has been repaired.
    D. The window broke by the boy has been repaired.

16. He spoke so fast that we couldn't understand him.
    Which has the same meaning?
    A. He spoke too fast to understand him.
    B. He spoke too fast for us to understand.

17. The students who are from Beijing are very friendly.

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (B)**
📌 定语从句 who is singing（主动、进行）→ 去掉 who is，保留**现在分词 singing**。
❌ A sang 是过去式，不能作定语；C to sing 表将来/目的；D 缺谓语，句子不完整。

---

**第2题 (B)**
📌 定语从句 that was discussed（被动）→ 去掉 that was，保留**过去分词 discussed**。
❌ A discussing 表主动；C to discuss 表目的/将来；D discuss 是动词原形。

---

**第3题 (B)**
📌 when he heard the news = 当他听到消息时 → **On hearing the news**（on + 动名词表"一……就"）。
❌ A 缺少介词，且 Heard 分词作状语时主语需一致；D Hearing 也可，但 B 的 on hearing 更地道表"一……就"；C to hear 表目的。

---

**第4题 (B)**
📌 because she was ill → **because of her illness**（because of + 名词短语）。
❌ A ill 是形容词，because of 后接名词；C for 表原因时用法不同；D due to 后接名词不接从句。

---

**第5题 (B)**
📌 hope that I can see → **hope to see**（hope + 不定式）。
❌ A hope 不接动名词；C hope 后接不定式 to do；D 无此搭配。

---

**第6题 (B)**
📌 who is talking（主动、进行）→ **talking**。
❌ A talked 表被动/完成；C to talk 表目的；D 缺谓语。

---

**第7题 (B)**
📌 that was built（被动）→ **built**。
❌ A building 表主动；C to build 表目的；D build 是动词原形。

---

**第8题 (B)**
📌 so that he could help us（目的状语从句）→ **to help us**（不定式表目的）。
❌ A for helping 应用 for 表用途时接名词；C so as 后接 to do；D for to 语法错误。

---

**第9题 (B)**
📌 that was written by（被动）→ **written by**。
❌ A writing 表主动；C to write 表目的；D wrote 是过去式，不能直接作定语。

---

**第10题 (B)**
📌 so...that I can't carry it → **too...to carry**。注意：carry 后的 it 必须省略（与主语 the box 重复）。
❌ A 多了 it；C very...that 搭配错误；D enough to 表"足够能"，与句意相反。

---

**第11题 (A)**
📌 what I should do → **what to do**（疑问词 + 不定式）。
❌ B 缺少 to；C how to do 后缺宾语；D 语序错误。

---

**第12题 (B)**
📌 who is running（主动、进行）→ **running**。
❌ A ran 不能作前置/后置定语表持续；C to run 表目的；D run 是动词原形。

---

**第13题 (B)**
📌 If there is no sunlight → **Without sunlight**（without + 名词）。
❌ A if 后缺谓语；C with no 与 without 意思不同；D 结构不自然。

---

**第14题 (B)**
📌 suggested that we (should) start → **suggested starting**（suggest + 动名词）。
❌ A suggest 不接不定式；C suggest 后接 doing 或 that 从句；D 无此搭配。

---

**第15题 (B)**
📌 that was broken by（被动）→ **broken by**。
❌ A breaking 表主动；C to break 表目的；D broke 不能作定语。

---

**第16题 (C)**
📌 so fast that we couldn't understand him → **too fast for us to understand him**。注意：understand 后宾语 him 需保留（与主语 he 不同）。
❌ A us 省略后 understand 缺宾语；B understand 后缺 him；D very...that 错误。

---

**第17题 (D)**
📌 who are from Beijing 可转换为 **from Beijing**（介词短语）或 **coming from Beijing**（分词短语）。
❌ A、C 都对；B 结构重复错误。

---

**第18题 (A)**
📌 After he finished → **After finishing**（after + 动名词，主语一致可省）。
❌ B Finishing 作状语时表"一边……一边"或因果，与原意"之后"略有不同；C to finish 表目的；D Finished 表被动/完成，不符。

---

**第19题 (B)**
📌 how I can get to → **how to get to**（疑问词 + 不定式）。
❌ A 缺 to；C 缺疑问词；D wonder 不接动名词。

---

**第20题 (B)**
📌 which was received（被动）→ **received**。
❌ A receiving 表主动；C to receive 表目的；D receive 是动词原形。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：句式"瘦身"三字诀——**定从看主被动**（主动用 doing，被动用 done）→ **状从看类型**（时间用 on doing，原因用 because of + n.，目的用 to do）→ **宾从看动词**（hope 接 to do，suggest 接 doing，疑问词 + to do）！💪
