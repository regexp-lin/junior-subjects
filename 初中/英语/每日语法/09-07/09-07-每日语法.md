# 🔄 主角与配角的互换——被动语态全攻略 (Passive Voice)

> 日期：2026年09月07日 | 语法分类：语态 | 难度：中等偏难

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 什么是被动语态？

英语有两种语态：**主动语态**（主语是动作执行者）和**被动语态**（主语是动作承受者）。

- 主动：Tom **broke** the window.（Tom 打破了窗户）
- 被动：The window **was broken** (by Tom).（窗户被打破了）

当我们强调**动作承受者**、不知道/不需要说明执行者时，用被动语态。

---

### 📋 被动语态的结构——be + 过去分词(done)

| 时态 | 被动结构 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| **一般现在时** | am/is/are + done | English **is spoken** in many countries. |
| **一般过去时** | was/were + done | The bridge **was built** in 2010. |
| **一般将来时** | will be + done | A new school **will be built** next year. |
| **现在进行时** | am/is/are + being + done | The road **is being repaired** now. |
| **现在完成时** | have/has + been + done | The work **has been finished**. |
| **含情态动词** | can/must/should... + be + done | Trees **should be planted** in spring. |

---

### 📋 主动变被动三步法

| 步骤 | 操作 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| **第一步** | 主动句宾语 → 被动句主语 | Tom cleans **the room**. → **The room**... |
| **第二步** | 主动句谓语 → be + done | cleans → **is cleaned** |
| **第三步** | 主动句主语 → by + 宾格（可省略） | ...(**by Tom**). |

完整转换：Tom cleans the room every day. → The room **is cleaned** (by Tom) every day.

---

### 📋 特殊被动结构

| 类型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **双宾语** | 间接宾语或直接宾语作主语 | He gave **me** a book. → **I** was given a book. / **A book** was given to me. |
| **省略 to 的不定式** | 被动中恢复 to | I saw him **cross** the road. → He was seen **to cross** the road. |
| **make/let/have sb do** | 被动中 → be made to do | They made him **work**. → He was made **to work**. |

---

### 📋 主动表被动——中考必考

| 类型 | 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| **sell/read/write/wash + well/easily** | The book **sells** well. | 表事物自身特征 |
| **need/want/require + doing** | The window **needs cleaning**. | = needs to be cleaned |
| **系动词 + adj** | The food **tastes** delicious. | taste/smell/feel/look/sound |

---

### 📋 不能用被动语态的动词

| 动词 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **happen / take place** | 发生（无宾语） | The accident **happened** yesterday. ❌ was happened |
| **belong to** | 属于 | This book **belongs to** me. ❌ is belonged to |
| **不及物动词** | appear, rise, fall, die, arrive 等 | 不能变被动 |

---

### ⚠️ 中考高频陷阱

| 陷阱 | 正解 |
|------|------|
| The book is sold well. | The book **sells** well.（主动表被动） |
| The accident was happened yesterday. | The accident **happened** yesterday.（happen 无被动） |
| He was made work hard. | He was made **to work** hard.（make 被动恢复 to） |
| The food is tasted delicious. | The food **tastes** delicious.（系动词无被动） |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

*全面覆盖被动语态各时态、主动变被动、主动表被动、特殊结构。*

1. English _____ in many countries around the world.
   A. speaks    B. is spoken    C. spoke    D. is speaking

2. A new library _____ in our school last year.
   A. built    B. was built    C. is built    D. will be built

3. Look! The children _____ how to use computers by their teacher.
   A. are taught    B. are being taught    C. have been taught    D. teach

4. This song _____ by young people. They love it.
   A. is loved    B. loves    C. was loved    D. is loving

5. The window _____ by Tom yesterday. He was very careless.
   A. broke    B. is broken    C. was broken    D. has broken

6. Trees _____ in spring every year.
   A. should plant    B. should be planted    C. should planted    D. should be planting

7. The TV set _____ in Japan. It's very popular here.
   A. is making    B. was made    C. makes    D. is made

8. The sweater _____ well because it is very comfortable.
   A. sells    B. is sold    C. sold    D. was sold

9. He was seen _____ the classroom just now.
   A. enter    B. to enter    C. entering    D. entered

10. The children were made _____ the room every day by their mother.
    A. clean    B. to clean    C. cleaning    D. cleaned

11. A lot of trees _____ along the road so far.
    A. have planted    B. have been planted    C. are planted    D. planted

12. The food _____ delicious. Would you like some?
    A. is tasted    B. tastes    C. is tasting    D. tasted

13. Many houses _____ in the earthquake, but they _____ already.
    A. destroyed; have rebuilt    B. were destroyed; have been rebuilt    C. have destroyed; rebuilt    D. destroyed; were rebuilt

14. The problem must _____ at once.
    A. solve    B. solved    C. be solved    D. is solved

15. The meeting _____ next Monday.
    A. will hold    B. will be held    C. held    D. is holding

16. When _____ the telephone _____?
    A. is; invent    B. was; invented    C. did; invent    D. has; invented

17. Cotton _____ in the south of China.
    A. grows    B. is grown    C. is growing    D. grew

18. The Great Wall _____ by millions of tourists every year.
    A. visit    B. visits    C. is visited    D. was visited

19. The accident _____ on a cold morning last winter.
    A. was happened    B. happened    C. is happened    D. has happened

20. —Can the work _____ in two days? —I'm afraid not.
    A. finish    B. be finished    C. finished    D. is finished

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般现在时被动。英语"被说"，主语 English 是承受者，用 is spoken。
❌ 易错点：选 A speaks 主动语态，但英语是"被说"的，不是自己说。

---

**第2题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般过去时被动。图书馆"被建造"，last year 表过去，用 was built。
❌ 易错点：选 A built 无 be 动词，不能构成被动；选 C/D 时态不符。

---

**第3题 (B)**
📌 考点：现在进行时被动。Look! 提示正在进行，孩子们"正在被教"，用 are being taught。
❌ 易错点：选 A are taught 一般现在时被动，缺少"正在"的含义。

---

**第4题 (A)**
📌 考点：一般现在时被动。这首歌"被年轻人喜爱"，用 is loved。
❌ 易错点：选 B loves 主动语态，但歌曲是"被喜爱"的。

---

**第5题 (C)**
📌 考点：一般过去时被动。窗户"被打碎"，yesterday 表过去，用 was broken。
❌ 易错点：选 A broke 主动语态，主语是 window（承受者），需被动。

---

**第6题 (B)**
📌 考点：含情态动词的被动。"树应该被种植"，should + be + done = should be planted。
❌ 易错点：选 A should plant 主动语态，但树是"被种"的。

---

**第7题 (D)**
📌 考点：一般现在时被动。电视机"是在日本制造的"，表产地特征用一般现在时 is made。
❌ 易错点：选 B was made 过去时也可能正确，但"It's popular here"暗示品牌特征（持续），用一般现在时更佳。

---

**第8题 (A)**
📌 考点：主动表被动。sell well 表"销路好"，用主动形式 sells。
❌ 易错点：选 B is sold 语法正确但不表示"销路好"的习惯用法。

---

**第9题 (B)**
📌 考点：see sb do → sb be seen to do。主动句省略的 to 在被动句中需恢复。
❌ 易错点：选 A enter 在被动句中必须加 to。

---

**第10题 (B)**
📌 考点：make sb do → sb be made to do。被动句中恢复 to，用 to clean。
❌ 易错点：选 A clean 在被动句中必须加 to；make 是特殊的感官/使役动词。

---

**第11题 (B)**
📌 考点：现在完成时被动。so far 提示完成时，树"被种植"用 have been planted。
❌ 易错点：选 A have planted 主动语态，但 trees 是"被种"的。

---

**第12题 (B)**
📌 考点：系动词无被动。taste 作系动词表"尝起来"，用主动形式 tastes delicious。
❌ 易错点：选 A is tasted 错误，系动词 taste/smell/feel/look/sound 不用被动。

---

**第13题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般过去时被动 + 现在完成时被动。房子"被摧毁"（过去）→ were destroyed；"已被重建"（结果）→ have been rebuilt。
❌ 易错点：两个空都需被动语态，因为 houses 是承受者。

---

**第14题 (C)**
📌 考点：情态动词被动。问题"必须被解决"，must + be + done = must be solved。
❌ 易错点：选 D is solved 无法与 must 并用；情态动词后接 be done。

---

**第15题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般将来时被动。会议"将被举行"，next Monday 表将来，用 will be held。
❌ 易错点：选 A will hold 主动语态，但 meeting 是"被举行"的。

---

**第16题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般过去时被动疑问句。"电话是什么时候被发明的？"When was the telephone invented?
❌ 易错点：选 C did invent 主动语态，但 telephone 是"被发明"的。

---

**第17题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般现在时被动。棉花"被种植"在中国南方，用 is grown。
❌ 易错点：选 A grows 可表"生长"（主动），但此处强调"被种植"。注意 grow 作及物动词表"种植"时可用被动。

---

**第18题 (C)**
📌 考点：一般现在时被动。长城"被参观"，every year 表经常性，用 is visited。
❌ 易错点：选 D was visited 过去时与 every year 不符。

---

**第19题 (B)**
📌 考点：happen 无被动语态。"事故发生了"只能用主动 happened。
❌ 易错点：选 A was happened 错误；happen/take place 没有被动语态。

---

**第20题 (B)**
📌 考点：情态动词被动疑问句。Can the work be finished? 情态动词提前，动词用 be done。
❌ 易错点：选 D is finished 无法与 Can 并用；Can + be + done 是固定结构。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：被动语态核心要点——① **结构**：be + done，各时态变化 be 的形式；② **三步法**：宾→主，谓→be done，主→by 宾格；③ **主动表被动**：sell/read/write well，taste/smell/look + adj；④ **特殊变化**：make/see/hear sb do → 被动恢复 to；⑤ **不能被动的动词**：happen, take place, belong to, 系动词。记住：**看主语是执行者还是承受者**来决定用主动还是被动！💪
