# 🗣️ 他说她说——直接引语与间接引语 (Direct & Indirect Speech)

> 日期：2026年09月04日 | 语法分类：句型转换 | 难度：中等偏难

---

## 一、知识点精讲

### 📌 什么是直接引语和间接引语？

- **直接引语**：原封不动地引用别人的话，加引号。→ He said, "I am happy."
- **间接引语**：用自己的话转述别人说的话，不加引号，通常变成宾语从句。→ He said that he was happy.

中考中直接引语变间接引语考查的核心是**三个变化**：时态、人称、时间/地点指示词。

---

### 📋 时态变化规则（主句过去时）

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|----------|----------|
| 一般现在时 am/is/are | 一般过去时 was/were |
| 现在进行时 am/is/are doing | 过去进行时 was/were doing |
| 一般将来时 will/shall | 过去将来时 would/should |
| 现在完成时 have/has done | 过去完成时 had done |
| 一般过去时 did | 过去完成时 had done |
| can / may | could / might |

**注意**：如果主句用**一般现在时**（如 He says...），间接引语时态**不变**。

**不变的情况**：客观真理、自然规律、习惯性动作 → 时态不变。
- He said, "The earth moves around the sun." → He said that the earth **moves** around the sun.

---

### 📋 人称变化规则——"一随主，二随宾，三不变"

| 直接引语中的人称 | 变化规则 | 例子 |
|------------------|----------|------|
| **第一人称 I/we** | 随主句主语变 | He said, "**I** am..." → He said **he** was... |
| **第二人称 you** | 随主句宾语变 | He said to me, "**You** are..." → He told me **I** was... |
| **第三人称 he/she/they** | 不变 | He said, "**She** is..." → He said **she** was... |

---

### 📋 时间/地点指示词变化

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|----------|----------|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| tonight | that night |
| yesterday | the day before |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| this week | that week |
| last year | the year before |
| next month | the next month / the following month |
| here | there |
| this | that |
| these | those |
| ago | before |
| come | go |

---

### 📋 不同句型的转换

| 句型 | 转换方式 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| **陈述句** | said (that)... | He said, "I like it." → He said (that) he liked it. |
| **一般疑问句** | asked if/whether... | He asked, "Are you ready?" → He asked if I was ready. |
| **特殊疑问句** | asked + 疑问词... | He asked, "Where do you live?" → He asked where I lived. |
| **祈使句（肯定）** | told/asked sb to do | He said, "Open the door." → He told me to open the door. |
| **祈使句（否定）** | told/asked sb not to do | He said, "Don't run." → He told me not to run. |

---

### ⚠️ 中考高频陷阱

| 陷阱 | 正解 |
|------|------|
| He asked did I like English. | He asked **if/whether** I liked English.（陈述语序） |
| He asked where did I live. | He asked where I **lived**.（陈述语序 + 时态后退） |
| He said he will come tomorrow. | He said he **would** come **the next day**. |
| She said the sun rose in the east. | She said the sun **rises** in the east.（客观真理不变） |

## 二、专项选择题（共 20 题）

*全面考查时态变化、人称转换、指示词替换、各种句型转化。*

1. He said, "I am writing a letter." → He said that he _____ a letter.
   A. is writing    B. was writing    C. writes    D. wrote

2. She said to me, "Are you from China?" → She asked me _____ from China.
   A. if I am    B. if I was    C. am I    D. was I

3. "Where do you live?" he asked. → He asked me where _____.
   A. do I live    B. did I live    C. I lived    D. I live

4. The teacher said, "The earth goes around the sun." → The teacher said that the earth _____ around the sun.
   A. went    B. goes    C. had gone    D. would go

5. "Don't play near the river," Mother said to us. → Mother told us _____ near the river.
   A. not play    B. not to play    C. don't play    D. to not play

6. He said, "I have finished my homework." → He said that he _____ his homework.
   A. has finished    B. had finished    C. finished    D. would finish

7. She said, "I will come tomorrow." → She said that she _____ come the next day.
   A. will    B. would    C. shall    D. should

8. Jack said to me, "You look very nice today." → Jack told me that _____ looked very nice _____.
   A. I; today    B. I; that day    C. you; today    D. you; that day

9. "Can you help me?" she asked. → She asked me _____ help her.
   A. if I can    B. if I could    C. can I    D. could I

10. He asked, "Did you go to the park yesterday?" → He asked me _____ to the park _____.
    A. if I went; yesterday    B. if I had gone; the day before    C. did I go; yesterday    D. had I gone; the day before

11. "Open the window, please," the teacher said to Tom. → The teacher asked Tom _____ the window.
    A. open    B. opening    C. to open    D. opened

12. He says, "I like swimming." → He says that he _____ swimming.
    A. liked    B. likes    C. has liked    D. would like

13. She said, "I was watching TV at that time." → She said that she _____ TV at that time.
    A. watched    B. was watching    C. had watched    D. had been watching

14. "When will you come back?" Father asked me. → Father asked me when _____ back.
    A. will I come    B. would I come    C. I will come    D. I would come

15. The boy said, "I'm going to visit my grandpa next week." → The boy said he _____ going to visit his grandpa _____.
    A. is; next week    B. was; the next week    C. is; the next week    D. was; next week

16. He asked me _____ I had been to Beijing before.
    A. that    B. what    C. whether    D. where

17. "Please sit down," she said to me. → She asked me _____.
    A. sit down    B. to sit down    C. sitting down    D. sat down

18. She said, "I met Tom here yesterday." → She said that she _____ Tom _____ the day before.
    A. had met; there    B. met; here    C. had met; here    D. met; there

19. He said to me, "I bought this book last week." → He told me that he _____ that book _____.
    A. bought; last week    B. had bought; the week before    C. bought; the week before    D. had bought; last week

20. "How old are you?" the doctor asked the boy. → The doctor asked the boy _____.
    A. how old was he    B. how old he was    C. how old is he    D. how old he is

### 🔍 错因深度解析

**第1题 (B)**
📌 考点：时态后退。直接引语 am writing（现在进行时）→ 间接引语 was writing（过去进行时）。
❌ 易错点：选 A 忘记时态后退；主句 said 为过去时，从句时态需后退一步。

---

**第2题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般疑问句→ if/whether + 陈述语序，时态后退。Are you → if I was。
❌ 易错点：选 C/D 用疑问语序，间接引语必须用陈述语序。

---

**第3题 (C)**
📌 考点：特殊疑问句→疑问词 + 陈述语序，时态后退。Where do you live → where I lived。
❌ 易错点：选 A/B 用疑问语序；间接引语中疑问词后必须是"主语+谓语"。

---

**第4题 (B)**
📌 考点：客观真理不变。"地球绕太阳转"为客观事实，时态保持一般现在时 goes。
❌ 易错点：选 A went 机械执行时态后退规则；客观真理、自然现象的时态不变。

---

**第5题 (B)**
📌 考点：祈使句否定→ told sb not to do。"别在河边玩"→ told us not to play。
❌ 易错点：选 D to not play 否定词位置错误，not 必须在 to 前。

---

**第6题 (B)**
📌 考点：时态后退。have finished（现在完成时）→ had finished（过去完成时）。
❌ 易错点：选 C finished 只后退了一步到过去时，完成时应退为过去完成时。

---

**第7题 (B)**
📌 考点：时态后退。will（一般将来时）→ would（过去将来时）。
❌ 易错点：选 A will 忘记后退；tomorrow 也应变为 the next day。

---

**第8题 (B)**
📌 考点：人称变化 + 指示词变化。you（第二人称，随宾语 me 变为 I）；today 变为 that day。
❌ 易错点：选 A 只改了人称没改时间；选 C/D 人称变化不对。

---

**第9题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般疑问句 + 情态动词后退。Can you → if I could。
❌ 易错点：选 C/D 用疑问语序；间接引语必须用陈述语序。

---

**第10题 (B)**
📌 考点：一般疑问句过去时 → if + 过去完成时。Did you go → if I had gone；yesterday → the day before。
❌ 易错点：选 A 未做时态和指示词变化。

---

**第11题 (C)**
📌 考点：祈使句肯定→ asked sb to do。"请打开窗户"→ asked Tom to open。
❌ 易错点：选 A open 缺少 to；祈使句转换用 tell/ask sb to do。

---

**第12题 (B)**
📌 考点：主句一般现在时（says），从句时态不变。He says → likes 保持原时态。
❌ 易错点：选 A liked 误以为所有间接引语都要后退时态；says 是现在时，不需要后退。

---

**第13题 (D)**
📌 考点：时态后退。was watching（过去进行时）→ had been watching（过去完成进行时）。
❌ 易错点：选 B 不后退时态；过去进行时后退为过去完成进行时。

---

**第14题 (D)**
📌 考点：特殊疑问句 + 陈述语序 + 时态后退。When will you come → when I would come。
❌ 易错点：选 A/B 用疑问语序；间接引语中疑问词后面必须是陈述语序。

---

**第15题 (B)**
📌 考点：时态后退 + 指示词变化。am going → was going；next week → the next week。
❌ 易错点：选 A/C 未做时态后退；选 D 未改时间指示词。

---

**第16题 (C)**
📌 考点：一般疑问句用 whether/if 引导。"你以前是否去过北京"用 whether。
❌ 易错点：选 A that 用于陈述句转换；一般疑问句用 if/whether。

---

**第17题 (B)**
📌 考点：祈使句肯定→ asked sb to do。"请坐"→ asked me to sit down。
❌ 易错点：选 A 缺少 to；请求性祈使句用 ask sb to do。

---

**第18题 (A)**
📌 考点：时态后退 + 指示词变化。met → had met；here → there；yesterday → the day before。
❌ 易错点：选 D 只改了指示词没退时态；选 B/C 指示词未全部变化。

---

**第19题 (B)**
📌 考点：时态后退 + 指示词全变。bought → had bought；this → that；last week → the week before。
❌ 易错点：选 A 完全没变化；选 C 时态未退；选 D 时间指示词未变。

---

**第20题 (B)**
📌 考点：特殊疑问句 + 陈述语序 + 时态后退。How old are you → how old he was。
❌ 易错点：选 A 疑问语序；间接引语用陈述语序"主语+谓语"。

---

🎯 **今日总结**：直接引语变间接引语牢记"三变"——① **变时态**：主句过去时，从句时态后退一步（现在→过去，过去→过去完成，will→would），客观真理不变；② **变人称**：一随主、二随宾、三不变；③ **变指示词**：today→that day, here→there, this→that 等。句型转换：陈述句用 that，一般疑问用 if/whether，特殊疑问保留疑问词，祈使句用 tell/ask sb (not) to do。所有间接引语都用**陈述语序**！💪
