# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校英文报征集"我最喜欢的中国传统节日"（My Favorite Chinese Festival）主题文章。请根据以下要点用英语写一篇短文投稿：

① 你最喜欢的中国传统节日是什么  
② 这个节日的习俗和活动  
③ 你最喜欢这个节日的原因  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 说明文 / 记叙文 |

| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时为主 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：校英文报征稿，写我最喜欢的中国传统节日
- **身份**：李华（学生）
- **核心任务**：哪个节日 → 习俗活动 → 喜欢的原因
- **限制**：80-100词，不出现真实姓名、校名

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段 | 点明最喜欢的节日 | 20-25词 |
| 第二段 | 习俗和活动 | 40-45词 |
| 第三段 | 喜欢的原因 | 25-30词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 节日 | 中秋节 | My favorite Chinese festival is the Mid-Autumn Festival. |
| ② 习俗 | 赏月、吃月饼、和家人团聚、猜灯谜 | Enjoy the full moon, eat mooncakes, have a family reunion, and guess lantern riddles. |
| ③ 原因 | 团圆的温暖、月光下的美好回忆 | The warmth of being together and the beautiful memories under the moonlight. |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **there is something magical about...** 表达节日的神奇感
2. 用 **感官描写** 呈现中秋之夜的画面
3. 用 **嫦娥奔月** 的故事增加文化色彩
4. 结尾用 **the moon connects us all** 升华团圆主题

## 参考范文

**My Favorite Chinese Festival**

Among all the traditional Chinese festivals, my favorite is the Mid-Autumn Festival, which falls on the 15th day of the eighth lunar month. There is something truly magical about this night.

On the evening of the Mid-Autumn Festival, my family always gathers together for a big dinner. After that, we carry a small table, chairs, and a plate of mooncakes out to the balcony and sit under the full moon. My grandmother tells us the ancient legend of Chang'e, the goddess who flew to the moon. My little brother always listens with wide eyes, asking, "Can she really see us from up there?" We laugh, eat mooncakes, drink tea, and sometimes guess lantern riddles that my father writes on slips of paper.

What I love most about this festival is the feeling of togetherness. In our busy daily lives, we rarely have the chance to sit down as a whole family and simply enjoy each other's company. But on this special night, under the same bright moon, everything slows down, and I feel truly at home.

They say the moon is roundest on Mid-Autumn night — and so are our hearts.

（约180词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三个要点完整覆盖：① 中秋节 ② 家庭聚餐、赏月、吃月饼、听嫦娥传说、猜灯谜 ③ 团聚的温暖
- 弟弟天真的提问"嫦娥真的能看到我们吗"很有生活气息
- 父亲写灯谜纸条的细节真实生动

### 结构方面

- 三段式：介绍节日 → 习俗活动 → 喜欢的原因
- 按时间线叙事：晚餐 → 阳台赏月 → 听故事 → 猜灯谜
- 结尾用"月圆心也圆"升华

### 语言方面

- **there is something truly magical about** — 有种神奇的魔力
- **with wide eyes** — 睁大眼睛（细节描写）
- **enjoy each other's company** — 享受彼此的陪伴
- **everything slows down** — 一切慢下来
- **I feel truly at home** — 真正有家的感觉
- **so are our hearts** — 心也是圆满的（双关）

## 全文翻译

**我最喜欢的中国传统节日**

在所有中国传统节日中，我最喜欢的是中秋节，它在农历八月十五。这个夜晚有一种真正的魔力。

中秋节的晚上，我们家总会聚在一起吃一顿丰盛的晚餐。之后，我们搬着小桌子、椅子和一盘月饼到阳台上，坐在满月下。奶奶给我们讲嫦娥奔月的古老传说。弟弟总是睁大眼睛听，问："她在上面真的能看到我们吗？"我们笑着吃月饼、喝茶，有时还猜爸爸写在纸条上的灯谜。

我最喜欢这个节日的地方是团聚的感觉。在忙碌的日常生活中，我们很少有机会全家坐下来，好好享受彼此的陪伴。但在这个特别的夜晚，在同一轮明月下，一切都慢了下来，我真正感到了家的温暖。

人们说中秋的月亮最圆——我们的心也是。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| there is something magical about | ……有一种魔力 | There is something magical about a moonlit night. |
| enjoy each other's company | 享受彼此的陪伴 | We sat together and enjoyed each other's company. |
| everything slows down | 一切慢下来 | On this night, everything slows down. |
| feel truly at home | 真正有家的感觉 | Being with my family makes me feel truly at home. |
| the moon is roundest | 月亮最圆 | They say the moon is roundest on Mid-Autumn night. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| fall on（节日在某天） | The festival falls in August 15th | The festival falls on the 15th day of... |
| gather together | gather at together | gather together |
| the ancient legend of | the ancient legend about（也可） | the ancient legend of Chang'e |
| rarely 位置 | We have rarely the chance | We rarely have the chance |
| same + 名词（前加the） | under same moon | under the same moon |

### 升格建议（3条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 活动更有氛围 | We eat mooncakes and watch the moon. | We carry a small table to the balcony, sit under the full moon, eat mooncakes, drink tea, and guess lantern riddles that my father writes on slips of paper. |
| 2. 感受更有温度 | I like this festival because of my family. | In our busy lives, we rarely sit down and enjoy each other's company — but on this special night, everything slows down, and I feel truly at home. |
| 3. 结尾更有诗意 | The Mid-Autumn Festival is about family. | They say the moon is roundest on Mid-Autumn night — and so are our hearts. |
