# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你们学校英文报正在征集关于网络学习的讨论文章。请根据以下要点写一篇短文投稿：

① 网络学习的好处  
② 网络学习的不足  
③ 你的看法和建议  

**注意：** 词数 80–100；不得出现真实姓名和校名；围绕网络学习利弊展开讨论。

## 写作思考

### 一、审题

- 情境：学校英文报征文，讨论网络学习
- 要点：利 / 弊 / 看法与建议
- 重点：对比利弊，表达自己的观点并给出建议

### 二、结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 开头 | 引出话题：网络学习普及，有人支持有人担心 | 约 25 词 |
| 主体 | 利：方便、资源丰富、可反复学习等 | 约 35 词 |
| 主体 | 弊：缺乏互动、易分心、伤眼等 | 约 25 词 |
| 结尾 | 看法与建议：合理利用、注意自律等 | 约 15 词 |

### 三、表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达 |
|------|------|----------|
| ① 网络学习的好处 | 灵活方便、资源丰富、可回放 | convenient, rich resources, can review anytime |
| ② 网络学习的不足 | 缺少面对面交流、易 distracted、伤眼 | lack face-to-face interaction, easily distracted, harm eyes |
| ③ 看法和建议 | 合理利用、自律、注意护眼 | use wisely, self-discipline, protect eyes |

### 四、可加分亮点（4 条）

1. 使用过渡词：on the one hand...on the other hand / however / in my opinion
2. 适度使用从句：which makes it..., so that we can...
3. 提出具体建议：set a study schedule / take breaks every 30 minutes
4. 结尾总结有力：with proper use, online learning can be very helpful

---

## 参考范文（约 150–200 词）

**Online Learning: Advantages and Disadvantages**

Online learning has become increasingly popular, especially in recent years. Different people hold different views about it. Here is my discussion.

On the one hand, online learning has many advantages. First, it is very convenient. We can study at any time and anywhere as long as we have a computer or a phone. Second, there are rich learning resources on the Internet. We can find videos, articles, and exercises for almost every subject. Third, we can pause, rewind, and replay the lessons as many times as we need, which helps us understand difficult topics better.

On the other hand, online learning also has some disadvantages. It lacks face-to-face interaction with teachers and classmates, so we may feel lonely sometimes. Besides, we can easily be distracted by games, social media, or other things. What's more, staring at the screen for too long may harm our eyes.

In my opinion, online learning is useful if we use it wisely. I suggest we set a study schedule, avoid distractions, and take a break every 30 minutes to protect our eyes. With proper self-discipline, online learning can be very helpful for our study.

| 维度 | 亮点说明 |
|------|----------|
| **内容** | 三个要点均有覆盖，利（convenient, rich resources, replay）与弊（lack interaction, distracted, harm eyes）对比清晰，结尾给出具体建议 |
| **结构** | 总分总结构清楚：引入 → 利 → 弊 → 看法与建议；使用 On the one hand...On the other hand 和 In my opinion 过渡自然 |

## 全文翻译

**网络学习：利与弊**

网络学习近年来越来越流行。不同的人对此有不同的看法。以下是我的讨论。

一方面，网络学习有很多优点。首先，它非常方便。只要有电脑或手机，我们就可以随时随地学习。其次，网络上学习资源丰富，几乎每个科目都能找到视频、文章和练习题。第三，我们可以根据需要多次暂停、回放和重看课程，这有助于我们更好地理解难点。

另一方面，网络学习也有一些不足。它缺少与老师和同学面对面的交流，所以我们有时会感到孤单。此外，我们容易受到游戏、社交媒体等事物的干扰。再者，长时间盯着屏幕可能损害视力。

在我看来，如果我们合理利用，网络学习是有用的。我建议制定学习计划、避免分心，并每 30 分钟休息以保护眼睛。只要自律得当，网络学习对我们的学习会很有帮助。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5 行表格）

| 表达 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| convenient | 方便的 | Online learning is convenient for busy students. |
| rich resources | 丰富资源 | The Internet offers rich resources for learning. |
| face-to-face | 面对面的 | We miss face-to-face interaction in online classes. |
| be distracted | 分心 | It's easy to be distracted when studying online. |
| self-discipline | 自律 | Self-discipline is important in online learning. |

### 易错点（5 个 ✅❌ 对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误示例 | ✅ 正确示例 |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| 主谓一致 | Online learning have many advantages. | Online learning **has** many advantages. |
| 不可数用法 | We need to avoid many distractions. | We need to avoid **being** distracted. |
| 动词搭配 | We should pay attention **at** our eyes. | We should pay attention **to** our eyes. |
| 时态混用 | Online learning became popular. (单独陈述事实) | Online learning **has become** popular. |
| 连接词 | It is convenient, and we can study anywhere. | It is convenient **because** we can study anywhere. |

### 升格建议（3 条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 1. 用定语从句丰富表达 | 避免简单句堆砌，用 which / who 扩展 | Online learning allows us to review lessons anytime, **which is helpful for weak students**. |
| 2. 用过渡词增强逻辑 | 使段落间衔接自然 | **To sum up**, online learning has both advantages and disadvantages. / **Therefore**, we should use it wisely. |
| 3. 建议部分更具体 | 不只说“要自律”，可给出具体做法 | **Instead of** studying for hours without breaks, we should take a 5–10 minute rest every 30 minutes. |
