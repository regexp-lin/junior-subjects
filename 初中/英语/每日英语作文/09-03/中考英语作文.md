# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校英文报正在举办"保护环境，从我做起"（Protecting the Environment Starts with Me）主题征文。请根据以下要点用英语写一篇短文投稿：

① 目前存在的环境问题（至少两个）  
② 你在日常生活中是怎样保护环境的  
③ 呼吁同学们一起行动  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：学校英文报征文，主题为环保
- **身份**：李华（学生）
- **核心任务**：指出问题 → 说明做法 → 发出呼吁
- **限制**：80-100词，不出现真实姓名、校名

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段 | 引出话题 + 环境问题 | 30-35词 |
| 第二段 | 自己的环保做法 | 30-35词 |
| 第三段 | 呼吁同学们行动 | 20-30词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 环境问题 | 空气污染严重、河流被垃圾污染、树木被砍伐 | Air pollution is getting worse; rivers are polluted by rubbish; trees are being cut down. |
| ② 我的做法 | 骑自行车上学、随手关灯、不使用一次性筷子 | Ride a bike to school, turn off the lights when leaving, refuse disposable chopsticks. |
| ③ 呼吁 | 从小事做起，每个人都能改变世界 | Start from small things; everyone can make a difference. |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **it's high time that...** 表达"是时候……"
2. 用 **not only...but also...** 连接两个环保做法
3. 用被动语态描述环境问题（rivers are polluted）
4. 结尾用 **make a difference** 体现环保理念

## 参考范文

**Protecting the Environment Starts with Me**

Nowadays, environmental problems are becoming more and more serious. The air is polluted by factory smoke and car exhaust. Rivers and lakes are full of rubbish. Many trees are being cut down, which makes the land dry and bare. It's high time that we did something to protect our planet.

As a student, I try my best to be "green" in daily life. I ride my bike to school instead of taking a car. I always turn off the lights and the tap when I don't need them. Besides, I use my own chopsticks and water bottle to reduce waste.

Small actions can make a big difference. If everyone does a little, the world will become much more beautiful. Let's join hands and protect our home together!

（约120词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三个要点完整覆盖：① 环境问题（空气污染、水污染、树木砍伐）② 个人做法（骑车、关灯关水龙头、自带餐具）③ 呼吁（join hands）
- 问题有三个具体例子，做法也有三个具体行动
- 结尾号召有力

### 结构方面

- 三段式：问题 → 做法 → 呼吁
- 用 **besides** 衔接环保做法
- 用 **if everyone does a little** 引出结论

### 语言方面

- **more and more serious** — 比较级表示趋势
- **it's high time that we did** — 虚拟语气（加分项）
- **instead of taking a car** — instead of + doing
- **make a big difference** — 地道表达
- 被动语态运用自然：**is polluted**、**are being cut down**

## 全文翻译

**保护环境从我做起**

如今，环境问题变得越来越严重。空气被工厂烟雾和汽车尾气污染。河流和湖泊里满是垃圾。许多树木正在被砍伐，这使得土地干燥而荒芜。是时候我们该做些什么来保护我们的地球了。

作为一名学生，我在日常生活中尽量做到"绿色"。我骑自行车上学，而不是坐车。我总是在不需要的时候关灯和关水龙头。此外，我使用自己的筷子和水杯来减少浪费。

小小的行动能带来大大的改变。如果每个人都做一点点，世界将变得更加美好。让我们携手保护我们的家园吧！

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| more and more serious | 越来越严重 | Environmental problems are becoming more and more serious. |
| it's high time that... | 是时候…… | It's high time that we took action to protect the environment. |
| instead of | 代替，而不是 | I ride my bike instead of taking a car. |
| make a difference | 有影响，起作用 | Small actions can make a big difference. |
| join hands | 携手，合作 | Let's join hands and protect our home together. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| more and more 用法 | The problem is more and more serious | The problem is becoming more and more serious |
| it's time that 虚拟语气 | It's high time that we do something | It's high time that we did something |
| instead of 后接动名词 | I walk instead of take a bus | I walk instead of taking a bus |
| protect 的宾语 | We should protect | We should protect the environment |
| pollution 不可数 | There are many pollutions | There is much pollution |

### 升格建议（3条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 问题更具画面感 | Rivers are dirty. | Rivers that once sparkled in the sunlight are now full of floating rubbish. |
| 2. 做法加入感受 | I turn off the lights. | Every time I turn off the lights before leaving, I feel I'm doing my small part for the Earth. |
| 3. 呼吁更有力 | Let's protect the environment. | Let's join hands — because the Earth is not just where we live, but the home we leave to the next generation. |
