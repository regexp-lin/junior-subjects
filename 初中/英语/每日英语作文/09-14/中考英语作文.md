# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校英文报征集"如何交朋友"（How to Make Friends）主题文章。请根据以下要点用英语写一篇短文投稿：

① 朋友对你来说意味着什么  
② 你认为交到好朋友的方法（至少两条）  
③ 你和好朋友之间的一个小故事  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 议论文 + 记叙文 |
| 话题 | 如何交朋友（How to Make Friends） |
| 人称 | 第一人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时 + 一般过去时（故事） |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：校英文报征稿，写如何交朋友
- **身份**：李华（有好朋友的学生）
- **核心任务**：朋友的意义 → 交友方法 → 友谊故事
- **限制**：80-100词，不出现真实姓名、校名

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段 | 朋友的意义 | 20-25词 |
| 第二段 | 交友方法 | 30-35词 |
| 第三段 | 一个小故事 + 总结 | 30-40词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 朋友的意义 | 朋友是生活中不可缺少的，好朋友让生活更美好 | Friends are an important part of our life; good friends make life better. |
| ② 交友方法 | 真诚待人、学会倾听、乐于助人 | Be honest, be a good listener, and be willing to help others. |
| ③ 小故事 | 我生病时好朋友每天来帮我补课 | When I was ill, my best friend came to my home every day to help me with my lessons. |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **a true friend is someone who...** 定义真正的朋友
2. 用 **in my opinion** 引出观点
3. 用 **not only...but also...** 连接交友方法
4. 用名言 "A friend in need is a friend indeed" 点睛

## 参考范文

**How to Make Friends**

As the saying goes, "A friend in need is a friend indeed." Friends play an important role in our lives. A true friend can share your happiness and help you through difficult times.

So how can we make good friends? In my opinion, first, we should be honest and kind. Nobody likes a person who often tells lies. Second, learn to listen. When your friends talk to you about their problems, listen carefully and show that you care. Third, be willing to help others without expecting anything in return.

I'm lucky to have a best friend named Xiao Ming. Last year, I was ill and had to stay home for two weeks. He came to my house every day after school to bring me notes and explain what I had missed. His kindness touched me deeply. That's when I truly understood what friendship means.

A good friend is like a mirror — they help you see the best in yourself.

（约150词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三个要点完整覆盖：① 朋友的意义 ② 三条交友方法（真诚、倾听、乐于助人）③ 故事（生病时好友帮补课）
- 开头用名言引入，直击主题
- 故事真实具体，结尾比喻新颖

### 结构方面

- 三段式：意义 → 方法 → 故事
- 用 **first, second, third** 有条理
- 结尾用比喻（friend is like a mirror）升华

### 语言方面

- **A friend in need is a friend indeed** — 经典谚语
- **play an important role in** — 起重要作用
- **without expecting anything in return** — 不求回报
- **a good friend is like a mirror** — 比喻修辞
- 定语从句（who often tells lies）运用自如

## 全文翻译

**如何交朋友**

正如那句话说的："患难见真情。"朋友在我们的生活中起着重要作用。真正的朋友能分享你的快乐，帮助你度过困难时期。

那我们怎样才能交到好朋友呢？在我看来，首先要真诚和善良。没有人喜欢经常说谎的人。其次，学会倾听。当朋友向你倾诉烦恼时，认真听并表现出关心。第三，乐于助人，不求回报。

我很幸运有一个好朋友叫小明。去年我生病了，不得不在家休息两周。他每天放学后来我家，给我带课堂笔记并讲解我落下的功课。他的善良深深打动了我。那时我才真正理解了友谊的含义。

好朋友就像一面镜子——他们帮助你看到最好的自己。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| A friend in need is a friend indeed | 患难见真情 | True friendship is tested in hard times. |
| play an important role in | 在……中起重要作用 | Friends play an important role in our lives. |
| be willing to | 愿意做某事 | We should be willing to help others. |
| without expecting anything in return | 不求回报 | A true friend helps you without expecting anything in return. |
| A good friend is like a mirror | 好朋友像一面镜子 | A good friend reflects the best version of yourself. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| play a role in 介词 | play an important role at our lives | play an important role in our lives |
| be willing to 后接原形 | be willing to helping others | be willing to help others |
| without 后接动名词 | without to expect anything | without expecting anything |
| tell lies 固定搭配 | say lies / speak lies | tell lies |
| touch 的过去式 | His kindness touch me | His kindness touched me |

### 升格建议（3条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 定义更有深度 | Friends are important. | A true friend is someone who stays when everyone else leaves, and believes in you when you've stopped believing in yourself. |
| 2. 故事更有细节 | He helped me with my homework. | Every evening, he sat at my bedside with a notebook in hand, patiently explaining every question I had missed. |
| 3. 结尾更有韵味 | Good friends are great. | A good friend is like a mirror — they don't change who you are, but they help you see the best in yourself. |
