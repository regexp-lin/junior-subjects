# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校英文报征集"我最喜欢的季节"（My Favorite Season）主题文章。请根据以下要点用英语写一篇短文投稿：

① 你最喜欢哪个季节  
② 这个季节有什么特点，你喜欢做什么  
③ 这个季节带给你什么感受  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：校英文报征稿，写我最喜欢的季节
- **身份**：李华（学生）
- **核心任务**：最喜欢的季节 → 特点及活动 → 感受
- **限制**：80-100词，不出现真实姓名、校名

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段 | 最喜欢哪个季节及简要原因 | 25-30词 |
| 第二段 | 季节特点、喜欢的活动 | 40-45词 |
| 第三段 | 带给自己的感受 | 20-25词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 最喜欢的季节 | 秋天，不冷不热，景色最美 | Autumn is my favorite — it's neither too hot nor too cold, and the scenery is at its best. |
| ② 特点和活动 | 叶子变黄变红、果实成熟，喜欢爬山、采枫叶 | Leaves turn yellow and red; fruits ripen. I love climbing mountains and collecting maple leaves. |
| ③ 感受 | 感觉平静、充满希望 | It makes me feel peaceful and full of hope. |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **when it comes to...** 引出话题
2. 用 **the air is crisp** 等感官描写增强画面感
3. 用 **as if the whole world is painted in gold** 比喻
4. 结尾用 **a gift from nature** 升华

## 参考范文

**My Favorite Season**

When it comes to my favorite season, autumn wins without doubt. It's neither too hot nor too cold — the air is crisp, the sky is clear, and nature seems to put on its most beautiful dress before winter arrives.

What I love most about autumn is the changing colors. Leaves turn from green to golden yellow and deep red, as if the whole world is painted in warm colors. In my city, there's a park famous for its maple trees. Every weekend, I go there to take a walk, collect fallen leaves for my scrapbook, and sometimes climb the small hills to see the sunset. The smell of ripe fruits from nearby orchards fills the air, and it always reminds me of the Mid-Autumn Festival — the time when my family gets together to enjoy mooncakes under the full moon.

Autumn makes me feel peaceful and hopeful. It teaches me that change can be beautiful — just like the leaves that fall to make room for new growth in spring. To me, autumn is not just a season; it's a gift from nature.

（约175词）

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三个要点完整覆盖：① 秋天，不冷不热、景色最美 ② 叶子变色、公园散步、采枫叶、果园 ③ 平静、希望、变化之美
- 具体场景：公园枫叶、果园香气、中秋团聚
- 从感官到情感层层递进

### 结构方面

- 三段式：总述 → 特点与活动 → 感受升华
- 由外及内：自然 → 活动 → 感悟
- 结尾从季节升华到人生哲理

### 语言方面

- **when it comes to...** — 说到……时
- **put on its most beautiful dress** — 穿上最美的衣裳（拟人）
- **as if the whole world is painted in warm colors** — 仿佛整个世界被涂上暖色
- **the smell of ripe fruits fills the air** — 成熟果实的香气弥漫空中
- **change can be beautiful** — 变化也可以是美好的
- **a gift from nature** — 自然的馈赠

## 全文翻译

**我最喜欢的季节**

说到我最喜欢的季节，秋天当之无愧。它既不热也不冷——空气清爽，天空澄澈，大自然仿佛在冬天来临前穿上了最美的衣裳。

我最爱秋天的是那变幻的色彩。叶子从绿色变成金黄和深红，仿佛整个世界被涂上了暖色调。我所在的城市有一个以枫树闻名的公园。每个周末，我都会去那里散步，收集落叶做剪贴簿，有时还会爬上小山看日落。附近果园里成熟果实的香气弥漫在空气中，总是让我想起中秋节——那时我们一家人聚在一起，在满月下享用月饼。

秋天让我感到平静而充满希望。它教会我，变化也可以是美好的——就像落叶为春天的新生腾出空间一样。对我来说，秋天不仅仅是季节；它是自然的馈赠。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| when it comes to | 说到……时 | When it comes to seasons, autumn is my favorite. |
| put on its beautiful dress | 穿上美丽的衣裳（拟人） | Nature puts on its most beautiful dress in autumn. |
| the air is crisp | 空气清爽 | In autumn, the air is crisp and the sky is clear. |
| fill the air | 弥漫在空中 | The smell of flowers filled the air. |
| a gift from nature | 自然的馈赠 | Every season is a gift from nature. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| when it comes to + 名词 | when it comes to choose | when it comes to my favorite season |
| neither...nor | neither too hot or too cold | neither too hot nor too cold |
| turn + 颜色 | turn to yellow | turn yellow / turn from green to yellow |
| remind sb. of | remind me the festival | remind me of the festival |
| make room for | make a room for | make room for（room 不可数） |

### 升格建议（3条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 季节描写更有画面 | Autumn is beautiful. | The air is crisp, the sky is clear, and leaves turn golden — as if the whole world is painted in warm colors. |
| 2. 活动描写更具体 | I like walking in autumn. | Every weekend I go to the park to take a walk, collect fallen leaves for my scrapbook, and climb the hills to see the sunset. |
| 3. 结尾更有哲理 | I love autumn. | Autumn teaches me that change can be beautiful — like leaves that fall to make room for new growth. To me, autumn is a gift from nature. |
