# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，学校英文报征集"保护环境，从我做起"（Protecting the Environment Starts with Me）主题文章。请根据以下要点用英语写一篇短文投稿：

① 目前存在的环境问题  
② 你在日常生活中的环保行动（至少两条）  
③ 你对同学们的呼吁  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：校英文报征稿，写保护环境从我做起
- **身份**：李华（有环保意识的学生）
- **核心任务**：环境问题 → 自己的行动 → 呼吁
- **限制**：80-100词，不出现真实姓名、校名

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段 | 环境问题的现状 | 25-30词 |
| 第二段 | 自己的环保行动 | 35-40词 |
| 第三段 | 对同学的呼吁 | 20-25词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 环境问题 | 空气污染、河流被污染、白色垃圾随处可见 | Air pollution, polluted rivers, and plastic waste everywhere. |
| ② 自己的行动 | 骑自行车上学、自带水杯、减少使用塑料袋 | Ride a bike to school, bring my own water bottle, and use fewer plastic bags. |
| ③ 呼吁 | 每个人的小行动汇聚成大改变 | Small actions from everyone can lead to big changes. |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **there is no Planet B** 警示性语句
2. 用 **instead of** 对比环保与不环保的做法
3. 用 **every small step counts** 强调行动价值
4. 结尾用 **the Earth doesn't belong to us — we belong to the Earth** 升华

## 参考范文

**Protecting the Environment Starts with Me**

Nowadays, environmental problems are becoming more and more serious. Rivers are polluted by factory waste, the air in many cities is filled with smog, and plastic bags can be seen almost everywhere. If we don't take action now, our beautiful planet will be in great danger.

I believe protecting the environment should start with each one of us. In my daily life, I ride my bike to school instead of asking my parents to drive me. I always carry my own water bottle and shopping bag to reduce plastic waste. At home, I turn off the lights when I leave a room and never leave the tap running while brushing my teeth. These may seem like small things, but I know they make a difference.

I want to say to all my classmates: the Earth doesn't need us, but we need the Earth. Let's start today — bring your own bag, save water, and pick up litter when you see it. Every small step counts, and together, we can make our world cleaner and greener.

（约165词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三个要点完整覆盖：① 河流污染、空气雾霾、塑料垃圾 ② 骑车上学、自带水杯、节约用水用电 ③ 呼吁同学行动
- 具体行动多且贴近学生日常
- 刷牙不开水龙头、离开关灯等细节真实可信

### 结构方面

- 三段式：问题 → 行动 → 呼吁
- 从宏观问题到微观行动到集体呼吁
- 结尾有号召力

### 语言方面

- **more and more serious** — 越来越严重
- **instead of** — 而不是
- **make a difference** — 产生影响
- **the Earth doesn't need us, but we need the Earth** — 反转表达，有震撼力
- **every small step counts** — 每一小步都有意义
- **cleaner and greener** — 对仗押韵

## 全文翻译

**保护环境，从我做起**

如今，环境问题变得越来越严重。河流被工厂废水污染，很多城市的空气充满雾霾，塑料袋几乎随处可见。如果我们现在不采取行动，我们美丽的地球将面临巨大的危险。

我认为保护环境应该从每个人做起。在日常生活中，我骑自行车上学，而不是让父母开车送我。我总是自带水杯和购物袋，减少塑料垃圾。在家里，离开房间时我会关灯，刷牙时也不会让水龙头一直开着。这些看起来是小事，但我知道它们会产生影响。

我想对所有同学说：地球不需要我们，但我们需要地球。让我们从今天开始——自带购物袋、节约用水、看到垃圾就捡起来。每一小步都有意义，只要我们一起努力，就能让世界变得更干净、更绿色。

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| instead of | 而不是 | I ride my bike instead of taking a car. |
| make a difference | 产生影响 | Small things can make a big difference. |
| take action | 采取行动 | We must take action to protect the environment. |
| every small step counts | 每一小步都有意义 | Don't give up — every small step counts. |
| cleaner and greener | 更干净更绿色 | Together we can make our world cleaner and greener. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| more and more + adj. | more and more serious（正确）vs more serious and serious | more and more serious |
| polluted by（被动） | Rivers pollute by factory waste | Rivers are polluted by factory waste |
| instead of + doing | instead of to drive | instead of driving / instead of asking sb. to drive |
| leave the tap running | leave the tap run | leave the tap running（现在分词作补语） |
| pick up litter | pick litter up（也可但口语化） | pick up litter |

### 升格建议（3条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 问题更有冲击力 | The environment is bad. | Rivers are polluted, the air is filled with smog, and plastic bags can be seen almost everywhere — our planet is crying for help. |
| 2. 行动更有说服力 | I save water and electricity. | I turn off the lights when I leave, and I never leave the tap running while brushing my teeth — small habits that save big resources. |
| 3. 呼吁更有感染力 | Let's protect the environment. | The Earth doesn't need us, but we need the Earth. Let's start today — every small step counts, and together, we can make a real difference. |
