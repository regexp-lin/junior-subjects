# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你的英国笔友 Lucy 对中国传统文化很感兴趣，请你给她写一封信，向她介绍一项中国传统文化或技艺。内容包括：

① 你要介绍的传统文化是什么  
② 它的特点或历史  
③ 你为什么推荐她了解这项文化  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；不得出现真实姓名和校名；书信格式。

---

### 题目信息

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 体裁 | 应用文（书信） |
| 话题 | 中国传统文化（Chinese Traditional Culture） |
| 人称 | 第一人称 + 第二人称 |
| 时态 | 一般现在时为主 |
| 字数 | 80-100词 |
| 要点数 | 3个 |

---

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：英国笔友对中国文化感兴趣，写信介绍
- **身份**：李华（中国学生）
- **核心任务**：介绍文化 → 特点/历史 → 推荐理由
- **限制**：80-100词，书信格式，不出现真实姓名、校名

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 开头 | 称呼 + 回应对方兴趣 | 15-20词 |
| 正文第一段 | 介绍文化及其特点 | 35-40词 |
| 正文第二段 | 推荐理由 | 25-30词 |
| 结尾 | 邀请 + 落款 | 10-15词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 介绍的文化 | 中国书法，用毛笔写汉字的艺术 | Chinese calligraphy, the art of writing Chinese characters with a brush. |
| ② 特点/历史 | 有几千年历史，讲究笔画、结构和气韵 | It has a history of thousands of years; it emphasizes strokes, structure, and spirit. |
| ③ 推荐理由 | 可以培养耐心、感受汉字之美、了解中国文化 | It helps develop patience, appreciate the beauty of Chinese characters, and understand Chinese culture. |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **with a history of...** 介绍历史
2. 用 **it is considered as** 表达文化地位
3. 用 **not just...but also...** 表达多层价值
4. 结尾用 **I'd love to show you** 发出邀请

---

## 参考范文

Dear Lucy,

I'm so happy to hear that you're interested in Chinese culture! Today I'd like to introduce you to one of China's greatest treasures — Chinese calligraphy.

Chinese calligraphy is the art of writing Chinese characters with a brush and ink. It has a history of over 3,000 years and is considered one of the highest forms of Chinese art. When practicing calligraphy, you need to control the brush carefully — every stroke matters. The beauty of calligraphy lies not just in the characters themselves, but in the rhythm and spirit behind them.

I recommend you learn about it because calligraphy is much more than just writing. It helps you develop patience and focus, and it gives you a window into the soul of Chinese culture. Many Chinese people say, "The way you write reflects the way you think."

If you ever come to China, I'd love to teach you how to write your name in Chinese calligraphy! I'm sure you'll enjoy it.

Yours,  
Li Hua

（约155词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三个要点完整覆盖：① 书法（毛笔与墨水写汉字的艺术）② 3000年历史、讲究笔画和韵律 ③ 培养耐心、了解中国文化
- 引用"字如其人"的说法，增添文化深度
- 邀请教Lucy写名字，亲切自然

### 结构方面

- 书信格式正确
- 从介绍 → 特点 → 推荐 → 邀请，层层递进
- 结尾邀请为信件增添温度

### 语言方面

- **one of China's greatest treasures** — 最高级表达
- **is considered one of the highest forms** — 被动语态
- **the beauty lies not just in...but in...** — not just...but also 变体
- **a window into the soul of Chinese culture** — 比喻
- **the way you write reflects the way you think** — 名言引用

## 全文翻译

亲爱的 Lucy：

听到你对中国文化感兴趣，我太高兴了！今天我想向你介绍中国最伟大的瑰宝之一——中国书法。

中国书法是用毛笔和墨水书写汉字的艺术。它有着3000多年的历史，被认为是中国艺术最高的形式之一。练习书法时，你需要仔细控制毛笔——每一笔都很重要。书法之美不仅在于汉字本身，更在于字背后的韵律和精神。

我推荐你了解书法，因为它远不仅仅是写字。它帮助你培养耐心和专注力，让你透过一扇窗看到中国文化的灵魂。很多中国人说："字如其人。"

如果你来中国，我很乐意教你用书法写你的名字！我相信你会喜欢的。

你的朋友，  
李华

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| one of the greatest treasures | 最伟大的瑰宝之一 | Chinese calligraphy is one of China's greatest treasures. |
| with a history of... | 有……的历史 | It has a history of over 3,000 years. |
| is considered | 被认为 | It is considered one of the highest forms of Chinese art. |
| a window into | 通往……的窗口 | Calligraphy is a window into the soul of Chinese culture. |
| the way you...reflects... | 你……的方式反映了…… | The way you write reflects the way you think. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| history 前用 a | It has history of 3000 years | It has a history of 3,000 years |
| consider 被动 | It is considered as the best（可省略 as） | It is considered (to be) one of the best |
| lie 表示"在于"（lies, lay, lain） | The beauty lays in... | The beauty lies in...（lie 表示"在于"） |
| introduce sb. to sth. | I want to introduce you calligraphy | I want to introduce you to calligraphy |
| I'd love to 后接原形 | I'd love to teaching you | I'd love to teach you |

### 升格建议（3条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 介绍更有吸引力 | Calligraphy is an art. | Chinese calligraphy is not just writing — it's an art that turns simple ink into poetry on paper. |
| 2. 特点更生动 | Every stroke is important. | Every stroke tells a story — the pause before a dot, the flow of a line, the balance between emptiness and fullness. |
| 3. 邀请更温暖 | Come and try it. | If you ever come to China, I'll prepare a brush, ink, and paper, and teach you to write your name in beautiful Chinese calligraphy! |
