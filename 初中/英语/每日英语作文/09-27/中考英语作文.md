# 中考英语作文生成

## 作文题目（情境+内容要求+注意事项）

假设你是李华，你校将组织一次校园安全知识宣传活动。请你为学校英文网站写一篇关于"校园安全"（Campus Safety）的短文，内容包括：

① 校园中常见的安全隐患  
② 如何保障校园安全（至少两条建议）  
③ 呼吁同学们重视安全  

**注意：** 词数 80-100；不得出现真实姓名和校名。

---

## 写作思考

### 审题

- **情境**：校英文网站征文，写校园安全
- **身份**：李华（学生）
- **核心任务**：隐患 → 建议 → 呼吁
- **限制**：80-100词，不出现真实姓名、校名

### 结构安排

| 段落 | 内容 | 预计词数 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段 | 引出话题 + 安全隐患 | 25-30词 |
| 第二段 | 保障安全的建议 | 35-40词 |
| 第三段 | 呼吁重视安全 | 20-25词 |

### 表达准备（三要点中英文）

| 要点 | 中文 | 英文表达参考 |
|------|------|--------------|
| ① 安全隐患 | 课间追逐打闹、楼梯拥挤、触碰电器、食品安全 | Running in corridors, crowded stairs, touching electrical equipment, food safety issues. |
| ② 建议 | 走路不跑、上下楼梯靠右行、不吃三无食品、学会急救 | Walk instead of run, keep right on stairs, avoid unsafe food, and learn first aid. |
| ③ 呼吁 | 安全第一，人人有责 | Safety first; everyone is responsible. |

### 可加分亮点（4条）

1. 用 **safety comes first** 开头引出主题
2. 用 **it is necessary for us to...** 表达必要性
3. 用 **keep in mind that** 强调注意事项
4. 结尾用 **safety is no accident** 双关语

## 参考范文

**Campus Safety**

School is our second home, and safety should always come first. However, there are some hidden dangers on campus that we should pay attention to.

For example, some students run and chase each other during breaks, which may cause falls and injuries. The stairs can be very crowded between classes, and pushing may lead to accidents. Some students also eat snacks from unknown sources, which is bad for their health. These may seem like small things, but they can lead to serious consequences.

To keep our campus safe, here are some suggestions. First, walk instead of running in corridors and on stairs — always keep to the right side. Second, never touch electrical equipment without permission. Third, only eat food from the school canteen or trusted stores. Last, it's a good idea to learn some basic first aid, such as how to treat a cut or a burn.

Remember, safety is no accident — it's a habit. Let's all be careful and make our school a safer place!

（约155词）

---

## 范文亮点分析

### 内容方面

- 三个要点完整覆盖：① 隐患（追跑、楼梯拥挤、食品安全）② 四条建议（靠右行、勿触电、吃安全食品、学急救）③ 呼吁（安全是习惯）
- 问题和建议一一对应
- 结尾双关语（safety is no accident）巧妙

### 结构方面

- 三段式：隐患 → 建议 → 呼吁
- 举例具体（追跑、楼梯、零食）
- 用 **First, Second, Third, Last** 有条理

### 语言方面

- **safety comes first** — 安全第一
- **hidden dangers** — 隐患
- **lead to serious consequences** — 导致严重后果
- **safety is no accident** — 安全不是偶然的（双关）
- 情态动词运用丰富：may, should, never

## 全文翻译

**校园安全**

学校是我们的第二个家，安全始终应该放在第一位。然而，校园中有一些我们应该注意的安全隐患。

例如，一些同学在课间追逐打闹，可能导致摔倒和受伤。课间楼梯非常拥挤，推搡可能引发事故。一些同学还吃来源不明的零食，这对健康有害。这些看似小事，但可能导致严重后果。

为了保障校园安全，以下是一些建议。首先，在走廊和楼梯上走路而不是跑——始终靠右行走。其次，未经允许不要触碰电器设备。第三，只吃学校食堂或正规商店的食品。最后，学一些基本的急救知识，比如怎样处理割伤或烫伤，是个好主意。

记住，安全不是偶然的——它是一种习惯。让我们都多加小心，让校园成为更安全的地方！

## 可继续补充

### 好词好句（5行表格）

| 表达 | 释义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| safety comes first | 安全第一 | No matter what we do, safety comes first. |
| hidden dangers | 安全隐患 | There are some hidden dangers on campus. |
| lead to | 导致 | Running in corridors may lead to injuries. |
| keep to the right | 靠右行 | Always keep to the right on the stairs. |
| safety is no accident | 安全不是偶然的 | Safety is no accident — it requires our daily attention. |

### 易错点（5个 ✅❌对比）

| 易错点 | ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| pay attention to 后接名词/动名词 | pay attention to study hard | pay attention to studying hard / pay attention to safety |
| lead to 后接名词 | lead to be hurt | lead to injuries / lead to accidents |
| without permission | without permit | without permission |
| it's a good idea to 后接原形 | It's a good idea learning first aid | It's a good idea to learn first aid |
| corridor 拼写 | corrider / corridoor | corridor |

### 升格建议（3条，带英文例句）

| 建议 | 普通表达 | 升格表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1. 隐患更有冲击 | Some students run in corridors. | A moment of carelessness — a push on the stairs, a dash through the corridor — can change someone's life in an instant. |
| 2. 建议更有说服力 | Don't run in school. | Walk instead of running, keep to the right, and always look where you're going — these simple habits can prevent 90% of campus accidents. |
| 3. 呼吁更有力 | Safety is important. | Safety is not a rule we follow for others — it's a promise we make to ourselves and to the people who love us. |
