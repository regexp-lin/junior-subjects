> **06-18 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Choir Wants New Members!**

② Do you love singing? Would you like to perform on stage with others? Our school choir is now looking for new members. Join us and make beautiful music together!

③ **Who can join?** All students in Grade 7 and Grade 8 are welcome. You do not need to have sung in a choir before. What matters most is your love for music and your willingness to learn. We have both beginners and experienced singers. Everyone grows together.

④ **What do we do?** We practise twice a week — on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons from 4:00 pm to 5:30 pm in the Music Room (Building B, Room 305). We sing pop songs, traditional songs, and sometimes songs in English. In December, we will perform at the School Art Festival. It will be a great experience!

⑤ **How to join?** Sign your name on the list outside the Music Room before September 20th. Or send an email to choir@school.edu with your name, class and grade. Our teacher, Ms. Chen, will contact you. Do not miss this chance to be part of something special!

**注释：** choir 合唱团；willingness 意愿；traditional 传统的

---

### 选择题

1. Who can join the school choir?
   A. Grade 7 and Grade 8 students who love music.
   B. Only Grade 9 students.
   C. Only students who have sung in a choir before.
   D. Only students who are good at singing.

2. When does the choir practise?
   A. Every day in the morning.
   B. On Tuesday and Thursday afternoons, 4:00 — 5:30 pm.
   C. Only on weekends.
   D. From Monday to Friday, 4:00 — 5:30 pm.

3. Where is the Music Room?
   A. Building A, Room 201.
   B. The school library.
   C. Building B, Room 305.
   D. The school hall.

4. How can students join the choir?
   A. They must pass a singing test first.
   B. They need to ask their parents to register.
   C. They must attend the School Art Festival first.
   D. They sign on the list or send an email by September 20th.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The choir will perform at the School Art Festival in December.
   B. The choir only sings in English.
   C. Beginners are not welcome.
   D. Students must pay to join the choir.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "All students in Grade 7 and Grade 8 are welcome... What matters most is your love for music and your willingness to learn."

**【解析】** 文章明确说明七八年级学生均可加入，最重要的是对音乐的热爱和学习意愿。A 项正确。B 项仅九年级错误；C 项"必须有合唱经验"与"You do not need to have sung in a choir before"矛盾；D 项"必须擅长唱歌"未提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "We practise twice a week — on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons from 4:00 pm to 5:30 pm"

**【解析】** 合唱团每周二和周四下午 4:00—5:30 练习。B 项正确。A 项每天上午、C 项仅周末、D 项周一至周五均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "in the Music Room (Building B, Room 305)"

**【解析】** 音乐室位于 B 栋 305 室。C 项正确。A 项 A 栋 201 室、B 项图书馆、D 项礼堂均错误。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Sign your name on the list outside the Music Room before September 20th. Or send an email to choir@school.edu with your name, class and grade."

**【解析】** 加入方式有二：9 月 20 日前在音乐室外名单上签字，或发邮件至 choir@school.edu 注明姓名、班级和年级。D 项正确。A 项需通过歌唱考试、B 项家长登记、C 项先参加艺术节均未提及。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "In December, we will perform at the School Art Festival. It will be a great experience!"

**【解析】** 文中明确写出 12 月将在学校艺术节上表演。A 项为事实性信息，可由此推断。B 项"只唱英文歌"错误，文中说 pop songs, traditional songs, sometimes in English；C 项"新手不欢迎"与 beginners 可加入矛盾；D 项"需付费"未提及。故选 A。
