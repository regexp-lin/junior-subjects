# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① In many countries, students wear school uniforms every day. In China, most middle schools require students to wear uniforms from Monday to Friday. Some students are happy about this, but others wish they could wear their own clothes. Should students wear school uniforms? People have different opinions.

② Those who support school uniforms give several reasons. First, uniforms make all students look the same, so no one will feel bad about not having expensive clothes. This helps reduce comparison (攀比) among students. Second, getting dressed in the morning becomes much easier — students don't have to spend time choosing what to wear. Third, uniforms can help build a sense of belonging (归属感) to the school and encourage teamwork.

③ However, some people are against school uniforms. They believe that wearing the same clothes every day stops students from showing who they really are. Teenagers are at an age when they want to express themselves, and clothing is one important way to do that. Besides, many students complain that their school uniforms are uncomfortable and ugly. If students don't feel good in what they wear, it may affect their mood and even their interest in going to school.

④ There is also a middle ground. Some schools now allow students to design their own uniforms by voting. Students can choose the color, style, and material they like best. In this way, the uniforms still keep everyone looking neat and equal, but students also feel that their voices are heard.

⑤ In my opinion, school uniforms are necessary, but schools should listen to students' ideas when designing them. After all, students are the ones who wear them every day. When students feel comfortable and proud of their uniforms, they will be happier at school.

**注释：** comparison 攀比；belonging 归属感；material 材料

### 选择题

1. According to Paragraph ②, school uniforms can help students ________.
   A. get better grades in exams
   B. save time in the morning
   C. make more friends at school
   D. learn to design clothes

2. What is the main idea of Paragraph ③?
   A. Students should design their own clothes.
   B. School uniforms are too expensive for some families.
   C. Some people think school uniforms have disadvantages.
   D. Teenagers don't care about what they wear.

3. What does the underlined phrase "middle ground" in Paragraph ④ probably mean?
   A. A playground in the middle of the school.
   B. A solution that both sides can accept.
   C. A ground floor of a building.
   D. A new rule made by the government.

4. What is the writer's attitude towards school uniforms?
   A. Students should never wear uniforms.
   B. Schools should force students to love uniforms.
   C. Uniforms are needed but should consider students' opinions.
   D. Only expensive uniforms are worth wearing.

5. Where would you most probably read this passage?
   A. In a travel guidebook.
   B. In a science fiction novel.
   C. In a school newspaper.
   D. In a cooking magazine.

### 参考答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "getting dressed in the morning becomes much easier — students don't have to spend time choosing what to wear."

**【解析】** 原文明确说穿校服可以让早上穿衣变得更简单，不用花时间选择穿什么，即节省早晨的时间。A 项"考试成绩更好"原文未提及，属于无中生有；C 项"交更多朋友"文中未涉及；D 项"学习设计衣服"与原文内容无关。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "However, some people are against school uniforms." 及全段内容。

**【解析】** 第③段首句即点明"一些人反对校服"，随后列举了反对的理由：限制个性表达、不舒适不好看、影响心情。因此该段主旨是"一些人认为校服有弊端"。A 项"学生应该自己设计衣服"是第④段的内容，张冠李戴；B 项"校服太贵"原文未提及；D 项"青少年不在意穿什么"与原文相反，原文说青少年想通过穿着表达自己。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "There is also a middle ground. Some schools now allow students to design their own uniforms by voting."

**【解析】** 前文第②段讲支持校服的观点，第③段讲反对校服的观点，第④段提出"middle ground"，即让学生投票参与校服设计，既保留校服又尊重学生意见，可推断"middle ground"意为"双方都能接受的折中方案"。A 项"学校中间的操场"是字面意思的错误理解；C 项"建筑的底层"与语境无关；D 项"政府制定的新规"原文未提及。故选 B。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, school uniforms are necessary, but schools should listen to students' ideas when designing them."

**【解析】** 作者在末段明确表达观点：校服是必要的，但学校在设计时应该听取学生的意见。这与 C 项"校服是需要的，但应考虑学生的意见"一致。A 项"学生不应该穿校服"与作者观点相反；B 项"学校应强迫学生喜欢校服"过于极端，原文说"listen to students' ideas"；D 项"只有贵的校服才值得穿"原文未提及，属于无中生有。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文。文章讨论"学生是否应该穿校服"这一校园话题，列举正反两方观点并给出折中建议。

**【解析】** 文章围绕校服这一校园生活话题展开讨论，语言平实，结构为"提出问题—正方观点—反方观点—折中方案—个人看法"，是典型的校园报刊议论文。A 项"旅行指南"与校园话题无关；B 项"科幻小说"体裁不符；D 项"烹饪杂志"与内容完全不相关。故选 C。
