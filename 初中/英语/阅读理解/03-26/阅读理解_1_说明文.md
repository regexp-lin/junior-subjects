# 阅读理解 第1篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① When you look up at the sky on a windy day, you might see colorful kites flying high. Kite flying is a popular activity around the world today, but do you know that kites were first invented in China over 2,000 years ago?

② According to historical records, the earliest kites were made of wood. They were not toys — they were used for military (军事的) purposes. Soldiers used kites to send messages and measure distances. Later, during the Tang Dynasty, people began to make kites out of paper and silk. These lighter materials made kites easier to fly, and kite flying gradually became a fun activity for ordinary people.

③ In Chinese culture, kites carry special meanings. For example, during the Qingming Festival, people fly kites and then cut the string (线) to let them go. They believe this can take away bad luck and bring good health. Some kites are shaped like dragons, butterflies, or birds, and each shape has its own meaning. A dragon kite stands for power and good fortune, while a butterfly kite means happiness.

④ The city of Weifang in Shandong Province is known as the "Kite Capital of the World." Every April, an international kite festival is held there. People from more than 40 countries come to show their kites and compete. Some kites at the festival are over 100 meters long! The festival has helped spread Chinese kite culture to the rest of the world.

⑤ Today, kite flying is not just a tradition — it is also a healthy outdoor activity. Running with a kite is good exercise. Looking up at a kite in the sky can help relax your eyes and mind. Next time you have a free afternoon, why not go outside and fly a kite?

**注释：** military 军事的；measure 测量；string 线

---

### 选择题

1. According to Paragraph ②, the earliest kites in China were made of ________.
   A. paper
   B. silk
   C. wood
   D. cloth

2. Why do people cut the kite string during the Qingming Festival?
   A. Because they want to give the kite to someone else.
   B. Because they believe it can take away bad luck.
   C. Because the kite is too old to use again.
   D. Because it is a rule of the kite competition.

3. What can we learn about the Weifang Kite Festival?
   A. It is held every month in spring.
   B. Only Chinese people can join the festival.
   C. It takes place in Shandong Province.
   D. All kites at the festival are over 100 meters long.

4. What does the underlined word "spread" in Paragraph ④ probably mean?
   A. Changed.
   B. Protected.
   C. Passed on to more places.
   D. Kept as a secret.

5. What is the best title for this passage?
   A. How to Make a Kite at Home
   B. The Qingming Festival and Its Traditions
   C. Chinese Kites: From History to Today
   D. Outdoor Activities for Teenagers

---

### 参考答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "According to historical records, the earliest kites were made of wood."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出最早的风筝是用木头制成的。A 项"paper（纸）"和 B 项"silk（丝绸）"是唐朝之后才使用的材料，属于张冠李戴；D 项"cloth（布）"原文未提及，属于无中生有。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "people fly kites and then cut the string to let them go. They believe this can take away bad luck and bring good health."

**【解析】** 原文说人们在清明节放风筝后剪断线，相信这样可以带走坏运气、带来好健康。A 项"把风筝给别人"原文未提及；C 项"风筝太旧了"属于无中生有；D 项"风筝比赛规则"与清明节习俗无关，属于偷换概念。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1-2句 "The city of Weifang in Shandong Province is known as the 'Kite Capital of the World.' Every April, an international kite festival is held there."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出潍坊在山东省，风筝节在那里举办，C 项正确。A 项"每月举办"错误，原文说"Every April"即每年四月；B 项"只有中国人能参加"与原文"People from more than 40 countries"矛盾；D 项"所有风筝都超过100米"过度推断，原文只说"Some kites"。故选 C。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "The festival has helped spread Chinese kite culture to the rest of the world."

**【解析】** 根据上下文，来自40多个国家的人参加风筝节，风筝节帮助把中国风筝文化"spread"到世界其他地方，结合语境可推断"spread"意为"传播到更多地方"。A 项"改变了"不符合语境；B 项"保护了"语义不匹配；D 项"作为秘密保守"与向世界传播的语境相反。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文。文章从风筝的历史起源（第①②段）、文化含义（第③段）、国际风筝节（第④段）到现代价值（第⑤段）进行了全面介绍。

**【解析】** 文章围绕中国风筝展开，从历史到现代进行了系统介绍，C 项"Chinese Kites: From History to Today"最能概括全文主旨。A 项"如何在家做风筝"文中未涉及制作方法，以偏概全；B 项"清明节及其传统"只涉及第③段部分内容，以偏概全；D 项"青少年户外活动"过于宽泛，未体现文章核心——风筝。故选 C。
