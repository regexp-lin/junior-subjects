> **09-12 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should boys and girls study in separate classes? Some schools have tried "single-sex" classes, where boys and girls learn in different rooms. This idea has caused much debate among teachers, parents and students.

② Those who support separate classes say that boys and girls learn in different ways. Boys may be more active and enjoy hands-on activities, while girls often prefer quiet reading and group discussion. In separate classes, teachers can use teaching methods that suit each group better. Some also believe that without the opposite sex in the classroom, students can focus more on their studies and less on social pressure.

③ However, those who disagree also have good reasons. They say real life is mixed. When students grow up, they will work and live with people of both genders. Learning together helps them develop social skills and understand different viewpoints. In mixed classes, students can learn from each other. Boys and girls often have different strengths; working together can make the whole class stronger. Besides, separating students may increase gender stereotypes. Some people may think "boys are better at math" or "girls are better at languages." Such ideas are not always true and can harm students' confidence.

④ Research on this topic is mixed. Some studies show that single-sex classes can improve exam scores in certain subjects. Others find no clear difference. The results often depend on the school, the teacher, and the students themselves.

⑤ In my opinion, there is no perfect answer. The most important thing is that every student feels safe and has a chance to learn. Schools should listen to students and parents, and choose the system that works best for their community.

**注释：** stereotype 刻板印象；gender 性别；debate 辩论

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people support separate classes for boys and girls?
   A. Because mixed classes cost more money.
   B. Because boys and girls learn differently and teachers can suit each group better.
   C. Because parents want smaller class sizes.
   D. Because the school building has no space for big classes.

2. What do those who disagree say about learning together?
   A. Mixed classes make students too noisy.
   B. Learning together helps students develop social skills and understand different viewpoints.
   C. Boys and girls never work well together.
   D. Mixed classes are only good for older students.

3. What does the word "They" in Paragraph ③ refer to?
   A. Teachers.
   B. Students.
   C. Those who oppose separate classes.
   D. Parents.

4. What can we learn from Paragraph ④?
   A. All research shows single-sex classes are better.
   B. Research results on this topic are not always the same.
   C. Girls always do better in single-sex classes.
   D. Boys never improve in mixed classes.

5. What is the writer's opinion at the end?
   A. Single-sex classes should be used in all schools.
   B. Mixed classes are always the best choice.
   C. There is no perfect answer; schools should choose what works for their community.
   D. Only girls should have separate classes.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "boys and girls learn in different ways... In separate classes, teachers can use teaching methods that suit each group better"

**【解析】** 支持者认为男女学习方式不同，分班后教师可因材施教。A项"花费更多"、C项"家长想要小班"、D项"校舍空间不足"原文均未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Learning together helps them develop social skills and understand different viewpoints"

**【解析】** 反对者认为合班学习有助于培养社交能力、理解不同观点。A项"太吵闹"、C项"男女从不能合作"和D项"只对高年级好"原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "However, those who disagree also have good reasons. They say real life is mixed."

**【解析】** 根据上下文，第③段讨论反对分班的观点，"They"指代反对者。A、B、D项在语境中不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Research on this topic is mixed. Some studies show... Others find no clear difference. The results often depend on..."

**【解析】** 原文指出研究结论不一，有的显示单性别班有利，有的未发现明显差异，结果因校、因师、因人而异。A项"所有研究都支持分班"与"Others find no clear difference"矛盾；C、D项过度推断。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, there is no perfect answer... Schools should listen to students and parents, and choose the system that works best for their community"

**【解析】** 作者认为没有绝对正确答案，学校应听取意见并选择适合本校的方式。A、B项过于绝对；D项无中生有。故选C。
