# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Is After-School Service Useful?**

① In recent years, many primary and middle schools in China have started "after-school service" (课后服务). Students stay at school for one or two hours after class. They do homework, join clubs, or take part in sports. But is it really useful? People have different opinions.

② Supporters say after-school service helps working parents. Most parents finish work at 5 or 6 p.m. They cannot pick up their children at 3 or 4 p.m. If children stay at school, they are safe and parents feel at ease. Besides, students can finish homework at school with teachers' help. They do not need to rush through it at home. Some students also discover new interests through clubs and activities.

③ However, some people worry that after-school service adds too much pressure. Students already spend six or seven hours at school. Staying longer may make them tired. They have less time to play, rest, or be with their families. Some parents also say the quality of after-school activities varies. Not every school has good clubs or enough teachers.

④ In my view, after-school service can be useful if it is done well. Schools should offer a mix of homework help and fun activities. Students should be able to choose what they join, not be forced. The service should be free or low-cost so that all families can afford it. Teachers need enough support too, so they do not feel overloaded.

⑤ There is no perfect solution. What works for one school may not work for another. The key is to listen to students, parents, and teachers, and keep improving the programme.

**注释：** varies 各不相同；overloaded 负担过重

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do supporters like after-school service?
   A. Because it makes schools richer.
   B. Because it helps working parents and lets students finish homework with teachers' help.
   C. Because students never get tired.
   D. Because it replaces all homework.

2. Which is NOT a concern mentioned in Paragraph ③?
   A. Students may get tired from staying longer at school.
   B. Students have less time for family.
   C. The quality of activities may vary.
   D. After-school service is too expensive for everyone.

3. What does the writer suggest in Paragraph ④?
   A. Schools should force all students to stay.
   B. After-school service can be useful if schools offer a good mix and let students choose.
   C. Only homework help should be offered.
   D. The service should be very expensive.

4. What does "at ease" mean in Paragraph ②?
   A. Worried.
   B. Feeling calm and not worried.
   C. Angry.
   D. Very tired.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. After-school service will be cancelled.
   B. Schools should listen to different voices and keep improving the programme.
   C. All schools must do the same thing.
   D. No one cares about after-school service.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "helps working parents... students can finish homework at school with teachers' help"
**【解析】** 支持者认为课后服务帮助上班族家长，学生可在校完成作业并得到老师帮助。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 提到 tired, less time for family, quality varies
**【解析】** 第三段担忧包括：学生更累、与家人时间减少、活动质量不一。D项"对所有人太贵"未在第三段提及。故选D。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "after-school service can be useful if it is done well... offer a mix... Students should be able to choose"
**【解析】** 作者建议：做得好就有效；应兼顾作业辅导与兴趣活动；学生应能自主选择。B项正确。A、C、D项与建议不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第②段 "If children stay at school, they are safe and parents feel at ease"
**【解析】** 孩子在校安全，家长"feel at ease"即放心、不担心。B项正确。A、C、D项与语境不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "listen to students, parents, and teachers, and keep improving the programme"
**【解析】** 结尾指出要听取各方意见并持续改进。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
