> **10-18 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Choir — New Members Wanted**

① Dear students,

② The Sunshine School Choir is now recruiting new members for the new term. If you love singing and want to make new friends, join us! We practise every Wednesday and Friday afternoon from 4:00 to 5:30 in the music room (Building B, Room 301).

③ **Who can join?** Students in Grades 7–9 who enjoy singing. You do not need to read music, but you should be able to sing in tune. We will teach you the rest.

④ **What do we do?** We learn songs in English and Chinese. We perform at school events, such as the New Year Concert and the Arts Festival. Last year we won the first prize in the city choir competition. This year we aim to do even better!

⑤ **How to join?** Come to our try-out on October 25th at 4:00 p.m. in the music room. Prepare a short song (about one minute) to sing. Our teacher, Ms Wang, will listen and give you feedback. The results will be posted on the noticeboard on October 28th. For any questions, email us at choir@sunshineschool.edu.cn.

We look forward to hearing your voice!

Sunshine School Choir  
October 15th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the choir practise?
   A. Every Monday and Thursday.
   B. Every Wednesday and Friday afternoon.
   C. Only on weekends.
   D. Every day after school.

2. Who can join the choir?
   A. Only students who can read music.
   B. Students in Grades 7–9 who enjoy singing.
   C. Only Grade 9 students.
   D. Teachers and parents.

3. What did the choir achieve last year?
   A. They performed at the Olympics.
   B. They won the first prize in the city choir competition.
   C. They travelled abroad.
   D. They recorded an album.

4. What should students prepare for the try-out?
   A. A long speech.
   B. A dance performance.
   C. A short song (about one minute).
   D. Nothing at all.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. The choir is only for professional singers.
   B. Beginners are welcome — the choir will teach singing skills.
   C. Try-outs are not required.
   D. The choir never performs at school.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "We practise every Wednesday and Friday afternoon from 4:00 to 5:30"
**【解析】** 合唱团每周三、周五下午4:00–5:30排练。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Students in Grades 7–9 who enjoy singing"
**【解析】** 招收七至九年级、喜爱唱歌的学生。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Last year we won the first prize in the city choir competition"
**【解析】** 去年在市合唱比赛中获一等奖。B项正确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Prepare a short song (about one minute) to sing"
**【解析】** 试唱需准备一首约一分钟的短歌。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第③段 "You do not need to read music... We will teach you the rest"
**【解析】** 不需识谱，合唱团会教授其余技能，可见欢迎初学者。B项正确。A、C、D项与通知内容矛盾。故选B。
