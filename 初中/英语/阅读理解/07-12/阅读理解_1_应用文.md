# 阅读理解（应用文）

# Campus Environmental Protection Knowledge Contest Notice

① **Green Future Student Union** invites all students to take part in the **Campus Environmental Protection Knowledge Contest**. This contest aims to raise students' awareness (意识) of environmental protection and encourage everyone to take action for a greener campus.

② **Date and place:** The contest will be held on Friday, June 15th, from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. in the school hall (礼堂). Students who want to join should sign up (报名) at the Student Union office before June 10th.

③ **Rules:** The contest is for teams of three. Each class can send at most two teams. Topics include recycling (回收利用), saving water and electricity, reducing plastic waste, and green living. Teams will answer questions and complete short tasks. The top three teams will get prizes and certificates (证书).

④ **Preparation:** You do not need to memorize everything. Read our Green Guide booklet (小册子) and check the school website for tips. Working with your team and discussing ideas will help you the most. Have fun and learn together!

⑤ We hope many students will join. Protecting our Earth starts from small actions in our daily life. See you at the contest!

**注释：** awareness 意识；sign up 报名；recycling 回收利用；certificate 证书；booklet 小册子

---

### 选择题

1. Who organizes the Campus Environmental Protection Knowledge Contest?
   A. The school library.
   B. The science teachers.
   C. Parents' association.
   D. Green Future Student Union.

2. When and where will the contest take place?
   A. On Friday, June 15th, 2:00–4:00 p.m. in the school hall.
   B. On June 10th in the classroom.
   C. On Saturday morning in the playground.
   D. Next month in the library.

3. According to the notice, how many students can be in one team?
   A. One.
   B. Three.
   C. Two.
   D. Four.

4. What should students do before June 10th if they want to join?
   A. Buy a Green Guide booklet.
   B. Visit the school website only.
   C. Sign up at the Student Union office.
   D. Prepare prizes for the contest.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The contest encourages students to care about environmental protection and take action.
   B. Only the top team will get a prize.
   C. Students need to memorize all the environmental facts to win.
   D. The contest is only for students in Grade 9.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "Green Future Student Union invites all students to take part in the Campus Environmental Protection Knowledge Contest."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明 Green Future Student Union 邀请学生参加竞赛，即该学生会组织了此次活动。D项正确。A、B、C项均未在文中提及。故选D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "The contest will be held on Friday, June 15th, from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. in the school hall."

**【解析】** 原文清楚写明竞赛于6月15日周五下午2:00–4:00在学校礼堂举行。A项正确。B项时间地点错误；C项周六上午在操场、D项下月图书馆均与原文不符。故选A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The contest is for teams of three."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明每队三人。B项正确。A项一人、C项两人、D项四人均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Students who want to join should sign up at the Student Union office before June 10th."

**【解析】** 原文指出想参加的学生须在6月10日前到学生会办公室报名。C项正确。A项购买小册子、B项仅访问网站、D项准备奖品均与报名要求不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第①段 "raise students' awareness of environmental protection and encourage everyone to take action for a greener campus" 及第⑤段 "Protecting our Earth starts from small actions in our daily life."

**【解析】** 文章旨在提高环保意识、鼓励学生为更绿色校园采取行动，并强调保护地球从小事做起。A项准确概括活动目的。B项"仅第一名获奖"与原文"前三名"矛盾；C项"须背诵全部内容"与第④段"不必全部记忆"矛盾；D项"仅九年级"文中未提及。故选A。
