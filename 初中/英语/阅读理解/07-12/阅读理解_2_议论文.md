# 阅读理解（议论文）

# Should Every Student Learn a Musical Instrument?

① In many schools, music is part of the curriculum (课程). Some people believe that every student should learn to play at least one musical instrument. Others think it is not necessary. What do you think?

② **Those who support (支持) learning an instrument** say that music helps develop the brain. Studies show that children who learn music often do better in maths and languages. Playing an instrument also improves memory, concentration (专注) and patience. Besides, music brings joy and helps students relax after a long day of study. It can also build confidence when students perform (表演) in front of others. Learning music is not just a skill—it shapes (塑造) the whole person.

③ **Those who disagree** argue that not every child is interested in music. Some students may prefer sports, art, or other hobbies. Forcing them to learn an instrument can cause stress and even make them hate music. Moreover, lessons and instruments can be expensive. Not all families can afford (负担得起) them. Schools should offer music as an option (选择), but students should have the freedom to choose what they want to learn.

④ **A balanced view:** Music education has real benefits, but it should not be forced on everyone. Schools can provide chances for students to try different instruments and discover their interests. Those who enjoy it can continue; those who do not can choose other activities. The aim (目标) is to help each student find something they love, whether it is music, sport, or something else.

⑤ In the end, learning an instrument is a wonderful experience for many students, but it is not the only path to success. Respect students' interests and give them choices. That is the best way to support their growth.

**注释：** curriculum 课程；concentration 专注；perform 表演；shape 塑造；afford 负担得起；option 选择；aim 目标

### 选择题

1. According to supporters, what benefits can learning an instrument bring?
   A. It makes students taller.
   B. It helps students run faster.
   C. It reduces the need for homework.
   D. It helps develop the brain and improves memory, concentration and patience; it also brings joy and builds confidence.

2. Why do some people think students should NOT be forced to learn an instrument?
   A. Because not every child is interested; forcing them may cause stress; lessons and instruments can be expensive.
   B. Because instruments are too light.
   C. Because teachers do not know how to teach music.
   D. Because music is not important at all.

3. What does the writer suggest in the "balanced view"?
   A. All students must learn the piano.
   B. Schools should offer chances for students to try and discover interests; those who enjoy it can continue, others can choose different activities.
   C. Schools should cancel all music lessons.
   D. Only rich students can learn music.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Every student must learn an instrument to pass exams.
   B. Music lessons are free in all schools.
   C. The writer believes that students should have freedom to choose their interests, and music is one option but not the only path.
   D. Learning music has no benefits at all.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To discuss whether every student should learn an instrument and present different views.
   B. To persuade all students to buy expensive instruments.
   C. To tell students to stop learning music.
   D. To introduce the history of music education.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "music helps develop the brain... children who learn music often do better in maths and languages... improves memory, concentration and patience... music brings joy and helps students relax... build confidence when students perform in front of others."

**【解析】** 文中列出多项支持学习乐器的理由：开发大脑、提高数学和语言表现、改善记忆力、专注力和耐心、带来快乐、建立自信等。D项全面概括。A项变高、B项跑更快、C项减少作业均未提及。故选D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "not every child is interested in music... Forcing them to learn an instrument can cause stress and even make them hate music... lessons and instruments can be expensive. Not all families can afford them."

**【解析】** 反对强制学习乐器的理由包括：并非每个孩子都有兴趣；强迫可能导致压力和厌烦；课程和乐器费用高，并非所有家庭都能负担。A项准确归纳。B、C、D项均与原文不符。故选A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Schools can provide chances for students to try different instruments and discover their interests. Those who enjoy it can continue; those who do not can choose other activities."

**【解析】** 作者提出折中观点：学校提供尝试机会让学生发现兴趣，喜欢者可继续，不喜欢者可选择其他活动。B项正确。A项强制学钢琴、C项取消音乐课、D项仅富人家孩子可学均与作者观点矛盾。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Music education has real benefits, but it should not be forced on everyone" 及第⑤段 "learning an instrument is a wonderful experience for many students, but it is not the only path to success. Respect students' interests and give them choices."

**【解析】** 作者认可音乐教育的好处，但强调不应强制，应尊重学生兴趣并给予选择，乐器学习并非唯一成功之路。C项正确。A项必须学才能通过考试、B项音乐课免费、D项毫无益处均与文章观点相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第①段提出问题；第②段支持方观点；第③段反对方观点；第④⑤段平衡观点与总结。

**【解析】** 文章围绕"是否每个学生都应学一门乐器"展开讨论，呈现正反观点及折中建议。A项准确概括写作目的。B项劝说购买贵重乐器、C项停止学音乐、D项介绍音乐教育史均偏离主旨。故选A。
