# 阅读理解（说明文）

**What Is Digital Currency?**

① You may have heard of "digital currency" or "digital yuan." What is it? Simply put, it is money that exists only in digital form. You cannot hold it in your hand like a coin or a paper note. It lives in computers and phones. You use it to pay for things online or with your phone. Digital currency is becoming more common in our lives.

② There are different types of digital money. First, there is the money in your bank account. When you use a bank card or a payment app to pay, you are using digital currency. The bank keeps a record. Your balance goes down when you spend. Second, there is the digital yuan, launched by China. It is like cash, but digital. The central bank issues it. It is legal tender. You can use it to buy things in shops, online, or on apps. Third, there are cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin. These are not issued by governments. They use special technology called "blockchain." Their value can go up and down a lot. They are risky for ordinary people.

③ How do you use digital currency? You need a digital wallet. It is an app on your phone. You put money into the wallet — by linking your bank card or getting digital yuan from a bank. When you pay, you scan a QR code or tap your phone. The money goes from your wallet to the seller's. It is fast and convenient. You do not need to carry cash.

④ What are the advantages? Digital payment is quick. No need to count coins or wait for change. It is safe — you do not lose physical money. It is easy to track. You can see where your money went. For the government, it helps reduce tax evasion and illegal money flow. Digital currency can also reach people in remote areas who do not have easy access to banks.

⑤ Digital currency is the future of money. But we must use it wisely. Keep your password safe. Do not share it with anyone. Be careful with strangers who ask for money online. With the right knowledge, digital currency can make life easier.

**注释：** balance 余额；legal tender 法定货币；blockchain 区块链；wallet 钱包；evasion 逃避

---

**Questions:**

1. What is digital currency?
   A. Money you can only hold in your hand.
   B. Money that exists only in digital form, in computers and phones.
   C. Only paper money.
   D. Foreign currency only.

2. Who issues the digital yuan?
   A. Private companies.
   B. The central bank of China.
   C. Shops.
   D. Phone makers.

3. What do you need to use digital currency?
   A. Only a bank card.
   B. A digital wallet — an app on your phone.
   C. Only cash.
   D. A credit card only.

4. Which is NOT an advantage of digital payment?
   A. It is quick.
   B. It is safe — you do not lose physical money.
   C. It is easy to track.
   D. It is issued by no one and has no rules.

5. What should you do to use digital currency safely?
   A. Share your password with friends.
   B. Keep your password safe; be careful with strangers asking for money online.
   C. Never use it.
   D. Give money to everyone who asks.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "it is money that exists only in digital form... It lives in computers and phones."

**【解析】** 数字货币指以数字形式存在、存在于电脑和手机中的货币。A、C、D项均与定义矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "there is the digital yuan, launched by China... The central bank issues it."

**【解析】** 数字人民币由中国人民银行（央行）发行。A项私营企业、C项商店、D项手机厂商均不正确。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "You need a digital wallet. It is an app on your phone."

**【解析】** 使用数字货币需要数字钱包，即手机上的应用。A、C、D项表述不完整或不准确。故选B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段列举优势：quick, safe, easy to track, 以及政府监管方面的好处

**【解析】** 快速、安全、易追踪是优势；"issued by no one and has no rules"既非优势，也与数字人民币等法定数字货币不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Keep your password safe. Do not share it with anyone. Be careful with strangers who ask for money online."

**【解析】** 安全使用建议：保管好密码、不与人分享、对网上索要钱财的陌生人保持警惕。A、C、D项均与安全建议相反。故选B。
