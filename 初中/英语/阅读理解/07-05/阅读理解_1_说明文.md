# 阅读理解（说明文）

## How Plant Roots Absorb Water

① When you look at a plant, you see its leaves and stems above the ground. But under the soil, there is another important part: the roots. Roots grow downward and spread out. They help the plant stand firm in the ground. More importantly, roots are the part that takes in water and nutrients from the soil. Without roots, plants cannot live.

② Roots have thousands of tiny structures called root hairs. These root hairs look like very small threads. They grow on the surface of the roots and make the roots much bigger in total area. Why does this matter? Because water and minerals in the soil enter the plant through these root hairs. The more root hairs a plant has, the more water it can absorb. Some plants have roots that spread as wide as the plant is tall.

③ How does water move from the soil into the roots? The soil holds water in small spaces between soil particles. The root hairs touch the water in the soil. Through a process called osmosis, water moves from the soil into the root cells. The root cells have a higher concentration of dissolved substances than the soil water. So water naturally flows into the roots. Minerals dissolve in the water and enter the plant together.

④ Different plants have different root systems. Some plants, like carrots, have one main root that goes deep into the ground. We call this a taproot. Other plants, like grass, have many thin roots that spread in all directions. These are called fibrous roots. Both types work well for absorbing water, but they work best in different kinds of soil.

⑤ When there is not enough rain, the soil becomes dry. Roots must grow deeper to find water. That is why farmers sometimes water plants less to make roots grow stronger and deeper. Understanding how roots work helps us grow healthier plants and use water more wisely.

**注释：** nutrient 营养物质；root hair 根毛；osmosis 渗透；taproot 主根；fibrous 须状的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what is the main job of roots?
   A. To help the plant stand firm and take in water and nutrients from the soil.
   B. To make the plant look beautiful above the ground.
   C. To grow flowers and fruits.
   D. To protect the plant from insects.

2. Why are root hairs important for plants?
   A. They make the roots look longer.
   B. They increase the total area and help plants absorb more water.
   C. They keep the soil warm.
   D. They produce minerals for the plant.

3. According to paragraph ③, how does water enter the roots?
   A. Farmers pour water directly into the roots.
   B. Roots drink water like animals.
   C. Water moves from the soil into root cells through osmosis.
   D. Leaves take in water and send it to roots.

4. What is the difference between a taproot and fibrous roots?
   A. Taproots are thinner than fibrous roots.
   B. A taproot is one main deep root; fibrous roots are many thin roots spreading in different directions.
   C. Fibrous roots grow only in dry soil.
   D. Taproots absorb less water than fibrous roots.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Watering plants less sometimes helps roots grow deeper and stronger.
   B. Plants never need water.
   C. Dry soil is always bad for plants.
   D. Farmers should never water their plants.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "They help the plant stand firm in the ground. More importantly, roots are the part that takes in water and nutrients from the soil."

**【解析】** 根的作用是帮助植物稳固站立，更重要的是从土壤中吸收水分和营养物质。A 项正确。B 项美观、C 项开花结果、D 项防虫均非根的主要作用。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The more root hairs a plant has, the more water it can absorb."

**【解析】** 根毛增加根系总表面积，根毛越多，植物吸收的水分越多。B 项正确。A、C、D 项均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Through a process called osmosis, water moves from the soil into the root cells."

**【解析】** 水分通过渗透作用从土壤进入根细胞。C 项正确。A、B、D 项均未在文中提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some plants, like carrots, have one main root that goes deep into the ground. We call this a taproot. Other plants, like grass, have many thin roots that spread in all directions. These are called fibrous roots."

**【解析】** 主根是单一的深根；须根是许多细根向各方伸展。B 项准确。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "When there is not enough rain, the soil becomes dry. Roots must grow deeper to find water. That is why farmers sometimes water plants less to make roots grow stronger and deeper."

**【解析】** 土壤干旱时根会向深处生长；农民有时少浇水正是为了促进根系长得更深更壮。A 项正确。B、C、D 项与原文矛盾。故选 A。
