# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Jumping Rope Together

① Last month, our school held a rope skipping competition. Each class had to form a team of eight students. The rule was simple: the team that kept jumping the longest without any mistakes would win. Li Ming was one of our team members. He was good at sports, but rope skipping was not his best skill. At first, he often broke the rhythm and made the whole team stop. We lost several practice rounds because of him.

② "I'm sorry. I'm dragging the team down," Li Ming said after another failed practice. His face looked sad. Wang Fang, our team captain, patted him on the shoulder. "We are a team. We win together and we learn together. Nobody jumps alone," she said. She asked Li Ming to practice with her after class. Every afternoon, they jumped together in the playground. Wang Fang counted the rhythm aloud so Li Ming could follow. Slowly, Li Ming got better.

③ The day of the competition arrived. We stood in a line, waiting for the signal. My heart was beating fast. "One, two, three, jump!" Wang Fang called out. We jumped in perfect rhythm. Li Ming did not miss a single beat. The other teams dropped out one by one. We kept jumping. The crowd began to cheer. Finally, the judge announced, "Stop!" We had won first place. We hugged each other and laughed. Li Ming's eyes were bright with joy.

④ After the competition, our teacher said, "This is what teamwork looks like. When one person has difficulty, the whole team helps. That is how you achieve something great." Li Ming stood up and thanked Wang Fang and everyone. "I could not have done it without you," he said. We all knew it was true. One person's success in a team is everyone's success.

⑤ That rope skipping competition taught me an important lesson. We did not win because we had the best jumper. We won because we learned to work together, support each other, and never give up on any member of our team.

**注释：** rhythm 节奏；captain 队长；signal 信号；achieve 实现，获得

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, why did the team lose practice rounds at first?
   A. The rope was too short.
   B. Li Ming often broke the rhythm and made the team stop.
   C. Wang Fang was not a good captain.
   D. There were not enough team members.

2. What did Wang Fang do to help Li Ming?
   A. She practiced with him after class and counted the rhythm aloud.
   B. She asked him to leave the team.
   C. She found a new rope for him.
   D. She asked the teacher to change the rules.

3. How did Li Ming feel after the competition?
   A. He felt tired and wanted to rest.
   B. He felt sad because he thought he was still not good enough.
   C. His eyes were bright with joy; he was very happy.
   D. He felt angry at his teammates.

4. What did the teacher say about teamwork?
   A. The best jumper always wins.
   B. Students should practice alone.
   C. Rope skipping is the most important sport.
   D. When one person has difficulty, the whole team helps; that is how you achieve something great.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Teamwork, support, and never giving up on each other lead to success.
   B. Rope skipping is easy to learn.
   C. Li Ming is the best jumper in the school.
   D. Competitions are not important.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "At first, he often broke the rhythm and made the whole team stop. We lost several practice rounds because of him."

**【解析】** 最初 Li Ming 经常打断节奏导致全队停下，多次练习因此失败。B 项正确。A、C、D 项均未在文中提及。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "She asked Li Ming to practice with her after class. Every afternoon, they jumped together in the playground. Wang Fang counted the rhythm aloud so Li Ming could follow."

**【解析】** Wang Fang 放学后陪 Li Ming 练习，在操场上一起跳，并大声数节奏让他跟上。A 项正确。B、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Li Ming's eyes were bright with joy."

**【解析】** 比赛后 Li Ming 眼中充满喜悦，说明他非常高兴。C 项正确。A、B、D 项与原文描述相反。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "When one person has difficulty, the whole team helps. That is how you achieve something great."

**【解析】** 老师指出：当一个人遇到困难时，整个团队帮助他，这样才能成就大事。D 项准确。A、B、C 项与老师的话不符。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "We did not win because we had the best jumper. We won because we learned to work together, support each other, and never give up on any member of our team."

**【解析】** 文章通过跳绳比赛说明：获胜靠的是团队合作、互相支持、永不放弃任何成员，而非个人技术。A 项概括主旨。B、C、D 项均非文章重点。故选 A。
