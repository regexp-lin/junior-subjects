# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Dinosaurs (恐龙) lived on Earth for about 165 million years. Then, about 66 million years ago, they suddenly disappeared. What caused the extinction (灭绝) of dinosaurs? Scientists have been studying this question for many years. Today, most experts agree that a giant asteroid (小行星) hit the Earth, but there are other ideas too.

② The asteroid theory is the most widely accepted. According to this theory, a large asteroid, about 10 kilometres wide, struck the Earth near what is now Mexico. The impact (撞击) was so huge that it created a crater (环形山) 180 kilometres across. The explosion threw dust, rocks, and gas high into the sky. This dust blocked the sunlight for months or even years. Without enough sunlight, plants could not grow well. Many plants died. Plant-eating dinosaurs lost their food. Meat-eating dinosaurs then lost their prey (猎物). The whole food chain collapsed (崩溃). In addition, the impact may have caused huge forest fires, tsunamis, and extreme cold. The world became a very difficult place for dinosaurs to survive.

③ Some scientists also point to volcanic (火山的) activity. Around the same time, there were massive volcanic eruptions in India. These eruptions released large amounts of gas and dust into the air, which could also have changed the climate and harmed life. It is possible that both the asteroid and the volcanoes played a part. They may have worked together to make the environment unbearable for dinosaurs.

④ Why did some animals survive while dinosaurs died? Birds, small mammals (哺乳动物), and many other creatures lived through the disaster. Scientists think that smaller animals needed less food and could hide underground or in water. Some could eat seeds or insects that were still available. Dinosaurs were mostly large and needed a lot of food. When the plants died, they had no way to survive.

⑤ The dinosaur extinction teaches us an important lesson: sudden changes in the environment can have a huge effect on life. Understanding the past helps us protect the Earth today.

**注释：** dinosaur 恐龙；extinction 灭绝；asteroid 小行星；impact 撞击；crater 环形山；prey 猎物；collapse 崩溃；volcanic 火山的；mammal 哺乳动物

**Questions:**

1. According to the passage, when did dinosaurs become extinct?
   A. About 165 million years ago.
   B. About 66 million years ago.
   C. About 10 million years ago.
   D. Last year.

2. What happened when the asteroid hit the Earth?
   A. Nothing serious.
   B. Dust blocked sunlight; plants died; the food chain collapsed; there were fires, tsunamis, and extreme cold.
   C. Dinosaurs grew bigger.
   D. The asteroid brought more food.

3. Besides the asteroid, what else might have caused the extinction?
   A. Human activity.
   B. Massive volcanic eruptions in India that changed the climate.
   C. Too much rain.
   D. Cold winters only.

4. Why did some animals survive while dinosaurs died?
   A. Because they were stronger.
   B. Because they were smaller, needed less food, and could hide or eat seeds and insects.
   C. Because they lived in space.
   D. Because dinosaurs let them live.

5. What lesson does the writer say we can learn from dinosaur extinction?
   A. Dinosaurs were stupid.
   B. Sudden environmental changes can have a huge effect on life; understanding the past helps us protect the Earth.
   C. Asteroids are harmless.
   D. Volcanos are good for life.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "about 66 million years ago, they suddenly disappeared"

**【解析】** 原文明确说恐龙约在 6600 万年前消失。A、C、D 项时间错误。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "This dust blocked the sunlight... plants could not grow... The whole food chain collapsed... caused huge forest fires, tsunamis, and extreme cold"

**【解析】** 撞击导致尘埃遮蔽阳光、植物死亡、食物链崩溃及火灾、海啸、严寒。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "there were massive volcanic eruptions in India. These eruptions released... gas and dust... could also have changed the climate"

**【解析】** 印度大规模火山喷发释放气体和尘埃，可能改变气候。A、C、D 项原文未提及。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "smaller animals needed less food and could hide underground or in water. Some could eat seeds or insects... Dinosaurs were mostly large and needed a lot of food"

**【解析】** 小型动物需求少、可藏身、可食用种子或昆虫；恐龙体大食量大，难以存活。A、C、D 项与原文逻辑不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Sudden changes in the environment can have a huge effect on life. Understanding the past helps us protect the Earth today"

**【解析】** 作者指出环境剧变对生命影响巨大，了解过去有助于今日保护地球。A、C、D 项与主题无关。故选 B。
