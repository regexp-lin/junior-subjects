# 阅读理解（说明文）

**How Rainbows Are Formed**

① After a rain shower on a sunny day, you may see a beautiful rainbow in the sky. Rainbows have amazed people for thousands of years. But what exactly causes them? The answer lies in the way light behaves when it meets water.

② Sunlight looks white, but it is actually made up of seven colours: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet. These colours are always hidden inside white light. When sunlight enters a raindrop, something interesting happens: the light bends. This bending is called refraction.

③ Once inside the raindrop, the light hits the back wall of the drop and bounces back — this is called reflection. As the light leaves the raindrop, it bends again. Because each colour bends at a slightly different angle, the colours separate. Red light bends the least, so it appears on the outer edge of the rainbow. Violet light bends the most, so it appears on the inner edge.

④ To see a rainbow, you need three things: sunlight, raindrops, and the right position. The sun must be behind you, and the rain must be in front of you. That is why you usually see rainbows in the late afternoon, when the sun is low in the sky and there is rain in the opposite direction.

⑤ Sometimes you can see two rainbows at once. The brighter one is called the primary rainbow. Above it, there is sometimes a fainter one called the secondary rainbow. In the secondary rainbow, the order of colours is reversed — red is on the inside and violet is on the outside. This happens because light reflects twice inside the raindrop.

**注释：** refraction 折射；reflection 反射；indigo 靛蓝色；violet 紫色

**Questions:**

1. How many colours make up sunlight?
   A. Three.
   B. Five.
   C. Seven.
   D. Ten.

2. What is "refraction"?
   A. Light disappearing.
   B. Light bending when it enters or leaves a raindrop.
   C. Light becoming brighter.
   D. Light changing into heat.

3. Why do the colours separate inside a raindrop?
   A. Because the raindrop is very large.
   B. Because each colour bends at a slightly different angle.
   C. Because the rain is cold.
   D. Because the sun is very hot.

4. What conditions are needed to see a rainbow?
   A. The sun must be in front of you.
   B. It must be raining heavily with no sun.
   C. The sun must be behind you, with rain in front.
   D. You must be standing in a river.

5. What is special about the secondary rainbow?
   A. It has more colours.
   B. It is always brighter than the primary rainbow.
   C. The order of colours is reversed compared to the primary rainbow.
   D. It can only be seen at night.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "it is actually made up of seven colours: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet."

**【解析】** 原文明确列出了七种颜色。A项三种、B项五种、D项十种均与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第②段 "When sunlight enters a raindrop, something interesting happens: the light bends. This bending is called refraction."

**【解析】** 原文解释说光线进入雨滴时发生弯曲，这种弯曲就叫折射。A项"光消失"、C项"光变亮"、D项"光变成热"均不是折射的定义。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Because each colour bends at a slightly different angle, the colours separate."

**【解析】** 原文说因为每种颜色弯曲的角度略有不同，所以颜色会分离。A项"雨滴很大"、C项"雨冷"、D项"太阳很热"均不是颜色分离的原因。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The sun must be behind you, and the rain must be in front of you."

**【解析】** 原文说太阳必须在你身后，雨在你前方。A项太阳在前方与原文相反；B项大雨无阳光则看不到彩虹；D项站在河里原文未提及。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In the secondary rainbow, the order of colours is reversed — red is on the inside and violet is on the outside."

**【解析】** 副虹的颜色顺序与主虹相反。A项"颜色更多"不对，颜色数量相同；B项原文说副虹更暗（fainter），不是更亮；D项"只能在夜晚看到"不对，彩虹需要阳光。故选C。
