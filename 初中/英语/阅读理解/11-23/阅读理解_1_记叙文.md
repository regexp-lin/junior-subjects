> **11-23 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Helping a Lost Grandmother**

① It was a cold afternoon in November. I was walking home from school when I noticed an old woman standing at a crossroads. She looked confused and kept turning around, as if she did not know which way to go. I stopped and asked, "Grandma, are you all right? Do you need help?"

② She looked at me with worried eyes. "I... I can't remember how to get home," she said in a shaky voice. "I went to buy vegetables, but now everything looks different." I could see she was frightened. I asked her if she remembered her address or her family's phone number. She shook her head slowly.

③ I did not know what to do at first. Then I noticed she was carrying a small cloth bag. I asked if I could look inside for any clues. She nodded. In the bag, I found a medicine box with a sticker on it. The sticker had a name and a phone number — it was probably from the hospital. I quickly called the number, but it was the hospital's reception, not her family.

④ I decided to take her to the nearest police station, which was only five minutes away. On the way, I held her arm gently so she would not stumble. She kept saying, "Thank you, child. You are so kind." At the police station, the officers searched their system and found her son's phone number. They called him, and he arrived within twenty minutes. He was almost in tears. "Mum has Alzheimer's disease," he explained. "She sometimes forgets where she is."

⑤ When they left, the grandmother turned around and waved at me with a smile. That smile warmed my heart on that cold day. I was glad I stopped to help. Sometimes a small act of kindness can make a big difference in someone's life.

**注释：** crossroads 十字路口；stumble 绊倒；Alzheimer's disease 阿尔茨海默症（老年痴呆症）

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did the writer meet the old woman?
   A. At a hospital.
   B. At a crossroads.
   C. At a police station.
   D. At a vegetable market.

2. Why was the old woman confused?
   A. She lost her bag.
   B. She could not remember how to get home.
   C. She was looking for a shop.
   D. She wanted to find a school.

3. What clue did the writer find in the old woman's bag?
   A. A map.
   B. A letter from her son.
   C. A medicine box with a name and phone number on a sticker.
   D. Her home address.

4. What did the old woman's son explain?
   A. His mother often goes shopping alone.
   B. His mother has Alzheimer's disease and sometimes forgets where she is.
   C. His mother does not like going out.
   D. His mother was visiting friends.

5. What can we learn from the story?
   A. Old people should never go out alone.
   B. The police cannot help lost people.
   C. A small act of kindness can make a big difference.
   D. The writer was late for school because of the old woman.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I was walking home from school when I noticed an old woman standing at a crossroads."

**【解析】** 原文说作者在十字路口看到了老人。A项医院、C项警察局、D项菜市场均非相遇地点。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "'I... I can't remember how to get home,' she said in a shaky voice."

**【解析】** 老人自己说不记得回家的路了。A项"丢了包"不对，包在手上；C项"找商店"和D项"找学校"均非原因。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I found a medicine box with a sticker on it. The sticker had a name and a phone number"

**【解析】** 作者在包里找到了一个药盒，上面的贴纸写有姓名和电话号码。A项地图、B项儿子的信、D项家庭地址均非原文所述。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "'Mum has Alzheimer's disease,' he explained. 'She sometimes forgets where she is.'"

**【解析】** 儿子解释说母亲患有阿尔茨海默症，有时会忘记自己在哪里。A项"经常独自购物"虽然可能存在但不是他说明的内容；C项"不喜欢出门"和D项"去拜访朋友"原文均未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Sometimes a small act of kindness can make a big difference in someone's life."

**【解析】** 作者在文末总结：一个小小的善举可以对某人的生活产生重大影响。A项"老人永远不该独自外出"过于绝对且非文章主旨；B项"警察帮不了走失的人"与文中警察成功联系家属相反；D项"上学迟到"不对，作者是放学回家途中。故选C。
