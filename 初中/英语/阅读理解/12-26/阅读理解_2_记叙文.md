> **12-26 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**The Old Photo in the Drawer**

① Last Sunday, I was cleaning my room. I opened the bottom drawer of my desk and found an old photo. It was faded and a bit yellow. I held it up and looked closely. There was a little boy standing in front of a small house. He was wearing a grey coat and a big smile. Next to him stood a tall woman in a blue dress. They were my grandfather and grandmother. I had never seen them so young.

② I took the photo to my father. "When was this taken?" I asked. He looked at it and smiled. "That was in 1975," he said. "Your grandfather was eight years old. The small house was where he grew up. It was in the countryside. There was no running water. They used a well. In winter, it was very cold. But your grandfather said those were the happiest days of his life."

③ I was surprised. "Why were they the happiest? Life was so hard." My father said, "Because the family was together. Grandfather had four brothers and two sisters. They played in the fields, helped on the farm, and ate meals together every day. Grandma cooked simple food, but everyone shared it. There was love and laughter. That is what mattered."

④ I looked at the photo again. The small boy and the woman in the blue dress seemed to look at me. I thought about my own life. I have a warm home, good food, and a mobile phone. But do I have what they had? Do I spend enough time with my family? I put the photo on my desk. I decided to visit my grandparents more often and listen to their stories.

⑤ That old photo taught me something. It is not the things we have that make us happy. It is the people we love and the time we spend with them. I will keep this photo forever.

**注释：** drawer 抽屉；fade 褪色；well 井；laughter 笑声

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did the writer find the old photo?
   A. In a book.
   B. In the bottom drawer of the desk.
   C. In the garden.
   D. In grandmother's house.

2. When was the photo taken?
   A. 1965.
   B. 1975.
   C. 1985.
   D. 1995.

3. Why did grandfather say those were the happiest days?
   A. Because he had a lot of money.
   B. Because the family was together with love and laughter.
   C. Because the house was big.
   D. Because there was running water.

4. What did the writer decide to do after looking at the photo?
   A. Throw away the photo.
   B. Visit grandparents more often and listen to their stories.
   C. Buy a new photo.
   D. Move to the countryside.

5. What did the writer learn from the old photo?
   A. Old photos are more valuable than new ones.
   B. Happiness comes from the people we love and the time we spend with them.
   C. Life was better in the past.
   D. We need bigger houses.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I opened the bottom drawer of my desk and found an old photo."

**【解析】** 作者在书桌最底层抽屉里发现了老照片。A项书里、C项花园、D项祖母家均不正确。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "That was in 1975," he said.

**【解析】** 父亲说这张照片摄于1975年。A、C、D项年份均错误。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Because the family was together... There was love and laughter. That is what mattered."

**【解析】** 祖父说最幸福是因为一家人在一起，有爱与欢笑。A项有很多钱、C项房子大、D项有自来水均非原因。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "I decided to visit my grandparents more often and listen to their stories."

**【解析】** 作者决定多看望祖父母并听他们讲故事。A项扔掉照片、C项买新照片、D项搬到乡下均与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "It is not the things we have that make us happy. It is the people we love and the time we spend with them."

**【解析】** 作者领悟到幸福来自所爱之人与相伴的时光，而非物质。A、C、D项均未准确概括这一感悟。故选B。
