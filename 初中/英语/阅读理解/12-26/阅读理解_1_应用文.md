# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Save Water — Tips for Students and Families**

① Water is precious. We need it for drinking, cooking, washing, and growing food. Yet many people waste water every day. Here are some simple tips to save water at home and at school.

**Tips at home:**

| Place | What to do |
|-------|------------|
| Bathroom | Take shorter showers; turn off the tap when brushing teeth |
| Kitchen | Use a bowl when washing vegetables; reuse rice water for plants |
| Laundry | Wash full loads only; fix leaks quickly |
| Garden | Water plants early morning or evening; use a watering can |

② **Shorter showers.** A ten-minute shower can use about 100 litres of water. Try to keep it under five minutes. While you put on shampoo or soap, turn off the water. You can save a lot.

③ **Fix leaks.** A dripping tap can waste 20 litres of water a day. Check your taps and pipes. If you see a leak, tell your parents. A quick fix can save thousands of litres each year.

④ **Reuse when possible.** When you wash vegetables, collect the water in a bowl. Use it to water plants. Rice water from cooking is good for plants too. Do not pour it down the drain. Use it in the garden or for potted plants.

⑤ **At school.** Report any leaking taps or toilets to the school office. In the canteen, take only the water you will drink. Do not fill a cup and then throw it away. Every drop counts. Small actions, when done by many people, can make a big difference.

**注释：** litre 升；leak 漏水；shampoo 洗发水；drain 排水管

---

**Questions:**

1. How much water can a ten-minute shower use?
   A. About 20 litres.
   B. About 50 litres.
   C. About 100 litres.
   D. About 200 litres.

2. Why should you turn off the tap when brushing teeth?
   A. To save electricity.
   B. To save water.
   C. To make brushing faster.
   D. To keep the tap clean.

3. How much water can a dripping tap waste in a day?
   A. 5 litres.
   B. 20 litres.
   C. 100 litres.
   D. 1000 litres.

4. What can you do with rice water from cooking?
   A. Drink it.
   B. Use it to water plants.
   C. Pour it down the drain only.
   D. Use it for washing clothes.

5. What should students do in the school canteen to save water?
   A. Fill many cups with water.
   B. Take only the water they will drink.
   C. Never drink water at school.
   D. Throw away old water every day.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "A ten-minute shower can use about 100 litres of water."

**【解析】** 原文说十分钟淋浴约用100升水。A、B、D项数量均不正确。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 表格 "turn off the tap when brushing teeth" 及全文主题为节水

**【解析】** 刷牙时关掉水龙头是为了节约用水。A项省电、C项刷更快、D项保持龙头干净均非主要目的。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "A dripping tap can waste 20 litres of water a day."

**【解析】** 滴水龙头一天可浪费20升水。A、C、D项数量均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Rice water from cooking is good for plants too. Do not pour it down the drain. Use it in the garden or for potted plants."

**【解析】** 煮饭的米水可浇花，不要直接倒掉。A项饮用、C项只倒下水道、D项洗衣服均非建议。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In the canteen, take only the water you will drink. Do not fill a cup and then throw it away."

**【解析】** 在食堂只接要喝的水，不要接满又倒掉。A、C、D项均与节约用水建议矛盾。故选B。
