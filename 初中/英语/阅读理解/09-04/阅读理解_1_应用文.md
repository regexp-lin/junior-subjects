> **09-04 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Community Volunteer (志愿者) Recruitment**

② Dear residents (居民),

③ Green Lake Community Center is looking for volunteers to help with our autumn programs. We need your support to make our community a better place for everyone. Whether you have a lot of time or just a few hours, your help is welcome!

④ **Volunteer positions:**

| Position | Task | Time needed | Contact |
|----------|------|-------------|---------|
| Reading Partner | Read with primary school children every Saturday morning | 2 hours/week | Ms. Liu 138-xxxx-5678 |
| Garden Helper | Help take care of the community garden (watering, weeding) | Flexible | Mr. Zhang 139-xxxx-1234 |
| Senior Companion | Visit elderly people living alone, chat with them | 1–2 hours/week | Ms. Chen 137-xxxx-9012 |
| Event Helper | Help at community festivals and activities | On event days | Office 8765-4321 |

⑤ **Requirements:**
- You must be at least 14 years old
- You should be patient, responsible, and willing to help others
- For the Reading Partner role: you need to enjoy working with children

⑥ **How to apply:**
- Call or visit the Community Center (No. 88 Lake Road) during office hours (9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m., Monday–Friday)
- Or fill in the online form on our website: www.greenlake-community.org
- Application deadline: October 15, 2024

⑦ **What you will get:**
- A volunteer certificate after 20 hours of service
- Training before you start
- New friends and a sense of belonging

⑧ If you have any questions, please contact us. We look forward to working with you!

Green Lake Community Center  
September 1, 2024

**注释：** resident 居民；volunteer 志愿者

---

**Questions:**

1. What is Green Lake Community Center looking for?
   A. New residents to move in.
   B. Volunteers to help with autumn programs.
   C. People to buy the community garden.
   D. Teachers for primary schools.

2. If someone wants to read with children, who should they contact?
   A. Mr. Zhang.
   B. Ms. Chen.
   C. Ms. Liu.
   D. The office.

3. What is the minimum age for volunteers?
   A. 10 years old.
   B. 12 years old.
   C. 14 years old.
   D. 18 years old.

4. How can people apply to be volunteers?
   A. Only by sending an email.
   B. By calling, visiting the center, or filling in the online form.
   C. By writing a letter only.
   D. They cannot apply after October 15.

5. What will volunteers get after 20 hours of service?
   A. A lot of money.
   B. A volunteer certificate.
   C. A free house.
   D. A job at the center.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Green Lake Community Center is looking for volunteers to help with our autumn programs."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明社区中心正在招募秋季项目志愿者。A项"新居民入住"、C项"购买社区花园"和D项"小学教师"均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段表格 "Reading Partner | ... | Ms. Liu 138-xxxx-5678"

**【解析】** 表格中阅读伙伴岗位的联系人为 Ms. Liu。A项为园艺助手；B项为老人陪伴；D项为活动协助。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "You must be at least 14 years old"

**【解析】** 原文明确要求志愿者至少14岁。A、B项年龄过低；D项"18岁"与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Call or visit the Community Center... Or fill in the online form on our website"

**【解析】** 申请方式包括致电、亲访或填写网站在线表格。A项"仅发邮件"不完整；C项"仅写信"与原文不符；D项"10月15日后不能申请"表述不准确，截止日期为10月15日。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "A volunteer certificate after 20 hours of service"

**【解析】** 原文明确写满20小时服务后可获得志愿者证书。A项"很多钱"、C项"免费房子"和D项"中心工作"均未提及。故选B。
