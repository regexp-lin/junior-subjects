> **11-25 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Moving to a New City**

① Last September, my family moved from a small town in Hunan to Guangzhou because of my dad's job. I did not want to go. I had lived in my hometown for thirteen years. All my friends were there. My favourite park, my school, my grandparents — everything I loved was there.

② The first week in Guangzhou was hard. The city was huge and noisy. People spoke Cantonese, which I could not understand. At my new school, everyone already had their own friend groups. During lunch, I sat alone. I missed my old friends so much that I called them every night.

③ Things began to change in the second week. A boy named Chen Hao sat next to me in class. He noticed I was quiet and asked, "Are you new here? Where are you from?" I told him I was from Hunan. He smiled and said, "My mum is from Hunan too! She makes amazing spicy food." We started talking about food, and before I knew it, we were laughing together.

④ Chen Hao introduced me to his friends. They took me to eat dim sum, taught me a few Cantonese words, and showed me around the neighbourhood. Slowly, I began to feel at home. I also joined the school basketball team, which helped me meet even more people. By the end of the first month, I had a group of friends I could count on.

⑤ Moving to a new city is never easy. You leave behind familiar things and face the unknown. But I learned that home is not just a place — it is the people around you. As long as you open your heart, you can build a new home anywhere.

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer's family move to Guangzhou?
   A. Because of his mum's job.
   B. Because of his dad's job.
   C. Because he wanted to go to a better school.
   D. Because his grandparents lived there.

2. What was the writer's biggest problem in the first week?
   A. He could not find the school.
   B. He had no friends and felt lonely.
   C. The weather was too hot.
   D. He did not like the food.

3. How did Chen Hao start a conversation with the writer?
   A. He asked the writer to play basketball.
   B. He gave the writer a gift.
   C. He asked where the writer was from.
   D. He invited the writer to his house.

4. What helped the writer make more friends?
   A. Moving back to Hunan.
   B. Joining the school basketball team.
   C. Calling his old friends every night.
   D. Learning to cook dim sum.

5. What is the main message of the story?
   A. Big cities are better than small towns.
   B. You should never move to a new place.
   C. Home is not just a place; it is the people around you.
   D. Making friends is impossible in a new city.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "my family moved from a small town in Hunan to Guangzhou because of my dad's job."

**【解析】** 原文说因为爸爸工作的原因搬到广州。A项妈妈的工作不对；C项上更好的学校和D项爷爷奶奶在那里均非原因。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "At my new school, everyone already had their own friend groups. During lunch, I sat alone. I missed my old friends so much that I called them every night."

**【解析】** 从独自吃午饭、每晚给老朋友打电话等细节可以推断作者最大的问题是没有朋友、感到孤独。A项找不到学校、C项天气太热、D项不喜欢食物均非主要问题。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "He noticed I was quiet and asked, 'Are you new here? Where are you from?'"

**【解析】** 陈浩问作者是不是新来的、从哪里来，以此开始对话。A项打篮球、B项送礼物、D项邀请去家里均非开始对话的方式。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "I also joined the school basketball team, which helped me meet even more people."

**【解析】** 原文说加入校篮球队帮助他认识了更多人。A项搬回湖南不对，他留在了广州；C项给老朋友打电话不能帮他交新朋友；D项学做点心原文未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I learned that home is not just a place — it is the people around you."

**【解析】** 作者最终领悟到家不仅是一个地方，更是身边的人。A项"大城市更好"不是文章主旨；B项"永远不该搬家"与作者的成长经历矛盾；D项"在新城市交朋友不可能"与作者成功交友的事实矛盾。故选C。
