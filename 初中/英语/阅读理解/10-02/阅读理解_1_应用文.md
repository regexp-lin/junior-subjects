# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **City History Museum — Visitor Guide**

② Welcome to the City History Museum! We have over 50,000 objects that tell the story of our city from ancient times to the present day. To make your visit enjoyable, please follow this guide.

③ **Opening Hours:**
- Tuesday to Sunday: 9:00 a.m. — 5:00 p.m. (last entry 4:30 p.m.)
- Closed on Mondays (except public holidays)
- Free admission (免费入场) for children under 6 and visitors over 70

④ **How to Visit:**
- **Tickets:** Buy at the entrance or book online at www.citymuseum.gov. Adults ¥30, students (with ID) ¥15.
- **Maps:** Pick up a free map at the information desk.
- **Guided Tours:** Free tours at 10:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m. Meet at the main hall. Each tour lasts about 90 minutes.
- **Audio Guides:** Rent for ¥10 at the entrance. Available in Chinese and English.
- **Photography:** You may take photos in most areas, but no flash (闪光灯). No photos in the Special Exhibition Hall.

⑤ **Rules:**
- No food or drinks inside the halls. There is a café on the first floor.
- No touching the exhibits (展品). Stay behind the rope lines.
- Keep your voice low. Mobile phones on silent mode.
- Bags larger than 40 cm must be left at the cloakroom (存包处).
- No running or playing inside the museum.

⑥ **Facilities:**
- Toilets and drinking fountains on each floor
- Wheelchair access and lifts for visitors with disabilities
- Gift shop near the exit
- Free WiFi (password: museum2024)

**注释：** admission 入场；flash 闪光灯；exhibit 展品；cloakroom 存包处

**Questions:**

1. When is the museum closed?
   A. Every day at 5:00 p.m.
   B. On Mondays (except public holidays).
   C. On Sundays.
   D. Only in the morning.

2. How much does a student pay for a ticket?
   A. ¥30.
   B. ¥15 with student ID.
   C. ¥10.
   D. Free.

3. When do the free guided tours start?
   A. At 9:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m.
   B. At 10:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m.
   C. Only on weekends.
   D. Every hour.

4. Where can visitors buy food or drinks?
   A. In any hall.
   B. In the café on the first floor (no food or drinks inside the halls).
   C. At the entrance only.
   D. In the gift shop.

5. What must visitors do with bags larger than 40 cm?
   A. Take them into the halls.
   B. Leave them at the cloakroom.
   C. Give them to the guide.
   D. Put them outside the museum.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Closed on Mondays (except public holidays)"

**【解析】** 博物馆周一闭馆（公共假日除外）。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "students (with ID) ¥15"

**【解析】** 持学生证票价 ¥15。A、C、D 项金额或条件错误。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Free tours at 10:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 免费讲解在上午 10 点和下午 2 点。A、C、D 项时间错误。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "No food or drinks inside the halls. There is a café on the first floor"

**【解析】** 展厅内禁止饮食，一层设咖啡馆，可推知饮食应在咖啡馆。A、C、D 项与规定矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Bags larger than 40 cm must be left at the cloakroom"

**【解析】** 超过 40 厘米的包须寄存。A、C、D 项与规定不符。故选 B。
