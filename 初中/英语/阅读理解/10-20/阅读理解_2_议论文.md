# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Only Child vs. Children with Siblings**

① In China, the one-child policy ended in 2016. Now families can have two or three children. But many parents still choose to have only one. Is it better to be an only child or to grow up with siblings? There are different views.

② Those who prefer having only one child say it has advantages. The child gets more attention and resources from parents. There is no competition for love or toys. Parents can afford better education and activities. The child may become more independent because he or she often plays alone. Some only children are very successful in their careers.

③ On the other hand, people who support having more than one child argue that siblings teach important lessons. Brothers and sisters learn to share, to solve problems, and to care for others. They have playmates at home and are less lonely. When parents grow old, the children can share the responsibility. Growing up with siblings can also prepare children for working in teams later in life.

④ However, there are downsides to both. Only children may feel lonely and lack social skills. Children with many siblings may get less attention each. Siblings sometimes fight and argue. There is no perfect family size. Each family is different. What works for one may not work for another.

⑤ In the end, love and care matter more than the number of children. Whether a child is alone or has siblings, what counts is that parents give enough attention, teach good values, and create a warm home. Every child deserves to feel loved and supported.

**注释：** sibling 兄弟姐妹；downside 缺点

---

**Questions:**

1. When did China's one-child policy end?
   A. In 2010.
   B. In 2015.
   C. In 2016.
   D. In 2020.

2. According to Paragraph ②, which is an advantage of being an only child?
   A. The child has to share everything.
   B. The child gets more attention and resources from parents.
   C. The child never feels lonely.
   D. The child cannot be independent.

3. What can siblings help children learn?
   A. Only how to fight.
   B. To share, solve problems, and care for others.
   C. To stay away from parents.
   D. To avoid teamwork.

4. What does the writer think matters most?
   A. The number of children in a family.
   B. Love and care, regardless of family size.
   C. Having as many children as possible.
   D. Only children are always better.

5. What can we infer from Paragraph ④?
   A. Only children are always happier.
   B. Both only children and those with siblings can face challenges; there is no ideal family size.
   C. Siblings never fight.
   D. All families should have the same number of children.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "the one-child policy ended in 2016"
**【解析】** 独生子女政策于2016年结束。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The child gets more attention and resources from parents"
**【解析】** 独生子女获得父母更多关注和资源。B项准确。A、C、D项与独生子女优势不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Brothers and sisters learn to share, to solve problems, and to care for others"
**【解析】** 兄弟姐妹能学习分享、解决问题和关心他人。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "love and care matter more than the number of children... what counts is that parents give enough attention, teach good values, and create a warm home"
**【解析】** 作者认为爱与关怀比孩子数量更重要。B项正确。A、C、D项与作者观点不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第④段 "There are downsides to both... There is no perfect family size. Each family is different"
**【解析】** 独生子女与多子女各有弊端，没有完美家庭规模，每家情况不同。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
