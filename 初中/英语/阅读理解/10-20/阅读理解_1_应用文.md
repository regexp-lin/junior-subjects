# 阅读理解（应用文）

**City Marathon — Runner's Guide**

① Welcome to the 2024 City Marathon! Please read this guide carefully before race day.

**Race Day: November 10th, 2024**

② **Start time and place:** The half marathon (21 km) starts at 7:00 a.m. at the Central Square. The 5-km fun run starts at 7:30 a.m. at the same place. Please arrive at least 45 minutes early to collect your number and warm up.

③ **What to bring:** Your ID card, the confirmation email, and comfortable running shoes. We will provide a T-shirt, a cap, and water along the route. Do not bring bags or heavy items. There is a bag-drop area near the start line.

④ **Route:** The half marathon goes through the city centre, along the river, and finishes at the Sports Park. The 5-km run is a shorter loop around the square. Maps are available on our website: www.citymarathon.com.

⑤ **After the race:** Free water and fruit will be provided at the finish line. A medal will be given to every runner who completes the race. Results will be posted online within 24 hours. For any questions, call 020-88881234 or email info@citymarathon.com.

Have a great run!

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the half marathon start?
   A. At 6:30 a.m.
   B. At 7:00 a.m.
   C. At 7:30 a.m.
   D. At 8:00 a.m.

2. Where can runners leave their bags?
   A. At home.
   B. At the bag-drop area near the start line.
   C. Along the route.
   D. At the finish line only.

3. Which of the following will NOT be provided by the organiser?
   A. A T-shirt and cap.
   B. Water along the route.
   C. Comfortable running shoes.
   D. A medal for finishers.

4. How long is the half marathon?
   A. 5 km.
   B. 10 km.
   C. 21 km.
   D. 42 km.

5. What can we infer from the guide?
   A. Runners can start at any time they like.
   B. Runners need to prepare and arrive early for a smooth race experience.
   C. No medals are given.
   D. The 5-km run is longer than the half marathon.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The half marathon (21 km) starts at 7:00 a.m."
**【解析】** 半程马拉松7:00开跑。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "There is a bag-drop area near the start line"
**【解析】** 起点附近有存包区。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "We will provide a T-shirt, a cap, and water... Do not bring bags"；第⑤段 "A medal will be given"
**【解析】** 组委会提供T恤、帽子、水和奖牌。跑步鞋需自备。C项正确。A、B、D均为组委会提供。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The half marathon (21 km)"
**【解析】** 半程马拉松为21公里。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "arrive at least 45 minutes early"；全文对流程、装备、路线的说明
**【解析】** 指南要求提前到达、自备装备、了解路线，可见需提前准备以保证顺利参赛。B项正确。A、C、D项与指南内容矛盾。故选B。
