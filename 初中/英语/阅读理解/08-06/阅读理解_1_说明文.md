## Why Did Dinosaurs Disappear?

①About 66 million years ago, dinosaurs ruled the Earth. These huge animals had lived on our planet for over 150 million years. Then, in a short time, most of them disappeared. Scientists have been studying this mystery for a long time. Today, they have some possible answers.

②The most widely accepted idea is that a large asteroid hit the Earth. This space rock was about 10 kilometres wide. When it crashed into the ocean near Mexico, it caused huge explosions. Dust and rocks flew into the sky and covered the sun for months. Without enough sunlight, plants could not grow well. Many animals that ate plants died. The dinosaurs that ate those animals died too.

③Some scientists think volcanic eruptions also played a part. At that time, there were very strong volcanic activities in India. The volcanoes sent out large amounts of gas and dust. This made the air and water warmer and more polluted. The changing environment made it hard for many living things to survive.

④Climate change may have been another cause. After the asteroid hit, the temperature on Earth dropped quickly. The warm-blooded dinosaurs could not get used to the cold weather. Some small animals, like birds and mammals, could hide in holes or find food more easily. They survived and later became the animals we know today.

⑤We may never know exactly what happened. Perhaps it was the asteroid, or the volcanoes, or both together. What we do know is that the dinosaurs' story teaches us an important lesson: our planet can change in surprising ways. We must protect the Earth and all the living things on it.

- **mammal**: 哺乳动物

**【答案】1. A  2. D  3. A  4. D  5. C**

**【1题解析】**
- **题型**：细节理解题
- **定位**：第①段 "These huge animals had lived on our planet for over 150 million years."
- **解析**：原文明确说明恐龙在地球上生活了超过1.5亿年，故答案选A。

**【2题解析】**
- **题型**：细节理解题
- **定位**：第②段 "Dust and rocks flew into the sky and covered the sun for months."
- **解析**：小行星撞击后，尘埃和岩石飞入天空，遮蔽阳光数月，故答案选D。

**【3题解析】**
- **题型**：细节理解题
- **定位**：第③段 "The volcanoes sent out large amounts of gas and dust. This made the air and water warmer and more polluted. The changing environment made it hard for many living things to survive."
- **解析**：火山喷发释放大量气体和尘埃，污染空气和水，改变环境，使生物难以生存，故答案选A。

**【4题解析】**
- **题型**：推理判断题
- **定位**：第④段 "Some small animals, like birds and mammals, could hide in holes or find food more easily. They survived and later became the animals we know today."
- **解析**：可推理出鸟类和哺乳动物等小动物因能更好地适应新环境而存活下来，故答案选D。

**【5题解析】**
- **题型**：写作意图题
- **定位**：全文，尤其是第⑤段 "our planet can change in surprising ways. We must protect the Earth and all the living things on it."
- **解析**：文章介绍恐龙灭绝的可能原因，并在结尾呼吁人类保护地球，故答案选C。
