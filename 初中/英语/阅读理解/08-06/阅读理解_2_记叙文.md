> **08-06 第2篇**

## Lucy's Lemonade Stand

①Last Saturday, our school held a charity sale to raise money for children in need. Many students brought old books, toys and clothes to sell. But Lucy had a different idea—she wanted to sell homemade lemonade.

②Lucy got up at six in the morning. She squeezed fresh lemons, added sugar and water, and made a large jug of lemonade. She also made a colourful sign that said "Lucy's Lemonade—Help Those in Need." Her mother drove her to school with the jug and some paper cups.

③At first, few people stopped at her table. Lucy felt a little worried. Then she stood up and called out, "Fresh lemonade! Only two yuan a cup! All money goes to children who need help!" Her friendly smile and loud voice caught people's attention. Soon, a long queue formed in front of her stand.

④By noon, Lucy had sold all her lemonade. She had collected 186 yuan. Her teacher was very proud. "You did a wonderful job, Lucy!" she said. "Your creative idea and hard work really made a difference." Lucy felt happy. She knew that every cup she sold would help someone far away.

⑤That evening, Lucy told her family about the day. "I learned that we don't need a lot of money to do something good," she said. "Sometimes a simple idea and a little effort can bring warmth to others." Her father smiled. "You're right. And you've shown us all how one student can make a real difference."

---

**Notes**
- **charity**: 慈善
- **squeeze**: 榨（汁）
- **jug**: 大壶；罐
- **queue**: 队伍；队列

---

**1.** What did Lucy decide to sell at the school charity sale?

A. Homemade lemonade.
B. Old books and toys.
C. Clothes and shoes.
D. Fresh fruit.

**2.** What did Lucy do when few people stopped at her table at first?

A. She gave up and went home.
B. She asked her mother to help her sell.
C. She lowered the price to one yuan.
D. She stood up and called out to attract people, and smiled at them.

**3.** How much money did Lucy collect by noon?

A. 86 yuan.
B. 266 yuan.
C. 186 yuan.
D. 286 yuan.

**4.** What can we infer from Paragraph 5?

A. Lucy realized that small actions can also help others in meaningful ways.
B. Lucy's family did not support her idea.
C. Lucy decided never to join a charity sale again.
D. Lucy's father thought her idea was too simple.

**5.** What does the writer mainly want to tell us through Lucy's story?

A. Lemonade is the best drink for a charity sale.
B. Parents should always drive their children to school activities.
C. One student's creative idea and effort can make a difference to those in need.
D. Charity sales are only successful when many people join.

---

**【答案】1. A  2. D  3. C  4. A  5. C**

**【1题解析】**
- **题型**：细节理解题
- **定位**：第①段 "But Lucy had a different idea—she wanted to sell homemade lemonade."
- **解析**：Lucy 决定在义卖活动中卖自制的柠檬水，故答案选A。

**【2题解析】**
- **题型**：细节理解题
- **定位**：第③段 "Then she stood up and called out... Her friendly smile and loud voice caught people's attention."
- **解析**：Lucy 站起来大声吆喝并保持友好微笑吸引顾客，故答案选D。

**【3题解析】**
- **题型**：细节理解题
- **定位**：第④段 "By noon, Lucy had sold all her lemonade. She had collected 186 yuan."
- **解析**：中午前 Lucy 卖完所有柠檬水，共筹得 186 元，故答案选C。

**【4题解析】**
- **题型**：推理判断题
- **定位**：第⑤段 "I learned that we don't need a lot of money to do something good... Sometimes a simple idea and a little effort can bring warmth to others."
- **解析**：可推理出 Lucy 领悟到简单的想法和一点努力也能帮助他人、带来温暖，故答案选A。

**【5题解析】**
- **题型**：主旨大意题
- **定位**：全文，尤其是第④、⑤段老师与父亲的评价及 Lucy 的感悟
- **解析**：文章通过 Lucy 卖自制柠檬水筹款的故事，传达"一个学生的创意和努力也能帮助他人"的主旨，故答案选C。
