# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Bees are small insects, but they play a very important role in our ecosystem (生态系统). Without bees, many of the foods we eat every day would disappear. Scientists say that about one-third of the food we consume (消费) depends on pollination (传粉), and bees are the main pollinators.

② When bees fly from flower to flower to collect nectar (花蜜), they carry pollen (花粉) on their legs and bodies. This pollen sticks to the next flower they visit, which helps plants produce fruits and seeds. Apples, oranges, strawberries, almonds (杏仁), and many vegetables like tomatoes and cucumbers all need bees to grow. In this way, bees help farmers and feed billions of people around the world.

③ Bees also make honey, which humans have enjoyed for thousands of years. Honey is not only sweet and delicious but also has health benefits. It can help soothe (舒缓) a sore throat and is sometimes used in traditional medicine. Beekeepers (养蜂人) take care of bee colonies and collect honey without harming the bees too much.

④ Unfortunately, bee populations are declining (下降) in many places. The reasons include loss of natural habitat (栖息地), use of pesticides (杀虫剂), climate change, and diseases. When fewer bees exist, fewer plants get pollinated, and food production drops. This affects not only humans but also other animals that depend on these plants.

⑤ What can we do to help? We can plant more flowers in our gardens or on balconies (阳台) to give bees food. We can avoid using harmful chemicals in our gardens. We can also support local beekeepers by buying their honey. Every small action counts. Protecting bees means protecting our future food supply.

**注释：** ecosystem 生态系统；consume 消费；pollination 传粉；nectar 花蜜；pollen 花粉；almond 杏仁；soothe 舒缓；beekeeper 养蜂人；decline 下降；habitat 栖息地；pesticide 杀虫剂；balcony 阳台

**Questions:**

1. According to the passage, how much of our food depends on pollination?
   A. About one-tenth.
   B. About one-third.
   C. About one-half.
   D. About two-thirds.

2. What do bees do when they move from flower to flower?
   A. They only eat nectar.
   B. They carry pollen and help plants produce fruits and seeds.
   C. They build their nests.
   D. They fight with other insects.

3. Which of the following is NOT a reason for the decline of bees?
   A. Loss of natural habitat.
   B. Use of pesticides.
   C. Planting more flowers.
   D. Climate change.

4. What can people do to help bees?
   A. Use more chemicals in gardens.
   B. Plant more flowers and avoid harmful chemicals.
   C. Catch all the bees.
   D. Stop eating honey.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Bees are dangerous and should be avoided.
   B. Bees are important for our ecosystem and food supply; we should protect them.
   C. Honey is the only reason we need bees.
   D. Bees only live in forests.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "about one-third of the food we consume depends on pollination"

**【解析】** 原文明确说约三分之一的人类食物依赖传粉。A、C、D项比例均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When bees fly from flower to flower to collect nectar, they carry pollen... This pollen sticks to the next flower they visit, which helps plants produce fruits and seeds"

**【解析】** 原文说明蜜蜂在花间采蜜时携带花粉，帮助植物结出果实和种子。A、C、D项不完整或错误。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The reasons include loss of natural habitat, use of pesticides, climate change, and diseases"

**【解析】** 蜜蜂减少的原因包括栖息地丧失、杀虫剂、气候变化和疾病。C项"多种花"是帮助蜜蜂的行为，非减少原因。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "We can plant more flowers... We can avoid using harmful chemicals in our gardens"

**【解析】** 原文建议多种花、避免使用有害化学品。A项与原文相反；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段强调蜜蜂重要性，第④段指出蜜蜂减少问题，第⑤段提出保护措施

**【解析】** 全文围绕蜜蜂对生态和粮食的重要性以及保护蜜蜂展开。A、C、D项片面或错误。故选B。
