# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Have you ever seen a robot in a restaurant or a hospital? In recent years, robots have become a common part of our lives. They are not just toys anymore. They are smart machines that can help humans do many different things.

② In fact, robots have been used in factories (工厂) for a long time. They are very good at doing the same job again and again without getting tired. For example, robots can put parts of cars together or pack boxes quickly. Because they don't need to sleep or take a rest, they can work day and night. This helps factories make things much faster and cheaper.

③ Today, new robots are designed (设计) for our homes and daily lives. Some small round robots can move around the house and clean the floor by themselves. In some modern restaurants, robot waiters can bring food to your table and even say "enjoy your meal" to you. In hospitals, doctors use special medical robots to help them perform difficult surgeries (手术) more safely and accurately (准确地).

④ However, some people are worried about robots. They fear that robots will take away human jobs. While it is true that robots can do some simple jobs, they cannot completely replace (代替) humans. Robots don't have human feelings or creativity (创造力). They can only follow the programs written by scientists.

⑤ In the future, robots and humans will probably work together. As technology develops, robots will become our helpful partners rather than our enemies (敌人).

**注释：** surgery 手术；accurately 准确地；replace 代替；creativity 创造力

---

### 选择题

1. Where have robots been used for a long time according to the passage?
A. In space.
B. In hospitals.
C. In factories.
D. In schools.

2. Why are robots very useful in factories?
A. Because they can talk to the workers.
B. Because they can do the same job again and again without getting tired.
C. Because they need a lot of sleep every night.
D. Because they order cheap food from restaurants.

3. Form paragraph ③, what can a robot waiter do?
A. It can cook delicious meals in the kitchen.
B. It can bring food to your table and talk to you.
C. It can clean the floor in your house.
D. It can perform difficult surgeries on patients.

4. Why can't robots completely replace humans?
A. Because they are too small and round.
B. Because they don't have human feelings or creativity.
C. Because they often feel tired and need to sleep.
D. Because they can only clean the floor.

5. What does the writer believe about the future of robots and humans?
A. Robots will take away all human jobs.
B. Humans will stop making new robots.
C. Robots will become our enemies and fight humans.
D. Robots and humans will work together as helpful partners.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "In fact, robots have been used in factories (工厂) for a long time."

**【解析】** 原文中明明白白地说机器人在工厂（factories）里已经被使用很长时间了。A（太空）、B（医院）、D（学校）要么文中未提，要么是近期才有或只有某种特殊类型的医疗机器人协助手术等（如第三段），不属于很久以前就用的。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第二句 "They are very good at doing the same job again and again without getting tired."

**【解析】** 原文表明机器人十分擅长一次又一次不疲倦地（without getting tired）重复同一件工作。A、D项是胡编乱造，C选项说机器人需要大量睡眠，这也完全与原文“它们不需要睡觉休息”意思相反。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "In some modern restaurants, robot waiters can bring food to your table and even say 'enjoy your meal' to you."

**【解析】** 在被描述的餐厅机器人服务员中，明确提到的两个功能是：可以把食物端到你桌上（bring food to your table），并对你说祝你用餐愉快（talk to you）。A（在厨房做饭）、C（在家里打扫地板）、D（做手术）张冠李戴，分属别的机器人的能力。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3-4句 "While it is true that robots can do some simple jobs, they cannot completely replace (代替) humans. Robots don't have human feelings or creativity (创造力)."

**【解析】** 原文指明机器人没法完全替代人类的原因是：它们没有人类的感情或创造力（human feelings or creativity）。A说是小而圆的家庭扫地器；C和D显然不符合这一个段落的陈列。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 作者意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段。

**【解析】** 在文末，作者表明态度：在未来，机器人和人类可能会一起工作（work together）。机器人会变成人类得力的助手（helpful partners）而不是敌人。这同D项意义完全一致。A、B、C项都是负面或者违背作者意思的叙述。故选D。
