> **04-15 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① The sound of the starting gun fired. Jack ran as fast as he could. This was the 800-meter race in the school sports meeting. Jack had trained for three months just for this moment. His goal was to win a gold medal for his class.

② For the first 400 meters, Jack was in the second place. The runner in front of him, a tall boy from Class 5, was very fast. Jack kept his pace (节奏) and thought, "I can catch up with him in the last lap (圈)." 

③ Suddenly, a terrible thing happened. As Jack turned a corner, the boy next to him accidentally (意外地) stepped on his shoe. Jack lost his balance and fell heavily onto the hard track (跑道). His knee started to bleed (流血). 

④ Many students in the audience gasped (倒吸一口气). Jack felt a sharp pain in his leg, and for a second, he wanted to give up. The other runners quickly passed him. But then he heard a loud voice from the crowd, "Come on, Jack! Stand up!" It was his best friend, Peter. Soon, his whole class started shouting, "Don't give up, Jack!"

⑤ Ignoring (不顾) the pain, Jack slowly stood up. He gritted his teeth (咬紧牙关) and started to run again. Although he didn't win the race, he crossed the finish line. His classmates rushed to him with cheers and a warm towel. At that moment, Jack realized something: finishing the race bravely is sometimes a bigger victory (胜利) than winning a gold medal.

**注释：** accidentally 意外地；bleed 流血；audience 观众；victory 胜利

---

### 选择题

1. What did Jack want to get in the 800-meter race?
A. A new pair of running shoes.
B. A gold medal for his class.
C. A warm towel.
D. A free trip to the hospital.

2. In the first 400 meters, what position (位置) was Jack running in?
A. He was in the first place.
B. He was in the second place.
C. He was in the last place.
D. He was far behind everyone.

3. Why did Jack fall down onto the track?
A. Because he was too tired to run.
B. Because he was looking at the audience.
C. Because a tall boy from Class 5 pushed him.
D. Because the boy next to him stepped on his shoe by accident.

4. What happened after Jack fell?
A. He gave up and went home immediately.
B. He started to cry loudly on the track.
C. He stood up and continued to run after hearing his class cheering for him.
D. The tall boy from Class 5 stopped to help him up.

5. What does the writer want to tell us in the last paragraph?
A. Everyone needs a best friend like Peter.
B. Only people who run fast can get a warm towel.
C. School sports meetings are very dangerous for students.
D. Bravely finishing the race can be a greater victory than winning.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后句 "His goal was to win a gold medal for his class."

**【解析】** 原文开篇交代了杰克（Jack）跑800米的目标（goal），是为了给班级赢得一枚金牌（a gold medal）。其他的全是无关选项。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第一句 "For the first 400 meters, Jack was in the second place."

**【解析】** 在前400米，杰克处在第二名（in the second place）。跑在前面的是五班的高个子男孩。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "...the boy next to him accidentally (意外地) stepped on his shoe."

**【解析】** 小杰跌倒的原因是“拐弯时旁边的一个男孩意外踩到了他的鞋子（stepped on his shoe）”，导致他失去平衡重重摔在了跑道上。A、B没在文中体现；C虽然提到五班男孩，但五班男孩是在前面跑，踩鞋的没说是五班男孩而是“the boy next to him”，动作也是被踩到了鞋子而非被推（pushed）。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段的呼唤声及第⑤段首句 "Ignoring the pain, Jack slowly stood up. He gritted his teeth and started to run again."

**【解析】** 杰克摔倒流血原本想放弃。但听到了最好朋友彼得及全班大声鼓励，“不顾一切疼痛，慢慢站了起来...重新跑起来”。A与B项皆错。D项文中也没有讲那个跑得快的男生停下来帮他。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段结尾的一句话："finishing the race bravely is sometimes a bigger victory than winning a gold medal."

**【解析】** 文章结尾主人公杰克虽然没能拿到金牌，但也越过了终点，同学仍然涌向他为他欢呼。他在此刻体会到：有勇气完成比赛比赢得金牌还要能算作一场伟大的胜利。D项精炼地概括了该主旨。A（人人都需如彼得的朋友）、C（校运会太危险）均为对某部分细节过度解读的选项。故选D。
