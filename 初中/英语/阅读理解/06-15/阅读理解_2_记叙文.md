# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last summer, Wang Lin, a 14-year-old student from a small town, took his first flight to visit his uncle in Beijing. He had never been on a plane before. "I was excited but also a little nervous," Wang Lin said. His mother went with him to the airport and helped him check in. She reminded him to listen to the flight attendants (乘务员) and stay in his seat during the flight.

② At the gate, Wang Lin showed his boarding pass (登机牌) and walked through the tunnel into the plane. He found his seat by the window. A friendly flight attendant smiled and said, "First time flying? Don't worry. If you need anything, just press the button above your head." Wang Lin nodded and fastened his seat belt. He looked out of the window and watched the ground become smaller as the plane rose higher and higher.

③ During the flight, the plane met some clouds and shook a bit. Wang Lin held the armrests (扶手) tightly. He felt his heart beating fast. Then he remembered what his mother had said: "Turbulence (颠簸) is normal. Just breathe slowly." He closed his eyes, took a few deep breaths, and soon felt better. A man sitting next to him said, "You're doing great. My first flight was much scarier."

④ About two hours later, the pilot announced that they would land in twenty minutes. Wang Lin saw the buildings and roads of Beijing below. When the plane touched the ground, everyone clapped. Wang Lin smiled. He had made it! His uncle was waiting at the arrival hall. "How was the flight?" his uncle asked. "It was amazing," Wang Lin replied. "I want to fly again."

⑤ Back home a week later, Wang Lin told his classmates about his trip. "Flying seemed scary at first, but it wasn't so bad. If you stay calm and follow the rules, you'll be fine," he said. His friends listened and some said they also wanted to try flying one day.

**注释：** flight attendant 乘务员；boarding pass 登机牌；armrest 扶手；turbulence 颠簸

---

### 选择题

1. Why did Wang Lin go to Beijing?
   A. To visit his uncle.
   B. To take a flight for the first time.
   C. To meet his classmates.
   D. To work at the airport.

2. According to paragraph ②, what did the flight attendant tell Wang Lin to do if he needed help?
   A. Call his mother.
   B. Press the button above his head.
   C. Walk to the front of the plane.
   D. Raise his hand.

3. According to paragraph ③, what did Wang Lin do when the plane shook?
   A. He shouted for help.
   B. He ran to the flight attendant.
   C. He held the armrests and took deep breaths.
   D. He unfastened his seat belt.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Wang Lin?
   A. He did not enjoy the flight at all.
   B. He would never fly again.
   C. He was angry that the flight was delayed.
   D. He felt proud and happy that he had completed his first flight.

5. What does Wang Lin want to tell his classmates through his story?
   A. Flying is always dangerous.
   B. Staying calm and following rules can help you get through new experiences.
   C. Only adults should take flights.
   D. Beijing is the best city to visit.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Wang Lin... took his first flight to visit his uncle in Beijing."

**【解析】** 原文明确写明王林乘飞机去北京是为了看望叔叔。A 项正确。B 项"首次坐飞机"是方式，非目的；C 项"见同学"和 D 项"在机场工作"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "If you need anything, just press the button above your head."

**【解析】** 乘务员告诉王林如需帮助可按头顶上方的按钮。B 项正确。A 项"打电话给妈妈"、C 项"走到飞机前面"和 D 项"举手"均未提及。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Wang Lin held the armrests (扶手) tightly... He closed his eyes, took a few deep breaths, and soon felt better."

**【解析】** 原文描述王林紧紧抓住扶手，闭眼做深呼吸后感觉好转。C 项正确。A 项"呼救"、B 项"跑去找乘务员"和 D 项"解开安全带"均未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "When the plane touched the ground, everyone clapped. Wang Lin smiled. He had made it!... 'It was amazing,' Wang Lin replied. 'I want to fly again.'"

**【解析】** 落地时众人鼓掌，王林微笑，认为自己成功了，并说想再飞一次，可推知他为完成首次飞行感到骄傲和开心。D 项正确。A、B、C 项与原文情感和态度相反。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 Wang Lin 的话 "Flying seemed scary at first, but it wasn't so bad. If you stay calm and follow the rules, you'll be fine."

**【解析】** 王林通过自身经历告诉同学，保持冷静、遵守规则就能顺利度过新体验。B 项正确。A 项"飞行总是危险"与文章基调相反；C 项"只有成人才能坐飞机"未提及；D 项"北京最佳"非文章主旨。故选 B。
