# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Teeth are important parts of our body. They help us bite, chew (咀嚼), and speak clearly. An adult usually has 32 teeth, including incisors (门齿), canines (犬齿), premolars (前磨牙), and molars (磨牙). Each type has its own job. Incisors cut food, canines tear it, and molars grind (磨碎) it into small pieces so we can swallow safely.

② A tooth has two main parts: the crown (齿冠) that we can see above the gum (牙龈), and the root that lies inside the jaw (颌). The crown is covered by enamel (牙釉质), which is the hardest material in our body. It protects the tooth from damage when we eat. Under the enamel is dentin (牙本质), a softer layer. The innermost part is the pulp (牙髓), where nerves and blood vessels are found.

③ Good oral care is the best way to keep teeth healthy. Dentists suggest brushing teeth at least twice a day — in the morning and before bed. Use a soft brush and fluoride toothpaste. Brush for about two minutes and don't forget the back teeth and the gum line. Flossing (用牙线) once a day helps remove food stuck between teeth where a brush cannot reach.

④ We should also watch what we eat and drink. Sugary foods and drinks like candy, cookies, and soda can cause cavities (蛀牙). Bacteria in the mouth feed on sugar and produce acid that damages the enamel. Eating more vegetables, fruits, and foods rich in calcium (钙) helps strengthen teeth. Drinking water, especially after meals, washes away some food and keeps the mouth clean.

⑤ Visiting a dentist every six to twelve months is important too. The dentist can check for problems early and clean teeth professionally. Remember: healthy teeth last a lifetime if we take good care of them.

**注释：** chew 咀嚼；grind 磨碎；crown 齿冠；gum 牙龈；jaw 颌；enamel 牙釉质；dentin 牙本质；pulp 牙髓；flossing 用牙线；cavity 蛀牙；calcium 钙

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what is the main job of molars?
   A. To grind food into small pieces.

   A. The root.
   B. The enamel.
   C. The dentin.
   D. The pulp.

3. How often should we brush our teeth according to the text?
   A. Once a week.
   B. Three times a day.
   C. At least twice a day.
   D. Only before meals.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about sugary foods?
   A. They help strengthen teeth.
   B. They are good for the gums.
   C. They clean the mouth after meals.
   D. They can lead to cavities by producing acid that damages the enamel.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To introduce tooth structure and how to keep teeth healthy.
   B. To tell a story about visiting a dentist.
   C. To compare different types of teeth.
   D. To sell toothbrushes and toothpaste.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "and molars grind (磨碎) it into small pieces so we can swallow safely."

**【解析】** 原文明确写明磨牙的作用是将食物磨成小块便于吞咽。A 项正确。B 项为门齿功能；C 项为犬齿功能；D 项"保护牙龈"文中未提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The crown is covered by enamel (牙釉质), which is the hardest material in our body."

**【解析】** 原文指出牙釉质是人体最坚硬的材料。B 项正确。A 项牙根、C 项牙本质和 D 项牙髓均非最坚硬部分。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Dentists suggest brushing teeth at least twice a day — in the morning and before bed."

**【解析】** 文中建议每天至少刷牙两次（早晚各一次）。C 项正确。A 项"每周一次"、B 项"每天三次"和 D 项"仅餐前"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Sugary foods and drinks... can cause cavities (蛀牙). Bacteria in the mouth feed on sugar and produce acid that damages the enamel."

**【解析】** 糖类食物可致蛀牙，口腔细菌以糖为食并产生腐蚀牙釉质的酸。可推知 D 项正确。A、B 项与原文相反；C 项文中指喝水有清洁作用，非糖类食物。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图 / 主旨理解

**【定位】** 全文：第①—②段介绍牙齿结构与类型，第③—⑤段介绍口腔保健方法。

**【解析】** 本文为说明文，旨在介绍牙齿结构和保持牙齿健康的方法。A 项正确。B 项非叙事文；C 项比较仅在第一段略有涉及，非主旨；D 项无推销意图。故选 A。
