# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Christmas in Different Countries — A Short Guide**

① Christmas is celebrated around the world, but customs vary from country to country. Here is a quick look at how people in some countries enjoy this festival.

**Country customs:**

| Country | Special custom | Traditional food |
|---------|----------------|------------------|
| UK | Christmas trees and cards | Turkey, Christmas pudding |
| USA | Santa Claus, stockings | Turkey, cookies |
| Germany | Advent calendar, Christmas markets | Gingerbread, roasted goose |
| China | Giving apples (平安果) on Christmas Eve | Apples, chocolate |

② In the UK, families often put up a Christmas tree and send cards to friends. They eat turkey and Christmas pudding for dinner. In the USA, children hang stockings and wait for Santa Claus to bring gifts. Many American families bake cookies for Santa.

③ In Germany, children use Advent calendars to count down the days before Christmas. Christmas markets sell toys, sweets, and decorations. Germans enjoy gingerbread and roasted goose at Christmas dinner. In China, many young people give apples to friends on Christmas Eve. The word for apple (苹果) sounds like "peace" (平安), so it stands for wishing peace and health.

④ No matter which country you are in, Christmas is a time for family, warmth, and joy. Learning about different customs can make the festival more interesting!

**注释：** customs 习俗；stocking 长筒袜；advent 降临节；gingerbread 姜饼

---

**Questions:**

1. Which country has Christmas markets?
   A. The UK.
   B. The USA.
   C. Germany.
   D. China.

2. Why do Chinese people give apples on Christmas Eve?
   A. Because apples are cheap.
   B. Because the word for apple sounds like "peace" in Chinese.
   C. Because apples are healthy.
   D. Because Santa likes apples.

3. What do American children do at Christmas?
   A. Use Advent calendars.
   B. Hang stockings and wait for Santa Claus.
   C. Give apples to friends.
   D. Eat roasted goose.

4. Which food is traditional in both the UK and USA?
   A. Gingerbread.
   B. Roasted goose.
   C. Turkey.
   D. Apples.

5. What does the writer think about learning different Christmas customs?
   A. It is a waste of time.
   B. It can make the festival more interesting.
   C. It is only for adults.
   D. It makes people feel tired.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格及第③段 "In Germany... Christmas markets sell toys, sweets, and decorations."

**【解析】** 原文明确说德国的圣诞市集出售玩具、糖果和装饰品。A、B、D项均未提及圣诞市集。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The word for apple (苹果) sounds like 'peace' (平安), so it stands for wishing peace and health."

**【解析】** 苹果在中文中与"平安"谐音，代表祝愿平安健康。A项便宜、C项健康、D项圣诞老人喜欢均非主要原因。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "In the USA, children hang stockings and wait for Santa Claus to bring gifts."

**【解析】** 美国孩子挂袜子等待圣诞老人送礼物。A项Advent calendar是德国的；C项送苹果是中国的；D项吃烤鹅是德国的。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 UK 和 USA 行的 "Traditional food" 列均有 "Turkey"

**【解析】** 表格显示英国和美国的传统圣诞食物都有火鸡。A项姜饼是德国的；B项烤鹅是德国的；D项苹果是中国的。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Learning about different customs can make the festival more interesting!"

**【解析】** 作者认为了解不同习俗能让节日更有趣。A项浪费时间的反面；C项只给成人、D项让人累均与原文不符。故选B。
