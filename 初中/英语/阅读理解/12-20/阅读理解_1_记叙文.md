> **12-20 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My New Year's Resolution**

① The last day of December is always exciting. When the clock strikes twelve, a new year begins. This year, I decided to make a real plan for myself. I sat down with a piece of paper and wrote three things I wanted to change.

② First, I planned to read more books. Last year I was too busy with homework and forgot to read for fun. So I set a goal: at least two books every month. I would start with some short stories and then try longer ones. Reading before bed would help me relax too.

③ Second, I wanted to exercise more. I used to play basketball with my friends, but I had stopped for a long time. I decided to join the school running club and run three times a week. My mum said she would run with me on weekends. That made me feel happy because we could spend time together.

④ Third, I hoped to learn to cook. My grandmother taught me how to make dumplings last summer. I loved it. This year I wanted to learn to cook rice and a few simple dishes. Maybe I could help my parents when they came home tired from work.

⑤ Writing down my plans was easy. The hard part would be keeping them. I put the paper on my desk so I could see it every day. I believe that small steps can lead to big changes. Let's see how I do by the end of the year!

**注释：** resolution 决心；strike 敲响；goal 目标

---

**Questions:**

1. When did the writer decide to make a plan?
   A. On the first day of January.
   B. On the last day of December.
   C. In the middle of the year.
   D. During the summer holiday.

2. What was the writer's first goal?
   A. To play more basketball.
   B. To read at least two books every month.
   C. To cook dumplings.
   D. To run every day.

3. Why did the writer want to join the running club?
   A. Because the teacher asked him to.
   B. Because he had stopped exercising for a long time.
   C. Because he wanted to win a race.
   D. Because he had nothing else to do.

4. What did the writer learn from his grandmother?
   A. How to make rice.
   B. How to run faster.
   C. How to make dumplings.
   D. How to read more books.

5. Why did the writer put the paper on his desk?
   A. To keep it safe.
   B. To see his plans every day and remember to follow them.
   C. To show his friends.
   D. To use it for homework.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The last day of December is always exciting... This year, I decided to make a real plan for myself."

**【解析】** 原文说十二月的最后一天很令人兴奋，今年他决定为自己制定真正的计划。A项1月1日、C项年中、D项暑假均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "So I set a goal: at least two books every month."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出第一个目标是每月至少读两本书。A项打更多篮球是第二个目标；C项包饺子是祖母教的；D项每天跑步与"每周三次"不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I used to play basketball with my friends, but I had stopped for a long time. I decided to join the school running club"

**【解析】** 作者曾和朋友打篮球但很久没运动了，所以决定加入跑步俱乐部。A项老师要求、C项想赢比赛、D项无事可做均无依据。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "My grandmother taught me how to make dumplings last summer."

**【解析】** 原文说祖母去年夏天教他做饺子。A项做米饭是今年的计划；B项跑得更快、D项读更多书均非祖母所教。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I put the paper on my desk so I could see it every day."

**【解析】** 作者把计划纸放在桌上是为了每天能看到并记住执行。A项保管、C项给朋友看、D项做作业用均非目的。故选B。
