# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Winter Science Camp — Registration Open!**

① Love science? Want to explore, experiment, and have fun during the winter holiday? Join our Winter Science Camp!

**Camp Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Dates | January 13–17, 2025 (5 days) |
| Time | 9:00 a.m. – 3:30 p.m. daily |
| Place | Guangzhou Science Centre, Building B |
| Ages | 12–15 years old |
| Fee | 380 yuan (includes materials and lunch) |
| Spots available | 40 students (first come, first served) |

② **Daily Schedule:**

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00–9:30 | Morning warm-up & science quiz |
| 9:30–11:30 | Hands-on experiment (different topic each day) |
| 11:30–12:30 | Lunch break |
| 12:30–1:30 | Science movie or guest talk |
| 1:30–3:00 | Group project work |
| 3:00–3:30 | Daily summary & sharing |

③ **Experiment Topics:**
- Day 1: Making a simple robot
- Day 2: Water purification experiment
- Day 3: Building a bridge with paper
- Day 4: Solar energy and light
- Day 5: Team challenge — design your own invention

④ **How to register:** Fill in the online form at www.gzscience.cn/wintercamp before December 25, 2024. You will receive a confirmation email within 3 working days. Bring the printed confirmation on the first day.

⑤ **Note:** Each student should bring a notebook and wear comfortable clothes. All materials and tools are provided. A certificate of completion will be given on the last day.

Guangzhou Science Centre  
November 2024

**Questions:**

1. How long does the Winter Science Camp last?
   A. 3 days.
   B. 5 days.
   C. 7 days.
   D. 10 days.

2. What is the fee for the camp?
   A. 180 yuan.
   B. 280 yuan.
   C. 380 yuan.
   D. 480 yuan.

3. What experiment will students do on Day 3?
   A. Making a simple robot.
   B. Water purification.
   C. Building a bridge with paper.
   D. Solar energy and light.

4. When is the registration deadline?
   A. November 24, 2024.
   B. December 25, 2024.
   C. January 13, 2025.
   D. January 17, 2025.

5. What will students receive on the last day?
   A. A free lunch.
   B. A new robot.
   C. A certificate of completion.
   D. A refund.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Dates" 一行 "January 13–17, 2025 (5 days)"

**【解析】** 表格明确标注为5天。A项3天、C项7天、D项10天均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Fee" 一行 "380 yuan (includes materials and lunch)"

**【解析】** 费用明确标注为380元，含材料和午餐。A项180元、B项280元、D项480元均不正确。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Day 3: Building a bridge with paper"

**【解析】** 第三天的实验是用纸建桥。A项制作机器人是第一天；B项水净化是第二天；D项太阳能与光是第四天。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Fill in the online form at www.gzscience.cn/wintercamp before December 25, 2024."

**【解析】** 报名截止日期为2024年12月25日。A项11月24日是通知大概发布时间；C项1月13日是开营日期；D项1月17日是结营日期。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "A certificate of completion will be given on the last day."

**【解析】** 原文说最后一天会颁发结业证书。A项免费午餐每天都有不是最后一天特别的；B项新机器人原文未说赠送；D项退款无依据。故选C。
