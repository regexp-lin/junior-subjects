# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Teenagers Keep Pets?**

① Many teenagers dream of having a pet — a dog, a cat, a hamster, or even a fish. Having a pet can be wonderful, but it also comes with responsibilities. Should teenagers be allowed to keep pets?

② Those who say yes believe that pets teach important life lessons. Taking care of an animal means feeding it on time, keeping it clean, and making sure it is healthy. These daily tasks help teenagers learn responsibility. Pets also provide companionship. Many teens feel lonely or stressed, and a pet can be a great comfort. Studies show that spending time with animals can lower blood pressure and reduce anxiety. For teenagers who feel shy, a pet can also help them make friends — walking a dog in the park is a great way to meet people.

③ However, there are reasons to be cautious. Pets require time and money. Vet visits, food, and grooming are not cheap. If the family cannot afford it, the pet may not receive proper care. Some teenagers lose interest after a few months and leave the work to their parents. This is unfair to both the parents and the pet. Also, some apartments are too small for large animals, and keeping them in a tiny space is not good for their health.

④ There are also health concerns. Some people are allergic to animal hair. If someone in the family has allergies, having a pet at home may cause serious health problems. Pets can also carry diseases if they are not vaccinated and kept clean.

⑤ I believe that keeping a pet can be good for a teenager, but only if the whole family agrees and is ready to share the work. Before getting a pet, teenagers should research what the pet needs and make a plan. A pet is not a toy — it is a living being that deserves love and care.

**注释：** companionship 陪伴；allergic 过敏的；vaccinate 接种疫苗

---

**Questions:**

1. According to the supporters, what can pets teach teenagers?
   A. How to study better.
   B. How to earn money.
   C. Responsibility through daily care tasks.
   D. How to play sports.

2. How can pets help teens who feel lonely?
   A. Pets can do homework for them.
   B. Pets provide companionship and comfort.
   C. Pets take them to school.
   D. Pets buy gifts for them.

3. What is one problem mentioned about keeping pets?
   A. Pets make the house too clean.
   B. Pets require time and money that some families may not have.
   C. Pets always make teenagers more popular.
   D. Pets help teenagers exercise too much.

4. Why might pets cause health problems for some families?
   A. Pets eat too much food.
   B. Some family members may be allergic to animal hair.
   C. Pets take up too much space.
   D. Pets are always noisy.

5. What is the writer's final opinion?
   A. No teenager should ever have a pet.
   B. All teenagers must have a pet.
   C. Keeping a pet can be good if the family agrees and is prepared.
   D. Pets are only suitable for adults.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Taking care of an animal means feeding it on time, keeping it clean, and making sure it is healthy. These daily tasks help teenagers learn responsibility."

**【解析】** 原文说照顾动物的日常任务帮助青少年学习责任感。A项"学习更好"、B项"赚钱"、D项"运动"均非养宠物教给青少年的内容。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Pets also provide companionship. Many teens feel lonely or stressed, and a pet can be a great comfort."

**【解析】** 原文说宠物提供陪伴，对感到孤独或有压力的青少年是很好的安慰。A项"帮做作业"、C项"送去上学"、D项"买礼物"均属于无中生有。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Pets require time and money. Vet visits, food, and grooming are not cheap. If the family cannot afford it, the pet may not receive proper care."

**【解析】** 原文指出养宠物需要时间和金钱，有些家庭可能负担不起。A项"让房子太干净"无依据；C项"让人更受欢迎"是正面影响不是问题；D项"运动太多"原文未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some people are allergic to animal hair. If someone in the family has allergies, having a pet at home may cause serious health problems."

**【解析】** 原文说有些人对动物毛发过敏，家里养宠物可能导致严重健康问题。A项"吃太多"、C项"占太多空间"虽有提及但不是健康问题；D项"总是吵闹"原文未说。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "keeping a pet can be good for a teenager, but only if the whole family agrees and is ready to share the work."

**【解析】** 作者认为养宠物对青少年有益，但前提是全家同意并准备好分担工作。A项"不应养"和D项"只适合成年人"过于绝对；B项"必须养"也过于极端。故选C。
