# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Tea is one of the most popular drinks in the world. But do you know that the story of tea began in China? Drinking tea has a very long history and it is an important part of Chinese culture.

② According to an old Chinese story, tea was first discovered (发现) by Shen Nong about 5,000 years ago. One day, he was boiling water to drink while sitting under a tea tree. Some leaves fell from the tree into his pot. The water turned a pale brown color. He tasted it and found it very refreshing (提神的). This is how tea was invented.

③ Over the years, people found out that tea was not just a tasty drink, but also very good for health. It can help people stay awake and think clearly. There are many different kinds of Chinese tea, such as green tea, black tea, and Oolong tea. Green tea is the oldest and most popular kind in China.

④ In ancient (古代的) times, tea was carried from China to other countries along the famous Silk Road. Later, in the 17th century, ships took tea to Europe. the British people liked tea so much that they created their own "Afternoon Tea" tradition.

⑤ Today, no matter where you go in the world, you can always find people enjoying a cup of tea. It brings people together and gives them a moment to relax in their busy lives.

**注释：** discover 发现；refreshing 提神的；ancient 古代的

---

### 选择题

1. Where did the story of tea begin according to the passage?
A. In Europe.
B. In Britain.
C. In China.
D. Along the Silk Road.

2. How was tea discovered according to the old story?
A. Shen Nong planted the first tea tree in his garden.
B. Some leaves fell into Shen Nong's boiling water by chance (偶然).
C. Shen Nong bought some tea leaves from a market.
D. A doctor told Shen Nong to drink brown water.

3. Which kind of tea is the oldest and most popular in China?
A. Black tea.
B. Oolong tea.
C. Green tea.
D. Milk tea.

4. What does the underlined word "tradition" in paragraph ④ mean?
A. An old and important custom (习俗) that has continued for a long time.
B. A very new type of food.
C. A fast way of traveling by ship.
D. A beautiful song that everyone sings.

5. What does the passage mainly tell us?
A. How to grow different tea trees.
B. Why British people like Afternoon Tea.
C. The history of the Silk Road.
D. The origin (起源) and history of tea.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "But do you know that the story of tea began in China?"

**【解析】** 原文明确说到茶的故事开始于中国（in China）。A、B、D项在文中均有提及，但它们分别是茶传向的地方，而非起源地。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "Some leaves fell from the tree into his pot."

**【解析】** 根据中国古老的故事，神农氏在树下煮水时，有些叶子（偶然地）掉进了他的壶里，由此发现了茶。A项（种树）、C项（市场上买）、D项（医生建议）均不符合故事描述。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "Green tea is the oldest and most popular kind in China."

**【解析】** 第三段指出，绿茶（Green tea）是中国最古老、最受欢迎的茶种类。文中也提到了红茶和乌龙茶，但并非最古老的。故选C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "...the British people liked tea so much that they created their own 'Afternoon Tea' tradition."

**【解析】** 英国人非常喜欢茶，以至于创造了他们自己的“下午茶（Afternoon Tea）”文化/传统。"tradition"作为传统，最接近的解释是“持续了很长时间的古老而重要的习俗”。B出新种类食物、C乘船旅行快方式、D全员合唱等都不符合此句语境。故选A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文结构及首段 "Drinking tea has a very long history..."

**【解析】** 本文是一篇说明文，按照时间叙述：第一、二段讲茶起源于中国和神农氏的传说，第三段讲茶的种类和益处，第四、五段讲茶怎样传播到世界各地。它主要向我们介绍了茶的起源和历史。A（怎么种茶）、B（英国人为何爱下午茶 - 偏细节）、C（有关丝绸之路的历史）皆属以偏概全或偏离主题。故选D。
