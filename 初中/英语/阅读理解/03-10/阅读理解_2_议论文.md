# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① In many schools today, students spend most of their time studying. Physical education (PE) classes are often only once or twice a week, and some students even skip them to do homework instead. But is this a good idea? Many experts believe that students need more time for sports, not less.

② Regular exercise is very important for young people's health. Studies show that teenagers who exercise at least one hour a day are less likely to become overweight. Exercise also makes bones and muscles stronger. Without enough physical activity, students may develop health problems early in life, such as poor eyesight and back pain from sitting too long.

③ Sports are not just good for the body — they are also good for the mind. Scientists have found that exercise helps the brain work better. After playing sports, students can focus more easily in class and remember things more clearly. In fact, a study in the UK showed that students who exercised regularly got better grades than those who didn't.

④ Playing sports also teaches students important life skills. Team sports like basketball and football help students learn to work with others and communicate better. Individual sports like running and swimming teach patience and self-discipline. When students lose a game, they learn to deal with failure. When they win, they learn to stay humble. These are all skills that will help them in the future.

⑤ Some people worry that spending more time on sports will take time away from studying. However, the truth is that exercise actually helps students study better. A healthy body supports a healthy mind. Instead of cutting PE classes, schools should give students more chances to move, play, and enjoy sports every day.

---

**注释：** overweight 超重的；muscle 肌肉；self-discipline 自律；humble 谦虚的

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ①, what do some students do during PE classes?
   A. They play computer games.
   B. They skip them to do homework.
   C. They sleep in the classroom.
   D. They talk with their friends.

2. According to Paragraph ②, what can regular exercise help prevent?
   A. Losing friends at school.
   B. Getting bad grades in exams.
   C. Becoming overweight.
   D. Forgetting to do homework.

3. What did the study in the UK show?
   A. Students who exercised regularly got better grades.
   B. Exercise made students feel more tired in class.
   C. UK students didn't enjoy PE classes.
   D. Studying is more important than sports.

4. The underlined word "humble" in Paragraph ④ probably means "________."
   A. 骄傲的
   B. 谦虚的
   C. 紧张的
   D. 孤独的

5. What is the writer's main idea in this passage?
   A. PE classes should be removed from schools.
   B. Studying is more important than playing sports.
   C. Sports are only good for students' health.
   D. Students need more time for sports, which benefits both body and mind.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Physical education (PE) classes are often only once or twice a week, and some students even skip them to do homework instead."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出一些学生甚至逃掉体育课去做作业。B 项"他们逃课去做作业"与原文完全一致。A 项"玩电脑游戏"原文未提及；C 项"在教室里睡觉"属于无中生有；D 项"和朋友聊天"也未在原文中出现。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "Studies show that teenagers who exercise at least one hour a day are less likely to become overweight."

**【解析】** 原文指出每天至少运动一小时的青少年不太可能超重，说明规律运动有助于预防肥胖。C 项"变得超重"与原文对应。A 项"在学校失去朋友"原文未讨论运动与社交的关系；B 项"考试成绩差"——虽然第③段提到运动有助于成绩，但第②段讨论的是健康方面的好处；D 项"忘记做作业"与本段内容无关。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "In fact, a study in the UK showed that students who exercised regularly got better grades than those who didn't."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明英国的一项研究表明，经常锻炼的学生成绩比不锻炼的学生更好。A 项与原文完全吻合。B 项"运动让学生在课上更累"与原文"can focus more easily in class"矛盾；C 项"英国学生不喜欢体育课"属于无中生有——研究讨论的是运动与成绩的关系，未涉及学生对体育课的态度；D 项"学习比运动更重要"是对研究结论的曲解——研究恰恰证明运动有助于学习。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段第5-6句 "When students lose a game, they learn to deal with failure. When they win, they learn to stay humble."

**【解析】** 原文将"lose a game"和"win"对比排列：输了学会应对失败，赢了学会保持"humble"。根据对比结构，"输了→接受失败"对应"赢了→保持某种品质"。赢了比赛后需要保持的正面品质应该是"谦虚"，而非骄傲或其他。B 项"谦虚的"最符合语境。A 项"骄傲的"与上下文传达的积极品质不符——文章强调运动教会的是正面技能；C 项"紧张的"不符合赢了比赛后的情境；D 项"孤独的"与比赛获胜的情境无关。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 词义猜测题可以利用上下文的对比或并列结构来推断。本题中"lose → deal with failure"与"win → stay humble"形成对照，帮助推断"humble"是赢了之后应保持的正面品质。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文结构：第①段提出问题（学生运动时间不足）→ 第②段运动对身体的好处 → 第③段运动对大脑和学习的好处 → 第④段运动培养生活技能 → 第⑤段总结：学校应给学生更多运动机会。

**【解析】** 全文围绕"学生需要更多体育运动时间"这一核心观点展开，从身体健康、大脑功能、生活技能三个方面论证了运动对学生身心的全面益处，最后呼吁学校增加运动机会。D 项"学生需要更多的运动时间，运动对身心都有益"最全面地概括了文章主旨。A 项"应该取消体育课"与文章观点完全相反——作者呼吁增加而非减少体育课；B 项"学习比运动更重要"与文章论点矛盾；C 项"运动只对学生的健康有好处"以偏概全——文章同时论述了运动对大脑、学习和生活技能的益处，不仅仅是健康。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 议论文的主旨通常在首段末尾或末段中明确呈现。本文第①段末句"students need more time for sports, not less"和第⑤段的呼吁遥相呼应，构成了完整的论点。注意主旨选项需涵盖文章各方面论据，过于狭窄的选项往往是干扰项。
