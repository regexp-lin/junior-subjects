> **03-10 第1篇**

# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Zhang Ming was not happy when his teacher asked the class to volunteer at the Sunshine Community Center every Saturday. "Why do I have to spend my weekend helping strangers?" he thought. But since it was a school requirement, he had no choice.

② On his first Saturday, Zhang Ming was asked to help in the activity room where elderly people spent their afternoons. He felt awkward and didn't know what to say. An old man sitting by the window waved at him. "Come, young man. Do you play chess?" the man asked with a kind smile. His name was Mr. Liu, and he was 82 years old.

③ Zhang Ming sat down and they began to play. Mr. Liu was a very good player, and Zhang Ming lost every game. But Mr. Liu was patient. He taught Zhang Ming different strategies and told him stories about his life. Mr. Liu had been a math teacher for over 40 years. "Teaching was the happiest thing in my life," he said, "because I could help young people grow."

④ Week after week, Zhang Ming looked forward to his Saturday visits. He and Mr. Liu became close friends. Zhang Ming helped Mr. Liu write letters to his grandson who lived in Canada, and Mr. Liu helped Zhang Ming improve his chess skills. One day, Mr. Liu said, "You know, Zhang Ming, the best way to find happiness is to bring it to others."

⑤ By the end of the school year, Zhang Ming had changed completely. He was no longer the boy who didn't want to help strangers. He continued volunteering even after the school requirement ended. He realized that helping others was not just about giving — it was also about receiving something far more valuable: purpose and joy.

---

**注释：** volunteer 做志愿者；community center 社区中心；strategy 策略；purpose 目标，意义

---

### 题目

1. Why did Zhang Ming go to the Sunshine Community Center?
   A. Because he wanted to learn chess.
   B. Because it was a school requirement.
   C. Because his parents told him to.
   D. Because he loved helping old people.

2. What was Mr. Liu's job before he retired?
   A. A doctor.
   B. A chess player.
   C. A writer.
   D. A math teacher.

3. What did Zhang Ming help Mr. Liu do?
   A. Write letters to his grandson.
   B. Cook meals every Saturday.
   C. Clean his room at the center.
   D. Read newspapers to him.

4. What did Mr. Liu mean by "the best way to find happiness is to bring it to others"?
   A. Happiness is very hard to find.
   B. Only old people understand true happiness.
   C. Making others happy brings happiness to yourself.
   D. You should always put yourself first.

5. What can we learn from Zhang Ming's story?
   A. Chess is a great game for making friends.
   B. School requirements are always boring.
   C. Old people are lonely and need help.
   D. Helping others can bring unexpected joy and meaning.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "But since it was a school requirement, he had no choice."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出张明去社区中心是因为这是学校的要求（school requirement），他没有选择。B 项与原文完全一致。A 项"因为他想学下棋"——下棋是到了社区中心之后才发生的事情，不是他去的原因；C 项"因为他父母要求他去"原文未提及父母；D 项"因为他喜欢帮助老人"与原文第①段张明不情愿的态度矛盾。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第4句 "Mr. Liu had been a math teacher for over 40 years."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明刘爷爷当了40多年的数学老师。D 项"A math teacher"与原文完全对应。A 项"医生"原文未提及；B 项"棋手"——虽然刘爷爷棋下得很好，但原文说他的职业是教师，下棋只是爱好；C 项"作家"属于无中生有。故选 D。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3句 "Zhang Ming helped Mr. Liu write letters to his grandson who lived in Canada..."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到张明帮助刘爷爷给住在加拿大的孙子写信。A 项与原文完全吻合。B 项"每周六做饭"原文未提及任何烹饪相关内容；C 项"打扫他在中心的房间"属于无中生有；D 项"给他读报纸"也未在原文中出现。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "The best way to find happiness is to bring it to others."

**【解析】** 刘爷爷这句话的字面意思是"找到幸福的最好方法是把幸福带给别人"。结合上下文——刘爷爷以教书育人为最大乐事（第③段），张明在帮助他人的过程中也收获了快乐（第⑤段）——可以推断这句话表达的是"让别人开心，自己也会感到幸福"。C 项"让别人开心会给自己带来幸福"最准确地概括了这层含义。A 项"幸福很难找到"与原句强调"方法"的积极语气不符；B 项"只有老年人才能理解真正的幸福"属于过度推断；D 项"你应该总是把自己放在第一位"与原句"bring it to others"的利他精神完全相反。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 理解人物话语含义需结合上下文情境和人物经历。刘爷爷自身的经历（教书40年感到幸福）就是这句话最好的例证。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "He realized that helping others was not just about giving — it was also about receiving something far more valuable: purpose and joy."

**【解析】** 全文讲述了张明从不情愿参加志愿活动，到与刘爷爷建立深厚友谊，最终领悟到帮助别人也是在丰富自己的故事。结尾点明主旨：助人不仅仅是付出，更能收获比付出更有价值的东西——目标和快乐。D 项"帮助他人能带来意想不到的快乐和意义"最准确地概括了全文主旨。A 项"下棋是交友的好方式"以偏概全——下棋只是故事中建立友谊的一个细节，不是主旨；B 项"学校的要求总是无聊的"与文章传达的积极信息相反——正是这个"要求"让张明收获了成长；C 项"老年人孤独需要帮助"只涉及一个侧面，未能涵盖张明自身的成长和感悟。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 记叙文主旨题要关注主人公的转变过程和文末的感悟总结。本文中张明从"不愿意"到"主动继续"的态度转变，以及最后一句的总结性感悟，共同揭示了故事的核心主题。
