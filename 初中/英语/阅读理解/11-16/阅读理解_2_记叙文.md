> **11-16 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**The Surprise Birthday Party**

① Last Friday was my best friend Li Wei's birthday. He had been feeling down lately because his parents were very busy with work and could not spend much time with him. I decided to do something special — I would throw him a surprise birthday party.

② I started planning a week before. First, I talked to Li Wei's mum on the phone. She loved the idea and said she would make sure Li Wei came home at 5:00 p.m. on Friday. Then I invited ten classmates. Everyone wanted to come. We agreed to arrive at Li Wei's house at 4:30 p.m., before he got home. I asked each person to bring a small gift and to keep the plan secret.

③ On Friday afternoon, we all arrived on time. Li Wei's mum let us in. We decorated the living room with balloons and a big banner that said "Happy Birthday, Li Wei!" We hid behind the sofa and the door. My heart was beating fast. At 5:05 p.m., we heard the key turn in the lock. The door opened. Li Wei walked in and turned on the light.

④ "SURPRISE!" we all shouted and jumped out. Li Wei stood there with his mouth open. For a moment, he could not say a word. Then his eyes filled with tears — happy tears. "I... I thought nobody remembered," he whispered. We all hugged him. His mum brought out a big chocolate cake with candles. We sang "Happy Birthday" together.

⑤ That evening, we ate cake, played games, and laughed a lot. Li Wei told me later that it was the best birthday he had ever had. Seeing his smile made me realise that the best gift we can give is our time and love. I was so glad I did it.

---

**Questions:**

1. Why had Li Wei been feeling down lately?
   A. He failed an important exam.
   B. His parents were busy and could not spend time with him.
   C. He had a fight with his friends.
   D. He was sick and stayed at home.

2. When did the classmates arrive at Li Wei's house?
   A. At 5:05 p.m.
   B. At 5:00 p.m.
   C. At 4:30 p.m.
   D. At 4:00 p.m.

3. What did the writer ask each classmate to do?
   A. Buy a big cake.
   B. Decorate their own homes.
   C. Bring a small gift and keep the plan secret.
   D. Write a long letter.

4. How did Li Wei react when he saw the surprise?
   A. He was angry and left the room.
   B. He laughed loudly right away.
   C. He was so moved that happy tears filled his eyes.
   D. He was not surprised at all.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Birthday parties are too expensive.
   B. The best gift we can give is our time and love.
   C. Li Wei does not like surprises.
   D. It is better not to plan parties.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He had been feeling down lately because his parents were very busy with work and could not spend much time with him."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出李伟最近心情低落是因为父母工作忙，没时间陪他。A项"考试没考好"原文未提及；C项"和朋友吵架"无依据；D项"生病在家"属于无中生有。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We agreed to arrive at Li Wei's house at 4:30 p.m., before he got home."

**【解析】** 原文说大家约定4:30到达，赶在李伟回家之前。A项5:05是李伟到家的时间；B项5:00是李伟妈妈确保李伟回来的时间；D项4:00原文无此信息。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "I asked each person to bring a small gift and to keep the plan secret."

**【解析】** 原文说作者让每人带一个小礼物并保密。A项"买大蛋糕"是李伟妈妈准备的，不是每人要做的事；B项"装饰自己家"张冠李戴；D项"写长信"原文未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "For a moment, he could not say a word. Then his eyes filled with tears — happy tears. 'I... I thought nobody remembered,' he whispered."

**【解析】** 李伟先是说不出话，然后流下了感动的眼泪，说以为没人记得他的生日，可以推断他非常感动。A项"生气离开"与原文完全相反；B项"立刻大笑"不准确，他先是说不出话；D项"一点也不惊讶"与描述的反应矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Seeing his smile made me realise that the best gift we can give is our time and love."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段直接表达了感悟：我们能给予的最好礼物是时间和爱。A项"派对太贵"原文未提及费用问题；C项"李伟不喜欢惊喜"与事实相反；D项"最好不要策划派对"与文章传达的正面信息矛盾。故选B。
