> **08-22 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last September, a new boy named Leo joined our class. He had just moved from another city, and he looked shy and quiet. During the first week, he always sat alone at lunch and hardly spoke to anyone. I noticed that he enjoyed drawing in his notebook when he had free time.

② One afternoon, our art teacher asked us to work in pairs for a poster project. Nobody had chosen Leo yet, so I walked over and asked if he would like to be my partner. He looked surprised at first, but then he smiled and said yes. We spent the next few days drawing and painting together. I found out that Leo was actually very funny and had lots of interesting ideas. He told me about his old school and his favourite cartoon characters.

③ As we became friends, I introduced Leo to my other friends. We started eating lunch together and playing basketball after class. Slowly, Leo became more confident. He even joined the school drawing club and won a prize in a competition. I was so proud of him!

④ One day, Leo said to me, "Thank you for being the first person to talk to me. I was really nervous when I came here. Your kindness made everything easier." I was touched. I had never thought that such a simple action could mean so much to someone.

⑤ Now Leo is one of my best friends. This experience taught me that a small act of kindness can change someone's life. We should never be afraid to reach out to those who look lonely. Maybe they are just waiting for someone to say hello.

**注释：** poster 海报；character 角色；competition 竞赛；reach out 主动伸出援手

---

**Questions:**

1. How did Leo feel when he first joined the class?
   A. Happy and excited.
   B. Shy and quiet.
   C. Angry and upset.
   D. Tired and bored.

2. What did the writer notice about Leo in the first week?
   A. Leo was good at basketball.
   B. Leo liked drawing in his notebook.
   C. Leo had many friends.
   D. Leo was the best student in class.

3. Why did Leo say "Thank you" to the writer?
   A. Because the writer helped him with homework.
   B. Because the writer was the first person to talk to him and made him feel welcome.
   C. Because the writer gave him a gift.
   D. Because the writer invited him to a party.

4. What happened after Leo and the writer became friends?
   A. Leo moved to another school.
   B. Leo became more confident and even won a drawing competition.
   C. The writer stopped talking to Leo.
   D. Leo decided to quit the drawing club.

5. What lesson did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. New students are always difficult to get along with.
   B. A small act of kindness can make a big difference to someone.
   C. It is important to win competitions.
   D. Drawing is the best way to make friends.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "He had just moved from another city, and he looked shy and quiet."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述Leo初到时显得害羞、安静。A项"开心兴奋"、C项"生气沮丧"和D项"疲惫无聊"均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "I noticed that he enjoyed drawing in his notebook when he had free time."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到作者注意到Leo有空时喜欢在本子上画画。A、C、D项均未在文中提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Thank you for being the first person to talk to me. I was really nervous when I came here. Your kindness made everything easier."

**【解析】** Leo感谢作者是第一个和他说话的人，让初到新环境的他感到轻松。A、C、D项均非Leo感谢的真正原因。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "He even joined the school drawing club and won a prize in a competition."

**【解析】** 原文明确说Leo加入绘画俱乐部并在比赛中获奖。A、C、D项均与原文描述相反或未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "This experience taught me that a small act of kindness can change someone's life."

**【解析】** 作者从这段经历中领悟到：一个小小的善举可以改变他人生活。A项与作者感悟相反；C、D项过度解读，非作者强调的重点。故选B。
