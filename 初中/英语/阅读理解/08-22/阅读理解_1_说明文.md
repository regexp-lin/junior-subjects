# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Garbage sorting (垃圾分类) has become very important in many cities in China. It helps protect our environment and save resources. But do you know how to sort your garbage correctly? Let us learn about the four main types.

② First, recyclable (可回收的) waste includes paper, plastic bottles, glass, and metal. These things can be used again after they are processed. Before you throw them away, remember to clean them. For example, empty milk boxes should be washed and flattened (压平) to save space.

③ Second, kitchen waste is food and other organic (有机的) matter. Fruit peels, vegetable leaves, leftover rice, and tea leaves all belong here. This type of waste will be made into fertilizer (肥料) or biogas (沼气). Do not put plastic bags into this bin, because they cannot break down easily.

④ Third, harmful waste includes batteries, medicine, thermometers (温度计), and paint. These things can be dangerous to people and the environment. You should put them in special red bins and never mix them with other garbage.

⑤ Fourth, other waste is everything that does not fit the above three types. Used tissues, broken ceramics (陶瓷), and dirty plastic bags go here. When you are not sure which bin to use, you can check the signs on the bins or ask your parents for help. Everyone can do their part to make our world cleaner!

**注释：** recyclable 可回收的；flatten 压平；organic 有机的；fertilizer 肥料；thermometer 温度计；ceramics 陶瓷

---

**Questions:**

1. What type of waste do fruit peels and vegetable leaves belong to?
   A. Recyclable waste.
   B. Kitchen waste.
   C. Harmful waste.
   D. Other waste.

2. Why should we clean bottles before throwing them in the recyclable bin?
   A. To make them look better.
   B. So they can be processed and used again easily.
   C. To save money.
   D. Because the law requires it.

3. Where should we put used batteries?
   A. In the kitchen waste bin.
   B. In the recyclable bin.
   C. In special red bins for harmful waste.
   D. In the other waste bin.

4. According to the passage, what should we NOT do with kitchen waste?
   A. Put fruit peels in it.
   B. Put vegetable leaves in it.
   C. Put plastic bags in it.
   D. Put tea leaves in it.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell people where to buy garbage bins.
   B. To teach people how to sort garbage correctly.
   C. To complain about dirty streets.
   D. To introduce different types of plastic.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Fruit peels, vegetable leaves, leftover rice, and tea leaves all belong here."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明果皮、菜叶属于厨余垃圾（kitchen waste）。A项"可回收垃圾"、C项"有害垃圾"和D项"其他垃圾"均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "These things can be used again after they are processed. Before you throw them away, remember to clean them."

**【解析】** 原文指出可回收物经处理后能再利用，投放前应清洗。清洗是为了便于加工再利用。A项"更好看"、C项"省钱"和D项"法律要求"均非原文主旨。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "harmful waste includes batteries, medicine, thermometers, and paint... You should put them in special red bins"

**【解析】** 原文明确说电池属于有害垃圾，应放入专门的红色垃圾桶。A、B、D项所涉垃圾桶类型均错误。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Do not put plastic bags into this bin, because they cannot break down easily."

**【解析】** 原文明确禁止将塑料袋放入厨余垃圾桶。A、B、D项中的果皮、菜叶、茶叶均为厨余垃圾，只有C项塑料袋不应放入。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第①段 "Let us learn about the four main types" 及第⑤段 "Everyone can do their part"

**【解析】** 全文介绍四种垃圾分类及正确投放方法，目的是教人们如何正确分类垃圾。A项"买垃圾桶"、C项"抱怨街道脏"和D项"介绍塑料类型"均非主旨。故选B。
