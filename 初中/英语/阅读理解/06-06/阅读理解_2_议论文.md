# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Many parents wonder whether they should check their children's phones. Teenagers often spend hours on their phones — chatting, watching videos, or playing games. Some parents believe it is their duty to know what their kids are doing online. Others think checking phones invades (侵犯) privacy and may harm trust. Let us look at both sides.

② **Those who support checking phones** say that parents need to protect their children. The internet can be a dangerous place. Children may meet strangers, see unhealthy content, or spend too much time on games. By checking phones, parents can find problems early and talk to their children about them. For younger teenagers especially, parental supervision (监督) can help keep them safe.

③ **Those who are against it** argue that teenagers need privacy. If parents check phones without telling their children, teenagers may feel they are not trusted. They might hide more things or stop sharing their feelings with their parents. Trust between parents and children is very important. Some experts also say that constant checking can make teenagers feel stressed and may damage the parent-child relationship.

④ **What we can learn:** There is no simple answer. Some families choose to agree on rules together — for example, parents can look at phones at certain times, but they tell their children first. Open communication is key. Parents should talk with their children about online safety and why they care. Teenagers need to understand that parents check phones out of love, not to control them. Setting clear rules and respecting each other can help both sides.

⑤ In the end, every family is different. What works for one may not work for another. The most important thing is that parents and children talk openly, build trust, and find a balance that works for their family.

**注释：** invade 侵犯；supervision 监督；constant 持续的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, why do some parents support checking children's phones?
   A. They hope to protect children from online dangers and find problems early.
   B. They want to control everything their children do.
   C. They do not trust their children at all.
   D. They want to use their children's phones for free.

2. What might happen if parents check phones without telling their children, according to paragraph ③?
   A. Children will study harder.
   B. Children may feel untrusted and hide more things from their parents.
   C. Children will stop using phones.
   D. Children will be happier.

3. What does the writer suggest about checking phones?
   A. Parents should never check their children's phones.
   B. Parents should always check phones secretly.
   C. Only experts should check children's phones.
   D. Families can make rules together and communicate openly.

4. The underlined word "constant" in paragraph ③ probably means ______.
   A. happening all the time
   B. sometimes
   C. never
   D. quick

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Checking phones is always wrong.
   B. Checking phones is always right.
   C. Teenagers should never use phones.
   D. There is no simple answer; open communication and finding a balance matter most.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "parents need to protect their children... By checking phones, parents can find problems early and talk to their children about them."

**【解析】** 支持者认为检查手机是为了保护孩子、及早发现问题并与孩子沟通。A 项正确。B 项"完全控制"、C 项"完全不信"、D 项"免费用手机"均非支持者理由。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "If parents check phones without telling their children, teenagers may feel they are not trusted. They might hide more things or stop sharing their feelings."

**【解析】** 背着孩子查手机会让孩子感到不被信任，从而隐瞒更多或不再分享感受。B 项正确。A、C、D 项与反对者论据不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some families choose to agree on rules together... Open communication is key... Setting clear rules and respecting each other can help both sides."

**【解析】** 作者建议家庭共同制定规则、坦诚沟通、尊重彼此。D 项正确。A 项"绝不检查"、B 项"总是偷偷检查"与建议矛盾；C 项"仅专家检查"未提及。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "constant checking can make teenagers feel stressed"

**【解析】** 持续不断地检查会使青少年感到压力，constant 意为"持续的、频繁的"。A 项 happening all the time 与之相符。B 项 sometimes、C 项 never、D 项 quick 皆不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "There is no simple answer"；第⑤段 "every family is different... The most important thing is that parents and children talk openly, build trust, and find a balance"

**【解析】** 作者观点：没有单一答案，重要的是坦诚沟通、建立信任、找到适合家庭的平衡。D 项准确概括。A、B 项"总是错/对"过于绝对；C 项"禁用手机"非主旨。故选 D。
