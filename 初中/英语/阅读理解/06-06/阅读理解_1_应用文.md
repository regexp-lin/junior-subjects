> **06-06 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Green Action — Save Water and Electricity**

② Dear students, our school is starting a Green Action campaign to protect the environment. We need everyone to take part. Saving water and electricity is not only good for our planet, but also helps the school cut down costs.

③ **Save water:** Turn off the tap when you brush your teeth or wash your hands. If you see a leaking tap, please report it to the school office. Take shorter showers and do not leave the water running when you soap up. Use a cup when you brush your teeth instead of letting water run.

④ **Save electricity:** Turn off the lights and fans when you leave a classroom. Do not forget to switch off computers and projectors after class. On sunny days, try to use natural light instead of turning on lights. Unplug (拔掉插头) chargers when your phone or laptop is fully charged.

⑤ Let us work together. Every small action counts. Join our Green Club if you want to do more. Together we can make our school a greener place!

**注释：** campaign 活动；leaking 漏水的；charge 充电

---

### 选择题

1. What is the main purpose of this notice?
   A. To encourage students to save water and electricity at school.
   B. To ask students to join the Green Club only.
   C. To tell students about a new building.
   D. To announce a holiday.

2. According to paragraph ③, what should students do when they see a leaking tap?
   A. Try to fix it themselves.
   B. Ignore it.
   C. Report it to the school office.
   D. Tell their parents only.

3. Which of the following is a way to save electricity mentioned in the passage?
   A. Use more fans in summer.
   B. Keep computers on all day.
   C. Charge phones overnight every night.
   D. Turn off lights when leaving a classroom.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ⑤?
   A. Small actions can make a difference.
   B. Only the Green Club can help the environment.
   C. Students must pay for the campaign.
   D. The school will close soon.

5. The underlined word "unplug" in paragraph ④ probably means ______.
   A. to turn on
   B. to connect
   C. to buy
   D. to remove the plug from the socket

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第②段 "our school is starting a Green Action campaign... Saving water and electricity is not only good for our planet"

**【解析】** 倡议书旨在号召学生节约用水用电。A 项正确。B 项"仅加入绿社"过于狭隘；C 项新建筑、D 项假期与内容无关。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "If you see a leaking tap, please report it to the school office."

**【解析】** 看到漏水龙头应报告给学校办公室。C 项正确。A 项自行修理、B 项忽视、D 项只告诉父母均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Turn off the lights and fans when you leave a classroom."

**【解析】** 离开教室时关灯关风扇为文中提到的节电方式。D 项正确。A 项多用风扇、B 项整天开电脑、C 项每天整夜充电均为浪费行为。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Every small action counts."

**【解析】** "每个小行动都有意义"可推知细微行动也能带来改变。A 项正确。B 项"只有绿社能环保"与 join if you want 矛盾；C 项付费、D 项学校关闭均未提及。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "Unplug chargers when your phone or laptop is fully charged." 及注释 "拔掉插头"

**【解析】** 充电完成后应拔掉 charger 的插头，unplug 即"从插座上拔下"。D 项 remove the plug from the socket 正确。A 项打开、B 项连接、C 项购买皆不符。故选 D。
