> **04-19 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Mark and Ben had been best friends since Grade 1. They played basketball together, did homework together, and shared all their secrets. However, last Wednesday, something unhappy happened between them.

② It was the day of the science test results. Mark got an "A," but Ben got a "C." When the teacher gave back the test papers, Mark jumped up cheerfully and shouted, "Yes! It's so easy!" Ben looked at his own paper, feeling very sad and embarrassed (尴尬的). He thought Mark was laughing at him. After that class, Ben didn't wait for Mark and walked home alone.

③ For two days, they didn't speak to each other. Mark felt confused (困惑的). He didn't know what he had done wrong. Every time Mark tried to talk to him, Ben just turned his face away. The classroom felt cold and boring without their usual jokes.

④ On Friday afternoon, Mark found a note in his desk. It was from Ben. The note said, "*I know you are smart. But when you said the test was so easy, it made me feel very stupid (愚蠢的) because I failed it.*"

⑤ Mark finally understood. He ran out of the classroom to catch up with Ben. "Ben! Stop!" Mark shouted. He ran to his friend and breathed heavily. "I am so sorry. I didn't mean to make you feel bad. I was just happy about my own score, but I didn't think about your feelings. Will you forgive (原谅) me? I can help you with science next time."

⑥ Ben looked at Mark's sincere (真诚的) face and smiled. "Okay, but you have to buy me a big ice cream," Ben laughed. Their friendship was saved, stronger than before. They learned that understanding and apologizing (道歉) are the keys to a true friendship.

**注释：** embarrassed 尴尬的；confused 困惑的；stupid 愚蠢的；forgive 原谅；sincere 真诚的

---

### 选择题

1. How long had Mark and Ben been best friends?
A. Since Grade 1.
B. For a few weeks.
C. Since last Wednesday.
D. Only for two days.

2. Why did Ben walk home alone last Wednesday?
A. Because he wanted to buy a big ice cream.
B. Because he got an "A" and Mark got a "C".
C. Because the teacher punished him to stay late.
D. Because he got a bad grade and thought Mark was laughing at him.

3. How did Mark feel when Ben didn't speak to him for two days?
A. He was very happy and excited.
B. He felt confused because he didn't know what he had done wrong.
C. He was very angry and wanted to fight Ben.
D. He felt bored and read many books at home.

4. Form paragraph ④, how did Mark find out why Ben was upset?
A. The science teacher told Mark.
B. Mark's parents guessed the reason.
C. Mark found a note from Ben in his desk.
D. Ben's other friends came to blame (责备) Mark.

5. What did Mark and Ben learn from this misunderstanding (误会)?
A. That science is an easy subject for everyone.
B. That true friends never talk to each other.
C. That buying ice cream is the only way to make friends.
D. That understanding each other and apologizing are the keys to a true friendship.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "Mark and Ben had been best friends since Grade 1."

**【解析】** 开头第一句清楚表明，他们从一年级起（since Grade 1）就是最好的朋友。故选A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解推断

**【定位】** 第②段 "...Ben got a 'C'... Mark jumped up cheerfully and shouted, 'Yes! It's so easy!' Ben looked at his own paper, feeling very sad and embarrassed. He thought Mark was laughing at him. After that class, Ben didn't wait for Mark and walked home alone."

**【解析】** Ben为何独自回家？因为他得了个差成绩（C），且看到Mark大喊“太简单了”，以为Mark在嘲笑他。这对应了选项D的情境。A（想买冰淇淋）、B（弄反了得分）、C（被老师罚留堂）与原文全不相干。故选D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "Mark felt confused. He didn't know what he had done wrong."

**【解析】** 面对好朋友的两天冷战，Mark的感受是感到困惑（felt confused），因为他不知道自己做错了什么事。这直接对应了B项。A、C、D项均与文中描写的Mark迷惑不解的心情不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1-2句 "On Friday afternoon, Mark found a note in his desk. It was from Ben."

**【解析】** 第四段揭晓解开谜团的途径：Mark在书桌里发现了一张来自Ben的字条（a note）。A老师说、B父母猜出、D其他朋友来责备，皆未在文中发生。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑥段最后一句 "They learned that understanding and apologizing (道歉) are the keys to a true friendship."

**【解析】** 全文结尾处点出这个故事传达的主旨大意：他俩明白了一个道理，相互理解并为自己的疏忽行为真诚道歉（understanding and apologizing）才是维系真正友谊的钥匙。A、B项违背文意甚至常理，C项用买冰淇淋交友属于小题大做的偏见。故选D。
