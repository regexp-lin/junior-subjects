# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Almost everyone loves chocolate. We eat it as a snack, drink it hot in winter, and give it to our friends on special days. But do you know where chocolate comes from and how it is made? The answer might surprise you. Chocolate actually grows on trees!

② Chocolate comes from the cacao (可可) tree. These trees only grow in hot, rainy environments, mostly near the equator (赤道). A cacao tree produces large, colorful pods (豆荚). When farmers open these pods, they find soft white fruit with many purple seeds inside. These seeds are the cacao beans, the magical things that make chocolate.

③ However, if you taste a fresh cacao bean, you will spit (吐) it out immediately. It is very bitter (苦的)! To change the bitter beans into sweet chocolate, an interesting process (过程) is needed. First, farmers leave the beans under the hot sun to dry. After a few days, the beans turn brown and start to smell a little like chocolate.

④ Next, the beans are sent to chocolate factories. There, they are cleaned, roasted (烘烤), and pressed. The roasting process brings out the rich chocolate flavor. After that, workers mix the beans with milk and sugar to make the sweet milk chocolate we usually eat. Without milk and a lot of sugar, it would be dark chocolate, which has a stronger and slightly bitter taste.

⑤ So, the next time you enjoy a piece of delicious chocolate, think about the long journey it has traveled—from a bitter seed in a hot forest to a sweet treat in your hand.

**注释：** equator 赤道；pod 豆荚；spit 吐；bitter 苦的；process 过程；flavor 风味，味道

### 选择题

1. Where does chocolate originally come from according to paragraph ①?
A. Deep under the ocean.
B. Animals on a farm.
C. Special machines in a factory.
D. Trees in hot, rainy environments.

2. In paragraph ②, what do farmers find inside the cacao pods?
A. Hot chocolate drinks.
B. Soft white fruit with purple seeds.
C. Dark chocolate bars.
D. Tiny chocolate candies.

3. Why wouldn't you like to eat a fresh cacao bean?
A. Because it is too cold.
B. Because it is too sweet.
C. Because it is very bitter.
D. Because it is very hard.

4. What happens to the beans when they are left under the hot sun?
A. They turn brown and start smelling like chocolate.
B. They change into sweet milk chocolate right away.
C. They grow into new cacao trees quickly.
D. They melt (融化) and become hot water.

5. What makes milk chocolate different from dark chocolate according to paragraph ④?
A. Milk chocolate is made without roasting the beans.
B. Milk chocolate is made without using cacao beans.
C. Milk chocolate is mixed with milk and sugar, while dark chocolate has a stronger, slightly bitter taste.
D. Milk chocolate is picked directly from the tree branches.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段末两句 "...does chocolate come from... Chocolate actually grows on trees!" 及第②段第2句 "These trees only grow in hot, rainy environments..."

**【解析】** 原文开篇即表明巧克力实际上长在树上（Chocolate actually grows on trees）。第二段又补充这是在炎热多雨的环境里生长的可可树。A（海底）、B（农场动物）、C（机器生产）都偏离了起源地。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段末尾两句 "When farmers open these pods, they find soft white fruit with many purple seeds inside."

**【解析】** 当农民打开豆荚（pods）时，他们会在里面发现柔软的白色果肉，以及很多紫色的种子（purple seeds）。A项熟可可饮品，C项黑巧克力块，D项巧克力糖果，均非原生态果实里的东西。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段头两句 "However, if you taste a fresh cacao bean, you will spit (吐) it out immediately. It is very bitter (苦的)!"

**【解析】** 原文表明了为什么你不愿意吃新鲜的可可豆：你会马上吐出来，因为它非常苦（It is very bitter）。所以A太冷、B太甜、D太硬均不相符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "First, farmers leave the beans under the hot sun to dry. After a few days, the beans turn brown and start to smell a little like chocolate."

**【解析】** 农民把可可豆放在烈日下晾干。几天后它们会变成棕色，并且开始闻起来有点像巧克力了（turn brown and start to smell a little like chocolate）。B、C、D项过于魔幻，违背了事实与上文描述。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段末尾 "...mix the beans with milk and sugar to make the sweet milk chocolate we usually eat. Without milk and a lot of sugar, it would be dark chocolate, which has a stronger and slightly bitter taste."

**【解析】** 工人将豆子与牛奶、糖混合制成牛奶巧克力。若无奶少糖则是黑巧克力，黑巧克力味道更浓且带点苦味。因此C项对两者成份及口味的区别概括得最精准。故选C。
