# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should Students Wash Their Own Clothes?**

② As children grow older, many parents wonder: should students do their own laundry (洗衣服)? Some say it takes time away from study. Others believe it helps build independence (独立). Let us look at both sides of the argument.

③ **Those who support (支持) students washing their own clothes** point out several benefits. First, it teaches young people important life skills. When they leave home for high school or university, they will need to take care of themselves. Learning to wash clothes now makes that transition (过渡) easier. Second, doing housework helps students understand how much their parents do for them. It can make them more thankful. Third, doing simple tasks like laundry can help students feel a sense of achievement (成就感) and become more confident (自信的).

④ **Those who disagree** argue that students already have heavy schoolwork. Washing clothes takes time and energy. They worry that children may feel tired and do poorly in their exams. Some parents also think that teenagers are not careful enough—they might damage (损坏) clothes or use too much water. These parents prefer to wash clothes themselves so their children can focus on study.

⑤ **The conclusion:** There is no single right answer. The best way may depend on the student's age, how much homework they have, and the family situation. However, most experts suggest that students can start with small tasks—for example, washing their own socks or T-shirts. This way, they learn step by step without too much pressure. As students grow, taking on more responsibility at home can prepare them well for the future.

**注释：** laundry 洗衣服；independence 独立；transition 过渡；achievement 成就感；confident 自信的；damage 损坏

---

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, what is one benefit of students washing their own clothes?
   A. It teaches them life skills and makes it easier when they leave home later.
   B. It helps them earn money for the family.
   C. It allows parents to have more free time to watch TV.
   D. It makes their clothes last longer.

2. Why do some people think students should NOT wash their own clothes?
   A. Because washing machines are too expensive.
   B. Because students do not like clean clothes.
   C. Because students have heavy schoolwork and may feel tired; some also worry teenagers might damage clothes.
   D. Because it is against the school rules.

3. What do experts suggest students do at first?
   A. Wash all the family's clothes every week.
   B. Buy a new washing machine.
   C. Ask their teachers for help.
   D. Start with small tasks like washing their own socks or T-shirts.

4. What does the writer think about whether students should wash their own clothes?
   A. There is no single right answer; it depends on age, homework, and family situation.
   B. All students must wash clothes every day.
   C. No student should ever wash clothes.
   D. Only girls should learn to wash clothes.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Students should never do any housework.
   B. Parents must do all the laundry.
   C. Learning to take responsibility at home step by step can help students prepare for their future life.
   D. Washing clothes has no connection to a student's future.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "it teaches young people important life skills... Learning to wash clothes now makes that transition (过渡) easier."

**【解析】** 文中指出自己洗衣服能教授生活技能，且日后离家时过渡更容易。A项正确。B项赚钱、C项家长看电视、D项衣物更耐用均未提及。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "students already have heavy schoolwork... They worry that children may feel tired... teenagers are not careful enough—they might damage (损坏) clothes or use too much water."

**【解析】** 反对者认为学生课业繁重、可能疲惫影响成绩，且青少年可能不够仔细会损坏衣物或浪费水。C项准确概括。A项洗衣机贵、B项不喜欢干净衣服、D项违反校规均未提及。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "students can start with small tasks—for example, washing their own socks or T-shirts."

**【解析】** 文中明确建议从小任务起步，如洗自己的袜子和T恤。D项正确。A项洗全家衣服、B项买洗衣机、C项向老师求助均与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断/主旨理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "There is no single right answer. The best way may depend on the student's age, how much homework they have, and the family situation."

**【解析】** 作者总结不存在唯一正确答案，最佳方式取决于学生年龄、作业量和家庭情况。A项准确概括作者观点。B、C、D项均为绝对化表述，与作者辩证态度不符。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "taking on more responsibility at home can prepare them well for the future."

**【解析】** 文中指出逐步承担更多家庭责任可为未来做好准备，可推知在家学会负责能帮助学生为今后生活做准备。C项正确。A、B项与原文建议相反；D项与"prepare them well for the future"矛盾。故选C。
