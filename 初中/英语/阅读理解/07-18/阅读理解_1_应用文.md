# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Community Library Summer Film Show**

② Our community library is pleased to invite you to our summer film show! From July 15 to August 20, we will show one family-friendly film every Friday evening at 7:00 p.m. in the reading hall. All are welcome and it is free.

③ **What to expect:** Each film is about 90 minutes long. We will choose popular movies that both children and adults can enjoy. Before each film, our librarian will give a short introduction (介绍) to help you understand the story better.

④ **How to join:** You do not need to buy tickets. Just come to the library before 7:00 p.m. on Friday evenings. Seats are limited to 80 people, so please arrive early. You may bring your own snacks, but we ask you to keep the hall clean.

⑤ **Contact us:** For the full film list or if you have any questions, please visit our website or call 8756-2341. We look forward to seeing you this summer!

**注释：** introduction 介绍

---

### 选择题

1. When does the summer film show take place?
   A. Every Friday evening.
   B. Every Monday evening.
   C. Every Saturday morning.
   D. Every Sunday afternoon.

2. How long does each film last?
   A. About 60 minutes.
   B. About 120 minutes.
   C. About 90 minutes.
   D. About 150 minutes.

3. What should you do if you want to join the film show?
   A. Buy a ticket in advance.
   B. Call the library to book a seat first.
   C. Bring your library card to show at the door.
   D. Just come to the library before 7:00 p.m. on Friday.

4. How many people can watch the film at one time?
   A. 80.
   B. 50.
   C. 60.
   D. 100.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The film show is only for children.
   B. The film show is free and aims to bring the community together.
   C. The library will serve free snacks during the film.
   D. You must be a member of the library to join.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "we will show one family-friendly film every Friday evening at 7:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明每周五晚上7点放映电影。A项正确。B项周一、C项周六上午、D项周日下午均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Each film is about 90 minutes long."

**【解析】** 文中直接写明每部电影约90分钟。C项正确。A、B、D项时长均错误。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "You do not need to buy tickets. Just come to the library before 7:00 p.m. on Friday evenings."

**【解析】** 文中说明无需购票，只需在周五晚上7点前到达图书馆即可。D项正确。A项需提前购票与原文矛盾；B项需电话预约、C项需出示借书证均未提及。故选D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Seats are limited to 80 people."

**【解析】** 文中明确写出座位限制为80人。A项正确。B、C、D项人数均与原文不符。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "All are welcome and it is free." 及全文

**【解析】** 文中强调所有人欢迎、免费参与，且为社区图书馆举办的家庭友好型活动，可推知活动免费且旨在将社区凝聚在一起。B项正确。A项仅限儿童与"family-friendly"矛盾；C项提供免费零食未提及，仅允许自带；D项需成为会员与"All are welcome"矛盾。故选B。
