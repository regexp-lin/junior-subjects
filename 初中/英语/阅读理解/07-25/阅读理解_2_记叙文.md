> **07-25 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① On a cold winter morning, heavy snow fell and covered the whole neighborhood. Zhang Wei, a middle school student, woke up early and looked out of the window. The ground was white and thick with snow. His parents had already gone to work, so he had some free time. He thought about his neighbor, Grandma Liu, who lived alone. Her driveway (车道) would be difficult to walk on with all that snow.

② Zhang Wei put on his warm jacket and gloves, took a shovel (铲子) from the garage, and walked to Grandma Liu's house. When he knocked on the door, Grandma Liu was surprised to see him. "Young man, what are you doing here in such bad weather?" she asked. Zhang Wei smiled and said, "I've come to shovel the snow for you. The path is too slippery (滑的) for you to walk safely."

③ Without waiting for a reply, Zhang Wei started to work. He cleared the snow from the driveway and the path to her door. It took him nearly forty minutes, and his hands grew cold, but he did not stop. Grandma Liu stood by the window, watching with tears in her eyes. She had not expected such kindness from a young neighbor.

④ When Zhang Wei finished, Grandma Liu invited him in for a warm cup of tea. She thanked him again and again. "You are such a good boy," she said. "Your parents must be very proud of you." Zhang Wei felt happy and a little shy. He said, "My parents always teach me to help others when I can. I'm glad I could help."

⑤ That afternoon, when Zhang Wei's mother came home, Grandma Liu called her to say how moved she was by Zhang Wei's help. Zhang Wei did not tell anyone what he had done — he simply felt that helping a neighbor was the right thing to do. A small act of kindness can warm many hearts on a cold winter day.

**注释：** driveway 车道；shovel 铲子；slippery 滑的；garage 车库

---

### 选择题

1. Why did Zhang Wei decide to help Grandma Liu on that snowy morning?
   A. His parents asked him to do it.
   B. He had free time and thought of Grandma Liu, who lived alone; her driveway would be difficult to walk on.
   C. Grandma Liu called him for help.
   D. He wanted to earn money from her.

2. According to paragraph ②, what did Zhang Wei bring when he went to Grandma Liu's house?
   A. Only his jacket and gloves.
   B. Tea and cookies.
   C. A shovel from the garage.
   D. Nothing at all.

3. How long did it take Zhang Wei to clear the snow?
   A. About ten minutes.
   B. About twenty minutes.
   C. More than an hour.
   D. Nearly forty minutes.

4. What can we infer from Zhang Wei's words "My parents always teach me to help others when I can"?
   A. His family values helping others, which influenced his kind behavior.
   B. His parents never help anyone.
   C. He was forced to help by his parents.
   D. He helped Grandma Liu only to please his parents.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Snow is dangerous and everyone should stay indoors.
   B. Old people should never live alone.
   C. Shoveling snow is good exercise for students.
   D. A small act of kindness can make a big difference and warm people's hearts.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He thought about his neighbor, Grandma Liu, who lived alone. Her driveway (车道) would be difficult to walk on with all that snow."

**【解析】** 原文说明张伟有空闲时间，想到独居的刘奶奶，她的车道在大雪后难以行走。B项准确概括他决定帮忙的原因。A项"父母要求"、C项"刘奶奶致电求助"、D项"为了赚钱"均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He put on his warm jacket and gloves, took a shovel (铲子) from the garage, and walked to Grandma Liu's house."

**【解析】** 原文清楚列出张伟穿上外套和手套，从车库拿了铲子去刘奶奶家。C项"从车库拿的铲子"正确。A项"只有外套手套"不完整；B项"茶和饼干"、D项"什么都没带"与原文矛盾。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "It took him nearly forty minutes, and his hands grew cold, but he did not stop."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明铲雪花了将近四十分钟。D项正确。A、B、C项时间均与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "My parents always teach me to help others when I can."

**【解析】** 张伟提到父母常教导他有能力时帮助他人，可推知家庭重视助人，这影响了他主动铲雪的行为。A项正确。B项"父母从不助人"与他的话矛盾；C项"被父母强迫"、D项"仅为讨好父母"均不符原文。故选A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图/主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文及第⑤段 "A small act of kindness can warm many hearts on a cold winter day."

**【解析】** 文章通过张伟雪天帮邻居铲雪的故事，传达"小小的善举能温暖人心"的道理，D项最能概括作者意图。A项侧重雪的危险；B项"老人不应独居"非主旨；C项"铲雪是运动"过于表面。故选D。
