# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should Students Eat Lunch at School?**

② In China, most students have lunch at school. The school provides meals in the canteen (食堂). But some parents wonder if this is a good choice. They worry about whether school lunch is healthy enough and whether their children eat well. Let us look at both sides of this question.

③ **Why eating at school is a good idea:** School lunch saves time. Students do not need to go home or leave the campus at noon. They can rest in the classroom after eating, and they will have more energy for afternoon classes. The school canteen usually offers different dishes every day, and the food is prepared in a clean kitchen. The government also has rules to make sure school meals are safe and nutritious (有营养的).

④ **Why some people disagree:** Some parents say school lunch tastes boring and their children do not like it. They prefer to bring homemade food or let their children eat outside. However, food from outside may not be as safe as school meals. Some students also waste time buying snacks (零食) at the school gate instead of eating a proper lunch. This is bad for their health.

⑤ **My opinion:** I think eating lunch at school is a better choice for most students. It is safe, saves time, and helps students form good eating habits. Of course, schools should try to make the food taste better and provide more choices. If students and parents work with the school, we can make school lunch a healthy and enjoyable part of the school day.

**注释：** canteen 食堂；nutritious 有营养的；snack 零食

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what do some parents worry about?
   A. The school canteen closes too early.
   B. Students have too much homework at noon.

   D. Whether school lunch is healthy enough and whether their children eat well.

2. What advantage does eating at school have, according to the passage?
   A. School lunch saves time and students can rest and have more energy for afternoon classes.
   B. Students can play games all lunch time.

3. Why do some parents disagree with school lunch?
   A. They think the school building is too old.
   B. They want their children to stay home all day.
   C. They think teachers eat too much.
   D. They believe school lunch tastes boring and their children do not like it.

4. What does the writer think about eating lunch at school?
   A. It is a bad choice and should be stopped.
   B. Students should never eat at school.
   C. Only young students should eat at school.
   D. It is a better choice for most students because it is safe, saves time, and helps form good eating habits.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell readers how to cook school lunch.
   B. To complain about the price of school meals.
   C. To introduce the best restaurants near schools.
   D. To discuss whether students should eat lunch at school and give the writer's opinion.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "They worry about whether school lunch is healthy enough and whether their children eat well."

**【解析】** 文中明确写出部分家长担心学校午餐是否足够健康、孩子是否吃得舒心。D 项正确。A 项食堂关门早、B 项中午作业多、C 项价格高均未提及。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "School lunch saves time... They can rest in the classroom after eating, and they will have more energy for afternoon classes."

**【解析】** 在校用午餐可节省时间，饭后可在教室休息，下午更有精力。A 项正确。B 项整午餐玩游戏、C 项免费、D 项不用排队均非原文内容。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some parents say school lunch tastes boring and their children do not like it."

**【解析】** 部分家长认为学校午餐味道单调，孩子不喜欢。D 项正确。A 项教学楼旧、B 项希望孩子全天在家、C 项老师吃太多均与原文不符。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I think eating lunch at school is a better choice for most students. It is safe, saves time, and helps students form good eating habits."

**【解析】** 作者认为对大多数学生而言，在校用餐是更好选择，因为它安全、省时并有助于养成良好饮食习惯。D 项正确。A、B、C 项均与作者立场相反。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Should Students Eat Lunch at School?" 及全文结构（正反观点 + 作者结论）

**【解析】** 文章围绕“学生是否应在校吃午餐”展开讨论，提出正反观点并给出作者意见。D 项准确概括主旨。A 项教烹饪、B 项抱怨价格、C 项介绍校外餐馆均非主旨。故选 D。
