> **06-28 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Class Duty Schedule — Cleaning Arrangement Notice**

② Hello everyone! Here is our new class duty schedule for this month. Each week, we need different students to help keep our classroom clean. Please read carefully and remember your duty days.

③ **Week 1 (March 4–8):** Li Ming and Wang Fang — sweep the floor and empty the bins (垃圾桶). Zhang Hua — clean the blackboard and wipe the desks. Chen Wei — water the plants.

④ **Week 2 (March 11–15):** Liu Na and Xu Lei — sweep and mop the floor. Zhao Jing — clean the windows. Yang Tao — tidy the bookshelf.

⑤ If you cannot do your duty on your day, please tell the monitor (班长) at least one day before. Switch with another student if possible. Let us work together to make our classroom a nice place to study! Thank you for your help.

**注释：** sweep 打扫；empty 清空；wipe 擦拭；monitor 班长

---

### 选择题

1. What is this notice mainly about?
   A. A class duty schedule for cleaning.
   B. A sports meeting at school.
   C. A new homework plan.
   D. A school trip next week.

2. Who should sweep the floor and empty the bins in Week 1?
   A. Zhang Hua.
   B. Li Ming and Wang Fang.
   C. Chen Wei.
   D. Zhao Jing.

3. In Week 2, who is in charge of cleaning the windows?
   A. Liu Na.
   B. Xu Lei.
   C. Yang Tao.
   D. Zhao Jing.

4. What should you do if you cannot do your duty on your day?
   A. Just stay at home and do nothing.
   B. Ask someone else to do it without telling anyone.
   C. Tell the monitor at least one day before and try to switch with another student.
   D. Come to school late so you miss the duty.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The duty schedule changes every week.
   B. Students only need to clean once a month.
   C. Only one student cleans the classroom each day.
   D. Students do not need to water the plants.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解

**【定位】** 标题 "Class Duty Schedule — Cleaning Arrangement Notice" 及第②段 "Here is our new class duty schedule for this month."

**【解析】** 通知标题和开头明确说明是班级值日表及卫生安排。A 项正确。B 项运动会、C 项作业计划、D 项郊游均非本文内容。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Week 1 (March 4–8): Li Ming and Wang Fang — sweep the floor and empty the bins (垃圾桶)."

**【解析】** 第一周负责扫地、倒垃圾桶的是 Li Ming 和 Wang Fang。B 项正确。A 项 Zhang Hua 负责擦黑板和桌子、C 项 Chen Wei 负责浇花、D 项 Zhao Jing 是第二周值日，均不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Week 2 (March 11–15):... Zhao Jing — clean the windows."

**【解析】** 第二周负责擦窗户的是 Zhao Jing。D 项正确。A、B 项负责扫地拖地、C 项负责整理书架。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If you cannot do your duty on your day, please tell the monitor (班长) at least one day before. Switch with another student if possible."

**【解析】** 若无法值日，须至少提前一天告知班长，并尽量与其他同学调换。C 项正确。A 项待在家里、B 项偷偷找人替、D 项迟到逃避均与要求不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③④段分别列出 Week 1 和 Week 2 的值日安排，每周人员不同。

**【解析】** 文章展示了两周不同的值日安排，可推知值日表按周轮换。A 项正确。B 项每月一次、C 项每天一人、D 项无需浇花均与原文矛盾。故选 A。
