# 阅读理解（说明文）

**How Does a Microwave Oven Work?**

① Most families have a microwave oven in the kitchen. It can heat food in just a few minutes. But have you ever wondered how it works? The answer has to do with something invisible — microwaves.

② Microwaves are a type of electromagnetic wave, similar to radio waves and light waves, but you cannot see them. Inside a microwave oven, there is a device called a magnetron. When you press the start button, the magnetron produces microwaves. These waves bounce around inside the metal walls of the oven.

③ When microwaves hit food, they make the water molecules inside the food vibrate very quickly — billions of times per second. This vibration creates heat, and the heat cooks or warms the food. That is why food with more water heats up faster. A bowl of soup gets hot quickly, but a dry piece of bread takes longer.

④ You may have noticed that the plate inside a microwave oven turns slowly. This is to make sure microwaves reach every part of the food evenly. Without the turning plate, some parts would be hot while other parts would still be cold.

⑤ There are a few important safety rules. Never put metal objects inside a microwave oven. Metal reflects microwaves and can cause sparks or even a fire. Also, be careful when taking out heated food — it may be much hotter than you expect, especially liquids. Used properly, a microwave oven is a safe and useful tool in daily life.

**注释：** electromagnetic wave 电磁波；magnetron 磁控管；molecule 分子；vibrate 振动

---

**Questions:**

1. What does a magnetron do in a microwave oven?
   A. It cools the food down.
   B. It produces microwaves.
   C. It turns the plate.
   D. It adds water to the food.

2. Why does food with more water heat up faster?
   A. Water is heavier.
   B. Microwaves make water molecules vibrate and produce heat.
   C. Water changes colour when heated.
   D. Water does not absorb microwaves.

3. Why does the plate inside a microwave oven turn?
   A. To make the food look nicer.
   B. To save energy.
   C. To make sure microwaves reach every part of the food evenly.
   D. To mix the food together.

4. What should you NOT put inside a microwave oven?
   A. A bowl of soup.
   B. A glass of milk.
   C. A metal spoon.
   D. A piece of bread.

5. What is the purpose of this passage?
   A. To teach people how to cook.
   B. To explain how a microwave oven works.
   C. To sell microwave ovens.
   D. To warn people about the dangers of microwaves.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Inside a microwave oven, there is a device called a magnetron. When you press the start button, the magnetron produces microwaves."

**【解析】** 原文明确说磁控管的作用是产生微波。A项"冷却食物"与原文功能相反；C项"转动托盘"是另一个部件的功能；D项"给食物加水"属于无中生有。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "When microwaves hit food, they make the water molecules inside the food vibrate very quickly... This vibration creates heat"

**【解析】** 微波使食物中的水分子快速振动产生热量，所以水分越多的食物加热越快。A项"水更重"与加热原理无关；C项"水变色"原文未提及；D项"水不吸收微波"与原文相反。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "This is to make sure microwaves reach every part of the food evenly."

**【解析】** 原文说转盘转动是为了让微波均匀到达食物的每个部分。A项"让食物更好看"无依据；B项"节省能源"原文未提及；D项"搅拌食物"不是转盘的目的。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Never put metal objects inside a microwave oven. Metal reflects microwaves and can cause sparks or even a fire."

**【解析】** 原文明确警告不能把金属物品放进微波炉，因为会引起火花甚至火灾。金属勺子属于金属物品。A项汤、B项牛奶、D项面包都是可以微波加热的食物。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文从第①段提出问题"微波炉如何工作"，到第②③④⑤段逐步解释原理和注意事项

**【解析】** 文章的核心目的是解释微波炉的工作原理。A项"教人做饭"不是本文目的，文章讲的是原理；C项"卖微波炉"不是科普文的目的；D项"警告微波的危险"只是第⑤段的部分内容，不是全文主旨。故选B。
