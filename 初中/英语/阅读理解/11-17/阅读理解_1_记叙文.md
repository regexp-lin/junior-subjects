> **11-17 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Learning to Cook for My Family**

① My mum got sick last October. She had to stay in bed for two weeks. My dad works in another city and only comes home on weekends. That meant I had to take care of myself and my younger sister after school. The biggest problem was cooking.

② I had never cooked a real meal before. On the first evening, I tried to make egg fried rice. I put too much oil in the pan. The rice stuck to the bottom and turned brown. My sister looked at the plate and said, "Is this food?" I felt embarrassed, but I also laughed. We ate instant noodles that night.

③ The next day, I called my grandma and asked her to teach me over the phone. She told me step by step how to make tomato and egg soup. "Use two eggs. Cut three tomatoes. Add water first, then put in the tomatoes. When the water boils, pour in the beaten eggs slowly." I followed her instructions carefully. This time, the soup was not bad at all. My sister even asked for a second bowl.

④ Over the next two weeks, I learned to cook several simple dishes: stir-fried vegetables, steamed fish, and porridge. Every evening, I set the table and we ate together. Sometimes the food was too salty or too bland, but my sister always said, "Thank you, brother." Those words meant everything to me.

⑤ When Mum got better, she tasted my cooking and smiled. "You've grown up," she said. I realised that taking care of others is not just a duty — it is also a way to show love. Now I cook for the family every weekend, and I enjoy it.

**注释：** instant noodles 方便面；bland 淡的

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer need to cook?
   A. He wanted to become a chef.
   B. His mum was sick and his dad worked in another city.
   C. His sister asked him to cook.
   D. He had a cooking class at school.

2. What happened when the writer first tried to cook?
   A. The food was delicious.
   B. He made perfect fried rice.
   C. The rice stuck to the pan and turned brown.
   D. His sister helped him cook.

3. Who taught the writer how to make tomato and egg soup?
   A. His mum.
   B. His dad.
   C. His grandma.
   D. His teacher.

4. What can we learn about the writer's sister?
   A. She was ungrateful and complained a lot.
   B. She refused to eat her brother's food.
   C. She was supportive and thanked her brother.
   D. She cooked better than her brother.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Cooking is too difficult for students.
   B. Taking care of others is a way to show love.
   C. Instant noodles are the best food.
   D. He should never cook again.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "My mum got sick last October. She had to stay in bed for two weeks. My dad works in another city and only comes home on weekends."

**【解析】** 原文说妈妈生病卧床，爸爸在外地工作只周末回来，所以作者不得不负责做饭。A项"想当厨师"原文未提及；C项"妹妹让他做"不是真正原因；D项"学校有烹饪课"属于无中生有。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The rice stuck to the bottom and turned brown."

**【解析】** 原文描述第一次做蛋炒饭时油放太多，米饭粘锅变焦。A项"很好吃"和B项"做了完美的炒饭"与事实相反；D项"妹妹帮忙做"原文未提及。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I called my grandma and asked her to teach me over the phone."

**【解析】** 原文明确说是打电话给奶奶，让她通过电话教做番茄蛋汤。A项妈妈当时在卧床；B项爸爸在外地；D项老师原文未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Sometimes the food was too salty or too bland, but my sister always said, 'Thank you, brother.' Those words meant everything to me."

**【解析】** 尽管食物有时太咸或太淡，妹妹总是说"谢谢哥哥"，可以推断她很支持和感恩。A项"忘恩负义、抱怨多"与原文相反；B项"拒绝吃"也与原文矛盾，她甚至喝了第二碗汤；D项"做饭比哥哥好"原文无此信息。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I realised that taking care of others is not just a duty — it is also a way to show love."

**【解析】** 作者在文末总结感悟：照顾他人不仅是责任，也是表达爱的方式。A项"做饭对学生太难"与文章积极主题矛盾；C项"方便面最好"只是文中一个小细节；D项"再也不做饭"与原文相反，他现在每周末都做饭。故选B。
