> **10-10 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Talent Show — Registration Notice**

① Dear students,

② Our school will hold the 2024 Autumn Talent Show on October 20th. We are now calling for performers! If you can sing, dance, play an instrument, or have any other talent, this is your chance to shine on stage.

**How to sign up:**
- **Time:** October 10th – October 15th
- **Place:** Room 203, Building B (Student Union office)
- **What to bring:** Your student ID card and a short note about your performance (what you will do and how long it will take, about 2–5 minutes)
- **Who can join:** All students from Grade 7 to Grade 9

③ We need all kinds of acts: singing, dancing, magic, piano, guitar, poetry reading, and more. The show will last about two hours. Each performer will get a certificate and the top three acts will receive small prizes.

④ Please sign up before October 15th. Late applications will not be accepted. We will send you the final programme by October 18th.

⑤ For any questions, contact Li Ming at the Student Union or email us at studentunion@school.edu.cn. We look forward to seeing your talent!

Student Union  
October 8th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When is the Talent Show?
   A. October 8th.
   B. October 10th.
   C. October 15th.
   D. October 20th.

2. Where should students go to sign up?
   A. The school hall.
   B. Room 203, Building B.
   C. The library.
   D. Their classroom.

3. What should students bring when they sign up?
   A. A photo and their parent's phone number.
   B. Their student ID card and a note about their performance.
   C. Only their name.
   D. A video of their performance.

4. How long should each act last?
   A. At least 10 minutes.
   B. About 2–5 minutes.
   C. Exactly 2 hours.
   D. One hour.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Only Grade 9 students can take part.
   B. Students who sign up after October 15th may still be accepted.
   C. The Student Union organises the event and welcomes all kinds of talents.
   D. There are no prizes for performers.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】D**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold the 2024 Autumn Talent Show on October 20th."
**【解析】** 原文明确说明才艺秀于10月20日举行。A为通知发布日期；B、C为报名时间。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "**Place:** Room 203, Building B (Student Union office)"
**【解析】** 报名地点为B栋203室学生会办公室。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "**What to bring:** Your student ID card and a short note about your performance (what you will do and how long it will take, about 2–5 minutes)"
**【解析】** 报名需携带学生证和关于表演的简短说明。B项准确。A、C、D项原文均未要求。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "about 2–5 minutes"
**【解析】** 每段表演时长约为2–5分钟。B项正确。A、C、D项时长与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第③段 "We need all kinds of acts"；第⑤段 "contact Li Ming at the Student Union"
**【解析】** 通知由学生会发布，欢迎各类才艺，可推知C项。A项"仅九年级"与"Grade 7 to Grade 9"矛盾；B项与"Late applications will not be accepted"矛盾；D项与"top three acts will receive small prizes"矛盾。故选C。
