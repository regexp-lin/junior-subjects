# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Green Week — Activity Schedule**

① Dear students and teachers,

② Our school will hold a Green Week from November 4th to 10th. The goal is to raise awareness about the environment and encourage everyone to live a greener life. All students are welcome to take part.

**Activity Schedule:**

| Date        | Activity              | Place        | Time     |
|-------------|-----------------------|--------------|----------|
| Nov 4 (Mon) | Opening ceremony      | School hall  | 9:00 a.m.|
| Nov 5 (Tue) | Waste sorting workshop| Classroom 201| 4:00 p.m.|
| Nov 6 (Wed) | Plant a tree          | School garden| 3:30 p.m.|
| Nov 7 (Thu) | Poster design contest | Art room     | 4:00 p.m.|
| Nov 8 (Fri) | Green fashion show    | School hall  | 2:00 p.m.|
| Nov 10 (Sun)| Closing & prize-giving| School hall  | 10:00 a.m.|

③ Students can sign up for activities at the Students' Union office before November 2nd. Each student may join up to three activities. Please wear comfortable clothes for the tree-planting activity.

④ For the poster design contest, each class should send at least one entry. The theme is "Save Water, Save Life." Winners will receive certificates and small gifts.

⑤ If you have any questions, please contact Mr Li at li@school.edu.cn or call 020-12345678. Let us work together for a greener campus!

School Green Week Committee  
October 20th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the Green Week begin?
   A. November 2nd.
   B. November 4th.
   C. November 5th.
   D. November 10th.

2. Where will the waste sorting workshop take place?
   A. School hall.
   B. Classroom 201.
   C. School garden.
   D. Art room.

3. What is the theme of the poster design contest?
   A. Save paper.
   B. Save Water, Save Life.
   C. Plant more trees.
   D. Reduce waste.

4. How many activities can each student join at most?
   A. One.
   B. Two.
   C. Three.
   D. All of them.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. The Green Week is only for teachers.
   B. Students must join all activities.
   C. The school wants students to care more about the environment.
   D. The tree-planting activity requires formal clothes.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold a Green Week from November 4th to 10th"
**【解析】** 环保周从11月4日开始。B项正确。A为报名截止日，C、D为其他活动日期。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Waste sorting workshop 对应 Place 为 "Classroom 201"
**【解析】** 垃圾分类工作坊在201教室举行。B项正确。A、C、D为其他活动地点。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "The theme is 'Save Water, Save Life.'"
**【解析】** 海报设计比赛的主题是"节约用水，拯救生命"。B项正确。A、C、D项未在文中出现。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Each student may join up to three activities"
**【解析】** 每位学生最多可参加三项活动。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "The goal is to raise awareness about the environment and encourage everyone to live a greener life"
**【解析】** 环保周目的是提高环保意识、鼓励绿色生活，可推知学校希望学生更关注环境。C项正确。A、B、D项与文意不符。故选C。
