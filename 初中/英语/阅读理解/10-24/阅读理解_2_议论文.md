# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Students Take Part in Community Service?**

① Many schools now ask students to do community service. Some students visit old people's homes. Others clean parks or help at animal shelters. But should students really spend their time on such activities? People have different opinions.

② Those who support community service say it teaches important values. Students learn to care for others and understand the needs of their community. They also develop useful skills. For example, organising a charity event can improve teamwork and leadership. Besides, doing something for free can make students feel good. It builds character and a sense of responsibility. Many colleges and employers like to see community service on a student's record.

③ However, some people worry that community service takes time away from study. Students already have heavy homework and exams. Adding community service may make them tired and stressed. Some students might only do it for a grade or certificate, not because they really want to help. In that case, the activity loses its meaning. There is also the question of fairness. Not all families can afford the time or transport for their children to take part.

④ In my view, community service is valuable, but it should not be forced. Schools could offer it as an option and make it flexible. For example, students could choose activities that fit their schedule. Schools could also work with local organisations to provide projects near the campus. This way, more students can join without extra burden.

⑤ Community service can help students grow as caring citizens. The key is to design it in a way that benefits both the community and the students themselves.

**注释：** shelter 收容所；charity 慈善

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, what is one benefit of community service?
   A. Students get higher grades in exams.
   B. Students learn to care for others and develop useful skills.
   C. Students never need to do homework.
   D. Students can avoid going to school.

2. Why do some people worry about community service?
   A. Because it is too easy.
   B. Because it may take time from study and some students may do it only for a grade.
   C. Because there are no activities to join.
   D. Because it costs too much money.

3. What does the writer suggest about community service?
   A. Make it compulsory for all students.
   B. Offer it as an option and make it flexible.
   C. Cancel it completely.
   D. Only allow top students to join.

4. What does "builds character" mean in Paragraph ②?
   A. Builds buildings.
   B. Makes students stronger physically.
   C. Helps develop good qualities like responsibility.
   D. Teaches students to draw.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Community service is useless.
   B. When well designed, community service can help both the community and students.
   C. Schools should stop all community programmes.
   D. Only adults should do community service.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "it teaches important values... learn to care for others... develop useful skills"
**【解析】** 社区服务能让学生学会关心他人、培养有用技能。B项准确。A、C、D项未在支持理由中提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "community service takes time away from study... Some students might only do it for a grade or certificate"
**【解析】** 反对者担心占用学习时间，且部分学生仅为成绩或证书而参与。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "community service is valuable, but it should not be forced. Schools could offer it as an option and make it flexible"
**【解析】** 作者建议将其作为可选活动，并保持灵活。B项正确。A、C、D项与建议相反。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第②段 "builds character and a sense of responsibility"
**【解析】** 上下文指培养责任感等良好品质。C项准确。A、B、D项与文意不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The key is to design it in a way that benefits both the community and the students themselves"
**【解析】** 结尾强调合理设计可使社区与学生双受益。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
