# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Chopsticks (筷子) have been used in China for over 3,000 years. Long ago, people used wooden sticks to pick up food from boiling pots because it was too hot to touch. Over time, these sticks became thinner and longer. They spread to other Asian countries such as Japan and Korea. Today, chopsticks are still widely used in daily meals.

② Chopsticks can be made from different materials. Wood and bamboo are common and cheap. Some families use metal chopsticks. In the past, rich people often had chopsticks made of silver (银) because they believed silver would turn dark if the food was poisonous (有毒的). Now people choose chopsticks by their own taste and needs.

③ There are rules when using chopsticks. First, do not stick chopsticks straight up in your rice bowl. In Chinese culture, this reminds people of funerals (葬礼) and is seen as rude. Second, do not use chopsticks to point at others or tap them on the bowl. Third, when picking up food, hold the chopsticks in the middle and move them gently. It takes practice, but soon you will use them well.

④ When eating with others, it is polite to wait until everyone has food before you start. Also, never dig through the dishes with your chopsticks to find the best pieces. Take food from the part of the plate in front of you. Sharing food at the table is a way to show care and respect in Chinese culture.

⑤ Chopsticks are not only tools for eating but also a symbol of Chinese culture. Learning these customs helps people understand and respect each other better when they sit down to eat together.

**注释：** silver 银；poisonous 有毒的；funeral 葬礼

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Long ago, people used wooden sticks to pick up food from boiling pots because it was too hot to touch."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明古人用木棍夹取食物是因为锅中食物太烫无法用手触碰，A 项正确。B 项"不会用刀叉"、C 项"木棍更美观"和 D 项"筷子先在日本发明"均未提及或与原文矛盾。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "In the past, rich people often had chopsticks made of silver (银) because they believed silver would turn dark if the food was poisonous (有毒的)."

**【解析】** 原文写明过去富人使用银筷是因为相信食物有毒时银会变黑，C 项正确。A 项"银筷更轻"、B 项"银筷更便宜"和 D 项"银筷能保温"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "do not stick chopsticks straight up in your rice bowl... this reminds people of funerals (葬礼) and is seen as rude."

**【解析】** 原文指出将筷子竖直插入饭碗会让人联想到葬礼，被认为不礼貌，B 项正确。A 项"握住筷子中部"、C 项"轻动筷子夹取"和 D 项"从面前的盘边取菜"均为正确或礼貌做法，非不礼貌行为。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "it is polite to wait until everyone has food before you start... never dig through the dishes... Sharing food at the table is a way to show care and respect in Chinese culture."

**【解析】** 文中提到等人齐再动筷、不翻拣菜肴、在餐桌上分享食物体现关怀与尊重，可推知遵循餐桌礼仪和分享食物在中国文化中体现尊重，C 项正确。A 项"尽快吃完"、B 项"翻拣找菜"和 D 项"不必等人"均与原文相反。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段筷子历史，第②段材质，第③④段使用礼仪，第⑤段文化意义。

**【解析】** 文章介绍筷子的历史、材质及使用礼仪，并强调其文化象征，D 项准确概括主旨。A 项"教制作筷子"、B 项"比较刀叉"和 C 项"劝买昂贵筷子"均非主旨。故选 D。
