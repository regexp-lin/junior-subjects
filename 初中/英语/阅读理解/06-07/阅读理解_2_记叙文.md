> **06-07 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last summer, 14-year-old Zhang Wei spent two weeks at his uncle's fruit farm in the countryside. His uncle grew apples, peaches (桃子) and pears. It was harvest (收获) time, and many workers were needed. Zhang Wei decided to help. He woke up at five every morning and went to the orchard (果园) with the other workers.

② The first few days were not easy. The sun was strong and Zhang Wei's arms hurt from picking fruit. He had to climb ladders to reach the highest branches. Sometimes he dropped an apple and it rolled away. "Be careful and take your time," his uncle said. "Every apple counts." Zhang Wei nodded and kept trying. Slowly, he learned to pick fruit without damaging it.

③ By the end of the first week, Zhang Wei could work as fast as the experienced workers. He filled basket after basket with red apples and golden pears. During the break, the workers shared their lunch under a big tree. They told stories and laughed together. Zhang Wei felt tired but happy. He had never understood how hard farmers worked until then.

④ One afternoon, a tourist came to the farm. She asked Zhang Wei, "Why are you working here? You are so young." Zhang Wei smiled and said, "I want to help my uncle and learn about farming. Now I know that the fruit we buy in the city does not appear by itself. Someone has to pick it under the hot sun." The tourist nodded and bought a basket of apples.

⑤ When Zhang Wei returned home, he told his mother, "I will never waste food again. I know how much work goes into every apple and every pear." That summer on the farm was short, but the lesson stayed with him for a long time.

**注释：** peach 桃子；harvest 收获；orchard 果园

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, why did Zhang Wei go to his uncle's fruit farm last summer?
   A. To spend his holiday and relax.
   B. To help with the harvest because many workers were needed.
   C. To learn how to grow fruit trees.
   D. To sell fruit to tourists.

2. According to paragraph ②, what did Zhang Wei find difficult at first?
   A. The strong sun, sore arms, climbing ladders, and sometimes dropping fruit.
   B. Getting up early every day.
   C. Talking to other workers.
   D. Eating the food on the farm.

3. According to paragraph ③, what did Zhang Wei learn from the experience?
   A. How to grow apple trees.
   B. How to sell fruit to tourists.
   C. How hard farmers work and how to pick fruit as fast as experienced workers.
   D. How to repair ladders.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Zhang Wei?
   A. He did not like talking to tourists.
   B. He wanted to become a farmer in the future.
   C. He thought the tourist was rude.
   D. He had grown to understand and value the work of farmers through his experience.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Hands-on work can teach young people to appreciate farmers' hard work and not waste food.
   B. Summer is the best time to visit farms.
   C. All students should work on farms every summer.
   D. Fruit is cheaper in the countryside.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "It was harvest (收获) time, and many workers were needed. Zhang Wei decided to help."

**【解析】** 原文写明收获季节需要人手，张伟决定去帮忙，B 项正确。A 项"度假放松"、C 项"学种果树"和 D 项"向游客卖水果"均非张伟去农场的初衷。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The first few days were not easy. The sun was strong and Zhang Wei's arms hurt from picking fruit. He had to climb ladders... Sometimes he dropped an apple."

**【解析】** 原文说明起初困难：太阳烈、手臂酸痛、需爬梯子、有时会掉苹果，A 项正确。B 项仅提早起、C 项"与工人交谈"和 D 项"吃农场食物"并非主要困难。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "By the end of the first week, Zhang Wei could work as fast as the experienced workers... He had never understood how hard farmers worked until then."

**【解析】** 文中写到一周后张伟能像老工人一样快速摘果，且此前从未体会到农民如此辛苦，C 项正确。A 项"如何种苹果树"、B 项"如何向游客卖果"和 D 项"如何修梯子"均未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 Zhang Wei 对游客的回答："I want to help my uncle and learn about farming. Now I know that the fruit we buy in the city does not appear by itself. Someone has to pick it under the hot sun."

**【解析】** 张伟的回应表明他通过劳动理解了城市里的水果来之不易、需有人在烈日下采摘，可推知他懂得并尊重农民的劳作，D 项正确。A 项"不喜欢和游客说话"、B 项"想当农民"和 C 项"认为游客无礼"均无依据。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文及第⑤段 "I will never waste food again. I know how much work goes into every apple and every pear... the lesson stayed with him for a long time."

**【解析】** 文章通过张伟帮果农摘果的经历，传达实践劳动能让少年体谅农民辛苦并珍惜粮食，A 项正确。B 项"夏天最适合参观农场"、C 项"所有学生每年夏天都应务农"和 D 项"乡下水果更便宜"均非作者意图。故选 A。
