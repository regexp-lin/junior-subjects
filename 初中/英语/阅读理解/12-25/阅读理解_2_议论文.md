# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Why Teenagers Need Enough Sleep**

① Many teenagers go to bed late and wake up early for school. They feel sleepy in class. They find it hard to focus. Some even fall asleep at their desks. Is this normal? No. Teenagers need eight to ten hours of sleep each night. But surveys show that most get only six or seven. Why does sleep matter so much? Let us look at the reasons.

② First, sleep helps your brain work better. When you sleep, your brain processes what you learned during the day. It sorts memories and stores important information. If you do not sleep enough, you may forget what you studied. Your brain cannot work at its best when it is tired. That is why you feel slow and cannot think clearly after a bad night's sleep.

③ Second, sleep helps your body grow. Growth hormone is mostly released when you sleep. Teenagers are still growing. Lack of sleep can affect your height and health. It can also weaken your immune system. You may get sick more often. Your mood can change too. You may feel more easily angry or sad. Some studies link lack of sleep to anxiety and depression in teenagers.

④ Third, screens before bed are a problem. Phones and computers emit blue light. This light tells your brain it is still daytime. Your brain does not prepare for sleep. You lie in bed but cannot fall asleep easily. Experts suggest: stop using screens at least one hour before bed. Read a book or listen to soft music instead. Keep your bedroom dark and cool.

⑤ So, make sleep a priority. Set a regular bedtime. Avoid caffeine in the afternoon and evening. Do not do homework in bed. Your bed should be for sleep only. Good sleep is not a luxury. It is a necessity for a healthy and successful school life.

**注释：** survey 调查；hormone 激素；immune 免疫；anxiety 焦虑；caffeine 咖啡因

---

**Questions:**

1. How many hours of sleep do teenagers need each night?
   A. Four to five hours.
   B. Six to seven hours.
   C. Eight to ten hours.
   D. Twelve hours.

2. What does sleep help the brain do?
   A. Eat more food.
   B. Process what you learned, sort memories, and store information.
   C. Play games better.
   D. Talk louder.

3. Why should teenagers avoid screens before bed?
   A. Because screens are expensive.
   B. Because blue light from screens tells the brain it is daytime and makes it hard to fall asleep.
   C. Because screens break easily.
   D. Because parents do not allow it.

4. What does the writer suggest doing instead of using screens before bed?
   A. Playing games.
   B. Reading a book or listening to soft music.
   C. Doing homework.
   D. Eating snacks.

5. What is the main idea of the passage?
   A. Teenagers should play more and sleep less.
   B. Sleep is vital for teenagers' brain, body, and health; they should prioritise it.
   C. Screens are bad and should be banned.
   D. Only adults need good sleep.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Teenagers need eight to ten hours of sleep each night."

**【解析】** 青少年每晚需要8–10小时睡眠。A项4–5小时、B项6–7小时是多数人实际睡眠、D项12小时均不正确。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When you sleep, your brain processes what you learned during the day. It sorts memories and stores important information."

**【解析】** 睡眠时大脑会处理白天所学、整理记忆、存储重要信息。A项吃更多、C项游戏更好、D项说话更大声均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Phones and computers emit blue light. This light tells your brain it is still daytime. Your brain does not prepare for sleep."

**【解析】** 电子屏发出的蓝光让大脑以为仍是白天，不利于入睡。A项贵、C项易坏、D项家长不允许均非主要原因。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Experts suggest: stop using screens at least one hour before bed. Read a book or listen to soft music instead."

**【解析】** 专家建议睡前至少一小时不用电子设备，改为读书或听轻音乐。A项玩游戏、C项做作业、D项吃零食均与建议相反。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第⑤段 "Good sleep is not a luxury. It is a necessity for a healthy and successful school life."

**【解析】** 主旨为睡眠对青少年大脑、身体和健康至关重要，应把睡眠放在优先位置。A项多玩少睡错误；C、D项过于片面。故选B。
