# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Science Lab Safety Rules**

② Welcome to the school science laboratory (实验室). To keep everyone safe, all students must follow these rules. Please read them carefully before you start any experiment.

③ **Before the experiment:** First, you must wear safety goggles (护目镜) when working with chemicals or fire. Second, tie back long hair and do not wear loose clothes. Third, listen to your teacher's instructions. Never start an experiment by yourself. Fourth, keep food and drinks out of the lab. Eating or drinking in the lab is not allowed.

④ **During the experiment:** Work quietly and carefully. Do not run or play in the lab. If you spill (洒出) something, tell your teacher at once. Never touch chemicals with your hands. Use the right tools for each task.

⑤ **After the experiment:** Clean your work area. Wash your hands with soap and water. Put all equipment back in the right place. If you get hurt or feel unwell, report (报告) to your teacher immediately. Your safety is our first concern.

**注释：** laboratory 实验室；goggles 护目镜；spill 洒出；report 报告

---

### 选择题

1. When must students wear safety goggles?
   A. When working with chemicals or fire.
   B. Only when eating.
   C. After cleaning the lab.
   D. When talking to the teacher.

2. According to the passage, what should students do with long hair?
   A. Cut it short.
   B. Tie it back.
   C. Cover it with a hat.
   D. Wash it first.

3. What should students do if they spill something?
   A. Clean it up by themselves.
   B. Ignore it and continue.
   C. Tell their teacher at once.
   D. Leave the lab quickly.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Students can bring lunch to eat in the lab.
   B. Students may start experiments without the teacher.
   C. Students do not need to clean up after experiments.
   D. The school thinks student safety is very important.

5. What is this passage mainly about?
   A. Rules for staying safe in the school science lab.
   B. How to do science experiments.
   C. The best chemicals to use at school.
   D. How to become a science teacher.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "First, you must wear safety goggles (护目镜) when working with chemicals or fire."

**【解析】** 文中明确要求在使用化学品或涉及火源时必须佩戴护目镜。A项正确。B项进食时、C项清洁后、D项与老师交谈时均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Second, tie back long hair and do not wear loose clothes."

**【解析】** 文中规定长发须扎起，不可穿宽松衣物。B项正确。A项剪短、C项戴帽、D项先洗均未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "If you spill (洒出) something, tell your teacher at once."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明洒出东西时应立刻告知老师。C项正确。A项自行清理、B项忽略继续、D项快速离开均违背安全规则。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "To keep everyone safe" 及第⑤段 "Your safety is our first concern."

**【解析】** 全文反复强调安全，文末点明"安全是我们最关心的事"，可推知学校高度重视学生安全。D项正确。A项与"禁止在实验室进食"矛盾；B、C项与规则相反。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解

**【定位】** 标题 "School Science Lab Safety Rules" 及第②段 "all students must follow these rules"

**【解析】** 文章围绕学校科学实验室安全规则展开，列举实验前、中、后的各项要求。A项准确概括主旨。B项如何做实验、C项最佳化学品、D项如何成为教师均非主旨。故选A。
