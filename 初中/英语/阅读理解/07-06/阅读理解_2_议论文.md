# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Video Games: Good or Bad for Teenagers?**

② Video games have become very popular among teenagers. Some people say they are harmful, while others believe they can be useful. Let us look at both sides of the argument.

③ **The benefits of video games:** First, many games help improve hand-eye coordination (协调) and problem-solving skills. Players need to think quickly and make decisions. Second, some games encourage teamwork. When teenagers play with friends, they learn to cooperate and communicate. Third, games can be a way to relax after a long day of study. A short break can help students feel less stressed. Fourth, some educational games can even help students learn languages, history, or science in a fun way.

④ **The disadvantages of video games:** On the other hand, spending too much time on games can be harmful. Some teenagers become addicted (上瘾) and forget to do their homework. They may stay up late and feel tired the next day. Too much screen time can also hurt their eyes. Besides, some games show violence (暴力) which may not be suitable for young people. Another problem is that when teenagers sit and play for hours, they get less exercise, which is bad for their health.

⑤ **A balanced view:** So, are video games good or bad? The answer depends on how we use them. Playing games for one or two hours a day may be fine. But parents and teenagers should set limits together. Choosing games that are educational or require teamwork is a good idea. Remember: games should be a part of life, not the whole of it.

**注释：** coordination 协调；addicted 上瘾；violence 暴力

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ③, which is a benefit of video games?
   A. They make teenagers study longer.
   B. They help improve hand-eye coordination and problem-solving skills.
   C. They help teenagers sleep earlier.
   D. They replace all school subjects.

2. Why can spending too much time on games be harmful?
   A. Teenagers may become addicted, stay up late, and get less exercise.
   B. Games are too expensive.
   C. All games show violence.
   D. Games cannot be played with friends.

3. What does the writer suggest about playing video games?
   A. Teenagers should never play any games.
   B. Parents must play games with teenagers every day.
   C. All games are equally good for teenagers.
   D. Playing for one or two hours a day may be acceptable, and limits should be set.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The writer thinks video games are only bad.
   B. No teenagers enjoy educational games.
   C. The writer believes how we use games matters more than whether we play them.
   D. Violence in games has no effect on young people.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To discuss both the advantages and disadvantages of video games for teenagers and suggest a balanced approach.
   B. To tell readers which video games are the best.
   C. To ask schools to ban all video games.
   D. To advertise a new video game for students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "First, many games help improve hand-eye coordination (协调) and problem-solving skills."

**【解析】** 文中明确指出电子游戏有助于提高手眼协调和解决问题的能力。B项正确。A项学习更久、C项更早睡觉、D项替代所有学科均非文中提及的好处。故选B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some teenagers become addicted (上瘾) and forget to do their homework. They may stay up late and feel tired the next day... when teenagers sit and play for hours, they get less exercise."

**【解析】** 文中列举过度游戏的危害：上瘾、忘记作业、熬夜、眼睛受损、运动减少。A项综合了这些内容。B项游戏贵、C项所有游戏都有暴力、D项不能与朋友玩均与原文不符。故选A。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Playing games for one or two hours a day may be fine. But parents and teenagers should set limits together."

**【解析】** 作者建议每天玩一两个小时可以接受，但家长与孩子应共同设定限制。D项正确。A项完全禁止、B项家长每天陪同、C项所有游戏 equally good 均与作者观点不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The answer depends on how we use them."

**【解析】** 作者指出游戏好坏取决于如何使用，主张适度与选择，可推知作者认为使用方式比是否玩游戏更重要。C项正确。A项完全否定、B项无人喜欢教育游戏、D项暴力无影响均与作者立场矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Video Games: Good or Bad for Teenagers?" 及全文结构（利与弊 + 平衡建议）

**【解析】** 文章围绕电子游戏对青少年的利与弊展开讨论，列举优点与缺点，最后提出平衡使用的建议。A项准确概括主旨。B项推荐最佳游戏、C项要求学校禁止、D项宣传新游戏均非主旨。故选A。
