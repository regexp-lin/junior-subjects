# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Time on a Plane**

① I had always dreamed of flying. When I was ten, I got my chance. My family was going to visit my aunt in Beijing, and for the first time we were taking a plane instead of the train. I was so excited that I could not sleep the night before.

② We arrived at the airport early in the morning. Everything was new to me — the huge building, the security check, the screens showing flight information. I held my boarding pass tightly, afraid of losing it. When we walked through the long corridor to the plane, my heart was beating fast.

③ The plane was much bigger inside than I had imagined. I found my seat by the window and looked out. Workers were loading luggage below. Soon the captain's voice came through the speaker: "Good morning, ladies and gentlemen. Welcome aboard Flight CA1234 to Beijing." The engines started, and the plane moved slowly down the runway.

④ Then came the moment I would never forget. The plane sped up and lifted off the ground. I felt my body pressed into the seat. In seconds, we were in the air! I looked out the window and saw the buildings and roads getting smaller and smaller. Then we flew above the clouds. They looked like a white ocean stretching in every direction. I whispered, "It's so beautiful."

⑤ Two hours later, we landed safely in Beijing. As I walked off the plane, I thought about how amazing it was to fly above the clouds. That first flight opened my eyes to the world and made me want to travel to more places. I told my mum, "One day, I want to fly around the world."

**注释：** boarding pass 登机牌；runway 跑道；corridor 走廊

---

**Questions:**

1. Why was the writer's family going to Beijing?
   A. To go on a school trip.
   B. To visit the writer's aunt.
   C. To attend a meeting.
   D. To see the Great Wall.

2. How did the writer feel before the flight?
   A. Scared and wanted to cancel.
   B. Bored and uninterested.
   C. So excited that he could not sleep.
   D. Angry about the early morning.

3. What did the writer see when the plane took off?
   A. Other planes crashing.
   B. Buildings and roads getting smaller.
   C. Snow falling from the sky.
   D. Birds flying next to the window.

4. What did the clouds look like from above?
   A. Like grey smoke.
   B. Like a dark river.
   C. Like a white ocean stretching in every direction.
   D. Like colourful balloons.

5. What did the experience inspire the writer to do?
   A. Become a pilot.
   B. Never fly again.
   C. Travel to more places and see the world.
   D. Write a book about planes.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "My family was going to visit my aunt in Beijing"

**【解析】** 原文说全家去北京是为了拜访姑姑。A项学校旅行、C项参加会议、D项看长城均非原因。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I was so excited that I could not sleep the night before."

**【解析】** 原文说作者因为太兴奋而前一晚睡不着。A项害怕想取消、B项无聊、D项生气均与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "I looked out the window and saw the buildings and roads getting smaller and smaller."

**【解析】** 作者从窗口看到建筑和道路越来越小。A项飞机坠毁、C项下雪、D项鸟在旁边飞均属于无中生有。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "They looked like a white ocean stretching in every direction."

**【解析】** 原文描述云层像白色的海洋向各个方向延展。A项灰色烟雾、B项黑色河流、D项彩色气球均与原文描述不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "That first flight opened my eyes to the world and made me want to travel to more places. I told my mum, 'One day, I want to fly around the world.'"

**【解析】** 第一次飞行打开了作者的眼界，让他想去更多地方旅行。A项成为飞行员虽相关但原文未明确表达此愿望；B项不再飞与原文相反；D项写书原文未提及。故选C。
