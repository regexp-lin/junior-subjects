# 阅读理解（说明文）

## The History of the Bicycle

① The bicycle is one of the most popular means of transport in the world. But do you know how it began? More than 200 years ago, in 1817, a German man named Karl von Drais invented a wooden machine with two wheels. People had to push it with their feet on the ground because it had no pedals (踏板). It was called the "running machine" or "hobby horse." Although it looked simple, it was the first step toward the modern bicycle.

② In the 1860s, French inventors added pedals to the front wheel. Riders could now move forward by pedaling instead of walking. These early bicycles were heavy and hard to ride. They became popular in Europe, especially among young people who enjoyed the new feeling of speed. However, the front wheel was often very large, which made the bicycle difficult to balance.

③ The design changed greatly in the 1880s. John Kemp Starley, an English inventor, created the "safety bicycle." It had two wheels of the same size and a chain (链条) that connected the pedals to the back wheel. This design was much safer and easier to ride. Soon, more and more people began to use bicycles for travel and work.

④ By the early 1900s, bicycles had become common in many countries. They were cheap, clean, and did not need fuel. In China, the bicycle became a symbol of daily life. Millions of people rode bicycles to work, to school, or to visit friends. Even today, in some cities, you can still see large groups of people cycling together.

⑤ From a simple wooden toy to a worldwide transport tool, the bicycle has come a long way. Today, electric bicycles and shared bikes have made cycling more convenient than ever. The bicycle continues to play an important role in our lives — for exercise, for the environment, and for simple joy.

**注释：** pedal 踏板；chain 链条；fuel 燃料；convenient 方便的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what was the first bicycle-like machine like?
   A. It was made of wood, had two wheels, and people pushed it with their feet on the ground.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "a German man named Karl von Drais invented a wooden machine with two wheels. People had to push it with their feet on the ground because it had no pedals."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明1817年发明的第一辆自行车式机器是木制的、有两个轮子，且人们需用脚在地上推动前进（因为没有踏板）。A项准确概括。B项"有踏板且易骑"与原文矛盾；C项时间地点错误；D项"用电驱动"原文未提及。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "In the 1860s, French inventors added pedals to the front wheel."

**【解析】** 原文清楚指出踏板是在19世纪60年代由法国发明家加上的。B项正确。A项1817年是首辆无踏板车诞生；C项1880年代是安全自行车出现；D项1900年代初是自行车普及时期。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "the 'safety bicycle.' It had two wheels of the same size and a chain (链条) that connected the pedals to the back wheel."

**【解析】** 原文具体描述安全自行车的革新：两轮同尺寸，链条连接踏板与后轮。C项准确复述。A项"前轮更大"是更早期的设计；B项发明地在英国；D项"仅铁制"原文未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "In China, the bicycle became a symbol of daily life. Millions of people rode bicycles to work, to school, or to visit friends."

**【解析】** 原文指出自行车成为中国日常生活象征，数百万人骑车上班、上学、访友，可推知它已成为许多人日常生活的重要部分。D项正确。A项"仅富人使用"与"数百万人"矛盾；B项"从未流行"与事实相反；C项"仅用于运动"不准确。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段起源，第②段踏板发明，第③段安全自行车，第④段普及，第⑤段总结。

**【解析】** 全文按时间顺序叙述自行车从200多年前的简单木制机器发展到当今重要交通工具的历程，A项最能概括主旨。B项过度突出个人；C项只涉及现代部分；D项与原文"世界各地流行"不符。故选A。
