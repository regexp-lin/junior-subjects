# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## The Voice on Stage

① Li Ming had always loved to sing. At home, he would sing when no one was around. But the thought of singing in front of others made his heart beat fast and his hands shake. He had stage fright (怯场). When the school announced a singing competition, his music teacher, Ms. Chen, encouraged him to join. "You have a beautiful voice," she said. "You just need to believe in yourself." Li Ming was not sure, but he decided to try.

② For the next three weeks, Li Ming practiced every day. He stood in front of the mirror and sang as if he were on a real stage. His friends helped him by listening and giving advice. Sometimes he still felt nervous, but he did not give up. Ms. Chen taught him to take deep breaths before singing. "When you feel afraid," she said, "remember that the audience wants you to succeed."

③ The day of the competition finally came. Li Ming waited backstage (后台), his knees trembling. When his name was called, he walked slowly to the center of the stage. The bright lights made it hard to see the audience, but he could hear the silence. For a moment, he wanted to run away.

④ Then he thought of Ms. Chen's words. He took a deep breath, closed his eyes for a second, and began to sing. His voice was clear and steady. As the music filled the hall, his fear slowly faded. When he finished, the audience clapped loudly. Li Ming smiled. He had done it — he had overcome his fear.

⑤ That day, Li Ming did not win first place. But he won something more important: the courage to stand on stage and share his gift. From then on, he knew that with practice and belief, he could face any challenge. Sometimes the bravest thing we can do is to try, even when we are afraid.

**注释：** stage fright 怯场；backstage 后台；tremble 颤抖；steady 平稳的

### 选择题

1. Why did Li Ming feel nervous about singing in public?
   A. He did not like music at all.
   B. He had stage fright — the thought of singing in front of others made his heart beat fast and hands shake.
   C. His teacher forced him to join the competition.
   D. He had never practiced before.

2. According to paragraph ②, what did Ms. Chen teach Li Ming to do when he felt afraid?
   A. To run away from the stage.
   B. To take deep breaths before singing and remember that the audience wants him to succeed.
   C. To sing without practicing.
   D. To avoid looking at the audience.

3. What did Li Ming do when he first stood on the stage?
   A. He ran away immediately.
   B. He forgot all the words of the song.
   C. He took a deep breath, closed his eyes for a second, and began to sing.
   D. He asked the audience to help him.

4. What can we infer from the passage about Li Ming's performance?
   A. He won first place in the competition.
   B. Nobody liked his singing.
   C. His performance was successful — the audience clapped loudly and his fear faded.
   D. He never sang again after that day.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Only people who win first place are successful.
   B. Teachers should never encourage shy students.
   C. Singing competitions are too difficult for middle school students.
   D. With practice and belief, we can overcome fear and face challenges; trying bravely is more important than winning.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "But the thought of singing in front of others made his heart beat fast and his hands shake. He had stage fright."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明李明一想到在他人面前唱歌就心跳加速、双手发抖，他有怯场问题。B项准确概括。A项"不喜欢音乐"与"热爱唱歌"矛盾；C项"老师强迫"与"鼓励"不符；D项"从未练习"与后文矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Ms. Chen taught him to take deep breaths before singing. 'When you feel afraid,' she said, 'remember that the audience wants you to succeed.'"

**【解析】** 原文清楚写出陈老师教李明唱歌前深呼吸，并在害怕时记住观众希望他成功。B项完整概括。A项"逃跑"、C项"不练习就唱"、D项"避免看观众"均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "He took a deep breath, closed his eyes for a second, and began to sing."

**【解析】** 原文描述李明站在舞台上时，深呼吸、短暂闭眼，然后开始唱歌。C项准确复述。A项"立即逃跑"与后文矛盾；B项"忘词"、D项"请观众帮忙"原文未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "His voice was clear and steady... his fear slowly faded. When he finished, the audience clapped loudly. Li Ming smiled. He had done it — he had overcome his fear."

**【解析】** 原文描写李明声音清晰平稳、恐惧消退、观众热烈鼓掌、他成功克服恐惧，可推知演出是成功的。C项正确。A项"获一等奖"与第⑤段"did not win first place"矛盾；B项"没人喜欢"与掌声矛盾；D项"再也没唱过"与"from then on"成长主题矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图/主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "He won something more important: the courage to stand on stage and share his gift... with practice and belief, he could face any challenge. Sometimes the bravest thing we can do is to try, even when we are afraid."

**【解析】** 文章通过李明克服舞台恐惧的故事，传达"练习与信念可助我们克服恐惧、面对挑战；勇敢尝试比获胜更重要"的道理。D项最能概括作者意图。A项与"did not win first place"矛盾；B项、C项与文章主旨相反。故选D。
