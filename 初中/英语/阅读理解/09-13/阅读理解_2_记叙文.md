> **09-13 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last Saturday afternoon, I went to the city park with my parents. The weather was fine, and many families were there. Children were playing on the grass, and some people were flying kites near the lake. I was sitting on a bench, reading a book, when I heard a child crying.

② I looked up and saw a little girl, about four or five years old, standing alone under a big tree. She was crying loudly and calling "Mummy! Mummy!" I walked over and knelt down beside her. "Don't be afraid. What's your name? Where is your mummy?" I asked gently. She was so scared that she could hardly speak. "My name is Lily... I can't find Mummy," she said between sobs.

③ I held her hand and told her we would find her mother together. I asked her what her mother was wearing. "A red dress," she said. We walked around the park, and I kept looking for a woman in a red dress. After about five minutes, I saw a woman running toward us. Her face was pale, and she was shouting "Lily! Lily!"

④ "Mummy!" the little girl cried and rushed into her mother's arms. The woman hugged her daughter tightly and thanked me again and again. "I turned around for just a moment to buy some water, and when I looked back, she was gone. I was so worried! Thank you for helping her." She asked for my name and said she would tell my school about my kindness. I said it was nothing and that I was glad I could help.

⑤ That day, I learned that a small act of kindness can make a big difference. I felt happy and proud. If you ever see someone in need, don't hesitate to offer your help.

**注释：** sob 抽泣；hesitate 犹豫

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did the story happen?
   A. At school.
   B. In a shopping mall.
   C. In the city park.
   D. At home.

2. What was the writer doing when she heard the child crying?
   A. Flying a kite.
   B. Playing on the grass.
   C. Reading a book on a bench.
   D. Walking around the lake.

3. Why was the little girl crying?
   A. Because she fell down.
   B. Because she lost her toy.
   C. Because she could not find her mother.
   D. Because she was hungry.

4. How did the writer help find the mother?
   A. By calling the police.
   B. By asking the girl's name and her mother's clothes, then walking around to look for her.
   C. By asking other people for help.
   D. By staying in one place and waiting.

5. What can we learn from the passage?
   A. Children should never go to the park alone.
   B. A small act of kindness can make a big difference.
   C. Buying water at the park is dangerous.
   D. The writer's school gave her a prize for helping.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last Saturday afternoon, I went to the city park with my parents"

**【解析】** 原文明确说故事发生在城市公园。A项学校、B项商场、D项家中均与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I was sitting on a bench, reading a book, when I heard a child crying"

**【解析】** 原文说作者当时正坐在长椅上看书。A、B、D项均为错误信息。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "I can't find Mummy," she said between sobs

**【解析】** 小女孩说找不到妈妈。A项摔倒、B项丢了玩具、D项饿了均未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I asked her what her mother was wearing. 'A red dress,' she said. We walked around the park, and I kept looking for a woman in a red dress"

**【解析】** 作者询问母亲衣着（红裙子），然后带女孩在公园里走动寻找。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "That day, I learned that a small act of kindness can make a big difference"

**【解析】** 作者从中领悟到小小的善举可以产生很大影响。A项"孩子不该独自去公园"过于绝对；C项曲解原文；D项"学校颁奖"原文未提及。故选B。
