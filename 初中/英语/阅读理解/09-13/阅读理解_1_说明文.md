# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Polar bears are large white bears that live in the Arctic (北极). They are powerful swimmers and spend most of their lives on sea ice. The ice is not just their home — it is where they hunt for seals (海豹), their main food. Without sea ice, polar bears cannot catch enough food and may starve (挨饿).

② In recent years, scientists have noticed a serious problem: the Arctic is getting warmer. This is caused by climate change. When the Earth heats up, the sea ice melts (融化) earlier in spring and forms later in autumn. Polar bears need the ice to last for many months so they can hunt. When the ice period becomes shorter, the bears have less time to find food. Many polar bears are now thinner than before, and some mother bears struggle to feed their cubs (幼崽).

③ Climate change also makes the weather less predictable. Sometimes the ice breaks suddenly, leaving polar bears stranded (困住的) far from land. They may have to swim for days to reach the next piece of ice. Long swimming can make them very tired and weak, especially young cubs, which may not survive such a journey.

④ What can we do to help? We can reduce our use of energy — turn off lights when we leave a room, walk or bike instead of driving when possible, and recycle. We can also tell our family and friends about polar bears and climate change. Every small action counts. If we work together, we can slow down climate change and give polar bears a better chance to survive.

⑤ Polar bears are a symbol of the Arctic. Protecting them means protecting our planet. The choices we make today will affect not only polar bears but also future generations.

**注释：** Arctic 北极；seal 海豹；starve 挨饿；melt 融化；cub 幼崽；stranded 困住的

**Questions:**

1. Where do polar bears mainly live?
   A. In the forest.
   B. In the ocean.
   C. In the Arctic.
   D. In the desert.

2. Why is sea ice important for polar bears?
   A. Because it keeps them cool in summer.
   B. Because it is where they hunt for seals, their main food.
   C. Because they sleep under the ice.
   D. Because they use ice to build nests.

3. According to the passage, what happens when the Arctic gets warmer?
   A. Polar bears grow bigger.
   B. The sea ice melts earlier and forms later, giving bears less time to hunt.
   C. More seals appear in the Arctic.
   D. Polar bears learn to live without ice.

4. What does the writer suggest we do to help polar bears?
   A. Build more zoos for polar bears.
   B. Reduce energy use, recycle, and tell others about climate change.
   C. Move polar bears to warmer places.
   D. Feed polar bears with fish every day.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Polar bears will disappear soon no matter what we do.
   B. Protecting polar bears is connected to protecting our planet and future.
   C. Only scientists can save polar bears.
   D. Polar bears can easily adapt to warm weather.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "Polar bears are large white bears that live in the Arctic"

**【解析】** 原文明确说北极熊生活在北极(Arctic)。A项森林、B项海洋、D项沙漠均与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The ice is not just their home — it is where they hunt for seals, their main food"

**【解析】** 原文说明海冰是北极熊猎捕海豹（其主要食物）的场所。A项"保持凉爽"、C项"在冰下睡觉"、D项"用冰筑巢"均未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When the Earth heats up, the sea ice melts earlier in spring and forms later in autumn... the bears have less time to find food"

**【解析】** 原文说明气候变暖导致冰更早融化、更晚形成，北极熊捕猎时间减少。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "We can reduce our use of energy... walk or bike instead of driving... recycle... tell our family and friends about polar bears and climate change"

**【解析】** 作者建议节约能源、环保出行、回收利用、向他人宣传。A、C、D项原文均未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Protecting them means protecting our planet. The choices we make today will affect not only polar bears but also future generations"

**【解析】** 原文将保护北极熊与保护地球、影响未来联系起来。A项过于悲观；C项"只有科学家能拯救"与作者呼吁公众行动矛盾；D项与全文气候变化威胁的主题矛盾。故选B。
