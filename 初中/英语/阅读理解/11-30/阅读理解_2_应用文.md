# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Green Community Day — Join Us!**

① Hello, neighbours! Our community will hold a "Green Community Day" on December 14, 2025. We invite all residents, especially families with children, to take part. Together we can make our neighbourhood cleaner and greener.

**Event Details:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Saturday, December 14, 2025 |
| Time | 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. |
| Meet at | Community centre gate |
| What to bring | Gloves, reusable water bottle, comfortable shoes |
| Age | All ages (children under 10 must be with an adult) |

② **Activities:**

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00–9:20 | Registration & safety briefing |
| 9:20–10:30 | Tree planting in the north park |
| 10:30–10:45 | Short break |
| 10:45–11:30 | Litter picking around the community |
| 11:30–12:00 | Group photo & small gift for participants |

③ **Tree planting:** We will plant 50 young trees. Volunteers will work in teams of 4–5. Tools will be provided. Please wear long sleeves and trousers to protect your skin.

④ **How to sign up:** Send your name, phone number, and number of people (adults and children) to green@ourcommunity.cn before December 10. Or visit the community office (Room 101) during 9 a.m.–5 p.m. on weekdays. Places are limited to 80 people. First come, first served.

⑤ **Note:** In case of heavy rain, the event will be moved to December 21. We will send a message to all participants one day before. If you have questions, call 020-12345678.

Community Committee  
November 30, 2025

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Green Community Day be held?
   A. December 10, 2025.
   B. December 14, 2025.
   C. December 21, 2025.
   D. November 30, 2025.

2. What should participants bring?
   A. Trees.
   B. Gloves, reusable water bottle, and comfortable shoes.
   C. Food and drinks.
   D. Money only.

3. How long will the tree planting last?
   A. About 20 minutes.
   B. About 70 minutes (9:20–10:30).
   C. About 2 hours.
   D. The whole day.

4. How can people sign up?
   A. By phone only.
   B. By sending email to green@ourcommunity.cn or visiting the community office.
   C. By posting a letter.
   D. Only at the event.

5. What happens if it rains heavily?
   A. The event is cancelled.
   B. The event will be moved to December 21.
   C. Everyone gets a refund.
   D. The event will be held indoors only.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Date" 一行 "Saturday, December 14, 2025"

**【解析】** 活动日期为2025年12月14日。A项12月10日是报名截止日；C项12月21日是雨天备选日期；D项11月30日是通知发布日期。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "What to bring" 一行 "Gloves, reusable water bottle, comfortable shoes"

**【解析】** 需自备手套、可重复使用的水瓶和舒适的鞋子。A项树苗由活动提供；C项食品饮料、D项仅金钱原文未要求。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "9:20–10:30" 对应 "Tree planting in the north park"

**【解析】** 植树时间为9:20至10:30，约70分钟。A项20分钟是签到与安全说明时间；C项2小时、D项整天均不准确。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Send your name, phone number, and number of people to green@ourcommunity.cn before December 10. Or visit the community office (Room 101)"

**【解析】** 可通过邮件或到社区办公室报名。A项仅电话、C项寄信、D项仅现场报名均不完整。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In case of heavy rain, the event will be moved to December 21."

**【解析】** 遇大雨活动将改期至12月21日。A项取消、C项退款、D项仅室内举行均与原文不符。故选B。
