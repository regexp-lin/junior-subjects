> **11-30 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Why Teenagers Should Do Housework**

① Some teenagers think that doing housework is boring or that it is only for adults. They prefer to spend time on phones, games, or homework. But research shows that teenagers who help with housework do better in many ways. Let me explain why.

② First, housework teaches important life skills. When you leave home for university or work one day, you will need to cook, clean, and wash clothes. If you never learn these skills at home, you will struggle later. Washing dishes, folding clothes, and cooking simple meals are not difficult. They become easy once you practise. Learning them early saves you a lot of trouble in the future.

③ Second, doing housework helps the whole family. When everyone shares the work, no one feels overworked. Your parents may be tired after a long day. If you tidy your room, set the table, or take out the rubbish, you are showing that you care. Small actions make a big difference. Your family will appreciate it.

④ Third, housework can improve your mood and health. Studies show that people who keep their space clean tend to feel less stressed. A tidy room helps you focus better when you study. Doing physical tasks like sweeping or washing also gives you light exercise. You do not need to go to the gym — housework can keep you active.

⑤ Of course, housework should not take all your time. School work comes first. But spending 15 to 30 minutes a day helping at home is reasonable. Start with small tasks: make your bed, put your clothes away, help with dinner. As you grow older, you can take on more. The habits you build now will stay with you for life.

**注释：** fold 折叠；overwork 过度劳累；sweep 扫地

---

**Questions:**

1. What does research show about teenagers who help with housework?
   A. They watch more TV.
   B. They do better in many ways.
   C. They have fewer friends.
   D. They sleep less.

2. Why are life skills important?
   A. They help you play games better.
   B. You will need them when you leave home for university or work.
   C. They make you famous.
   D. They help you buy phones.

3. How does housework help the family?
   A. It makes everyone angry.
   B. When everyone shares the work, no one feels overworked.
   C. It takes away free time.
   D. It causes arguments.

4. What benefit does a tidy room bring?
   A. You can have more parties.
   B. It helps you focus better when you study.
   C. You need fewer books.
   D. Your friends will visit more.

5. How much time does the writer suggest spending on housework each day?
   A. 1 hour.
   B. 2 hours.
   C. 15 to 30 minutes.
   D. 5 minutes only.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "research shows that teenagers who help with housework do better in many ways"

**【解析】** 研究表明参与家务的青少年在许多方面表现更好。A项看更多电视、C项朋友更少、D项睡得更少均与"do better"不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When you leave home for university or work one day, you will need to cook, clean, and wash clothes."

**【解析】** 离开家上大学或工作时需要这些生活技能。A项玩游戏、C项出名、D项买手机均非技能的重要性。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "When everyone shares the work, no one feels overworked."

**【解析】** 大家分担家务，没人会感觉过度劳累。A项让人生气、C项剥夺空闲时间、D项引发争吵均与原文含义相反。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "A tidy room helps you focus better when you study."

**【解析】** 整洁的房间有助于学习时更专注。A项办更多派对、C项需要更少书、D项朋友更多来访均非原文所述。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "spending 15 to 30 minutes a day helping at home is reasonable"

**【解析】** 作者建议每天花15到30分钟帮忙做家务。A项1小时、B项2小时过多；D项仅5分钟过少。故选C。
