# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Many parents and teachers are worried about teenagers spending too much time in front of screens — phones, computers, tablets and TVs. Should young people reduce their screen time? This question has led to a lot of discussion.

② **Those who support reducing screen time** believe it brings several benefits. First, too much screen use can harm eyesight. Second, sitting for a long time may lead to health problems such as back pain or being overweight. Third, spending hours on social media (社交媒体) can make some teenagers feel unhappy or anxious when they compare themselves with others. Last, cutting down on screen time leaves more time for outdoor activities, exercise, reading and face-to-face communication with family and friends.

③ **On the other hand, some people argue that** screen time is not always bad. The Internet and digital devices can help students learn. They can search for information, watch educational videos and do homework online. Many teenagers also use screens to stay in touch with friends, especially when they cannot meet in person. In addition, some apps and games can improve thinking skills. The key, they say, is not to avoid screens completely, but to use them wisely — for example, setting a time limit each day.

④ **In my view**, both sides have reasonable points. Screen time itself is neither good nor bad. What matters is how we use it. We should balance study, rest and entertainment. It is a good idea to take regular breaks when using screens, avoid using them before bed, and make sure we have enough time for exercise and real-world activities. Parents and schools can help by teaching young people healthy habits.

⑤ In the end, the goal is not to get rid of screens, but to use them in a way that supports our health and well-being.

**注释：** tablet 平板电脑；anxious 焦虑的；limit 限制；balance 平衡；well-being 身心健康

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, which is a benefit of reducing screen time?
   A. Teenagers can use social media more often.
   B. More time for outdoor activities, exercise and face-to-face communication.
   C. It helps teenagers compare themselves with others online.
   D. It makes students spend more money on phones.

2. Why do some people think screen time is not always bad?
   A. Because all teenagers feel happy when using screens.
   B. Because screens can help with learning, staying in touch with friends, and some apps can improve thinking skills.
   C. Because parents want teenagers to use screens 24 hours a day.
   D. Because screens are cheaper than books.

3. What health problem might too much screen use cause, according to the passage?
   A. Hearing loss.
   B. Harm to eyesight or back pain from sitting too long.
   C. Catching a cold easily.
   D. Losing the ability to speak.

4. What is the writer's opinion about screen time?
   A. Teenagers should never use any screens.
   B. Screen time is bad and must be stopped.
   C. Screen time is neither good nor bad by itself; what matters is how we use it, and we should balance our life.
   D. All screen use is helpful for everyone.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer wants teenagers to throw away all their devices.
   B. The writer believes the goal is to use screens in a healthy way that supports well-being, not to remove them completely.
   C. The writer thinks screens have no use at all.
   D. The writer suggests schools should ban all screens.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "cutting down on screen time leaves more time for outdoor activities, exercise, reading and face-to-face communication with family and friends."

**【解析】** 减少屏幕时间的好处之一是有更多时间进行户外活动、锻炼、阅读及与家人朋友的面对面交流。A项"更多使用社媒"与减少屏幕时间相反；B项正确；C项"帮助与他人比较"属负面影响；D项"花更多钱买手机"未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The Internet and digital devices can help students learn... stay in touch with friends... some apps and games can improve thinking skills."

**【解析】** 支持方认为屏幕可辅助学习、保持联系、部分应用可提升思维。B项准确概括。A项"所有青少年都开心"过于绝对；C项"24小时使用"与"use them wisely"矛盾；D项"比书便宜"未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "too much screen use can harm eyesight" 及 "sitting for a long time may lead to health problems such as back pain or being overweight."

**【解析】** 原文指出过度使用屏幕可损害视力，久坐可能导致背痛或肥胖等健康问题。A项"听力损失"、C项"易感冒"、D项"丧失言语能力"均未提及；B项正确。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第④段 "In my view, both sides have reasonable points. Screen time itself is neither good nor bad. What matters is how we use it. We should balance study, rest and entertainment."

**【解析】** 作者认为双方都有道理，屏幕时间本身无所谓好坏，关键是如何使用，并应平衡学习、休息与娱乐。C项正确。A、B项"永不使用"/"必须停止"与"not to get rid of screens"矛盾；D项"全部有益"过于绝对。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "the goal is not to get rid of screens, but to use them in a way that supports our health and well-being."

**【解析】** 目标是并非摒弃屏幕，而是以有助于身心健康的方式使用。可推知应健康合理使用，而非完全禁止。B项正确。A项"扔掉设备"、C项"毫无用处"、D项"学校禁屏"均与作者立场相反。故选B。
