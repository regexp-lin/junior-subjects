> **05-29 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Notice: New Rules for the School Library**

② Dear students, our school library has made some changes. From next Monday, the following new rules will take effect. Please read carefully and follow them.

③ **Opening hours:** The library will open from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekdays. It will be closed on weekends for cleaning and book organization. During exam weeks, we may extend the hours. We will put up a notice if there are any changes.

④ **Borrowing rules:** Each student can borrow up to three books at a time. You may keep them for two weeks. If you need them longer, you can renew (续借) them once at the library desk or online. Please return books on time. There will be a fine of 1 yuan per day for each late book. No food or drinks are allowed in the reading room. Keep quiet and put your phone on silent mode.

⑤ For any questions, please visit the library office or send an email to library@school.edu.cn. We hope these rules will help everyone enjoy a better reading environment.

**注释：** renew 续借；fine 罚款

---

### 选择题

1. When will the new library rules start?
   A. This Monday.
   B. Next Monday.
   C. After the exam week.
   D. On weekends only.

2. How many books can each student borrow at a time?
   A. One book.
   B. Two books.
   C. Three books.
   D. As many as they want.

3. What will happen if a student returns a book late?
   A. They cannot borrow books again.
   B. They have to pay 1 yuan per day for each late book.
   C. They must renew the book online.
   D. The library will be closed to them.

4. What can we infer from the passage about the library?
   A. The library is open 24 hours a day.
   B. Students can eat snacks while reading.
   C. The library cares about a clean and quiet environment.
   D. No one can renew books.

5. How can students ask questions about the new rules?
   A. By calling the library on the phone.
   B. By visiting the library office or sending an email to library@school.edu.cn.
   C. By writing a letter to the principal.
   D. By asking teachers in class only.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "From next Monday, the following new rules will take effect."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出新规定将于"下周一"(next Monday)生效。A项"本周一"时间错误；B项正确；C项"考试周后"文中未提及具体生效日；D项"仅周末"与"周末闭馆"矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Each student can borrow up to three books at a time."

**【解析】** 每位学生每次最多可借三本书。A、B项数量错误；C项正确；D项"想借多少借多少"与"up to three"矛盾。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "There will be a fine of 1 yuan per day for each late book."

**【解析】** 逾期还书每本书每天罚款1元。A项"不能再借"、C项"必须在线续借"、D项"图书馆对其关闭"均未在文中提及；B项正确。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "No food or drinks are allowed" 及 "Keep quiet"；第⑤段 "enjoy a better reading environment"。

**【解析】** 禁止食物饮料、保持安静、希望提供更好的阅读环境，可推知图书馆注重清洁安静的氛围。A项"24小时开放"与"8:00 AM to 6:00 PM"矛盾；B项"可吃零食"与"No food"矛盾；C项正确；D项"无人能续借"与"renew them once"矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "please visit the library office or send an email to library@school.edu.cn."

**【解析】** 咨询方式为前往图书馆办公室或发送邮件至library@school.edu.cn。A项"打电话"、C项"写信给校长"、D项"仅在课堂上问老师"均非原文提供的方式；B项正确。故选B。
