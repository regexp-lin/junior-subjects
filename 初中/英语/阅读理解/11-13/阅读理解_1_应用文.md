> **11-13 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Campus Mental Health Week — Event Schedule**

① Dear students,

② Our school will hold a Mental Health Week from December 9th to December 15th, 2024. The aim is to help us understand and care for our mental health. Here is the schedule:

**Schedule:**

| Date       | Event               | Time      | Place        |
|------------|---------------------|-----------|--------------|
| Dec 9 (Mon)| Opening Talk        | 4:00–4:40 p.m. | School Hall |
| Dec 10 (Tue)| Stress Workshop    | 4:00–4:45 p.m. | Room 301   |
| Dec 11 (Wed)| Mindful Drawing    | 4:00–4:40 p.m. | Art Room  |
| Dec 12 (Thu)| Peer Support Talk  | 4:00–4:45 p.m. | Room 201 |
| Dec 13 (Fri)| Relaxation Day     | 3:30–4:30 p.m. | Playground |
| Dec 15 (Sun)| Closing & Sharing  | 9:00–10:00 a.m. | School Hall |

③ **Opening Talk:** A psychologist will speak about what mental health means and why it matters. **Stress Workshop:** Learn simple ways to reduce stress and manage your time. **Mindful Drawing:** Use art to relax and express your feelings. **Peer Support Talk:** Students share their stories. You are not alone. **Relaxation Day:** Outdoor games, music, and quiet time. **Closing & Sharing:** Wrap up the week; anyone can share their thoughts.

④ All events are free. No need to sign up. Just come and join. If you feel sad or worried, you can also visit the school counselling room any weekday from 2:00 to 5:00 p.m. Our counsellors are there to help.

⑤ Let us care for our minds as we care for our bodies. We look forward to seeing you!

School Counselling Centre  
December 1st, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the Mental Health Week take place?
   A. December 1st to 7th.
   B. December 9th to 15th.
   C. December 15th only.
   D. Every week in December.

2. What happens on December 11th?
   A. Opening Talk.
   B. Stress Workshop.
   C. Mindful Drawing.
   D. Peer Support Talk.

3. Where is the Stress Workshop held?
   A. School Hall.
   B. Room 301.
   C. Art Room.
   D. Playground.

4. What can students do if they feel sad or worried?
   A. Stay at home.
   B. Visit the school counselling room on weekdays from 2:00 to 5:00 p.m.
   C. Only go to the week's events.
   D. Ignore their feelings.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Students must pay to join.
   B. The school cares about students' mental health and wants to offer support.
   C. Only teachers can attend.
   D. The counselling room is closed all week.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold a Mental Health Week from December 9th to December 15th"
**【解析】** 心理健康周为12月9日至15日。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Dec 11 (Wed) 对应 Mindful Drawing
**【解析】** 12月11日为"正念绘画"活动。C项正确。A、B、D项对应其他日期。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Stress Workshop 的 Place 为 Room 301
**【解析】** 压力工作坊在301室举行。B项正确。A、C、D项与表格不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "visit the school counselling room any weekday from 2:00 to 5:00 p.m."
**【解析】** 感到难过或担忧时可于工作日下午2:00–5:00前往心理咨询室。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "help us understand and care for our mental health"；第④段 "Our counsellors are there to help"
**【解析】** 学校关注学生心理健康，并希望提供支持。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。
