> **09-07 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last Saturday was my mother's birthday. I wanted to do something special for her and my father. They work very hard every day and often cook meals for our family. I decided to cook dinner for them for the first time.

② I chose to make tomato and egg noodles because it seemed easier than other dishes. I looked at a recipe online and wrote down the steps. First, I washed the tomatoes and cut them into small pieces. Then I beat two eggs in a bowl. I turned on the stove and heated some oil in a pan. When the oil was hot, I put in the eggs and stirred them. After that, I added the tomatoes and a little salt and sugar. Finally, I boiled the noodles in another pot and mixed everything together.

③ To be honest, I made some mistakes. I added too much salt at first and had to add more water. One tomato fell on the floor and I had to wash it again. But I did not give up. I kept trying until the dish was ready.

④ When my parents came home, they were very surprised to see the food on the table. My mother's eyes became red. She said, "This is the best birthday gift I have ever received." My father tried the noodles and said they tasted great, though I knew they were not perfect. We sat together and had a warm family dinner.

⑤ That day I learned that cooking for the people you love is a special way to show you care. It does not matter if the food is perfect. What matters is the love behind it. I will try to cook for my family more often from now on.

**注释：** recipe 食谱；stir 搅拌

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer decide to cook for his/her parents?
   A. Because he/she had nothing else to do.
   B. Because it was his/her mother's birthday and he/she wanted to do something special.
   C. Because his/her parents asked him/her to learn cooking.
   D. Because the family had no food at home.

2. What dish did the writer make?
   A. Rice and vegetables.
   B. Tomato and egg noodles.
   C. Fried chicken.
   D. Soup with meat.

3. What happened while the writer was cooking?
   A. The stove broke and the writer had to stop.
   B. The writer added too much salt and a tomato fell on the floor.
   C. The writer burned his/her hand badly.
   D. The parents came home too early.

4. How did the writer's mother feel when she saw the dinner?
   A. She was angry because the food did not look good.
   B. She was surprised and touched.
   C. She was worried about the writer's safety.
   D. She was tired and did not want to eat.

5. What does the writer want to tell us?
   A. Cooking is very difficult and should be left to parents.
   B. Cooking for loved ones is a way to show care; perfection is not the most important.
   C. Everyone should learn to cook from the internet.
   D. Birthday gifts must be expensive.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last Saturday was my mother's birthday. I wanted to do something special for her and my father. They work very hard every day... I decided to cook dinner for them for the first time."

**【解析】** 原文说明因母亲生日，作者想为父母做点特别的事，于是决定第一次为他们做饭。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "I chose to make tomato and egg noodles because it seemed easier than other dishes."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明作者做了西红柿鸡蛋面。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I added too much salt at first and had to add more water. One tomato fell on the floor and I had to wash it again."

**【解析】** 文中提到加了太多盐以及一个番茄掉在地上。B项正确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "When my parents came home, they were very surprised to see the food on the table. My mother's eyes became red. She said, 'This is the best birthday gift I have ever received.'"

**【解析】** 父母看到饭菜很惊讶，母亲眼眶发红并称这是收到的最好的生日礼物，可推知母亲既惊讶又感动。A、C、D项与文中情感描写不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "That day I learned that cooking for the people you love is a special way to show you care. It does not matter if the food is perfect. What matters is the love behind it."

**【解析】** 文末点明主旨：为所爱的人做饭是表达关心的方式，食物是否完美不重要，重要的是背后的爱。B项准确概括。A、C、D项均偏离主旨。故选B。
