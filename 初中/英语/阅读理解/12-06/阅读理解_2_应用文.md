# 阅读理解（应用文）

**City Science Museum — Visitor Guide**

① Welcome to the City Science Museum! To help you have a safe and enjoyable visit, please follow the rules below.

**Opening Hours & Tickets:**

| Day | Opening Time | Last Entry |
|-----|--------------|------------|
| Tuesday–Sunday | 9:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m. | 4:00 p.m. |
| Monday | Closed | — |

- **Ticket prices:** Adults 30 yuan; Students (with ID) 15 yuan; Children under 1.2 m: free
- **Group visits:** Book at least 3 days ahead. Call 020-87654321.

② **Inside the museum:**
- Do not touch the exhibits (展品) unless there is a "Please Touch" sign.
- Do not use flash when taking photos. Flash may damage some items.
- Keep your voice down. Do not run or shout.
- Eat and drink only in the café on the first floor.

③ **Exhibition floors:**
- Floor 1: Nature & Animals
- Floor 2: Technology & Invention
- Floor 3: Space & Earth
- Floor 4: Hands-on experiments (experiments 动手实践)

④ **Special events:**
- Weekend science shows: 10:30 a.m. and 2:30 p.m. (free with ticket)
- Holiday workshops: Check our website or ask at the information desk.

⑤ Lost something? Visit the Lost and Found desk near the main entrance. Need help? Look for staff in blue uniforms. Have a great visit!

**注释：** exhibit 展品

---

**Questions:**

1. When is the museum closed?
   A. On Tuesday.
   B. On Sunday.
   C. On Monday.
   D. On Saturday.

2. How much does a student with ID pay for a ticket?
   A. 30 yuan.
   B. 15 yuan.
   C. Free.
   D. 20 yuan.

3. When can visitors touch the exhibits?
   A. Anytime they want.
   B. Only when there is a "Please Touch" sign.
   C. Never.
   D. Only on the first floor.

4. Where can visitors eat and drink?
   A. Anywhere in the museum.
   B. Only at the entrance.
   C. Only in the café on the first floor.
   D. On Floor 3.

5. What should visitors do if they lose something?
   A. Call the police.
   B. Go to the Lost and Found desk near the main entrance.
   C. Ask staff on Floor 4.
   D. Leave the museum at once.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Monday | Closed"

**【解析】** 博物馆周一闭馆。A项周二、B项周日、D项周六均为开放日。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Students (with ID) 15 yuan"

**【解析】** 持学生证票价15元。A项30元为成人价；C项免费为1.2米以下儿童；D项20元文中未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Do not touch the exhibits unless there is a 'Please Touch' sign."

**【解析】** 除非有"请触摸"标识，否则不得触摸展品。A项随时可摸、C项绝不可摸、D项仅一楼可摸均不准确。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Eat and drink only in the café on the first floor."

**【解析】** 饮食仅限一楼咖啡厅。A项 anywhere 与 only 矛盾；B、D项均错误。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Lost something? Visit the Lost and Found desk near the main entrance."

**【解析】** 遗失物品应到主入口附近的失物招领处。A项报警、C项问4楼工作人员、D项立即离开均不合适。故选B。
