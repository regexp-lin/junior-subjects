# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Summer Care Class — Course Schedule**

② Our school will offer a summer care program from July 15 to August 9. Parents can sign up (报名) for their children before July 10. The program runs from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. every weekday. Here is the weekly course schedule.

③ **Morning (8:30–11:30):** Monday and Wednesday — Reading and English practice. Tuesday and Thursday — Math and science. Friday — Art and music. All morning classes focus on learning and creativity.

④ **Afternoon (2:00–5:00):** Monday, Wednesday, Friday — Sports and outdoor activities. Tuesday and Thursday — Homework help and study skills.

⑤ Lunch break is from 11:30 to 2:00. Students can bring their own lunch or buy food at the school canteen. Each class has no more than 25 students. Our teachers will help with both study and safety during the program. If you have any questions, please call the school office at 8765-4321.

**注释：** sign up 报名；outdoor 户外的；canteen 食堂

---

### 选择题

1. By when should parents sign up for the program?
   A. Before July 10.
   B. Before July 15.
   C. Before August 9.
   D. Before August 15.

2. What subjects do students have on Tuesday morning?
   A. Reading and English.
   B. Art and music.
   C. Math and science.
   D. Sports.

3. How long is the lunch break?
   A. 1 hour.
   B. 1.5 hours.
   C. 2 hours.
   D. 2.5 hours.

4. Which of the following is TRUE according to the passage?
   A. The program runs on weekends.
   B. Students must bring their own lunch.
   C. Each class has no more than 25 students.
   D. Parents can sign up after July 15.

5. What is this passage mainly about?
   A. A school sports meeting.
   B. A summer care class course schedule.
   C. A new homework plan.
   D. How to cook lunch at school.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Parents can sign up (报名) for their children before July 10."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明家长须在7月10日前为子女报名。A项正确。B项7月15日是开班日、C项8月9日是结束日、D项未提及。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Tuesday and Thursday — Math and science."

**【解析】** 周二和周四上午安排数学和科学课程。C项正确。A项为周一、周三、B项为周五、D项为下午活动。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Lunch break is from 11:30 to 2:00."

**【解析】** 午餐休息从11:30至14:00，时长为2.5小时。D项正确。A、B、C项时长均与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Each class has no more than 25 students."

**【解析】** 文中写明每班不超过25人。C项正确。A项错误，项目在工作日运行，不含周末；B项错误，学生可自带午餐或在食堂购买；D项错误，报名须在7月10日前完成。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解

**【定位】** 标题 "School Summer Care Class — Course Schedule" 及第②段 "Here is the weekly course schedule."

**【解析】** 文章标题和内容均为介绍学校暑期托管班的课程安排。B项准确概括主旨。A项运动会、C项作业计划、D项在校烹饪均非本文内容。故选B。
