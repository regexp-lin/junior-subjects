# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should Students Be Encouraged to Write Diaries?**

② Writing a diary (日记) is a habit that many people had when they were young. But today, fewer students keep a diary. Some teachers and parents wonder whether we should encourage students to write diaries. Let us think about the advantages and disadvantages.

③ **Reasons for encouraging diary writing:** First, writing a diary helps students practice their English or Chinese. The more they write, the better their writing skills become. Second, a diary is a private place where students can express their feelings and thoughts. They can write about happy things, sad things, or things that worry them. This can help them feel better and understand themselves. Third, a diary is a good way to record daily life. When students look back at their diaries years later, they can remember what happened and how they grew up.

④ **Reasons against diary writing:** Some people say that students already have too much homework. Adding diary writing may make them feel tired and stressed. Others worry that if teachers or parents check the diary, it is no longer private. Students may not write honestly. Besides, not every student likes writing. Forcing them to write may make them dislike writing even more.

⑤ **My view:** I think we should encourage students to write diaries, but we should not force them. Schools can offer diary-writing clubs or activities for those who are interested. Parents and teachers should never read a student's diary without permission. A diary should be a safe and free space for students. When writing becomes a choice rather than a task, more students may discover the joy of keeping a diary.

**注释：** diary 日记；express 表达；permission 允许；task 任务

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ③, which is NOT a benefit of writing a diary?
   A. It helps students practice writing skills.
   B. It helps students express feelings.
   C. It helps students remember daily life.
   D. It helps students finish homework faster.

2. Why do some people disagree with encouraging diary writing?
   A. They think diaries are too expensive.
   B. They worry students have too much homework and diary writing may add stress.
   C. They believe diaries are only for adults.
   D. They think schools should close down.

3. What does the writer suggest about reading students' diaries?
   A. Teachers must read all diaries every week.
   B. Parents should check diaries to see if students write correctly.
   C. Parents and teachers should never read a student's diary without permission.
   D. Students must show their diaries to teachers every day.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Every student already keeps a diary.
   B. Diary writing should be forced in all schools.
   C. Schools have stopped teaching writing.
   D. A diary can help students in many different ways.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To discuss whether students should be encouraged to write diaries and give the writer's opinion.
   B. To teach readers how to write a perfect diary.
   C. To complain about too much homework.
   D. To introduce the best diary brands for students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "First, writing a diary helps students practice their English or Chinese... Second, a diary is a private place where students can express their feelings and thoughts... Third, a diary is a good way to record daily life."

**【解析】** 文中列出三大好处：练习写作、表达情感、记录生活。D项"帮助更快完成作业"未在支持写日记的理由中出现。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some people say that students already have too much homework. Adding diary writing may make them feel tired and stressed."

**【解析】** 反对者认为学生作业已太多，增加日记写作会让他们感到疲倦和压力。B项正确。A项日记贵、C项仅限成人、D项关闭学校均未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Parents and teachers should never read a student's diary without permission."

**【解析】** 作者明确指出家长和老师未经允许不得翻阅学生的日记。C项正确。A、B、D项均与作者观点相反。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段列举写日记的多种好处：练习写作、表达情感、记录生活。

**【解析】** 文中从写作能力、情感表达、生活记录等多方面说明日记的好处，可推知日记能以多种方式帮助学生。D项正确。A项人人已写日记、B项应强制写日记、C项学校停教写作均与原文矛盾。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Should Students Be Encouraged to Write Diaries?" 及全文结构（正反观点 + 作者结论）

**【解析】** 文章围绕"是否应鼓励学生写日记"展开讨论，列举赞成与反对理由并给出作者观点。A项准确概括主旨。B项教写完美日记、C项抱怨作业多、D项推荐日记品牌均非主旨。故选A。
