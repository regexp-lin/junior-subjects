# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Notice: School Safety Drill (Fire & Earthquake)**

① To improve students' safety skills, our school will hold an emergency drill on September 25th, 2024. All students and teachers must take part. Please read the following information carefully.

**Time & Date:** Wednesday, September 25th, 2024  
- 9:00 a.m. — First bell (alarm sounds)  
- 9:01 a.m. — Students leave classroom in order  
- 9:05 a.m. — All gather on the playground  
- 9:15 a.m. — Drill ends, return to class

**What to Do:**

② **When you hear the alarm:**
- Stay calm. Do not run or push.
- Follow your teacher's instructions.
- Leave your things behind. Take only yourself.
- Line up quickly and walk to the nearest exit.
- Go down the stairs; do not use the lift.

③ **On the playground:**
- Stand with your class.
- Your teacher will check the number of students.
- Wait until you are told to go back.

④ **Important:** This is a practice. There is no real danger. But please take it seriously. In a real emergency, these steps can save lives.

⑤ If you have any questions, ask your class teacher before September 24th.

School Safety Office  
September 18th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the safety drill take place?
   A. On September 18th, 2024.
   B. On September 24th, 2024.
   C. On September 25th, 2024.
   D. On September 26th, 2024.

2. What should students do when they hear the alarm?
   A. Run as fast as they can to the playground.
   B. Stay calm, follow the teacher, and leave in order.
   C. Take all their books and bags with them.
   D. Use the lift to go down quickly.

3. Where should students gather during the drill?
   A. In the classroom.
   B. In the library.
   C. On the playground.
   D. At the school gate.

4. How long does the drill last from the first bell to the end?
   A. About 5 minutes.
   B. About 10 minutes.
   C. About 15 minutes.
   D. About 20 minutes.

5. Why does the school hold this drill?
   A. To give students a break from class.
   B. To improve students' safety skills for real emergencies.
   C. To test the school building.
   D. To clean the playground.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "our school will hold an emergency drill on September 25th, 2024" 及 Time & Date 部分

**【解析】** 原文明确说明演练于2024年9月25日举行。A项9月18日是通知发布日期；B项9月24日是提问截止日；D项原文未提及。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When you hear the alarm: Stay calm. Do not run or push. Follow your teacher's instructions... Line up quickly and walk to the nearest exit."

**【解析】** 原文要求：保持冷静、听从指挥、有序撤离。B项准确。A项"尽快跑"与"do not run"矛盾；C项"带走所有物品"与"Leave your things behind"矛盾；D项"使用电梯"与"do not use the lift"矛盾。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** Time & Date 部分 "All gather on the playground" 及第③段 "On the playground"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明全体师生在操场集合。A、B、D项均为错误地点。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** Time & Date 部分 "9:00 a.m. — First bell" 与 "9:15 a.m. — Drill ends"

**【解析】** 从9:00第一次响铃到9:15演练结束，共15分钟。A、B、D项时间计算错误。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第①段 "To improve students' safety skills" 及第④段 "In a real emergency, these steps can save lives."

**【解析】** 通知开篇说明目的为提高学生安全技能，文末强调在真实紧急情况下这些步骤能救命，可推知B项正确。A、C、D项均偏离通知目的。故选B。
