# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should students be fans of celebrities? Many middle school students admire singers, actors, or sports stars. They buy their photos, watch their shows, and follow their news online. But is it good for students to "chase" stars like this? People have different opinions.

② Some people think it is fine. They say that having a role model can help young people work harder. For example, if a student admires a basketball player, he or she might practice more and become healthier. Stars often share positive messages about never giving up and chasing dreams. Besides, being a fan can be fun. It gives students something to talk about with friends and helps them relax after study.

③ However, others worry about the problems. First, chasing stars can take too much time and money. Some students spend hours every day watching videos of their idols. They buy expensive concert tickets or gifts, which their parents may not be able to afford. Second, students might care more about stars than about their studies. Grades may drop. Third, not all celebrities are good examples. Some make mistakes or behave badly. Young fans might copy the wrong things.

④ In my view, it is okay to like a star, but we need to keep a balance. Admire their hard work and talent, not just their looks or fame. Do not let chasing stars affect your study or your family. Use your pocket money wisely. Remember, the best idol is often yourself when you try your best to become a better person.

⑤ So, should students chase stars? The answer is: you can, but do it in a smart and healthy way.

**注释：** celebrity 名人；idol 偶像

**Questions:**

1. What is the passage mainly about?
   A. How to become a famous star.
   B. Whether students should be fans of celebrities.
   C. The best singers and actors for students.
   D. How to make money to buy concert tickets.

2. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people think it is okay to chase stars?
   A. Because it is free and easy.
   B. Because role models can help young people work harder and it can be fun.
   C. Because all stars are perfect.
   D. Because parents want their children to chase stars.

3. What is one problem with chasing stars mentioned in Paragraph ③?
   A. Stars do not like young fans.
   B. It can take too much time and money, and may affect study.
   C. There are no good stars to follow.
   D. Schools ban students from liking stars.

4. What does the writer suggest in Paragraph ④?
   A. Students should never like any star.
   B. Students should spend all their money on stars.
   C. Students should keep a balance and admire stars in a smart way.
   D. Students should only admire stars' looks.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer believes students must stop chasing stars.
   B. The writer thinks chasing stars can be acceptable if done wisely.
   C. The writer does not care about this topic.
   D. The writer thinks all students chase stars in a wrong way.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "Should students be fans of celebrities?... But is it good for students to 'chase' stars like this? People have different opinions."

**【解析】** 文章围绕"学生是否应该追星"展开，讨论正反观点及作者看法。A、C、D项均偏离主题。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "They say that having a role model can help young people work harder... Stars often share positive messages... Besides, being a fan can be fun."

**【解析】** 支持方认为偶像能激励年轻人努力、传递正能量，且追星本身有乐趣。B项概括准确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "First, chasing stars can take too much time and money... Second, students might care more about stars than about their studies. Grades may drop."

**【解析】** 担忧包括：耗费过多时间和金钱、影响学业导致成绩下降。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "In my view, it is okay to like a star, but we need to keep a balance. Admire their hard work and talent... Do not let chasing stars affect your study or your family."

**【解析】** 作者建议保持平衡、理性欣赏明星的才华与努力，不让追星影响学习和家庭。C项准确。A、B、D项与作者观点相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The answer is: you can, but do it in a smart and healthy way."

**【解析】** 结尾表明追星可以，但应以聪明、健康的方式进行，可推知作者认为理性追星是可接受的。A项"必须停止"与文意相反；C、D项无依据。故选B。
