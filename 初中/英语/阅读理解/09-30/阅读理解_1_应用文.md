> **09-30 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **National Day Holiday Notice — School Arrangements**

② Dear students and parents,

③ In accordance with (根据) the National Day holiday arrangements, our school will be closed from October 1 to October 7. Classes will resume (恢复) on October 8 (Tuesday). Please note the following:

④ **Holiday Dates:**
- Start: October 1 (Wednesday)
- End: October 7 (Tuesday)
- Back to school: October 8 (Tuesday) — please check the timetable for any changes

⑤ **Reminders for Students:**
- Complete your homework before the holiday ends. Teachers will collect it on October 8.
- Keep a regular schedule: do not stay up too late or sleep until noon.
- Stay safe: do not go to dangerous places (rivers, construction sites, etc.). Do not play with fire.
- Help with housework when you can. Spend quality time with your family.
- If you travel, take care of your belongings and follow local rules. Wear a mask in crowded places if needed.

⑥ **For Parents:**
- Please make sure your child returns to school on time on October 8.
- If your child cannot return due to illness or other reasons, please inform the class teacher before October 7.
- We wish you and your family a happy and safe National Day!

⑦ School Office  
September 28, 2024

**注释：** in accordance with 根据；resume 恢复

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the school reopen after the National Day holiday?
   A. October 1.
   B. October 7.
   C. October 8.
   D. October 9.

2. How long is the National Day holiday for the school?
   A. 5 days.
   B. 7 days (October 1 to October 7).
   C. 10 days.
   D. 3 days.

3. What should students do about homework?
   A. Forget about it during the holiday.
   B. Complete it before the holiday ends; teachers will collect it on October 8.
   C. Hand it in after the holiday is over.
   D. Do it only on October 8.

4. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a reminder for students?
   A. Complete homework.
   B. Keep a regular schedule.
   C. Do not go to dangerous places.
   D. Join a sports team.

5. What should parents do if their child cannot return to school on time?
   A. Do nothing.
   B. Inform the class teacher before October 7.
   C. Call the police.
   D. Keep the child at home for another week.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Classes will resume on October 8 (Tuesday)" 及第④段 "Back to school: October 8"

**【解析】** 原文明确说 10 月 8 日复课。A、B、D 项日期错误。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③、④段 "closed from October 1 to October 7" 及 "Start: October 1... End: October 7"

**【解析】** 假期为 10 月 1 日至 7 日，共 7 天。A、C、D 项时长错误。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Complete your homework before the holiday ends. Teachers will collect it on October 8"

**【解析】** 须在假期结束前完成作业，10 月 8 日上交。A、C、D 项与要求不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 列举了完成作业、保持作息、远离危险场所、帮做家务、出行注意等，未提及加入运动队

**【解析】** A、B、C 项均为提醒内容；D 项未提及。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "If your child cannot return due to illness or other reasons, please inform the class teacher before October 7"

**【解析】** 若不能按时返校，须在 10 月 7 日前告知班主任。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。
