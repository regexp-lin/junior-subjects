# 阅读理解（议论文）

① "I am too tired." Many of my classmates often say these words. Many students today spend too much time sitting in the classroom or playing computer games at home. As a result, they often feel tired and sick. In fact, doing sports is very important for our health and mind.

② First, exercise can keep us fit. When we run, swim, or play basketball, our bodies will become stronger. A strong body helps us avoid getting ill easily. It is better than taking much medicine.

③ Second, doing sports is a good way to relax. After a long day of studying hard, the brain needs a rest. Sweating (出汗) on the sports field can make us feel happy and relaxed. It also helps us sleep better at night. A good sleep means enough energy for the next day.

④ Finally, we can make new friends through sports. When we play games in a team, we have to talk and work with others. Teamwork teaches us how to share and how to help each other. We are not just running after a ball; we are learning social skills.

⑤ In a word, exercise brings us many benefits. Let's put down our phones, close our computers, and spend at least one hour a day on sports. 

**注释：** sweat 出汗；social skill 社交技能

---

### 选择题

1. Why do many students feel tired and sick today?
A. Because they do too much homework.
B. Because they don't eat healthily.
C. Because they spend too much time on computers or sitting.
D. Because they sleep too much.

2. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a benefit of doing sports?
A. Keeping our bodies fit.
B. Helping us relax and sleep better.
C. Making us strong enough to win any fight.
D. Helping us make new friends.

3. What does teamwork teach us on a sports team?
A. How to run fast.
B. How to share and help each other.
C. How to finish difficult homework.
D. How to sleep for a long time.

4. The underlined word "fit" in paragraph ② means ______?
A. healthy
B. tired
C. weak
D. angry

5. What does the writer advise us to do in the last paragraph?
A. Sleep at least 10 hours a day.
B. Spend at least one hour a day on sports.
C. Use phones and computers for an hour.
D. Read books on the sports field.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-3句 "Many students today spend too much time sitting in the classroom or playing computer games at home. As a result, they often feel tired and sick."

**【解析】** 原文指出许多学生因为花太多时间坐在教室或玩电脑游戏，结果导致他们经常感到疲惫和生病。A、B、D项都不是第一段提到的直接原因。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第②、③、④段。

**【解析】** A项（保持身体健康）在第②段提到；B项（帮助放松和更好睡眠）在第③段提到；D项（帮助交朋友）在第④段提到。C项“让我们强壮得能打赢任何一场架”文中并未提及，属于无中生有。题目要求选出没有提到的一项。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3句 "Teamwork teaches us how to share and how to help each other."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到团队合作教会我们如何分享和如何互相帮助。A项（跑得快）、C项（做难的作业）、D项（长时间睡眠）都不是团队合作带给我们的直接教益。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第②段第一句 "First, exercise can keep us fit," 以及后文提到的 "stronger"、"avoid getting ill easily"。

**【解析】** 锻炼的第一个好处是让我们“fit”。后文解释说我们的身体会变得更强壮，能帮我们避免生病。因此"fit"必定是"健康、健壮"的意思（healthy）。B项（疲劳）、C项（虚弱）、D项（生气）都不符合。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后两句 "...and spend at least one hour a day on sports."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段中提出建议（advise），呼吁大家放下手机、关掉电脑，每天至少花一小时进行体育锻炼。C项刚好与原文相反，A项和D项文中并未号召。故选B。
