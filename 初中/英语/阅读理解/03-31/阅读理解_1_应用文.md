> **03-31 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① Welcome to Sunshine English Summer Camp! Here is the daily schedule (日程安排) for Group A. Please read it carefully and be on time for all activities.

② **Morning**
- **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM**: Breakfast and Morning Reading. Let's start the day with delicious food and good English sentences!
- **9:30 AM - 11:30 AM**: English Speaking Class (Room 101). Our Canadian teacher, Mr. Smith, will teach you how to speak like a native speaker.

③ **Afternoon**
- **12:00 PM - 1:00 PM**: Lunch (Dining Hall). We provide both Chinese and Western food. 
- **2:00 PM - 4:00 PM**: Outdoor Activities and Games (School Field). Wear your sports shoes! We will play basketball and have fun team games.

④ **Evening**
- **6:00 PM - 7:00 PM**: Dinner.
- **7:30 PM - 9:00 PM**: English Movie Night (School Hall). We have prepared a great English movie with bilingual (双语的) subtitles for you to enjoy.

⑤ We hope you have a wonderful and meaningful week at the Sunshine English Summer Camp! If you have any questions, please find your group leader.

**注释：** bilingual 双语的；subtitle 字幕

---

### 选择题

1. What will Group A do at 10:00 AM?
A. Have breakfast.
B. Watch a movie.
C. Have an English Speaking Class.
D. Play outdoor games.

2. Where can the students have their lunch?
A. In Room 101.
B. In the Dining Hall.
C. On the School Field.
D. In the School Hall.

3. How long do the outdoor activities last?
A. One hour.
B. Two hours.
C. Three hours.
D. Four hours.

4. Form the text, we can infer (推断) that during the movie night, ________.
A. the students must wear sports shoes
B. they will eat Western food
C. students can learn English easily
D. they can see both English and Chinese words on the screen

5. What is the passage mainly about?
A. Activities for a family trip.
B. The timetable for a summer camp.
C. How to learn English well.
D. Famous foreign movies.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "9:30 AM - 11:30 AM: English Speaking Class (Room 101)"

**【解析】** 根据原文，早上 9:30 到 11:30 是英语口语课，所以上午 10:00 学生们正在上口语课。A项（吃早饭）、B项（看电影）、D项（做户外游戏）的时间均不在此时间段内。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch (Dining Hall)."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出午餐地点在餐厅（Dining Hall）。A项是上课的教室；C项是户外活动的场地；D项是看电影的地方。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Outdoor Activities and Games"

**【解析】** 从下午两点到四点进行户外活动，持续时间为两小时（Two hours）。故选B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "We have prepared a great English movie with bilingual (双语的) subtitles for you to enjoy."

**【解析】** 原文提到电影夜会放映带有“双语字幕”的电影。既然是双语，说明屏幕上既有英语也有中文（或另外一种语言的文字），所以推测学生能在屏幕上看到英文和中文。A项穿运动鞋是下午活动的建议；B项吃西餐是在午餐；C项“轻松学好英语”推断过远。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文及第①段 "Here is the daily schedule (日程安排) for Group A."

**【解析】** 这是一篇应用文形式的日程安排，主要介绍了夏令营（summer camp）一天的活动安排。A项“家庭旅行的活动”、C项“如何学好英语”、D项“著名的外国电影”均属于以偏概全或偏离主题。故选B。
