# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Community Gym: Opening Hours & Member Rules**

② Welcome to Sunshine Community Gym! We are open to all residents (居民) in our neighbourhood. Our gym provides fitness equipment, a swimming pool, and a basketball court. To use our facilities, you need to become a member first.

③ **Opening Hours:** Monday to Friday: 6:00 a.m.—10:00 p.m. On weekends and public holidays: 7:00 a.m.—9:00 p.m. The gym is closed on the first Monday of each month for cleaning and maintenance (维护). We will put up a notice one week before the closing day.

④ **Member Rules:** Members must show their membership card when entering. Please wear proper sports shoes. Food and drinks are not allowed in the exercise areas, but you may use the rest area. Children under 12 must be with an adult. If you lose your card, report it to the front desk right away. A new card costs 30 yuan.

⑤ **Annual membership** is 600 yuan per person. Students and the elderly (65 and above) get a 20% discount. For more information, visit our office or call 020-87654321. We look forward to seeing you!

**注释：** resident 居民；maintenance 维护

---

### 选择题

1. Who can use the Sunshine Community Gym?
   A. Only people who live in the neighbourhood and are members.
   B. Only students in the neighbourhood.
   C. Anyone who pays the fee.
   D. Only adults over 65.

2. When is the gym closed?
   A. Every Monday.
   B. All weekends.
   C. The first Monday of each month.
   D. On public holidays.

3. What must members do when entering the gym?
   A. Bring their own sports shoes only.
   B. Pay 30 yuan each time.
   C. Bring children under 12.
   D. Show their membership card.

4. How much does a student pay for annual membership?
   A. 600 yuan.
   B. 480 yuan.
   C. 120 yuan.
   D. 30 yuan.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The gym gives notice before the monthly closing day so members can plan.
   B. Members can eat and drink freely in the exercise areas.
   C. Children under 12 cannot enter the gym at all.
   D. Elderly people must pay the full 600 yuan.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We are open to all residents in our neighbourhood" "To use our facilities, you need to become a member first"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明健身房面向社区居民开放，且使用设施须先成为会员。A项准确。B项仅限学生、C项任意付费者、D项仅限65岁以上老人均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The gym is closed on the first Monday of each month for cleaning and maintenance"

**【解析】** 健身房在每月第一个周一关闭进行清洁和维护。C项正确。A项每周一、B项所有周末、D项节假日均与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Members must show their membership card when entering"

**【解析】** 会员进入时必须出示会员卡。D项正确。A项仅提及需穿运动鞋，非进入条件；B项30元为补办卡费；C项12岁以下儿童需成人陪同，非进入时必须。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解/推理

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Annual membership is 600 yuan per person. Students and the elderly (65 and above) get a 20% discount"

**【解析】** 年费600元，学生和65岁以上老人享8折优惠：600×(1-20%)=480元。B项正确。A项为原价；C、D项计算错误。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "We will put up a notice one week before the closing day"

**【解析】** 文中说明在关闭日一周前会张贴通知，可推知健身房提前告知以便会员安排时间。A项正确。B项与"Food and drinks are not allowed in the exercise areas"矛盾；C项与"Children under 12 must be with an adult"矛盾；D项与老人8折优惠矛盾。故选A。
