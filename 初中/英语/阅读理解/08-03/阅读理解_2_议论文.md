# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should Schools Extend the Lunch Break?**

② In many middle schools in China, students have a lunch break of about 50 to 60 minutes. During this time, they eat lunch, rest, and maybe do some homework. In recent years, there has been a discussion: should schools make the lunch break longer? Some people say yes; others say no. Let us look at both sides.

③ **Those who support a longer break** argue that students need more time to rest. After a morning of classes, young people feel tired. A short break is not enough for them to digest (消化) their food properly or to relax their minds. Research shows that proper rest improves memory and helps students do better in afternoon classes. Supporters also point out that many students finish lunch quickly and then rush to do homework. They hardly have time to take a walk, talk with friends, or even close their eyes. A longer break would allow students to recharge (恢复精力) and return to class with more energy.

④ **Those who disagree** say that extending the break would shorten the school day or push classes later into the afternoon. Some parents worry that if students go home later, they will have less time for evening study or family activities. Besides, a longer break does not mean students will use the time wisely—they might just spend more time on phones or games. Critics also argue that 50 to 60 minutes is already enough if students use the time well. The problem might not be the length of the break, but how students choose to spend it.

⑤ **In my opinion,** the lunch break should be a bit longer—perhaps 70 to 80 minutes. Students today face a lot of pressure, and rest is important for both health and learning. Schools could try this in some classes first and see if it works. At the same time, students should learn to use their break time wisely: eat slowly, take a short rest, and avoid rushing through lunch to do homework. A good break helps us learn better.

**注释：** digest 消化；recharge 恢复精力

---

### 选择题

1. According to supporters of a longer break, why do students need more rest time?
   A. To digest food properly, relax, improve memory, and do better in afternoon classes.
   B. So they can play more games.
   C. Because teachers want shorter classes.
   D. To go home earlier.

2. What do those who disagree worry about if the break is extended?
   A. Students will eat too much.
   B. Students will not eat lunch at all.
   C. Teachers will have less work.
   D. The school day would shorten or end later; students might waste time on phones or games; 50–60 minutes may already be enough.

3. How long does the writer suggest the lunch break should be?
   A. 50 to 60 minutes.
   B. 2 hours.
   C. 70 to 80 minutes.
   D. 30 minutes.

4. What does the writer advise students to do during the break?
   A. Eat slowly, take a short rest, and avoid rushing through lunch to do homework.
   B. Eat quickly and do homework.
   C. Use phones and play games.
   D. Skip lunch and sleep.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell students to never rest.
   B. To discuss whether schools should extend the lunch break and present different views.
   C. To introduce different kinds of school lunches.
   D. To encourage students to play more sports.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students need more time to rest... digest their food properly or to relax their minds... Research shows that proper rest improves memory and helps students do better in afternoon classes"

**【解析】** 支持者认为更长休息可助学生消化食物、放松身心，研究显示适当休息可改善记忆、提升下午课堂表现。A项全面概括。B、C、D项均与支持者观点不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "extending the break would shorten the school day or push classes later... if students go home later... a longer break does not mean students will use the time wisely—they might just spend more time on phones or games... 50 to 60 minutes is already enough if students use the time well"

**【解析】** 反对者指出：延长休息会缩短上课时间或推迟放学；家长担忧回家更晚；更长休息未必被善用，可能更多玩手机或游戏；若善用时间，50–60分钟已够。D项准确。A、B、C项均与原文不符。故选D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "the lunch break should be a bit longer—perhaps 70 to 80 minutes"

**【解析】** 作者建议午休时间稍长一些，约70至80分钟。C项正确。A项为现状；B、D项与作者建议不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "students should learn to use their break time wisely: eat slowly, take a short rest, and avoid rushing through lunch to do homework"

**【解析】** 作者建议学生善用休息时间：细嚼慢咽、短暂休息、避免匆匆吃完就去写作业。A项正确。B、C、D项与作者建议矛盾。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全篇，尤其是第②段 "there has been a discussion: should schools make the lunch break longer? Some people say yes; others say no. Let us look at both sides"

**【解析】** 文章围绕"学校是否应延长午休"展开讨论，呈现支持与反对观点及作者看法。B项准确概括写作目的。A、C、D项与文章主旨不符。故选B。
