# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Learning to Accept Failure

① Li Wei had practiced badminton for three years. He trained hard every day and dreamed of winning the school championship. When the competition day came, he felt confident. "I've prepared so well. I can do this," he told himself.

② The match began. Li Wei won the first game easily. His opponent (对手), a boy from another class, seemed nervous. But in the second game, everything changed. The other boy became more focused and played better. Li Wei made several mistakes. He lost the second game. In the final game, Li Wei tried his best, but he was too anxious. He lost the match 2-1.

③ Li Wei walked off the court feeling terrible. He had let himself down. He found a quiet corner and sat there alone. His coach came over and sat beside him. "Losing is part of sports," the coach said. "Every champion has lost many times before winning. What matters is what you learn from it."

④ Li Wei thought about the coach's words. He realized that he had been too proud after winning the first game. He had stopped paying attention and made careless mistakes. Failure taught him that he needed to stay focused until the very end. He also understood that one loss did not define (定义) who he was. He could still improve and try again next year.

⑤ The next day, Li Wei went back to training. He worked even harder and became more humble (谦虚的). A few months later, he won a smaller competition. This time, he stayed calm throughout the whole match. Li Wei learned that accepting failure is not giving up — it is the first step toward becoming stronger.

**注释：** opponent 对手；championship 锦标赛；court 球场；define 定义；humble 谦虚的

---

### 选择题

1. What was Li Wei's dream before the competition?
   A. To become a professional badminton player.
   B. To win the school badminton championship.
   C. To join the national team.
   D. To beat his coach in a match.

2. What happened in the second game of the match?
   A. Li Wei played better and won easily.
   B. The opponent became nervous and made many mistakes.
   C. The opponent became more focused and played better; Li Wei made mistakes and lost.
   D. The match was cancelled because of rain.

3. What did the coach say to Li Wei after he lost?
   A. Li Wei should give up badminton.
   B. The opponent had cheated during the match.
   C. Li Wei was not good enough for the competition.
   D. Losing is part of sports, and what matters is what you learn from it.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Li Wei's self-reflection?
   A. He realized he had been overconfident after winning the first game and needed to stay focused.
   B. He decided to switch to another sport.
   C. He blamed his opponent for winning.
   D. He thought the coach's advice was not useful.

5. What does the passage want to tell us?
   A. We should never take part in sports competitions.
   B. Coaches always know how to help students win.
   C. Winning is the only important thing in sports.
   D. Accepting failure can help us learn and grow, and it is the first step to becoming stronger.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He trained hard every day and dreamed of winning the school championship."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明李伟每天刻苦训练，梦想赢得校锦标赛冠军。B 项正确。A 项"成为职业选手"、C 项"加入国家队"、D 项"打败教练"均非原文所述梦想。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "But in the second game, everything changed. The other boy became more focused and played better. Li Wei made several mistakes. He lost the second game."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明第二局局势逆转：对手更专注、表现更好，李伟出现多次失误并输掉该局。C 项正确。A 项"李伟表现更好"与原文相反；B 项"对手紧张失误"描述的是第一局；D 项"因雨取消"原文未提及。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "'Losing is part of sports,' the coach said. 'Every champion has lost many times before winning. What matters is what you learn from it.'"

**【解析】** 原文直接引用教练的话：失败是运动的一部分，每位冠军在获胜前都曾多次失败，重要的是从失败中学到什么。D 项正确。A 项"应放弃羽毛球"、B 项"对手作弊"、C 项"不够好"均与教练原意不符。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "He realized that he had been too proud after winning the first game. He had stopped paying attention and made careless mistakes. Failure taught him that he needed to stay focused until the very end."

**【解析】** 李伟反思自己第一局获胜后过于骄傲，注意力分散导致粗心失误，失败教会他必须专注到最后一刻。可推知他意识到首局后的自负及保持专注的重要性。A 项正确。B 项"改练其他项目"、C 项"责怪对手"、D 项"认为教练建议无用"均与原文矛盾。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第①②段写比赛失利，第③④段写教练开导与自我反思，第⑤段写接受失败后的成长与再战成功。

**【解析】** 全文围绕李伟在羽毛球比赛中失利后学会接受失败、从中学习并变得更强的经历展开，D 项"接受失败能帮助我们学习与成长，是变得更强的第一步"准确概括文章主旨。A 项"不应参加体育竞赛"、B 项"教练总能帮学生赢"、C 项"获胜才重要"均与文章寓意不符。故选 D。
