> **07-10 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **English Corner: Join Us This Friday!**

② Dear students,

③ We are happy to invite you to our school English Corner this Friday afternoon. It will take place in the school library from 3:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. All students who love English are welcome to join us.

④ At the English Corner, you will have a chance to practice speaking English in a relaxed way. There will be fun activities such as free talk, role-play, and English songs. You can also share your ideas about learning English with other students. Our English teachers will be there to help you. Do not worry if your English is not perfect. The English Corner is a safe place to practice and make mistakes.

⑤ Please sign your name on the notice board before Wednesday if you want to join. Bring your enthusiasm (热情) and a smile. We look forward to seeing you there!

School English Club

**注释：** sign 签名；enthusiasm 热情

---

### 选择题

1. When will the English Corner be held?
   A. From 3:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. on Friday.
   B. On Wednesday afternoon.
   C. In the morning.
   D. Every day after school.

2. Where will the English Corner take place?
   A. In the classroom.
   B. In the school playground.
   C. In the teachers' office.
   D. In the school library.

3. What activities can students join at the English Corner?
   A. Only listening to English songs.
   B. Reading books only.
   C. Free talk, role-play, and English songs.
   D. Taking English tests.

4. What should students do if they want to join the English Corner?
   A. Call the English teacher.
   B. Sign their name on the notice board before Wednesday.
   C. Send an email to the club.
   D. Bring a friend to the library.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The English Corner aims to help students practice speaking without fear of mistakes.
   B. Only students with perfect English can join.
   C. Teachers will not attend the activity.
   D. Students must pay to join the English Corner.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "It will take place in the school library from 3:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 文中明确写出英语角时间为周五下午 3:30 至 5:00。A项正确。B项周三下午、C项上午、D项每天放学后均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "It will take place in the school library"

**【解析】** 文中清楚说明活动地点为学校图书馆。D项正确。A项教室、B项操场、C项教师办公室均未提及。故选D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "There will be fun activities such as free talk, role-play, and English songs."

**【解析】** 文中列举的活动包括自由交谈、角色扮演和英文歌曲。C项完整概括。A项仅听歌、B项仅读书、D项考试均不完整或错误。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Please sign your name on the notice board before Wednesday if you want to join."

**【解析】** 文中要求想参加的学生在周三之前在布告栏签名报名。B项正确。A项打电话、C项发邮件、D项带朋友均与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Do not worry if your English is not perfect. The English Corner is a safe place to practice and make mistakes."

**【解析】** 文中强调英语不完美也不必担心，英语角是练习和犯错的安全场所，可推知活动旨在帮助学生放心练习口语。A项正确。B项仅限英语完美者与原文矛盾；C、D项未提及。故选A。
