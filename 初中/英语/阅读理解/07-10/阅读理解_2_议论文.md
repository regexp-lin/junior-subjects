# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Paper Books or Audiobooks: Which Is Better for Learning?**

② Today, students have more ways to read than ever before. They can choose paper books or audiobooks (有声书). Which one is better for learning? Different people have different opinions.

③ **Those who prefer paper books** say that reading with your eyes helps you remember better. When you hold a book and turn the pages, your brain works in a special way. You can easily underline (画线) important sentences or write notes in the margins (页边空白). Paper books do not need batteries or the Internet. You can read them anywhere, even in places with no electricity. Some studies show that students who read paper books often get higher marks in exams.

④ **Those who prefer audiobooks** argue that listening saves time. You can listen while walking, doing sports, or traveling. Audiobooks are good for people who find it hard to sit and read for a long time. The narrator's (朗读者) voice can help you understand the meaning and mood of the story. For language learners, listening to audiobooks can improve pronunciation and listening skills. However, audiobooks need a phone or a device, and sometimes it is easy to get distracted (分心).

⑤ **The conclusion:** There is no single best way. Paper books may work better when you need to focus and take notes. Audiobooks are useful when you want to use spare time or practice listening. Smart learners often use both. Choose the way that fits your situation best.

**注释：** underline 画线；margins 页边空白；narrator 朗读者；distracted 分心

---

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, why do some people prefer paper books?
   A. Reading with your eyes helps remember better, and you can take notes easily.
   B. Paper books are cheaper than audiobooks.
   C. Paper books are lighter to carry.
   D. Everyone can read paper books faster.

2. What advantage do audiobooks have?
   A. They never need batteries.
   B. They are free for all students.
   C. They help you sleep better.
   D. You can listen while doing other things like walking or traveling.

3. What is mentioned as a drawback (缺点) of audiobooks?
   A. They are too long.
   B. They have no narrators.
   C. They need a device and may distract you.
   D. They cannot improve listening skills.

4. What does the writer suggest at the end?
   A. Students should only use paper books.
   B. There is no single best way; smart learners use both according to their needs.
   C. Audiobooks are always better for learning.
   D. Schools should ban audiobooks.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To compare paper books and audiobooks and help readers choose wisely.
   B. To advertise a new audiobook app.
   C. To tell readers that paper books will disappear soon.
   D. To explain how to make audiobooks.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "reading with your eyes helps you remember better... You can easily underline important sentences or write notes in the margins."

**【解析】** 文中指出纸质书支持视觉阅读以助记忆，且便于画线、在页边做笔记。A项概括了这些优点。B项价格、C项更轻、D项读得更快均非文中主要理由。故选A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "listening saves time. You can listen while walking, doing sports, or traveling."

**【解析】** 文中说明有声书可节省时间，可在走路、运动或出行时收听。D项正确。A项不需要电池与原文"need a phone or a device"矛盾；B、C项未提及。故选D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "However, audiobooks need a phone or a device, and sometimes it is easy to get distracted (分心)."

**【解析】** 文中指出有声书需要手机或设备，且容易让人分心。C项正确。A项太长、B项无朗读者、D项无法提高听力均与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "There is no single best way... Smart learners often use both. Choose the way that fits your situation best."

**【解析】** 作者总结没有唯一最佳方式，聪明学习者常两者兼用，应根据自身情况选择。B项准确概括。A项只用纸质书、C项有声书一定更好、D项禁止有声书均与作者立场相反。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Paper Books or Audiobooks: Which Is Better for Learning?" 及全文结构（双方观点 + 结论建议）

**【解析】** 文章围绕纸质书与有声书展开对比，分析各自优缺点，最后给出选择建议。A项准确概括主旨。B项推广应用、C项纸质书将消失、D项如何制作有声书均非主旨。故选A。
