# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Typhoons (台风) are powerful storms that form over warm ocean waters, usually in summer and autumn. When the sun heats the sea surface (海面), warm air rises quickly and creates areas of low pressure (低压). Cool air rushes in to fill the space, and with the Earth's rotation (自转), these winds begin to spin in a circular pattern. When wind speeds reach over 119 kilometers per hour, the storm is called a typhoon.

② A typhoon has three main parts: the eye, the eye wall, and the rain bands. The eye is the calm center of the storm — the sky may even be clear there. But the eye wall around it has the strongest winds and the heaviest rain. The rain bands are the clouds and rain that spiral (盘旋) outward from the center. These bands can stretch for hundreds of kilometers and bring heavy rainfall to coastal cities.

③ Typhoons often cause serious damage. Strong winds can knock down trees and destroy buildings. Heavy rain leads to floods (洪水) in low-lying areas. Storm surges (风暴潮) — when seawater is pushed onto the shore — can sweep away cars and damage coastal roads. In China, provinces like Guangdong, Fujian, and Zhejiang are most often affected because they lie along the coast.

④ To stay safe during a typhoon, people should follow official warnings. Listen to the radio or TV for the latest information. Store enough food, water, and medicine at home. Close all windows and doors tightly. If you live near the sea or in a low area, move to a higher and safer place before the typhoon arrives. Do not go outside during the storm — flying objects can cause serious injuries.

⑤ Understanding typhoons helps us prepare better and reduce harm. Schools sometimes close when a typhoon is coming, and families should have an emergency plan (应急预案) ready. Remember: being prepared is the best way to protect yourself and your loved ones.

**注释：** surface 表面；pressure 压力；rotation 自转；flood 洪水；surge 涌浪；spiral 盘旋；emergency 紧急的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, where do typhoons form?
   A. In the middle of deserts.
   B. In large cities by the sea.
   C. Over warm ocean waters.
   D. Over cold mountain areas.

2. What is true about the "eye" of a typhoon?
   A. It has the strongest winds in the entire storm.
   B. It brings the heaviest rain to the coast.
   C. It stretches for hundreds of kilometers.
   D. It is the calm center and the sky may be clear there.

3. According to paragraph ④, what should people do before a typhoon arrives?
   A. Go to the beach to watch the storm.
   B. Store food, water, and medicine at home.
   C. Open all windows to let fresh air in.
   D. Drive to the seaside for safety.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ③ about coastal areas in China?
   A. They never experience typhoons.
   B. They are more likely to be hit by typhoons than inland areas.
   C. Typhoons only affect Guangdong Province.
   D. Storm surges do not happen in China.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To introduce the history of Chinese weather forecasting.
   B. To encourage people to travel during typhoon season.
   C. To describe a fun day at the beach.
   D. To explain how typhoons form and how to stay safe.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "Typhoons are powerful storms that form over warm ocean waters"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明台风形成于温暖的海面之上。A 项"沙漠中部"、B 项"海边大城市"和 D 项"寒冷山区"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The eye is the calm center of the storm — the sky may even be clear there."

**【解析】** 原文指出台风眼是风暴的平静中心，天空甚至可能是晴朗的。A 项"风力最强"描述的是眼墙（eye wall），不是台风眼；B 项"带来最强降雨"同样属于眼墙；C 项"延伸数百公里"描述的是雨带（rain bands）。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Store enough food, water, and medicine at home."

**【解析】** 原文明确建议在家中储备足够的食物、水和药品。A 项"去海滩观潮"与安全建议相反；C 项"打开窗户"与"紧密关闭门窗"矛盾；D 项"开车去海边"会将自己置于更危险的境地。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "In China, provinces like Guangdong, Fujian, and Zhejiang are most often affected because they lie along the coast."

**【解析】** 原文指出广东、福建、浙江等沿海省份最常受台风影响，因其地处海岸，可推断沿海地区比内陆更容易遭遇台风。A 项"从不经历台风"与原文矛盾；C 项"仅影响广东"过于片面；D 项"中国不发生风暴潮"与第③段内容矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①-②段介绍台风形成与结构，第③段说明危害，第④-⑤段讲述预防与安全措施。

**【解析】** 全文围绕台风的形成、结构和预防安全展开，D 项最能概括主旨。A 项"气象预报历史"、B 项"鼓励台风季出行"和 C 项"海滩游玩"均与文章内容不符。故选 D。
