# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last summer, Zhang Ming went on a train trip to visit his grandparents. The journey was long — twelve hours — and he had brought a lot of food: sandwiches, fruit, biscuits, and two bottles of water. His mother had packed everything carefully, saying, "You might get hungry on the way."

② Around noon, Zhang Ming noticed an elderly woman sitting across from him. She was traveling alone and had not eaten anything since the train left. She looked tired and kept looking at her bag as if searching for something. Zhang Ming felt his heart sink. He remembered his grandmother's words: "When you share what you have, you receive more than you give."

③ Zhang Ming stood up and walked over to the woman. "Auntie, would you like some lunch?" he asked gently, holding out a sandwich and an apple. The woman looked surprised at first. "Oh, young man, that's very kind of you, but I don't want to trouble you." Zhang Ming smiled. "It's no trouble at all. I have plenty. Please take it."

④ The woman accepted the food with tears in her eyes. "Thank you so much. I forgot to bring food because I left in a hurry. My son is in the hospital, and I had to catch this train." She shared her story while they ate together. Other passengers nearby watched and smiled. One of them offered the woman a bottle of water. Another shared some cookies.

⑤ When the train finally arrived, the woman held Zhang Ming's hand and said, "You have a kind heart. Your parents must be very proud of you." Zhang Ming felt warm inside. He had only shared a little food, but it had made a big difference. That day, he learned that a small act of kindness can bring warmth to a long journey — and to many hearts.

**注释：** journey 旅程；elderly 年长的；sandwich 三明治；biscuit 饼干；passenger 乘客；cookie 饼干；kindness 善意

---

### 选择题

1. Where was Zhang Ming going?
   A. To visit his grandparents by train.
   B. To the hospital to visit a patient.
   C. To meet his mother at the train station.
   D. To buy food at a station.

2. What did Zhang Ming offer to the elderly woman?
   A. Money for lunch.
   B. Only a bottle of water.
   C. A sandwich and an apple.
   D. Some biscuits and cookies.

3. Why had the woman forgotten to bring food?
   A. She was too old to remember.
   B. She left in a hurry because her son was in the hospital.
   C. She thought the train would provide free meals.
   D. She did not like eating on trains.

4. What can we infer from the behavior of other passengers?
   A. They thought the woman should have prepared better.
   B. They did not notice what was happening.
   C. They were angry with Zhang Ming for sharing food.
   D. They were inspired by Zhang Ming and also showed kindness.

5. What did Zhang Ming learn from this experience?
   A. Food is the most important thing on a long trip.
   B. Elderly people should not travel alone.
   C. Train journeys are always difficult and boring.
   D. A small act of kindness can bring warmth to many people.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "Last summer, Zhang Ming went on a train trip to visit his grandparents."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明张明乘火车去探望祖父母。B 项"去医院探望病人"是那位老太太的目的地，张冠李戴；C 项"去火车站接母亲"和 D 项"去车站买吃的"均无依据。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "'Auntie, would you like some lunch?' he asked gently, holding out a sandwich and an apple."

**【解析】** 张明递给老太太的是一个三明治和一个苹果。A 项"钱"文中未提及；B 项"只有一瓶水"是其他乘客 later 提供的；D 项"饼干"是另一位乘客分享的。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "I forgot to bring food because I left in a hurry. My son is in the hospital, and I had to catch this train."

**【解析】** 老太太亲口说明她匆忙出发忘了带吃的，因为儿子住院，她必须赶上这趟火车。A 项"年龄大记不住"属于主观猜测；C 项"以为火车提供免费餐"和 D 项"不喜欢在火车上吃东西"均无依据。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Other passengers nearby watched and smiled. One of them offered the woman a bottle of water. Another shared some cookies."

**【解析】** 周围乘客看到张明的善举后微笑，有人递水，有人分享饼干，可见他们受到感染也表现出善意。A 项"认为老太太应该准备更好"文中未体现；B 项"没注意到"与后文"offered""shared"矛盾；C 项"生气"与"smiled"矛盾。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "He had only shared a little food, but it had made a big difference. That day, he learned that a small act of kindness can bring warmth to a long journey — and to many hearts."

**【解析】** 张明领悟到一个小小的善举能为漫长的旅程和许多心灵带来温暖。A 项"食物最重要"偏离主题；B 项"老人不应独自旅行"过于绝对；C 项"火车旅途总是枯燥"并非文章重点。故选 D。
