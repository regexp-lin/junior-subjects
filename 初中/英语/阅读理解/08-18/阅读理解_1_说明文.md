# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Tea is one of the most popular drinks in the world, and China is where it all began. According to legend (传说), tea was discovered by Emperor Shen Nong about 5,000 years ago. One day, some tea leaves fell into his pot of boiling water by accident. He tasted the water and found it delicious. Since then, tea has become an important part of Chinese culture.

② There are many types of Chinese tea, but the most common ones are green tea, black tea, oolong tea (乌龙茶), and white tea. Green tea is the most widely drunk in China. It has a light, fresh taste and is believed to be good for health. Black tea has a stronger flavor and is popular in western countries too. Oolong tea is somewhere between green tea and black tea. White tea is the least processed (加工的) and has a very mild taste.

③ In Chinese culture, tea is much more than just a drink. It is a way to show respect and friendship. When guests visit a Chinese home, the host usually offers tea first. At important events such as weddings, the bride and groom serve tea to their parents to show gratitude (感恩). Tea also plays a role in business. People often discuss work over a cup of tea because the relaxed atmosphere (氛围) helps build trust.

④ The Chinese tea ceremony (茶道) is a special art. It involves carefully choosing the tea, heating the water to the right temperature, and pouring the tea in a particular way. The ceremony is not just about drinking tea — it is about slowing down, enjoying the moment, and finding peace in a busy world. Many Chinese people believe that making and drinking tea can calm the mind and improve focus.

⑤ Today, Chinese tea culture continues to spread around the world. Tea houses can be found in many countries, and more people are learning about the different types of Chinese tea. Whether you prefer it hot or cold, sweet or plain, tea connects people across cultures and generations.

**注释：** legend 传说；emperor 皇帝；oolong 乌龙；processed 加工的；mild 温和的；gratitude 感恩；atmosphere 氛围；ceremony 茶道

---

**Questions:**

1. According to the legend, who discovered tea?
   A. A farmer.
   B. Emperor Shen Nong.
   C. A foreign traveler.
   D. A tea seller.

2. Which type of tea is the most widely drunk in China?
   A. Black tea.
   B. White tea.
   C. Oolong tea.
   D. Green tea.

3. Why do Chinese hosts offer tea to guests?
   A. To show they are rich.
   B. To show respect and friendship.
   C. To sell tea to their guests.
   D. To keep guests from feeling cold.

4. What is the Chinese tea ceremony mainly about?
   A. Drinking tea as fast as possible.
   B. Competing to see who makes the best tea.
   C. Slowing down and enjoying the moment.
   D. Testing whether the tea is safe to drink.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Chinese tea culture is becoming less popular.
   B. People in other countries do not like Chinese tea.
   C. Chinese tea culture is spreading and connecting people worldwide.
   D. Tea houses only exist in China.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "According to legend, tea was discovered by Emperor Shen Nong about 5,000 years ago."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明传说中茶是由神农帝发现的。A项"农民"、C项"外国旅行者"和D项"茶叶商人"原文均未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "Green tea is the most widely drunk in China."

**【解析】** 原文直接说明绿茶是中国饮用最广泛的茶。A项"红茶"文中说在西方也受欢迎但未说是中国最普遍的；B项"白茶"加工最少；C项"乌龙茶"介于绿茶和红茶之间。故选D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "It is a way to show respect and friendship. When guests visit a Chinese home, the host usually offers tea first."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明敬茶是表达尊重和友谊的方式。A项"显示富有"属于无中生有；C项"卖茶给客人"属于无中生有；D项"防止客人着凉"原文未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "it is about slowing down, enjoying the moment, and finding peace in a busy world."

**【解析】** 原文明确说茶道的核心是慢下来、享受当下、在忙碌世界中找到平静。A项"尽快喝完茶"与原意完全相反；B项"比赛谁泡的茶最好"属于无中生有；D项"测试茶是否安全"原文无依据。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Chinese tea culture continues to spread around the world" 及 "tea connects people across cultures and generations."

**【解析】** 最后一段提到中国茶文化在全球传播，茶馆遍布多国，茶连接了不同文化和代际的人，可推断茶文化正在走向世界并连接人们。A项"中国茶文化越来越不受欢迎"与原文相反；B项"其他国家的人不喜欢中国茶"与文意矛盾；D项"茶馆只存在于中国"与原文"Tea houses can be found in many countries"矛盾。故选C。
