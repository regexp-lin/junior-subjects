# 阅读理解（议论文）

**The Power of Reading**

① Books can take us to different worlds. When we read, we meet new characters, visit new places, and experience new ideas — all without leaving our chairs. Reading is one of the best ways to learn, grow, and understand the world around us.

② For students, reading has clear benefits. It builds vocabulary. The more you read, the more words you see and remember. Reading also improves your writing. When you read good sentences and paragraphs, you learn how to express yourself better. Many top students say that reading widely helped them do well in exams. It is not only about language — reading about history, science, and different cultures opens your mind.

③ Some students say they are too busy to read. "I have too much homework." "I'd rather watch videos." But even 15 or 20 minutes of reading a day can make a difference. You can read on the bus, before bed, or during lunch break. Start with books that interest you — stories, comics, or magazines. Once you get into the habit, you may find that you enjoy it more than you expected.

④ What should we read? There is no single answer. Fiction (小说) and non-fiction (非虚构) both have value. Stories make us imagine and feel. Non-fiction teaches us facts and ideas. Try different types. Ask your teacher or librarian for suggestions. Your school library or local library often has books for free.

⑤ In the age of phones and games, reading might seem old-fashioned. But a good book never runs out of battery. It is always there when you need it. Pick up a book today and see where it takes you.

**注释：** fiction 小说；non-fiction 非虚构

---

**Questions:**

1. What does the writer say about reading in the first paragraph?
   A. Reading is boring.
   B. Reading can take us to different worlds and help us learn and understand the world.
   C. Reading is only for adults.
   D. Reading takes too much time.

2. How does reading help students, according to the passage?
   A. It makes them sleepy.
   B. It builds vocabulary, improves writing, and opens the mind.
   C. It helps them play better.
   D. It replaces homework.

3. What does the writer suggest for busy students?
   A. Never read.
   B. Even 15–20 minutes of reading a day can help; start with books that interest you.
   C. Only read during holidays.
   D. Read only textbooks.

4. What does the writer say about fiction and non-fiction?
   A. Only fiction is useful.
   B. Only non-fiction is useful.
   C. Both have value; try different types.
   D. Neither is useful.

5. What does "a good book never runs out of battery" mean?
   A. Books need electricity.
   B. Books are always available and do not need charging like phones.
   C. Books are heavy.
   D. Books are expensive.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Books can take us to different worlds... Reading is one of the best ways to learn, grow, and understand the world around us."

**【解析】** 阅读能带我们去不同世界，是学习、成长和认识世界的很好方式。A、C、D项与作者观点相反或片面。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "It builds vocabulary... improves your writing... opens your mind"

**【解析】** 阅读能积累词汇、提高写作、开阔思维。A项使人困、C项玩得更好、D项替代作业均与益处不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "even 15 or 20 minutes of reading a day can make a difference... Start with books that interest you"

**【解析】** 作者建议每天读15–20分钟也有帮助，可从感兴趣的书开始。A、C、D项与建议矛盾。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Fiction and non-fiction both have value. Try different types."

**【解析】** 小说与非虚构都有价值，应尝试不同类型。A、B、D项过于片面或错误。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "a good book never runs out of battery. It is always there when you need it"

**【解析】** 好书无需充电，随时可取用，是相对手机等的比喻。A项书要用电、C项书重、D项贵均非比喻含义。故选B。
