# 阅读理解（说明文）

**Saving Water in Daily Life**

① Water is precious (珍贵的). Our bodies need it to live. We use it to cook, wash, and grow food. Yet in many places, water is becoming scarce (稀缺的). We must learn to save water in our daily life.

② How much water do we use? A ten-minute shower can use about 50–80 litres. Brushing your teeth with the tap running wastes about 6 litres each time. A leaking (漏水的) tap can waste up to 20 litres a day. Small habits can add up to a lot of waste.

③ What can we do at home? First, turn off the tap when you brush your teeth or soap your hands. Second, take shorter showers — try to keep it under 5 minutes. Third, fix leaking taps as soon as you notice them. Fourth, reuse water when possible. For example, use the water from washing rice or vegetables to water plants. Fifth, use a full load when you run the washing machine. Do not run it for just a few clothes.

④ At school, remind your classmates to save water. Report leaking taps to the school office. Do not leave taps running in the toilet or after washing hands. Some schools have started "water-saving weeks" to teach students good habits. You can suggest this to your teacher.

⑤ Every drop counts. Saving water is not just for today — it is for our future. When we save water, we help protect the environment and make sure there is enough water for everyone. Start with one small change today.

**注释：** precious 珍贵的；scarce 稀缺的；leak 漏水

---

**Questions:**

1. Why should we save water, according to the passage?
   A. Because it is free.
   B. Because water is becoming scarce in many places.
   C. Because our teachers say so.
   D. Because it tastes good.

2. How much water can a leaking tap waste in a day?
   A. About 6 litres.
   B. About 20 litres.
   C. About 50 litres.
   D. About 80 litres.

3. Which is a good way to save water when brushing teeth?
   A. Leave the tap running.
   B. Turn off the tap when not using it.
   C. Brush for a long time.
   D. Use hot water.

4. How can we reuse water at home?
   A. Drink the same water twice.
   B. Use water from washing rice or vegetables to water plants.
   C. Never wash vegetables.
   D. Use the washing machine every day.

5. What does "Every drop counts" mean?
   A. We should count drops.
   B. Every small effort to save water matters.
   C. Drops are expensive.
   D. We need to drink more water.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "in many places, water is becoming scarce. We must learn to save water"

**【解析】** 许多地方水资源日益稀缺，因此需要节约用水。A、C、D项均非根本原因。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "A leaking tap can waste up to 20 litres a day."

**【解析】** 漏水的龙头每天可浪费多达20升水。A项约6升为刷牙时开着龙头；C、D项为淋浴用量。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "turn off the tap when you brush your teeth or soap your hands"

**【解析】** 刷牙或搓手时关闭龙头可节水。A项一直开着会浪费；C、D项与节水无关。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "use the water from washing rice or vegetables to water plants"

**【解析】** 可用淘米水、洗菜水浇花。A、C、D项不合理或与节水无直接关系。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Every drop counts... Start with one small change today."

**【解析】** "每一滴都算数"意为每一次小的节水努力都有意义。A项数水滴、C项水滴贵、D项多喝水均偏离本意。故选B。
