> **06-01 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last September, Li Xia moved to a new city with her family. She had to leave her old school and friends behind. On her first day at Sunshine Middle School, she felt nervous and lonely. She sat alone at lunch and did not talk to anyone. "Will I ever make new friends here?" she wondered.

② The next day, during the PE class, Li Xia was asked to join a basketball game. She was not very good at it, but she tried her best. A girl named Wang Mei passed the ball to her and shouted, "Run, Li Xia! You can do it!" Li Xia caught the ball and made a shot. Though she missed, Wang Mei smiled and said, "Nice try! Let's practice together after class."

③ After class, Wang Mei came to Li Xia's desk. "I'm new here too. I transferred last year," Wang Mei said. "I know how you feel. If you like, we can have lunch together tomorrow." Li Xia's heart felt warm. She said yes. That afternoon, Wang Mei introduced her to two more students, Lin Tao and Chen Hui. They all welcomed Li Xia and asked her to join their study group.

④ In the following weeks, Li Xia ate lunch with her new friends every day. They helped her with homework and invited her to join the school art club. Gradually, Li Xia began to enjoy her new school life. She even joined the basketball team with Wang Mei. "I was afraid of being alone," Li Xia said to her mother one evening. "But now I have real friends. I am glad I gave it a chance."

⑤ Looking back, Li Xia learned that change can be difficult, but it can also bring new opportunities. A kind gesture (举动) from a stranger can turn into lasting friendship. She will never forget Wang Mei's smile on that PE class — it was the beginning of something wonderful.

**注释：** transfer 转学；gesture 举动

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, how did Li Xia feel on her first day at the new school?
   A. She felt nervous and lonely, and sat alone at lunch.
   B. She felt excited and happy.
   C. She felt angry with her parents.
   D. She felt bored and sleepy.

2. According to paragraph ②, what happened during the PE class?
   A. Li Xia refused to join the basketball game.
   B. Li Xia made a perfect shot and won the game.
   C. Wang Mei passed the ball to Li Xia, encouraged her, and offered to practice together after class.
   D. Wang Mei laughed at Li Xia for missing the shot.

3. According to paragraph ③, why did Wang Mei understand how Li Xia felt?
   A. Wang Mei had read about it in a book.
   B. Wang Mei's teacher told her.
   C. Wang Mei had known Li Xia before.
   D. Wang Mei was also a transfer student who had moved the year before.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Li Xia's change?
   A. With the help of her new friends, she overcame her loneliness and began to enjoy school life.
   B. She became the best student in the class.
   C. She decided to leave the new school.
   D. She stopped liking basketball.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Students should not move to new cities.
   B. Change can be hard, but a kind gesture can lead to new friends and opportunities.
   C. Basketball is the best way to make friends.
   D. Only transfer students need friends.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "On her first day at Sunshine Middle School, she felt nervous and lonely. She sat alone at lunch and did not talk to anyone."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出李霞第一天感到紧张、孤单，午餐时独自坐着、不与任何人交谈，A 项正确。B 项"兴奋开心"、C 项"对父母生气"和 D 项"无聊困倦"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Wang Mei passed the ball to her and shouted, 'Run, Li Xia! You can do it!'... Wang Mei smiled and said, 'Nice try! Let's practice together after class.'"

**【解析】** 原文写明王梅把球传给李霞并鼓励她，李霞未投中后王梅微笑说"很好的尝试"，并提议课后一起练习，C 项正确。A 项"李霞拒绝参加"、B 项"李霞投中完美一球"和 D 项"王梅嘲笑李霞"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I'm new here too. I transferred last year... I know how you feel."

**【解析】** 王梅说明自己去年也转学来此，因此理解李霞的感受，D 项正确。A 项"书中读过"、B 项"老师告知"和 C 项"之前就认识"均未在文中提及。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Li Xia ate lunch with her new friends every day... They helped her with homework... Gradually, Li Xia began to enjoy her new school life... 'But now I have real friends.'"

**【解析】** 李霞与新朋友共进午餐、一起学习、加入社团，逐渐享受新生活并称有了真正的朋友，可推知她在新朋友帮助下走出孤独、适应了新环境，A 项正确。B 项"成为班里第一"、C 项"决定离开"和 D 项"不再喜欢篮球"均与原文矛盾。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文及第⑤段 "Change can be difficult, but it can also bring new opportunities. A kind gesture (举动) from a stranger can turn into lasting friendship."

**【解析】** 文章通过李霞转学后结识新朋友的故事，传达转变虽难却会带来新机遇，陌生人的善意可能化为 lasting friendship，B 项正确。A 项"不应搬家"、C 项"篮球是交友最佳方式"和 D 项"只有转学生需要朋友"均非作者意图。故选 B。
