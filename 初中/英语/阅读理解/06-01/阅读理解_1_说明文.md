# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Tea is one of the most popular drinks in the world. It has a long history of more than 5,000 years. According to a Chinese legend (传说), tea was first discovered by Emperor Shen Nong when some tea leaves fell into his boiling water. Since then, tea has become an important part of Chinese culture and daily life.

② Tea was brought to many other countries over the centuries. In the 9th century, Japanese monks (僧侣) learned about tea from China and brought it back to Japan. They developed the famous Japanese tea ceremony. In the 17th century, tea reached Europe. The British began to enjoy afternoon tea and made it a special custom. Today, people in India, Turkey, the UK and many other countries drink tea every day.

③ There are different types of tea. Green tea, black tea, white tea and oolong tea all come from the same plant. The difference lies in how the leaves are processed (加工). Green tea keeps its natural color and taste. Black tea is fully fermented (发酵的) and has a stronger flavor. People choose different teas according to their taste and health needs.

④ Tea is not only a drink but also a symbol of peace and friendship. In China, serving tea to guests is a way to show respect. In Japan, the tea ceremony teaches people to find peace in simple things. In Britain, people often invite friends for afternoon tea to chat and relax. No matter where you go, a cup of tea can bring people together.

⑤ From ancient China to modern times, tea has crossed oceans and connected cultures. It reminds us that something simple can become something great when shared with others around the world.

**注释：** legend 传说；monk 僧侣；ferment 发酵

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, where was tea first discovered?
   A. In Japan.
   B. In Britain.
   C. In China, according to the legend about Emperor Shen Nong.
   D. In India.

2. According to paragraph ②, how did tea spread to different countries?
   A. Japanese monks brought it from China in the 9th century; the British started drinking it in the 17th century.
   B. Only Chinese people drank tea for thousands of years.
   C. Tea was invented in Europe.
   D. Tea was never popular outside China.

3. According to paragraph ③, what makes green tea different from black tea?
   A. They come from different plants.
   B. Black tea is healthier than green tea.
   C. Green tea is only drunk in China.
   D. Green tea keeps its natural color and taste; black tea is fully fermented and has a stronger flavor.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about tea?
   A. Tea is only used for drinking.
   B. The British invented the tea ceremony.
   C. In different cultures, tea is used to show respect, find peace, or bring people together.
   D. Tea is not popular in Asia anymore.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To introduce the history and culture of tea around the world.
   B. To explain how to make tea.
   C. To compare tea with coffee.
   D. To tell people to drink more tea.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "According to a Chinese legend (传说), tea was first discovered by Emperor Shen Nong when some tea leaves fell into his boiling water."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到根据中国传说，神农氏发现了茶，C 项正确。A 项"日本"、B 项"英国"和 D 项"印度"均非茶的发源地。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "In the 9th century, Japanese monks (僧侣) learned about tea from China and brought it back to Japan... In the 17th century, tea reached Europe. The British began to enjoy afternoon tea."

**【解析】** 原文说明 9 世纪日本僧侣从中国带回茶，17 世纪茶传入欧洲、英国人开始喝下午茶，A 项正确。B 项"只有中国人喝了几千年"与传播史矛盾；C 项"欧洲发明"错误；D 项"从未在海外流行"与原文不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Green tea keeps its natural color and taste. Black tea is fully fermented (发酵的) and has a stronger flavor."

**【解析】** 原文写出绿茶保持天然色味、红茶完全发酵且味更浓，D 项正确。A 项"来自不同植物"与 "all come from the same plant" 矛盾；B 项"红茶更健康"、C 项"绿茶只在中国喝"均未提及。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "In China, serving tea to guests is a way to show respect... In Japan, the tea ceremony teaches people to find peace... In Britain, people often invite friends for afternoon tea to chat and relax... a cup of tea can bring people together."

**【解析】** 文中列举中国以茶待客表尊重、日本茶道求静、英国下午茶聚会聊天，可推知不同文化中茶用于表达尊重、寻求宁静或凝聚人心，C 项正确。A 项"仅用于饮用"过于片面；B 项"英国发明茶道"与 "Japanese tea ceremony" 矛盾；D 项"亚洲不再流行茶"与全文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段茶的起源，第②段传播，第③段种类，第④段文化意义，第⑤段总结。

**【解析】** 文章介绍茶的历史、传播、种类及在世界各地的文化意义，A 项准确概括主旨。B 项"教泡茶"、C 项"比较茶与咖啡"和 D 项"劝人多喝茶"均非主旨。故选 A。
