# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Teaching Grandpa Zhang to Use a Smartphone

① Last summer, our school organized a volunteer activity. We would go to the community centre and help elderly people learn to use smartphones. I joined the activity because I wanted to do something useful. On the first day, I met Grandpa Zhang. He was about seventy years old with kind eyes. He held his new smartphone carefully and said, "My daughter bought this for me. She lives in another city. I want to video call her, but I don't know how." His voice was a little sad.

② I sat down next to him and showed him step by step. "First, open this app. Then find your daughter's name. Look, you tap here to start a video call." Grandpa Zhang watched carefully. He tried to tap the screen, but his finger touched the wrong place. The screen showed something else. He looked worried. "I'm sorry. I'm too old to learn this," he said. I smiled and said, "You're not too old. Let's try again. Everyone makes mistakes at first."

③ We practiced for an hour. Grandpa Zhang learned how to make a video call, send messages, and take photos. When he successfully made his first video call to his daughter, his face lit up with a big smile. "I can see her! She can see me too!" he said excitedly. His daughter waved at him through the screen. Grandpa Zhang's eyes became a little wet. "Thank you, young friend. You are very patient," he said to me.

④ In the following weeks, I continued to help Grandpa Zhang and other elderly people. They learned to use maps, read news online, and order food. Every time someone learned something new, they felt proud and happy. I also learned something important: helping others can bring real joy. The elderly people thanked us, but I felt I had received more than I had given.

⑤ That volunteer experience changed my view. Technology can connect people, but sometimes they need someone to show them the way. I am glad I could be that person for Grandpa Zhang and others. A little help can mean a lot to someone who needs it.

**注释：** volunteer 志愿者；community 社区；video call 视频通话；patient 有耐心的

---

### 选择题

1. Why did Grandpa Zhang want to learn to use a smartphone?
   A. He wanted to buy a new phone.
   B. He wanted to play games online.
   C. His school asked him to learn.
   D. His daughter lived in another city and he wanted to video call her.

2. What happened when Grandpa Zhang first tried to tap the screen?
   A. His finger touched the wrong place and the screen showed something else.
   B. He made a video call successfully.
   C. The phone turned off.
   D. His daughter called him.

3. What did Grandpa Zhang learn to do after practicing for an hour?
   A. Only how to make a video call.
   B. How to buy a new phone.
   C. How to make a video call, send messages, and take photos.
   D. How to repair the phone.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④?
   A. The writer only helped Grandpa Zhang.
   B. Elderly people cannot learn new things.
   C. The writer stopped volunteering after the first day.
   D. The writer felt he had received more than he had given from the volunteer experience.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Technology can connect people, and helping others learn to use it can bring joy to both sides.
   B. Smartphones are expensive and difficult to use.
   C. Young people should not help elderly people.
   D. Video calls are the most important use of smartphones.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "My daughter bought this for me. She lives in another city. I want to video call her, but I don't know how."

**【解析】** 张爷爷的女儿在另一座城市，他想和她视频通话但不知如何操作。D 项正确。A、B、C 项均未在文中提及。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He tried to tap the screen, but his finger touched the wrong place. The screen showed something else."

**【解析】** 张爷爷第一次点击时手指点错位置，屏幕显示了别的内容。A 项正确。B、C、D 项与原文描述不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Grandpa Zhang learned how to make a video call, send messages, and take photos."

**【解析】** 练习一小时后，张爷爷学会了视频通话、发信息和拍照。C 项正确。A 项过于片面；B、D 项未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "I also learned something important: helping others can bring real joy" 及 "I felt I had received more than I had given"

**【解析】** 作者在志愿活动中收获快乐，感到自己得到的比付出的更多。D 项正确。A 项与 "other elderly people" 矛盾；B 项与老人们成功学会新技能矛盾；C 项与 "continued to help" 矛盾。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第④段 "helping others can bring real joy" 及第⑤段 "Technology can connect people, but sometimes they need someone to show them the way" "A little help can mean a lot"

**【解析】** 文章通过学生帮助老人学用手机的经历，说明科技能连接人与人，而帮助他人学习使用科技能给双方带来快乐。A 项准确概括主旨。B、C、D 项均非文章重点。故选 A。
