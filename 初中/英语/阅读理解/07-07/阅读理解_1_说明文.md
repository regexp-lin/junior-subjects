# 阅读理解（说明文）

## AI in Our Daily Life

① Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is no longer something from science fiction movies. Today, AI is everywhere in our daily life. From the moment we wake up to when we go to sleep, we may use AI without even noticing it. Smartphones, smart speakers, and many apps we use every day rely on AI technology. It helps us in ways that were impossible just a few years ago.

② Many people start their day by asking a smart speaker for the weather. "What will the weather be like today?" The speaker answers in seconds because AI understands your words and finds the right information. When you take a photo with your phone, AI helps to make the picture clearer. When you type a message, AI suggests the next words you might want to say. These small things make our life easier and save us time.

③ In the classroom, AI can also help students learn. Some learning apps can give different exercises based on each student's level. If a student finds maths difficult, the app may offer more practice and explain the steps more slowly. Teachers use AI tools to check homework faster, so they have more time to help students face to face. AI does not replace teachers; it works with them.

④ AI also helps in our homes. Smart TVs suggest shows you might like. Robot vacuum cleaners (吸尘器) know how to avoid furniture and clean the floor by themselves. Some families use AI to turn on lights, control the air conditioning, or lock the door with just a voice command. Life at home becomes more convenient.

⑤ Of course, we should use AI wisely. It is a useful tool, but we still need to think for ourselves and protect our privacy. As AI keeps developing, it will bring more changes to our lives. Understanding how it works helps us use it better and prepare for the future.

**注释：** artificial 人工的；fiction 科幻；privacy 隐私；vacuum cleaner 吸尘器

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what can we learn about AI?
   A. AI is widely used in our daily life today.

   A. AI can completely replace teachers.

   B. AI will stop developing soon.

   A. The different uses of AI in our daily life and how we should use it wisely.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Today, AI is everywhere in our daily life." 及 "we may use AI without even noticing it"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明 AI 已融入日常生活、无处不在。A 项正确。B 项"仅存在于科幻电影"与 "no longer something from science fiction" 矛盾；C 项"从不注意"过于绝对；D 项"多年前就很流行"与 "just a few years ago" 不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When you take a photo with your phone, AI helps to make the picture clearer."

**【解析】** 文中明确提到用手机拍照时 AI 帮助让图片更清晰。B 项正确。A、C、D 项均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some learning apps can give different exercises based on each student's level" 及 "AI does not replace teachers; it works with them."

**【解析】** AI 根据学生水平提供不同练习，并与教师配合工作而非取代教师。C 项正确。A 项与 "does not replace teachers" 矛盾；B 项 "same exercises" 与 "different" 矛盾；D 项 "only to prepare lessons" 过于片面。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "we should use AI wisely" "we still need to think for ourselves and protect our privacy"

**【解析】** 末段提醒应理性使用 AI、独立思考、保护隐私。D 项正确。A 项与原文矛盾；B 项 "stop developing" 无依据；C 项 "not important" 与 "Understanding...helps us" 矛盾。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解

**【定位】** 全文：①AI 普及 ②日常生活应用 ③课堂应用 ④家庭应用 ⑤理性使用

**【解析】** 文章介绍 AI 在日常、课堂、家庭等多场景的应用，并强调应理性使用。A 项准确概括主旨。B、C、D 项均非文章重点。故选 A。
