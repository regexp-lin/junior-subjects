# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last October, our class went on a hiking trip to Green Mountain. Our teacher, Mr. Wang, divided us into groups of five. My group included my best friend Jack, two girls named Mei and Tina, and a quiet boy called Leo who rarely talked to anyone.

② The trail was supposed to take three hours. At first, everything was fun — we sang songs and took photos of the autumn leaves. But after about an hour, we realized we had taken a wrong turn. The path we were on became narrower (更窄) and was covered with fallen branches. "I think we're lost," Mei said, her voice shaking.

③ Everyone started to panic (恐慌) except Leo. He calmly took out a small compass (指南针) from his backpack. "My grandfather taught me how to use this," he said quietly. "The meeting point is to the south. If we follow this direction, we should find the main trail again." We all looked at each other in surprise — none of us had even thought to bring a compass.

④ Leo led the way. The path was difficult, with steep (陡峭的) rocks and slippery mud. At one point, Tina slipped and hurt her ankle (脚踝). Without hesitation (犹豫), Jack offered to carry her backpack, and Mei and I helped her walk slowly. Leo kept checking the compass and encouraging us: "We're getting closer. Don't give up."

⑤ After about forty minutes, we finally heard voices ahead — it was the other groups waiting at the meeting point! Mr. Wang was worried and was about to call for help. When we told him how Leo had guided us back, Mr. Wang smiled and said, "This is the best lesson of the day. Teamwork and staying calm can get you through any difficulty." From that day on, Leo was no longer the quiet kid nobody noticed. He became one of the most respected students in our class.

**注释：** trail 小径；compass 指南针；steep 陡峭的；slippery 滑的；ankle 脚踝；hesitation 犹豫

---

### 选择题

1. How many students were there in the writer's group?
   A. Three.
   B. Four.
   C. Five.
   D. Six.

2. What problem did the group face during the hike?
   A. They forgot to bring food and water.
   B. They took a wrong turn and got lost.
   C. Their teacher got injured on the trail.
   D. It started raining heavily and they had to stop.

3. How did Leo help the group find the right way?
   A. He called their teacher for help on his phone.
   B. He climbed a tall tree to look around.
   C. He used a compass his grandfather taught him to use.
   D. He followed the sound of birds to find the trail.

4. What can we infer about Leo's character from the story?
   A. He was careless and liked to show off.
   B. He was calm, well-prepared, and helpful.
   C. He was afraid of nature and wanted to go home.
   D. He was selfish and only cared about himself.

5. What lesson did Mr. Wang say the students learned?
   A. Always bring a phone when hiking.
   B. Never go hiking in the mountains in autumn.
   C. Teamwork and staying calm can help you through any difficulty.
   D. It is better to hike alone than in a group.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Mr. Wang divided us into groups of five. My group included my best friend Jack, two girls named Mei and Tina, and a quiet boy called Leo..."

**【解析】** 原文说老师把学生分成五人一组，作者的小组成员包括 Jack、Mei、Tina、Leo 和作者自己，共五人。A 项"三人"、B 项"四人"和 D 项"六人"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "we realized we had taken a wrong turn" 以及 Mei 所说的 "I think we're lost"

**【解析】** 原文明确提到他们走错了路并迷路了。A 项"忘带食物和水"文中未提及；C 项"老师受伤"不是他们小组的问题；D 项"下大雨"也未在文中出现。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "He calmly took out a small compass from his backpack. 'My grandfather taught me how to use this,' he said quietly."

**【解析】** Leo 冷静地从背包里拿出祖父教他使用的指南针来辨别方向。A 项"打电话给老师"文中未提及 Leo 使用电话；B 项"爬树"和 D 项"跟着鸟叫声"均属于无中生有。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "Everyone started to panic except Leo. He calmly took out a small compass..." 以及第④段 "Leo kept checking the compass and encouraging us: 'We're getting closer. Don't give up.'"

**【解析】** 当所有人恐慌时只有 Leo 保持冷静，他提前准备了指南针且不断鼓励同学，可以推断他沉着冷静、有准备且乐于助人。A 项"粗心爱炫耀"与原文"quietly""calmly"矛盾；C 项"害怕自然"与他主动带路矛盾；D 项"自私"与他帮助同伴的行为矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Teamwork and staying calm can get you through any difficulty."

**【解析】** 王老师的原话是"团队合作和保持冷静能帮你度过任何困难"。A 项"总是带手机"文中老师未提及；B 项"秋天不要爬山"属于无中生有；D 项"独自爬山比结伴好"与团队合作的教训完全相反。故选 C。
