# 阅读理解（议论文）

## **Should Teenagers Help with Housework?**

①"Clean your room!" "Wash the dishes!" "Take out the rubbish!" Many parents say these words every day. But should teenagers really help with housework? Some students think homework and exams are enough. They believe housework is a waste of time. I disagree. I think every teenager should do some housework, and here is why.

②First, housework teaches us life skills. When we grow up and leave home, we need to cook, wash clothes, and keep our room clean. If we never practice at home, we will struggle when we live alone. Learning these skills as teenagers makes the future easier. My cousin went to university last year. She could not even boil an egg. She had to learn everything from the beginning. It was hard for her.

③Second, doing housework is fair. Our parents work all day. When they come home, they still cook, clean, and take care of us. If we never help, they have to do everything. That is not fair. Sharing the work shows we care. Even small tasks like setting the table or folding clothes can make a difference.

④Third, housework can reduce stress. After sitting at a desk for hours, doing something physical can clear your mind. I always feel better after I tidy my room. It is like a break from studying. Some students say they have no time. But ten minutes a day is enough. Make your bed in the morning. Put your dishes in the sink after meals. These small habits do not take much time.

⑤Housework is not punishment. It is part of growing up. When we help at home, we learn responsibility, show love to our family, and prepare for the future. So yes, teenagers should help with housework. Start small, and you will see the benefits.

---

- fold /fəʊld/ v. 折叠
- responsibility /rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪləti/ n. 责任

## 题目

1. What is the writer's opinion on teenagers doing housework?
   A. It is a waste of time.
   B. Every teenager should do some housework.
   C. Only girls should do housework.
   D. Housework should be done only on weekends.

2. According to paragraph 2, why are life skills important?
   A. They help us get better grades.
   B. We will need them when we grow up and live alone.
   C. They are only for girls.
   D. Parents will pay us for doing them.

3. Why does the writer say doing housework is fair?
   A. Because we get paid.
   B. Because our parents work all day and we should share the work.
   C. Because teachers ask us to.
   D. Because it is easier than homework.

4. How can housework help with stress?
   A. It cannot help at all.
   B. Doing something physical after sitting can clear your mind.
   C. It makes us more stressed.
   D. We need to do housework for hours.

5. What does the writer suggest for students who say they have no time?
   A. Quit all hobbies.
   B. Ten minutes a day is enough—small habits like making the bed.
   C. Do housework instead of homework.
   D. Ask parents to do everything.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】观点态度题

【定位】第①段

【解析】
- 第①段明确写：“I think every teenager should do some housework.”
- A 是部分学生的观点，与作者相反。C、D 未提及。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第②段

【解析】
- 第②段写：长大后独自生活时需要会做饭、洗衣、整理；若从不练习，独居时会很吃力（“we will need to cook, wash clothes... If we never practice... we will struggle when we live alone”）。
- A、C、D 与原文不符。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第③段

【解析】
- 第③段写：父母整天工作，回家还要做饭、打扫、照顾我们；如果我们从不帮忙，他们就要承担全部，这不公平。
- A“会得到报酬”、C“老师要求”、D“比作业简单”均未提及。故答案为 B。

**4. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第④段

【解析】
- 第④段写：久坐后做些体力活动能放松头脑（“doing something physical can clear your mind”）。
- A、C、D 与原文相反或过度推断。故答案为 B。

**5. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第④段

【解析】
- 第④段写：“But ten minutes a day is enough. Make your bed in the morning. Put your dishes in the sink after meals.”
- A、C、D 与原文不符。故答案为 B。
