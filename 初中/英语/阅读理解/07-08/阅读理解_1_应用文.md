> **07-08 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Notice: Student Health Check-up**

② Dear students and parents,

③ Our school will organize a health check-up for all students next Monday and Tuesday (March 18-19). The check-up will help us understand your health condition and give advice for a healthier lifestyle. Please read the following information carefully.

④ **Time and place:** The check-up will take place in the school gym from 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Students in Grade Seven will come on Monday, and students in Grade Eight and Nine will come on Tuesday. Each class has a fixed time. Your class teacher will tell you the exact time.

⑤ **What to prepare:** Please wear loose clothes. Do not eat breakfast before the check-up if you need a blood test. Bring your student ID card. Parents need to sign the form and return it to school before Friday. If you have any health problems, please let the doctor know. We hope all students can take part in this important activity.

**注释：** check-up 体检；condition 状况；gym 体育馆；form 表格

---

### 选择题

1. When will the health check-up take place?
   A. Next Monday and Tuesday.
   B. This Friday and Saturday.
   C. Next Wednesday and Thursday.
   D. This weekend.

2. Where will the check-up be held?
   A. In the school library.
   B. In the school gym.
   C. In the hospital.
   D. In each classroom.

3. What should students bring on the day of the check-up?
   A. Breakfast and snacks.
   B. A signed form from parents.
   C. Their student ID card.
   D. Sports clothes only.

4. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Students in Grade Nine come on Monday.
   B. Parents must come to school for the check-up.
   C. The check-up finishes at 6:00 p.m.
   D. The school cares about students' health.

5. What is the main purpose of this notice?
   A. To inform students and parents about the health check-up arrangements.
   B. To ask students to join a sports meeting.
   C. To teach students how to stay healthy.
   D. To remind parents to pay for the check-up.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Our school will organize a health check-up for all students next Monday and Tuesday (March 18-19)."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明体检时间为下周一和周二。A项正确。B项本周五周六、C项下周三周四、D项本周末均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The check-up will take place in the school gym from 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m."

**【解析】** 文中清楚写明体检地点为学校体育馆。B项正确。A项图书馆、C项医院、D项各班教室均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Bring your student ID card."

**【解析】** 文中要求学生携带学生证。C项正确。A项早餐零食与"如需抽血勿吃早餐"矛盾；B项家长签名的表格需周五前交回，不是体检当天携带；D项只提到宽松衣服，未要求运动服。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "help us understand your health condition and give advice for a healthier lifestyle" 及第⑤段 "this important activity"

**【解析】** 通知强调体检有助于了解健康状况并给出健康建议，称之为"重要活动"，可推知学校重视学生健康。D项正确。A项九年级周一与原文"八九年级周二"矛盾；B项家长必须到场未提及；C项6点结束与原文4:30矛盾。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Notice: Student Health Check-up" 及全文内容

**【解析】** 文章是一则学生体检通知，向学生和家长说明时间、地点、年级安排及准备事项。A项准确概括写作意图。B项运动会、C项教授健康知识、D项提醒缴费均非主旨。故选A。
