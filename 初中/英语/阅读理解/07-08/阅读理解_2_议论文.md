# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should Smoking Be Banned in Public Places?**

② Smoking in public places has become a hot topic in many countries. Some people think smokers have the right to smoke anywhere, while others believe smoking should be banned in public to protect everyone's health. Let us look at both sides of the argument.

③ **Reasons for banning smoking in public:** First, secondhand smoke (二手烟) is harmful to people who do not smoke. Studies show that breathing in others' smoke can cause serious diseases, including heart problems and lung cancer. Children and old people are especially at risk. Second, smoking in places like buses, restaurants, and hospitals forces non-smokers to breathe dirty air. They did not choose to smoke, so why should they suffer? Third, smoke-free public places encourage smokers to quit. When they cannot smoke easily, some may decide to give up smoking for good. Fourth, banning smoking in public helps keep the air clean and makes cities more pleasant to live in.

④ **Reasons against a complete ban:** On the other hand, some people say that smoking is a personal choice and adults have the right to do what they want. They argue that creating special smoking areas in public places could be a better solution. In this way, smokers can smoke without disturbing others. However, many health experts point out that smoke can still travel from these areas and affect people nearby. Besides, workers who serve in smoking areas, such as waiters, may still face health risks.

⑤ **Conclusion:** In my opinion, smoking should be banned in most public places. The health of the public is more important than the freedom to smoke anywhere. Non-smokers, especially children, deserve to breathe clean air. Governments should pass laws to protect people from secondhand smoke. At the same time, we should help smokers quit by offering support and programs. A smoke-free environment benefits everyone in the end.

**注释：** secondhand smoke 二手烟；lung cancer 肺癌；quit 戒烟

---

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, who is especially at risk from secondhand smoke?
   A. Only adult smokers.
   B. People who work in restaurants.
   C. Children and old people.
   D. Government workers.

2. What is one reason for banning smoking in public places?
   A. Smoking areas are too expensive to build.
   B. Secondhand smoke harms non-smokers and can cause serious diseases.
   C. All smokers want to quit.
   D. Public places are too crowded.

3. What do some people suggest as an alternative to a complete ban?
   A. Building more hospitals for smokers.
   B. Creating special smoking areas in public places.
   C. Asking all smokers to stay at home.
   D. Making cigarettes more expensive.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The writer supports a complete ban on smoking in most public places.
   B. Health experts think smoking areas fully protect non-smokers.
   C. The writer thinks smokers' freedom is more important than public health.
   D. No country has ever banned smoking in public.

5. What is the writer's main purpose in writing this passage?
   A. To tell people how to quit smoking.
   B. To discuss whether smoking should be banned in public places and express support for the ban.
   C. To introduce different kinds of cigarettes.
   D. To ask governments to close all tobacco factories.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Children and old people are especially at risk."

**【解析】** 文中明确指出儿童和老年人尤其面临二手烟风险。C项正确。A项仅成年吸烟者、B项餐厅工作人员、D项政府工作人员均与原文"especially at risk"的表述不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Secondhand smoke (二手烟) is harmful to people who do not smoke. Studies show that breathing in others' smoke can cause serious diseases, including heart problems and lung cancer."

**【解析】** 文中列举支持禁烟的理由之一：二手烟危害非吸烟者，可致心脏病、肺癌等严重疾病。B项正确。A项吸烟区建造昂贵、C项所有吸烟者都想戒烟、D项公共场所太拥挤均非文中提到的理由。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "They argue that creating special smoking areas in public places could be a better solution."

**【解析】** 反对完全禁烟的一方提出，在公共场所设立专门的吸烟区可能是更好的解决办法。B项正确。A项建更多医院、C项让吸烟者待在家、D项提高 cigarette 价格均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, smoking should be banned in most public places. The health of the public is more important than the freedom to smoke anywhere."

**【解析】** 作者在结论中明确表态：大多数公共场所应禁烟，公众健康比吸烟自由更重要。可推知作者支持在公共场所实施禁烟。A项正确。B项专家认为吸烟区完全保护非吸烟者，与文中"smoke can still travel"矛盾；C项与作者观点相反；D项原文未提及。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Should Smoking Be Banned in Public Places?" 及全文结构（支持禁烟理由、反对理由、作者结论）

**【解析】** 文章围绕公共场所是否应禁烟展开讨论，呈现正反双方观点，最后作者表明支持禁烟立场。B项准确概括主旨和写作目的。A项如何戒烟、C项香烟种类、D项关闭烟草厂均非主旨。故选B。
