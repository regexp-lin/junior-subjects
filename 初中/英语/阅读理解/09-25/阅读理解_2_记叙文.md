> **09-25 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last summer, my family moved to a new city because of my father's job. I had to leave my old school and my friends. On the first day at the new school, I felt lonely. Everyone seemed to know each other. I sat alone at lunch and did not know what to say. "Will I ever make friends here?" I wondered.

② One afternoon, our class had a science project. We needed to work in pairs. I stood by the wall, hoping someone would choose me. A girl with short hair and a warm smile walked over. "Hi, I'm Mei. Want to be partners?" I nodded, feeling relieved (松了口气). During the project, we talked about our favourite books and hobbies. Mei loved drawing and I enjoyed reading. We found we both liked detective (侦探) stories. Time flew and we finished the project quickly.

③ After that, Mei introduced me to her friends — Lin, who played basketball, and Xia, who was good at maths. We sometimes had lunch together. Lin asked me to join the basketball team. I had never played much before, but I said yes. At first I was clumsy (笨拙的), but they did not laugh. They passed me the ball and cheered when I scored. Slowly, I began to feel that I belonged.

④ One weekend, Mei invited me to her home. Her mother made dumplings and we all helped. We shared stories about our old schools and our dreams. Mei said, "Moving is hard. I moved here two years ago. It gets better, I promise." Her words made me feel less alone. I realized that making new friends takes time and courage. You have to be willing to reach out and try.

⑤ Now I have good friends in the new city. I still miss my old friends, and we video call sometimes. But I have learned that home is not only a place — it is also the people who make you feel welcome. I am grateful for Mei and the others who opened their hearts to me.

**注释：** relieved 松了口气；detective 侦探；clumsy 笨拙的；belong 归属

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer's family move to a new city?
   A. Because they wanted a bigger house.
   B. Because of the father's job.
   C. Because the writer did not like the old school.
   D. Because of the weather.

2. How did the writer feel on the first day at the new school?
   A. Very happy and excited.
   B. Lonely; everyone seemed to know each other.
   C. Angry at the teacher.
   D. Tired of studying.

3. Who first became the writer's partner for the science project?
   A. Lin.
   B. Xia.
   C. Mei.
   D. The teacher.

4. What did Mei say to comfort the writer?
   A. "You should go back to your old school."
   B. "Moving is hard. I moved here two years ago. It gets better, I promise."
   C. "Nobody likes new students."
   D. "You should never make friends."

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Moving is always easy.
   B. Home is not only a place — it is also the people who make you feel welcome; making friends takes time and courage.
   C. Old friends are not important.
   D. New schools are always boring.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "my family moved to a new city because of my father's job"

**【解析】** 搬家是因父亲工作变动。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "On the first day at the new school, I felt lonely. Everyone seemed to know each other"

**【解析】** 开学第一天作者感到孤独，觉得大家彼此熟悉。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "A girl with short hair and a warm smile walked over. 'Hi, I'm Mei. Want to be partners?'"

**【解析】** 梅主动邀请作者做科学项目搭档。A、B、D 项与原文不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "'Moving is hard. I moved here two years ago. It gets better, I promise.'"

**【解析】** 梅以自己的搬家经历安慰作者，并承诺情况会好起来。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④、⑤段 "making new friends takes time and courage... home is not only a place — it is also the people who make you feel welcome"

**【解析】** 作者体会到：交朋友需要时间和勇气；家不仅是地点，更是让你感到被接纳的人。A、C、D 项与作者感悟矛盾。故选 B。
