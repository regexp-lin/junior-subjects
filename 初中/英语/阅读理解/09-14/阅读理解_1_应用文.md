> **09-14 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Mid-Autumn Festival School Party — Notice**

② Dear students and teachers,

③ Our school will hold a Mid-Autumn Festival party to celebrate this traditional Chinese festival. We hope you can join us for an evening of fun and culture.

④ **When and Where:**
- **Date:** September 15, 2025 (Monday)
- **Time:** 6:00 p.m. – 8:30 p.m.
- **Place:** School Gymnasium (体育馆)

⑤ **Program:**
| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 6:00 – 6:20 | Opening speech by our headmaster |
| 6:20 – 6:50 | Mooncake tasting (品尝月饼) — free mooncakes for everyone |
| 6:50 – 7:30 | Class performances: songs, dances, short plays |
| 7:30 – 8:00 | Lantern riddles (猜灯谜) — prizes for winners |
| 8:00 – 8:30 | Group photo and closing |

⑥ **What to Bring:**
- Your student ID card (for entrance)
- A reusable cup if you want drinks (we encourage green habits)
- Your smile and enthusiasm!

⑦ **Notes:**
- Please arrive by 5:50 p.m. so we can start on time.
- Keep the gym clean. Put waste in the bins.
- If your class has a performance, please report to the backstage (后台) by 6:30 p.m.
- Parents are welcome to watch. No ticket needed.

⑧ We look forward to seeing you there. Happy Mid-Autumn Festival!

Student Union  
September 10, 2025

**注释：** gymnasium 体育馆；riddle 谜语；backstage 后台

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Mid-Autumn Festival party be held?
   A. On September 10.
   B. On September 15.
   C. On September 20.
   D. On October 1.

2. Where will the party take place?
   A. In the classroom.
   B. In the dining hall.
   C. In the school gymnasium.
   D. In the playground.

3. What activity is scheduled from 6:50 to 7:30?
   A. Opening speech.
   B. Mooncake tasting.
   C. Class performances: songs, dances, short plays.
   D. Lantern riddles.

4. What should students bring for entrance?
   A. A mooncake.
   B. A reusable cup.
   C. Their student ID card.
   D. A ticket.

5. Which of the following is TRUE according to the notice?
   A. Students must pay for mooncakes.
   B. Parents need to buy tickets to watch.
   C. Students with performances should report to the backstage by 6:30 p.m.
   D. The party ends at 8:00 p.m.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Date: September 15, 2025 (Monday)"

**【解析】** 通知明确写出派对日期为9月15日。A项9月10日是通知发布日；C、D项为错误日期。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Place: School Gymnasium"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明地点为学校体育馆。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段表格 "6:50 – 7:30 | Class performances: songs, dances, short plays"

**【解析】** 6:50–7:30 时段安排的是班级表演（歌曲、舞蹈、短剧）。A、B、D项分别为其他时段活动。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Your student ID card (for entrance)"

**【解析】** 原文要求学生携带学生证入场。A项月饼、B项杯子、D项门票均非入场必需。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "If your class has a performance, please report to the backstage by 6:30 p.m."

**【解析】** 原文明确说有表演的班级需在6:30前到后台报到。A项"月饼需付费"与"free mooncakes"矛盾；B项"家长需购票"与"No ticket needed"矛盾；D项结束时间为8:30而非8:00。故选C。
