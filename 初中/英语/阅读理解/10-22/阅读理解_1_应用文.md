> **10-22 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Community Book Corner — Book Donation Activity**

① Dear residents,

② Our community is setting up a Book Corner in the activity room (Building 3, 1st floor). We hope to create a space where everyone can read and share books. To make this happen, we need your help!

**Book Donation**

③ We are collecting used books in good condition. We welcome: storybooks, science books, magazines, and children's books. Please do NOT donate: torn or dirty books, outdated textbooks (older than 5 years), or books with missing pages.

④ **When and where to donate:** Bring your books to the community office (Building 1) from October 28th to November 5th, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. You can also leave them at the Book Corner during opening hours (weekends 10:00 a.m.–4:00 p.m.). Each donor will receive a thank-you card.

⑤ The Book Corner will open on November 10th. Everyone is welcome to borrow books for free. No need to register in advance. For questions, contact Ms Li at 020-76543210 or community@greenfield.edu.cn.

Thank you for your support!

Greenfield Community Office  
October 20th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. Where will the Book Corner be?
   A. In Building 1.
   B. In the activity room, Building 3, 1st floor.
   C. In a school.
   D. In a library.

2. Which kind of book should NOT be donated?
   A. Storybooks in good condition.
   B. Torn or dirty books.
   C. Children's books.
   D. Science magazines.

3. When can residents donate books?
   A. Only on weekends.
   B. From October 28th to November 5th, 9:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m. at the office, or during Book Corner hours.
   C. Only at night.
   D. Only on November 10th.

4. What will donors receive?
   A. Money.
   B. New books.
   C. A thank-you card.
   D. A magazine.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. People must pay to borrow books.
   B. The community encourages sharing and reading; the Book Corner is free for everyone.
   C. Only children can use the Book Corner.
   D. Registration is required before borrowing.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "setting up a Book Corner in the activity room (Building 3, 1st floor)"
**【解析】** 图书角设在3号楼1层活动室。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Please do NOT donate: torn or dirty books, outdated textbooks... or books with missing pages"
**【解析】** 不要捐赠破损、脏污、过旧或缺页的图书。B项正确。A、C、D项为可捐赠类型。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "October 28th to November 5th, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m." 及 "during opening hours (weekends 10:00 a.m.–4:00 p.m.)"
**【解析】** 可在10月28日至11月5日办公时间到办公室捐赠，或于周末图书角开放时间捐赠。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Each donor will receive a thank-you card"
**【解析】** 每位捐赠者可获得一张感谢卡。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "create a space where everyone can read and share"；第⑤段 "borrow books for free... No need to register"
**【解析】** 社区意在营造共享阅读空间，借书免费且无需提前注册。B项正确。A、C、D项与通知内容矛盾。故选B。
