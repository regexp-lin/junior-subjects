# 阅读理解（议论文）

## Express Delivery and Food Delivery: Good or Bad for Our Life?

① In recent years, express delivery (快递) and food delivery (外卖) have become a common part of daily life in China. You can get almost anything delivered to your door—clothes, books, food, and more. Many people find this very convenient. However, others worry about the effects on our health, our habits and the environment. Let us look at both sides.

② **The benefits are clear.** First, delivery saves us a lot of time. Instead of going to a shop or a restaurant, we can order online and wait at home. This is especially helpful when we are busy with work or study. Second, we have more choices. We can order food from restaurants far away, or buy things from online stores in other cities. Third, delivery makes life easier for the elderly and people who cannot go out easily. They can get what they need without leaving home.

③ **Yet there are drawbacks.** One problem is health. Food delivery often means fast food—oily, salty and high in calories. Eating too much of it can lead to weight gain and other health issues. Another problem is that we move less. When everything is delivered, we seldom walk to shops or go out for meals. A third worry is the environment. Delivery uses many boxes, bags and plastic containers, which creates a lot of waste.

④ **What should we do?** We do not need to give up delivery completely. It is a useful tool when we are short of time. But we should use it wisely. When ordering food, try to choose healthier options. Do not forget to exercise—walk, run or play sports regularly. When we receive packages, we can reuse or recycle the boxes. Every small action can help.

⑤ In short, express delivery and food delivery bring both convenience and challenges. The key is to enjoy the benefits while avoiding the harms. Use them when necessary, but do not let them control our life.

**注释：** delivery 配送；container 容器；recycle 回收利用

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, what is one advantage of delivery mentioned?
   A. It saves time and offers more choices; it also helps the elderly.
   B. It makes people lazy.
   C. It reduces traffic.
   D. It makes all food healthier.

2. What health problem might food delivery cause?
   A. It makes people taller.
   B. Eating too much fast food from delivery can lead to weight gain and health issues.
   C. It improves memory.
   D. It helps people sleep better.

3. Why does the passage say "we move less" when we use delivery a lot?
   A. Because we prefer to run instead of walk.
   B. Because we are too busy to move.
   C. Because we seldom walk to shops or go out for meals—everything comes to our door.
   D. Because delivery workers move for us.

4. What does the writer suggest we do when using delivery?
   A. Give it up completely.
   B. Order as much as possible.
   C. Choose healthier food, exercise regularly, and reuse or recycle packaging.
   D. Never order food, only other things.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Delivery is always bad and should be stopped.
   B. Delivery workers have an easy job.
   C. Only young people should use delivery.
   D. Delivery brings convenience and challenges; we should use it wisely and avoid its harms.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "First, delivery saves us a lot of time... Second, we have more choices... Third, delivery makes life easier for the elderly and people who cannot go out easily"

**【解析】** 文中列举三大好处：省时、选择更多、便利老人及不便外出者。A项概括准确。B项使人变懒为弊端；C、D项非文中所述优势。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Food delivery often means fast food—oily, salty and high in calories. Eating too much of it can lead to weight gain and other health issues"

**【解析】** 外卖多为高油高盐高热量快餐，过量食用可能导致体重增加和健康问题。B项正确。A、C、D项均与健康危害描述不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "When everything is delivered, we seldom walk to shops or go out for meals"

**【解析】** 一切送货上门时，人们很少步行去商店或外出就餐，因而运动减少。C项准确推理。A、B、D项均非"we move less"的原因。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "try to choose healthier options... Do not forget to exercise—walk, run or play sports regularly... we can reuse or recycle the boxes"

**【解析】** 作者建议：选择更健康的食物、定期运动、重复利用或回收包装盒。C项正确。A项完全放弃与"We do not need to give up"矛盾；B、D项与建议不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第①段引入话题、第②③段利弊分析、第④⑤段"use it wisely"及"In short, express delivery and food delivery bring both convenience and challenges. The key is to enjoy the benefits while avoiding the harms"

**【解析】** 文章讨论快递外卖的利弊，指出应智慧使用、趋利避害。D项准确概括主旨。A项全面否定、B项讨论送餐员、C项限制使用对象均偏离主旨。故选D。
