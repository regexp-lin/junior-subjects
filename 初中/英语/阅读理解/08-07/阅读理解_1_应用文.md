# 阅读理解（应用文）

## School Anniversary Events — Schedule and Notice

① Our school will celebrate its 80th anniversary on Saturday, October 12th. All students, teachers and parents are welcome to join us. Here is the schedule for the day.

② **Morning Program (9:00 a.m.—12:00 p.m.):** The opening ceremony will begin at 9:00 a.m. in the school hall. The headmaster will give a speech, and outstanding alumni (校友) will share their stories. After that, there will be a photo exhibition about the school's history in the library. You can learn how our school has developed over the past 80 years.

③ **Lunch (12:00—1:30 p.m.):** Free lunch will be provided in the dining hall. Please bring your student card or invitation ticket. Vegetarian options are also available.

④ **Afternoon Activities (2:00—5:00 p.m.):** There will be different activities in different areas. In the sports ground, you can watch or join in basketball and football matches. In the art room, students will show their paintings and handicrafts. In the music room, the school choir (合唱团) will perform. You may choose any activity you like.

⑤ **Evening Party (6:30—8:30 p.m.):** The day will end with an evening party in the hall. There will be singing, dancing and short plays. Please arrive before 6:20 p.m. to find a seat. We look forward to celebrating with you!

**注释：** alumni 校友；choir 合唱团

---

### 选择题

1. When will the school anniversary take place?
   A. On a weekday in October.
   B. On Saturday, October 12th.
   C. On Sunday in September.
   D. On the first day of the new term.

2. Where can visitors learn about the school's history?
   A. In the sports ground.
   B. In the dining hall.
   C. In the library.
   D. In the music room.

3. What is provided for lunch on that day?
   A. Only vegetarian food.
   B. No food at all.
   C. Free lunch in the dining hall; vegetarian options available.
   D. Food that visitors must pay for.

4. What can visitors do in the afternoon?
   A. Only watch basketball matches.
   B. Only listen to the choir.
   C. Choose from basketball/football, art shows, or choir performance.
   D. They must stay in the hall.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Only students can attend the anniversary.
   B. The evening party starts at 6:30 p.m., so visitors need to arrive before that to get a seat.
   C. There are no activities in the morning.
   D. The headmaster will not attend the ceremony.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Our school will celebrate its 80th anniversary on Saturday, October 12th"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明校庆于10月12日周六举行。B项正确。A项未限定具体日期；C项周日、九月均错误；D项开学第一天未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "there will be a photo exhibition about the school's history in the library. You can learn how our school has developed over the past 80 years"

**【解析】** 校史照片展览在图书馆举行，参观者可了解学校80年发展。C项正确。A、B、D项地点均与校史展览不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Free lunch will be provided in the dining hall... Vegetarian options are also available"

**【解析】** 食堂提供免费午餐，另有素食选项。C项准确。A项仅素食与原文不符；B项无食物错误；D项需付费与"Free lunch"矛盾。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "In the sports ground... basketball and football matches. In the art room... paintings and handicrafts. In the music room, the school choir will perform. You may choose any activity you like"

**【解析】** 下午有体育场球赛、艺术室展览、音乐室合唱表演，可自由选择。C项正确。A、B项仅列单项；D项须留在大厅与"You may choose"矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The day will end with an evening party in the hall... Please arrive before 6:20 p.m. to find a seat"

**【解析】** 晚会6:30开始，建议6:20前到场找座，可推知需提前到达才能找到座位。B项正确。A项与"All students, teachers and parents are welcome"矛盾；C项与第②段上午活动矛盾；D项与第②段"headmaster will give a speech"矛盾。故选B。
