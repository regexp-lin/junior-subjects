# 阅读理解（说明文）

## Recyclable Plastics and Plastic Pollution

① Plastic is everywhere in our daily life. We use it for bottles, bags, and packaging. It is cheap and easy to make. But plastic also brings a serious problem—plastic pollution. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste end up in the ocean and on the land. This harms animals, plants, and our environment.

② Not all plastics are the same. Some can be recycled, but others cannot. Recyclable plastics often have a small number on them. For example, bottles and food containers usually carry the numbers 1 or 2. These plastics can be collected and turned into new products. However, many people still throw recyclable plastic into the wrong bins. When plastic is mixed with other waste, it becomes harder to recycle.

③ Plastic pollution is dangerous because plastic does not break down easily. It can stay in the environment for hundreds of years. In the ocean, plastic harms sea animals. Fish and birds may eat small pieces of plastic by mistake. Some animals get caught in plastic bags and cannot move. Scientists say that by 2050, there might be more plastic than fish in the ocean if we do nothing.

④ What can we do to help? First, reduce the amount of plastic we use. Bring your own bags when you go shopping. Use a reusable water bottle instead of buying bottled water. Second, recycle correctly. Learn which plastics can be recycled in your city and put them in the right place. Third, never throw plastic on the ground or into rivers. Small actions can make a big difference.

⑤ Protecting our planet is everyone's job. When we choose to reduce, reuse, and recycle plastic, we are taking steps toward a cleaner and healthier world for future generations.

**注释：** recyclable 可回收的；packaging 包装；reusable 可重复使用的

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "But plastic also brings a serious problem—plastic pollution... This harms animals, plants, and our environment."

**【解析】** 塑料带来严重问题——塑料污染，危害动物、植物和我们的环境。B 项正确。A 项太贵、C 项不能制瓶、D 项使生活太轻松均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Recyclable plastics often have a small number on them... bottles and food containers usually carry the numbers 1 or 2. These plastics can be collected and turned into new products."

**【解析】** 可回收塑料通常带数字，瓶子和食品容器常标 1 或 2。A 项正确。B 项无数字、C 项所有塑料袋、D 项混合垃圾均不准确。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Plastic pollution is dangerous because plastic does not break down easily. It can stay in the environment for hundreds of years. In the ocean, plastic harms sea animals."

**【解析】** 塑料难以分解，可在环境中存留数百年，海洋中的塑料危害海洋动物。C 项正确。A、B、D 项均与原文矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Bring your own bags when you go shopping. Use a reusable water bottle instead of buying bottled water."

**【解析】** 作者建议购物自带袋子、使用可重复使用的水瓶代替购买瓶装水。A 项正确。B、C、D 项均为错误做法。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第④⑤段

**【解析】** 文章介绍了塑料污染问题及其危害，并提出减少、重复使用、回收等建议，旨在号召读者参与环保。D 项准确概括写作目的。A、B、C 项曲解主旨。故选 D。
