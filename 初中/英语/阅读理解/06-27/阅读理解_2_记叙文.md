# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Helping a Lost Tourist Find the Way

① Last Saturday afternoon, Li Ming was walking home from the English club. When he passed the subway station, he saw a foreign man standing by the street with a map in his hand. The man looked worried. He kept looking at the map and then at the buildings around him. Li Ming realized that the man might be lost.

② Li Ming walked up to him and said, "Excuse me, do you need help?" The man looked surprised at first, but then he smiled. "Yes, please! I'm looking for the City Museum. I've been walking for half an hour but I can't find it," he said. His English had a strong accent, but Li Ming could understand him. Li Ming had learned about the City Museum in his geography class. It was only two blocks away.

③ "It's not far. Go straight along this street, turn left at the first crossing, and you'll see a big white building. That's the City Museum," Li Ming said slowly and clearly. The man repeated the directions to make sure he understood. "Straight, left at the first crossing, white building. Got it! Thank you so much!" He shook Li Ming's hand warmly.

④ Before the man left, he asked, "Are you a student? Your English is very good." Li Ming felt a little shy but proud. "I'm in junior high. I practice English every day because I want to communicate with people from different countries," he replied. The man smiled again and said, "You're doing a great job. Keep it up!" Then he waved goodbye and walked away.

⑤ On his way home, Li Ming thought about what had happened. Helping others made him feel happy. He also realized that learning English was not just for exams—it could help him connect with people around the world. That day, a simple act of kindness became a special memory for both of them.

**注释：** subway 地铁；accent 口音；communicate 交流

### 选择题

1. Where did Li Ming see the foreign man?
   A. Near the subway station.
   B. At the English club.
   C. At the City Museum.
   D. In his geography class.

2. What was the foreign man looking for?
   A. The City Museum.
   B. The subway station.
   C. The English club.
   D. A white building.

3. How did Li Ming know where the City Museum was?
   A. He had been there many times.
   B. He learned about it in his geography class.
   C. The foreign man told him.
   D. He read it on the map.

4. What did the foreign man think of Li Ming's English?
   A. He thought it was not good enough.
   B. He thought Li Ming spoke too slowly.
   C. He said Li Ming's English was very good.
   D. He could not understand Li Ming at all.

5. What did Li Ming learn from this experience?
   A. Geography is more important than English.
   B. Learning English is only for passing exams.
   C. Helping others and learning English can help him connect with people around the world.
   D. Foreign tourists are always lost.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "When he passed the subway station, he saw a foreign man standing by the street with a map in his hand."

**【解析】** Li Ming 在地铁站附近见到这位外国男士。B 项正确。A 项英语俱乐部、C 项市博物馆、D 项地理课均不是见面地点。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Yes, please! I'm looking for the City Museum."

**【解析】** 外国男士在找市博物馆。B 项正确。A、C、D 项均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Li Ming had learned about the City Museum in his geography class."

**【解析】** Li Ming 在地理课上了解过市博物馆。B 项正确。A 项去过多次、C 项外国人告知、D 项在地图上看均未提及。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Are you a student? Your English is very good."

**【解析】** 外国男士称赞 Li Ming 的英语很好。C 项正确。A、B、D 项均与原文相反。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Helping others made him feel happy. He also realized that learning English was not just for exams—it could help him connect with people around the world."

**【解析】** Li Ming 体会到帮助他人令人快乐，且学英语不仅为考试，还能与世界各地的人交流。C 项准确概括。A 项未提及；B、D 项与主旨相反。故选 C。
