# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## A Speech I Will Never Forget

① It was the last day of junior high school. The graduation ceremony was held in the school hall. Hundreds of students, teachers, and parents sat quietly, waiting for the speeches. I felt both excited and a little sad. Excited because we were about to start a new journey. Sad because we would say goodbye to our friends and teachers.

② Then it was Tom's turn. Tom was one of the best students in our class. He walked slowly to the stage. His hands were shaking. He took a deep breath and began to speak. "Three years ago, I came to this school as a shy boy. I was afraid to talk to anyone. I thought I would spend the whole three years alone," he said. His voice was soft but clear. The hall became very quiet. Everyone listened carefully.

③ "But on my first day, a boy named Jack sat next to me. He smiled and said, 'Hi, I'm Jack. Want to be friends?' That simple question changed everything. Jack introduced me to other classmates. We studied together, laughed together, and helped each other when things were difficult. Because of him, I learned that friendship can turn the darkest days into the brightest ones."

④ Tom's eyes became red. "Today, we are leaving this school. We may go to different high schools. But I want to say to every one of you: thank you. Thank you for being my friends. Thank you for the memories we shared. Wherever we go, these memories will stay in our hearts forever." When he finished, the whole hall stood up and clapped. Many students had tears in their eyes, including me.

⑤ That speech made me understand something important. School is not only a place to learn. It is also a place where we find friends who stay with us for life. I will never forget Tom's words and the warm feeling in the hall that day.

**注释：** ceremony 典礼；stage 舞台；memory 记忆；clapped 鼓掌

### 选择题

1. Where was the graduation ceremony held?
   A. In the playground.
   B. In the school hall.
   C. In the classroom.
   D. In the library.

2. What was Tom like when he first came to the school?
   A. He was confident and outgoing.
   B. He was the best student at once.
   C. He had many friends already.
   D. He was shy and afraid to talk to others.

3. What did Jack do on Tom's first day?
   A. He smiled and asked Tom if he wanted to be friends.
   B. He ignored Tom completely.
   C. He helped Tom with his homework only.
   D. He introduced Tom to the teachers.

4. How did the audience react when Tom finished his speech?
   A. They left the hall quickly.
   B. They asked Tom more questions.
   C. They stood up and clapped; many had tears in their eyes.
   D. They felt bored and sleepy.

5. What did the writer learn from Tom's speech?
   A. School is a place to find friends who may stay with us for life.
   B. School is only for studying.
   C. Friends are not important.
   D. Graduation means forgetting everything.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The graduation ceremony was held in the school hall."

**【解析】** 毕业典礼在学校礼堂举行。B 项正确。A 项操场、C 项教室、D 项图书馆均未提及。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Three years ago, I came to this school as a shy boy. I was afraid to talk to anyone."

**【解析】** Tom 初入学时害羞，不敢与任何人说话。D 项正确。A 项自信外向、B 项立刻成为最好学生、C 项已有许多朋友均与原文相反。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "a boy named Jack sat next to me. He smiled and said, 'Hi, I'm Jack. Want to be friends?'"

**【解析】** Jack 对 Tom 微笑并问 "Want to be friends?"。A 项正确。B 项忽视、C 项仅辅导作业、D 项介绍给老师均不准确。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "When he finished, the whole hall stood up and clapped. Many students had tears in their eyes, including me."

**【解析】** Tom 演讲结束后，全场起立鼓掌，许多学生眼中含泪。C 项准确概括听众反应。A、B、D 项均与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "That speech made me understand something important. School is not only a place to learn. It is also a place where we find friends who stay with us for life."

**【解析】** 作者从 Tom 的演讲中领悟到：学校不仅是学习之地，也是找到一生挚友的地方。A 项准确概括。B 项 "仅学习" 过于狭隘；C、D 项与主旨相反。故选 A。
