# 阅读理解（记叙文）

# The Secret Letters in the Classroom

① It was a normal Monday morning. Emma walked into her classroom as usual and sat down at her desk. As she opened her textbook, something fell out — a small white envelope with her name written on it in neat handwriting. Emma was surprised. Who had left this here?

② She looked around the classroom. No one seemed to notice. Emma slowly opened the envelope and pulled out a note. It said: "Dear Emma, I believe you can do great things. Don't give up on your dreams. — A friend." Emma could not believe her eyes. She had been feeling worried about the upcoming exam, and this note made her feel warm and hopeful inside.

③ Over the next few days, Emma found more letters on her desk. Sometimes they appeared before class, sometimes after the break. Each note contained kind words of encouragement. "You are smarter than you think." "Every expert was once a beginner." Emma began to look forward to finding these mysterious messages. They gave her the courage to study harder and believe in herself.

④ One afternoon, Emma stayed late to finish her homework. As she packed her bag, she saw her teacher, Mr. Wang, walking towards her desk. He had a small pile of envelopes in his hand. Emma's heart beat fast. Could it be? Mr. Wang smiled and said, "I see you've found my little secret. I wanted to help you feel more confident. I write these notes for all my students who need a little extra support."

⑤ Emma was moved to tears. She had never expected that her strict but caring teacher would do something so thoughtful. From that day on, Emma not only worked harder in class but also tried to encourage others when they felt down. A simple act of kindness had changed her whole attitude towards learning.

**注释：** envelope 信封；encouragement 鼓励；confident 自信的；support 支持

---

### 选择题

1. What did Emma find when she opened her textbook?
   A. A small white envelope with her name.
   B. A photo of her family.
   C. A piece of homework.
   D. A pen from a classmate.

2. How did Emma feel after reading the first note?
   A. Angry and confused.
   B. Warm and hopeful.
   C. Worried and tired.
   D. Bored and sleepy.

3. When did Emma discover who had written the letters?
   A. On the first day when she found the envelope.
   B. When she asked her classmates.
   C. One afternoon when she stayed late and saw Mr. Wang.
   D. During a parent-teacher meeting.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Mr. Wang?
   A. He cared about his students and wanted to encourage them.
   B. He only wrote notes for Emma.
   C. He was angry that Emma found his secret.
   D. He did not like teaching.

5. What change did Emma have after learning the truth?
   A. She decided to change her teacher.
   B. She began to encourage others who felt down.
   C. She stopped studying hard.
   D. She asked for more letters from Mr. Wang.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "As she opened her textbook, something fell out — a small white envelope with her name written on it in neat handwriting."

**【解析】** 原文明确描写艾玛打开课本时掉出一个写着她名字的小白信封。A 项正确。B 项"家人照片"、C 项"作业"和 D 项"同学的钢笔"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "She had been feeling worried about the upcoming exam, and this note made her feel warm and hopeful inside."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明艾玛读完第一张纸条后感到温暖且充满希望。B 项正确。A 项"生气困惑"、C 项"担忧疲倦"和 D 项"无聊困倦"均与原文情感色彩不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "One afternoon, Emma stayed late to finish her homework... she saw her teacher, Mr. Wang, walking towards her desk. He had a small pile of envelopes in his hand."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述艾玛某天下午留下来写作业时，看到王老师拿着一摞信封走向她的课桌，从而发现了真相。C 项正确。A、B、D 项所述时间或方式均与原文不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 Mr. Wang 的话 "I wanted to help you feel more confident. I write these notes for all my students who need a little extra support."

**【解析】** 王老师说他写这些纸条是为了让学生更自信，并为所有需要额外支持的学生写。由此可推知他关心学生、想要鼓励他们。A 项正确。B 项"只为艾玛写"与"all my students"矛盾；C 项"生气"与文中他"smiled"矛盾；D 项"不喜欢教书"无依据。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "From that day on, Emma not only worked harder in class but also tried to encourage others when they felt down."

**【解析】** 原文说明艾玛此后不仅更努力学习，还会在他人低落时鼓励他们。D 项准确概括了这一变化。A 项"换老师"、B 项"不再努力"和 C 项"向老师要更多信"均与原文相反或无关。故选 D。
