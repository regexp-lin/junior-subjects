# 阅读理解（议论文）

## Should Teenagers Do Part-time Jobs?

①Nowadays, more and more teenagers want to do part-time jobs. Some work in shops, restaurants or deliver newspapers. Their parents and teachers often ask: Is it good for young people to work while they are still students? Different people have different ideas.

②**Those who support it** say that part-time jobs can teach teenagers many useful skills. They learn how to manage time—they must finish homework and go to work. They learn to communicate with different people, such as customers and workmates. They also learn the value of money. When they earn money by themselves, they understand that money does not come easily. Some teenagers use the money they earn to buy books or save for the future. Doing a job can also build their confidence and make them more independent.

③**Those who worry about it** argue that study should come first. Teenagers have a lot of homework and exams. If they spend too much time working, they may feel tired and their grades may drop. Besides, some jobs may be too hard or even dangerous for young people. They may meet difficult customers or work long hours. There is also a risk that teenagers might care more about money than about learning. Schools and parents need to make sure that work does not take the place of study.

④**What is the balance?** Experts suggest that if teenagers do part-time jobs, the work should be light and safe. They should not work more than a few hours a week during the school term. The job should not affect their sleep or study time. Parents and teachers should talk with teenagers about their work and offer support. In the holidays, they may work a bit more, but rest and family time are still important.

⑤In conclusion, part-time jobs can be both helpful and harmful. The key is to choose the right kind of job and limit the working hours. When teenagers work in a proper way, they can gain experience and skills without hurting their studies. When it goes too far, it may cause problems. So, the answer is: it depends on the situation.

**注释：** deliver 递送；value 价值；earn 赚取；confidence 自信；independent 独立的；balance 平衡；affect 影响

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, what is one benefit of teenagers doing part-time jobs?
   A. They can skip homework.
   B. They learn time management, communication, the value of money, and may become more confident and independent.
   C. They never get tired.
   D. They do not need to go to school.

2. Why do some people worry about teenagers doing part-time jobs?
   A. Because teenagers earn too much money.
   B. Because study may be affected; some jobs may be hard or dangerous; teenagers might care more about money than learning.
   C. Because all jobs are dangerous.
   D. Because parents do not like it.

3. What do experts suggest about teenagers and part-time work?
   A. Teenagers should never work.
   B. The job should be light and safe; work should not exceed a few hours a week during the school term; it should not affect sleep or study.
   C. Teenagers should work full-time.
   D. Only boys should work.

4. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Part-time jobs are always bad for teenagers.
   B. Whether part-time jobs are good or not depends on the situation—the right job and limited hours can help; too much can cause problems.
   C. All teenagers must do part-time jobs.
   D. Parents should choose jobs for teenagers.

5. What is the writer's main point about teenagers and part-time jobs?
   A. Teenagers should never work.
   B. Part-time jobs can be helpful when done properly, but harmful when overdone; the key is balance.
   C. Only holidays are good for part-time jobs.
   D. All teenagers should work full-time.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "part-time jobs can teach teenagers many useful skills... learn how to manage time... learn to communicate... learn the value of money... build their confidence and make them more independent"

**【解析】** 文中列举多项好处：时间管理、与人沟通、认识金钱价值、增强自信与独立性。B项概括正确。A、C、D项均与好处描述不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "study should come first... If they spend too much time working... grades may drop... some jobs may be too hard or even dangerous... teenagers might care more about money than about learning"

**【解析】** 反对者担心：学业受影响、过度工作导致成绩下降、工作可能艰苦或危险、可能更看重金钱而非学习。B项正确。A、C、D项均非文中所述的担忧。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "the work should be light and safe. They should not work more than a few hours a week during the school term. The job should not affect their sleep or study time"

**【解析】** 专家建议：工作应轻松安全、学期内每周不超过几小时、不影响睡眠和学习。B项正确。A完全反对、C全职工作、D仅男生可工作均与专家建议不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Part-time jobs can be both helpful and harmful. The key is to choose the right kind of job and limit the working hours... it depends on the situation"

**【解析】** 兼职有利有弊，关键在于选择合适工作、控制工时；好坏取决于具体情况。可推知B项。A、C、D项均与结论矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第⑤段总结

**【解析】** 文章讨论青少年是否应做兼职，分析利弊后指出：适度、得当的兼职有益，过度则有害，关键在于平衡。B项准确概括主旨。A、C、D项均偏离作者观点。故选B。
