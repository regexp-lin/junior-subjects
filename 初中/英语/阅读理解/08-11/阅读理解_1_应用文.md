# 阅读理解（应用文）

## Class Bulletin Board Competition Notice

①Dear students,

Our school will hold a Class Bulletin Board Competition next month. All classes in Grade 7, 8 and 9 are welcome to take part. Here are the details.

②**Theme:** "My Beautiful School Life" — Show your class's best moments, such as sports day, art shows, study trips, or friendship stories. Be creative!

③**Requirements:**
- Each class should prepare one bulletin board (板报) in your classroom or the corridor (走廊) outside.
- Size: at least 2 metres wide and 1 metre high.
- You may use photos, drawings, handwritten articles and coloured paper.
- The work must be done by students. Teachers can give advice but cannot do the work for you.
- Finish your board by **November 15th**. Late entries will not be accepted.

④**Judging:** A team of teachers and student representatives will visit each class from November 18th to 20th. They will check the design, content and creativity. The results will be announced on November 25th. The top six classes will receive certificates (证书) and a small prize. The best three will have their photos displayed in the school hall.

⑤If you have any questions, please contact Ms. Wang in the Student Office before November 10th. We look forward to seeing your wonderful work!

School Student Union  
October 28th

**注释：** bulletin board 板报；corridor 走廊；certificate 证书；representative 代表

---

### 选择题

1. Who can take part in the competition?
   A. Only Grade 9 students.
   B. All classes in Grade 7, 8 and 9.
   C. Only students who like drawing.
   D. Teachers and students together.

2. What is the theme of the bulletin board competition?
   A. Our school building.
   B. My beautiful school life.
   C. Famous people.
   D. Science and technology.

3. By when must each class finish their bulletin board?
   A. November 10th.
   B. November 15th.
   C. November 18th.
   D. November 25th.

4. Who will judge the competition?
   A. Only teachers.
   B. Only student representatives.
   C. A team of teachers and student representatives.
   D. Parents and teachers.

5. What will the top six classes get?
   A. Money only.
   B. Certificates and a small prize; the best three will have photos displayed in the hall.
   C. A trip to another school.
   D. Nothing.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "All classes in Grade 7, 8 and 9 are welcome to take part"

**【解析】** 七八九年级所有班级均可参加。B项正确。A仅九年级、C仅喜欢绘画的学生、D教师与学生共同作为参赛单位均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Theme: 'My Beautiful School Life'"

**【解析】** 主题为"我的美好校园生活"。B项正确。A、C、D项均与主题不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Finish your board by **November 15th**. Late entries will not be accepted"

**【解析】** 板报须于11月15日前完成，逾期不收。B项正确。A咨询截止、C评审开始、D结果公布均非完成截止日。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "A team of teachers and student representatives will visit each class"

**【解析】** 由教师和学生代表组成的团队进行评比。C项正确。A仅教师、B仅学生代表、D家长与教师均与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The top six classes will receive certificates and a small prize. The best three will have their photos displayed in the school hall"

**【解析】** 前六名获得证书和小奖品，前三名照片将在学校大厅展示。B项正确。A、C、D项均与奖励内容不符。故选B。
