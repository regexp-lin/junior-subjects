# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **City Public Bike — User Guide**

② Welcome to the city public bike service! Public bikes are a green and convenient way to travel short distances. Follow this guide to use them safely and correctly.

③ **How to Rent a Bike:**

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Download the "GreenRide" app or use your transport card |
| 2 | Find a bike station (there are over 200 stations across the city) |
| 3 | Scan the QR code on the bike or tap your card at the station |
| 4 | Take the bike and ride (the first 60 minutes are free!) |

④ **How to Return a Bike:**
- Return the bike to any station (you do not need to return it to the same place).
- Push the bike firmly into the lock until you hear a beep (蜂鸣声).
- Check the app or screen to make sure the return is complete.
- If the station is full, find another station nearby. Do not leave the bike anywhere else.

⑤ **Rules and Tips:**
- You must be 16 or older to rent a bike.
- Wear a helmet (头盔) for safety. Children under 12 cannot use public bikes.
- Do not ride on sidewalks (人行道). Use bike lanes or the road.
- One bike per person. Do not carry another person on the bike.
- Bikes are for short trips. If you keep a bike for more than 24 hours, you may get a fine (罚款).

⑥ **Pricing:**
- First 60 minutes: Free
- 61–120 minutes: ¥1 per 30 minutes
- Over 120 minutes: ¥2 per 30 minutes

⑦ Need help? Call 400-123-4567 or visit www.greenride.city.gov. Enjoy your ride!

**注释：** beep 蜂鸣声；helmet 头盔；sidewalk 人行道；fine 罚款

**Questions:**

1. What is the first step to rent a public bike?
   A. Find a bike station.
   B. Download the "GreenRide" app or use your transport card.
   C. Scan the QR code on the bike.
   D. Take the bike and ride.

2. How long is the free ride time?
   A. 30 minutes.
   B. 45 minutes.
   C. 60 minutes.
   D. 90 minutes.

3. What should you do when returning a bike?
   A. Return it only to the same station where you got it.
   B. Push the bike firmly into the lock until you hear a beep, and check that the return is complete.
   C. Leave the bike anywhere when the station is full.
   D. Give the bike to another person.

4. Who can rent a public bike?
   A. Anyone of any age.
   B. Only people over 18.
   C. People aged 16 or older.
   D. Only students.

5. If you keep a bike for 90 minutes, how much will you pay (excluding the free period)?
   A. ¥0.
   B. ¥1.
   C. ¥2.
   D. ¥3.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Step 1 | Download the 'GreenRide' app or use your transport card"

**【解析】** 租车第一步是下载 App 或使用公交卡。A、C、D 项为后续步骤。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "the first 60 minutes are free" 及第⑥段 "First 60 minutes: Free"

**【解析】** 原文明确说前 60 分钟免费。A、B、D 项时长错误。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Push the bike firmly into the lock until you hear a beep. Check the app or screen to make sure the return is complete"

**【解析】** 还车时应将车推入锁位直至听到蜂鸣，并确认还车完成。A 项与"any station"矛盾；C 项与"find another station"矛盾；D 项无中生有。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "You must be 16 or older to rent a bike"

**【解析】** 租车须年满 16 岁。A 项"任何年龄"错误；B 项 18 岁、D 项仅学生均与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "First 60 minutes: Free" 和 "61–120 minutes: ¥1 per 30 minutes"

**【解析】** 前 60 分钟免费，61–90 分钟为 30 分钟，按 ¥1/30 分钟计费，共 ¥1。A 项未计超时费；C、D 项计算错误。故选 B。
