# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Many schools have a small shop, or canteen stall, where students can buy snacks and drinks. In recent years, some people have called for a ban (禁止) on school shops. Should campus shops be banned? This question has stirred (引发) much debate.

② Those who support a ban argue that campus shops sell mostly unhealthy food — chips, candy, sugary drinks, and fried snacks. Students who eat too much of this food may gain weight and have health problems. Poor diet can also affect concentration (专注力) in class. Besides, some students spend too much pocket money at the shop and have nothing left for books or other needs. They say that if schools remove the shop, students will eat healthier meals in the canteen and bring fruit from home instead.

③ However, those who oppose the ban have different views. They point out that students need snacks sometimes — for example, when they have sports or stay late for club activities. A small shop is convenient. Not everything sold is unhealthy; many shops now offer milk, bread, fruit, and nuts. In addition, the shop gives students a chance to learn about money. They can practice deciding what to buy and how much to spend. If we ban the shop, we may push students to buy snacks from outside the school gate, where food safety can be even harder to control.

④ In my view, a complete ban may not be the best solution. Instead, schools could work with the shop to improve what is sold. They could ask the shop to offer more healthy choices — fewer chips and candy, more fruit and low-sugar drinks. Schools could also teach students about healthy eating and limit when the shop is open — for example, only during break time, not before class. This way, students can still enjoy some freedom while making better choices.

⑤ The goal should be to help students develop healthy habits, not to control every choice they make. Balance and education often work better than a simple ban.

**注释：** ban 禁止；stir 引发；concentration 专注力；convenient 方便的

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people want to ban campus shops?
   A. Because the shops are too small.
   B. Because shops sell mostly unhealthy food and students may spend too much pocket money.
   C. Because shops have no fruit.
   D. Because teachers do not like shops.

2. What do those who oppose the ban say?
   A. Students never need snacks.
   B. Students need snacks sometimes (e.g. after sports), and shops offer convenience; not all food is unhealthy.
   C. All campus shop food is very healthy.
   D. Shops should only sell books.

3. What does the writer suggest as a better solution than a complete ban?
   A. Close all schools.
   B. Work with the shop to offer more healthy choices and teach students about healthy eating.
   C. Let students buy whatever they want.
   D. Build more shops.

4. The word "they" in Paragraph ③ ("They can practice deciding what to buy") refers to _____.
   A. teachers
   B. shop owners
   C. students
   D. parents

5. What is the writer's main message?
   A. Campus shops must be banned immediately.
   B. Balance and education often work better than a simple ban; the goal is to help students develop healthy habits.
   C. Students should never eat snacks.
   D. Schools should sell only candy.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "campus shops sell mostly unhealthy food... Students who eat too much of this food may gain weight... some students spend too much pocket money at the shop"

**【解析】** 支持禁止者认为店铺多售不健康食品，且学生易过度消费零花钱。A、C、D 项非主要理由。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students need snacks sometimes — for example, when they have sports or stay late... A small shop is convenient. Not everything sold is unhealthy; many shops now offer milk, bread, fruit, and nuts"

**【解析】** 反对者认为学生有时需要零食，店铺方便，且并非所有商品都不健康。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "schools could work with the shop to improve what is sold... offer more healthy choices... teach students about healthy eating and limit when the shop is open"

**【解析】** 作者建议与店铺合作增加健康选项，并开展健康饮食教育。A、C、D 项与建议矛盾。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "the shop gives students a chance to learn about money. They can practice deciding what to buy"

**【解析】** 根据上下文，有机会学习用钱、练习做购买决定的是学生，"They"指 students。A、B、D 项不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/推理判断

**【定位】** 第④、⑤段 "Balance and education often work better than a simple ban. The goal should be to help students develop healthy habits"

**【解析】** 作者主张平衡与教育优于简单禁止，目标是帮学生养成健康习惯。A、C、D 项与作者观点不符。故选 B。
