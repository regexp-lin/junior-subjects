# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Online Shopping vs Shopping in Stores**

① More and more people buy things online. They shop on phones or computers. They can compare prices easily and get things delivered to their doors. But some people still prefer going to real stores. So which is better? Let us look at both sides.

② Those who prefer online shopping give several reasons. First, it is convenient. You can shop anytime, anywhere. No need to travel or wait in queues. Second, there are more choices online. You can find products from all over the world. Third, prices are often lower. Online shops have fewer costs, so they can offer discounts. Fourth, you can read reviews from other buyers before you decide. This helps you make better choices.

③ However, people who like shopping in stores have their reasons too. You can see, touch, and try products before buying. You do not have to wait for delivery. If something is wrong, you can return it right away. Shopping in stores is also a social activity. You can go with friends or family and enjoy the experience together. Some people find it more fun to walk around a mall than to stare at a screen.

④ Both ways have disadvantages. Online shopping: you cannot try clothes on; delivery can be slow or wrong; returning items can be troublesome. Store shopping: it takes more time; you may not find what you want; prices can be higher.

⑤ In my view, it depends on what you need. For daily goods or things you know well, online shopping saves time. For clothes, shoes, or gifts, going to a store might be better. The best approach is to use both. Enjoy the convenience of online shopping when it fits, and enjoy the experience of real stores when you have time.

**注释：** queue 排队；delivery 配送

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do people like online shopping?
   A. They hate stores.
   B. It is convenient, with more choices and often lower prices.
   C. They cannot leave home.
   D. Stores are always closed.

2. What advantage of store shopping is mentioned in Paragraph ③?
   A. You can shop at midnight.
   B. You can see, touch, and try products before buying.
   C. You never need to pay.
   D. Stores are always empty.

3. What does the writer think about both ways in Paragraph ④?
   A. Both are perfect.
   B. Both have disadvantages.
   C. Online shopping has no problems.
   D. Store shopping is always better.

4. What does the writer suggest in the last paragraph?
   A. Only shop online.
   B. Only shop in stores.
   C. Use both ways depending on what you need.
   D. Never shop at all.

5. What is the writer's purpose in writing this passage?
   A. To tell people to stop shopping.
   B. To compare online and store shopping and help readers choose wisely.
   C. To praise online shops only.
   D. To criticize real stores.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "It is convenient... more choices... prices are often lower"
**【解析】** 网购方便、选择多、价格通常更低。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "You can see, touch, and try products before buying"
**【解析】** 实体店购物可在购买前看到、触摸、试用商品。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Both ways have disadvantages"
**【解析】** 作者指出两种方式都有缺点。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The best approach is to use both. Enjoy the convenience of online shopping when it fits, and enjoy the experience of real stores when you have time."
**【解析】** 作者建议根据需求灵活使用两种方式。C项正确。A、B、D项与作者观点矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 写作意图
**【定位】** 全文
**【解析】** 全文对比网购与实体店购物，分析利弊，帮助读者根据需求选择。B项准确。A、C、D项与写作目的不符。故选B。
