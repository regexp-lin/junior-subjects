> **11-09 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Winter Sports Day Notice**

① Dear students and teachers,

② Our school will hold the Winter Sports Day on December 15th, 2024. The event will take place on the school playground from 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. All students are welcome to take part.

**Events:**

| Event          | Time      | Participants      |
|----------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Opening Ceremony | 8:30–9:00 | All               |
| 100m Race      | 9:15–9:45 | Grade 7–9         |
| Long Jump      | 10:00–11:00| Grade 7–9        |
| Relay Race     | 11:15–12:00| All grades       |
| Tug of War     | 14:00–15:00| Class teams      |
| Closing & Prizes | 15:30–16:00| All               |

③ Please wear your sports clothes and trainers. Bring your own water bottle. Students who want to join must sign up with your PE teacher by December 8th. Each class can send up to 5 students for individual events.

④ Parents are welcome to watch. Please stay in the audience area. No food or drinks on the field. Let us cheer for our classmates and enjoy the day!

⑤ For any questions, contact Mr. Wang (PE office) or email sports@ourschool.edu.cn.

School Sports Committee  
November 25th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Winter Sports Day be held?
   A. November 25th.
   B. December 8th.
   C. December 15th.
   D. January 15th.

2. Which event is for "All grades" according to the table?
   A. 100m Race.
   B. Long Jump.
   C. Relay Race.
   D. Tug of War.

3. What should students bring?
   A. Food and drinks.
   B. Their own water bottle.
   C. A camera only.
   D. Nothing.

4. By when must students sign up if they want to join?
   A. November 25th.
   B. December 8th.
   C. December 15th.
   D. December 16th.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Parents cannot come to watch.
   B. The school wants students to take part in sports and enjoy the event together.
   C. Only Grade 9 students can join.
   D. The event will be held indoors.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold the Winter Sports Day on December 15th, 2024"
**【解析】** 冬季运动会定于12月15日举行。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Relay Race 的 Participants 为 "All grades"
**【解析】** 接力跑面向所有年级。C项正确。A、B、D项限定于特定年级或班级。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Bring your own water bottle."
**【解析】** 通知要求自带水杯。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "sign up with your PE teacher by December 8th"
**【解析】** 报名截止日期为12月8日。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "All students are welcome to take part"；第④段 "Let us cheer for our classmates and enjoy the day!"
**【解析】** 通知欢迎全体学生参与，并希望大家为同学加油、享受活动。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。
