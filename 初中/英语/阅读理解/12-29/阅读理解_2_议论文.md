# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Traditional Food: Why It Still Matters**

① In every culture, there are traditional foods. They are recipes passed down from grandparents to parents to children. Dumplings in China, sushi in Japan, pizza in Italy — each has a long history. Today, fast food and ready-made meals are everywhere. Many young people prefer burgers and fries to traditional dishes. Yet I believe traditional food still matters. Here is why.

② First, traditional food connects us to our roots. When we eat the same food our grandparents ate, we feel a link to the past. We remember family gatherings, festivals, and stories. Food is more than nutrition. It carries culture and memory. In my family, we make dumplings every Spring Festival. My grandmother teaches us how to fold the wrapper. We sit together and talk. That hour is more valuable than any restaurant meal.

③ Second, traditional food is often healthier. Many traditional dishes use fresh, simple ingredients. They are cooked at home with less oil, salt, and sugar than fast food. Think of steamed fish, vegetable soup, or boiled eggs. They have been part of human diet for centuries. Our bodies are used to them. Of course, not all traditional food is healthy. But in general, homemade traditional meals are better for us than packaged snacks and fried takeaways.

④ Third, learning to cook traditional food is a useful skill. When you grow up and live alone, you can cook for yourself. You do not have to depend on takeaways every day. You can invite friends and share a home-cooked meal. Cooking also brings families together. Parents and children work in the kitchen. They talk, laugh, and create memories. These moments strengthen family bonds.

⑤ So, do not forget traditional food. Try to learn one or two family recipes. Cook with your parents or grandparents. Taste the flavours of your culture. In a world that changes fast, traditional food is an anchor. It reminds us who we are and where we come from.

**注释：** recipe 食谱；ingredient 配料；steam 蒸；takeaway 外卖；anchor 锚（比喻根基）

---

**Questions:**

1. What does the writer think about traditional food?
   A. It is old-fashioned and useless.
   B. It still matters and connects us to culture and roots.
   C. It is less tasty than fast food.
   D. Only old people like it.

2. Why does the writer say food is more than nutrition?
   A. Because it is expensive.
   B. Because it carries culture and memory; it connects us to the past.
   C. Because it has many colours.
   D. Because it is easy to cook.

3. According to the passage, why is traditional food often healthier?
   A. Because it is older.
   B. Because it often uses fresh, simple ingredients with less oil, salt, and sugar.
   C. Because it is always boiled.
   D. Because only grandmothers cook it.

4. How can cooking traditional food help families?
   A. It makes children stay away from parents.
   B. It brings families together; they work, talk, and create memories.
   C. It saves money only.
   D. It replaces school lessons.

5. What does the writer suggest young people do?
   A. Never eat traditional food.
   B. Learn family recipes and cook with parents or grandparents.
   C. Only eat fast food.
   D. Forget their culture.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "Yet I believe traditional food still matters. Here is why."

**【解析】** 作者认为传统食物仍然重要，并与文化和根基相连。A、C、D项均与作者观点相反。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Food is more than nutrition. It carries culture and memory."

**【解析】** 食物不仅是营养，还承载文化与记忆，连接过去。A项贵、C项颜色多、D项易做均非"more than nutrition"的含义。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Many traditional dishes use fresh, simple ingredients. They are cooked at home with less oil, salt, and sugar than fast food."

**【解析】** 传统菜多用新鲜简单配料，在家烹饪，油盐糖通常少于快餐。A、C、D项均非主要健康原因。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Cooking also brings families together. Parents and children work in the kitchen. They talk, laugh, and create memories."

**【解析】** 做饭能凝聚家人，一起在厨房劳作、聊天、创造回忆。A项让孩子远离父母错误；C、D项过于片面。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Try to learn one or two family recipes. Cook with your parents or grandparents."

**【解析】** 作者建议学一两道家传菜，和父母或祖父母一起做。A、C、D项均与建议相反。故选B。
