# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Summer Days at Grandma's Village

① When I was ten years old, my parents sent me to spend the summer with Grandma in her small village. I felt a little sad at first because I would miss my friends in the city. But Grandma's warm smile at the train station made me feel better. Her house stood beside a green field. A small river ran behind the garden. Everything looked fresh and quiet.

② Every morning, Grandma woke me up early. "The best time to enjoy the village is in the morning," she said. We would walk to the vegetable garden together. Grandma grew tomatoes, cucumbers, and corn. I helped her water the plants and pick the ripe vegetables. She taught me the names of different birds that sang in the trees. I had never noticed such things in the city.

③ Afternoons were for playing. I made friends with two village boys. We ran through the fields, caught butterflies, and climbed the old tree near the river. Sometimes we swam in the clear, cool water. Grandma always prepared a simple but delicious lunch for us. Fresh vegetables from her garden and eggs from the neighbor's chickens made the best meals I had ever eaten.

④ In the evenings, Grandma and I sat outside under the stars. She told me stories about her childhood and how the village had changed over the years. I listened and laughed. The stars seemed brighter there than in the city. I felt peaceful and happy. Those nights became my favorite memories.

⑤ Now I am grown up, but I still think about that summer often. Grandma is no longer with us, but her love and those warm village days stay in my heart. Sometimes when life gets busy and noisy, I close my eyes and remember the green fields, the river, and Grandma's smile. Those simple, happy days were the best gift she ever gave me.

**注释：** cucumber 黄瓜；ripe 成熟的；butterfly 蝴蝶

### 选择题

1. How did the writer feel when he first knew he would spend the summer at Grandma's?
   A. He felt a little sad because he would miss his city friends.
   B. He felt very excited and could not wait.
   C. He felt angry with his parents.
   D. He felt nothing special.

2. What did the writer do with Grandma in the morning?
   A. He helped water plants and pick vegetables in the garden.
   B. He slept until noon.
   C. He played video games.
   D. He went fishing in the river.

3. According to paragraph ③, who did the writer play with in the afternoons?
   A. Only Grandma.
   B. His parents.
   C. Two village boys.
   D. The neighbor's chickens.

4. What did Grandma and the writer do in the evenings?
   A. They watched TV inside the house.
   B. They worked in the garden.
   C. They visited the city.
   D. They sat outside, and Grandma told stories about her childhood.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this passage?
   A. Simple days spent with loved ones can create lasting, warm memories.
   B. City life is better than village life.
   C. Grandmas always tell boring stories.
   D. Summer is the only good season for visiting relatives.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I felt a little sad at first because I would miss my friends in the city."

**【解析】** 作者起初有些难过，因为会想念城里的朋友。A 项正确。B 项非常兴奋、C 项对父母生气、D 项无特别感受均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We would walk to the vegetable garden together... I helped her water the plants and pick the ripe vegetables."

**【解析】** 早上作者与奶奶去菜园，帮忙浇水和采摘成熟的蔬菜。A 项正确。B 项睡到中午、C 项打游戏、D 项钓鱼均未提及。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I made friends with two village boys."

**【解析】** 下午作者与两个村里的男孩一起玩耍。C 项正确。A 项仅奶奶、B 项父母、D 项邻居的鸡均不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "In the evenings, Grandma and I sat outside under the stars. She told me stories about her childhood and how the village had changed over the years."

**【解析】** 傍晚两人坐在屋外星空下，奶奶讲述童年与村庄变化的故事。D 项正确。A 项看电视、B 项干活、C 项进城均与原文不符。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "her love and those warm village days stay in my heart... Those simple, happy days were the best gift she ever gave me."

**【解析】** 文章通过回忆乡下奶奶家度过的暑假，表达与亲人共度的简单时光会留下温暖持久的记忆。A 项概括主旨。B、C、D 项均非作者意图。故选 A。
