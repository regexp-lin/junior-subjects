## Coral Reefs: A Colorful World Under the Sea

① Coral reefs are like underwater cities. They are made of tiny animals called coral polyps. These small creatures build hard skeletons around themselves. Over many years, millions of polyps join together and form large reef structures. Coral reefs grow in clear, warm, shallow waters. They need sunlight to stay healthy and grow.

② A coral reef is home to thousands of different living things. Colorful fish swim among the corals. Sea turtles visit the reefs to find food. Small crabs and shrimps hide in the spaces between the coral branches. Seaweed and other plants grow on the reefs too. Each living thing plays a part in this ecosystem. They depend on one another for food and shelter.

③ Coral reefs are very important for both sea life and people. They protect coastlines from strong waves during storms. Many fish that people eat come from reef areas. Reefs also attract tourists who love to swim and dive. Scientists say that coral reefs support about one quarter of all ocean life. Without reefs, the ocean would lose much of its beauty and richness.

④ Sadly, coral reefs are in danger today. When ocean water gets too warm, corals turn white and may die. This is called bleaching. Pollution from factories and farms runs into the sea and harms the reefs. Some people catch too many fish near reefs, which upsets the balance of the ecosystem. If we do nothing, many reefs could disappear in the future.

⑤ The good news is that we can help. We should reduce pollution and use less plastic. We can support laws that protect coral reef areas. When we visit the sea, we should not touch or damage the corals. By working together, we can keep these colorful underwater worlds alive for future generations.

**注释：** coral 珊瑚；polyp 珊瑚虫；bleach 白化；ecosystem 生态系统

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what do coral reefs need to stay healthy and grow?
   A. Sunlight, clear water, and warm temperatures.
   B. Cold water and darkness.
   C. Many large fish around them.
   D. Tourists to visit them.

2. Which of the following is mentioned in paragraph ② as living in or around coral reefs?
   A. Polar bears.
   B. Desert plants.
   C. Sea turtles and colorful fish.
   D. Mountain goats.

3. According to paragraph ③, why are coral reefs important for people?
   A. They make the ocean colder.
   B. They protect coastlines, provide food fish, and attract tourists.
   C. They produce plastic for daily use.
   D. They help ships travel faster.

4. What does "bleaching" mean in paragraph ④?
   A. Corals grow stronger when water is warm.
   B. People clean the reefs with special tools.
   C. Fish change their colors near the reefs.
   D. Corals turn white and may die when the ocean gets too warm.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Coral reefs will definitely disappear soon.
   B. There is nothing humans can do to help reefs.
   C. Only scientists need to care about coral reefs.
   D. We can protect reefs by reducing pollution and being careful when we visit the sea.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Coral reefs grow in clear, warm, shallow waters. They need sunlight to stay healthy and grow."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出珊瑚礁生长在清澈、温暖、浅水区域，需要阳光才能保持健康生长。A 项正确。B 项冷水与 darkness、C 项大鱼、D 项游客均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Colorful fish swim among the corals. Sea turtles visit the reefs to find food."

**【解析】** 第②段提到彩色鱼类在珊瑚间游动，海龟到礁区觅食。C 项正确。A 项北极熊、B 项沙漠植物、D 项山羊均为陆生生物，文中未提及。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They protect coastlines from strong waves... Many fish that people eat come from reef areas. Reefs also attract tourists who love to swim and dive."

**【解析】** 珊瑚礁能保护海岸、提供可食用鱼类、吸引潜水游客。B 项概括准确。A、C、D 项均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "When ocean water gets too warm, corals turn white and may die. This is called bleaching."

**【解析】** 白化（bleaching）指海水过热时珊瑚变白并可能死亡的现象。D 项正确。A 项说变强、B 项人为清洁、C 项鱼类变色均错误。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "We should reduce pollution and use less plastic... we should not touch or damage the corals."

**【解析】** 末段提出减少污染、少用塑料、参观时不触碰珊瑚等保护措施，说明人类可通过行动保护礁区。D 项推断正确。A、B 过于消极；C 项与"我们都能帮忙"矛盾。故选 D。
