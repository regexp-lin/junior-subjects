> **09-23 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① My bicycle had a flat tire (漏气的轮胎) last Saturday. I was going to throw it away and ask for a new one. But my father said, "Let's fix it together. You might learn something." I was not very interested. Fixing things sounded boring. But my father had a kind smile, so I agreed.

② We went to the garage and found the tools: a pump (打气筒), a wrench (扳手), and a patch kit（补胎工具）. First, Dad showed me how to remove the wheel. "Hold the bike steady (稳定的)," he said. I held it while he loosened the nuts. Then we took off the tire and found the hole. "See? A small piece of glass." He used sandpaper to clean the area around the hole, then stuck a patch on it. "Now we put the tire back and pump some air." I helped him put the wheel back on and pump. After a few minutes, the tire was firm again.

③ I was surprised. "That's it? It was so easy!" Dad laughed. "Nothing is too hard if you take it step by step. When I was your age, my father taught me the same thing." I had never thought about my father as a young boy learning from his father. It made me feel closer to him.

④ We rode our bikes to the park that afternoon. The weather was nice. Dad said, "Fixing things teaches us patience (耐心). When something is broken, we don't have to give up. We can try to fix it first." I nodded. I understood what he meant — it was not just about the bike.

⑤ From that day on, when something breaks at home — a button, a chair, a lamp — I try to fix it before asking for help. My father's lesson stays with me: take it step by step, and don't give up too soon.

**注释：** flat tire 漏气的轮胎；pump 打气筒；wrench 扳手；patch 补丁；steady 稳定的；patience 耐心

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer want to throw away the bicycle at first?
   A. Because it was too old.
   B. Because it had a flat tire.
   C. Because he wanted a newer model.
   D. Because his father told him to.

2. What did they use to fix the hole in the tire?
   A. Only a pump.
   B. Sandpaper to clean the area and a patch to cover the hole.
   C. Glue only.
   D. A new wheel.

3. What did the writer learn from his grandfather's story?
   A. Grandfathers are better than fathers.
   B. His father also learned to fix things from his father when young; this made him feel closer to his dad.
   C. Bicycles are easy to break.
   D. Old tools don't work.

4. What did the father say fixing things teaches us?
   A. How to be lazy.
   B. Patience; when something is broken, we can try to fix it first instead of giving up.
   C. How to spend money.
   D. Nothing useful.

5. What does the writer do now when something breaks at home?
   A. He always throws it away.
   B. He tries to fix it first before asking for help.
   C. He never fixes anything.
   D. He asks his mother only.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "My bicycle had a flat tire last Saturday. I was going to throw it away"

**【解析】** 自行车胎漏气，作者原打算扔掉。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He used sandpaper to clean the area around the hole, then stuck a patch on it"

**【解析】** 用砂纸清洁破洞周围，再贴上补丁。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "When I was your age, my father taught me the same thing.' I had never thought about my father as a young boy learning from his father. It made me feel closer to him"

**【解析】** 父亲讲述自己少年时从爷爷那儿学修车，这让作者对父亲更有亲近感。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Fixing things teaches us patience. When something is broken, we don't have to give up. We can try to fix it first"

**【解析】** 父亲指出修东西能培养耐心，遇到损坏不必轻易放弃，可先尝试修理。A、C、D 项与原文矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "when something breaks at home... I try to fix it before asking for help"

**【解析】** 作者现在遇到东西损坏会先尝试自己修理，再寻求帮助。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。
