# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Youth Volunteer Fair — Information Sheet**

① Are you looking for volunteer opportunities? Come to the Youth Volunteer Fair!

**Event Information:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Date | Saturday, December 7, 2024 |
| Time | 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. |
| Place | City Youth Centre, 2nd Floor Hall |
| Who can come | Students aged 12–18 |
| Fee | Free entry |

② At the fair, you can meet representatives from 15 volunteer organisations. They will introduce their projects and answer your questions. Here are some of them:

| Organisation | Project | Location |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Green Earth | Park clean-up every Saturday | Riverside Park |
| Happy Readers | Read to elderly people at care homes | Sunshine Care Home |
| Tech Helpers | Teach old people how to use smartphones | Community Centre |
| Animal Friends | Help care for stray animals | City Animal Shelter |
| Little Stars | Tutor primary school children | Bright Future School |

③ **How to sign up:** Visit any booth at the fair and fill in a form. You can sign up for more than one project. A parent or guardian must sign the form if you are under 16.

④ **What you get:** All volunteers will receive a certificate after 20 hours of service. Outstanding volunteers (50+ hours) will receive a special award at the end of the year.

⑤ For more information, follow us on WeChat: YouthVolunteer2024, or call 0755-6688-7799.

City Youth Centre  
November 18, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. Where will the Youth Volunteer Fair be held?
   A. At Riverside Park.
   B. At Bright Future School.
   C. At City Youth Centre, 2nd Floor Hall.
   D. At Sunshine Care Home.

2. What does the "Happy Readers" organisation do?
   A. Clean up parks.
   B. Read to elderly people at care homes.
   C. Teach old people to use smartphones.
   D. Take care of animals.

3. What must students under 16 do to sign up?
   A. Pay a fee.
   B. Bring their school ID.
   C. Have a parent or guardian sign the form.
   D. Write a letter to the organisation.

4. How many hours of service do you need to get a certificate?
   A. 10 hours.
   B. 15 hours.
   C. 20 hours.
   D. 50 hours.

5. Which of the following is TRUE according to the passage?
   A. Only students aged 16–18 can attend the fair.
   B. You can only sign up for one project.
   C. Outstanding volunteers with 50+ hours will receive a special award.
   D. The fair is held on Sunday.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Place" 一行 "City Youth Centre, 2nd Floor Hall"

**【解析】** 表格明确标注地点为市青年中心二楼大厅。A项Riverside Park是Green Earth活动的地点；B项Bright Future School是Little Stars辅导的地点；D项Sunshine Care Home是Happy Readers活动的地点。这些都是志愿项目的活动地点，不是博览会地点。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段表格 "Happy Readers" 一行 "Read to elderly people at care homes"

**【解析】** 表格清楚标明Happy Readers的项目是在养老院为老人读书。A项清理公园是Green Earth的项目；C项教老人用手机是Tech Helpers的项目；D项照顾动物是Animal Friends的项目。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "A parent or guardian must sign the form if you are under 16."

**【解析】** 原文明确要求16岁以下的学生需要家长或监护人签名。A项"付费"与Free entry矛盾；B项"带学生证"原文未要求；D项"写信"属于无中生有。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "All volunteers will receive a certificate after 20 hours of service."

**【解析】** 原文说服务满20小时可获得证书。A项10小时、B项15小时均不正确；D项50小时是获得特别奖项的要求，不是证书的要求。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Outstanding volunteers (50+ hours) will receive a special award at the end of the year."

**【解析】** 原文明确说服务超过50小时的优秀志愿者将获得年终特别奖，C项正确。A项原文说12-18岁都可参加，不仅限16-18岁；B项原文说"You can sign up for more than one project"，可以报多个项目；D项活动在周六（Saturday），不是周日。故选C。
