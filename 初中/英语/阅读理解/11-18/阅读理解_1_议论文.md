# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Students Learn a Musical Instrument?**

① In many countries, learning a musical instrument is a common part of growing up. Some parents send their children to piano, violin, or guitar lessons from a young age. But is it really necessary for every student to learn an instrument? People have different opinions.

② Those who support it believe music has many benefits. First, learning an instrument trains the brain. Studies show that playing music improves memory, concentration, and maths skills. The brain works hard when reading notes, moving fingers, and listening at the same time. Second, music helps students relax. After a long day of study, playing a piece of music can reduce stress. Third, learning an instrument teaches patience and discipline. Progress is slow, and students must practise regularly. These qualities are useful in all areas of life.

③ However, some people think it is not necessary for everyone. Learning an instrument takes a lot of time. If a student already has heavy schoolwork, adding music practice may cause more stress, not less. Private music lessons can also be expensive. Not every family can afford them. Besides, some children are simply not interested in music. Forcing them to learn may make them unhappy and even hate music.

④ In my view, schools should offer music classes so that every student has a chance to try. But whether to take extra lessons outside school should depend on the child's interest and the family's situation. Music is a wonderful thing, but it should bring joy, not pressure.

⑤ After all, the purpose of learning music is to enrich our lives. If a child loves it, great — support them. If not, there are many other ways to grow.

**注释：** discipline 自律；enrich 丰富

**Questions:**

1. What is the main topic of this passage?
   A. How to play the piano well.
   B. Whether students should learn a musical instrument.
   C. The history of musical instruments.
   D. Why music is dangerous for students.

2. According to Paragraph 2, what benefit does learning an instrument bring to the brain?
   A. It makes students taller.
   B. It improves memory, concentration, and maths skills.
   C. It helps students eat better.
   D. It makes students sleep longer.

3. Why do some people think learning an instrument is not necessary?
   A. Because music is boring for everyone.
   B. Because it takes time, costs money, and not every child is interested.
   C. Because all instruments are too loud.
   D. Because teachers do not like music.

4. What is the writer's opinion?
   A. Every student must learn the piano.
   B. Schools should ban all music classes.
   C. Schools should offer music classes, but extra lessons depend on the child's interest.
   D. Music is a waste of time.

5. What does the writer mean by "music should bring joy, not pressure" in Paragraph 4?
   A. Music is only for happy people.
   B. Learning music should be enjoyable, not stressful.
   C. Pressure helps students learn music faster.
   D. Students should only play music when they are happy.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段末句 "But is it really necessary for every student to learn an instrument? People have different opinions."

**【解析】** 全文围绕"学生是否应该学乐器"展开正反讨论。A项"如何弹好钢琴"过于具体；C项"乐器的历史"文中未涉及；D项"为什么音乐对学生有害"与文章基调不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Studies show that playing music improves memory, concentration, and maths skills."

**【解析】** 原文明确列出了改善记忆力、专注力和数学能力。A项"长高"、C项"吃得更好"、D项"睡得更长"均与原文无关。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Learning an instrument takes a lot of time." "Private music lessons can also be expensive." "some children are simply not interested in music."

**【解析】** 第③段从三个方面论述了反对理由：耗时、费钱、并非每个孩子都有兴趣。A项"音乐对每个人都无聊"以偏概全；C项"乐器太吵"原文未提及；D项"老师不喜欢音乐"属于无中生有。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "schools should offer music classes so that every student has a chance to try. But whether to take extra lessons outside school should depend on the child's interest and the family's situation."

**【解析】** 作者认为学校应开设音乐课让每个学生有机会尝试，但课外是否额外学习取决于孩子兴趣和家庭情况。A项"每个学生必须学钢琴"过于绝对；B项"学校应禁止音乐课"与原文相反；D项"音乐浪费时间"与作者态度不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Music is a wonderful thing, but it should bring joy, not pressure."

**【解析】** 结合上下文，作者认为学音乐应该是快乐的体验，而非压力来源，即学习音乐应该让人享受而非焦虑。A项"音乐只属于快乐的人"曲解原文；C项"压力帮助学得更快"与作者观点相反；D项"只在快乐时才弹"过于字面化理解。故选B。
