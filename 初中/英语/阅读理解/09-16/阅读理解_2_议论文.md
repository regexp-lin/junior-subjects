# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Will Paper Newspapers Disappear?**

① Many people now read news on their phones or computers. They get updates in seconds. So some people ask: will paper newspapers one day disappear? This question has two sides.

② Those who think paper newspapers will disappear have reasons. First, online news is fast and free. You can read the latest report at any time. Second, paper costs money. Trees are cut down to make newspapers. Many young people have never bought a paper. They grew up with screens. Third, it is easier to share news online. A link can reach thousands of people in a moment. Why would anyone still pay for old paper?

③ However, others believe paper newspapers will not die. Some people enjoy holding a real newspaper. They like the smell of print and the feel of turning pages. For them, reading a paper is a habit and a pleasure. Also, not everyone has a phone or the Internet. In some places, paper is still the main way to get news. Besides, many older readers trust paper more. They think online news can be wrong or fake. Paper has editors who check the facts. So paper may stay, at least for a while.

④ In my view, paper newspapers may become less common, but they will not vanish completely. Like books, they may become something special — for those who love the experience of reading on paper. Online news will stay popular for speed. But paper will remain for those who value tradition and quality. Both can exist together.

⑤ So, will paper newspapers disappear? Maybe not. They may change, but they are unlikely to vanish. The world has room for both digital and paper. What matters is that people can get the news they need.

**注释：** vanish 消失；digital 数字的

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people think paper newspapers will disappear?
   A. Because online news is fast, free, and easy to share; many young people prefer screens.
   B. Because paper is too expensive to make.
   C. Because newspapers have no readers.
   D. Because trees are not used anymore.

2. According to Paragraph ③, why do some people prefer paper newspapers?
   A. Because paper is cheaper than phones.
   B. Because they cannot read.
   C. Because they enjoy the experience — holding paper, turning pages — and some trust paper more than online news.
   D. Because paper has no ads.

3. What does the writer think will happen to paper newspapers?
   A. They will disappear completely very soon.
   B. They will become more popular than online news.
   C. They may become less common but will not vanish completely; both paper and digital can exist.
   D. They will replace online news.

4. What does "vanish" mean in Paragraph ④?
   A. Become popular.
   B. Become cheaper.
   C. Become bigger.
   D. Disappear completely.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Paper will definitely disappear next year.
   B. Online news will be banned.
   C. Young people will never read newspapers again.
   D. Both paper and digital news can coexist; the important thing is that people get the news they need.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "online news is fast and free... You can read the latest report at any time... Many young people have never bought a paper. They grew up with screens... it is easier to share news online"

**【解析】** 支持"纸质报纸会消失"的一方认为：网络新闻快速、免费、易分享，年轻人习惯屏幕阅读。A项概括准确。B、C、D项或片面或与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some people enjoy holding a real newspaper. They like the smell of print and the feel of turning pages... many older readers trust paper more"

**【解析】** 偏好纸质报的人喜欢实体阅读体验，部分年长读者更信赖纸质媒体。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "paper newspapers may become less common, but they will not vanish completely... Both can exist together"

**【解析】** 作者认为纸质报纸会减少但不会完全消失，纸质与数字媒体可以并存。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "they will not vanish completely" 及文末注释 "vanish 消失"

**【解析】** 结合上下文及注释，vanish 意为完全消失。D项正确。A、B、C项与词义不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The world has room for both digital and paper. What matters is that people can get the news they need"

**【解析】** 结尾指出数字与纸质新闻可以共存，重要的是人们能获得所需信息。D项正确。A、B、C项与文意不符。故选D。
