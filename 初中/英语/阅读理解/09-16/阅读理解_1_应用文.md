# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Canteen Menu Survey — Your Opinion Matters!**

② Dear students,

③ Our school is planning to improve the canteen menu. We want to know what you like and what you hope to see. Please take a few minutes to complete this survey. Your answers will help us make better choices for everyone.

④ **Part 1: What do you usually have for lunch?**

| Option | Choose one |
|--------|------------|
| A. Rice with dishes | ☐ |
| B. Noodles or dumplings | ☐ |
| C. Sandwiches or burgers | ☐ |
| D. Other (please write) | ☐ |

⑤ **Part 2: What would you like us to add?**

- [ ] More vegetables (e.g. broccoli, carrots)
- [ ] More fruit (e.g. apples, bananas, oranges)
- [ ] Vegetarian (素食) options
- [ ] Spicy food
- [ ] Desserts (e.g. pudding, fruit salad)
- [ ] Other ideas: ________________

⑥ **Part 3: How important is the price?**

- [ ] Very important — I hope meals are cheaper
- [ ] Somewhat important
- [ ] Not very important — quality matters more
- [ ] I have no strong view

⑦ **Part 4: Any other comments?**

Use the space below to tell us what you think. For example: opening hours, queue time (排队时间), seating, or anything else.

___________________________________________

⑧ **How to submit (提交):**
- Hand in this form to your class monitor by Friday, September 20.
- Or scan the QR code and fill in the online form.

Thank you for your help!

School Canteen Committee  
September 16, 2025

**注释：** vegetarian 素食的；submit 提交；queue 排队

---

**Questions:**

1. What is the main purpose of this survey?
   A. To sell more food in the canteen.
   B. To collect students' opinions to improve the canteen menu.
   C. To find out how much money students spend.
   D. To choose new canteen workers.

2. Which of the following is NOT listed as a lunch option in Part 1?
   A. Rice with dishes.
   B. Noodles or dumplings.
   C. Pizza.
   D. Sandwiches or burgers.

3. What does "vegetarian options" mean in the survey?
   A. Food with more meat.
   B. Food without meat (for those who don't eat meat).
   C. Food that is very cheap.
   D. Food from other countries.

4. By when should students hand in the form?
   A. By September 16.
   B. By September 18.
   C. By Friday, September 20.
   D. By October 1.

5. How can students submit the survey?
   A. Only by handing the form to the class monitor.
   B. Only by filling in the online form.
   C. Either by handing the form to the class monitor or by filling in the online form.
   D. By sending an email to the teacher.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段标题及第③段 "Our school is planning to improve the canteen menu. We want to know what you like and what you hope to see... Your answers will help us make better choices"

**【解析】** 调查目的是收集学生意见以改进食堂菜单。A项促销、C项了解花费、D项招聘员工均非主要目的。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段表格：A. Rice with dishes; B. Noodles or dumplings; C. Sandwiches or burgers; D. Other

**【解析】** 选项中列有米饭配菜、面条/饺子、三明治/汉堡、其他，未单独列出披萨。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Vegetarian options" 及注释 "vegetarian 素食的"

**【解析】** vegetarian 指素食，"vegetarian options"即不含肉的菜品，供不吃肉的人选择。A、C、D项与词义不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑧段 "Hand in this form to your class monitor by Friday, September 20"

**【解析】** 原文明确要求于周五9月20日前交给班长。A、B、D项日期错误。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑧段 "Hand in this form to your class monitor by Friday, September 20. Or scan the QR code and fill in the online form"

**【解析】** 学生可交纸质表给班长，或扫码填写在线表单，两种方式均可。A、B项只认可一种方式；D项原文未提及。故选C。
