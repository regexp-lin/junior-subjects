> **09-10 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Teachers' Day感恩活动 — Activity Plan**

① Dear students,

② Teachers' Day is on September 10th. To thank our teachers for their hard work, the Student Union will hold a series of activities. We invite all students to join in.

**Activity 1: Thank-you Cards**
- **Time:** Sept. 5–9 (bring your card to class)
- **What to do:** Make a thank-you card for your favorite teacher. Write your thanks and best wishes.
- **Where to hand in:** Give it to your class teacher by Sept. 9. We will pass all cards to teachers on Sept. 10.

**Activity 2: "One Minute for Teachers" Speech**
- **Time:** Sept. 10, 3:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m.
- **Place:** School Hall
- **What to do:** Each class chooses one student to give a 1-minute speech about "Why I Thank My Teacher."
- **Prize:** The top 3 speakers will get a small gift.

**Activity 3: Flower & Greeting**
- **Time:** Sept. 10, morning (before first class)
- **What to do:** Students stand at the school gate and give a flower and a greeting to each teacher when they arrive.
- **Note:** Flowers will be prepared by the school. You only need to come and help hand them out.

③ Let us work together to make our teachers feel loved and appreciated!

④ For questions, contact the Student Union office or your class monitor.

Student Union  
September 1st, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When is Teachers' Day?
   A. September 1st.
   B. September 5th.
   C. September 9th.
   D. September 10th.

2. What should students do for Activity 1?
   A. Buy a card from the school.
   B. Make a thank-you card and give it to the class teacher by Sept. 9.
   C. Send an email to the teacher.
   D. Give a speech in the hall.

3. Where will the "One Minute for Teachers" speech be held?
   A. In the classroom.
   B. In the library.
   C. In the School Hall.
   D. At the school gate.

4. Who will prepare the flowers for Activity 3?
   A. The students themselves.
   B. The teachers.
   C. The school.
   D. The parents.

5. What is the purpose of these activities?
   A. To raise money for the school.
   B. To thank teachers and make them feel appreciated.
   C. To choose new class monitors.
   D. To prepare for the mid-term exam.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Teachers' Day is on September 10th."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明教师节在9月10日。A、B、C项日期均与原文不符。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** Activity 1 部分 "Make a thank-you card for your favorite teacher... Give it to your class teacher by Sept. 9."

**【解析】** 活动1要求制作感谢卡并在9月9日前交给班主任。B项准确。A项"从学校购买"与"Make"矛盾；C、D项为其他活动内容。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** Activity 2 部分 "**Place:** School Hall"

**【解析】** 演讲活动地点在School Hall。A、B、D项均为错误地点。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** Activity 3 部分 "**Note:** Flowers will be prepared by the school. You only need to come and help hand them out."

**【解析】** 注释明确说明鲜花由学校准备，学生只需帮忙分发。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 第②段 "To thank our teachers for their hard work" 及第③段 "Let us work together to make our teachers feel loved and appreciated!"

**【解析】** 活动目的是感谢老师辛勤付出，让老师感受到关爱与认可。B项准确。A、C、D项均偏离目的。故选B。
