# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Today, more and more people use their phones to pay when they buy things. They scan a QR code or tap their phone, and the payment is done. This is called electronic payment. But some people still prefer to use cash — paper money and coins. So which is better: electronic payment or cash? Let us look at both.

② Electronic payment has many advantages. First, it is convenient. You do not need to carry a wallet full of money. Your phone is enough. Second, it is fast. You can pay in seconds. No need to wait for change. Third, it is safe in some ways. If you lose your wallet, the money is gone. But if you lose your phone, you can lock your payment app and your money may still be safe. Fourth, it is easy to track your spending. You can see where your money went every month.

③ However, cash also has its benefits. Some older people are not used to smartphones. They find it easier to use cash. In small towns or villages, not every shop accepts electronic payment. Cash works everywhere. Besides, when you use cash, you can actually "feel" the money. Some experts say this helps people spend more carefully. They think twice before breaking a big note. With electronic payment, money seems less real, and people might spend too much without noticing.

④ In my opinion, both have their place. Electronic payment is great for city life and online shopping. Cash is still useful when you travel to places with poor internet or when you want to control your spending. The best way might be to use both: use your phone for daily buys, but keep some cash for emergencies or small shops. What matters is that we use money wisely, no matter which way we pay.

⑤ So, electronic payment or cash? There is no single best answer. Choose what works for you and your situation.

**注释：** QR code 二维码；scan 扫描

**Questions:**

1. What is the main topic of this passage?
   A. How to use a smartphone.
   B. Whether electronic payment is better than cash.
   C. The history of money.
   D. How to save money.

2. According to Paragraph ②, which is NOT an advantage of electronic payment?
   A. It is convenient — you do not need to carry cash.
   B. It is fast and you can track your spending.
   C. It is safer because you can lock your app if you lose your phone.
   D. It works in every shop in the world.

3. Why do some people prefer cash?
   A. Because cash is heavier and more fun to carry.
   B. Because they are not used to phones, or cash works in more places, or it helps them spend more carefully.
   C. Because electronic payment is banned.
   D. Because cash is free.

4. What does the writer suggest in Paragraph ④?
   A. People should only use electronic payment.
   B. People should only use cash.
   C. Both are useful; use electronic payment for daily life but keep some cash for emergencies.
   D. People should never use phones to pay.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Electronic payment will replace cash soon.
   B. The best choice depends on each person's situation; there is no one answer for everyone.
   C. Cash will disappear in the future.
   D. The writer does not like electronic payment.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "So which is better: electronic payment or cash? Let us look at both."

**【解析】** 文章围绕电子支付与现金支付孰优孰劣展开讨论。B项准确。A、C、D项均偏离主题。故选B。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 所列优点：convenient, fast, safe (can lock app), easy to track spending

**【解析】** A、B、C项均为文中提到的电子支付优点。D项"在全球每家店都能用"原文未提及，且第③段提到"not every shop accepts electronic payment"，与之矛盾。故选D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some older people are not used to smartphones... In small towns or villages, not every shop accepts electronic payment. Cash works everywhere... when you use cash, you can actually 'feel' the money... helps people spend more carefully."

**【解析】** 偏好现金的原因包括：不习惯手机、部分地方不支持电子支付、现金有助更谨慎消费。B项概括全面。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The best way might be to use both: use your phone for daily buys, but keep some cash for emergencies or small shops."

**【解析】** 作者建议两种方式结合：日常用手机支付，同时备一些现金应对紧急情况或小商店。C项准确。A、B、D项与作者建议相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "There is no single best answer. Choose what works for you and your situation."

**【解析】** 结尾指出没有唯一正确答案，应根据个人情况选择，可推知B项"最佳选择因人而异，没有普适答案"。A、C、D项均属过度推断。故选B。
