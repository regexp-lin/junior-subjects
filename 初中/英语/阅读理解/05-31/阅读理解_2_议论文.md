# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In many schools, teachers share exam rankings with the whole class after each test. Some students feel proud when they see their names at the top, while others feel ashamed when their scores are low. The question is: should exam rankings be made public? People have different opinions about this.

② **Those who support public rankings** believe it can encourage students to work harder. When students know where they stand compared with their classmates, they may try to improve. Public rankings also help parents understand how their children are doing in school. Moreover, some argue that in real life, we often face competition — learning to accept rankings early can prepare students for the future.

③ **Those who are against public rankings** think it may hurt students' self-confidence (自信心). Students who often rank low may feel discouraged and lose interest in learning. Some may even be laughed at by classmates. Privacy (隐私) is another concern — not everyone wants others to know their scores. Besides, rankings only show test results; they do not show a student's creativity, kindness, or other important qualities.

④ **What might work better?** Some schools have tried a different way: teachers share rankings only with each student and their parents privately. In this way, students still know their progress, but they do not have to face the pressure of being compared publicly. Some teachers also focus more on helping each student improve than on comparing them with others.

⑤ In my view, there is no perfect answer. The best way may depend on the age of the students, the culture of the school, and the feelings of the families. What matters most is that we help every student grow and feel valued, not only by their exam scores.

**注释：** self-confidence 自信心；privacy 隐私；creativity 创造力

---

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, why do some people support public exam rankings?
   A. Because it can encourage students to work harder and help parents understand their children's progress.
   B. Because it can hurt students' confidence.
   C. Because it protects students' privacy.
   D. Because it shows students' creativity and kindness.

2. What problem might public rankings cause for students who often rank low?
   A. They may feel proud and work less.
   B. They may feel discouraged and lose interest in learning.
   C. They may get more help from teachers immediately.
   D. They may never take exams again.

3. What alternative (替代方案) do some schools use instead of public rankings?
   A. They never tell students their scores.
   B. They post rankings on the school website for everyone to see.
   C. They compare students with those in other schools.
   D. They share rankings only with each student and their parents privately.

4. According to paragraph ④, what do some teachers focus on more?
   A. Posting rankings on the board every week.
   B. Comparing students with each other publicly.
   C. Helping each student improve rather than comparing them with others.
   D. Making sure every student gets the same score.

5. What is the writer's main point in the last paragraph?
   A. Public rankings are always the best choice.
   B. Private rankings are always the best choice.
   C. There is no perfect answer; what matters most is helping every student grow and feel valued.
   D. Schools should never use any kind of ranking.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Those who support public rankings believe it can encourage students to work harder... Public rankings also help parents understand how their children are doing in school."

**【解析】** 支持公开排名者认为：可激励学生更努力，且有助于家长了解孩子在校表现。A 项正确。B 项"打击自信"为反对理由；C 项"保护隐私"为反对方观点；D 项"创造力、善良"对应反对方说的排名无法体现的内容。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Students who often rank low may feel discouraged and lose interest in learning."

**【解析】** 经常排名靠后的学生可能沮丧并失去学习兴趣。B 项正确。A 项"骄傲、更少努力"与"rank low"情境矛盾；C 项"立刻获得老师帮助"未提及；D 项"不再考试"过度推断。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "teachers share rankings only with each student and their parents privately."

**【解析】** 部分学校改为仅私下告知每位学生及其家长排名。D 项正确。A 项"从不告知成绩"与"share rankings"矛盾；B 项"网站公开"仍属公开；C 项"与其他学校比较"未提及。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some teachers also focus more on helping each student improve than on comparing them with others."

**【解析】** 部分教师更注重帮助每位学生进步，而非相互比较。C 项正确。A、B 项与"focus more on helping"矛盾；D 项"人人同分"未提及。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my view, there is no perfect answer... What matters most is that we help every student grow and feel valued, not only by their exam scores."

**【解析】** 作者认为没有完美答案，最重要的是帮助每位学生成长并感到被重视，而不仅靠考试分数。C 项准确概括。A、B 项"总是最佳"与"no perfect answer"矛盾；D 项"绝不使用任何排名"过度推断。故选 C。
