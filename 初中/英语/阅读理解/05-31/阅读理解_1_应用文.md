# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **City Public Bike & Shared Bike Use Guide**

② In many cities, you can now find public bikes and shared bikes on almost every street corner. They are a green way to travel short distances. Using bikes instead of cars helps reduce air pollution (污染) and keeps our city clean. Before you ride, here are some useful tips.

③ **How to use:** First, download the official app on your phone. Then scan (扫描) the QR code on the bike with your phone. The lock will open and you can start your ride. Remember to park the bike in a proper place when you finish — do not leave it in the middle of the sidewalk or block the way for others.

④ **Fees:** The first 30 minutes are usually free. After that, you pay about 1 yuan for every 15 minutes. If you return the bike to a parking area (停车区), you may get extra free time. Always check the app for the latest rules.

⑤ **Safety:** Wear a helmet if possible. Follow traffic rules and ride in the bike lane. Do not ride with two people on one bike. Children under 12 should not use shared bikes alone. Keep your hands on the handlebars and never use your phone while riding.

**注释：** scan 扫描；helmet 头盔；handlebar 车把手

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 主旨理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "They are a green way to travel short distances. Using bikes instead of cars helps reduce air pollution and keeps our city clean."

**【解析】** 原文指出公共/共享自行车是短途出行的绿色方式，有助于减少空气污染、保持城市清洁。A 项"为城市赚钱"未提及；B 项正确概括；C 项"替代所有汽车"过于绝对；D 项"帮儿童学骑车"非主旨。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "scan the QR code on the bike with your phone. The lock will open."

**【解析】** 用手机扫描车上的二维码即可开锁。A 项正确。B 项投币、C 项致电服务中心、D 项使用卡均未在文中出现。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "park the bike in a proper place when you finish — do not leave it in the middle of the sidewalk or block the way for others."

**【解析】** 还车时应停放在合适位置，切勿停在人行道中央或阻碍他人通行。C 项正确。A 项"人行道中央"与"do not leave it"矛盾；B、D 项"家中""anywhere"与原文不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "If you return the bike to a parking area, you may get extra free time."

**【解析】** 若将车还至停车区，可能获得额外免费时长，可推知停在正确区域有优惠。D 项正确。A 项"第一分钟就付费"与"first 30 minutes are usually free"矛盾；B、C 项价格固定或不变，文中未提及。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解（排除型）

**【定位】** 第⑤段所列安全规则：戴头盔、遵守交规、骑车道、不载双人、12岁以下不单独使用、双手握把、骑行时不看手机。

**【解析】** B、C、D 三项均对应原文。A 项"必须穿亮色外套"文中未提到，属无中生有。题干问"哪项未提及"，故选 A。
