# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Lily had been playing the piano since she was six years old. Now, at fourteen, she was about to enter the city's Youth Piano Competition (青少年钢琴比赛) for the first time. She practiced for two hours every day after school, and her fingers sometimes hurt so much that she had to put them in cold water.

② "Are you sure you're ready?" her mother asked one evening, watching Lily close the piano lid with a tired face. "There will be many talented players there." Lily nodded. "I know, Mom. But I've worked really hard. I just want to try."

③ On the day of the competition, Lily arrived at the concert hall early. She saw other competitors warming up backstage (在后台). Some of them looked very confident, laughing and chatting with their friends. Lily felt her heart beating fast. She sat alone in a corner, quietly going over the music in her head.

④ When her name was called, Lily walked onto the stage. The bright lights made her blink (眨眼). She sat down, took a deep breath, and placed her hands on the keys. For the first few notes, her fingers were shaking. But as the music continued, something changed. She forgot about the judges, the audience, and the other competitors. She was lost in the music, playing from her heart.

⑤ Lily did not win first place that day — she came in third. Some people might think third place is not good enough, but Lily was smiling when she came off the stage. "I played my best," she told her mother. "And I learned that being brave enough to try is already a kind of victory." Her mother hugged her tightly and whispered, "I'm so proud of you."

**注释：** competition 比赛；competitor 参赛者；confident 自信的；judge 评委；audience 观众；victory 胜利；whisper 低声说

---

### 选择题

1. How long has Lily been playing the piano?
   A. For six years.
   B. For eight years.
   C. For ten years.
   D. For fourteen years.

2. What did Lily do to prepare for the competition?
   A. She hired a famous piano teacher.
   B. She practiced two hours every day after school.
   C. She watched videos of other pianists online.
   D. She bought a brand-new piano for practice.

3. How did Lily feel when she saw other competitors backstage?
   A. She felt angry and wanted to leave.
   B. She felt relaxed and started chatting with them.
   C. She felt nervous and sat alone in a corner.
   D. She felt bored and fell asleep.

4. What can we infer about Lily's performance on stage from paragraph ④?
   A. She made many mistakes and stopped playing.
   B. She was nervous at first but became fully focused on the music.
   C. She forgot how to play and left the stage early.
   D. She played perfectly from the very first note.

5. What did Lily learn from the competition?
   A. Winning first place is the only thing that matters.
   B. She should give up playing the piano.
   C. Being brave enough to try is already a kind of victory.
   D. She will never enter a competition again.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1-2句 "Lily had been playing the piano since she was six years old. Now, at fourteen..."

**【解析】** Lily 从6岁开始弹钢琴，现在14岁，所以她已经弹了8年。A 项"6年"混淆了开始学琴的年龄和弹琴的时长；C 项"10年"和 D 项"14年"均计算错误。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "She practiced for two hours every day after school"

**【解析】** 原文明确提到 Lily 每天放学后练琴两小时。A 项"请了著名钢琴老师"原文未提及；C 项"在网上看其他钢琴家的视频"属于无中生有；D 项"买了全新钢琴"同样没有依据。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Lily felt her heart beating fast. She sat alone in a corner, quietly going over the music in her head."

**【解析】** 看到其他选手热身后，Lily 心跳加速（紧张），独自坐在角落里默默回忆乐谱。A 项"生气想离开"、B 项"放松并与他们聊天"和 D 项"无聊睡着了"均与原文描写不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "For the first few notes, her fingers were shaking. But as the music continued, something changed. She forgot about the judges, the audience, and the other competitors. She was lost in the music, playing from her heart."

**【解析】** 原文描述一开始手指在抖（紧张），但随着音乐继续她忘掉了一切、全身心投入演奏，可以推断她从紧张过渡到完全专注。A 项"犯了很多错误并停止演奏"文中未提及任何错误；C 项"忘了怎么弹提前离开"与原文矛盾；D 项"从第一个音符就完美"与"手指抖"矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I learned that being brave enough to try is already a kind of victory."

**【解析】** Lily 在比赛中获得第三名，但她笑着说勇于尝试本身就是一种胜利，这就是她从比赛中学到的道理。A 项"只有第一名才重要"与 Lily 的态度完全相反；B 项"应该放弃钢琴"和 D 项"再也不参加比赛"均属于无中生有，与原文积极态度矛盾。故选 C。
