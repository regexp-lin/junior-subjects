> **04-04 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Nowadays, many parents do everything for their children. They wash their children's clothes, clean their rooms, and even pack their schoolbags. They think doing chores (家务) will take up too much time and affect (影响) their children's studies. But I strongly disagree with this idea. I believe doing chores is very important for teenagers.

② Doing chores helps teenagers learn important life skills. Washing dishes, cooking simple meals, and cleaning the floor are all basic skills we need for living alone in the future. If a teenager doesn't know how to boil an egg or wash a pair of socks, how can he or she live independently (独立地) when going to college?

③ Furthermore, doing chores teaches teenagers a sense of responsibility (责任感). A family is like a small team. Everyone in the team should do their part. By helping with housework, teenagers learn that they have a duty to keep the house clean and comfortable. They will understand their parents' hard work and become more thankful.

④ Lastly, doing chores can also be a good way to relax. After sitting at a desk and studying hard all day, moving our bodies to sweep the floor can give our brains a much-needed break. It's much better than playing mobile games.

⑤ In a word, doing chores is not a waste of time. It is an important lesson in our growth. Parents should encourage their children to do some housework every day.

**注释：** chore 家务；affect 影响；independently 独立地；responsibility 责任感

### 选择题

1. Why do some parents do all the chores for their children?
A. Because they like cleaning very much.
B. Because their children are too small.
C. Because they think doing chores will affect children's studies.
D. Because doing chores is too dangerous for teenagers.

2. What life skills are mentioned in paragraph ②?
A. Playing basketball and swimming.
B. Washing dishes, cooking simple meals, and cleaning the floor.
C. Speaking English and writing stories.
D. Using computers and fixing cars.

3. How does doing chores teach teenagers responsibility?
A. By making them finish their homework faster.
B. By showing them how to make money.
C. By letting them know they have a duty to the family.
D. By stopping them from playing with friends.

4. What can we infer (推断) from paragraph ④?
A. Sweeping the floor is a very difficult job.
B. Parents always force their children to do chores.
C. Teenagers should play more mobile games.
D. Doing physical work like sweeping can help the brain rest.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
A. Doing chores is important and good for teenagers.
B. Parents are too tired to do housework.
C. Going to college is very difficult.
D. Sweeping the floor is the best sport for students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "They think doing chores (家务) will take up too much time and affect (影响) their children's studies."

**【解析】** 原文指出，许多父母认为做家务会占用太多时间并影响（affect）孩子的学业，因此他们包揽了所有家务。A、B、D项在文中均未作为原因被提及。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "Washing dishes, cooking simple meals, and cleaning the floor are all basic skills..."

**【解析】** 在教导生活技能这一小节中，提到了洗碗、做简单的饭菜和扫地。这与B项直接对应。A项是运动技能；C项是语言技能；D项是其它技术类技能。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3-4句 "Everyone in the team should do their part. By helping with housework, teenagers learn that they have a duty to keep the house clean and comfortable."

**【解析】** 第③段讲述了在这个类似团队的家庭里，帮做家务能让青少年明白他们有责任（have a duty）保持家里整洁舒适，从而教给他们责任感。A项（做作业快）、B项（赚钱）、D项（阻止他们交际）均不符合文意。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段末尾 "...moving our bodies to sweep the floor can give our brains a much-needed break."

**【解析】** 本段的主旨是做家务是一种放松的好方法。原文提到经过一天坐着苦读后，动动身体扫扫地能给大脑提供必要的休息。由此可推断进行扫地这样的体力劳动有助于大脑休息。A项（非常难）与原文语境不符；B项与原文无关；C项（玩更多的手机游戏）与原文最后一句话明确相反。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 首段中心句 "...I believe doing chores is very important for teenagers." 及全文结构。

**【解析】** 本文是一篇议论文。第一段提出“做家务对青少年很重要”的中心论点，接着从三个方面（学习生活技能、培养责任感、帮助放松大脑）展开论述，并在最后一段总结重申这一观点。B项、C项属于以偏概全或偏离主题，D项只谈到了一小点。故选A。
