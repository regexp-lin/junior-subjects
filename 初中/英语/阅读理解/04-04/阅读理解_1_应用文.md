# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Join the Sunshine English Club!**

② Do you want to improve your spoken English? Do you want to make friends with students from different grades? The Sunshine English Club is the right place for you! We are welcome all students in our school to join us.

③ **What do we do?**
- **English Corner (Every Tuesday afternoon):** We meet at the school library. We talk about our favorite books, movies, and hobbies in English.
- **English Movie Night (Once a month):** We watch popular English movies and learn new words from them.
- **Speech Contest (Every term):** We hold a speech contest to help students become confident speakers. The winners will get beautiful English books as prizes!

④ **Who can join?**
Anyone who loves English can join! You don't have to be top of your class in English. We just want students who are brave enough to speak out.

⑤ **How to join?**
Please come to Room 302 before next Friday, April 10th. You need to fill out a short form (表格). If you have any questions, you can call our club leader, Tom, at 678-2345.

Come and join us! Let's enjoy learning English together!

**注释：** speech contest 演讲比赛；confident 自信的；form 表格

---

### 选择题

1. Where does the English Corner meet?
A. In Room 302.
B. On the school playground.
C. In the school library.
D. At Tom's house.

2. How often does the club have an English Movie Night?
A. Every Tuesday afternoon.
B. Once a week.
C. Every term.
D. Once a month.

3. What can the winners of the speech contest get?
A. Some money.
B. Beautiful English books.
C. A free movie ticket.
D. A new schoolbag.

4. Form the text, what kind of students is the club looking for?
A. Students who are the best at English in their class.
B. Students who have a lot of money to buy books.
C. Students who are brave enough to speak English.
D. Students who can sing English songs very well.

5. What must a student do to join the club?
A. Talk to the club leader on the phone for an hour.
B. Pay some money to the club before April 10th.
C. Write a long story in English.
D. Go to Room 302 and fill out a short form.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "English Corner (Every Tuesday afternoon): We meet at the school library."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到英语角是在学校图书馆（school library）碰面。A项是报名的地方；其余选项为无中生有。故选C。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "English Movie Night (Once a month)"

**【解析】** 在“英语电影夜”这一项的括号中，明确写着是每月一次（Once a month）。A项是英语角的时间；C项是演讲比赛的频率。故选D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "... The winners will get beautiful English books as prizes!"

**【解析】** 关于演讲比赛（Speech Contest），原文提到获胜者将会得到精美的英文书作为奖品。A项（钱）、C项（免费电影票）、D项（新书包）都与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段中 "You don't have to be top of your class in English. We just want students who are brave enough to speak out."

**【解析】** 关于招新对象，原文强调“你不必是班上英语最好的。我们只想要那些有勇气开口说的学生（brave enough to speak out）”。A项要求必须是英语最好的，与原文相悖；B项和D项未提及。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1-2句 "Please come to Room 302 before next Friday, April 10th. You need to fill out a short form (表格)."

**【解析】** 原文讲到报名的具体步骤是：在4月10日（下周五）前去 302 教室并填写一份简短的表格（fill out a short form）。打电话（A项）只是有疑问时的联系方式；文中根本未提到交钱（B项）或写长篇英语故事（C项）。故选D。
