> **12-30 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**A Summer in Grandpa's Village**

① Last summer, my parents sent me to my grandfather's village. They said I spent too much time on my phone and needed a change. I was not happy at first. The village had no Wi-Fi. What would I do there for a whole month? But Grandpa welcomed me with a big smile. "You will see," he said. "This place has something the city does not have."

② The first week was hard. I woke up at six because the rooster crowed. There was no air conditioning. At night, I lay on the bed and listened to the insects. But slowly, I began to enjoy it. Grandpa took me to the fields. I saw how rice, vegetables, and fruits grew. I learned that farming needs patience and hard work. We watered the plants, pulled out weeds, and picked tomatoes. My hands got dirty. My back ached. But when we ate the tomatoes at lunch, they tasted sweeter than any I had bought in the city.

③ Grandpa also took me to visit the neighbours. An old lady, Aunt Chen, lived next door. She had a small garden full of flowers. She gave me a cup of tea and told me stories about the village when she was young. She said the village had changed a lot. New roads were built. Some young people had come back to start businesses. They sold local products online. The village was becoming beautiful again. I had never thought about "rural revitalisation" before. Now I understood: it meant people caring for their homeland and building a better life together.

④ In the last week, Grandpa taught me to make a kite. We flew it on the hill. The wind was strong. The kite went high into the blue sky. I looked at the green mountains and the small houses below. I felt peaceful. I did not miss my phone anymore.

⑤ When I went back to the city, I told my friends about the village. I said, "You should visit the countryside. There is another kind of life there." That summer taught me to slow down, respect nature, and value simple joys. Maybe one day, I will go back and do something for that village too.

**注释：** rooster 公鸡；weed 杂草；rural 乡村的；revitalisation 振兴；homeland 家乡

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer's parents send him to the village?
   A. Because they did not want him at home.
   B. Because he spent too much time on his phone and needed a change.
   C. Because the village had good schools.
   D. Because Grandpa was ill.

2. What did the writer think of the first week?
   A. It was easy and fun.
   B. It was hard at first.
   C. It was boring.
   D. He wanted to leave immediately.

3. What did Aunt Chen say about the village?
   A. Nothing had changed.
   B. The village had changed a lot; new roads, young people returning, selling products online.
   C. Everyone had left.
   D. The village was worse than before.

4. What did Grandpa teach the writer to do?
   A. To farm rice.
   B. To make and fly a kite.
   C. To sell products online.
   D. To build roads.

5. What did the writer learn from the summer?
   A. The city is better.
   B. To slow down, respect nature, and value simple joys.
   C. Farming is easy.
   D. Wi-Fi is unnecessary everywhere.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "They said I spent too much time on my phone and needed a change."

**【解析】** 父母说他花太多时间玩手机，需要换换环境。A、C、D项均非父母送他去村里的原因。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The first week was hard. I woke up at six because the rooster crowed."

**【解析】** 第一周很不容易，六点被公鸡吵醒。A项容易有趣与"hard"矛盾；C项无聊、D项立刻想走过于笼统。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "She said the village had changed a lot. New roads were built. Some young people had come back to start businesses. They sold local products online."

**【解析】** 陈阿姨说村里变化很大，修了新路，年轻人回乡创业、网上卖农产品。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Grandpa taught me to make a kite. We flew it on the hill."

**【解析】** 祖父教他做风筝，并在山上放风筝。A项种稻、C项网上卖货、D项修路均非祖父教他的内容。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "That summer taught me to slow down, respect nature, and value simple joys."

**【解析】** 那个夏天让他学会放慢节奏、尊重大自然、珍惜简单快乐。A、C、D项均未准确概括这一体会。故选B。
