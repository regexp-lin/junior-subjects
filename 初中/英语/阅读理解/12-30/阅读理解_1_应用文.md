# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Club Recruitment — Spring Term 2026**

① Dear students,

We are now recruiting new members for our school clubs. All Grade 7 and 8 students are welcome to join. Each student can join up to two clubs. Please read the information below and sign up by January 10, 2026.

**Club list:**

| Club | Meeting time | Place | Leader |
|------|--------------|-------|--------|
| Reading Club | Tue & Thu 4:00–5:00 p.m. | Room 201 | Mr Wang |
| Science Club | Mon & Wed 4:00–5:00 p.m. | Lab 1 | Miss Li |
| Photography Club | Fri 4:00–5:30 p.m. | Room 305 | Mr Chen |
| Drama Club | Wed 4:30–6:00 p.m. | Hall | Miss Zhang |
| Chess Club | Tue & Thu 4:30–5:30 p.m. | Room 108 | Mr Liu |

② **Reading Club:** We read and discuss books every week. You will share your ideas and hear others' thoughts. We also visit the city library once a month. Bring your favourite book to the first meeting.

③ **Science Club:** We do experiments and build small projects. Topics include chemistry, physics, and robotics. You will learn by doing. Wear clothes that can get dirty. Safety first!

④ **Photography Club:** Bring your own camera or phone. We learn how to take better photos: light, angles, and composition. We will have a photo exhibition at the end of the term.

⑤ **How to sign up:** Fill in the form on the school website (www.ourschool.edu.cn/clubs) or get a paper form from the Student Office. Return it by January 10. Clubs will start from January 13. If you have questions, contact the Student Union.

Student Union

**Questions:**

1. Who can join these clubs?
   A. All students in the school.
   B. Grade 7 and 8 students only.
   C. Grade 9 students only.
   D. Teachers only.

2. How many clubs can each student join?
   A. One only.
   B. Up to two.
   C. Three or more.
   D. As many as they want.

3. When does the Science Club meet?
   A. Tuesday and Thursday.
   B. Monday and Wednesday.
   C. Friday only.
   D. Wednesday only.

4. What should students bring to the first Reading Club meeting?
   A. A camera.
   B. Their favourite book.
   C. Sportswear.
   D. Chemistry equipment.

5. By when should students return the sign-up form?
   A. December 30.
   B. January 10.
   C. January 13.
   D. February 1.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "All Grade 7 and 8 students are welcome to join."

**【解析】** 七年级和八年级学生可报名加入。A项全校学生范围过大；C项仅九年级、D项仅教师均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Each student can join up to two clubs."

**【解析】** 每位学生最多可加入两个社团。A项仅一个、C项三个以上、D项任意多个均不正确。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Science Club | Mon & Wed 4:00–5:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 科学社周一和周三下午4:00–5:00活动。A项是阅读社和棋社；C项是摄影社；D项是戏剧社。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Bring your favourite book to the first meeting."

**【解析】** 第一次阅读社会议需带自己最喜欢的书。A项相机是摄影社；C项运动服未提及；D项化学器材是科学社。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Return it by January 10."

**【解析】** 报名表需在1月10日前交回。A项12月30日是通知发布日期；C项1月13日是社团开始日；D项2月1日未提及。故选B。
