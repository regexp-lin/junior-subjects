# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should Handwritten Homework Be Replaced by Typing on Computers?**

② Today, more and more students use computers to do their homework. They type instead of writing by hand. Some people say that handwritten homework should be replaced by computer typing because it is faster and tidier. But is this really a good idea? Let us think about both sides.

③ **Those who support computer typing** say that it saves time. Students can type much faster than they write. Their work looks neat (整洁的) and is easy to read. Teachers can also read and mark homework on the computer. Besides, in the future, people will use computers more often at work. Learning to type well is a useful skill. Some students also find that they can correct their mistakes more easily when typing.

④ **Those who support handwriting** believe that writing by hand helps students remember things better. Many studies show that when we write by hand, our brain works harder. This helps us learn and remember. Handwriting also improves our motor skills (动手能力) and patience. In China, we have a long tradition of calligraphy (书法). Beautiful handwriting is still valued. Moreover, not all students have a computer at home. If homework must be typed, it may be unfair to some families.

⑤ **My opinion:** I think we should keep both. Handwriting is important for learning and for our culture. Typing is useful for speed and for future work. Schools could let students handwrite some homework and type others. In this way, students get the best of both worlds. We should not replace handwriting completely — we should use both skills in our daily study.

**注释：** neat 整洁的；motor skill 动手能力；calligraphy 书法

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ③, which is NOT an advantage of computer typing mentioned in the passage?
   A. It saves time.
   B. The work looks neat and is easy to read.
   C. Teachers can read and mark homework on the computer.
   D. It improves students' handwriting skills.

2. Why do some people think handwriting is important for learning?
   A. Because handwriting is faster than typing.
   B. Because writing by hand helps students remember things better.
   C. Because everyone has a computer at home.
   D. Because typing is too difficult for students.

3. What problem might arise if all homework must be typed?
   A. Students will write more beautifully.
   B. Teachers will have less work to do.
   C. Some students may not have a computer at home, which could be unfair.
   D. Students will spend more time outdoors.

4. What is the writer's opinion about handwritten homework and computer typing?
   A. Both handwriting and typing should be kept; students can use both for different tasks.
   B. Handwriting should be completely replaced by typing.
   C. Only typing should be used in schools.
   D. Students should never use computers for homework.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To teach readers how to type faster.
   B. To introduce the history of calligraphy.
   C. To complain about too much homework.
   D. To discuss whether handwritten homework should be replaced by typing and give a balanced view.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段列举打字优势："saves time"、"looks neat and is easy to read"、"Teachers can read and mark homework on the computer"、"Learning to type well is a useful skill"、"correct their mistakes more easily when typing"。

**【解析】** A、B、C 项均为文中提及的打字优势。D 项"提高书写技能"是手写的优势，非打字优势，故为本题答案。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Those who support handwriting believe that writing by hand helps students remember things better. Many studies show that when we write by hand, our brain works harder."

**【解析】** 支持手写者认为手写有助于记忆，研究表明手写时大脑更活跃，利于学习与记忆。B 项正确。A 项手写更快与常识不符；C、D 项均未在文中体现。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Moreover, not all students have a computer at home. If homework must be typed, it may be unfair to some families."

**【解析】** 并非所有学生家中有电脑，若作业必须打字完成，对部分家庭可能不公平。C 项正确。A、B、D 项均与原文逻辑不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 主旨理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I think we should keep both... Schools could let students handwrite some homework and type others. In this way, students get the best of both worlds."

**【解析】** 作者主张二者兼用：部分作业手写、部分打字，使学生兼顾两种技能的益处。A 项正确。B、C、D 项均与作者观点相反。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Should Handwritten Homework Be Replaced by Typing on Computers?" 及全文结构（支持打字观点 + 支持手写观点 + 作者平衡结论）

**【解析】** 文章围绕"手写作业是否应被电脑打字取代"展开讨论，呈现正反观点并给出平衡看法。D 项准确概括主旨。A 项教打字技巧、B 项介绍书法史、C 项抱怨作业多均非主旨。故选 D。
