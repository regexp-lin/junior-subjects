# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Heat Safety Tips**

② When summer comes, the weather gets very hot. It is important to know how to stay safe and cool. Here are some useful tips to help you enjoy the summer without getting sick.

③ **Drink plenty of water.** In hot weather, your body loses water quickly through sweat (汗水). You should drink water often, even if you do not feel thirsty. Avoid sugary drinks like cola. Plain water is the best choice.

④ **Wear light-coloured and loose clothes.** Dark colours take in more heat. Light colours like white or light blue can help you feel cooler. Loose clothes let air move around your body. Always wear a hat or use an umbrella when you go out in the sun. Do not stay in the sun for too long, especially between 11 a.m. and 3 p.m. when the sun is strongest.

⑤ **Stay in a cool place.** If you feel dizzy (头晕) or weak, move to a shaded or air-conditioned place at once. Rest and drink some water. If you feel worse, tell an adult and ask for help. Summer is fun — let us stay safe and healthy together!

**注释：** sweat 汗水；dizzy 头晕

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解

**【定位】** 标题 "Summer Heat Safety Tips" 及第②段 "Here are some useful tips to help you enjoy the summer without getting sick."

**【解析】** 文章标题和开头明确表明是关于夏季防暑降温、安全度夏的小贴士。B 项正确。A 项度假方式、C 项夏季运动、D 项旅游地点均非主旨。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "In hot weather, your body loses water quickly through sweat (汗水). You should drink water often, even if you do not feel thirsty."

**【解析】** 文中明确指出炎热天气下身体会通过出汗快速流失水分，因此应经常喝水。A 项正确。B、C、D 项均未在文中提及。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Wear light-coloured and loose clothes... Light colours like white or light blue can help you feel cooler. Loose clothes let air move around your body."

**【解析】** 文章建议穿浅色、宽松的衣服，浅色有助于散热，宽松衣服利于通风。C 项正确。A 项深色紧身衣、B 项厚外套、D 项黑帽长袍均与建议相反。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Do not stay in the sun for too long, especially between 11 a.m. and 3 p.m. when the sun is strongest."

**【解析】** 文中明确写出上午 11 时至下午 3 时阳光最强，应避免长时间暴晒。D 项正确。A、B、C 项时间与原文不符。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If you feel dizzy (头晕) or weak, move to a shaded or air-conditioned place at once. Rest and drink some water."

**【解析】** 若感到头晕或乏力，应立即移至阴凉或有空调的地方，休息并饮水。A 项正确。B 项快跑回家、C 项继续在阳光下走动、D 项多吃冰淇淋均与建议不符。故选 A。
