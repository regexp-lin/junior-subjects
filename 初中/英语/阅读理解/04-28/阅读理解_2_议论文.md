# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should middle school students have to do homework during the summer holidays? This is an old puzzle that causes much debate (辩论) between teachers and students every year. While many students jump with joy when summer comes, hoping for zero homework, I believe that a reasonable (合理的) amount of holiday homework is actually necessary.

② First, a long summer holiday without any studying can cause the "summer slide" (暑期滑坡). After two months of purely playing, relaxing, and sleeping late, students often forget a lot of the knowledge they learned in the past term. When the new school year starts in September, teachers have to spend weeks reviewing old lessons. A little homework over the summer keeps the brain active and helps students remember what they have learned.

③ Second, summer homework teaches teenagers self-management (自我管理). During the school term, students' lives are arranged (安排) by bells and teachers' orders. But during the holiday, they have a lot of free time. If they get some assignments (任务), they must learn how to plan their own time: deciding when to play and when to sit down and study. This kind of planning skill is very important for their future work and life.

④ However, the key word here is "reasonable". Summer holidays are, after all, a time for rest. Schools should not give students heavy tasks that take up their whole day. Homework shouldn't be endless math papers. Instead, it can be reading a few good books, carrying out a fun science experiment, or keeping an English diary. 

⑤ In conclusion, holiday homework should not be a terrible burden (负担), but a helpful bridge. It connects the old term to the new one, keeping our minds sharp (敏锐的) while still allowing us to enjoy the beautiful summer days.

**注释：** debate 辩论；reasonable 合理的；arrange 安排；assignment 任务；burden 负担；sharp 敏锐的

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what do many students hope for when summer comes?
A. Zero homework.
B. A reasonable amount of homework.
C. A lot of math papers.
D. Extra classes at school.

2. What is the "summer slide" mentioned in paragraph ②?
A. A fun water slide in the amusement park.
B. Students forgetting a lot of knowledge after a long holiday of just playing.
C. A new kind of sport played by teenagers in the summer.
D. The feeling of being very tired in the hot summer.

3. How does summer homework help students with self-management?
A. By pushing them to wake up at 6:00 AM every day.
B. By making them listen to teachers' orders from home.
C. By forcing them to plan their own free time between studying and playing.
D. By asking their parents to arrange everything for them.

4. What kind of summer homework does the writer think is suitable (合适的)?
A. Endless math papers to practice calculation.
B. Only playing computer games all day long.
C. Helping parents cook the dinner every evening.
D. Reading books, doing fun science experiments, or keeping a diary.

5. What does the writer compare "holiday homework" to in the last paragraph?
A. A terrible burden.
B. A helpful bridge.
C. A sharp knife.
D. A beautiful summer day.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "While many students jump with joy when summer comes, hoping for zero homework..."

**【解析】** 开卷点明话题，当夏天（暑假）到来时，很多学生欢呼雀跃，希望的是“零作业（zero homework）”。而作者认为需要适量作业（B项是作者立场非学生期盼的）。C和D更是南辕北辙。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测 / 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "...summer holiday without any studying can cause the 'summer slide'. After two months of purely playing... students often forget a lot of the knowledge they learned..."

**【解析】** 第二段详细解释了所谓的“暑期滑坡（summer slide）”效应：就是由于纯玩纯睡长达俩月，导致学生开学后把上学期学的知识忘光了不少的现象。这并不是说公园里的水滑梯（A）、一种新夏天运动（C）或是夏天觉得口渴疲惫（D）。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解推断

**【定位】** 第③段第3-4句 "If they get some assignments (任务), they must learn how to plan their own time: deciding when to play and when to sit down and study."

**【解析】** 暑假作业是如何锻炼自我管理能力的呢？文中指出是在有了作业任务后，由于空余时间极多，学生必须学会去主导规划自己的时间，决定何时痛快玩何时静心学。这就完全契合了C项的意思。让父母安排（D）或全听老师的（B）都不能教自我规划。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后两句 "Homework shouldn't be endless math papers. Instead, it can be reading a few good books, carrying out a fun science experiment, or keeping an English diary."

**【解析】** 作者强调了作业量的关键词是“合理的（reasonable）”，作业不该是无尽的数学卷子（A错），而可以是读几本好书、做个有趣的科学实验或是写写英文日记（选项D原样转述）。B全天玩游戏没作业，C做晚饭文中未作为适宜的典型作业列出。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1-2句 "In conclusion, holiday homework should not be a terrible burden, but a helpful bridge. It connects the old term to the new one..."

**【解析】** 最末段做总结词时作者运用了比喻：假期作业不应成为可怖的重担（A是反向描述），而应是一座“有益的桥梁（a helpful bridge）”，把旧学期与新学期连接在一起。因此比喻本体是桥梁。C与D纯属望文生义。故选B。
