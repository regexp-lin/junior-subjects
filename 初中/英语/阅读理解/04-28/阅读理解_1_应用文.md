> **04-28 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Spring Clean-up Volunteering Day**

② Spring is here, and it is time to make our beautiful town clean and green again! Are you ready to do something good for our community (社区)? Join us for the annual Spring Clean-up Volunteering Day. 

③ **When and Where?**
- **Date:** Saturday, May 14th
- **Time:** 8:00 AM - 11:30 AM
- **Meeting Point:** The central square in City Park (City Park 中心广场)

④ **What will we do?**
We will divide (划分) the volunteers into three groups:
- **Group A (Riverside):** Pick up rubbish along the Long River.
- **Group B (Parks):** Clean the benches (长椅) and sweep the paths in City Park.
- **Group C (Streets):** Remove the small advertisements (广告) on the walls of Main Street.
*You can choose a group when you sign up.*

⑤ **What do you need to bring?**
Please wear comfortable clothes and sports shoes. We will provide (提供) water, gloves, and rubbish bags for everyone. Remember to bring a hat and put on some suncream (防晒霜) if the weather is sunny.

⑥ **Who can join?**
Anyone above 10 years old can join. Children under 12 must come with their parents. This is a great chance for families to work together for the environment (环境).

⑦ **How to sign up?**
Please call Mrs. Smith at 555-0198 before Thursday, May 12th. You can also send an email to *cleanup@ourtown.org*. Let's work together to keep our town beautiful!

**注释：** community 社区；divide 划分；provide 提供；environment 环境

---

### 选择题

1. What is the main purpose of the "Spring Clean-up Volunteering Day"?
A. To sell old clothes.
B. To teach students how to swim in the river.
C. To make the town clean and green again.
D. To have a big family party in the park.

2. Which group should you join if you want to clean the benches?
A. Group A.
B. Group B.
C. Group C.
D. Any group.

3. What will the organizers (组织者) provide for the volunteers?
A. Comfortable clothes and sports shoes.
B. Small advertisements and pens.
C. Water, gloves, and rubbish bags.
D. Hats and suncream.

4. Form paragraph ⑥, what is the rule for an 11-year-old child to join?
A. He or she cannot join the event at all.
B. He or she must pay $10 to join.
C. He or she must be good at picking up rubbish.
D. He or she must come with their parents.

5. How can you sign up for the event according to the notice?
A. By calling Mrs. Smith or sending an email before May 12th.
B. By visiting the school office tomorrow.
C. By telling the teachers in the meeting point.
D. By going to the City Park center early on Saturday.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解与主旨初探

**【定位】** 第②段 "...it is time to make our beautiful town clean and green again!... Join us for the annual Spring Clean-up Volunteering Day."

**【解析】** 招募志愿者清洁日的首要目的在开篇即已阐明：“是时候让咱们美丽的小镇再次焕发洁净和绿意了！”（make our beautiful town clean and green again）。去卖旧衣服、教游泳、开派对都是毫无根据的错误选项。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2组任务 "Group B (Parks): Clean the benches (长椅) and sweep the paths in City Park."

**【解析】** 如果你想清理长椅（Clean the benches），应该申请加入哪一组呢？按照分工，这属于清理城市公园长椅和扫路的B组（Group B）的任务。A组是河滨捡垃圾，C组是除街边墙癣广告。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2句 "We will provide (提供) water, gloves, and rubbish bags for everyone."

**【解析】** 主办方会给大家提供什么呢？文中明确写了会提供水、手套和垃圾袋（water, gloves, and rubbish bags）。衣服和运动鞋，以及帽子和防晒霜，都是建议参与者自带的。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段第1-2句 "Anyone above 10 years old can join. Children under 12 must come with their parents."

**【解析】** 题目问一个11岁的小孩要想参加有什么规矩？他满足了10岁以上可以参加的门槛，但也符合“未满12岁必须与父母同行”（Children under 12 must come with their parents）的规定。因此一定要由家长陪同。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段前两句 "Please call Mrs. Smith at 555-0198 before Thursday, May 12th. You can also send an email to cleanup@ourtown.org."

**【解析】** 通告提供了两种报名途径：在5月12日周四前打电话给史密斯太太，或发电邮至指定邮箱。这与A选项完美契合。B去学校办公室、C在碰头处告诉老师、D跑到广场报名均为偏离原文信息的无稽之谈。故选A。
