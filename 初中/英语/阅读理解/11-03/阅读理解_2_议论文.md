# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Parents Read Their Children's Diaries?**

① Many teenagers keep diaries. They write down their thoughts, feelings, and secrets. It is a private space where they can be themselves. But some parents want to read their children's diaries. They say they do it out of love and worry. So should parents be allowed to read their children's diaries? Let us look at both sides.

② Those who support parents reading diaries argue that parents need to know what is going on in their children's lives. Teenagers may face problems like bullying, stress, or bad habits. If parents do not know, they cannot help. Reading a diary can be a way to protect children from danger. Some parents say they only read when they suspect something is wrong. They believe it is their duty to keep their children safe.

③ However, others believe that reading a child's diary without permission is wrong. First, diaries are private. Everyone has the right to privacy, including teenagers. When parents read diaries secretly, they break their children's trust. Second, if children find out, they may stop writing honestly. They might hide their true feelings or stop keeping a diary at all. Third, there are better ways to communicate. Parents can talk to their children openly, spend time with them, and create a safe space where children feel they can share their problems freely.

④ In my view, trust is the most important thing. If parents and children trust each other, children are more likely to share their worries. Instead of reading diaries, parents can try to have regular conversations. They can ask, "How was your day? Is there anything you want to talk about?" Showing respect for privacy often leads to better communication. Of course, if a child is in real danger, parents may need to step in. But in most cases, building trust works better than secretly reading diaries.

⑤ The question has no simple answer. Different families have different situations. What we can agree on is that love and trust should go hand in hand. Parents want to protect their children. Children need space to grow. Finding the balance is the key.

**注释：** privacy 隐私；permission 许可

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some parents read their children's diaries?
   A. To find money.
   B. To know what is going on and protect their children from danger.
   C. To punish their children.
   D. To show off to friends.

2. What is one problem with reading diaries without permission, according to Paragraph ③?
   A. Diaries are too long.
   B. It breaks children's trust and may make them stop writing honestly.
   C. Parents get bored.
   D. Children write better.

3. What does the writer suggest parents do instead of reading diaries?
   A. Never talk to their children.
   B. Have regular conversations and create a safe space for children to share.
   C. Lock the diary away.
   D. Give children more homework.

4. What does the writer think is most important between parents and children?
   A. Money.
   B. Trust.
   C. Diaries.
   D. Secrets.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. All parents should read diaries.
   B. There is no single right answer; each family needs to find a balance between protection and privacy.
   C. Children should never keep diaries.
   D. Trust is not important.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "parents need to know what is going on in their children's lives... Reading a diary can be a way to protect children from danger"
**【解析】** 支持者认为父母需要了解孩子情况，阅读日记有助于保护孩子安全。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "they break their children's trust. Second, if children find out, they may stop writing honestly"
**【解析】** 未经允许阅读会破坏信任，孩子可能不再如实书写。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "parents can try to have regular conversations... create a safe space where children feel they can share"
**【解析】** 作者建议通过日常交流、营造安全氛围，让孩子愿意主动分享。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "In my view, trust is the most important thing"
**【解析】** 作者认为信任最重要。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The question has no simple answer... Finding the balance is the key"
**【解析】** 结尾指出没有绝对答案，关键在于在保护与隐私之间找到平衡。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
