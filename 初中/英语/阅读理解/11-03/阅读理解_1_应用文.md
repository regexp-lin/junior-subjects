> **11-03 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Mid-term Exam Schedule Notice**

① Dear students and parents,

② Our school will hold the mid-term examinations from November 10th to November 12th, 2024. Please read the following schedule and arrangements carefully.

**Exam Schedule:**

| Date | Morning (9:00–11:00) | Afternoon (2:30–4:30) |
|------|----------------------|------------------------|
| Nov 10 (Mon) | Chinese | English |
| Nov 11 (Tue) | Mathematics | Physics (Grade 9 only) |
| Nov 12 (Wed) | History (Grade 8–9) | — |

③ **Important Notes:**
- Students should arrive at the exam room 15 minutes before the exam starts.
- Bring your student ID card and necessary stationery (pens, pencils, erasers). No mobile phones or smart watches are allowed in the exam room.
- Grade 7 students do not take Physics or History. Grade 8 students take History but not Physics. Grade 9 students take both.
- If you are ill and cannot take the exam, please contact your class teacher and bring a doctor's note. Make-up exams will be arranged later.

④ **Result Release:** Exam results will be announced on November 18th. Parents can check the school website or receive a notice from the class teacher.

⑤ For any questions, please contact the Teaching Office (Room 101, Building A) or call 020-8765-4321. We wish all students good luck!

Teaching Office  
November 1st, 2024

**注释：** stationery 文具；make-up exam 补考

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the mid-term exam period start?
   A. November 10th.
   B. November 1st.
   C. November 12th.
   D. November 18th.

2. Which exam is held on the morning of November 11th?
   A. Chinese.
   B. English.
   C. Mathematics.
   D. History.

3. Who needs to take the Physics exam?
   A. Grade 9 students only.
   B. All students.
   C. Grade 7 students only.
   D. Grade 8 students only.

4. What should students bring to the exam room?
   A. Mobile phones and smart watches.
   B. Student ID card and necessary stationery (pens, pencils, erasers).
   C. Only a pen.
   D. A doctor's note.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Students can use their phones during the exam.
   B. Results will be announced on November 12th.
   C. Grade 7 students take both Physics and History.
   D. Students who miss the exam due to illness can take a make-up exam later.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold the mid-term examinations from November 10th to November 12th"

**【解析】** 期中考试自11月10日开始。A项正确。B为通知发布日期；C为结束日；D为成绩公布日。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段表格 "Nov 11 (Tue) | Morning (9:00–11:00) | Mathematics"

**【解析】** 11月11日上午考数学。C项正确。A、B、D项科目或时间与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Physics (Grade 9 only)" 及 "Grade 9 students take both"

**【解析】** 物理仅九年级学生参加。A项正确。B、C、D项与原文不符。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Bring your student ID card and necessary stationery (pens, pencils, erasers)"

**【解析】** 考生需携带学生证及文具。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文要求不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "If you are ill and cannot take the exam... bring a doctor's note. Make-up exams will be arranged later"

**【解析】** 因病缺考者提交医生证明后可参加补考。D项正确。A与"no mobile phones"矛盾；B与"November 18th"矛盾；C与"Grade 7 do not take Physics or History"矛盾。故选D。
