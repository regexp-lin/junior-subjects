# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## A Bargain at the Market

①Last Sunday morning, Li Ming went to the local market with his mother. He was looking at some fruit when he noticed a foreign man standing in front of a seller's stall. The man seemed confused. He pointed at a bag of oranges and asked, "How much?" in slow English. The seller held up ten fingers and said, "Ten yuan!" But the man did not understand and looked worried.

②Li Ming had learned English at school for three years. He walked over and said to the foreigner in English, "These oranges are ten yuan per bag. Would you like to buy them?" The man's face lit up. "Oh, thank you! Can you help me ask if he can lower the price a little? I've heard that bargaining is common here." Li Ming nodded and turned to the seller. "Could you sell it for eight yuan? This gentleman is a visitor from abroad." After a short talk, the seller agreed.

③The foreigner was very happy. He bought the oranges and shook Li Ming's hand. "You're very kind. I'm from Australia. This is my first time in China. Without your help, I wouldn't have known how to communicate." Li Ming smiled. "You're welcome! I'm glad my English could help." The man took out his phone and asked if he could take a photo with Li Ming. Li Ming agreed. They said goodbye and went their separate ways.

④When Li Ming told his mother what had happened, she was proud of him. "You did a good job. Using English to help others is a wonderful thing." Li Ming felt happy. He had always thought English was just for exams. Now he understood—it could really connect people from different countries and make someone's day better.

⑤That small experience at the market made Li Ming more interested in English. He decided to practise speaking it more often. Who knows? Maybe next time he could help another visitor, or even make a new friend from across the world.

**注释：** stall 摊位；bargain 讨价还价；abroad 在国外；communicate 沟通；connect 连接

### 选择题

1. Where did the story take place?
   A. In a supermarket.
   B. At the local market.
   C. In a fruit shop.
   D. At Li Ming's school.

2. Why did the foreigner look confused at first?
   A. He could not find the market.
   B. He did not understand the seller's language and the price.
   C. He had no money.
   D. He did not like the oranges.

3. What did Li Ming do to help the foreigner?
   A. He bought the oranges for him.
   B. He translated between the foreigner and the seller, and helped bargain for a lower price.
   C. He showed the foreigner around the market.
   D. He taught the foreigner Chinese.

4. What can we infer from Paragraph 4?
   A. Li Ming's mother was angry with him.
   B. Li Ming used to think English was only useful for exams, but now he sees it can help people in real life.
   C. Li Ming decided to stop learning English.
   D. The foreigner gave Li Ming some money.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Oranges at the market are cheap.
   B. A student used his English to help a foreign visitor at a market, which changed his view of learning English.
   C. Foreigners always need help when shopping in China.
   D. Bargaining is not allowed in Chinese markets.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last Sunday morning, Li Ming went to the local market with his mother"

**【解析】** 故事发生在当地集市。B项正确。A超市、C水果店、D学校均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The man seemed confused... The seller held up ten fingers and said, 'Ten yuan!' But the man did not understand and looked worried"

**【解析】** 外国人不理解卖家的语言和报价方式（用手指比划十元），因而困惑。B项正确。A、C、D项均非文中原因。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Li Ming... said to the foreigner in English... helped ask if he could lower the price... Li Ming nodded and turned to the seller. 'Could you sell it for eight yuan?'... the seller agreed"

**【解析】** 李明用英语翻译并与卖家沟通，帮外国人砍价，最终以八元成交。B项正确。A、C、D项均与李明实际做的事不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "He had always thought English was just for exams. Now he understood—it could really connect people from different countries and make someone's day better"

**【解析】** 李明曾认为英语只为考试，通过此次经历认识到英语能在现实生活中帮助他人、连接不同国家的人。B项正确。A、C、D项均与原文矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第④段李明感悟及第⑤段决定更多练习口语

**【解析】** 文章讲述学生在集市用英语帮外国人砍价的故事，以及此事如何改变他对英语学习的看法。B项准确概括主旨。A、C、D项均偏离主题。故选B。
