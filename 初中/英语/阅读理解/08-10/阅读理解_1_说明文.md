# 阅读理解（说明文）

## Forests: Their Roles and How to Protect Them

①Forests cover about one third of our planet's land. They are often called the "lungs of the Earth" because trees take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen. Without forests, life on Earth would be very different. Let us look at the important roles forests play and what we can do to protect them.

②First, forests help clean the air. Trees take in harmful gases and release fresh oxygen that we breathe. A single tree can produce enough oxygen for two to four people every day. Forests also help control the weather. They take in rain and keep water in the soil. This prevents floods and keeps rivers full during dry seasons. Many animals and plants also live in forests. When we destroy forests, we lose their homes.

③However, forests are in danger. Every year, large areas of forest are cut down for wood, farmland or cities. Forest fires, often started by human activities, destroy millions of trees. Pollution from factories and cars also harms the air and water that forests need to grow. If we do nothing, we may lose more forests and face more serious environmental problems.

④What can we do to protect forests? We should use paper wisely—write on both sides, reuse notebooks and recycle waste paper. We can plant trees in our school or community. When we travel to forests, we must follow the rules: do not make fires in wrong places, and do not throw rubbish. We should also tell our friends and family why forests matter.

⑤Forests give us clean air, water and a home for countless living things. Protecting them is protecting our own future. Every small action counts. Let us work together to keep our forests green and healthy.

**注释：** oxygen 氧气；carbon dioxide 二氧化碳；soil 土壤；recycle 回收利用

### 选择题

1. Why are forests called the "lungs of the Earth"?
   A. Because they look like lungs.
   B. Because trees take in carbon dioxide and release oxygen, similar to how lungs work.
   C. Because forests are found only in mountains.
   D. Because they produce carbon dioxide.

2. According to Paragraph 2, what do forests do to help prevent floods?
   A. They produce more rain.
   B. They take in rain and keep water in the soil.
   C. They block the sun.
   D. They cut down rivers.

3. What are the main dangers to forests mentioned in the passage?
   A. Too many animals living in forests.
   B. Cutting down trees, forest fires, and pollution.
   C. Too much sunlight.
   D. People planting too many trees.

4. Which of the following is NOT suggested to protect forests?
   A. Using paper wisely and recycling.
   B. Planting trees.
   C. Making fires anywhere in the forest when camping.
   D. Following rules when visiting forests and not throwing rubbish.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Forests are not important for human life.
   B. Protecting forests is protecting our future; everyone can help with small actions.
   C. Only adults can protect forests.
   D. Forests will disappear completely in a few years.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "They are often called the 'lungs of the Earth' because trees take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明森林被称为"地球之肺"是因为树木吸收二氧化碳、释放氧气，与肺部气体交换功能相似。B项正确。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Forests also help control the weather. They take in rain and keep water in the soil. This prevents floods"

**【解析】** 森林吸收雨水、将水分保持在土壤中，从而预防洪水。B项正确。A项"产生更多雨水"、C项"阻挡阳光"、D项"截断河流"均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Every year, large areas of forest are cut down... Forest fires... destroy millions of trees. Pollution... harms the air and water"

**【解析】** 文中列举三大威胁：砍伐、森林火灾、污染。B项正确。A、C、D项均非文中所述的危险。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断/细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "do not make fires in wrong places, and do not throw rubbish"

**【解析】** 文中建议不要在不当处生火、不要乱扔垃圾。C项"在森林露营时随处生火"与保护森林的建议相反，故为未建议的做法。C项正确。A、B、D均为文中建议。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Protecting them is protecting our own future. Every small action counts. Let us work together"

**【解析】** 保护森林即保护我们的未来，每一个小行动都有意义，需共同行动。可推知每个人都能通过小行动为保护森林出力。B项正确。A、C、D项均与原文主旨矛盾。故选B。
