# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Homework Be Cancelled?**

① Homework has been a part of school life for a long time. Students do exercises at home to practise what they learned in class. But in recent years, some people have asked: should we cancel homework? They say it causes stress and leaves no time for rest or hobbies. So what are the arguments for and against homework?

② Those who want to cancel homework have several reasons. First, students already spend six or seven hours at school. After school, they need time to rest, play, and spend time with family. Too much homework can make students tired and unhappy. Second, some homework is repetitive. Students do the same type of exercises again and again. This may not really help them learn better. Third, homework can increase the gap between students. Those with parents who can help may do well. Those without help may fall behind. This is not fair.

③ However, others believe homework is important. It helps students review and consolidate what they learned. Practice makes perfect. Without homework, students might forget what they learned quickly. Homework also teaches students to work independently and manage their time. These skills are useful for life. Besides, not all homework is boring. Good teachers design interesting tasks that make students think and create. The key might not be to cancel homework completely, but to make it better: less quantity, higher quality, and more meaningful.

④ In my view, the answer is not simply "yes" or "no." Some homework is useful. It reinforces learning. But too much homework, or homework that is too dull, does more harm than good. Schools should think about the amount and quality of homework. They could also offer different types: reading, projects, or creative tasks, not just written exercises. Parents and students should have a voice too. If homework causes serious stress, something needs to change.

⑤ Homework will probably stay, but it may change. The goal of education is to help students learn and grow. Homework should serve that goal, not work against it. Whether we keep it, reduce it, or rethink it, we should always put students' wellbeing first.

**注释：** consolidate 巩固；reinforce 强化

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people want to cancel homework?
   A. Because homework is too easy.
   B. Because it causes stress and leaves no time for rest or hobbies.
   C. Because all students love homework.
   D. Because teachers give too little homework.

2. Which is an advantage of homework mentioned in Paragraph ③?
   A. Homework is always fun.
   B. It helps students review, consolidate learning, and develop independent work skills.
   C. Homework takes no time.
   D. Parents do all the homework.

3. What does the writer suggest in Paragraph ④?
   A. Cancel all homework at once.
   B. Improve homework quality: less quantity, higher quality, and more variety.
   C. Give more homework every day.
   D. Let students choose not to go to school.

4. What does "consolidate" mean in Paragraph ③?
   A. Forget.
   B. Strengthen or reinforce what was learned.
   C. Cancel.
   D. Copy.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Homework will disappear completely.
   B. The focus should be on students' wellbeing; homework should serve learning, not harm it.
   C. No one cares about homework.
   D. Students should never rest.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "They say it causes stress and leaves no time for rest or hobbies"
**【解析】** 反对者认为作业带来压力，挤占休息和爱好时间。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "It helps students review and consolidate what they learned... teaches students to work independently and manage their time"
**【解析】** 作业有助于复习巩固、培养独立学习和时间管理能力。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Schools should think about the amount and quality of homework... offer different types: reading, projects, or creative tasks"
**【解析】** 作者建议改善作业：控制量、提高质量、丰富形式。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第③段 "It helps students review and consolidate what they learned"
**【解析】** consolidate 与 review 并列，意为巩固、强化所学。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "We should always put students' wellbeing first... Homework should serve that goal, not work against it"
**【解析】** 应优先考虑学生身心健康，作业应服务于学习目标而非起反作用。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
