> **11-07 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Winter Holiday Community Clean-up Day**

① Dear residents and students,

② The Community Committee will hold a Clean-up Day during the winter holiday. We invite all residents, especially students, to join us. The event will take place on January 25th from 9:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. We will clean the parks, streets, and public areas in our community.

③ Volunteers will meet at the Community Centre at 8:50 a.m. We will provide gloves, rubbish bags, and cleaning tools. Please wear comfortable clothes and sports shoes. Students can get a volunteer certificate after the activity. This may be useful for your school records.

④ To sign up, please visit the Community Centre or call 0755-34567890 before January 20th. You can also register on our WeChat public account. Both individuals and families are welcome. Children under 12 should be accompanied by an adult.

⑤ Let us work together to make our community cleaner and more beautiful. We look forward to seeing you!

Community Committee  
November 7th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Clean-up Day be held?
   A. November 7th.
   B. January 20th.
   C. January 25th.
   D. February 1st.

2. What will volunteers clean?
   A. Only their own homes.
   B. Parks, streets, and public areas in the community.
   C. Only the Community Centre.
   D. Schools.

3. What will the Community Committee provide for volunteers?
   A. Lunch only.
   B. Gloves, rubbish bags, and cleaning tools.
   C. Money.
   D. New clothes.

4. How can people sign up for the activity?
   A. Only by phone.
   B. By visiting the Community Centre, calling, or registering on WeChat.
   C. Only on WeChat.
   D. By sending a letter.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Only adults can join.
   B. Students can join and get a volunteer certificate for their records.
   C. The activity is only for one person.
   D. No registration is needed.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The event will take place on January 25th from 9:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m."
**【解析】** 清洁日活动于1月25日举行。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "We will clean the parks, streets, and public areas in our community"
**【解析】** 将打扫社区的公园、街道和公共区域。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "We will provide gloves, rubbish bags, and cleaning tools"
**【解析】** 社区提供手套、垃圾袋和清洁用具。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "visit the Community Centre or call 0755-34567890... or register on our WeChat public account"
**【解析】** 可到社区中心、拨打电话或通过微信公众号报名。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②③段：特别邀请学生参加；学生可获志愿者证书并用于学业记录
**【解析】** 学生可参与活动，并获得可用于学业记录的志愿者证书。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。
