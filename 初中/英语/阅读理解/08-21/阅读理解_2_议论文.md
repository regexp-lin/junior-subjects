# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In most Chinese middle schools, students are required to wear school uniforms (校服) every day. Some students enjoy wearing them, while others wish they could wear their own clothes. Should students wear school uniforms? This topic has been discussed for many years.

② Those who support school uniforms give several reasons. First, uniforms make everyone look the same, so students do not compare clothes or feel bad about not having expensive outfits (服装). This helps create a fair and friendly environment. Second, wearing a uniform saves time in the morning. Students do not need to think about what to wear. Third, uniforms help build a sense of belonging (归属感). When students wear the same clothes, they feel like part of a team. Finally, uniforms make it easy to identify (识别) students, which improves school safety.

③ However, those who disagree also have good points. They say uniforms limit (限制) students' freedom to express themselves. Teenagers are at an age when they want to show their personality (个性), and clothes are one way to do that. Some students also complain that school uniforms are not stylish or comfortable. The designs are often too simple, and the fabric (面料) may not be suitable for very hot or cold weather. Besides, some families find it expensive to buy several sets of uniforms every year.

④ In some countries, students do not wear uniforms at all. In the UK, most schools require them, while in the US, many public schools let students choose their own clothes. Each system has its advantages and disadvantages.

⑤ In my view, school uniforms are a good idea overall, but they can be improved. Schools could let students help design the uniforms so that they are more modern and comfortable. They could also allow students to wear casual (休闲的) clothes on certain days, such as Fridays. This way, students can follow the rules while still having some freedom to express who they are.

**注释：** uniform 校服；outfit 服装；belonging 归属感；identify 识别；limit 限制；personality 个性；fabric 面料；casual 休闲的

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, wearing school uniforms helps students _______.
   A. look more fashionable
   B. save money on food
   C. avoid comparing clothes with others
   D. get higher grades in exams

2. Why do some students dislike school uniforms?
   A. Because uniforms are too expensive for all families.
   B. Because uniforms limit their freedom to express themselves.
   C. Because uniforms make them late for school.
   D. Because uniforms are dangerous.

3. What does the word "them" in Paragraph ④ refer to?
   A. Students.
   B. Teachers.
   C. School uniforms.
   D. Countries.

4. What is the writer's opinion about school uniforms?
   A. They should be completely cancelled.
   B. They are good but could be improved.
   C. All countries should require uniforms.
   D. Only girls should wear uniforms.

5. Which of the following would the writer most likely agree with?
   A. Students should never wear casual clothes at school.
   B. Schools should let students help design uniforms.
   C. Uniforms should be the same in every country.
   D. Students should buy their uniforms online.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "uniforms make everyone look the same, so students do not compare clothes or feel bad about not having expensive outfits."

**【解析】** 原文说校服让大家看起来一样，避免了攀比。A项"看起来更时尚"原文未提及；B项"省下食物费"属于无中生有；D项"考试得更高分"原文未涉及。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "They say uniforms limit students' freedom to express themselves."

**【解析】** 原文明确说反对者认为校服限制了学生表达自我的自由。A项"太贵"虽在段末提到但不是主要不满原因；C项"导致上学迟到"属于无中生有；D项"危险"原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "In the UK, most schools require them"

**【解析】** 根据上下文，本段讨论的是各国对校服的不同要求，"them"指代的是前文提到的school uniforms。A项"学生"、B项"老师"和D项"国家"在语境中不合逻辑。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "school uniforms are a good idea overall, but they can be improved."

**【解析】** 作者认为校服总体是好的，但可以改进。A项"完全取消"与作者观点相反；C项"所有国家都应要求"原文未提及；D项"只有女生该穿"属于无中生有。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Schools could let students help design the uniforms so that they are more modern and comfortable."

**【解析】** 作者在第⑤段建议让学生参与设计校服，因此最可能同意B项。A项"永远不该穿休闲服"与"allow students to wear casual clothes on certain days"矛盾；C项"各国校服应相同"原文无此观点；D项"网上买校服"属于无中生有。故选B。
