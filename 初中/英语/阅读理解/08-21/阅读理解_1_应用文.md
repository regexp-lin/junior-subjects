# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Starlight Middle School Library — Guide for Students**

② Welcome to our school library! To make sure everyone has a good reading experience, please follow these rules.

③ **Opening Hours:**

| Day | Time |
|-----|------|
| Monday – Friday | 8:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m. |
| Saturday | 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. |
| Sunday & Public Holidays | Closed |

④ **Borrowing Rules:**
- Each student may borrow up to **3 books** at a time
- Books can be kept for **14 days**
- If you need more time, you may renew (续借) once for another 7 days
- Overdue (逾期的) books: **¥0.50 per day** per book
- Lost or damaged books must be paid for at the original price

⑤ **Library Rules:**
- Keep quiet — do not talk loudly or make phone calls
- No food or drinks are allowed inside the library
- Return books to the **return box** near the entrance — do not put them back on the shelves yourself
- Handle books with care — do not write in them or fold the pages

⑥ **Special Services:**
- **Study rooms**: 4 small rooms are available for group study (book in advance at the front desk)
- **E-book corner**: Use the library computers to read e-books and search for articles
- **Story hour**: Every Friday at 4:00 p.m., a teacher reads a story aloud in the children's reading area (open to Grade 7 students)

⑦ If you have any questions, please ask the librarian at the front desk. Enjoy reading!

**注释：** renew 续借；overdue 逾期的；entrance 入口；handle 处理；librarian 图书管理员

---

**Questions:**

1. When is the library open on Saturday?
   A. 8:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.
   B. 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.
   C. 9:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.
   D. 8:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.

2. How many books can a student borrow at one time?
   A. 1 book.
   B. 2 books.
   C. 3 books.
   D. 5 books.

3. What should students do with the books they want to return?
   A. Put them back on the shelves.
   B. Give them to other students.
   C. Leave them on the desk.
   D. Put them in the return box near the entrance.

4. If a student keeps a book for 20 days without renewing, how much should he pay in overdue fees?
   A. ¥1.00.
   B. ¥3.00.
   C. ¥5.00.
   D. ¥10.00.

5. What can we learn from the "Special Services" section?
   A. Students can eat snacks in the study rooms.
   B. Grade 7 students can listen to stories on Fridays.
   C. The library has no computers.
   D. Study rooms do not need to be booked.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Saturday: 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 表格明确标注周六开放时间为上午9点到下午5点。A项是周一到周五的时间；C项和D项均为错误组合。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Each student may borrow up to 3 books at a time"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明每次最多借3本书。A项"1本"、B项"2本"和D项"5本"均与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Return books to the return box near the entrance — do not put them back on the shelves yourself"

**【解析】** 原文要求学生将书放入入口附近的还书箱。A项"放回书架"恰好是原文明确禁止的；B项"给其他学生"和C项"放在桌上"属于无中生有。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Books can be kept for 14 days" 和 "Overdue books: ¥0.50 per day per book"

**【解析】** 借阅期为14天，持书20天则超期6天。每天每本0.50元，6天即0.50×6=3.00元。A项"1元"计算天数错误；C项"5元"和D项"10元"计算均不正确。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Every Friday at 4:00 p.m., a teacher reads a story aloud in the children's reading area (open to Grade 7 students)"

**【解析】** 原文明确说每周五下午4点有故事时间，面向七年级学生。A项"在自习室吃零食"与第⑤段"No food or drinks"矛盾；C项"图书馆没有电脑"与"Use the library computers"矛盾；D项"自习室不需要预约"与"book in advance"矛盾。故选B。
