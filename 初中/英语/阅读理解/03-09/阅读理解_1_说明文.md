# 阅读理解 第1篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Have you ever imagined a home that can think for itself? With the development of technology, smart homes are becoming more and more common around the world. A smart home uses the internet to connect different devices in the house, making life easier and more comfortable.

② So how does a smart home work? You can use your phone or even your voice to control many things at home. For example, you can turn on the lights, change the room temperature, lock the door, or start the washing machine — all without leaving your sofa. Some smart homes can also learn your daily habits over time. If you always wake up at 7:00 am, the system will open the curtains and start making coffee for you automatically at that time every morning.

③ Smart homes can also help people save energy. The system can learn when you are at home and when you are away. It will lower the heating or turn off the air conditioning when nobody is home, which helps cut down on electricity bills. Smart lights can turn off by themselves when you leave a room, so no energy is wasted.

④ Safety is another big advantage. Smart cameras can watch your home 24 hours a day. If someone tries to break in, the system will send a warning to your phone right away. If there is a fire, the system can also call for help automatically.

⑤ Of course, smart homes are not perfect. They can be expensive to set up, and some people worry about privacy — what if hackers get into the system and steal personal information? But as technology improves and prices go down, more families are expected to enjoy the benefits of smart homes in the future.

---

**注释：** device 设备；privacy 隐私；hacker 黑客

---

### 题目

1. What does a smart home use to connect different devices?
   A. Radio signals.
   B. Television cables.
   C. Telephone lines.
   D. The internet.

2. According to Paragraph ②, what can a smart home do if you always wake up at 7:00 am?
   A. It will open the curtains and make coffee for you.
   B. It will wake you up with loud music.
   C. It will make your breakfast and pack your bag.
   D. It will send a message to your teacher.

3. How do smart lights help save energy according to Paragraph ③?
   A. They use special batteries.
   B. They are brighter than normal lights.
   C. They change color to use less power.
   D. They turn off by themselves when you leave a room.

4. We can infer from Paragraph ⑤ that in the future, smart homes will ________.
   A. become more expensive than now
   B. become more common as prices go down
   C. be replaced by other technology
   D. only be used by rich families

5. What is the passage mainly about?
   A. How to set up a smart home by yourself.
   B. Why technology is bad for families.
   C. The features, benefits, and future of smart homes.
   D. The history of the internet.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "A smart home uses the internet to connect different devices in the house, making life easier and more comfortable."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明智能家居使用互联网（the internet）来连接家中的不同设备。D 项与原文完全一致。A 项"无线电信号"原文未提及；B 项"电视电缆"属于无中生有；C 项"电话线"也未在原文中出现。故选 D。

---

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "If you always wake up at 7:00 am, the system will open the curtains and start making coffee for you automatically at that time every morning."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明如果你总是7点起床，系统会自动为你拉开窗帘并开始煮咖啡。A 项"会拉开窗帘并为你煮咖啡"与原文完全吻合。B 项"用大声的音乐叫醒你"原文未提及；C 项"做早餐并帮你整理书包"属于过度推断——原文只提到煮咖啡和开窗帘；D 项"给你的老师发消息"完全无中生有。故选 A。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "Smart lights can turn off by themselves when you leave a room, so no energy is wasted."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出智能灯可以在你离开房间时自动关闭，从而不浪费能源。D 项"当你离开房间时它们会自动关闭"与原文完全对应。A 项"它们使用特殊电池"原文未提及电池；B 项"它们比普通灯更亮"原文未讨论亮度；C 项"它们通过变色来减少能耗"也属于无中生有。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "But as technology improves and prices go down, more families are expected to enjoy the benefits of smart homes in the future."

**【解析】** 原文表示随着技术进步和价格下降，预计未来将有更多家庭享受智能家居的好处。由此可以推断智能家居将随着价格降低而变得更加普及。B 项"随着价格下降会变得更普遍"是对原文的合理推断。A 项"比现在更贵"与原文"prices go down"矛盾；C 项"被其他技术取代"原文未提及替代问题；D 项"只有富裕家庭才能使用"与"more families"的表述矛盾——原文暗示普及面会更广。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 推理判断题中 "as...go down, more families..." 的因果逻辑是关键线索：价格下降→更多家庭使用→更加普及。注意区分合理推断与过度推断。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文结构：第①段引入智能家居概念 → 第②段介绍工作方式 → 第③段节能优势 → 第④段安全优势 → 第⑤段不足与未来展望。

**【解析】** 全文围绕智能家居展开，先介绍了什么是智能家居以及它如何运作，接着从节能和安全两个方面阐述了其优势，最后讨论了存在的问题和未来发展前景。C 项"智能家居的功能、好处和未来"最全面地概括了文章内容。A 项"如何自己搭建智能家居"——文章是科普介绍而非操作指南；B 项"为什么科技对家庭有害"与文章主要传达的积极态度相反；D 项"互联网的历史"以偏概全——互联网只是智能家居的技术基础之一。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 说明文主旨题需纵观全文各段的核心内容。正确选项应能涵盖文章的主要方面，而非仅涉及某一段的细节。可以用"各段首句归纳法"快速锁定主旨。
