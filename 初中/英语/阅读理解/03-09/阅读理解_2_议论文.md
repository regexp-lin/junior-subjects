> **03-09 第2篇**

# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Reading is important for everyone, especially for students. But with the development of technology, more and more people are choosing to read on screens — phones, tablets, and e-readers — instead of reading paper books. So which is better: paper books or e-books?

② Some people believe that e-books are more convenient. First, you can carry thousands of books on one small device. This is much easier than carrying heavy paper books in your school bag. Second, e-books are usually cheaper than paper books, and some are even free to download. Third, you can change the size of the words on a screen, which is helpful for people with poor eyesight.

③ However, many people still prefer paper books. Studies show that people who read paper books remember more information than those who read on screens. This may be because holding a real book and turning pages helps the brain pay more attention. Also, reading on screens for a long time can be bad for your eyes. The blue light from screens can cause eye tiredness and headaches, and may even affect your sleep.

④ There is also something special about paper books that e-books cannot offer. Many readers love the smell of a new book and the feeling of turning pages. Sharing a favorite book with a friend or keeping it on a bookshelf at home gives people a sense of happiness. As one famous writer said, "A room without books is like a body without a soul."

⑤ In my opinion, both paper books and e-books have their advantages. The most important thing is not how you read, but that you read. Whether you pick up a paper book or open an e-book on your phone, reading will always make you a better person.

---

**注释：** e-reader 电子阅读器；eyesight 视力；soul 灵魂

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ②, why are e-books convenient for students?
   A. Because students can read them in the dark.
   B. Because e-books have more interesting stories.
   C. Because students can change the cover of e-books.
   D. Because students can carry many books on one small device.

2. What does Paragraph ③ tell us about reading on screens for a long time?
   A. It can cause eye tiredness and headaches.
   B. It helps people remember more information.
   C. It is better for your sleep.
   D. It improves your eyesight.

3. What is special about paper books according to Paragraph ④?
   A. They are cheaper than e-books.
   B. They are easier to carry around.
   C. People love the smell and feeling of them.
   D. They have bigger words than e-books.

4. The underlined sentence "A room without books is like a body without a soul" in Paragraph ④ means ________.
   A. books are not needed in every room
   B. books are very important and give life meaning
   C. rooms without books are always empty
   D. people should exercise more to stay healthy

5. What is the writer's opinion about paper books and e-books?
   A. E-books will replace paper books soon.
   B. Paper books are always better than e-books.
   C. Both are bad for students' health.
   D. Both have advantages, and reading itself is what matters most.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "First, you can carry thousands of books on one small device. This is much easier than carrying heavy paper books in your school bag."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出电子书方便之处在于可以在一个小设备上携带数千本书，这比背着沉重的纸质书轻松得多。D 项"因为学生可以在一个小设备上携带很多书"与原文对应。A 项"在黑暗中阅读"原文未提及；B 项"有更有趣的故事"偷换概念——电子书的优势是载体便捷，而非内容更好；C 项"可以改变封面"属于无中生有。故选 D。

---

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第4-5句 "Also, reading on screens for a long time can be bad for your eyes. The blue light from screens can cause eye tiredness and headaches, and may even affect your sleep."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明长时间看屏幕可能伤害眼睛，屏幕蓝光会导致眼睛疲劳和头痛，甚至影响睡眠。A 项"会导致眼睛疲劳和头痛"与原文完全吻合。B 项"帮助人们记住更多信息"与原文相反——原文说的是读纸质书记得更多；C 项"对睡眠更好"与原文"may even affect your sleep"矛盾；D 项"改善视力"与原文"can be bad for your eyes"直接矛盾。故选 A。

---

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "Many readers love the smell of a new book and the feeling of turning pages."

**【解析】** 原文指出纸质书的特别之处在于很多读者喜欢新书的气味和翻页的感觉，这是电子书无法提供的。C 项"人们喜欢纸质书的气味和触感"与原文对应。A 项"比电子书便宜"与第②段信息矛盾——原文说电子书通常更便宜；B 项"更容易携带"同样与原文矛盾——电子书在便携性上更有优势；D 项"字比电子书更大"原文未提及，且第②段说电子书可以调节字体大小。故选 C。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "As one famous writer said, 'A room without books is like a body without a soul.'"

**【解析】** 这句话用了比喻修辞：将没有书的房间比作没有灵魂的身体。灵魂（soul）是人的精神核心，没有灵魂的身体就失去了生命意义。同理，没有书的房间也缺少了精神和文化的滋养。因此这句话表达的是"书非常重要，赋予生活意义"。B 项最准确地传达了这一含义。A 项"不是每个房间都需要书"与原句的强调意义相反；C 项"没有书的房间总是空的"将比喻误解为字面意义——原文不是在说物理空间的空与满；D 项"人们应该多锻炼以保持健康"与原文内容完全无关，将"body"误解为身体健康的话题。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 遇到名言或比喻句的理解题，要分析比喻的本体和喻体之间的关系。本题中"room without books"对应"body without a soul"，关键在于理解"soul"（灵魂/精神核心）被用来类比"books"的重要性。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, both paper books and e-books have their advantages. The most important thing is not how you read, but that you read."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段表明自己的观点：纸质书和电子书各有优势，最重要的不是用什么方式阅读，而是阅读本身。D 项"两者各有优势，阅读本身才是最重要的"准确概括了作者的核心观点。A 项"电子书将很快取代纸质书"与作者两者并重的态度矛盾；B 项"纸质书总是比电子书好"以偏概全——作者承认两者都有优势，并未厚此薄彼；C 项"两者都对学生健康有害"歪曲了原文——只有长时间看屏幕可能伤眼，纸质书并不被认为有害。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 议论文中作者的观点通常出现在最后一段的总结性语句中。"In my opinion"是明确的观点信号词，后面的内容即为作者的核心立场。注意主旨题要选最全面的概括，不能只选涵盖部分段落内容的选项。
