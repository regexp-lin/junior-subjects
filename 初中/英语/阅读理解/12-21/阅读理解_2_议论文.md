# 阅读理解（议论文）

**What Does "Sports Spirit" Really Mean?**

① We often hear the phrase "sports spirit" at school or on TV. But what does it really mean? Is it only about winning? Or is there something more important? Let me share my thoughts.

② First, sports spirit means never giving up. In a match, you may fall behind. The other team may score more points. But true athletes do not stop trying. They run faster, pass the ball better, and encourage their teammates. Even when they lose, they learn from the game and come back stronger next time. This attitude helps us in life too. When we face difficulties in study or work, we remember: keep trying.

③ Second, sports spirit means fair play. Rules matter. We should not cheat to win. We respect the referee and our opponents. Shaking hands after a match, whether we win or lose, shows respect. Fair play builds trust and makes sports meaningful. If everyone cheated, sports would lose their value.

④ Third, sports spirit means teamwork. In basketball, football, or relay races, no one can win alone. Players must pass, support, and trust each other. The best teams are those where every member works for the team, not for themselves. Teamwork teaches us how to get along with others and achieve goals together.

⑤ So, sports spirit is not just about winning. It is about effort, fairness, and working together. These values can guide us both on the field and in everyday life. That is the real power of sports.

**注释：** phrase 短语；referee 裁判；opponent 对手；relay 接力

---

**Questions:**

1. According to the writer, what does "never giving up" mean in sports?
   A. Winning every match.
   B. Keeping trying even when falling behind; learning from failure.
   C. Never playing again after losing.
   D. Only playing when you are sure to win.

2. Why is fair play important?
   A. Because it helps us run faster.
   B. Because it builds trust and makes sports meaningful.
   C. Because it makes us stronger.
   D. Because the referee likes it.

3. What should players do after a match to show respect?
   A. Argue with the referee.
   B. Shake hands whether they win or lose.
   C. Leave the field quickly.
   D. Only shake hands when they win.

4. Why can no one win alone in team sports?
   A. Because the rules say so.
   B. Because players must pass, support, and trust each other.
   C. Because the coach asks them to.
   D. Because there are too many people.

5. What is the main idea of the passage?
   A. Winning is the only thing that matters in sports.
   B. Sports spirit includes effort, fairness, and teamwork; it guides us in life.
   C. Teamwork is more important than individual skill.
   D. We should never play sports if we might lose.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "But true athletes do not stop trying... Even when they lose, they learn from the game and come back stronger next time."

**【解析】** 永不放弃意味着落后时继续努力、从失败中学习。A项每场都赢过于绝对；C项输后不再玩与原文相反；D项只在你确定能赢时玩与永不放弃矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Fair play builds trust and makes sports meaningful."

**【解析】** 公平竞争建立信任并赋予体育意义。A项跑更快、C项变更强、D项裁判喜欢均非公平竞争的主要意义。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Shaking hands after a match, whether we win or lose, shows respect."

**【解析】** 无论输赢，赛后握手表达尊重。A项与裁判争吵是错误行为；C项快速离开、D项只赢时握手均不符合原文。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "In basketball, football, or relay races, no one can win alone. Players must pass, support, and trust each other."

**【解析】** 团体运动中需要传接、互助、信任，无法独自获胜。A项规则、C项教练、D项人多均非根本原因。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "So, sports spirit is not just about winning. It is about effort, fairness, and working together. These values can guide us both on the field and in everyday life."

**【解析】** 主旨是体育精神包含努力、公平、团队合作，能指导我们在赛场和生活中的行为。A项与"not just about winning"矛盾；C、D项过于片面。故选B。
