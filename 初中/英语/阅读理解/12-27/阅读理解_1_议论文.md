# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Global Warming: What Can We Do?**

① The Earth is getting warmer. Scientists call this "global warming." Ice at the North and South Poles is melting. Sea levels are rising. Some places have more floods and storms. Others have longer droughts. Why is this happening? Mainly because of human activities. We burn coal, oil, and gas for energy. We cut down too many trees. These actions release a lot of carbon dioxide into the air. The gas traps heat and makes the Earth hotter.

② Some people think that global warming is a problem for governments and big companies only. They are wrong. Each one of us can make a difference. Small changes, when millions of people do them, can have a big effect. So what can we do in our daily life?

③ First, save energy. Turn off the lights when you leave a room. Use energy-saving bulbs. Unplug your phone charger when it is not in use. Take the bus or ride a bike instead of asking your parents to drive you short distances. Every kilowatt-hour of electricity we save means less coal burned and less carbon dioxide in the air.

④ Second, reduce waste. Buy only what you need. Reuse bags, bottles, and containers. Recycle paper, plastic, and cans. When we throw away less, we produce less. Making new things from recycled materials uses less energy than making them from scratch. So recycling helps reduce carbon emissions too.

⑤ Third, plant trees. Trees take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen. They are natural "air cleaners." If your school has a tree-planting activity, join it. If your family has a garden, plant a tree or some flowers. Encourage others to care for the environment. The Earth is our home. Protecting it is everyone's job.

**注释：** melt 融化；drought 干旱；carbon dioxide 二氧化碳；trap 困住；emit 排放

---

**Questions:**

1. What is the main cause of global warming according to the passage?
   A. Natural disasters.
   B. Human activities: burning fuels, cutting trees, releasing carbon dioxide.
   C. Too many plants.
   D. Cold weather.

2. Why does the writer say each person can make a difference?
   A. Because only rich people can help.
   B. Because small changes by millions of people can have a big effect.
   C. Because governments do nothing.
   D. Because companies will solve the problem.

3. How can we save energy at home?
   A. Leave lights on all day.
   B. Turn off lights when leaving, use energy-saving bulbs, unplug chargers when not in use.
   C. Use more air conditioning.
   D. Drive everywhere.

4. How does recycling help fight global warming?
   A. It does not help.
   B. Making things from recycled materials uses less energy, so less carbon is emitted.
   C. Recycling makes things heavier.
   D. Recycling is only for companies.

5. Why are trees called natural "air cleaners"?
   A. Because they are green.
   B. Because they take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen.
   C. Because they grow fast.
   D. Because they need no care.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Mainly because of human activities. We burn coal, oil, and gas for energy. We cut down too many trees. These actions release a lot of carbon dioxide into the air."

**【解析】** 全球变暖主要由人类活动导致：燃烧化石燃料、砍伐森林、排放大量二氧化碳。A项自然灾害、C项植物过多、D项寒冷天气均非主要原因。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Small changes, when millions of people do them, can have a big effect."

**【解析】** 每个人的小改变，乘以数百万人，就能产生大影响。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Turn off the lights when you leave a room. Use energy-saving bulbs. Unplug your phone charger when it is not in use."

**【解析】** 节能做法包括：离开关灯、使用节能灯、不用时拔掉充电器。A项整天开灯、C项多用空调、D项到处开车均与节能相反。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Making new things from recycled materials uses less energy than making them from scratch. So recycling helps reduce carbon emissions too."

**【解析】** 用回收材料制造比从零生产更省能源，从而减少碳排放。A项不帮助错误；C、D项与减排无关。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Trees take in carbon dioxide and give out oxygen. They are natural 'air cleaners.'"

**【解析】** 树木吸收二氧化碳、释放氧气，因此被称为天然空气清洁器。A项颜色、C项长得快、D项无需照料均非"清洁空气"的原因。故选B。
