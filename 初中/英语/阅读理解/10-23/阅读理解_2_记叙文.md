# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Summer Camp — Living on My Own**

① Last summer, my parents sent me to a week-long summer camp. It was the first time I had slept away from home. I was twelve years old. I felt excited but also nervous. What if I could not fall asleep? What if I missed my family too much?

② On the first day, we arrived at the camp by bus. The camp was in the mountains, surrounded by trees. We shared a room with four other boys. I had to make my own bed, arrange my things, and wake up at 6:30 every morning. At home, my mum always did these for me. Now I had to do everything myself.

③ The first night was the hardest. I lay in bed and thought about home. I almost cried. But I did not want the other boys to see. I closed my eyes and tried to sleep. The next morning, we had outdoor activities — hiking, teamwork games, and a campfire. I made new friends. We laughed and played together. I started to feel better.

④ By the third day, I could make my bed quickly. I helped clean our room. I even taught a younger boy how to tie his shoelaces. One evening, we had a talent show. I sang a song. Everyone clapped. I felt proud. For the first time, I realised I could do things on my own.

⑤ When the week ended, I did not want to leave. I hugged my new friends and promised to keep in touch. On the way home, I told my parents, "I am not a little kid anymore. I can take care of myself." That summer camp taught me that independence is something we grow into, step by step.

**注释：** campfire 篝火；shoelace 鞋带

---

**Questions:**

1. How long did the summer camp last?
   A. One day.
   B. One week.
   C. One month.
   D. Two weeks.

2. What made the first night difficult for the writer?
   A. The room was too cold.
   B. He missed his family and lay in bed thinking about home.
   C. The other boys were noisy.
   D. He was hungry.

3. What did the writer do at the talent show?
   A. He danced.
   B. He told a joke.
   C. He sang a song.
   D. He played the piano.

4. What did the writer teach a younger boy?
   A. How to make a bed.
   B. How to tie his shoelaces.
   C. How to sing.
   D. How to hike.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer disliked the camp.
   B. The camp helped the writer grow; independence develops step by step.
   C. The writer will never go to camp again.
   D. Parents should not send children to camp.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "a week-long summer camp"
**【解析】** 夏令营为期一周。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "I lay in bed and thought about home. I almost cried"
**【解析】** 第一晚作者躺在床上想家，几乎哭出来。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "we had a talent show. I sang a song"
**【解析】** 才艺表演上作者唱了一首歌。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "I even taught a younger boy how to tie his shoelaces"
**【解析】** 作者教一个更小的男孩系鞋带。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I am not a little kid anymore. I can take care of myself... independence is something we grow into, step by step"
**【解析】** 夏令营让作者意识到自己在成长，独立是逐步习得的。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
