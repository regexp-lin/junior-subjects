# 阅读理解（说明文）

**The Meaning of the Beijing Winter Olympics**

① The 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics was a special event. It was the first Winter Games to be held in China. But its meaning goes far beyond sports. It left a lasting impact on the country and the world.

② First, the Games brought the world together. At a time when the pandemic made travel difficult, thousands of athletes from around the world came to Beijing. They competed, shared their cultures, and built friendships. The Olympic spirit of "together" was shown in full. People from different countries connected through sport.

③ Second, the Games promoted winter sports in China. Before 2022, few Chinese people had tried skiing or skating. The Olympics raised interest in these activities. Now many new ski resorts and ice rinks have been built. More children learn to ski and skate. Winter sports are no longer just for the north; they have spread to the south too.

④ Third, the Games showed China's ability to hold a major international event. From the high-tech venues to the friendly volunteers, the world saw a modern and welcoming China. The "Ice Ribbon" speed skating venue and the "Snowflake" cauldron became famous. The Games also promoted green and sustainable ideas. Much of the ice was made with new eco-friendly technology.

⑤ The Beijing Winter Olympics was more than a competition. It was a bridge between nations, a push for winter sports, and a display of China's progress. Its legacy will continue for years to come.

**注释：** venue 场馆；cauldron 圣火台；legacy 遗产

**Questions:**

1. Why was the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics special?
   A. Because it was the longest Games.
   B. Because it was the first Winter Games held in China.
   C. Because no athletes came.
   D. Because it was cancelled.

2. According to Paragraph ②, what did the Olympics show during the pandemic?
   A. Only sports mattered.
   B. The Olympic spirit of "together" — people from different countries connected through sport.
   C. Travel was easy.
   D. No one cared about the Games.

3. What change did the Games bring to winter sports in China?
   A. Winter sports became less popular.
   B. More people became interested in skiing and skating; new venues were built.
   C. All ski resorts were closed.
   D. Only northern China has winter sports now.

4. What is the "Ice Ribbon"?
   A. A type of food.
   B. The speed skating venue.
   C. A kind of snow.
   D. The Olympic mascot.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The Olympics had no effect on China.
   B. The Beijing Winter Olympics had multiple meanings: connecting nations, promoting winter sports, and showing China's progress.
   C. Winter sports will disappear in China.
   D. The Games were only about winning medals.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "It was the first Winter Games to be held in China"
**【解析】** 北京冬奥会是首次在中国举办的冬季奥运会。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The Olympic spirit of 'together' was shown in full. People from different countries connected through sport"
**【解析】** 奥运展现了"更团结"的精神，各国通过体育相连。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "The Olympics raised interest... many new ski resorts and ice rinks have been built. More children learn to ski and skate"
**【解析】** 冬奥提升了人们对滑雪、滑冰的兴趣，新建了许多场馆，更多孩子参与冰雪运动。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "The 'Ice Ribbon' speed skating venue"
**【解析】** "冰丝带"是速度滑冰场馆。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "a bridge between nations, a push for winter sports, and a display of China's progress"
**【解析】** 北京冬奥会具有多重意义：连通各国、推动冰雪运动、展示中国发展。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
