# 阅读理解（议论文）

## Should Students Be Allowed to Bring Snacks to School?

① In many schools, students are not allowed to bring snacks from home. They can only buy food from the school canteen (食堂) or eat meals provided by the school. However, some parents and students think this rule is too strict. They believe that students should be allowed to bring their own snacks. Is it a good idea? Let us look at both sides of the argument.

② **Those who support allowing snacks have their reasons.** First, some students have special health needs and may need to eat at a certain time. Second, school canteen food may not suit everyone; snacks give more choices. Third, bringing snacks from home can save money when canteen prices are high. Fourth, parents can pack healthy snacks such as fruit and nuts (坚果).

③ **Those who are against it also have strong points.** One worry is unhealthy food—chips, candy, and sugary drinks. Eating too much can lead to obesity (肥胖) and tooth problems. Another problem is that wrappers (包装纸) and leftovers can make the classroom messy and attract ants. A third concern is fairness: some students may bring expensive snacks and show off, making others feel bad. Besides, eating in class can distract (使分心) students from their lessons.

④ **What might be a balanced solution?** Schools could allow only certain snacks—fruit, vegetables, nuts, or plain biscuits. Junk food and sugary drinks could be banned. Students could eat only during the break, not during class. Teachers and parents could work together to teach healthy eating. In this way, students gain some freedom while the school keeps the environment clean and healthy.

⑤ In conclusion, there are both advantages and disadvantages to allowing snacks at school. The key is to find a balance: permit healthy snacks, set clear rules, and help students form good eating habits. A one-size-fits-all (一刀切) rule may not work for everyone.

**注释：** canteen 食堂；nut 坚果；obesity 肥胖；wrapper 包装纸；distract 使分心；one-size-fits-all 一刀切的

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, which of the following is a reason to support allowing snacks?
   A. Snacks always make the classroom dirty.
   B. Students with special health needs may need to eat at certain times; snacks can also save money.
   C. All snacks are unhealthy.
   D. Snacks help students focus better in class.

2. What health problem might unhealthy snacks cause?
   A. They make students study harder.
   B. They can lead to obesity and tooth problems.
   C. They improve students' memory.
   D. They help students grow taller.

3. Why do some people worry about "fairness" when students bring snacks?
   A. Because all students have the same snacks.
   B. Because some students may bring expensive snacks and show off, making others feel bad.
   C. Because snacks are free for everyone.
   D. Because teachers cannot eat snacks.

4. What does the writer suggest as a balanced solution?
   A. Ban all snacks completely.
   B. Allow only healthy snacks like fruit and nuts; ban junk food; eat only during break.
   C. Let students eat anything they want in class.
   D. Close the school canteen.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Snacks are always bad and should never be allowed.
   B. Schools must provide free snacks for all students.
   C. There are pros and cons; we should find a balance—allow healthy snacks, set rules, and help form good habits.
   D. Only rich students should bring snacks.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "some students have special health needs and may need to eat at a certain time... bringing snacks from home can save money when canteen prices are high"

**【解析】** 支持理由包括：特殊健康需求需定时进食、增加选择、为家庭省钱等。B项概括正确。A项为反对理由；C项绝对化错误；D项未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Eating too much can lead to obesity (肥胖) and tooth problems"

**【解析】** 过量食用不健康零食可导致肥胖和牙齿问题。B项正确。A、C、D项均为正面效果，与健康危害不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "A third concern is fairness: some students may bring expensive snacks and show off, making others feel bad"

**【解析】** 公平性担忧指部分学生可能带昂贵零食炫耀，使其他学生感到不适。B项准确。A、C项与"show off"矛盾；D项与教师无关。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Schools could allow only certain snacks—fruit, vegetables, nuts, or plain biscuits. Junk food and sugary drinks could be banned. Students could eat only during the break, not during class"

**【解析】** 作者建议：仅允许健康零食、禁止垃圾食品和含糖饮料、仅课间食用。B项正确。A项完全禁止与建议矛盾；C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第①段引入话题、第②③段正反观点、第④⑤段"In conclusion... find a balance: permit healthy snacks, set clear rules, and help students form good eating habits"

**【解析】** 文章讨论校园带零食利弊，主张平衡方案：允许健康零食、制定规则、培养良好饮食习惯。C项准确概括主旨。A项全面禁止、B项学校提供、D项仅富家学生均偏离主旨。故选C。
