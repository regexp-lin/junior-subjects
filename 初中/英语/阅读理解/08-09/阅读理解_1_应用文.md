# 阅读理解（应用文）

## Summer Reading Check-in Activity — Rules and Guide

① To help students develop good reading habits during the summer holiday, our school will hold a reading check-in (打卡) activity from July 15th to August 25th. All students in Grades 7–9 are welcome to join. Read the rules below before you take part.

② **How to Join:** First, follow our school's official WeChat account. Then, send a photo of the book cover and the page you are reading each day. Write the date and the book title in your post. You must read for at least 30 minutes every day to count as one successful check-in.

③ **What to Read:** You may choose any book you like—storybooks, science books, history books, or magazines. The book should be suitable (适合) for your age. Comic books and picture books without much text do not count. You can read in Chinese or in English.

④ **Prizes and Certificates:** Students who complete 30 days of check-in will get a certificate (证书). Students who complete 40 days or more will get a small gift—a bookmark or a notebook. The top 10 readers with the most check-in days will be invited to share their reading experience at the opening ceremony in September.

⑤ **Important Notes:** Do not copy others' photos. The school will check your posts. If you break the rules, your check-in record will be cancelled. If you have any questions, please contact Ms. Chen at chen@school.edu before July 10th. Happy reading!

**注释：** check-in 打卡；suitable 适合的；certificate 证书

---

### 选择题

1. When will the summer reading activity take place?
   A. From June 15th to July 25th.
   B. From July 15th to August 25th.
   C. During the winter holiday.
   D. Only on weekends in July and August.

2. What should students do to complete one check-in?
   A. Just read any book for 5 minutes.
   B. Send a photo with the date and book title, and read for at least 30 minutes.
   C. Only follow the school's WeChat account.
   D. Read comic books every day.

3. What kind of books does the passage say do NOT count?
   A. Storybooks and science books.
   B. Comic books and picture books without much text.
   C. History books and magazines.
   D. Books in English.

4. What will students get if they complete 40 days of check-in?
   A. Only a certificate.
   B. A small gift such as a bookmark or a notebook.
   C. A free book from the school.
   D. Nothing special.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Students can copy others' photos as long as nobody finds out.
   B. The activity is only for Grade 9 students.
   C. Students must contact Ms. Chen before July 10th if they want to ask questions.
   D. Comic books are the best choice for the activity.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "our school will hold a reading check-in activity from July 15th to August 25th"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明活动时间为7月15日至8月25日。B项正确。A项日期错误；C项寒假、D项仅周末均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "send a photo of the book cover and the page you are reading each day. Write the date and the book title in your post. You must read for at least 30 minutes every day to count as one successful check-in"

**【解析】** 需发送封面和阅读页照片、注明日期和书名，且每日至少阅读30分钟才算打卡成功。B项准确。A项5分钟错误；C项仅关注公众号不完整；D项漫画书不符合要求。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Comic books and picture books without much text do not count"

**【解析】** 漫画书和文字过少的图画书不计入。B项正确。A、C项均为可选的类型；D项英语书可以。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Students who complete 40 days or more will get a small gift—a bookmark or a notebook"

**【解析】** 完成40天及以上可获得书签或笔记本等小礼品。B项正确。A项证书为30天即可获得；C、D项未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If you have any questions, please contact Ms. Chen at chen@school.edu before July 10th"

**【解析】** 若有疑问需在7月10日前联系陈老师，可推知咨询截止日期为7月10日。C项正确。A项与"Do not copy others' photos"矛盾；B项与"All students in Grades 7–9"矛盾；D项与漫画书不计入矛盾。故选C。
