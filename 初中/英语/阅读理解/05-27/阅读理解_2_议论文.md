# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should middle school students attend after-school interest classes? This question has caused much debate among parents, teachers and students. Some believe such classes help children develop skills and hobbies, while others worry they add too much pressure.

② **Those who support after-school interest classes** point out several advantages. First, students can discover and develop their talents — in music, sports, art or science. These classes can build confidence and make school life more colourful. Second, interest classes provide chances to meet new friends who share similar hobbies. Third, learning a skill like playing the piano or swimming can bring joy and relaxation, which may actually help students feel less stressed about their studies.

③ **However, some people argue that** middle school students already have heavy workloads. They spend long hours in class and doing homework. Adding more lessons in the evening or at weekends can make students feel tired and leave them little time to rest or play. Moreover, some parents choose classes for their children based on their own wishes, not the child's real interests. This may lead to boredom or even dislike of learning. In addition, interest classes often cost money, which can be a burden for some families.

④ **In my view**, there is no simple yes-or-no answer. Interest classes can be helpful if students truly enjoy them and have enough time for rest. The key is to respect children's choices and not overload them. Parents should talk with their children before signing up for any class. One or two interest classes may be enough; more than that might do more harm than good.

⑤ In short, after-school interest classes can support growth and happiness when chosen wisely. But students need time to relax and enjoy their childhood too.

**注释：** debate 辩论；talent 天赋；workload 课业负担；burden 负担；overload 使负担过重

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, which benefit do after-school interest classes bring?
   A. They help students finish homework faster.
   B. They allow students to discover talents, build confidence and make new friends with similar hobbies.
   C. They reduce the cost of education for families.
   D. They replace all school classes.

2. Why do some people oppose interest classes for middle school students?
   A. Because students already have heavy workloads, and extra classes may make them tired and leave little time to rest; some parents also choose classes based on their own wishes.
   B. Because students never enjoy any hobbies.
   C. Because interest classes are always free.
   D. Because schools do not allow such classes.

3. What problem might happen when parents choose interest classes for their children based on their own wishes?
   A. The classes will become cheaper.
   B. Children may feel bored or dislike learning.
   C. Schools will close down.
   D. Children will make more friends.

4. What does the writer suggest parents should do before signing up for an interest class?
   A. Talk with their children and respect their choices; avoid overloading them.
   B. Buy the most expensive class available.
   C. Never let children join any interest class.
   D. Choose classes based only on parents' wishes.

5. What can we infer from the passage about the writer's attitude?
   A. The writer strongly disagrees with all after-school interest classes.
   B. The writer believes interest classes are only for rich families.
   C. The writer thinks students should attend as many interest classes as possible.
   D. The writer takes a balanced view: interest classes can be good when chosen wisely and not overdone, but students also need time to relax.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "students can discover and develop their talents... build confidence and make school life more colourful" 以及 "interest classes provide chances to meet new friends who share similar hobbies."

**【解析】** 支持方认为兴趣班能帮助学生发现和培养天赋、树立自信、结交志同道合的朋友。B项概括准确。A项"更快完成作业"未提及；C项"降低教育成本"与文中"cost money"矛盾；D项"取代学校课程"未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "middle school students already have heavy workloads... Adding more lessons... can make students feel tired and leave them little time to rest" 以及 "some parents choose classes for their children based on their own wishes, not the child's real interests."

**【解析】** 反对方认为学生课业负担已重，额外课程会使其疲惫、缺乏休息；部分家长按自己意愿选课。A项准确概括。B项"学生从不享受爱好"过于绝对；C项"总是免费"与"often cost money"矛盾；D项"学校不允许"未提及。故选A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "some parents choose classes for their children based on their own wishes, not the child's real interests. This may lead to boredom or even dislike of learning."

**【解析】** 原文指出家长按自己意愿选课可能导致孩子厌倦甚至厌学。B项正确。A、C、D项均未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The key is to respect children's choices and not overload them. Parents should talk with their children before signing up for any class."

**【解析】** 作者建议家长在报名前与孩子沟通，尊重孩子的选择，避免使孩子负担过重。A项正确。B项"选最贵的"、C项"绝不参加"、D项"只按家长意愿"均与作者观点相反。故选A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断 / 作者态度

**【定位】** 第④段 "In my view, there is no simple yes-or-no answer. Interest classes can be helpful if students truly enjoy them and have enough time for rest." 及第⑤段 "when chosen wisely" 与 "students need time to relax and enjoy their childhood too."

**【解析】** 作者持平衡观点：兴趣班在选得明智、不过量的情况下有益，但学生也需要休息和享受童年。D项正确。A项"强烈反对"、B项"只为富裕家庭"、C项"越多越好"均与作者立场不符。故选D。
