# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Community Flea Market & Charity Sale — Join Us!**

② Dear neighbors, the Green Garden Community will hold a flea market and charity sale on Saturday, June 7, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. The event will take place in the central square (广场) of our community. All families are welcome to join. You can sell your old toys, books, clothes, or other unused items. The money from the charity sale will go to the Sunshine Children's Home (儿童福利院) to help children in need.

③ **How to take part:** Register at the Community Office before June 1. Each family can get a free table. Please bring your own tablecloth (桌布) and price tags (价格标签). Sellers should arrive at 8:30 AM to set up. Remember to keep your area clean and take away your rubbish after the event.

④ **What you can bring:** Toys in good condition, second-hand books, clothes that are still wearable, small furniture, and hand-made crafts. We do not accept broken or dirty items. All items should be safe for children. If you are not sure, please ask our staff at the office.

⑤ For more information, call the Community Office at 020-88881234 or visit our website www.greengarden.org. Let's make our community a warmer place together!

**注释：** flea market 跳蚤市场；charity 慈善；condition 状况

---

### 选择题

1. When and where will the flea market and charity sale be held?
   A. On June 1 at the Community Office.
   B. On Saturday, June 7, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM in the central square.
   C. Every Saturday in the community.
   D. On June 7 in the Sunshine Children's Home.

2. Where will the money from the charity sale go?
   A. To the Community Office.
   B. To the sellers' families.
   C. To the Sunshine Children's Home to help children in need.
   D. To buy new tables for the community.

3. What should sellers do before the event?
   A. Register at the Community Office before June 1.
   B. Pay for the table.
   C. Call the Children's Home first.
   D. Bring only new items.

4. What can we infer from the passage about the items?
   A. Sellers can bring any items, including broken ones.
   B. Items must be in good condition and safe for children.
   C. Only new clothes are allowed.
   D. Hand-made crafts are not accepted.

5. How can people get more information about the event?
   A. By visiting the Sunshine Children's Home.
   B. By going to the central square on June 7.
   C. By calling 020-88881234 or visiting www.greengarden.org.
   D. By asking their neighbors only.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "hold a flea market and charity sale on Saturday, June 7, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM" 及 "in the central square of our community".

**【解析】** 原文明确写出时间为 6 月 7 日周六 9:00–16:00，地点为社区中心广场。A 项 6 月 1 日为报名截止日，地点错误；B 项正确；C 项"每周六"与一次性活动不符；D 项地点为儿童福利院，与中心广场不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The money from the charity sale will go to the Sunshine Children's Home to help children in need."

**【解析】** 义卖所得将捐给阳光儿童福利院，用于帮助需要帮助的儿童。A、B、D 项文中均未提及；C 项正确。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Register at the Community Office before June 1."

**【解析】** 参加者须在 6 月 1 日前到社区办公室报名。A 项正确。B 项"付费租桌"与"free table"矛盾；C 项联系儿童福利院文中未提；D 项"只带新品"与"second-hand""unused items"矛盾。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "We do not accept broken or dirty items. All items should be safe for children."

**【解析】** 不接受破损或脏污物品，所有物品须对儿童安全，可推知物品需状况良好且安全。A 项"可带破损物"与"do not accept broken"矛盾；B 项正确；C 项"仅限新衣"与"second-hand clothes"矛盾；D 项"不接受手工艺品"与"hand-made crafts"矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "call the Community Office at 020-88881234 or visit our website www.greengarden.org."

**【解析】** 获取更多信息的方式为致电 020-88881234 或访问 www.greengarden.org。A 项去儿童福利院、B 项 6 月 7 日去广场、D 项仅问邻居均非原文提供方式；C 项正确。故选 C。
