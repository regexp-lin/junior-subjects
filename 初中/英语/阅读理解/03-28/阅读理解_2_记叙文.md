# The Race I'll Never Forget

① Last October, our school held its annual Sports Day. I had never been good at sports, so I usually just watched from the side. But this year, my PE teacher, Mr. Wang, asked me to run in the 400-meter race. "Give it a try, Li Wei," he said. "You might surprise yourself." I wanted to say no, but something in his voice made me agree.

② For two weeks before Sports Day, I practiced running every afternoon. At first, I could hardly finish one lap around the track without stopping. My legs hurt and I was always out of breath. But Mr. Wang ran beside me every day and taught me how to control my speed. "Don't start too fast," he said. "Save your energy for the last 100 meters." Slowly, my time began to improve.

③ The big day finally came. When I stood at the starting line, my heart was beating so fast that I could hear it in my ears. There were seven other runners, and most of them looked much stronger than me. The whistle blew, and we all rushed forward. I remembered Mr. Wang's advice and kept a steady pace while others sprinted ahead.

④ By the halfway point, I was in sixth place. I felt tired, but I kept going. As we entered the last 100 meters, I still had energy left. I pushed my legs harder and harder. One runner... two runners... I passed them one by one. The crowd was cheering loudly. I crossed the finish line in third place! I couldn't believe it. I had won a bronze medal.

⑤ That evening, I held the medal in my hand and thought about what I had learned. It wasn't really about winning or losing. It was about having the courage to try something difficult and not giving up. Mr. Wang's words came back to me: "You might surprise yourself." He was right. Sometimes we are stronger than we think.

## 阅读理解题

1. Why did Li Wei usually just watch Sports Day from the side?
   A. Because he was not interested in sports.
   B. Because he had never been good at sports.
   C. Because his parents didn't allow him to take part.
   D. Because he had a leg problem.

2. What advice did Mr. Wang give Li Wei about running?
   A. Run as fast as possible from the start.
   B. Stop and rest during the race.
   C. Save energy for the last 100 meters.
   D. Follow closely behind the first runner.

3. How did Li Wei feel at the starting line?
   A. Calm and relaxed.
   B. Bored and sleepy.
   C. Angry and upset.
   D. Nervous and excited.

4. What can we learn from Paragraph ④?
   A. Li Wei gave up during the race.
   B. Li Wei was in the lead from the beginning.
   C. Li Wei passed several runners near the end.
   D. Li Wei finished the race in last place.

5. What is the best title for this passage?
   A. How to Win a Gold Medal
   B. A Strict PE Teacher
   C. Stronger Than I Thought
   D. The History of Sports Day

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "I had never been good at sports, so I usually just watched from the side."

**【解析】** 原文明确说李伟从来不擅长运动，所以总是在旁边看。B 项与原文一致。A 项"对运动不感兴趣"原文未提及，不擅长不等于不感兴趣；C 项"父母不允许参加"属于无中生有；D 项"腿有问题"原文未提及。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第5句 "Don't start too fast," he said. "Save your energy for the last 100 meters."

**【解析】** 王老师明确告诉李伟不要起跑太快，把体力留给最后100米。C 项正确。A 项"从一开始就尽可能快跑"与王老师的建议完全相反；B 项"比赛中停下来休息"原文未提及；D 项"紧跟第一名跑"属于无中生有。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "When I stood at the starting line, my heart was beating so fast that I could hear it in my ears."

**【解析】** 原文描写李伟站在起跑线上时心跳加速、能听到自己的心跳声，这是紧张的表现。结合他第一次参赛的背景，可推断他既紧张又兴奋。A 项"平静放松"与心跳加速矛盾；B 项"无聊困倦"与比赛场景不符；C 项"生气沮丧"原文无此情感线索。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段第4-5句 "I pushed my legs harder and harder. One runner... two runners... I passed them one by one."

**【解析】** 第④段描写了李伟在最后100米发力，从第六名一个一个超越对手，最终获得第三名。C 项"李伟在接近终点时超过了几名选手"准确概括了段落内容。A 项"李伟放弃了比赛"与事实相反；B 项"李伟从一开始就领先"与原文矛盾，他开始时在第六名；D 项"李伟最后一名完赛"与第三名的结果不符。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后两句 "'You might surprise yourself.' He was right. Sometimes we are stronger than we think."

**【解析】** 全文讲述李伟从不擅长运动到勇敢尝试、坚持不懈最终获得铜牌的故事，核心主题是"我们比自己想象的更强大"。C 项"Stronger Than I Thought"最贴切地概括了文章主旨。A 项"如何赢得金牌"不准确，李伟赢的是铜牌，且文章重点不在技巧；B 项"严格的体育老师"只涉及文章部分内容，不是主旨；D 项"运动会的历史"与文章内容无关。故选 C。
