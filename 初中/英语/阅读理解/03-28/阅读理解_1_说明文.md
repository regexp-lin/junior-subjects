# The Busy Life of Bees

① Have you ever watched a bee flying from flower to flower in a garden? You might think it is just looking for food. In fact, bees do much more than that. They are some of the most important workers in nature. Without bees, our world would look very different.

② Bees help plants grow through a process called pollination. When a bee lands on a flower, tiny pieces of pollen stick to its body. As it moves to the next flower, some of the pollen falls off. This allows the plant to produce seeds and grow fruit. Scientists say that about one third of the food we eat depends on bee pollination. Many of our favorite fruits, like apples, strawberries, and watermelons, would not exist without the help of bees.

③ A honeybee colony is like a small city. There are three types of bees in a colony: the queen, the workers, and the drones. The queen's only job is to lay eggs — she can lay up to 2,000 eggs a day! Worker bees are all female. They collect food, build the honeycomb, and protect the colony. Drones are male bees whose main job is to help the queen produce young bees.

④ Sadly, bee populations around the world have been dropping in recent years. There are several reasons for this. The use of chemicals on farms can poison bees. Climate change affects the flowers that bees depend on. Also, as cities grow larger, bees lose their natural homes. When bees disappear, the plants that need them for pollination are also in danger.

⑤ The good news is that everyone can do something to help bees. Planting flowers in your garden or on your balcony gives bees more places to find food. Avoiding the use of harmful chemicals is also important. Some schools have even started keeping beehives to teach students about these amazing insects. By taking small steps, we can make a big difference for bees — and for ourselves.

**注释：** pollination 授粉；pollen 花粉；colony 群体；honeycomb 蜂巢；drone 雄蜂；chemical 化学物质；beehive 蜂箱

## 阅读理解题

1. What happens when a bee lands on a flower?
   A. It eats the flower's leaves.
   B. Pollen sticks to its body.
   C. It builds a small nest there.
   D. It lays eggs inside the flower.

2. How much of the food we eat depends on bee pollination?
   A. About half.
   B. About one fourth.
   C. About one third.
   D. About two thirds.

3. Which of the following is TRUE about a honeybee colony?
   A. The queen collects food for the colony.
   B. Worker bees are male bees.
   C. Drones help the queen produce young bees.
   D. There are four types of bees in a colony.

4. What does the underlined word "dropping" in Paragraph ④ probably mean?
   A. Growing quickly.
   B. Becoming smaller.
   C. Staying the same.
   D. Getting stronger.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell a story about a beekeeper.
   B. To explain why bees are important and how to help them.
   C. To compare different kinds of insects.
   D. To describe how honey is made.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "When a bee lands on a flower, tiny pieces of pollen stick to its body."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到蜜蜂落在花上时，微小的花粉会粘在它身上。A 项"吃花的叶子"原文未提及；C 项"在那里筑巢"属于无中生有；D 项"在花里产卵"与原文不符，产卵是蜂后的工作。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第4句 "Scientists say that about one third of the food we eat depends on bee pollination."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出科学家说我们吃的食物大约有三分之一依赖蜜蜂授粉。A 项"大约一半"、B 项"大约四分之一"、D 项"大约三分之二"均与原文数据不符。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "Drones are male bees whose main job is to help the queen produce young bees."

**【解析】** 原文说雄蜂的主要工作是帮助蜂后繁殖后代，C 项正确。A 项错误，采集食物是工蜂的工作，不是蜂后；B 项错误，原文说工蜂都是雌性（all female）；D 项错误，原文说有三种蜜蜂（three types），不是四种。故选 C。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "Sadly, bee populations around the world have been dropping in recent years."

**【解析】** 根据上下文，该段用"Sadly"开头表达遗憾，后文提到化学物质毒害蜜蜂、气候变化影响花朵、城市扩张使蜜蜂失去家园，说明蜜蜂数量在减少。因此"dropping"意为"变少、下降"，与 B 项"becoming smaller"意思一致。A 项"快速增长"与语境矛盾；C 项"保持不变"与 sadly 的情感色彩不符；D 项"变得更强"与原文信息相反。故选 B。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第②段（蜜蜂对授粉的重要性）和第⑤段（如何帮助蜜蜂）。

**【解析】** 全文围绕两个核心展开：蜜蜂为什么重要（授粉、食物链）以及我们如何帮助保护蜜蜂。B 项准确概括了文章主旨。A 项"讲述养蜂人的故事"不符合文章体裁和内容；C 项"比较不同种类的昆虫"文中只讨论了蜜蜂，未做昆虫对比；D 项"描述蜂蜜如何制造"原文未涉及蜂蜜制作过程。故选 B。
