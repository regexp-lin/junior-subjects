> **10-11 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Time as a Flag Raiser**

① It was a Monday morning in September. The headteacher called me to her office. "We have chosen you to raise the flag next week," she said. "You have done well in your studies and you are a good example to others." I could not believe it. Raising the flag at the weekly ceremony was an honor. I felt proud but also very nervous.

② For the next few days, I practised with the school guard, Uncle Zhang. He taught me how to hold the rope, how to pull it at the right speed, and how to make sure the flag went up smoothly. "The most important thing is timing," he said. "The flag must reach the top when the national anthem ends." I practised again and again. Sometimes I was too fast; sometimes too slow. Uncle Zhang was patient and kept encouraging me.

③ On the big day, I woke up early. I wore my best school uniform and checked that everything was clean. When I stood in front of the flagpole, my heart was beating so fast. All the students and teachers were watching. I took a deep breath and remembered Uncle Zhang's words.

④ The music started. I pulled the rope slowly and steadily. The flag rose inch by inch. I kept my eyes on it and focused on the rhythm. When the last note of the anthem played, the flag reached the top. Perfect. A feeling of pride rushed through me. I had done it.

⑤ That morning, I learned that with practice and courage, we can do things we never thought we could. Being a flag raiser was more than a task — it was a responsibility and an honor. I will never forget that day.

**注释：** flagpole 旗杆；anthem 国歌

---

**Questions:**

1. Why was the writer chosen to raise the flag?
   A. Because he/she was the tallest.
   B. Because he/she had done well in studies and was a good example.
   C. Because he/she asked the headteacher.
   D. Because no one else wanted to do it.

2. What did Uncle Zhang say was the most important thing?
   A. Wearing a clean uniform.
   B. Holding the rope tightly.
   C. Timing — the flag must reach the top when the anthem ends.
   D. Standing straight.

3. How did the writer feel before raising the flag on the big day?
   A. Calm and relaxed.
   B. Nervous, with a fast-beating heart.
   C. Bored.
   D. Angry.

4. What happened when the anthem ended?
   A. The flag was still halfway up.
   B. The flag reached the top at the right moment.
   C. The writer made a mistake.
   D. The music stopped too early.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Flags are heavy.
   B. Uncle Zhang is a strict teacher.
   C. With practice and courage, we can do things we never thought we could.
   D. Monday mornings are always special.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "We have chosen you to raise the flag next week... You have done well in your studies and you are a good example to others."
**【解析】** 校长说明因学业表现好且为他人树立榜样而入选。B项准确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The most important thing is timing,' he said. 'The flag must reach the top when the national anthem ends.'"
**【解析】** 张叔叔强调最重要的是时机，国旗须在国歌结束时升到顶端。C项正确。A、B、D项非其强调重点。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "When I stood in front of the flagpole, my heart was beating so fast."
**【解析】** 作者站在旗杆前时心跳很快，表现出紧张。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "When the last note of the anthem played, the flag reached the top. Perfect."
**【解析】** 国歌最后一个音符响起时，国旗恰好升至顶端，作者认为"完美"。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I learned that with practice and courage, we can do things we never thought we could."
**【解析】** 作者由此懂得：通过练习与勇气，能做到原先以为做不到的事。C项正确概括。A、B、D项偏离主旨。故选C。
