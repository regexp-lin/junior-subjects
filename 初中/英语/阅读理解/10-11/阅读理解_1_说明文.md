# 阅读理解（说明文）

**The Social Structure of Ants**

① Ants are tiny insects, but they build some of the most complex societies in the animal world. An ant colony can have thousands or even millions of members. How do they work together so well? The answer lies in their social structure.

② In an ant colony, there are different types of ants with different jobs. The queen is the most important. She lays all the eggs. There is usually only one queen in a colony. Worker ants are the most common. They are all female. They look for food, build the nest, take care of the young, and protect the colony. Soldier ants have larger heads and strong jaws. Their job is to fight enemies and guard the nest. Male ants have only one job: to mate with the queen. After that, they die. Each ant knows its role and does its part.

③ Worker ants communicate in many ways. They use chemicals called pheromones. When one ant finds food, it leaves a trail of pheromones. Other ants follow the trail and find the food. They also tap each other with their antennae to pass information. Ants can "talk" without making a sound.

④ Ants are very hard-working. A worker ant can carry something fifty times its own weight. They work day and night. When the colony is in danger, they will fight to protect it. Some ants even give their lives to save the colony.

⑤ The ant society shows us that small creatures can achieve big things when they work together. Every ant, from the queen to the smallest worker, plays a part. No single ant could survive alone, but together they build a strong and organised community.

**注释：** colony 群体；pheromone 信息素；antennae 触角（antenna的复数）

**Questions:**

1. What is the main role of the queen ant?
   A. To look for food.
   B. To fight enemies.
   C. To lay all the eggs.
   D. To build the nest.

2. According to Paragraph ②, which statement is TRUE about worker ants?
   A. They are all male.
   B. They only look for food.
   C. They are female and do many jobs like finding food and caring for the young.
   D. There is only one worker in each colony.

3. How do ants communicate when one finds food?
   A. They shout loudly.
   B. They use a trail of pheromones for other ants to follow.
   C. They send emails.
   D. They use phones.

4. What can we infer from Paragraph ④?
   A. Ants are lazy and sleep all day.
   B. Ants are strong, hard-working, and willing to protect their colony.
   C. Ants never work at night.
   D. Ants only care about themselves.

5. What does the writer want to tell us in the last paragraph?
   A. Ants are the strongest animals.
   B. Small creatures can achieve big things when they work together and each plays a part.
   C. The queen is the only important ant.
   D. Ants can live alone without others.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The queen is the most important. She lays all the eggs."
**【解析】** 蚁后在群体中最重要，负责产卵。C项正确。A、B、D项为工蚁或兵蚁的职责。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Worker ants are the most common. They are all female. They look for food, build the nest, take care of the young, and protect the colony."
**【解析】** 工蚁均为雌性，负责觅食、筑巢、照顾幼蚁、保卫群体等多项工作。C项准确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "When one ant finds food, it leaves a trail of pheromones. Other ants follow the trail and find the food."
**【解析】** 发现食物的蚂蚁会留下信息素轨迹，其他蚂蚁循迹找到食物。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第④段 "Ants are very hard-working... can carry something fifty times its own weight... work day and night... will fight to protect it... give their lives to save the colony."
**【解析】** 段落描述蚂蚁勤劳、有力、日夜工作、为保护群体而战斗甚至牺牲，可推知B项。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 主旨大意
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The ant society shows us that small creatures can achieve big things when they work together. Every ant... plays a part."
**【解析】** 结尾点明主旨：小生物通过分工协作可以成就大事，每只蚂蚁都起作用。B项准确。A、C、D项偏离主旨。故选B。
