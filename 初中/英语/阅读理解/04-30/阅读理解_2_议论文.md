# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should students have part-time jobs (兼职工作) after school? This question is often discussed by teenagers, parents, and teachers. Some parents worry that working will take away time from studying. Although this is a reasonable worry, I believe that having a part-time job brings more positive effects to teenagers than negative ones.

② The most obvious benefit of having a part-time job is that teenagers can learn the value of money. When students receive pocket money from their parents, they might spend it quickly without thinking. However, when they earn $10 by washing cars or delivering newspapers, they will understand how hard it is to make money. This helps them learn to save money and spend it smartly.

③ Secondly, a part-time job provides valuable real-world experience. In a classroom, students learn from books, but in a workplace, they learn how to deal with real people. For example, working as a shop assistant teaches teenagers how to communicate politely with customers (顾客) and how to work well with co-workers. These are important social skills that cannot be easily taught with textbooks.

④ Some people might argue that working makes students too tired to study. But actually, dealing with a part-time job helps students improve their time-management skills (时间管理技能). A working student knows that he only has two hours for homework every night, so he will stop playing video games and concentrate (集中注意力) on his studies.

⑤ Of course, education should always come first. A part-time job shouldn't take up more than 10 to 15 hours a week. As long as it is balanced (平衡的) well with schoolwork, teenagers should be encouraged to experience the world of work.

**注释：** obvious 明显的；value 价值；deliver 递送；customer 顾客；social skill 社交技能；concentrate 集中注意力；balance 平衡

---

### 选择题

1. What is the writer's main opinion in paragraph ①?
A. Students should never have a part-time job.
B. Parents should not give pocket money to their children.
C. Having a part-time job brings more positive effects to teenagers.
D. Studying is a waste of time compared to making money.

2. How does a part-time job help teenagers understand the value of money?
A. It gives them endless free money to spend quickly.
B. It teaches them how to ask their parents for more pocket money.
C. It shows them how hard it is to make money and helps them spend it smartly.
D. It helps them to steal money from banks.

3. What valuable lesson can a teenager learn by working as a shop assistant?
A. How to communicate politely with customers and work with co-workers.
B. How to hide from angry customers inside the shop.
C. How to copy homework from other smart students.
D. How to sleep behind the desk without being noticed.

4. Form paragraph ④, how might a part-time job improve a student's time-management skills?
A. By giving them excuses to sleep during classes.
B. By forcing them to play video games faster.
C. By making them concentrate on homework because their free time is limited.
D. By letting teachers finish the homework for them.

5. What is the writer's final advice (建议) about part-time jobs for students?
A. They should work 40 hours a week to earn more money.
B. The job should be balanced well with school and take up no more than 10 to 15 hours a week.
C. They should drop out of school to become shop assistants.
D. They should work only during the math classes.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 作者观点初定/细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后半句 "...I believe that having a part-time job brings more positive effects to teenagers than negative ones."

**【解析】** 在引出一些家长担心干兼职影响学习的顾虑后，作者直接表明立场：我相信干兼职工作给青少年带来积极正面的效应多过负面效应。A是完全反对、B和D均未在其主张之列。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "...when they earn $10 by washing cars... they will understand how hard it is to make money. This helps them learn to save money and spend it smartly."

**【解析】** 干兼职如何帮助孩子明白金钱的价值？原文说明是通过自己千辛万苦洗车赚到10元钱后，才懂得赚钱多不易，从而学会存钱并理性消费（C项完整复述了这两点）。A花钱如流水和B伸手要钱都不是兼职带来的变化。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3句 "For example, working as a shop assistant teaches teenagers how to communicate politely with customers and how to work well with co-workers."

**【解析】** 作为售货员（shop assistant）这真实世界的职场体验能教给青少年什么？原文举例写道：教会他们如何礼貌地与顾客沟通，以及如何与同事和睦相处。这与A项完全一致。绝不是去躲避顾客（B）或是在桌后偷睡（D）。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "A working student knows that he only has two hours for homework every night, so he will stop playing video games and concentrate on his studies."

**【解析】** 本段反驳了兼职影响学习的说法，指出由于打工，学生知道自己回家剩下的写作业时间是有限的（只有两小时），因此反而会停止打游戏而集中注意力（concentrate）去学习。C选项就是这层意思的准确表述。A找借口睡觉、B打游戏更快，显然逻辑荒谬。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段首三句 "...education should always come first. A part-time job shouldn't take up more than 10 to 15 hours a week. As long as it is balanced (平衡的) well with schoolwork..."

**【解析】** 在最末段，作者提出了忠告与建议：教育无论何时还是第一位的。工作不能一周占据超过10到15小时。只要它能与课业平衡（balanced）好就没有问题。因此B项是不容置疑的正确答案。A全职打工、C辍学打工都不符合教育优先的基准。故选B。
