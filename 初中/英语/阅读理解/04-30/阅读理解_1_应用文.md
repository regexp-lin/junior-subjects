# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Welcome to the City Science Museum!**

② Are you curious about the world? Do you want to know how rockets fly or how robots think? The City Science Museum is the perfect place for you to explore (探索) and learn! We have prepared many amazing exhibitions (展览) for visitors of all ages.

③ **Main Exhibitions:**
- **Floor 1: The Magic of Electricity (电的魔力)**
Look at the dancing lightning and learn how electricity is made. You can even try to generate (发电) electricity yourself by riding a special bicycle!
- **Floor 2: Space Exploration (太空探索)**
Step inside a real spaceship model. Our 3D cinema will take you on a journey to Mars. *Note: Cinema tickets cost an extra $5.*
- **Floor 3: Future Robots (未来机器人)**
Meet our friendly robot guide, Robo-X. He can answer your questions, sing songs, and even play chess with you!

④ **Opening Hours:**
- Tuesday to Sunday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Monday: Closed (Except national holidays)

⑤ **Ticket Prices:**
- Adults: $15
- Students (with student ID): $8
- Children under 6: Free!
- Family Ticket (2 adults + 2 students): $40

⑥ **Visitor Rules:**
- Please do not run or shout in the museum.
- You can take photos, but please do not use flash (闪光灯) because it might damage some old machines.
- Food and drinks are only allowed in the cafeteria (自助餐厅) on Floor 1.

⑦ We hope you have a wonderful time discovering the secrets of science! For more information, call us at 800-123-4567.

**注释：** explore 探索；exhibition 展览；electricity 电；generate 产生，发电；flash 闪光灯；cafeteria 自助餐厅

---

### 选择题

1. Where should you go if you want to play chess with a robot?
A. Floor 1.
B. Floor 2.
C. Floor 3.
D. The cafeteria.

2. How much does a student need to pay for a museum ticket and a 3D cinema ticket in total?
A. $5.
B. $8.
C. $13.
D. $15.

3. When can you visit the City Science Museum?
A. On a normal Monday morning.
B. On a Wednesday afternoon at 3:00 PM.
C. On a Sunday evening at 8:00 PM.
D. Anytime during the week.

4. Which of the following is true about a family of two adults and two students?
A. They must pay $46 for their tickets.
B. They can buy a Family Ticket for $40 to save money.
C. Only the two students need to buy tickets.
D. They are not allowed to enter the 3D cinema.

5. What is the rule about taking photos in the museum?
A. Taking photos is completely forbidden (完全禁止).
B. You can take photos anywhere and use any kind of light.
C. You can take photos but must not use flash.
D. You can only take photos in the cafeteria on Floor 1.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3个展览 "Floor 3: Future Robots (未来机器人) Meet our friendly robot guide, Robo-X. He can... play chess with you!"

**【解析】** 题目问去哪层楼可以和机器人下象棋。原文明确指出三楼的未来机器人展区里，导游机器人Robo-X能陪你下棋（play chess）。所以去三楼。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解与简单计算

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Students (with student ID): $8" 结合 第③段第2个展览的提示 "*Note: Cinema tickets cost an extra $5.*"

**【解析】** 本题需要简单计算：一个学生买门票需要花费8美元，去3D电影院还要额外花5美元。因此加起来一共是 8 + 5 = 13 美元。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Tuesday to Sunday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Monday: Closed..."

**【解析】** 关于开馆时间，正常的周一闭馆（排除A）；从周二到周日上午9点开至下午5点（B项周三下午3点刚好在此期间）。C选项周日晚上8点早已闭馆；D项随时都行更是错的。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一条 "Family Ticket (2 adults + 2 students): $40"

**【解析】** 原文中明文规定了家庭套票（2名成人+2名学生）的价格是40美元。如果分开买，2成人是30美元，加2学生16美元，要46美元。显然家庭套票40美元能省钱（to save money）。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段第2条 "You can take photos, but please do not use flash (闪光灯)..."

**【解析】** 访客规则里对拍照（taking photos）的规定是：你可以拍照，但是请不要使用闪光灯以免损坏旧机器。这正是C项的表述。A完全禁止拍、B随便用任何灯、D只在餐厅拍都是扭曲了原文规定。故选C。
