# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Are pets truly good for our families? More and more people are keeping dogs, cats, or even turtles as pets. While some people worry that pets might bring dirt or diseases (疾病) into the house, I strongly believe that having a pet brings many wonderful benefits to our lives.

② First of all, pets are great companions (同伴). In today's busy world, many people, especially the elderly and single people, often feel lonely. A pet can always be there for you. When you get home after a tiring day, a dog wagging its tail or a cat purring by your side can easily make you smile. They give us pure love and never judge us.

③ Second, keeping a pet teaches children responsibility (责任感). A pet is not a toy; it is a living animal. Children have to learn how to feed it, clean up after it, and take it for a walk. Through these daily tasks, kids will understand what it means to care for another life. This will help them become more caring and responsible adults in the future.

④ Furthermore, pets encourage us to exercise more. If you have a dog, you need to take it out for a walk every day, whether it is sunny or raining. This means you will leave the sofa, get some fresh air, and do physical exercise without even thinking about it. Studies have shown that pet owners usually have stronger hearts.

⑤ Of course, keeping a pet requires money, time, and patience. But the happiness, love, and health they bring us are far more valuable than the trouble. Therefore, I highly suggest welcoming a furry friend into your family if you are ready to love it.

**注释：** disease 疾病；companion 同伴；wag 摇摆；responsibility 责任感

### 选择题

1. What do some people worry about having a pet according to paragraph ①?

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "While some people worry that pets might bring dirt or diseases (疾病) into the house..."

**【解析】** 文章开篇陈述，部分人担心宠物可能会给家里带来弄脏环境以及疾病。原文明明白白点出B项。A（吃食物多）、C（太吵）、D（咬小孩子）等原文皆不作提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第②段全文，特别是 "A pet can always be there for you... They give us pure love and never judge us."

**【解析】** 本段介绍了宠物作为同伴的好处，特别提到了它们能在我们下班后带来抚慰，消除孤独，给人纯粹无条件的爱。A（找工作）、B（做家务）、D（看家护院）皆不符合本段核心。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段整段，核心句 "Second, keeping a pet teaches children responsibility (责任感)." 以及 "Through these daily tasks, kids will understand what it means to care for another life."

**【解析】** 原文表明养宠物可以教会孩子学会肩负责任，以及怎样去照顾另一个生命。A、B、C项都是毫无根据的乱编。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段部分 "If you have a dog, you need to take it out for a walk every day... meaning you will leave the sofa, get some fresh air, and do physical exercise..." 

**【解析】** 因为狗每天都要出去走走（无论是晴天下雨），这就促成了主人也会被迫地进行体力运动或者呼吸新鲜空气。据此，B项能准确概括。A说看电视体育节目，C说给专业教练照顾狗，D说只在晴天遛狗等，完全无法从原段推演出或者与原段句意直接相悖。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第一段结尾论点和第五段结尾重申。 

**【解析】** 本文首段引出主旨“大举赞成拥有一只宠物，能带来很多美好”，接下来的段落层层递进地展开讲述。结尾作总结，指出尽管需付出精力物质，但对于做好准备的人来说是非常好的选择。A项将作者观点扭转为负面；B项断章取义仅仅取出一个单小方面来放大；D更是只提出不同动物间的盲目对比。故选C。
