> **04-12 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Dear Mr. Smith,**

② I am writing this letter to express my deepest thanks to you. Next week, I will graduate (毕业) from middle school and go to a new high school. Before I leave, I want to say how much I appreciate (感激) your help in the past three years.

③ When I first came to your English class, I was a very quiet and shy boy. My English grades were terrible. I didn't dare to speak English in front of others because I was afraid of making mistakes. Do you remember the speech contest in Grade 7? You encouraged me to join it. You spent many afternoons helping me practice my pronunciation (发音) and gave me a lot of confidence.

④ Although I didn't win the first prize, that experience changed my life. I started to believe in myself. Now, I am no longer afraid to speak English, and my grades have improved a lot. I have even made friends with some foreign students online.

⑤ You are not only a great teacher but also a good friend. Your patience and kindness mean the world to me. I will surely come back to visit you when I have time in the future.

⑥ Wish you good health and happiness every day!

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**

**注释：** graduate 毕业；appreciate 感激；pronunciation 发音；sincerely 真诚地

---

### 选择题

1. Why is Li Ming writing this letter to Mr. Smith?
A. To ask for help with his English homework.
B. To invite Mr. Smith to a birthday party.
C. To express his thanks before graduating from middle school.
D. To complain about the speech contest.

2. How was Li Ming when he first came to Mr. Smith's class?
A. He was very active and outgoing.
B. He was quiet, shy, and poor at English.
C. He spoke excellent English and had good grades.
D. He had many foreign friends.

3. What did Mr. Smith help Li Ming do in Grade 7?
A. Participate in a speech contest and practice pronunciation.
B. Win the first prize in an English exam.
C. Meet foreign students in the city center.
D. Build a new high school.

4. Form paragraph ④, what was the result of the speech contest for Li Ming?
A. He won the first prize easily.
B. He completely gave up learning English.
C. He became deeply angry with his teacher.
D. He started to believe in himself, though he didn't win first prize.

5. What does Li Ming plan to do in the future according to paragraph ⑤?
A. He plans to become an English teacher like Mr. Smith.
B. He plans to visit Mr. Smith when he has time.
C. He plans to write a book about his middle school life.
D. He plans to travel to a foreign country.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第②段第1-2句 "I am writing this letter to express my deepest thanks to you. Next week, I will graduate (毕业) from middle school..."

**【解析】** 根据原文开头李明的交代，写这封信的目的是为了在初中毕业前向史密斯老师表达最深深的感谢（to express my deepest thanks）。A（问作业）、B（邀请参加生日派对）、D（抱怨比赛）都与文意相悖。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1-2句 "When I first came to your English class, I was a very quiet and shy boy. My English grades were terrible."

**【解析】** 作者回忆刚进入这个班级时，是一个非常安静、害羞（quiet and shy）、英语成绩很差（grades were terrible）的男孩。A项的活跃外向，C项英语绝佳、成绩好，以及D项结交了外国好友等皆不是当时的状况，或者恰恰相反。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第4-5句 "Do you remember the speech contest in Grade 7? You encouraged me to join it. You spent many afternoons helping me practice my pronunciation..."

**【解析】** 七年级时老师鼓励他参加演讲比赛，并且花了许多个下午帮他练习发音（pronunciation）。B项并未发生（他没赢第一名）；C和D项在本文里纯属无关选项。故选A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第④段第1-2句 "Although I didn't win the first prize, that experience changed my life. I started to believe in myself."

**【解析】** 虽然他没有拿到第一名，但是经历改变了他的生活，他开始相信自己（started to believe in myself）。A项轻松获得第一名，与原文事实不符。B、C项是负面结果的选项，都错误。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后1句 "I will surely come back to visit you when I have time in the future."

**【解析】** 在写明自己的感激之情后，他计划如果将来有时间，一定会回来看望老师（come back to visit you）。他并没有在信中提到将成为英语老师（A项）、出书（C项）或出国（D项）。故选B。
