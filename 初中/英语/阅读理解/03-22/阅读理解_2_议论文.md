# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Today, many middle school students have a heavy burden (负担) of homework and exams. Because of this, some parents do everything for their children, including all the house chores (家务). They think students should only focus on their studies. However, is this really good for the children? Should teenagers do chores at home?

② Some parents believe that teenagers shouldn't do housework. Their reason is simple: kids are too busy. "My daughter studies hard every day. After school, she still has piano and drawing lessons. She has no time for chores," says Mrs. Wang. These parents worry that doing dishes or cleaning rooms will take away valuable reading or resting time.

③ On the other hand, many educators (教育家) hold a different opinion. They argue that doing chores is a very important part of growing up. First of all, doing chores helps teenagers learn life skills. If a child never learns how to wash clothes or cook a simple meal, how can they take care of themselves when they go to university in the future?

④ Secondly, doing chores teaches teenagers a sense of responsibility (责任感). A family is like a team, and every member should play a role. When teenagers help sweep the floor or take out the rubbish, they understand that they carry responsibilities for their family. It also helps them understand how hard their parents work every day.

⑤ In my opinion, teenagers should definitely help with house chores. Of course, parents shouldn't give them too much work, as study is still their main task. Helping out for just 15 to 20 minutes a day is enough. It not only builds character but also makes the family much closer.

---

**注释：** burden 负担；chore 家务；educator 教育家；sense of responsibility 责任感；character 品格

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some parents do all the chores for their children?
   A. Because children are not good at doing chores.
   B. Because they think children are too busy with studies and classes.
   C. Because doing chores is too dangerous for children.
   D. Because children don't want to help their parents at all.

2. In the eyes of many educators, what is the first benefit (益处) of doing chores?
   A. It helps children make more money.
   B. It teaches children how to understand their teachers better.
   C. It helps teenagers learn important life skills.
   D. It gives teenagers more time to play games.

3. What does doing chores teach teenagers according to Paragraph ④?
   A. A sense of responsibility.
   B. How to get better grades in school.
   C. How to build a bigger house.
   D. A quick way to finish homework.

4. What is the writer's opinion in the last paragraph?
   A. Teenagers should do at least two hours of chores every day.
   B. Teenagers should leave school and just do housework.
   C. Parents should not let their children do any chores.
   D. Teenagers should help with some chores, but not too much.

5. What is the main idea of the passage?
   A. Teenagers are too tired and shouldn't do any work.
   B. Piano and drawing lessons are important for students.
   C. Discussing whether teenagers should do chores at home.
   D. How to choose a good university for children.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2-3句 "Their reason is simple: kids are too busy. 'My daughter studies hard every day... She has no time for chores...'"

**【解析】** 原文提到一些父母认为青少年不应该做家务，因为孩子们太忙了，他们忙于学习或上各种辅导班（如钢琴课、绘画课）。B 项与此完全一致。A 项说孩子不擅长做；C 项说做家务太危险；D 项说是孩子完全不想帮，这三点在文中都没有作为父母帮做家务的理由出现。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3句 "First of all, doing chores helps teenagers learn life skills."

**【解析】** 教育家认为做家务的好处"首先"（First of all）正是"帮助青少年学习生活技能"（learn life skills）。C 项与原文句意一致。A、B、D 项在文中均无相关表述，属于无中生有。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "Secondly, doing chores teaches teenagers a sense of responsibility."

**【解析】** 第④段明确指出，做家务可以培养青少年的责任感（a sense of responsibility）。A 项准确对应了原文词句。B 项（获取好成绩）、C 项（建大房子）、D 项（快点做完作业的方法）与第四段讨论的内容毫不相干。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断/观点态度

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1-3句 "In my opinion, teenagers should definitely help with house chores. Of course, parents shouldn't give them too much work... Helping out for just 15 to 20 minutes a day is enough."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段表达了自己的观点：青少年绝对应该帮忙做家务（支持做家务），但也强调父母不应该给他们太多工作，每天15到20分钟就足够了。D 项"青少年应该帮助做一些家务，但不要太多"最准确传达了作者的观点。A 项（每天至少2小时）、B 项（退学专门做家务）、C 项（坚决不让做）要么扭曲了时间长度，要么走向了极端。故选 D。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文整体结构。

**【解析】** 全文采用议论文结构：第①段引出话题"中学生是否应该做家务"；第②段阐述反对方（部分家长）的观点；第③、④段阐述支持方（教育家）的理由；第⑤段作者给出总结及自己的平衡观点。因此，整篇文章是围绕"讨论青少年是否应该在家做家务"这一主题展开的。C 项最精准概括。A 项偏袒了反方且过度绝对；B、D 项是从文中个别词汇引申的无关主题。故选 C。
