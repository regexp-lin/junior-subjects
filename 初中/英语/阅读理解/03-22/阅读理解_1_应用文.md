# 阅读理解 第1篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **School Trip to the Ancient Water Town**

Dear Grade 8 students,

Next week, we are going on a special school trip to Wuzhen, a famous ancient water town. This is a great chance to learn about Chinese history and enjoy beautiful traditional houses. Here is our plan for the day:

② **Trip Itinerary (行程安排):**

**Date:** Friday, April 10
**Meeting Point:** The school gate  

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 7:30 am | Meet at the school gate and get on the bus. |
| 8:00 am | Leave for Wuzhen. (The bus ride takes about 2 hours.) |
| 10:00 am | Arrive at Wuzhen. Take a walk along the old streets and take photos. |
| 11:30 am | Enjoy local food for lunch at a traditional restaurant. |
| 1:00 pm | Visit the Woodcarving Museum (木雕博物馆) to see beautiful art. |
| 2:30 pm | Take a boat ride along the river. (Free time to buy souvenirs.) |
| 4:00 pm | Meet at the entrance of the town. |
| 4:30 pm | Take the bus back to school. |

③ **Notice:**
1. Please wear comfortable shoes because we will do a lot of walking.
2. You can bring your phones or cameras to take photos, but please keep them safe.
3. Bring an umbrella — the weather report says it might rain in the afternoon.
4. If you have a student ID card, please bring it to get a discount (折扣) on boat tickets.

④ If you have any questions, please contact Mr. Smith at the Teachers' Office before next Wednesday.

---

**注释：** ancient 古老的；itinerary 行程安排；woodcarving 木雕；souvenir 纪念品；entrance 入口；discount 折扣

---

### 题目

1. When will the students leave for Wuzhen?
   A. At 7:30 am.
   B. At 8:00 am.
   C. At 10:00 am.
   D. At 4:30 pm.

2. What will the students do first after they arrive in Wuzhen?
   A. Have lunch at a traditional restaurant.
   B. Take a boat ride along the river.
   C. Walk along the old streets and take photos.
   D. Visit the Woodcarving Museum.

3. Why should the students bring an umbrella?
   A. Because it may rain in the afternoon.
   B. Because the sun will be very strong all day.
   C. Because they need it to get a discount on tickets.
   D. Because they will use it as a photo prop (道具).

4. Which of the following is TRUE about the trip?
   A. The bus ride from the school to Wuzhen takes 3 hours.
   B. Students are NOT allowed to bring their phones.
   C. Students have some free time to buy souvenirs in the afternoon.
   D. Students must meet Mr. Smith at the entrance at 2:30 pm.

5. Where can you probably read this passage?
   A. In a geography book.
   B. On a school notice board.
   C. In a fashion magazine.
   D. On a restaurant menu.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段表格第二行 "8:00 am | Leave for Wuzhen."

**【解析】** 表格中清楚地写着，早上 8:00 "Leave for Wuzhen"（出发去乌镇）。B 项符合。A 项 7:30 是集合和上车的时间；C 项 10:00 是到达的时间；D 项 4:30 是返回学校的时间。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段表格中 10:00 am 的安排："Arrive at Wuzhen. Take a walk along the old streets and take photos."

**【解析】** 题目问学生们到达乌镇后"首先"（first）做什么环节。10点到达后的活动是在老街散步并拍照。C 项与原文一致。A 项吃午餐是 11:30 的活动；D 项参观博物馆是下午 1:00 的活动；B 项乘船是下午 2:30 的活动。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 Notice 第3点："Bring an umbrella — the weather report says it might rain in the afternoon."

**【解析】** 注意事项里说明带伞的原因是天气预报说下午可能会下雨（it might rain in the afternoon）。A 项与原文原因完全一致。B 项是为了防烈日（原文未提）；C 项说靠伞打折（打折需要学生证）；D 项说当拍照道具，均为无中生有。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段表格中 2:30 pm 的安排："Take a boat ride along the river. (Free time to buy souvenirs.)"

**【解析】** 根据表格下午 2:30 的行程括注，学生有"买纪念品的自由时间"。C 项"学生下午有一些买纪念品的自由时间"与原文完全一致，是正确的描述。A 项说乘车需要3小时（原文说 The bus ride takes about 2 hours）；B 项说不许带手机（原文第③段说 You can bring your phones...）；D 项说 2:30 在入口处集合（原文集合时间是 4:00 pm）。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图/文章出处

**【定位】** 全文整体格式与内容。最开头的 "Dear Grade 8 students" 和末尾的 "contact Mr. Smith at the Teachers' Office"。

**【解析】** 本文是一份写给八年级学生的学校春游旅行通知，包含了集合时间、行程表和相关注意事项。因此，这篇文章最可能出现在学校的公告栏上（school notice board）。B 项最符合。A 项（地理书）、C 项（时尚杂志）和 D 项（餐厅菜单）显然都不是发布校园活动通知的地方。故选 B。
