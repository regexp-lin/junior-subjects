# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Chocolate is one of the most popular foods in the world. People in many countries love it. But do you know where chocolate comes from? The story of chocolate begins about 3,000 years ago in Central America. The ancient Mayan people were the first to use the cacao (可可) plant. They made a bitter drink from cacao beans and believed it was a gift from the gods.

② In the 16th century, Spanish explorers took cacao beans back to Europe. At first, chocolate was only for rich people because it was expensive. Later, in the 1800s, people found new ways to make chocolate. A Dutch scientist learned how to turn cacao into a fine powder. A Swiss man mixed chocolate with milk and created milk chocolate. These inventions made chocolate cheaper and more popular around the world.

③ Today, chocolate is made in factories. The process has several steps. First, workers collect ripe cacao pods from trees. The pods are opened to get the beans inside. The beans are then dried in the sun for about a week. Next, they are roasted (烘烤) to bring out the chocolate flavor. After roasting, the beans are crushed into a thick paste. Sugar and milk are added, and the mixture is heated and stirred until it becomes smooth. Finally, the liquid chocolate is poured into molds and cooled to become solid bars.

④ Chocolate has become part of culture in both the East and the West. In Western countries, people often give chocolate as a gift on Valentine's Day. In China, more and more people enjoy chocolate, especially among young people. Chocolate shops and brands from around the world can now be found in Chinese cities. It is a treat that brings people together across cultures.

⑤ Whether you prefer dark chocolate, milk chocolate, or white chocolate, each bite carries a long history. Next time you enjoy a piece of chocolate, remember the journey it has made — from ancient Central America to your hand.

**注释：** cacao 可可；roast 烘烤；mold 模具

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, who were the first people to use the cacao plant?
   A. The ancient Mayan people.
   B. Spanish explorers.
   C. Dutch scientists.
   D. Swiss chocolate makers.

2. What happened to chocolate in the 1800s, according to paragraph ②?
   A. It was only used as medicine.
   B. It became a drink only for kings.
   C. People stopped eating it in Europe.
   D. New ways of making it made it cheaper and more popular.

3. What is the correct order of making chocolate, according to paragraph ③?
   A. Dry → roast → crush → add sugar and milk → pour into molds.
   B. Roast → dry → crush → add sugar and milk.
   C. Crush → roast → dry → cool.
   D. Add milk → roast → dry → pour into molds.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about chocolate in China?
   A. Only old people eat chocolate in China.
   B. Chocolate is becoming more common and popular in China.
   C. Chocolate was invented in China.
   D. Chinese people never liked chocolate.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To teach readers how to make chocolate at home.
   B. To sell chocolate products to readers.
   C. To introduce the history of chocolate and how it is made.
   D. To compare different types of chocolate.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The ancient Mayan people were the first to use the cacao plant."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出古代玛雅人最先使用可可植物，A 项正确。B 项"西班牙探险家"是将可可带回欧洲的人；C 项"荷兰科学家"和 D 项"瑞士巧克力制作者"是后来的发明者，均非最先使用者。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "in the 1800s, people found new ways to make chocolate... These inventions made chocolate cheaper and more popular around the world."

**【解析】** 原文说明 19 世纪人们发明了新的制作方法，使巧克力更便宜、在全球更受欢迎，D 项正确。A 项"仅作药用"、B 项"仅国王饮用"和 C 项"欧洲人不再食用"均与文章不符。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "First, workers collect... The pods are opened... The beans are then dried in the sun for about a week. Next, they are roasted... After roasting, the beans are crushed into a thick paste. Sugar and milk are added... Finally, the liquid chocolate is poured into molds and cooled."

**【解析】** 制作顺序为：采集→取豆→晒干→烘烤→碾碎→加糖和牛奶→倒入模具→冷却，A 项正确。B 项顺序错误（先烘烤后晒干）；C 项缺少添加糖奶和模具步骤；D 项顺序混乱。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "In China, more and more people enjoy chocolate, especially among young people. Chocolate shops and brands from around the world can now be found in Chinese cities."

**【解析】** 文中提到中国越来越多的人喜欢巧克力，尤其是年轻人，世界各地巧克力店和品牌已进入中国城市，可推断巧克力在中国日益普及和受欢迎，B 项正确。A 项"只有老人吃"与"especially among young people"矛盾；C 项"巧克力在中国发明"与第①段玛雅人最先使用矛盾；D 项"中国人从不喜欢"与"more and more people enjoy"矛盾。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段可可起源，第②段传入欧洲及 19 世纪发展，第③段现代制作流程，第④段东西方文化，第⑤段总结。

**【解析】** 全文围绕巧克力的历史起源、传播演变和现代制作工艺展开，C 项准确概括主旨。A 项"教读者在家制作"、B 项"向读者销售产品"和 D 项"比较不同类型"均非文章主要目的。故选 C。
