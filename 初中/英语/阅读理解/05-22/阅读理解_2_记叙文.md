# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Lin Na was a shy girl. She always sat at the back of the classroom and rarely raised her hand to answer questions. When the teacher called her name, her face would turn red and her voice would become very soft. She had few friends because she was too afraid to start a conversation. "I wish I could be more like those confident students," she often thought.

② One day, the school announced that there would be a drama show at the end of the term. Each class needed to prepare a short play. Lin Na's English teacher, Ms. Chen, encouraged every student to take part. "Even a small role can help you grow," she said. Lin Na wanted to join but felt too nervous. She did not think she could stand on stage in front of so many people.

③ Wang Ming, a friendly boy in her class, came to her. "Our play needs someone to play the queen. You have a calm voice — why not try?" Lin Na was surprised. No one had ever asked her to do something important before. After thinking for a few days, she agreed. She practiced her lines at home every evening. At first, her hands were shaking. But as she repeated the words again and again, she began to feel a little better.

④ The day of the performance came. Lin Na stood behind the curtain, her heart beating fast. When the music started, she took a deep breath and walked onto the stage. To her surprise, she was not as afraid as she had thought. She spoke her lines clearly and even added a small smile. The audience cheered when the play ended. Lin Na felt something she had never felt before — pride and confidence.

⑤ After the drama show, Lin Na began to change. She raised her hand more often in class. She made new friends and even joined the school's speaking club. When asked what helped her the most, she said, "That small role on stage taught me that I could do more than I believed. Sometimes you just need to take the first step."

**注释：** drama 戏剧；curtain 帷幕；audience 观众

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, why did Lin Na have few friends?
   A. She was too afraid to start a conversation.
   B. She was too busy with homework.
   C. She did not like other students.
   D. Her grades were not good.

2. What did Wang Ming ask Lin Na to do, according to paragraph ③?
   A. To help organize the drama show.
   B. To write the script for the play.
   C. To play the role of the queen in the play.
   D. To design the stage and costumes.

3. How did Lin Na feel when she first practiced her lines at home?
   A. She felt very confident and relaxed.
   B. She thought it was too easy.
   C. She wanted to give up immediately.
   D. Her hands were shaking at first.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Lin Na's performance?
   A. She made many mistakes during the show.
   B. She did better than she expected and felt proud.
   C. The audience did not enjoy the play.
   D. She forgot her lines and had to stop.

5. What is the writer's main purpose in writing this passage?
   A. To encourage shy students to try new things and build confidence.
   B. To tell readers how to prepare for a drama show.
   C. To introduce the school's drama club activities.
   D. To compare Lin Na with other confident students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "She had few friends because she was too afraid to start a conversation."

**【解析】** 原文直接说明林娜朋友少是因为她太害怕开启对话，A 项正确。B 项"作业太忙"、C 项"不喜欢其他同学"和 D 项"成绩不好"均未在文中提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "'Our play needs someone to play the queen. You have a calm voice — why not try?'"

**【解析】** 王明邀请林娜在剧中扮演女王角色，C 项正确。A 项"组织戏剧表演"、B 项"写剧本"和 D 项"设计舞台和服装"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "She practiced her lines at home every evening. At first, her hands were shaking."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述林娜在家练习台词时，起初双手会发抖，D 项正确。A 项"非常自信放松"与"hands were shaking"矛盾；B 项"觉得太简单"和 C 项"立刻想放弃"原文未提及。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "To her surprise, she was not as afraid as she had thought. She spoke her lines clearly and even added a small smile. The audience cheered when the play ended. Lin Na felt something she had never felt before — pride and confidence."

**【解析】** 林娜发现自己不像预想中那样害怕，清晰说完台词并微笑，观众喝彩，她感受到从未有过的骄傲和自信，可推断她表现超出预期并为之自豪，B 项正确。A 项"犯了很多错误"、C 项"观众不喜欢"和 D 项"忘记台词"均与原文描述相反。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：害羞女孩林娜通过参加戏剧表演克服恐惧、获得自信，最后在学习和交友上都有改变；第⑤段林娜感悟"你只需要迈出第一步"。

**【解析】** 文章通过林娜从害羞到自信的成长故事，意在鼓励害羞的学生勇于尝试新事物、建立自信，A 项正确。B 项"教读者如何准备戏剧表演"过于具体；C 项"介绍学校戏剧社活动"和 D 项"比较林娜与自信学生"均非主要意图。故选 A。
