> **04-13 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① When I was ten years old, my family moved to a house near a big lake. It was beautiful, but I had one big problem: I was terrified (感到恐惧的) of water. While other kids were swimming and laughing in the lake, I could only sit on the beach and watch them.

② My father noticed my sadness. One sunny Saturday, he bought a new pair of swimming goggles (泳镜) and handed them to me. "Let's go, Tommy. Today is the day you conquer (克服) your fear," he said with a warm smile. I shook my head nervously, but my father held my hand and we walked slowly towards the water.

③ At first, I only let the water touch my feet. It was cool. My father didn't push me. He just stood beside me patiently. "Take a deep breath," he said softly. "The water is not your enemy (敌人). It can lift you up." 

④ Slowly, I walked deeper. The water reached my knees, and then my waist. My father taught me how to float (漂浮) on my back. I lay back in the water, looking up at the blue sky. I felt light and peaceful. For the first time, I wasn't afraid. Then, under his careful guidance, I learned how to move my arms and kick my legs. The water splashed (溅起), but I was actually swimming!

⑤ I was so proud of myself. The lake was no longer a scary monster (怪物) to me. I learned that fear is just a wall we build in our minds. With a little courage and someone who believes in us, we can break it down.

**注释：** terrified 感到恐惧的；conquer 克服；enemy 敌人；float 漂浮

---

### 选择题

1. What was the writer's big problem when he was ten?
A. He didn't have any friends.
B. He was terrified of water.
C. He didn't like his new house.
D. He lost his swimming goggles.

2. How did the writer's father help him at first?
A. He pushed the writer into the deep lake suddenly.
B. He asked the other kids to teach the writer.
C. He bought the writer new goggles and held his hand to walk towards the water.
D. He told the writer to stay on the beach.

3. How did the water make the writer feel when he floated on his back?
A. Light and peaceful.
B. Cold and sick.
C. Angry and nervous.
D. Heavy and tired.

4. What does the underlined word "it" in the last paragraph refer to?
A. The big lake.
B. The scary monster.
C. The blue sky.
D. The wall built by fear.

5. What is the main message of this story?
A. Moving to a new house is very difficult for a ten-year-old child.
B. Swimming is the most dangerous sport in the world.
C. With courage and support, we can overcome our fears.
D. We should never swim in a lake alone.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "...but I had one big problem: I was terrified (感到恐惧的) of water."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到作者当时最大的问题是极度害怕水。A、C、D项均不是文中所述的问题。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第二句及段末 "One sunny Saturday, he bought a new pair of swimming goggles... my father held my hand and we walked slowly towards the water."

**【解析】** 父亲给作者买了新泳镜，并握着他的手慢慢走向水里。A项“突然推入深水”与第③段父亲的耐心表现（didn't push me）完全相反；B、D也是不符合原文的描述。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3-4句 "I lay back in the water, looking up at the blue sky. I felt light and peaceful."

**【解析】** 作者描述自己在仰面漂浮时，感到轻盈和宁静（light and peaceful），而且这是他第一次不感到害怕。其他选项的情绪均为负面干扰。故选A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义指代

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后两句 "I learned that fear is just a wall we build in our minds. With a little courage and someone who believes in us, we can break it down."

**【解析】** 前一句话里说恐惧只是我们在脑海中筑起的一堵墙（a wall we build in our minds），紧接着说有了勇气和支持我们就能把它打破（break it down）。此处的"it"必然指代前文提到的那堵由恐惧筑成的墙（The wall built by fear）。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全篇故事以及第⑤段作者的感悟。

**【解析】** 这是一篇记叙文。讲述了作者在父亲耐心帮助下克服恐水症学会游泳的故事。文章末尾点明了主旨：恐惧不过是我们心中的一堵墙，有了勇气和信任我们的人的支持，我们就能战胜恐惧（overcome our fears）。A、B、D项没能把握文章的真正核心启示。故选C。
