# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Do you sometimes forget where you put your keys? Or do you find it hard to remember new English words? Having a good memory is very important for our daily lives and studies. Luckily, our memory is like a muscle (肌肉). We can make it stronger with some practice.

② First, pay attention. Your brain cannot remember something if it doesn't notice it in the first place. When a teacher is explaining a math problem, you should look at the teacher and listen carefully. If you are playing with your pen or thinking about lunch, the information will never enter your brain.

③ Second, try to connect (联系) new information with things you already know. For example, if you meet a new friend named "Rose", you can picture a beautiful red rose flower in your mind. This makes the new name much easier to remember. Grouping things together is also helpful. Instead of remembering a long list of shopping items one by one, you can group them into "fruits", "vegetables", and "drinks".

④ Third, get enough sleep. Sleep is the time when your brain organizes and stores (储存) the information you learned during the day. If you stay up late to study for a test, you might actually forget more! So, a good night's sleep is the best secret to a strong memory.

⑤ Finally, you need to review (复习) the information. Most people forget things quickly if they don't look at them again. By reviewing your notes regularly, you can keep the knowledge in your mind for a very long time.

**注释：** muscle 肌肉；picture 想象，在脑海中描绘；store 储存

### 选择题

1. What does the writer compare (把……比作) our memory to in paragraph ①?
A. A key.
B. A muscle.
C. A math problem.
D. A beautiful flower.

2. According to paragraph ②, why might you forget what the teacher explained?
A. Because the math problem is too easy.
B. Because you are too tired to speak.
C. Because you didn't pay attention and notice the information.
D. Because the teacher spoke too fast.

3. How can you remember a new friend named "Rose" easily?
A. By asking her name again and again.
B. By writing her name on a piece of paper.
C. By giving her a real red rose flower.
D. By connecting her name with the image of a rose flower in your mind.

4. What happens in our brain when we get enough sleep?
A. The brain forgets all the bad things.
B. The brain stops working completely to have a rest.
C. The brain organizes and stores the learned information.
D. The brain creates some strange new words.

5. What would be the best title for this passage?
A. How to Make New Friends
B. The Best Time to Sleep
C. How to Improve Your Memory
D. Why Math is Difficult

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第4句 "Luckily, our memory is like a muscle (肌肉)."

**【解析】** 原文中明确表示，我们的记忆就像肌肉（muscle），可以通过练习变强。A项（钥匙）、C项（数学题）、D项（漂亮的花）均属干扰选项。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1-2句 "First, pay attention. Your brain cannot remember something if it doesn't notice it in the first place." 以及后文列举的玩笔、想午饭的例子。

**【解析】** 本段说明为了记住事情必须首先“集中注意力（pay attention）”。如果你不留意（didn't pay attention and notice），信息根本进不了大脑，所以自然就忘了老师讲的东西。其他选项在此段中均无体现。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "For example, if you meet a new friend named 'Rose', you can picture a beautiful red rose flower in your mind."

**【解析】** 本段论述的是将新知识与已有认知相联系。举的例子正是把新人名"Rose"在脑海中与一朵玫瑰花（rose flower in your mind）联系起来。C项“送她真实的玫瑰”并非文中所指的记忆方法。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "Sleep is the time when your brain organizes and stores (储存) the information you learned during the day."

**【解析】** 根据原文，睡眠是我们的大脑组织并储存白天所学信息的时间。A（忘掉坏事）、B（完全停止工作）、D（创造新词）与原文的科学论证相悖。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意题

**【定位】** 贯穿全文的方法列举。

**【解析】** 第一段引出话题——如何让记忆力变强，接着二、三、四、五段分别用"First"、"Second"、"Third"、"Finally"给出了四个增强记忆的方法（集中注意力、建立联系、充足睡眠、定期复习）。因此，"如何提高你的记忆力（How to Improve Your Memory）"是最合适的标题。故选C。
