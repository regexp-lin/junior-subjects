# 阅读理解（议论文）

**The Importance of Good Habits**

① Why do some students get better grades while others find study hard? Often, the answer lies in habits. Good habits help us do things well without thinking too much. They save time and make our lives easier.

② Study habits matter a lot. For example, reviewing your lessons every day is better than studying everything the night before an exam. Taking short breaks when you feel tired helps your brain work better. Writing down homework and checking it when you finish can help you remember what you need to do. These small habits, when done every day, bring big results over time.

③ Good habits are not only for study. Healthy habits like eating breakfast, drinking enough water, and getting enough sleep keep your body strong. Washing your hands before meals and exercising regularly help you stay away from illness. Habits like keeping your room tidy and putting things back after use make life more comfortable.

④ The hard part is starting. At first, a new habit may feel strange. You may forget to do it. But if you keep trying for about three weeks, it becomes easier. Start with one small habit. Do it at the same time every day. Tell a friend or your parent so they can remind you. Soon, you will do it without thinking.

⑤ Remember: you are never too young or too old to build good habits. Start today, and your future self will thank you.

**Questions:**

1. According to the passage, what helps some students get better grades?
   A. Being very clever.
   B. Good habits.
   C. Having more money.
   D. Going to a better school.

2. Which study habit does the writer suggest?
   A. Studying everything the night before an exam.
   B. Reviewing lessons every day.
   C. Never taking breaks.
   D. Not writing down homework.

3. What do healthy habits help with?
   A. Making more money.
   B. Keeping your body strong and staying away from illness.
   C. Winning games.
   D. Making more friends.

4. How long does it take for a new habit to become easier?
   A. One day.
   B. One week.
   C. About three weeks.
   D. One year.

5. What does the writer suggest doing when starting a new habit?
   A. Start with many habits at once.
   B. Start with one small habit, do it at the same time every day, and ask someone to remind you.
   C. Never tell anyone.
   D. Only try when you feel like it.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Often, the answer lies in habits. Good habits help us do things well without thinking too much."

**【解析】** 原文指出好成绩常源于习惯，好习惯使人事半功倍。A项聪明、C项有钱、D项上好学校均非文章观点。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Reviewing your lessons every day is better than studying everything the night before an exam."

**【解析】** 作者建议每天复习功课，而非考前一夜突击。A项与建议相反；C项不休息、D项不记作业均不合理。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Healthy habits like eating breakfast... keep your body strong... help you stay away from illness."

**【解析】** 健康习惯能强健身体、减少生病。A项挣钱、C项赢比赛、D项交朋友均非健康习惯主要作用。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "But if you keep trying for about three weeks, it becomes easier."

**【解析】** 原文说坚持约三周后习惯会变容易。A项一天、B项一周、D项一年均与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Start with one small habit. Do it at the same time every day. Tell a friend or your parent so they can remind you."

**【解析】** 作者建议从小习惯开始、每天固定时间、请人提醒。A项同时培养多个习惯与"one small habit"矛盾；C、D项与建议不符。故选B。
