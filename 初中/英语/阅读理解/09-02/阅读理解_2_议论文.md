# 阅读理解（议论文）

① After school, many students rush to tutoring classes (辅导班). They study math, English, physics, or other subjects with private teachers. Some students attend two or three such classes every week. But are these after-school classes really necessary? Parents and teachers have different views.

② Those who support tutoring say it can help students catch up or get ahead. If a student is weak in a subject, one-on-one teaching can give him more attention than a crowded classroom. Bright students may also benefit — they can learn faster and explore (探索) harder topics. Besides, in today's competitive world, good grades often lead to better schools and jobs. Many parents believe that tutoring is an investment (投资) in their child's future.

③ However, critics (批评者) point out several problems. First, tutoring costs a lot of money. Not every family can afford it. This may create a gap between rich and poor students. Second, too much tutoring can make students tired. They already have long school days and heavy homework. Adding more classes may leave them with no time to rest, play, or develop other interests. Third, some students may depend too much on tutoring and stop trying in regular class. They think, "I will learn it in the tutoring class anyway." This habit can hurt their ability to learn independently.

④ So what is the right approach? Experts suggest that tutoring should not replace school. It should only be used when a student really needs help — for example, when he falls behind in a subject or wants to prepare for a special exam. The amount of tutoring should be limited. Students also need time for sports, hobbies, and rest. Parents should talk with teachers first to see if tutoring is really needed, instead of following what other parents do.

⑤ In my view, tutoring is a tool, not a must. It can help some students, but it is not the answer for everyone. The most important thing is that students learn to study by themselves and enjoy learning. If tutoring takes away a student's free time and makes him hate school, it may do more harm than good.

**注释：** tutoring 辅导；explore 探索；investment 投资；critic 批评者

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people support tutoring classes?
   A. Because tutoring is free.
   B. Because tutoring can help students catch up or get ahead, and good grades lead to better opportunities.
   C. Because tutors do homework for students.
   D. Because schools do not teach well.

2. What is one problem that critics mention about tutoring?
   A. Tutoring is too easy.
   B. Too much tutoring can make students tired and leave no time for rest or other interests.
   C. Tutoring is only for rich students.
   D. Tutoring teachers are not qualified.

3. What do experts suggest about tutoring?
   A. All students should have tutoring.
   B. Tutoring should replace school.
   C. Tutoring should only be used when a student really needs help, and the amount should be limited.
   D. Students should have tutoring every day.

4. What does the writer mean by saying "tutoring is a tool, not a must"?
   A. Tutoring is required by law.
   B. Tutoring can be helpful for some but is not necessary for everyone.
   C. Tutoring is useless.
   D. Only tools can be used for tutoring.

5. When might tutoring do more harm than good according to the writer?
   A. When it is cheap.
   B. When it takes away a student's free time and makes him hate school.
   C. When the tutor is strict.
   D. When it is recommended by teachers.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "tutoring can help students catch up or get ahead... good grades often lead to better schools and jobs... tutoring is an investment in their child's future"

**【解析】** 原文指出辅导能帮助学生追赶或超前，好成绩带来更好机会，家长视其为投资。A项"免费"与"投资"矛盾；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "too much tutoring can make students tired... Adding more classes may leave them with no time to rest, play, or develop other interests"

**【解析】** 批评者认为过多辅导会令学生疲劳，挤占休息和兴趣发展时间。A项"太简单"、C项"只给富家子弟"、D项"教师不合格"均非原文主要问题。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "tutoring should not replace school. It should only be used when a student really needs help... The amount of tutoring should be limited"

**【解析】** 专家建议辅导不能取代学校，仅在真正需要时使用，且应控制量。A、B、D项与专家建议相反。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义理解/写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "tutoring is a tool, not a must. It can help some students, but it is not the answer for everyone"

**【解析】** "工具而非必须"意为辅导对部分人有帮助，但非人人必需。A项"法律规定"、C项"无用"、D项"工具用于辅导"均曲解原意。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "If tutoring takes away a student's free time and makes him hate school, it may do more harm than good"

**【解析】** 原文明确说当辅导剥夺自由时间并使学生厌恶学校时，可能弊大于利。A、C、D项原文未作为有害情形提及。故选B。
