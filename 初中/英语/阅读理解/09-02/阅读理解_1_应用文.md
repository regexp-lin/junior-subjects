> **09-02 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Campus Flea Market (跳蚤市场) — Notice**

② Dear students,

③ The School Student Union will hold a Campus Flea Market on the afternoon of September 14 (Saturday). This is a great chance to sell things you no longer need and find something useful at a low price. Let's make good use of our old things and help the environment!

④ **Time and Place:**
- **Date:** September 14, 2024 (Saturday)
- **Time:** 2:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.
- **Place:** School playground (if it rains, we will use the gym)

⑤ **What you can sell:**
- Books, magazines, and notebooks
- Toys, sports equipment (运动器材), and games
- Clothes and accessories (配饰) that are still in good condition
- Small electronic items (e.g. headphones, chargers)
- **Note:** No food, drinks, or dangerous items

⑥ **How to join:**
- Sign up at the Student Union office (Room 101) before September 10
- Each class can have up to 3 stalls (摊位). Students can form groups to share a stall.
- Bring your own tablecloth (桌布) and price tags. The school will provide tables.
- All money from sales goes to the sellers. A 5% donation (捐赠) to the school charity fund is encouraged but not required.

⑦ **Rules:**
- Be honest. Do not sell broken or damaged items without telling buyers.
- Keep the place clean. Take away your rubbish when you leave.
- Be polite and fair when dealing with others.

⑧ We hope to see you there!

School Student Union  
September 5, 2024

**注释：** equipment 器材；accessory 配饰；stall 摊位；tablecloth 桌布；donation 捐赠

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Campus Flea Market be held?
   A. On September 5.
   B. On September 10.
   C. On September 14.
   D. On September 15.

2. Where will the flea market be held if it rains?
   A. In the library.
   B. In the gym.
   C. In the classroom.
   D. It will be cancelled.

3. Which of the following can students sell at the flea market?
   A. Fresh fruit and snacks.
   B. Old books and toys in good condition.
   C. Knives and other dangerous items.
   D. Expensive new electronics.

4. What should students do if they want to join?
   A. Just come on September 14.
   B. Sign up at the Student Union office before September 10.
   C. Send an email to the head teacher.
   D. Pay a fee to the school first.

5. What does the notice say about the money from sales?
   A. All money must go to the school.
   B. Sellers keep the money; a 5% donation to charity is encouraged.
   C. The school will take 50% of the money.
   D. Students cannot keep any money.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Date: September 14, 2024 (Saturday)"

**【解析】** 原文明确写出跳蚤市场日期为9月14日。A项为通知发布日期；B项为报名截止日；D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Place: School playground (if it rains, we will use the gym)"

**【解析】** 原文说明若遇雨则改在体育馆举行。A项"图书馆"、C项"教室"和D项"取消"均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "What you can sell: Books, magazines... Toys... Clothes... in good condition" 及 "No food, drinks, or dangerous items"

**【解析】** 可出售书籍、玩具、状况良好的衣物等，禁止食物和危险品。A项"新鲜水果零食"被禁止；C项"刀等危险品"被禁止；D项"昂贵新电子品"未在推荐之列。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Sign up at the Student Union office (Room 101) before September 10"

**【解析】** 申请方式为9月10日前到学生会办公室报名。A项"直接9月14日来"与流程不符；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "All money from sales goes to the sellers. A 5% donation to the school charity fund is encouraged but not required."

**【解析】** 销售收入归卖家所有，鼓励（非强制）将5%捐给学校慈善基金。A项"全部交学校"、C项"学校抽成50%"和D项"学生不能留钱"均与原文不符。故选B。
