> **04-11 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last month, our class had a very exciting football match against Class 3. The winner of this match would become the champion (冠军) of the school sports festival. Everyone in my class was looking forward to it.

② Our team captain, Mike, practiced very hard with us for two weeks before the match. "We must work together!" he often shouted. On the afternoon of the match, the weather was sunny but a bit windy. Many students and teachers stood around the football field cheering. I felt both nervous and excited when the game began.

③ In the first half, Class 3 played really well and scored the first goal. We felt a little disappointed (失望的). However, Mike didn't give up. During the half-time break, he gathered (聚集) us and said, "Don't lose heart, guys! We are a strong team. Just trust each other and run faster!" Hearing his words, we all got our energy back.

④ In the second half, we played much better. I passed the ball to Mike, and he quickly kicked it into the net (网). We scored! The score was 1-1. The game was close to the end, and both teams were tired. Suddenly, our goalkeeper, John, caught the ball nicely from the other team and threw it far to me. I ran as fast as a cheetah (猎豹) and scored the winning goal at the last minute!

⑤ We won the championship! All my classmates rushed onto the field, hugging and jumping. It was an unforgettable match. It taught me that as long as we never give up and work as a team, we can overcome any difficulty.

**注释：** champion 冠军；disappointed 失望的；gather 聚集

---

### 选择题

1. Who was the writer's class playing against in the football match?
A. Class 1.
B. Class 2.
C. Class 3.
D. The teachers' team.

2. Why did the writer's class play the match?
A. Because they wanted to win some money.
B. Because it was their PE class.
C. Because they wanted to become the champion of the school sports festival.
D. Because the headmaster told them to do so.

3. How did the writer's team feel when Class 3 scored the first goal?
A. Very happy and excited.
B. A little disappointed.
C. Very angry and wanting to fight.
D. Too tired to run.

4. Who scored the winning goal at the last minute?
A. Mike.
B. John.
C. A player from Class 3.
D. The writer.

5. What is the most important lesson the writer learned from this match?
A. Running fast like a cheetah is the only way to win.
B. We should never play football when it is windy.
C. Never give up and teamwork can help us overcome any difficulty.
D. The goalkeeper is the most important player in a team.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段首句 "Last month, our class had a very exciting football match against Class 3."

**【解析】** 原文中明确表示，作者班级是对阵三班（against Class 3）。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "The winner of this match would become the champion of the school sports festival."

**【解析】** 原文表明这场比赛的胜者将会成为学校体育节的冠军。因此他们参加这场比赛就是为了体育节的冠军（champion）头衔。A、B、D项纯属编造。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1-2句 "In the first half, Class 3 played really well and scored the first goal. We felt a little disappointed (失望的)."

**【解析】** 根据文意，当对手三班在上半场进第一个球时，作者队伍感到有些失望（We felt a little disappointed）。生气想打架、感到开心兴奋等完全没有逻辑，或者与原文南辕北辙。故选B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断 / 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段的最后描述 "Suddenly, our goalkeeper, John, caught the ball... and threw it far to me. I ran as fast as a cheetah and scored the winning goal at the last minute!"

**【解析】** 门将John把球扔给“我（作者）”，“我”像猎豹一样跑并踢进了制胜球。因此是作者自己（The writer）射门的，而Mike进了将比分扳为1-1的一球，并非制胜球。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后1句 "It taught me that as long as we never give up and work as a team, we can overcome any difficulty."

**【解析】** 作者在总结这个令人难忘的时刻时，明确表示他学到了极其重要的一课：只要我们永不放弃（never give up）并作为团队合作（work as a team），我们就能克服任何困难。这直接印证了C选项的内容。其他的均为无关要领的细节或者误导。故选C。
