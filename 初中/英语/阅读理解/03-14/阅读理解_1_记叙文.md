# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Sun Mei loved painting more than anything else. Every day after school, she would sit by the window and draw pictures of birds, flowers, and the mountains behind her village. Her dream was to study at a famous art school in Beijing one day.

② However, Sun Mei's family was not rich. Her parents were farmers, and they worried about the cost of art supplies and art school. "Painting is just a hobby," her father said one evening. "You should focus on subjects that can help you find a stable job." Sun Mei felt sad, but she didn't give up her dream.

③ One day, her art teacher, Ms. Li, noticed Sun Mei's talent. She gave Sun Mei some extra paints and brushes and encouraged her to enter a national art competition for young students. "You have a gift, Sun Mei. Don't let it go to waste," she said. Sun Mei practiced every spare moment — during lunch breaks, after finishing homework, even on weekends.

④ Months later, the results came. Sun Mei won first prize in the competition. The prize included a scholarship that would pay for two years of art school in Beijing. When she told her parents, her father stood quietly for a moment. Then he hugged her tightly and said, "I'm sorry I didn't believe in you earlier. I'm so proud of you."

⑤ Today, Sun Mei is studying at the art school she always dreamed of. She still paints the birds and mountains from her village, but now her paintings hang in galleries for everyone to see. She often remembers Ms. Li's words: "A dream is worth fighting for, as long as you never give up."

---

**注释：** stable 稳定的；scholarship 奖学金；gallery 画廊

---

### 题目

1. What was Sun Mei's dream?
   A. To become a teacher like Ms. Li.
   B. To travel around the world.
   C. To study at an art school in Beijing.
   D. To become a famous farmer.

2. Why did Sun Mei's father want her to stop focusing on painting?
   A. Because he thought painting was just a hobby and couldn't help find a stable job.
   B. Because Sun Mei was not good at painting at all.
   C. Because art supplies were too heavy to carry.
   D. Because he wanted Sun Mei to become a doctor.

3. What did the competition prize include?
   A. A trip to Beijing for one week.
   B. A scholarship for two years of art school.
   C. A new set of expensive paint brushes.
   D. A chance to meet a famous artist.

4. How did Sun Mei's father react when he heard the news?
   A. He was angry and disappointed.
   B. He told her to choose a different career.
   C. He didn't care about the result at all.
   D. He felt proud and sorry for not believing in her earlier.

5. What can we learn from Sun Mei's story?
   A. Art schools are too expensive for most families.
   B. Parents should always decide their children's future.
   C. Hard work and determination can help achieve dreams.
   D. Competitions are the only way to succeed in life.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "Her dream was to study at a famous art school in Beijing one day."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出孙梅的梦想是有朝一日去北京一所著名的美术学校学习。C 项与原文完全一致。A 项"成为像李老师一样的老师"——虽然李老师对她影响很大，但原文未说她想成为老师；B 项"环游世界"属于无中生有；D 项"成为著名农民"与她的绘画梦想无关。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3-4句 "'Painting is just a hobby,' her father said one evening. 'You should focus on subjects that can help you find a stable job.'"

**【解析】** 原文清楚记录了父亲的话：他认为画画只是一个爱好，孙梅应该把精力放在能帮她找到稳定工作的科目上。A 项准确概括了父亲的观点。B 项"因为孙梅画得不好"与第③段"Ms. Li noticed Sun Mei's talent"矛盾；C 项"美术用品太重"属于无中生有；D 项"他想让孙梅当医生"原文未提及具体职业方向。故选 A。

---

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-3句 "Sun Mei won first prize in the competition. The prize included a scholarship that would pay for two years of art school in Beijing."

**【解析】** 原文说明比赛奖品包括一份可支付两年北京美术学校学费的奖学金。B 项与原文完全对应。A 项"去北京一周旅行"属于无中生有；C 项"一套昂贵的新画笔"——画笔是之前李老师送的，不是比赛奖品，属于张冠李戴；D 项"见一位著名艺术家的机会"原文未提及。故选 B。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段最后两句 "Then he hugged her tightly and said, 'I'm sorry I didn't believe in you earlier. I'm so proud of you.'"

**【解析】** 父亲先是静静地站了一会儿，然后紧紧拥抱了她，说"对不起我之前没有相信你，我为你感到骄傲"。从"I'm sorry"可以推断他感到愧疚，从"I'm so proud"可以看出他为女儿自豪。D 项"他感到骄傲并对之前的怀疑感到抱歉"最准确地概括了父亲的反应。A 项"他感到愤怒和失望"与原文拥抱和道歉的行为完全矛盾；B 项"他让她选择另一条职业道路"与父亲的道歉和骄傲态度不符；C 项"他根本不在意结果"与他紧紧拥抱女儿的举动相反。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 推理判断题需综合人物的语言和动作来判断其情感。本题中"hugged her tightly"（动作）+"I'm sorry... I'm so proud"（语言）共同揭示了父亲内心复杂而深沉的情感：既有愧疚，又有骄傲。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "A dream is worth fighting for, as long as you never give up." 及全文故事线索。

**【解析】** 全文讲述了孙梅从家庭条件有限、父亲不支持，到在老师鼓励下刻苦练习，最终赢得比赛、获得奖学金、实现梦想的故事。核心主题是"通过努力和坚持可以实现梦想"。C 项"努力和决心能帮助实现梦想"最准确地概括了全文主旨。A 项"美术学校对大多数家庭来说太贵了"以偏概全——经济困难只是故事的背景之一，不是主旨；B 项"父母应该总是决定孩子的未来"与文章传达的信息相反——孙梅坚持自己的选择并取得了成功；D 项"比赛是成功的唯一途径"过度推断——比赛是孙梅故事中的转折点，但文章并未暗示它是唯一途径。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 记叙文主旨往往在结尾以人物感悟或引言形式呈现。本文结尾"A dream is worth fighting for, as long as you never give up"直接点明了核心主题。
