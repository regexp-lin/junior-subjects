# 阅读理解（说明文）

① An earthquake is a sudden shaking of the ground. It happens when rocks underground break or move. The Earth's surface is made of large pieces called plates (板块). These plates are always moving slowly. When two plates meet and push against each other, energy builds up. When the energy becomes too strong, the rocks break and an earthquake begins. That is why we often hear about earthquakes near mountains or under the sea.

② Earthquakes can cause a lot of damage. Buildings may fall down, and roads may crack. In strong earthquakes, people can get hurt or lose their homes. However, we cannot stop earthquakes from happening. What we can do is learn how to protect ourselves when they come.

③ Before an earthquake, you should prepare an emergency kit (应急包). Put water, food, a flashlight, and a first-aid kit in a bag. Keep it in a place that is easy to reach. You should also know the safe spots in your home. Stay away from windows, tall furniture, and heavy things that might fall. A strong table or desk can protect you. Practice "Drop, Cover, and Hold" with your family — drop to the ground, get under something solid, and hold on.

④ During an earthquake, do not run outside. Stay indoors and get under a table or bed. Cover your head and neck with your arms. If you are outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings and trees. Do not use the elevator. Stay calm and wait until the shaking stops.

⑤ After the shaking stops, check yourself and others for injuries. Leave the building carefully. Watch out for broken glass and fallen objects. Listen to the radio or news for updates. Remember, being prepared and knowing what to do can save lives. Earthquakes are natural events, but with the right knowledge, we can stay safer.

**注释：** plate 板块；emergency kit 应急包；elevator 电梯

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "It happens when rocks underground break or move... When two plates meet and push against each other, energy builds up. When the energy becomes too strong, the rocks break and an earthquake begins."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明地震发生在地下岩石破裂或移动时，即板块挤压导致能量积聚，能量过强时岩石断裂，地震发生。A 项准确概括。B 项"天气变热"、C 项"人们盖太多房子"和 D 项"海浪太强"均未在文中提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Put water, food, a flashlight, and a first-aid kit in a bag."

**【解析】** 原文清楚列出应急包应包含水、食物、手电筒和急救包。A 项"钱、钥匙、手机"、C 项"书、衣服、玩具"和 D 项"电脑、电池"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "During an earthquake, do not run outside. Stay indoors and get under a table or bed. Cover your head and neck with your arms."

**【解析】** 原文明确要求地震时待在室内，躲到桌子或床下，用手臂护住头和颈。A 项"尽快跑出去"与"do not run outside"矛盾；B 项"乘电梯离开"与"Do not use the elevator"矛盾；D 项"站在窗边看"与防护要求相反。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Remember, being prepared and knowing what to do can save lives. Earthquakes are natural events, but with the right knowledge, we can stay safer."

**【解析】** 原文强调提前准备和掌握正确知识能拯救生命、让我们更安全，可推断做好准备、知道该怎么做有助于人们更安全。A 项"地震可以被阻止"与"We cannot stop earthquakes"矛盾；C 项"震后应留在建筑内"与"Leave the building carefully"矛盾；D 项"只有成人需要学"原文未提及。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段讲地震成因，第②段讲危害与应对必要性，第③④⑤段讲震前、震中、震后防护措施。

**【解析】** 全文围绕地震的成因与防护知识展开，D 项准确概括主旨。A 项"描述世界地震史"、B 项"比较各国地震"和 C 项"说明地震多发地"均非文章核心内容。故选 D。
