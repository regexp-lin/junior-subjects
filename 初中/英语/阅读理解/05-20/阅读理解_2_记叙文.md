# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Xiao Ming was eight years old when he first went to the swimming pool with his father. He had watched other children swim and play in the water many times, but he always stayed far from the edge. The idea of putting his face under water made him feel scared. His father noticed this and signed him up for swimming lessons at the community center.

② The first lesson was difficult. Xiao Ming stood at the shallow end, holding the wall tightly. His teacher, Miss Chen, was kind and patient. She did not push him to jump in. Instead, she asked him to sit on the steps and let the water touch his feet. "Take your time," she said. "The water is your friend, not your enemy." Slowly, Xiao Ming put his feet in. The water felt cool and soft. Nothing bad happened.

③ Week after week, Xiao Ming made small steps. He learned to blow bubbles in the water. Then he practiced putting his face in the water for just one second. Each time he tried, he felt a little less afraid. Miss Chen always smiled and said, "You are doing great!" One day, she held his hands and walked him into deeper water. Xiao Ming's heart beat fast, but he did not give up. He kicked his legs and moved his arms. For the first time, he was swimming.

④ By the end of summer, Xiao Ming could swim across the pool. He even jumped off the diving board. His father watched from the stands with a big smile. "I'm proud of you," his father said. "You faced your fear and you won." Xiao Ming grinned. He knew that the water had never been his enemy. The only enemy was the fear inside himself.

⑤ Today, Xiao Ming loves swimming. He goes to the pool every weekend. He often thinks about that first lesson and Miss Chen's words. He learned that fear is something we can overcome if we take small steps and never give up. Sometimes the thing we are afraid of is not as scary as we think.

**注释：** shallow 浅的；bubble 气泡；overcome 克服

---

### 选择题

1. Why did Xiao Ming stay far from the edge of the pool at first, according to paragraph ①?
   A. He was afraid of putting his face under water.
   B. The water was too cold for him.
   C. His father did not allow him to go near the water.
   D. The pool was too crowded.

2. What did Miss Chen ask Xiao Ming to do in the first lesson?
   A. Jump into the deep end at once.
   B. Swim across the pool.
   C. Sit on the steps and let the water touch his feet.
   D. Hold his breath for a long time.

3. What happened when Xiao Ming first swam, according to paragraph ③?
   A. He swam for five minutes without stopping.
   B. Miss Chen held his hands and walked him into deeper water; he kicked and moved his arms and swam.
   C. He fell and drank a lot of water.
   D. He gave up and left the pool.

4. What can we infer from the father's words "You faced your fear and you won"?
   A. Xiao Ming had won a swimming race.
   B. Xiao Ming had overcome his fear of water and achieved something he could not do before.
   C. The father had helped Xiao Ming swim.
   D. Xiao Ming would never be afraid again.

5. What did Xiao Ming learn from his experience?
   A. Swimming is the best sport for children.
   B. Fathers should always sign their children up for lessons.
   C. Fear can be overcome by taking small steps and never giving up.
   D. Teachers are more important than parents when learning new things.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He always stayed far from the edge. The idea of putting his face under water made him feel scared."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明小明远离池边，因为把脸埋进水里的想法让他感到害怕。B 项"水太冷"、C 项"父亲不允许"和 D 项"池子太挤"均未在文中提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Instead, she asked him to sit on the steps and let the water touch his feet."

**【解析】** 原文清楚描述陈老师让小明坐在台阶上，让水触到他的脚。A 项"立刻跳进深水区"与"She did not push him to jump in"矛盾；B 项"游过泳池"是后来的成果；D 项"长时间憋气"是后续练习内容。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "One day, she held his hands and walked him into deeper water... He kicked his legs and moved his arms. For the first time, he was swimming."

**【解析】** 原文描述陈老师拉着他的手走进深水区，小明蹬腿划臂，第一次游了起来。A 项"连续游五分钟"原文未提及；C 项"呛水"和 D 项"放弃离开"与"He did not give up"及成功游泳的情节矛盾。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "You faced your fear and you won." 结合前文小明从恐水到学会游泳的过程。

**【解析】** 父亲的话"你面对了恐惧，你赢了"结合小明克服恐水、学会游泳的经历，可推断父亲是在肯定他克服了对水的恐惧、做到了以前做不到的事。A 项"赢得了比赛"原文未提及；B 项"父亲帮他游泳"与老师教学、小明自己努力不符；C 项"再也不会害怕"过于绝对。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "He learned that fear is something we can overcome if we take small steps and never give up. Sometimes the thing we are afraid of is not as scary as we think."

**【解析】** 小明学到的是：恐惧可以通过小步前进、永不放弃来克服；有时我们害怕的东西并不像想象中那么可怕。C 项准确概括这一主旨。A 项"游泳是儿童最佳运动"、B 项"父亲应常给孩子报名"和 D 项"学新东西时老师比父母重要"均非文章要传递的核心道理。故选 C。
