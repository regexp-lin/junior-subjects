> **10-25 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Sweeping Snow with the Street cleaners**

① Last winter, a heavy snow fell overnight. When I woke up, the whole city was white. I was excited and wanted to play in the snow. But when I looked out of the window, I saw street cleaners in orange jackets working hard to clear the roads. They had been there since early morning. I felt I should do something to help.

② I put on my warm coat and gloves and went outside. There was an older man sweeping near our gate. "Can I help you?" I asked. He looked surprised but smiled. "Thank you, young man. But it's cold. You should go home." I said I wanted to help. He handed me a broom and showed me how to push the snow to the side of the road.

③ At first, my hands were freezing and the work was tiring. But after a while, I got used to it. The cleaner and I worked together. We talked a little. He told me he had to start at four in the morning so that people could walk and drive safely. "Someone has to do it," he said. "When you see a clean road, remember there are people behind it."

④ More neighbours came out to help. Some brought hot tea. Others took brooms and joined us. By noon, our street was clear. The street cleaner thanked everyone. His face was red from the cold, but he looked happy.

⑤ That day I learned that small actions can make a big difference. The street cleaners do a difficult job every day, often without much thanks. Helping them for just a few hours made me understand their work better. Now, whenever it snows, I think of that morning and the people who keep our city running.

**注释：** cleaner 清洁工；broom 扫帚

---

**Questions:**

1. What did the writer see when he woke up?
   A. A sunny day.
   B. Street cleaners working to clear the snow.
   C. Children playing in the snow.
   D. No one outside.

2. Why did the street cleaner at first tell the writer to go home?
   A. Because the writer was lazy.
   B. Because it was cold and he did not want the writer to get sick.
   C. Because there was no broom.
   D. Because the work was finished.

3. When does the street cleaner usually start work according to the passage?
   A. At noon.
   B. At four in the morning.
   C. In the evening.
   D. After school.

4. What happened by noon?
   A. It started to snow again.
   B. More people came to help, and the street was clear.
   C. The writer went home alone.
   D. The street cleaner went home.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Snow is always fun.
   B. Small actions can help, and street cleaners do important work.
   C. Sweeping is easy.
   D. Neighbours never help each other.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "I saw street cleaners in orange jackets working hard to clear the roads"
**【解析】** 作者醒来时看到穿橙色外套的清洁工在扫雪。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "He looked surprised but smiled. 'Thank you, young man. But it's cold. You should go home.'"
**【解析】** 清洁工说天冷、让作者回家，体现关心作者健康。B项正确。A、C、D项与对话含义不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "He told me he had to start at four in the morning"
**【解析】** 清洁工通常凌晨四点开始工作。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "More neighbours came out to help... By noon, our street was clear"
**【解析】** 更多邻居加入帮忙，到中午街道已清理干净。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I learned that small actions can make a big difference... understand their work better"
**【解析】** 作者体会到小举动也有大作用，并对清洁工的工作有了更深理解。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
