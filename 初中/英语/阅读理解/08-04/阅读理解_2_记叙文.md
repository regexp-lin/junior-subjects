# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## A Small Game That Changed Everything

① Last summer holiday, Li Ming had two whole months free. His parents were busy at work, so he spent most of his time at home. One day, he saw a video online about making games with code. He thought, "Why not try it myself?"

② Li Ming had never learned programming before. He found some free lessons on the Internet and started from the beginning. The first week was difficult. He made many mistakes and felt like giving up. But when he saw his name appear on the screen after writing a few lines of code, he was excited and decided to keep going.

③ Every day he spent two or three hours learning. He learned about variables (变量), loops (循环) and how to make simple games. By the end of the fourth week, he had made a small game—a snake that eats food and grows longer. It was simple, but it worked! His younger sister loved it and played it for hours.

④ When the new term started, Li Ming showed his game to his computer teacher. The teacher was surprised and asked him to share his experience with the class. Li Ming felt proud. He realized that learning something new on your own is not impossible. You just need patience and practice.

⑤ Now Li Ming is learning more about programming. He dreams of making a bigger game one day. His holiday experience taught him that the best way to learn is to start and never stop trying.

**注释：** variable 变量；loop 循环

---

### 选择题

1. What did Li Ming do during last summer holiday?
   A. He stayed at home and learned programming by himself.
   B. He went travelling with his parents.
   C. He played games all day.
   D. He went to a programming camp.

2. How did Li Ming feel in the first week of learning?
   A. Very excited and confident.
   B. It was difficult and he wanted to give up.
   C. Bored and sleepy.
   D. Happy because he finished quickly.

3. What kind of game did Li Ming make by the end of the fourth week?
   A. A racing game with cars.
   B. A shooting game.
   C. A puzzle game with numbers.
   D. A snake game that eats food and grows longer.

4. How did Li Ming's computer teacher react when he saw the game?
   A. The teacher thought it was too simple.
   B. The teacher asked him to stop playing games.
   C. The teacher was surprised and asked him to share with the class.
   D. The teacher gave him a low score.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Self-learning needs patience and practice, and can lead to success.
   B. Li Ming's parents taught him programming.
   C. Making games is easier than learning other subjects.
   D. Li Ming will never make a bigger game.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last summer holiday, Li Ming had two whole months free... One day, he saw a video online about making games with code. He thought, 'Why not try it myself?'" 及第②段 "Li Ming had never learned programming before. He found some free lessons on the Internet and started from the beginning"

**【解析】** 文中说明李明暑假待在家，看到视频后自学编程。A项正确。A项父母忙于工作未与他旅行；C、D项未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The first week was difficult. He made many mistakes and felt like giving up."

**【解析】** 第一周很难，犯了很多错误，想放弃。B项正确。A项与"felt like giving up"矛盾；C、D项未体现。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "By the end of the fourth week, he had made a small game—a snake that eats food and grows longer."

**【解析】** 第四周结束时他做了一款蛇吃食物变长的游戏。D项正确。A、C、D项游戏类型均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The teacher was surprised and asked him to share his experience with the class."

**【解析】** 老师感到惊讶并让他与全班分享经历。C项正确。A项认为太简单、C项让他别玩、D项给低分均与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "He realized that learning something new on your own is not impossible. You just need patience and practice." 及第⑤段 "His holiday experience taught him that the best way to learn is to start and never stop trying."

**【解析】** 李明从自学经历中领悟到：自学新事物需要耐心和练习，最好的学习方式是开始并坚持。可推理出自学具备这些品质能带来成功。A项正确。A项父母未教他；C项难度比较未提及；D项与"dreams of making a bigger game"矛盾。故选B。
