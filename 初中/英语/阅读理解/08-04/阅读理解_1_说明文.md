# 阅读理解（说明文）

## How Tornadoes Form and How to Stay Safe

① A tornado is a very strong wind that spins (旋转) in a circle like a funnel (漏斗). It can move houses, break trees and cause serious damage. Tornadoes often appear in spring and summer. Do you know how they form and what you should do to stay safe?

② Tornadoes form when warm, wet air meets cold, dry air. The warm air rises quickly, and the cold air moves down. This creates a spinning movement. When the spinning becomes stronger, a funnel shape appears. The funnel can touch the ground and move at high speed. Some tornadoes last only a few minutes, but they can destroy everything in their path.

③ Scientists can often predict when conditions are right for tornadoes. When there is a tornado warning, you should act quickly. The most important thing is to find a safe place.

④ If you are at home, go to the basement (地下室) or the lowest floor. Stay away from windows. If there is no basement, go to a small room in the center of the house, like a bathroom. Get under a strong table and cover your head with your arms. If you are outside, lie flat in a low place like a ditch (沟渠). Never try to run away from a tornado in a car—it can move faster than you.

⑤ Tornadoes are dangerous, but knowing how they form and what to do can help save lives. Listen to the radio or TV for warnings. Stay calm and follow the safety rules. Your life is the most important.

**注释：** spin 旋转；funnel 漏斗；basement 地下室；ditch 沟渠

### 选择题

1. When do tornadoes often happen?
   A. In spring and summer.
   B. In winter and autumn.
   C. Only at night.
   D. Only in the morning.

2. How does a tornado form according to the passage?
   A. When it rains heavily for a long time.
   B. When people build too many houses.
   C. When warm, wet air meets cold, dry air and creates spinning movement.
   D. When the sun is very strong.

3. What should you do if you are at home during a tornado warning?
   A. Stand near the window to watch it.
   B. Run outside as fast as you can.
   C. Call your friends on the phone.
   D. Go to the basement or lowest floor and stay away from windows.

4. Why does the writer say "Never try to run away from a tornado in a car"?
   A. A tornado can move faster than a car.
   B. Cars are too expensive to lose.
   C. Cars need gas to run.
   D. It is more fun to stay at home.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell interesting stories about wind.
   B. To compare tornadoes with other weather.
   C. To introduce different types of storms.
   D. To explain how tornadoes form and teach people safety measures.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Tornadoes often appear in spring and summer"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明龙卷风常在春季和夏季出现。A项正确。A项冬季秋季、C项仅夜间、D项仅早晨均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Tornadoes form when warm, wet air meets cold, dry air. The warm air rises quickly, and the cold air moves down. This creates a spinning movement."

**【解析】** 原文直接说明龙卷风形成于暖湿空气与冷干空气相遇，产生旋转运动。C项准确概括该过程。A、C、D项均未在文中提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "If you are at home, go to the basement or the lowest floor. Stay away from windows."

**【解析】** 在家时应前往地下室或最低层，并远离窗户。D项正确。A项靠近窗户观察危险；C项跑出去错误；D项打电话非安全措施。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Never try to run away from a tornado in a car—it can move faster than you"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明龙卷风移动速度可能快于汽车，因此不能开车逃离。可推理出不这样做的原因是龙卷风比车快。A项正确。A、C、D项均非作者意图。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图/主旨

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第①段问句及第⑤段 "knowing how they form and what to do can help save lives"

**【解析】** 文章既介绍龙卷风形成原理，又详细说明安全防护措施，主旨是科普知识并教授人们如何避险。D项准确概括。A、C、D项均偏离主旨。故选B。
