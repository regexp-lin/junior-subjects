# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Studying Abroad at a Young Age: Pros and Cons**

① More and more Chinese students go abroad to study when they are still in middle school or even primary school. Some parents think it helps their children learn English and get a better education. Others worry about the cost and the risk of children being too far from home. What are the pros and cons of studying abroad at a young age?

② Supporters say that young students can learn a foreign language faster. Living in another country forces them to use English every day. They also experience different cultures and ways of thinking. This can make them more open-minded and independent. Some believe that a foreign diploma will help when applying for jobs or universities later.

③ However, critics point out several problems. First, it is very expensive. Tuition, living costs, and flights can add up to millions of yuan. Not all families can afford it. Second, young students may feel lonely and homesick. They miss their family and friends. Some find it hard to adapt and become unhappy. Third, if they leave too early, they may lose touch with Chinese culture and language. That could be a loss for both the child and the family.

④ In my view, there is no one-size-fits-all answer. For some children, studying abroad young can work well. For others, it may be better to wait until they are older and more mature. Parents should think carefully about their child's personality, their budget, and their long-term goals. It is also important to stay in touch and support the child emotionally if they do go.

⑤ Studying abroad can be a valuable experience, but it is not the only path to success. Whatever choice families make, the child's happiness and well-being should come first.

**注释：** diploma 文凭；homesick 想家的；mature 成熟的

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, what is one advantage of studying abroad young?
   A. It is cheaper.
   B. Students can learn a foreign language faster and become more open-minded.
   C. Parents can go too.
   D. There are no disadvantages.

2. What do critics worry about?
   A. Students learn too slowly.
   B. High cost, loneliness, and possible loss of Chinese culture.
   C. Students become too smart.
   D. Schools are too close to home.

3. What does the writer suggest?
   A. All children should go abroad.
   B. There is no single answer; parents should consider the child's needs and situation.
   C. No one should study abroad.
   D. Only rich families should consider it.

4. What does "homesick" mean in Paragraph ③?
   A. Sick at home.
   B. Missing home and family.
   C. Loving school.
   D. Very healthy.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Studying abroad is the best choice for everyone.
   B. The child's happiness and well-being should be the priority when making this decision.
   C. Success only comes from studying abroad.
   D. Parents should never let children go abroad.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "learn a foreign language faster... more open-minded and independent"
**【解析】** 支持者认为能更快学语言、更开阔独立。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "expensive... lonely and homesick... lose touch with Chinese culture"
**【解析】** 反对者担心费用高、孤独及文化断层。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "no one-size-fits-all answer... think carefully about their child's personality"
**【解析】** 作者认为无统一答案，家长应结合孩子和家庭情况考虑。B项正确。A、C、D项与建议不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第③段 "miss their family and friends" 与 homesick 并列
**【解析】** 指想念家乡和亲人。B项准确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "child's happiness and well-being should come first"
**【解析】** 结尾强调孩子幸福与健康应优先。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
