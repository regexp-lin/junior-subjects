# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Halloween English Party — You're Invited!**

① Dear students,

② The English Club is holding a Halloween-themed English party! It will be a great chance to practise your English, make new friends, and have fun.

**Event details:**

| Item        | Details                    |
|-------------|----------------------------|
| Date        | October 31st (Thursday)     |
| Time        | 4:00 p.m. – 6:00 p.m.      |
| Place       | School hall                 |
| Dress code  | Costume encouraged          |
| Activities  | Games, storytelling, prizes |

③ There will be English games, a costume contest, and a story-telling round. Winners get small gifts. Please bring your own snacks to share. Drinks will be provided. No scary masks that cover the full face — we want everyone to feel safe and happy.

④ Sign up at the English Club desk before October 28th. Only 80 seats are available. First come, first served. If you have questions, ask Mr Zhang in Room 305 or email englishclub@school.edu.cn.

⑤ We hope to see you there! Happy Halloween!

English Club  
October 20th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Halloween party be held?
   A. October 20th.
   B. October 28th.
   C. October 31st.
   D. November 1st.

2. Where will the party take place?
   A. Room 305.
   B. School hall.
   C. English Club desk.
   D. Library.

3. What should students bring according to the notice?
   A. Drinks only.
   B. Their own snacks to share.
   C. Full-face masks.
   D. Nothing.

4. By when should students sign up?
   A. October 20th.
   B. October 28th.
   C. October 31st.
   D. November 1st.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. The party is only for teachers.
   B. The party mixes English practice with Halloween fun and has limited spaces.
   C. There are no activities at the party.
   D. Costumes are not allowed.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Date 为 "October 31st (Thursday)"
**【解析】** 派对于10月31日举行。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Place 为 "School hall"
**【解析】** 派对在学校礼堂举行。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Please bring your own snacks to share"
**【解析】** 需自备零食分享。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Sign up... before October 28th"
**【解析】** 报名截止10月28日。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "practise your English"；第④段 "Only 80 seats"
**【解析】** 派对结合英语练习与万圣节乐趣，且名额有限。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
