# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Food Festival 2026 — Taste the World!**

② Welcome to our School Food Festival! This year we will celebrate food and culture from different countries. The festival will take place on **May 28, 2026** in the school playground from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM. All students, teachers and parents are invited to join us.

③ **Activities:** You can taste traditional dishes from China, Japan, America, Italy and more. Each class will prepare a food booth (摊位) to share special dishes. There will also be a cooking show at 3:00 PM and a short culture talk about table manners (餐桌礼仪) around the world.

④ **How to take part:** If your class wants to join, please sign up at the Student Office before May 20. Each class needs at least 3 students to help at the booth. Bring your own plates and cups for environmental protection. We also welcome parents to bring homemade food to share.

⑤ For more information, please visit www.schoolfood.edu.cn or call Mr. Zhang at 020-88881234. Come and enjoy a delicious afternoon with us!

**注释：** booth 摊位；manner 礼仪；homemade 自制的

---

### 选择题

1. When and where will the Food Festival be held?
   A. On May 28 from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM in the school playground.
   B. On May 20 in the Student Office.
   C. On May 28 at 3:00 PM in the school hall.
   D. From May 20 to May 28 in each classroom.

2. According to the poster, what can visitors do at the festival?
   A. Only watch a cooking show.
   B. Taste dishes from different countries and attend a culture talk.
   C. Sign up for new classes.
   D. Buy expensive phones and watches.

3. What must classes do if they want to join the festival?
   A. Prepare at least 5 different dishes.
   B. Pay a fee of 100 yuan.
   C. Sign up at the Student Office before May 20 and have at least 3 students help at the booth.
   D. Bring only water and snacks.

4. What can we infer from the passage about bringing plates and cups?
   A. The school will provide all tableware for free.
   B. Only parents need to bring plates.
   C. Plates and cups are not allowed at the festival.
   D. Students should bring their own to help protect the environment.

5. What is the main purpose of this poster?
   A. To invite people to join the School Food Festival and learn about food and culture.
   B. To sell food to students and parents.
   C. To announce the closing of the Student Office.
   D. To ask students to prepare for exams.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The festival will take place on **May 28, 2026** in the school playground from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM."

**【解析】** 原文明确交代时间、地点和时段。A 项 5 月 20 日为报名截止日，地点为 Student Office，与活动本身不符；B 项完全对应原文；C 项 3:00 PM 仅为 cooking show 时间，非整体时段，hall 与 playground 不符；D 项时间范围与教室地点均错误。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "You can taste traditional dishes from China, Japan, America, Italy and more" 及 "a short culture talk about table manners around the world."

**【解析】**  visitors 可品尝各国传统美食，并参加关于餐桌礼仪的文化讲座。B 项正确。A 项"仅能观看烹饪秀"不完整，遗漏品尝与讲座；C 项"报名新班级"无依据；D 项"购买手机手表"与活动主题无关。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "please sign up at the Student Office before May 20. Each class needs at least 3 students to help at the booth."

**【解析】** 班级须在 5 月 20 日前于学生会办公室报名，且至少有 3 名学生帮忙摆摊。B 项正确。A 项"至少 5 道菜"未提及；C 项缴费 100 元文中无；D 项"只带水和零食"与"homemade food"等描述不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Bring your own plates and cups for environmental protection."

**【解析】** 要求自备餐盘和杯子是为环保。可推断学校提倡减少一次性用品，学生自带餐具以保护环境。A 项"学校免费提供"与"bring your own"矛盾；B 项"仅家长需带"与"your"（指参与者）不符；C 项"不允许携带"与原文相反；D 项正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图 / 主旨归纳

**【定位】** 全文标题 "Taste the World!"、第②段 "celebrate food and culture"、第⑤段 "Come and enjoy a delicious afternoon"

**【解析】** 海报旨在邀请师生家长参加校园美食节，体验各国美食与文化。A 项准确概括目的。B 项"售卖食物"与"share"等表述不符；C 项"宣布学生会关门"无依据；D 项"备考"与主题无关。故选 A。
