> **05-25 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① With the development of the Internet, more and more students choose to study online. Some people say online learning is better than classroom learning, while others hold the opposite view. So, which is actually better?

② **Supporters of online learning** believe it has many advantages. First, students can study at any time and any place. They do not need to travel to school, which saves both time and money. Second, online courses often provide a lot of videos and materials that students can watch or read again and again. If they miss something in class, they can easily review it. Third, shy students may feel more comfortable asking questions online because they do not have to speak in front of the whole class.

③ **However, supporters of classroom learning** argue that face-to-face teaching is more effective. In a real classroom, teachers can see students' faces and know immediately if they understand or not. Students can also discuss with classmates and work on group projects together, which helps them develop teamwork skills. Besides, schools offer a clear schedule (日程) and a quiet place to study. At home, students may be distracted (分心) by games, phones or family members, which makes it harder to stay focused.

④ In my opinion, both ways have their own strengths. Online learning is suitable when we need flexibility (灵活性) or when we want to review materials. Classroom learning works better when we need direct interaction (互动) with teachers and peers, or when we have difficulty staying focused at home. The best way might be to combine both — use online tools to support what we learn in the classroom. In this way, we can get the benefits of both worlds.

⑤ What do you think? Perhaps the answer is different for different people. The most important thing is to choose the way that works best for you.

**注释：** schedule 日程；distract 使分心；flexibility 灵活性；interaction 互动

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, which is NOT an advantage of online learning?
   A. Students can study anytime and anywhere.
   B. Students can watch videos and materials again to review.
   C. Shy students may feel more comfortable asking questions online.
   D. Students always learn better than in a classroom.

2. Why do supporters of classroom learning think face-to-face teaching is more effective?
   A. Because teachers can see students' faces and know if they understand, and students can work in groups.
   B. Because it costs less money.
   C. Because all students are shy and never ask questions.
   D. Because online materials are not helpful at all.

3. What problem might students face when studying at home?
   A. The classroom is too noisy.
   B. Teachers are too strict.
   C. They may be distracted by games, phones or family members.
   D. There are no videos to watch.

4. What is the writer's opinion about online learning and classroom learning?
   A. Both have strengths; the best way might be to combine both.
   B. Online learning is always better.
   C. Classroom learning is always better.
   D. Neither way is useful for students.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer thinks there is only one correct answer for everyone.
   B. The writer believes that all students must study online.
   C. The writer wants to stop both online and classroom learning.
   D. The writer suggests that different people may choose different ways, and the key is to find what works best for oneself.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解（排除型）

**【定位】** 第②段列举在线学习三大优点：anytime/anywhere、可反复观看复习、害羞者更易提问。

**【解析】** A、B、C 三项均对应原文所述优点。D 项"学生总是学得比课堂更好"文中未提及，且与"which is actually better"的讨论立场矛盾，非段落中列出的优势。题干问"哪个不是优点"，故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "teachers can see students' faces and know immediately if they understand or not" 及 "Students can also discuss with classmates and work on group projects together, which helps them develop teamwork skills."

**【解析】** 课堂学习支持者认为：教师能观察学生反应判断是否理解，学生可小组讨论与合作完成项目。A 项准确概括。B 项"成本更低"文中未比较；C 项"所有学生都害羞"与"shy students"部分群体矛盾；D 项"在线材料毫无帮助"过于绝对，非原文观点。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "At home, students may be distracted by games, phones or family members, which makes it harder to stay focused."

**【解析】** 在家学习可能被游戏、手机或家人分散注意力，难以集中。C 项正确。A 项"教室太吵"与在家场景不符；B 项"老师太严"未提及；D 项"没有视频"与在线学习资源相反。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第④段 "In my opinion, both ways have their own strengths" 及 "The best way might be to combine both — use online tools to support what we learn in the classroom."

**【解析】** 作者认为两种方式各有优势，最佳做法或许是结合二者，用在线工具辅助课堂所学。A 项正确。B、C 项"总是更好"与"both have strengths"矛盾；D 项"都无用"与作者立场相反。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Perhaps the answer is different for different people. The most important thing is to choose the way that works best for you."

**【解析】** 作者指出答案可能因人而异，最重要的是选择最适合自己的方式。可推断不同人可选不同方式，关键是找到对自己最有效的。D 项正确。A 项"唯一正确答案"与"different for different people"矛盾；B 项"必须在线学习"过度推断；C 项"停止两种方式"与全文主旨相反。故选 D。
