# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Sunny Café — Menu & Ordering**

② Welcome to Sunny Café! We serve breakfast, lunch, and light snacks. Our café opens at 7:00 a.m. and closes at 8:00 p.m. You can order at the counter or use our app for takeaway.

③ **Breakfast (7:00–10:30):** We have sandwiches, eggs and toast, fruit salad, and coffee or milk. The set breakfast costs ¥28. **Lunch (11:00–14:00):** Try our beef noodles (¥32), chicken rice (¥25), or vegetable soup with bread (¥22). Students with a student ID get 10% off lunch.

④ **Drinks:** Black tea ¥12, green tea ¥12, orange juice ¥15, milk shake ¥18. **Desserts:** Ice cream ¥10, cake slice ¥15. Please note: We do not serve alcohol. Children under 12 eat for half price on weekends.

⑤ **How to order:** 1) Choose your food from the menu. 2) Tell the staff your table number or say "takeaway." 3) Pay at the counter. 4) Wait for your number to be called. For groups of more than 6, please call 020-8888-6666 to book a table in advance.

**注释：** takeaway 外卖；in advance 提前

---

### 选择题

1. What time does Sunny Café close?
   A. At 7:00 p.m.
   B. At 8:00 p.m.
   C. At 10:30 a.m.
   D. At 14:00.

2. According to the menu, how can students save money at lunch?
   A. By ordering breakfast instead.
   B. By booking a table in advance.
   C. By showing a student ID to get 10% off.
   D. By ordering takeaway.

3. Which of the following is TRUE about Sunny Café?
   A. It serves alcohol.
   B. Children under 12 always eat for half price.
   C. Groups of more than 6 should call to book a table.
   D. The café only opens in the morning.

4. How much does a set breakfast cost?
   A. ¥25.
   B. ¥28.
   C. ¥32.
   D. ¥22.

5. What is the main purpose of this text?
   A. To tell a story about a café.
   B. To introduce a café's menu and ordering information.
   C. To compare different restaurants.
   D. To teach readers how to cook.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Our café opens at 7:00 a.m. and closes at 8:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 文中明确写明咖啡馆晚上 8 点关门。B 项正确。A 项 7:00 p.m. 为关门时间混淆；C、D 项分别为早餐结束时间和午餐结束时间。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Students with a student ID get 10% off lunch."

**【解析】** 持学生证的学生午餐可享 10% 优惠。C 项正确。A 项改点早餐与优惠无关；B 项订桌针对 6 人以上团队；D 项外卖无折扣说明。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "For groups of more than 6, please call 020-8888-6666 to book a table in advance."

**【解析】** 6 人以上需提前电话订座。C 项正确。A 项与 "We do not serve alcohol" 矛盾；B 项仅在周末享受半价，非"总是"；D 项营业时间为 7:00–20:00，不止早上。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The set breakfast costs ¥28."

**【解析】** 套餐早餐价格为 ¥28。B 项正确。A 项 ¥25 为鸡肉饭价格；C 项 ¥32 为牛肉面价格；D 项 ¥22 为蔬菜汤套餐价格。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图 / 主旨理解

**【定位】** 全文：标题 "Menu & Ordering"，介绍营业时间、菜品、价格、点餐方式等。

**【解析】** 本文为餐厅菜单与点餐说明，旨在介绍菜品及点餐流程。B 项正确。A 项非叙事文体；C 项未比较多家餐厅；D 项非烹饪教学。故选 B。
