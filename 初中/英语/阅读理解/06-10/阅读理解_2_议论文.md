# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should schools ban junk food? This question is discussed in many countries. Junk food usually means food with lots of sugar, salt, or fat but little nutrition, such as chips, candy, and sweet drinks. Some people think schools should stop selling it. Others believe students should have the right to choose what they eat.

② **Those who support a ban** say that junk food is bad for students' health. Eating too much of it can lead to obesity, tooth problems, and difficulty in concentrating at school. Schools should help students form healthy eating habits. If junk food is not sold at school, students may eat more fruit, vegetables, and proper meals. Health experts often advise schools to offer healthier choices in canteens and vending machines.

③ **Those who oppose a ban** argue that students can still buy junk food outside school. A ban at school does not mean students will eat healthily. Besides, some think it is the parents' job to teach children about food, not the school's. Others say that students should learn to make their own choices. If we ban everything that is not perfect, where do we stop? A little junk food now and then may not cause serious harm.

④ **What some schools have done:** In some places, schools have removed junk food from canteens and replaced it with salads, sandwiches, and fruit. Reports show that students' health has improved. However, some students miss their favorite snacks and buy them after school instead. There is also the problem that "healthy" food can be more expensive, which may worry some families.

⑤ In conclusion, both sides have valid points. Banning junk food at school may help some students eat better, but it cannot solve the whole problem. Education about healthy eating and support from families are also important. Perhaps the best way is for schools to offer mainly healthy food while still allowing students to understand why some choices are better than others.

**注释：** obesity 肥胖；vending machine 自动售货机；valid 有效的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, why do some people support banning junk food at school?
   A. Because junk food is too expensive.
   B. Because it can harm students' health and schools should help form healthy habits.
   C. Because students do not like junk food.
   D. Because there is not enough space in canteens.

2. What do those who oppose a ban think?
   A. Junk food is good for students.
   B. Students can buy junk food outside school; parents and students should make their own choices.
   C. Schools should sell more junk food.
   D. Healthy food is always cheaper.

3. What have some schools found after removing junk food from canteens?
   A. All students stopped eating junk food.
   B. Students' health improved, but some still buy junk food after school.
   C. Parents became very angry.
   D. Healthy food became much cheaper.

4. The underlined word "valid" in paragraph ⑤ probably means ______.
   A. wrong
   B. weak
   C. reasonable and acceptable
   D. strange

5. What is the writer's main idea about banning junk food at school?
   A. Schools must ban all junk food.
   B. A ban alone cannot solve the problem; education and family support also matter.
   C. Students should never eat any junk food.
   D. Only parents should decide what students eat.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "junk food is bad for students' health... can lead to obesity, tooth problems... Schools should help students form healthy eating habits."

**【解析】** 支持禁售者认为垃圾食品危害健康，学校应帮助学生养成良好饮食习惯。B 项正确。A 项价格、C 项学生喜好、D 项食堂空间均未在支持理由中出现。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students can still buy junk food outside school... parents' job to teach children... students should learn to make their own choices."

**【解析】** 反对者认为校外仍可购买，教育责任在家长，学生应学会自主选择。B 项正确。A 项"垃圾食品对学生好"与反对者立场不符；C、D 项文中未提及。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Reports show that students' health has improved. However, some students miss their favorite snacks and buy them after school instead."

**【解析】** 学校禁售后学生健康有改善，但部分学生课后仍会购买。B 项正确。A 项"全部停止"过于绝对；C 项家长愤怒未提及；D 项健康食品更便宜与原文"可能更贵"矛盾。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "both sides have valid points"

**【解析】** 语境表明双方观点都有道理。valid 意为合理、可接受的。C 项 reasonable and acceptable 正确。A、B、D 项与语境相反。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "A ban alone cannot solve the whole problem. Education about healthy eating and support from families are also important."

**【解析】** 作者认为仅禁售不够，健康饮食教育和家庭配合同样重要。B 项准确概括。A 项"必须全面禁止"过于绝对；C 项"绝不吃"不现实；D 项仅强调家长，与原文"学校+家庭"不符。故选 B。
