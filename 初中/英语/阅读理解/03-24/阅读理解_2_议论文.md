# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① In most middle schools in China, the lunch break lasts for about one to one and a half hours. During this time, students eat their lunch and take a short nap (午睡). However, some students and experts have recently suggested that schools should extend (延长) the lunch break to two full hours. Should the lunch break be extended? This question has started a hot discussion.

② Those who support the longer lunch break have very good reasons. They point out that teenagers are in a period of rapid physical growth and need plenty of rest. Getting up early and taking classes all morning can make students very tired. A longer lunch break would allow them to eat slowly and have at least a 45-minute solid nap. With a good rest, they will be much more energetic and focused for their afternoon classes.

③ What is more, a longer break could give students time to join in some relaxing activities. For example, they could talk with friends, listen to soft music, or take a short walk on the playground. These activities are helpful for reducing their study stress (压力).

④ On the other hand, some teachers and parents do not think extending the lunch break is a good idea. They argue that if the lunch break is longer, the school day will have to end much later. Students will go home late, meaning they will have dinner late and start their homework late. In the end, they might have to sleep later at night, which is exactly opposite to the goal of getting more rest.

⑤ To sum up, both sides make sense. A longer lunch break brings a better daytime rest, but it might stretch (拉长) the whole school day. Perhaps schools should listen to the voices of their own students and parents to find a timetable that works best for everyone.

---

**注释：** extend 延长；nap 午睡；rapid 快速的；physical 身体的；solid 扎实的，充分的；stress 压力

---

### 题目

1. What are students mainly doing during the lunch break now?
   A. They only eat lunch and talk with friends.
   B. They eat lunch and take a short nap.
   C. They play basketball on the playground.
   D. They finish all their homework for the day.

2. Why do some people think students need a longer lunch break?
   A. Because students want more time to prepare for exams.
   B. Because they believe students are growing fast and need plenty of rest.
   C. Because the school food is too hot and takes a long time to eat.
   D. Because teachers need more time to mark homework.

3. According to the supporters, what is a benefit of doing relaxing activities during a longer break?
   A. It helps students reduce their study stress.
   B. It makes students feel less hungry in the afternoon.
   C. It improves students' musical skills.
   D. It helps students lose weight by running.

4. What do opponents (反对者) worry about if the lunch break is extended?
   A. Students will forget what they learned in the morning.
   B. Students might lose interest in their afternoon classes.
   C. Students will get too fat from sleeping after eating.
   D. The school day will end later and push students to sleep later at night.

5. What is the writer's attitude towards this discussion?
   A. The writer completely supports a longer lunch break.
   B. The writer totally agrees with the worried parents.
   C. The writer believes schools should find a balance that suits their own situations.
   D. The writer thinks the lunch break should be canceled.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "During this time, students eat their lunch and take a short nap."

**【解析】** 原文表明，目前的午休时间里，学生们吃午饭并进行短暂的午睡。B 项与原文一致。A 项（只吃饭和朋友聊天）、C 项（打篮球）、D 项（完成所有作业）均在第一段现状介绍中未被提及。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "They point out that teenagers are in a period of rapid physical growth and need plenty of rest."

**【解析】** 支持延长午休的人给出的理由是，青少年正处于身体快速生长期，需要充足的休息。B 项是对该句的同义转述，完全符合题意。A 项（为了准备考试）、C 项（饭菜太烫）、D 项（老师要批改作业）都不是文中支持者的理由。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3句 "These activities are helpful for reducing their study stress."

**【解析】** 第三段指出较长的休息时间能让学生参加放松活动（如聊天、听音乐、散步），并明确说明这些活动有助于"减轻他们的学习压力"（reducing their study stress）。A 项正确。B 项（中午不易饿）、C 项（提高音乐技能）、D 项（跑步减肥）均属无中生有。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-4句 "They argue that if the lunch break is longer, the school day will have to end much later. ... In the end, they might have to sleep later at night..."

**【解析】** 反对者（老师和家长）担心的核心问题是，午休拉长会导致放学变晚，进而引起吃晚饭晚、做作业晚，最终导致晚上睡觉更晚（与想要多休息的目标反而背道而驰）。D 项"放学晚并迫使学生晚上睡得更晚"准确概括了这一担忧。A、B、C 项的担忧点在文中均未提及。故选 D。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 观点态度

**【定位】** 第⑤段（总结段）"To sum up, both sides make sense. ... Perhaps schools should listen to the voices of their own students and parents to find a timetable that works best for everyone."

**【解析】** 在最后一段，作者表示"双方都有道理"（both sides make sense），并建议学校倾听自己学生和家长的声音，找到最适合大家的作息时间表。这体现了作者客观、中立、因地制宜的态度。C 项"作者认为学校应该找到适合自身情况的平衡点"最符合这一立场。A、B 项是只倾倒向一方的偏激态度；D 项（取消午休）明显违背常理及文义。故选 C。
