# 阅读理解 第1篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Plastic pollution in the ocean is a huge problem. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste end up in our seas. It hurts sea animals and pollutes our beautiful beaches. But how can we clean such a wide and deep ocean? A brilliant young man from the Netherlands, Boyan Slat, came up with a bold idea.

② When Boyan was 16, he went diving (潜水) in Greece and saw more plastic bags than fish. This shocking experience made him determined to find a solution. Traditional methods of using boats and nets to catch plastic are too slow, too expensive, and they also catch sea life by accident. So, Boyan thought: "Instead of going after the plastic, why not let the plastic come to us?"

③ He started a project called "The Ocean Cleanup." He and his team designed a giant floating (漂浮的) tube. This U-shaped tube, which is 600 meters long, moves slowly on the surface of the ocean. Below the tube is a hanging screen that goes 3 meters deep into the water. The screen stops the plastic, but allows sea animals to swim safely underneath it.

④ Driven by the wind, waves, and ocean currents (洋流), the system moves slightly faster than the plastic. This allows it to sweep up the floating garbage like a giant Pac-Man. Once enough plastic is collected, a ship comes to take it back to land for recycling. 

⑤ Today, this system is slowly but surely cleaning up the Great Pacific Garbage Patch — a huge area of plastic waste in the ocean. Although it might take years to see totally clean oceans, Boyan's invention brings hope to our planet. It shows us that with creative thinking, even the biggest problems can be solved.

---

**注释：** brilliant 才华横溢的；bold 大胆的；solution 解决办法；tube 管子；underneath 在……下面；current 洋流

---

### 题目

1. What did Boyan Slat experience in Greece?
   A. He caught many big fish with his friends.
   B. He found a creative way to use old nets.
   C. He designed his first floating tube.
   D. He saw a lot of plastic bags while diving.

2. Why did Boyan think traditional methods of cleaning the ocean are NOT good?
   A. They are too fast and difficult to control.
   B. They are too slow, expensive, and hurt sea animals.
   C. They only work on the surface of the ocean.
   D. They only catch big plastic bottles.

3. How does the screen below the tube work?
   A. It stops both the plastic and all the sea animals.
   B. It catches sea animals but lets the plastic pass through.
   C. It stops the plastic but lets sea animals swim underneath.
   D. It pushes the plastic deep down into the bottom of the ocean.

4. What pushes the cleaning system to move in the ocean?
   A. The wind, waves, and ocean currents.
   B. A giant ship that pulls the tube.
   C. Hundreds of hard-working volunteers.
   D. An electric engine inside the tube.

5. What can we learn from Boyan Slat's story according to the last paragraph?
   A. Ocean plastic can be easily cleaned up in just a few days.
   B. People should stop going diving in the ocean.
   C. The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is the only polluted area.
   D. Creative thinking can help solve very big problems.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "When Boyan was 16, he went diving in Greece and saw more plastic bags than fish."

**【解析】** 原文提到 Boyan 在希腊潜水时，看到的塑料袋比鱼还多。D 项与原文事实完全一致。A、B、C项的事情在希腊潜水时都没有发生（设计管子是在他后来创立项目时）。故选 D。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "Traditional methods of using boats and nets to catch plastic are too slow, too expensive, and they also catch sea life by accident."

**【解析】** 原文清楚地指出了传统方法（用船和网捕捞）的缺点：太慢、太贵，而且会意外捕获（伤害）海洋生物。B 项完美概括了这三个缺点。A 项的"太快"与原文的"too slow"相反；C、D 项均是无中生有。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "The screen stops the plastic, but allows sea animals to swim safely underneath it."

**【解析】** 讲到管子下方的"屏障"（screen）是如何运作的，原文明说这层屏障能拦住塑料，但允许海洋动物安全地从它下面游过。C 项与原文句意一致。A 项说连动物一起拦住、B 项说拦动物漏塑料、D 项说把塑料压入海底，均与这句描述相矛盾。故选 C。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "Driven by the wind, waves, and ocean currents, the system moves slightly faster than the plastic."

**【解析】** 原文指出，这个系统是由"风、波浪和洋流"（wind, waves, and ocean currents）驱动的。A 项精确对应。B 项（大船拉）、C 项（志愿者）、D 项（电动马达）在文中均未提及。大船（ship）是在塑料收集够了之后来运走塑料的，不是用来驱动管子的。故选 A。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "It shows us that with creative thinking, even the biggest problems can be solved."

**【解析】** 文章最后一段总结了这个发明的作用及对我们的启示：它向我们展示了只要有创造性思维，连最大的问题都能被解决。D 项与这句启示完全吻合。A 项说几天内就能轻松清理完（与原文 "take years" 几年的时间矛盾）；B、C 项的说法在文中没有依据。故选 D。
