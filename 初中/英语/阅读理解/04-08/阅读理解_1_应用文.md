# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Welcome to Sunshine Sports Center!**

② Are you looking for a place to play sports on weekends? Do you want to keep fit and make new friends? Sunshine Sports Center is your best choice! We are newly open and welcome everyone to join us.

③ **What do we have?**
- **Swimming Pool (First Floor):** A large indoor swimming pool. The water is clean and warm all year round.
- **Basketball Court (Second Floor):** Two huge basketball courts. You can bring your own ball or borrow one from us for free.
- **Gym (Third Floor):** Modern exercise machines and professional (专业的) coaches to help you keep healthy.

④ **Opening Hours:**
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
- Saturday and Sunday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

⑤ **Special Offer for Students!**
Show your student ID card, and you can get a 50% discount (折扣) on your first visit! A normal ticket is $20, so you only need to pay $10. 
We also offer a Monthly Card for $100. With this card, you can come every day!

⑥ **How to find us:**
Address: No. 123, Green Street. (Just next to the City Library.)
Phone: 456-7890

**注释：** professional 专业的；discount 折扣

---

### 选择题

1. Where should you go if you want to play basketball?
A. The first floor.
B. The second floor.
C. The third floor.
D. The fourth floor.

2. When does the sports center close on Saturday?
A. At 8:00 AM.
B. At 9:00 AM.
C. At 9:00 PM.
D. At 10:00 PM.

3. How much should a student pay for the first visit if a normal ticket is $20?
A. $10.
B. $20.
C. $50.
D. $100.

4. Form the text, what can we know about the Sunshine Sports Center?
A. It is an old sports center with a long history.
B. People must bring their own basketballs.
C. It is located right next to the City Library.
D. The water in the swimming pool is cold in winter.

5. Who is this advertisement (广告) mainly written for?
A. Only professional basketball players.
B. People who want to find a job as a coach.
C. People who want to do sports and keep fit.
D. Students who want to learn how to swim.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "Basketball Court (Second Floor): Two huge basketball courts."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到篮球场（Basketball Court）在二楼（Second Floor）。故选B。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段中 "Saturday and Sunday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM"

**【解析】** 原文提到周六和周日的开放时间是早上8:00到晚上10:00。因此周六关门时间是晚上10点。故选D。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段中 "Show your student ID card, and you can get a 50% discount... A normal ticket is $20, so you only need to pay $10."

**【解析】** 原文提到出示学生证首次去可以打五折（50% discount），正常票价20美元，所以学生只需付10美元（$10）。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑥段中 "Address: No. 123, Green Street. (Just next to the City Library.)"

**【解析】** A项错误，因为第②段说它是新开的（newly open）；B项错误，因为第③段说可以免费借篮球；D项错误，因为提到水全年温暖（warm all year round）；C项符合原文，提到的地址正好在大图书馆旁边（next to the City Library）。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图 / 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第②段 "Are you looking for a place to play sports on weekends? Do you want to keep fit and make new friends?"

**【解析】** 广告中提到游泳池、篮球场、健身房等设施，针对的人群是“想在周末做运动、想保持健康和交朋友”的人（People who want to do sports and keep fit）。A、B、D项都过于片面，且文中未说明这是只给职业球员或只教学生游泳的地方。故选C。
