> **04-08 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Cooking is an essential (必不可少的) life skill. However, many students today cannot cook even a simple meal. Some parents think that cooking takes up too much time and it is dangerous for kids to use knives or fire. They believe children should only focus on studying. I don't agree with them. I think every teenager should learn how to cook.

② To begin with, learning to cook helps teenagers become independent (独立的). Sooner or later, we will leave our parents and go to college or work in another city. If we only know how to eat food from restaurants or order takeaway (外卖), it will cost a lot of money and is not good for our health. Knowing how to cook means we can easily feed ourselves.

③ Furthermore, cooking is a fun and creative (有创造力的) activity. The kitchen is like a magic lab. When we put different vegetables, meat, and spices (香料) together, we can make delicious dishes. Cooking can help us relax after a long day of studying. We can also share our food with family and friends. This brings a lot of happiness.

④ Finally, cooking teaches us to appreciate (感激) our parents' hard work. Preparing a meal takes time and energy. When we wash vegetables and cook meat ourselves, we will understand how tired our parents are after working all day in the kitchen.

⑤ In short, cooking is not a waste of time. It is a necessary skill for a better future. Parents should give their children chances to learn cooking at home.

**注释：** essential 必不可少的；independent 独立的；takeaway 外卖；appreciate 感激

---

### 选择题

1. Why do some parents stop their children from cooking?
A. Because the kitchen is too small.
B. Because children don't like eating food.
C. Because they think it is a waste of time and dangerous.
D. Because they want to eat takeaway every day.

2. What is the first reason the writer gives for learning to cook?
A. It helps teenagers become independent.
B. It helps teenagers make money.
C. It is better than doing homework.
D. It helps teenagers get better grades in school.

3. According to paragraph ③, how does cooking make people feel?
A. Tired and hungry.
B. Sad and lonely.
C. Relaxed and happy.
D. Anxious and angry.

4. What does the underlined word "appreciate" in paragraph ④ mean?
A. To get angry with someone.
B. To understand the value of something and be thankful.
C. To laugh at something loudly.
D. To forget something completely.

5. What is the writer's attitude (态度) towards teenagers learning to cook?
A. He thinks it is a bad idea.
B. He supports it strongly.
C. He doesn't care about it.
D. He thinks only girls should learn it.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3-4句 "Some parents think that cooking takes up too much time and it is dangerous for kids to use knives or fire... children should only focus on studying."

**【解析】** 原文提到，部分家长认为做饭占用太多时间，且小孩子用刀用火非常危险，觉得孩子应该专心学习。因此他们不让孩子做饭（A、B、D皆未提及）。故选C。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "To begin with, learning to cook helps teenagers become independent (独立的)."

**【解析】** 作者给出的第一个理由段落开头就明示了：学习做饭有助于青少年变得独立（independent）。B项赚钱；C项比写作业好；D项能得好成绩等观点并未在该段出现。故选A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段第4-6句 "Cooking can help us relax after a long day of studying... This brings a lot of happiness."

**【解析】** 原文说明做饭是一项有趣并能带来创造力的活动，可以帮助我们放松（relax），当分享食物给家人朋友时还会带来大量的快乐（happiness）。A、B、D选项呈现的情绪与原文表述相反。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段全文。特别是 "...we will understand how tired our parents are..."

**【解析】** 句子前半段是“cooking teaches us to appreciate our parents' hard work”，后文解释当自己亲自洗菜做饭时，就能理解父母一天劳作后的辛苦。由此可知 appreciate 的含义为“感激，理解...价值”。B项表示“理解某物价值并感恩”最契合；A项发怒、C项嘲笑、D项遗忘都不符合。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 作者态题

**【定位】** 第①段末尾 "I think every teenager should learn how to cook." 及文章最后一句 "Parents should give their children chances to learn cooking at home."

**【解析】** 作者开篇亮明观点，不支持家长的担忧，认为每个青少年都该学习做饭。并在文末呼吁家长给孩子学习做饭的机会。这表明作者非常强烈地支持（support it strongly）青少年学做饭。故选B。
