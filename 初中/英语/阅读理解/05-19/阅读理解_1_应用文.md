# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Sunshine School Volunteer Program 2026**

② Do you want to help others and learn new skills at the same time? Sunshine School is now recruiting (招募) volunteers for our Spring Semester Program. We welcome all students in Grades 7 to 9 who are kind, patient and willing to share.

③ **Volunteer Positions:**

| Position | Time | Main Task | Requirement |
|----------|------|-----------|-------------|
| Library Helper | Mon/Wed/Fri 4:00—5:00 PM | Shelve books, help students find materials | Love reading |
| Study Buddy | Tue/Thu 4:30—5:30 PM | Help younger students with homework | Good grades in math or English |
| Garden Keeper | Saturday 9:00—11:00 AM | Plant flowers, water plants, keep garden clean | Enjoy outdoor work |
| Reading Guide | Sunday 2:00—4:00 PM | Read stories to children at the community center | Clear voice, patient |

④ Each volunteer will receive a certificate (证书) after completing 20 hours of service. You can also make new friends and gain useful experience. Students who join our program often say they feel happier and more confident (自信的).

⑤ To sign up, please fill in the online form on our school website before March 25. You can choose one or two positions. If you have any questions, send an email to **volunteer@sunshineschool.edu** or visit the Student Office.

**注释：** recruit 招募；shelve 整理上架；certificate 证书；confident 自信的

---

### 选择题

1. Who can join the Sunshine School Volunteer Program?
   A. Only Grade 7 students.
   B. Students in Grades 7 to 9.
   C. Teachers and parents.
   D. Anyone from the community.

2. If Tom wants to help younger students with homework on Tuesday afternoon, which position should he choose?
   A. Library Helper.
   B. Study Buddy.
   C. Garden Keeper.
   D. Reading Guide.

3. What does a Garden Keeper need to do?
   A. Shelve books in the library.
   B. Read stories to children.
   C. Plant flowers, water plants and keep the garden clean.
   D. Help students with math or English homework.

4. What can volunteers get after completing 20 hours of service?
   A. A free book.
   B. A certificate.
   C. Money for their work.
   D. A new school uniform.

5. How can students sign up for the program?
   A. Call the Student Office before March 25.
   B. Write a letter to the school.
   C. Fill in the online form on the school website before March 25.
   D. Visit the community center on Sunday.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We welcome all students in Grades 7 to 9 who are kind, patient and willing to share."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明招募对象为七至九年级学生。A 项仅限七年级学生，范围过窄；C 项老师和家长、D 项社区任何人，均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Study Buddy | Tue/Thu 4:30—5:30 PM | Help younger students with homework"

**【解析】** 帮助低年级学生辅导作业的岗位是 Study Buddy，时间为周二和周四下午 4:30—5:30。A 项 Library Helper 负责整理书籍；C 项 Garden Keeper 负责园艺；D 项 Reading Guide 为周日下午在社区中心读书。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Garden Keeper | Saturday 9:00—11:00 AM | Plant flowers, water plants, keep garden clean"

**【解析】** Garden Keeper 的主要任务是种花、浇水和保持花园整洁。A 项整理书籍属于 Library Helper；B 项给儿童读书属于 Reading Guide；D 项辅导作业属于 Study Buddy。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Each volunteer will receive a certificate after completing 20 hours of service."

**【解析】** 原文写明完成 20 小时服务后可获得证书。A 项免费书籍、C 项报酬、D 项新校服均未在文中提及。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "To sign up, please fill in the online form on our school website before March 25."

**【解析】** 报名方式为在 3 月 25 日前在学校网站填写在线表格。A 项致电学生会原文未提及；B 项写信给学校与"online form"不符；D 项周日去社区中心是 Reading Guide 的工作地点，非报名方式。故选 C。
