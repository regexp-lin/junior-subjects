# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Why Honesty Matters**

① Honesty means telling the truth and not cheating. It is one of the most important values in life. When we are honest, people trust us. When we lie, we lose that trust, and it is hard to get it back.

② At school, honesty matters in many ways. If you copy someone's homework or cheat in an exam, you may get a good grade for once. But you do not really learn anything. Your teacher and classmates may find out, and they will not trust you again. What is worse, you may start to believe that cheating is OK, and that will harm your future.

③ Honesty also shapes our relationships. Friends who are honest with each other build strong friendships. If you lie to a friend, even about a small thing, they may feel hurt when they find out. Parents and teachers want to help us, but they can only do that if we tell them the truth. Hiding problems or making up stories only makes things worse.

④ Some people say that telling a "white lie" is sometimes necessary — for example, to avoid hurting someone's feelings. That may be true in a few cases. But most of the time, we can find a kind way to tell the truth. We do not have to be rude. We can choose our words carefully and still be honest.

⑤ In short, honesty builds trust, helps us learn, and keeps our relationships healthy. It may not always be the easiest choice, but it is the right one. Start with small things: own up when you make a mistake, and tell the truth even when it is difficult. Your character will grow stronger, and people will respect you more.

**Questions:**

1. What happens when we lie, according to the passage?
   A. We become smarter.
   B. We lose people's trust and it is hard to get it back.
   C. We make more friends.
   D. We feel more relaxed.

2. Why is copying homework or cheating in exams bad?
   A. It saves time.
   B. You do not really learn, and people may stop trusting you.
   C. It helps you get into a good school.
   D. Teachers like it.

3. How does honesty affect friendships?
   A. Honesty makes friendships weaker.
   B. Honest friends build strong friendships; lies can hurt friends.
   C. Honesty is not important between friends.
   D. Friends never lie to each other.

4. What does the writer say about "white lies"?
   A. We should always tell white lies.
   B. We should never tell any kind of lie.
   C. In a few cases it may be necessary, but most of the time we can tell the truth in a kind way.
   D. White lies are worse than big lies.

5. What does the writer suggest we do to build honesty?
   A. Never make mistakes.
   B. Own up when we make a mistake and tell the truth even when it is difficult.
   C. Only tell the truth to our parents.
   D. Avoid difficult situations.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "When we lie, we lose that trust, and it is hard to get it back."

**【解析】** 原文说撒谎会失去信任且难以挽回。A项变聪明、C项交更多朋友、D项更放松均与撒谎后果不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "you may get a good grade for once. But you do not really learn anything... they will not trust you again... that will harm your future."

**【解析】** 抄袭或作弊让你学不到东西、失去信任、损害未来。A项省时、C项上好学校、D项老师喜欢均与观点矛盾。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Friends who are honest with each other build strong friendships. If you lie to a friend... they may feel hurt when they find out."

**【解析】** 诚实有助于建立牢固友谊，撒谎会伤害朋友。A项削弱、C项不重要、D项从不撒谎均不准确。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "That may be true in a few cases. But most of the time, we can find a kind way to tell the truth."

**【解析】** 作者认为少数情形下善意的谎言可能必要，但多数时候可以委婉地说真话。A、B、D项过于绝对或偏离。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Start with small things: own up when you make a mistake, and tell the truth even when it is difficult."

**【解析】** 作者建议从小事做起：犯错时承认、困难时也说真话。A项从不犯错不现实；C、D项范围过窄或与建议不符。故选B。
