# 阅读理解（说明文）

**Sleep and Your Health**

① Sleep is as important as food and water. When we sleep, our body rests and repairs itself. Our brain sorts through the things we learned during the day and stores them in memory. Without enough sleep, we feel tired, find it hard to focus, and may get sick more easily.

② How much sleep do we need? Children aged 6–12 need about 9–12 hours each night. Teenagers (13–18) need about 8–10 hours. Many students do not get enough sleep because they go to bed late. Using phones or computers before bed can make it harder to fall asleep. The blue light from screens tells our brain that it is still daytime.

③ What happens when we do not sleep enough? In the short term, we feel sleepy in class and find it difficult to learn. We may become easily upset or angry. Over a long time, lack of sleep can lead to problems like weaker immunity (免疫力), weight gain, and even heart disease. Students who sleep well often do better at school than those who stay up late.

④ How can we sleep better? First, try to go to bed and get up at around the same time every day, even on weekends. Second, avoid screens (phones, tablets, computers) for at least one hour before bed. Third, keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool. Fourth, do not drink coffee or tea in the afternoon or evening. Caffeine keeps you awake.

⑤ Good sleep habits take time to build. Start with one change — for example, putting your phone away one hour before bed. When you sleep well, you will feel better, think more clearly, and have more energy for the new day.

**注释：** immunity 免疫力；caffeine 咖啡因

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "When we sleep, our body rests and repairs itself. Our brain sorts through the things we learned during the day and stores them in memory."

**【解析】** 睡眠时身体休息修复，大脑整理并储存所学。A项吃得更多、C项长高、D项玩得更好均非主要作用。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Teenagers (13–18) need about 8–10 hours."

**【解析】** 青少年每晚需约8–10小时睡眠。A项6–9是部分年龄段；C项9–12为6–12岁儿童；D项10–14文中未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Using phones or computers before bed can make it harder to fall asleep. The blue light from screens tells our brain that it is still daytime."

**【解析】** 睡前使用电子设备产生的蓝光会干扰入睡。A项读书、C项喝水、D项保持黑暗通常有助于睡眠。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Over a long time, lack of sleep can lead to problems like weaker immunity, weight gain, and even heart disease."

**【解析】** 长期睡眠不足可导致免疫力下降、体重增加、心脏病等。A、C、D项均与原文矛盾或无关。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "avoid screens (phones, tablets, computers) for at least one hour before bed"

**【解析】** 建议睡前至少一小时不看屏幕。A项睡前用手机与建议相反；C项晚上喝咖啡会妨碍睡眠；D项每天改变作息与"same time"矛盾。故选B。
