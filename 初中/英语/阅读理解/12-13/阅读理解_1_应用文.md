# 阅读理解（应用文）

## **First Aid Guide for Students**

①Accidents can happen anywhere, including at school. Knowing some basic first aid can help you save a life or reduce pain before a doctor arrives. Here is a simple guide for common situations.

②**For a small cut or wound:** First, wash your hands. Then clean the cut with clean water. Press a clean cloth on the cut to stop the bleeding. Cover it with a bandage. Do not touch the wound with dirty hands.

③**For a nosebleed:** Sit down and lean your head forward. Do not lean back. Pinch the soft part of your nose for about 10 minutes. Breathe through your mouth. If the bleeding does not stop after 20 minutes, ask an adult for help.

④**For a sprained ankle:** Remember RICE. Rest—do not walk on it. Ice—put ice in a cloth and hold it on the ankle for 15 minutes. Compression—wrap it with a bandage. Elevation—keep the foot raised higher than the heart.

⑤If someone is badly hurt or you are not sure what to do, call 120 for help at once. Never move a person who may have a broken bone. Stay calm and wait for the ambulance.

| Situation    | Main Steps                                      |
|-------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Small cut   | Wash hands → Clean wound → Stop bleeding → Bandage |
| Nosebleed   | Sit, lean forward → Pinch nose 10 min → Mouth breathe |
| Sprained ankle | RICE: Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation      |

## 题目

1. What is this passage mainly about?
   A. How to become a doctor.
   B. Basic first aid for common accidents.
   C. School rules for safety.
   D. When to call the police.

2. What should you do first when someone has a small cut?
   A. Call 120.
   B. Wash your hands.
   C. Put ice on it.
   D. Move the person.

3. For a nosebleed, what should you NOT do?
   A. Sit down.
   B. Lean your head forward.
   C. Lean your head back.
   D. Breathe through your mouth.

4. What does "RICE" stand for in first aid?
   A. Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation.
   B. Run, Ice, Call, Elevation.
   C. Rest, Inhale, Compression, Exit.
   D. Read, Ice, Clean, Elevation.

5. When should you call 120?
   A. For any small cut.
   B. When someone has a nosebleed.
   C. When someone is badly hurt or you are unsure.
   D. Only when a bone is broken.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】主旨大意题

【定位】第①段及全文

【解析】
- 第①段指出：掌握基本急救知识可在医生到来前救人、减轻痛苦，后文列举小伤口、流鼻血、扭伤等常见情况的处理步骤。
- A“如何成为医生”、C“学校安全规则”、D“何时报警”均非主旨。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第②段

【解析】
- 第②段处理小伤口的步骤为：“First, wash your hands”（首先洗手）。
- A 适用于严重伤情。C 适用于扭伤。D 与“不要移动可能骨折的人”相关，非小伤口步骤。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：C**

【题型】细节判断题

【定位】第③段

【解析】
- 第③段明确写：“Do not lean back”（不要向后仰）。
- A、B、D 均为正确做法。C 为应避免的做法。故答案为 C。

**4. 答案：A**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第④段及表格

【解析】
- 第④段和表格均写明：RICE 代表 Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation。
- B、C、D 中的词语与原文不符。故答案为 A。

**5. 答案：C**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第⑤段

【解析】
- 第⑤段写：“If someone is badly hurt or you are not sure what to do, call 120”（若伤势严重或不知如何处置，立即拨打 120）。
- A、B 为可自行处理的小问题。D 过于狭隘，文中还包括“严重受伤”或“不确定”的情况。故答案为 C。
