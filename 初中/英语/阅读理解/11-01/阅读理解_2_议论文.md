# 阅读理解（议论文）

**How Ads Affect Teenagers**

① We see ads everywhere: on TV, on our phones, in magazines, and on the streets. For teenagers, these ads can have a strong influence. Companies know that young people like to buy things their friends have. So they design ads to attract teenagers. But is this good or bad for us?

② On one hand, ads can be useful. They tell us about new products and help us compare different choices. Good ads can also spread positive messages. For example, some ads encourage us to exercise or eat healthily. Ads can make us aware of important issues like protecting the environment. In this way, ads can teach us something useful.

③ On the other hand, ads can cause problems for teenagers. Many ads make us want things we do not really need. We may feel unhappy if we cannot afford the latest phone or the coolest shoes. Ads often show "perfect" lives — beautiful people, nice houses, and exciting trips. This can make us feel that our own life is not good enough. Some teens spend too much money trying to keep up with what ads show. They may also develop poor eating habits if they see too many ads for fast food and sweets.

④ So what can we do? First, we should learn to think critically. Ask yourself: "Do I really need this, or does the ad just make me want it?" Second, talk to your parents or teachers when you feel pressured by ads. Third, limit the time you spend watching ads. The less we see them, the less they influence us.

⑤ Ads are a part of modern life. We cannot avoid them completely. But we can choose how we react to them. By being aware of how ads work, we can make smarter choices and stay true to ourselves.

**注释：** critically 批判性地；afford 买得起

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ①, why do companies design ads to attract teenagers?
   A. Because teenagers never buy anything.
   B. Because young people like to buy things their friends have.
   C. Because ads are free.
   D. Because teenagers do not watch TV.

2. Which is a positive effect of ads mentioned in Paragraph ②?
   A. Ads make us spend more money.
   B. Some ads encourage exercise or healthy eating.
   C. Ads always show perfect lives.
   D. Ads never teach us anything.

3. What problem can ads cause for teenagers according to Paragraph ③?
   A. Teenagers stop watching TV.
   B. Teens may want things they do not need and feel unhappy if they cannot afford them.
   C. Ads make everyone rich.
   D. Teenagers never eat fast food.

4. What does the writer suggest teenagers do in Paragraph ④?
   A. Never watch any ads.
   B. Ask "Do I really need this?" and talk to parents or teachers when feeling pressured.
   C. Buy everything they see in ads.
   D. Avoid talking to parents.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell us that all ads are bad.
   B. To help teenagers understand ads and make smarter choices.
   C. To encourage teenagers to buy more things.
   D. To ban ads for teenagers.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "Companies know that young people like to buy things their friends have. So they design ads to attract teenagers"
**【解析】** 公司知道年轻人喜欢买朋友有的东西，因此设计面向青少年的广告。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Good ads can also spread positive messages... some ads encourage us to exercise or eat healthily"
**【解析】** 部分广告传播积极信息，鼓励运动或健康饮食。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Many ads make us want things we do not really need... We may feel unhappy if we cannot afford the latest phone"
**【解析】** 广告会让人想要并不真正需要的东西，买不起时可能感到不快乐。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Ask yourself: 'Do I really need this...?' Second, talk to your parents or teachers when you feel pressured by ads"
**【解析】** 作者建议自问是否需要，并在感到压力时与父母或老师交流。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 写作意图
**【定位】** 全文，尤其第④⑤段
**【解析】** 文章介绍广告对青少年的影响，提出应对建议，旨在帮助青少年理解广告并做出更明智选择。B项正确。A、C、D项与文章目的不符。故选B。
