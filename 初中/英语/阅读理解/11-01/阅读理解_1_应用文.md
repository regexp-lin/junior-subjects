> **11-01 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Autumn Running Competition — Registration Notice**

① Dear students and parents,

② Our school will hold the Autumn Running Competition on November 15th. This event is open to all students in Grades 7–9. The race will start at 9:00 a.m. on the school playground. Students who wish to join must sign up before November 8th.

③ The competition has two categories: the 1,000-metre race for boys and the 800-metre race for girls. Top three runners in each group will receive certificates and small prizes. All participants will get a souvenir badge.

④ To register, please fill in the form and hand it to your PE teacher, or sign up online on the school website before 5:00 p.m. on November 8th. Students should wear sportswear and sports shoes on the day of the race.

⑤ For any questions, please contact Mr. Zhang at the PE office or call 0755-12345678. We hope to see many of you on the track!

School PE Department  
November 1st, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Autumn Running Competition be held?
   A. November 1st.
   B. November 8th.
   C. November 15th.
   D. November 20th.

2. What is the race distance for girls?
   A. 1,000 metres.
   B. 800 metres.
   C. 1,500 metres.
   D. 2,000 metres.

3. What will the top three runners receive?
   A. Only a badge.
   B. Certificates and small prizes.
   C. Money.
   D. A new pair of shoes.

4. How can students register for the competition?
   A. Only by handing in a form to the PE teacher.
   B. Only online.
   C. By filling in a form for the PE teacher or signing up online.
   D. By calling Mr. Zhang.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. The competition is only for Grade 9 students.
   B. All students in Grades 7–9 can take part if they register in time.
   C. Registration ends on the day of the race.
   D. Participants do not need to wear sportswear.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold the Autumn Running Competition on November 15th"
**【解析】** 比赛于11月15日举行。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "the 800-metre race for girls"
**【解析】** 女生参加800米跑。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Top three runners in each group will receive certificates and small prizes"
**【解析】** 每组前三名获得证书和小奖品。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "fill in the form and hand it to your PE teacher, or sign up online on the school website"
**【解析】** 可将表格交给体育老师或在学校网站网上报名。C项正确。A、B、D项表述不全或与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "open to all students in Grades 7–9"；第④段 "before November 8th"
**【解析】** 七至九年级均可参加，需在11月8日前报名。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。
