> **06-05 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① It was the school sports day. Zhang Ming stood at the starting line for the 800-meter race. He had practiced for weeks and was ready to give his best. When the gun went off, he ran forward with the other runners. In the first lap, he kept a steady pace and stayed in the middle of the group.

② Halfway through the second lap, something went wrong. Zhang Ming's foot hit a small stone on the track. He lost his balance and fell heavily onto the ground. His knee hurt badly and his hands were scratched. For a moment, he lay there, feeling shocked and embarrassed. Some runners passed him. A teacher rushed over and asked if he wanted to stop. "Can you continue?" she asked worriedly.

③ Zhang Ming looked at the track ahead. Many runners had already crossed the finish line. His knee was bleeding, but he stood up slowly. "I will finish the race," he said to the teacher. He began to run again, but each step was painful. Some students in the stands started to clap and cheer for him. "Keep going, Zhang Ming! You can do it!" their voices encouraged him.

④ Step by step, Zhang Ming kept moving forward. He was the last one on the track, but he did not give up. When he finally crossed the finish line, the whole crowd stood up and cheered. His classmates ran to him and helped him sit down. The PE teacher patted his shoulder and said, "Zhang Ming, you are a true athlete. Finishing the race matters more than winning."

⑤ Zhang Ming will never forget that day. He learned that sometimes life knocks you down, but what matters is whether you choose to stand up again. The applause (掌声) he received was not for being the fastest, but for having the courage to finish what he had started.

**注释：** lap 圈；scratch 划伤；applause 掌声

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what did Zhang Ming do before the race?
   A. He had practiced for weeks and was ready to do his best.
   B. He hurt his knee during practice.
   C. He had never run 800 meters before.
   D. He was not confident and wanted to quit.

2. According to paragraph ②, what happened to Zhang Ming during the race?
   A. He ran too fast and got tired.
   B. He argued with another runner.
   C. His foot hit a stone, he lost balance, fell, and hurt his knee and hands.
   D. The teacher asked him to stop running.

3. According to paragraph ③, what did Zhang Ming decide to do after he fell?
   A. He went to see a doctor immediately.
   B. He stood up and continued running to finish the race.
   C. He decided to quit and sit on the side.
   D. He waited for someone to carry him to the finish line.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about the crowd's reaction?
   A. They respected his determination and cheered for his courage to finish.
   B. They felt sorry for Zhang Ming because he lost.
   C. They were angry that he ran so slowly.
   D. They thought he was foolish to continue.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Winning a race is the most important thing.
   B. Students should never run in sports events.
   C. Teachers should stop injured students from continuing.
   D. Life may knock you down, but having the courage to stand up and finish what you started matters more.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He had practiced for weeks and was ready to give his best."

**【解析】** 原文明确写张明练习了好几周、准备全力以赴，A 项正确。B 项"练习时伤了膝盖"、C 项"从未跑过 800 米"和 D 项"不自信想放弃"均未在文中提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Zhang Ming's foot hit a small stone on the track. He lost his balance and fell heavily onto the ground. His knee hurt badly and his hands were scratched."

**【解析】** 原文写出脚踩到石头、失去平衡、重重摔倒、膝盖受伤、手被划伤，C 项正确。A 项"跑太快累了"、B 项"与别人争吵"和 D 项"老师让他停跑"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "He stood up slowly. 'I will finish the race,' he said to the teacher. He began to run again."

**【解析】** 原文写明张明站起来、表示要完成比赛、再次开跑，B 项正确。A 项"立刻去看医生"、C 项"放弃坐在一旁"和 D 项"等人抬到终点"均与原文矛盾。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "the whole crowd stood up and cheered... The PE teacher patted his shoulder and said, 'Zhang Ming, you are a true athlete. Finishing the race matters more than winning.'" 及第⑤段 "The applause he received was not for being the fastest, but for having the courage to finish."

**【解析】** 观众起立欢呼、体育老师赞扬其完成比赛比获胜更重要、掌声是为勇气而给，可推知大家敬重他的坚持和完成比赛的勇气，A 项正确。B 项"因他输了而难过"、C 项"对他跑得慢生气"和 D 项"认为他愚蠢"均与原文情感基调不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文及第⑤段 "He learned that sometimes life knocks you down, but what matters is whether you choose to stand up again... having the courage to finish what he had started."

**【解析】** 文章通过张明摔跤后坚持完赛的故事，传达"人生会把你击倒，但重要的是选择再站起来、有勇气完成已经开始的事"，D 项正确。A 项"获胜最重要"与老师的话矛盾；B 项"学生不应参加跑步"、C 项"老师应阻止受伤学生"均非作者意图。故选 D。
