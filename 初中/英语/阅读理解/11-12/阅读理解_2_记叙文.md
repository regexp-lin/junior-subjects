> **11-12 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Time Going to the Hospital Alone**

① Last month, I woke up with a bad sore throat and fever. My parents had already left for work. I felt too sick to go to school. I had to see a doctor, but I had never been to a hospital alone before. I was nervous. What if I got lost? What if I did not know what to say?

② I took a deep breath and looked up the nearest hospital online. I wrote down the address and the bus route. I packed my ID card and health insurance card. Then I set off. On the bus, my hands were shaking. I kept telling myself, "You can do this."

③ When I arrived at the hospital, I went to the registration desk. A nurse asked me, "What's wrong?" I said, "Sore throat and fever." She gave me a form to fill in and told me to go to the waiting area. I waited for about twenty minutes. When my number was called, I went into the doctor's room. The doctor was kind. She checked my throat, took my temperature, and said I had a cold. She gave me a prescription and told me to rest and drink more water.

④ I paid at the cashier and got my medicine from the pharmacy. On the way home, I felt proud. I had done it. I had gone to the hospital alone and everything was fine.

⑤ That day taught me something important. We are often more capable than we think. Facing our fears is the first step to growing up. Now I know I can handle more things on my own.

**注释：** prescription 处方；pharmacy 药房

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer need to go to the hospital?
   A. To visit a friend.
   B. He had a sore throat and fever.
   C. His parents asked him to.
   D. To do homework.

2. How did the writer feel before going?
   A. Excited and happy.
   B. Nervous and unsure.
   C. Very confident.
   D. Bored.

3. What did the writer do to prepare?
   A. He called his parents.
   B. He looked up the hospital online, wrote down the address and bus route, and packed his ID and insurance card.
   C. He did nothing.
   D. He asked a friend to go with him.

4. What did the doctor tell the writer?
   A. He needed an operation.
   B. He had a cold; rest and drink more water.
   C. He was very healthy.
   D. He should never go out again.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer will never go to the hospital again.
   B. Facing fears helps us grow and discover we are more capable than we think.
   C. Going to the hospital is always easy.
   D. The writer will never be sick again.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "I woke up with a bad sore throat and fever"
**【解析】** 作者因咽喉痛和发烧需就医。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "I was nervous"；第②段 "my hands were shaking"
**【解析】** 作者出发前紧张、不确定，车上手在抖。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "I looked up the nearest hospital online. I wrote down the address and the bus route. I packed my ID card and health insurance card"
**【解析】** 作者查医院、记地址和公交路线、带好证件。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "She said I had a cold. She gave me a prescription and told me to rest and drink more water"
**【解析】** 医生诊断为感冒，嘱咐休息、多喝水。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "We are often more capable than we think. Facing our fears is the first step to growing up"
**【解析】** 面对恐惧有助于成长，我们会发现自己比想象中更有能力。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
