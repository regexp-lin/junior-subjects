> **11-05 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Creative Market — Stall Registration Notice**

① Dear students,

② Our school will hold a Creative Market on the playground on November 22nd. We are now inviting students to apply for stalls. If you have handmade crafts, second-hand books, artwork, or other items to sell, this is your chance! The market will run from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.

③ Each stall costs 20 yuan. The money will be used for school charity. You can apply alone or with a group (up to 4 people per stall). Please prepare your own table and chair. The school will provide a space of about 2 metres for each stall.

④ To apply, fill in the form with your name, class, and a short description of what you plan to sell. Hand the form to the Student Union office before November 15th. We have 50 stalls in total. Places will be given on a first-come, first-served basis.

⑤ For questions, contact Li Ming from Class 9A or visit the Student Union office at break time. We look forward to your creative ideas!

Student Union  
November 5th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Creative Market be held?
   A. November 5th.
   B. November 15th.
   C. November 22nd.
   D. December 1st.

2. What can students sell at the market?
   A. Only books.
   B. Handmade crafts, second-hand books, artwork, or other items.
   C. Nothing — it is just for looking.
   D. Only food.

3. How much does each stall cost?
   A. 10 yuan.
   B. 20 yuan.
   C. 50 yuan.
   D. It is free.

4. When is the deadline for applying?
   A. November 5th.
   B. November 15th.
   C. November 22nd.
   D. November 25th.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Students must apply in groups of 4.
   B. Students need to apply in time and bring their own table and chair.
   C. The market is only for teachers.
   D. There are unlimited stalls.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold a Creative Market on the playground on November 22nd"
**【解析】** 创意集市于11月22日举行。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "handmade crafts, second-hand books, artwork, or other items to sell"
**【解析】** 可售卖手工艺品、二手书、艺术品等。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Each stall costs 20 yuan"
**【解析】** 每个摊位收费20元。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Hand the form to the Student Union office before November 15th"
**【解析】** 申请须在11月15日前交表。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第③段 "Please prepare your own table and chair"；第④段 "before November 15th... first-come, first-served"
**【解析】** 需自备桌椅，且名额有限、先到先得，故需及时申请。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。
