# 阅读理解（应用文）

**English Corner — Join Us Every Friday!**

① Dear students,

② The English Department is excited to announce our weekly English Corner! Come and practise your English in a fun, relaxed environment. Everyone is welcome — beginners and advanced speakers alike.

**Details:**

| Item | Information |
|------|------------|
| Time | Every Friday, 4:30–5:30 p.m. |
| Place | School Garden (Room 102 if it rains) |
| Who can join | All students in Grades 7–9 |
| Teacher in charge | Ms. Chen & Mr. Brown (foreign teacher) |

③ **What we do:**

- **Week 1 & 3:** Free Talk — Choose a topic and chat with others in small groups.
- **Week 2:** English Games — Word puzzles, role play, tongue twisters, and more.
- **Week 4:** Movie Clip Discussion — Watch a short clip and share your thoughts.
- **Special events:** Guest speakers, English song contests, and holiday parties (announced in advance).

④ **Why you should join:**

- Improve your spoken English in a no-pressure setting.
- Make new friends from different classes.
- Get a participation stamp each time. Collect 10 stamps and receive a small prize from the English Department.

⑤ No sign-up needed. Just show up! Bring your smile and your courage. Remember: making mistakes is part of learning.

For questions, find Ms. Chen in Office 308 or email englishcorner@school.edu.cn.

English Department  
November 20, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. How often is the English Corner held?
   A. Every day.
   B. Every Monday.
   C. Every Friday.
   D. Twice a month.

2. Where does the English Corner take place when it rains?
   A. In the School Garden.
   B. In Room 102.
   C. In the library.
   D. In the gym.

3. What activity is planned for Week 2?
   A. Free Talk.
   B. English Games.
   C. Movie Clip Discussion.
   D. English song contest.

4. How many stamps do students need to collect to get a prize?
   A. 5.
   B. 8.
   C. 10.
   D. 15.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Students must pass a test to join.
   B. Only Grade 9 students can participate.
   C. The English Corner creates a friendly atmosphere for practising English.
   D. Students must sign up one week before.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Time" 一行 "Every Friday, 4:30–5:30 p.m."

**【解析】** 表格明确标注每周五举行。A项每天、B项每周一均不正确；D项每月两次也不对，是每周一次。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Place" 一行 "School Garden (Room 102 if it rains)"

**【解析】** 原文括号内说明下雨时在Room 102举行。A项学校花园是晴天的地点；C项图书馆和D项体育馆原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Week 2: English Games — Word puzzles, role play, tongue twisters, and more."

**【解析】** 第二周的活动是英语游戏。A项Free Talk是第一和第三周的活动；C项Movie Clip Discussion是第四周的活动；D项英语歌曲比赛是特别活动，不是固定周次安排。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Collect 10 stamps and receive a small prize from the English Department."

**【解析】** 原文说集满10个印章可以获得小奖品。A项5个、B项8个、D项15个均与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "Come and practise your English in a fun, relaxed environment." 第④段 "Improve your spoken English in a no-pressure setting." 第⑤段 "Bring your smile and your courage. Remember: making mistakes is part of learning."

**【解析】** 从"轻松有趣的环境""无压力""犯错是学习的一部分"等表述可以推断英语角营造了一个友好的英语练习氛围。A项原文说"No sign-up needed"，不需要考试；B项所有7-9年级学生都可参加；D项不需要提前报名。故选C。
