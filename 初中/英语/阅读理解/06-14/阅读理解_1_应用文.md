# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Tech Exhibition 2025 — Visitor Guide**

② Welcome to the City Science Museum's annual Tech Exhibition! This year we show the latest inventions in robotics (机器人), AI, and green energy. The exhibition opens from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm from June 10 to June 20. Tickets cost ¥30 for adults and ¥15 for students. Children under 1.2 meters can enter for free.

③ **Getting there:** The museum is at No. 88 Science Road. You can take Bus No. 15 or the Metro Line 3 and get off at Science Park Station. There is a parking lot for visitors who drive. It costs ¥10 per hour.

④ **What to see:** On the first floor, you will find robot shows every hour. On the second floor, there are AI games you can try. The third floor has a green energy display — you can learn how solar panels work. Remember to bring your student card if you are a student; you need it for the cheaper ticket.

⑤ **Visitor tips:** Please do not touch the exhibits (展品) unless there is a sign that says "Try it". Photography is allowed in most areas, but no flash. There is a café on the second floor. You can also bring your own water bottle — we have free water stations. Enjoy your visit!

**注释：** robotics 机器人技术；exhibit 展品；solar panel 太阳能板

### 选择题

1. When is the Tech Exhibition open?
   A. From 9:00 am to 6:00 pm, June 10 to June 20.
   B. Every day from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.
   C. Only on weekends in June.
   D. From June 1 to June 15.

2. How much does a student need to pay for a ticket?
   A. ¥30.
   B. ¥15.
   C. ¥10.
   D. Free.

3. According to the passage, how can you get to the museum?
   A. Only by bus.
   B. Only by metro.
   C. By Bus No. 15, Metro Line 3, or by car (with parking).
   D. Only by walking.

4. What can visitors do on the third floor?
   A. Watch robot shows.
   B. Try AI games.
   C. Learn how solar panels work.
   D. Buy food at the café.

5. What can we learn from the passage?
   A. Visitors should not touch any exhibits.
   B. Visitors can bring water bottles and use free water stations.
   C. Photography is not allowed in the museum.
   D. The café is on the first floor.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The exhibition opens from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm from June 10 to June 20."

**【解析】** 文章明确说明展览时间为每天 9:00 am 至 6:00 pm，展期为 6 月 10 日至 6 月 20 日。A 项正确。B 项时间错误；C 项 "仅周末" 未提及；D 项日期错误。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Tickets cost ¥30 for adults and ¥15 for students."

**【解析】** 学生票价为 ¥15。B 项正确。A 项 ¥30 为成人价；C 项 ¥10 为每小时停车费；D 项免费针对 1.2 米以下儿童。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "You can take Bus No. 15 or the Metro Line 3... There is a parking lot for visitors who drive."

**【解析】** 出行方式包括：15 路公交、地铁 3 号线、自驾（有停车场）。C 项正确。A、B、D 项均片面。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The third floor has a green energy display — you can learn how solar panels work."

**【解析】** 三楼为绿色能源展示区，可了解太阳能板工作原理。C 项正确。A 项一楼机器人；B 项二楼 AI 游戏；D 项咖啡馆在二楼。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "You can also bring your own water bottle — we have free water stations."

**【解析】** 可自带水瓶，馆内有免费饮水站。B 项正确。A 项有 "Try it" 标志的可触摸；C 项大部分区域允许拍照，仅禁闪光；D 项咖啡馆在二楼。故选 B。
