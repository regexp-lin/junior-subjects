# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Will printed newspapers disappear? Many people ask this question as more and more readers turn to smartphones and computers for news. Some believe that paper newspapers will soon be a thing of the past. Others think they will stay. Let us look at both sides.

② **Why printed newspapers are declining:** The number of people who read paper newspapers has dropped a lot in recent years. Most young people get news from the Internet. Online news is free, fast, and easy to share. It also updates all the time. Why buy a paper that is printed once a day when you can read the latest news on your phone anytime? Many newspapers have closed because they could not make enough money. Advertisers prefer to put their ads online where more people can see them.

③ **Why some people still prefer paper newspapers:** However, printed newspapers still have their fans. Older readers often find paper easier to read than a screen. They like the habit of reading the newspaper with a cup of tea in the morning. Paper newspapers do not need WiFi or electricity. You can read them on a train or in a park without worrying about battery life. Some people also trust paper more — they feel that online news can be changed too easily. Paper feels more serious and reliable.

④ **What might happen in the future:** Experts say printed newspapers will probably not disappear completely, but they will become less common. They may become something special — like a weekend edition that people buy for deeper reading. Smaller local newspapers might close, but big national newspapers may survive by offering both paper and online versions. The key is to change with the times.

⑤ In the end, we cannot be sure what will happen. What we know is that the way people get news has changed, and it will keep changing. Whether paper newspapers stay or go, good journalism (新闻工作) will always be important.

**注释：** decline 减少；reliable 可靠的；journalism 新闻工作

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, why are printed newspapers declining?
   A. Because they are too cheap.
   B. Because young people prefer online news — it is free, fast, and updates all the time.
   C. Because paper is bad for the environment.
   D. Because newspapers are too thick.

2. Why do some older readers still prefer paper newspapers?
   A. Because paper is cheaper than a phone.
   B. Because they find paper easier to read and like the morning reading habit.
   C. Because they cannot use smartphones.
   D. Because paper has more news.

3. What do experts think about the future of printed newspapers?
   A. They will disappear completely soon.
   B. They will become more popular than online news.
   C. They will probably become less common but may survive in special forms.
   D. They will only be read by young people.

4. The underlined word "reliable" in paragraph ③ probably means ______.
   A. easy to change
   B. worth trusting
   C. very old
   D. free of charge

5. What is the writer's main idea?
   A. Paper newspapers will definitely disappear in five years.
   B. The way people get news has changed; good journalism matters no matter what form news takes.
   C. Online news is always better than paper newspapers.
   D. Only old people should read paper newspapers.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Most young people get news from the Internet. Online news is free, fast, and easy to share. It also updates all the time."

**【解析】** 纸质报纸衰落的主要原因：年轻人转向网络新闻，因其免费、快速、易分享、实时更新。B 项正确。A 项 "太便宜" 与 decline 逻辑不符；C 项环保未提及；D 项 "太厚" 未提及。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Older readers often find paper easier to read than a screen. They like the habit of reading the newspaper with a cup of tea in the morning."

**【解析】** 年长读者偏好纸质报的原因：纸质更易阅读、喜爱晨读习惯。B 项正确。A 项价格未比较；C 项 "不会用手机" 过度推断；D 项 "内容更多" 未提及。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Experts say printed newspapers will probably not disappear completely, but they will become less common. They may become something special — like a weekend edition."

**【解析】** 专家认为纸质报不会完全消失，但会更少见，可能以特殊形式存在（如周末版）。C 项正确。A 项 "完全消失" 与原文矛盾；B 项 "更流行" 不符；D 项 "仅年轻人读" 与年长读者偏好矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "Paper feels more serious and reliable."

**【解析】** reliable 与 "serious" 并列，且与前文 "online news can be changed too easily" 形成对比，意为 "可信的、可靠的"。B 项 worth trusting 符合。A 项 "易改变" 为对立义；C 项 "很老" 无关；D 项 "免费" 无关。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The way people get news has changed, and it will keep changing. Whether paper newspapers stay or go, good journalism will always be important."

**【解析】** 作者总结：获取新闻的方式在变且会持续变化；无论纸质报存亡，优质新闻工作始终重要。B 项准确概括主旨。A 项 "五年内必消失" 过于绝对；C 项 "网络新闻总更好" 非作者观点；D 项 "仅老人应读纸质" 片面。故选 B。
