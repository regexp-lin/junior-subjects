# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **How to Write a Good Message in Your Class Yearbook**

② Graduation is coming soon. Many students will write messages in their class yearbook (同学录) or graduation album (纪念册). Do you know how to make your message special and meaningful?

③ **Be sincere.** Write from your heart. Tell your classmates what they mean to you. Instead of just saying "Good luck," you can add something personal. For example: "I will never forget the day we worked together on the science project. Thank you for your help."

④ **Keep it short but warm.** You do not need to write a long letter. Two or three sentences are enough. Use simple, friendly words that show your true feelings. You can also share a memory (回忆) that you both enjoyed.

⑤ **Add your best wishes.** Wish your friends success in their future studies and life. Leave your contact (联系方式) so you can keep in touch. Sign your name at the end. Your classmates will treasure (珍惜) these words for years.

**注释：** yearbook 同学录；memory 回忆；contact 联系方式；treasure 珍惜

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "How to Write a Good Message in Your Class Yearbook" 及全文内容

**【解析】** 文章通篇围绕如何在同学录/毕业纪念册中写好留言展开，提供写作建议。A项准确概括主旨。B项如何选择、C项何时送、D项何处购买均非文章重点。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Be sincere. Write from your heart... Instead of just saying 'Good luck,' you can add something personal."

**【解析】** 文中强调要真诚、发自内心书写，并加入个性化的内容。B项正确。A项从网上抄袭、C项写很长、D项用难词均与原文建议不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Two or three sentences are enough."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明两到三句话即可，不必写得很长。D项正确。A项尽可能长、B项仅一句、C项至少一页均与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Add your best wishes... Leave your contact (联系方式) so you can keep in touch. Sign your name at the end."

**【解析】** 文末建议写上美好祝愿、留下联系方式以保持联系，并签名。C项正确。A项仅照片、B项不写其他、D项写笑话均不完整或错误。故选C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Your classmates will treasure (珍惜) these words for years."

**【解析】** 文中指出同学会多年珍惜这些留言，可推知真诚温暖的留言会被同学们长期重视。A项正确。B项不重要与原文矛盾；C、D项未提及。故选A。
