# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Does Too Much Homework Hurt Students' Creativity?**

② Many parents and teachers believe that homework helps students learn. However, some people worry that too much homework may affect (影响) students' creativity. What is the truth?

③ **Those who support (支持) less homework** say that students need free time to think and explore. When children spend hours every night doing homework, they have little time left for hobbies, reading for fun, or just playing. Creativity often comes when the mind is relaxed, not when it is tired from too much work. Some countries have already reduced homework and found that students become more curious (好奇的) and creative. They learn better when they have time to discover things by themselves.

④ **Those who support homework** argue that practice is important. Homework helps students remember what they learn in class. It also teaches them to be responsible (有责任心的) and manage their time. Without enough practice, some students may fall behind. The problem may not be homework itself, but how much and what kind of homework. Good homework should be meaningful, not just repetitive (重复的) exercises. It can encourage (鼓励) students to think, not just copy answers.

⑤ **The conclusion:** Balance is the key. Students need both practice and free time. Too much homework may hurt creativity and cause stress. Too little may lead to weak basic skills. Schools and parents should work together to find the right amount. Let homework be useful, not a burden (负担).

**注释：** affect 影响；curious 好奇的；responsible 有责任心的；repetitive 重复的；encourage 鼓励；burden 负担

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, why do some people think less homework is better?
   A. Because homework is too expensive.
   B. Because teachers do not like to mark homework.
   C. Because parents want to do homework themselves.
   D. Because students need free time to explore and creativity often comes when the mind is relaxed.

2. What have some countries found after reducing homework?
   A. Students become more curious and creative.
   B. Students get more homework from other subjects.
   C. Students stop going to school.
   D. Parents become more worried.

3. What do supporters of homework believe?
   A. Practice helps students remember and teaches responsibility; the problem may be the amount and type of homework.
   B. Homework has no use at all.
   C. Students should never do any homework.
   D. Only smart students need homework.

4. What does the writer suggest at the end?
   A. Schools should give no homework at all.
   B. Parents should do all the homework.
   C. Balance is important; find the right amount of meaningful homework.
   D. Homework should be as long as possible.

5. What does the word "burden" in the last paragraph probably mean?
   A. Something heavy that causes worry or difficulty.
   B. Something helpful.
   C. A type of game.
   D. A kind of reward.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students need free time to think and explore... Creativity often comes when the mind is relaxed, not when it is tired from too much work."

**【解析】** 文中指出支持减少作业的人认为学生需要自由时间去思考和探索，创造力常在思维放松时产生而非疲惫时。D项正确。A项费用、B项教师不愿批改、C项家长自己做均未提及。故选D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some countries have already reduced homework and found that students become more curious (好奇的) and creative."

**【解析】** 文中明确写出部分国家减少作业后发现学生变得更好奇、更有创造力。A项正确。B、C、D项均与原文不符。故选A。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Homework helps students remember what they learn in class... The problem may not be homework itself, but how much and what kind of homework."

**【解析】** 支持作业的一方认为作业帮助记忆、培养责任感，问题可能在于作业量和类型而非作业本身。A项准确概括。B项完全无用、C项不应有任何作业、D项仅聪明学生需要均与原文矛盾。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Balance is the key... Schools and parents should work together to find the right amount. Let homework be useful, not a burden."

**【解析】** 作者总结平衡是关键，应找到合适的作业量，让作业有用而非负担。C项正确。A项完全取消、B项家长做、D项尽可能多均与作者建议相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Let homework be useful, not a burden (负担)."

**【解析】** 文中括号已给出"负担"的释义。burden 指造成忧虑或困难的重负。A项"something heavy that causes worry or difficulty"与释义相符。B项有帮助的、C项游戏、D项奖励均不符。故选A。
