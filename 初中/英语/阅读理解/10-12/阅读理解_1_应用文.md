> **10-12 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Weekend Cinema Special — Student Discount**

① Dear students,

② City Cinema is offering a weekend special for students! From October 12th to October 14th, bring your student ID and enjoy half-price tickets for all films.

**What you need to know:**

- **Dates:** Friday Oct 12 – Sunday Oct 14
- **Discount:** 50% off the normal ticket price (e.g. a 60-yuan ticket becomes 30 yuan)
- **How to get it:** Show your student ID at the ticket counter when you buy your ticket
- **Films included:** All films showing during these three days, including both Chinese and English films

③ The cinema opens at 10:00 a.m. and closes at 10:00 p.m. You can book tickets online or buy them at the cinema. For online booking, choose the "Student Discount" option and bring your student ID when you collect your ticket.

④ This offer is only for full-time students. One discount per person per film. The offer cannot be used together with other promotions.

⑤ For more information, visit www.citycinema.com or call 020-12345678. Enjoy the films!

City Cinema  
October 10th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. How long does the weekend cinema special last?
   A. One day.
   B. Two days.
   C. Three days.
   D. One week.

2. How much does a student pay for a ticket that normally costs 60 yuan?
   A. 60 yuan.
   B. 50 yuan.
   C. 30 yuan.
   D. 15 yuan.

3. What must students show to get the discount?
   A. A photo.
   B. Their student ID.
   C. A parent's ID.
   D. Nothing.

4. What does the notice say about online booking?
   A. Online booking is not allowed.
   B. Students must choose "Student Discount" online and show student ID when collecting the ticket.
   C. Students can only buy tickets at the cinema.
   D. The discount does not work for online bookings.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. Only primary school students can get the discount.
   B. The discount can be used with other promotions.
   C. Students can enjoy half-price for all films during the three-day period.
   D. The cinema is open 24 hours.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "From October 12th to October 14th" 及 "**Dates:** Friday Oct 12 – Sunday Oct 14"
**【解析】** 活动从10月12日至14日，共三天。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "50% off the normal ticket price (e.g. a 60-yuan ticket becomes 30 yuan)"
**【解析】** 半价优惠，60元票变为30元。C项正确。A、B、D项计算错误。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Show your student ID at the ticket counter when you buy your ticket"
**【解析】** 购票时需出示学生证。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "For online booking, choose the 'Student Discount' option and bring your student ID when you collect your ticket."
**【解析】** 网上购票需选择"学生优惠"，取票时出示学生证。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "All films showing during these three days"；第④段 "This offer is only for full-time students... cannot be used together with other promotions"
**【解析】** 三天内所有影片均可享学生半价，但不可与其他促销叠加。C项正确。A项"仅小学生"与"full-time students"矛盾；B项与"cannot be used together"矛盾；D项与"10:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m."矛盾。故选C。
