# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Is Afternoon Nap Necessary for Students?**

① In many Chinese schools, students have a nap time after lunch. They rest for 20 to 30 minutes before afternoon classes. But in some countries, schoolchildren do not nap at school. They go straight from lunch to the next lesson. So is an afternoon nap really necessary for students? People have different opinions.

② Those who support napping say it helps students in many ways. First, young people need more sleep than adults. Many students stay up late to do homework or use their phones. A short nap can make up for lost sleep. Second, a nap improves memory and attention. After a rest, students can focus better in afternoon classes. Third, napping reduces stress. Students face a lot of pressure from exams and homework. A quiet break in the middle of the day helps them relax.

③ However, some people disagree. They say that napping can make students feel drowsy when they wake up. It may take time to "wake up" fully, so the first class after nap might not be effective. Also, if students nap for too long, they may have trouble falling asleep at night. Besides, not every student likes to nap. Some prefer to read or do quiet activities during break time.

④ In my view, a short nap of 20 to 30 minutes can be helpful for most students, as long as it does not replace good night sleep. The key is balance. Students should try to get enough sleep at night first. If they still feel tired at noon, a short nap is a good idea. But if napping makes them feel worse in the afternoon, they can choose other ways to rest, such as walking or closing their eyes for a few minutes without sleeping.

⑤ So, there is no one answer for everyone. Schools that offer nap time give students a choice. What matters is that students find what works for them and stay healthy and energetic for learning.

**注释：** drowsy 困倦的；energetic 精力充沛的

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, which is NOT a reason to support napping?
   A. Students need more sleep than adults.
   B. Napping improves memory and attention.
   C. Napping reduces stress.
   D. Napping is cheaper than other activities.

2. Why do some people think napping is not good?
   A. Because it costs money.
   B. Because students may feel drowsy after waking up, and a long nap may affect night sleep.
   C. Because schools do not have enough space.
   D. Because parents do not allow it.

3. What does the writer suggest in Paragraph ④?
   A. All students must nap for at least one hour.
   B. A short nap of 20–30 minutes can help, but students should get enough night sleep first.
   C. Students should never nap.
   D. Napping is the only way to rest.

4. What does "balance" mean in Paragraph ④?
   A. Standing on one foot.
   B. Finding the right way between napping and night sleep, not relying only on nap.
   C. Napping as long as possible.
   D. Not sleeping at all.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Every student must nap.
   B. There is no single best answer; students should find what works for them.
   C. Schools should cancel nap time.
   D. Napping is harmful to health.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】D**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 支持午睡的理由：need more sleep, improves memory and attention, reduces stress
**【解析】** A、B、C均为支持午睡的理由。D项"午睡更省钱"原文未提及。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "napping can make students feel drowsy when they wake up... if students nap for too long, they may have trouble falling asleep at night"
**【解析】** 反对者认为午睡后可能困倦，午睡过长可能影响夜间睡眠。B项准确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "a short nap of 20 to 30 minutes can be helpful for most students, as long as it does not replace good night sleep... Students should try to get enough sleep at night first."
**【解析】** 作者建议短期午睡有益，但前提是保证夜间睡眠充足。B项正确。A、C、D项与建议不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第④段 "The key is balance... get enough sleep at night first... a short nap is a good idea"
**【解析】** 上下文指在夜间睡眠与午睡之间找到合适做法，不能只靠午睡弥补。B项准确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "There is no one answer for everyone... What matters is that students find what works for them"
**【解析】** 结尾指出没有普适答案，关键是学生找到适合自己的方式。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
