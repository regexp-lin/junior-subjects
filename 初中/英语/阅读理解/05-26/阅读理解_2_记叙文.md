# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Every Saturday afternoon, ten-year-old Li Wei would visit his grandfather. Grandpa Li lived in a small house with a wooden table in the yard. Under the old tree, they would sit together. Grandpa would take out a chessboard and say with a smile, "Ready for another lesson, Wei Wei?"

② Li Wei did not know how to play Chinese chess at first. Grandpa taught him one piece at a time. "This is the general. He must stay in his palace. This is the chariot — it moves in straight lines." Grandpa's voice was patient and kind. When Li Wei made a wrong move, Grandpa never got angry. He would nod and say, "Think again. What would happen if you move here?" Little by little, Li Wei began to understand the game.

③ Sometimes Li Wei would lose many times in a row. He felt frustrated and wanted to give up. "I'm no good at this," he said. Grandpa put his hand on Li Wei's shoulder. "When I was your age, I lost to my grandfather every time. Losing teaches you more than winning. The important thing is that we are here, playing together." Those words made Li Wei feel warm inside. He picked up a piece and tried again.

④ As months passed, Li Wei grew better at chess. But he noticed something — Grandpa seemed to make more mistakes. Once, Grandpa moved his general into danger without thinking. Li Wei won that game. Later, his mother told him, "Your grandfather has been playing chess for fifty years. He did not make that mistake by accident." Li Wei understood. Grandpa was letting him win to encourage him. It was Grandpa's way of love.

⑤ Now Li Wei is a teenager. Grandpa is too old to play often. But when they meet, they still talk about those Saturday afternoons. "Those were the best times," Grandpa says. Li Wei agrees. It was never really about winning or losing. It was about sitting under the tree, learning from Grandpa, and feeling the warmth of family.

**注释：** chessboard 棋盘；chariot 车（象棋）；frustrated 沮丧的

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, where did Li Wei and his grandfather usually play chess?
   A. In a chess club.
   B. In the school playground.
   C. Under an old tree in Grandpa's yard.
   D. In Grandpa's living room.

2. How did Grandpa react when Li Wei made a wrong move, according to paragraph ②?
   A. He never got angry and asked Li Wei to think again.
   B. He got angry and stopped the game.
   C. He gave Li Wei the answer directly.
   D. He decided not to teach Li Wei anymore.

3. According to paragraph ③, what did Grandpa say when Li Wei wanted to give up?
   A. "You must win next time."
   B. "Chess is not for you."
   C. "Losing teaches you more than winning. The important thing is we are here, playing together."
   D. "Let's play something else."

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Grandpa's "mistake"?
   A. Grandpa made the mistake on purpose to let Li Wei win and feel encouraged.
   B. Grandpa was too old to play well.
   C. Grandpa did not know how to play chess.
   D. Grandpa was tired and not paying attention.

5. What is the writer's main purpose in writing this passage?
   A. To teach readers how to play Chinese chess.
   B. To compare Li Wei with other chess players.
   C. To introduce the history of Chinese chess.
   D. To show the warm love between grandfather and grandson through teaching chess.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Grandpa Li lived in a small house with a wooden table in the yard. Under the old tree, they would sit together."

**【解析】** 原文说明爷孙二人在爷爷院子里的老树下对弈，C 项正确。A 项"象棋俱乐部"、B 项"学校操场"和 D 项"爷爷的客厅"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When Li Wei made a wrong move, Grandpa never got angry. He would nod and say, 'Think again. What would happen if you move here?'"

**【解析】** 原文描述爷爷从不生气，会点头并让李伟再想一想，A 项正确。B 项"生气并停止"与"never got angry"矛盾；C 项"直接给答案"和 D 项"不再教"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Grandpa put his hand on Li Wei's shoulder. 'When I was your age, I lost to my grandfather every time. Losing teaches you more than winning. The important thing is that we are here, playing together.'"

**【解析】** 爷爷安慰李伟时说了"输比赢教会你更多""重要的是我们在这里一起下棋"等话，C 项正确。A 项"下次必须赢"、B 项"象棋不适合你"和 D 项"换个别的玩"均未在文中出现。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Grandpa seemed to make more mistakes... 'Your grandfather has been playing chess for fifty years. He did not make that mistake by accident.' Li Wei understood. Grandpa was letting him win to encourage him."

**【解析】** 母亲揭示爷爷有五十年棋龄，失误并非偶然；李伟领悟到爷爷是故意输棋以鼓励他，可推断爷爷有意为之，A 项正确。B 项"太老下不好"、C 项"不会下棋"和 D 项"累了没注意"均与母亲的话矛盾。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：爷爷耐心教孙子下棋、在孙子沮丧时给予鼓励、故意让棋表达关爱，以及结尾"从未真正关乎输赢，而是树下的陪伴与家的温暖"。

**【解析】** 文章通过爷爷教孙子下棋的温情片段，展现祖孙之间通过教学互动传递的亲情与关爱，D 项正确。A 项"教读者下象棋"、B 项"比较李伟与其他棋手"和 C 项"介绍象棋历史"均非主要意图。故选 D。
