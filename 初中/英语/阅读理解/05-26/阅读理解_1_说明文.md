# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Every year, millions of birds fly long distances from one place to another. This journey is called migration (迁徙). Some birds travel thousands of kilometers to find warm places in winter or to look for food. The Arctic tern (北极燕鸥) holds the record — it flies from the Arctic to Antarctica and back each year. That is like flying around the Earth twice!

② Why do birds migrate? The main reason is to find food and a safe place to live. When winter comes, many birds cannot find enough food in their home. They must fly to warmer places where insects, seeds, and fruits are still available. In spring, they fly back to have their young. These places have longer days and more food, which helps the baby birds grow strong.

③ How do birds know where to go? Scientists have studied this for many years. Some birds use the sun and stars to find their way. Others can sense the Earth's magnetic field (磁场). Young birds often follow older birds on their first migration. It seems that this ability is partly born in their brains and partly learned from experience.

④ Migration is not easy. Birds face many dangers during their journey. Strong winds and bad weather can make them tired. They may lose their way in cities full of bright lights. Some hunters catch them for food. Climate change also affects their migration. Warmer winters make some birds stay too long in the north, and they may run out of food before they leave.

⑤ Today, many countries work together to protect migrating birds. They set up safe places for birds to rest and eat along their routes. People are also asked to turn off lights at night during migration season to help birds not lose their way. By understanding the mystery of bird migration, we can do more to protect these amazing travelers.

**注释：** migration 迁徙；Arctic tern 北极燕鸥；magnetic field 磁场

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The Arctic tern (北极燕鸥) holds the record — it flies from the Arctic to Antarctica and back each year."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出北极燕鸥保持着迁徙距离的记录，B 项正确。A 项"麻雀"、C 项"乌鸦"和 D 项"燕子"均未提及为迁徙距离纪录保持者。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Why do birds migrate? The main reason is to find food and a safe place to live."

**【解析】** 原文说明鸟类迁徙的主要原因是寻找食物和安全的居住地，A 项正确。B 项"与其他鸟会面"、C 项"躲避猎人"和 D 项"教幼鸟飞行"均非主要原因。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some birds use the sun and stars to find their way. Others can sense the Earth's magnetic field (磁场)."

**【解析】** 原文列举了鸟类导航的多种方式：利用太阳和星星，或感知地球磁场，A 项正确。B 项"向其他动物求助"、C 项"使用地图和 GPS"和 D 项"跟随人类向导"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Migration is not easy. Birds face many dangers during their journey. Strong winds and bad weather... may lose their way in cities full of bright lights. Some hunters catch them... Climate change also affects their migration."

**【解析】** 文中列举了强风、恶劣天气、城市灯光迷失、猎人捕猎、气候变化等多种困难和危险，可推断鸟类在迁徙过程中面临诸多挑战，D 项正确。A 项"安全轻松"与"not easy"矛盾；B 项"气候变化无影响"与原文相反；C 项"城市灯光帮助认路"与"lose their way"矛盾。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段迁徙现象，第②段迁徙原因，第③段导航方式，第④段面临危险，第⑤段保护措施。

**【解析】** 全文介绍候鸟迁徙的奥秘（原因、方式、挑战），并以保护呼吁作结，C 项准确概括主旨。A 项"鼓励养鸟"、B 项"比较不同鸟类"和 D 项"解释如何捕捉"均非文章目的。故选 C。
