# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Elective Courses for the New Term**

② Welcome back! The new term will start on September 1. This year we offer several elective courses for students in Grade 7, 8 and 9. Each student can choose one or two courses. Please read the information below and choose wisely.

③ **Art & Design:** This course is for those who love drawing and making things. You will learn basic painting skills and try different materials like paper, cloth and clay. Lessons are on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons from 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. in Room 301.

④ **Science Club:** Do you enjoy doing experiments? Join our Science Club! We will explore topics about plants, simple machines and the weather. The club meets every Wednesday afternoon from 3:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m. in the science lab. A maximum of 25 students can join.

⑤ **Sports & Health:** We have basketball, badminton and table tennis. You can pick one sport or try all of them. Training is on Monday and Friday afternoons from 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. on the sports ground. All you need is a pair of sports shoes.

⑥ **How to Choose:** Go to the school website and fill in the form before August 25. Places are limited. If you have any questions, call the office at 020-87654321 or visit Room 101.

**注释：** elective 选修的；clay 黏土

---

### 选择题

1. Who can choose the elective courses?
   A. Students in Grade 7, 8 and 9.
   B. Only students in Grade 9.
   C. Teachers and parents.
   D. All students in primary school.

2. When does the Art & Design course take place?
   A. On Monday and Wednesday afternoons.
   B. On Tuesday and Thursday afternoons from 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.
   C. Every Friday morning.
   D. Only on Tuesday afternoon.

3. What is the maximum number of students for the Science Club?
   A. 20.
   B. 30.
   C. 25.
   D. 40.

4. What do students need to bring for the Sports & Health course?
   A. A basketball and badminton racket.
   B. Sports clothes and a water bottle.
   C. Special equipment for all three sports.
   D. A pair of sports shoes.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Students must choose all three elective courses.
   B. Students should register online before August 25 because places are limited.
   C. The Art & Design course is only for Grade 9 students.
   D. The Science Club has no limit on the number of students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "we offer several elective courses for students in Grade 7, 8 and 9"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明选修课面向七、八、九年级学生。A项正确。B项仅限九年级与原文不符；C项教师和家长未提及；D项小学生未纳入范围。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Lessons are on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons from 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 艺术与设计课程在周二、周四下午4:00至5:00。B项准确。A、C、D项时间或星期均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "A maximum of 25 students can join"

**【解析】** 科学俱乐部最多招收25名学生。C项正确。A、B、D项人数与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "All you need is a pair of sports shoes"

**【解析】** 体育与健康课程只需自带一双运动鞋。D项正确。A项篮球和羽毛球拍、B项运动服和水瓶虽合理但非文中明确要求；C项三项运动全部装备过于夸张。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "fill in the form before August 25. Places are limited"

**【解析】** 文中强调8月25日前填写表格，且名额有限，可推知学生应尽早完成网上报名以免名额报满。B项正确。A项必须选三门与"one or two courses"矛盾；C项艺术课仅限九年级、D项科学俱乐部无人数限制均与原文不符。故选B。
