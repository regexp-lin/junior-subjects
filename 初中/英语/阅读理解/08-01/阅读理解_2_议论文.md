# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should We Limit Teenagers' Daily Social Media Use?**

② Social media has become a big part of teenage life. Many young people spend hours each day on apps like TikTok, WeChat and Weibo. Some parents and experts worry that too much screen time may harm teenagers' health and studies. The question is: should we limit how long teenagers can use social media each day? This topic has caused heated debate.

③ **Those who support limiting screen time** say that too much social media can lead to several problems. First, it may affect teenagers' eyes and sleep. Staring at screens for long hours can hurt the eyes, and many teens stay up late scrolling through their phones. Second, heavy users may find it hard to focus on homework or reading. Their grades might drop. Third, some studies show that too much social media can make young people feel lonely or anxious (焦虑的) when they compare themselves with others online. Supporters believe that setting a daily limit—for example, two hours—can help teenagers balance their time and protect their health.

④ **Those who oppose limits** argue that social media is not all bad. Teenagers use it to chat with friends, share ideas, and learn about the world. It helps them stay connected and can be useful for group projects and homework. They also say that parents cannot control everything—teenagers will find ways to use their phones anyway. Besides, some teens use social media to learn new skills, such as drawing or music. Opponents think that rather than limiting time, parents should teach their children how to use social media wisely instead.

⑤ **In my opinion**, both sides have valid points. Social media is a tool—it can be helpful or harmful depending on how we use it. I think teenagers do need some guidance. Parents and schools can work together to help young people develop good habits. This might include having "screen-free" times at home, such as during dinner or before bed. The goal should not be to ban social media completely, but to help teenagers use it in a healthy and balanced way.

**注释：** anxious 焦虑的；scroll 滚动浏览

---

### 选择题

1. According to supporters of limiting screen time, what problem can too much social media cause?
   A. Teenagers will make fewer friends.
   B. It may affect eyes, sleep, and ability to focus; some teens may feel lonely or anxious.
   C. Teenagers will stop using phones completely.
   D. Schools will close down.

2. What example do supporters give for a daily limit?
   A. One hour.
   B. Two hours.
   C. Three hours.
   D. No limit at all.

3. Why do those who oppose limits think social media is useful?
   A. Teenagers use it to chat with friends, share ideas, learn about the world, stay connected, and it can help with group projects and homework.
   B. It is the only way to learn.
   C. All teenagers love it.
   D. It replaces school entirely.

4. What do opponents suggest parents should do instead of limiting time?
   A. Take away all phones from teenagers.
   B. Let teenagers do whatever they want.
   C. Ban social media apps at school only.
   D. Teach teenagers how to use social media wisely.

5. What is the writer's view on the topic?
   A. Social media should be completely banned for all teenagers.
   B. Teenagers should use social media without any rules.
   C. Both sides have valid points; the goal is to help teenagers use social media in a healthy and balanced way, not to ban it.
   D. Only schools should decide how long teenagers can use social media.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "too much social media can lead to several problems. First, it may affect teenagers' eyes and sleep... Second, heavy users may find it hard to focus on homework... Third, some studies show that too much social media can make young people feel lonely or anxious"

**【解析】** 支持限制者指出过度使用社媒会损害眼睛和睡眠、影响专注力和成绩、使青少年感到孤独或焦虑。B项全面概括。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "setting a daily limit—for example, two hours"

**【解析】** 支持者举例建议将每日使用时间限制为两小时。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文举例不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Teenagers use it to chat with friends, share ideas, and learn about the world. It helps them stay connected and can be useful for group projects and homework"

**【解析】** 反对者认为社媒可用于交友聊天、分享想法、了解世界、保持联系，并有助于小组作业和功课。A项准确概括。B、C、D项与原文观点不符。故选A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "rather than limiting time, parents should teach their children how to use social media wisely instead"

**【解析】** 反对者建议父母应教会孩子明智使用社媒，而非单纯限制时间。D项正确。A项没收手机、B项放任不管、C项仅在学校禁用均非原文观点。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断/主旨

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, both sides have valid points... The goal should not be to ban social media completely, but to help teenagers use it in a healthy and balanced way"

**【解析】** 作者认为双方皆有道理，社媒是工具，关键在于如何使用；目标不是完全禁止，而是帮助青少年健康、平衡地使用。C项准确概括作者观点。A、B、D项均与作者立场矛盾。故选C。
