# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**The Charity Sale for Poor Students**

① Last month, our class decided to hold a charity sale. We wanted to raise money for poor students in the mountains who could not afford books and school supplies. I was a bit nervous at first. I had never sold things before. But my teacher said, "Every little bit helps. Let us try our best."

② We spent a week preparing. Some students brought old books and toys from home. Others made handicrafts like bracelets and bookmarks. I collected some nice postcards and a few storybooks. We set up a stall in the school playground on a Saturday morning. The weather was fine. Many parents and students came.

③ At first, few people stopped at our stall. I felt a little discouraged. Then my classmate Mei had an idea. "Let us call out and tell people what we are doing," she said. We started to say, "Charity sale for poor students! Your purchase can help a child go to school!" Soon, more people came. A woman bought five books. "My daughter has read these. I am happy they can help others," she said.

④ By afternoon, we had sold almost everything. We counted the money — 2,350 yuan! We could not believe it. Our teacher helped us send the money to a charity that works with rural schools. A few weeks later, we received photos of students holding new books. They were smiling. I felt so proud.

⑤ That experience taught me that we can all do something to help. You do not need to be rich or grown-up. A small sale, a few kind words, or a little time can make a real difference to someone else's life.

**注释：** handicraft 手工艺品；bracelet 手镯；rural 农村的

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the class hold a charity sale?
   A. To make money for themselves.
   B. To raise money for poor students who could not afford books and supplies.
   C. To sell expensive things.
   D. To practise English.

2. What did the writer bring to the sale?
   A. Only toys.
   B. Postcards and storybooks.
   C. Handicrafts only.
   D. Nothing.

3. What did Mei suggest when few people stopped at first?
   A. To close the stall.
   B. To call out and tell people about the charity purpose.
   C. To lower all prices.
   D. To leave early.

4. How much money did they raise?
   A. 350 yuan.
   B. 2,350 yuan.
   C. 3,500 yuan.
   D. 1,000 yuan.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Selling is very easy.
   B. Anyone can help others in small ways and make a difference.
   C. Charity sales never work.
   D. Poor students do not need help.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "We wanted to raise money for poor students in the mountains who could not afford books and school supplies"
**【解析】** 义卖目的是为买不起书本和文具的山村贫困学生筹款。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "I collected some nice postcards and a few storybooks"
**【解析】** 作者带去了明信片和故事书。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Let us call out and tell people what we are doing"
**【解析】** 梅建议大声招呼并告诉路人义卖目的。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "We counted the money — 2,350 yuan!"
**【解析】** 筹得2350元。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "we can all do something to help" 及 "can make a real difference to someone else's life"
**【解析】** 作者体会到人人都可用小举动帮助他人、产生影响。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
