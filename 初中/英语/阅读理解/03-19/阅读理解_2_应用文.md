# 阅读理解 第2篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **LOST AND FOUND BOARD - SUNSHINE COMMUNITY**

**Notice 1: Lost Puppy**
- **Name:** Max
- **Description:** A small white dog with a black spot over his left eye. He is wearing a red collar with a silver bell. 
- **Lost When:** Wednesday afternoon, around 4:00 PM.
- **Lost Where:** Near the Children’s Playground in the community park.
- **Reward:** ¥500 for his safe return!
- **Contact:** Mr. Miller at 555-0812 or Room 302, Building 4. Max is friendly but might be frightened. Please do not run after him.

② **Notice 2: Found Backpack**
- **Item:** A dark blue school backpack with a Spiderman picture on the front.
- **Found When:** Thursday morning, at 7:30 AM.
- **Found Where:** Left on bus No. 15 (going towards Central School).
- **Contents inside:** Math textbooks for Grade 7, a yellow pencil box, and a pair of reading glasses. (No ID card found inside).
- **Contact:** Please call Mrs. Green at 555-0994 to get it back. You must describe the glasses to prove it is yours.

③ **Notice 3: Lost Keys**
- **Item:** A set of 3 keys with a cute panda keychain. 
- **Lost When:** Friday evening.
- **Contact:** If you find them, please leave them at the Community Security Office (保安室). Thank you! — Lisa (Room 105, Building 2) 

---

**注释：** spot 斑块；collar 项圈；frightened 害怕的，受惊的；prove 证明；security 安全

---

### 题目

1. What does the lost dog Max look like?
   A. A big black dog with a red collar.
   B. A small white dog with a black spot.
   C. A small brown dog with a silver bell.
   D. A big white dog with a black spot over his right eye.

2. If you see Max in the park, what should you NOT do?
   A. Call Mr. Miller at 555-0812.
   B. Notice the red collar around his neck.
   C. Run after him quickly to catch him.
   D. Go to Room 302, Building 4 to tell the owner.

3. Where was the dark blue backpack found?
   A. In the Children’s Playground.
   B. At the Community Security Office.
   C. In Room 105, Building 2.
   D. On bus No. 15.

4. How can the owner get the backpack back from Mrs. Green?
   A. By showing his or her ID card.
   B. By describing the reading glasses inside.
   C. By paying a ¥500 reward.
   D. By bringing a math textbook to her.

5. Which notice was written by a person trying to find his or her lost keys?
   A. Notice 1.
   B. Notice 2.
   C. Notice 3.
   D. Both Notice 1 and Notice 3.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 Notice 1 中的 Description："A small white dog with a black spot over his left eye."

**【解析】** 根据原文描述，Max 是一只小白狗（A small white dog），左眼上方有一块黑斑（with a black spot）。B 项完全符合。A 项（大黑狗）、C 项（小棕狗）、D 项（右眼有黑斑）都与原文不符。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向选择）

**【定位】** 第①段 Notice 1 结尾处："Max is friendly but might be frightened. Please do not run after him."

**【解析】** 题目问"如果你看到Max，你不应该做什么？"原文明确提示"Please do not run after him"（请不要追赶他）。C 项"快速追逐去抓住他"正是告示中要求不应该做的事。A 项（打电话给米勒先生）、B 项（注意红项圈）、D 项（去他家告知）都是正常或允许的行为。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 Notice 2 中的 Found Where："Left on bus No. 15..."

**【解析】** 原文明确提及这个深蓝色书包是在15路公交车上被发现的。D 项与原文一致。A 项是丢狗的地点；B 项是交还钥匙的地点；C 项是丢钥匙的Lisa的住处。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 Notice 2 中的 Contact 栏最后一句话："You must describe the glasses to prove it is yours."

**【解析】** 原文要求失主必须描述书包里的眼镜以证明这是自己的包。B 项（通过描述里面的老花镜）与原文要求一致。A 项（出示身份证）无法实现，因为原文特别说明了"No ID card found inside"；C 项（付500元奖金）是 Notice 1 找狗的条件；D 项与联系方式里的要求不符。故选 B。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 各告示的标题（Item）。 

**【解析】** 题目问"哪则告示是由试图找回自己丢失钥匙的人写的？" 第③段 Notice 3 的标题是 "Lost Keys"（丢失钥匙），正文内容为 "If you find them... Thank you! — Lisa"，明显是寻找自己丢失的钥匙。C 项（Notice 3）符合要求。Notice 1 是找狗，Notice 2 是捡到书包寻找失主。故选 C。
