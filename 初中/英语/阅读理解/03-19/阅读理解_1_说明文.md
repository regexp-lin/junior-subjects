# 阅读理解 第1篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Nature is full of dangers. For many animals, staying alive in the wild is a daily struggle. To protect themselves from being eaten, some animals have developed an amazing skill: camouflage (伪装). This means they can hide in plain sight by looking like their surroundings.

② One of the most famous masters of camouflage is the chameleon. Many people think chameleons change color to match their background, but that is not completely true. They actually change color to show their mood or to control their body temperature. When they are angry or cold, their colors change. But even their normal colors help them blend in perfectly with the green leaves and brown branches of trees.

③ Another incredible animal is the stick insect. Just as its name suggests, it looks exactly like a small wooden stick! When a stick insect stays still on a tree branch, it is almost impossible for a bird to notice it. Even if the wind blows, the stick insect will sway gently, moving just like a real twig in the wind.

④ Some animals use camouflage not to hide from enemies, but to hunt for food. For example, the polar bear lives in the snowy Arctic. Its white fur makes it very hard to see against the white snow. This helps the polar bear sneak up on seals without being noticed. 

⑤ Camouflage is a perfect example of how animals adapt to their environment. Whether they are hiding from danger or hunting for their next meal, this special skill helps them survive in a difficult world. 

---

**注释：** struggle 挣扎，斗争；chameleon 变色龙；blend in 融入；sway 摇摆；twig 小树枝；sneak up on 偷偷接近；seal 海豹；adapt 适应

---

### 题目

1. What is "camouflage" according to Paragraph ①?
   A. A skill to run faster than enemies.
   B. A way to change body temperature.
   C. A skill to hide by looking like the surroundings.
   D. A special way to communicate with others.

2. Why do chameleons mainly change their color?
   A. To match the color of the trees perfectly.
   B. To show their mood or control their body temperature.
   C. To hide from dangerous snakes and birds.
   D. To look more beautiful during the mating season.

3. How does the stick insect trick its enemies?
   A. It bites the birds strongly.
   B. It makes a loud noise when the wind blows.
   C. It drops down to the ground quickly.
   D. It looks and moves like a small tree branch.

4. Why is the polar bear's white fur helpful?
   A. It helps the bear hide from hunters.
   B. It makes the bear look larger and stronger.
   C. It helps the bear catch seals more easily.
   D. It keeps the bear warm in the cold snow.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Camouflage helps animals survive in the wild.
   B. Chameleons are the smartest animals in the world.
   C. Polar bears are in danger because of the white snow.
   D. Animals in the wild are always fighting with each other.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测/细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3-4句 "...some animals have developed an amazing skill: camouflage. This means they can hide in plain sight by looking like their surroundings."

**【解析】** 原文对"camouflage"作了解释："This means they can hide in plain sight by looking like their surroundings."（这意味着它们可以通过看起来像周围环境而在众目睽睽之下隐藏起来）。C 项与该解释一致。A 项（跑得比敌人快的技能）、B 项（改变体温的方法）、D 项（交流的特殊方式）均不符合原文解释。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2-3句 "Many people think chameleons change color to match their background, but that is not completely true. They actually change color to show their mood or to control their body temperature."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出变色龙改变颜色"actually"（实际上）是为了"展示他们的情绪或控制体温"，并非人们普遍认为的"只是为了匹配背景"。B 项与原文事实完全一致。A 项是人们的误解；C、D 项原文并未提及。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句及第4句 "...it looks exactly like a small wooden stick! ...the stick insect will sway gently, moving just like a real twig in the wind."

**【解析】** 讲竹节虫时说它看起来像根小木棍，起风时它还会像风中的真树枝一样轻轻摇晃。D 项"它看起来并移动得像一根小树枝"综合了原文信息，准确说明了它欺骗敌人的方式。A 项（咬鸟）、B 项（发出大声）、C 项（快速掉到地上）在文中均未提及，属无中生有。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段第2-4句 "Its white fur makes it very hard to see against the white snow. This helps the polar bear sneak up on seals without being noticed."

**【解析】** 原文说北极熊白色的毛让它在白雪背景下很难被发现，这帮助它"在不被注意的情况下偷偷接近海豹"（sneak up on seals）。海豹是它的猎物。由此推断，白毛有助于它捕猎海豹。C 项"它帮助北极熊更容易地抓住海豹"符合语意逻辑。A 项（躲避猎人）、B 项（看起来更大更强）、D 项（保暖）原文在本段解释伪装用途时并未提及。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文整体结构及第⑤段总结 "Camouflage is a perfect example of how animals adapt... this special skill helps them survive in a difficult world."

**【解析】** 全文介绍了动物的伪装术，并举了变色龙、竹节虫（为了躲避危险/适应环境）和北极熊（为了捕猎）的例子，最后总结伪装术帮助动物在野外生存。A 项"伪装帮助动物在野外生存"最准确地概括了主旨。B 项只概括了其中一个小例子；C 项将北极熊置于危险的理解与原文事实相反；D 项"野生动物总是在互相斗争"没有点出"伪装"这一核心话题。故选 A。
