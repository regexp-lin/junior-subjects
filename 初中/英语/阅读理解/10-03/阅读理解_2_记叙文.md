> **10-03 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last March, our school organised a tree-planting activity in a park near the city. Our teacher said, "Spring is the best time to plant trees. Today we will do something good for our environment." I had never planted a tree before. I was excited and could not wait to start.

② When we arrived at the park, each class got a few small trees and some tools. My group had a young willow tree. We found a good spot with enough sunlight and soft soil. First, we dug a hole that was wide and deep enough for the roots. It was hard work — my arms ached after a while. But my classmates and I took turns, and we did it together.

③ Then we carefully placed the tree in the hole and filled the hole with soil. We made sure the roots were covered and the tree stood straight. Our teacher told us to press the soil gently so that the roots could get air and water. After that, we poured some water around the tree. "Water it well in the first few weeks," the teacher said. "Then it will grow strong."

④ We wrote our class name and the date on a small sign and put it next to our tree. "In a few years, this tree will be tall and green," my friend said. "We can come back and see it." I smiled. The idea that we had helped a tree grow made me feel proud. I imagined birds resting on its branches and people enjoying its shade one day.

⑤ That afternoon, we planted over a hundred trees in the park. On the bus ride back to school, I felt tired but happy. Planting trees is not only good for the environment — it also teaches us to work together and care for nature. I hope to join more activities like this in the future.

**注释：** willow 柳树；shade 树荫

---

**Questions:**

1. When did the tree-planting activity take place?
   A. In winter.
   B. In spring (March).
   C. In summer.
   D. In autumn.

2. What kind of tree did the writer's group plant?
   A. A pine tree.
   B. A willow tree.
   C. An oak tree.
   D. A fruit tree.

3. What did they do after placing the tree in the hole?
   A. They left immediately.
   B. They filled the hole with soil, pressed it gently, and poured water around the tree.
   C. They only wrote their names.
   D. They cut the branches.

4. How did the writer feel after the activity?
   A. Bored and disappointed.
   B. Tired but happy, and proud of helping the environment.
   C. Angry because it was too hard.
   D. Sad because the tree might die.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer will never plant trees again.
   B. The writer learned the value of teamwork and caring for nature, and wants to join similar activities.
   C. The writer thinks planting trees is useless.
   D. The writer only cared about getting a good grade.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last March, our school organised a tree-planting activity"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明活动在三月（春天）举行。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "My group had a young willow tree"

**【解析】** 作者所在小组种植的是柳树。B项正确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "we filled the hole with soil... press the soil gently... we poured some water around the tree"

**【解析】** 放好树后，他们填土、轻压实、浇水。B项完整准确。A、C、D项与步骤不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I felt tired but happy... made me feel proud" 及第④段

**【解析】** 作者感到疲惫但快乐，并为帮助环境感到自豪。B项准确。A、C、D项与作者感受相反。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Planting trees is not only good for the environment — it also teaches us to work together and care for nature. I hope to join more activities like this in the future"

**【解析】** 可推知作者体会到合作与关爱自然的意义，并希望参与类似活动。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
