# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Holding an Umbrella for a Stranger at the Bus Stop**

① It was a rainy afternoon. I was waiting for the bus at the stop near my school. The rain was heavy. I had my umbrella, so I was fine. But then I noticed an old lady standing a few metres away. She had no umbrella. Her clothes were getting wet. She was holding a bag of vegetables and looking worried.

② I walked over to her. "Would you like to share my umbrella?" I asked. She looked surprised at first. Then she smiled. "Thank you, young man. That is very kind of you." I held the umbrella over both of us. We stood there together, waiting for the bus. The rain kept falling, but we stayed dry.

③ We started to talk. She told me she was going to visit her daughter. She had bought some vegetables to cook for her. "My daughter works late. I want to make her a nice meal," she said. I told her about my school and my plans for the weekend. Time passed quickly. When the bus finally came, she thanked me again and got on. I felt good inside.

④ That small act of kindness did not take much effort. I did not do anything special. I just shared my umbrella. But her smile and her "thank you" stayed with me. Sometimes we think we need to do something big to help others. But often, a small act of kindness can make a real difference.

⑤ I still take the bus from that stop. Whenever it rains, I remember that afternoon. I hope that if someone else needs help, I will be there to offer it. Kindness does not cost anything. It only needs a willing heart.

**Questions:**

1. Where did the story take place?
   A. At school.
   B. At a bus stop in the rain.
   C. At home.
   D. In a shop.

2. Why did the old lady look worried?
   A. She had lost her money.
   B. She had no umbrella and was getting wet.
   C. She missed the bus.
   D. She could not find her daughter.

3. What did the writer do to help the lady?
   A. He gave her some money.
   B. He shared his umbrella with her.
   C. He called a taxi for her.
   D. He took her home.

4. Why was the lady going to visit her daughter?
   A. To borrow money.
   B. To cook a meal for her daughter who works late.
   C. To stay at her daughter's house forever.
   D. To buy vegetables.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Umbrellas are expensive.
   B. A small act of kindness can make a real difference.
   C. Old people should never go out in the rain.
   D. Buses are always late.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "I was waiting for the bus at the stop near my school... It was a rainy afternoon"
**【解析】** 故事发生在学校附近的下雨公交站。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "an old lady standing a few metres away. She had no umbrella. Her clothes were getting wet... looking worried"
**【解析】** 老奶奶没有伞，衣服被淋湿，因而着急。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Would you like to share my umbrella?... I held the umbrella over both of us"
**【解析】** 作者主动提出与老奶奶共撑一把伞。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "She was going to visit her daughter... I want to make her a nice meal... My daughter works late"
**【解析】** 老奶奶去女儿家，为加班晚归的女儿做饭。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第④段 "a small act of kindness can make a real difference"
**【解析】** 作者体会到小小的善举也能带来真正的改变。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
