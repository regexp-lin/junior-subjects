> **12-22 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**City Science Museum — Student Visit Notice**

① Dear students and teachers,

Our school will organize a visit to the City Science Museum on Saturday, January 4, 2026. All Grade 8 students are invited. Please read the following information.

**Visit Details:**

| Item | Information |
|------|--------------|
| Date | Saturday, January 4, 2026 |
| Time | 9:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. |
| Meeting place | School gate |
| Transport | School bus |
| Fee | Free (lunch not included) |

② **Schedule:**
- 9:00 a.m. Meet at school gate, get on the bus
- 10:00 a.m. Arrive at museum, guided tour starts
- 12:00 p.m. Lunch break (bring your own food or buy at the museum café)
- 1:00 p.m. Free visit in small groups (2–4 students)
- 2:30 p.m. Meet at the main entrance, return to school
- 3:00 p.m. Arrive at school

③ **Rules:**
- Do not touch the exhibits unless the guide says you can.
- Do not run or shout in the museum.
- Stay with your group. Do not leave alone.
- Bring your student ID. You need it to enter the museum.

④ **What to bring:**
- Student ID
- Lunch or money for food
- A pen and notebook (for taking notes)
- A water bottle

⑤ Please tell your class teacher by December 28 if you want to join. The number of places is limited to 120 students. We will confirm your place by December 30.

School Office  
December 22, 2025

---

**Questions:**

1. Who can join this visit?
   A. All students.
   B. Grade 7 students only.
   C. Grade 8 students only.
   D. Grade 9 students only.

2. How much do students need to pay for the visit?
   A. 50 yuan.
   B. 100 yuan.
   C. Free, but lunch is not included.
   D. The notice does not say.

3. When does the guided tour start?
   A. At 9:00 a.m.
   B. At 10:00 a.m.
   C. At 12:00 p.m.
   D. At 1:00 p.m.

4. What must students bring to enter the museum?
   A. A camera.
   B. Student ID.
   C. A lunch box.
   D. A mobile phone.

5. By when should students tell their teacher if they want to join?
   A. December 22.
   B. December 28.
   C. December 30.
   D. January 4.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "All Grade 8 students are invited."

**【解析】** 原文明确说八年级学生受邀参加。A项所有学生、B项七年级、D项九年级均不正确。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Fee | Free (lunch not included)"

**【解析】** 参观免费，但不含午餐。A、B项收费金额无依据；D项未说明与表格内容矛盾。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "10:00 a.m. Arrive at museum, guided tour starts"

**【解析】** 10点到达博物馆，导览开始。A项9点是集合；C项12点是午餐；D项1点是自由参观。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Bring your student ID. You need it to enter the museum."

**【解析】** 进入博物馆必须带学生证。A项相机、C项饭盒、D项手机均非入场必需。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Please tell your class teacher by December 28 if you want to join."

**【解析】** 想参加需在12月28日前告知班主任。A项12月22日是通知发布日期；C项12月30日是确认日期；D项1月4日是参观日。故选B。
