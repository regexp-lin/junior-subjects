> **12-22 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Break Time at Our School**

① The bell rings at 10:10 a.m. Every morning, this is the most exciting moment for us. The break lasts twenty minutes. We rush out of the classroom and run to the playground. The air is fresh and the sun is warm. We cannot wait to play.

② Some of my classmates like to play basketball. They divide into two teams and have a quick match. The court is always full. Others prefer to jump rope. A few girls stand in a line and take turns. They count together: one, two, three... When someone misses, everyone laughs. It is so much fun.

③ I usually play football with my friends. We use a small area near the trees. There are no real goal posts, so we use our schoolbags. We kick the ball, run, and shout. Sometimes the ball goes into the flowers and we have to get it back quickly before the teacher sees us. We always return to class with red faces and big smiles.

④ Some students do not like sports. They sit on the benches and chat. They talk about homework, new books, or funny stories. A few students even use the break to finish their homework. But most of us think break time is for rest and play. We need to relax so we can focus better in the next class.

⑤ When the bell rings again at 10:30, we walk slowly back to the classroom. Our bodies are tired but our minds are clear. Break time is short, but it gives us energy for the rest of the day. I love these twenty minutes!

**注释：** court 球场；bench 长凳；focus 集中注意力

---

**Questions:**

1. How long does the morning break last?
   A. Ten minutes.
   B. Fifteen minutes.
   C. Twenty minutes.
   D. Thirty minutes.

2. What do some girls do during the break?
   A. Play basketball.
   B. Jump rope.
   C. Play football.
   D. Sit and do homework.

3. What do the writer and his friends use as goal posts when playing football?
   A. Real goal posts.
   B. Trees.
   C. Schoolbags.
   D. Benches.

4. Why do most students think break time is important?
   A. Because they can finish homework.
   B. Because they need to rest and play so they can focus better in class.
   C. Because the teacher asks them to relax.
   D. Because they have nothing else to do.

5. How does the writer feel after the break?
   A. Tired and sleepy.
   B. Bored and unhappy.
   C. Tired in body but clear in mind; full of energy.
   D. Worried about the next class.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The break lasts twenty minutes."

**【解析】** 原文明确说课间休息二十分钟。A项十分钟、B项十五分钟、D项三十分钟均不正确。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Others prefer to jump rope. A few girls stand in a line and take turns."

**【解析】** 一些女生喜欢跳绳，排队轮流跳。A项打篮球是另一些同学；C项踢足球是"我"和朋友们；D项做作业是少数学生。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "There are no real goal posts, so we use our schoolbags."

**【解析】** 没有真正的球门，所以用书包当球门。A项与原文矛盾；B项树是场地旁边；D项长凳是坐的地方。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "But most of us think break time is for rest and play. We need to relax so we can focus better in the next class."

**【解析】** 大多数人认为课间用于休息和玩耍，放松后下一节课能更专注。A项完成作业是少数人；C项老师要求、D项无事可做均非主要原因。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Our bodies are tired but our minds are clear. Break time is short, but it gives us energy for the rest of the day."

**【解析】** 身体疲惫但头脑清醒，课间虽短却为一天注入活力。A项困倦、B项无聊不高兴、D项担心下节课均与原文不符。故选C。
