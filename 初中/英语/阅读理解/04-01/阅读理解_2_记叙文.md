> **04-01 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last Friday evening, our school held a talent show in the main hall. Many students came to watch it. I was one of the performers (表演者). I decided to play a quiet piano piece for everyone.

② When it was my turn, I felt very nervous. My hands were shaking (发抖). I walked onto the stage slowly. My heart beat fast. What if I played the wrong notes? What if people laughed at me? Then I saw my best friend, Lily, sitting in the front row. She smiled at me and gave me a "thumbs up" (竖起大拇指).

③ Seeing her smile, I took a deep breath (深呼吸) and began to play. I closed my eyes, and the music flowed from my fingers. I forgot my nervousness and put all my feelings into the music. When I finished the last note, the hall was quiet for a second, and then everyone cheered and clapped warmly.

④ It was an unforgettable night. Although I didn't win the first prize, I won something more important — confidence (自信) and a great memory. I realized that the courage to stand on a stage is the real success.

**注释：** performer 表演者；shake 发抖；stage 舞台

---

### 选择题

1. Where was the talent show held?
A. On the school playground.
B. In the main hall.
C. In a music classroom.
D. At Lily's house.

2. What did the writer perform at the talent show?
A. She sang a quiet song.
B. She told a funny story.
C. She played the piano.
D. She danced on the stage.

3. Why did the writer's heart beat fast?
A. Because she was running.
B. Because it was too hot in the hall.
C. Because she was very nervous.
D. Because Lily was angry with her.

4. What happened right after the writer finished playing?
A. Everyone left the hall quickly.
B. The hall was quiet for a second, and then people cheered.
C. Several students laughed at her loudly.
D. Her parents told her to stop.

5. According to the last paragraph, what did the writer gain (获得) from the show?
A. The first prize.
B. Confidence and a great memory.
C. A beautiful new piano.
D. A lot of money.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "...our school held a talent show in the main hall."

**【解析】** 原文提到学校的才艺表演是在大厅（main hall）里举行的。A项操场，C项音乐教室，D项Lily的家皆与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "I decided to play a quiet piano piece for everyone."

**【解析】** 原文明确了作者选择为大家演奏一首安静的钢琴曲。A项（唱歌）、B项（讲笑话）、D项（跳舞）均不是作者表演的项目。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段第一句 "When it was my turn, I felt very nervous." 及后面的 "My heart beat fast."

**【解析】** 从上文得知，轮到作者上场时她感到非常紧张（nervous），甚至还会害怕出错被人笑，这导致了她心跳加速。A项（因为跑步）、B项（因为大厅太热）、D项（因为Lily生气）都是不相干的信息。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "When I finished the last note, the hall was quiet for a second, and then everyone cheered and clapped warmly."

**【解析】** 原文描述在她弹完最后一个音符时，大厅安静了一秒钟，紧接着大家都开始欢呼和热烈鼓掌。B项完整概括了此细节。A项（大家迅速离场）、C项（学生大声笑她）、D项（父母叫她停下）皆属于无中生有或与原文相反。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "Although I didn't win the first prize, I won something more important — confidence (自信) and a great memory."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段表示，虽然没拿到第一名（A项错误），但赢得了更重要的东西：自信和一段美好的记忆。其余选项为生编硬造的内容。故选B。
