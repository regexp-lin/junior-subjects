> **04-01 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Smartphones are everywhere today. Almost everyone has one. They have changed our lives in many ways. But are they really good for us? 

② On the one hand, smartphones are very useful. With a smartphone, we can easily talk to friends and family anywhere in the world. We can also find information quickly when we need help with our homework. Besides, maps on the phone can prevent us from getting lost in a new city.

③ On the other hand, smartphones can cause problems. Many students spend too much time on their phones playing games or watching short videos. Because of this, they have less time for study and sports. Some students even use phones late at night, which makes them feel sleepy the next day. Also, staring at the screen for a long time is bad for our eyes.

④ In short, a smartphone is a helpful tool if we use it in the right way. We shouldn't let it rule our lives. We need to control our screen time and spend more time talking with people face to face.

**注释：** stare 凝视，盯着看

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, which of the following is an advantage (好处) of using a smartphone?
A. We can play exciting games with it.
B. We can find information quickly.
C. We can watch short videos.
D. We can sleep better at night.

2. Why do some students feel sleepy the next day?
A. Because they study too late.
B. Because their eyes are bad.
C. Because they use their phones late at night.
D. Because they don't like going to school.

3. What does the underlined word "staring" in paragraph ③ probably mean?
A. Looking at something for a long time.
B. Touching something with clean hands.
C. Thinking of something difficult.
D. Hiding something in a dark place.

4. What can we infer (推断) from the passage?
A. We should throw away all smartphones.
B. Smartphones have both good and bad sizes.
C. Students should only use phones for playing games.
D. Every student must get a new smartphone.

5. What is the writer's advice (建议) in the last paragraph?
A. Stop using smartphones at all times.
B. Spend more time playing short videos.
C. Throw your map away and buy a phone.
D. Make good use of the phone and talk face to face more.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "We can also find information quickly when we need help with our homework."

**【解析】** 原文中第②段专门论述智能手机的好处（优势），其中提到我们可以快速找到我们需要的信息。A项（玩令人兴奋的游戏）、C项（看短视频）是文中指出的导致学生花太多时间的“问题”（第③段）。D项（晚上睡得更好）与第③段中晚睡导致犯困的内容矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "Some students even use phones late at night, which makes them feel sleepy the next day."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到一些学生深夜用手机，这导致他们第二天感到困倦（sleepy）。A项说因为他们学习太晚，文意是玩手机而不是学习；B、D项更不是原文提及犯困的原因。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "Also, staring at the screen for a long time is bad for our eyes."

**【解析】** 本句指出“...屏幕很长时间对我们的眼睛有害”。结合常识和"for a long time"的语境，这里的"staring"意为“长时间盯着（屏幕）看”，最符合A项。B项是触摸；C项是思考；D项是藏匿。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②、③段的结构("On the one hand... On the other hand...")和第④段总结。

**【解析】** 文章第②段讲了手机的益处，第③段讲了手机带来的坏处。由此可以推断出：智能手机既有好的一面也有坏的一面。A项目要求“扔掉所有手机”，文章表示要在正确的路线使用它即可；C项目和D项目均与文意不符或太极端。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "We need to control our screen time and spend more time talking with people face to face."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段中提出：智能手机是一个有用的工具（前提是用对方法），建议控制看屏幕的时间，并多花时间和人面对面交谈。D项的“好好利用手机，多面对面交流”与这句话贴合。A项太极端且违背作者原意；B、C项更都不是文中的建议。故选D。
