# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Trees are an important part of our world. They offer us much more than just a beautiful view. However, many people don't fully understand how much we need them. We often forget that our lives depend on trees in many ways.

② First, trees are great air cleaners. We humans breathe in oxygen (氧气) and breathe out carbon dioxide (二氧化碳). Trees do the opposite. They take in the carbon dioxide from the air and produce oxygen for us to breathe. Without trees, the air would become too dirty for us to live in. 

③ Second, trees provide homes and food for a lot of animals. Birds build their nests in the branches (树枝), and many insects live on the leaves and in the wood. Squirrels (松鼠) and monkeys find their food, like nuts and fruits, mainly from trees. If we cut down trees, these animals will lose their homes and have nothing to eat.

④ Finally, trees help keep the temperature pleasant. In summer, the leaves of trees can block the hot sunlight and make the air cooler. They can change the environment around our houses. 

⑤ Sadly, millions of trees are cut down every year to make paper or build houses. The number of trees on Earth is going down quickly. It is time for us to take action. We should use less paper, recycle old books, and most importantly, plant more trees. Every little effort helps protect our green friends.

**注释：** oxygen 氧气；carbon dioxide 二氧化碳；branch 树枝；squirrel 松鼠

### 选择题

1. What do trees take in from the air?
A. Oxygen.
B. Carbon dioxide.
C. Clean water.
D. Sunlight.

2. Which animals are mentioned to build nests in the branches?
A. Monkeys.
B. Squirrels.
C. Birds.
D. Insects.

3. How do trees help us in summer according to the passage?
A. They stop the strong wind.
B. They give us sweet fruits.
C. They block the sunlight to make it cooler.
D. They provide a lot of wood for a fire.

4. What can you infer (推断) from the last paragraph?
A. People are planting enough trees to save the Earth.
B. Making paper uses up many trees.
C. We should not build houses anymore.
D. It is too late to save the environment.

5. What is the writer's main purpose (目的) in writing this passage?
A. To introduce different kinds of animals.
B. To tell a story about planting trees.
C. To teach us how to make paper.
D. To encourage people to protect trees.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后第二句 "They take in the carbon dioxide from the air and produce oxygen for us to breathe."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到树木从空气中吸收二氧化碳（carbon dioxide）并产生氧气。A项是树木产生的气体；C项和D项并非文中提到的“吸收”的内容。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Birds build their nests in the branches..."

**【解析】** 原文提到鸟在树枝上筑巢。A项（猴子）、B项（松鼠）、D项（昆虫）虽然文中也提到了，但筑巢在树枝上的是Bird（鸟）。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "In summer, the leaves of trees can block the hot sunlight and make the air cooler."

**【解析】** 根据原文，树叶能够阻挡夏天炎热的阳光，从而让空气变得更凉爽。A项、B项、D项均不是文章第四段提到的夏天树木的作用。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "Sadly, millions of trees are cut down every year to make paper or build houses."

**【解析】** 原文提到每年有数百万棵树被砍伐用来造纸或建房子。由此可以推断出，制造纸张消耗了大量的树木。A项与文章所述“树木数量急剧下降”矛盾；C项过于绝对，文章并未说不能建房子，只是要采取行动保护树木；D项“太迟了”与作者呼吁大家采取行动的态度相矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文及第⑤段最后一句 "We should use less paper, recycle old books, and most importantly, plant more trees."

**【解析】** 这是一篇说明文，文章分析了树木的几个重要作用，并在最后指出现实中树木被大量砍伐的问题，呼吁大家一起采取行动。因此作家的主要写作用意是鼓励人们保护树木。A项、B项、C项均不是文章主要意图。故选D。
