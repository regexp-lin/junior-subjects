# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Tom was a fourteen-year-old boy. Like many boys of his age, he was active and playful. However, he had a bad habit—he never kept his room clean and tidy. His mother often said, "Tom, please clean your room. A tidy room will help you think better and find things quickly." But Tom just shook his head and replied, "I know where my things are, Mom. I don't need to put them in order."

② One Saturday morning, Tom woke up early because he was going to a science museum with his friends. They had planned this trip for weeks. He needed his student ID card to get a free ticket. But when he looked for it on his desk, it wasn't there. Then he searched his schoolbag, the chair, and even the floor. His room was in such a mess that he couldn't find the small card.

③ Time was running out. His friends were waiting for him at the bus stop. Tom became very anxious (焦虑的) and started to cry. Hearing the noise, his mother walked in. Seeing the messy room, she sighed (叹气) and decided to help him. After ten minutes of searching, they finally found the ID card under a pile of old newspapers.

④ Tom was very thankful to his mother. He finally understood what his mother meant. "Mom, I am sorry," he said. "I will clean my room as soon as I come back." From that day on, Tom's room was always clean and tidy.

**注释：** anxious 焦虑的；sigh 叹气

---

### 选择题

1. What was Tom's bad habit?
A. He liked playing with his friends.
B. He didn't like getting up early.
C. He never kept his room clean.
D. He often forgot his homework.

2. Why did Tom wake up early that Saturday morning?
A. He had to finish his science homework.
B. He planned to clean his room.
C. He wanted to buy a ticket to a museum.
D. He was going to a science museum with his friends.

3. Where did Tom and his mother finally find his student ID card?
A. On his desk.
B. Under some old newspapers.
C. In his schoolbag.
D. Under a chair.

4. What does the underlined word "mess" in paragraph ② mean?
A. A state of untidiness (不整洁).
B. A state of danger.
C. A beautiful place.
D. A quiet space.

5. What does the passage mainly tell us?
A. Going to museums is good for students.
B. Keeping our living space tidy is important.
C. Mothers always know where to find things.
D. We shouldn't put newspapers under the bed.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "However, he had a bad habit—he never kept his room clean and tidy."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到汤姆的坏习惯是他从不保持房间整洁。A项“他喜欢和朋友玩”、B项“他不喜欢早起”、D项“他经常忘记做家庭作业”均为无中生有或非关键信息。故选C。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "One Saturday morning, Tom woke up early because he was going to a science museum with his friends."

**【解析】** 原文交代汤姆星期六早起的原因是打算和朋友们去科技博物馆。A项“做完科学作业”与原文矛盾；B项“打算打扫房间”与原文不符；C项“想买博物馆门票”属于偷换概念，原文是他要有学生证才能“免费免票”。故选D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "...they finally found the ID card under a pile of old newspapers."

**【解析】** 原文提到他们找了十分钟后，终于在一堆旧报纸下面找到了学生证。A项“在书桌上”、C项“在书包里”，是他一开始没找到的地方；D项“在椅子下面”与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "His room was in such a mess that he couldn't find the small card."

**【解析】** 根据上文他到处找学生证以及他平时的坏习惯可知，他的房间处于一种非常杂乱（untidiness）的状态，导致他找不到小小的卡片。B项“危险的状态”、C项“美丽的地方”、D项“安静的空间”均不符合语境。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，特别是第①段母亲的话："A tidy room will help you think better and find things quickly." 以及第④段汤姆的改变。

**【解析】** 这是一篇记叙文。文章讲述了汤姆因为不整理房间差点错过和朋友去科技馆的故事，最后他意识到保持房间整洁的重要性。A项“去博物馆对学生有好处”偏离主题；C项“母亲总能知道去哪里找东西”只是文中的一个细节；D项“我们不应该把报纸放在床下”属于以偏概全。B项“保持我们的生活空间整洁很重要”是整篇文章要传达的中心思想。故选B。
