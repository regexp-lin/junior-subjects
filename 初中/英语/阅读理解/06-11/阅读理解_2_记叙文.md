> **06-11 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last month, our school held a charity bazaar (义卖会) to help students from poor families. Li Ming, a quiet boy from Class 3, decided to sell handmade bookmarks and paper flowers that he had made with his mother at home. He spent two weeks preparing. Every night after homework, he folded paper, drew pictures, and tied ribbons (丝带) on each bookmark. His mother said, "These are beautiful. I'm sure students will love them."

② On the day of the bazaar, Li Ming set up a small table near the school gate. He laid out his bookmarks and paper flowers neatly. At first, few people stopped. Li Ming felt a bit nervous. Then a teacher walked over and picked up a bookmark. "Did you make this yourself?" she asked. Li Ming nodded. "It's lovely. I'll take five." The teacher paid and encouraged other students to come and look. Soon, more and more people gathered around Li Ming's table.

③ By noon, Li Ming had sold almost everything. He counted the money: 380 yuan. His eyes brightened. He knew that 380 yuan could buy books and supplies for a poor classmate. "I never thought my hobby could help others," Li Ming said to himself. A senior student who had bought a paper flower said, "Your work is creative. You should be proud."

④ In the afternoon, the school announced the total money raised: 12,000 yuan. The headteacher said, "Thanks to students like Li Ming who worked hard and shared their skills, we can support more students in need." Li Ming stood in the crowd, feeling both shy and happy. He had used his own hands to make a difference.

⑤ That night, Li Ming told his mother about the bazaar. "Mom, I want to make more crafts (手工艺品) next time. Maybe I can teach other students how to make them too." His mother smiled and said, "You've learned something important today: helping others can start with small things we do with our hearts."

**注释：** bazaar 义卖会；ribbon 丝带；craft 手工艺品

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what did Li Ming prepare for the charity bazaar?
   A. Toys and snacks from a shop.
   B. Handmade bookmarks and paper flowers made with his mother.
   C. Old books and clothes.
   D. Food and drinks.

2. According to paragraph ②, what happened after the teacher bought five bookmarks?
   A. Li Ming closed his table and went home.
   B. Fewer people came to his table.
   C. More and more people gathered around Li Ming's table.
   D. The teacher asked Li Ming to lower his prices.

3. According to paragraph ③, how much money did Li Ming raise by noon?
   A. 12,000 yuan.
   B. 380 yuan.
   C. 5 yuan.
   D. 2,000 yuan.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Li Ming?
   A. He did not care about helping poor students.
   B. He felt proud that his efforts had contributed to helping students in need.
   C. He wanted to quit school and sell crafts full-time.
   D. He was angry that the headteacher did not mention his name.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Charity bazaars are only for rich students.
   B. Small actions done with heart can help others and make a difference.
   C. Handmade items are always more expensive than bought ones.
   D. Students should never sell things at school.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Li Ming... decided to sell handmade bookmarks and paper flowers that he had made with his mother at home."

**【解析】** 原文写明李明售卖与母亲在家中制作的手工书签和纸花，B 项正确。A 项"商店玩具零食"、C 项"旧书旧衣"和 D 项"食物饮料"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The teacher paid and encouraged other students to come and look. Soon, more and more people gathered around Li Ming's table."

**【解析】** 原文说明老师购买后鼓励他人来看，越来越多人聚到李明桌前，C 项正确。A 项"收摊回家"、B 项"人变少"和 D 项"要求降价"均未提及。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "He counted the money: 380 yuan."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出李明数到的金额为 380 元，B 项正确。A 项 12,000 元为全校筹款总额，C、D 项均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Thanks to students like Li Ming who worked hard and shared their skills, we can support more students in need... Li Ming stood in the crowd, feeling both shy and happy. He had used his own hands to make a difference."

**【解析】** 校长感谢像李明这样努力并分享技能的学生，李明感到害羞且开心，认为自己用双手带来了改变，可推知他为自己的贡献感到自豪，B 项正确。A 项"不关心贫困生"、C 项"想退学全职卖手工"和 D 项"对校长未提其名生气"均无依据。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文及第⑤段母亲的话 "helping others can start with small things we do with our hearts."

**【解析】** 文章通过李明义卖手工品助人的故事，传达用心做的小事也能帮助他人、产生改变，B 项正确。A 项"义卖只给富学生"、C 项"手工品总更贵"和 D 项"学生不应在校卖东西"均与文章主旨相反。故选 B。
