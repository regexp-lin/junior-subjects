# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Solar energy (太阳能) comes from the sun. It is a clean and renewable (可再生的) source of power. Unlike coal or oil, the sun will shine for billions of years. That is why more and more countries are using solar energy to produce electricity. Solar panels (太阳能板) are often placed on roofs of houses and buildings. When sunlight hits these panels, they turn light into electricity that we can use at home or at school.

② Solar energy has many advantages. First, it does not pollute the air or water. Burning coal and gas causes air pollution and harms our health. Solar power is clean and safe. Second, once solar panels are installed (安装), the energy is nearly free. The sun does not send you a bill! Third, solar energy can be used in remote (偏远的) places where there is no power line. Villages in the mountains can now have light and power with solar panels.

③ Some people worry that solar panels are expensive at first. It is true that buying and installing them costs money. However, over time, families can save a lot on electricity bills. In sunny places, some homes even produce more electricity than they need. They can sell the extra power back to the grid (电网). In this way, solar panels can pay for themselves in several years.

④ Solar technology keeps improving. New panels are lighter, cheaper, and work better even on cloudy days. Many schools in China have started using solar panels on their buildings. Students learn about clean energy in class and see it in action every day. This helps young people understand the importance of protecting our environment.

⑤ As we face climate change and pollution, solar energy offers a hopeful future. It is clean, endless, and becoming more affordable. Choosing solar power is not only good for our wallets but also for our planet.

**注释：** renewable 可再生的；panel 板；install 安装；remote 偏远的；grid 电网

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what do solar panels do?
   A. They burn coal to make electricity.
   B. They turn sunlight into electricity.
   C. They store oil for later use.
   D. They cool buildings in summer.

2. According to paragraph ②, which is NOT an advantage of solar energy?
   A. It does not pollute the air or water.
   B. The energy is nearly free after panels are installed.
   C. It works only in big cities with power lines.
   D. It can be used in remote places without power lines.

3. According to paragraph ③, what can families in sunny places do with extra solar electricity?
   A. Throw it away.
   B. Store it in batteries only.
   C. Sell the extra power back to the grid.
   D. Give it to their neighbors for free.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about solar technology?
   A. Solar panels only work on sunny days.
   B. Solar technology is improving and more schools are using it to teach students about clean energy.
   C. New panels are heavier and more expensive than before.
   D. Students are not interested in solar energy.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell people to stop using electricity.
   B. To compare solar energy with coal and oil in detail.
   C. To introduce solar energy and its advantages for a cleaner future.
   D. To explain how to install solar panels step by step.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "When sunlight hits these panels, they turn light into electricity that we can use at home or at school."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明太阳能板将光转化为可用电力，B 项正确。A 项"燃烧煤炭"、C 项"储存石油"和 D 项"给建筑降温"均与太阳能板工作原理不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解（反向/排除）

**【定位】** 第②段列举太阳能三大优势：不污染空气与水；安装后几乎免费；可在偏远无电网处使用。

**【解析】** C 项"仅在有电网的大城市可用"与原文"can be used in remote places where there is no power line"相反，故为 NOT 的答案。A、B、D 三项均为文中提到的优势。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "some homes even produce more electricity than they need. They can sell the extra power back to the grid (电网)."

**【解析】** 原文写明光照充足的家庭可将多余电力卖回电网，C 项正确。A 项"扔掉"、B 项"仅存电池"和 D 项"免费给邻居"均未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Solar technology keeps improving. New panels are lighter, cheaper, and work better even on cloudy days. Many schools in China have started using solar panels... Students learn about clean energy in class... This helps young people understand the importance of protecting our environment."

**【解析】** 综合可得：太阳能技术不断改进，学校使用太阳能并借此教授清洁能源，帮助学生理解环保，B 项正确。A 项"仅晴天可用"与"work better even on cloudy days"矛盾；C 项"更重更贵"与"lighter, cheaper"相反；D 项"学生不感兴趣"无依据。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段太阳能原理，第②段优势，第③段经济性，第④段技术发展与教育应用，第⑤段环保意义。

**【解析】** 文章整体介绍太阳能及其优势，呼吁清洁未来，C 项准确概括主旨。A 项"不用电"、B 项"详细对比化石能源"和 D 项"逐步安装指南"均非主旨。故选 C。
