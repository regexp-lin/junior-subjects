> **08-20 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① During the National Day holiday, twelve-year-old Lin Hao went hiking with his father in the mountains near their hometown. It was a beautiful autumn day. The leaves were red and gold, and the air was fresh. Lin Hao was excited because it was his first time hiking on such a high mountain.

② They followed a narrow path through the forest. After walking for about two hours, they stopped by a stream (小溪) to rest and eat lunch. Lin Hao noticed a butterfly with bright blue wings and ran after it. He chased it deeper and deeper into the trees. When the butterfly finally flew away, Lin Hao looked around and realized he could no longer see the path — or his father.

③ At first, Lin Hao felt scared. His heart beat fast, and he wanted to cry. But then he remembered what his father had taught him: "If you ever get lost in the mountains, stay calm. Find a high place, stay where you are, and make noise so people can find you." Lin Hao took a deep breath and climbed onto a large rock. He shouted, "Dad! I'm here!" again and again.

④ After about twenty minutes, he heard his father's voice in the distance. "Lin Hao! Keep shouting! I'm coming!" A few minutes later, his father appeared through the bushes (灌木丛). He looked worried but relieved (松了一口气). He hugged Lin Hao tightly. "Are you all right?" Lin Hao nodded. "I did what you told me, Dad."

⑤ On the way back, his father said, "I'm proud of you. You stayed calm and used your head. That's what matters most in a difficult situation." Lin Hao smiled. The hike had not gone as planned, but he had learned something important: when you are afraid, staying calm and thinking clearly can help you find a way out.

**注释：** path 小路；stream 小溪；butterfly 蝴蝶；chase 追；relieved 松了一口气的；bush 灌木丛

---

**Questions:**

1. When did Lin Hao go hiking?
   A. During the Spring Festival.
   B. During the summer holiday.
   C. During the National Day holiday.
   D. During the winter holiday.

2. Why did Lin Hao get lost?
   A. He followed the wrong path.
   B. He ran after a butterfly and went too far.
   C. His father left him behind.
   D. It became too dark to see.

3. What did Lin Hao do after he realized he was lost?
   A. He kept running through the forest.
   B. He sat down and cried.
   C. He climbed onto a rock and shouted for help.
   D. He called his father on the phone.

4. How did Lin Hao's father feel when he found his son?
   A. Angry and disappointed.
   B. Worried but relieved.
   C. Surprised and confused.
   D. Sad and hopeless.

5. What lesson did Lin Hao learn from this experience?
   A. Hiking is too dangerous for children.
   B. Butterflies should not be chased.
   C. Staying calm and thinking clearly can help in difficult situations.
   D. He should never go hiking with his father again.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "During the National Day holiday, twelve-year-old Lin Hao went hiking with his father"

**【解析】** 原文明确说是国庆假期。A项"春节"、B项"暑假"和D项"寒假"均与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Lin Hao noticed a butterfly with bright blue wings and ran after it. He chased it deeper and deeper into the trees."

**【解析】** 原文明确说林浩追蝴蝶追得太远才迷路的。A项"走错路"不是迷路原因；C项"父亲把他丢下"与原文不符；D项"天黑了看不见"原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Lin Hao took a deep breath and climbed onto a large rock. He shouted, 'Dad! I'm here!' again and again."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述他爬上大石头并不停呼喊。A项"继续在森林里跑"与"stay where you are"矛盾；B项"坐下哭"虽然想哭但并未这样做；D项"给父亲打电话"原文未提及手机。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "He looked worried but relieved."

**【解析】** 原文直接描述父亲的表情是"担心但松了一口气"。A项"愤怒失望"原文无此描述；C项"惊讶困惑"不准确；D项"悲伤绝望"与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "when you are afraid, staying calm and thinking clearly can help you find a way out."

**【解析】** 文章结尾明确点题：在困难面前保持冷静、清晰思考能帮助你找到出路。A项"徒步对孩子太危险"以偏概全；B项"不应该追蝴蝶"虽有相关但不是核心教训；D项"再也不和父亲去徒步"与故事传递的正面信息相悖。故选C。
