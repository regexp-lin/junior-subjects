# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Sleep is one of the most important things for our health, yet many teenagers do not get enough of it. Experts say that middle school students need 8 to 10 hours of sleep every night. However, studies show that many Chinese students sleep only 6 to 7 hours because of homework, mobile phones, and early school start times.

② Why is sleep so important? First, sleep helps the brain work properly. During sleep, the brain organizes the information learned during the day and stores it in long-term memory. This is why students who sleep well often do better on exams. Second, sleep is necessary for physical growth. The body releases (释放) growth hormones (生长激素) mainly during deep sleep. For growing teenagers, this is especially important. Third, good sleep strengthens the immune system (免疫系统), which helps the body fight illness.

③ What happens when we do not get enough sleep? Lack of sleep can lead to many problems. Students may find it hard to concentrate (集中注意力) in class. They may feel moody, easily annoyed, or even sad. Over time, poor sleep can cause weight gain because the body produces more hormones that make us feel hungry. In serious cases, long-term sleep loss can weaken the heart and increase the risk of disease.

④ So how can teenagers improve their sleep? First, try to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends. This helps set the body's internal clock (生物钟). Second, avoid using phones or computers for at least 30 minutes before bed. The blue light from screens can trick the brain into thinking it is still daytime. Third, keep the bedroom dark, quiet, and cool. A comfortable environment makes it easier to fall asleep.

⑤ Sleep is not a waste of time — it is fuel (燃料) for the body and mind. By making small changes to daily habits, students can sleep better and feel more energetic (精力充沛的) during the day. Remember, a good night's sleep is the first step to a good day.

**注释：** hormone 激素；immune system 免疫系统；concentrate 集中注意力；internal clock 生物钟；fuel 燃料；energetic 精力充沛的

**Questions:**

1. How many hours of sleep do experts say middle school students need?
   A. 5 to 6 hours.
   B. 6 to 7 hours.
   C. 8 to 10 hours.
   D. 10 to 12 hours.

2. According to Paragraph ②, why does sleep help students do better on exams?
   A. Because sleep makes students forget useless information.
   B. Because the brain organizes and stores information during sleep.
   C. Because students can study in their dreams.
   D. Because sleep makes the exam questions easier.

3. What is one result of not getting enough sleep?
   A. Students become taller.
   B. Students feel more energetic.
   C. Students may find it hard to concentrate in class.
   D. Students become better at sports.

4. Why should teenagers avoid using phones before bed?
   A. Because phones are too expensive.
   B. Because blue light can affect the brain and make it hard to sleep.
   C. Because parents do not allow it.
   D. Because phones will break if used at night.

5. What does the writer mean by "Sleep is not a waste of time — it is fuel for the body and mind"?
   A. Sleeping is boring and useless.
   B. Sleep provides energy and is very important for health.
   C. Students should sleep during class.
   D. Fuel is more important than sleep.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Experts say that middle school students need 8 to 10 hours of sleep every night."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出专家建议初中生每晚需要8到10小时的睡眠。A项"5-6小时"过少；B项"6-7小时"是实际睡眠时间而非建议；D项"10-12小时"过多。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "During sleep, the brain organizes the information learned during the day and stores it in long-term memory. This is why students who sleep well often do better on exams."

**【解析】** 原文说明睡眠时大脑会整理和储存白天学到的信息，因此睡眠好的学生考试表现更好。A项"忘记无用信息"偷换概念；C项"在梦中学习"属于无中生有；D项"让考题更简单"属于无中生有。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Students may find it hard to concentrate in class."

**【解析】** 原文列举了睡眠不足的后果之一是难以在课堂上集中注意力。A项"变高"与"睡眠不足"的负面影响相反；B项"更有精力"与文意相反；D项"体育更好"原文未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "The blue light from screens can trick the brain into thinking it is still daytime."

**【解析】** 原文解释屏幕蓝光会让大脑误以为还是白天，从而影响入睡，因此建议睡前避免使用手机。A项"手机太贵"无关；C项"父母不允许"原文未提及；D项"手机晚上会坏"属于无中生有。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "Sleep is not a waste of time — it is fuel for the body and mind."

**【解析】** 作者用"燃料"比喻睡眠为身体和大脑提供能量，强调睡眠非常重要。A项"睡觉无聊没用"与作者观点完全相反；C项"学生应在课堂上睡觉"属于曲解；D项"燃料比睡眠重要"误解了比喻含义。故选B。
