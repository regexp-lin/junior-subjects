# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should high schools start classes later in the morning? Currently, most high schools begin around 7:30 or 8:00 AM. This early start means many students have to wake up at 6:00 AM or even earlier. While early classes have been the tradition for decades, I argue that pushing the start time to 8:30 AM or 9:00 AM would be much better for teenagers' health and learning.

② The primary (首要的) reason for a later start time relates to teenagers' natural sleep cycles. Medical experts point out that as children become teenagers, their biological clocks (生物钟) change. They naturally feel tired later at night, usually around 11:00 PM, and need to sleep until 8:00 AM to get enough rest. Forcing them to wake up at 6:00 AM goes against their biology, making them chronically (长期地) sleep-deprived.

③ Secondly, sleep-deprived teenagers cannot learn well. When students sitting in the first period class are half-asleep, trying desperately to keep their eyes open, they are not absorbing (吸收) information. Research shows that schools which moved their start times later have seen great improvements in students' grades. When students are well-rested, their concentration improves, their memories are sharper, and they are more active in discussions.

④ Moreover, a lack of sleep affects not only grades but also mental health (心理健康) and safety. Teenagers who don't get enough sleep are more likely to suffer from anxiety (焦虑) and depression. Also, for those older teenagers who drive themselves to school, driving while sleepy is a huge risk that can lead to traffic accidents.

⑤ Opponents (反对者) say late start times will mess up the bus schedules and parents' work routines. However, these administrative (行政的) problems can be solved with careful planning. The health and future success of our students are far more important than keeping an outdated schedule. It is time to let teenagers sleep a little longer.

**注释：** tradition 传统；primary 首要的；biological clock 生物钟；absorb 吸收；anxiety 焦虑；opponent 反对者；schedule 日程表

---

### 选择题

1. What is the writer's main opinion in paragraph ①?
A. High schools should start classes even earlier, like at 6:00 AM.
B. The traditional start time of 7:30 AM is perfect for teenagers.
C. Starting classes later (like 8:30 or 9:00 AM) would be better for teenagers' health and learning.
D. High schools should remove morning classes totally.

2. According to medical experts in paragraph ②, why do teenagers naturally need to sleep later in the morning?
A. Because they spend too much time watching TV at night.
B. Because they have too much homework to do every evening.
C. Because their biological clocks change, making them feel tired later at night.
D. Because they eat too much sugar before going to bed.

3. How does starting school later affect students' learning according to paragraph ③?
A. It makes them forget all the information they learned the day before.
B. It improves their grades because their concentration and memory become sharper.
C. It makes them talk too much and disturb the teachers during discussions.
D. It has no effect on their learning at all.

4. What is the danger for older teenagers who lack sleep and drive to school?
A. They might forget where the school is.
B. They might drive while sleepy, which is a huge risk for traffic accidents.
C. They might drive too fast to show off their cars.
D. They might fall asleep before they even leave the house.

5. How does the writer respond to the opponents' (反对者的) arguments in the last paragraph?
A. The writer agrees with them that bus schedules are more important than sleep.
B. The writer says that administrative problems can be solved and students' health is more important.
C. The writer suggests that parents should change their jobs.
D. The writer thinks opponents are completely foolish and refuses to answer them.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 作者观点初定/细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "...I argue that pushing the start time to 8:30 AM or 9:00 AM would be much better for teenagers' health and learning."

**【解析】** 开卷点明主题：考虑到现在极早的开学时间，作者主张将上课时间推迟到早上8点半或9点，这对青少年的健康和学习会更好。这正是C项的表述。起得更早（A）、传统就好（B）或取消早课（D）均非其主张。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2-3句 "Medical experts point out that... their biological clocks (生物钟) change. They naturally feel tired later at night... and need to sleep until 8:00 AM..."

**【解析】** 医学专家为何指出青少年天然需要晚睡晚起？原文说是因为随着成长，他们的生物钟发生了改变，导致他们晚上更晚（11点左右）才感到困倦。这与C选项完全吻合。A看电视、B作业多、D吃糖多都不是专家指出的生理根本原因。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3-4句 "Research shows that schools which moved their start times later have seen great improvements in students' grades. When students are well-rested, their concentration improves, their memories are sharper..."

**【解析】** 推迟上课对学习有什么影响？第三段写明，研究表明推迟上课的学校发现学生的成绩有了巨大提升（improvements in students' grades），因为休息得好，他们的专注力提升，记忆力更敏锐（sharper）。B项准确复述了这些好处。A遗忘、C过多说话扰乱课堂、D毫无影响，均与文意相悖。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "Also, for those older teenagers who drive themselves to school, driving while sleepy is a huge risk that can lead to traffic accidents."

**【解析】** 缺乏睡眠对于那些自己开车上学的年长青少年有何危险？原文直言：疲劳驾驶是一个会导致交通事故（traffic accidents）的巨大风险。选项B就是这个意思的重现。A忘路、C飙车炫耀、D出门前就睡着等都不是文中提出的驾车风险。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1-3句 "Opponents say late start times will mess up the bus schedules and parents' work routines. However, these administrative problems can be solved... The health and future success of our students are far more important..."

**【解析】** 作者在末段如何反驳反对者（关于打乱公交表和家长作息）的观点的？作者回应说，这些行政上的问题（administrative problems）是可以通过仔细规划来解决的，而学生的健康比维持过时的时间表重要得多。此反驳逻辑与B项一致。A同意反方、C让家长换工作、D纯骂人不回，都不是文中采用的论辩方式。故选B。
