> **05-07 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Lost Pet Poster: Help us find Max!**

② Dear neighbors,

③ Our beloved family dog, Max, went missing near Maple Street on Friday evening, October 15th. We are heartbroken and need your help to bring him back home.

④ **Description of Max:**
- **Breed (品种):** Golden Retriever (金毛寻回犬)
- **Age and Size:** He is 3 years old and weighs about 30 kilograms. He is quite large but very gentle.
- **Appearance (外貌):** He has thick, light golden hair. There is a small white spot on his chest.
- **Accessories (配饰):** He was wearing a blue nylon collar (尼龙项圈) with a silver bone-shaped name tag. The tag has his name "MAX" and our phone number on it.

⑤ **Last Seen:**
Max was last seen playing with some children in the small park at the end of Maple Street around 6:30 PM. We think he might have chased a squirrel (松鼠) into the woods behind the park.

⑥ **Behavior (行为特点):**
Max is extremely friendly and loves children. He is not dangerous at all. If you call his name or offer him a treat (especially cheese), he will probably come to you wagging his tail.

⑦ **Reward (悬赏):**
We are offering a $200 reward for anyone who finds Max and brings him back safely, or gives us information that leads directly to his return.

⑧ **Contact Information:**
Please check your gardens and garages (车库). If you have seen a dog matching this description, please call Robert or Sarah at 555-0987 immediately. You can call anytime, day or night.

⑨ Thank you for keeping an eye out for our dear Max!

**注释：** breed 品种；chest 胸部；accessory 配饰；collar 项圈；squirrel 松鼠；garage 车库

---

### 选择题

1. What is the main purpose of this poster?
A. To sell a large Golden Retriever for $200.
B. To find a missing family dog named Max.
C. To invite neighbors to a dog training class.
D. To warn people about a dangerous dog on Maple Street.

2. Which of the following is NOT true about Max's appearance or accessories?
A. He has thick, light golden hair.
B. He has a small white spot on his chest.
C. He was wearing a red leather collar.
D. His name tag is shaped like a silver bone.

3. Where was Max last seen before he went missing?
A. In a garage on Maple Street at midnight.
B. In a pet shop buying cheese.
C. In the woods chasing a cat on Saturday morning.
D. In the small park at the end of Maple Street around 6:30 PM.

4. Form paragraph ⑥, what should you do if you see Max?
A. Run away quickly because he is very dangerous.
B. Throw water at him to make him stop.
C. Call his name or offer him a treat like cheese.
D. Hide in your house and wait for him to leave.

5. What will Robert and Sarah do if someone finds Max?
A. They will give that person a $200 reward.
B. They will buy the finder a new Golden Retriever.
C. They will cook a big meal with cheese for the neighbors.
D. They will call the police immediately.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨意图推断

**【定位】** 第①段标题 "Lost Pet Poster: Help us find Max!" 及第③段 "Our beloved family dog, Max, went missing... We are heartbroken and need your help to bring him back home."

**【解析】** 从标题“走失宠物海报”以及文中的求助信息可以看出，这张海报的主要目的是寻找走失的家庭宠物狗Max。A卖狗、C狗训练班、D警告有恶犬，均非原文意图。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解与判断

**【定位】** 第④段关于外貌和配饰的描写 "He has thick, light golden hair. There is a small white spot on his chest... wearing a blue nylon collar with a silver bone-shaped name tag."

**【解析】** 题目要求找出关于Max外貌或配饰描述**不正确**的一项。A（浅金毛发）、B（胸口白斑）、D（银色骨头状名牌）在文中均有明确对应。而C选项说他戴着红皮项圈（red leather collar），但原文写的是“蓝色尼龙项圈（blue nylon collar）”。本题选NOT的项，故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段首句 "Max was last seen playing with some children in the small park at the end of Maple Street around 6:30 PM."

**【解析】** Max最后一次被看到是在哪儿？文中清晰写到，是在下午6点半左右（around 6:30 PM），枫树街尽头的小公园里（in the small park at the end of Maple Street）和孩子们玩耍。这直接与D选项吻合。A车库半夜、B宠物店买奶酪、C树林追猫均不是正确答案。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段第2-3句 "He is not dangerous at all. If you call his name or offer him a treat (especially cheese), he will probably come to you..."

**【解析】** 如果你看到Max该怎么办？行为特点一栏提到，他根本不危险（排除A逃命和D躲屋里），如果你呼喊他的名字或者给他一点零食（尤其是奶酪cheese），他很可能摇着尾巴就过来了。选项C准确给出了应对建议。故选C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "We are offering a $200 reward for anyone who finds Max and brings him back safely..."

**【解析】** 罗伯特和莎拉承诺如果有人找到狗会怎样？文中讲了悬赏金：为找到并安全带回Max的人提供200美元奖金（a $200 reward）。选项A与原文相符。B买新狗、C请吃奶酪大餐、D报警均错误。故选A。
