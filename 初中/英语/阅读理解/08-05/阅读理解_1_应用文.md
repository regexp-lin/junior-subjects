# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Notice: E-reader Borrowing at the School Library**

② Dear students, our school library now offers e-readers (电子阅读器) for borrowing. This service helps you read e-books in a more comfortable way. You can borrow one e-reader at a time from the library.

③ **Borrowing rules:** You must show your student card when borrowing. Each student can keep an e-reader for up to 14 days. If you need to keep it longer, you may renew (续借) it once online or at the library counter, unless someone else has reserved it. Late returns will cost 1 yuan per day.

④ **How to borrow:** Visit the library counter between 8:00 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. from Monday to Friday. Our staff will help you choose an e-reader and explain how to use it. Remember to return it in good condition. Do not drop it or get it wet. If the e-reader is damaged or lost, you must pay for the repair or replacement.

⑤ Please take care of the e-readers so more students can enjoy this service. For questions, call the library at 020-88881234 or send an e-mail to library@school.edu.cn.

**注释：** e-reader 电子阅读器；renew 续借

---

### 选择题

1. Who can borrow e-readers from the school library?
   A. Teachers and staff only.
   B. Any student with a student card.
   C. Only students in Grade 9.
   D. Parents of students.

2. How long can a student keep an e-reader at most if he or she renews it once?
   A. 7 days.
   B. 14 days.
   C. 28 days.
   D. 30 days.

3. What will happen if a student returns the e-reader late?
   A. He or she cannot borrow again.
   B. He or she will pay 1 yuan per day.
   C. The student card will be taken away.
   D. He or she must buy a new e-reader.

4. When can students go to the library to borrow e-readers?
   A. Any day of the week.
   B. Saturday and Sunday only.
   C. Monday to Friday, 8:00 a.m.—5:30 p.m.
   D. Every day from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Students can borrow as many e-readers as they want at once.
   B. The library hopes students take good care of the e-readers so others can use them.
   C. Students do not need to show any ID when borrowing.
   D. E-readers can be returned even if they are damaged.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "You must show your student card when borrowing"；第②段 "our school library now offers e-readers for borrowing"

**【解析】** 文中明确要求借阅时出示学生证，且服务面向本校学生。A项正确。B项仅限教职员工、C项仅限九年级、D项家长均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Each student can keep an e-reader for up to 14 days. If you need to keep it longer, you may renew it once"

**【解析】** 首次借阅最多14天，续借一次可再延长14天，共28天。B项正确。A项7天、C项14天仅为首次期限、D项30天超出规定。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Late returns will cost 1 yuan per day"

**【解析】** 逾期归还需每天支付1元。C项正确。A、B、D项文中均未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Visit the library counter between 8:00 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. from Monday to Friday"

**【解析】** 借阅时间为周一至周五上午8:00至下午5:30。D项正确。A、B、C项与原文时间不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Please take care of the e-readers so more students can enjoy this service"

**【解析】** 文中呼吁学生爱护电子阅读器，以便更多学生能使用该服务，可推知图书馆希望学生妥善保管、共享资源。A项正确。B项与"one e-reader at a time"矛盾；C项与"show your student card"矛盾；D项与"return it in good condition"及损坏需赔偿的规定矛盾。故选A。
