# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should We Have PE Class Every Day?**

② In many schools in China, students have physical education (PE) class two or three times a week. Some people think this is not enough. They say schools should hold PE class every day. Others believe that daily PE would take too much time from other subjects. Let us look at both sides of the argument.

③ **Those who support daily PE** argue that exercise is important for students' health. Young people today spend a lot of time sitting in class and doing homework. Many of them do not get enough exercise. Research shows that regular physical activity helps students stay healthy, sleep better, and even do better in their studies. Supporters also point out that PE class teaches teamwork and builds good habits. If students exercise every day, they are more likely to stay active when they grow up. A healthy body leads to a healthy mind (健康的身体带来健康的心态).

④ **Those who disagree** say that schools already have a busy schedule. Adding PE to every day would mean less time for maths, Chinese, English, and other important subjects. Some students and parents worry that students' grades might go down. Besides, not every school has enough sports facilities (设施) or PE teachers. Daily PE would require more space, equipment, and staff. Critics also argue that two or three PE classes a week, plus exercise at home, may already be enough if students use the time well.

⑤ **In my view,** having PE class every day is a good idea, but schools need to plan it well. PE does not have to be a full 45-minute class every day. Schools could add short activity breaks (活动时间), such as 15 to 20 minutes of running or simple exercises between classes. In this way, students get more movement without losing much study time. At the same time, schools should improve their sports facilities and train more PE teachers. Health and study are both important. We should find a balance between them.

**注释：** facility 设施；physical activity 体育锻炼

### 选择题

1. According to supporters of daily PE, what are the benefits of regular exercise for students?
   A. Better health, better sleep, and better performance in studies.
   B. Fewer homework tasks.
   C. Longer lunch breaks.
   D. More time to play video games.

2. What do those who oppose daily PE worry about?
   A. Students will become too tired.
   B. Parents will complain about the weather.
   C. PE teachers will earn too much money.
   D. Less time for other subjects, possible lower grades, and lack of enough facilities and teachers.

3. What does the writer suggest schools do?
   A. Add short activity breaks of 15 to 20 minutes between classes, and improve facilities and teacher training.
   B. Remove PE class completely.
   C. Make PE class 2 hours long every day.
   D. Only offer PE class on weekends.

4. Why does the writer think short activity breaks are a good idea?
   A. Because they give teachers more free time.
   B. Because students get more movement without losing much study time.
   C. Because they cost less money.
   D. Because students dislike long PE classes.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell students to avoid all sports.
   B. To introduce different kinds of sports.
   C. To encourage students to become PE teachers.
   D. To discuss whether schools should have PE class every day and present different views with the writer's opinion.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Research shows that regular physical activity helps students stay healthy, sleep better, and even do better in their studies"

**【解析】** 支持者引用研究指出，规律运动有助于学生保持健康、改善睡眠、提高学业表现。A项准确概括。B、C、D项均与支持者观点不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Adding PE to every day would mean less time for maths, Chinese, English... Some students and parents worry that students' grades might go down... not every school has enough sports facilities or PE teachers... Daily PE would require more space, equipment, and staff"

**【解析】** 反对者指出：每日体育会占用其他科目时间；家长和学生担心成绩下降；学校可能缺乏场地、设施和体育教师；实施每日体育需要更多空间、设备和人员。D项全面概括。A、B、C项与原文不符。故选D。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Schools could add short activity breaks, such as 15 to 20 minutes of running or simple exercises between classes... schools should improve their sports facilities and train more PE teachers"

**【解析】** 作者建议增加课间15–20分钟的短时活动，并改善体育设施、培训更多体育教师。A项正确。B、C、D项与作者建议矛盾。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In this way, students get more movement without losing much study time"

**【解析】** 作者认为短时活动既能增加运动量，又不会占用太多学习时间，兼顾健康与学业。B项正确。A、C、D项文中均未作为主要原因提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全篇，尤其是第②段 "Some people think... Others believe... Let us look at both sides" 及第⑤段作者观点

**【解析】** 文章围绕"体育课是否应每日开设"展开讨论，呈现支持与反对观点，并给出作者的折中建议。D项准确概括写作目的。A、B、C项与文章主旨不符。故选D。
