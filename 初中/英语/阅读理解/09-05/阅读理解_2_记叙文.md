# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① It was a rainy afternoon in autumn. Lisa sat by the window, watching the heavy rain fall. School had just ended, and she was worried. Her best friend Emma did not bring an umbrella today. Emma's home was far from the school, and the bus stop was about ten minutes away. Lisa knew that Emma would get very wet if she walked to the bus stop alone. Without thinking twice, Lisa decided to do something.

② She took her umbrella and ran out into the rain. The wind was strong and blew cold drops onto her face. Lisa did not mind. She hurried along the path to the bus stop and saw Emma standing there under the small shelter, wet and cold. Emma looked surprised when Lisa handed her the umbrella and asked if she was alright.

③ "Why did you come?" Emma asked. "Your shoes and coat are all wet now. You will catch a cold."

④ "You're my friend," Lisa said with a warm smile. "I couldn't let you walk home in the rain alone. We can share this umbrella together."

⑤ Emma felt very touched. She thanked Lisa and they walked to the bus side by side. On the bus, Emma said, "You are the kindest person I know. I will never forget what you did today." From that day on, their friendship became even stronger. Sometimes, a small act of kindness can mean the world to someone.

**注释：** touched 感动的

---

**Questions:**

1. Why was Lisa worried that afternoon?
   A. She forgot her homework at school.
   B. Emma did not bring an umbrella and had to walk in the rain.
   C. The bus would not come because of the rain.
   D. She could not find her own umbrella.

2. What did Lisa do to help Emma?
   A. She called Emma's parents to pick her up.
   B. She lent Emma some dry clothes.
   C. She took her umbrella and went to meet Emma at the bus stop.
   D. She stayed with Emma until the rain stopped.

3. How did Emma feel when Lisa gave her the umbrella?
   A. Angry because Lisa was late.
   B. Surprised and then touched.
   C. Sad because she got wet.
   D. Afraid of the heavy rain.

4. What can we infer about Lisa's character?
   A. She cares about her friends and is willing to help them.
   B. She is always late for the bus.
   C. She does not like rainy days.
   D. She prefers to stay at home.

5. What is the main message of the story?
   A. Rainy days are bad for friendships.
   B. A small act of kindness can strengthen friendship.
   C. Umbrellas are important on rainy days.
   D. Friends should always go to school together.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3-4句 "Her best friend Emma did not bring an umbrella today. Emma's home was far from the school, and the bus stop was about ten minutes away."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明Lisa担心是因为Emma没带伞，且家离学校远、到公交站要十分钟。B项准确概括。A项"忘记作业"、C项"公交车因雨不来"和D项"找不到自己的伞"均原文未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "She took her umbrella and ran out into the rain... She hurried to the bus stop and saw Emma standing there... Lisa handed her the umbrella."

**【解析】** 原文描述Lisa拿伞冒雨赶往公交站，把伞递给Emma。C项准确。A项"打电话叫Emma父母接"、B项"借干衣服"和D项"陪到雨停"均与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "Emma looked surprised when Lisa handed her the umbrella" 及第⑤段 "Emma felt very touched."

**【解析】** 原文先写Emma看到Lisa递伞时"surprised"，后写她"felt very touched"，由此可推知Emma先是惊讶，随后被感动。A、C、D项均与文中情感描写不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第①段 "Lisa decided to do something" 及第④段 Lisa的话 "You're my friend... I couldn't let you walk home in the rain alone."

**【解析】** Lisa主动冒雨送伞给朋友，并说"不能让朋友独自淋雨回家"，体现她关心朋友、乐于助人的品质。B、C、D项均无法从文中推知。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "Sometimes, a small act of kindness can mean the world to someone."

**【解析】** 文末点明主题：一个小小的善举对他人可能意义重大。B项"小小的善举能增进友谊"准确概括主旨。A项与主题相反；C项过于具体；D项非文章重点。故选B。
