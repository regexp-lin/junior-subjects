# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Butterflies are beautiful insects that we often see in gardens and parks. But have you ever wondered how they become so lovely? Butterflies go through a special change called metamorphosis (变态发育). This means they do not grow bigger simply — they change their whole body form from one stage to another.

② A butterfly's life starts as a tiny egg. The mother butterfly lays eggs on leaves where the young can find food easily. After a few days, a small caterpillar (毛毛虫) comes out of the egg. The caterpillar is the larva (幼虫) stage. It eats leaves all day and grows very fast. Some caterpillars can double their size in just one day.

③ When the caterpillar is big enough, it enters the pupa (蛹) stage. It builds a hard covering around itself called a chrysalis (蝶蛹). Inside the chrysalis, the caterpillar's body breaks down and changes completely. This process may take from one week to several months, depending on the type of butterfly.

④ Finally, the adult butterfly comes out of the chrysalis. Its wings are wet and folded at first. It must wait for the wings to dry and unfold before it can fly. Once ready, the butterfly can fly away to find flowers, drink nectar (花蜜), and start its own family by laying eggs. The life cycle then begins again.

⑤ Metamorphosis is important because each stage helps the butterfly survive in different ways. The egg keeps the young safe. The caterpillar focuses on eating and growing. The chrysalis protects the butterfly while it changes. And the adult butterfly can fly far to find food and a mate. This amazing process has helped butterflies live on Earth for millions of years.

**注释：** metamorphosis 变态发育；caterpillar 毛毛虫；larva 幼虫；pupa 蛹；chrysalis 蝶蛹；nectar 花蜜

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what does the caterpillar mainly do?
   A. Lay eggs on leaves.
   B. Build a chrysalis around itself.
   C. Eat leaves and grow quickly.
   D. Fly to find flowers.

2. What happens inside the chrysalis according to paragraph ③?
   A. The caterpillar sleeps and rests.
   B. The caterpillar's body breaks down and changes completely.
   C. The caterpillar eats more leaves.
   D. The caterpillar grows larger wings.

3. Why must a new butterfly wait before it can fly?
   A. It needs to find food first.
   B. It must build a nest.
   C. It is too tired to fly.
   D. Its wings are wet and folded at first.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ⑤ about metamorphosis?
   A. Each stage has its own purpose for the butterfly's survival.
   B. Only the adult stage is important.
   C. Butterflies do not need the caterpillar stage.
   D. The chrysalis stage is the longest.

5. What is the best title for this passage?
   A. How Butterflies Eat Leaves
   B. The Butterfly's Life Cycle: Metamorphosis
   C. Gardens and Parks
   D. Insects in Nature

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "The caterpillar is the larva (幼虫) stage. It eats leaves all day and grows very fast."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明毛毛虫是幼虫阶段，整天吃叶子并快速生长。A 项"在叶子上产卵"是雌蝶的行为；B 项"用蝶蛹包裹自己"属于蛹阶段；D 项"飞去找花"是成虫阶段的行为。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Inside the chrysalis, the caterpillar's body breaks down and changes completely."

**【解析】** 原文清楚指出在蝶蛹内部，毛毛虫的身体分解并完全改变。A 项"睡觉休息"文中未提及；C 项"吃更多叶子"与蛹阶段不符，蛹不进食；D 项"长更大的翅膀"表述不准确，原文强调整个身体的变化。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "Its wings are wet and folded at first. It must wait for the wings to dry and unfold before it can fly."

**【解析】** 原文说明刚羽化的蝴蝶翅膀是湿的且折叠的，必须等待翅膀变干并展开后才能飞行。A 项"需要先找食物"、B 项"必须筑巢"、C 项"太累飞不了"均与原文不符。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "Metamorphosis is important because each stage helps the butterfly survive in different ways."

**【解析】** 原文指出变态发育之所以重要，是因为每个阶段都以不同方式帮助蝴蝶生存。A 项"每个阶段都有其生存目的"与原文一致。B 项"只有成虫阶段重要"与原文矛盾；C 项"蝴蝶不需要毛毛虫阶段"错误；D 项"蛹阶段最长"原文未明确比较。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文各段：第①段引入变态发育概念，第②—④段依次介绍卵、幼虫、蛹、成虫四个阶段，第⑤段总结变态发育的意义。

**【解析】** 全文围绕蝴蝶的生命周期和变态发育过程展开，B 项"蝴蝶的生命周期：变态发育"最能概括主旨。A 项"蝴蝶如何吃叶子"以偏概全；C 项"花园和公园"过于宽泛；D 项"自然中的昆虫"范围过大。故选 B。
