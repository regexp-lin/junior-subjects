> **05-13 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① These days, more and more students use e-books (电子书) to study and read for fun. Some people think e-books will soon replace (取代) paper books completely. But is that really going to happen? Let's look at both sides.

② E-books have many advantages. First, they are very convenient (方便的). You can carry thousands of books on one small device (设备), such as a tablet or a phone. Second, e-books are often cheaper than paper books, and some are even free to download. Third, e-books are better for the environment because they save paper and reduce the number of trees cut down every year.

③ However, paper books also have their own strengths. Many readers say they enjoy the feeling of holding a real book and turning the pages. Studies have shown that people often remember information better when they read it on paper. Also, reading on a screen for a long time can hurt your eyes, while paper books do not have this problem. For young children especially, paper books with colorful pictures are more attractive (吸引人的) and easier to share with family.

④ Another important point is that not everyone can afford electronic devices. In some areas, families do not have tablets or computers at home. Paper books can be borrowed from libraries for free, and they do not need electricity (电) or the Internet to work. This makes them more accessible (容易获得的) to everyone.

⑤ In my opinion, both e-books and paper books have their place in our lives. Instead of saying one is better than the other, we should use both according to our needs. E-books are great for traveling and quick reading, while paper books are perfect for deep study and the joy of turning real pages. The most important thing is not what form a book takes, but that we keep the habit of reading.

**注释：** replace 取代；convenient 方便的；device 设备；download 下载；screen 屏幕；attractive 吸引人的；accessible 容易获得的；electricity 电

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what is one advantage of e-books?
   A. They smell nicer than paper books.
   B. They can be shared easily with family.
   C. They are often cheaper than paper books.
   D. They help people remember information better.

2. Why are paper books better for young children according to paragraph ③?
   A. Because paper books are always free.
   B. Because paper books with colorful pictures are more attractive and easier to share.
   C. Because children cannot use electronic devices.
   D. Because paper books are smaller and lighter.

3. What does the word "accessible" in paragraph ④ most probably mean?
   A. Expensive to buy.
   B. Hard to understand.
   C. Easy to get or use.
   D. Dangerous to touch.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④?
   A. All families in the world have computers at home.
   B. Libraries do not lend paper books anymore.
   C. Paper books are more practical for people who cannot afford electronic devices.
   D. E-books are the only choice for students in poor areas.

5. What is the writer's main opinion about e-books and paper books?
   A. E-books are much better and should replace paper books.
   B. Paper books are old-fashioned and should be thrown away.
   C. Both have their strengths, and we should use them according to our needs.
   D. Students should stop reading and do more sports instead.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "Second, e-books are often cheaper than paper books, and some are even free to download."

**【解析】** 原文列举电子书的优点之一是价格通常比纸质书便宜，有些甚至可以免费下载。A 项"闻起来更好"文中未提及；B 项"容易和家人分享"是第③段对纸质书的描述，张冠李戴；D 项"帮助记忆信息更好"恰恰是第③段中纸质书的优势。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "For young children especially, paper books with colorful pictures are more attractive and easier to share with family."

**【解析】** 原文说纸质书对幼儿来说更有吸引力，彩色图片更容易与家人分享。A 项"纸质书总是免费"过于绝对，原文未这样说；C 项"儿童不能使用电子设备"属于无中生有；D 项"更小更轻"文中未提及。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "Paper books can be borrowed from libraries for free, and they do not need electricity or the Internet to work. This makes them more accessible to everyone."

**【解析】** 前文提到纸质书可以免费从图书馆借，不需要电和网络，因此对所有人来说更"accessible"。结合上下文，accessible 意为"容易获得的/使用的"。A 项"购买昂贵"与上下文"免费"矛盾；B 项"难以理解"与语境不符；D 项"危险的"毫无关联。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "not everyone can afford electronic devices. In some areas, families do not have tablets or computers at home. Paper books can be borrowed from libraries for free..."

**【解析】** 原文指出不是所有人都买得起电子设备，有些地区家里没有平板或电脑，而纸质书可以从图书馆免费借到，因此可以推断纸质书对买不起电子设备的人来说更实用。A 项"所有家庭都有电脑"与原文直接矛盾；B 项"图书馆不再借纸质书"与"can be borrowed"矛盾；D 项"电子书是贫困地区学生的唯一选择"与原文论述相反。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, both e-books and paper books have their place in our lives. Instead of saying one is better than the other, we should use both according to our needs."

**【解析】** 作者在结论段明确表示两者各有优势，应根据需求搭配使用。A 项"电子书好得多应该取代纸质书"与作者观点矛盾；B 项"纸质书过时应该丢弃"同样偏激；D 项"学生应该停止阅读多运动"完全脱离主题。故选 C。
