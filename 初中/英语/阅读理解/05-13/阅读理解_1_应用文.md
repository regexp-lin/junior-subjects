# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Sunshine City Library — Summer Reading Challenge 2026**

② Love reading? Join our Summer Reading Challenge and win amazing prizes (奖品)! The challenge runs from **July 1 to August 31**. It is open to all students aged 10–15.

③ **How to Join:**
- Visit Sunshine City Library or go to our website: *www.sunshinelibrary.org/summer*
- Sign up for free and get your **Reading Passport** (阅读护照)
- Every time you finish a book, bring it to the library and tell a librarian (图书管理员) about it — they will stamp (盖章) your passport

④ **Prize Levels:**

| Stamps | Prize |
|--------|-------|
| 5 stamps | A cool bookmark set |
| 10 stamps | A mystery book bag (a surprise book chosen for you!) |
| 15 stamps | A $20 gift card for City Bookstore |
| 20 stamps | A **Gold Reader Certificate** (金牌读者证书) + invitation to the Reading Star Party on September 5 |

⑤ **Special Events During the Challenge:**
- **July 10 (Saturday), 2:00 PM:** Meet the Author — Children's writer Sarah Hill will talk about her new book *The Secret Garden Club* and answer questions.
- **August 5 (Wednesday), 10:00 AM:** Book Swap Day — Bring a book you've finished and trade (交换) it for another one. One book per person.
- **Every Friday, 4:00 PM:** Reading Circle — Join a group discussion about the book of the week. Snacks provided!

⑥ For more information, call us at **555-3210** or email **read@sunshinelibrary.org**. Let's make this the best summer ever — one book at a time!

**注释：** prize 奖品；passport 护照；librarian 图书管理员；stamp 盖章；certificate 证书；mystery 神秘的；swap 交换

### 选择题

1. Who can join the Summer Reading Challenge?
   A. Only children under 10 years old.
   B. Students aged 10 to 15.
   C. Adults who love reading.
   D. Only members of Sunshine City Library.

2. What do you need to do after finishing a book?
   A. Write a book report and mail it to the library.
   B. Return the book and pay a small fee.
   C. Bring the book to the library and tell a librarian about it.
   D. Post a review on the library website.

3. How many stamps do you need to get a $20 gift card?
   A. 5 stamps.
   B. 10 stamps.
   C. 15 stamps.
   D. 20 stamps.

4. What will happen on August 5?
   A. A famous writer will give a talk about her new book.
   B. Students can exchange books with each other.
   C. There will be a Reading Star Party for all participants.
   D. The library will give away free snacks to everyone.

5. What is the purpose of this passage?
   A. To sell books at a lower price.
   B. To invite students to join a summer reading activity.
   C. To teach students how to write book reports.
   D. To introduce the history of Sunshine City Library.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "It is open to all students aged 10–15."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明该活动面向所有10至15岁的学生。A 项"只有10岁以下儿童"年龄范围错误；C 项"喜爱阅读的成人"不符合年龄要求；D 项"只限图书馆会员"原文未提及此限制，报名是免费的。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Every time you finish a book, bring it to the library and tell a librarian about it — they will stamp your passport"

**【解析】** 原文要求读完书后把书带到图书馆，向管理员讲述书的内容，管理员会在护照上盖章。A 项"写读书报告并邮寄"、B 项"还书并付费"和 D 项"在网站上发评论"均属于无中生有。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段奖品表格 "15 stamps — A $20 gift card for City Bookstore"

**【解析】** 根据表格，15个印章可获得价值20美元的书店礼品卡。A 项5个印章对应书签套装；B 项10个印章对应神秘书袋；D 项20个印章对应金牌读者证书和派对邀请。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "August 5 (Wednesday), 10:00 AM: Book Swap Day — Bring a book you've finished and trade it for another one."

**【解析】** 8月5日是图书交换日，学生可以带一本读完的书来交换另一本。A 项描述的是7月10日的作家见面会，张冠李戴；C 项阅读之星派对在9月5日；D 项免费零食是每周五阅读圈的活动。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文整体：标题"Summer Reading Challenge 2026"，第②段发出邀请，第③-⑤段介绍参与方式和活动。

**【解析】** 全文以通知/海报形式，介绍暑期阅读挑战活动的参与方式、奖品和特别活动，目的是邀请学生参加这一阅读活动。A 项"低价卖书"文中未涉及售书；C 项"教写读书报告"文中未提及；D 项"介绍图书馆历史"与内容不符。故选 B。
