> **04-02 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **LOST DOG: PLEASE HELP!**

② We lost our beloved family dog, Max, on the afternoon of April 1st. He ran away while we were playing in Central Park near the lake. We have been looking for him everywhere but still cannot find him.

③ **What does Max look like?**
- He is a clever Golden Retriever (金毛犬).
- He is three years old and weighs about 25 kilos.
- He has light golden hair and big brown eyes.
- He is wearing a blue collar (项圈) with his name and a phone number on it.

④ **Reward (酬金):**
We are offering a reward of $500 for anyone who finds Max and brings him back safely. He is not just a pet; he is a part of our family. Our children are crying every day because they miss him so much.

⑤ **Contact Information:**
If you see a dog that looks like Max, please do not catch him. He might be scared. Just call us right away at **555-0198** or email us at **findmax@email.com**. Any information will be helpful!

Thank you for your help!

**注释：** beloved 挚爱的；collar 项圈

---

### 选择题

1. Where was Max lost?
A. At the children's school.
B. In Central Park near the lake.
C. On his way to the animal hospital.
D. Near a famous museum.

2. Which of the following describes (描述) Max?
A. He is five years old.
B. He has black hair and blue eyes.
C. He weighs about 15 kilos.
D. He wears a blue collar with a name and a number on it.

3. Why are the children in the family crying every day?
A. Because they have too much homework.
B. Because they were bitten by a dog.
C. Because they miss their dog very much.
D. Because they don't have $500.

4. According to the notice (启事), what should you do if you see Max?
A. Feed him some delicious food immediately.
B. Catch him and bring him back carefully.
C. Run away and call the police.
D. Call the number right away.

5. What is the reward for finding Max?
A. $100.
B. $200.
C. $500.
D. $1000.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "He ran away while we were playing in Central Park near the lake."

**【解析】** 原文表明 Max 是在原主人带他在中央公园（靠近湖边）玩耍时跑丢的。A项（在孩子们学校里）、C项（去动物医院路上）、D项（博物馆附近）均属子虚乌有。故选B。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中的各项列表特征。

**【解析】** 本题考查 Max 的外貌特征。A项错误，原文说三岁（three years old）；B项错误，原文说浅金色毛发和大棕眼（light golden hair and big brown eyes）；C项错误，原文说大约重25公斤（25 kilos）。D项符合，戴着蓝色项圈上面有名字和电话。故选D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "Our children are crying every day because they miss him so much."

**【解析】** 原文说明孩子们每天哭诉是因为他们太想念他们的狗了。A项（有很多作业）、B项（被狗咬了）、D项（因为没有500美金）既不符合原文，更违背主人寻狗的心情。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断 / 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1-3句 "If you see a dog that looks like Max, please do not catch him. He might be scared. Just call us right away at 555-0198..."

**【解析】** 原文明确提醒看到像Max的狗“不要去抓他”，因为他可能会受惊害怕。给出的建议动作是“马上打电话”。A项未提及喂食；B项的“catch him”与原文“不要抓”的要求正好相反；C项没有说要逃跑和报案。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "We are offering a reward of $500 for anyone who finds Max and brings him back safely."

**【解析】** 原文中“Reward”这部分提供给任何找到并安全送回Max的人的酬金是500美元。A、B、D皆不符合数值。故选C。
