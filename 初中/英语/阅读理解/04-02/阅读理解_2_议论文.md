# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In the digital age, more and more people choose to read on electronic screens like e-readers, tablets or smartphones. However, I still believe that reading printed (印刷的) books is the best choice for us.

② First, reading printed books is much better for our eyes. The light from electronic screens can make our eyes feel tired and dry quickly. If we stare at screens for too long before bed, it can even cause sleep problems. Printed books do not have this problem. They give us a much more comfortable reading experience.

③ Second, printed books help us focus better. When we read on a phone or tablet, it is very easy to be distracted (分散注意力的). For example, a message from a friend or an interesting game can easily stop us from reading. But a printed book has no such things. We can dive deep into the story without any interruptions.

④ Finally, holding a real book gives us a special feeling that an e-reader cannot give. The smell of paper and the sound of turning pages make reading a real pleasure. 

⑤ In conclusion, although electronic reading is very convenient, reading printed books should still be an important part of our daily life. Let's pick up a real book and enjoy the beauty of reading.

**注释：** electronic 电子的；distracted 分散注意力的；interruption 打断

### 选择题

1. What do many people choose to read on in the modern digital age?
A. Electronic screens.
B. Old newspapers.
C. Thick dictionaries.
D. Only printed books.

2. Why is reading printed books better for our eyes according to the writer?
A. Because printed books have brighter colors.
B. Because printed books don't have screen light that makes eyes tired.
C. Because printed books have bigger words.
D. Because our eyes like the smell of paper.

3. What does the underlined word "distracted" in paragraph ③ mean?
A. Made to feel very angry.
B. Encouraged to study hard.
C. Stopped from paying attention to what you are doing.
D. Allowed to go out to play.

4. What can you infer (推断) from paragraph ③?
A. Printed books are much cheaper than phones.
B. Reading on a phone makes it hard to focus on the story.
C. We should not have any friends when we read.
D. Interesting games are better than books.

5. What is the writer's attitude (态度) towards reading printed books?
A. He thinks it is a waste of time.
B. He believes it is too expensive for most people.
C. He thinks it should be replaced by e-readers completely.
D. He believes it is the best choice and encourages people to do it.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "...more and more people choose to read on electronic screens like e-readers, tablets or smartphones."

**【解析】** 由第一句可知，数字时代越来越多人选择在电子屏幕（electronic screens）上阅读。B项（老报纸）、C项（厚字典）、D项（只有纸质书）都不符合现在的阅读趋势或原文表述。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "The light from electronic screens can make our eyes feel tired and dry quickly... Printed books do not have this problem."

**【解析】** 作者认为读纸质书对眼睛好，是因为电子屏幕发出的光会让眼睛很快感到疲劳和干涩，而纸质书没有这个问题。A项（颜色更鲜艳）、C项（字更大）、D项（眼睛喜欢纸的味道）均非文中解释。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "When we read on a phone or tablet, it is very easy to be distracted..." 及后文举例（比如朋友的消息或游戏会让人停止阅读）。

**【解析】** 从紧接着的例子（朋友发消息或有趣的游戏轻易打断阅读大盘）可以猜测出 "distracted" 的意思是注意力被分散、被打断。A项（感到生气）、B项（被鼓励去学习）、D项（获准出去玩）皆与语境不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段整段论述：“使用手机阅读很容易注意力被分散”。

**【解析】** 根据本段主旨，可以推断在使用手机等设备时由于外界干扰（消息、游戏等），人们很难保持专注来读故事。A项（纸质书比手机便宜）、C项（我们读书时不该有朋友）、D项（有趣的游戏比书更好）都不符合这段论证或者不切合实际。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 作者意图 / 态度题

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "...reading printed books is the best choice for us."，以及第⑤段最后呼吁 "Let's pick up a real book..."

**【解析】** 通读全文，特别是首尾段落，作者一再强调阅读纸质书籍仍然对我们至关重要，是最佳选择，并在段尾鼓励人们去阅读真正的纸质书籍。这明显展现了他对纸质阅读的积极和鼓励态度。A项浪费时间、B项太贵、C项完全被电子书替代，均与作者所持立场背道而驰。故选D。
