# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Have you ever looked closely at a butterfly? Butterflies are some of the most beautiful insects in the world. They have colorful wings with amazing patterns (图案). However, a butterfly does not start its life looking like this. Its life has four very different stages (阶段).

② The first stage is the egg. A mother butterfly lays her tiny eggs on a leaf. Usually, the eggs are very small and round. After a few days, the second stage begins. A hungry caterpillar (毛毛虫) comes out of the egg. The caterpillar's only job is to eat. It eats the leaf it was born on and then moves to other leaves. As it eats, it grows very fast.

③ When the caterpillar is big enough, it stops eating and starts the third stage. It hangs upside down from a branch and forms a hard shell (壳) around itself. This shell is called a chrysalis (蛹). Inside the chrysalis, a magical change is happening. The caterpillar's body is changing completely. 

④ After a week or two, the chrysalis opens, and the final stage begins. A beautiful butterfly comes out! At first, its wings are wet and soft. It must wait for the sun to dry its wings before it can fly. Once its wings are ready, the butterfly takes off to find flowers and drink sweet nectar (花蜜).

⑤ This amazing process is called metamorphosis (变态发育). It shows us how wonderful and magical nature can be. The next time you see a butterfly in a garden, remember the long journey it took to get there.

**注释：** stage 阶段；caterpillar 毛毛虫；chrysalis 蛹；nectar 花蜜；metamorphosis 变态发育

---

### 选择题

1. What does a butterfly look like in the first stage of its life?
A. A beautiful insect with colorful wings.
B. A hungry caterpillar.
C. A hard shell called a chrysalis.
D. A tiny, round egg on a leaf.

2. What is the only job of a caterpillar according to paragraph ②?
A. To fly around the garden.
B. To drink sweet nectar from flowers.
C. To eat leaves and grow fast.
D. To hang upside down from a branch.

3. What happens inside the chrysalis (蛹)?
A. The caterpillar is sleeping peacefully forever.
B. The caterpillar's body is changing completely.
C. The mother butterfly is laying eggs.
D. The caterpillar is eating a lot of leaves.

4. Form the text, what must a new butterfly do before it can fly?
A. It must wait for the sun to dry its wet and soft wings.
B. It must find some sweet nectar to drink immediately.
C. It must learn how to walk on the leaves.
D. It must find another butterfly to guide it.

5. What does the word "metamorphosis" mean in the last paragraph?
A. The process of a butterfly flying in the sky.
B. The amazing process of an egg changing into a butterfly.
C. The sweet liquid (液体) that butterflies drink from flowers.
D. The colorful patterns on a butterfly's wings.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1-2句 "The first stage is the egg. A mother butterfly lays her tiny eggs on a leaf."

**【解析】** 原文中明确表示，蝴蝶生命的第一阶段是卵（egg），而且是停留在叶子上的微小、圆形的卵。A项是第四阶段；B项是第二阶段；C项是第三阶段。故选D。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "The caterpillar's only job is to eat... As it eats, it grows very fast."

**【解析】** 原文讲得很清楚，毛毛虫唯一的工作就是吃（eat），并且在吃的同时快速生长（grow very fast）。A和B是成虫蝴蝶的事情；D是准备化蛹时的动作。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段末尾这两句 "Inside the chrysalis, a magical change is happening. The caterpillar's body is changing completely."

**【解析】** 在蛹的内部，正在发生神奇的改变：毛毛虫的身体正在发生完全的改变（changing completely）。A项（永远安眠）、C项（母蝴蝶产卵）、D项（吃大量叶子）都与破茧成蝶这神奇的体内变化毫无关联或者是前一阶段的行为。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-3句 "At first, its wings are wet and soft. It must wait for the sun to dry its wings before it can fly."

**【解析】** 刚破茧而出的蝴蝶，翅膀是湿润且柔软的。它必须等太阳把它的翅膀晒干（dry its wings），然后才能飞翔。B项是飞翔后寻找的花蜜；C项和D项文中没提到。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测 / 总结

**【定位】** 第⑤段首句 "This amazing process is called metamorphosis (变态发育)." 并结合全文四个阶段发展的描述。

**【解析】** 本文花了②至④段讲述蝴蝶从卵、毛毛虫、蛹到成虫的四个截然不同的发育阶段，而第五段总结“这个令人惊叹的过程（process）被称为metamorphosis”。因此，该词含义即是从卵变成蝴蝶的这个奇妙的发育过程。A、C、D项都不是指代这个完整的过程。故选B。
