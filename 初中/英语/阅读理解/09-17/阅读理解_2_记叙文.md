> **09-17 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Our class was going to take part in the school choir (合唱) competition. At first, nobody thought we could win. We had only two weeks to prepare, and some students could not sing well. "We will never beat Class 3. They have won three years in a row," said Tom. I felt the same way.

② Our music teacher, Ms. Chen, did not give up. "Every voice matters," she said. "When we sing together, we become one. Let's work as a team." She divided us into groups: high voices, low voices, and those who needed extra practice. Everyone had a role. Those who sang best helped others. Those who were shy at first slowly gained confidence. We practised every day after school. Sometimes we argued about which song to choose or how to stand, but we always found a way to agree.

③ The day before the competition, Wang Lin caught a cold and lost his voice. He was our lead singer for one part. We were worried. "Don't worry," said Li Ming. "I've been practising that part with Wang Lin. I can do it." He stepped up and learned the part overnight. The next day, we stood on the stage together. When the music started, we sang our hearts out. We looked at each other, smiled, and felt the power of our unity (团结).

④ We did not win first place — Class 3 did. But we won the "Best Team Spirit" award. The headmaster said, "Class 5 showed us what it means to work together. They supported each other and never gave up." We were proud. We had learned something more important than winning: when we stand together, we are stronger.

⑤ I will never forget that experience. It taught me that success is not only about being the best — it is about helping each other and growing together.

**注释：** choir 合唱；unity 团结；spirit 精神

---

**Questions:**

1. What competition was the class going to join?
   A. A football match.
   B. A singing competition.
   C. The school choir competition.
   D. A science quiz.

2. How did the students feel at the beginning?
   A. They were very confident.
   B. They did not think they could win.
   C. They thought Class 3 was weak.
   D. They refused to take part.

3. What happened to Wang Lin the day before the competition?
   A. He forgot the words.
   B. He caught a cold and lost his voice.
   C. He went home early.
   D. He joined another class.

4. Who took Wang Lin's place as the lead singer?
   A. Tom.
   B. Ms. Chen.
   C. Li Ming.
   D. The headmaster.

5. What award did the class win?
   A. First place.
   B. Best singer.
   C. Best Team Spirit.
   D. Best song.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Our class was going to take part in the school choir competition"

**【解析】** 原文明确说参加的是学校合唱比赛。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "At first, nobody thought we could win" 及 "We will never beat Class 3"

**【解析】** 起初大家认为不可能赢。A项"很有信心"与原文相反；C、D项未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The day before the competition, Wang Lin caught a cold and lost his voice"

**【解析】** 王林在比赛前一天感冒失声。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I've been practising that part with Wang Lin. I can do it.' He stepped up and learned the part overnight" — "He" 指 Li Ming

**【解析】** 李明主动顶替王林担任领唱部分。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "we won the 'Best Team Spirit' award"

**【解析】** 原文明确说班级获得"最佳团队精神"奖。A项一等奖由3班获得；B、D项未提及。故选C。
