# 阅读理解（说明文）

① An earthquake is a sudden shaking of the Earth's surface. It can be strong or weak. Strong earthquakes can cause buildings to fall and people to get hurt. Understanding how earthquakes happen and how to stay safe can save lives.

② Why do earthquakes happen? The Earth's outer layer is made up of huge pieces called plates (板块). These plates slowly move and sometimes push against each other. When the pressure becomes too great, the plates suddenly slip (滑动), and energy is released. This energy travels through the ground as waves, and we feel the shaking. Most earthquakes happen along the edges of these plates, especially in places like Japan, California, and parts of China.

③ Earthquakes can cause secondary (次要的) dangers. Buildings may collapse. Fires can start if gas pipes break. In coastal areas, earthquakes under the sea can cause tsunamis (海啸) — huge waves that can flood the land. That is why it is important to be prepared.

④ How can we protect ourselves during an earthquake? If you are indoors, do not run outside. Stay away from windows, mirrors, and heavy furniture that might fall. Get under a strong table or desk, and hold on. Protect your head and neck with your arms. If you are outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings, trees, and power lines. If you are in a car, stop in a safe place and stay inside until the shaking stops. After an earthquake, be careful of broken glass and fallen objects. Listen to the radio or TV for safety information.

⑤ Schools often hold earthquake drills (演练). Practicing what to do helps you react quickly when a real earthquake happens. Remember: stay calm, protect yourself, and help others when it is safe to do so.

**注释：** plate 板块；slip 滑动；secondary 次要的；tsunami 海啸；drill 演练

**Questions:**

1. What causes an earthquake according to the passage?
   A. Heavy rain and strong wind.
   B. The Earth's plates pushing against each other and suddenly slipping, releasing energy.
   C. Too many people living in one place.
   D. Buildings that are too tall.

2. Where do most earthquakes happen?
   A. In the middle of the ocean only.
   B. Along the edges of the Earth's plates, e.g. Japan, California, parts of China.
   C. Only in deserts.
   D. Only in cold countries.

3. Which of the following is a secondary danger of earthquakes?
   A. The plates moving slowly.
   B. Buildings collapsing, fires, and tsunamis in coastal areas.
   C. People reading books.
   D. Schools holding drills.

4. What should you do if you are indoors during an earthquake?
   A. Run outside as fast as you can.
   B. Stay away from windows, get under a strong table, and protect your head and neck.
   C. Stand near the window to see what is happening.
   D. Hide in the elevator.

5. Why do schools hold earthquake drills?
   A. To make students tired.
   B. To practice what to do so students can react quickly in a real earthquake.
   C. To test the building.
   D. To cancel classes.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The Earth's outer layer is made up of huge pieces called plates... When the pressure becomes too great, the plates suddenly slip, and energy is released. This energy travels through the ground as waves"

**【解析】** 原文说明地震由板块相互挤压、突然滑动并释放能量引起。A、C、D项与成因无关。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Most earthquakes happen along the edges of these plates, especially in places like Japan, California, and parts of China"

**【解析】** 原文指出多数地震发生在板块边缘，如日本、加州、中国部分地区。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Earthquakes can cause secondary dangers. Buildings may collapse. Fires can start... earthquakes under the sea can cause tsunamis"

**【解析】** 原文列举建筑物倒塌、火灾、海啸等次生灾害。A项是地震成因；C、D项不是灾害。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "If you are indoors, do not run outside. Stay away from windows, mirrors, and heavy furniture... Get under a strong table or desk, and hold on. Protect your head and neck with your arms"

**【解析】** 室内避险应远离窗、镜和重物，躲在结实桌下，护住头颈。A、C、D项与安全建议矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Practicing what to do helps you react quickly when a real earthquake happens"

**【解析】** 演练是为了在真实地震中快速做出正确反应。A、C、D项曲解演练目的。故选B。
