> **04-06 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Lost and Found Notice**

② **Found:**
A pair of glasses was found in the school library on Friday afternoon, April 5th. They were running on the desk near the history books. They are black with round frames (镜框) and they look very new. There is a small blue box next to them.
- If these are your glasses, please come to the teachers' office (Room 205) on the second floor to get them.
- Contact (联系人): Mr. Green
- Available time: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

③ **Lost:**
I lost my English dictionary on my way to the school dining hall on Thursday morning. It is a thick red book with the name "David Scott" on the first page. Inside the dictionary, there are also some important notes for my English exam. 
- I am very worried because the exam is next week.
- If you find it, please return it to Class 3, Grade 8.
- Phone number: 887-3490 (David).
- I will give a small gift to the person who finds it to show my thanks.

**注释：** frame 镜框；contact 联系人

---

### 选择题

1. Where were the glasses found?
A. In the teachers' office.
B. On the way to the dining hall.
C. In Class 3, Grade 8.
D. In the school library.

2. What do the lost glasses look like?
A. They have black frames and look very new.
B. They are red and thick.
C. They have round frames and look very old.
D. They are in a big blue box.

3. Why is David very worried about his dictionary?
A. Because it was a gift from his father.
B. Because it is a very expensive dictionary.
C. Because there are important notes for his exam inside it.
D. Because Mr. Green wants it back.

4. If you find David's dictionary, what should you do?
A. Give it to Mr. Green in Room 205.
B. Return it to Class 3, Grade 8.
C. Throw it away.
D. Leave it in the school library.

5. From the notice, we can know that __________.
A. Mr. Green's office is on the third floor.
B. The finder can get the glasses back at 8:00 PM on Friday.
C. David lost his dictionary on Thursday morning.
D. The glasses were found near the science books.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "A pair of glasses was found in the school library on Friday afternoon..."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到眼镜是在学校图书馆（school library）被发现的。A项是去认领的地方；B项和大卫丢字典的地方有关；C项是大卫所在的班级。故选D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "They are black with round frames (镜框) and they look very new."

**【解析】** 原文描述眼镜是黑色圆框，看起来很新。A项符合；B项描述的是丢失的字典（red, thick）；C项中的old与原文new矛盾；D项说是big blue box（大蓝盒子），而原文是small blue box（小蓝盒子）。故选A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "I am very worried because the exam is next week." 以及前一句 "...there are also some important notes for my English exam."

**【解析】** 大卫在寻找启事中提到，字典里有他英语考试的重要笔记，而考试就在下周，这才是他非常着急的原因。A项和B项是无中生有；D项是另一则启事的联系人。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段要求中 "If you find it, please return it to Class 3, Grade 8."

**【解析】** 在Lost的寻物启事中，大卫告诉捡到的人把它还给八年级3班（return it to Class 3, Grade 8）。A项是去找失主眼镜的地方。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断 / 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "I lost my English dictionary... on Thursday morning."

**【解析】** A项错误，Mr. Green的办公室在二楼（the second floor）；B项错误，领取眼镜的时间是 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM，晚上8点拿不到；C项正确，原文明确指出大卫因为周四早上（Thursday morning）把字典弄丢了；D项错误，眼镜是在历史书（history books）附近而不是科学书附近找到的。故选C。
