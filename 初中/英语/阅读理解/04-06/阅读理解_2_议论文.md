# 阅读理解（议论文）

① For many years, schools have given students a lot of homework. Some teachers and parents believe that more homework means better grades. But is this really true? Recently, many experts (专家) are starting to think that too much homework is bad for students.

② On the one hand, a huge amount of homework causes a lot of stress (压力). After a long day of learning at school, students are already very tired. Taking another three or four hours to finish homework at night leaves them with no time to relax. This heavy pressure can make students hate learning and feel anxious (焦虑的). In the long run, their health will be affected.

③ On the other hand, more homework doesn't always lead to better test results. Studies show that when students are too tired, their brains cannot remember information well. Quality (质量) is much more important than quantity (数量). Two carefully designed math problems can teach a student more than twenty simple, repetitive (重复的) ones.

④ Besides, students need time for other activities. They need time to read books they love, play sports, draw pictures, or simply spend time talking with their families. These activities are also very important for a student's fully development.

⑤ In conclusion, schools should reduce (减少) the amount of homework. We should give students more free time to explore the world and enjoy their youth. Learning should be a joy, not a heavy burden (负担).

**注释：** expert 专家；repetitive 重复的；burden 负担

---

### 选择题

1. What do some teachers and parents usually believe about homework?
A. Homework is a waste of time.
B. More homework means better grades.
C. Students should only do homework at school.
D. Homework causes too much stress.

2. According to paragraph ②, what is the bad result of too much homework?
A. Students will have more time to relax.
B. Students will become much healthier.
C. Students may hate learning and feel anxious.
D. Students will get higher marks in all subjects.

3. What does paragraph ③ mainly tell us?
A. Homework quality is more important than quantity.
B. Math is the most difficult subject to learn.
C. Students' brains can remember everything.
D. We need to do at least twenty math problems a day.

4. Form the text, what activities are also important for fully development?
A. Doing simple and repetitive homework.
B. Reading, playing sports, and spending time with family.
C. Staying up late and watching TV all night.
D. Taking many exams every week.

5. What does the writer suggest schools do in the last paragraph?
A. Give more homework to students.
B. Cancel (取消) all the exams.
C. Make the homework more difficult.
D. Reduce the amount of homework.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Some teachers and parents believe that more homework means better grades."

**【解析】** 原文提到一些老师和家长认为，越多的家庭作业意味着越好的成绩。A、C、D项都不是这些家长和老师的观点（D反而是一些专家的观点）。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "This heavy pressure can make students hate learning and feel anxious (焦虑的)."

**【解析】** 根据第②段，作者指出沉重的作业压力会导致学生讨厌学习且感到焦虑（C项符合）。A、B、D项均与这段表述的“危害（bad result）”相矛盾。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意 / 段落大意

**【定位】** 第③段整段，尤其是核心句 "Quality (质量) is much more important than quantity (数量)."

**【解析】** 第③段通过“学习疲劳影响记忆”以及“两道精心设计的数学题胜过二十道简单重复的题目”的例子，论证的核心是：作业的质量比数量更重要。B、C、D项均属曲解细节。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "They need time to read books they love, play sports, draw pictures, or simply spend time talking with their families."

**【解析】** 作者认为对学生全面发展很重要的活动包括看喜爱的书、运动、画画和家人聊天等。B项是对该句的概括总结。A、C、D项是文中反对或未提及的内容。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "In conclusion, schools should reduce (减少) the amount of homework."

**【解析】** 原文结论句明确给出了学校应当“减少（reduce）”家庭作业的总量的建议。A、C项正好意思相反；B项（取消所有考试）文中并未呼吁。故选D。
