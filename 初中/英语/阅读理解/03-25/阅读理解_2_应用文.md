# 阅读理解 第2篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **Be a Creator! — Join the Starfish Summer Tech Camp**

Do you love playing video games? Have you ever dreamed of making your own game or robot? If so, the Starfish Summer Tech Camp is the perfect place for you!

② **Who can join?** 
Students aged 12 to 15 who are interested in science, coding (编程), and teamwork. No prior coding experience is needed!

③ **What will you do?**
- **Week 1 (July 5 - July 9):** *Game Design Basics.* Learn how to use simple coding blocks to create your very first 2D video game.
- **Week 2 (July 12 - July 16):** *Robot Building.* Work in teams of 4 to design, build, and control a small intelligent robot using LEGO pieces and sensors.
- **Camp Exhibition (July 17):** Parents and friends are invited to come and see the amazing games and robots you have created. A "Best Design Award" will be given out!

④ **Schedule & Location:**
- **Time:** 9:00 am to 3:30 pm daily (Lunch is included).
- **Place:** The Computer Lab on the 3rd floor of the City Library.

⑤ **How to apply (申请)?**
The camp fee is ¥800 per student. We only have 30 places available this summer. Early bird discount (早鸟特惠): Pay before June 15th to get ¥100 off! Please visit *www.starfishtechcamp.org* to fill out the form and finish the payment.

Don't wait! Turn your ideas into reality this summer.

---

**注释：** creator 创造者；prior 优先的，先前的（no prior experience 无需任何经验）；exhibition 展览；intelligent 智能的；sensor 传感器；discount 折扣

---

### 题目

1. Who is the Starfish Summer Tech Camp mainly planned for?
   A. Primary school students who love playing outdoors.
   B. Teenagers aged 12 to 15 with an interest in coding.
   C. Parents who want to learn how to play video games.
   D. Teachers who teach science and coding at school.

2. Does a student need to know how to code before joining the camp?
   A. Yes, they must pass a coding test first.
   B. Yes, they need to have 2 years of coding experience.
   C. No, they just need to bring their own computers.
   D. No, no prior coding experience is needed.

3. What will the students do during Week 2?
   A. They will use simple blocks to create a 2D game.
   B. They will learn how to play newly invented video games.
   C. They will work in teams to design and build a small robot.
   D. They will visit the robot factory in the city.

4. If Mike applies and pays for the camp on June 10th, how much should he pay?
   A. ¥700.
   B. ¥800.
   C. ¥900.
   D. ¥100.

5. Which of the following is TRUE about the Camp Exhibition?
   A. It will be held on July 16, the last day of Week 2.
   B. Parents and friends are welcomed to attend it.
   C. Students must pay an extra ticket to join the exhibition.
   D. Every student will receive a "Best Design Award".

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Who can join? Students aged 12 to 15 who are interested in science, coding, and teamwork."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明参加者是12到15岁、对科学、编程和团队合作有兴趣的学生（Teenagers）。B 项与之完全吻合。A 项（喜欢户外的小学生）、C 项（想学打游戏的父母）、D 项（教编程的老师）均不符合招募对象的描述。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "No prior coding experience is needed!"

**【解析】** 原文用感叹号特别提示：不需要任何先前的编程经验（No prior coding experience is needed!）。D 项是对这句话的直接回答。A、B 项说需要经验或考试，与原文矛盾；C 项"只需要自带电脑"在原文中并未提及（原文说了地点在图书馆电脑室）。故选 D。

---

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 Week 2 的安排："Robot Building. Work in teams of 4 to design, build, and control a small intelligent robot..."

**【解析】** 第二周的主题是建造智能机器人，学生需要以4人为一组进行设计、建造和控制。C 项"他们将团队合作设计和建造一个小机器人"准确概括了这一活动。A 项（做2D游戏）是第一周的安排；B 项（玩新游戏）和 D 项（参观机器人工厂）都不属于夏令营的活动内容。故选 C。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 数学计算/细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The camp fee is ¥800 per student... Early bird discount: Pay before June 15th to get ¥100 off!"

**【解析】** 原本的夏令营费用是每个学生 800 元。但如果"在 6 月 15 日之前付款，可以减免 100 元 (get ¥100 off)"。Mike 在 6 月 10 日申请并付款，符合早鸟特惠条件，因此他需要支付 800 - 100 = 700 元。A 项正确。故选 A。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 Camp Exhibition 安排："Parents and friends are invited to come and see the amazing games and robots you have created."

**【解析】** 原文提到在成果展当天，父母和朋友被邀请来看看学生们创造的神奇游戏和机器人。B 项"父母和朋友被欢迎参加"与原文一致。A 项说在7月16日，而原文展览是 July 17；C 项要求额外买票，属无中生有；D 项说每个人都能得到大奖，而原文是说颁发"一个"最佳设计奖（A 'Best Design Award' will be given out），并非人人有份。故选 B。
