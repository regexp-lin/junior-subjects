# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① One rainy afternoon on my way back from school, I heard a weak sound coming from a cardboard box near the street corner. I walked closer and found a tiny, dirty kitten inside. It was shaking with cold. Without thinking twice, I took off my jacket, wrapped the kitten carefully, and carried it home.

② When my mother saw the kitten, she frowned (皱眉). "We can't keep a pet, David," she said firmly. "You are too busy with your studies, and I have no time to look after it." I begged her, "Please, Mom! Just for a few days, until I find a good home for it. I promise I will do everything—feeding, cleaning, and taking it for walks." Finally, my mother agreed.

③ I named the kitten "Lucky." Taking care of Lucky was much harder than I thought. Every morning, I had to wake up half an hour earlier to feed him and clean his litter box (猫砂盆). At first, I felt very tired. Sometimes I even regretted bringing him home. But when Lucky rubbed his soft head against my hand and purred (打呼噜) happily, all my tiredness disappeared. 

④ Two weeks later, my aunt came to visit us. She fell in love with Lucky immediately and offered to adopt (收养) him. I should have been happy because I didn't have to clean the litter box anymore. However, when my aunt put Lucky into her car, my eyes filled with tears. 

⑤ That night, the house felt strangely quiet. I realized that while I was busy taking care of Lucky, he was actually giving me something precious in return: a sense of responsibility and pure companionship (陪伴). 

---

**注释：** cardboard box 纸板箱；frown 皱眉；firmly 坚定地；regret 后悔；adopt 收养；precious 珍贵的

---

### 题目

1. Where did David find the kitten?
   A. In a pet shop near his school.
   B. Under an old car on a rainy day.
   C. In a cardboard box near the street corner.
   D. At his aunt's house in the countryside.

2. Why did David's mother disagree to keep the kitten at first?
   A. Because she was allergic (过敏) to cat hair.
   B. Because they were too busy to take care of it.
   C. Because David had already kept a pet dog.
   D. Because their house was too small for a pet.

3. How did David feel about taking care of Lucky at the very beginning?
   A. He felt it was quite easy and relaxing.
   B. He felt angry because his mother didn't help.
   C. He felt bored and wanted his aunt to take it away.
   D. He felt tired and sometimes regretted his decision.

4. What happened when David's aunt took Lucky away?
   A. David felt very happy and relieved (轻松的).
   B. David bought another kitten for himself.
   C. David cried because he had grown to love the kitten.
   D. His mother tried to stop his aunt from taking Lucky.

5. What did David learn from this experience?
   A. Keeping a pet is the best way to improve grades.
   B. Responsibility and companionship are precious gifts.
   C. Cats are much easier to take care of than dogs.
   D. We should never bring homeless animals home.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第一句 "I heard a weak sound coming from a cardboard box near the street corner. I walked closer and found a tiny, dirty kitten inside."

**【解析】** 原文明说作者是在街角附近的一个纸板箱里（in a cardboard box near the street corner）发现的小猫。C项与原文完全一致。A、B、D项均与原文地点不符。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第二、三句 "...she said firmly. 'You are too busy with your studies, and I have no time to look after it.'"

**【解析】** 妈妈起初不同意的理由是：David忙于学习，而她也没有时间照顾它（大家都很忙）。B项"因为他们太忙了没时间照顾它"最符合原意。A项（过敏）、C项（已经有狗）、D项（房子太小）都是无中生有。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第二至四句 "Taking care of Lucky was much harder than I thought... At first, I felt very tired. Sometimes I even regretted bringing him home."

**【解析】** 刚开始照顾 Lucky 时，David 觉得比想象的难，感到很累，甚至有时会"后悔"把他带回家。D 项"他感到很累，有时后悔他的决定"与原文准确对应。A 项（觉得轻松）、B 项（生妈妈不帮忙的气）、C 项（觉得无聊想送人）都不符合原文描述。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段末句 "However, when my aunt put Lucky into her car, my eyes filled with tears."

**【解析】** 当阿姨把幸运儿放进车里准备带走时，David的眼里"充满了泪水"（eyes filled with tears）。结合下文他感受到的"依恋"，可以推断出他哭了是因为他已经渐渐爱上了这只小猫、舍不得它走。C 项最符合这一情景反应。A 项（感到高兴轻松）与流泪相反；B 项（又买了一只）和 D 项（妈妈试图阻止）文里没有提及。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段末句 "I realized that while I was busy taking care of Lucky, he was actually giving me something precious in return: a sense of responsibility and pure companionship."

**【解析】** 在故事的最后，David 意识到自己学到了重要的一课：他照顾小猫的同时，小猫也以"责任感和纯粹的陪伴"作为珍贵的回报。B 项"责任和陪伴是珍贵的礼物"是对文章升华部分的精准概括。A 项（养宠物提高成绩）、C 项（猫比狗好养）、D 项（绝不该带流浪动物回家）均与文章积极和温情的主题背道而驰。故选 B。
