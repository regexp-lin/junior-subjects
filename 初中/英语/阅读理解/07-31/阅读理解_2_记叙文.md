# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Letters Across the Distance

① Last summer, Zhang Wei's family moved to a new city because of his father's job. Zhang Wei had to leave his old school and his best friend, Liu Yang. They had been friends since the first grade. On the day he left, Liu Yang came to say goodbye at the bus station. "We will always be friends," Liu Yang said. Zhang Wei nodded, but his eyes were wet. He was not sure how they could stay close when they lived so far apart.

② The first weeks in the new city were hard. Zhang Wei missed his old neighborhood, his old school, and Liu Yang the most. He tried to make new friends, but it was not easy. Sometimes he sat alone and thought about the days they spent playing basketball or doing homework together. He felt lonely.

③ One evening, Zhang Wei's mother gave him a pen and some paper. "Why not write to Liu Yang?" she said. "Letters can cross any distance." Zhang Wei had never written a real letter before. He sat down and began to write. He told Liu Yang about his new school, his new classmates, and how much he missed their friendship. A week later, he received a letter back. Liu Yang had written about his life too. Reading it, Zhang Wei smiled. It felt as if his friend were right there with him.

④ Now Zhang Wei and Liu Yang write to each other once or twice a month. They share their stories, their dreams, and their worries. Sometimes they send photos or small gifts in the letters. Zhang Wei has made new friends in his new school, but his friendship with Liu Yang remains special. "A letter takes time to write," Zhang Wei says. "But when you receive one, you know someone truly cares."

⑤ Moving away does not have to mean saying goodbye forever. Zhang Wei learned that friendship can cross any distance — as long as we are willing to reach out. A simple letter can keep the bond between friends strong, no matter how far apart they are.

**注释：** nod 点头；neighborhood 街区；bond 纽带

### 选择题

1. Why did Zhang Wei move to a new city?
   A. His father got a new job.
   B. He wanted to study in a better school.
   C. His family wanted to live near the sea.
   D. Liu Yang asked him to move.

2. According to paragraph ②, how did Zhang Wei feel during his first weeks in the new city?
   A. He felt very happy and excited.
   B. He made many new friends quickly.
   C. He forgot about Liu Yang soon.
   D. He felt lonely and missed Liu Yang and his old life the most.

3. Who suggested that Zhang Wei write a letter to Liu Yang?
   A. Zhang Wei's mother.
   B. Liu Yang himself.
   C. Zhang Wei's father.
   D. A new classmate.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Zhang Wei's view on letters?
   A. He thinks letters are boring and useless.
   B. He believes writing letters takes effort, but receiving them shows that someone cares.
   C. He prefers phone calls to letters.
   D. He only writes letters when he has nothing else to do.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Moving to a new city is always a bad thing.
   B. Friendship can last across distance if we are willing to keep in touch; letters can strengthen the bond.
   C. We should never make new friends when we move.
   D. Letters are better than all other ways of communication.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last summer, Zhang Wei's family moved to a new city because of his father's job."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明张伟一家因父亲的工作而搬家。A项准确复述。B项"想上更好的学校"、C项"想住海边"、D项"刘洋要求他搬"均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The first weeks in the new city were hard. Zhang Wei missed his old neighborhood, his old school, and Liu Yang the most... He felt lonely."

**【解析】** 原文清楚描写张伟初到新城市的几周很难熬，他最想念老街、旧学校和刘洋，并且感到孤独。D项准确概括。A项"很开心兴奋"与"hard"和"lonely"矛盾；B项"很快交到很多朋友"与"it was not easy"矛盾；C项"很快忘了刘洋"与"missed Liu Yang the most"矛盾。故选D。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "One evening, Zhang Wei's mother gave him a pen and some paper. 'Why not write to Liu Yang?' she said."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出是张伟的母亲递给他纸笔并建议给刘洋写信。A项正确。B项刘洋本人、C项父亲、D项新同学均与原文不符。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "'A letter takes time to write,' Zhang Wei says. 'But when you receive one, you know someone truly cares.'"

**【解析】** 张伟认为写信需要花时间，但收到信时能感受到对方真心在乎，可推知他认为写信需付出努力，而收信能体现被关心。B项正确。A项"无聊无用"与原文相反；C项"更喜欢打电话"原文未提及；D项"无事可做才写信"与定期通信矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图/主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Moving away does not have to mean saying goodbye forever... friendship can cross any distance — as long as we are willing to reach out. A simple letter can keep the bond between friends strong."

**【解析】** 文章通过张伟与刘洋写信保持友谊的故事，传达"搬家不意味着永别；只要愿意联系，友谊可以跨越距离；一封简单的信能维系友谊"的道理。B项最能概括作者意图。A项"搬家总是坏事"过于消极；C项"不应交新朋友"与原文"made new friends"矛盾；D项"信优于一切沟通方式"过于绝对。故选B。
