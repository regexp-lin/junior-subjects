# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Li Wei was a quiet boy in Grade 8. He seldom raised his hand in class and always sat at the back. When the teacher asked a question, his face would turn red and his heart would beat fast. He had many good ideas, but he was too shy to share them. His classmates thought he was quiet and a bit lonely.

② One day, Mr. Chen, the English teacher, came to him after class. "Li Wei, our school has a debate team. I think you would do well. You have a clear mind and good arguments. Will you try?" Li Wei was surprised. "Me? I can't speak in front of people." Mr. Chen smiled. "Everyone feels nervous at first. The debate team needs different voices. Yours could be one of them."

③ Li Wei joined the debate team. In the first practice, his hands were shaking and his voice was soft. He forgot what he wanted to say and stood there in silence. He wanted to leave. But his teammates did not laugh at him. They said, "We all started like this. Keep trying." So Li Wei stayed. He practiced at home in front of the mirror. He read more books to support his arguments. Week by week, he felt a little less nervous.

④ The big day came — the school debate competition. Li Wei's team was against the other class. When it was his turn to speak, he took a deep breath. He looked at his teammates and remembered their support. Then he spoke. His voice was clear and strong. He made his points one by one. When he finished, the audience clapped. His team won the first prize.

⑤ Li Wei is no longer the boy who hides at the back. He still feels a bit nervous sometimes, but he knows that confidence grows when we step out of our comfort zone. He has learned that trying new things can change who we are. Now he often raises his hand in class and helps new members of the debate team. He tells them, "If I can do it, you can too."

**注释：** debate 辩论；argument 论据；audience 观众；comfort zone 舒适区

---

### 选择题

1. What was Li Wei like at the beginning of the story?
   A. He was outgoing and liked to speak in class.
   B. He was quiet, shy, and seldom spoke in class.
   C. He was the best student in his class.
   D. He had no ideas and did not like school.

2. Why did Mr. Chen ask Li Wei to join the debate team?
   A. Because Li Wei had a clear mind and good arguments.
   B. Because Li Wei had no friends and needed something to do.
   C. Because the team had no members and needed anyone.
   D. Because Li Wei asked to join first.

3. What happened during Li Wei's first practice?
   A. He spoke clearly and won everyone's praise.
   B. His hands were shaking, his voice was soft, and he forgot his words.
   C. His teammates laughed at him and asked him to leave.
   D. He refused to speak and left the room.

4. What can we infer from the support of Li Wei's teammates?
   A. They wanted to make fun of him later.
   B. They did not care whether he stayed or left.
   C. They had experienced the same nervousness and encouraged him to keep trying.
   D. They thought he was the best speaker on the team.

5. What does Li Wei learn from his experience?
   A. Debate is the most important activity in school.
   B. Confidence grows when we step out of our comfort zone and try new things.
   C. Winning the competition is the only goal.
   D. We should never feel nervous in front of others.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Li Wei was a quiet boy in Grade 8. He seldom raised his hand in class and always sat at the back... He had many good ideas, but he was too shy to share them."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述李伟最初是个安静、害羞的男孩，很少举手发言，总坐在后排。B 项正确。A 项"班里最好"、C 项"没想法不喜欢学校"、D 项"外向爱发言"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "You have a clear mind and good arguments. Will you try?"

**【解析】** 陈老师邀请李伟加入辩论队是因为他有清晰的头脑和好的论据。A 项正确。B 项"没有朋友需要事做"、C 项"李伟先提出加入"、D 项"队伍没人需要任何人"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "In the first practice, his hands were shaking and his voice was soft. He forgot what he wanted to say and stood there in silence."

**【解析】** 第一次练习时李伟手抖、声音小、忘记要说的话、沉默站立。D 项正确。A 项"清晰赢得赞扬"与第一次表现矛盾；B 项"队友嘲笑"与"They did not laugh at him"相反；C 项"拒绝说话离开"与"So Li Wei stayed"矛盾。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "But his teammates did not laugh at him. They said, 'We all started like this. Keep trying.'"

**【解析】** 队友没有嘲笑他，反而说"我们最初也是这样，继续努力"，可推知他们也经历过同样的紧张，并鼓励他坚持。C 项正确。A 项"后来取笑"、B 项"不在乎他是否留下"、D 项"认为他最好"均无依据。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "he knows that confidence grows when we step out of our comfort zone. He has learned that trying new things can change who we are."

**【解析】** 李伟领悟到当我们走出舒适区时自信会增长，尝试新事物能改变我们。B 项正确。A 项"辩论最重要"过于绝对；C 项"夺冠是唯一目标"偏离成长主题；D 项"永不紧张"与"still feels a bit nervous sometimes"矛盾。故选 B。
