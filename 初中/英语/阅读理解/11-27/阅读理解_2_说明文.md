# 阅读理解（说明文）

**What Causes Smog?**

① On some winter mornings, you may look out of the window and see a thick grey blanket covering the city. The air looks dirty, and it may be hard to see buildings far away. This is smog — a mix of smoke and fog that can harm our health. But what causes it?

② Smog is mainly caused by air pollution. When we burn coal, oil, or gas for heating and electricity, tiny particles and harmful gases go into the air. Cars and buses also produce a lot of exhaust fumes. In winter, many people use coal or gas to warm their homes. All these add to the pollution. When the weather is calm and there is little wind, the dirty air cannot escape. It stays close to the ground and forms smog.

③ Weather plays an important role. During smog days, there is often an "inversion layer" — a layer of warm air that sits above cold air near the ground. This warm layer acts like a lid. It traps the polluted air below, so the smog cannot rise and spread. This often happens on cold, windless winter mornings.

④ Smog can cause health problems. Tiny particles in smog can enter our lungs and cause coughing, sore throats, and breathing difficulties. Old people and children are more at risk. On heavy smog days, schools sometimes close or cancel outdoor activities to protect students. Doctors advise people to wear masks outdoors and avoid exercising outside when the air quality is poor.

⑤ To reduce smog, we need to cut down on pollution. Using cleaner energy, taking buses instead of cars, and saving electricity at home can all help. Governments also set rules for factories and cars to reduce harmful emissions. When everyone works together, the sky can become clear again.

**注释：** smog 雾霾；particle 颗粒；exhaust 排气；emission 排放

**Questions:**

1. What is smog?

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "This is smog — a mix of smoke and fog that can harm our health."

**【解析】** 原文定义雾霾是烟和雾的混合物，会危害健康。A项洁净空气、C项云、D项纯水汽均不正确。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Cars and buses also produce a lot of exhaust fumes."

**【解析】** 汽车和公交车产生大量尾气。A项树木、C项花朵、D项雨水均不产生尾气。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "This warm layer acts like a lid. It traps the polluted air below."

**【解析】** 逆温层像盖子一样把污染空气困在近地面。A项净化空气、C项带来雨水、D项加大风力均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Old people and children are more at risk."

**【解析】** 老人和儿童更易受影响。A项仅年轻人、C项仅运动员、D项没人均与原文矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Using cleaner energy, taking buses instead of cars, and saving electricity at home can all help."

**【解析】** 原文建议使用清洁能源、乘公交代替开车、节约用电。A项多烧煤、B项多开车会增加污染；D项多用电会增加发电需求，可能加重污染。故选C。
