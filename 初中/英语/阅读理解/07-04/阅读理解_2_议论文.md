# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Is Group Work Really Helpful in Class?**

② In many schools, teachers ask students to work in groups. They believe that group work helps students learn from each other and develop teamwork skills. But does group work really work? Let us look at both sides of the debate.

③ **Arguments for group work:** Supporters say that when students work together, they can share ideas and help each other. Students who are good at a subject can help those who find it difficult. This builds confidence and friendship. Group work also teaches students how to listen to others and how to discuss. These skills are useful both in school and in future jobs. Besides, group work makes learning more interesting. Students often feel less bored when they work with classmates.

④ **Arguments against group work:** Critics worry that in a group, some students do most of the work while others do little. The hard-working students may feel unfair, and the lazy ones may not learn much. Sometimes group work takes more time than working alone. Students may chat (闲聊) instead of focusing on the task. Another problem is that shy students may not speak up in a group. They might feel left out and learn less.

⑤ **Conclusion:** Group work can be helpful, but it needs to be done in the right way. Teachers should make sure that every student has a clear role in the group. They should also set a time limit so that students stay on task. For shy students, teachers can give them easier or smaller parts to do first. When group work is well organized, it can help most students learn better. When it is not, it may waste time and create problems. The key is balance.

**注释：** debate 辩论；confidence 自信；critic 批评者；role 角色

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ③, which is a benefit of group work?
   A. Students can share ideas and help each other.
   B. Students can finish homework faster alone.
   C. Teachers have less work to do.
   D. Schools can save money on books.

2. What problem do critics mention about group work?
   A. Group work is too expensive.
   B. Group work is only for science class.
   C. Some students do most of the work while others do little.
   D. Students cannot choose their group members.

3. What does the writer suggest teachers should do?
   A. Give every student a clear role in the group.
   B. Let students work in groups all the time.
   C. Never use group work in class.
   D. Only let shy students work in groups.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Group work is always better than working alone.
   B. All students dislike group work.
   C. Teachers should never set time limits for group work.
   D. Group work can work well only when it is well organized.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell students how to make friends in class.
   B. To discuss whether group work is effective and give suggestions.
   C. To compare different schools.
   D. To advertise a new teaching method.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Supporters say that when students work together, they can share ideas and help each other."

**【解析】** 支持者认为小组合作时学生可以分享想法、互相帮助。A项正确。B项独自完成更快、C项减轻老师负担、D项节省书本费用均非文中提到的好处。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Critics worry that in a group, some students do most of the work while others do little."

**【解析】** 批评者指出小组中部分学生承担大部分工作而其他人出力很少。C项正确。A项费用高、B项仅限科学课、D项不能选组员均非文中所提问题。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Teachers should make sure that every student has a clear role in the group."

**【解析】** 作者建议教师应确保每位学生在小组中有明确的角色。A项正确。B项始终分组、C项永不使用、D项仅让害羞学生分组均与建议不符。故选A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Group work can be helpful, but it needs to be done in the right way... When group work is well organized, it can help most students learn better. When it is not, it may waste time and create problems."

**【解析】** 作者强调小组合作有效需以正确方式开展，组织得当则效果好，否则会浪费时间和制造问题。可推知小组合作只有在组织得当时才能发挥良好效果。D项正确。A项总是更好、B项人人讨厌、C项不应设时限均与原文矛盾。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 标题 "Is Group Work Really Helpful in Class?" 及全文结构（支持与反对论点 + 结论与建议）

**【解析】** 文章围绕"小组合作是否有效"展开讨论，列举正反观点并给出组织建议。B项准确概括主旨。A项如何交友、C项比较学校、D项宣传新教法均非主旨。故选B。
