# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Visitor Rules for City Zoo and Aquarium**

② Welcome to City Zoo and Aquarium! To keep our animals safe and make your visit enjoyable, please follow these rules. The zoo opens at 9:00 a.m. and closes at 6:00 p.m. every day. Children under 1.2 meters tall can enter for free when they visit with an adult.

③ **Do not feed the animals.** Our animals get special food from our workers. Feeding them yourself may make them sick. **Do not touch the animals** or put your hands through the fences (围栏). Some animals may bite or scratch. **Do not use flash (闪光) when taking photos.** Bright light can scare the animals.

④ Please walk on the paths and do not climb over any barriers. Keep your voice down so that the animals stay calm. If you bring bags or food, please put rubbish in the bins. Do not leave anything behind.

⑤ If you or your child feel unwell, go to the first-aid (急救) room near the main entrance. Our staff will help you. For more information, visit our website or call 5678-1234. We hope you have a wonderful day!

**注释：** fence 围栏；flash 闪光；first-aid 急救

---

### 选择题

1. When does the zoo close every day?
   A. At 6:00 p.m.
   B. At 5:00 p.m.
   C. At 7:00 p.m.
   D. At 8:00 p.m.

2. Why should visitors NOT feed the animals?
   A. The animals do not like visitors.
   B. Feeding them may make the animals sick.
   C. The zoo sells special animal food.
   D. Visitors must save their own food.

3. What should visitors do when taking photos?
   A. Use flash to get clearer pictures.
   B. Stand very close to the animals.
   C. Not use flash because it can scare the animals.
   D. Feed the animals first to attract them.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Children can visit alone without adults.
   B. The zoo has no first-aid room.
   C. Visitors can touch animals if they are gentle.
   D. Visitors should keep quiet and stay on the paths.

5. What is this passage mainly about?
   A. Rules for visitors at a zoo and aquarium.
   B. How to become a zoo worker.
   C. The best time to visit the zoo.
   D. Different kinds of animals in the zoo.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The zoo opens at 9:00 a.m. and closes at 6:00 p.m. every day."

**【解析】** 文中明确指出动物园每天下午6点关门。B项正确。A、C、D项时间均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Feeding them yourself may make them sick."

**【解析】** 文中说明游客自行投喂可能使动物生病。B项正确。A项动物不喜欢游客、C项动物园售卖动物食品、D项游客需节省食物均未在文中提及或与主旨不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Do not use flash (闪光) when taking photos. Bright light can scare the animals."

**【解析】** 文中明确要求拍照时不要使用闪光灯，因为强光会吓到动物。C项正确。A、B、D项均与规则相反。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Please walk on the paths... Keep your voice down so that the animals stay calm."

**【解析】** 文中要求游客在指定道路行走并降低音量以保持动物平静，可推知游客应保持安静并按规定路线行走。B项正确。A项儿童可单独参观与"with an adult"矛盾；C项无急救室与第⑤段矛盾；D项可触摸动物与"Do not touch"矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解

**【定位】** 标题 "Visitor Rules for City Zoo and Aquarium" 及全文内容（开闭园时间、禁止投喂、禁止触摸、勿用闪光、保持安静等规则）

**【解析】** 文章介绍动物园和水族馆的游客须知及各项规则。B项准确概括主旨。A项如何成为员工、C项最佳参观时间、D项动物园动物种类均非本文重点。故选B。
