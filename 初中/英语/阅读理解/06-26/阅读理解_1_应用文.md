# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Reading Corner — Rules for All Students**

② Welcome to our school reading corner! We built this quiet space to help every student enjoy reading and improve their English. To make it a pleasant (令人愉快的) place for everyone, please follow these rules.

③ **Opening time:** The reading corner is open from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. on school days. It is closed on weekends and holidays. You can borrow at most two books at a time. Each book can be kept for two weeks. If you want to keep it longer, please come to the desk and renew (续借) it.

④ **While you are here:** Keep your voice down. Do not eat or drink inside. Put your phone on silent (静音) so you will not disturb (打扰) others. When you finish a book, put it back on the right shelf. Damaged (损坏的) books should be reported to the teacher in charge.

⑤ **Returning books:** Return your books on time. If you lose a book, you need to pay for it. Join our "Reading Star" activity: read five books in a month and you can get a small gift. Happy reading!

**注释：** pleasant 令人愉快的；renew 续借；silent 静音；disturb 打扰；damage 损坏

---

### 选择题

1. When is the reading corner open?
   A. All day and night.
   B. Only in the morning.
   C. From 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. on school days.
   D. Only on weekends.

2. How many books can a student borrow at most at one time?
   A. Only one.
   B. Two.
   C. Five.
   D. As many as they want.

3. What should students do when they are in the reading corner?
   A. Eat snacks and talk loudly with friends.
   B. Run around and play games.
   C. Keep quiet and put their phone on silent.
   D. Leave their books everywhere.

4. What will happen if a student loses a book?
   A. They can never borrow books again.
   B. Nothing will happen.
   C. They will get a gift.
   D. They need to pay for it.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell students the rules of the school reading corner.
   B. To introduce the history of the school.
   C. To encourage students to buy more books.
   D. To complain about students who damage books.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The reading corner is open from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. on school days."

**【解析】** 文章明确说明读书角在工作日早上8点至下午6点开放。C项正确。A项全天候、B项仅上午、D项仅周末均与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "You can borrow at most two books at a time."

**【解析】** 学生每次最多可借两本书。B项正确。A项一本、C项五本、D项不限数量均错误。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Keep your voice down... Put your phone on silent (静音) so you will not disturb (打扰) others."

**【解析】** 在读书角应保持安静，手机调静音以免打扰他人。C项正确。A项吃零食大声说话、B项奔跑游戏、D项乱放书籍均违反规则。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If you lose a book, you need to pay for it."

**【解析】** 遗失书籍需赔偿。D项正确。A项永不能借书、B项无后果、C项获得礼物均未提及。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文标题 "School Reading Corner — Rules for All Students" 及第②段 "please follow these rules."

**【解析】** 本文旨在介绍学校读书角的使用规则，供全体学生遵守。A项准确概括。B项介绍校史、C项鼓励购书、D项抱怨损坏行为均非主旨。故选A。
