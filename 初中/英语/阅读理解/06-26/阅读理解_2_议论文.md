# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Is Travel the Best Way to Learn?**

② "Read ten thousand books and travel ten thousand miles." This old saying (俗语) is popular in many countries. It suggests that travel helps us learn about the world in a way that books cannot. But is travel really the best way to learn? Different people have different ideas.

③ **Those who support travel as learning:** Many people believe that travel is the best teacher. When you visit a new place, you see its culture (文化), food, and way of life with your own eyes. You meet local people and practice a new language. For example, spending a week in London can teach you more about British culture than reading many books. Travel also makes you more open-minded (思想开放的) and helps you understand that the world is full of different but equally valuable (有价值的) ways of life.

④ **Those who disagree:** However, not everyone can travel often. Travel costs a lot of money and time. Some students and families cannot afford (负担得起) it. Besides, we can learn a great deal from books, videos, and the Internet without leaving home. A good book about a country can also bring its history and culture to life. What matters is whether we are willing to learn — not where we learn it. Some people travel everywhere but learn nothing because they do not observe (观察) carefully or ask questions.

⑤ **A balanced view:** Travel can be a wonderful way to learn, but it is not the only way. Reading, watching documentaries (纪录片), and talking to people from other countries can also broaden (拓宽) our minds. The best learners use all these ways together. Whether you travel or stay at home, keep an open heart and a curious (好奇的) mind — that is the true key to learning.

**注释：** saying 俗语；culture 文化；open-minded 思想开放的；valuable 有价值的；afford 负担得起；observe 观察；documentary 纪录片；broaden 拓宽；curious 好奇的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what does the old saying suggest?
   A. We should only read books and never travel.
   B. Reading is useless compared to travel.
   C. We must travel ten thousand miles every year.
   D. Travel helps us learn about the world in a way that books cannot.

2. Why do some people think travel is the best way to learn?
   A. Because travel is free and costs nothing.
   B. Because you can see culture, food, and way of life with your own eyes and practice a new language.
   C. Because everyone can travel as often as they want.
   D. Because books are boring and useless.

3. What problem does the passage mention about travel?
   A. Travel costs a lot of money and time, and not everyone can afford it.
   B. Travel makes people too tired to learn.
   C. Travel is dangerous and nobody should try it.
   D. Travel makes people forget their own culture.

4. The underlined word "broaden" in paragraph ⑤ probably means ______.
   A. make something wider or larger; to expand
   B. make something narrower or smaller
   C. destroy or break
   D. hide or cover

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Travel is the only way to learn and everyone must travel.
   B. People who cannot travel will never learn anything.
   C. The best learners use many ways to learn — travel, reading, and talking to others.
   D. Books and the Internet are useless for learning about other cultures.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "It suggests that travel helps us learn about the world in a way that books cannot."

**【解析】** 俗语"读万卷书，行万里路"暗示旅行能以书籍无法替代的方式帮助我们认识世界。D项正确。A项只读书不旅行、B项读书无用、C项每年必行万里路均非原文含义。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "When you visit a new place, you see its culture (文化), food, and way of life with your own eyes. You meet local people and practice a new language."

**【解析】** 支持者认为亲身体验当地文化、美食和生活方式，并与当地人交流、练习新语言，是旅行的学习价值所在。B项正确。A项旅行免费、C项人人可随心旅行、D项书籍无用均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Travel costs a lot of money and time. Some students and families cannot afford (负担得起) it."

**【解析】** 文章指出旅行的局限：花费大量金钱和时间，并非人人都能负担。A项正确。B项使人疲劳、C项危险、D项使人遗忘本族文化均未提及。故选A。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Reading, watching documentaries... can also broaden (拓宽) our minds."

**【解析】** 根据括号注释"拓宽"及上下文"开阔眼界"，broaden 意为使更宽广、扩展。A项 make something wider or larger; to expand 与之最贴合。B项变窄、C项破坏、D项隐藏均不符。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The best learners use all these ways together. Whether you travel or stay at home, keep an open heart and a curious (好奇的) mind — that is the true key to learning."

**【解析】** 作者主张最佳学习方式是将旅行、阅读、看纪录片、与人交谈等多种途径结合使用。C项正确。A项旅行唯一、B项不旅行就无法学习、D项书籍网络无用均与作者"多种方式并重"的观点矛盾。故选C。
