# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Sunshine Bookstore — Member Card Guide**

① Welcome to Sunshine Bookstore! Our member card gives you special benefits. Read below to learn more.

**Member Benefits:**

| Card Type | Fee | Discount | Points | Free Shipping |
|-----------|-----|----------|--------|---------------|
| Silver    | Free | 5% off  | 1 point per 10 yuan | Over 100 yuan |
| Gold      | 50 yuan/year | 10% off | 2 points per 10 yuan | Over 80 yuan |
| Platinum  | 100 yuan/year | 15% off | 3 points per 10 yuan | Free |

② **How points work:** Every 100 points can be exchanged for 10 yuan off your next purchase. Points are valid for one year. You can check your points balance at our counter or on our app.

③ **How to get a card:**
- **Silver (free):** Fill in the form at any Sunshine Bookstore. No fee. Get your card at once.
- **Gold & Platinum:** Visit our website www.sunshinebooks.cn or ask our staff. Payment can be made by WeChat, Alipay, or cash.

④ **What you need:** A valid ID (student card, ID card, or passport). For students under 16, a parent can apply for you.

⑤ **Member days:** On the first Sunday of every month, all members get an extra 5% off. Show your member card at the counter to enjoy the discount.

Sunshine Bookstore — Read more, pay less!

---

**Questions:**

1. How much does the Gold card cost?
   A. Free.
   B. 50 yuan per year.
   C. 100 yuan per year.
   D. 80 yuan per year.

2. Which card offers 15% off?
   A. Silver.
   B. Gold.
   C. Platinum.
   D. None of them.

3. How can you use 100 points?
   A. Get a free book.
   B. Exchange for 10 yuan off your next purchase.
   C. Get free shipping only.
   D. Renew your card for free.

4. When do members get an extra 5% off?
   A. Every Saturday.
   B. Every Monday.
   C. On the first Sunday of every month.
   D. Every day.

5. Who can apply for a card for students under 16?
   A. Only the student himself.
   B. A parent.
   C. A teacher.
   D. A friend.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 Gold 一行 "50 yuan/year"

**【解析】** 表格明确标注金卡年费50元。A项免费是银卡；C项100元是铂金卡；D项80元是金卡免运费的金额门槛。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 Platinum 一行 "15% off"

**【解析】** 铂金卡享受15%折扣。A项银卡5%；B项金卡10%；D项与表格内容不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Every 100 points can be exchanged for 10 yuan off your next purchase."

**【解析】** 每100积分可兑换下次购物减10元。A项免费书、C项仅免运费、D项免费续卡均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "On the first Sunday of every month, all members get an extra 5% off."

**【解析】** 每月第一个星期天会员额外享受5%折扣。A项每周六、B项每周一、D项每天均不正确。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "For students under 16, a parent can apply for you."

**【解析】** 16岁以下学生可由家长代为申请。A项仅学生本人与"a parent can apply"矛盾；C项老师、D项朋友原文未提及。故选B。
