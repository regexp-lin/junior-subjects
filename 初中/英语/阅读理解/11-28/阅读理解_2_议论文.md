# 阅读理解（议论文）

**The Benefits of Waking Up Early**

① "Early to bed and early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy, and wise." This old saying has been passed down for hundreds of years. But does waking up early really bring benefits? Let us look at what science and experience tell us.

② One clear benefit is better health. Our bodies have a natural rhythm called the circadian rhythm. When we wake up early and go to bed at a reasonable time, our body clock stays in balance. This helps us sleep better, feel more energetic during the day, and even improve our immune system. Studies show that early risers tend to eat breakfast more often, which is good for health and concentration at school.

③ Early mornings also offer peace and quiet. Before the rest of the family gets up, the house is calm. There are no messages, no phone calls, and no distractions. This is the best time to study, read, or plan your day. Many successful people say they use the early hours to do their most important work. For students, reviewing lessons in the morning can be very effective because the mind is fresh.

④ Furthermore, waking up early gives you more control over your time. If you get up at 6 a.m. instead of 7:30 a.m., you gain an extra hour and a half. You can use it for exercise, homework, or hobbies. You will not need to rush to school or miss breakfast. Starting the day calmly helps you feel less stressed and more prepared for the challenges ahead.

⑤ Of course, "early" does not mean 4 a.m. for everyone. Teenagers need about 8 to 10 hours of sleep. The key is to find a routine that works for you — perhaps waking at 6:30 or 7:00 and going to bed by 10:00 or 10:30. Once you form the habit, you may find that early mornings become your favourite part of the day.

**注释：** circadian 昼夜节律的；immune 免疫的；distraction 干扰

**Questions:**

1. What does the circadian rhythm refer to?
   A. A type of exercise.
   B. The body's natural rhythm for sleep and wake.
   C. A kind of food.
   D. A study method.

2. Why do early risers tend to eat breakfast more often?
   A. They have more time in the morning.
   B. Their parents force them to.
   C. They want to stay in bed longer.
   D. They do not like lunch.

3. Why is early morning a good time to study?
   A. Because it is cold.
   B. Because the house is calm, with no distractions, and the mind is fresh.
   C. Because teachers ask students to.
   D. Because there is no breakfast.

4. What is one benefit of gaining extra time in the morning?
   A. You can watch more TV.
   B. You can use it for exercise, homework, or hobbies; you will not need to rush.
   C. You can sleep longer.
   D. You can skip school.

5. How much sleep do teenagers need?
   A. 4 hours.
   B. 5 to 6 hours.
   C. 8 to 10 hours.
   D. 12 hours.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Our bodies have a natural rhythm called the circadian rhythm. When we wake up early and go to bed at a reasonable time, our body clock stays in balance."

**【解析】** 昼夜节律是身体自然的睡眠与清醒节奏。A项运动、C项食物、D项学习方法均与定义不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "early risers tend to eat breakfast more often" 及第④段 "you gain an extra hour and a half... You will not need to rush to school or miss breakfast."

**【解析】** 早起者有更多早晨时间，不必匆忙，因而更可能吃早餐。B项父母强迫、C项想多睡、D项不喜欢午餐均无依据。故选A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "the house is calm... no distractions" 及 "the mind is fresh"

**【解析】** 早晨安静无打扰，头脑清醒，适合学习。A项因为冷、C项老师要求、D项没早餐均非原文理由。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "You can use it for exercise, homework, or hobbies. You will not need to rush to school or miss breakfast."

**【解析】** 多出的时间可用于运动、作业或爱好，不必匆忙。A项看更多电视、C项睡更久、D项逃学均与建议不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Teenagers need about 8 to 10 hours of sleep."

**【解析】** 原文说青少年需要约8到10小时睡眠。A项4小时、B项5-6小时、D项12小时均不正确。故选C。
