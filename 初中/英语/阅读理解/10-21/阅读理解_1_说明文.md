# 阅读理解（说明文）

**How Plastic Harms Sea Animals**

① Every year, millions of tonnes of plastic waste end up in the ocean. Plastic does not break down easily. It can stay in the sea for hundreds of years. This plastic causes serious harm to marine life.

② Sea animals often mistake plastic for food. Sea turtles may eat plastic bags because they look like jellyfish. Birds and fish swallow small pieces of plastic. These pieces fill their stomachs but provide no nutrition. The animals feel full but are actually starving. Many die from hunger or from blocked digestive systems.

③ Plastic can also trap and injure animals. Old fishing nets, called "ghost nets", float in the water. Fish, dolphins, and seals can get caught in them. They cannot escape and may drown or be seriously hurt. Plastic rings from drink packs can get stuck around animals' necks or bodies as they grow, causing pain and deformities.

④ Another problem is microplastics. These are tiny pieces of plastic, smaller than a grain of rice. They come from larger plastic that breaks into pieces, or from products like face wash and toothpaste. Microplastics are found everywhere in the ocean. Small sea creatures eat them. Then bigger fish eat the small creatures. In the end, microplastics may enter our own food chain.

⑤ We can all help. Use fewer single-use plastic items. Choose reusable bags, bottles, and containers. Recycle properly. Join or support beach clean-up activities. Every small action counts. Together we can reduce plastic pollution and protect our oceans.

**注释：** marine 海洋的；microplastic 微塑料；deformity 畸形

**Questions:**

1. Why does plastic stay in the ocean for a long time?
   A. Because fish eat it quickly.
   B. Because plastic does not break down easily.
   C. Because people collect it every day.
   D. Because the ocean is too small.

2. Why do sea turtles eat plastic bags?
   A. Because plastic tastes good.
   B. Because they look like jellyfish, which turtles eat.
   C. Because there is no other food.
   D. Because plastic is nutritious.

3. What are "ghost nets"?
   A. Nets that catch ghosts.
   B. Old fishing nets that float in the water and can trap animals.
   C. New safe nets.
   D. Nets used only by dolphins.

4. According to Paragraph ④, how do microplastics enter our food chain?
   A. We drink them directly from the ocean.
   B. Small creatures eat them, bigger fish eat small creatures, and we may eat the fish.
   C. They fly into our food.
   D. They are only in toothpaste.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Plastic pollution only harms fish.
   B. Plastic pollution affects the whole ocean ecosystem and even humans; we need to act.
   C. There is nothing we can do.
   D. Plastic is good for the ocean.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "Plastic does not break down easily. It can stay in the sea for hundreds of years"
**【解析】** 塑料不易降解，可在海洋中存留数百年。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Sea turtles may eat plastic bags because they look like jellyfish"
**【解析】** 海龟可能误食塑料袋，因其外形像水母。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 词义/细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Old fishing nets, called 'ghost nets', float in the water. Fish, dolphins, and seals can get caught in them"
**【解析】** "ghost nets"指漂浮在海上的废旧渔网，会使鱼、海豚、海豹等被缠住。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Small sea creatures eat them. Then bigger fish eat the small creatures. In the end, microplastics may enter our own food chain"
**【解析】** 小生物摄入微塑料，大鱼吃小生物，微塑料可能进入人类食物链。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 全文，尤其第④段食物链及第⑤段 "We can all help"
**【解析】** 塑料污染影响整个海洋生态直至人类，但我们可以通过减少使用、回收、清理等行动改善。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
