> **10-21 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Speech — Running for Class Monitor**

① Last month, our teacher said we would hold a class monitor election. Anyone could give a speech and the class would vote. I wanted to try, but I was afraid. I had never spoken in front of so many people. My hands would shake and my voice would tremble. What if I made a fool of myself?

② I spent three days writing my speech. I wrote about why I wanted to help the class, my ideas for improvement, and my promise to listen to everyone. I practised in front of the mirror every night. I asked my mum to listen and give me feedback. She said, "You speak clearly. Just be yourself."

③ On the day of the election, my heart was pounding. When my name was called, I walked to the front slowly. I took a deep breath and began. The first few sentences were okay. Then I forgot a line. I paused. For a moment I wanted to run away. But I looked at my classmates. They were listening quietly. No one laughed. I took another breath and went on. I finished my speech and bowed. The class clapped. I felt relieved.

④ I did not win the election. Another student got more votes. At first I was a little sad. But then I thought: I had tried something I was afraid of. I had stood in front of forty people and spoken. That was a victory for me. My teacher said, "Well done. You were brave. There will be more chances."

⑤ Looking back, I am glad I tried. Fear is natural, but we can overcome it step by step. Next time I will be more prepared and maybe a little less nervous. The important thing is to take the first step.

**注释：** tremble 颤抖；pound 剧烈跳动

---

**Questions:**

1. Why was the writer afraid at first?
   A. Because he did not like the teacher.
   B. Because he had never spoken in front of so many people and feared making a mistake.
   C. Because he did not want to be monitor.
   D. Because the election was cancelled.

2. What did the writer's mum say to encourage him?
   A. "Do not try."
   B. "You speak clearly. Just be yourself."
   C. "You will certainly win."
   D. "No one will listen."

3. What happened when the writer forgot a line during the speech?
   A. Everyone laughed at him.
   B. He ran away.
   C. He paused, then took a breath and continued.
   D. He gave up and sat down.

4. Did the writer win the election?
   A. Yes, he won.
   B. No, another student got more votes.
   C. The election was a tie.
   D. No one won.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer will never try again.
   B. The writer learned that overcoming fear is a process and taking the first step matters.
   C. Winning is the only thing that matters.
   D. The writer does not care about the experience.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "I had never spoken in front of so many people. My hands would shake... What if I made a fool of myself?"
**【解析】** 作者从未在这么多人面前演讲，害怕出错、出丑。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "She said, 'You speak clearly. Just be yourself.'"
**【解析】** 妈妈鼓励他说话清楚，做自己就好。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "I forgot a line. I paused... I took another breath and went on"
**【解析】** 忘词时作者停顿，深吸一口气后继续讲下去。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "I did not win the election. Another student got more votes"
**【解析】** 作者未当选，另一名同学得票更多。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Fear is natural, but we can overcome it step by step... The important thing is to take the first step"
**【解析】** 作者领悟到恐惧是正常的，可以逐步克服，关键迈出第一步。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
