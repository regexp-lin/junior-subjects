# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Green Campus Adoption Activity — No. 5 Middle School**

② Our school is starting a new program to make our campus greener and encourage students to take care of the environment. We invite all classes to adopt (认养) a tree or a small garden area on our campus. By taking part, you will learn how plants grow and feel proud of caring for nature.

③ **How to Join:**
- Sign up as a class before **September 15**
- Your class will be given a tree or a garden plot (地块) with a name plate
- Each class needs to water, weed (除草), and take care of their adopted area at least twice a week
- The school will provide basic tools such as watering cans and gloves

④ **What You Will Do:**
| Task | Who | When |
|------|-----|------|
| Water plants | All class members take turns | Tuesday & Friday afternoons |
| Remove weeds | Groups of 3–4 students | Once every two weeks |
| Record growth | Class leader | Once a month, with photos |

⑤ **Prizes:**
At the end of the school year, we will choose the "Best Green Guardian Class" (最佳护绿班级) based on plant health, records, and teamwork. The winning class will receive a certificate and a field trip to the city botanical garden (植物园).

⑥ If you have any questions, please contact Mr. Wang at the Student Affairs Office or email **green@no5school.edu.cn**. Let's work together to make our school a beautiful green home!

**注释：** adopt 认养；plot 地块；weed 除草；guardian 守护者；botanical 植物的

---

### 选择题

1. Who can join the Green Campus Adoption Activity?
   A. All classes in the school.
   B. Only students who love plants.
   C. Students from other schools.
   D. Teachers and parents only.

2. What do students need to do for their adopted area?
   A. Water and weed at least twice a week.
   B. Only take photos once a month.
   C. Pay a fee to join the program.
   D. Work alone without classmates.

3. When should classes water their adopted plants?
   A. Every day in the morning.
   B. On Monday and Thursday afternoons.
   C. On Tuesday and Friday afternoons.
   D. Once every two weeks.

4. What will the winning class receive?
   A. Free plants for their classroom.
   B. A holiday from school work.
   C. Money and new tools.
   D. A certificate and a trip to the botanical garden.

5. What is the main purpose of this notice?
   A. To introduce the city botanical garden.
   B. To sell plants to students.
   C. To invite classes to adopt and care for campus greenery.
   D. To ask students to clean the school building.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We invite all classes to adopt a tree or a small garden area on our campus."

**【解析】** 原文邀请所有班级参与认养活动。B 项"只限喜欢植物的学生"原文未设此限制；C 项"其他学校学生"文中未提及；D 项"仅限教师和家长"错误，活动面向班级/学生。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Each class needs to water, weed, and take care of their adopted area at least twice a week"

**【解析】** 原文要求每班至少每周两次浇水、除草和照管认养区域。B 项"只每月拍照"是记录任务，不是主要照料职责；C 项"付费参加"原文未提及；D 项"独自完成"与"class"及轮流分工矛盾。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段表格 "Water plants — Tuesday & Friday afternoons"

**【解析】** 根据表格，浇水安排在周二和周五下午。A 项"每天早上"、B 项"周一和周四下午"和 D 项"每两周一次"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The winning class will receive a certificate and a field trip to the city botanical garden."

**【解析】** 获奖班级将获得证书和前往市植物园的实地考察机会。A 项"教室用免费植物"、B 项"免做作业假期"和 C 项"金钱和新工具"均无依据。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：标题及第②段邀请班级认养校园绿植并参与照护。

**【解析】** 全文以通知形式介绍校园绿化认养活动，目的是邀请各班认养并照料校园绿植。A 项"介绍市植物园"、B 项"向学生售植物"和 D 项"打扫校舍"均与主旨不符。故选 C。
