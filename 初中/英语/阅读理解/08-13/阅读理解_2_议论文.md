# 阅读理解（议论文）

① During holidays, should teachers give students reading tasks? This question has been discussed by parents and teachers for a long time. Some people support it, while others disagree. Let's look at both sides of the argument.

② Those who support holiday reading say it has many benefits. First, reading helps students keep their brains active during the long break. Without schoolwork, some students may spend too much time on phones or games. A reading list can guide them toward good books and useful knowledge. Second, reading improves language skills. The more students read, the better they become at understanding and writing. Third, reading during holidays can form a lifelong habit. When students grow up, they will already know that reading is a pleasant way to spend free time.

③ However, those who are against it have their reasons. Holidays are meant for rest and relaxation. Students work hard during the term and need a real break to recharge (充电). Forcing them to read may make them dislike reading instead of enjoying it. Besides, some students have to travel with their families or take part in other activities. They may not have enough time to finish the reading list. If they cannot complete it, they may feel worried or even copy reports from the Internet, which does not help learning at all.

④ In my view, the key is balance. Reading during holidays can be helpful, but it should not feel like homework. Teachers could offer a list of suggested books instead of requiring everyone to read the same ones. Students could choose books that interest them. Also, the tasks could be light — for example, reading one or two books and sharing their thoughts in a relaxed way, such as a short talk or a drawing, rather than a long written report.

⑤ Whether we agree or not, we should remember that the goal of reading is to open minds and bring joy. If holiday reading does that, it is worth trying. If it only adds stress, we need to think again.

**注释：** benefit 益处；lifelong 终生的；recharge 充电；relaxation 放松；balance 平衡

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what is one benefit of holiday reading?
   A. It helps students keep their brains active during the break.
   B. It allows students to play more games.
   C. It reduces the number of books in the library.
   D. It helps students travel more with their families.

2. Why do some people oppose holiday reading?
   A. Because reading is too easy and not useful.
   B. Because teachers do not have time to check the work.
   C. Because they think all students hate reading.
   D. Because holidays should be for rest, and forced reading may make students dislike it.

3. What does the writer suggest for holiday reading tasks?
   A. All students must read the same books and write long reports.
   B. Teachers should give no reading tasks at all.
   C. Students could choose interesting books and share thoughts in relaxed ways.
   D. Students should read at least ten books every holiday.

4. What does the word "recharge" in paragraph ③ most probably mean?
   A. To charge a phone or a battery.
   B. To study harder than before.
   C. To pay for something again.
   D. To get back energy or strength through rest.

5. What is the writer's main opinion about holiday reading?
   A. All students must read the same number of books.
   B. Reading is useless and students should only rest.
   C. It should be balanced — helpful but not like heavy homework.
   D. Holiday reading should be completely banned.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "First, reading helps students keep their brains active during the long break."

**【解析】** 原文指出支持者认为假期间阅读的好处之一是帮助学生在大假期间保持大脑活跃。B 项"玩更多游戏"与阅读益处相反；C 项"减少图书馆藏书"与主题无关；D 项"多和家人旅行"文中未将之归为阅读益处。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Holidays are meant for rest and relaxation... Forcing them to read may make them dislike reading instead of enjoying it."

**【解析】** 反对者认为假期是休息放松的时间，强制阅读可能让学生讨厌阅读而非享受。A 项"阅读太简单无用"、B 项"老师没时间检查"和 C 项"所有学生都讨厌阅读"并非反对理由。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Students could choose books that interest them... sharing their thoughts in a relaxed way, such as a short talk or a drawing, rather than a long written report."

**【解析】** 作者建议学生自选感兴趣的书，以轻松方式分享心得（如短谈或图画），而非长篇书面报告。A 项"读同一本书写长报告"与作者主张相反；B 项"完全不给阅读任务"过于极端；D 项"至少读十本"文中未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "Students work hard during the term and need a real break to recharge."

**【解析】** 语境中学生学期辛苦，需要真正休息来"recharge"，recharge 在此意为通过休息恢复精力。A 项"给手机充电"、B 项"更努力学习"和 C 项"再次付款"均不符语境。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第④段 "In my view, the key is balance. Reading during holidays can be helpful, but it should not feel like homework."

**【解析】** 作者观点是把握平衡：假期阅读有好处，但不应像繁重作业。A 项"必须读同样数量"文中未主张；B 项"阅读无用只应休息"偏激；D 项"完全禁止"与作者立场矛盾。故选 C。
