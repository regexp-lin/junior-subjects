# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Every year, millions of birds fly from one place to another. This journey is called migration. Birds that make these long trips are called migratory birds. They fly to find warmer weather, better food, or a safe place to raise their young. Migration is one of the most amazing events in nature.

② Why do birds migrate? In autumn, when the weather gets colder, food becomes harder to find in northern areas. Birds fly south to warmer places where they can find insects, seeds, and fruit. In spring, when the north becomes warm again, they fly back. Some birds travel very far. For example, the Arctic tern flies from the Arctic to the Antarctic and back every year. That is a distance of about 40,000 kilometers.

③ How do birds find their way? Scientists believe that birds use several methods. They may use the sun and stars to guide them. They may also sense the Earth's magnetic field. Some birds remember the way from the year before. Young birds often follow older birds that have made the trip before.

④ Migration is not easy. Birds face many dangers along the way. Bad weather can make them tired or lose their way. They need places to rest and eat. Lakes, wetlands, and forests are important "rest stops" for migrating birds. If these places are destroyed, birds may not have enough energy to finish their journey.

⑤ People can help protect migrating birds. We can keep wetlands and forests healthy. We can also reduce light pollution at night, because bright lights can confuse birds. By protecting these amazing travelers, we protect a precious part of our natural world.

**注释：** migration 迁徙；migratory 迁徙的；Arctic tern 北极燕鸥

**Questions:**

1. What is migration?
   A. Birds building nests in trees.
   B. Birds flying from one place to another every year.
   C. Birds learning to sing new songs.
   D. Birds making friends with other birds.

2. Why do birds fly south in autumn?
   A. Because they dislike cold weather.
   B. Because they want to find warmer places with more food.
   C. Because they are afraid of snow.
   D. Because people ask them to leave.

3. How do birds find their way during migration according to the passage?
   A. They use maps and GPS.
   B. They follow cars and planes.
   C. They may use the sun, stars, the Earth's magnetic field, or memory.
   D. They ask other animals for directions.

4. What danger do migrating birds face?
   A. They have too much food to eat.
   B. Bad weather and lack of rest stops can make their journey difficult.
   C. Other birds will attack them.
   D. People always catch them.

5. What can people do to help migrating birds?
   A. Feed them every day during migration.
   B. Keep wetlands and forests healthy and reduce light pollution at night.
   C. Build more tall buildings for them to rest.
   D. Catch them and keep them in zoos.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-3句 "Every year, millions of birds fly from one place to another. This journey is called migration."

**【解析】** 原文说明鸟类每年从一地飞往另一地的旅程叫作迁徙。B项准确。A、C、D项与migration定义不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2-3句 "In autumn, when the weather gets colder, food becomes harder to find in northern areas. Birds fly south to warmer places where they can find insects, seeds, and fruit."

**【解析】** 秋天北方变冷、食物减少，鸟类南飞寻找更温暖、食物更丰富的地方。B项正确。A项不完整；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They may use the sun and stars to guide them. They may also sense the Earth's magnetic field. Some birds remember the way from the year before."

**【解析】** 文中提到太阳、星星、地磁场和记忆等方式。C项概括全面。A、B、D项原文未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Bad weather can make them tired or lose their way. They need places to rest and eat... If these places are destroyed, birds may not have enough energy to finish their journey."

**【解析】** 迁徙中的困难包括恶劣天气、迷路以及缺乏休息和觅食地。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "We can keep wetlands and forests healthy. We can also reduce light pollution at night, because bright lights can confuse birds."

**【解析】** 建议包括保护湿地和森林、减少夜间光污染。B项准确。A、C、D项原文未提及或与建议相反。故选B。
