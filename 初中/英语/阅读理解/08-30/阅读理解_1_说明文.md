# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Water is one of the most important resources on Earth. About 71% of our planet's surface is covered by water, but most of it is salt water in the oceans. Only about 2.5% of the world's water is fresh water, and much of that is locked in ice at the North and South Poles. This means that the water we can actually use — from rivers, lakes, and underground — is very limited.

② Why is water so important? All living things need water to survive. Humans use water for drinking, cooking, washing, and farming. Plants need water to grow. Without enough clean water, people can get sick, crops can fail, and wildlife can die. In many parts of the world, people still do not have easy access to safe drinking water. Women and children often walk long distances every day just to get water for their families.

③ What are the main problems with water today? First, water pollution is serious. Factories and farms sometimes pour waste into rivers and lakes. Plastic and other trash end up in the ocean, harming marine (海洋的) life. Second, many places face water shortage. As the population grows and cities expand (扩大), demand for water increases. Third, climate change is making some areas drier while others get more floods. This makes it harder to manage water resources.

④ What can we do to protect and save water? At home, we can turn off the tap when we brush our teeth, take shorter showers, and fix leaking pipes. At school, we can spread the message about saving water and avoid wasting it when washing hands. We can also reduce pollution by not throwing litter into rivers or the sea. Governments and companies need to work together to clean up polluted water and use water more efficiently.

⑤ Water is a precious gift. We must use it wisely and protect it for future generations. Every small action we take today can make a big difference tomorrow.

**注释：** marine 海洋的；expand 扩大；precious 珍贵的

**Questions:**

1. What percentage of the world's water is fresh water?
   A. About 71%.
   B. About 2.5%.
   C. About 50%.
   D. About 10%.

2. According to Paragraph ②, why do some women and children walk long distances every day?
   A. To go to school.
   B. To get water for their families.
   C. To find jobs.
   D. To exercise.

3. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a water problem?
   A. Water pollution.
   B. Water shortage.
   C. Climate change affecting water.
   D. Too much rain everywhere.

4. What can we do at home to save water?
   A. Pour waste into rivers.
   B. Turn off the tap when brushing teeth and take shorter showers.
   C. Throw plastic into the ocean.
   D. Use as much water as possible.

5. What is the main idea of the passage?
   A. Water is limited and precious; we should protect and save it.
   B. The ocean is very large and we need not worry about water.
   C. Only factories cause water problems.
   D. Children should not use water.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Only about 2.5% of the world's water is fresh water"

**【解析】** 原文明确写出淡水资源约占全球水量的2.5%。A项71%是地球表面被水覆盖的比例；C项和D项原文未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Women and children often walk long distances every day just to get water for their families."

**【解析】** 原文说明妇女和儿童每天长途跋涉是为了给家人取水。A项"上学"、C项"找工作"和D项"锻炼"均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段列举了 water pollution、water shortage、climate change

**【解析】** 文中提到的水问题包括污染、短缺和气候变化的影响。D项"到处雨水太多"与原文"some areas drier while others get more floods"不符，且非主要并列问题。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "we can turn off the tap when we brush our teeth, take shorter showers, and fix leaking pipes"

**【解析】** 原文列举了刷牙时关水、缩短淋浴时间、修理漏水管道等家庭节水方法。A项和C项为污染行为；D项与节约用水相反。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第①段对水资源有限的说明、第④段保护措施及第⑤段 "Water is a precious gift. We must use it wisely and protect it."

**【解析】** 文章围绕水资源有限、珍贵，呼吁保护与节约展开。B项与文意相反；C项以偏概全；D项属于无中生有。故选A。
