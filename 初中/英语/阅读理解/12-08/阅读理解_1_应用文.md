# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Club Recruitment — Join Us!**

① Dear students,

The new term has started. Our school clubs are now recruiting (招募) new members. Find a club that suits you and sign up before December 15!

**Club List:**

| Club | Activity | Meeting Time | Room |
|------|----------|--------------|------|
| Photography Club | Take photos, learn editing | Tue & Thu 4:00 p.m. | Art Room 201 |
| Chess Club | Play chess, join tournaments | Mon & Wed 4:30 p.m. | Room 305 |
| Cooking Club | Learn easy recipes | Friday 4:00 p.m. | Home Ec Room |
| Robot Club | Build and programme robots | Sat 9:00 a.m. | Science Lab |

② **How to join:**
- Fill in the form at the Student Centre or online at www.ourschool.edu.cn/clubs
- Each student can join up to 2 clubs
- No experience needed for most clubs — beginners welcome!

③ **Important:**
- Club meetings start from the week of December 16
- If a club has too many sign-ups, we will hold a simple interview or draw lots
- Questions? Contact Mr Li at room 104 or email clubs@ourschool.edu.cn

④ We hope you will find a club you love. See you there!

Student Union  
December 5, 2025

**Questions:**

1. By when should students sign up for a club?
   A. December 5.
   B. December 15.
   C. December 16.
   D. January 1.

2. When does the Chess Club meet?
   A. Tuesday and Thursday at 4:00 p.m.
   B. Monday and Wednesday at 4:30 p.m.
   C. Friday at 4:00 p.m.
   D. Saturday at 9:00 a.m.

3. Where does the Cooking Club meet?
   A. Room 305.
   B. Art Room 201.
   C. Home Ec Room.
   D. Science Lab.

4. How many clubs can each student join?
   A. Only one.
   B. Up to two.
   C. Three.
   D. As many as they want.

5. What happens if a club has too many sign-ups?
   A. The club will be closed.
   B. There will be a simple interview or draw lots.
   C. Everyone can join.
   D. Students must pay extra.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "sign up before December 15!"

**【解析】** 报名截止日期为12月15日。A项12月5日是通知日期；C项12月16日是社团活动开始周；D项1月1日文中未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 Chess Club 一行 "Mon & Wed 4:30 p.m."

**【解析】** 棋类社团周一、周三下午4:30活动。A项为摄影社时间；C项为烹饪社；D项为机器人社。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 Cooking Club 一行 "Home Ec Room"

**【解析】** 烹饪社在家政教室活动。A、B、D项分别为棋类、摄影、机器人社场地。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Each student can join up to 2 clubs"

**【解析】** 每位学生最多可加入2个社团。A项仅一个、C项三个、D项不限均与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "If a club has too many sign-ups, we will hold a simple interview or draw lots"

**【解析】** 报名人数过多时会进行简单面试或抽签。A项关闭、C项全部录取、D项额外付费均与原文不符。故选B。
