> **12-08 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Camping Trip**

① Last summer, my class went on a two-day camping trip to a mountain near our city. I had never slept in a tent before. I was excited but also a little nervous.

② We arrived at the campsite (营地) in the afternoon. Our teacher showed us how to put up a tent. We worked in groups of four. At first, we could not find the right poles and the tent kept falling down. After several tries, we finally did it. My group high-fived each other. Then we collected dry wood for a campfire. The teacher said we would need it to cook and keep warm at night.

③ When it got dark, we sat around the campfire. We told stories and sang songs. Someone brought a guitar and we sang until our voices were hoarse. The stars above were so bright — you never see so many in the city. I lay back and looked up at the sky. For the first time, I felt very close to nature.

④ That night, I could not sleep well at first. The ground was hard and I heard strange sounds outside. "It's just the wind and maybe some insects," my tent-mate said. I tried to relax and finally fell asleep. When I woke up, the birds were singing and the sun was rising. I had never felt so refreshed (精神焕发的).

⑤ The camping trip taught me that we can enjoy simple things. We don't need Wi-Fi or games to have fun. Sometimes, being in nature with good friends is enough. I hope we can go camping again next year.

**注释：** campsite 营地；hoarse 沙哑的；refreshed 精神焕发的

---

**Questions:**

1. How long was the camping trip?
   A. One day.
   B. Two days.
   C. One week.
   D. Three days.

2. What problem did the writer's group have when putting up the tent?
   A. They had no tent.
   B. They could not find the right poles and the tent kept falling down.
   C. The teacher did not help them.
   D. It was too dark.

3. What did the writer do around the campfire?
   A. Only cooked food.
   B. Told stories, sang songs, and someone played the guitar.
   C. Slept early.
   D. Played phone games.

4. Why couldn't the writer sleep well at first that night?
   A. The tent was too small.
   B. The ground was hard and he heard strange sounds outside.
   C. His tent-mate was noisy.
   D. It was too cold.

5. What did the writer learn from the trip?
   A. Camping is dangerous.
   B. We can enjoy simple things; being in nature with friends can be enough.
   C. Wi-Fi is important.
   D. He never wants to go camping again.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "my class went on a two-day camping trip"

**【解析】** 原文明确是两天的露营。A项一天、C项一周、D项三天均错误。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "At first, we could not find the right poles and the tent kept falling down."

**【解析】** 搭帐篷时找不到合适的杆子，帐篷一直倒下。A项没帐篷、C项老师不帮、D项太暗均非主要原因。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "We told stories and sang songs. Someone brought a guitar and we sang until our voices were hoarse."

**【解析】** 围着篝火讲故事、唱歌，有人弹吉他。A项只做饭不完整；C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The ground was hard and I heard strange sounds outside."

**【解析】** 地面硬、听到外面有奇怪声音导致初时难入睡。A项帐篷小、C项同伴吵、D项太冷均非原文所述。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The camping trip taught me that we can enjoy simple things... being in nature with good friends is enough."

**【解析】** 作者领悟到可以享受简单事物，与朋友亲近自然就足够了。A、C、D项与文章主旨相反。故选B。
