# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In many countries, students are required to wear school uniforms (校服). Some students love them, while others wish they could wear their own clothes every day. Should schools require uniforms? People have different opinions on this question.

② Those who support school uniforms believe they bring many benefits. First, uniforms make all students look the same, which helps reduce bullying (欺凌) based on clothing. When everyone wears the same outfit (服装), no one is judged for wearing expensive or cheap clothes. Second, uniforms can save families money in the long run because parents do not have to buy many fashionable (时尚的) clothes for their children. Third, uniforms create a sense of belonging (归属感) and school pride.

③ On the other hand, some people argue that uniforms take away students' freedom to express (表达) themselves. Clothing is one way young people show their personality (个性) and creativity (创造力). If everyone looks the same, students may feel like they have no voice. Also, some uniforms are not comfortable — they may be too hot in summer or too thin in winter. A few students also complain (抱怨) that their school uniforms are ugly and make them feel unhappy.

④ There is also a practical concern. School uniforms can actually cost quite a lot of money in some schools, especially when families must buy separate uniforms for summer and winter, plus sports uniforms. For low-income (低收入的) families, this can be a real burden (负担). So the argument that uniforms save money does not always hold true.

⑤ In my view, school uniforms have more advantages than disadvantages. However, schools should listen to students' opinions when designing uniforms. If the uniform is comfortable, reasonably priced, and looks nice, most students would be happy to wear it. The key is to find a balance (平衡) between rules and students' needs.

**注释：** uniform 校服；bullying 欺凌；outfit 服装；fashionable 时尚的；belonging 归属感；personality 个性；creativity 创造力；complain 抱怨；burden 负担；balance 平衡

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, how do uniforms help reduce bullying?
   A. By making all students study the same subjects.
   B. By making all students look the same so no one is judged by clothing.
   C. By punishing students who bully others.
   D. By teaching students to be kind in class.

2. What is one argument against school uniforms in paragraph ③?
   A. Uniforms are too expensive for all families.
   B. Uniforms make students study harder.
   C. Uniforms take away students' freedom to express themselves.
   D. Uniforms cause students to fight with each other.

3. What does the word "burden" in paragraph ④ most probably mean?
   A. Something that is easy and fun.
   B. A heavy load or difficulty.
   C. A kind of school rule.
   D. A type of clothing.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④?
   A. All school uniforms are very cheap.
   B. Families never need to buy more than one uniform.
   C. The claim that uniforms always save money is not completely true.
   D. Low-income families prefer to buy more uniforms.

5. What is the writer's final opinion about school uniforms?
   A. Schools should stop requiring uniforms immediately.
   B. Uniforms have more advantages, but schools should consider students' opinions.
   C. Students should design their own uniforms at home.
   D. Only rich schools should require uniforms.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "uniforms make all students look the same, which helps reduce bullying based on clothing. When everyone wears the same outfit, no one is judged for wearing expensive or cheap clothes."

**【解析】** 原文说校服让所有学生看起来一样，这样就没有人因为穿着贵贱而被评判，从而减少欺凌。A 项"学相同科目"与穿着无关；C 项"惩罚欺凌者"文中未提及；D 项"教学生善良"也不是文中的论点。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "some people argue that uniforms take away students' freedom to express themselves."

**【解析】** 反对校服的一个论点是它剥夺了学生表达自我的自由。A 项"太贵"是第④段的内容，不是第③段的论点；B 项"让学生学习更努力"原文未提及；D 项"导致打架"属于无中生有。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "For low-income families, this can be a real burden."

**【解析】** 前文提到低收入家庭需要购买多套校服花费不小，因此 burden 在此语境中意为"沉重的负担/困难"。A 项"轻松有趣"与上下文矛盾；C 项"校规"和 D 项"一种服装"均不符合语境。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "School uniforms can actually cost quite a lot of money in some schools... So the argument that uniforms save money does not always hold true."

**【解析】** 作者指出某些学校校服实际花费不少，因此"校服省钱"的说法并不总是成立，可推断这一说法不完全正确。A 项"所有校服都很便宜"与原文矛盾；B 项"家庭不需要买多套"与"separate uniforms for summer and winter, plus sports uniforms"矛盾；D 项"低收入家庭更愿意买更多校服"属于无中生有。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "school uniforms have more advantages than disadvantages. However, schools should listen to students' opinions when designing uniforms."

**【解析】** 作者认为校服利大于弊，但学校应在设计校服时听取学生意见。A 项"立即取消校服"与作者观点相反；C 项"学生在家自己设计"原文未提及；D 项"只有富裕学校才需要"属于无中生有。故选 B。
