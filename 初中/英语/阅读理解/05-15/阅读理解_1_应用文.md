# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Maple Street Middle School — Second-Hand Market**

② Got things you no longer need? Want to find a bargain (便宜货)? Come to our school's Second-Hand Market! It's a great way to save money, reduce waste, and have fun.

③ **Date & Time:** Saturday, May 24, 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
**Place:** School Playground (in case of rain, it will be moved to the school gym)

④ **Rules for Sellers:**

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| Who can sell? | All students in Grades 7–9 and their parents |
| Table fee | ¥5 per table (sign up at the Student Council office before May 20) |
| What can you sell? | Books, clothes, toys, sports equipment, small electronics |
| What can't you sell? | Food, drinks, medicine, or anything broken or dangerous |
| Price limit | Every item must be priced at ¥50 or below |

⑤ **Rules for Buyers:**
- Bring your own shopping bag — no plastic bags will be provided
- Cash (现金) only — no online payment accepted
- All sales are final (最终的) — no returns or exchanges (不退不换)

⑥ **Special Activity:** A "Lucky Draw" (抽奖) will be held at 12:00 PM. Every buyer who spends ¥20 or more will receive one free lucky draw ticket. Prizes include school notebooks, water bottles, and a **grand prize** — a brand-new basketball!

⑦ All profits (利润) from table fees will be donated (捐赠) to the school's Helping Hands Fund, which supports students in need. So come and join us — shop smart, help others, and have a wonderful Saturday!

**注释：** bargain 便宜货；equipment 设备；electronic 电子产品；cash 现金；profit 利润；donate 捐赠

---

### 选择题

1. Where will the Second-Hand Market be held if the weather is fine?
   A. In the school library.
   B. In the school gym.
   C. On the school playground.
   D. In the school canteen.

2. Which of the following items can you sell at the market?
   A. A bottle of juice.
   B. Some cold medicine.
   C. A broken chair.
   D. A pair of old sports shoes.

3. What must buyers bring to the market?
   A. Their student ID card.
   B. Their own shopping bag.
   C. A credit card for payment.
   D. A permission letter from their parents.

4. How can a buyer get a lucky draw ticket?
   A. By arriving before 9:00 AM.
   B. By signing up at the Student Council office.
   C. By spending ¥20 or more at the market.
   D. By selling at least five items.

5. What will happen to the money collected from table fees?
   A. It will be used to buy new playground equipment.
   B. It will be given back to the sellers after the market.
   C. It will be donated to support students in need.
   D. It will be used to pay for the Lucky Draw prizes.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Place: School Playground (in case of rain, it will be moved to the school gym)"

**【解析】** 天气好时在学校操场举行，下雨才转移到体育馆。A 项"图书馆"和 D 项"食堂"原文未提及；B 项"体育馆"是雨天备选地点，不是天气好时的场地。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段表格 "What can you sell? Books, clothes, toys, sports equipment, small electronics" 以及 "What can't you sell? Food, drinks, medicine, or anything broken or dangerous"

**【解析】** 可售物品包括书籍、衣服、玩具、体育用品和小型电子产品，旧运动鞋属于衣物/体育用品类。A 项果汁属于饮料不可卖；B 项感冒药属于药品不可卖；C 项坏椅子属于损坏物品不可卖。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Bring your own shopping bag — no plastic bags will be provided"

**【解析】** 原文要求买家自带购物袋，因为不提供塑料袋。A 项"学生证"、C 项"信用卡"（原文说只收现金）和 D 项"家长许可信"均未在买家规则中提及。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Every buyer who spends ¥20 or more will receive one free lucky draw ticket."

**【解析】** 原文明确说消费满20元即可获得一张免费抽奖券。A 项"9点前到达"、B 项"在学生会登记"（那是卖家报名）和 D 项"卖出至少5件物品"均与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "All profits from table fees will be donated to the school's Helping Hands Fund, which supports students in need."

**【解析】** 原文说桌位费的全部利润将捐给学校的"爱心基金"，用于资助有需要的学生。A 项"买操场设备"、B 项"退还给卖家"和 D 项"支付抽奖奖品"均属于无中生有。故选 C。
