# 阅读理解（应用文）

**City Zoo — Tickets and Opening Hours**

① Welcome to the City Zoo! We are home to over 2,000 animals from around the world. Before your visit, please read the following information.

**Ticket Prices:**

| Visitor Type    | Price (per person) |
|-----------------|--------------------|
| Adult (18–59)   | 80 yuan            |
| Child (6–17)    | 40 yuan            |
| Senior (60+)    | 40 yuan            |
| Child (under 6) | Free              |

② The zoo is open every day except Mondays. Opening hours are 8:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. Last entry is at 4:30 p.m. Please allow at least three hours for a full visit.

③ You can buy tickets at the gate or online at www.cityzoo.com. Online tickets are 5 yuan cheaper. Students with a valid student ID get a 20% discount. Group bookings (20 people or more) can call 020-88881234 for a special rate.

④ Inside the zoo, you will find a cafe, gift shop, and rest areas. Feeding the animals is not allowed. Please keep the park clean. Photography is welcome, but flash is not allowed in the indoor areas.

⑤ For the best experience, visit on weekdays when it is less crowded. We look forward to seeing you!

City Zoo  
October 25th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. How much does an adult ticket cost?
   A. 40 yuan.
   B. 80 yuan.
   C. 5 yuan.
   D. Free.

2. When is the zoo closed?
   A. On Sundays.
   B. On Mondays.
   C. On weekends.
   D. Never.

3. What is the last entry time?
   A. 5:30 p.m.
   B. 4:30 p.m.
   C. 8:30 a.m.
   D. 6:00 p.m.

4. How can students get a discount?
   A. By visiting on Monday.
   B. By showing a valid student ID.
   C. By buying food at the cafe.
   D. By feeding the animals.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. The zoo is only for children.
   B. Visitors should plan enough time and follow the rules for a good experience.
   C. Flash photography is allowed everywhere.
   D. Weekends are the best time to visit.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Adult (18–59) 对应 80 yuan
**【解析】** 成人票80元。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The zoo is open every day except Mondays"
**【解析】** 动物园每周一闭馆。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Last entry is at 4:30 p.m."
**【解析】** 最后入园时间为下午4:30。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Students with a valid student ID get a 20% discount"
**【解析】** 持有效学生证可享8折。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "allow at least three hours"；第④段 禁止喂食、禁止室内闪光；第⑤段 "visit on weekdays"
**【解析】** 文中建议预留时间、遵守规则、平日参观，可推知游客需合理规划并守规才能获得更好体验。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
