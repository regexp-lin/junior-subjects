# 阅读理解 A（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① When I was thirteen, my family moved to a new city. I had to leave my old friends behind and start over at a new school. On my first day, I felt so nervous that I could hardly speak to anyone. During lunchtime, I sat alone at a table in the corner of the dining hall.

② A girl with short hair and bright eyes walked over to me. "Hi, I'm Lucy. Is this seat taken?" she asked with a warm smile. I shook my head and she sat down. She told me that she had also been a new student two years ago, so she understood how I felt. "It's hard at first, but it gets better," she said.

③ Over the next few weeks, Lucy introduced me to her friends and showed me around the school. She helped me join the school art club because she knew I loved drawing. Slowly, I began to feel that the new school wasn't so bad after all. I even started to enjoy going to school every day.

④ One day, I found out that Lucy was moving to another city because of her father's job. I felt very sad. On her last day, I gave her a drawing I had made — it was a picture of the two of us sitting at that lunch table where we first met. Lucy looked at it and her eyes became wet. "I'll keep it forever," she whispered.

⑤ That was three years ago. Lucy and I still keep in touch through video calls every week. I've learned that true friendship doesn't depend on distance. Lucy taught me something important: a small act of kindness can change someone's life completely.

---

**注释：** keep in touch 保持联系

---

### 题目

1. How did the writer feel on the first day at the new school?
   A. Excited and happy.
   B. Nervous and lonely.
   C. Angry and upset.
   D. Bored and tired.

2. Why did Lucy understand how the writer felt?
   A. Because Lucy had read about it in a book.
   B. Because Lucy's teacher told her about the writer.
   C. Because Lucy had been a new student before.
   D. Because Lucy had moved to the city with the writer.

3. What did the writer give Lucy on her last day?
   A. A photo of the school.
   B. A drawing of them together.
   C. A letter about their friendship.
   D. A book about art.

4. The underlined word "whispered" in Paragraph ④ probably means "________."
   A. said something in a very quiet voice
   B. shouted loudly with excitement
   C. laughed happily with others
   D. cried out in a sad voice

5. What is the best title for the passage?
   A. My New School Life
   B. The Art Club
   C. A Small Act of Kindness
   D. Moving to a New City

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "On my first day, I felt so nervous that I could hardly speak to anyone." 及第4句 "During lunchtime, I sat alone at a table in the corner of the dining hall."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到作者在新学校的第一天非常紧张（nervous），几乎不敢和任何人说话，午饭时独自坐在餐厅角落的桌子旁，由此可以判断作者既紧张又孤独（lonely）。A 项"兴奋和开心"与原文情感相反；C 项"愤怒和不安"原文未提及愤怒的情绪；D 项"无聊和疲倦"原文无依据。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第4句 "She told me that she had also been a new student two years ago, so she understood how I felt."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明 Lucy 两年前也曾是新生，所以她能理解作者的感受。A 项"从书上读到过"原文未提及；B 项"老师告诉她的"属于无中生有；D 项"和作者一起搬来的"与原文不符，Lucy 是两年前来的。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-3句 "On her last day, I gave her a drawing I had made — it was a picture of the two of us sitting at that lunch table where we first met."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出作者送给 Lucy 的是一幅画（a drawing），画的是她们两人坐在第一次见面时的那张餐桌旁。A 项"一张学校照片"张冠李戴；C 项"一封关于友谊的信"和 D 项"一本关于美术的书"均属于无中生有。故选 B。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "Lucy looked at it and her eyes became wet. 'I'll keep it forever,' she whispered."

**【解析】** 根据上下文，Lucy 看到画后眼眶湿润（eyes became wet），说明她非常感动，在这种情感状态下说"我会永远保存它"，语气应当是轻柔的、低声的。A 项"用很小的声音说"符合语境；B 项"兴奋地大声喊"与感动流泪的语境不符；C 项"和别人一起开心地笑"原文无依据；D 项"用悲伤的声音哭喊"程度过重，whisper 强调"低声"而非"哭喊"。故选 A。

**【技巧点拨】** 词义猜测题需结合上下文情境推断。注意分析该词所在句子的前后语境和情感氛围。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段末句 "Lucy taught me something important: a small act of kindness can change someone's life completely."

**【解析】** 文章讲述了作者转学后因 Lucy 的主动友善（一个小小的善意举动——主动搭话）而融入新环境并建立深厚友谊的故事。最后一段点题，强调"一个小小的善意行为可以完全改变一个人的生活"。C 项"一个小小的善举"最能概括全文主旨。A 项"我的新学校生活"过于宽泛，未突出核心主题；B 项"美术社"只是文中一个细节；D 项"搬到新城市"只是故事的背景，不是主旨。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 主旨题应关注文章首尾段，特别是最后一段的总结性语句，往往直接揭示文章主旨。
