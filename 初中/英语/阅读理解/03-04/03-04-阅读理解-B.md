# 阅读理解 B（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Sleep is very important for teenagers. According to scientists, teenagers between 13 and 18 need about eight to ten hours of sleep every night. However, a recent study shows that over 70% of middle school students in China sleep less than eight hours on school nights. This has become a serious health problem.

② Why don't teenagers get enough sleep? There are several reasons. First, many students have too much homework. They often stay up late to finish it. Second, the use of electronic devices (电子设备) before bed is a big problem. The blue light from phones and tablets makes it harder for the brain to relax and fall asleep. Third, some students have after-school classes that don't end until 9 or 10 p.m., leaving very little time for sleep.

③ What happens when teenagers don't get enough sleep? The results can be worrying. Students who sleep less often have trouble paying attention in class. Their memory becomes weaker, which makes it more difficult for them to learn new things. Lack of sleep can also make teenagers feel unhappy or even angry easily. In the long run, it may lead to health problems like getting sick more often.

④ So how can teenagers improve their sleep? Experts suggest some useful ways. First, try to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends. This helps the body build a regular sleep pattern. Second, stop using phones or computers at least 30 minutes before going to bed. Instead, try reading a book or listening to soft music. Third, do some exercise during the day, but not too close to bedtime. Exercise can help the body feel tired in a natural way. Finally, keep the bedroom dark and quiet to create a good sleeping environment.

⑤ Getting enough sleep is not a waste of time. In fact, it is one of the best things teenagers can do for their studies and health. As a popular saying goes, "Early to bed and early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy and wise."

---

**注释：** lack 缺乏；experts 专家；pattern 模式

---

### 题目

1. According to the study mentioned in Paragraph ①, what is the problem with middle school students?
   A. They sleep too much on weekends.
   B. Most of them sleep less than eight hours on school nights.
   C. They don't like to go to bed early.
   D. They need more than ten hours of sleep.

2. How many reasons are given in Paragraph ② to explain why teenagers don't sleep enough?
   A. Two.
   B. Three.
   C. Four.
   D. Five.

3. According to Paragraph ③, which of the following is NOT a result of lack of sleep?
   A. Having trouble paying attention in class.
   B. Getting better grades at school.
   C. Feeling unhappy or angry easily.
   D. Getting sick more often.

4. We can infer from Paragraph ④ that ________.
   A. teenagers should exercise right before going to bed
   B. using phones before bed helps teenagers fall asleep
   C. a regular sleep schedule is helpful for good sleep
   D. sleeping in a bright room is good for teenagers

5. The writer wrote this passage mainly to ________.
   A. tell an interesting story about sleep
   B. introduce some new electronic devices
   C. explain why homework is bad for students
   D. help teenagers understand and improve their sleep

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "However, a recent study shows that over 70% of middle school students in China sleep less than eight hours on school nights."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出，一项最近的研究表明超过70%的中学生在上学日晚上睡眠不足8小时。B 项与原文信息完全吻合。A 项"周末睡太多"原文未提及；C 项"他们不喜欢早睡"是一种主观推测，原文未涉及喜好问题；D 项"他们需要超过10小时的睡眠"与原文"8到10小时"不符，且问的是"问题"而非"需求"。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "First, many students have too much homework." "Second, the use of electronic devices before bed is a big problem." "Third, some students have after-school classes..."

**【解析】** 第②段用 First、Second、Third 分别列举了三个原因：作业太多、睡前使用电子设备、课外班结束太晚。共计三个原因。A 项"两个"不完整；C 项"四个"和 D 项"五个"超出原文内容。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 细节计数题需要在原文中逐一找到并标记关键信号词（First, Second, Third, Also, Besides 等），确保不遗漏也不多算。

---

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向选择）

**【定位】** 第③段列举了睡眠不足的后果："have trouble paying attention in class"、"memory becomes weaker"、"feel unhappy or even angry easily"、"getting sick more often"。

**【解析】** 本题要求选出"不是"睡眠不足后果的选项。A 项"上课注意力不集中"、C 项"容易感到不开心或愤怒"、D 项"更容易生病"均在原文中有明确对应。B 项"在学校取得更好的成绩"原文不仅未提及，而且与原文信息相矛盾——睡眠不足会导致记忆力下降、学习困难，成绩应该是下降而非提高。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 逆向选择题（选 NOT TRUE 的选项）需要逐一核实每个选项与原文的对应关系，用排除法找出无依据或矛盾的选项。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段第2-3句 "First, try to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends. This helps the body build a regular sleep pattern."

**【解析】** 原文建议每天（包括周末）在固定时间入睡和起床，这有助于身体建立规律的睡眠模式。由此可以推断，规律的作息时间表有利于获得良好的睡眠。C 项"规律的睡眠时间表有助于良好睡眠"是对原文的合理推断。A 项"睡前应该运动"与原文"but not too close to bedtime"矛盾；B 项"睡前使用手机有助于入睡"与原文"stop using phones...at least 30 minutes before going to bed"矛盾；D 项"在明亮的房间里睡觉对青少年有好处"与原文"keep the bedroom dark and quiet"矛盾。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文结构：第①段引出问题（青少年睡眠不足）→ 第②段分析原因 → 第③段阐述危害 → 第④段提出建议 → 第⑤段总结呼吁。

**【解析】** 纵观全文，作者围绕青少年的睡眠问题展开，先提出问题，再分析原因和危害，最后给出改善建议。整篇文章的目的是帮助青少年理解睡眠的重要性并改善他们的睡眠状况。D 项"帮助青少年理解并改善他们的睡眠"最准确地概括了写作意图。A 项"讲述一个关于睡眠的有趣故事"体裁不符，本文是说明文而非记叙文；B 项"介绍一些新的电子设备"以偏概全，电子设备只是导致睡眠不足的原因之一；C 项"解释为什么家庭作业对学生有害"以偏概全，作业也只是原因之一，且文章主旨不在于批评作业。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 写作意图题需要从全文结构和主题出发，而非局限于某一段落的细节。注意区分"文中提到的具体信息"和"作者写这篇文章的根本目的"。
