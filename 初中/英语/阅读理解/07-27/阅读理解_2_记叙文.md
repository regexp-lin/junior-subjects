# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Li Ming loved spending time in the school library. One Tuesday afternoon, he was looking for a book on the history shelf when he noticed something unusual. An old diary lay behind a few thick books. Its cover was dusty and the pages had turned yellow. He wondered who had left it there and how long it had been hidden.

② Li Ming picked up the diary carefully. On the first page, he saw a name: Wang Xiaomei, Class 3, Grade 2, 2008. It had been left there sixteen years ago! He started to read. The diary described simple daily life: classes, homework, friends, and small dreams. Wang Xiaomei wrote about her wish to become a teacher one day. She also wrote about her best friend and the fun they had together.

③ Li Ming felt touched. He wanted to find the owner. He asked the librarian if there were any records of students from 2008. The librarian smiled and said, "I remember Wang Xiaomei. She's now a teacher at a primary school in our city. She often comes back to visit the library." She gave Li Ming a phone number.

④ That evening, Li Ming called Wang Xiaomei. When he told her about the diary, she was very surprised and happy. "I thought I had lost it forever!" she said. "Thank you for finding it. Would you like to meet? I'd like to thank you in person."

⑤ The next Saturday, Wang Xiaomei came to the school. She and Li Ming met in the library. She thanked him and said, "This diary reminds me of my teenage years. You've done something wonderful." Li Ming felt proud. A simple act of returning a lost item had brought joy to someone and made a new friend.

**注释：** diary 日记；dusty 布满灰尘的；primary 初级的；teenage 青少年的

### 选择题

1. Where did Li Ming find the old diary?
   A. On the history shelf in the school library, behind some thick books.
   B. Under his desk in the classroom.
   C. In his bag.
   D. On the librarian's desk.

2. According to paragraph ②, what did Wang Xiaomei write about in her diary?
   A. Only her homework.
   B. Classes, homework, friends, small dreams, her wish to become a teacher, and fun with her best friend.
   C. Nothing about her life.
   D. Only her exam scores.

3. How did the librarian help Li Ming?
   A. She asked him to keep the diary.
   B. She said she had no records at all.
   C. She told him Wang Xiaomei was now a teacher and gave him her phone number.
   D. She refused to help.

4. What can we infer from Wang Xiaomei's words "I thought I had lost it forever!"?
   A. She had been looking for the diary and missed it very much.
   B. She never cared about the diary.
   C. She had thrown it away on purpose.
   D. She gave it to someone else long ago.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Old diaries are worth a lot of money.
   B. Students should never touch things in the library.
   C. Librarians know everything about past students.
   D. Returning lost things and showing kindness can bring joy and create meaningful connections.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "he was looking for a book on the history shelf when he noticed something unusual. An old diary lay behind a few thick books."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明李明在图书馆历史书架找书时，发现几本厚书后面有一本旧日记。A项准确复述发现地点。B项"课桌下"、C项"书包里"、D项"图书管理员桌上"均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The diary described simple daily life: classes, homework, friends, and small dreams. Wang Xiaomei wrote about her wish to become a teacher one day. She also wrote about her best friend and the fun they had together."

**【解析】** 原文列出日记内容：课堂、作业、朋友、小梦想、想当老师的愿望、与好友的趣事。B项全面概括。A项"仅作业"、C项"与生活无关"、D项"仅考试成绩"均不完整或错误。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "'I remember Wang Xiaomei. She's now a teacher at a primary school in our city... She often comes back to visit the library.' She gave Li Ming a phone number."

**【解析】** 原文说明图书管理员记得王小梅，告诉李明她现为小学教师，并提供了电话号码。C项正确。A项"让他留着日记"、B项"说没有记录"、D项"拒绝帮助"均与原文矛盾。故选C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "I thought I had lost it forever!"

**【解析】** 王小梅说"我还以为永远丢了"，可推知她曾寻找或怀念这本日记，对失而复得非常惊喜。A项正确。B项"从不在意"、C项"故意扔掉"、D项"早已送给别人"均与"lost it forever"的情感不符。故选A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 写作意图/主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文及第⑤段 "A simple act of returning a lost item had brought joy to someone and made a new friend."

**【解析】** 文章通过李明归还日记、与王小梅结识的故事，传达"归还失物、展现善意能带来快乐并建立有意义联系"的主题。D项最能概括。A项"旧日记值钱"、B项"不应动图书馆物品"、C项"管理员无所不知"均非主旨。故选D。
