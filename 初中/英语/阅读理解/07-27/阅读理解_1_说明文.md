# 阅读理解（说明文）

① A sandstorm is a natural disaster that happens when strong winds blow large amounts of sand and dust into the air. The sky turns yellow or brown, and it becomes difficult to see clearly. Sandstorms often occur in dry areas where there is little rain and the ground has no grass or trees to hold the soil.

② Why do sandstorms happen? There are several main causes. First, dry weather and little rainfall make the land very dry. The soil becomes loose and easy to blow away. Second, too much farming and overgrazing (过度放牧) damage the grasslands. Without enough plants to protect the soil, the wind can easily carry it away. Third, cutting down too many trees also leads to this problem. Trees and their roots help keep the soil in place. When they are gone, the land becomes a desert.

③ Sandstorms can cause many problems. They make the air dirty and hard to breathe. People may get sick, especially those with breathing problems. Sandstorms can also damage crops and buildings. Sometimes they even cause traffic accidents because drivers cannot see the road clearly.

④ How can we protect ourselves when a sandstorm comes? First, stay indoors and close all windows and doors. If you must go outside, wear a mask to cover your mouth and nose. Wear glasses or goggles to protect your eyes. Second, avoid outdoor activities during sandstorms. Third, drink more water to keep your throat wet and comfortable.

⑤ To reduce sandstorms in the future, we should plant more trees, protect grasslands, and use land in a wiser way. Everyone can do something to make our environment better. Small actions can lead to big changes.

**注释：** sandstorm 沙尘暴；overgrazing 过度放牧；goggle 护目镜

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, when do sandstorms usually happen?
   A. In places with heavy rain and many trees.
   B. In dry areas with little rain and few plants to hold the soil.

   D. In cities with tall buildings.

2. Which of the following is mentioned in paragraph ② as a cause of sandstorms?
   A. Planting too many trees.
   B. Cutting down too many trees that help keep the soil in place.
   C. Heavy rain and floods.
   D. Building too many roads.

3. According to paragraph ③, what problems can sandstorms cause?
   A. They make people hungry.
   B. They make the air dirty, harm people's health, damage crops and buildings, and may cause traffic accidents.

   D. They help plants grow better.

4. What should we do to protect ourselves when a sandstorm comes, according to paragraph ④?
   A. Go outside and enjoy the wind.
   B. Stay indoors, close windows and doors; if going out, wear a mask and protect eyes.
   C. Run as fast as we can.
   D. Leave home and drive to another city.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To encourage people to move away from dry areas.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Sandstorms often occur in dry areas where there is little rain and the ground has no grass or trees to hold the soil."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明沙尘暴常发生在降雨少、地面上没有草或树木固定土壤的干旱地区。A项准确复述该信息。B项"降雨多、树木多"与原文相反；C项"仅在冬季"、D项"有高层建筑的城市"原文未提及。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Third, cutting down too many trees also leads to this problem. Trees and their roots help keep the soil in place."

**【解析】** 原文指出第三个原因是砍伐过多树木，树木和树根有助于固定土壤。B项正确。A项"种太多树"与减少沙尘暴矛盾；C项"暴雨洪水"、D项"建太多公路"未在原因中提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They make the air dirty and hard to breathe... People may get sick... can also damage crops and buildings... cause traffic accidents because drivers cannot see the road clearly."

**【解析】** 原文列举了沙尘暴的危害：污染空气、难呼吸、使人患病、损坏农作物和建筑、导致交通事故。C项全面概括。A项"使人饥饿"、B项"使天气更热"、D项"帮助植物生长"均与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "First, stay indoors and close all windows and doors. If you must go outside, wear a mask to cover your mouth and nose. Wear glasses or goggles to protect your eyes."

**【解析】** 原文说明应留在室内、关好门窗；若必须外出则佩戴口罩保护口鼻、戴护目镜保护眼睛。A项完整概括防护措施。B、C、D项与原文建议相反或无关。故选A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第①段沙尘暴定义及发生地，第②段成因，第③段危害，第④段防护措施，第⑤段预防建议。

**【解析】** 全文介绍了沙尘暴的成因、危害及自我防护方法，D项准确概括写作目的。A项"鼓励搬迁"、B项"沙尘暴不危险"、C项"教沙漠种作物"均偏离主旨。故选D。
