# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Welcome to the 10th Annual School Talent Show!**

② Do you have a special talent? Can you sing like a pop star, dance like a professional, or tell jokes that make everyone laugh? The 10th Annual School Talent Show is coming, and we are looking for the brightest stars in our school!

③ **Who can join?**
All students from Grade 7 to Grade 9 are welcome. You can perform by yourself or form a group with your friends. (A group can have no more than 5 members.)

④ **How to sign up (报名)?**
Please go to the Students' Union Office on the third floor and fill out an application form. The deadline for signing up is Friday, May 20th. Don't be late!

⑤ **When and Where?**
- **Date:** Friday, June 3rd
- **Time:** 6:30 PM - 8:30 PM
- **Place:** The School Auditorium (学校礼堂)

⑥ **Prizes!**
Our teachers will be the judges (评委). They will choose the top 3 performances.
- **First Prize:** A $50 bookstore gift card and a beautiful cup.
- **Second Prize:** A $30 bookstore gift card and a notebook.
- **Third Prize:** A $15 bookstore gift card and a pen.
Even if you don't win a prize, every performer will get a small toy as a gift for participating.

⑦ Come and show us what you can do! We can't wait to see your amazing talents.

**注释：** annual 年度的；auditorium 礼堂；judge 评委；gift card 礼品卡；participate 参与

---

### 选择题

1. Who is this poster written for?
A. Only teachers in the school.
B. Only students who can sing like pop stars.
C. All students from Grade 7 to Grade 9.
D. Parents and friends of the students.

2. What is the rule for forming a group according to paragraph ③?
A. A group must have exactly 5 members.
B. A group cannot have more than 5 members.
C. A group must consist of students from different grades.
D. Only boys can form a group.

3. Where should you go if you want to sign up for the talent show?
A. To the library.
B. To the teachers' office.
C. To the school auditorium.
D. To the Students' Union Office on the third floor.

4. Form paragraph ⑥, what will the student who wins the second prize get?
A. A cup and a notebook.
B. A $50 bookstore gift card.
C. A $30 bookstore gift card and a notebook.
D. A $15 bookstore gift card and a small toy.

5. What will you get if you perform but don't win any of the top 3 prizes?
A. Nothing at all.
B. A $15 bookstore gift card.
C. A small toy as a gift.
D. A chance to be a judge next year.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "All students from Grade 7 to Grade 9 are welcome."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出招募对象是谁可以参加（Who can join）：七年级到九年级的所有学生都是受迎的。A、D项全非学生；B项只局限于唱歌好的人，太狭隘。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段末尾括号内的话 "(A group can have no more than 5 members.)"

**【解析】** 原文说明如果你要组队，一个队伍不能多于5个成员（no more than 5 members）。A项说是必须精确5名队员，错误。C、D项无中生有。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "Please go to the Students' Union Office on the third floor and fill out an application form."

**【解析】** 关于怎么报名（How to sign up），文中说请去三楼的学生会办公室填写申请表。A项的图书馆，B项的教师办公室，C项的学校大礼堂（这是演出的地方）等均不正确。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段第二等奖介绍部分 "Second Prize: A $30 bookstore gift card and a notebook."

**【解析】** 本题只需对号入座，二等奖（Second Prize）是可以获得30美元书店礼品卡和一个笔记本。A、B、D三个选项均为错误匹配或其他奖项。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段最后一句 "Even if you don't win a prize, every performer will get a small toy as a gift for participating."

**【解析】** 即使你没有赢下前三的奖项，作为每一个表演者，也会得到一个“小玩具”（a small toy）作为参与的小纪念品。A项（什么也没有）不符合事实；B项是三等奖；D项（做明年评委）没提到。故选C。
