# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should students have to wear school uniforms (校服) every day? This is a question widely discussed among parents, teachers, and students. Some students complain that uniforms are ugly and limit (限制) their freedom to show their own styles. However, wearing school uniforms is actually a good idea and brings many benefits.

② Firstly, school uniforms can make students feel equal (平等的). When everyone wears the same clothes, nobody can judge (评判) others by their expensive shoes or beautiful dresses. In a school with uniforms, the kids from poor families will not feel embarrassed (尴尬的) because their clothes are not fashionable. This creates a friendly and fair learning environment.

③ Secondly, uniforms save time for both students and parents. Every morning, teenagers often spend a lot of time standing in front of the mirror, thinking about what to wear to school. With school uniforms, this problem disappears completely. Parents also don't need to spend extra money tracking colorful, modern clothes for their children just for school.

④ Thirdly, wearing a uniform helps students focus on their studies. Clothes can sometimes be a big distraction (令人分心的事物). If students don't need to care about who has the coolest t-shirt, they will pay more attention to their teachers and books. Also, a uniform reminds students of their identity (身份)—they are at school to learn, not to attend a fashion show.

⑤ In conclusion, although uniforms might not be the most fashionable clothes, they play an important role in making schools fairer, simpler, and more focused. Students should understand these true values behind their simple uniforms.

**注释：** limit 限制；equal 平等的；judge 评判；fashionable 时尚的；distraction 令人分心的事物；identity 身份

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what do some students complain about school uniforms?
A. They are too expensive.
B. They make them feel hot in summer.
C. They are ugly and limit their freedom to show personal styles.
D. They are always dirty and hard to wash.

2. How do school uniforms make students feel equal?
A. Because everyone is given a lot of money to buy clothes.
B. Because nobody can judge others by expensive or fashionable clothes.
C. Because all students have the same hairstyle.
D. Because the teachers give everyone the same grades.

3. Why do uniforms save time in the morning?
A. Because students run faster when wearing uniforms.
B. Because parents help them put on the uniforms.
C. Because they don't have to spend time choosing what to wear.
D. Because school starts later for students in uniforms.

4. What does the word "identity" in paragraph ④ refer to in the text?
A. A card that shows a student's name.
B. The feeling of being the coolest kid.
C. The fact that their role is a student whose main job is to learn.
D. A secret number to open the school gate.

5. What is the writer's attitude towards school uniforms?
A. The writer thinks they should only be worn by boys.
B. The writer completely opposes (反对) wearing them.
C. The writer supports wearing them because they bring benefits.
D. The writer thinks we should wear them only on Mondays.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "Some students complain that uniforms are ugly and limit (限制) their freedom to show their own styles."

**【解析】** 本文开头提到部分学生的抱怨：认为校服很丑且限制了他们秀出个人风格的自由。A、B、D项纯属无关陈述，原文均未作为学生们抱怨的痛点出现。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解推断

**【定位】** 第②段第二句 "When everyone wears the same clothes, nobody can judge others by their expensive shoes or beautiful dresses."

**【解析】** 原文中论述校服让人感到平等的逻辑在于：当所有人都穿一样的衣服时，就没有人能通过昂贵的鞋子或漂亮的裙子来评判他人。从而让穷家庭孩子不尴尬。A项（给大家钱买衣）、C项（同样发型）、D项（同样成绩）均为无中生有的干扰项。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "Every morning, teenagers often spend a lot of time... thinking about what to wear to school. With school uniforms, this problem disappears completely."

**【解析】** 校服节约时间的根本原因在于：学生每天早晨不再需要花大量时间去想“今天穿什么去学校”。C选项清晰准确。A说跑得快，B说父母帮穿，D说更晚上学，这些都毫无道理。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "...a uniform reminds students of their identity (身份)—they are at school to learn, not to attend a fashion show."

**【解析】** 在这段中，破折号后面是对 identity(身份) 的具体注释："they are at school to learn"（他们是在学校学习的）。这个“身份”指代的是他们作为学生的角色定位，核心工作是学习。并非指有名的身份卡片（A）或最酷的感觉（B）亦或秘密号码（D）。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 作者观点态度

**【定位】** 第一段结尾与第五段全面总结。

**【解析】** 首段结尾破题：“然而，穿校服实际上是个好主意并带来诸多益处”。末段总结：“...它们在让学校变得更公平、简单、专注方面扮演重要角色。”显然，作者是支持穿校服的。B的完全反对，和A、D的加上荒谬条件的限制皆为错误认知。故选C。
