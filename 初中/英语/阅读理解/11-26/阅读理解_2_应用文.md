# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Art Festival 2025 — Notice**

① Dear students and parents,

Our school will hold the annual Art Festival from December 10 to 12, 2025. All students are welcome to take part. Please read the following information carefully.

**Event Overview:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Theme | "Colours of Youth" |
| Dates | December 10–12, 2025 |
| Venue | School hall, art rooms, and playground |
| Participants | All grades (Grade 7–9) |

② **Activity Schedule:**

| Date | Morning (9:00–11:30) | Afternoon (2:00–4:00) |
|------|----------------------|------------------------|
| Dec 10 | Opening ceremony & dance show | Painting competition |
| Dec 11 | Choir concert | Drama performance |
| Dec 12 | Calligraphy exhibition | Closing & award ceremony |

③ **How to join:**
- **Dance/Choir/Drama:** Sign up with your class teacher before December 1. Each class can send one team.
- **Painting:** Bring your work (A3 size) to the art office by December 5. Topic: "My Beautiful School Life."
- **Calligraphy:** Sign up online at www.ourschool.edu.cn/artfest by November 28.

④ **Important notes:**
- Students taking part in performances should arrive 30 minutes early for preparation.
- Parents are welcome to watch. Seats are limited; please get a ticket from the school office from December 5.
- All students must wear school uniform during the festival days.

⑤ If you have any questions, please contact Ms Zhang at room 302 or email artfest@ourschool.edu.cn.

Student Union  
November 26, 2025

**Questions:**

1. What is the theme of the Art Festival?
   A. "My School."

   A. On the morning of December 10.
   B. On the morning of December 11.
   C. On the afternoon of December 11.
   D. On the morning of December 12.

3. What is the topic for the painting competition?
   A. "My Family."
   B. "My Beautiful School Life."
   C. "Nature."
   D. "Future City."

4. By when should students sign up for calligraphy?
   A. November 26.
   B. November 28.

   D. December 5.

5. What should students wear during the festival?
   A. Colourful clothes.
   B. Sportswear.
   C. School uniform.
   D. Their favourite clothes.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Theme" 一行 "Colours of Youth"

**【解析】** 表格明确标注主题为"Colours of Youth"（青春的色彩）。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 Dec 11 一行 "Choir concert" 在 Morning 列

**【解析】** 合唱音乐会安排在12月11日上午。A项12月10日上午是开幕式和舞蹈；C项12月11日下午是戏剧表演；D项12月12日上午是书法展览。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Topic: 'My Beautiful School Life.'"

**【解析】** 绘画比赛主题为"My Beautiful School Life"。A项我家、C项自然、D项未来城市均不正确。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Sign up online at www.ourschool.edu.cn/artfest by November 28."

**【解析】** 书法项目需在11月28日前网上报名。A项11月26日是通知发布日期；C项12月1日是舞蹈/合唱/戏剧报名截止；D项12月5日是绘画作品提交截止。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "All students must wear school uniform during the festival days."

**【解析】** 原文明确要求 festival 期间必须穿校服。A项彩色衣服、B项运动服、D项最喜欢的衣服均与要求不符。故选C。
