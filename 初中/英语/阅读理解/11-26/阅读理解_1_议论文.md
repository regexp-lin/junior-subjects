# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Are Online Games Good or Bad for Teenagers?**

① Teenagers spend a lot of time playing online games. Some parents worry that games will harm their children's studies and health. But others think games can be useful. Is playing online games good or bad for teenagers?

② Supporters say that online games can develop useful skills. Many games require players to think quickly, solve problems, and work as a team. Strategy games, for example, help improve logical thinking. Some games are even used in schools to make learning more interesting. Besides, playing games with friends online can strengthen friendships and reduce stress after a busy day at school.

③ However, there are clear risks. Spending too much time on games may lead to poor eyesight and less exercise. Some teenagers stay up late playing games and feel tired in class the next day. Their grades may drop. What is more, some young people find it hard to control themselves. They skip meals, forget homework, and argue with family members about game time. In serious cases, they may lose interest in real-life activities.

④ Another concern is the content of some games. Violent games may affect how teenagers behave. Games with in-app purchases can also cause young players to spend money without thinking. Parents need to check game ratings and set rules for when and how long their children can play.

⑤ In my view, online games are not all bad, but teenagers should use them wisely. Set a time limit — no more than one hour on school days. Choose games that are educational or encourage teamwork. Never let games take the place of sleep, exercise, or face-to-face time with family and friends. Balance is the key.

**注释：** strategy 策略；logical 逻辑的；in-app purchase 应用内购买

---

**Questions:**

1. What skill can strategy games help develop?
   A. How to cook.
   B. Logical thinking.
   C. How to sing.
   D. How to draw.

2. According to supporters, how can online games help friendships?
   A. Games make teenagers study harder.
   B. Playing games with friends online can strengthen friendships.
   C. Games teach teenagers to cook.
   D. Games help teenagers sleep better.

3. What is one risk of playing games too much?
   A. Teenagers become better at sports.
   B. Teenagers may have poor eyesight and less exercise.
   C. Teenagers eat more healthy food.
   D. Teenagers make more friends.

4. Why should parents check game ratings?
   A. To find cheaper games.
   B. Because some games have violent content that may affect teenagers.
   C. To choose games with more levels.
   D. To help teenagers play faster.

5. What does the writer suggest teenagers do?
   A. Never play any online games.
   B. Play games for at least three hours every day.
   C. Set a time limit and choose games wisely; balance is the key.
   D. Only play violent games.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Strategy games, for example, help improve logical thinking."

**【解析】** 原文说策略类游戏有助于提高逻辑思维能力。A项做饭、C项唱歌、D项画画均非策略游戏培养的能力。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "playing games with friends online can strengthen friendships"

**【解析】** 原文说和朋友一起在线玩游戏可以增进友谊。A项学习更努力、C项教做饭、D项睡更好均与友谊无直接关系。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Spending too much time on games may lead to poor eyesight and less exercise."

**【解析】** 原文指出玩游戏过多可能导致视力下降和运动减少。A项"更擅长运动"与"less exercise"矛盾；C项"吃更多健康食品"无依据；D项"交更多朋友"是可能的正面影响非风险。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Another concern is the content of some games. Violent games may affect how teenagers behave. Parents need to check game ratings"

**【解析】** 某些游戏含有暴力内容可能影响青少年，因此家长需要查看游戏分级。A项找更便宜的游戏、C项选更多关卡的、D项帮玩更快均非查分级的原因。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Set a time limit — no more than one hour on school days. Choose games that are educational or encourage teamwork... Balance is the key."

**【解析】** 作者建议设定时间限制、选择有教育意义或促进团队合作的游戏，平衡是关键。A项"从不玩"过于绝对；B项"每天至少三小时"与建议相反；D项"只玩暴力游戏"与建议矛盾。故选C。
