# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In many families, parents give their children pocket money (零花钱) every week. Some people think it is a great idea because it teaches children how to manage money. However, others argue that giving pocket money will make children spoiled (被宠坏的) and spend money blindly. In my opinion, giving pocket money is necessary, but it must be done in the right way.

② On the one hand, pocket money provides a good chance for children to learn financial (财务的) skills. When children have their own money, they have to decide how to use it. If a boy wants to buy a new bicycle, he has to save his pocket money for several months. During this process, he will learn the value of saving and understand that money doesn't come easily. This is an important lesson that textbooks cannot teach.

③ On the other hand, if parents give too much money without any rules, it can cause problems. Children might use the money to buy unhealthy snacks, expensive video games, or even compare their wealth (财富) with classmates. This will lead to bad spending habits.

④ Therefore, parents should set clear rules when giving pocket money. First, the amount of money should be reasonable (合理的) based on the family's situation and the child's age. Second, parents can ask children to do some housework to "earn" (赚得) the money, so they will value it more. Third, parents should guide children to keep a record of what they buy.

⑤ In conclusion, pocket money is a useful tool for education. If parents guide their children correctly, pocket money will help them become responsible adults in the future.

**注释：** pocket money 零花钱；spoiled 被宠坏的；financial 财务的；wealth 财富；reasonable 合理的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what do some people worry about giving pocket money?
A. It takes up too much of parents' time.
B. It makes children spoiled and spend money blindly.
C. Children will lose the money quickly.
D. Children will use the money to pay for school.

2. In paragraph ②, what financial skill can a boy learn from buying a new bicycle?
A. How to ask his parents for more money.
B. How to ride a bicycle safely on the street.
C. The value of saving money and understanding it's hard to get.
D. How to borrow money from his friends.

3. Why is giving too much money without rules a bad idea?
A. Because it may cause bad spending habits like buying unhealthy snacks.
B. Because it will make parents very poor.
C. Because children will hide the money under their beds.
D. Because teachers will take the money away.

4. Form the text, what is ONE of the rules parents should follow when giving pocket money?
A. Give children as much money as they want.
B. Ask children to do some housework to earn the money.
C. Tell children never to buy anything.
D. Ask teachers to keep the money for children.

5. What does the writer think of giving pocket money?
A. He thinks it is a waste of time and money.
B. He strongly disagrees with the idea of giving pocket money.
C. He thinks only older teenagers should get pocket money.
D. He thinks it is necessary and a useful tool for education if done correctly.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "However, others argue that giving pocket money will make children spoiled (被宠坏的) and spend money blindly."

**【解析】** 原文开篇提及，那些反对给零花钱的人担心这会让孩子被宠坏，并且盲目花钱（B项中描述一致）。A项占用父母时间、C项快速丢失、D项为学校付钱，原文都没提到。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第②段中 "If a boy wants to buy a new bicycle, he has to save his pocket money for several months. During this process, he will learn the value of saving and understand that money doesn't come easily."

**【解析】** 本段举男孩买自行车的例子，是为了说明孩子为了达到目标而攒钱几个月，这叫他学会存钱的价值并理解金钱来之不易（The value of saving money and understanding it's hard to get）。而不是为了教他怎么乞求更多的钱、怎么安全骑车、或者向朋友借钱。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "Children might use the money to buy unhealthy snacks, expensive video games... This will lead to bad spending habits."

**【解析】** 本段分析没有规则地给太多钱会带来的问题：买不健康的零食、昂贵的电子游戏或者和同学攀比，这会导致花钱的坏习惯。A项概括了购买不健康零食等坏消费习惯；B、C、D皆属胡说八道。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3句 "Second, parents can ask children to do some housework to 'earn' (赚得) the money..."

**【解析】** 作者提出了几条给零花钱的规则，其中第二条提到父母可以要求孩子做一些家务以“赚取”零用钱。A、C、D项不仅违背了文中的常理与建议，原文也压根没提。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 作者意图 / 态度题

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "In my opinion, giving pocket money is necessary, but it must be done in the right way." 和第⑤段全文。

**【解析】** 综观全文，作者的观点在第一段抛出：给零花钱是必要的，但必须以正确的方式进行；在结论段中又重申，它是一个有用的教育工具，父母正确引导下能帮助孩子成为有责任心的成年人。因此选D。A、B、C都扭曲了作者表明的态度。故选D。
