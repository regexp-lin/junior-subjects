# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **City Zoo Volunteer Program**

② Do you love animals? Do you want to spend your weekends doing something meaningful? Join the City Zoo Volunteer Program! We are looking for young people aged 15-18 to help us during the summer holidays.

③ **What will you do?**
- **Help Animal Keepers:** You will help clean the cages and prepare food for friendly animals like rabbits, monkeys, and colorful birds. (Don't worry, you won't work with dangerous animals like lions or tigers.)
- **Guide Visitors:** You will stand near the entrance (入口) to give maps to visitors and answer their questions.
- **Story Telling:** Every afternoon at 3:00 PM, you will read animal stories to young children at the Zoo Library.

④ **What can you get?**
- A free lunch every day you work.
- Two free tickets to the zoo every month for your family or friends.
- A special certificate (证书) at the end of the summer, which is very helpful for your future college application.

⑤ **How to apply?**
If you are interested, please visit our website at *www.cityzoo-volunteer.com* and fill out the online form. The deadline (截止日期) is May 15th. We will call you for an interview within a week.

⑥ Come and make a difference for our animal friends!

**注释：** meaningful 有意义的；certificate 证书；application 申请；deadline 截止日期

---

### 选择题

1. How old should you be if you want to join the volunteer program?
A. 10-14.
B. 15-18.
C. 19-25.
D. Over 25.

2. Which animals will the volunteers NOT work with?
A. Rabbits.
B. Monkeys.
C. Lions and tigers.
D. Colorful birds.

3. What are the volunteers asked to do at 3:00 PM every afternoon?
A. Clean the cages.
B. Read animal stories to young children at the Zoo Library.
C. Prepare food for animals.
D. Give maps to visitors near the entrance.

4. What is one of the things volunteers can get according to paragraph ④?
A. A free dinner every day.
B. Money for college.
C. A free map of the city.
D. A special certificate at the end of the summer.

5. What must you do before May 15th if you want to be a volunteer?
A. Call the zoo immediately.
B. Visit the website and fill out the online form.
C. Buy two tickets to the zoo.
D. Go to the zoo for an interview.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "We are looking for young people aged 15-18 to help us during the summer holidays."

**【解析】** 原文很清楚地招募对象：“在15-18岁之间的年轻人（aged 15-18）”。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1点括号内容 "(Don't worry, you won't work with dangerous animals like lions or tigers.)"

**【解析】** 原文提到志愿者将协助饲养员照顾兔子、猴子和鸟，但不必去照顾危险的动物比如狮子和老虎（lions or tigers）。A、B、D项是志愿者会接触的动物。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3点 "Story Telling: Every afternoon at 3:00 PM, you will read animal stories to young children at the Zoo Library."

**【解析】** 原文明确了每天下午3点的工作是：在动物园图书馆给小孩子读动物故事。A和C项是帮助饲养员的工作；D项是给游客当向导的工作。故选B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第④段各项内容。

**【解析】** 根据第④段，志愿者可以得到的有：免费午餐（A项说是晚餐，错）；每月两张免费门票；以及夏末的一份特殊证书（D项对）。B项（读大学的钱）、C项（免费城市地图）均没在文中提到。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1-2句 "...visit our website at www.cityzoo-volunteer.com and fill out the online form. The deadline is May 15th."

**【解析】** 原文中，如何申请（How to apply）的内容是在截止日期（5月15日）之前访问网站并填写在线表格。A项马上打电话（文中是说园方一周内打电话来）；C项买门票以及D项马上面试，都与原文申请步骤不符。故选B。
