# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① I never thought I would stand on a stage and speak English in front of hundreds of people. I am usually shy. When our English teacher asked who wanted to join the school speech competition, I stayed quiet. But my best friend Lucy encouraged me. "You write well. You can do it," she said. I signed up, but my hands were shaking.

② I had two weeks to prepare. I chose a topic: "The Power of Kindness." I wrote my speech and practised it every day. At first, when I practised alone in my room, I was fine. But when I tried in front of my family, my voice trembled (颤抖) and I forgot some lines. My father said, "Imagine the audience is a group of friends. Just talk to them." I tried again. It was still hard, but a little better.

③ The day before the competition, I could not sleep. What if I forgot everything? What if everyone laughed at me? Lucy called me. "Remember why you're doing this — not to win, but to grow. You've already won by trying." Her words made me calm down. I breathed slowly and told myself, "I can do this."

④ On the competition day, I walked onto the stage. The lights were bright. I saw many faces in the audience. My heart beat fast. Then I remembered my father's words — imagine they are friends. I took a deep breath and began to speak. The first few sentences were a bit shaky (颤抖的), but soon I found my rhythm. I looked at the audience and saw people nodding. When I finished, there was applause (掌声). I did not win first prize, but I did not mind. I had overcome my fear.

⑤ That experience taught me that courage is not the absence of fear — it is acting despite fear. I am still shy, but now I know I can do things that scare me. Thank you, Lucy and Dad.

**注释：** tremble 颤抖；shaky 颤抖的；applause 掌声；rhythm 节奏

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer join the speech competition at first?
   A. The teacher forced her to.
   B. Lucy encouraged her, saying she wrote well and could do it.
   C. She wanted to win a prize.
   D. Her father told her to.

2. What was the writer's speech topic?
   A. How to learn English.
   B. The Power of Kindness.
   C. My Best Friend.
   D. Travel around the world.

3. What problem did the writer have when practising in front of her family?
   A. She spoke too fast.
   B. Her voice trembled and she forgot some lines.
   C. She did not prepare.
   D. Her family did not listen.

4. What did Lucy say to help the writer before the competition?
   A. "You must win."
   B. "Remember why you're doing this — not to win, but to grow. You've already won by trying."
   C. "Don't go."
   D. "The competition is cancelled."

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Winning is the only thing that matters.
   B. Courage is acting despite fear; she can do things that scare her.
   C. She should never speak in public again.
   D. English competitions are too hard.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "'You write well. You can do it,' she said. I signed up"

**【解析】** 露西鼓励作者，认为她文笔好、能胜任，作者因此报名。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "I chose a topic: 'The Power of Kindness'"

**【解析】** 作者选择的演讲主题是"善良的力量"。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "when I tried in front of my family, my voice trembled and I forgot some lines"

**【解析】** 在家人面前练习时，作者声音发抖并忘词。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "'Remember why you're doing this — not to win, but to grow. You've already won by trying.'"

**【解析】** 露西提醒作者参赛重在成长而非获奖，敢于尝试本身就是胜利。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "courage is not the absence of fear — it is acting despite fear... I can do things that scare me"

**【解析】** 作者领悟到勇气不是没有恐惧，而是虽惧仍行；她相信自己能完成曾经害怕的事。A、C、D 项与作者感悟矛盾。故选 B。
