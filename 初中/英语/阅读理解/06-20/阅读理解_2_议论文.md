# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should middle school students learn to do housework? This question is being discussed by many parents and teachers. Some believe that students should only focus on their studies. Others think that doing housework is necessary for their growth. Let us look at both sides.

② **Reasons for learning housework:** First, doing housework helps students develop a sense of responsibility. When they tidy their rooms, wash dishes, or help cook meals, they learn to take care of themselves and their family. Second, housework teaches practical life skills. Students who can cook and clean will be more independent when they grow up and live on their own. Third, sharing housework can make family life happier. Parents often feel tired after work, and when children help out, the whole family benefits. Studies also show that students who do housework regularly tend to do better at school, perhaps because they learn to manage their time well.

③ **Reasons against housework for students:** Some parents worry that housework will take time away from homework and exam preparation. Students in China already have a heavy study load. Adding housework might make them too busy and stressed. Another concern is safety — cooking with fire or using sharp tools could be dangerous for young people. Also, some parents prefer to do everything themselves so that their children can study without any disturbance (打扰).

④ **What experts suggest:** Researchers say that students do not need to spend hours on housework every day. Fifteen to thirty minutes a day — such as making the bed, folding clothes, or helping set the table — is enough. The key is to start with simple tasks and let students do what they can. Parents should praise their efforts instead of complaining about mistakes. When housework becomes a habit, it helps students become more responsible and confident.

⑤ In conclusion, learning to do housework is good for middle school students. It does not mean they must do everything at home, but doing a little each day can prepare them for the future and make family life more harmonious (和谐的). The question is not whether to do it, but how much and in what way.

**注释：** disturbance 打扰；harmonious 和谐的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, which is a benefit of doing housework?
   A. Students can avoid doing homework.
   B. Students will never need to study.
   C. Students develop a sense of responsibility and practical life skills.
   D. Students can stay at home all day.

2. Why do some parents not want their children to do housework?
   A. Because housework is too expensive.
   B. Because they worry it takes time from study and exam preparation.
   C. Because children are too young to understand.
   D. Because there is no housework to do.

3. How much housework do experts suggest students do each day?
   A. At least two hours.
   B. Only on weekends.
   C. None at all.
   D. Fifteen to thirty minutes, doing simple tasks.

4. The underlined word "harmonious" in paragraph ⑤ probably means ______.
   A. peaceful and friendly
   B. busy and noisy
   C. difficult and boring
   D. quiet and lonely

5. What is the writer's main idea?
   A. Learning a little housework each day is good for students and family life.
   B. Students should never do housework and only study.
   C. Parents should do all the housework.
   D. Housework is more important than school work.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "doing housework helps students develop a sense of responsibility... housework teaches practical life skills"

**【解析】** 文中明确提到做家务有助于培养责任感和实用生活技能。C 项正确。A 项逃避作业、B 项不必学习、D 项整天待在家均与原文不符。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some parents worry that housework will take time away from homework and exam preparation. Students in China already have a heavy study load."

**【解析】** 部分家长担心家务占用学习与备考时间，学生学业负担已重。B 项正确。A 项家务太贵、C 项孩子太小、D 项没有家务可做均未提及。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Fifteen to thirty minutes a day — such as making the bed, folding clothes, or helping set the table — is enough. The key is to start with simple tasks."

**【解析】** 专家建议每天十五至三十分钟，做些简单家务即可。D 项正确。A 项两小时、B 项仅在周末、C 项完全不做均与原文矛盾。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "make family life more harmonious"

**【解析】** 根据上下文，学做家务能让家庭生活更和谐。harmonious 意为 "和谐的、和睦的"。A 项 peaceful and friendly 最贴近。B 项忙碌吵闹、C 项困难无聊、D 项安静孤独均不符语境。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Learning to do housework is good for middle school students... doing a little each day can prepare them for the future and make family life more harmonious."

**【解析】** 作者认为中学生适当学做家务有益，每日少量家务可为未来做准备并增进家庭和睦。A 项准确概括。B 项绝不做事与作者观点相反；C 项家长全包、D 项家务重于学业均非作者立场。故选 A。
