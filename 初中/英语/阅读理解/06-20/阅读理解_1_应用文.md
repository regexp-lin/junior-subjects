# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Safety Tips — Stay Away from Drowning**

② Summer is the season when many students enjoy swimming. However, drowning (溺水) is one of the top causes of death among children. To keep yourselves safe, please follow these important rules.

③ **Never swim alone.** Always swim with at least one adult or friend who can swim well. If something goes wrong, they can help you. Never swim in rivers, lakes or the sea without an adult nearby. These places can be very dangerous.

④ **Know the danger signs.** If you feel tired, cold, or your legs cramp (抽筋) while swimming, get out of the water right away. Do not try to swim when the weather is bad. Strong winds and rain can make the water unsafe. If you see a "No Swimming" sign, never enter the water.

⑤ **Learn basic skills.** Before you swim, warm up your body. If someone is in trouble in the water, do not jump in to save them — you might get into danger too. Shout for help and throw them something that can float, like a life ring or a plastic bottle. Remember: your safety comes first!

**注释：** drowning 溺水；cramp 抽筋；float 漂浮

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, why is summer dangerous for children?
   A. Because drowning is one of the main causes of death among children.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "drowning (溺水) is one of the top causes of death among children"

**【解析】** 文章明确说明溺水是儿童死亡的主要原因之一。B 项正确。A 项吃冰淇淋、C 项太阳太强、D 项玩游戏过多均与原文主旨不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Always swim with at least one adult or friend who can swim well."

**【解析】** 学生游泳时应有至少一名成人或会游泳的朋友陪同。B 项正确。A 项独自游泳、C 项独自去河里游泳、D 项只在夜里游泳均违背安全提示。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "If you feel tired, cold, or your legs cramp while swimming, get out of the water right away."

**【解析】** 游泳时若感到疲劳、寒冷或腿抽筋，应立即上岸。C 项正确。A 项继续游泳、B 项打电话、D 项让朋友拉你均与安全提示矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If someone is in trouble in the water, do not jump in to save them... Shout for help and throw them something that can float, like a life ring or a plastic bottle."

**【解析】** 有人水中遇险时，不要贸然下水施救，应大声呼救并抛掷可漂浮物品（如救生圈、塑料瓶）。D 项正确。A 项直接跳入与原文 "do not jump in" 矛盾；B、C 项均不符合安全指引。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文标题 "Summer Safety Tips — Stay Away from Drowning" 及第②段 "To keep yourselves safe, please follow these important rules."

**【解析】** 本文旨在提供暑期安全提示，尤其是防溺水建议，保护学生安全。A 项准确概括。B 项鼓励多游泳、C 项教游泳冠军技巧、D 项介绍游泳池均非主旨。故选 A。
