# 阅读理解（说明文）

**The Growth of the Sharing Economy**

① The sharing economy has grown quickly in recent years. It is a new way of using things. Instead of owning everything, people share bikes, cars, rooms, and even tools with strangers. Apps and websites make sharing easy and safe. This idea is changing how we live and work.

② Bike-sharing is one of the most popular forms. In many Chinese cities, you can find shared bikes on almost every street. You scan a QR code, unlock the bike, ride it, and leave it when you finish. No need to buy or maintain a bike. Car-sharing works in a similar way. People rent cars by the hour or day. It saves money and reduces the number of cars on the road.

③ Sharing also helps the environment. When we share things, we use fewer resources. Fewer bikes and cars are made. Less space is needed for parking. Sharing means less waste. Many young people like this idea because they care about our planet.

④ Of course, there are challenges. Some users damage shared things. Bikes are left in wrong places. Trust between strangers can be a problem. Companies are working on better rules and technology to solve these issues.

⑤ The sharing economy is still growing. More services will appear in the future. It offers convenience, saves money, and protects the environment. As long as people use it responsibly, it can benefit us all.

**注释：** QR code 二维码；maintain 维护

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ①, what makes the sharing economy possible?
   A. Apps and websites that make sharing easy and safe.
   B. Owning more things.
   C. Living alone.
   D. Working in offices.

2. How does bike-sharing work according to Paragraph ②?
   A. You must buy a bike first.
   B. You can only use bikes at home.
   C. You scan a code, unlock, ride, and leave the bike when done.
   D. You need to repair the bike yourself.

3. Why does the sharing economy help the environment?
   A. It makes more cars.
   B. We use fewer resources and produce less waste.
   C. It increases parking spaces.
   D. People drive more.

4. What problem is mentioned in Paragraph ④?
   A. Sharing is too cheap.
   B. Some users damage shared things and leave bikes in wrong places.
   C. Too many people want to share.
   D. Apps do not work.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The sharing economy will disappear soon.
   B. Only young people use shared services.
   C. Sharing has no challenges at all.
   D. If used responsibly, the sharing economy can bring benefits to society.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】A**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "Apps and websites make sharing easy and safe."
**【解析】** 应用和网站使共享变得简单而安全，成为共享经济发展的基础。A项准确。B、C、D项与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "You scan a QR code, unlock the bike, ride it, and leave it when you finish."
**【解析】** 共享单车的使用流程为扫码、解锁、骑行、用毕停放。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文矛盾。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "When we share things, we use fewer resources... Sharing means less waste."
**【解析】** 共享减少资源消耗，产生更少废弃物。B项准确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Some users damage shared things. Bikes are left in wrong places."
**【解析】** 文中提到部分用户损坏共享物品、随意停放单车等问题。B项正确。A、C、D项未在文中体现。故选B。

**5. 【答案】D**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "As long as people use it responsibly, it can benefit us all."
**【解析】** 结尾指出若负责任地使用，共享经济可造福大家。D项正确。A、B、C项与文意不符。故选D。
