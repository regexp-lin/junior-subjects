# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Students Wear School Uniforms?**

① In most Chinese schools, students are required to wear school uniforms. Some students like them, while others wish they could wear their own clothes. This topic has been discussed for many years. Should students wear school uniforms?

② Supporters say school uniforms have many benefits. First, uniforms create a sense of equality. No matter whether a student's family is rich or poor, everyone looks the same. This helps reduce bullying based on clothing and brand names. Second, uniforms save time. Students do not need to spend time every morning choosing what to wear. Third, uniforms help build school spirit and a sense of belonging. When students wear the same uniform, they feel they are part of a team.

③ Those against school uniforms argue differently. They believe that clothing is a way to express personality. Wearing the same outfit every day can feel boring and limit creativity. Some students also complain that uniforms are uncomfortable, especially in summer when the fabric is too thick. Others say that uniforms do not actually prevent comparison — students still compare shoes, bags, and other accessories.

④ There is also a middle ground. Some schools allow students to choose between two or three uniform styles, giving them some freedom while keeping a standard look. Others let students wear casual clothes on Fridays as a reward. These approaches try to respect students' feelings while keeping order.

⑤ In my opinion, school uniforms are useful, but schools should listen to students' ideas about design and comfort. A uniform that students like and feel good in is more likely to be worn with pride.

**注释：** equality 平等；accessory 配饰；personality 个性

---

**Questions:**

1. What is one benefit of school uniforms according to supporters?
   A. They make students look fashionable.
   B. They create a sense of equality among students.
   C. They are always comfortable.
   D. They help students get better grades.

2. Why do some students dislike school uniforms?
   A. Because they are too expensive.
   B. Because they look too beautiful.
   C. Because uniforms can feel boring and limit creativity.
   D. Because teachers do not wear them.

3. What is the "middle ground" mentioned in Paragraph 4?
   A. Students never wear uniforms.
   B. Schools allow some choice in uniform styles or casual days.
   C. Students design their own uniforms.
   D. Schools cancel all dress rules.

4. What does the writer think schools should do?
   A. Get rid of all uniforms.
   B. Force students to wear the same uniform every day without any changes.
   C. Listen to students' ideas about design and comfort.
   D. Let students wear whatever they want.

5. What is the writer's attitude towards school uniforms?
   A. Completely against them.
   B. Thinks they are useful but should consider student opinions.
   C. Believes they are perfect as they are.
   D. Does not care about the topic.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "First, uniforms create a sense of equality. No matter whether a student's family is rich or poor, everyone looks the same."

**【解析】** 原文说校服创造平等感，无论贫富大家看起来一样。A项"时尚"未提及；C项"总是舒适"第③段反而提到有人抱怨不舒服；D项"成绩更好"原文无此论述。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Wearing the same outfit every day can feel boring and limit creativity."

**【解析】** 反对者认为每天穿一样的衣服无聊且限制创造力。A项"太贵"原文未提及；B项"太漂亮"与抱怨不符；D项"老师不穿"不是反对校服的理由。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some schools allow students to choose between two or three uniform styles... Others let students wear casual clothes on Fridays as a reward."

**【解析】** 折中方案是提供2-3种校服款式选择或周五穿便装。A项"从不穿校服"过于极端；C项"学生自己设计"原文未说；D项"取消所有着装规则"也过于极端。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "schools should listen to students' ideas about design and comfort."

**【解析】** 作者认为学校应该听取学生对设计和舒适度的意见。A项"取消所有校服"不是作者立场；B项"强制穿同样校服不改变"与作者建议矛盾；D项"想穿什么穿什么"也非作者观点。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "school uniforms are useful, but schools should listen to students' ideas about design and comfort."

**【解析】** 作者认为校服有用，但应考虑学生意见，态度是"支持但需改进"。A项"完全反对"不对；C项"完美无需改变"与建议改进矛盾；D项"不关心"与作者给出明确意见矛盾。故选B。
