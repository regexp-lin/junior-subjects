# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Flea Market — Sell, Buy & Have Fun!**

① Our school will hold a Flea Market next Saturday. This is a great chance to sell things you no longer need and find treasures at low prices!

**Event Details:**

| Item | Information |
|------|------------|
| Date | Saturday, November 30, 2024 |
| Time | 1:00–4:00 p.m. |
| Place | School Playground |
| Raincheck | If it rains, the event moves to the gym |
| Who can join | All students and teachers |

② **For Sellers:**
- Sign up at the Student Council Office (Room 106) before November 27.
- Each seller gets a table (1.2m × 0.6m). Bring your own tablecloth.
- You may sell: books, toys, stationery, handmade crafts, small electronics (working condition only).
- You may NOT sell: food, drinks, or any dangerous items.
- Table fee: 5 yuan (donated to the school charity fund).

③ **For Buyers:**
- No sign-up needed. Just come and enjoy!
- Bring cash. Most sellers do not accept mobile payments.
- Prices are usually between 1 and 30 yuan.

④ **Special Activities:**
- **Lucky Draw** (2:30 p.m.): Spend 20 yuan or more to get a lucky draw ticket. Prizes include notebooks, water bottles, and movie tickets.
- **Best Stall Award**: The stall with the most creative decoration wins a prize.
- **Charity Corner**: Donate items you want to give away for free. All donated items go to children in need.

⑤ Let's make this a fun and meaningful day! See you at the Flea Market!

Student Council  
November 22, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When is the School Flea Market?
   A. November 22.
   B. November 27.
   C. November 30.
   D. December 1.

2. What can sellers NOT sell at the market?
   A. Books and toys.
   B. Handmade crafts.
   C. Food and drinks.
   D. Stationery.

3. Where does the 5-yuan table fee go?
   A. To the seller.
   B. To the school charity fund.
   C. To the Student Council members.
   D. To the headmaster.

4. How can a buyer get a lucky draw ticket?
   A. By signing up in advance.
   B. By arriving before 1:00 p.m.
   C. By spending 20 yuan or more.
   D. By donating items.

5. Which of the following is TRUE?
   A. Buyers must sign up before November 27.
   B. If it rains, the market will be cancelled.
   C. Mobile payment is the main way to pay.
   D. Donated items in the Charity Corner go to children in need.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Date" 一行 "Saturday, November 30, 2024"

**【解析】** 表格明确标注日期为11月30日。A项11月22日是通知发布日期；B项11月27日是卖家报名截止日期；D项12月1日原文无此信息。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "You may NOT sell: food, drinks, or any dangerous items."

**【解析】** 原文明确禁止销售食物、饮料和危险物品。A项书和玩具、B项手工艺品、D项文具都是允许售卖的物品。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Table fee: 5 yuan (donated to the school charity fund)."

**【解析】** 原文说5元摊位费捐给学校慈善基金。A项卖家自己、C项学生会成员、D项校长均非收款方。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Spend 20 yuan or more to get a lucky draw ticket."

**【解析】** 原文说消费满20元可以获得抽奖券。A项"提前报名"、B项"1点前到达"、D项"捐赠物品"均不是获得抽奖券的条件。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Charity Corner: Donate items you want to give away for free. All donated items go to children in need."

**【解析】** 原文说爱心角捐赠的物品会送给有需要的儿童，D项正确。A项买家不需要报名，只有卖家需要；B项下雨不会取消，会移到体育馆；C项原文说"Most sellers do not accept mobile payments"，手机支付不是主要方式。故选D。
