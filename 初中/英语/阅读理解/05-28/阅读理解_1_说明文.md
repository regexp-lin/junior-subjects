# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Water is one of the most important things on Earth. More than 70% of our body is water. We need it to drink, to cook, and to keep ourselves clean. Without water, we cannot live for more than a few days. Plants and animals need water too. Almost all life on Earth depends on water.

② Why do we need so much water? Water helps our body work well. It carries nutrients (养分) to every part of our body. It also helps remove waste from our body. When we feel thirsty, it means our body needs more water. Doctors advise us to drink six to eight glasses of water every day to stay healthy.

③ Yet, clean water is becoming scarce (稀缺的) in many places. Some factories pour waste water into rivers and lakes. Farmers use too much fertilizer, which pollutes the water. Climate change brings long droughts (干旱) in some areas. As a result, millions of people around the world cannot get safe drinking water.

④ What can we do to protect water? We should turn off the tap when we brush our teeth. We should take shorter showers and fix leaky taps. At school or home, we can reuse water — for example, use water from washing vegetables to water plants. We should never throw rubbish into rivers or lakes. Governments and factories must also follow laws to keep our water clean.

⑤ Water is a gift from nature. We cannot make more of it. If we waste and pollute it, we will have less for the future. Every small action counts. Let us all work together to save water and protect our planet.

**注释：** nutrient 养分；scarce 稀缺的；drought 干旱

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, why is water important?
   A. Over 70% of our body is water, and almost all life depends on it.
   B. It helps us sleep better.
   C. It is only needed for cooking.
   D. Plants do not need water.

2. According to paragraph ②, what does water do in our body?
   A. It only makes us feel full.
   B. It helps us run faster.
   C. It carries nutrients and helps remove waste from our body.
   D. It is only needed when we are sick.

3. According to paragraph ③, what causes water to become scarce in some places?
   A. People drink too much water.
   B. Too many people use showers.
   C. Plants use all the water.
   D. Factories pour waste into rivers, too much fertilizer pollutes water, and climate change brings droughts.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about saving water?
   A. Only governments can save water.
   B. Individual actions like turning off taps and reusing water can help protect water.
   C. We should never take showers.
   D. Fixing taps is the only way to save water.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To explain why humans need water and call for water protection.
   B. To tell people how to build factories.
   C. To compare different types of water.
   D. To encourage people to drink less water.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "More than 70% of our body is water... Almost all life on Earth depends on water."

**【解析】** 原文说明人体 70% 以上是水，地球上几乎所有生命都依赖水，B 项正确。A 项"助眠"未提及；C 项"仅用于烹饪"过于片面；D 项"植物不需要水"与 "Plants... need water too" 矛盾。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Water carries nutrients (养分) to every part of our body. It also helps remove waste from our body."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出水输送养分到全身并帮助排出废物，B 项正确。A 项"让人饱"、C 项"跑得更快"和 D 项"仅生病时需要"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some factories pour waste water into rivers and lakes. Farmers use too much fertilizer, which pollutes the water. Climate change brings long droughts (干旱) in some areas."

**【解析】** 原文列举了三类原因：工厂排污、化肥污染、气候变化导致干旱，B 项正确。A 项"人们喝水太多"、C 项"用淋浴的人多"和 D 项"植物用掉全部水"均非原文所述。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "We should turn off the tap when we brush our teeth... take shorter showers... fix leaky taps... reuse water... never throw rubbish into rivers."

**【解析】** 文中列举了个人可采取的多种行动，可推知每个人的小行动都能为保护水资源出力，B 项正确。A 项"只有政府能节水"与 "Every small action counts" 矛盾；C 项"不应淋浴"与 "shorter showers" 矛盾；D 项"修水龙头是唯一方式"过于绝对。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段水的重要性，第②段人体对水的需要，第③段水资源问题，第④段保护措施，第⑤段呼吁。

**【解析】** 文章阐述人类为何需要水以及水资源面临的挑战，并以保护水资源作结，B 项准确概括主旨。A 项"建工厂"、C 项"比较水类型"和 D 项"鼓励少喝水"均非主旨。故选 B。
