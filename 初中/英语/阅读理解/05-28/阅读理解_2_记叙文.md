> **05-28 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① It was a Monday afternoon. Zhang Ming, a junior high school student, was walking home after class. The sky turned dark quickly. Soon, heavy rain started to pour down. Zhang Ming had an umbrella, but the wind was so strong that he could hardly hold it. He ran towards a bus stop to take shelter (避雨).

② Under the roof of the bus stop, Zhang Ming saw an old lady standing alone. She had no umbrella. Her clothes were wet and she was shaking from the cold. She looked worried. Without hesitation (犹豫), he walked up to her.

③ "Grandma, please use my umbrella," Zhang Ming said. "I'll wait here with you until the bus comes." The old lady was surprised. "But you will get wet too, child," she said. Zhang Ming smiled. "I'm young and strong. You need it more than I do." He held the umbrella over her head and stood in the rain himself. The old lady's eyes turned red. She held his hand and said, "Thank you, good boy."

④ About twenty minutes later, the bus arrived. The old lady got on safely. Before she left, she asked for Zhang Ming's name and school. "I want to write a letter to your school to thank you," she said. Zhang Ming shook his head. "You don't have to. Helping others makes me happy. That's enough." He waved goodbye and walked home in the rain, but his heart was warm.

⑤ That night, Zhang Ming told his mother what had happened. His mother patted his shoulder and said, "I'm proud of you, Ming. A small act of kindness can make a big difference." Zhang Ming agreed. Helping a stranger in the rain was a small thing, but it was the right thing to do.

**注释：** shelter 避难所；hesitation 犹豫

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what was the weather like when Zhang Ming was walking home?
   A. The sky turned dark and heavy rain started to pour down.
   B. It was sunny and warm.
   C. It was snowing.
   D. It was a cool autumn evening.

2. According to paragraph ②, why did Zhang Ming decide to help the old lady?
   A. She asked him for help.
   B. He wanted to get on the same bus.
   C. She had no umbrella, was wet and shaking, and looked worried.
   D. His teacher told him to help others.

3. What did Zhang Ming do when the old lady worried about him getting wet?
   A. He left and went home.
   B. He called the police for help.
   C. He gave her his coat instead.
   D. He said he was young and strong and held the umbrella over her head while standing in the rain himself.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Zhang Ming?
   A. He wanted to get a thank-you letter from the school.
   B. He helped because it made him happy, without expecting reward.
   C. He helped the lady because he expected something in return.
   D. He was afraid of getting into trouble.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. A small act of kindness can make a difference; helping others is the right thing to do.
   B. We should always carry an umbrella when it rains.
   C. Old people should not go out in the rain.
   D. We should only help people we know.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The sky turned dark quickly. Soon, heavy rain started to pour down."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述天色变暗、随后大雨倾盆，A 项正确。B 项"晴朗温暖"、C 项"下雪"和 D 项"凉爽秋夜"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "an old lady standing alone... had no umbrella. Her clothes were wet and she was shaking from the cold. She looked worried."

**【解析】** 原文描写老人在雨中没有伞、衣服湿透、冷得发抖、神情忧虑，张明因此决定帮助她，C 项正确。A 项"她主动求助"、B 项"想同乘公交"和 D 项"老师要求"均未在文中提及。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I'm young and strong. You need it more than I do.' He held the umbrella over her head and stood in the rain himself."

**【解析】** 张明回应自己年轻体壮、老人更需要伞，并把伞撑在老人头顶，自己站在雨中，D 项正确。A 项"离开回家"、B 项"报警"和 C 项"给她外套"均与原文不符。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "You don't have to. Helping others makes me happy. That's enough."

**【解析】** 张明婉拒老人的感谢信，表示帮助别人让他快乐、这就够了，可推知他助人不求回报，B 项正确。A 项"想要感谢信"与婉拒矛盾；C 项"期待回报"与 "That's enough" 矛盾；D 项"怕惹麻烦"文中未体现。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：张明雨中助人、不图回报，及结尾母亲所说 "A small act of kindness can make a big difference" 与张明的认同。

**【解析】** 文章通过张明雨中帮助陌生老人的故事，传达善举虽小却能带来改变、助人是正确选择的主旨，A 项正确。B 项"应带伞"、C 项"老人不应雨中出行"和 D 项"只帮熟人"均非作者意图。故选 A。
