# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Weekend Movie Recommendation**

① **Film: The Little Explorer**  
*Genre: Adventure / Family*  
*Duration: 95 minutes*  
*Rating: 4.5/5*

② Jake is a 12-year-old boy who loves science. When his family moves to a small town, he feels lonely. One day, he finds an old map in his grandfather's attic. The map leads him to a hidden cave in the nearby forest. Inside, he discovers a secret garden full of rare plants and a friendly fox that seems to understand him. Together, they uncover a mystery that has been hidden for 50 years.

③ **Why we recommend it:**
- Great for the whole family — fun for kids, meaningful for adults
- Beautiful scenes of nature; no violence or scary parts
- Themes: courage, friendship, and protecting nature
- Suitable for ages 8 and up

④ **When & Where:**
- Cinema A: Sat & Sun 10:00 a.m., 2:00 p.m., 6:00 p.m.
- Cinema B: Sat & Sun 11:00 a.m., 4:00 p.m.
- Ticket: 35 yuan (child); 45 yuan (adult)
- Online booking: www.filmtown.com

⑤ Take your family to see it this weekend. You won't be disappointed!

**注释：** attic 阁楼

---

**Questions:**

1. What kind of film is *The Little Explorer*?
   A. Horror.
   B. Adventure and family.
   C. Science fiction only.
   D. Comedy only.

2. How does Jake find the hidden cave?
   A. His friend tells him.
   B. He finds an old map in his grandfather's attic.
   C. He follows a fox.
   D. His parents take him there.

3. Who is the film suitable for?
   A. Only adults.
   B. Only children under 8.
   C. Ages 8 and up; good for the whole family.
   D. Teenagers only.

4. When can you see the film at Cinema A on Saturday?
   A. Only at 10:00 a.m.
   B. At 10:00 a.m., 2:00 p.m., and 6:00 p.m.
   C. At 11:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m.
   D. Only at 6:00 p.m.

5. How much does an adult ticket cost?
   A. 35 yuan.
   B. 45 yuan.
   C. 50 yuan.
   D. Free for families.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Genre: Adventure / Family"

**【解析】** 影片类型为冒险与家庭类。A项恐怖、C项仅科幻、D项仅喜剧均不符合。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "he finds an old map in his grandfather's attic. The map leads him to a hidden cave"

**【解析】** Jake在祖父阁楼发现旧地图，地图指引他找到隐藏洞穴。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Suitable for ages 8 and up" 及 "Great for the whole family"

**【解析】** 适合8岁及以上观众，适合全家观看。A、B、D项范围过窄或错误。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Cinema A: Sat & Sun 10:00 a.m., 2:00 p.m., 6:00 p.m."

**【解析】** Cinema A周六日放映时间为10:00、14:00、18:00。A、C、D项不完整或错误。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Ticket: 35 yuan (child); 45 yuan (adult)"

**【解析】** 成人票45元。A项35元为儿童票；C、D项文中未提及。故选B。
