# 阅读理解（说明文）

# How Plants Spread Their Seeds

① Plants cannot walk or fly, but they still need to spread their seeds to new places. If all seeds fell near the parent plant, they would have to compete for light, water, and nutrients. Nature has given plants many clever ways to move their seeds far away.

② Some plants use wind. Dandelion seeds have light, fluffy hairs that act like tiny parachutes. When the wind blows, they float through the air and may land kilometers away. Maple trees have seeds with thin wings. When they fall from the tree, they spin like small helicopters and can travel a long distance before touching the ground.

③ Other plants depend on animals. Birds and squirrels eat fruits with seeds inside. The seeds pass through their bodies and are dropped in new places with natural fertilizer. Some seeds have hooks or sticky surfaces. They stick to animal fur or people's clothes and get a free ride to new areas.

④ Water also helps some plants. Coconut palms grow near the sea. Their seeds have hard shells and can float on water for weeks. Ocean currents carry them to distant beaches where they can grow into new trees. Plants that live by rivers may drop their seeds into the water, which carries them downstream.

⑤ Each method has its advantages. Wind and water can carry seeds very far, but many may never find a good place to grow. Animals often drop seeds in rich soil where they have a better chance. No matter which way plants choose, seed dispersal helps them survive and spread across the land.

**注释：** seed 种子；dandelion 蒲公英；parachute 降落伞；maple 枫树；dispersal 传播；squirrel 松鼠；fertilizer 肥料；current 水流；downstream 顺流而下

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, how do dandelion seeds travel?

   A. Their heavy weight.

   A. They cannot float on water.

   C. Their hard shells help them float and survive a long journey by sea.

   A. All plants use the same way to spread seeds.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "Dandelion seeds have light, fluffy hairs that act like tiny parachutes. When the wind blows, they float through the air..."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明蒲公英种子有轻盈蓬松的茸毛，像小降落伞一样，风起时会在空中飘浮。B项正确。A项"由动物携带"、C项"在水中漂浮"、D项"在地上滚动"均非蒲公英种子的传播方式。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Maple trees have seeds with thin wings. When they fall from the tree, they spin like small helicopters and can travel a long distance before touching the ground."

**【解析】** 原文指出枫树种子有薄薄的翅膀，落下时会像小直升机一样旋转，能飞很远才落地。B项正确。A项"沉重的重量"不利于飞行；C项"鸟吃它们"、D项"钩子粘毛"是动物的传播方式，非枫树。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some seeds have hooks or sticky surfaces. They stick to animal fur or people's clothes and get a free ride to new areas."

**【解析】** 原文说明部分种子有钩子或黏性表面，能粘在动物毛发或人衣服上，借此"搭便车"到新地方。B项正确。A、C、D项文中均未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Coconut palms grow near the sea. Their seeds have hard shells and can float on water for weeks. Ocean currents carry them to distant beaches..."

**【解析】** 原文说明椰子种子有坚硬外壳，能在水上漂浮数周，洋流将它们带至远方海滩。可推知坚硬外壳有助于其漂浮并经受长途海上旅行。C项正确。A项与"can float"矛盾；B项椰子由水非风传播；D项椰子长在海边非河中。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段提出植物需传播种子，第②段风传播，第③段动物传播，第④段水传播，第⑤段总结各法利弊。

**【解析】** 全文介绍植物种子通过风、动物、水等多种方式传播，B项最能概括主旨。A项"植物用同一方式"与全文矛盾；C项以蒲公英为例过于片面；D项与第①段"need to spread... far away"矛盾。故选B。
