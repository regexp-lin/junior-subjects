# 阅读理解（记叙文）

# A Warm Afternoon at the Nursing Home

① Last summer, Zhang Ming, a 14-year-old student, joined a volunteer program at a nursing home. At first, he felt nervous. "What could I do to help the elderly?" he wondered. He had no experience and did not know what to expect.

② On his first day, Zhang Ming was asked to read newspapers to Grandpa Wang, who had poor eyesight. As he read, Grandpa Wang listened quietly. When Zhang Ming finished an article about the weather, Grandpa Wang smiled and said, "Your voice reminds me of my grandson. He lives in another city and rarely visits." Zhang Ming saw tears in the old man's eyes. At that moment, he understood that the elderly needed more than care — they needed company and someone to talk to.

③ The next week, Zhang Ming brought his guitar. He played simple songs for the elderly. Grandma Li, who seldom spoke, began to hum along. Soon, other residents gathered around. When Zhang Ming played an old folk song, Grandma Li's eyes lit up. "I used to sing this when I was young," she said softly. Her wrinkled face broke into a warm smile.

④ One rainy afternoon, Zhang Ming visited Grandpa Wang again. The old man was sitting alone by the window, looking out at the rain. Zhang Ming sat down beside him and started a conversation. They talked about Grandpa Wang's youth, his job, and his family. "You made my day," Grandpa Wang said when Zhang Ming was about to leave. "Come again, will you?" Zhang Ming promised he would.

⑤ The volunteer program ended in August, but Zhang Ming did not stop. He still visits the nursing home twice a month. "Those afternoons changed me," he said. "I learned that a little time and care can mean so much. The elderly have so many stories to share. We just need to listen." His experience taught him the true meaning of respect and love for the old.

**注释：** nursing home 敬老院；volunteer 志愿者；elderly 年长者；company 陪伴；resident 居民；wrinkled 布满皱纹的；respect 尊敬

---

### 选择题

1. What did Zhang Ming do on his first day at the nursing home?
   A. Read newspapers to Grandpa Wang.
   B. Played the guitar for everyone.
   C. Cleaned the rooms.
   D. Cooked lunch for the residents.

2. According to paragraph ②, why did Grandpa Wang have tears in his eyes?
   A. The newspaper article was very sad.
   B. Zhang Ming's voice reminded him of his grandson who rarely visits.
   C. He was feeling unwell that day.
   D. He was angry with Zhang Ming.

3. What happened when Zhang Ming played an old folk song for Grandma Li?
   A. She asked him to stop and leave.
   B. She fell asleep in her chair.
   C. She began to hum along and said she used to sing it when she was young.
   D. She left the room without a word.

4. What did Grandpa Wang say when Zhang Ming was about to leave that rainy afternoon?
   A. "Don't bother to come again."
   B. "You are too young to understand us."
   C. "You made my day. Come again, will you?"
   D. "I prefer to be alone."

5. Which of the following is NOT mentioned in the passage?
   A. Zhang Ming still visits the nursing home twice a month after the program.
   B. The elderly need company and someone to talk to.
   C. Zhang Ming learned the meaning of respect and love for the old.
   D. Zhang Ming received payment for his volunteer work.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "On his first day, Zhang Ming was asked to read newspapers to Grandpa Wang, who had poor eyesight."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明张明第一天被安排给王爷爷读报纸，因为王爷爷视力不好。A项正确。B项"给大家弹吉他"是第二周的事；C项"打扫房间"、D项"做午餐"文中均未提及。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "Your voice reminds me of my grandson. He lives in another city and rarely visits." Zhang Ming saw tears in the old man's eyes.

**【解析】** 王爷爷说张明的声音让他想起孙子，孙子住在另一城市很少探望，且眼中含泪。可推知王爷爷因思念难得见面的孙子而落泪。B项正确。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Grandma Li, who seldom spoke, began to hum along... When Zhang Ming played an old folk song, Grandma Li's eyes lit up. 'I used to sing this when I was young,' she said softly."

**【解析】** 原文说明李奶奶开始跟着哼唱，当张明演奏老民歌时她眼前一亮，并轻声说年轻时曾唱过这首歌。C项正确。A、B、D项均与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "'You made my day,' Grandpa Wang said when Zhang Ming was about to leave. 'Come again, will you?'"

**【解析】** 原文清楚记录王爷爷在张明离开时说的原话："You made my day" 和 "Come again, will you?"。C项正确。A、B、D项均与王爷爷的感激态度相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解（排除/未提及）

**【定位】** 全文——文中提到张明继续每月探望两次（A）、老人需要陪伴（B）、张明学会尊重与关爱（C），但未提到张明获得报酬。

**【解析】** 全文为志愿服务经历，无任何关于报酬、工资或payment的表述。D项"张明因志愿工作获得报酬"文中未提及，故D为正确答案。A、B、C项均在文中有对应内容。故选D。
