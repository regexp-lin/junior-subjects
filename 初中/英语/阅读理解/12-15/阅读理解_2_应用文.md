# 阅读理解（应用文）

## **Spring Festival Activity Plan**

**Date:** January 28 – February 3  
**Venue:** City Youth Center  
**Theme:** Celebrating Traditional Chinese New Year

①Welcome to our Spring Festival week! We have prepared many fun activities for students and families. Please read the plan below and join us.

②**Lantern Making (Jan 28–29)**  
Learn to make traditional paper lanterns. Our volunteers will teach you step by step. All materials are provided. Each family can take home one lantern. Time: 2:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m. both days. Sign up at the front desk.

③**Chinese Calligraphy Workshop (Jan 30)**  
Practice writing Spring Festival couplets (春联) with brushes and ink. No experience needed. We have teachers to guide you. Bring old newspapers to practice. Time: 10:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. Limited to 30 people. Book your place online.

④**Dumpling Making (Jan 31)**  
Make jiaozi with us! We will provide flour, filling, and tools. Wear old clothes—it might get messy. Time: 3:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m. Free for students under 15. Adults: 20 yuan per person.

⑤**Cultural Show (Feb 2)**  
Lion dance, folk songs, and traditional dances. Performances start at 7:00 p.m. No ticket needed. Seats are limited—come early! For more information, visit our website or call 8765-4321.

| Activity          | Date     | Time       | Note                    |
|-------------------|----------|------------|-------------------------|
| Lantern Making    | Jan 28–29| 2–4 p.m.   | Sign up at desk         |
| Calligraphy       | Jan 30   | 10 a.m.–12 p.m. | Book online, 30 people |
| Dumpling Making   | Jan 31   | 3–5 p.m.   | Free for under 15       |
| Cultural Show     | Feb 2    | 7 p.m.     | No ticket, come early   |

## 题目

1. Where will the Spring Festival activities take place?
   A. In a school.
   B. At the City Youth Center.
   C. In a museum.
   D. At a restaurant.

2. What do you need to bring for the Calligraphy Workshop?
   A. Your own brushes and ink.
   B. Old newspapers for practice.
   C. A lantern.
   D. Flour and filling.

3. For the Dumpling Making activity, who can join for free?
   A. Everyone.
   B. Adults only.
   C. Students under 15.
   D. Only volunteers.

4. How can you get a place for the Calligraphy Workshop?
   A. Sign up at the front desk.
   B. Come early on the day.
   C. Book online.
   D. Call 8765-4321 only.

5. What should you do for the Cultural Show?
   A. Buy a ticket first.
   B. Wear old clothes.
   C. Come early because seats are limited.
   D. Bring your own chair.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第①段及表头

【解析】
- 第①段与表格中 Venue 均写：“City Youth Center”（市青年中心）。
- A、C、D 未提及。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第③段

【解析】
- 第③段写：“Bring old newspapers to practice”（自备旧报纸练习）。
- A“自备笔墨”与“We have teachers to guide you”及活动说明不符。C、D 属于其他活动。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：C**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第④段及表格

【解析】
- 第④段写：“Free for students under 15”（15 岁以下学生免费）。
- A“所有人”与“Adults: 20 yuan”矛盾。B、D 与原文不符。故答案为 C。

**4. 答案：C**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第③段及表格

【解析】
- 第③段与表格均写：“Book your place online”（网上预约）。
- A 适用于 Lantern Making。B“当天早到”适用于 Cultural Show。D“仅打电话”与“Book online”不符。故答案为 C。

**5. 答案：C**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第⑤段

【解析】
- 第⑤段写：“Seats are limited—come early!”（座位有限，请尽早到场）。
- A 与“No ticket needed”矛盾。B 适用于 Dumpling Making。D 未提及。故答案为 C。
