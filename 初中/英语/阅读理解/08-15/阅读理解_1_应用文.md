> **08-15 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Back-to-School Preparation Checklist — Sunshine Middle School**

② Welcome back! The new term starts on September 1. To help you get ready, we have prepared a checklist. Please complete these items before you return to school.

③ **Things to Bring:**
- Schoolbag, textbooks, notebooks, and stationery (文具)
- PE clothes and sports shoes for PE classes
- A water bottle to stay healthy during the day
- Your summer homework and any signed (签名的) forms from parents

④ **Things to Do:**
- Check the new class schedule online on our school website after August 28
- Set your alarm clock and go to bed early a few days before school starts
- Review what you learned last term so you can join in class quickly
- If you have changed your phone number or address, please tell the school office before August 30

⑤ **Important Notes:**
School gates open at 7:20 a.m. The first class begins at 8:00 a.m. Please arrive on time. If you feel sick or cannot come to school, your parent must call the office before 8:30 a.m. We hope you have a great new term!

**注释：** checklist 清单；stationery 文具；sign 签字；schedule 课程表

---

### 选择题

1. When does the new term start?
   A. August 28.
   B. August 30.
   C. September 15.
   D. September 1.

2. What should students bring for PE classes?
   A. PE clothes and sports shoes.
   B. Textbooks and notebooks.
   C. Water bottle and snacks.
   D. Summer homework only.

3. When can students check the new class schedule online?
   A. Before August 1.
   B. After August 28.
   C. On September 1.
   D. At the end of the term.

4. What should parents do if their child cannot come to school?
   A. Send an email to the teacher.
   B. Tell the office after school starts.
   C. Write a note and give it next week.
   D. Call the office before 8:30 a.m.

5. What is the main purpose of this notice?
   A. To help students prepare for the new term.
   B. To introduce the school website.
   C. To ask parents to buy new books.
   D. To change the school timetable.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The new term starts on September 1."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明新学期于9月1日开始。A 项"8月28日"是查课程表的时间；B 项"8月30日"是更新联系方式截止日；C 项"9月15日"未提及。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "PE clothes and sports shoes for PE classes"

**【解析】** 清单中明确列出体育课需带体育服和运动鞋。B 项"课本和笔记本"是通用用品；C 项"水杯和零食"中的零食未提及；D 项"仅暑期作业"不完整。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Check the new class schedule online on our school website after August 28"

**【解析】** 原文要求学生于8月28日后在学校官网查询新学期课程表。A 项"8月1日前"、C 项"9月1日"、D 项"学期末"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If you feel sick or cannot come to school, your parent must call the office before 8:30 a.m."

**【解析】** 原文规定若学生生病或不能到校，家长须在上午8:30前致电办公室。A 项"发邮件给老师"、B 项"开学后再告诉办公室"、C 项"下周补交便条"均不符合要求。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第②段 "To help you get ready, we have prepared a checklist."

**【解析】** 全文以返校须知形式列出开学前需完成的事项，目的是帮助学生为新学期做好准备。B 项"介绍学校网站"仅为其中一项内容；C 项"让家长买新书"、D 项"修改课程表"与主旨不符。故选 A。
