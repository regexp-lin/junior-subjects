> **05-02 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Every Friday afternoon, old Mr. Henderson sat on his front porch (门廊) with a big box of old watches. For over forty years, he had been the town's best watchmaker. Now retired, fixing broken watches was just his hobby.

② One sunny Friday, a young boy named Leo walked up to the porch. He looked about ten years old and was holding a small, dirty pocket watch. "Excuse me, sir," Leo said nervously. "My grandfather gave me this before he passed away. It doesn't tik-tok anymore. Can you fix it?"

③ Mr. Henderson took the watch carefully. It was covered in dust and its glass face was cracked (破裂). He opened the back and studied the tiny golden wheels inside. "It's an old beauty, son," he said kindly. "But it has been silent for a long time. It will take a lot of work to make it sing again."

④ "How much will it cost?" Leo asked, looking down at his worn-out shoes. "I only have three dollars." 

⑤ Mr. Henderson smiled. "Three dollars is exactly what it costs for parts. But for the labor (人工), I need something else." Leo looked up in surprise. "What is it?"

⑥ "You see, my eyes are not as sharp as before, and my front garden looks terrible. If you come here every Saturday morning to pull out the weeds (杂草) and water my roses, we will call it even." Leo agreed immediately. 

⑦ For the next month, Leo kept his promise. He worked hard in the garden while Mr. Henderson worked on the watch on the porch. The old man would tell Leo stories about the history of the town and how different watches worked. On the fourth Saturday, Mr. Henderson handed the shiny pocket watch back to Leo. 

⑧ Leo put it near his ear. *Tik-tok, tik-tok.* The sweet sound brought tears to his eyes. He thanked the old man and ran home happily. Mr. Henderson sat back in his chair, looking at his beautiful, weed-free garden. He realized he hadn't just fixed a watch; he had also grown a friendship.

**注释：** porch 门廊；watchmaker 钟表匠；cracked 破裂的；labor 劳动/人工；weed 杂草；promise 承诺

---

### 选择题

1. What was Mr. Henderson doing on his front porch every Friday afternoon?
A. He was selling new watches to the townspeople.
B. He was fixing broken watches as a hobby.
C. He was teaching the town children how to plant roses.
D. He was building a new wooden fence for his garden.

2. Why did Leo bring a pocket watch to Mr. Henderson?
A. Because he found it on the street and wanted to sell it to Mr. Henderson.
B. Because he wanted to learn how to make an old dirty watch.
C. Because his grandfather gave it to him and it was broken.
D. Because Mr. Henderson asked him to clean the cracked glass face.

3. How much did Mr. Henderson charge (收费) Leo for fixing the watch?
A. He charged three dollars for parts.
B. He asked Leo to pull weeds and water roses every Saturday morning.
C. He charged three dollars plus weeding the garden.
D. He fixed the watch for free because Leo was a poor boy.

4. What did Mr. Henderson and Leo do during the following month?
A. They sat on the porch doing nothing but listening to watches.
B. Leo worked in the garden while Mr. Henderson fixed the watch, and they shared stories.
C. They both pulled weeds in the garden until they were tired.
D. Leo taught Mr. Henderson how to ride a bike around the town.

5. How did Leo feel when he got the fixed watch back?
A. He was angry because he thought three dollars was too much.
B. He was moved to tears by the sweet *tik-tok* sound and ran home happily.
C. He was so tired from gardening that he didn't care about the watch.
D. He was surprised because the watch looked exactly the same as before.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-3句 "For over forty years, he had been the town's best watchmaker. Now retired, fixing broken watches was just his hobby."

**【解析】** 本题问亨德森老先生每个周五下午在门廊上做什么。第一段交代到他在小镇当了四十年最好的钟表匠，现在退休了，修理怀表成了他的一项业余爱好（fixing broken watches was just his hobby）。这与B选项直接对应。A卖新表、C教种玫瑰、D建栅栏都不对。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "...Leo said nervously. 'My grandfather gave me this before he passed away. It doesn't tik-tok anymore. Can you fix it?'"

**【解析】** 里奥为什么要带怀表找老先生？里奥自己说，这是爷爷去世前给他的，但是它已经不走字了（doesn't tik-tok anymore），所以想请老先生修好它。这和C选项意义贴合。A捡来的想卖、B想学造旧表、D被要求来擦破玻璃均为无稽之谈。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第⑤、⑥段 "Mr. Henderson smiled. 'Three dollars is exactly what it costs for parts. But for the labor... need something else... If you come here every Saturday morning to pull out the weeds and water my roses...'"

**【解析】** 关于修表收费，老先生收了里奥身上的三块钱来买零件，而他所要求的人工费（labor），是让里奥接下来每个周六早上来帮他拔草浇花。所以总收费是三块钱加上清理花园的劳动。A项只提三块钱不全、B项只提拔草也不全，D项完全免费错误。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "He worked hard in the garden while Mr. Henderson worked on the watch on the porch. The old man would tell Leo stories..."

**【解析】** 在接下来的一个月内两人都在干啥？书上写里奥在花园干活，亨德森在门廊修表。而且老先生会给里奥讲小镇历史和表的工作原理。选项B完美囊括了这两种活动。A无所事事、C一起拔草、D教骑车都不符合实际。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑧段第2-4句 "The sweet sound brought tears to his eyes. He thanked the old man and ran home happily."

**【解析】** 拿到修好的表时，里奥听到那清脆的滴答声，感动得热泪盈眶（brought tears to his eyes），连声道谢后开心地跑回了家（ran home happily）。这与B选项完美对应。A不满价格、C累到不关心、D一样脏皆错。故选B。
