# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Save the Earth: Join Our Recycling (回收) Program!**

② **Did you know?**
Every year, our city produces thousands of tons of rubbish. A lot of this rubbish goes into the earth or the sea, causing serious pollution (污染) to our beautiful environment. But you can help change this by joining the "Green Hope Recycling Program" in our school!

③ **What can you recycle?**
Please do not throw everything into the same bin! We accept the following items:
- **Paper:** Old newspapers, magazines, and used notebooks. (Please make sure they are dry.)
- **Plastic:** Empty water bottles and clean food boxes.
- **Glass:** Drink bottles and jars. (Be careful not to break them!)
*Note: We do NOT accept food waste or old clothes in this program.*

④ **When and Where?**
- **Time:** Every Friday afternoon from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM.
- **Place:** The school playground, near the main gate. 

⑤ **Get Rewarded (获得奖励)!**
For every 10 items you bring, you will get a "Green Star" sticker. When you collect 5 stickers, you can exchange (兑换) them for a beautiful notebook made of recycled paper or a free ticket to the school cinema!

⑥ Small actions make a big difference. Let's work together to make our city cleaner and greener! 
If you want to be a volunteer for this program, please contact Mrs. White in the Science Office.

**注释：** recycling 回收；pollution 污染；exchange 兑换

---

### 选择题

1. Why is the "Green Hope Recycling Program" started according to paragraph ②?
A. To make students do more homework.
B. To help reduce rubbish and pollution in the city.
C. To sell old things for money to build a library.
D. To teach students how to make paper correctly.

2. Which of the following items is NOT accepted in this recycling program?
A. Old magazines.
B. Empty plastic water bottles.
C. Used clothes.
D. Glass drink bottles.

3. When can students take their things to the recycling place?
A. Every Monday morning.
B. Every Friday afternoon.
C. During lunch break every day.
D. On weekends only.

4. What can a student get if he collects 5 "Green Star" stickers?
A. Five dollars and a new schoolbag.
B. A box of expensive food.
C. A notebook made of recycled paper or a free cinema ticket.
D. A free trip to the local recycling factory.

5. Who should you talk to if you want to be a volunteer for the program?
A. The headmaster of the school.
B. The science teacher, Mrs. White.
C. The city cleaner near the main gate.
D. Any student in your class.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "...goes into the earth or the sea, causing serious pollution... But you can help change this by joining the 'Green Hope Recycling Program'..."

**【解析】** 本段说明我们的城市每年产生大量的垃圾，从而造成了严重的污染，而这个回收项目正是为了帮助改变这种状况。因此B项能正确概括这一开启项目的原因。其他如A、C、D项纯属毫无根据的虚构选项。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段及括号中的注解 "*Note: We do NOT accept food waste or old clothes in this program.*"

**【解析】** 原文中列出的不被接受的项目（do NOT accept）明确点出了餐厨垃圾（food waste）和旧衣服（old clothes）。其余A、B、D三个选项均为文章鼓励回收的具体废品样例。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段的时间部分 "Time: Every Friday afternoon from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM."

**【解析】** 原文中说明同学们在周五下午（Every Friday afternoon）才可以交上自己攒的回收物。因此选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2句话 "When you collect 5 stickers, you can exchange (兑换) them for a beautiful notebook made of recycled paper or a free ticket to the school cinema!"

**【解析】** 本段指出了搜集5张绿色星星贴纸后能得到的奖励是兑换（exchange）一种由回收纸张精制的笔记本或者一张免费的校内观影票（A free ticket to the school cinema）。因此C选项与原文分毫不差地对应上。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段最后1句 "If you want to be a volunteer for this program, please contact Mrs. White in the Science Office."

**【解析】** 关于想当志愿者怎么联系，文中指引需前往科学办公室拜访怀特夫人（Mrs. White）。A项是校长；C项是在大门旁的城市保洁员；D项是同班同学。故选B。
