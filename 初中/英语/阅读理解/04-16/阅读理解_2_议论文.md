# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Today, almost every teenager plays video games. Some parents think video games are terrible. They believe games are a waste of time and make children lazy or violent (暴力的). However, is playing video games completely bad? I don't think so. In fact, video games can be good for us in several ways.

② First, video games can help improve our problem-solving skills (解决问题的能力). Many games are like difficult puzzles. Players have to think fast, make plans, and try different ways to pass a level (关卡). This helps the brain work faster. A study showed that teenagers who play strategy (策略) games often do better in logic and math tests.

③ Second, playing games can build teamwork and communication skills. Many modern games are played online with others. To win a game, players must talk to their teammates, share information, and help each other. They learn how to work with people they have never met before.

④ Finally, games are a great way to relax and reduce stress (缓解压力). After a long day of school and heavy homework, a short game can give the mind a break. Laughing and having fun in a game makes us feel happy and refreshed.

⑤ Of course, spending too much time on games is definitely bad for our eyes and studies. We should manage our time well and not let games control our lives. But we should also stop treating all video games like monsters. If played properly, they are a useful and fun tool.

**注释：** violent 暴力的；strategy 策略；reduce 减少，缓解

---

### 选择题

1. What do some parents think about video games according to paragraph ①?
A. They are a good way to relax.
B. They can help children make more friends.
C. They are a waste of time and make children lazy.
D. They should be used by teachers in schools.

2. How do strategy games help teenagers according to a study mentioned?
A. They make teenagers better at logic and math tests.
B. They teach teenagers how to cook different meals.
C. They make teenagers want to sleep longer.
D. They help teenagers run faster in PE class.

3. What can players learn when they play modern online games together?
A. How to build real houses.
B. Teamwork and communication skills.
C. How to fix a broken computer.
D. How to spend more money online.

4. Why does the writer say a short game is a great way to relax?
A. Because it gives the mind a break after hard work.
B. Because it makes students forget their homework completely.
C. Because parents will also play together.
D. Because you don't need to use your eyes.

5. What is the writer's final advice (最后的建议) in the last paragraph?
A. Never play video games anymore.
B. Play games all day if you want to be happy.
C. Manage time well and don't treat all games like monsters.
D. Ask parents to buy more video games as rewards.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-3句 "Some parents think video games are terrible. They believe games are a waste of time and make children lazy or violent (暴力的)."

**【解析】** 文章一开始提到一些家长的看法：认为电子游戏浪费时间（a waste of time）并让人变得懒惰且充满暴力。A、B选项均是将后文中说游戏的好处错误归结到了家长头上；D也是并未在段落中体现的一个教育建议。故选C。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "A study showed that teenagers who play strategy (策略) games often do better in logic and math tests."

**【解析】** 本题考查一项关于策略游戏的研究所揭示的好处：它能让青少年在逻辑与数学考试中表现更好（do better in logic and math tests）。B项的烹饪，C项的爱睡觉以及D项的跑得更快均是无中生有的错误解释。故选A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1-2句 "Second, playing games can build teamwork and communication skills. Many modern games are played online with others."

**【解析】** 第二个好处点就是现代在线游戏能锻炼人们的团队合作与沟通交流技巧（teamwork and communication skills）。这需要队伍间互相交谈、互通信息。这清楚地对应了B项。盖房子、修理电脑、乃至网络购物（A、C、D项）在这并不发生。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "After a long day of school and heavy homework, a short game can give the mind a break."

**【解析】** 对作者而言，一段短暂的游戏能让紧绷辛劳一天（上学、写重负作业）的大脑得到休息放松（give the mind a break）。忘掉全部作业是不可能的负面推断（B错）；文中也没强调父母参与（C错）；D不用眼更是荒谬（原文后文紧接着还承认了看屏幕太多对眼不利）。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 写作态度

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2-3句 "We should manage our time well and not let games control our lives. But we should also stop treating all video games like monsters."

**【解析】** 最后一段总结建议：不要整日沉迷，应恰当管理时间不让游戏掌控自己（manage our time well），并且不应把所有游戏视作“有害的怪物”（stop treating... like monsters）。作者持正面而适度的态度。这完全契合了C项对结尾主旨及规劝的归纳。A全盘否认，B完全放纵，这分别偏向了两个极端；D要求老妈买游戏做奖励纯属曲解。故选C。
