# 阅读理解 第2篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① It was a Friday evening. My family was sitting in the living room, each of us staring at our own screens. My dad was watching a video on his tablet, my mom was replying to work emails on her laptop, and I was playing a game on my phone. The room was completely silent. 

② Suddenly, everything went black. The TV, the Wi-Fi, the lights — all gone. A power cut (停电)! "Oh no!" I shouted. "I was just about to win my game!" My mom and dad also complained about not being able to finish their tasks. For a few minutes, we just sat in the dark, wondering what to do.

③ Then, my dad got up and brought a flashlight and some candles from the kitchen. The soft, yellow light danced on the walls. "Well," Dad smiled, putting his tablet away. "Since we can't use our screens, why don't we just talk?"

④ At first, it felt a little strange. We hadn’t really talked to each other without looking at our phones for a long time. Mom started first. She told us a funny story about her childhood, riding a pig on her grandparents' farm. We both burst out laughing. Then, Dad shared his adventure of climbing a mountain when he was in university. Finally, I told them about a school play I was secretly nervous about. My parents listened carefully and gave me some great advice.

⑤ Two hours passed quickly before the lights suddenly came back on. The screens lit up again, calling to us like magic. But this time, none of us rushed to pick them up. We looked at each other and smiled. The power cut had taken away our screens, but it brought our family back together.

---

**注释：** stare 凝视，盯着看；laptop 笔记本电脑；complain 抱怨；flashlight 手电筒；childhood 童年；burst out laughing 突然大笑起来；adventure 冒险

---

### 题目

1. What was everyone doing BEFORE the power cut?
   A. They were talking about their day.
   B. They were eating dinner together.
   C. They were looking at their own electronic screens.
   D. They were putting candles on the table.

2. How did the writer feel immediately after the power went out?
   A. Very happy because he could go to sleep early.
   B. Angry or upset because he was about to win his game.
   C. Scared because it was completely dark in the room.
   D. Excited because he could play some interesting board games.

3. What did the mother talk about during the power cut?
   A. A funny childhood story about riding a pig.
   B. Her difficult tasks at work.
   C. Climbing a mountain in university.
   D. Her favorite TV show on her tablet.

4. How long did the power cut last?
   A. A few minutes.
   B. Half an hour.
   C. One hour.
   D. Two hours.

5. What does the story mainly want to tell us?
   A. Sometimes losing connection to the internet can bring people closer.
   B. Modern electronic devices like phones are completely bad for us.
   C. We should always prepare enough candles at home for sudden power cuts.
   D. Parents should never force their children to stop playing games.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-3句 "My family was sitting in the living room, each of us staring at our own screens. My dad was watching a video... mom was replying... I was playing a game..."

**【解析】** 停电前，全家人正坐在客厅里，各自盯着自己的屏幕（爸爸看平板，妈妈用电脑回邮件，作者用手机玩游戏）。C 项"他们都看着各自的电子屏幕"完全符合原文。A、B、D 项在第一段描绘的场景中均未出现。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2-3句 "A power cut! 'Oh no!' I shouted. 'I was just about to win my game!'"

**【解析】** 停电刚发生时，作者立刻大喊"哦不！我马上就要赢这场游戏了！" 这表现出十分懊恼、烦躁的情绪。B 项"生气或难过，因为他正要赢得游戏"准确描绘了这一反应。A、C、D 项原文均未提及；他们最初只是坐着不知所措，抱怨无法完成屏幕上的事。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3-4句 "Mom started first. She told us a funny story about her childhood, riding a pig on her grandparents' farm."

**【解析】** 在烛光中聊天的环节，妈妈讲述了自己小时候在祖父母农场骑猪的趣事。A 项与原文一致。B 项（工作任务）是停电前她在笔记本上做的事；C 项（大学爬山）是爸爸讲述的冒险故事；D 项（喜欢的电视节目）属无中生有。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "Two hours passed quickly before the lights suddenly came back on."

**【解析】** "两个小时飞速流逝，随后灯突然亮了"，这句直接点明了停电持续的时间是两小时。D 项（两个小时）与原文数据一对一吻合。A 项（几分钟）是他们刚停电时在黑暗中不知所措的时间；B、C 项均非正确时间。故选 D。

---

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文对比（从开头满屋无言各看屏幕到结尾没电时温馨聊天）及第⑤段最后一句 "The power cut had taken away our screens, but it brought our family back together."

**【解析】** 本文通过一次突如其来的停电，让一家人放下了手中的电子设备，在黑暗中交流心声，找回了久违的家庭温暖。结尾点题：停电拿走了屏幕，却把家庭重新聚拢在了一起。A 项"有时失去网络连接反而能让人与人之间更亲近"精确提炼了全文主旨。B 项（电子设备完全是坏的）过于绝对；C 项（防备停电多备蜡烛）偏离了情感核心；D 项（父母不应强迫孩子停止游戏）偏离中心。故选 A。
