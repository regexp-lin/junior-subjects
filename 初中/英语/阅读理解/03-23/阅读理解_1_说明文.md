> **03-23 第1篇**

# 阅读理解 第1篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Have you ever wondered how your favorite chocolate bar is made? The journey of chocolate from a tree to a sweet treat in your hand is a long and interesting process. 

② It all starts with the cacao (可可) tree, which grows in hot and wet places near the equator (赤道). The tree produces large, colorful pods (豆角). Inside these pods are the cacao beans. When the pods are ripe, farmers cut them open and take out the wet beans.

③ The next step is fermentation (发酵). Farmers put the beans in large wooden boxes and cover them with banana leaves for about a week. This step is very important because it helps the beans develop their rich chocolate flavor. Without it, the beans would just taste bitter. After that, the beans are spread out in the sun to dry for another week.

④ Once dry, the beans are packed into bags and shipped to chocolate factories all over the world. At the factory, the beans are cleaned and roasted (烘烤) in big ovens. Roasting brings out the chocolate smell that we all love. Then, the beans are broken into small pieces, and the shells are removed. 

⑤ Finally, the inside of the beans is crushed (压碎) into a thick, dark liquid. Sugar and milk are added to make it sweet and creamy. After a lot of mixing and cooling, the liquid turns into solid chocolate bars ready for you to enjoy. Next time you eat a piece of chocolate, remember the hardworking farmers and the long journey it took to reach you!

---

**注释：** process 过程；equator 赤道；ripe 成熟的；flavor 味道，风味；bitter 苦的；oven 烤箱；solid 固体的

---

### 题目

1. Where do cacao trees usually grow?
   A. In high, snowy mountains.
   B. In hot and wet places near the equator.
   C. In cold and dry places far from the equator.
   D. In traditional chocolate factories.

2. Why is the fermentation step very important?
   A. Because it makes the cacao beans dry faster.
   B. Because it turns the beans into solid chocolate.
   C. Because it helps the beans develop their chocolate flavor.
   D. Because it removes the colorful shells of the pods.

3. What happens to the beans first when they arrive at the chocolate factory?
   A. They are mixed with sugar and milk.
   B. They are crushed into a thick, dark liquid.
   C. They are put in wooden boxes for a week.
   D. They are cleaned and roasted in big ovens.

4. How does the thick liquid become sweet and creamy?
   A. By roasting the beans in the sun.
   B. By adding sugar and milk to it.
   C. By wrapping it in banana leaves.
   D. By keeping it hot in the oven.

5. What does the passage mainly talk about?
   A. How to grow cacao trees near the equator.
   B. The history of chocolate in different countries.
   C. Why chocolate is bad for health if you eat too much.
   D. The process of making chocolate from beans to bars.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "It all starts with the cacao tree, which grows in hot and wet places near the equator."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出可可树生长在赤道附近炎热潮湿的地方。B 项与原文完全一致。A、C 项的气候和地理位置描述都与原文相反；D 项说生长在巧克力工厂里，显然不符合常理和原文。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "This step is very important because it helps the beans develop their rich chocolate flavor. Without it, the beans would just taste bitter."

**【解析】** 原文提到发酵（fermentation）非常重要，因为"它帮助豆子发展出浓郁的巧克力风味"。没有这一步，豆子尝起来会是苦的。C 项准确反映了发酵的重要性。A 项（让豆子干得更快）、B 项（变成固体巧克力）和 D 项（去掉彩色的壳）在文中并未作为发酵的目的被提及。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "At the factory, the beans are cleaned and roasted in big ovens."

**【解析】** 原文提到豆子运到工厂后，首先被清理并在大烤箱里烘烤（cleaned and roasted in big ovens）。D 项与原文一致。A、B 项是最后阶段（第⑤段）的操作；C 项是发酵阶段（第③段）在产地进行的操作，并非在工厂里进行的。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2句 "Sugar and milk are added to make it sweet and creamy."

**【解析】** 据第⑤段描述，在内部豆粒被压碎成浓稠的深色液体后，"加入糖和牛奶使其变得香甜如奶油般细腻"（sweet and creamy）。B 项符合该描述。A、C、D 项的做法均不能使液体变甜或含有奶香。故选 B。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 首段引语 "The journey of chocolate from a tree to a sweet treat... is a long and interesting process." 以及全文内容（从摘豆、发酵、晾干、烘烤到加入糖浆成型）。

**【解析】** 全文按时间顺序详细描述了从可可树上的豆子变成巧克力块的一系列加工步骤，重点在工艺流程（process）。D 项"从豆子到巧克力块的制作过程"准确概括了主旨。A 项（如何种植）只是制作前的一小部分；B 项（历史）虽然 03-11 有类似主题但本文重点不在历史，而在现代工艺；C 项（吃多了对健康不好）文章根本没有提及。故选 D。
