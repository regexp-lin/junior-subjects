# 阅读理解（说明文）

**Why Plants Grow Toward the Light**

① Have you noticed that houseplants often bend toward the window? Or that sunflower heads turn to follow the sun? This is not by chance. Plants have a special ability to grow toward light. Scientists call this "phototropism" — "photo" means light, and "tropism" means turning.

② Plants need light to make food. Inside the leaves, there is a green substance called chlorophyll. It uses sunlight, water, and CO₂ from the air to make sugar. This process is called photosynthesis. Without light, plants cannot make enough food and will become weak or die. So it is important for plants to get as much light as they can.

③ How do plants "know" where the light is? Plant stems and leaves have cells that can sense light. When light shines from one side, these cells send a signal. A hormone called auxin moves to the darker side of the stem. Auxin makes cells grow longer. So the darker side grows faster than the lighter side. The stem bends toward the light.

④ You can try a simple experiment at home. Put a young plant near a window. After a few days, you will see its leaves and stem lean toward the window. If you turn the pot around, the plant will gradually bend back toward the light again. This shows how strongly plants "want" to reach the light.

⑤ Understanding phototropism helps farmers and gardeners. When we grow vegetables or flowers, we should place them where they can get enough sunlight. Rotating pots from time to time can help plants grow straighter. Nature is full of such clever designs!

**注释：** phototropism 向光性；chlorophyll 叶绿素；photosynthesis 光合作用；hormone 激素；auxin 生长素；rotate 旋转

**Questions:**

1. What does "phototropism" mean?
   A. Plants grow toward the dark.
   B. Plants grow toward the light.
   C. Plants grow toward water.
   D. Plants grow toward the ground.

2. Why do plants need light?
   A. To keep warm.
   B. To make food through photosynthesis.
   C. To attract insects.
   D. To look green.

3. What makes the stem bend toward the light?
   A. The wind blows it.
   B. Auxin moves to the darker side and makes those cells grow longer.
   C. The roots pull it.
   D. The leaves are too heavy.

4. What will happen if you turn a pot around after the plant has bent toward the window?
   A. The plant will die.
   B. The plant will grow straight.
   C. The plant will gradually bend back toward the light again.
   D. The plant will stop growing.

5. How can gardeners help plants grow straighter?
   A. Put them in the dark.
   B. Rotate the pots from time to time.
   C. Water them less.
   D. Cut the stems.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Plants have a special ability to grow toward light. Scientists call this 'phototropism'"

**【解析】** 向光性指植物朝光生长的特性。A项向暗、C项向水、D项向地均与定义不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Plants need light to make food... This process is called photosynthesis."

**【解析】** 植物需要光进行光合作用制造养分。A项保暖、C项吸引昆虫、D项显得绿色均非主要原因。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "A hormone called auxin moves to the darker side of the stem. Auxin makes cells grow longer. So the darker side grows faster... The stem bends toward the light."

**【解析】** 生长素移向背光侧使细胞伸长，背光侧生长更快，茎因此弯向光。A、C、D项均与原理不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "If you turn the pot around, the plant will gradually bend back toward the light again."

**【解析】** 转动花盆后植物会逐渐再次弯向光源。A、B、D项均与原文矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Rotating pots from time to time can help plants grow straighter."

**【解析】** 定期转动花盆有助于植物长得更直。A项放暗处、C项少浇水、D项剪茎均非该建议。故选B。
