# 阅读理解（议论文）

**The Benefits of Waking Up Early**

① "Early to bed and early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy, and wise." This old saying is still true today. Waking up early has many benefits for students. Let us look at some of them.

② Early morning is often the best time for study. Your brain is fresh after a good night's sleep. There are fewer distractions — no messages, no noise from the street. Many students find that they remember new words and formulas better when they study in the morning. If you wake up one hour earlier and use that time to review, you may see better results in your exams.

③ Waking up early also gives you time for exercise. A short run, some stretches, or a quick workout can make you feel energetic for the whole day. Exercise in the morning helps you focus better in class. You do not have to do it for a long time; even 15 or 20 minutes can make a difference.

④ Another benefit is less stress. When you get up late, you have to hurry. You skip breakfast, forget things, and arrive at school feeling rushed. That adds stress. When you wake up early, you have time to eat well, pack your bag, and leave home calmly. You start the day in control, not in a panic.

⑤ Of course, waking up early means going to bed early. If you usually sleep at 11 p.m., try 10:30 p.m. Turn off your phone and computer earlier. Give yourself a week to adjust. At first it may be hard, but soon your body will get used to it. You will feel more rested and ready for each new day.

---

**Questions:**

1. Why does the writer say early morning is good for study?
   A. Because teachers prefer it.
   B. Because the brain is fresh and there are fewer distractions.
   C. Because it is warmer.
   D. Because classmates are not there.

2. What can morning exercise help with?
   A. Making you sleep more.
   B. Making you feel energetic and focus better in class.
   C. Helping you eat less.
   D. Helping you stay up late.

3. Why does waking up late add stress?
   A. Because you have more time.
   B. Because you have to hurry, skip breakfast, and feel rushed.
   C. Because the weather is worse.
   D. Because you miss the bus.

4. What should you do if you want to wake up early?
   A. Stay up later.
   B. Go to bed early and turn off phones and computers earlier.
   C. Drink more coffee.
   D. Set ten alarms.

5. What does the writer suggest about adjusting to early rising?
   A. Give up if it is hard the first day.
   B. Give yourself a week to adjust; your body will get used to it.
   C. Only try on weekends.
   D. Wake up at 5 a.m. from the first day.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Your brain is fresh after a good night's sleep. There are fewer distractions — no messages, no noise from the street."

**【解析】** 早晨大脑清醒、干扰少（如无消息、无街道噪音），适合学习。A、C、D项均非原文理由。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "A short run... can make you feel energetic for the whole day. Exercise in the morning helps you focus better in class."

**【解析】** 晨练能让人整天有精神、上课更专注。A项睡更多、C项吃得少、D项熬夜均与晨练效果矛盾。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "When you get up late, you have to hurry. You skip breakfast, forget things, and arrive at school feeling rushed. That adds stress."

**【解析】** 晚起导致匆忙、不吃早餐、遗忘、到校匆忙，从而增加压力。A项时间更多与晚起矛盾；C、D项非主要论述。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "waking up early means going to bed early... Turn off your phone and computer earlier."

**【解析】** 要早起就需早睡，并提早关闭电子设备。A项晚睡与早睡矛盾；C、D项与建议无直接关系。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Give yourself a week to adjust... your body will get used to it."

**【解析】** 作者建议给自己一周适应期，身体会逐渐习惯。A项第一天难就放弃、C项仅周末尝试、D项首日就5点起均与建议不符。故选B。
