# 阅读理解（说明文）

## Wind Power: How It Works and Its Future

① Wind power is one of the cleanest forms of energy on Earth. Unlike coal or oil, it does not produce smoke or harmful gases. The wind blows, and we can turn that moving air into electricity. More and more countries are building wind farms to power homes, schools, and factories.

② How does wind make electricity? The answer lies in wind turbines. A wind turbine is a tall tower with large blades at the top. When the wind blows, it pushes the blades to spin. The spinning blades are connected to a generator inside the tower. The generator turns the movement into electrical energy. The stronger the wind, the more electricity we can produce.

③ Wind farms are usually built in places where the wind blows strongly and often—on hills, near the coast, or in open fields. One large wind turbine can produce enough electricity for hundreds of homes. Many turbines together form a wind farm. In China, you can find wind farms in Inner Mongolia, Xinjiang, and along the eastern coast.

④ Wind power has many advantages. It is renewable, meaning we will not run out of it as long as the wind blows. It causes little harm to the environment. However, wind does not blow all the time. On calm days, we need other sources of energy, such as solar power or stored energy, to keep the lights on.

⑤ In the future, wind power will play a bigger role. Scientists are developing larger and more efficient turbines. Offshore wind farms—built in the sea where winds are stronger—are growing fast. As technology improves and costs go down, wind energy will help us build a greener and cleaner world for the next generation.

**注释：** turbine 涡轮机；blade 叶片；renewable 可再生的；offshore 近海的

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Unlike coal or oil, it does not produce smoke or harmful gases."

**【解析】** 与煤和石油不同，风力发电不产生烟雾或有害气体。A 项正确。B 项便宜、C 项仅夜间工作、D 项用水均未在原文提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When the wind blows, it pushes the blades to spin. The spinning blades are connected to a generator inside the tower. The generator turns the movement into electrical energy."

**【解析】** 风吹动叶片旋转，旋转的叶片连接塔内发电机，发电机将运动转化为电能。C 项正确。A 项太阳能、B 项雨水、D 项人工转动均与原文不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Wind farms are usually built in places where the wind blows strongly and often—on hills, near the coast, or in open fields."

**【解析】** 风电场通常建在风大且常吹的地方，如山丘、海岸附近或开阔田野。A 项正确。B 项大城市、C 项地下、D 项森林均与原文矛盾。故选 A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "However, wind does not blow all the time. On calm days, we need other sources of energy, such as solar power or stored energy, to keep the lights on."

**【解析】** 文章指出风并非一直吹，无风日需依赖太阳能、储能等其他能源。可推知仅靠风力不足，需配合其他能源。D 项正确。A、B 项过于绝对，C 项成本未提及。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第②段原理、第⑤段前景

**【解析】** 文章介绍了风力发电的原理、优势与局限性，并展望其未来作用，旨在说明风力发电如何运作及其光明前景。D 项准确概括。A 项反对、B 项仅对比、C 项描述天气均偏离主旨。故选 D。
