# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Learning Responsibility from a Stray Cat

① Last spring, Li Xiaofei found a small orange cat outside her school gate. The cat was thin and dirty. Its fur was matted, and it looked hungry. Li Xiaofei felt sorry for it. She decided to take it home. She was only in Grade 8 and had never cared for an animal before. She did not know that this little cat would change her life.

② At first, taking care of the cat was not easy. Li Xiaofei had to wake up early every morning to prepare food and water. She had to clean the cat's box and take the cat to the vet when it was sick. Sometimes she was so tired that she wanted to give up. "Why did I bring this trouble home?" she thought. But every time she saw the cat's bright eyes and heard its gentle meow, she knew she could not leave it alone.

③ Weeks passed. The cat grew stronger and healthier. Li Xiaofei's parents noticed a change in their daughter too. She used to leave her homework until the last minute. Now she finished it on time because she had to take care of the cat first. She used to forget to put away her things. Now she kept her room tidy so the cat would not get hurt. "The cat has taught you responsibility," her mother said with a smile.

④ One day, Li Xiaofei's friend asked her, "Why do you spend so much time on that cat? Don't you have better things to do?" Li Xiaofei thought for a moment and said, "Before I met this cat, I only thought about myself. Now I understand that when you promise to take care of something, you must keep your promise. That is responsibility."

⑤ Now the orange cat is part of Li Xiaofei's family. She has learned that responsibility means doing what you have promised, even when it is hard. She hopes that one day she can help more stray animals find a home. "Everyone can learn to be responsible," she says. "You just need something or someone to care for."

**注释：** stray 流浪的；matted 缠结的；vet 兽医；meow 喵（猫叫）

### 选择题

1. What was the cat like when Li Xiaofei first found it?
   A. It was thin, dirty, and hungry.
   B. It was fat and clean.
   C. It was very friendly to everyone.
   D. It had already found a home.

2. What did Li Xiaofei have to do to take care of the cat?
   A. Only feed it once a week.
   B. Prepare food and water, clean its box, and take it to the vet when sick.
   C. Let the cat stay outside all day.
   D. Ask her parents to do everything.

3. What change did Li Xiaofei's parents notice in their daughter?
   A. She became lazier and did less homework.
   B. She stopped caring about the cat.
   C. She spent all her time playing games.
   D. She finished homework on time and kept her room tidy.

4. What can we infer from Li Xiaofei's words in paragraph ④?
   A. She regrets taking the cat home.
   B. She thinks the cat is too much trouble.
   C. She used to be selfish but now understands the meaning of responsibility.
   D. She will give the cat away soon.

5. What does Li Xiaofei want to do in the future?
   A. She wants to help more stray animals find a home.
   B. She wants to stop caring for animals.
   C. She wants to become a vet only.
   D. She wants to forget about the cat.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The cat was thin and dirty. Its fur was matted, and it looked hungry."

**【解析】** 作者初次见到猫咪时，它又瘦又脏，毛发缠结，看起来饥饿。A 项正确。B 项胖且干净、C 项对所有人友好、D 项已有家均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Li Xiaofei had to wake up early every morning to prepare food and water. She had to clean the cat's box and take the cat to the vet when it was sick."

**【解析】** 李晓飞需要每天早上早起准备食物和水，清洁猫咪的便盆，生病时带它去看兽医。B 项正确。A 项每周喂一次、C 项整天放外面、D 项全由父母做均与原文矛盾。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "She used to leave her homework until the last minute. Now she finished it on time because she had to take care of the cat first. She used to forget to put away her things. Now she kept her room tidy so the cat would not get hurt."

**【解析】** 父母注意到李晓飞以前拖到最后一刻做作业，现在会按时完成；以前忘记收东西，现在保持房间整洁。D 项正确。A、B、C 项与原文相反。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Before I met this cat, I only thought about myself. Now I understand that when you promise to take care of something, you must keep your promise. That is responsibility."

**【解析】** 李晓飞坦言以前只想到自己，现在明白承诺照顾就要信守承诺，这就是责任感。可推知她从以自我为中心转变为理解责任含义。C 项正确。A、B、D 项情感与态度与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "She hopes that one day she can help more stray animals find a home."

**【解析】** 李晓飞希望将来能帮助更多流浪动物找到家。A 项正确。B 项停止照顾、C 项仅当兽医、D 项忘记猫咪均与原文矛盾。故选 A。
