# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Will Robots Take Over Human Jobs?**

② With the rapid development of technology, robots and AI (人工智能) are becoming more common in our daily life. They work in factories, serve in restaurants, and even drive cars. Many people worry: will robots replace human workers one day? This question has caused a lot of discussion.

③ **Those who believe robots will replace humans** point out that machines can do many jobs faster and more accurately than people. For example, robots in factories can work 24 hours without rest. They do not make mistakes because of tiredness. Some jobs that used to need hundreds of workers can now be done by a few machines. In the future, more jobs may disappear, and many people might lose their jobs.

④ **Those who disagree** argue that robots cannot replace humans in every way. First, robots lack creativity and emotions. They cannot invent new ideas, show kindness, or understand people's feelings. Jobs that need care, such as nursing, teaching, and art, still need human workers. Second, when some jobs disappear, new jobs will appear. For example, people are needed to design, build, and repair robots. Third, humans and machines can work together. Robots can do boring or dangerous work, while humans can focus on tasks that need thinking and communication.

⑤ **What should we do?** Instead of being afraid, we should learn new skills and adapt to the changing world. Students today should not only study textbooks but also learn how to use technology and think creatively. The future will belong to those who can work well with both humans and machines. Robots may change our jobs, but they cannot replace the value of human creativity and care.

**注释：** accurately 精确地；creativity 创造力；adapt 适应

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, where can we find robots today?
   A. In factories, restaurants, and even in cars as drivers.
   B. Only in science museums.
   C. Only in schools.
   D. Only in hospitals.

2. Why do some people think robots will replace human workers?
   A. Because robots need more money.
   B. Because robots can work faster, more accurately, and without rest.
   C. Because humans do not want to work.
   D. Because robots are kinder than humans.

3. According to paragraph ④, which jobs still need human workers?
   A. Only jobs in factories.
   B. Only jobs that use computers.
   C. Jobs that require creativity, emotions, and care, such as nursing and teaching.
   D. All jobs will be done by robots.

4. What does the writer suggest students do to face the future?
   A. Stop using technology completely.
   B. Only study textbooks and nothing else.
   C. Refuse to work with machines.
   D. Learn new skills, use technology, and think creatively.

5. The underlined word "adapt" in paragraph ⑤ probably means ______.
   A. refuse to change
   B. get used to and fit in with something new
   C. forget everything
   D. leave the place

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "They work in factories, serve in restaurants, and even drive cars."

**【解析】** 文章明确列举机器人在工厂、餐厅工作，甚至能驾驶汽车。A 项正确。B 项仅博物馆、C 项仅学校、D 项仅医院均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "machines can do many jobs faster and more accurately than people... robots in factories can work 24 hours without rest. They do not make mistakes because of tiredness."

**【解析】** 支持者认为机器人能更快、更精确地工作，可24小时运作且不受疲劳影响。B 项正确。A 项需更多钱、C 项人类不想工作、D 项机器人更善良均未提及。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "robots cannot replace humans... robots lack creativity and emotions... Jobs that need care, such as nursing, teaching, and art, still need human workers."

**【解析】** 机器人缺乏创造力和情感，护理、教学、艺术等需关爱的工作仍需人类。C 项正确。A、B 项过于片面；D 项与反对者观点矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断 / 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "we should learn new skills and adapt to the changing world... Students today should not only study textbooks but also learn how to use technology and think creatively."

**【解析】** 作者建议学习新技能、适应变化，学生除课本外还应掌握技术和创造性思维。D 项正确。A 项完全不用技术、B 项只学课本、C 项拒绝与机器合作均与作者建议相反。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "we should learn new skills and adapt to the changing world"

**【解析】** 根据上下文，面对变化的世界，人们应学习新技能并"适应"变化。adapt 意为"适应、调整"。B 项 get used to and fit in with something new 最贴近。A 项拒绝改变、C 项忘记一切、D 项离开均不符语境。故选 B。
