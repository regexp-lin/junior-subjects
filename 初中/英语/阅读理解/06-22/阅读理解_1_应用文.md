# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Radio Station Needs Young Reporters!**

② Our school radio station is looking for student reporters. If you love writing, enjoy interviewing others, and want to share news with your classmates, this is a great chance for you! We broadcast every Monday and Thursday during the lunch break. The programs include school news, music, sports, and student stories.

③ **What we need:** You should be in Grade 7 or 8, have good English skills, and be able to work with others. You need to write short reports and sometimes interview teachers or students. No experience? No problem! We will teach you how to do it.

④ **How to apply:** Please write a short self-introduction (about 80 words) and tell us why you want to join. Send it to schoolradio@ourschool.com before June 30. We will invite some students for a short interview in early July. The results will be announced on our school website.

⑤ Join us and make your voice heard! For more information, visit Room 302 or ask Ms. Wang in the teachers' office.

**注释：** broadcast 广播；interview 采访；apply 申请

---

### 选择题

1. When does the school radio station broadcast?
   A. Every Monday and Thursday during the lunch break.
   B. Every day in the morning.
   C. Only on weekends.
   D. Every Friday afternoon.

2. What kind of students is the radio station looking for?
   A. Students who are only good at music.
   B. Students in Grade 7 or 8 who love writing and enjoy interviewing.
   C. Students who have worked as reporters before.
   D. Students who can only work alone.

3. How can students apply for the position?
   A. By calling the school office.
   B. By visiting Room 302 in person only.
   C. By writing a self-introduction and sending it to the given email before June 30.
   D. By asking Ms. Wang to recommend them.

4. What will happen to some students after they send their applications?
   A. They will start working at once.
   B. They will receive the results by phone.
   C. They will have to take an exam.
   D. They will be invited for a short interview in early July.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To invite students to join the school radio station as young reporters.
   B. To introduce the school radio programs.
   C. To tell students how to write news reports.
   D. To advertise the school website.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We broadcast every Monday and Thursday during the lunch break."

**【解析】** 文章明确说明广播站每周一和周四午休时间播出。A 项正确。B 项每天早晨、C 项仅周末、D 项每周五下午均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "You should be in Grade 7 or 8, have good English skills... If you love writing, enjoy interviewing others"

**【解析】** 招募对象为七八年级、热爱写作、乐于采访、善于合作的学生。B 项正确。A 项仅擅长音乐、C 项必须有经验、D 项只能单独工作均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Please write a short self-introduction (about 80 words)... Send it to schoolradio@ourschool.com before June 30."

**【解析】** 申请方式为撰写约80词自我介绍并说明加入原因，6月30日前发送至指定邮箱。C 项正确。A 项打电话、B 项仅现场报名、D 项需老师推荐均未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "We will invite some students for a short interview in early July."

**【解析】** 部分申请者将在七月初被邀请参加简短面试。D 项正确。A 项立即开始工作、B 项电话通知结果、C 项需参加考试均与原文不符。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文标题及第②段 "Our school radio station is looking for student reporters."

**【解析】** 本文旨在招募校园广播站小记者，邀请符合条件的学生加入。A 项准确概括写作目的。B 项介绍节目、C 项教写报道、D 项宣传网站均非主旨。故选 A。
