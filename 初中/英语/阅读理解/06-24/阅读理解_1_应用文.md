# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **How to Take the Subway and Bus — A Simple Guide**

② Taking the subway or bus is a cheap and convenient way to travel in the city. Before you start your trip, you need to know a few things. This guide will help you travel safely and easily.

③ **Buying a ticket:** For the subway, you can buy a ticket at the station. Look for the ticket machine or the ticket office. You can pay with cash or use your phone. For the bus, you need to prepare exact change (零钱) or use a bus card. Put your card or money in the box when you get on. Some buses also let you pay by scanning a QR code (扫码).

④ **During the ride:** On the subway, stand behind the yellow line when you wait for the train. Give your seat to the old, the sick, or pregnant (怀孕的) women. Do not eat or drink on the train. On the bus, hold the handrail (扶手) when the bus is moving. Keep your bags close so they do not block the way.

⑤ **Getting off:** Listen to the announcement (广播) to know which station or stop is next. Press the stop button on the bus before your stop if nobody else has pressed it. When the doors open, wait for people to get off first, then get on. Have a nice trip!

**注释：** exact change 零钱；QR code 二维码；pregnant 怀孕的；handrail 扶手；announcement 广播

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, what is a good way to travel in the city?
   A. By subway or bus — it is cheap and convenient.
   B. By car every day.
   C. By taxi to save time.
   D. By bicycle only.

2. How can you pay for the bus?
   A. Only with a credit card.
   B. With exact change, a bus card, or by scanning a QR code.
   C. You need to buy a ticket one day before.
   D. The bus is free for everyone.

3. What should you do when you wait for the subway train?
   A. Stand in the middle of the platform.
   B. Eat your breakfast there.
   C. Stand behind the yellow line.
   D. Run to catch the train.

4. Who should you give your seat to on the subway?
   A. Your best friend.
   B. Young people who are playing games.
   C. Nobody — keep the seat for yourself.
   D. The old, the sick, or pregnant women.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To introduce the best tourist spots in the city.
   B. To tell readers how to travel by subway and bus safely.
   C. To complain about crowded public transport.
   D. To teach readers how to drive a bus.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Taking the subway or bus is a cheap and convenient way to travel in the city."

**【解析】** 文章开头明确说明乘坐地铁或公交是城市中便宜且便捷的出行方式。A 项正确。B 项开车、C 项打车、D 项仅骑自行车均非原文所述。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "For the bus, you need to prepare exact change (零钱) or use a bus card... Some buses also let you pay by scanning a QR code (扫码)."

**【解析】** 乘公交可采用零钱、公交卡或扫码支付。B 项正确。A 项仅信用卡、C 项提前一天购票、D 项免费均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "On the subway, stand behind the yellow line when you wait for the train."

**【解析】** 等候地铁时应站在黄线后面，以保证安全。C 项正确。A 项站台中间、B 项吃早餐、D 项跑着赶车均违背乘车规范。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Give your seat to the old, the sick, or pregnant (怀孕的) women."

**【解析】** 应主动给老人、病人或孕妇让座。D 项正确。A 项好友、B 项玩游戏青年、C 项不让座均与文明乘车要求不符。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文标题 "How to Take the Subway and Bus — A Simple Guide" 及第②段 "This guide will help you travel safely and easily."

**【解析】** 本文旨在提供地铁与公交乘车指南，帮助读者安全、便捷出行。B 项准确概括。A 项介绍景点、C 项抱怨拥挤、D 项教开车均非主旨。故选 B。
