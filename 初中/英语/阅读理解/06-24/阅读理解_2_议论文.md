# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Does a Good University Mean a Good Future?**

② Many parents and students in China believe that going to a famous university is the only path to success. They work very hard to get into top schools like Tsinghua or Peking University. The idea that "a good university equals a good future" is deeply rooted (根植) in people's minds. But is this really true?

③ **The advantages of famous universities:** There is no doubt that famous universities offer excellent teaching and resources. Students there can learn from the best professors and use modern libraries and labs. Graduates from these schools often find it easier to get jobs because employers (雇主) trust their degrees. The campus environment and connections with other talented students can also help young people grow.

④ **The other side of the story:** However, a famous degree does not always lead to happiness or success. Many successful people did not graduate from top universities. Some dropped out of school and still achieved great things. What matters more is one's attitude (态度), hard work, and willingness to learn. A person from an ordinary university who works hard and keeps improving can do better than someone from a famous school who stops learning after graduation. Success depends on many factors — not just the name of your university.

⑤ **A balanced view:** We should not worship (崇拜) famous universities blindly. A good university can open doors, but it cannot guarantee (保证) a good life. What truly shapes our future is our character, our skills, and our determination. Whether you go to a famous school or not, remember: your future is in your own hands.

**注释：** rooted 根植的；employer 雇主；attitude 态度；worship 崇拜；guarantee 保证

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what do many people in China believe?
   A. University education is not important at all.
   B. Going to a famous university is the only path to success.
   C. Students should never study too hard.
   D. Success comes only from working without rest.

2. What advantage do famous universities offer, according to the passage?
   A. They cost less money than other schools.
   B. They have shorter school years.
   C. They offer excellent teaching, resources, and make it easier for graduates to find jobs.
   D. They do not give any homework.

3. What does the writer think matters more than the name of a university?
   A. How much money your parents have.
   B. The city where the university is located.
   C. The number of friends you make at school.
   D. One's attitude, hard work, and willingness to learn.

4. The underlined word "guarantee" in paragraph ⑤ probably means ______.
   A. promise to make sure something happens
   B. forget or ignore
   C. refuse to accept
   D. doubt or question

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Everyone must go to Tsinghua or Peking University.
   B. A person from an ordinary school cannot succeed.
   C. Famous universities are useless and we should avoid them.
   D. Hard work and character can matter more than a famous degree.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Many parents and students in China believe that going to a famous university is the only path to success."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明许多中国家长和学生认为考上名校是成功的唯一道路。B 项正确。A 项大学教育不重要、C 项不应刻苦学习、D 项只工作不休息均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "famous universities offer excellent teaching and resources... Graduates from these schools often find it easier to get jobs because employers trust their degrees."

**【解析】** 名校提供优质教学和资源，毕业生更易就业因雇主认可其学历。C 项正确。A 项费用更低、B 项学制更短、D 项无作业均未提及。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "What matters more is one's attitude (态度), hard work, and willingness to learn."

**【解析】** 作者认为比名校招牌更重要的是态度、努力和求知欲。D 项正确。A 项父母财力、B 项大学所在城市、C 项交友数量均非作者强调因素。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "it cannot guarantee (保证) a good life"

**【解析】** 根据括号注释及上下文，guarantee 意为 "保证"。A 项 promise to make sure something happens 与之最贴切。B 项忘记或忽视、C 项拒绝、D 项怀疑均不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "What matters more is one's attitude, hard work, and willingness to learn... A person from an ordinary university who works hard and keeps improving can do better than someone from a famous school who stops learning after graduation."

**【解析】** 文章强调努力与品格比名校招牌更重要，普通人通过持续努力可超越名校毕业却止步不前者。D 项正确。A 项必须上清北、B 项普通人无法成功、C 项名校无用均与作者立场矛盾。故选 D。
