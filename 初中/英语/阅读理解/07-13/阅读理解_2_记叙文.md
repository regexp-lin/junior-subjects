# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last month, our school held an International Food Festival. Students could enter a cooking competition and share their favorite dishes. Chen Ming, a Grade 9 student from Guangdong, decided to take part. He wanted to make "Lo Mai Gai" (糯米饭) — sticky rice with chicken wrapped in lotus leaves. It was his grandmother's recipe and a dish he had eaten at home since childhood.

② Chen Ming practiced for two weeks. His grandmother taught him every step: how to prepare the rice, mix the ingredients, and wrap everything in lotus leaves. "The best food comes from the heart. Cook it with love," she said. Chen Ming tried again and again until the taste was just right.

③ On the competition day, Chen Ming felt nervous. There were twenty teams with dishes from France, Italy, Japan, and other countries. His stall was simple — rice, tea, and a sign about Lo Mai Gai's history. When the judges came, he served warm rice with a smile and told them about his grandmother and hometown.

④ To his surprise, Chen Ming won the first prize. One judge said, "The taste is authentic (地道的) and full of warmth." Another praised him for sharing the story behind the food. Chen Ming called his grandmother immediately to share the good news.

⑤ Later, Chen Ming said the award was not just for the food. "It was for our culture, for my grandmother, and for everyone who has passed down these recipes," he said. The experience made him proud of his roots and more confident about sharing Chinese culture.

**注释：** Cantonese 粤式的；recipe 配方；ingredient 原料；lotus 荷花；authentic 地道的；root 根源

---

### 选择题

1. What did Chen Ming decide to make for the cooking competition?
   A. Italian pasta.
   B. Japanese sushi.
   C. A French dessert.
   D. Lo Mai Gai, a traditional Cantonese dish.

2. According to paragraph ②, what did Chen Ming's grandmother teach him?
   A. Every step of making Lo Mai Gai, including preparing rice and wrapping in lotus leaves.
   B. How to set up a restaurant.
   C. How to cook French food.
   D. How to write a recipe book.

3. What happened when the judges tasted Chen Ming's dish?
   A. They said the rice was too cold.
   B. Chen Ming won the first prize.
   C. They asked him to add more salt.
   D. They suggested he change the recipe.

4. What can we infer from Chen Ming's words in paragraph ⑤?
   A. He only cared about winning the prize.
   B. He decided not to cook Cantonese food again.
   C. He wanted to open a restaurant overseas.
   D. He felt the award stood for tradition, family, and culture, not just the food.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. International food festivals are more popular in Guangdong.
   B. Grandmothers are the best cooking teachers.
   C. Lo Mai Gai is the most delicious dish in China.
   D. A student wins a cooking competition by making his hometown dish and learns to be proud of his culture.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He wanted to make a traditional Cantonese dish called 'Lo Mai Gai' (糯米饭) — sticky rice with chicken wrapped in lotus leaves."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明陈明决定做粤式传统菜"糯米鸡"，即用荷叶包裹的糯米鸡。D 项正确。A 项"意面"、B 项"寿司"、C 项"法式甜点"均非陈明所选。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "His grandmother taught him every step: how to prepare the rice, how to mix the ingredients, and how to wrap everything in lotus leaves."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明奶奶教他每一步：如何准备米饭、混合原料、用荷叶包裹。A 项正确。B 项"开餐厅"、C 项"做法餐"、D 项"写食谱"文中未提及。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "To his surprise, Chen Ming won the first prize."

**【解析】** 原文直接说明陈明出乎意料地获得一等奖。B 项正确。A 项"米饭太凉"、C 项"要求加盐"、D 项"建议改配方"均与评委评价不符，评委称赞味道地道、充满温暖。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "the award was not just for the food itself. 'It was for our culture, for my grandmother, and for everyone who has passed down these recipes through the years'"

**【解析】** 陈明表示奖项不单属于食物本身，更属于文化、奶奶以及代代传承配方的人。可推知他认为奖项代表传统、家庭与文化。A 项"只关心获奖"与"not just for the food"矛盾；B 项"不再做粤菜"与"proud of his roots"矛盾；C 项"海外开餐厅"原文未提及。D 项正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段引入陈明决定，第②③段写筹备与经历，第④⑤段写收获与感悟。

**【解析】** 全文围绕陈明在美食比赛中做家乡菜获奖的经历及其文化认同与成长展开，D 项"一名学生通过做家乡菜在烹饪比赛中获奖，并学会为自己的文化骄傲"最能概括主旨。A 项侧重节日受欢迎程度；B 项侧重奶奶教学；C 项过于绝对，均非全文重点。故选 D。
