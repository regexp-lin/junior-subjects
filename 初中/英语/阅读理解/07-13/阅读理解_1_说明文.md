# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Spider silk is one of the most amazing materials in nature. Although a spider's thread looks thin and weak, it is actually stronger than steel of the same thickness. Scientists have studied spider silk for many years and found that it has some unusual properties that make it very special.

② A single spider can produce several kinds of silk for different purposes. Some silk is used to catch insects for food. This silk is sticky so that flies and other small animals cannot escape. Another kind of silk is used to build the frame of the web. This silk is strong and elastic (有弹性的), which helps the web stay in shape even when a large insect hits it.

③ One reason spider silk is so strong is its structure. It is made of protein molecules (蛋白质分子) that are arranged in a special way. The molecules form long chains that can stretch without breaking. When scientists compare spider silk with steel, they find that spider silk can absorb more energy before it breaks. This means it is excellent for catching prey (猎物) that moves quickly.

④ People have tried to use spider silk in many ways. In the past, some cultures used it to make fishing nets and wound dressings. Today, scientists are working on creating artificial spider silk in labs because spiders are difficult to keep in large numbers. If we can make spider silk in factories, it could be used to make stronger and lighter clothes, medical materials, and even parts for airplanes.

⑤ Spider silk remains a wonder of nature. As research continues, we may discover more uses for this incredible material. It shows us how much we can learn from the natural world around us.

**注释：** elastic 有弹性的；molecule 分子；prey 猎物；artificial 人造的；wound dressing 伤口敷料

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what makes spider silk special?
   A. It is the thinnest material in nature.
   B. It can only be found in certain countries.
   C. It was discovered by scientists last year.
   D. It is stronger than steel of the same thickness.

2. According to paragraph ②, why is some spider silk sticky?
   A. To catch insects for food.
   B. To help the spider climb up and down.
   C. To make the web look more beautiful.
   D. To protect the spider from rain.

3. What does paragraph ③ tell us about the structure of spider silk?
   A. It is made of metal like steel.
   B. It breaks easily when it is stretched.
   C. Its molecules form long chains that can stretch without breaking.
   D. It cannot absorb any energy.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about using spider silk?
   A. Spiders are easy to keep in large numbers for silk production.
   B. Artificial spider silk may be used in clothes and medicine in the future.
   C. Spider silk has never been used by humans before.
   D. Factories already produce large amounts of spider silk today.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To compare spiders with other animals.
   B. To encourage people to destroy spider webs.
   C. To explain the amazing properties of spider silk and its possible uses.
   D. To tell readers how to keep spiders as pets.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Although a spider's thread looks thin and weak, it is actually stronger than steel of the same thickness."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明蜘蛛丝看起来细弱，但同等厚度下比钢铁更强。D 项正确。A 项"自然界最薄的材料"文中未提及；B 项"只在某些国家存在"、C 项"去年才被科学家发现"均与原文不符。故选 D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Some silk is used to catch insects for food. This silk is sticky so that flies and other small animals cannot escape."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明某些蜘蛛丝用于捕捉昆虫作为食物，因此具有黏性以便困住苍蝇等小动物。A 项正确。B 项"帮助蜘蛛攀爬"、C 项"使网更美观"、D 项"防雨"均非黏丝的用途。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "It is made of protein molecules that are arranged in a special way. The molecules form long chains that can stretch without breaking."

**【解析】** 原文指出蜘蛛丝由蛋白质分子按特殊方式排列组成，分子形成可拉伸不断裂的长链。C 项正确。A 项"由金属制成"错误；B 项"拉伸易断"与"stretch without breaking"矛盾；D 项"不能吸收能量"与"can absorb more energy"相反。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "scientists are working on creating artificial spider silk in labs... If we can make spider silk in factories, it could be used to make stronger and lighter clothes, medical materials, and even parts for airplanes."

**【解析】** 原文指出科学家正研发人造蜘蛛丝，若能工厂化生产，可用于服装、医疗材料甚至飞机部件。可推知人造蜘蛛丝未来或应用于服装与医疗。A 项"蜘蛛易大量饲养"与"spiders are difficult to keep in large numbers"相反；B 项"人类从未使用过"与"some cultures used it"矛盾；C 项"工厂已大量生产"与"working on"不符。D 项正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第①段引出蜘蛛丝特性，第②③段介绍结构与种类，第④段介绍应用与研发，第⑤段总结。

**【解析】** 全文围绕蜘蛛丝的神奇特性及潜在应用展开，C 项"解释蜘蛛丝的神奇特性及其可能的用途"准确概括写作目的。A 项"比较蜘蛛与其他动物"、B 项"鼓励人们破坏蛛网"、D 项"教读者养蜘蛛"均与文章内容不符。故选 C。
