# 阅读理解（说明文）

**The Five Senses of Human Beings**

① Human beings have five main senses: sight, hearing, smell, taste, and touch. Each sense helps us understand the world around us. Without them, our daily life would be very different.

② Sight is our strongest sense. We use our eyes to see colours, shapes, and movements. About 80% of what we learn comes through our eyes. Hearing helps us enjoy music and talk to each other. Our ears pick up sounds from near and far. Smell is closely linked to memory. A certain smell can remind us of a place or a person from the past. Taste works with smell. When we have a cold, food often tastes dull because we cannot smell well.

③ Touch is different from the other senses. Our skin has many nerve endings. We can feel hot, cold, pain, and pressure. Touch helps babies grow. Studies show that babies who are held and touched often become healthier and happier. Touch also shows care. A hug or a hand on the shoulder can make someone feel better.

④ Sometimes our senses can fool us. For example, optical illusions trick our eyes. Loud noise can damage our hearing. Too much spicy food can make our tongue feel like it is on fire. So we need to protect our senses and use them wisely.

⑤ Our five senses work together to give us a full picture of the world. They help us stay safe, enjoy life, and connect with others. We should be thankful for these wonderful gifts.

**注释：** optical illusion 视错觉；nerve ending 神经末梢

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why does food taste dull when we have a cold?
   A. Because we are too sick to eat.
   B. Because we cannot smell well, and taste works with smell.
   C. Because our tongue stops working.
   D. Because we lose our sense of touch.

2. What does Paragraph ③ say about touch and babies?
   A. Babies do not like to be touched.
   B. Babies who are held often become healthier and happier.
   C. Touch is harmful to babies.
   D. Babies cannot feel touch.

3. Which sense is described as "our strongest sense"?
   A. Hearing.
   B. Smell.
   C. Sight.
   D. Taste.

4. What can we infer from Paragraph ④?
   A. Our senses never make mistakes.
   B. We should protect our senses because they can sometimes fool us or be damaged.
   C. Optical illusions are bad for everyone.
   D. We should never eat spicy food.

5. What is the main idea of the passage?
   A. Only sight matters.
   B. The five senses work together to help us understand the world and we should value them.
   C. Babies need no senses.
   D. Touch is the most important sense.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Taste works with smell. When we have a cold, food often tastes dull because we cannot smell well."
**【解析】** 味觉与嗅觉相连，感冒时嗅觉变差，食物因此显得寡淡。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Studies show that babies who are held and touched often become healthier and happier."
**【解析】** 研究显示，常被抱和触摸的婴儿更健康、更快乐。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Sight is our strongest sense."
**【解析】** 视觉被描述为"我们最强的感官"。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第④段 "Sometimes our senses can fool us... Loud noise can damage our hearing... we need to protect our senses and use them wisely"
**【解析】** 感官会欺骗我们，噪声会损害听力，因此要保护好感官并明智使用。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 主旨大意
**【定位】** 全文，尤其第①段和第⑤段
**【解析】** 全文介绍五感及其作用，结尾强调五感共同帮助我们认识世界，应珍惜它们。B项概括准确。A、C、D项过于片面。故选B。
