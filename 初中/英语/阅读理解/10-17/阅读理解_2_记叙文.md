> **10-17 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Helping a Lost Foreigner Find the Way**

① Last Saturday afternoon, I was walking home from the library when I saw a man standing at the corner. He held a map and looked around with a worried expression. He had blue eyes and fair hair. I guessed he was a foreigner.

② "Excuse me, do you speak English?" he asked. "Yes, a little," I replied. I was nervous because I had never talked to a foreigner alone before. But I wanted to help. He said he was from France and was looking for the Art Museum. He had been walking for twenty minutes but could not find it.

③ I knew where the Art Museum was. It was about ten minutes' walk away. I offered to take him there. He was very grateful. On the way, we chatted a bit. He told me he was an artist and had come to China for an exhibition. He said he loved our city and the people were very friendly.

④ When we arrived at the museum, he shook my hand and said, "Thank you so much! You are very kind." He gave me a small painting as a gift. I was surprised and happy. I never thought that helping a stranger could bring such joy.

⑤ That day I learned something important. A little help can make a big difference. Language is not a barrier when we have a kind heart. I am proud that I could help someone in need, and I will never forget his smile.

**注释：** exhibition 展览；barrier 障碍

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did the writer meet the foreigner?
   A. At the Art Museum.
   B. At the library.
   C. On the way home from the library.
   D. In France.

2. What was the foreigner looking for?
   A. A library.
   B. A hotel.
   C. The Art Museum.
   D. A bus stop.

3. How did the writer feel when the foreigner first spoke to him?
   A. Angry.
   B. Nervous but willing to help.
   C. Bored.
   D. Very confident.

4. What did the foreigner give to the writer as a gift?
   A. A map.
   B. Some money.
   C. A small painting.
   D. A book.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer will never help strangers again.
   B. Kindness can cross language barriers and make a difference.
   C. Foreigners are always lost.
   D. The writer only helps people from France.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "I was walking home from the library when I saw a man standing at the corner"
**【解析】** 作者在从图书馆回家的路上遇见这位外国人。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "He said he was from France and was looking for the Art Museum"
**【解析】** 外国人来自法国，正在找美术馆。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "I was nervous because I had never talked to a foreigner alone before. But I wanted to help."
**【解析】** 作者紧张，因为从未单独和外国人交流过，但仍愿意帮忙。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "He gave me a small painting as a gift"
**【解析】** 外国人送给作者一幅小画作为礼物。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "A little help can make a big difference. Language is not a barrier when we have a kind heart."
**【解析】** 作者领悟到，一点帮助也能带来很大改变，善良可以跨越语言障碍。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
