> **04-07 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① One afternoon, a poor boy who was selling goods from door to door to pay his way through school found he had only one dime (十分硬币) left. He was very hungry. He decided he would ask for a meal at the next house. However, when a young and beautiful woman opened the door, he only had the courage to ask for a glass of water. 

② The young woman saw that he looked very hungry, so she brought him a large glass of milk instead of water. He drank it slowly and then asked, "How much do I owe (欠) you?" "You don't owe me anything," she replied. "Mother has taught us never to accept pay for a kindness." He said, "Then I thank you from my heart." As the boy, Howard Kelly, left that house, he felt stronger, and his belief in God and man was renewed.

③ Many years later, that young woman became very sick. The local doctors were unable to cure (治愈) her illness. Finally, she was sent to a big city hospital. Dr. Howard Kelly was called in for the consultation (会诊). When he heard the name of the town she came from, a strange light filled his eyes. 

④ Immediately, he went down the hall to her room. He recognized her at once. He went back to the consultation room completely determined to do his best to save her life. After a long struggle (努力), the battle was won; she recovered (康复) nicely. 

⑤ At last, the hospital bill was sent to her room. She looked at it, fearing it would take her whole life to pay for it all. On the edge of the bill, there were some words. She read: "Paid in full with one glass of milk. —Dr. Howard Kelly." Tears of joy flooded her eyes.

**注释：** dime 十分硬币；owe 欠；cure 治愈；consultation 会诊；recover 康复

---

### 选择题

1. Why did the poor boy go from door to door?
A. He wanted to collect old clothes.
B. He was begging for food.
C. He was selling things to pay for his school.
D. He wanted to meet a beautifully young woman.

2. What did the woman give the boy when he asked for water?
A. A dime.
B. A large glass of milk.
C. A meal.
D. A hot soup.

3. Why didn't the woman ask the boy to pay for the milk?
A. Because the boy had only one dime left.
B. Because it was not fresh milk.
C. Because she was a very rich person.
D. Because her mother taught her not to accept pay for a kindness.

4. Form the story, what can we guess about Howard Kelly when he saw the woman again?
A. He was very angry with her.
B. He didn't know who she was.
C. He wanted her to pay back his money.
D. He had become a doctor and wanted to save her life.

5. What is the most important message (信息) we can learn from this story?
A. If you help others, others will help you in return.
B. A glass of milk is very expensive.
C. Doctors always work in big city hospitals.
D. Selling goods from door to door is hard.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "...a poor boy who was selling goods from door to door to pay his way through school..."

**【解析】** 原文开篇交代了背景，这个贫穷的小男孩挨家挨户推销商品（selling goods from door to door）是为了挣学费读完学业（pay his way through school）。A、B、D项与文意不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "The young woman saw that he looked very hungry, so she brought him a large glass of milk instead of water."

**【解析】** 小男孩原本只敢要一杯水，但善良的年轻女子看出他饿了，于是并没有只给水，而是端出了一大杯牛奶（a large glass of milk）。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "Mother has taught us never to accept pay for a kindness."

**【解析】** 当小男孩问需要付多少钱时，女子的回答是“妈妈教导我们，行善不图回报”（不要为善意收钱）。A、B、C项虽然文中有男孩的经济状况陈述或者符合某种社会认知，但不是这名女子不收钱的本意和原话。故选D。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断 / 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3-4句 "Dr. Howard Kelly was called in... a strange light filled his eyes." 及第④段。

**【解析】** 多年后，女子病重进了大医院，当初的男孩已经成了Dr. Howard Kelly。当他认出女子的来历时，大开杀戒地想要努力挽救她的生命。这一切说明他当上了医生，并且想要拯救当年的恩人。A、B、C项的猜测全部与后文（包括最后的账单事件）不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意题

**【定位】** 首尾呼应的故事情节和情感升华处。

**【解析】** 这篇记叙文展现了“善有善报”。多年前女子用一杯牛奶行善，多年后这杯牛奶作为医疗费的回报，救了她一命。全文明线是“善良与回报”（...paid in full with one glass of milk.）。A项准确描述了这一传递善意、反哺恩情的主题；B、C、D项或是太肤浅、或是抓取微不足道的细枝末节。故选A。
