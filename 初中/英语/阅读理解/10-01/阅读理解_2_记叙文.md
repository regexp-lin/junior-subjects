> **10-01 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① October 1 is National Day in China. This year, my family decided to go to Tian'anmen Square in Beijing to watch the flag-raising ceremony (仪式). We had always seen it on TV, but we wanted to experience it in person. Dad said, "We need to get up very early. Thousands of people will be there." We set our alarms for 4:00 a.m.

② When the alarm rang, I could hardly open my eyes. It was still dark outside. But the thought of the national flag flying up into the morning sky gave me strength. We took a taxi to the square. The streets were quiet. When we arrived, I was surprised. There were already many people — families with children, elderly couples, and young people with cameras. Everyone was waiting quietly. The air was cool and fresh. I felt a sense of excitement and respect.

③ At around 6:10 a.m., the square became still. The honour guard (仪仗队) marched out with perfect steps. They carried the national flag. When the music of the national anthem (国歌) began, everyone stood straight and sang. I sang too. I had sung the anthem many times at school, but this time it was different. I was standing in the heart of our country, with thousands of people around me, all sharing the same moment. I felt proud. I felt that I was part of something bigger than myself.

④ As the flag rose slowly, the sun came up behind us. The red flag waved in the golden light. Many people had tears in their eyes. Mum held my hand. Dad stood straight with his hand on his chest. For those few minutes, we were not just a family — we were one with every Chinese person who loves this land.

⑤ We stayed for a while after the ceremony. We took photos and walked around the square. On our way home, Dad said, "Remember this day. Our country has gone through many difficulties to become what it is today. We should be grateful and work hard for a better future." I nodded. That National Day morning would stay in my heart forever.

**注释：** ceremony 仪式；honour guard 仪仗队；anthem 国歌

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer's family go to Tian'anmen Square on National Day?
   A. To go shopping.
   B. To watch the flag-raising ceremony in person.
   C. To meet friends.
   D. To have breakfast.

2. What time did they set their alarms?
   A. 6:00 a.m.
   B. 4:00 a.m.
   C. 5:00 a.m.
   D. 7:00 a.m.

3. How did the writer feel when singing the national anthem at the square?
   A. Bored and tired.
   B. Proud and part of something bigger; different from singing at school.
   C. Afraid of the crowd.
   D. Hungry.

4. What happened as the flag rose?
   A. It started to rain.
   B. The sun came up; the red flag waved in the golden light; many people had tears.
   C. Everyone went home.
   D. The music stopped.

5. What did Dad want the writer to remember?
   A. To buy souvenirs.
   B. That our country has gone through many difficulties; we should be grateful and work hard for a better future.
   C. To wake up early every day.
   D. To take more photos.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "my family decided to go to Tian'anmen Square in Beijing to watch the flag-raising ceremony... we wanted to experience it in person"

**【解析】** 全家去天安门广场是为亲眼看升旗仪式。A、C、D 项与目的不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "We set our alarms for 4:00 a.m."

**【解析】** 原文明确说闹钟设为凌晨 4 点。A、C、D 项时间错误。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "I felt proud. I felt that I was part of something bigger than myself... this time it was different"

**【解析】** 作者感到骄傲，觉得自己融入更大的集体，与在学校演唱感受不同。A、C、D 项与情感不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "As the flag rose slowly, the sun came up behind us. The red flag waved in the golden light. Many people had tears in their eyes"

**【解析】** 升旗时日出，红旗在晨光中飘扬，许多人眼含热泪。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Remember this day. Our country has gone through many difficulties to become what it is today. We should be grateful and work hard for a better future"

**【解析】** 父亲希望记住祖国历经艰难才有今天，要感恩并为美好未来努力。A、C、D 项与父亲的话不符。故选 B。
