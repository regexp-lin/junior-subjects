# 阅读理解（说明文）

① China has made great progress in manned (载人的) spaceflight. In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut to fly into space in the Shenzhou-5 spacecraft. Since then, China has sent more astronauts into space, built a space station, and carried out spacewalks and scientific experiments. These achievements have made the whole nation proud.

② The Chinese space station, called Tiangong, is now orbiting (环绕) the Earth. Astronauts live and work there for months at a time. They do scientific experiments, repair equipment, and take spacewalks to work outside the station. The space station has several modules (舱段) that were launched (发射) one by one and joined together in space. This shows China's advanced technology in space engineering.

③ China's manned space programme has achieved many "firsts." In 2008, Zhai Zhigang performed China's first spacewalk. In 2012, Liu Yang became China's first woman in space. In 2021, the first astronauts entered the Tiangong space station. In 2024, Chinese astronauts stayed in space for record-breaking periods. They grew vegetables, studied how the body changes in space, and tested new technologies. Every mission (任务) brings new knowledge and new hope.

④ Why does China invest (投入) in manned spaceflight? First, space research helps us understand the universe and our planet. Experiments in space can lead to new discoveries in medicine, materials, and energy. Second, it inspires (激励) young people to study science and dream big. Many students say they want to become astronauts after watching the launches. Third, it shows China's ability to work on difficult projects and encourages national pride. Space exploration is a symbol of a country's strength and vision (远见).

⑤ China's space journey is far from over. Plans include sending astronauts to the Moon and building a lunar (月球的) base in the future. The stars are not the limit — they are the destination.

**注释：** manned 载人的；orbit 环绕；module 舱段；launch 发射；mission 任务；invest 投入；inspire 激励；vision 远见；lunar 月球的

---

**Questions:**

1. Who was the first Chinese astronaut to fly into space?
   A. Zhai Zhigang.
   B. Yang Liwei in 2003 with Shenzhou-5.
   C. Liu Yang.
   D. No one yet.

2. What is Tiangong?
   A. A Chinese satellite.
   B. The Chinese space station orbiting the Earth where astronauts live and work.
   C. A type of rocket.
   D. A space museum.

3. Which of the following is a "first" achieved by China's space programme?
   A. The first country to use airplanes.
   B. Zhai Zhigang's first spacewalk in 2008; Liu Yang as first Chinese woman in space in 2012.
   C. The first country to have a railway.
   D. The first country to use the internet.

4. According to the passage, why does China invest in manned spaceflight?
   A. Only to beat other countries.
   B. For space research and new discoveries; to inspire young people; to show national ability and pride.
   C. To make money.
   D. Because other countries asked China to do it.

5. What does the writer say about China's future in space?
   A. China will stop space programmes.
   B. China plans to send astronauts to the Moon and build a lunar base; the stars are the destination.
   C. China will only stay in low orbit.
   D. Space is too expensive.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "In 2003, Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut to fly into space in the Shenzhou-5 spacecraft"

**【解析】** 2003 年杨利伟乘神舟五号成为中国首位航天员。A、C、D 项与"首位"不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The Chinese space station, called Tiangong, is now orbiting the Earth. Astronauts live and work there"

**【解析】** 天宫为中国空间站，航天员在其中生活工作。A、C、D 项与定义不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "In 2008, Zhai Zhigang performed China's first spacewalk. In 2012, Liu Yang became China's first woman in space"

**【解析】** 原文列举翟志刚首次出舱、刘洋成为中国首位女航天员等。A、C、D 项与太空计划无关。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "space research helps us understand... inspires young people... shows China's ability... encourages national pride"

**【解析】** 原文说明科研探索、激励青少年、展示国力、增强民族自豪感等目的。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Plans include sending astronauts to the Moon and building a lunar base... The stars are not the limit — they are the destination"

**【解析】** 作者指出中国计划登月、建月球基地，星辰是目标。A、C、D 项与作者表述相反。故选 B。
