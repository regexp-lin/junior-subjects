## Fresh Water on Earth

①About 70% of the Earth's surface is covered by water, but most of it is salt water in the oceans. Only about 2.5% of the world's water is fresh water that we can drink and use. This small amount is very important for all living things on our planet.

②Where does fresh water come from? Most of it is found in ice and snow at the North and South Poles. These ice sheets hold about 68% of all fresh water. About 30% is stored underground in aquifers. Rivers and lakes hold less than 1% of the world's fresh water, but they are the main source we use every day.

③The problem is that fresh water is not evenly distributed. Some countries have plenty of water, while others face serious water shortages. In dry areas, people may walk for hours each day just to get clean water. Climate change is making this worse. When glaciers melt, less water is stored for the dry season.

④What can we do to save water? Simple actions help a lot. Turn off the tap when you brush your teeth. Fix leaking taps at home. Take shorter showers. We should also protect rivers and lakes from pollution so that our water stays clean and safe for everyone.

⑤Fresh water is a precious resource. We must use it wisely and protect it for future generations. Every drop counts.

*glacier: 冰川  aquifer: 含水层*

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B 【题型】细节理解 【定位】第①段第二句 【解析】** 文中明确提到"Only about 2.5% of the world's water is fresh water"，全球仅有约2.5%的水是淡水。

**2. 【答案】C 【题型】细节理解 【定位】第②段第二、三句 【解析】** 文章指出"Most of it is found in ice and snow at the North and South Poles. These ice sheets hold about 68% of all fresh water"，大部分淡水储存在南北极的冰雪中。

**3. 【答案】B 【题型】细节理解 【定位】第③段倒数第二句 【解析】** 文中写道"Climate change is making this worse"，气候变化使淡水短缺问题加剧。

**4. 【答案】C 【题型】细节理解 【定位】第④段 【解析】** 文章提到的节水方法有：刷牙时关水龙头、修理漏水的水龙头、缩短淋浴时间。回收塑料瓶未在文中提及。
