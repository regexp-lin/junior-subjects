> **07-23 第2篇**

## A Special Summer

①Last summer, my parents had to work in another city for two months. They asked me to look after my younger brother and sister. At first, I felt worried. I was only fourteen. How could I take care of a ten-year-old boy and a seven-year-old girl?

②The first week was difficult. My brother refused to eat vegetables. My sister cried every night because she missed Mum and Dad. I had to cook, clean the house, and help them with their homework. Sometimes I felt so tired that I wanted to give up. But I knew my parents were counting on me.

③Gradually, things became easier. I learned to make simple meals that both of them liked. We set up a routine: breakfast at eight, homework in the morning, and games in the afternoon. My brother even started to try new vegetables when I told him they would make him stronger. My sister stopped crying when I read her a story before bed each night.

④By the end of the summer, I had changed a lot. I understood how hard my parents work every day. I learned to be patient and responsible. One evening, my sister said, "You're the best big brother in the world." That made me feel proud and happy.

⑤When my parents came home, they were surprised to see how well we had managed. Mom hugged me and said, "You've really grown up." Looking back, that summer was the hardest but also the most meaningful time of my life.

---

1. Why did the writer have to take care of his brother and sister?
   A. His brother and sister were ill.  B. His parents worked in another city.
   C. He wanted to earn some money.  D. It was a school project.

2. How did the writer feel at the beginning?
   A. Excited  B. Worried  C. Proud  D. Happy

3. What helped the writer's sister stop crying at night?
   A. Playing games  B. Eating vegetables  C. Reading stories  D. Doing homework

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The writer enjoyed cooking from the start.  B. The writer became more responsible.
   C. The brother never ate vegetables.  D. The parents came back after one month.

5. What does "grown up" mean in the last paragraph?
   A. Become taller  B. Become more mature  C. Become older  D. Become stronger

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B 【题型】细节理解 【定位】第①段第一、二句 【解析】** 文中明确说"They asked me to look after my younger brother and sister"是因为"my parents had to work in another city for two months"。

**2. 【答案】B 【题型】细节理解 【定位】第①段第三句 【解析】** 文章写道"At first, I felt worried"，作者一开始感到担忧。

**3. 【答案】C 【题型】细节理解 【定位】第③段最后一句 【解析】** 文中提到"My sister stopped crying when I read her a story before bed each night"，每晚睡前给妹妹讲故事帮助她停止哭泣。

**4. 【答案】B 【题型】推理判断 【定位】第④段 【解析】** 作者说"I learned to be patient and responsible"，母亲也称赞"You've really grown up"，可推知作者变得更负责任。

**5. 【答案】B 【题型】词义猜测 【定位】第⑤段 【解析】** 结合上下文，母亲在作者独立照顾弟妹两个月后说他"grown up"，此处指心智成熟、变得更懂事，而非字面的"长高"或"变老"。
