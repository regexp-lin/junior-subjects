> **08-14 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① It was a sunny afternoon in May. The students of Class 3 were having a PE lesson on the playground. They were practicing the long jump. Everyone was excited and taking turns to jump into the sand pit (沙坑). Li Wei, a tall boy who was good at sports, had just finished his turn and was walking back to the line.

② Suddenly, he heard a cry. "Ouch! My ankle (脚踝)!" Xiao Ming had landed badly and fallen to the ground. He was holding his left ankle with both hands, his face pale with pain. The PE teacher, Mr. Chen, was on the other side of the playground helping another group. He had not noticed what had happened yet.

③ Li Wei did not hesitate. He ran over to Xiao Ming at once. "Are you okay? Don't move. Let me help you." He knelt down beside his classmate and asked him to sit still. Then he waved to Mr. Chen to come over. While waiting, Li Wei spoke to Xiao Ming in a calm voice to comfort him. "It's going to be okay. The teacher is coming. Just breathe slowly."

④ When Mr. Chen arrived, he checked Xiao Ming's ankle and said it might be a sprain (扭伤). He asked two students to help carry Xiao Ming to the school clinic. Li Wei volunteered to go with them. At the clinic, the nurse gave Xiao Ming first aid. Li Wei stayed with him until the nurse said everything was fine.

⑤ Back on the playground, Mr. Chen gathered all the students. "I want to praise Li Wei today," he said. "When Xiao Ming got hurt, Li Wei was the first to help. He stayed calm, acted quickly, and took care of his classmate. That is what we need — students who care about others." Li Wei felt a little shy but also proud. Helping a friend in need had made the day meaningful.

**注释：** pit 坑；ankle 脚踝；hesitate 犹豫；kneel 跪；comfort 安慰；sprain 扭伤；clinic 医务室；volunteer 自愿；first aid 急救；meaningful 有意义的

---

### 选择题

1. What were the students doing when the accident happened?
   A. Practicing the long jump.
   B. Running a race.
   C. Playing basketball.
   D. Doing warm-up exercises.

2. Who got hurt during the PE lesson?
   A. Li Wei.
   B. Mr. Chen.
   C. The nurse.
   D. Xiao Ming.

3. What did Li Wei do first when he saw Xiao Ming get hurt?
   A. He went to find the nurse.
   B. He ran over to Xiao Ming and asked him not to move.
   C. He shouted for Mr. Chen from far away.
   D. He carried Xiao Ming to the clinic alone.

4. Why did Mr. Chen praise Li Wei?
   A. Because Li Wei was the best at long jump.
   B. Because Li Wei had known Xiao Ming for a long time.
   C. Because Li Wei was the tallest boy in the class.
   D. Because Li Wei was the first to help, stayed calm, and took care of his classmate.

5. What can we infer about Li Wei from the story?
   A. He often gets hurt during PE lessons.
   B. He wanted to show off in front of Mr. Chen.
   C. He cares more about sports than about his classmates.
   D. He is a caring person who is willing to help others in need.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "They were practicing the long jump... taking turns to jump into the sand pit."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明学生们当时正在练习跳远，轮流跳入沙坑。B 项"跑步比赛"、C 项"打篮球"和 D 项"做热身运动"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Xiao Ming had landed badly and fallen to the ground. He was holding his left ankle with both hands."

**【解析】** 原文指出小明落地不当摔倒，双手捂着左脚踝。A 项"李伟"是帮助者；B 项"陈老师"是老师；C 项"护士"是后来在医务室出现的。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "He ran over to Xiao Ming at once... 'Don't move. Let me help you.' He knelt down beside his classmate and asked him to sit still."

**【解析】** 原文描述李伟第一时间跑向小明，让他不要动并帮助他，跪在旁边让他静坐。A 项"去找护士"是之后才去的医务室；B 项"远处喊陈老师"与"waved to Mr. Chen"不符；D 项"独自抬去医务室"与"two students to help carry"矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "When Xiao Ming got hurt, Li Wei was the first to help. He stayed calm, acted quickly, and took care of his classmate."

**【解析】** 陈老师表扬李伟的原因是他最先帮助、保持冷静、行动迅速并照顾了同学。A 项"跳远最好"、B 项"认识小明很久"和 C 项"全班最高"均非表扬理由。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 全文：第③段"Li Wei did not hesitate""ran over... at once""comfort him"；第④段"volunteered to go""stayed with him until... fine"；第⑤段陈老师的评价。

**【解析】** 李伟毫不犹豫地帮助、主动陪去医务室、一直陪伴至确认无碍，可推断他是关心他人、乐于助人的。A 项"常受伤"无依据；B 项"想炫耀"与"felt a little shy"及默默照顾的细节不符；C 项"更关心体育"与助人行为矛盾。故选 D。
