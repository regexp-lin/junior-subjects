# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Look around you. How many plastic things can you see? Plastic bags, plastic bottles, plastic boxes... Plastic is everywhere in our daily life. It is cheap and easy to use, so people love it. However, plastic is causing serious problems for our planet.

② Every year, about 8 million tons of plastic waste ends up in the ocean. Sea animals like turtles and fish often mistake plastic for food and eat it. This can make them sick or even kill them. Scientists have found tiny pieces of plastic inside fish that people eat. This means plastic pollution may also affect human health.

③ Another problem is that plastic takes a very long time to break down. A plastic bag may take 20 to 1,000 years to disappear completely. This means that almost every piece of plastic ever made still exists somewhere on Earth. It fills up landfills, pollutes rivers, and harms wildlife.

④ So what can we do? Actually, small changes in our daily life can make a big difference. We can bring our own shopping bags instead of using plastic ones. We can carry a reusable water bottle instead of buying plastic bottles. We can also say "no" to plastic straws when we order drinks. Some schools have already started "plastic-free" programs, and students are learning to reduce, reuse, and recycle.

⑤ Protecting the environment is everyone's responsibility. We may not be able to solve the plastic problem overnight, but every small step counts. As the saying goes, "We do not inherit the Earth from our parents — we borrow it from our children."

---

**注释：** turtle 海龟；landfill 垃圾填埋场；straw 吸管；reusable 可重复使用的；inherit 继承

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ②, how much plastic waste goes into the ocean each year?
   A. About 8 million tons.
   B. About 80 million tons.
   C. About 800 tons.
   D. About 8 thousand tons.

2. How long may it take for a plastic bag to break down completely?
   A. 2 to 10 years.
   B. 10 to 100 years.
   C. 20 to 1,000 years.
   D. 100 to 10,000 years.

3. Which of the following is mentioned as a way to reduce plastic use?
   A. Stop drinking water outside.
   B. Use a reusable water bottle.
   C. Never go shopping at stores.
   D. Buy more expensive products.

4. The underlined sentence "every small step counts" in Paragraph ⑤ means ________.
   A. we should count how many steps we take
   B. we need to walk more to protect the environment
   C. small steps are not enough to solve the problem
   D. even small actions are important and helpful

5. What is the writer trying to tell us in this passage?
   A. Plastic is the most useful material in the world.
   B. We should take action to reduce plastic pollution.
   C. Scientists have already solved the plastic problem.
   D. Only schools can help protect the environment.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "Every year, about 8 million tons of plastic waste ends up in the ocean."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出每年约有800万吨（8 million tons）塑料垃圾进入海洋。A 项与原文数据完全一致。B 项"About 80 million tons"（8000万吨）数据错误，比原文多了一个数量级；C 项"About 800 tons"（800吨）数据过小；D 项"About 8 thousand tons"（8000吨）同样远低于原文数据。故选 A。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "A plastic bag may take 20 to 1,000 years to disappear completely."

**【解析】** 原文明确说一个塑料袋可能需要20到1000年才能完全分解消失。C 项"20 to 1,000 years"与原文完全吻合。A 项"2 to 10 years"时间过短；B 项"10 to 100 years"的范围与原文不符；D 项"100 to 10,000 years"的范围偏离原文数据。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3句 "We can carry a reusable water bottle instead of buying plastic bottles."

**【解析】** 原文列举了减少塑料使用的具体方法，其中包括"携带可重复使用的水瓶，而不是购买塑料瓶"。B 项"Use a reusable water bottle"与原文对应。A 项"在外面不喝水"属于过度推断——原文说的是换用可重复水瓶，而非不喝水；C 项"不去商店购物"同样过度推断——原文建议自带购物袋，而非不购物；D 项"买更贵的产品"原文完全未提及。故选 B。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2句 "We may not be able to solve the plastic problem overnight, but every small step counts."

**【解析】** 根据上下文语境，前半句说"我们可能无法一夜之间解决塑料问题"，but 表示转折，后半句应传达积极的、与前半句对比的含义。结合第④段列举的各种"小改变"（自带购物袋、用可重复水瓶、拒绝塑料吸管），可知"every small step counts"的意思是"每一个小行动都很重要、有帮助"。D 项"即使是小行动也是重要且有帮助的"最准确。A 项将"counts"理解为"计数"，是对该词的字面误读；B 项"需要多走路来保护环境"将"step"误解为字面意义的"步行"；C 项"小步骤不足以解决问题"与转折语气传达的积极含义相矛盾。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 遇到含比喻或习语的词义猜测题，要特别注意上下文的逻辑关系。本题中的 but 转折词是关键线索——前面说"无法一夜解决"，转折后应传达"但小行动仍然重要"的积极信息。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文结构：第①段引出塑料问题 → 第②段塑料对海洋和健康的危害 → 第③段塑料难以分解 → 第④段提出减少塑料使用的具体做法 → 第⑤段总结呼吁行动。

**【解析】** 全文围绕塑料污染展开：先阐述塑料造成的环境和健康危害，再提出减少塑料使用的具体方法，最后呼吁每个人承担环保责任并付诸行动。作者的核心目的是让读者认识到塑料污染的严重性，并呼吁采取行动减少塑料使用。B 项"我们应该采取行动减少塑料污染"最准确地概括了写作意图。A 项"塑料是世界上最有用的材料"只提到了塑料的一个方面，且文章主旨是强调其危害而非用途；C 项"科学家已经解决了塑料问题"与原文"We may not be able to solve the plastic problem overnight"矛盾；D 项"只有学校才能帮助保护环境"以偏概全——学校只是文中提到的一个参与者，原文强调的是"everyone's responsibility"。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 议论文的主旨大意通常体现在首尾段的总结性语句中。本文第①段末句提出问题，第⑤段发出呼吁，两段结合即可锁定主旨。注意主旨概括应涵盖全文而非某一段落。
