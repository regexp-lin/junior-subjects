> **03-07 第1篇**

# 阅读理解 第1篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **Sunshine Middle School — Reading Week**

**April 14–18, 2026**

Dear students,

We are excited to announce our first Reading Week! Join us for a week of fun activities to discover the joy of reading.

② **Activities:**

| Day | Activity | Time | Place |
|-----|----------|------|-------|
| Monday | Book Exchange | 12:00–1:00 pm | School Library |
| Tuesday | Story Writing Contest | 3:30–5:00 pm | Room 302 |
| Wednesday | "Meet the Author" Talk | 4:00–5:30 pm | School Hall |
| Thursday | Reading Corner (bring your favorite book!) | 12:00–1:00 pm | School Garden |
| Friday | Awards Ceremony | 4:00–5:00 pm | School Hall |

③ **Reading Challenge:**
- Read at least **2 books** during Reading Week
- Write a short book review (100–150 words) for each book
- Hand in your reviews to your English teacher by **Friday morning**
- The top 10 reviews will win prizes!

④ **Prizes:**
- 1st place: A set of Harry Potter books
- 2nd–3rd place: A ¥100 bookshop gift card
- 4th–10th place: A beautiful notebook

⑤ For more information, please visit the school library or ask your English teacher. Let's read together and make this the best week of the year!

— School Library Committee

---

### 题目

1. When will the Story Writing Contest take place?
   A. Monday at noon.
   B. Tuesday afternoon.
   C. Wednesday afternoon.
   D. Friday afternoon.

2. If you want to exchange books with others, where should you go?
   A. Room 302.
   B. School Hall.
   C. School Garden.
   D. School Library.

3. What will the 1st-place winner of the Reading Challenge get?
   A. A set of Harry Potter books.
   B. A ¥100 bookshop gift card.
   C. A beautiful notebook.
   D. Two free movie tickets.

4. We can infer from the passage that ________.
   A. only English teachers can join Reading Week activities
   B. students must buy new books for the book exchange
   C. the school wants to encourage students to read more
   D. every student who joins will receive a prize

5. Where would you most probably see this passage?
   A. In a science magazine.
   B. On a school notice board.
   C. In a travel guidebook.
   D. In a cooking book.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段活动表格中 Tuesday 一行："Story Writing Contest | 3:30–5:00 pm | Room 302"

**【解析】** 根据表格信息，Story Writing Contest（故事写作比赛）安排在周二下午3:30至5:00。B 项"Tuesday afternoon"与原文一致。A 项"Monday at noon"是周一的 Book Exchange 时间，属于张冠李戴；C 项"Wednesday afternoon"是"Meet the Author" Talk 的时间；D 项"Friday afternoon"是 Awards Ceremony 的时间。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段活动表格中 Monday 一行："Book Exchange | 12:00–1:00 pm | School Library"

**【解析】** 原文表格明确标注 Book Exchange（图书交换）活动的地点是 School Library（学校图书馆）。D 项与原文完全对应。A 项 Room 302 是写作比赛的地点；B 项 School Hall 是"Meet the Author" Talk 和 Awards Ceremony 的地点；C 项 School Garden 是周四 Reading Corner 的地点。这三个选项都属于张冠李戴。故选 D。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1条 "1st place: A set of Harry Potter books"

**【解析】** 原文清楚列出第一名的奖品是一套哈利·波特丛书。A 项与原文完全一致。B 项"¥100 书店礼品卡"是第2–3名的奖品；C 项"一本精美笔记本"是第4–10名的奖品；D 项"两张免费电影票"原文未提及，属于无中生有。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第①段 "Join us for a week of fun activities to discover the joy of reading." 及第③段的 Reading Challenge 要求。

**【解析】** 学校专门组织了为期一周的读书活动，包括图书交换、作家见面会、阅读挑战等丰富活动，还设置了奖品来激励学生。这些都说明学校希望通过 Reading Week 鼓励学生多读书。C 项"学校想鼓励学生多读书"是对全文信息的合理推断。A 项"只有英语老师可以参加"与原文矛盾——活动面向全体学生（Dear students）；B 项"学生必须为图书交换购买新书"原文未提及需要买新书，exchange 本身就是用旧书交换；D 项"每个参加的学生都会获奖"与原文"The top 10 reviews will win prizes"矛盾。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文整体结构：校名标题 → 活动日程表 → 参与规则 → 奖品信息 → 联系方式，末尾署名"School Library Committee"。

**【解析】** 本文是一则学校活动通知，包含活动安排、参与方式、奖品等信息，发布方是学校图书馆委员会，目的是通知学生参加 Reading Week。这类通知最可能出现在学校公告栏上。B 项"On a school notice board"最合理。A 项"科学杂志"的内容以科普为主，与读书活动通知不符；C 项"旅游指南"与校园活动无关；D 项"烹饪书"更是与主题毫无关联。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 判断应用文出处时，关注文章的发布主体（署名）和内容类型。校园通知类文章通常出现在学校公告栏、校报或学校网站上。
