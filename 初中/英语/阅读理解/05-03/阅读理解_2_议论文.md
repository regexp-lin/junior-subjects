# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In recent years, more and more middle schools have started to offer cooking classes to their students. Some parents support this idea warmly, while others think cooking is a waste of time and schools should focus only on academic (学术的) subjects like math and science. In my opinion, cooking classes are extremely necessary and should be compulsory (必修的) for all middle school students.

② A major reason why cooking classes are important is that they teach an essential life skill. Every person, no matter how clever they are at math or science, needs to eat to survive. If teenagers leave home for university in the future and don't know how to boil an egg or cook a simple meal, they will have to rely on fast food or expensive restaurants. Knowing how to cook ensures that they can feed themselves healthily and affordably.

③ Furthermore, learning to cook can actually improve students' understanding of other school subjects. Cooking is very much like a science experiment (实验). When making a cake, students must measure the ingredients (配料) accurately using math skills. They also learn about chemical reactions when they see how baking powder makes bread rise, or how heat changes a raw egg into a solid one. Cooking brings dry textbook knowledge into real, practical life.

④ Finally, cooking is a great way to relieve (缓解) stress. After a long day of hard studying, chopping vegetables or stirring a pot of soup can help students relax. The feeling of producing a delicious meal from raw materials gives teenagers a strong sense of achievement (成就感) and builds up their confidence.

⑤ In conclusion, cooking is not a waste of time. It teaches survival skills, connects with academic knowledge, and helps reduce stress. Therefore, every middle school should welcome cooking classes into their timetable. 

**注释：** academic 学术的；compulsory 必修的；essential 基本的/必不可少的；ingredient 配料；reaction 反应；relieve 缓解；achievement 成就

### 选择题

1. What is the debate (辩论) about in paragraph ①?
A. Whether students should eat fast food for lunch.
B. Whether cooking classes should be offered in middle schools.
C. Whether math is more important than science.
D. Whether teachers should cook for students every day.

2. In the writer's opinion, what is the major reason for teaching cooking?
A. It helps students become professional chefs in the future.
B. It is an essential life skill that allows students to feed themselves healthily and affordably.
C. It allows parents to stop cooking for their children immediately.
D. It is much easier to learn than boiling an egg.

3. How does cooking help students with their math skills?
A. By asking them to count all the small beans in a jar.
B. By forcing them to recite the multiplication table (乘法表) while cooking.
C. By measuring the ingredients accurately when making things like a cake.
D. By calculating how much money they save from not going to expensive restaurants.

4. What chemical reaction example is mentioned in paragraph ③?
A. Baking powder making bread rise and heat changing a raw egg into a solid one.
B. Sugar turning into salt when added to hot water.
C. Ice melting into water under the sun.
D. Vegetables changing colors when they are chopped into small pieces.

5. How does cooking make teenagers feel at the end of a hard studying day?
A. It makes them feel even more tired and stressed.
B. It makes them angry because it takes up their free time.
C. It gives them a strong sense of achievement, builds confidence, and helps them relax.
D. It makes them feel extremely hungry and impatient.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解与主旨定位

**【定位】** 第①段第1-2句 "...more and more middle schools have started to offer cooking classes... Some parents support this idea warmly, while others think cooking is a waste of time..."

**【解析】** 开篇提到的争论核心就是：越来越多的初中开始开设烹饪课，一些家长十分支持，而另一些人则认为这是浪费时间该专注学术。因此争论的议题是关于是否应该在初中开设烹饪课（Whether cooking classes should be offered in middle schools）。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "...reason why cooking classes are important is that they teach an essential life skill... Knowing how to cook ensures that they can feed themselves healthily and affordably."

**【解析】** 作者认为最重要的理由是什么？段首点明烹饪教授了一项基本的生存技能（an essential life skill）。哪怕上大学后，自己掌握烹饪技能可以确保自己能吃得健康且实惠（healthily and affordably）。这正对应B选项。绝不是为了当厨师（A）、让父母解放（C）、或认为这只是比煮蛋更容易的事（D）。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3句 "When making a cake, students must measure the ingredients (配料) accurately using math skills."

**【解析】** 烹饪怎样帮助学生巩固数学技能呢？文中特地举了做蛋糕的例子，指出学生必须运用数学技巧去精准称量（measure）和配比原配料（ingredients）。C项完美再现了这个意思。A数豆子、B背乘法表、D算省了多少钱都是望文生义的选项。故选C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第4句 "They also learn about chemical reactions when they see how baking powder makes bread rise, or how heat changes a raw egg into a solid one."

**【解析】** 第三段提到了哪些化学反应的例子？原文说他们能看到发酵粉如何让面包发起来胀大，或者热量如何把生鸡蛋变成固态熟鸡蛋。这和A选项所罗列的内容丝毫没差。B糖变盐、C冰化水、D切菜变色均未在文中出现或发生逻辑错误。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段后面两句 "...can help students relax. The feeling of producing a delicious meal... gives teenagers a strong sense of achievement and builds up their confidence."

**【解析】** 苦学一天后做饭会让青少年的感觉如何？文末倒数第二段说切菜搅汤能帮助放松，且做出一顿美餐带来的成就感极大，能建立他们的自信心。C（给他们强烈的成就感，建立自信并有助放松）就是对此论述的最佳浓缩。A加剧疲劳、B感到愤怒、D更饿没耐心都是与文意相反的。故选C。
