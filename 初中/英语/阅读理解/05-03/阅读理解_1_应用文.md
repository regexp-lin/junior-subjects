> **05-03 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Lost & Found Notice: A Very Special Teddy Bear**

② Dear students and teachers,

③ On Monday afternoon, June 5th, I lost a small teddy bear somewhere on the school campus (校园). I am writing this notice to ask for your help because this teddy bear is extremely important to me.

④ **What does it look like?**
- It is a small, brown teddy bear, about the size of a water bottle.
- It is wearing a tiny red sweater with a yellow letter "T" on the front.
- Its left ear has a small patch (补丁) made of green cloth.

⑤ **Where might it be?**
I remember having it with me during lunch near the school cafeteria. After that, I went to the library to read for an hour, and then I played basketball on the outdoor courts. I only realized it was missing when I got on the school bus to go home. So, it could be near the cafeteria, inside the library, or around the basketball courts.

⑥ **Why is it so special?**
This bear is not a new toy you can buy from a store. It was given to me by my grandmother ten years ago when I was just a baby. She passed away last summer, and this teddy bear is my most precious (珍贵的) memory of her. 

⑦ If anyone finds it or has seen it, please contact me immediately. I will offer a cash reward (现金奖励) of $50 to the person who returns it to me.

⑧ **My Contact Information:**
- **Name:** Tom Miller (Class 8, Grade 7)
- **Phone:** 555-4321
- **Email:** *tom.m@schoolmail.com*

⑨ Thank you so much for your help!

**注释：** campus 校园；patch 补丁；cafeteria 自助餐厅；precious 珍贵的；reward 奖励；contact 联系

---

### 选择题

1. What is the main purpose of this notice?
A. To sell an old, small, brown teddy bear for $50.
B. To invite students to a basketball game on June 5th.
C. To ask for help to find a lost, very special teddy bear.
D. To tell a story about a little boy and his grandmother.

2. Which of the following is NOT a feature (特征) of the lost teddy bear?
A. It is wearing a tiny red sweater.
B. It is about the size of a water bottle.
C. Its right ear has a small yellow patch.
D. There is a yellow letter "T" on the front of its sweater.

3. Where did Tom probably lose his teddy bear according to paragraph ⑤?
A. Only on the school bus.
B. Inside a big toy store.
C. At his grandmother's house.
D. Near the cafeteria, inside the library, or around the basketball courts.

4. Why is the teddy bear extremely important to Tom?
A. Because it cost a lot of money to buy.
B. Because it is his most precious memory of his late grandmother.
C. Because he needs it to finish his school homework.
D. Because it has fifty dollars hidden inside it.

5. What will Tom do if someone returns the teddy bear to him?
A. He will give that person $50 as a cash reward.
B. He will buy them a brand new teddy bear from a store.
C. He will treat them to a big meal in the school cafeteria.
D. He will write them a long thank-you letter.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨意图推断

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "I am writing this notice to ask for your help because this teddy bear is extremely important to me."

**【解析】** 从标题《寻物启事（Lost & Found Notice）》及文中“我写这则启事是为了寻求各位的帮助，因为这只泰迪熊对我极为重要”可以直接看出，本文的主要目的是求助寻找丢失的特殊泰迪熊。A卖熊、B邀人打球、D只为讲故事均不准确。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解与判断

**【定位】** 第④段第3句 "Its left ear has a small patch (补丁) made of green cloth."

**【解析】** 题目问下列哪一项不是熊的特征。文中说它戴着红毛衣（A项是）、大约水壶大小（B项是）、胸前有个黄色的T字母（D项是）。但是关于耳朵的补丁，原大意是“左耳（left ear）有一个绿布（green cloth）打的补丁”。而C项说的是“右耳有个黄色补丁”，与事实相反。本题选NOT，故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后句 "So, it could be near the cafeteria, inside the library, or around the basketball courts."

**【解析】** 汤姆可能把熊丢在了哪儿？他理顺了自己的行程后得出结论：所以它有可能在自助餐厅附近、图书馆里面或者是篮球场周围。这直接与D选项对应。A校车上只是他发现丢失的地方，B玩具店和C奶奶家完全不在其校园活动范围内。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段第2、3句 "It was given to me by my grandmother ten years ago... She passed away last summer, and this teddy bear is my most precious (珍贵的) memory of her."

**【解析】** 这熊为什么极其重要？因为那是他十年前还是婴儿时奶奶送的，而奶奶去年过世了，这熊是他对奶奶最珍贵的回忆。B选项将这层含义完整复述。A（买得贵）、C（为了写作业）、D（里头藏了五十块钱）均为无中生有。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段第2句 "I will offer a cash reward (现金奖励) of $50 to the person who returns it to me."

**【解析】** 如果有人归还，汤姆会怎么做？通告里明确承诺，他将向归还者提供50美元的现金奖励（cash reward of $50）。A选项完全符合承诺。B买只新熊送人、C请客吃饭、D只写封长信均不符合原文所设重谢方式。故选A。
