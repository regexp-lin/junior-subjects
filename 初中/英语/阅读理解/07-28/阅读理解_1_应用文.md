# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Volunteer Service Certificate—Application Guide**

② If you took part in volunteer work during the summer holiday and would like to get a certificate (证书), please follow this guide. The certificate can help you in your future study and job applications. Applications are accepted from September 1st to September 15th each year.

③ **Step 1: Check if you are eligible (符合资格的).** You must be a middle school student. Your volunteer work should be at least 20 hours in total during the summer. The work can be done at a community center, a library, a hospital, or other approved places.

④ **Step 2: Prepare your materials.** You need to fill in an online application form. You also need a photo of yourself in volunteer clothes. Most importantly, you need a signed letter from your volunteer supervisor (负责人). The letter must include your name, the place where you volunteered, the dates, and the total hours of service.

⑤ **Step 3: Submit and wait.** Submit your application before September 15th. You will receive an email within 10 working days. If your application is successful, you can collect your certificate at the student office. For any questions, please visit our website or call 020-12345678.

**注释：** certificate 证书；eligible 符合资格的；supervisor 负责人

### 选择题

1. When can students apply for the summer volunteer certificate?
   A. From September 1st to September 15th.
   B. From August 1st to August 15th.
   C. Only on the first day of September.
   D. Anytime during the school year.

2. What is the minimum number of volunteer hours required?
   A. 10 hours.
   B. 20 hours.
   C. 15 hours.
   D. 30 hours.

3. What materials must students prepare according to the guide?
   A. Only an online form.
   B. A photo and a letter only.
   C. An online form, a photo, and a signed letter from the supervisor.
   D. A certificate from the school.

4. After submitting the application, when can students expect a reply?
   A. On the same day.
   B. Within 5 working days.
   C. Within one month.
   D. Within 10 working days.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The certificate is useful for students' future study and job applications.
   B. Students can get the certificate without doing any volunteer work.
   C. Students can only volunteer at hospitals.
   D. The application form must be handed in by post.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Applications are accepted from September 1st to September 15th each year"

**【解析】** 文中明确写明申请时间为每年9月1日至9月15日。A项正确。B项8月、C项仅9月1日、D项整个学年均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Your volunteer work should be at least 20 hours in total during the summer"

**【解析】** 志愿服务时长要求为暑假期间至少总计20小时。B项正确。A、C、D项均为错误数字。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "You need to fill in an online application form. You also need a photo of yourself in volunteer clothes. Most importantly, you need a signed letter from your volunteer supervisor"

**【解析】** 所需材料包括：在线申请表、穿志愿服的本人照片、志愿活动负责人签字的证明信。C项完整概括。A项缺材料；B项缺申请表；D项证书是申请后获得，非申请材料。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "You will receive an email within 10 working days"

**【解析】** 提交申请后10个工作日内会收到邮件回复。D项正确。A、B、C项的时间表述均与原文不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "The certificate can help you in your future study and job applications"

**【解析】** 文中指出该证书有助于学生未来升学和求职，可推知其对学生发展有实际帮助。A项正确。B项与"至少20小时志愿服务"要求矛盾；C项与"community center, library, hospital, or other approved places"矛盾；D项与"online application form"矛盾。故选A。
