# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Social Practice Program — Recruitment (招募) Notice**

② Hello, students! The school is looking for volunteers to take part in our Summer Social Practice Program. This is a great chance to learn outside the classroom and help the community. We hope you will join us!

③ **Program details:**
- **Time:** July 15 – July 25 (10 days)
- **Place:** Sunshine Community Center and nearby villages
- **Activities:** Teach children English, help elderly people, clean up parks, and visit a local farm
- **Number of students needed:** 30 (Grade 8 and Grade 9 only)

④ **Requirements:**
- You must be healthy and able to work as a team
- You need your parents' permission (许可)
- You should be willing to help others and learn new things

⑤ **How to apply:**
- Fill in the application form (you can get it from the school office or our website)
- Hand in the form to Ms. Li (Room 205) before June 20
- We will hold a short interview on June 25. Please come on time.
- Results will be posted on June 28

⑥ **What you will gain:**
- A certificate (证书) of participation
- Practical experience and new friends
- A deeper understanding of society

⑦ If you have any questions, please email us at practice@school.edu or call 8765-4321. We look forward to hearing from you!

School Social Practice Office  
May 30, 2024

**注释：** recruitment 招募；permission 许可；certificate 证书

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Summer Social Practice Program take place?
   A. From June 15 to June 25.
   B. From June 20 to June 28.
   C. From July 15 to July 25.
   D. From July 20 to July 25.

2. Which grades can apply for this program?
   A. Grade 6 and Grade 7.
   B. Grade 7 and Grade 8.
   C. Grade 8 and Grade 9.
   D. All grades.

3. What should students do if they want to join?
   A. Just send an email.
   B. Fill in the form, hand it in before June 20, and attend an interview on June 25.
   C. Call Ms. Li directly.
   D. Go to the community center on July 15.

4. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as an activity?
   A. Teach children English.
   B. Help elderly people.
   C. Cook meals for the community.
   D. Clean up parks.

5. What can students get after joining the program?
   A. A lot of money.
   B. A certificate of participation and practical experience.
   C. Free textbooks.
   D. A new computer.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Time: July 15 – July 25 (10 days)"

**【解析】** 原文明确写出活动时间为7月15日至7月25日。A项和B项为申请相关时间；D项为错误时间段。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Number of students needed: 30 (Grade 8 and Grade 9 only)"

**【解析】** 原文限定招募对象为八年级和九年级学生。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Fill in the application form... Hand in the form to Ms. Li (Room 205) before June 20... We will hold a short interview on June 25"

**【解析】** 申请流程包括填表、6月20日前交表、6月25日参加面试。A项"仅发邮件"不完整；C项"直接打电话"与流程不符；D项为活动开始日，非申请步骤。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Activities: Teach children English, help elderly people, clean up parks, and visit a local farm"

**【解析】** 所列活动包括教英语、帮助老人、清理公园和参观当地农场，未提及"为社区做饭"。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "What you will gain: A certificate of participation; Practical experience and new friends; A deeper understanding of society"

**【解析】** 原文写明可获得参与证书、实践经验和新朋友。A项"很多钱"、C项"免费教材"和D项"新电脑"均未提及。故选B。
