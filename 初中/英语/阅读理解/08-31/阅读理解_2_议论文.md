# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should students take part in housework? This question has been discussed for many years. Some parents think that children should only focus on study and leave all housework to adults. Others believe that doing chores helps students grow. Let us look at both sides.

② Those who support students doing housework say it teaches important life skills. When students wash dishes, sweep the floor, or make their beds, they learn to take care of themselves. These skills will be useful when they grow up and live alone. Housework also teaches responsibility. If a student forgets to water the plants or feed the fish, he will learn that his actions have results. Besides, sharing housework can make family life happier. When everyone helps, no one feels overworked (过度劳累).

③ However, those who disagree worry about students' time. Middle school students already have heavy homework and many exams. They may stay up late to finish their studies. Adding housework could make them more tired and affect their grades. Some parents also say that housework is not difficult — they can do it quickly themselves. Why should children waste time on something adults can do better?

④ In my opinion, students should do some housework, but not too much. A little housework — perhaps thirty minutes a day — will not hurt their studies. It can even help them relax after long hours of reading. Parents can start with simple tasks: setting the table, folding clothes, or taking out the rubbish. As students grow older, they can learn to cook simple meals or clean their own rooms. The key is balance. Study is important, but so is learning to be a responsible person.

⑤ To sum up, housework is not a waste of time. It prepares students for the future and brings families closer. Parents and children should talk together and decide what chores are suitable. A student who can both study well and help at home will be ready for whatever life brings.

**注释：** chore 家务活；overworked 过度劳累的

**Questions:**

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Those who support students doing housework say it teaches important life skills... Housework also teaches responsibility"

**【解析】** 原文明确说做家务能培养生活技能和责任感。A项"提高成绩"、C项"熬夜"、D项"作业变简单"原文均未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Middle school students already have heavy homework and many exams... Adding housework could make them more tired and affect their grades"

**【解析】** 反对者认为学生课业负担重，加做家务会加重疲劳、影响成绩。A、C、D项均非原文反对理由。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "students should do some housework, but not too much. A little housework — perhaps thirty minutes a day — will not hurt their studies"

**【解析】** 作者建议适量做家务，如每天约半小时。A项"全部做"、B项"完全不做"、D项"仅周末"与作者观点不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "A student who can both study well and help at home will be ready for whatever life brings"

**【解析】** 作者指出既会学习又能帮忙做家务的学生更能应对未来生活，可推断其认为这类学生准备更充分。A项与作者观点相反；C、D项过于绝对。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文

**【解析】** 文章围绕"学生是否应参加家务劳动"展开正反论证并给出作者看法，B项准确概括主题。A项偏重方法；C项偏离主题；D项范围过窄。故选B。
