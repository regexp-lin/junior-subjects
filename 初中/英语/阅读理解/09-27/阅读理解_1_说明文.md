# 阅读理解（说明文）

① The sun sends a huge amount of energy to the Earth every day. Solar energy is clean, free, and will not run out for billions of years. In recent years, people have found more and more ways to use this energy. Solar power is becoming an important part of our modern life.

② How does solar energy work? Solar panels (太阳能板) are made of special materials. When sunlight hits these materials, it creates electricity. The panels can be placed on the roofs of houses, on the ground, or even on some cars. The electricity they produce can power lights, TVs, computers, and many other devices. It can also be stored in batteries (电池) for use at night or on cloudy days.

③ Solar energy has many advantages. First, it does not produce any pollution. Unlike coal or oil, solar power does not release smoke or harmful gases into the air. Second, the sun is free. Once you install (安装) solar panels, you can get energy for many years with little extra cost. Third, solar energy can be used in remote (偏远的) areas where there is no power grid. In some villages in Africa and Asia, solar panels help families light their homes and charge their phones. Fourth, solar technology is improving. Panels are becoming cheaper and more efficient (高效的), so more people can afford them.

④ Of course, solar energy also has some limits. Solar panels work best when the sun is shining. On cloudy or rainy days, they produce less electricity. At night, they produce none. That is why we need batteries or other power sources to fill the gap. In addition, making solar panels needs materials and energy, and old panels need to be recycled properly. But scientists are working on these problems.

⑤ Solar energy will play a bigger role in our future. As we look for cleaner ways to power our world, the sun is one of our best friends.

**注释：** panel 板；battery 电池；install 安装；remote 偏远的；efficient 高效的

**Questions:**

1. According to the passage, what is solar energy?
   A. Energy from the wind.
   B. Energy from the sun; it is clean, free, and will not run out soon.
   C. Energy from coal.
   D. Energy from water.

2. How do solar panels produce electricity?
   A. By burning oil.
   B. When sunlight hits special materials in the panels, it creates electricity.
   C. By using batteries only.
   D. By turning water into steam.

3. Which of the following is an advantage of solar energy mentioned in the passage?
   A. It works best at night.
   B. It does not produce pollution, and the sun is free; it can also be used in remote areas.
   C. Solar panels are always very cheap.
   D. It needs no maintenance (保养) at all.

4. Why do we need batteries or other power sources when using solar energy?
   A. Because batteries are cheaper than panels.
   B. Because solar panels produce little or no electricity on cloudy days or at night.
   C. Because the sun is too strong.
   D. Because solar panels are heavy.

5. What does the writer think about the future of solar energy?
   A. Solar energy will become less important.
   B. Solar energy will play a bigger role as we look for cleaner ways to power our world.
   C. Solar panels will never be improved.
   D. The sun will stop shining soon.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The sun sends a huge amount of energy to the Earth... Solar energy is clean, free, and will not run out for billions of years"

**【解析】** 太阳能来自太阳，清洁、免费且长期不会枯竭。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "When sunlight hits these materials, it creates electricity"

**【解析】** 阳光照射面板中的特殊材料时产生电能。A、C、D 项与原理不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "it does not produce any pollution... the sun is free... solar energy can be used in remote areas"

**【解析】** 原文列举无污染、免费、可用于偏远地区等优势。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Solar panels work best when the sun is shining. On cloudy or rainy days, they produce less electricity. At night, they produce none. That is why we need batteries or other power sources"

**【解析】** 阴雨天和夜间发电少或不发电，因此需要电池或其他电源补充。A、C、D 项与原因无关。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Solar energy will play a bigger role in our future. As we look for cleaner ways to power our world, the sun is one of our best friends"

**【解析】** 作者认为太阳能在未来将更重要，是清洁能源的重要选择。A、C、D 项与作者观点相反。故选 B。
