> **09-27 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last summer, on a stormy (暴风雨的) afternoon, I was walking home when I heard a weak meow (喵) from under a parked car. I bent down and saw a small, wet cat. Its fur was dirty and it was shaking from the cold. The rain was getting heavier. I could not just leave it there.

② I took off my jacket and carefully wrapped (包) the cat. At first, it tried to run away, but it was too weak. I carried it home. My mum was surprised when she saw the cat. "Where did you find it?" she asked. I told her the story. She smiled and said, "We need to dry it and give it some food. But we cannot keep it forever — we should try to find it a home."

③ For the next few days, the cat stayed with us. We fed it, gave it a warm box to sleep in, and named it Stormy because we found it in the storm. Stormy was shy at first, but slowly it began to trust us. It would curl up (蜷缩) beside me when I did my homework. My mum took photos of Stormy and put them on a community (社区) website. "Looking for a home for a friendly cat found in the storm," she wrote. Many people asked about Stormy, but we wanted to find the best home.

④ One week later, an elderly woman called. She lived alone and had just lost her old cat. She said she had a quiet home and would love to give Stormy a good life. We visited her house. It was clean and warm, with a sunny window and a soft bed for the cat. Stormy walked around, smelled the air, and then jumped onto the woman's lap. The woman's eyes lit up. "It feels like my old friend has come back," she said.

⑤ We said goodbye to Stormy that day. I was a bit sad, but I knew Stormy had found a real home. Sometimes the best thing we can do is not to keep what we find, but to help it find where it belongs.

**注释：** stormy 暴风雨的；meow 喵；wrap 包；curl up 蜷缩；community 社区

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did the writer find the cat?
   A. In a pet shop.
   B. Under a parked car on a stormy afternoon.
   C. At school.
   D. In a park.

2. What did the writer do when he first saw the cat?
   A. He left it alone and went home.
   B. He wrapped it with his jacket and carried it home.
   C. He called the police.
   D. He gave it to a stranger.

3. Why did the writer's mum put photos of Stormy on a community website?
   A. To show off the cat.
   B. To find a new home for the cat.
   C. To sell the cat.
   D. To look for the cat's first owner.

4. Why did the writer and his mum choose the elderly woman as Stormy's new owner?
   A. Because she paid the most money.
   B. Because she lived alone, had a quiet home, lost her old cat, and Stormy seemed to like her.
   C. Because she lived nearby.
   D. Because she was a vet (兽医).

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Cats are better than dogs.
   B. Sometimes the best thing is to help something find where it belongs, not to keep it for oneself.
   C. Storms are dangerous.
   D. Old women need cats.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I heard a weak meow from under a parked car... I bent down and saw a small, wet cat... The rain was getting heavier"

**【解析】** 作者在暴风雨天的停车场车底下发现这只猫。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "I took off my jacket and carefully wrapped the cat... I carried it home"

**【解析】** 作者用外套包住猫并带回家。A、C、D 项与原文行为不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "My mum took photos of Stormy and put them on a community website. 'Looking for a home for a friendly cat found in the storm,' she wrote"

**【解析】** 母亲发帖是为了给猫找一个新家。A、C、D 项与目的不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "an elderly woman... lived alone... had just lost her old cat... had a quiet home... Stormy walked around... jumped onto the woman's lap"

**【解析】** 老人独居、刚失去老猫、家有安静环境，且 Stormy 亲近她，因此被选为新主人。A、C、D 项原文未提及。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Sometimes the best thing we can do is not to keep what we find, but to help it find where it belongs"

**【解析】** 作者领悟到最好的做法是帮它找到归属，而非据为己有。A、C、D 项与主题无关。故选 B。
