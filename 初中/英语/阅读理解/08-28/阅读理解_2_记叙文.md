> **08-28 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① My grandfather lives in the countryside. Last summer, I spent two weeks with him. Grandfather has a small vegetable garden behind his house. He grows tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots, and green beans. I had never grown anything before, but Grandfather said he would teach me.

② Every morning, we woke up early and went to the garden. Grandfather showed me how to water the plants. "Not too much, not too little," he said. "Plants are like people — they need the right amount of care." We also pulled out weeds (杂草) and removed pests (害虫) from the leaves. At first, I thought it was hard work and my back ached. But Grandfather told me that farming had been his job for many years, and he still loved it.

③ One day, Grandfather gave me a small patch (小块地) of my own. He said I could grow whatever I wanted. I chose to grow sunflowers (向日葵) because they are bright and cheerful. I planted the seeds, watered them every day, and waited. Grandfather watched and gave me tips. "Be patient," he said. "Good things take time."

④ After two weeks, my sunflowers had not flowered yet, but they had grown taller. I had to go back to the city for school. Grandfather promised to look after my sunflowers and send me photos. A month later, he sent a picture: my sunflowers were blooming (开花)! They were so beautiful. I felt proud that I had grown them with my own hands.

⑤ That summer, I learned more than just how to grow vegetables. I learned about patience, hard work, and spending time with family. Grandfather's garden is not just a place to grow food — it is a place where we grow love and memories. I cannot wait to visit him again next summer.

**注释：** weed 杂草；pest 害虫；patch 小块地；sunflower 向日葵；bloom 开花

---

**Questions:**

1. Where does the writer's grandfather live?
   A. In a big city.
   B. In the countryside.
   C. In a foreign country.
   D. In an apartment building.

2. What did Grandfather teach the writer to do in the garden?
   A. Only to water the plants.
   B. To water plants, pull weeds, and remove pests.
   C. To cook vegetables.
   D. To sell vegetables.

3. What did the writer choose to grow in his own patch?
   A. Tomatoes.
   B. Sunflowers.
   C. Carrots.
   D. Green beans.

4. When did the writer see his sunflowers blooming?
   A. After two weeks at Grandfather's house.
   B. When he was still in the countryside.
   C. A month later, in a photo Grandfather sent.
   D. He never saw them bloom.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Farming is easy.
   B. Patience, hard work, and spending time with family.
   C. Sunflowers grow in one day.
   D. The countryside is boring.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "My grandfather lives in the countryside"

**【解析】** 原文明确说爷爷住在乡下。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Grandfather showed me how to water the plants... We also pulled out weeds and removed pests from the leaves"

**【解析】** 原文说明爷爷教作者浇水、拔草、除虫。A项不完整；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I chose to grow sunflowers because they are bright and cheerful"

**【解析】** 原文明确说作者选择种向日葵。A、C、D项为爷爷花园中已有的蔬菜。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "I had to go back to the city for school... A month later, he sent a picture: my sunflowers were blooming!"

**【解析】** 作者回城上学，一个月后爷爷发来照片，向日葵已开花。A、B、D项与时间线不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I learned about patience, hard work, and spending time with family"

**【解析】** 作者总结自己学会了耐心、勤劳和与家人共度时光。A、C、D项与作者感悟不符。故选B。
