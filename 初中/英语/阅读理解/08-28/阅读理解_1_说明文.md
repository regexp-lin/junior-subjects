# 阅读理解（说明文）

① 3D printing (3D打印) is a technology that creates real objects from digital (数字的) designs. Instead of cutting or shaping material by hand, a 3D printer builds things layer by layer. It is like stacking (堆叠) very thin slices of material on top of each other until the object is complete. This technology has become more and more popular in recent years.

② How does it work? First, you need a 3D model — a design made on a computer. You can create it yourself with design software (软件) or download it from the Internet. Then, you send the design to the printer. The printer heats a special material, usually plastic, and squeezes (挤出) it out through a small nozzle (喷嘴). It moves back and forth, building one thin layer at a time. After several hours or even days, depending on the size, the object is finished.

③ 3D printing has many uses. In medicine, doctors use it to make custom (定制的) tools and even models of patients' bones before surgery. In schools, students use 3D printers to build prototypes (原型) for science projects. In daily life, people can print toys, phone cases, and spare parts (零件) for broken things at home. Some companies also use 3D printing to make cars and airplane parts.

④ The advantages of 3D printing include flexibility (灵活性). You can make almost any shape you can imagine. It also reduces waste — you only use the material you need. However, 3D printing has limits. Large objects take a long time to make. The materials and printers can be expensive. Not everything can be printed; some objects still need traditional methods.

⑤ In the future, 3D printing may become as common as ink printers. Perhaps one day we will print our own clothes, food, or even houses! For now, it is an exciting technology that is changing the way we make things.

**注释：** digital 数字的；stack 堆叠；squeeze 挤出；nozzle 喷嘴；custom 定制的；prototype 原型；spare part 零件；flexibility 灵活性

**Questions:**

1. What is 3D printing according to the passage?
   A. A way to print documents on paper.
   B. A technology that creates real objects from digital designs by building layer by layer.
   C. A type of drawing software.
   D. A machine that cuts wood.

2. What material does a 3D printer usually use?
   A. Paper.
   B. Metal only.
   C. Plastic.
   D. Wood.

3. Which is NOT a use of 3D printing mentioned in the passage?
   A. Making models for surgery in medicine.
   B. Building prototypes for science projects in schools.
   C. Printing documents for the office.
   D. Making toys and spare parts at home.

4. What is an advantage of 3D printing?
   A. It is always very fast.
   B. It can make almost any shape and reduces waste.
   C. It costs nothing.
   D. It works without electricity.

5. What does the writer think about the future of 3D printing?
   A. It will disappear soon.
   B. It may become as common as ink printers and change how we make things.
   C. It will only be used in hospitals.
   D. It is too difficult for ordinary people.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "3D printing is a technology that creates real objects from digital designs... builds things layer by layer"

**【解析】** 原文定义3D打印为由数字设计逐层制作实体物的技术。A、C、D项与3D打印概念不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The printer heats a special material, usually plastic"

**【解析】** 原文明确说常用材料为塑料。A、B、D项均非典型3D打印材料。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段列举：医学手术模型、学校科学项目原型、家居玩具和零件

**【解析】** A、B、D项均为文中提到的用途。C项"打印办公室文件"为普通打印机用途，非3D打印。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The advantages... include flexibility. You can make almost any shape... It also reduces waste"

**【解析】** 原文指出3D打印的优点是可制作各种形状且减少浪费。A、C、D项与原文描述矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "3D printing may become as common as ink printers... it is an exciting technology that is changing the way we make things"

**【解析】** 作者认为3D打印可能像喷墨打印机一样普及，并正在改变制造方式。A、C、D项与作者观点不符。故选B。
