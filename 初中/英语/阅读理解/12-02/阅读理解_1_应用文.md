# 阅读理解（应用文）

**City Museum — Visitor Guide**

① Welcome to the City Museum! To help you enjoy your visit, please read the following information.

**Opening Hours & Tickets:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Opening days | Tuesday – Sunday (closed on Mondays) |
| Opening hours | 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. (last entry 4:30 p.m.) |
| Ticket price | Adults: 30 yuan; Students (with ID): 15 yuan; Children under 6: Free |
| Group (10+ people) | 20% off — book at least 2 days ahead |

② **Rules for visitors:**
- No food or drinks inside the exhibition halls. You may eat in the café on the first floor.
- Do not touch the exhibits. Some are very old and fragile.
- No flash photography. You may take photos without flash.
- Keep your voice down. Other visitors are here to enjoy the quiet atmosphere.
- Bags larger than 40 cm must be left at the cloakroom near the entrance.

③ **Floor plan:**
- **1F:** Ticket office, café, gift shop, toilets
- **2F:** History exhibition (Ancient China)
- **3F:** Art exhibition (Paintings & Calligraphy)
- **4F:** Science corner (For children)

④ **How to get here:**
- By bus: No. 15, 28, 103 — get off at "City Museum" stop
- By Metro: Line 2, exit C
- By car: Parking available (10 yuan for 2 hours). Spaces are limited at weekends.

⑤ **Special offer:** Show your student card at the ticket office and get a free guide map. For more information, visit www.citymuseum.gov.cn or call 020-87654321.

**Questions:**

1. When is the museum closed?
   A. On Sundays.
   B. On Mondays.
   C. On Tuesdays.
   D. Every day.

2. How much does a student pay for a ticket?
   A. 30 yuan.
   B. 15 yuan (with student ID).
   C. Free.
   D. 20 yuan.

3. What are visitors not allowed to do inside the exhibition halls?
   A. Take photos without flash.
   B. Eat or drink.
   C. Walk quietly.
   D. Look at exhibits.

4. Where can visitors find the History exhibition?
   A. On the first floor.
   B. On the second floor.
   C. On the third floor.
   D. On the fourth floor.

5. How can a student get a free guide map?
   A. By buying a ticket online.
   B. By showing the student card at the ticket office.
   C. By visiting the café.
   D. By parking the car.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Opening days" 一行 "Tuesday – Sunday (closed on Mondays)"

**【解析】** 博物馆周一闭馆。A项周日开馆；C项周二开馆；D项每天闭馆与原文矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Students (with ID): 15 yuan"

**【解析】** 持学生证可享15元票价。A项30元为成人价；C项免费为6岁以下儿童；D项20元无依据。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "No food or drinks inside the exhibition halls."

**【解析】** 展厅内禁止饮食。A项无闪光拍照是允许的；C项安静行走、D项观看展品均为允许行为。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "2F: History exhibition (Ancient China)"

**【解析】** 历史展览在二楼。A项一楼是售票处、 café 等；C项三楼是艺术展；D项四楼是科学角。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Show your student card at the ticket office and get a free guide map."

**【解析】** 在售票处出示学生证可免费领取导览图。A项网上购票、C项去 café、D项停车均非领取方式。故选B。
