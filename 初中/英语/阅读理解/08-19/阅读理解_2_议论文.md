# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Every evening, many Chinese middle school students sit at their desks for hours doing homework. Some students do not finish until 10 or 11 o'clock at night. Parents and teachers often argue about whether students have too much homework. Is all this homework really necessary?

② Those who support a heavy homework load have their reasons. First, practice makes perfect. The more students practice, the more skilled they become. Math problems, English grammar exercises, and Chinese writing all improve with repetition (重复). Second, homework helps students review what they learned in class. Without it, they may quickly forget the knowledge. Third, doing homework teaches students self-discipline (自律). It helps them learn to manage time and complete tasks on their own.

③ On the other hand, many people believe that too much homework does more harm than good. When students spend the whole evening on homework, they have no time for sports, hobbies, or family activities. This can affect their physical health and happiness. Some students become anxious (焦虑的) or even hate studying because of the heavy workload. Besides, if homework is too much, students may simply copy from classmates or the Internet instead of thinking by themselves. In that case, homework loses its real purpose.

④ Research also shows that the quality of homework matters more than the quantity (数量). A few well-designed exercises can be more helpful than pages of repetitive tasks. For example, one creative writing task may teach a student more about English than fifty fill-in-the-blank questions. Teachers should think carefully about what kind of homework truly helps students learn.

⑤ In conclusion, homework is important, but it should not take up all of a student's free time. A reasonable amount of meaningful homework can help students grow. But too much homework, especially boring and repetitive work, may do the opposite. The goal should be to make homework a tool for learning, not a burden (负担) for students.

**注释：** repetition 重复；self-discipline 自律；anxious 焦虑的；quantity 数量；burden 负担

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why is homework considered helpful?
   A. It gives students more free time.
   B. It helps students review and practice.
   C. It allows students to play more games.
   D. It makes teachers' jobs easier.

2. What problem may too much homework cause?
   A. Students become better at sports.
   B. Students have more time for hobbies.
   C. Students may copy answers instead of thinking.
   D. Students learn to manage time well.

3. What does the word "quantity" in Paragraph ④ probably mean?
   A. Quality.
   B. Type.
   C. Amount.
   D. Difficulty.

4. What does the writer think about homework?
   A. All homework should be cancelled.
   B. Homework should be meaningful and not too much.
   C. Students should do homework for at least five hours a day.
   D. Only math homework is useful.

5. What is the best title for this passage?
   A. Why Students Love Homework
   B. How to Finish Homework Faster
   C. Homework: How Much Is Too Much?
   D. The Best Homework in the World

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "practice makes perfect" 及 "homework helps students review what they learned in class."

**【解析】** 原文指出作业帮助学生复习和练习。A项"给学生更多自由时间"与原文不符；C项"玩更多游戏"属于无中生有；D项"使老师的工作更轻松"原文未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students may simply copy from classmates or the Internet instead of thinking by themselves."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出作业过多会导致学生抄袭而非自己思考。A项"体育更好"与原文"affect their physical health"相反；B项"更多时间给爱好"与"no time for sports, hobbies"矛盾；D项"学会管理时间"是作业支持者的观点，不是作业过多的问题。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "the quality of homework matters more than the quantity"

**【解析】** 此句将quality（质量）与quantity对比，结合后文"A few well-designed exercises can be more helpful than pages of repetitive tasks"，可判断quantity指的是"数量"。A项"质量"与quality重复，不合逻辑；B项"类型"和D项"难度"不符合对比关系。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "A reasonable amount of meaningful homework can help students grow. But too much homework...may do the opposite."

**【解析】** 作者认为作业应该适量且有意义。A项"所有作业都应取消"过于极端；C项"每天至少做5小时"与作者观点相反；D项"只有数学作业有用"属于无中生有。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文

**【解析】** 文章围绕"作业是否过多"展开正反讨论，C项"作业：多少才算太多？"最能概括全文主旨。A项"学生为什么喜欢作业"与文意不符；B项"如何更快完成作业"不是文章讨论的内容；D项"世界上最好的作业"属于无中生有。故选C。
