> **08-19 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Community Recycling Day — Let's Go Green Together!**

② Dear residents (居民),

Our neighborhood committee (居委会) will hold a Community Recycling Day on **Saturday, September 20, 2026**, from **9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.** at **Sunshine Community Square**. Everyone is welcome to take part!

③ **What Can You Bring?**

| Accepted Items | Not Accepted |
|---------------|-------------|
| Old newspapers and magazines | Food waste |
| Plastic bottles and cans | Broken glass |
| Old clothes and shoes (clean) | Batteries and electronics |
| Used books and textbooks | Paint and chemicals |

④ **How It Works:**
- Bring your recyclable items to the collection point at the square
- Volunteers will help you sort (分类) the items
- Each family will receive **1 point** for every kilogram of items donated
- Points can be exchanged for small gifts at the reward table

⑤ **Gifts Available:**

| Points | Gift |
|--------|------|
| 5 points | Eco-friendly shopping bag |
| 10 points | Potted plant (盆栽) |
| 20 points | Stainless steel water bottle |

⑥ **Volunteers Needed!**
We need 20 student volunteers to help on that day. Volunteers will work from 8:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Each volunteer will receive a certificate (证书) and 10 bonus points. If you are interested, please sign up at the community office before September 15.

⑦ Let's work together to reduce waste and protect our environment. See you there!

**注释：** resident 居民；committee 委员会；recyclable 可回收的；sort 分类；stainless steel 不锈钢

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Community Recycling Day be held?
   A. September 15.
   B. September 20.
   C. September 25.
   D. October 1.

2. Which of the following can people bring to the recycling event?
   A. Broken glass.
   B. Used batteries.
   C. Old newspapers.
   D. Food waste.

3. How many points does a family get for donating 3 kilograms of items?
   A. 1 point.
   B. 3 points.
   C. 5 points.
   D. 10 points.

4. What will student volunteers receive?
   A. A free lunch and a T-shirt.
   B. A certificate and 10 bonus points.
   C. A water bottle and a shopping bag.
   D. Money and a potted plant.

5. What is the purpose of this notice?
   A. To sell recycled products to residents.
   B. To invite residents to join a recycling event and volunteer.
   C. To teach people how to sort garbage at home.
   D. To announce the opening of a new community office.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Saturday, September 20, 2026"

**【解析】** 原文明确标注活动日期为9月20日。A项"9月15日"是志愿者报名截止日；C项"9月25日"和D项"10月1日"原文未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Accepted Items" 列 "Old newspapers and magazines"

**【解析】** 表格"可接受物品"一栏列出了旧报纸和杂志。A项"碎玻璃"、B项"旧电池"和D项"食物残渣"均在"不接受"一栏中。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Each family will receive 1 point for every kilogram of items donated"

**【解析】** 每公斤1分，3公斤即为3分。A项"1分"只是1公斤的积分；C项"5分"和D项"10分"数字不对。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Each volunteer will receive a certificate and 10 bonus points."

**【解析】** 原文明确说志愿者将获得证书和10个额外积分。A项"免费午餐和T恤"、C项"水杯和购物袋"和D项"钱和盆栽"原文均未提及作为志愿者奖励。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第②段 "Everyone is welcome to take part!" 及第⑥段志愿者招募

**【解析】** 这则通知的目的是邀请居民参加社区回收活动并招募志愿者。A项"出售回收产品"属于无中生有；C项"教人在家分类"虽有涉及但不是主要目的；D项"宣布新社区办公室开业"完全无关。故选B。
