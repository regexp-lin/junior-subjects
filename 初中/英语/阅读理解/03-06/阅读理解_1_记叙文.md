> **03-06 第1篇**

# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Wang Wei was a quiet boy. He loved music and could play the guitar very well, but he was too shy to play in front of others. Whenever someone asked him to perform, his face would turn red and his hands would shake. He always said, "No, I can't do it."

② One day, his music teacher, Ms. Chen, told the class about the school talent show. "I think Wang Wei should enter the competition," she said with a smile. The whole class agreed and clapped their hands. Wang Wei wanted to say no, but when he saw the encouraging looks on his classmates' faces, he decided to give it a try.

③ For the next three weeks, Wang Wei practiced every day after school. He played his guitar in front of the mirror, then in front of his parents, and finally in front of a few close friends. Each time, he felt a little less nervous. His mother told him, "Being brave doesn't mean you're not afraid. It means you keep going even when you are."

④ The night of the talent show arrived. Wang Wei stood behind the stage curtain, and his heart was beating so fast that he could hear it. When his name was called, he walked onto the stage with shaking legs. The bright lights made him want to run away. But then he saw Ms. Chen sitting in the front row, smiling and nodding at him. He took a deep breath, closed his eyes, and began to play.

⑤ The music flowed from his fingers like a gentle river. When he finished, the hall was silent for a moment — and then everyone started clapping and cheering. Wang Wei opened his eyes and smiled. For the first time, he understood that the thing he feared most could also bring him the greatest joy.

---

**注释：** talent show 才艺表演；curtain 幕布

---

### 题目

1. What was Wang Wei's problem?
   A. He couldn't play any musical instrument.
   B. He was too shy to perform in front of others.
   C. He didn't get along with his classmates.
   D. He didn't like music at all.

2. Why did Wang Wei decide to enter the talent show?
   A. Because Ms. Chen forced him to do so.
   B. Because he wanted to win a big prize.
   C. Because he was encouraged by his teacher and classmates.
   D. Because his parents asked him to.

3. What did Wang Wei's mother mean by her words in Paragraph ③?
   A. Wang Wei should stop practicing guitar.
   B. Playing guitar in front of others is easy.
   C. Wang Wei should not feel afraid at all.
   D. True courage is acting even when you are afraid.

4. The underlined phrase "flowed from his fingers like a gentle river" in Paragraph ⑤ means ________.
   A. the music was loud and exciting
   B. the music sounded smooth and beautiful
   C. Wang Wei played very fast on stage
   D. something was wrong with the guitar

5. What can we learn from Wang Wei's story?
   A. Facing our fears can bring us unexpected happiness.
   B. Music is the most important subject at school.
   C. Teachers should not push students to try new things.
   D. Only talented people can perform on stage.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "He loved music and could play the guitar very well, but he was too shy to play in front of others."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出 Wang Wei 虽然吉他弹得很好，但他太害羞了，不敢在别人面前演奏。B 项"他太害羞而不敢在别人面前表演"与原文完全吻合。A 项"他不会演奏任何乐器"与原文"could play the guitar very well"矛盾；C 项"他和同学相处不好"原文未提及；D 项"他根本不喜欢音乐"与原文"He loved music"直接矛盾。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "Wang Wei wanted to say no, but when he saw the encouraging looks on his classmates' faces, he decided to give it a try."

**【解析】** 原文说明 Ms. Chen 建议 Wang Wei 参加才艺表演，全班同学鼓掌支持，Wang Wei 看到同学们鼓励的眼神后决定试一试。因此是老师和同学的鼓励促使他做出了决定。A 项"Ms. Chen 强迫他"程度过重，原文用的是"I think...should"是建议而非强迫；B 项"他想赢得大奖"原文未提及任何奖品；D 项"他父母要求他参加"也属于无中生有。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "Being brave doesn't mean you're not afraid. It means you keep going even when you are."

**【解析】** 妈妈的原话意思是"勇敢并不意味着你不害怕，而是即使害怕你也要继续前进"。这说明真正的勇气是在感到恐惧的情况下仍然坚持行动。D 项"真正的勇气是在害怕时仍然行动"准确概括了妈妈话语的深层含义。A 项"Wang Wei 应该停止练习吉他"与妈妈鼓励的语气完全相反；B 项"在别人面前弹吉他很容易"妈妈并未表达此意；C 项"Wang Wei 不应该感到害怕"恰恰与原文矛盾——妈妈承认害怕是正常的，关键是不要被恐惧阻止。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 推理判断题要注意抓住原句的核心逻辑关系。本题的关键是"doesn't mean...It means..."的转折对比结构，强调的是后半句的真正含义。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "The music flowed from his fingers like a gentle river."

**【解析】** 作者用比喻手法（like a gentle river）将音乐比作一条温柔的小河，表达音乐从他的指间流淌而出。"gentle river"（温柔的河流）给人的感觉是平缓、流畅、优美的。结合全文语境——Wang Wei 克服紧张后成功演奏，观众先是安静随后热烈鼓掌——可知他的演奏非常出色、动听。B 项"音乐听起来流畅而美妙"最准确地传达了这一比喻的含义。A 项"音乐响亮而激动人心"与"gentle"（温柔的）不符；C 项"Wang Wei 在台上弹得很快"将比喻误解为速度描写；D 项"吉他出了问题"完全曲解原意。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 遇到比喻类的词义猜测题，要关注比喻词（like/as）后面的意象特征。"gentle river"的特征是柔和、流畅，以此推断被比喻事物的特点。

---

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "For the first time, he understood that the thing he feared most could also bring him the greatest joy."

**【解析】** 全文讲述了 Wang Wei 从不敢在人前演奏，到在老师和同学的鼓励下参加才艺表演，最终成功演出并收获快乐的故事。文章结尾点题：他最害怕的事情也能带给他最大的快乐。A 项"面对恐惧能带来意想不到的快乐"最准确地概括了全文主旨。B 项"音乐是学校最重要的科目"以偏概全，文章主题是克服恐惧而非强调音乐的学科地位；C 项"老师不应该推动学生尝试新事物"与文章传达的正面信息相反；D 项"只有有天赋的人才能上台表演"属于过度推断，文章强调的是勇气而非天赋。故选 A。

**【技巧点拨】** 记叙文的主旨大意题要特别关注文章最后一段的总结性感悟语句，通常直接揭示故事的主题或寓意。
