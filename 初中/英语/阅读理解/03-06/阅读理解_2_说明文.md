# 阅读理解 第2篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Dolphins are one of the most loved animals in the world. They live in oceans and rivers, and they are known for their intelligence and friendly nature. There are over 40 different kinds of dolphins. The most common one is the bottlenose dolphin, which you may have seen in aquariums or on TV shows.

② Dolphins are mammals, not fish. This means they breathe air just like humans do. They must come to the surface of the water to breathe. A dolphin can hold its breath for about 8 to 10 minutes before it needs to come up for air. Baby dolphins are born alive, and their mothers feed them milk — another sign that they are mammals.

③ One of the most amazing things about dolphins is how they communicate with each other. They use a system of clicks, whistles and body movements to "talk." Scientists have found that each dolphin has its own special whistle, like a name. When a dolphin hears its special whistle, it will answer. This shows that dolphins can recognize each other by sound.

④ Dolphins are also very social animals. They live in groups called "pods," which can have from 2 to over 100 members. In a pod, dolphins help each other find food, take care of young dolphins, and protect each other from danger. If one dolphin is sick or hurt, others in the pod will swim under it and push it to the surface so that it can breathe.

⑤ Today, dolphins face some serious problems. Water pollution, fishing nets, and noise from ships can all harm dolphins. Many countries have passed laws to protect them. We should also do our part to keep the oceans clean, because protecting dolphins means protecting our planet.

---

**注释：** dolphin 海豚；mammal 哺乳动物；bottlenose dolphin 宽吻海豚；aquarium 水族馆；whistle 哨声；pod（海豚）群

---

### 题目

1. How many kinds of dolphins are there in the world?
   A. Over 40.
   B. About 20.
   C. About 10.
   D. Over 100.

2. Why must dolphins come to the surface of the water?
   A. To find food.
   B. To breathe air.
   C. To play with other dolphins.
   D. To keep warm in the sun.

3. The underlined word "recognize" in Paragraph ③ probably means "________."
   A. 忘记
   B. 伤害
   C. 辨认
   D. 训练

4. According to Paragraph ④, what will other dolphins do if one dolphin is hurt?
   A. They will leave it alone and swim away.
   B. They will try to find a human to help.
   C. They will bring it food from far away.
   D. They will push it to the surface to help it breathe.

5. What is the passage mainly about?
   A. How to train dolphins in aquariums.
   B. The life, abilities, and protection of dolphins.
   C. Why dolphins are better than other sea animals.
   D. The problems that dolphins cause in the ocean.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "There are over 40 different kinds of dolphins."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出世界上有超过40种不同的海豚。A 项"Over 40"与原文完全一致。B 项"About 20"和 C 项"About 10"均低于原文数据；D 项"Over 100"是第④段中提到的海豚群（pod）的最大成员数，属于张冠李戴。故选 A。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "They must come to the surface of the water to breathe."

**【解析】** 原文解释海豚是哺乳动物而非鱼类，它们像人类一样需要呼吸空气，因此必须浮出水面来呼吸。B 项"To breathe air"与原文完全对应。A 项"找食物"原文未将觅食与浮出水面联系；C 项"和其他海豚玩耍"属于无中生有；D 项"在阳光下保暖"原文同样没有提及。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段第4-5句 "When a dolphin hears its special whistle, it will answer. This shows that dolphins can recognize each other by sound."

**【解析】** 根据上下文，前文说每只海豚有自己独特的哨声（like a name），当海豚听到属于自己的哨声时会做出回应。这说明海豚能够通过声音"recognize"彼此——即分辨出、识别出对方是谁。C 项"辨认"最符合语境。A 项"忘记"与能回应特定哨声的行为矛盾；B 项"伤害"与友好互动的语境完全不符；D 项"训练"不符合这里描述的是海豚之间的自然交流行为。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 词义猜测题要充分利用上下文的因果逻辑。本题中"each dolphin has its own special whistle"和"it will answer"构成了推断"recognize"含义的关键线索。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "If one dolphin is sick or hurt, others in the pod will swim under it and push it to the surface so that it can breathe."

**【解析】** 原文清楚描述了当一只海豚生病或受伤时，群里的其他海豚会游到它下方，把它推到水面以便它能呼吸。D 项与原文描述完全一致。A 项"丢下它独自游走"与原文互助行为完全相反；B 项"试图找人类来帮忙"属于无中生有，原文描述的是海豚之间的互助行为；C 项"从远处给它带食物"原文未提及这种行为。故选 D。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文结构：第①段介绍海豚的基本信息 → 第②段说明海豚是哺乳动物 → 第③段介绍海豚的沟通方式 → 第④段描述海豚的社会行为 → 第⑤段谈论海豚面临的威胁与保护。

**【解析】** 全文从海豚的种类、生物特征、沟通能力、社会行为到面临的威胁和保护需求进行了全面介绍。B 项"海豚的生活、能力和保护"最全面准确地概括了文章的主要内容。A 项"如何在水族馆训练海豚"文章并未讨论训练方法，水族馆只是第①段的一个细节提及；C 项"为什么海豚比其他海洋动物更好"文章没有进行比较；D 项"海豚在海洋中造成的问题"与文章内容相反——文中讨论的是人类活动对海豚造成的问题，而非海豚对海洋的影响。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 说明文的主旨题需要综合各段内容进行概括。注意排除"以偏概全"型干扰项——只涵盖某一段内容的选项通常不是正确答案。正确选项应能覆盖文章各段的核心内容。
