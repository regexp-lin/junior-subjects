# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Have you ever wondered how plants make their own food? The answer is photosynthesis. This is one of the most important processes on Earth. Without it, there would be no plants, no animals, and no us. Let us learn how it works.

② The word "photosynthesis" comes from two parts: "photo" means light, and "synthesis" means putting together. So photosynthesis means "putting together with light." Plants use sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide from the air to make their food. They take in water through their roots and carbon dioxide through small holes in their leaves. Then, with the help of sunlight and a green substance called chlorophyll, they turn these into sugar and oxygen.

③ Chlorophyll is what makes plants green. It is found in the leaves. It can catch sunlight and use its energy. Think of it as a small factory inside each leaf. When sunlight hits the leaf, the chlorophyll uses the light energy to break water and carbon dioxide apart. Then it puts the parts back together in a new way — as sugar that the plant uses for food, and oxygen that goes back into the air.

④ Why do we care about photosynthesis? First, plants produce the oxygen we breathe. About half of the oxygen in the air comes from the ocean, and the rest mainly from plants on land. Second, plants are the base of the food chain. Animals eat plants, and we eat both plants and animals. Without photosynthesis, none of this would exist. Third, plants take in carbon dioxide. This helps reduce the amount of this gas in the atmosphere, which is good for our environment.

⑤ Photosynthesis happens every day, in every green plant, from small grass to tall trees. It is a quiet, powerful process that keeps life on Earth going. Next time you see a green leaf, remember: it is busy making food and oxygen for all of us.

**注释：** photosynthesis 光合作用；chlorophyll 叶绿素

---

**Questions:**

1. What does photosynthesis need to work?
   A. Only water.
   B. Only sunlight.
   C. Sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide.
   D. Soil and fertilizer.

2. What makes plants green?
   A. Sugar.
   B. Oxygen.
   C. Chlorophyll.
   D. Carbon dioxide.

3. What do plants produce during photosynthesis?
   A. Only water.
   B. Sugar and oxygen.
   C. Only carbon dioxide.
   D. Sunlight.

4. Why is photosynthesis important for humans?
   A. Because we can eat chlorophyll.
   B. Because plants produce oxygen we breathe and are the base of the food chain.
   C. Because plants need us to help them.
   D. Because photosynthesis only happens in summer.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Photosynthesis only happens in big trees.
   B. Photosynthesis is a daily process in green plants that supports life on Earth.
   C. Green leaves are not important.
   D. We should not look at green plants.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Plants use sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide from the air to make their food."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明光合作用需要阳光、水和空气中的二氧化碳。C项准确。A、B项不完整；D项"土壤和肥料"原文未提及。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "Chlorophyll is what makes plants green."

**【解析】** 原文说明叶绿素使植物呈绿色。C项正确。A、B、D项与植物颜色无关。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "they turn these into sugar and oxygen" 及第③段 "as sugar that the plant uses for food, and oxygen that goes back into the air"

**【解析】** 光合作用的产物是糖（供植物使用）和氧气（释放到空气中）。B项正确。A、C、D项与产物不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "First, plants produce the oxygen we breathe... Second, plants are the base of the food chain. Animals eat plants, and we eat both plants and animals."

**【解析】** 光合作用对人类的意义包括：植物产生我们呼吸的氧气、植物是食物链基础。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Photosynthesis happens every day, in every green plant, from small grass to tall trees. It is a quiet, powerful process that keeps life on Earth going."

**【解析】** 文末说明光合作用每日发生在各种绿色植物中，是维持地球生命运转的过程，可推知B项正确。A项"只在大树中"与"from small grass to tall trees"矛盾；C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
