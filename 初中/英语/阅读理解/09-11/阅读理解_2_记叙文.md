> **09-11 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last summer, my parents had to go on a business trip. They could not take me with them because of work. So they decided to send me to my grandmother's city by plane — alone. I had never traveled by myself before. I was only thirteen years old. I felt both excited and a little scared.

② At the airport, my father helped me check in and get my boarding pass. He showed me where to go and what to do. "Remember, the flight attendant will help you if you need anything," he said. "Just ask. And call us when you land." My mother held my hand tightly and said, "You can do this. We are proud of you." Then it was time to say goodbye. I walked through the security check alone, my heart beating fast.

③ On the plane, I found my seat by myself. A kind lady sat next to me. She saw that I was young and traveling alone. She smiled and said, "First time flying alone? You are brave!" Her words made me feel better. During the flight, I read a book and looked out of the window. The clouds below looked like white cotton. I started to enjoy the trip.

④ When the plane landed, I turned on my phone and called my parents. "I did it, Mom and Dad!" I said. They were so happy. My grandmother was waiting for me at the airport. She gave me a big hug. "You are growing up," she said with a smile.

⑤ That summer, I stayed with my grandmother for three weeks. We had a wonderful time. But what I remember most is not the trip itself, but the feeling of doing something by myself for the first time. I learned that I could be independent. I could face new things and not be afraid. That summer, I grew a little more.

**注释：** boarding pass 登机牌；flight attendant 乘务员

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer travel by plane alone?
   A. Because he/she wanted to show off to friends.
   B. Because his/her parents had a business trip and sent him/her to the grandmother's city.
   C. Because the grandmother asked him/her to come.
   D. Because he/she was too old to travel with parents.

2. How did the writer feel before the trip?
   A. Only scared.
   B. Only excited.
   C. Both excited and a little scared.
   D. Bored and tired.

3. What did the kind lady next to the writer do?
   A. She helped the writer find the grandmother.
   B. She gave the writer some money.
   C. She smiled and encouraged the writer, saying he/she was brave.
   D. She taught the writer how to fly a plane.

4. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Flying is always fun.
   B. He/she could be independent and face new things without fear.
   C. Grandmothers are the best travel partners.
   D. Parents should not send children to travel alone.

5. What is the best title for this passage?
   A. How to Book a Plane Ticket
   B. My First Solo Flight: A Summer of Growth
   C. A Visit to My Grandmother
   D. The Kind Lady on the Plane

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last summer, my parents had to go on a business trip. They could not take me with them because of work. So they decided to send me to my grandmother's city by plane — alone."

**【解析】** 原文说明父母因出差无法同行，于是安排作者独自乘飞机去奶奶所在的城市。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "I felt both excited and a little scared."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述作者既兴奋又有一点害怕。C项正确。A、B项只提及一种情绪；D项原文未提及。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "A kind lady sat next to me. She saw that I was young and traveling alone. She smiled and said, 'First time flying alone? You are brave!' Her words made me feel better."

**【解析】** 女士对作者微笑并鼓励他/她，称赞其勇敢，让作者放松了一些。C项准确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "what I remember most is not the trip itself, but the feeling of doing something by myself for the first time. I learned that I could be independent. I could face new things and not be afraid."

**【解析】** 作者从中体会到可以独立、敢于面对新事物而不畏惧。B项正确概括。A、C、D项或过于片面或与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第①段"traveled by myself"、第⑤段"doing something by myself for the first time"和"I grew a little more"

**【解析】** 文章以首次独自乘机为主线，突出成长主题，B项"我的第一次独自飞行：一个成长的夏天"最贴切。A项过于技术化；C、D项仅涉及局部内容。故选B。
