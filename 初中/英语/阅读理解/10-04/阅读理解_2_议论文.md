> **10-04 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Where is it better to live — in a city or in the countryside? This question has been discussed for a long time. Both places have their advantages and disadvantages. Let us look at some of them.

② City life has many benefits. Cities offer more jobs and better pay. There are more schools, hospitals, and universities. You can find almost everything you need — shops, restaurants, cinemas, and libraries — within a short distance. Public transport is convenient: buses, subways, and taxis can take you anywhere quickly. For young people who want to study or work, cities often provide more chances. In addition, cities have rich cultural life: museums, concerts, and sports events are easy to find.

③ However, city life has its problems. Cities can be noisy and crowded. Traffic jams are common, and the air is often polluted. Housing is expensive — many people spend a large part of their income on rent. Life can be stressful and fast-paced. Some people feel lonely in a big city even though they are surrounded by many people.

④ Country life is different. The countryside is usually quiet and clean. The air is fresh, and there is more space. People often know their neighbours and feel a sense of community. Housing is cheaper, and you can have a garden or even a small farm. Children can play outdoors safely. Many people find life in the countryside more relaxed and healthy.

⑤ Yet the countryside has disadvantages too. There are fewer jobs, especially skilled jobs. Schools and hospitals may be far away. Shops and services are limited. Young people often move to cities for work or study, so villages can feel empty. In the end, the choice depends on what you value most: opportunities and convenience, or peace and nature. There is no single "best" answer — it varies from person to person.

**注释：** community 社区；skilled 需要技能的

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, which is an advantage of city life?
   A. Cities are always quiet.
   B. Cities offer more jobs, schools, and rich cultural life.
   C. Housing in cities is very cheap.
   D. Cities have no traffic problems.

2. What problem does the writer mention about city life?
   A. Cities have no hospitals.
   B. Cities can be noisy, crowded, and polluted; housing is expensive.
   C. Everyone in cities is happy.
   D. Cities have no schools.

3. Why do some people prefer the countryside?
   A. Because there are more jobs there.
   B. Because it is quiet, clean, with fresh air and a sense of community; life is more relaxed.
   C. Because the countryside has more universities.
   D. Because everyone moves to the countryside.

4. What is a disadvantage of country life?
   A. The air is too fresh.
   B. There are fewer jobs; schools and hospitals may be far; young people often leave for cities.
   C. People are too friendly.
   D. There are too many shops.

5. What does the writer think about the question "city or countryside"?
   A. City is always better.
   B. Countryside is always better.
   C. The choice depends on what you value; there is no single best answer.
   D. Nobody should live in either place.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "more jobs and better pay... more schools, hospitals, and universities... rich cultural life: museums, concerts, and sports events"

**【解析】** 城市优势包括更多工作、学校、医疗及丰富文化休闲。B项概括准确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Cities can be noisy and crowded. Traffic jams are common, and the air is often polluted. Housing is expensive"

**【解析】** 城市问题包括喧闹拥挤、交通拥堵、污染、住房昂贵。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "quiet and clean... fresh air... sense of community... more relaxed and healthy"

**【解析】** 乡村生活安静、空气好、有社区归属感、更放松健康。B项准确。A、C、D项与乡村特点不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "There are fewer jobs... Schools and hospitals may be far away... Young people often move to cities"

**【解析】** 乡村劣势包括就业少、学校医院较远、年轻人外流。B项正确。A、C、D项非文中提到的劣势。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "the choice depends on what you value most... There is no single 'best' answer — it varies from person to person"

**【解析】** 作者认为选择取决于个人价值观，没有唯一最佳答案。C项正确。A、B、D项与作者观点不符。故选C。
