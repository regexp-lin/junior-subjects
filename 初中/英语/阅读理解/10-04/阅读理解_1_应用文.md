> **10-04 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **City Junior English Writing Competition 2024**

② The City Education Bureau and the English Teachers' Association are pleased to invite all junior high school students to take part in the 2024 English Writing Competition. This is a great chance to show your writing skills and share your ideas in English.

③ **Theme:** "My Favourite Place" — Write about a place that is special to you. It can be your school, your hometown, a park, or anywhere you love. Tell us why it is important to you (150–200 words).

④ **Rules:**
- Each student may submit only one piece of work.
- Your writing must be original. No copying from the Internet or other students.
- Hand in your work to your English teacher before **October 20, 2024**.
- Write your name, school, class, and student ID on the top of your paper.

⑤ **Prizes:**
- First prize (3 winners): Certificate + ¥500 book voucher (购书券)
- Second prize (10 winners): Certificate + ¥200 book voucher
- Third prize (20 winners): Certificate + a gift

⑥ Results will be announced on November 5, 2024 on the Education Bureau website. Good luck to all participants!

**注释：** voucher 券；original 原创的

---

**Questions:**

1. Who can take part in this competition?
   A. Primary school students only.
   B. Senior high school students only.
   C. All junior high school students.
   D. Only English teachers.

2. What is the theme of the writing competition?
   A. My Favourite Food.
   B. My Favourite Place.
   C. My Favourite Sport.
   D. My Favourite Holiday.

3. How many words should each piece of writing have?
   A. 50–100 words.
   B. 100–150 words.
   C. 150–200 words.
   D. 200–300 words.

4. When is the deadline to hand in the work?
   A. September 20, 2024.
   B. October 20, 2024.
   C. November 5, 2024.
   D. December 1, 2024.

5. What can the first prize winners get?
   A. Only a certificate.
   B. A certificate and a ¥500 book voucher.
   C. A certificate and ¥200.
   D. A gift only.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "invite all junior high school students to take part in"

**【解析】** 征稿对象为全体初中生。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Theme: 'My Favourite Place'"

**【解析】** 比赛主题为"我最喜欢的地方"。B项正确。A、C、D项非本次主题。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "150–200 words"

**【解析】** 字数要求为150–200词。C项正确。A、B、D项字数不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Hand in your work to your English teacher before **October 20, 2024**"

**【解析】** 截止日期为2024年10月20日。B项正确。A、C、D项日期错误。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "First prize (3 winners): Certificate + ¥500 book voucher"

**【解析】** 一等奖获得证书和500元购书券。B项准确。A项缺少购书券；C、D项与奖项内容不符。故选B。
