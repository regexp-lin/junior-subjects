# 阅读理解（说明文）

## **Why We Need to Protect the Ocean**

①The ocean covers about 70% of our planet. It is home to millions of fish, whales, dolphins, and other sea life. The ocean also gives us food, helps control the weather, and produces more than half of the oxygen we breathe. Without a healthy ocean, life on Earth would be very different. Yet today, the ocean is facing serious problems.

②Pollution is one of the biggest threats. Every year, millions of tonnes of plastic end up in the ocean. Sea animals eat the plastic by mistake and get sick or die. Some plastic breaks into tiny pieces called microplastics. These pieces enter the food chain and can end up on our dinner tables. We must reduce our use of plastic bags and bottles.

③Overfishing is another problem. When we catch too many fish, there are not enough left to reproduce. Some fish species are disappearing. We need to fish in a sustainable way. That means catching only what we need and leaving enough fish in the sea for the future.

④Climate change also affects the ocean. Warmer water hurts coral reefs. Many sea animals lose their homes. The ice at the North and South Poles is melting. This causes sea levels to rise and threatens coastal cities. We can help by using less energy and reducing our carbon footprint.

⑤What can we do as students? We can start small: use less plastic, recycle, and save water. We can learn more about the ocean and tell others. Every small action counts. The ocean belongs to all of us, and it is our job to protect it for future generations.

## 题目

1. What does the passage mainly discuss?
   A. How to swim in the ocean.
   B. Why and how we should protect the ocean.
   C. The best places to visit by the sea.
   D. Types of fish in the ocean.

2. According to paragraph 2, why is plastic dangerous to sea animals?
   A. It makes the water too cold.
   B. Sea animals eat it by mistake and get sick or die.
   C. It blocks the sunlight.
   D. It makes the water taste bad.

3. What does "sustainable fishing" mean in paragraph 3?
   A. Catching as many fish as possible.
   B. Catching only what we need and leaving enough for the future.
   C. Never fishing at all.
   D. Fishing only in winter.

4. How does climate change affect the ocean?
   A. It makes fish grow bigger.
   B. Warmer water hurts coral reefs and causes sea level rise.
   C. It creates more oxygen.
   D. It has no effect on the ocean.

5. What can students do to help protect the ocean?
   A. Only adults can help.
   B. Use less plastic, recycle, and save water.
   C. Stop eating fish completely.
   D. Move away from coastal cities.

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】主旨大意题

【定位】全文

【解析】
- 文章先说明海洋的重要性和面临的问题（污染、过度捕捞、气候变化），最后提出学生可以采取的保护措施。
- A“如何游泳”、C“最佳海边景点”、D“鱼类种类”均非主旨。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第②段

【解析】
- 第②段写：“Sea animals eat the plastic by mistake and get sick or die”（海洋动物误食塑料后生病或死亡）。
- A、C、D 未在文中提及。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：B**

【题型】词义理解/细节题

【定位】第③段

【解析】
- 第③段解释：可持续捕捞指“catching only what we need and leaving enough fish in the sea for the future”（只捕捞所需数量，为将来保留足够鱼类）。
- A 与可持续理念相反。C“永不捕鱼”过于极端。D“只在冬季捕鱼”未提及。故答案为 B。

**4. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第④段

【解析】
- 第④段指出：海水变暖损害珊瑚礁，极地冰雪融化导致海平面上升，威胁沿海城市。
- A、C、D 与原文不符。故答案为 B。

**5. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第⑤段

【解析】
- 第⑤段建议学生：减少塑料使用、回收利用、节约用水等（“use less plastic, recycle, and save water”）。
- A 与“What can we do as students”矛盾。C、D 未提及且不符合常识。故答案为 B。
