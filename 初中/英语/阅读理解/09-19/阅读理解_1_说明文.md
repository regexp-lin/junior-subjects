# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Many students spend a lot of time reading, using phones, or working on computers. Without proper care, our eyes can easily get tired and our eyesight may get worse. Here are some useful tips to help protect your eyes and keep them healthy.

② First, follow the "20-20-20" rule. Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds. This gives your eyes a short rest and reduces eye strain (疲劳). When you are reading or studying, make sure the room has enough light. Do not read in places that are too dark or too bright. Keep the screen of your phone or computer at arm's length and slightly below eye level.

③ Second, blink (眨眼) often. When we look at screens, we blink less. Blinking keeps our eyes wet and comfortable. If your eyes feel dry, close them for a moment or use eye drops (眼药水) if needed. Also, remember to get enough sleep. Lack of sleep can make your eyes red and tired. Most teenagers need about eight to nine hours of sleep each night.

④ Third, eat healthy food. Foods rich in vitamin A, such as carrots, eggs, and green vegetables, are good for your eyes. Fish and nuts contain omega-3, which also helps keep your eyes healthy. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

⑤ Last, have your eyes checked regularly. Even if you think your eyesight is fine, a yearly check can help find problems early. If you wear glasses, wear them as your doctor advises. Protecting your eyes is a habit — start today and your future self will thank you.

**注释：** strain 疲劳；blink 眨眼；eye drops 眼药水；vitamin 维生素；hydrated 水分充足的

---

**Questions:**

1. What is the "20-20-20" rule?
   A. Study for 20 minutes, rest for 20 seconds, repeat 20 times.
   B. Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds.
   C. Use screens for no more than 20 minutes a day.
   D. Sleep 20 minutes every 2 hours.

2. Why should we blink often when using screens?
   A. To make our eyes bigger.
   B. Because we blink less when looking at screens; blinking keeps eyes wet and comfortable.
   C. To show that we are not sleepy.
   D. To help us read faster.

3. Which food is good for protecting eyes according to the passage?
   A. Chips and candy.
   B. Carrots, eggs, and green vegetables.
   C. Fried chicken.
   D. Ice cream.

4. How often does the writer suggest having your eyes checked?
   A. Never.
   B. Every month.
   C. About once a year.
   D. Only when you feel pain.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell funny stories about eyes.
   B. To give tips on how to protect eyes and keep them healthy.
   C. To sell eye drops.
   D. To compare different types of glasses.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds"

**【解析】** 原文明确给出 20-20-20 法则的定义。A、C、D 项均为曲解。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "When we look at screens, we blink less. Blinking keeps our eyes wet and comfortable"

**【解析】** 看屏幕时眨眼减少，眨眼能使眼睛保持湿润舒适。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Foods rich in vitamin A, such as carrots, eggs, and green vegetables, are good for your eyes"

**【解析】** 富含维生素 A 的食物如胡萝卜、鸡蛋、绿色蔬菜对眼睛有益。A、C、D 项均非护眼食物。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "a yearly check can help find problems early"

**【解析】** 原文建议每年检查一次眼睛。A、B、D 项与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "Here are some useful tips to help protect your eyes and keep them healthy" 及全文结构

**【解析】** 全文围绕护眼技巧展开，旨在帮助读者保护视力、保持眼睛健康。A、C、D 项非主旨。故选 B。
