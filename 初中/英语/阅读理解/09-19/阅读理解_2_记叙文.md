# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last term, a new student named Xiao Ming joined our class. He uses a wheelchair (轮椅) because he cannot walk. At first, some of us did not know how to talk to him or how to help. We were a bit shy.

② One afternoon, our teacher asked us to go to the school library to find books for a project. Xiao Ming wanted to come with us, but the library is on the second floor and there is no lift. "I can help carry your books," I said. He smiled and said, "Thank you, but I'd like to go up myself if possible." I did not understand at first.

③ Then I saw him use the ramp (斜坡) at the side of the building. It took him longer, but he reached the second floor by himself. Inside the library, the shelves were too high for him. I asked, "Can I get the books for you?" He said, "Yes, please. I'll tell you which ones." We worked together — I reached for the books and he chose them. He was smart and knew exactly what he needed. We found all the books and even had time to chat. I learned that he loves science and dreams of becoming an engineer.

④ From that day on, we became good friends. I often help him with things he cannot reach, and he helps me with my maths. He never complains (抱怨) about his situation. He says, "Everyone has something they cannot do. The important thing is to try our best and help each other."

⑤ That experience taught me a lot. Helping someone does not mean doing everything for them. Sometimes, the best help is to understand what they need and support them in their own way. I am glad I met Xiao Ming.

**注释：** wheelchair 轮椅；ramp 斜坡；complain 抱怨；shelf 书架

---

**Questions:**

1. Why does Xiao Ming use a wheelchair?
   A. He is lazy.
   B. He cannot walk.
   C. He likes to sit.
   D. The school asked him to.

2. How did Xiao Ming get to the second floor of the library?
   A. Someone carried him.
   B. He used the ramp at the side of the building.
   C. He took the lift.
   D. He did not go to the second floor.

3. Why did the writer help Xiao Ming in the library?
   A. Because the teacher ordered him to.
   B. Because the shelves were too high for Xiao Ming to reach.
   C. Because Xiao Ming asked for money.
   D. Because the library was closed.

4. What does Xiao Ming want to be when he grows up?
   A. A teacher.
   B. A doctor.
   C. An engineer.
   D. A librarian.

5. What did the writer learn from helping Xiao Ming?
   A. Helping means doing everything for someone.
   B. The best help is to understand what they need and support them in their own way.
   C. People in wheelchairs cannot do anything.
   D. We should never ask for help.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He uses a wheelchair because he cannot walk"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明小明因无法行走而使用轮椅。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "I saw him use the ramp at the side of the building. It took him longer, but he reached the second floor by himself"

**【解析】** 小明通过大楼侧面的斜坡自行上到二楼。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Inside the library, the shelves were too high for him. I asked, 'Can I get the books for you?'"

**【解析】** 图书馆书架过高，小明够不到，作者主动提出帮忙取书。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "He loves science and dreams of becoming an engineer"

**【解析】** 小明热爱科学，梦想成为工程师。A、B、D 项与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Helping someone does not mean doing everything for them. Sometimes, the best help is to understand what they need and support them in their own way"

**【解析】** 作者领悟到：帮助不是包办一切，而是理解对方需求并以恰当方式支持。A、C、D 项与作者感悟矛盾。故选 B。
