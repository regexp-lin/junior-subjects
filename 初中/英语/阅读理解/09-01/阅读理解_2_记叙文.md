> **09-01 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① It was the first day of the new term. I walked into Class 3 with a mix of excitement and worry. We had heard that we would have a new head teacher. Who would she be? Would she be strict? I found my seat and waited quietly.

② When the bell rang, a young woman in a blue dress entered the room. She had a warm smile and kind eyes. "Good morning, everyone. I'm Miss Chen, your new head teacher," she said. Her voice was gentle but clear. She told us she had just moved to our city and this was her first year at our school. "I may be new here, but I am ready to learn with you. Let's make this a great year together."

③ Miss Chen asked each of us to stand up and say our names and one thing we liked. I was nervous, but when my turn came, I said I liked drawing. Miss Chen smiled and said, "Wonderful! We have an art lover. Maybe we can have a class exhibition later." Her words made me feel that she really cared about us. Some of my classmates said they liked sports, reading, or music. Miss Chen wrote down every answer on the board and said we would use these interests in our lessons.

④ After the introductions, Miss Chen showed us the class rules. There were only five rules, and they were simple: be respectful, be on time, try your best, help each other, and have fun while learning. "Rules are not to make you afraid," she said. "They are to help us work well together." She also told us that her door was always open. If we had any problems, we could talk to her anytime.

⑤ When the class ended, I felt much more relaxed. Miss Chen was not scary at all. She was friendly and made us feel welcome. I thought to myself that the new term might be better than I had expected. A good teacher can really make a difference. I could not wait for the next class with Miss Chen.

**注释：** exhibition 展览；respectful 有礼貌的

---

**Questions:**

1. How did the writer feel before meeting the new head teacher?
   A. Only excited.
   B. Only worried.
   C. A mix of excitement and worry.
   D. Very angry.

2. What did Miss Chen ask the students to do?
   A. She asked them to write an essay.
   B. She asked each student to stand up, say their name, and one thing they liked.
   C. She asked them to take a test.
   D. She asked them to clean the classroom.

3. What did Miss Chen say about the writer's hobby?
   A. She said drawing was useless.
   B. She said they might have a class exhibition later.
   C. She said the writer should focus on math.
   D. She said nothing about it.

4. According to the passage, what is the purpose of class rules according to Miss Chen?
   A. To make students afraid.
   B. To help everyone work well together.
   C. To give more homework.
   D. To punish bad students.

5. What can we infer about the writer's attitude at the end?
   A. The writer still feels worried about the new teacher.
   B. The writer feels welcomed and looks forward to the new term.
   C. The writer wants to change to another class.
   D. The writer does not like Miss Chen.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "I walked into Class 3 with a mix of excitement and worry"

**【解析】** 原文明确说作者怀着兴奋与担忧的复杂心情进入教室。A项"仅兴奋"、B项"仅担忧"、D项"愤怒"均不完整或与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "Miss Chen asked each of us to stand up and say our names and one thing we liked"

**【解析】** 原文明确说陈老师让每位学生起立说出姓名和一项爱好。A项"写作文"、C项"考试"、D项"打扫教室"均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Miss Chen smiled and said, 'Wonderful! We have an art lover. Maybe we can have a class exhibition later.'"

**【解析】** 陈老师称赞作者并提议可能举办班级画展。A、C项与陈老师态度相反；D项"未提及"与原文矛盾。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "'Rules are not to make you afraid,' she said. 'They are to help us work well together.'"

**【解析】** 陈老师说规则不是让人恐惧，而是帮助大家良好协作。A项与陈老师原意相反；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I felt much more relaxed... She was friendly and made us feel welcome... the new term might be better than I had expected... I could not wait for the next class"

**【解析】** 作者感到轻松、被欢迎，期待新学期和下次课，可推断其态度积极、充满期待。A、C、D项与结尾情感不符。故选B。
