> **11-15 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Is It Good for Students to Have a Part-time Job?**

① In some countries, many middle school students work part-time after school or during holidays. They work in shops, restaurants, or help neighbours. In China, this is less common, but some students do small jobs like tutoring younger children. Is it a good idea for students to have a part-time job?

② Some people think part-time jobs are helpful. First, working helps students learn important life skills. They learn how to manage time, communicate with different people, and solve problems. These skills are hard to learn only from textbooks. Second, earning money teaches young people the value of hard work. When you spend money you earned yourself, you think more carefully. Third, work experience can help students understand society better and think about what they want to do in the future.

③ However, others worry about the problems. Students' main task is studying. If they spend too much time working, their grades may drop. Middle school is a key period. Falling behind in study is hard to fix later. Also, some jobs may not be safe for young people. They may face unfair treatment or work too many hours. Besides, some students may care too much about money and forget the importance of learning.

④ In my opinion, a small amount of work can be good, but it must not affect study. Students should choose safe, simple jobs and work only a few hours a week — perhaps during weekends or holidays. Parents and teachers should give advice and make sure the student's health and study come first.

⑤ Balance is the key. Work can teach us things that school cannot, but school must always be the top priority for students.

**注释：** tutor 辅导；priority 优先事项

**Questions:**

1. What is the passage mainly about?
   A. How to find a good part-time job.
   B. Whether students should have part-time jobs.
   C. Why students must not work.
   D. The best part-time jobs for students.

2. According to Paragraph 2, what can students learn from working?
   A. How to play sports.
   B. How to cook food.
   C. Time management, communication, and problem-solving skills.
   D. How to get higher grades.

3. What is one worry mentioned in Paragraph 3?
   A. Students will make too many friends.
   B. Students may spend too much time working and their grades may drop.
   C. All jobs are dangerous.
   D. Parents will not allow students to study.

4. What does the writer suggest students do?
   A. Work full-time during school days.
   B. Never work at all.
   C. Choose safe, simple jobs and work only a few hours during weekends or holidays.
   D. Earn as much money as possible.

5. What does the underlined word "Balance" in Paragraph 5 most likely mean?
   A. Working as hard as possible.
   B. Giving up study for work.
   C. Finding the right amount between two things.
   D. Choosing one side and ignoring the other.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段末句 "Is it a good idea for students to have a part-time job?" 及全文结构

**【解析】** 文章围绕"学生是否应该做兼职"展开正反两面讨论。A项"如何找好兼职"过于具体，文中未详细讨论；C项"学生不能工作"过于绝对，只是反方观点之一；D项"最好的兼职"文中未涉及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "They learn how to manage time, communicate with different people, and solve problems."

**【解析】** 原文明确列出三种技能：时间管理、沟通和解决问题。A项运动、B项做饭均未提及；D项"更高分数"与原文不符，原文说的是学到课本上学不到的技能。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "If they spend too much time working, their grades may drop."

**【解析】** 原文担忧花太多时间工作会导致成绩下降。A项"交太多朋友"原文未提及；C项"所有工作都危险"过于绝对，原文只说"some jobs may not be safe"；D项"家长不允许学习"属于无中生有。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Students should choose safe, simple jobs and work only a few hours a week — perhaps during weekends or holidays."

**【解析】** 作者建议选择安全简单的工作，每周只工作几个小时，最好在周末或假期。A项"全天工作"与原文矛盾；B项"完全不工作"不符合作者观点，作者认为适量工作有益；D项"尽量多赚钱"与作者"平衡"的主张相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Balance is the key. Work can teach us things that school cannot, but school must always be the top priority for students."

**【解析】** 根据上下文，作者强调工作和学习都有价值，但要把握好两者之间的"度"，因此"Balance"意为在两件事之间找到合适的平衡点。A项"尽可能努力工作"只强调一方面；B项"放弃学习"与原文矛盾；D项"选一边忽略另一边"恰恰是作者反对的做法。故选C。
