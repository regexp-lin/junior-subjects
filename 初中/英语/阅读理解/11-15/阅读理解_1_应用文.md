> **11-15 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Sunshine Library — Winter Reading Programme**

① Dear readers,

② Sunshine Library is happy to announce our Winter Reading Programme! From January 6th to February 14th, 2025, we invite students aged 12–15 to join us. Read books, share your ideas, and win prizes!

**Programme Details:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Time | Jan 6 – Feb 14, 2025 |
| Who can join | Students aged 12–15 |
| How to join | Sign up at the front desk or online at www.sunshinelib.com |
| Sign-up deadline | December 31, 2024 |
| Fee | Free |

**Activities:**

| Activity | Date & Time | Place |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Opening Ceremony | Jan 6, 10:00 a.m. | Main Hall |
| Book Discussion (every Saturday) | 2:00–3:30 p.m. | Room 205 |
| Writing Workshop | Jan 20, 10:00–11:30 a.m. | Room 108 |
| Movie & Book Night | Feb 7, 6:00–8:00 p.m. | Main Hall |
| Closing & Prize Ceremony | Feb 14, 10:00 a.m. | Main Hall |

③ **Rules:** Each student must read at least 3 books during the programme. After finishing each book, write a short review (100–150 words) and hand it in at the front desk. The top 10 readers with the best reviews will win a gift card worth 100 yuan.

④ **Note:** Parents are welcome to join the Opening and Closing Ceremonies. The library is closed on Mondays. For questions, call 020-8836-5577 or email info@sunshinelib.com.

⑤ We hope to see you this winter. Let's enjoy the magic of reading together!

Sunshine Library  
November 15, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the Winter Reading Programme start?
   A. December 31, 2024.
   B. January 6, 2025.
   C. February 14, 2025.
   D. November 15, 2024.

2. How can students sign up for the programme?
   A. Only at the front desk.
   B. Only online.
   C. At the front desk or online.
   D. By calling the library.

3. What must each student do during the programme?
   A. Read at least 5 books.
   B. Write a 200-word review for each book.
   C. Read at least 3 books and write a short review for each.
   D. Watch 3 movies and write reviews.

4. When is the library closed?
   A. On Sundays.
   B. On Saturdays.
   C. On Mondays.
   D. On Fridays.

5. What can we learn from the passage?
   A. Only students can attend the Opening Ceremony.
   B. The programme lasts about six weeks.
   C. Every student will get a gift card.
   D. The programme costs 100 yuan per person.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "From January 6th to February 14th, 2025, we invite students aged 12–15 to join us."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出项目从1月6日开始。A项12月31日是报名截止日期，不是开始日期；C项2月14日是结束日期；D项11月15日是通知发布日期。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格中 "How to join" 一行 "Sign up at the front desk or online at www.sunshinelib.com"

**【解析】** 表格明确列出两种报名方式：前台或网上。A项只说前台，以偏概全；B项只说网上，也不完整；D项打电话报名在原文中无依据。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Each student must read at least 3 books during the programme. After finishing each book, write a short review (100–150 words)..."

**【解析】** 原文要求每人至少读3本书，每本写100-150字的书评。A项"至少5本"与原文不符；B项"200字"与原文"100-150词"不符；D项"看3部电影"属于无中生有。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The library is closed on Mondays."

**【解析】** 原文明确说图书馆周一闭馆。A项周日、B项周六、D项周五均与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "From January 6th to February 14th, 2025" 及第④段 "Parents are welcome to join the Opening and Closing Ceremonies."

**【解析】** 从1月6日到2月14日约为六周，B项正确。A项原文说家长也可以参加开幕和闭幕式，并非只有学生；C项只有前10名最佳书评获得礼品卡，不是每人都有；D项原文明确说免费（Free）。故选B。
