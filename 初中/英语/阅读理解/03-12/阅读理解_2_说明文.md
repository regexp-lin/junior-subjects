# 阅读理解 第2篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① When you see a bee flying around, you might feel scared and try to run away. But did you know that bees are some of the most important creatures on Earth? Without bees, our world would be a very different place.

② Bees play a key role in growing the food we eat. When a bee visits a flower to collect nectar, it also picks up tiny grains of pollen on its body. When the bee flies to another flower, some of this pollen falls off. This process is called pollination, and it is necessary for many plants to produce fruits and seeds. In fact, about one third of the food we eat — including apples, strawberries, and tomatoes — depends on bee pollination.

③ Bees are important for the environment, too. By pollinating wild plants, bees help forests and grasslands stay healthy. These plants provide food and shelter for many other animals. So when bee populations are healthy, the whole ecosystem benefits.

④ Sadly, the number of bees around the world has been dropping in recent years. There are several reasons for this. Pesticides used on farms can poison bees. Climate change is making some areas too hot or too dry for bees to live in. Also, as cities grow bigger, bees are losing the fields and gardens where they used to find food.

⑤ The good news is that everyone can help. Planting flowers in your garden or on your balcony gives bees more places to find food. Avoiding the use of harmful chemicals in your garden also helps. Even something as simple as putting out a small dish of sugar water can help a tired bee recover. Remember: saving the bees means saving ourselves.

---

**注释：** creature 生物；nectar 花蜜；pollen 花粉；pollination 授粉；ecosystem 生态系统；pesticide 农药；balcony 阳台

---

### 题目

1. What is pollination according to the passage?
   A. The process of pollen moving between flowers with the help of bees.
   B. Bees collecting honey for their own food.
   C. Bees building their homes inside flowers.
   D. Flowers growing into big trees.

2. About how much of our food depends on bee pollination?
   A. About half.
   B. About one quarter.
   C. About one third.
   D. Nearly all of it.

3. Which of the following is NOT a reason for the decrease in bee numbers?
   A. The use of pesticides on farms.
   B. Climate change making areas too hot or dry.
   C. The growth of cities taking away fields and gardens.
   D. Too many flowers being planted in gardens.

4. The underlined word "recover" in Paragraph ⑤ probably means "________."
   A. 飞走
   B. 恢复
   C. 躲藏
   D. 睡觉

5. What is the passage mainly about?
   A. How bees make honey for people to eat.
   B. The best ways to grow flowers at home.
   C. Why bees are important and how we can help them.
   D. Why most people are afraid of bees.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2-4句 "When a bee visits a flower to collect nectar, it also picks up tiny grains of pollen on its body. When the bee flies to another flower, some of this pollen falls off. This process is called pollination..."

**【解析】** 原文详细描述了授粉过程：蜜蜂采集花蜜时身上沾上花粉，飞到另一朵花时花粉落下，这个过程就是授粉。A 项"花粉在蜜蜂帮助下在花朵之间转移的过程"准确概括了这一定义。B 项"蜜蜂为自己采集蜂蜜"——采蜜是蜜蜂的行为，不是授粉的定义；C 项"蜜蜂在花朵里筑巢"属于无中生有；D 项"花朵长成大树"与授粉的概念无关。故选 A。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "In fact, about one third of the food we eat — including apples, strawberries, and tomatoes — depends on bee pollination."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出我们所吃食物的约三分之一依赖蜜蜂授粉。C 项"About one third"与原文完全一致。A 项"约一半"数据偏高；B 项"约四分之一"数据偏低；D 项"几乎全部"严重夸大了原文数据。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向选择）

**【定位】** 第④段列举的原因："Pesticides used on farms can poison bees." "Climate change is making some areas too hot or too dry for bees to live in." "...bees are losing the fields and gardens where they used to find food."

**【解析】** 本题要求选出"不是"蜜蜂数量减少原因的选项。A 项"农场使用农药"、B 项"气候变化导致地区过热或干燥"、C 项"城市扩张占去了田地和花园"均在第④段中有明确对应。D 项"花园里种了太多花"不仅未在原文中提及，而且与第⑤段"种花帮助蜜蜂"的建议相矛盾——种花是帮助蜜蜂的方式，不是导致蜜蜂减少的原因。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 逆向选择题需要逐一将选项与原文对照，用排除法找出无依据或矛盾的选项。本题中 D 项不仅原文无依据，还与文中其他信息（种花帮助蜜蜂）构成矛盾，是典型的干扰项。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后第2句 "Even something as simple as putting out a small dish of sugar water can help a tired bee recover."

**【解析】** 根据上下文，一只疲倦的蜜蜂（a tired bee）喝了糖水后能够"recover"。联系生活常识和语境：疲倦→补充能量→恢复体力，因此"recover"应指"恢复"。B 项"恢复"最符合语境。A 项"飞走"——飞走是恢复后可能的行为，但不是 recover 本身的含义；C 项"躲藏"与"补充糖水"的情境不符；D 项"睡觉"虽然与疲倦有关，但放一碟糖水的目的不是让蜜蜂睡觉，而是帮它恢复体力。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 词义猜测题需要关注目标词前后的因果关系。本题中"tired"（疲倦）→"sugar water"（糖水补充能量）→"recover"（?），根据逻辑链可推断"recover"意为"恢复体力"。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文结构：第①段引出蜜蜂的重要性 → 第②段蜜蜂对食物生产的作用 → 第③段蜜蜂对环境的作用 → 第④段蜜蜂数量减少的原因 → 第⑤段我们可以采取的行动。

**【解析】** 全文首先从食物和环境两方面论述了蜜蜂的重要性，然后分析了蜜蜂数量下降的原因，最后呼吁人们采取行动帮助蜜蜂。C 项"为什么蜜蜂很重要以及我们如何帮助它们"最全面地涵盖了文章的两大核心内容。A 项"蜜蜂如何为人们制造蜂蜜"——文章讨论的是授粉而非产蜜过程，以偏概全；B 项"在家种花的最佳方法"——种花只是第⑤段建议中的一条，远不能概括全文；D 项"为什么大多数人害怕蜜蜂"——怕蜜蜂只是第①段的引入话题，不是文章主旨。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 说明文的主旨概括需要覆盖全文各段的核心内容。注意选项中的"and"或"both"结构——如本题 C 项包含"why important"和"how we can help"两个方面，恰好对应文章前半部分（重要性）和后半部分（行动建议）。
