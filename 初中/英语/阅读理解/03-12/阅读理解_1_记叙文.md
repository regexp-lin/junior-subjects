> **03-12 第1篇**

# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Chen Wei didn't like his new English teacher, Mr. Wang. Unlike his old teacher who was always smiling and gentle, Mr. Wang was serious and strict. He made the students read aloud every morning and checked their homework very carefully. If anyone made a mistake, Mr. Wang would ask them to correct it three times. Chen Wei thought Mr. Wang was too hard on them.

② One day, Mr. Wang asked each student to give a short speech in English in front of the class. Chen Wei was nervous and made many mistakes. Some classmates laughed. Chen Wei felt embarrassed and sat down quickly. After class, he expected Mr. Wang to criticize him, but instead, Mr. Wang said, "You were brave to stand up there. That takes courage. Let's practice together after school."

③ For the next month, Mr. Wang spent 30 minutes after school every day helping Chen Wei practice speaking English. He taught him how to pronounce difficult words and how to speak with confidence. Mr. Wang never got angry when Chen Wei made mistakes. "Mistakes are your best teachers," he always said.

④ At the end-of-term English speaking competition, Chen Wei won second prize. When he walked off the stage, Mr. Wang was waiting for him with a warm smile — the first real smile Chen Wei had ever seen on his face. "I always knew you could do it," Mr. Wang said. Chen Wei's eyes filled with tears.

⑤ Years later, Chen Wei became an English teacher himself. On his desk, he kept a photo of Mr. Wang. He often told his students, "The teachers who push you the hardest are often the ones who care about you the most."

---

**注释：** strict 严格的；pronounce 发音；confidence 自信

---

### 题目

1. Why didn't Chen Wei like Mr. Wang at first?
   A. Because Mr. Wang was too young to teach.
   B. Because Mr. Wang didn't teach English well.
   C. Because Mr. Wang was serious and strict.
   D. Because Mr. Wang often laughed at students.

2. What did Mr. Wang say about Chen Wei's speech?
   A. He said Chen Wei should give up English.
   B. He said Chen Wei was brave to stand up.
   C. He said Chen Wei was the worst student.
   D. He said Chen Wei didn't need to practice.

3. What did Chen Wei win at the speaking competition?
   A. First prize.
   B. Third prize.
   C. No prize.
   D. Second prize.

4. What did Chen Wei do when he grew up?
   A. He became an English teacher.
   B. He became a doctor.
   C. He moved to another country.
   D. He stopped learning English.

5. What is the best title for the passage?
   A. My First English Speech
   B. How to Win a Speaking Competition
   C. A Strict Teacher Who Truly Cared
   D. Why English Is the Most Important Subject

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-3句 "Unlike his old teacher who was always smiling and gentle, Mr. Wang was serious and strict. He made the students read aloud every morning and checked their homework very carefully."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出与之前温柔爱笑的老师不同，王老师性格严肃且严格，要求学生每天早上大声朗读、仔细检查作业。C 项"因为王老师又严肃又严格"与原文完全吻合。A 项"因为王老师太年轻"原文未提及年龄；B 项"因为王老师教得不好"原文没有评价教学水平；D 项"因为王老师经常嘲笑学生"——笑陈伟的是同学而非老师，属于张冠李戴。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段倒数第2句 "Mr. Wang said, 'You were brave to stand up there. That takes courage.'"

**【解析】** 原文说明陈伟演讲出错后，王老师并没有批评他，而是肯定他"站上去就很勇敢，那需要勇气"。B 项"他说陈伟勇敢地站了上去"与原文对应。A 项"他说陈伟应该放弃英语"与王老师鼓励的态度完全相反；C 项"他说陈伟是最差的学生"属于无中生有；D 项"他说陈伟不需要练习"与原文"Let's practice together after school"矛盾。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "At the end-of-term English speaking competition, Chen Wei won second prize."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出陈伟在期末英语演讲比赛中获得了二等奖。D 项"Second prize"与原文一致。A 项"一等奖"与原文不符；B 项"三等奖"也不正确；C 项"没有获奖"与原文矛盾。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "Years later, Chen Wei became an English teacher himself."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明多年后陈伟自己也成了一名英语老师，桌上还保留着王老师的照片。由此可以推断，王老师对陈伟产生了深远的影响，使他走上了同样的教育道路。A 项与原文完全对应。B 项"成了医生"原文无依据；C 项"搬到了另一个国家"属于无中生有；D 项"停止学习英语"与他成为英语老师的事实完全矛盾。故选 A。

**【技巧点拨】** 虽然本题答案在原文中有直接表述，但题目设计的意图在于让学生理解王老师的影响——陈伟选择同一职业正是受到王老师的感染和启发。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "The teachers who push you the hardest are often the ones who care about you the most." 及全文情节线索。

**【解析】** 全文讲述了陈伟从不喜欢严格的王老师，到王老师主动帮他练习英语、最终在比赛中获奖，直至多年后自己也成为老师并感恩王老师的故事。核心主题是"严格的老师其实最关心学生"。C 项"A Strict Teacher Who Truly Cared"（一位真正关心学生的严格老师）最准确地概括了全文主旨。A 项"我的第一次英语演讲"以偏概全——演讲只是故事中的一个事件；B 项"如何赢得演讲比赛"偏离主旨——文章重点不在技巧，而在师生关系；D 项"为什么英语是最重要的科目"原文未做此判断。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 选择最佳标题时，应寻找贯穿全文的核心人物或核心关系。本文的核心人物是王老师，核心关系是师生之间从误解到感恩的情感变化。标题应反映这一核心线索。
