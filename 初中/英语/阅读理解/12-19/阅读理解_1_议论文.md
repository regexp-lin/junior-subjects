# 阅读理解（议论文）

## **Are After-School Classes Really Necessary?**

①Many students today go to after-school classes. They study English, maths, art, or music outside of school. Some parents think these classes help their children get ahead. Others believe they add too much pressure. So, are after-school classes really necessary? Let me share my thoughts.

②On the one hand, after-school classes can be helpful. If a student is weak in a subject, extra practice with a tutor can help them catch up. Some classes offer skills that schools do not teach—like piano or coding. They can open new interests and talents. For students who enjoy learning, these classes can be fun and rewarding.

③On the other hand, too many classes can cause problems. Students need time to rest and play. If every afternoon and weekend is full of classes, when do they relax? When do they spend time with family and friends? I have a classmate who goes to five after-school classes every week. She is always tired. She told me she has no time to read for fun or just lie down. That does not sound healthy.

④I think the key is balance. Not every student needs after-school classes. If you are doing well at school and you have hobbies you enjoy, maybe you do not need more. If you are struggling in one subject, one or two classes might help. But we should not fill every hour with study. Free time is important too. It helps us grow in other ways—creativity, social skills, and mental health.

⑤Parents and students should talk together. What does the child really need? What do they enjoy? Every child is different. There is no single "right" answer. The best choice is the one that fits the child, not the one that fits the neighbour's child.

## 题目

1. What is the passage mainly about?
   A. How to choose the best after-school class.
   B. Whether after-school classes are necessary and the need for balance.
   C. All students must take after-school classes.
   D. After-school classes are always bad.

2. According to paragraph 2, when can after-school classes be helpful?
   A. When students want to sleep more.
   B. When a student is weak in a subject or wants to learn new skills.
   C. When students have no friends.
   D. Only when they are free.

3. What problem does the writer mention about too many classes?
   A. They cost too much.
   B. Students have no time to rest, play, or be with family.
   C. Teachers get tired.
   D. Schools will close.

4. What does the writer think is the key?
   A. Take as many classes as possible.
   B. Balance—not everyone needs classes; free time matters too.
   C. Never take any after-school classes.
   D. Only study maths and English.

5. What does the writer suggest parents and students do?
   A. Copy what neighbours do.
   B. Talk together and choose what fits the child.
   C. Never discuss it.
   D. Always choose the cheapest class.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】主旨大意题

【定位】第①段及全文

【解析】
- 文章讨论课外辅导班是否必要，分析其利弊，强调平衡与个性化选择。
- A 偏重“如何选择”。C“必须上”、D“总是坏”均过于极端。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第②段

【解析】
- 第②段写：弱科可藉此补差；部分课程教授学校未开的技能，能拓展兴趣与天赋。
- A、C、D 未提及。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第③段

【解析】
- 第③段写：课程过多会压缩休息、玩乐、与家人朋友相处的时间；举例同学每周五门课、一直很累。
- A、C、D 未在文中作为主要问题提出。故答案为 B。

**4. 答案：B**

【题型】观点态度题

【定位】第④段

【解析】
- 第④段写：“I think the key is balance... Not every student needs after-school classes... Free time is important too.”
- A、C、D 与原文矛盾。故答案为 B。

**5. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第⑤段

【解析】
- 第⑤段写：家长和孩子应一起商量，根据孩子的需要和兴趣选择；“The best choice is the one that fits the child, not the one that fits the neighbour's child.”
- A 与“not the neighbour's child”矛盾。C、D 与原文不符。故答案为 B。
