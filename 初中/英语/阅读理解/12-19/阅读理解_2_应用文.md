# 阅读理解（应用文）

## **Healthy Eating Guide for Students**

①Eating well helps you stay healthy and focus in class. Here are some simple tips for students. Follow them, and you will feel better and study better too.

②**Eat a good breakfast.** Do not skip breakfast. Your body and brain need energy after a long night. A healthy breakfast can include eggs, bread, milk, or fruit. Avoid eating too much sugar in the morning. Candy and sweet drinks may give you a quick boost, but you will feel tired soon after.

③**Choose balanced meals.** Try to have a mix of different foods: grains (rice, noodles, bread), vegetables, fruit, and some meat or beans. Do not eat only one type of food. Too much fried food or junk food is bad for your health. Save them for occasional treats, not every day.

④**Drink enough water.** Many students forget to drink water. When you feel thirsty, you are already a bit dehydrated. Bring a water bottle to school. Drink small amounts often. Avoid too many soft drinks. They have a lot of sugar and can harm your teeth.

⑤**Food safety tips.** Wash your hands before eating. Check the date on packaged food—do not eat food that is expired. If food looks or smells bad, throw it away. When eating out, choose clean places. Your health comes first.

| Tip             | Key Point                                |
|-----------------|------------------------------------------|
| Breakfast       | Do not skip; include eggs, milk, fruit   |
| Balanced meals  | Mix of grains, vegetables, fruit, protein |
| Water           | Drink enough; bring a bottle; less soda   |
| Food safety     | Wash hands; check dates; avoid bad food   |

---

- dehydrated /ˌdiːhaɪˈdreɪtɪd/ adj. 脱水的

## 题目

1. What is this passage mainly about?
   A. How to cook food.
   B. Healthy eating tips for students.
   C. The best restaurants for students.
   D. How to lose weight.

2. Why should students eat breakfast?
   A. Breakfast is cheaper.
   B. The body and brain need energy after a long night.
   C. Teachers require it.
   D. It takes less time than lunch.

3. According to paragraph 3, what should a balanced meal include?
   A. Only fried food.
   B. A mix of grains, vegetables, fruit, and protein (meat or beans).
   C. Only candy and soft drinks.
   D. Only meat.

4. What does the writer say about soft drinks?
   A. They are the best choice for hydration.
   B. They have a lot of sugar and can harm your teeth.
   C. Students must drink them every day.
   D. They are cheaper than water.

5. Which is a food safety tip?
   A. Eat as much junk food as you want.
   B. Wash hands before eating; check dates; do not eat expired food.
   C. Never eat out.
   D. Only eat breakfast.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】主旨大意题

【定位】第①段及全文

【解析】
- 第①段点题：吃得好有助于健康和专注，后文围绕早餐、平衡膳食、饮水、食品安全等给出建议。
- A、C、D 非主旨。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第②段

【解析】
- 第②段写：“Your body and brain need energy after a long night.”
- A、C、D 未提及。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第③段及表格

【解析】
- 第③段与表格均写：应包含谷物、蔬菜、水果及肉类或豆类等蛋白质。
- A、C、D 与原文矛盾。故答案为 B。

**4. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第④段

【解析】
- 第④段写：“Avoid too many soft drinks. They have a lot of sugar and can harm your teeth.”
- A、C、D 与原文相反。故答案为 B。

**5. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第⑤段及表格

【解析】
- 第⑤段写：饭前洗手、查看包装日期、不吃过期或变质食物、外出选择干净店家。
- A、C、D 与原文矛盾。故答案为 B。
