# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Schools Offer More Vegetarian Options?**

① Many schools serve meat in their canteens every day. But in recent years, some people have suggested that schools should offer more vegetarian meals. They say it is healthier and better for the environment. Others disagree. So should schools promote vegetarian food? Let us look at both sides.

② Supporters of vegetarian options have several reasons. First, a plant-based diet can reduce the risk of heart disease and obesity. Many students eat too much meat and too few vegetables. Offering more vegetarian choices can encourage better eating habits. Second, raising animals for food uses a lot of land and water. Producing vegetables creates fewer greenhouse gases. So eating less meat can help protect the environment. Third, some students choose not to eat meat for religious or personal reasons. They deserve to have good meals at school too.

③ However, those who oppose the idea point out problems. Meat provides important nutrients like protein and iron that growing children need. If schools cut down on meat too much, some students might not get enough of these nutrients. Also, many students and parents simply prefer meat. Forcing vegetarian food on everyone could lead to waste — students might throw away food they do not like. Besides, vegetarian meals can cost more if they use special ingredients.

④ In my view, schools should not remove meat completely. Instead, they could add more vegetarian options so that students have a choice. For example, there could be both a meat dish and a vegetarian dish each day. Schools could also teach students about balanced diets. This way, everyone can eat what suits them, and no one is forced to change.

⑤ The key is balance. Schools should offer a variety of foods and help students make healthy choices, whether they prefer meat, vegetables, or both.

**注释：** vegetarian 素食的；obesity 肥胖；greenhouse gas 温室气体

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do supporters want more vegetarian options?
   A. Because meat is too expensive.
   B. Because it can be healthier and better for the environment, and some students need it for personal reasons.
   C. Because all students hate meat.
   D. Because schools want to save money.

2. What do opponents worry about?
   A. Vegetarian food tastes better.
   B. Students might not get enough protein and iron, and there could be more waste.
   C. There are too many vegetables.
   D. Meat is bad for health.

3. What does the writer suggest?
   A. Remove all meat from school canteens.
   B. Add more vegetarian options and let students choose.
   C. Only serve vegetables.
   D. Close the school canteen.

4. What does "plant-based diet" mean in Paragraph ②?
   A. A diet that includes only plants and no animal products.
   B. A diet of planting trees.
   C. A diet that grows plants.
   D. A diet for farmers only.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Schools should force all students to eat the same food.
   B. Schools should offer variety and help students make healthy choices.
   C. Vegetarian food is the only good choice.
   D. Meat should never be served.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "healthier and better for the environment... religious or personal reasons"
**【解析】** 支持理由包括健康、环保以及部分学生的宗教或个人需求。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "might not get enough of these nutrients... could lead to waste"
**【解析】** 反对者担心营养不足和食物浪费。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "add more vegetarian options so that students have a choice"
**【解析】** 作者建议增加素食选项，让学生有选择权。B项正确。A、C、D项与建议相反。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第②段 "plant-based diet" 后文 "eating less meat"
**【解析】** 指以植物为主、减少动物制品的饮食。A项准确。B、C、D项与文意不符。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "offer a variety of foods and help students make healthy choices"
**【解析】** 结尾强调提供多样食物并帮助学生做出健康选择。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
