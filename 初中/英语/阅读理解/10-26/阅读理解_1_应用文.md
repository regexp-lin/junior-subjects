# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Teen First Aid Training Course**

① The Red Cross Youth Centre is offering a first aid course for teenagers aged 12 to 16. Learning first aid can help you save lives in emergencies. Join us and become a young first aider!

**Course Information:**

| Item        | Details                          |
|-------------|----------------------------------|
| Dates       | November 16, 23, 30 (three Saturdays) |
| Time        | 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.           |
| Place       | Red Cross Building, 56 Peace Road|
| Fee         | 300 yuan (includes materials)    |
| Maximum     | 30 students                      |

② The course covers basic skills: how to stop bleeding, what to do when someone is choking, and how to perform CPR. You will also learn how to call for help correctly and keep calm in an emergency. All lessons include practice with manikins.

③ No previous experience is needed. Wear comfortable clothes and sports shoes. Bring a notebook and a pen. Registration closes on November 10th. To sign up, fill in the form at www.redcross-youth.org or visit our office from Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

④ All students who complete the course will receive a certificate. This certificate is recognised by many schools and can count towards your community service hours.

⑤ For questions, email training@redcross.org or call 020-55551234. Spaces are limited. Register now!

Red Cross Youth Centre  
October 22nd, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. Who can join the first aid course?
   A. Children under 10.
   B. Teenagers aged 12 to 16.
   C. Adults only.
   D. Anyone of any age.

2. How many Saturdays will the course run?
   A. One.
   B. Two.
   C. Three.
   D. Four.

3. What will students learn in the course?
   A. How to cook.
   B. How to stop bleeding, help choking, and perform CPR.
   C. How to swim.
   D. How to drive.

4. When does registration close?
   A. November 16th.
   B. November 10th.
   C. November 30th.
   D. December 1st.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. The course is free.
   B. Students who finish the course get a certificate that may help with community service.
   C. No certificate is given.
   D. The course is only for doctors.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "a first aid course for teenagers aged 12 to 16"
**【解析】** 课程面向12–16岁青少年。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Dates 为 "November 16, 23, 30 (three Saturdays)"
**【解析】** 课程在三个周六进行。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "how to stop bleeding, what to do when someone is choking, and how to perform CPR"
**【解析】** 课程内容包括止血、异物窒息急救和心肺复苏。B项准确。A、C、D项未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Registration closes on November 10th"
**【解析】** 报名截止于11月10日。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第④段 "students who complete the course will receive a certificate... can count towards your community service hours"
**【解析】** 完成课程可获得证书，且该证书可计入社区服务时长。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。
