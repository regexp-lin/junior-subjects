> **05-04 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Charlie was a dog that loved one thing more than anything else in the world: digging holes. He lived in a house with a huge, green backyard. For his owners, Mr. and Mrs. Parker, his digging was a terrible nightmare (噩梦). 

② Every morning, Mr. Parker would walk out to his carefully planted rose garden and find a new, deep hole waiting for him. "Charlie, no!" he would shout. Charlie would just sit there, wagging his tail happily, covered in dirt. 

③ Mrs. Parker tried everything to stop him. She bought him lots of chew toys (咀嚼玩具). She took him for long runs in the park to make him tired. She even built a small sandbox specifically for him to dig in. But Charlie ignored the sandbox. He preferred the thrill of digging in the wet, black soil near the tomato plants.

④ One Saturday morning, Charlie was digging his usual hole near the old oak tree. Suddenly, his paws hit something hard. It wasn't a rock. Charlie barked loudly. He kept digging until an old, rusty (生锈的) metal box appeared.

⑤ Hearing the barking, Mr. Parker ran out, ready to shout again. But when he saw the muddy box, he stopped. He pulled it out and opened it slowly. Inside the box were several black-and-white photos and a beautiful silver watch. 

⑥ It turned out the box belonged to Mr. Parker's grandfather, who had buried it fifty years ago as a "time capsule" (时间胶囊) and later forgot exactly where it was. Mr. Parker gently cleaned the photos with a smile and patted the dirty dog on the head. "Good boy, Charlie," he said softly. 

⑦ Charlie wagged his tail happily. However, the next morning, he went back to digging next to Mrs. Parker's roses. After all, old habits are hard to break.

**注释：** nightmare 噩梦；dirt 泥土/灰尘；thrill 刺激/乐趣；rusty 生锈的；capsule 胶囊

---

### 选择题

1. What was Charlie's favorite thing to do?
A. Chasing cats in the street.
B. Eating expensive dog food.
C. Digging holes in the backyard.
D. Sleeping on Mr. Parker's bed.

2. How did Mr. and Mrs. Parker feel about Charlie's hobby at first?
A. They thought it was very helpful for their garden.
B. They thought it was a terrible nightmare.
C. They were so happy that they bought him a silver watch.
D. They ignored it completely.

3. What did Mrs. Parker do to try to stop Charlie from digging in the garden?
A. She tied him to the old oak tree.
B. She sold him to another family.
C. She bought chew toys, took him for long runs, and built a sandbox.
D. She covered the garden with large stones.

4. What did Charlie find near the old oak tree?
A. A large bone.
B. A new chew toy.
C. A box full of gold coins.
D. An old, rusty metal box.

5. Why wasn't Mr. Parker angry when he saw the box?
A. Because he was too tired to shout.
B. Because it was the time capsule buried by his grandfather fifty years ago.
C. Because he realized the hole was very small.
D. Because the box belonged to his neighbor.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "Charlie was a dog that loved one thing more than anything else in the world: digging holes."

**【解析】** 开头第一句便指出，查理这条狗在这世上最喜欢做的事就是：在后院里挖洞（digging holes）。这与C选项完全对应。追猫（A）、吃狗粮（B）、睡床上（D）都不是它的最爱。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "For his owners, Mr. and Mrs. Parker, his digging was a terrible nightmare (噩梦)."

**【解析】** 开头，帕克夫妇对于狗狗爱挖洞的爱好有什么感受？文中描述这对于他们来说是场“可怕的噩梦（a terrible nightmare）”。B项正确复述。他们起初并没有觉得有帮助（A）或者感到高兴（C）或是无视（D）。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-4句 "She bought him lots of chew toys... She took him for long runs... She even built a small sandbox..."

**【解析】** 为了阻止查理在花园乱挖，帕克太太都尝试了什么办法？原文列举了三个措施：买咀嚼玩具、带它长跑消耗体力，甚至建了个专用的沙箱。这正是C项所述的内容。A绑树、B卖掉、D铺满大石头均不是文中所采取的措施。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第④段末尾两句 "...Charlie barked loudly. He kept digging until an old, rusty (生锈的) metal box appeared."

**【解析】** 查理在老橡树附近挖到了什么？文中明言随着他的吠叫和深挖，出现了一个又旧又生锈的金属盒子（an old, rusty metal box）。这恰好符合选项D的描述。大骨头（A）、新玩具（B）、装满金币的盒子（C）皆属于错误信息。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第⑥段第1句 "It turned out the box belonged to Mr. Parker's grandfather, who had buried it fifty years ago as a 'time capsule'..."

**【解析】** 当帕克先生看见盒子时怒火为何平息？第六段揭开了谜底：因为这个盒子竟是他祖父五十年前埋下的“时间胶囊”，里头装着老照片和银表。因此B选项（因为这是他祖父做的时间胶囊）为正确原因。A太累、C洞小、D邻居盒子皆非原因。故选B。
