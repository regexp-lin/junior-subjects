# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Sunnyville Community Sports Club**
**Join us and get moving!**

② Are you looking for a fun way to stay healthy? Do you want to meet new friends in our neighborhood? The Sunnyville Community Sports Club is the right place for you! We offer different sports activities for people of all ages. 

③ **Our Programs:**

- **Swimming (Every Tuesday & Thursday, 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM)**
We have a large indoor pool. Our excellent coaches will teach you how to swim safely. This class is for beginners only.
  
- **Basketball (Every Saturday, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)**
Enjoy playing basketball with your neighbors! We have both kids' teams (ages 8-12) and adults' teams. Please bring your own basketball shoes.
  
- **Yoga (Every Sunday morning, 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM)**
Start your Sunday with a peaceful mind and a healthy body. This class is held on the grass near the community center. We provide yoga mats (瑜伽垫).

④ **Membership Fees (会员费):**
- **Monthly Pass (月卡):** $30 (You can join any two programs)
- **Yearly Pass (年卡):** $250 (You can join all programs freely)
- **Single Class Ticket:** $5 per class

⑤ **Special Notice:**
If you sign up for a Yearly Pass before the end of this month, you will receive a free sports bag!

⑥ **How to Register (注册):**
Please visit our office on the first floor of the Sunnyville Community Center from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. You can also call Mr. Green at 555-6789 for more details.

**注释：** community 社区；coach 教练；beginner 初学者；mat 垫子；membership 会员资格；register 报名/注册

---

### 选择题

1. What is the main purpose of this poster?
A. To sell sports shoes and yoga mats.
B. To invite people to join the Sunnyville Community Sports Club.
C. To find a missing basketball in the neighborhood.
D. To tell people how to build a new indoor swimming pool.

2. Who can join the swimming class on Tuesday and Thursday?
A. Only professional swimmers.
B. Only kids aged 8-12.
C. Anyone who wants to learn, because it is for beginners only.
D. Only people who bring their own yoga mats.

3. When does the Yoga class take place?
A. Every Tuesday evening.
B. Every Saturday afternoon.
C. Every Sunday morning.
D. Every Monday morning.

4. If a person wants to buy a Monthly Pass (月卡), how much does it cost?
A. $5.
B. $30.
C. $250.
D. It is free for everyone.

5. What will you get if you buy a Yearly Pass before the end of this month?
A. A free sports bag.
B. A free pair of basketball shoes.
C. A free set of swimming clothes.
D. A free ticket to a professional basketball game.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨意图推断

**【定位】** 第①段标题和第②段 "Sunnyville Community Sports Club... The Sunnyville Community Sports Club is the right place for you! We offer different sports activities..."

**【解析】** 从显眼的标题“加入并动起来”以及第二段的招募词可以清楚地看出，这张海报的主要目的是号召人们加入桑尼维尔社区体育俱乐部（invite people to join the Sunnyville Community Sports Club）。A卖鞋、C找球、D建泳池全错。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段游泳项目（Swimming） "This class is for beginners only."

**【解析】** 题目问谁能参加周二周四的游泳课。依据原文介绍的条款：这个课程仅限初学者（beginners only）。选项C准确表达了这点。A（专业人员）正好相反；B是篮球对年龄的要求；D带瑜伽垫是瑜伽课的要求。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段瑜伽项目（Yoga） "Every Sunday morning, 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM"

**【解析】** 瑜伽课什么时间上？原文清晰标出在每个周日早晨（Every Sunday morning）。A（周二晚）是游泳的时间，B（周六下午）是篮球的时间，D（周一早）没有课。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段会员费 "Monthly Pass (月卡): $30"

**【解析】** 办理一个月卡（Monthly Pass）需要多少钱？价目表中清楚写着是30美元（$30）。A的5美元是单次课票价，C的250美元是年卡，D免费也是错的。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段特别通知 "If you sign up for a Yearly Pass before the end of this month, you will receive a free sports bag!"

**【解析】** 如果本月底前办理了年卡能得到什么？特别通知（Special Notice）中交代，会得到一个免费的运动包（a free sports bag）。与A选项表述一致。B、C、D赠品均系伪造。故选A。
