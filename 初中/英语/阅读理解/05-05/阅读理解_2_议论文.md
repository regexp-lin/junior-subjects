# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should parents pay their children for doing household chores (家务)? In many families, children get a small amount of money, or "allowance (零花钱)", when they wash the dishes, sweep the floor, or take out the rubbish. Some people agree with this method, but I strongly believe that children should do chores without being paid.

② The first reason is that keeping the house clean is a family responsibility (责任). A home belongs to everyone who lives in it. Parents work hard outside to make money and also do chores at home without getting extra pay. Therefore, children, as members of the family, should also share the housework naturally. By doing this, they learn to love and care for their family, not just work for money.

③ Secondly, paying for chores can give children the wrong idea about money and duties. If a child believes that every little task must be rewarded (奖励) with cash, what will happen in the future? When a teacher asks them to clean the blackboard, or when a friend needs help, they might ask, "How much will you pay me?" True helpfulness should come from the heart, not from the wallet.

④ Some parents argue that paying for chores is a good way to teach children how to manage money. While learning money management is important, parents can give a fixed (固定的) weekly allowance that is not tied to chores. This way, children can still learn how to save and spend money wisely.

⑤ In conclusion, doing chores should be seen as a basic duty of being part of a family. It teaches teamwork and responsibility. Parents should stop paying their children for taking out the rubbish and start teaching them the true meaning of family contribution (贡献).

**注释：** allowance 零花钱；responsibility 责任；reward 奖励；fixed 固定的；contribution 贡献

### 选择题

1. What is the main idea of paragraph ①?
A. The writer believes children should be paid highly for doing chores.
B. The writer strongly believes that children should do chores without being paid.
C. The writer thinks parents should do all the chores themselves.
D. The writer believes sweeping the floor is the hardest chore.

2. Why does the writer think keeping the house clean is a family responsibility?
A. Because a home belongs to everyone in the family, and children should share the housework naturally.
B. Because it is a legal requirement set by the government.
C. Because children have nothing else to do after school.
D. Because parents are too lazy to clean the house.

B. They will learn how to cook delicious food perfectly.

A. By giving them money every time they finish their homework.
B. By giving them a fixed weekly allowance that is not tied to chores.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 作者观点初定/细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "...but I strongly believe that children should do chores without being paid."

**【解析】** 在开篇提出“是否应该干家务给钱”的争论后，作者旗帜鲜明地摆明立场：我强烈认为孩子们做家务不应该拿报酬（children should do chores without being paid）。B选项真实传达了作者的观点。A恰好反了，C、D都不是第一段谈论的核心。故选B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段前三句 "The first reason is that keeping the house clean is a family responsibility. A home belongs to everyone who lives in it... children, as members of the family, should also share the housework naturally."

**【解析】** 为什么保持房屋清洁是家庭责任？作者解释道，家属于住在这里的每一个人，不仅父母在干，作为家庭成员的孩子也理所应当（naturally）分担家务。这是A选项所述。B政府硬性要求、C孩子没事干、D父母太懒，皆属于牵强附会。故选A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "If a child believes that every little task must be rewarded with cash... When a teacher asks them to clean... they might ask, 'How much will you pay me?'"

**【解析】** 如果总给孩子家务报酬会引发什么后果？作者在第三段假设：如果他们觉得每点小活都必有现金回报，那以后无论是帮老师擦黑板还是帮朋友，他们都可能会开口问“你打算付我多少钱？”。这会让他们产生凡事讲钱的错误观念。C项高度概括了这种可能性。A首富、B厨神、D以后不要零花钱了，均是错误解读。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "...parents can give a fixed (固定的) weekly allowance that is not tied to chores."

**【解析】** 有家长认为付钱能教理财，作者对此的反方建议是什么？他说哪怕理财重要，父母也可以给一种“固定的周零花钱（a fixed weekly allowance）”，且这种钱是不与做家务挂钩的（not tied to chores）。这完全吻合B选项。A写完作业给、C外头找真工作、D完全不给，均非作者的建议。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解/主旨归纳

**【定位】** 总结段第⑤段第2句 "It teaches teamwork and responsibility."

**【解析】** 结尾总结论点时，作者指出作为家庭一分子的基本责任，做家务能够教会孩子们“团队合作与责任感（teamwork and responsibility）”。这对应了选项C。绝非A教吵架、B教当保洁员、D教算钱。故选C。
