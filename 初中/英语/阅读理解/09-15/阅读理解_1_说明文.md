# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Have you ever seen a rainbow in the sky? A rainbow is a beautiful arc (弧) of colors that appears after rain when the sun is shining. It often shows seven colors: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo (靛蓝), and violet (紫罗兰色). But how does a rainbow form?

② A rainbow is formed by sunlight and water. When the sun shines through rain or mist (薄雾), the light passes through small drops of water in the air. Each drop acts like a tiny prism (棱镜). White light from the sun is actually a mix of all the colors. When it enters a water drop, the light bends (折射). Different colors bend at different angles, so they separate. We see red on the outside of the rainbow and violet on the inside.

③ The rainbow you see depends on where you stand. The sunlight passes through the water drops, and the colors reflect back to your eyes. If you move, the rainbow seems to move too — but actually, the rainbow is not in a fixed place. It is a trick of light. You can never reach the end of a rainbow because it is not a real object. It is only light that your eyes see in a certain way.

④ For a rainbow to appear, you need three things: sunlight, water drops in the air, and you must be standing with the sun behind you and the rain in front of you. That is why rainbows often appear in the morning or late afternoon when the sun is low in the sky. Sometimes you can see a rainbow near a waterfall or a fountain (喷泉) because the mist from the water creates the same effect.

⑤ Rainbows have been a symbol of hope and beauty for thousands of years. Next time you see one after a storm, you will know the science behind its magic!

**注释：** arc 弧；indigo 靛蓝；violet 紫罗兰色；mist 薄雾；prism 棱镜；bend 折射；fountain 喷泉

---

**Questions:**

1. How many colors does a rainbow usually show?
   A. Five.
   B. Six.
   C. Seven.
   D. Eight.

2. What are needed for a rainbow to form?
   A. Moonlight and clouds.
   B. Sunlight and water drops in the air.
   C. Fire and smoke.
   D. Snow and wind.

3. Why do different colors separate when light passes through a water drop?
   A. Because water drops are different sizes.
   B. Because different colors bend at different angles.
   C. Because the sun is very hot.
   D. Because the rain is heavy.

4. Why can we never reach the end of a rainbow?
   A. Because rainbows move too fast.
   B. Because rainbows are too far away.
   C. Because a rainbow is not a real object; it is only light that we see in a certain way.
   D. Because rainbows disappear quickly.

5. Where might you see a rainbow besides after rain?
   A. Inside a dark room.
   B. Near a waterfall or fountain because of the mist.
   C. At midnight.
   D. In the desert without water.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "It often shows seven colors: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet"

**【解析】** 原文明确说彩虹通常呈现七种颜色。A、B、D项数字均错误。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "A rainbow is formed by sunlight and water... light passes through small drops of water in the air" 及第④段 "you need three things: sunlight, water drops in the air"

**【解析】** 彩虹形成需要阳光与空气中的水滴。A项月光、C项火与烟、D项雪与风均不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Different colors bend at different angles, so they separate"

**【解析】** 原文说明不同颜色的光折射角不同，因而分离。A、C、D项与光学原理无关。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "You can never reach the end of a rainbow because it is not a real object. It is only light that your eyes see in a certain way"

**【解析】** 彩虹不是实物，只是光线在一定条件下进入眼睛形成的视觉现象。A、B、D项均未准确解释原因。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Sometimes you can see a rainbow near a waterfall or a fountain because the mist from the water creates the same effect"

**【解析】** 瀑布或喷泉附近的水雾可产生类似效果，也能形成彩虹。A、C、D项与形成条件不符。故选B。
