# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**A Surprise Cake for Mum's Birthday**

① Mum's birthday was on Saturday. My sister and I wanted to give her a surprise. We decided to make a birthday cake ourselves. We had never made a cake before, but we were excited to try.

② On Friday evening, when Mum was at work, we went to the shop and bought flour, eggs, sugar, and butter. We also bought some strawberries for the top. At home, we found Mum's cookbook and read the recipe carefully. "Preheat the oven to 180 degrees. Mix the flour, sugar, and butter. Add the eggs one by one." We followed every step. It was not easy. I mixed the batter while my sister greased the cake pan. Soon the kitchen was a bit messy, but we did not mind.

③ We put the cake in the oven and waited. Twenty minutes later, a warm smell filled the kitchen. The cake looked good! We let it cool down. Then we spread cream on top and decorated it with strawberries. We wrote "Happy Birthday Mum" with chocolate. It was not perfect, but we were proud of it. We hid the cake in the fridge.

④ On Saturday morning, Mum came into the kitchen. "What's that smell?" she asked. We smiled and brought out the cake. Her eyes went wide. "Did you make this for me?" We nodded. She hugged us both. "This is the best birthday surprise I've ever had!" She blew out the candles and we sang "Happy Birthday" together.

⑤ That day, we learned that the best gifts are not always the most expensive. A cake made with love can mean more than anything bought from a shop. Mum said she would remember that birthday forever.

**注释：** batter 面糊；grease 涂油；oven 烤箱

---

**Questions:**

1. When did the children go to buy the ingredients for the cake?
   A. On Friday evening when Mum was at work.
   B. On Saturday morning.
   C. On Sunday.
   D. They did not go to the shop.

2. What did the children buy from the shop?
   A. A ready-made cake.
   B. Only chocolate.
   C. Flour, eggs, sugar, butter, and strawberries.
   D. Candles and balloons.

3. What did the children do to make the cake?
   A. They asked Dad to help.
   B. They bought a cake and pretended to make it.
   C. They gave up because it was too hard.
   D. They followed the recipe in the cookbook step by step.

4. How did Mum feel when she saw the cake?
   A. She was surprised and very happy; she said it was the best birthday surprise.
   B. She was angry because the kitchen was messy.
   C. She did not like it.
   D. She asked them to buy a new one.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Expensive gifts are always better.
   B. Making cakes is always easy.
   C. Gifts made with love can be more meaningful than bought ones.
   D. Mum did not like surprises.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "On Friday evening, when Mum was at work, we went to the shop and bought flour, eggs, sugar, and butter"

**【解析】** 原文明确说周五晚上在妈妈工作时，孩子们去商店购买原料。A项正确。B、C、D项与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "we went to the shop and bought flour, eggs, sugar, and butter. We also bought some strawberries for the top"

**【解析】** 原文明确列出所购材料：面粉、鸡蛋、糖、黄油和草莓。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We found Mum's cookbook and read the recipe carefully... We followed every step"

**【解析】** 孩子们按食谱逐步操作。D项正确。A、B、C项与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Her eyes went wide... 'This is the best birthday surprise I've ever had!'"

**【解析】** 妈妈看到蛋糕时很惊讶、高兴，并说是最好的生日惊喜。A项正确。B、C、D项与原文不符。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "the best gifts are not always the most expensive. A cake made with love can mean more than anything bought from a shop"

**【解析】** 文末点明：用心制作的礼物比花钱买的更有意义。C项正确。A、B、D项与文意相反或不符。故选C。
