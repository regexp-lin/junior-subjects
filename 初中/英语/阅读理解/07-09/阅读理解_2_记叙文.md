> **07-09 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last Spring Festival, my cousin Lin Tao decided to try something new. Instead of just spending his holiday playing games at home, he wanted to earn some money by himself. With his parents' permission, he set up a small stall (摊位) at the local festival market, selling handmade paper lanterns.

② At first, Lin Tao felt nervous. He had never sold anything before and did not know if people would like his products. He spent two weeks making over fifty lanterns with different shapes — rabbits, flowers, and traditional Chinese patterns. His mother helped him fix the prices and taught him how to talk to customers politely.

③ The market opened on the first day of the new year. For the first hour, few people stopped at his stall. Lin Tao began to worry. Then an elderly woman came and asked about his lanterns. She bought three and said they would make lovely gifts for her grandchildren. Her kind words encouraged Lin Tao. Soon, more customers came. Some bought lanterns for their homes; others bought them as festival gifts.

④ By the end of the three-day market, Lin Tao had sold almost all his lanterns. He counted his money and found that he had earned about 800 yuan. More importantly, he learned many things: how to prepare products, how to price them, and how to communicate with different people. He also understood that earning money was not easy and that he should value what his parents had given him.

⑤ Lin Tao used part of the money to buy a gift for his grandmother and saved the rest for his school supplies. He said the experience was more valuable than the money itself. This year, he plans to return to the market with new ideas for his stall.

**注释：** stall 摊位；lantern 灯笼；permission 许可；pattern 图案；customer 顾客；supply 用品

---

### 选择题

1. What did Lin Tao decide to do during last Spring Festival?
   A. Sell handmade paper lanterns at the festival market.
   B. Play games at home as usual.
   C. Visit his grandmother with gifts.
   D. Help his mother at home.

2. According to paragraph ②, how did Lin Tao prepare for his stall?
   A. He bought lanterns from a factory.
   B. He made over fifty lanterns in two weeks with different shapes.
   C. He asked his father to make the lanterns.
   D. He only sold lanterns with rabbit shapes.

3. What happened when the elderly woman bought three lanterns?
   A. She said the lanterns were too expensive.
   B. She complained about the quality of the lanterns.
   C. She asked Lin Tao to lower the price.
   D. Her kind words encouraged Lin Tao and more customers came.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Lin Tao?
   A. He cared only about making money and did not learn anything.
   B. He decided never to sell anything again.
   C. He realized that earning money was hard and valued his parents' support more.
   D. He spent all the money on games.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. A student's experience of earning money at a festival market and what he learned.
   B. Spring Festival is the best time to buy lanterns.
   C. How to make paper lanterns step by step.
   D. Parents should always help their children with their business.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "he wanted to earn some money by himself... he set up a small stall at the local festival market, selling handmade paper lanterns"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明林涛想在节日市场上摆摊卖手工纸灯笼来赚零花钱。A 项正确。B 项"像往常一样在家玩游戏"与原文"Instead of just spending his holiday playing games"相反；C 项"带礼物看望奶奶"是赚到钱之后的行为；D 项"在家帮妈妈"文中未提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He spent two weeks making over fifty lanterns with different shapes — rabbits, flowers, and traditional Chinese patterns."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明林涛花了两周时间制作了五十多个不同形状的灯笼，包括兔子、花朵和传统中国图案。A 项"从工厂购买"与 handmade 矛盾；C 项"让父亲制作"原文说是自己制作；D 项"只卖兔子形状"与 different shapes 不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Her kind words encouraged Lin Tao. Soon, more customers came."

**【解析】** 老太太的善意话语鼓励了林涛，随后更多顾客前来光顾。A 项"说太贵"、B 项"抱怨质量"、C 项"要求降价"均与原文"kind words"和"encouraged"不符。D 项正确。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "he learned many things... He also understood that earning money was not easy and that he should value what his parents had given him."

**【解析】** 原文指出林涛领悟到赚钱不易，并更珍惜父母所给予的一切。A 项"只关心赚钱"与"learned many things"矛盾；B 项"再也不卖东西"与第⑤段"今年计划再次摆摊"矛盾；C 项正确；D 项"把钱全花在游戏上"与"买礼物给奶奶、存钱买学习用品"矛盾。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段引入林涛的决定，第②③段写筹备与经历，第④⑤段写收获与感悟。

**【解析】** 全文围绕林涛在节日市场上做小生意赚钱的经历及其成长收获展开，A 项"一名学生在节日市场赚钱的经历及所学到的东西"最能概括主旨。B 项侧重买灯笼时机；C 项侧重制作步骤；D 项侧重家长帮助，均非全文重点。故选 A。
