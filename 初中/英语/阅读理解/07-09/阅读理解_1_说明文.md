# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Lightning is one of the most powerful natural forces on Earth. It appears as a bright flash in the sky during a thunderstorm. Every year, lightning strikes the ground millions of times around the world. Understanding how lightning forms and how to stay safe can help protect you and your family.

② Lightning forms when ice particles and water droplets inside a cloud rub against each other. This rubbing creates an electric charge (电荷). The top of the cloud becomes positively charged, while the bottom becomes negatively charged. When the charge becomes strong enough, it tries to reach the ground or another cloud. The flash we see is actually the discharge of this built-up electricity, and it can reach a temperature of about 30,000 degrees Celsius — even hotter than the surface of the Sun.

③ When you hear thunder, it means lightning has already struck somewhere nearby. Thunder is the sound of the air expanding quickly because of the heat from the lightning. Light travels faster than sound, so we see the flash before we hear the thunder. You can estimate how far away the storm is by counting the seconds between the flash and the thunder. Every five seconds means the lightning is about one mile away. If the time is less than thirty seconds, you should seek shelter immediately.

④ To stay safe during a thunderstorm, never stand under a tree or near tall objects. Stay away from water, including swimming pools and lakes. If you are outdoors and cannot find a building or a car, crouch (蹲下) low with your feet close together. Do not lie flat on the ground. Indoors, avoid using phones with cords, taking showers, or touching metal pipes during a storm.

⑤ Lightning safety rules are simple but important. Remember: when thunder roars, go indoors. Following these steps can greatly reduce your risk of being hurt by lightning.

**注释：** charge 电荷；discharge 放电；positively charged 带正电的；negatively charged 带负电的；shelter 避难处；crouch 蹲下

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, how does lightning form?
   A. When the Sun heats the clouds too much.
   B. When ice and water rub together and create an electric charge.
   C. When the wind blows very fast through the clouds.
   D. When rain falls from the clouds to the ground.

2. What does the writer tell us about thunder in paragraph ③?
   A. Thunder comes before lightning in a storm.
   B. Thunder is the sound of air expanding because of lightning's heat.
   C. Thunder only happens when lightning strikes the ground.
   D. Thunder means the storm is far away and safe.

3. If you count ten seconds between the lightning flash and thunder, how far is the storm?
   A. About one mile.
   B. About two miles.
   C. About five miles.
   D. About ten miles.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about lightning safety?
   A. Standing under a tree keeps you dry and safe.
   B. Staying indoors means you are completely safe from lightning.
   C. Being near water increases the danger during a thunderstorm.
   D. Lying flat on the ground is the best way to avoid lightning.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To explain how lightning forms and how to stay safe.
   B. To describe beautiful lightning photos.
   C. To compare different types of storms.
   D. To encourage people to watch thunderstorms.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "Lightning forms when ice particles and water droplets inside a cloud rub against each other. This rubbing creates an electric charge."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明闪电是云层内的冰粒和水滴相互摩擦产生电荷而形成的。A 项正确。B 项"太阳过度加热云层"文中未提及；C 项"风快速吹过云层"属于无中生有；D 项"雨从云落到地面"与闪电成因无关。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Thunder is the sound of the air expanding quickly because of the heat from the lightning."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明雷声是闪电产生的热量使空气迅速膨胀所发出的声音。A 项"雷在闪电之前"与事实相反，光速快于声速；C 项"只有闪电击中地面才有雷"过于绝对，原文未限定；D 项"雷声意味着风暴很远很安全"与原文"少于三十秒应立刻避难"矛盾。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "Every five seconds means the lightning is about one mile away."

**【解析】** 原文指出每五秒约等于一英里距离。十秒则约等于两英里。A 项一英里对应五秒；B 项五英里对应二十五秒；C 项两英里对应十秒，正确；D 项十英里对应五十秒。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Stay away from water, including swimming pools and lakes."

**【解析】** 原文明确要求远离水体，包括游泳池和湖泊，说明靠近水会增加雷雨天的危险。A 项"树下避雨更安全"与原文"不要站在树下"矛盾；B 项"室内完全安全"过于绝对，原文还提到室内要避免使用带线电话等；C 项"平躺在地"与原文"不要平躺"相反；D 项正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第①段引出主题，第②段解释闪电成因，第③④段介绍安全知识，第⑤段总结。

**【解析】** 全文围绕闪电的形成机制与避雷安全知识展开，A 项"解释闪电如何形成以及如何保证安全"准确概括写作目的。B 项"描述闪电照片"、C 项"比较不同风暴类型"、D 项"鼓励观看雷暴"均与文章内容不符。故选 A。
