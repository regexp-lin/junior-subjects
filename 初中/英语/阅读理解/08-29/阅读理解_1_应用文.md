> **08-29 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Notice: School Art Festival 2024**

② Dear students and teachers,

③ The School Art Festival will be held from September 20 to September 22 this year. All students are welcome to take part! This is a great chance to show your talents and enjoy the beauty of art.

④ **Schedule:**

| Date | Activity | Place | Time |
|------|----------|-------|------|
| Sept 20 (Fri) | Opening Ceremony & Chorus (合唱) Competition | School Hall | 2:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m. |
| Sept 21 (Sat) | Dance Show & Painting Exhibition | Hall & Art Room | 9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m. |
| Sept 22 (Sun) | Drama Performance & Closing | School Hall | 10:00 a.m. – 12:00 noon |

⑤ **How to join:**
- **Chorus competition:** Sign up with your music teacher before Sept 10. Each class may send one team.
- **Dance show:** Register at the students' office by Sept 12. Individual or group performances are both welcome.
- **Painting exhibition:** Hand in your artworks to the art teacher by Sept 15. Theme: "My Beautiful School."
- **Drama:** Contact Mr. Wang (Room 301) for drama group registration before Sept 10.

⑥ **Important reminders:**
- Wear your school uniform on the opening and closing days.
- Parents are welcome to watch on Sept 21 and 22.
- No food or drinks are allowed in the school hall during performances.

⑦ We look forward to seeing you there!

School Student Union  
August 28, 2024

**注释：** chorus 合唱；exhibition 展览；register 登记；drama 戏剧

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the School Art Festival take place?
   A. From August 20 to August 22.
   B. From September 20 to September 22.
   C. From September 10 to September 15.
   D. From September 21 to September 22.

2. Where will the chorus competition be held?
   A. In the art room.
   B. In the students' office.
   C. In the school hall.
   D. In Room 301.

3. If a student wants to show his painting, what should he do?
   A. Sign up with the music teacher.
   B. Contact Mr. Wang before Sept 10.
   C. Hand in his artwork to the art teacher by Sept 15.
   D. Register at the students' office by Sept 12.

4. Which of the following is true according to the notice?
   A. Parents can only watch on the opening day.
   B. Students may eat snacks in the school hall.
   C. The painting theme is "My Beautiful School."
   D. Each class may send two teams for the chorus.

5. What is the main purpose of this notice?
   A. To ask students to pay for the festival.
   B. To invite students and teachers to join the School Art Festival.
   C. To tell students about the new school rules.
   D. To introduce the school hall.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The School Art Festival will be held from September 20 to September 22 this year."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出艺术节时间为9月20日至9月22日。A项为8月日期；C项为报名截止日期；D项仅为部分日期。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段表格 "Chorus Competition | School Hall"

**【解析】** 表格中合唱比赛的举办地为School Hall。A项为绘画展览地之一；B项为学生办公室；D项为戏剧报名联系处。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Painting exhibition: Hand in your artworks to the art teacher by Sept 15. Theme: 'My Beautiful School.'"

**【解析】** 原文明确说绘画展览需在9月15日前将作品交给美术老师。A项为合唱报名方式；B项为戏剧报名方式；D项为舞蹈报名方式。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Theme: 'My Beautiful School.'" 及第⑥段 "No food or drinks are allowed in the school hall"

**【解析】** 绘画主题确为"My Beautiful School"。A项与"Parents are welcome to watch on Sept 21 and 22"矛盾；B项与禁止饮食的规定矛盾；D项与"Each class may send one team"矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第③段 "All students are welcome to take part!" 和第⑦段 "We look forward to seeing you there!"

**【解析】** 通知旨在邀请师生参加学校艺术节。A项"收费"原文未提及；C项"新校规"与内容不符；D项"介绍礼堂"只是文中部分信息。故选B。
