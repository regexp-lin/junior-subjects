# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should parents limit (限制) how much time their children spend on the Internet? This question is being discussed in many families today. Some say yes, because too much screen time is bad for children's health and study. Others say no, because the Internet can be a useful tool for learning and communication.

② Those who support limits give several reasons. First, spending too much time online can harm children's eyes. Studies show that many teenagers now have eye problems because they stare at screens for hours each day. Second, the Internet can be a distraction (干扰). When students are online, they often watch videos or play games instead of doing homework. Third, some content on the Internet is not suitable for young people. Parents worry that their children may see something harmful.

③ However, those who disagree also have good points. They say the Internet is necessary for modern study. Students need to search for information, watch educational videos, and communicate with teachers and classmates online. Besides, learning to use the Internet wisely is an important skill for the future. If parents control everything, children may not learn to manage their own time. Some teenagers also feel that strict limits show a lack of trust.

④ So what can parents do? Experts suggest finding a balance. Parents can set clear rules, such as "no phones at dinner" or "only one hour of screen time on school nights." But they should also talk with their children about why these rules exist. When children understand the reasons, they are more likely to follow them. Parents can also encourage children to join sports or other activities so that they have less time to spend online.

⑤ In the end, there is no perfect answer. Every family is different. What works for one child may not work for another. The most important thing is that parents and children communicate openly and work together to find a healthy balance.

**注释：** limit 限制；distraction 干扰；suitable 合适的；balance 平衡

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, what is one reason to limit children's Internet time?
   A. The Internet is too expensive.
   B. Too much screen time can harm children's eyes.
   C. Children cannot learn from the Internet.
   D. The Internet has no useful content.

2. Why do some people think parents should NOT limit Internet time too strictly?
   A. Because children need to learn to use the Internet for study and the future.
   B. Because the Internet is always safe.
   C. Because children have no homework.
   D. Because parents have no right to set rules.

3. What do experts suggest parents do?
   A. Take away all electronic devices from children.
   B. Find a balance and set clear rules while explaining the reasons.
   C. Let children decide everything by themselves.
   D. Never allow children to use the Internet.

4. The word "balance" in Paragraph ④ probably means _______.
   A. something that is heavy
   B. a good mix of two things
   C. a type of exercise
   D. a rule made by schools

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. All families should use the same rules.
   B. Parents and children should work together to find what works for their family.
   C. There is only one correct way to solve this problem.
   D. Children should never use the Internet.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "First, spending too much time online can harm children's eyes."

**【解析】** 原文明确列举限制上网的原因之一是危害视力。A项"网络太贵"原文未提及；C项"无法从网络学习"与第③段观点矛盾；D项"没有有用内容"与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They say the Internet is necessary for modern study... learning to use the Internet wisely is an important skill for the future."

**【解析】** 反对严格限制者认为互联网是现代学习和未来技能所需。B项"网络总是安全"原文未如此断言；C项"没有作业"和D项"父母无权"均与原文不符。故选A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Experts suggest finding a balance. Parents can set clear rules... But they should also talk with their children about why these rules exist."

**【解析】** 专家建议寻找平衡、制定规则并解释原因。A项"拿走所有电子设备"过于极端；C项"完全让孩子决定"与专家建议矛盾；D项"永远不许上网"原文未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "Experts suggest finding a balance" 及后文对规则与沟通的描述

**【解析】** 根据上下文，"balance"指既有限制又有沟通、既保护又信任的合理搭配。A项"重物"、C项"运动类型"和D项"校规"均与语境不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Every family is different... The most important thing is that parents and children communicate openly and work together to find a healthy balance."

**【解析】** 文章指出每家情况不同，最重要的是父母与孩子坦诚沟通、共同找到健康平衡。A项"所有家庭应用相同规则"与"Every family is different"矛盾；C项"只有一种正确方法"与文意相反；D项"孩子绝不上网"属于过度推断。故选B。
