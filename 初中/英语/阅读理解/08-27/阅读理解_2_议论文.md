# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Many students love animals and wish they could have a pet. Dogs, cats, fish, or birds — pets can be lovely friends. But is it a good idea for middle school students to keep pets? People have different opinions.

② Those who support students having pets say that pets teach responsibility (责任). Students need to feed their pets, clean their cages or beds, and take them for walks. This helps students learn to care for others. Pets can also reduce stress. Playing with a cat or dog after a long day of study can make students feel relaxed. Besides, pets offer companionship (陪伴). Some students feel lonely, and a pet can be a good friend who is always there.

③ However, those who disagree point out several problems. First, students are busy with homework and exams. They may not have enough time to look after a pet properly. A pet that is not well cared for may become sick or unhappy. Second, pets cost money — food, toys, and visits to the vet (兽医) can be expensive. Not every family can afford this. Third, some students may be allergic (过敏的) to animal fur. Fourth, pets need a stable (稳定的) environment. If a student goes to a boarding school or moves house, what will happen to the pet?

④ So, what is the best approach? Experts suggest that students and parents discuss together before getting a pet. Consider the type of pet — a fish may be easier than a dog. Make sure the whole family agrees and is willing to share the work. Set clear rules: who will feed the pet, who will clean, and what happens during exams or holidays. If a student cannot promise to care for a pet for its whole life, perhaps it is better to wait.

⑤ In my view, whether students should have pets depends on the situation. If a student has time, support from family, and a real love for animals, a pet can be a wonderful experience. But if not, it is better to visit a friend's pet or volunteer at an animal shelter (收容所) instead. Being responsible means knowing when we are ready.

**注释：** responsibility 责任；companionship 陪伴；vet 兽医；allergic 过敏的；stable 稳定的；shelter 收容所

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, which is a benefit of having pets for students?
   A. Pets can do homework for students.
   B. Pets teach responsibility and can reduce stress.
   C. Pets cost nothing.
   D. Pets never get sick.

2. Why might some people think students should NOT have pets?
   A. Because pets are not cute.
   B. Because students may not have enough time to care for them properly.
   C. Because all students hate animals.
   D. Because pets are illegal.

3. What do experts suggest before getting a pet?
   A. Buy the most expensive pet.
   B. Students and parents should discuss and consider the type of pet and who will do the work.
   C. Keep it a secret from parents.
   D. Get a pet first and think later.

4. What does the writer think about students having pets?
   A. All students must have a pet.
   B. No student should ever have a pet.
   C. It depends on the situation; students need time, family support, and real love for animals.
   D. Only rich students can have pets.

5. What does "being responsible" mean in the last paragraph?
   A. Having many pets.
   B. Knowing when we are ready to care for a pet properly.
   C. Never visiting animal shelters.
   D. Giving pets to others when we are busy.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "pets teach responsibility... Pets can also reduce stress"

**【解析】** 原文明确说养宠物能培养责任感并缓解压力。A、C、D项原文未提及或与事实矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students are busy with homework and exams. They may not have enough time to look after a pet properly"

**【解析】** 反对者认为学生课业繁忙，可能无法充分照顾宠物。A、C、D项均非反对理由。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "students and parents discuss together before getting a pet. Consider the type of pet... Make sure the whole family agrees... Set clear rules"

**【解析】** 专家建议家长与孩子共同商议、考虑宠物类型、明确分工和规则。A、C、D项与建议不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "whether students should have pets depends on the situation. If a student has time, support from family, and a real love for animals, a pet can be a wonderful experience"

**【解析】** 作者认为是否养宠物应视情况而定，需有时间、家庭支持和真心喜爱动物。A、B、D项过于绝对或片面。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Being responsible means knowing when we are ready"

**【解析】** 文中"负责任"指懂得在准备好、有能力照料宠物时再养。A、C、D项与作者强调的"准备就绪"含义不符。故选B。
