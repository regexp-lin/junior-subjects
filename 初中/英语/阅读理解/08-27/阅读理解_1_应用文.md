# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Swimming Course for Kids — 2024**

② Learn to swim in a fun and safe way! Our swimming course is open to children aged 7–14. No experience needed — we have classes for both beginners and those who want to improve their skills.

③ **Course details:**

| Item | Information |
|------|--------------|
| Duration | July 8 – August 2, 2024 (4 weeks) |
| Schedule | Monday, Wednesday, Friday |
| Time | 9:00 a.m. – 10:00 a.m. |
| Place | City Sports Center Swimming Pool |
| Price | ¥800 (includes teaching and pool fee) |
| Class size | Max 12 students per class |

④ **What to bring:**
- Swimsuit and swimming cap (required)
- Goggles (泳镜) and towel
- Water bottle
- Sunscreen (防晒霜) if swimming outdoors

⑤ **Safety:**
- All our teachers are certified (持证) lifeguards
- Parents must stay nearby during the first two classes
- Students with health problems should tell us before the course starts
- In bad weather, classes may be cancelled; we will inform you by phone

⑥ **How to sign up:**
- Visit our office at City Sports Center (Room 101)
- Or call 8765-4321 before July 1st
- Bring your child's ID or student card
- Pay the fee when you register (报名)

⑦ Don't miss this chance! Swimming is a great skill for life. Sign up now!

**注释：** goggles 泳镜；sunscreen 防晒霜；certify 持证；register 报名

---

**Questions:**

1. How long does the swimming course last?
   A. 2 weeks.
   B. 3 weeks.
   C. 4 weeks.
   D. 6 weeks.

2. On which days are the classes held?
   A. Tuesday and Thursday.
   B. Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.
   C. Every day.
   D. Saturday and Sunday only.

3. How much does the course cost?
   A. ¥600.
   B. ¥800.
   C. ¥1000.
   D. Free.

4. What must parents do during the first two classes?
   A. Leave the pool area.
   B. Stay nearby.
   C. Swim with their children.
   D. Pay extra money.

5. How can people sign up for the course?
   A. Only online.
   B. By visiting the office or calling before July 1st.
   C. Only at the pool.
   D. By sending an email after August.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Duration | July 8 – August 2, 2024 (4 weeks)"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明课程为期4周。A、B、D项时长均错误。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Schedule | Monday, Wednesday, Friday"

**【解析】** 原文明确说课程在周一、周三、周五进行。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Price | ¥800 (includes teaching and pool fee)"

**【解析】** 原文明确标注费用为800元，包含教学和泳池使用费。A、C、D项金额错误。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Parents must stay nearby during the first two classes"

**【解析】** 原文明确要求家长在前两节课期间须在附近。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Visit our office at City Sports Center (Room 101)" 及 "Or call 8765-4321 before July 1st"

**【解析】** 原文提供两种报名方式：到办公室或电话报名，且需在7月1日前完成。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。
