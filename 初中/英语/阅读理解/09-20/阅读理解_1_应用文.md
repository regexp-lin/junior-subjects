---

**Questions:**

1. How many tour options does the advertisement offer?
   A. One.
   B. Two.
   C. Three.
   D. Four.

2. Which tour includes a boat ride on the Li River?
   A. Tour A.
   B. Tour B.
   C. Tour C.
   D. None of them.

3. If a family of two adults and one child (aged 10) choose Tour C, how much will they pay?
   A. ¥2,380.
   B. ¥4,760.
   C. ¥5,950. (2×2380 + 1×1190)
   D. ¥7,140.

4. How can you get a 10% discount?
   A. By calling after September 25.
   B. By booking before September 25.
   C. By going alone.
   D. By staying longer.

5. Which tour would be best for someone who wants to see ancient Chinese buildings?
   A. Tour B.
   B. Tour C.
   C. Tour A (Great Wall, Forbidden City, Temple of Heaven).
   D. Both B and C.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Choose from three amazing tours"

**【解析】** 广告提供三种旅行套餐选择。A、B、D 项数量错误。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Cruise on the Li River... Includes... boat ride"

**【解析】** Tour B 包含漓江游船项目。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Price: ¥2,380 per person (children under 12: half price)"

**【解析】** 两位成人：2×2380=4760；一名10岁儿童半价：1190；合计 5950 元。A、B、D 项计算错误。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Book before September 25 to get a 10% discount"

**【解析】** 9月25日前预订可享10%折扣。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "Visit the Great Wall, the Forbidden City, and the Temple of Heaven"

**【解析】** Tour A 参观长城、故宫、天坛，均为中国古代建筑。B 为桂林自然风光；C 为上海现代城市。故选 C。
