# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In many schools, students take tests almost every week. Some parents and teachers think there are too many tests. They argue that schools should reduce the number of exams. Others believe that frequent testing helps students learn. This topic has caused much discussion.

② Those who want fewer tests say that too much testing creates stress (压力). Students worry about their scores and lose sleep. Some become afraid of exams and perform worse than they should. Besides, time spent on tests could be used for more creative activities — projects, experiments, or group work. Testing mainly measures memory, not deep understanding or critical thinking. If we cut down exams, students might enjoy learning more and feel less anxious.

③ On the other hand, supporters of frequent testing argue that tests help students and teachers. Tests show what students have learned and what they still need to work on. Without regular exams, some students may not study until the last minute. Tests encourage them to review and practice. For teachers, test results provide useful feedback — they can see which topics need more teaching. In this view, reducing exams might lead to poorer study habits and lower achievement.

④ I think the key is balance. We do not need to choose between "many tests" and "no tests." Schools could reduce the number of formal exams but use other ways to check learning — for example, short quizzes (小测验), class discussions, or project presentations. This way, students still get feedback without feeling overwhelmed（不堪重负）. We could also make tests less stressful by allowing students to retake some parts or by focusing on improvement rather than just scores.

⑤ What matters most is that we help students learn and grow, not that we test them as often as possible. Finding the right balance will benefit everyone.

**注释：** stress 压力；quiz 小测验；overwhelmed 不堪重负；feedback 反馈

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people want fewer tests?
   A. Because tests are too easy.
   B. Because too much testing creates stress; time could be used for creative activities; tests mainly measure memory.
   C. Because teachers do not like grading.
   D. Because schools have no paper.

2. What do supporters of frequent testing believe?
   A. Tests are useless.
   B. Tests help show what students have learned, encourage review, and give teachers feedback.
   C. Students should never take tests.
   D. All tests should be very long.

3. What does the writer suggest as a balanced solution?
   A. Stop all tests.
   B. Reduce formal exams and use quizzes, discussions, or project presentations instead.
   C. Have a test every day.
   D. Only test the best students.

4. The word "feedback" in Paragraph ③ probably means _____.
   A. food for students
   B. information about how well someone is doing
   C. a type of exam
   D. a school building

5. What is the writer's main message?
   A. All tests should be banned.
   B. Finding the right balance between testing and other ways of checking learning benefits everyone.
   C. More tests are always better.
   D. Tests are the only way to learn.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "too much testing creates stress... time spent on tests could be used for more creative activities... Testing mainly measures memory"

**【解析】** 支持减少考试者认为：考试过多带来压力；考试占用的时间可用于创意活动；考试主要考查记忆。A、C、D 项非主要理由。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Tests show what students have learned... Tests encourage them to review and practice... test results provide useful feedback"

**【解析】** 支持频繁考试者认为：测验能反映学习情况、督促复习、为教师提供反馈。A、C、D 项与原文矛盾。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Schools could reduce the number of formal exams but use other ways to check learning — for example, short quizzes, class discussions, or project presentations"

**【解析】** 作者建议减少正式考试，改用小测验、课堂讨论、项目展示等方式评估学习。A、C、D 项与建议不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "test results provide useful feedback — they can see which topics need more teaching"

**【解析】** 根据上下文，反馈指教师能了解学生表现、判断哪些内容需加强教学，即"关于表现如何的信息"。A、C、D 项与语境不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第④、⑤段 "The key is balance... Finding the right balance will benefit everyone"

**【解析】** 作者强调在考试与其他评估方式之间找到平衡对大家都有益。A、C、D 项与作者观点矛盾。故选 B。
