# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Helping a New Student Fit Into Our Class**

① Last month, a new student named Lin Tao joined our class. He had just moved from another city. On his first day, he sat alone at the back. He did not talk to anyone. During break, he just read a book. I could see he felt lonely and shy.

② I decided to do something. I walked over and said, "Hi, I'm Wang Lei. Welcome to our class! Do you want to sit with us at lunch?" He looked surprised, then nodded. At lunch, I introduced him to some of my friends. We talked about school, hobbies, and the city. Lin Tao said he liked basketball. "Great! We play after school. Want to join us?" I asked. He smiled and said yes.

③ The next day, Lin Tao came to play basketball with us. He was good at it. Soon, everyone liked him. I also asked our teacher if Lin Tao could help with the class wall newspaper. He was good at drawing. The teacher agreed. Lin Tao made beautiful designs. The whole class was impressed. He started to feel more at home.

④ A few weeks later, Lin Tao came to me and said, "Thank you for being so kind. When I first came here, I was scared. I did not know anyone. But you made me feel welcome. Now I have friends. I feel happy." I felt warm inside. I had not done anything special. I had just reached out a hand.

⑤ That experience taught me something important. A new student may feel lost and lonely. A simple "hello," an invitation to lunch, or a chance to join an activity can make a big difference. We can all help someone feel they belong. Sometimes the smallest act of kindness can change someone's school life.

**注释：** impressed 留下深刻印象的；belong 归属

**Questions:**

1. Why did Lin Tao feel lonely on his first day?
   A. Because he was bad at studying.
   B. Because he had just moved and did not know anyone.
   C. Because he did not like the school.
   D. Because the teacher was strict.

2. What did Wang Lei do to help Lin Tao?
   A. He gave Lin Tao money.
   B. He invited Lin Tao to lunch, introduced him to friends, and asked him to play basketball.
   C. He did Lin Tao's homework.
   D. He changed seats with Lin Tao.

3. How did Lin Tao show his talent?
   A. In singing.
   B. In basketball and drawing.
   C. In running.
   D. In maths.

4. What did Lin Tao say to Wang Lei a few weeks later?
   A. He wanted to leave the school.
   B. He thanked Wang Lei for making him feel welcome and helping him find friends.
   C. He did not like basketball anymore.
   D. He was still scared.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. New students always fit in quickly without help.
   B. Small acts of kindness can help new students feel they belong.
   C. Only teachers can help new students.
   D. Basketball is the most important way to make friends.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "He had just moved from another city... He did not talk to anyone... I could see he felt lonely and shy"
**【解析】** 林涛刚从外地转来，不认识任何人，感到孤独害羞。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "I walked over... Welcome to our class! Do you want to sit with us at lunch?... I introduced him to some of my friends... Want to join us?"
**【解析】** 王磊主动打招呼、邀请共进午餐、介绍朋友并邀请打篮球。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "He was good at it [basketball]"；"He was good at drawing... made beautiful designs"
**【解析】** 林涛擅长打篮球和绘画。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Thank you for being so kind... you made me feel welcome. Now I have friends. I feel happy"
**【解析】** 林涛感谢王磊让他感到被接纳并结交了朋友。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
