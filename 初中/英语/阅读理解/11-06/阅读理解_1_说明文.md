# 阅读理解（说明文）

**How We Hear: The Work of Our Ears**

① We use our ears every day. We listen to music, to our friends, and to the sounds of nature. But how do our ears actually work? How does a sound travel from outside into our brain? Let us take a closer look.

② Sound is a type of wave. When someone speaks or a bird sings, the air vibrates. These vibrations travel through the air like waves in water. When the waves reach your ear, the journey of hearing begins. The outer part of your ear, called the pinna, catches these sound waves. It funnels them into the ear canal, a small tube that leads inside your head.

③ At the end of the ear canal, the sound waves hit the eardrum. The eardrum is a thin piece of skin that looks like a drum. When sound waves hit it, the eardrum vibrates. Behind the eardrum are three tiny bones. They are called the hammer, anvil, and stirrup. These bones pass the vibrations along, making them stronger. They send the sound into the cochlea, a small curled tube filled with liquid.

④ Inside the cochlea, there are thousands of tiny hair cells. When the liquid moves, these hairs move too. They turn the vibrations into electrical signals. These signals travel along the auditory nerve to the brain. The brain then interprets the signals as sounds. We "hear" when our brain understands these signals.

⑤ Our ears are delicate. Loud noises can damage the hair cells. Once damaged, they do not grow back. That is why we should protect our ears. Do not listen to music too loudly. Wear earplugs when there is loud noise. Taking care of our ears helps us enjoy the sounds of life for many years.

**注释：** cochlea 耳蜗；auditory 听觉的

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, what is sound?
   A. A type of light.

   A. Nothing.
   B. The eardrum vibrates.

   A. In the pinna.

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Sound is a type of wave... These vibrations travel through the air"
**【解析】** 声音是一种波，在空气中传播。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The outer part of your ear, called the pinna, catches these sound waves. It funnels them into the ear canal"
**【解析】** 耳廓收集声波并将其导入耳道。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "When sound waves hit it, the eardrum vibrates"
**【解析】** 声波撞击鼓膜时，鼓膜产生振动。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Inside the cochlea, there are thousands of tiny hair cells... They turn the vibrations into electrical signals"
**【解析】** 耳蜗内的毛细胞将振动转换为电信号。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Loud noises can damage the hair cells. Once damaged, they do not grow back... we should protect our ears"
**【解析】** 噪音会损伤毛细胞，损伤不可逆，因此需保护耳朵。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
