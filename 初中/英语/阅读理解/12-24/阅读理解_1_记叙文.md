> **12-24 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**My First Music Festival**

① Last summer, our city held a youth music festival. I had never been to one before. When my friend Lily asked me to go with her, I was a bit nervous. What if the music was too loud? What if I did not understand the songs? But I said yes. I am glad I did.

② We arrived at the park at 3 p.m. The stage was in the centre. Colourful lights hung everywhere. Thousands of young people stood in front of the stage. Some held signs with their favourite band's name. The air was full of excitement. Lily and I found a good place and waited.

③ At 4 p.m., the first band came on. They played pop music. Everyone started to sing along. I did not know all the words, but I could feel the energy. People waved their hands. Some danced. When the second band played rock music, the crowd became even more excited. The beat was strong. I found myself moving with the music too. I had never felt so free and happy.

④ Between the performances, we walked around. There were food stands selling snacks and drinks. We bought some juice and sat on the grass. We talked about the bands and the songs. Lily said she wanted to learn to play the guitar. I said maybe I would try drumming. We both laughed. The festival was not just about music. It was about friendship and dreams.

⑤ When the festival ended at 10 p.m., we were tired but happy. On the way home, Lily and I promised to come again next year. That night, I lay in bed and thought: sometimes you have to try new things. You might discover something wonderful.

**注释：** festival 音乐节；stage 舞台；beat 节拍；drum 打鼓

---

**Questions:**

1. How did the writer feel when Lily first asked her to the music festival?
   A. Very excited.
   B. A bit nervous.
   C. Sad.
   D. Angry.

2. What did the writer do when the second band played rock music?
   A. She left the park.
   B. She sat down and rested.
   C. She found herself moving with the music.
   D. She went to buy food.

3. What did Lily say she wanted to learn?
   A. To sing.
   B. To play the guitar.
   C. To dance.
   D. To play the drums.

4. According to the writer, what was the festival also about?
   A. Only music.
   B. Friendship and dreams.
   C. Food and drinks.
   D. Winning prizes.

5. What did the writer learn from the experience?
   A. Music festivals are too loud.
   B. Sometimes you have to try new things; you might discover something wonderful.
   C. Rock music is the best.
   D. Going home early is better.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "When my friend Lily asked me to go with her, I was a bit nervous."

**【解析】** 莉莉邀她去音乐节时她有点紧张。A项非常兴奋、C项伤心、D项生气均与"nervous"不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "When the second band played rock music... I found myself moving with the music too."

**【解析】** 第二支乐队演奏摇滚时，她不由自主地随音乐动起来。A项离开、B项坐下休息、D项去买吃的均与原文矛盾。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Lily said she wanted to learn to play the guitar."

**【解析】** 莉莉说想学吉他。A项唱歌、C项跳舞、D项打鼓是"我"可能想尝试的。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "The festival was not just about music. It was about friendship and dreams."

**【解析】** 音乐节不仅是音乐，也是友谊与梦想。A项仅音乐与"not just"矛盾；C项食物、D项获奖均非作者强调的。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "sometimes you have to try new things. You might discover something wonderful."

**【解析】** 作者体会到要勇于尝试新事物，可能发现美好。A、C、D项均未体现这一感悟。故选B。
