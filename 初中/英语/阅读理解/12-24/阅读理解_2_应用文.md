# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Online Etiquette Guide for Students**

① When you chat, post, or study online, you need to follow some rules. These rules are called "online etiquette" or "netiquette." They help you stay safe and respect others. Here are some tips for students.

**Do's and Don'ts:**

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use kind and polite words | Use rude or mean words |
| Think before you post | Share others' private information |
| Respect different opinions | Argue angrily online |
| Use proper grammar when possible | Use too many emojis in serious talks |
| Ask before sharing someone's photo | Post anything without checking |

② **Be kind.** Words online can hurt as much as words face to face. Before you send a message, ask yourself: Would I say this in person? If the answer is no, do not send it. Do not spread rumours. Do not make fun of others. If someone is unkind to you, try not to reply in anger. You can report bad behaviour to the website or a trusted adult.

③ **Protect your privacy.** Do not share your password with anyone. Do not tell strangers your real name, address, or phone number. Be careful about what you post. Once something is online, it can be hard to delete. Think about how your future teachers or employers might see your posts.

④ **Respect others' time.** When you send a message, do not expect an immediate reply. People may be busy. In group chats, do not send too many messages at once. Avoid typing in ALL CAPS — it often means you are shouting. Use "please" and "thank you" when you ask for help.

⑤ Following these rules makes the online world a better place for everyone. Good etiquette online is just as important as good manners in real life.

**注释：** etiquette 礼仪；netiquette 网络礼仪；rumour 谣言；privacy 隐私

---

**Questions:**

1. What does "netiquette" mean?
   A. Rules for playing games.
   B. Rules for online behaviour; online etiquette.
   C. Rules for doing homework.
   D. Rules for eating.

2. According to the guide, what should you do before posting?
   A. Ask a friend first.
   B. Think before you post.
   C. Post quickly.
   D. Use many emojis.

3. Why should you not share your password?
   A. Because it is too long.
   B. To protect your privacy.
   C. Because the teacher said so.
   D. Because no one needs it.

4. What does typing in ALL CAPS usually mean?
   A. You are very happy.
   B. You are shouting.
   C. You are tired.
   D. You are joking.

5. What is the main purpose of this guide?
   A. To teach students how to use social media.
   B. To help students stay safe and respect others online.
   C. To stop students from going online.
   D. To sell more computers.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "These rules are called 'online etiquette' or 'netiquette.'"

**【解析】** Netiquette 指网络行为规则、在线礼仪。A项游戏规则、C项作业规则、D项饮食规则均不正确。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Think before you post" 及第②段 "Before you send a message, ask yourself: Would I say this in person?"

**【解析】** 发帖或发消息前要三思。A项先问朋友、C项快速发、D项用很多表情均非建议。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "Protect your privacy. Do not share your password with anyone."

**【解析】** 不分享密码是为了保护隐私。A项太长、C项老师说的、D项没人需要均非主要原因。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Avoid typing in ALL CAPS — it often means you are shouting."

**【解析】** 全部大写通常表示在大喊。A项很开心、C项很累、D项开玩笑均与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "They help you stay safe and respect others."

**【解析】** 指南目的是帮助学生上网时保持安全、尊重他人。A项教用社交媒体过于狭隘；C项禁止上网、D项卖电脑均与原文矛盾。故选B。
