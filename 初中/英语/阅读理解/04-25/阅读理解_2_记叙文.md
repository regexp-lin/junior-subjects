# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① My grandmother, Maria, was famous in our neighborhood for her amazing strawberry jam. Every summer, when the strawberries in her garden turned bright red and juicy, all the neighbors would wait for her to start making it. The sweet smell of cooking strawberries would fill the whole street.

② I loved watching her make the jam. One afternoon, when I was eight years old, I asked her, "Grandma, why is your jam so much better than the ones we buy in the supermarket?" 

③ She smiled and stopped stirring (搅拌) the big pot. "There are three secrets, my dear," she said, showing me three fingers. "First, you must use only the freshest strawberries. If they are even a little bit bad, throw them away. Second, you must have patience (耐心). Good jam cannot be hurried. It needs to cook slowly on low heat for hours."

④ I waited for the third secret. She leaned in closer and whispered, "The most important secret is love. When you make food for people you care about, you pour a little bit of your heart into the pot. A machine in a factory cannot do that."

⑤ I thought about her words for a long time. Years later, when Grandma Maria was too old to cook, I decided to make the strawberry jam myself for her birthday. I picked the freshest berries, cooked them with endless patience, and thought of how much I loved her the whole time. 

⑥ When she tasted my jam, tears came to her eyes. "You have learned all my secrets," she said softly. That day, I realized that her words weren't just about making jam. They were a recipe (秘诀) for living a good life.

**注释：** jam 果酱；stir 搅拌；patience 耐心；whisper 小声说；recipe 烹饪法，秘诀

---

### 选择题

1. What was Grandma Maria famous for in her neighborhood?
A. Growing the biggest tomatoes.
B. Making amazing strawberry jam.
C. Baking delicious chocolate cakes.
D. Singing beautiful songs in the garden.

2. What did the writer ask the grandmother when he was eight?
A. "Why are the strawberries so small this year?"
B. "Can I eat all the jam in the pot?"
C. "Why is your jam so much better than the supermarket's?"
D. "How do you make the jam so fast?"

3. According to Grandma Maria, what is the FIRST secret to making good jam?
A. Using only the freshest strawberries.
B. Adding a lot of sugar and salt.
C. Cooking the jam very fast on high heat.
D. Buying the strawberries from the supermarket.

4. What did the grandmother say a factory machine CANNOT do when making jam?
A. Pick the best strawberries automatically.
B. Stir the big pot for many hours.
C. Pour a little bit of heart and love into the food.
D. Sell the jam at a high price to neighbors.

5. What did the writer learn at the end of the story?
A. That machines make better jam than humans.
B. That his grandmother's secrets were only useful for cooking.
C. That the grandmother's words were also a recipe for living a good life.
D. That making jam is too difficult and tiring.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1句 "My grandmother, Maria, was famous in our neighborhood for her amazing strawberry jam."

**【解析】** 开头很清楚地说明，作者的祖母因为她令人惊叹的自制草莓酱（amazing strawberry jam）而在社区周围闻名遐迩。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "...Grandma, why is your jam so much better than the ones we buy in the supermarket?"

**【解析】** 八岁那年，小作者问祖母：为什么您的果酱比我们在超市买的都要好那么多？这正好对应了C选项的问话内情。A、B、D三个问题文中小作者并没有提。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段大引号中 "First, you must use only the freshest strawberries."

**【解析】** 祖母说的三个秘诀中的第一条（FIRST secret）：你必须只用最新鲜的草莓。哪怕有一点坏都得扔掉。A项（只用最新鲜的草莓）无误地转写了这段原话。加糖、大火快煮（C项与"low heat for hours"矛盾）、去买（D），这些皆被原文推翻。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段中 "'The most important secret is love. When you make food for people you care about, you pour a little bit of your heart into the pot. A machine in a factory cannot do that.'"

**【解析】** 当谈及那最重要也是第三个“秘诀”——爱的时候，祖母悄声说：工厂里冷冰冰的机器是没法像你为牵挂之人做饭时那样，在锅里倾注进真心与爱的（pour a little bit of your heart into the pot）。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 故事结尾第⑥段后两句话 "That day, I realized that her words weren't just about making jam. They were a recipe for living a good life."

**【解析】** 作者多年后为老祖母煮出了饱含着新鲜、耐心和深深爱意的果酱。当时他不仅得到了祖母满含泪水的认可，还觉悟了：祖母当年传授的话不仅仅是怎么熬好果酱的秘方，还是一则怎么把日子过得醇厚甜美的生活秘诀（recipe for living a good life）。A项全盘否定机器，B项说只对烹饪有用，D说做酱太难等，全都悖离文意主旨中心。故选C。
