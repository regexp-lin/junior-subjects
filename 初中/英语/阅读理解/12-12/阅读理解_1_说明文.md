# 阅读理解（说明文）

## **How AI Helps Us in Daily Life**

①Artificial Intelligence (AI) is no longer something we only see in science fiction movies. Today, AI has become part of our everyday life. From the moment we wake up to the time we go to bed, AI tools can help us in many ways. Smartphones use AI to understand our voice commands. Maps apps use AI to find the best route when we travel. AI is everywhere around us.

②AI helps students with their studies too. Many learning apps use AI to give personal practice. When you answer a question wrong, the app remembers and gives you more questions like that. This helps you improve faster. Some apps can even check your English pronunciation and tell you how to say words better.

③At home, AI makes life easier. Smart speakers can play music, tell you the weather, or set alarms. Robot vacuums can clean the floor while you do homework. Smart fridges can remind you when food is going bad. These are all examples of AI working for us.

④However, we should use AI in a right way. AI is a tool, not a replacement for thinking. We still need to study hard and use our own brains. AI can help us save time, but we should not depend on it to do our homework for us. The best way is to use AI as a helper, not as a crutch.

⑤The future of AI is bright. Scientists are working on making AI even smarter and more helpful. As middle school students, we should learn about AI and how to use it well. This will prepare us for a world where AI plays a bigger role in our lives.

## 题目

1. What does the passage mainly talk about?
   A. How to use smartphones.
   B. How AI helps us in daily life.
   C. Science fiction movies about AI.
   D. The history of AI.

2. How does AI help students with their studies?
   A. It does homework for students.
   B. It gives personal practice based on mistakes.
   C. It replaces teachers in class.
   D. It only plays music for students.

3. According to paragraph 3, which of the following is NOT mentioned as an AI helper at home?
   A. Smart speakers.
   B. Robot vacuums.
   C. Smart fridges.
   D. Smart cars.

4. What does the writer think we should do with AI?
   A. Use it to do all our homework.
   B. Depend on it completely.
   C. Use it as a helper, not a crutch.
   D. Avoid using it at all.

5. What can we learn from the last paragraph?
   A. AI will replace human workers soon.
   B. Students need to learn how to use AI.
   C. AI will become less important in the future.
   D. Scientists have stopped improving AI.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】主旨大意题

【定位】全文，尤其是第①段

【解析】
- 文章开篇即指出 AI 已进入日常生活，并从学习、家居、正确使用方式、未来发展等多方面展开说明。
- A 项“如何使用智能手机”仅为第①段提及的一例，非主旨。C 项“科幻电影中的 AI”与第①段“no longer something we only see in science fiction”意思相反。D 项“AI 的历史”文中未提及。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第②段

【解析】
- 第②段明确说明：学习类 APP 会根据错误给出针对性练习（“When you answer a question wrong, the app remembers and gives you more questions like that”）。
- A 项“替学生做作业”与第④段“we should not depend on it to do our homework for us”矛盾。C 项“代替老师”和 D 项“只播放音乐”均未提及。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：D**

【题型】细节判断题

【定位】第③段

【解析】
- 第③段提到的家庭 AI 工具有：smart speakers、robot vacuums、smart fridges。
- D 项“智能汽车”文中未提及。A、B、C 均为原文内容。故答案为 D。

**4. 答案：C**

【题型】观点态度题

【定位】第④段

【解析】
- 第④段指出：AI 是工具，不是思维的替代；应把 AI 当助手而非拐杖（“The best way is to use AI as a helper, not as a crutch”）。
- A、B 与文中“不应依赖它做作业、完全依赖”相悖。D 项“完全不用”与文章提倡“正确使用”不符。故答案为 C。

**5. 答案：B**

【题型】推理判断题

【定位】第⑤段

【解析】
- 第⑤段建议：中学生应了解并学会正确使用 AI，为未来做准备（“we should learn about AI and how to use it well”）。
- A 项“很快取代人类”未提及。C 项“AI 将变得不重要”与“AI plays a bigger role”相反。D 项“科学家已停止改进 AI”与“Scientists are working on making AI even smarter”矛盾。故答案为 B。
