# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## **Learning to Manage My Time**

①Last year, I often stayed up late to finish my homework. I felt tired every morning and could not focus in class. My grades started to drop. My mother noticed and asked me, "Why don't you plan your time better?" I did not know how. She gave me a simple notebook and said, "Write down what you need to do each day. Then decide when to do each thing."

②At first, I thought it was silly. But I decided to try. Every Sunday evening, I wrote a weekly plan. I listed all my homework, club activities, and free time. I found that I had three hours of TV time every day but only one hour for homework. No wonder I always ran out of time! I cut my TV time to one hour and gave two hours to homework. The first week was hard, but I stuck to my plan.

③After a month, I began to see changes. I finished homework before 9 p.m. and went to bed at 10. I woke up feeling fresh. My teacher said my answers in class were much better. My mother smiled when she saw my improved report card. I felt proud of myself.

④Now I still use the notebook. I have learned that good time management is not about working all the time. It is about choosing what is important and saying no to things that waste time. I also leave some time for rest and fun. Life is not just about grades; it is about balance.

⑤I want to tell other students: start with a simple plan. You do not need a fancy app or a big change. A notebook and fifteen minutes each week can make a big difference. Try it, and you might surprise yourself!

---

**生词注释**
- focus /ˈfəʊkəs/ v. 集中注意力
- balance /ˈbæləns/ n. 平衡
- fancy /ˈfænsi/ adj.  fancy 花哨的

## 题目

1. Why did the writer's grades start to drop at first?
   A. He did not like his teacher.
   B. He spent too much time watching TV and could not focus.
   C. His mother did not help him with homework.
   D. He had too many club activities.

2. What did the writer's mother suggest?
   A. Buying a new computer.
   B. Writing down tasks and planning when to do them.
   C. Quitting all club activities.
   D. Staying up later to finish homework.

3. What change did the writer make in the first week?
   A. He stopped watching TV completely.
   B. He reduced TV time and gave more time to homework.
   C. He quit his club activities.
   D. He asked his teacher for less homework.

4. What did the writer learn about time management?
   A. You must work all the time.
   B. It is about choosing what is important and leaving time for rest.
   C. Grades are the only important thing.
   D. A fancy app is necessary.

5. What does the writer want to tell other students?
   A. They need expensive tools to manage time.
   B. A simple plan with a notebook can help a lot.
   C. Time management does not work for students.
   D. They should study all day and night.

---

## 答案与解析

**1. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第①段

【解析】
- 第①段说明：作者常熬夜写作业，早上很累、上课无法集中，成绩下降（“I often stayed up late... I felt tired every morning and could not focus... My grades started to drop”）。第②段补充：以前每天看 3 小时电视，只留 1 小时做作业。
- A 项不喜欢老师、C 项母亲不帮忙、D 项社团活动太多均未作为主因提及。故答案为 B。

**2. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第①段

【解析】
- 母亲建议：“Write down what you need to do each day. Then decide when to do each thing”（把要做的事写下来，再决定什么时候做）。
- A、C、D 均未在文中出现。故答案为 B。

**3. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第②段

【解析】
- 第②段写：将看电视时间从 3 小时减到 1 小时，把做作业时间增加到 2 小时（“I cut my TV time to one hour and gave two hours to homework”）。
- A 项“完全停止看电视”与“减到 1 小时”不符。C、D 未提及。故答案为 B。

**4. 答案：B**

【题型】细节理解题

【定位】第④段

【解析】
- 第④段指出：好的时间管理不是一直工作，而是分清重要的事、拒绝浪费时间的事，并留出休息和娱乐时间（“about choosing what is important... leave some time for rest and fun... balance”）。
- A 与“not about working all the time”矛盾。C 只强调成绩，不符“balance”。D 与第⑤段“You do not need a fancy app”矛盾。故答案为 B。

**5. 答案：B**

【题型】主旨/建议题

【定位】第⑤段

【解析】
- 第⑤段建议：从一个简单的计划开始，不需要花哨的 APP，一个本子和每周 15 分钟就能带来很大改变（“A notebook and fifteen minutes each week can make a big difference”）。
- A 与“do not need a fancy app”矛盾。C、D 与全文主旨相反。故答案为 B。
