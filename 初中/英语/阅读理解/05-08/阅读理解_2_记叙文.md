# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① In a busy city, there was a small bakery (面包店) owned by Mrs. Higgins. She baked the most delicious bread in the neighborhood. Every morning, the sweet smell of fresh bread floated down the street, waking everyone up with a smile. However, Mrs. Higgins had one strict rule: whatever was not sold by the end of the day was to be thrown away. "Stale (不新鲜的) bread ruins my good name," she always said.

② A young boy named Tim lived with his sick mother in a poor building nearby. They were very poor, and sometimes Tim went to bed hungry. Every evening, when Mrs. Higgins closed her shop, Tim would stand across the street, watching her throw big bags of unsold bread into the rubbish bin. It broke his heart.

③ One cold evening in winter, Tim gathered all his courage (勇气) and walked into the bakery just as Mrs. Higgins was closing. "Excuse me, ma'am," Tim said softly, shivering (发抖) from the cold. "Could I please have one of the breads you are going to throw away? My mother is very hungry."

④ Mrs. Higgins stopped and looked at the dirty, shivering boy. She frowned (皱眉). "I only sell fresh bread. Giving away stale bread ruins my reputation (名声)." She grabbed a bag of day-old bread and marched towards the back door. Tim's eyes filled with tears, and he turned to leave.

⑤ "Wait!" Mrs. Higgins called out suddenly. She looked at the bag in her hand, then at the freezing boy. For the first time, she realized that her pride in her "good name" was less important than a child's hunger. 

⑥ She put the bag of old bread down. Instead, she walked to the glass counter, took out two freshly baked, warm meat pies, and handed them to Tim. "Tell your mother these are fresh from Mrs. Higgins," she said gently. "And come back tomorrow morning. I need a smart boy to help me sweep the floor before school, and the pay is a hot breakfast."

⑦ Tim smiled brightly, holding the warm pies like a treasure. From that evening on, fewer bags of bread ended up in the rubbish bin, and Mrs. Higgins' bakery became known not just for the best bread, but for the kindest heart.

**注释：** bakery 面包店；stale 不新鲜的；ruin 毁坏；courage 勇气；shivering 发抖；frown 皱眉；reputation 名声

---

### 选择题

1. What was Mrs. Higgins' strict rule about her bread in paragraph ①?
A. She only sold bread to rich people.
B. Whatever was not sold by the end of the day must be thrown away.
C. She always gave free bread to everyone in the morning.
D. She only baked one type of bread every day.

2. What did Tim usually do every evening when the bakery closed?
A. He sneaked (溜进) into the shop to steal money.
B. He stood across the street, watching Mrs. Higgins throw away unsold bread.
C. He helped Mrs. Higgins sweep the floor.
D. He threw stones at the bakery's windows.

3. Why did Tim walk into the bakery on that cold winter evening?
A. To ask if he could have some of the bread she was going to throw away.
B. To buy the most expensive meat pie for his mother.
C. To borrow some money from Mrs. Higgins.
D. To complain about the sweet smell of the bread.

4. Form paragraph ④ and ⑤, what changed Mrs. Higgins' mind when she was marching to the back door?
A. She found out she threw away her keys by mistake.
B. She realized the boy was actually a rich prince.
C. She realized her pride in her good name was less important than a child's hunger.
D. She thought the old bread was filled with poison.

5. What did Mrs. Higgins do for Tim at the end of the story?
A. She gave him the bag of stale bread and asked him to leave.
B. She called the police to take him away.
C. She gave him fresh, warm meat pies and offered him a morning job for a hot breakfast.
D. She gave him the recipe (食谱) to bake his own bread.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后两句 "However, Mrs. Higgins had one strict rule: whatever was not sold by the end of the day was to be thrown away."

**【解析】** 希金斯太太在面包店立下的严格规定是什么？第一段明言：任何一天结束时还没卖掉的面包都要被扔掉（because “Stale bread ruins my good name”）。这符合B项的意思。A只卖富人、C早晨乱送、D只烤一种面包均非其规矩。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "Every evening, when Mrs. Higgins closed her shop, Tim would stand across the street, watching her throw big bags of unsold bread into the rubbish bin."

**【解析】** 每天傍晚关店时，穷苦的小男孩蒂姆通常在做什么？他总是站在街对面，眼巴巴看着希金斯太太把大袋未售出的面包扔进垃圾桶（这让他很心碎）。B选项是对这一场景的准确描述。A溜进去偷钱、C帮扫地、D扔石头皆非其行为。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "'Could I please have one of the breads you are going to throw away? My mother is very hungry.'"

**【解析】** 那个寒冷的冬夜，蒂姆鼓起勇气走进店里是为了干啥？他小声地请求能否把要扔掉的面包给他一个，因为生病的老母亲很饿。A选项（想索要打算扔掉的面包）符合他的请求。B买最贵的、C借钱、D抱怨气味都不符合。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "For the first time, she realized that her pride in her 'good name' was less important than a child's hunger."

**【解析】** 希金斯太太拎着包走向后门时，又为什么喊了停并改变主意？文中给出了她的内心转折：她第一次意识到，她对所谓“好名声”的骄傲，其实远远没有一个孩子的饥饿感来得重要。C选项完美重述了这一觉醒过程。A扔错钥匙、B男孩是王子、D旧面包有毒等都是无稽之谈。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段第2、4句 "...took out two freshly baked, warm meat pies, and handed them to Tim... 'And come back tomorrow morning. I need a smart boy to help me sweep the floor before school, and the pay is a hot breakfast.'"

**【解析】** 故事结尾，老板娘为蒂姆做了什么？她放下了要扔的旧面包，从柜台里拿了两个刚出炉的温热肉饼给他，并且雇佣他明早来扫地，酬劳是一顿热气腾腾的早餐（offered him a morning job for a hot breakfast）。选项C精准概括了她的善举。A只给旧面包并赶走、B报警、D给食谱均与结局不符。故选C。
