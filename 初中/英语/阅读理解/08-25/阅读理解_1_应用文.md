# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **English Corner — Welcome Everyone!**

② Do you want to improve your English speaking? Join our English Corner! We meet every **Thursday afternoon from 4:00 to 5:00** in the school garden. All students in Grades 7–9 are welcome. No need to sign up — just come when you are free!

③ **What we do:**
- Practise speaking English in small groups
- Discuss interesting topics (e.g., hobbies, festivals, school life)
- Play word games and sing English songs
- Share your ideas — mistakes are OK, we learn together!

④ **This month's topics:**
- Week 1: My favourite book
- Week 2: How I spend my weekends
- Week 3: A place I want to visit
- Week 4: My dream job

⑤ **Rules:**
- Try to speak only in English (it is fine if you mix a little Chinese when you are stuck)
- Be kind and listen to others
- Bring a notebook and pen to write down new words
- No phones during the activity

⑥ **Where:** School garden, near the reading pavilion (亭子)  
**When:** Thursdays, 4:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.  
**Contact:** Miss Chen (English teacher) — Room 302

⑦ Come and have fun with English! See you there!

**注释：** pavilion 亭子

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the English Corner take place?
   A. Every Monday afternoon.
   B. Every Thursday afternoon from 4:00 to 5:00.
   C. Every Friday evening.
   D. Every Saturday morning.

2. What do students do at the English Corner?
   A. Only take tests.
   B. Practise speaking, discuss topics, play games, and sing songs.
   C. Watch movies only.
   D. Do homework together.

3. What is the topic for Week 3?
   A. My favourite book.
   B. How I spend my weekends.
   C. A place I want to visit.
   D. My dream job.

4. According to the poster, what should students bring?
   A. A phone.
   B. A notebook and pen.
   C. Food and drinks.
   D. A dictionary only.

5. Where exactly is the English Corner held?
   A. In the library.
   B. In Room 302.
   C. In the school garden, near the reading pavilion.
   D. In the classroom building.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We meet every Thursday afternoon from 4:00 to 5:00"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明英语角于每周四下午4:00–5:00举行。A、C、D项时间或星期均错误。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段所列活动 "Practise speaking... Discuss interesting topics... Play word games and sing English songs"

**【解析】** 原文列出了练习口语、讨论话题、玩游戏、唱英文歌等活动。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Week 3: A place I want to visit"

**【解析】** 原文明确标注第三周主题为"我想去的地方"。A、B、D项分别为第1、2、4周主题。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Bring a notebook and pen to write down new words"

**【解析】** 原文要求带笔记本和笔记录新词。A项"手机"与"No phones"矛盾；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Where: School garden, near the reading pavilion"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明地点为校园花园、阅读亭附近。A、B、D项均为错误地点。故选C。
