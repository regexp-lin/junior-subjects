# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Morning Run Competition — Rules and Arrangements**

② Our school will hold the annual (每年一度的) morning run competition on October 15. All classes from Grade 7 to Grade 9 will take part. Please read the following rules carefully.

③ **Competition Rules:**
- Each class forms one team. All students in the class must join (except those with a doctor's note).
- Students must wear the school sports uniform and sports shoes.
- The run is 800 metres for girls and 1000 metres for boys.
- Students must run in their class queue. No pushing, no running in the wrong direction.
- The class whose last student crosses the finish line first wins. Teamwork is important!
- Any class that breaks the rules will lose points or be disqualified (取消资格).

④ **Schedule:**
| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 7:30 a.m. | All classes gather on the playground |
| 7:45 a.m. | Headmaster's speech |
| 8:00 a.m. | Grade 7 runs first, then Grade 8, then Grade 9 |
| 9:30 a.m. | Results and awards |
| 10:00 a.m. | Competition ends |

⑤ **Awards:**
- First, second, and third place for each grade
- "Best Team Spirit" award for the class with the best unity (团结) and attitude
- Every student who finishes will receive a certificate (证书)

⑥ **Important Notes:**
- Bring a water bottle. Do not eat a big meal one hour before the run.
- If you feel unwell during the run, stop and tell a teacher at once.
- Parents are welcome to watch from the stands (看台). Do not run onto the track.

**注释：** annual 每年一度的；disqualify 取消资格；unity 团结；certificate 证书；stand 看台

---

**Questions:**

1. Who can take part in the morning run competition?
   A. Only Grade 9 students.
   B. All classes from Grade 7 to Grade 9 (students with a doctor's note may not join).
   C. Only boys.
   D. Only teachers.

2. How does a class win the competition?
   A. The fastest student wins.
   B. The class whose last student crosses the finish line first wins.
   C. The class with the most students wins.
   D. The class that runs the longest distance wins.

3. When does Grade 8 run?
   A. At 7:30 a.m.
   B. After Grade 7 and before Grade 9 (around 8:00 a.m.).
   C. At 10:00 a.m.
   D. Only in the afternoon.

4. What award can a class win besides first, second, or third place?
   A. Best uniform.
   B. Best Team Spirit for the class with the best unity and attitude.
   C. Fastest runner.
   D. Most creative class.

5. What should a student do if he feels unwell during the run?
   A. Keep running until he finishes.
   B. Stop and tell a teacher at once.
   C. Go home alone.
   D. Sit down and wait for the competition to end.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "All classes from Grade 7 to Grade 9 will take part" 及 "All students in the class must join (except those with a doctor's note)"

**【解析】** 七至九年级所有班级参加，持医生证明者可豁免。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The class whose last student crosses the finish line first wins"

**【解析】** 以班级最后一名越过终点的先后定胜负。A、C、D 项与规则不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段表格 "Grade 7 runs first, then Grade 8, then Grade 9" 及 "8:00 a.m."

**【解析】** 8 点在七年级之后、九年级之前开跑。A、C、D 项时间或顺序错误。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "'Best Team Spirit' award for the class with the best unity and attitude"

**【解析】** 原文明确为"最佳团队精神"奖，表彰团结和态度最好的班级。A、C、D 项未提及。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "If you feel unwell during the run, stop and tell a teacher at once"

**【解析】** 身体不适应立即停止并告知老师。A、C、D 项与安全提示矛盾。故选 B。
