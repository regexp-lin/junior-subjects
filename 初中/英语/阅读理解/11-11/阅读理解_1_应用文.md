> **11-11 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**A Letter: Rational Consumption on Double 11**

① Dear students,

② Double 11 is coming. Many shops offer big discounts. It is easy to buy things we do not need just because they are cheap. We write to ask you to spend wisely.

③ **Think before you buy.** Ask yourself: Do I really need this? Will I use it? If the answer is no, do not buy it. A low price is not a reason to buy something useless.

④ **Make a list.** Before you shop, write down what you need. Stick to your list. Do not add things just because they look good or are on sale.

⑤ **Set a budget.** Decide how much you can spend. Do not use money for meals, books, or other important things. Never borrow money to shop.

⑥ **Avoid impulse buying.** Do not buy because everyone else is buying. Do not buy because you are bored. Wait a day. If you still want it tomorrow, then consider it.

⑦ We hope you enjoy the festival but stay rational. Smart shopping makes you happy. Blind buying often brings regret. Let us be wise consumers!

Student Union  
November 8th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. Who is this letter written for?
   A. Teachers only.
   B. Parents only.
   C. Students.
   D. Shop owners.

2. What is the main message of the letter?
   A. Do not shop on Double 11.
   B. Spend wisely and buy only what you need.
   C. Buy as much as you can.
   D. Never use money.

3. What does "Think before you buy" mean in Paragraph ③?
   A. Buy everything you see.
   B. Ask yourself if you really need it and will use it before buying.
   C. Never think about prices.
   D. Always buy the cheapest.

4. What should you do before shopping according to Paragraph ④?
   A. Borrow money from friends.
   B. Make a list of what you need and stick to it.
   C. Buy whatever is on sale.
   D. Do not make any plans.

5. What can we infer from the letter?
   A. Double 11 has no discounts.
   B. The writers want students to avoid impulse buying and regret.
   C. Students should never shop online.
   D. Making a list is unnecessary.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "Dear students"
**【解析】** 倡议书的对象是学生。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 主旨大意
**【定位】** 第②段 "We write to ask you to spend wisely"；全文
**【解析】** 全文呼吁理性消费，只买所需。B项准确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Ask yourself: Do I really need this? Will I use it? If the answer is no, do not buy it."
**【解析】** "三思而后买"即在购买前自问是否真的需要、是否会使用。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Make a list... write down what you need. Stick to your list."
**【解析】** 购物前应列出所需物品并坚持按清单购买。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Avoid impulse buying"；第⑦段 "Blind buying often brings regret"
**【解析】** 倡议者希望学生避免冲动消费及随之而来的后悔。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
