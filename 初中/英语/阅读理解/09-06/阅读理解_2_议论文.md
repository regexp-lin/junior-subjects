# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should middle schools cancel the mid-term exam? This question has been discussed a lot among students, parents, and teachers. Some people say yes, while others say no. Let us look at both sides.

② Those who support canceling the exam have their reasons. First, they think that students face too much pressure. With homework, after-school classes, and several big tests each term, many students feel tired and worried. Second, they believe that one exam cannot show a student's real ability. Some students are good at learning but get nervous during tests. Third, without the mid-term exam, students can spend more time exploring their interests, such as sports, music, or art.

③ However, those who oppose canceling the exam also make strong points. They argue that exams help students check what they have learned. Without regular tests, some students may not study hard until the final exam. The mid-term exam gives students a chance to find their weak points early and improve. Besides, exams prepare students for future challenges. In high school and university, students will take many important exams. Learning to face exams in middle school can help them get ready.

④ In my opinion, the mid-term exam should stay, but we need to make it less stressful. Schools could reduce the number of subjects tested or make the exam shorter. Teachers could also use different ways to judge students, such as class projects and daily performance, not only exam scores. The goal is to help students learn better, not to make them afraid of tests.

⑤ What do you think? Should we keep the mid-term exam or cancel it? There is no perfect answer, but talking about it helps us find a better way.

**Questions:**

1. What is the main topic of this passage?
   A. How to prepare for exams.
   B. Whether middle schools should cancel the mid-term exam.
   C. The history of school exams in China.
   D. How to reduce students' homework.

2. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people want to cancel the mid-term exam?
   A. Because the exam is too easy.
   B. Because students feel too much pressure and one exam cannot show real ability.
   C. Because teachers do not like grading exams.
   D. Because parents ask for it.

3. What do people who oppose canceling the exam believe?
   A. Exams are useless for learning.
   B. Exams help students check their learning and find weak points.
   C. All students hate exams.
   D. Schools should have more exams.

4. What is the writer's opinion in Paragraph ④?
   A. The mid-term exam should be cancelled at once.
   B. The exam should stay but be made less stressful.
   C. Students should not take any exams.
   D. Only the final exam is important.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer has found the perfect solution.
   B. Different people may have different views, and discussion can lead to better ideas.
   C. No one cares about this topic.
   D. The exam will certainly be cancelled soon.

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①段 "Should middle schools cancel the mid-term exam? This question has been discussed a lot..."

**【解析】** 文章开篇即提出核心议题：中学是否应取消期中考试，全文围绕正反观点及作者看法展开。A、C、D项均偏离主题。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "First, they think that students face too much pressure... Second, they believe that one exam cannot show a student's real ability."

**【解析】** 支持取消的理由包括：学生压力大、单次考试无法体现真实能力。B项准确概括。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They argue that exams help students check what they have learned... The mid-term exam gives students a chance to find their weak points early and improve."

**【解析】** 反对取消的观点认为考试能帮助学生检查所学、尽早发现薄弱点并改进。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文相反或未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "In my opinion, the mid-term exam should stay, but we need to make it less stressful."

**【解析】** 作者明确表示期中考试应保留，但应减轻其带来的压力。A项"立即取消"与作者观点相反；C、D项原文未表述。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "What do you think? Should we keep the mid-term exam or cancel it? There is no perfect answer, but talking about it helps us find a better way."

**【解析】** 结尾以设问引发思考，指出"没有完美答案"，但讨论有助于找到更好方法，可推知B项"不同人有不同看法，讨论能带来更好的思路"。A项"已找到完美方案"与"There is no perfect answer"矛盾；C、D项无依据。故选B。
