> **09-06 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**School Reading Week — Notice**

① Dear students and teachers,

② Our school will hold a Reading Week from October 14th to October 20th. The theme of this year is "Books Open the World." We hope every student will join in and enjoy the joy of reading.

**Activity Schedule:**

| Date | Activity | Place |
|------|----------|-------|
| Oct. 14 (Mon) | Opening Ceremony | School Hall |
| Oct. 15–17 | Book Fair (buy or exchange books) | Library |
| Oct. 16 (Wed) | Meet the Author (writer Mr. Zhang will visit) | Room 301 |
| Oct. 18 (Fri) | Reading Sharing Competition | Classroom 2-1 |
| Oct. 20 (Sun) | Closing & Prize-giving | School Hall |

**How to Join:**

- **Book Fair:** Bring your old books to exchange. Each student can get up to 3 new books.
- **Reading Sharing:** Prepare a 3-minute talk about your favorite book. Sign up before Oct. 15.
- **Prize:** The best 10 sharers will get a book as a gift.

③ For more information, please visit the school website or ask your class teacher.

④ We wish you a wonderful Reading Week!

School Library  
October 1st, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. How long does the Reading Week last?
   A. Five days.
   B. Six days.
   C. Seven days.
   D. Eight days.

2. Where will students meet the author Mr. Zhang?
   A. In the School Hall.
   B. In the Library.
   C. In Room 301.
   D. In Classroom 2-1.

3. What can students do at the Book Fair?
   A. Only buy new books.
   B. Buy or exchange books.
   C. Listen to the author's talk.
   D. Join the sharing competition.

4. If a student wants to join the Reading Sharing Competition, what must they do?
   A. Bring 3 old books to the library.
   B. Prepare a 3-minute talk and sign up before Oct. 15.
   C. Visit the school website first.
   D. Ask the author for help.

5. What is the purpose of this notice?
   A. To tell students about exam dates.
   B. To introduce the Reading Week activities and invite students to join.
   C. To ask teachers to buy books.
   D. To announce the closing of the library.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school will hold a Reading Week from October 14th to October 20th" 及表格

**【解析】** 从10月14日到10月20日，共7天（14、15、16、17、18、19、20）。A项5天、B项6天、D项8天均计算错误。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格中 "Meet the Author (writer Mr. Zhang will visit) | Room 301"

**【解析】** 表格明确显示与作者见面活动地点在Room 301。A项School Hall是开幕和闭幕式；B项Library是书展；D项Classroom 2-1是分享比赛。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Book Fair (buy or exchange books)" 及 How to Join 部分 "Bring your old books to exchange"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明书展可购买或交换书籍。A项"只能买新书"不全面；C项和D项是其他活动。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** How to Join 部分 "**Reading Sharing:** Prepare a 3-minute talk about your favorite book. Sign up before Oct. 15."

**【解析】** 参加阅读分享需准备3分钟演讲并须在10月15日前报名。A项是书展要求；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第②段 "We hope every student will join in" 及第④段 "We wish you a wonderful Reading Week!"

**【解析】** 本文旨在介绍读书周的活动安排并邀请师生参加。A项"考试日期"、C项"老师买书"、D项"图书馆关闭"均与写作目的不符。故选B。
