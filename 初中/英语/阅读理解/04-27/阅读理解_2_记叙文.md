# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Emma was a very shy 13-year-old girl. While her classmates were always chatting and laughing loudly in the hallways, Emma preferred to stay quiet, reading a book in the corner. She loved to draw, but she kept all her beautiful pictures hidden in a small blue notebook.

② One day, the school announced an art competition (比赛). The theme (主题) was "The Colors of Friendship." The winner's painting would be hung in the school library. When Emma heard this, her heart beat fast. She wanted to join, but she was too afraid. "What if people laugh at my drawing?" she thought.

③ Her art teacher, Mrs. Clark, noticed Emma looking thoughtfully at the poster. "You should enter, Emma. You have a real gift," Mrs. Clark said with an encouraging (鼓励的) smile. "Art is not about being perfect. It is about sharing your feelings with the world."

④ That evening, Emma opened her blue notebook. She remembered how Mrs. Clark's words made her feel warm. She picked up her brushes and started to paint. She didn't draw real faces; instead, she painted two colorful hands holding each other tightly (紧紧地). One hand was painted in cool blue and green, like a quiet river, and the other was in bright orange and yellow, like the warm sun. To Emma, this was true friendship—different, but supporting each other.

⑤ When the competition results came out a week later, Emma couldn't believe her eyes. Her painting, "The Warmth of Differences," won first prize! As her classmates clapped (鼓掌) for her in the hall, Emma finally smiled. She realized that she didn't need to be loud to be heard; her art had spoken for her. 

**注释：** hallway 走廊；hidden 藏起来的；competition 比赛；theme 主题；encouraging 鼓励的；tightly 紧紧地

---

### 选择题

1. What did Emma prefer to do in her free time at school?
A. Chatting loudly with her friends in the hallways.
B. Playing basketball on the playground.
C. Singing loudly in the music room.
D. Staying quiet and reading a book in the corner.

2. Why was Emma afraid to join the art competition at first?
A. Because she thought painting was too boring.
B. Because she had lost her small blue notebook.
C. Because she was worried that people might laugh at her drawing.
D. Because she didn't know what the theme of the competition was.

3. What did the art teacher, Mrs. Clark, tell Emma?
A. That she should focus on her math homework instead.
B. That her drawings were not good enough for the library.
C. That art is about sharing feelings with the world, not just being perfect.
D. That she must draw a picture of a large garden.

4. What did Emma paint for the competition according to paragraph ④?
A. A quiet river and a big sun.
B. Real faces of her classmates laughing.
C. A blue notebook sitting on a table.
D. Two colorful hands holding each other tightly.

5. What did Emma realize (意识到) at the end of the story?
A. That she needed to speak louder to make friends.
B. That winning a prize was the only way to be happy.
C. That she didn't need to be loud to be heard, as her art spoke for her.
D. That her classmates still didn't like her painting.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "...Emma preferred to stay quiet, reading a book in the corner."

**【解析】** 文章开头交代，相较于在走廊里大声嬉笑（A项）的同学，生性腼腆的Emma更喜欢安静地待在角落里读书（staying quiet and reading a book）。这与D选项完全吻合。故选D。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "'What if people laugh at my drawing?' she thought."

**【解析】** 当听到主题后她很想参加但很害怕，她内心的独白是：“万一人家嘲笑我的画怎么办？”（What if people laugh at my drawing）。因为这点担心，她起初怯步了。A（觉得太无聊）、B（本子丢了）、D（不知道主题）皆非其真实心态。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段末尾 "'Art is not about being perfect. It is about sharing your feelings with the world.'"

**【解析】** 艺术老师克拉克夫人发现了她的心思并鼓励她：去参加吧，你有真正的天赋（A项让她专心做数学非原话，B项更是背道而驰）。艺术并不是说一定要完美无瑕，而是向世界分享你的感受。这句劝导的话跟C选项表述一致。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第4句 "She didn't draw real faces; instead, she painted two colorful hands holding each other tightly (紧紧地)."

**【解析】** 那天晚上，她并非真切地画出同学面庞的笑脸（B项错误），而是画了两只颜色不同却紧紧握在一起的手（D项）来寓意虽然颜色不同彼此互补，这才是真正的友谊（一半蓝绿如河，一半橘黄如日，但画面主体是手）。A项（只画风景）及C项（画个蓝本子）均不妥。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 主旨归纳

**【定位】** 故事结尾第⑤段最后一句 "She realized that she didn't need to be loud to be heard; her art had spoken for her."

**【解析】** 在被全体同学鼓掌肯定之后，Emma终于领悟到：她其实并不需要扯着嗓门大声讲话才能被大家“听”见（受人瞩目与理解）；她的艺术作品已经成功地替她道出了心声（her art had spoken for her）。A（需要更大声交流）、B（只为获奖）和D（大家依然不喜欢她的画）全属错误结论。故选C。
