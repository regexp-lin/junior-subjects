# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Taking Care of My Sick Sister Alone**

① Last month, my parents had to travel for my grandfather's health. They would be away for three days. "Look after your little sister," Mum said. "She has a cold. Give her medicine at eight in the morning and eight at night. Call us if anything is wrong." I nodded. I was fourteen. I had never taken care of anyone alone before.

② On the first morning, I woke up early. My sister Lily was still asleep. Her face was a bit red. I touched her forehead — it was warm. I found the medicine and a glass of water. "Lily, time to take your medicine," I said softly. She opened her eyes and made a face. "I don't want to." "You have to. Mum said so." She drank it. I felt a little proud.

③ I made breakfast — simple porridge and an egg. Lily ate a little. I read her a story. She fell asleep again. I cleaned the house and did the laundry. It was harder than I thought. When evening came, I gave her the medicine again. I called Mum. "She's fine," I said. "We're doing OK."

④ On the second day, Lily felt better. She wanted to play. I played board games with her. We drew pictures. She laughed. I realised that taking care of her was not just about medicine. It was about being there, being patient, and making her feel loved.

⑤ When my parents came home, Lily ran to Mum. "Brother took good care of me!" Mum hugged me. "Thank you. You did a great job." I learned that responsibility is not always easy, but when you care for someone you love, it can also be rewarding.

**注释：** porridge 粥；laundry 洗衣

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer have to take care of his sister?
   A. Because his sister wanted to play.
   B. Because his parents had to travel for grandfather's health.
   C. Because he was bored.
   D. Because his sister asked him to.

2. What medicine schedule did the writer follow?
   A. Once a day.
   B. At 8 a.m. and 8 p.m.
   C. Only when Lily asked.
   D. Three times a day.

3. What did the writer do besides giving medicine?
   A. Nothing.
   B. Made breakfast, read stories, cleaned, and played with Lily.
   C. Only slept.
   D. Went out to play.

4. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Taking care of others is always boring.
   B. Responsibility can be hard but rewarding when you care for someone.
   C. Medicine is not important.
   D. Parents should never leave home.

5. What can we infer about the writer?
   A. He did not care about his sister.
   B. He took his responsibility seriously and tried his best.
   C. He wanted his parents to stay away longer.
   D. He never helped at home.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B****【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "Look after your little sister... They would be away for three days"
**【解析】** 父母因祖父健康需出差三天，嘱作者照顾妹妹。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "Give her medicine at eight in the morning and eight at night"
**【解析】** 早晚八点各服药一次。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②③④段 做早餐、读故事、清洁、洗衣、玩游戏等
**【解析】** 作者除喂药外还做饭、读故事、做家务、陪玩。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "responsibility is not always easy, but when you care for someone you love, it can also be rewarding"
**【解析】** 作者体会到责任虽难但照顾所爱之人也很有收获。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 全文，作者按时喂药、做饭、陪伴、主动汇报
**【解析】** 作者认真尽责、尽力照顾妹妹。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
