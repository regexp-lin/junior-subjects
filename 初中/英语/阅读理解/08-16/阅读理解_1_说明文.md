# 阅读理解（说明文）

① China's high-speed railway, also known as "gaotie," has become one of the country's proudest achievements. By 2024, China had built more than 45,000 kilometers of high-speed rail lines — the longest network in the world. These trains can travel at speeds of up to 350 kilometers per hour, making them among the fastest on Earth.

② The first high-speed railway in China opened in 2008. It connected Beijing and Tianjin, covering a distance of 120 kilometers in just 30 minutes. Before that, the same journey by regular train took about two hours. Since then, the network has grown quickly. Today, high-speed trains connect most major cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Chengdu. A trip from Beijing to Shanghai, which used to take over ten hours, now takes only about four and a half hours.

③ High-speed trains have changed the way Chinese people travel. Many people now choose the train over the airplane for trips under 1,000 kilometers because it is more convenient. You can buy tickets on your phone and go through the station quickly with just your ID card. The trains are clean, comfortable, and run on time. There is also Wi-Fi on many trains, so passengers can work or watch videos during the ride.

④ The benefits go beyond convenience. High-speed railways help smaller cities grow. When a new station opens in a small town, more tourists and business people come to visit. Local shops and hotels do better, and young people find more job opportunities. This helps reduce the gap between big cities and the countryside.

⑤ China is still building more high-speed lines and developing even faster trains. Scientists are working on maglev (磁悬浮) trains that could reach 600 kilometers per hour. The story of China's high-speed railway shows what can happen when technology, hard work, and big dreams come together.

**注释：** network 网络；convenient 方便的；opportunity 机会；reduce 减少；maglev 磁悬浮

---

**Questions:**

1. How long is China's high-speed rail network?
   A. More than 35,000 kilometers.
   B. More than 45,000 kilometers.
   C. About 120,000 kilometers.
   D. About 350,000 kilometers.

2. When did the first high-speed railway in China open?
   A. In 2004.
   B. In 2006.
   C. In 2008.
   D. In 2010.

3. Why do many people choose the train over the airplane for trips under 1,000 kilometers?
   A. Because the train is cheaper than the airplane.
   B. Because the train is more convenient.
   C. Because the airplane is not safe.
   D. Because the airplane does not fly to small cities.

4. What can we learn from Paragraph ④?
   A. High-speed railways only help big cities.
   B. Small towns do not want high-speed stations.
   C. High-speed railways help smaller cities develop.
   D. Young people prefer to leave small towns.

5. What is the best title for this passage?
   A. The History of Trains in the World
   B. China's High-Speed Railway: Fast and Far
   C. How to Buy a Train Ticket Online
   D. Maglev Trains: The Future of Travel

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "By 2024, China had built more than 45,000 kilometers of high-speed rail lines"

**【解析】** 原文明确提到中国已建成超过45,000公里的高铁线路。A项"35,000公里"数字错误；C项"120,000公里"和D项"350,000公里"均与原文不符，属于无中生有。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "The first high-speed railway in China opened in 2008."

**【解析】** 原文直接说明中国第一条高铁于2008年开通。A项"2004年"、B项"2006年"和D项"2010年"均与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Many people now choose the train over the airplane for trips under 1,000 kilometers because it is more convenient."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明原因是高铁更方便。A项"更便宜"原文未提及价格比较；C项"飞机不安全"属于无中生有；D项"飞机不飞小城市"原文未涉及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "High-speed railways help smaller cities grow. When a new station opens in a small town, more tourists and business people come to visit."

**【解析】** 第④段整段论述了高铁如何帮助小城市发展——带来游客、商机和就业机会，由此可推断高铁促进了小城市的发展。A项"只帮助大城市"与段意相反；B项"小城镇不想要高铁站"属于无中生有；D项"年轻人更喜欢离开小城镇"与原文"young people find more job opportunities"矛盾。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文

**【解析】** 文章围绕中国高铁的发展历程、速度优势、便利性和对城市发展的促进作用展开，B项"中国高铁：又快又远"最能概括全文主旨。A项"世界火车的历史"范围过大且偏离主题；C项"如何网上买火车票"只涉及文中一个细节；D项"磁悬浮列车：旅行的未来"仅是文末一小部分内容，以偏概全。故选B。
