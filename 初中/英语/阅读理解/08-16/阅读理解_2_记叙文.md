> **08-16 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last July, our school organized a volunteer activity at Sunshine Nursing Home (养老院). I signed up with my best friend Wang Lei. To be honest, I was a little nervous because I had never spent time with elderly people outside my family.

② When we arrived, a nurse led us to the main hall. About twenty old people were sitting in chairs, some reading newspapers and others just looking out of the window. Wang Lei and I walked up to a grandmother named Mrs. Li. She was ninety years old and lived alone in the nursing home because her children worked in another city. She smiled warmly when we said hello.

③ Wang Lei helped Mrs. Li fold paper cranes (纸鹤) while I read a story aloud from a book she loved. She listened carefully and laughed at the funny parts. Then she told us about her own childhood — how she walked five kilometers to school every morning and studied by candlelight at night. "Education was hard to get in those days," she said, "so you must treasure (珍惜) what you have now."

④ After lunch, we helped clean the rooms and watered the flowers in the garden. Some students performed songs and dances for the old people. The whole nursing home was filled with laughter. When it was time to leave, Mrs. Li held my hand tightly and said, "Thank you for coming. It gets lonely here sometimes. Your visit made my whole week brighter."

⑤ On the bus home, I kept thinking about Mrs. Li's words. I realized that volunteering was not just about helping others — it also taught me to be grateful and caring. Since that day, Wang Lei and I have gone back to the nursing home every month. Each visit reminds us that a little company can mean the world to someone who is alone.

**注释：** nursing home 养老院；crane 鹤；treasure 珍惜；candlelight 烛光；grateful 感恩的

---

**Questions:**

1. Why was the writer nervous before going to the nursing home?
   A. Because he did not like old people.
   B. Because he had never spent time with elderly people outside his family.
   C. Because he was afraid of the nurse.
   D. Because Wang Lei did not want to go.

2. What did the writer do for Mrs. Li?
   A. He sang songs for her.
   B. He cooked lunch for her.
   C. He read a story aloud for her.
   D. He taught her to fold paper cranes.

3. What can we learn about Mrs. Li's childhood?
   A. She lived near her school.
   B. She had an easy life as a child.
   C. She studied hard under difficult conditions.
   D. She did not go to school at all.

4. What did Mrs. Li mean by saying "Your visit made my whole week brighter"?
   A. The students brought her a bright lamp.
   B. The weather was sunny that week.
   C. The visit made her feel happy and less lonely.
   D. She wanted the students to clean her room again.

5. What did the writer learn from the volunteer activity?
   A. Old people do not need company.
   B. Volunteering is only about helping others.
   C. He should visit the nursing home to get prizes.
   D. Volunteering taught him to be grateful and caring.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "I was a little nervous because I had never spent time with elderly people outside my family."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明作者紧张的原因是从未和家人以外的老人相处过。A项"不喜欢老人"原文未提及，且与后文态度矛盾；C项"害怕护士"属于无中生有；D项"王磊不想去"与原文"I signed up with my best friend Wang Lei"矛盾。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "I read a story aloud from a book she loved."

**【解析】** 原文明确说作者为李奶奶朗读故事。A项"唱歌"是其他学生的活动；B项"做午饭"原文未提及；D项"教折纸鹤"张冠李戴，是王磊帮李奶奶折纸鹤。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "she walked five kilometers to school every morning and studied by candlelight at night. 'Education was hard to get in those days'"

**【解析】** 李奶奶每天走5公里上学、晚上点蜡烛学习，说明她在艰苦条件下刻苦读书。A项"住在学校附近"与"走5公里"矛盾；B项"童年生活轻松"与原文描述相反；D项"根本没上学"与原文矛盾。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "It gets lonely here sometimes. Your visit made my whole week brighter."

**【解析】** 李奶奶说自己有时很孤独，学生的来访让她整周都更"明亮"了，这里"brighter"是比喻用法，意思是来访让她感到开心、不再那么孤独。A项"带来台灯"是对brighter的字面误解；B项"天气晴朗"无关；D项"想让学生再次打扫"原文无依据。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2句 "I realized that volunteering was not just about helping others — it also taught me to be grateful and caring."

**【解析】** 原文直接说明作者从志愿活动中学到了感恩和关爱。A项"老人不需要陪伴"与全文主旨相反；B项"志愿服务只是帮助别人"恰好是作者否定的观点；C项"为了得奖而去"属于无中生有。故选D。
