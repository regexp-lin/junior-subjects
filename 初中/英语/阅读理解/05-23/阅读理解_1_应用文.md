# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **2026 Summer Camp Registration Guide**

② Our school will organize a one-week summer camp from July 15 to 21, 2026. Students in Grades 7 and 8 are welcome to join. The camp aims to help you develop life skills, make new friends, and enjoy outdoor activities. All activities will take place at the Sunshine Education Center (阳光教育中心), about 30 minutes by bus from our school.

③ **How to Register:**

- **Step 1:** Fill in the online form at www.schoolcamp.edu.cn before June 25.
- **Step 2:** Parents should sign the permission form (许可单) and hand it to the Student Office.
- **Step 3:** Pay the fee of 380 yuan. It includes meals, accommodation (住宿) and all activity materials. Please pay at the school finance office or through our online payment system.
- **Step 4:** Attend the safety meeting on July 10 at 3:00 PM in the school hall. All campers must be present.

④ **What to Bring:** A backpack, comfortable sports clothes, a water bottle, personal items (牙刷、毛巾等), and a notebook. Do not bring valuable things such as expensive phones or watches. The camp is not responsible for lost items.

⑤ If you have any questions, please contact Miss Li at **licamp@school.edu** or call 020-87654321. We look forward to seeing you at the camp!

**注释：** permission 许可；accommodation 住宿；valuable 贵重的

---

### 选择题

1. Who can register for the summer camp?
   A. All students in the school.
   B. Students in Grades 7 and 8.
   C. Only parents and teachers.
   D. Students from any school in the city.

2. According to the guide, what does the 380 yuan fee include?
   A. Only meals.
   B. Meals, accommodation and all activity materials.
   C. Transportation to the center.
   D. Personal items like toothbrush and towel.

3. When must students attend the safety meeting?
   A. On July 15 before the camp starts.
   B. On June 25 when registration closes.
   C. On July 10 at 3:00 PM in the school hall.
   D. Every day during the camp.

4. What can we infer from the passage about bringing valuable things?
   A. Students should bring expensive phones to take photos.
   B. The camp encourages students to bring their best watches.
   C. Students will need to show their phones during the safety meeting.
   D. Valuable items may get lost and the camp will not take responsibility.

5. How many steps are needed to complete the registration?
   A. Four.
   B. Two.
   C. Three.
   D. Five.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Students in Grades 7 and 8 are welcome to join."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明七、八年级学生可报名参加夏令营。A 项正确。B 项"全校学生"范围过宽；C 项"家长和教师"与原文不符；D 项"全市任何学校"与"our school"限定矛盾。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 Step 3 "It includes meals, accommodation and all activity materials."

**【解析】** 费用 380 元包含餐饮、住宿及所有活动材料。A 项仅含餐饮不完整；B 项正确；C 项交通费、D 项个人用品文中均未列入费用范围。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 Step 4 "Attend the safety meeting on July 10 at 3:00 PM in the school hall."

**【解析】** 安全说明会时间为 7 月 10 日下午 3 点，地点为学校礼堂。A 项 7 月 15 日为开营日；B 项 6 月 25 日为报名截止日；D 项"每天"与一次性会议不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Do not bring valuable things such as expensive phones or watches. The camp is not responsible for lost items."

**【解析】** 文中明确建议勿带贵重物品，且营地对遗失物品概不负责。可推断贵重物品可能丢失且营地不担责。A、B 项与"do not bring"矛盾；C 项安全会展示手机文中无依据；D 项正确。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 Step 1 至 Step 4

**【解析】** 报名流程分为四步：填表、家长签字、缴费、参加安全会。A 项正确；B 两项、C 三项、D 五项均与原文不符。故选 A。
