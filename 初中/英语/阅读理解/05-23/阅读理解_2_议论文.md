# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should teenagers have pocket money? This question causes a lot of discussion in many families. Some parents think teenagers are too young to manage money, so they buy everything for their children. However, I believe that giving teenagers a proper amount of pocket money is important for their growth, especially for developing financial literacy (理财能力).

② First, pocket money helps teenagers learn how to manage money in real life. When a teenager receives a fixed amount each week or month, he or she must decide how to spend it: buy a snack now or save for a book? This kind of practice teaches them to make choices and understand that money is limited. Without this experience, teenagers may struggle when they grow up and have to manage their own salary (薪水) for the first time.

③ Second, pocket money helps teenagers learn the value of saving. If they want to buy something that costs more than their weekly allowance, they must save for several weeks. During this process, they learn patience and the joy of reaching a goal. Many banks now offer special savings accounts for teenagers, which can help them form a good habit of setting money aside for the future.

④ Of course, parents should guide their teenagers. They can discuss together how much pocket money is reasonable and what it can be used for. Parents can also teach their children to separate money into three parts: for daily use, for saving, and for helping others. In this way, teenagers not only learn to spend wisely but also develop a sense of responsibility (责任感).

⑤ In conclusion, pocket money is a useful tool for teaching teenagers about money. It is not just about buying things — it is about learning to plan, save, and make smart decisions. When teenagers learn these skills early, they will be better prepared for their future life.

**注释：** literacy 能力；salary 薪水；responsibility 责任

### 选择题

1. What is the writer's main opinion about teenagers and pocket money?
   A. Teenagers should never receive any pocket money.
   B. Giving teenagers proper pocket money helps develop their financial literacy.
   C. Parents should buy everything for their children.
   D. Teenagers are too young to learn about money.

2. According to paragraph ②, how does pocket money help teenagers learn to manage money?
   A. By letting them earn money from doing chores.
   B. By making them decide how to spend a fixed amount and understand money is limited.
   C. By allowing them to ask for more money anytime.
   D. By teaching them to borrow money from banks.

3. What does paragraph ③ say about saving money?
   A. Teenagers should never save money.
   B. Banks do not allow teenagers to open accounts.
   C. Saving helps teenagers learn patience and the joy of reaching goals.
   D. Teenagers only need to save for one week.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about parents' role?
   A. Parents should not give any guidance to teenagers.
   B. Parents should control exactly how teenagers spend every coin.
   C. Parents must give teenagers as much money as they want.
   D. Parents can guide teenagers by discussing reasonable amounts and teaching them to divide money for different uses.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To argue that teenagers should work to earn money.
   B. To explain why pocket money helps teenagers develop financial skills and prepare for the future.
   C. To tell parents to stop giving pocket money.
   D. To compare different ways of saving money.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第①段 "I believe that giving teenagers a proper amount of pocket money is important for their growth, especially for developing financial literacy."

**【解析】** 作者在开篇亮明观点：给予青少年适量零花钱有利于其成长，尤其是培养理财能力。A 选项准确概括。B 项"绝不给零花钱"与作者立场相反；C 项"父母全包"是作者不赞同的做法；D 项"太小不懂钱"也非作者看法。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "he or she must decide how to spend it: buy a snack now or save for a book? This kind of practice teaches them to make choices and understand that money is limited."

**【解析】** 有固定零花钱后，青少年需自己决定如何花，这种实践能教他们做选择并理解金钱有限。B 项与之相符。A 项"通过家务赚钱"属 05-05 议论文角度，本文侧重理财培养；C 项"随时要更多"、D 项"向银行借钱"文中未提。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "During this process, they learn patience and the joy of reaching a goal."

**【解析】** 存钱过程中，青少年学会耐心和达成目标的喜悦。C 项正确。A 项"绝不该存"与段落主旨矛盾；B 项银行不允许青少年开户与"special savings accounts for teenagers"矛盾；D 项"只需存一周"过度简化。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Parents can discuss together how much pocket money is reasonable and what it can be used for" 及 "teach their children to separate money into three parts: for daily use, for saving, and for helping others."

**【解析】** 父母可通过讨论合理金额及用途、教孩子把零花钱分三部分等方式进行引导。D 项正确概括。A 项"不给指导"与"guide"矛盾；B 项"控制每分钱"过度控制，非作者建议；C 项"要多少给多少"与"reasonable"相悖。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图 / 主旨归纳

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第①段 "developing financial literacy"、第⑤段 "learning to plan, save, and make smart decisions" 及 "better prepared for their future life."

**【解析】** 全文旨在说明零花钱有助于青少年培养理财能力、做出明智决策，为未来生活做准备。C 项准确概括写作目的。A 项"打工赚钱"非本文主题；B 项"停止给零花钱"与作者立场相反；D 项仅比较存钱方式，范围过窄。故选 C。
