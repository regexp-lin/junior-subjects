> **12-04 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Winter Holiday Safety Notice**

① Dear students and parents,

The winter holiday will start on January 15 and end on February 28. To keep everyone safe and healthy, please read the following tips.

**Safety Tips:**

| Area | Tips |
|------|------|
| At home | Do not use the stove alone. Keep away from heaters. |
| Outdoors | Do not play near rivers or ponds. Wear warm clothes. |
| Online | Do not share personal information with strangers. Limit screen time. |
| Travel | Follow traffic rules. Stay with your parents. |

② **Fire safety:** Do not play with fire or lighters. If you see a fire, call 119 at once and tell an adult.

③ **Food safety:** Eat healthy meals. Do not eat food that has been left out for too long. Wash your hands before meals.

④ **Emergency contacts:**
- School office: 0755-12345678 (Jan 15–Feb 5)
- Police: 110
- Hospital: 120

⑤ Have a wonderful and safe winter holiday!

School Office  
January 10, 2025

---

**Questions:**

1. When does the winter holiday end?
   A. January 15.
   B. January 28.
   C. February 15.
   D. February 28.

2. What should students do near rivers or ponds?
   A. Play and have fun.
   B. Do not play near them.
   C. Swim with friends.
   D. Take photos.

3. Who should students stay with when travelling?
   A. Friends only.
   B. Their parents.
   C. Teachers.
   D. No one.

4. What number should students call if they see a fire?
   A. 110.
   B. 119.
   C. 120.
   D. 12345.

5. How long is the school office open during the holiday?
   A. Until January 28.
   B. Until February 5.
   C. Until February 28.
   D. All holiday long.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The winter holiday will start on January 15 and end on February 28."

**【解析】** 原文明确寒假于2月28日结束。A项1月15日是开始日期；B、C项文中未提及。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 Outdoors 一行 "Do not play near rivers or ponds."

**【解析】** 原文要求不要在河边或池塘边玩耍。A项玩耍、C项游泳、D项拍照均与安全提示相反。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 Travel 一行 "Stay with your parents."

**【解析】** 外出旅行时需与父母同行。A项仅与朋友、C项与老师、D项独自均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "If you see a fire, call 119 at once"

**【解析】** 原文规定发现火灾应拨打119。A项110为报警；C项120为急救；D项12345为政府热线。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "School office: 0755-12345678 (Jan 15–Feb 5)"

**【解析】** 校办在1月15日至2月5日期间可联系。A项1月28日、C项2月28日、D项整个假期均不准确。故选B。
