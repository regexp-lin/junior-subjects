# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Should Students Travel During Summer Holiday?**

① Summer holiday lasts about two months. For many students, it is a long break from school. Some spend it at home, doing homework and playing. Others travel with their families to different cities or even other countries. So, should students travel during the summer holiday? People have different views.

② Those who support summer travel say it has many benefits. First, travel broadens the mind. Students can see new places, meet different people, and learn about other cultures. What they learn from travel cannot be found in textbooks. Second, travel brings families together. Parents are often busy with work during the school year. Summer is a good time to spend quality time with children. Third, travel can improve language skills. If students go to an English-speaking country, they have to use English in real life. Practice makes perfect.

③ However, some people think students should not travel too much. Travel costs money. Not every family can afford it. Some students need to help at home or do part-time jobs during the holiday. Besides, travel can be tiring. After a long trip, students might feel too tired to focus when the new term starts. Also, some argue that students should use the holiday to study, join training classes, or prepare for exams. Time is limited, and they must choose.

④ In my opinion, travel is valuable, but it is not a must for everyone. If a family can afford it and the child wants to go, a trip during the holiday can be a great experience. But if money or time is limited, staying home can also be meaningful. Students can read books, learn new skills, or spend time with grandparents. The key is to use the holiday well, whether at home or away.

⑤ So, there is no single answer. Travel can enrich a student's life, but it is not the only way to have a good holiday. What matters is balance and what works best for each family.

**注释：** broaden 扩展；quality time 高质量相处时间

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, which is a benefit of summer travel?
   A. Travel makes students tired.
   B. Travel broadens the mind and helps students learn about new places and cultures.
   C. Travel costs no money.
   D. Travel keeps students away from families.

2. Why do some people think students should NOT travel too much?
   A. Because travel is always boring.
   B. Because travel costs money, not every family can afford it, and it can be tiring.
   C. Because students never want to travel.
   D. Because travel is illegal.

3. What does the writer think about summer travel?
   A. All students must travel every summer.
   B. Travel is valuable but not a must; use the holiday well whether at home or away.
   C. Students should never travel.
   D. Only rich students should travel.

4. What does "enrich" mean in the last paragraph?
   A. To make poor.
   B. To make richer or more meaningful.
   C. To make tired.
   D. To make angry.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Travel is the only good way to spend summer.
   B. Balance and what works for each family matter; travel is one option, not the only one.
   C. No student should travel.
   D. All families can easily afford travel.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Travel broadens the mind. Students can see new places, meet different people, and learn about other cultures."
**【解析】** 旅行开阔视野，学生可见识新地方、接触不同的人、了解其他文化。B项准确。A、C、D项与段落内容相反或未提及。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Travel costs money. Not every family can afford it... travel can be tiring."
**【解析】** 反对者认为旅行花钱、非每家都能承受、且可能令人疲惫。B项准确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Travel is valuable, but it is not a must for everyone... The key is to use the holiday well, whether at home or away."
**【解析】** 作者认为旅行有价值但非必须，关键是无论在家或外出都要用好假期。B项正确。A、C、D项与作者观点不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Travel can enrich a student's life" 结合语境
**【解析】** "enrich"意为使更丰富、更有意义。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "there is no single answer... What matters is balance and what works best for each family"
**【解析】** 结尾强调平衡与家庭实际情况的重要性，旅行是选项之一而非唯一。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
