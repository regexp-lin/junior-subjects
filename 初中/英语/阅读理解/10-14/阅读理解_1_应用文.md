> **10-14 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Campus Tech Festival — Activity Schedule**

① Dear students and teachers,

② Our school will hold the 2024 Campus Tech Festival from October 21st to October 23rd. The theme is "Technology and Life". We have prepared a variety of activities for everyone. Please read the schedule below and join in!

**Schedule:**

**Day 1 (Oct 21, Monday):**
- 9:00–10:30 Opening ceremony & robot show (School Hall)
- 10:30–12:00 Science exhibition — students' inventions (Building A, 1st floor)
- 14:00–16:00 Coding workshop for beginners (Computer Room 1)

**Day 2 (Oct 22, Tuesday):**
- 9:00–11:00 3D printing demo and hands-on experience (Science Lab)
- 14:00–15:30 Talk: "AI in Our Daily Life" by Dr Wang (School Hall)
- 15:30–17:00 Drone flying experience (School playground)

**Day 3 (Oct 23, Wednesday):**
- 9:00–11:00 Science quiz competition (School Hall)
- 14:00–16:00 Tech project display and awards ceremony

③ All activities are free. No need to sign up in advance for most activities. For the coding workshop (limited to 30 students) and drone experience (limited to 50 students), please sign up at the Student Union office before October 18th.

④ We hope this festival will help you discover the fun of technology. For questions, contact Mr Chen at chen@school.edu.cn.

School Tech Festival Committee  
October 12th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. What is the theme of the Tech Festival?
   A. Sports and Health.
   B. Technology and Life.
   C. Art and Music.
   D. Reading and Writing.

2. Where will the robot show be held?
   A. In the Science Lab.
   B. On the playground.
   C. In the School Hall.
   D. In Computer Room 1.

3. Which activity needs advance sign-up?
   A. The opening ceremony.
   B. The science exhibition.
   C. The coding workshop and drone experience.
   D. The science quiz.

4. How many students can join the coding workshop?
   A. 20.
   B. 30.
   C. 50.
   D. 100.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. The festival is only for science teachers.
   B. Students need to pay to join the activities.
   C. The festival aims to help students discover the fun of technology.
   D. There are no limits for any activity.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "The theme is 'Technology and Life'"
**【解析】** 科技节主题为"科技与生活"。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "9:00–10:30 Opening ceremony & robot show (School Hall)"
**【解析】** 机器人展示在学校礼堂举行。C项正确。A、B、D项为其他活动地点。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "For the coding workshop (limited to 30 students) and drone experience (limited to 50 students), please sign up at the Student Union office before October 18th"
**【解析】** 编程工作坊和无人机体验需要提前报名。C项正确。A、B、D项多数无需报名。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "coding workshop (limited to 30 students)"
**【解析】** 编程工作坊限30人。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第④段 "We hope this festival will help you discover the fun of technology."
**【解析】** 通知希望学生发现科技的乐趣，可推知C项。A项与"students and teachers"矛盾；B项与"All activities are free"矛盾；D项与"limited to 30/50 students"矛盾。故选C。
