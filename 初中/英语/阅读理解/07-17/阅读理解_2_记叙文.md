# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Li Wei, a Grade 8 student, never liked group projects. He thought he could do everything better alone. When his science teacher announced a model-building competition — teams of four had to build a bridge model in one week — Li Wei sighed. "I'll just do most of the work myself," he thought.

② On the first day, Li Wei cut the wood pieces alone while his teammates looked at the design. Soon he realized the pieces were too thick. His teammate Wang Na suggested using thinner wood for the middle. "I read that lighter materials work better for bridges," she said. Li Wei tried her idea, and it worked. He started to listen.

③ The next problem was connecting the parts. Zhang Kai was good at using glue and tools. He showed Li Wei a better way to join the pieces so they would not break. Meanwhile, Chen Yu drew a clear plan so everyone knew what to do next. For the first time, Li Wei felt that working together actually saved time.

④ On the last day, they finished the model ahead of time. When they tested it, the bridge held 15 books — more than any other team. Their teacher said, "This is what teamwork looks like." Li Wei looked at his teammates and smiled. He had learned something important.

⑤ Later, Li Wei said, "I used to think I was the best by working alone. Now I know that a team can achieve much more than one person. Everyone has something to offer." The model won second prize, but for Li Wei, the real prize was understanding the power of teamwork.

**注释：** model 模型；teammate 队友；design 设计；material 材料；glue 胶水；achieve 取得；teamwork 团队协作

---

### 选择题

1. What did Li Wei think about group projects at first?
   A. He believed he could do things better alone.
   B. He enjoyed working with others.
   C. He thought teamwork was always fun.
   D. He wanted to help his teammates as much as possible.

2. According to paragraph ②, what suggestion did Wang Na give?
   A. To use thinner wood for the middle of the bridge.
   B. To build the bridge higher.
   C. To change the competition rules.
   D. To give up the project.

3. What was Zhang Kai good at, according to paragraph ③?
   A. Drawing plans.
   B. Cutting wood.
   C. Using glue and tools to connect parts.
   D. Writing the report.

4. What can we infer from Li Wei's change in the passage?
   A. He will continue to prefer working alone.
   B. He has learned to value teamwork and what others can offer.
   C. He decided not to join any more competitions.
   D. He thinks his teammates did not help at all.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Science competitions are more important than other activities.
   B. A student learns the power of teamwork while building a model with his team.
   C. Bridge models should always use thick wood.
   D. Teachers should not assign group projects.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1、2句 "Li Wei... never liked group projects. He thought he could do everything better alone."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明李伟起初不喜欢小组项目，认为自己一个人能做得更好。A项正确。B项"喜欢与他人合作"与原文矛盾；C项"认为团队合作总是有趣"、D项"想尽力帮助队友"均非李伟最初的想法。故选A。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "His teammate Wang Na suggested using thinner wood for the middle. 'I read that lighter materials work better for bridges,' she said."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明王娜建议在桥的中间使用更薄的木片，因为较轻的材料对桥梁更有利。A项正确。B项"建更高的桥"、C项"改变比赛规则"、D项"放弃项目"文中均未提及。故选A。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Zhang Kai was good at using glue and tools. He showed Li Wei a better way to join the pieces..."

**【解析】** 原文指出张凯擅长使用胶水和工具，并教李伟更好的连接方法。C项正确。A项"画计划"是陈雨的工作；B项"削木头"是李伟做的；D项"写报告"文中未提及。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "I used to think I was the best by working alone. Now I know that a team can achieve much more than one person. Everyone has something to offer."

**【解析】** 李伟表示以前认为独自工作最好，现在明白了团队能取得比个人更多的成就，每个人都有可贡献之处。可推知他学会了重视团队协作和他人贡献。B项正确。A项"继续偏好独自工作"与"Now I know"矛盾；C项"不再参加比赛"和D项"认为队友毫无帮助"均与原文相反。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段引入李伟不爱合作，第②③段写团队合作过程，第④⑤段写成果与感悟。

**【解析】** 全文围绕李伟在做手工桥模型时从独行到学会团队协作的转变展开，B项"一名学生在与团队制作模型中领悟到团队协作的力量"最能概括主旨。A项侧重竞赛重要性；C项"桥模型应用厚木头"与王娜建议矛盾；D项"教师不应布置小组作业"与文章主题相反。故选B。
