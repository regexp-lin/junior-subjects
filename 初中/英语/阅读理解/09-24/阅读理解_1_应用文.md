# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Photography Contest — Capture Your Campus!**

② Love taking photos? Join our annual (一年一度的) School Photography Contest! Show us your view of school life — classrooms, sports, festivals, friends, or the beauty of our campus in different seasons.

③ **Contest Rules:**

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Theme | "My School, My Story" |
| Who can join | All students (Grades 7–9) |
| Submission (提交) | Send your photos to photo@school.edu by Oct 15 |
| Format | JPG or PNG, no more than 5MB per photo |
| Number of entries | Each student may submit 1–3 photos |
| Prizes | First prize: digital camera; Second: art supplies; Third: certificate + book |

④ **Important notes:**
- Photos must be taken by you. No copied work.
- Photos must be suitable (合适的) for all ages — no violent or inappropriate content.
- The school may use winning photos in newsletters, websites, or exhibitions.
- Results will be announced on Oct 25. Winners will be notified by email.

⑤ **How to submit:**
1. Name your file: Class_Grade_YourName_1.jpg (e.g. Class2_Grade8_ZhangMing_1.jpg)
2. Write a short caption (说明文字) (20–50 words) for each photo
3. Attach the photo and caption to an email and send to photo@school.edu

⑥ Questions? Contact the Art Club at Room 205 or email artclub@school.edu. Good luck and happy shooting!

**注释：** annual 一年一度的；submission 提交；suitable 合适的；caption 说明文字

---

**Questions:**

1. What is the theme of the photography contest?
   A. Travel around the world.
   B. My School, My Story.
   C. Animals and nature.
   D. Family photos only.

2. By when must students send their photos?
   A. September 15.
   B. October 15.
   C. October 25.
   D. November 15.

3. How many photos can each student submit?
   A. Only one.
   B. One to three.
   C. At least five.
   D. Unlimited.

4. What prize does the first-place winner get?
   A. A book.
   B. Art supplies.
   C. A digital camera.
   D. A certificate only.

5. What must students do when submitting a photo?
   A. Only send the photo.
   B. Write a short caption (20–50 words) for each photo and send with the photo.
   C. Pay a fee.
   D. Hand in a printed copy only.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Theme | 'My School, My Story'"

**【解析】** 比赛主题为"My School, My Story"。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Submission | Send your photos to photo@school.edu by Oct 15"

**【解析】** 作品须在10月15日前提交。A、C、D 项日期错误。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Number of entries | Each student may submit 1–3 photos"

**【解析】** 每位学生可提交1至3张照片。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "First prize: digital camera"

**【解析】** 一等奖奖品为数码相机。A、B、D 项为其他奖项。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Write a short caption (20–50 words) for each photo... Attach the photo and caption to an email"

**【解析】** 每张照片需配20–50字说明，并与照片一并发送。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。
