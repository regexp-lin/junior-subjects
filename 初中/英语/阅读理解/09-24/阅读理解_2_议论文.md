# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should students have pocket money? Some parents give their children a weekly or monthly allowance (零花钱). Others think children should not have money until they are older. This is a common topic in many families.

② Those who support pocket money say that it teaches children about money. When children have their own money, they learn to budget（预算） — they must decide what to buy and what to save. If they spend too much too soon, they have nothing left. That is a real lesson. Pocket money also gives children a sense of independence. They can buy a small gift for a friend or save up for something they really want. In addition, some parents link pocket money to chores (家务) — for example, "If you help with the dishes, you earn five yuan." This helps children understand that money comes from work.

③ However, opponents (反对者) argue that pocket money can cause problems. Some children may spend it all on snacks, games, or things they do not need. They might compare how much they get with their friends and feel unhappy if they get less. Young children may not understand the value of money and could lose or waste it. Besides, if children get money too easily, they might not learn to work hard. They may think that money is always available without effort.

④ I believe that pocket money can be helpful if parents use it wisely. The amount should be suitable for the child's age. Parents can guide their children — for example, suggest saving part of the money each week. They can talk about what things cost and why we save. Pocket money should not be used to replace love or attention. It should be a tool for learning. When children make mistakes with their money, that is a chance to teach them, not to take the money away forever.

⑤ In the end, the goal is to raise children who understand and respect money. Pocket money, used well, can be one step toward that goal.

**注释：** allowance 零花钱；budget 预算；chore 家务；opponent 反对者

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, what benefit does pocket money provide?
   A. Children never need to think.
   B. It teaches children about money, budgeting, and gives a sense of independence; some link it to chores.
   C. Children will always save money.
   D. Parents do not need to buy anything.

2. Why do some people oppose pocket money?
   A. Because they have no money.
   B. Children may spend it unwisely, compare with friends, or not understand its value.
   C. Because schools ban it.
   D. Because it is too heavy to carry.

3. What does the writer suggest about pocket money?
   A. Never give any pocket money.
   B. Use it wisely: suitable amount, guide children to save, use it as a learning tool.
   C. Give as much as possible.
   D. Only give money when children cry.

4. The word "opponents" in Paragraph ③ refers to people who _____.
   A. support pocket money
   B. are against pocket money
   C. give money to children
   D. are children

5. What is the writer's main message?
   A. Pocket money is always bad.
   B. Pocket money, used wisely, can help raise children who understand and respect money.
   C. All children should get the same amount.
   D. Parents should never talk about money with children.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "it teaches children about money... they learn to budget... gives children a sense of independence... some parents link pocket money to chores"

**【解析】** 支持者认为零花钱可教孩子理财、预算、培养独立感，有的家庭将其与家务挂钩。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some children may spend it all on snacks, games... They might compare how much they get... Young children may not understand the value of money"

**【解析】** 反对者担心孩子乱花钱、攀比、不理解金钱价值。A、C、D 项非主要理由。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "pocket money can be helpful if parents use it wisely... The amount should be suitable... Parents can guide... suggest saving... It should be a tool for learning"

**【解析】** 作者建议合理使用零花钱：金额适当、引导储蓄、作为学习工具。A、C、D 项与建议不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "However, opponents argue" 与第②段 "Those who support" 形成对比

**【解析】** opponents 与 support 相对，指反对（给零花钱）的人。A、C、D 项与语境不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第④、⑤段 "Pocket money can be helpful if parents use it wisely... The goal is to raise children who understand and respect money. Pocket money, used well, can be one step toward that goal"

**【解析】** 作者认为合理使用零花钱有助于培养孩子理解与尊重金钱。A、C、D 项与作者观点矛盾。故选 B。
