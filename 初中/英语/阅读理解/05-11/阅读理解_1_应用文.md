# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Welcome to the Green Earth Recycling Program!**

② Do you want to help save our planet? The Green Earth Recycling (回收利用) Program is starting in our neighborhood this month. Together, we can reduce waste, protect animals, and make our living environment cleaner.

③ **What can you recycle?**
Please sort (分类) your waste into the following three colored bins:
- **Blue Bin (Paper & Cardboard):** Old newspapers, magazines, empty cereal boxes, and printing paper. *Note: Please do not put dirty pizza boxes here because the oil ruins the paper.*
- **Yellow Bin (Plastic & Metal):** Plastic water bottles, milk jugs, soda cans, and clean food tins. *Note: Wash the bottles and cans before throwing them in.*
- **Green Bin (Glass):** Glass bottles and jars of any color. *Note: Broken glass (碎玻璃) should be wrapped in paper and thrown in the normal rubbish bin, NOT the Green Bin, to keep our workers safe.*

④ **Pick-up Schedule (收运时间表):**
Our trucks will collect the recycling bins every Wednesday morning. Please put your bins outside your house before 8:00 AM. 

⑤ **Reward System (奖励制度):**
To encourage everyone to join, we have a special reward system. Every time you recycle correctly, your house will earn 10 "Green Points". 
- **100 Points:** You can exchange them for a set of reusable shopping bags.
- **300 Points:** You can get a free movie ticket.
- **500 Points:** We will plant a real tree in City Park with your family's name on it!

⑥ If you need new recycling bins or have any questions, please contact our office at 555-7788 or visit *www.greenearth-recycle.org*. Let's work together for a greener future!

**注释：** recycle 回收利用；cereal 麦片；jug 坛 / 壶 (常见为塑料提手瓶)；tin 罐头；reward 奖励；exchange 兑换

---

### 选择题

1. Where should you put an old newspaper according to paragraph ③?
A. In the Blue Bin.
B. In the Yellow Bin.
C. In the Green Bin.
D. In the normal rubbish bin.

2. Why shouldn't you put dirty pizza boxes in the Blue Bin?
A. Because they are too big for the bin.
B. Because the oil on them ruins the paper.
C. Because they belong in the Yellow Bin with metal.
D. Because animals will come to eat them.

3. What should you do if you have broken glass (碎玻璃)?
A. Throw it directly into the Green Bin.
B. Hide it inside a plastic milk jug.
C. Leave it in the street for the trucks to pick up.
D. Wrap it in paper and put it in the normal rubbish bin.

4. When should the recycling bins be put outside the house?
A. Every Sunday night before sleeping.
B. Every Wednesday morning before 8:00 AM.
C. Every Friday afternoon at 5:00 PM.
D. Everyday whenever the bins are full.

5. Form paragraph ⑤, what can you get if you collect 500 "Green Points"?
A. A set of reusable shopping bags.
B. A free ticket to a movie.
C. A real tree planted in City Park with your family's name on it.
D. $500 in cash.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1类回收箱 "Blue Bin (Paper & Cardboard): Old newspapers..."

**【解析】** 根据分类规则，旧报纸（Old newspapers）属于纸制品，应该放进蓝色的纸和纸板回收箱中（In the Blue Bin）。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段Blue Bin的注意事项 "Note: Please do not put dirty pizza boxes here because the oil ruins the paper."

**【解析】** 为什么不能把脏披萨盒放进蓝色箱子？原文注意事项里解释说，因为上面的油迹会毁了其他纸张（the oil ruins the paper）。选项B与此完全吻合。A太大、C属于金属、D招动物等均属于乱编。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第③段Green Bin的注意事项 "Note: Broken glass (碎玻璃) should be wrapped in paper and thrown in the normal rubbish bin, NOT the Green Bin, to keep our workers safe."

**【解析】** 如果有碎玻璃该怎么处理？为了工人的安全，原文明确要求：应该用纸裹起来扔进普通垃圾桶，绝不能扔进主要装玻璃的绿箱子。只有D选项（用纸包起来放到普通垃圾桶里）遵循了这一安全指南。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段收运时间表 "Our trucks will collect the recycling bins every Wednesday morning. Please put your bins outside your house before 8:00 AM."

**【解析】** 回收箱应该什么时候放到房子外面？时间表里写得很清楚：因为卡车每周三早上来收，所以请在周三早上8点前（every Wednesday morning before 8:00 AM）将箱子放在门外。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第3级奖励 "500 Points: We will plant a real tree in City Park with your family's name on it!"

**【解析】** 如果你收集了500个也是最高档的“绿点”，你能得到什么奖励？原文说明：我们将在市级公园里种一棵真正冠以你家人名字的树。选项C完美对应。A项是100点的奖励，B项是300点的奖励，D项给钱未曾出现。故选C。
