# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Lost and Found Notice**

② Have you lost something recently, or did you find something that doesn't belong to you? Our school's **Lost and Found Office** is here to help! 

③ **Where are we?**
We are located in Room 102 on the first floor of the Main Building, right next to the school clinic (医务室).

④ **Open Hours:**
Monday to Friday: 12:00 PM – 1:30 PM (During lunch break)
*Note: We are closed on weekends and during class time.*

⑤ **Items currently in our office:**
- A black sports bag with a pair of white tennis shoes inside. Found near the playground on Monday.
- A red umbrella with cartoon patterns. Found in the library on Tuesday afternoon.
- A silver watch. Found in the boys' washroom on Wednesday morning.
- Two keys on a yellow key ring (钥匙圈). Found near the main gate.

⑥ **How to claim (认领) your lost items?**
If any of the things above belong to you, please come to our office during the open hours. You will need to tell us some details (细节) about the item to prove (证明) it is yours. For example, if you lost a watch, you should tell us its brand (品牌) or the exact time it stopped.

⑦ **Did you find something?**
If you find a lost item on the school campus, please bring it to us as soon as possible. Your small act of kindness might save someone a lot of trouble!

**注释：** belong 属于；clinic 医务室；claim 认领；detail 细节；prove 证明；brand 品牌

---

### 选择题

1. Where is the school's Lost and Found Office located?
A. On the second floor, next to the library.
B. In Room 102, right next to the school clinic.
C. Near the playground.
D. At the main gate.

2. When can students go to the Lost and Found Office?
A. Every day in the morning.
B. On weekends from 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM.
C. Monday to Friday during lunch break (12:00 PM – 1:30 PM).
D. Any time during classes.

3. Where was the red umbrella with cartoon patterns found?
A. Near the playground.
B. In the library.
C. In the boys' washroom.
D. Near the main gate.

4. What must you do to claim (认领) a lost watch?
A. Tell the office some details like its brand or the time it stopped.
B. Pay some money to the office.
C. Bring a new watch to exchange (交换) for the lost one.
D. Ask your parents to come with you.

5. What should a student do according to paragraph ⑦ if he finds someone's notebook?
A. Keep it for himself.
B. Throw it into the rubbish bin.
C. Bring it to the Lost and Found Office as soon as possible.
D. Try to find the owner by loudly calling out in every classroom.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "We are located in Room 102 on the first floor of the Main Building, right next to the school clinic (医务室)."

**【解析】** 原文明示失物招领处（Lost and Found Office）就位于主教学楼一楼102室，紧挨着校医务室。A（图书馆旁）、C（操场旁）、D（正大门）都是找到遗失物的地方而非该办公室所在位置。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Open Hours: Monday to Friday: 12:00 PM – 1:30 PM (During lunch break)... We are closed on weekends and during class time."

**【解析】** 办公室开放时间明确为：周一至周五的中午休息时间（12:00 PM – 1:30 PM）。周末和上课时不开放（A、B、D皆因为时间段相悖被排除）。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2条 "A red umbrella with cartoon patterns. Found in the library on Tuesday afternoon."

**【解析】** 本题对找到各遗失物地点的匹配。原文说明带有卡通图案的红伞是在周二下午的图书馆里（In the library）找到的。故选B。如果是男孩洗手间（C）那是手表；在操场边（A）是黑包。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段最后一句 "For example, if you lost a watch, you should tell us its brand (品牌) or the exact time it stopped."

**【解析】** 在教你如何认领（How to claim）那段中，明确举例若认领手表，需报出一些细节来证明它是你的，如手表的品牌或者停走的具体时间。交钱（B项）、拿新表换（C项）、叫父母一起来（D项）都未体现于文中。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 问题推断

**【定位】** 第⑦段第1-2句 "If you find a lost item on the school campus, please bring it to us as soon as possible."

**【解析】** 段末号召大家，若你在校园里捡到他人遗失物品（好比题目中所指的笔记本），应尽快把它交到失物招领处（bring it to us as soon as possible）。A自己留着、B扔垃圾桶、D在各班大吼，要么品德恶劣要么方法笨拙。故只能选C。
