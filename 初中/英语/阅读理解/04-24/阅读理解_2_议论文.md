# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In recent years, many middle schools have started to offer online classes as a part of their daily education. Some students are excited about this change, but others miss the traditional (传统的) classroom. Is online learning better than traditional learning? People hold different views on this topic.

② On the positive side, online learning is very flexible (灵活的). Students can study anytime and anywhere as long as they have a computer and an Internet connection. They don't have to spend an hour taking a bus to school every morning. What's more, if a student doesn't understand a math problem during an online lesson, he can watch the recorded video again and again until he gets it. This is very good for students who learn a bit slower.

③ However, online learning also has its clear disadvantages (缺点). The biggest problem is the lack (缺乏) of real social interaction (互动). In a physical classroom, students can talk, play, and share secrets face to face. It is much easier to make friends and learn teamwork skills in a real school. Staring at a screen all day can make students feel lonely and isolated (孤立的).

④ Another problem is self-control. When taking online classes at home, there are many distractions (令人分心的事物). A student might start playing video games, chatting on their phone, or easily fall asleep on the sofa when the teacher is speaking through the screen. Without a teacher in the same room to watch over them, some students find it hard to pay attention.

⑤ In conclusion, online learning brings both convenience (便利) and challenges. In my view, the best way is to combine (结合) the two: using online tools for extra learning and review, while keeping the traditional classroom as the main place for students to study and grow together.

**注释：** traditional 传统的；flexible 灵活的；disadvantage 缺点；lack 缺乏；interaction 互动；isolate 使孤立；combine 结合

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what change has happened in many middle schools recently?
A. They have stopped teaching math.
B. They have started to offer online classes.
C. They have asked all students to wear traditional clothes.
D. They have sold all their computers.

2. Why is online learning considered "flexible" according to paragraph ②?
A. Because students do not need to do any homework.
B. Because students can learn faster than teachers.
C. Because students can study anytime and anywhere with the Internet.
D. Because all the videos are recorded by famous actors.

3. What is the biggest problem with online learning according to the writer?
A. The Internet connection is always too slow.
B. Students have to buy very expensive computers.
C. There are too many difficult math problems.
D. There is a lack of real social interaction.

4. Why is self-control an issue in online learning?
A. Because students can easily get distracted without a teacher in the same room.
B. Because students often lose their passwords for the online lessons.
C. Because teachers always forget to record the videos.
D. Because parents will sit next to the students all day.

5. What is the writer's final suggestion on this topic?
A. Schools should completely close and only use online learning.
B. Students should combine both online learning and the traditional classroom.
C. The traditional classroom should be totally abandoned (被抛弃).
D. Only slow learners should be allowed to use online learning.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段首句 "In recent years, many middle schools have started to offer online classes as a part of their daily education."

**【解析】** 原文中开篇即说明了发生的新变化：许多中学已经开始开设在线课程（online classes）。这与B选项完全对应。A、C、D均属无稽之谈。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段前两句 "...online learning is very flexible (灵活的). Students can study anytime and anywhere as long as they have a computer and an Internet connection."

**【解析】** 本段解释在线学习具有灵活性的基本原因是，只要有电脑和网络连接，学生就能随时随地学习（anytime and anywhere with the Internet），这是其灵活的根本。A、B、D皆无根据或偷换概念。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句话 "The biggest problem is the lack (缺乏) of real social interaction (互动)."

**【解析】** 原文中直接挑明作者认为线上学习带来的最大缺点（biggest problem）就是缺乏真实的面对面社交互动。D项“There is a lack of real social interaction”一字不差地概括了该点。A及B虽可能是现实障碍但在文中未作为最大问题列出，C明显不是主要毛病。故选D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解与推断

**【定位】** 第④段里 "When taking online classes at home, there are many distractions... Without a teacher in the same room to watch over them, some students find it hard to pay attention."

**【解析】** 本段讨论自我约束力（self-control）为何是一项挑战。原因在于家中诱惑（玩游戏、手机聊天、甚至困了在沙发睡觉等）多，且没有老师在同一个房间里盯着看着（without a teacher in the same room），学生很容易分心（get distracted）。因此A最吻合。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 作者观点/主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "In my view, the best way is to combine (结合) the two: using online tools for extra learning... while keeping the traditional classroom as the main place..."

**【解析】** 总结段作者指出了自己的最终建议（suggestion）：将这两种方式结合起来（combine the two），线上工具辅助学习和复习，线下实体教室保留为主要场所。所以B项（结合这两种）完全正确，摒弃了A的唯线上论及C的废弃实体课堂说。故选B。
