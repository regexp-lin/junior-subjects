# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Counseling Center (心理咨询室) — Welcome!**

② Everyone faces stress sometimes — exams, friendships, family, or future plans. It is normal to feel worried or sad. Our School Counseling Center is here to help. We provide free, confidential (保密的) support to all students.

③ **What we offer:**

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| One-on-one counseling | Talk privately with a trained counselor about your feelings and concerns |
| Group sessions | Join small groups to share experiences and learn coping skills |
| Crisis support | If you feel very upset or in danger, we are here 24/7 |
| Workshops | Learn about stress management, sleep, and healthy habits |

④ **When to visit:**
- When you feel anxious (焦虑的) or stressed for a long time
- When you have trouble sleeping or eating
- When you feel lonely or find it hard to make friends
- When you need someone to listen without judging

⑤ **How to make an appointment:**
- Visit Room 301 in Building B (Monday–Friday, 8:00–17:00)
- Or send an email to counseling@school.edu
- Or call 020-1234-5678 (during office hours)
- You can also ask your class teacher to help you book

⑥ Your privacy is important. We will not share your information with teachers or parents unless you give permission or there is a serious safety concern. You are not alone — we care about you.

**注释：** confidential 保密的；counseling 咨询；anxious 焦虑的；cope 应对

---

**Questions:**

1. Who can use the School Counseling Center?
   A. Only teachers.
   B. Only parents.
   C. All students.
   D. Only senior students.

2. What does "confidential" mean in this passage?
   A. Free of charge.
   B. Private; your information will not be shared without permission.
   C. Very fast.
   D. Only for boys.

3. Which service helps students learn about stress management?
   A. One-on-one counseling only.
   B. Group sessions only.
   C. Workshops.
   D. Crisis support only.

4. How can a student make an appointment?
   A. Only by visiting Room 301.
   B. By visiting, emailing, calling, or asking the class teacher to book.
   C. Only by phone.
   D. Only at night.

5. When will the center share a student's information with others?
   A. Always.
   B. Never.
   C. Only when the student gives permission or there is a serious safety concern.
   D. With all teachers every week.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "We provide free, confidential support to all students"

**【解析】** 心理咨询室为全体学生提供免费保密支持。A、B、D 项与原文不符。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第②段 "confidential" 及第⑥段 "We will not share your information with teachers or parents unless you give permission"

**【解析】** 根据上下文，confidential 意为信息保密、未经同意不向他人透露。A、C、D 项非该词含义。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Workshops | Learn about stress management, sleep, and healthy habits"

**【解析】** 工作坊涵盖压力管理、睡眠与健康习惯等内容。A、B、D 项未明确提及压力管理学习。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Visit Room 301... Or send an email... Or call... Or ask your class teacher to help you book"

**【解析】** 预约方式包括：到访、发邮件、打电话、请班主任代订。A、C、D 项表述不完整。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "We will not share your information with teachers or parents unless you give permission or there is a serious safety concern"

**【解析】** 仅在学生同意或存在重大安全风险时，才会向他人透露信息。A、B、D 项与原文不符。故选 C。
