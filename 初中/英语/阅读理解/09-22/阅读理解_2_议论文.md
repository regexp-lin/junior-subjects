# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Group Work or Studying Alone — Which Is Better?**

① In school, students often work in groups. Sometimes they study by themselves. Which way helps you learn better? Teachers and students have different opinions.

② Those who prefer group work say it has many advantages. In a group, you can share ideas and learn from others. If you do not understand something, your classmates can explain it. You also learn to communicate and work as a team. These skills are useful in life. Group work can make learning more fun. When you discuss with others, you remember things better. Besides, in the real world, people often work together. So practising in school prepares you for the future.

③ However, others believe that studying alone is more effective. When you study by yourself, you can focus without noise. You go at your own pace. Fast learners do not wait; slow learners take their time. In a group, some people may talk too much and do little. Others may feel shy and not speak. Studying alone means you are responsible for your own progress. You learn to think independently. Many top students say they do their best work when they are alone.

④ In my view, both ways have value. For some tasks, like projects or presentations, group work is better. For others, like reading or practising maths, studying alone may work better. The best approach depends on the task and the person. Good students often use both — they study alone to build a strong base, then join groups to discuss and deepen their understanding.

⑤ So, is group work or independent study better? There is no single answer. Each has its strengths. The key is to know when to work with others and when to work alone. A balanced mix often leads to the best results.

**注释：** effective 有效的；independently 独立地

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, what is one advantage of group work?
   A. You can share ideas, learn from others, and develop communication and teamwork skills.
   B. You never need to study.
   C. You always get the highest grades.
   D. You do not need a teacher.

2. According to Paragraph ③, why do some people prefer studying alone?
   A. Because they hate everyone.
   B. Because groups are too quiet.
   C. Because they can focus better, go at their own pace, and learn to think independently.
   D. Because teachers tell them to.

3. What does the writer think about group work and studying alone?
   A. Only group work is good.
   B. Only studying alone is good.
   C. Neither is useful.
   D. Both have value; the best approach depends on the task and the person; using both can lead to the best results.

4. For which kind of task does the writer say studying alone may work better?
   A. Projects and presentations.
   B. Group discussions only.
   C. Playing sports.
   D. Reading or practising maths.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. All students should always study in groups.
   B. Independent study is always better.
   C. Group work has no value at all.
   D. Choosing the right method (group or alone) for different situations is important for learning.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "you can share ideas and learn from others... you also learn to communicate and work as a team. These skills are useful in life"

**【解析】** 小组学习的优点包括：分享想法、相互学习、锻炼沟通和团队合作能力。A项概括准确。B、C、D项与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "you can focus without noise. You go at your own pace... Studying alone means you are responsible... You learn to think independently"

**【解析】** 独立学习的优势：注意力更集中、按自己的节奏学习、学会独立思考。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "both ways have value... The best approach depends on the task and the person... use both — they study alone... then join groups"

**【解析】** 作者认为两种方式都有价值，应根据任务和个人情况选择，最好结合使用。D项正确。A、B、C项与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "For others, like reading or practising maths, studying alone may work better"

**【解析】** 作者认为阅读或练习数学等任务更适合独自学习。D项正确。A、B、C项与原文不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④⑤段 "The best approach depends on the task and the person... The key is to know when to work with others and when to work alone"

**【解析】** 文意强调根据情境选择合适的学习方式很重要。D项正确。A、B、C项过于绝对，与文意不符。故选D。
