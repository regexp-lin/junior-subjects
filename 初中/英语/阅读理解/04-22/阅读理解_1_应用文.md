# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Join the "Star Voices" School Choir!**

② Do you enjoy singing in the shower? Do you love listening to music? Our school is starting a new choir (合唱团) named "Star Voices", and we want YOU to join us!

③ **Who are we looking for?**
We are looking for students from Grade 7 and Grade 8. You don't need to be a professional (专业的) singer. As long as you have a passion (热情) for music and are willing to learn, you are welcome!

④ **When and Where do we practice?**
- **Time:** Every Tuesday and Thursday afternoon, from 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM.
- **Place:** The Music Room on the second floor of the teaching building.

⑤ **Why should you join us?**
- **Make new friends:** You will meet many students who love music as much as you do.
- **Learn new skills:** Mr. Davis, our experienced music teacher, will teach you how to read music notes (乐谱) and how to control your voice.
- **Performance chances:** Our choir will perform at the School New Year Party and the City Spring Music Festival in March!

⑥ **How to join?**
We will have a simple audition (试音) next Wednesday at 12:30 PM in the Music Room. Just prepare a short song you like. It doesn't have to be perfect. If you want to come to the audition, please sign your name on the list outside the Music Room before next Monday.

⑦ Don't miss this great chance to let your voice shine!

**注释：** choir 合唱团；professional 专业的；passion 热情；audition 试音

---

### 选择题

1. What is the name of the new school choir according to the poster?
A. Super Stars
B. Star Voices
C. Magic Music
D. The Music Room

2. Who can join the "Star Voices" choir?
A. Only teachers who can sing very well.
B. All students in the school.
C. Students from Grade 7 and Grade 8 who have a passion for music.
D. Only students who are already professional singers.

3. Where will the choir practice?
A. In the school gym.
B. In the library on the third floor.
C. At Mr. Davis's home.
D. In the Music Room on the second floor.

4. What will Mr. Davis teach the students in the choir?
A. How to play the piano and guitar.
B. How to dance like a pop star.
C. How to read music notes and control their voice.
D. How to organize a big New Year Party.

5. What should a student do if he wants to join the audition next Wednesday?
A. Pay $50 to Mr. Davis immediately.
B. Sign his name on the list outside the Music Room before next Monday.
C. Bring a new music book to the classroom.
D. Write a long letter to the headmaster.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段标题 "Join the 'Star Voices' School Choir!" 和第②段第2句 "Our school is starting a new choir named 'Star Voices'..."

**【解析】** 原文中无论是大字标题还是第二段的介绍里都明确写出了这个合唱团的名字叫 "Star Voices"（星空之声/星之声）。故选B符合原文。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1-3句 "We are looking for students from Grade 7 and Grade 8. You don't need to be a professional (专业的) singer. As long as you have a passion (热情) for music..."

**【解析】** 本文明确说明这支队伍寻找的对象（Who are we looking for?）：只要是对音乐有热情的七年级和八年级学生即可，并非要求一定要是专业歌手（A、D错），也并未表明全校包括所有年级皆可（B错）。选项C完全概括了招募条件。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段里 "Place: The Music Room on the second floor of the teaching building."

**【解析】** 该段明确交代排练地点位于教学楼二楼的音乐教室里（The Music Room on the second floor）。A项体育馆、B项三楼图书馆、C项老师家里没有在文中提及。故选D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2个好处 "Learn new skills: Mr. Davis... will teach you how to read music notes (乐谱) and how to control your voice."

**【解析】** 文中指出，经验丰富的戴维斯老师将会教授各位如何识谱以及如何控制嗓音（如何发声发音）。并没有提到A教弹琴、B教跳舞或是D教组织派对。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段末尾 "If you want to come to the audition, please sign your name on the list outside the Music Room before next Monday."

**【解析】** 此处详细告知想参选（试音）同学的正确操作：在此前（下周一）去音乐教室外面的名单列表上签上自己的名字即可。并不是去交报名费给老师、带一本新书或者写信给校长。故选B。
