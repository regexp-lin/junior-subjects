> **04-22 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should teenagers do housework? Today, many middle school students are so busy with their studies that they never do any chores at home. Some parents think it's fine. They say, "My child's only job is to study hard." However, I disagree with this opinion. I believe doing housework is an important part of growing up.

② Doing housework teaches teenagers how to take care of themselves. Sweeping the floor, washing dishes, and cooking simple meals are basic survival (生存) skills. In a few years, these teenagers will go to university or move out to live on their own. If they don't learn how to do chores now, their rooms will become a big mess, and they will only eat expensive takeaway food in the future.

③ Moreover, doing household chores helps students learn responsibility (责任感). A family is like a team, and everyone should contribute (做贡献) to it. When teenagers take out the rubbish or fold their clothes, they realize that they are an important part of the family. They learn that keeping the house clean is not just their mother's job, but everyone's duty.

④ Also, doing housework is a good way to relax from schoolwork. After sitting at a desk and studying math for hours, washing dishes can actually let the brain rest. It involves physical movement, which is better than continually watching a computer screen.

⑤ Of course, studying is important, and parents shouldn't ask their kids to do all the heavy work. But spending 15 or 20 minutes a day on simple chores won't hurt their grades. Instead, it will help teenagers become independent and responsible adults.

**注释：** chore 家务事；survival 生存；mess 混乱；contribute 做贡献；duty 责任，义务；independent 独立的

---

### 选择题

1. What do some parents think is their child's "only job" according to paragraph ①?
A. To sweep the floor.
B. To study hard.
C. To play games.
D. To take out the rubbish.

2. Why does the writer think cooking simple meals is important?
A. Because teenagers can make money by cooking.
B. Because it is a basic survival skill for when they live on their own.
C. Because it is much easier than doing homework.
D. Because the school asks them to cook every day.

3. What can teenagers learn when they realize everyone in the family should contribute?
A. They learn responsibility and that keeping the house clean is everyone's duty.
B. They learn how to use a computer very fast.
C. They learn that their mother will do all the heavy work forever.
D. They learn how to buy cheap clothes.

4. Form paragraph ④, why does the writer say washing dishes is a good way to relax?
A. Because washing dishes is a very exciting sport.
B. Because you can listen to loud music while washing.
C. Because it involves physical movement and gives the brain a rest after studying.
D. Because watching a computer screen is too boring.

5. How much time does the writer suggest teenagers spend on chores a day?
A. As much time as possible.
B. 15 or 20 minutes.
C. At least two hours.
D. Only five minutes a week.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-3句 "...Some parents think it's fine. They say, 'My child's only job is to study hard.'"

**【解析】** 开卷第一段讲到，现在的初中生不干家务，有些父母还挺支持的，他们宣称自己孩子唯一的职责（only job）就是努力学习（to study hard）。A、C、D项与父母的这种观念大相径庭。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "Sweeping the floor, washing dishes, and cooking simple meals are basic survival (生存) skills... If they don't learn how to do chores now... they will only eat expensive takeaway food in the future."

**【解析】** 此段落指明了像扫地、刷碗和做简单的饭菜都是关乎将来搬出去住时所需掌握的基本“生存技能”（basic survival skills）。学会了才不会天天吃昂贵外卖。这对应了B选项的内容。绝不是A为了能做饭赚钱、也不是选项C比作业简单或D学校天天要求做饭。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解与判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "...doing household chores helps students learn responsibility... realize that they are an important part of the family. They learn that keeping the house clean is not just their mother's job, but everyone's duty."

**【解析】** 依据第三段内容分析，本段主张做家务能让学生学到责任感，明白自己是家庭里的一份子，认识到保持房屋整洁是所有人的共同义务（everyone's duty）而不单单是妈妈的工作。所以A项完美转述了这一观念。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "...washing dishes can actually let the brain rest. It involves physical movement, which is better than continually watching a computer screen."

**【解析】** 该段认为在伏案苦读数小时后，洗个碗可以通过身体的活动（physical movement）从而让大脑得到休整和放松（gives the brain a rest），这总比坐在书桌前继续盯着屏幕要强得多。这就印证了选项C。A项说是激动人心的运动，B听震耳欲聋音乐，D看屏幕太没劲全是错误的解释。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2句 "But spending 15 or 20 minutes a day on simple chores won't hurt their grades."

**【解析】** 结语中作者给出的家务时间建议是，每天花个15或20分钟（15 or 20 minutes）做些简单的家务，其非但不会伤害成绩反而有助于使青少年成为更具责任感的大人。所以B项的这组数字完全匹配。故选B。
