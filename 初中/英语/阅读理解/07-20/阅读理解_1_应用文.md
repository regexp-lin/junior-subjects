# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Rules for Using the Park Picnic Area**

② Welcome to our park picnic area! To make sure everyone can enjoy a pleasant (愉快的) outdoor experience, please read the following rules carefully before you come.

③ **Opening hours:** The picnic area is open from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. every day. No one is allowed to stay after 6:00 p.m. Please plan your visit within these hours.

④ **Reservation (预约):** If your group has more than 15 people, you need to book a picnic table in advance. You can call the park office at 3689-5120 or visit our website. Small groups can use any free table without booking. Tables are shared—please do not keep a table empty for over 30 minutes if others are waiting.

⑤ **During your stay:** Please keep the area clean. Put all rubbish into the bins. Do not make open fires. Do not bring pets. If you have music, please keep the volume (音量) low so others can relax. Thank you for your cooperation!

**注释：** pleasant 愉快的；reservation 预约；volume 音量

---

### 选择题

1. What are the opening hours of the park picnic area?
   A. 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
   B. 7:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
   C. 9:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.
   D. 8:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.

2. When does a group need to make a reservation?
   A. When there are more than 10 people.
   B. When there are more than 15 people.
   C. When there are more than 20 people.
   D. All groups must book in advance.

3. What should visitors do with their rubbish?
   A. Leave it on the table.
   B. Take it home with them.
   C. Put it into the bins.
   D. Burn it in the park.

4. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the picnic area?
   A. Bringing your own food.
   B. Playing quiet music.
   C. Sharing a table with strangers.
   D. Making open fires.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The picnic area is only for large groups.
   B. You must pay to use the picnic tables.
   C. Pets are welcome in the picnic area.
   D. The rules are made to help everyone enjoy the park in a clean and peaceful way.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The picnic area is open from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. every day."

**【解析】** 文中明确写出开放时间为每天上午8点至下午6点。A项正确。B、C、D项时段均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "If your group has more than 15 people, you need to book a picnic table in advance."

**【解析】** 文中说明超过15人的团体需提前预约野餐桌。B项正确。A项10人、C项20人、D项所有团体均需预约均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Put all rubbish into the bins."

**【解析】** 文中要求将所有垃圾放入垃圾桶。C项正确。A项放桌上、B项带回家、D项焚烧均与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Do not make open fires."

**【解析】** 文中明确禁止明火。D项正确。A项自带食物未禁止；B项播放轻音乐允许，仅需降低音量；C项与陌生人共桌未禁止。故选D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "To make sure everyone can enjoy a pleasant outdoor experience" 及全文规则

**【解析】** 开篇说明规则为保证大家享受愉快的户外体验，全文围绕清洁、秩序、噪音等规则展开，可推知这些规则旨在让每个人在干净、安宁的环境中享受公园。D项正确。A项仅限大型团体与原文矛盾；B项需付费未提及；C项宠物欢迎与"Do not bring pets"矛盾。故选D。
