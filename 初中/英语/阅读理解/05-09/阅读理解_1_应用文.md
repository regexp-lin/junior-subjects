# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Talent Show Auditions (海选) !**

② Calling all stars! Can you sing, dance, play an instrument, or perform magic tricks? The annual Oakwood Middle School Talent Show is back! This is your chance to shine on stage and show everyone what you can do.

③ **What kind of acts are we looking for?**
- Singing (Solo or in a group)
- Dancing (Ballet, hip-hop, traditional, etc.)
- Playing musical instruments (Piano, guitar, violin, etc.)
- Special talents (Magic, stand-up comedy (单口喜剧), juggling (杂耍), etc.)
*Note: All acts must be safe and suitable for a school audience.*

④ **Audition Details:**
- **Date:** Next Wednesday and Thursday (May 17th & 18th)
- **Time:** 3:30 PM - 5:30 PM
- **Place:** The School Music Room

⑤ **Important Rules for the Audition:**
1. Each act must not be longer than 3 minutes.
2. If you need special equipment (like a CD player or a microphone), please tell us when you sign up.
3. You must fill out an application form (申请表) before the audition.

⑥ **Prizes for the Final Show!**
The main Talent Show will be held on Friday, June 2nd. The top three acts will win amazing prizes:
- **1st Prize:** A $100 gift card to City Music Store.
- **2nd Prize:** Two free tickets to the new amusement park.
- **3rd Prize:** A big box of high-quality artist's paints.

⑦ **How to sign up?**
Please get an application form from Mr. Davis in the teachers' office (Room 205). You must return the completed form by Next Monday (May 15th). Don't miss this exciting opportunity!

**注释：** audition 海选/试镜；act 节目/表演；suitable 合适的；equipment 设备；application form 申请表；opportunity 机会

### 选择题

1. What is the purpose of this notice?
A. To sell tickets for the new amusement park.
B. To look for students to perform in the school Talent Show.
C. To teach students how to play the piano and guitar.
D. To ask parents to buy high-quality artist's paints.

2. Which of the following acts is NOT mentioned in the notice?
A. Singing in a group.
B. Playing the violin.
C. Cooking a delicious meal.
D. Performing magic tricks.

3. How long can each audition act be?
A. Exactly 5 minutes.
B. Between 3 to 5 minutes.
C. As long as the student wants.
D. Not longer than 3 minutes.

4. Form paragraph ⑥, what will the winner of the 1st prize get?
A. Two free tickets to the amusement park.
B. A $100 gift card to a music store.
C. A box of high-quality artist's paints.
D. A brand new microphone and CD player.

5. What must a student do before next Monday if he or she wants to join the audition?
A. Practice the performance in Room 205.
B. Buy an application form from City Music Store.
C. Get a form from Mr. Davis and return it completed.
D. Buy a ticket for the final show on June 2nd.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨意图推断

**【定位】** 第①段和第②段 "School Talent Show Auditions... The annual Oakwood Middle School Talent Show is back! This is your chance to shine..."

**【解析】** 标题及开篇招募语一目了然：这是学校才艺秀的海选（Auditions），呼唤会唱歌跳舞变魔术的学生报名来展示自己。因此发布这则通告的目的是寻找能在才艺秀上表演的学生（look for students to perform）。A、C、D均不符合通告主体。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解与判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "What kind of acts are we looking for? - Singing... - Dancing... - Playing musical instruments... - Special talents (Magic...)"

**【解析】** 题目问在寻找的才艺表演（acts）中哪个没有被提及。A（群体合唱）、B（拉小提琴）、D（变魔术）在文中都有原词提及。而C选项“做一顿美味的饭菜（Cooking a delicious meal）”并没出现在才艺清单里。本题选NOT，故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段海选规则第1条 "1. Each act must not be longer than 3 minutes."

**【解析】** 海选的重要规则中第一条明确规定：每个节目的时间不得超过3分钟（must not be longer than 3 minutes）。选项D是对这一规定的准确复述。A、B、C都与规定不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段奖品说明 "1st Prize: A $100 gift card to City Music Store."

**【解析】** 最终表演的头奖（1st Prize）奖品是什么？原文写得很请清楚：是一张价值100美元的城市音乐商店礼品卡。A是二等奖，C是三等奖，D是海选提供的设备非奖品。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "Please get an application form from Mr. Davis... You must return the completed form by Next Monday..." 结合 第⑤段第3条 "You must fill out an application form before the audition."

**【解析】** 根据最后一段的报名指南，如果学生想参加海选，必须在下周一之前做何准备？必须去205室戴维斯老师那里拿到申请表，并在下周一前将填好的表交回去（return the completed form）。这和C选项一致。A去办公室排练、B去商店买表、D买总决赛门票等全错。故选C。
