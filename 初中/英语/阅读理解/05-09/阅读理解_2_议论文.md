# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should fast food advertisements (广告) be banned (禁止) on children's television channels? This is a hot topic for debate. Many fast food companies argue that they have the right to advertise their products. However, considering the rising problem of childhood obesity (肥胖) and poor health, I powerfully believe that marketing fast food to children should be stopped.

② The main problem with fast food ads is that they are designed to be extremely attractive to young minds. These commercials (商业广告) don't just show hamburgers or fries; they show bright colors, happy cartoon characters, and promise free toys hidden inside the meal boxes. Children, who are not mature (成熟的) enough to make healthy choices, are easily tricked by these fun images. They end up begging their parents to buy them unhealthy meals just to get the toy.

③ Furthermore, fast food is packed with high levels of sugar, salt, and fat, but very little real nutrition (营养). When children eat these meals frequently, it leads to serious health problems. More and more kids today are suffering from obesity, putting them at risk for heart disease and diabetes (糖尿病) later in life. By allowing these ads on children's TV, we are quietly encouraging unhealthy eating habits.

④ Some people might say that it is the parents' job to say "no" to their children. While parents certainly have a responsibility, it is unfair to make them fight a daily battle against billion-dollar advertising companies. These companies spend huge amounts of money studying how to make children want their food. Banning these ads would make the parents' job of teaching healthy eating much easier.

⑤ In conclusion, children's health is far more important than fast food companies' profits (利润). Banning fast food advertisements on children's TV is a necessary step to protect our younger generation from the dangers of unhealthy eating.

**注释：** advertisement 广告；ban 禁止；obesity 肥胖；commercial 商业广告；mature 成熟的；diabetes 糖尿病；profit 利润

---

### 选择题

1. What is the writer's main opinion in paragraph ①?
A. Fast food companies have the right to advertise everywhere.
B. Children should be encouraged to eat more fast food.
C. Fast food advertisements should be banned on children's TV.
D. Television is a bad invention for children's eyes.

2. How do fast food commercials attract children according to paragraph ②?
A. By showing the nutritional details of the hamburgers and fries.
B. By showing bright colors, cartoon characters, and promising free toys.
C. By telling children that fast food makes them taller and smarter.
D. By playing sad music to make children feel sympathy (同情).

3. What are the health negative effects (负面影响) of eating fast food frequently?
A. It makes children lose too much weight.
B. It causes children to develop a strong distaste for toys.
C. It leads to obesity and increases the risk of heart disease and diabetes.
D. It makes children forget how to watch television.

4. Why does the writer think it is unfair to just blame parents (责怪父母) in paragraph ④?
A. Because parents are too busy working to watch TV with their children.
B. Because parents also like to eat fast food very much.
C. Because billion-dollar companies spend huge money to trick children, making it a hard battle for parents.
D. Because parents don't know how to cook healthy food themselves.

5. What does the writer compare in the conclusion (paragraph ⑤)?
A. The price of hamburgers and the price of toys.
B. Children's health and fast food companies' profits.
C. Television channels and internet websites.
D. Healthy vegetables and fast food fries.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 作者观点初定/细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "...I powerfully believe that marketing fast food to children should be stopped."

**【解析】** 开篇直接点名论题后，作者强力表态：向儿童推销快餐的行为应该被停止。也即是在儿童电视频道禁止快餐广告。这与选项C（Fast food advertisements should be banned on children's TV）完全契合。A为快餐公司的说辞，B、D均非作者观点。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2-3句 "These commercials don't just show hamburgers or fries; they show bright colors, happy cartoon characters, and promise free toys... Children... are easily tricked by these fun images."

**【解析】** 快餐广告是怎样吸引小孩子的？第二段指出，广告不仅仅展示食物，更主要是展示鲜艳的颜色、欢乐的卡通人物，并且承诺餐盒里藏有免费玩具。这恰恰是B选项的描述。A展示营养细节、C说能长高变聪明、D放悲伤音乐，都是不符合广告手法的伪造项。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "When children eat these meals frequently, it leads to serious health problems. More and more kids today are suffering from obesity, putting them at risk for heart disease and diabetes..."

**【解析】** 经常吃快餐会带来哪些负面健康影响？原文写道，高糖高盐高脂带来严重的健康问题：越来越多的孩子患上肥胖症（obesity），并增加了以后患心脏病和糖尿病（heart disease and diabetes）的风险。选项C（导致肥胖并增加心脏病及糖尿病风险）是精确归纳。A减肥过多、B讨厌玩具、D忘记怎么看电视，全是错的。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-3句 "...it is unfair to make them fight a daily battle against billion-dollar advertising companies. These companies spend huge amounts of money studying how to make children want their food."

**【解析】** 为什么作者觉得只让父母去对孩子说“不”是不公平的？因为这是让父母每天去对抗投入巨资钻研如何引诱孩子的、价值十亿美元级别的广告公司。这是一场极不平等的战争。C项准确捕捉到了这层逻辑：巨头公司砸重金引诱，让父母防不胜防。A太忙、B父母自己爱吃、D父母不会做健康餐等理由文中并未提及。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 总结段第⑤段第1句 "In conclusion, children's health is far more important than fast food companies' profits (利润)."

**【解析】** 在最后一段结论中，作者拿什么和什么进行了权衡对比？原文说：孩子们的健康远比快餐公司的利润（children's health is far more important than fast food companies' profits）更为重要。B选项完美对应了这一对比对象。故选B。
