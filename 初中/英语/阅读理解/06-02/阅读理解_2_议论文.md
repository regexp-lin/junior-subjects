# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should zoos still exist? This question has caused much debate in recent years. Some people think zoos are necessary for education and animal protection. Others believe keeping animals in cages is wrong. Let's look at both sides of the argument.

② Those who support zoos say they bring important benefits. First, zoos help children and adults learn about animals they might never see in the wild. Seeing a real elephant or panda can make people care more about protecting them. Second, many zoos work on conservation (保护) — they help save endangered (濒危的) animals and some even return them to nature. Third, zoos give medical care to sick or hurt animals. Without zoos, some rare species might disappear forever.

③ However, those who are against zoos have strong reasons too. They say that animals need to live in their natural environment. Life in a cage, no matter how large, can never be the same as life in the wild. Some animals suffer from stress or illness when they cannot follow their natural habits. Besides, some zoos in the past cared more about making money than about animal welfare (福利). This has made many people lose trust in zoos.

④ So what should we do? Many experts suggest that zoos must change. Modern zoos should focus on education and conservation, not entertainment alone. They should provide bigger spaces and better living conditions that are closer to the animals' natural homes. In this way, zoos can still play a useful role in society while treating animals with more respect.

⑤ In conclusion, the question is not simply "zoos yes or no." The real question is: can zoos become better places that truly help both people and animals? If zoos continue to improve, they may have a place in our future. If not, more and more people will call for them to close.

**注释：** conservation 保护；endangered 濒危的；welfare 福利

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what benefit do zoos bring for education?
   A. Seeing real animals can help people care more about protecting them.
   B. Zoos teach people how to hunt animals.
   C. Zoos provide free food for visitors.
   D. Zoos help students finish homework faster.

2. What do people against zoos believe?
   A. Zoos make too much money.
   B. Animals need to live in their natural environment, not in cages.
   C. Zoos are too expensive to run.
   D. Zoos should be built in every city.

3. What do many experts suggest zoos should do?
   A. Close down all zoos at once.
   B. Focus only on making money.
   C. Change to focus on education and conservation, with better living conditions.
   D. Stop accepting visitors.

4. The underlined word "welfare" in paragraph ③ probably means ______.
   A. food and drink
   B. entertainment
   C. money
   D. health and well-being

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. The writer thinks all zoos must close immediately.
   B. The writer believes zoos have no future at all.
   C. The writer thinks zoos can have a future if they improve and treat animals better.
   D. The writer supports zoos without any conditions.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Seeing a real elephant or panda can make people care more about protecting them."

**【解析】** 支持者认为在动物园看到真实动物能让人更关心保护它们。A 项正确。B 项"教人们打猎"、C 项"提供免费食物"、D 项"帮助完成作业"均与教育效益不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They say that animals need to live in their natural environment. Life in a cage, no matter how large, can never be the same as life in the wild."

**【解析】** 反对者认为动物应生活在自然环境中，笼中生活无法与野外相比。B 项正确。A、C、D 项均非反对者核心理由。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Many experts suggest that zoos must change. Modern zoos should focus on education and conservation, not entertainment alone. They should provide bigger spaces and better living conditions that are closer to the animals' natural homes."

**【解析】** 专家建议动物园应改革：侧重教育和保护，提供更大空间与更贴近自然的环境。C 项正确。A 项"立即关闭"、B 项"只赚钱"、D 项"不再接待游客"均与专家建议矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段 "cared more about making money than about animal welfare"

**【解析】** welfare 与 making money 相对，指动物所受到的照料与福祉。D 项 health and well-being（健康与福祉）与之相符。A 项"食物饮料"、B 项"娱乐"、C 项"金钱"皆不符语境。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The real question is: can zoos become better places that truly help both people and animals? If zoos continue to improve, they may have a place in our future. If not, more and more people will call for them to close."

**【解析】** 作者未完全反对或支持，而是提出条件：动物园若能持续改进、真正兼顾人与动物利益，则仍有存在价值；否则将面临关闭呼声。C 项准确概括此立场。A 项"立即关闭"、B 项"毫无未来"、D 项"无条件支持"均与作者观点不符。故选 C。
