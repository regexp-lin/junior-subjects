# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Swimming Classes – Now Open for Registration (报名)!**

② Do you want to learn to swim during the summer holiday? Sunshine Sports Center is now offering swimming classes for children aged 6 to 14. Swimming is a great way to stay healthy and safe. It can help you build a strong body and learn important life-saving skills. Our classes are taught by experienced coaches who have helped hundreds of children learn to swim safely.

③ **Class Times & Levels:** We have beginner, middle and advanced classes. Beginner classes are held every Monday and Wednesday from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. Middle and advanced classes run on Tuesday and Thursday at the same time. Each class has no more than 12 students, so each child gets enough attention from the coach.

④ **Safety First:** Safety is our top priority (首要任务). All our pools are cleaned every day and checked by professional staff. Every child must wear a swim cap. Life jackets are provided for beginners. Parents are welcome to watch from the waiting area. We also teach water safety rules in every lesson.

⑤ **How to Sign Up:** Visit our center at 128 Green Street or call 0755-2345678. The fee is 800 yuan for 10 lessons. Early registration (报名) before June 15th gets a 10% discount. Do not wait — places are limited!

**注释：** registration 报名；priority 首要任务；coach 教练

---

### 选择题

1. Who can join the swimming classes?
   A. Children aged 4 to 10.
   B. Children aged 6 to 14.
   C. Adults only.
   D. Anyone who likes swimming.

2. When are the beginner classes held?
   A. Every Tuesday and Thursday from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m.
   B. Every Monday and Wednesday from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m.
   C. Every day in the afternoon.
   D. Only on weekends.

3. Which of the following is mentioned as a safety rule?
   A. Children must bring their own life jackets.
   B. Parents cannot watch the class.
   C. Every child must wear a swim cap.
   D. The pool is cleaned once a week.

4. What can we infer from the last paragraph about early registration?
   A. Early registration costs more money.
   B. There are unlimited places for all students.
   C. Registering before June 15th can help you save some money.
   D. You can only sign up after June 15th.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To tell people how to swim safely.
   B. To introduce a summer swimming class and encourage people to sign up.
   C. To explain why swimming is good for health.
   D. To compare different sports centers.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "swimming classes for children aged 6 to 14."

**【解析】** 原文明确写出招收 6 至 14 岁儿童。A 项"4 至 10 岁"年龄不符；B 项正确；C 项"仅成人"与原文相反；D 项"任何喜欢游泳的人"范围过泛，原文限定年龄。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Beginner classes are held every Monday and Wednesday from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m."

**【解析】** 初级班时间为周一和周三上午 9:00—10:00。B 项正确。A 项"周二周四"为中级和高级班时间；C 项"每天下午"、D 项"仅周末"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Every child must wear a swim cap."

**【解析】** 原文规定每位孩子必须佩戴泳帽。C 项正确。A 项"自带救生衣"与"Life jackets are provided"矛盾；B 项"家长不能观看"与"Parents are welcome to watch"矛盾；D 项"每周清洁一次"与"cleaned every day"矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Early registration before June 15th gets a 10% discount."

**【解析】** 6 月 15 日前报名享 10% 优惠，可推知提早报名能省钱。C 项正确。A 项"早报名更贵"与 discount 矛盾；B 项"名额无限"与"places are limited"矛盾；D 项"只能 6 月 15 日后报名"与原文相反。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文为招生广告，介绍课程、时间、安全及报名方式，结尾呼吁报名。

**【解析】** 本文为游泳班招生广告，旨在介绍课程并吸引家长/学生报名。B 项正确。A 项"讲解安全游泳方法"仅为内容之一；C 项"解释游泳对健康的好处"仅为吸引点；D 项"比较不同体育中心"未提及。故选 B。
