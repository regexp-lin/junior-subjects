# Summer Reading Challenge — Greenfield Public Library

①Welcome to the Greenfield Public Library's Summer Reading Challenge! This year's theme is "Read Around the World." From July 1st to August 31st, young readers aged 10–15 are invited to join us on an exciting reading journey.

**② How It Works**

| Level | Books to Read | Prize |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| Bronze | 3 books | A bookmark set |
| Silver | 6 books | A notebook + pen |
| Gold | 10 books | A $20 gift card for the bookstore |

③To join, simply sign up at the front desk or on our website (www.greenfieldlibrary.org). You will receive a reading passport. Each time you finish a book, visit the library and tell a librarian about the story. They will stamp your passport.

**④ Special Events**

- **July 10 (Sat):** Meet the Author — children's writer Sarah Miller will talk about her new book *The Secret Island*.
- **July 24 (Sat):** World Culture Day — dress up in clothes from different countries and share a short story about that culture.
- **August 14 (Sat):** Movie Afternoon — watch a film based on a famous children's book (free popcorn!).

⑤All events are free, but you must sign up in advance because space is limited. Each event can hold up to 50 people. Don't miss out — register early!

⑥For more information, call us at 555-0178 or email summerread@greenfieldlibrary.org. We can't wait to see you this summer!

**1. Who can take part in the Summer Reading Challenge?**
   A. Children under 5 years old.
   B. Young people between 10 and 15.
   C. Adults who love reading.
   D. Only students from Greenfield School.

**2. What will you get if you read 6 books?**
   A. A bookmark set.
   B. A notebook and a pen.
   C. A $20 gift card.
   D. A free movie ticket.

**3. How can readers record the books they have read?**
   A. By writing a book report online.
   B. By sending an email to the library.
   C. By getting a stamp on their reading passport.
   D. By taking a photo of each book.

**4. What can you do on July 24?**
   A. Meet a famous children's writer.
   B. Watch a film based on a book.
   C. Learn about different cultures and share stories.
   D. Get free popcorn and drinks.

**5. What can we learn from the passage?**
   A. The reading challenge lasts for three months.
   B. There is no limit on the number of people for each event.
   C. You need to pay for the special events.
   D. You should register for the events ahead of time.

## 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "young readers aged 10–15 are invited to join us on an exciting reading journey."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到活动邀请 10 至 15 岁的年轻读者参加。A 项"5岁以下儿童"与原文年龄范围不符；C 项"热爱阅读的成人"原文未提及；D 项"仅限 Greenfield 学校的学生"属于无中生有，原文没有学校限制。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段表格 Silver 级别 "6 books — A notebook + pen"

**【解析】** 根据表格信息，读完 6 本书达到 Silver 级别，奖品是笔记本和钢笔。A 项"书签套装"是 Bronze 级别（3本书）的奖品；C 项"20美元礼品卡"是 Gold 级别（10本书）的奖品；D 项"免费电影票"在表格中不存在。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "You will receive a reading passport. Each time you finish a book, visit the library and tell a librarian about the story. They will stamp your passport."

**【解析】** 原文说明读者会收到一本阅读护照，每读完一本书，图书馆员会在护照上盖章来记录。A 项"在网上写读书报告"原文未提及；B 项"给图书馆发邮件"不是记录方式；D 项"给每本书拍照"属于无中生有。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "July 24 (Sat): World Culture Day — dress up in clothes from different countries and share a short story about that culture."

**【解析】** 7月24日是"世界文化日"，参加者可以穿不同国家的服装并分享关于该文化的短故事。A 项"见著名儿童作家"是 7月10日的活动；B 项"看电影"是 8月14日的活动；D 项"获得免费爆米花和饮料"张冠李戴，免费爆米花是 8月14日电影活动的，且未提及饮料。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "All events are free, but you must sign up in advance because space is limited."

**【解析】** 原文说"所有活动免费，但必须提前报名，因为名额有限"，因此可推断需要提前注册。A 项"阅读挑战持续三个月"有误，活动从7月1日到8月31日，共两个月；B 项"每场活动人数无限制"与原文"Each event can hold up to 50 people"矛盾；C 项"特别活动需要付费"与"All events are free"矛盾。故选 D。
