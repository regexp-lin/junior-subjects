# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **City Art Museum — Winter Visit Guide 2026**

② Welcome to City Art Museum! We are happy to invite you to explore art from both China and the West. Before you visit, please read the following information.

③ **Opening Hours:**
| Day | Time |
|-----|------|
| Tuesday — Friday | 9:00 AM — 5:00 PM |
| Saturday & Sunday | 9:00 AM — 6:00 PM |
| Monday | Closed |

④ **Ticket Prices:**
- Adults: ¥30
- Students (with student ID): ¥15
- Children under 6: Free
- Free for all visitors on the first Sunday of every month

⑤ **Special Activities in February:**
- **Feb 7 (Saturday), 2:00 PM:** Chinese Traditional Painting Workshop — Learn to paint bamboo and plum flowers. All materials provided.
- **Feb 14 (Saturday), 10:00 AM:** Western Art Talk — A guide will introduce famous Western artists and their works.
- **Feb 21 (Saturday), 3:00 PM:** Family Art Day — Parents and children work together to make paper cuttings (剪纸).

⑥ **Visit Tips:**
- No food or drinks inside the exhibition halls (展厅).
- Photos are allowed, but do not use flash (闪光灯).
- Please keep quiet and show respect for other visitors.

⑦ For group visits (10 people or more), please call **555-7890** or email **visit@cityartmuseum.org** at least three days in advance.

**注释：** exhibition 展览；hall 大厅；flash 闪光灯；workshop 工作坊；material 材料

---

### 选择题

1. When is the museum closed?
   A. On Monday.
   B. On Tuesday.
   C. On Saturday.
   D. On Sunday.

2. Who does NOT need to pay for a ticket?
   A. Adults.
   B. Students with student ID.
   C. Children under 6.
   D. Visitors on weekdays.

3. What can visitors do on February 7?
   A. Listen to a talk about Western art.
   B. Join a Chinese painting workshop and learn to paint bamboo.
   C. Take part in Family Art Day with their parents.
   D. Take photos with flash inside the hall.

4. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the museum?
   A. Eating snacks in the exhibition halls.
   B. Taking photos without using flash.
   C. Keeping quiet in the halls.
   D. Visiting with family.

5. How many people need to book in advance for a group visit?
   A. 5 or more.
   B. 8 or more.
   C. 9 or more.
   D. 10 or more.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Monday — Closed"

**【解析】** 根据开放时间表格，周一为闭馆日。B 项周二、C 项周六、D 项周日均有开放时间，只有周一明确标注"Closed"。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Children under 6: Free"

**【解析】** 原文明确标注6岁以下儿童免费。A 项成人需付30元；B 项持学生证学生需付15元；D 项"工作日访客"不区分年龄，非全部免费，只有每月首个周日才是全免费日。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Feb 7 (Saturday), 2:00 PM: Chinese Traditional Painting Workshop — Learn to paint bamboo and plum flowers."

**【解析】** 2月7日的活动是中国传统绘画工作坊，学习画竹子和梅花。A 项西方艺术讲座在2月14日；C 项家庭艺术日在2月21日；D 项使用闪光灯拍照是参观须知中明确禁止的。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "No food or drinks inside the exhibition halls."

**【解析】** 题干问"以下哪项不被允许"，原文明确禁止在展厅内进食或饮用，故 A 项"在展厅内吃零食"不被允许。B 项不闪灯拍照是允许的；C 项保持安静是要求做的，非禁止；D 项与家人参观未禁止。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "For group visits (10 people or more), please call..."

**【解析】** 原文明确要求10人及以上团体需提前预约。A 项5人、B 项8人、C 项9人均低于原文规定的10人；只有 D 项"10人及以上"与原文一致。故选 D。
