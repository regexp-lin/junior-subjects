# 阅读理解 第2篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **Welcome to City Science Museum!**

Discover the wonders of science through our exciting exhibitions and hands-on activities. Whether you love dinosaurs, space, or robots, there is something for everyone!

② **Opening Hours & Tickets:**

|  | Weekdays (Tue–Fri) | Weekends & Holidays |
|---|---|---|
| Opening Hours | 9:00 am – 5:00 pm | 9:00 am – 6:00 pm |
| Adult Ticket | ¥60 | ¥80 |
| Student Ticket (with student ID) | ¥30 | ¥40 |
| Children under 6 | Free | Free |

**Note:** The museum is **closed on Mondays**.

③ **Floor Guide:**

| Floor | Exhibition |
|-------|-----------|
| 1F | Dinosaur World — See life-size dinosaur models! |
| 2F | Space Adventure — Learn about planets and stars |
| 3F | Robot Lab — Build and program your own robot |
| 4F | Ocean Discovery — Explore life under the sea |

④ **Museum Rules:**
- No food or drinks inside the exhibition halls
- No flash photography
- Please do not touch the exhibits unless marked "Hands-on"
- Children under 12 must be with an adult

⑤ **Special Event This Month:**

"Night at the Museum" — **March 15, 7:00–9:00 pm**

Watch the stars through our giant telescope on the rooftop!

**Limited to 50 people. Book online at www.citysciencemuseum.com**

---

**注释：** exhibition 展览；telescope 望远镜；flash photography 闪光灯拍摄

---

### 题目

1. When is the museum closed?
   A. On Tuesdays.
   B. On Mondays.
   C. On Saturdays.
   D. On Sundays.

2. How much will a student pay for a ticket on Sunday?
   A. ¥30.
   B. ¥60.
   C. ¥40.
   D. ¥80.

3. If you are interested in sea animals, which floor should you visit?
   A. 1F.
   B. 2F.
   C. 3F.
   D. 4F.

4. We can infer that the "Night at the Museum" event ________.
   A. needs to be booked ahead of time
   B. takes place every night of the week
   C. is open to unlimited visitors
   D. is only for museum workers

5. Which of the following is NOT allowed in the museum?
   A. Taking photos without flash.
   B. Visiting with a 5-year-old child.
   C. Bringing drinks into the exhibition halls.
   D. Touching exhibits marked "Hands-on."

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段注释 "The museum is closed on Mondays."

**【解析】** 原文在开放时间表格下方明确注明"博物馆周一闭馆"。B 项"On Mondays"与原文完全一致。A 项"On Tuesdays"与原文矛盾——周二属于工作日开放时间（Tue–Fri）；C 项"On Saturdays"和 D 项"On Sundays"均属于周末时段，博物馆在周末开放且延长至下午6点。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段表格中 Student Ticket 一行，"Weekends & Holidays"列：¥40。

**【解析】** 周日属于周末（Weekends），学生票价为¥40。C 项与表格数据完全吻合。A 项¥30是工作日的学生票价，属于张冠李戴；B 项¥60是工作日的成人票价；D 项¥80是周末的成人票价。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 应用文中的表格题需要准确定位行和列的交叉点。本题需要同时确认"Student Ticket"（行）和"Weekends & Holidays"（列）两个条件。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段楼层导览表中 4F 一行："Ocean Discovery — Explore life under the sea"

**【解析】** 原文楼层导览明确标注4楼是"Ocean Discovery"（海洋发现），主题为"探索海底生命"，与对海洋动物感兴趣的需求对应。D 项 4F 正确。A 项 1F 是"Dinosaur World"（恐龙世界）；B 项 2F 是"Space Adventure"（太空探险）；C 项 3F 是"Robot Lab"（机器人实验室）。这三个选项都属于张冠李戴。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "Limited to 50 people. Book online at www.citysciencemuseum.com"

**【解析】** 原文注明该活动"限50人"且需要"在线预订"（Book online），由此可以推断参加这个活动需要提前预约。A 项"需要提前预订"是对原文信息的合理推断。B 项"每天晚上都举办"与原文"March 15"（仅限3月15日一天）矛盾；C 项"对参观者人数不限"与"Limited to 50 people"直接矛盾；D 项"只面向博物馆工作人员"属于无中生有，原文未做此限制。故选 A。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向选择）

**【定位】** 第④段博物馆规则："No food or drinks inside the exhibition halls"、"No flash photography"、"Please do not touch the exhibits unless marked 'Hands-on'"。

**【解析】** 本题要求选出博物馆中"不被允许"的行为。A 项"不使用闪光灯拍照"——规则禁止的是"flash photography"（闪光灯拍摄），不用闪光灯拍照是允许的；B 项"带5岁的孩子参观"——规则要求12岁以下儿童须有成人陪同，5岁儿童有成人陪伴即可进入，且6岁以下免费，说明是允许的；D 项"触摸标记为'Hands-on'的展品"——规则说"除标记为'Hands-on'的展品外不得触摸"，意即标记了的可以触摸，是允许的。C 项"将饮料带入展厅"——规则明确禁止"No food or drinks inside the exhibition halls"，这是不被允许的。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 逆向选择题（选 NOT allowed 的选项）需要逐一将每个选项与原文规则对照。特别注意包含 "unless"（除非）等条件词的规则——它们表示在特定条件下行为是被允许的。
