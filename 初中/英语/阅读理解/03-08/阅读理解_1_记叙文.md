> **03-08 第1篇**

# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Li Hua didn't want to spend her winter vacation at her grandmother's house in the countryside. She thought it would be boring — no shopping centers, no fast internet, and none of her friends nearby. But her parents insisted, so she packed her bag and went.

② On the first morning, Grandma woke her up early. "Come, help me make dumplings for the Spring Festival," she said. Li Hua had never made dumplings before. She watched as Grandma's wrinkled hands moved quickly, folding each piece of dough into a perfect shape. "Your great-grandmother taught me this when I was your age," Grandma said softly.

③ Li Hua tried to copy her grandmother, but her first few dumplings looked terrible — some were too flat, and others fell apart. She felt frustrated and wanted to give up. But Grandma just laughed gently and said, "Don't worry. Nobody gets it right the first time. The secret is patience." She held Li Hua's hands and guided her step by step.

④ By the afternoon, Li Hua could finally make dumplings that stayed together. They weren't as beautiful as Grandma's, but she felt proud of every single one. That evening, the whole family sat together and ate the dumplings. "These are the best dumplings I've ever had," said her father with a big smile. Li Hua knew he was just being kind, but his words made her heart warm.

⑤ When it was time to leave, Li Hua hugged her grandmother tightly. "I'll come back next year," she promised. On the train home, she realized that the best memories are not made in shopping centers or on the internet. They are made with the people you love.

---

**注释：** wrinkled 布满皱纹的；dough 面团；frustrated 沮丧的；patience 耐心

---

### 题目

1. Why didn't Li Hua want to go to her grandmother's house?
   A. Because she was afraid of the countryside.
   B. Because she thought it would be boring.
   C. Because she didn't like her grandmother.
   D. Because she had too much homework to do.

2. Who taught Grandma how to make dumplings?
   A. Li Hua's great-grandmother.
   B. A cooking teacher at school.
   C. Li Hua's mother.
   D. Grandma learned by herself.

3. What did Grandma say was the secret to making good dumplings?
   A. Using the best flour.
   B. Watching cooking videos online.
   C. Learning from a recipe book.
   D. Having patience.

4. What can we infer from the father's words in Paragraph ④?
   A. The dumplings really tasted terrible.
   B. He wanted Li Hua to feel encouraged and happy.
   C. He thought Li Hua should practice more.
   D. He didn't want to eat any more dumplings.

5. What did Li Hua learn from her visit?
   A. Making dumplings is easy if you practice enough.
   B. The countryside is a better place to live than the city.
   C. The best memories come from time spent with loved ones.
   D. The internet is not important for teenagers.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "She thought it would be boring — no shopping centers, no fast internet, and none of her friends nearby."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出李华不想去奶奶家，因为她觉得那里会很无聊——没有购物中心、没有快速网络、朋友也不在身边。B 项"因为她觉得会很无聊"与原文完全吻合。A 项"因为她害怕农村"原文未提及恐惧的情绪；C 项"因为她不喜欢奶奶"原文无依据，后文反而体现了祖孙情深；D 项"因为她作业太多"属于无中生有。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "Your great-grandmother taught me this when I was your age," Grandma said softly.

**【解析】** 奶奶亲口说"你的曾祖母在我像你这么大的时候教了我这个"，说明是李华的曾祖母（great-grandmother）教奶奶包饺子的。A 项与原文完全一致。B 项"学校的烹饪老师"属于无中生有；C 项"李华的妈妈"辈分关系错误——李华的妈妈是奶奶的儿媳，不可能在奶奶小时候教她；D 项"奶奶自学的"与原文矛盾。故选 A。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第4句 "The secret is patience."

**【解析】** 当李华因为包不好饺子而感到沮丧时，奶奶告诉她"秘诀是耐心"。D 项"Having patience"与原文完全对应。A 项"用最好的面粉"原文未提及面粉的品质；B 项"在网上看烹饪视频"与奶奶家的传统手工教学场景不符，且原文未提及；C 项"从食谱书里学"也属于无中生有。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段第3-4句 "'These are the best dumplings I've ever had,' said her father with a big smile. Li Hua knew he was just being kind, but his words made her heart warm."

**【解析】** 爸爸说"这是我吃过的最好的饺子"并露出了大大的微笑。虽然李华知道爸爸只是出于好意（being kind），但这些话让她的心里觉得温暖。由此可以推断，爸爸说这番话的目的是为了鼓励李华、让她感到开心和自豪。B 项"他想让李华感到被鼓励和开心"是合理的一步推断。A 项"饺子真的很难吃"与爸爸夸奖并微笑的行为矛盾；C 项"他觉得李华应该多练习"原文未表达这层意思——爸爸是在肯定而非要求；D 项"他不想再吃了"与"the best dumplings"的赞美语气相反。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 推理判断题需要结合人物的语言、动作和情感进行综合推断。本题的关键线索是"with a big smile"和"made her heart warm"——这些细节揭示了爸爸话语背后的真正目的。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后两句 "She realized that the best memories are not made in shopping centers or on the internet. They are made with the people you love."

**【解析】** 文章讲述了李华从不情愿到乡下过寒假，到和奶奶一起包饺子、感受家庭温暖，最终领悟到"最好的回忆是和你爱的人一起创造的"。C 项"最好的回忆来自与所爱之人共度的时光"准确概括了全文主旨。A 项"只要多练习，包饺子就很容易"以偏概全，包饺子只是故事的情节，不是主旨；B 项"农村比城市更适合居住"过度推断——文章只是说李华在农村有了美好回忆，并未比较两地的居住条件；D 项"网络对青少年不重要"以偏概全——原文提到网络只是为了和有温度的家庭时光做对比，并非否定网络的重要性。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 记叙文的主旨往往在文章结尾以主人公的感悟或总结性语句呈现。本文最后两句是"画龙点睛"式的主旨句，直接揭示了故事要传达的核心信息。
