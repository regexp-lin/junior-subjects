# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School IT Room Rules**

② Dear students, welcome to the school IT room. To make sure everyone can use the computers safely and well, please follow the rules below.

③ **Before you use a computer:** First, wash your hands or use hand sanitizer (消毒液). Food and drinks are not allowed in the IT room. Second, sit properly and keep your eyes at a good distance from the screen. Third, tell the teacher if you find any problem with the computer or the Internet.

④ **When you are using a computer:** Only visit websites that your teacher tells you to use. Do not download (下载) anything without permission. Do not change any settings on the computer. When you finish your work, save your files in your own folder and log out properly.

⑤ **When you leave:** Turn off the computer in the correct way. Push your chair under the desk. Put rubbish in the bin. Remember that the IT room is for all students—take good care of it!

**注释：** sanitizer 消毒液；download 下载

---

### 选择题

1. What should students do before using a computer?
   A. Eat a snack to have more energy.
   B. Wash hands or use hand sanitizer, and sit properly.
   C. Download some useful software first.
   D. Change the computer settings.

2. What can students do in the IT room?
   A. Eat and drink while using the computer.
   B. Visit any website they like.
   C. Save files in their own folder and log out when finished.
   D. Leave the computer on when they go away.

3. What must students do when they leave the IT room?
   A. Leave their files on the desktop.
   B. Leave the screen on for the next student.
   C. Tell the teacher about every website they visited.
   D. Turn off the computer, push the chair under the desk, and put rubbish in the bin.

4. According to the passage, why are these rules important?
   A. To make sure students can use the computers safely and well, and the room stays good for everyone.
   B. To stop students from using the IT room.
   C. To help teachers work faster.
   D. To reduce the number of computers.

5. What can we infer from the rules?
   A. Students can bring their own computers to the IT room.
   B. The school wants students to use the IT room in a safe and responsible way.
   C. Students are not allowed to use the Internet at all.
   D. The IT room is only open to teachers.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "First, wash your hands or use hand sanitizer... Second, sit properly and keep your eyes at a good distance from the screen"

**【解析】** 使用电脑前应洗手或使用消毒液，并保持正确坐姿。B项正确。A项吃零食与"Food and drinks are not allowed"矛盾；C、D项均为文中禁止的行为。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "When you finish your work, save your files in your own folder and log out properly"

**【解析】** 文中明确要求完成工作后将文件保存到个人文件夹并正确退出登录。C项正确。A项与"Food and drinks are not allowed"矛盾；B项与"Only visit websites that your teacher tells you"矛盾；D项与离开时关闭电脑的要求矛盾。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Turn off the computer in the correct way. Push your chair under the desk. Put rubbish in the bin"

**【解析】** 离开时需正确关闭电脑、将椅子推回桌下、把垃圾放入垃圾桶。D项完整概括。A项将文件留在桌面与"save in your own folder"不符；B、C项均未提及。故选D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨/写作意图

**【定位】** 第②段 "To make sure everyone can use the computers safely and well, please follow the rules below" 及第⑤段 "the IT room is for all students—take good care of it"

**【解析】** 规则旨在确保学生安全、规范使用电脑，并保持IT室良好状态供所有人使用。A项正确。B、C、D项均与原文意图不符。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 全文，尤第②段 "use the computers safely and well"、第④段 "Only visit... Do not download... Do not change"、第⑤段 "take good care of it"

**【解析】** 全文规则围绕安全使用、规范操作、爱护公物展开，可推知学校希望学生以安全、负责任的方式使用IT室。B项正确。A、C、D项均与规则内容不符。故选B。
