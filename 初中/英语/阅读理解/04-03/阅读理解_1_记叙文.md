> **04-03 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① It was a hot Sunday afternoon. David was playing a basketball game on his computer. He was having a lot of fun. Suddenly, he heard a loud noise from next door. He knew that an old lady, Mrs. Smith, lived there alone. Her children worked in another city and seldom came back.

② David stopped playing his game and quickly ran to Mrs. Smith's house. The front door was open. He went inside and found Mrs. Smith sitting on the kitchen floor. There were broken glasses around her. "Are you okay, Mrs. Smith?" David asked, feeling very worried. 

③ "Oh, David! I am so glad you are here," she said with a weak voice. "I was trying to get a cup of water, but I felt dizzy (头晕的) and fell down. My leg hurts so much that I can't stand up."

④ David didn't know what to do at first. Then he remembered his mother's words: "In an emergency (紧急情况), dial 120 directly." He quickly took out his phone and called the hospital. After that, he carefully cleaned up the broken glasses so that Mrs. Smith wouldn't get hurt again. He stayed with her and kept talking to her until the doctors arrived. 

⑤ At the hospital, the doctor said Mrs. Smith's leg wasn't broken, but she needed to rest for a few days. Mrs. Smith thanked David again and again. David smiled and said, "It's nothing. I'm just happy to help." From that day on, David often visited Mrs. Smith after school to help her with housework.

**注释：** dizzy 头晕的；emergency 紧急情况；dial 拨打

---

### 选择题

1. What was David doing before he heard the loud noise?
A. He was doing his homework.
B. He was playing basketball outdoors.
C. He was playing a computer game.
D. He was drinking water in the kitchen.

2. Why did Mrs. Smith fall down on the kitchen floor?
A. Because the floor was too wet and slippery.
B. Because someone pushed her from behind.
C. Because she fell asleep while walking.
D. Because she felt dizzy when trying to get water.

3. What did David do first after knowing Mrs. Smith couldn't stand up?
A. He called the hospital.
B. He cleaned up the broken glasses.
C. He ran back home to tell his mother.
D. He asked for her children's phone number.

4. What can we infer (推断) from the passage about David?
A. He wants to be a doctor in the future.
B. He is a kind and helpful boy.
C. He likes breaking glasses very much.
D. He doesn't like his own mother.

5. What does the writer mainly want to show us through the story?
A. Playing computer games is a waste of time.
B. Old people should not drink water alone.
C. We should be ready to help others in need.
D. How to clean up broken glasses safely.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "David was playing a basketball game on his computer."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到大卫当时正在电脑上玩篮球游戏。A项（做作业）、D项（在厨房喝水）未提及；B项是只看到"basketball"但忽略了"on his computer"，他不是在户外打球。故选C。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "...I felt dizzy (头晕的) and fell down."

**【解析】** 史密斯太太自己解释了原因：她想拿杯水，但感到头晕（dizzy）就摔倒了。A项（地板太湿滑）、B项（有人从背后推她）、C项（走着睡着了）与原文不符。故选D。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-3句 "In an emergency... dial 120 directly." 和 "He quickly took out his phone and called the hospital."

**【解析】** 在发现史密斯太太站不起来后，大卫想起妈妈的话，做的第一件事是拿出手机给医院打电话（叫救护车）。B项（清理碎玻璃）是他打完电话后做的；C项、D项都没在文中发生。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是最后一段 "David often visited Mrs. Smith after school to help her with housework."

**【解析】** 大卫在邻居发生意外时毫不犹豫地冲过去帮忙，事后还经常去帮忙做家务，由此推断他是个善良、乐于助人的男孩。A项（想当医生）文中无依据；C项、D项完全错误甚至荒谬。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 整个故事的情节走向和结尾。

**【解析】** 这是一篇记叙文，通过大卫帮助邻居独居老奶奶的故事，赞扬了这种助人为乐的精神。作者想传达的核心思想是我们应该随时准备帮助需要帮助的人（We should be ready to help others in need）。A、B、D项都不是文章想表达的中心思想。故选C。
