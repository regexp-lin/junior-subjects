# 阅读理解（说明文）

① "You are what you eat." This famous saying tells us that the food we eat has a huge effect on our health. Having a healthy diet (饮食) is one of the most important things we can do for our bodies.

② First, we should eat plenty of fruits and vegetables. They are full of vitamins (维生素) and minerals. These things can keep our skin beautiful and make our immune system (免疫系统) strong. For example, oranges give us Vitamin C, which helps us fight off a cold. It is a good idea to eat at least five different colors of fruits and vegetables every day.

③ Second, we need to choose whole grains (全谷物) over refined grains. Whole wheat bread, brown rice, and oats (燕麦) are much better than white bread or white rice. Whole grains give us more fiber (纤维). Fiber is very good for our stomachs and can keep us feeling full for a longer time, so we won't want to eat too many unhealthy snacks.

④ Third, drink enough water. Many people like drinking sweet drinks like cola or fruit juice. However, these drinks have too much sugar. Too much sugar can make us fat and cause bad teeth. Water is always the best choice. Our bodies need a lot of water to work properly.

⑤ Finally, don't forget to eat regular meals, especially breakfast. A healthy breakfast gives us the energy we need to start the day. By planning our meals well and choosing the right food, we can build a strong and healthy body.

**注释：** vitamin 维生素；immune system 免疫系统；grain 谷物；fiber 纤维

### 选择题

1. According to the famous saying in paragraph ①, why is our diet important?
A. Because it tells us what words to say.
B. Because it has a huge effect on our health.
C. Because it helps us become famous.
D. Because it is the hardest thing to do.

2. Why is Vitamin C good for us?
A. It makes our teeth white.
B. It helps us sleep well at night.
C. It keeps us feeling full for a long time.
D. It helps us fight off a cold.

3. Which of the following does the writer NOT suggest us to eat?
A. Oranges.
B. Brown rice.
C. Too many unhealthy snacks.
D. Whole wheat bread.

4. What does the underlined word "refined" in paragraph ③ probably mean?
A. Processed to remove some parts (被加工过的).
B. Grown in the dark places.
C. Very expensive and hard to find.
D. Completely raw and natural.

5. What is the best drink for our body according to paragraph ④?
A. Sweet cola.
B. Sweet fruit juice.
C. Pure water.
D. Hot coffee.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第1-2句 "This famous saying tells us that the food we eat has a huge effect on our health."

**【解析】** 原文指出“人如其食”这句名言告诉我们，我们吃的食物对我们的健康有巨大的影响。A、C、D项都不是第一段提到的饮食重要的原因。故选B。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第4句 "For example, oranges give us Vitamin C, which helps us fight off a cold."

**【解析】** 原文以橘子为例，说其中含有的维生素C能帮助我们抵御感冒（fight off a cold）。A、B项未提及；C项是纤维（fiber）的作用，见第③段。故选D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第②、③段提及的健康食品和不健康食品。

**【解析】** 作者在文中建议吃橘子（A）、糙米（B）和全麦面包（D）。在第③段明确提到纤维能让我们保持饱腹感，从而“不会想吃太多不健康的零食（unhealthy snacks）”。由此可知，作者不建议吃C项。题目考察“NOT suggest”，故选C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段第1-2句 "...choose whole grains (全谷物) over refined grains. Whole wheat bread, brown rice, and oats (燕麦) are much better than white bread or white rice."

**【解析】** 文中将“whole grains（全谷物）”与“refined grains”进行了对比，举出的例子中，全麦面包、糙米是对身体好的，而白面包、白米饭（经过加工提纯去掉了谷皮等部分）反之较差。由此推断 refined 意为“精细加工过的”。A项意为“被加工以去除某些部分”，符合文意。B项（在黑暗中生长）、C项（昂贵）、D项（生鲜自然的）均不符合。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第4句 "Water is always the best choice."

**【解析】** 第四段论述了喝水的问题，明确指出水永远是最佳选择。甜可乐（A）和果汁（B）被指出含有过多糖分，咖啡（D）未曾提及。故选C。
