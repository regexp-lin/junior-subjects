# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Cultural Tour: Beijing & Xi'an (8 Days / 7 Nights)**

② Join our summer tour and explore two ancient Chinese cities! We will visit the Great Wall, the Forbidden City, and the Terracotta Army (兵马俑). The tour is perfect for students and families who want to learn about Chinese history and culture. Our English-speaking guide will help you understand each place.

③ **Day 1–2: Beijing** — Arrive in Beijing. On the first day, we will visit Tian'anmen Square and the Forbidden City. On the second day, we climb the Great Wall at Mutianyu. In the evening, enjoy Peking Duck (北京烤鸭) at a local restaurant.

④ **Day 3–5: Xi'an** — Take a high-speed train to Xi'an. We will spend two days visiting the Terracotta Army and the Big Wild Goose Pagoda (大雁塔). On the last day, walk through the old city wall and try local street food.

⑤ **Price:** ¥3,980 per person (students get 10% off). The price includes hotels, meals, entrance tickets, and train tickets. Sign up before June 15 to get a free T-shirt. Call 400-123-4567 or visit www.summer-tour.cn.

**注释：** Terracotta Army 兵马俑；Peking Duck 北京烤鸭；entrance ticket 门票

### 选择题

1. What cities will the tour visit?
   A. Beijing and Xi'an.
   B. Shanghai and Hangzhou.
   C. Guangzhou and Shenzhen.
   D. Nanjing and Suzhou.

2. According to the passage, when do tourists visit the Great Wall?
   A. On Day 1.
   B. On Day 3.
   C. On Day 2.
   D. On Day 5.

3. What is included in the tour price?
   A. Free T-shirt only.
   B. Air tickets from abroad.
   C. Shopping money.
   D. Hotels, meals, entrance tickets, and train tickets.

4. How can students save money on this tour?
   A. By signing up after June 15.
   B. By getting 10% off the price.
   C. By visiting fewer places.
   D. By bringing their own food.

5. What is the main purpose of this brochure?
   A. To compare different Chinese cities.
   B. To teach Chinese history in school.
   C. To introduce a summer cultural tour and encourage people to join.
   D. To sell Peking Duck.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段标题 "Summer Cultural Tour: Beijing & Xi'an" 及 "explore two ancient Chinese cities"

**【解析】** 宣传册明确介绍北京和西安两座城市的行程。A 项正确。B、C、D 项所涉城市均未在文中出现。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Day 1–2: Beijing... On the second day, we climb the Great Wall at Mutianyu."

**【解析】** 长城游览安排在第二天（Day 2）。C 项正确。A 项第一天为天安门和故宫；B、D 项为西安行程。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The price includes hotels, meals, entrance tickets, and train tickets."

**【解析】** 费用包含酒店、餐食、门票和火车票。D 项正确。A 项 T-shirt 需 6 月 15 日前报名才赠送；B 项国际机票未提及；C 项购物费用未提及。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "students get 10% off"

**【解析】** 学生享有 10% 优惠。B 项正确。A 项 6 月 15 日后报名无法获赠 T-shirt，与省钱无关；C、D 项文中未提及。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图 / 主旨理解

**【定位】** 全文：介绍暑期文化游行程、景点、价格及报名方式。

**【解析】** 本文为旅游宣传册，意在介绍行程并吸引读者报名参加。B 项正确。A 项“比较城市”非主要目的；C 项“在学校教授历史”与宣传册性质不符；D 项“销售北京烤鸭”仅为行程中的餐饮安排。故选 B。
