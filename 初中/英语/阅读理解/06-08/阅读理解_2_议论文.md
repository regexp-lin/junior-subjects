# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In many schools, students have only 10 minutes of break time between classes. Some educators and parents have started to ask: Should students have more time to rest during the school day? This question has caused a lot of discussion.

② **Those who support longer breaks** believe that students need more time to relax. Sitting in class for 40 or 45 minutes can make students feel tired. A short break is not enough to use the bathroom, drink water, or move around. Longer breaks can help students feel refreshed and ready to learn in the next class. Besides, research shows that regular rest helps the brain work better. Students who have enough break time may concentrate more and do better in exams.

③ **Those who oppose longer breaks** worry that more break time means less time for study. They think students may waste the extra time chatting or playing instead of relaxing. Some also say that longer breaks could make it harder for teachers to finish their lessons. If the school day becomes longer to add breaks, students might go home later and feel even more tired.

④ **What some schools have done:** A few schools have tried 15-minute breaks and found good results. Students report feeling less tired and more focused. Teachers notice that students pay more attention after a short rest. However, every school is different. The best solution may depend on the number of classes, the age of students, and the length of each lesson.

⑤ In conclusion, there is no simple answer. Both sides have strong reasons. The key is to find a balance — enough rest for health and focus, but not so much that learning time is lost. Schools, parents, and students should work together to find what works best.

**注释：** refreshed 精神焕发的；concentrate 集中注意力；oppose 反对

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, why do some people support longer breaks?
   A. Because students need more time to relax, use the bathroom, and feel refreshed.
   B. Because they want students to study less.
   C. Because they want to make the school day shorter.
   D. Because teachers need more rest.

2. What do those who oppose longer breaks worry about?
   A. Students will feel too relaxed.
   B. Students will study too hard.
   C. The school will have to close earlier.
   D. Students may waste the extra time and teachers may not finish lessons.

3. What have some schools found after trying 15-minute breaks?
   A. Students feel more tired.
   B. Students report feeling less tired and more focused; teachers notice better attention.
   C. Teachers cannot finish their lessons.
   D. Parents are unhappy.

4. The underlined word "refreshed" in paragraph ② probably means ______.
   A. sleepy
   B. bored
   C. full of energy again
   D. tired

5. What is the writer's main idea about break time?
   A. All schools should have 15-minute breaks.
   B. Longer breaks are always better than shorter ones.
   C. Students should never have any break time.
   D. There is no simple answer; schools should find a balance that works.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "students need more time to relax... A short break is not enough to use the bathroom, drink water, or move around. Longer breaks can help students feel refreshed and ready to learn."

**【解析】** 支持者认为学生需要更多时间放松、去洗手间、喝水、活动，并恢复精力。A 项正确。B 项"希望学生学得更少"非支持理由；C 项"缩短上学时间"未提及；D 项"教师需要休息"非焦点。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They think students may waste the extra time chatting or playing... longer breaks could make it harder for teachers to finish their lessons."

**【解析】** 反对者担心学生浪费休息时间闲聊或玩耍，且教师难以完成教学。D 项正确。A 项"太放松"表述不清；B、C 项文中未提及。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "A few schools have tried 15-minute breaks and found good results. Students report feeling less tired and more focused. Teachers notice that students pay more attention after a short rest."

**【解析】** 尝试 15 分钟课间休息的学校发现：学生更少疲劳、更专注，教师也观察到学生注意力更集中。B 项正确。A、C、D 项与原文所述效果相反。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第②段 "Longer breaks can help students feel refreshed and ready to learn in the next class."

**【解析】** 后文 "ready to learn" 表明 refreshed 意为精神恢复、精力充沛。C 项 full of energy again 与之相符。A 项 sleepy、B 项 bored、D 项 tired 均与语境矛盾。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "there is no simple answer... The key is to find a balance — enough rest for health and focus, but not so much that learning time is lost. Schools, parents, and students should work together to find what works best."

**【解析】** 作者认为没有唯一答案，关键在于寻找平衡，各方应共同探索最适合的方案。D 项准确概括。A 项"所有学校都该 15 分钟"过于绝对；B 项"越长越好"与 balance 矛盾；C 项"不应有任何休息"与作者立场相反。故选 D。
