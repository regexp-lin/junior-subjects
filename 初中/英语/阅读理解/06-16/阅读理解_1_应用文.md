> **06-16 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Lost and Found Notice**

② We found several items in the school library and canteen last week. If you lost any of the following things, please come to the Student Office (Room 201) to claim them. You need to bring your student card and describe your item clearly.

③ **Lost items:** (1) A blue school bag with books and a pencil case. The pencil case has a cartoon character on it. (2) A black sports watch. The band is red. (3) A set of keys with a small bear keyring (钥匙扣). (4) A white water bottle with the school logo. (5) A pair of black glasses in a blue case.

④ **Opening hours:** Monday to Friday, 8:30 am — 11:30 am and 2:00 pm — 5:00 pm. The office is closed at weekends. If no one claims these items by June 30th, they will be given to the school charity sale.

⑤ **Contact:** If you cannot come in person, you may ask a teacher to help you. Please call 0755-12345678 during office hours. We hope all items can find their owners soon!

**注释：** claim 认领；keyring 钥匙扣；charity sale 慈善义卖

---

### 选择题

1. Where can students get their lost items back?
   A. In the Student Office (Room 201).
   B. In the school library.
   C. In the canteen.
   D. In the school shop.

2. What must students bring when they come to claim their items?
   A. A parent.
   B. Their student card and a clear description of the item.
   C. Only a photo of the item.
   D. Money.

3. Which item has a red band?
   A. The blue school bag.
   B. The white water bottle.
   C. The black sports watch.
   D. The keys.

4. When is the Student Office open?
   A. Every day from 8:30 am to 5:00 pm.
   B. Only on Saturdays.
   C. 24 hours a day.
   D. Monday to Friday, 8:30 — 11:30 am and 2:00 — 5:00 pm.

5. What will happen to the unclaimed items after June 30th?
   A. They will be thrown away.
   B. They will be sold to raise money for charity.
   C. They will be kept in the office forever.
   D. Teachers will take them home.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "please come to the Student Office (Room 201) to claim them"

**【解析】** 文章明确说明学生需到学生处（201 室）认领失物。A 项正确。B 项图书馆、C 项食堂是发现物品的地点，非认领地点；D 项学校商店未提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "You need to bring your student card and describe your item clearly."

**【解析】** 认领时需携带学生证并清晰描述物品。B 项正确。A 项家长、C 项物品照片、D 项金钱均未要求。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "A black sports watch. The band is red."

**【解析】** 黑色运动手表的表带是红色的。C 项正确。A 项书包、B 项水瓶、D 项钥匙均无红色表带描述。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Monday to Friday, 8:30 am — 11:30 am and 2:00 pm — 5:00 pm."

**【解析】** 学生处开放时间为周一至周五上午 8:30—11:30 和下午 2:00—5:00。D 项正确。A 项 "每天 8:30—5:00" 未区分中午休息；B 项周六错误；C 项 24 小时错误。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "If no one claims these items by June 30th, they will be given to the school charity sale."

**【解析】** 6 月 30 日前无人认领的物品将交给学校慈善义卖。B 项 "出售以筹集善款" 正确。A 项扔掉、C 项永久保管、D 项教师带走均与原文不符。故选 B。
