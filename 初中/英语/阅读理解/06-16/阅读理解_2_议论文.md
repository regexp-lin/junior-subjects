# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Should students take part in community service? This is a question that many schools and families are discussing today. Some say it is a waste of time. Others believe it is very important for students' growth. Let us look at both sides of the argument.

② **Reasons for community service:** First, it helps students understand the world outside school. When students help clean up parks, visit the elderly, or teach younger children to read, they learn what real life is like. Second, community service develops important skills such as teamwork, responsibility, and communication. These skills will be useful when they grow up and look for jobs. Third, many students feel happy and proud when they help others. It gives them a sense of purpose. Some schools have even made community service part of their curriculum (课程) because they see how much it helps students grow.

③ **Reasons against community service:** Some people worry that community service takes too much time away from study. Students already have much homework and many exams. Adding community service may make them more tired and stressed. Another concern is that some students may not really want to help — they only do it because they have to. Forced service may not teach the right values. Also, parents sometimes worry about safety when their children go out to serve the community.

④ **What experts suggest:** Researchers say that a little community service — perhaps a few hours each month — can be good for students without affecting their grades. The key is to let students choose activities they are interested in. When they choose freely, they learn more and feel more motivated. Schools should work with parents to make sure activities are safe and meaningful.

⑤ In conclusion, community service can be a valuable experience for students when it is done in the right way. It should not replace study, but it can add something important to it: a chance to learn about society, develop character, and become better people. The question is not whether to do it, but how to do it well.

**注释：** curriculum 课程；motivated 有动力的；character 品格

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, which is NOT a benefit of community service?
   A. Getting higher exam scores directly.
   B. Understanding the world outside school.
   C. Developing teamwork and responsibility.
   D. Feeling happy and proud when helping others.

2. Why do some people disagree with community service for students?
   A. Because they think it is too easy.
   B. Because it may take time away from study and make students more tired.
   C. Because students enjoy it too much.
   D. Because it costs too much money.

3. What do experts suggest about community service?
   A. Students should do it for many hours every day.
   B. Students should be forced to do it.
   C. A few hours each month is enough, and students should choose activities they like.
   D. Only parents should decide what activities to do.

4. The underlined word "motivated" in paragraph ④ probably means ______.
   A. willing to do something and interested in it
   B. tired and bored
   C. angry and upset
   D. slow and lazy

5. What is the writer's main idea?
   A. Community service is a waste of time and should be stopped.
   B. Students should never do community service.
   C. Only schools should organize community service.
   D. Community service can be valuable when done properly; the key is how to do it well.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段列举 community service 的好处：理解校外世界、培养团队合作与责任感、帮助他人时感到快乐和自豪。

**【解析】** B、C、D 均为文中明确提到的益处。A 项 "直接提高考试成绩" 未提及，文中仅说有助于成长和技能培养，未说直接影响分数。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Some people worry that community service takes too much time away from study... Adding community service may make them more tired and stressed."

**【解析】** 反对者担心社区服务占用学习时间，增加学生疲劳和压力。B 项正确。A 项 "太简单"、C 项 "太享受"、D 项 "花钱多" 均未提及。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "a little community service — perhaps a few hours each month — can be good for students... The key is to let students choose activities they are interested in."

**【解析】** 专家建议每月几小时即可，关键是让学生选择感兴趣的活动。C 项正确。A 项 "每天很多小时" 与原文矛盾；B 项 "强迫" 与 choose freely 矛盾；D 项 "仅家长决定" 未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "When they choose freely, they learn more and feel more motivated."

**【解析】** 学生自由选择时会学得更多、感到更有动力。motivated 意为 "有动力的、积极主动的"。A 项 willing to do something and interested in it 最贴近。B、C、D 均为负面含义，与语境不符。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "community service can be a valuable experience... when it is done in the right way... The question is not whether to do it, but how to do it well."

**【解析】** 作者认为社区服务在正确实施时很有价值，关键是如何做好而非是否要做。D 项准确概括。A 项 "浪费时间应停止" 与作者观点相反；B 项 "绝不参加" 过于极端；C 项 "仅学校组织" 文中强调学校与家长合作。故选 D。
