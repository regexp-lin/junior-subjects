# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Sunnydale English Summer Camp**

② Do you want to improve your English while having fun? Do you want to make friends from different places? Join our Sunnydale English Summer Camp this July! It will be an unforgettable (难忘的) experience for you.

③ **Camp Details (详情):**
- **Dates:** July 15th to July 25th (10 days)
- **Place:** Sunnydale International School
- **For:** Students aged 12 to 15

④ **What will we do?**
- **Morning (9:00 AM - 11:30 AM):** English language classes. Our friendly foreign teachers will help you with your speaking and listening skills through interesting games and role-plays. No boring grammar exercises!
- **Afternoon (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM):** Outdoor activities. We will have sports competitions, nature walks, and a special treasure hunt (寻宝游戏) in the school forest.
- **Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM):** Movie nights, English songs singing, and a big closing party on the last day!

⑤ **Cost and What's Included:**
The camp costs $300 per student. This price includes (包含):
- All English classes and activity materials
- Three delicious meals a day
- A shared bedroom (two students in one room)
- A special camp T-shirt

⑥ **How to apply (申请)?**
Please visit our website at *www.sunnydalecamp.com* to fill out the application form. You must send the form before June 30th. Spaces are limited (有限的) to 50 students, so apply early!

⑦ Come and spend a meaningful summer with us!

**注释：** unforgettable 难忘的；competition 比赛；treasure hunt 寻宝游戏；include 包含；apply 申请

---

### 选择题

1. Who can join the Sunnydale English Summer Camp?
A. Children under 10 years old.
B. Students aged 12 to 15.
C. Only foreign english teachers.
D. University students.

2. How long will the summer camp last?
A. For one week.
B. For 10 days.
C. For a whole month.
D. From June 30th to July 15th.

3. What will the students do in the morning according to paragraph ④?
A. Learn English through games and role-plays without boring grammar exercises.
B. Go for nature walks in the forest.
C. Have outdoor sports competitions.
D. Watch English movies.

4. Which of the following is NOT included in the $300 cost?
A. A shared bedroom for two students.
B. Three meals a day.
C. A train ticket to Sunnydale.
D. A special camp T-shirt.

5. When is the deadline (最后期限) to apply for the camp?
A. July 15th.
B. July 25th.
C. June 15th.
D. June 30th.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第3条 "For: Students aged 12 to 15"

**【解析】** 关于招生对象，海报详情栏直接点明是面向12到15岁的学生。A（10岁以下的儿童）、C（只有外教）D（大学生）皆不符要求。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1条 "Dates: July 15th to July 25th (10 days)"

**【解析】** 夏令营的持续时间原文已经明确给出并用括号标注了是 10 days（10天）。A（一周）、C（一整月）不对，D（6.30到7.15）混淆了报名截止日和开营日。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段早间安排 "Morning (9:00 AM - 11:30 AM): English language classes. Our friendly foreign teachers will help you... through interesting games and role-plays. No boring grammar exercises!"

**【解析】** 上午的活动内容是上英语课，外教会通过有趣的游戏和角色扮演（games and role-plays）来教，且没有无聊的语法练习。这与A选项的描述完全一致。B和C是下午的活动；D是晚上的。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "This price includes (包含): All English classes... Three delicious meals... A shared bedroom... A special camp T-shirt"

**【解析】** 收费300美元里包括的部分罗列得很清楚：包含全部课程和活动材料、一日三餐（B）、双人共享的卧室（A）、以及一件特制的夏令营T恤（D）。并没有提及包含“去Sunnydale的火车票”（C）。此题问不包含的，故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第⑥段第2句 "You must send the form before June 30th."

**【解析】** 海报最后告知了报名申请的相关截止信息：必须在6月30日（June 30th）之前递交申请表。因此最后期限是D项。A项是开营日子，B项是闭营日子。故选D。
