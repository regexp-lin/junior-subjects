# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Everyone knows that keeping pets is very popular. Today, many families have a dog, a cat, or even a small bird. Some people believe that keeping pets takes too much time and money, but I strongly hold the opinion that having a pet brings great benefits (好处) to people, especially to children.

② To begin with, pets are great companions (同伴). In modern society, many people live alone or feel lonely. A dog that wags (摇) its tail when you come home can make you feel loved. A cat sleeping on your lap can bring you peace. Pets listen to us without judging, which helps reduce stress and makes us happier.

③ Secondly, keeping a pet teaches children responsibility (责任感). A pet relies (依赖) on its owner for everything: food, clean water, and exercise. When parents ask their children to feed the dog every morning or clean the cat's box, the children learn how to care for another living thing. This experience is very valuable for their growth. They will understand that having a pet is not just about playing, but also about giving care patiently.

④ Furthermore, pets encourage us to be active and healthy. If you have a dog, you must take it out for a walk every day, no matter what the weather is like. This daily walking helps pet owners get more exercise and fresh air. It is much better than sitting on the sofa watching TV all day.

⑤ In conclusion, although keeping a pet needs time and money, the love, responsibility, and health benefits they bring far outweigh (胜过) the cost. A pet is not just an animal; it is a true member of the family.

**注释：** companion 同伴；wag 摇（尾巴）；stress 压力；rely 依赖；valuable 有价值的；outweigh 胜过，比……重要

---

### 选择题

1. What is the writer's main opinion in paragraph ①?
A. Keeping pets is a waste of time and money.
B. Only lonely old people should keep pets.
C. Having a pet brings great benefits, especially to children.
D. People should not keep birds as pets.

2. How do pets help reduce our stress and make us happy according to paragraph ②?
A. By earning money for the family.
B. By helping us do the housework.
C. By listening to us without judging and being great companions.
D. By barking loudly to scare away thieves.

3. Why is keeping a pet valuable for children's growth?
A. Because it teaches them how to sing songs to animals.
B. Because it teaches them responsibility by caring for a living thing.
C. Because children can use pets to finish their science homework.
D. Because pets can take children to school automatically.

4. How does having a dog encourage a person to be active and healthy?
A. The person must take the dog out for a daily walk.
B. The dog will cook healthy food for the owner.
C. The person will stop watching TV forever.
D. The dog will teach the person how to swim.

5. What does the word "outweigh" mean in the last paragraph?
A. To be less important than something.
B. To be heavier than something in weight.
C. To be more important or valuable than something else.
D. To throw something away.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解与主旨定调

**【定位】** 第①段最后半句 "...I strongly hold the opinion that having a pet brings great benefits (好处) to people, especially to children."

**【解析】** 开篇直接标明了作者核心观点：强烈地认为养宠物能给人带来巨大的好处，尤其是对孩子们。这正对应了C选项的内容。A项是反方观点，B和D系捏造。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "Pets listen to us without judging, which helps reduce stress and makes us happier."

**【解析】** 第二段详细说明了宠物作为好同伴可以倾听我们而不加评判（listen to us without judging），这有助于减轻压力和带给我们快乐。C选项完美契合。并非由于挣钱（A）、做家务（B）、吓跑小偷（D）。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段第1题与第2题 "Secondly, keeping a pet teaches children responsibility... the children learn how to care for another living thing. This experience is very valuable for their growth."

**【解析】** 养宠对孩子成长的核心价值被明确定位为：教会孩子责任感（responsibility）以及去照料另一个生命（care for another living thing）。B项准确复述了这点。A（教动物唱歌）、C（干家庭作业）、D（自动带孩上学）均为错谬荒唐的选项。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "If you have a dog, you must take it out for a walk every day..."

**【解析】** 养狗之所以能让人更活跃更健康是因为：你必须每天带它出去散步（take it out for a walk every day），从而获得了更多锻炼及呼吸新鲜空气的机会。这与A选项意思一致。B（狗做饭）、D（狗教游泳）太玄幻；C（永远不看电视）过于绝对。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测推断

**【定位】** 第⑤段首句 "...the love, responsibility, and health benefits they bring far outweigh (胜过) the cost."

**【解析】** 作者在总结陈词中提到：尽管需要金钱和时间（cost），但是它们所带来的爱、责任感及健康益处远远地“outweigh（胜过）”这成本底线。即：前者比后者更有价值或更重要（To be more important or valuable than something else）。故选C。
