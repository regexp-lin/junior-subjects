# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Mr. Harris was the grumpiest (最脾气暴躁的) man on Elm Street. He lived alone in a large house with a beautiful, perfect front yard. Not a single blade of grass was out of place. He spent his days chasing away birds, shouting at the postman, and strictly warning children never to step on his lawn (草坪). 

② "Keep off the grass, you little monsters!" he would yell, waving his wooden walking stick at any child who kicked a ball too close to his fence. Because of this, the children in the neighborhood were terrified of him. They called his house "The Dragon's Castle".

③ One hot afternoon, a seven-year-old girl named Lily was playing with her new, bright yellow kite in the street. A sudden strong wind caught the kite and carried it high into the air. Lily watched in horror as the string broke, and the beautiful yellow kite landed safely but stuck high in the branches of the large apple tree—right in the middle of Mr. Harris's perfect front yard.

④ Lily began to cry. She loved that kite, but she was too afraid of "The Dragon" to go and get it. She stood on the sidewalk, wiping her tears. 

⑤ Suddenly, the front door of the large house opened. Mr. Harris walked out, leaning heavily on his walking stick. He looked at the crying girl, then looked up at the yellow kite in his tree. Lily froze in fear, expecting him to shout. 

⑥ Instead, Mr. Harris slowly walked to the garage and pulled out a long ladder. With great effort, he climbed up a few steps and reached the kite. He carefully untangled (解开) the string from the branches and walked back to the fence. 

⑦ He handed the yellow kite to Lily. The grumpy lines on his face softened (柔和) into a small, rusty smile. "Here you go, little one. Next time, tie the string tighter," he said gently. Lily stopped crying, took the kite, and smiled back. "Thank you, Mr. Dragon... I mean, Mr. Harris!" 

⑧ After that day, the neighborhood children still didn't step on his grass, but they stopped running away when they saw him. Sometimes, Mr. Harris even waved back.

**注释：** grumpy 脾气暴躁的；lawn 草坪；terrified 害怕的，恐惧的；stick 卡住；ladder 梯子；untangle 解开

---

### 选择题

1. How did Mr. Harris usually treat the neighborhood children?
A. He invited them to play balls on his perfect lawn.
B. He gave them free apples from his tree.
C. He shouted at them and warned them to keep off his grass.
D. He told them wonderful fairy tales about dragons.

2. Why were the children terrified of Mr. Harris?
A. Because he kept a real dragon in his house.
B. Because he yelled at them and waved his walking stick.
C. Because his house looked black and scary.
D. Because he always stole their toys and kites.

3. What happened to Lily's bright yellow kite?
A. Mr. Harris broke its string because it annoyed him.
B. It was stolen by an older boy on the street.
C. A dog bit it and tore it into pieces.
D. The wind blew it away, and it got stuck in a tree in Mr. Harris's yard.

4. Why did Mr. Harris go to his garage?
A. To get a ladder to help Lily bring down the kite.
B. To find his walking stick to hit the kite.
C. To hide from the crying girl.
D. To take his car and drive away from the noise.

5. What changed after Mr. Harris helped Lily?
A. The children started playing on his lawn every day.
B. The children stopped running away from him, and he sometimes waved back.
C. Mr. Harris chopped down the apple tree.
D. Lily bought him a bright yellow kite as a gift.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段末至第②段 "He spent his days... strictly warning children never to step on his lawn. 'Keep off the grass...!' he would yell..."

**【解析】** 暴躁的亨里斯先生平时是怎么对待街区里的小孩的？原文描写他经常严格警告小孩绝不准踩草坪，并挥舞拐杖大喊大叫（shouted at them and warned them to keep off his grass）。符合选项C的描述。A邀人踢球、B送苹果、D讲童话，与他那“恶龙”的脾气截然相反。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "...yell, waving his wooden walking stick at any child who kicked a ball too close... Because of this, the children... were terrified of him."

**【解析】** 孩子们为什么如此怕他？因为只要球稍微踢近些，他就会冲着孩子们吼叫并挥舞拐杖（he yelled at them and waved his walking stick）。这直接对应B选项。A真有龙、C房子黑、D偷玩具等均为错误猜测项。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "A sudden strong wind caught the kite... string broke, and the beautiful yellow kite landed safely but stuck high in the branches... right in the middle of Mr. Harris's perfect front yard."

**【解析】** 莉莉的黄色风筝遭遇了什么？文中提到一阵大风刮断了线，风筝卡在了亨里斯先生前院的苹果树枝上。D项是对这个意外事件的准确还原。A老头拽断、B老男孩偷、C狗咬烂，皆不符全文线索。故选D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段第1-2句 "...Mr. Harris slowly walked to the garage and pulled out a long ladder. With great effort, he climbed up a few steps and reached the kite."

**【解析】** 亨里斯老头为什么大冷脸跑去车库？因为他拉出了一把长梯登高，去帮莉莉把卡在树上的风筝给取下来（To get a ladder to help Lily bring down the kite）。A项正确。B找拐杖打风筝、C躲避哭声、D开车逃离，均曲解了老人的善意举动。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 结尾第⑧段 "After that day, the neighborhood children still didn't step on his grass, but they stopped running away when they saw him. Sometimes, Mr. Harris even waved back."

**【解析】** 经过这次助人事件以后发生了什么改变？结尾写明，虽然孩子们还是不踩草坪，但见到他时不再吓得跑开了，有时老头甚至还会挥手回应。B项概括了这一温馨的转变。A每天去踩草坪、C砍树、D买新风筝都没发生。故选B。
