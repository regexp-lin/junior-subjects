> **10-09 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last year, I hurt my knee during a basketball game. The doctor said I had to stop playing for at least three months and do rehabilitation (康复) exercises every day. At first, I was very upset. Basketball was my favourite sport. How could I stay away from it for so long? I did not want to do those boring exercises. I just wanted to get back on the court.

② The first week was the hardest. Every morning, I had to do stretches and small movements. My leg felt weak and sometimes hurt. I often thought about giving up. "What is the point?" I asked myself. "I will never play as well as before." But my mother encouraged me. "Recovery takes time," she said. "You have to be patient. Every exercise you do now will help you get stronger." She sat with me and counted the reps. Her presence made me feel less alone.

③ Slowly, I began to see progress. After two weeks, my knee felt less stiff. After a month, I could walk without limping. The physiotherapist (理疗师) said I was doing well. "Keep it up," she told me. "You are building strength. Soon you can try light jogging." Her words gave me hope. I started to take the exercises seriously. I followed the plan every day, even when I did not feel like it.

④ Three months later, the doctor said I could return to basketball — but I had to start slowly. The first time I stepped back onto the court, I was nervous. Would my knee hold up? I started with simple drills. Then I joined a casual game with friends. I was not as fast as before, but I could play. And I did not feel any pain. I was so happy that I almost cried.

⑤ That experience taught me an important lesson. When things go wrong, we may want to give up. But if we keep going and work hard, we can overcome difficulties. Rehabilitation was not easy, but it was worth it. Now I appreciate my health more. I also know that patience and persistence can lead to success — not only in sports, but in life.

**注释：** rehabilitation 康复；rep 重复次数；physiotherapist 理疗师；stiff 僵硬的

---

**Questions:**

1. Why did the writer have to stop playing basketball?
   A. Because he/she did not like basketball anymore.
   B. Because he/she hurt his/her knee during a game and needed rest and rehabilitation.
   C. Because the doctor liked other sports.
   D. Because the school banned basketball.

2. How did the writer feel at first about the rehabilitation exercises?
   A. Excited and eager.
   B. Upset and unwilling; he/she did not want to do the boring exercises.
   C. Very happy.
   D. Bored but not upset.

3. Who encouraged the writer during the first week?
   A. The basketball coach.
   B. The writer's mother.
   C. A classmate.
   D. The doctor.

4. When could the writer return to basketball?
   A. After one week.
   B. After one month.
   C. After three months.
   D. After one year.

5. What did the writer learn from this experience?
   A. Basketball is the only important thing.
   B. Giving up is the best choice.
   C. Patience and persistence can help overcome difficulties; recovery takes hard work but is worth it.
   D. Doctors always give wrong advice.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I hurt my knee during a basketball game. The doctor said I had to stop playing for at least three months and do rehabilitation exercises every day"

**【解析】** 作者在比赛中伤到膝盖，医生要求停赛至少三个月并每日做康复训练。B项准确。A、C、D项与原因不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "I was very upset... I did not want to do those boring exercises. I just wanted to get back on the court"

**【解析】** 起初作者沮丧、不愿做看似枯燥的康复训练，只想着重回球场。B项正确。A、C、D项与情绪不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "But my mother encouraged me... She sat with me and counted the reps"

**【解析】** 母亲在第一周鼓励作者，陪在身边并帮忙数次数。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Three months later, the doctor said I could return to basketball"

**【解析】** 三个月后医生准许作者恢复打篮球。C项正确。A、B、D项与时间不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "if we keep going and work hard, we can overcome difficulties... patience and persistence can lead to success — not only in sports, but in life"

**【解析】** 作者体会到坚持与耐心有助于克服困难，康复虽辛苦但值得，并在生活中同样适用。C项正确。A、B、D项与文意不符。故选C。
