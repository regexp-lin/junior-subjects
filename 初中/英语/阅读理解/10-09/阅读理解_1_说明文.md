# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), or中医药 in Chinese, is a system of medicine that has been used in China for over 2,000 years. It includes herbal medicine, acupuncture (针灸), massage, and dietary therapy. TCM views the human body differently from Western medicine. It focuses on balance and the flow of energy, or "qi" (气), through the body.

② In TCM, health means balance. The body has two opposing forces: yin (阴) and yang (阳). When yin and yang are in balance, we feel well. When they are out of balance, we may get sick. TCM doctors try to restore this balance. They look at the whole person — not just the sick part — and consider lifestyle, diet, emotions, and the environment. This holistic (整体的) approach is one of TCM's key features.

③ Herbal medicine is the most common form of TCM treatment. Doctors use plants, roots, and sometimes animal or mineral products to make medicine. Different herbs are combined in a formula (配方) to treat a specific condition. For example, ginseng is often used to boost energy, while chrysanthemum tea is used to calm the mind and clear heat. Herbs can be taken as teas, pills, or soups. Some formulas have been used for hundreds of years and are still popular today.

④ Acupuncture is another well-known TCM practice. Thin needles are inserted into specific points on the body. These points are believed to be connected to channels through which qi flows. By stimulating these points, the doctor aims to restore the flow of qi and relieve pain or other symptoms. Acupuncture is now used in many countries around the world. The World Health Organisation has recognised it as a useful treatment for certain conditions.

⑤ TCM is an important part of Chinese culture. It has influenced medicine in many Asian countries. Today, TCM and Western medicine often work together. Some hospitals in China offer both. Patients can choose the treatment that best fits their needs. Understanding TCM helps us appreciate a different way of thinking about health and healing.

**注释：** acupuncture 针灸；holistic 整体的；formula 配方；ginseng 人参；chrysanthemum 菊花；channel 经络

**Questions:**

1. How long has TCM been used in China?
   A. About 100 years.
   B. About 500 years.
   C. Over 2,000 years.
   D. Only in modern times.

2. What does TCM believe about health?
   A. Health means taking many pills.
   B. Health means balance between yin and yang; when balanced, we feel well.
   C. Health means never getting sick.
   D. Health has nothing to do with the body.

3. What is ginseng often used for in TCM?
   A. To calm the mind.
   B. To boost energy.
   C. To clear heat.
   D. To help sleep only.

4. How does acupuncture work, according to the passage?
   A. By giving pills to the patient.
   B. By inserting thin needles into specific points to stimulate qi flow and relieve symptoms.
   C. By massaging the body only.
   D. By using herbs only.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. TCM is no longer used.
   B. TCM is part of Chinese culture, has influenced other countries, and now often works with Western medicine.
   C. Western medicine has replaced TCM completely.
   D. No one believes in TCM anymore.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "has been used in China for over 2,000 years"

**【解析】** 中医药在中国已有两千多年历史。C项正确。A、B、D项与时间不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "In TCM, health means balance. The body has two opposing forces: yin and yang. When yin and yang are in balance, we feel well"

**【解析】** 中医药认为健康即阴阳平衡，平衡则身体康健。B项准确。A、C、D项与理念不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "ginseng is often used to boost energy"

**【解析】** 人参常用于增强精力、补气。B项正确。A、C、D项与功效不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Thin needles are inserted into specific points... By stimulating these points, the doctor aims to restore the flow of qi and relieve pain or other symptoms"

**【解析】** 针灸通过针刺特定穴位以疏通气血、缓解症状。B项正确。A、C、D项与针灸原理不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "important part of Chinese culture... influenced medicine in many Asian countries... TCM and Western medicine often work together"

**【解析】** 可推知中医药是中华文化一部分，影响多国，且常与西医协同使用。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
