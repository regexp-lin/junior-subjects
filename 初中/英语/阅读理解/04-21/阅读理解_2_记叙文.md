# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Last summer, my class went on a three-day camping trip to the Green Valley mountains. Our science teacher, Mr. Green, led the trip. The main task was to learn about wild plants and how to survive (生存) in nature. I was in a team with my best friend, Tom, and a quiet girl named Lily.

② On the first day, we arrived at the campsite and set up our tents. It was hard work! Then, Mr. Green gave each team a map and a compass (指南针). "Your task this afternoon is to find a small lake hidden in the forest. You must be back before dinner," he ordered. We excitedly started our journey.

③ The forest was huge and green. We walked for an hour following the map. Suddenly, Tom stopped. "Wait," he said, looking at the compass confusingly. "I think we are going the wrong way. The lake should be in the north, but we are heading east." 

④ I looked at the map, but I couldn't understand it very well. We started to argue. Tom wanted to turn left, but I wanted to keep going straight. Just when we were getting angry, Lily spoke up softly. "Actually (事实上)," she pointed at the moss (青苔) on a big tree, "moss usually grows on the north side of the trees in this area. If that is north, Tom is right. We should turn left." 

⑤ We followed Lily's advice. Twenty minutes later, we heard the sound of water. Through the trees, we saw the beautiful, shining lake! We cheered loudly and took many pictures for our science report.

⑥ During the dinner around the campfire, Mr. Green praised (表扬) our team for finding the lake the fastest. Tom and I smiled at Lily. Without her careful observation (观察) and knowledge of nature, we might have been lost in the woods. I learned that everyone has a special strength, and we must listen to each other to succeed.

**注释：** survive 生存；compass 指南针；argue 争吵；moss 青苔；observation 观察；strength 长处

---

### 选择题

1. What was the main task of the camping trip?
A. To catch fish in the lake.
B. To pick up rubbish in the forest.
C. To learn about wild plants and how to survive in nature.
D. To practice building large wooden houses.

2. What did Mr. Green ask the teams to do on the first afternoon?
A. To find the campsite by themselves.
B. To find a hidden small lake before dinner.
C. To set up the tents without any help.
D. To cook a large dinner for everyone.

3. Why did Tom and the writer start to argue according to paragraph ④?
A. Because they couldn't agree on which way to go.
B. Because Tom wanted to eat his snacks.
C. Because the writer lost the compass.
D. Because they both wanted to take a picture of the tree.

4. How did Lily know which direction was north?
A. She used a special app on her mobile phone.
B. Mr. Green called her and told her the answer.
C. She asked another group of students passing by.
D. She observed that moss usually grows on the north side of trees.

5. What did the writer learn from this camping experience?
A. That a compass is never useful in a forest.
B. That girls are always better at reading maps than boys.
C. That we must listen to each other because everyone has a special strength.
D. That it is very dangerous to talk loudly in the mountains.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "The main task was to learn about wild plants and how to survive (生存) in nature."

**【解析】** 开头明确交代了这次野营活动的主要任务：了解野生植物以及如何在自然里生存。这与C选项完全对应，A抓鱼、B捡垃圾、D建大木屋均非主要目标。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第②段中先生的原话 "'Your task this afternoon is to find a small lake hidden in the forest. You must be back before dinner,' he ordered."

**【解析】** 格林老师第一天下午给出的任务是寻找一个隐藏在森林里的小湖（find a small lake），并且要在晚餐前赶回来（before dinner）。A（自己找营地）、C（无人帮助搭帐篷）、D（为大家做饭）统统不是他们这一下午的任务。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段里 "We started to argue. Tom wanted to turn left, but I wanted to keep going straight."

**【解析】** 作者和好友汤姆开始争吵（started to argue），起因是因为指路针和地图的理解不同，汤姆想左转，作者想直走。这导致了他们在走哪条路的问题上达不成共识（couldn't agree on which way to go）。故选A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第④段里Lily的发言 "'Actually (事实上),' she pointed at the moss (青苔) on a big tree, 'moss usually grows on the north side of the trees in this area...'"

**【解析】** Lily指出树上的青苔（moss）这个线索，提到这一带青苔通常长在树的北面。通过此自然常识观察，大家才搞清了北在哪里。这印证了D选项（她通过观察青苔在树的北面生长而得知方向）。其他三项显然不合群及在山林无信号中常理。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 结尾第⑥段最后一句 "I learned that everyone has a special strength, and we must listen to each other to succeed."

**【解析】** 作者在篝火晚宴上领悟到了这件事情给他上的一课：每个人都有其特殊的长项（没人敢轻视安静的Lily了），要想取得成功，我们需要倾听彼此的意见。C项不仅是该句的同义复述，也切合了文意。其他A指北针没用、B女生总比男生好、D大声讲话危险都不是文章传达的价值观和核心启示。故选C。
