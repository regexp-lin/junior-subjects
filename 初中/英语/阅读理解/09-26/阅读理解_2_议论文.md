# 阅读理解（议论文）

① In many Chinese middle schools, students have a 10-minute break between classes. During this time, they can rest, go to the toilet, or prepare for the next lesson. However, in recent years, some people have asked: Is a 10-minute break long enough? Should schools give students more time?

② Those who think 10 minutes is enough say that students need to stay focused on learning. A short break helps them relax without losing their study mood. If the break is too long, students may play too much and find it hard to calm down when class starts again. Besides, the school timetable is already full. Longer breaks would mean fewer lessons or a later school day. Many parents also worry that if students have more free time at school, they might get into trouble.

③ On the other hand, those who want longer breaks have strong reasons. First, students often need to walk to another classroom, go to the toilet, and get a drink of water — all in 10 minutes. Sometimes they have to queue (排队) and cannot finish in time. Second, students sit for a long time in class. Their eyes and bodies need more rest. Doctors say that looking at the board or books for too long can harm the eyes. A longer break would let students go outside, look at green trees, and move around. Third, students need time to talk with friends. Social time is important for their mental health (心理健康).

④ In my view, 10 minutes may be too short for many students. Schools could try a 15-minute break after every two or three classes. This would give students enough time to move, rest their eyes, and chat with friends, without taking too much time from lessons. Schools could also make sure there are enough toilets and water stations so that students do not have to queue for long. The key is to find a balance between study and rest.

⑤ Every student is different. Some need more rest; some can manage with 10 minutes. But we should listen to what students say and make changes when necessary.

**注释：** queue 排队；mental health 心理健康

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people think 10 minutes is enough?
   A. Because students like short breaks.
   B. Because a short break helps students relax without losing focus; longer breaks may make it hard to calm down.
   C. Because schools have no space for longer breaks.
   D. Because parents want more lessons.

2. What problem do those who want longer breaks mention?
   A. Students never need to go to the toilet.
   B. Students often need to change classrooms, use the toilet, and get water — 10 minutes may not be enough; they also need more rest for their eyes and body.
   C. Longer breaks cost too much money.
   D. Teachers do not like breaks.

3. What does the writer suggest?
   A. Cancel all breaks.
   B. Try a 15-minute break after every two or three classes, and provide enough toilets and water stations.
   C. Let students decide the break length.
   D. Make breaks only 5 minutes long.

4. The word "manage" in Paragraph ⑤ ("some can manage with 10 minutes") probably means _____.
   A. fail
   B. complain
   C. deal with or cope with
   D. forget

5. What is the writer's main opinion about break time?
   A. 10 minutes is always enough.
   B. 10 minutes may be too short; schools should consider longer breaks and listen to students.
   C. Breaks should be banned.
   D. Only teachers should decide break length.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "A short break helps them relax without losing their study mood. If the break is too long, students may play too much and find it hard to calm down"

**【解析】** 支持者认为短休息有助于放松且不分散注意力，过长会使学生难以收心。A、C、D 项非主要理由。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students often need to walk to another classroom, go to the toilet, and get a drink of water — all in 10 minutes... Their eyes and bodies need more rest... A longer break would let students go outside"

**【解析】** 反对者指出换教室、如厕、饮水可能不够时间，且需更多护眼和身体休息。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Schools could try a 15-minute break after every two or three classes... make sure there are enough toilets and water stations"

**【解析】** 作者建议尝试每两三节课后 15 分钟休息，并增加厕所和饮水处。A、C、D 项与建议矛盾。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Some need more rest; some can manage with 10 minutes" — 与 "need more rest" 对照

**【解析】** 上下文对比：有人需更久休息，有人 10 分钟即可应付。manage 意为"应付、应付得了"。A、B、D 项不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第④、⑤段 "10 minutes may be too short... Schools could try a 15-minute break... we should listen to what students say"

**【解析】** 作者认为 10 分钟可能不够，建议考虑延长并听取学生意见。A、C、D 项与作者立场不符。故选 B。
