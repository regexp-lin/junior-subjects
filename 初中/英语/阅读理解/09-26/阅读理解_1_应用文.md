# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Sunny Family Farm — Weekend Parent-Child Activity**

② Join us for a fun weekend on the farm! Our parent-child farm experience helps families enjoy nature and learn about farm life together. Children can feed animals, pick vegetables, and try simple farm work. Parents and kids work as a team and spend quality time outdoors.

③ **Date & Time:** Every Saturday and Sunday, 9:00 a.m. — 4:00 p.m.

④ **What to Do:**
- Feed the sheep, ducks, and rabbits
- Pick fresh tomatoes and strawberries in the farm garden
- Learn to make simple bread with farm flour
- Ride a tractor (拖拉机) around the farm (children must be over 6 years old)
- Take photos in the sunflower field

⑤ **What to Bring:**
- Comfortable clothes and shoes (you may get dirty)
- Hat and sunscreen (防晒霜) for sunny days
- Water bottle and snacks (lunch is not included)
- Camera for photos

⑥ **Price:** ¥120 per person (children under 3 free). Groups of 5 or more: ¥100 per person. Book at least 3 days before your visit. Call 0755-8888-1234 or visit www.sunnyfarm.com to reserve (预订).

**注释：** tractor 拖拉机；sunscreen 防晒霜；reserve 预订

---

**Questions:**

1. What is this activity about?
   A. A school trip to a museum.
   B. A weekend parent-child farm experience with animals and vegetables.
   C. A cooking class at home.
   D. A sports competition.

2. When can families join the activity?
   A. Only on weekdays.
   B. Every Saturday and Sunday from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
   C. Only in the morning.
   D. At night.

3. What must children do before riding the tractor?
   A. Bring their own hat.
   B. Be over 6 years old.
   C. Pay extra money.
   D. Book one week earlier.

4. Which of the following is NOT suggested to bring?
   A. Comfortable clothes and shoes.
   B. Hat and sunscreen.
   C. A full lunch box.
   D. Water bottle and snacks.

5. How much would a family of 4 (two adults and two children) pay if they book with another family of 3?
   A. ¥480.
   B. ¥700.
   C. ¥600.
   D. ¥840.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第①、②段 "Sunny Family Farm — Weekend Parent-Child Activity" 及 "parent-child farm experience helps families enjoy nature... feed animals, pick vegetables"

**【解析】** 文章介绍周末亲子农场体验活动，包括喂动物、采摘蔬菜等。A、C、D 项与主题不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Every Saturday and Sunday, 9:00 a.m. — 4:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明活动时间为每周六日 9:00-16:00。A、C、D 项与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Ride a tractor around the farm (children must be over 6 years old)"

**【解析】** 儿童须年满 6 岁才能乘坐拖拉机。A、C、D 项未提及或与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解/推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Water bottle and snacks (lunch is not included)"

**【解析】** 建议带水壶和零食，且说明午餐不包含，即不要求带完整便当；文章未建议带 full lunch box。A、B、D 项均为建议携带。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Groups of 5 or more: ¥100 per person"；4+3=7 人，符合团体优惠

**【解析】** 两家共 7 人，属于 5 人及以上团体，每人 ¥100，共 7×100=¥700。A 项按原价计算；C、D 项计算错误。故选 B。
