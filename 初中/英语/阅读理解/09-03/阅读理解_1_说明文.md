# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is a technology that lets machines "think" and learn like humans in some ways. You may have already used AI without knowing it. For example, when you ask your phone a question and it answers you, or when a music app recommends (推荐) songs you might like, that is AI at work. AI is becoming part of our daily life, and it is useful to understand what it is and how it works.

② How does AI work? AI systems are trained with a lot of data. They look at examples — like millions of pictures of cats — and learn to recognize (识别) patterns. Once trained, they can do tasks such as finding a cat in a new photo, translating languages, or playing chess. Some AI can even create text, images, or music. The more data and the better the program, the smarter the AI can become.

③ AI has many uses. In education, AI can help students learn. Some apps can give you practice questions based on your weak points. In medicine, AI can help doctors find diseases earlier by looking at X-rays and other images. In daily life, AI powers voice assistants, navigation (导航) maps, and smart home devices. In factories, AI can control robots to do repetitive (重复的) work, so humans can focus on more creative tasks.

④ However, AI also brings challenges. Some people worry about their jobs. If machines can do certain tasks, will humans still be needed? Others worry about privacy (隐私). AI needs data to learn, and sometimes that data includes personal information. There are also worries about AI making mistakes or being used in bad ways. That is why many countries are making rules to guide how AI is developed and used.

⑤ AI is a powerful tool. It can help us solve problems and make life easier. But we must use it wisely. Learning about AI and how to use it well will be important for young people in the future. The key is to be both curious and careful.

**注释：** recommend 推荐；recognize 识别；navigation 导航；repetitive 重复的；privacy 隐私

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ①, which is an example of AI in daily life?
   A. Reading a paper book.
   B. A music app recommending songs you might like.
   C. Writing with a pen.
   D. Riding a bicycle.

2. How do AI systems learn?
   A. By reading one book.
   B. By being trained with a lot of data and learning patterns.
   C. By asking humans every question.
   D. By doing nothing.

3. Which use of AI is mentioned in the passage?
   A. AI can help doctors find diseases earlier.
   B. AI can replace all human jobs.
   C. AI can make humans live forever.
   D. AI can control the weather.

4. What do some people worry about regarding AI?
   A. AI is too cheap.
   B. Jobs, privacy, and AI making mistakes or being misused.
   C. AI is too slow.
   D. AI will make everyone rich.

5. What does the writer suggest about AI?
   A. We should be afraid of AI and never use it.
   B. We should use AI wisely and learn about it for the future.
   C. AI is not useful at all.
   D. Only adults should learn about AI.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "when a music app recommends songs you might like, that is AI at work"

**【解析】** 原文举例说明音乐应用推荐歌曲是AI的应用之一。A项"读纸质书"、C项"用钢笔写字"和D项"骑自行车"均非AI应用。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "AI systems are trained with a lot of data. They look at examples... and learn to recognize patterns."

**【解析】** 原文说明AI通过大量数据进行训练并学习模式。A项"读一本书"、C项"向人类询问每个问题"和D项"什么都不做"均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "In medicine, AI can help doctors find diseases earlier by looking at X-rays and other images."

**【解析】** 原文明确提到AI可辅助医生通过影像更早发现疾病。B项"取代所有人类工作"、C项"让人永生"和D项"控制天气"均未提及。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Some people worry about their jobs... Others worry about privacy... There are also worries about AI making mistakes or being used in bad ways."

**【解析】** 文中列举的担忧包括就业、隐私、错误及滥用。A项"太便宜"、C项"太慢"和D项"让人致富"均与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "We must use it wisely. Learning about AI and how to use it well will be important for young people in the future. The key is to be both curious and careful."

**【解析】** 作者建议明智使用AI、学习相关知识，并对年轻人寄予期望。A项"恐惧并不使用"与文意相反；C项"完全无用"与全文矛盾；D项"仅成人应学习"与"young people"不符。故选B。
