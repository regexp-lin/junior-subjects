# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Have you ever looked at a rainbow in the sky after a rainstorm? It is one of the most beautiful magic shows in nature. But do you know how a rainbow is made? It is actually all about sunlight and water drops.

② Sunlight looks white, but it is actually made of seven different colors: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo (靛蓝), and violet (紫罗兰色). We usually cannot see these colors separately. However, when sunlight passes through a glass prism (三棱镜), the white light bends (折射) and separates into these seven colors.

③ A rainbow works in the same way. When it rains, there are millions of tiny water drops hanging in the air. Each water drop acts like a very small prism. When the sun comes out, sunlight enters these water drops. The water drops bend the light and split (把……分开) it into the seven beautiful colors. Then, the colors bounce (反射) back to our eyes. We see all these colors together as a big, curved (弯曲的) rainbow in the sky.

④ You can only see a rainbow if the sun is shining behind you and it is raining in front of you. Because of this, rainbows usually appear in the early morning or late afternoon, when the sun is lower in the sky. 

⑤ Next time it rains while the sun is shining, remember to look up. If you are lucky, you might just catch the magical colors of nature's biggest bridge!

**注释：** prism 三棱镜；separately 分别地；split 把……分开；bounce 反射；curved 弯曲的

---

### 选择题

1. How many colors is sunlight actually made of according to paragraph ②?
A. Three.
B. Five.
C. Seven.
D. Nine.

2. What acts like a small glass prism when a rainbow forms in nature?
A. A white cloud.
B. The warm sun.
C. A tall tree.
D. A tiny water drop in the air.

3. Form paragraph ③, what happens when sunlight enters the water drops?
A. The water drops turn the light into heavy rain.
B. The water drops dry up quickly in the air.
C. The water drops bend the light and split it into different colors.
D. The water drops make the light completely white and bright.

4. If you want to see a rainbow, where should the sun be?
A. Right in front of your eyes.
B. Shining behind your back.
C. Hidden quietly behind dark clouds.
D. High up in the middle of the sky at noon.

5. What does the writer compare (把……比作) a rainbow to in the last paragraph?
A. A colorful glass prism.
B. A small water drop.
C. Nature's biggest bridge.
D. A white light in the morning.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "...but it is actually made of seven different colors:"

**【解析】** 原文中清楚列举出了七种颜色并且说阳光是由七种（seven）不同的颜色组成的。故选C。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Each water drop acts like a very small prism."

**【解析】** 据原文所述，在彩虹形成过程中充当微小三棱镜作用的是空气中悬浮的小水珠（A tiny water drop in the air）。其他如云朵、太阳、树均不是文中提到的“三棱镜”。故选D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段中 "When the sun comes out, sunlight enters these water drops. The water drops bend the light and split (把……分开) it into the seven beautiful colors."

**【解析】** 当太阳光进入水珠后，水珠会使光线折射并且将其分成（bend the light and split it into）七种美丽的色彩。A（变成大雨），B（迅速蒸发干），D（使之全白刺眼）均不是水珠对此所产生的作用。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段首句 "You can only see a rainbow if the sun is shining behind you and it is raining in front of you."

**【解析】** 原文中观赏彩虹的条件说得非常明确：阳光必须在你的身背或者身后（shining behind you）照射，且你的正前方在下雨。如果正对着你（A），或者中午当头（D），是不容易看到彩虹的。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段末尾 "...you might just catch the magical colors of nature's biggest bridge!"

**【解析】** 在文末，作者让读者下雨天抬头看，如果幸运没准儿就能捕捉到“大自然最大的桥”（nature's biggest bridge）那奇幻的色彩。因此是把彩虹比作了这座大桥。A、B、D三个比喻在最后一段均未出现。故选C。
