> **04-17 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① The old grandfather clock in our living room was my grandfather's favorite thing. It was taller than my dad, made of dark brown wood, and had a beautiful golden face. Every hour, it would strike (敲打) loudly. *Dong! Dong! Dong!* Since my grandfather passed away last year, the clock had been totally silent. It was broken.

② We missed the sound of the clock, especially my grandmother. She often looked at it sadly. My dad tried to fix it many times, but he couldn't find the problem. He said we might have to throw it away or put it in the dark garage.

③ I couldn't let that happen. One Sunday afternoon, while my parents were out shopping, I decided to open the back of the clock. It was very dusty inside. I used a small flashlight (手电筒) and carefully looked at all the little metal wheels. Then, I saw it! A tiny feather (羽毛) was stuck between two moving parts. 

④ I found a pair of thin tweezers (镊子) and gently pulled the feather out. After that, I gave the golden pendulum (钟摆) a little push. *Tick, tock, tick, tock.* The sound was soft at first, but then it grew strong and steady. Suddenly, it was 5:00 PM. The clock began to strike. *Dong! Dong! Dong!*

⑤ When my parents and grandmother returned home, they couldn't believe their ears. My grandmother had tears of joy in her eyes. I smiled proudly. The heartbeat of our house was back, and I felt like a little piece of my grandfather was with us again.

**注释：** pass away 逝世；garage 车库；flashlight 手电筒；stuck 卡住的；pendulum 钟摆

---

### 选择题

1. What did the writer's grandfather love most in the living room?
A. A small golden watch.
B. The dark garage.
C. A beautiful white table.
D. The old grandfather clock.

2. Why had the clock been totally silent since last year?
A. Because the writer's father turned it off.
B. Because it was broken after the grandfather passed away.
C. Because the grandmother didn't want to hear it.
D. Because it was moved to the dusty garage.

3. What did the writer's father say they might have to do with the clock?
A. Clean it with a flashlight.
B. Give it to a neighbor.
C. Throw it away or put it in the garage.
D. Ask the grandfather to fix it.

4. What caused the problem with the clock?
A. The golden face was missing.
B. A tiny feather was stuck between two moving parts.
C. The pendulum was taken away by the parents.
D. It had too much dark brown wood.

5. How did the grandmother feel when she returned home according to paragraph ⑤?
A. She was very angry at the writer.
B. She was so happy that she had tears of joy.
C. She was afraid of the loud *dong* sound.
D. She felt tired because she went shopping for a long time.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段首句 "The old grandfather clock in our living room was my grandfather's favorite thing."

**【解析】** 原文讲得很直接：客厅那座老式落地钟（grandfather clock）是爷爷生前最喜爱的东西。A（小金表）、B（黑车库）、C（白桌子）都不是提及的最爱物件。故选D。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段末尾 "...the clock had been totally silent. It was broken."

**【解析】** 原文中陈述自从去年爷爷去世之后，那钟就再也不响（totally silent）了，因为钟坏了（It was broken）。A（被老爸关掉）、C（奶奶不想听）以及D（搬去车库才不响的）都不符合首段给出的事实。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段末尾 "He said we might have to throw it away or put it in the dark garage."

**【解析】** 因为老爸修不好钟，他说也许只得把它扔掉（throw it away）或者是丢进黑暗的车库里（put it in the dark garage）。这就排除了A（用电筒洗）、B（拿去送人）、D（叫爷爷来修）。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段末尾 "A tiny feather (羽毛) was stuck between two moving parts."

**【解析】** 作者自己探查发现钟罢工不走的真正元凶是有一根小羽毛卡在（was stuck）了两个运动零部件中间而停了下来。A（金色钟面丢失），C（钟摆拿走），D（棕木头太多）皆纯属虚构干扰项。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2句 "My grandmother had tears of joy in her eyes."

**【解析】** 听到钟声重新在屋里荡漾，回到家的奶奶眼里噙满了喜悦的泪水（tears of joy），这也是代表了她被作者修好钟一事感动极其开心。A（大发脾气）、C（害怕那声音）、D（逛街太累）皆不是奶奶归家进门听到钟响落泪的真实情绪理由。故选B。
