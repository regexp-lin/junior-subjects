> **04-31 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Maya looked at her reflection (倒影) in the mirror and sighed (叹气). Tomorrow was the school's annual Talent Show. Maya had practiced playing the piano for months. She knew every note perfectly in her bedroom, but the thought of playing in front of hundreds of people made her hands shake. 

② At dinner, Maya pushed her food around her plate. Her older brother, Sam, noticed her pale face. "Are you worried about the Talent Show?" he asked. Maya nodded slowly. "I'm afraid I will forget everything the moment I step on the stage (舞台). My mind will go completely blank."

③ Sam smiled and put down his fork. "Do you remember when I learned to ride a bicycle?" Maya thought for a moment. "Yes, you fell off many times. You even cried once because you scraped (擦伤) your knee."

④ "Exactly," Sam said. "But did I stop trying?" Maya shook her head. "No, you got back on and learned it." Sam patted (拍) her shoulder. "That's the secret, Maya. Everyone feels scared before doing something important. It is perfectly normal. Bravery (勇敢) doesn't mean you are not scared. It means you are scared, but you do it anyway."

⑤ The next evening, Maya stood behind the heavy red curtains of the stage. Her heart was beating like a fast drum. When the host called her name, she took a deep breath. She remembered Sam's words: *You are scared, but you do it anyway.* 

⑥ Maya walked to the piano, sat down, and touched the cool keys. She closed her eyes for a second, blocking out the large crowd. Then, her fingers began to move. The music flowed out smoothly and beautifully, filling the whole hall. When she finished the last note, the room was silent for a moment before bursting into loud cheers and applause (掌声). Maya smiled widely. She had beaten her fear.

**注释：** sigh 叹气；stage 舞台；scrape 擦伤；bravery 勇敢；applause 掌声

### 选择题

1. How did Maya feel about the upcoming Talent Show in paragraph ①?
A. She was very excited and couldn't wait.
B. She was so angry that she wanted to leave the school.
C. She was extremely confident because she practiced for months.
D. She was very nervous and her hands shook at the thought of it.

2. What was Maya's biggest worry according to paragraph ②?
A. That the piano on the stage would be broken.
B. That she would forget everything when she stepped on the stage.
C. That her older brother Sam wouldn't come to watch her.
D. That she was too hungry to perform well.

3. What story did Sam use to comfort (安慰) Maya?
A. The story of him winning a singing competition.
B. The story of them playing in the rain last year.
C. The story of him learning to ride a bicycle and falling off.
D. The story of him failing a math test.

4. What is Sam's definition (定义) of bravery?
A. Being perfectly prepared and never making a mistake.
B. Never feeling scared or nervous about anything.
C. Not doing things that you are afraid of to stay safe.
D. Feeling scared but doing the thing anyway.

5. What happened right after Maya finished her performance?
A. The crowd threw tomatoes at her.
B. She ran off the stage crying.
C. The room fell silent, and then there were loud cheers and applause.
D. Sam rushed to the stage and carried her away.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "...but the thought of playing in front of hundreds of people made her hands shake."

**【解析】** 文中交代，玛雅虽然在卧室里练得很完美，但一想到要在数百人面前演奏，手就吓得直发抖（made her hands shake）。由此可见她非常紧张害怕。故选D。A和C的激动自信不符合原文；B项愤怒更是无中生有。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "'I'm afraid I will forget everything the moment I step on the stage. My mind will go completely blank.'"

**【解析】** 当哥哥萨姆问玛雅是不是在担心才艺秀时，她点点头并说出她的最大担忧：怕自己一登上舞台就忘掉所有的乐谱，大脑完全一片空白。B选项是她这句话的直接转述。故选B。A（钢琴坏掉）、C（哥哥不来）、D（太饿）等担忧并未在文中出现。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1-2句 "Sam smiled and put down his fork. 'Do you remember when I learned to ride a bicycle?'"

**【解析】** 哥哥萨姆为了安慰玛雅，举了自己当年学骑自行车（learn to ride a bicycle）并且摔倒擦破膝盖的往事作为例子。A（唱歌比赛）、B（雨中玩耍）、D（数学考砸）都不是哥哥提起的故事。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后两句 "Bravery (勇敢) doesn't mean you are not scared. It means you are scared, but you do it anyway."

**【解析】** 这是全文最为点题的核心金句。萨姆给出的关于勇敢的定义是：勇敢并不意味着你不害怕。它的意思是虽然你很害怕，但你无论如何都要硬着头皮去干（you are scared, but you do it anyway）。这与D选项完美契合，同时反驳了题干中的A、B、C项。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段最后两小句 "...the room was silent for a moment before bursting into loud cheers and applause. Maya smiled widely."

**【解析】** 当她弹完最后一个音符后，大厅里先是安静了一瞬，随后爆发出响亮的欢呼声和掌声（silent for a moment before bursting into loud cheers and applause）。A丢番茄、B哭着跑开、D哥哥冲上来抱走，皆不符合这成功且感人的结局场面。故选C。
