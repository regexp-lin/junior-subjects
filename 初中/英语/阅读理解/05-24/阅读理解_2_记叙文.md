# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Zhang Wei was a quiet boy who loved science. He spent most of his free time reading science books and doing small experiments in his room. His parents thought he was a dreamer. "Why don't you go out and play with other children?" his mother often asked. But Zhang Wei preferred to stay indoors and explore how things worked.

② When his school announced the annual science fair, Zhang Wei decided to take part. He wanted to build a simple solar-powered fan (太阳能风扇) that could work without electricity. He spent two months designing and testing his project. At first, the fan did not spin (转动) well. He tried different materials and positions for the solar panel. After many failures, he finally made it work.

③ On the day of the science fair, the gym was full of students, teachers, and parents. Zhang Wei stood beside his project, feeling nervous. He explained his idea to the judges: "I want to help people in places without electricity. A small solar fan can keep people cool on hot days." The judges asked him several questions, and he answered confidently. One judge even smiled and said, "This is a creative and useful idea."

④ When the results were announced, Zhang Wei could not believe his ears. He had won the first prize! His parents, who had come to watch, hugged him with tears in their eyes. "We are so proud of you," his father said. Zhang Wei's classmates cheered and crowded around him to congratulate him. For the first time, he felt that his "dreaming" had led to something real.

⑤ Later, Zhang Wei was invited to share his project at a city-level science meeting. He told other students, "Don't give up when you fail. Every experiment teaches you something. If you love what you do, keep trying." His story encouraged many young people to follow their interests in science.

**注释：** solar-powered 太阳能的；spin 转动

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what did Zhang Wei like to do in his free time?
   A. Read science books and do experiments.
   B. Play with other children outdoors.
   C. Watch TV and play computer games.
   D. Help his parents with housework.

2. What did Zhang Wei build for the science fair, according to paragraph ②?
   A. A wind-powered light.
   B. A solar-powered fan.
   C. A water purifier.
   D. A robot that could clean rooms.

3. According to paragraph ③, what did the judge say about Zhang Wei's project?
   A. The project was too simple.
   B. It needed more testing.
   C. It was creative and useful.
   D. The solar panel was in the wrong position.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Zhang Wei?
   A. He had won many prizes before.
   B. He often felt proud of himself at school.
   C. His parents had not always supported his interest in science, but they were proud of his achievement.
   D. His classmates did not like him at all.

5. What is the writer's main purpose in writing this passage?
   A. To encourage students to follow their interests and never give up.
   B. To introduce different types of science fair projects.
   C. To explain how solar panels work.
   D. To compare Zhang Wei with other students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He spent most of his free time reading science books and doing small experiments in his room."

**【解析】** 原文直接说明张伟把大部分课余时间用于读科学书和做小实验，A 项正确。B 项"和小朋友户外玩"与"preferred to stay indoors"矛盾；C 项"看电视玩游戏"和 D 项"帮父母做家务"均未在文中提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He wanted to build a simple solar-powered fan that could work without electricity."

**【解析】** 张伟计划制作一台可在无电情况下工作的简易太阳能风扇，B 项正确。A 项"风力灯"、C 项"净水器"和 D 项"清洁机器人"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "One judge even smiled and said, 'This is a creative and useful idea.'"

**【解析】** 评委明确评价张伟的作品"有创意且实用"，C 项正确。A 项"太简单"、B 项"需要更多测试"和 D 项"太阳能板位置错误"均与评委原话相悖。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第①段 "His parents thought he was a dreamer. 'Why don't you go out and play with other children?'"；第④段 "His parents, who had come to watch, hugged him with tears in their eyes. 'We are so proud of you,' his father said."

**【解析】** 第①段显示父母曾认为张伟是"空想家"并建议他出去玩，暗示此前对他沉迷科学的态度并非完全支持；第④段父母眼含热泪拥抱他并说"我们为你骄傲"，说明获奖后他们为他的成就感到自豪，C 项推断合理。A 项"以前拿过很多奖"与"could not believe his ears"矛盾；B 项"常感到自豪"与"For the first time"矛盾；D 项"同学不喜欢他"与"classmates cheered and crowded around him"矛盾。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：张伟坚持科学爱好→克服困难完成项目→获奖→受邀分享→第⑤段寄语"Don't give up when you fail... If you love what you do, keep trying."

**【解析】** 文章通过张伟从被视作"空想家"到获奖、受邀分享的成长经历，意在鼓励学生坚持兴趣、勇于尝试、不轻言放弃，A 项正确。B 项"介绍各类科学展项目"过于具体；C 项"解释太阳能板原理"和 D 项"比较张伟与他人"均非主要写作意图。故选 A。
