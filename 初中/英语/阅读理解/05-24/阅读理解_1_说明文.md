# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Have you ever wondered how plants get their food? Unlike people and animals, plants cannot go to the supermarket or hunt for something to eat. Instead, they make their own food through a process called photosynthesis (光合作用). This amazing process happens inside the leaves of green plants.

② For photosynthesis to work, a plant needs three things: sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide. Sunlight comes from the sun. Water is absorbed (吸收) by the roots from the soil and moves up to the leaves through special tubes. Carbon dioxide enters the plant through tiny holes on the underside of the leaves. When these three things meet inside the leaf, something wonderful happens.

③ Inside the leaves, there are small parts called chloroplasts. They contain a green substance called chlorophyll (叶绿素), which gives plants their green color. Chlorophyll can catch sunlight and use its energy. When sunlight hits the chlorophyll, it starts the process of making food. This is why plants need to be in a place with enough light — without light, they cannot make food.

④ During photosynthesis, the plant uses sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide to produce two important things. First, it makes glucose (葡萄糖), a kind of sugar that provides energy for the plant to grow. Second, it releases oxygen into the air. This oxygen is what we and other animals breathe. So when you breathe in fresh air, you can thank the plants around you.

⑤ Photosynthesis is one of the most important processes on Earth. It provides food for plants, and plants are the base of the food chain. Many animals eat plants, and other animals eat those animals. Without photosynthesis, life on our planet would not exist as we know it.

**注释：** photosynthesis 光合作用；absorb 吸收；chlorophyll 叶绿素；glucose 葡萄糖

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, how do plants get their food?
   A. They make their own food through photosynthesis.
   B. They go to the supermarket like people.
   C. They hunt for food like animals.
   D. They eat soil and rocks.

2. According to paragraph ②, what does a plant need for photosynthesis?
   A. Only sunlight and water.
   B. Sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide.
   C. Only carbon dioxide and soil.
   D. Water, soil, and oxygen.

3. According to paragraph ③, why do plants need to be in a place with light?
   A. Because light helps them absorb water faster.
   B. Because light keeps the leaves warm.
   C. Because chlorophyll needs sunlight to start making food.
   D. Because light helps carbon dioxide enter the leaves.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④?
   A. Plants produce oxygen that humans and animals need to breathe.
   B. Glucose is only used by animals.
   C. Plants do not need oxygen at all.
   D. Carbon dioxide is produced by plants during photosynthesis.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To teach readers how to grow plants at home.
   B. To compare plants with animals.
   C. To describe different types of leaves.
   D. To explain how plants make food through photosynthesis.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Instead, they make their own food through a process called photosynthesis."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出植物通过光合作用自己制造食物，A 项正确。B 项"去超市"、C 项"像动物一样狩猎"和 D 项"吃土壤和石头"均与原文"Unlike people and animals, plants cannot go to the supermarket or hunt"矛盾。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "For photosynthesis to work, a plant needs three things: sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide."

**【解析】** 原文明确列出光合作用所需三要素：阳光、水和二氧化碳，B 项正确。A 项缺少二氧化碳；C 项"土壤和二氧化碳"错误，需要的是阳光、水、二氧化碳；D 项"氧气"错误，植物释放氧气而非需要氧气参与光合作用。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Chlorophyll can catch sunlight and use its energy... This is why plants need to be in a place with enough light — without light, they cannot make food."

**【解析】** 叶绿素需要捕获阳光才能启动制造食物的过程，因此植物必须置于有光处，C 项正确。A 项"加快吸水"、B 项"保持叶片温暖"和 D 项"帮助二氧化碳进入"均非原文所述原因。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Second, it releases oxygen into the air. This oxygen is what we and other animals breathe. So when you breathe in fresh air, you can thank the plants around you."

**【解析】** 植物在光合作用中释放氧气，而人类和动物需要呼吸这种氧气，可推断植物产生的氧气对人和动物的呼吸至关重要，A 项正确。B 项"葡萄糖仅被动物使用"错误，葡萄糖供植物生长；C 项"植物不需要氧气"与呼吸作用矛盾；D 项"植物产生二氧化碳"错误，植物消耗二氧化碳、释放氧气。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段引入光合作用概念，第②段光合作用三要素，第③段叶绿素与光的作用，第④段产物，第⑤段光合作用重要性。

**【解析】** 全文围绕植物如何通过光合作用制造食物展开，D 项准确概括主旨。A 项"教读者在家种植物"、B 项"比较植物与动物"和 C 项"描述不同叶片类型"均非文章主要目的。故选 D。
