# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Healthy Eating Guide for Teenagers**

① A balanced diet helps you grow, stay strong, and focus better at school. Here are some tips for teenagers.

**Daily food guide:**

| Food type | How much | Examples |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Grains | 5–8 servings | Rice, bread, noodles |
| Vegetables & fruit | 5+ servings | Broccoli, carrots, apples, bananas |
| Protein | 2–3 servings | Eggs, fish, meat, beans, milk |
| Fats & sugar | A little | Oil, sweets — not too much |

② **Eat breakfast.** Do not skip breakfast. Your brain needs fuel after a long night. A good breakfast can include: eggs, milk, oatmeal, or bread with fruit. Students who eat breakfast often do better in class. They have more energy and can concentrate longer.

③ **Choose healthy snacks.** Many teenagers like chips, candy, and cola. These taste good but have too much sugar, salt, or fat. They can make you gain weight and feel tired. Instead, try: fruit, nuts, yogurt, or whole-grain biscuits. Drink water or milk instead of sweet drinks. If you must have a soft drink, choose a small size and not every day.

④ **Limit junk food.** Junk food is quick and cheap, but it is not good for your body. Fast food, fried food, and sugary drinks should be "sometimes" food, not "every day" food. Cook at home when you can. Homemade meals are usually healthier. Help your parents in the kitchen. You might discover that cooking is fun!

⑤ **Eat regularly.** Do not starve yourself to lose weight. Do not eat too much at one meal. Three main meals plus one or two small snacks a day is a good plan. Listen to your body. When you are hungry, eat. When you are full, stop. Healthy eating is a habit. Start small, and it will get easier.

**注释：** balanced 均衡的；serving 一份；oatmeal 燕麦粥；yogurt 酸奶；junk food 垃圾食品

---

**Questions:**

1. How many servings of grains should teenagers have each day?
   A. 1–2.
   B. 2–3.
   C. 5–8.
   D. 10 or more.

2. Why is breakfast important?
   A. Because it is cheap.
   B. Because the brain needs fuel after a long night; students who eat it often do better.
   C. Because teachers ask you to eat it.
   D. Because it tastes the best.

3. What should teenagers choose instead of chips and candy for snacks?
   A. More chips.
   B. Fruit, nuts, yogurt, or whole-grain biscuits.
   C. Only cola.
   D. Nothing.

4. What does "sometimes" food mean?
   A. Food you eat sometimes during the day.
   B. Food you eat only once in a while, not every day.
   C. Food you never eat.
   D. Food you eat for breakfast.

5. What is a good eating plan?
   A. Skip meals to lose weight.
   B. Three main meals plus one or two small snacks a day; eat when hungry, stop when full.
   C. Eat only once a day.
   D. Eat as much as possible at every meal.

---

**Answers and Explanations:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 表格 "Grains | 5–8 servings"

**【解析】** 表格显示谷物类每天5–8份。A、B、D项数量均不正确。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Your brain needs fuel after a long night. A good breakfast... Students who eat breakfast often do better in class."

**【解析】** 大脑经过一夜需要能量，吃早餐的学生往往表现更好。A项便宜、C项老师要求、D项最好吃均非主要原因。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Instead, try: fruit, nuts, yogurt, or whole-grain biscuits."

**【解析】** 应用水果、坚果、酸奶或全麦饼干代替薯片和糖果。A、C、D项均与建议相反。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段 "Fast food, fried food, and sugary drinks should be 'sometimes' food, not 'every day' food."

**【解析】** "sometimes" food 指偶尔吃、非每天吃的食物。A项一天中有时吃、C项从不吃、D项早餐吃均不准确。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Three main meals plus one or two small snacks a day is a good plan... When you are hungry, eat. When you are full, stop."

**【解析】** 好计划是一日三餐加一两份小零食，饿了就吃、饱了即停。A、C、D项均与健康饮食建议矛盾。故选B。
