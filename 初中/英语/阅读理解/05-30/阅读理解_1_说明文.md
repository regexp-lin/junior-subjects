# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Polar bears (北极熊) are one of the largest animals living on land. They spend most of their lives on the sea ice in the Arctic (北极), where they hunt for seals (海豹) to eat. The white fur on their bodies helps them hide in the snow and wait for their prey (猎物). However, these powerful animals are now facing a serious problem — their home is disappearing.

② The main reason is climate change. As our planet gets warmer, the Arctic sea ice melts (融化) more quickly in summer and takes longer to form in winter. Polar bears need the ice to travel, hunt, and raise their young. Without enough ice, they cannot reach the seals that swim under the water. Scientists have found that polar bears are becoming thinner and weaker because they cannot find enough food.

③ Some polar bears have to swim very long distances to find ice. This uses up a lot of their energy. When a mother bear cannot get enough food, her cubs (幼崽) may not survive. In recent years, the number of polar bears has dropped in some areas. Experts worry that if the ice keeps melting, polar bears could become endangered or even disappear in the future.

④ What can we do to help? We need to slow down climate change by using less energy, driving less, and choosing products that do not harm the environment. Small actions, when done by many people, can make a big difference. Schools and families can also learn more about polar bears and spread the word about protecting them.

⑤ The story of the polar bear reminds us that every living thing on Earth is connected. When we protect the Arctic, we protect not only the polar bears but also our own future. It is never too late to start caring for our planet.

**注释：** Arctic 北极；seal 海豹；prey 猎物；melt 融化；cub 幼崽；endangered 濒危的

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what helps polar bears hide when hunting?
   A. Their strong body.
   B. Their white fur.
   C. Their loud voice.
   D. Their fast speed.

2. Why are polar bears becoming thinner according to paragraph ②?
   A. They do not like to eat seals anymore.
   B. The sea ice is melting and they cannot find enough food.
   C. They spend too much time swimming for fun.
   D. There are too many polar bears and they must share food.

3. What may happen to polar bear cubs when mother bears cannot get enough food?
   A. They will grow faster.
   B. They may not survive.
   C. They will move to other countries.
   D. They will learn to hunt alone.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about helping polar bears?
   A. Only scientists can help polar bears.
   B. Ordinary people cannot do anything useful.
   C. Small actions by many people can make a difference.
   D. We need to build new homes for polar bears in cities.

5. What is the writer's main purpose in writing this passage?
   A. To tell an exciting story about polar bears.
   B. To encourage readers to care about the environment and protect polar bears.
   C. To describe how polar bears hunt seals.
   D. To compare polar bears with other Arctic animals.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "The white fur on their bodies helps them hide in the snow and wait for their prey."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明北极熊白色的毛帮助它们在雪中隐藏、等待猎物。A 项"强壮的身体"虽为事实，但与题干"帮助捕猎时隐藏"无关；C 项"洪亮的声音"文中未提及；D 项"快速"也与隐藏无关。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "Scientists have found that polar bears are becoming thinner and weaker because they cannot find enough food."

**【解析】** 原文清楚指出北极熊变瘦变弱是因为找不到足够的食物，而前文已说明海冰融化导致它们无法接近海豹。A 项"不再喜欢吃海豹"属于无中生有；C 项"游泳消遣时间太多"和 D 项"北极熊太多要分享食物"均与原文矛盾。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "When a mother bear cannot get enough food, her cubs may not survive."

**【解析】** 原文直接表述母熊食物不足时，幼崽可能无法存活。A 项"长得更快"与常识及原文逻辑相反；C 项"搬到其他国家"和 D 项"学会独自捕猎"文中均未提及。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Small actions, when done by many people, can make a big difference."

**【解析】** 原文强调许多人的小行动可以产生大影响，可推知普通人也可以为保护北极熊做贡献。A 项"只有科学家能帮"与原文倡导人人参与矛盾；B 项"普通人做不了有用的事"与原文直接相反；D 项"在城市为北极熊建新家"属于无中生有。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第①—③段介绍北极熊困境，第④—⑤段呼吁人们行动、关心地球。

**【解析】** 作者既介绍北极熊的生存挑战，又在文末呼吁人们关爱环境、保护北极熊，B 项"鼓励读者关心环境、保护北极熊"最能概括写作意图。A 项"讲北极熊的精彩故事"以偏概全，文章偏科普与呼吁；C 项"描述北极熊如何捕海豹"仅涉及局部内容；D 项"比较北极熊与其他北极动物"文中未涉及。故选 B。
