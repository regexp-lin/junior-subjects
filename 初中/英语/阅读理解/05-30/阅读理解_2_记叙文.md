# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Zhang Wei was a 15-year-old boy who had never cooked before. His parents always prepared meals for the family. One weekend, when his mother was ill in bed and his father was busy at work, Zhang Wei decided to make dinner for his parents. "I want to do something for them," he thought.

② He opened the fridge and found eggs, tomatoes, and some rice. He remembered his mother often made tomato and egg stir-fry (炒番茄鸡蛋), so he decided to try it. First, he washed the tomatoes and cut them into small pieces. Then he beat the eggs in a bowl. When he turned on the stove (炉灶), his hands were shaking a little because he was nervous.

③ Zhang Wei added oil to the pan and waited for it to get hot. He poured in the eggs first and stirred (搅拌) them quickly. Then he put in the tomatoes. He forgot to add salt at first, so he added it later. The dish did not look as pretty as his mother's, but it smelled good. He also cooked some rice in the rice cooker.

④ When his father came home, he was surprised to see the food on the table. "Did you make this, Wei?" his father asked. Zhang Wei nodded and served his mother a bowl of rice in her room. His mother's eyes were a little red when she tasted the food. "It's delicious," she said softly. "I'm so proud of you."

⑤ That evening, Zhang Wei learned something important. Cooking for his parents was not just about the food — it was a way to show his love. "I will help more at home from now on," he promised himself. His parents smiled and agreed. It was a simple meal, but it filled the whole house with warmth.

**注释：** stir-fry 炒；stove 炉灶；stir 搅拌；serve 端上；delicious 美味的；warmth 温暖

### 选择题

1. Why did Zhang Wei decide to cook dinner?
   A. His teacher asked him to do a cooking project.
   B. His mother was ill and his father was busy, so he wanted to help.
   C. He wanted to win a cooking competition.
   D. There was no food in the fridge except eggs and tomatoes.

2. What ingredients did Zhang Wei use for his dish according to paragraph ②?
   A. Meat, carrots, and rice.
   B. Eggs, tomatoes, and rice.
   C. Fish, vegetables, and noodles.
   D. Potatoes, eggs, and bread.

3. How did Zhang Wei feel when he first turned on the stove?
   A. He felt excited and could not wait to eat.
   B. He felt bored and wanted to give up.
   C. His hands were shaking because he was nervous.
   D. He felt angry because the stove was too hot.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Zhang Wei's mother?
   A. She did not like the food and wanted to throw it away.
   B. She was moved by her son's care and effort.
   C. She was still too ill to eat anything.
   D. She thought the food needed more salt.

5. What did Zhang Wei learn from this experience?
   A. Cooking is very difficult and he should never try again.
   B. He should become a chef when he grows up.
   C. Cooking for his parents was a way to show his love, and he would help more at home.
   D. His mother's cooking is always better than his.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "One weekend, when his mother was ill in bed and his father was busy at work, Zhang Wei decided to make dinner for his parents."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明张伟在妈妈生病卧床、爸爸忙于工作时，决定为父母做晚饭。A 项"老师布置烹饪作业"文中未提及；C 项"想赢得烹饪比赛"属于无中生有；D 项"冰箱里只有鸡蛋和番茄"不是他做饭的原因，只是当时有的食材。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He opened the fridge and found eggs, tomatoes, and some rice... He decided to try it [tomato and egg stir-fry]... He also cooked some rice"

**【解析】** 原文提到张伟用鸡蛋、番茄做了番茄炒蛋，还用米饭煲煮了饭。A 项"肉、胡萝卜、米饭"、C 项"鱼、蔬菜、面条"和 D 项"土豆、鸡蛋、面包"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "When he turned on the stove, his hands were shaking a little because he was nervous."

**【解析】** 原文清楚描述打开炉灶时他手在抖，因为他紧张。A 项"兴奋迫不及待想吃"、B 项"无聊想放弃"和 D 项"生气炉灶太热"均与原文描写不符。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "His mother's eyes were a little red when she tasted the food. 'It's delicious,' she said softly. 'I'm so proud of you.'"

**【解析】** 妈妈眼眶发红、轻声说好吃、表示骄傲，可推断她被儿子的用心和付出感动。A 项"不喜欢想扔掉"与"delicious"和"proud"矛盾；C 项"病得吃不下"与"她品尝了食物"矛盾；D 项"觉得需要更多盐"文中未提及，属过度推断。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Cooking for his parents was not just about the food — it was a way to show his love. 'I will help more at home from now on,' he promised himself."

**【解析】** 张伟领悟到为父母做饭是表达爱的方式，并承诺以后在家多帮忙。A 项"做饭很难再也不试"与文中积极态度矛盾；B 项"长大后当厨师"属于无中生有；D 项"妈妈做的更好"虽可能为真，但非他从经历中学到的主要内容。故选 C。
