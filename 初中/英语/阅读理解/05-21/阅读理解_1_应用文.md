# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **2026 School English Speech Competition**

② Dear students, we are excited to announce our annual English Speech Competition. The event will be held on May 28, 2026 at the school hall. All students in Grades 7 to 9 are welcome to take part. This competition aims to improve your English speaking skills and build your confidence (自信) in public speaking.

③ **Rules and Requirements:**

- **Topic:** "My Dream for the Future" or "An Unforgettable Experience"
- **Time:** Each speech should be 3 to 5 minutes
- **Language:** English only
- **Registration:** Please sign up before May 20 at the Student Office or online

④ The judges will give scores based on content (内容), pronunciation, fluency (流利度) and body language. The top six winners will receive prizes: the first prize is a dictionary and a certificate (证书), and all winners will get a chance to join the city-level competition in June.

⑤ If you want to practice before the competition, you may join our free training sessions every Friday afternoon at 4:30 in Room 301. For more information, please contact Mr. Wang at **wang@ourschool.edu** or call 0755-12345678. We look forward to hearing your voice!

**注释：** confidence 自信；fluency 流利度；certificate 证书

---

### 选择题

1. Who can take part in the English Speech Competition?
   A. Students in Grades 7 to 9.
   B. Only Grade 9 students.
   C. Teachers and parents.
   D. All students in the city.

2. When and where will the competition be held?
   A. On May 20 at the Student Office.
   B. On May 28 at the school hall.
   C. On June 28 at Room 301.
   D. Every Friday at 4:30 PM.

3. According to the notice, what will the first prize winner get?
   A. Money only.
   B. A free training session.
   C. A dictionary and a certificate.
   D. A chance to call Mr. Wang.

4. How can students sign up for the competition?
   A. By sending an email only.
   B. By joining the training sessions.
   C. By calling Mr. Wang on May 28.
   D. At the Student Office or online before May 20.

5. What can we infer from the passage about the training sessions?
   A. They are held to help students prepare for the competition.
   B. They are only for prize winners.
   C. They cost money.
   D. They take place every day.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "All students in Grades 7 to 9 are welcome to take part."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明七至九年级学生均可参加。B 项仅限九年级，范围过窄；C 项教师和家长、D 项全市学生，均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The event will be held on May 28, 2026 at the school hall."

**【解析】** 比赛时间为 5 月 28 日，地点为学校礼堂。A 项 5 月 20 日是截止报名日期，Student Office 是报名地点；C 项 6 月是市赛时间，301 室是培训地点；D 项周五下午是培训时间。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "the first prize is a dictionary and a certificate"

**【解析】** 原文写明一等奖奖品为词典和证书。A 项奖金、B 项免费培训、D 项致电王老师均非一等奖奖品。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Please sign up before May 20 at the Student Office or online"

**【解析】** 报名方式为 5 月 20 日前到学生会或网上报名。A 项仅邮件报名不完整；B 项参加培训不等于报名；C 项 5 月 28 日是比赛日，非报名截止日。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If you want to practice before the competition, you may join our free training sessions every Friday afternoon"

**【解析】** 培训 sessions 旨在帮助学生比赛前练习。B 项"仅限获奖者"与"want to practice"矛盾；C 项"收费"与 "free" 矛盾；D 项"每天举行"应为每周五。故选 A。
