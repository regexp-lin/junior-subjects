> **03-11 第2篇**

# 阅读理解 第2篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **From:** emma.green@email.co.uk
**To:** wanglin2012@email.cn
**Date:** March 8, 2026
**Subject:** Come and visit me this summer!

② Dear Wang Lin,

How are you? I'm so excited to write to you! My parents said you can come and stay with us in London this summer. They would love to meet you. Can you come from July 20 to August 3? That's two weeks!

③ I've already planned some fun things for us to do:

- **Week 1:** We'll visit the British Museum, ride the London Eye, and walk along the River Thames. I'll also take you to Camden Market — it's a famous market with food from all over the world!
- **Week 2:** We'll take a day trip to Oxford to see the beautiful old university. We can also go to a West End musical (a show with singing and dancing). My mum can get us tickets!

④ Here are some things you should know before you come:

- The weather in London can be cool even in summer (about 15–25°C), so bring a light jacket
- You don't need to bring a sleeping bag — we have a guest room ready for you
- British people drive on the left side of the road, so be careful when crossing the street!

⑤ I can't wait to see you! Please write back and let me know if the dates work for you. If you have any questions, just ask!

Your friend,
Emma

---

**注释：** the London Eye 伦敦眼（摩天轮）；the River Thames 泰晤士河；West End musical 伦敦西区音乐剧

---

### 题目

1. How long can Wang Lin stay in London?
   A. One week.
   B. Two weeks.
   C. Three weeks.
   D. One month.

2. What will they do during Week 2?
   A. Visit the British Museum.
   B. Ride the London Eye.
   C. Go to a West End musical.
   D. Walk along the River Thames.

3. Why does Emma suggest Wang Lin bring a light jacket?
   A. Because London is very cold in summer.
   B. Because it rains every day in London.
   C. Because jackets are expensive in London.
   D. Because the weather can be cool even in summer.

4. We can infer from the email that Emma and Wang Lin ________.
   A. are pen pals who haven't met in person yet
   B. are classmates at the same school
   C. have traveled to London together before
   D. don't really know each other well

5. What is the main purpose of this email?
   A. To teach Wang Lin about British culture.
   B. To invite Wang Lin to visit London this summer.
   C. To ask Wang Lin to help with a school project.
   D. To tell Wang Lin about London's weather.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后两句 "Can you come from July 20 to August 3? That's two weeks!"

**【解析】** 原文明确说明 Emma 邀请 Wang Lin 从7月20日到8月3日来伦敦，并注明这是两周时间。B 项"Two weeks"与原文完全一致。A 项"一周"与原文"two weeks"不符；C 项"三周"和 D 项"一个月"均超出原文时间范围。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 Week 2 部分 "We can also go to a West End musical (a show with singing and dancing)."

**【解析】** 根据 Emma 的计划，第二周的活动包括去牛津一日游和看西区音乐剧。C 项"Go to a West End musical"是第二周的安排。A 项"参观大英博物馆"、B 项"乘坐伦敦眼"和 D 项"沿泰晤士河散步"都是 Week 1 的活动安排，属于张冠李戴。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1条 "The weather in London can be cool even in summer (about 15–25°C), so bring a light jacket"

**【解析】** 原文说明伦敦即使在夏天天气也可能凉爽（约15–25°C），因此建议带一件薄外套。D 项"因为即使在夏天天气也可能凉爽"与原文完全吻合。A 项"因为伦敦夏天非常冷"程度过重——原文用的是"cool"（凉爽）而非"very cold"（非常冷），且15–25°C不算很冷；B 项"因为伦敦每天都下雨"原文未提及降雨问题；C 项"因为外套在伦敦很贵"属于无中生有。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "They would love to meet you." 及第⑤段 "I can't wait to see you!"

**【解析】** Emma 的父母说"很想见到你"（would love to meet you），暗示他们之前没有见过 Wang Lin。Emma 说"迫不及待想见到你"（can't wait to see you），也说明两人尚未见面。此外，Emma 详细介绍伦敦的景点和注意事项（如英国靠左行驶），这些都是向一个从未来过伦敦的人介绍的信息。两人通过邮件交流，可以推断她们是尚未见过面的笔友。A 项"是还没有见过面的笔友"是合理推断。B 项"是同一所学校的同学"——一人在英国、一人在中国，不可能是同校同学；C 项"之前一起去过伦敦"与 Emma 详细介绍伦敦的行为矛盾；D 项"彼此不太了解"——从邮件的热情语气和详细的行程安排来看，两人关系亲密，应该很了解彼此。故选 A。

**【技巧点拨】** 推理判断题需要综合多处细节进行推断。本题的关键线索包括：邮件往来形式（笔友）、"would love to meet you"（未见过面）、以及详细介绍伦敦注意事项（Wang Lin 未去过伦敦）。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 邮件主题 "Come and visit me this summer!" 及第②段 "My parents said you can come and stay with us in London this summer."

**【解析】** 从邮件主题"今年夏天来看我吧！"就可以看出这封邮件的主要目的是邀请 Wang Lin 暑假来伦敦做客。邮件内容围绕这一目的展开：告知时间安排、列出活动计划、提供行前须知。B 项"邀请 Wang Lin 今年夏天来伦敦"最准确地概括了邮件的核心目的。A 项"教 Wang Lin 英国文化"——虽然邮件涉及一些文化信息（如靠左行驶），但这只是附带说明，不是邮件的主要目的；C 项"请 Wang Lin 帮忙做学校项目"原文完全未提及；D 项"告诉 Wang Lin 伦敦的天气"以偏概全——天气只是行前须知中的一条信息。故选 B。

**【技巧点拨】** 应用文的写作目的通常可以从标题（Subject）或开头段落中快速判断。本题中邮件主题"Come and visit me this summer!"直接点明了写作目的。
