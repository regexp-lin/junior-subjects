# 阅读理解（说明文）

**Wind Power: A Clean Energy for the Future**

① The wind has been used by humans for thousands of years. Long ago, people used wind to sail ships and turn windmills to grind grain. Today, we use wind in a new way — to make electricity. Wind power is one of the most important renewable energy sources in the world.

② How does wind power work? Large structures called wind turbines are built in windy places. Each turbine has three large blades. When the wind blows, it turns the blades. The blades are connected to a generator inside the turbine. As the blades spin, the generator produces electricity. The stronger the wind, the more electricity is made. This electricity is then sent through power lines to our homes, schools, and factories.

③ Wind power has many advantages. First, it is clean. It does not produce smoke or harmful gases like coal or gas power plants. Second, wind is renewable. We will never run out of wind. The sun heats the air, the air moves, and we get wind. As long as the sun shines, we will have wind. Third, once a wind farm is built, the "fuel" — the wind — is free. We do not need to dig for coal or buy oil from other countries.

④ However, wind power also has some limits. Wind does not blow all the time. On calm days, turbines produce little or no electricity. We need to store the energy or use other power sources when there is no wind. Also, wind farms need a lot of space. Some people worry that turbines can harm birds or create noise. Scientists are working to make turbines safer and quieter.

⑤ Despite these challenges, wind power is growing quickly around the world. Many countries are building more wind farms to reduce pollution and fight climate change. Wind power is a key part of our clean energy future.

**注释：** turbine 涡轮机；renewable 可再生的

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ①, how did people use wind long ago?
   A. To make electricity.
   B. To sail ships and turn windmills to grind grain.
   C. To cool their homes.
   D. To fly kites only.

2. What happens when the wind blows the blades of a wind turbine?
   A. The turbine stops.
   B. The blades spin and the generator produces electricity.
   C. The turbine catches fire.
   D. Nothing.

3. Which is NOT an advantage of wind power mentioned in Paragraph ③?
   A. It is clean and does not produce harmful gases.
   B. Wind is renewable and we will not run out of it.
   C. The wind is free once the wind farm is built.
   D. Wind turbines work all the time with no breaks.

4. What is a limit of wind power according to Paragraph ④?
   A. Wind is too expensive.
   B. Wind does not blow all the time, so we need backup when there is no wind.
   C. Wind power is too fast.
   D. No one wants to use wind power.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Wind power is being given up by all countries.
   B. Wind power is growing and will play an important role in clean energy.
   C. Wind power has no future.
   D. Coal is better than wind power.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "Long ago, people used wind to sail ships and turn windmills to grind grain."
**【解析】** 古人用风力航行、驱动风车磨谷物。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "When the wind blows, it turns the blades... The blades are connected to a generator... As the blades spin, the generator produces electricity."
**【解析】** 风吹动叶片旋转，带动发电机发电。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 优点：clean, renewable, wind is free
**【解析】** A、B、C均为文中提到的优点。D项"风机无休止运转"与第④段"Wind does not blow all the time"矛盾，非优点。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "Wind does not blow all the time. On calm days, turbines produce little or no electricity. We need to store the energy or use other power sources when there is no wind."
**【解析】** 风不稳定，无风时发电少，需要储能或备用电源。B项正确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "wind power is growing quickly around the world... Wind power is a key part of our clean energy future."
**【解析】** 风电在全球快速发展，是清洁能源的重要组成部分，可推知B项。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
