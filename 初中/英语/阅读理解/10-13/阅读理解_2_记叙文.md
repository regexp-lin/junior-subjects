> **10-13 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

**Helping an Old Lady at the Supermarket**

① Last Saturday afternoon, I went to the supermarket with my mother to buy some food for the week. The supermarket was crowded. As we walked past the vegetable section, I noticed an old lady standing by the tomatoes. She had a full shopping cart and two heavy bags in her hands. She looked tired and a little worried.

② "Can I help you?" I asked. The lady smiled and said, "Young man, would you mind helping me carry these bags to the checkout? My back hurts and I cannot hold them for long." I quickly said yes. I took the two bags from her and walked with her towards the checkout counter. She moved slowly, so I slowed down to match her speed.

③ On the way, she told me she lived alone. Her children worked in other cities and only came back during holidays. She did her shopping by herself every week. "It is not easy," she said, "but I like to stay active." I felt a little sad when I heard that. I thought of my own grandmother and how I should visit her more often.

④ When we reached the checkout, I helped her put the bags on the conveyor belt. After she paid, I carried her bags to the exit and helped her into a taxi. "Thank you so much," she said. "You are a kind boy. Your parents must be proud of you." Her words made me feel warm inside.

⑤ That day, I did not do anything great. I just helped an old lady with her bags. But sometimes small acts of kindness can mean a lot. I learned that we can all make a difference by simply being helpful when we see someone in need.

**注释：** conveyor belt 传送带；checkout 结账处

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did the story happen?
   A. In a library.
   B. At a supermarket.
   C. In a school.
   D. At home.

2. Why did the old lady need help?
   A. Because she lost her money.
   B. Because her back hurt and she could not hold the bags for long.
   C. Because she did not know the way.
   D. Because she was in a hurry.

3. What did the writer learn about the old lady?
   A. She had many children living with her.
   B. She lived alone and did shopping by herself every week.
   C. She never went to the supermarket.
   D. She was a supermarket worker.

4. What did the old lady say that made the writer feel warm?
   A. She asked for more help.
   B. She said "You are a kind boy. Your parents must be proud of you."
   C. She gave the writer some money.
   D. She complained about the supermarket.

5. What is the main lesson the writer learned from this experience?
   A. Supermarkets are always crowded.
   B. Old people should not shop alone.
   C. Small acts of kindness can make a difference when we help others in need.
   D. Carrying bags is good exercise.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "I went to the supermarket with my mother... As we walked past the vegetable section"
**【解析】** 故事发生在超市。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "My back hurts and I cannot hold them for long."
**【解析】** 老奶奶因背痛无法长时间提重物而求助。B项正确。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "she told me she lived alone... She did her shopping by herself every week"
**【解析】** 老奶奶独自居住，每周自己购物。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "You are a kind boy. Your parents must be proud of you.' Her words made me feel warm inside."
**【解析】** 老奶奶的称赞让作者内心感到温暖。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Sometimes small acts of kindness can mean a lot. I learned that we can all make a difference by simply being helpful when we see someone in need."
**【解析】** 作者体会到微小的善意也很有意义，在他人需要时伸出援手就能带来改变。C项正确。A、B、D项偏离主旨。故选C。
