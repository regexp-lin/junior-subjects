# 阅读理解（说明文）

**Coral Reef Ecosystems**

① Coral reefs are often called the "rainforests of the sea". They are home to thousands of kinds of fish, plants, and other sea life. Coral reefs form in warm, shallow waters. They are made by tiny animals called coral polyps. These small creatures build hard skeletons over many years. Millions of polyps together create the beautiful reefs we see.

② Coral reefs are important for many reasons. First, they provide food and shelter for about one quarter of all ocean species. Fish, crabs, and sea turtles depend on reefs. Second, reefs protect coastlines from storms. They act like a wall that reduces the force of waves. Third, reefs attract tourists. Diving and snorkelling bring money to coastal communities.

③ However, coral reefs are in danger. Climate change is warming the ocean. When water gets too warm, corals turn white. This is called "coral bleaching". Bleached corals are weak and may die. Pollution also harms reefs. Rubbish and chemicals from farms flow into the sea. Overfishing removes too many fish, which upsets the balance of the reef.

④ What can we do? We can reduce our use of plastic to cut pollution. We can save energy to slow climate change. When we visit the sea, we should not touch or step on coral. We can also support groups that work to protect reefs.

⑤ Coral reefs took thousands of years to form. They cannot be replaced easily. If we act now, we can help save these wonderful underwater worlds for future generations.

**注释：** polyp 珊瑚虫；bleaching 白化；skeleton 骨骼

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第①段 "They are made by tiny animals called coral polyps"
**【解析】** 珊瑚礁由名为珊瑚虫的微小动物建造。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "reefs protect coastlines from storms. They act like a wall that reduces the force of waves"
**【解析】** 礁体像墙壁一样削弱风暴时海浪的冲击，保护海岸。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "When water gets too warm, corals turn white. This is called 'coral bleaching'. Bleached corals are weak and may die"
**【解析】** 海水过热会导致珊瑚白化，白化后珊瑚变弱甚至死亡。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "reduce our use of plastic... save energy to slow climate change"
**【解析】** 文中建议减少塑料使用、节约能源以减缓气候变化。C项正确。A、B、D项与建议相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Coral reefs took thousands of years to form. They cannot be replaced easily. If we act now, we can help save these wonderful underwater worlds"
**【解析】** 珊瑚礁形成漫长、难以替代，现在行动可帮助为后代保存它们。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。
