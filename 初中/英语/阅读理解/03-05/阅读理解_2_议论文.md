> **03-05 第2篇**

# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① These days, many parents do everything for their children at home. They cook, clean, wash clothes and even pack school bags for them. Some parents think children should spend all their time on studying. But is this really good for children? I don't think so.

② In my opinion, students should learn to do housework. First of all, doing housework teaches children important life skills. If a student goes to college one day, he or she will need to cook, clean and take care of himself or herself. Students who never do housework at home may find it very hard to live on their own.

③ Second, doing housework helps children understand how hard their parents work every day. When a child spends an hour cleaning the house, he or she will realize that keeping a home tidy is not easy. This can help children feel thankful for what their parents do for them.

④ Third, research shows that children who do housework regularly do better in school. This may sound surprising, but it makes sense. Housework teaches children to manage their time, be responsible, and finish tasks carefully — all of which are useful for studying, too.

⑤ Of course, students shouldn't spend too much time on housework. About 30 minutes a day is enough. The key is to find a good balance between studying and doing housework. As an old saying goes, "You can't grow up without learning to take care of yourself."

---

**注释：** regularly 有规律地；manage 管理；responsible 有责任感的

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ①, what do some parents think children should do?
   A. Do housework every day.
   B. Spend all their time on studying.
   C. Learn to cook at home.
   D. Play sports after school.

2. Why does the writer think doing housework is important? (Choose the reason mentioned in Paragraph ②.)
   A. It can help children make more money.
   B. It is more important than studying.
   C. It teaches children important life skills.
   D. It makes parents feel happy.

3. The underlined word "balance" in Paragraph ⑤ probably means ________.
   A. a competition between two sides
   B. a big difference between two things
   C. a problem that is hard to solve
   D. a situation where two things are given equal attention

4. According to the passage, which of the following is TRUE?
   A. Doing housework can help children become more responsible.
   B. Children who do housework usually do worse in school.
   C. Most parents want their children to do more housework.
   D. The writer suggests students should do housework for about 2 hours a day.

5. What is the writer's main purpose of writing this passage?
   A. To describe how parents do housework at home.
   B. To compare different ways of doing housework.
   C. To explain why students should learn to do housework.
   D. To ask parents to stop helping their children completely.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "Some parents think children should spend all their time on studying."

**【解析】** 原文明确指出一些家长认为孩子应该把所有的时间都花在学习上。B 项与原文完全对应。A 项"每天做家务"与这些家长的观点相反——他们恰恰不让孩子做家务；C 项"在家学做饭"原文未提及这些家长支持此做法；D 项"放学后做运动"属于无中生有。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "First of all, doing housework teaches children important life skills."

**【解析】** 第②段提出的第一个理由是做家务能教会孩子重要的生活技能，并以上大学后需要自己照顾自己为例进行了说明。C 项准确概括了这一理由。A 项"帮助孩子赚更多的钱"原文未提及经济方面的好处；B 项"比学习更重要"属于过度推断，原文强调的是做家务"也"很重要，而非"比学习更重要"；D 项"让父母感到开心"虽然可能是做家务的一个附带效果，但并非第②段提出的理由。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段第3句 "The key is to find a good balance between studying and doing housework."

**【解析】** 根据上下文语境，作者在第⑤段表达的意思是：学生不应该花太多时间做家务，每天大约30分钟就够了。关键是在学习和做家务之间找到一个好的"balance"。结合"between studying and doing housework"可知，这里强调的是两者之间合理分配、同等重视。D 项"一种两件事被给予同等关注的状况"最符合"平衡"的含义。A 项"两方之间的竞争"不符合语境，学习和做家务不是竞争关系；B 项"两件事之间的巨大差异"与原文语意相反；C 项"一个难以解决的问题"也不符合上下文。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 词义猜测题要重点关注目标词所在句子的上下文线索。本题中 "between studying and doing housework" 提示了两者并列的关系，帮助推断"balance"的含义。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段第3句 "Housework teaches children to manage their time, be responsible, and finish tasks carefully..."

**【解析】** 原文第④段明确提到做家务能教会孩子管理时间、变得有责任感（be responsible）和认真完成任务。由此可以推断做家务确实能帮助孩子变得更有责任感。A 项是对原文的合理推断。B 项"做家务的孩子在学校成绩通常更差"与原文"do better in school"直接矛盾；C 项"大多数家长希望孩子做更多家务"与第①段内容矛盾，原文说许多家长为孩子包办一切；D 项"作者建议学生每天做约2小时家务"与原文"About 30 minutes a day is enough"不符。故选 A。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文结构：第①段提出问题（家长为孩子包办家务是否合理）→ 第②③④段从三个角度论证学生应该学做家务 → 第⑤段总结并提出建议。

**【解析】** 纵观全文，作者的核心观点是学生应该学做家务，并从生活技能、理解父母、促进学习三个方面进行了论证。因此写作目的是解释为什么学生应该学做家务。C 项最准确地概括了写作意图。A 项"描述父母如何做家务"与文章主旨不符，文章不是在描述父母做家务的方式；B 项"比较做家务的不同方法"文中未进行此类比较；D 项"要求父母完全停止帮助孩子"属于过度推断——作者只是建议让孩子适当做家务，并未要求父母"完全停止"帮助。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 议论文的写作意图通常与作者的核心论点一致。可通过分析文章首尾段的观点句来快速锁定写作目的。注意区分"作者的核心观点"和"某一段的具体细节"。
