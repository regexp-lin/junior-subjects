# 阅读理解 第2篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **Sunshine Middle School — Annual Sports Day**

**Date:** Friday, April 18, 2026
**Time:** 8:30 am – 4:00 pm
**Place:** School Sports Field

Dear students and parents,

We are pleased to invite you to our school's Annual Sports Day! Come and enjoy a day of fun, competition, and teamwork.

② **Schedule:**

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| 8:30 – 9:00 am | Opening Ceremony |
| 9:00 – 10:30 am | Track Events (100m, 200m, 400m, relay) |
| 10:30 – 11:00 am | Break & Snack Time |
| 11:00 am – 12:00 pm | Field Events (long jump, high jump, shot put) |
| 12:00 – 1:00 pm | Lunch Break |
| 1:00 – 2:30 pm | Fun Games (three-legged race, tug of war, sack race) |
| 2:30 – 3:30 pm | Basketball & Football Matches (Grade 7 vs Grade 8) |
| 3:30 – 4:00 pm | Awards Ceremony & Closing |

③ **Important Information:**
- All students must wear their PE uniforms and sports shoes
- Parents are welcome to watch from the stands
- Water and snacks will be sold near the entrance
- Please bring sunscreen and a hat — it may be sunny!
- No classes on Sports Day — regular classes resume on Monday

④ **Special Awards:**
- **Best Athlete:** One boy and one girl with the most points
- **Best Team Spirit:** The class that shows the best teamwork and sportsmanship
- **Most Improved:** A student who tries hard and shows great progress

⑤ Let's make this the best Sports Day ever! If you have any questions, please ask your PE teacher or visit the school office.

— Sunshine Middle School Sports Committee

---

**注释：** relay 接力赛；shot put 铅球；tug of war 拔河；sack race 麻袋赛跑；sportsmanship 体育精神

---

### 题目

1. When will the Opening Ceremony start?
   A. At 8:00 am.
   B. At 8:30 am.
   C. At 9:00 am.
   D. At 9:30 am.

2. What events will take place from 1:00 to 2:30 pm?
   A. Track Events like 100m and 200m.
   B. Field Events like long jump and high jump.
   C. Basketball and Football Matches.
   D. Fun Games like three-legged race and tug of war.

3. What must all students wear on Sports Day?
   A. School uniforms and leather shoes.
   B. PE uniforms and sports shoes.
   C. Any comfortable clothes they like.
   D. White T-shirts and black trousers.

4. We can infer from the notice that ________.
   A. only students can watch the sports events
   B. students will have regular classes in the morning
   C. the weather might be sunny on Sports Day
   D. all students must join at least one sport

5. Where would you most probably see this notice?
   A. On the school notice board or website.
   B. In a cooking magazine.
   C. In a travel guidebook.
   D. In a math textbook.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段日程表第1行："8:30 – 9:00 am | Opening Ceremony"

**【解析】** 根据日程表，开幕式（Opening Ceremony）的时间为上午8:30至9:00。B 项"At 8:30 am"与原文完全一致。A 项"8:00 am"早于日程中的任何活动；C 项"9:00 am"是 Track Events 的开始时间，属于张冠李戴；D 项"9:30 am"不在日程表中。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段日程表中 1:00 – 2:30 pm 一行："Fun Games (three-legged race, tug of war, sack race)"

**【解析】** 根据日程表，下午1:00至2:30的活动是趣味运动（Fun Games），包括三人两足赛、拔河和麻袋赛跑。D 项与原文完全对应。A 项"Track Events"是上午9:00–10:30的活动；B 项"Field Events"是上午11:00–12:00的活动；C 项"Basketball and Football Matches"是下午2:30–3:30的活动。这三个选项都属于张冠李戴。故选 D。

---

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1条 "All students must wear their PE uniforms and sports shoes"

**【解析】** 原文明确要求所有学生必须穿体育校服和运动鞋。B 项与原文完全一致。A 项"校服和皮鞋"——通知要求的是体育校服（PE uniforms）而非日常校服，且皮鞋（leather shoes）不适合运动；C 项"任何舒适的衣服"与"must wear"的强制要求矛盾；D 项"白色T恤和黑色裤子"原文未指定具体颜色和款式。故选 B。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段第4条 "Please bring sunscreen and a hat — it may be sunny!"

**【解析】** 原文建议学生带防晒霜和帽子，因为"天气可能是晴天"。由此可以推断运动会当天天气可能是晴朗的。C 项"运动会当天天气可能是晴天"是合理推断。A 项"只有学生可以观看比赛"与原文"Parents are welcome to watch from the stands"直接矛盾——家长也可以观看；B 项"学生上午会有正常课程"与原文"No classes on Sports Day"矛盾；D 项"所有学生必须参加至少一项运动"原文未做此要求，属于无中生有。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 推理判断题注意从"建议"推断"原因"。通知建议带防晒霜和帽子→说明可能是晴天。这类因果推断在应用文中非常常见。

---

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文结构：校名标题 → 日期/时间/地点 → 活动日程表 → 注意事项 → 特别奖项 → 联系方式，末尾署名"Sunshine Middle School Sports Committee"。

**【解析】** 本文是一则学校运动会通知，包含时间安排、活动日程、着装要求、奖项设置等信息，发布方为学校体育委员会。这类校内活动通知最可能出现在学校公告栏或学校网站上。A 项"On the school notice board or website"最合理。B 项"烹饪杂志"与运动会主题完全无关；C 项"旅游指南"不会刊登校内活动通知；D 项"数学课本"内容以数学知识为主，与运动会通知无关。故选 A。

**【技巧点拨】** 判断应用文出处时，关注发布主体（署名）和文体特征（通知/广告/邮件等）。校内通知→校园公告栏/网站；社区广告→社区公告栏；产品宣传→商业网站/杂志。
