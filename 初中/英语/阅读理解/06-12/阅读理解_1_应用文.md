> **06-12 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Starlight Cinema — June Movie Guide**

② Welcome to Starlight Cinema! This month we have three new films for you. **Ocean Dreams** is a family cartoon about a brave fish. It will show every day at 2:00 pm and 4:30 pm. **City Lights** is a love story. It shows at 6:00 pm and 8:30 pm from Monday to Friday. **Space Hunter** is our action film. It shows at 7:00 pm and 9:30 pm on weekends.

③ **Ticket prices:** Adults (adult 成人): ¥45. Students with a student card: ¥35. Children under 1.2 meters: free. A family ticket (2 adults + 2 children) costs ¥100 and saves you ¥20.

④ **How to buy tickets:** You can buy tickets at our box office (售票处), on our website www.starlight-cinema.com, or through our WeChat app. Please arrive at least 15 minutes before the film starts. If you are late, we may not let you in until the next break.

⑤ **Important rules:** No food or drinks inside the cinema hall. Please turn off your mobile phone. Students can get a free small popcorn (爆米花) when they show their student card on Saturdays.

**注释：** box office 售票处；popcorn 爆米花

---

### 选择题

1. When can you watch *Space Hunter*?
   A. On weekends at 7:00 pm and 9:30 pm.
   B. Every day at 7:00 pm.
   C. From Monday to Friday at 7:00 pm.
   D. Only on Saturday evening.

2. How much does a student need to pay for one ticket?
   A. ¥45.
   B. ¥35.
   C. ¥20.
   D. Free.

3. According to the passage, how can you buy tickets?
   A. Only at the cinema.
   B. By calling the cinema.
   C. At the box office, on the website, or through WeChat.
   D. Only by WeChat.

4. What can students get for free on Saturdays?
   A. A family ticket.
   B. A free film ticket.
   C. A drink.
   D. A small popcorn when they show their student card.

5. What can we learn from the passage?
   A. You must arrive at least 15 minutes before the film or you may not enter on time.
   B. You can bring your own food into the cinema.
   C. *Ocean Dreams* is an action film.
   D. Children over 1.2 meters pay the same as adults.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "**Space Hunter** is our action film. It shows at 7:00 pm and 9:30 pm on weekends."

**【解析】** 文章明确说明 *Space Hunter* 在周末 7:00 pm 和 9:30 pm 放映。A 项正确。B 项 "每天" 错误，应为周末；C 项 周一至周五错误；D 项 "仅周六晚上" 不完整，周末含周六、周日。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Students with a student card: ¥35."

**【解析】** 持学生证的学生票价为 ¥35。B 项正确。A 项 ¥45 为成人价；C 项 ¥20 为家庭票优惠金额；D 项免费针对 1.2 米以下儿童。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "You can buy tickets at our box office, on our website www.starlight-cinema.com, or through our WeChat app."

**【解析】** 购票方式有三种：售票处、官网、微信。C 项正确。A 项 "仅电影院" 过于片面；B 项电话购票文中未提及；D 项 "仅微信" 不完整。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Students can get a free small popcorn when they show their student card on Saturdays."

**【解析】** 周六持学生证可免费领取小爆米花。D 项正确。A 项家庭票需付费；B 项免费电影票未提及；C 项饮料未提及。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Please arrive at least 15 minutes before the film starts. If you are late, we may not let you in until the next break."

**【解析】** 需至少提前 15 分钟到达，迟到者可能需等到下次休息才能入场，即可能无法按时入场。A 项正确。B 项与 "No food or drinks" 矛盾；C 项 *Ocean Dreams* 为家庭卡通；D 项身高超 1.2 米儿童未说明与成人同价。故选 A。
