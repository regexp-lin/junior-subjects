# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Is homework good or bad for students? This question has been discussed for many years. Some people say homework helps students learn. Others believe it brings too much pressure. Let us look at both sides.

② **Why homework may be helpful:** Homework gives students a chance to practice what they have learned in class. When they do exercises at home, they can find their weak points and improve. Many teachers say that students who do homework carefully often get better grades. Homework also teaches students to manage their time and work independently. These skills are useful not only in school but also in their future lives.

③ **Why homework may be harmful:** Too much homework can make students feel tired and stressed. Some students spend three or four hours on homework every night. They have little time to rest, play sports, or spend time with family. When students are too tired, they cannot concentrate well in class the next day. Some studies even show that too much homework does not always lead to better results. Sometimes, less homework can help students learn more effectively.

④ **What researchers suggest:** Experts say the amount of homework should match the age of students. For example, Grade 7 students might do about 70 minutes of homework each day. The homework should be meaningful — not just repeating the same exercises. It is also important for parents and teachers to talk and make sure students are not overloaded.

⑤ In the end, homework itself is neither all good nor all bad. The key is to find the right amount and the right kind. Good homework helps students learn; too much homework hurts them. Schools and families should work together to make homework useful instead of a burden.

**注释：** concentrate 集中注意力；overloaded 负担过重的；burden 负担

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what is one benefit of homework?
   A. It helps students practice, find weak points, and improve.
   B. It makes students stay at school longer.
   C. It helps students make more friends.
   D. It lets students watch more TV.

2. Why might too much homework be harmful?
   A. Because students enjoy it too much.
   B. Because teachers do not like to correct it.
   C. Because it can make students tired, stressed, and unable to concentrate the next day.
   D. Because it is too easy.

3. What do experts suggest about the amount of homework?
   A. All students should have the same amount.
   B. Students should have no homework at all.
   C. It should match the age of students and be meaningful.
   D. Homework should take at least four hours each night.

4. The underlined word "burden" in paragraph ⑤ probably means ______.
   A. something helpful
   B. something interesting
   C. something free
   D. something heavy or difficult to carry

5. What is the writer's main idea?
   A. The right amount and kind of homework helps; too much hurts.
   B. Homework is completely bad and should be stopped.
   C. Homework is always good and students should do more.
   D. Only parents should decide how much homework students have.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Homework gives students a chance to practice what they have learned in class. When they do exercises at home, they can find their weak points and improve."

**【解析】** 作业的好处包括：练习所学、发现薄弱点、提高。A 项正确。B 项 "在学校待更久" 未提及；C 项 "交更多朋友" 未提及；D 项 "看更多电视" 与作业无关。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Too much homework can make students feel tired and stressed... When students are too tired, they cannot concentrate well in class the next day."

**【解析】** 作业过多会导致疲劳、压力，且第二天难以集中注意力。C 项正确。A 项 "学生太喜欢" 与 harmful 矛盾；B 项教师改作业未提及；D 项 "太简单" 未提及。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Experts say the amount of homework should match the age of students... The homework should be meaningful."

**【解析】** 专家建议作业量应与学生年龄相符，且应具有意义。C 项正确。A 项 "所有学生相同" 与 match the age 矛盾；B 项 "完全不要作业" 未提及；D 项 "至少四小时" 与建议相悖。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "make homework useful instead of a burden"

**【解析】** 文中 burden 与 useful 形成对比，意为 "负担、重压"。D 项 something heavy or difficult to carry 符合。A 项 "有帮助的" 与 instead of 矛盾；B 项 "有趣的" 不准确；C 项 "免费的" 无关。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "homework itself is neither all good nor all bad. The key is to find the right amount and the right kind. Good homework helps students learn; too much homework hurts them."

**【解析】** 作者认为作业本身有好有坏，关键是适量和高质量；适量有益，过量有害。A 项准确概括。B 项 "完全不好应取消" 过于极端；C 项 "越多越好" 与作者观点矛盾；D 项 "仅家长决定" 文中强调学校与家庭共同协作。故选 A。
