# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## A Helping Hand at Break Time

① It was a sunny Tuesday afternoon. The bell rang and students rushed out of the classroom for the ten-minute break. Li Ming walked to the bicycle area near the school gate. He was about to ride home when he heard someone call his name. He turned around and saw Zhang Wei, a classmate from Class 3, standing beside his bicycle with a worried look on his face.

② "What's wrong?" Li Ming asked. Zhang Wei pointed at his bicycle. "The chain (链条) has come off," he said sadly. "I don't know how to fix it. My father usually does it at home." Zhang Wei lived far from school and had to ride for twenty minutes every day. Without his bicycle, he would have to walk for more than an hour.

③ Li Ming had learned how to fix a bicycle from his grandfather during the summer holiday. "Don't worry," he said. "I can help you." He put down his schoolbag and knelt beside the bicycle. His hands soon became dirty with oil, but he did not care. He carefully put the chain back onto the gear (齿轮) and turned the pedals to check. After a few minutes, the bicycle was working again.

④ Zhang Wei could not believe it. "Thank you so much, Li Ming! You're really kind!" he said with a big smile. Li Ming smiled back. "It was nothing. We're classmates. We should help each other." The two boys rode home together that afternoon. Since then, they have become good friends.

⑤ Sometimes a small act of kindness during a short break can change a lot. Li Ming did not think he had done anything special, but to Zhang Wei, that help meant a lot. Friendship often grows from such simple moments.

**注释：** chain 链条；gear 齿轮

---

### 选择题

1. When did the story happen?
   A. On a sunny Tuesday afternoon, during the break.
   B. On a rainy Monday morning.
   C. During class time.
   D. In the evening after school.

2. What was wrong with Zhang Wei's bicycle?
   A. The tyre was flat.
   B. The brake did not work.
   C. The chain had come off.
   D. The lock was broken.

3. Where did Li Ming learn to fix a bicycle?
   A. From his father at home.
   B. From his grandfather during the summer holiday.
   C. From a repair shop.
   D. From his teacher at school.

4. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. Zhang Wei knew how to fix bicycles very well.
   B. Li Ming and Zhang Wei were already good friends before this happened.
   C. Zhang Wei appreciated Li Ming's help and they became friends because of it.
   D. Li Ming thought fixing the bicycle was very difficult.

5. What does the writer want to tell us through this story?
   A. Students should always bring tools to school.
   B. A small act of kindness can build friendship and mean a lot to others.
   C. Bicycles often break down at school.
   D. Break time is too short to do anything useful.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "It was a sunny Tuesday afternoon. The bell rang and students rushed out of the classroom for the ten-minute break"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明故事发生在周二下午、课间休息时。A项正确。B项雨天、周一上午错误；C项上课时间错误；D项放学后与"break"不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The chain has come off"

**【解析】** 张伟的自行车链条掉了。C项正确。A项轮胎瘪、B项刹车坏、D项锁坏均未提及。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Li Ming had learned how to fix a bicycle from his grandfather during the summer holiday"

**【解析】** 李明是暑假期间向爷爷学会修自行车的。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Thank you so much... Since then, they have become good friends" 及第⑤段 "that help meant a lot... Friendship often grows from such simple moments"

**【解析】** 张伟十分感激，二人因此成为好友；末段点明小善举能促成友谊、对他人意义重大。可推知C项正确。A项张伟不擅长修车；B项二人此前非好友；D项李明未觉困难。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第⑤段 "Sometimes a small act of kindness during a short break can change a lot... Friendship often grows from such simple moments"

**【解析】** 文章通过课间修车的小事，传达"小善举能建立友谊、对他人意义重大"的主旨。B项正确。A、C、D项均非作者意图。故选B。
