# 阅读理解（说明文）

## The History of Human Space Exploration

① Human beings have dreamed of reaching the stars for thousands of years. But it was not until the 20th century that we truly began to explore space. The space age started on October 4, 1957, when the Soviet Union (苏联) launched Sputnik 1, the first artificial satellite, into space. It orbited (环绕) the Earth for about three months and changed the world forever.

② In 1961, Soviet astronaut Yuri Gagarin became the first person to travel into space. His flight lasted only 108 minutes, but it was a huge step for all humankind. Just eight years later, on July 20, 1969, American astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin landed on the Moon. When Armstrong stepped onto the Moon's surface, he said the famous words: "That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind."

③ Since then, many countries have sent spacecraft and astronauts into space. Space stations were built so that scientists could live and work in space for a long time. The International Space Station (ISS), built by many nations including the USA, Russia, China and European countries, has been orbiting Earth since 1998. Astronauts from different countries work together there to do scientific experiments.

④ In recent years, China has made great progress in space exploration. In 2003, Yang Liwei became China's first astronaut. China has also sent probes (探测器) to the Moon and Mars. In 2021, China's space station began to take shape. These achievements show that space exploration is no longer just a dream—it is becoming a reality for more and more countries.

⑤ Looking to the future, scientists hope to send humans to Mars and even deeper into space. The history of space exploration is a story of courage, technology and the human spirit. It reminds us that when we work together, we can achieve amazing things.

**注释：** Soviet Union 苏联；orbit 环绕（运行）；probe 探测器

### 选择题

1. When did the space age begin?
   A. On October 4, 1957, when Sputnik 1 was launched.
   B. In 1961 when the first person went into space.
   C. In 1969 when humans landed on the Moon.
   D. In 1998 when the ISS was built.

2. Who was the first person to travel into space?
   A. Neil Armstrong.
   B. Buzz Aldrin.
   C. Yuri Gagarin.
   D. Yang Liwei.

3. According to the passage, what is the International Space Station used for?
   A. Only for tourism.
   B. To send satellites into space.
   C. For astronauts from one country to rest.
   D. For scientists to live and do experiments in space.

4. What can we infer from Paragraph 4?
   A. China has never sent astronauts into space.
   B. China joined space exploration later but has achieved a lot.
   C. Only the USA and Russia can build space stations.
   D. China was the first country to land on the Moon.

5. What does the writer mainly want to tell us?
   A. The history of space exploration shows human courage and the power of working together.
   B. Space travel is very expensive.
   C. Only a few countries can explore space.
   D. The Moon is the only place humans have visited in space.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "The space age started on October 4, 1957, when the Soviet Union launched Sputnik 1"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明太空时代始于1957年10月4日苏联发射斯普特尼克1号。A项正确。B项1961年是首次载人航天；C项1969年是登月；D项1998年是国际空间站建成。故选A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "In 1961, Soviet astronaut Yuri Gagarin became the first person to travel into space"

**【解析】** 加加林是首位进入太空的人。C项正确。A、B项为美国登月宇航员；D项为中国首位航天员。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Space stations were built so that scientists could live and work in space for a long time... Astronauts from different countries work together there to do scientific experiments"

**【解析】** 空间站供科学家长期居住并在太空开展实验。D项正确。A项旅游业、B项发射卫星、C项单一国家休息均与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "In recent years, China has made great progress in space exploration... 2003... first astronaut... probes to the Moon and Mars... 2021... space station"

**【解析】** 中国2003年才有首位航天员，起步较晚，但近年取得月球探测、火星探测、空间站等重要成就，可推知中国虽后发但成就显著。B项正确。A、C、D项均与原文矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第⑤段 "The history of space exploration is a story of courage, technology and the human spirit. It reminds us that when we work together, we can achieve amazing things"

**【解析】** 文章回顾人类太空探索历程，末段点明主旨：展现勇气、科技和人类精神，强调团结协作可成就非凡。A项正确。B、C、D项均非文章主旨。故选A。
