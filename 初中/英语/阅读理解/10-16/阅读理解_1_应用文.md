> **10-16 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

**Youth Centre Interest Classes — Autumn Enrollment**

① Dear parents and students,

② The City Youth Centre will open autumn interest classes in November. We offer a wide range of activities for children aged 6 to 15. Registration starts on October 20th and ends on November 5th.

**Classes available:**

| Class         | Age   | Time          | Fee (per term) |
|---------------|-------|---------------|----------------|
| Drawing       | 6–12  | Sat 9:00–10:30| 800 yuan       |
| Calligraphy   | 8–14  | Sat 10:30–12:00| 600 yuan      |
| Robot Making  | 9–15  | Sun 9:00–11:00| 1,200 yuan     |
| Chess         | 7–14  | Sun 14:00–15:30| 500 yuan      |
| Dance         | 6–12  | Sat 14:00–15:30| 900 yuan      |

③ Each class runs for 12 weeks. All classes are taught by experienced teachers. Students need to bring their own materials for Drawing and Calligraphy (e.g. brushes, paper). Materials for Robot Making are included in the fee.

④ To sign up, visit our office at 128 Zhongshan Road, or register online at www.youthcentre.com. Please bring your child's ID when you come. Classes begin on November 9th.

⑤ For more information, call 020-87654321 or email youth@city.gov.cn. We look forward to seeing your children!

City Youth Centre  
October 14th, 2024

---

**Questions:**

1. When does registration for the autumn classes end?
   A. October 14th.
   B. October 20th.
   C. November 5th.
   D. November 9th.

2. Which class is the most expensive per term?
   A. Drawing.
   B. Calligraphy.
   C. Robot Making.
   D. Chess.

3. What do students need to bring for the Drawing class?
   A. Nothing.
   B. Their own materials like brushes and paper.
   C. A robot.
   D. A chess set.

4. Where can parents sign up their children?
   A. Only online.
   B. Only at the office.
   C. At the office or online.
   D. At the school.

5. What can we infer from the notice?
   A. All classes are for adults only.
   B. The Youth Centre offers different activities for children of different ages.
   C. Classes are free for everyone.
   D. Registration has already ended.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "Registration starts on October 20th and ends on November 5th"
**【解析】** 报名于11月5日截止。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 表格中 Robot Making 为1,200 yuan，高于其他项目
**【解析】** 机器人制作课程每期1200元，为最贵。C项正确。A、B、D项价格更低。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 "Students need to bring their own materials for Drawing and Calligraphy (e.g. brushes, paper)"
**【解析】** 绘画与书法需自备材料，如画笔、纸张。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "visit our office at 128 Zhongshan Road, or register online at www.youthcentre.com"
**【解析】** 报名方式为到办公室或网上注册。C项正确。A、B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第②段 "for children aged 6 to 15"；表格中各类课程有不同年龄段
**【解析】** 少年宫为6–15岁儿童提供多种课程，不同课程有不同年龄要求。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文矛盾。故选B。
