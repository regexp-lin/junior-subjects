# 阅读理解（议论文）

**Handwritten Homework vs Electronic Homework**

① More and more schools are asking students to do homework on computers or tablets. They type their answers, submit them online, and get feedback quickly. But some teachers and parents still prefer traditional handwritten homework. So which is better: handwriting or typing? Let us look at both sides.

② Those who support handwritten homework have several reasons. First, writing by hand helps memory. Studies show that when we write with a pen, our brain works harder. We remember things better than when we type. Second, handwriting improves fine motor skills. Young children need to practise holding a pen and forming letters. This helps their hand-eye coordination. Third, handwritten work is personal. You can see the student's thinking through their notes and corrections. It is harder to cheat when you have to write by hand in an exam. So practising handwriting at home prepares students for exams.

③ However, supporters of electronic homework point out its advantages. Typing is faster. Students can finish more in less time. Electronic work is easy to store and search. Teachers can use software to check for mistakes quickly. Besides, we live in a digital world. Most jobs today need computer skills. Learning to type and use technology from a young age can help students in the future. Also, electronic homework is good for the environment. No paper is wasted.

④ In my view, both have their place. For subjects like maths where you need to show your steps, or for young children learning to write, handwriting is important. For long essays or research, typing can save time. The best approach might be a mix: use handwriting for some tasks and electronics for others. Schools should not give up one for the other completely.

⑤ So, there is no simple answer. Handwriting and electronic homework each have benefits. What matters is that we use the right tool for the right task and help students develop both skills for the future.

**注释：** motor skills 动作技能；coordination 协调

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why does writing by hand help memory?
   A. Because pens are cheaper than computers.
   B. Because our brain works harder when we write with a pen, and we remember better.
   C. Because everyone likes handwriting.
   D. Because typing is banned.

2. Which is NOT an advantage of electronic homework mentioned in Paragraph ③?
   A. Typing is faster.
   B. Easy to store and search.
   C. Good for the environment.
   D. Handwriting is more beautiful.

3. What does the writer suggest in Paragraph ④?
   A. Use only handwriting for everything.
   B. Use only electronics for everything.
   C. Use a mix of both — handwriting for some tasks and electronics for others.
   D. Give up homework completely.

4. What does "fine motor skills" mean in Paragraph ②?
   A. Driving a car fast.
   B. Small hand movements like holding a pen and forming letters.
   C. Running quickly.
   D. Speaking loudly.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. Handwriting will disappear soon.
   B. Both handwriting and electronic skills are valuable; choose the right tool for each task.
   C. Electronic homework is always better.
   D. Schools should only use paper.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第②段 "when we write with a pen, our brain works harder. We remember things better than when we type"
**【解析】** 手写时大脑更费力，记忆效果优于打字。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】D**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第③段 电子作业优点：faster, easy to store and search, good for the environment
**【解析】** A、B、C均为文中提到的电子作业优点。D项"手写更美观"非电子作业优点，且未在第三段出现。故选D。

**3. 【答案】C**
**【题型】** 细节理解
**【定位】** 第④段 "The best approach might be a mix: use handwriting for some tasks and electronics for others"
**【解析】** 作者建议两种方式结合，根据任务选择手写或电子。C项正确。A、B、D项与作者建议不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 词义猜测
**【定位】** 第②段 "Young children need to practise holding a pen and forming letters. This helps their hand-eye coordination"
**【解析】** 上下文指握笔、写字等手部小动作能力。B项准确。A、C、D项与文意不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**
**【题型】** 推理判断
**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Handwriting and electronic homework each have benefits. What matters is that we use the right tool for the right task"
**【解析】** 结尾指出两种方式各有优势，关键是因任务选择合适工具，并培养学生两项技能。B项正确。A、C、D项与文意相反。故选B。
