# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Many families around the world keep pets. Dogs, cats, fish, and even rabbits are popular choices. Some people think keeping a pet is wonderful, while others believe it causes more problems than it solves. So, should families keep pets?

② People who support keeping pets say that pets bring joy and companionship to a family. Studies show that playing with a pet can reduce stress and make people feel happier. For children, having a pet can teach them responsibility. They learn to feed, clean, and care for another living thing, which helps them become more thoughtful and kind.

③ Pets can also be good for people's health. Research has found that dog owners walk more than people without dogs, because they need to take their dogs outside every day. This extra exercise helps keep them fit. Some hospitals even bring therapy dogs to visit patients, because being around animals can help people feel better and recover faster.

④ However, not everyone agrees that keeping pets is a good idea. First, pets can be expensive. The cost of food, medical care, and other supplies can add up to thousands of yuan every year. Second, some pets need a lot of time and attention. If a family is too busy, the pet may not get enough care, which is unfair to the animal. Third, some people are allergic to pet hair, which can cause health problems.

⑤ In my opinion, keeping a pet can be a great experience, but only if a family is truly ready for it. Before getting a pet, families should think carefully about whether they have enough time, money, and space. A pet is not a toy — it is a living thing that deserves love and care.

---

**注释：** companionship 陪伴；therapy dog 治疗犬；allergic 过敏的

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ②, what can children learn from keeping a pet?
   A. How to cook meals for the family.
   B. How to be more responsible.
   C. How to make more friends at school.
   D. How to study better at home.

2. Why do dog owners walk more than other people according to Paragraph ③?
   A. Because their doctors tell them to walk more.
   B. Because walking is the cheapest form of exercise.
   C. Because they enjoy walking as a hobby.
   D. Because they need to take their dogs outside.

3. Which of the following is NOT a problem of keeping pets mentioned in Paragraph ④?
   A. Pets can be expensive to keep.
   B. Some people are allergic to pet hair.
   C. Pets may bite or hurt family members.
   D. Busy families may not give pets enough care.

4. The underlined word "deserves" in Paragraph ⑤ probably means "________."
   A. 值得
   B. 拒绝
   C. 害怕
   D. 需要

5. What is the writer's opinion about keeping pets?
   A. Keeping pets is always a bad idea for families.
   B. All families should keep at least one pet.
   C. Families should only keep pets if they are truly ready.
   D. Pets should only live in the wild, not at home.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3-4句 "For children, having a pet can teach them responsibility. They learn to feed, clean, and care for another living thing, which helps them become more thoughtful and kind."

**【解析】** 原文指出养宠物可以教会孩子责任感——他们学习喂养、清洁和照顾另一个生命，从而变得更体贴和善良。B 项"如何更有责任感"与原文完全对应。A 项"如何为家人做饭"原文未提及烹饪；C 项"如何在学校交更多朋友"与养宠物的内容无关；D 项"如何在家学习得更好"也属于无中生有。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Research has found that dog owners walk more than people without dogs, because they need to take their dogs outside every day."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明养狗的人比不养狗的人走得更多，原因是他们每天需要带狗出去。D 项"因为他们需要带狗出门"与原文因果关系完全吻合。A 项"因为医生让他们多走路"原文未提及医嘱；B 项"因为步行是最便宜的运动方式"偷换了原因——走路多是因为遛狗，不是因为便宜；C 项"因为他们把走路当爱好"也不正确——走路多的原因是遛狗的需要，而非个人爱好。故选 D。

---

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向选择）

**【定位】** 第④段列举的三个问题："pets can be expensive"；"some pets need a lot of time and attention"；"some people are allergic to pet hair"。

**【解析】** 本题要求选出第④段中"未提到"的养宠物问题。A 项"养宠物费用高"对应原文"pets can be expensive"；B 项"有人对宠物毛发过敏"对应原文"some people are allergic to pet hair"；D 项"忙碌的家庭可能无法给宠物足够照顾"对应原文"If a family is too busy, the pet may not get enough care"。C 项"宠物可能咬伤或弄伤家人"在第④段中完全没有提及，属于无中生有。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 逆向选择题需要逐一在原文中核实每个选项。建议用"√"标记能找到对应的选项，用"×"标记找不到的，最终选择"×"的选项。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "A pet is not a toy — it is a living thing that deserves love and care."

**【解析】** 原文将宠物与玩具对比，强调宠物是有生命的，后面紧跟"deserves love and care"。从语境来看，作者认为宠物作为生命体，应该得到/配得上爱与关怀。A 项"值得"最符合语境——宠物"值得"被爱和照顾。B 项"拒绝"语义相反——宠物不会拒绝爱和关怀；C 项"害怕"不符合上下文逻辑；D 项"需要"虽然语义上部分接近，但"deserves"强调的是"配得上、应该得到"，比单纯的"需要"更有道德内涵。故选 A。

**【技巧点拨】** 词义猜测题中，当多个选项语义接近时，要通过上下文的情感色彩来精确判断。本句的语气是呼吁善待宠物，"值得"比"需要"更能传达这种呼吁的力度。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, keeping a pet can be a great experience, but only if a family is truly ready for it. Before getting a pet, families should think carefully about whether they have enough time, money, and space."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段明确表达了自己的观点：养宠物可以是很好的体验，但前提是家庭真正做好了准备，需要在时间、金钱和空间方面认真考虑。C 项"家庭只有在真正准备好的时候才应该养宠物"最准确地概括了作者的立场。A 项"养宠物总是个坏主意"过于绝对——作者认为养宠物"可以是很好的体验"；B 项"所有家庭都应该至少养一只宠物"同样过于绝对——作者强调了条件和准备；D 项"宠物只应该生活在野外"原文未表达此观点。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 议论文中作者的观点通常出现在"In my opinion"等观点信号词之后。注意区分"有条件的支持"（本文）和"无条件的支持/反对"——含"but only if"等限制条件的表述往往对应中间立场。
