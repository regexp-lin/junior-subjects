# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## An Umbrella on a Rainy Day

① It was a cloudy Wednesday afternoon. Zhang Wei sat in the classroom and looked out of the window. Dark clouds filled the sky. "It's going to rain soon," he thought. He had brought his umbrella to school that morning because his mother had reminded him. When the bell rang at four o'clock, he packed his bag and walked to the door.

② As soon as Zhang Wei stepped outside, heavy rain started to fall. He opened his umbrella and was about to walk home when he saw Chen Lin standing under the roof of the school building. She had no umbrella. She looked at the rain with a worried face. Zhang Wei remembered that Chen Lin lived far from school. She usually took the bus, but the bus stop was a five-minute walk away. In this rain, she would get very wet.

③ Zhang Wei walked over to her. "Chen Lin, do you have an umbrella?" he asked. She shook her head. "I forgot to bring one today. I'll wait here until the rain stops," she said. Zhang Wei thought for a moment. His home was only ten minutes away. He could walk quickly and not get too wet. "Here, take my umbrella," he said. "I live close by. You need it more than I do."

④ Chen Lin was surprised. "But you'll get wet!" she said. "It's okay. I can run home. You have a long way to go," Zhang Wei replied with a smile. He handed her the umbrella and ran into the rain. Chen Lin stood there, holding the umbrella. Her heart felt warm. The next day, she found Zhang Wei and thanked him again. "You are such a kind friend," she said. From that day on, they became good friends.

⑤ Sometimes a small act of kindness can change everything. Zhang Wei did not think he did anything special. He just saw someone in need and decided to help. But for Chen Lin, that umbrella on a rainy day was a gift she would never forget.

**注释：** remind 提醒；roof 屋檐；surprised 惊讶的

### 选择题

1. Why did Zhang Wei bring an umbrella to school that day?
   A. The teacher asked him to bring one.
   B. His mother reminded him to bring it.
   C. Chen Lin asked him to bring one for her.
   D. He saw dark clouds in the morning.

2. Where did Zhang Wei see Chen Lin when it started to rain?
   A. At the bus stop.
   B. At home.
   C. Under the roof of the school building.
   D. In the classroom.

3. Why did Zhang Wei decide to give his umbrella to Chen Lin?
   A. Because he did not like the umbrella.
   B. Because Chen Lin lived far from school and would get very wet without it.
   C. Because the teacher told him to share.
   D. Because he had two umbrellas.

4. How did Chen Lin feel when Zhang Wei gave her the umbrella?
   A. She felt angry because she wanted to wait.
   B. She felt surprised and her heart felt warm.
   C. She felt worried that Zhang Wei would catch a cold.
   D. She felt nothing special.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Rainy days are bad for students.
   B. Mothers always remember to bring umbrellas.
   C. A small act of kindness can mean a lot and even lead to friendship.
   D. Students should never forget their umbrellas.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "He had brought his umbrella to school that morning because his mother had reminded him."

**【解析】** Zhang Wei 带伞是因为妈妈早上提醒了他。B 项正确。A、C、D 项均未在原文提及。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "he saw Chen Lin standing under the roof of the school building."

**【解析】** Zhang Wei 看到 Chen Lin 站在教学楼屋檐下。C 项正确。A 项公交站、B 项家里、D 项教室里均不符。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②③段 "She lived far from school... the bus stop was a five-minute walk away. In this rain, she would get very wet" 及 "You need it more than I do."

**【解析】** Chen Lin 住得远，去公交站要步行五分钟，大雨中会淋湿；Zhang Wei 认为她比自己更需要伞。B 项正确。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Chen Lin was surprised... Her heart felt warm."

**【解析】** Chen Lin 先是惊讶，随后心里感到温暖。B 项准确。A 项生气、C 项仅担心感冒、D 项无特别感受均不全面。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Sometimes a small act of kindness can change everything... that umbrella on a rainy day was a gift she would never forget."

**【解析】** 文章通过雨天送伞的故事说明小小的善举意义重大，甚至可以促成友谊。C 项概括主旨。A、B、D 项均非文章重点。故选 C。
