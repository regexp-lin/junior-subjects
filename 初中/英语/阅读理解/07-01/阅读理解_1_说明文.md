# 阅读理解（说明文）

## How Volcanoes Form and Their Different Types

① A volcano is a mountain or hill with an opening at the top. Deep inside the Earth, it is very hot. There is melted rock called magma. When pressure builds up, the magma pushes upward. It finds a way to reach the surface. When the magma comes out, we call it lava. This is how a volcano forms.

② Volcanoes can be active, dormant, or extinct. An active volcano may erupt at any time. A dormant volcano has not erupted for a long time, but it might erupt again in the future. An extinct volcano has not erupted for thousands of years and is unlikely to erupt again. Scientists study these volcanoes to keep people safe.

③ There are different types of volcanoes. Shield volcanoes are wide and flat like a shield. They are usually not very tall. Their lava flows slowly and spreads over a large area. Another type is the composite volcano. It is tall and cone-shaped. It is made of layers of lava and ash. When it erupts, it can be very powerful and dangerous.

④ When a volcano erupts, it sends out lava, ash, and gases. Lava can destroy buildings and forests. Ash can travel far in the air and make it hard to breathe. People who live near active volcanoes need to be ready to leave quickly if necessary. Scientists use special tools to watch volcanoes and give early warnings.

⑤ Volcanoes also bring benefits. The soil around volcanoes is often very rich and good for growing crops. Some people choose to live near volcanoes because the land is fertile. Understanding how volcanoes work helps us prepare for eruptions and appreciate the powerful forces of our planet.

**注释：** magma 岩浆；lava 熔岩；dormant 休眠的；erupt 喷发

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, what causes a volcano to form?
   A. Cold rocks deep inside the Earth.
   B. Magma pushes upward and comes out as lava when pressure builds up.
   C. Scientists build volcanoes for study.
   D. Rain and wind create openings in mountains.

2. What is the difference between a dormant volcano and an extinct volcano?
   A. Dormant volcanoes are bigger.
   B. A dormant volcano might erupt again, but an extinct one is unlikely to.
   C. Extinct volcanoes are more dangerous.
   D. There is no difference between them.

3. Which type of volcano is described as "wide and flat like a shield" in paragraph ③?
   A. Composite volcano.
   B. Active volcano.
   C. Shield volcano.
   D. Extinct volcano.

4. According to paragraph ④, why do people need to be ready to leave quickly near active volcanoes?
   A. Because the weather is always bad there.
   B. Because lava, ash, and gases from eruptions can be dangerous and destroy things.
   C. Because there are too many tourists.
   D. Because the soil is not good for farming.

5. What can we infer from the last paragraph?
   A. All volcanoes are harmful and should be avoided.
   B. Volcanoes only bring danger to people.
   C. Volcanoes can have both risks and benefits; the soil around them is often fertile.
   D. Scientists know nothing about volcanoes.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "When pressure builds up, the magma pushes upward... When the magma comes out, we call it lava. This is how a volcano forms."

**【解析】** 当压力积聚，岩浆向上涌出，喷出地表后称为熔岩，火山由此形成。B 项正确。A 项冷岩、C 项科学家建造、D 项雨水和风均与原文不符。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "A dormant volcano has not erupted for a long time, but it might erupt again in the future. An extinct volcano has not erupted for thousands of years and is unlikely to erupt again."

**【解析】** 休眠火山长时间未喷发但可能再次喷发；死火山数千年未喷发且不太可能再喷发。B 项准确。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Shield volcanoes are wide and flat like a shield."

**【解析】** 盾状火山宽而平，形如盾牌。C 项正确。A 项复合火山为锥形，B、D 项为火山状态而非类型。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "When a volcano erupts, it sends out lava, ash, and gases. Lava can destroy buildings and forests. Ash can travel far in the air and make it hard to breathe. People who live near active volcanoes need to be ready to leave quickly if necessary."

**【解析】** 喷发会释放熔岩、火山灰和气体，可毁坏建筑和森林，火山灰影响呼吸，故附近居民需做好快速撤离准备。B 项正确。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Volcanoes also bring benefits. The soil around volcanoes is often very rich and good for growing crops."

**【解析】** 火山既有风险也有益处，其周边土壤肥沃，利于耕作。C 项准确推断。A、B 项只强调危害；D 项与"科学家用工具监测"矛盾。故选 C。
