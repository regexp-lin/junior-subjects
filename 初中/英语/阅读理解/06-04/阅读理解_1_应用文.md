# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **2025 School Sports Day Schedule**

② Our school sports day will be held on Friday, March 28. All students and teachers are welcome to join. The event starts at 8:00 a.m. and ends at 5:00 p.m. Please arrive at the playground by 7:45 a.m. to get ready for the opening ceremony (开幕式).

③ **Morning events (8:30 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.):** The 100-meter race and the long jump (跳远) will take place first. Then we will have the relay race (接力赛) for each grade. Students who join the games should wear sports clothes and comfortable shoes.

④ **Afternoon events (2:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.):** In the afternoon, there will be basketball games and the high jump (跳高). The closing ceremony and prize-giving will start at 4:30 p.m. Parents are invited to watch and take photos.

⑤ **Important notes:** Bring your own water bottle. Do not run or play when you feel too tired. If the weather is bad, we may change the date. Check the school notice board for any updates.

**注释：** ceremony 仪式；relay race 接力赛；notice board 公告栏

---

### 选择题

1. When does the sports day start?
   A. At 7:45 a.m.
   B. At 8:00 a.m.
   C. At 8:30 a.m.
   D. At 2:00 p.m.

2. Which event takes place in the morning?
   A. Basketball games.
   B. The high jump.
   C. The 100-meter race.
   D. The closing ceremony.

3. According to the passage, what should students wear for the games?
   A. School uniform only.
   B. Sports clothes and comfortable shoes.
   C. Fancy clothes for photos.
   D. Warm coats.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ⑤?
   A. The sports day will never be cancelled.
   B. Students must bring their own food.
   C. The school may change the date if the weather is bad.
   D. Parents cannot come to watch.

5. What is the purpose of this notice?
   A. To invite parents to join the games.
   B. To tell students and teachers about the sports day schedule and rules.
   C. To ask students to buy new shoes.
   D. To announce the winners of last year's games.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The event starts at 8:00 a.m. and ends at 5:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 运动会开始时间为上午 8:00。B 项正确。A 项 7:45 为要求到达时间；C 项 8:30 为上午项目开始时间；D 项 2:00 为下午项目开始时间。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Morning events (8:30 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.): The 100-meter race and the long jump will take place first. Then we will have the relay race."

**【解析】** 上午项目包括 100 米跑、跳远和接力赛。C 项 100 米跑属上午项目，正确。A、B 项篮球赛和跳高在下午；D 项闭幕式在下午 4:30。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Students who join the games should wear sports clothes and comfortable shoes."

**【解析】** 参加比赛的学生应穿运动服和舒适的运动鞋。B 项正确。A 项仅校服、C 项 fancy clothes、D 项 warm coats 均与原文不符。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "If the weather is bad, we may change the date."

**【解析】** 若天气恶劣，活动日期可能调整。C 项正确。A 项"永不取消"与 may change 矛盾；B 项"自带食物"未提及，文中仅要求带水杯；D 项"家长不能观看"与第④段 invite parents 矛盾。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 写作意图 / 主旨理解

**【定位】** 全文：介绍运动会日期、时间、项目安排及注意事项。

**【解析】** 本通知旨在告知学生和教师运动会的时间安排、项目及规则。B 项正确。A 项邀请家长参加比赛错误，家长是来观看；C 项要求买新鞋过度推断；D 项宣布去年获奖者与内容无关。故选 B。
