> **06-04 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Many teenagers like to follow stars — singers, actors, or athletes. They buy their posters, watch their shows, and try to learn everything about them. Some parents and teachers worry: Is it harmful for young people to idolize (崇拜) celebrities? Different people have different views.

② **Those who think it is harmful** say that teenagers may spend too much time and money on stars. Some students skip (错过) their homework to watch live streams or concerts. They may also copy unhealthy habits from celebrities, such as staying up late or caring too much about looks. Besides, some stars do not always set a good example. When teenagers admire the wrong person, they may learn bad things.

③ **Those who think it can be helpful** believe that stars can encourage teenagers to work hard. For example, an athlete's story of success may inspire (激励) a young person to practice harder in sports or study. Stars can also bring joy — listening to music or watching movies can help students relax after a busy day. As long as teenagers do not go too far, following stars can be a normal part of growing up.

④ **What experts suggest:** It is okay to admire someone, but teenagers should keep a balance. Do not let your hobbies affect your study or health. Choose stars who have positive values — those who work hard, help others, or overcome difficulties. Parents and teachers can guide young people to think critically about what they see and hear.

⑤ In conclusion, following stars is not always bad. The key is to do it in a sensible (理智的) way — enjoy the good things, avoid the bad ones, and never forget your own goals and values.

**注释：** idolize 崇拜；inspire 激励；sensible 理智的

---

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, what might happen if teenagers spend too much time on stars?
   A. They will become stars themselves soon.
   B. They may skip homework to watch live streams or concerts.
   C. They will get more support from their parents.
   D. They will have more free time for study.

2. What positive effect can following stars have, according to those who support it?
   A. Teenagers will spend more money.
   B. Stars can encourage teenagers to work hard and help them relax.
   C. Teenagers will never care about their health.
   D. Teenagers will stop studying.

3. What do experts suggest teenagers do?
   A. Never follow any star.
   B. Spend all their time watching concerts.
   C. Keep a balance and choose stars with positive values.
   D. Copy everything that stars do.

4. The underlined word "sensible" in paragraph ⑤ probably means ______.
   A. crazy
   B. lazy
   C. reasonable and wise
   D. expensive

5. What is the writer's main conclusion about following stars?
   A. It is always harmful and should be stopped.
   B. It is always helpful for everyone.
   C. It is not always bad; the key is to do it in a sensible way.
   D. Parents should choose stars for their children.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Some students skip their homework to watch live streams or concerts."

**【解析】** 部分学生为看直播或演唱会而错过/不做作业。B 项正确。A 项"很快成为明星"、C 项"获得更多父母支持"、D 项"有更多学习时间"均与担忧内容相反。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "stars can encourage teenagers to work hard... Stars can also bring joy — listening to music or watching movies can help students relax after a busy day."

**【解析】** 支持者认为明星可激励青少年努力，音乐和电影也能帮助放松。B 项正确。A、C、D 项均与正面效应不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "It is okay to admire someone, but teenagers should keep a balance... Choose stars who have positive values."

**【解析】** 专家建议保持平衡，选择具有积极价值观的明星。C 项正确。A 项"绝不追星"过于绝对；B 项"把所有时间看演唱会"与 keep a balance 矛盾；D 项"完全模仿"与 think critically 矛盾。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "do it in a sensible way — enjoy the good things, avoid the bad ones"

**【解析】** 后文"享受好的、避免坏的"说明 sensible 意为理性、明智的。C 项 reasonable and wise 与之相符。A 项 crazy、B 项 lazy、D 项 expensive 皆不符语境。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 作者观点

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "following stars is not always bad. The key is to do it in a sensible way — enjoy the good things, avoid the bad ones, and never forget your own goals and values."

**【解析】** 作者结论：追星并不总是有害，关键在于理智对待——取长避短，不忘自身目标与价值观。C 项准确概括。A、B 项"总是有害/有益"过于绝对；D 项"父母选星"未作为结论提出。故选 C。
