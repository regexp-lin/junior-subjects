# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① When Lin Xia first stepped into the English speaking class, her heart was pounding (砰砰跳). She was a quiet girl who rarely spoke in class. The teacher, Mr. Chen, asked each student to introduce themselves in English. When it was Lin Xia's turn, her face turned red and her voice trembled (颤抖). She could barely say a few words before she sat down, feeling embarrassed.

② Mr. Chen noticed Lin Xia's nervousness. After class, he talked to her and said, "Everyone makes mistakes. The more you practice, the better you'll become." He suggested that she try speaking for just one minute every day in front of a mirror. Lin Xia decided to give it a try. At first, she felt silly talking to herself, but slowly she grew more comfortable with the sounds of English.

③ Week by week, Lin Xia practiced. She joined a small group in class where students helped each other. Her group mates were kind and never laughed when she made mistakes. They encouraged her to share her opinions. One day, when the topic was "My Favorite Place," Lin Xia spoke for two full minutes about a park near her home. She was surprised at herself. So was Mr. Chen, who gave her a thumbs up.

④ By the end of the term, Lin Xia had become one of the most active speakers in class. She even volunteered to give a short speech about her summer trip. Her classmates clapped warmly when she finished. Lin Xia realized that the key was not to be perfect but to keep trying. Making mistakes was part of learning.

⑤ Looking back, Lin Xia said, "I used to think speaking English was too scary. Now I know that practice and courage matter more than being perfect. If I could change, anyone can." She hopes to help other shy students find their voice one day.

**注释：** pound 砰砰跳；tremble 颤抖； embarrassed 尴尬的；volunteer 自愿；courage 勇气

---

### 选择题

1. How did Lin Xia feel when she first spoke in the English class?
   A. Excited and confident.
   B. Happy and relaxed.
   C. Angry at the teacher.
   D. Nervous and embarrassed; her face turned red and her voice trembled.

2. What did Mr. Chen suggest Lin Xia do to improve her speaking?
   A. Speak for one minute every day in front of a mirror.
   B. Write more English essays.
   C. Only listen to English tapes.
   D. Skip the speaking class.

3. According to paragraph ③, what helped Lin Xia become more confident?
   A. She stopped going to class.
   B. She joined a small group where classmates encouraged her and helped each other.
   C. She changed her teacher.
   D. She only practiced alone at home.

4. What can we infer from Lin Xia's change by the end of the term?
   A. She will never make mistakes again.
   B. She decided to stop learning English.
   C. She learned that practice and courage are more important than being perfect.
   D. She thinks speaking is not useful.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. Teachers should never ask shy students to speak.
   B. English is the easiest subject to learn.
   C. Mirrors help everyone learn languages.
   D. A shy student gains confidence and becomes fluent in an English speaking class through practice and support.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "her face turned red and her voice trembled (颤抖). She could barely say a few words before she sat down, feeling embarrassed."

**【解析】** 原文明确描述林霞首次发言时脸红、声音颤抖、几乎说不出口，坐下后感到尴尬。D项"紧张且尴尬；脸红、声音颤抖"准确概括。A项"兴奋自信"与原文相反；B项"开心放松"、C项"对老师生气"均未提及。故选D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "He suggested that she try speaking for just one minute every day in front of a mirror."

**【解析】** 原文清楚说明陈老师建议她每天在镜子前练习说一分钟英语。A项正确。B项"多写英语作文"、C项"只听英语磁带"、D项"缺课"均非老师建议。故选A。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "She joined a small group in class where students helped each other. Her group mates were kind and never laughed when she made mistakes. They encouraged her to share her opinions."

**【解析】** 原文指出林霞加入小组，组员友善、不嘲笑错误、鼓励她表达，互相帮助。B项正确。A项"不再上课"、C项"换老师"、D项"只在家练习"与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④⑤段 "the key was not to be perfect but to keep trying. Making mistakes was part of learning" 及 "practice and courage matter more than being perfect."

**【解析】** 林霞认识到关键不在于完美而在于坚持尝试，犯错是学习的一部分；她总结"练习和勇气比完美更重要"。可推知她领悟到练习与勇气的重要性。C项正确。A项"不再犯错"过于绝对；B项"停止学英语"、D项"认为口语无用"与原文相反。故选C。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段害羞开端，第②段老师建议与自我练习，第③段小组支持与突破，第④⑤段进步与感悟。

**【解析】** 全文叙述林霞在英语口语课上从害羞到流利发言的成长过程，D项"一名害羞学生在英语口语课上通过练习和支持获得自信、变得流利"最能概括主旨。A项与文章鼓励开口的主题相反；B项"英语最容易学"非主旨；C项以偏概全。故选D。
