# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Penguins are birds that cannot fly, but they are excellent swimmers. They live mostly in the Southern Hemisphere (南半球), especially in cold areas like Antarctica (南极洲). There are about 18 different kinds of penguins, and each type has its own way of life. Understanding penguins helps us learn more about our planet.

② Penguins spend most of their time in the sea, hunting for fish, squid, and small sea animals. Their bodies are built for swimming: their wings have turned into flippers (鳍状肢), and their black and white coloring helps them hide from enemies. Underwater, a penguin can move very fast — some types can swim as fast as 25 kilometers per hour. On land, they often walk in groups and slide on their bellies (腹部) across the ice to save energy.

③ Not all penguins live in the coldest places. Some live in warmer areas such as South Africa, Australia, and South America. The Galápagos penguin lives near the equator and is one of the smallest kinds. Emperor penguins, on the other hand, live only in Antarctica and are the largest — they can stand over one meter tall. Different species choose different homes based on what they need to survive.

④ Penguins usually form large groups called colonies (聚居地) when they nest and raise their young. Parents take turns keeping eggs warm and finding food. In cold climates, male emperor penguins hold the egg on their feet for about two months while the female goes to the sea for food. This teamwork helps their chicks (雏鸟) survive the harsh (严酷的) winter.

⑤ Today, climate change and pollution affect penguin populations. Warmer temperatures make it harder for them to find food, and plastic in the ocean can harm them. Scientists work to protect penguins and their habitats. By learning about these amazing birds, we can better understand why we must care for our environment.

**注释：** Antarctica 南极洲；flipper 鳍状肢；belly 腹部；colony 聚居地；chick 雏鸟；harsh 严酷的

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "They live mostly in the Southern Hemisphere (南半球), especially in cold areas like Antarctica (南极洲)."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明企鹅主要生活在南半球，尤其在像南极洲这样的寒冷地区。B项正确。A项"热带海洋"、C项"北美和欧洲"、D项"河流湖泊"均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Their bodies are built for swimming: their wings have turned into flippers (鳍状肢)..."

**【解析】** 原文指出企鹅的身体适合游泳，翅膀已演化为鳍状肢。B项准确概括企鹅善游的原因。A项"大喙"、C项"只吃小动物"、D项"不需休息"均非原文所述。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "The Galápagos penguin lives near the equator and is one of the smallest kinds."

**【解析】** 文章以加拉帕戈斯企鹅为例，说明并非所有企鹅都生活在最寒冷地区，有些生活在赤道附近的温暖区域。A项正确。B项"所有企鹅都小"以偏概全；C项和D项与举例目的无关。故选A。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Parents take turns keeping eggs warm..." 及 "male emperor penguins hold the egg on their feet for about two months while the female goes to the sea for food. This teamwork helps their chicks survive the harsh winter."

**【解析】** 原文描述企鹅父母轮流孵蛋、雄性帝企鹅独自孵卵两月等行为，体现依靠合作养育后代，尤其在严酷环境中。B项正确。A项"从不合作"与原文矛盾；C项"仅雌性照顾蛋"与雄性孵蛋情节矛盾；D项与原文不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文：第①段引入企鹅分布，第②段生活习性，第③段不同种类分布，第④段养育方式，第⑤段保护呼吁。

**【解析】** 全文介绍企鹅的生活习性、全球分布及面临的保护问题，结尾呼吁关注环境。A项"介绍企鹅的习性、分布及保护需求"最能概括写作目的。B项侧重旅游；C项仅涉及一小点；D项与鱼类比较非主旨。故选A。
