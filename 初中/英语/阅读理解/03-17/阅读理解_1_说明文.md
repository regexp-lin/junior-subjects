# 阅读理解 第1篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Do you often feel tired during the day? Do you find it hard to pay attention in class? If your answer is "yes," you might not be getting enough sleep. For teenagers, sleep is just as important as healthy food and exercise.

② Scientists say that students aged 13 to 18 need about 8 to 10 hours of sleep every night. However, many teenagers sleep for less than 7 hours. Why? Some students stay up late to finish heavy homework. Others spend too much time playing games or chatting with friends on their phones before bedtime. 

③ Lack of sleep can cause many problems. First, it affects your brain. When you are asleep, your brain is resting and organizing what you learned during the day. If you don't sleep enough, you may forget things easily and do badly in exams. Second, it affects your mood. Tired students often feel angry or sad for no reason. Finally, poor sleep can make you sick more often because your body doesn't have enough time to repair itself.

④ So, how can you get a good night's sleep? Here are some simple tips. Try to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends. Keep your bedroom quiet, dark, and cool. Most importantly, turn off all electronic devices, like phones and computers, at least an hour before you go to sleep.

⑤ Remember, a good night's sleep is the secret to a happy and energetic day. Start making changes tonight and see the difference tomorrow!

---

**注释：** organize 整理，组织；mood 情绪；repair 修复；electronic device 电子设备

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ①, what is as important as healthy food and exercise for teenagers?
   A. Playing games.
   B. Getting enough sleep.
   C. Chatting with friends.
   D. Paying attention in class.

2. How many hours of sleep do teenagers need every night according to scientists?
   A. Less than 7 hours.
   B. 7 to 8 hours.
   C. 8 to 10 hours.
   D. More than 10 hours.

3. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a problem caused by lack of sleep?
   A. Forgetting things easily.
   B. Feeling angry or sad for no reason.
   C. Getting sick more often.
   D. Eating too much junk food.

4. What is the writer's advice for getting a good night's sleep?
   A. Turn off phones right before falling asleep.
   B. Go to bed at different times on weekends.
   C. Keep the bedroom bright and warm.
   D. Turn off electronic devices at least an hour before bedtime.

5. What would be the best title for this passage?
   A. How to Do Well in Exams
   B. The Dangers of Electronic Devices
   C. Why Teenagers Need Enough Sleep
   D. The Secret to Healthy Food

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "For teenagers, sleep is just as important as healthy food and exercise."

**【解析】** 原文明确提及对于青少年来说，睡眠与健康饮食和锻炼一样重要。B 项与原文一致。A 项和 C 项（玩游戏、和朋友聊天）是第②段中提到的导致睡眠不足的原因；D 项（在课堂上集中注意力）是睡眠充足的好处之一，但这里问的是"什么和健康饮食、锻炼一样重要"。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "Scientists say that students aged 13 to 18 need about 8 to 10 hours of sleep every night."

**【解析】** 原文提到科学家表示13到18岁的学生每晚需要大约8到10小时的睡眠。C 项与原文数据完全一致。A 项"少于7小时"是很多青少年的实际睡眠时间，而不是需要的睡眠时间；B 项"7到8小时"和 D 项"超过10小时"的数据与原文不符。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向选择）

**【定位】** 第③段整段内容。

**【解析】** 第③段列举了睡眠不足导致的三个问题：一、影响大脑，可能会轻易忘记事情（A项符合）；二、影响情绪，经常无缘无故感到生气或悲伤（B项符合）；三、更容易生病（C项符合）。文中并未提到睡眠不足会导致"吃太多垃圾食品"（D项），属于无中生有。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "Most importantly, turn off all electronic devices, like phones and computers, at least an hour before you go to sleep."

**【解析】** 作者在第④段给出建议，最重要的一点是在睡前至少一小时关闭所有电子设备。D 项与原文一致。A 项"在睡着前关掉手机"与原文"至少提前一小时"的时间要求不符；B 项"周末在不同的时间睡觉"与原文"even on weekends"（周末也要在同一时间睡觉）矛盾；C 项"保持卧室明亮温暖"与原文"Keep your bedroom quiet, dark, and cool"（保持安静、黑暗和凉爽）矛盾。故选 D。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文整体结构。

**【解析】** 全文围绕青少年的"睡眠"展开：第①段引出睡眠的重要性；第②段指出青少年睡眠不足的现状及原因；第③段详细阐述睡眠不足带来的后果；第④段给出改善睡眠的建议；最后一段总结呼吁。核心主题是探讨睡眠对青少年的重要性。C 项"为什么青少年需要足够的睡眠"最能概括文章中心。A 项只片面涉及了第③段提到的一点；B 项"电子设备的危险"也只是局部细节；D 项与文章主旨无关。故选 C。

**【技巧点拨】** 选择文章最佳标题时，要确保标题能够涵盖全文的主要内容，不能以偏概全或偏离主题。本文每一段都是围绕"睡眠"展开论述的，因此标题必须包含这一核心词。
