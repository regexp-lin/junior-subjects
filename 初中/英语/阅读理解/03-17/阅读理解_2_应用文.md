# 阅读理解 第2篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① **Green Earth Club — Spring Park Cleanup**

Dear friends,

Spring is here, and it’s time to make our city beautiful! Join us for our annual Spring Park Cleanup at Riverside Park. This is a great chance to help the environment, meet new people, and enjoy the fresh air.

② **Event Details:**
- **Date:** Sunday, March 22
- **Time:** 9:00 am – 12:00 pm
- **Meeting Point:** The main gate of Riverside Park

③ **What to bring:**
- Comfortable clothes and shoes (they might get dirty!)
- A reusable water bottle
- Sun hat

_Note: We will provide gloves, trash bags, and tools._

④ **Schedule:**
| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 9:00 am | Registration & Grouping (分组) |
| 9:30 am | Cleanup starts |
| 11:30 am | Sorting the trash (分类垃圾) |
| 12:00 pm | Group photo & Free sandwiches |

⑤ **Who can join?**
Everyone is welcome! Students, parents, and teachers can all take part. However, children under 10 must be with an adult. If you want to join, please sign up online before Friday, March 20. 

For more information or to sign up, visit our website at www.greenearthclub.org or call 555-0198. Let's work together for a cleaner earth!

---

**注释：** cleanup 清理；reusable 可重复使用的；registration 登记；sign up 报名

---

### 题目

1. What is the purpose of the event?
   A. To plant trees in the park.
   B. To clean up Riverside Park.
   C. To have a picnic with friends.
   D. To celebrate the coming of spring.

2. What do the volunteers need to bring?
   A. Gloves and trash bags.
   B. Tools for cleaning.
   C. A reusable water bottle.
   D. Sandwiches for lunch.

3. According to the schedule, what will people do at 11:30 am?
   A. Register and join a group.
   B. Take a group photo.
   C. Start cleaning the park.
   D. Sort the collected trash.

4. Who CANNOT join the cleanup alone?
   A. An 8-year-old boy.
   B. A 12-year-old girl.
   C. A university student.
   D. An English teacher.

5. How can people sign up for the event?
   A. By going to the park gate on Sunday.
   B. By calling 555-0198 on Saturday.
   C. By visiting the website before Friday.
   D. By talking to their teachers in school.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Join us for our annual Spring Park Cleanup at Riverside Park."

**【解析】** 原文标题及正文都明确指出这是一次在河滨公园（Riverside Park）进行的"春季公园清理活动"（Spring Park Cleanup）。B 项与活动目的完全吻合。A 项"在公园种树"原文未提及；C 项"和朋友去野餐"属于无中生有，虽然活动最后有免费三明治，但这并非主要目的；D 项"庆祝春天的到来"是对"Spring is here"的过度引伸。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "What to bring" 列表中的第2项 "A reusable water bottle" 及底部的注释 "We will provide gloves, trash bags, and tools."

**【解析】** 原文列出了需要志愿者自带的物品，包括舒适的衣服鞋子、可重复使用的水瓶和太阳帽。C 项与原文一致。A 项（手套和垃圾袋）和 B 项（清理工具）在第③段注释中都标明了"我们将提供"（We will provide），所以不需要自带；D 项"午餐三明治"在第④段日程表的最后一步显示是活动方提供的（Free sandwiches）。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段日程表（Schedule）中 "11:30 am" 对应的一行："Sorting the trash (分类垃圾)"

**【解析】** 根据表格安排，11:30 进行的活动是"分类垃圾"（Sorting the trash）。D 项是对该活动的准确描述。A 项"登记和分组"是 9:00 进行的；B 项"拍合影"是 12:00 进行的；C 项"开始清理"是 9:30 进行的。这三个选项均属于张冠李戴。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向思维）

**【定位】** 第⑤段第3句 "However, children under 10 must be with an adult."

**【解析】** 原文规定，10岁以下的儿童必须由成人陪同（不能单独参加）。A 项"一个8岁的男孩"属于10岁以下，因此不能单独参加（alone）。B 项、C 项、D 项的人群都不受此年龄限制，可以单独参加。故选 A。

**【技巧点拨】** 本题考查对条件限制的理解。"CANNOT... alone" 等同于"must be with an adult"。遇到年龄或身份限制条件时，要仔细比对选项与限制条件的对应关系。

---

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第4句 "If you want to join, please sign up online before Friday, March 20."

**【解析】** 原文明确了报名方式：如果想参加，请在3月20日（星期五）之前在线报名（sign up online before Friday）。C 项"通过在周五前访问网站"（呼应后文的 visit our website）完全符合原文要求。A 项"周日去公园门口"活动当天才去属于迟到了，错过了报名截止时间；B 项"周六打电话"也晚于"周五前"的截止时间；D 项"在学校和老师谈谈"原文未提供此种报名方式。故选 C。
