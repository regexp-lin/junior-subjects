# 阅读理解 第1篇（应用文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

### Passage: Teen Weekend Skills Camp

① Do you want to learn useful life skills and make new friends? Blue Sky Youth Center will hold a **Teen Weekend Skills Camp** on April 12-13 for students aged 12-15.

② The camp has four workshops: Basic Cooking, Bike Repair, First Aid, and Public Speaking. Each student can choose **two** workshops. Every workshop has only 20 seats, so early booking is important.

③ Time plan:
Saturday 9:00-11:00 Workshop 1
Saturday 14:00-16:00 Team Game
Sunday 9:00-11:00 Workshop 2
Sunday 14:00-15:00 Show Time

④ Camp fee: 120 yuan (lunch and materials included). Students who bring their own water bottles can get 10 yuan back at the end of the camp. If you sign up before April 5, you can also get a camp T-shirt.

⑤ To sign up, send a message with your name, school, and phone number to 138-6677-2045. For safety reasons, each student must arrive with a parent on Saturday morning.

### 题目

1. Who can join the camp?
   A. College students aged 18.
   B. Teachers from local schools.
   C. Teenagers between 12 and 15.
   D. Children under 10 years old.

2. What should students do if they want to take part in the camp?
   A. Send personal information by message.
   B. Buy a bike from the youth center.
   C. Call after arriving on Saturday.
   D. Write a letter to the center.

3. Which activity is arranged on Saturday afternoon?
   A. Workshop 2.
   B. Show Time.
   C. Basic Cooking test.
   D. Team Game.

4. What does the underlined word "booking" in Paragraph ② probably mean?
   A. Cooking food.
   B. Making a reservation.
   C. Borrowing materials.
   D. Changing workshops.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To teach students how to give speeches.
   B. To share a student's camp diary.
   C. To introduce a weekend camp and invite students.
   D. To compare different youth centers.

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Blue Sky Youth Center will hold a Teen Weekend Skills Camp ... for students aged 12-15."

**【解析】** 原文明确写了年龄范围是 12-15 岁，所以 C 正确。A 项 18 岁超出范围；B 项文中没有提到教师参加；D 项 10 岁以下不符合条件，属于无中生有。故选 C。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1句 "To sign up, send a message with your name, school, and phone number..."

**【解析】** 报名方式是发送包含个人信息的短信，因此 A 正确。B 项买自行车与报名无关；C 项"到场后再打电话"不符合文意；D 项写信并未在文中出现。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段时间表 "Saturday 14:00-16:00 Team Game"

**【解析】** 时间表清楚标明周六下午是 Team Game，D 正确。A 项 Workshop 2 在周日上午；B 项 Show Time 在周日下午；C 项"烹饪测试"并未出现在安排中。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "Every workshop has only 20 seats, so early booking is important."

**【解析】** 前文说每个工作坊只有 20 个名额，因此 "early booking" 应指提前占位、提前预约，B 正确。A 项"做饭"只对应某一课程，不符合上下文；C 项"借材料"与 seats 无关；D 项"更换课程"不是该词本义。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第①段活动介绍与第⑤段报名方式

**【解析】** 文章给出活动时间、内容、费用、优惠和报名方式，目的是发布活动信息并吸引学生参加，C 正确。A 项只涉及其中一个工作坊；B 项不是日记体；D 项没有比较不同中心。故选 C。
