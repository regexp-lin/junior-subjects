# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

### Passage: Should Students Use AI Study Tools?

① Today, many middle school students use AI study tools on phones or computers. These tools can explain math steps, check grammar, and give quick answers. Some students say AI saves time and helps them study alone when parents are busy.

② Supporters believe AI can be a good "after-school helper." First, it gives students different ways to understand one problem. If a student does not understand a teacher's explanation, AI may offer a simpler one. Second, AI can give practice questions at different levels, so both strong and weak students can find suitable tasks.

③ However, not everyone agrees. Some teachers worry that students may copy answers without thinking. If students depend too much on AI, they may stop asking "why" and only look for "what." Learning then becomes faster but shallower. In long-term study, this habit can hurt real problem-solving ability.

④ There is also a safety question. To use some apps, students must share personal information, such as age, school, or learning records. Without proper rules, this may cause privacy problems. So schools and families should teach students to choose safe tools and never share unnecessary information online.

⑤ In my opinion, AI is neither a "magic teacher" nor a "dangerous enemy." It is a tool. A pencil can write a great story or copy homework; AI is the same. Students should first think by themselves, then use AI to check ideas and find mistakes. Used in this way, AI can support learning instead of replacing it.

**注释：** privacy 隐私；replace 代替

### 题目

1. What is one advantage of AI study tools mentioned in the passage?
   A. They can fully replace school teachers.
   B. They can help explain problems in different ways.
   C. They can make all students get full marks.
   D. They can shorten school class time.

2. What may happen if students depend too much on AI?
   A. They may think less deeply.
   B. They may become more creative.
   C. They may remember more facts.
   D. They may ask more questions.

3. Why does the writer mention "age, school, or learning records" in Paragraph ④?
   A. To show what data teachers need.
   B. To explain how to open an app quickly.
   C. To prove students should use more apps.
   D. To warn about personal information safety.

4. What does the writer think students should do first?
   A. Ask AI for full answers directly.
   B. Stop using AI tools completely.
   C. Think on their own before using AI.
   D. Share learning records with all apps.

5. Which of the following is the best title for the passage?
   A. AI in School: Use It Wisely
   B. Why Phones Should Be Banned
   C. The History of Computer Learning
   D. How to Become an App Designer

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "First, it gives students different ways to understand one problem."

**【解析】** 文中明确指出 AI 可以提供不同讲解方式，帮助理解同一问题，B 正确。A 项"完全替代老师"与第⑤段观点矛盾；C 项"人人满分"属于过度推断；D 项缩短课时在文中未提及。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段第2-4句 "students may copy answers without thinking... only look for 'what'... learning becomes faster but shallower."

**【解析】** 根据"不思考地抄答案"和"学习变浅"可推断：过度依赖 AI 会让学生思考不深入，A 正确。B、C 两项都在说学习能力提升，与原文担忧方向相反；D 项"问更多问题"也与"停止追问 why"相矛盾。故选 A。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1-3句 "There is also a safety question... this may cause privacy problems."

**【解析】** 作者列举年龄、学校、学习记录，是为了说明可能存在隐私风险，提醒信息安全，因此 D 正确。A 项把对象变成教师需求，偷换概念；B 项"快速开 app"与段落主旨无关；C 项"应使用更多 app"与原文谨慎态度不符。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第4句 "Students should first think by themselves, then use AI..."

**【解析】** 句中 directly 表达了先后顺序：先独立思考，再借助 AI 检查和纠错，C 正确。A 项"直接要完整答案"与作者态度相反；B 项"完全停用"过于绝对；D 项分享学习记录与安全建议矛盾。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其第⑤段 "It is a tool... support learning instead of replacing it."

**【解析】** 文章先写 AI 的好处，再写风险，最后给出"理性使用"的平衡观点，A 项最能概括主旨。B 项只强调禁用，片面；C 项是历史话题，文中未涉及；D 项讲职业设计，与文章中心无关。故选 A。
