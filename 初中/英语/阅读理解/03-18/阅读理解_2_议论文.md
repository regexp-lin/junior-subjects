# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① After a long day at school, how much free time do students really have? For many middle school students, the answer is "almost none." Between homework, after-school classes, and reading tasks, their evenings are often completely full. But should students have more free time? 

② Some parents and teachers believe that keeping students busy is good. They think that if teenagers have too much free time, they might spend it all playing video games or watching TV. To these adults, more study time means better grades and a brighter future. They worry that "free time" is just wasted time.

③ However, many child experts disagree. They argue that free time is not wasted time at all. It is essential for a teenager's development. When students have unstructured time — time without a plan or a teacher — they learn how to manage themselves. They can explore their own interests, whether it's drawing, playing a musical instrument, or just relaxing with a good book. 

④ Furthermore, teenagers' brains and bodies need a break. Continuous studying without rest can lead to stress and health problems. Just like sleep, free time allows the brain to rest and recharge. A student who has time to relax is often happier and more focused during school hours.

⑤ In conclusion, while studying is certainly important, it should not take up every minute of a student's day. Finding a balance is key. Giving students a little more free time might actually help them do better in the long run.

---

**注释：** unstructured 没有固定结构的，无计划的；manage 管理；explore 探索；continuous 连续不断的；recharge 充电，恢复精力

---

### 题目

1. According to Paragraph ①, why do many middle school students have almost no free time?
   A. Because they like studying the most.
   B. Because they spend all their time playing games.
   C. Because their evenings are full of homework and classes.
   D. Because their schools are too far from home.

2. Why do some adults want to keep students busy?
   A. They think it will lead to better grades and a brighter future.
   B. They want students to learn how to manage their time.
   C. They hope students will read more books about science.
   D. They don't want students to talk to their friends.

3. The underlined word "essential" in Paragraph ③ most probably means ________.
   A. dangerous
   B. very important
   C. boring
   D. difficult to find

4. According to child experts, what is a benefit of having unstructured time?
   A. Students can get higher scores in all exams.
   B. Students can learn to explore their own interests.
   C. Students will spend more time watching TV.
   D. Students can finish their homework much faster.

5. What is the writer's attitude towards free time for students?
   A. The writer thinks free time is a complete waste of time.
   B. The writer believes students should study every minute of the day.
   C. The writer thinks students should never do any homework.
   D. The writer supports giving students more free time to find a balance.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "Between homework, after-school classes, and reading tasks, their evenings are often completely full."

**【解析】** 原文指出，在作业、课后辅导班和阅读任务之间，他们的晚上通常是排满的，导致他们几乎没有自由时间。C 项与原文一致。A 项不符合实际，文中未提；B 项是父母担心的事，而非没有时间的原因；D 项"学校离家太远"属于无中生有。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第3句 "To these adults, more study time means better grades and a brighter future."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到，对这些成年人（父母和老师）来说，更多的学习时间意味着更好的成绩和更光明的未来。A 项与原文吻合。B 项是专家认为自由时间带来的好处，而不是大人让学生忙碌的原因；C 项和 D 项均属于无中生有。故选 A。

---

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第③段第2-3句 "They argue that free time is not wasted time at all. It is essential for a teenager's development."

**【解析】** 前文说专家认为自由时间"根本不是浪费时间"，后文举例说明自由时间可以让学生自我管理、探索兴趣。由此推断，这段时间对青少年的发展是"非常重要的"。"essential"的意思是"必不可少的，极其重要的"，B 项（very important）最贴切。A 项（危险的）、C 项（无聊的）和 D 项（难找的）都与句意逻辑相悖。故选 B。

---

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段最后一句 "They can explore their own interests..."

**【解析】** 专家在第③段解释"无计划时间"（unstructured time）的好处时指出，学生可以在这段时间里探索自己的兴趣（explore their own interests）。B 项与原文一致。A 项和 D 项未被专家提及；C 项是父母担忧的负面结果，而非专家眼中的好处。故选 B。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 观点态度

**【定位】** 第⑤段（总结段）全文基调 "Finding a balance is key. Giving students a little more free time might actually help them do better in the long run."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段总结了自己的观点：找到平衡是关键，给学生多一点自由时间长远来看实际上可能有助于他们表现得更好。这说明作者支持给学生更多自由时间以达到平衡。D 项准确表达了作者的支持态度。A 项和 B 项是部分大人的负面观点，与作者态度相反；C 项"学生永远不该做作业"过于绝对极端，原文说的是"寻找平衡"，并非完全不学习。故选 D。

**【技巧点拨】** 判断作者态度题（Writer's attitude）时，要重点关注文章首尾段落、总结性语句以及转折词（如 However）之后的内容。本作结尾的 "Finding a balance is key" 直接揭示了中立但偏向支持适度自由时间的态度。
