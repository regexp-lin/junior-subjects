# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① "Next Tuesday, everyone must give a five-minute speech about their favorite book," said Mr. Davis. When Liu Yan heard this, her heart sank. She loved reading, but she hated speaking in front of people. Whenever all eyes were on her, she would forget her words and her face would turn red.

② That evening, Liu Yan sat at her desk, looking at her favorite book, *The Little Prince*. She knew the story so well, but how could she talk about it in front of thirty classmates? Her older brother, who was in high school, noticed she was upset. "What’s wrong, Yan?" he asked. 

③ She told him about the speech. He smiled and said, "I used to be afraid of public speaking too. The secret is practice. Practice until the words flow out naturally." He offered to be her audience. For the next few days, Liu Yan practiced her speech every night. At first, she looked at her notes all the time. But slowly, she began to look up and make eye contact with her brother.

④ Finally, Tuesday arrived. When Mr. Davis called her name, her legs felt a little weak. But as she stood at the front of the classroom and started talking about the little prince and his rose, she remembered her brother's advice. She took a deep breath and spoke clearly. To her surprise, her classmates were listening carefully.

⑤ When she finished, everyone clapped loudly. Mr. Davis gave her a big smile. Liu Yan smiled back. The fear was still there, but she knew she could face it now.

---

**注释：** speech 演讲；sink 下沉；flow 流淌，顺畅地流出；audience 观众；eye contact 眼神交流

---

### 题目

1. How did Liu Yan feel when Mr. Davis announced the speech?
   A. Excited.
   B. Worried.
   C. Angry.
   D. Confident.

2. What was Liu Yan going to talk about in her speech?
   A. Her brother's high school life.
   B. A book she had just bought.
   C. Her favorite book, *The Little Prince*.
   D. How to become a better speaker.

3. How did Liu Yan prepare for her speech?
   A. She watched videos of famous speakers.
   B. She wrote a completely new story.
   C. She asked Mr. Davis for help after class.
   D. She practiced in front of her brother every night.

4. What happened when Liu Yan gave her speech in class?
   A. Her face turned red and she forgot all her words.
   B. Her classmates didn't listen to her at all.
   C. She was still a little nervous but spoke clearly.
   D. Her brother came to the classroom to help her.

5. What does the story mainly tell us?
   A. *The Little Prince* is a great book for students.
   B. Practice and courage can help us overcome our fears.
   C. Teachers should not ask shy students to give speeches.
   D. Older brothers always know how to solve problems.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2-4句 "When Liu Yan heard this, her heart sank... she hated speaking in front of people."

**【解析】** 当老师宣布要发表演讲时，刘燕的心"沉了下去"（heart sank），因为她讨厌在别人面前讲话，这说明她感到非常担忧和害怕。B 项（Worried，担忧的）最符合原文语境。A 项（兴奋的）、C 项（生气的）、D 项（自信的）均与原文描述的心情不符。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "...looking at her favorite book, *The Little Prince*." 及 第①段老师的要求 "give a five-minute speech about their favorite book".

**【解析】** 老师要求讲自己最喜欢的书，而刘燕最喜欢的书是《小王子》（*The Little Prince*）。C 项与原文完全一致。A、B、D 项均不是演讲的主题。故选 C。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第4-5句 "He offered to be her audience. For the next few days, Liu Yan practiced her speech every night."

**【解析】** 原文提到她的哥哥主动当她的观众，接下来的几天里刘燕每晚都在练习演讲。D 项与原文一致。A 项（看著名演讲者的视频）、B 项（写一个全新的故事）、C 项（课后向老师求助）原文均未提及，属无中生有。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-4句 "When Mr. Davis called her name, her legs felt a little weak. But... She took a deep breath and spoke clearly."

**【解析】** 当老师叫她名字时，她感觉腿有些发软（说明还是有点紧张），但深呼吸后她讲得很清晰。C 项符合原文描述。A 项（脸变红并忘词）是她一开始害怕发生的事（第①段），但实际上并未发生；B 项（同学根本不听）与原文"her classmates were listening carefully"矛盾；D 项（哥哥来教室帮她）属于无中生有。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文整体内容及第⑤段最后一句 "The fear was still there, but she knew she could face it now."

**【解析】** 文章讲述了害怕公开演讲的刘燕，在哥哥的鼓励下通过反复练习，最终成功完成演讲并克服了恐惧心理的故事。B 项"练习和勇气能帮助我们克服恐惧"最能概括文章的主旨。A 项太片面，只提到了书名；C 项显然错误，老师的要求最终帮助了她成长；D 项也是片面的细节，不是核心主旨。故选 B。
