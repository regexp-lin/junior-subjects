# 阅读理解（议论文）

① "Reading makes a full man," said the famous writer Francis Bacon. Today, however, many teenagers would rather play video games or watch short videos on their mobile phones than read a book. Some students argue that reading books is boring and outdated (过时的). But I strongly believe that reading habits are more important than ever in our modern lives.

② Firstly, reading books improves our concentration (专注力). When we watch short videos, the scenes change every few seconds. This makes our brains used to getting quick information without deep thinking. When reading a book, especially a novel or a science book, we must stay focused for a long time to follow the story and understand the logic (逻辑). Good concentration is a very useful skill for our study and future work.

③ Secondly, reading broadens (拓宽) our horizons. Through books, we can travel to different countries, experience ancient histories, and explore the universe without leaving our bedrooms. A boy sitting in a small town can learn about the busy streets of New York or the wild animals in Africa just by opening a book. Books give us a chance to see the world through other people's eyes.

④ Some teenagers complain that reading takes too much time. However, reading a few pages before bed is a wonderful way to relax. It calms the mind down after a busy day on digital screens and helps us sleep better.

⑤ In short, reading is not an outdated hobby. It is a powerful tool to build our brains and expand (扩大) our world. Every teenager should put down their phones for at least half an hour a day and pick up a good book.

**注释：** outdated 过时的；concentration 专注力；logic 逻辑；broaden 拓宽；horizon 眼界；expand 扩大

---

### 选择题

1. What do many teenagers prefer to do today according to paragraph ①?
A. Read thick science books.
B. Play video games or watch short videos on mobile phones.
C. Write letters to famous writers.
D. Go out to travel to different countries.

2. How do short videos affect our brains differently from reading?
A. They make our brains used to deep thinking for a long time.
B. They help us understand complex (复杂的) logic.
C. They make our brains used to getting quick information without deep thinking.
D. They improve our concentration to the highest level.

3. What does "reading broadens our horizons" mean in paragraph ③?
A. Reading makes our eyes bigger.
B. Reading helps us build a new bedroom.
C. Reading allows us to learn about the wide world and different cultures without leaving home.
D. Reading teaches us how to travel for free.

4. Why does the writer suggest reading a few pages before going to bed?
A. Because books are easier to see in the dark.
B. Because it is the only time teenagers have free time.
C. Because it tires the eyes quickly.
D. Because it calms the mind down and helps us sleep better.

5. What is the writer's final advice to teenagers in the passage?
A. To throw away all their mobile phones immediately.
B. To read books only when they have nothing else to do.
C. To read short videos instead of long novels.
D. To put down their phones and read a good book for at least half an hour a day.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Today, however, many teenagers would rather play video games or watch short videos on their mobile phones than read a book."

**【解析】** 开篇提到，比起读书，当今许多青少年宁愿（would rather）玩电子游戏或者在手机上看短视频（play video games or watch short videos on their mobile phones）。这与B项的陈述一致。A、C、D均非如今青少年的普遍首选。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节推断理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "...watch short videos, the scenes change every few seconds. This makes our brains used to getting quick information without deep thinking. When reading a book... we must stay focused for a long time..."

**【解析】** 短视频和看书对大脑的影响有何不同？原文解释说短视频场景几秒一变，使得大脑习惯于获取快速信息而缺乏深度思考（getting quick information without deep thinking）。而读书则需要长时间专注。因此C项准确阐述了短视频带来了怎样的不同影响。A、B、D都是读书带来的好处，而非短视频。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 句意理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "Through books, we can travel to different countries, experience ancient histories, and explore the universe without leaving our bedrooms."

**【解析】** “阅读拓宽了我们的视野”这句话的意思，紧接着的一句做了完美的解答：通过书本，我们足不出户就能游历各国、体验历史、探索宇宙。这与C选项（让我们足不出户就能了解广阔世界和不同文化）完全吻合。A（眼睛变大）、B（建新卧室）、D（免门票旅游）都是字面无厘头翻译。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "It calms the mind down after a busy day on digital screens and helps us sleep better."

**【解析】** 对于抱怨看书太费时间的人，作者建议睡前读几页。原因是什么？文中给出的理由是这能让在屏幕前忙碌了一天的大脑平静下来，并且有助于我们更好地睡眠（helps us sleep better）。选项D正是这个理由。其他选项文中并未提出。故选D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨意图理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后一句 "Every teenager should put down their phones for at least half an hour a day and pick up a good book."

**【解析】** 在文末总结处，作者向所有青少年发出了这篇议论文的最终倡议：每个青少年都应该每天至少放下手机半小时，拿起一本好书来读（put down their phones for at least half an hour a day and pick up a good book）。D项完全相符。A项（扔掉手机）走极端非作者意图。故选D。
