# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Grand Opening of Starry Cinema!**

② Are you a big fan of movies? Do you love eating popcorn while watching exciting stories on a giant screen? The wait is over! Starry Cinema is finally opening this weekend in the center of Star City. We invite you to join our Grand Opening Party!

③ **When is the Grand Opening?**
- **Date:** Saturday, May 1st to Sunday, May 2nd
- **Time:** 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM

④ **What makes Starry Cinema special?**
- **Super Big Screens:** We have the largest IMAX screen in the city. You will feel like you are inside the movie!
- **Comfortable Seats:** All our seats are soft leather chairs that can be leaned back (向后倾斜).
- **Delicious Snacks:** Besides traditional popcorn and cola, we also offer hot dogs, ice cream, and fresh fruit juice.

⑤ **Opening Weekend Special Offers (特惠):**
To celebrate our opening, we have some great gifts for our first customers!
- **Buy One Get One Free:** Buy one movie ticket, and get another one for free! (Only for movies before 5:00 PM).
- **Free Popcorn:** The first 100 customers each day will get a free box of sweet popcorn.
- **Lucky Draw (抽奖):** Every ticket has a number. We will draw three lucky numbers every night at 8:00 PM. The winners will get a free movie pass for a whole year!

⑥ **Where to find us?**
Address: No. 128, Main Street, Star City (Next to the Central Library)
For ticket booking, please visit our website: *www.starrycinema.com* or call 555-8899.

⑦ Come and enjoy the magic of movies with us this weekend!

**注释：** opening 开业；leather 皮革；offer 提供/特惠；customer 顾客；lucky draw 抽奖

---

### 选择题

1. Where is the Starry Cinema located?
A. Inside the Central Library.
B. Far away from Star City.
C. Next to the Central Library on Main Street.
D. On the second floor of a supermarket.

2. What is special about the seats in Starry Cinema according to paragraph ④?
A. They are made of hard wood.
B. They are soft leather chairs that can be leaned back.
C. They can move like a roller coaster.
D. They are all painted in bright yellow.

3. If you buy a movie ticket at 2:00 PM on the opening weekend, what special offer will you get?
A. You will get another movie ticket for free.
B. You will get a free hot dog and fresh fruit juice.
C. You will meet a famous movie star.
D. You will get a free movie pass for a whole year immediately.

4. Form paragraph ⑤, how can you get a free box of sweet popcorn?
A. By buying three tickets at the same time.
B. By winning the lucky draw at 8:00 PM.
C. By being one of the first 100 customers to enter each day.
D. By calling the cinema and singing a song.

5. How many lucky winners will get a free movie pass for a whole year every night?
A. One.
B. Two.
C. Three.
D. One hundred.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段 "Address: No. 128, Main Street, Star City (Next to the Central Library)"

**【解析】** 原文的地址栏中明确标出星空影院（Starry Cinema）位于主街128号，也就是“挨着中心图书馆（Next to the Central Library）”。并不是在它里面（A项错）。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2条 "Comfortable Seats: All our seats are soft leather chairs that can be leaned back (向后倾斜)."

**【解析】** 本题问电影院座位有何特别之处，原文解释说所有的座椅都是柔软的皮椅且可以向后倾斜（soft leather chairs that can be leaned back）。A硬木、C像过山车一样动、D全涂成亮黄均是无中生有。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1条特惠 "Buy One Get One Free... (Only for movies before 5:00 PM)."

**【解析】** 开业周末的特惠活动指出，5点之前的电影票可以享受“买一赠一（Buy One Get One Free）”。若你是下午2:00（在5点前）买票，自然就能获得另一张免费门票。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第2条特惠 "Free Popcorn: The first 100 customers each day will get a free box of sweet popcorn."

**【解析】** 关于如何获得免费的甜爆米花，原文的规定是：每天进入影院的前100名顾客（The first 100 customers）将会得到一盒。因此C项符合。A买三张票、B赢晚8点抽奖、D打电话唱歌皆不符合文中条件。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第3条特惠 "Lucky Draw (抽奖): ...We will draw three lucky numbers every night at 8:00 PM. The winners will get a free movie pass for a whole year!"

**【解析】** 每天晚上会有几位幸运儿获得全年免费观影卡？原文说每晚抽“三个（three）”幸运号码，赢家得年卡。所以数字是3。故选C。
