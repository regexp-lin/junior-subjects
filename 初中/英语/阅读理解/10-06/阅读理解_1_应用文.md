# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Community Sports Centre — Fitness Class Schedule (October 2024)**

② Welcome to the Community Sports Centre! We offer various fitness classes for all ages. All classes are held in the main hall. Please arrive 10 minutes before class. Wear comfortable sportswear and bring a water bottle. Members: free. Non-members: ¥30 per class.

③ **Weekly Schedule:**

| Day | Time | Class | For |
|-----|------|-------|-----|
| Monday | 7:00–7:45 a.m. | Morning Yoga | Adults |
| Monday | 4:30–5:30 p.m. | Kids Basketball | Children 8–12 |
| Tuesday | 6:00–6:45 p.m. | Zumba Dance | All ages |
| Wednesday | 7:00–7:45 a.m. | Tai Chi | Seniors (55+) |
| Wednesday | 5:00–6:00 p.m. | Teen Fitness | Teens 13–17 |
| Thursday | 6:00–6:45 p.m. | Zumba Dance | All ages |
| Friday | 4:30–5:30 p.m. | Kids Basketball | Children 8–12 |
| Saturday | 9:00–10:00 a.m. | Family Yoga | All ages |
| Sunday | 10:00–11:00 a.m. | Swimming Lesson | Children 6–12 |

④ **Notes:**
- Tai Chi and Morning Yoga are suitable for beginners. No experience needed.
- Swimming lessons require booking one week in advance. Call 0755-88881234.
- The centre is closed on public holidays. Check our website for updates.

⑤ **Address:** No. 88, Sports Road, Community Centre. Bus: No. 15, 28, 102. Get off at "Sports Centre" stop.

**注释：** Zumba 尊巴舞；Tai Chi 太极拳

---

**Questions:**

1. How much does a non-member pay for one fitness class?
   A. Free.
   B. ¥15.
   C. ¥30.
   D. ¥50.

2. Which class is for seniors (people 55 or older)?
   A. Morning Yoga.
   B. Kids Basketball.
   C. Tai Chi.
   D. Zumba Dance.

3. If a 14-year-old wants to join a fitness class, which day and time can he/she choose?
   A. Monday 4:30 p.m. — Kids Basketball.
   B. Wednesday 5:00 p.m. — Teen Fitness.
   C. Saturday 9:00 a.m. — Family Yoga.
   D. Both B and C.

4. What do participants need to do before a swimming lesson?
   A. Nothing special.
   B. Book one week in advance.
   C. Bring their own swimming pool.
   D. Be over 12 years old.

5. How can people get to the sports centre by bus?
   A. Take bus No. 5 only.
   B. Take bus No. 15, 28, or 102 and get off at "Sports Centre" stop.
   C. Take any bus.
   D. Buses do not go there.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Non-members: ¥30 per class"

**【解析】** 非会员每节课30元。C项正确。A为会员价格；B、D项与原文不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Wednesday | 7:00–7:45 a.m. | Tai Chi | Seniors (55+)"

**【解析】** 太极拳课面向55岁及以上老年人。C项正确。A、B、D项受众与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段表格：Teen Fitness 为 13–17 岁；Family Yoga 为 All ages

**【解析】** 14岁可参加 Teen Fitness（周三17:00）或 Family Yoga（周六9:00）。D项"Both B and C"正确。A项 Kids Basketball 为8–12岁；B、C单独不完整。故选D。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Swimming lessons require booking one week in advance"

**【解析】** 游泳课需提前一周预约。B项正确。A、C、D项与说明不符。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Bus: No. 15, 28, 102. Get off at 'Sports Centre' stop"

**【解析】** 可乘15、28、102路公交车，在"Sports Centre"站下车。B项准确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。
