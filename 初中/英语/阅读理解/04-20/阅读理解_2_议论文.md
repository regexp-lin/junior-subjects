> **04-20 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Mobile phones are everywhere today. Almost every middle school student has a smartphone. But should students be allowed (被允许) to use their mobile phones in school? This question has caused a lot of arguments between teachers, parents, and students. I strongly believe that mobile phones should not be allowed in the classroom.

② First of all, mobile phones are a big distraction (令人分心的事物). In a classroom, students should focus on what the teacher is saying. However, if a student has a phone on the desk, they will keep checking messages, looking at social media, or playing games. Even if the phone is on silent mode, the shaking of a new message can easily break a student's attention. A busy mind cannot learn well.

③ Second, allowing phones in school can lead to bad behavior. For example, some students might use their phones to cheat (作弊) during a test by searching for answers on the Internet or sending text messages to friends. Furthermore, taking pictures of other students without their permission (允许) and posting them online can cause serious bullying (欺凌) problems.

④ On the other hand, some students argue that they need phones for safety reasons or to call their parents after school. This is a reasonable point. But schools can easily solve this problem by asking students to leave their phones in their bags or lockers during class time. They can only use them after the school bell rings. If there is an emergency (紧急情况) during the day, parents can always call the school office.

⑤ In short, the school is a place for learning, not for playing on screens. Banning (禁止) mobile phones in the classroom is the best way to help students pay attention to their studies and communicate with real people face to face.

**注释：** allow 允许；distraction 分心；cheat 作弊；permission 允许；bullying 欺凌；emergency 紧急情况；ban 禁止

---

### 选择题

1. What is the writer's main opinion about mobile phones in paragraph ①?
A. Every student should buy an expensive smartphone.
B. Teachers should use mobile phones to teach classes.
C. Students should not be allowed to use mobile phones in the classroom.
D. Parents should buy more phones for their children.

2. Why are mobile phones a big "distraction" in the classroom?
A. Because they are heavy and take up too much space on the desk.
B. Because students might check messages or play games instead of listening to teachers.
C. Because they often ring loudly and make the teacher angry.
D. Because watching the phone screen is good for the eyes.

3. Form paragraph ③, what bad behavior might happen if students use phones during a test?
A. They might cheat by searching for answers.
B. They might take beautiful photos of the teacher.
C. They might listen to fast racing music.
D. They might fall asleep on the desk.

4. How does the writer suggest solving the problem of students needing phones for safety?
A. By letting students hold their phones in their hands all day.
B. By asking the parents to stay at the school gate.
C. By telling students never to bring phones to school.
D. By asking students to keep phones in bags during class and use them after school.

5. What does the writer believe is the major purpose (主要目的) of a school in the last paragraph?
A. A place for playing on screens.
B. A place for learning and communicating face to face.
C. A place for online searching and cheating.
D. A place for keeping bags and lockers safe.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解与主旨初探

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "I strongly believe that mobile phones should not be allowed in the classroom."

**【解析】** 开篇直接点破其观点并持反对态度：强烈认为智能手机不应该被允许（should not be allowed）带入教室。A项要求全买昂贵的手机、B项让大家使用手机及D项皆为歪曲。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段中 "However, if a student has a phone on the desk, they will keep checking messages, looking at social media, or playing games."

**【解析】** 手机这“大分心的东西”在文中的特指解释是：上课时学生不去听老师而在查看短信、刷社媒或打游戏。A项体积大沉重、D项屏幕护眼完全违背常理。C项说是大声鸣响，但文中强调了“即使静音模式（on silent mode）震动也会打断注意力”，与仅仅因为响声不同。故选B。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "For example, some students might use their phones to cheat (作弊) during a test by searching for answers on the Internet..."

**【解析】** 本段对不轨行为（bad behavior）着重进行了举例。首当其冲的就是一些学生可能在考试里作弊（cheat）用网络去搜题找答案。这跟A选项的意思如出一辙。B、C、D项均非作弊且亦无于段落提及。故选A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3-4句 "But schools can easily solve this problem by asking students to leave their phones in their bags or lockers during class time. They can only use them after the school bell rings."

**【解析】** 为了兼顾安全性考虑和课堂纪律，作者提出的解决建议是——上课期间把搜集放进书包和储物柜里；只能在放学铃响过后方可拿出来使用。A项全天拿着、B项让父母守定校门、C项禁止带来全是片面或是无中生有的策略。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解与主旨升华

**【定位】** 第⑤段末尾："In short, the school is a place for learning, not for playing on screens. Banning (禁止) mobile phones in the classroom is the best way to help students pay attention to their studies and communicate with real people face to face."

**【解析】** 在结语中作者清晰明确地给出了学校的职能：“学校是供学习使用的场所，不是在屏幕上玩乐的地方”；并且认为禁手机也是为了大家能够静心学习与“面对面真实的人交流”（communicate with real people face to face）。显然A（在屏幕上玩闹）、C（为作弊在线搜索的场所）、D项都没能抓住这句核心的宗旨。故选B。
