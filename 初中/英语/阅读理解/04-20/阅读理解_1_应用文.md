# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **City Central Library Rules & Information**

② Welcome to the City Central Library! We have added thousands of new books this year and hope you will enjoy reading here. To make our library a better place for everyone, please read the following rules carefully before you enter.

③ **Opening Hours:**
- Monday to Friday: 8:30 AM - 8:30 PM
- Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
*(Note: The library is closed on public holidays like New Year's Day and National Day.)*

④ **Borrowing Books:**
- Every reader must have a Library Card to borrow books. You can make a card at the front desk with your ID card and a $10 deposit (押金).
- You can borrow up to 5 books at one time.
- The borrowing time is 30 days. If you want to keep the books longer, you can renew (续借) them online for another 15 days.

⑤ **Returning Books:**
- Please return the books on time. If you bring them back late, you will have to pay a fine (罚款) of $0.50 per day for each book.
- If you lose or heavily damage (损坏) a book, you must pay for a new one.

⑥ **Library Rules:**
- Keep quiet in the reading rooms. Soft reading music is okay if you use earphones.
- Please do not eat or drink (except pure water) in the library to keep the books clean.
- Take care of your own bags and phones. The library is not responsible for any lost personal items.

⑦ For more information, please visit our website at *www.citycentrallibrary.com*. Happy reading!

**注释：** deposit 押金；renew 续借；fine 罚款；damage 损坏

---

### 选择题

1. When does the library open acting to paragraph ③ on a Saturday?
A. At 8:30 AM.
B. At 9:00 AM.
C. At 6:00 PM.
D. At 8:30 PM.

2. What do you need to borrow books from the library?
A. Ten dollars and a new book.
B. An ID card and a parent's letter.
C. A Library Card.
D. A computer and an earphone.

3. How many days can you keep a borrowed book if you DO NOT renew it?
A. 15 days.
B. 30 days.
C. 45 days.
D. 50 days.

4. What will happen if you return a book 2 days late?
A. You will not be allowed to enter the library anymore.
B. You must pay a $1.00 fine.
C. You must buy a new book for the library.
D. The library staff will call your parents.

5. Which of the following behavior (行为) is allowed (允许) in the reading rooms?
A. Eating noisy snacks.
B. Drinking hot coffee or sweet juice.
C. Listening to music with earphones.
D. Shouting to your friends across the table.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第二条 "Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM"

**【解析】** 根据原文写明每周六周日是早上9点成开馆。A项是工作日的；C项和D项都是早已经闭馆的傍晚和晚上时间。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第1条 "Every reader must have a Library Card to borrow books."

**【解析】** 在借阅须知里，首句话就明确规定每位读者必须拥有一张借阅卡（a Library Card）才能借书。需要用身份证和10元押金办理的只是此卡，但借书凭据是借阅卡。A是一本新书与十元、B有家长信要求、D有电脑及耳机等选项全非其意。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第④段第3条 "The borrowing time is 30 days. If you want to keep the books longer, you can renew (续借) them online for another 15 days."

**【解析】** 题目问在不续借（DO NOT renew it）的情况下能持有书多少天。原文说借阅期为30天。因此B项准确无误。A项15天为续期时长；C项45天为总的最长结合时长。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解与简单计算

**【定位】** 第⑤段第1条 "If you bring them back late, you will have to pay a fine (罚款) of $0.50 per day for each book."

**【解析】** 根据退还图书规则中的第一条指示：逾期每日每本需缴纳罚款 $0.50。本书中假设晚还两日，就是 $0.50 * 2 = $1.00。故选B。如果丢失或损坏才需要买新书（C）。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑥段头两条 "Keep quiet in the reading rooms. Soft reading music is okay if you use earphones. Please do not eat or drink (except pure water)..."

**【解析】** 图书馆守则指示允许使用头戴式耳机听播放低音量的柔和音乐（C项正是指这点）。A项（吃吵闹零食）、B项（饮用热咖啡或甜果汁，而原文明言除了纯净水外禁止餐饮）、D项（隔桌大叫，无视“Keep quiet”）等都明令不许。故选C。
