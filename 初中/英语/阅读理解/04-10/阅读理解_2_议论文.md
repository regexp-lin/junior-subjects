# 阅读理解（议论文）

① "Travel broadens the mind (旅行开阔视野)." We have often heard this saying. When holidays come, some students prefer throwing themselves into heavy homework or staying at home to play video games. However, I think reading mountains of books is not enough; we should step out of our homes and travel to different places.

② First, traveling teaches us things that we cannot learn from textbooks. Books can tell us how tall Mount Qomolangma is, or how long the Great Wall is. But until you actually climb the mountain or walk on the wall, you can never truly feel its greatness. Traveling lets us experience the world with our own eyes and feet, making the知识 (knowledge) from books come alive.

③ Second, traveling helps us understand and respect (尊重) different cultures. Every place has its own special food, language, and customs. When we visit a new city or country, we meet people who live differently from us. For example, eating with chopsticks in China or using a knife and fork in Europe. By experiencing these differences, we learn to be more open-minded and friendly to people from different backgrounds.

④ Finally, traveling makes us more independent and confident (自信的). During a trip, we might face some unexpected (意想不到的) problems, like getting lost or losing an umbrella. We have to learn how to solve these problems by ourselves. Every time we solve a problem successfully, we grow stronger.

⑤ For these reasons, I encourage everyone to pack a bag and travel whenever there is a chance. The world is a large, beautiful book. If you do not travel, you only read one page.

**注释：** broaden 开阔 (视野)；custom 习俗；open-minded 思想开明的；independent 独立的；unexpected 意想不到的

---

### 选择题

1. What do some students prefer doing during holidays according to paragraph ①?
A. Climbing high mountains.
B. Traveling to different places.
C. Doing a lot of homework or playing video games.
D. Reading about the Great Wall.

2. Why is traveling better than just reading textbooks, according to the writer?
A. Traveling costs less money than buying books.
B. Textbooks usually have wrong numbers or information.
C. We should never read textbooks when we travel.
D. Traveling lets us experience and feel the knowledge from books in the real world.

3. What does traveling teach us about different cultures?
A. It teaches us to hate other people's customs.
B. It teaches us to understand and respect them.
C. It shows us that everyone in the world eats exactly the same food.
D. It stops us from making friends with people from Europe.

4. Form paragraph ④, we can know that solving unexpected problems during a trip can make us _________.
A. angrier and poorer
B. more independent and confident
C. more worried and sad
D. weaker and lazier

5. What does the sentence "The world is a large, beautiful book. If you do not travel, you only read one page" mean?
A. The world is a real book made of paper and ink.
B. We should only read one page of a book every day to remember it better.
C. Only by traveling can we fully experience and understand the rich and wonderful world.
D. Books are more important and beautiful than real mountains and rivers.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "When holidays come, some students prefer throwing themselves into heavy homework or staying at home to play video games."

**【解析】** 原文中讲到一到假期，一些学生更喜欢投入繁重的作业中，或者待在家里玩电子游戏。A项和B项是作者倡导的内容，并非文中所述“些许(some)学生”假期里的偏好；D项未提及。故选C。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段中 "But until you actually climb... you can never truly feel its greatness. Traveling lets us experience the world with our own eyes and feet, making the knowledge from books come alive."

**【解析】** 作者对比了死读书和亲身经历后指出，旅行能让我们用自己的眼睛和脚去真实感受和体验世界，让书本知识鲜活起来（come alive）。A项花费少、B项教科书信息错误、C项旅途决不看教科书，这些在文中均没有根据，或者说法太片面偏激。故选D。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第1句 "Second, traveling helps us understand and respect (尊重) different cultures."

**【解析】** 在谈论文化时，文章表明旅行帮助我们理解和尊重（understand and respect）不同的文化，让我们能心胸开阔（open-minded）。A（讨厌别人习俗）、C（食物完全相同）、D（不和欧洲人交友）这三项恰恰是与文章倡导的“包容、尊重差异”的中心意思背道而驰的歪理。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第④段起首部分 "Finally, traveling makes us more independent and confident." 以及后续在讲述如何解决突发事件时的照应。

**【解析】** 第④段全段就是论证在旅途中遇到如迷路等突发事件（unexpected problems）并依靠自己去解决它，这样能让我们变得更独立和自信。A项（更生气更穷）、C项（更担心更伤心）、D项（更虚弱更懒惰）都是负面的干扰词。故选B。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断 / 句意理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段最后两句。

**【解析】** “世界是一本巨大而美丽的书，如果你不旅行，你只读了其中的一页。”这句话是比喻，意思是如果你总待在一个地方，你就无法领略外面世界的广阔与精彩。“你只读了一页”寓意着你的见识和经历被局限了。因此C选项“只有通过旅行，我们才能充分体验和理解这个丰富美好的世界”最准确地表达了这句名言的含义。A项（纸质真书）、B项（每天只读一页）、D项（书比山河更重要）均是对这句话字面或者错误逻辑的误读。故选C。
