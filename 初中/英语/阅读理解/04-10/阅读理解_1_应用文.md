> **04-10 第1篇**

# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **To:** All Students of Class 4, Grade 8
**From:** Sally, the Art Club Leader
**Date:** April 8

② Dear classmates,

Our school's Art Club is going to hold a special Students' Art Exhibition (展览) next week. We are writing to invite all of you to come and enjoy the beautiful artworks created by our talented (有天赋的) students.

③ **What to expect (期待什么)?**
- More than 100 excellent paintings, drawings, and paper-cutting works (剪纸作品) will be shown.
- A voting box (投票箱) will be set up. You can vote for your favorite piece of art. The winner will get the "Best Young Artist" prize!
- Free snacks and juice will be provided for everyone.

④ **Time and Place:**
- **Time:** Next Wednesday, April 15th, from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM. (Right after school ends.)
- **Place:** The School Hall on the first floor.

⑤ **How to join?**
You don't need to buy a ticket. It is completely free! However, please let me know if you can come before this Friday so that we can prepare enough food and drinks. You can just reply (回复) to this email.

⑥ We hope to see you there! Let's celebrate creativity and art together!

Best wishes,
Sally

**注释：** exhibition 展览；talented 有天赋的；voting box 投票箱；reply 回复

---

### 选择题

1. Who wrote this email?
A. A teacher of Class 4.
B. The headmaster of the school.
C. Sally, the leader of the Art Club.
D. A famous young artist.

2. What can students do at the Students' Art Exhibition?
A. Learn how to draw beautiful pictures.
B. Buy some paper-cutting works cheaply.
C. Vote for their favorite artwork.
D. Play loud music with the Art Club members.

3. When will the exhibition be held?
A. On April 8.
B. Next Wednesday, April 15th, from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM.
C. This Friday afternoon.
D. On Monday morning before classes start.

4. How much is the ticket for the exhibition?
A. $5.
B. $10.
C. $20.
D. It's free.

5. Why does Sally ask students to reply before this Friday?
A. Because the tickets will be sold out quickly.
B. Because she needs to prepare enough snacks and juice.
C. Because there is not enough room in the School Hall.
D. Because the "Best Young Artist" prize needs to be given out on Friday.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段邮件开头 "From: Sally, the Art Club Leader" 以及结尾落款 "Sally"。

**【解析】** 原文明确表示发件人（From）和写邮件的人是艺术俱乐部负责人萨莉（Sally）。A项老师、B项校长、D项著名年轻艺术家均与原文信息不符。故选C。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2分句 "A voting box (投票箱) will be set up. You can vote for your favorite piece of art."

**【解析】** 本题考查学生在展览上能做的事（What to expect?）。原文明确提到同学们可以为自己最喜欢的艺术作品投票（vote for...）。A项学画画、B项买便宜打折商品、D项玩大声的音乐，文中均无提及或与原文违背（如展览纯展示并无买卖）。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第一条 "Time: Next Wednesday, April 15th, from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM."

**【解析】** 在时间与地点板块，白纸黑字写着是下周三下午3点半到5点半（放学后）。A项是这封邮件写信的日期；C项和D项也同样与真实时间不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段首句 "You don't need to buy a ticket. It is completely free!"

**【解析】** 明确说到不需要买票，这是完全免费的（completely free）。A、B、C项任何收费数额皆不对。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段第3句 "...please let me know if you can come before this Friday so that we can prepare enough food and drinks."

**【解析】** 原文中 “so that (为了)” 后面接的正是要求周五前回复的原因——“以便我们准备足够的食物和饮料”（即第③段提及的snacks and juice）。A项（票卖光）与免费无门票矛盾；C、D也全都不是文中的理由。故选B。
