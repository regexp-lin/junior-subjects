# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Welcome to Our School Psychological Counseling Room**

② Are you feeling stressed about exams or worried about making friends? Do you sometimes feel sad or angry and don't know who to talk to? Our school has opened a psychological counseling room (心理咨询室) to help you. It is a safe place where you can share your thoughts and feelings.

③ **Location and time:** The room is on the third floor of the teaching building, next to the library. It is open every weekday from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. You can come during the lunch break or after class. No need to make an appointment (预约) in advance—just knock on the door.

④ **What we offer:** Our counselors (咨询师) are friendly and professional. They will listen to you carefully and keep your talk private. They can help you with exam stress, friendship problems, family difficulties, or any worry you have. Everything you say stays between you and the counselor.

⑤ **How to get help:** If you feel nervous about going alone, you can ask a trusted teacher or friend to go with you. Remember, asking for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. We are here to support you.

**注释：** counseling 咨询；appointment 预约；counselor 咨询师

---

### 选择题

1. Who can use the school psychological counseling room?
   A. Any student who needs someone to talk to.
   B. Only teachers and parents.
   C. Only students with serious problems.
   D. Only students from Grade 9.

2. Where is the counseling room?
   A. On the first floor, next to the gym.
   B. On the third floor, next to the library.
   C. In the school gate building.
   D. In the students' dormitory (宿舍).

3. According to the passage, when is the counseling room open?
   A. Only on weekends.
   B. Only in the morning.
   C. Only in the evening.
   D. Every weekday from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

4. What does the passage say about the things students talk about with the counselor?
   A. The counselor will tell the teacher everything.
   B. The counselor will write a report for the school.
   C. Students must share their talk with parents.
   D. Everything stays private between the student and the counselor.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The school cares about students' mental health and wants to support them.
   B. Students who ask for help are weak.
   C. The counseling room is only for exam stress.
   D. Students must make an appointment before visiting.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Our school has opened a psychological counseling room to help you... where you can share your thoughts and feelings."

**【解析】** 文章介绍学校心理咨询室面向有需要倾诉的学生开放，未限定年级或问题严重程度。A项"任何需要倾诉的学生"准确。B项仅教师家长、C项仅严重问题学生、D项仅初三学生均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "The room is on the third floor of the teaching building, next to the library."

**【解析】** 文中明确说明心理咨询室在教学楼三楼，图书馆旁边。B项正确。A项一楼体育馆旁、C项校门口、D项宿舍均与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "It is open every weekday from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m."

**【解析】** 文中明确写出开放时间为每个工作日上午9点至下午5点。D项正确。A项仅周末、B项仅上午、C项仅晚上均与原文不符。故选D。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "They will listen to you carefully and keep your talk private... Everything you say stays between you and the counselor."

**【解析】** 文中强调咨询师会保守秘密，学生所说内容仅限学生与咨询师之间。D项正确。A项告知老师、B项写报告给学校、C项必须分享给家长均与"private/stays between"矛盾。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段、第⑤段 "Our school has opened... to help you" "We are here to support you" "asking for help is a sign of strength"

**【解析】** 学校开设心理咨询室、强调"support you"、肯定求助是勇敢表现，可推知学校关心学生心理健康并希望提供支持。A项正确。B项与"sign of strength"矛盾；C项仅限考试压力过于狭隘；D项与"No need to make an appointment"矛盾。故选A。
