# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## Learning Patience from a Flower

① Li Ming was always in a hurry. He wanted everything to happen at once. When he did his homework, he gave up quickly if a question was difficult. His teacher, Mr. Wang, noticed this. One day, he gave Li Ming a small packet of sunflower seeds (种子) and said, "Try to grow a flower. Come back and tell me what you learn."

② Li Ming did not know how to grow flowers. He asked his grandfather for help. His grandfather taught him to put the seeds in soft soil, water them a little every day, and put the pot in a sunny place. "But when will the flower appear?" Li Ming asked. "You have to wait," his grandfather said with a smile. "Flowers need time."

③ For the first few days, Li Ming watered the seeds every morning. Nothing happened. He felt bored and wanted to stop. "Maybe the seeds are dead," he thought. His grandfather reminded him, "Patience, Li Ming. Good things take time." Li Ming decided to keep trying. After about two weeks, a small green shoot (嫩芽) appeared. Li Ming was so excited! He ran to show his grandfather.

④ As the days passed, the plant grew taller and taller. Li Ming kept watering it and watching it carefully. One morning, he saw a yellow bud (花蕾). A few days later, the bud opened into a bright sunflower. Li Ming felt proud. He had never waited for something for so long before. "I understand now," he told Mr. Wang. "You cannot rush a flower. You have to be patient."

⑤ From that day on, Li Ming changed. When he met difficult questions, he did not give up easily. He would think quietly and try again. "Growing a flower taught me patience," he said. "And patience helps me in everything I do."

**注释：** seed 种子；shoot 嫩芽；bud 花蕾

### 选择题

1. Why did Mr. Wang give Li Ming some sunflower seeds?
   A. Because he wanted Li Ming to learn something through growing a flower.
   B. Because Li Ming liked eating seeds.
   C. Because the school had a gardening competition.
   D. Because Li Ming asked for them.

2. What did Li Ming's grandfather tell him about growing flowers?
   A. Flowers grow in one day.
   B. Put seeds in soil, water them every day, and put the pot in the sun — flowers need time.
   C. Only water the seeds once.
   D. Keep the pot in a dark place.

3. How did Li Ming feel when nothing happened for the first few days?
   A. Very excited and happy.
   B. Proud and confident.
   C. Angry with his grandfather.
   D. Bored and wanted to stop.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Li Ming?
   A. He learned that patience is important and it takes time for good things to happen.
   B. He did not care about the sunflower at all.
   C. He never wanted to grow flowers again.
   D. He thought Mr. Wang's idea was useless.

5. What change did Li Ming have after growing the flower?
   A. He stopped doing homework.
   B. He forgot what he had learned.
   C. He became more patient and did not give up easily when facing difficulties.
   D. He only liked gardening from then on.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "His teacher, Mr. Wang, noticed this. One day, he gave Li Ming a small packet of sunflower seeds (种子) and said, 'Try to grow a flower. Come back and tell me what you learn.'"

**【解析】** 王老师发现李明做事急躁，于是给他葵花籽让他种花，并期待他从中有所收获。A 项正确。B 项 "喜欢吃种子"、C 项 "园艺比赛"、D 项 "李明索要" 均未提及。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "His grandfather taught him to put the seeds in soft soil, water them a little every day, and put the pot in a sunny place... 'You have to wait,' his grandfather said with a smile. 'Flowers need time.'"

**【解析】** 爷爷教李明将种子放入松软泥土、每日适量浇水、置于阳光处，并强调花需要时间生长。B 项正确。A 项 "一天长成" 与 need time 矛盾；C 项 "只浇一次水" 错误；D 项 "放在阴暗处" 与 sunny place 矛盾。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "For the first few days... Nothing happened. He felt bored and wanted to stop. 'Maybe the seeds are dead,' he thought."

**【解析】** 最初几天毫无变化，李明感到无聊并想放弃，还怀疑种子已死。D 项正确。A 项 "非常兴奋" 与 bored 矛盾；B 项 "骄傲自信" 为 later 阶段；C 项 "对爷爷生气" 未提及。故选 D。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "He had never waited for something for so long before. 'I understand now,' he told Mr. Wang. 'You cannot rush a flower. You have to be patient.'"

**【解析】** 李明首次长时间等待并看到花开，告诉老师他明白了：不能急于求成，必须有耐心。可推知他领悟到耐心的重要性。A 项正确。B、C、D 项与原文态度相反。故选 A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解 / 主旨理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "From that day on, Li Ming changed... When he met difficult questions, he did not give up easily... 'Growing a flower taught me patience,' he said. 'And patience helps me in everything I do.'"

**【解析】** 李明此后变得更有耐心，遇到难题不再轻易放弃，并将种花所得的耐心应用到日常生活中。C 项正确。A 项 "不做作业"、B 项 "忘记所学"、D 项 "只喜欢园艺" 均与原文不符。故选 C。
