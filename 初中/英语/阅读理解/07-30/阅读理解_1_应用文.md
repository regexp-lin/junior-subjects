# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Table Tennis Training Camp 2025**

② Do you love table tennis? Do you want to improve your skills during the summer holiday? Join our Summer Table Tennis Training Camp! The camp will run from July 15 to August 10. It is open to students aged 12 to 16. Our experienced coaches will help you learn basic skills and advanced techniques.

③ **Schedule and Activities:** Training takes place from 9:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m., Monday to Friday. Every morning, we do warm-up exercises first. Then we practise serving (发球), receiving, and playing matches. On Fridays, we hold friendly games. Students can also make new friends who share the same interest.

④ **What to Bring:** Please wear sports clothes and bring your own racket if you have one. The camp provides balls. Bring a water bottle and a small towel. Lunch is not included; students should eat at home or bring their own lunch.

⑤ **How to Register:** The fee is 800 yuan for the full camp. Early registration before July 1 gets a 50-yuan discount. To register, visit our school website or call 020-12345678. Places are limited to 40 students. Sign up soon!

**注释：** racket 球拍；register 报名

---

### 选择题

1. Who can join the Summer Table Tennis Training Camp?
   A. Any student who likes sports.
   B. Only students from the host school.
   C. Adults who want to learn table tennis.
   D. Students aged 12 to 16 who are interested in table tennis.

2. When does the training take place each day?
   A. From 9:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. on weekdays.
   B. From 2:00 p.m. to 4:30 p.m.
   C. Only on weekends.
   D. All day from morning to evening.

3. What special activity do students have on Fridays?
   A. They watch professional matches.
   B. They meet famous players.
   C. They take a rest.
   D. They hold friendly games.

4. How can students save 50 yuan on the camp fee?
   A. By bringing their own lunch.
   B. By bringing their own racket.
   C. By joining with a friend.
   D. By registering before July 1.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The camp provides lunch for all students.
   B. The camp is mainly for beginners who have never played table tennis.
   C. Students need to register quickly because there are limited places.
   D. Training only focuses on basic skills.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "It is open to students aged 12 to 16"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明训练营面向12至16岁学生开放。D项正确。A项年龄范围过宽；B项"仅限主办学校"未提及；C项成年人不符合条件。故选D。

**2. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Training takes place from 9:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m., Monday to Friday"

**【解析】** 训练时间为周一至周五上午9:00至11:30。A项准确。B、C、D项时间或频率均与原文不符。故选A。

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "On Fridays, we hold friendly games"

**【解析】** 周五会举行友谊赛。D项正确。A项观看职业比赛、B项见著名运动员、C项休息均未在文中出现。故选D。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Early registration before July 1 gets a 50-yuan discount"

**【解析】** 7月1日前报名可享受50元优惠。D项正确。A、B、C项与折扣条件无关。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Places are limited to 40 students. Sign up soon!"

**【解析】** 文中强调名额仅限40人并提示"尽快报名"，可推知学生需尽早报名以免错过机会。C项正确。A项午餐由营地提供与"Lunch is not included"矛盾；B项"仅限从未打过球的初学者"过于片面；D项"仅练基本功"与"basic skills and advanced techniques"矛盾。故选C。
