# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Accidents can happen at any time — at school, on the street, or even at home. When someone is hurt, the first few minutes are very important. This is why some people believe that teenagers should learn first aid (急救). But not everyone agrees. Should first aid training be a part of every teenager's education?

② Those who support the idea say that learning first aid can save lives. If a classmate suddenly falls down and stops breathing, a student who knows CPR (心肺复苏) could keep the person alive until an ambulance (救护车) arrives. In fact, studies show that bystanders (旁观者) who know first aid can greatly increase a patient's chance of survival (存活). Also, learning first aid helps teenagers stay calm in emergencies instead of panicking.

③ Some people, however, think first aid training is not suitable for teenagers. They argue that young people may not be strong enough or mature (成熟的) enough to handle real emergencies. A wrong action could even make the situation worse. Besides, schools already have many subjects to teach, and adding first aid training would take up valuable class time.

④ Others suggest a middle way. Instead of requiring full medical training, schools could teach basic skills like how to stop bleeding (止血), how to treat a burn, and when to call 120 for help. These simple steps are easy to learn and could make a big difference in an emergency.

⑤ In my opinion, every teenager should learn at least the basics of first aid. We do not need to become doctors, but knowing how to help someone in trouble is a life skill that everyone should have. A few hours of training could one day save a life — and that is worth every minute.

**注释：** first aid 急救；CPR 心肺复苏；ambulance 救护车；bystander 旁观者；survival 存活；mature 成熟的；bleeding 止血

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ②, why do some people support first aid training for teenagers?
   A. Because it can help teenagers get better grades.
   B. Because learning first aid can save lives.
   C. Because it is required by law in most countries.
   D. Because it makes teenagers physically stronger.

2. What does the word "panicking" in paragraph ② most probably mean?
   A. Laughing loudly.
   B. Feeling very scared and losing control.
   C. Running away quickly.
   D. Falling asleep suddenly.

3. What is one argument against first aid training for teenagers in paragraph ③?
   A. Teenagers are too busy with homework to learn anything new.
   B. First aid training is too expensive for schools.
   C. Young people may not be mature enough to handle real emergencies.
   D. Doctors do not think first aid is useful.

4. What does paragraph ④ suggest as a middle way?
   A. Only high school students should learn first aid.
   B. Schools should hire doctors to teach first aid.
   C. Schools could teach basic and simple first aid skills.
   D. Teenagers should learn first aid from their parents at home.

5. What is the writer's main opinion about first aid training?
   A. It is a waste of time for teenagers.
   B. Only adults should learn first aid.
   C. Every teenager should learn at least the basics of first aid.
   D. First aid training should replace science classes.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "Those who support the idea say that learning first aid can save lives."

**【解析】** 原文直接指出支持者认为学习急救可以挽救生命。A 项"提高成绩"文中未提及；C 项"法律要求"属于无中生有；D 项"增强体质"与急救培训无关。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "learning first aid helps teenagers stay calm in emergencies instead of panicking."

**【解析】** 前文说学急救能帮助青少年在紧急情况下保持冷静，"instead of panicking"表示与保持冷静相反的状态，因此 panicking 意为"极度恐慌、失去控制"。A 项"大声笑"、C 项"快速逃跑"和 D 项"突然睡着"均不符合语境。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "They argue that young people may not be strong enough or mature enough to handle real emergencies."

**【解析】** 反对者认为年轻人可能不够成熟，无法应对真正的紧急情况。A 项"作业太忙"原文未提及；B 项"费用太高"属于无中生有；D 项"医生不认为急救有用"文中未提及。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Instead of requiring full medical training, schools could teach basic skills like how to stop bleeding, how to treat a burn, and when to call 120 for help."

**【解析】** 第④段建议学校教授简单的基本急救技能，而非完整的医疗培训。A 项"只有高中生"原文未限定年级；B 项"聘请医生教课"属于无中生有；D 项"在家由父母教"与原文不符。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, every teenager should learn at least the basics of first aid."

**【解析】** 作者在结论段明确表示每个青少年至少应该学习急救基础知识。A 项"浪费时间"与作者观点相反；B 项"只有成年人应该学"原文未这样说；D 项"急救取代科学课"属于过度推断。故选 C。
