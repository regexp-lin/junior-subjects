# 阅读理解 第1篇（说明文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Have you ever heard of a "carbon footprint" (碳足迹)? You can't see it on the ground like a real footprint, but everyone leaves one on the Earth. A carbon footprint is the amount of carbon dioxide (CO2) we produce in our daily lives. 

② When we do things like driving a car, using electricity, or even buying new clothes, we add CO2 to the air. Too much CO2 in the air builds up like a blanket, keeping the Sun's heat inside the Earth's atmosphere. This causes global warming, which makes ice melt and sea levels rise, bringing danger to animals like polar bears and penguins.

③ The size of your carbon footprint depends on how you live. For example, if you ride to school in a private car every day, your footprint is large. If you take a bus or ride a bike, your footprint is much smaller. Also, eating local food creates a smaller footprint than eating food brought from other countries, because transporting food by planes or ships produces a lot of CO2.

④ How can we reduce our carbon footprint? It is actually quite simple. First, turn off lights, computers, and TVs when you are not using them. Second, try to walk, bike, or take public transport instead of asking your parents to drive you. Third, recycle paper, plastic, and glass to save energy. Finally, plant a tree! Trees are great helpers because they can absorb (吸收) CO2 from the air and give us fresh oxygen.

⑤ We only have one Earth. By making small changes in our daily habits, everyone can help reduce their carbon footprint and protect our beautiful planet for the future.

---

**注释：** carbon dioxide 二氧化碳；atmosphere 大气层；melt 融化；transport 运输；oxygen 氧气

---

### 题目

1. What is a "carbon footprint" according to the passage?
   A. A real footprint you leave on the dirt ground.
   B. A type of blanket that keeps the Earth warm.
   C. The amount of carbon dioxide we produce every day.
   D. The number of trees a person plants in their life.

2. What happens to the Earth when there is too much CO2 in the air?
   A. The air becomes fresher for people to breathe.
   B. Global warming happens and ice begins to melt.
   C. Polar bears and penguins grow stronger.
   D. The Earth's temperature drops very quickly.

3. Which of the following activities creates the SMALLEST carbon footprint?
   A. Riding a bike to school.
   B. Taking a private car to school.
   C. Buying food transported from other countries.
   D. Leaving the TV on all day long.

4. Why are trees called "great helpers" in Paragraph ④?
   A. Because they provide fruit for animals to eat.
   B. Because they can give us shade (阴凉) in summer.
   C. Because they can stop cars from making too much noise.
   D. Because they absorb CO2 and release oxygen.

5. What is the writer's main purpose in writing this passage?
   A. To teach teenagers how to plant trees in their gardens.
   B. To introduce what a carbon footprint is and how to reduce it.
   C. To explain why taking a bus is always better than driving a car.
   D. To ask people to stop eating food from other countries.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "A carbon footprint is the amount of carbon dioxide (CO2) we produce in our daily lives."

**【解析】** 原文明确解释了"碳足迹"是我们日常生活中产生的二氧化碳（CO2）的数量。C 项与原文解释完全一致。A 项是我们在地上留下的真实脚印，与首句"You can't see it on the ground like a real footprint"相反；B 项是文中对 CO2 聚集的生动比喻；D 项与"碳足迹"定义无关。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后一句 "This causes global warming, which makes ice melt and sea levels rise..."

**【解析】** 原文指出空气中过多的 CO2 会导致全球变暖（global warming），进而导致冰层融化（ice melt）和海平面上升。B 项与原文事实一致。A 项、C 项、D 项（气温快速下降）都与文中提到的危害截然相反。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段第3句 "If you take a bus or ride a bike, your footprint is much smaller." 及第④段建议不要一直开电视。

**【解析】** 题目问哪项活动产生的碳足迹"最小"（SMALLEST）。文中说乘私家车（B项）碳足迹大，买国外运来的食物（C项）会产生很多 CO2，电视一直开着（D项违背了在不用时关掉它的建议）也会浪费能源。而"骑自行车"（A项）被文中特别指出会使碳足迹变小，是低碳环保的行为。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段最后一句 "Trees are great helpers because they can absorb CO2 from the air and give us fresh oxygen."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明树木被称为"好帮手"是因为它们能吸收空气中的二氧化碳，并给我们提供新鲜的氧气。D 项与原文完全一致。A 项（提供水果）、B 项（提供阴凉）、C 项（阻挡噪音）虽然也是树木可能的好处，但文章并未提及，不构成此处的依据。故选 D。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文整体结构。第①自然段引出概念，第②段讲原理与危害，第③段举例影响大小，第④段给出建议，第⑤段总结呼吁。

**【解析】** 作者撰写此文的目的是全面介绍"碳足迹"这一概念，包括它的含义、危害、影响因素，并重点给出如何减少碳足迹的建议。B 项"介绍什么是碳足迹以及如何减少它"最精准全面。A 项只片面涉及第④段提到的一条建议（种树）；C 项（解释乘公交比开车好）和 D 项（叫人不要吃外国食物）都是局部的例子，不能作为全文主旨。故选 B。
