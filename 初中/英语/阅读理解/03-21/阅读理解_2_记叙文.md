> **03-21 第2篇**

# 阅读理解 第2篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① Mother's Day was coming, and I wanted to give my mom a special surprise. Instead of buying a gift, I decided to cook a big dinner for her. I found a recipe (食谱) for tomato and egg noodles online. It looked very easy: chop tomatoes, fry eggs, mix them together, and boil noodles. "A piece of cake!" I thought to myself.

② On Sunday afternoon, while my mom was resting in the living room, I sneaked into the kitchen. I washed the tomatoes and started cutting them. But the knife slipped, and tomato juice splashed all over my white T-shirt. Then I turned on the stove to fry the eggs. Since it was my first time, I was too afraid to get close to the hot oil. I threw the eggs into the pan from far away. The hot oil splashed everywhere, making a terrible mess. 

③ Worst of all, I put too much salt in the noodles. When I finally brought the bowls to the dining table, the kitchen looked like a battlefield. My mom walked in, and her eyes went wide. She looked at the messy kitchen, my dirty T-shirt, and the not-so-good noodles. I lowered my head, feeling like a complete failure. "I'm sorry, Mom. I ruined your Mother's Day," I whispered.

④ But my mom didn't get angry. She sat down, picked up her chopsticks, and tasted the noodles. Even though they were too salty, she ate half a bowl and smiled warmly. "Thank you, sweetheart," she said gently. "This is the most thoughtful gift I've ever received. But next time, maybe we can cook together."

⑤ I hugged her tightly. The dinner was a disaster, but it taught me something important: the value of a gift lies not in how perfect it is, but in the love behind it.

---

**注释：** recipe 食谱；sneak 悄悄溜进；splash 泼溅，飞溅；stove 炉子；battlefield 战场；disaster 灾难

---

### 题目

1. What did the writer decide to do for Mother's Day?
   A. Buy an expensive gift.
   B. Clean the living room.
   C. Cook a big dinner.
   D. Write a long letter.

2. Why did the hot oil splash everywhere?
   A. Because the writer put too much salt in it.
   B. Because the writer threw the eggs into the pan from far away.
   C. Because the writer used a wet knife to cut the tomatoes.
   D. Because the pan was too small for the eggs.

3. How did the writer feel when the mom saw the kitchen and the noodles?
   A. Proud and happy.
   B. Surprised and excited.
   C. Angry and tired.
   D. Sorry and very disappointed in oneself.

4. What did the mom do after seeing the mess and tasting the noodles?
   A. She shouted at the writer and refused to eat.
   B. She told the writer to clean the kitchen right away.
   C. She ate some noodles, smiled, and thanked the writer.
   D. She threw the salty noodles into the rubbish bin.

5. What does the story mainly tell us?
   A. Cooking noodles is much harder than cleaning a house.
   B. Mothers should always teach their children how to cook.
   C. Children should not go into the kitchen without their parents.
   D. Love is the most important part of any gift, even an imperfect one.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第2句 "Instead of buying a gift, I decided to cook a big dinner for her."

**【解析】** 原文明说作者决定为妈妈做一顿丰盛的晚餐，而不是买礼物。C 项与原文一致。A 项与"Instead of buying a gift"矛盾；B、D 项在文中均未涉及。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第4-6句 "Since it was my first time, I was too afraid to get close to the hot oil. I threw the eggs into the pan from far away. The hot oil splashed everywhere..."

**【解析】** 原文表明，因为是第一次做饭不敢靠近热油，作者是从远处把鸡蛋扔进锅里的，这就导致了热油四溅。B 项准确反映了这一原因。A 项放盐过多是煮面的问题并非油溅的原因；C、D 项均属无中生有。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段最后两句 "I lowered my head, feeling like a complete failure. 'I'm sorry, Mom. I ruined your Mother's Day,' I whispered."

**【解析】** 当妈妈看到一团糟的厨房和面条时，作者低下了头觉得自己完全是个失败者，并小声道歉说毁了母亲节。这表明作者感到非常抱歉且对自己很失望。D 项"抱歉且对自己非常失望"最准确地描绘了作者当时的感受。A、B、C 项都与"lowered my head"和"complete failure"的情感状态相反。故选 D。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2-3句 "She sat down, picked up her chopsticks, and tasted the noodles. Even though they were too salty, she ate half a bowl and smiled warmly. 'Thank you, sweetheart,' she said gently."

**【解析】** 妈妈并没有生气，而是坐下品尝了面条。尽管面条太咸，她还是吃了半碗，温暖地微笑着并温柔地感谢了作者。C 项完全概括了妈妈的反应。A、B、D 项描述的负面反应均与文中妈妈宽容温柔的应对事实不符。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段末句 "...it taught me something important: the value of a gift lies not in how perfect it is, but in the love behind it."

**【解析】** 文章结尾处点明了作者在这个失败的烹饪经历中学到的重要一课：礼物的价值不在于它有多完美，而在于它背后的那份爱。D 项"即使是不完美的礼物，爱也是它最重要的一部分"是对主旨的精准概括。A 项偏离核心；B、C 项都是表面的说教，没抓住"礼物与爱"的价值核心。故选 D。
