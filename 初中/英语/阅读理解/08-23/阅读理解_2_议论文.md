# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Smartphones have become a big part of our daily life. Many middle school students use them to contact parents, look up information, or listen to music. However, the question of whether phones help or hurt learning is debated by teachers and parents.

② Those who support students bringing phones to school argue that phones are useful tools. Students can search for information when they have questions. They can also use educational apps (应用) to practise English or maths. In emergencies, phones allow students to call their parents quickly. Some teachers use phone apps in class for quizzes and group activities, which makes lessons more interesting.

③ On the other hand, many people worry that phones distract (使分心) students from learning. Students may check messages or play games during class instead of listening to the teacher. Some students stay up late watching videos, so they feel tired the next day. Phone addiction (成瘾) can also affect eyesight and sleep. Besides, phones may cause problems such as copying answers during tests or taking photos without permission.

④ Some schools have rules: phones must be turned off and kept in bags during class. Students can use them only at break time or after school. This way, students still have their phones for safety, but they do not interfere (干扰) with lessons. Other schools do not allow phones at all.

⑤ In my opinion, phones can be helpful if we use them correctly. The key is self-control. Students should focus on learning when in class and use phones only when necessary. Parents and teachers need to guide students to form good habits. Technology itself is not bad; it is how we use it that matters.

**注释：** smartphone 智能手机；app 应用；distract 使分心；addiction 成瘾；interfere 干扰

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people support students bringing phones to school?
   A. Phones can help students play games.
   B. Phones are useful for learning and safety.
   C. Phones help students sleep better.
   D. Phones are cheaper than books.

2. What problem may phones cause during class?
   A. Students may use them to do homework.
   B. Students may be distracted and not listen to the teacher.
   C. Students may lose their phones.
   D. Students may share phones with friends.

3. What do some schools do to deal with phones?
   A. They encourage students to use phones in class.
   B. They ask students to turn off phones and keep them in bags during class.
   C. They give every student a new phone.
   D. They do not allow any break time.

4. What does the writer think is the key to using phones correctly?
   A. Buying expensive phones.
   B. Using phones only for games.
   C. Self-control and good habits.
   D. Never bringing phones to school.

5. Which of the following best summarizes (概括) the writer's view?
   A. Phones are always bad for students.
   B. Phones are always good for students.
   C. Phones can be helpful if used correctly; how we use them matters.
   D. Schools should never make any rules about phones.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "phones are useful tools... search for information... use educational apps... In emergencies, phones allow students to call their parents quickly"

**【解析】** 原文列举了支持方观点：手机可用于查资料、学习应用、紧急联系等，对学习和安全都有帮助。A、C、D项均非支持方理由。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "phones distract students from learning... Students may check messages or play games during class instead of listening to the teacher"

**【解析】** 原文明确说手机会让学生分心，上课时刷消息或玩游戏而不听讲。A、C、D项或与原文矛盾或未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "phones must be turned off and kept in bags during class. Students can use them only at break time or after school"

**【解析】** 原文说明部分学校规定上课时关机并存于包内，课间或放学后才可使用。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The key is self-control. Students should focus on learning... Parents and teachers need to guide students to form good habits"

**【解析】** 作者认为正确使用手机的关键是自我控制及养成良好习惯。A、B、D项均非作者观点。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "phones can be helpful if we use them correctly... Technology itself is not bad; it is how we use it that matters"

**【解析】** 作者观点是：正确使用则手机有益，关键在于我们如何使用。A、B项过于绝对，D项与原文学校规则描述矛盾。故选C。
