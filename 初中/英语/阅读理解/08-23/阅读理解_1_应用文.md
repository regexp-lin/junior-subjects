# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Sunshine Middle School — Sports Day Notice**

② Dear students and parents,

③ Our school Sports Day will be held on **Saturday, October 15th**. All students in Grades 7, 8 and 9 are welcome to join. Please read the following information carefully.

④ **Schedule:**

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| 8:00 – 8:30 | Opening ceremony (开幕式) |
| 8:30 – 11:30 | Track and field events (田径比赛) |
| 11:30 – 12:00 | Break |
| 12:00 – 1:00 | Lunch (bring your own or buy at the canteen) |
| 1:00 – 3:30 | Team games (relay races, tug-of-war, etc.) |
| 3:30 – 4:00 | Closing ceremony and prize-giving (颁奖) |

⑤ **Important reminders:**
- Wear your sports uniform and sports shoes
- Bring a water bottle and a hat if the weather is sunny
- Students who take part in events must report to the teachers 10 minutes before their event
- Parents may watch from the stands; no one is allowed on the field during events
- In case of rain, Sports Day will be moved to October 22nd

⑥ For more details, please contact Mr. Wang (PE teacher) at the school office. We look forward to seeing you there!

⑦ Sunshine Middle School  
October 1st, 2024

**注释：** ceremony 仪式；canteen 食堂；relay 接力；tug-of-war 拔河；stands 看台

---

**Questions:**

1. When will the Sports Day be held?
   A. On a weekday.
   B. On Saturday, October 15th.
   C. On Sunday, October 15th.
   D. On Saturday, October 22nd.

2. What will happen from 8:30 to 11:30?
   A. Opening ceremony.
   B. Lunch break.
   C. Track and field events.
   D. Closing ceremony.

3. What should students bring according to the notice?
   A. Sports uniform, water bottle, and a hat if sunny.
   B. Only sports shoes.
   C. Lunch from home only.
   D. Nothing special.

4. If it rains on October 15th, what will happen?
   A. Sports Day will be cancelled.
   B. Sports Day will be held indoors.
   C. Sports Day will be moved to October 22nd.
   D. Students will go home.

5. Where can parents watch the events?
   A. On the field.
   B. In the canteen.
   C. From the stands.
   D. In the classroom.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Our school Sports Day will be held on Saturday, October 15th."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明运动会于10月15日周六举行。A项"工作日"错误；C项"周日"错误；D项"10月22日"为雨天备选日期。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段表格 "8:30 – 11:30 | Track and field events"

**【解析】** 表格明确标注8:30–11:30为田径比赛时间。A项开幕式为8:00–8:30；B项午休为11:30–12:00；D项闭幕式为15:30–16:00。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Wear your sports uniform and sports shoes" 及 "Bring a water bottle and a hat if the weather is sunny"

**【解析】** 原文要求穿运动服和运动鞋，带水瓶，晴天时带帽子。B、C、D项信息不完整或错误。故选A。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In case of rain, Sports Day will be moved to October 22nd"

**【解析】** 原文明确说若遇雨天，运动会将推迟至10月22日。A、B、D项均与原文不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Parents may watch from the stands; no one is allowed on the field during events"

**【解析】** 原文说明家长可在看台观看，比赛期间任何人不得进入场地。A项"在场地"与"no one is allowed on the field"矛盾；B、D项原文未提及。故选C。
