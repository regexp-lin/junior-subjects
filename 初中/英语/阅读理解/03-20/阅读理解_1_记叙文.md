# 阅读理解 第1篇（记叙文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① On the first day of the new term, a new boy named Tom came to our class. He was tall, thin, and very quiet. During the break, while everyone was chatting and laughing, Tom just sat alone at his desk, reading a thick book. He didn't seem to want to make friends with anyone.

② The next week, our school held a sports meeting. Our class needed one more person for the 4×100m relay race (接力赛) because Jim was ill. Our monitor, Li Hua, asked everyone, but no one wanted to take Jim's place. Suddenly, Tom stood up and said quietly, "I can run."

③ Everyone was surprised. We had never seen Tom run or do any sports. Some of my classmates whispered to each other, worried that he might slow the team down. But Li Hua patted Tom on the shoulder and said, "Great! Let's practice together."

④ During the race on Friday, Tom was the last runner of our team. When he got the baton (接力棒) from Li Hua, our class was in the third place. To our surprise, Tom ran like the wind. He passed the second runner, and then, just before the finish line, he passed the first one! We won the race!

⑤ The whole class cheered and ran toward him. We lifted him in the air. For the first time, I saw a big, bright smile on Tom's face. After that day, Tom was no longer the quiet boy in the corner. He became one of our best friends. It taught me a lesson: you can never really know a person until you give them a chance to show who they are.

---

**注释：** relay race 接力赛；whisper 窃窃私语；pat 轻拍；baton 接力棒

---

### 题目

1. What was Tom doing during the break on his first day?
   A. Chatting with his new classmates.
   B. Running on the playground alone.
   C. Sitting at his desk and reading a book.
   D. Asking Li Hua about the sports meeting.

2. Why did the class need someone for the 4×100m relay race?
   A. Because Li Hua didn't want to run.
   B. Because Jim was sick and couldn't run.
   C. Because Tom wanted to show his running speed.
   D. Because the teacher asked them to find a new runner.

3. How did the classmates feel when Tom offered to run?
   A. They were surprised and a little worried.
   B. They were angry and asked him to sit down.
   C. They were very excited and cheered for him.
   D. They were sad because they didn't like him.

4. What place was the team in when Tom got the baton?
   A. First place.
   B. Second place.
   C. Third place.
   D. Last place.

5. What can we learn from the story?
   A. Reading books is more important than doing sports.
   B. A quiet student is always the fastest runner in a class.
   C. Winning a race is the only way to make good friends.
   D. We shouldn't judge a person without really knowing them.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "During the break... Tom just sat alone at his desk, reading a thick book."

**【解析】** 原文明确说到课间时只有汤姆独自坐在课桌旁看厚书。C项与原文一致。A 项与他在看书不聊天的事实相反；B 项（在操场独自跑步）和 D 项（询问关于运动会的事）第一天都没有发生。故选 C。

---

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "Our class needed one more person for the 4×100m relay race because Jim was ill."

**【解析】** 原文交代需要一个人来参加接力赛的原因是因为 Jim 生病了。B 项完全符合。A 项（李华不想跑）、C 项（Tom 想炫耀速度）、D 项（老师要求）均与原文不符。故选 B。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段第一、二、三句 "Everyone was surprised... Some of my classmates whispered to each other, worried that he might slow the team down."

**【解析】** 汤姆主动请缨时，大家因为没见过他运动而感到"惊讶"（surprised），并且有人私下里"担心"（worried）他会拖累队伍。A 项准确概括了同学们当时惊讶且有些担忧的心态。B 项（生气）和 D 项（伤心）明显不符；C 项（兴奋并欢呼）是比赛获胜后的反应，而非他刚提出时的反应。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "When he got the baton from Li Hua, our class was in the third place."

**【解析】** 原文清楚地写出当汤姆从李华手中接过接力棒时，班级队伍处于第三名。C 项与原文事实完全一致。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段末尾的主旨句 "...you can never really know a person until you give them a chance to show who they are."

**【解析】** 文章结尾点明了故事传达的道理：在给别人机会展示真实的自己之前，你永远无法真正了解一个人（即不要轻易评判或看扁一个不了解的人）。D 项"在未真正了解一个人之前不应该评判他"与主旨相符。A 项（读书比运动重要）与文章核心无关；B 项（安静的学生总是跑得最快的）过于绝对且并非文章要讲的道理；C 项（赢得比赛是交友的唯一途径）太绝对且片面。故选 D。
