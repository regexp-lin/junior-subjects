> **03-20 第2篇**

# 阅读理解 第2篇（议论文）

## 阅读下面短文，从每题所给的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中，选出最佳选项。

---

① For many years, paper dictionaries have been the best friends of language learners. Whenever students met a new word, they would turn the pages to look it up. But today, things are changing. With the development of technology, electronic dictionaries (电子词典) and dictionary apps on smartphones are becoming more and more popular. This makes people wonder: will paper dictionaries disappear one day?

② Some people think that paper dictionaries will soon be a thing of the past. They argue that electronic dictionaries are much faster and more convenient. Instead of looking through hundreds of pages, you just need to type the word, and the meaning appears in a second. What's more, electronic dictionaries are light and easy to carry. A heavy paper dictionary can make a student's schoolbag too heavy, but an app on a phone takes up no physical space at all. 

③ Also, electronic dictionaries can do things that paper ones cannot. For example, most apps provide audio pronunciation (语音发音). Students can listen to how a word is spoken by a native speaker. Many apps also offer pictures and videos to help users understand the meaning better.

④ However, others still stand by paper dictionaries. They believe that using a paper dictionary helps students remember words better. When you look up a word on paper, you often see other words on the same page, which can help increase your vocabulary. Without the screen light, paper is also better for students' eyes. More importantly, when studying with a paper dictionary, students won't be distracted by games or social media messages.

⑤ In short, both types of dictionaries have their own advantages. Electronic dictionaries are fast and easy to carry, while paper dictionaries are better for deep learning and eye protection. Perhaps the best way is to use them together, depending on what we need.

---

**注释：** convenient 方便的；take up 占据；physical space 物理空间；pronunciation 发音；vocabulary 词汇量；distract 使分心

---

### 题目

1. What makes people wonder if paper dictionaries will disappear?
   A. The fact that books are becoming more expensive.
   B. The popularity of electronic dictionaries and apps.
   C. The new rules made by some schools to ban paper dictionaries.
   D. The advice from doctors to protect students' eyes.

2. Which of the following is NOT given as an advantage of electronic dictionaries?
   A. They are fast to look up words.
   B. They are light and easy to carry.
   C. They have audio pronunciation for words.
   D. They can help students avoid playing games.

3. How does a paper dictionary help students learn more words according to Paragraph ④?
   A. By letting them see other words on the same page.
   B. By providing more example sentences than apps.
   C. By showing clear pictures of new words.
   D. By playing interesting word games with users.

4. What does the underlined word "distracted" in Paragraph ④ probably mean?
   A. Unable to see clearly.
   B. Unable to sleep well.
   C. Unable to pay attention.
   D. Unable to speak loudly.

5. What is the writer's final advice?
   A. Students should completely stop using paper dictionaries.
   B. Students should use both types of dictionaries depending on their needs.
   C. Schools should only allow electronic dictionaries in classrooms.
   D. Parents should buy the heaviest dictionaries for their children.

---

### 答案与解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3-5句 "With the development of technology, electronic dictionaries and dictionary apps... are becoming more and more popular. This makes people wonder: will paper dictionaries disappear one day?"

**【解析】** 原文指出是因为电子词典和智能手机上的词典应用越来越普及（popularity），才让人们产生疑问纸质词典是否会消失。B 项完全符合原文。A 项（书变贵了）、C 项（学校禁书新规）、D 项（医生的护眼建议）文中均未提及。故选 B。

---

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解（逆向选择）

**【定位】** 第②段、第③段、第④段。

**【解析】** 题目问哪一项"不"是电子词典的优点。A 项"查词快"对应第②段（much faster / appears in a second）；B 项"轻便易携"对应第②段（light and easy to carry）；C 项"有语音发音"对应第③段（provide audio pronunciation）。这三项都是原文提及的电子词典优点。而 D 项"能帮助学生避免玩游戏"错，防分心（不会被游戏信息打扰）实际上是第④段介绍的**纸质词典**的优点。故选 D。

---

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第3句 "When you look up a word on paper, you often see other words on the same page, which can help increase your vocabulary."

**【解析】** 原文表明，查纸质词典时因为经常能看到同一页上的其他单词，这有助于增加词汇量。A 项"通过让他们看到同一页上的其他单词"与原文一致。B 项（提供更多例句）属无中生有；C 项（图解）是电子词典的功能；D 项（玩文字游戏）更是无稽之谈。故选 A。

---

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段末句 "...when studying with a paper dictionary, students won't be distracted by games or social media messages."

**【解析】** 原文说查纸质词典的好处之一，是学生不会被游戏或社交媒体信息所 "distracted"。在有游戏和信息的干扰下，学生最容易"分心、注意力不集中"。C 项（无法集中注意力）最符合上下文逻辑。A 项（视力不清）、B 项（睡眠不好）、D 项（难以大声说话）均不符合逻辑。故选 C。

---

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 观点态度

**【定位】** 第⑤段（结论段）末句 "Perhaps the best way is to use them together, depending on what we need."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段总结了双方的优点，并提出最好的方法是"根据我们需要的情况，将两者结合使用"。B 项"根据需求两种都使用"准确反映了作者最终的建议。A 项、C 项、D 项都过于极端，违背了作者寻找平衡、各取所长的本意。故选 B。
