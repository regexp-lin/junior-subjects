# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Summer Science Camp 2026 — Open for Registration!**

② Are you interested in science and technology? Join our Summer Science Camp and explore the amazing world of science! The camp is open to all middle school students aged 12–15.

③ **Camp Details:**

| Item | Information |
|------|------------|
| **Date** | August 1–7, 2026 (7 days) |
| **Place** | Guangzhou Science Center |
| **Fee** | ¥1,200 per person (includes meals and materials) |
| **Class size** | 30 students per group |
| **Registration deadline** | July 15, 2026 |

④ **What You Will Do:**
- Build and program a simple robot
- Visit the planetarium (天文馆) and learn about stars and planets
- Do hands-on experiments in chemistry and physics
- Present your project on the last day

⑤ **What to Bring:**
- A notebook and pens
- Comfortable clothes and sports shoes
- A water bottle and sunscreen (防晒霜)
- Your student ID card

⑥ **How to Register:**
- Fill in the online form at www.gzsciencecamp.cn
- Pay the fee through WeChat Pay or bank transfer (转账)
- You will receive a confirmation (确认) email within 3 working days

⑦ **Note:** No refund (退款) after July 10. If the camp is cancelled due to bad weather, the full fee will be returned. For questions, call 020-8888-6666 or email camp@gzsc.cn.

**注释：** registration 注册；planetarium 天文馆；sunscreen 防晒霜；transfer 转账；confirmation 确认；refund 退款

---

**Questions:**

1. Who can join the Summer Science Camp?
   A. Primary school students aged 6–12.
   B. Middle school students aged 12–15.
   C. High school students aged 15–18.
   D. All students under 20.

2. How much does the camp cost per person?
   A. ¥700.
   B. ¥1,000.
   C. ¥1,200.
   D. ¥1,500.

3. What will students do during the camp?
   A. Learn to cook Chinese food.
   B. Build and program a simple robot.
   C. Travel to Beijing Science Museum.
   D. Take a swimming test.

4. Which of the following is TRUE about registration?
   A. Students must register before July 15.
   B. Students can only pay by cash.
   C. The confirmation email comes in one day.
   D. Students do not need a student ID card.

5. What happens if the camp is cancelled because of bad weather?
   A. Students will get half of the fee back.
   B. Students will get a free T-shirt instead.
   C. The full fee will be returned.
   D. The camp will move to another city.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "The camp is open to all middle school students aged 12–15."

**【解析】** 原文明确说夏令营面向12-15岁的初中生。A项"小学生6-12岁"年龄段错误；C项"高中生15-18岁"不符合；D项"20岁以下所有学生"范围过大。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Fee: ¥1,200 per person"

**【解析】** 表格中明确标注费用为每人1200元。A项"700元"、B项"1000元"和D项"1500元"均与原文不符。故选C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Build and program a simple robot"

**【解析】** 活动内容列表第一项即为"制作和编程一个简单机器人"。A项"学做中餐"、C项"去北京科技馆旅行"和D项"参加游泳测试"原文均未提及，属于无中生有。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段表格 "Registration deadline: July 15, 2026" 及第⑥段

**【解析】** 报名截止日期为7月15日，因此学生须在此日期前注册。B项"只能用现金付款"与原文"WeChat Pay or bank transfer"矛盾；C项"确认邮件一天内到达"与原文"within 3 working days"不符；D项"不需要学生证"与第⑤段"Your student ID card"矛盾。故选A。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑦段 "If the camp is cancelled due to bad weather, the full fee will be returned."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明因天气原因取消则全额退款。A项"退一半"与"full fee"矛盾；B项"免费T恤"属于无中生有；D项"转移到另一个城市"原文未提及。故选C。
