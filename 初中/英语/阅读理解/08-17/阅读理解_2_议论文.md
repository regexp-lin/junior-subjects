> **08-17 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（议论文）

① With the development of technology, e-books have become more and more popular. Many students now read books on tablets (平板电脑) or phones instead of carrying paper books. But which is better — e-books or paper books? People have different opinions.

② Supporters of e-books point out several advantages. First, e-books are easy to carry. You can store thousands of books on one small device. This is very helpful for students who need many textbooks. Second, e-books are often cheaper than paper books. Some websites even offer free e-books. Third, e-books are better for the environment because they save paper and reduce the need to cut down trees. Also, you can adjust the font size on a screen, which is useful for people with poor eyesight.

③ However, many people still prefer paper books. They believe that reading on paper is more comfortable for the eyes. Staring at a screen for too long can cause eye problems, headaches, and poor sleep. Paper books also give readers a special feeling — the smell of the pages, the weight of the book, and the sound of turning pages. These experiences cannot be replaced by a screen. Besides, studies show that people remember information better when they read it on paper rather than on a screen.

④ There is also a practical concern. E-books need electricity and a device. If your phone runs out of power or breaks down, you cannot read. Paper books do not have this problem — you can read them anytime and anywhere, even without electricity. On the other hand, paper books take up space. If you have too many, your bookshelf (书架) may not be big enough.

⑤ In my opinion, both e-books and paper books have their place. For quick reading or traveling, e-books are a good choice. For serious study or enjoying literature (文学), paper books may be better. The most important thing is not the form of the book, but whether we develop the habit of reading. After all, reading itself is what makes us wiser and better.

**注释：** device 设备；adjust 调节；font 字体；eyesight 视力；literature 文学

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, what is one advantage of e-books?
   A. They smell nice.
   B. They are easy to carry.
   C. They help people sleep better.
   D. They never need to be updated.

2. Why do some people prefer paper books?
   A. Because paper books are free.
   B. Because paper books need electricity.
   C. Because reading on paper is more comfortable for the eyes.
   D. Because paper books can store thousands of titles.

3. What is the "practical concern" mentioned in Paragraph ④?
   A. Paper books are too heavy to carry.
   B. E-books are too expensive to buy.
   C. E-books need electricity and a device to work.
   D. Paper books cannot be read at night.

4. What is the writer's attitude toward e-books and paper books?
   A. E-books are much better than paper books.
   B. Paper books should be completely replaced.
   C. Both have their own advantages and are worth using.
   D. Neither is good for students.

5. What is the main idea of this passage?
   A. E-books will replace paper books very soon.
   B. Students should stop using e-books.
   C. Both e-books and paper books have pros and cons, and reading matters most.
   D. Paper books are the only good way to learn.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第2句 "First, e-books are easy to carry."

**【解析】** 原文列举的第一个优点即为"电子书容易携带"。A项"闻起来好"是纸质书的特点而非电子书；C项"帮助人们睡得更好"原文未提及；D项"不需要更新"属于无中生有。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段第2句 "They believe that reading on paper is more comfortable for the eyes."

**【解析】** 原文明确说人们更喜欢纸质书的原因之一是纸质阅读对眼睛更舒适。A项"纸质书免费"原文未提及；B项"纸质书需要电"说反了，是电子书需要电；D项"纸质书能存储数千本书"是电子书的特点，张冠李戴。故选C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 词义猜测

**【定位】** 第④段第1-2句 "There is also a practical concern. E-books need electricity and a device."

**【解析】** "practical concern"后紧接着解释电子书需要电和设备，这就是所说的"实际问题"。A项"纸质书太重"虽在段末提到但不是"practical concern"所指；B项"电子书太贵"与第②段"cheaper"矛盾；D项"纸质书晚上不能读"原文无依据。故选C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "In my opinion, both e-books and paper books have their place."

**【解析】** 作者在最后一段明确表示两者各有优势，都值得使用。A项"电子书好得多"过于片面；B项"纸质书应被完全取代"与作者观点相反；D项"两者都对学生没好处"属于无中生有。故选C。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文，尤其是第⑤段 "The most important thing is not the form of the book, but whether we develop the habit of reading."

**【解析】** 文章分别论述了电子书和纸质书的优缺点，最终强调阅读习惯本身最重要。A项"电子书很快会取代纸质书"过于绝对；B项"学生应停止使用电子书"与文意不符；D项"纸质书是唯一好的学习方式"以偏概全。故选C。
