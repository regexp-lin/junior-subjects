> **08-24 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① One Saturday afternoon, Li Ming was walking home from the library when he saw a brown wallet (钱包) on the ground near a bus stop. He picked it up and looked inside. There was some money, a student card, and a few photos. The student card showed a girl named Zhang Wei from Sunshine Middle School.

② Li Ming did not know what to do at first. He could keep the wallet, but he knew that would be wrong. The girl might be very worried. He decided to find her and return it. He looked at the student card again — it had the school name but no address. So he went to Sunshine Middle School and asked the guard at the gate for help.

③ The guard was kind. He called the school office, and soon a teacher came. The teacher said that Zhang Wei was a Grade 8 student. She had lost her wallet that morning and had been very upset. The teacher took Li Ming to Zhang Wei's classroom. When Zhang Wei saw her wallet, her eyes lit up (发光). She thanked Li Ming again and again.

④ "I was so worried," Zhang Wei said. "There was money for my books, and those photos are very important to me. Thank you for being so honest!" She asked Li Ming for his name and school. Li Ming said he was from Green Tree Middle School and that he was happy to help. Zhang Wei wrote him a thank-you note and gave him a small gift — a bookmark (书签) she had made herself.

⑤ When Li Ming got home, he told his mother what had happened. His mother smiled and said, "You did the right thing. Honesty is one of the best qualities a person can have." Li Ming felt proud. He knew that returning the wallet was a small act, but it had made a big difference to someone. Doing the right thing always feels good.

**注释：** wallet 钱包；lit up 发光；bookmark 书签

---

**Questions:**

1. Where did Li Ming find the wallet?
   A. In the library.
   B. Near a bus stop.
   C. At Sunshine Middle School.
   D. At home.

2. What did Li Ming find inside the wallet?
   A. Only money.
   B. Money, a student card, and some photos.
   C. Books and a pen.
   D. A phone and a key.

3. How did Li Ming find the owner of the wallet?
   A. He asked his mother for help.
   B. He went to Sunshine Middle School and asked the guard and teacher for help.
   C. He put up a notice in the street.
   D. He called the police.

4. How did Zhang Wei feel when she got her wallet back?
   A. She was angry.
   B. She was sad.
   C. She was grateful and relieved.
   D. She did not care.

5. What did Li Ming's mother say about his action?
   A. She was disappointed.
   B. She said honesty is one of the best qualities.
   C. She asked him to keep the money.
   D. She told him never to pick up things on the street again.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "he saw a brown wallet on the ground near a bus stop"

**【解析】** 原文明确说Li Ming在公交站附近地上发现钱包。A、C、D项均为错误地点。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "There was some money, a student card, and a few photos"

**【解析】** 原文列举了钱包内物品：钱、学生证和几张照片。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "he went to Sunshine Middle School and asked the guard at the gate for help" 及第③段 "The guard... called the school office, and soon a teacher came"

**【解析】** 原文叙述Li Ming到学校求助门卫和老师找到失主。A、C、D项均与原文不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "her eyes lit up" 及 "She thanked Li Ming again and again" 和第④段 "I was so worried... Thank you for being so honest!"

**【解析】** Zhang Wei看到钱包时眼睛发亮、再三感谢、表达之前的担心，可见她既感激又释然。A、B、D项与文中情感不符。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "You did the right thing. Honesty is one of the best qualities a person can have"

**【解析】** 母亲明确肯定Li Ming的行为，并指出诚实是最宝贵的品质之一。A、C、D项均与母亲的话矛盾。故选B。
