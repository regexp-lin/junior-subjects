# 阅读理解（说明文）

① The Dragon Boat Festival (端午节) is one of the most important traditional festivals in China. It falls on the fifth day of the fifth month in the lunar calendar (农历). In 2024, it will be in June. People have celebrated this festival for over 2,000 years.

② The festival is famous for dragon boat racing. Teams row (划) long boats that look like dragons. Each boat has a drummer who beats a drum to keep the rowers in sync (同步). Thousands of people gather by the rivers to watch the exciting races. The sport is not only fun but also shows the spirit of teamwork.

③ Zongzi (粽子) is the traditional food for this festival. It is made of sticky rice (糯米) wrapped in bamboo or reed (芦苇) leaves. Inside, there can be beans, meat, or dates. Different regions have different flavours. In the south, zongzi are often salty with pork; in the north, they are usually sweet with red beans or dates. Making zongzi with family is a popular activity before the festival.

④ There are several stories about why we celebrate the Dragon Boat Festival. The most famous one is about Qu Yuan (屈原), a great poet and minister. He loved his country but was not trusted by the king. When his country was in danger, he jumped into a river. People rowed boats to look for him and threw zongzi into the water so that the fish would not eat his body. Today, we eat zongzi and watch dragon boat races in memory of him.

⑤ The Dragon Boat Festival is also a time to stay healthy. In the past, people hung special herbs (草药) and wore perfumed pouches (香囊) to keep away diseases. Some families still follow these customs today. The festival brings families together and helps us remember our culture.

**注释：** lunar 农历的；row 划；sync 同步；sticky rice 糯米；reed 芦苇；herb 草药；pouch 香囊

**Questions:**

1. When does the Dragon Boat Festival take place?
   A. On the first day of the first lunar month.
   B. On the fifth day of the fifth lunar month.
   C. On the fifteenth day of the eighth lunar month.
   D. On the ninth day of the ninth lunar month.

2. What is the role of the drummer in a dragon boat?
   A. To control the boat.
   B. To keep the rowers in sync.
   C. To throw zongzi into the water.
   D. To look for Qu Yuan.

3. How do zongzi in the south often taste?
   A. Sweet with red beans.
   B. Salty with pork.
   C. Sour with vinegar.
   D. Spicy with chilli.

4. Why did people throw zongzi into the river according to the story?
   A. To feed the fish so they would not eat Qu Yuan's body.
   B. To make the water cleaner.
   C. To celebrate the festival.
   D. To race with other boats.

5. What custom do some families still follow to stay healthy during the festival?
   A. Eating only meat.
   B. Hanging herbs and wearing perfumed pouches.
   C. Swimming in the river.
   D. Making zongzi every day.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "It falls on the fifth day of the fifth month in the lunar calendar"

**【解析】** 原文明确说端午节在农历五月初五。A项为春节；C项为中秋节；D项为重阳节。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Each boat has a drummer who beats a drum to keep the rowers in sync"

**【解析】** 原文说明鼓手击鼓是为了让划手保持节奏一致。A、C、D项均非鼓手职责。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "In the south, zongzi are often salty with pork; in the north, they are usually sweet with red beans or dates"

**【解析】** 原文明确说南方粽子多为咸味加猪肉，北方多为甜味加红豆或枣。A项为北方口味；C、D项原文未提及。故选B。

**4. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 " threw zongzi into the water so that the fish would not eat his body"

**【解析】** 原文说明投粽子入水是为了让鱼不吃屈原的身体。B、C、D项均非传说中投粽子的目的。故选A。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "people hung special herbs and wore perfumed pouches to keep away diseases. Some families still follow these customs today"

**【解析】** 原文说人们挂草药、佩香囊以驱病，部分家庭至今仍保留此习俗。A、C、D项原文未提及。故选B。
