> **05-18 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① It was Friday, and Li Wei's birthday was the next day. He had told his classmates about it a week earlier, but no one had mentioned it since then. During the break, he saw two girls whispering in the corner. When he walked closer, they stopped talking and quickly walked away. Li Wei felt a little sad. "Maybe they have forgotten my birthday," he thought.

② On Saturday morning, Li Wei woke up feeling down. His parents had gone on a short trip and would not be back until Sunday. The apartment was quiet. He made some noodles for breakfast and sat alone at the table. Just as he finished eating, the doorbell rang. He opened the door and was completely surprised. Standing outside were five of his classmates, including the two girls he had seen whispering. They were holding a birthday cake and shouting, "Happy Birthday, Li Wei!"

③ "We wanted to surprise you!" said Wang Lin, one of his best friends. "That's why we pretended we forgot. We've been planning this for days." Li Wei could not believe his eyes. They came in, put the cake on the table, and sang the birthday song. His sadness disappeared at once. He felt so happy that his eyes became wet.

④ They spent the whole afternoon together. They played games, shared the cake, and talked about school life. Li Wei learned that the girls had been whispering because they were discussing the surprise plan. "I'm sorry I misunderstood you," he said. They all laughed. Before they left, Wang Lin gave him a small gift — a book about friendship. "We're always here for you," Wang Lin said.

⑤ That night, Li Wei lay in bed with a smile on his face. He had learned something important: things are not always what they seem. Sometimes what looks like being forgotten is actually being remembered in a special way. True friends find ways to show they care, even when it takes a little extra effort to create a surprise.

**注释：** whisper 低声说话；pretend 假装；misunderstand 误解

---

### 选择题

1. Why did Li Wei feel sad on Friday, according to paragraph ①?
   A. He thought his classmates had forgotten his birthday.
   B. His parents were going on a trip.
   C. The two girls would not talk to him.
   D. He had too much homework to do.

2. What did Li Wei find when he opened the door on Saturday morning?
   A. His parents had come back early.
   B. Five classmates were outside with a birthday cake.
   C. A delivery man brought him a package.
   D. No one was there.

3. Why had the two girls been whispering, according to paragraph ④?
   A. They were talking about a test.
   B. They did not want to be Li Wei's friends.
   C. They were discussing the surprise birthday plan.
   D. They were planning to go home early.

4. What can we infer from Wang Lin's gift and words?
   A. Wang Lin did not really care about Li Wei.
   B. Wang Lin wanted Li Wei to read more books.
   C. The gift was very expensive.
   D. The friends valued their friendship and wanted Li Wei to know they supported him.

5. What did Li Wei learn from this experience?
   A. Birthdays are the most important day of the year.
   B. Parents should never leave children alone on their birthdays.
   C. Things are not always what they seem; true friends may show care in surprising ways.
   D. It is better to tell everyone about your birthday to avoid misunderstanding.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第①段 "no one had mentioned it since then... When he walked closer, they stopped talking and quickly walked away. Li Wei felt a little sad. 'Maybe they have forgotten my birthday,' he thought."

**【解析】** 文中李伟发现无人再提生日、女生见他就停止说话走开，他感到难过并心想"也许他们忘了我的生日"，可推断他伤心的原因是以为同学忘了他的生日，A 项正确。B 项"父母要出游"是周六早上的背景，非周五难过的主因；C 项"女生不和他说话"是现象，根本原因仍是对"被遗忘"的担心；D 项"作业太多"原文未提。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Standing outside were five of his classmates, including the two girls he had seen whispering. They were holding a birthday cake and shouting, 'Happy Birthday, Li Wei!'"

**【解析】** 原文明确描述李伟开门后看到五名同学站在门外，其中有两个窃窃私语的女生，他们拿着生日蛋糕并喊"生日快乐"。A 项"父母提前回来"与"父母周日才回"矛盾；C 项"快递员送包裹"和 D 项"门外没人"均与原文不符。故选 B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Li Wei learned that the girls had been whispering because they were discussing the surprise plan."

**【解析】** 文中直接说明女生当时窃窃私语是因为在商量惊喜计划。A 项"讨论考试"、B 项"不想和李伟做朋友"和 D 项"计划早点回家"均无依据。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Wang Lin gave him a small gift — a book about friendship. 'We're always here for you,' Wang Lin said."

**【解析】** 王林送了一本关于友谊的书并说"我们一直在你身边"，可推断朋友们很看重这段友谊，想让李伟知道他们支持他，D 项正确。A 项"王林并不真的在乎"与送书和说的话矛盾；B 项"想让李伟多读书"过于片面，礼物主题是友谊；C 项"礼物很贵"原文未提及且强调是"small gift"。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "He had learned something important: things are not always what they seem. Sometimes what looks like being forgotten is actually being remembered in a special way. True friends find ways to show they care, even when it takes a little extra effort to create a surprise."

**【解析】** 李伟的感悟是：事情往往不像表面那样；看似被遗忘，实则被用一种特别的方式记得；真正的朋友会用心表达关心，哪怕要多花力气制造惊喜。C 项准确概括这一主旨。A 项"生日是一年最重要的一天"、B 项"父母不应在生日把小孩留在家"和 D 项"最好告诉大家生日以免误会"均非文章要传递的核心道理。故选 C。
