# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Community First Aid Training Course—Enrollment Now Open**

② Would you like to learn how to help others in an emergency? Our community center is offering a free first aid (急救) training course for all residents. Knowing first aid can save lives. Join us and become a helpful person in your neighborhood!

③ **What you will learn:** We will teach you how to do CPR (心肺复苏), how to stop bleeding (止血), and what to do when someone chokes (窒息). You will also learn how to use a first aid kit and call for help correctly. All lessons are given by trained doctors and nurses.

④ **Course details:** The course will run for four Saturdays, from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m., starting on August 10th. It will be held at the community center, Room 201. There are only 30 places, so please sign up early. You must be at least 14 years old to join.

⑤ To enroll (报名), please visit our website www.communityaid.edu or call 0755-1234567 before August 5th. We look forward to seeing you!

**注释：** first aid 急救；CPR 心肺复苏；bleeding 出血；choke 窒息；enroll 报名

---

### 选择题

1. Who can join the first aid training course?
   A. Any resident who is at least 14 years old.
   B. Only doctors and nurses.
   C. Only teenagers under 18.
   D. People who have studied medicine.

2. What will students learn in the course?
   A. How to cook healthy meals.
   B. CPR, stopping bleeding, helping someone who chokes, and using a first aid kit.
   C. How to drive an ambulance.
   D. Computer skills for hospital workers.

3. When and where will the course be held?
   A. Every Sunday at the hospital.
   B. Every day in August at the library.
   C. Four Saturdays, 9:00 a.m.–12:00 p.m., at the community center, Room 201.
   D. Only on August 10th at the school.

4. What should people do if they want to join the course?
   A. Send an e-mail after August 5th.
   B. Go to Room 201 on August 10th.
   C. Write a letter to the community center.
   D. Visit the website or call before August 5th.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The course is very expensive.
   B. The community wants more people to learn first aid so they can help others in emergencies.
   C. Only 14 people can join the course.
   D. The course will be taught by students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "for all residents" 及第④段 "You must be at least 14 years old to join"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明面向所有居民招生，且须年满14岁方可参加。A项正确。B项仅限医护、C项仅限18岁以下、D项须学过医学均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "how to do CPR... how to stop bleeding... what to do when someone chokes... how to use a first aid kit and call for help correctly"

**【解析】** 课程内容包括心肺复苏、止血、窒息处理、使用急救包及正确呼救。B项完整概括。A、C、D项均为与课程无关的内容。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "The course will run for four Saturdays, from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m., starting on August 10th. It will be held at the community center, Room 201"

**【解析】** 课程时间为四个周六上午9:00至12:00，8月10日起开课，地点为社区中心201室。C项正确。A、B、D项时间或地点均有误。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "visit our website www.communityaid.edu or call 0755-1234567 before August 5th"

**【解析】** 报名方式为访问网站或拨打电话，且须在8月5日前完成。D项正确。A项8月5日后不符；B项8月10日去201室为上课日非报名；C项写信未提及。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第②段 "Knowing first aid can save lives. Join us and become a helpful person in your neighborhood"

**【解析】** 文中强调学习急救能救命、成为社区中有用的人，可推知社区希望通过培训让更多人掌握急救技能，以便在紧急情况帮助他人。B项正确。A项昂贵与"free"矛盾；C项14人误读（应为30个名额）；D项学生授课与"trained doctors and nurses"矛盾。故选B。
