# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Should Schools Cancel Final Exams?**

② In recent years, some schools have tried to cancel or reduce final exams (期末考试). They believe exams cause too much stress (压力) for students. But many teachers and parents disagree. They think exams are still necessary. Let us look at both sides of the argument.

③ **Those who support canceling exams** say that students feel worried and tired before exams. Some students stay up late to prepare, which is bad for their health. They also argue that a single exam cannot really show what a student has learned. Some students are good at taking exams but weak in real-life skills. Instead of exams, schools can use class work, projects, and daily performance to grade students. This way, students may feel less stressed and enjoy learning more.

④ **Those who support keeping exams** believe that exams help students review what they have learned. When students know there is an exam, they will work harder and take their studies seriously. Exams also help teachers find out which students need more help. Besides, exams prepare students for future tests like the high school entrance exam (中考). If schools cancel all exams, students may not get used to taking important tests when they grow up. In real life, people often face tests—at work, when applying for a job, or when getting a driver's license. Learning to deal with exam pressure is a useful skill.

⑤ **The conclusion:** There is no simple answer. Both sides have good points. Perhaps the best way is to find a balance. Schools could reduce the number of exams or make them less stressful. They could also mix exams with other ways of assessment (评估), such as projects and class discussions. The goal should be to help students learn well without making them too stressed or tired.

**注释：** stress 压力；assessment 评估

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, why do some people want to cancel final exams?
   A. Because exams cause stress, students may stay up late, and a single exam may not show real learning; they prefer using class work and projects instead.
   B. Because exams are too easy.
   C. Because there are too many teachers.
   D. Because students prefer to take exams on computers.

2. What do supporters of keeping exams believe?
   A. Exams are useless and should never be used.
   B. Exams help students review, work harder, and prepare for future tests like the high school entrance exam.
   C. Only the best students should take exams.
   D. Exams should only be given once every five years.

3. According to the passage, how can exams prepare students for the future?
   A. They help students get better jobs immediately.
   B. They help students travel abroad.
   C. They help students get used to taking important tests and dealing with pressure, which they will face in real life.
   D. They help students make more friends.

4. What does the writer suggest as a solution?
   A. Cancel all exams in every school.
   B. Give more exams to make students work harder.
   C. Let students decide whether to take exams or not.
   D. Find a balance: reduce exams or make them less stressful, and mix exams with projects and class discussions.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The writer believes that exams should be completely removed from all schools.
   B. The writer thinks both sides have valid points and a balanced approach may work best.
   C. The writer thinks that stress is always bad for students.
   D. The writer believes that only exams can show what students have learned.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "students feel worried and tired before exams... Some students stay up late to prepare, which is bad for their health... a single exam cannot really show what a student has learned... Instead of exams, schools can use class work, projects, and daily performance to grade students"

**【解析】** 支持取消者认为考试带来压力、学生熬夜备考有害健康、一次考试无法反映真实学习，主张用平时作业、项目和日常表现替代。A项准确概括。B、C、D项均未提及。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "exams help students review what they have learned... they will work harder... Exams also help teachers find out which students need more help... exams prepare students for future tests like the high school entrance exam"

**【解析】** 支持保留考试者认为考试有助复习、督促用功、便于教师发现需辅导学生、为学生应对中考等未来考试做准备。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "exams prepare students for future tests... In real life, people often face tests—at work, when applying for a job, or when getting a driver's license. Learning to deal with exam pressure is a useful skill"

**【解析】** 文中指出考试能帮学生适应未来的重要测试，生活中常需面对工作考核、求职面试、驾照考试等，学会应对考试压力是有用的技能。可推知C项正确。A、B、D项均未提及或过度推断。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Perhaps the best way is to find a balance. Schools could reduce the number of exams or make them less stressful. They could also mix exams with other ways of assessment (评估), such as projects and class discussions"

**【解析】** 作者建议寻找平衡：减少考试数量或降低压力，将考试与项目、课堂讨论等评估方式结合。D项正确。A、B、C项与作者建议不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "There is no simple answer. Both sides have good points. Perhaps the best way is to find a balance"

**【解析】** 作者认为没有简单答案，双方都有道理，最佳做法是寻求平衡。可推知作者认为双方观点都有合理性，平衡的做法可能最有效。B项正确。A、C、D项与作者立场矛盾。故选B。
