> **04-05 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① I will never forget my 14th birthday. It was a Saturday, but it started terrible. In the morning, it rained heavily. My parents had told me they would take me to the amusement park (游乐园), but bad weather ruined (毁掉) our plan. I sat by the window, feeling very sad and disappointed. 

② "I'm sorry, dear," my mom said, clearly noticing my tears. "We can't go out in such heavy rain. We will celebrate it at home." "It's okay," I sighed, though inside I felt my special day was completely ruined.

③ I went back to my room and read a book alone. Hours passed. Around 6:00 PM, my father knocked on my door. "Dinner is ready," he said. When I walked into the dining room, I was completely shocked! The room was decorated (装饰) with beautiful balloons and colorful lights. 

④ But the biggest surprise was sitting around the table. My grandparents, who lived in another city, were there! "Surprise! Happy birthday!" they shouted together. My best friends, Lisa and Jack, were also there smiling at me. It turned out that my parents had planned this secret party for weeks. They invited my loved ones over and prepared my favorite chocolate cake.

⑤ We ate, laughed, and played games together. The house was filled with warmth and joy. I realized that a wonderful birthday didn't need a trip to the amusement park. Having the people I love around me was the best gift ever. It was the most perfect day.

**注释：** amusement park 游乐园；ruin 毁掉；disappointed 失望的；decorate 装饰

---

### 选择题

1. How did the writer feel on the morning of his 14th birthday?
A. Excited and happy.
B. Sad and disappointed.
C. Tired and sleepy.
D. Angry and hungry.

2. Why was the writer's plan ruined?
A. Because he was ill.
B. Because his parents were too busy.
C. Because it rained heavily.
D. Because the amusement park was closed.

3. Who planned the secret party?
A. The writer's parents.
B. The writer's grandparents.
C. The writer's friends, Lisa and Jack.
D. The writer's teachers.

4. What can we infer (推断) from paragraph ④?
A. The writer's grandparents visit them every day.
B. Lisa and Jack brought the chocolate cake.
C. The writer didn't like the chocolate cake at all.
D. The writer didn't expect his grandparents and friends to be there.

5. What did the writer learn at the end of the story?
A. Chocolate cake is the best food in the world.
B. Going to the amusement park is a waste of money.
C. Having loved ones around is the most wonderful gift.
D. He shouldn't read books on his birthday.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段最后一句 "I sat by the window, feeling very sad and disappointed." 

**【解析】** 原文描述由于下大雨计划泡汤，作者坐在窗边感到非常伤心和失望（sad and disappointed）。A项（兴奋开心）、C项（疲惫困倦）、D项（生气饥饿）均与原文不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3-4句 "In the morning, it rained heavily... bad weather ruined our plan."

**【解析】** 由于早上下起了大雨这种坏天气毁掉了去游乐园的计划。A项（他病了）、B项（父母太忙）、D项（游乐园关门）等并不是直接原因。故选C。

**3. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段倒数第2句 "It turned out that my parents had planned this secret party for weeks."

**【解析】** 原文清楚地交代，是作者的父母（my parents）策划了这场神秘的派对。B项、C项、D项均非策划者。故选A。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段第1句 "But the biggest surprise was sitting around the table." 和后文描述。

**【解析】** 当作者走进餐厅看到外地的祖父母和好朋友们时，文中用词是最大的惊喜（biggest surprise... they shouted together）。由此推断，作者事先并不知道他们会来（Didn't expect ... to be there）。A项（每天来拜访）与文中不在同一城市矛盾；B项未提及蛋糕是朋友带的，而是父母备的；C项不符合文意。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第⑤段第3-4句 "I realized that a wonderful birthday didn't need a trip to the amusement park. Having the people I love around me was the best gift ever."

**【解析】** 作者在文末的感悟（realize）是：一个美好的生日不需要去游乐园之类的地方，爱自己的人陪伴在身边就是最好的礼物。A项和D项未在此段落表达出类似中心；B项并非作者本意，他只是觉得家人陪伴更胜一筹。故选C。
