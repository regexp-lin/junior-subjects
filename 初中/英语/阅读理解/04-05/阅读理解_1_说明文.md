# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Do you often feel tired during the day? Do you find it hard to focus on your studies? If your answer is "yes", you might not be getting enough sleep. For teenagers, sleep is just as important as food and water.

② Scientists say that teenagers need about 8 to 10 hours of sleep every night. However, a recent survey (调查) shows that many students only sleep for 6 or 7 hours. Why do they stay up so late? Some of them have too much homework to do. Others spend a lot of time playing games or chatting with friends on their phones before bed.

③ Lack of sleep (缺乏睡眠) can cause many problems. First, it affects our memory. When we sleep, our brain organizes the information we learn during the day. If we don't sleep well, it is hard for us to remember things clearly. Second, a tired brain makes us feel easily angry or sad. We might argue with our parents or friends over small things. Third, missing sleep can also make our immune system (免疫系统) weak, so we will get sick more easily.

④ To get a good night's sleep, here are some useful tips. First, try to go to bed at the same time every night. Second, do not drink tea or coffee in the evening. Lastly, keep your bedroom quiet, dark, and cool. Turn off your phone an hour before going to bed. 

⑤ Remember, a good sleep is the secret to a healthy body and a bright mind.

**注释：** survey 调查；immune system 免疫系统

---

### 选择题

1. How many hours of sleep do teenagers need every night according to scientists?
A. 4 to 5 hours.
B. 6 to 7 hours.
C. 8 to 10 hours.
D. 11 to 12 hours.

2. Why do many students stay up late?
A. Because they have to do evening exercise.
B. Because of too much homework or playing on phones.
C. Because they like to watch the stars.
D. Because they need to help their parents clean the house.

3. What might happen if we lack sleep?
A. Our memory gets better.
B. We get sick more easily.
C. We will become very happy.
D. We can run much faster.

4. Which of the following is a good tip for getting a good sleep?
A. Drinking much coffee in the evening.
B. Playing exciting mobile games in bed.
C. Keeping the bedroom noisy and bright.
D. Going to bed at the same time every night.

5. What does the passage mainly talk about?
A. The importance of having breakfast.
B. How to use a phone correctly.
C. The importance of sleep for teenagers and tips for it.
D. The result of the latest science survey on studying.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段第1句 "Scientists say that teenagers need about 8 to 10 hours of sleep every night."

**【解析】** 原文清晰地指出科学家表示青少年每晚需要大约8到10小时（8 to 10 hours）的睡眠。A项和D项未提及；B项是近期某调查显示的实际情况（6或7小时），并非科学家建议所需时长。故选C。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段最后两句 "Some of them have too much homework to do. Others spend a lot of time playing games or chatting with friends on their phones before bed."

**【解析】** 文章解释学生熬夜的原因是：有太多的作业要做；睡前花大量时间玩游戏或在手机上和朋友聊天。A项（做夜间锻炼）、C项（喜欢看星星）、D项（帮父母打扫房子）都不是文中说明的原因。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解判断

**【定位】** 第③段整段，尤其是最后一句 "...make our immune system weak, so we will get sick more easily."

**【解析】** 原文中提到缺乏睡眠（lack of sleep）会导致的一系列问题。A项反了，是使人们难以记清事物；C项也反了，会让人容易感到生气或悲伤；D项与原文无关；B项“容易生病”有原文依据。故选B。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段第2句 "First, try to go to bed at the same time every night." 

**【解析】** 这段给出了几条睡眠建议。A项（晚上喝咖啡）、B项（在床上玩刺激的手机游戏）、C项（保持卧室嘈杂和明亮）均与文中的“不要喝茶或咖啡” “把手机关掉” “保持安静、黑暗、凉爽” 等建议相反。D项（每晚同一时间上床睡觉）是原文明确提出的建议。故选D。

**5. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 首段中心句 "For teenagers, sleep is just as important as food and water." 及全文结构。

**【解析】** 本文是一篇说明文，结构清晰。先指出睡眠不足的问题，再解释睡眠对青少年的重要性和缺乏睡眠带来的坏影响（第③段），最后给出改善睡眠的建议（第④段）。因此，本文主要讨论睡眠对青少年的重要性及其相关建议。A、B、D项均属无中生有或生搬硬扯。故选C。
