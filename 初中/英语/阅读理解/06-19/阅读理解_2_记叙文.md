# 阅读理解（记叙文）

## A Surprise Card for Mrs. Chen

① Mrs. Chen has been our English teacher for two years. She is kind and patient. She always makes our lessons interesting and never gets angry when we make mistakes. Last month, she fell ill and had to stay at home for a week. The whole class missed her very much.

② One afternoon, our monitor Lily had an idea. "Let's make a surprise card for Mrs. Chen," she said. "We can write our wishes and draw pictures. When she comes back, we'll give it to her." Everyone agreed and we decided to do it during the lunch break.

③ The next day, we brought colorful paper, crayons, and markers to school. Each of us wrote a short message. Some wrote "Get well soon!" Some drew flowers and hearts. Tom, who is usually quiet, drew a big smiley face and wrote "Thank you for teaching us. We love you!" We worked together and finished the card in twenty minutes.

④ When Mrs. Chen returned to school on Monday, we gathered around her desk. Lily gave her the card and said, "This is from all of us. We hope you feel better now." Mrs. Chen opened it slowly. Her eyes became wet. She said, "Thank you, everyone. This is the best gift I have ever received. I'm so lucky to have students like you."

⑤ That day, we learned something important. A small act of kindness can mean a lot to someone. Mrs. Chen smiled the whole lesson, and we knew our card had made her day. Sometimes the simplest things — like a handmade card — can bring the greatest joy.

**注释：** patient 耐心的；monitor 班长；crayon 蜡笔；gather 聚集

---

### 选择题

1. Why did the class miss Mrs. Chen?
   A. Because she was strict with them.
   B. Because she fell ill and stayed at home for a week.
   C. Because she left the school.
   D. Because she had a new job.

2. Whose idea was it to make a surprise card?
   A. Mrs. Chen's.
   B. Tom's.
   C. Lily's (the monitor).
   D. The headteacher's.

3. What did Tom write on the card?
   A. "Get well soon!"
   B. "Thank you for teaching us. We love you!"
   C. "We will study harder."
   D. He only drew a picture.

4. How did Mrs. Chen feel when she received the card?
   A. She felt sad and disappointed.
   B. She felt surprised and moved; her eyes became wet.
   C. She felt angry because we missed the lesson.
   D. She felt nothing special.

5. What did the students learn from this experience?
   A. Making cards is very difficult.
   B. A small act of kindness can mean a lot to someone.
   C. Teachers never get sick.
   D. Students should not make cards during lunch.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "Last month, she fell ill and had to stay at home for a week. The whole class missed her very much."

**【解析】** 陈老师生病在家待了一周，全班都很想念她。B 项正确。A 项 "严厉" 与 kind 矛盾；C 项 "离开学校"、D 项 "新工作" 均未提及。故选 B。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "our monitor Lily had an idea. 'Let's make a surprise card for Mrs. Chen,' she said."

**【解析】** 班长 Lily 提议制作惊喜贺卡。C 项正确。A 项陈老师本人、B 项 Tom、D 项校长均非提议者。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Tom, who is usually quiet, drew a big smiley face and wrote 'Thank you for teaching us. We love you!'"

**【解析】** Tom 画了笑脸并写下 "Thank you for teaching us. We love you!"。B 项正确。A 项是其他人的留言；C、D 项未提及。故选 B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "Her eyes became wet. She said, 'Thank you, everyone. This is the best gift I have ever received.'"

**【解析】** 陈老师眼眶湿润，称这是她收到过最好的礼物，可见她既惊喜又感动。B 项正确。A 项悲伤、C 项生气、D 项无感均与原文不符。故选 B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨理解 / 写作意图

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "we learned something important. A small act of kindness can mean a lot to someone."

**【解析】** 学生们从中领悟到：小小的善举对他人的意义可能很大。B 项准确概括。A 项做卡很难、C 项老师不生病、D 项午饭时不应做卡均非主旨。故选 B。
