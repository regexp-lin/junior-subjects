# 阅读理解（议论文）

① Plastic packaging is everywhere — in our food, our toys, our clothes. It is cheap, light, and convenient. But plastic waste has become a serious problem. It pollutes the ocean, harms wildlife, and takes hundreds of years to break down. Should we reduce plastic packaging? This question is widely debated today.

② Those who support reducing plastic packaging argue that it is necessary for the environment. Every year, millions of tons of plastic end up in the ocean. Fish and other sea animals sometimes eat plastic by mistake and die. Plastic also breaks into tiny pieces called microplastics, which can enter our food and water. Reducing packaging would mean less waste and a healthier planet. In addition, many alternatives (替代品) exist: paper bags, reusable containers, and biodegradable (可降解的) materials. Some countries have already banned single-use plastic bags, and it has worked well.

③ However, those who oppose a rapid reduction have different concerns. They say that plastic packaging keeps food fresh and safe. Without it, more food might go bad before it reaches the consumer, which could lead to waste and higher prices. Plastic is also lighter than glass or metal, so it costs less to transport. For poor countries, switching to other materials might be expensive. Jobs in the plastic industry could be lost. They think we need a gradual change, not a sudden ban.

④ In my view, we should reduce plastic packaging step by step. We can start with things we do not really need — for example, plastic straws, single-use bags, and excessive wrappers on fruits and vegetables. We can use reusable bags when we shop and choose products with less packaging. Governments can encourage companies to use recycled or biodegradable materials. Education is also important: when people understand the problem, they are more likely to change their habits.

⑤ The goal is not to end all plastic at once, but to use it more wisely. Small changes by many people can make a big difference. The future of our planet depends on the choices we make today.

**注释：** alternative 替代品；biodegradable 可降解的；microplastic 微塑料

---

**Questions:**

1. According to Paragraph ②, why do some people support reducing plastic packaging?
   A. Because plastic is too expensive.
   B. Because it pollutes the ocean, harms wildlife, and takes long to break down; alternatives exist.
   C. Because plastic is too heavy.
   D. Because nobody uses plastic.

2. What problem does plastic cause for sea animals?
   A. They enjoy eating plastic.
   B. They sometimes eat plastic by mistake and die.
   C. They need plastic to live.
   D. Plastic makes them stronger.

3. Why do some people oppose a rapid reduction in plastic packaging?
   A. Because they hate the environment.
   B. Because plastic keeps food fresh; switching might be expensive; jobs could be lost — they prefer gradual change.
   C. Because there are no alternatives.
   D. Because plastic is free.

4. What does the writer suggest we can do first?
   A. Ban all plastic immediately.
   B. Reduce unnecessary items like plastic straws, single-use bags, and excessive wrappers; use reusable bags.
   C. Use more plastic.
   D. Do nothing.

5. What is the writer's main message?
   A. We must stop using plastic today.
   B. Use plastic more wisely; small changes by many people can make a big difference.
   C. Plastic is not a problem.
   D. Only rich countries should reduce plastic.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "pollutes the ocean, harms wildlife, and takes hundreds of years to break down... many alternatives exist... paper bags, reusable containers, biodegradable materials"

**【解析】** 支持者认为塑料污染海洋、危害生物、降解慢，且已有替代品。B项概括准确。A、C、D项非主要理由。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Fish and other sea animals sometimes eat plastic by mistake and die"

**【解析】** 海洋动物可能误食塑料而死亡。B项正确。A、C、D项与原文不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "plastic packaging keeps food fresh and safe... switching to other materials might be expensive... Jobs... could be lost... we need a gradual change"

**【解析】** 反对者担心食品保鲜、替代成本高、就业流失，主张渐进式改变。B项正确。A、C、D项与观点不符。故选B。

**4. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "start with things we do not really need — plastic straws, single-use bags, excessive wrappers... use reusable bags... choose products with less packaging"

**【解析】** 作者建议先减少不必要用品，使用环保袋，选择少包装产品。B项正确。A、C、D项与建议矛盾。故选B。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 第④、⑤段 "use it more wisely. Small changes by many people can make a big difference"

**【解析】** 作者主张更明智地使用塑料，强调每个人的小改变能产生大影响。B项正确。A、C、D项与主旨不符。故选B。
