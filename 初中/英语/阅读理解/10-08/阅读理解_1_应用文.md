# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **Autumn Study Trip — Notice to Students and Parents**

② Our school will organise an autumn study trip for all Grade 8 and Grade 9 students. The trip will take place on **November 8–9, 2024** (Friday and Saturday). Students will visit the City Science Museum and a local farm to learn about science and farming. This is a required school activity. All students are expected to take part unless they are ill or have a valid reason.

③ **Schedule:**
- **Day 1 (Nov 8):** Meet at school gate at 7:30 a.m. Bus departs at 8:00 a.m. Visit the Science Museum (9:00–12:00). Lunch at the museum canteen. Afternoon: farm visit. Return to school at 5:30 p.m.
- **Day 2 (Nov 9):** Meet at school gate at 8:00 a.m. Visit the farm again for hands-on activities (planting, feeding animals). Lunch at the farm. Return to school at 3:00 p.m.

④ **What to bring:**
- School uniform (must be worn)
- Lunch money: ¥50 for two days (or bring your own food)
- Water bottle, hat, and comfortable shoes
- Notebook and pen for taking notes
- Do NOT bring valuable items (e.g. expensive phones, jewellery)

⑤ **Important:** Students must follow the teachers' instructions at all times. No students may leave the group without permission. Parents who have questions may contact the school office (0755-88880000) before November 5.

We look forward to a safe and educational trip!

**注释：** valid 有效的；depart 出发；valuable 贵重的

---

**Questions:**

1. Who is this notice for?
   A. Only Grade 7 students.
   B. Grade 8 and Grade 9 students and their parents.
   C. Only teachers.
   D. Primary school students.

2. When will the study trip take place?
   A. October 8–9, 2024.
   B. November 8–9, 2024.
   C. December 8–9, 2024.
   D. January 8–9, 2025.

3. Where will students go on the first day?
   A. Only the farm.
   B. The Science Museum and a local farm.
   C. Only the Science Museum.
   D. The zoo.

4. What should students NOT bring?
   A. A water bottle.
   B. A notebook and pen.
   C. Valuable items such as expensive phones or jewellery.
   D. A hat and comfortable shoes.

5. When should the bus leave school on Day 1?
   A. At 7:30 a.m.
   B. At 8:00 a.m.
   C. At 9:00 a.m.
   D. At 12:00 p.m.

---

**Answers and Analysis:**

**1. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段标题 "Notice to Students and Parents" 及第②段 "for all Grade 8 and Grade 9 students"

**【解析】** 通知面向八九年级学生及家长。B项正确。A、C、D项与对象不符。故选B。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "November 8–9, 2024 (Friday and Saturday)"

**【解析】** 研学旅行时间为2024年11月8–9日。B项正确。A、C、D项日期错误。故选B。

**3. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "visit the City Science Museum and a local farm" 及第③段 Day 1 安排

**【解析】** 首日行程包括科学博物馆和农场。B项正确。A、C、D项不完整或错误。故选B。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Do NOT bring valuable items (e.g. expensive phones, jewellery)"

**【解析】** 明确要求不得携带贵重物品（如贵重手机、首饰）。C项正确。A、B、D项为建议携带物品。故选C。

**5. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Bus departs at 8:00 a.m"

**【解析】** 第一天早上8点发车。B项正确。A为集合时间；C、D项与发车时间不符。故选B。
