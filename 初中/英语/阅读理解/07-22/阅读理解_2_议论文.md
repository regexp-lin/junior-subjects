# 阅读理解（议论文）

① **Do After-School Interest Classes Take Away Children's Rest Time?**

② In recent years, more and more parents send their children to after-school interest classes (课外兴趣班)—music, art, sports, and so on. But a question often comes up: do these classes take away children's rest time and harm their health? People have different views on this.

③ **Those who support after-school classes** believe they help children develop in many ways. Learning a musical instrument or a sport can make children more confident and creative. It also helps them find what they really enjoy. Besides, when children are doing something they like, they may feel relaxed instead of tired. Some parents say that their children are happier after joining a dance or football class because they have made new friends there.

④ **Those who are worried** argue that children already spend long hours at school. After school, they need time to rest, play freely, and sleep enough. Too many interest classes can make children feel stressed and sleepy. A recent study showed that some primary school students sleep less than eight hours because they have classes until 6 or 7 p.m. Lack of rest can affect (影响) both their health and their performance at school.

⑤ **The conclusion:** There is no simple yes or no answer. The key is balance. Children need both time to learn new skills and time to rest. Parents should listen to their children and not arrange (安排) too many classes. One or two interest classes a week may be fine, but filling every afternoon with classes is not a good idea. Rest and sleep are important for growing children—we should never forget that.

**注释：** affect 影响；arrange 安排

---

### 选择题

1. According to the passage, what do supporters think about after-school interest classes?
   A. They can help children become more confident and creative, and may help them feel relaxed when doing what they enjoy.
   B. They are a waste of money.
   C. They are only useful for older students.
   D. They should replace all school lessons.

2. What worry do some people have about after-school classes?
   A. The classes are too cheap.
   B. Children already spend long hours at school and need time to rest; too many classes can cause stress and lack of sleep.
   C. There are not enough interest classes available.
   D. Teachers in these classes are not well trained.

3. What did a recent study show?
   A. All children love going to interest classes.
   B. Parents prefer art classes to sports classes.
   C. Some primary school students sleep less than eight hours because they have classes until late.
   D. Interest classes have no effect on children's health.

4. What does the writer suggest parents do?
   A. Send their children to as many classes as possible.
   B. Never let their children join any interest class.
   C. Choose only music or art classes.
   D. Listen to their children and avoid arranging too many classes; one or two a week may be fine.

5. What can we infer from the passage?
   A. The writer thinks balance between learning and rest is important for children's health and growth.
   B. All after-school classes are harmful to children.
   C. Children should never attend any interest classes.
   D. Sleep is not important for primary school students.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Learning a musical instrument or a sport can make children more confident and creative... when children are doing something they like, they may feel relaxed instead of tired"

**【解析】** 支持者认为兴趣班能帮助孩子更自信、更有创造力，做喜欢的事时可能感到放松而非疲惫。A项正确。B项浪费钱、C项仅对大龄学生有用、D项替代学校课程均未提及。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "children already spend long hours at school. After school, they need time to rest... Too many interest classes can make children feel stressed and sleepy... some primary school students sleep less than eight hours"

**【解析】** 反对者担忧学生在校时间已很长，需要休息；过多兴趣班会让孩子压力大、睡眠不足。B项准确概括。A、C、D项均未提及。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "A recent study showed that some primary school students sleep less than eight hours because they have classes until 6 or 7 p.m."

**【解析】** 文中写明近期研究表明部分小学生因晚上6、7点仍有课而睡眠不足8小时。C项正确。A、B、D项均与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "Parents should listen to their children and not arrange (安排) too many classes. One or two interest classes a week may be fine"

**【解析】** 作者建议家长倾听孩子、不要安排太多课程，每周一两个兴趣班即可。D项正确。A项尽可能多与建议相反；B项完全不上、C项只选音乐或艺术均与原文不符。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "The key is balance. Children need both time to learn new skills and time to rest... Rest and sleep are important for growing children"

**【解析】** 作者强调关键在于平衡，孩子既需学习技能也需休息，休息和睡眠对成长中的孩子很重要。可推知作者认为学习与休息的平衡对儿童健康与成长至关重要。A项正确。B、C项过于绝对；D项与作者强调睡眠重要矛盾。故选A。
