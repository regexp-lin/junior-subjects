# 阅读理解（应用文）

① **School Canteen Menu Update Notice**

② Dear students and teachers, we are happy to tell you that our school canteen will start a new menu from September 1st. We have listened to your ideas and made some changes to offer you healthier and more tasty food.

③ **What is new:** First, we add more vegetables and fruit to every meal. Salad (沙拉) and fresh fruit will be available every day. Second, we reduce the amount of oil and salt in our dishes. Third, we now have a special area for students who need vegetarian (素食的) meals.

④ **Meal times stay the same:** Breakfast 7:00–7:30 a.m., Lunch 12:00–12:40 p.m., and Dinner 5:30–6:30 p.m. The prices will not change. You can still use your meal card to pay.

⑤ If you have any questions or suggestions about the new menu, please send an e-mail to canteen@school.edu or visit the canteen office. We hope you will enjoy the new dishes!

**注释：** salad 沙拉；vegetarian 素食的

---

### 选择题

1. When will the new canteen menu start?
   A. August 15th.
   B. September 1st.
   C. September 15th.
   D. October 1st.

2. What change has the canteen made to the meals?
   A. It has added more vegetables, fruit, and vegetarian choices.
   B. It has increased the price of all meals.
   C. It has closed the salad area.
   D. It has changed the meal times.

3. How can students pay for their meals?
   A. They must pay in cash.
   B. They can use their meal card.
   C. They need to buy tickets first.
   D. Meals are free for everyone.

4. What can we learn about the meal times?
   A. Lunch is served from 12:00 to 12:40 p.m.
   B. Breakfast ends at 8:00 a.m.
   C. Dinner is only available on weekdays.
   D. The meal times have been changed.

5. What is the main purpose of this notice?
   A. To ask students to work in the canteen.
   B. To introduce a new canteen menu and encourage students to try it.
   C. To tell students the canteen will be closed.
   D. To invite parents to visit the canteen.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "our school canteen will start a new menu from September 1st"

**【解析】** 文中明确说明新菜单自9月1日起开始。A项正确。B、C、D项日期均与原文不符。故选A。

**2. 【答案】B**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "we add more vegetables and fruit... Salad and fresh fruit will be available every day... we now have a special area for students who need vegetarian meals"

**【解析】** 文中说明增加了更多蔬菜水果、每天供应沙拉和新鲜水果，并增设素食专区。B项正确。A项涨价与"prices will not change"矛盾；C项关闭沙拉区与原文相反；D项改变用餐时间与第④段不符。故选B。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "You can still use your meal card to pay."

**【解析】** 文中写明仍可使用餐卡支付。C项正确。A项现金支付、B项先购票、D项免费均未提及或与原文不符。故选C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "Lunch 12:00–12:40 p.m."

**【解析】** 文中明确写午餐时间为12:00至12:40。D项正确。A项早餐8点结束与"7:00–7:30"不符；B项晚餐仅工作日、C项时间已改均未提及。故选D。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 主旨/写作意图

**【定位】** 全文，尤第②段 "we are happy to tell you... made some changes... healthier and more tasty food" 及第⑤段 "We hope you will enjoy the new dishes!"

**【解析】** 全文介绍食堂菜单改版内容、新特点及反馈方式，目的在于推出新菜单并鼓励师生品尝。A项正确。B、C、D项均与公告内容不符。故选A。
