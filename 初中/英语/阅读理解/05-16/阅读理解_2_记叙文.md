> **05-16 第2篇**

# 阅读理解（记叙文）

① Zhang Ming lived in a small apartment building in the city. His next-door neighbor was an old man named Mr. Wang, who lived alone. Mr. Wang was over seventy years old. He walked slowly and sometimes had trouble carrying heavy things. Zhang Ming often saw him going up and down the stairs with difficulty.

② One Saturday morning, Zhang Ming noticed that Mr. Wang's door was closed and he did not hear any sound from inside for two days. He felt a little worried. He knocked on the door and asked, "Mr. Wang, are you OK?" After a few seconds, Mr. Wang opened the door. He looked pale and weak. "I have a cold," he said. "I haven't been able to buy food or medicine. I'm too tired to go out."

③ Zhang Ming immediately went to the supermarket and bought some rice, vegetables, and fruit for Mr. Wang. He also bought medicine for his cold. When he came back, he helped Mr. Wang cook a simple meal. Mr. Wang's eyes were wet. "Thank you, young man," he said. "I have no family here. You are like a grandson to me."

④ From that day on, Zhang Ming decided to help Mr. Wang regularly. Every weekend, he would knock on Mr. Wang's door to see if he needed anything. Sometimes he helped carry groceries (日用品) upstairs. Sometimes he sat with Mr. Wang and talked with him so the old man would not feel lonely. Zhang Ming's parents supported him. "Helping others makes our community a better place," his mother said.

⑤ One day, Mr. Wang told Zhang Ming, "You have taught me something important. Kindness can make a big difference in someone's life." Zhang Ming smiled. He did not help Mr. Wang to get a reward — he did it because it was the right thing to do. And in return, he learned that even small acts of kindness can bring warmth to both the helper and the one who receives help.

**注释：** pale 苍白的；groceries 日用品；community 社区；reward 回报；warmth 温暖

---

### 选择题

1. Who is Mr. Wang according to paragraph ①?
   A. Zhang Ming's next-door neighbor, an old man who lives alone.
   B. Zhang Ming's grandfather.
   C. A doctor who helps old people.
   D. Zhang Ming's teacher.

2. Why did Zhang Ming knock on Mr. Wang's door that Saturday?
   A. He wanted to borrow something from Mr. Wang.
   B. Mr. Wang invited him to visit.
   C. He needed to return a book to Mr. Wang.
   D. He was worried because he had not heard any sound for two days.

3. What did Zhang Ming do to help Mr. Wang that day?
   A. He called a doctor to come and see Mr. Wang.
   B. He took Mr. Wang to the hospital.
   C. He bought food and medicine, and helped cook a meal.
   D. He asked his parents to look after Mr. Wang.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ④ about Zhang Ming?
   A. He only helped Mr. Wang once.
   B. He was forced by his parents to help Mr. Wang.
   C. He made helping Mr. Wang a regular habit and cared about his loneliness.
   D. He stopped helping after Mr. Wang got better.

5. What did Zhang Ming learn from helping Mr. Wang?
   A. Small acts of kindness can bring warmth to both the helper and the one who receives help.
   B. Old people should live with their families.
   C. Helping others can bring money as a reward.
   D. Living alone is dangerous for old people.

---

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段 "His next-door neighbor was an old man named Mr. Wang, who lived alone. Mr. Wang was over seventy years old."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明 Mr. Wang 是张明的隔壁邻居，一位独居的七十多岁老人。A 项正确。B 项"张明的爷爷"原文未提及，文中 Mr. Wang 说"你就像我的孙子"是比喻；C 项"帮助老人的医生"和 D 项"张明的老师"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "Zhang Ming noticed that Mr. Wang's door was closed and he did not hear any sound from inside for two days. He felt a little worried. He knocked on the door"

**【解析】** 张明发现王先生的门关着、两天没听到任何声音，感到担心，于是敲门。D 项正确。A 项"想借东西"、B 项"王先生邀请他"和 C 项"还书"均无依据。故选 D。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第③段 "Zhang Ming immediately went to the supermarket and bought some rice, vegetables, and fruit for Mr. Wang. He also bought medicine for his cold... he helped Mr. Wang cook a simple meal"

**【解析】** 原文清楚描述张明去超市买了大米、蔬菜、水果和感冒药，并帮王先生做了简餐。C 项正确。A 项"打电话叫医生"、B 项"带王先生去医院"和 D 项"让父母照顾"均未在文中出现。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第④段 "From that day on, Zhang Ming decided to help Mr. Wang regularly. Every weekend, he would knock on Mr. Wang's door... Sometimes he sat with Mr. Wang and talked with him so the old man would not feel lonely"

**【解析】** 从那天起张明决定定期帮助王先生，每周末都会去看看是否需要什么，有时陪他聊天以免老人孤独，可推断他把帮助王先生当成了习惯并关心其孤独感。C 项正确。A 项"只帮了一次"与"regularly""every weekend"矛盾；B 项"被父母逼迫"与"父母支持他"矛盾；D 项"王先生好后就不帮了"原文未提及。故选 C。

**5. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第⑤段 "he learned that even small acts of kindness can bring warmth to both the helper and the one who receives help"

**【解析】** 张明从这件事学到：即使微小的善举也能给帮助者和被帮助者都带来温暖。A 项正确。B 项"老人应和家人同住"是观点陈述，非张明所学；C 项"帮助他人能得到金钱回报"与"他不是为了得到回报"矛盾；D 项"老人独居危险"过于片面，非文章主旨。故选 A。
