# 阅读理解（说明文）

① Sleep is very important for everyone, but it is especially important for teenagers. Why? Because teenagers are growing quickly, and their bodies and brains need more rest than adults. Many studies show that teenagers should get about 8 to 10 hours of sleep each night. However, a lot of young people only sleep 6 or 7 hours because of homework, phone games, or other activities.

② When teenagers do not get enough sleep, they may find it hard to focus in class. They may feel tired during the day and even fall asleep at their desks. Their memory can also become weaker, so it takes longer to remember new words or understand difficult subjects. Some students think staying up late to study will help them get better grades, but the opposite is true — good sleep actually helps the brain work better the next day.

③ Lack of sleep can also affect teenagers' health. It may make them feel more stressed or worried. Some teenagers who sleep too little become easier to get angry or sad. Their bodies may also get sick more often because sleep helps the immune system (免疫系统) stay strong. When we sleep, our body repairs itself and prepares for the next day.

④ How can teenagers improve their sleep? First, try to go to bed at the same time every night. Second, do not use phones or computers for at least one hour before sleep, because the blue light from screens can make it harder to fall asleep. Third, keep the bedroom dark and quiet. Fourth, avoid drinking coffee or tea in the afternoon or evening.

⑤ Good sleep is not a waste of time — it is a key part of a healthy life. Parents and schools should help teenagers understand this and create better habits. When young people sleep well, they feel happier, learn better, and stay healthier. That is why we should all take sleep seriously.

**注释：** immune 免疫的；repair 修复；screen 屏幕

### 选择题

1. According to paragraph ①, how many hours of sleep should teenagers get each night?
   A. 8 to 10 hours.
   B. 6 to 7 hours.
   C. 5 to 6 hours.
   D. 10 to 12 hours.

2. What may happen when teenagers do not get enough sleep, according to paragraph ②?
   A. They will get better grades.
   B. They will feel more energetic during the day.
   C. They may have trouble focusing and weaker memory.
   D. They will study more effectively at night.

3. According to paragraph ④, what should teenagers avoid before sleep?
   A. Going to bed at the same time every night.
   B. Keeping the bedroom dark and quiet.
   C. Using phones or computers within one hour before going to bed.
   D. Drinking coffee or tea in the afternoon or evening.

4. What can we infer from paragraph ③ about sleep and health?
   A. Sleep has little effect on teenagers' feelings.
   B. Teenagers who sleep little never get sick.
   C. Sleep only affects the brain, not the body.
   D. Good sleep helps the body stay strong and fight illness.

5. What is the main purpose of this passage?
   A. To compare sleep habits of adults and teenagers.
   B. To tell teenagers to sleep as long as possible every day.
   C. To introduce the history of sleep research.
   D. To explain why sleep is important for teenagers and how to improve it.

### 详细解析

**1. 【答案】A**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第①段第3句 "Many studies show that teenagers should get about 8 to 10 hours of sleep each night."

**【解析】** 原文明确说明研究表明青少年每晚应睡8到10小时。A 项正确。B 项"6到7小时"是文中提到的许多年轻人实际睡眠时长，而非建议值；C 项"5到6小时"和 D 项"10到12小时"均与原文不符。故选 A。

**2. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第②段 "They may find it hard to focus in class... Their memory can also become weaker"

**【解析】** 原文指出睡眠不足会导致上课难以集中注意力、记忆力变弱。C 项正确。A 项"成绩会更好"与原文"相反的是，良好睡眠才有助于第二天大脑工作"矛盾；B 项"白天更有精神"与"白天感到疲倦"相反；D 项"晚上学习更有效"属于无中生有。故选 C。

**3. 【答案】C**

**【题型】** 细节理解

**【定位】** 第④段 "do not use phones or computers for at least one hour before sleep, because the blue light from screens can make it harder to fall asleep"

**【解析】** 原文建议睡前至少一小时不要使用手机或电脑，因屏幕蓝光会影响入睡。C 项"睡前使用手机或电脑"正是应避免的行为。A 项"每晚固定时间睡觉"和 B 项"保持卧室黑暗安静"是建议去做的事，非避免项；D 项"下午或晚上喝咖啡或茶"虽是第四点建议，但题干强调"before sleep"（睡前），C 项更直接对应睡前时段。故选 C。

**4. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 推理判断

**【定位】** 第③段 "Their bodies may also get sick more often because sleep helps the immune system stay strong. When we sleep, our body repairs itself"

**【解析】** 原文说明睡眠有助于免疫系统保持强壮、身体在睡眠时自我修复，可推断良好睡眠帮助身体保持强壮、抵御疾病。D 项正确。A 项"睡眠对情绪影响很小"与"更容易生气或难过"矛盾；B 项"睡眠少的人从不生病"过于绝对且与原文相反；C 项"睡眠只影响大脑不影响身体"与"身体更容易生病""身体自我修复"矛盾。故选 D。

**5. 【答案】D**

**【题型】** 主旨大意

**【定位】** 全文：第①段强调睡眠对青少年的重要性，第②③段说明睡眠不足的影响，第④段提出改善建议，第⑤段总结应认真对待睡眠。

**【解析】** 全文围绕青少年睡眠的重要性、不良影响及改善方法展开，D 项准确概括主旨。A 项"比较成人和青少年睡眠习惯"文中未重点比较；B 项"尽可能多睡"过于片面，文中建议是8-10小时；C 项"介绍睡眠研究历史"文中未涉及。故选 D。
