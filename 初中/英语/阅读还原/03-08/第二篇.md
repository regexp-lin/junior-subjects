# 03-08 第二篇

**The Amazing Power of Teamwork**

Have you ever tried to move a heavy wooden desk all by yourself? It is probably very difficult, if not impossible. [1] This simple everyday example clearly shows us the amazing power of teamwork.

In today's modern society, working together to reach a common goal is an essential skill. [2] One of the biggest advantages of teamwork is that it brings different minds together. Every person in a group has their own unique strengths and past experiences. [3] When people share their thoughts, they can often find much better solutions to complex problems.

Furthermore, working in a team naturally helps you learn from others. For example, in a science project at school, you might be great at writing, while your partner is excellent at drawing. [4] Your partner's amazing drawing techniques might inspire you, and your clear writing skills could help them express their thoughts much better.

Moreover, teamwork provides important emotional support. When you face a huge challenge completely alone, it is easy to feel stressed and want to give up early. [5] Having friendly teammates to cheer you on gives you the courage and inner strength to keep going until you finally succeed.

## 选项

A. But if you invite some friends to push together, the heavy desk moves easily.
B. This means you do not need to listen to other people's opinions anymore.
C. By sharing the work, you both get a great chance to learn something new.
D. It allows people to achieve big things that they could never do on their own.
E. However, when you have a supportive team behind you, the pressure is shared.
F. By combining these different talents, the group becomes powerfully creative.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] D   [3] F   [4] C   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：语境转折与情景呼应。空前一句提出了一个问题并自行回答：一个人搬沉重的木桌子如果不是不可能的话也必然非常困难。A选项以“But”进行转折：“But if you invite some friends to push together, the heavy desk moves easily”（但如果你邀请一些朋友一起推，沉重的桌子就能轻松移动）。“heavy desk”实现原词复现。这种“一个人干不成，几个人在一起做就很轻松”的反差，完美引出了空后的核心句：“这个简单的日常例子清楚地向我们展示了团队合作的惊人力量”。
- **[2] D**：
  - **线索**：代词指代与概念阐释。空前提到“working together to reach a common goal is an essential skill”（为实现共同目标而合作是一项基本技能）。D选项中的代词“It”指代了前文的“working together / teamwork”（团队合作），且“allows people to achieve big things that they could never do on their own”（它允许人们完成他们靠自己永远无法完成的大事）正是对团队合作这项技能核心威力的解释，承上启下，逻辑贯通。
- **[3] F**：
  - **线索**：同义指代与因果逻辑。空前提到了每个人都有自己独特的优势（strengths）和过往经验。F选项中的“these different talents”（这些不同的才能）完美替换并指代了前面的“strengths and past experiences”。且F选项的意思（通过结合这些才能，团队具有了强大的创造力）自然而然地过渡到了空后句（当人们分享想法时，通常能找到解决复杂问题的更好方法），这正是一种群体创造力的具体体现。
- **[4] C**：
  - **线索**：举例印证与主题复现。本段的主题句是首句“团队合作帮你向他人学习”。空前给出了一个具体的科学项目例子（你擅长写作，伙伴擅长画画）。C选项“By sharing the work, you both get a great chance to learn something new”（通过分担工作，你们都获得了学习新事物的好机会）紧紧扣住了“learn from others”的段落主旨。同时，也为后面说明“你从伙伴那学到了画画技巧，伙伴从你这学到了写作技巧”这一双连贯的动作作了铺垫。
- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：逻辑转折与情感对比。空前极速描绘了“独自面临巨大挑战时，容易感到压力大并想提早放弃”的消极场景。E选项利用“However”（然而）发生强转折，“when you have a supportive team behind you, the pressure is shared”（当你身后有一个支持你的团队时，压力就会被分担）。这不仅对应了“一个人感到 stressed（压力）”，还引出了空后“队友为你加油鼓劲带给你勇气坚持下去”的情感支持，前后情感色彩对比鲜明。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **B. This means you do not need to listen to other people's opinions anymore.**：该选项中带有一些常见副词“anymore”以及看似结论性的“This means”，容易让考生当作结语。但其字面意思“这意味着你不再需要听取别人的意见”充满了自大和孤立主义，与文章整体极力倡导的“teamwork（团队合作）”、“share thoughts（分享想法）”的核心思想背道而驰。这是利用价值观反向误导的典型干扰项。
