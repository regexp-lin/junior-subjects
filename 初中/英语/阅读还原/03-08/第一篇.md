# 03-08 第一篇

**The Benefits of Reading Paper Books**

In today's digital world, many people prefer to read on their phones or tablets. [1] However, a growing number of readers are returning to traditional paper books. It turns out that holding a real book in your hands offers several unique advantages. 

First of all, reading a physical book is much better for your eyes. Staring at bright screens for a long time can be very harmful. [2] In contrast, the natural light reflected from printed pages is gentle and comfortable. This allows you to read for a longer period of time without feeling tired.

Secondly, paper books help you concentrate better. When you read on a screen, you are often distracted by sudden notifications. [3] A physical book, on the other hand, offers a quiet space free from these digital interruptions. As a result, you can become fully immersed in the story and understand it more deeply.

Finally, reading paper books before sleep can actually help you rest better. [4] Electronic screens produce blue light, which tricks your brain into staying awake. Without this harmful light, your body can relax naturally, allowing you to fall asleep much faster and wake up feeling refreshed.

All in all, traditional reading habits still have a very special place in our modern lives. The feeling of turning real pages is something a screen can never provide. [5] Your eyes and brain will certainly thank you for this healthy choice!

## 选项

A. These sudden pop-up messages pull your attention away from what you are reading.
B. In fact, experts strongly recommend reading a printed page before bedtime.
C. Therefore, it is no surprise that traditional reading is completely disappearing.
D. They offer amazing convenience because you can carry thousands of stories in your pocket.
E. So, next time you want to relax, pick up a real book instead of your phone.
F. As a result, many people suffer from dry eyes and painful headaches.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] D   [2] F   [3] A   [4] B   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] D**：
  - **线索**：代词指代与逻辑转折。空前提到了“many people prefer to read on their phones or tablets”（很多人喜欢在手机或平板电脑上阅读）。D选项中的代词“They”完美指代了前面的“phones or tablets”，并解释了人们喜欢它们的原因“offer amazing convenience”（提供了惊人的便利）。然后紧接着的空后一句以“However”（然而）转折，引出文章的主题：越来越多的人正在回归传统的纸质书阅读。
- **[2] F**：
  - **线索**：因果衔接与语义对比。空前指出“Staring at bright screens for a long time can be very harmful”（长时间盯着明亮的屏幕是有害的）。F选项使用了因果连词“As a result”（结果），“many people suffer from dry eyes and painful headaches”（许多人患有干眼症和痛苦的头痛）正是不良习惯造成的具体危害后果。空后的“In contrast”（相比之下）又把话题拉回了纸质书反射光的温和舒适，逻辑严密。
- **[3] A**：
  - **线索**：同义替换与语境联系。空前描述了屏幕阅读的缺点：“you are often distracted by sudden notifications”（你经常被突然的通知分心）。A选项中的“These sudden pop-up messages”（这些突然弹出的消息）完美同义替换并指代了前一句的“notifications”，并进一步说明了这些消息带来的负面影响“pull your attention away”（转移你的注意力）。从而突显了后一句纸质书能够提供“安静空间”的优势。
- **[4] B**：
  - **线索**：观点支撑与逻辑递进。空前提出了一个观点：“reading. paper books before sleep can actually help you rest better”（睡前读纸质书实际上有助于你更好地休息）。B选项“In fact, experts strongly recommend reading a printed page before bedtime”（事实上，专家强烈建议睡前阅读印刷页面）用专家的权威建议进一步强化了这个观点。随后空后的内容详细解释了为什么看屏幕不能好好休息（蓝光欺骗大脑），正反对比证明了专家的建议是正确的。
- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：总结呼应与提出建议。前面整个自然段都在总结纸质阅读的独特地位和翻页的真实触感。E选项“So, next time you want to relax, pick up a real book instead of your phone”（所以，下次你想放松时，拿起一本真正的书而不是你的手机）是一个典型的总结性建议句。填入E项后，与空后的“你的眼睛和大脑肯定会感谢你的健康选择！”完美呼应，构成了完整的文末升华。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **C. Therefore, it is no surprise that traditional reading is completely disappearing.**：此选项由于带有“Therefore”以及“traditional reading”等词汇，容易在粗心阅读时被选入首段或尾段。但其句意指的是“传统阅读完全消失并不令人意外”。这与第一段核心句“a growing number of readers are returning to traditional paper books”以及全文大肆赞扬纸质书好处的主题完全相反，属于主题相悖的典型干扰选项。
