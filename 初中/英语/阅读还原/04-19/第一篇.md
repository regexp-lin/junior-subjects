# 04-19 第一篇

**Solar Energy: A Bright Future for Our Planet**

The sun sends enough energy to Earth in one hour to power the whole world for a year. Yet for a long time, people did not know how to use this energy well. [1] In recent years, solar panels（太阳能板） have become cheaper and more efficient. Now millions of homes and buildings around the world get their electricity from the sun. Solar power has grown from a dream into a real solution for clean energy. It shows that we can meet our needs without harming the planet.

How do solar panels work? They are made of special materials that turn sunlight directly into electricity. [2] When the sun shines, electrons（电子） in these materials start to move and create an electric current. This process produces no smoke, no pollution, and no greenhouse gases. Unlike coal or oil, solar energy is renewable—the sun will keep shining for billions of years. That is why many countries are now investing heavily in solar technology.

Solar power is not only good for the environment, but also for people's wallets. [3] After the initial cost of buying and installing panels, the electricity they produce is almost free. Homeowners can save a lot of money on their power bills over time. In some places, families can even sell extra electricity back to the power company. As a result, solar panels have become a popular choice for both new buildings and old houses.

Of course, solar energy has some challenges. The sun does not shine at night, and cloudy days produce less power. [4] Scientists are working on better ways to store solar energy in large batteries so that we can use it when the sun is not out. They are also improving the design of panels to work better in weak light. These advances will make solar power more reliable and useful in the years to come.

[5] From powering small homes to lighting whole cities, solar energy is changing how we live. It offers hope for a cleaner, greener world. By choosing solar and other clean energy sources, we take an important step toward protecting our planet for future generations.

## 选项

A. Today, things have changed in an exciting way.
B. Coal and oil will run out one day, but the sun will not.
C. The basic idea is simple: light hits the panel and creates electricity.
D. In fact, many families find that solar power pays for itself within a few years.
E. Research and technology are solving these problems step by step.
F. In short, solar energy is no longer just an idea—it is part of our daily life.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] C   [3] D   [4] E   [5] F

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：过去与现在的对比过渡。空前描述"for a long time, people did not know how to use this energy well"，指出过去的困境。A选项"Today, things have changed in an exciting way"（如今，情况以令人振奋的方式发生了变化）中"Today"与空前"for a long time"形成时间对比，"changed in an exciting way"自然引出空后"solar panels have become cheaper and more efficient...grown from a dream into a real solution"，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] C**：
  - **线索**：太阳能板工作原理解释。空前提问"How do solar panels work?"并说明"turn sunlight directly into electricity"，空后详述"When the sun shines, electrons...move and create an electric current"。C选项"The basic idea is simple: light hits the panel and creates electricity"（基本思路很简单：光照射到板上就产生电）与空前问句对应，用"basic idea"总领，空后"electrons...create an electric current"具体展开，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] D**：
  - **线索**：经济收益与用户实惠。空前提出"solar power is not only good for the environment, but also for people's wallets"，空后详述"initial cost...electricity they produce is almost free...save a lot of money...sell extra electricity"。D选项"In fact, many families find that solar power pays for itself within a few years"（事实上，许多家庭发现太阳能几年内就能回本）与空前"for people's wallets"及空后省钱、卖电形成因果，衔接自然。

- **[4] E**：
  - **线索**：挑战与解决方向。空前列出挑战"The sun does not shine at night...cloudy days produce less power"，空后举例"working on better ways to store...improving the design...These advances will make solar power more reliable"。E选项"Research and technology are solving these problems step by step"（研究和技术正在逐步解决这些问题）中"these problems"指代空前挑战，"solving...step by step"与空后具体措施呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[5] F**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后列举"From powering small homes to lighting whole cities"并总结"offers hope for a cleaner, greener world...protecting our planet"。F选项"In short, solar energy is no longer just an idea—it is part of our daily life"（简而言之，太阳能不再只是设想，它已成为我们日常生活的一部分）用"In short"总结全文，"part of our daily life"与空后应用场景完美呼应，收束得当。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **B. Coal and oil will run out one day, but the sun will not.**：对比化石能源与太阳能的可持续性，易被误填[2]或[4]。但空[2]空前问的是"how do solar panels work"，需回答工作原理，C更贴切；空[4]空前已明确提到"renewable"及空后内容聚焦储存与弱光设计，B属能源对比类，与具体技术解决无关。B属"主题相关但逻辑错位"型干扰。
