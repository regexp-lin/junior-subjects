# 04-19 第二篇

**Why Honesty Matters: Building Trust in Every Relationship**

Honesty is one of the most valued qualities in any society. When we tell the truth, we show others that we respect them and that our words can be trusted. [1] A lie might seem easier at the moment, but it often leads to more trouble later. Hidden lies can damage friendships, hurt family bonds, and even destroy trust that took years to build. In contrast, telling the truth, even when it is difficult, strengthens our relationships and helps us sleep better at night.

Children learn about honesty from a very young age. [2] Parents and teachers often use simple stories to show why lying is wrong and why telling the truth is right. When a child admits a mistake instead of hiding it, adults usually praise the child for being brave. This early teaching shapes how young people think about right and wrong for the rest of their lives. Honesty becomes a habit when it is practiced from the start.

In school and at work, honesty plays a key role in success. [3] Students who copy from others may get good grades once, but they do not really learn anything. In the end, they may fail exams or lose the trust of their teachers. Workers who tell lies to their bosses or teammates rarely keep their jobs for long. Being honest might sometimes feel harder, but it is the only way to earn real respect and build a strong reputation.

What if we have already told a lie? Is it too late to change? [4] Admitting that we were wrong and saying sorry can be difficult and embarrassing. However, most people are willing to forgive when they see that we are truly sorry. A sincere apology can repair damaged trust and sometimes make a relationship even stronger than before. The sooner we tell the truth, the easier it is to fix the harm we caused.

[5] Whether at home, in school, or at work, honesty forms the foundation of trust. It costs nothing to tell the truth, but a single lie can cost us everything. By choosing honesty every day, we build a better life for ourselves and for those around us.

## 选项

A. However, the consequences of lying are usually much worse.
B. They learn by watching how adults act and by hearing stories about honesty.
C. Cheating and lying might bring short-term gains, but they always fail in the long run.
D. The good news is that it is never too late to start being honest.
E. To conclude, honesty is not just a moral choice—it is the key to lasting relationships.
F. In the beginning, young children do not always understand why honesty matters.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：谎言与真相的对比。空前描述"tell the truth...respect them...words can be trusted"，指出诚实的好处。A选项"However, the consequences of lying are usually much worse"（然而，说谎的后果通常更糟）用"However"转折，与空前形成对比，空后"A lie might seem easier...but it often leads to more trouble...damage friendships...hurt family bonds"具体展开谎言的后果，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：儿童如何学习诚实。空前提出"Children learn about honesty from a very young age"，空后详述"Parents and teachers often use simple stories...When a child admits a mistake...adults usually praise...This early teaching shapes..."。B选项"They learn by watching how adults act and by hearing stories about honesty"（他们通过观察成人行为、听取关于诚实的故事来学习）与空后"stories"、"adults praise"等一一对应，说明学习途径，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：学校与职场中诚实与作弊的对比。空前提出"honesty plays a key role in success"，空后举例"Students who copy...may get good grades once, but...do not really learn...may fail...Workers who tell lies...rarely keep their jobs"。C选项"Cheating and lying might bring short-term gains, but they always fail in the long run"（作弊和说谎可能带来短期收益，但长远来看总会失败）与空后学生作弊、工人说谎的后果完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：补救谎言与道歉。空前提问"What if we have already told a lie? Is it too late to change?"，空后详述"Admitting...can be difficult...most people are willing to forgive...sincere apology can repair...The sooner we tell the truth, the easier...to fix"。D选项"The good news is that it is never too late to start being honest"（好消息是，开始诚实永远不会太晚）直接回答空前疑问，与空后补救措施形成因果，衔接顺畅。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后列举"Whether at home, in school, or at work"并总结"forms the foundation of trust...costs nothing...can cost us everything"。E选项"To conclude, honesty is not just a moral choice—it is the key to lasting relationships"（总之，诚实不仅是道德选择，更是维系长久关系的关键）用"To conclude"总结全文，"key to lasting relationships"与空后"foundation of trust"完美呼应，收束得当。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. In the beginning, young children do not always understand why honesty matters.**：描述幼儿对诚实的认知，易被误填[2]。但空[2]空前已说"Children learn about honesty"，空后重点在"Parents and teachers use stories...praise...early teaching shapes"，强调学习过程与途径，F强调"不理解"与后文"通过故事和表扬来教"矛盾。B的"learn by watching...hearing stories"与空后完全契合。F属"主题相关但语义冲突"型干扰。
