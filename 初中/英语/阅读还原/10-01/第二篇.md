**The Educational Value of Travel**

Travel has long been considered one of the best teachers. When we leave our familiar surroundings and visit new places, we encounter different cultures, languages, and ways of life. This experience broadens our horizons in ways that no textbook alone can achieve. [1] Over time, many travelers find that their worldview has changed and they become more curious about the rest of the world.

One of the most obvious benefits of travel for students is the improvement of language skills. When you visit a country where another language is spoken, you have the chance to practice listening and speaking in real situations. [2] This kind of practice often leads to faster progress than studying alone at home, because every conversation becomes a lesson.

Travel also teaches independence and problem-solving. Planning a trip, navigating unfamiliar places, and handling unexpected situations all require young people to think on their feet. [3] With each challenge overcome, they grow more capable of managing their own lives and making smart decisions when things do not go as planned.

Moreover, travel fosters empathy and cultural understanding. Meeting people from different backgrounds helps us appreciate diversity and recognize that there are many valid ways to live and think. [4] Such experiences can chip away at stereotypes and help young people form friendships that cross cultural boundaries.

Some families worry that travel is too expensive or distracting from schoolwork. However, even short trips to nearby cities or natural areas can offer valuable learning experiences. [5] What matters most is the attitude we bring: when we travel with open eyes and an open mind, even a short journey can teach us something new about ourselves and the world.

## 选项

A. Travel is not just about seeing famous sights—it is about opening our minds to new ideas and developing a global perspective that lasts a lifetime.
B. Using a language in its natural setting is often more effective than memorizing vocabulary in a classroom, as it connects words to real experiences and emotions.
C. These real-world challenges help students build confidence and learn skills that will serve them well in adulthood, far beyond what they can learn from books.
D. Exposure to different cultures at a young age can reduce prejudice and encourage a more open-minded attitude toward the world and its people.
E. The key is to approach travel as an opportunity for growth, combining exploration with reflection, so that every journey becomes a lesson in itself.
F. For example, a student who visits Beijing might practice Mandarin with local vendors and learn about Chinese history at the same time.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述旅行开阔眼界、接触不同文化，空后说"their worldview has changed and they become more curious"。A的"opening our minds to new ideas and developing a global perspective"与空后世界观转变、好奇心增强自然衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说在目的语国家有机会在真实情境中练习听说，空后说"leads to faster progress than studying alone at home"。B的"Using a language in its natural setting"承接前文实践场景，"more effective than memorizing vocabulary in a classroom"与空后"faster progress than studying alone"呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举旅行中需应对的挑战（规划、导航、处理意外），空后说"With each challenge overcome, they grow more capable"。C的"These real-world challenges"指代前文，"build confidence and learn skills"与空后"grow more capable of managing their own lives"衔接。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说接触不同背景的人有助于欣赏多样性，空后说"chip away at stereotypes and help young people form friendships"。D的"Exposure to different cultures"与"reduce prejudice"承接前文，"open-minded attitude"与空后"friendships that cross cultural boundaries"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前提出对旅行费用和影响的担忧，并指出短途旅行也有价值，空后说"when we travel with open eyes and an open mind, even a short journey can teach us something new"。E的"approach travel as an opportunity for growth"与空后态度、收获呼应，"every journey becomes a lesson"适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, a student who visits Beijing might practice Mandarin with local vendors and learn about Chinese history at the same time.**：以For example开头，属具体举例（北京、商贩、历史），各空均为概括或过渡句；易误放[2]，但[2]需的是语言实践与课堂学习的概括对比，非个例说明。
