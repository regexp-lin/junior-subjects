**Goodbyes and New Beginnings**

Life is full of endings and new starts. We leave behind old schools, old friends, and old habits as we move forward. Saying goodbye can be hard—we may feel sad, scared, or uncertain about what comes next. [1] Yet every ending also brings the chance for a new beginning, and learning to embrace both is an important part of growing up.

When we graduate from school or move to a new place, we leave behind familiar faces and routines. It is natural to feel nostalgic and to miss what we had. [2] Allowing ourselves to feel these emotions helps us process the change, but we should not let sadness keep us from looking ahead with hope.

New beginnings often require us to step out of our comfort zone. We might meet new people, try new things, or face new challenges. [3] Although this can feel scary at first, it is through these experiences that we grow stronger, learn more about ourselves, and discover new possibilities.

Some goodbyes are permanent—we may lose someone we love or something we cherished. Those losses hurt deeply. [4] With time, support from others, and our own courage, we can learn to carry the memory with us while still moving forward in life.

Every new chapter in life starts with an ending. As we say goodbye to the past, we open the door to the future. [5] Embracing change—with its mix of sadness and hope—is how we turn endings into opportunities and keep growing throughout our lives.

## 选项

A. Understanding that endings and beginnings are connected can help us face transitions with more courage and less fear.
B. It is healthy to acknowledge our feelings, and it is equally important to welcome what lies ahead.
C. Stepping into the unknown is often the path to discovering who we are and what we can become.
D. Grieving is a necessary part of healing, and healing does not mean forgetting.
E. Learning to accept both the pain of goodbye and the promise of new starts is a skill that serves us for life.
F. For example, moving from primary school to middle school can be exciting but also nerve-wracking.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说告别带来难过、担忧、不确定，空后强调"every ending also brings the chance for a new beginning"。A的"Understanding that endings and beginnings are connected"与"embrace both"对应，"face transitions with more courage and less fear"与"learning to embrace"一致，概括该段主旨。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前描述离开熟悉人事后的怀旧与想念，空后说"Allowing ourselves to feel these emotions... but we should not let sadness keep us from looking ahead"。B的"acknowledge our feelings"与"feel these emotions"对应，"welcome what lies ahead"与"looking ahead with hope"一致，概括该段建议。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说新开始常需走出舒适区、面对新人与挑战，空后说"Although this can feel scary... we grow stronger... discover new possibilities"。C的"Stepping into the unknown"与"step out of comfort zone"对应，"path to discovering who we are"与"learn more about ourselves"一致，概括该段主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说某些告别是永久的、失去会带来深痛，空后说"With time, support... we can learn to carry the memory... while still moving forward"。D的"Grieving is a necessary part of healing"与"carry the memory"呼应，"healing does not mean forgetting"与"moving forward"形成平衡，概括该段主旨。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前总结新旧章节的交替，空后无。E的"accept both the pain of goodbye and the promise of new starts"与全文主题呼应，"skill that serves us for life"升华主题，适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, moving from primary school to middle school can be exciting but also nerve-wracking.**：以For example开头，与过渡情境相关，但属具体举例，各空均为概括或过渡句；易误放[1]或[2]，但两空分别需总论与情绪处理的概括，非举例。
