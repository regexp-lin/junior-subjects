# 09-21 第二篇

**Protecting Your Digital Privacy**

We live much of our lives online. We shop, bank, chat, and share on the internet. But every click and every post can leave a trail of personal information. [1] Understanding how our data is collected and used is the first step toward taking control of our digital privacy.

Companies and websites collect data to understand their users better. Some of this helps them improve their services. But data can also be sold, shared, or leaked to others. [2] Being aware of who has access to our information and how it might be used helps us make smarter choices about what we share online.

Passwords are our first line of defense. Weak or reused passwords make it easy for hackers to break into our accounts. Using strong, unique passwords and enabling two-factor authentication can greatly improve security. [3] Strong account security is essential for keeping our personal information and online identity safe from attackers.

We should also think about what we post. Once something is online, it can be copied, shared, and hard to delete. [4] Being careful about what we share and with whom can prevent our private moments from becoming public without our consent.

Laws and regulations are slowly catching up. Some countries have passed stronger data protection rules. Still, we cannot rely only on laws. [5] Protecting our digital privacy requires a combination of personal habits, smart choices, and support from better laws and policies.

## 选项

A. Learning how our personal information is gathered and used online is essential for protecting our privacy in the digital age.
B. Knowing who can see our data and what they might do with it allows us to share more wisely.
C. Good password habits and extra security steps are key to defending our accounts and personal information.
D. Thinking before we post and controlling who sees our content can help us avoid unwanted exposure of our private lives.
E. Keeping our data private demands effort from individuals, better choices in technology use, and stronger legal protections.
F. For example, many social media apps track users' location and activity even when the app is not being used.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述生活在线化、每次点击和发帖都会留下个人信息，空后强调理解数据如何被收集和使用是掌控隐私的第一步。A的"Learning how our personal information is gathered and used online"与"Understanding how our data is collected and used"呼应，"essential for protecting our privacy"与"taking control of our digital privacy"一致。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说公司收集数据有利有弊，数据可能被出售、共享或泄露，空后强调了解谁在接触信息及用途有助于做出更明智分享决策。B的"Knowing who can see our data and what they might do with it"与"who has access to our information and how it might be used"直接对应，"share more wisely"与"make smarter choices about what we share"一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说弱密码或重复密码易被破解，强密码和双因素认证可提升安全，空后强调账户安全对保护个人信息的必要性。C的"Good password habits and extra security steps"与"strong, unique passwords and enabling two-factor authentication"呼应，"key to defending our accounts"与"essential for keeping our personal information safe"一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说网络内容易被复制分享、难以删除，空后强调谨慎分享和限制可见对象可避免隐私泄露。D的"Thinking before we post and controlling who sees our content"与"Being careful about what we share and with whom"呼应，"avoid unwanted exposure of our private lives"与"prevent our private moments from becoming public without our consent"一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说法律在跟进、不能只靠法律，空后总结保护隐私需要个人习惯、明智选择与法律政策共同作用。E的"effort from individuals, better choices in technology use, and stronger legal protections"与"personal habits, smart choices, and support from better laws and policies"对应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, many social media apps track users' location and activity even when the app is not being used.**：以For example开头，是具体应用行为案例；易误放[2]，但[2]需概括数据知情与分享决策的普遍原则，非单一追踪行为举例。
