# 09-21 第一篇

**The Rise of Solar Energy**

Fossil fuels have powered our world for over a century, but they come with serious costs. They pollute the air, contribute to climate change, and will eventually run out. Solar energy offers a clean and endless alternative. [1] Turning sunlight into electricity is becoming one of the most promising ways to meet our energy needs while protecting the planet.

Solar panels have become much cheaper and more efficient in recent years. What once seemed expensive and limited is now affordable for many homes and businesses. [2] As technology improves and costs drop, solar power is becoming a practical choice for more people around the world.

Solar energy can be used in many ways. Rooftop panels can power a single home. Large solar farms can supply electricity to whole cities. In remote areas without power lines, solar can bring light and power where it was never available before. [3] Solar technology is flexible enough to serve both small-scale and large-scale energy needs.

Storing solar energy for use at night or on cloudy days has been a challenge. Batteries are getting better, and new solutions are emerging. [4] Advances in energy storage are making solar power more reliable and useful throughout the day and in all weather conditions.

Governments and businesses are investing heavily in solar. Policies that support clean energy and falling costs are driving rapid growth. [5] The shift to solar and other renewables is accelerating, offering hope for a cleaner and more sustainable energy future for everyone.

## 选项

A. Harnessing the power of the sun to produce electricity is increasingly seen as a key solution for clean and sustainable energy.
B. Falling prices and better technology are making solar energy a realistic option for a growing number of users.
C. From single buildings to entire regions, solar power can meet a wide range of energy demands.
D. Improvements in how we store solar energy are helping overcome one of its biggest limitations.
E. The transition to solar and other renewable sources is speeding up, bringing us closer to a greener energy system.
F. For example, China has built some of the world's largest solar farms, covering vast areas of land with solar panels.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前对比化石燃料问题与太阳能优势，空后强调将阳光转化为电力是有前景的清洁能源途径。A的"Harnessing the power of the sun to produce electricity"与"Turning sunlight into electricity"呼应，"key solution for clean and sustainable energy"与"protecting the planet"一致。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说太阳能板更便宜高效、更多家庭和企业可负担，空后强调技术进步和成本下降使太阳能更实用。B的"Falling prices and better technology"与"cheaper and more efficient"、"costs drop"呼应，"realistic option for a growing number of users"与"practical choice for more people"一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举太阳能的多种应用（屋顶、大型电站、偏远地区），空后强调技术灵活可满足不同规模需求。C的"From single buildings to entire regions"与"Rooftop panels"、"Large solar farms"、"remote areas"对应，"wide range of energy demands"与"flexible enough to serve both small-scale and large-scale"一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说夜间和阴天储电曾是挑战，电池在进步，空后强调储能进步使太阳能更可靠。D的"Improvements in how we store solar energy"与"Advances in energy storage"呼应，"helping overcome one of its biggest limitations"与"Storing solar energy for use at night or on cloudy days has been a challenge"一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说政府企业加大投资、政策支持与成本下降推动增长，空后总结向太阳能等清洁能源转型加速。E的"The transition to solar and other renewable sources is speeding up"与"The shift to solar and other renewables is accelerating"直接对应，"greener energy system"与"cleaner and more sustainable energy future"一致。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, China has built some of the world's largest solar farms, covering vast areas of land with solar panels.**：以For example开头，是具体国家案例；易误放[3]或[5]，但[3]需概括 solar 的规模灵活性，[5]需转型趋势的概括，非单一国家项目举例。
