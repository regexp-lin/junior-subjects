# 12-14 第一篇

**Life in the Deep Ocean: A World of Darkness and Wonder**

The deep ocean is one of the least explored places on Earth. Below 200 meters, sunlight cannot reach, and the water is cold and dark. Yet life exists there. Strange creatures have adapted to survive under extreme pressure and with almost no light. Scientists estimate that we have explored less than five percent of the ocean floor. There is still much to discover. [1]

Creatures of the deep look like they belong in another world. Many are transparent or glow in the dark to attract prey or find mates. The anglerfish uses a glowing lure on its head to draw in smaller fish. Giant squid can grow to enormous sizes in the depths. Some fish have huge mouths and stretchy stomachs to eat whatever food comes their way. Survival in the deep requires unusual adaptations. [2]

Exploring the deep is difficult and expensive. Submarines and remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) must withstand crushing pressure. Modern technology has allowed us to film and study deep-sea life without harming it. Each expedition can reveal new species and behaviors. The discovery of hydrothermal vents—hot springs on the seafloor—showed that life can thrive even without sunlight, using chemical energy instead. [3]

The deep ocean matters for the whole planet. It stores large amounts of carbon and helps regulate the climate. Deep-sea ecosystems may hold clues for new medicines. However, human activities such as deep-sea mining and pollution threaten these fragile environments. Understanding the deep ocean is key to protecting it. [4]

We are only beginning to understand the mysteries of the deep. Each dive brings new surprises. The more we learn, the more we realize how much we still do not know. [5]

## 选项

A. The deep ocean remains largely unknown, yet it is full of life that has adapted to extreme conditions.
B. These adaptations make deep-sea animals some of the most unusual and fascinating creatures on Earth.
C. Advances in technology have opened a window into this hidden world and its discoveries.
D. Protecting the deep ocean is important for both humanity and the future of our planet.
E. This ongoing exploration reminds us that our planet still holds many secrets waiting to be uncovered.
F. For example, the vampire squid is a deep-sea animal that glows in the dark and has been called a "living fossil."

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍深海极少被探索、200米以下无光寒冷、却有生命、生物适应高压无光、人类探索不足5%、尚多未知，空后需总结深海的未知与生命适应。A的"remains largely unknown, yet it is full of life that has adapted to extreme conditions"与前文对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说深海生物形态奇特、透明或发光、鮟鱇鱼用发光诱饵、巨型乌贼、大口伸缩胃、生存需特殊适应，空后需总结适应的独特与迷人。B的"These adaptations make deep-sea animals some of the most unusual and fascinating creatures on Earth"与前文呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说探索困难昂贵、潜水器与ROV需抗压、技术可拍摄研究而不伤害、每次远征有新发现、热液喷口证明无光也可用化学能生存，空后需总结技术推动探索与发现。C的"Advances in technology have opened a window into this hidden world and its discoveries"与前文对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说深海对全球重要、储碳调气候、可能含新药线索、深海底采矿与污染威胁、理解是保护关键，空后需总结保护重要性。注意D项是中文，应改为英文。需修正为：D. Protecting the deep ocean is important for both humanity and the future of our planet.

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说我们刚起步理解深海、每次下潜带来新惊喜、学得越多越觉未知，空后需总结持续探索的意义。E的"This ongoing exploration reminds us that our planet still holds many secrets waiting to be uncovered"与前文对应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the vampire squid...**：以For example开头，为具体物种案例（吸血鬼乌贼）。各空均为概括或过渡句；[2]易被误选因涉及发光生物，但该空需总结适应性的整体意义，而非补充某一物种的特性。
