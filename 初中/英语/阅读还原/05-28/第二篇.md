# 05-28 第二篇

**The Ancient Olympic Games**

The Olympics we watch today have a long history. They began in ancient Greece over 2,700 years ago. The first Olympic Games were held in Olympia, a sacred（神圣的）place where Greeks worshipped（崇拜）the gods. [1] That is because the Games were both a religious festival and a sports contest. Athletes competed to honor Zeus, the king of the gods. Wars between Greek cities would stop during the Games so that everyone could travel safely. The event brought Greeks together and celebrated human strength and skill. In this way, the ancient Olympics were more than just races and wrestling—they were a symbol of peace and unity. The idea of the Olympics has inspired the world for thousands of years.

Who could take part in the ancient Olympics? [2] In other words, only free Greek men were allowed to compete. Women could not join the Games—they could not even watch. Slaves and foreigners were also excluded. Athletes had to train for months and arrive at Olympia a month before the event. They competed in the nude（赤身）to show that they relied on skill, not clothes or wealth. The rules were strict, and cheaters were punished. Winners received a crown made of olive leaves（橄榄叶）—no money, just honor. The ancient Olympics valued excellence and fair play above all. For the Greeks, winning was about glory, not gold.

What sports were in the ancient Olympics? [3] That is why running, jumping, and throwing were at the heart of the Games. The main events included foot races of different lengths, the long jump, the discus（铁饼）, and the javelin（标枪）. Wrestling and boxing were also popular. One of the fiercest events was the pankration（古希腊式搏击）—a mix of wrestling and boxing with few rules. Chariot races（战车赛）and horse races were held too. The ancient Olympics had fewer events than today, but they tested strength, speed, and courage. Many events are still part of the modern Olympic program. The spirit of the Games has stayed the same across the ages.

Why did the ancient Olympics end? [4] It is true that Rome conquered Greece and changed the Games. The Romans admired Greek culture but added their own customs. In 393 AD, the Christian emperor（皇帝）banned the Olympics because they were linked to pagan（异教徒的）gods. The Games stopped for over 1,500 years. It was not until 1896 that the modern Olympics began again in Athens. Today, the Olympics welcome athletes from all countries, men and women alike. The ancient Games are gone, but their legacy lives on. We still celebrate the same ideals: excellence, fairness, and peace.

[5] They showed that sports could bring people together and that excellence could be celebrated across cultures. From olive crowns to gold medals, the dream of the Olympics has never faded. So the next time you watch the Olympic Games, remember: you are watching a tradition that started over 2,700 years ago. The flame still burns.

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：总领古代奥运会的性质与意义。空前描述"The Olympics we watch today have a long history...The first Olympic Games were held in Olympia, a sacred place where Greeks worshipped the gods"，空后说明"Athletes competed to honor Zeus, the king of the gods...Wars between Greek cities would stop during the Games...The event brought Greeks together and celebrated human strength and skill...the ancient Olympics were more than just races and wrestling—they were a symbol of peace and unity"。A选项"That is because the Games were both a religious festival and a sports contest"（那是因为古代奥运会既是宗教节日也是体育竞赛）中"That is because"解释奥运会性质，"religious festival and sports contest"与空后honor Zeus、wars would stop、symbol of peace精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：古代奥运会的参赛资格——仅限希腊自由男子。空前设问"Who could take part in the ancient Olympics?"，空后详述"Women could not join the Games—they could not even watch. Slaves and foreigners were also excluded...Athletes had to train for months...They competed in the nude...The rules were strict...Winners received a crown made of olive leaves"。B选项"In other words, only free Greek men were allowed to compete"（换言之，只有自由的希腊男子可以参赛）中"In other words"概括空后限制条件，"only free Greek men"与空后Women could not、Slaves and foreigners excluded完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：古代奥运会的核心项目——跑、跳、投。空前设问"What sports were in the ancient Olympics?"，空后列举"The main events included foot races of different lengths, the long jump, the discus, and the javelin. - **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：古代奥运会终结的原因——罗马征服与基督教禁止。空前设问"Why did the ancient Olympics end?"，空后说明"Rome conquered Greece and changed the Games...In 393 AD, the Christian emperor banned the Olympics because they were linked to pagan gods.   - **线索**：全文收束与古代奥运会的遗产。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"They showed that sports could bring people together...excellence could be celebrated across cultures...you are watching a tradition that started over 2,700 years ago.
