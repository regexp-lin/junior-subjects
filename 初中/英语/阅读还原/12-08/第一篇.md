**Digital Currency: Money in the Digital Age**

Digital currency is money that exists only in electronic form. Unlike cash or coins, you cannot hold it in your hand. The most famous example is Bitcoin, but there are many other digital currencies today. They are stored in digital wallets on phones or computers and can be sent or received over the internet. [1]

Digital currencies use a technology called blockchain. A blockchain is like a public record book that tracks every transaction. No single person or company controls it; instead, many computers work together to check and record each transfer. This makes it hard for anyone to cheat or fake transactions. Supporters say it is more transparent and secure than traditional banking. [2]

People use digital currency for different reasons. Some invest in it hoping its value will rise. Others use it to pay for goods and services online. In some countries, people use it when their local currency is unstable. Sending money across borders can be faster and cheaper than using banks. However, prices can change sharply, so it is also risky. [3]

Governments and banks are taking notice. Some countries have started their own digital currencies. Central banks are exploring digital versions of their national money. Rules and laws are still being developed to protect users and prevent illegal use. The future of money may look very different from today. [4]

Digital currency is changing how we think about money. It raises questions about privacy, security, and who controls our financial system. Whether it becomes mainstream or remains a niche tool, it has already started a global conversation about the future of finance. [5]

## 选项

A. In short, digital currency is a new form of money that lives entirely online.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍数字货币——仅以电子形式存在、区别于现金硬币、比特币为例、存储在数字钱包、可网上收发，空后需总结数字货币的基本定义与特征。A的"In short"承接前文，"new form of money that lives entirely online"与前文对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说使用区块链技术、区块链如公共账本记录每笔交易、去中心化多机验证、难以作弊或伪造、支持者称更透明安全，空后需总结技术与传统银行货币的差异。B的"This technology"指代区块链，"what makes digital currency different from ordinary bank money"与前文对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说不同用途——投资增值、网上购物、本币不稳时的替代、跨国转账更快更便宜、但价格波动大风险高，空后需总结机遇与风险并存。C的"These uses"指代前文，"both the promise and the risks of digital currency"与前文呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说政府与银行关注——部分国家推官方数字货币、央行探索法定货币数字化、法规仍在完善、未来货币形态可能不同，空后需总结支付与储蓄方式的演变。D的"How we pay and save may evolve"与前文对应，"digital and traditional systems merge"与前文呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说数字货币改变对货币的看法、引发隐私安全与控制权问题、无论主流与否都已引发全球讨论，空后需总结这些问题将如何影响未来。E的"These questions"指代前文，"will shape how money works in the decades to come"与前文呼应。
