**Noise Pollution: The Hidden Danger**

Noise pollution is unwanted or harmful sound that affects our health and well-being. It comes from traffic, construction, loud music, aircraft, and many other sources. Unlike air or water pollution, we often overlook noise because we cannot see it. Yet long-term exposure to loud noise can damage hearing, disturb sleep, and increase stress. [1]

Traffic is one of the main sources of noise in cities. Cars, buses, and trucks produce constant noise, especially during rush hours. People living near busy roads or highways often report difficulty sleeping and higher levels of stress. Children in noisy areas may have trouble concentrating at school. The problem grows as cities expand and traffic increases. [2]

Noise affects both body and mind. It can raise blood pressure and heart rate. Lack of sleep due to noise leads to fatigue and poor concentration. Constant noise can make people feel anxious or irritable. In workplaces and schools, noise reduces productivity and learning. Finding quiet spaces becomes harder in densely populated areas. [3]

There are ways to reduce noise pollution. Buildings can be designed with soundproof walls and windows. Planting trees along roads can absorb some sound. Laws can limit noise levels from construction, concerts, and vehicles. Using earplugs or headphones in noisy environments can protect your ears. Awareness is the first step toward change. [4]

Noise pollution is a growing problem that deserves more attention. As our world becomes busier and noisier, protecting our ears and our peace of mind becomes increasingly important. Small changes in how we design cities and manage sound can make a big difference for everyone. [5]

## 选项

A. For these reasons, noise pollution is a serious issue that we should not ignore.
B. As a result, urban noise has become a daily challenge for millions of people.
C. In this way, noise touches almost every aspect of our health and daily life.
D. Fortunately, we can take steps to lessen noise and its effects.
E. That is why reducing noise pollution should be a priority for communities everywhere.
F. For example, white noise machines can help some people sleep better in noisy environments.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍噪音污染——不受欢迎或有害的声音、来自交通建筑施工飞机等、常被忽视因看不见、长期暴露可损害听力干扰睡眠增加压力，空后需总结噪音污染的严重性与不可忽视性。A的"For these reasons"承接前文，"serious issue that we should not ignore"与前文对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说交通是城市主要噪音源、早晚高峰尤甚、沿路居民反映失眠与压力、儿童难以集中、城市扩张使问题加重，空后需总结城市噪音对大众的日常影响。B的"As a result"承接前文，"urban noise has become a daily challenge for millions of people"与前文呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说噪音影响身心——升高血压心率、失眠导致疲劳与注意力差、持续噪音使人焦虑易怒、工作学习效率下降、密集区域难觅安静，空后需总结噪音对健康与生活的广泛影响。C的"In this way"承接前文，"touches almost every aspect of our health and daily life"与前文对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前提出减噪方法——隔音设计、沿路绿化、立法限噪、耳塞或耳机、意识先行，空后需总结可采取的行动。D的"Fortunately"与前文转折呼应，"we can take steps to lessen noise and its effects"与前文具体建议对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说噪音污染日益严重值得关注、世界更忙更吵、保护听力与内心平静更重要、城市设计与声音管理的小改变可带来大不同，空后需总结社区层面的优先行动。E的"That is why reducing noise pollution should be a priority for communities everywhere"与前文呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, white noise machines can help some people sleep...**：以For example开头，为具体应对案例（白噪音机助眠）。各空均为概括或过渡句；[4]易被误选因涉及减噪应对，但该空需总结可采取的总体步骤，而非补充单一设备的举例。
