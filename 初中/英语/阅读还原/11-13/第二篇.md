# 11-13 第二篇

**Responsible Pet Care: A Commitment for Life**

Pets bring joy, comfort, and companionship to millions of families. Dogs, cats, rabbits, fish, and other animals can become beloved members of the household. They offer unconditional love and often help reduce stress. However, owning a pet is a serious responsibility that should not be taken lightly. [1]

Before getting a pet, think carefully about your situation. Do you have enough space? Is your home suitable for the type of animal you want? Can you afford food, vet visits, and other expenses? Do you have time for daily walks, feeding, and play? Some pets live for many years—a dog might be with you for over a decade. [2]

Daily care is essential. Pets need regular feeding, fresh water, exercise, and a clean living space. Dogs require walks and mental stimulation. Cats need scratching posts and safe places to climb. Small animals like hamsters need appropriate cages and bedding. Regular visits to the vet help prevent illness and catch problems early. [3]

Pets also need love and attention. They are social creatures that bond with their owners. Spending quality time with your pet—playing, grooming, or simply being together—strengthens your relationship. Neglected pets can become anxious, bored, or develop bad habits. Teaching children to treat animals gently and respectfully helps them grow into caring adults. [4]

Sadly, some people abandon their pets when they move, get busy, or find it too difficult to care for them. Animal shelters work hard to rescue and rehome abandoned animals, but the best solution is prevention. Before getting a pet, be sure you are ready for the commitment. If your circumstances change, try to find a new home for your pet rather than leaving them behind. [5]

## 选项

A. Pet ownership is a long-term commitment that requires time, money, care, and love—and it lasts for the entire life of the animal.
B. Choosing the right pet for your lifestyle and being fully prepared before bringing one home is the first step toward a happy and healthy relationship.
C. Meeting your pet's basic needs every day is the foundation of responsible ownership and helps ensure your animal stays healthy and happy.
D. Beyond meeting physical needs, pets thrive when they feel loved, safe, and valued as part of the family.
E. Responsible pet ownership means thinking ahead, honoring your commitment, and never treating an animal as disposable—because to them, you are their whole world.
F. For instance, the average lifespan of a domestic cat is around 15 years, and some cats have been known to live well into their twenties with proper care.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说宠物的益处——带来快乐、安慰与陪伴，成为家庭成员，提供无条件的爱、缓解压力，同时指出养宠物是严肃责任；空后需总结养宠物的长期承诺本质。A的"long-term commitment that requires time, money, care, and love"与前文"serious responsibility"呼应，"lasts for the entire life of the animal"与前文 lasting 关系对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举养宠前需考虑——空间、家居适合性、经济、时间、宠物寿命可达十年以上；空后需总结选择与准备的意义。B的"Choosing the right pet for your lifestyle and being fully prepared"与前文各项考量呼应，"first step toward a happy and healthy relationship"与"beloved members"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说日常照料——定时喂食、饮水、运动、清洁环境，不同动物有不同需求（遛狗、猫爬架、仓鼠笼等），定期体检；空后需总结满足基本需求的基础作用。C的"Meeting your pet's basic needs every day"与前文呼应，"foundation of responsible ownership"与"essential"对应，"healthy and happy"与前文 prevent illness 对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说情感需求——社交动物、需陪伴与互动、忽视会导致焦虑或不良习惯、教孩子善待动物；空后需总结物质之外的情感满足。D的"Beyond meeting physical needs"与前文 basic care 形成递进，"pets thrive when they feel loved, safe, and valued"与"love and attention"和"bond"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说遗弃问题——有人因搬迁、忙碌或困难而遗弃，收容所尽力救助，最佳方案是预防、事前准备、情况变化时寻找新家；空后需总结负责任养宠的深层含义。E的"Responsible pet ownership means thinking ahead, honoring your commitment"与前文呼应，"never treating an animal as disposable"与"abandon"和"leaving them behind"对应，"you are their whole world"是情感升华，是合理收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For instance, the average lifespan of a domestic cat is around 15 years, and some cats have been known to live well into their twenties with proper care.**：以 For instance 开头，为猫寿命的具体数据举例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[1] 或 [2] 易被误选因涉及养宠时间，但[1]需概括"承诺"主题，[2]需概括"选择与准备"，均非某一物种寿命的具体数据举例。
