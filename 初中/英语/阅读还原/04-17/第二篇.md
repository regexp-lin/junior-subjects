# 04-17 第二篇

**The Nature of Friendship: What Makes a True Friend**

Many people have friends, but not everyone knows what makes a true friend. Some friends are only there when life is easy. [1] A true friend is someone who stays with you through both good and bad times. They do not disappear when you face difficulties. Understanding the real meaning of friendship can help you build stronger and deeper connections with others.

Trust is the base of every real friendship. [2] When you share a secret with a true friend, you can feel sure that they will keep it. They will not tell others what you said in private. Without trust, a friendship is like a house built on sand—it may fall apart easily when troubles come. True friends prove their trustworthiness（可信赖）over time by what they do, not just what they say.

A true friend also supports you when you need help. [3] If you are sad or worried, a real friend will listen to you and try to understand. They may not have all the answers, but they will stand beside you. Friends who only appear when everything goes well are not really friends. The best friends are those who walk with you through the hardest days.

Honesty is another key quality of real friendship. [4] A true friend will tell you the truth even when it is not easy to hear. They care enough about you to point out your mistakes, because they want you to grow and become better. Friends who always say "yes" to everything might not be doing you a favor. Sometimes the bravest thing a friend can do is to say "no" when you are wrong.

[5] Real friendship is not built in a day. It needs time, patience, and effort from both sides. When you find a friend who is trustworthy, supportive, and honest, you have found something precious. Take good care of such friendships, for they make life richer and more meaningful.

## 选项

A. However, the difference lies in who stays when things get difficult.
B. Friends may have different hobbies, but that does not stop them from being close.
C. They are willing to share your joy and your pain.
D. They would rather hurt your feelings a little than lie to you.
E. In short, a true friend is worth keeping for a lifetime.
F. Trust grows slowly, but it can be lost in a moment.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] F   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：转折对比。空前描述"Many people have friends...Some friends are only there when life is easy"（有些朋友只在顺利时出现）。A选项"However, the difference lies in who stays when things get difficult"（然而，区别在于谁在困难时留下）中"However"承接对比，"the difference"呼应空前"not everyone knows what makes a true friend"，"who stays when things get difficult"自然引出空后"A true friend...stays with you through both good and bad times"，逻辑严密。

- **[2] F**：
  - **线索**：信任的特性与重要性。空前提出"Trust is the base of every real friendship"，空后举例"When you share a secret with a true friend, you can feel sure that they will keep it"。F选项"Trust grows slowly, but it can be lost in a moment"（信任慢慢积累，却可能瞬间丧失）从正反两面阐释信任的特点，与空前"base"及空后"keep the secret"形成呼应，强调为何要珍惜并证明信任。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：支持的内涵。空前描述"A true friend also supports you when you need help"，空后详述"If you are sad or worried, a real friend will listen...stand beside you...walk with you through the hardest days"。C选项"They are willing to share your joy and your pain"（他们愿意分享你的喜悦与痛苦）中"share...pain"与空后"sad or worried...listen...stand beside you"精准对应，"joy and pain"概括"both good and bad times"，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：诚实的勇气。空前提出"Honesty is another key quality...A true friend will tell you the truth even when it is not easy to hear"，空后解释"They care enough...to point out your mistakes...Friends who always say 'yes' might not be doing you a favor"。D选项"They would rather hurt your feelings a little than lie to you"（他们宁愿稍伤你心也不愿骗你）与空前"truth...not easy to hear"及空后"point out mistakes...say 'no' when you are wrong"完美对应，突出诚实的勇气。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"Real friendship is not built in a day...Take good care of such friendships"。E选项"In short, a true friend is worth keeping for a lifetime"（总之，真正的朋友值得终生珍惜）用"In short"总结全文，"worth keeping for a lifetime"与空后"precious...take good care"呼应，收束得当。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **B. Friends may have different hobbies, but that does not stop them from being close.**：描述朋友可以爱好不同却依然亲密，与友谊主题相关，易被误填[1]或[3]。但空[1]空前强调"only there when life is easy"，需转折引出"who stays when things get difficult"，A的对比逻辑更贴切；空[3]空前讲"supports you when you need help"，空后讲"sad or worried...listen...stand beside you"，需体现"共担苦难"之意，C的"share your joy and your pain"更契合。B属"主题相关但逻辑错位"型干扰，强调爱好差异，与各空前后文的"困难时刻""信任""支持""诚实"均无直接衔接。
