**Green Building Design**

Buildings use a lot of energy for heating, cooling, and lighting. They also produce much of the world's waste and pollution. Green building design aims to create structures that are kinder to the environment and healthier for people who live and work in them. [1] In other words, green buildings are designed to save resources, reduce harm to nature, and improve the quality of life. This approach is becoming more popular as we face climate change and growing cities.

Green buildings use many smart ideas. [2] For example, they often have large windows and skylights to let in natural light, which cuts down on the need for electric lights during the day. They may use solar panels on the roof to produce clean energy, collect rainwater for flushing toilets and watering plants, and use materials that do not release harmful chemicals. Good insulation（隔热）helps keep rooms warm in winter and cool in summer, so less energy is needed for air conditioning and heating. All these features work together to make a building more efficient.

The location and surroundings of a building matter too. [3] Green buildings are often placed near public transport, so people can walk, cycle, or take the bus instead of driving. Some have roofs covered with plants, which helps cool the building and provides space for birds and insects. Trees and gardens around the building can create shade and improve air quality. Designers also think about how to reuse materials when the building is torn down someday, so fewer resources go to waste.

Building green can cost more at first, but it often pays off over time. [4] Lower energy and water bills mean that the extra cost can be recovered in a few years. Green buildings also tend to be more comfortable and have better air quality, which can make workers more productive and students learn better. Many governments now offer support or tax breaks for green construction. As more people choose green buildings, the cost of green materials and technology is going down.

[5] Green building is not just a trend—it is a necessary step towards a sustainable future. By designing and building in ways that respect the Earth, we can reduce our impact on the environment while creating better places to live and work. The buildings we build today will shape the world for generations to come.

## 选项

A. In other words, green buildings save resources, reduce environmental harm, and improve people's lives.
B. That is because they use natural light, solar power, rainwater, and efficient materials to cut energy use.
C. In fact, green design includes transport links, green roofs, and plans for reusing materials.
D. These benefits show that green buildings can save money and improve well-being over time.
E. In short, green building is a necessary step towards a sustainable and better future.
F. For example, concrete is one of the most common building materials used worldwide.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：概括绿色建筑设计的核心目标。空前描述"Buildings use lot of energy...produce waste and pollution...Green building design aims to create structures kinder to environment, healthier for people"，空后说明"green buildings are designed to save resources, reduce harm to nature, improve quality of life...approach becoming more popular...climate change, growing cities"。A选项"In other words, green buildings save resources, reduce environmental harm, and improve people's lives"（换言之，绿色建筑节约资源、减少对环境的危害并改善人们的生活）中"In other words"概括空前目标，"save resources, reduce harm, improve lives"与空后"save resources, reduce harm, improve quality of life"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：绿色建筑采用的节能与环保技术。空前提出"Green buildings use many smart ideas"，空后详述"Large windows, skylights...natural light...cuts electric lights...solar panels...clean energy...collect rainwater...materials without harmful chemicals...insulation...warm in winter, cool in summer...less energy...air conditioning, heating...features work together...efficient"。B选项"That is because they use natural light, solar power, rainwater, and efficient materials to cut energy use"（那是因为它们利用自然光、太阳能、雨水和高效材料来减少能源消耗）中"That is because"解释智能设计，"natural light, solar power, rainwater, efficient materials...cut energy use"与空后各项技术完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：选址、绿化与材料再利用。空前提出"The location and surroundings of a building matter too"，空后列举"Near public transport...walk, cycle, bus instead of driving...roofs covered with plants...cool building, space for birds and insects...trees, gardens...shade, improve air quality...reuse materials when torn down...fewer resources to waste"。C选项"In fact, green design includes transport links, green roofs, and plans for reusing materials"（事实上，绿色设计包括交通衔接、绿色屋顶及材料再利用计划）中"In fact"强化选址与周边设计，"transport links, green roofs, reusing materials"与空后 public transport、roofs with plants、reuse materials 精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：绿色建筑的经济与健康回报。空前提出"Building green can cost more at first, but often pays off over time"，空后描述"Lower energy and water bills...extra cost recovered in few years...more comfortable, better air quality...workers more productive, students learn better...governments offer support, tax breaks...cost of green materials and technology going down"。D选项"These benefits show that green buildings can save money and improve well-being over time"（这些好处表明绿色建筑能长期省钱并改善健康福祉）中"These benefits"指代空后各项回报，"save money...improve well-being"与空后 lower bills、recovered cost、comfortable、air quality 完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与可持续发展呼吁。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"Green building not just trend...necessary step towards sustainable future...design and build in ways that respect Earth...reduce impact...better places to live and work...buildings today will shape world for generations"。E选项"In short, green building is a necessary step towards a sustainable and better future"（简言之，绿色建筑是迈向可持续与更美好未来的必要一步）用"In short"总结全文，"necessary step...sustainable...better future"与空后"necessary step...sustainable future...better places"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, concrete is one of the most common building materials used worldwide.**：涉及混凝土为常用建材，易被误填[2]。但F以"For example"引出单一材料，而空[2]讨论的是多种绿色技术（自然光、太阳能、雨水、环保材料等），F的"concrete...common"并未体现绿色或环保特性，与"natural light, solar, rainwater, materials without harmful chemicals"的环保技术焦点不符。F属"主题相关但论述层次错位"型干扰。
