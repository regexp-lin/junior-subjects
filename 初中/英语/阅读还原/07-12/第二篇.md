# 07-12 第二篇

**The Benefits of Running for Body and Mind**

Running is one of the simplest forms of exercise. You need no gym membership, no special equipment—just a pair of shoes and some open space. People have been running for thousands of years, and today it remains one of the most popular activities worldwide. [1] The benefits of running extend beyond physical fitness. Regular runners often report better mood, clearer thinking, and a stronger sense of well-being. Science supports what runners have long known: running is good for both body and mind.

Running strengthens the heart and builds endurance. [2] When you run regularly, your heart becomes more efficient at pumping blood. Your lungs work better, and your muscles—especially in your legs and core—grow stronger. Over time, simple activities like climbing stairs or walking longer distances become easier. Running also helps maintain a healthy weight by burning calories. These physical changes improve your overall health and energy levels for daily life.

Running has powerful effects on mental health. [3] Many runners describe a "runner's high"—a feeling of calm and happiness during or after a run. Exercise triggers the release of endorphins, natural chemicals that boost mood and reduce stress. Studies show that regular aerobic exercise like running can help reduce anxiety and symptoms of mild depression. For many people, a morning run sets a positive tone for the entire day.

Running can also be a social activity. [4] Joining a running group or finding a running partner adds motivation and makes the activity more enjoyable. You can chat while jogging, share tips, and celebrate progress together. Races and fun runs bring communities together and give runners goals to work toward. The social side of running often helps people stick with the habit when motivation alone might not be enough.

[5] Running offers a simple, affordable path to better health. Whether you run for fitness, stress relief, or the joy of movement, the benefits are real. Start slowly, listen to your body, and give yourself time to build the habit. Every step you take is a step toward a healthier, happier you.

## 选项

A. Running improves not only the body but also the mind and emotions.
B. The physical effects of running include a stronger heart and better fitness.
C. Running has been shown to improve mood and reduce stress.
D. Running with others can add motivation and make the experience more fun.
E. All in all, running is an accessible way to improve your overall well-being.
F. For example, marathon running has become increasingly popular in recent years.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍跑步的简便性与普及性，空后强调"benefits extend beyond physical fitness"，并列举情绪更好、思维更清晰、幸福感更强，科学支持身心兼益。A选项"Running improves not only the body but also the mind and emotions"以"not only...but also"承接前文，概括后文身心双重益处，承上启下。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段主题是跑步对身体的益处，空后具体说明心脏更高效、肺功能改善、腿部与核心肌群增强、体重管理、日常活动更轻松。B选项"The physical effects of running include a stronger heart and better fitness"精准概括：跑步的生理效果包括更强的心脏和更好的体能。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段讲跑步对心理健康的影响，空后描述"runner's high"、内啡肽释放、缓解焦虑与轻度抑郁、晨跑带来一天好状态。C选项"Running has been shown to improve mood and reduce stress"概括本段：跑步被证明能改善情绪、减轻压力。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讨论跑步的社交面向，空后说明加入跑团、找跑伴增加动力、跑步时聊天分享、比赛与趣味跑凝聚社区、社交有助于坚持。D选项"Running with others can add motivation and make the experience more fun"准确概括：与他人一起跑能增加动力、让体验更有趣。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：最后一段需总结升华，空前已分述身体、心理、社交益处，空后归纳"simple, affordable path""start slowly, listen to your body"。E选项"All in all, running is an accessible way to improve your overall well-being"用"All in all"收束，强调跑步是改善整体健康的易行方式。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, marathon running has become increasingly popular in recent years.**：以"For example"开头，是举例型干扰。虽与跑步相关，但马拉松只是跑步的一种形式，本段需概括跑步的整体价值或某一方面（身体/心理/社交）。F若放入[4]处，马拉松可算社交跑步的一种，但本段重点是"跑团、跑伴、社区"，马拉松流行度是现象描述，非段落主题概括，论述层次错位。
