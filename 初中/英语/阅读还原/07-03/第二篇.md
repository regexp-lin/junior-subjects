# 07-03 第二篇

**Healthy Eating: Fuel Your Body Right**

Eating well is one of the most important things you can do for your health. The food you eat affects your energy, mood, and ability to learn. [1] A balanced diet gives your body the nutrients it needs to grow strong and stay healthy. Teenagers especially need good nutrition because their bodies are still developing. Skipping meals or eating too much junk food can lead to tiredness, poor concentration, and health problems later in life. Making smart food choices now will benefit you for years to come.

[2] Fruits and vegetables should make up a large part of your daily meals. They are full of vitamins, minerals, and fiber that keep your body working well. Try to eat different colors—red tomatoes, green spinach, orange carrots—because each color offers different nutrients. Aim for at least five portions of fruits and vegetables every day. You can add fruit to your breakfast, have a salad at lunch, and include vegetables with your dinner. It is easier than you think to reach this goal.

[3] Protein helps build and repair your muscles and keeps you feeling full. Good sources include chicken, fish, eggs, beans, and nuts. Teenagers who play sports or are very active need more protein to support their growth. However, you do not need to eat meat at every meal. Beans and tofu are excellent plant-based options. Try to have protein at each meal to maintain your energy throughout the day. Variety is key to getting all the nutrients your body needs.

[4] Limit foods high in sugar, salt, and unhealthy fats. Candy, soft drinks, chips, and fried foods taste good, but they offer little nutrition. Eating too much of these can lead to weight gain, tooth decay, and an increased risk of diseases. This does not mean you can never have treats—enjoying a small dessert or snack sometimes is fine. The key is to make these foods the exception, not the rule. Save them for special occasions rather than eating them every day.

[5] Developing healthy eating habits takes time, but it is worth the effort. Start with small changes, like adding one extra vegetable to your dinner or swapping a sugary drink for water. Plan your meals ahead so you are less likely to grab unhealthy options when you are hungry. Remember that eating well is not about being perfect—it is about making better choices most of the time. Your future self will thank you for taking care of your body today.

## 选项

A. A balanced diet provides the foundation for growth and long-term health.
B. Eating plenty of fruits and vegetables is essential for getting the nutrients you need.
C. Getting enough protein from various sources supports your body's growth and energy.
D. Reducing sugary and fatty foods helps protect your health in the long run.
E. Building healthy eating habits gradually is the best approach to good nutrition.
F. For example, a chicken salad with mixed greens provides both protein and vitamins.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前引入饮食对健康的重要性（影响精力、情绪、学习），空后具体说明均衡饮食的好处及不良饮食的后果。A选项"A balanced diet provides the foundation for growth and long-term health"承上启下：将前文的"吃得好"具体化为"均衡饮食"，并点明其为生长发育和长期健康的基础，与后文"营养素""青少年发育"紧密衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段全段论述蔬果的重要性——富含维生素、矿物质、纤维，建议多种颜色、每日五份、具体食用方式。B选项"Eating plenty of fruits and vegetables is essential for getting the nutrients you need"准确概括段落主题。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段聚焦蛋白质：构建肌肉、饱腹感、优质来源（肉类、豆类、坚果）、运动者需求、植物蛋白。C选项"Getting enough protein from various sources supports your body's growth and energy"完美总结蛋白质的多重作用及多种来源。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讨论应限制高糖、高盐、高脂食物，及其危害（肥胖、蛀牙、疾病风险），并强调适度原则。D选项"Reducing sugary and fatty foods helps protect your health in the long run"精准概括段落主旨。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结全文，提出循序渐进的建议——小改变、提前规划、不必完美。E选项"Building healthy eating habits gradually is the best approach to good nutrition"准确概括了段落的总结性建议。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：以"For example"开头，举了"鸡肉沙拉配混合蔬菜同时提供蛋白质和维生素"的例子。虽与健康饮食相关，但它是支撑第二或第三段论点的具体论据，而非段落的概括句。若放入[2]或[3]会造成层次错位——F是例证层，正确选项是论点/主题概括层。
