# 07-03 第一篇

**Protecting Our Oceans: A Call to Action**

The ocean covers over 70% of our planet's surface and is home to countless species. It provides us with food, regulates our climate, and produces more than half of the oxygen we breathe. [1] However, human activities have put this precious resource in great danger. Plastic waste, overfishing, and pollution are threatening marine life and ocean health. Each year, millions of tons of plastic end up in the sea, harming fish, birds, and other creatures. If we do not take action now, future generations may never see the beauty of a healthy ocean.

[2] Single-use plastics like straws, bags, and bottles are among the biggest threats. These items take hundreds of years to break down, and they often end up in the ocean. Sea turtles mistake plastic bags for jellyfish and eat them. Fish swallow tiny plastic particles that enter the food chain. We can all help by refusing single-use plastics. Bring your own bags when shopping, use a reusable water bottle, and say no to plastic straws. Small changes in our daily habits can make a big difference.

[3] Overfishing is another serious problem. When we catch fish faster than they can reproduce, fish populations collapse. Some species have already disappeared from certain areas. Sustainable fishing practices are essential. We need to support seafood that comes from well-managed fisheries. Look for labels that show the fish was caught in an environmentally friendly way. Eating less seafood or choosing sustainable options helps protect ocean ecosystems.

[4] Climate change also affects our oceans. Rising temperatures cause coral bleaching, which kills the colorful coral reefs that many sea creatures depend on. Melting ice raises sea levels and threatens coastal communities. Reducing our carbon footprint can help. We can walk, bike, or use public transport instead of driving. Saving energy at home and supporting renewable energy are also important steps. Every effort counts in the fight against climate change.

[5] We all have a role to play in protecting the ocean. Governments must pass laws to reduce pollution and protect marine areas. Businesses should find ways to produce goods without harming the environment. As individuals, we can educate others and make responsible choices. The ocean has given us so much; it is time to give back. Together, we can ensure that our oceans remain healthy and full of life for years to come.

## 选项

A. Yet, our oceans are facing serious threats from human activities, and we must act to save them.
B. Reducing our use of plastic is one of the most effective ways to protect marine life.
C. Choosing sustainable seafood helps keep fish populations healthy for the future.
D. Fighting climate change is also crucial for the survival of ocean ecosystems.
E. Everyone—from governments to individuals—must work together to protect our oceans.
F. For example, many countries have banned plastic bags in supermarkets and stores.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述了海洋的巨大价值（覆盖地球70%、提供食物和氧气），空后转折指出人类活动使海洋面临严重危险（塑料、过度捕捞、污染）。A选项"Yet, our oceans are facing serious threats from human activities, and we must act to save them"完美承上启下：用"Yet"转折，承接前文对海洋价值的描述，引出后文人类威胁的详细论述，并点明"我们必须采取行动"的主题。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段全文围绕一次性塑料的危害展开——吸管、袋子、瓶子如何进入海洋、伤害海龟和鱼类，以及我们如何减少使用。B选项"Reducing our use of plastic is one of the most effective ways to protect marine life"精准概括了段落核心：减少塑料使用是保护海洋生物的有效方法。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段讨论过度捕捞问题：捕鱼速度超过繁殖速度、种群崩溃、需可持续捕捞。C选项"Choosing sustainable seafood helps keep fish populations healthy for the future"正是段落主旨的总结——选择可持续海鲜有助于保持鱼类种群的未来健康。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段聚焦气候变化对海洋的影响：升温导致珊瑚白化、海平面上升、碳减排措施。D选项"Fighting climate change is also crucial for the survival of ocean ecosystems"准确概括了段落主题——应对气候变化对海洋生态系统生存至关重要。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结全文，指出政府、企业、个人各自的责任，强调"共同守护"。E选项"Everyone—from governments to individuals—must work together to protect our oceans"精准呼应段落的总结性主旨。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：以"For example"开头，列举了"许多国家禁止超市使用塑料袋"这一具体事例。虽然与塑料污染主题相关，但它是支撑第二段论点的论据性句子，而非段落的概括总结句。若放在[2]处会与后文重复（后文已详细展开），且层次错位——F是论据层，正确选项B是论点/概括层。
