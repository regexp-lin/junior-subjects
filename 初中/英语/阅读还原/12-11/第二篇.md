**Urban Planning: Designing Cities for People**

Urban planning is the process of designing and managing how cities grow and function. It involves decisions about where to build homes, roads, parks, schools, and factories. Good planning can make cities more liveable, efficient, and sustainable. Poor planning can lead to traffic jams, pollution, and neighbourhoods where people struggle to meet their basic needs. [1]

Cities face many challenges as they grow. Population increase puts pressure on housing, transport, and public services. Land is limited, so planners must decide how to use space wisely. They must balance economic development with environmental protection. They also need to think about who benefits from new projects—and who might be left behind. [2]

Green spaces are essential in urban planning. Parks, trees, and gardens provide places for exercise, relaxation, and community events. They help reduce heat in summer, improve air quality, and support wildlife. Cities with more green space often report better mental health and higher property values. Integrating nature into cities makes them more pleasant and resilient. [3]

Transport is another key area. Well-designed public transport reduces traffic and pollution. Bike lanes and pedestrian-friendly streets encourage healthier and cleaner ways to get around. Some cities are experimenting with car-free zones or low-emission areas. The goal is to move more people with fewer vehicles and less environmental impact. [4]

Urban planning requires input from many people. Planners, engineers, and officials work with residents, businesses, and community groups. Public meetings and surveys help gather ideas and concerns. When citizens participate, planning is more likely to reflect their needs and create cities where everyone can thrive. [5]

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍城市规划——设计与管理城市发展的过程、涉及住房道路公园学校工厂布局、好的规划使城市更宜居高效可持续、差的规划导致拥堵污染与难以满足基本需求，空后需总结规划对城市生活质量的影响。A的"For this reason"承接前文，"direct impact on the quality of life in cities"与前文对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说城市增长面临诸多挑战——人口增加对住房交通与公共服务施压、土地有限须合理用地、需平衡经济发展与环境保护、考虑谁受益谁被忽视，空后需总结应对挑战所需的能力。B的"These challenges"指代前文，"require careful decisions and long-term vision"与前文呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说绿地在城市规划中必不可少——公园树木花园供锻炼休闲与社区活动、降温改善空气支持野生动物、更多绿地常伴随更好心理健康与房产价值、将自然融入城市使其更宜居更有韧性，空后需总结自然与健康城市的关系。C的"In this way"承接前文，"nature becomes a vital part of a healthy and attractive city"与前文对应。

- **[4] D**：

- **[5] E**：
