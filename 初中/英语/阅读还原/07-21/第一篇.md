**The Modern Transformation of Libraries**

When we think of libraries, many of us picture quiet rooms filled with shelves of books—a place to borrow and read. While that image is still partly true, libraries around the world have been changing to meet the needs of today's communities. [1] These changes reflect a broader shift in how libraries serve the public. Modern libraries offer not only books but also computers, Wi-Fi, meeting rooms, and programs for all ages. They have become community hubs where people learn, create, and connect with others. In many cities, the library is one of the few free spaces where anyone can spend time, use technology, and attend events.

[2] Technology has played a major role in transforming libraries. Most libraries now provide free internet access, computer workstations, and online resources such as e-books and databases. Visitors can borrow e-books and audiobooks without leaving home. Libraries also help people who lack access to technology at home—students doing homework, job seekers filling out applications, or seniors staying connected with family. By offering these services, libraries work to reduce the "digital divide" between those who have technology and those who do not.

[3] Libraries today host a wide variety of programs and activities. Story times for children, book clubs for adults, language classes, and makerspaces with 3D printers are just a few examples. Some libraries offer job training, tax help, or even seed-lending programs for gardeners. These activities turn libraries into lively community centers rather than quiet warehouses of books. People come not only to borrow materials but also to learn new skills, meet neighbors, and participate in local culture.

[4] Despite all these changes, the core mission of libraries remains the same: to provide free access to information and knowledge for everyone. Whether in print or digital form, whether through books or programs, libraries continue to support learning and curiosity. They welcome people of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities. In a time when information is abundant but not always reliable, libraries also help people find trustworthy sources and develop critical thinking skills.

[5] The library of the twenty-first century is both traditional and new—a place that honors its past while embracing the future. By adapting to new technologies and community needs, libraries have shown that they remain essential public institutions. For anyone seeking knowledge, connection, or simply a quiet place to think, the modern library has something to offer. The transformation of libraries proves that some institutions can evolve without losing their most important values.

## 选项

A. Libraries have expanded their services to become much more than places that store and lend books.
B. Digital tools and online resources have become central to how libraries serve their communities.
C. A wide range of programs and events has made libraries active community centers.
D. The fundamental goal of providing free access to knowledge for all remains unchanged.
E. Libraries have successfully combined tradition with innovation to stay relevant today.
F. For example, some libraries now lend out tools and musical instruments in addition to books.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前指出图书馆在变化以满足当代需求，空后列举电脑、Wi-Fi、会议室、各类活动等新服务。A 句 "Libraries have expanded their services to become much more than places that store and lend books" 承接前文 "changing"，并自然过渡到后文对现代图书馆多元功能的展开，承上启下。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：段落主题是技术在图书馆转型中的作用。空后描述网络、电脑、电子书、缩小数字鸿沟等。B 句 "Digital tools and online resources have become central to how libraries serve their communities" 准确概括本段主旨。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：段落介绍图书馆举办的各类项目与活动。空后列举儿童故事时间、读书俱乐部、创客空间等。C 句 "A wide range of programs and events has made libraries active community centers" 与后文 "lively community centers" 完全呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：段落强调图书馆核心使命不变。空后说明无论形式如何，图书馆仍支持学习、欢迎所有人、帮助甄别信息。D 句 "The fundamental goal of providing free access to knowledge for all remains unchanged" 精准总结本段。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：结尾段总结图书馆兼具传统与创新、与时俱进。E 句 "Libraries have successfully combined tradition with innovation to stay relevant today" 与前文 "both traditional and new"、"honors its past while embracing the future" 自然衔接。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：出借工具和乐器虽体现图书馆服务扩展，但属于具体举例，应嵌入第 3 段 "makerspaces" 或 "seed-lending" 附近。作为独立选项，其层级为例证而非段落概括，易因主题相关而误选。
