# 10-03 第一篇

**Animal Shelters and Stray Animal Care**

Every year, countless cats and dogs are abandoned or lost and end up living on the streets. These stray animals often suffer from hunger, disease, and harsh weather. Fortunately, animal shelters provide a safe place where they can be fed, sheltered, and cared for until they find a new home. [1] In doing so, they play a vital role in promoting animal welfare and reducing the suffering of creatures who have nowhere else to go.

Shelters rely heavily on volunteers and donations to operate. People can help by walking dogs, cleaning cages, or simply spending time with the animals to keep them socialized. Financial donations help pay for food, medical care, and building upkeep. [2] For this reason, every contribution—whether time or money—makes a real difference in the lives of animals waiting for a home.

One of the best ways to reduce the number of stray animals is to promote responsible pet ownership. This includes spaying and neutering pets to prevent unwanted litters, as well as microchipping them so they can be identified if lost. [3] Over time, such practices can significantly lower the number of stray animals and ease the pressure on shelters across the country.

Adoption is at the heart of what shelters do. When someone adopts a stray animal, they give it a permanent home and a loving family. Many adopted pets become loyal companions for years to come. [4] In addition, adoption frees up space at the shelter so that another stray can receive care while waiting for its own forever home.

Animal welfare is a shared responsibility. Governments can pass laws to protect stray animals and support shelters, while schools can teach children about kindness to animals. [5] Together, these efforts can create a culture of compassion that extends to all living beings and improves our community as a whole.

## 选项

A. Animal shelters do more than offer temporary housing—they give homeless animals a second chance at life and help build a more compassionate society.
B. Without the support of the community, many shelters would struggle to survive, and thousands of animals would have nowhere to turn for help.
C. When owners take these steps, they not only protect their own pets but also reduce the burden on shelters and the stray population in their area.
D. Adopting from a shelter is often cheaper than buying from a breeder, and it saves a life while making room for another animal in need.
E. As more people become aware of the issue and take action, we move closer to a world where every animal has a chance to live a safe and happy life.
F. For example, a shelter in Shanghai rescued over 500 stray dogs last year and found homes for more than half of them.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述收容所为流浪动物提供食宿与照料直到被领养，空后说"they play a vital role in promoting animal welfare"。A的"do more than offer temporary housing"与前文"safe place"呼应，"give homeless animals a second chance"与空后"reducing the suffering"衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举志愿与捐赠对收容所的支撑，空后说"For this reason, every contribution—whether time or money—makes a real difference"。B的"Without the support of the community"承接前文，空后"every contribution"与B的"support"呼应，强调社区支持的重要性。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说绝育与芯片等负责任养宠做法，空后说"such practices can significantly lower the number of stray animals"。C的"When owners take these steps"指代前文，"reduce the burden on shelters and the stray population"与空后直接呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说领养给动物永久家庭、成为忠诚伴侣，空后说"In addition, adoption frees up space... so that another stray can receive care"。D的"saves a life while making room for another animal"与空后"frees up space"完全对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说政府立法、学校教育，空后说"Together, these efforts can create a culture of compassion"。E的"As more people become aware of the issue and take action"承接多方努力，"every animal has a chance to live a safe and happy life"与空后"compassion that extends to all living beings"衔接。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, a shelter in Shanghai rescued over 500 stray dogs last year and found homes for more than half of them.**：以For example开头，属具体案例与数据，各空均为概括或过渡句；易误放[1]或[4]，但两空分别需收容所意义的概括与领养好处的概括，非个例说明。
