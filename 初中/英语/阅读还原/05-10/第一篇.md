# 05-10 第一篇

**3D Printing: A New Way to Create**

3D printing is a technology that builds objects layer by layer from digital designs. [1] Instead of cutting or shaping materials like traditional factories do, a 3D printer adds material bit by bit until a complete object is formed. You can print toys, phone cases, jewelry, and even parts for machines. Some people use 3D printers at home, while doctors and engineers use them in hospitals and labs. This technology has changed the way we think about making things.

Why is 3D printing so useful? [2] One advantage is that it allows us to create custom items quickly. In the past, if you needed a special part for a broken tool, you had to order it and wait for days or weeks. With a 3D printer, you can download a design from the internet and print the part in a few hours. Designers can test their ideas by printing models before producing them in large numbers. That is why 3D printing is popular in both personal and professional settings.

3D printing has brought great benefits to medicine. [3] In fact, doctors now use 3D printers to create custom prosthetics（假肢） for patients who have lost a limb. Dentists print exact copies of teeth to plan treatments. Surgeons sometimes practice on 3D-printed models of a patient's organs before performing difficult operations. These advances help save lives and improve the quality of care. For this reason, 3D printing is considered one of the most important developments in modern healthcare.

However, 3D printing also has some limitations. [4] The materials used in 3D printing are often plastic, which may not be as strong as metal or other materials. Printing large objects can take many hours or even days. There are also concerns about copyright—people could copy and print products without permission. Despite these challenges, researchers are working to develop stronger materials and faster printers. In the future, 3D printing may become even more common in our daily lives.

[5] In short, 3D printing is a powerful tool that is changing how we create and fix things. From homemade projects to medical breakthroughs, this technology offers endless possibilities. Whether you are a student, an artist, or an engineer, 3D printing might soon become a part of your life too.

## 选项

A. In other words, it works by adding material step by step instead of removing it.
B. That is because it makes it easy to produce unique items in a short time.

E. In conclusion, 3D printing is reshaping the world of manufacturing and beyond.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：解释3D打印的工作原理。空前描述"3D printing is a technology that builds objects layer by layer from digital designs"，空后说明"Instead of cutting or shaping materials like traditional factories do, a 3D printer adds material bit by bit until a complete object is formed"。A选项"In other words, it works by adding material step by step instead of removing it"（换言之，它是通过逐步添加材料而非去除材料来运作的）中"In other words"对上文进行概括性解释，"adding material step by step instead of removing"与空后"adds material bit by bit"及"instead of cutting or shaping"精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：3D打印的快速定制优势。空前设问"Why is 3D printing so useful?"，空后列举"One advantage is that it allows us to create custom items quickly...In the past, you had to order and wait for days or weeks...With a 3D printer, you can download a design and print the part in a few hours...Designers can test their ideas...print models before producing in large numbers"。B选项"That is because it makes it easy to produce unique items in a short time"（那是因为它让在短时间内生产独特物品变得容易）中"That is because"回答空前问题，"unique items in a short time"与空后"custom items quickly"及"a few hours"完美呼应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：3D打印在医学领域的应用。空前提出"3D printing has brought great benefits to medicine"，空后详述"In fact, doctors now use 3D printers to create custom prosthetics...Dentists print exact copies of teeth...Surgeons practice on 3D-printed models...These advances help save lives...3D printing is considered one of the most important developments in modern healthcare"。C选项"In fact, the medical field has been one of the biggest beneficiaries of this technology"（事实上，医疗领域是这项技术的最大受益者之一）中"In fact"加强衔接，"biggest beneficiaries"与空后一系列医学应用及"most important developments"精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：3D打印的局限与挑战。空前指出"However, 3D printing also has some limitations"，空后列举"The materials...often plastic, which may not be as strong as metal...Printing large objects can take many hours or even days...concerns about copyright...Despite these challenges, researchers are working...In the future, 3D printing may become even more common"。D选项"It is true that this technology still has some drawbacks"（的确，这项技术仍有一些缺点）中"It is true that"与空前"However"呼应，"some drawbacks"总领空后材料强度、耗时、版权等具体问题，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与展望。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"3D printing is a powerful tool that is changing how we create and fix things...From homemade projects to medical breakthroughs...endless possibilities...3D printing might soon become a part of your life too"。E选项"In conclusion, 3D printing is reshaping the world of manufacturing and beyond"（总之，3D打印正在重塑制造业及更多领域）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"reshaping...manufacturing and beyond"与空后"changing how we create...homemade to medical"形成呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some schools have set up 3D printing labs for students.**：涉及学校设立3D打印实验室，易被误填[2]。但F以"For example"引出教育场景的具体应用，而空[2]前后文讨论的是3D打印的快速定制优势（个人与专业场景），空后强调的是"create custom items quickly"和"designers can test ideas"，并非教育推广；F属于3D打印的应用场景之一，与空[2]的"advantage"论述重点不匹配。F属"主题相关但衔接重点错位"型干扰。
