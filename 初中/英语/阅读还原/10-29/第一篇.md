# 10-29 第一篇

**The Benefits of Extracurricular Activities**

School is not only about textbooks and exams. What students do outside the classroom—sports, music, art, clubs, and volunteer work—can shape their character, build new skills, and open doors to future opportunities. Extracurricular activities are an important part of a well-rounded education, and they offer benefits that classroom learning alone cannot provide. [1] Extracurricular activities help students discover their interests, develop talents, and learn to work with others in ways that prepare them for life beyond school.

Sports and physical activities teach more than fitness. They help students learn about teamwork, discipline, and handling both victory and defeat. Whether it is basketball, swimming, or running, regular exercise improves health and concentration, and many students find that they do better in class when they are active. [2] The habits and values learned through sports often last a lifetime, influencing not only physical health but also how students approach challenges and cooperate with others in adulthood.

Arts and creative activities—such as music, drama, and painting—give students a way to express themselves and explore their imagination. These activities can reduce stress, build confidence, and improve skills like focus and problem-solving. Some students discover a passion that becomes a career; others simply enjoy the joy of creating something with their own hands or voice. [3] Whether students pursue the arts as a hobby or a profession, the experience enriches their lives and helps them see the world in new ways.

Clubs and groups bring together students who share common interests. A science club might run experiments; a debate team might practice public speaking. These activities help students build friendships, develop leadership skills, and learn to organize and plan. [4] Through clubs and team activities, students gain experience that goes beyond grades—experience in communication, responsibility, and working toward shared goals.

Balancing schoolwork and activities can be challenging. Students need to manage their time and sometimes make choices about what to prioritize. But the effort is usually worth it. [5] Students who take part in extracurricular activities tend to do well in school, feel more connected to their community, and develop a sense of purpose that helps them succeed in whatever path they choose.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍课外活动种类及其对性格、技能、机遇的作用，空后需概括课外活动的核心价值。A的"discover their interests, develop talents, and learn to work with others"与前文多元活动对应，"prepare them for life beyond school"与"well-rounded education"呼应，定义课外活动意义。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说体育运动教团队精神、纪律、胜败观，运动改善健康与专注、促进学业，空后需总结体育的长期影响。B的"The habits and values learned through sports often last a lifetime"与"teach more than fitness"呼应，"influencing... how students approach challenges and cooperate"与"teamwork, discipline"衔接。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说艺术活动——音乐、戏剧、绘画——帮助表达、减轻压力、建立自信，有人发展为职业有人仅为爱好，空后需总结无论职业与否的价值。C的"Whether students pursue the arts as a hobby or a profession"与前文两种路径呼应，"enriches their lives and helps them see the world in new ways"与"express... explore imagination"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说社团活动——科学社、辩论队——促进友谊、领导力、组织与计划能力，空后需总结超越学业分数的收获。D的"Through clubs and team activities, students gain experience that goes beyond grades"与"bring together students"呼应，"communication, responsibility, and working toward shared goals"与前文能力列举衔接。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说平衡学业与活动有挑战，但努力值得，空后需总结参与课外活动的综合好处。E的"Students who take part in extracurricular activities tend to do well in school"与"do better in class when they are active"呼应，"feel more connected... develop a sense of purpose"是全文收尾的合理升华。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, a student who joins the school band might learn to read music, practice with others, and perform in front of an audience.**：以For example开头，为具体活动举例（乐队）。各空均为概括或过渡句；[3]虽涉及艺术活动，但需的是"无论爱好或职业的普遍价值"概括，而非单一乐队案例。
