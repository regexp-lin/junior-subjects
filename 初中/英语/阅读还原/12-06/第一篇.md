**The Development of Film**

Films began over a hundred years ago as short, silent black-and-white clips. Early movies showed simple scenes like a train arriving at a station or workers leaving a factory. There was no sound, so live music was often played in cinemas to accompany the images. Audiences were amazed by the moving pictures on the screen. [1]

Sound arrived in the late 1920s. "The Jazz Singer" (1927) was one of the first films with spoken dialogue. Colour film became common in the 1930s and 1940s. These changes made movies more lifelike and immersive. Directors could tell deeper stories and create stronger emotional connections with viewers. [2]

In recent decades, technology has transformed film again. Computer-generated imagery (CGI) allows filmmakers to create worlds that would be impossible in real life. Digital cameras have replaced film, making shooting and editing faster and cheaper. Blockbusters like superhero movies rely heavily on visual effects to attract audiences. [3]

Streaming has changed how we watch films. People can now watch movies at home on their phones, tablets, or TVs. Cinemas still offer a shared experience—watching with others in a dark room on a big screen—but many choose convenience over the big screen. The film industry continues to adapt to new habits and technologies. [4]

Film remains one of the most powerful ways to tell stories. From silent shorts to today's 3D blockbusters, it has always reflected our dreams, fears, and hopes. Whether in a cinema or at home, films will keep evolving to entertain and inspire us. [5]

## 选项

A. In this way, film started as a simple experiment and grew into a worldwide form of entertainment.
B. Each advance gave filmmakers new tools to tell stories and engage their audiences.
C. These tools have opened up endless possibilities for imagination and storytelling.
D. How we watch films has changed as much as how they are made.
E. Through all these changes, film has stayed at the heart of human storytelling.
F. For example, the film "Avatar" used advanced CGI to create its fictional planet of Pandora.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍电影起源——百余年前的无声黑白短片、简单场景、现场伴奏、观众惊叹，空后需总结从简单实验到全球娱乐的发展。A的"In this way"承接前文，"simple experiment"与"short, silent black-and-white clips"对应，"grew into a worldwide form of entertainment"与前文呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说有声电影出现（《爵士歌手》）、彩色普及、更逼真更沉浸、导演可讲更深故事并与观众建立情感联系，空后需总结技术进步对创作的赋能。B的"Each advance"指代有声与彩色，"new tools to tell stories and engage their audiences"与前文对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说近年技术变革——CGI创造不可能场景、数码取代胶片、大片依赖视效吸睛，空后需总结技术带来的创作自由。C的"These tools"指代CGI与数码技术，"endless possibilities for imagination and storytelling"与前文呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说流媒体改变观影方式、家庭多终端观看、影院仍有共享体验但更多人选择便利、产业持续适应，空后需总结观影方式的变革。D的"How we watch films has changed as much as how they are made"与前文对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说电影仍是讲故事最有力方式、从默片到3D大片一直反映梦想恐惧希望、影院或家中都会继续演化，空后需总结电影在变革中的核心地位。E的"Through all these changes"承接全文，"stayed at the heart of human storytelling"与前文呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the film "Avatar" used advanced CGI...**：以For example开头，为具体电影案例（《阿凡达》CGI）。各空均为概括或过渡句；[3]易被误选因涉及CGI，但该空需总结技术带来的整体可能性，而非单一影片的视效举例。
