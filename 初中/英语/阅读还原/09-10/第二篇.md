# 09-10 第二篇

**Protecting Wild Animals**

Wild animals—from tigers and elephants to birds and butterflies—play vital roles in nature. They help pollinate plants, control pests, and keep ecosystems in balance. Yet many species are in danger. Habitat loss, pollution, and hunting have pushed countless animals toward extinction. [1] Understanding why wildlife matters and what threatens it is the first step toward taking action to protect it.

One major threat is the loss of natural habitats. When forests are cut down or wetlands are filled in, animals lose their homes and food sources. Protecting and restoring these places gives wildlife a chance to survive. [2] Conservation areas such as national parks and nature reserves provide safe spaces where animals can live and breed without human disturbance.

Another threat comes from illegal trade. Poachers hunt rare animals for their skin, horns, or meat, often driving species to the edge of extinction. Laws and international agreements aim to stop this trade. [3] Strong enforcement, together with education about the harm of buying wildlife products, can help reduce demand and save vulnerable species.

Climate change adds more pressure. Rising temperatures and changing weather patterns affect where animals can live and what they can eat. Some species must move to cooler areas or higher ground; others cannot adapt fast enough. [4] Addressing climate change benefits not only people but also the countless species that share our planet.

Every person can contribute to animal protection. We can support conservation organizations, avoid products made from endangered species, and learn more about the wildlife around us. [5] The choices we make today will shape whether future generations inherit a world rich in wildlife or one that has lost much of its natural wonder.

## 选项

A. Learning about the value of wildlife and the dangers it faces is essential before we can help.
B. These protected spaces are often the last hope for species that have nowhere else to go.
C. Combining legal action with public awareness offers a path to curb poaching and protect at-risk animals.
D. Actions that slow global warming also help preserve the habitats and food chains that wildlife depend on.
E. Small actions, when multiplied by millions of people, can create real change for the future of wildlife.
F. For example, the giant panda was once critically endangered but has recovered thanks to protection efforts in China.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述野生动物的重要性和面临的威胁（栖息地丧失、污染、狩猎），空后强调"Understanding why wildlife matters and what threatens it is the first step toward taking action"。A的"Learning about the value of wildlife and the dangers it faces is essential before we can help"与"first step"和"taking action"完美呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说保护与恢复栖息地、设立保护区，空后说"Conservation areas... provide safe spaces where animals can live and breed"。B的"These protected spaces are often the last hope for species that have nowhere else to go"与"safe spaces"和"without human disturbance"呼应，概括保护区的作用。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前描述非法捕猎与贸易、法律与国际协定，空后说"Strong enforcement, together with education... can help reduce demand and save vulnerable species"。C的"Combining legal action with public awareness offers a path to curb poaching"与执法和教育结合的逻辑一致，概括该段主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论气候变化对动物栖息地和食物的影响，空后说"Addressing climate change benefits not only people but also... species"。D的"Actions that slow global warming also help preserve the habitats and food chains that wildlife depend on"准确概括应对气候变化对野生动物的益处。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前列举个人可做之事，空后说"The choices we make today will shape whether future generations inherit..."。E的"Small actions, when multiplied by millions of people, can create real change for the future of wildlife"升华主题，与"choices we make"和"future generations"完美对应，适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the giant panda was once critically endangered but has recovered thanks to protection efforts in China.**：以For example开头，属具体物种保护举例；各空均为概括或过渡句，F与动物保护相关但为个案，易误放[2]或[5]，但两空分别需保护区概括与个人行动升华，非举例。
