# 10-25 第一篇

**Architecture as Art**

Buildings are more than places where people live and work. Great architecture can inspire us, express cultural values, and shape how we feel about the places around us. From ancient temples to modern skyscrapers, buildings tell the story of human creativity. [1] Architecture blends art and science, using design, materials, and light to create spaces that are both useful and beautiful.

Throughout history, different cultures have developed their own architectural styles. Traditional Chinese buildings often feature wooden structures, curved roofs, and courtyards that connect with nature. In contrast, Western architecture has emphasized columns, domes, and grand facades. [2] Each style reflects the beliefs, climate, and technology of its time, helping us understand how people lived and thought in the past.

Today, architects face new challenges. Cities are growing, and we need buildings that save energy, reduce pollution, and improve the quality of life for millions. Green buildings with solar panels, green roofs, and smart designs are becoming more common. [3] Sustainable architecture is no longer a choice but a necessity, as we work to protect the environment for future generations.

Good design also considers how people use space. Schools need bright classrooms and safe playgrounds. Hospitals need calm, welcoming areas that help patients feel at ease. Offices need spaces that encourage teamwork and creativity. [4] When architects put people's needs at the center of their work, the results can transform daily life for the better.

Architecture will continue to evolve as our needs and technologies change. New materials, digital tools, and ideas about community are shaping the buildings of tomorrow. [5] The best architecture serves both the present and the future, creating lasting works that future generations will admire and enjoy.

## 选项

A. Architecture blends art and science, using design, materials, and light to create spaces that are both useful and beautiful.
B. Each style reflects the beliefs, climate, and technology of its time, helping us understand how people lived and thought in the past.
C. Sustainable architecture is no longer a choice but a necessity, as we work to protect the environment for future generations.
D. When architects put people's needs at the center of their work, the results can transform daily life for the better.
E. The best architecture serves both the present and the future, creating lasting works that future generations will admire and enjoy.
F. For example, the Forbidden City in Beijing shows how traditional Chinese architecture combined beauty with political symbolism.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍建筑作为艺术的概念——能启发灵感、表达文化、塑造感受，空后需要承接"艺术与功能"的概括。A的"Architecture blends art and science... both useful and beautiful"对前文建筑双重属性做总结，与"design, materials, and light"呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前对比中西方建筑风格差异（中式木结构、庭院 vs 西方柱式、穹顶），空后需说明各风格的意义。B的"Each style reflects the beliefs, climate, and technology of its time"承接前文多种风格，"understand how people lived and thought"与历史建筑的文化价值呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说现代建筑面临节能、环保、城市扩张等挑战，绿色建筑兴起，空后需概括可持续建筑的地位。C的"Sustainable architecture is no longer a choice but a necessity"与空后"protect the environment for future generations"形成因果，强调必要性。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前举例学校、医院、办公室的不同空间需求，空后需总结以人为本的设计效果。D的"When architects put people's needs at the center"承接前文各类使用者需求，"transform daily life for the better"与"bright classrooms、calm areas、teamwork spaces"的积极影响对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说建筑随需求与技术演变，新材料与数字工具塑造未来，空后需总结优秀建筑的持久价值。E的"serves both the present and the future"与"evolve"呼应，"lasting works that future generations will admire"与文末展望形成收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Forbidden City in Beijing shows how traditional Chinese architecture combined beauty with political symbolism.**：以For example开头，为具体举例（故宫个案）。各空均为概括句或过渡句；[2]虽涉及中式建筑，但需的是"各风格反映时代"的概括，而非单个建筑案例。
