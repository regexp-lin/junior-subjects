**The Intelligence of Dolphins**

Dolphins are among the most intelligent animals on Earth. They live in social groups, communicate through clicks and whistles, and have shown the ability to solve problems and use tools. [1] In other words, dolphins display complex thinking and social behaviour that fascinate scientists and the public alike. Understanding dolphin intelligence helps us appreciate the richness of life in the oceans and reminds us to protect these remarkable creatures.

Dolphins have a sophisticated（复杂的）way of communicating. [2] Each dolphin has its own unique whistle that works like a name—other dolphins can recognise and call to specific individuals. They use different sounds for different situations: alarms when danger is near, clicks for finding food through echolocation（回声定位）, and various whistles for social interaction. Researchers have found that dolphins can understand basic human instructions and even learn to follow rules in experiments. Their communication system is one of the most advanced in the animal kingdom.

Dolphins show signs of creativity and cooperation. [3] In the wild, some dolphins use sea sponges as tools to protect their noses while searching for food on the ocean floor—a skill passed down from mother to child. They hunt together in groups, sometimes creating "nets" of bubbles to trap fish. Dolphins have also been observed helping injured companions, supporting them at the surface so they can breathe. Such behaviour suggests not only intelligence but also empathy and strong social bonds.

Dolphins face serious threats. [4] Pollution, fishing nets, ship traffic, and noise from human activities harm dolphin populations around the world. Some species are endangered; others suffer from habitat loss and declining food sources. Marine protected areas, stricter rules on fishing and shipping, and public education can help. When we learn about dolphins' intelligence and social lives, we are more likely to care about their survival. Protecting dolphins means protecting the health of the entire ocean ecosystem.

[5] Dolphins remind us that intelligence and empathy exist beyond the human world. By studying and protecting them, we not only enrich our understanding of nature but also take responsibility for the oceans we share. The next time you see a dolphin—whether in the wild, at an aquarium, or in a film—remember that you are looking at a creature capable of complex thought, communication, and care for others.

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出海豚的智慧与社交能力及其吸引力。空前描述"Dolphins are among the most intelligent animals...live in social groups, communicate through clicks and whistles, solve problems, use tools"，空后说明"dolphins display complex thinking and social behaviour that fascinate scientists and public...Understanding helps appreciate ocean life...remind us to protect these remarkable creatures"。A选项"In other words, dolphins show advanced cognitive and social abilities that attract widespread interest"（换言之，海豚展现出吸引广泛关注的高级认知与社交能力）中"In other words"概括空前，"advanced cognitive and social abilities...attract widespread interest"与空后"complex thinking and social behaviour...fascinate"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：海豚多样的声音交流及对人类指令的响应。空前提出"Dolphins have a sophisticated way of communicating"，空后详述"Each dolphin unique whistle like a name...recognise and call specific individuals...different sounds: alarms, clicks for echolocation, whistles for social interaction...Researchers found understand basic human instructions, learn to follow rules in experiments...communication system one of most advanced"。B选项"That is because they use a variety of sounds and can learn to respond to human cues in experiments"（那是因为它们使用多种声音并能在实验中学会对人类提示做出反应）中"That is because"解释交流的复杂性，"variety of sounds...respond to human cues"与空后 different sounds、understand instructions、follow rules 完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：海豚的工具使用、协作与互助行为。空前提出"Dolphins show signs of creativity and cooperation"，空后列举"Use sea sponges as tools...skill passed mother to child...hunt together, create bubble nets to trap fish...helping injured companions, support at surface to breathe...suggests intelligence, empathy, strong social bonds"。C选项"In fact, dolphins use tools, hunt cooperatively, and assist injured group members"（事实上，海豚使用工具、协作狩猎并帮助受伤的群体成员）中"In fact"强化创意与协作，"use tools, hunt cooperatively, assist injured"与空后 sponges、bubble nets、help injured 精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：人类活动对海豚的威胁及保护必要。空前提出"Dolphins face serious threats"，空后描述"Pollution, fishing nets, ship traffic, noise harm populations...Some species endangered...habitat loss, declining food...Marine protected areas, stricter rules, public education can help...learn about intelligence and social lives...more likely to care...Protecting dolphins means protecting ocean ecosystem"。D选项"These threats show that human activities endanger dolphins and require conservation efforts"（这些威胁表明人类活动危及海豚并需要保护行动）中"These threats"指代空后污染、渔网、航运、噪音等，"endanger...require conservation"与空后 harm、endangered、conservation 完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与海豚智慧、同理心及保护呼吁。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"Dolphins remind us intelligence and empathy exist beyond human world...studying and protecting...enrich understanding...take responsibility for oceans...creature capable of complex thought, communication, care for others"。E选项"In short, dolphins represent intelligence and empathy in nature and deserve our protection"（简言之，海豚代表自然界中的智慧与同理心，值得我们保护）用"In short"总结全文，"intelligence and empathy...deserve our protection"与空后"intelligence and empathy exist...protecting"形成呼应，收束精准。
