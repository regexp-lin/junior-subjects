# 05-21 第一篇

**Renewable Energy: Power for a Greener Future**

Fossil fuels like coal, oil, and natural gas have powered our world for over a century. But they produce a lot of carbon dioxide when burned, which contributes to climate change. They are also running out. [1] In fact, renewable energy comes from sources that never run out or can be replaced naturally—such as sunlight, wind, water, and heat from the earth. Unlike fossil fuels, these sources produce little or no pollution. That is why more and more countries are turning to renewable energy to reduce pollution and fight climate change.

What are the main types of renewable energy? [2] Solar power uses panels to turn sunlight into electricity. Wind power uses large turbines（涡轮机）to capture the energy of the wind. Hydroelectric power comes from the flow of water, often from dams（大坝）. Geothermal（地热的）energy uses heat from inside the Earth. Each has its own advantages: solar works on sunny days, wind power needs windy areas, and hydroelectric plants can store energy in reservoirs（水库）. In other words, different renewable sources suit different places and conditions, and they can work together to provide steady, clean power.

Why is the shift to renewable energy important? [3] Burning less fossil fuel means less air pollution and fewer greenhouse gases. This helps protect our health and slow down global warming. Renewable energy also creates new jobs—in building and maintaining solar farms, wind farms, and other facilities. Some countries have already succeeded in getting most of their electricity from renewables. For this reason, investing in renewable energy is not only good for the environment but also for the economy and our future.

Challenges still remain. [4] Solar and wind power depend on the weather, so they are not always available when we need them. Building large wind farms or hydropower dams can affect local wildlife and communities. The cost of renewable technology has dropped a lot, but building new systems still requires money and time. So we need better ways to store energy—such as batteries—and smarter grids that can balance supply and demand. Research and innovation continue to make renewable energy more reliable and affordable.

[5] In short, renewable energy is key to a sustainable future. By using more sun, wind, and water, we can reduce our dependence on fossil fuels and protect the planet for future generations. Governments, businesses, and individuals all have a role to play. Remember: the choices we make about energy today will shape the world we live in tomorrow. Every solar panel on a roof and every wind turbine on a hill is a step toward a cleaner, greener world.

## 选项

A. That is because renewable energy comes from natural sources that do not run out.
B. In other words, different renewable sources fit different needs and can work together.
C. That is why renewable energy benefits both the environment and the economy.
D. It is true that we still need to improve storage and systems for renewable energy.
E. In conclusion, renewable energy is essential for a sustainable future.
F. For example, some schools have installed solar panels on their roofs.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：总领可再生能源的定义与特性。空前描述"Fossil fuels like coal, oil, and natural gas have powered our world for over a century. But they produce a lot of carbon dioxide when burned, which contributes to climate change. They are also running out"，空后说明"In fact, renewable energy comes from sources that never run out or can be replaced naturally—such as sunlight, wind, water, and heat from the earth...Unlike fossil fuels, these sources produce little or no pollution...more and more countries are turning to renewable energy to reduce pollution and fight climate change"。A选项"That is because renewable energy comes from natural sources that do not run out"（那是因为可再生能源来自不会耗尽的自然资源）中"That is because"解释可再生能源的特点，"natural sources that do not run out"与空后never run out、replaced naturally、sunlight/wind/water精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：各类可再生能源的适用性与协同。空前设问"What are the main types of renewable energy?"，空后列举"Solar power uses panels to turn sunlight into electricity...Wind power uses large turbines to capture the energy of the wind...Hydroelectric power comes from the flow of water...Geothermal energy uses heat from inside the Earth...Each has its own advantages: solar works on sunny days, wind power needs windy areas, and hydroelectric plants can store energy in reservoirs...different renewable sources suit different places and conditions, and they can work together to provide steady, clean power"。B选项"In other words, different renewable sources fit different needs and can work together"（换言之，不同可再生能源适应不同需求并能协同工作）中"In other words"概括空后内容，"fit different needs and can work together"与空后advantages、suit different places、work together完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：可再生能源对环境与经济的双重益处。空前设问"Why is the shift to renewable energy important?"，空后说明"Burning less fossil fuel means less air pollution and fewer greenhouse gases...This helps protect our health and slow down global warming...Renewable energy also creates new jobs—in building and maintaining solar farms, wind farms, and other facilities...Some countries have already succeeded in getting most of their electricity from renewables...investing in renewable energy is not only good for the environment but also for the economy and our future"。C选项"That is why renewable energy benefits both the environment and the economy"（因此可再生能源对环境和经济都有益）中"That is why"承接空前及空后结论，"benefits both the environment and the economy"与空后less pollution、new jobs、good for environment and economy精准对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：可再生能源面临的挑战与改进方向。空前提出"Challenges still remain"，空后详述"Solar and wind power depend on the weather, so they are not always available when we need them...Building large wind farms or hydropower dams can affect local wildlife and communities...The cost of renewable technology has dropped a lot, but building new systems still requires money and time...we need better ways to store energy—such as batteries—and smarter grids that can balance supply and demand...Research and innovation continue to make renewable energy more reliable and affordable"。D选项"It is true that we still need to improve storage and systems for renewable energy"（的确，我们仍需改进可再生能源的储能和系统）中"It is true that"加强衔接，"improve storage and systems"与空后batteries、smarter grids、balance supply and demand完美呼应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与可持续未来的呼吁。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"renewable energy is key to a sustainable future...By using more sun, wind, and water, we can reduce our dependence on fossil fuels and protect the planet for future generations...Governments, businesses, and individuals all have a role to play...the choices we make about energy today will shape the world we live in tomorrow...Every solar panel on a roof and every wind turbine on a hill is a step toward a cleaner, greener world"。E选项"In conclusion, renewable energy is essential for a sustainable future"（总之，可再生能源对可持续未来至关重要）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"essential for a sustainable future"与空后key to、reduce dependence、protect the planet形成呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some schools have installed solar panels on their roofs.**：涉及学校屋顶安装太阳能板，易被误填[2]或[5]。但F讨论的是具体应用场景，而空[2]列举的是太阳能、风能、水能、地热等能源类型及各自特点，空[5]呼吁政府、企业与个人的共同参与；F属于个案举例，与空[2]的能源类型划分、空[5]的宏观呼吁论述层级不同。F属"主题相关但论述层级错位"型干扰。
