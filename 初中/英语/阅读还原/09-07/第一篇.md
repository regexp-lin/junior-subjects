# 09-07 第一篇

**Robots in Our Daily Lives**

Robots are no longer just characters in science fiction movies. They are already a part of our daily lives, working in factories, hospitals, homes, and even schools. From vacuum-cleaning robots to surgical assistants, these machines are changing the way we live and work. [1] As technology advances, robots will become even more common, raising exciting possibilities and important questions about the future.

In factories, robots have been used for decades to build cars, electronics, and other products. They can work faster, more accurately, and for longer hours than humans. [2] This has made manufacturing cheaper and more efficient, but it has also led to concerns about job losses in some industries.

Robots are increasingly used in healthcare. Surgical robots help doctors perform delicate operations with greater precision. Robots also assist in delivering medicine, disinfecting rooms, and caring for elderly patients. [3] These medical robots are not replacing doctors—they are giving them better tools to save lives and improve patient care.

At home, smart robots are becoming more popular. Robotic vacuums, lawn mowers, and even cooking assistants are making everyday tasks easier. [4] As these home robots become smarter and more affordable, more families will welcome them as helpful additions to their households.

The rise of robots also raises ethical questions. If robots take over many jobs, what will people do for work? How do we ensure that robots are used safely and fairly? [5] Thinking carefully about these questions now will help us build a future where humans and robots work together for the benefit of all.

## 选项

A. The growing presence of robots in society is transforming industries and reshaping how we think about work and life.
B. However, automation also creates new types of jobs that require skills in programming, maintenance, and design.
C. By assisting rather than replacing human professionals, robots enhance the quality of services we receive.
D. The convenience they offer is making people more open to the idea of sharing their living spaces with machines.
E. Answering these questions requires thoughtful planning, fair policies, and open conversations among all members of society.
F. For example, Japan has developed robots that can hold conversations and provide companionship to elderly people.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前列举机器人在各领域的应用，空后说技术进步使机器人更普及。A的"growing presence of robots in society"承接广泛应用，"transforming industries and reshaping how we think about work and life"概括深远影响。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说工厂机器人更快更精确，空后说制造业更高效但也引发失业担忧。B的"However, automation also creates new types of jobs"平衡失业担忧，"programming, maintenance, and design"指出新就业方向。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举医疗机器人的功能（手术、送药、消毒、护理老人），空后说这些机器人不是取代医生而是给予更好工具。C的"By assisting rather than replacing human professionals"承接"not replacing"概念，"enhance the quality of services"概括作用。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前列举家用智能机器人（扫地、割草、烹饪助手），空后说随着变得更智能更便宜将被更多家庭接受。D的"The convenience they offer"承接前文便利性，"more open to sharing their living spaces with machines"引出接受度提高。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前提出伦理问题（就业、安全、公平），空后说现在认真思考有助于未来。E的"Answering these questions requires thoughtful planning, fair policies"承接伦理问题，"open conversations among all members of society"概括解决路径。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, Japan has developed robots that can hold conversations and provide companionship to elderly people.**：以For example开头，属具体国家案例；易误放[3]或[4]，但两空分别需医疗机器人辅助角色的概括和家用机器人便利性的概括，非个案。
