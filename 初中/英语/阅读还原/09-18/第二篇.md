# 09-18 第二篇

**Traditional Chinese Medicine: Wisdom from the Past**

Traditional Chinese Medicine, or TCM, has been used in China for thousands of years. It includes herbal medicine, acupuncture, massage, and exercises like tai chi. [1] TCM offers a different way of thinking about health, focusing on balance and harmony within the body rather than only treating symptoms.

TCM is based on ideas such as yin and yang and the flow of qi, or life energy. Practitioners believe that illness often comes from imbalance. [2] Restoring balance is seen as the key to health, and treatments aim to support the body's natural ability to heal itself.

Herbal medicine is a major part of TCM. Many plants and natural substances are used to treat various conditions. Some of these remedies have been studied by modern science and found to have real effects. [3] Combining ancient knowledge with modern research can help us understand and use TCM more safely and effectively.

TCM is also used for prevention. Practices like tai chi and qigong are believed to improve strength, flexibility, and mental well-being. [4] These gentle exercises have gained popularity worldwide as people seek ways to stay healthy without relying only on medicine.

Today, TCM is used alongside modern medicine in many places. It is not meant to replace Western medicine but to complement it. [5] The best approach to health often involves respecting both traditional wisdom and modern science, using each where it can help the most.

## 选项

A. TCM represents an alternative approach to wellness that values whole-body balance over quick fixes.
B. The philosophy behind TCM views healing as a process of returning the body to its natural state of balance.
C. Bridging traditional practices with scientific study can unlock the true potential of herbal medicine.
D. Mind-body practices from TCM have become global tools for maintaining health and preventing disease.
E. Integrating the best of both medical traditions can lead to better outcomes for patients.
F. For example, acupuncture has been used for over 2,000 years to relieve pain and treat various health problems.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍TCM包含草药、针灸、推拿、太极等，空后说TCM提供不同健康观，侧重身体平衡。A的"alternative approach to wellness"与"different way of thinking about health"呼应，"whole-body balance over quick fixes"与"balance and harmony within the body rather than only treating symptoms"一致。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说TCM基于阴阳、气等概念，认为疾病多源于失衡，空后说恢复平衡被视为健康关键。B的"Restoring balance is seen as the key to health"与"Restoring balance"直接对应，"returning the body to its natural state of balance"与"support the body's natural ability to heal"逻辑一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说草药是TCM重要部分，部分经现代科学验证有效，空后强调结合古今知识可更安全有效使用。C的"Bridging traditional practices with scientific study"与"Combining ancient knowledge with modern research"呼应，"unlock the true potential of herbal medicine"概括本段主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说太极、气功等用于预防、改善身心，空后强调这些温和运动在全球受欢迎。D的"Mind-body practices from TCM"与"tai chi and qigong"对应，"global tools for maintaining health"与"gained popularity worldwide"一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说TCM与西医并用、互补而非取代，空后总结最佳健康策略是兼顾传统智慧与现代科学。E的"Integrating the best of both medical traditions"与"respecting both traditional wisdom and modern science"呼应，"better outcomes for patients"与"using each where it can help the most"一致。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, acupuncture has been used for over 2,000 years to relieve pain and treat various health problems.**：以For example开头，是具体疗法案例；易误放[1]或[3]，但[1]需整体健康观概括，[3]需古今结合的研究概括，非单一疗法介绍。
