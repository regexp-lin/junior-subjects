# 06-23 第一篇

**Dragon Boat Festival: Racing on the Water**

The Dragon Boat Festival, or Duanwu Festival（端午节）, is one of China's oldest traditions. It falls on the fifth day of the fifth month of the lunar calendar, usually in June. People celebrate it with dragon boat races, zongzi（粽子）, and stories about the poet Qu Yuan. [1] In other words, the festival combines sport, food, and history, bringing families and communities together every year. It is a time to remember the past and enjoy the spirit of teamwork and competition.

Dragon boat racing is the most exciting part of the festival. [2] Teams of paddlers sit in long, narrow boats shaped like dragons and race to the beat of a drum. The drummer at the front sets the rhythm, and everyone must row in time. Races can be short sprints or longer races of several kilometres. Winning requires not only strength but also perfect coordination—if one person is out of sync, the whole boat slows down. Watching dragon boat races has become a popular activity, and many cities hold competitions that attract thousands of spectators.

The festival is also linked to the story of Qu Yuan. [3] Qu Yuan was a loyal minister and poet who lived over 2,000 years ago. When his kingdom fell, he was so sad that he threw himself into a river. According to legend, people raced their boats to save him and threw zongzi into the water so that the fish would not eat his body. Today, eating zongzi—sticky rice wrapped in bamboo leaves—is a way to honour his memory. The story teaches us about loyalty, patriotism, and the importance of remembering those who sacrificed for their country.

Families and communities prepare for the festival in many ways. [4] People hang herbs such as wormwood（艾草）and calamus on their doors to drive away evil spirits and insects. Some parents tie coloured threads around their children's wrists for good luck. In southern China, where dragon boat racing is most popular, villages and towns form teams months in advance and train hard for the races. The festival is also a time for family reunions—relatives gather to make zongzi, watch races, and share a meal.

[5] Whether you are racing, watching, or enjoying zongzi with your family, the festival offers something for everyone. The next time you see a dragon boat cutting through the water, remember the history behind it and the community spirit it represents.

## 选项

A. In other words, the festival combines sport, food, and history, bringing families and communities together every year.
B. Teams of paddlers sit in long, narrow boats shaped like dragons and race to the beat of a drum.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：概括端午节的多重内涵与节日意义。空前描述"Dragon Boat Festival...Duanwu...one of China's oldest traditions...fifth day fifth month lunar calendar, usually June...celebrate with dragon boat races, zongzi, stories about Qu Yuan"，空后说明"It is a time to remember the past and enjoy spirit of teamwork and competition"。A选项"In other words, the festival combines sport, food, and history, bringing families and communities together every year"（换言之，这个节日融合了体育、美食与历史，每年将家庭和社区凝聚在一起）中"In other words"概括空前，"combines sport, food, history...families and communities together"与空前 dragon boat races、zongzi、Qu Yuan 及空后 teamwork 精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：龙舟赛的具体形式与规则。空前提出"Dragon boat racing is the most exciting part of the festival"，空后详述"Teams of paddlers sit in long, narrow boats shaped like dragons...race to beat of drum...drummer at front sets rhythm...everyone must row in time...races short sprints or longer several kilometres...Winning requires strength and perfect coordination...out of sync, whole boat slows down...Watching popular...many cities hold competitions...thousands of spectators"。B选项"Teams of paddlers sit in long, narrow boats shaped like dragons and race to the beat of a drum"（队员们坐在狭长的龙形船中，随着鼓点竞渡）中"Teams...paddlers...long narrow boats...shaped like dragons...race...beat of drum"与空后内容完美对应，直接承接"most exciting part"，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：屈原传说与赛龙舟、吃粽子的由来。空前提出"The festival is also linked to the story of Qu Yuan"，空后详述"Qu Yuan loyal minister and poet...over 2,000 years ago...kingdom fell...threw himself into river...legend: people raced boats to save him, threw zongzi so fish would not eat body...Today eating zongzi...honour his memory...story teaches loyalty, patriotism, remembering those who sacrificed"。C选项"Qu Yuan was a loyal minister and poet; his story explains why people race boats and eat zongzi today"（屈原是一位忠臣和诗人；他的故事解释了人们如今为何赛龙舟、吃粽子）中"Qu Yuan...minister and poet...story explains...race boats...eat zongzi today"与空后叙述精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：家庭与社区的节前准备与习俗。空前提出"Families and communities prepare for the festival in many ways"，空后列举"People hang herbs wormwood, calamus on doors...drive away evil spirits, insects...parents tie coloured threads on children's wrists for good luck...southern China...villages form teams months in advance, train hard...festival time for family reunions...make zongzi, watch races, share meal"。D选项"People hang herbs on their doors, tie threads on children's wrists, and form teams to train for races"（人们在门上挂艾草、在孩子手腕上系彩线、组队训练参赛）中"hang herbs...tie threads...form teams...train for races"与空后习俗完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与端午节作为活传统的价值。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后为"Whether you are racing, watching, or enjoying zongzi with your family, the festival offers something for everyone. The next time you see a dragon boat cutting through the water, remember the history behind it and the community spirit it represents."。E选项"The Dragon Boat Festival is more than a holiday—it is a living tradition that connects sport, food, and cultural memory"（端午节不仅是假日——它是连接体育、美食与文化记忆的活传统）用"more than a holiday"升华主题，"living tradition...connects sport, food, cultural memory"与空后"racing, watching, zongzi...history...community spirit"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, many countries now hold dragon boat races as a summer sport.**：涉及海外国家举办龙舟赛，易被误填[1]或[2]。但F以"For example"引出国际推广，而空[1]讨论的是端午节的总体内涵（体育、美食、历史、家庭社区），F的"many countries"属于国际层面，与"China's...families and communities"的本土节日焦点不同；空[2]讨论龙舟赛本身的形式（船形、鼓点、节奏），F的"countries hold...as summer sport"属于传播现象，与"paddlers...boats...drum"的具体描写层次不符。F属"主题相关但论述层次错位"型干扰。
