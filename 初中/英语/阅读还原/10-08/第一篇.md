# 10-08 第一篇

**School Lunch and Nutrition**

Many students eat at least one meal a day at school. For some, especially those from lower-income families, school lunch may be the most balanced meal they get. What schools serve therefore matters a great deal for children's health and their ability to learn. [1] Studies have shown that students who eat a balanced midday meal score higher on tests and are less likely to miss school due to illness.

A healthy lunch gives students the energy to stay alert and concentrate in the afternoon. Research shows that children who eat nutritious meals perform better academically and have fewer behavioral problems. [2] On the other hand, a diet high in sugar and fat can cause energy crashes in the afternoon, making it harder for students to pay attention and complete their work.

Some schools have struggled to offer healthy food. Budget limits, lack of kitchen staff, and pressure to serve what kids will actually eat have sometimes led to menus high in fat, salt, and sugar. [3] Some schools have found success by partnering with local farms, using fresh ingredients, and involving students in taste tests to find healthy options they enjoy.

Schools can make a difference by including more fruits, vegetables, and whole grains, and by limiting sugary drinks and snacks. Teaching children about nutrition in the classroom can also help them make better choices. [4] Parents can reinforce these lessons at home by stocking healthy snacks and involving children in meal planning, so that good habits extend beyond the school day.

Good nutrition at school sets children up for a healthier future. It can reduce the risk of obesity, diabetes, and other diet-related diseases later in life. [5] For every dollar spent on improving school meals, society saves many times more in reduced healthcare costs and higher productivity in adulthood.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

  - **线索**：空前说学校供餐对儿童健康与学习重要，空后说"Studies have shown that students who eat a balanced midday meal score higher on tests"。A的"providing the nutrients that young bodies and minds need to grow, focus, and succeed"与空后成绩提升、出勤改善呼应。

  - **线索**：空前列举预算、人手、口味压力等困难，空后说"Some schools have found success by partnering with local farms..."。C的"Finding ways to serve healthy, appealing meals within limited budgets"与空后成功做法对应，"creativity... input from parents and nutrition experts"与"involving students in taste tests"呼应。

  - **线索**：空前说学校改进菜单、开展营养教育，空后说"Parents can reinforce these lessons at home... so that good habits extend beyond the school day"。D的"When schools, parents, and the community work together"与空后家长配合衔接。

  - **线索**：空前说学校营养降低肥胖等长期风险，空后说"For every dollar spent... society saves many times more in reduced healthcare costs"。E的"Investing in school lunch programs... pays off not only in better health"与空后投资回报呼应。
