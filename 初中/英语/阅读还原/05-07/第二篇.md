# 05-07 第二篇

**The Role of Libraries in Modern Life**

Libraries have been around for thousands of years, but they are more important than ever today. [1] In fact, libraries are not just places to borrow books—they offer free internet access, study rooms, and even classes for people of all ages. Many libraries also host reading clubs, film screenings（电影放映）, and workshops on topics like photography and coding. Whether you are a student preparing for exams or an elderly person learning to use a computer, libraries welcome everyone.

One of the biggest benefits of libraries is that they provide equal access to information. [2] Not everyone can afford to buy books or pay for expensive online resources. At a library, all materials are free to use. This means that a child from a poor family has the same chance to learn and dream as a child from a rich family. In other words, libraries help level the playing field and give every person the opportunity to grow.

Libraries also create a quiet space for people to work and think. [3] In today's busy world, it can be hard to find a peaceful place to concentrate. Libraries offer comfortable desks, good lighting, and a rule of silence that allows people to focus. Students often go to libraries to do homework, and many adults use library rooms for online meetings or personal projects. For this reason, libraries have become essential spaces for both learning and productivity.

Some people wonder if libraries are still needed when we have the internet. [4] The answer is yes, because libraries offer something the internet cannot: human help. Librarians can help you find the right book, teach you how to do research, and guide you through difficult questions. They also know their local community and can suggest resources that match your interests. That is why libraries remain valuable even in the digital age.

[5] In conclusion, libraries continue to play a vital role in our communities. They provide free access to knowledge, create spaces for learning, and connect people with the help they need. So the next time you pass a library, consider stepping inside—you might discover something wonderful.

## 选项

A. That is because they have evolved to serve many different needs.
B. In other words, they make knowledge available to everyone, rich or poor.
C. That is why libraries attract so many people who need a place to focus.
D. However, libraries offer services that go beyond what technology can provide.
E. In short, libraries are still one of the best resources we have.
F. For instance, some libraries now lend tools like hammers and sewing machines.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出图书馆的多元功能。空前描述"libraries have been around for thousands of years, but they are more important than ever today"，空后列举"libraries are not just places to borrow books—they offer free internet access, study rooms...reading clubs, film screenings, workshops...welcome everyone"。A选项"That is because they have evolved to serve many different needs"（那是因为它们已经发展成为满足多种不同需求的地方）中"That is because"解释空前"more important than ever"的原因，"evolved to serve many different needs"与空后多元服务（互联网、自习室、课程、读书俱乐部等）完美呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：图书馆促进信息公平。空前提出"they provide equal access to information"，空后说明"Not everyone can afford to buy books...At a library, all materials are free to use...a child from a poor family has the same chance...as a child from a rich family...libraries help level the playing field"。B选项"In other words, they make knowledge available to everyone, rich or poor"（换言之，它们使无论贫富的每个人都能获得知识）中"In other words"概括空前"equal access"，"everyone, rich or poor"与空后"poor family...rich family"精准对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：图书馆提供专注空间。空前提出"Libraries also create a quiet space for people to work and think"，空后描述"In today's busy world, it can be hard to find a peaceful place...Libraries offer comfortable desks, good lighting, rule of silence...Students...do homework...adults use library rooms for online meetings"。C选项"That is why libraries attract so many people who need a place to focus"（因此图书馆吸引了众多需要专注空间的人）中"That is why"承接空前"quiet space"，"need a place to focus"与空后"peaceful place to concentrate"及学生做作业、成年人开会的用途完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：回应"图书馆是否仍 needed"的质疑。空前设问"Some people wonder if libraries are still needed when we have the internet"，空后回答"The answer is yes, because libraries offer something the internet cannot: human help...Librarians can help...teach...guide...suggest resources...libraries remain valuable even in the digital age"。D选项"However, libraries offer services that go beyond what technology can provide"（然而，图书馆提供的服务超越了技术所能提供的）中"However"转折回应质疑，"go beyond what technology can provide"与空后"something the internet cannot: human help"精准对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"libraries continue to play a vital role...provide free access to knowledge, create spaces for learning, connect people with help...next time you pass a library, consider stepping inside"。E选项"In short, libraries are still one of the best resources we have"（简言之，图书馆仍是我们拥有的最佳资源之一）用"In short"总结全文，"best resources"与空后"vital role"及"discover something wonderful"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For instance, some libraries now lend tools like hammers and sewing machines.**：涉及图书馆出借工具的新服务，易被误填[1]。但F以"For instance"引出具体例子（出借锤子、缝纫机），而空[1]前后文列举的是免费网络、自习室、课程、读书俱乐部、电影放映、工作坊等常规服务，并未涉及工具出借；F属于图书馆"tool library"这一细分类型，与空后列举的服务层级和主题不完全匹配。F属"主题相关但举例维度错位"型干扰。
