# 05-07 第一篇

**Bees and Pollination: Small Insects, Big Role**

Bees are among the most important creatures on Earth. [1] Without bees, many of the fruits and vegetables we eat every day would not exist. When bees move from flower to flower collecting nectar（花蜜）, they accidentally carry pollen（花粉） from one plant to another. This process, called pollination（授粉）, helps plants produce fruits and seeds. In fact, about one-third of the world's food crops depend on bee pollination.

Why are bees so good at this job? [2] Bees have special body parts that make them perfect for spreading pollen. Their fuzzy bodies catch pollen easily as they land on flowers. Bees also visit many flowers in one trip, which means they can pollinate a large number of plants in a short time. That is why farmers and gardeners welcome bees to their fields and gardens—these small insects do work that would be very difficult for humans to do by hand.

Sadly, bee populations have been declining in recent years. [3] This is mainly due to pesticides（杀虫剂）, loss of wildflowers, and climate change. When fewer bees are around, farmers get smaller harvests and food prices may rise. Scientists are working to find ways to protect bees, such as planting more wildflowers along roadsides and reducing the use of harmful chemicals in farming.

What can we do to help? [4] Even small actions can make a difference. You can plant flowers that attract bees in your garden or on your balcony. Avoid using pesticides in your backyard. If you see a tired bee resting on the ground, you can offer it a little sugar water to help it recover. Every little bit helps when it comes to saving these important insects.

[5] In short, bees may be small, but they play a huge part in our food supply and the health of our planet. By understanding their role and taking steps to protect them, we can ensure that future generations will continue to enjoy the fruits and vegetables that bees help to create.

## 选项

A. In other words, bees are essential for the food we eat.
B. That is because their bodies and behavior are designed for this task.
C. In fact, several factors have led to this worrying situation.
D. For example, you do not need a large garden to make a difference.
E. In conclusion, we should all appreciate and protect these tiny workers.
F. However, some people are afraid of bees and try to avoid them.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出蜜蜂的重要性。空前描述"Bees are among the most important creatures on Earth"，空后具体说明"Without bees, many of the fruits and vegetables we eat every day would not exist...about one-third of the world's food crops depend on bee pollination"。A选项"In other words, bees are essential for the food we eat"（换言之，蜜蜂对我们吃的食物至关重要）中"In other words"对上文"most important"进行概括，"essential for the food we eat"与空后"fruits and vegetables...would not exist"和"food crops depend on"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：解释蜜蜂擅长授粉的原因。空前设问"Why are bees so good at this job?"，空后列举"Bees have special body parts...Their fuzzy bodies catch pollen easily...visit many flowers in one trip...can pollinate a large number of plants"。B选项"That is because their bodies and behavior are designed for this task"（那是因为它们的身体和行为是为这项任务而设计的）中"That is because"直接回答空前问题，"bodies and behavior"与空后"special body parts"和"visit many flowers"完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：说明蜜蜂数量下降的原因。空前指出"bee populations have been declining in recent years"，空后详述"This is mainly due to pesticides, loss of wildflowers, and climate change"。C选项"In fact, several factors have led to this worrying situation"（事实上，多种因素导致了这一令人担忧的局面）中"In fact"加强衔接，"several factors"总领空后的pesticides、loss of wildflowers、climate change三大原因，"worrying situation"与空前"declining"呼应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：引出普通人可采取的小行动。空前设问"What can we do to help?"，空后列举"You can plant flowers...Avoid using pesticides...offer it a little sugar water...Every little bit helps"。D选项"For example, you do not need a large garden to make a difference"（例如，你不需要大花园也能有所作为）中"For example"引出空后具体做法，"do not need a large garden"与空后"garden or balcony"及"Every little bit helps"形成呼应，鼓励读者从小事做起，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"bees may be small, but they play a huge part...By understanding their role and taking steps to protect them, we can ensure that future generations...continue to enjoy the fruits and vegetables"。E选项"In conclusion, we should all appreciate and protect these tiny workers"（总之，我们都应该欣赏和保护这些微小的工作者）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"appreciate and protect"与空后"understanding their role and taking steps to protect them"精准呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. However, some people are afraid of bees and try to avoid them.**：涉及人们对蜜蜂的恐惧心理，易被误填[3]或[4]。但F讨论的是人们惧怕蜜蜂、刻意躲避的态度，而空[3]前后文讨论的是蜜蜂数量下降的原因（农药、野花减少、气候变化），空[4]讨论的是普通人如何帮助蜜蜂，两者均不涉及人们对蜜蜂的恐惧；F属于"主题相关但逻辑偏离"型干扰。
