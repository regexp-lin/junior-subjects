# 09-17 第一篇

**Technology and Education: A New Partnership**

Classrooms today look very different from those of twenty years ago. Computers, tablets, and the internet have changed how students learn and how teachers teach. [1] Technology has opened up new possibilities for education, making learning more interactive, accessible, and connected to the real world.

One benefit is access to information. Students can now look up facts, watch educational videos, and explore topics that interest them with just a few clicks. This was impossible for earlier generations. [2] The internet has turned every classroom into a gateway to knowledge from around the globe.

Technology also supports different learning styles. Some students learn best by reading, others by watching or listening. Digital tools can offer videos, audio, and interactive exercises for all kinds of learners. [3] Flexible learning materials help more students find the methods that work best for them.

Teachers can use technology to save time and improve their lessons. Grading software, online quizzes, and digital lesson plans reduce routine work. [4] When teachers spend less time on administrative tasks, they can focus more on understanding and supporting each student.

Still, technology alone is not enough. Good teachers, supportive families, and a love of learning remain as important as ever. [5] The best results come when we use technology as a tool to enhance education, not as a replacement for the human connection at the heart of teaching.

## 选项

A. Combining traditional teaching with modern technology creates a richer and more effective learning experience.
B. Never before has it been so easy for students to find and use information from countless sources.
C. Personalized approaches to learning can improve outcomes for a wider range of students.
D. More efficient tools free up teachers to spend quality time with their students.
E. Technology should serve education rather than take over the roles that only people can fill.
F. For example, many schools now use smart boards that allow teachers to display and interact with digital content in real time.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述教室里电脑、平板和网络改变了教与学，空后说技术为教育开辟了新可能。A的"Combining traditional teaching with modern technology"承接新旧对比，"richer and more effective learning experience"与"interactive, accessible, and connected"呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说学生可以查阅资料、看视频、探索兴趣，空后强调互联网使每间教室成为知识之门。B的"Never before has it been so easy"与"with just a few clicks"呼应，"information from countless sources"与"knowledge from around the globe"一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举不同学习风格（阅读、观看、听力）及数字工具提供多种形式，空后强调灵活材料帮助更多学生找到适合自己的方法。C的"Personalized approaches"与"different learning styles"呼应，"wider range of students"与"more students"一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说评分软件、在线测验等减少常规工作，空后说教师能把更多精力放在理解和支持学生上。D的"More efficient tools free up teachers"与"reduce routine work"呼应，"spend quality time with their students"与"focus more on understanding and supporting"一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前强调好老师、家庭支持和学习热情依然重要，空后总结最佳效果来自把技术当作增强教育的工具。E的"Technology should serve education"与"technology as a tool"呼应，"not as a replacement for the human connection"承接"human connection at the heart of teaching"。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, many schools now use smart boards that allow teachers to display and interact with digital content in real time.**：以For example开头，是具体设备案例；易误放[1]或[4]，但[1]需概括技术对教育的整体作用，[4]需效率与教师精力转向的概括，非单一设备举例。
