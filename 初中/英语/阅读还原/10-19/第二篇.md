# 10-19 第二篇

**The Ocean Cleanup Movement**

The world's oceans are full of plastic. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste enter the sea. This pollution harms marine life, damages coral reefs, and can even enter the food chain, affecting human health. The problem seems huge, but people around the world are taking action. [1] The ocean cleanup movement is a global effort to remove plastic from the sea and prevent more from entering—and it is growing stronger every year.

One way to clean the ocean is to collect plastic directly from the water. Special ships and nets can catch floating plastic. Some projects use barriers in rivers to stop plastic before it reaches the ocean. Technology is improving: drones and satellites help find plastic hotspots. [2] These methods show that we can remove plastic from the ocean, but the real solution also requires stopping plastic at its source—on land.

Reducing plastic use is equally important. Every piece of plastic we avoid using is one less piece that could end up in the ocean. People can choose reusable bags, bottles, and containers. Companies can design products with less packaging. Governments can ban single-use plastics or charge fees for them. [3] When individuals, businesses, and governments work together to cut plastic waste, the amount reaching the ocean drops sharply.

Education plays a key role. When people understand where plastic goes and how it harms marine life, they are more likely to change their habits. Schools teach students about the importance of recycling and reducing waste. Community events like beach cleanups bring people together and raise awareness. [4] The more people know about the problem, the more they care—and the more they are willing to do something about it.

The ocean cleanup movement has already achieved a lot. Beaches are cleaner in many places. Some rivers and coastlines have seen less plastic. New laws and policies are being passed. [5] There is still a long way to go, but with continued effort from everyone—scientists, governments, businesses, and ordinary people—the oceans can become healthier again for future generations.

## 选项

A. From local beach cleanups to large-scale ocean projects, people are joining forces to protect the seas and the life they support.
B. Cleaning up what is already in the ocean is important, but preventing new plastic from entering is the only way to solve the problem in the long run.
C. Solving the plastic crisis depends on everyone—from the choices we make at home to the policies governments put in place.
D. Awareness leads to action, and action leads to change—that is why teaching people about ocean pollution matters so much.
E. The fight for cleaner oceans is far from over, but every step forward brings hope for a healthier planet.
F. For example, the Great Pacific Garbage Patch is a huge area of floating plastic in the Pacific Ocean, estimated to contain millions of pieces of debris.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍海洋塑料污染危害及人们开始行动，空后说"The ocean cleanup movement is a global effort to remove plastic from the sea and prevent more from entering—and it is growing stronger every year"。A的"From local beach cleanups to large-scale ocean projects, people are joining forces to protect the seas"与空后global effort、growing stronger呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说船只捕捞、河流屏障、无人机卫星等技术手段，空后说"These methods show that we can remove plastic from the ocean, but the real solution also requires stopping plastic at its source—on land"。B的"Cleaning up what is already in the ocean is important, but preventing new plastic from entering is the only way to solve the problem in the long run"与空后remove vs. stop at source完美衔接。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说个人、企业、政府减少塑料使用的做法，空后说"When individuals, businesses, and governments work together to cut plastic waste, the amount reaching the ocean drops sharply"。C的"Solving the plastic crisis depends on everyone—from the choices we make at home to the policies governments put in place"与空后多方合作呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说教育的重要性、学校教学、社区海滩清洁等活动，空后说"The more people know about the problem, the more they care—and the more they are willing to do something about it"。D的"Awareness leads to action, and action leads to change—that is why teaching people about ocean pollution matters so much"与空后know→care→act逻辑呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说已取得进展（海滩更干净、塑料减少、新法规），空后说"There is still a long way to go, but with continued effort from everyone... the oceans can become healthier again"。E的"The fight for cleaner oceans is far from over, but every step forward brings hope for a healthier planet"与空后"long way to go"与"hope"呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Great Pacific Garbage Patch is a huge area of floating plastic...**：以For example开头，是具体地点举例。各空均需概括性或过渡性句子，F为举例型干扰项。
