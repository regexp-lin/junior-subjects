# 05-18 第一篇

**Artificial Intelligence in Our Daily Life**

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is no longer something we only see in science fiction movies. It has become a part of our everyday life in ways we might not even notice. [1] In fact, AI helps us in many areas—from the voice assistant that answers our questions to the apps that suggest songs we might like. When we search on the Internet, use a map to find the best route, or get movie recommendations, AI is often working behind the scenes. That is why understanding how AI works can help us use it wisely and stay safe online.

How does AI learn? [2] AI systems are trained using large amounts of data. For example, a translation app learns from millions of sentences in different languages. A face-recognition system learns by studying countless pictures of faces. The more data the system gets, the better it becomes at its task. In other words, AI improves through practice, just like we do when we learn a new skill. However, the data must be good and fair; otherwise, AI can make mistakes or even show bias（偏见）.

AI can make life easier and faster. [3] Smart home devices can turn on lights, adjust the temperature, or play music when we ask. Chatbots（聊天机器人）can answer customer questions at any time of the day. In hospitals, AI helps doctors read X-rays and find signs of disease. In schools, some programs help teachers grade homework or personalise lessons for each student. For this reason, AI is changing how we work, learn, and live—often for the better.

But we should also be careful. [4] AI collects and uses a lot of our personal data. We need to think about who can see our information and how it is used. Some people worry that AI might replace their jobs. Others wonder if we rely on AI too much and forget how to think for ourselves. So it is important to use AI as a tool, not as a replacement for our own judgment and efforts. We should stay informed and make wise choices.

[5] In short, AI is here to stay and will play an even bigger role in our future. It can bring great benefits if we use it correctly. By learning about AI, asking questions, and using it responsibly, we can enjoy its advantages while avoiding its risks. Remember: technology serves us; we should not serve technology. The future belongs to those who know how to work with AI wisely.

## 选项

A. That is because AI is already helping us in many invisible ways.
B. In other words, AI learns from data and gets smarter over time.

F. For example, some robots can now cook simple meals at home.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：总领AI在日常生活中的渗透。空前描述"Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is no longer something we only see in science fiction movies. It has become a part of our everyday life in ways we might not even notice"，空后说明"In fact, AI helps us in many areas—from the voice assistant...to the apps that suggest songs...When we search on the Internet, use a map to find the best route, or get movie recommendations, AI is often working behind the scenes...understanding how AI works can help us use it wisely and stay safe online"。A选项"That is because AI is already helping us in many invisible ways"（那是因为AI已经以许多看不见的方式帮助我们）中"That is because"解释AI的日常存在，"invisible ways"与空后"behind the scenes"及多种应用场景精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：AI如何通过数据学习。空前设问"How does AI learn?"，空后详述"AI systems are trained using large amounts of data...a translation app learns from millions of sentences...A face-recognition system learns by studying countless pictures of faces...The more data the system gets, the better it becomes at its task...AI improves through practice, just like we do...However, the data must be good and fair; otherwise, AI can make mistakes or even show bias"。B选项"In other words, AI learns from data and gets smarter over time"（换言之，AI从数据中学习并随时间变得更聪明）中"In other words"概括空后内容，"learns from data...gets smarter over time"与空后trained using data、more data—better、improves through practice完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：AI对工作、学习与生活的变革。空前提出"AI can make life easier and faster"，空后列举"Smart home devices can turn on lights...Chatbots can answer customer questions...In hospitals, AI helps doctors read X-rays...In schools, some programs help teachers grade homework or personalise lessons...AI is changing how we work, learn, and live—often for the better"。C选项"That is why AI is transforming work, education, and daily routines"（因此AI正在改变工作、教育和日常生活）中"That is why"承接空前及空后结论，"transforming work, education, and daily routines"与空后智慧家居、医院、学校及"changing how we work, learn, and live"精准对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：使用AI时的隐私与理性考量。空前提出"But we should also be careful"，空后详述"AI collects and uses a lot of our personal data...We need to think about who can see our information and how it is used...Some people worry that AI might replace their jobs...Others wonder if we rely on AI too much...it is important to use AI as a tool, not as a replacement for our own judgment and efforts...We should stay informed and make wise choices"。D选项"It is true that we must use AI thoughtfully and protect our privacy"（的确，我们必须审慎使用AI并保护隐私）中"It is true that"加强衔接，"thoughtfully and protect our privacy"与空后data、information、judgment、wise choices完美呼应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与明智运用AI的总结。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"AI is here to stay and will play an even bigger role in our future...It can bring great benefits if we use it correctly...By learning about AI, asking questions, and using it responsibly, we can enjoy its advantages while avoiding its risks...technology serves us; we should not serve technology...The future belongs to those who know how to work with AI wisely"。E选项"In conclusion, we should embrace AI wisely and use it for good"（总之，我们应明智地接纳AI并善用其利）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"embrace AI wisely and use it for good"与空后correctly、responsibly、advantages、work with AI wisely形成呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some robots can now cook simple meals at home.**：涉及家用烹饪机器人，易被误填[1]或[3]。但F讨论的是单一应用（烹饪机器人），而空[1]强调多种看不见的应用，空[3]列举智慧家居、客服、医疗、教育等多元领域；F属于具体举例，与空[1]的概括性、空[3]的多领域变革论述层级不同。F属"主题相关但论述广度错位"型干扰。
