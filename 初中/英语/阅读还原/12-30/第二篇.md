**Water Sports: Fun and Fitness on the Water**

Water sports include activities done on or in water—swimming, surfing, sailing, kayaking, and more. They offer a great way to exercise, enjoy nature, and beat the heat. Many people learn to swim as children, but water sports can be taken up at any age. From calm pools to wild waves, there is something for everyone. [1]

Swimming is one of the best all-round exercises. It works nearly every muscle without putting stress on joints. People with injuries or joint problems often use swimming for rehabilitation. Competitive swimming is an Olympic sport with events from 50 metres to marathon distances. Learning to swim is also a vital life skill that can save lives. [2]

Surfing, kayaking, and sailing bring you closer to nature. Surfers ride waves and feel the power of the ocean. Kayakers explore rivers, lakes, and coastlines at their own pace. Sailors use wind to move across the water. These sports require skill and respect for the water. Understanding weather, currents, and safety rules is essential before heading out. [3]

Water sports build character. They teach patience—waiting for the right wave or wind. They require concentration and quick decisions when conditions change. Team sports like rowing or water polo develop communication and trust. Many young people join clubs or camps and make friends for life through shared experiences on the water. [4]

Whether you prefer the pool, the sea, or a lake, water sports offer endless possibilities. They improve physical health, reduce stress, and connect us to the natural world. With proper training and respect for safety, anyone can discover the joy of being on or in the water. [5]

## 选项

A. In short, water sports combine fitness, nature, and fun for people of all ages and abilities.
B. For these reasons, swimming is valued both as a sport and as a practical skill for life.
C. As a result, these activities demand not only physical skill but also knowledge of the environment.
D. That is why water sports are often praised for building discipline, teamwork, and lasting friendships.
E. In other words, water sports open a world of benefits for body, mind, and spirit when done safely.
F. For example, the butterfly stroke is one of the four main swimming styles used in competitive races.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍水上运动范围——游泳冲浪帆船皮划艇等、可锻炼享受自然避暑、任何年龄可参与、从平静泳池到汹涌波浪都可选择，空后需总结水上运动对各类人群的吸引力。A的"combine fitness, nature, and fun for people of all ages and abilities"与前文对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说游泳是全身运动、不伤关节、适合康复、竞泳为奥运项目、学会游泳是救命技能，空后需总结游泳的双重价值。B的"valued both as a sport and as a practical skill for life"与前文呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说冲浪皮划艇帆船让人亲近自然、各具特色、需技术与对水的敬畏、出发前需了解天气洋流与安全规则，空后需总结这些运动对体能与环境知识的要求。C的"demand not only physical skill but also knowledge of the environment"与前文对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说水上运动塑造品格——教会耐心、需要专注与应变、团队项目如赛艇水球培养沟通与信任、年轻人通过俱乐部或营地结交朋友，空后需总结对纪律团队与友谊的培养。D的"building discipline, teamwork, and lasting friendships"与前文呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说无论泳池大海还是湖泊都有选择、提升健康减轻压力联结自然、在正确训练与安全意识下可享受水上乐趣，空后需总结水上运动在安全前提下的身心益处。E的"open a world of benefits for body, mind, and spirit when done safely"与前文对应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the butterfly stroke is one of the four main swimming styles...**：以For example开头，为具体泳姿案例（蝶泳）。各空均为概括或过渡句；[2]易被误选因涉及游泳技术，但该空需总结游泳作为运动与生存技能的双重价值，而非某一泳姿的说明。
