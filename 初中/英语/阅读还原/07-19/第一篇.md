# 07-19 第一篇

**The Amazing Adaptations of Plants**

Plants cannot move from place to place like animals, yet they have found remarkable ways to survive and thrive in almost every environment on Earth. From deserts to rainforests, plants have developed special features that allow them to face extreme conditions. [1] These adaptations often take years or even centuries to develop, but they show how nature finds creative solutions to difficult problems. Some plants store water in their stems or leaves to survive long dry seasons, while others have roots that reach deep into the ground to find moisture.

[2] In hot and dry deserts, plants face the challenge of conserving water while still getting enough sunlight. Cacti have thick stems that store water and sharp spines instead of leaves to reduce water loss. Their roots spread widely near the surface to quickly absorb any rainwater. Many desert plants also open their pores only at night when the air is cooler, which helps them save precious water during the day.

[3] Rainforest plants face a different set of challenges: heavy rain, limited light under the canopy, and fierce competition for space. Some plants grow on the branches of tall trees instead of in the soil, absorbing nutrients from the air and rainwater. Others have large leaves to catch the little sunlight that filters through the canopy. The variety of shapes and strategies in the rainforest shows how plants adapt to their specific conditions.

[4] Plants in cold regions must deal with freezing temperatures and short growing seasons. Some produce a kind of natural antifreeze in their cells to survive the winter. Others drop their leaves in autumn and remain dormant until spring, preserving their energy until conditions improve. These survival strategies allow plants to live in places where few other organisms can.

[5] The ability of plants to adapt to their surroundings reminds us of the resilience of life on Earth. By studying these adaptations, scientists can learn not only about the natural world but also about how we might grow crops in changing climates. Understanding plants helps us appreciate the diversity of life and the importance of protecting different ecosystems around the world.

## 选项

A. Over time, different species have evolved unique solutions that suit their particular environments.
B. Desert plants have developed a range of features to survive with very little water.
C. In the rainforest, plants use creative strategies to compete for light and nutrients.
D. Cold-weather plants rely on special mechanisms to withstand harsh winter conditions.
E. This knowledge can inspire both scientific research and our efforts to protect the environment.
F. For example, the Venus flytrap catches insects to get nutrients from poor soil.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前概括植物在各地适应环境，空后列举储水、深根等具体策略。A 句 "Over time... evolved unique solutions that suit their particular environments" 承接前文 "remarkable ways to survive"，并自然过渡到后文对具体适应方式的举例，承上启下。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：段落主题是沙漠植物的抗旱策略。空后具体描述仙人掌储水、刺替代叶片、浅层根系等。B 句 "Desert plants have developed a range of features to survive with very little water" 准确概括本段主旨。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：段落讨论雨林植物在遮荫、竞争下的适应。空后提到附生、大叶片等策略。C 句 "In the rainforest, plants use creative strategies to compete for light and nutrients" 与段落内容完全对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：段落讲寒冷地区植物的生存机制。空后描述抗冻物质、落叶休眠等。D 句 "Cold-weather plants rely on special mechanisms to withstand harsh winter conditions" 精准总结本段。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：结尾段总结植物适应力的意义，并联系科研与环保。E 句 "This knowledge can inspire both scientific research and our efforts to protect the environment" 与前文 "studying these adaptations" 及 "protecting different ecosystems" 自然衔接。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：食虫植物虽是植物适应的一例，但属于具体举例，应嵌入某段内部。其 "poor soil" 与第 3 段雨林营养获取有部分关联，但层级为细节例证，不宜作为段落概括句填空，易因 "nutrients" 与多段相关而误选。
