# 04-21 第二篇

**Why Doing Chores at Home Matters for Kids**

Many parents wonder whether they should ask their children to help with household chores. Some worry that chores will take time away from homework or play. [1] Yet research suggests that children who do regular chores at home often do better in school and develop important life skills. Doing dishes, making the bed, or taking out the trash may seem small, but these tasks help prepare kids for the future. In fact, chores are a form of education that happens right at home.

Chores teach responsibility. When a child has a regular task, such as feeding the pet or setting the table, they learn that others depend on them. [2] If they forget or do a poor job, the family may notice and the child learns from the result. Over time, this helps build a sense of duty and reliability. Children who grow up with chores are more likely to become adults who keep their promises and finish what they start.

Chores also help children learn practical skills. Cooking a simple meal, folding laundry（洗衣物）, or organizing a room are all useful abilities that schools rarely teach. [3] These skills not only make daily life easier when children grow up, but also build confidence. A teenager who knows how to cook can prepare a meal when parents are busy. Such independence is valuable in both school life and future careers.

Some parents are unsure how to start. They may think their children are too young or too busy. [4] The key is to choose age-appropriate（适合年龄的）tasks and start small. A five-year-old can put toys away; a ten-year-old can help with the dishes. Even a few minutes each day can make a difference. As children get older, they can take on more difficult chores. The goal is not perfection, but habit-building.

[5] From building responsibility to gaining life skills, chores are more than just helping around the house. They are a way for parents to raise independent, capable young people who are ready for whatever life brings.

## 选项

A. In short, chores are an important part of raising children.
B. Studies show that children who help at home feel more connected to their family.
C. There is no need to worry about that.
D. Their actions have real consequences.
E. Fortunately, there are simple steps any parent can take.
F. In other words, what children learn from chores goes far beyond the task itself.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] C   [2] D   [3] F   [4] E   [5] A

## 深度解析

- **[1] C**：
  - **线索**：回应家长担忧并转折引出研究结论。空前描述"Some worry that chores will take time away from homework or play"，表达家长顾虑。C选项"There is no need to worry about that"（不必为此担心）直接回应空前担忧，自然过渡到空后"Yet research suggests that children who do regular chores...often do better in school"，形成"担忧—不必担心—研究证明有益"的逻辑链，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] D**：
  - **线索**：责任感的培养与行为后果。空前描述"regular task...others depend on them...If they forget or do a poor job, the family may notice"，空后说明"the child learns from the result...build a sense of duty and reliability"。D选项"Their actions have real consequences"（他们的行为会产生真实的后果）中"Their"指孩子，"real consequences"与空前"forget...poor job...family may notice"及空后"learns from the result"完美对应，强调行为与后果的因果关系，衔接自然。

- **[3] F**：
  - **线索**：家务技能的多重价值总结。空前列举"Cooking...folding laundry...organizing...useful abilities...schools rarely teach"，空后详述"not only make daily life easier...but also build confidence...independence...valuable in school life and future careers"。F选项"In other words, what children learn from chores goes far beyond the task itself"（换言之，孩子从家务中学到的远远超出任务本身）中"In other words"承接空前技能列举，"goes far beyond the task itself"与空后"confidence...independence...valuable"形成呼应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] E**：
  - **线索**：家长如何开始的过渡。空前描述"Some parents are unsure how to start...too young or too busy"，空后详述"The key is to choose age-appropriate tasks...start small...A five-year-old can...ten-year-old can...The goal is...habit-building"。E选项"Fortunately, there are simple steps any parent can take"（幸运的是，任何家长都可以采取简单的步骤）中"Fortunately"承接家长顾虑，"simple steps"自然引出空后"The key is to choose age-appropriate tasks and start small"，衔接顺畅。

- **[5] A**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后列举"From building responsibility to gaining life skills"并总结"more than just helping...raise independent, capable young people...ready for whatever life brings"。A选项"In short, chores are an important part of raising children"（简言之，家务是养育孩子的重要一环）用"In short"总结全文，"important part of raising children"与空后"raise independent, capable young people"完美呼应，收束得当。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **B. Studies show that children who help at home feel more connected to their family.**：强调家务与家庭归属感，易被误填[1]或[3]。但空[1]空前为"take time away from homework or play"的担忧，需直接回应"不必担心"并引出研究，C更贴切；B的"feel more connected"与空[1]后"do better in school...life skills"焦点不同。空[3]讨论的是"practical skills...confidence...independence"，B的"connected to family"属情感维度，与空后技能、自信、独立性逻辑链不匹配。属"主题相关但逻辑焦点错位"型干扰。
