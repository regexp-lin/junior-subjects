# 03-22 第一篇

**The Surprising World of Ants**

If you look closely at the ground on a warm day, you will probably see a line of tiny ants marching in perfect order. [1] In fact, these small insects have built some of the most organized and advanced societies on the entire planet.

An ant colony (蚁群) can contain millions of individual ants, and every single one has a specific job. The queen's only task is to lay eggs. Worker ants search for food and build tunnels. [2] Without this clear division of labor, the whole colony would quickly fall apart.

One of the most amazing things about ants is how they communicate. They use special chemicals called pheromones (信息素) to send messages to each other. [3] Other ants follow this invisible trail and quickly find the food source. This is why you often see ants walking in a neat, straight line.

Ants are also incredibly strong for their tiny size. A single ant can carry an object that is fifty times heavier than its own body. [4] If a human had the same relative strength, they could easily lift a car over their head with one hand!

[5] They teach us that even the smallest creatures can accomplish great things when they cooperate and work together.

## 选项

A. For example, when a worker ant finds food, it leaves a chemical trail on the ground.
B. Soldier ants, on the other hand, protect the colony from dangerous enemies.
C. In short, ants are truly one of nature's most impressive and hardworking creatures.
D. This incredible physical power makes them one of the strongest animals in the world.
E. However, scientists have recently proven that ants are not actually very intelligent.
F. Most people think of them as simple, unimportant bugs that ruin summer picnics.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] F   [2] B   [3] A   [4] D   [5] C

## 深度解析

- **[1] F**：
  - **线索**：对比转折与刻板印象打破。空前描述了看到蚂蚁排成整齐队列行进的场景。F选项"Most people think of them as simple, unimportant bugs that ruin summer picnics"（大多数人认为它们是破坏夏季野餐的简单、不重要的虫子）提出了人们对蚂蚁的刻板偏见。这完美引出了空后的转折"In fact, these small insects have built some of the most organized and advanced societies"（事实上，这些小昆虫建立了地球上最有组织、最先进的社会之一），形成"偏见→事实"的经典对比结构。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：并列补充与结构完整。空前列举了蚁群中不同角色的分工——蚁后产卵、工蚁觅食筑巢。B选项"Soldier ants, on the other hand, protect the colony from dangerous enemies"（另一方面，兵蚁保护蚁群免受危险敌人的侵害）用"on the other hand"补充了第三类角色——兵蚁。空后"Without this clear division of labor, the whole colony would quickly fall apart"（没有这种清晰的分工，整个蚁群会很快崩溃）中的"this clear division of labor"正是对前文三种角色分工的总结。

- **[3] A**：
  - **线索**：举例说明与代词衔接。空前介绍了蚂蚁通过信息素进行交流，"send messages to each other"（互相发送信息）。A选项"For example, when a worker ant finds food, it leaves a chemical trail on the ground"（例如，当一只工蚁找到食物时，它在地面上留下化学痕迹）以"For example"引出了信息素沟通的一个具体场景。空后"Other ants follow this invisible trail"（其他蚂蚁跟随这条看不见的小路）中的"this invisible trail"直接指代A选项中的"chemical trail"。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：总结评价与类比引出。空前给出了一个惊人数据：一只蚂蚁能搬运自身体重五十倍的物体。D选项"This incredible physical power makes them one of the strongest animals in the world"（这种不可思议的体力使它们成为世界上最强壮的动物之一）中的"This incredible physical power"指代了前文的搬运能力数据。空后"If a human had the same relative strength, they could easily lift a car"（如果人类有同样的相对力量，他们能轻松举起一辆车）则是通过类比进一步强调D选项所说的"最强壮"之程度。

- **[5] C**：
  - **线索**：全文总结与价值升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首。前文分别展示了蚂蚁的社会组织（第二段）、沟通方式（第三段）和惊人力量（第四段）。C选项"In short, ants are truly one of nature's most impressive and hardworking creatures"（简而言之，蚂蚁确实是大自然中最令人印象深刻和最勤劳的生物之一）用"In short"总结全篇赞美。空后"They teach us that even the smallest creatures can accomplish great things when they cooperate"是由C选项引发的更深层启示。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **E. However, scientists have recently proven that ants are not actually very intelligent.**：该选项含有转折词"However"和"scientists""ants"等文章高频词。但其意思是"科学家最近证明蚂蚁实际上并不是很聪明"，这与全文始终颂扬蚂蚁惊人的组织能力、沟通智慧和力量的基调完全矛盾。属于"转折词误导+主旨背离"的双重干扰。
