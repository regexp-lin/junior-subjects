**The Importance of Sleep for Teenagers**

Sleep is as important as food and water for our bodies. During sleep, our brains process what we have learned, our muscles repair themselves, and our bodies grow. For teenagers, who are going through rapid physical and mental changes, getting enough sleep is especially critical. [1] Yet many teenagers around the world are not getting the sleep they need, and this has real consequences for their health and performance.

Experts recommend that teenagers get eight to ten hours of sleep each night. However, homework, social media, and early school start times often cut into sleep. [2] When teenagers stay up late and wake up early for school, they build up a "sleep debt" that is hard to repay, even on weekends.

Lack of sleep affects teenagers in many ways. It makes it harder to concentrate in class, remember information, and solve problems. [3] Over time, chronic sleep loss can also weaken the immune system, increase the risk of anxiety and depression, and lead to weight gain.

What can teenagers do to sleep better? Setting a regular bedtime, avoiding screens before sleep, and keeping the bedroom dark and cool all help. [4] Small changes in daily habits can lead to big improvements in sleep quality and overall wellbeing.

Schools and parents also play a role. Some schools have moved to later start times, which has helped students get more rest. [5] When families, schools, and communities work together to prioritize sleep, teenagers are healthier, happier, and better prepared to succeed in school and in life.

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说青少年睡眠尤其重要，空后说"many teenagers... are not getting the sleep they need"。A的"Despite knowing the importance of sleep, many teens sacrifice it"承接重要性与现实的矛盾，"without realizing the harm"引出后文的后果讨论。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举影响睡眠的因素（作业、社交媒体、早起上学），空后说积累"睡眠债"。B的"These factors combine to create a cycle of tiredness"概括前文多种因素的叠加效应，"affects almost every part of a teenager's day"引出后续影响。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说缺乏睡眠影响注意力、记忆和解题能力，空后说长期睡眠不足还会削弱免疫系统等。C的"effects go beyond just feeling sleepy"承上，"touch learning, emotions, and long-term health"概括更广泛的影响。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前列举改善睡眠的建议（固定作息、远离屏幕、保持黑暗凉爽），空后说小改变带来大改善。D的"Building a calming bedtime routine"概括前文建议，"signals the body that it is time to wind down"解释原理。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说学校和家长的作用及推迟上学时间的成效，空后总结各方合作的益处。E的"A well-rested student is more focused, more creative, and more resilient"概括充足睡眠的好处，升华主题。
