# 07-16 第二篇

**The Pros and Cons of Social Media**

Social media has changed how we communicate, share news, and connect with others. Platforms like WeChat, TikTok, and Instagram allow us to stay in touch with friends and family across the world. We can discover new ideas, learn new skills, and find communities that share our interests. For many people, social media has become a part of daily life. [1]

[2] Social media makes it easy to share moments with others. Photos, videos, and updates help us feel connected even when we are far apart. During the pandemic, video calls and group chats helped people stay connected when meeting in person was not possible. For people who move to new cities or countries, these tools can reduce feelings of loneliness.

[3] On the other hand, spending too much time on social media has been linked to anxiety and depression, especially among young people. Constant comparison with others' highlight reels can damage self-esteem. Cyberbullying is another serious problem. Some users also find it hard to focus on real-life tasks when notifications keep pulling their attention away.

[4] To use social media in a healthy way, experts suggest setting time limits. Turning off notifications during study or work hours can help. It is also important to remember that what we see online is often curated—people usually share their best moments, not their everyday struggles. Following accounts that inspire rather than discourage us can make a difference.

[5] Social media is a tool, and like any tool, its impact depends on how we use it. With awareness and self-control, we can enjoy its benefits while avoiding its pitfalls. The choice is ours to make.

## 选项

A. Yet as useful as it can be, we must also consider its downsides.
B. The benefits of social media are easy to see.
C. Unfortunately, the negatives are equally real.
D. There are ways to manage our relationship with social media.
E. In the end, balance is the key to a healthy digital life.
F. For example, many influencers post several times a day to keep their audience engaged.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：第一段介绍社交媒体改变沟通方式、连接全球、发现新知、融入日常生活。空格前描述其便利与普及。选项A "Yet as useful as it can be, we must also consider its downsides" 用"Yet"转折，从前文益处过渡到后文需审视的弊端，承上启下。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段具体讲益处：分享瞬间、保持联系、疫情期间作用、缓解孤独。选项B "The benefits of social media are easy to see" 概括本段主题，引出后文对好处的展开。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段讲负面影响：焦虑抑郁、攀比伤自尊、网络霸凌、注意力分散。选项C "Unfortunately, the negatives are equally real" 与第二段"benefits"形成对应，自然引出弊端论述。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讲应对策略：设时限、关通知、理性看待内容、关注激励性账号。选项D "There are ways to manage our relationship with social media" 概括本段为"管理方法"，与后文专家建议、具体做法一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结社交媒体是工具、取决于用法、有意识与自控可趋利避害。选项E "In the end, balance is the key to a healthy digital life" 点明"平衡"这一核心，收束全文。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, many influencers post several times a day to keep their audience engaged.**：与社交媒体使用相关，可作例证。但各段空格均为主题句或过渡句，插具体"网红"案例会打断利与弊、应对策略的论述层次；且该例未明确支撑某一论点，属层次错位型干扰。
