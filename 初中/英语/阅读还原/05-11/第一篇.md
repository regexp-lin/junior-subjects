# 05-11 第一篇

**Food Safety: Keeping What We Eat Safe and Healthy**

Food safety is important for everyone. When we buy food from a store or eat at a restaurant, we trust that it is safe to eat. [1] In fact, food can become unsafe if it is not stored, prepared, or cooked properly. Bacteria（细菌） can grow quickly in food that is left at room temperature for too long. Raw meat and eggs can carry harmful germs if they are not cooked to the right temperature. That is why following basic food safety rules can protect our health and the health of our families.

What can we do at home to stay safe? [2] First, always wash your hands before handling food. Keep raw meat separate from other foods to avoid spreading bacteria. Cook meat, poultry（禽肉）, and eggs thoroughly until they reach a safe temperature. Store leftovers in the refrigerator within two hours. In other words, a few simple habits in the kitchen can prevent most food-related illnesses.

Food safety is also a responsibility of producers and sellers. [3] Farmers must use safe methods to grow crops and raise animals. Food factories need to follow strict hygiene（卫生） standards. Restaurants are required to keep their kitchens clean and train their workers properly. Governments around the world have laws to ensure that food sold in markets and stores meets safety requirements. For this reason, the food we buy is generally safer today than it was in the past.

However, food safety challenges still exist. [4] Climate change can affect how food is grown and make some crops more vulnerable to disease. Long supply chains mean that food travels far before it reaches our plates, which increases the risk of contamination（污染）. When outbreaks of foodborne illness occur, health authorities work quickly to find the source and prevent further spread. Despite these challenges, awareness of food safety has grown, and people are learning to make better choices.

[5] In short, food safety depends on everyone—from farmers and factories to cooks at home. By understanding the risks and following simple rules, we can enjoy our meals without worry. Remember: when in doubt, throw it out. It is better to waste a little food than to risk getting sick.

## 选项

A. That is because many factors can make food dangerous if we are not careful.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出食物可能不安全的多种原因。空前描述"When we buy food from a store or eat at a restaurant, we trust that it is safe to eat"，空后说明"In fact, food can become unsafe if it is not stored, prepared, or cooked properly...Bacteria can grow quickly...Raw meat and eggs can carry harmful germs...following basic food safety rules can protect our health"。A选项"That is because many factors can make food dangerous if we are not careful"（那是因为许多因素在我们不小心的情况下会使食物变得危险）中"That is because"解释为何需重视食品安全，"many factors...make food dangerous"与空后不当储存、制备、烹调及细菌、病菌等具体因素精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：总领家庭食品安全措施。空前设问"What can we do at home to stay safe?"，空后列举"First, always wash your hands...Keep raw meat separate...Cook meat, poultry, and eggs thoroughly...Store leftovers in the refrigerator within two hours...a few simple habits in the kitchen can prevent most food-related illnesses"。B选项"In other words, following a few basic steps can keep your kitchen safe"（换言之，遵循几个基本步骤就能让厨房保持安全）中"In other words"概括空后内容，"a few basic steps"与空后列举的洗手、分离生肉、充分烹调、冷藏等形成呼应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：生产与销售环节的食品安全责任。空前提出"Food safety is also a responsibility of producers and sellers"，空后详述"Farmers must use safe methods...Food factories need to follow strict hygiene standards...Restaurants are required to keep kitchens clean...Governments have laws to ensure...food sold...meets safety requirements...food we buy is generally safer today"。C选项"That is why there are strict rules at every stage of the food chain"（因此食品链的每个环节都有严格规定）中"That is why"承接空前"responsibility of producers and sellers"，"strict rules at every stage"与空后农民、工厂、餐馆、政府等各环节的规范精准对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：食品安全面临的新挑战。空前指出"However, food safety challenges still exist"，空后列举"Climate change can affect how food is grown...make some crops more vulnerable to disease...Long supply chains...increase the risk of contamination...When outbreaks occur, health authorities work quickly...Despite these challenges, awareness has grown"。D选项"It is true that new challenges have emerged in recent years"（的确，近年来出现了新的挑战）中"It is true that"与空前"However"呼应，"new challenges have emerged"总领空后气候变化、长供应链、疫情等具体挑战，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与责任重申。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"food safety depends on everyone—from farmers and factories to cooks at home...By understanding the risks and following simple rules, we can enjoy our meals without worry...when in doubt, throw it out"。E选项"In conclusion, we all play a part in ensuring the food we eat is safe"（总之，我们在确保所吃食物安全方面都有一份责任）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"we all play a part"与空后"depends on everyone"及"from farmers...to cooks at home"完美呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some people prefer to buy organic food from local farms.**：涉及购买有机和本地食品，易被误填[2]或[4]。但F讨论的是消费者的个人偏好选择，而空[2]前后文是家庭烹饪的具体操作步骤（洗手、分离、烹调、冷藏），空[4]讨论的是气候变化、供应链等系统性挑战；F属于消费选择层面，与空[2]的操作指南、空[4]的宏观挑战均不匹配。F属"主题相关但论述层级错位"型干扰。
