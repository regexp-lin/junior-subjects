# 06-25 第二篇

**The Power of Working Together**

Whether in sports, school projects, or the workplace, teamwork is everywhere. One person alone can achieve a lot, but when people work together towards a shared goal, they can often achieve much more. [1] In other words, teamwork combines individual strengths and creates something greater than the sum of its parts. From a winning football team to a successful science project, good teamwork depends on trust, communication, and a willingness to support each other.

Teams need clear roles and shared goals. [2] When everyone knows their task and how it fits into the bigger picture, work goes more smoothly. For example, in a group project, one person might research, another might write, and another might design the presentation. Each role matters, and when one person struggles, others can offer help. Teams also need to agree on their goal—what are we trying to achieve? Without a clear aim, team members may pull in different directions. Regular meetings or check-ins can help everyone stay on track and solve problems early.

Communication is the key to good teamwork. [3] Team members must share ideas, listen to others, and give honest feedback. Misunderstandings can lead to mistakes and frustration. It is important to speak up when you have a suggestion or see a problem—staying silent can hurt the whole team. At the same time, listening to others' ideas shows respect and often leads to better solutions. Good communication also means being honest about difficulties and asking for help when needed. When people feel heard and respected, they are more likely to contribute their best.

Conflict in teams is normal, but it can be managed. [4] People have different opinions and working styles, and sometimes these clash. The best teams do not avoid conflict—they deal with it calmly. Talking about differences, finding common ground, and sometimes compromising can help the team move forward. A team leader or a neutral person can help when discussions get stuck. Remember: the goal is not to "win" an argument but to find the best solution for the team. When conflicts are resolved well, the team often becomes stronger.

[5] Learning to work well with others—in class, in clubs, or in future jobs—will serve you for life. The next time you work in a team, notice how different people bring different strengths. Together, we can achieve more than we ever could alone.

## 选项

A. In other words, teamwork combines individual strengths and creates something greater than the sum of its parts.
B. When everyone knows their role and the team has a clear goal, work goes more smoothly.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：概括团队合作的核心价值。空前描述"Whether sports, school projects, workplace...teamwork everywhere...one person achieve lot...people work together towards shared goal...achieve much more"，空后说明"From winning football team to successful science project...good teamwork depends on trust, communication, willingness to support"。A选项"In other words, teamwork combines individual strengths and creates something greater than the sum of its parts"（换言之，团队合作凝聚个人优势，创造出大于各部分之和的结果）中"In other words"概括空前，"combines individual strengths...greater than sum of parts"与空后"trust, communication, support"及整体价值精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：明确角色与共同目标的重要性。空前提出"Teams need clear roles and shared goals"，空后详述"When everyone knows task, how fits bigger picture...work goes smoothly...group project: one research, another write, another design...each role matters...when one struggles, others help...agree on goal—what achieve?...Without clear aim...pull different directions...Regular meetings, check-ins...stay on track, solve problems early"。B选项"When everyone knows their role and the team has a clear goal, work goes more smoothly"（当每个人清楚自己的角色且团队有明确目标时，工作会更顺畅）中"knows role...clear goal...work smoothly"与空后内容完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：沟通在团队合作中的关键作用。空前提出"Communication is the key to good teamwork"，空后描述"Share ideas, listen, give honest feedback...Misunderstandings lead to mistakes, frustration...speak up when suggestion or problem...staying silent hurt team...listening shows respect, better solutions...honest about difficulties, ask for help...when heard, respected...contribute best"。C选项"Team members need to share ideas, listen to others, and communicate honestly to avoid misunderstandings"（团队成员需要分享想法、倾听他人并坦诚沟通以避免误解）中"share ideas...listen...communicate honestly...avoid misunderstandings"与空后内容精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：冲突的普遍性与积极处理方式。空前提出"Conflict in teams is normal, but it can be managed"，空后列举"People different opinions, working styles...sometimes clash...best teams don't avoid—deal calmly...talking about differences, finding common ground, compromising...team move forward...leader or neutral person when stuck...goal not win argument but best solution...conflicts resolved well...team stronger"。D选项"Conflict is normal, but teams can manage it by talking, finding common ground, and sometimes compromising"（冲突很正常，但团队可以通过交流、寻求共同点、有时妥协来应对）中"Conflict normal...manage...talking...common ground...compromising"与空后表述完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与团队合作作为终身技能的价值。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后为"Learning to work well with others—in class, in clubs, or in future jobs—will serve you for life.
