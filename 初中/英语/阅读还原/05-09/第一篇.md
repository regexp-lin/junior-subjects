# 05-09 第一篇

**Traditional Festivals: Connecting Past and Present**

Every culture around the world has its own traditional festivals. [1] These special days help us remember our history, honor our ancestors（祖先）, and pass our values from one generation to the next. The Mid-Autumn Festival, for example, brings families together to admire the full moon and share mooncakes. The Spring Festival celebrates the start of a new year with feasts, red lanterns, and the hope for good luck. In other words, festivals are not just for fun—they carry deep meaning.

Why do we still celebrate these old traditions in modern times? [2] One reason is that festivals give us a sense of belonging. When we take part in the same customs our grandparents practiced, we feel connected to our family and community. Festivals also provide a break from our busy daily routines. During these holidays, we slow down, spend time with loved ones, and create memories that we will treasure for years. That is why many people look forward to festival seasons.

Traditional festivals often involve special food. [3] In fact, food plays a central role in most celebrations. Dumplings are a must during the Spring Festival in many parts of China. Turkey is served at Thanksgiving in America. In Mexico, people enjoy special bread and hot chocolate during the Day of the Dead. These dishes are not just delicious—they tell stories and remind us of our roots. Sharing food during festivals strengthens the bonds between family members and friends.

However, some young people today pay less attention to traditional festivals. [4] They may prefer to spend holidays shopping or watching movies rather than following old customs. This has led to concern about whether these traditions will survive. Fortunately, many schools and organizations are working to introduce festivals to young people through storytelling, handicraft（手工艺） workshops, and cultural events. By making festivals more interesting and relevant, they hope to keep these traditions alive for future generations.

[5] In short, traditional festivals are a bridge between the past and the present. They remind us who we are and where we come from. Whether we celebrate with food, family gatherings, or special rituals（仪式）, these festivals help us stay connected to our culture and to each other.

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：总领传统节日的意义。空前描述"Every culture around the world has its own traditional festivals"，空后列举"These special days help us remember our history, honor our ancestors, pass our values...Mid-Autumn Festival...Spring Festival...festivals are not just for fun—they carry deep meaning"。A选项"In other words, they help us understand and preserve our cultural identity"（换言之，它们帮助我们理解和保护我们的文化认同）中"In other words"概括空前信息，"understand and preserve our cultural identity"与空后"remember history, honor ancestors, pass values"及"carry deep meaning"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：解释现代人仍庆祝传统节日的原因。空前设问"Why do we still celebrate these old traditions in modern times?"，空后列举"One reason is that festivals give us a sense of belonging...feel connected to our family and community...provide a break from our busy daily routines...slow down, spend time with loved ones, create memories...look forward to festival seasons"。B选项"That is because festivals offer both emotional connection and a chance to relax"（那是因为节日既提供情感联结，也提供放松的机会）中"That is because"直接回答空前问题，"emotional connection"与"sense of belonging...connected"对应，"chance to relax"与"break...slow down"对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：食物在节日中的核心地位。空前提出"Traditional festivals often involve special food"，空后详述"In fact, food plays a central role in most celebrations...Dumplings...Turkey...special bread and hot chocolate...These dishes...tell stories and remind us of our roots"。C选项"In fact, food and festivals have been linked for as long as we can remember"（事实上，食物和节日之间的联系由来已久）中"In fact"加强衔接，"food and festivals have been linked"与空后"food plays a central role"及各国节日食物形成呼应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：年轻人疏远传统引发的担忧与应对。空前说明"some young people today pay less attention to traditional festivals...prefer shopping or watching movies...rather than following old customs"，空后描述"This has led to concern...whether these traditions will survive...Fortunately, many schools and organizations are working to introduce festivals...through storytelling, handicraft workshops...hope to keep these traditions alive"。D选项"This is a worrying trend that has prompted efforts to revive interest in traditions"（这是一个令人担忧的趋势，促使人们努力重燃对传统的兴趣）中"This...worrying trend"承接空前现象，"prompted efforts to revive interest"与空后"schools and organizations are working...introduce festivals"完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"traditional festivals are a bridge between the past and the present...remind us who we are and where we come from...help us stay connected to our culture and to each other"。E选项"In conclusion, traditional festivals remain an important part of who we are"（总之，传统节日仍然是我们身份的重要组成部分）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"important part of who we are"与空后"remind us who we are...stay connected to our culture"精准呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
