# 11-12 第二篇

**Language Learning: Opening Doors to the World**

Learning a new language opens many doors. It helps you communicate with people from other countries, understand different cultures, and see the world from new perspectives. In today's global society, being able to speak more than one language can also improve your job opportunities and help you make friends around the world. [1]

There is no single "best" way to learn a language. Some people learn well from textbooks and grammar exercises. Others prefer watching movies, listening to music, or chatting with native speakers online. Many find that combining different methods works best. For example, you might study vocabulary with an app, practice speaking with a friend, and read simple books or news articles. [2]

Practice is essential. Reading, writing, listening, and speaking are all important. Do not be afraid to make mistakes—they are a natural part of learning. The more you use the language, the more confident you become. Try to use it in real situations: order food in a restaurant, ask for directions, or have a short conversation with a visitor from another country. [3]

Language learning takes time and patience. You will not become fluent overnight. There may be days when you feel stuck or forget words you thought you knew. That is normal. Setting small, achievable goals—like learning five new words a day or finishing a short article—can help you stay motivated. Celebrating small wins keeps you moving forward. [4]

In the end, learning a language is about connection. It is about understanding others and being understood. It is about discovering new ways to express ideas and to appreciate the richness of human culture. [5]

A. Language learning is more than a school subject—it is a bridge that connects us to billions of people and countless opportunities across the globe.

C. Regular practice, including making and learning from mistakes, builds the fluency and confidence that turn a foreign language into a tool you can actually use.
D. With patience and persistence, language learning becomes a journey of gradual improvement rather than a race to the finish.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说学习新语言的好处——与外国人交流、理解不同文化、新视角看世界、提升就业、广交朋友；空后需总结语言学习超越学科的本质。A的"more than a school subject"与前文多种益处呼应，"bridge that connects us to billions of people and countless opportunities"与 communicate、job opportunities、make friends 对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说学习方法多样——课本语法、电影音乐、与母语者聊天、综合多种方法、举例 app+对话+阅读；空后需总结找到适合方法并坚持的意义。B的"Finding the methods that work for you and using them consistently"与前文"combining different methods"和举例呼应，"key to making real progress"与"works best"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前强调练习——读写听说都重要、别怕犯错、多用更自信、真实情境使用；空后需总结练习与犯错对流利度和自信的作用。C的"Regular practice, including making and learning from mistakes"与前文呼应，"builds the fluency and confidence"与"the more confident you become"对应，"turn a foreign language into a tool you can actually use"与"use it in real situations"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说学习需时间耐心——不会一夜流利、会有卡住或遗忘、正常现象、小目标与庆祝进步有助于坚持；空后需总结耐心与坚持带来的学习观。D的"With patience and persistence"与前文"takes time and patience"呼应，"journey of gradual improvement rather than a race"与"small, achievable goals"和"celebrating small wins"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说语言学习关乎联结——理解与被理解、发现新表达、欣赏文化丰富性；空后需总结无论出于何种目的的长期价值。E的"Whether you are learning for school, travel, or simply curiosity"与前文"connection"呼应，"lasts a lifetime and enriches every experience"与"opening doors"和"connection"对应，是合理收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For instance, the Duolingo app has over 500 million users worldwide and offers courses in more than 40 languages, from Spanish and French to Japanese and Klingon.**：以 For instance 开头，为 Duolingo app 的具体数据举例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[2] 易被误选因涉及学习方法，但该空需概括"找到适合方法并坚持"的主题，而非某一学习工具的用户数据举例。
