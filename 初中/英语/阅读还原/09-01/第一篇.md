# 09-01 第一篇

**The Power of Reading**

Reading is one of the most valuable habits a person can develop. It opens doors to new ideas, different cultures, and countless worlds of imagination. Whether you read novels, newspapers, or online articles, every piece of reading adds to your knowledge and understanding. [1] In a world full of fast-moving information, the ability to read well and think critically is more important than ever.

Reading improves our language skills in many ways. It expands our vocabulary, helps us understand grammar naturally, and teaches us how good sentences are structured. [2] The more we read, the better we become at expressing ourselves, both in writing and in speaking.

Books can also help us understand other people's lives and feelings. When we read stories, we step into someone else's shoes and see the world from their point of view. [3] This ability to empathize—to feel what others feel—makes us more thoughtful and compassionate members of society.

Not everyone finds it easy to enjoy reading. Some people struggle with it, and others simply haven't found the right book yet. [4] Libraries, teachers, and friends can all help by recommending books that match a person's interests and reading level, making the experience more enjoyable and rewarding.

In today's digital age, we have more ways to read than ever before—e-books, audiobooks, and online platforms offer endless choices. [5] No matter what form it takes, reading remains one of the best ways to learn, grow, and connect with the wider world around us.

## 选项

A. Reading is not just a skill for school—it is a lifelong tool that shapes how we think and communicate.
B. Exposure to well-written texts naturally improves our own ability to use language effectively.
C. Developing empathy through reading can improve our relationships and help us become better citizens.
D. The key is to find reading material that sparks curiosity and makes the reader want to turn the next page.
E. With so many options available, there has never been a better time to make reading a daily habit.
F. For example, many successful writers say they became good at writing by reading widely as children.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述阅读带来知识与理解，空后强调"the ability to read well and think critically is more important than ever"。A的"not just a skill for school—it is a lifelong tool"概括阅读的长远价值，"shapes how we think and communicate"与"think critically"呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举阅读如何提升语言能力（词汇、语法、句式），空后说"the better we become at expressing ourselves"。B的"Exposure to well-written texts"承接前文阅读内容，"improves our own ability to use language effectively"概括该段主旨。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说阅读让我们站在他人角度看世界，空后说"This ability to empathize... makes us more thoughtful and compassionate"。C的"Developing empathy through reading"与前文empathize对应，"improve our relationships and help us become better citizens"与"members of society"呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前指出有人觉得阅读困难或没找到合适的书，空后说图书馆、教师、朋友可以推荐合适的书。D的"find reading material that sparks curiosity"与"match a person's interests"对应，"makes the reader want to turn the next page"概括解决方案的核心。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前列举数字时代多种阅读方式，空后总结阅读的意义。E的"With so many options available"承接前文选择多样，"never been a better time to make reading a daily habit"升华主题，适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, many successful writers say they became good at writing by reading widely as children.**：以For example开头，属具体举例，与阅读促进写作相关；易误放[2]，但[2]需的是阅读提升语言能力的概括，非个例说明。
