# 05-17 第二篇

**Museums: Places That Teach and Inspire**

Museums are not just buildings that store old objects. They are living classrooms where we can learn about history, art, science, and culture in ways that books and screens cannot offer. [1] In fact, museums help us understand the past, explore different cultures, and discover how the world has changed over time. When we see a real dinosaur bone or an ancient pottery（陶器）with our own eyes, the experience stays in our memory far longer than reading about it. That is why many schools take students on museum trips every year.

What makes museums special? [2] Unlike a textbook, a museum lets you stand in front of real objects—a painting, a fossil（化石）, or a traditional costume（服饰）. You can look at them from different angles and think about the stories behind them. Some museums also offer hands-on activities: you can touch replicas（复制品）, try on clothes from the past, or do simple science experiments. In other words, learning in a museum is active and experiential, not passive. This kind of learning often sparks curiosity and creativity.

Museums also connect us to our roots. [3] By showing how people lived long ago, what they believed, and what they created, museums help us see that we are part of a long chain of human history. We can learn about our own culture and appreciate cultures from other countries. Understanding others builds respect and reduces prejudice. For this reason, museums play an important role in building a more peaceful and tolerant world.

Technology has made museums even more interesting. [4] Many museums now use audio guides, videos, and interactive screens to explain exhibits. Some have apps that let you explore collections at home. Virtual tours allow people who cannot visit in person to "walk" through galleries online. So technology has made museum learning more accessible and fun for everyone, including those who live far away or have limited mobility（行动能力）.

[5] In short, museums are powerful tools for education. They bring history and culture to life, encourage us to ask questions, and help us understand the world better. Whether you visit a science museum, an art gallery, or a history museum, you are sure to leave with new ideas and a wider view of the world. Next time you have a free afternoon, why not pay a museum a visit?

## 选项

A. That is because museums offer unique ways to learn that go beyond traditional classrooms.
B. In other words, museums let you learn by seeing and doing, not just by reading.
C. That is why museums matter for building understanding between people and cultures.
D. It is true that modern technology has changed how we experience museums.
E. In conclusion, museums are valuable for education and personal growth.
F. For example, some museums hold special night events for families.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：总领博物馆的独特教育价值。空前描述"Museums are not just buildings that store old objects. They are living classrooms where we can learn about history, art, science, and culture in ways that books and screens cannot offer"，空后说明"In fact, museums help us understand the past, explore different cultures, and discover how the world has changed over time...When we see a real dinosaur bone or an ancient pottery with our own eyes, the experience stays in our memory far longer...many schools take students on museum trips every year"。A选项"That is because museums offer unique ways to learn that go beyond traditional classrooms"（那是因为博物馆提供超越传统课堂的独特学习方式）中"That is because"解释博物馆为何是"living classrooms"，"unique ways...go beyond"与空后亲身观看、记忆持久及学校组织参观精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：博物馆体验式学习的特质。空前设问"What makes museums special?"，空后详述"Unlike a textbook, a museum lets you stand in front of real objects...You can look at them from different angles...Some museums also offer hands-on activities: you can touch replicas, try on clothes from the past, or do simple science experiments...learning in a museum is active and experiential, not passive...This kind of learning often sparks curiosity and creativity"。B选项"In other words, museums let you learn by seeing and doing, not just by reading"（换言之，博物馆让你通过看和做来学习，而不仅仅是读）中"In other words"概括空后内容，"seeing and doing, not just by reading"与空后real objects、hands-on、active and experiential完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：博物馆对跨文化理解的促进作用。空前提出"Museums also connect us to our roots"，空后详述"By showing how people lived long ago, what they believed, and what they created, museums help us see that we are part of a long chain of human history...We can learn about our own culture and appreciate cultures from other countries...Understanding others builds respect and reduces prejudice...museums play an important role in building a more peaceful and tolerant world"。C选项"That is why museums matter for building understanding between people and cultures"（因此博物馆对于建立人与人、文化与文化之间的理解很重要）中"That is why"承接空前及空后结论，"building understanding between people and cultures"与空后respect、reduce prejudice、peaceful and tolerant精准对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：技术如何改变博物馆体验。空前提出"Technology has made museums even more interesting"，空后列举"Many museums now use audio guides, videos, and interactive screens to explain exhibits...Some have apps that let you explore collections at home...Virtual tours allow people who cannot visit in person to 'walk' through galleries online...technology has made museum learning more accessible and fun for everyone, including those who live far away or have limited mobility"。D选项"It is true that modern technology has changed how we experience museums"（的确，现代技术改变了我们体验博物馆的方式）中"It is true that"加强衔接，"changed how we experience"与空后音频导览、APP、虚拟导览及"more accessible"完美呼应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与博物馆价值的总结。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"museums are powerful tools for education...They bring history and culture to life, encourage us to ask questions, and help us understand the world better...Whether you visit a science museum, an art gallery, or a history museum, you are sure to leave with new ideas and a wider view of the world...Next time you have a free afternoon, why not pay a museum a visit?"。E选项"In conclusion, museums are valuable for education and personal growth"（总之，博物馆对教育和个人成长很有价值）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"valuable for education and personal growth"与空后powerful tools、new ideas、wider view形成呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some museums hold special night events for families.**：涉及博物馆家庭之夜活动，易被误填[2]或[4]。但F讨论的是特定活动形式（夜间活动），而空[2]聚焦体验式学习方式（看、做），空[4]聚焦技术手段（音频、APP、虚拟导览）；F属于活动类型举例，与空[2]的学习方式、空[4]的技术变革论述层级不同。F属"主题相关但论述维度错位"型干扰。
