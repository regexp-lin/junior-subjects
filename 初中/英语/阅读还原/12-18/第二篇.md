**The Science of Dreams: What Happens When We Sleep**

We all dream, even if we do not always remember our dreams. Dreams occur mainly during a stage of sleep called REM—rapid eye movement. During REM, the brain is very active, almost as active as when we are awake. Eyes move quickly behind closed lids. Scientists have studied dreams for decades, but many questions remain. Why do we dream? Do dreams have meaning? The answers are not simple. [1]

Several theories try to explain why we dream. One idea is that dreams help us process emotions and memories. We might "replay" the day's events and sort through feelings. Another theory suggests dreams help the brain prepare for future challenges—like a rehearsal for real life. Some researchers think dreams are just random brain activity with no special purpose. The truth may combine elements of all these views. [2]

Dream content varies widely. Some people dream in color; others in black and white. Dreams can feel very real or strange and illogical. Common themes include being chased, flying, or showing up somewhere unprepared. Stress, health, and daily experiences often influence what we dream about. Cultural background can shape dream symbols and stories. [3]

Lucid dreaming is when you know you are dreaming while still asleep. Some people can learn to control their dreams to some degree. Lucid dreamers might choose to fly, explore, or change the dream's plot. Researchers study lucid dreaming to understand consciousness and the brain. It remains a fascinating and partly mysterious area of sleep science. [4]

Dreams connect us to our inner world. Whether they help us learn, heal, or simply entertain, they are a natural part of being human. The more we learn about sleep and dreams, the more we understand ourselves. [5]

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍人人都会做梦即使不记得、主要发生在REM阶段、REM时脑活跃、眼快速转动、科学家研究数十年仍有多问、为何做梦有无意义答案复杂，空后需总结梦的正常性与未解之谜。A的"a normal part of sleep, especially during REM, but much about them is still not fully understood"与前文对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说多种理论解释、一为处理情绪记忆、重播当日梳理感受、二为大脑预演未来、三为随机活动无特殊目的、真相可能综合，空后需总结理论的多元与综合。B的"Scientists have proposed different explanations, and the real purpose of dreams may involve several factors"与前文呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说梦内容多样、有人彩色有人黑白、或真实或荒谬、常见主题被追飞 unprepared、压力健康经历影响、文化影响象征与故事，空后需总结梦内容的来源。C的"What we dream about can be shaped by our experiences, emotions, and culture"与前文对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说清明梦是知梦仍睡、有人可学会部分控制、选择飞探索改剧情、研究者用以理解意识与大脑、仍是迷人且部分神秘的领域，空后需总结清明梦的研究价值。D的"Lucid dreaming offers a unique window into how the mind works during sleep"与前文呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说梦连接内心、无论助学习疗愈或娱乐、都是人类自然部分、对睡眠与梦了解越多越理解自己，空后需总结梦在人类经验中的地位。E的"Dreams, whatever their purpose, are an important and intriguing part of human experience"与前文对应。
