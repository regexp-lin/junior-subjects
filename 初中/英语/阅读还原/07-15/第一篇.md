# 07-15 第一篇

**The Benefits of Cycling as a Way to Travel**

Cycling has become increasingly popular in cities around the world. More and more people are choosing to ride bicycles instead of driving cars for their daily commute. Bikes produce no pollution, cost little to maintain, and provide exercise at the same time. For short to medium distances, cycling can often be faster than sitting in traffic. Cities that invest in bike lanes see fewer accidents and happier citizens. [1]

[2] Regular cycling strengthens the heart and lungs. It also helps build muscle in the legs and improves balance. Unlike driving, cycling burns calories instead of fuel. Many people who switch to biking report better sleep and less stress. The physical benefits are clear, and they come with no gym membership required.

[3] Cars take up a lot of space on roads and in parking lots. A single bike needs only a small fraction of that space. When more people cycle, there are fewer vehicles on the road, which means less traffic for everyone. Some cities have found that adding bike lanes can actually improve the flow of car traffic by reducing the number of cars.

[4] Riding a bike costs much less than owning and operating a car. There are no fuel bills, no parking fees in many places, and minimal repair costs. For students and young workers, a bicycle can be an affordable way to stay mobile. The money saved can go toward other important goals.

[5] Cycling is not just a means of transport—it is a choice that benefits our health, our wallets, and our planet. As cities continue to improve cycling infrastructure, the choice to ride becomes easier and safer for everyone.

## 选项

A. These advantages make cycling an excellent choice for urban life.
B. The health benefits of cycling are well documented.
C. Cycling also helps reduce traffic and save space.
D. Financially, cycling is one of the most economical options.
E. With so many benefits, it is no wonder that cycling is gaining popularity worldwide.
F. For example, Amsterdam is known for having more bicycles than people.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：第一段介绍骑行普及、无污染、低成本、锻炼、省时、城市投入自行车道等优势。空格前已列举多项益处。选项A "These advantages make cycling an excellent choice for urban life" 中"These advantages"指代前文，"excellent choice"总结并承上启下，引出后文分述。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段讲骑行对心肺、肌肉、平衡、卡路里消耗、睡眠和压力的益处。选项B "The health benefits of cycling are well documented" 作为主题句，自然引出后文对身体好处的具体展开。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段讲占地少、减少车辆、缓解拥堵、自行车道改善车流。选项C "Cycling also helps reduce traffic and save space" 概括本段两大要点——节省空间与缓解交通，与后文逻辑一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讲骑行经济：省油费、停车费、维修少、学生年轻人可负担。选项D "Financially, cycling is one of the most economical options" 点明本段从经济角度论述，与后文"costs much less"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结骑行不仅是交通方式，还惠及健康、钱包、地球，并展望设施改善。选项E "With so many benefits, it is no wonder that cycling is gaining popularity worldwide" 回扣全文多重益处，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, Amsterdam is known for having more bicycles than people.**：与自行车城市相关，可作例证。但各段空格均为主题句或总结句位置，插具体城市案例会打断论证层次；且阿姆斯特丹之例更适合作"城市投资自行车道"的例证，若随意插入会显得突兀。属主题相关但论述层次错位型干扰。
