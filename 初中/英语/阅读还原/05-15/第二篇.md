# 05-15 第二篇

**The Future of Public Transport: Smarter and Greener Ways to Travel**

Cities around the world are growing, and so is the need for better ways to move people. Cars cause traffic jams and air pollution. [1] In fact, buses, trains, and subways can carry many more people using less space and less energy. A single bus can replace dozens of cars on the road. Trains running on electricity produce far less pollution than petrol（汽油） cars. That is why improving public transport is one of the best ways to make cities cleaner and less crowded.

Technology is changing how we use public transport. [2] In many cities, you can now use your phone to plan a trip, buy tickets, and check when the next bus or train will arrive. Some places have introduced shared bikes and scooters（滑板车） that connect with buses and trains for the "last mile" of a journey. Real-time apps make it easier to avoid waiting in the rain or cold. In other words, technology is making public transport more convenient and easier to use than ever before.

Electric and clean-energy vehicles are the next step. [3] More and more cities are buying electric buses that produce no exhaust fumes（尾气）. Some trains already run on solar or wind power. In the future, we may see more hydrogen-powered buses and even driverless shuttles. These changes will help cut down pollution and fight climate change. For this reason, the shift to clean energy is a key part of the future of public transport.

Public transport also helps build stronger communities. [4] When people ride buses and trains together, they share the same space and sometimes the same experiences. Good transport links make it easier for people to reach jobs, schools, and hospitals, especially for those who cannot afford a car. In many cities, better bus and train services have brought new life to areas that were once cut off. So public transport is not only about moving—it is about connecting people and places.

[5] In short, the future of public transport looks bright: smarter technology, cleaner energy, and stronger connections. By choosing buses and trains over cars when we can, we help reduce traffic and pollution and make our cities better places to live. The next time you travel, consider going green—your choice can make a difference.

## 选项

A. That is because public transport can move more people with less impact on the environment.
B. In other words, new technology is making public transport easier and more user-friendly.
C. That is why switching to clean energy is important for the future of transport.
D. It is true that public transport brings people and communities closer together.
E. In conclusion, we can all help build a better future by using public transport more.
F. For example, some cities have built underground parking lots to reduce street parking.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：公共交通在运力与环保上的优势。空前描述"Cities around the world are growing...need for better ways to move people...Cars cause traffic jams and air pollution"，空后说明"In fact, buses, trains, and subways can carry many more people using less space and less energy...A single bus can replace dozens of cars on the road...Trains running on electricity produce far less pollution than petrol cars...improving public transport is one of the best ways to make cities cleaner and less crowded"。A选项"That is because public transport can move more people with less impact on the environment"（那是因为公共交通能以更少的环境影响运送更多人）中"That is because"解释为何发展公共交通，"move more people with less impact"与空后运力、节地、节能与减污精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：技术提升公共交通的便利性。空前提出"Technology is changing how we use public transport"，空后列举"you can now use your phone to plan a trip, buy tickets, and check when the next bus or train will arrive...shared bikes and scooters that connect with buses and trains for the 'last mile'...Real-time apps make it easier to avoid waiting...technology is making public transport more convenient and easier to use than ever before"。B选项"In other words, new technology is making public transport easier and more user-friendly"（换言之，新技术正使公共交通更便捷、更易用）中"In other words"概括空前"technology is changing"，"easier and more user-friendly"与空后手机购票、共享接驳、实时查询等完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：清洁能源在公交未来中的关键地位。空前提出"Electric and clean-energy vehicles are the next step"，空后详述"More and more cities are buying electric buses that produce no exhaust fumes...Some trains already run on solar or wind power...we may see more hydrogen-powered buses and even driverless shuttles...These changes will help cut down pollution and fight climate change...the shift to clean energy is a key part of the future"。C选项"That is why switching to clean energy is important for the future of transport"（因此向清洁能源转型对交通的未来很重要）中"That is why"承接空前"electric and clean-energy vehicles"，"switching to clean energy"与空后电动、太阳能、氢能及"shift to clean energy"精准对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：公共交通的社会连接功能。空前提出"Public transport also helps build stronger communities"，空后说明"When people ride buses and trains together, they share the same space and sometimes the same experiences...Good transport links make it easier for people to reach jobs, schools, and hospitals...better bus and train services have brought new life to areas that were once cut off...public transport is not only about moving—it is about connecting people and places"。D选项"It is true that public transport brings people and communities closer together"（的确，公共交通让人们和社区更紧密相连）中"It is true that"加强衔接，"brings people and communities closer"与空后共享空间、通达就业与医疗、振兴偏远地区及"connecting people and places"完美呼应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与个人行动呼吁。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"the future of public transport looks bright: smarter technology, cleaner energy, and stronger connections...By choosing buses and trains over cars when we can, we help reduce traffic and pollution and make our cities better places to live...The next time you travel, consider going green—your choice can make a difference"。E选项"In conclusion, we can all help build a better future by using public transport more"（总之，我们都可以通过更多地使用公共交通来帮助建设更美好的未来）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"help build a better future by using public transport more"与空后"choosing buses and trains"及"your choice can make a difference"形成呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some cities have built underground parking lots to reduce street parking.**：涉及地下停车场建设，易被误填[1]或[4]。但F讨论的是私家车停车设施，而空[1]前后文强调公共交通相比私家车在运力与环保上的优势，空[4]讨论公共交通的社会连接功能；F属于围绕私家车的设施规划，与公共交通的论述主线不符。F属"主题相关但论述对象错位"型干扰。
