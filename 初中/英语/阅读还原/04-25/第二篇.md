# 04-25 第二篇

**Why Public Transportation Matters**

In many cities around the world, buses, subways, and trains carry millions of people every day. Public transportation helps people get to work, school, and other places without driving their own cars. [1] In fact, taking the bus or train can save both time and money. When roads are crowded, a subway can move through traffic much faster than a car. For those who cannot afford a car, public transport is often the only way to travel. It connects different parts of the city and makes life easier for everyone.

One of the biggest benefits of public transportation is that it helps protect the environment. [2] A full bus can carry dozens of people, but it uses far less fuel than dozens of cars would. Similarly, electric trains and trams（有轨电车） produce little or no air pollution. When more people choose to use public transport instead of driving, cities have cleaner air and fewer traffic jams. This is good for both our health and the planet.

Public transportation also brings economic benefits to cities and their people. [3] It creates jobs for drivers, mechanics, and station workers. It also helps businesses grow because workers and customers can easily reach shops and offices. In addition, people who use public transport save money on fuel, parking, and car repairs. They can spend that money on other things, which helps the local economy. Both the city and its residents benefit in the long run.

Beyond convenience and the environment, public transportation builds stronger communities. [4] On a bus or train, people from different neighbourhoods and backgrounds meet and travel together. Children learn to be independent when they take the bus to school. Elderly people can stay connected to friends and family when they no longer drive. Public transport gives everyone—young and old, rich and poor—a fair way to get around. In this way, it brings people closer and makes cities more inclusive（包容的）.

[5] In summary, public transportation is more than just a way to get from place to place. It saves money, reduces pollution, creates jobs, and strengthens our communities. As cities grow, investing in better buses, trains, and subways will become even more important. Good public transport is a sign of a healthy, livable city for all.

## 选项

A. That is why many cities are working to improve their public transport systems.
B. In other words, one vehicle can do the job of many, which greatly cuts down on emissions（排放物）.
C. In fact, it offers real advantages to both individuals and the city as a whole.
D. In short, public transport is a key part of a modern and sustainable city.
E. It brings people together and helps create a sense of community.
F. Research shows that cities with good public transport attract more visitors and investors（投资者）.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] C   [2] B   [3] A   [4] E   [5] D

## 深度解析

- **[1] C**：
  - **线索**：引出公共交通对个人的直接好处。空前描述"Public transportation helps people get to work, school...without driving their own cars"，空后具体说明"In fact, taking the bus or train can save both time and money...subway can move through traffic much faster...For those who cannot afford a car...only way to travel...connects different parts...makes life easier"。C选项"In fact, it offers real advantages to both individuals and the city as a whole"（事实上，它给个人和整座城市都带来实实在在的好处）中"In fact"承接空前，"advantages to both individuals and the city"与空后"save time and money...only way to travel...makes life easier"形成概括性过渡，为后文具体举例做铺垫，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：公共交通环保效益的机制说明。空前描述"One of the biggest benefits...helps protect the environment"，空后详述"A full bus can carry dozens of people, but it uses far less fuel than dozens of cars would...electric trains and trams produce little or no air pollution...more people choose public transport...cleaner air and fewer traffic jams"。B选项"In other words, one vehicle can do the job of many, which greatly cuts down on emissions"（换言之，一辆车能完成多辆车的任务，从而大大减少排放）中"In other words"对上文"protect the environment"进行具体化，"one vehicle...job of many...cuts down on emissions"与空后"full bus...far less fuel than dozens of cars...little or no air pollution"完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] A**：
  - **线索**：公共交通经济价值与城市发展的因果。空前列举"creates jobs for drivers, mechanics, station workers...helps businesses grow...workers and customers can easily reach...people who use public transport save money...spend that money on other things...helps the local economy...Both the city and its residents benefit"，空后总结"Both the city and its residents benefit in the long run"。A选项"That is why many cities are working to improve their public transport systems"（因此许多城市正在努力改善公交系统）中"That is why"承接空前列举的诸多经济益处，"many cities...improve their public transport"与空后"city and residents benefit"形成因果呼应，说明城市为何重视公交建设，衔接精准。

- **[4] E**：
  - **线索**：公共交通的社会与社区功能。空前说明"Beyond convenience and the environment, public transportation builds stronger communities...On a bus or train, people from different neighbourhoods and backgrounds meet...Children learn to be independent...Elderly people can stay connected...gives everyone a fair way to get around...brings people closer...makes cities more inclusive"。E选项"It brings people together and helps create a sense of community"（它把人们聚在一起，有助于营造社区感）中"brings people together"与空前"people from different...meet and travel together"对应，"sense of community"与空后"brings people closer...inclusive"完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[5] D**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后列举"more than just a way to get from place to place...saves money, reduces pollution, creates jobs, strengthens communities...investing in better buses, trains, subways...Good public transport is a sign of a healthy, livable city"。D选项"In short, public transport is a key part of a modern and sustainable city"（简言之，公共交通是现代可持续城市的关键组成部分）用"In short"总结全文，"key part of a modern and sustainable city"与空后"saves money, reduces pollution, creates jobs, strengthens communities...healthy, livable city"形成升华，收束得当。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. Research shows that cities with good public transport attract more visitors and investors.**：强调公交对城市吸引力的影响，易被误填[3]。但F以"Research shows"开头，引入研究结论的表述方式，与空[3]前后文"creates jobs...helps businesses grow...people save money...helps the local economy"的列举式因果叙述风格不一致；空[3]需承接诸多经济益处并过渡到"city and residents benefit"，A的"That is why many cities are working to improve"更符合因果总结。F属"语义相关但表述风格与衔接词不匹配"型干扰。
