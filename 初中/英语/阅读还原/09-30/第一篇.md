# 09-30 第一篇

**The Rise of Drone Technology**

Drones, or unmanned aerial vehicles, were once mainly used by the military. Today, they deliver packages, take photos, inspect power lines, and help farmers check their crops. From hobbyists to businesses, more and more people are finding uses for these flying machines. [1] What started as a high-tech tool for few has become a versatile tool that is changing how many industries work.

Drones can go where people cannot easily go. They fly over dangerous disaster zones to search for survivors. They enter buildings that are too risky for humans. Farmers use them to spray crops and monitor fields from above. [2] The ability to reach remote or dangerous places without putting lives at risk is one of the biggest advantages of drone technology.

As drones become more common, rules are needed to keep everyone safe. Many countries require drone operators to have licenses, avoid flying near airports, and respect people's privacy. Some areas are off-limits to drones. [3] Following these rules protects both the public and the future of legal drone use.

Drones are also used for fun. Aerial photography and videography have become popular hobbies. Racing drones compete in events around the world. Yet flying responsibly means knowing your limits and respecting others. [4] Balancing the joy of flying with responsibility for safety and privacy is important for everyone who uses drones.

The future of drones is still unfolding. Delivery drones, flying taxis, and drones for medical supply drops are being tested. Young people interested in technology, engineering, or aviation may find careers in this growing field. [5] The drone industry is growing fast, and those who understand and master this technology today will help shape how we live and work tomorrow.

## 选项

A. Drones have moved from niche use to widespread application across many fields.
B. Drones offer a safe way to do jobs that would be risky or impossible for humans alone.
C. Responsible use of drones depends on knowing and obeying the law.
D. Enthusiasts can enjoy drones while still being careful about how and where they fly.
E. The next wave of drone innovation will be built by today's learners and inventors.
F. For example, some companies use drones to deliver medicine to remote villages that lack roads.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前指出无人机从军用拓展到物流、摄影、巡检、农业等，空后说"What started as a high-tech tool for few has become a versatile tool that is changing how many industries work"。A的"Drones have moved from niche use to widespread application across many fields"概括无人机应用范围的拓展，与"versatile tool"和"changing... industries"完美呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举无人机可到达人类难以到达之处（灾区、危险建筑、农田），空后强调"The ability to reach remote or dangerous places without putting lives at risk is one of the biggest advantages"。B的"Drones offer a safe way to do jobs that would be risky or impossible for humans alone"概括无人机在危险与远程作业中的安全性优势，与"without putting lives at risk"逻辑一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举无人机监管要求（执照、禁飞区、隐私等），空后说"Following these rules protects both the public and the future of legal drone use"。C的"Responsible use of drones depends on knowing and obeying the law"准确概括合规使用的重要性，与"Following these rules"和"protects"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论无人机的娱乐用途及负责任飞行的要求，空后强调"Balancing the joy of flying with responsibility for safety and privacy is important for everyone who uses drones"。D的"Enthusiasts can enjoy drones while still being careful about how and where they fly"概括爱好者在享受乐趣的同时保持谨慎，与"Balancing"和"responsibility"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前展望无人机在物流、交通、医疗等领域的未来，空后说"The drone industry is growing fast, and those who understand and master this technology today will help shape how we live and work tomorrow"。E的"The next wave of drone innovation will be built by today's learners and inventors"升华主题，与"those who understand and master"和"shape"完美对应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some companies use drones to deliver medicine to remote villages that lack roads.**：以For example开头，属具体举例；各空均为概括或过渡句，F为无人机送药到偏远地区的个案，不适用于任何空位，易误放[2]，但该空需无人机安全到达危险/远程之处的概括句，非举例。
