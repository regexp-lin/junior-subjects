**The Many Uses of Drones in Modern Life**

Drones, or unmanned aerial vehicles, were once mainly used by the military. Today, they have become common tools in many different fields. From filming movies to delivering packages, drones are changing how we work and live. [1] Their ability to reach places that are dangerous or difficult for humans makes them especially valuable in emergencies. As technology improves, we can expect even more creative applications in the years ahead.

In agriculture, drones are helping farmers work smarter. [2] Equipped with cameras and sensors, they can fly over large fields and collect data on crop health, soil moisture, and pest problems. This information helps farmers decide exactly where to water, fertilize, or spray, saving both time and resources. Some farmers report significant increases in yield after adopting drone technology.

Drones are also transforming the way we respond to disasters. [3] After earthquakes or floods, drones can quickly survey damaged areas and locate people who need rescue. They can deliver medical supplies to places where roads are blocked. Rescue teams use drone footage to plan their operations safely. In many situations, drones have saved lives that would have been lost without them.

The film and media industry has embraced drones for their ability to capture stunning aerial shots. [4] What used to require expensive helicopters and crews can now be done with a single drone and operator. Documentaries, news reports, and even wedding videos benefit from the smooth, dynamic perspectives that drones provide. The cost of aerial photography has dropped dramatically.

[5] Drones are here to stay, but their growth brings new challenges. Privacy concerns, noise complaints, and airspace safety must be addressed through clear rules and responsible use. By balancing innovation with responsibility, we can enjoy the benefits of drones while minimizing their downsides.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍无人机多领域应用及技术发展，空后预言更多创意用途。A项"这一优势解释了为何它们越来越多地用于搜救"中"this advantage"呼应前文reach dangerous/difficult places，并为后文emergencies做铺垫，承上启下。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：本段主题为农业无人机，描述搭载相机和传感器收集作物、土壤、虫害数据。B项"空中收集的数据帮助农民在地面做出精确决策"与crop health、soil moisture、decide where to water/fertilize逻辑一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：段首点明灾害响应，后文写survey、locate、deliver supplies、plan operations。C项"得益于无人机，快速灵活的灾害响应成为可能"准确概括本段功能。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：段中对比过去需直升机与现在单机操作，提及纪录片、新闻、婚礼视频。D项"创意从业者发现无人机是他们工作的变革者"概括了影视媒体领域的革新。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：结尾段以[5]开头，后文列举隐私、噪音、空域安全及需制定规则。E项"社会必须找到方法管理无人机技术带来的风险"总领全段，点明挑战与应对方向。

  - **F. For example...**：以For example开头，虽与无人机摄影相关，但仅为房地产摄影的单一案例，与任一段落的概括或承启功能不符，论述层次错位。
