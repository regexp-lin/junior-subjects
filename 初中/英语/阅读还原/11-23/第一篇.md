**Green Buildings: Building a Sustainable Future**

Green buildings are designed to use fewer resources and cause less harm to the environment. They save energy through better insulation, efficient lighting, and smart heating and cooling systems. Many green buildings also collect rainwater for reuse and use materials that are recycled or come from sustainable sources. This approach reduces pollution and lowers long-term costs for building owners. [1] Green buildings combine smart design with eco-friendly technology to protect the environment while saving money and resources for years to come.

The idea of green building is not new. People have long built homes that stay cool in summer and warm in winter using natural materials and local climate. Today, architects and engineers use science and technology to do this on a much larger scale. They consider the direction of the sun, the flow of wind, and the quality of materials before construction begins. [2] Modern green building draws on both traditional wisdom and new technology to create structures that work in harmony with nature, rather than against it.

Green buildings often have special features such as rooftop gardens, solar panels, and large windows that let in plenty of natural light. Indoor plants can improve air quality, and low-flow taps help save water. Some buildings even generate their own electricity or heat. These choices make buildings healthier for people who live and work inside them. [3] By bringing together design, technology, and nature, green buildings offer a cleaner and more comfortable environment for both people and the planet.

Schools, offices, and homes can all be built in a greener way. Many cities now require new public buildings to meet certain environmental standards. Students can learn about sustainability in green school buildings, and companies find that workers are more productive in well-designed, naturally lit spaces. The demand for green building is growing as people become more aware of climate change. [4] As awareness of environmental issues grows, green building is becoming an important part of urban planning and daily life in communities around the world.

The future of construction lies in balance—between growth and responsibility, between comfort and conservation. Green building is not only for rich countries or special projects. Simple changes, such as better windows or energy-efficient appliances, can make any building greener. [5] Building in a sustainable way is a choice that benefits everyone: it protects our planet, improves our health, and creates a better world for future generations.

## 选项

A. Green buildings combine smart design with eco-friendly technology to protect the environment while saving money and resources for years to come.
B. Modern green building draws on both traditional wisdom and new technology to create structures that work in harmony with nature, rather than against it.
C. By bringing together design, technology, and nature, green buildings offer a cleaner and more comfortable environment for both people and the planet.
D. As awareness of environmental issues grows, green building is becoming an important part of urban planning and daily life in communities around the world.
E. Building in a sustainable way is a choice that benefits everyone: it protects our planet, improves our health, and creates a better world for future generations.
F. For example, the Empire State Building in New York underwent a major renovation to reduce its energy use by nearly 40 percent.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍绿色建筑的定义——少用资源、减少环境危害，通过保温、高效照明、智能暖冷系统节能，收集雨水、使用可回收或可持续材料，减少污染并降低长期成本，空后需概括绿色建筑在环保与经济效益上的综合价值。A的"combine smart design with eco-friendly technology"与前文各项设计措施呼应，"protect the environment while saving money and resources"与前文"reduces pollution"和"lowers long-term costs"对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说绿色建筑理念历史悠久——人们曾用天然材料与当地气候营造冬暖夏凉，如今建筑师与工程师用科技在更大规模上实现，考虑日照方向、风向与材料质量，空后需总结传统智慧与新技术结合。B的"draws on both traditional wisdom and new technology"与前文"people have long built"和"today... use science and technology"呼应，"work in harmony with nature"与"direction of the sun, flow of wind"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举绿色建筑特色——屋顶花园、太阳能板、大窗采光、室内植物改善空气、低流量水龙头、部分建筑自发电或供热，使建筑对居住者更健康，空后需总结设计、技术与自然结合带来的舒适环境。C的"By bringing together design, technology, and nature"与前文各类特征呼应，"cleaner and more comfortable environment for both people and the planet"与"healthier for people"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说学校、办公室、住宅均可绿色建造，多市要求新公共建筑达环保标准，学生可在绿色学校学可持续，公司在自然采光空间生产率更高，需求随气候意识增长，空后需总结绿色建筑在城镇规划与日常生活中的地位。D的"As awareness of environmental issues grows"与前文"people become more aware of climate change"呼应，"important part of urban planning and daily life"与前文各类建筑类型及城市要求对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说建设未来在于平衡——增长与责任、舒适与节约，绿色建筑非仅富国或特殊项目，换窗或节能电器等简单改动即可变绿，空后需总结可持续建造的普遍益处。E的"Building in a sustainable way is a choice that benefits everyone"与前文"not only for rich countries"和"simple changes"呼应，"protects our planet, improves our health, and creates a better world for future generations"是合理收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Empire State Building in New York underwent a major renovation to reduce its energy use by nearly 40 percent.**：以For example开头，为帝国大厦改造的具体案例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[1]或[3]易被误选因涉及节能或改造，但该空需概括绿色建筑整体价值或设计特色，而非某一建筑的具体改造数据。
