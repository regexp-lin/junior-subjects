**Wind Energy: Harnessing the Power of the Wind**

Wind energy is one of the fastest-growing sources of electricity in the world. Wind turbines capture the movement of air and convert it into clean, renewable power without burning fuels or producing harmful gases. Unlike coal or oil, wind does not run out—it will keep blowing as long as the sun heats the Earth. This makes wind energy a key part of the fight against climate change. [1] Wind energy provides a clean and endless source of power that helps reduce our reliance on fossil fuels and protects the environment for future generations.

Wind turbines can be built on land or at sea. Onshore wind farms are often found in open areas such as plains, hills, or deserts where the wind blows steadily. Offshore wind farms sit in the ocean, where winds are often stronger and more constant. Each turbine has tall towers and large blades that spin when the wind blows. The spinning motion drives a generator that produces electricity. [2] Whether on land or at sea, wind turbines use the natural force of the wind to generate electricity in a way that is both efficient and environmentally friendly.

Wind energy has many advantages. It does not pollute the air or water. It creates jobs in manufacturing, construction, and maintenance. It can be used in remote areas where it is hard to bring power from faraway plants. Many countries are investing heavily in wind power as they shift away from coal and gas. [3] The benefits of wind energy go beyond clean power—it supports local economies, creates employment, and brings electricity to places that need it most, all while keeping the air and water clean.

Of course, wind energy also has limits. The wind does not blow all the time, so backup power or storage is needed when it is calm. Some people worry about the sound of turbines or the effect on birds. Scientists and engineers are working on quieter designs and better placement to reduce these impacts. Wind farms also need space, which can affect land use or views. [4] Understanding both the strengths and the challenges of wind energy helps us use it wisely and improve it over time, rather than ignoring its drawbacks or giving up too soon.

The future of energy will likely mix many sources—wind, solar, and others. Wind power is already providing a large share of electricity in countries like Denmark and parts of China. As technology improves, turbines become more efficient and costs keep falling. [5] Wind energy will continue to play an important role in the global transition to clean, sustainable power, helping to meet our energy needs while caring for the planet we call home.

## 选项

A. Wind energy provides a clean and endless source of power that helps reduce our reliance on fossil fuels and protects the environment for future generations.
B. Whether on land or at sea, wind turbines use the natural force of the wind to generate electricity in a way that is both efficient and environmentally friendly.
C. The benefits of wind energy go beyond clean power—it supports local economies, creates employment, and brings electricity to places that need it most, all while keeping the air and water clean.
D. Understanding both the strengths and the challenges of wind energy helps us use it wisely and improve it over time, rather than ignoring its drawbacks or giving up too soon.
E. Wind energy will continue to play an important role in the global transition to clean, sustainable power, helping to meet our energy needs while caring for the planet we call home.
F. For instance, a single large wind turbine can power hundreds of homes for a year when the wind blows steadily.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说风能是全球增长最快的电力来源之一，风力涡轮机将空气运动转化为清洁可再生的电，无需燃烧燃料或产生有害气体，风如太阳加热地球般不会枯竭，是应对气候变化的关键，空后需概括风能清洁、可再生及对环境的正面作用。A的"clean and endless source of power"与前文"does not run out"和"renewable"呼应，"reduce our reliance on fossil fuels and protects the environment"与前文"without burning fuels"和"fight against climate change"对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说风机可建在陆地或海上，陆上风电场在平原丘陵沙漠等开阔地，海上风电场在海洋中风更强更稳，每台风机有高塔和大叶片，旋转驱动发电机发电，空后需总结陆海两种场址下风能的利用方式。B的"Whether on land or at sea"与前文"on land or at sea"及"onshore"与"offshore"呼应，"use the natural force of the wind to generate electricity"与"capture the movement of air"和"spin when the wind blows"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举风能优点——不污染空气和水、创造制造建设维护岗位、可用于偏远地区、多国加大投资、从煤和天然气转型，空后需总结风能超出清洁电力以外的综合益处。C的"The benefits of wind energy go beyond clean power"与前文"many advantages"呼应，"supports local economies, creates employment, and brings electricity to places that need it most"与"creates jobs"和"remote areas"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说风能局限——风不常吹需备用电源或储能、有人担忧噪音或对鸟类影响、科学家改进静音设计及选址减少影响、风电场需占地影响土地利用或景观，空后需总结认识优缺点以明智利用并持续改进。D的"Understanding both the strengths and the challenges"与前文"has limits"及各项挑战呼应，"use it wisely and improve it over time, rather than ignoring its drawbacks or giving up too soon"与"Scientists and engineers are working on"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说未来能源将混合多种来源——风能、太阳能等，风能在丹麦及中国部分地区已占相当比例，技术改进使涡轮更高效、成本持续下降，空后需总结风能在全球清洁能源转型中的长期角色。E的"Wind energy will continue to play an important role"与前文"already providing a large share"呼应，"global transition to clean, sustainable power"与"fight against climate change"及"mix many sources"对应，是合理收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For instance, a single large wind turbine can power hundreds of homes for a year when the wind blows steadily.**：以For instance开头，为单台大型风机发电量的具体案例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[2]易被误选因涉及风机发电，但该空需概括陆海场址下风能利用的整体方式，而非某一台风机的具体发电能力。
