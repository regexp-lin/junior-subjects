# 03-14 第二篇

**The Importance of Drinking Enough Water**

Everyone knows that water is essential for life. However, many people still do not drink nearly enough of it every day. [1] Since our bodies are made mostly of water, even a small drop in water levels can cause big problems.

One of the first signs that you need more water is a feeling of tiredness. [2] Without enough water, your heart has to work much harder to pump oxygen to your organs. This extra effort quickly takes away your energy and leaves you feeling completely exhausted.

Drinking plenty of water is also necessary for a healthy brain. Research indicates that dehydration (脱水) can strongly affect a student's performance at school. [3] Staying hydrated, on the other hand, helps you focus better and think much faster during a difficult math test.

Besides keeping your energy up and your brain sharp, water is a powerful beauty secret. [4] It helps wash away unhealthy waste from inside your body, which can prevent bad skin problems. Therefore, models and actors often call a glass of water their most important skincare product.

[5] A good habit is to carry a reusable water bottle with you wherever you go. By taking a few extra sips throughout the day, you are giving your body the simple care it deserves.

## 选项

A. As a result, you might find it very difficult to remember new vocabulary or pay attention in class.
B. So, how can we make sure we are drinking enough of this magical liquid?
C. It acts as a natural cleaner that keeps your skin looking fresh and clear.
D. When you do not drink enough, your blood actually becomes much thicker.
E. Therefore, drinking sweet fruit juice or soda is a much better choice than plain water.
F. Ignoring your thirst can result in headaches, dry skin, and a lack of energy.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] F   [2] D   [3] A   [4] C   [5] B

## 深度解析

- **[1] F**：
  - **线索**：具体表述与因果逻辑。空前说到很多人每天喝水都不够（do not drink nearly enough）。F选项“Ignoring your thirst can result in headaches, dry skin, and a lack of energy”（忽视口渴会导致头痛、皮肤干燥和缺乏精力），非常具体地列出了喝水不够（忽视口渴）会带来的各种负面问题。这也完美解释了空后一句“因为我们主要由水构成，水位的微小下降也会引起大问题（big problems指代的正是F项中的众多症状）”。
- **[2] D**：
  - **线索**：逻辑条件与医学解释。本段主旨是缺水让人感觉疲劳（tiredness）。D选项“When you do not drink enough, your blood actually becomes much thicker”（当你喝水不够时，你的血液实际上变得粘稠得多）充当了因果链条的关键一环。正因为血液变粘稠了，所以空后的句子自然接上：“Without enough water, your heart has to work much harder to pump oxygen”（你的心脏必须更努力地泵送氧气），从而导致“精力被耗尽（exhausted）”。
- **[3] A**：
  - **线索**：因果衔接与语境对应。空前指出了核心科研结论“脱水会强烈影响学生在学校的表现”。A选项“As a result, you might find it very difficult to remember new vocabulary or pay attention in class”（结果，你可能会发现很难记住新词汇或在课堂上集中注意力）正是对这种学生糟糕表现的具体描绘（记不住词汇、不专心）。这与空后“on the other hand（另一方面）”引出的多喝水能“更好地专注和思考”，形成了直接的正反对比。
- **[4] C**：
  - **线索**：代词指代与比喻解释。空前引出了水是一个强大的美丽秘诀（beauty secret）。C选项“It acts as a natural cleaner that keeps your skin looking fresh and clear”（它像一种天然清洁剂，保持你的皮肤看起来清爽干净），代词“It”指代前文的主语“water”。“清洁剂”、“清爽干净”是对“美丽秘诀”的具体解释。此外，这句话里的“cleaner”与空后的“helps wash away unhealthy waste（帮助冲洗掉不健康的废物）”语义极度贴合。
- **[5] B**：
  - **线索**：总结呼应与提出问题。空[5]位于文章结尾段的段首。前面三段分别论述了从精力、大脑到皮肤三个方面喝水的重要性。B选项“So, how can we make sure we are drinking enough of this magical liquid?”（那么，我们怎样才能确保喝足够多的这种神奇液体呢？）起到了总结上文和引出下文解决办法的桥梁作用。“this magical liquid”就是对前面“水”的总结性指代。随后空后给出的建议“带一个可重复使用的水瓶”正是对这个问题的直接回答。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **E. Therefore, drinking sweet fruit juice or soda is a much better choice than plain water.**：该选项包含“Therefore（因此）”和高频词汇“water（水）”、“drink（喝）”。但是它的结论是荒谬的：因此，喝甜果汁或汽水是比喝白开水更好的选择。通篇文章全是在赞颂“plain water”（纯净的水，大自然的神奇液体、天然清洁剂）对身体的巨大益处。这一选项在核心立意和健康常识上都构成了彻底的反向干扰，意在迷惑那些只看个别单词而不看全句逻辑的考生。
