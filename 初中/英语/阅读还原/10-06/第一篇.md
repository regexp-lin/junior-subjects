# 10-06 第一篇

**The Spirit of Sports**

Sports are more than just games. They teach us about teamwork, discipline, and the value of hard work. Whether we win or lose, the lessons we learn on the field or in the gym can stay with us for life. Many athletes say that sports helped them build character and overcome challenges. [1] This mindset—of focusing on growth rather than only on scores—is what makes sports a powerful tool for personal development at any age.

Team sports like basketball and soccer require players to work together. Each person has a role, and success depends on everyone doing their part. Players learn to communicate, trust their teammates, and put the team's goals above personal glory. [2] Employers and teachers often notice that students who play team sports tend to work well with others and handle group projects more effectively.

Sports also teach us to handle failure. Not every game can be won, and not every shot goes in. Learning to lose gracefully and to learn from mistakes builds resilience. [3] Athletes who have experienced defeat often say it made them stronger and more determined to succeed the next time.

Fair play is at the heart of sports. Cheating, even when no one is watching, undermines the whole point of competition. Respecting the rules and the officials shows integrity. [4] Young fans watching their heroes play by the rules learn that success achieved through fairness is more meaningful than victory won by cheating.

Not everyone will become a champion, but everyone can benefit from being active. Regular exercise improves physical health, reduces stress, and boosts mood. Schools and communities that support sports programs give young people a healthy outlet for their energy. [5] Whether we play for fun or for medals, the habits we form and the character we build through sports will serve us long after the final whistle blows.

## 选项

A. The spirit of sports is not just about winning—it is about giving our best effort, respecting our opponents, and growing stronger through both victory and defeat.
B. These skills translate well beyond the playing field, helping young people in school, work, and relationships throughout their lives.
C. The ability to bounce back after a setback is one of the most valuable lessons that sports can offer, and it applies to challenges we face in all areas of life.
D. When athletes compete fairly and honorably, they set an example for others and help ensure that sports remain a positive force in society.
E. In the end, the true measure of sports is not only the trophies we win but the better people we become through the journey of training, competing, and striving to improve.
F. For example, the Olympic Games bring together athletes from over 200 countries to compete in the spirit of friendship and fair play.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述体育教团队合作、纪律与努力，空后说"This mindset—of focusing on growth rather than only on scores"。A的"not just about winning—it is about giving our best effort... growing stronger"与空后"focusing on growth"呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举团队运动中的协作与信任，空后说"Employers and teachers often notice that students who play team sports tend to work well with others"。B的"These skills translate well beyond the playing field"与空后employers、teachers的观察衔接。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说体育教我们面对失败、从错误中学习，空后说"Athletes who have experienced defeat often say it made them stronger"。C的"The ability to bounce back after a setback"与空后"made them stronger"直接对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前强调公平竞争、尊重规则与裁判，空后说"Young fans watching their heroes play by the rules learn that success... is more meaningful than victory won by cheating"。D的"When athletes compete fairly and honorably... set an example"与空后"heroes play by the rules"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说学校与社区支持体育项目，空后说"Whether we play for fun or for medals, the habits we form... will serve us long after the final whistle blows"。E的"the true measure of sports is not only the trophies... but the better people we become"与空后"habits... character"衔接。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Olympic Games bring together athletes from over 200 countries...**：以For example开头，属具体赛事举例，各空均为概括或过渡句；易误放[1]或[4]，但两空分别需体育精神的概括与公平竞争的概括，非个例说明。
