**Yoga: Ancient Practice, Modern Benefits**

Yoga is an ancient practice that began in India over 5,000 years ago. Today, millions of people around the world practice yoga for its physical and mental benefits. It combines gentle movements, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve flexibility, strength, and calmness. Whether in a gym, a park, or at home, people of all ages can enjoy yoga and adapt it to their own needs. [1]

The physical benefits of yoga are well known. Regular practice helps stretch muscles, improve balance, and build core strength. Unlike many sports, yoga is low-impact, which means it is easier on the joints. People who sit at desks all day find that yoga helps relieve back pain and tension. Even beginners can benefit from simple poses and gradually progress to more challenging ones. [2]

Yoga is not only about the body; it also supports mental health. Deep breathing during yoga calms the nervous system and reduces stress. Many practitioners report feeling more focused and less anxious after a session. Some studies suggest that yoga can help with sleep problems and mild depression. [3]

There are many styles of yoga, each with a different focus. Hatha yoga is gentle and good for beginners. Vinyasa links movement with breath and feels like a flowing dance. Power yoga is more intense and builds strength. People can choose the style that best fits their goals and fitness level. [4]

Getting started with yoga is easier than many people think. A mat, comfortable clothes, and a few minutes a day are enough to begin. Free online videos and apps make it possible to practice at home. Joining a class can help learners get feedback and stay motivated. [5]

## 选项

A. Yoga has stood the test of time because it offers something for everyone—strength, peace, and a sense of connection between mind and body.
B. For those seeking a gentler way to stay fit, yoga provides a path that is both effective and sustainable.
C. These mental benefits make yoga a powerful tool for managing the stresses of modern life.
D. With so many options, anyone can find a type of yoga that works for them.
E. No matter how you begin, the key is to start slowly and listen to your body.
F. For example, the "downward dog" pose stretches the back and legs while building arm strength.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍瑜伽古老起源、今日全球流行、结合体式呼吸冥想、老少皆宜可因人制宜，空后需总结瑜伽的普适价值。A的"stood the test of time"与"over 5,000 years ago"呼应，"offers something for everyone"与"people of all ages can enjoy"对应，"strength, peace, and connection between mind and body"概括身心双重益处。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举瑜伽的体能益处——柔韧、平衡、核心力量、低冲击、缓解久坐背痛、初学者可循序渐进，空后需总结适合人群。B的"gentler way to stay fit"与"low-impact"呼应，"effective and sustainable"与前文益处对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说瑜伽促进心理健康——深呼吸镇静、减轻压力、提高专注、减少焦虑、或有助于睡眠与轻度抑郁，空后需总结心理益处。C的"These mental benefits"指代前文，"powerful tool for managing the stresses of modern life"概括心理层面的作用。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前列举哈他、流瑜伽、力量瑜伽等不同流派及其特点，空后需总结选择空间。D的"With so many options"与前文多种流派对应，"anyone can find a type of yoga that works for them"与前文"choose the style that best fits"衔接。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说入门简单——垫子舒适衣物、每日几分钟、在线资源、参加课程获得指导，空后需给出建议。E的"No matter how you begin"承接前文多种入门方式，"start slowly and listen to your body"是合理的初学者建议。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the "downward dog" pose...**：以For example开头，为具体体式案例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[2]易被误选因涉及体式，但该空需总结瑜伽对寻求温和健身方式的适用性，而非补充某一姿势的细节。
