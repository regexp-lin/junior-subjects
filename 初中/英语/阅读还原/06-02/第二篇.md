# 06-02 第二篇

**Comics and Creativity: More Than Just Pictures**

Comics are often seen as simple entertainment for children. But they are actually a powerful form of storytelling that combines words and images in unique ways. [1] Comics can tell complex stories, teach difficult concepts, and even help people learn new languages. From superhero adventures to history lessons, comics have something for everyone.

How do comics work? Each panel（画格） is like a moment frozen in time. The space between panels—called the "gutter"（留白）—is where the reader's imagination fills in what happens next. [2] This requires readers to actively connect the images and guess the story between the frames. Unlike films or videos, comics let you control the pace. You can spend as long as you want on a single panel, or flip back to check earlier details. This makes reading comics an active and creative experience.

Comics are also a great tool for education. [3] Teachers use graphic novels（图文小说） to explain science, history, and social issues in ways that are easier for students to understand. Visual storytelling can break down complex ideas into simple steps. Many students who find traditional textbooks boring enjoy learning through comics. Some schools have even added comic-making to their art classes to encourage creativity.

Creating comics helps develop important skills. [4] When you draw a comic, you need to plan the story, design the characters, and decide how to arrange each scene. This exercises your imagination, writing ability, and visual thinking. You do not need to be a great artist—stick figures and simple drawings work perfectly. The focus is on telling a story and expressing your ideas. Many famous writers and filmmakers started by making comics when they were young.

[5] Comics are a rich and flexible art form that deserves more attention. They can entertain, educate, and inspire. Whether you enjoy reading them or creating your own, comics offer a world of possibilities. So the next time you see a comic book, remember—it might be more powerful than it looks.

## 选项

A. In other words, comics are a versatile medium that serves many purposes beyond simple fun.
B. That is because comics demand the reader to participate in building the story.
C. In fact, comics have become an effective teaching tool in many classrooms.
D. This means that making comics can strengthen both your mind and your creativity.
E. In short, comics deserve to be taken seriously as both art and education.
F. For instance, Japanese manga has influenced animation and film around the world.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出漫画的多重功能。空前描述"Comics are often seen as simple entertainment...But they are actually a powerful form of storytelling"，空后列举"Comics can tell complex stories, teach difficult concepts, help people learn new languages...From superhero adventures to history lessons"。A选项"In other words, comics are a versatile medium that serves many purposes beyond simple fun"（换言之，漫画是一种多功能的媒介，其用途远超简单的娱乐）中"In other words"概括空前对漫画的重新定义，"versatile...many purposes beyond simple fun"与空后多重功能精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：漫画阅读的参与性。空前说明"Each panel is like a moment frozen in time...The space between panels—the gutter—is where the reader's imagination fills in what happens next"，空后描述"Unlike films or videos, comics let you control the pace...spend as long as you want...flip back...active and creative experience"。B选项"That is because comics demand the reader to participate in building the story"（那是因为漫画要求读者参与构建故事）中"That is because"解释留白与节奏控制的本质，"participate in building the story"与空后"imagination fills in...control the pace...active"完美呼应，衔接紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：漫画在教育中的运用。空前提出"Comics are also a great tool for education"，空后详述"Teachers use graphic novels to explain science, history, social issues...Visual storytelling can break down complex ideas...schools have even added comic-making"。C选项"In fact, comics have become an effective teaching tool in many classrooms"（事实上，漫画已成为许多课堂中的有效教学工具）中"In fact"加强空前论点，"effective teaching tool in many classrooms"与空后教师使用、学校开设课程等内容精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：创作漫画对能力的锻炼。空前描述"Creating comics helps develop important skills...plan the story, design characters, arrange each scene...exercises imagination, writing ability, visual thinking"，空后说明"You do not need to be a great artist...focus is on telling a story and expressing ideas"。D选项"This means that making comics can strengthen both your mind and your creativity"（这意味着创作漫画能同时锻炼你的思维和创造力）中"This means"承接空前技能发展，"strengthen mind and creativity"与空后"exercises imagination, writing ability, visual thinking"呼应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"rich and flexible art form...can entertain, educate, and inspire...world of possibilities...more powerful than it looks"。E选项"In short, comics deserve to be taken seriously as both art and education"（简言之，漫画值得作为艺术和教育被认真对待）用"In short"总结全文，"taken seriously as both art and education"与空后"entertain, educate, inspire"及"rich art form"呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For instance, Japanese manga has influenced animation and film around the world.**：涉及日本漫画对动画电影的影响，易被误填[3]或[1]。但F以"For instance"引出日本漫画这一地域/类型案例，而空[1]需要的是对漫画多功能性的概括句，F仅涉及文化影响，未涵盖"tell stories, teach concepts, learn languages"等；空[3]讨论的是课堂教育应用，F的"animation and film"属于产业影响，与教学场景不匹配。F属"主题相关但衔接维度错位"型干扰。
