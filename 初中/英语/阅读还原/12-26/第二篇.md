**Cross-Cultural Communication: Building Bridges**

When people from different countries and cultures meet, they bring different customs, values, and ways of speaking. Sometimes misunderstandings happen because of language barriers or different ideas about politeness. However, learning about other cultures helps us connect and work better together. Today, many schools teach cultural awareness, and businesses send staff abroad to learn local practices. [1]

Language is more than just words. Tone, gestures, and body language can mean different things in different cultures. In some places, direct eye contact shows respect; in others, it may be seen as rude. A "yes" might mean agreement in one culture but only politeness in another. Knowing these differences reduces confusion and helps people feel more comfortable with each other. [2]

Listening and asking questions are key skills. Instead of assuming everyone thinks the same way, we should try to understand others' points of view. Showing interest in someone's culture—asking about traditions or festivals—often opens the door to friendship. When we admit we do not know everything, we create space for real learning and trust. [3]

Technology has made cross-cultural exchange easier. Video calls, social media, and online learning connect people across the world. Students can join virtual exchange programmes and talk to peers in other countries. These tools do not replace face-to-face meetings, but they help people start conversations and build relationships before meeting in person. [4]

In a globalised world, cross-cultural skills matter more than ever. They help us avoid conflict, make better decisions, and create more inclusive environments. Whether you travel, study abroad, or work with international teams, investing in cultural understanding pays off for everyone. [5]

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍跨文化接触带来不同习俗与价值观、易产生误解、但学习文化有助于联结与合作，学校与企业重视文化意识，空后需总结跨文化交流的双重性。A的"both challenging and rewarding"与前文呼应，"more people are learning its importance"与前文对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说语言不止于词汇——语调、手势、肢体在不同文化中含义不同，举例眼神与"yes"的差异，空后需总结非语言信号的重要性。B的"paying attention to non-verbal signals"与前文对应，"essential when communicating across cultures"与前文呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说倾听与提问很重要、不假设思维一致、对他人文化表示兴趣可增进友谊、承认未知可建立信任，空后需总结好奇心与谦逊的价值。C的"curiosity and humility"与前文对应，"best tools for building cross-cultural connections"与前文呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说科技促进跨文化交流——视频、社交媒体、在线学习、虚拟交换项目，工具不能取代面对面但有助于开启对话，空后需总结平台带来的便利。D的"these platforms"指代前文，"learn about other cultures without leaving home"与前文对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说全球化下跨文化技能更重要、可避免冲突、改善决策、营造包容环境，无论旅行留学还是国际合作都有回报，空后需总结理解与尊重文化差异的长期价值。E的"ability to understand and respect cultural differences"与前文呼应，"a skill worth developing for life"与前文对应。
