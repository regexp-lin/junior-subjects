# 05-16 第一篇

**Plastic Pollution: A Growing Problem and What We Can Do**

Plastic is everywhere in our daily life—in bottles, bags, packages, and even in our clothes. It is cheap, light, and lasts for a very long time. [1] In fact, that is also the problem: plastic does not disappear easily. It can stay in the environment for hundreds of years. Rivers and oceans are full of plastic waste that harms fish, birds, and other animals. That is why plastic pollution has become one of the biggest environmental challenges of our time.

Where does all this plastic come from? [2] A large part comes from single-use items—things we use once and throw away. Plastic bags, straws, food containers, and drink bottles are examples. In many places, people still throw plastic into rivers or leave it on beaches. Rain and wind can carry plastic from land into the sea. In other words, our daily choices and habits are a major source of plastic pollution.

The harm to wildlife is serious. [3] Animals often mistake plastic for food and eat it, which can block their stomachs or make them sick. Sea turtles get tangled in plastic bags. Birds build nests with plastic strings and may choke their young. As plastic breaks into tiny pieces called microplastics, it enters the food chain and may even reach our plates. For this reason, reducing plastic waste is not just about saving animals—it affects human health too.

What can we do to help? [4] We can bring our own bags when shopping instead of using plastic bags. We can choose drinks in reusable bottles and refuse plastic straws when we do not need them. Supporting shops that use less packaging and joining community clean-up events also make a difference. Small changes by many people can lead to big results. So everyone has a role to play in fighting plastic pollution.

[5] In short, plastic pollution is a serious problem, but we are not helpless. By reducing our use of single-use plastic, reusing what we can, and recycling properly, we can help protect the environment for future generations. Remember: every plastic bag we refuse and every bottle we reuse is a step toward a cleaner planet.

## 选项

A. That is because plastic lasts a very long time and causes great harm to the environment.
B. In other words, our everyday actions are a big cause of plastic pollution.
C. That is why we must act to reduce plastic for the sake of animals and ourselves.
D. It is true that each of us can take simple steps to reduce plastic waste.
E. In conclusion, we can all contribute to solving the plastic problem.
F. For example, some countries have started to use paper bags instead of plastic.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：总领塑料持久性与环境危害。空前描述"Plastic is everywhere...cheap, light, and lasts for a very long time"，空后说明"In fact, that is also the problem: plastic does not disappear easily...It can stay in the environment for hundreds of years...Rivers and oceans are full of plastic waste that harms fish, birds, and other animals...plastic pollution has become one of the biggest environmental challenges"。A选项"That is because plastic lasts a very long time and causes great harm to the environment"（那是因为塑料存留时间极长并对环境造成巨大危害）中"That is because"解释塑料便利性背后的隐患，"lasts...causes great harm"与空后不易分解、长期存留及危害动植物精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：塑料污染的人为来源与日常习惯。空前设问"Where does all this plastic come from?"，空后列举"A large part comes from single-use items...Plastic bags, straws, food containers, drink bottles...people still throw plastic into rivers or leave it on beaches...Rain and wind can carry plastic from land into the sea...our daily choices and habits are a major source of plastic pollution"。B选项"In other words, our everyday actions are a big cause of plastic pollution"（换言之，我们的日常行为是塑料污染的一大来源）中"In other words"概括空后内容，"everyday actions...big cause"与空后一次性用品、乱扔及"daily choices and habits"完美呼应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：减少塑料对动物与人类健康的必要性。空前提出"The harm to wildlife is serious"，空后详述"Animals often mistake plastic for food...block their stomachs or make them sick...Sea turtles get tangled in plastic bags...Birds build nests with plastic strings and may choke their young...microplastics enter the food chain and may reach our plates...reducing plastic waste is not just about saving animals—it affects human health too"。C选项"That is why we must act to reduce plastic for the sake of animals and ourselves"（因此我们必须为动物和我们自己行动起来减少塑料）中"That is why"承接空前及空后结论，"for the sake of animals and ourselves"与空后动物受害及"affects human health"精准对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：个人可采取的减塑行动。空前设问"What can we do to help?"，空后列举"We can bring our own bags when shopping...choose drinks in reusable bottles and refuse plastic straws...Supporting shops that use less packaging and joining community clean-up events...Small changes by many people can lead to big results...everyone has a role to play in fighting plastic pollution"。D选项"It is true that each of us can take simple steps to reduce plastic waste"（的确，我们每个人都能采取简单步骤减少塑料垃圾）中"It is true that"加强衔接，"each of us...simple steps"与空后自备袋、可重复使用瓶、拒吸管、支持减包装及"everyone has a role"完美呼应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与积极行动总结。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"plastic pollution is a serious problem, but we are not helpless...By reducing our use of single-use plastic, reusing what we can, and recycling properly, we can help protect the environment for future generations...every plastic bag we refuse and every bottle we reuse is a step toward a cleaner planet"。E选项"In conclusion, we can all contribute to solving the plastic problem"（总之，我们都能为解决塑料问题做出贡献）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"we can all contribute"与空后减量、再利用、回收及"every...refuse...reuse is a step"形成呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some countries have started to use paper bags instead of plastic.**：涉及用纸袋替代塑料袋，易被误填[2]或[4]。但F讨论的是国家或商家层面的替代材料政策，而空[2]前后文强调个人日常选择与习惯（一次性用品、乱扔行为），空[4]列举的是个人可做的具体行动；F属于政策或商业层面的措施，与空[2]的个人习惯、空[4]的个人步骤论述层级不同。F属"主题相关但论述主体与层级错位"型干扰。
