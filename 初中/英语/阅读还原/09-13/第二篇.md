# 09-13 第二篇

**Exploring the Deep Ocean**

The ocean covers more than 70 percent of our planet, yet we have explored only a small fraction of it. The deep sea—the zone where sunlight cannot reach—is one of the last frontiers on Earth. It is dark, cold, and under great pressure, but it is also full of strange and wonderful life. [1] Unlocking the secrets of the deep ocean could teach us about life in extreme conditions, new medicines, and the history of our planet.

Technology has made deep-ocean exploration possible. Submarines, underwater robots, and special cameras can now reach depths of thousands of meters. Scientists use these tools to map the seafloor, study deep-sea animals, and discover hydrothermal vents where life thrives without sunlight. [2] Advances in technology are gradually revealing a world that was once invisible to us, opening new fields of science and discovery.

The deep ocean holds resources that could be useful. Minerals on the seafloor, genetic material from deep-sea creatures, and potential sources of energy are all subjects of research. [3] Balancing the desire to use these resources with the need to protect fragile ecosystems is one of the great challenges facing ocean exploration today.

Pollution and climate change also affect the deep sea. Plastic waste is sinking to the bottom; warming waters are changing ocean currents. Protecting the deep ocean means reducing pollution and slowing climate change. [4] What happens in the deep ocean matters for the whole planet, and our choices on land have consequences far below the waves.

Young people can play a part in protecting the ocean. Learning about marine life, reducing plastic use, and supporting ocean conservation are steps everyone can take. [5] The next generation of explorers and scientists may hold the key to understanding—and protecting—this vast and mysterious part of our world.

## 选项

A. Discovering what lies beneath the waves could answer questions we have never been able to ask before.
B. These tools have transformed our ability to study the deep sea and to learn from its mysteries.
C. Finding the right balance between use and protection will shape the future of the ocean and of humanity.
D. The deep ocean is connected to the rest of the world, and its health affects us all.
E. Those who care about the ocean today may become the guardians of its future tomorrow.
F. For example, the Mariana Trench is the deepest part of the ocean, reaching nearly 11 kilometers below sea level.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述深海探索的难度与价值，空后强调"Unlocking the secrets of the deep ocean could teach us about life in extreme conditions, new medicines, and the history of our planet"。A的"Discovering what lies beneath the waves could answer questions we have never been able to ask before"概括深海探索的意义，与"teach us"和"secrets"完美呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举潜艇、机器人、相机等深海技术，空后说"Advances in technology are gradually revealing a world that was once invisible to us, opening new fields of science and discovery"。B的"These tools have transformed our ability to study the deep sea and to learn from its mysteries"与技术进步带来新发现的主题一致，概括该段主旨。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论深海资源利用的可能性，空后说"Balancing the desire to use these resources with the need to protect fragile ecosystems is one of the great challenges"。C的"Finding the right balance between use and protection will shape the future of the ocean and of humanity"准确概括开发与保护的平衡，与"great challenges"呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论污染与气候变化对深海的影响，空后说"What happens in the deep ocean matters for the whole planet, and our choices on land have consequences far below the waves"。D的"The deep ocean is connected to the rest of the world, and its health affects us all"与全球关联、陆地选择的远程影响的逻辑对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前列举青少年可参与的保护行动，空后强调"The next generation of explorers and scientists may hold the key to understanding—and protecting—this vast and mysterious part of our world"。E的"Those who care about the ocean today may become the guardians of its future tomorrow"升华主题，与"next generation"和"understanding—and protecting"完美对应，适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Mariana Trench is the deepest part of the ocean, reaching nearly 11 kilometers below sea level.**：以For example开头，属具体地理数据举例；各空均为概括或过渡句，F与深海相关但为个案，易误放[1]或[2]，但两空分别需探索意义概述与技术作用概括，非举例。
