# 09-16 第二篇

**Protecting Wildlife in the Modern World**

Wildlife faces many threats today. Habitat loss, pollution, and climate change are putting animals at risk. Some species have already disappeared, and many more could follow if we do not take action. [1] Understanding why wildlife matters and how we can help is the first step toward making a real difference for the planet's creatures.

Habitat destruction is perhaps the biggest danger. When forests are cut down or wetlands are filled in, animals lose their homes and food sources. They must move to new areas or starve. [2] Protecting natural spaces and creating wildlife reserves give endangered species safe places to live and breed.

Hunting and illegal trade also harm wildlife. Some people hunt animals for sport or for body parts that are sold at high prices. Laws against poaching exist, but they are not always enforced. [3] Stronger laws and better enforcement are needed to stop the illegal hunting and trading of protected animals.

Pollution affects wildlife in many ways. Plastic waste in the oceans harms sea creatures. Chemicals in rivers and lakes poison fish and the birds that eat them. [4] Reducing pollution and cleaning up our environment helps restore healthy ecosystems where wildlife can thrive once again.

Everyone can play a part in saving wildlife. We can support conservation groups, reduce our use of single-use plastic, and choose products that do not harm animals or their habitats. [5] Small actions by many people add up to big changes, and together we can give wildlife a better chance to survive and flourish.

## 选项

A. Raising awareness and taking responsibility for conservation are essential for the survival of countless species.
B. Setting aside land for wildlife is one of the most effective ways to ensure animals have a future.
C. Without stricter measures, the illegal wildlife trade will continue to push many species toward extinction.
D. A cleaner environment means healthier habitats and a greater chance of recovery for affected species.
E. Individual choices and collective efforts can create a powerful force for wildlife protection.
F. For example, the giant panda was once critically endangered but has improved thanks to protection programs in China.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前列举野生动物面临的威胁（栖息地丧失、污染、气候变化），空后引出采取行动的必要性。A的"Raising awareness"与"Understanding why wildlife matters"呼应，"taking responsibility for conservation"自然过渡到"how we can help"。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说栖息地破坏使动物失去家园，空后强调保护自然空间和建立保护区的意义。B的"Setting aside land for wildlife"与" Protecting natural spaces and creating wildlife reserves"同义，有效概括本段核心措施。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前指出盗猎法律存在但执行不力，空后呼吁更强法律和更好执行。C的"Without stricter measures"承接执法不足，"illegal wildlife trade will continue"与"stop the illegal hunting and trading"逻辑一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前列举污染对海洋生物、河流鱼类及鸟类的影响，空后说减少污染有助于恢复健康生态系统。D的"A cleaner environment"概括减排和清理，"healthier habitats and recovery"与"restore healthy ecosystems"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前列举个人可做的具体行动（支持保护组织、减少塑料、选择无害产品），空后说众人小行动汇聚成大改变。E的"Individual choices and collective efforts"精准概括，"powerful force for wildlife protection"与"give wildlife a better chance"呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the giant panda was once critically endangered but has improved thanks to protection programs in China.**：以For example开头，是具体物种案例；易误放[2]或[5]，但[2]需概括性保护措施，[5]需个人与集体行动的概括，非单一物种的成功故事。
