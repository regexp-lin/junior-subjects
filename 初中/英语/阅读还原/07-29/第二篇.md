# 07-29 第二篇

**A Healthy Breakfast**

Breakfast is often called the most important meal of the day, and for good reason. After a long night of sleep, your body needs fuel to start the morning. [1] Skipping breakfast can leave you tired and unable to concentrate. A proper morning meal sets you up for a productive day.

What makes a healthy breakfast? [2] Aim for a balance of protein, fiber, and healthy fats. Eggs, yogurt, and nuts provide protein that keeps you full. Whole grains and fruits add fiber and natural sugars for energy. Avoid sugary cereals and pastries—they cause energy crashes later.

The timing of breakfast matters too. [3] Try to eat within an hour of waking up. This helps regulate your metabolism and blood sugar. If you are not hungry early, start with something small like a banana or a glass of milk. Consistency is more important than perfection.

Busy mornings make it tempting to skip breakfast. [4] Prepare ingredients the night before, or keep simple options like oatmeal and fruit on hand. A breakfast smoothie takes only a few minutes to make. Planning ahead removes the excuse of "no time."

A good breakfast benefits both body and mind. [5] Students who eat breakfast tend to perform better in school. Adults who eat breakfast often have more energy and make healthier choices throughout the day. Making breakfast a habit is one of the simplest ways to improve your overall health.

## 选项

A. Those who skip it often feel sluggish and find it hard to focus.
B. A mix of nutrients gives your body what it needs to function well.
C. Eating soon after waking helps your body work properly all day.
D. With a little planning, you can fit breakfast into any schedule.
E. The benefits extend to learning, work, and daily decision-making.
F. For example, many people enjoy a cup of coffee in the morning.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说明身体经过一夜需要能量；空后强调正确早餐带来高效一天。A 项“不吃早餐的人常感到困倦且难以集中注意力”承接“跳过早餐”的不良后果，与后文形成对比。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前提问什么是健康早餐；空后具体列出蛋白质、纤维、健康脂肪及各类食物。B 项“多种营养素的搭配为身体提供良好运作所需”概括健康早餐的组成原则，与后文总分一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前提出早餐时间的重要性；空后建议起床后一小时内进食并说明原因。C 项“醒来后不久进食有助于身体全天正常运作”概括时间与代谢、血糖的关系，衔接流畅。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前指出早晨忙碌易导致不吃早餐；空后给出应对策略（提前准备、燕麦水果、奶昔等）。D 项“稍作规划，就能把早餐纳入任何日程”总结该段主旨，与后文具体建议对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说良好早餐对身心有益；空后举例学生成绩更好、成人精力更足、选择更健康。E 项“益处延伸到学习、工作和日常决策”概括该段主旨，与后文举例呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, many people enjoy a cup of coffee...**：咖啡虽与早餐相关，但未涉及营养、时间或规划等核心论述，仅为日常习惯举例，与各空位逻辑不符。
