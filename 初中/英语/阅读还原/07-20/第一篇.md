# 07-20 第一篇

**How Movies Shape Our Thoughts and Feelings**

Movies are one of the most popular forms of entertainment in the world. We watch them for fun, to escape reality for a while, or to experience stories that move us. But films do more than entertain—they can influence how we think, feel, and even behave. [1] Understanding this power helps us become more thoughtful viewers. Movies can introduce us to new cultures, spark our imagination, and open our minds to different perspectives. A single film can stay with us long after we leave the cinema, shaping our opinions and conversations.

[2] Movies often affect our emotions in powerful ways. A well-told story can make us laugh, cry, or feel afraid. Films use music, camera angles, and editing to create these emotional responses. When we identify with a character, we may feel their joy or sadness as if it were our own. This emotional connection is why movies can inspire us, comfort us, or help us process our own experiences.

[3] Films can also influence our beliefs and attitudes about the world. The characters we admire, the values they represent, and the choices they make can shape our own views. Movies can challenge stereotypes, raise awareness about important issues, or—if we are not careful—reinforce harmful ideas. That is why it matters what kinds of stories we watch and how we think about them afterward.

[4] For young people especially, movies can play a role in identity formation. Teens often look to characters in films as models for how to act, dress, or handle difficult situations. Parents and educators sometimes worry about negative influences, but films can also present positive examples of courage, kindness, and resilience. The key is to discuss what we watch and to think critically about the messages films send.

[5] Movies are a powerful medium that can enrich our lives when we approach them with awareness. By choosing films thoughtfully and reflecting on their messages, we can enjoy the art of cinema while staying mindful of its influence. Whether we watch for entertainment or for deeper meaning, understanding how movies affect us helps us get the most from the experience.

## 选项

A. Being aware of this influence allows us to enjoy films more consciously and critically.
B. The emotional impact of films comes from the way they combine story, sound, and image.
C. The values and ideas we see on screen can shape our views in both positive and negative ways.
D. Films can serve as both mirrors and models for young viewers as they figure out who they are.
E. Approaching movies with reflection and choice helps us benefit from their power responsibly.
F. For example, many people have been inspired to travel after watching films set in other countries.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前指出电影不仅娱乐还能影响我们的思维与行为，空后列举电影可介绍文化、激发想象、拓展视角等。A 句 "Being aware of this influence" 承接前文对电影影响力的讨论，"enjoy films more consciously and critically" 自然过渡到后文对电影多重作用的展开，承上启下。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：段落主题是电影对情绪的影响。空后描述笑声、泪水、恐惧，以及配乐、镜头、剪辑如何制造这些反应。B 句 "The emotional impact... combine story, sound, and image" 准确概括本段核心机制。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：段落讨论电影如何影响我们的信念和态度。空后提到角色、价值观可挑战刻板印象或强化偏见。C 句 "The values and ideas we see on screen can shape our views in both positive and negative ways" 精准对应本段主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：段落讲电影在青少年身份形成中的作用。空后提到青少年以角色为榜样，电影既有消极影响也有积极榜样。D 句 "mirrors and models for young viewers as they figure out who they are" 与段落内容完全吻合。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：结尾段总结电影的影响力，建议以自觉、反思的方式观影。E 句 "Approaching movies with reflection and choice helps us benefit from their power responsibly" 与前文 "awareness"、"choosing thoughtfully"、"reflecting" 自然衔接。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：虽说明电影影响（激发旅行），但属于具体举例，应嵌入第 1 段 "open our minds to different perspectives" 或 "introduce us to new cultures" 附近。作为独立选项，其层级为例证而非段落概括，易因主题相关而误选。
