# 06-08 第二篇

**Why Good Neighbours Matter**

In a world of busy schedules and screen time, it is easy to forget about the people who live next door. Yet strong neighbourhood connections can make a real difference in our lives. [1] In other words, good neighbours create a sense of community that makes us feel safer, happier, and more supported. Building a positive relationship with those around us does not require much—often a smile, a greeting, or a small act of kindness is enough to start.

Trust grows from small actions. [2] When you borrow sugar, return a package that was delivered to the wrong door, or water a neighbour's plants while they travel, you show that you care. These acts build trust over time. People who trust their neighbours are more likely to help each other in emergencies—whether it is watching a child for a few minutes or calling for help when someone is unwell. A friendly neighbourhood becomes a network of support that everyone can rely on.

Neighbourhood activities strengthen bonds. [3] Block parties, community gardens, or simple gatherings in a shared yard bring people together. Children make friends with kids nearby instead of staying indoors alone. Older residents feel less lonely when they can chat with others on the street. Working together on a neighbourhood project—like planting trees or cleaning a common space—creates shared memories and a sense of pride. These activities turn a group of strangers living close by into a real community.

Conflict can happen anywhere. [4] Noise, parking, or different lifestyles sometimes cause tension between neighbours. The key is to address problems calmly and respectfully—perhaps by talking in person rather than complaining online. Most issues can be solved with honest communication. Avoiding each other or spreading rumours only makes things worse. A neighbourhood where people can talk through disagreements is a healthier place for everyone.

[5] Good neighbours are a valuable resource. They make our daily lives more pleasant and our communities more resilient. You do not need to become best friends with everyone on your street—a little kindness and communication can go a long way. So the next time you see a neighbour, say hello. You might be surprised how much that simple act can change the atmosphere around you.

## 选项

A. In other words, neighbourly connections improve our wellbeing and sense of belonging.
B. That is because everyday kindness builds trust that pays off when help is needed.
C. In fact, shared activities and events help turn neighbours into a real community.
D. These situations require patience and good communication to resolve.
E. In short, investing in neighbour relations benefits everyone in the area.
F. For example, some cities offer programmes that match new residents with local volunteers.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出邻里关系对幸福感与归属感的提升。空前描述"busy schedules and screen time...easy to forget people next door...strong neighbourhood connections can make real difference"，空后说明"good neighbours create sense of community...feel safer, happier, more supported...Building positive relationship...smile, greeting, small act of kindness enough"。A选项"In other words, neighbourly connections improve our wellbeing and sense of belonging"（换言之，邻里关系提升我们的幸福感和归属感）中"In other words"概括空前价值，"wellbeing and sense of belonging"与空后"safer, happier, supported...community"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：日常善意如何建立信任并在需要时发挥作用。空前提出"Trust grows from small actions"，空后列举"borrow sugar, return package, water plants...show you care...build trust over time...trust neighbours more likely to help in emergencies...watching child, calling for help...network of support"。B选项"That is because everyday kindness builds trust that pays off when help is needed"（那是因为日常善意建立信任，在需要帮助时会有回报）中"That is because"解释信任来源，"everyday kindness...pays off when help needed"与空后借糖、还包裹、浇水及紧急互助完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：共同活动将邻里转变为真正社区。空前提出"Neighbourhood activities strengthen bonds"，空后详述"Block parties, community gardens, gatherings...bring people together...Children make friends...Older residents less lonely...Working together on project...planting trees, cleaning...shared memories, pride...strangers into real community"。C选项"In fact, shared activities and events help turn neighbours into a real community"（事实上，共同的活动和事件有助于将邻居转变为真正的社区）中"In fact"强化活动作用，"turn neighbours into real community"与空后"strangers into real community"精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：邻里冲突需要耐心与沟通来解决。空前提出"Conflict can happen anywhere"，空后描述"Noise, parking, lifestyles...cause tension...address problems calmly and respectfully...talking in person rather than complaining online...honest communication...Avoiding or spreading rumours makes worse...talk through disagreements...healthier place"。D选项"These situations require patience and good communication to resolve"（这些情况需要耐心和良好沟通来解决）中"These situations"指代空后冲突类型，"patience and good communication"与空后 calmly、respectfully、talking in person、honest communication 完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与邻里投入的益处。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"Good neighbours valuable resource...daily lives more pleasant...communities more resilient...do not need to become best friends...little kindness and communication go long way...say hello...change atmosphere"。E选项"In short, investing in neighbour relations benefits everyone in the area"（简言之，在邻里关系上投入对社区每个人都有益）用"In short"总结全文，"investing...benefits everyone"与空后"valuable resource...resilient...kindness goes long way"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some cities offer programmes that match new residents with local volunteers.**：涉及城市为新居民配对志愿者的项目，易被误填[3]或[5]。但F以"For example"引出政府或机构项目，而空[3]讨论的是邻里自发活动（街区派对、社区花园、一起植树等），F的"city programmes"属于自上而下的安排，与"neighbourhood activities"的草根性质不同；空[5]总结个人层面的善意与沟通，F侧重城市政策，与"you...say hello"的个体行动焦点不符。F属"主题相关但主体层级错位"型干扰。
