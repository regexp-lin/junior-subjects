# 09-14 第二篇

**The Power of Friendship**

Friends are the people we choose to share our lives with. They celebrate our successes, comfort us when we struggle, and make ordinary days feel special. Research shows that strong friendships improve our health, happiness, and even how long we live. [1] Investing time and care in our friendships is one of the most rewarding things we can do for ourselves and for those we call friends.

True friendship is built on trust. We share our thoughts, our fears, and our dreams with people we believe will listen without judging. Being a good friend means keeping confidences, showing up when it matters, and apologizing when we make mistakes. [2] Trust grows when both people feel safe to be themselves, and that safety makes the bond between friends strong and lasting.

Friendships take effort. Busy schedules, moving to new places, and changing interests can pull friends apart. Staying in touch—through messages, calls, or meetups—takes time and intention. [3] Like a plant that needs water and sunlight, friendship flourishes when we give it the attention it deserves.

Conflict is normal in any relationship. Friends may disagree or hurt each other without meaning to. What matters is how we handle those moments—by talking honestly, listening with an open heart, and forgiving when we can. [4] Working through disagreements, rather than avoiding them, often deepens friendship and makes it more resilient.

Friendship is a gift we give and receive. It enriches our lives in ways that material things cannot. [5] Cherishing our friends and being a good friend in return is a choice that pays back in joy, support, and a life that feels richer and more meaningful.

## 选项

A. Making friendship a priority brings benefits that reach far beyond casual fun and companionship.
B. This sense of safety and acceptance is what allows friendships to deepen and endure over time.
C. Without regular care and contact, even the closest friendships can fade away.
D. Honest communication and willingness to repair hurt can strengthen friendships rather than weaken them.
E. Choosing to value and nurture friendship is one of the wisest investments we can make in our own wellbeing.
F. For example, studies show that people with strong social connections tend to live longer and recover faster from illness.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述友谊的价值及研究结论，空后强调"Investing time and care in our friendships is one of the most rewarding things we can do"。A的"Making friendship a priority brings benefits that reach far beyond casual fun and companionship"概括重视友谊的回报，与"rewarding"和"health, happiness"完美呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论信任、倾诉与作为好朋友的含义，空后说"Trust grows when both people feel safe to be themselves, and that safety makes the bond between friends strong and lasting"。B的"This sense of safety and acceptance is what allows friendships to deepen and endure over time"与安全感、信任、持久纽带的逻辑一致，概括该段主旨。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论维持友谊需要努力，空后说"Like a plant that needs water and sunlight, friendship flourishes when we give it the attention it deserves"。C的"Without regular care and contact, even the closest friendships can fade away"从反面强调维护的必要性，与"flourishes when we give it attention"呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论冲突处理与修复，空后说"Working through disagreements, rather than avoiding them, often deepens friendship and makes it more resilient"。D的"Honest communication and willingness to repair hurt can strengthen friendships rather than weaken them"准确概括积极面对分歧的作用，与"working through"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前总结友谊的珍贵，空后强调"Cherishing our friends and being a good friend in return is a choice that pays back in joy, support, and a life that feels richer and more meaningful"。E的"Choosing to value and nurture friendship is one of the wisest investments we can make in our own wellbeing"升华主题，与"choice that pays back"完美对应，适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, studies show that people with strong social connections tend to live longer and recover faster from illness.**：以For example开头，属研究结论举例；各空均为概括或过渡句，F与友谊健康益处相关但为具体数据，易误放[1]，但该空需投资友谊的概括性论述，非举例。
