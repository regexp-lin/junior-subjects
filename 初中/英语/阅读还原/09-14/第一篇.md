# 09-14 第一篇

**Protecting Cultural Heritage**

Every culture has treasures—ancient buildings, traditional dances, historic documents, and stories passed down through generations. These form our cultural heritage, the shared memory of who we are and where we come from. Yet war, neglect, natural disasters, and rapid development threaten to destroy this heritage. [1] Preserving what we have inherited from the past is vital for understanding our history, honoring our ancestors, and building a sense of identity for future generations.

Some heritage sites are famous—the Great Wall of China, the Pyramids of Egypt, the temples of Angkor. But small places matter too: a neighborhood shrine, a village square, a family recipe. [2] Whether grand or humble, these places and traditions connect us to our roots and remind us of the values and stories that shaped our communities.

Protection takes many forms. Governments can list sites as protected monuments and fund repairs. Communities can teach young people traditional skills and languages. Individuals can visit, learn, and share what they know. [3] A combination of legal protection, community effort, and individual care gives heritage the best chance of survival in a changing world.

Tourism can both help and harm heritage. Visitors bring money and attention, which can fund restoration. But too many visitors can wear down ancient floors, touch fragile walls, or disturb local life. [4] Managing tourism responsibly—limiting numbers, teaching respect, and sharing benefits with local people—can turn heritage sites into sources of pride and income without destroying what makes them special.

Cultural heritage belongs to all of us. It tells us where we have been and can guide us toward where we are going. [5] The work of preserving it is never finished, but every effort we make today adds to the legacy we leave for those who come after us.

## 选项

A. Keeping our cultural heritage safe from loss and destruction is a duty we owe to the past and the future.
B. All levels of heritage, from world-famous monuments to local traditions, deserve our attention and care.
C. When different actors work together, heritage stands a better chance of being passed on to the next generation.
D. Finding ways to welcome visitors while protecting what they come to see is a challenge that many sites now face.
E. Each generation has a role to play in safeguarding the treasures that define who we are as a people.
F. For example, the ancient city of Pompeii was buried by a volcanic eruption but has been partially excavated and preserved for visitors.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述文化遗产及其面临威胁，空后强调"Preserving what we have inherited from the past is vital for understanding our history, honoring our ancestors, and building a sense of identity"。A的"Keeping our cultural heritage safe from loss and destruction is a duty we owe to the past and the future"概括保护遗产的责任，与"vital"和"future generations"完美呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前对比著名遗址与小型场所，空后说"Whether grand or humble, these places and traditions connect us to our roots"。B的"All levels of heritage, from world-famous monuments to local traditions, deserve our attention and care"与大小遗产同等重要的主题一致，概括该段主旨。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举政府、社区、个人的多方保护措施，空后说"A combination of legal protection, community effort, and individual care gives heritage the best chance of survival"。C的"When different actors work together, heritage stands a better chance of being passed on to the next generation"与多方协作的逻辑对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论旅游对遗产的双重影响，空后说"Managing tourism responsibly—limiting numbers, teaching respect, and sharing benefits with local people—can turn heritage sites into sources of pride and income without destroying what makes them special"。D的"Finding ways to welcome visitors while protecting what they come to see is a challenge that many sites now face"准确概括旅游业与保护之间的平衡，与" Managing tourism responsibly"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前总结遗产的普遍意义，空后强调"The work of preserving it is never finished, but every effort we make today adds to the legacy we leave for those who come after us"。E的"Each generation has a role to play in safeguarding the treasures that define who we are as a people"升华主题，与"legacy"和"those who come after us"完美对应，适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the ancient city of Pompeii was buried by a volcanic eruption but has been partially excavated and preserved for visitors.**：以For example开头，属具体遗址举例；各空均为概括或过渡句，F与遗产保护相关但为个案，易误放[1]或[4]，但两空分别需保护意义概述与旅游管理概括，非举例。
