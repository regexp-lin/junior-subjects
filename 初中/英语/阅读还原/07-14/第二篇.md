# 07-14 第二篇

**The Meaning of Travel in Our Lives**

Travel has always been a way for people to discover new places and cultures. In the past, only the wealthy could afford long journeys. Today, with planes, trains, and the internet, more people than ever can explore the world. Travel broadens our minds and challenges us to see life from different perspectives. It teaches us that our way of living is just one of many. [1]

[2] When we travel, we step outside our comfort zone. We may struggle with a new language, try unfamiliar foods, or navigate unknown streets. These challenges force us to adapt and grow. Many travelers say that their most memorable experiences came from moments when things did not go as planned.

[3] Travel also deepens our understanding of history and culture. Walking through ancient ruins, visiting museums, and talking with locals bring textbook knowledge to life. We learn not only about other places but also about ourselves. Reflecting on our own culture from a distance often gives us fresh insights.

[4] In recent years, however, "overtourism" has become a concern. Some popular destinations suffer from too many visitors, damaging natural sites and disrupting local life. Travelers can make a difference by choosing off-season trips, respecting local customs, and supporting small businesses. Responsible travel ensures that beautiful places remain for future generations.

[5] Whether we explore a neighboring city or a distant country, travel changes us. It builds confidence, creates lasting memories, and connects us to people across the globe. In a world that often feels divided, these connections remind us of our shared humanity.

## 选项

A. This is why travel remains one of the most valuable experiences we can have.
B. One of travel's greatest gifts is the opportunity to grow through challenge.
C. Beyond personal growth, travel offers cultural and historical learning.
D. As travel becomes more common, we must also consider its impact.
E. The benefits of travel go far beyond the photographs we bring home.
F. For example, the Eiffel Tower receives millions of visitors each year.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：第一段从历史到现代谈旅行普及，强调旅行开阔眼界、换位思考、让我们认识生活方式多元。空格前完成对旅行价值的阐述。选项A "This is why travel remains one of the most valuable experiences we can have" 承接"teaches us"的结论，总结价值，承上启下。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段讲走出舒适区、面对挑战、适应成长、意外经历难忘。选项B "One of travel's greatest gifts is the opportunity to grow through challenge" 概括本段核心——通过挑战成长，与后文"challenges force us to adapt and grow"相呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段讲历史文化学习：遗迹、博物馆、与当地人交流、反思自身文化。选项C "Beyond personal growth, travel offers cultural and historical learning" 用"Beyond"从个人成长过渡到文化历史维度，层次清晰。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段转向 overtourism 问题及负责任旅行。空格前为旅行益处，本段为反思。选项D "As travel becomes more common, we must also consider its impact" 承接"more people than ever"的现实，自然引出负面影响的讨论。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结：旅行改变我们、建立信心、创造回忆、连接全球。选项E "The benefits of travel go far beyond the photographs we bring home" 升华主题，指出旅行收获超越表面，收束全文。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Eiffel Tower receives millions of visitors each year.**：与 overtourism 相关，可作例证。但第四段空格在段首，需引出"过度旅游"这一话题的过渡句，而非具体案例；且埃菲尔铁塔仅为一例，插在段首会让段落重心偏移。属层次错位型干扰。
