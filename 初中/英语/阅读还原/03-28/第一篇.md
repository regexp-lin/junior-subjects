# 03-28 第一篇

**Why Breakfast Matters for Students**

Many students rush out the door in the morning without eating breakfast. They think they are saving time, but skipping the first meal of the day can actually hurt their performance at school. [1] Your brain needs fuel to work well, and breakfast provides the energy you need to think clearly and stay focused during morning classes.

Studies have shown that students who eat breakfast do better on tests than those who do not. When you go without food for many hours, your blood sugar drops and your body feels tired. [2] A simple breakfast like oatmeal, eggs, or fruit can give you the nutrients your brain needs to learn and remember new information.

Breakfast also affects your mood. Students who skip breakfast often feel irritable or impatient by mid-morning. [3] When you start the day with a healthy meal, you are more likely to stay calm and positive, which helps you get along better with classmates and teachers.

Some students say they are not hungry in the morning. [4] Your body gets used to eating at certain times, so if you start having a light breakfast every day, your appetite will gradually adjust. Even a small banana or a piece of toast is better than nothing.

[5] Try waking up ten minutes earlier to prepare something quick, or pack a breakfast you can eat on the way to school. Making breakfast a habit is one of the best things you can do for your grades and your health.

## 选项

A. In fact, your hunger in the morning can be trained over time.
B. Therefore, schools should require all students to eat breakfast at school every day.
C. The solution is simpler than you might think—you just need to plan ahead a little.
D. Without breakfast, your brain runs low on energy, just like a car without gasoline.
E. Eating breakfast helps balance your mood and keeps you in a better state for the whole morning.
F. Having something to eat in the morning can prevent this and keep your mind sharp.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] D   [2] F   [3] E   [4] A   [5] C

## 深度解析

- **[1] D**：
  - **线索**：比喻说明与语义呼应。空前指出"Your brain needs fuel to work well"，强调大脑需要能量。D选项"Without breakfast, your brain runs low on energy, just like a car without gasoline"（不吃早餐，大脑会像没油的汽车一样能量不足）用"car without gasoline"的比喻生动阐释空前的"need fuel"。空后"breakfast provides the energy you need"与D中"runs low on energy"形成对比衔接——不吃则缺能，吃则供能。

- **[2] F**：
  - **线索**：因果递进与代词指代。空前描述不吃早餐的后果——血糖下降、身体疲劳。F选项"Having something to eat in the morning can prevent this and keep your mind sharp"（早上吃点东西能避免这种情况并保持思维敏捷）中"prevent this"直接指代前文的"blood sugar drops""feel tired"。"keep your mind sharp"与空后"learn and remember new information"形成同义呼应，且空后举例"oatmeal, eggs, or fruit"正是"something to eat"的具体化。

- **[3] E**：
  - **线索**：主题衔接与同义对应。空前说跳过早餐的学生容易"feel irritable or impatient by mid-morning"。E选项"Eating breakfast helps balance your mood and keeps you in a better state for the whole morning"（吃早餐有助于平衡情绪，让你整个上午都保持更好状态）直接回应情绪主题。空后"When you start the day with a healthy meal, you are more likely to stay calm and positive"与E中"balance your mood""better state"完美对应，"calm and positive"正是"better state"的具体表现。

- **[4] A**：
  - **线索**：问题—解答与逻辑衔接。空前提出学生常见借口"they are not hungry in the morning"。A选项"In fact, your hunger in the morning can be trained over time"（事实上，早上的饥饿感可以随时间被培养出来）用"In fact"转折回应，破除"不饿"的借口。空后"Your body gets used to eating at certain times...your appetite will gradually adjust"正是对A中"can be trained over time"的展开说明，逻辑严密。

- **[5] C**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与行动号召。空[5]位于结尾段段首，前文已论证早餐对成绩、情绪、习惯的重要性。C选项"The solution is simpler than you might think—you just need to plan ahead a little"（解决办法比你想的简单——你只需稍微提前规划）用"The solution"总结前文问题，引出空后的具体建议"waking up ten minutes earlier""pack a breakfast"。空后"Try...or pack..."正是"plan ahead a little"的实操体现。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **B. Therefore, schools should require all students to eat breakfast at school every day.**：含"schools""students""breakfast"等文章关键词和因果连词"Therefore"。但全文从未讨论学校强制要求吃早餐，而是从个人角度呼吁学生重视早餐。该选项属"过度引申+偷换主语"型干扰——将个人选择偷换为学校政策，且"require"与全文温和建议的语气不符。
