**Earth Day: A Global Day for Our Planet**

Every year on April 22, people around the world celebrate Earth Day. This special day was first held in 1970 in the United States, when millions of people came together to demand action to protect the environment. [1] Earth Day has grown into one of the largest environmental events on the planet, with over a billion people in more than 190 countries taking part each year. Schools, businesses, and communities organize activities to raise awareness about climate change, pollution, and the importance of protecting nature.

What do people do on Earth Day? The activities are as varied as the people who join them. [2] In many cities, volunteers gather to plant trees, clean up parks and beaches, and remove litter from rivers. Students often learn about recycling, saving energy, and how small daily choices can make a big difference. Some communities hold marches or festivals to draw attention to environmental issues. The goal is to encourage everyone to think about their impact on the Earth and to take action.

Young people play a key role in Earth Day. [3] Many schools use this day to teach students about sustainability（可持续性） and to start environmental projects. Students might build a school garden, set up recycling bins, or organize a "lights off" hour to save electricity. These activities help young people feel that they can make a difference. Some teenagers have even started their own environmental clubs and led campaigns in their communities.

Earth Day is not just about one day. [4] The real purpose is to inspire lasting habits—such as using reusable bags, turning off lights when leaving a room, and choosing to walk or bike instead of driving short distances. Many people use Earth Day as a starting point to make changes that last all year. Small steps, when taken by millions of people, can add up to a real impact on our planet.

[5] Earth Day reminds us that we all share one planet and that each of us has a responsibility to protect it. Whether you plant a tree, join a cleanup, or simply learn something new about the environment, you are part of a global movement. Together, we can build a healthier future for ourselves and for generations to come.

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出地球日的规模与影响力。空前描述"1970...millions of people came together to demand action to protect the environment"，空后说明"has grown into one of the largest environmental events...over a billion people in more than 190 countries...Schools, businesses, and communities organize activities"。A选项"In other words, it has become a worldwide movement that brings people together for a common cause"（换言之，它已成为一个将人们团结起来为共同事业奋斗的全球运动）中"In other words"概括空前起源，"worldwide movement...common cause"与空后"billion people...190 countries...organize activities"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：地球日活动的多样性与可参与性。空前提出"What do people do on Earth Day? The activities are as varied as the people who join them"，空后列举"volunteers gather to plant trees, clean up parks and beaches...Students learn about recycling...communities hold marches or festivals...encourage everyone to think...take action"。B选项"That is because Earth Day activities are designed to be practical and easy for everyone to join"（那是因为地球日活动被设计得实用且便于人人参与）中"That is because"解释活动多样的原因，"practical and easy for everyone"与空后植树、清洁、学习、游行等各类人群可参与的活动对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：学校在地球日中的角色。空前提出"Young people play a key role in Earth Day"，空后详述"Many schools use this day to teach...sustainability...start environmental projects...build school garden, set up recycling bins, lights off hour...teenagers started environmental clubs"。C选项"In fact, schools often use Earth Day as a chance to involve students in hands-on environmental projects"（事实上，学校常把地球日作为让学生参与动手环保项目的机会）中"In fact"加强空前论点，"hands-on environmental projects"与空后建花园、设回收桶、关灯、环保社团等实践项目精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：地球日与日常习惯的延续。空前说明"Earth Day is not just about one day"，空后列举"inspire lasting habits—reusable bags, turn off lights, walk or bike...use Earth Day as a starting point to make changes that last all year"。D选项"Its main message is that we should carry the spirit of Earth Day into our everyday lives"（其主要信息是应将地球日的精神带入日常生活）中"main message"呼应空前"not just about one day"，"carry...into everyday lives"与空后"lasting habits...changes that last all year"完美呼应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"we all share one planet...responsibility to protect it...plant a tree, join a cleanup, or learn...part of a global movement...build a healthier future"。E选项"In short, Earth Day is a powerful reminder that we can all contribute to a better world"（简言之，地球日有力地提醒我们都能为更美好的世界作出贡献）用"In short"总结全文，"contribute to a better world"与空后"responsibility...global movement...healthier future"呼应，收束精准。
