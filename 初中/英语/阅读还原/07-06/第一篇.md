# 07-06 第一篇

**Saving Water: Every Drop Counts**

Water is essential for life. We need it to drink, to grow food, and to keep ourselves clean. [1] Despite its importance, fresh water is a limited resource, and many places around the world already face water shortages. Climate change is making droughts more common. As the world's population grows, the demand for water increases. If we do not use water wisely today, future generations may not have enough. Saving water is not just a choice—it is a responsibility we all share. Every small action we take can make a difference.

[2] At home, there are many simple ways to use less water. Turn off the tap while you brush your teeth or soap your hands. Fix leaky faucets and pipes—a small drip can waste gallons of water over time. Take shorter showers instead of long baths. When washing dishes, fill the sink instead of letting the water run. These habits require almost no extra effort, but they can save a significant amount of water each month. Encourage your family members to do the same and make saving water a household habit.

[3] In the garden and yard, water can be saved too. Water plants early in the morning or late in the evening when less water evaporates in the heat. Use a watering can or drip system instead of a hose, which often wastes water. Choose plants that need less water and can survive in your local climate. Collecting rainwater in a barrel is another great idea—you can use it for watering plants instead of tap water. A little planning can reduce outdoor water use without harming your plants.

[4] Schools and communities can also play a big role. Schools can teach students about the importance of water conservation and install water-efficient fixtures. Communities can organize campaigns to raise awareness and reward families that save water. Reporting leaky public pipes or broken fountains helps the whole community. When everyone works together, the impact multiplies. Young people can be powerful advocates—you can start a water-saving club at school or share tips with friends and neighbors.

[5] Saving water is a habit that benefits everyone. The water we save today remains available for future use. It also reduces the energy needed to treat and transport water, which helps the environment. Start with one or two changes and add more over time. Remember that you do not have to be perfect—every effort counts. By valuing every drop, we show respect for our planet and for the people who will live on it tomorrow. Together, we can ensure that clean water remains available for all.

## 选项

A. Yet fresh water is limited, and we must act now to protect this precious resource.
B. Simple changes at home can significantly reduce your water usage.
C. Outdoor water use can also be reduced with smart gardening practices.
D. Schools and communities can work together to promote water conservation.
E. Making water-saving a habit benefits both people and the planet.
F. For example, a running toilet can waste up to 200 gallons of water per day.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前强调水的重要性（饮用、种植、清洁），空后转折指出淡水有限、多地缺水、气候变化、人口增长、未来可能不足。A选项"Despite its importance, fresh water is a limited resource, and many places around the world already face water shortages"完美承上启下：用"Despite its importance"承接前文，转折引出淡水有限与缺水的现实，与后文"act to save"紧密衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段列举家庭节水方法——关水龙头、修漏、短淋浴、洗碗方式，强调简单习惯即可省水。B选项"Simple changes at home can significantly reduce your water usage"准确概括段落主题。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段聚焦户外/园艺节水——早晚浇水、滴灌、耐旱植物、雨水收集。C选项"Outdoor water use can also be reduced with smart gardening practices"精准总结段落主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讨论学校与社区的作用——教育、节水设施、宣传活动、报告泄漏、青年倡导。D选项"Schools and communities can work together to promote water conservation"准确概括段落核心。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结全文，强调节水习惯的益处——留给未来、节约能源、从小事做起。E选项"Making water-saving a habit benefits both people and the planet"精准呼应段落的总结性主旨。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：以"For example"开头，举漏水马桶每天浪费约200加仑的例子。虽与家庭节水相关，但它是支撑第二段"修漏"论点的具体论据，而非段落的概括句。若放入[2]会造成层次错位——F是例证层，正确选项B是论点/主题概括层。
