# 07-06 第二篇

**How Music Shapes Our Lives**

Music is everywhere—in our phones, in shops, at parties, and in our homes. [1] It has a powerful effect on our emotions, our minds, and even our behavior. A sad song can make us feel down, while an upbeat tune can lift our spirits. Music can help us focus when we study or relax when we are stressed. Scientists have found that music activates many parts of the brain and can improve memory and mood. Understanding how music affects us can help us use it in positive ways in our daily lives.

[2] Music can improve our ability to concentrate and learn. Many students like to listen to music while doing homework. Certain types of music, especially instrumental or classical pieces, can create a calm background that helps focus the mind. However, lyrics in songs can sometimes distract us when we are reading or writing. It is worth trying different styles to see what works best for you. Some people prefer silence, while others do better with soft music. The key is to find what helps you concentrate without pulling your attention away from the task.

[3] Music is also a powerful tool for managing emotions. When you feel sad or anxious, listening to music that matches your feelings can help you process those emotions. When you want to feel more energetic, upbeat music can boost your mood. Many people use music to relax before sleep or to get pumped up before a game or exam. Creating playlists for different moods—calm, focused, or energetic—can make it easier to use music as a tool for emotional well-being. Music does not solve problems, but it can support your mental health.

[4] Music brings people together and helps build connections. Singing in a choir, playing in a band, or simply sharing favorite songs with friends creates shared experiences. Music is a universal language that crosses cultures and ages. At concerts and festivals, strangers can bond over a love of the same artist or genre. Learning to play an instrument can connect you with other musicians and open doors to new friendships. Music reminds us that we are not alone—others feel and express themselves through music too.

[5] Making music part of your daily life is easy and rewarding. You do not need to be a skilled musician to enjoy its benefits. Listen to different genres and discover what speaks to you. Try learning an instrument if you are curious—even basic skills can bring joy. Use music intentionally: play calming music when you study, upbeat music when you exercise, and peaceful music when you sleep. However you choose to enjoy it, music can make your life richer, more meaningful, and more connected to others.

## 选项

A. Music influences our feelings, thinking, and actions in significant ways.
B. The right kind of music can help you focus and study more effectively.
C. Music can support your emotional health and help you manage stress.
D. Music creates bonds between people and strengthens social connections.
E. Integrating music into your daily routine can improve your quality of life.
F. For example, streaming apps like Spotify offer millions of songs to choose from.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述音乐的普遍存在，空后具体说明音乐对情绪、心理、行为的影响——悲伤/欢快的感受、助专注与放松、激活大脑、改善记忆与 mood。A选项"It has a powerful effect on our emotions, our minds, and even our behavior"承上启下：将前文的"无处不在"深化为" powerful effect"，并概括后文将从情绪、思维、行为多角度展开。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段论述音乐与学习专注——做作业时听音乐、器乐/古典助专注、歌词可能干扰、因人而异。B选项"The right kind of music can help you focus and study more effectively"准确概括段落主题。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段聚焦音乐与情绪管理——匹配情绪听歌、提神、助眠、考前激励、按情绪建歌单。C选项"Music can support your emotional health and help you manage stress"精准总结段落主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讨论音乐的社会联结——合唱、乐队、分享歌曲、跨越文化、音乐会联结、学乐器交友。D选项"Music creates bonds between people and strengthens social connections"准确概括段落核心。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结全文，给出将音乐融入生活的建议——聆听、尝试乐器、按场景选音乐。E选项"Integrating music into your daily routine can improve your quality of life"精准呼应段落的总结性主旨。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：以"For example"开头，举Spotify等流媒体提供海量歌曲的例子。虽与听音乐相关，但它是支撑"音乐无处不在"或"易获取"的具体论据，而非段落的概括句。若放入[1]会造成层次错位——F是例证层，正确选项A是主题概括层。
