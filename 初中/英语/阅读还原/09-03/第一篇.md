# 09-03 第一篇

**Volunteering and Community Service**

Volunteering means giving your time and energy to help others without expecting anything in return. All around the world, people volunteer in hospitals, schools, shelters, and parks. Whether it is helping elderly neighbors, tutoring younger students, or cleaning up a beach, every act of volunteering makes a difference. [1] Beyond helping others, volunteering also brings great benefits to the volunteers themselves.

One of the biggest rewards of volunteering is the sense of purpose it gives. When you help someone in need, you feel that your actions matter. [2] This feeling of making a real difference in someone's life can boost your confidence and give you a more positive outlook on the world.

Volunteering also helps you develop important skills. Working with different people teaches communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. [3] These are skills that employers value highly, so volunteering can also help you in your future career.

For teenagers, volunteering can be an especially valuable experience. It exposes them to real-world issues like poverty, hunger, and environmental damage. [4] Seeing these problems firsthand helps young people become more aware, more responsible, and more motivated to create positive change.

Communities thrive when people help each other. Volunteering strengthens the bonds between neighbors and creates a culture of kindness and mutual support. [5] In a world that can sometimes feel divided, volunteering reminds us that we are all connected and that small acts of generosity can lead to big, lasting change.

## 选项

A. The act of giving back benefits not only the community but also the personal growth of each volunteer.
B. Knowing that you have contributed something meaningful creates a deep sense of satisfaction and happiness.
C. Gaining hands-on experience in real situations is something that textbooks alone cannot provide.
D. This direct exposure to social challenges shapes their character and deepens their understanding of the world.
E. When people come together to serve their community, they build trust and create a stronger, more caring society.
F. For example, some schools require students to complete a certain number of volunteer hours before graduation.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述志愿服务的各种形式，空后说"volunteering also brings great benefits to the volunteers themselves"。A的"benefits not only the community but also the personal growth"概括双赢效果，与后文"benefits to the volunteers"呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说帮助他人让你觉得行动有意义，空后说"This feeling... can boost your confidence"。B的"Knowing that you have contributed something meaningful"承接前文，"deep sense of satisfaction and happiness"与"boost your confidence"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举志愿服务培养的技能（沟通、团队合作、解决问题），空后说这些技能受雇主重视。C的"Gaining hands-on experience in real situations"概括实践学习的价值，"textbooks alone cannot provide"突出志愿服务的独特优势。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说青少年志愿服务使他们接触贫困、饥饿、环境破坏等现实问题，空后说亲眼目睹这些问题使年轻人更有意识和责任感。D的"direct exposure to social challenges"承接前文，"shapes their character and deepens their understanding"概括影响。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说志愿服务加强邻里纽带、创造互助文化，空后说志愿服务提醒我们相互连接。E的"When people come together to serve their community"承上，"build trust and create a stronger, more caring society"概括社区效益。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some schools require students to complete a certain number of volunteer hours before graduation.**：以For example开头，属具体政策举例；易误放[4]，但[4]需的是青少年志愿服务影响的概括，非学校要求的具体举例。
