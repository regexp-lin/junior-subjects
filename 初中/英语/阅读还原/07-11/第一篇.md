# 07-11 第一篇

**Renewable Energy: Powering a Sustainable Future**

For over a century, we have depended on fossil fuels—coal, oil, and natural gas—to power our lives. These energy sources have driven industrial growth and modern comfort, but they also cause serious problems. Burning fossil fuels releases greenhouse gases that warm the planet and pollute the air. [1] The shift to renewable energy is no longer optional—it is essential. Solar, wind, hydro, and other renewable sources offer a cleaner path forward. They produce little or no pollution and their "fuel" will never run out.

Solar power has become one of the fastest-growing energy sources. [2] Sunlight is free and available almost everywhere. Solar panels convert light directly into electricity with no moving parts and minimal maintenance. In many regions, the cost of solar electricity has dropped so much that it is now cheaper than coal or gas. Rooftop solar allows homes and businesses to produce their own power, reducing reliance on the traditional grid.

Wind energy taps into a powerful natural resource. [3] Wind turbines use the motion of air to spin generators and produce electricity. Wind farms can be built on land or offshore, where winds are often stronger and more consistent. Advances in technology have made wind turbines more efficient and less expensive. In countries like Denmark and Germany, wind already supplies a large share of national electricity needs.

Other renewable sources also play important roles. [4] Hydropower uses flowing water to generate electricity—it has been used for over a century and remains the world's largest source of renewable electricity. Geothermal energy draws heat from inside the Earth for heating and power. Biomass converts plant and animal waste into fuel. Each source has its strengths and is suited to different regions and needs.

[5] Renewable energy is the key to a sustainable future. It reduces pollution, fights climate change, and creates new jobs in growing industries. The transition will take time and investment, but the benefits are clear. Every year, more countries commit to using more renewables. The path ahead is bright—powered by the sun, wind, and water.

## 选项

A. Moving to renewables is now a necessity, not a choice.
B. Solar energy turns abundant sunlight into clean, affordable electricity.
C. Wind power harnesses a plentiful and widely available natural force.
D. A variety of renewable sources complement each other in the energy mix.
E. To sum up, renewables offer the best path toward a cleaner planet.
F. For example, electric cars are becoming more popular in many cities.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述化石燃料带来工业化与便利，同时造成温室气体、空气污染等问题，空后提出"太阳能、风能、水能等可再生能源提供更清洁的路径"。A选项"Moving to renewables is now a necessity, not a choice"承接前文 fossil fuel 的问题，强调转向可再生能源已是必然选择，与后文"essential""cleaner path"紧密衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段主题是太阳能，空后说明阳光免费且广泛可得、光伏板直接将光转化为电、成本已低于煤炭天然气、屋顶光伏可自发电。B选项"Solar energy turns abundant sunlight into clean, affordable electricity"精准概括：太阳能将充沛阳光转化为清洁、可负担的电力。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段讲风能，空后描述风力涡轮机利用空气运动发电、陆上与海上风场、技术提升效率与成本、丹麦德国等国风能占比高。C选项"Wind power harnesses a plentiful and widely available natural force"概括：风能利用丰富且广泛的自然力。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段列举多种可再生能源：水电、地热、生物质，并说明各自用途和适用地区。D选项"A variety of renewable sources complement each other in the energy mix"准确概括：多种可再生能源在能源结构中相互补充。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：最后一段需总结全文，空前已分述各类可再生能源，空后归纳减污、应对气候变化、创造就业、各国承诺增加可再生能源。E选项"To sum up, renewables offer the best path toward a cleaner planet"用"To sum up"收束，与后文"key to sustainable future"呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, electric cars are becoming more popular in many cities.**：以"For example"开头，是举例型干扰。电动汽车与清洁能源相关，但本文讨论的是发电能源（太阳能、风能、水电等），而非用电终端。电动汽车属于能源使用方式，与各段主旨——不同可再生能源的特点——层次错位，无法充当主题概括句。
