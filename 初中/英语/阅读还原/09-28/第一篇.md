# 09-28 第一篇

**The Power of Traditional Music**

In a world full of pop songs and digital playlists, traditional music might seem old-fashioned. Yet the songs and melodies passed down through generations carry something that modern hits often lack: a direct link to history, culture, and identity. From Chinese guzheng to Scottish bagpipes, traditional instruments and tunes tell the stories of communities. [1] This music connects people to their roots and reminds them where they come from.

Traditional music is often learned by ear, from parent to child or from teacher to student. There may be no written score, only memory and practice. Each performer may add their own style while keeping the core of the piece. [2] The way traditional music is passed on keeps it alive but also allows it to grow and change with each generation.

Different regions have different musical traditions. The rhythms of African drums, the melodies of Indian sitar, and the harmonies of European folk songs each reflect the land, climate, and history of their people. [3] Understanding these connections helps us appreciate not only the music itself but also the cultures that created it.

Young people today can play a key role in keeping traditional music alive. Learning an instrument, joining a folk band, or simply listening and sharing can make a difference. When young musicians blend traditional sounds with modern styles, they create something new. [4] The future of traditional music depends on those who are willing to learn, respect, and carry it forward.

Traditional music is more than a hobby. It is a living record of human creativity and community. In an age of globalization, it reminds us that every culture has something unique to offer. [5] Valuing and preserving traditional music is a way to honor the past while building a richer future for everyone.

## 选项

A. Traditional music serves as a bridge between the past and the present for communities around the world.
B. This oral tradition keeps the music flexible and connected to real people and places.
C. Music and culture are deeply woven together, and one helps us understand the other.
D. Young people who take an interest in tradition can ensure that this heritage continues.
E. Protecting this heritage enriches both local identity and global understanding.
F. For example, the Chinese erhu has been played for over a thousand years and is still popular today.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前指出传统音乐承载历史、文化与身份，空后说"This music connects people to their roots and reminds them where they come from"。A的"Traditional music serves as a bridge between the past and the present for communities around the world"概括传统音乐的纽带作用，与"connects people to their roots"完美呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前描述口传心授的传承方式及表演者的个性化，空后强调"The way traditional music is passed on keeps it alive but also allows it to grow and change"。B的"This oral tradition keeps the music flexible and connected to real people and places"概括口传传统的灵活性与人文连接，与"passed on"和"grow and change"逻辑一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举不同地区音乐传统（非洲鼓、印度西塔琴、欧洲民谣），空后说"Understanding these connections helps us appreciate not only the music itself but also the cultures that created it"。C的"Music and culture are deeply woven together, and one helps us understand the other"准确概括音乐与文化的交织关系，与"cultures that created it"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论年轻人学习、演奏、融合传统的角色，空后强调"The future of traditional music depends on those who are willing to learn, respect, and carry it forward"。D的"Young people who take an interest in tradition can ensure that this heritage continues"概括青年传承的重要性，与"future... depends on"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前总结传统音乐是创造力与社区的活记录，全球化时代凸显文化独特性，空后说"Valuing and preserving traditional music is a way to honor the past while building a richer future"。E的"Protecting this heritage enriches both local identity and global understanding"升华主题，与"honor the past"和"richer future"完美对应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Chinese erhu has been played for over a thousand years and is still popular today.**：以For example开头，属具体举例；各空均为概括或过渡句，F为二胡历史的个案，不适用于任何空位，易误放[1]或[3]，但两空分别需传统音乐连接作用的概括与音乐文化关系的概括，非举例。
