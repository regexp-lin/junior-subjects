**Bicycle Culture: Pedaling Toward a Greener Future**

Bicycles have been around for over two hundred years, and they remain one of the most efficient ways to get around. Riding a bike produces no pollution, costs little after the initial purchase, and provides healthy exercise. In many cities around the world, bicycles are becoming a popular choice for short trips, reducing traffic and improving air quality. [1]

Different people ride for different reasons. Some use bikes to commute to school or work. Others enjoy cycling for fun, exploring parks and countryside on weekends. In some countries, cycling is a major sport, with millions watching professional races each year. Families often ride together, making it a shared activity that strengthens bonds. [2]

Cities are making it easier to cycle. Dedicated bike lanes separate riders from car traffic, making cycling safer. Bike-sharing programs let people rent bikes for short trips without owning one. Some cities have built bike parking stations and repair shops. Schools and workplaces are adding bike racks and showers for cyclists. [3]

Safety is important when cycling. Always wear a helmet to protect your head. Use lights and reflectors when riding at night or in low light. Follow traffic rules and signal before turning. Keep your bike in good condition—check brakes and tires regularly. Being visible and predictable helps drivers avoid accidents. [4]

The bicycle has stood the test of time. It was invented before the car and is still going strong. As we face climate change and traffic congestion, the humble bike may be more relevant than ever. [5]

## 选项

A. Bicycles offer a simple, clean, and healthy way to travel that benefits both individuals and the planet.
B. Whether for transport, sport, or leisure, cycling serves many purposes and can fit into almost any lifestyle.
C. As cities invest in bicycle-friendly infrastructure, more people discover the benefits of choosing two wheels over four.
D. Taking safety seriously protects both riders and others on the road, making cycling a choice everyone can enjoy with confidence.
E. The bicycle is proof that sometimes the simplest solutions can be the most powerful—and that a small change in how we move can make a big difference for our health and our planet.
F. For example, the Netherlands has over 35,000 kilometers of dedicated cycling paths, and more than a quarter of all trips in the country are made by bicycle.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说自行车历史与特点——两百多年、高效、无污染、低成本、有助锻炼，城市中成为短途热门选择、减少拥堵改善空气；空后需总结自行车对个人与地球的综合价值。A的"simple, clean, and healthy way to travel"与前文呼应，"benefits both individuals and the planet"与 no pollution、exercise、improving air quality 对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举不同骑行目的——通勤、休闲探索、观赛、家庭共骑；空后需总结骑行多用途与适应性。B的"Whether for transport, sport, or leisure"与前文各类用途对应，"serves many purposes and can fit into almost any lifestyle"与"different reasons"和"different people"呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说城市支持——专用道、共享单车、停车与修理、学校单位增设设施；空后需总结城市投入与更多人选择的良性循环。C的"As cities invest in bicycle-friendly infrastructure"与前文 bike lanes、bike-sharing、bike parking 呼应，"more people discover the benefits of choosing two wheels over four"与"making it easier to cycle"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说骑行安全——头盔、夜间灯光反光、遵守交规、车辆保养、保持可见；空后需总结重视安全对骑行者与他人的保护作用。D的"Taking safety seriously protects both riders and others on the road"与前文各项安全措施呼应，"everyone can enjoy with confidence"与"making cycling safer"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说自行车经久不衰——早于汽车发明仍流行，面对气候变化与拥堵可能更显重要；空后需总结自行车的象征意义与影响。E的"simplest solutions can be the most powerful"与前文"efficient"和"simple"呼应，"small change in how we move can make a big difference"与 climate、traffic、health 对应，是合理收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Netherlands has over 35,000 kilometers of dedicated cycling paths, and more than a quarter of all trips in the country are made by bicycle.**：以 For example 开头，为荷兰自行车设施与使用率的具体数据举例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[3] 易被误选因涉及城市自行车设施，但该空需概括"城市投入与更多人选择"的因果，而非某一国家的具体数据举例。
