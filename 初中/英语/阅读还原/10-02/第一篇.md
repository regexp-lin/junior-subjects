# 10-02 第一篇

**Understanding an Aging Society**

In many countries around the world, people are living longer than ever before. Medical advances, better nutrition, and improved living conditions have all contributed to this change. However, when a larger share of the population is over 65, societies face new challenges and opportunities. [1] This shift will affect everything from healthcare and pensions to family structure and the workforce, and we must prepare for it now.

Older adults have much to offer. Many have years of experience, skills, and wisdom that can benefit younger generations. Some continue to work part-time or volunteer, while others help care for grandchildren. [2] For this reason, many experts argue that society should create more opportunities for older adults to stay involved and share their knowledge.

At the same time, an aging society puts pressure on healthcare systems. As people live longer, more of them develop chronic conditions that need ongoing care. Hospitals and clinics must prepare for this shift. [3] In response, some countries are training more nurses and doctors who specialize in care for the elderly, while also promoting healthy lifestyles to reduce the need for medical treatment later in life.

Another concern is pensions and retirement. When fewer young people are working and more seniors are drawing pensions, the system can become strained. [4] Some countries have already raised the retirement age or adjusted benefit levels, though such changes must be made fairly and with adequate notice to those affected.

Despite these challenges, there is also reason for optimism. Many older people today are healthier and more active than previous generations. Technology can help them stay connected, manage health, and participate in society. [5] In the end, how we respond to aging will define not only the quality of life for today's seniors but also the kind of society we leave for future generations.

## 选项

A. An aging population is not a problem to be solved but a reality that requires careful planning and creative responses from governments, communities, and families alike.
B. Recognizing the value of older citizens leads to policies that support their active participation in society, rather than treating them as burdens or dependents.
C. Governments and healthcare providers need to invest in prevention, early detection, and community-based care to ensure that aging populations remain healthy and independent for as long as possible.
D. Finding the right balance between supporting retirees and maintaining economic growth is one of the key challenges that policymakers must address in the coming decades.
E. With the right approach, an aging society can become a society that values people of all ages and benefits from the contributions that everyone can make.
F. For example, in Japan, nearly 30 percent of the population is now over 65, making it one of the oldest societies in the world.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述人口老龄化现象及社会面临的新挑战与机遇，空后说"This shift will affect everything from healthcare and pensions..."。A的"not a problem to be solved but a reality that requires careful planning"与前文呼应，"creative responses"与空后"we must prepare for it now"衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举老年人的贡献（经验、技能、照顾孙辈等），空后说"For this reason, many experts argue that society should create more opportunities..."。B的"Recognizing the value of older citizens"承接前文，"support their active participation"与空后"opportunities to stay involved"呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说老龄化给医疗系统带来压力、医疗机构须做好准备，空后说"In response, some countries are training more nurses and doctors..."。C的"invest in prevention, early detection, and community-based care"概括应对方向，与空后具体举措衔接。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前指出养老金与退休制度面临的负担，空后说"Some countries have already raised the retirement age or adjusted benefit levels..."。D的"Finding the right balance between supporting retirees and maintaining economic growth"概括核心挑战，空后举例说明各国如何应对。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前提出乐观理由（老年人更健康、技术助力），空后说"In the end, how we respond to aging will define..."。E的"With the right approach"承接前文，"values people of all ages"与空后"quality of life"及"kind of society"衔接。
