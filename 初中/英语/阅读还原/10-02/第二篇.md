# 10-02 第二篇

**The Promise of Renewable Energy**

Fossil fuels like coal, oil, and gas have powered the world for over a century. Yet burning these fuels releases greenhouse gases that contribute to climate change and air pollution. As a result, more and more countries are turning to renewable energy sources such as wind, solar, and hydropower. [1] This transition is already underway in many parts of the world, and it is expected to accelerate in the years to come.

Solar power has become one of the fastest-growing energy sources. The cost of solar panels has dropped greatly in recent years, making it more affordable for homes and businesses. Sunlight is free and abundant in most parts of the world. [2] For these reasons, solar power is increasingly chosen by households and companies that want to cut their energy bills and reduce their carbon footprint.

Wind energy is another important renewable source. Wind turbines can be built on land or offshore, where winds are often stronger and more constant. Many countries have invested heavily in wind farms. [3] In some regions, wind has already become the cheapest source of new electricity, and the industry continues to grow as more turbines are installed each year.

However, renewable energy also has challenges. Solar and wind power depend on weather conditions and are not always available when needed. Storing energy for use when the sun does not shine or the wind does not blow remains a technical hurdle. [4] Success in this area could make solar and wind power reliable enough to supply most of our electricity, even when the weather is unfavorable.

The shift to renewable energy will not happen overnight. It requires investment, policy support, and changes in how we produce and use power. Nevertheless, the trend is clear. [5] The question is no longer whether we will shift to clean energy, but how quickly we can do it and how well we can ensure that the benefits reach everyone.

## 选项

A. Renewable energy is not only cleaner than fossil fuels—it also offers a path toward a more sustainable and secure future for our planet and its people.
B. As technology improves and prices continue to fall, solar energy is becoming a realistic option for millions of people who previously had no access to reliable electricity.
C. Wind power now provides a significant share of electricity in several nations, helping to reduce dependence on coal and gas while creating jobs in manufacturing and maintenance.
D. Researchers and engineers are working on better batteries and storage systems to make renewable energy more reliable and capable of meeting demand around the clock.
E. Every year, more solar panels go up, more wind turbines spin, and more countries commit to a future powered by clean energy—a future that is within reach if we act with determination.
F. For example, China has built the world's largest solar farm in the Qinghai desert, covering an area of over 600 square kilometers.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前对比化石燃料与可再生能源，空后说"This transition is already underway... and it is expected to accelerate"。A的"not only cleaner... but also offers a path toward a more sustainable future"概括转向可再生能源的意义，与空后"transition"衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说太阳能成本下降、阳光充足，空后说"For these reasons, solar power is increasingly chosen..."。B的"As technology improves and prices continue to fall"承接前文，"realistic option for millions"与空后"households and companies"的选择呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说多国投资风电场，空后说"In some regions, wind has already become the cheapest source... and the industry continues to grow"。C的"provides a significant share of electricity"概括风电成效，"reduce dependence on coal and gas while creating jobs"与空后产业成长衔接。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前指出储能难题（天气依赖、存储技术），空后说"Without better storage, renewable energy may struggle to fully replace fossil fuels"。D的"working on better batteries and storage systems"直接回应前文挑战，与空后"better storage"对应，"meeting demand around the clock"解决空后提出的困难。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说转型需时间但趋势明朗，空后说"The question is no longer whether we will shift to clean energy, but how quickly..."。E的"more solar panels go up, more wind turbines spin, more countries commit"承接前文趋势，"future that is within reach if we act with determination"与空后"how quickly we can do it"呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, China has built the world's largest solar farm in the Qinghai desert, covering an area of over 600 square kilometers.**：以For example开头，属具体项目举例，各空均为概括或过渡句；易误放[2]或[3]，但两空分别需太阳能普及的概括与风电成效的概括，非个例数据。
