# 03-19 第二篇

**The Magic of Fireflies**

On a warm summer night, you might suddenly see tiny, glowing lights dancing in the dark air. These magical little creatures are called fireflies (萤火虫). [1] Even though they are called "flies", they are actually a type of winged beetle. 

What makes fireflies so special is their amazing ability to produce their own light. This wonderful process is called bioluminescence (生物发光). [2] It creates a "cold light," meaning that the glowing tail does not produce any heat at all. If it did produce heat like a light bulb, the poor bug would easily burn itself up!

Why do fireflies flash their lights? The main reason is to find a perfect partner. [3] The female fireflies wait quietly in the grass and reply with their own specific flashes if they are interested. 

Unfortunately, people are seeing fewer and fewer fireflies every single summer. One of the biggest reasons for this is "light pollution." [4] Because our cities are filled with bright street lamps and neon signs, fireflies can no longer see each other's tiny flashes in the dark. 

[5] By turning off bright outdoor lights during the summer, we can help these beautiful bugs find their partners and light up our nights once again.

## 选项

A. They happen when a special chemical inside the insect's body mixes with oxygen.
B. Consequently, they often mistakenly fly into our bright houses and get completely lost.
C. Each type of firefly actually has its own unique flashing rhythm and pattern.
D. Therefore, planting beautiful flowers is the only way to save these glowing beetles.
E. Fortunately, there are some simple things we can do to protect them.
F. For many children, catching them in a glass jar is a favorite childhood memory.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] F   [2] A   [3] C   [4] B   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] F**：
  - **线索**：同义代指与情感铺垫。空前描绘了夏夜黑暗空气中舞动的微小光芒（萤火虫）。F选项“For many children, catching them in a glass jar is a favorite childhood memory”（对许多孩子来说，把它们抓在玻璃瓶里是最喜欢的童年记忆）中的代词“them”紧密指代前文的复数名词“fireflies（萤火虫）”。且提到童年抓萤火虫的画面非常具有画面感和情感共力。随后空后用“Even though...”句型开始稍微转折进入了昆虫学的科普“虽然叫苍蝇（蝇类），其实它们是有翅膀的甲虫”。
- **[2] A**：
  - **线索**：因果论证与名词复现。空前指出了段落核心主旨：萤火虫能自己发光（这是生物发光）。但这光是怎么发出来的呢？A选项“They happen when a special chemical inside the insect's body mixes with oxygen”（当昆虫体内一种特殊的化学物质与氧气混合时，它们就会发生）给出了解答。其中“They”指代上述的发光过程。接着，这个混合发光的过程直接引申出了空后的奇点：“它创造了一种‘冷光’，这意味着发光的尾巴根本不产生任何热量……不然它会被自己烤焦”。
- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：铺垫对应与结构逻辑。空前解释了萤火虫发光的主要原因是“to find a perfect partner（寻找完美伴侣）”。C选项“Each type of firefly actually has its own unique flashing rhythm and pattern”（实际上每种萤火虫都有自己独特的闪烁节奏和模式）顺理成章地解释了找伴侣的方式（通过不同的密码/节奏）。这也完美为接下来的句子做了前置准备：“雌性萤火虫安静地在草丛等候，如果有兴趣就发回特有的闪光回应（reply with their own specific flashes）”。
- **[4] B**：
  - **线索**：因果衔接与代词回指。空前提出了导致萤火虫越来越少的最主要原因是“light pollution（光污染）”。由于后一句马上接上了“我们的城市充满了明亮的路灯...导致萤火虫再也看不见彼此微小的闪光”。B选项“Consequently, they often mistakenly fly into our bright houses and get completely lost”（结果是，它们经常误飞入我们明亮的房子里并彻底迷失）完美承接了“光污染”的直接恶劣后果。这三句话构成了一个不可分割的三段论述：光污染->被明亮的建筑物迷惑->再也看不见配偶。
- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：结论定调与引出呼吁。空[5]位于结束段的段首。在上一段分析了导致萤火虫减少的可悲原因之后。E选项“Fortunately, there are some simple things we can do to protect them”（幸运的是，为了保护它们，我们可以做一些简单的事情）用一个充满希冀的转折词“Fortunately”拉回了积极的解决建议视角。紧身其后的一句就是这件具体的小事：“在夏天关掉明亮的室外灯...（By turning off bright outdoor lights...）”。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **D. Therefore, planting beautiful flowers is the only way to save these glowing beetles.**：该选项结构上包含“Therefore（因此）”一词，以及“beetles（甲虫，文章第一段说明过它是甲虫）”的复现。但是句意声称“种美丽的花是救它们的唯一方式”。一方面原文点名它被威胁的最大成因是“光污染（light pollution）”，因此解决方案是“关灯（turn off lights）”而不是“种花”。另一方面又是“the only way”这种常见于错误选项的绝对口吻，是一眼可证伪的低级逻辑错误。
