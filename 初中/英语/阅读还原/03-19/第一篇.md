# 03-19 第一篇

**The Power of Smiling**

We all know that a warm smile can make someone else's day a little brighter. [1] However, scientists have discovered that the simple act of smiling actually has an amazing effect on your own body and mind, too. 

First of all, smiling is completely free, natural medicine. When you smile, your brain immediately releases special "feel-good" chemicals like dopamine and serotonin (多巴胺和血清素). [2] As these chemicals flow through your body, your heart rate slows down and you start to feel much more relaxed. 

Even a fake smile can trick your brain into feeling better. [3] To prove this, scientists asked a group of students to hold a pencil between their teeth, which forced their facial muscles into a smiling shape. The results were surprising: these "smiling" students felt much less stressed during a difficult test than the students who kept a straight face.

Furthermore, smiling makes you appear more attractive and trustworthy to others. [4] A friendly smile sends a clear signal that you are open and happy to talk. This is why people who smile often naturally attract more friends and enjoy better relationships at school or work.

[5] So, the next time you are feeling nervous or sad, take a deep breath and quickly turn the corners of your mouth up. You might be surprised by how much better you feel!

## 选项

A. They act as powerful natural painkillers and quickly wash away your stress.
B. Research shows that we naturally prefer to be around happy, positive people.
C. In short, a smile is a powerful tool that benefits both you and the world around you.
D. Therefore, smiling is the only possible way to successfully pass a difficult test.
E. You do not even need to be genuinely happy to enjoy some of these physical benefits.
F. It is usually the first thing we notice when we meet a friendly stranger.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] F   [2] A   [3] E   [4] B   [5] C

## 深度解析

- **[1] F**：
  - **线索**：指代归属与语境承接。空前说“温暖的微笑能让别人的生活更加明亮”。F选项“It is usually the first thing we notice when we meet a friendly stranger”（它通常是我们遇到友善陌生人时第一件注意到的事）中的“It”完美指代了“a warm smile（温暖的微笑）”。前句说微笑的好处，F项补充其社交表象，顺势引出后面的转折结构：“However（然而），科学家发现这种简单的行为实际上对你自己的身心也有惊人的影响”。从别人转向自己，承上启下流畅自然。
- **[2] A**：
  - **线索**：同义解释与代词双指。空前抛出了一个生理机制：“微笑时，大脑会释放特殊的‘感觉良好’化学物质”。A选项“They act as powerful natural painkillers and quickly wash away your stress”（它们充当强大的天然止痛药并快速洗去你的压力）中，“They”指代“feel-good chemicals”。并且这句解释了这些化学物质有什么用（止痛、洗去压力）。这也为后文的展开做了充分铺垫：“看着这些化学物质流经身体，心率下降并感到更放松”。
- **[3] E**：
  - **线索**：同义替换与引出实证。空前是一句引人注目的观点：“连假笑也能欺骗大脑感觉更好”。E选项“You do not even need to be genuinely happy to enjoy some of these physical benefits”（你甚至不需要是真正快乐的，也能享受其中一些身体带来的好处）是对前句“假笑”的完美同义解释（not genuinely happy = fake smile）。之后空后直接说“为了证明这个论点（To prove this），科学家做了一个叼铅笔强制笑脸的实验...”，层层推进。
- **[4] B**：
  - **线索**：对比连接与主旨呼应。空前的主旨是：“此外，微笑能让你对他人显得更有吸引力和更值得信赖”。B选项“Research shows that we naturally prefer to be around happy, positive people”（研究表明，我们天生更喜欢和快乐积极的人待在一起）提供了社会偏好的大背景支持。因为别人喜欢快乐的人，所以你只要释放出“友好微笑（A friendly smile...）”的信号，也就顺理成章地达成了后文“微笑多的人自然吸引更多朋友”的热烈结局。
- **[5] C**：
  - **线索**：归纳全篇与引入建议。空[5]位于结尾段段首。前文分别介绍了微笑对他人（第一段）、对自身生理（第二/三段）、对自身社交（第四段）的三重好处。C选项“In short, a smile is a powerful tool that benefits both you and the world around you”（简而言之，微笑是一种强大的工具，让你和周围的世界都受益）用了“In short（简而言之）”和“both you and the world（你和世界）”实现了对前文极其完美的总结收拢。也引出了文末“所以下次伤心时笑一笑吧”的实际行动的建议。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **D. Therefore, smiling is the only possible way to successfully pass a difficult test.**：虽然包含“Therefore”结论词，且第三段确实提到了“difficult test（困难的考试）”与微笑着缓解压力的实验，但该选项字面意思是“因此，微笑是成功通过困难考试的唯一可能途径”。这种将“有助于减压”强行绝对化为“唯一通关手段（the only possible way）”的逻辑，极其荒谬不符合现实，属于明显的逻辑极端型干扰项。
