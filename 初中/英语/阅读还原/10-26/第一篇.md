# 10-26 第一篇

**Volunteer Travel: Seeing the World While Giving Back**

More and more young people are choosing to travel in a different way. Instead of just visiting famous sights, they join volunteer programs that let them work on farms, teach in schools, or help protect wildlife. This kind of travel is often called "voluntourism," and it offers a chance to experience new cultures while making a positive impact. [1] Volunteer travel combines adventure with purpose, allowing travelers to learn from local communities and contribute their time and skills to meaningful projects.

Before signing up for a program, it is important to do some research. Some organizations focus on short-term projects that may not create lasting change. Others work closely with local people and ensure that volunteers support, rather than replace, local workers. [2] Choosing the right program matters greatly, as a poorly planned project can sometimes do more harm than good, while a well-designed one can truly benefit the community.

Volunteers often gain as much as they give. They learn new languages, develop practical skills, and see the world from a different point of view. Many say that the friendships they make and the challenges they face change how they think about their own lives and future plans. [3] The experience can be life-changing, opening minds to new possibilities and encouraging a lifelong interest in helping others.

There are opportunities for people of all ages and backgrounds. High school students can join summer programs; university students often take gap years to volunteer abroad. Some programs welcome families or older adults who want to contribute in their free time. [4] With so many options available, anyone who is willing to give their time can find a way to participate and make a difference.

Volunteer travel is not a solution to every problem, but it can be a powerful way to connect people across cultures and build understanding. When done thoughtfully, it benefits both the travelers and the communities they serve. [5] As more people seek meaningful experiences, volunteer travel is likely to grow, creating new opportunities for global cooperation and friendship.

## 选项

A. Volunteer travel combines adventure with purpose, allowing travelers to learn from local communities and contribute their time and skills to meaningful projects.
B. Choosing the right program matters greatly, as a poorly planned project can sometimes do more harm than good, while a well-designed one can truly benefit the community.
C. The experience can be life-changing, opening minds to new possibilities and encouraging a lifelong interest in helping others.
D. With so many options available, anyone who is willing to give their time can find a way to participate and make a difference.
E. As more people seek meaningful experiences, volunteer travel is likely to grow, creating new opportunities for global cooperation and friendship.
F. For example, volunteers in Costa Rica might help protect sea turtles by monitoring beaches and educating tourists about conservation.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍"voluntourism"的概念——边旅行边做志愿，体验文化并产生积极影响，空后需概括这类旅行的主要特点。A的"combines adventure with purpose"呼应"travel"与"volunteer"，"learn from local communities and contribute"与空后"experience new cultures while making a positive impact"完全对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说报名前需调研，有的组织项目短期效果有限，有的与当地合作更好，空后需强调选对项目的重要性。B的"Choosing the right program matters greatly"承接前文调研，"poorly planned... harm... well-designed... benefit"与前文好坏对比呼应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说志愿者收获语言、技能、友谊和视角改变，空后需总结经历的深远影响。C的"The experience can be life-changing"承接"gain as much as they give"，"opening minds... lifelong interest in helping others"与"change how they think about their own lives"呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前列举高中、大学生、家庭、老年人等各类人群的参与机会，空后需总结参与门槛低、人人可参与。D的"With so many options available"与"all ages and backgrounds"呼应，"anyone who is willing to give their time can find a way"与前文各种项目类型衔接。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说义工旅行虽非万能，但能促进跨文化理解，空后需展望其发展趋势。E的"As more people seek meaningful experiences"与"powerful way to connect"呼应，"volunteer travel is likely to grow"和"global cooperation and friendship"是全文收尾的合理升华。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, volunteers in Costa Rica might help protect sea turtles by monitoring beaches and educating tourists about conservation.**：以For example开头，为具体国家与项目举例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[1]或[3]易被误选，但两空分别需概括义工旅行特点与经历影响，不需要具体案例说明。
