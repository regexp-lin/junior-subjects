# 05-03 第一篇

**Robots in Our Daily Lives**

Robots are no longer just characters in science fiction movies. Today, they are becoming a part of our everyday lives. [1] From factories to hospitals, from classrooms to living rooms, robots are helping people in ways that were hard to imagine just a few decades ago. As technology continues to develop, robots will play an even bigger role in the future.

In factories, robots have been used for many years to do jobs that are dangerous or boring for humans. [2] They can work 24 hours a day without getting tired, and they make fewer mistakes than human workers. Car factories, for example, use robots to weld（焊接） metal parts and paint car bodies. These robots are fast, precise, and never need a break. Thanks to them, products can be made more quickly and at a lower cost.

Robots are also entering our homes. [3] Robot vacuum cleaners（扫地机器人） can clean the floor while you are at work or school. Smart speakers can answer your questions, play music, and even control the lights in your house. Some families use robot pets to keep elderly people company. These home robots are designed to make daily life easier and more comfortable.

In the field of medicine, robots are helping doctors perform operations（手术） with greater accuracy. [4] Surgical robots can make very small cuts that human hands cannot, reducing pain and recovery time for patients. Robots are also used in hospitals to deliver medicine and meals to patients' rooms. In the future, robots may even help diagnose（诊断） diseases by analyzing medical data faster than any human doctor.

[5] Although robots cannot completely replace humans, they are becoming valuable helpers in many areas of life. As we learn to work together with robots, we can look forward to a future that is safer, healthier, and more convenient.

## 选项

A. In fact, robots are already helping people in more ways than most of us realize.
B. That is because robots can perform repetitive tasks with high speed and accuracy.
C. Some of the most useful robots are the ones that help with household chores.
D. This is especially true in operations that require extremely precise movements.
E. In conclusion, the age of robots is not coming—it is already here.
F. For instance, Japan has developed robots that can teach basic English to young children.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出机器人在日常生活中的广泛应用。空前描述"Robots are no longer just characters in science fiction movies...becoming a part of our everyday lives"，空后列举"From factories to hospitals, from classrooms to living rooms, robots are helping people in ways that were hard to imagine"。A选项"In fact, robots are already helping people in more ways than most of us realize"（事实上，机器人已经在比大多数人意识到的更多方面帮助人们）中"In fact"加强空前论点，"more ways than most of us realize"自然引出空后多个领域的具体列举，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：解释工厂使用机器人的原因。空前说明"robots have been used for many years to do jobs that are dangerous or boring for humans"，空后具体描述"They can work 24 hours a day without getting tired...make fewer mistakes...fast, precise, and never need a break...made more quickly and at a lower cost"。B选项"That is because robots can perform repetitive tasks with high speed and accuracy"（那是因为机器人能以高速度和高精度执行重复性任务）中"That is because"承接空前使用机器人的事实，"high speed and accuracy"与空后"fast, precise"完美呼应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：引出家用机器人的具体类型。空前提出"Robots are also entering our homes"，空后列举"Robot vacuum cleaners can clean the floor...Smart speakers can answer your questions...robot pets to keep elderly people company"。C选项"Some of the most useful robots are the ones that help with household chores"（最有用的机器人之一是那些帮助做家务的）中"most useful robots...household chores"承接空前"entering our homes"，自然引出空后扫地机器人、智能音箱等具体家用产品，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：医疗机器人的精准性优势。空前说明"robots are helping doctors perform operations with greater accuracy"，空后具体描述"Surgical robots can make very small cuts that human hands cannot, reducing pain and recovery time...deliver medicine and meals...diagnose diseases"。D选项"This is especially true in operations that require extremely precise movements"（这在需要极其精确动作的手术中尤其如此）中"This...especially true"承接空前"greater accuracy"，"extremely precise movements"与空后"very small cuts that human hands cannot"精准对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"robots cannot completely replace humans...valuable helpers...work together with robots...a future that is safer, healthier, and more convenient"。E选项"In conclusion, the age of robots is not coming—it is already here"（总之，机器人时代不是即将到来——它已经到来了）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"already here"与空后"becoming valuable helpers"及"look forward to a future"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For instance, Japan has developed robots that can teach basic English to young children.**：涉及日本教育机器人的具体案例，易被误填[1]或[3]。但F以"For instance"引出单一案例（日本教学机器人），而空[1]后已有从工厂到医院的宏观列举，F过于具体；空[3]讨论家用机器人，F的"teach English...young children"属教育领域而非家庭生活。F属"内容相关但衔接层级与主题不匹配"型干扰。
