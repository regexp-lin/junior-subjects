**The Benefits of Outdoor Camping**

Many people spend most of their time indoors—at school, at work, or at home. Screens and artificial light fill their days. Yet spending time outdoors can improve both body and mind. Camping is one of the best ways to connect with nature. [1] That is because camping offers fresh air, exercise, and a break from daily stress. When you camp, you breathe clean air, walk on trails（小径）, and sleep under the stars. Your body gets natural vitamin D from sunlight. You also step away from phones and computers. In this way, camping helps you feel more relaxed and energetic. It is no wonder that families and friends often choose camping for their holidays. The benefits of being outside are real and lasting.

What skills can you learn from camping? [2] In other words, camping teaches you to plan, adapt, and work with others. You need to choose the right gear（装备）, pack your bags, and set up a tent. If the weather changes, you must adjust your plans. Camping with others means sharing tasks—someone cooks, someone fetches water, someone builds a fire. These experiences build problem-solving skills and teamwork. Many young people say that their first camping trip taught them more about independence than a whole year of school. Camping is a classroom without walls.

How does camping affect your mood? [3] That is why spending nights in nature can reduce stress and improve sleep. Studies show that being in green spaces lowers anxiety and helps people feel calmer. The sounds of birds, wind, and water can slow your heart rate and clear your mind. Without the glow of screens, your brain produces melatonin（褪黑激素）more naturally, which helps you sleep better. For this reason, doctors sometimes suggest camping or hiking for people who feel stressed or have trouble sleeping. Nature is a powerful healer.

Why is camping good for relationships? [4] It is true that camping brings people together without distractions. When you camp, you talk face-to-face instead of texting. You share meals, tell stories, and solve problems as a team. There are no TVs or video games to pull everyone apart. Parents and children often bond more during a camping trip than during a regular weekend at home. Friends who camp together usually become closer. Camping creates memories that last a lifetime. In short, the simple life of camping strengthens the connections between people.

[5] In conclusion, camping is much more than just sleeping outside. It improves your health, teaches useful skills, reduces stress, and brings people closer. Whether you camp in a forest, by a lake, or in a national park, you will gain something valuable. So the next time you have a chance to go camping, take it—your body and mind will thank you. Remember: nature is waiting, and the benefits of camping are yours to discover.

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：总领露营对身心健康的益处。空前描述"Many people spend most of their time indoors...Yet spending time outdoors can improve both body and mind. Camping is one of the best ways to connect with nature"，空后说明"When you camp, you breathe clean air, walk on trails, and sleep under the stars...Your body gets natural vitamin D from sunlight...You also step away from phones and computers...camping helps you feel more relaxed and energetic"。A选项"That is because camping offers fresh air, exercise, and a break from daily stress"（那是因为露营能提供新鲜空气、运动和日常压力的解脱）中"That is because"解释露营好处，"fresh air, exercise, and a break"与空后breathe clean air、walk on trails、step away from phones精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：露营对情绪与睡眠的积极影响。空前设问"How does camping affect your mood?"，空后说明"Studies show that being in green spaces lowers anxiety and helps people feel calmer...The sounds of birds, wind, and water can slow your heart rate and clear your mind...Without the glow of screens, your brain produces melatonin more naturally...doctors sometimes suggest camping or hiking for people who feel stressed or have trouble sleeping"。C选项"That is why spending nights in nature can reduce stress and improve sleep"（因此在自然中过夜能减轻压力、改善睡眠）中"That is why"承接空前及空后结论，"reduce stress and improve sleep"与空后lowers anxiety、melatonin、trouble sleeping精准对应，逻辑紧密。

  - **线索**：露营对人际关系的促进。空前设问"Why is camping good for relationships?"，空后说明"When you camp, you talk face-to-face instead of texting...There are no TVs or video games to pull everyone apart...Parents and children often bond more during a camping trip...Friends who camp together usually become closer...the simple life of camping strengthens the connections between people"。D选项"It is true that camping brings people together without distractions"（的确，露营让人在没有干扰的情况下聚在一起）中"It is true that"加强衔接，"brings people together without distractions"与空后face-to-face、no TVs、bond more完美呼应，衔接得当。
