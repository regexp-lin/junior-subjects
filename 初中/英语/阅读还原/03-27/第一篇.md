# 03-27 第一篇

**The Benefits of Meditation and Mindfulness**

In today's fast-paced world, many students feel stressed and find it hard to focus on their studies. Meditation and mindfulness have become popular ways to deal with these challenges. [1] They are simple techniques that anyone can learn, and you do not need any special equipment—just a quiet place and a few minutes each day.

First of all, meditation helps calm your mind. When you sit quietly and pay attention to your breathing, your heart rate slows down and your body relaxes. [2] This practice can reduce stress and anxiety, making it easier to face difficult tasks such as exams or presentations.

Mindfulness also improves your ability to concentrate. Instead of letting your thoughts jump from one thing to another, you learn to stay focused on the present moment. [3] Many students who practice mindfulness report that they finish their homework faster and remember what they study better.

What is more, meditation can improve your sleep quality. When your mind is always busy with worries, it is hard to fall asleep at night. [4] Regular meditation before bed helps quiet your thoughts and prepares your body for rest. You may find yourself waking up feeling more refreshed.

[5] Start with just five minutes of quiet breathing each morning or evening. With time, you will discover that this small habit can bring big changes to your life—less stress, better focus, and a calmer mind.

## 选项

A. For example, when you feel nervous before a test, taking a few deep breaths can help you feel calmer.
B. The good news is that getting started with meditation is easier than you might think.
C. In fact, research shows that meditation can make the brain stronger in areas related to attention and memory.
D. Scientists have proven that meditation is the only way to achieve success in school.
E. Taking a few minutes each day to meditate can make a real difference in how you feel at night.
F. You do not need to meditate for hours to see benefits—just a few minutes each day can help.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] B   [2] A   [3] C   [4] E   [5] F

## 深度解析

- **[1] B**：
  - **线索**：概括引入与同义呼应。空前介绍冥想的背景——学生压力大，冥想成为流行方式。B选项"The good news is that getting started with meditation is easier than you might think"（好消息是开始冥想比你想象的要容易）承接话题并引出空后的具体说明。"easier than you might think"与后文"simple techniques that anyone can learn...just a quiet place and a few minutes"形成同义呼应，强调入门之简易。

- **[2] A**：
  - **线索**：举例说明与因果衔接。空前描述冥想时心跳放缓、身体放松的生理反应。A选项"For example, when you feel nervous before a test, taking a few deep breaths can help you feel calmer"（例如，考试前紧张时，做几次深呼吸能帮你平静下来）以"For example"给出具体场景。空后"This practice can reduce stress and anxiety"中的"stress and anxiety"与A中"nervous""feel calmer"同义衔接，且"exams or presentations"与A中"before a test"呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：递进论证与科学佐证。空前指出正念能提高专注力，学会"stay focused on the present moment"。C选项"In fact, research shows that meditation can make the brain stronger in areas related to attention and memory"（事实上，研究表明冥想能使大脑在注意力与记忆相关区域更强）用科学证据支撑。"attention"与前文"concentrate""focused"同义；"memory"自然引出空后"remember what they study better"。

- **[4] E**：
  - **线索**：问题—解决与段落主题。空前提出"mind busy with worries"导致难以入睡。E选项"Taking a few minutes each day to meditate can make a real difference in how you feel at night"（每天花几分钟冥想能真正改善你晚上的感受）直接回应睡眠主题。"at night"与空后"before bed""fall asleep""waking up"形成语义连贯。

- **[5] F**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与行动引导。空[5]位于结尾段段首，前文已从减压、专注、睡眠三方面论证冥想好处。F选项"You do not need to meditate for hours to see benefits—just a few minutes each day can help"（你不需要冥想数小时才能见效——每天几分钟就有帮助）破除"费时"的顾虑。空后"Start with just five minutes"正是对F中"just a few minutes"的具体落实，逻辑紧密。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **D. Scientists have proven that meditation is the only way to achieve success in school.**：含"Scientists""meditation""school"等高频词，但使用绝对化表述"the only way"。全文从未声称冥像是成功之唯一途径，只说它是"popular""helpful"方法之一。属于"绝对化表述+夸大主旨"型干扰。
