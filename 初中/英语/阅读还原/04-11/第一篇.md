# 04-11 第一篇

**The Power of Digital Detox**

In today's world, we spend hours every day looking at our phones, tablets, and computers. While technology connects us to friends and information, too much screen time can leave us tired, stressed, and even unhappy. [1] A "digital detox" means taking a break from your devices for a set period—a few hours, a day, or even a week. The goal is to give your mind and body a chance to rest and recharge, just like you need sleep after a long day of work.

Why is it so hard to put down our phones? Apps and social media are designed to keep us scrolling. [2] Notifications, likes, and endless feeds create a cycle where we feel we must always check for updates. Scientists have found that this constant checking can lead to lower attention spans and higher levels of anxiety. When we take a digital detox, we step away from this cycle and give our brains space to focus on what really matters.

How can you start a digital detox? First, decide how long you want to disconnect. [3] You might try turning off your phone during meals or leaving it in another room at bedtime. Some people choose a "screen-free Sunday," while others set daily limits of one or two hours of device use. The key is to start small and make it a habit. Tell your family and friends about your plan so they know you are not ignoring them.

What can you do instead of scrolling? The best part of a digital detox is discovering other activities. [4] You might read a book, go for a walk, play a sport, or simply sit quietly and think. Many people find that they sleep better, feel more relaxed, and have more meaningful conversations when they are not glued to a screen. You may even rediscover hobbies you had forgotten about.

[5] You do not have to give up technology forever—it is a useful tool. But by taking regular breaks, you can use it in a healthier way. Start with a short detox this weekend and see how you feel. Your mind and body might thank you for it.

## 选项

A. In short, a digital detox is not about giving up technology completely.
B. For example, you could set a rule of no phones at the dinner table.
C. They use bright colors, sounds, and rewards to keep us coming back for more.

E. It is a simple idea that more and more people are trying.
F. In fact, spending time without screens can improve your mood and creativity.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] E   [2] C   [3] B   [4] D   [5] A

## 深度解析

- **[1] E**：
  - **线索**：概念引入与自然过渡。空前介绍"digital detox"的定义与目标，空后具体解释"A 'digital detox' means taking a break from your devices...The goal is to give your mind and body a chance to rest"。E选项"It is a simple idea that more and more people are trying"（这是一个越来越多人在尝试的简单想法）中"It"指代前文"taking a break"的概念，"simple idea"与空后"A 'digital detox' means"的通俗解释呼应，"more and more people are trying"引出这一做法的普遍性，衔接自然。若填C则与前文"technology connects us...too much screen time"的铺垫衔接不当，空[1]需先确立"digital detox"概念。

- **[2] C**：
  - **线索**：原因解释与设计机制。空前指出"Apps and social media are designed to keep us scrolling"，空后举例"Notifications, likes, and endless feeds create a cycle"。C选项"They use bright colors, sounds, and rewards to keep us coming back for more"（它们用明亮的颜色、声音和奖励让我们不断回来）中"They"指代"Apps and social media"，"keep us coming back"与空前"keep us scrolling"呼应，"bright colors, sounds, rewards"与空后"Notifications, likes...feeds"形成具体化展开，解释"为何难以放下手机"的机制。

- **[3] B**：
  - **线索**：具体方法举例。空前提出"First, decide how long you want to disconnect"，空后给出多种方式"You might try turning off your phone during meals...'screen-free Sunday'...daily limits"。B选项"For example, you could set a rule of no phones at the dinner table"（例如，你可以设定晚饭时不用手机的规矩）中"For example"引出例证，"no phones at the dinner table"与空后"turning off your phone during meals"直接对应，同为具体可行的小步骤，逻辑顺畅。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：替代活动与收束建议。空前问"What can you do instead of scrolling?"并指出"discovering other activities"，空后列举"read a book, go for a walk, play a sport...sit quietly...sleep better...meaningful conversations"。D选项"The trick is to fill that time with activities that bring real joy and connection"（诀窍在于用带来真正快乐和联结的活动充实那些时间）中"fill that time"对应"instead of scrolling"，"activities that bring real joy and connection"与空后"read...walk...sport...meaningful conversations"完美呼应，自然引入具体举例。

- **[5] A**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后"You do not have to give up technology forever—it is a useful tool...taking regular breaks...use it in a healthier way"。A选项"In short, a digital detox is not about giving up technology completely"（简言之，数字排毒并非完全放弃科技）用"In short"总结，"not about giving up completely"与空后"do not have to give up forever...useful tool...use in healthier way"完美呼应，收束得当。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. In fact, spending time without screens can improve your mood and creativity.**：强调不接触屏幕的好处（情绪与创意），与主题相关，易被误填[4]或[5]。但空[4]空前为"discovering other activities"，空后需引出"用什么活动替代"，D的"fill that time with activities"直接作答；F若填[4]则侧重结果（improve mood）而非方法（what to do），与空后"read...walk...sport"的举例衔接不及D自然。空[5]需总结"digital detox"的本质并过渡到"不必完全放弃"，A的"not about giving up completely"与空后"do not have to give up forever"完美对应；F侧重效果，与收束功能不符。属"主题相关但功能错位"型干扰。
