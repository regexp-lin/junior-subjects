# 04-24 第二篇

**Teenage Entrepreneurship: From Ideas to Action**

Some people think that starting a business is only for adults. But in recent years, more and more teenagers have shown that age is not a limit. [1] With the help of the Internet and smartphones, young people can turn their hobbies and ideas into real projects. A student who loves baking might sell homemade cookies online. Another who is good at coding might create a simple app. These teen entrepreneurs（创业者） are learning important skills—not just about money, but about planning, solving problems, and working with others.

Starting a business as a teenager is not easy. School work, family duties, and social life all take time. [2] Teen entrepreneurs often have to balance many things at once. They might work on their projects after school or during weekends. Some get support from parents or teachers who believe in their ideas. Without good time management and support, it can be hard to keep a small business going while still doing well in school. Yet many teens have found ways to make it work.

What do teenagers gain from starting a business? Beyond earning some pocket money, they learn by doing. [3] They face real challenges—like how to attract customers, what to do when a product does not sell well, or how to deal with negative feedback（反馈）. Each problem becomes a chance to think creatively and try again. These experiences help build confidence and resilience（韧性）, which are useful not only in business but in life.

Of course, teen businesses often start small. Many sell handmade crafts, digital designs, or tutoring services. [4] The goal is usually not to become rich overnight but to learn and grow. Some teen ventures（尝试） fail, and that is okay—failure teaches lessons too. What matters is that young people get a taste of what it means to take an idea, work on it, and see it come to life. That mindset will serve them well in the future, whether they become entrepreneurs or not.

[5] In short, teenage entrepreneurship is about more than making money. It is about building confidence, learning from mistakes, and discovering what we are capable of. When teens dare to try, they often surprise themselves—and the world—with what they can achieve.

## 选项

A. In other words, running a small business gives teens real-life lessons that cannot be learned from a textbook.
B. In fact, technology has made it easier than ever for young people to start their own ventures.
C. Adults who started businesses as teens often say that failure taught them more than success did.
D. In summary, teen entrepreneurship is a powerful way for young people to explore their potential.
E. Success rarely comes without effort, and teen entrepreneurs learn this early.
F. That is why many schools now offer courses or clubs to support students with business ideas.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] B   [2] F   [3] A   [4] E   [5] D

## 深度解析

- **[1] B**：
  - **线索**：引出青少年创业的时代背景与可行性。空前说明"more and more teenagers have shown that age is not a limit"，空后举例"With the help of the Internet and smartphones...turn their hobbies and ideas into real projects...sell homemade cookies online...create a simple app"。B选项"In fact, technology has made it easier than ever for young people to start their own ventures"（事实上，技术使年轻人创业比以往任何时候都更容易）中"In fact"承接空前"age is not a limit"，"technology has made it easier"与空后"Internet and smartphones...turn hobbies into projects"精准对应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] F**：
  - **线索**：青少年创业的困难与支持来源。空前描述"Starting a business as a teenager is not easy...School work, family duties, and social life all take time...balance many things at once...work after school or during weekends...get support from parents or teachers"，空后说明"Without good time management and support, it can be hard to keep a small business going...Yet many teens have found ways to make it work"。F选项"That is why many schools now offer courses or clubs to support students with business ideas"（因此许多学校现在开设课程或俱乐部来支持有创业想法的学生）中"That is why"承接空前困难与需求，"schools offer courses or clubs to support"与空后"support from parents or teachers...good time management"形成呼应，说明支持的多种来源，衔接自然。

- **[3] A**：
  - **线索**：青少年创业带来的学习与成长。空前提问"What do teenagers gain from starting a business?"并说明"Beyond earning...they learn by doing"，空后列举"They face real challenges—attract customers...product does not sell...negative feedback...Each problem becomes a chance...build confidence and resilience"。A选项"In other words, running a small business gives teens real-life lessons that cannot be learned from a textbook"（换言之，经营小生意给青少年带来课本学不到的真实人生课程）中"In other words"承接空前"learn by doing"，"real-life lessons that cannot be learned from a textbook"与空后"face real challenges...confidence and resilience"完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] E**：
  - **线索**：青少年创业的目标与态度。空前说明"teen businesses often start small...goal is usually not to become rich overnight but to learn and grow...Some teen ventures fail, and that is okay—failure teaches lessons too"，空后总结"What matters is that young people get a taste of...That mindset will serve them well"。E选项"Success rarely comes without effort, and teen entrepreneurs learn this early"（成功很少不付出努力就能获得，青少年创业者很早就体会到这一点）中"Success rarely comes without effort"与空前"fail...failure teaches lessons"呼应，"learn this early"与空后"get a taste of...mindset will serve them"形成过渡，衔接顺畅。

- **[5] D**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后列举"about more than making money...building confidence, learning from mistakes, discovering what we are capable of...surprise themselves—and the world—with what they can achieve"。D选项"In summary, teen entrepreneurship is a powerful way for young people to explore their potential"（总之，青少年创业是年轻人探索自身潜能的强大途径）用"In summary"总结全文，"explore their potential"与空后"discovering what we are capable of...what they can achieve"完美呼应，收束得当。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **C. Adults who started businesses as teens often say that failure taught them more than success did.**：强调失败的教育意义，易被误填[4]。但C以"Adults who started..."开头，引入成年人回忆的视角，与空[4]前后文聚焦"teen businesses...goal...learn and grow...Some fail"的当下青少年视角不一致；空[4]需承接"start small...fail...that is okay"并过渡到"mindset will serve them"，E的"Success rarely comes without effort...learn this early"更贴合青少年主体。C属"语义相关但主语与视角错位"型干扰。
