# 11-10 第二篇

**Sports and Fitness: Building a Healthy Body and Mind**

Regular physical activity is essential for good health. Sports and exercise strengthen our muscles and bones, improve our heart and lungs, and help us maintain a healthy weight. But the benefits go beyond the body. Exercise can lift our mood, reduce stress, and help us sleep better. Many students find that being active actually improves their focus and performance in class. [1] Sports and fitness offer benefits for both body and mind, making them a key part of a healthy and balanced life at any age.

There are many ways to stay active. Team sports like basketball, soccer, and volleyball teach cooperation and communication. Individual activities such as swimming, running, or cycling can be done alone or with friends. Dancing, martial arts, and yoga offer variety and can be enjoyed indoors or outdoors. The best form of exercise is one you enjoy and will stick with. [2] Whether you prefer team competition or solo activities, finding a form of exercise that fits your personality and schedule is the first step toward building a lasting fitness habit.

Getting started can be the hardest part. Some people feel they are "not sporty" or worry about looking clumsy. The truth is that everyone starts somewhere, and improvement comes with practice. You do not have to be the best—you just have to show up. Schools and community centers often offer low-cost or free programs for beginners. [3] Overcoming the fear of starting and accepting that progress takes time can help anyone begin their fitness journey, regardless of their current level or past experience.

Staying safe while exercising is important. Warm up before activity to prevent injuries. Drink water before, during, and after exercise. Rest when your body needs it—overtraining can lead to burnout or injury. Wearing the right gear, such as supportive shoes, can also make a big difference. [4] Listening to your body and taking care of it properly allows you to enjoy sports and fitness for the long term, rather than risking harm through neglect or pushing too hard.

Physical activity should be a part of life from childhood through old age. The habits we build when we are young often stay with us. Parents and schools can help by providing opportunities to try different sports and by being active role models. [5] Making sports and fitness a natural part of daily life, rather than something we do only when we have to, is a choice that pays off in health, happiness, and vitality for years to come.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前说规律运动的重要性——强健肌肉骨骼、改善心肺、保持体重，并指出益处不止于身体——改善情绪、减轻压力、助眠、提高课堂注意力，空后需概括运动对身心双方面的价值。A的"benefits for both body and mind"与前文身体与心理益处对应，"key part of a healthy and balanced life at any age"与前文"essential for good health"呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举多种运动方式——篮球足球排球等集体项目培养合作与沟通，游泳跑步骑车等个人项目可独自或结伴，舞蹈武术瑜伽等提供多样选择，强调最好选自己喜欢并能坚持的，空后需总结选择适合自己运动形式的意义。B的"Whether you prefer team competition or solo activities"与前文团队与个人项目对应，"finding a form of exercise that fits your personality and schedule"与"one you enjoy and will stick with"呼应，"first step toward building a lasting fitness habit"与前文坚持对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说起步最难——有人觉得"不擅长运动"或怕出丑，强调人人都有起点、进步靠练习、不必最优秀只需参与，学校和社区常有低价或免费入门项目，空后需总结克服起步恐惧、接受渐进的意义。C的"Overcoming the fear of starting and accepting that progress takes time"与前文"hardest part"和"everyone starts somewhere"呼应，"regardless of their current level or past experience"与"not sporty"和"clumsy"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说运动安全——热身防伤、补水、适当休息、穿戴合适装备如支撑性鞋，空后需总结倾听身体与正确照顾的长期价值。D的"Listening to your body and taking care of it properly"与前文各项安全建议呼应，"enjoy sports and fitness for the long term, rather than risking harm"与"prevent injuries"和"overtraining can lead to burnout or injury"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说运动应贯穿一生——童年到老年，年轻时养成的习惯常伴随一生，家长与学校可通过提供尝试机会和以身作则来帮助，空后需总结将运动融入日常的长期回报。E的"Making sports and fitness a natural part of daily life"与前文"part of life from childhood through old age"呼应，"rather than something we do only when we have to"与"habits we build when we are young often stay with us"对应，"pays off in health, happiness, and vitality"是合理收尾。
