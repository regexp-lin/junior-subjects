# 07-05 第二篇

**The Benefits of Outdoor Exercise**

Spending time outdoors and being physically active are both important for your health. [1] When you combine them—exercising outside instead of in a gym—you can enjoy double the benefits. Outdoor activities like running, cycling, hiking, or playing sports in a park offer fresh air, natural scenery, and the chance to connect with nature. Many people find that working out outdoors is more enjoyable and easier to stick with than indoor exercise. Whether you prefer team sports or solo activities, there is an outdoor option that can work for you.

[2] Outdoor exercise is great for your physical health. Running, cycling, and swimming strengthen your heart and lungs. Walking or hiking builds leg muscles and improves balance. Playing basketball, football, or badminton with friends adds fun to your workout and burns calories. Unlike gym machines that often work only one part of your body, outdoor activities often involve your whole body. The varied terrain—hills, trails, or sand—challenges your body in different ways and can make you stronger and fitter over time.

[3] Being in nature also benefits your mental health. Studies show that spending time in green spaces can reduce stress and improve mood. The sounds of birds, the sight of trees, and the feel of sunlight can help you relax and feel more peaceful. After a busy day of school or study, a walk in the park or a bike ride by the river can clear your mind. Many people find that outdoor exercise helps them sleep better at night. Nature has a calming effect that indoor environments often lack.

[4] Outdoor activities can also strengthen your social connections. Joining a running group, playing football with classmates, or going on a family hike are all ways to exercise and spend time with others. Shared experiences in nature often create strong memories and bonds. You might meet new friends who share your interests. For teenagers, outdoor sports can be a healthy way to hang out with friends instead of only staying indoors. The social side of outdoor exercise is one reason many people prefer it to solo gym sessions.

[5] Getting started with outdoor exercise is easier than you might think. You do not need special equipment—a pair of comfortable shoes is enough for walking or jogging. Start with short sessions and gradually increase the time. Choose activities you enjoy so you are more likely to keep doing them. Even 20 minutes of outdoor activity a day can make a difference. The key is to make it a habit. Pick a regular time, invite a friend if you like, and step outside. Your body and mind will thank you.

## 选项

A. Exercising outdoors offers both physical and mental advantages over indoor workout.
B. Outdoor activities help improve your fitness and strengthen your body.
C. Spending time in nature can reduce stress and improve your mood.
D. Outdoor exercise is also a great way to connect with friends and family.
E. Starting outdoor exercise is simple—all you need is willingness and basic gear.
F. For example, many cities have built new parks with running tracks and bike paths.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前提出户外与运动对健康的重要，空后具体说明户外运动的独特优势——新鲜空气、自然景观、与自然连接、更具趣味和可持续性。A选项"When you combine them—exercising outside instead of in a gym—you can enjoy double the benefits"承上启下：将"户外"与"运动"结合，点明比室内运动更受益，与后文具体优势紧密衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段全段论述户外运动对身体健康的好处——强心健肺、锻炼腿部、平衡、团队运动燃脂、全身参与、多变地形。B选项"Outdoor activities help improve your fitness and strengthen your body"准确概括段落主题。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段聚焦户外对心理健康的影响——减压、改善情绪、放松、改善睡眠、自然的 calming 作用。C选项"Spending time in nature can reduce stress and improve your mood"精准总结段落主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讨论户外活动的社交价值——跑步小组、球类、家庭远足、建立记忆与纽带、结识朋友。D选项"Outdoor exercise is also a great way to connect with friends and family"准确概括段落核心。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段给出如何开始户外运动的建议——无需复杂装备、循序渐进、选喜欢的活动、养成习惯。E选项"Starting outdoor exercise is simple—all you need is willingness and basic gear"精准呼应段落的总结性建议。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：以"For example"开头，举许多城市新建公园配备跑道和自行车道的例子。虽与户外运动场所相关，但它是支撑某段论点的具体论据，而非段落的概括句。若放入[2]或[5]会造成层次错位——F是例证层，正确选项是论点/主题概括层。
