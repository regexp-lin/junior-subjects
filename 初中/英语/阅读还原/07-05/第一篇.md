# 07-05 第一篇

**Protecting Traditional Culture in a Modern World**

Traditional culture—including festivals, crafts, music, and customs—connects us to our history and gives us a sense of identity. [1] Yet many traditions are at risk of disappearing as the world becomes more globalized and modern. Young people often prefer new trends over old practices. Some traditional skills have very few people left who can teach them. If we lose these traditions, we lose a valuable part of who we are. Protecting and passing on traditional culture is a responsibility we all share, and it requires conscious effort from each generation.

[2] Understanding why traditions matter is the first step to preserving them. Traditions tell the stories of our ancestors and explain why we do things in certain ways. They bring families and communities together during festivals and celebrations. Traditional arts like calligraphy, pottery, or folk music express unique beauty that modern art cannot replace. When we understand the meaning behind a custom, we are more likely to value it and want to keep it alive. Education plays a key role—schools and families can help young people learn about their cultural heritage.

[3] Learning and practicing traditional skills helps keep them alive. Many crafts, such as paper-cutting, embroidery, or traditional cooking, require years of practice to master. Young people who are interested can seek out masters or elders to learn from. Some communities offer classes or workshops where you can try these skills. Even if you do not become an expert, trying once helps you appreciate the effort and skill involved. Every person who learns a little bit contributes to the survival of that tradition.

[4] Using modern technology can help spread traditional culture to more people. Social media, videos, and websites can share stories, music, and crafts with audiences around the world. A young person might create a short video about a traditional festival and reach thousands of viewers. Museums and cultural centers are also digitizing their collections so people can explore them online. Technology does not have to replace tradition—it can be a tool to make tradition more visible and accessible to the next generation.

[5] We all have a role in keeping traditional culture alive. Support local festivals by attending them. Buy handmade crafts from traditional artisans when you can. Share what you learn with your friends and family. Most importantly, take pride in your heritage and pass it on. Traditional culture is not something that belongs only in museums—it is alive when people practice it, celebrate it, and teach it to others. By valuing our traditions today, we ensure they remain a vibrant part of our world tomorrow.

## 选项

A. However, traditional culture faces serious threats in today's fast-changing world.
B. Knowing the value of traditions helps us appreciate and protect them.
C. Learning traditional skills from masters ensures these practices continue.
D. Technology can be used to promote and preserve traditional culture.
E. Everyone can contribute to protecting traditions through simple daily actions.
F. For example, the Mid-Autumn Festival is celebrated with mooncakes and family reunions.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍传统文化的内涵（节日、工艺、音乐、习俗）及其意义（连接历史、身份认同），空后转折指出传统面临消失的风险——全球化、年轻人偏好、传承人稀少。A选项"Yet many traditions are at risk of disappearing as the world becomes more globalized and modern"完美承上启下：用"Yet"转折，承接前文对传统的正面描述，引出后文对其面临威胁的论述。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段论述理解传统价值的重要性——承载祖先故事、凝聚家庭社区、传统艺术的独特美感、理解后更愿传承、教育的关键作用。B选项"Knowing the value of traditions helps us appreciate and protect them"准确概括段落主旨。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段聚焦学习与实践传统技艺——拜师学艺、社区课程、初尝即有助于传承。C选项"Learning traditional skills from masters ensures these practices continue"精准总结段落核心。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段讨论现代科技在传播传统文化中的作用——社交媒体、视频、网站、博物馆数字化。D选项"Technology can be used to promote and preserve traditional culture"准确概括段落主题。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结全文，列举个人可做的贡献——参与节日、购买手工艺、分享所学、传承后代。E选项"Everyone can contribute to protecting traditions through simple daily actions"精准呼应段落的总结性主旨。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：以"For example"开头，举中秋节点心与家人团聚的例子。虽与传统节日相关，但它是支撑第二段"传统凝聚家庭"的具体论据，而非段落的概括句。若放入[2]会造成层次错位——F是例证层，正确选项B是论点/主题概括层。
