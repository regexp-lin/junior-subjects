# 05-08 第一篇

**Saving Water: Small Steps, Big Impact**

Water is essential for life, yet many people take it for granted. [1] In fact, fresh water is a limited resource—only about 3% of the world's water is safe to drink, and much of it is frozen in ice or hidden underground. As the global population grows, the demand for water increases. That is why saving water at home is not just good for your wallet; it is also good for the planet.

There are many simple ways to reduce water use in your daily life. [2] For example, turning off the tap while you brush your teeth can save up to 8 liters of water each time. Taking shorter showers instead of long baths also helps. A ten-minute shower uses about half the water of a full bathtub. You can also fix dripping taps promptly, because a small leak can waste hundreds of liters of water every month. These small changes add up quickly.

Schools and communities can make a bigger difference. [3] They can install low-flow taps and toilets that use less water with each use. Some schools have started rainwater collection systems to water their gardens. Communities can also organize campaigns to teach people about water conservation（节约用水）. When everyone works together, the impact can be enormous. In other words, collective action leads to meaningful results.

Why does saving water matter so much? [4] In many parts of the world, people walk for hours each day just to fetch clean water. Droughts（干旱） are becoming more common due to climate change, and farmers struggle to grow crops when there is not enough rain. By using less water at home, we leave more for others and for nature. Every drop we save today helps ensure that future generations will have enough water to meet their needs.

[5] In short, water conservation is something everyone can participate in. From turning off the tap to supporting community projects, there are countless ways to make a difference. Remember: the water you save today could be the water someone else needs tomorrow.

## 选项

A. That is because the supply of usable water is much smaller than many people realize.
B. In other words, simple habits can lead to significant savings.
C. That is why larger groups should take the lead in water-saving efforts.
D. The reason is that water shortages affect millions of people around the world.
E. In conclusion, we all have a role to play in protecting this precious resource.
F. However, some countries have already run out of drinking water completely.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出淡水资源有限的事实。空前描述"Water is essential for life, yet many people take it for granted"，空后说明"fresh water is a limited resource—only about 3% of the world's water is safe to drink, and much of it is frozen in ice or hidden underground...demand for water increases...saving water at home...good for the planet"。A选项"That is because the supply of usable water is much smaller than many people realize"（那是因为可用水资源的供应量比许多人意识到的要少得多）中"That is because"解释为何不该take for granted，"supply...much smaller than...realize"与空后"only about 3%...safe to drink"及"limited resource"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：总领日常节水小技巧。空前提出"There are many simple ways to reduce water use in your daily life"，空后列举"turning off the tap while brushing teeth...Taking shorter showers...fix dripping taps...These small changes add up quickly"。B选项"In other words, simple habits can lead to significant savings"（换言之，简单的习惯可以带来显著的节约）中"In other words"概括空前"simple ways"，"simple habits...significant savings"与空后具体做法及"small changes add up quickly"完美对应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：学校与社区层面的节水行动。空前提出"Schools and communities can make a bigger difference"，空后列举"They can install low-flow taps and toilets...rainwater collection systems...organize campaigns...When everyone works together, the impact can be enormous"。C选项"That is why larger groups should take the lead in water-saving efforts"（因此更大的群体应该在节水行动中发挥带头作用）中"That is why"承接空前，"larger groups...take the lead"与空后"schools and communities"及"install...organize...everyone works together"形成呼应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：说明节水为何重要的全球视角。空前设问"Why does saving water matter so much?"，空后详述"In many parts of the world, people walk for hours...just to fetch clean water...Droughts are becoming more common...farmers struggle to grow crops...By using less water at home, we leave more for others"。D选项"The reason is that water shortages affect millions of people around the world"（原因是水资源短缺影响着全球数百万人）中"The reason is that"直接回答问题，"water shortages affect millions"与空后"people walk for hours...fetch clean water"及"droughts...farmers struggle"精准对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与行动呼吁。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"water conservation is something everyone can participate in...From turning off the tap to supporting community projects...the water you save today could be the water someone else needs tomorrow"。E选项"In conclusion, we all have a role to play in protecting this precious resource"（总之，我们在保护这一宝贵资源方面都有一份责任）用"In conclusion"总结全文，"we all have a role to play"与空后"everyone can participate in"及"countless ways to make a difference"完美呼应，收束有力。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. However, some countries have already run out of drinking water completely.**：涉及某些国家已完全缺水，易被误填[4]。但F表述过于极端（"run out...completely"），而空[4]前后文描述的是"people walk for hours...fetch clean water"和"droughts becoming more common"，意在说明水资源紧张和分配不均，并非完全枯竭；F的绝对化表述与原文 moderate 的论述风格不符。F属"程度夸大、与语境不符"型干扰。
