**Artificial Intelligence: Changing Our World**

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is no longer something we only see in science fiction movies. From voice assistants on our phones to recommendations on video apps, AI is already part of our daily lives. It can recognize faces, translate languages, and even beat humans at complex games like chess. But what exactly is AI, and how is it changing the way we live and work? [1] AI refers to machines and computer programs that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

In education, AI is helping both students and teachers. Some apps can answer students' questions at any time of day, while others provide practice exercises that adjust to each student's level. Teachers use AI to grade assignments faster and identify which topics need more attention in class. These tools save time and can make learning more personalized. [2] As AI continues to develop, it has the potential to transform how we learn, making education more accessible and tailored to each learner's needs.

AI is also being used in medicine and health care. Doctors use it to analyze medical images and detect diseases earlier than before. Researchers use AI to discover new drugs and predict how different treatments might work for different patients. In some hospitals, AI helps manage appointments and reminds patients to take their medicine. [3] The application of AI in health care is growing rapidly, offering new ways to improve diagnosis, treatment, and patient care around the world.

However, AI also raises important questions. Will machines take away people's jobs? How can we make sure AI is used fairly and safely? Some people worry about privacy when companies collect large amounts of data. Governments and companies are working on rules and guidelines to address these concerns. [4] Balancing the benefits of AI with its risks is one of the biggest challenges society faces today, and it requires thoughtful discussion and cooperation among many groups.

Looking to the future, AI will likely become even more powerful and widespread. Self-driving cars, smarter home devices, and new kinds of robots are already being tested. Young people today will grow up in a world where AI is everywhere. [5] Learning to understand and use AI responsibly will be essential for the next generation, as it becomes a key skill in almost every field of work and life.

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍AI已融入日常生活——语音助手、视频推荐、人脸识别、翻译、下棋等，并设问"什么是AI、如何改变生活"，空后需给出AI的定义。A的"AI refers to machines and computer programs"直接回答设问，"perform tasks that normally require human intelligence"与前文列举的能力对应，"learning, reasoning, and problem-solving"概括AI的核心特征。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说AI在教育中的应用——答疑应用、自适应练习、教师批改作业、识别薄弱环节、个性化学习，空后需总结AI对教育的变革潜力。B的"transform how we learn"与前文具体应用呼应，"more accessible and tailored to each learner's needs"与"personalized"和"adjust to each student's level"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前描述AI在医疗中的应用——分析医学影像、及早发现疾病、研发新药、预测疗效、管理预约、用药提醒，空后需总结医疗AI的发展与价值。C的"The application of AI in health care is growing rapidly"与前文多种应用呼应，"improve diagnosis, treatment, and patient care"概括了前文各场景的益处。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前提出AI带来的问题——取代工作、公平与安全、隐私担忧、政府与企业制定规则，空后需总结平衡利弊的挑战。D的"Balancing the benefits of AI with its risks"与前文问题呼应，"requires thoughtful discussion and cooperation among many groups"与"governments and companies are working on rules"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前展望AI未来——自动驾驶、智能家居、新型机器人、AI无处不在，空后需总结对下一代的要求。E的"Learning to understand and use AI responsibly"承接前文，"essential for the next generation"与"Young people today"呼应，"key skill in almost every field"是合理收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
