**Smart Cities: Using Technology to Improve Urban Life**

A smart city uses technology to make life better for its people. Sensors, cameras, and data systems help manage traffic, save energy, and provide safer public spaces. For example, smart traffic lights can change based on real-time conditions to reduce jams. Streetlights can dim when no one is around to save electricity. These innovations help cities run more smoothly and use resources more wisely. [1] Smart cities use digital tools and data to improve daily life, making urban areas safer, more efficient, and more livable for everyone who calls them home.

The idea behind smart cities is to connect different parts of city life—transport, energy, waste, safety, and more—so they work together. When buses and trains share real-time data, people can plan journeys better. When buildings share energy data, the whole system can reduce waste. This connectivity allows city leaders to make better decisions and respond faster to problems. [2] By connecting services and sharing information, smart cities create a more coordinated and responsive urban environment that serves citizens more effectively than traditional systems.

Smart cities also focus on inclusion and fairness. Technology should help everyone—not only those who can afford the latest gadgets. Public Wi-Fi in parks, apps that help people find jobs or housing, and digital services in community centers can bridge the gap. Cities must also protect people's privacy and keep data secure as they collect more information. [3] A truly smart city is one that uses technology to support all its citizens, including those who are often left behind, while respecting privacy and building trust.

Many cities around the world are already testing smart ideas. Singapore uses sensors to manage traffic and monitor pollution. Barcelona has smart parking and street lighting. In China, several cities use mobile apps for buses, bikes, and payments. These examples show that smart cities can look different depending on local needs and culture. [4] From Asia to Europe and beyond, cities are finding their own ways to adopt smart technology, showing that there is no single model—only shared goals of efficiency, sustainability, and quality of life.

The future of our planet will be largely urban. More than half of all people already live in cities, and that share is rising. Smart city technology can help manage this growth—reducing pollution, saving energy, and making crowded spaces safer and more pleasant. [5] As cities grow and face new challenges, smart technology offers powerful tools to build a more sustainable, efficient, and human-centered urban future for generations to come.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍智慧城市定义——用科技改善市民生活，传感器、摄像头、数据系统管理交通、节能、提升公共安全，举例智能红绿灯与可调光路灯，使城市运行更顺畅、资源利用更合理，空后需概括智慧城市通过数字工具与数据改善日常生活的价值。A的"use digital tools and data to improve daily life"与前文"sensors, cameras, and data systems"呼应，"safer, more efficient, and more livable"与前文各项应用对应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说智慧城市理念——将交通、能源、垃圾、安全等城市生活各部分连接起来协同工作，公交与地铁共享实时数据便于出行规划，建筑共享能源数据可减少浪费，使决策更好、问题响应更快，空后需总结服务互联与信息共享带来的协调与响应能力。B的"By connecting services and sharing information"与前文"connect different parts"和"share real-time data"呼应，"coordinated and responsive urban environment"与"work together"和"respond faster"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说智慧城市注重包容与公平——技术应惠及所有人而非仅能负担新设备者，公园公网、求职住房类app、社区中心数字服务可弥合差距，同时需保护隐私与数据安全，空后需总结真正智慧城市应兼顾全民支持与隐私信任。C的"uses technology to support all its citizens, including those who are often left behind"与前文"help everyone"和"bridge the gap"呼应，"respecting privacy and building trust"与前文"protect people's privacy and keep data secure"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前举例全球城市实践——新加坡用传感器管理交通与监测污染、巴塞罗那智能停车与路灯、中国多城市用APP管理公交单车支付，说明智慧城市可根据本地需求与文化呈现不同形态，空后需总结各地因地制宜采用智慧技术的共通目标。D的"From Asia to Europe and beyond, cities are finding their own ways"与前文各城市案例呼应，"no single model—only shared goals of efficiency, sustainability, and quality of life"与"can look different depending on local needs"对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说未来世界将高度城市化——过半人口已住城市且比例上升，智慧城市技术可帮助管理增长、减少污染、节能、使拥挤空间更安全舒适，空后需总结智慧技术对可持续、高效、以人为本的城市未来的作用。E的"As cities grow and face new challenges"与前文"that share is rising"呼应，"sustainable, efficient, and human-centered urban future"与"reduce pollution, save energy, and making crowded spaces safer"对应，是合理收尾。
