# 09-15 第一篇

**The Benefits of Spending Time Outdoors**

Many of us spend most of our days indoors—in classrooms, at home, or on buses. Screens keep us connected, but they also keep us inside. Meanwhile, nature is waiting just outside the door: parks, forests, beaches, and even a walk around the block. [1] Making time to get outside is not a luxury; it is essential for our physical health, mental wellbeing, and our connection to the natural world.

Exercise outdoors often feels easier and more enjoyable than exercise indoors. The changing scenery, fresh air, and sunlight can make a walk or a run feel less like work and more like an adventure. Physical activity outside also tends to last longer because we are not watching the clock. [2] The combination of movement and nature can boost both fitness and mood in ways that gyms and screens cannot match.

Studies show that time in nature reduces stress and anxiety. The sights and sounds of the outdoors—birds, trees, water—have a calming effect on the brain. Even a short break outside can improve focus and creativity when we return to our tasks. [3] Regular contact with nature acts like a reset button for our minds, helping us feel more balanced and ready to face challenges.

Spending time outside also teaches us about the environment. When we see plants, animals, and weather up close, we care more about protecting them. [4] Experiences in nature often lead to a deeper appreciation of the planet and a stronger desire to take care of it for future generations.

Getting outdoors does not require a long trip or expensive gear. A lunch break in a park, a weekend hike, or a game of football with friends can make a difference. [5] The key is to make outdoor time a regular part of life—not something we do only on rare holidays, but a habit that supports our health and happiness year-round.

## 选项

A. Prioritizing time in nature is one of the best things we can do for our bodies, minds, and spirits.
B. Moving your body while surrounded by nature offers benefits that indoor exercise alone cannot provide.
C. Giving your brain a break in natural settings can restore energy and improve how you think and feel.
D. What we learn and feel in nature often motivates us to become better stewards of the Earth.
E. Building outdoor habits into daily and weekly routines can bring lasting benefits without large costs or effort.
F. For example, many schools have started "forest schools" where children learn outdoors in woodland settings.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述现代人久居室内、接触自然不足，空后强调"Making time to get outside is not a luxury; it is essential for our physical health, mental wellbeing, and our connection to the natural world"。A的"Prioritizing time in nature is one of the best things we can do for our bodies, minds, and spirits"概括户外时间的必要性，与"essential"完美呼应。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前比较户外与室内运动的体验，空后说"The combination of movement and nature can boost both fitness and mood in ways that gyms and screens cannot match"。B的"Moving your body while surrounded by nature offers benefits that indoor exercise alone cannot provide"准确概括户外运动的独特优势，与"gyms and screens cannot match"对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论自然对减压、专注与创造力的作用，空后说"Regular contact with nature acts like a reset button for our minds, helping us feel more balanced and ready to face challenges"。C的"Giving your brain a break in natural settings can restore energy and improve how you think and feel"与"reset button"和"more balanced"逻辑一致，概括该段主旨。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说接触自然有助于关心环保，空后说"Experiences in nature often lead to a deeper appreciation of the planet and a stronger desire to take care of it"。D的"What we learn and feel in nature often motivates us to become better stewards of the Earth"与"appreciation"和"desire to take care"呼应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说户外活动无需远行或昂贵装备，空后强调"The key is to make outdoor time a regular part of life... a habit that supports our health and happiness year-round"。E的"Building outdoor habits into daily and weekly routines can bring lasting benefits without large costs or effort"升华主题，与"regular part"和"habit"完美对应，适合作全文收束。

- **干扰项分析**：
