# 08-18 第二篇

**Being Grateful to Our Parents**

Our parents do more for us than we often realize. They work hard to provide food, clothes, and a warm home. They teach us right from wrong and support us when we face difficulties. [1] Taking time to appreciate them not only strengthens our family bonds but also makes us happier and more positive people.

One simple way to show gratitude is to say "thank you" more often. When your mother prepares a meal or your father drives you to school, a sincere thank-you can mean a lot. [2] These small acts of appreciation add up over time and show your parents that you notice their efforts. They may not ask for praise, but they surely appreciate it.

Another way is to help with household chores. Doing the dishes, cleaning your room, or taking out the trash lightens your parents' load. [3] When you share the work, you are telling them that you care about their well-being, not just receiving their care. Action often speaks louder than words.

Spending quality time together also matters. Put down your phone during family meals and really listen when your parents share their thoughts. [4] These moments create lasting memories and deepen your understanding of each other. In our busy lives, such time together is a true gift that money cannot buy.

Gratitude is not a one-time act. [5] Make it a habit to notice and appreciate what your parents do every day. You might write a thank-you note, give a hug, or simply tell them how much they mean to you. Whatever you choose, the important thing is to let your gratitude show.

## 选项

A. It is a mindset that we can practice and grow every single day.
B. A simple "I appreciate you" can brighten their day and strengthen your connection.
C. Parents often remember these moments long after we have forgotten them.
D. This kind of respect and attention shows that you value their presence in your life.
E. Start small and make gratitude a natural part of your daily routine.
F. For example, some families set aside Sunday evening as family game night.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前列举父母为我们所做的种种，空后强调"花时间感恩不仅能增进家庭纽带，还能让我们更快乐、更积极"。选项A"这种心态我们可以每天实践并不断培养"与"Taking time to appreciate"呼应，将感恩定位为可持续的实践，引出后文具体方法。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前建议多说"谢谢"，举例母亲做饭、父亲接送，空后说"这些小小的感恩举动日积月累，让父母感到你注意到他们的付出"。选项B"一句真诚的谢谢意义重大"与前文"sincere thank-you"形成复现，与后文"small acts"自然衔接。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说分担家务能减轻父母负担，空后说"分担工作就是在告诉他们你在乎他们的幸福"。选项C"当你分担工作，你是在告诉他们你关心他们的幸福，而不只是接受他们的照顾"与空后"care about their well-being"形成同义表达，逻辑一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前建议放下手机、认真倾听父母，空后说"这些时刻创造持久记忆、加深彼此理解"。选项D"这种尊重和关注表明你珍惜他们在你生活中的存在"诠释了"认真倾听"背后的情感含义，与"lasting memories"形成因果链。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说"感恩不是一次性行为"，空后列举具体做法并强调"让感恩流露出来"。选项E"从小事做起，让感恩成为日常的自然部分"与前文"habit"呼应，与后文"write a note, give a hug"等具体行动形成"习惯—行动"的递进。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, some families set aside Sunday evening as family game night**：以For example开头的典型干扰项。与"quality time"相关，容易被误选为[4]。但该选项是具体家庭活动举例，而[4]处需要的是对"倾听与关注"的情感升华；且F的"family game night"与"放下手机倾听"的语境层次不同，属于主题相关但论述错位。
