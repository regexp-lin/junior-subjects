# 08-01 第二篇

**Exercise and Mental Health**

We all know that exercise is good for the body. Running, swimming, and playing sports strengthen our muscles and hearts. [1] But physical activity does far more than keep us fit. Scientists have found strong links between exercise and mental well-being. Moving your body can lift your mood and clear your mind.

Exercise helps reduce stress and anxiety. [2] When you work out, your body releases endorphins—chemicals that create feelings of happiness. Physical activity also lowers cortisol, the stress hormone. Even a short walk can make you feel calmer. Many people find that exercise is one of the best ways to manage daily pressure.

Regular exercise improves sleep and focus. [3] People who exercise often sleep better and wake up more refreshed. Physical activity during the day helps regulate your sleep cycle. Better sleep leads to clearer thinking and improved concentration. Students and workers who exercise tend to perform better.

Exercise can also build confidence and social bonds. [4] Reaching a fitness goal—like running a longer distance—gives you a sense of achievement. Team sports and group classes connect you with others. Sharing a workout with friends makes it more fun and motivating. You gain both physical strength and social support.

You do not need to be an athlete to benefit. [5] Start with small steps: take the stairs, walk after lunch, or dance to your favorite music. Find an activity you enjoy, and make it part of your routine. Your body and mind will thank you. Exercise is one of the most powerful tools we have for mental health.

## 选项

A. Its benefits extend to our emotions, stress levels, and mental clarity.
B. The chemistry of exercise naturally helps us feel better and less tense.
C. Movement during the day supports better rest and sharper thinking at night.
D. Physical challenges and group activities boost both self-esteem and connection.
E. Even modest amounts of activity can make a real difference over time.
F. For example, yoga combines physical poses with breathing and meditation.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前承认运动对身体有益；空后转入运动与心理健康的联系及改善情绪、清醒头脑。A 项“其益处延伸到情绪、压力水平和思维清晰度”承接“远不止保持健康”，与后文研究结论自然衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前提出运动帮助减轻压力和焦虑；空后解释内啡肽、皮质醇及短时散步的效果。B 项“运动的化学机制自然帮助我们感觉更好、更少紧张”概括生理机制，与后文具体解释对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前提出规律运动改善睡眠和专注；空后说明运动者的睡眠质量、日间活动与睡眠节律、更好睡眠带来的思维与专注提升。C 项“白天的活动有助于夜间更好的休息和更敏锐的思维”概括运动—睡眠—思维的因果链，衔接流畅。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前提出运动可建立自信与社会联系；空后举例达成健身目标、团体运动与课程、与朋友一起锻炼。D 项“身体挑战和团体活动都能提升自尊和联结感”概括该段主旨，与后文举例对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说明不需成为运动员也能受益；空后给出从爬楼梯、饭后散步、听歌跳舞等小事起步的建议。E 项“即使适量的活动假以时日也能带来真实改变”总结该段主旨，与后文“小步骤”“享受并养成习惯”呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, yoga combines physical poses with breathing and meditation**：虽与运动及心理相关，但仅为单一运动形式举例，未概括某段主旨或起到承上启下作用，与各空位逻辑不符。
