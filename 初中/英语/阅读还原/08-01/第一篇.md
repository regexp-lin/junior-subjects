# 08-01 第一篇

**Climate Change and Daily Life**

Climate change is no longer a distant problem for future generations. It affects our weather, our health, and how we live today. [1] Rising temperatures and extreme weather events are becoming more common. Understanding these changes helps us adapt and take action.

Our daily habits contribute to climate change. [2] Driving cars, using electricity, and eating certain foods all produce greenhouse gases. The average person's carbon footprint adds up quickly. Small changes—like walking short distances or turning off lights—can reduce your impact. Awareness is the first step toward change.

Extreme weather affects our safety and comfort. [3] Heat waves make it harder to work and sleep. Heavy rain and floods damage homes and roads. Droughts hurt farmers and raise food prices. Communities need to prepare for these events. Early warning systems and better infrastructure can save lives.

Climate change also impacts our health. [4] Hotter days increase the risk of heatstroke and breathing problems. Longer allergy seasons make life difficult for many people. Diseases carried by insects may spread to new areas. Doctors and communities are learning to respond to these new challenges.

Everyone has a role to play. [5] Governments must invest in clean energy and protect natural areas. Businesses can reduce waste and use sustainable materials. Individuals can vote, speak up, and make greener choices. Climate action is not just for experts—it starts with each of us.

## 选项

A. The effects of a warming planet are already visible in our lives.
B. Everyday choices we make add to the problem—and can be part of the solution.
C. From heat to floods to drought, weather extremes are causing real damage.
D. A warmer world brings new health risks that we must learn to address.
E. Solving this problem requires effort from all levels of society.
F. For example, polar ice caps are melting at an alarming rate.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前指出气候变化不再遥远，已影响天气、健康与生活；空后列举气温升高与极端天气增多。A 项“变暖星球的影响已在我们生活中显现”承接“影响今日生活”，与后文具体现象衔接。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前提出日常生活习惯会加剧气候变化；空后举例开车、用电、饮食产生温室气体及可做的改变。B 项“我们的日常选择既加剧问题，也可成为解决方案的一部分”概括该段主旨，与后文正反两面论述对应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前提出极端天气影响安全与舒适；空后具体说明热浪、暴雨洪涝、干旱及其后果。C 项“从高温到洪水到干旱，极端天气正在造成实际损失”概括该段主旨，与后文举例呼应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前提出气候变化还影响健康；空后列举中暑、呼吸问题、过敏季延长、虫媒病扩散等。D 项“更暖的世界带来我们必须学会应对的新的健康风险”概括该段主旨，与后文总分关系明确。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前提出每个人都有角色；空后分别说明政府、企业和个人的责任。E 项“解决此问题需要社会各层面的努力”总结该段主旨，与后文多方参与对应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, polar ice caps are melting...**：虽与气候变化相关，但属宏观现象举例，未对应“日常生活”这一聚焦点，与各空位段内逻辑层次不符。
