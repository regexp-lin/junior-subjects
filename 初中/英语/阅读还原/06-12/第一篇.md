# 06-12 第一篇

**Dental Health and Oral Hygiene**

Taking care of your teeth is important for your overall health, not just for a bright smile. Good oral hygiene（口腔卫生）helps prevent cavities（蛀牙）, gum disease, and other problems that can affect your whole body. [1] In other words, healthy teeth and gums support both your confidence and your physical well-being. Starting good habits early and keeping them for life can save you from pain, costly treatments, and serious health issues later.

Brushing correctly makes a big difference. [2] Dentists recommend brushing at least twice a day—morning and night—for about two minutes each time. Use a soft brush and fluoride（氟化物）toothpaste, and brush all surfaces of your teeth, including the backs and along the gum line. Replace your toothbrush every three months or when the bristles（刷毛）wear out. Rinsing（漱口）your mouth after meals, especially sugary snacks, can also help reduce the risk of decay.

Flossing and diet matter too. [3] Flossing once a day removes food and plaque between teeth where a brush cannot reach. A balanced diet—low in sugary drinks and snacks—protects your teeth from decay. Calcium-rich foods like milk and cheese strengthen teeth; crunchy fruits and vegetables like apples and carrots help clean surfaces naturally. Drinking water, especially fluoridated tap water, is one of the simplest ways to support dental health.

Regular check-ups are essential. [4] Even if your teeth feel fine, seeing a dentist every six to twelve months can catch small problems before they become painful or expensive to fix. The dentist can clean your teeth professionally, apply protective treatments, and give advice tailored to your needs. Many schools offer free or low-cost dental screenings. Do not wait for pain to appear—prevention is always easier and cheaper than cure.

[5] Good oral hygiene is a lifelong habit worth building. The few minutes you spend brushing and flossing each day can prevent years of trouble. Share what you learn with family and friends, and encourage younger siblings to start early. Your future self will thank you for the care you take of your teeth today.

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出牙齿健康对自信与整体健康的双重价值。空前描述"Taking care of teeth important for overall health, not just bright smile...Good oral hygiene helps prevent cavities, gum disease...affect whole body"，空后说明"healthy teeth and gums support both your confidence and your physical well-being...Starting good habits early...save from pain, costly treatments, serious health issues"。A选项"In other words, healthy teeth and gums contribute to both self-confidence and general health"（换言之，健康的牙齿和牙龈既有利于自信也有利于整体健康）中"In other words"概括空前，"self-confidence and general health"与空后"confidence and physical well-being"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：正确刷牙的方法、频率与时长影响效果。空前提出"Brushing correctly makes a big difference"，空后详述"Dentists recommend brushing twice a day—morning and night—about two minutes...soft brush, fluoride toothpaste...brush all surfaces, backs, gum line...Replace brush every three months...Rinsing after meals, especially sugary snacks...reduce decay risk"。B选项"That is because proper brushing technique, frequency, and timing greatly affect how well you protect your teeth"（那是因为正确的刷牙技巧、频率和时间会显著影响你保护牙齿的效果）中"That is because"解释差异原因，"technique, frequency, and timing"与空后 twice a day、two minutes、all surfaces 等完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：牙线与饮食对防龋的作用。空前提出"Flossing and diet matter too"，空后列举"Flossing once a day removes food and plaque between teeth...balanced diet—low in sugary drinks and snacks—protects from decay...Calcium-rich foods strengthen teeth...crunchy fruits and vegetables clean surfaces...water, fluoridated tap water"。C选项"In fact, daily flossing and a tooth-friendly diet play key roles in preventing decay"（事实上，每日牙线清洁和护牙饮食在预防蛀牙中起关键作用）中"In fact"强化两者重要性，"daily flossing and tooth-friendly diet"与空后 flossing once a day、balanced diet、calcium-rich、low sugar 精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：定期检查可及早发现问题。空前提出"Regular check-ups are essential"，空后描述"Even if teeth feel fine, seeing dentist every six to twelve months can catch small problems before painful or expensive...dentist can clean professionally, apply treatments, give advice...Many schools offer free screenings...Do not wait for pain—prevention easier and cheaper than cure"。D选项"These practices show that regular dental visits can prevent problems before they become serious"（这些做法表明定期牙科检查可在问题变严重前加以预防）中"These practices"指代空后定期检查、专业清洁、早发现等，"prevent before serious"与空后"catch small problems before...prevention easier"完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与口腔护理的长期回报。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"Good oral hygiene is lifelong habit worth building...few minutes brushing and flossing each day can prevent years of trouble...Share with family and friends...encourage siblings...future self thank you"。E选项"In short, oral care is a simple daily habit that pays off for a lifetime"（简言之，口腔护理是值得终身坚持的简单日常习惯）用"In short"总结全文，"simple daily habit that pays off for a lifetime"与空后"lifelong habit...few minutes...prevent years of trouble"形成呼应，收束精准。
