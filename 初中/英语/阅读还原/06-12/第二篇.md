**The History of the Bicycle**

The bicycle is one of the most successful inventions in human history. It has changed how people travel, exercise, and move around cities for over two hundred years. [1] In other words, the bicycle is a simple but powerful machine that has shaped modern life in countless ways. From its early, clumsy（笨拙的）forms to the sleek（流线型的）models we see today, the bike has gone through many changes to become what it is now.

The first bicycles looked very different from today's. [2] In 1817, a German inventor built a "running machine" with two wheels but no pedals—riders pushed it along with their feet. Later, in the 1860s, the first pedal-powered bicycles appeared in France. These early models had a large front wheel and a tiny back wheel, which made them difficult and dangerous to ride. It was not until the 1880s that the "safety bicycle"—with two equal-sized wheels and a chain drive—became popular and finally made cycling practical for ordinary people.

The bicycle spread around the world. [3] By the early 20th century, bicycles were common in Europe, America, and Asia. They gave workers a cheap way to reach their jobs and allowed women more freedom to travel on their own. In China, the bicycle became a symbol of everyday life; at its peak, cities had millions of cyclists. Today, in many countries, bike-sharing programmes have brought bicycles back into urban transport, reducing traffic and pollution.

The bicycle keeps evolving. [4] Modern bikes use lighter materials like aluminium and carbon fibre, better gears, and improved brakes. Electric bicycles add a small motor to help riders on hills or long distances. Designers continue to experiment with folding bikes, cargo bikes for carrying goods, and bikes adapted for different terrains. The basic idea—two wheels, human power—stays the same, but the technology keeps improving.

[5] The bicycle remains one of the most efficient and environmentally friendly ways to get around. It requires no fuel, produces no pollution, and benefits both personal health and the planet. Whether used for commuting, sport, or simple pleasure, the bicycle shows that sometimes the simplest ideas can have the longest-lasting impact.

## 选项

A. In other words, the bicycle is a remarkable invention that has influenced modern transport and lifestyle.
B. That is because the first models evolved from pedal-less machines to safer, more practical designs over several decades.
C. In fact, bicycles became widespread globally and played important roles in work, freedom, and daily life.
D. These developments show that bicycle technology continues to advance while keeping the core concept.
E. In short, the bicycle is an efficient, green form of transport that benefits health and the environment.
F. For example, the Tour de France is one of the most famous bicycle races in the world.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：引出自行车作为重要发明对人类生活的影响。空前描述"The bicycle is one of the most successful inventions...changed how people travel, exercise, move around cities for over two hundred years"，空后说明"the bicycle is a simple but powerful machine that has shaped modern life in countless ways...From early clumsy forms to sleek models...gone through many changes"。A选项"In other words, the bicycle is a remarkable invention that has influenced modern transport and lifestyle"（换言之，自行车是影响现代交通与生活方式的杰出发明）中"In other words"概括空前，"remarkable invention...influenced modern transport and lifestyle"与空后"powerful machine...shaped modern life"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：早期自行车由无踏板到更安全实用设计的演变。空前提出"The first bicycles looked very different from today's"，空后详述"1817 German 'running machine' with two wheels, no pedals...1860s first pedal-powered in France...large front wheel, tiny back wheel...difficult and dangerous...1880s 'safety bicycle' with equal wheels and chain drive...popular, practical for ordinary people"。B选项"That is because the first models evolved from pedal-less machines to safer, more practical designs over several decades"（那是因为早期型号在几十年内从无踏板机器演变为更安全实用的设计）中"That is because"解释外观差异原因，"pedal-less...to safer, practical designs"与空后 running machine、safety bicycle 演变完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：自行车在全球普及及其社会作用。空前提出"The bicycle spread around the world"，空后列举"By early 20th century common in Europe, America, Asia...cheap way to reach jobs...women more freedom...In China symbol of everyday life...millions of cyclists...Today bike-sharing...urban transport, reducing traffic and pollution"。C选项"In fact, bicycles became widespread globally and played important roles in work, freedom, and daily life"（事实上，自行车在全球普及并在工作、自由和日常生活中扮演重要角色）中"In fact"强化普及与作用，"widespread globally...work, freedom, daily life"与空后 cheap commute、women freedom、China symbol、bike-sharing 精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：自行车技术持续革新但核心理念不变。空前提出"The bicycle keeps evolving"，空后描述"Modern bikes: lighter materials aluminium, carbon fibre...better gears, improved brakes...Electric bicycles add motor...folding bikes, cargo bikes, different terrains...basic idea—two wheels, human power—stays same, technology keeps improving"。D选项"These developments show that bicycle technology continues to advance while keeping the core concept"（这些发展表明自行车技术在保持核心理念的同时持续进步）中"These developments"指代空后轻量化、电动、折叠等，"advance while keeping core concept"与空后"basic idea stays same, technology keeps improving"完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与自行车作为环保高效交通方式的价值。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"remains one of the most efficient and environmentally friendly ways...no fuel, no pollution...benefits personal health and planet...commuting, sport, pleasure...simplest ideas can have longest-lasting impact"。E选项"In short, the bicycle is an efficient, green form of transport that benefits health and the environment"（简言之，自行车是一种高效、环保的交通方式，有益健康与环境）用"In short"总结全文，"efficient, green...benefits health and environment"与空后"efficient, environmentally friendly...benefits health and planet"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Tour de France is one of the most famous bicycle races in the world.**：涉及环法自行车赛，易被误填[3]或[5]。但F以"For example"引出赛事案例，而空[3]讨论的是自行车在日常生活中的普及与作用，F的"Tour de France"属于竞技体育，与"cheap commute、women freedom、China symbol"的日常用途焦点不同；空[5]总结自行车作为交通与环保工具，F的"race"侧重体育竞赛，与"efficient transport...no pollution"的环保论述不符。F属"主题相关但用途层次错位"型干扰。
