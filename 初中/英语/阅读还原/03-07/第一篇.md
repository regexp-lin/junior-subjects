# 03-07 第一篇

**Reducing Plastic Pollution in Our Lives**

Plastic is everywhere in our daily lives, from shopping bags to water bottles. [1] Because it is cheap, light, and useful, we use a huge amount of it every single day. However, this convenience comes at a very high cost to our beautiful planet.

The biggest problem with plastic is that it hardly ever goes away. [2] Instead of safely returning to nature, it stays in the environment, polluting the soil and the water. These plastic items are often washed into our oceans, causing serious harm to wildlife. [3] As a result, the natural food chain is damaged, and the entire ocean ecosystem is in danger.

So, what can we do to solve this worldwide crisis? The answer starts with our everyday choices. [4] For example, using a reusable cloth bag instead of a plastic one at the supermarket can make a big difference. We can also say no to plastic straws and choose water bottles made of glass or metal.

Of course, recycling is also an important part of the solution. [5] But the absolute best way to protect our environment is simply to use less plastic in the first place. By making small changes in our habits, we can all help to keep our Earth clean and green for future generations.

## 选项

A. It often takes hundreds of years for a single plastic bottle to break down.
B. Sadly, many sea animals mistake these bright pieces for food and eat them.
C. Therefore, throwing plastic into the sea is the best way to clean our cities.
D. It is true that this man-made material has made modern life much easier.
E. By doing so, we can prevent old plastic items from polluting the environment.
F. We should try to refuse single-use plastics whenever it is possible.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] D   [2] A   [3] B   [4] F   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] D**：
  - **线索**：代词指代与逻辑顺承。空前提到“Plastic is everywhere in our daily lives”（塑料在我们日常生活中无处不在）。D选项中的“this man-made material”（这种人造材料）是对“plastic”的同义指代，并且“has made modern life much easier”（让现代生活更轻松）与后句的“Because it is cheap, light, and useful”（因为它便宜、轻便且有用）在逻辑上构成天然的因果递进关系，说明了塑料被广泛使用的原因。
- **[2] A**：
  - **线索**：同义复现与上下文呼应。空前指出“The biggest problem with plastic is that it hardly ever goes away”（塑料最大的问题是它几乎永远不会消失）。A选项“It often takes hundreds of years... to break down”（降解通常需要几百年）是用具体的数据例子来支撑前一句的观点。同时，A项填入后，与后一句“Instead of safely returning to nature, it stays in the environment”（它没有安全地回归自然，而是留在环境中）形成完美的语义连贯。
- **[3] B**：
  - **线索**：代词指代与因果逻辑。空前提到塑料被冲入海洋，“causing serious harm to wildlife”（对野生动物造成严重伤害）。B选项非常具体地描述了这种伤害是如何发生的：“many sea animals mistake these bright pieces for food and eat them”（许多海洋动物误食它们）。且B项中的“these bright pieces”指代前文的“These plastic items”。填入后，自然引出空后句“As a result, the natural food chain is damaged”（结果，自然食物链受损）。
- **[4] F**：
  - **线索**：总分结构与举例说明。空前提问怎么解决危机，并回答“The answer starts with our everyday choices”（答案始于我们的日常选择）。F选项“We should try to refuse single-use plastics whenever it is possible”（我们应尽可能拒绝一次性塑料）是给出的一个总的指导原则。而紧随其后的“For example”（例如）举的例子（用环保布袋代替塑料袋）正是对F项“拒绝一次性塑料”的绝佳例证。
- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：词义匹配与代词指代。空前提到“recycling is also an important part of the solution”（回收也是解决方案的重要部分）。E选项中的“By doing so”（通过这样做）完美指代了前一句的“recycling（回收）”行为；且E项解释了回收的好处“prevent old plastic items from polluting the environment”（防止旧塑料物品污染环境）。随后用“But”转折引出核心观点：最好的办法是一开始就少用。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **C. Therefore, throwing plastic into the sea is the best way to clean our cities.**：该选项使用了总结性连词“Therefore”，并提到了“plastic”和“the sea”等高频词汇。但是，它的句意是“因此，把塑料扔进海里是清洁城市的最佳方式”，这一反科学且极度不环保的荒唐逻辑，与全文“减少塑料污染、保护海洋生态”的环保主旨完全对立。此为逻辑荒谬的干扰项。
