# 03-12 第二篇

**The Value of Learning to Cook**

Nowadays, with the rise of food delivery apps, ordering dinner is as easy as pressing a button on your phone. [1] However, learning how to cook your own meals is still one of the most valuable skills you can develop in life.

The most obvious benefit of cooking at home is that it saves a lot of money. Eating at restaurants or ordering delivery every day can be extremely expensive. [2] Over a month or a year, this small daily saving adds up to a large amount of money.

Furthermore, cooking gives you complete control over your health. When you buy food from a restaurant, you never really know how much salt, sugar, or oil is in it. [3] This makes it much easier to keep a balanced diet and stay in good physical shape.

Cooking can also be a wonderful way to express your creativity. The kitchen is like an artist's studio, and the ingredients are your colors. [4] By experimenting with different vegetables and meats, you might discover fantastic new flavors that you never knew existed.

Lastly, sharing a home-cooked meal is a great way to bring people together. [5] When you take the time to prepare food for someone, you are showing them love and care in a way that ordered food never can.

## 选项

A. As a result, many young people have completely forgotten how to prepare food.
B. However, cooking complicated meals requires a lot of hard practice.
C. It allows you to mix different spices and create your own unique dishes.
D. Therefore, restaurants are trying to use healthier ingredients to attract customers.
E. In your own kitchen, however, you can choose exactly what goes into your body.
F. By buying fresh ingredients from the supermarket, you spend much less per meal.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] F   [3] E   [4] C   [5] B (Wait, [5] needs to match bringing people together.)

# 03-12 第二篇

**The Value of Learning to Cook**

Nowadays, with the rise of food delivery apps, ordering dinner is as easy as pressing a button on your phone. [1] However, learning how to cook your own meals is still one of the most valuable skills you can develop in life.

The most obvious benefit of cooking at home is that it saves a lot of money. Eating at restaurants or ordering delivery every day can be extremely expensive. [2] Over a month or a year, this small daily saving adds up to a large amount of money.

Furthermore, cooking gives you complete control over your health. When you buy food from a restaurant, you never really know how much salt, sugar, or oil is in it. [3] This makes it much easier to keep a balanced diet and stay in good physical shape.

Cooking can also be a wonderful way to express your creativity. The kitchen is like an artist's studio, and the ingredients are your colors. [4] By experimenting with different vegetables and meats, you might discover fantastic new flavors that you never knew existed.

Lastly, sharing a home-cooked meal is a great way to bring people together. [5] When you take the time to prepare food for someone, you are showing them love and care in a way that ordered food never can.

## 选项

A. As a result, many young people never even try to prepare their own food.
B. There is nothing quite like sitting around a dinner table with family and friends.
C. It allows you to mix different spices and create your own unique recipes.
D. Therefore, restaurants are actively trying to invent new flavors for customers.
E. In your own kitchen, however, you can choose exactly what goes into your body.
F. By buying fresh ingredients from the supermarket, you spend much less per meal.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] F   [3] E   [4] C   [5] B

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：因果顺承与语境铺垫。空前提到了一个极其普遍的现代现象：“随着外卖软件的兴起，点晚餐就像在手机上按个按钮一样简单”。A选项利用因果连词“As a result（结果）”顺势得出现状：“many young people never even try to prepare their own food（许多年轻人甚至从未尝试自己准备食物）”。这不仅客观描述了现象，也为其后“However（然而），自己学做饭仍是最有价值技能之一”的强烈反差式中心论点提供了背景铺垫。
- **[2] F**：
  - **线索**：对比逻辑与词义复现。空前指出段落主旨“省钱”，并说明了“去餐厅或点外卖极度昂贵”。F选项“By buying fresh ingredients from the supermarket, you spend much less per meal”（通过从超市购买新鲜食材，你每顿饭的花费要少得多）是典型的省钱措施，也是对外卖昂贵的对比。随后空后的句子提到“this small daily saving（这微小的日常节省）”，完美确立了代词“this saving”所指代的就是F项中的“spend much less（花得少）”的行为。
- **[3] E**：
  - **线索**：反面转折与语意连贯。空前提及外卖的弊端：“When you buy food from a restaurant, you never really know how much salt... is in it”（外卖你不知道里头放了多少盐糖油）。E选项“In your own kitchen, however, you can choose exactly what goes into your body”（然而在自己的厨房里，你能确切选择什么进入你的身体）利用“however”与前文形成强烈的内外出路对比。并且“选择吃进去什么”毫无违和地引出下一句“这使保持均衡饮食更加容易”。
- **[4] C**：
  - **线索**：比喻扩展与代词指代。空前段落主旨是“表达创造力”，把厨房比作艺术家工作室，食材比作颜色。C选项“It allows you to mix different spices and create your own unique recipes”（它允许你混合不同的香料并创造属于你自己的独特食谱）是对这段“创造力”的具体展开。代词“It”指代“Cooking（烹饪）”，“create unique recipes（创造独特食谱）”正好与后文“experimenting with... discover fantastic new flavors（实验...发现美妙新风味）”意思一致，结构紧凑。
- **[5] B**：
  - **线索**：核心词复现与语境升华。空前提到做饭的最后一大好处：“bring people together（把人们聚在一起）”。B选项“There is nothing quite like sitting around a dinner table with family and friends”（没有什么能比得上和家人朋友围坐在餐桌旁了）是对“bring people together”的最具象化、最温馨的场景描写。“family and friends”就是“people”，“sitting around a dinner table”就是“together”。也顺畅引出了文末对这种做法体现爱与关怀的总结。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **D. Therefore, restaurants are actively trying to invent new flavors for customers.**：该选项包含“Therefore”结论词以及“restaurants”和“flavors”这样的高频话题词。如果粗心阅读第三段或第四段，可能会受这两个词误导。但其句意是“因此，餐厅正在积极尝试为顾客发明新口味”，全文立意是“鼓励读者在家做饭的益处（省钱、健康、创造力、情感）”，选项内容完全站在了文章倡导的反面（也就是维护餐厅利益）。通过把握全篇核心立意可以直接排除。
