# 03-12 第一篇

**The Mystery of the Pyramids**

For thousands of years, the Great Pyramid of Giza has stood tall in the Egyptian desert. Built as a tomb for the pharaoh (法老) Khufu, it is the oldest and largest of all the pyramids. [1] It remains one of the seven wonders of the ancient world. Even today, scientists and historians are still trying to uncover its many secrets.

The biggest mystery is how the ancient Egyptians actually built it. The pyramid is made of over two million huge stone blocks. [2] Some of these enormous stones weigh as much as eighty elephants! Without modern machines like cranes or trucks, moving them seems like an impossible task.

Many theories have been suggested to explain this amazing achievement. [3] According to this popular idea, thousands of workers pulled the heavy stones up the slope using thick ropes and wooden sleds (雪橇). Water was poured on the sand to make the sleds slide more easily.

However, recent discoveries have brought up new questions. In 2017, scientists using exciting new technology found a large, hidden space inside the pyramid. [4] It is located just above the Grand Gallery, and researchers are currently trying to figure out what might be hidden inside.

[5] Although we have learned a lot about these ancient structures, there is always more to discover. Perhaps one day, the Great Pyramid will finally tell us all its ancient secrets.

## 选项

A. The exact purpose of this mysterious room is still completely unknown.
B. Therefore, most experts agree that aliens must have built the pyramids.
C. Some researchers believe that they built long ramps made of mud and brick.
D. Until then, these magnificent stone monuments will continue to amaze us.
E. Amazingly, it was the tallest man-made structure on Earth for over 3,800 years.
F. Each of these building blocks is incredibly heavy and hard to transport.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] E   [2] F   [3] C   [4] A   [5] D

## 深度解析

- **[1] E**：
  - **线索**：同义递进与语境连贯。空前提到了金字塔是“the oldest and largest（最古老和最大的）”。E选项“Amazingly, it was the tallest man-made structure on Earth for over 3,800 years”（令人惊叹的是，它在3800多年的时间里一直是地球上最高的人造建筑）进一步用具体的数据和“tallest（最高的）”延续并强化了前面关于其宏大规模的描述。且与后面“它是古代世界七大奇迹之一”的连贯性极佳。
- **[2] F**：
  - **线索**：同义复现与代词指代。空前提到“The pyramid is made of over two million huge stone blocks”（金字塔由超过两百万块巨大的石块组成）。F选项“Each of these building blocks is incredibly heavy and hard to transport”（这些建筑砌块每一块都极其沉重且难以运输）中的“these building blocks”完美指代了前面的“huge stone blocks”。同时，F项提出了“incredibly heavy（极其沉重）”的概念，直接引出了空后的一句具体形容：“其中一些巨大的石头重达八十头大象！”。
- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：举例说明与代词指代。空前指出“Many theories have been suggested...”（许多理论被提出来...）。C选项“Some researchers believe that they built long ramps made of mud and brick”（一些研究人员认为他们建造了由泥土和砖块制成的长坡道）正是前面“Many theories（许多理论）”中的一个具体例子。而且，空后句子以“According to this popular idea... up the slope”（根据这个流行的想法...上坡...）开头，不仅“this popular idea”完美指代C项介绍的观点，“the slope（坡）”也正是呼应了C项中的“ramps（坡道）”。
- **[4] A**：
  - **线索**：词汇同现与逻辑解释。空前提到了科学有了新发现：“found a large, hidden space inside the pyramid”（在金字塔内发现了一个巨大的隐藏空间）。A选项“The exact purpose of this mysterious room is still completely unknown”（这个神秘房间的确切用途仍然完全未知）中，“this mysterious room（这个神秘的房间）”同义替换并指代了前文的“hidden space（隐藏空间）”。空后“researchers are currently trying to figure out what might be hidden inside”也是对这句“完全未知所以努力探寻”的逻辑延续。
- **[5] D**：
  - **线索**：时态呼应与段落统领。空[5]位于文章结论段的段首。空后的两句都在表达虽然学到很多但还有更多等待发现，并在最后一句说“Perhaps one day, the Great Pyramid will finally tell us...”（也许有一天，大金字塔终会告诉我们...）。D选项“Until then, these magnificent stone monuments will continue to amaze us”（在那之前，这些宏伟的石头纪念碑将继续让我们惊叹）中的“Until then（在那之前）”与最后一句的“Perhaps one day（也许有一天）”形成了完美的时态逻辑呼应（在揭开所有秘密的那一天到来之前，它们将一直让我们惊叹）。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **B. Therefore, most experts agree that aliens must have built the pyramids.**：这一项极具迷惑性。前文提到了没有现代化机器建造金字塔似乎是“impossible task（不可能的任务）”，同时选项使用了“Therefore（因此）”。但是！选项的句意是“大多数专家同意金字塔必定是外星人建造的”，这与后文第三段重点讲述“流行观点认为是古代工人用坡道、绳子等建造”的科学阐述完全冲突。外星人建造在严肃科普文章中属于明显的逻辑谬误干扰选项。
