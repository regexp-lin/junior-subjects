**The Story of Kites: From China to the World**

Kites have a long history of more than two thousand years. They were first invented in China, where they were used not only for fun but also for sending messages and even measuring distances. [1] Chinese people made kites from silk and bamboo, materials that were light yet strong enough to fly in the wind. Over time, kite flying became a popular activity during festivals such as the Spring Festival. Today, kite making is still considered an important form of traditional Chinese art.

From China, kites spread to other parts of Asia and later to Europe. [2] In Japan, kites are often flown during the Children's Day festival. In Thailand, people hold kite fights where teams try to cut each other's kite strings. In many Western countries, kites are a symbol of freedom and joy, especially when families fly them at the beach or in parks on windy days. No matter where they go, kites seem to bring people together and create moments of happiness.

Why do kites fly? The answer lies in the wind and the shape of the kite. [3] When the wind blows against a kite, it creates an upward force called lift. The tail of the kite helps keep it stable and pointing in the right direction. A kite that is too heavy or has the wrong shape will fall. That is why kite makers spend a lot of time designing the right balance between weight, shape, and the length of the tail. A well-made kite can soar high in the sky with just a gentle breeze.

Flying kites is not only fun but also good for health. [4] When you fly a kite, you walk or run to keep it in the air, which is a form of exercise. You also spend time outdoors, breathing fresh air and enjoying the sunshine. In addition, focusing on the kite in the sky can help calm the mind and reduce stress. Many doctors suggest kite flying as a relaxing activity for both children and adults. It is a hobby that benefits both body and mind.

[5] From ancient China to modern parks around the world, kites have connected people across time and place. They remind us that simple things can bring great joy. Whether you fly a kite for the first time or the hundredth time, the feeling of watching it rise into the sky never gets old.

## 选项

A. In short, kites are more than toys—they are a bridge between cultures and generations.
B. Different countries have developed their own kite traditions and customs.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] D   [2] B   [3] C   [4] E   [5] A

## 深度解析

- **[1] D**：
  - **线索**：引出中国风筝的制作材料与工艺。空前说明"kites were first invented in China...used for fun, sending messages, measuring distances"，空后描述"Chinese people made kites from silk and bamboo...light yet strong...fly in the wind...kite making is still considered traditional Chinese art"。D选项"The science behind kite flying is simple, but it takes skill to master it"（风筝飞行的科学原理很简单，但掌握技巧需要功夫）中"science...simple...takes skill"与空后"materials that were light yet strong...kite making...art"形成呼应——材料与工艺需要技巧，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：风筝在不同国家的传播与本土化。空前说明"From China, kites spread to other parts of Asia and later to Europe"，空后列举"In Japan...Children's Day...In Thailand...kite fights...In many Western countries...symbol of freedom and joy...families fly them...". B选项"Different countries have developed their own kite traditions and customs"（不同国家发展出各自的风筝传统与习俗）中"Different countries...own traditions and customs"与空后日本、泰国、西方各国不同用法精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：解释风筝飞行的原理。空前提出"Why do kites fly? The answer lies in the wind and the shape"，空后详述"When the wind blows against a kite...creates lift...tail helps keep it stable...too heavy or wrong shape will fall...balance between weight, shape, tail"。C选项"In other words, flying a kite is a game between you, the kite, and the wind"（换言之，放风筝是你、风筝和风之间的配合）中"In other words"对上文进行概括，"game between you, kite, and wind"与空后"wind...lift...tail...balance"所体现的相互作用呼应，逻辑紧密。

- **[4] E**：
  - **线索**：放风筝对健康的益处。空前说明"Flying kites is not only fun but also good for health"，空后列举"walk or run...form of exercise...outdoors, fresh air, sunshine...calm the mind, reduce stress...relaxing activity...benefits both body and mind"。E选项"Kite flying can be a gentle way to stay active and feel closer to nature"（放风筝是一种温和的保持活力、亲近自然的方式）中"gentle way...stay active...closer to nature"与空后"exercise...outdoors...fresh air...calm the mind"完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] A**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后列举"From ancient China to modern parks...connected people across time and place...simple things can bring great joy...feeling...never gets old"。A选项"In short, kites are more than toys—they are a bridge between cultures and generations"（简言之，风筝不仅是玩具——它们是连接不同文化与世代的一座桥）用"In short"总结全文，"more than toys...bridge between cultures and generations"与空后"connected people across time and place...simple things...great joy"形成升华，收束精准。
