# 07-28 第一篇

**Mysteries of the Underwater World**

The ocean covers more than 70 percent of Earth's surface, yet we have explored only a small part of it. Scientists believe that millions of species still remain undiscovered in the deep sea. [1] Understanding these creatures helps us learn about life's ability to survive in extreme conditions.

The deep ocean is a world of darkness and pressure. Most of the seabed lies beyond the reach of sunlight. [2] Creatures like anglerfish have developed bioluminescence—they produce their own light to attract prey. Many deep-sea animals have large eyes or no eyes at all, depending on their environment.

The food chain in the deep sea works differently from that on land. [3] Organic matter from the surface slowly sinks to the bottom. Some organisms depend on "marine snow"—tiny particles of dead plants and animals that drift downward. Others feed on bacteria that live near hydrothermal vents.

Exploring the deep ocean faces many challenges. [4] Special submarines and robots can withstand the enormous pressure. Scientists use cameras and sensors to study life without disturbing it. Each dive brings new discoveries that change our understanding of marine biology.

The ocean holds secrets that may benefit humanity. [5] New medicines, sustainable food sources, and climate insights could come from studying the deep sea. Protecting these unexplored ecosystems is essential for future generations.

## 选项

A. This knowledge can guide our search for life elsewhere and inspire new technologies.
B. Some animals have adapted to find food in complete darkness.
C. This "rain" of nutrients supports entire communities of deep-sea life.
D. Advanced technology is required to reach and study these depths.
E. Research in these areas could lead to important discoveries for our planet.
F. For example, the Mariana Trench is the deepest point in the world's oceans.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍海洋探索的有限性及未发现物种；空后转入深层海洋的黑暗与压力。A 项“这种知识可指导我们寻找地外生命并启发新技术”承接“理解这些生物”的探索意义，自然引出下文对深海环境的描述。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前讲深海无光；空后以灯笼鱼发光为例。B 项“部分动物已适应在完全黑暗中觅食”点明适应主题，与后文具体例证衔接紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说深海食物链不同于陆地；空后提到有机物下沉和“海洋雪”。C 项“这种营养‘雨’支撑着整个深海生命群落”概括有机物下沉的作用，与后文形成总分关系。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前提出探索深海的挑战；空后列举潜水艇、机器人和仪器。D 项“需要先进技术才能到达并研究这些深处”概括技术需求，统领后文具体手段。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前提出海洋可能造福人类；空后展开新药、可持续食物和气候洞察。E 项“这些领域的研究可能为地球带来重要发现”总结该段主旨，与后文并列举例呼应。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the Mariana Trench...**：与深海探索相关，但属具体地理举例，未对应任何空位的承上启下功能，且与各段逻辑层次不符，易被误选。
