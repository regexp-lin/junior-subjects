# 05-22 第二篇

**Why Breakfast Matters: Fuel for Your Day**

Many people skip breakfast because they are in a hurry in the morning. They grab a snack later or wait until lunch to eat. But experts say that breakfast is the most important meal of the day. [1] That is because your body has gone many hours without food overnight. When you wake up, your brain and muscles need energy to work well. Eating a good breakfast helps you think clearly, focus better in class, and stay active. In fact, students who eat breakfast often do better in school than those who do not. So starting the day with a healthy meal sets you up for success.

What makes a healthy breakfast? [2] A balanced breakfast includes different types of food: some whole grains（全谷物）like oatmeal or whole-wheat bread, some protein like eggs or milk, and some fruit or vegetables. This mix gives your body the energy and nutrients it needs. Avoid eating only sweets or sugary drinks—they may give you a quick burst of energy, but you will feel tired soon after. In other words, the right combination of foods keeps you full and energised until lunch. It does not take long to prepare, and it makes a real difference to how you feel.

What happens if you skip breakfast? [3] Without breakfast, you may feel tired, irritable（易怒的）, or unable to concentrate. Your body has to use stored energy, and your blood sugar can drop, which affects your mood and performance. Some people who skip breakfast tend to overeat at lunch or snack on unhealthy food later. That is why skipping breakfast is not a good way to save time. In the long run, it can harm your health and your ability to learn. Eating breakfast is a simple habit that pays off every day.

How can busy people fit breakfast into their morning? [4] You can prepare things the night before—for example, cut up fruit, make overnight oats（隔夜燕麦）, or set the table. Keep simple foods on hand: bananas, yoghurt, nuts, and whole-grain cereal. Even a small breakfast is better than none. So with a little planning, breakfast can become part of your daily routine. Once you get into the habit, you will wonder how you ever started the day without it.

[5] In short, breakfast is essential for your body and mind. It gives you the energy to learn, play, and face the day. Whether you prefer a quick bowl of cereal or a more relaxed meal with your family, taking time for breakfast is one of the best choices you can make. Remember: you would not drive a car without fuel—do not start your day without breakfast either. So tomorrow morning, take a few minutes to eat something healthy. Your body and your grades will thank you.
