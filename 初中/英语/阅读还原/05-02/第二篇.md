# 05-02 第二篇

**The Story of Chocolate: From Bean to Bar**

Chocolate is one of the most popular treats in the world. Millions of people enjoy it every day, but few know the long journey it takes from a tropical tree to the candy bar in your hand. [1] The story of chocolate begins in the hot, rainy forests near the equator（赤道）, where cacao（可可） trees grow. These trees produce large pods（豆荚） that contain cacao beans—the main ingredient of all chocolate.

After the pods are harvested, the cacao beans inside are removed and left to ferment（发酵） for several days. [2] During fermentation, the beans develop the rich flavors that we recognize as chocolate. Without this step, the beans would taste bitter and unpleasant. After fermenting, the beans are dried in the sun, then packed and shipped to chocolate factories around the world.

At the factory, the beans go through several more steps. They are first roasted（烘烤） at high temperatures, which brings out even more flavor. [3] Then the roasted beans are cracked open and the outer shells are removed. The inner part, called the nib（可可仁）, is ground into a thick paste known as chocolate liquor（可可浆）. This paste is the base for all types of chocolate, from dark to milk to white.

To make the final product, sugar, milk, and other ingredients are added to the chocolate liquor. [4] The mixture is then heated, stirred, and cooled in a careful process called tempering（调温）. Tempering gives chocolate its smooth texture and shiny appearance. Finally, the chocolate is poured into molds（模具） and wrapped for sale. The whole process, from bean to bar, can take several weeks.

[5] Next time you unwrap a piece of chocolate, take a moment to think about the long journey it has made. From a tropical forest to your taste buds（味蕾）, every bite is the result of hard work, science, and a little bit of magic.

## 选项

A. This process is one of the most important steps in making chocolate.
B. In fact, the journey is quite complex and involves many stages.
C. The exact recipe depends on the type of chocolate being made.
D. As a result, chocolate has become a multi-billion-dollar industry worldwide.
E. This step is similar to baking bread, where heat changes the taste of the ingredients.
F. All in all, the story behind your favorite treat is more fascinating than you might expect.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] B   [2] A   [3] E   [4] C   [5] F

## 深度解析

- **[1] B**：
  - **线索**：引出巧克力的复杂制作过程。空前描述"Millions of people enjoy it every day, but few know the long journey it takes"，空后说明"The story of chocolate begins in the hot, rainy forests near the equator...cacao trees grow...produce large pods...contain cacao beans"。B选项"In fact, the journey is quite complex and involves many stages"（事实上，这段旅程非常复杂，涉及许多阶段）中"In fact"加强空前"few know the long journey"，"quite complex...many stages"自然引出空后从热带森林开始的详细叙述，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] A**：
  - **线索**：强调发酵的重要性。空前描述"the cacao beans inside are removed and left to ferment for several days"，空后说明"During fermentation, the beans develop the rich flavors...Without this step, the beans would taste bitter and unpleasant"。A选项"This process is one of the most important steps in making chocolate"（这个过程是制作巧克力最重要的步骤之一）中"This process"指代发酵，"most important steps"与空后"develop the rich flavors"和"Without this step...bitter"的因果关系完美呼应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] E**：
  - **线索**：烘烤对风味的作用。空前说明"They are first roasted at high temperatures, which brings out even more flavor"，空后描述"Then the roasted beans are cracked open and the outer shells are removed...ground into a thick paste"。E选项"This step is similar to baking bread, where heat changes the taste of the ingredients"（这一步类似于烤面包，热量改变了原料的味道）中"This step"指代烘烤，"similar to baking bread...heat changes the taste"与空前"roasted at high temperatures...brings out flavor"形成类比呼应，过渡到空后的下一步骤，衔接自然。

- **[4] C**：
  - **线索**：不同种类巧克力的配方差异。空前说明"sugar, milk, and other ingredients are added to the chocolate liquor"，空后描述"The mixture is then heated, stirred, and cooled...tempering gives chocolate its smooth texture and shiny appearance...poured into molds and wrapped"。C选项"The exact recipe depends on the type of chocolate being made"（确切的配方取决于所制作的巧克力种类）中"exact recipe"承接空前添加不同配料，"type of chocolate"与上段末尾"from dark to milk to white"呼应，过渡到空后统一的调温工艺，衔接得当。

- **[5] F**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与升华。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后呼吁"take a moment to think about the long journey...From a tropical forest to your taste buds, every bite is the result of hard work, science, and a little bit of magic"。F选项"All in all, the story behind your favorite treat is more fascinating than you might expect"（总而言之，你最爱的零食背后的故事比你想象的更精彩）用"All in all"总结全文，"story behind your favorite treat...more fascinating"与空后"long journey...hard work, science, and a little bit of magic"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **D. As a result, chocolate has become a multi-billion-dollar industry worldwide.**：涉及巧克力的商业价值，易被误填[1]。但D侧重"multi-billion-dollar industry"（商业产业），而空[1]前后文讨论的是巧克力从豆到棒的制作旅程，空后具体描述热带森林、可可树等制作起点；D的商业视角与全文"制作过程"主线不符，且缺乏与空后"The story...begins in the hot, rainy forests"的衔接逻辑。D属"主题相关但视角偏移"型干扰。
