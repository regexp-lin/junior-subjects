# 03-20 第二篇

**The Lost Art of Deep Listening**

In today's fast-paced, highly-connected world, true communication is becoming increasingly rare. We are constantly surrounded by text messages, emails, and social media notifications. [1] However, speaking and sending messages is not the same as truly understanding someone else.

The problem is that most of us have forgotten how to listen deeply. Often, when another person is talking, we are not really paying attention to their words. [2] This kind of "fake listening" makes the speaker feel unimportant and completely ignored.

Deep listening, on the other hand, is a skill that requires patience and total focus. It means putting away your smartphone and looking directly at the speaker's eyes. [3] Try to understand not just their actual words, but the hidden emotions behind those words. If they are talking about a difficult problem at school, notice if their voice sounds sad or frustrated.

Furthermore, a good listener does not quickly interrupt with advice. [4] Sometimes, people simply need a safe space to share their heavy feelings without being judged or told what to do immediately. By offering them quiet support and an open heart, you are giving them a wonderful gift.

[5] It builds much stronger friendships, creates deep trust, and makes both the speaker and the listener feel more connected to each other.

## 选项

A. They just want someone to sit patiently beside them and truly hear their story.
B. It is a powerful practice that can completely change our daily relationships.
C. Therefore, using the latest smartphone is the best way to improve communication.
D. You must give them your undivided attention without checking the time.
E. Instead, we are busy secretly planning what we are going to say next in our minds.
F. It seems that everyone is loudly sharing their opinions all the time.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] F   [2] E   [3] D   [4] A   [5] B

## 深度解析

- **[1] F**：
  - **线索**：对比转折与语境铺陈。空前描述了在快节奏的现代社会，真正的沟通很少见，我们被短信邮件包围。F选项“It seems that everyone is loudly sharing their opinions all the time”（似乎每个人都在大声分享他们的观点）描绘了一种“所有人都急于表达自己”的狂躁现状。这一现状刚好引出了空后一句的强转折“However, speaking and sending messages is not the same as truly understanding someone else（然而，说话和发信息并不等同于真正理解他人）”。一呼一应，逻辑浑然天成。
- **[2] E**：
  - **线索**：状态对比与因果联系。空前指出了我们的通病：当别人说话时，我们并没有真正在听。既然没在听，那我们在干什么呢？E选项“Instead, we are busy secretly planning what we are going to say next in our minds”（相反，我们正忙着在脑海中暗自计划接下来我们要说什么）非常生动地填补了这个动作空白。正因为我们心里忙着想自己的词，使得空后非常自然地得出一个无情的定性评判：“这种‘虚假的倾听（fake listening）’让说话者感到不被重视”。
- **[3] D**：
  - **线索**：顺承并列与动作细化。空前提出了深度倾听的初步要求：收起手机，直视对方的眼睛。这一物理动作要求后，跟进的是精神专注的要求。D选项“You must give them your undivided attention without checking the time”（你必须给予他们全神贯注而不去看时间）。随后空后的句子“不仅尝试去理解他们的词语，还要理解词语背后的隐藏情感”则是倾听状态的进一步深化。三句话由浅入深，将“如何做到深度倾听”讲得一清二楚。
- **[4] A**：
  - **线索**：语义递进与心理代入。空前提到“好倾听者不会急于插嘴给建议”。为什么不给建议呢？A选项“They just want someone to sit patiently beside them and truly hear their story”（他们只是想要有人耐心地坐在他们身边，真正听他们的故事）点明了倾诉者的核心诉求（只要有人听就行）。这与空后一句“Sometimes（有时），人们只是需要一个安全的空间分享情绪，而不是被评价或立刻被教导怎么做”构成了意思上的完美重合与递推。
- **[5] B**：
  - **线索**：总论定调与代词引导。空[5]位于文章结束段首。B选项“It is a powerful practice that can completely change our daily relationships”（这是一种强大的实践，可以彻底改变我们的日常关系）。“It”指代前文一直在讨论的核心主题“Deep listening（深度倾听）”。这句话作为文章的总起收尾，高度赞扬了深度倾听的价值。紧随其后的句子刚好列举了改变关系的具体表现：“它建立更牢固的友谊、建立深度信任，让说者和听者联系更紧密”。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **C. Therefore, using the latest smartphone is the best way to improve communication.**：该选项包含因果词“Therefore（因此）”且含有“smartphone”和“communication”等高频词。但其语义为“因此，使用最新款智能手机是改善沟通的最佳方式”。全文尤其是第三段明确主张了“putting away your smartphone（收起智能手机）”才是深度沟通的核心要义之一。这个干扰选项明显利用了与科技相反的主题背景制造迷惑，如果只是眼熟单词而不顾主旋律，极易中招。
