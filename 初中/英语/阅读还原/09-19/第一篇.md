# 09-19 第一篇

**Ethics in the Age of Artificial Intelligence**

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is changing our world quickly. It helps us in medicine, education, transportation, and many other areas. But as AI becomes more powerful, we must ask: How should we use it responsibly? [1] Thinking about the ethics of AI is no longer optional; it is necessary for ensuring that technology serves humanity in safe and fair ways.

One concern is fairness. AI systems learn from data, and if that data is biased, the AI may treat some groups unfairly. For example, a hiring tool might favor certain applicants over others. [2] Ensuring that AI is fair and does not discriminate requires careful attention to the data we use and the way we design these systems.

Privacy is another important issue. AI can collect and analyze huge amounts of personal information. This can be useful, but it can also be misused. [3] Protecting people's privacy while still allowing AI to bring benefits is a challenge that lawmakers and companies must address together.

There is also the question of jobs. AI can automate many tasks, which may replace some workers. At the same time, it can create new kinds of jobs. [4] Society needs to prepare for these changes by educating people for new roles and supporting those whose jobs are affected.

Finally, who is responsible when AI makes a mistake? If a self-driving car causes an accident, who is to blame? [5] Clear rules and shared responsibility are needed so that AI is used safely and those affected by its failures receive fair treatment.

## 选项

A. Addressing the ethical questions around AI is essential for building a future where technology benefits everyone fairly.
B. Fair and unbiased AI depends on using diverse, representative data and designing systems with equality in mind.
C. Finding a balance between the power of AI and the protection of personal information is an urgent task for society.
D. Preparing workers for an AI-driven economy and supporting those in transition are key to a smooth and fair shift.
E. Establishing accountability for AI systems will help ensure their safe use and proper handling of harm when it occurs.
F. For example, some AI chatbots have been found to give biased or harmful responses when trained on unfiltered internet data.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍AI广泛应用并引出如何负责任使用的问题，空后强调思考AI伦理的必要性。A的"Addressing the ethical questions around AI"与"Thinking about the ethics of AI"呼应，"benefits everyone fairly"与"serves humanity in safe and fair ways"一致。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说AI从数据学习，有偏数据会导致不公平，空后强调确保公平、避免歧视需要关注数据与设计。B的"Fair and unbiased AI depends on"与"Ensuring that AI is fair"呼应，"diverse, representative data"与"careful attention to the data"一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说AI可收集分析大量个人数据，有利有弊，空后强调在保护隐私与发挥AI效益之间取得平衡的挑战。C的"Finding a balance"与"Protecting people's privacy while still allowing AI to bring benefits"直接对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说AI可自动化任务取代部分工作，也可创造新岗位，空后强调社会需通过教育和支持来应对变化。D的"Preparing workers for an AI-driven economy"与"educating people for new roles"呼应，"supporting those in transition"与"supporting those whose jobs are affected"一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前举自动驾驶事故为例，问谁应担责，空后强调需要明确规则与责任分担。E的"Establishing accountability for AI systems"与"Clear rules and shared responsibility"呼应，"safe use and proper handling of harm"与"those affected by its failures receive fair treatment"一致。
