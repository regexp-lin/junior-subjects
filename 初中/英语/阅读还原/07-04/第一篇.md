# 07-04 第一篇

**The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Our Lives**

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is changing the world in ways we could hardly imagine a few years ago. From smartphones that understand our voice to cars that can drive themselves, AI is everywhere. [1] While AI brings many benefits, it also raises important questions about how we should use this powerful technology. It can help doctors find diseases earlier and help students learn in new ways. But it can also affect jobs and make decisions that were once made by humans. Understanding both the opportunities and challenges of AI is essential for everyone today.

[2] In education, AI is transforming how we learn. Smart learning apps can adapt to each student's level and provide personalized practice. Some programs can even grade essays and give instant feedback. This allows teachers to focus more on guiding students and less on repetitive tasks. However, we must be careful. Relying too much on AI might reduce critical thinking skills. Students still need to learn how to solve problems on their own. AI should assist learning, not replace the joy of discovering new ideas.

[3] AI is also changing the world of work. Machines can now do many tasks that used to require humans, from answering customer questions to analyzing large amounts of data. This can make businesses more efficient and create new types of jobs in technology. At the same time, some jobs may disappear as machines take over. Workers may need to learn new skills to stay employed. Society must find ways to help people adapt to these changes and ensure that the benefits of AI are shared by everyone.

[4] Privacy and fairness are major concerns with AI. AI systems collect and use huge amounts of data about us. Sometimes this data is used in ways we do not expect or approve of. There are also worries that AI might treat some people unfairly because of biases in the data it learns from. Rules and laws are needed to protect people's rights and ensure AI is used responsibly. As AI becomes more powerful, we must make sure it serves humanity, not the other way around.

[5] The future of AI depends on the choices we make today. We should embrace AI's potential to solve problems and improve our lives. At the same time, we must think carefully about its risks and set clear boundaries. Young people will live in a world shaped by AI, so learning about it now is important. By understanding both its power and its limits, we can help build a future where technology truly benefits all of humanity.

## 选项

A. AI offers great advantages, but we must also consider its challenges and use it wisely.
B. AI can support learning in powerful ways, yet human thinking and effort remain essential.
C. The rise of AI will reshape jobs and require workers to adapt with new skills.
D. Protecting privacy and ensuring fairness are key to using AI responsibly.
E. How we develop and use AI today will shape the world for future generations.
F. For example, voice assistants like Siri and Alexa can answer questions and play music.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍AI的普及（语音助手、自动驾驶等），空后转折列举AI的双面性：帮助医疗和教育，但也影响就业和人类决策。A选项"AI offers great advantages, but we must also consider its challenges and use it wisely"完美承上启下：用"advantages"呼应前文的便利，用"but"引出后文对机遇与挑战的讨论，并点明"明智使用"的主题。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：第二段论述AI在教育中的应用——个性化学习、即时反馈、减轻教师负担，但强调不能过度依赖、需保留批判性思维。B选项"AI can support learning in powerful ways, yet human thinking and effort remain essential"准确概括了段落的辩证观点。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：第三段讨论AI对就业的影响：替代部分工作、提高效率、创造新岗位、工人需学习新技能。C选项"The rise of AI will reshape jobs and require workers to adapt with new skills"精准总结段落核心。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：第四段聚焦隐私与公平问题——数据使用、偏见风险、需要法规。D选项"Protecting privacy and ensuring fairness are key to using AI responsibly"准确概括段落主旨。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：末段总结全文，强调今日选择决定AI未来、年轻人需了解AI、平衡潜力与风险。E选项"How we develop and use AI today will shape the world for future generations"精准呼应段落的总结性主旨。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example...**：以"For example"开头，列举Siri和Alexa能回答问题、播放音乐。虽与AI相关，但这是支撑第一段"AI无处不在"的具体例证，而非段落的概括句。若放入[1]会造成层次错位——F是论据层，正确选项A是承上启下的主题概括层。
