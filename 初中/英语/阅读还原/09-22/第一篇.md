# 09-22 第一篇

**The Value of Cross-Cultural Exchange**

We live in a world that is more connected than ever. People from different countries work, study, and travel across borders every day. Yet cultural differences can sometimes lead to misunderstandings. [1] Learning about other cultures and developing cultural awareness can help us build better relationships and work more effectively in our global community.

Language is one bridge between cultures. Speaking another language opens doors to new ideas, stories, and ways of thinking. Even learning a few phrases can show respect and build trust. [2] Language learning goes beyond words; it helps us understand the values and customs of people from different backgrounds.

Food, music, and art also bring people together. Sharing a meal, listening to traditional music, or visiting a museum can create connections that words alone cannot. [3] Cultural experiences allow us to appreciate diversity and find common ground with others, even when our traditions differ.

Being open-minded is essential. We may not always agree with or understand every custom we encounter. The key is to approach differences with curiosity rather than judgment. [4] A respectful and curious attitude toward other cultures reduces conflict and opens the door to genuine friendship and cooperation.

Schools and organizations can support cross-cultural learning. Exchange programs, language clubs, and cultural events offer opportunities to engage with people from around the world. [5] When we actively seek to learn from and connect with different cultures, we enrich our own lives and help create a more peaceful and united world.

## 选项

A. Gaining knowledge of other cultures and improving our cultural sensitivity are key to thriving in an interconnected world.
B. Learning a language opens a window into the minds and traditions of those who speak it.
C. Through shared cultural activities, we can celebrate our differences and discover what we have in common.
D. Approaching other cultures with respect and curiosity rather than criticism helps us build bridges instead of walls.
E. Making an effort to learn from and connect with diverse cultures benefits both ourselves and the world we share.
F. For example, international students often form lasting friendships with local students through shared activities and conversations.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述世界互联加强、文化差异可能引发误解，空后强调学习其他文化和提高文化意识的价值。A的"Gaining knowledge of other cultures and improving our cultural sensitivity"与"Learning about other cultures and developing cultural awareness"呼应，"key to thriving in an interconnected world"与"build better relationships and work more effectively"一致。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前说语言是文化桥梁、学外语可接触新观念、几句短语也能表达尊重，空后强调语言学习超越词汇本身。B的"Learning a language opens a window into the minds and traditions"与"Language learning goes beyond words"、"understand the values and customs"呼应。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前说食物、音乐、艺术能凝聚人，分享体验可建立难以言说的联系，空后强调文化体验让我们欣赏差异并发现共同点。C的"Through shared cultural activities"与"Sharing a meal, listening to traditional music"对应，"celebrate our differences and discover what we have in common"与"appreciate diversity and find common ground"一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说可能不理解每种习俗，关键在于以好奇而非评判面对差异，空后强调尊重好奇的态度可减少冲突、促进友谊合作。D的"Approaching other cultures with respect and curiosity rather than criticism"与"A respectful and curious attitude"呼应，"build bridges instead of walls"与"reduces conflict and opens the door"一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前列举学校组织可提供的跨文化学习机会，空后强调主动学习与联结不同文化可丰富自身并推动世界和平。E的"Making an effort to learn from and connect with diverse cultures"与"actively seek to learn from and connect with different cultures"直接对应，"benefits both ourselves and the world we share"与"enrich our own lives and help create a more peaceful and united world"一致。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, international students often form lasting friendships with local students through shared activities and conversations.**：以For example开头，是具体人群案例；易误放[3]或[5]，但[3]需概括文化体验的作用，[5]需主动学习与联结的概括，非单一群体举例。
