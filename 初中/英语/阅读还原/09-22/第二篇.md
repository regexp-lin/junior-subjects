# 09-22 第二篇

**Secrets of the Deep Ocean**

More than seventy percent of Earth's surface is covered by ocean, yet we have explored only a small part of it. The deep sea, where sunlight cannot reach, is one of the least understood places on our planet. [1] The deep ocean holds many mysteries, and scientists are only beginning to discover what lies beneath the waves.

The deep ocean is extremely harsh. There is no light, the water is near freezing, and the pressure is crushing. Yet life exists there. Strange fish, glowing creatures, and worms that feed on chemicals thrive in the darkness. [2] Despite the extreme conditions, the deep sea is home to an amazing variety of life that has adapted to survive in one of Earth's most challenging environments.

New technology has changed our ability to explore. Underwater robots, deep-sea cameras, and special submarines can now reach depths that were once impossible. [3] Better tools are allowing scientists to study deep-sea life and habitats in ways that were unimaginable just a few decades ago.

The deep ocean may also hold answers to important questions. Some deep-sea organisms produce substances that could help in medicine. The seafloor contains minerals and resources that might be useful in the future. [4] Exploring the deep ocean could lead to new discoveries that benefit science, medicine, and industry.

We must also protect what we find. The deep ocean is fragile. Pollution, climate change, and deep-sea mining could damage ecosystems we have barely begun to understand. [5] As we learn more about the deep sea, we have a responsibility to use that knowledge to protect it for future generations.

## 选项

A. The deep ocean remains largely unknown, and each new exploration brings us closer to understanding its secrets.
B. Life in the deep sea has evolved in remarkable ways to survive under conditions that would be deadly for most other organisms.
C. Advances in technology are opening up the deep ocean to scientific study like never before.
D. The deep ocean may offer valuable resources and insights that could change how we live and heal.
E. With greater knowledge of the deep ocean comes a duty to safeguard it from harm for those who come after us.
F. For example, the anglerfish uses a glowing lure to attract prey in the pitch-black depths of the ocean.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前描述海洋覆盖地球大部分、深海阳光难达且知之甚少，空后强调深海藏有许多谜团、科学家才开始探索。A的"The deep ocean remains largely unknown"与"least understood places"呼应，"each new exploration brings us closer to understanding its secrets"与"only beginning to discover"一致。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前描述深海极端环境（无光、近冰点、高压）及奇特种类的存在，空后强调尽管条件恶劣，深海仍养育了多样适应生命。B的"Life in the deep sea has evolved in remarkable ways to survive"与"life has adapted to survive"呼应，"conditions that would be deadly for most other organisms"与"one of Earth's most challenging environments"一致。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前列举新技术（水下机器人、深潜相机、潜艇）使到达深渊成为可能，空后强调更好工具使科学家能以从前难以想象的方式研究。C的"Advances in technology are opening up the deep ocean"与"Better tools are allowing scientists"呼应，"like never before"与"unimaginable just a few decades ago"一致。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前说深海底可能有医药物质、矿物和未来资源，空后强调探索深海可能带来科学、医药和工业新发现。D的"The deep ocean may offer valuable resources and insights"与"hold answers to important questions"、"produce substances"、"contains minerals"呼应，"could change how we live and heal"与"benefit science, medicine, and industry"一致。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前说深海脆弱、污染和开采可能破坏尚未完全理解的生态系统，空后强调在了解更多之时有责任予以保护。E的"With greater knowledge of the deep ocean comes a duty to safeguard it"与"As we learn more...we have a responsibility to...protect it"直接对应，"for those who come after us"与"for future generations"一致。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, the anglerfish uses a glowing lure to attract prey in the pitch-black depths of the ocean.**：以For example开头，是具体物种案例；易误放[2]，但[2]需概括深海生物适应性的普遍规律，非单一物种习性举例。
