# 11-06 第二篇

**Social Media: Benefits and Challenges**

Social media has changed the way we communicate. Platforms like WeChat, TikTok, Instagram, and others let us share photos, send messages, and stay in touch with friends and family across the world. We can follow news, learn new skills from videos, and join communities that share our interests. For many people, social media is a useful tool for connection and self-expression. [1] When used wisely, social media can enrich our lives by keeping us connected and informed in ways that were never possible before.

Social media also offers opportunities for learning and creativity. Students can find educational videos on almost any subject. Artists and musicians can share their work with a global audience. Small businesses can reach customers without expensive advertising. During the pandemic, many schools used social media to continue teaching when classrooms were closed. [2] The potential of social media as a tool for education, creativity, and opportunity is vast, but it depends on how we choose to use it.

However, social media has downsides too. Spending too much time scrolling can affect sleep, focus, and mood. Some people feel anxious or left out when they compare their lives to the polished images others post. Cyberbullying is a serious problem, especially among young users. False information spreads quickly online and can cause confusion or harm. [3] Being aware of these risks helps us use social media more safely and avoid its negative effects on our mental and physical well-being.

Parents and educators often wonder how to guide young people in the digital world. Setting limits on screen time, discussing online safety, and teaching critical thinking about what we see online are important steps. Young people themselves can learn to notice when social media makes them feel bad and take breaks when needed. [4] Finding a healthy balance between connection and overload is key, and it requires both support from adults and responsible choices from users.

Looking ahead, social media will continue to evolve. New apps and features will appear, and the ways we interact online will keep changing. The question is not whether to use social media—for most of us, it is part of modern life—but how to use it in a way that serves us well. [5] Developing good digital habits early will help young people navigate the online world confidently and make the most of its benefits while minimizing its risks.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：空前介绍社交媒体的普及——微信、TikTok、Instagram等，分享、沟通、全球联络、关注新闻、学习技能、加入兴趣社区，强调对多数人是有用的联结与表达工具，空后需概括合理使用时的正面价值。A的"When used wisely"引出条件，"enrich our lives by keeping us connected and informed"与前文"connection and self-expression"及"follow news, learn"对应，"ways that were never possible before"突出其革新性。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：空前列举社交媒体的学习与创造功能——教育视频、艺术家与音乐人展示作品、小企业营销、疫情期间线上教学，空后需总结其作为教育、创意与机遇工具的潜力。B的"education, creativity, and opportunity"与前文各例对应，"depends on how we choose to use it"为下段讨论风险做过渡。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：空前描述社交媒体的消极面——过度使用影响睡眠与注意力、攀比焦虑、网络欺凌、虚假信息传播，空后需提出应对这些风险的态度。C的"Being aware of these risks"与前文列举呼应，"use social media more safely and avoid its negative effects"与"mental and physical well-being"对应。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：空前讨论家长与教师的角色——限制屏幕时间、讨论网络安全、培养批判性思维，以及青少年自我调节——察觉负面情绪、适时休息，空后需总结平衡联结与过载的重要性。D的"Finding a healthy balance between connection and overload"与前文正面与负面内容呼应，"support from adults and responsible choices from users"与前文家长引导与青少年选择对应。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：空前展望社交媒体未来发展、新应用与新功能，强调问题不是用不用而是如何用好，空后需总结对青少年的长期建议。E的"Developing good digital habits early"与"young people"和"navigate the online world"呼应，"make the most of its benefits while minimizing its risks"总结全文利弊主题，是合理收尾。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For instance, some influencers have millions of followers and earn money by promoting products in their posts and videos.**：以For instance开头，为网红营销的具体案例。各空均为概括或过渡句；[2]易被误选因涉及社交媒体机会，但该空需概括教育、创意、机遇等方面的总体潜力，而非网红变现的单一案例。
