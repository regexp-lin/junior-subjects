**The Many Uses of Bamboo**

Bamboo is a type of grass that grows in Asia, Africa, and the Americas. It can grow very quickly—some species add a metre in a single day—and it is one of the strongest natural materials in the world. For thousands of years, people have used bamboo for building, cooking, writing, and countless other purposes. [1] In other words, bamboo is a versatile plant that has served human needs in many ways. Today, as we look for sustainable alternatives to plastic and wood, bamboo is attracting new attention.

Bamboo has long been used in construction. [2] In many Asian countries, bamboo has been used to build houses, bridges, and scaffolding（脚手架）because it is strong yet flexible. It can bend in strong winds without breaking, making it ideal for earthquake-prone areas. Bamboo poles are also used to make fences, baskets, and furniture. In recent years, engineers have developed bamboo as a modern building material—pressed into panels and boards that can replace wood in some uses. This "engineered bamboo" is as strong as some kinds of hardwood and grows much faster than trees.

Bamboo is also important for food and daily life. [3] Young bamboo shoots are a popular vegetable in Asian cooking, rich in vitamins and fibre. The hollow stems can store water or be cut into cups and containers. In the past, bamboo was used to make paper, musical instruments such as flutes, and even early forms of bicycles. Today, bamboo is used to make cutting boards, toothbrushes, clothing, and flooring. Its hollow structure makes it light and easy to work with, while its natural growth without fertilisers or pesticides makes it a favourite among those who care about the environment.

As a renewable resource, bamboo has environmental advantages. [4] It grows quickly and can be harvested every few years without killing the plant—new shoots grow from the same roots. Unlike many trees, bamboo absorbs a lot of carbon dioxide as it grows, which helps reduce greenhouse gases. However, turning bamboo into products still needs energy and transport, so it is not perfectly "green." Choosing bamboo products from sustainable sources and using them for a long time can help make the most of its benefits.

[5] Bamboo is a gift of nature that has served humans for thousands of years and continues to offer solutions for a sustainable future. From ancient buildings to modern eco-friendly products, this remarkable plant shows that the best ideas sometimes come from the natural world. The next time you see bamboo—in a garden, a product, or a dish—remember its long history and its many roles in human life.

## 选项

A. In other words, bamboo is a versatile plant that has served human needs in many ways.
B. That is because it is strong and flexible, used for buildings, scaffolding, and now modern panels and boards.
C. In fact, bamboo is used for food, containers, instruments, and many everyday products.
D. These qualities make bamboo a renewable and eco-friendly choice, though not without some impact.
E. In short, bamboo has served humans for ages and continues to offer solutions for a sustainable future.
F. For example, pandas in China eat bamboo as their main food.

## 答案键（Answer Key）

[1] A   [2] B   [3] C   [4] D   [5] E

## 深度解析

- **[1] A**：
  - **线索**：概括竹子的多用途特性。空前描述"Bamboo type of grass...grows in Asia, Africa, Americas...grows very quickly...strongest natural materials...thousands of years used for building, cooking, writing, countless purposes"，空后说明"bamboo is versatile plant that has served human needs in many ways...Today...sustainable alternatives to plastic and wood...attracting new attention"。A选项"In other words, bamboo is a versatile plant that has served human needs in many ways"（换言之，竹子是一种用途广泛的植物，以多种方式满足人类需求）中"In other words"概括空前，"versatile...served human needs in many ways"与空后"versatile plant...served human needs in many ways"精准呼应，衔接顺畅。

- **[2] B**：
  - **线索**：竹子在建筑与结构中的使用。空前提出"Bamboo has long been used in construction"，空后详述"In many Asian countries...build houses, bridges, scaffolding...strong yet flexible...bend in strong winds without breaking...ideal for earthquake-prone areas...fences, baskets, furniture...recent years...engineered bamboo...panels, boards...replace wood...as strong as hardwood, grows much faster"。B选项"That is because it is strong and flexible, used for buildings, scaffolding, and now modern panels and boards"（那是因为它既坚固又柔韧，被用于建筑、脚手架，以及如今的现代板材）中"That is because"解释建筑用途，"strong and flexible...buildings, scaffolding...modern panels and boards"与空后 strong、flexible、scaffolding、panels、boards 完美对应，逻辑紧密。

- **[3] C**：
  - **线索**：竹子在饮食与日用品中的用途。空前提出"Bamboo is also important for food and daily life"，空后列举"Young shoots popular vegetable...vitamins, fibre...hollow stems store water, cups, containers...past: paper, musical instruments, flutes, early bicycles...today: cutting boards, toothbrushes, clothing, flooring...hollow structure light, easy to work...natural growth without fertilisers, pesticides...favourite for environment-conscious"。C选项"In fact, bamboo is used for food, containers, instruments, and many everyday products"（事实上，竹子被用于食物、容器、乐器及许多日常用品）中"In fact"强化用途多样性，"food, containers, instruments, everyday products"与空后 shoots、containers、instruments、cutting boards 等精准对应，衔接自然。

- **[4] D**：
  - **线索**：竹子作为可再生资源的环保优势与局限。空前提出"As a renewable resource, bamboo has environmental advantages"，空后描述"Grows quickly...harvested every few years without killing...new shoots from same roots...absorbs lot of carbon dioxide...helps reduce greenhouse gases...However turning into products still needs energy, transport...not perfectly green...choosing from sustainable sources, using long time...make most of benefits"。D选项"These qualities make bamboo a renewable and eco-friendly choice, though not without some impact"（这些特性使竹子成为可再生且环保的选择，尽管并非全无影响）中"These qualities"指代空后生长快、固碳等，"renewable...eco-friendly...not without some impact"与空后 renewable、absorbs CO2、not perfectly green 完美对应，衔接得当。

- **[5] E**：
  - **线索**：全文收束与竹子的历史与未来价值。空[5]位于结尾段段首，空后总结"Bamboo gift of nature...served humans thousands of years...continues to offer solutions for sustainable future...ancient buildings to modern eco-friendly products...remarkable plant...best ideas from natural world...next time see bamboo...remember long history, many roles"。E选项"In short, bamboo has served humans for ages and continues to offer solutions for a sustainable future"（简言之，竹子长久以来服务于人类，并持续为可持续未来提供解决方案）用"In short"总结全文，"served humans for ages...solutions for sustainable future"与空后"served...thousands of years...solutions for sustainable future"形成呼应，收束精准。

- **干扰项分析**：
  - **F. For example, pandas in China eat bamboo as their main food.**：涉及熊猫以竹为主食，易被误填[3]。但F以"For example"引出动物食用竹子，而空[3]讨论的是人类对竹子的利用（食物指竹笋、容器、乐器、日用品等），F的"pandas...main food"属于动物饮食，与"food, containers, instruments, products"的人类用途焦点不同。F属"主题相关但论述层次错位"型干扰。
