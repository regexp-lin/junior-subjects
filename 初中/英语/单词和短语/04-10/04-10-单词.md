## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **verify** /ˈverɪfaɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：核实；证实；验证
- **英文释义**：to check or prove that something is true or correct
- **例句**：
  - Please verify your email address by clicking the link.
  - 请通过点击链接验证你的邮箱地址。
  - The police verified the suspect's alibi with security cameras.
  - 警方用监控摄像头核实了嫌疑人的不在场证明。
- **助记**：veri（真实）+ fy（使）→ 使变得真实 → 核实；名词：verification（验证）

### 2. **demolish** /dɪˈmɒlɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：拆除；推翻；摧毁
- **英文释义**：to completely destroy a building or structure; to prove that an idea is wrong
- **例句**：
  - The old bridge was demolished to make way for a new one.
  - 旧桥被拆除以给新桥让路。
  - The lawyer demolished the witness's argument in court.
  - 律师在法庭上推翻了证人的论点。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ mol（磨/碎）+ ish → 完全碾碎 → 拆除；名词：demolition（拆除）

### 3. **delegate** /ˈdelɪɡeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：委派；授权；代表
- **英文释义**：to give duties or responsibilities to someone; a person sent to represent others
- **例句**：
  - The manager delegated the task to his assistant.
  - 经理把任务委派给了他的助手。
  - Each class chose a delegate to attend the school meeting.
  - 每个班选了一名代表参加学校会议。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ leg（送/派）+ ate → 向下派遣 → 委派；名词：delegation（代表团）

### 4. **sentiment** /ˈsentɪmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：情感；感情；观点
- **英文释义**：a feeling or opinion, especially based on emotions rather than reason
- **例句**：
  - Public sentiment is strongly against building a factory near the school.
  - 公众强烈反对在学校附近建工厂。
  - The card expressed warm sentiments of friendship.
  - 贺卡表达了温暖的友谊情感。
- **助记**：sent（感觉）+ iment → 感觉到的东西 → 情感；形容词：sentimental（多愁善感的）

### 5. **diagnosis** /ˌdaɪəɡˈnəʊsɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：诊断；判断
- **英文释义**：the identification of an illness or problem by examining the symptoms
- **例句**：
  - The doctor made a diagnosis after examining the patient carefully.
  - 医生仔细检查病人后做出了诊断。
  - Early diagnosis of the disease can save many lives.
  - 疾病的早期诊断可以挽救许多生命。
- **助记**：dia（穿过）+ gnos（知道）+ is → 通过了解来判断 → 诊断；动词：diagnose（诊断）

### 6. **notorious** /nəʊˈtɔːriəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：臭名昭著的；声名狼藉的
- **英文释义**：famous for something bad
- **例句**：
  - The area is notorious for its heavy traffic during rush hour.
  - 该地区因高峰时段交通拥堵而臭名昭著。
  - He is a notorious criminal wanted by police in several countries.
  - 他是一个被多国警方通缉的臭名昭著的罪犯。
- **助记**：not（知道）+ orious → 因坏事而被人知道的 → 臭名昭著的；反义词：famous（著名的，正面）

### 7. **superficial** /ˌsuːpəˈfɪʃl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：表面的；肤浅的；浅层的
- **英文释义**：appearing to be true or real only until examined more closely; not deep
- **例句**：
  - He only has a superficial understanding of the subject.
  - 他对这个课题只有肤浅的理解。
  - The wound was only superficial and healed quickly.
  - 伤口只是表面的，很快就愈合了。
- **助记**：super（在上面）+ fic（表面）+ ial → 在表面上的 → 表面的/肤浅的

### 8. **allegedly** /əˈledʒɪdli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：据称；据说
- **英文释义**：used to say that something is claimed to be true but has not been proved
- **例句**：
  - He allegedly stole the money from the store, but there's no proof.
  - 据称他偷了商店的钱，但没有证据。
  - The painting is allegedly worth millions of dollars.
  - 据说这幅画价值数百万美元。
- **助记**：allege（声称）+ d + ly → 据声称地 → 据称；动词：allege（声称）

### 9. **formulate** /ˈfɔːmjuleɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：制定；构想；明确表达
- **英文释义**：to create or prepare a plan, idea, or method carefully
- **例句**：
  - The team formulated a new strategy to improve sales.
  - 团队制定了一项提高销售额的新策略。
  - It took him a while to formulate his thoughts clearly.
  - 他花了一段时间才清楚地表达出自己的想法。
- **助记**：formula（公式）+ ate → 用公式来制作 → 制定；名词：formulation（制定）

### 10. **endeavor** /ɪnˈdevə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：努力；尝试；事业
- **英文释义**：to try hard to do or achieve something
- **例句**：
  - We should endeavor to protect the environment for future generations.
  - 我们应该努力为子孙后代保护环境。
  - Scientific endeavor has led to many great discoveries.
  - 科学探索带来了许多伟大的发现。
- **助记**：en（使）+ deavor（尽力）→ 使自己尽力 → 努力；近义词：strive（努力）, attempt（尝试）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **meticulous** /məˈtɪkjələs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的；细致的
- **英文释义**：showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - She is meticulous about keeping her room clean and tidy.
  - 她对保持房间整洁一丝不苟。
  - The scientist conducted meticulous research before publishing the paper.
  - 科学家在发表论文前进行了细致的研究。
- **助记**：met（超越）+ icul（小）+ ous → 连小事都超越常人关注的 → 一丝不苟的

### 12. **corroborate** /kəˈrɒbəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：证实；佐证
- **英文释义**：to provide evidence or information that supports a claim
- **例句**：
  - The witness's testimony corroborated the victim's story.
  - 目击者的证词佐证了受害者的陈述。
  - Scientific data corroborated the theory proposed by the researcher.
  - 科学数据证实了研究人员提出的理论。
- **助记**：cor（共同）+ robor（力量）+ ate → 共同加力 → 证实；名词：corroboration（证实）

### 13. **remuneration** /rɪˌmjuːnəˈreɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：报酬；薪酬
- **英文释义**：money paid to someone for work or services
- **例句**：
  - The remuneration for the position is quite competitive.
  - 该职位的薪酬很有竞争力。
  - Fair remuneration is essential to attract talented employees.
  - 公平的报酬对于吸引有才华的员工至关重要。
- **助记**：re（回）+ muner（赠予）+ ation → 给予回报 → 报酬；动词：remunerate（支付报酬）

### 14. **clandestine** /klænˈdestɪn/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：秘密的；暗中的
- **英文释义**：done secretly, especially something that is not officially allowed
- **例句**：
  - The spies held a clandestine meeting in an abandoned warehouse.
  - 间谍在一个废弃仓库里举行了秘密会议。
  - Clandestine activities are often associated with espionage.
  - 秘密活动通常与间谍活动有关。
- **助记**：clan（暗中）+ destine（确定）→ 暗中确定的 → 秘密的；近义词：covert, secret

### 15. **substantiate** /səbˈstænʃieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：证实；证明…有根据
- **英文释义**：to provide evidence to prove that something is true
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer asked for more evidence to substantiate the claim.
  - 律师要求提供更多证据来证实这一说法。
  - Can you substantiate your accusations with specific examples?
  - 你能用具体的例子证实你的指控吗？
- **助记**：sub（在下面）+ stant（站立）+ iate → 让某事站得住脚 → 证实；名词：substantiation（证实）
