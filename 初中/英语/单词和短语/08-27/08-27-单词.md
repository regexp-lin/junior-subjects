## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **linger** /ˈlɪŋɡər/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：逗留；徘徊
- **英文释义**：to stay somewhere longer than necessary; to take a long time to leave or disappear
- **例句**：
  - She lingered at the door, not wanting to say goodbye.
  - 她在门口逗留，不舍得告别。
  - The smell of coffee lingered in the kitchen.
  - 咖啡的香气在厨房里久久不散。
- **助记**：源自古英语 lengen（延长）→ 延长逗留的时间 → 逗留；徘徊

### 2. **liquid** /ˈlɪkwɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：液体；液态的
- **英文释义**：a substance that flows freely, like water or oil; in the form of a liquid, not solid or gas
- **例句**：
  - Water is a liquid at room temperature.
  - 水在室温下是液体。
  - Pour the liquid into a clean glass.
  - 把液体倒入干净的玻璃杯。
- **助记**：liquidus（流动的）→ 能流动的物质 → 液体；液态的

### 3. **literacy** /ˈlɪtərəsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：读写能力
- **英文释义**：the ability to read and write; knowledge of a particular subject
- **例句**：
  - The government is working to improve literacy rates.
  - 政府正致力于提高识字率。
  - Digital literacy is essential in the modern world.
  - 数字素养在现代社会必不可少。
- **助记**：liter（文字）+ -acy（状态）→ 能读能写的状态 → 读写能力

### 4. **literal** /ˈlɪtərəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：字面的
- **英文释义**：taking words in their usual or most basic sense; exactly as stated, without metaphor or exaggeration
- **例句**：
  - I'm not speaking in the literal sense.
  - 我不是从字面意思上说的。
  - The literal translation sounds awkward.
  - 字面翻译听起来很拗口。
- **助记**：liter（文字）+ -al → 按照字面意思的 → 字面的

### 5. **litter** /ˈlɪtər/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：垃圾；乱扔
- **英文释义**：pieces of paper, cans, etc. that people have left in a public place; to scatter rubbish around
- **例句**：
  - Don't drop litter on the street.
  - 不要在大街上乱扔垃圾。
  - The beach was covered with litter after the festival.
  - 节日过后海滩上到处是垃圾。
- **助记**：源自法语 lit（床）→ 古代指垫窝的稻草 → 引申为散落的杂物 → 垃圾；乱扔

### 6. **loan** /ləʊn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：贷款；借出
- **英文释义**：money that you borrow from a bank; the act of lending something; to lend
- **例句**：
  - He took out a loan to buy a car.
  - 他贷款买了一辆车。
  - Can you loan me some money until Friday?
  - 你能借我点钱到周五吗？
- **助记**：源自古诺尔斯语 lán → 借出或借入的东西 → 贷款；借出

### 7. **lobby** /ˈlɒbi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：大厅；游说
- **英文释义**：a large area inside the entrance of a public building; to try to persuade politicians to support a particular cause
- **例句**：
  - I'll wait for you in the hotel lobby.
  - 我在酒店大厅等你。
  - Environmental groups lobby for stricter pollution laws.
  - 环保组织游说要求更严格的污染法规。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 lobium（走廊）→ 入口处的大空间 → 大厅；引申为在议会走廊活动 → 游说

### 8. **logic** /ˈlɒdʒɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：逻辑
- **英文释义**：a way of thinking or explaining something that seems reasonable; the science of reasoning
- **例句**：
  - There's no logic in his argument.
  - 他的论点没有逻辑。
  - I fail to see the logic of your plan.
  - 我看不出你计划的逻辑何在。
- **助记**：源自希腊语 logos（言语、推理）→ 推理的学问 → 逻辑

### 9. **loyal** /ˈlɔɪəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：忠诚的
- **英文释义**：staying firm in your support of a person, organization, or belief
- **例句**：
  - He has been a loyal friend for many years.
  - 多年来他一直是个忠诚的朋友。
  - The dog is loyal to its owner.
  - 狗对主人很忠诚。
- **助记**：源自法语 loi（法律）→ 遵守誓言、法律 → 忠诚的

### 10. **luggage** /ˈlʌɡɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：行李
- **英文释义**：bags, suitcases, etc. that you take with you when you travel
- **例句**：
  - Please collect your luggage from the carousel.
  - 请到传送带上取您的行李。
  - I've packed my luggage for the trip.
  - 我已经收拾好出行的行李了。
- **助记**：lug（拖、拉）+ -age（名词后缀）→ 需要拖拉携带的东西 → 行李

### 高一词汇

### 11. **melancholy** /ˈmelənkəli/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：忧郁
- **英文释义**：a feeling of sadness, especially for no obvious reason; sad and thoughtful
- **例句**：
  - A sense of melancholy filled the room as they said goodbye.
  - 告别时，一种忧郁的气氛弥漫房间。
  - She has a melancholy temperament.
  - 她性情忧郁。
- **助记**：melan（黑）+ chol（胆汁）→ 古希腊认为黑胆汁过多导致忧郁 → 忧郁

### 12. **meticulous** /məˈtɪkjələs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：一丝不苟的
- **英文释义**：paying great attention to detail; very careful and precise
- **例句**：
  - She is meticulous about her work.
  - 她对工作一丝不苟。
  - The report was prepared with meticulous care.
  - 这份报告是经过一丝不苟的精心准备的。
- **助记**：metic（恐惧）+ -ulous（充满……的）→ 对差错充满恐惧 → 一丝不苟的

### 13. **mitigate** /ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：减轻；缓和
- **英文释义**：to make something less harmful, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Measures were taken to mitigate the effects of the drought.
  - 采取了措施以减轻干旱的影响。
  - Nothing could mitigate the severity of the loss.
  - 没有什么能减轻这次损失的严重性。
- **助记**：mitis（温和）+ ag（做）→ 使变温和 → 减轻；缓和

### 14. **mundane** /mʌnˈdeɪn/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：平凡的；世俗的
- **英文释义**：ordinary and not interesting; relating to the physical world rather than spiritual matters
- **例句**：
  - I'm tired of these mundane daily tasks.
  - 我厌倦了这些平凡的日常琐事。
  - The film explores both mundane and magical aspects of life.
  - 影片探讨了生活中平凡与神奇两面。
- **助记**：mundus（世界）+ -ane → 属于世俗世界的 → 平凡的；世俗的

### 15. **naive** /naɪˈiːv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：天真的；幼稚的
- **英文释义**：lacking experience, wisdom, or judgment; innocent and trusting
- **例句**：
  - She was young and naive when she first came to the city.
  - 她初到这座城市时年轻天真。
  - It was naive of him to believe their promises.
  - 他相信他们的承诺真是太幼稚了。
- **助记**：源自法语 naïf（天生的、自然的）→ 未经世故的 → 天真的；幼稚的
