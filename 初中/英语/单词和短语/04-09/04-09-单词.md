## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **associate** /əˈsəʊʃieɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：联想；联系；交往
- **英文释义**：to connect someone or something in your mind with someone or something else
- **例句**：
  - People often associate spring with new beginnings and hope.
  - 人们常常把春天与新的开始和希望联系在一起。
  - She doesn't want to be associated with that group anymore.
  - 她不想再和那个团体有联系了。
- **助记**：as（朝）+ soci（同伴）+ ate → 朝同伴方向 → 联想/交往；名词：association（联系；协会）

### 2. **flourish** /ˈflʌrɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：繁荣；茁壮成长；兴旺
- **英文释义**：to grow or develop well; to be very successful
- **例句**：
  - The plants flourished in the warm, sunny weather.
  - 植物在温暖阳光的天气中茁壮成长。
  - Small businesses can flourish with the right support.
  - 有了合适的支持，小企业可以蓬勃发展。
- **助记**：flour（花）+ ish → 像花一样绽放 → 繁荣/茁壮成长

### 3. **instruct** /ɪnˈstrʌkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：指导；教授；指示
- **英文释义**：to teach someone a subject or skill; to tell someone officially to do something
- **例句**：
  - The coach instructed the players on how to improve their technique.
  - 教练指导球员如何提高技术。
  - Passengers were instructed to fasten their seatbelts.
  - 乘客被告知系好安全带。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ struct（建造）→ 在内心建造知识 → 指导；名词：instruction（指导；说明书）

### 4. **resemble** /rɪˈzembl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：像；类似于
- **英文释义**：to look like or be similar to someone or something
- **例句**：
  - The daughter closely resembles her mother in appearance.
  - 女儿在外表上很像她的妈妈。
  - This building resembles an ancient Greek temple.
  - 这座建筑看起来像一座古希腊神庙。
- **助记**：re（再）+ semble（相似）→ 再次相似 → 像；名词：resemblance（相似之处）

### 5. **frontier** /frʌnˈtɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：边界；前沿；新领域
- **英文释义**：a border between two countries; the limit of what is known or achieved
- **例句**：
  - Artificial intelligence is the new frontier of technology.
  - 人工智能是技术的新前沿。
  - The explorers traveled across the frontier into unknown territory.
  - 探险者穿过边界进入了未知领域。
- **助记**：front（前面）+ ier → 最前面的地方 → 边界/前沿

### 6. **appliance** /əˈplaɪəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：电器；器具；设备
- **英文释义**：a machine or device used in the home for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - Modern kitchen appliances make cooking much easier.
  - 现代厨房电器使烹饪变得容易得多。
  - The store sells a wide range of household appliances.
  - 这家商店出售各种家用电器。
- **助记**：appli（应用）+ ance → 应用的东西 → 器具/电器；动词：apply（应用）

### 7. **primitive** /ˈprɪmətɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：原始的；简陋的；早期的
- **英文释义**：belonging to a very early stage of development; very basic or old-fashioned
- **例句**：
  - Primitive humans used stone tools for hunting and gathering.
  - 原始人使用石器进行狩猎和采集。
  - The living conditions in the remote village were quite primitive.
  - 偏远村庄的生活条件相当简陋。
- **助记**：prim（第一/最初）+ itive → 最初的 → 原始的

### 8. **identical** /aɪˈdentɪkl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：完全相同的；一模一样的
- **英文释义**：exactly the same in every detail
- **例句**：
  - The twins wore identical dresses to the party.
  - 双胞胎穿着一模一样的裙子去参加聚会。
  - The two reports are almost identical in content.
  - 这两份报告在内容上几乎完全相同。
- **助记**：ident（相同）+ ical → 完全相同的；名词：identity（身份；一致）

### 9. **inevitably** /ɪnˈevɪtəbli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：不可避免地；必然地
- **英文释义**：in a way that cannot be prevented or avoided
- **例句**：
  - If you don't study, you will inevitably fall behind.
  - 如果你不学习，你将不可避免地落后。
  - Change inevitably comes with both challenges and opportunities.
  - 变化不可避免地带来挑战和机遇。
- **助记**：able 去掉后加 bly → in + evit（避免）+ ably → 无法避免地；形容词：inevitable（不可避免的）

### 10. **stumble** /ˈstʌmbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：绊倒；跌跌撞撞；偶然发现
- **英文释义**：to trip or nearly fall while walking; to discover something by chance
- **例句**：
  - She stumbled on a stone and almost fell down.
  - 她被一块石头绊了一下，差点摔倒。
  - I stumbled upon an interesting book in the library.
  - 我在图书馆偶然发现了一本有趣的书。
- **助记**：st + umble → 发出"嗵"的声音跌了一下 → 绊倒

### 高一词汇

### 11. **alleviate** /əˈliːvieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：减轻；缓解
- **英文释义**：to make suffering, pain, or a problem less severe
- **例句**：
  - The medicine helped to alleviate the patient's pain.
  - 药物帮助减轻了病人的疼痛。
  - The government took measures to alleviate poverty in rural areas.
  - 政府采取措施缓解农村地区的贫困。
- **助记**：al（朝向）+ levi（轻）+ ate → 使变轻 → 减轻；名词：alleviation（缓解）

### 12. **peripheral** /pəˈrɪfərəl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：外围的；次要的；边缘的
- **英文释义**：relating to the edge of an area; not of central importance
- **例句**：
  - The issue is peripheral to the main discussion.
  - 这个问题对主要讨论来说是次要的。
  - Peripheral vision helps us notice movement to the side.
  - 周边视觉帮助我们注意到侧面的运动。
- **助记**：peri（周围）+ pheral → 周围的 → 外围的/边缘的；名词：periphery（边缘）

### 13. **exemplify** /ɪɡˈzemplɪfaɪ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：举例说明；是…的典范
- **英文释义**：to serve as a typical example of; to illustrate by example
- **例句**：
  - Her career exemplifies the importance of hard work and determination.
  - 她的事业体现了努力工作和决心的重要性。
  - The teacher used real-life stories to exemplify the lesson.
  - 老师用现实生活中的故事来举例说明课程内容。
- **助记**：example（例子）→ exemplify → 用例子说明；名词：example（例子）

### 14. **lucrative** /ˈluːkrətɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有利可图的；赚钱的
- **英文释义**：producing a lot of money; profitable
- **例句**：
  - The technology industry offers many lucrative career opportunities.
  - 科技行业提供许多有利可图的职业机会。
  - Online shopping has become a lucrative business.
  - 网上购物已成为一项有利可图的生意。
- **助记**：lucr（利润）+ ative → 有利润的 → 有利可图的；近义词：profitable（盈利的）

### 15. **precarious** /prɪˈkeəriəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不稳定的；危险的；不确定的
- **英文释义**：not safe or certain; likely to fall or change
- **例句**：
  - The company is in a precarious financial situation.
  - 公司处于不稳定的财务状况。
  - He made a precarious living by doing odd jobs.
  - 他靠打零工维持不稳定的生活。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ car（小心）+ ious → 需要预先小心的 → 不稳定的；副词：precariously
