## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **formula** /ˈfɔːmjələ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：公式；配方
- **英文释义**：a mathematical rule expressed in symbols; a set of instructions for making something
- **例句**：
  - Can you remember the formula for calculating the area of a circle?
  - 你记得计算圆面积的公式吗？
  - The chemist developed a new formula for the cleaning product.
  - 化学家研发了这款清洁产品的新配方。
- **助记**：form（形式、形状）+ -ula（小）→ 用符号表示的形式化规则 → 公式；配方

### 2. **foster** /ˈfɒstə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：培养；促进
- **英文释义**：to help something develop or grow; to encourage or promote
- **例句**：
  - The school aims to foster students' creativity and critical thinking.
  - 学校致力于培养学生的创造力和批判性思维。
  - A good environment fosters learning and personal growth.
  - 良好的环境有助于学习和个人成长。
- **助记**：foster 与 food（食物）同源 → 提供养分、养育 → 培养；促进

### 3. **fraction** /ˈfrækʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：分数；小部分
- **英文释义**：a numerical quantity that is not a whole number; a small part of something
- **例句**：
  - Only a fraction of the students passed the difficult exam.
  - 只有一小部分学生通过了这场难考。
  - She spent a fraction of her salary on books last month.
  - 她上个月只花了一小部分工资买书。
- **助记**：fract（打破）+ -ion（名词）→ 打破整体后得到的一部分 → 分数；小部分

### 4. **fragile** /ˈfrædʒaɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：脆弱的；易碎的
- **英文释义**：easily broken or damaged; delicate
- **例句**：
  - Please handle the glassware with care — it's very fragile.
  - 请小心搬运这些玻璃器皿——它们非常易碎。
  - The ecosystem in this area is fragile and needs protection.
  - 这个地区的生态系统很脆弱，需要保护。
- **助记**：frag（打破）+ -ile（易…的）→ 容易被打破的 → 脆弱的；易碎的

### 5. **fragment** /ˈfræɡmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：碎片；片段
- **英文释义**：a small piece broken off from something larger; an incomplete part
- **例句**：
  - Archaeologists found fragments of ancient pottery at the site.
  - 考古学家在遗址发现了古代陶器碎片。
  - I only caught a fragment of their conversation.
  - 我只听到了他们对话的片段。
- **助记**：frag（打破）+ -ment（名词）→ 被打破后的小块 → 碎片；片段

### 6. **fraud** /frɔːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：欺诈；骗子
- **英文释义**：the crime of deceiving people to gain money or benefits; a person who does this
- **例句**：
  - He was charged with fraud for stealing millions from investors.
  - 他因从投资者处骗取数百万而被控欺诈。
  - Don't trust that website — it might be a fraud.
  - 别相信那个网站——可能是骗局。
- **助记**：fraud 与 deceive（欺骗）相关 → 欺骗行为或欺骗者 → 欺诈；骗子

### 7. **freight** /freɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：货物；货运
- **英文释义**：goods carried by ship, plane, train, or truck; the transport of goods
- **例句**：
  - The freight will arrive at the port next Monday.
  - 货物将于下周一抵达港口。
  - Shipping freight by train is often cheaper than by air.
  - 铁路货运通常比空运便宜。
- **助记**：freight 与 fraught（充满）相关 → 装满的、被运送的货物 → 货物；货运

### 8. **friction** /ˈfrɪkʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：摩擦；摩擦力
- **英文释义**：the resistance when one surface moves over another; disagreement or conflict
- **例句**：
  - Friction between the tires and the road helps the car brake.
  - 轮胎与路面之间的摩擦力有助于汽车刹车。
  - There has been friction between the two departments for months.
  - 这两个部门之间的摩擦已经持续了几个月。
- **助记**：frict（摩擦）+ -ion（名词）→ 相互摩擦产生的力 → 摩擦；摩擦力

### 9. **frontier** /ˈfrʌntɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：边界；前沿
- **英文释义**：the border between two countries; the limit of knowledge or achievement
- **例句**：
  - Scientists are pushing the frontiers of medical research.
  - 科学家正在拓展医学研究的前沿。
  - The town lies on the frontier between the two nations.
  - 这座小镇位于两国边境上。
- **助记**：front（前方）+ -ier → 最前方的边界或领域 → 边界；前沿

### 10. **frustrate** /frʌˈstreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使沮丧；挫败
- **英文释义**：to make someone feel disappointed or discouraged; to prevent from succeeding
- **例句**：
  - Constant failures frustrated his attempts to pass the exam.
  - 屡次失败让他通过考试的努力受挫。
  - It frustrates me when the computer freezes in the middle of work.
  - 电脑在工作时卡住让我很沮丧。
- **助记**：frustr（落空）+ -ate（动词）→ 使希望落空 → 使沮丧；挫败

### 高一词汇

### 11. **hamper** /ˈhæmpə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：妨碍；阻碍
- **英文释义**：to prevent someone from doing something easily; to hinder or impede
- **例句**：
  - Heavy traffic hampered our progress to the airport.
  - 交通拥堵妨碍了我们前往机场。
  - The lack of funding is hampering the development of the project.
  - 资金不足阻碍了项目的发展。
- **助记**：hamper 与 hamper（有盖篮子）同形 → 像被关在篮子里一样受限制 → 妨碍；阻碍

### 12. **haphazard** /hæpˈhæzəd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：随意的；无计划的
- **英文释义**：lacking any obvious principle of organization; random
- **例句**：
  - Books were stacked in a haphazard manner on the floor.
  - 书本随意地堆放在地板上。
  - His haphazard approach to studying led to poor exam results.
  - 他毫无计划的学习方式导致考试成绩不佳。
- **助记**：hap（运气）+ hazard（危险、偶然）→ 凭运气、碰巧的 → 随意的；无计划的

### 13. **harbinger** /ˈhɑːbɪndʒə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：先兆；预兆
- **英文释义**：something that foreshadows a future event; a sign that something is coming
- **例句**：
  - The first snow is often seen as a harbinger of winter.
  - 初雪常被视为冬季来临的先兆。
  - Rising prices may be a harbinger of inflation.
  - 物价上涨可能是通胀的预兆。
- **助记**：harbinger 源自"先行者、报信人"→ 提前到来预示某事的东西 → 先兆；预兆

### 14. **haughty** /ˈhɔːti/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：傲慢的；自大的
- **英文释义**：having or showing excessive pride in oneself; arrogantly superior
- **例句**：
  - She gave a haughty look and walked away without replying.
  - 她傲慢地看了一眼，没有回答就走了。
  - His haughty attitude made him unpopular among his classmates.
  - 他傲慢的态度让他在同学中不受欢迎。
- **助记**：haught 与 high（高）相关 → 把自己看得比别人高 → 傲慢的；自大的

### 15. **hedonism** /ˈhiːdənɪzəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：享乐主义
- **英文释义**：the belief that pleasure is the most important thing in life
- **例句**：
  - He lives a life of hedonism, pursuing only pleasure and comfort.
  - 他过着享乐主义的生活，只追求快乐和舒适。
  - Hedonism emphasizes the pursuit of happiness above all else.
  - 享乐主义强调把追求幸福置于一切之上。
- **助记**：hedon（快乐）+ -ism（主义）→ 以快乐为核心的主义 → 享乐主义
