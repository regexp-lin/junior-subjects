## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **accomplish** /əˈkʌmplɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：完成；实现；达到
- **英文释义**：to succeed in doing or completing something
- **例句**：
  - She accomplished her goal of running a marathon before turning 18.
  - 她在18岁之前完成了跑马拉松的目标。
  - We can accomplish more by working together as a team.
  - 团队合作我们可以完成更多的事情。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ complish（完成）→ 朝着完成的方向 → 完成；名词：accomplishment

### 2. **border** /ˈbɔːdə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：边界；国界；边缘
- **英文释义**：the line that separates two countries or areas
- **例句**：
  - The river forms the border between the two countries.
  - 这条河构成了两国之间的边界。
  - She planted flowers along the border of the garden.
  - 她沿着花园的边缘种了花。
- **助记**：board（板子/边）→ border → 边界

### 3. **caution** /ˈkɔːʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：小心；谨慎；警告
- **英文释义**：great care to avoid danger; a warning
- **例句**：
  - You should drive with caution on rainy days.
  - 雨天开车应该小心谨慎。
  - The sign says "Caution: Wet Floor."
  - 标牌上写着"小心：地面湿滑。"
- **助记**：caut（小心）+ ion → 小心的状态 → 谨慎；形容词：cautious（谨慎的）

### 4. **destroy** /dɪˈstrɔɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：破坏；毁灭；消灭
- **英文释义**：to damage something so badly that it cannot be used or no longer exists
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake destroyed many buildings in the city.
  - 地震摧毁了城市里的许多建筑物。
  - Pollution is destroying the natural habitats of wild animals.
  - 污染正在破坏野生动物的自然栖息地。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ stroy（建造/结构）→ 把建造的东西推倒 → 破坏；名词：destruction

### 5. **enthusiasm** /ɪnˈθjuːziæzəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：热情；热忱；热心
- **英文释义**：a strong feeling of excitement and interest
- **例句**：
  - She showed great enthusiasm for learning English.
  - 她对学习英语表现出极大的热情。
  - His enthusiasm for sports is contagious — it inspires everyone around him.
  - 他对运动的热情很有感染力——激励了周围的每个人。
- **助记**：en（在...里）+ thus（神）+ iasm → 心中有神灵的力量 → 热情；形容词：enthusiastic

### 6. **faith** /feɪθ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：信任；信心；信仰
- **英文释义**：complete trust or confidence in someone or something; a strong religious belief
- **例句**：
  - I have faith in you — I believe you can do it.
  - 我信任你——我相信你能做到。
  - We should never lose faith in ourselves even during hard times.
  - 即使在困难时期，我们也不应该对自己失去信心。
- **助记**：faith 与 faithful（忠实的）同源；固定搭配：have faith in（对...有信心）/ lose faith in（对...失去信心）

### 7. **negative** /ˈneɡətɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：消极的；否定的；负面的
- **英文释义**：not good; expressing disagreement or refusal
- **例句**：
  - Try not to have a negative attitude towards difficulties.
  - 不要对困难持消极态度。
  - Negative comments can hurt people's feelings.
  - 负面评论可能伤害人的感情。
- **助记**：neg（否定）+ ative → 否定的 → 消极的；反义词：positive（积极的）

### 8. **permit** /pəˈmɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：v. 允许；许可；n. 许可证
- **英文释义**：v. to allow something to happen; n. an official document giving permission
- **例句**：
  - Smoking is not permitted in the school building.
  - 学校建筑内不允许吸烟。
  - You need a permit to park in this area.
  - 在这个区域停车需要许可证。
- **助记**：per（通过）+ mit（送）→ 让它通过 → 允许；名词：permission（许可）；注意：permit sb. to do sth.

### 9. **resource** /rɪˈsɔːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：资源；物资
- **英文释义**：a supply of something that a country or person has and can use
- **例句**：
  - We should protect natural resources such as water and forests.
  - 我们应该保护水和森林等自然资源。
  - The Internet is a valuable resource for learning.
  - 互联网是学习的宝贵资源。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ source（源泉）→ 可以反复使用的源泉 → 资源；形容词：resourceful（足智多谋的）

### 10. **signal** /ˈsɪɡnəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 信号；标志；v. 发信号
- **英文释义**：n. a sound, action, or light that gives information; v. to give a signal
- **例句**：
  - The traffic signal turned red, so we stopped the car.
  - 交通信号灯变红了，所以我们停了车。
  - She signaled for the waiter to bring the menu.
  - 她向服务员示意拿菜单来。
- **助记**：sign（标记）+ al → 用来做标记的 → 信号

### 高一词汇

### 11. **illustrate** /ˈɪləstreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：说明；阐明；给...配插图
- **英文释义**：to explain something by giving examples; to add pictures to a book
- **例句**：
  - The teacher used a simple experiment to illustrate the concept.
  - 老师用一个简单的实验来说明这个概念。
  - The book is beautifully illustrated with colorful drawings.
  - 这本书配有精美的彩色插图。
- **助记**：il（进入）+ lustr（光/照亮）+ ate → 照亮使清楚 → 说明；名词：illustration

### 12. **portable** /ˈpɔːtəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：便携的；手提的
- **英文释义**：small and light enough to be carried or moved easily
- **例句**：
  - A portable speaker is very useful for outdoor activities.
  - 便携式音箱在户外活动中非常有用。
  - She brought a portable charger in case her phone ran out of battery.
  - 她带了一个移动电源以防手机没电。
- **助记**：port（搬运）+ able（能...的）→ 能搬运的 → 便携的；联想 transport（运输）

### 13. **symptom** /ˈsɪmptəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：症状；征兆
- **英文释义**：a change in the body that shows you are ill; a sign of a problem
- **例句**：
  - A high fever is one of the common symptoms of the flu.
  - 高烧是流感的常见症状之一。
  - Rising unemployment is a symptom of deeper economic problems.
  - 失业率上升是更深层经济问题的征兆。
- **助记**：sym（共同）+ ptom（落下）→ 同时出现的现象 → 症状

### 14. **vulnerable** /ˈvʌlnərəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：脆弱的；易受伤害的
- **英文释义**：weak and easily hurt physically or emotionally
- **例句**：
  - Children and the elderly are more vulnerable to the cold weather.
  - 儿童和老年人更容易受到寒冷天气的伤害。
  - The company is vulnerable to changes in the global economy.
  - 这家公司容易受到全球经济变化的影响。
- **助记**：vulner（伤害）+ able → 容易被伤害的 → 脆弱的；名词：vulnerability

### 15. **welfare** /ˈwelfeə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：福利；幸福；福祉
- **英文释义**：the health, happiness, and general well-being of a person or group
- **例句**：
  - The government should pay more attention to the welfare of children.
  - 政府应该更关注儿童的福利。
  - Animal welfare is an important issue in modern society.
  - 动物福利是现代社会的一个重要议题。
- **助记**：wel（好的，well）+ fare（走/过日子）→ 日子过得好 → 福利
