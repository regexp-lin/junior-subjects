## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **represent** /ˌreprɪˈzent/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：代表；象征；描绘
- **英文释义**：to act or speak officially for a group; to be a symbol of something
- **例句**：
  - She was chosen to represent our class in the speech competition.
  - 她被选为代表我们班参加演讲比赛。
  - The dove represents peace in many cultures.
  - 鸽子在许多文化中象征和平。
- **助记**：re（再）+ present（呈现）→ 再次呈现 → 代表；名词：representative（代表）

### 2. **analyze** /ˈænəlaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：分析；分解
- **英文释义**：to examine something carefully in order to understand it
- **例句**：
  - The scientist analyzed the data from the experiment.
  - 科学家分析了实验数据。
  - We need to analyze the causes of the problem before finding a solution.
  - 在找到解决方案之前，我们需要分析问题的原因。
- **助记**：ana（分开）+ lyze（解开）→ 拆解开来看 → 分析；名词：analysis（分析）

### 3. **reaction** /riˈækʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：反应；回应
- **英文释义**：something done or felt in response to something else
- **例句**：
  - What was your first reaction when you heard the news?
  - 你听到这个消息时的第一反应是什么？
  - The audience's reaction to the performance was very positive.
  - 观众对表演的反应非常积极。
- **助记**：re（回）+ act（行动）+ ion → 对行动的回应 → 反应；动词：react（反应）

### 4. **technique** /tekˈniːk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：技术；技巧；方法
- **英文释义**：a particular way of doing something, especially one that requires skill
- **例句**：
  - He has developed a new technique for learning vocabulary.
  - 他开发了一种学习词汇的新技巧。
  - Good painting technique takes years of practice to master.
  - 好的绘画技巧需要多年练习才能掌握。
- **助记**：techn（技艺）+ ique → 技艺的方法 → 技巧；形容词：technical（技术的）

### 5. **strict** /strɪkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：严格的；严厉的
- **英文释义**：demanding that rules are obeyed; very careful and exact
- **例句**：
  - Our math teacher is very strict but fair.
  - 我们的数学老师非常严格但很公正。
  - The school has strict rules about using phones in class.
  - 学校对课堂上使用手机有严格的规定。
- **助记**：strict 发音像"死追课特"→ 老师死追着要求很特别 → 严格的；be strict with sb.（对某人严格）

### 6. **wealthy** /ˈwelθi/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：富有的；富裕的
- **英文释义**：having a lot of money or possessions; rich
- **例句**：
  - The wealthy businessman donated a lot of money to the hospital.
  - 这位富有的商人向医院捐了很多钱。
  - Not all wealthy people are happy.
  - 并非所有富人都快乐。
- **助记**：wealth（财富）+ y → 有财富的 → 富有的；名词：wealth（财富）

### 7. **awkward** /ˈɔːkwəd/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：尴尬的；笨拙的
- **英文释义**：making you feel embarrassed; difficult to deal with
- **例句**：
  - There was an awkward silence after he told the joke.
  - 他讲完笑话后出现了一阵尴尬的沉默。
  - It was awkward meeting my old friend after so many years.
  - 这么多年后见到老朋友感觉有些尴尬。
- **助记**：awk（歪）+ ward（方向）→ 方向歪了 → 笨拙的/尴尬的

### 8. **eventually** /ɪˈventʃuəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：最终；终于
- **英文释义**：in the end; after a period of time
- **例句**：
  - After many attempts, she eventually passed the driving test.
  - 经过多次尝试，她终于通过了驾照考试。
  - The rain stopped eventually and the sun came out.
  - 雨终于停了，太阳出来了。
- **助记**：event（事件）+ ually → 所有事件之后 → 最终；形容词：eventual（最终的）

### 9. **display** /dɪˈspleɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：展示；陈列；展览
- **英文释义**：to show something to people; a collection of things for people to look at
- **例句**：
  - The students' artwork was displayed in the school hall.
  - 学生们的艺术作品在学校大厅展示。
  - There is a beautiful display of flowers in the park.
  - 公园里有一场美丽的花卉展览。
- **助记**：dis（展开）+ play（摆放）→ 展开摆放 → 展示

### 10. **wander** /ˈwɒndə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：漫步；闲逛；走神
- **英文释义**：to walk slowly without a clear direction or purpose
- **例句**：
  - We wandered around the old town and enjoyed the beautiful scenery.
  - 我们在老城区闲逛，欣赏美丽的风景。
  - My mind started to wander during the boring lecture.
  - 无聊的讲座期间我开始走神了。
- **助记**：wand（棍子/魔杖）+ er → 像拿着魔杖到处走 → 漫步；注意区分：wonder（想知道；惊奇）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **implement** /ˈɪmplɪment/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：实施；执行；贯彻
- **英文释义**：to put a plan or system into action
- **例句**：
  - The government plans to implement new environmental policies next year.
  - 政府计划明年实施新的环保政策。
  - It is important to implement the rules fairly for all students.
  - 对所有学生公平执行规则很重要。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ ple（满）+ ment → 使充满行动 → 实施；名词：implementation（实施）

### 12. **profound** /prəˈfaʊnd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：深刻的；深远的；意义重大的
- **英文释义**：very great; having a strong influence; showing deep thought
- **例句**：
  - The book had a profound effect on my way of thinking.
  - 这本书对我的思维方式产生了深刻的影响。
  - She made a profound observation about human nature.
  - 她对人性做出了深刻的观察。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ found（底部）→ 深入到底部 → 深刻的

### 13. **regulate** /ˈreɡjuleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：管理；调节；控制
- **英文释义**：to control something by means of rules; to adjust
- **例句**：
  - The government regulates the quality of food sold in supermarkets.
  - 政府管控超市出售食品的质量。
  - Our body temperature is regulated by the brain.
  - 我们的体温由大脑调节。
- **助记**：reg（统治/规则）+ ulate → 用规则来管理 → 调节；名词：regulation（规章；调节）

### 14. **fragile** /ˈfrædʒaɪl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：脆弱的；易碎的
- **英文释义**：easily broken or damaged; delicate
- **例句**：
  - Be careful with the glass — it's very fragile.
  - 小心这个玻璃杯——它很易碎。
  - The ecosystem in the Arctic region is extremely fragile.
  - 北极地区的生态系统极其脆弱。
- **助记**：frag（打碎）+ ile → 容易打碎的 → 脆弱的；近义词：vulnerable（易受伤的）; 反义词：robust（坚固的）

### 15. **innovation** /ˌɪnəˈveɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：创新；革新
- **英文释义**：a new idea, method, or invention
- **例句**：
  - Technological innovation has changed the way we communicate.
  - 技术创新改变了我们的沟通方式。
  - The company encourages innovation among its employees.
  - 公司鼓励员工创新。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ nov（新）+ ation → 引入新事物 → 创新；动词：innovate（创新）；形容词：innovative（创新的）
