## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **emerge** /ɪˈmɜːdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：出现；浮现
- **英文释义**：to come out of or appear from something; to become known
- **例句**：
  - The sun emerged from behind the clouds.
  - 太阳从云层后面出现了。
  - New evidence has emerged in the investigation.
  - 调查中浮现了新证据。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ merge（沉没）→ 从沉没中出来 → 出现

### 2. **emission** /ɪˈmɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：排放；排放物
- **英文释义**：the production or release of something, especially gas or radiation
- **例句**：
  - Carbon emissions from cars contribute to global warming.
  - 汽车的碳排放加剧了全球变暖。
  - The factory must reduce its emissions to meet new standards.
  - 工厂必须减少排放以满足新标准。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ miss（发送）+ -ion → 发送出去的东西 → 排放

### 3. **emphasis** /ˈemfəsɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：强调；重点
- **英文释义**：special importance or attention given to something
- **例句**：
  - The school places great emphasis on reading skills.
  - 学校非常重视阅读能力。
  - He spoke with emphasis on the need for teamwork.
  - 他着重强调了团队合作的必要性。
- **助记**：em-（在…中）+ phas（显示）+ -is → 在其中显示出来 → 强调

### 4. **empire** /ˈempaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：帝国；大企业集团
- **英文释义**：a group of countries ruled by one leader; a large business organisation
- **例句**：
  - The Roman Empire lasted for centuries.
  - 罗马帝国延续了数百年。
  - He built a business empire from nothing.
  - 他白手起家建立了一个商业帝国。
- **助记**：empire → emperor（皇帝）→ 皇帝统治的 → 帝国

### 5. **enable** /ɪˈneɪbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使能够；使成为可能
- **英文释义**：to give someone the ability or means to do something
- **例句**：
  - Technology enables us to communicate with people around the world.
  - 技术使我们能够与世界各地的人交流。
  - The scholarship enabled her to attend university.
  - 奖学金使她能够上大学。
- **助记**：en-（使）+ able（能够）→ 使能够 → 使成为可能

### 6. **encounter** /ɪnˈkaʊntə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：遇到；遭遇
- **英文释义**：to unexpectedly meet or experience something
- **例句**：
  - We encountered heavy traffic on the way to the airport.
  - 我们在去机场的路上遇到了堵车。
  - She encountered many difficulties during her first year abroad.
  - 她出国第一年遭遇了许多困难。
- **助记**：en-（进入）+ counter（对面）→ 面对面碰上 → 遇到

### 7. **endanger** /ɪnˈdeɪndʒə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：危及；使处于危险
- **英文释义**：to put someone or something at risk of harm
- **例句**：
  - Pollution endangers the lives of marine animals.
  - 污染危及海洋动物的生命。
  - Reckless driving endangers everyone on the road.
  - 鲁莽驾驶危及道路上每个人的安全。
- **助记**：en-（使）+ danger（危险）→ 使陷入危险 → 危及

### 8. **endorse** /ɪnˈdɔːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：赞同；代言
- **英文释义**：to declare public approval or support of; to promote a product
- **例句**：
  - Many celebrities endorse sports brands.
  - 许多名人为体育品牌代言。
  - The committee endorsed the new plan unanimously.
  - 委员会一致赞同了新计划。
- **助记**：en-（在…上）+ dorse（背）→ 在背后支持 → 赞同

### 9. **enforce** /ɪnˈfɔːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：执行；强制实施
- **英文释义**：to compel observance of a law or rule
- **例句**：
  - The police are there to enforce the law.
  - 警察的职责是执法。
  - Schools enforce strict rules about mobile phone use.
  - 学校严格执行关于手机使用的规定。
- **助记**：en-（使）+ force（力量）→ 用力量使执行 → 强制实施

### 10. **enhance** /ɪnˈhɑːns/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：提高；增强
- **英文释义**：to intensify, increase, or improve the quality or value of something
- **例句**：
  - Regular exercise can enhance your health.
  - 经常锻炼能增强你的健康。
  - The new software enhances the quality of photos.
  - 新软件提高了照片质量。
- **助记**：en-（使）+ hance（高）→ 使更高 → 提高

### 高一词汇

### 11. **eloquent** /ˈeləkwənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：雄辩的；有说服力的
- **英文释义**：fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing
- **例句**：
  - Martin Luther King Jr. was an eloquent speaker.
  - 马丁·路德·金是一位雄辩的演说家。
  - Her eloquent essay won the writing competition.
  - 她有说服力的文章赢得了写作比赛。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ loqu（说）+ -ent → 说出来很有力 → 雄辩的

### 12. **elusive** /ɪˈluːsɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：难以捉摸的；难以达到的
- **英文释义**：difficult to find, catch, or achieve
- **例句**：
  - Happiness can be elusive if you chase it too hard.
  - 如果你追得太急，幸福反而难以捉摸。
  - The elusive fox escaped the hunters once again.
  - 狡猾的狐狸又一次逃脱了猎人。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ lus（玩耍）+ -ive → 玩着就溜走了 → 难以捉摸的

### 13. **emanate** /ˈeməneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：散发；发出
- **英文释义**：to come out from a source; to originate from
- **例句**：
  - A delicious smell emanated from the kitchen.
  - 厨房里散发出美味的香气。
  - Confidence emanated from her as she walked on stage.
  - 她走上台时，自信从她身上散发出来。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ man（流）+ -ate → 流出来 → 散发

### 14. **embezzle** /ɪmˈbezl/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：挪用；侵吞（公款）
- **英文释义**：to steal money that has been entrusted to your care
- **例句**：
  - The accountant was caught embezzling company funds.
  - 会计师被发现挪用公司资金。
  - He embezzled millions of dollars over several years.
  - 他在几年间侵吞了数百万美元。
- **助记**：em-（进入）+ bezzle（偷拿）→ 偷偷拿进自己口袋 → 挪用

### 15. **eminent** /ˈemɪnənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：杰出的；著名的
- **英文释义**：famous and respected within a particular field
- **例句**：
  - She is an eminent scientist who has won the Nobel Prize.
  - 她是一位获得诺贝尔奖的杰出科学家。
  - The university invited an eminent professor to give a lecture.
  - 大学邀请了一位著名教授来做讲座。
- **助记**：emin（突出）+ -ent → 突出的 → 杰出的
