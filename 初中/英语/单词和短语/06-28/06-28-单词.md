## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **brandish** /ˈbrændɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：挥舞；炫耀
- **英文释义**：to hold or wave something, especially a weapon, in a threatening way
- **例句**：
  - The robber brandished a knife at the cashier.
  - 劫匪向收银员挥舞着刀。
  - He brandished his trophy proudly in front of the crowd.
  - 他在人群前骄傲地炫耀着奖杯。
- **助记**：brand（剑）+ -ish → 像挥剑一样 → 挥舞（brand 古义为 sword）

### 2. **hinder** /ˈhɪndə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍
- **英文释义**：to make it difficult for someone to do something or for something to happen
- **例句**：
  - The heavy traffic hindered our progress.
  - 拥堵的交通阻碍了我们的行程。
  - Poor health hindered him from completing the project.
  - 健康状况不佳妨碍了他完成项目。
- **助记**：hinder → "恨的"（谐音）→ 恨得你动弹不得 → 阻碍（hind 后 + -er）

### 3. **pester** /ˈpestə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：纠缠；烦扰
- **英文释义**：to annoy someone, especially by asking them something many times
- **例句**：
  - The kids kept pestering their mother for ice cream.
  - 孩子们一直缠着妈妈要冰淇淋。
  - Please stop pestering me with questions.
  - 请别再拿问题来烦我了。
- **助记**：pester → pest（害虫）+ -er → 像害虫一样烦人 → 纠缠

### 4. **trample** /ˈtræmpl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：践踏；蹂躏
- **英文释义**：to step heavily on something so that you crush it
- **例句**：
  - The crowd trampled the flowers as they rushed forward.
  - 人群向前冲时践踏了花朵。
  - His rights were trampled by the unfair ruling.
  - 他的权利被不公正的裁决践踏了。
- **助记**：trample → "踹破"（谐音）→ 踹破踩碎 → 践踏

### 5. **lagoon** /ləˈɡuːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：泻湖；环礁湖
- **英文释义**：an area of salt water separated from the sea by a reef or sandbank
- **例句**：
  - We swam in the crystal-clear lagoon of the tropical island.
  - 我们在热带岛屿清澈见底的泻湖里游泳。
  - The resort is built beside a tranquil lagoon.
  - 度假村建在一片宁静的环礁湖畔。
- **助记**：lagoon → "拉古恩"（谐音）→ 拉来古旧的湖水形成 → 泻湖（源自拉丁语 lacuna"湖"）

### 6. **thatch** /θætʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：茅草屋顶
- **英文释义**：dry vegetation such as straw or reeds used for roofing
- **例句**：
  - The cottage had a traditional thatch roof.
  - 那座小屋有传统的茅草屋顶。
  - They live in a house with a thatch made of palm leaves.
  - 他们住在一栋用棕榈叶铺顶的房子里。
- **助记**：thatch → "撒砌"（谐音）→ 撒草砌成屋顶 → 茅草屋顶

### 7. **stance** /stæns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：立场；姿态
- **英文释义**：an opinion or view about something; the way someone stands
- **例句**：
  - The government has taken a firm stance on environmental protection.
  - 政府在环境保护上采取了坚定立场。
  - The boxer's defensive stance helped him avoid the punch.
  - 拳击手的防守姿态帮助他躲过了那一拳。
- **助记**：stance → stand（站立）→ 站立的方式 → 姿态；立场

### 8. **nimble** /ˈnɪmbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：敏捷的；灵活的
- **英文释义**：able to move quickly and easily; able to think or understand quickly
- **例句**：
  - The nimble gymnast performed amazing acrobatics.
  - 敏捷的体操运动员表演了惊人的特技。
  - She has a nimble mind and learns new things quickly.
  - 她思维敏捷，学新东西很快。
- **助记**：nimble → "能博"（谐音）→ 能博得灵活之名 → 敏捷的

### 9. **frantic** /ˈfræntɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：疯狂的；慌乱的
- **英文释义**：done or arranged in a hurried and chaotic way because of urgency
- **例句**：
  - She made frantic phone calls trying to find her lost child.
  - 她疯狂地打电话试图找到走丢的孩子。
  - There was frantic activity in the office before the deadline.
  - 截止日期前办公室里一片慌乱忙碌。
- **助记**：frantic → "疯癫啼哭"（谐音）→ 疯癫到啼哭 → 疯狂的（源自希腊语 phrenitis"脑炎"）

### 10. **therein** /ˌðeərˈɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：在其中；在那方面
- **英文释义**：in that place, document, or thing
- **例句**：
  - The contract stated the terms, and therein lay the problem.
  - 合同写明了条款，问题就出在这里。
  - He found the key therein and opened the box.
  - 他在其中找到了钥匙并打开了盒子。
- **助记**：there + in → 在那里面的 → 在其中

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ruminate** /ˈruːmɪneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：沉思；反刍
- **英文释义**：to think carefully and for a long time about something; (of animals) to bring up food from the stomach and chew it again
- **例句**：
  - He sat by the window, ruminating on the day's events.
  - 他坐在窗边，沉思着当天发生的事情。
  - Cows ruminate their food before digesting it.
  - 牛在消化前会反刍食物。
- **助记**：rumin-（反刍）+ -ate → 像牛反刍一样反复咀嚼 → 沉思

### 12. **invigorate** /ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使充满活力；激励
- **英文释义**：to make someone feel more energetic and healthy
- **例句**：
  - The cold shower invigorated him in the morning.
  - 冷水澡让他在早晨精神焕发。
  - The new manager's ideas invigorated the whole team.
  - 新经理的想法让整个团队充满活力。
- **助记**：in-（使）+ vigor（活力）+ -ate → 使有活力 → 使充满活力

### 13. **tenuous** /ˈtenjuəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：脆弱的；微弱的
- **英文释义**：very weak and possibly not lasting; very thin
- **例句**：
  - The connection between the two events is tenuous.
  - 这两件事之间的联系很微弱。
  - He held onto a tenuous hope of survival.
  - 他怀着一丝脆弱的生存希望。
- **助记**：tenu-（细、薄）+ -ous → 细薄的 → 脆弱的（源自拉丁语 tenuis）

### 14. **capricious** /kəˈprɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：反复无常的；善变的
- **英文释义**：changing mood or behavior suddenly and unexpectedly
- **例句**：
  - The capricious weather ruined our picnic plans.
  - 反复无常的天气毁了我们的野餐计划。
  - She was known for her capricious nature.
  - 她以善变的性格著称。
- **助记**：capric-（山羊，跳跃）+ -ious → 像山羊一样跳跃不定 → 反复无常的

### 15. **hyperbole** /haɪˈpɜːbəli/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：夸张；夸张手法
- **英文释义**：a way of speaking or writing that makes something sound better, more important, etc. than it really is
- **例句**：
  - "I've told you a million times" is an example of hyperbole.
  - "我告诉过你一百万次了"是夸张手法的例子。
  - The advertisement used hyperbole to attract customers.
  - 广告使用夸张手法来吸引顾客。
- **助记**：hyper-（超过）+ bole（抛）→ 抛过头 → 夸张（源自希腊语）
