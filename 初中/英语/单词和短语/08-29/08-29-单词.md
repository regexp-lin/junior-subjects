## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **medal** /ˈmedl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：奖牌
- **英文释义**：a flat piece of metal given as a prize or reward for winning a competition or for bravery
- **例句**：
  - She won a gold medal at the Olympics.
  - 她在奥运会上赢得了金牌。
  - The soldier was awarded a medal for his courage.
  - 这名士兵因勇敢而被授予奖章。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 metallum（金属）→ 金属制成的奖励 → 奖牌

### 2. **memoir** /ˈmemwɑːr/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：回忆录
- **英文释义**：a written account of someone's life, especially one written by that person
- **例句**：
  - She published her memoirs last year.
  - 她去年出版了自己的回忆录。
  - The president's memoir became a bestseller.
  - 总统的回忆录成了畅销书。
- **助记**：memor（记忆）+ -oir → 记忆的记录 → 回忆录

### 3. **mental** /ˈmentl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：精神的；心理的
- **英文释义**：relating to the mind; involving the process of thinking
- **例句**：
  - He has been suffering from mental health issues.
  - 他一直饱受心理健康问题的困扰。
  - Doing puzzles is good for your mental development.
  - 做拼图对你的智力发展有好处。
- **助记**：ment（心智）+ -al → 与心智有关的 → 精神的；心理的

### 4. **mentor** /ˈmentɔːr/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：导师
- **英文释义**：an experienced person who advises and helps someone with less experience
- **例句**：
  - She found a mentor who guided her through her career.
  - 她找到了一位在职业道路上指导她的导师。
  - He works as a mentor for young entrepreneurs.
  - 他担任年轻企业家的导师。
- **助记**：源自希腊神话人物 Mentor（奥德修斯的良师益友）→ 指有经验的指导者 → 导师

### 5. **mercy** /ˈmɜːsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：怜悯；仁慈
- **英文释义**：kindness or forgiveness shown towards someone whom you have the power to punish
- **例句**：
  - They begged for mercy from the court.
  - 他们恳求法庭宽恕。
  - She showed no mercy to her enemies.
  - 她对敌人毫不留情。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 misericordia（同情心）→ 对他人苦难的同情与宽恕 → 怜悯；仁慈

### 6. **merit** /ˈmerɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：优点；值得
- **英文释义**：the quality of being good and deserving praise; to deserve something
- **例句**：
  - The plan has its merits and drawbacks.
  - 这个计划有优点也有缺点。
  - The case merits further investigation.
  - 这个案子值得进一步调查。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 meritum（应得的报酬）→ 值得赞扬的品质 → 优点；值得

### 7. **method** /ˈmeθəd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：方法
- **英文释义**：a particular way of doing something
- **例句**：
  - There are many methods of learning a language.
  - 学习语言有很多方法。
  - His teaching method is very effective.
  - 他的教学方法非常有效。
- **助记**：源自希腊语 methodos（跟随路径）→ meta（在…之后）+ hodos（路）→ 循路而行 → 方法

### 8. **migrate** /maɪˈɡreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：迁移
- **英文释义**：to move from one place to another, especially to live in a different country or area
- **例句**：
  - Many birds migrate south in winter.
  - 许多鸟类在冬天南迁。
  - People migrated to the cities in search of work.
  - 人们迁往城市寻找工作。
- **助记**：migr（移动）+ -ate → 从一个地方转移到另一个地方 → 迁移

### 9. **military** /ˈmɪlɪtəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：军事的
- **英文释义**：relating to soldiers or armed forces
- **例句**：
  - He comes from a military family.
  - 他出身军人家庭。
  - The country has strong military power.
  - 该国军事力量强大。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 militarius（与士兵有关）→ miles（士兵）→ 军事的

### 10. **mineral** /ˈmɪnərəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：矿物
- **英文释义**：a natural substance such as iron, coal, or gold that is found in the earth
- **例句**：
  - The region is rich in minerals.
  - 该地区矿物资源丰富。
  - Mineral water is good for your health.
  - 矿泉水对健康有益。
- **助记**：mine（矿）+ -ral → 从矿中开采出的物质 → 矿物

### 高一词汇

### 11. **nuance** /ˈnjuːɑːns/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：细微差别
- **英文释义**：a very slight difference in meaning, feeling, or appearance
- **例句**：
  - She is skilled at capturing the nuance of human emotions.
  - 她善于捕捉人类情感的细微差别。
  - The translation loses the nuance of the original text.
  - 翻译丢失了原文的细微差别。
- **助记**：源自法语 nuance（色调的渐变）→ 细微的色彩变化 → 细微差别

### 12. **oblivious** /əˈblɪviəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：毫不知情的
- **英文释义**：not aware of something; not noticing what is happening around you
- **例句**：
  - He seemed oblivious to the danger.
  - 他似乎对危险毫不知情。
  - She was completely oblivious to the stares from others.
  - 她完全没有注意到别人的目光。
- **助记**：ob-（除去）+ liv（遗忘）+ -ious → 被遗忘的、未察觉的 → 毫不知情的

### 13. **obscure** /əbˈskjʊər/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj./v.
- **中文释义**：模糊的；掩盖
- **英文释义**：not well known; difficult to understand; to make something difficult to see
- **例句**：
  - He cited an obscure 18th-century poet.
  - 他引用了一位鲜为人知的18世纪诗人。
  - Clouds obscured the sun.
  - 云层遮住了太阳。
- **助记**：ob-（在…上）+ scur（覆盖）→ 被覆盖住的 → 模糊的；掩盖

### 14. **obstinate** /ˈɒbstɪnət/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：固执的
- **英文释义**：refusing to change your mind or your actions despite persuasion
- **例句**：
  - He was too obstinate to admit he was wrong.
  - 他太固执，不肯承认自己错了。
  - She has an obstinate streak in her character.
  - 她的性格里有固执的一面。
- **助记**：ob-（ against）+ stin（站）+ -ate → 坚决站定不动 → 固执的

### 15. **ominous** /ˈɒmɪnəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不祥的
- **英文释义**：suggesting that something bad is going to happen
- **例句**：
  - There were ominous clouds on the horizon.
  - 天边有不祥的云层。
  - His silence was ominous.
  - 他的沉默令人不安。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 omen（预兆）+ -ous → 有预兆意味的 → 不祥的
