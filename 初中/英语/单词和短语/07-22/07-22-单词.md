## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **heave** /hiːv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：举起；喘息
- **英文释义**：to lift or pull something heavy with great effort; to breathe heavily
- **例句**：
  - The students heaved the heavy boxes onto the truck.
  - 学生们费力地把沉重的箱子搬上卡车。
  - After running the marathon, she sat down heaving.
  - 跑完马拉松后，她坐下来喘着粗气。
- **助记**：heave → "黑屋"（谐音）→ 在黑屋中费力举起东西 → 举起；喘息

### 2. **heed** /hiːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：注意；听从
- **英文释义**：to pay attention to something, especially advice or a warning
- **例句**：
  - You should heed your teacher's advice about studying.
  - 你应该听从老师关于学习的建议。
  - He failed to heed the warning and got into trouble.
  - 他没有注意到警告，结果惹上了麻烦。
- **助记**：heed → " heed "（与 need 押韵）→ 需要 heed（heed=注意）→ 注意；听从

### 3. **heritage** /ˈherɪtɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：遗产；传统
- **英文释义**：features belonging to the culture of a particular society, such as traditions, languages, or buildings
- **例句**：
  - We should protect our cultural heritage for future generations.
  - 我们应该为子孙后代保护我们的文化遗产。
  - The ancient temple is part of our country's heritage.
  - 这座古庙是我们国家遗产的一部分。
- **助记**：herit（继承）+ -age（名词后缀）→ 继承下来的东西 → 遗产

### 4. **hectic** /ˈhektɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：繁忙的；忙乱的
- **英文释义**：full of activity, or very busy and fast
- **例句**：
  - The week before the exam was hectic for everyone.
  - 考试前的一周对每个人来说都很忙乱。
  - She had a hectic schedule with classes and club activities.
  - 她的日程很忙，有课还有社团活动。
- **助记**：hectic → " hectic "（音近 hectic）→ 忙得团团转 → 繁忙的

### 5. **helmet** /ˈhelmɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：头盔；安全帽
- **英文释义**：a strong hard hat that covers and protects the head
- **例句**：
  - Always wear a helmet when riding a bicycle.
  - 骑自行车时一定要戴头盔。
  - The construction workers put on their helmets before starting work.
  - 建筑工人开工前戴上了安全帽。
- **助记**：helm（舵）+ et（小）→ 保护头部的小"舵" → 头盔

### 6. **herd** /hɜːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：兽群；牧群
- **英文释义**：a large group of animals of the same type that live and feed together
- **例句**：
  - A herd of elephants crossed the grassland.
  - 一群大象穿过草原。
  - The shepherd watched over his herd of sheep.
  - 牧羊人照看着他的羊群。
- **助记**：herd → "喝的"（谐音）→ 兽群一起找水喝 → 兽群

### 7. **heyday** /ˈheɪdeɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：全盛期；鼎盛时期
- **英文释义**：the period of greatest success, popularity, or power
- **例句**：
  - In the heyday of the band, they sold millions of albums.
  - 乐队在全盛时期卖出了数百万张专辑。
  - The 1990s were the heyday of this basketball star.
  - 1990年代是这位篮球明星的鼎盛时期。
- **助记**：hey（嗨）+ day（日子）→ 嗨，那些美好的日子 → 全盛期

### 8. **hint** /hɪnt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：暗示；线索
- **英文释义**：a slight or indirect indication or suggestion
- **例句**：
  - She gave me a hint about the surprise party.
  - 她给了我关于惊喜派对的暗示。
  - There was a hint of sadness in his voice.
  - 他的声音里有一丝悲伤。
- **助记**：hint → " hint "（细微的提示）→ 小小的提示 → 暗示

### 9. **horizon** /həˈraɪzn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：地平线；视野
- **英文释义**：the line where the sky seems to meet the earth; the limit of what you can imagine or achieve
- **例句**：
  - The sun slowly rose above the horizon.
  - 太阳徐徐升到地平线之上。
  - Traveling abroad broadened my horizons.
  - 出国旅行开阔了我的视野。
- **助记**：horizon → " horizontal "（水平的）→ 水平的那条线 → 地平线

### 10. **hustle** /ˈhʌsl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：匆忙；催促
- **英文释义**：to move or work quickly and energetically; to push someone roughly
- **例句**：
  - We had to hustle to catch the last bus.
  - 我们得赶紧才能赶上末班车。
  - The teacher hustled the students into the classroom.
  - 老师催促学生们进教室。
- **助记**：hustle → " hustle "（音近 " hustle and bustle " 熙熙攘攘）→ 匆忙忙碌 → 匆忙

### 高一词汇

### 11. **hiatus** /haɪˈeɪtəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：间断；暂停
- **英文释义**：a pause or break in continuity
- **例句**：
  - The band went on hiatus after their world tour.
  - 乐队在世界巡演后进入了休整期。
  - After a short hiatus, she returned to school.
  - 短暂休学后，她重返校园。
- **助记**：hiatus → " hi-ate-us "（我们吃掉了时间）→ 时间被"吃掉"形成间隙 → 间断

### 12. **homogeneous** /ˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：同质的；均匀的
- **英文释义**：consisting of parts or members that are all the same
- **例句**：
  - The class seemed homogeneous in terms of ability.
  - 这个班级在能力方面似乎比较均衡。
  - The mixture should be homogeneous before baking.
  - 烘烤前混合物应该是均匀的。
- **助记**：homo-（相同）+ geneous（种类）→ 相同种类的 → 同质的

### 13. **hospitality** /ˌhɒspɪˈtæləti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：好客；殷勤招待
- **英文释义**：the quality of being friendly and welcoming to guests
- **例句**：
  - The hospitality of the host family made us feel at home.
  - 寄宿家庭的好客让我们宾至如归。
  - Chinese people are known for their hospitality.
  - 中国人以好客闻名。
- **助记**：hospital（医院）+ -ity → 古时 hospital 指招待所 → 好客

### 14. **humiliate** /hjuːˈmɪlieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使蒙羞；羞辱
- **英文释义**：to make someone feel ashamed or embarrassed
- **例句**：
  - He was humiliated when he forgot his lines on stage.
  - 他在台上忘词时感到十分丢脸。
  - No one should be humiliated in front of others.
  - 不该让任何人在众人面前蒙羞。
- **助记**：humili（低、卑）+ -ate → 使某人低下 → 使蒙羞

### 15. **hypothetical** /ˌhaɪpəˈθetɪkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：假设的；假定的
- **英文释义**：based on a situation that is not real, but that might happen
- **例句**：
  - Let's consider a hypothetical situation: what if you won the lottery?
  - 让我们考虑一个假设情况：如果你中了彩票会怎样？
  - The exam included some hypothetical questions.
  - 考试包含了一些假设性问题。
- **助记**：hypothesis（假设）+ -ical → 假设的
