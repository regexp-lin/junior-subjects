## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **amplify** /ˈæmplɪfaɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：放大；扩大；增强
- **英文释义**：to make something louder, bigger, or more powerful
- **例句**：
  - The speaker used a microphone to amplify his voice.
  - 演讲者用麦克风放大了他的声音。
  - Social media can amplify both positive and negative messages.
  - 社交媒体可以放大正面和负面的信息。
- **助记**：ampli（大）+ fy（使）→ 使变大 → 放大；名词：amplification（放大）

### 2. **relinquish** /rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：放弃；交出；让出
- **英文释义**：to voluntarily give up something such as power, control, or a claim
- **例句**：
  - He relinquished his position as team captain at the end of the season.
  - 赛季结束时他交出了队长职务。
  - She was unwilling to relinquish control of the project.
  - 她不愿意放弃对项目的控制权。
- **助记**：re（回）+ linqu（离开）+ ish → 离开回去 → 放弃

### 3. **disperse** /dɪˈspɜːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：分散；散开；驱散
- **英文释义**：to spread over a wide area; to cause a group to break up
- **例句**：
  - The crowd dispersed peacefully after the concert ended.
  - 音乐会结束后人群和平散去。
  - The wind dispersed the clouds and the sun came out.
  - 风吹散了云，太阳出来了。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ perse（散）→ 散开 → 分散；名词：dispersal（散布）

### 4. **invoke** /ɪnˈvəʊk/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：援引；调用；祈求
- **英文释义**：to refer to a law, principle, or authority to support an argument; to call upon for help
- **例句**：
  - The lawyer invoked the Constitution to defend his client's rights.
  - 律师援引宪法为客户的权利辩护。
  - She invoked the help of her friends to organize the event.
  - 她请求朋友们帮忙组织活动。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ voke（声音/呼叫）→ 呼叫进来 → 援引/祈求

### 5. **catalyst** /ˈkætəlɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：催化剂；促进因素
- **英文释义**：something that causes an important change or event to happen
- **例句**：
  - The invention of the internet was a catalyst for global communication.
  - 互联网的发明是全球通信的催化剂。
  - Her speech served as a catalyst for social change.
  - 她的演讲成为社会变革的促进因素。
- **助记**：cata（向下）+ lyst（松开）→ 把反应松开加速 → 催化剂

### 6. **aftermath** /ˈɑːftəmæθ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：后果；余波；善后
- **英文释义**：the consequences or after-effects of a significant event
- **例句**：
  - In the aftermath of the earthquake, thousands of people were left homeless.
  - 地震过后，数千人无家可归。
  - The country struggled with the aftermath of the financial crisis.
  - 这个国家在金融危机的余波中挣扎。
- **助记**：after（之后）+ math（收获）→ 事后收获的结果 → 后果

### 7. **futile** /ˈfjuːtaɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的
- **英文释义**：having no useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - It was futile to try to change his mind once he had decided.
  - 一旦他做了决定，试图改变他的想法是徒劳的。
  - Their futile attempts to escape were quickly stopped by guards.
  - 他们徒劳的逃跑企图很快被警卫阻止了。
- **助记**：fut（流/倒）+ ile → 像水一样流走了 → 徒劳的；名词：futility（徒劳）

### 8. **stringent** /ˈstrɪndʒənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：严格的；严厉的
- **英文释义**：very strict and that must be obeyed
- **例句**：
  - The government introduced stringent regulations on food safety.
  - 政府对食品安全实施了严格的规定。
  - Stringent measures were taken to prevent cheating during the exam.
  - 采取了严格的措施防止考试作弊。
- **助记**：string（绳子/拉紧）+ ent → 拉得很紧的 → 严格的

### 9. **inadvertently** /ˌɪnədˈvɜːtəntli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：不经意地；无意中
- **英文释义**：without intending to; by accident
- **例句**：
  - She inadvertently deleted the important file from her computer.
  - 她无意中从电脑上删除了重要文件。
  - He inadvertently revealed the surprise party to the birthday girl.
  - 他不经意间把惊喜派对的事告诉了寿星。
- **助记**：in（不）+ advertently（注意地）→ 不注意地 → 不经意地；形容词：inadvertent（无意的）

### 10. **mediate** /ˈmiːdieɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：调解；斡旋；居中调停
- **英文释义**：to help settle a disagreement between two or more people or groups
- **例句**：
  - The teacher mediated between the two students who were arguing.
  - 老师在两个争吵的学生之间进行调解。
  - The United Nations tried to mediate the conflict between the two countries.
  - 联合国试图调解两国之间的冲突。
- **助记**：medi（中间）+ ate → 站在中间 → 调解；名词：mediation（调解）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **commensurate** /kəˈmenʃərət/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：相称的；相当的
- **英文释义**：corresponding in size, extent, or degree; proportionate
- **例句**：
  - The salary should be commensurate with the level of experience.
  - 薪水应与经验水平相称。
  - The punishment should be commensurate with the severity of the crime.
  - 惩罚应与罪行的严重程度相当。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ mensur（测量）+ ate → 共同测量出来一样的 → 相称的

### 12. **unilateral** /ˌjuːnɪˈlætərəl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：单方面的；单边的
- **英文释义**：done by or affecting only one side, group, or country
- **例句**：
  - The company made a unilateral decision without consulting employees.
  - 公司在未咨询员工的情况下做出了单方面的决定。
  - Unilateral actions can sometimes lead to international tensions.
  - 单方面行动有时会导致国际紧张局势。
- **助记**：uni（单一）+ lateral（侧面的）→ 单侧的 → 单方面的；反义词：bilateral（双边的）

### 13. **vindicate** /ˈvɪndɪkeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：证明…正当；为…辩护；证实
- **英文释义**：to prove that someone is not guilty or that something is correct
- **例句**：
  - The new evidence vindicated the suspect, proving his innocence.
  - 新证据证明嫌疑人是清白的。
  - History has vindicated her decision to invest in renewable energy.
  - 历史证明了她投资可再生能源的决定是正确的。
- **助记**：vind（力量/正义）+ icate → 用力量证明正义 → 证明正当；名词：vindication

### 14. **spurious** /ˈspjʊəriəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：虚假的；伪造的；不真实的
- **英文释义**：not genuine; false or fake
- **例句**：
  - The police rejected the suspect's spurious alibi.
  - 警方驳回了嫌疑人虚假的不在场证明。
  - Be careful of spurious claims found on unverified websites.
  - 小心在未经验证的网站上发现的虚假信息。
- **助记**：spur（刺激/假冒）+ ious → 假冒的 → 虚假的；近义词：false, counterfeit

### 15. **ramification** /ˌræmɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：后果；影响；分支
- **英文释义**：a consequence of an action or event, usually complex or unwelcome
- **例句**：
  - The political ramifications of the decision were far-reaching.
  - 这一决定的政治后果是深远的。
  - We must consider the ramifications before making any changes.
  - 我们必须在做任何改变之前考虑其影响。
- **助记**：rami（分支）+ fication → 分出去的枝条 → 分支/后果
