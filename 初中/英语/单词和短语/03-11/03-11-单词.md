## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **encourage** /ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：鼓励；激励；支持
- **英文释义**：to give someone confidence or hope; to support someone to do something
- **例句**：
  - My parents always encourage me to try new things.
  - 我的父母总是鼓励我尝试新事物。
  - The teacher encouraged the shy student to speak in class.
  - 老师鼓励那个害羞的学生在课堂上发言。
- **助记**：en（使...）+ courage（勇气）→ 使有勇气 → 鼓励；反义词：discourage（使气馁）

### 2. **experience** /ɪkˈspɪəriəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 经验；经历；体验；v. 经历；体验
- **英文释义**：n. knowledge or skill gained from doing something; v. to have something happen to you
- **例句**：
  - He has rich experience in teaching English.
  - 他在英语教学方面有丰富的经验。
  - She experienced different cultures during her trip to Europe.
  - 她在欧洲旅行期间体验了不同的文化。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ peri（尝试）+ ence → 从尝试中走出来 → 经验

### 3. **compare** /kəmˈpeə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：比较；对比；比喻
- **英文释义**：to examine things to see how they are similar or different
- **例句**：
  - Don't compare yourself with others — everyone is unique.
  - 不要把自己和别人比较——每个人都是独一无二的。
  - Compared with last year, his grades have improved a lot.
  - 与去年相比，他的成绩提高了很多。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pare（平等）→ 放在一起看是否平等 → 比较；固定搭配：compare...with/to...

### 4. **familiar** /fəˈmɪliə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：熟悉的；常见的
- **英文释义**：well known to you; easy to recognize because you have seen or experienced it before
- **例句**：
  - This song sounds very familiar — I think I've heard it before.
  - 这首歌听起来很熟悉——我想我以前听过。
  - Are you familiar with the rules of this game?
  - 你熟悉这个游戏的规则吗？
- **助记**：family（家庭）→ familiar → 像家人一样熟悉的；固定搭配：be familiar with（对...熟悉）

### 5. **tradition** /trəˈdɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：传统；惯例
- **英文释义**：a belief, custom, or way of doing something that has existed for a long time
- **例句**：
  - It is a tradition in China to eat dumplings during the Spring Festival.
  - 在中国，春节吃饺子是一个传统。
  - We should protect our cultural traditions while embracing modern life.
  - 我们应该在拥抱现代生活的同时保护文化传统。
- **助记**：tradit（传递）+ ion → 代代传递下来的东西 → 传统；形容词：traditional

### 6. **material** /məˈtɪəriəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：材料；素材；物质
- **英文释义**：a substance used for making things; information used for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - Wood is a common building material in many countries.
  - 木材在许多国家是一种常见的建筑材料。
  - The teacher prepared a lot of reading material for the class.
  - 老师为课堂准备了很多阅读材料。
- **助记**：mater（母亲）+ ial → 母亲般提供基础的东西 → 材料

### 7. **practical** /ˈpræktɪkl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：实际的；实用的；切实可行的
- **英文释义**：connected with real situations; likely to succeed or be effective
- **例句**：
  - The book offers practical advice on how to improve study habits.
  - 这本书提供了关于如何改善学习习惯的实用建议。
  - We need a practical solution to this problem, not just ideas.
  - 我们需要一个切实可行的解决方案，而不仅仅是想法。
- **助记**：practice（实践）+ al → 与实践相关的 → 实际的

### 8. **difficulty** /ˈdɪfɪkəlti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：困难；难题；难处
- **英文释义**：a problem or situation that is hard to deal with
- **例句**：
  - She had great difficulty in understanding the math question.
  - 她在理解那道数学题时有很大困难。
  - Don't be afraid of difficulties — they help you grow.
  - 不要害怕困难——困难帮助你成长。
- **助记**：difficult（困难的）+ y → 困难的状态 → 困难；固定搭配：have difficulty (in) doing sth.

### 9. **improve** /ɪmˈpruːv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：提高；改善；改进
- **英文释义**：to make something better, or to become better
- **例句**：
  - Practice speaking every day and your English will improve quickly.
  - 每天练习口语，你的英语会很快提高。
  - The government is trying to improve the air quality in big cities.
  - 政府正在努力改善大城市的空气质量。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ prove（证明好）→ 证明变好了 → 改善；名词：improvement

### 10. **ability** /əˈbɪləti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：能力；才能；本领
- **英文释义**：the power or skill to do something
- **例句**：
  - She has the ability to solve complex math problems.
  - 她有解决复杂数学题的能力。
  - Teamwork can help develop your ability to communicate with others.
  - 团队合作可以帮助培养你与人沟通的能力。
- **助记**：able（能...的）+ ity（名词后缀）→ 能做到的状态 → 能力；反义词：inability（无能）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **potential** /pəˈtenʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：adj. 潜在的；可能的；n. 潜力；潜能
- **英文释义**：adj. possible but not yet actual; n. the possibility of developing into something
- **例句**：
  - Every student has the potential to succeed if given the right support.
  - 如果给予正确的支持，每个学生都有成功的潜力。
  - We should be aware of the potential risks of using social media.
  - 我们应该意识到使用社交媒体的潜在风险。
- **助记**：potent（有力的）+ ial → 有力量但还未展现 → 潜在的；副词：potentially

### 12. **contemporary** /kənˈtemprəri/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：当代的；同时代的；现代的
- **英文释义**：belonging to the present time; existing at the same time as something else
- **例句**：
  - Contemporary art often uses new materials and technology.
  - 当代艺术经常使用新材料和新技术。
  - Shakespeare and Cervantes were contemporaries — they lived at the same time.
  - 莎士比亚和塞万提斯是同时代的人——他们生活在同一时期。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ tempor（时间）+ ary → 同一时间的 → 当代的；同义词：modern

### 13. **accommodate** /əˈkɒmədeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：容纳；为...提供住处；适应
- **英文释义**：to provide enough space or room for; to give someone a place to stay
- **例句**：
  - The new library can accommodate up to 500 students at a time.
  - 新图书馆一次可以容纳500名学生。
  - The hotel accommodated all the guests during the holiday season.
  - 假期期间，这家酒店为所有客人提供了住处。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ com（一起）+ mod（模式/方式）+ ate → 调整方式使大家在一起 → 容纳；名词：accommodation

### 14. **elaborate** /ɪˈlæbərət/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：精心制作的；复杂的；详尽的
- **英文释义**：very detailed and complicated; carefully prepared
- **例句**：
  - The students prepared an elaborate presentation for the science fair.
  - 学生们为科学展览准备了一个精心制作的演示文稿。
  - The wedding had an elaborate decoration with thousands of flowers.
  - 婚礼有精心设计的装饰，用了数千朵花。
- **助记**：e（出）+ labor（劳动）+ ate → 付出大量劳动制作出来的 → 精心制作的；动词用法 /ɪˈlæbəreɪt/（详细阐述）

### 15. **perspective** /pəˈspektɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：视角；观点；透视法
- **英文释义**：a particular way of thinking about something; a point of view
- **例句**：
  - Try to see the problem from a different perspective.
  - 试着从不同的角度看这个问题。
  - Traveling abroad can give you a new perspective on life.
  - 出国旅行可以给你一个全新的人生视角。
- **助记**：per（穿过）+ spect（看）+ ive → 穿过表面去看 → 视角；同义词：viewpoint, point of view
