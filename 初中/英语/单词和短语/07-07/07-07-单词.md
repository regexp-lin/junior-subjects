## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **hobble** /ˈhɒbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：蹒跚而行；跛行
- **英文释义**：to walk with difficulty because your feet or legs hurt
- **例句**：
  - He hobbled home on a twisted ankle after the game.
  - 比赛后他扭了脚踝，一瘸一拐地走回家。
  - The old man hobbled along the street with a walking stick.
  - 老人拄着拐杖蹒跚地沿街走着。
- **助记**：hobble → "好跛"（谐音）→ 走路好跛 → 跛行

### 2. **ransack** /ˈrænsæk/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：洗劫；彻底搜查
- **英文释义**：to search a place thoroughly, causing damage; to steal things from a place
- **例句**：
  - Burglars ransacked the house while the family was on holiday.
  - 一家人度假时，窃贼洗劫了房子。
  - She ransacked her bag looking for the lost key.
  - 她翻遍了包找丢失的钥匙。
- **助记**：ran（跑）+ sack（袋子）→ 跑来抢袋子 → 洗劫

### 3. **swindle** /ˈswɪndl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：诈骗；骗取
- **英文释义**：to cheat someone in order to get money or property from them
- **例句**：
  - The fake salesman swindled elderly people out of their savings.
  - 假销售员骗走了老人们的积蓄。
  - He was swindled into buying a fake painting.
  - 他被骗买了一幅假画。
- **助记**：swindle → "死问到"（谐音）→ 死缠烂打问到钱 → 诈骗

### 4. **whittle** /ˈwɪtl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：削；逐渐减少
- **英文释义**：to cut a piece of wood into a shape by repeatedly cutting small pieces from it; to gradually reduce something
- **例句**：
  - The craftsman whittled a small bird out of a piece of wood.
  - 工匠用一块木头削出了一只小鸟。
  - They managed to whittle down the list of candidates to five.
  - 他们设法将候选人名单缩减到五人。
- **助记**：whittle → "为特了"（谐音）→ 为了特别的东西而削木头 → 削

### 5. **dungeon** /ˈdʌndʒən/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：地牢；地下监狱
- **英文释义**：a dark underground room used as a prison, especially in a castle
- **例句**：
  - The prisoners were thrown into the dark dungeon beneath the castle.
  - 囚犯被投进了城堡下的黑暗地牢。
  - The adventure game starts in a mysterious dungeon.
  - 这个冒险游戏从一个神秘的地牢开始。
- **助记**：dungeon → "蹲坑"（谐音）→ 在地牢里蹲着 → 地牢

### 6. **amber** /ˈæmbə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：琥珀；琥珀色
- **英文释义**：a hard clear yellowish-brown substance used in jewellery; a yellowish-brown colour
- **例句**：
  - The museum displayed a piece of amber with an insect inside.
  - 博物馆展示了一块里面有昆虫的琥珀。
  - The traffic light turned amber before changing to red.
  - 交通灯变成琥珀色（黄灯）后就转红了。
- **助记**：amber → "暗伯"（谐音）→ 暗黄色的宝物 → 琥珀

### 7. **beret** /ˈbereɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：贝雷帽
- **英文释义**：a round, flat cap made of soft material
- **例句**：
  - The artist wore a black beret while painting in the park.
  - 画家戴着黑色贝雷帽在公园写生。
  - French soldiers are often seen wearing berets.
  - 法国士兵常被看到戴着贝雷帽。
- **助记**：beret → 发音本身就是"贝雷" → 贝雷帽

### 8. **savvy** /ˈsævi/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：精明的；有见识的
- **英文释义**：having practical knowledge and understanding of something
- **例句**：
  - She's a tech-savvy student who builds her own websites.
  - 她是一个精通科技的学生，自己建网站。
  - You need to be more financially savvy if you want to save money.
  - 如果想存钱，你需要在理财方面更精明些。
- **助记**：savvy → "杀V"（谐音）→ 能杀出V形反转的精明人 → 精明的

### 9. **arid** /ˈærɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：干旱的；贫瘠的
- **英文释义**：very dry, with little or no rain; lacking interest or excitement
- **例句**：
  - Few plants can survive in the arid desert climate.
  - 很少有植物能在干旱的沙漠气候中生存。
  - The lecture was so arid that half the class fell asleep.
  - 讲座太枯燥了，全班有一半人睡着了。
- **助记**：arid → "阿日的"（谐音）→ 被太阳晒的干旱之地 → 干旱的

### 10. **forthwith** /ˌfɔːθˈwɪð/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：立即；毫不迟延地
- **英文释义**：immediately; without delay
- **例句**：
  - The principal ordered the students to return to class forthwith.
  - 校长命令学生立即回到教室。
  - The contract was terminated forthwith after the breach was discovered.
  - 违约行为被发现后，合同立即被终止。
- **助记**：forth（向前）+ with（伴随）→ 伴随着立刻向前 → 立即

### 高一词汇

### 11. **expostulate** /ɪkˈspɒstjuleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：劝诫；争辩
- **英文释义**：to express strong disapproval or disagreement with someone
- **例句**：
  - The teacher expostulated with the student about his poor behavior.
  - 老师就学生不良行为对其进行了劝诫。
  - She expostulated against the unfair rule, but no one listened.
  - 她对不公平的规则提出了抗议，但没人听。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ postul（要求）+ -ate → 强烈提出要求 → 劝诫；争辩

### 12. **recapitulate** /ˌriːkəˈpɪtʃuleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：概括；扼要重述
- **英文释义**：to repeat the main points of something that has been said or discussed
- **例句**：
  - Let me recapitulate the main points of today's lesson.
  - 让我概括一下今天课程的重点。
  - The coach recapitulated the game plan before the match.
  - 教练在比赛前扼要重述了战术计划。
- **助记**：re-（再次）+ capit（头/要点）+ -ulate → 再次讲要点 → 概括；重述

### 13. **obstreperous** /əbˈstrepərəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：吵闹的；难以控制的
- **英文释义**：noisy and difficult to control
- **例句**：
  - The obstreperous crowd made it impossible for the speaker to continue.
  - 吵闹的人群让演讲者无法继续。
  - The teacher struggled to manage the obstreperous class.
  - 老师费力地管理这个吵闹的班级。
- **助记**：ob-（对着）+ strep（噪音）+ -erous → 对着发噪音的 → 吵闹的

### 14. **pedantic** /pɪˈdæntɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：学究气的；过分拘泥细节的
- **英文释义**：too concerned with small details or rules, especially when learning or teaching
- **例句**：
  - His pedantic corrections of everyone's grammar annoyed the whole group.
  - 他对每个人语法的学究式纠正惹恼了全组人。
  - Don't be so pedantic — you know what I meant.
  - 别那么钻牛角尖——你知道我什么意思。
- **助记**：pedant（学究）+ -ic → 像学究一样的 → 学究气的

### 15. **demagogue** /ˈdeməɡɒɡ/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：煽动者；蛊惑民心的政客
- **英文释义**：a political leader who gains support by appealing to people's emotions rather than using rational arguments
- **例句**：
  - The demagogue stirred up hatred to gain votes.
  - 这个煽动者通过煽动仇恨来获取选票。
  - History has shown that demagogues can be very dangerous.
  - 历史表明煽动者可以非常危险。
- **助记**：dem（人民）+ agogue（引导者）→ 引导人民的人（贬义）→ 煽动者
