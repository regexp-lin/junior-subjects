## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **afford** /əˈfɔːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：买得起；负担得起；提供
- **英文释义**：to have enough money or time to be able to do something
- **例句**：
  - They can't afford to buy a new car this year.
  - 他们今年买不起新车。
  - I can't afford to waste any more time on this project.
  - 我不能再在这个项目上浪费时间了。
- **助记**：af（朝向）+ ford（渡口/能力）→ 有能力到达 → 负担得起；固定搭配：can/can't afford to do sth.

### 2. **apologize** /əˈpɒlədʒaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：道歉；赔不是
- **英文释义**：to say you are sorry for doing something wrong
- **例句**：
  - He apologized to his teacher for being late.
  - 他因迟到向老师道歉。
  - You should apologize for breaking the window.
  - 你应该为打破窗户道歉。
- **助记**：apolog（辩解）+ ize → 为自己的错误辩解 → 道歉；名词：apology；固定搭配：apologize to sb. for sth.

### 3. **contact** /ˈkɒntækt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 联系；接触；v. 联系；联络
- **英文释义**：n. communication with someone; v. to get in touch with someone
- **例句**：
  - Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions.
  - 如果你有任何问题，请随时联系我。
  - We lost contact with each other after graduation.
  - 毕业后我们失去了联系。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ tact（触摸）→ 相互接触 → 联系

### 4. **deliver** /dɪˈlɪvə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：递送；发表（演讲）；接生
- **英文释义**：to take something to a person or place; to give a speech
- **例句**：
  - The postman delivers letters to our house every morning.
  - 邮递员每天早上把信送到我们家。
  - She delivered an excellent speech at the graduation ceremony.
  - 她在毕业典礼上发表了一篇精彩的演讲。
- **助记**：de（向下/完成）+ liver（释放）→ 完成释放/送达 → 递送；名词：delivery

### 5. **employ** /ɪmˈplɔɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：雇用；使用
- **英文释义**：to pay someone to work for you; to use something
- **例句**：
  - The company employs more than 500 people.
  - 这家公司雇用了500多人。
  - We should employ different strategies to solve different problems.
  - 我们应该使用不同的策略来解决不同的问题。
- **助记**：em（使...）+ ploy（折叠/使用）→ 使之为己所用 → 雇用；名词：employee（雇员）/ employer（雇主）/ employment（就业）

### 6. **fault** /fɔːlt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：过错；缺点；故障
- **英文释义**：responsibility for something wrong; a weakness or defect
- **例句**：
  - It's not your fault that we missed the bus.
  - 我们错过公交车不是你的错。
  - Every person has their own faults and strengths.
  - 每个人都有自己的缺点和优点。
- **助记**：fault 本身就是"错误"，联想 default（违约/默认）

### 7. **gesture** /ˈdʒestʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：手势；姿态；表示
- **英文释义**：a movement of the hand or body to express an idea or feeling
- **例句**：
  - He made a gesture to show that he agreed with the plan.
  - 他做了一个手势表示他同意这个计划。
  - Sending flowers is a nice gesture of friendship.
  - 送花是表达友谊的好举动。
- **助记**：gest（携带/做）+ ure → 用身体做出表示 → 手势

### 8. **honest** /ˈɒnɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：诚实的；正直的
- **英文释义**：telling the truth and not trying to deceive people
- **例句**：
  - To be honest, I don't think this plan will work.
  - 说实话，我不认为这个计划行得通。
  - An honest person always tells the truth, even when it's difficult.
  - 诚实的人总是说真话，即使很难开口。
- **助记**：honest 中 h 不发音，和 honor（荣誉）同源 → 有荣誉感的 → 诚实的；名词：honesty

### 9. **journey** /ˈdʒɜːni/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：旅行；旅程；历程
- **英文释义**：the act of traveling from one place to another, especially over a long distance
- **例句**：
  - The journey from Beijing to Shanghai by train takes about 5 hours.
  - 从北京坐火车到上海大约需要5个小时。
  - Learning a new language is a long but rewarding journey.
  - 学习一门新语言是一段漫长但有意义的旅程。
- **助记**：jour（天，如 journal 日志）+ ney → 一天天走的路 → 旅行

### 10. **local** /ˈləʊkl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：当地的；本地的
- **英文释义**：relating to a particular place, especially the area where you live
- **例句**：
  - We like to eat at the local restaurant near our school.
  - 我们喜欢在学校附近的本地餐馆吃饭。
  - The local government is building a new park for residents.
  - 当地政府正在为居民建一个新公园。
- **助记**：loc（地方）+ al（形容词后缀）→ 与某个地方相关的 → 当地的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **essential** /ɪˈsenʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：必要的；本质的；基本的
- **英文释义**：completely necessary; extremely important
- **例句**：
  - Water is essential for all living things.
  - 水对所有生物都是必不可少的。
  - It is essential that students develop good study habits early.
  - 学生尽早养成良好的学习习惯是非常必要的。
- **助记**：essent（存在，essence 的词根）+ ial → 与存在相关的 → 本质的、必要的

### 12. **flexible** /ˈfleksəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：灵活的；可变通的；柔韧的
- **英文释义**：able to change easily; able to bend without breaking
- **例句**：
  - We need a more flexible schedule to meet everyone's needs.
  - 我们需要一个更灵活的时间表来满足每个人的需要。
  - Yoga can make your body more flexible.
  - 瑜伽可以让你的身体更柔韧。
- **助记**：flex（弯曲）+ ible（能...的）→ 能弯曲的 → 灵活的；名词：flexibility

### 13. **innocent** /ˈɪnəsnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无辜的；天真的；无害的
- **英文释义**：not guilty of a crime; not having much experience of the world
- **例句**：
  - The court proved that he was innocent of the crime.
  - 法庭证明了他是无罪的。
  - She gave an innocent smile that made everyone trust her.
  - 她露出天真的微笑，让每个人都信任她。
- **助记**：in（不）+ nocent（有害的）→ 没有害处的 → 无辜的；名词：innocence

### 14. **negotiate** /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：谈判；协商
- **英文释义**：to discuss something formally in order to reach an agreement
- **例句**：
  - The two countries negotiated a peace agreement after months of discussion.
  - 经过数月的讨论，两国谈判达成了和平协议。
  - She successfully negotiated a higher salary with her new employer.
  - 她成功地与新雇主协商了更高的薪水。
- **助记**：neg（不）+ oti（休闲）+ ate → 没有休闲，忙于商谈 → 谈判；名词：negotiation

### 15. **stimulate** /ˈstɪmjuleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：刺激；激发；促进
- **英文释义**：to encourage something to develop or happen; to make someone feel interested
- **例句**：
  - Good questions from the teacher can stimulate students' thinking.
  - 老师的好问题可以激发学生的思考。
  - The government introduced new policies to stimulate economic growth.
  - 政府出台新政策来刺激经济增长。
- **助记**：stimul（刺）+ ate → 用刺去激发 → 刺激；名词：stimulation
