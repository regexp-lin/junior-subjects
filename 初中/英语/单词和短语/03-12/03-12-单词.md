## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **examine** /ɪɡˈzæmɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：检查；审查；考试
- **英文释义**：to look at something carefully to check it; to test someone's knowledge
- **例句**：
  - The doctor examined the patient carefully before giving any medicine.
  - 医生在开药之前仔细检查了病人。
  - The teacher will examine us on Unit 5 next Monday.
  - 老师下周一将测试我们第五单元的内容。
- **助记**：exam（考试）+ ine → 检查/考试；名词：examination

### 2. **curious** /ˈkjʊəriəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：好奇的；求知欲强的
- **英文释义**：wanting to know about something; interested in learning new things
- **例句**：
  - Children are naturally curious about the world around them.
  - 孩子们天生对周围的世界充满好奇。
  - I'm curious about how this machine works.
  - 我很好奇这台机器是怎么工作的。
- **助记**：cur（关心）+ ious → 非常关心 → 好奇的；名词：curiosity；固定搭配：be curious about

### 3. **organize** /ˈɔːɡənaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：组织；安排；整理
- **英文释义**：to plan and arrange an event or activity; to put things in order
- **例句**：
  - Who will organize the school sports meeting this year?
  - 今年谁来组织学校运动会？
  - You need to organize your notes before the exam.
  - 考试前你需要整理你的笔记。
- **助记**：organ（器官/机构）+ ize → 像器官一样有序排列 → 组织；名词：organization

### 4. **respect** /rɪˈspekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 尊重；敬意；v. 尊敬；尊重
- **英文释义**：n. a feeling of admiration for someone; v. to feel or show admiration for someone
- **例句**：
  - We should show respect for the elderly.
  - 我们应该尊重老人。
  - I respect her because she always keeps her promises.
  - 我尊敬她，因为她总是信守承诺。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ spect（看）→ 值得再看一眼 → 尊敬；形容词：respectful

### 5. **prepare** /prɪˈpeə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：准备；预备
- **英文释义**：to make something or someone ready for use or an event
- **例句**：
  - She spent the whole weekend preparing for the speech contest.
  - 她花了整个周末为演讲比赛做准备。
  - My mother is preparing dinner in the kitchen.
  - 我妈妈正在厨房准备晚饭。
- **助记**：pre（提前）+ pare（安排）→ 提前安排 → 准备；名词：preparation；固定搭配：prepare for

### 6. **predict** /prɪˈdɪkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：预测；预言
- **英文释义**：to say what will happen in the future based on information you have now
- **例句**：
  - It's difficult to predict what the weather will be like next week.
  - 很难预测下周天气会怎么样。
  - Scientists predict that sea levels will rise in the coming decades.
  - 科学家预测在未来几十年海平面将会上升。
- **助记**：pre（提前）+ dict（说）→ 提前说出来 → 预测；名词：prediction

### 7. **behave** /bɪˈheɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：表现；举止；行为端正
- **英文释义**：to act in a particular way; to act properly
- **例句**：
  - The students behaved very well during the school trip.
  - 学生们在学校旅行期间表现得很好。
  - Please behave yourself at the dinner table.
  - 请在餐桌上举止得体。
- **助记**：be + have（拥有）→ 拥有的表现 → 行为举止；名词：behavior/behaviour

### 8. **concern** /kənˈsɜːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 担心；关心；v. 使担忧；涉及
- **英文释义**：n. a feeling of worry; v. to make someone feel worried; to be about something
- **例句**：
  - Air pollution is a major concern for people in big cities.
  - 空气污染是大城市人们的一个主要担忧。
  - This problem concerns every one of us.
  - 这个问题涉及我们每一个人。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ cern（关心）→ 共同关心 → 关注、担忧；形容词：concerned（担心的）

### 9. **temperature** /ˈtemprətʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：温度；气温；体温
- **英文释义**：how hot or cold something is
- **例句**：
  - The temperature dropped to -10°C last night.
  - 昨晚气温降到了零下10度。
  - The nurse took my temperature and said I had a fever.
  - 护士量了我的体温，说我发烧了。
- **助记**：temper（温度/脾气）+ ature → 温度的程度 → 温度；固定搭配：take one's temperature（量体温）

### 10. **ceremony** /ˈserəməni/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：仪式；典礼
- **英文释义**：a formal event held on a special occasion
- **例句**：
  - The opening ceremony of the sports meeting was very impressive.
  - 运动会的开幕式非常令人印象深刻。
  - They held a graduation ceremony at the end of the school year.
  - 他们在学年结束时举行了毕业典礼。
- **助记**：cere（蜡，古代仪式用蜡烛）+ mony → 点蜡烛的庄严场合 → 仪式

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ambitious** /æmˈbɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有雄心的；有抱负的
- **英文释义**：having a strong desire to be successful or powerful
- **例句**：
  - She is an ambitious student who aims to attend a top university.
  - 她是一个有雄心的学生，目标是考上一流大学。
  - The government has an ambitious plan to reduce carbon emissions.
  - 政府有一个雄心勃勃的减少碳排放计划。
- **助记**：ambition（抱负）+ ous → 充满抱负的 → 有雄心的

### 12. **emphasize** /ˈemfəsaɪz/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：强调；着重
- **英文释义**：to give special importance to something
- **例句**：
  - The teacher emphasized the importance of reviewing before exams.
  - 老师强调了考前复习的重要性。
  - I want to emphasize that safety comes first in this experiment.
  - 我想强调在这个实验中安全是第一位的。
- **助记**：em（使...）+ phas（显示）+ ize → 使显示出来 → 强调；名词：emphasis

### 13. **guarantee** /ˌɡærənˈtiː/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 保证；担保；v. 保证；担保
- **英文释义**：n. a promise that something will be done; v. to promise that something will happen
- **例句**：
  - Hard work doesn't guarantee success, but it increases your chances.
  - 努力不能保证成功，但可以增加你的机会。
  - The company offers a one-year guarantee on all its products.
  - 该公司对其所有产品提供一年质保。
- **助记**：guaran（保护）+ tee → 提供保护 → 保证

### 14. **reliable** /rɪˈlaɪəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：可靠的；可信赖的
- **英文释义**：able to be trusted; consistently good in quality
- **例句**：
  - She is a reliable friend — you can always count on her.
  - 她是一个可靠的朋友——你总是可以依靠她。
  - Is this source of information reliable enough for our research?
  - 这个信息来源对我们的研究来说足够可靠吗？
- **助记**：rely（依靠）+ able（能...的）→ 能依靠的 → 可靠的；反义词：unreliable

### 15. **tendency** /ˈtendənsi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：趋势；倾向；趋向
- **英文释义**：a way of behaving or a likelihood of something happening
- **例句**：
  - There is a tendency for young people to spend more time on social media.
  - 年轻人有花更多时间在社交媒体上的倾向。
  - He has a tendency to forget things when he's busy.
  - 他忙的时候容易忘记事情。
- **助记**：tend（倾向于）+ ency（名词后缀）→ 倾向的状态 → 趋势
