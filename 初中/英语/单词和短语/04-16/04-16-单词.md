## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **replenish** /rɪˈplenɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：补充；重新装满
- **英文释义**：to fill something up again; to restore to a previous level
- **例句**：
  - We need to replenish our water supply before continuing the hike.
  - 我们需要在继续徒步前补充饮用水。
  - The store replenishes its shelves every morning.
  - 这家商店每天早上补货上架。
- **助记**：re（再）+ plen（满）+ ish → 再次使满 → 补充；名词：replenishment（补充）

### 2. **scrutinize** /ˈskruːtənaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：仔细审查；细看
- **英文释义**：to examine something very carefully
- **例句**：
  - The accountant scrutinized every detail of the financial report.
  - 会计仔细审查了财务报告的每一个细节。
  - Voters should scrutinize the candidates' promises before making a choice.
  - 选民在做选择之前应仔细审视候选人的承诺。
- **助记**：scrutin（检查）+ ize → 进行检查 → 仔细审查

### 3. **overhaul** /ˌəʊvəˈhɔːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：大修；全面检修；彻底改革
- **英文释义**：to examine carefully and make necessary repairs or changes
- **例句**：
  - The car engine needs a complete overhaul.
  - 汽车发动机需要全面检修。
  - The government plans to overhaul the education system.
  - 政府计划全面改革教育体系。
- **助记**：over（全面）+ haul（拖/拉）→ 全面拉出来检查 → 大修

### 4. **calibrate** /ˈkælɪbreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：校准；校正；标定
- **英文释义**：to check or adjust a measuring instrument for accuracy
- **例句**：
  - Scientists must calibrate their instruments before starting an experiment.
  - 科学家在开始实验前必须校准仪器。
  - The teacher calibrated the grading system to be fair to all students.
  - 老师调整了评分系统以对所有学生公平。
- **助记**：calibr（口径/标准）+ ate → 按标准调整 → 校准；名词：calibration（校准）

### 5. **jurisdiction** /ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：管辖权；管辖范围；司法权
- **英文释义**：the official power to make legal decisions; the area where this power is valid
- **例句**：
  - The case falls outside the jurisdiction of this court.
  - 这个案子不在这个法院的管辖范围内。
  - Different countries have different jurisdictions over international waters.
  - 不同国家对国际水域有不同的管辖权。
- **助记**：juris（法律）+ dict（说/宣告）+ ion → 宣告法律的权力 → 管辖权

### 6. **discrepancy** /dɪˈskrepənsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：差异；不一致；矛盾
- **英文释义**：a difference between two things that should be the same
- **例句**：
  - There was a discrepancy between the two reports on the same topic.
  - 关于同一话题的两份报告之间存在差异。
  - The teacher noticed a discrepancy in the student's test scores.
  - 老师注意到学生考试成绩中的不一致。
- **助记**：dis（不同）+ crep（声音）+ ancy → 发出不同声音 → 差异/不一致

### 7. **plausible** /ˈplɔːzəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：合理的；可信的；似乎有道理的
- **英文释义**：seeming reasonable or probable; appearing to be true
- **例句**：
  - His explanation sounded plausible, but I still had doubts.
  - 他的解释听起来合理，但我仍有疑虑。
  - The detective considered several plausible theories about the crime.
  - 侦探考虑了几种关于犯罪的合理理论。
- **助记**：plaus（鼓掌/赞同）+ ible → 值得鼓掌赞同的 → 合理的

### 8. **impeccable** /ɪmˈpekəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无可挑剔的；完美的
- **英文释义**：without any mistakes or flaws; faultless
- **例句**：
  - She spoke English with impeccable pronunciation.
  - 她说英语的发音无可挑剔。
  - The hotel is known for its impeccable service.
  - 这家酒店以无可挑剔的服务闻名。
- **助记**：im（不）+ pecc（犯罪/错误）+ able → 不犯错的 → 无可挑剔的

### 9. **henceforth** /ˌhensˈfɔːθ/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：从此以后；今后
- **英文释义**：from this time on; starting from now
- **例句**：
  - Henceforth, all students must wear their ID cards on campus.
  - 从今以后，所有学生必须在校园内佩戴校卡。
  - The new rule will be enforced henceforth.
  - 新规定从此以后将被执行。
- **助记**：hence（从此）+ forth（向前）→ 从此向前 → 从今以后

### 10. **instigate** /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：煽动；教唆；发起
- **英文释义**：to cause something to happen, especially something bad; to encourage someone to do something wrong
- **例句**：
  - He was accused of instigating the fight between the two groups.
  - 他被指控煽动两个团体之间的斗殴。
  - The company instigated an investigation into the safety concerns.
  - 公司发起了一项关于安全问题的调查。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ stig（刺）+ ate → 从内部刺激 → 煽动；名词：instigation（煽动）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **delineate** /dɪˈlɪnieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：勾画；描述；界定
- **英文释义**：to describe or portray something precisely; to indicate the boundary
- **例句**：
  - The map clearly delineates the borders between the two provinces.
  - 地图清楚地界定了两个省之间的边界。
  - The report delineated the main causes of the environmental problem.
  - 报告详细描述了环境问题的主要原因。
- **助记**：de（加强）+ line（线）+ ate → 用线勾画出来 → 勾画/界定；名词：delineation

### 12. **predispose** /ˌpriːdɪˈspəʊz/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使倾向于；使易受影响
- **英文释义**：to make someone more likely to behave in a particular way or to have a particular condition
- **例句**：
  - Genetics can predispose a person to certain diseases.
  - 基因可以使人容易患某些疾病。
  - A positive attitude predisposes people to succeed.
  - 积极的态度使人更容易成功。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ dispose（倾向）→ 预先有倾向 → 使倾向于；名词：predisposition（倾向）

### 13. **surreptitious** /ˌsʌrəpˈtɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：偷偷摸摸的；秘密的
- **英文释义**：done secretly, especially because it would not be approved of
- **例句**：
  - She took a surreptitious glance at her phone during the meeting.
  - 她在会议中偷偷地瞥了一眼手机。
  - The student made a surreptitious attempt to copy from his neighbor's paper.
  - 那个学生偷偷地试图抄袭旁边同学的试卷。
- **助记**：sur（在下面）+ rept（爬）+ itious → 在下面偷偷爬行的 → 偷偷摸摸的

### 14. **retrospective** /ˌretrəˈspektɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：回顾的；追溯的
- **英文释义**：looking back on or relating to the past
- **例句**：
  - The museum held a retrospective exhibition of the artist's work.
  - 博物馆举办了一场该艺术家作品的回顾展。
  - In retrospective analysis, the decision proved to be correct.
  - 在回顾分析中，这一决定被证明是正确的。
- **助记**：retro（回）+ spect（看）+ ive → 回头看的 → 回顾的；名词：retrospect（回顾）

### 15. **inadmissible** /ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不可接受的；不能采纳的
- **英文释义**：not allowed to be used as evidence in a court of law; unacceptable
- **例句**：
  - The judge ruled that the confession was inadmissible as evidence.
  - 法官裁定该供词不可作为证据采纳。
  - Such behavior is inadmissible in a professional workplace.
  - 这种行为在专业的工作环境中是不可接受的。
- **助记**：in（不）+ admissible（可接纳的）→ 不可接纳的；反义词：admissible（可接受的）
