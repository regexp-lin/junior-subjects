## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **famine** /ˈfæmɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：饥荒
- **英文释义**：extreme scarcity of food affecting a large population
- **例句**：
  - The famine in that region left many people without enough to eat.
  - 那场饥荒使该地区许多人吃不饱。
  - Charities raised money to help victims of the famine.
  - 慈善机构筹款帮助饥荒灾民。
- **助记**：fam（饥饿）+ -ine（名词）→ 大范围饥饿 → 饥荒

### 2. **fantasy** /ˈfæntəsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：幻想；想象
- **英文释义**：the faculty of imagining things that are impossible or improbable; a daydream
- **例句**：
  - Reading fantasy novels helps me escape into magical worlds.
  - 读幻想小说让我沉浸在魔法世界里。
  - It was just a fantasy — I never thought it would come true.
  - 那只是幻想——我从没想过它会成真。
- **助记**：fantas（想象）+ -y（名词）→ 脑海中的想象 → 幻想

### 3. **fare** /feə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：票价；费用
- **英文释义**：the money a passenger pays for a journey; the cost of travel
- **例句**：
  - The bus fare has increased to three yuan per trip.
  - 公交车票价已涨到每程三元。
  - Students can get a discount on train fares with a valid student card.
  - 持有效学生证可以享受火车票价折扣。
- **助记**：fare 与 far（远）相关 → 去远方要付的钱 → 票价；费用

### 4. **fascinate** /ˈfæsɪneɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使着迷；迷住
- **英文释义**：to attract and hold the attention of someone intensely
- **例句**：
  - The documentary about space fascinated the whole class.
  - 关于太空的纪录片让全班着迷。
  - She has always been fascinated by ancient Chinese history.
  - 她一直对古代中国历史着迷。
- **助记**：fascin（古罗马"魔眼"）+ -ate（动词）→ 像被魔眼盯住 → 使着迷

### 5. **fatigue** /fəˈtiːɡ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：疲劳；疲倦
- **英文释义**：extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion
- **例句**：
  - After the marathon, he felt extreme fatigue and needed to rest.
  - 马拉松比赛后他感到极度疲劳，需要休息。
  - Lack of sleep can cause fatigue and affect your concentration.
  - 睡眠不足会导致疲劳并影响注意力。
- **助记**：fatigu（劳累）+ -e → 身体或精神劳累的状态 → 疲劳

### 6. **feasible** /ˈfiːzəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：可行的
- **英文释义**：possible and practical to do
- **例句**：
  - The teacher said our project plan was feasible and we could start.
  - 老师说我们的项目计划可行，可以开始了。
  - Is it feasible to finish the report by Friday?
  - 周五前完成报告可行吗？
- **助记**：feas（做）+ -ible（能…的）→ 能做的 → 可行的

### 7. **feast** /fiːst/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：盛宴；宴会
- **英文释义**：a large and elaborate meal, typically one in celebration
- **例句**：
  - The wedding feast was held in a beautiful garden.
  - 婚宴在美丽的花园里举行。
  - During the Spring Festival, families prepare a feast for reunion dinner.
  - 春节期间，各家准备盛宴作为团圆饭。
- **助记**：feast 与 festival（节日）同源 → 节日里的丰盛聚餐 → 盛宴

### 8. **feature** /ˈfiːtʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：特征；特色
- **英文释义**：a distinctive attribute or aspect of something
- **例句**：
  - The main feature of this phone is its long battery life.
  - 这款手机的主要特色是续航时间长。
  - Each culture has its own unique features and customs.
  - 每种文化都有其独特的特征和习俗。
- **助记**：feat（做）+ -ure（名词）→ 做出的样子 → 特征；特色

### 9. **fiction** /ˈfɪkʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：小说；虚构
- **英文释义**：literature in the form of prose that describes imaginary events; something invented
- **例句**：
  - I prefer reading fiction to nonfiction because I enjoy stories.
  - 比起非虚构，我更喜欢读小说，因为我喜欢故事。
  - The character in the movie is pure fiction — he never existed.
  - 电影里的角色纯属虚构——他从未存在过。
- **助记**：fict（塑造）+ -ion（名词）→ 塑造出来的故事 → 小说；虚构

### 10. **fierce** /fɪəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：凶猛的；激烈的
- **英文释义**：violently aggressive or frightening; having or displaying intense energy
- **例句**：
  - The tiger is a fierce predator in the wild.
  - 老虎是野外的凶猛捕食者。
  - There was a fierce competition for the top prize.
  - 为争夺最高奖项展开了激烈竞争。
- **助记**：fierce 与 fire（火）相关 → 像火一样猛 → 凶猛的；激烈的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **forthcoming** /ˌfɔːθˈkʌmɪŋ/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：即将来临的
- **英文释义**：about to happen; approaching in time
- **例句**：
  - The forthcoming exam has made everyone nervous.
  - 即将到来的考试让大家都很紧张。
  - Details about the forthcoming event will be announced next week.
  - 关于即将举行的活动的详情将于下周公布。
- **助记**：forth（向前）+ coming（来临）→ 正往前来的 → 即将来临的

### 12. **frivolous** /ˈfrɪvələs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：轻浮的；琐碎的
- **英文释义**：not having any serious purpose or value; silly
- **例句**：
  - Don't waste time on frivolous gossip — focus on your studies.
  - 别把时间浪费在无聊的八卦上——专心学习。
  - The judge dismissed the case as frivolous.
  - 法官以琐碎为由驳回了此案。
- **助记**：frivol（无价值）+ -ous（形容词）→ 没有价值的 → 轻浮的；琐碎的

### 13. **frugal** /ˈfruːɡl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：节俭的
- **英文释义**：sparing or economical with money or food
- **例句**：
  - My grandmother is very frugal; she saves every penny.
  - 我奶奶非常节俭，每一分钱都存着。
  - Living a frugal lifestyle helped him save enough for university.
  - 节俭的生活方式帮他攒够了上大学的钱。
- **助记**：frug（果实、利用）+ -al → 充分利用每一分 → 节俭的

### 14. **futile** /ˈfjuːtaɪl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：徒劳的；无用的
- **英文释义**：incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
- **例句**：
  - It would be futile to try to change his mind — he's very stubborn.
  - 想改变他的主意是徒劳的——他很固执。
  - All our efforts to save the old tree proved futile.
  - 我们拯救那棵老树的一切努力都白费了。
- **助记**：fut（流走）+ -ile（形容词）→ 像水一样流走无果 → 徒劳的

### 15. **galvanize** /ˈɡælvənaɪz/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：激励；使震惊
- **英文释义**：to shock or excite someone into taking action; to stimulate
- **例句**：
  - The coach's speech galvanized the team into winning the match.
  - 教练的演讲激励全队赢得了比赛。
  - The crisis galvanized the community to work together.
  - 这场危机激励社区团结协作。
- **助记**：源自 galvanism（伽伐尼电流）→ 像被电击一样被激励 → 激励；使震惊
