## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **exhaust** /ɪɡˈzɔːst/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使筋疲力尽；耗尽
- **英文释义**：to make someone very tired; to use up completely
- **例句**：
  - The long hike exhausted everyone in the group.
  - 长途徒步让队里每个人都筋疲力尽。
  - We have exhausted all possible solutions.
  - 我们已经用尽了所有可能的解决方案。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ haust（抽干）→ 把力气抽干 → 使筋疲力尽

### 2. **exhibit** /ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：展览；展示
- **英文释义**：to display something publicly; a collection of objects displayed for viewing
- **例句**：
  - The museum will exhibit rare fossils next month.
  - 博物馆下个月将展览稀有化石。
  - Her artwork won first prize at the school exhibit.
  - 她的作品在学校展览上获得了一等奖。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ hibit（拿）→ 拿出来给人看 → 展览

### 3. **exile** /ˈeksaɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：流放；放逐
- **英文释义**：the state of being forced to live away from one's country; to send someone into exile
- **例句**：
  - The poet lived in exile for many years during the war.
  - 这位诗人在战争期间流亡海外多年。
  - The king was exiled from his kingdom after the revolution.
  - 革命后，国王被放逐出境。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ ile（走）→ 被赶出去 → 流放

### 4. **exotic** /ɪɡˈzɒtɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：异国的；奇异的
- **英文释义**：originating from a distant foreign country; strikingly unusual
- **例句**：
  - She loves exotic fruits like dragon fruit and passion fruit.
  - 她喜欢火龙果、百香果这类异国水果。
  - The exotic bird had bright colorful feathers.
  - 这只奇异的鸟有着鲜艳的羽毛。
- **助记**：exo-（外）+ -tic（形容词）→ 来自外部的 → 异国的

### 5. **expand** /ɪkˈspænd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：扩大；扩展
- **英文释义**：to become or make larger in size, scope, or range
- **例句**：
  - The company plans to expand into the Asian market.
  - 公司计划拓展亚洲市场。
  - Take a deep breath and expand your lungs.
  - 深呼吸，让肺部扩张。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ pand（ spread 张开）→ 向外张开 → 扩大

### 6. **expedition** /ˌekspɪˈdɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：远征；探险
- **英文释义**：a journey undertaken for a specific purpose, especially exploration or research
- **例句**：
  - They organized an expedition to climb Mount Everest.
  - 他们组织了一次攀登珠穆朗玛峰的探险。
  - The scientific expedition discovered new species in the rainforest.
  - 科学考察队在雨林中发现了新物种。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ ped（脚）+ -ition → 走出去的旅程 → 远征

### 7. **expense** /ɪkˈspens/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：费用；开支
- **英文释义**：the cost required for something; money spent
- **例句**：
  - The expense of traveling abroad can be quite high.
  - 出国旅行的费用可能很高。
  - We need to cut down on unnecessary expenses.
  - 我们需要削减不必要的开支。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ pense（支付）→ 付出去的钱 → 费用

### 8. **expertise** /ˌekspɜːˈtiːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：专业知识；专长
- **英文释义**：expert skill or knowledge in a particular field
- **例句**：
  - She has great expertise in computer programming.
  - 她在计算机编程方面很有专长。
  - We need someone with medical expertise for this project.
  - 这个项目需要具备医学专业知识的人。
- **助记**：expert（专家）+ -ise（名词）→ 专家的能力 → 专业知识

### 9. **explicit** /ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：明确的；清楚的
- **英文释义**：stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion
- **例句**：
  - The teacher gave explicit instructions for the assignment.
  - 老师对作业给出了明确的说明。
  - There are explicit rules against cheating in the exam.
  - 考试有明确规定禁止作弊。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ plic（折叠）+ -it → 完全展开的 → 明确的

### 10. **exploit** /ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：利用；剥削
- **英文释义**：to use something fully for benefit; to treat someone unfairly for one's own benefit
- **例句**：
  - We should exploit renewable energy to protect the environment.
  - 我们应该利用可再生能源来保护环境。
  - The factory was accused of exploiting child labor.
  - 这家工厂被指控剥削童工。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ ploit（折叠）→ 把潜力完全展开出来 → 利用

### 高一词汇

### 11. **expedite** /ˈekspɪdaɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：加速；促进
- **英文释义**：to make an action or process happen sooner or more quickly
- **例句**：
  - We need to expedite the delivery to meet the deadline.
  - 我们需要加速配送以满足截止日期。
  - The new software expedites the process of data analysis.
  - 新软件加速了数据分析的流程。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ ped（脚）+ -ite → 让脚步更快 → 加速

### 12. **exponential** /ˌekspəˈnenʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：指数的；急剧增长的
- **英文释义**：becoming faster and faster; involving a quantity raised to a power
- **例句**：
  - The virus spread at an exponential rate.
  - 病毒以指数级速度传播。
  - Technology has grown exponentially in the past decade.
  - 过去十年技术呈指数级增长。
- **助记**：exponent（指数）+ -ial → 呈指数变化的 → 急剧增长的

### 13. **extravagant** /ɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：奢侈的；铺张的
- **英文释义**：spending or costing too much money; exceeding reasonable limits
- **例句**：
  - The wedding was an extravagant affair with hundreds of guests.
  - 婚礼铺张奢华，有数百位宾客。
  - She has a reputation for extravagant spending on clothes.
  - 她以在服装上奢侈消费而闻名。
- **助记**：extra-（超出）+ vag（漫游）+ -ant → 超出常规漫游 → 奢侈的

### 14. **fabricate** /ˈfæbrɪkeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：捏造；编造
- **英文释义**：to invent or concoct something, especially a lie or false story
- **例句**：
  - The student fabricated an excuse for being late.
  - 学生为迟到编造了一个借口。
  - The witness was caught fabricating evidence in court.
  - 证人在法庭上被抓住捏造证据。
- **助记**：fabric（制造）+ -ate → 凭空制造 → 捏造

### 15. **facetious** /fəˈsiːʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：爱开玩笑的；滑稽的
- **英文释义**：treating serious issues with deliberate or inappropriate humor
- **例句**：
  - Don't be facetious — this is a serious matter.
  - 别开玩笑——这是严肃的事情。
  - His facetious remarks sometimes get him into trouble.
  - 他爱开玩笑的话有时会给他惹来麻烦。
- **助记**：face（脸）+ -tious → 脸上带着玩笑的表情 → 爱开玩笑的
