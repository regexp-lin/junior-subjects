## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **engage** /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：从事；参与；吸引
- **英文释义**：to take part in an activity; to attract and hold attention
- **例句**：
  - Students should engage in more outdoor activities after school.
  - 学生们放学后应该多参与户外活动。
  - The new game engaged the children's attention for hours.
  - 这个新游戏吸引了孩子们数小时的注意力。
- **助记**：en（使）+ gage（保证/参与）→ 使参与 → 从事；名词：engagement（参与；订婚）

### 2. **invest** /ɪnˈvest/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：投资；投入（时间/精力）
- **英文释义**：to put money, time, or effort into something to get a benefit
- **例句**：
  - My parents invested money in stocks and real estate.
  - 我父母把钱投资到了股票和房地产。
  - If you invest time in reading, your vocabulary will grow quickly.
  - 如果你投入时间阅读，你的词汇量会快速增长。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ vest（穿衣/投入）→ 把钱投入进去 → 投资；名词：investment（投资）

### 3. **interfere** /ˌɪntəˈfɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：干涉；妨碍；干扰
- **英文释义**：to get involved in a situation that does not concern you; to prevent something from working properly
- **例句**：
  - Please don't interfere in other people's private affairs.
  - 请不要干涉别人的私事。
  - The loud noise from the construction site interfered with our study.
  - 工地的噪音干扰了我们的学习。
- **助记**：inter（在…之间）+ fere（打击）→ 在中间打击 → 干涉；名词：interference（干涉）

### 4. **prospect** /ˈprɒspekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：前景；可能性；展望
- **英文释义**：the possibility that something will happen; the expected future situation
- **例句**：
  - The prospect of a long holiday made everyone excited.
  - 长假的前景让每个人都很兴奋。
  - Young graduates face good career prospects in the technology industry.
  - 年轻毕业生在科技行业有良好的职业前景。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ spect（看）→ 向前看 → 展望/前景；形容词：prospective（预期的）

### 5. **dignity** /ˈdɪɡnəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：尊严；庄重
- **英文释义**：the quality of being worthy of respect
- **例句**：
  - Everyone deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
  - 每个人都应该被有尊严地对待。
  - She maintained her dignity even in difficult times.
  - 即使在困难时期她也保持着尊严。
- **助记**：dign（值得/有价值）+ ity → 有价值的品质 → 尊严；形容词：dignified（有尊严的）

### 6. **massive** /ˈmæsɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：大规模的；巨大的；大量的
- **英文释义**：very large, heavy, or great in amount
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake caused massive destruction in the area.
  - 地震在该地区造成了大规模的破坏。
  - A massive crowd gathered at the concert.
  - 一大群人聚集在音乐会上。
- **助记**：mass（大量）+ ive → 大量的 → 大规模的

### 7. **mutual** /ˈmjuːtʃuəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：共同的；相互的
- **英文释义**：felt or done by two or more people equally
- **例句**：
  - Friendship is based on mutual trust and understanding.
  - 友谊建立在相互信任和理解的基础上。
  - They reached a mutual agreement after the discussion.
  - 经过讨论他们达成了共同协议。
- **助记**：mutu（交换）+ al → 互相交换的 → 相互的；副词：mutually（相互地）

### 8. **formerly** /ˈfɔːməli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：从前；以前
- **英文释义**：in the past; previously
- **例句**：
  - The building was formerly a hospital, but now it's a museum.
  - 这栋楼以前是一家医院，但现在是博物馆。
  - She was formerly a teacher before becoming a writer.
  - 她在成为作家之前曾是一名教师。
- **助记**：former（从前的）+ ly → 从前地；形容词：former（从前的）

### 9. **deposit** /dɪˈpɒzɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：存放；存款；押金；沉淀物
- **英文释义**：to put money into a bank; a sum of money paid as a first part of a larger payment
- **例句**：
  - She deposited her savings into the bank every month.
  - 她每个月把存款存入银行。
  - The landlord asked for a deposit of two months' rent.
  - 房东要求交两个月租金的押金。
- **助记**：de（下）+ posit（放置）→ 放下来 → 存放/存款

### 10. **initiate** /ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：发起；开始；启动
- **英文释义**：to cause something to begin; to start a process
- **例句**：
  - The government initiated a program to help poor families.
  - 政府发起了一个帮助贫困家庭的项目。
  - Who initiated the idea of having a class reunion?
  - 是谁发起了举办班级聚会的想法？
- **助记**：init（开始）+ iate → 使开始 → 发起；名词：initiative（主动性；倡议）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **demographic** /ˌdeməˈɡræfɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：人口统计的；人口学的
- **英文释义**：relating to the structure of populations
- **例句**：
  - China is facing significant demographic changes due to an aging population.
  - 中国正因人口老龄化面临重大人口结构变化。
  - The company analyzed demographic data before launching its new product.
  - 公司在推出新产品前分析了人口统计数据。
- **助记**：demo（人民）+ graphic（描述的）→ 描述人民的 → 人口统计的；名词：demographics（人口统计数据）

### 12. **benchmark** /ˈbentʃmɑːk/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：基准；标准；参照点
- **英文释义**：a standard or point of reference against which things may be compared
- **例句**：
  - The test serves as a benchmark for measuring student progress.
  - 这项测试作为衡量学生进步的基准。
  - Singapore's education system is often used as a benchmark for other countries.
  - 新加坡的教育体系常被用作其他国家的参照标准。
- **助记**：bench（长凳/工作台）+ mark（标记）→ 工作台上的标记 → 基准

### 13. **resonate** /ˈrezəneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：引起共鸣；产生共振；回响
- **英文释义**：to produce a strong feeling of agreement or sympathy; to echo
- **例句**：
  - The speaker's message resonated deeply with the audience.
  - 演讲者的信息在观众中引起了深深的共鸣。
  - The song resonated with millions of people around the world.
  - 这首歌引起了全球数百万人的共鸣。
- **助记**：re（回）+ son（声音）+ ate → 声音回来 → 回响/共鸣；名词：resonance（共鸣）

### 14. **constitute** /ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：构成；组成；设立
- **英文释义**：to form or make up something; to be considered as
- **例句**：
  - Women constitute nearly 60% of the university's student body.
  - 女性占该大学学生总数的近60%。
  - Such behavior constitutes a violation of school rules.
  - 这种行为构成了对校规的违反。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ stitute（建立）→ 共同建立起来 → 构成；名词：constitution（宪法；构成）

### 15. **pervasive** /pəˈveɪsɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：普遍的；渗透的；无处不在的
- **英文释义**：spreading widely throughout an area or group of people
- **例句**：
  - Social media has a pervasive influence on young people's lives.
  - 社交媒体对年轻人的生活有着普遍的影响。
  - The smell of fresh bread was pervasive throughout the bakery.
  - 新鲜面包的气味弥漫了整个面包店。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ vas（走）+ ive → 完全走遍的 → 无处不在的；动词：pervade（弥漫）
