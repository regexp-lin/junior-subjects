## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **revoke** /rɪˈvoʊk/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：撤销；取消；废除
- **英文释义**：to officially cancel or withdraw something, especially a law, license, or decision
- **例句**：
  - The government decided to revoke his driver's license after the accident.
  - 政府决定在事故发生后撤销他的驾照。
  - The company revoked the offer when they discovered the applicant had lied.
  - 公司发现申请人撒谎后撤销了录用通知。
- **助记**：re（回）+ voke（呼唤）→ 叫回去 → 撤销；名词：revocation（撤销）

### 2. **placate** /ˈpleɪkeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：安抚；平息；使息怒
- **英文释义**：to make someone less angry or hostile by being pleasant or apologetic
- **例句**：
  - The manager tried to placate the angry customer by offering a full refund.
  - 经理试图通过全额退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。
  - She placated her little brother with a piece of candy.
  - 她用一块糖果安抚了弟弟。
- **助记**：plac（使平静）+ ate → 使平静 → 安抚；形容词：placatory（安抚的）

### 3. **denigrate** /ˈdenɪɡreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：诋毁；贬低；污蔑
- **英文释义**：to criticize someone or something unfairly; to belittle
- **例句**：
  - It's wrong to denigrate someone's achievements just because you're jealous.
  - 仅仅因为嫉妒就诋毁别人的成就是不对的。
  - The article denigrated the efforts of volunteers in the community.
  - 这篇文章贬低了社区志愿者的努力。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ nigr（黑）+ ate → 使变黑 → 诋毁；名词：denigration（诋毁）

### 4. **impede** /ɪmˈpiːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to delay or prevent someone or something from making progress
- **例句**：
  - Heavy traffic impeded our progress to the airport.
  - 交通拥堵阻碍了我们前往机场的行程。
  - Lack of funding impeded the completion of the project.
  - 资金不足阻碍了项目的完成。
- **助记**：im（进入）+ ped（脚）→ 把脚缠住 → 阻碍；名词：impediment（障碍）

### 5. **acumen** /əˈkjuːmən/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：敏锐；精明；洞察力
- **英文释义**：the ability to make good judgments and quick decisions, especially in business
- **例句**：
  - She has great business acumen and has built a successful company.
  - 她很有商业头脑，创办了一家成功的公司。
  - His political acumen helped him win the election.
  - 他的政治敏锐度帮助他赢得了选举。
- **助记**：acu（尖锐）+ men → 头脑尖锐 → 敏锐；形容词：acuminous（敏锐的）

### 6. **manifesto** /ˌmænɪˈfestoʊ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：宣言；声明；纲领
- **英文释义**：a public declaration of policies, aims, or intentions, especially by a political party
- **例句**：
  - The party published its manifesto before the election.
  - 该党在选举前发表了宣言。
  - The artist's manifesto explained his new approach to painting.
  - 这位艺术家的宣言解释了他对绘画的新方法。
- **助记**：manifest（明显的）+ o → 使意图明显 → 宣言

### 7. **extraneous** /ɪkˈstreɪniəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无关的；外来的；多余的
- **英文释义**：not directly connected with or relevant to the subject being considered
- **例句**：
  - Please remove all extraneous information from your essay.
  - 请从你的文章中删除所有无关信息。
  - The judge asked the lawyer to avoid extraneous comments.
  - 法官要求律师避免发表无关的评论。
- **助记**：extra（外面）+ neous → 来自外面的 → 无关的

### 8. **idyllic** /aɪˈdɪlɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：田园诗般的；宁静美好的；理想的
- **英文释义**：extremely pleasant, simple, and peaceful, like a perfect countryside scene
- **例句**：
  - They spent an idyllic summer in the countryside.
  - 他们在乡村度过了一个田园诗般的夏天。
  - The small village offers an idyllic escape from city life.
  - 这个小村庄提供了一个逃离城市生活的理想去处。
- **助记**：idyl（田园诗）+ ic → 像田园诗一样的 → 田园诗般的；名词：idyll（田园诗）

### 9. **tersely** /ˈtɜːrsli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：简洁地；简短地；精炼地
- **英文释义**：in a brief and to-the-point manner; using few words
- **例句**：
  - She answered the question tersely and sat down.
  - 她简洁地回答了问题然后坐下。
  - The teacher tersely explained that late homework would not be accepted.
  - 老师简短地解释说迟交的作业不会被接受。
- **助记**：terse（简洁的）+ ly → 简洁地；形容词：terse（简洁的）

### 10. **judiciously** /dʒuːˈdɪʃəsli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：明智地；审慎地；有判断力地
- **英文释义**：in a way that shows good judgment; wisely and carefully
- **例句**：
  - Use your time judiciously during the exam.
  - 考试时要明智地利用时间。
  - The manager judiciously allocated resources to the most important projects.
  - 经理审慎地将资源分配给最重要的项目。
- **助记**：judicious（明智的）+ ly → 明智地；形容词：judicious（明智的）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **perfidious** /pərˈfɪdiəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：背信弃义的；不忠的；背叛的
- **英文释义**：deceitful and untrustworthy; betraying trust
- **例句**：
  - He was condemned for his perfidious betrayal of his country.
  - 他因背信弃义地背叛祖国而受到谴责。
  - The perfidious friend spread rumors behind her back.
  - 那个不忠的朋友在她背后散布谣言。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ fid（信任）+ ious → 完全违背信任 → 背信弃义的；名词：perfidy（背信弃义）

### 12. **ineffable** /ɪnˈefəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：难以言喻的；不可名状的；说不出的
- **英文释义**：too great or extreme to be expressed in words
- **例句**：
  - The beauty of the sunset was ineffable.
  - 日落的美丽难以言喻。
  - She felt an ineffable joy when she heard the good news.
  - 听到好消息时，她感到一种说不出的喜悦。
- **助记**：in（不）+ effable（可说的）→ 不可说的 → 难以言喻的

### 13. **lugubrious** /luːˈɡjuːbriəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：悲伤的；阴郁的；哀伤的
- **英文释义**：looking or sounding sad and dismal; mournful
- **例句**：
  - The lugubrious music matched the mood of the funeral.
  - 哀伤的音乐与葬礼的气氛相配。
  - He had a lugubrious expression on his face after failing the exam.
  - 考试失败后，他脸上露出悲伤的表情。
- **助记**：lugubr（哀悼）+ ious → 哀悼的 → 悲伤的

### 14. **vituperate** /vaɪˈtjuːpəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：责骂；辱骂；痛斥
- **英文释义**：to criticize or blame someone in an angry and abusive way
- **例句**：
  - The coach vituperated the players for their poor performance.
  - 教练痛斥了球员们的糟糕表现。
  - She vituperated against the unfair treatment she had received.
  - 她痛斥了自己所受到的不公平待遇。
- **助记**：vitu（恶）+ perate（准备）→ 准备恶言 → 责骂；名词：vituperation（责骂）

### 15. **soporific** /ˌsɒpəˈrɪfɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：令人昏昏欲睡的；催眠的；使人困倦的
- **英文释义**：causing or tending to cause sleep; drowsy
- **例句**：
  - The soporific effect of the medicine helped him fall asleep.
  - 药物的催眠效果帮助他入睡。
  - The teacher's soporific voice made it hard to stay awake in class.
  - 老师令人昏昏欲睡的声音让人很难在课堂上保持清醒。
- **助记**：sopor（睡眠）+ ific（产生…的）→ 产生睡眠的 → 催眠的
