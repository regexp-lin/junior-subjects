## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **absent** /ˈæbsənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：缺席的；不在的
- **英文释义**：not present at a place; not existing
- **例句**：
  - Three students were absent from class today because of illness.
  - 今天有三个学生因病缺课。
  - He had an absent look on his face during the meeting.
  - 会议期间他脸上有一种心不在焉的表情。
- **助记**：ab（离开）+ sent（在场）→ 不在场 → 缺席的；名词：absence；反义词：present

### 2. **brilliant** /ˈbrɪliənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：出色的；杰出的；明亮的
- **英文释义**：extremely good or clever; very bright
- **例句**：
  - She is a brilliant student who always gets top grades.
  - 她是一个出色的学生，总是名列前茅。
  - The fireworks created a brilliant display of light in the night sky.
  - 烟花在夜空中创造了一场明亮的灯光展示。
- **助记**：brill（闪耀）+ iant → 闪闪发光的 → 出色的

### 3. **cancel** /ˈkænsl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：取消；撤销
- **英文释义**：to decide that something planned will not happen
- **例句**：
  - The flight was cancelled because of the heavy fog.
  - 航班因大雾被取消了。
  - She had to cancel her appointment with the dentist.
  - 她不得不取消和牙医的预约。
- **助记**：cancel 可联想"刊塞"→ 把安排塞掉 → 取消；名词：cancellation

### 4. **crash** /kræʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 碰撞；坠毁；崩溃；v. 撞击；坠毁
- **英文释义**：n. a violent accident; v. to hit something hard and break
- **例句**：
  - The car crash happened at the crossroads yesterday afternoon.
  - 昨天下午在十字路口发生了一起车祸。
  - My computer crashed while I was writing my essay.
  - 我写作文时电脑崩溃了。
- **助记**：crash 拟声词 → 碰撞的声音 → 碰撞、坠毁

### 5. **deny** /dɪˈnaɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：否认；拒绝给予
- **英文释义**：to say that something is not true; to refuse to allow something
- **例句**：
  - He denied stealing the money from his classmate.
  - 他否认偷了同学的钱。
  - No one should be denied the right to education.
  - 不应剥夺任何人受教育的权利。
- **助记**：de（不）+ ny（承认，来自 acknowledge）→ 不承认 → 否认；名词：denial；注意：deny doing sth.（否认做过某事）

### 6. **equipment** /ɪˈkwɪpmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：设备；器材（不可数）
- **英文释义**：the things that are needed for a particular activity
- **例句**：
  - The school bought new sports equipment for the gym.
  - 学校为体育馆购买了新的运动器材。
  - Make sure you have all the necessary equipment before the experiment.
  - 实验前确保你有所有必要的设备。
- **助记**：equip（装备）+ ment → 装备的东西 → 设备；注意：不可数名词，不能用 a/an 或复数

### 7. **fashion** /ˈfæʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：时尚；时装；方式
- **英文释义**：a popular style of clothes or behavior at a particular time
- **例句**：
  - Young people are always interested in the latest fashion.
  - 年轻人总是对最新时尚感兴趣。
  - She dressed in a fashionable way for the school dance.
  - 她为学校舞会穿了时髦的衣服。
- **助记**：fash（做/塑造）+ ion → 当下流行的做法 → 时尚；形容词：fashionable；固定搭配：in fashion（流行的）/ out of fashion（过时的）

### 8. **grateful** /ˈɡreɪtfl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：感激的；感谢的
- **英文释义**：feeling or showing thanks to someone
- **例句**：
  - I'm very grateful to you for helping me with my homework.
  - 我非常感谢你帮我做作业。
  - She wrote a letter to express how grateful she was.
  - 她写了一封信来表达她是多么感激。
- **助记**：grate（感恩）+ ful（充满）→ 充满感恩 → 感激的；固定搭配：be grateful to sb. for sth.

### 9. **harvest** /ˈhɑːvɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 收获；收成；v. 收割
- **英文释义**：n. the time of year when crops are gathered; v. to gather crops
- **例句**：
  - Farmers are busy during the harvest season in autumn.
  - 农民在秋天的收获季节很忙。
  - They harvested a large amount of rice this year.
  - 他们今年收割了大量的水稻。
- **助记**：harvest 与 autumn（秋天）联系 → 秋天的收获

### 10. **judge** /dʒʌdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 法官；裁判；v. 判断；评价
- **英文释义**：n. a person who decides a legal case or a competition; v. to form an opinion about
- **例句**：
  - Don't judge a book by its cover.
  - 不要以貌取人（不要根据封面判断一本书）。
  - She was invited to be a judge at the singing competition.
  - 她被邀请担任歌唱比赛的评委。
- **助记**：judge 与 justice（正义）同源 → 做出公正判断；名词：judgment/judgement

### 高一词汇

### 11. **accumulate** /əˈkjuːmjəleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：积累；积聚
- **英文释义**：to collect or gather something over time
- **例句**：
  - Knowledge is accumulated through years of study and practice.
  - 知识是通过多年学习和实践积累的。
  - Dust has accumulated on the bookshelf over the past few months.
  - 过去几个月书架上积了灰尘。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ cumul（堆积）+ ate → 不断堆积 → 积累；名词：accumulation

### 12. **boundary** /ˈbaʊndri/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：边界；界限；分界线
- **英文释义**：a line that marks the limits of an area; a limit
- **例句**：
  - The river forms the boundary between the two provinces.
  - 这条河形成了两省之间的边界。
  - There are no boundaries to what you can achieve with hard work.
  - 通过努力，你的成就没有界限。
- **助记**：bound（界限）+ ary → 界限所在 → 边界

### 13. **correspond** /ˌkɒrɪˈspɒnd/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：相符合；通信；对应
- **英文释义**：to match or be similar to something; to exchange letters
- **例句**：
  - The results of the experiment correspond with our prediction.
  - 实验结果与我们的预测相符。
  - They corresponded with each other by email for over a year.
  - 他们通过电子邮件通信了一年多。
- **助记**：cor（共同）+ respond（回应）→ 相互回应 → 相符合/通信；名词：correspondence

### 14. **eliminate** /ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：消除；淘汰；排除
- **英文释义**：to remove or get rid of something completely
- **例句**：
  - We need to eliminate all errors before submitting the report.
  - 我们需要在提交报告前消除所有错误。
  - Three teams were eliminated in the first round of the competition.
  - 三支队伍在第一轮比赛中被淘汰了。
- **助记**：e（出）+ limin（门槛）+ ate → 踢出门槛外 → 淘汰；名词：elimination

### 15. **investigate** /ɪnˈvestɪɡeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：调查；研究
- **英文释义**：to try to find out the truth about something by examining facts
- **例句**：
  - The police are investigating the cause of the fire.
  - 警方正在调查火灾的原因。
  - Scientists are investigating new ways to treat cancer.
  - 科学家们正在研究治疗癌症的新方法。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ vestig（追踪）+ ate → 深入追踪 → 调查；名词：investigation
