## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **mist** /mɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：薄雾；雾霭
- **英文释义**：a cloud of tiny drops of water in the air, less thick than fog
- **例句**：
  - The valley was covered in a light mist this morning.
  - 今早山谷笼罩在薄雾中。
  - The mist lifted as the sun rose.
  - 太阳升起时薄雾散去。
- **助记**：mist → " 迷死他 "（谐音）→ 迷雾让人迷惑 → 薄雾

### 2. **mobile** /ˈməʊbaɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：可移动的；流动的
- **英文释义**：able to move or be moved easily
- **例句**：
  - She has a mobile phone in her pocket.
  - 她口袋里有一部手机。
  - The library has a mobile unit that visits rural schools.
  - 图书馆有流动车走访乡村学校。
- **助记**：mob（移动）+ -ile（能……的）→ 能移动的 → 可移动的

### 3. **mode** /məʊd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：方式；模式
- **英文释义**：a way of operating or behaving; a particular style
- **例句**：
  - Switch your phone to silent mode during class.
  - 上课时把手机调成静音模式。
  - She's in study mode — don't disturb her.
  - 她在学习状态，别打扰她。
- **助记**：mode → " 模的 "（谐音）→ 模式、方式

### 4. **moisture** /ˈmɔɪstʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：水分；潮湿
- **英文释义**：small amounts of water that are present in the air, in a substance, or on a surface
- **例句**：
  - Plants need moisture to grow.
  - 植物需要水分才能生长。
  - The moisture in the air made it feel humid.
  - 空气中的湿气让人感到潮湿。
- **助记**：moist（潮湿的）+ -ure → 潮湿的状态 → 水分

### 5. **molecule** /ˈmɒlɪkjuːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：分子
- **英文释义**：the smallest unit of a substance that can exist independently
- **例句**：
  - A water molecule consists of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom.
  - 水分子由两个氢原子和一个氧原子组成。
  - We learned about molecules in chemistry class.
  - 我们在化学课上学了分子。
- **助记**：mole（摩尔/小单位）+ -cule（小）→ 物质的小单位 → 分子

### 6. **monitor** /ˈmɒnɪtə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：监控；监督
- **英文释义**：to watch and check something over a period of time
- **例句**：
  - The teacher monitored the students during the test.
  - 考试时老师监督学生。
  - We need to monitor your progress closely.
  - 我们需要密切监控你的进步。
- **助记**：monit（提醒）+ -or → 提醒者 → 监控

### 7. **monopoly** /məˈnɒpəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：垄断；专卖权
- **英文释义**：exclusive control of a commodity or service
- **例句**：
  - The company has a monopoly on the market.
  - 这家公司垄断了市场。
  - No single player should have a monopoly on the game.
  - 不应该让一个玩家垄断游戏。
- **助记**：mono-（单一）+ poly（卖）→ 独家销售 → 垄断

### 8. **morale** /məˈrɑːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：士气；斗志
- **英文释义**：the level of confidence and satisfaction of a person or group
- **例句**：
  - The team's morale was high after the victory.
  - 获胜后队伍士气高涨。
  - Poor grades can affect a student's morale.
  - 成绩不好会影响学生的士气。
- **助记**：morale → "  morale "（与 moral 相关）→ 道德、精神 → 士气

### 9. **mourn** /mɔːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：哀悼；悲悼
- **英文释义**：to feel or show sadness for the death of someone
- **例句**：
  - The whole nation mourned the loss of the leader.
  - 举国哀悼这位领袖的逝世。
  - She is still mourning her grandmother.
  - 她仍在哀悼去世的祖母。
- **助记**：mourn → " 默哀 "（谐音）→ 默哀悼念 → 哀悼

### 10. **motion** /ˈməʊʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：运动；动作
- **英文释义**：the process of moving or the way something moves
- **例句**：
  - The motion of the boat made her feel seasick.
  - 船的晃动让她晕船。
  - He made a motion to open the door.
  - 他做了个开门的动作。
- **助记**：mot（移动）+ -ion → 移动的状态 → 运动

### 高一词汇

### 11. **narrative** /ˈnærətɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：叙述；故事
- **英文释义**：a story or description of events
- **例句**：
  - The novel has a compelling narrative.
  - 这部小说叙事引人入胜。
  - She gave a clear narrative of what happened.
  - 她清楚讲述了事情的经过。
- **助记**：narrat（讲述）+ -ive → 讲述的内容 → 叙述

### 12. **navigate** /ˈnævɪɡeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：导航；航行
- **英文释义**：to find your way; to direct the course of a ship or vehicle
- **例句**：
  - We used a map to navigate through the city.
  - 我们用地图在城市里找路。
  - The app helps you navigate to the nearest station.
  - 这个应用帮你导航到最近的车站。
- **助记**：nav（船）+ ig（驱动）+ -ate → 驾船 → 导航

### 13. **negligible** /ˈneɡlɪdʒəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：可忽略不计的；微不足道的
- **英文释义**：so small or unimportant as to be not worth considering
- **例句**：
  - The difference in price is negligible.
  - 价格差异可以忽略不计。
  - The impact on the environment was negligible.
  - 对环境的影响微乎其微。
- **助记**：neglig（忽视）+ -ible（可……的）→ 可以忽视的 → 微不足道的

### 14. **nominal** /ˈnɒmɪnl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：名义上的；极少的
- **英文释义**：in name only; (of a sum of money) very small
- **例句**：
  - He is the nominal head of the department.
  - 他是部门名义上的负责人。
  - A nominal fee is charged for the service.
  - 这项服务只收取象征性费用。
- **助记**：nomin（名字）+ -al → 只在名分上的 → 名义上的

### 15. **nourish** /ˈnʌrɪʃ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：滋养；养育
- **英文释义**：to provide with food or other substances necessary for growth
- **例句**：
  - A balanced diet nourishes the body.
  - 均衡饮食滋养身体。
  - Parents nourish their children with love and care.
  - 父母用爱和关怀养育子女。
- **助记**：nour（营养）+ -ish → 提供营养 → 滋养
