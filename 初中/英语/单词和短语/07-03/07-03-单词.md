## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **snarl** /snɑːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：咆哮；吼叫
- **英文释义**：to speak angrily or make an angry sound; (of an animal) to growl with bared teeth
- **例句**：
  - The dog snarled at the stranger who approached the gate.
  - 狗对靠近大门的陌生人咆哮。
  - "Get out of my room!" she snarled at her brother.
  - "滚出我的房间！"她对弟弟吼道。
- **助记**：snarl → "死闹"（谐音）→ 死命地闹叫 → 咆哮；吼叫

### 2. **sprinkle** /ˈsprɪŋkl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：撒；洒
- **英文释义**：to scatter small drops of liquid or small pieces of something over a surface
- **例句**：
  - She sprinkled some sugar on top of the cake.
  - 她在蛋糕上面撒了些糖。
  - The gardener sprinkled water over the flowers every morning.
  - 园丁每天早晨给花洒水。
- **助记**：spring（泉水）+ -kle → 像泉水一样洒出来 → 撒；洒

### 3. **confine** /kənˈfaɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：限制；禁闭
- **英文释义**：to keep someone or something within limits; to keep someone in a closed space
- **例句**：
  - Please confine your answers to the topic of the discussion.
  - 请把你的回答限制在讨论的主题范围内。
  - The prisoners were confined to their cells for 20 hours a day.
  - 囚犯每天被关在牢房里20小时。
- **助记**：con-（共同）+ fine（界限）→ 在界限之内 → 限制；禁闭

### 4. **pounce** /paʊns/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：猛扑；突然袭击
- **英文释义**：to jump or spring suddenly in order to attack or seize something
- **例句**：
  - The cat pounced on the mouse hiding under the table.
  - 猫猛扑向藏在桌子下面的老鼠。
  - The goalkeeper pounced on the ball before anyone else could reach it.
  - 守门员抢先扑向了球。
- **助记**：pounce → "扑恩死"（谐音）→ 猛扑过去 → 猛扑；突然袭击

### 5. **toll** /təʊl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：通行费；代价；伤亡人数
- **英文释义**：a charge for using a road or bridge; the damage or suffering caused by something
- **例句**：
  - You need to pay a toll to use this highway.
  - 使用这条高速公路需要缴纳通行费。
  - The earthquake took a heavy toll on the local community.
  - 地震给当地社区造成了巨大的损失。
- **助记**：toll → "投了"（谐音）→ 投了钱才能过桥 → 通行费；代价

### 6. **crater** /ˈkreɪtə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：火山口；弹坑
- **英文释义**：a large hole in the ground made by an explosion or by the impact of a meteorite; the mouth of a volcano
- **例句**：
  - We hiked to the edge of the volcanic crater.
  - 我们徒步走到了火山口的边缘。
  - The bomb left a huge crater in the middle of the road.
  - 炸弹在路中间留下了一个巨大的弹坑。
- **助记**：crater → "坑特"（谐音）→ 特别大的坑 → 火山口；弹坑

### 7. **ivy** /ˈaɪvi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：常春藤
- **英文释义**：an evergreen climbing plant that covers walls, trees, etc.
- **例句**：
  - The old building was covered with ivy.
  - 那栋老建筑被常春藤覆盖着。
  - Ivy League universities are among the best in the world.
  - 常春藤联盟的大学是世界顶尖学府。
- **助记**：ivy → "爱围"（谐音）→ 常春藤爱围着墙壁生长 → 常春藤

### 8. **drowsy** /ˈdraʊzi/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：昏昏欲睡的；困倦的
- **英文释义**：feeling sleepy and unable to think clearly
- **例句**：
  - The warm afternoon sun made me feel drowsy.
  - 温暖的午后阳光让我昏昏欲睡。
  - This medicine may make you drowsy, so don't drive after taking it.
  - 这种药可能让你犯困，服药后不要开车。
- **助记**：drowsy → "拽我睡"（谐音）→ 困得拽着我去睡 → 昏昏欲睡的

### 9. **mellow** /ˈmeləʊ/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：柔和的；成熟的
- **英文释义**：soft, warm, and pleasant; relaxed and calm because of age or experience
- **例句**：
  - The mellow light of the sunset filled the room.
  - 柔和的夕阳光充满了房间。
  - He has become more mellow with age.
  - 随着年龄增长，他变得更加温和了。
- **助记**：mellow → "美了"（谐音）→ 变得柔美了 → 柔和的；成熟的

### 10. **inland** /ˈɪnlənd/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：在内陆；向内地
- **英文释义**：in or towards the middle of a country, away from the coast
- **例句**：
  - The village is located 50 kilometers inland from the coast.
  - 这个村庄位于海岸内陆50公里处。
  - They traveled inland to explore the mountains.
  - 他们向内陆出发去探索山区。
- **助记**：in（里面）+ land（陆地）→ 陆地里面 → 在内陆

### 高一词汇

### 11. **remonstrate** /ˈremənstreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：抗议；规劝
- **英文释义**：to protest or argue firmly against something
- **例句**：
  - The parents remonstrated with the school about the new uniform policy.
  - 家长们就新校服政策向学校提出抗议。
  - She remonstrated against the unfair decision made by the judge.
  - 她对法官做出的不公正决定提出抗议。
- **助记**：re-（反）+ monstr（展示）+ -ate → 展示反对意见 → 抗议；规劝

### 12. **commiserate** /kəˈmɪzəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：表示同情；怜悯
- **英文释义**：to express sympathy or pity for someone
- **例句**：
  - Friends commiserated with her after she failed the driving test.
  - 她驾照考试没过后，朋友们纷纷表示同情。
  - We commiserated over our bad luck in the exam.
  - 我们互相安慰考试运气不好的事。
- **助记**：com-（共同）+ miser（可怜）+ -ate → 一起感到可怜 → 表示同情

### 13. **supercilious** /ˌsuːpəˈsɪliəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：傲慢的；目中无人的
- **英文释义**：behaving as if you are better than other people; showing arrogant superiority
- **例句**：
  - His supercilious attitude annoyed everyone in the class.
  - 他傲慢的态度惹恼了班上所有人。
  - She gave a supercilious smile and walked away without answering.
  - 她傲慢地笑了笑，没回答就走开了。
- **助记**：super（超越）+ cili（眉毛）+ -ous → 扬起眉毛看人 → 傲慢的

### 14. **indomitable** /ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不屈不挠的；坚不可摧的
- **英文释义**：impossible to defeat or discourage
- **例句**：
  - Her indomitable spirit helped her overcome all the difficulties.
  - 她不屈不挠的精神帮助她克服了所有困难。
  - The team showed indomitable courage in the final match.
  - 球队在决赛中展现出了不屈不挠的勇气。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ domit（驯服）+ -able → 不可驯服的 → 不屈不挠的

### 15. **archetype** /ˈɑːkɪtaɪp/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：原型；典范
- **英文释义**：the most typical or perfect example of something; an original model
- **例句**：
  - Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet is the archetype of a tragic love story.
  - 莎士比亚的《罗密欧与朱丽叶》是悲剧爱情故事的原型。
  - She is the archetype of a successful entrepreneur.
  - 她是成功企业家的典范。
- **助记**：arche-（最初的）+ type（类型）→ 最初的类型 → 原型；典范
