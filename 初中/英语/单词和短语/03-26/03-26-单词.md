## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **confirm** /kənˈfɜːm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：确认；证实
- **英文释义**：to show that something is definitely true; to say that something will happen as planned
- **例句**：
  - Can you confirm the time of tomorrow's meeting?
  - 你能确认明天会议的时间吗？
  - The test results confirmed that she was completely healthy.
  - 检测结果证实她完全健康。
- **助记**：con（加强）+ firm（坚定）→ 使更加坚定 → 确认；名词：confirmation（确认）

### 2. **survive** /səˈvaɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：幸存；存活；生还
- **英文释义**：to continue to live after a dangerous event; to manage to continue
- **例句**：
  - Only three passengers survived the plane crash.
  - 只有三名乘客在飞机失事中幸存。
  - Many small businesses struggle to survive in difficult economic times.
  - 许多小企业在经济困难时期艰难生存。
- **助记**：sur（超过）+ vive（活）→ 活过（灾难）→ 幸存；名词：survival（生存）; survivor（幸存者）

### 3. **celebrate** /ˈselɪbreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：庆祝；庆贺
- **英文释义**：to do something enjoyable because of a special occasion or achievement
- **例句**：
  - We celebrated my grandmother's 80th birthday last weekend.
  - 上周末我们庆祝了奶奶的八十岁生日。
  - The whole city celebrated when the team won the championship.
  - 当球队赢得冠军时，全城都在庆祝。
- **助记**：celeb（名人/著名）+ rate → 像名人一样被大家关注庆祝 → 庆祝；名词：celebration（庆祝活动）

### 4. **attempt** /əˈtempt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：尝试；企图
- **英文释义**：to try to do something, especially something difficult
- **例句**：
  - He attempted to climb the mountain but failed due to bad weather.
  - 他尝试攀登那座山，但因天气恶劣而失败。
  - After several attempts, she finally solved the difficult math problem.
  - 经过几次尝试，她终于解出了那道难题。
- **助记**：at（朝向）+ tempt（尝试/诱惑）→ 朝着目标去尝试 → 尝试；固定搭配：attempt to do sth. / make an attempt

### 5. **solution** /səˈluːʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：解决办法；解答；溶液
- **英文释义**：a way of solving a problem; the answer to a puzzle
- **例句**：
  - We need to find a solution to the traffic problem in our city.
  - 我们需要找到解决我们城市交通问题的办法。
  - The solution to this math equation is x = 5.
  - 这个数学方程的解是 x = 5。
- **助记**：solu（解开）+ tion → 解开问题 → 解决方案；动词：solve（解决）

### 6. **patience** /ˈpeɪʃns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：耐心；忍耐力
- **英文释义**：the ability to wait calmly or to deal with difficulties without getting angry
- **例句**：
  - Learning a new language requires a lot of patience.
  - 学习一门新语言需要很大的耐心。
  - The teacher showed great patience with the slow learners.
  - 老师对学习慢的学生表现出极大的耐心。
- **助记**：pati（忍受）+ ence → 忍受的能力 → 耐心；形容词：patient（有耐心的；病人）

### 7. **sensitive** /ˈsensətɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：敏感的；灵敏的；体贴的
- **英文释义**：easily affected by something; aware of other people's feelings
- **例句**：
  - She is very sensitive to criticism and gets upset easily.
  - 她对批评非常敏感，容易感到不安。
  - The camera is sensitive enough to take clear photos at night.
  - 这台相机灵敏度足够高，能在夜间拍出清晰的照片。
- **助记**：sens（感觉）+ itive → 感觉灵敏的 → 敏感的；名词：sensitivity（敏感性）

### 8. **fierce** /fɪəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：猛烈的；激烈的；凶猛的
- **英文释义**：very strong or violent; showing strong feelings or a lot of activity
- **例句**：
  - There was fierce competition among the students for the scholarship.
  - 学生们之间为了奖学金展开了激烈的竞争。
  - The fierce storm destroyed many houses along the coast.
  - 猛烈的暴风雨摧毁了沿海的许多房屋。
- **助记**：fierce 发音像"飞尔斯"→ 像飞箭一样凶猛 → 猛烈的；副词：fiercely（猛烈地）

### 9. **meanwhile** /ˈmiːnwaɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：同时；与此同时
- **英文释义**：during the same period of time; at the same time
- **例句**：
  - I'll prepare dinner. Meanwhile, you can set the table.
  - 我来准备晚饭。与此同时，你可以摆餐具。
  - The project is going well. Meanwhile, we need to plan for the next phase.
  - 项目进展顺利。同时，我们需要规划下一阶段。
- **助记**：mean（中间）+ while（一段时间）→ 在此期间 → 同时

### 10. **establish** /ɪˈstæblɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：建立；设立；确立
- **英文释义**：to start or create something; to make something accepted
- **例句**：
  - The school was established in 1950 and has a long history.
  - 这所学校成立于1950年，有着悠久的历史。
  - Scientists are trying to establish a link between diet and health.
  - 科学家们试图确立饮食与健康之间的联系。
- **助记**：e（向外）+ stab（稳定）+ lish → 使稳固地立于外 → 建立；名词：establishment（建立；机构）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **contradict** /ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：与…矛盾；反驳；否认
- **英文释义**：to say or show the opposite of something; to disagree with someone
- **例句**：
  - His actions contradict what he said earlier.
  - 他的行为与他之前说的相矛盾。
  - Don't contradict your teacher in front of the class.
  - 不要在全班面前反驳你的老师。
- **助记**：contra（相反）+ dict（说）→ 说相反的话 → 反驳；名词：contradiction（矛盾）

### 12. **arbitrary** /ˈɑːbɪtrəri/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：任意的；武断的；随机的
- **英文释义**：based on personal opinion rather than reason; random
- **例句**：
  - The decision seemed completely arbitrary and unfair.
  - 这个决定看起来完全是武断和不公平的。
  - The teacher made an arbitrary choice of students for the team.
  - 老师随意挑选了学生组成团队。
- **助记**：arbitr（裁判）+ ary → 像裁判一样独断的 → 武断的；副词：arbitrarily（任意地）

### 13. **supplement** /ˈsʌplɪment/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：补充（物）；增补；补充说明
- **英文释义**：something added to complete or improve something; to add something extra
- **例句**：
  - Many people take vitamin supplements to stay healthy.
  - 许多人服用维生素补充剂来保持健康。
  - She supplements her income by tutoring students after school.
  - 她通过课后辅导学生来补充收入。
- **助记**：sup（在下面）+ ple（满）+ ment → 在下面填满 → 补充；形容词：supplementary（补充的）

### 14. **spectrum** /ˈspektrəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：光谱；范围；系列
- **英文释义**：a range of different ideas, opinions, or types; the band of colors in light
- **例句**：
  - The issue affects a broad spectrum of society.
  - 这个问题影响了社会的广泛群体。
  - A rainbow shows the full spectrum of visible light.
  - 彩虹展现了可见光的完整光谱。
- **助记**：spect（看）+ rum → 看到的范围 → 光谱/范围；复数：spectra 或 spectrums

### 15. **notable** /ˈnəʊtəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：值得注意的；显著的；著名的
- **英文释义**：deserving attention because of being important or interesting
- **例句**：
  - There has been a notable improvement in his English grades.
  - 他的英语成绩有了显著的提高。
  - She is a notable scientist in the field of artificial intelligence.
  - 她是人工智能领域的著名科学家。
- **助记**：not（标记）+ able → 值得被标记的 → 值得注意的；副词：notably（显著地）
