## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **devote** /dɪˈvəʊt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：奉献；致力于
- **英文释义**：to give time, effort, or attention to something
- **例句**：
  - She devoted all her free time to studying English.
  - 她把所有空闲时间都奉献给了学英语。
  - He devoted himself to helping homeless animals.
  - 他致力于帮助流浪动物。
- **助记**：de-（加强）+ vote（发誓）→ 郑重发誓去做 → 奉献；致力于

### 2. **diagram** /ˈdaɪəɡræm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：图表；示意图
- **英文释义**：a simple drawing showing how something works or the relationship between parts
- **例句**：
  - The teacher drew a diagram to explain the water cycle.
  - 老师画了一个示意图来解释水循环。
  - Please label all the parts on the diagram.
  - 请在图表上标出所有部分。
- **助记**：dia-（穿过）+ gram（写、画）→ 用图画穿透知识 → 图表

### 3. **dialect** /ˈdaɪəlekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：方言；土话
- **英文释义**：a form of a language spoken in a particular area
- **例句**：
  - Cantonese is a dialect spoken in Guangdong province.
  - 粤语是广东省使用的一种方言。
  - It's hard to understand the local dialect when you first arrive.
  - 刚到的时候很难听懂当地方言。
- **助记**：dia-（之间）+ lect（说话）→ 某些人之间说的话 → 方言

### 4. **dignity** /ˈdɪɡnəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：尊严；庄重
- **英文释义**：the quality of being worthy of honour or respect
- **例句**：
  - Everyone should be treated with dignity and respect.
  - 每个人都应被有尊严地对待。
  - She accepted the defeat with dignity.
  - 她有尊严地接受了失败。
- **助记**：dign（值得）+ -ity（名词后缀）→ 值得尊重 → 尊严

### 5. **dilemma** /dɪˈlemə/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：困境；进退两难
- **英文释义**：a situation in which a difficult choice has to be made
- **例句**：
  - I faced a dilemma: study for the exam or attend my friend's birthday.
  - 我面临一个困境：复习考试还是参加朋友的生日会。
  - The government is in a dilemma over the new policy.
  - 政府在新政策上陷入了进退两难。
- **助记**：di-（两个）+ lemma（问题）→ 两个问题都难选 → 困境

### 6. **diploma** /dɪˈpləʊmə/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：文凭；毕业证书
- **英文释义**：a certificate awarded by a school or college
- **例句**：
  - She received her diploma at the graduation ceremony.
  - 她在毕业典礼上拿到了毕业证书。
  - A diploma in computer science can help you find a good job.
  - 计算机专业的文凭能帮你找到好工作。
- **助记**：diploma → "地铺了吗"（谐音）→ 文凭铺好了路 → 文凭

### 7. **disabled** /dɪsˈeɪbld/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：残疾的；有障碍的
- **英文释义**：having a physical or mental condition that limits movements, senses, or activities
- **例句**：
  - The school built a ramp for disabled students.
  - 学校为残疾学生修了坡道。
  - Disabled parking spaces should not be used by others.
  - 残疾人停车位不应被他人占用。
- **助记**：dis-（否定）+ able（能够）+ -d → 不能够的 → 残疾的

### 8. **discard** /dɪsˈkɑːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：丢弃；抛弃
- **英文释义**：to get rid of something that is no longer useful or wanted
- **例句**：
  - Please discard any expired food from the fridge.
  - 请把冰箱里过期的食物丢掉。
  - He discarded his old phone and bought a new one.
  - 他丢弃了旧手机，买了一部新的。
- **助记**：dis-（去掉）+ card（卡片）→ 把卡片扔掉 → 丢弃

### 9. **disclose** /dɪsˈkləʊz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：揭露；透露
- **英文释义**：to make something known publicly that was previously secret
- **例句**：
  - The journalist refused to disclose his source of information.
  - 记者拒绝透露消息来源。
  - The company disclosed its financial report last week.
  - 公司上周公布了财务报告。
- **助记**：dis-（打开）+ close（关闭）→ 把关着的打开 → 揭露

### 10. **disguise** /dɪsˈɡaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：伪装；假装
- **英文释义**：to change the appearance so as not to be recognised; a costume or way of hiding identity
- **例句**：
  - The detective wore a disguise to follow the suspect.
  - 侦探伪装后跟踪嫌疑人。
  - She disguised herself as an old woman to escape.
  - 她假扮成老太太逃走了。
- **助记**：dis-（去掉）+ guise（外表）→ 去掉真实外表 → 伪装

### 高一词汇

### 11. **devout** /dɪˈvaʊt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：虔诚的；忠实的
- **英文释义**：deeply religious or devoted to a belief or cause
- **例句**：
  - She is a devout Buddhist and meditates every morning.
  - 她是虔诚的佛教徒，每天早上打坐。
  - He is a devout supporter of environmental protection.
  - 他是环保事业的忠实拥护者。
- **助记**：devout → devote（奉献）的形容词形式 → 虔诚奉献的 → 虔诚的

### 12. **dexterity** /dekˈsterəti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：灵巧；熟练
- **英文释义**：skill in performing tasks, especially with the hands
- **例句**：
  - The pianist played with remarkable dexterity.
  - 钢琴家演奏得非常灵巧。
  - Surgeons need great manual dexterity.
  - 外科医生需要很高的手部灵巧度。
- **助记**：dexter（右手的、灵巧的）+ -ity → 右手般灵巧 → 灵巧

### 13. **dichotomy** /daɪˈkɒtəmi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：二分法；对立
- **英文释义**：a division into two completely different or opposed things
- **例句**：
  - There is a dichotomy between what he says and what he does.
  - 他说的和做的之间存在对立。
  - The dichotomy between science and art is often exaggerated.
  - 科学和艺术的二分法常常被夸大。
- **助记**：dicho-（两个）+ -tomy（切割）→ 切成两半 → 二分法

### 14. **didactic** /daɪˈdæktɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：教诲的；说教的
- **英文释义**：intended to teach or having the manner of a teacher
- **例句**：
  - The novel has a didactic tone that some readers find annoying.
  - 这部小说说教的语气让一些读者觉得烦。
  - His speech was more didactic than inspiring.
  - 他的演讲说教多于鼓舞。
- **助记**：didact（教导）+ -ic → 教导性质的 → 说教的

### 15. **diffident** /ˈdɪfɪdənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：缺乏自信的；羞怯的
- **英文释义**：modest or shy because of a lack of self-confidence
- **例句**：
  - She was too diffident to speak up in class.
  - 她太害羞了，不敢在课堂上发言。
  - His diffident manner made him seem unapproachable.
  - 他羞怯的举止让人觉得不好接近。
- **助记**：dif-（不）+ fid（信任）+ -ent → 不信任自己 → 缺乏自信的
