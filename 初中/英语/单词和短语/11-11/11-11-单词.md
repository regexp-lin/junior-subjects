## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **cheerful** /ˈtʃɪəfl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：快乐的；开朗的
- **英文释义**：happy and optimistic; in good spirits
- **例句**：
  - She has a cheerful personality.
  - 她性格开朗。
  - The room was painted in cheerful colors.
  - 房间漆成了明快的颜色。
- **助记**：cheer（欢呼）+ ful → 充满欢乐的 → 快乐的

### 2. **cheese** /tʃiːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：奶酪；干酪
- **英文释义**：a solid food made from milk
- **例句**：
  - I love cheese on my pizza.
  - 我喜欢披萨上的奶酪。
  - This cheese is from France.
  - 这奶酪产自法国。
- **助记**：cheese → cheeseburger（芝士汉堡）→ cheesecake（芝士蛋糕）

### 3. **chemical** /ˈkemɪkl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：化学的；化学物质
- **英文释义**：relating to chemistry; a substance produced by chemistry
- **例句**：
  - The chemical reaction produced heat.
  - 化学反应产生了热量。
  - These chemicals are dangerous.
  - 这些化学物质很危险。
- **助记**：chem（化学）+ ical → 化学的

### 4. **chemist** /ˈkemɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：化学家；药剂师
- **英文释义**：a scientist who studies chemistry; a person who dispenses medicines
- **例句**：
  - She works as a chemist in a lab.
  - 她在实验室当化学家。
  - I bought medicine at the chemist's.
  - 我在药店买了药。
- **助记**：chem（化学）+ ist（人）→ 化学家

### 5. **chemistry** /ˈkemɪstri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：化学；化学反应
- **英文释义**：the science of substances and their reactions; the attraction between people
- **例句**：
  - Chemistry is my favourite subject.
  - 化学是我最喜欢的科目。
  - There was instant chemistry between them.
  - 他们之间一见如故。
- **助记**：chem（化学）+ istry → 化学

### 6. **cheque** /tʃek/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：支票
- **英文释义**：a written order directing a bank to pay money
- **例句**：
  - He paid by cheque.
  - 他用支票付款。
  - Please make the cheque out to me.
  - 请把支票开给我。
- **助记**：cheque（英式）= check（美式，作支票义时）

### 7. **chess** /tʃes/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：国际象棋
- **英文释义**：a board game for two players
- **例句**：
  - Let's play chess.
  - 我们来下国际象棋吧。
  - He is the chess champion.
  - 他是国际象棋冠军。
- **助记**：chess → chessboard（棋盘）→ chess piece（棋子）

### 8. **chest** /tʃest/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：胸膛；胸腔；箱子；柜子
- **英文释义**：the front part of the body; a large box for storage
- **例句**：
  - He has chest pain.
  - 他胸痛。
  - She kept her clothes in a wooden chest.
  - 她把衣服放在木箱里。
- **助记**：chest of drawers（衣柜；五斗柜）是常见搭配

### 9. **chew** /tʃuː/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：咀嚼；嚼碎
- **英文释义**：to bite and grind with the teeth
- **例句**：
  - Chew your food slowly.
  - 慢慢咀嚼食物。
  - The dog chewed on the bone.
  - 狗在啃骨头。
- **助记**：chew the fat（闲聊）是常见俚语

### 10. **chicken** /ˈtʃɪkɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：鸡；鸡肉；胆小鬼
- **英文释义**：a domesticated bird; its meat; a coward
- **例句**：
  - We had chicken for dinner.
  - 我们晚餐吃了鸡肉。
  - Don't be a chicken!
  - 别当胆小鬼！
- **助记**：chicken out（临阵退缩）是常见搭配

### 高一词汇

### 11. **deride** /dɪˈraɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：嘲笑；讥笑
- **英文释义**：to express contempt for; to ridicule
- **例句**：
  - They derided his outdated ideas.
  - 他们嘲笑他过时的想法。
  - The proposal was derided by critics.
  - 这项提议受到批评者的讥讽。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ ride（笑）→ 用笑打压 → 嘲笑

### 12. **desiccate** /ˈdesɪkeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使干燥；脱水
- **英文释义**：to remove moisture from; to dry completely
- **例句**：
  - The sun desiccated the soil.
  - 阳光使土壤变得干燥。
  - Desiccated coconut is used in baking.
  - 椰丝用于烘焙。
- **助记**：de（去除）+ sicc（干）+ ate → 使变干 → 干燥

### 13. **desultory** /ˈdesəltri/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：散漫的；无条理的；零星的
- **英文释义**：lacking a plan; random and disconnected
- **例句**：
  - They had a desultory conversation.
  - 他们随意聊了聊。
  - He made desultory attempts to study.
  - 他零散地尝试学习。
- **助记**：de（偏离）+ sult（跳）+ ory → 跳来跳去 → 散漫的

### 14. **deterrent** /dɪˈterənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：威慑物；阻碍物
- **英文释义**：something that discourages or prevents action
- **例句**：
  - Nuclear weapons serve as a deterrent.
  - 核武器起到威慑作用。
  - Heavy fines are a deterrent to speeding.
  - 高额罚款能遏制超速。
- **助记**：deter（威慑）+ rent → 威慑物

### 15. **devout** /dɪˈvaʊt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：虔诚的；虔敬的
- **英文释义**：deeply religious; sincere and committed
- **例句**：
  - She is a devout believer.
  - 她是一位虔诚的信徒。
  - He gave devout thanks for his recovery.
  - 他为康复而虔诚感恩。
- **助记**：devout → devotion（虔诚）→ devote（奉献）
