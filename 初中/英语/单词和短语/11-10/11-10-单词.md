## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **chapter** /ˈtʃæptə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：章；章节；时期
- **英文释义**：a main division of a book; a period in history or in someone's life
- **例句**：
  - Please read Chapter 3 for homework.
  - 请阅读第三章作为作业。
  - He started a new chapter in his life.
  - 他开启了人生的新篇章。
- **助记**：chap（头）+ ter → 书的大段落 → 章节

### 2. **character** /ˈkærəktə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：性格；品格；人物；字符
- **英文释义**：the qualities that make someone or something distinctive; a person in a story; a letter or symbol
- **例句**：
  - She has a strong character.
  - 她性格坚强。
  - He played the main character in the film.
  - 他在电影中饰演主角。
- **助记**：character → characteristic（特征）→ characterize（刻画）

### 3. **charge** /tʃɑːdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：收费；指控；充电；负责
- **英文释义**：a price; an accusation; to ask for money; to entrust with a task
- **例句**：
  - There is no charge for delivery.
  - 送货免费。
  - He was charged with theft.
  - 他被指控盗窃。
- **助记**：in charge of（负责）、free of charge（免费）是常见搭配

### 4. **charity** /ˈtʃærəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：慈善；慈善机构；施舍
- **英文释义**：organizations that help the needy; kindness in giving
- **例句**：
  - She donates to charity every month.
  - 她每月向慈善机构捐款。
  - The charity helps homeless people.
  - 这家慈善机构帮助无家可归者。
- **助记**：char（爱，同 care）+ ity → 出于爱心 → 慈善

### 5. **charm** /tʃɑːm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：魅力；符咒；使着迷
- **英文释义**：the power of pleasing; a magic spell; to attract or delight
- **例句**：
  - She has great personal charm.
  - 她很有个人魅力。
  - The village charmed us with its beauty.
  - 村庄的美景让我们着迷。
- **助记**：charm → charming（迷人的）

### 6. **charter** /ˈtʃɑːtə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：宪章；包租；租赁
- **英文释义**：a formal document granting rights; to hire a vehicle or aircraft
- **例句**：
  - The UN Charter was signed in 1945.
  - 联合国宪章于1945年签署。
  - They chartered a boat for the trip.
  - 他们包租了一艘船去旅行。
- **助记**：chart（图表）+ er → 书面文件 → 宪章

### 7. **cheap** /tʃiːp/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：便宜的；廉价的；劣质的
- **英文释义**：low in price; of poor quality
- **例句**：
  - This shirt is cheap but good quality.
  - 这件衬衫便宜但质量好。
  - Don't buy cheap imitations.
  - 不要买廉价的仿品。
- **助记**：cheap → cheaply（便宜地）→ cheapness（廉价）

### 8. **cheat** /tʃiːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：欺骗；作弊；骗子
- **英文释义**：to act dishonestly; to deceive; a person who cheats
- **例句**：
  - He cheated in the exam.
  - 他在考试中作弊。
  - Don't cheat on your partner.
  - 不要欺骗你的伴侣。
- **助记**：cheat 与 deceive 同义，cheat on 指背叛

### 9. **check** /tʃek/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：检查；核对；支票；账单
- **英文释义**：to examine; to verify; a written order to a bank; a bill
- **例句**：
  - Please check your answers carefully.
  - 请仔细检查你的答案。
  - May I have the check, please?
  - 请给我账单。
- **助记**：check in（入住）、check out（结账离开）是常见搭配

### 10. **cheer** /tʃɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：欢呼；鼓舞；使高兴
- **英文释义**：to shout support; to make someone feel happier; a shout of approval
- **例句**：
  - The crowd cheered when he scored.
  - 他得分时人群欢呼起来。
  - Cheer up! Things will get better.
  - 振作起来！事情会好起来的。
- **助记**：cheer → cheerful（愉快的）→ cheer up（振作）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **decorum** /dɪˈkɔːrəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：得体；礼节；礼仪
- **英文释义**：behavior that is proper and in good taste
- **例句**：
  - He behaved with decorum at the formal dinner.
  - 他在正式宴会上举止得体。
  - The meeting was conducted with decorum.
  - 会议在庄重的气氛中进行。
- **助记**：decor（装饰）+ um → 合乎礼仪的装饰 → 得体

### 12. **deferential** /ˌdefəˈrenʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：恭敬的；顺从的
- **英文释义**：showing respect and willingness to comply
- **例句**：
  - She was deferential to her elders.
  - 她对长辈恭敬有礼。
  - His deferential manner impressed the judges.
  - 他恭敬的态度给评委留下了印象。
- **助记**：defer（遵从）+ ential → 顺从的

### 13. **delineate** /dɪˈlɪnieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：描绘；划定
- **英文释义**：to describe or outline precisely; to mark the boundaries
- **例句**：
  - The report delineates the company's future plans.
  - 报告详细阐述了公司的未来计划。
  - The boundary was clearly delineated.
  - 边界被清楚地划定了。
- **助记**：de（加强）+ line（线）+ ate → 用线条描绘 → 描绘；划定

### 14. **demure** /dɪˈmjʊə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：端庄的；矜持的
- **英文释义**：reserved and modest in manner
- **例句**：
  - She gave a demure smile.
  - 她露出了矜持的微笑。
  - Her demure appearance belied her strength.
  - 她端庄的外表掩盖了她的坚强。
- **助记**：de（加强）+ mure（墙）→ 像躲在墙后般含蓄 → 矜持的

### 15. **depreciate** /dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：贬值
- **英文释义**：to decrease in value over time
- **例句**：
  - Cars depreciate quickly after purchase.
  - 汽车购买后很快就贬值。
  - The currency has depreciated against the dollar.
  - 该货币对美元已贬值。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ preci（价值）+ ate → 价值下降 → 贬值
