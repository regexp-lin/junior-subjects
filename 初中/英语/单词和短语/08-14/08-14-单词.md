## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **fulfill** /fʊlˈfɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：履行；实现
- **英文释义**：to carry out or achieve something promised or expected; to satisfy or meet requirements
- **例句**：
  - She worked hard to fulfill her dream of becoming a doctor.
  - 她努力实现自己成为医生的梦想。
  - The company failed to fulfill its contract with the supplier.
  - 该公司未能履行与供应商的合同。
- **助记**：ful-（完全）+ fill（填满）→ 完全填满、达成 → 履行；实现

### 2. **function** /ˈfʌŋkʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：功能；运作
- **英文释义**：n. a natural or intended purpose; v. to work or operate properly
- **例句**：
  - The main function of the heart is to pump blood throughout the body.
  - 心脏的主要功能是向全身输送血液。
  - The old computer still functions well after years of use.
  - 这台旧电脑用了多年仍然运作正常。
- **助记**：funct（执行）+ -ion（名词）→ 执行的作用或行为 → 功能；运作

### 3. **funeral** /ˈfjuːnərəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：葬礼
- **英文释义**：a ceremony held when someone has died, usually including burial or cremation
- **例句**：
  - Hundreds of people attended the funeral to pay their respects.
  - 数百人参加了葬礼以表达敬意。
  - The funeral will be held at the local church on Saturday.
  - 葬礼将于周六在当地教堂举行。
- **助记**：funer（葬礼）+ -al（名词后缀）→ 与死者告别、安葬的仪式 → 葬礼

### 4. **furnish** /ˈfɜːnɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：提供；装备
- **英文释义**：to supply or provide something; to equip a room or building with furniture
- **例句**：
  - The hotel furnishes guests with towels and toiletries.
  - 酒店为客人提供毛巾和洗漱用品。
  - They furnished their new apartment with modern furniture.
  - 他们用现代家具装备了新公寓。
- **助记**：furnish 与 furniture（家具）同源 → 提供家具、装备用品 → 提供；装备

### 5. **fuss** /fʌs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：大惊小怪
- **英文释义**：unnecessary excitement or worry about something unimportant; to show such excitement
- **例句**：
  - Don't make a fuss — it's just a small cut.
  - 别大惊小怪的——只是个小伤口。
  - She always fusses over her appearance before going out.
  - 她出门前总对自己的外表大惊小怪。
- **助记**：fuss 拟声，像焦急时发出的声响 → 对小事的过度反应 → 大惊小怪

### 6. **gallery** /ˈɡæləri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：画廊；美术馆
- **英文释义**：a room or building for displaying works of art; a covered walkway
- **例句**：
  - We visited the national gallery to see the famous paintings.
  - 我们参观了国家美术馆欣赏名画。
  - The artist's works will be on display at the gallery next week.
  - 这位艺术家的作品将于下周在画廊展出。
- **助记**：gallery 源自 galeria（长廊）→ 可漫步观赏艺术的长廊空间 → 画廊；美术馆

### 7. **garbage** /ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：垃圾
- **英文释义**：waste material or unwanted things to be thrown away; rubbish
- **例句**：
  - Please take out the garbage before it starts to smell.
  - 请在垃圾发臭之前把它倒掉。
  - The streets were littered with garbage after the festival.
  - 节日过后街上到处是垃圾。
- **助记**：garb（废弃衣物）+ -age（名词后缀）→ 被丢弃的废物 → 垃圾

### 8. **gauge** /ɡeɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：计量器；判断
- **英文释义**：n. an instrument for measuring; v. to estimate or judge something
- **例句**：
  - The fuel gauge shows that the tank is almost empty.
  - 油表显示油箱快空了。
  - It's hard to gauge how the audience will react to the new policy.
  - 很难判断公众会对新政策作何反应。
- **助记**：gauge 源自 measure（测量）→ 测量用的仪器或估量行为 → 计量器；判断

### 9. **gender** /ˈdʒendə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：性别
- **英文释义**：the fact of being male or female; social or cultural roles associated with sex
- **例句**：
  - The form asks you to specify your gender.
  - 表格要求你填写性别。
  - Gender equality has become an important topic in modern society.
  - 性别平等已成为现代社会的重要议题。
- **助记**：gender 源自 genus（种类、出生）→ 按生理或社会属性区分的种类 → 性别

### 10. **genius** /ˈdʒiːniəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：天才
- **英文释义**：exceptional intellectual or creative power; a person who has such power
- **例句**：
  - Einstein was a genius who revolutionized physics.
  - 爱因斯坦是革新物理学的天才。
  - It was a stroke of genius to use social media for the campaign.
  - 用社交媒体做这次活动真是天才之举。
- **助记**：genius 源自拉丁语"守护神、天赋"→ 拥有非凡天赋的人 → 天才

### 高一词汇

### 11. **heresy** /ˈherəsi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：异端；邪说
- **英文释义**：a belief or opinion that contradicts established religious or official doctrine
- **例句**：
  - In the Middle Ages, questioning the Church was considered heresy.
  - 在中世纪，质疑教会被视为异端。
  - His views on the economy were dismissed as economic heresy.
  - 他对经济的看法被视为经济异端而被否定。
- **助记**：heresy 源自"选择"→ 选择与正统不同的立场 → 异端；邪说

### 12. **hierarchy** /ˈhaɪərɑːki/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：等级制度；层级
- **英文释义**：a system in which people or things are ranked according to importance or power
- **例句**：
  - The company has a strict hierarchy with clear levels of authority.
  - 公司有严格的等级制度，职权层次分明。
  - In the hierarchy of needs, safety comes after basic survival.
  - 在需求层级中，安全排在基本生存之后。
- **助记**：hier（神圣）+ archy（统治）→ 神圣的等级秩序 → 等级制度；层级

### 13. **hinder** /ˈhɪndə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：阻碍；妨碍
- **英文释义**：to make it difficult for someone to do something or for something to happen
- **例句**：
  - Bad weather hindered the rescue team from reaching the mountain village.
  - 恶劣天气阻碍了救援队抵达山村。
  - The lack of funding hindered the progress of the research project.
  - 资金不足妨碍了研究项目的进展。
- **助记**：hind（后面）+ -er（使…）→ 使落在后面 → 阻碍；妨碍

### 14. **holistic** /həʊˈlɪstɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：整体的；全面的
- **英文释义**：considering the whole rather than just the parts; comprehensive
- **例句**：
  - The doctor takes a holistic approach to patient care, considering body and mind.
  - 医生采取整体疗法，同时考虑患者的身体和心理。
  - We need a holistic solution that addresses all aspects of the problem.
  - 我们需要一个解决各方面问题的全面方案。
- **助记**：hol（整体）+ -istic（形容词）→ 把事物当作整体看待的 → 整体的；全面的

### 15. **homogeneous** /ˌhəʊməˈdʒiːniəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：同质的；均匀的
- **英文释义**：of the same kind or nature; uniform in structure or composition
- **例句**：
  - The population in that region is quite homogeneous in terms of culture.
  - 该地区人口在文化上相当同质。
  - Stir the mixture until it becomes homogeneous.
  - 搅拌混合物直至均匀。
- **助记**：homo-（相同）+ gene（种类）+ -ous（形容词）→ 同一种类的 → 同质的；均匀的
