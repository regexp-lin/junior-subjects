## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **stipulate** /ˈstɪpjuleɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：规定；约定；明确要求
- **英文释义**：to demand or specify something as part of an agreement
- **例句**：
  - The contract stipulates that payment must be made within 30 days.
  - 合同规定付款必须在30天内完成。
  - The school stipulates that students must wear uniforms on weekdays.
  - 学校规定学生在工作日必须穿校服。
- **助记**：stipul（约定）+ ate → 做出约定 → 规定；名词：stipulation（规定）

### 2. **allude** /əˈluːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：暗示；间接提及
- **英文释义**：to mention something indirectly; to hint at
- **例句**：
  - The speaker alluded to the recent scandal without naming anyone.
  - 演讲者暗示了最近的丑闻但没有点名。
  - She alluded to her difficult childhood in the interview.
  - 她在采访中间接提到了自己困难的童年。
- **助记**：al（朝向）+ lude（玩）→ 朝着某事玩弄暗示 → 暗示；名词：allusion（暗示）

### 3. **encroach** /ɪnˈkrəʊtʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：侵犯；侵蚀；蚕食
- **英文释义**：to gradually go beyond acceptable limits; to intrude on someone's territory
- **例句**：
  - The new buildings are encroaching on the green spaces in the city.
  - 新建筑正在蚕食城市的绿地。
  - Technology can sometimes encroach on our privacy.
  - 技术有时会侵犯我们的隐私。
- **助记**：en（进入）+ croach（钩/抓）→ 抓进来 → 侵犯；名词：encroachment（侵犯）

### 4. **ratify** /ˈrætɪfaɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：批准；认可（条约/协议）
- **英文释义**：to make an agreement officially valid by signing or voting for it
- **例句**：
  - The parliament ratified the trade agreement with the neighboring country.
  - 议会批准了与邻国的贸易协议。
  - Both parties must ratify the contract for it to take effect.
  - 双方必须批准合同才能使其生效。
- **助记**：rati（确认）+ fy（使）→ 使确认 → 批准；名词：ratification（批准）

### 5. **memoir** /ˈmemwɑː(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：回忆录；自传
- **英文释义**：a written account of someone's personal experiences
- **例句**：
  - The retired general published a memoir about his life in the military.
  - 退休将军出版了一部关于他军旅生涯的回忆录。
  - Her memoir became a bestseller and inspired many readers.
  - 她的回忆录成为畅销书，激励了许多读者。
- **助记**：mem（记忆）+ oir → 记忆的记录 → 回忆录

### 6. **anecdote** /ˈænɪkdəʊt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：轶事；趣闻
- **英文释义**：a short, interesting, or amusing story about a real person or event
- **例句**：
  - The teacher shared a funny anecdote about her student days.
  - 老师分享了一个关于她学生时代的有趣轶事。
  - His speech was full of amusing anecdotes that kept the audience entertained.
  - 他的演讲充满了有趣的轶事，让观众乐在其中。
- **助记**：anec（不出版的）+ dote（给予）→ 不正式出版的故事 → 轶事

### 7. **erratic** /ɪˈrætɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不稳定的；不规则的；反复无常的
- **英文释义**：not regular or predictable; changing often
- **例句**：
  - His erratic behavior worried his friends and family.
  - 他反复无常的行为让朋友和家人很担心。
  - The bus service in this area is erratic and unreliable.
  - 这个地区的公交服务不稳定且不可靠。
- **助记**：err（错误/偏离）+ atic → 偏离正常的 → 不稳定的

### 8. **verbose** /vɜːˈbəʊs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：冗长的；啰嗦的
- **英文释义**：using more words than needed; wordy
- **例句**：
  - His verbose writing style made the report very difficult to read.
  - 他冗长的写作风格使报告非常难读。
  - Try not to be verbose in your essay — be clear and concise.
  - 写作文时尽量不要啰嗦——要清晰简洁。
- **助记**：verb（词/话）+ ose → 话太多的 → 冗长的；名词：verbosity（冗长）

### 9. **irrevocably** /ɪˈrevəkəbli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：不可撤回地；不可挽回地
- **英文释义**：in a way that cannot be changed or reversed
- **例句**：
  - The decision irrevocably changed the course of history.
  - 这一决定不可挽回地改变了历史进程。
  - The environment has been irrevocably damaged by pollution.
  - 环境已经被污染不可挽回地破坏了。
- **助记**：ir（不）+ revocably（可撤回地）→ 不可撤回地；形容词：irrevocable（不可撤回的）

### 10. **propagate** /ˈprɒpəɡeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：传播；繁殖；宣传
- **英文释义**：to spread an idea, belief, or piece of information widely; to breed
- **例句**：
  - Social media makes it easy to propagate false information.
  - 社交媒体使传播虚假信息变得容易。
  - Plants propagate through seeds, cuttings, or other methods.
  - 植物通过种子、扦插或其他方法繁殖。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ pag（固定/种植）+ ate → 向前种植 → 传播/繁殖；名词：propagation

### 高一词汇

### 11. **efficacious** /ˌefɪˈkeɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有效的；灵验的
- **英文释义**：effective in producing a desired result
- **例句**：
  - The new treatment has proven efficacious in reducing symptoms.
  - 新的治疗方法已被证明能有效减轻症状。
  - An efficacious strategy is needed to tackle this complex problem.
  - 需要一个有效的策略来应对这个复杂的问题。
- **助记**：ef（出）+ fic（做）+ acious → 能做出效果的 → 有效的；名词：efficacy（效力）

### 12. **unequivocal** /ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：明确的；毫不含糊的
- **英文释义**：leaving no doubt; completely clear
- **例句**：
  - The evidence was unequivocal — the suspect was guilty.
  - 证据是明确的——嫌疑人有罪。
  - She gave an unequivocal answer: she would accept the offer.
  - 她给出了明确的答案：她会接受这个提议。
- **助记**：un（不）+ equivocal（模棱两可的）→ 不模棱两可的 → 明确的

### 13. **expropriate** /eksˈprəʊprieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：征用；没收（财产）
- **英文释义**：to take property from its owner for public use, usually with compensation
- **例句**：
  - The government expropriated the land to build a new highway.
  - 政府征用了这块土地来修建一条新公路。
  - Private properties were expropriated during the period of reform.
  - 改革期间私有财产被征用。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ propri（财产）+ ate → 把财产拿出来 → 征用；名词：expropriation

### 14. **anachronistic** /əˌnækrəˈnɪstɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：时代错误的；过时的
- **英文释义**：belonging to a different period of time; outdated
- **例句**：
  - Using typewriters in the digital age feels anachronistic.
  - 在数字时代使用打字机感觉是时代错误的。
  - Some of the traditions seem anachronistic in modern society.
  - 有些传统在现代社会看起来有些过时。
- **助记**：ana（向后）+ chron（时间）+ istic → 在时间上向后的 → 时代错误的；名词：anachronism

### 15. **quintessential** /ˌkwɪntɪˈsenʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：精髓的；典型的；最具代表性的
- **英文释义**：representing the most perfect example of something
- **例句**：
  - Beijing duck is the quintessential Chinese dish.
  - 北京烤鸭是最具代表性的中国菜。
  - She is the quintessential example of a dedicated teacher.
  - 她是一位敬业教师的最佳典范。
- **助记**：quint（第五）+ essential（本质的）→ 第五要素（古代第五种元素，即精华）→ 精髓的
