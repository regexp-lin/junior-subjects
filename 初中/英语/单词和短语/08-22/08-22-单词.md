## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **inject** /ɪnˈdʒekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：注射；注入
- **英文释义**：to put a liquid, especially a drug, into a person's body using a needle; to add something new to a situation
- **例句**：
  - The doctor injected the vaccine into his arm.
  - 医生把疫苗注射进他的手臂。
  - The coach injected enthusiasm into the team.
  - 教练给队伍注入了热情。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ ject（投、掷）→ 投进去 → 注射；注入

### 2. **innocent** /ˈɪnəsnt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无辜的；天真的
- **英文释义**：not guilty of a crime; having no knowledge of the bad things in life; naïve
- **例句**：
  - The jury found him innocent of all charges.
  - 陪审团裁定他所有指控无罪。
  - She has an innocent smile that lights up the room.
  - 她天真的笑容点亮了整个房间。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ noc（伤害）+ -ent → 没有伤害的 → 无辜的；天真的

### 3. **innovate** /ˈɪnəveɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：创新
- **英文释义**：to introduce new ideas, methods, or products; to make changes in something established
- **例句**：
  - The company continues to innovate in the tech industry.
  - 这家公司持续在科技领域创新。
  - We need to innovate our teaching methods.
  - 我们需要创新教学方法。
- **助记**：in-（加强）+ nov（新）+ -ate → 使更新 → 创新

### 4. **inquire** /ɪnˈkwaɪər/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：询问；调查
- **英文释义**：to ask for information; to investigate or look into something
- **例句**：
  - I'll inquire about the train schedules.
  - 我来问问火车时刻表。
  - The police inquired into the cause of the accident.
  - 警方调查了事故原因。
- **助记**：in-（ into）+ quire（寻求）→ 寻求进去 → 询问；调查

### 5. **insect** /ˈɪnsekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：昆虫
- **英文释义**：a small animal with six legs, a body divided into three parts, and often wings, such as ants, bees, or flies
- **例句**：
  - Bees are beneficial insects that help pollinate flowers.
  - 蜜蜂是有益昆虫，帮助花朵授粉。
  - The insect landed on the leaf.
  - 那只昆虫落在叶子上。
- **助记**：in-（ into）+ sect（切）→ 身体分段像被切开 → 昆虫

### 6. **insert** /ɪnˈsɜːrt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：插入
- **英文释义**：to put or place something into something else
- **例句**：
  - Insert the key into the lock and turn it.
  - 把钥匙插进锁里转动。
  - Please insert your card into the ATM.
  - 请将卡插入自动取款机。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ sert（放置）→ 放进去 → 插入

### 7. **insight** /ˈɪnsaɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：洞察力
- **英文释义**：the ability to understand the true nature of something; a deep understanding
- **例句**：
  - The book provides valuable insights into human behavior.
  - 这本书对人性提供了有价值的洞察。
  - She has great insight into the problem.
  - 她对这个问题有深刻的洞察力。
- **助记**：in-（ into）+ sight（看）→ 看进去 → 洞察力

### 8. **inspect** /ɪnˈspekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：检查；视察
- **英文释义**：to look at something carefully in order to check its quality or condition; to officially visit and examine
- **例句**：
  - The teacher inspected the students' homework.
  - 老师检查了学生的作业。
  - Health officials will inspect the restaurant tomorrow.
  - 卫生官员明天将视察这家餐馆。
- **助记**：in-（ into）+ spect（看）→ 看进去 → 检查；视察

### 9. **install** /ɪnˈstɔːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：安装
- **英文释义**：to put a piece of equipment in place and make it ready to use; to set up software on a computer
- **例句**：
  - We need to install a new air conditioner.
  - 我们需要安装一台新空调。
  - Have you installed the latest software update?
  - 你安装最新的软件更新了吗？
- **助记**：in-（ into）+ stall（放置）→ 放入固定位置 → 安装

### 10. **instant** /ˈɪnstənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：即时的；瞬间
- **英文释义**：happening or produced immediately; a very short moment of time
- **例句**：
  - The app provides instant translation.
  - 这款应用提供即时翻译。
  - For an instant, he thought he had won.
  - 有那么一瞬间，他以为自己赢了。
- **助记**：in-（ on）+ st（站）+ -ant → 就站在眼前的 → 即时的；瞬间

### 高一词汇

### 11. **intuitive** /ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：直觉的
- **英文释义**：based on feelings rather than facts or proof; easy to understand or use without explicit instruction
- **例句**：
  - She has an intuitive understanding of people.
  - 她对人有直觉的理解。
  - The software has an intuitive interface.
  - 这款软件有直观的界面。
- **助记**：intuit（直觉）+ -ive → 直觉的

### 12. **invincible** /ɪnˈvɪnsəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不可战胜的
- **英文释义**：too strong to be defeated or overcome
- **例句**：
  - The team seemed invincible that season.
  - 那赛季这支队伍似乎不可战胜。
  - He felt invincible after winning the championship.
  - 夺冠后他觉得自己所向无敌。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ vinc（征服）+ -ible → 不能被征服的 → 不可战胜的

### 13. **irate** /aɪˈreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：愤怒的
- **英文释义**：very angry; furious
- **例句**：
  - The irate customer demanded to speak to the manager.
  - 愤怒的顾客坚持要见经理。
  - He became irate when he heard the news.
  - 听到消息后他勃然大怒。
- **助记**：ira（愤怒，拉丁语）+ -ate → 愤怒的

### 14. **irrelevant** /ɪˈreləvənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无关紧要的
- **英文释义**：not connected with or relevant to what is being discussed or considered
- **例句**：
  - His comments were irrelevant to the topic.
  - 他的评论与主题无关紧要。
  - Age is irrelevant when it comes to talent.
  - 就才华而言，年龄无关紧要。
- **助记**：ir-（不）+ relevant（相关的）→ 不相关的 → 无关紧要的

### 15. **itinerant** /aɪˈtɪnərənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：巡回的；流动的
- **英文释义**：traveling from place to place, especially to work; not staying in one place
- **例句**：
  - Itinerant merchants used to travel from village to village.
  - 流动商人过去常在村落间巡回。
  - She works as an itinerant teacher in rural areas.
  - 她在农村地区做巡回教师。
- **助记**：itiner（旅程）+ -ant → 在路上走的 → 巡回的；流动的
