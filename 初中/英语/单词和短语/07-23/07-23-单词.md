## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **illusion** /ɪˈluːʒn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：幻觉；错觉
- **英文释义**：something that seems to exist but does not, or seems to be something that it is not
- **例句**：
  - The oasis in the desert was just an illusion caused by the heat.
  - 沙漠中的绿洲只是热气造成的幻象。
  - He had no illusion about how difficult the exam would be.
  - 他对考试的难度没有任何幻想。
- **助记**：ill（坏）+usion（与 vision 相关）→ 坏的视觉 → 幻觉；错觉

### 2. **immerse** /ɪˈmɜːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：沉浸；使专心于
- **英文释义**：to put something completely in a liquid; to become completely involved in something
- **例句**：
  - She immersed herself in her studies before the final exam.
  - 期末考试前她全身心投入学习。
  - Immerse the vegetables in cold water for a few minutes.
  - 把蔬菜在冷水中浸泡几分钟。
- **助记**：im-（进入）+ merse（浸）→ 浸入 → 沉浸

### 3. **incentive** /ɪnˈsentɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：动机；激励
- **英文释义**：something that encourages you to do something
- **例句**：
  - The prize is a great incentive for students to work hard.
  - 奖品是激励学生努力学习的强大动力。
  - Tax cuts can serve as an incentive for investment.
  - 减税可以作为投资的激励手段。
- **助记**：in- + cent（唱）+ -ive → 在内心"唱"起干劲 → 动机；激励

### 4. **indifferent** /ɪnˈdɪfrənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：漠不关心的；无所谓的
- **英文释义**：not caring about something; having no preference
- **例句**：
  - He seemed indifferent to the result of the match.
  - 他似乎对比赛结果毫不在乎。
  - She was indifferent to fashion and dressed simply.
  - 她对时尚漠不关心，穿得很朴素。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ different（不同）→ 不区分 → 漠不关心

### 5. **inherit** /ɪnˈherɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：继承；遗传
- **英文释义**：to receive money, property, or a title from someone who has died; to have a quality passed from parents
- **例句**：
  - She inherited her mother's talent for music.
  - 她继承了母亲的音乐天赋。
  - He will inherit the family business when he grows up.
  - 他长大后将继承家族企业。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ herit（继承）→ 继承进来 → 继承

### 6. **innovate** /ˈɪnəveɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：创新；革新
- **英文释义**：to introduce new ideas, methods, or things
- **例句**：
  - The company continues to innovate in technology.
  - 这家公司持续在技术上创新。
  - We need to innovate to stay competitive.
  - 我们需要创新才能保持竞争力。
- **助记**：in-（新）+ nov（新）+ -ate → 引入新的 → 创新

### 7. **insist** /ɪnˈsɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：坚持；坚决要求
- **英文释义**：to say firmly that something is true or that something must happen
- **例句**：
  - She insisted on paying for dinner.
  - 她坚持要付晚餐的钱。
  - The teacher insisted that we hand in our homework on time.
  - 老师坚持要求我们按时交作业。
- **助记**：in-（加强）+ sist（站）→ 坚定地站着 → 坚持

### 8. **instant** /ˈɪnstənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：即刻的；瞬间的
- **英文释义**：happening immediately; (of food) that can be prepared quickly
- **例句**：
  - The app gives instant feedback on your pronunciation.
  - 这个应用能即时反馈你的发音。
  - Instant noodles are popular among students.
  - 方便面在学生中很受欢迎。
- **助记**：in- + stant（站）→ 立刻站在眼前 → 即刻的

### 9. **instinct** /ˈɪnstɪŋkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：本能；直觉
- **英文释义**：a natural tendency to behave in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - Birds have an instinct to build nests.
  - 鸟类有筑巢的本能。
  - My instinct told me something was wrong.
  - 我的直觉告诉我出了什么问题。
- **助记**：in-（内）+ stinct（刺、激励）→ 内心驱动的 → 本能

### 10. **integral** /ˈɪntɪɡrəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：必不可少的；构成整体所需的
- **英文释义**：necessary and important as a part of a whole
- **例句**：
  - Teamwork is integral to our project's success.
  - 团队合作对我们项目成功不可或缺。
  - Exercise is an integral part of a healthy lifestyle.
  - 运动是健康生活方式不可或缺的一部分。
- **助记**：integer（完整）+ -al → 完整所需的 → 必不可少的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **intact** /ɪnˈtækt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：完整无缺的；未受损的
- **英文释义**：not damaged or impaired; complete
- **例句**：
  - The ancient building remained intact after the earthquake.
  - 地震后这座古建筑完好无损。
  - His reputation emerged intact from the scandal.
  - 他的名誉在丑闻中得以保全。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ tact（触、碰）→ 未被触碰损坏 → 完整无缺的

### 12. **intrigue** /ɪnˈtriːɡ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：激起……的兴趣；密谋
- **英文释义**：to make someone very interested; to make secret plans
- **例句**：
  - The mystery novel intrigued her from the first page.
  - 这本推理小说从第一页就吸引了她。
  - The idea of studying abroad intrigued many students.
  - 留学的想法引起了许多学生的兴趣。
- **助记**：in- + trigue（与 trick 相关）→ 用计谋引起兴趣 → 激起兴趣

### 13. **invaluable** /ɪnˈvæljuəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：极宝贵的；无价的
- **英文释义**：extremely useful or valuable
- **例句**：
  - Her experience was invaluable to the team.
  - 她的经验对团队来说极其宝贵。
  - The teacher's advice proved invaluable for the exam.
  - 老师的建议对考试来说非常宝贵。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ valuable（可估价的）→ 无法估价的 → 无价的

### 14. **irreversible** /ˌɪrɪˈvɜːsəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不可逆转的；无法挽回的
- **英文释义**：impossible to change back to a previous state
- **例句**：
  - Climate change may cause irreversible damage to the planet.
  - 气候变化可能对地球造成不可逆转的损害。
  - Once you click delete, the action is irreversible.
  - 一旦你点击删除，这个操作就无法撤销。
- **助记**：ir-（不）+ reversible（可逆的）→ 不可逆转的

### 15. **isolate** /ˈaɪsəleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：隔离；使孤立
- **英文释义**：to separate something or someone from other things or people
- **例句**：
  - Patients with the disease were isolated to prevent spread.
  - 患病者被隔离以防止传播。
  - He felt isolated after moving to a new school.
  - 转学后他感到很孤立。
- **助记**：isol（岛）+ -ate → 变成岛一样 → 隔离；孤立
