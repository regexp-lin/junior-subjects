## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **journey** /ˈdʒɜːrni/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：旅程
- **英文释义**：an act of travelling from one place to another, especially when the distance is long; a long trip
- **例句**：
  - The journey from Beijing to Shanghai takes about five hours by train.
  - 从北京到上海的旅程坐火车大约需要五小时。
  - Life is often compared to a journey.
  - 人生常被比作一场旅程。
- **助记**：journ（日）+ -ey → 原指"一天的行程" → 旅程

### 2. **junction** /ˈdʒʌŋkʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：交叉路口；枢纽
- **英文释义**：a place where two or more things join or meet; a place where roads or railway lines meet
- **例句**：
  - The accident happened at the junction of Main Street and Park Avenue.
  - 事故发生在主街和公园大道的交叉路口。
  - This city is an important railway junction.
  - 这座城市是重要的铁路枢纽。
- **助记**：junct（连接）+ -ion → 连接处 → 交叉路口；枢纽

### 3. **justice** /ˈdʒʌstɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：正义；公正
- **英文释义**：fair treatment of people; the quality of being fair and reasonable; the legal system
- **例句**：
  - We must fight for justice and equality.
  - 我们必须为正义和平等而斗争。
  - The judge ensured that justice was done.
  - 法官确保公正得以实现。
- **助记**：just（公正的）+ -ice → 正义；公正

### 4. **keen** /kiːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：渴望的；敏锐的
- **英文释义**：very interested or eager; having a sharp edge or point; quick to understand
- **例句**：
  - She is keen to learn new languages.
  - 她渴望学习新语言。
  - He has a keen eye for detail.
  - 他对细节有敏锐的观察力。
- **助记**：来自古英语，本义为"锋利的" → 引申为"敏锐的；渴望的"

### 5. **kidnap** /ˈkɪdnæp/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：绑架
- **英文释义**：to take someone away by force, especially in order to get money for their return
- **例句**：
  - The businessman was kidnapped and held for ransom.
  - 那名商人遭绑架并被索要赎金。
  - Kidnapping is a serious crime.
  - 绑架是重罪。
- **助记**：kid（小孩）+ nap（偷、抢）→ 原指偷拐小孩 → 绑架

### 6. **kingdom** /ˈkɪŋdəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：王国
- **英文释义**：a country ruled by a king or queen; a realm or domain
- **例句**：
  - The United Kingdom is a constitutional monarchy.
  - 英国是君主立宪制王国。
  - The animal kingdom includes all living creatures.
  - 动物界包括所有生物。
- **助记**：king（国王）+ -dom（领域、状态）→ 王国

### 7. **kneel** /niːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：跪下
- **英文释义**：to go down or rest on one or both knees
- **例句**：
  - He knelt down to propose to her.
  - 他跪下向她求婚。
  - The soldiers knelt in prayer before the battle.
  - 士兵们在战前跪地祈祷。
- **助记**：knee（膝盖）+ -l → 与膝盖相关 → 跪下

### 8. **label** /ˈleɪbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：标签；贴标签
- **英文释义**：a piece of paper or other material that gives information about something; to attach a label to something; to describe someone or something in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - Please read the label before taking the medicine.
  - 服药前请先阅读标签。
  - She labeled the boxes with their contents.
  - 她在箱子上贴上内容标签。
- **助记**：来自古法语 labelle（带子、条）→ 贴在上面的条状物 → 标签

### 9. **landscape** /ˈlændskeɪp/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：风景；地形
- **英文释义**：all the visible features of an area of land; a picture representing a view of the countryside
- **例句**：
  - The landscape of the countryside is breathtaking.
  - 乡间的风景令人叹为观止。
  - He paints landscapes and portraits.
  - 他画风景画和肖像画。
- **助记**：land（土地）+ -scape（景色）→ 土地上的景色 → 风景；地形

### 10. **launch** /lɔːntʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：发射；推出
- **英文释义**：to send something into the air or space; to start something new; an act of launching
- **例句**：
  - The rocket was launched successfully.
  - 火箭成功发射。
  - The company will launch a new product next month.
  - 公司下月将推出新产品。
- **助记**：源自法语 lancer（投掷）→ 发射；推出

### 高一词汇

### 11. **lethal** /ˈliːθl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：致命的
- **英文释义**：causing or able to cause death; extremely dangerous
- **例句**：
  - The poison is lethal in small amounts.
  - 这种毒药少量即可致命。
  - The disease can be lethal if left untreated.
  - 若不治疗，此病可能致命。
- **助记**：leth（死亡）+ -al → 与死亡相关的 → 致命的

### 12. **levity** /ˈlevəti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：轻率；轻浮
- **英文释义**：lack of seriousness; treating serious matters in a humorous way when you should be serious
- **例句**：
  - His levity was inappropriate at the funeral.
  - 他在葬礼上的轻率举止不合时宜。
  - We need to approach this with seriousness, not levity.
  - 我们需要认真对待此事，而非轻浮敷衍。
- **助记**：lev（轻）+ -ity → 轻的状态 → 轻率；轻浮

### 13. **liaison** /liˈeɪzn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：联络；联系人
- **英文释义**：communication between people or groups; a person who acts as a link between groups; a secret romantic relationship
- **例句**：
  - She works as a liaison between the two departments.
  - 她担任两个部门之间的联络人。
  - We need better liaison with our overseas partners.
  - 我们需要与海外伙伴加强联络。
- **助记**：源自法语 liaison（连接）→ 起连接作用的人或事 → 联络；联系人

### 14. **lucid** /ˈluːsɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：清晰的；头脑清醒的
- **英文释义**：clearly expressed and easy to understand; able to think clearly, especially when one is ill
- **例句**：
  - He gave a lucid explanation of the theory.
  - 他对该理论做了清晰的解释。
  - Despite the fever, she remained lucid.
  - 尽管发烧，她头脑依然清醒。
- **助记**：luc（光）+ -id → 如光照般清楚 → 清晰的；头脑清醒的

### 15. **luminous** /ˈluːmɪnəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：发光的；明亮的
- **英文释义**：producing or reflecting light; very bright; easy to understand
- **例句**：
  - The watch has a luminous dial for reading at night.
  - 这款表有夜光表盘方便夜间阅读。
  - The moon cast a luminous glow over the sea.
  - 月光在海面上投下明亮的光辉。
- **助记**：lumin（光）+ -ous → 发光的；明亮的
