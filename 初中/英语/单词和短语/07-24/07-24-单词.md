## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **jest** /dʒest/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：玩笑；戏谑
- **英文释义**：something said or done to cause laughter; a joke
- **例句**：
  - He said it in jest, not meaning to offend anyone.
  - 他是开玩笑说的，不是故意冒犯任何人。
  - Don't take it seriously — it was only a jest.
  - 别当真，只是玩笑而已。
- **助记**：jest → " jest "（与 rest 押韵）→ 休息时说的俏皮话 → 玩笑

### 2. **jewel** /ˈdʒuːəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：宝石；珍品
- **英文释义**：a precious stone; something or someone that is very valuable
- **例句**：
  - She wore a necklace with a precious jewel.
  - 她戴了一条镶着宝石的项链。
  - This book is a jewel of Chinese literature.
  - 这本书是中国文学的瑰宝。
- **助记**：jewel → "珠儿"（谐音）→ 珠宝宝石 → 宝石

### 3. **jog** /dʒɒɡ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：慢跑；轻推
- **英文释义**：to run slowly for exercise; to push something slightly
- **例句**：
  - She jogs in the park every morning.
  - 她每天早上在公园慢跑。
  - The sound jogged his memory of that day.
  - 这声音唤起了他对那天的记忆。
- **助记**：jog → " jog "（节奏感）→ 有节奏地慢跑 → 慢跑

### 4. **jolt** /dʒəʊlt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使颠簸；使震惊
- **英文释义**：to push or shake something suddenly and violently; to give someone a sudden shock
- **例句**：
  - The bus jolted when it hit a pothole.
  - 公交车撞到坑洼时颠了一下。
  - The bad news jolted him out of his complacency.
  - 坏消息把他从自满中惊醒。
- **助记**：jolt → " jump "（跳）+ " bolt "（ bolt）→ 突然跳起来 → 颠簸；震惊

### 5. **jubilant** /ˈdʒuːbɪlənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：欢欣鼓舞的；喜气洋洋的
- **英文释义**：feeling or expressing great happiness and triumph
- **例句**：
  - The team was jubilant after winning the championship.
  - 夺冠后全队欢欣鼓舞。
  - Jubilant crowds celebrated in the streets.
  - 欢欣鼓舞的人群在街头庆祝。
- **助记**：jubil（欢呼）+ -ant → 欢呼的 → 欢欣鼓舞的

### 6. **junction** /ˈdʒʌŋkʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：交叉点；连接处
- **英文释义**：a place where roads or railway lines meet
- **例句**：
  - Turn left at the next junction.
  - 在下一个路口左转。
  - There was an accident at the junction of the two highways.
  - 两条高速公路的交叉处发生了事故。
- **助记**：junct（连接）+ -ion → 连接的地方 → 交叉点

### 7. **keen** /kiːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：热衷的；敏锐的
- **英文释义**：very interested or enthusiastic; sharp or strong
- **例句**：
  - She is keen on playing basketball.
  - 她热衷于打篮球。
  - He has a keen eye for detail.
  - 他对细节有敏锐的洞察力。
- **助记**：keen → "看"（谐音）→  keen 地看 → 敏锐的；热衷的

### 8. **kernel** /ˈkɜːnl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：核心；果仁
- **英文释义**：the central or most important part; the inner part of a nut or seed
- **例句**：
  - There's a kernel of truth in what he said.
  - 他的话里有几分道理。
  - The kernel of the walnut is edible.
  - 核桃仁可以吃。
- **助记**：kernel → " core "（核心）→ 音近 core → 核心；果仁

### 9. **lavish** /ˈlævɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：奢侈的；慷慨的
- **英文释义**：spending or giving too much; very generous
- **例句**：
  - They threw a lavish party for her birthday.
  - 他们为她举办了一场奢侈的生日派对。
  - Her parents were lavish with praise for her achievements.
  - 父母对她的成就赞不绝口。
- **助记**：lav（洗）+ -ish → 像流水一样挥霍 → 奢侈的

### 10. **legacy** /ˈleɡəsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：遗产；遗留物
- **英文释义**：something that is left behind by someone who has died or by the past
- **例句**：
  - He left a lasting legacy in the field of science.
  - 他在科学领域留下了永恒的遗产。
  - The ancient ruins are a legacy of a lost civilization.
  - 古遗址是失落文明的遗迹。
- **助记**：leg（送、留）+ -acy → 留下来的东西 → 遗产

### 高一词汇

### 11. **lethal** /ˈliːθl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：致命的；毁灭性的
- **英文释义**：causing or able to cause death
- **例句**：
  - The snake's venom is lethal to humans.
  - 这种蛇的毒液对人类是致命的。
  - The combination of drugs could be lethal.
  - 这些药物组合可能致命。
- **助记**：leth（死，来自 Lethe 忘川）+ -al → 致死的 → 致命的

### 12. **leverage** /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：影响力；杠杆作用
- **英文释义**：the power to influence people or get results; the use of borrowed money to increase returns
- **例句**：
  - She used her position to gain leverage in the negotiation.
  - 她利用自己的职位在谈判中占得先机。
  - The company used financial leverage to expand.
  - 公司利用财务杠杆进行扩张。
- **助记**：lever（杠杆）+ -age → 杠杆的作用 → 影响力

### 13. **liability** /ˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：责任；负债
- **英文释义**：the state of being responsible for something; something that is a disadvantage
- **例句**：
  - The company accepted liability for the accident.
  - 公司承担了事故的责任。
  - His lack of experience was a liability in the job.
  - 缺乏经验是他在工作中的不利因素。
- **助记**：li（绑）+ ability（能力）→ 被绑定的义务 → 责任

### 14. **lobby** /ˈlɒbi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：大厅；游说团体
- **英文释义**：a large entrance hall; a group that tries to influence politicians
- **例句**：
  - We'll meet in the hotel lobby at 8 o'clock.
  - 我们八点在酒店大厅见。
  - The environmental lobby pushed for stronger pollution laws.
  - 环保游说团体推动更严格的污染法。
- **助记**：lobby → "老碧"（谐音）→ 大厅里人来人往 → 大厅

### 15. **luminous** /ˈluːmɪnəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：发光的；明亮的
- **英文释义**：producing or reflecting light; very bright
- **例句**：
  - The watch has a luminous dial so you can see it at night.
  - 这块表有夜光表盘，晚上也能看见。
  - The room was painted in luminous colors.
  - 房间漆成了明亮的颜色。
- **助记**：lumin（光）+ -ous → 有光的 → 发光的
