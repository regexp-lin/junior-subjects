## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **adventure** /ədˈventʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：冒险；冒险经历；奇遇
- **英文释义**：an unusual, exciting, and possibly dangerous experience
- **例句**：
  - Reading adventure stories is one of his favorite hobbies.
  - 阅读冒险故事是他最喜欢的爱好之一。
  - The trip to the mountains was a great adventure for the whole family.
  - 去山里的旅行对全家人来说是一次很棒的冒险。
- **助记**：advent（到来）+ ure → 未知事物的到来 → 冒险；形容词：adventurous（爱冒险的）

### 2. **protect** /prəˈtekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：保护；防护
- **英文释义**：to keep someone or something safe from harm or damage
- **例句**：
  - We must protect the environment for future generations.
  - 我们必须为后代保护环境。
  - Sunglasses can protect your eyes from the strong sunlight.
  - 太阳镜可以保护你的眼睛免受强烈阳光的伤害。
- **助记**：pro（在前面）+ tect（覆盖）→ 在前面覆盖 → 保护；名词：protection；固定搭配：protect...from...

### 3. **announce** /əˈnaʊns/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：宣布；公布；通知
- **英文释义**：to make something known publicly or officially
- **例句**：
  - The principal announced the results of the competition at the assembly.
  - 校长在集会上宣布了比赛结果。
  - It was announced that the school would be closed due to the heavy snow.
  - 据宣布，由于大雪学校将停课。
- **助记**：an（朝向）+ nounce（报告）→ 向大家报告 → 宣布；名词：announcement

### 4. **community** /kəˈmjuːnəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：社区；社会；群体
- **英文释义**：a group of people living in the same area or having something in common
- **例句**：
  - Our community organized a charity event to help the elderly.
  - 我们社区组织了一次慈善活动来帮助老人。
  - The online learning community has grown rapidly in recent years.
  - 在线学习社区近年来发展迅速。
- **助记**：commun（共同）+ ity → 共同生活的群体 → 社区

### 5. **treasure** /ˈtreʒə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 财宝；珍宝；v. 珍惜；珍视
- **英文释义**：n. a collection of valuable things; v. to value something very much
- **例句**：
  - The museum has many national treasures on display.
  - 博物馆展出了许多国宝。
  - I treasure the friendship we have built over the years.
  - 我珍惜我们多年来建立的友谊。
- **助记**：treas（储藏）+ ure → 储藏的珍贵物品 → 财宝

### 6. **perform** /pəˈfɔːm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：表演；执行；表现
- **英文释义**：to do something in front of an audience; to carry out a task
- **例句**：
  - The students will perform a play at the school festival.
  - 学生们将在校园节日上表演一场话剧。
  - The new employee performed very well in his first month.
  - 新员工在第一个月表现得非常好。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ form（形式）→ 完整地展现形式 → 表演；名词：performance

### 7. **satisfy** /ˈsætɪsfaɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使满意；满足
- **英文释义**：to make someone feel pleased by giving them what they want or need
- **例句**：
  - The answer didn't satisfy the teacher — she asked for more details.
  - 这个回答没有让老师满意——她要求更多细节。
  - Nothing can satisfy his curiosity about science.
  - 没有什么能满足他对科学的好奇心。
- **助记**：satis（足够）+ fy（使...）→ 使足够 → 满足；形容词：satisfied（感到满意的）/ satisfying（令人满意的）

### 8. **attitude** /ˈætɪtjuːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：态度；看法
- **英文释义**：the way you think and feel about something or someone
- **例句**：
  - A positive attitude can help you deal with difficulties.
  - 积极的态度可以帮助你应对困难。
  - What's your attitude towards learning a foreign language?
  - 你对学习外语的态度是什么？
- **助记**：attit（姿势）+ ude → 面对事物的姿势 → 态度；固定搭配：attitude towards/to

### 9. **operate** /ˈɒpəreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：操作；运转；做手术
- **英文释义**：to use and control a machine; to work or function; to perform surgery
- **例句**：
  - Do you know how to operate this washing machine?
  - 你知道怎么操作这台洗衣机吗？
  - The doctor operated on the patient for three hours.
  - 医生给病人做了三个小时的手术。
- **助记**：oper（工作）+ ate → 使工作 → 操作；名词：operation

### 10. **mystery** /ˈmɪstri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：神秘；谜；神秘的事物
- **英文释义**：something that is difficult to understand or explain
- **例句**：
  - The disappearance of the dinosaurs is still a mystery.
  - 恐龙的消失至今仍是一个谜。
  - She loves reading mystery novels in her free time.
  - 她喜欢在空闲时间阅读悬疑小说。
- **助记**：myster（神秘的）+ y → 神秘的事物 → 谜；形容词：mysterious（神秘的）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **anticipate** /ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：预料；期望；预先准备
- **英文释义**：to expect something to happen and prepare for it
- **例句**：
  - We didn't anticipate that so many people would come to the event.
  - 我们没有预料到会有这么多人来参加活动。
  - It's important to anticipate possible problems before they happen.
  - 在问题发生之前预料到可能的问题是很重要的。
- **助记**：anti（之前）+ cip（拿取）+ ate → 提前拿取/准备 → 预料；名词：anticipation

### 12. **competent** /ˈkɒmpɪtənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有能力的；胜任的；合格的
- **英文释义**：having enough skill or knowledge to do something well
- **例句**：
  - She is a competent teacher who can handle any classroom situation.
  - 她是一位有能力的老师，能处理任何课堂情况。
  - You need to be competent in both English and math to apply for this program.
  - 你需要在英语和数学方面都合格才能申请这个项目。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pet（追求）+ ent → 能跟上大家追求的水平 → 胜任的；名词：competence

### 13. **enhance** /ɪnˈhɑːns/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：提高；增强；增进
- **英文释义**：to improve the quality, value, or extent of something
- **例句**：
  - Technology can enhance the learning experience in the classroom.
  - 技术可以提升课堂的学习体验。
  - Regular reading will enhance your vocabulary greatly.
  - 经常阅读会大大增加你的词汇量。
- **助记**：en（使...）+ hance（提高）→ 使提高 → 增强；名词：enhancement

### 14. **integrate** /ˈɪntɪɡreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：整合；融合；使融入
- **英文释义**：to combine two or more things to work together as a whole
- **例句**：
  - The school tries to integrate technology into everyday teaching.
  - 学校努力将技术整合到日常教学中。
  - It takes time for new students to integrate into the class.
  - 新同学融入班级需要时间。
- **助记**：integr（完整的）+ ate → 使完整 → 整合；名词：integration

### 15. **sophisticated** /səˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：复杂的；精密的；老练的
- **英文释义**：complicated and advanced; having a lot of experience and knowledge
- **例句**：
  - Modern smartphones use very sophisticated technology.
  - 现代智能手机使用非常精密的技术。
  - She is a sophisticated woman with great taste in art.
  - 她是一位有品味、见多识广的女性。
- **助记**：sophist（智者）+ icated → 像智者一样复杂/老练 → 精密的、老练的
