## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **comment** /ˈkɒment/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 评论；意见；v. 评论
- **英文释义**：n. something you say to give your opinion; v. to express an opinion
- **例句**：
  - The teacher wrote some helpful comments on my essay.
  - 老师在我的作文上写了一些有用的评语。
  - Many people commented on the news article online.
  - 很多人在网上对这篇新闻报道发表了评论。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ ment（心智）→ 共同交流想法 → 评论

### 2. **complete** /kəmˈpliːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./v.
- **中文释义**：adj. 完整的；彻底的；v. 完成
- **英文释义**：adj. having all parts; v. to finish something
- **例句**：
  - Please complete the form and hand it in to the office.
  - 请填完表格并交到办公室。
  - The renovation of the school building is now complete.
  - 学校大楼的翻新现在已经完成了。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ plete（填满）→ 完全填满 → 完成/完整；副词：completely

### 3. **confuse** /kənˈfjuːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使困惑；使混淆
- **英文释义**：to make someone unable to think clearly or understand something
- **例句**：
  - The similar names of the two streets confused me.
  - 两条街道相似的名字把我搞混了。
  - Don't confuse "affect" with "effect" — they have different meanings.
  - 不要把"affect"和"effect"混淆——它们含义不同。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ fuse（倒/混合）→ 混在一起 → 混淆；形容词：confused（感到困惑的）/ confusing（令人困惑的）

### 4. **culture** /ˈkʌltʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：文化；文明
- **英文释义**：the customs, beliefs, art, and way of life of a particular group of people
- **例句**：
  - Chinese culture has a history of more than five thousand years.
  - 中国文化有五千多年的历史。
  - Learning about different cultures can broaden your horizons.
  - 了解不同的文化可以开阔你的眼界。
- **助记**：cult（培养/耕种）+ ure → 培养出来的精神成果 → 文化；形容词：cultural

### 5. **educate** /ˈedʒukeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：教育；培养
- **英文释义**：to teach someone, especially at a school or university
- **例句**：
  - Parents and teachers should work together to educate children.
  - 家长和老师应该共同努力教育孩子。
  - The program aims to educate the public about healthy eating.
  - 该项目旨在教育公众健康饮食。
- **助记**：e（出）+ duc（引导）+ ate → 引导出来 → 教育；名词：education

### 6. **generation** /ˌdʒenəˈreɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：一代人；产生
- **英文释义**：all people born and living at about the same time; the production of something
- **例句**：
  - The younger generation is more familiar with technology.
  - 年轻一代对科技更加熟悉。
  - Three generations of my family live in the same city.
  - 我家三代人都住在同一座城市。
- **助记**：gener（产生）+ ation → 产生新的一代 → 一代人

### 7. **ancient** /ˈeɪnʃənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：古老的；古代的
- **英文释义**：belonging to a period of history that is thousands of years in the past
- **例句**：
  - The Great Wall is an ancient wonder of the world.
  - 长城是世界古代奇迹。
  - Ancient China made great contributions to science and culture.
  - 古代中国对科学和文化做出了巨大贡献。
- **助记**：anci（在...之前）+ ent → 在很久之前的 → 古老的；反义词：modern

### 8. **native** /ˈneɪtɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：adj. 本地的；母语的；n. 本地人
- **英文释义**：adj. relating to the place where you were born; n. a person born in a particular place
- **例句**：
  - English is not his native language — he learned it at school.
  - 英语不是他的母语——他是在学校学的。
  - She is a native of Beijing and knows the city very well.
  - 她是北京本地人，对这座城市非常了解。
- **助记**：nat（出生）+ ive → 出生于此的 → 本地的；固定搭配：native speaker（母语使用者）

### 9. **possess** /pəˈzes/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：拥有；具有
- **英文释义**：to have or own something
- **例句**：
  - She possesses a talent for music and can play three instruments.
  - 她拥有音乐天赋，能演奏三种乐器。
  - Not everyone possesses the courage to speak in public.
  - 不是每个人都具有在公众面前讲话的勇气。
- **助记**：pos（能力）+ sess（坐/拥有）→ 坐拥 → 拥有；名词：possession

### 10. **repair** /rɪˈpeə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：v. 修理；修复；n. 修理
- **英文释义**：v. to fix something that is broken or damaged; n. the act of fixing
- **例句**：
  - He repaired the broken chair with some glue and nails.
  - 他用胶水和钉子修好了坏掉的椅子。
  - The road is under repair and will be closed until next week.
  - 这条路正在维修中，将关闭到下周。
- **助记**：re（重新）+ pair（准备）→ 重新准备好 → 修复

### 高一词汇

### 11. **abstract** /ˈæbstrækt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：抽象的；理论的
- **英文释义**：existing as an idea rather than something physical; not concrete
- **例句**：
  - Love and freedom are abstract concepts that are hard to define.
  - 爱和自由是很难定义的抽象概念。
  - Abstract art doesn't try to represent real objects.
  - 抽象艺术并不试图表现真实的物体。
- **助记**：abs（远离）+ tract（拉）→ 从具体事物中拉出来 → 抽象的；反义词：concrete（具体的）

### 12. **convention** /kənˈvenʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：惯例；会议；公约
- **英文释义**：a way of behaving that most people accept as normal; a large meeting
- **例句**：
  - It's a convention in many countries to shake hands when meeting someone.
  - 在很多国家，见面时握手是一种惯例。
  - An international convention on climate change was held in Paris.
  - 一次关于气候变化的国际大会在巴黎举行。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ vent（来）+ ion → 大家来到一起 → 大会/共识 → 惯例；形容词：conventional（传统的）

### 13. **exaggerate** /ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：夸张；夸大
- **英文释义**：to make something seem larger, better, or worse than it really is
- **例句**：
  - He always exaggerates when he tells stories.
  - 他讲故事时总是夸大其词。
  - Don't exaggerate the difficulty of the exam — it wasn't that hard.
  - 不要夸大考试的难度——没那么难。
- **助记**：ex（过度）+ agger（堆积）+ ate → 过度堆积 → 夸张；名词：exaggeration

### 14. **modest** /ˈmɒdɪst/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：谦虚的；适度的；不大的
- **英文释义**：not talking much about your abilities; not very large
- **例句**：
  - Despite his great achievements, he remains very modest.
  - 尽管取得了伟大的成就，他仍然非常谦虚。
  - They live in a modest house in the countryside.
  - 他们住在乡下一座不大的房子里。
- **助记**：mod（方式/节制）+ est → 有节制的 → 谦虚的；名词：modesty

### 15. **substantial** /səbˈstænʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：大量的；实质的；坚固的
- **英文释义**：large in amount or importance; real and significant
- **例句**：
  - She has made substantial progress in English this semester.
  - 她这学期在英语方面取得了很大的进步。
  - A substantial amount of money was donated to the charity.
  - 大量资金被捐赠给了慈善机构。
- **助记**：sub（在下面）+ stant（站立）+ ial → 站在下面支撑的 → 实质的、大量的；副词：substantially
