## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **compile** /kəmˈpaɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：编辑；汇编；收集
- **英文释义**：to collect information from different sources and arrange it in a book, list, or report
- **例句**：
  - She compiled a list of all the vocabulary words for the exam.
  - 她汇编了一份考试所有词汇的清单。
  - The researchers compiled data from over 100 studies.
  - 研究人员从100多项研究中收集了数据。
- **助记**：com（一起）+ pile（堆）→ 堆到一起 → 汇编；名词：compilation（汇编集）

### 2. **confiscate** /ˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：没收；充公
- **英文释义**：to officially take away someone's property, especially as a punishment
- **例句**：
  - The teacher confiscated the student's phone during class.
  - 老师在上课时没收了学生的手机。
  - Customs officers confiscated the illegal goods at the border.
  - 海关官员在边境没收了违禁品。
- **助记**：con（加强）+ fisc（国库）+ ate → 放入国库 → 没收；名词：confiscation（没收）

### 3. **deteriorate** /dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：恶化；变坏
- **英文释义**：to become worse in condition or quality
- **例句**：
  - His health began to deteriorate after months of overwork.
  - 几个月过度工作后，他的健康开始恶化。
  - The relationship between the two countries deteriorated rapidly.
  - 两国关系迅速恶化。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ terior（更坏）+ ate → 变得更坏 → 恶化；名词：deterioration（恶化）

### 4. **liberate** /ˈlɪbəreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：解放；释放
- **英文释义**：to set someone free from a situation in which their freedom is restricted
- **例句**：
  - The soldiers fought to liberate the city from enemy control.
  - 士兵们为从敌人控制中解放这座城市而战斗。
  - Education can liberate people from ignorance.
  - 教育可以把人们从无知中解放出来。
- **助记**：liber（自由）+ ate → 使自由 → 解放；名词：liberation（解放）

### 5. **apparatus** /ˌæpəˈreɪtəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：设备；器械；机构
- **英文释义**：the equipment or tools needed for a particular activity or task
- **例句**：
  - The laboratory is equipped with the latest scientific apparatus.
  - 实验室配备了最新的科学设备。
  - Firefighters wore breathing apparatus to enter the burning building.
  - 消防员戴着呼吸器进入燃烧的建筑物。
- **助记**：ap（准备）+ paratus（准备好的）→ 准备好的工具 → 设备

### 6. **dimension** /daɪˈmenʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：维度；尺寸；方面
- **英文释义**：a measurement of something in a particular direction; an aspect of a situation
- **例句**：
  - The room has the dimensions of 5 meters by 4 meters.
  - 这个房间的尺寸是5米乘4米。
  - This problem has an emotional dimension that we should consider.
  - 这个问题有一个情感方面我们应该考虑。
- **助记**：di（分开）+ mens（测量）+ ion → 分开测量 → 维度/尺寸

### 7. **eligible** /ˈelɪdʒəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有资格的；符合条件的
- **英文释义**：having the right to do or have something; meeting the requirements
- **例句**：
  - Students with a GPA above 3.5 are eligible for the scholarship.
  - GPA在3.5以上的学生有资格获得奖学金。
  - Only citizens over 18 are eligible to vote.
  - 只有18岁以上的公民才有资格投票。
- **助记**：e（出）+ lig（选择）+ ible → 可以被选出的 → 有资格的；名词：eligibility（资格）

### 8. **premium** /ˈpriːmiəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：保费；额外费用；优质的
- **英文释义**：an amount of money paid for insurance; of superior quality
- **例句**：
  - The insurance premium increased by 10% this year.
  - 今年保费增加了10%。
  - This brand offers premium quality products at higher prices.
  - 这个品牌以更高的价格提供优质产品。
- **助记**：premi（奖赏/第一）+ um → 第一等的 → 优质的/额外费用

### 9. **thereafter** /ˌðeərˈɑːftə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：此后；从那以后
- **英文释义**：after that; from that time on
- **例句**：
  - He graduated in 2020 and thereafter worked as an engineer.
  - 他于2020年毕业，此后担任工程师。
  - The company was founded in 1990, and thereafter grew rapidly.
  - 公司成立于1990年，此后迅速发展。
- **助记**：there（那里）+ after（之后）→ 在那之后 → 此后

### 10. **sanction** /ˈsæŋkʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：制裁；批准；认可
- **英文释义**：official permission or punishment imposed on a country or organization
- **例句**：
  - The United Nations imposed economic sanctions on the country.
  - 联合国对该国实施了经济制裁。
  - The plan was sanctioned by the school principal.
  - 该计划得到了校长的批准。
- **助记**：sanct（神圣）+ ion → 以神圣之名进行的 → 批准/制裁（双重含义）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **exacerbate** /ɪɡˈzæsəbeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：加剧；使恶化
- **英文释义**：to make a bad situation or problem even worse
- **例句**：
  - The drought was exacerbated by unusually high temperatures.
  - 异常高温加剧了干旱。
  - His angry response only exacerbated the conflict between them.
  - 他愤怒的回应只是加剧了他们之间的冲突。
- **助记**：ex（加强）+ acerb（尖刻）+ ate → 使更加尖刻 → 加剧；名词：exacerbation

### 12. **dichotomy** /daɪˈkɒtəmi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：二分法；对立面；截然不同
- **英文释义**：a division or contrast between two things that are opposite or very different
- **例句**：
  - There is a clear dichotomy between urban and rural life in China.
  - 在中国，城市和乡村生活之间存在明显的二元对立。
  - The dichotomy between theory and practice is a common challenge.
  - 理论与实践之间的对立是一个常见的挑战。
- **助记**：dicho（二）+ tomy（切）→ 切成两半 → 二分法

### 13. **extrapolate** /ɪkˈstræpəleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：推断；推知
- **英文释义**：to estimate or conclude something by extending known information
- **例句**：
  - Scientists extrapolated future climate trends from current data.
  - 科学家从当前数据推断出未来的气候趋势。
  - You cannot simply extrapolate these results to the entire population.
  - 你不能简单地将这些结果推断到整个人群。
- **助记**：extra（超出）+ polate（推）→ 超出已知范围推断 → 推断；名词：extrapolation

### 14. **salient** /ˈseɪliənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：显著的；突出的；重要的
- **英文释义**：most noticeable or important
- **例句**：
  - Let me highlight the salient points of the report.
  - 让我强调一下报告的重要之处。
  - The salient feature of this design is its simplicity.
  - 这个设计最显著的特点是它的简洁。
- **助记**：sali（跳）+ ent → 跳出来的 → 显著的；近义词：prominent, notable

### 15. **rudimentary** /ˌruːdɪˈmentri/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：初步的；基本的；未发展的
- **英文释义**：involving or limited to basic principles; very simple
- **例句**：
  - He has only a rudimentary knowledge of Chinese.
  - 他只有最基本的中文知识。
  - The rudimentary tools used by early humans were made of stone.
  - 早期人类使用的初级工具是用石头制成的。
- **助记**：rudi（基础的）+ mentary → 基础性的 → 初步的；名词：rudiment（基础；入门）
