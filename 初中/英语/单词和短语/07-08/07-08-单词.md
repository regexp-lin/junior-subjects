## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **slur** /slɜː(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：含糊不清地说；诽谤
- **英文释义**：to speak in an unclear way; to make a critical remark about someone
- **例句**：
  - The tired speaker slurred his words during the late-night presentation.
  - 疲惫的演讲者在深夜汇报时说话含糊不清。
  - The politician slurred his opponent's reputation in the debate.
  - 政客在辩论中诽谤对手的名誉。
- **助记**：slur → "死勒"（谐音）→ 舌头被勒住说不清 → 含糊地说

### 2. **clatter** /ˈklætə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：发出哗啦声；噼啪作响
- **英文释义**：to make a loud rattling noise when objects hit each other
- **例句**：
  - The dishes clattered to the floor when the tray tipped over.
  - 托盘打翻时，盘子哗啦一声掉在地上。
  - Hooves clattered on the cobblestone street.
  - 马蹄在鹅卵石街上得得作响。
- **助记**：clatter → "可拉特"（谐音）→ 可拉可拉特别响 → 哗啦作响

### 3. **tether** /ˈteðə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：拴住；束缚
- **英文释义**：to tie an animal to a post with a rope or chain; to restrict someone's freedom
- **例句**：
  - The farmer tethered the goat to a tree in the meadow.
  - 农夫把山羊拴在草地的一棵树上。
  - She felt tethered to her desk job and dreamed of traveling.
  - 她感觉被办公桌束缚住了，梦想着去旅行。
- **助记**：tether → "特德"（谐音）→ 特制的绳子拴住 → 拴住

### 4. **flinch** /flɪntʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：退缩；畏缩
- **英文释义**：to make a quick movement away from something because you are frightened or in pain
- **例句**：
  - He didn't flinch when the doctor gave him the injection.
  - 医生打针时他没有退缩。
  - She flinched at the sound of the explosion.
  - 爆炸声让她猛地一缩。
- **助记**：flinch → "拂灵驰"（谐音）→ 灵魂被拂动赶紧闪避 → 退缩

### 5. **talon** /ˈtælən/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：猛禽的爪；利爪
- **英文释义**：a sharp hooked claw of a bird of prey
- **例句**：
  - The eagle grasped the fish with its powerful talons.
  - 老鹰用强有力的利爪抓住了鱼。
  - The owl's talons are perfectly adapted for catching mice.
  - 猫头鹰的爪子非常适合抓老鼠。
- **助记**：talon → "塌龙"（谐音）→ 龙塌下来用爪抓 → 利爪

### 6. **gable** /ˈɡeɪbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：山墙；三角墙
- **英文释义**：the triangular upper part of a wall at the end of a ridged roof
- **例句**：
  - The old cottage had a red roof with white gables.
  - 那座老村舍有红色屋顶和白色山墙。
  - A weather vane stood on the gable of the barn.
  - 风向标立在谷仓的山墙上。
- **助记**：gable → "给伯"（谐音）→ 给房子盖个三角顶 → 山墙

### 7. **pelt** /pelt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：毛皮；动物的皮
- **英文释义**：the skin of an animal with the fur or hair still on it
- **例句**：
  - Traders used to exchange beaver pelts for goods.
  - 商人以前用海狸皮换货物。
  - The hunter collected the fox pelt to sell.
  - 猎人收集了狐狸皮去卖。
- **助记**：pelt → "皮特"（谐音）→ 皮的特别 → 毛皮

### 8. **gulp** /ɡʌlp/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：大口吞下；咽
- **英文释义**：to swallow large amounts of food or drink quickly
- **例句**：
  - He gulped down his breakfast and rushed to school.
  - 他大口吃完早餐就冲向学校。
  - She gulped when she heard the bad news.
  - 听到坏消息时她咽了口唾沫（紧张）。
- **助记**：gulp → "咕噜"（谐音）→ 咕噜一声吞下去 → 大口吞

### 9. **fortnight** /ˈfɔːtnaɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：两星期；十四天
- **英文释义**：a period of two weeks
- **例句**：
  - We'll be on holiday for a fortnight.
  - 我们要度假两个星期。
  - The project is due in a fortnight's time.
  - 项目两周后到期。
- **助记**：fortnight = fourteen nights → 十四个夜晚 → 两星期

### 10. **ramble** /ˈræmbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：漫步；漫谈
- **英文释义**：to walk for pleasure in the countryside; to talk or write in a confused way, going from one subject to another
- **例句**：
  - We rambled through the woods after school and found a secret stream.
  - 放学后我们在树林里漫步，发现了一条神秘的小溪。
  - Grandpa rambled on about his childhood stories during dinner.
  - 晚饭时爷爷漫谈到他童年的故事。
- **助记**：ramble → "漫步乐"（谐音）→ 漫步着很快乐 → 漫步；漫谈

### 高一词汇

### 11. **impersonate** /ɪmˈpɜːsəneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：冒充；模仿
- **英文释义**：to pretend to be another person, especially in order to deceive others; to imitate someone's appearance or behaviour
- **例句**：
  - The comedian impersonated our principal at the talent show and made everyone laugh.
  - 喜剧演员在才艺表演上模仿我们校长，把大家都逗笑了。
  - Never impersonate a teacher to skip class — it's against school rules.
  - 千万别冒充老师请假逃课——这违反校规。
- **助记**：im-（进入）+ person（人）+ -ate → 进入另一个人的角色 → 冒充；模仿

### 12. **oscillate** /ˈɒsɪleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：摇摆；犹豫
- **英文释义**：to move repeatedly from one side to another; to keep changing between two beliefs or feelings
- **例句**：
  - The pendulum oscillated back and forth in the physics lab.
  - 物理实验室里的摆锤来回摇摆。
  - I oscillated between choosing art club and robotics club for weeks.
  - 我在选美术社还是机器人社之间犹豫了好几周。
- **助记**：oscillate → "哦，思来忑"（谐音）→ 哦，思来想去忐忑不定 → 摇摆；犹豫

### 13. **rapturous** /ˈræptʃərəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：狂喜的；欣喜若狂的
- **英文释义**：feeling or expressing great pleasure or enthusiasm
- **例句**：
  - The fans gave a rapturous welcome when the band came on stage.
  - 乐队登上舞台时，歌迷们报以狂喜的欢呼。
  - We received rapturous applause after our class play performance.
  - 我们班的戏剧表演结束后，赢得了欣喜若狂的掌声。
- **助记**：raptur（rapture 狂喜）+ -ous（形容词后缀）→ 充满狂喜的 → 狂喜的；欣喜若狂的

### 14. **sanctimonious** /ˌsæŋktɪˈməʊniəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：假装圣洁的；道貌岸然的
- **英文释义**：making a show of being morally superior to other people
- **例句**：
  - His sanctimonious lectures about charity annoyed everyone.
  - 他关于慈善的道貌岸然的说教惹恼了所有人。
  - She gave a sanctimonious smile and refused to help.
  - 她露出虚伪的圣洁微笑，拒绝帮忙。
- **助记**：sanct（神圣）+ -imonious → 假扮神圣的 → 道貌岸然

### 15. **oligarchy** /ˈɒlɪɡɑːki/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：寡头统治；寡头政治
- **英文释义**：a form of government in which power is held by a small group of people
- **例句**：
  - The country was ruled by an oligarchy of wealthy families.
  - 这个国家由几个富有的家族寡头统治。
  - Critics say the tech industry has become an oligarchy.
  - 批评者称科技行业已成为寡头垄断。
- **助记**：olig-（少）+ archy（统治）→ 少数人统治 → 寡头政治
