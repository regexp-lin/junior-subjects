## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **donate** /dəʊˈneɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：捐赠；捐献
- **英文释义**：to give money, food, or other things to help a person or organisation
- **例句**：
  - Many people donated money to the earthquake relief fund.
  - 许多人向地震救灾基金捐了款。
  - She donated blood at the hospital last weekend.
  - 她上周末在医院献了血。
- **助记**：don（给予）+ -ate → 给予 → 捐赠

### 2. **dormant** /ˈdɔːmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：休眠的；静止的
- **英文释义**：not active or growing now but able to become active later
- **例句**：
  - The volcano has been dormant for over a hundred years.
  - 这座火山已经休眠了一百多年。
  - Many plants remain dormant during winter.
  - 许多植物在冬季处于休眠状态。
- **助记**：dorm（睡觉）+ -ant → 在睡觉的 → 休眠的

### 3. **dose** /dəʊs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：剂量；一剂
- **英文释义**：a measured amount of medicine taken at one time
- **例句**：
  - The doctor prescribed a daily dose of vitamins.
  - 医生开了每日一剂维生素。
  - Don't take more than the recommended dose.
  - 不要超过推荐剂量服用。
- **助记**：dose → "多少"（谐音）→ 吃药吃多少 → 剂量

### 4. **downfall** /ˈdaʊnfɔːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：衰败；垮台
- **英文释义**：a loss of power, prosperity, or status; the cause of ruin
- **例句**：
  - Greed was the downfall of the once-powerful company.
  - 贪婪导致了这家曾经强大的公司的垮台。
  - His arrogance led to his downfall.
  - 他的傲慢导致了他的衰败。
- **助记**：down（向下）+ fall（掉落）→ 往下掉 → 衰败

### 5. **drastic** /ˈdræstɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：剧烈的；激进的
- **英文释义**：likely to have a strong or far-reaching effect; extreme
- **例句**：
  - The school took drastic measures to prevent cheating.
  - 学校采取了激进的措施来防止作弊。
  - There has been a drastic change in the weather this week.
  - 这周天气发生了剧烈变化。
- **助记**：drastic → "追死他"（谐音）→ 追得很猛烈 → 剧烈的

### 6. **drift** /drɪft/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：漂流；漫无目的地移动
- **英文释义**：to be carried slowly by water or air; to move aimlessly
- **例句**：
  - The boat drifted along the river without anyone steering.
  - 小船无人掌舵，沿着河流漂流。
  - After graduation, he drifted from job to job.
  - 毕业后他漫无目的地换工作。
- **助记**：drift → "追浮特"（谐音）→ 追着浮在水上的东西 → 漂流

### 7. **drought** /draʊt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：干旱；旱灾
- **英文释义**：a long period of time with little or no rain
- **例句**：
  - The drought destroyed most of the crops this summer.
  - 今年夏天的干旱毁掉了大部分庄稼。
  - Many animals died during the severe drought.
  - 许多动物在严重旱灾中死亡。
- **助记**：drought → dry（干）的名词形式 → 干旱

### 8. **durable** /ˈdjʊərəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：耐用的；持久的
- **英文释义**：able to last a long time without becoming damaged
- **例句**：
  - This backpack is made of durable material and can last for years.
  - 这个背包用耐用材料制成，可以用好几年。
  - We need a durable solution, not a temporary fix.
  - 我们需要一个持久的解决方案，而不是临时修补。
- **助记**：dur（持续）+ -able（能…的）→ 能持续的 → 耐用的

### 9. **dwarf** /dwɔːf/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：矮人；侏儒
- **英文释义**：a person, animal, or plant much smaller than the usual size; a mythical small humanlike creature
- **例句**：
  - Snow White lived with seven dwarfs in the forest.
  - 白雪公主和七个小矮人住在森林里。
  - The ancient oak dwarfed all the other trees nearby.
  - 那棵古老的橡树使附近所有的树都显得矮小。
- **助记**：dwarf → "多矮夫"（谐音）→ 多矮的人啊 → 矮人

### 10. **dwell** /dwel/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：居住；细想
- **英文释义**：to live in a particular place; to think about something for a long time
- **例句**：
  - Many rare species dwell in the deep rainforest.
  - 许多珍稀物种居住在深处的雨林中。
  - Don't dwell on past mistakes — focus on the future.
  - 不要老是纠结过去的错误——关注未来。
- **助记**：dwell → "得喔"（谐音）→ 得在这里住 → 居住

### 高一词汇

### 11. **disparate** /ˈdɪspərət/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：截然不同的；无法比较的
- **英文释义**：essentially different in kind; not able to be compared
- **例句**：
  - The two sisters have disparate personalities — one is shy, the other outgoing.
  - 两姐妹性格截然不同——一个害羞，一个外向。
  - They tried to unite disparate groups under one cause.
  - 他们试图把截然不同的群体团结在一个目标下。
- **助记**：dis-（分开）+ par（相等）+ -ate → 不相等的 → 截然不同的

### 12. **disseminate** /dɪˈsemɪneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：传播；散布
- **英文释义**：to spread information, knowledge, or ideas widely
- **例句**：
  - The internet helps disseminate news around the world in seconds.
  - 互联网帮助将新闻在几秒内传播到全世界。
  - Teachers disseminate knowledge to students every day.
  - 老师每天向学生传播知识。
- **助记**：dis-（分散）+ semin（种子）+ -ate → 把种子撒开 → 传播

### 13. **dissipate** /ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：消散；挥霍
- **英文释义**：to disappear or cause to disappear; to waste resources
- **例句**：
  - The morning fog dissipated as the sun rose.
  - 早晨的雾随着太阳升起而消散了。
  - He dissipated his fortune on gambling.
  - 他把财产挥霍在赌博上。
- **助记**：dis-（分散）+ sip（吸）+ -ate → 被吸散了 → 消散

### 14. **dogmatic** /dɒɡˈmætɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：武断的；教条的
- **英文释义**：inclined to lay down principles as undeniably true without consideration of evidence
- **例句**：
  - His dogmatic attitude makes it hard to have a discussion with him.
  - 他武断的态度让人很难和他讨论。
  - A good scientist should never be dogmatic.
  - 好的科学家不应该是教条的。
- **助记**：dogma（教条）+ -tic → 像教条一样的 → 武断的

### 15. **dubious** /ˈdjuːbiəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：可疑的；不确定的
- **英文释义**：hesitating or doubtful; not to be relied upon
- **例句**：
  - The source of the information is dubious — don't trust it.
  - 这个信息的来源很可疑——别轻信。
  - I'm dubious about whether the plan will work.
  - 我对这个计划能否成功表示怀疑。
- **助记**：dub（二）+ -ious → 内心有"二"，拿不准 → 可疑的
