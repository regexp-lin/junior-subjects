## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **passion** /ˈpæʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：热情；激情
- **英文释义**：a very strong feeling of enthusiasm or excitement
- **例句**：
  - She has a passion for music.
  - 她对音乐充满热情。
  - He spoke with great passion about the project.
  - 他充满激情地谈论这个项目。
- **助记**：pass（承受）+ -ion → 内心承受着强烈的情感 → 热情；激情

### 2. **passive** /ˈpæsɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：被动的；消极的
- **英文释义**：accepting what happens without active response or resistance
- **例句**：
  - He took a passive role in the discussion.
  - 他在讨论中扮演了被动角色。
  - Passive smoking can also harm your health.
  - 被动吸烟也会损害健康。
- **助记**：pass（承受）+ -ive → 只是承受而不行动 → 被动的

### 3. **patience** /ˈpeɪʃns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：耐心；忍耐
- **英文释义**：the ability to wait calmly or accept difficulties without complaining
- **例句**：
  - Teaching children requires a lot of patience.
  - 教育孩子需要很多耐心。
  - My patience is running out.
  - 我的耐心快耗尽了。
- **助记**：pati（承受）+ -ence → 承受的能力 → 耐心

### 4. **pattern** /ˈpætn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：模式；图案
- **英文释义**：a repeated design or arrangement; a regular way something happens
- **例句**：
  - The wallpaper has a floral pattern.
  - 壁纸上有花卉图案。
  - There is a clear pattern in the data.
  - 数据中有一个明显的模式。
- **助记**：patr（父亲）+ -ern → 父辈传下来的范式 → 模式；图案

### 5. **pause** /pɔːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：暂停；停顿
- **英文释义**：to stop temporarily; a short period of time when something stops
- **例句**：
  - She paused before answering the question.
  - 她在回答问题前停顿了一下。
  - There was a long pause in the conversation.
  - 对话中有一段长时间的停顿。
- **助记**：paus（停止）+ -e → 暂时停止 → 暂停

### 6. **peak** /piːk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：顶峰；高峰期的
- **英文释义**：the highest point or level; at the highest level
- **例句**：
  - The mountain peak was covered in snow.
  - 山顶被雪覆盖。
  - Traffic is worst during peak hours.
  - 高峰时段交通最拥堵。
- **助记**：peak 与 pike（尖的）同源 → 最尖的顶端 → 顶峰

### 7. **penalty** /ˈpenəlti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：处罚；罚款
- **英文释义**：a punishment for breaking a law or rule
- **例句**：
  - The penalty for speeding is a fine.
  - 超速的处罚是罚款。
  - The team was given a penalty kick.
  - 球队获得了一个罚球。
- **助记**：pen（惩罚）+ -alty → 惩罚的结果 → 处罚

### 8. **pension** /ˈpenʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：养老金；退休金
- **英文释义**：money paid regularly to a retired person
- **例句**：
  - She lives on a small pension.
  - 她靠微薄的养老金生活。
  - The government plans to increase pensions.
  - 政府计划提高养老金。
- **助记**：pens（支付）+ -ion → 定期支付的款项 → 养老金

### 9. **perceive** /pəˈsiːv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：感知；认为
- **英文释义**：to notice or become aware of something; to think of something in a particular way
- **例句**：
  - She perceived a change in his attitude.
  - 她察觉到他态度的变化。
  - The proposal was perceived as unfair.
  - 这项提议被认为是不公平的。
- **助记**：per（完全）+ ceive（抓住）→ 完全抓住信息 → 感知

### 10. **percentage** /pəˈsentɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：百分比；比例
- **英文释义**：an amount expressed as a part of a hundred
- **例句**：
  - A high percentage of students passed the test.
  - 很高比例的学生通过了考试。
  - What percentage of the budget goes to education?
  - 预算中有多少比例用于教育？
- **助记**：per（每）+ cent（百）+ -age → 每一百中所占的量 → 百分比

### 高一词汇

### 11. **recluse** /rɪˈkluːs/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：隐士；隐居者
- **英文释义**：a person who lives alone and avoids other people
- **例句**：
  - The famous author became a recluse in his later years.
  - 这位著名作家晚年成了隐居者。
  - She lives like a recluse in the mountains.
  - 她像隐士一样住在山里。
- **助记**：re（回）+ cluse（关闭）→ 把自己关回去 → 隐居者

### 12. **redundant** /rɪˈdʌndənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：多余的；被裁减的
- **英文释义**：not needed; no longer employed because the job is no longer necessary
- **例句**：
  - Remove any redundant words from your essay.
  - 删除文章中多余的词语。
  - Many workers were made redundant after the merger.
  - 合并后很多工人被裁。
- **助记**：re（反复）+ dund（涌）+ -ant → 反复涌出、多到溢出 → 多余的

### 13. **relinquish** /rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：放弃；交出
- **英文释义**：to give up something, especially unwillingly
- **例句**：
  - He was forced to relinquish control of the company.
  - 他被迫交出公司的控制权。
  - She refused to relinquish her dream.
  - 她拒绝放弃自己的梦想。
- **助记**：re（回）+ linqu（留下）+ -ish → 把东西留回去 → 放弃；交出

### 14. **reminiscent** /ˌremɪˈnɪsnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：令人回忆的；使人联想到的
- **英文释义**：reminding you of something; similar to something in the past
- **例句**：
  - The song is reminiscent of the 1980s.
  - 这首歌让人想起80年代。
  - Her painting style is reminiscent of Monet.
  - 她的画风让人联想到莫奈。
- **助记**：re（再）+ min（记忆）+ -iscent → 再次引起记忆的 → 令人回忆的

### 15. **repercussion** /ˌriːpəˈkʌʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：反响；后果
- **英文释义**：an indirect and usually bad result of an action or event
- **例句**：
  - The decision had serious repercussions for the community.
  - 这个决定对社区产生了严重后果。
  - He didn't consider the repercussions of his words.
  - 他没有考虑自己话语的后果。
- **助记**：re（回）+ per（贯穿）+ cuss（撞击）+ -ion → 撞击反弹回来 → 反响；后果
