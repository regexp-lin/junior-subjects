## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **appliance** /əˈplaɪəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：电器；器械；用具
- **英文释义**：a device or machine for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - Modern kitchens are full of electrical appliances.
  - 现代厨房里满是电器。
  - The hospital has the latest medical appliances.
  - 医院拥有最新的医疗器械。
- **助记**：ap（向）+ pli（应用）+ ance → 应用于某用途的装置 → 电器

### 2. **applicant** /ˈæplɪkənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：申请人；求职者
- **英文释义**：a person who applies for something
- **例句**：
  - There were over 100 applicants for the job.
  - 有一百多人申请这份工作。
  - Successful applicants will be notified by email.
  - 申请成功者将通过邮件通知。
- **助记**：applic（申请）+ ant（人）→ 申请的人 → 申请人

### 3. **application** /ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：申请；应用；申请表
- **英文释义**：a formal request; the practical use of something
- **例句**：
  - Please submit your application before the deadline.
  - 请在截止日期前提交申请。
  - The application of new technology has improved efficiency.
  - 新技术的应用提高了效率。
- **助记**：applic（应用、申请）+ ation → 申请；应用

### 4. **appoint** /əˈpɔɪnt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：任命；指定；约定
- **英文释义**：to choose someone for a job; to fix a time or place
- **例句**：
  - She was appointed as the new manager.
  - 她被任命为新经理。
  - They appointed a time for the meeting.
  - 他们约定了会议时间。
- **助记**：ap（向）+ point（点）→ 指向某人/某时 → 任命；指定

### 5. **appointment** /əˈpɔɪntmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：约会；任命；职位
- **英文释义**：an arrangement to meet; the act of appointing; a job position
- **例句**：
  - I have a dentist appointment at 3 pm.
  - 我下午三点有牙医预约。
  - His appointment as CEO was announced yesterday.
  - 他出任CEO的消息昨天公布了。
- **助记**：appoint + ment →  appointments

### 6. **appreciation** /əˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：欣赏；感激；升值
- **英文释义**：recognition of value; gratitude; increase in value
- **例句**：
  - I'd like to express my appreciation for your help.
  - 我想表达对你帮助的感激。
  - She has a keen appreciation of art.
  - 她对艺术有敏锐的欣赏力。
- **助记**：ap（向）+ preci（价值）+ ation → 认识到价值 → 欣赏；感激

### 7. **approval** /əˈpruːvl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：批准；赞同
- **英文释义**：official agreement; positive opinion
- **例句**：
  - The plan needs approval from the board.
  - 该计划需要董事会批准。
  - She nodded in approval of his idea.
  - 她点头表示赞同他的想法。
- **助记**：approv（e）（赞同）+ al →  approval

### 8. **approve** /əˈpruːv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：批准；赞成
- **英文释义**：to officially agree to; to think something is good
- **例句**：
  - The committee approved the proposal.
  - 委员会批准了提案。
  - His parents don't approve of his girlfriend.
  - 他父母不赞成他的女朋友。
- **助记**：ap（向）+ prove（证明）→ 证明为可行 → 批准

### 9. **approximately** /əˈprɒksɪmətli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：大约；近似地
- **英文释义**：close to the exact amount or number
- **例句**：
  - The journey takes approximately two hours.
  - 旅程大约需要两小时。
  - Approximately 50 people attended the meeting.
  - 大约有50人参加了会议。
- **助记**：approxim（ate）（接近）+ ly →  approximately

### 10. **arena** /əˈriːnə/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：竞技场；活动领域
- **英文释义**：a place for sports or entertainment; a field of activity
- **例句**：
  - The concert was held in a large arena.
  - 演唱会在一个大型场馆举行。
  - She has dominated the political arena for years.
  - 她多年来主导政坛。
- **助记**：arena 源自拉丁语"沙地"→ 角斗场的沙地 → 竞技场

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ascetic** /əˈsetɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：苦行的；禁欲的；苦行者
- **英文释义**：practicing strict self-denial; a person who lives austerely
- **例句**：
  - The monk led an ascetic life in the mountains.
  - 僧人在山中过着苦修生活。
  - Ascetics reject worldly pleasures.
  - 苦行者摒弃世俗享乐。
- **助记**：ascet（练习）+ ic → 苦练修行 → 苦行的

### 12. **ascribe** /əˈskraɪb/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：归因于；归功于
- **英文释义**：to attribute something to a cause or source
- **例句**：
  - He ascribed his success to hard work.
  - 他把成功归因于努力。
  - The poem is usually ascribed to Shakespeare.
  - 这首诗通常被认为出自莎士比亚之手。
- **助记**：a（向）+ scribe（写）→ 写归于 → 归因于

### 13. **asseverate** /əˈsevəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：郑重声明；断言
- **英文释义**：to declare solemnly or emphatically
- **例句**：
  - He asseverated his innocence before the court.
  - 他在法庭上郑重声明自己无罪。
  - The witness asseverated that he had seen the accused.
  - 证人断言他见到了被告。
- **助记**：as（加强）+ sever（严肃）+ ate → 严肃地说 → 断言

### 14. **atrophy** /ˈætrəfi/ `高一`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：萎缩；衰退
- **英文释义**：to waste away; gradual loss of size or function
- **例句**：
  - Muscles can atrophy without exercise.
  - 肌肉不锻炼会萎缩。
  - The atrophy of the industry has led to job losses.
  - 行业的衰退导致了失业。
- **助记**：a（无）+ troph（营养）+ y → 缺乏营养 → 萎缩

### 15. **attrition** /əˈtrɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：损耗；消耗；减员
- **英文释义**：the process of gradually reducing strength through sustained pressure
- **例句**：
  - The war was won by attrition rather than decisive battles.
  - 战争是靠消耗战而非决战获胜的。
  - Staff attrition has been high this year.
  - 今年员工流失率很高。
- **助记**：at（向）+ trit（摩擦）+ ion → 摩擦消耗 →  attrition
