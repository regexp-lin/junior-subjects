## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **prod** /prɒd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：戳；刺激；催促
- **英文释义**：to push or poke someone or something with your finger or a pointed object; to encourage or remind someone to do something
- **例句**：
  - He prodded his friend with his elbow to get his attention.
  - 他用胳膊肘捅了朋友一下引起注意。
  - My mother prodded me to finish my homework before dinner.
  - 妈妈催促我在晚饭前完成作业。
- **助记**：prod → "扑弱得"（谐音）→ 扑过去弱弱地捅一下 → 戳；催促

### 2. **snare** /sneə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：诱捕；设陷阱
- **英文释义**：to catch an animal using a snare (a trap); to trick or trap someone
- **例句**：
  - The hunters snare rabbits in the forest.
  - 猎人们在森林里设陷阱捉兔子。
  - She felt snared by the online scam and lost her money.
  - 她觉得被网络诈骗诱骗了，损失了钱。
- **助记**：snare → "死奶"（谐音）→ 陷阱里死掉的奶（动物）→ 诱捕；设陷阱

### 3. **wring** /rɪŋ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：拧；绞；扭
- **英文释义**：to twist and squeeze something, especially to remove liquid from it
- **例句**：
  - She wrung the wet towel to remove the water.
  - 她拧干湿毛巾把水挤出来。
  - He wrung his hands nervously before the interview.
  - 面试前他紧张地搓着手。
- **助记**：wring → "拧"（谐音相近）→ 直接联想拧毛巾 → 拧；绞

### 4. **trek** /trek/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：长途跋涉
- **英文释义**：to make a long and difficult journey, especially on foot
- **例句**：
  - We trekked for three hours to reach the mountain top.
  - 我们长途跋涉三小时才到达山顶。
  - The explorers trekked through the jungle for days.
  - 探险家们在丛林中跋涉了好几天。
- **助记**：trek → "踹磕"（谐音）→ 一路踹着磕磕绊绊走 → 长途跋涉

### 5. **rubble** /ˈrʌbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：碎石；瓦砾
- **英文释义**：broken stones or bricks from a building that has been destroyed
- **例句**：
  - After the earthquake, the street was full of rubble.
  - 地震后街上到处都是瓦砾。
  - Workers cleared the rubble from the collapsed building.
  - 工人们清除了倒塌建筑的碎石。
- **助记**：rubble → " rubble ≈ rub（摩擦）+ ble" → 摩擦碎掉的东西 → 碎石；瓦砾

### 6. **knoll** /nəʊl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：小丘；圆丘
- **英文释义**：a small round hill
- **例句**：
  - We sat on the knoll and watched the sunset.
  - 我们坐在小丘上欣赏日落。
  - The sheep grazed on the green knoll.
  - 羊群在绿色的小丘上吃草。
- **助记**：knoll → "闹"（谐音）→ 小丘上风景好，人多热闹 → 小丘；圆丘

### 7. **brine** /braɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：盐水；海水
- **英文释义**：water containing a lot of salt, used especially for preserving food
- **例句**：
  - The pickles are preserved in brine.
  - 腌菜泡在盐水里保存。
  - Brine from the sea can be used to make salt.
  - 海水可以用来制盐。
- **助记**：brine → "不软"（谐音）→ 盐水泡过的东西不会软 → 盐水；海水

### 8. **stark** /stɑːk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：荒凉的；完全的
- **英文释义**：empty, plain, and often cold or depressing; complete and total
- **例句**：
  - The stark landscape had no trees or plants.
  - 荒凉的景色里没有任何树木或植物。
  - There was a stark contrast between his words and his actions.
  - 他的言行形成了鲜明对比。
- **助记**：stark → "死大刻"（谐音）→ 死寂般大而刻板的 → 荒凉的；完全的

### 9. **tepid** /ˈtepɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：温吞的；不热情的
- **英文释义**：slightly warm; not enthusiastic or interested
- **例句**：
  - The tea was tepid and not very enjoyable.
  - 茶温吞吞的，不太好喝。
  - The audience gave only tepid applause to the performance.
  - 观众对演出只报以冷淡的掌声。
- **助记**：tepid → "特皮的"（谐音）→ 特别皮、不冷不热 → 温吞的；不热情的

### 10. **thereupon** /ˌðeərəˈpɒn/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：于是；随即
- **英文释义**：immediately after that; as a result of that
- **例句**：
  - The bell rang, and thereupon the students rushed out of the classroom.
  - 铃声响了，于是学生们冲出了教室。
  - He agreed to help, and thereupon we began our work.
  - 他同意帮忙，随即我们开始了工作。
- **助记**：there（那）+ upon（在……上）→ 在那之后紧接着 → 于是；随即

### 高一词汇

### 11. **usurp** /juːˈzɜːp/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：篡夺；霸占
- **英文释义**：to take someone's position, power, or rights illegally or unfairly
- **例句**：
  - The general usurped the throne and became king.
  - 将军篡夺了王位成为国王。
  - He felt that his younger brother had usurped his parents' attention.
  - 他觉得弟弟霸占了父母对自己的关注。
- **助记**：usurp → " you + serp" → 你强夺（serpent 蛇般狡猾地夺）→ 篡夺；霸占

### 12. **venerate** /ˈvenəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：崇敬；尊敬
- **英文释义**：to have or show great respect for someone, especially because of age, wisdom, or holiness
- **例句**：
  - Students venerate their wise and kind teachers.
  - 学生们崇敬智慧而善良的老师。
  - Many people venerate the heroes who sacrificed for the country.
  - 许多人崇敬为国捐躯的英雄。
- **助记**：vener-（拉丁：尊敬）+ -ate → 尊敬 → 崇敬（联想 Venus 维纳斯女神）

### 13. **esoteric** /ˌesəˈterɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：深奥的；难懂的
- **英文释义**：understood by only a small number of people with special knowledge
- **例句**：
  - The professor's lecture was full of esoteric references.
  - 教授的讲座充满了深奥的引用。
  - Quantum physics seems esoteric to most beginners.
  - 量子物理对大多数初学者来说似乎很难懂。
- **助记**：esoteric → "爱搜铁力"（谐音）→ 爱搜铁力才能懂 → 深奥的；难懂的

### 14. **unequivocal** /ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：明确的；毫不含糊的
- **英文释义**：clear and having only one possible meaning; leaving no doubt
- **例句**：
  - The principal gave an unequivocal answer: no phones in class.
  - 校长给出了明确的答复：课堂上不许带手机。
  - Her unequivocal support encouraged him to keep trying.
  - 她毫不含糊的支持鼓励他继续努力。
- **助记**：un-（不）+ equivocal（模棱两可的）→ 不模棱两可 → 明确的

### 15. **quandary** /ˈkwɒndəri/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：困境；进退两难
- **英文释义**：a difficult situation in which you have to choose between different options
- **例句**：
  - She was in a quandary about whether to attend the party or study for the exam.
  - 她进退两难：是去参加派对还是备考。
  - The team faced a quandary: accept the loss or risk everything.
  - 团队面临困境：认输还是放手一搏。
- **助记**：quandary → "筐得累"（谐音）→ 背着一筐问题累得进退两难 → 困境
