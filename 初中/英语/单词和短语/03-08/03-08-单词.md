## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **succeed** /səkˈsiːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：成功；继任
- **英文释义**：to achieve what you are trying to do; to do well
- **例句**：
  - If you work hard, you will succeed in the end.
  - 如果你努力，最终会成功的。
  - She succeeded in passing the driving test on her first try.
  - 她第一次就成功通过了驾照考试。
- **助记**：suc（在下面）+ ceed（走）→ 跟在后面走到终点 → 成功；名词：success；形容词：successful

### 2. **quality** /ˈkwɒləti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：质量；品质；特性
- **英文释义**：the standard of something when compared to others; a characteristic of someone or something
- **例句**：
  - The quality of this product is much better than that one.
  - 这个产品的质量比那个好得多。
  - Honesty is an important quality for a good leader.
  - 诚实是一个好领导者的重要品质。
- **助记**：quali（什么样的）+ ty（名词后缀）→ 是什么样的 → 品质

### 3. **balance** /ˈbæləns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 平衡；均衡；v. 使平衡
- **英文释义**：n. a state in which different things are equal; v. to keep or put something in a steady position
- **例句**：
  - It's important to keep a balance between study and rest.
  - 保持学习和休息之间的平衡很重要。
  - She balanced the books on her head while walking.
  - 她边走边把书顶在头上保持平衡。
- **助记**：bal（两个，bi- 的变体）+ ance → 两边一样 → 平衡

### 4. **accept** /əkˈsept/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：接受；承认；同意
- **英文释义**：to agree to receive or take something; to believe that something is true
- **例句**：
  - He accepted the invitation to the birthday party.
  - 他接受了生日派对的邀请。
  - We should learn to accept different opinions from others.
  - 我们应该学会接受别人不同的意见。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ cept（拿取）→ 朝自己拿取 → 接受；反义词：reject（拒绝）

### 5. **connect** /kəˈnekt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：连接；联系；相关联
- **英文释义**：to join two or more things together; to have a link or relationship
- **例句**：
  - The bridge connects the two sides of the river.
  - 这座桥连接了河的两岸。
  - The Internet connects people from all over the world.
  - 互联网将全世界的人们连接在一起。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ nect（绑）→ 绑在一起 → 连接；名词：connection

### 6. **direction** /dəˈrekʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：方向；指导；说明
- **英文释义**：the way that someone or something is going or facing; instructions about what to do
- **例句**：
  - Can you tell me the direction to the nearest hospital?
  - 你能告诉我去最近医院的方向吗？
  - Follow the directions on the medicine bottle carefully.
  - 仔细按照药瓶上的说明服用。
- **助记**：direct（指引）+ ion（名词后缀）→ 指引的方向 → 方向

### 7. **suggest** /səˈdʒest/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：建议；暗示；表明
- **英文释义**：to put forward an idea or plan for someone to think about
- **例句**：
  - The doctor suggested that he should drink more water every day.
  - 医生建议他每天多喝水。
  - I suggest going to the museum this weekend.
  - 我建议这个周末去博物馆。
- **助记**：sug（在下面）+ gest（携带）→ 在下面偷偷传递想法 → 建议；注意：suggest 后接 that 从句用虚拟语气（should + 动词原形）

### 8. **creative** /kriˈeɪtɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有创造力的；创新的
- **英文释义**：having the ability to produce new and original ideas or things
- **例句**：
  - The students came up with many creative ideas for the school festival.
  - 学生们为校园节日想出了很多有创意的点子。
  - She is a very creative writer — her stories are always surprising.
  - 她是一位很有创造力的作家——她的故事总是出人意料。
- **助记**：create（创造）+ ive（形容词后缀）→ 能创造的 → 有创造力的

### 9. **wisdom** /ˈwɪzdəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：智慧；明智；才智
- **英文释义**：the ability to make good judgments based on experience and knowledge
- **例句**：
  - The old man is full of wisdom and always gives good advice.
  - 这位老人充满智慧，总能给出好建议。
  - There is great wisdom in this ancient Chinese saying.
  - 这句中国古语里蕴含着伟大的智慧。
- **助记**：wise（聪明的）+ dom（状态/领域）→ 聪明的状态 → 智慧

### 10. **available** /əˈveɪləbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：可获得的；有空的；可用的
- **英文释义**：able to be used or obtained; free to see or talk to people
- **例句**：
  - This book is available in the school library.
  - 这本书在学校图书馆可以借到。
  - Are you available this Saturday afternoon?
  - 你这个周六下午有空吗？
- **助记**：avail（有用，利用）+ able（能...的）→ 能被利用的 → 可用的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **preserve** /prɪˈzɜːv/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：保存；保护；维持
- **英文释义**：to keep something in its original state or in good condition
- **例句**：
  - We should take action to preserve traditional culture.
  - 我们应该采取行动保护传统文化。
  - The museum preserves many valuable works of art.
  - 博物馆保存了很多珍贵的艺术品。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ serve（保持）→ 预先保持 → 保存；名词：preservation

### 12. **relevant** /ˈreləvənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：相关的；有关的；切题的
- **英文释义**：connected with what is being discussed or considered
- **例句**：
  - Please only include information relevant to the topic.
  - 请只包含与主题相关的信息。
  - His experience is highly relevant to this job.
  - 他的经验与这份工作高度相关。
- **助记**：re（回来）+ lev（举起）+ ant → 能举回来对照的 → 相关的；反义词：irrelevant

### 13. **conscious** /ˈkɒnʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有意识的；自觉的；注意到的
- **英文释义**：aware of something; noticing that something exists or is happening
- **例句**：
  - We should be conscious of the importance of protecting the environment.
  - 我们应该意识到保护环境的重要性。
  - She made a conscious effort to improve her pronunciation.
  - 她有意识地努力改善自己的发音。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ sci（知道）+ ous → 内心知道的 → 有意识的；名词：consciousness

### 14. **demonstrate** /ˈdemənstreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：展示；证明；示范
- **英文释义**：to show something clearly; to prove something
- **例句**：
  - The experiment demonstrates that water boils at 100°C.
  - 这个实验证明了水在100°C时沸腾。
  - The teacher demonstrated how to use the new software.
  - 老师示范了如何使用新软件。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ monstr（展示）+ ate → 完全展示出来 → 证明；名词：demonstration

### 15. **interpret** /ɪnˈtɜːprɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：解释；理解；口译
- **英文释义**：to explain the meaning of something; to translate spoken words from one language to another
- **例句**：
  - Different people may interpret the same poem in different ways.
  - 不同的人可能以不同的方式解读同一首诗。
  - She interpreted for the foreign guests at the meeting.
  - 她在会议上为外国客人做口译。
- **助记**：inter（在...之间）+ pret（价值）→ 在事物之间找到价值/意义 → 解释；名词：interpretation
