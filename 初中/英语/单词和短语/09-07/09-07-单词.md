## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **persist** /pəˈsɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：坚持；持续
- **英文释义**：to continue doing something despite difficulty; to continue to exist
- **例句**：
  - She persisted in her efforts despite the setbacks.
  - 尽管遭遇挫折，她仍坚持努力。
  - The rain persisted throughout the day.
  - 雨下了一整天。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ sist（站立）→ 一直站着不倒 → 坚持；持续

### 2. **personality** /ˌpɜːsəˈnæləti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：个性；性格
- **英文释义**：the combination of qualities that make a person different from others
- **例句**：
  - She has a cheerful personality.
  - 她个性开朗。
  - His strong personality makes him a natural leader.
  - 他强烈的个性使他天生就是领导者。
- **助记**：person（人）+ -ality → 属于一个人独有的品质 → 个性

### 3. **perspective** /pəˈspektɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：视角；看法
- **英文释义**：a particular way of thinking about something; a viewpoint
- **例句**：
  - Try to see the problem from a different perspective.
  - 试着从不同的视角看待这个问题。
  - Travelling gave her a new perspective on life.
  - 旅行给了她对生活新的看法。
- **助记**：per（贯穿）+ spect（看）+ -ive → 看透事物 → 视角；看法

### 4. **phenomenon** /fɪˈnɒmɪnən/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：现象
- **英文释义**：a fact or event that can be observed; something remarkable
- **例句**：
  - Climate change is a global phenomenon.
  - 气候变化是一个全球现象。
  - The Northern Lights are a natural phenomenon.
  - 北极光是一种自然现象。
- **助记**：源自希腊语 phainomenon（显现的东西）→ 能被观察到的事物 → 现象

### 5. **pioneer** /ˌpaɪəˈnɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：先驱；开拓
- **英文释义**：a person who is the first to explore or develop something; to be the first to do something
- **例句**：
  - She was a pioneer in the field of robotics.
  - 她是机器人领域的先驱。
  - The company pioneered a new approach to marketing.
  - 这家公司开拓了一种新的营销方式。
- **助记**：pion（步兵）+ -eer → 走在最前面的步兵 → 先驱

### 6. **plea** /pliː/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：请求；恳求
- **英文释义**：an urgent or emotional request; a statement in court
- **例句**：
  - She made a plea for help.
  - 她发出了求助的恳求。
  - The defendant entered a plea of not guilty.
  - 被告作了无罪答辩。
- **助记**：plea 与 please 同源 → 讨好式地请求 → 请求；恳求

### 7. **pleasure** /ˈpleʒə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：快乐；乐事
- **英文释义**：a feeling of happiness or enjoyment; something that gives you joy
- **例句**：
  - It's a pleasure to meet you.
  - 很高兴见到你。
  - Reading is one of her greatest pleasures.
  - 阅读是她最大的乐事之一。
- **助记**：pleas（使高兴）+ -ure → 使人高兴的感觉 → 快乐

### 8. **pledge** /pledʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：誓言；承诺
- **英文释义**：a serious promise; to make a solemn promise
- **例句**：
  - He made a pledge to support the project.
  - 他承诺支持这个项目。
  - They pledged to reduce pollution by 50%.
  - 他们承诺将污染减少50%。
- **助记**：pledge 与 plight（困境中的承诺）同源 → 庄严的许诺 → 誓言

### 9. **plunge** /plʌndʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：跳入；骤降
- **英文释义**：to jump or dive quickly; to decrease suddenly and steeply
- **例句**：
  - She plunged into the pool.
  - 她跳入了游泳池。
  - Stock prices plunged after the announcement.
  - 公告发布后股价暴跌。
- **助记**：plunge 与 plumb（铅锤）同源 → 像铅锤一样直坠而下 → 跳入；骤降

### 10. **policy** /ˈpɒləsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：政策；方针
- **英文释义**：a plan or course of action adopted by a government or organization
- **例句**：
  - The government introduced a new education policy.
  - 政府推出了一项新的教育政策。
  - It's company policy to recycle waste.
  - 回收废物是公司的方针。
- **助记**：poli（城邦）+ -cy → 管理城邦的方法 → 政策

### 高一词汇

### 11. **reprehensible** /ˌreprɪˈhensəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：应受谴责的；可耻的
- **英文释义**：deserving strong criticism; morally wrong
- **例句**：
  - His behaviour was absolutely reprehensible.
  - 他的行为完全应受谴责。
  - Cheating on the exam is reprehensible.
  - 考试作弊是可耻的行为。
- **助记**：re（回）+ prehens（抓住）+ -ible → 应该被抓回来批判的 → 应受谴责的

### 12. **resilient** /rɪˈzɪliənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有韧性的；能迅速恢复的
- **英文释义**：able to recover quickly from difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - Children are remarkably resilient.
  - 孩子们的适应力和恢复力出奇地强。
  - The economy has proven to be resilient.
  - 经济证明了自身的韧性。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sili（跳）+ -ent → 跳回来 → 能迅速恢复的

### 13. **resolute** /ˈrezəluːt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：坚定的；果断的
- **英文释义**：determined and unwavering; having a firm purpose
- **例句**：
  - She remained resolute in her decision.
  - 她对自己的决定坚定不移。
  - The leader took resolute action against corruption.
  - 领导者对腐败采取了果断行动。
- **助记**：re（加强）+ solut（解开）+ -e → 果断地解开难题 → 坚定的

### 14. **resplendent** /rɪˈsplendənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：光辉灿烂的；华丽的
- **英文释义**：impressively beautiful or magnificent
- **例句**：
  - The palace was resplendent with gold decorations.
  - 宫殿金碧辉煌。
  - She looked resplendent in her wedding dress.
  - 她穿着婚纱光彩夺目。
- **助记**：re（加强）+ splend（闪耀）+ -ent → 极度闪耀的 → 光辉灿烂的

### 15. **reticent** /ˈretɪsnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：沉默寡言的；不愿透露的
- **英文释义**：not willing to speak about your thoughts or feelings
- **例句**：
  - He was reticent about his personal life.
  - 他对个人生活讳莫如深。
  - She is naturally reticent and prefers to listen.
  - 她天性寡言，更喜欢倾听。
- **助记**：ret（再）+ ic（沉默）+ -ent → 反复沉默 → 沉默寡言的
