## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **evidence** /ˈevɪdəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：证据；证明
- **英文释义**：facts or information indicating whether something is true
- **例句**：
  - The police found new evidence at the crime scene.
  - 警方在犯罪现场发现了新证据。
  - There is strong evidence that exercise improves health.
  - 有充分的证据表明运动能改善健康。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ vid（看）+ -ence → 能被看出来的东西 → 证据

### 2. **evil** /ˈiːvl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：邪恶的；罪恶
- **英文释义**：profoundly immoral and wicked; the force of moral badness
- **例句**：
  - The villain in the story had an evil plan to take over the world.
  - 故事中的恶棍有一个征服世界的邪恶计划。
  - We must fight against evil and injustice.
  - 我们必须与邪恶和不公正作斗争。
- **助记**：evil → live（生活）倒过来 → 生活反过来就是邪恶

### 3. **evolve** /ɪˈvɒlv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：进化；逐步发展
- **英文释义**：to develop gradually over time; to change through biological evolution
- **例句**：
  - Humans evolved from ancient primates millions of years ago.
  - 人类在数百万年前从古代灵长类动物进化而来。
  - The company's strategy has evolved over the years.
  - 公司的战略多年来逐步发展。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ volve（卷）→ 从卷曲中展开 → 进化

### 4. **exceed** /ɪkˈsiːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：超过；超出
- **英文释义**：to go beyond a particular limit or standard
- **例句**：
  - The cost of the project exceeded the budget by 20%.
  - 项目成本超出预算20%。
  - Her performance exceeded all expectations.
  - 她的表现超出了所有人的期望。
- **助记**：ex-（超出）+ ceed（走）→ 走出去超过 → 超过

### 5. **excess** /ɪkˈses/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：过量；多余的
- **英文释义**：an amount that is more than necessary or allowed
- **例句**：
  - An excess of sugar in your diet is bad for your health.
  - 饮食中糖分过量对健康有害。
  - You'll be charged for any excess baggage.
  - 超重行李需要额外收费。
- **助记**：ex-（超出）+ cess（走）→ 走出限度 → 过量

### 6. **exchange** /ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：交换；兑换
- **英文释义**：to give something and receive something in return
- **例句**：
  - The two students exchanged ideas about the project.
  - 两个学生交换了关于项目的想法。
  - I need to exchange some money before travelling abroad.
  - 出国旅行前我需要兑换一些钱。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ change（改变）→ 互相改变 → 交换

### 7. **exclaim** /ɪkˈskleɪm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：呼喊；惊叫
- **英文释义**：to cry out suddenly in surprise, anger, or pain
- **例句**：
  - "What a beautiful sunset!" she exclaimed.
  - "多美的日落啊！"她惊叹道。
  - He exclaimed in pain when he stubbed his toe.
  - 他踢到脚趾时痛得叫了起来。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ claim（喊）→ 喊出来 → 惊叫

### 8. **exclude** /ɪkˈskluːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：排除；排斥
- **英文释义**：to deliberately leave out or not include
- **例句**：
  - The price excludes delivery charges.
  - 价格不包含运费。
  - Don't exclude anyone from the group activity.
  - 不要把任何人排除在集体活动之外。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ clude（关闭）→ 关在外面 → 排除

### 9. **execute** /ˈeksɪkjuːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：执行；实施
- **英文释义**：to carry out a plan, order, or course of action
- **例句**：
  - The team executed the plan perfectly.
  - 团队完美地执行了计划。
  - The software executes the command in seconds.
  - 软件在几秒内执行了指令。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ ecute（跟随）→ 跟着做出来 → 执行

### 10. **exempt** /ɪɡˈzempt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./v.
- **中文释义**：免除的；豁免
- **英文释义**：free from an obligation or duty that applies to others
- **例句**：
  - Students under 12 are exempt from the entrance fee.
  - 12岁以下的学生免收入场费。
  - The charity is exempt from paying taxes.
  - 该慈善机构免缴税款。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ empt（拿）→ 被拿出来不用做 → 免除

### 高一词汇

### 11. **estrange** /ɪˈstreɪndʒ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使疏远；使陌生
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to be no longer close or friendly
- **例句**：
  - The argument estranged the two best friends for years.
  - 那次争吵让两个好朋友疏远了好几年。
  - He became estranged from his family after moving abroad.
  - 他移居海外后与家人疏远了。
- **助记**：e-（出）+ strange（陌生）→ 变得陌生 → 使疏远

### 12. **euphemism** /ˈjuːfəmɪzəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：委婉语；婉辞
- **英文释义**：a mild or indirect word used in place of one considered too harsh or direct
- **例句**：
  - "Passed away" is a euphemism for "died."
  - "去世"是"死亡"的委婉说法。
  - Politicians often use euphemisms to soften bad news.
  - 政客经常用委婉语来淡化坏消息。
- **助记**：eu-（好）+ phem（说）+ -ism → 好听的说法 → 委婉语

### 13. **exacerbate** /ɪɡˈzæsəbeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使恶化；加剧
- **英文释义**：to make a problem, situation, or feeling worse
- **例句**：
  - The heavy rain exacerbated the flooding in the city.
  - 大雨加剧了城市的洪水。
  - Arguing will only exacerbate the conflict.
  - 争吵只会使冲突恶化。
- **助记**：ex-（加强）+ acerb（尖锐）+ -ate → 使更加尖锐 → 使恶化

### 14. **exemplify** /ɪɡˈzemplɪfaɪ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：举例说明；是…的典型
- **英文释义**：to be a typical example of; to illustrate by giving an example
- **例句**：
  - This painting exemplifies the artist's unique style.
  - 这幅画体现了这位艺术家独特的风格。
  - Can you exemplify your argument with some facts?
  - 你能用一些事实来举例说明你的论点吗？
- **助记**：example（例子）+ -ify（使）→ 用例子来说明

### 15. **exonerate** /ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：免罪；证明无罪
- **英文释义**：to officially declare someone not guilty of a crime or wrongdoing
- **例句**：
  - New DNA evidence exonerated the man who had been wrongly imprisoned.
  - 新的DNA证据证明了那个被冤枉入狱的人无罪。
  - The investigation exonerated her from all charges.
  - 调查证明她所有的罪名都不成立。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ oner（负担）+ -ate → 把负担移出去 → 免罪
