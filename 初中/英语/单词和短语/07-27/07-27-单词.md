## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **applaud** /əˈplɔːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：鼓掌；赞扬
- **英文释义**：to show approval or praise by clapping one's hands; to express approval of
- **例句**：
  - The audience applauded loudly when the band finished their performance.
  - 乐队表演结束时，观众热烈鼓掌。
  - We should applaud her for her bravery in speaking up.
  - 我们应该赞扬她敢于发声的勇气。
- **助记**：applaud → "阿婆乐的"（谐音）→ 阿婆听得开心直鼓掌 → 鼓掌；赞扬

### 2. **antique** /ænˈtiːk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：古董；古物
- **英文释义**：an old object, especially one that is valuable because of its age
- **例句**：
  - The shop sells antiques from the Qing Dynasty.
  - 这家店出售清朝的古董。
  - My grandfather collected antique clocks as a hobby.
  - 我爷爷把收集古董钟当作爱好。
- **助记**：antique → "安体克"（谐音）→ 古老的物体安然放置 → 古董

### 3. **apprentice** /əˈprentɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：学徒；徒弟
- **英文释义**：a person who is learning a trade from a skilled employer
- **例句**：
  - He works as an apprentice in a carpenter's workshop.
  - 他在木匠作坊当学徒。
  - The young chef started as an apprentice five years ago.
  - 这位年轻厨师五年前从学徒做起。
- **助记**：apprentice → ap-（朝向）+ prent（学习）→ 在学习的人 → 学徒

### 4. **aspirin** /ˈæsprɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：阿司匹林（止痛药）
- **英文释义**：a common drug used to reduce pain, fever, and inflammation
- **例句**：
  - The doctor advised her to take aspirin for her headache.
  - 医生建议她吃点阿司匹林治头痛。
  - I always keep aspirin in my school bag in case of emergencies.
  - 我总是在书包里备着阿司匹林以防万一。
- **助记**：aspirin → 音译"阿司匹林"，直接记忆

### 5. **asteroid** /ˈæstərɔɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：小行星
- **英文释义**：a small rocky body orbiting the sun, mostly found between Mars and Jupiter
- **例句**：
  - Scientists discovered a new asteroid last month.
  - 科学家上个月发现了一颗新的小行星。
  - We learned about asteroids in our science class.
  - 我们在科学课上学习了关于小行星的知识。
- **助记**：aster-（星星）+ -oid（像……的）→ 像星星的物体 → 小行星

### 6. **audition** /ɔːˈdɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：试镜；试演
- **英文释义**：a short performance to test whether someone is suitable for a role
- **例句**：
  - She passed the audition and got the lead role in the school play.
  - 她通过了试镜，获得了校剧的女主角。
  - Hundreds of students showed up for the choir audition.
  - 数百名学生参加了合唱团的试演。
- **助记**：audit（听）+ -ion → 让人听你表演 → 试镜；试演

### 7. **avalanche** /ˈævəlɑːnʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：雪崩
- **英文释义**：a large amount of snow, ice, and rocks falling quickly down a mountain
- **例句**：
  - The skiers were warned about the risk of avalanche.
  - 滑雪者被告知有雪崩风险。
  - The avalanche destroyed several houses in the village.
  - 雪崩摧毁了村里的几间房屋。
- **助记**：avalanche → "阿瓦兰奇"（谐音）→ 雪块哗啦倾泻 → 雪崩

### 8. **backbone** /ˈbækbəʊn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：脊梁骨；支柱；骨气
- **英文释义**：the series of bones in the spine; the most important part of something; courage
- **例句**：
  - She injured her backbone in a sports accident.
  - 她在运动中伤了脊梁骨。
  - Education is the backbone of a nation's development.
  - 教育是国家发展的支柱。
- **助记**：back（背）+ bone（骨头）→ 背后的骨头 → 脊梁骨

### 9. **ballad** /ˈbæləd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：民谣；叙事歌
- **英文释义**：a slow, romantic song that tells a story
- **例句**：
  - She sang a beautiful ballad at the school concert.
  - 她在校音乐会上唱了一首动听的民谣。
  - The band's new album includes several ballads.
  - 乐队的新专辑包含多首民谣。
- **助记**：ballad → "芭乐"（谐音）→ 芭乐情歌 → 民谣

### 10. **banquet** /ˈbæŋkwɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：宴会；盛宴
- **英文释义**：a formal meal for many people, often to celebrate a special occasion
- **例句**：
  - The school held a banquet to celebrate the graduation.
  - 学校举办了宴会庆祝毕业。
  - We attended a banquet at the hotel last weekend.
  - 上周末我们参加了酒店的一场宴会。
- **助记**：banquet → "帮亏"（谐音）→ 帮人办宴席 → 宴会

### 高一词汇

### 11. **bureaucracy** /bjʊəˈrɒkrəsi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：官僚主义；官僚机构
- **英文释义**：a system of government or business with many complicated rules
- **例句**：
  - The bureaucracy slowed down the approval process.
  - 官僚主义拖慢了审批流程。
  - He complained about the red tape and bureaucracy at the office.
  - 他抱怨办公室的繁文缛节和官僚作风。
- **助记**：bureau（办公桌）+ -cracy（统治）→ 办公桌统治 → 官僚主义

### 12. **camaraderie** /ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：情谊；同志情谊
- **英文释义**：a feeling of friendship and trust among people who work together
- **例句**：
  - The team's camaraderie helped them win the championship.
  - 队伍的团结情谊帮助他们赢得了冠军。
  - There was a strong sense of camaraderie among the volunteers.
  - 志愿者们之间有着浓厚的情谊。
- **助记**：camarade（同志）+ -erie（状态）→ 同志般的情谊 → 情谊

### 13. **camouflage** /ˈkæməflɑːʒ/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：伪装；掩饰
- **英文释义**：the way an animal or soldier hides by matching its surroundings; to hide by disguising
- **例句**：
  - The soldiers wore green camouflage in the forest.
  - 士兵们在森林中穿着绿色迷彩服。
  - The chameleon uses camouflage to blend in with its environment.
  - 变色龙用伪装融入周围环境。
- **助记**：camouflage 来自法语，camoufler（伪装）→ 伪装

### 14. **carbohydrate** /ˌkɑːbəʊˈhaɪdreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：碳水化合物
- **英文释义**：a substance such as sugar or starch that provides energy
- **例句**：
  - Rice and bread are rich in carbohydrates.
  - 米饭和面包富含碳水化合物。
  - Athletes need to eat enough carbohydrates before a match.
  - 运动员在比赛前需要摄入足够的碳水化合物。
- **助记**：carbo-（碳）+ hydrate（水合物）→ 碳水合物 → 碳水化合物

### 15. **casualty** /ˈkæʒuəlti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：伤亡人员；受害者
- **英文释义**：a person killed or injured in a war or accident
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake caused many casualties.
  - 地震造成了大量伤亡。
  - He was a casualty of the company's downsizing.
  - 他是公司裁员的受害者。
- **助记**：casual（偶然的）+ -ty → 偶然遭遇不幸的人 → 伤亡人员
