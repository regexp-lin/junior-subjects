## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **challenge** /ˈtʃælɪndʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 挑战；难题；v. 向...挑战
- **英文释义**：n. something that tests your ability; v. to invite someone to compete
- **例句**：
  - Learning a new language is a big challenge for most people.
  - 学习一门新语言对大多数人来说是一个很大的挑战。
  - He challenged me to a game of chess after school.
  - 他放学后向我挑战下棋。
- **助记**：chall（呼唤）+ enge → 向对手呼唤 → 挑战；形容词：challenging（有挑战性的）

### 2. **recommend** /ˌrekəˈmend/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：推荐；建议
- **英文释义**：to suggest that something is good or suitable for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - Can you recommend a good English dictionary for beginners?
  - 你能推荐一本适合初学者的好英语词典吗？
  - The teacher recommended that we read more English novels.
  - 老师建议我们多读英文小说。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ commend（推荐）→ 再次推荐 → 强烈推荐；名词：recommendation

### 3. **attract** /əˈtrækt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：吸引；引起（注意）
- **英文释义**：to make someone interested in something or make them come to a place
- **例句**：
  - The beautiful scenery of Guilin attracts millions of tourists every year.
  - 桂林美丽的风景每年吸引数百万游客。
  - Her speech attracted everyone's attention at the meeting.
  - 她的演讲在会议上吸引了所有人的注意。
- **助记**：at（朝向）+ tract（拉）→ 朝自己拉 → 吸引；名词：attraction；形容词：attractive

### 4. **confident** /ˈkɒnfɪdənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：自信的；有信心的
- **英文释义**：feeling sure about your own ability to do things
- **例句**：
  - She felt confident about passing the English exam.
  - 她对通过英语考试很有信心。
  - A confident smile can help you make a good first impression.
  - 自信的微笑可以帮你留下好的第一印象。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ fid（信任）+ ent → 完全信任自己 → 自信的；名词：confidence

### 5. **attention** /əˈtenʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：注意；注意力；关注
- **英文释义**：the act of listening to, looking at, or thinking about something carefully
- **例句**：
  - The teacher asked the students to pay attention to the blackboard.
  - 老师要求学生注意看黑板。
  - He attracted a lot of attention with his creative project.
  - 他的创意项目引起了很多关注。
- **助记**：at（朝向）+ tent（伸展）+ ion → 把注意力伸展过去 → 关注

### 6. **reduce** /rɪˈdjuːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：减少；降低；缩小
- **英文释义**：to make something smaller or less in size, amount, or price
- **例句**：
  - We should reduce the use of plastic bags to protect the environment.
  - 我们应该减少塑料袋的使用来保护环境。
  - The store reduced the price of the jacket by 30%.
  - 这家店把夹克的价格降低了30%。
- **助记**：re（回来）+ duce（引导）→ 引导回缩 → 减少；名词：reduction

### 7. **describe** /dɪˈskraɪb/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：描述；形容；描写
- **英文释义**：to say what something or someone is like by giving details
- **例句**：
  - Can you describe what the thief looked like?
  - 你能描述一下小偷长什么样吗？
  - The passage describes the beautiful autumn scenery in Beijing.
  - 这篇文章描述了北京美丽的秋景。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ scribe（写）→ 写下来 → 描述；名词：description

### 8. **pleasant** /ˈpleznt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：令人愉快的；宜人的；友善的
- **英文释义**：enjoyable, attractive, or nice
- **例句**：
  - We had a pleasant time at the school picnic yesterday.
  - 昨天我们在学校野餐时过得很愉快。
  - The weather is pleasant in spring — not too hot and not too cold.
  - 春天天气宜人——不太热也不太冷。
- **助记**：pleas（取悦，please 的词根）+ ant（形容词后缀）→ 令人高兴的 → 愉快的；反义词：unpleasant

### 9. **method** /ˈmeθəd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：方法；办法；方式
- **英文释义**：a particular way of doing something
- **例句**：
  - What's the best method to learn English vocabulary?
  - 学习英语词汇最好的方法是什么？
  - The teacher uses different methods to make the class more interesting.
  - 老师使用不同的方法使课堂更有趣。
- **助记**：meth（追随）+ od（道路）→ 追随某条道路 → 方法；同义词：way, approach

### 10. **benefit** /ˈbenɪfɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 好处；利益；v. 对...有益；受益
- **英文释义**：n. an advantage or something good; v. to be useful or helpful to someone
- **例句**：
  - Regular exercise has many benefits for your health.
  - 经常锻炼对你的健康有很多好处。
  - Students can benefit a lot from reading English books.
  - 学生可以从阅读英语书籍中受益很多。
- **助记**：bene（好的）+ fit（做）→ 做好事 → 好处；固定搭配：benefit from（从...中受益）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **assumption** /əˈsʌmpʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：假设；假定；承担
- **英文释义**：something that you accept as true without proof
- **例句**：
  - Don't make assumptions about people before you know them well.
  - 在充分了解一个人之前，不要对他做出假设。
  - The assumption that everyone has Internet access is not correct.
  - 认为每个人都能上网的假设是不正确的。
- **助记**：assume（假设）+ tion → 假设的名词形式；assume: as（朝向）+ sume（拿取）→ 拿来当作真的 → 假设

### 12. **comprehensive** /ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：全面的；综合的；广泛的
- **英文释义**：including everything that is needed; complete
- **例句**：
  - The report provides a comprehensive analysis of the problem.
  - 这份报告对该问题进行了全面分析。
  - Students need to do comprehensive review before the final exam.
  - 学生在期末考试前需要进行全面复习。
- **助记**：com（全部）+ prehens（抓住）+ ive → 全部抓住 → 全面的；动词：comprehend（理解）

### 13. **distinguish** /dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：区分；辨别；使杰出
- **英文释义**：to recognize the difference between two or more things
- **例句**：
  - It's hard to distinguish the twins — they look exactly the same.
  - 很难区分这对双胞胎——他们看起来完全一样。
  - You should learn to distinguish facts from opinions.
  - 你应该学会区分事实和观点。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ tinguish（刺，标记）→ 分开做标记 → 区分；固定搭配：distinguish A from B

### 14. **inevitable** /ɪnˈevɪtəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不可避免的；必然的
- **英文释义**：certain to happen and unable to be avoided
- **例句**：
  - Making mistakes is an inevitable part of learning a new language.
  - 犯错是学习新语言中不可避免的一部分。
  - Change is inevitable — we must learn to adapt.
  - 变化是不可避免的——我们必须学会适应。
- **助记**：in（不）+ evit（避免）+ able → 不能避免的 → 必然的；副词：inevitably

### 15. **motivate** /ˈməʊtɪveɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：激发；激励；成为...的动机
- **英文释义**：to give someone a reason for doing something; to inspire someone to act
- **例句**：
  - The teacher's praise motivated the students to study harder.
  - 老师的表扬激励了学生更加努力学习。
  - What motivates you to get up early every morning?
  - 是什么激励你每天早起？
- **助记**：motive（动机）+ ate（动词后缀）→ 提供动机 → 激励；名词：motivation
