## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **perpetuate** /pəˈpetʃueɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使永存；使持续
- **英文释义**：to cause something to continue indefinitely
- **例句**：
  - We should not perpetuate harmful stereotypes about different cultures.
  - 我们不应该使关于不同文化的有害刻板印象永存。
  - The tradition has been perpetuated for hundreds of years.
  - 这个传统已经延续了数百年。
- **助记**：per（持续）+ petu（追求）+ ate → 持续追求下去 → 使永存；名词：perpetuation（永存）

### 2. **transgress** /trænzˈɡres/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：违反；越界；冒犯
- **英文释义**：to go beyond the limits of what is morally or legally acceptable
- **例句**：
  - Anyone who transgresses the law will face punishment.
  - 任何违反法律的人都将面临惩罚。
  - The journalist was criticized for transgressing ethical boundaries.
  - 这位记者因越过道德界限而受到批评。
- **助记**：trans（跨越）+ gress（走）→ 走过界限 → 违反；名词：transgression（违反）

### 3. **proliferate** /prəˈlɪfəreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：激增；迅速繁殖；扩散
- **英文释义**：to increase rapidly in number or amount
- **例句**：
  - Online shopping platforms have proliferated in recent years.
  - 近年来网购平台激增。
  - Weeds proliferated in the garden during the rainy season.
  - 雨季期间花园里杂草丛生。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ lifer（生命）+ ate → 生命向前大量产生 → 激增；名词：proliferation（扩散）

### 4. **mitigate** /ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：缓解；减轻
- **英文释义**：to make something less severe, serious, or painful
- **例句**：
  - Planting trees can help mitigate the effects of climate change.
  - 种植树木可以帮助减轻气候变化的影响。
  - The government took steps to mitigate the impact of the flood.
  - 政府采取措施减轻洪水的影响。
- **助记**：mit（温和）+ igate → 使变温和 → 缓解；名词：mitigation（缓解）

### 5. **nomenclature** /nəˈmenklətʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：命名法；术语体系
- **英文释义**：a system of names or terms used in a particular field
- **例句**：
  - Scientific nomenclature uses Latin names for plants and animals.
  - 科学命名法使用拉丁名称来命名动植物。
  - Understanding medical nomenclature is essential for healthcare students.
  - 理解医学术语对医学生来说至关重要。
- **助记**：nomen（名字）+ clature（叫）→ 叫名字的体系 → 命名法

### 6. **quandary** /ˈkwɒndəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：困惑；进退两难
- **英文释义**：a state of not knowing what to do; a difficult situation
- **例句**：
  - She was in a quandary about whether to accept the job offer abroad.
  - 她对是否接受国外的工作邀请感到进退两难。
  - The unexpected question put the student in a quandary.
  - 出乎意料的问题让学生陷入困惑。
- **助记**：quan（多少）+ dary → 不知道多少选择 → 困惑；近义词：dilemma, predicament

### 7. **incumbent** /ɪnˈkʌmbənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：在任的；现任者；义不容辞的
- **英文释义**：currently holding an official position; a person who currently holds a position
- **例句**：
  - The incumbent president is running for re-election.
  - 现任总统正在竞选连任。
  - It is incumbent upon us to protect the environment.
  - 保护环境是我们义不容辞的责任。
- **助记**：in（在）+ cumb（躺/占据）+ ent → 占据位置的 → 在任的

### 8. **indigenous** /ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：本土的；土著的；固有的
- **英文释义**：originating or occurring naturally in a particular place
- **例句**：
  - The panda is indigenous to China.
  - 大熊猫是中国本土的动物。
  - Indigenous cultures should be respected and preserved.
  - 土著文化应该受到尊重和保护。
- **助记**：indi（内部）+ gen（产生）+ ous → 在内部产生的 → 本土的

### 9. **categorically** /ˌkætəˈɡɒrɪkli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：断然地；明确地；绝对地
- **英文释义**：in a way that is completely clear and direct, with no doubt
- **例句**：
  - She categorically denied all the accusations against her.
  - 她断然否认了对她的所有指控。
  - The company categorically refused to negotiate with the workers.
  - 公司断然拒绝与工人协商。
- **助记**：categorical（绝对的）+ ly → 绝对地/断然地；形容词：categorical（明确的）

### 10. **adjunct** /ˈædʒʌŋkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：附属物；辅助的
- **英文释义**：something added to another thing but not essential; additional
- **例句**：
  - Physical exercise is a useful adjunct to a healthy diet.
  - 体育锻炼是健康饮食的有益补充。
  - She works as an adjunct professor at the university.
  - 她在大学担任兼职教授。
- **助记**：ad（朝向）+ junct（连接）→ 连接在旁边的 → 附属物

### 高一词汇

### 11. **idiosyncratic** /ˌɪdiəsɪŋˈkrætɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：独特的；特有的；与众不同的
- **英文释义**：peculiar to an individual; having unusual characteristics
- **例句**：
  - Each teacher has an idiosyncratic way of explaining concepts.
  - 每位老师都有独特的解释概念的方式。
  - His idiosyncratic sense of humor makes him very popular among friends.
  - 他独特的幽默感使他在朋友中非常受欢迎。
- **助记**：idio（自己的）+ syn（一起）+ cratic → 自己独有的特征 → 独特的；名词：idiosyncrasy

### 12. **protracted** /prəˈtræktɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：旷日持久的；延长的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a long time or longer than expected
- **例句**：
  - The protracted negotiations finally led to a peace agreement.
  - 旷日持久的谈判终于达成了和平协议。
  - A protracted drought affected crops across the entire region.
  - 持续的干旱影响了整个地区的农作物。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ tract（拉）+ ed → 向前拉长的 → 旷日持久的；动词：protract（延长）

### 13. **obfuscate** /ˈɒbfʌskeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：混淆；使困惑；使模糊
- **英文释义**：to make something unclear or difficult to understand
- **例句**：
  - The politician tried to obfuscate the issue with complicated language.
  - 政治家试图用复杂的语言来混淆问题。
  - Too much technical jargon can obfuscate the real meaning.
  - 太多的专业术语会使真正的含义变得模糊。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ fusc（暗）+ ate → 使变暗 → 混淆；名词：obfuscation（混淆）

### 14. **mollify** /ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：安慰；安抚；缓和
- **英文释义**：to calm someone's anger or anxiety; to soothe
- **例句**：
  - The manager tried to mollify the angry customer with a sincere apology.
  - 经理试图用真诚的道歉安抚愤怒的顾客。
  - His gentle words helped mollify the tense atmosphere.
  - 他温和的话语帮助缓和了紧张的气氛。
- **助记**：moll（柔软）+ ify（使）→ 使变柔软 → 安抚；近义词：appease, placate

### 15. **indelible** /ɪnˈdelɪbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不可磨灭的；难以忘记的
- **英文释义**：making a mark that cannot be removed; not able to be forgotten
- **例句**：
  - The trip to the Great Wall left an indelible impression on me.
  - 长城之旅给我留下了不可磨灭的印象。
  - Her kindness made an indelible mark on the lives of her students.
  - 她的善良在学生们的生活中留下了不可磨灭的印记。
- **助记**：in（不）+ del（删除）+ ible → 无法删除的 → 不可磨灭的；副词：indelibly
