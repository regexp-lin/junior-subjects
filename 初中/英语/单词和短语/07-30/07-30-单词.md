## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **courtesy** /ˈkɜːtəsi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：礼貌；殷勤
- **英文释义**：polite behavior; a polite action or remark
- **例句**：
  - He showed courtesy by holding the door open for others.
  - 他很有礼貌地为他人扶着门。
  - As a courtesy, please let the teacher know if you'll be absent.
  - 出于礼貌，请假请提前告知老师。
- **助记**：court（宫廷）+ -esy → 宫廷礼节 → 礼貌

### 2. **coverage** /ˈkʌvərɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：报道；覆盖范围
- **英文释义**：the reporting of news; the area or amount covered
- **例句**：
  - The event received wide coverage in the media.
  - 该事件得到了媒体的广泛报道。
  - Our school's WiFi coverage extends to the playground.
  - 我们学校的 WiFi 覆盖到操场。
- **助记**：cover（覆盖）+ -age → 覆盖范围；报道

### 3. **crackdown** /ˈkrækdaʊn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：严厉打击；镇压
- **英文释义**：severe action taken to stop something
- **例句**：
  - The government launched a crackdown on illegal activities.
  - 政府展开了对非法活动的严厉打击。
  - The school's crackdown on bullying made everyone feel safer.
  - 学校对霸凌的严厉整顿让大家更有安全感。
- **助记**：crack（击打）+ down（下来）→ 用力压下去 → 严厉打击

### 4. **craving** /ˈkreɪvɪŋ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：渴望；强烈的欲望
- **英文释义**：a strong desire for something
- **例句**：
  - She had a craving for chocolate after the exam.
  - 考试后她特别想吃巧克力。
  - His craving for knowledge drove him to read every day.
  - 他对知识的渴望驱使他每天阅读。
- **助记**：crave（渴望）+ -ing → 渴望（名词化）

### 5. **creditor** /ˈkredɪtə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：债权人；贷方
- **英文释义**：a person or organization that is owed money
- **例句**：
  - He had to pay back his creditors within six months.
  - 他必须在六个月内偿还债权人的钱。
  - The company's creditors demanded payment.
  - 公司的债权人要求还款。
- **助记**：credit（信贷）+ -or（人）→ 放贷的人 → 债权人

### 6. **cripple** /ˈkrɪpl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使残废；严重削弱
- **英文释义**：to cause someone to be unable to move; to damage something seriously
- **例句**：
  - The accident crippled his ability to walk.
  - 事故使他丧失了行走能力。
  - The strike crippled the city's transportation system.
  - 罢工严重影响了城市的交通系统。
- **助记**：cripple → "可如坡"（谐音）→ 像爬坡一样艰难 → 使残废

### 7. **critique** /krɪˈtiːk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：评论；批评性分析
- **英文释义**：a detailed analysis and assessment of something
- **例句**：
  - The teacher wrote a critique of my essay.
  - 老师给我的作文写了评语。
  - She published a critique of the new policy.
  - 她发表了一篇对新政策的评论。
- **助记**：crit-（判断）+ -ique → 判断分析 → 评论

### 8. **custody** /ˈkʌstədi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：监护；拘留
- **英文释义**：the legal right to care for someone; the state of being imprisoned
- **例句**：
  - After the divorce, the mother was granted custody of the children.
  - 离婚后，母亲获得了孩子的监护权。
  - The suspect was taken into police custody.
  - 嫌疑人被警方拘留。
- **助记**：custod-（看守）+ -y → 监护；拘留

### 9. **cynical** /ˈsɪnɪkl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：愤世嫉俗的；怀疑的
- **英文释义**：believing that people are motivated by self-interest; distrustful
- **例句**：
  - He has a cynical view of politicians.
  - 他对政客持愤世嫉俗的态度。
  - Don't be so cynical — not everyone has ulterior motives.
  - 别那么怀疑——不是每个人都有别的目的。
- **助记**：cynic（犬儒）+ -al → 犬儒的 → 愤世嫉俗的

### 10. **dawdle** /ˈdɔːdl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：磨蹭；闲逛
- **英文释义**：to waste time; to move slowly
- **例句**：
  - Stop dawdling and get dressed, or we'll be late for school!
  - 别磨蹭了，快穿好衣服，上学要迟到了！
  - She dawdled along the street looking at shop windows.
  - 她在街上闲逛，看着橱窗。
- **助记**：dawdle → "兜逗"（谐音）→ 兜来兜去逗留 → 磨蹭

### 高一词汇

### 11. **debacle** /deɪˈbɑːkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：惨败；崩溃
- **英文释义**：a sudden and complete disaster or failure
- **例句**：
  - The project was a complete debacle from start to finish.
  - 这个项目从头到尾彻底失败。
  - The team's debacle in the final shocked their fans.
  - 队伍在决赛中的惨败震惊了粉丝。
- **助记**：来自法语 débâcle（崩溃）→ 惨败

### 12. **decipher** /dɪˈsaɪfə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：破译；解读
- **英文释义**：to convert coded writing into normal language; to understand something difficult
- **例句**：
  - Scholars are trying to decipher the ancient script.
  - 学者们正在尝试破译古代文字。
  - I couldn't decipher his messy handwriting.
  - 我无法辨认他潦草的字迹。
- **助记**：de-（除去）+ cipher（密码）→ 除去密码 → 破译

### 13. **defection** /dɪˈfekʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：叛变；背弃
- **英文释义**：the act of deserting a cause, country, or organization
- **例句**：
  - The defection of key players weakened the team.
  - 关键球员的离队削弱了队伍实力。
  - His defection to the rival company surprised everyone.
  - 他跳槽到对手公司让所有人惊讶。
- **助记**：defect（叛变）+ -ion → 叛变（名词）

### 14. **deflect** /dɪˈflekt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使偏转；转移
- **英文释义**：to cause something to change direction; to turn aside
- **例句**：
  - The shield deflected the arrow.
  - 盾牌把箭挡偏了。
  - She deflected the question by changing the subject.
  - 她通过转移话题回避了那个问题。
- **助记**：de-（离开）+ flect（弯）→ 使弯开 → 偏转

### 15. **deficit** /ˈdefɪsɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：赤字；不足
- **英文释义**：the amount by which something is less than what is needed
- **例句**：
  - The country has a large budget deficit.
  - 该国面临巨额预算赤字。
  - There's a deficit of skilled workers in the industry.
  - 该行业缺乏熟练工人。
- **助记**：de-（向下）+ fic（做）+ -it → 做得不够 → 赤字；不足
