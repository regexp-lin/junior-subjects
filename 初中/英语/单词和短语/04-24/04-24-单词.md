## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **ponder** /ˈpɒndə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：深思；仔细考虑；琢磨
- **英文释义**：to think about something carefully for a period of time
- **例句**：
  - She pondered the question for a moment before answering.
  - 她仔细考虑了一会儿才回答这个问题。
  - He sat by the window, pondering his future career choices.
  - 他坐在窗边，思考着未来的职业选择。
- **助记**：pond（重量）+ er → 掂量重量 → 深思；常与 over/on 连用：ponder over a problem

### 2. **aspire** /əˈspaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：渴望；立志；追求
- **英文释义**：to have a strong desire to achieve something
- **例句**：
  - Many young people aspire to become successful entrepreneurs.
  - 许多年轻人渴望成为成功的企业家。
  - She aspires to study at a top university abroad.
  - 她立志到国外顶尖大学深造。
- **助记**：a（朝向）+ spir（呼吸）→ 朝向目标呼吸 → 渴望；常与 to 连用；名词：aspiration（抱负）

### 3. **refute** /rɪˈfjuːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：驳斥；反驳；证明…错误
- **英文释义**：to prove that a statement or theory is wrong
- **例句**：
  - The scientist refuted the claim with solid evidence.
  - 科学家用确凿的证据驳斥了这一说法。
  - He tried to refute the accusations against him in court.
  - 他试图在法庭上反驳针对他的指控。
- **助记**：re（回）+ fute（击打）→ 打回去 → 驳斥；名词：refutation（驳斥）

### 4. **validate** /ˈvælɪdeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：证实；使生效；验证
- **英文释义**：to prove or confirm that something is correct or legally acceptable
- **例句**：
  - The experiment validated our hypothesis.
  - 实验证实了我们的假设。
  - You need to validate your ticket before boarding the train.
  - 上车前你需要验票。
- **助记**：valid（有效的）+ ate → 使有效 → 验证；名词：validation（验证）

### 5. **notion** /ˈnəʊʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：概念；想法；观念
- **英文释义**：an idea, belief, or understanding of something
- **例句**：
  - I have a vague notion of what he meant.
  - 我对他说的意思有个模糊的概念。
  - The notion that money brings happiness is not always true.
  - 金钱带来幸福这种观念并不总是正确的。
- **助记**：not（知道）+ ion → 知道的东西 → 概念；形容词：notional（概念的）

### 6. **doctrine** /ˈdɒktrɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：教义；学说；主义
- **英文释义**：a belief or set of beliefs held and taught by a church, political party, or other group
- **例句**：
  - The doctrine of equality is fundamental to democracy.
  - 平等主义是民主的基石。
  - The school follows the doctrine of learning through practice.
  - 这所学校遵循在实践中学习的理念。
- **助记**：doct（教导）+ rine → 被教导的内容 → 教义；形容词：doctrinal（教义的）

### 7. **obsolete** /ˈɒbsəliːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：过时的；淘汰的；废弃的
- **英文释义**：no longer used or needed; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Typewriters have become obsolete with the rise of computers.
  - 随着电脑的普及，打字机已经过时了。
  - Many skills that were useful in the past are now obsolete.
  - 许多过去有用的技能现在已经淘汰了。
- **助记**：ob（ against）+solete（习惯的）→ 不再习惯的 → 过时的；名词：obsolescence（过时）

### 8. **redundant** /rɪˈdʌndənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：多余的；冗余的；被裁减的
- **英文释义**：not needed; superfluous; (of workers) no longer needed for a job
- **例句**：
  - The paragraph contains redundant information that can be cut.
  - 这段包含多余信息，可以删掉。
  - Hundreds of workers were made redundant when the factory closed.
  - 工厂关闭时，数百名工人被裁减。
- **助记**：re（再）+ dund（溢出）+ ant → 再次溢出的 → 多余的；名词：redundancy（冗余）

### 9. **versatile** /ˈvɜːsətaɪl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：多才多艺的；用途广泛的；多功能的
- **英文释义**：able to adapt to many different functions or activities
- **例句**：
  - She is a versatile athlete who excels in both swimming and running.
  - 她是一位多才多艺的运动员，游泳和跑步都很出色。
  - Smartphones are versatile devices that can do many things.
  - 智能手机是功能多样的设备，可以做很多事情。
- **助记**：vers（转）+ atile → 能转向多方面的 → 多才多艺的；名词：versatility（多才多艺）

### 10. **presumably** /prɪˈzjuːməbli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：大概； presumably；据推测
- **英文释义**：used to say that you think something is probably true
- **例句**：
  - Presumably, he will arrive by train.
  - 他大概是坐火车来的。
  - The meeting has been postponed, presumably due to the weather.
  - 会议被推迟了， presumably 是因为天气原因。
- **助记**：presume（假定）+ ably → 可以假定地 → 大概；动词：presume（假定）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ephemeral** /ɪˈfemərəl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：短暂的；瞬息的；朝生暮死的
- **英文释义**：lasting for a very short time; transient
- **例句**：
  - Fame can be ephemeral in the entertainment industry.
  - 在娱乐圈，名气可能是短暂的。
  - The ephemeral beauty of cherry blossoms attracts millions of visitors.
  - 樱花转瞬即逝的美吸引了数百万游客。
- **助记**：epi（在…上）+ hemer（日）+ al → 只活一天的 → 短暂的；名词：ephemera（短暂之物）

### 12. **capricious** /kəˈprɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：善变的；反复无常的；任性的
- **英文释义**：changing mood or behavior suddenly and unexpectedly
- **例句**：
  - The weather in spring is often capricious.
  - 春天的天气常常变幻莫测。
  - She was known for her capricious decisions that confused her team.
  - 她以善变的决定而闻名，让团队感到困惑。
- **助记**：capric（山羊，跳跃）+ ious → 像山羊一样跳跃 → 善变的；名词：caprice（任性）

### 13. **superfluous** /suːˈpɜːfluəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：多余的；过剩的；不必要的
- **英文释义**：more than is needed or wanted; unnecessary
- **例句**：
  - The extra decorations were superfluous for such a small room.
  - 对于这么小的房间来说，额外的装饰是多余的。
  - He deleted all superfluous words from his essay.
  - 他删除了文章中所有多余的词。
- **助记**：super（超过）+ flu（流）+ ous → 流得过多的 → 多余的；名词：superfluity（多余）

### 14. **austere** /ɔːˈstɪə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：简朴的；严峻的；苦行的
- **英文释义**：severely simple; strict and serious; without comfort
- **例句**：
  - The monk lived an austere life in the mountains.
  - 僧人在山中过着简朴的生活。
  - The austere expression on her face made everyone nervous.
  - 她脸上严峻的表情让每个人都紧张。
- **助记**：auster（严厉）+ e → 严厉的 → 简朴严峻的；名词：austerity（简朴；紧缩）

### 15. **resilient** /rɪˈzɪliənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有弹性的；能恢复的；坚韧的
- **英文释义**：able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - Children are often more resilient than adults think.
  - 孩子往往比大人想象的更坚韧。
  - The economy has shown itself to be resilient in the face of challenges.
  - 经济在面对挑战时表现出了韧性。
- **助记**：re（回）+ sil（跳）+ ient → 能跳回来的 → 有弹性的；名词：resilience（韧性）
