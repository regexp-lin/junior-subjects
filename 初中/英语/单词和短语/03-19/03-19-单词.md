## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **broad** /brɔːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：宽阔的；广泛的
- **英文释义**：wide; covering a large range
- **例句**：
  - The road is broad enough for four cars to pass at the same time.
  - 这条路够宽，可以同时通过四辆车。
  - She has a broad knowledge of history and literature.
  - 她有广泛的历史和文学知识。
- **助记**：broad 联想 abroad（在国外）→ 去到广阔的世界 → 宽阔的；动词：broaden（拓宽）

### 2. **capture** /ˈkæptʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：捕获；俘获；拍摄；吸引
- **英文释义**：to catch someone or something; to record or photograph
- **例句**：
  - The photographer captured the beautiful sunset on camera.
  - 摄影师用相机拍下了美丽的日落。
  - The story captures the spirit of the Chinese New Year celebration.
  - 这个故事捕捉到了中国新年庆典的精神。
- **助记**：capt（抓住）+ ure → 抓住 → 捕获；联想 captain（船长/队长）→ 抓住队伍的人

### 3. **eager** /ˈiːɡə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：渴望的；热切的
- **英文释义**：wanting very much to do or have something
- **例句**：
  - The children were eager to open their Christmas presents.
  - 孩子们迫不及待地想打开圣诞礼物。
  - She is eager to learn new things and always asks questions.
  - 她渴望学习新事物，总是提问。
- **助记**：eag（尖锐的）+ er → 像尖刺一样急切 → 渴望的；固定搭配：be eager to do sth. / be eager for sth.

### 4. **exist** /ɪɡˈzɪst/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：存在；生存
- **英文释义**：to be real; to be present; to live
- **例句**：
  - Do you believe that aliens exist somewhere in the universe?
  - 你相信外星人存在于宇宙的某个地方吗？
  - Some rare animals can only exist in specific environments.
  - 一些稀有动物只能在特定环境中生存。
- **助记**：ex（向外）+ ist（站立）→ 站在那里被看到 → 存在；名词：existence

### 5. **guilty** /ˈɡɪlti/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有罪的；内疚的
- **英文释义**：having done something wrong; feeling bad about something
- **例句**：
  - The court found him guilty of stealing.
  - 法庭判他偷窃罪成立。
  - She felt guilty about not visiting her grandmother more often.
  - 她因没有更常去看望奶奶而感到内疚。
- **助记**：guilt（罪过/内疚）+ y → 有罪过的 → 有罪的/内疚的；反义词：innocent

### 6. **humor** /ˈhjuːmə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：幽默；幽默感
- **英文释义**：the quality of being funny; the ability to find things funny
- **例句**：
  - His speech was full of humor and made everyone laugh.
  - 他的演讲充满幽默，让每个人都笑了。
  - A good sense of humor can help you make friends easily.
  - 良好的幽默感可以帮助你轻松交朋友。
- **助记**：humor 与 humour（英式拼写）相同 → 幽默；形容词：humorous（幽默的）

### 7. **limit** /ˈlɪmɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 限制；极限；v. 限制
- **英文释义**：n. the greatest amount allowed; v. to keep within a certain range
- **例句**：
  - There is a speed limit of 60 km/h on this road.
  - 这条路的限速是每小时60公里。
  - We should limit the time we spend on mobile phones.
  - 我们应该限制花在手机上的时间。
- **助记**：limit 本身就表示"界限" → 限制；形容词：limited（有限的）

### 8. **necessity** /nəˈsesəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：必要性；必需品
- **英文释义**：the fact of being needed; something essential
- **例句**：
  - Water and food are basic necessities for survival.
  - 水和食物是生存的基本必需品。
  - There is no necessity to rush — we have plenty of time.
  - 没有必要着急——我们有充足的时间。
- **助记**：necess（必要的）+ ity → 必要的状态 → 必要性；形容词：necessary

### 9. **recover** /rɪˈkʌvə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：恢复；康复；找回
- **英文释义**：to get well again after being ill; to get back something lost
- **例句**：
  - It took him two weeks to recover from the flu.
  - 他花了两周时间从流感中恢复。
  - The police helped her recover the stolen bicycle.
  - 警察帮她找回了被偷的自行车。
- **助记**：re（重新）+ cover（覆盖/遮盖）→ 重新恢复原状 → 恢复；名词：recovery

### 10. **section** /ˈsekʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：部分；部门；区域
- **英文释义**：one of the parts that something is divided into
- **例句**：
  - The reading section of the exam was the most difficult.
  - 考试的阅读部分是最难的。
  - The children's section of the library has many picture books.
  - 图书馆的儿童区有很多绘本。
- **助记**：sect（切割）+ ion → 切割出来的部分 → 部分

### 高一词汇

### 11. **acknowledge** /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：承认；确认；致谢
- **英文释义**：to accept or admit that something is true; to recognize someone's help
- **例句**：
  - He acknowledged that he had made a mistake.
  - 他承认自己犯了一个错误。
  - The author acknowledged the help of her friends in the book.
  - 作者在书中对朋友们的帮助表示了感谢。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ knowledge（知识/了解）→ 朝着了解的方向 → 承认；名词：acknowledgement

### 12. **deceive** /dɪˈsiːv/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：欺骗；蒙骗
- **英文释义**：to make someone believe something that is not true
- **例句**：
  - He tried to deceive his parents by hiding his bad grades.
  - 他试图通过隐藏差成绩来欺骗父母。
  - Don't be deceived by appearances — things are not always what they seem.
  - 不要被外表所欺骗——事情并不总是表面看起来的那样。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ ceive（拿取）→ 暗中拿走真相 → 欺骗；名词：deception；形容词：deceptive

### 13. **principle** /ˈprɪnsəpl/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：原则；原理；准则
- **英文释义**：a basic truth or rule that guides behavior; a law of science
- **例句**：
  - We should follow the principle of fairness in everything we do.
  - 我们做每件事都应该遵循公平的原则。
  - The principle of gravity was discovered by Newton.
  - 万有引力原理是牛顿发现的。
- **助记**：princ（首要的）+ iple → 首要的规则 → 原则；易混淆：principal（校长/主要的）

### 14. **status** /ˈsteɪtəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：地位；身份；状态
- **英文释义**：the social or professional position of someone; the situation at a particular time
- **例句**：
  - Education can help people improve their social status.
  - 教育可以帮助人们提高社会地位。
  - What's the current status of the project?
  - 项目目前的状态如何？
- **助记**：stat（站立）+ us → 站在什么位置 → 地位/状态

### 15. **transform** /trænsˈfɔːm/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：转变；改造；使变形
- **英文释义**：to change something completely in form or character
- **例句**：
  - The Internet has transformed the way people communicate.
  - 互联网改变了人们交流的方式。
  - The old factory was transformed into a modern art museum.
  - 旧工厂被改造成了一座现代艺术博物馆。
- **助记**：trans（跨越）+ form（形状）→ 跨越原来的形状 → 转变；名词：transformation
