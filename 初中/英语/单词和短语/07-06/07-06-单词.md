## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **trudge** /trʌdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：艰难跋涉；费力地走
- **英文释义**：to walk slowly with heavy steps, especially because you are tired
- **例句**：
  - We trudged through the snow for two hours to reach the village.
  - 我们在雪中艰难跋涉了两个小时才到达村庄。
  - The students trudged home after a long day of exams.
  - 漫长的考试日结束后，学生们拖着疲惫的脚步回家。
- **助记**：trudge → "拽着"（谐音）→ 拽着沉重的步伐走 → 艰难跋涉

### 2. **squash** /skwɒʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：压扁；挤压
- **英文释义**：to press something into a flat shape; to squeeze into a small space
- **例句**：
  - He accidentally squashed the cake when he sat on the box.
  - 他坐在盒子上不小心把蛋糕压扁了。
  - We all squashed into the back of the car.
  - 我们全都挤进了车后座。
- **助记**：squash → "死夸食"（谐音）→ 把食物压扁了 → 压扁

### 3. **twitch** /twɪtʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：抽搐；猛拉
- **英文释义**：to make a sudden small movement, often one that you cannot control
- **例句**：
  - His eye twitched nervously during the presentation.
  - 做演讲时他的眼睛紧张地跳动。
  - The rabbit's nose twitched as it sniffed the air.
  - 兔子嗅闻空气时鼻子一动一动的。
- **助记**：twitch → "突一吃"（谐音）→ 突然一抽动就吃一口 → 抽搐

### 4. **mingle** /ˈmɪŋɡl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：混合；交际
- **英文释义**：to mix or move among people at a social event; to combine together
- **例句**：
  - Guests mingled and chatted at the party.
  - 客人们在聚会上交际聊天。
  - The smell of coffee mingled with the scent of freshly baked bread.
  - 咖啡的香气和新鲜面包的香味交融在一起。
- **助记**：mingle → "民哥"（谐音）→ 民哥在人群中混 → 交际；混合

### 5. **alcove** /ˈælkəʊv/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：壁龛；凹室
- **英文释义**：a small area in a room that is formed by one part of a wall being further back than the rest
- **例句**：
  - There was a cozy reading alcove by the window.
  - 窗边有一个舒适的阅读凹室。
  - She placed a bookshelf in the alcove near the stairs.
  - 她在楼梯旁的壁龛里放了一个书架。
- **助记**：alcove → "暗口窝"（谐音）→ 暗处的一个窝 → 壁龛；凹室

### 6. **shingle** /ˈʃɪŋɡl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：瓦片；卵石滩
- **英文释义**：a flat piece of wood, etc. used to cover the roof or wall of a building; small rounded stones on a beach
- **例句**：
  - The old cottage had a roof covered with wooden shingles.
  - 那间老屋的屋顶铺着木瓦片。
  - We walked along the shingle beach collecting smooth stones.
  - 我们沿着卵石海滩散步捡光滑的石头。
- **助记**：shingle → "信哥"（谐音）→ 信哥说这块瓦可以盖房子 → 瓦片

### 7. **helm** /helm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：舵；掌舵位
- **英文释义**：the wheel or handle used to steer a boat or ship; a position of leadership
- **例句**：
  - The captain took the helm and steered the ship through the storm.
  - 船长掌舵驶船穿越风暴。
  - She is at the helm of the company as the new CEO.
  - 她作为新任CEO掌管公司。
- **助记**：helm → "海了没"（谐音）→ 掌舵看海到了没 → 舵

### 8. **coarse** /kɔːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：粗糙的；粗俗的
- **英文释义**：rough in texture; rude and not refined
- **例句**：
  - The coarse fabric scratched my skin.
  - 粗糙的布料刮伤了我的皮肤。
  - His coarse language shocked everyone at the dinner table.
  - 他粗俗的话语震惊了餐桌上的所有人。
- **助记**：coarse → "磕丝"（谐音）→ 摸上去磕磕的丝线 → 粗糙的

### 9. **drab** /dræb/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：单调乏味的；灰暗的
- **英文释义**：dull and boring, especially in colour or appearance
- **例句**：
  - The office walls were painted a drab grey colour.
  - 办公室的墙被漆成了单调的灰色。
  - Life in the small town was drab and uneventful.
  - 小镇上的生活单调乏味。
- **助记**：drab → "追吧"（谐音）→ 太无聊了追点东西吧 → 单调乏味的

### 10. **midway** /ˈmɪdweɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：在中途；在中间
- **英文释义**：in the middle of a period of time, journey, or process
- **例句**：
  - We stopped midway to rest and have lunch.
  - 我们在中途停下来休息吃午饭。
  - Midway through the film, I fell asleep.
  - 电影看到一半我就睡着了。
- **助记**：mid（中间）+ way（路）→ 路的中间 → 在中途

### 高一词汇

### 11. **incapacitate** /ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪteɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使无能力；使丧失行动能力
- **英文释义**：to make someone unable to work or function normally
- **例句**：
  - The injury incapacitated him for several months.
  - 受伤使他丧失行动能力好几个月。
  - A severe headache incapacitated her for the whole afternoon.
  - 严重的头痛让她整个下午都无法工作。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ capacity（能力）+ -ate → 使没有能力 → 使无能力

### 12. **mercenary** /ˈmɜːsənəri/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：雇佣兵；唯利是图的人
- **英文释义**：a soldier who fights for any country or group that pays them; a person interested only in money
- **例句**：
  - The king hired mercenaries to defend his borders.
  - 国王雇佣了雇佣兵来守卫边境。
  - His mercenary attitude towards friendships lost him many friends.
  - 他对待友谊唯利是图的态度让他失去了很多朋友。
- **助记**：mercenar-（雇佣）+ -y → 被雇佣来打仗的人 → 雇佣兵

### 13. **lackadaisical** /ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：懒散的；漫不经心的
- **英文释义**：lacking enthusiasm and determination; carelessly lazy
- **例句**：
  - His lackadaisical attitude towards homework frustrated the teacher.
  - 他对作业漫不经心的态度让老师很头疼。
  - The team's lackadaisical defense cost them the game.
  - 球队懒散的防守让他们输掉了比赛。
- **助记**：lackadaisical → "拉客的懒可"（联想）→ 拉客都很懒 → 懒散的

### 14. **rapacious** /rəˈpeɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：贪婪的；掠夺的
- **英文释义**：having a very strong desire for money or possessions; taking things by force
- **例句**：
  - The rapacious landlord raised the rent every month.
  - 贪婪的房东每个月都涨房租。
  - Rapacious logging has destroyed large areas of the rainforest.
  - 掠夺性伐木摧毁了大片雨林。
- **助记**：rap（抓取）+ -acious → 不断抓取的 → 贪婪的

### 15. **protagonist** /prəˈtæɡənɪst/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：主角；主要人物
- **英文释义**：the main character in a story, film, or play
- **例句**：
  - The protagonist of the novel is a young girl who overcomes many challenges.
  - 这部小说的主角是一个克服许多困难的年轻女孩。
  - Harry Potter is one of the most famous protagonists in literature.
  - 哈利·波特是文学中最著名的主角之一。
- **助记**：proto-（首要的）+ agonist（行动者）→ 首要的行动者 → 主角
