## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **explore** /ɪkˈsplɔː(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：探索；探险；调查
- **英文释义**：to travel to a new place to learn about it; to examine something carefully
- **例句**：
  - Scientists are exploring the deep sea to discover new species.
  - 科学家们正在探索深海以发现新物种。
  - Let's explore different ways to solve this problem.
  - 让我们探索不同的方法来解决这个问题。
- **助记**：ex（向外）+ plore（喊叫/探查）→ 向外探查 → 探索；名词：exploration；explorer（探险家）

### 2. **involve** /ɪnˈvɒlv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：涉及；包含；使参与
- **英文释义**：to include something as a necessary part; to make someone take part
- **例句**：
  - The project involves a lot of teamwork and communication.
  - 这个项目涉及大量的团队合作和交流。
  - More than 200 students were involved in the school play.
  - 超过200名学生参与了学校话剧。
- **助记**：in（在...里）+ volve（卷）→ 卷入其中 → 涉及、参与；固定搭配：be involved in

### 3. **argue** /ˈɑːɡjuː/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：争论；辩论；主张
- **英文释义**：to express disagreement with someone; to give reasons for your opinion
- **例句**：
  - They argued about which movie to watch on Friday night.
  - 他们为周五晚上看哪部电影争论不休。
  - She argued that students should have more free time.
  - 她主张学生应该有更多的自由时间。
- **助记**：argu（论证）+ e → 进行论证 → 争论；名词：argument

### 4. **specific** /spəˈsɪfɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：具体的；特定的；明确的
- **英文释义**：clearly defined or identified; particular
- **例句**：
  - Can you give me a specific example of what you mean?
  - 你能给我一个具体的例子来说明你的意思吗？
  - Each student has a specific task to complete in the group project.
  - 每个学生在小组项目中都有特定的任务要完成。
- **助记**：spec（看）+ ific → 仔细看清楚的 → 具体的；副词：specifically

### 5. **poverty** /ˈpɒvəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：贫穷；贫困
- **英文释义**：the state of being very poor
- **例句**：
  - Many children in poverty don't have enough food to eat.
  - 许多贫困中的孩子没有足够的食物吃。
  - Education is one of the best ways to fight against poverty.
  - 教育是对抗贫困的最好方式之一。
- **助记**：pover（穷的，poor 的变体）+ ty → 贫穷的状态 → 贫困

### 6. **ordinary** /ˈɔːdnri/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：普通的；平凡的；一般的
- **英文释义**：not special or different in any way; normal
- **例句**：
  - It was just an ordinary day — nothing special happened.
  - 这只是普通的一天——没有什么特别的事发生。
  - Ordinary people can also make extraordinary contributions to society.
  - 普通人也能对社会做出非凡的贡献。
- **助记**：order（秩序）→ ordinary → 按照正常秩序的 → 普通的；反义词：extraordinary（非凡的）

### 7. **translate** /trænsˈleɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：翻译；转化
- **英文释义**：to change words from one language into another
- **例句**：
  - Can you translate this sentence from English into Chinese?
  - 你能把这个句子从英语翻译成中文吗？
  - The novel has been translated into more than 30 languages.
  - 这部小说已被翻译成30多种语言。
- **助记**：trans（跨越）+ late（带）→ 把语言带到另一边 → 翻译；名词：translation

### 8. **convenient** /kənˈviːniənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：方便的；便利的
- **英文释义**：easy to use or suitable for what you need; not causing difficulty
- **例句**：
  - Online shopping is very convenient — you don't need to leave your home.
  - 网上购物非常方便——你不需要出门。
  - Is it convenient for you to meet at 3 o'clock this afternoon?
  - 今天下午三点见面对你方便吗？
- **助记**：con（共同）+ ven（来）+ ient → 一切都能来到身边 → 方便的；名词：convenience

### 9. **emotion** /ɪˈməʊʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：情感；情绪
- **英文释义**：a strong feeling such as love, fear, anger, or joy
- **例句**：
  - Music can express different emotions without words.
  - 音乐能不用语言就表达不同的情感。
  - It's healthy to talk about your emotions with someone you trust.
  - 和你信任的人谈论你的情绪是健康的。
- **助记**：e（向外）+ motion（运动）→ 内心向外运动的东西 → 情感；形容词：emotional

### 10. **ignore** /ɪɡˈnɔː(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：忽视；忽略；不理会
- **英文释义**：to pay no attention to something or someone
- **例句**：
  - You shouldn't ignore the teacher's advice about studying.
  - 你不应该忽视老师关于学习的建议。
  - She ignored my question and walked away.
  - 她无视我的问题走开了。
- **助记**：i（不）+ gnore（知道）→ 不想知道 → 忽视；名词：ignorance（无知）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **domestic** /dəˈmestɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：国内的；家庭的
- **英文释义**：relating to or inside a particular country; relating to the home
- **例句**：
  - Domestic tourism has become more popular in recent years.
  - 近年来国内旅游变得更受欢迎。
  - She doesn't like doing domestic chores such as cleaning and washing.
  - 她不喜欢做清洁和洗衣等家务事。
- **助记**：dom（家）+ estic → 与家相关的 → 家庭的、国内的；反义词：foreign / international

### 12. **privilege** /ˈprɪvəlɪdʒ/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：特权；荣幸；优待
- **英文释义**：a special right or advantage that only one person or group has
- **例句**：
  - Education should be a right, not a privilege.
  - 教育应该是一种权利，而不是特权。
  - It's a great privilege to be invited to speak at this conference.
  - 受邀在这次会议上发言是一种莫大的荣幸。
- **助记**：privi（私人的）+ lege（法律）→ 私人的法律权利 → 特权

### 13. **substitute** /ˈsʌbstɪtjuːt/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 替代品；替补；v. 替代
- **英文释义**：n. something used instead of something else; v. to use one thing in place of another
- **例句**：
  - Honey can be used as a substitute for sugar in many recipes.
  - 在很多食谱中蜂蜜可以用来替代糖。
  - The coach substituted the injured player with a fresh one.
  - 教练用一名替补球员换下了受伤的球员。
- **助记**：sub（在下面）+ stitute（站立）→ 站在下面等着上场 → 替代；名词：substitution

### 14. **controversy** /ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：争议；争论
- **英文释义**：a lot of disagreement and argument about something
- **例句**：
  - The new school rule caused a lot of controversy among students.
  - 新校规在学生中引起了很多争议。
  - There is still controversy over whether homework should be reduced.
  - 关于是否应该减少作业仍然存在争议。
- **助记**：contra（反对）+ versy（转）→ 意见向相反方向转 → 争议；形容词：controversial

### 15. **withdraw** /wɪðˈdrɔː/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：撤回；取（钱）；退出
- **英文释义**：to take something back; to take money out of a bank; to stop taking part
- **例句**：
  - He withdrew his application for the scholarship at the last minute.
  - 他在最后一刻撤回了奖学金申请。
  - She withdrew some money from the ATM to pay for the trip.
  - 她从ATM取了些钱来支付旅行费用。
- **助记**：with（向后）+ draw（拉）→ 向后拉 → 撤回；名词：withdrawal；过去式：withdrew
