## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **export** /ɪkˈspɔːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：出口；输出
- **英文释义**：to send goods to another country for sale; goods sent to another country
- **例句**：
  - China exports electronic products to many countries.
  - 中国向许多国家出口电子产品。
  - The country's main exports are tea and coffee.
  - 该国的主要出口商品是茶叶和咖啡。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ port（港口）→ 从港口运出去 → 出口

### 2. **expose** /ɪkˈspəʊz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：暴露；揭露
- **英文释义**：to make something visible or known; to reveal the truth about something
- **例句**：
  - Don't expose your skin to the sun for too long without sunscreen.
  - 没有防晒霜时不要长时间把皮肤暴露在阳光下。
  - The journalist exposed the corruption in the company.
  - 记者揭露了公司的腐败行为。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ pose（放）→ 放到外面让人看见 → 暴露

### 3. **extend** /ɪkˈstend/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：延伸；扩展
- **英文释义**：to make something longer or larger; to stretch out
- **例句**：
  - The teacher decided to extend the deadline for the project.
  - 老师决定延长项目的截止日期。
  - The road extends from the city center to the suburbs.
  - 这条路由市中心延伸到郊区。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ tend（伸展）→ 向外伸展 → 延伸

### 4. **external** /ɪkˈstɜːnl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：外部的；外面的
- **英文释义**：relating to or situated on the outside
- **例句**：
  - The external walls of the building need repainting.
  - 大楼的外墙需要重新粉刷。
  - We need to consider both internal and external factors.
  - 我们需要同时考虑内部和外部因素。
- **助记**：exter（外）+ -nal（形容词）→ 在外面的 → 外部的

### 5. **extinct** /ɪkˈstɪŋkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：灭绝的
- **英文释义**：no longer in existence; having died out
- **例句**：
  - Dinosaurs have been extinct for millions of years.
  - 恐龙已灭绝数百万年了。
  - We must protect endangered species before they become extinct.
  - 我们必须在濒危物种灭绝之前保护它们。
- **助记**：ex-（完全）+ stinct（熄灭）→ 完全熄灭 → 灭绝的

### 6. **extract** /ɪkˈstrækt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：提取；摘录
- **英文释义**：to remove or take out something; to select a passage from a text
- **例句**：
  - We can extract juice from oranges by squeezing them.
  - 我们可以通过挤压橙子来提取果汁。
  - The teacher asked us to extract the main ideas from the article.
  - 老师让我们从文章中摘录主要观点。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ tract（拉）→ 拉出来 → 提取

### 7. **extreme** /ɪkˈstriːm/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：极端的；极度的
- **英文释义**：reaching the highest or most intense degree
- **例句**：
  - The weather was extreme — it was either very hot or very cold.
  - 天气很极端——要么非常热，要么非常冷。
  - She takes extreme care when preparing for exams.
  - 她在准备考试时极其谨慎。
- **助记**：extre-（超出）+ -me → 超出寻常的 → 极端的

### 8. **fabric** /ˈfæbrɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：织物；布料
- **英文释义**：cloth or material made by weaving threads together
- **例句**：
  - The dress is made of a soft, comfortable fabric.
  - 这条裙子用的是柔软舒适的布料。
  - I need to buy some fabric to make a new curtain.
  - 我需要买些布料来做新窗帘。
- **助记**：fabric（制造）→ 制造出来的材料 → 织物

### 9. **facility** /fəˈsɪləti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：设施；设备
- **英文释义**：a place, building, or piece of equipment used for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - Our school has excellent sports facilities including a swimming pool.
  - 我们学校有出色的体育设施，包括游泳池。
  - The library facility is open 24 hours during exam week.
  - 考试周期间图书馆设施24小时开放。
- **助记**：facil（容易）+ -ity（名词）→ 让事情变容易的东西 → 设施

### 10. **factor** /ˈfæktə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：因素；因子
- **英文释义**：a circumstance, fact, or influence that contributes to a result
- **例句**：
  - Time management is an important factor in academic success.
  - 时间管理是学业成功的重要因素。
  - Many factors affect the price of a product.
  - 许多因素会影响产品的价格。
- **助记**：fact（做）+ -or（…的人/物）→ 促成结果的事物 → 因素

### 高一词汇

### 11. **fallacy** /ˈfæləsi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：谬误；谬论
- **英文释义**：a mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument
- **例句**：
  - The idea that money brings happiness is a common fallacy.
  - 金钱带来幸福是一种常见的谬论。
  - The teacher pointed out the logical fallacy in the student's argument.
  - 老师指出了学生论证中的逻辑谬误。
- **助记**：fall（跌倒）+ -acy → 思维"跌倒"出错 → 谬误

### 12. **fastidious** /fæˈstɪdiəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：挑剔的；一丝不苟的
- **英文释义**：very attentive to accuracy and detail; hard to please
- **例句**：
  - She is fastidious about her homework — every word must be perfect.
  - 她对作业极其挑剔——每个字都必须完美。
  - The fastidious editor found several tiny errors in the manuscript.
  - 一丝不苟的编辑在手稿中发现了多处细微错误。
- **助记**：fast（固定）+ idious → 对细节"固定"要求 → 挑剔的

### 13. **fathom** /ˈfæðəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：理解；领悟
- **英文释义**：to understand or comprehend something difficult
- **例句**：
  - I cannot fathom why he would give up such a good opportunity.
  - 我无法理解他为何会放弃这么好的机会。
  - It's hard to fathom the depth of the ocean without special equipment.
  - 没有专业设备很难探明海洋的深度。
- **助记**：fathom 原意"英寻"（测水深度单位）→ 深入探测 → 理解深意

### 14. **fervent** /ˈfɜːvənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：热烈的；热情的
- **英文释义**：having or displaying intense passion or enthusiasm
- **例句**：
  - She is a fervent supporter of environmental protection.
  - 她是环保的热烈支持者。
  - The team expressed their fervent hope to win the championship.
  - 队伍表达了他们赢得冠军的热切希望。
- **助记**：ferv（热）+ -ent → 内心火热的 → 热烈的

### 15. **formidable** /ˈfɔːmɪdəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：令人敬畏的；强大的
- **英文释义**：inspiring fear or respect through being impressively powerful
- **例句**：
  - The opposing team has a formidable defense — it won't be easy to score.
  - 对手队伍防守强大，进球不容易。
  - She faced the formidable challenge of mastering three languages in one year.
  - 她面临一年内掌握三门语言的巨大挑战。
- **助记**：formid（恐惧）+ -able（能…的）→ 能让人产生敬畏的 → 令人敬畏的
