## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **admire** /ədˈmaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：钦佩；赞赏；欣赏
- **英文释义**：to respect and approve of someone; to look at something with pleasure
- **例句**：
  - I admire her for her courage to follow her dream.
  - 我钦佩她追随梦想的勇气。
  - Tourists come from all over the world to admire the beautiful scenery.
  - 游客从世界各地赶来欣赏美丽的风景。
- **助记**：ad（朝向）+ mire（惊奇）→ 朝着令人惊奇的方向看 → 钦佩；名词：admiration

### 2. **compete** /kəmˈpiːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：竞争；比赛
- **英文释义**：to try to win something or be better than others
- **例句**：
  - Ten students will compete in the finals of the English contest.
  - 十名学生将参加英语竞赛的决赛。
  - Companies compete with each other for customers.
  - 公司之间为争夺客户而竞争。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pete（追求）→ 共同追求 → 竞争；名词：competition；形容词：competitive

### 3. **debate** /dɪˈbeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 辩论；争论；v. 辩论
- **英文释义**：n. a formal discussion on a topic; v. to discuss a topic formally
- **例句**：
  - There was a heated debate about whether students should use mobile phones at school.
  - 关于学生是否应该在学校使用手机，有一场激烈的辩论。
  - The students debated the advantages and disadvantages of online learning.
  - 学生们辩论了在线学习的优缺点。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ bate（打）→ 双方相互攻击对方观点 → 辩论

### 4. **gentle** /ˈdʒentl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：温柔的；和蔼的；轻柔的
- **英文释义**：kind, calm, and careful; not rough or violent
- **例句**：
  - She spoke in a gentle voice to calm the crying child.
  - 她用温柔的声音安抚哭泣的孩子。
  - A gentle breeze blew through the window on the spring morning.
  - 春天的早晨，一阵轻柔的微风吹过窗户。
- **助记**：gent（出生好的，gentleman 的词根）+ le → 出身好的人待人温和 → 温柔的；副词：gently

### 5. **hesitate** /ˈhezɪteɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：犹豫；踌躇
- **英文释义**：to be slow to speak or act because you feel uncertain
- **例句**：
  - Don't hesitate to ask for help when you need it.
  - 需要帮助时不要犹豫。
  - She hesitated for a moment before answering the question.
  - 她在回答问题前犹豫了一下。
- **助记**：hesit（粘住）+ ate → 像被粘住走不动 → 犹豫；名词：hesitation

### 6. **inspire** /ɪnˈspaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：激励；鼓舞；启发
- **英文释义**：to give someone the desire or confidence to do something
- **例句**：
  - The story of Helen Keller inspires millions of people around the world.
  - 海伦·凯勒的故事激励了全世界数百万人。
  - The beautiful painting inspired her to start learning art.
  - 这幅美丽的画作启发了她开始学习艺术。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ spire（呼吸）→ 注入灵气 → 激励；名词：inspiration

### 7. **obtain** /əbˈteɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：获得；取得
- **英文释义**：to get something, especially by effort
- **例句**：
  - She obtained a scholarship to study at a famous university.
  - 她获得了在一所著名大学学习的奖学金。
  - You can obtain more information from the school website.
  - 你可以从学校网站获取更多信息。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ tain（持有）→ 朝着目标持有 → 获得

### 8. **pollution** /pəˈluːʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：污染；污染物
- **英文释义**：damage caused to air, water, or land by harmful substances
- **例句**：
  - Air pollution is a serious problem in many large cities.
  - 空气污染是许多大城市的严重问题。
  - We must take action to reduce water pollution in rivers.
  - 我们必须采取行动减少河流中的水污染。
- **助记**：pollut（弄脏）+ ion → 弄脏的状态 → 污染；动词：pollute

### 9. **schedule** /ˈʃedjuːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：时间表；日程安排
- **英文释义**：a plan of activities with times for each one
- **例句**：
  - What's your schedule for this weekend?
  - 你这个周末的日程安排是什么？
  - The train arrived on schedule despite the bad weather.
  - 尽管天气恶劣，火车还是按时到达了。
- **助记**：schedule 可联想"时刻"表；固定搭配：on schedule（按时）/ ahead of schedule（提前）/ behind schedule（落后于计划）

### 10. **reward** /rɪˈwɔːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 奖赏；报酬；v. 奖赏
- **英文释义**：n. something given in return for good behavior or work; v. to give someone a reward
- **例句**：
  - Hard work will bring its own reward in the end.
  - 努力工作最终会带来回报。
  - The school rewarded the students who won the science competition.
  - 学校奖励了在科学竞赛中获胜的学生。
- **助记**：re（回来）+ ward（看守/关注）→ 关注后给予回报 → 奖赏；形容词：rewarding（有回报的）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **consequence** /ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：结果；后果
- **英文释义**：a result of something, especially a bad result
- **例句**：
  - You must accept the consequences of your own decisions.
  - 你必须接受自己决定的后果。
  - Climate change will have serious consequences for all life on Earth.
  - 气候变化将对地球上所有生命产生严重后果。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ sequ（跟随）+ ence → 跟随而来的事 → 后果；固定搭配：as a consequence（因此）

### 12. **occupation** /ˌɒkjuˈpeɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：职业；占用；消遣
- **英文释义**：a job or profession; the act of living in or using a place
- **例句**：
  - Please write your name, age, and occupation on the form.
  - 请在表格上填写你的姓名、年龄和职业。
  - Teaching is a noble occupation that shapes the future.
  - 教书是一种塑造未来的高尚职业。
- **助记**：occupy（占据/从事）+ ation → 从事的事情 → 职业

### 13. **participate** /pɑːˈtɪsɪpeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：参加；参与
- **英文释义**：to take part in an activity or event
- **例句**：
  - All students are encouraged to participate in after-school activities.
  - 鼓励所有学生参加课外活动。
  - She actively participated in the group discussion.
  - 她积极参与了小组讨论。
- **助记**：part（部分）+ icip + ate → 成为一部分 → 参与；名词：participation；固定搭配：participate in

### 14. **authentic** /ɔːˈθentɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：真实的；正宗的；可信的
- **英文释义**：real and genuine; not copied or fake
- **例句**：
  - This restaurant serves authentic Sichuan cuisine.
  - 这家餐厅供应正宗的四川菜。
  - The museum confirmed that the painting is authentic.
  - 博物馆确认这幅画是真品。
- **助记**：auth（真实的，author 同源）+ entic → 来自原作者的 → 真实的；名词：authenticity

### 15. **compromise** /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 妥协；折中；v. 妥协；让步
- **英文释义**：n. an agreement reached by each side giving up something; v. to settle a disagreement by making concessions
- **例句**：
  - They reached a compromise after a long discussion.
  - 经过长时间的讨论，他们达成了妥协。
  - Neither side was willing to compromise on this issue.
  - 双方都不愿意在这个问题上妥协。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ promise（承诺）→ 共同做出承诺让步 → 妥协
