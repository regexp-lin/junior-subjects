## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **cause** /kɔːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：引起；导致；造成
- **英文释义**：to make something happen, especially something bad
- **例句**：
  - Heavy rain caused the river to flood.
  - 大雨导致河水泛滥。
  - What caused the delay in the project?
  - 是什么导致了项目延期？
- **助记**：cause 本身也是名词「原因」；caus（原因）+ e → 使成为原因 → 引起；常用搭配：cause sb. to do sth.

### 2. **charge** /tʃɑːdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：收费；控告；充电；冲锋
- **英文释义**：to ask an amount of money for something; to accuse someone of a crime
- **例句**：
  - The hotel charges 200 yuan per night.
  - 这家酒店每晚收费200元。
  - He was charged with theft and taken to the police station.
  - 他被控盗窃并被带到警察局。
- **助记**：charge 多义：charge money（收费）、charge sb. with（控告）、charge the battery（充电）；名词：charge（费用；指控）

### 3. **choose** /tʃuːz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：选择；挑选；决定
- **英文释义**：to decide which one you want from a number of things
- **例句**：
  - You can choose any book from the shelf.
  - 你可以从书架上任意挑选一本书。
  - She chose to study abroad after graduation.
  - 她决定毕业后出国留学。
- **助记**：choose → chose（过去式）→ chosen（过去分词）；名词：choice（选择）

### 4. **combine** /kəmˈbaɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：结合；联合；合并
- **英文释义**：to join or mix two or more things together
- **例句**：
  - We need to combine our efforts to solve this problem.
  - 我们需要共同努力来解决这个问题。
  - The two companies combined to form a larger one.
  - 两家公司合并成一家更大的公司。
- **助记**：com（一起）+ bine（两个）→ 两个放在一起 → 结合；名词：combination（结合）

### 5. **chance** /tʃɑːns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：机会；可能性；偶然
- **英文释义**：a possibility of something happening; an opportunity
- **例句**：
  - This is your last chance to apply for the scholarship.
  - 这是你申请奖学金的最后机会。
  - There's a good chance that it will rain tomorrow.
  - 明天很可能下雨。
- **助记**：by chance（偶然）；take a chance（冒险一试）；chance to do（有机会做）

### 6. **character** /ˈkærəktə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：性格；品格；角色；字符
- **英文释义**：the qualities that make someone or something different from others; a person in a story
- **例句**：
  - She has a strong character and never gives up easily.
  - 她性格坚强，从不轻易放弃。
  - The main character in the novel is a young detective.
  - 小说中的主角是一位年轻的侦探。
- **助记**：character 多义：人物性格、小说角色、汉字字符；形容词：characteristic（典型的）

### 7. **choice** /tʃɔɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：选择；抉择；精选品
- **英文释义**：an act of choosing between two or more possibilities; something that you can choose
- **例句**：
  - You have no choice but to finish the work today.
  - 你别无选择，只能今天完成工作。
  - The menu offers a wide choice of dishes.
  - 菜单提供多种菜品选择。
- **助记**：动词 choose 的名词形式；have no choice but to do（别无选择只能…）

### 8. **chief** /tʃiːf/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：主要的；首席的；最重要的
- **英文释义**：most important; main; leading
- **例句**：
  - The chief reason for his success is hard work.
  - 他成功的主要原因是努力。
  - She is the chief editor of the magazine.
  - 她是这家杂志的主编。
- **助记**：chief 也可作名词「首领；酋长」；chiefly（主要地）

### 9. **civil** /ˈsɪvl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：公民的；民事的；文明的；有礼貌的
- **英文释义**：relating to the people who live in a country; polite
- **例句**：
  - Every citizen has civil rights and responsibilities.
  - 每个公民都有公民权利和义务。
  - Let's try to have a civil discussion about this.
  - 让我们试着文明地讨论这个问题。
- **助记**：civil 来自拉丁语 civis（公民）；civilization（文明）；civil war（内战）

### 10. **clearly** /ˈklɪəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：清楚地；明显地；无疑地
- **英文释义**：in a way that is easy to see, hear, or understand
- **例句**：
  - Please speak more clearly so everyone can hear you.
  - 请说得更清楚些，这样大家都能听到。
  - The results clearly show that the plan works.
  - 结果清楚地表明计划是有效的。
- **助记**：clear（清楚的）+ ly → 清楚地；形容词：clear（清晰的）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **coincide** /ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：同时发生；一致；重合
- **英文释义**：to happen at the same time or to be the same
- **例句**：
  - Our birthdays coincide—we were born on the same day.
  - 我们的生日是同一天。
  - His views don't coincide with mine on this issue.
  - 在这个问题上，他的观点与我不一致。
- **助记**：co（一起）+ in（在）+ cide（落下）→ 一起落下 →  coinciding；名词：coincidence（巧合）

### 12. **collapse** /kəˈlæps/ `高一`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：倒塌；崩溃；昏倒；（价格）暴跌
- **英文释义**：to fall down suddenly; to fail suddenly; to fall unconscious
- **例句**：
  - The old building collapsed during the earthquake.
  - 那座旧建筑在地震中倒塌了。
  - The patient collapsed in the street and was taken to hospital.
  - 病人在街上昏倒，被送往医院。
- **助记**：col（一起）+ lapse（滑落）→ 一起滑落 → 倒塌；economic collapse（经济崩溃）

### 13. **colleague** /ˈkɒliːɡ/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：同事；同僚
- **英文释义**：a person that you work with, especially in a profession
- **例句**：
  - She discussed the project with her colleagues.
  - 她和同事们讨论了项目。
  - My colleague from the sales department will join the meeting.
  - 销售部的同事将参加这次会议。
- **助记**：col（一起）+ league（联盟）→ 一起联盟的人 → 同事；同义：coworker

### 14. **complement** /ˈkɒmplɪment/ `高一`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：补充；补足；互补；（语法）补语
- **英文释义**：to add something that makes another thing complete or better
- **例句**：
  - The wine complements the flavor of the dish perfectly.
  - 这款酒完美地衬托了菜肴的味道。
  - The two skills complement each other well.
  - 这两种技能相辅相成。
- **助记**：com（完全）+ ple（填满）+ ment → 使完全 → 补充；注意与 compliment（赞美）区分

### 15. **confer** /kənˈfɜː(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：商议；讨论；授予（学位、荣誉等）
- **英文释义**：to discuss something with others; to give an honor, degree, etc. to someone
- **例句**：
  - The managers conferred about the new policy.
  - 经理们就新政策进行了商议。
  - The university will confer an honorary degree on the scientist.
  - 大学将授予这位科学家荣誉学位。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ fer（带来）→ 把意见带来一起 → 商议；名词：conference（会议）
