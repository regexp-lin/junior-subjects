## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **gradual** /ˈɡrædʒuəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：逐渐的
- **英文释义**：happening or changing slowly over a period of time
- **例句**：
  - The patient showed gradual improvement over the weeks.
  - 病人几周来逐渐好转。
  - We need to make a gradual transition to the new system.
  - 我们需要逐渐过渡到新系统。
- **助记**：grad（等级、步伐）+ -ual（形容词）→ 一步步进行的 → 逐渐的

### 2. **grant** /ɡrɑːnt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：授予；拨款
- **英文释义**：to give or allow something, especially officially; money given for a specific purpose
- **例句**：
  - The university granted her a scholarship for academic excellence.
  - 大学因她学业优秀授予她奖学金。
  - The government provided a grant for the research project.
  - 政府为研究项目提供了拨款。
- **助记**：grant 源自拉丁语"同意"→ 正式给予或允许 → 授予；拨款

### 3. **grasp** /ɡrɑːsp/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：抓住；理解
- **英文释义**：to hold firmly with the hand; to understand something completely
- **例句**：
  - He grasped the rope tightly to avoid falling.
  - 他紧紧抓住绳子以免掉落。
  - I couldn't grasp the concept at first, but now I understand it.
  - 起初我无法理解这个概念，但现在明白了。
- **助记**：grasp 与 grip 相近 → 用手抓住或用心"抓住"概念 → 抓住；理解

### 4. **grateful** /ˈɡreɪtfl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：感激的
- **英文释义**：feeling or showing thanks because someone has done something kind
- **例句**：
  - I'm grateful for your help during the difficult time.
  - 我感激你在困难时期的帮助。
  - She was grateful that the doctor had saved her child's life.
  - 她感激医生救了她孩子的命。
- **助记**：grat（恩惠、感谢）+ -ful（充满）→ 充满感激之情 → 感激的

### 5. **gravity** /ˈɡrævəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：重力；严重性
- **英文释义**：the force that pulls objects toward the earth; the seriousness or importance of something
- **例句**：
  - Gravity keeps us on the ground.
  - 重力让我们站在地面上。
  - The gravity of the situation requires immediate action.
  - 局势的严重性需要立即采取行动。
- **助记**：grav（重）+ -ity（名词）→ 物理上的重量之力或事情的"重量"→ 重力；严重性

### 6. **grief** /ɡriːf/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：悲伤；悲痛
- **英文释义**：deep sadness, especially caused by someone's death
- **例句**：
  - She was overwhelmed with grief after losing her mother.
  - 失去母亲后她悲痛欲绝。
  - The family expressed their grief at the funeral.
  - 家人在葬礼上表达了他们的悲痛。
- **助记**：grief 与 grieve（使悲痛）同源 → 沉重的心情 → 悲伤；悲痛

### 7. **grim** /ɡrɪm/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：严峻的；冷酷的
- **英文释义**：very serious and worrying; looking or sounding stern and forbidding
- **例句**：
  - The news about the economy was grim.
  - 关于经济的消息很严峻。
  - He had a grim expression on his face when he delivered the bad news.
  - 当他宣布坏消息时，脸上表情冷酷。
- **助记**：grim 与 grime（污垢）相关 → 黑暗、令人不悦的 → 严峻的；冷酷的

### 8. **grip** /ɡrɪp/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：紧握；控制
- **英文释义**：to hold something tightly; a firm hold; control or understanding
- **例句**：
  - She gripped the handlebars and rode down the hill.
  - 她紧握车把骑下山坡。
  - The new leader has a tight grip on the organization.
  - 新领导牢牢掌控着这个组织。
- **助记**：grip 与 grasp 同源 → 用力握住或控制 → 紧握；控制

### 9. **grocery** /ˈɡrəʊsəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：食品杂货店
- **英文释义**：a store that sells food and other household items
- **例句**：
  - I need to stop by the grocery on my way home.
  - 回家路上我得去趟食品杂货店。
  - She works part-time at a grocery near her house.
  - 她在离家不远的食品杂货店兼职。
- **助记**：groc（批发、大宗的）+ -ery（场所）→ 卖日常食品杂货的店铺 → 食品杂货店

### 10. **guarantee** /ˌɡærənˈtiː/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：保证；担保
- **英文释义**：to promise that something will happen or be done; a formal promise or assurance
- **例句**：
  - The company guarantees refunds within 30 days.
  - 公司保证30天内退款。
  - There's no guarantee that the plan will work.
  - 无法保证这个计划会成功。
- **助记**：guarant（保证人）+ -ee（名词）→ 作出保证的行为或承诺 → 保证；担保

### 高一词汇

### 11. **impeccable** /ɪmˈpekəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：完美无瑕的
- **英文释义**：without any fault or error; perfect
- **例句**：
  - She has impeccable taste in fashion.
  - 她的时尚品味完美无瑕。
  - The hotel prides itself on providing impeccable service.
  - 这家酒店以提供完美无瑕的服务为荣。
- **助记**：im-（不）+ pecc（犯罪、犯错）+ -able（形容词）→ 不会犯错的 → 完美无瑕的

### 12. **impede** /ɪmˈpiːd/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：妨碍；阻止
- **英文释义**：to delay or prevent someone or something by obstructing them
- **例句**：
  - Heavy traffic impeded our progress to the airport.
  - 交通拥堵妨碍了我们赶往机场。
  - Don't let fear impede you from pursuing your dreams.
  - 别让恐惧阻止你追求梦想。
- **助记**：im-（入）+ ped（脚）→ 脚被缠住 → 妨碍；阻止

### 13. **imperative** /ɪmˈperətɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：必要的；紧急的
- **英文释义**：extremely important and requiring immediate action; something that must be done
- **例句**：
  - It is imperative that we act now to protect the environment.
  - 我们现在必须采取行动保护环境，这很紧急。
  - Finding a solution has become an imperative for the team.
  - 找到解决方案已成为团队的当务之急。
- **助记**：imper（命令）+ -ative（形容词）→ 如命令般必须执行的 → 必要的；紧急的

### 14. **implicit** /ɪmˈplɪsɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：含蓄的；隐含的
- **英文释义**：implied or understood though not directly stated
- **例句**：
  - There was an implicit agreement between them, though nothing was written.
  - 他们之间有隐含的默契，尽管没有书面记录。
  - The criticism was implicit in her tone of voice.
  - 批评隐含在她的语气中。
- **助记**：im-（内）+ plic（折叠、卷入）+ -it（形容词）→ 折叠在内的、未明说的 → 含蓄的；隐含的

### 15. **impromptu** /ɪmˈprɒmptjuː/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj./adv.
- **中文释义**：即兴的
- **英文释义**：done without preparation or planning; spontaneously
- **例句**：
  - He gave an impromptu speech at the wedding.
  - 他在婚礼上发表了即兴演讲。
  - The band played an impromptu concert in the park.
  - 乐队在公园里进行了一场即兴演出。
- **助记**：im-（不）+ prompt（准备）→ 未经准备的 → 即兴的
