## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **arrange** /əˈreɪndʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：安排；整理；筹备
- **英文释义**：to plan or organize something; to put things in a particular order
- **例句**：
  - Could you arrange a meeting with the teacher for next Monday?
  - 你能安排下周一和老师见个面吗？
  - She arranged the books on the shelf by color.
  - 她按颜色把书架上的书整理好了。
- **助记**：ar（加强）+ range（排列）→ 使排列好 → 安排；常用搭配：arrange for sb to do sth（安排某人做某事）

### 2. **avoid** /əˈvɔɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：避免；逃避；躲开
- **英文释义**：to prevent something from happening; to stay away from someone or something
- **例句**：
  - We should avoid making the same mistakes again.
  - 我们应该避免再犯同样的错误。
  - He crossed the street to avoid meeting his former classmate.
  - 他穿过马路以避开遇到以前的同学。
- **助记**：a（离开）+ void（空）→ 躲到空处 → 避免；后接 doing，不接 to do

### 3. **borrow** /ˈbɒrəʊ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：借入；借用
- **英文释义**：to take and use something that belongs to someone else, with the intention of returning it
- **例句**：
  - Can I borrow your pen for a moment?
  - 我能借你的笔用一下吗？
  - She borrowed some money from her parents to buy the bike.
  - 她从父母那里借了些钱买自行车。
- **助记**：borrow 借入 ↔ lend 借出；borrow from sb（向某人借）

### 4. **breathe** /briːð/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：呼吸；喘气
- **英文释义**：to take air into and let it out of your lungs
- **例句**：
  - Take a deep breathe and relax before the exam.
  - 考试前深呼吸放松一下。
  - The room was so stuffy that it was hard to breathe.
  - 房间太闷了，呼吸都很困难。
- **助记**：breath（n. 呼吸）→ breathe（v. 呼吸）；注意拼写：breath 名词，breathe 动词

### 5. **accident** /ˈæksɪdənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：事故；意外；偶然
- **英文释义**：something that happens by chance, especially something bad
- **例句**：
  - He was injured in a car accident last week.
  - 他上周在车祸中受伤了。
  - I found the key by accident when I was cleaning the room.
  - 我打扫房间时偶然找到了钥匙。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ cid（落下）+ ent → 意外落下 → 事故；by accident = by chance（偶然）

### 6. **advantage** /ədˈvɑːntɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：优势；好处；利益
- **英文释义**：a condition that helps you to be better or more successful than others
- **例句**：
  - Being tall is an advantage in basketball.
  - 身高在篮球运动中是一个优势。
  - What are the advantages of studying abroad?
  - 出国留学有什么好处？
- **助记**：advantage 优势 ↔ disadvantage 劣势；take advantage of（利用）

### 7. **advice** /ədˈvaɪs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：建议；忠告；劝告
- **英文释义**：an opinion or recommendation offered as a guide to action
- **例句**：
  - My teacher gave me some good advice on how to improve my English.
  - 老师给了我一些提高英语的好建议。
  - I need your advice on which university to choose.
  - 我需要你帮我参谋选哪所大学。
- **助记**：advice 不可数名词；advise 是动词；a piece of advice（一条建议）

### 8. **active** /ˈæktɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：积极的；活跃的；主动的
- **英文释义**：involved in activity; energetic; taking action
- **例句**：
  - She is very active in class and always raises her hand.
  - 她在课堂上很积极，总是举手发言。
  - Regular exercise helps to keep you active and healthy.
  - 经常锻炼有助于保持活力和健康。
- **助记**：act（行动）+ ive → 有行动力的 → 积极的；名词：activity（活动）

### 9. **amazing** /əˈmeɪzɪŋ/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：令人惊叹的；了不起的
- **英文释义**：causing great surprise or wonder; very impressive
- **例句**：
  - The view from the top of the mountain was amazing.
  - 从山顶看到的景色令人惊叹。
  - You did an amazing job on the presentation!
  - 你的演讲做得太棒了！
- **助记**：amaze（使惊讶）+ ing → 令人惊讶的；动词：amaze（使惊奇）

### 10. **actually** /ˈæktʃuəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：实际上；事实上；的确
- **英文释义**：in fact; really; used to emphasize the truth
- **例句**：
  - I thought he was American, but actually he's from Canada.
  - 我以为他是美国人，但实际上他来自加拿大。
  - The movie was actually better than I expected.
  - 这部电影实际上比我想象的要好。
- **助记**：actual（实际的）+ ly → 实际上；常用于纠正或强调

### 高一词汇

### 11. **analogy** /əˈnælədʒi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：类比；比喻；相似
- **英文释义**：a comparison between two things that are similar in some way
- **例句**：
  - The teacher used an analogy between the human heart and a pump to explain circulation.
  - 老师用心脏和泵的类比来解释血液循环。
  - Drawing an analogy between learning and building a house can help us understand the process.
  - 把学习比作盖房子有助于我们理解这个过程。
- **助记**：ana（根据）+ log（比例）+ y → 按比例比较 → 类比；by analogy（通过类比）

### 12. **anonymous** /əˈnɒnɪməs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：匿名的；无名的；不具名的
- **英文释义**：without a name; of unknown name; not identified
- **例句**：
  - The donation was made by an anonymous benefactor.
  - 这笔捐款来自一位匿名捐助者。
  - You can leave anonymous feedback on the website.
  - 你可以在网站上留下匿名反馈。
- **助记**：an（无）+ onym（名字）+ ous → 没有名字的 → 匿名的；名词：anonymity（匿名）

### 13. **appendix** /əˈpendɪks/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：附录；阑尾
- **英文释义**：a section at the end of a book with extra information; a small organ in the body
- **例句**：
  - Please refer to the appendix for detailed data.
  - 请参阅附录获取详细数据。
  - The report includes an appendix with all the survey results.
  - 报告附有包含所有调查结果的附录。
- **助记**：ap（朝向）+ pend（悬挂）+ ix → 挂在后面的 → 附录；复数：appendices

### 14. **assimilate** /əˈsɪməleɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：吸收；同化；消化理解
- **英文释义**：to take in and understand information; to become part of a group
- **例句**：
  - It takes time to assimilate all the new vocabulary.
  - 吸收所有新词汇需要时间。
  - Many immigrants have assimilated into the local culture.
  - 许多移民已经融入了当地文化。
- **助记**：as（使）+ simil（相同）+ ate → 使相同 → 同化；名词：assimilation（同化）

### 15. **attribute** /əˈtrɪbjuːt/ v. 或 /ˈætrɪbjuːt/ n. `高一`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：v. 把…归因于；认为…属于 n. 属性；特质
- **英文释义**：v. to regard as caused by or belonging to; n. a quality or feature
- **例句**：
  - She attributes her success to hard work and perseverance.
  - 她把成功归因于努力和坚持。
  - Patience is an important attribute for a teacher.
  - 耐心是教师的重要品质。
- **助记**：at（朝向）+ tribute（给予）→ 把…归于；attribute A to B（把A归因于B）
