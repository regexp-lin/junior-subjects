## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **plead** /pliːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：恳求；辩护
- **英文释义**：to ask someone for something in an urgent and emotional way; to state in a court of law whether you are guilty or not
- **例句**：
  - She pleaded with her parents to let her go to the party.
  - 她恳求父母让她去参加聚会。
  - The lawyer advised his client to plead not guilty.
  - 律师建议他的当事人做无罪辩护。
- **助记**：plead → "扑来的"（谐音）→ 扑过来恳求 → 恳求；辩护

### 2. **gleam** /ɡliːm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：闪烁；发微光
- **英文释义**：to shine softly; to produce a small, bright light
- **例句**：
  - The stars gleamed in the dark sky.
  - 星星在夜空中闪烁。
  - Her eyes gleamed with excitement when she heard the good news.
  - 听到好消息时，她的眼睛闪烁着兴奋的光芒。
- **助记**：gleam → "光临"（谐音）→ 光芒来临 → 闪烁；发微光

### 3. **stagger** /ˈstæɡə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：蹒跚；使震惊
- **英文释义**：to walk or move unsteadily as if about to fall; to shock or surprise someone greatly
- **例句**：
  - The injured man staggered to the nearest hospital.
  - 受伤的人蹒跚着走向最近的医院。
  - I was staggered by the amount of homework we received.
  - 我被布置的作业量震惊了。
- **助记**：stagger → "石头哥"（谐音）→ 石头哥走路摇摇晃晃 → 蹒跚

### 4. **curb** /kɜːb/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：抑制；控制
- **英文释义**：to control or limit something, especially something undesirable
- **例句**：
  - The government took measures to curb pollution.
  - 政府采取措施抑制污染。
  - You need to curb your spending if you want to save money.
  - 如果想存钱，你需要控制开支。
- **助记**：curb → "刻薄"（谐音）→ 对自己刻薄就是抑制 → 抑制；控制

### 5. **petal** /ˈpetl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：花瓣
- **英文释义**：one of the coloured parts of a flower that surround its centre
- **例句**：
  - The rose petals fell gently to the ground.
  - 玫瑰花瓣轻轻落在地上。
  - She picked up a petal and put it in her book as a bookmark.
  - 她捡起一片花瓣夹进书里当书签。
- **助记**：petal → "拍他"（谐音）→ 花瓣拍打在身上 → 花瓣

### 6. **wreck** /rek/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：残骸；失事船只
- **英文释义**：a vehicle, building, or ship that has been badly damaged or destroyed
- **例句**：
  - Divers discovered a wreck at the bottom of the sea.
  - 潜水员在海底发现了一艘沉船残骸。
  - The car was a complete wreck after the accident.
  - 事故之后那辆车完全成了一堆废铁。
- **助记**：wreck → "弱客"（谐音）→ 弱不禁风的客船被摧毁 → 残骸

### 7. **riddle** /ˈrɪdl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：谜语；难解之事
- **英文释义**：a question or statement that is difficult to understand and has a clever or amusing answer; something mysterious
- **例句**：
  - Can you solve this riddle? "What has hands but can't clap?"
  - 你能猜出这个谜语吗？"什么有手却不能拍？"
  - The disappearance of the painting remains a riddle.
  - 那幅画的失踪至今仍是一个谜。
- **助记**：riddle → "日到"（谐音）→ 每日到来一个新谜语 → 谜语

### 8. **foggy** /ˈfɒɡi/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有雾的；模糊的
- **英文释义**：full of or covered with fog; not clear, confused
- **例句**：
  - It was a foggy morning, and we could hardly see the road.
  - 那是一个有雾的早晨，我们几乎看不见路。
  - I have only a foggy memory of what happened that day.
  - 我对那天发生的事只有模糊的记忆。
- **助记**：fog（雾）+ -gy → 有雾的 → 有雾的；模糊的

### 9. **thrifty** /ˈθrɪfti/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：节俭的；节约的
- **英文释义**：using money and other resources carefully and not wastefully
- **例句**：
  - My grandmother is very thrifty — she never wastes food.
  - 我奶奶非常节俭，从不浪费食物。
  - Being thrifty doesn't mean being cheap; it means spending wisely.
  - 节俭不等于小气，而是花钱有道。
- **助记**：thrift（节俭）+ -y → 节俭的

### 10. **namely** /ˈneɪmli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：即；也就是
- **英文释义**：used to introduce specific details about something just mentioned
- **例句**：
  - Only one student passed the exam, namely Tom.
  - 只有一个学生通过了考试，即汤姆。
  - She has two hobbies, namely reading and painting.
  - 她有两个爱好，即阅读和绘画。
- **助记**：name（名字）+ -ly → 点名道姓地说 → 即；也就是

### 高一词汇

### 11. **reverberate** /rɪˈvɜːbəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：回响；产生深远影响
- **英文释义**：to be repeated as an echo; to have a continuing and serious effect
- **例句**：
  - The thunder reverberated through the valley.
  - 雷声在山谷中回响。
  - The effects of the financial crisis reverberated around the world.
  - 金融危机的影响在全球产生了深远反响。
- **助记**：re-（反复）+ verber（振动）+ -ate → 反复振动 → 回响

### 12. **obliterate** /əˈblɪtəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：消灭；彻底摧毁
- **英文释义**：to destroy something completely so that nothing remains
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake obliterated the entire village.
  - 地震彻底摧毁了整个村庄。
  - The heavy rain obliterated the footprints on the path.
  - 大雨抹去了小路上的脚印。
- **助记**：ob-（反）+ liter（字母/文字）+ -ate → 把文字消除 → 消灭；彻底摧毁

### 13. **inscrutable** /ɪnˈskruːtəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：难以理解的；高深莫测的
- **英文释义**：impossible to understand or interpret
- **例句**：
  - The professor's inscrutable expression gave no hint of the exam results.
  - 教授高深莫测的表情丝毫没有透露考试成绩。
  - The ancient text was inscrutable to modern readers.
  - 这篇古文对现代读者来说晦涩难懂。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ scrut（审视）+ -able → 无法审视的 → 高深莫测的

### 14. **incredulous** /ɪnˈkredjələs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不相信的；怀疑的
- **英文释义**：unwilling or unable to believe something
- **例句**：
  - She gave me an incredulous look when I told her the news.
  - 当我告诉她这个消息时，她用不可置信的目光看着我。
  - The audience was incredulous at the magician's trick.
  - 观众对魔术师的把戏感到难以置信。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ cred（相信）+ -ulous → 不相信的 → 怀疑的

### 15. **epitaph** /ˈepɪtɑːf/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：墓志铭；挽联
- **英文释义**：a short text written in memory of a person who has died, especially on a gravestone
- **例句**：
  - The epitaph on the tombstone read "Forever in our hearts."
  - 墓碑上的墓志铭写着"永远活在我们心中"。
  - His greatest work became his epitaph — a lasting tribute to his genius.
  - 他最伟大的作品成了他的墓志铭——对他才华的永恒致敬。
- **助记**：epi-（在……上）+ taph（墓）→ 写在墓上的文字 → 墓志铭
