## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **incline** /ɪnˈklaɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：倾斜；倾向
- **英文释义**：to lean or slope; a tendency or preference towards something
- **例句**：
  - The road inclines steeply towards the mountain.
  - 这条路向山的方向陡峭倾斜。
  - I incline to believe that he is telling the truth.
  - 我倾向于相信他说的是真的。
- **助记**：in-（ towards）+ cline（倾斜）→ 朝某方向倾斜 → 倾斜；倾向

### 2. **index** /ˈɪndeks/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：索引；指数
- **英文释义**：an alphabetical list at the end of a book; a number that shows the level or value of something
- **例句**：
  - Look up the topic in the index at the back of the book.
  - 在书后的索引中查找该主题。
  - The consumer price index rose by 2% last month.
  - 上个月消费者物价指数上涨了2%。
- **助记**：index 源自拉丁语 index"指示者"→ 指向内容位置 → 索引；指数

### 3. **indicate** /ˈɪndɪkeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：表明；指示
- **英文释义**：to show or suggest something; to point to or signal
- **例句**：
  - The survey indicates that most people prefer online shopping.
  - 调查表明大多数人更喜欢网购。
  - He indicated left before turning the corner.
  - 拐弯前他打了左转向灯。
- **助记**：in-（加强）+ dic（说）+ -ate → 明确说出 → 表明；指示

### 4. **indulge** /ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：沉溺；纵容
- **英文释义**：to allow oneself or someone else to have or do something enjoyable; to satisfy a desire
- **例句**：
  - She indulged in a long hot bath after work.
  - 下班后她尽情享受了一个长长的热水澡。
  - The parents indulged their child with too many toys.
  - 父母太纵容孩子，给他买了太多玩具。
- **助记**：indulge 源自拉丁语 indulgere"宽容"→ 对欲望宽容 → 沉溺；纵容

### 5. **infant** /ˈɪnfənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：婴儿
- **英文释义**：a very young child or baby
- **例句**：
  - The infant was sleeping peacefully in the cradle.
  - 婴儿在摇篮里睡得正香。
  - Infant mortality has decreased significantly in recent decades.
  - 近几十年来婴儿死亡率显著下降。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ fant（说话）→ 还不会说话的 → 婴儿

### 6. **inflate** /ɪnˈfleɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：膨胀；使充气
- **英文释义**：to fill with air or gas; to increase in size or amount
- **例句**：
  - He inflated the tires before the long trip.
  - 长途旅行前他给轮胎充了气。
  - The government was accused of inflating the unemployment figures.
  - 政府被指控虚报失业数据。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ flat（吹气）+ -e → 把气吹进去 → 膨胀；使充气

### 7. **inform** /ɪnˈfɔːrm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：通知；告知
- **英文释义**：to tell someone about something; to give information
- **例句**：
  - Please inform me if there are any changes to the schedule.
  - 如有日程变动请通知我。
  - The police informed the family of the accident.
  - 警方将事故情况告知了家属。
- **助记**：in-（ into）+ form（形状）→ 把信息赋予形式 → 通知；告知

### 8. **inhabit** /ɪnˈhæbɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：居住于
- **英文释义**：to live in a place
- **例句**：
  - Many species inhabit the tropical rainforest.
  - 许多物种居住在热带雨林中。
  - The building is inhabited by over 500 families.
  - 这栋楼里住着500多户人家。
- **助记**：in-（ in）+ habit（居住）→ 在…内居住 → 居住于

### 9. **inherit** /ɪnˈherɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：继承
- **英文释义**：to receive money, property, or qualities from someone who has died or from one's parents
- **例句**：
  - She inherited the house from her grandmother.
  - 她从祖母那里继承了这栋房子。
  - He inherited his father's business sense.
  - 他继承了父亲的商业头脑。
- **助记**：in-（ in）+ herit（继承人）→ 成为继承人 → 继承

### 10. **initial** /ɪˈnɪʃl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：最初的；首字母
- **英文释义**：happening at the beginning; the first letter of a name
- **例句**：
  - The initial response to the plan was positive.
  - 对该计划的初步反响是积极的。
  - Please write your initials at the bottom of the form.
  - 请在表格底部签上您的姓名首字母。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ it（走）+ -ial → 刚开始进入的 → 最初的；首字母

### 高一词汇

### 11. **integrity** /ɪnˈteɡrəti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：正直；完整性
- **英文释义**：the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; the state of being whole and undivided
- **例句**：
  - He is a man of integrity and would never lie.
  - 他是个正直的人，绝不会撒谎。
  - We must maintain the territorial integrity of our country.
  - 我们必须维护国家领土的完整。
- **助记**：integer（完整）+ -ity → 完整无损的状态 → 正直；完整性

### 12. **intermittent** /ˌɪntərˈmɪtnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：间歇的
- **英文释义**：stopping and starting at intervals; not continuous
- **例句**：
  - He suffered from intermittent pain in his back.
  - 他背部有间歇性疼痛。
  - The intermittent rain made the journey difficult.
  - 断断续续的雨使行程变得困难。
- **助记**：inter-（ between）+ mitt（送）+ -ent → 在中间停下来的 → 间歇的

### 13. **intrepid** /ɪnˈtrepɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无畏的
- **英文释义**：fearless; very brave and not afraid of dangerous situations
- **例句**：
  - The intrepid explorer ventured into the unknown jungle.
  - 这位无畏的探险家深入未知的丛林。
  - She was an intrepid journalist who reported from war zones.
  - 她是一位无畏的记者，曾在战区报道。
- **助记**：in-（不）+ trepid（害怕）→ 不害怕的 → 无畏的

### 14. **intricate** /ˈɪntrɪkət/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：复杂的；精细的
- **英文释义**：having many small parts or details; very complicated
- **例句**：
  - The watch has an intricate mechanical design.
  - 这块表有着精细的机械设计。
  - She explained the intricate details of the legal case.
  - 她解释了这起法律案件错综复杂的细节。
- **助记**：in-（内）+ tric（复杂）+ -ate → 内部缠结的 → 复杂的；精细的

### 15. **intrinsic** /ɪnˈtrɪnzɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：固有的；内在的
- **英文释义**：belonging naturally; essential part of something
- **例句**：
  - The intrinsic value of gold has attracted investors for centuries.
  - 黄金的固有价值几个世纪以来一直吸引着投资者。
  - Learning has intrinsic rewards that go beyond grades.
  - 学习具有超越成绩的内在回报。
- **助记**：intrin-（内部）+ sic（跟随）→ 跟随内部的 → 固有的；内在的
