## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **opportunity** /ˌɒpəˈtjuːnəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：机会；时机
- **英文释义**：a chance to do something that you want to do
- **例句**：
  - Don't miss the opportunity to study abroad.
  - 不要错过出国留学的机会。
  - The competition gives students an opportunity to show their talents.
  - 这次比赛给了学生一个展示才能的机会。
- **助记**：op（朝向）+ port（港口）+ unity → 船朝港口开来 → 机会来了；同义词：chance

### 2. **recognize** /ˈrekəɡnaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：认出；识别；承认
- **英文释义**：to know who someone is or what something is because you have seen them before
- **例句**：
  - I didn't recognize her because she had changed her hairstyle.
  - 我没认出她，因为她换了发型。
  - The government recognized the importance of education.
  - 政府认识到了教育的重要性。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ cogn（知道）+ ize → 再次知道 → 认出；名词：recognition

### 3. **express** /ɪkˈspres/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：表达；表示；快递的
- **英文释义**：to show a feeling, opinion, or fact through words, looks, or actions
- **例句**：
  - It's difficult for him to express his feelings in English.
  - 他很难用英语表达自己的感受。
  - She expressed her thanks to the teacher at graduation.
  - 毕业时她向老师表达了感谢。
- **助记**：ex（向外）+ press（按压）→ 把内心的想法按压出来 → 表达；名词：expression

### 4. **technology** /tekˈnɒlədʒi/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：技术；科技
- **英文释义**：scientific knowledge used in practical ways, especially in industry
- **例句**：
  - Modern technology has changed the way we communicate.
  - 现代科技改变了我们的交流方式。
  - China has made great progress in space technology.
  - 中国在航天技术方面取得了巨大进步。
- **助记**：techno（技术）+ logy（学科）→ 技术学科 → 科技

### 5. **responsible** /rɪˈspɒnsəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：负责任的；有责任心的
- **英文释义**：having a duty to deal with something; sensible and able to be trusted
- **例句**：
  - A responsible student always finishes homework on time.
  - 一个有责任心的学生总是按时完成作业。
  - We are all responsible for keeping our classroom clean.
  - 我们都有责任保持教室干净。
- **助记**：response（回应）+ ible（能...的）→ 能做出回应的 → 负责任的；名词：responsibility

### 6. **develop** /dɪˈveləp/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：发展；开发；培养
- **英文释义**：to grow or change into a more advanced form; to make something new
- **例句**：
  - Reading can help develop your imagination.
  - 阅读可以帮助培养你的想象力。
  - The city has developed rapidly in the past twenty years.
  - 这座城市在过去二十年中发展迅速。
- **助记**：de（去除）+ velop（包裹）→ 去除包裹，展开 → 发展；名词：development

### 7. **imagine** /ɪˈmædʒɪn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：想象；设想
- **英文释义**：to form a picture in your mind of something that is not real or present
- **例句**：
  - Can you imagine life without the Internet?
  - 你能想象没有互联网的生活吗？
  - I can't imagine what the city will look like in 50 years.
  - 我无法想象这座城市 50 年后会是什么样子。
- **助记**：imag（形象，image 的词根）+ ine → 在脑中形成形象 → 想象；名词：imagination

### 8. **discover** /dɪsˈkʌvə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：发现；发觉
- **英文释义**：to find something or learn about something for the first time
- **例句**：
  - Columbus discovered America in 1492.
  - 哥伦布于1492年发现了美洲。
  - Scientists have discovered a new way to treat this disease.
  - 科学家们发现了治疗这种疾病的新方法。
- **助记**：dis（去除）+ cover（覆盖）→ 去掉覆盖物 → 发现；名词：discovery

### 9. **effort** /ˈefət/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：努力；费力的事
- **英文释义**：physical or mental energy that you need to do something
- **例句**：
  - With great effort, she finished the 1000-meter race.
  - 经过很大的努力，她完成了1000米赛跑。
  - You should make an effort to learn at least one new word every day.
  - 你应该努力每天至少学一个新单词。
- **助记**：ef（向外）+ fort（力量）→ 向外使力 → 努力；固定搭配：make an effort（努力）

### 10. **continue** /kənˈtɪnjuː/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：继续；持续
- **英文释义**：to keep doing something without stopping; to start again after a pause
- **例句**：
  - The rain continued for three days without stopping.
  - 雨连续下了三天没有停。
  - After a short break, they continued to work on the project.
  - 短暂休息后，他们继续做项目。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ tin（持有）+ ue → 一直持有 → 继续；形容词：continuous（持续不断的）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **adequate** /ˈædɪkwət/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：足够的；充分的；合格的
- **英文释义**：enough in quantity or good enough in quality for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - Make sure you get adequate sleep before the exam.
  - 确保考试前你有充足的睡眠。
  - The school provides adequate facilities for students.
  - 学校为学生提供了足够的设施。
- **助记**：ad（朝向）+ equ（相等）+ ate → 达到相等的水平 → 足够的；反义词：inadequate

### 12. **cultivate** /ˈkʌltɪveɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：培养；耕种；陶冶
- **英文释义**：to develop a quality, attitude, or skill; to prepare land for growing crops
- **例句**：
  - Schools should cultivate students' interest in reading.
  - 学校应该培养学生的阅读兴趣。
  - Farmers cultivate rice in the southern parts of China.
  - 中国南方的农民种植水稻。
- **助记**：cult（耕种，文化）+ iv + ate → 耕种培育 → 培养；名词：cultivation；联想 culture（文化）

### 13. **discipline** /ˈdɪsəplɪn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：纪律；自制力；学科
- **英文释义**：the practice of training people to obey rules; a subject of study
- **例句**：
  - Self-discipline is the key to success in any field.
  - 自律是任何领域成功的关键。
  - Physics and chemistry are two important scientific disciplines.
  - 物理和化学是两门重要的科学学科。
- **助记**：disciple（门徒）+ ine → 门徒需要遵守的规矩 → 纪律

### 14. **remarkable** /rɪˈmɑːkəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：非凡的；显著的；引人注目的
- **英文释义**：unusual or surprising in a way that causes people to take notice
- **例句**：
  - She has made remarkable progress in learning Chinese.
  - 她在学习中文方面取得了显著的进步。
  - The Great Wall is a remarkable achievement of ancient China.
  - 长城是中国古代一项了不起的成就。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ mark（标记）+ able → 值得再次标记的 → 引人注目的

### 15. **pursue** /pəˈsjuː/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：追求；追赶；从事
- **英文释义**：to follow or chase someone or something; to try to achieve something
- **例句**：
  - He decided to pursue his dream of becoming a musician.
  - 他决定追求成为音乐家的梦想。
  - Many young people pursue higher education after high school.
  - 很多年轻人高中毕业后追求更高的教育。
- **助记**：pur（向前）+ sue（跟随）→ 向前跟随 → 追求；名词：pursuit
