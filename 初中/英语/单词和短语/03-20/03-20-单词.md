## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **anxiety** /æŋˈzaɪəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：焦虑；忧虑；不安
- **英文释义**：a feeling of worry or nervousness about something
- **例句**：
  - Many students feel anxiety before important exams.
  - 许多学生在重要考试前感到焦虑。
  - Her anxiety about the interview kept her awake all night.
  - 她对面试的焦虑让她整夜未眠。
- **助记**：anxi（焦急）+ ety → 焦急的状态 → 焦虑；形容词：anxious（焦虑的）

### 2. **brief** /briːf/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：简短的；短暂的
- **英文释义**：lasting only a short time; using few words
- **例句**：
  - The teacher gave a brief introduction of the new student.
  - 老师简短地介绍了新同学。
  - After a brief pause, she continued her speech.
  - 短暂的停顿后，她继续了演讲。
- **助记**：brief 与 abbreviate（缩写）同源 → 简短的；副词：briefly；固定搭配：in brief（简言之）

### 3. **demand** /dɪˈmɑːnd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 需求；要求；v. 要求
- **英文释义**：n. a strong request or need; v. to ask for something firmly
- **例句**：
  - There is a growing demand for online education these days.
  - 如今对在线教育的需求不断增长。
  - The workers demanded higher wages and better working conditions.
  - 工人们要求更高的工资和更好的工作条件。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ mand（命令）→ 完全命令 → 要求；形容词：demanding（要求高的）

### 4. **donate** /dəʊˈneɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：捐赠；捐献
- **英文释义**：to give something, especially money, to help people or organizations
- **例句**：
  - Many people donated money to help the earthquake victims.
  - 很多人捐款帮助地震灾民。
  - She donated her old clothes to the charity shop.
  - 她把旧衣服捐给了慈善商店。
- **助记**：don（给予）+ ate → 给予 → 捐赠；名词：donation

### 5. **escape** /ɪˈskeɪp/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：v. 逃脱；逃避；n. 逃跑
- **英文释义**：v. to get away from a place or bad situation; n. the act of getting away
- **例句**：
  - The bird escaped from its cage and flew away.
  - 那只鸟从笼子里逃出来飞走了。
  - Reading is a good way to escape from the stress of daily life.
  - 阅读是逃避日常生活压力的好方法。
- **助记**：es（出）+ cape（斗篷）→ 甩掉斗篷逃跑 → 逃脱

### 6. **frequent** /ˈfriːkwənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：频繁的；经常的
- **英文释义**：happening often
- **例句**：
  - He makes frequent trips to the library to borrow books.
  - 他经常去图书馆借书。
  - Frequent practice is the key to improving your English.
  - 频繁的练习是提高英语的关键。
- **助记**：frequ（经常）+ ent → 经常的 → 频繁的；副词：frequently；名词：frequency

### 7. **instrument** /ˈɪnstrəmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：乐器；仪器；工具
- **英文释义**：a tool used for a particular purpose; a device for making music
- **例句**：
  - She can play several musical instruments, including the piano and violin.
  - 她会演奏几种乐器，包括钢琴和小提琴。
  - Scientists use special instruments to measure air quality.
  - 科学家使用专门的仪器来测量空气质量。
- **助记**：instru（建造/准备）+ ment → 用来建造的东西 → 工具/乐器

### 8. **original** /əˈrɪdʒənl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：原来的；原创的；独创的
- **英文释义**：existing from the beginning; new and different from anything before
- **例句**：
  - The original plan was to go hiking, but it rained.
  - 原来的计划是去远足，但下雨了。
  - She always comes up with original ideas for her art projects.
  - 她总是为她的艺术项目想出独创的点子。
- **助记**：origin（起源）+ al → 与起源相关的 → 原始的/原创的；副词：originally

### 9. **relief** /rɪˈliːf/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：宽慰；解脱；救济
- **英文释义**：a feeling of comfort when something unpleasant stops; help given to people in need
- **例句**：
  - It was a great relief to hear that everyone was safe after the earthquake.
  - 听到地震后每个人都安全的消息真是大大松了一口气。
  - The government sent relief supplies to the flood-hit area.
  - 政府向洪灾地区发送了救援物资。
- **助记**：re（回来）+ lief（轻松）→ 重新轻松 → 宽慰；动词：relieve；固定搭配：to one's relief（令某人宽慰的是）

### 10. **research** /rɪˈsɜːtʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 研究；调查；v. 研究
- **英文释义**：n. a detailed study of a subject; v. to study something carefully
- **例句**：
  - She is doing research on the effects of social media on teenagers.
  - 她正在研究社交媒体对青少年的影响。
  - The scientist researched the problem for many years.
  - 这位科学家研究这个问题很多年了。
- **助记**：re（反复）+ search（寻找）→ 反复寻找答案 → 研究；名词：researcher

### 高一词汇

### 11. **apparent** /əˈpærənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：明显的；表面上的
- **英文释义**：clearly seen or understood; seeming to be true but not necessarily so
- **例句**：
  - It was apparent that he hadn't studied for the test.
  - 很明显他没有为考试学习。
  - Her apparent confidence hid her inner nervousness.
  - 她表面上的自信掩盖了内心的紧张。
- **助记**：ap（朝向）+ par（出现）+ ent → 出现在眼前的 → 明显的；副词：apparently

### 12. **controversial** /ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有争议的；引起争论的
- **英文释义**：causing a lot of disagreement or argument
- **例句**：
  - The use of AI in education is a controversial topic.
  - 人工智能在教育中的使用是一个有争议的话题。
  - The government made a controversial decision to raise taxes.
  - 政府做出了一个有争议的加税决定。
- **助记**：controversy（争议）+ al → 引起争议的 → 有争议的

### 13. **dominate** /ˈdɒmɪneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：支配；主导；占据优势
- **英文释义**：to have control over or be the most important part of something
- **例句**：
  - Social media now dominates the way young people communicate.
  - 社交媒体现在主导了年轻人的交流方式。
  - The team dominated the game from start to finish.
  - 这支队伍从头到尾主导了比赛。
- **助记**：domin（主人）+ ate → 像主人一样控制 → 支配；名词：domination；形容词：dominant

### 14. **minimum** /ˈmɪnɪməm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./adj.
- **中文释义**：n. 最小值；最低限度；adj. 最小的
- **英文释义**：the smallest amount or number allowed or possible
- **例句**：
  - The minimum score to pass the exam is 60 points.
  - 通过考试的最低分数是60分。
  - You should study a minimum of two hours every day.
  - 你每天至少应该学习两个小时。
- **助记**：mini（最小）+ mum → 最小的 → 最低限度；反义词：maximum（最大值）

### 15. **sacrifice** /ˈsækrɪfaɪs/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：n. 牺牲；献祭；v. 牺牲；献出
- **英文释义**：n. giving up something valuable for a more important purpose; v. to give up something important
- **例句**：
  - Parents often make sacrifices for their children's education.
  - 父母经常为孩子的教育做出牺牲。
  - She sacrificed her free time to help her classmates prepare for the exam.
  - 她牺牲了自己的空闲时间来帮同学备考。
- **助记**：sacri（神圣的）+ fice（做）→ 为神圣的事物付出 → 牺牲
