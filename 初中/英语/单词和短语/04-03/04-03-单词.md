## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **postpone** /pəʊstˈpəʊn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：推迟；延期
- **英文释义**：to arrange for something to happen at a later time
- **例句**：
  - The football match was postponed due to heavy rain.
  - 足球比赛因大雨而延期。
  - We decided to postpone the trip until next month.
  - 我们决定把旅行推迟到下个月。
- **助记**：post（在后面）+ pone（放置）→ 放到后面 → 推迟；近义词：delay（延迟）, put off（推迟）

### 2. **calculate** /ˈkælkjuleɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：计算；估算
- **英文释义**：to find out a number or amount using mathematics
- **例句**：
  - Can you calculate the area of this triangle?
  - 你能计算出这个三角形的面积吗？
  - Scientists calculated that the asteroid would pass close to Earth.
  - 科学家计算出这颗小行星将会近距离掠过地球。
- **助记**：calc（石头/计算）+ ulate → 用石子计算 → 计算；名词：calculation（计算）; calculator（计算器）

### 3. **occupy** /ˈɒkjupaɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：占据；使忙碌；占领
- **英文释义**：to fill or take up a space or time; to live or work in a place
- **例句**：
  - The new shopping mall occupies a large area in the city center.
  - 新购物中心占据了市中心的大片区域。
  - Reading books occupies most of her free time.
  - 阅读书籍占据了她大部分空闲时间。
- **助记**：oc（朝向）+ cupy（抓住）→ 抓住地方 → 占据；名词：occupation（职业；占用）

### 4. **declare** /dɪˈkleə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：宣布；声明；宣告
- **英文释义**：to say something officially or publicly
- **例句**：
  - The mayor declared the new park officially open.
  - 市长宣布新公园正式开放。
  - She declared that she would run for class president.
  - 她宣布要竞选班长。
- **助记**：de（加强）+ clare（清楚）→ 使更加清楚 → 宣布；名词：declaration（宣言）

### 5. **export** /ɪkˈspɔːt; ˈekspɔːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：出口；输出
- **英文释义**：to sell goods to another country; goods sold to another country
- **例句**：
  - China exports a large amount of electronic products to the world.
  - 中国向世界出口大量电子产品。
  - Tea was one of China's most important exports in history.
  - 茶叶是中国历史上最重要的出口商品之一。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ port（港口）→ 从港口运出 → 出口；反义词：import（进口）

### 6. **landscape** /ˈlændskeɪp/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：风景；景色；地形
- **英文释义**：a view of the countryside; the appearance of an area of land
- **例句**：
  - The landscape of Guilin is famous for its beautiful mountains and rivers.
  - 桂林的风景以美丽的山水闻名。
  - The city's landscape has changed greatly over the past ten years.
  - 过去十年城市的面貌发生了巨大变化。
- **助记**：land（土地）+ scape（景色）→ 土地的景色 → 风景

### 7. **appropriate** /əˈprəʊpriət/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：合适的；恰当的
- **英文释义**：suitable or proper for the situation
- **例句**：
  - Please wear appropriate clothes for the school ceremony.
  - 请穿着适合校庆的衣服。
  - It's not appropriate to use your phone during class.
  - 上课时使用手机是不恰当的。
- **助记**：ap（朝向）+ propri（自己的）+ ate → 适合自己的 → 合适的；反义词：inappropriate（不恰当的）

### 8. **ultimate** /ˈʌltɪmət/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：最终的；根本的；极致的
- **英文释义**：last or final; the best or most extreme
- **例句**：
  - The ultimate goal of education is to prepare students for life.
  - 教育的最终目标是为学生的人生做准备。
  - Winning the gold medal was the ultimate achievement for the athlete.
  - 赢得金牌是这位运动员的终极成就。
- **助记**：ultim（最后）+ ate → 最后的 → 最终的/极致的；副词：ultimately（最终）

### 9. **precisely** /prɪˈsaɪsli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：精确地；恰好
- **英文释义**：exactly and accurately
- **例句**：
  - The train arrived at precisely 8:30 in the morning.
  - 火车在早上8:30准时到达。
  - That is precisely what I was trying to explain.
  - 那正是我试图解释的。
- **助记**：precis（精确的）+ ely → 精确地；形容词：precise（精确的）; 名词：precision（精确度）

### 10. **prohibit** /prəˈhɪbɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：禁止；阻止
- **英文释义**：to officially say that something is not allowed
- **例句**：
  - Smoking is prohibited in all public buildings.
  - 所有公共建筑内禁止吸烟。
  - The school prohibits students from using phones during exams.
  - 学校禁止学生在考试期间使用手机。
- **助记**：pro（在前面）+ hibit（拥有/持有）→ 在前面挡住不让拥有 → 禁止；名词：prohibition（禁止）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **discourse** /ˈdɪskɔːs/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：话语；论述；演讲
- **英文释义**：written or spoken communication about a particular topic
- **例句**：
  - The professor's discourse on climate change lasted over an hour.
  - 教授关于气候变化的演讲持续了一个多小时。
  - Public discourse about education reform has become more heated recently.
  - 近来关于教育改革的公共讨论变得更加激烈。
- **助记**：dis（分散）+ course（跑/过程）→ 思想的分散流动 → 话语/论述

### 12. **provisional** /prəˈvɪʒənl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：临时的；暂时的
- **英文释义**：arranged for the present time, likely to be changed later
- **例句**：
  - The government set up a provisional committee to handle the emergency.
  - 政府成立了一个临时委员会来处理紧急情况。
  - These are only provisional results — the final ones will come next week.
  - 这些只是临时结果——最终结果下周公布。
- **助记**：provis（预先提供）+ ional → 预先临时提供的 → 临时的；名词：provision（供应；规定）

### 13. **aggravate** /ˈæɡrəveɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：加剧；恶化；激怒
- **英文释义**：to make a bad situation worse; to annoy someone
- **例句**：
  - The drought was aggravated by the unusually hot summer.
  - 异常炎热的夏天加剧了干旱。
  - His constant interruptions aggravated the teacher.
  - 他不断的打扰激怒了老师。
- **助记**：ag（加强）+ grav（重）+ ate → 使更加沉重 → 加剧；名词：aggravation（加剧；恼怒）

### 14. **unanimous** /juˈnænɪməs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：一致的；全体同意的
- **英文释义**：agreed on by everyone; with no one disagreeing
- **例句**：
  - The decision to help the flood victims was unanimous.
  - 帮助洪灾受害者的决定是全体一致通过的。
  - The jury reached a unanimous verdict of not guilty.
  - 陪审团一致裁定无罪。
- **助记**：un（一个）+ anim（心灵）+ ous → 一条心的 → 一致的；副词：unanimously（一致地）

### 15. **threshold** /ˈθreʃhəʊld/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：门槛；起点；界限
- **英文释义**：the level at which something starts to happen; a doorway
- **例句**：
  - The noise level exceeded the safety threshold set by the government.
  - 噪音水平超过了政府设定的安全界限。
  - We are on the threshold of a new era of artificial intelligence.
  - 我们正处于人工智能新时代的门槛上。
- **助记**：thresh（打谷）+ hold（保持）→ 门口打谷的地方 → 门槛/界限
