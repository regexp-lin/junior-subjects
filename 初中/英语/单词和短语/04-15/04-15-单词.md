## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **exempt** /ɪɡˈzempt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./adj.
- **中文释义**：免除；豁免的
- **英文释义**：to free from a duty or obligation; not affected by something
- **例句**：
  - Students under 12 are exempt from paying the entrance fee.
  - 12岁以下的学生免交入场费。
  - The charity is exempt from paying taxes.
  - 这个慈善机构免缴税款。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ empt（拿）→ 拿出去 → 免除；名词：exemption（豁免）

### 2. **designate** /ˈdezɪɡneɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：指定；命名；指派
- **英文释义**：to officially choose someone or something for a particular purpose
- **例句**：
  - The area has been designated as a national park.
  - 该地区已被指定为国家公园。
  - She was designated as the team leader for the project.
  - 她被指定为该项目的团队负责人。
- **助记**：de（加强）+ sign（标记）+ ate → 加以标记 → 指定；名词：designation（指定）

### 3. **interrogate** /ɪnˈterəɡeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：审问；质问；讯问
- **英文释义**：to ask someone a lot of questions, especially in an aggressive way
- **例句**：
  - The police interrogated the suspect for several hours.
  - 警方审问了嫌疑人数小时。
  - Students are encouraged to interrogate ideas rather than simply accept them.
  - 鼓励学生对观点进行质疑而不是简单地接受。
- **助记**：inter（之间）+ rog（问）+ ate → 在中间反复问 → 审问；名词：interrogation（审讯）

### 4. **presume** /prɪˈzjuːm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：推测；假定；认为
- **英文释义**：to suppose that something is true without having proof
- **例句**：
  - I presume you've already heard the news about the school trip.
  - 我想你已经听说了关于学校旅行的消息。
  - A person is presumed innocent until proven guilty.
  - 一个人在被证明有罪之前被假定为无罪。
- **助记**：pre（预先）+ sume（拿/假设）→ 预先假设 → 推测；名词：presumption（假定）

### 5. **itinerary** /aɪˈtɪnərəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：行程；旅行计划
- **英文释义**：a planned route or journey; a list of places to visit
- **例句**：
  - Our travel itinerary includes visits to Beijing, Xi'an, and Shanghai.
  - 我们的旅行行程包括参观北京、西安和上海。
  - The tour guide gave us a detailed itinerary for the day.
  - 导游给了我们当天的详细行程。
- **助记**：itiner（旅行）+ ary → 旅行的计划 → 行程

### 6. **magnitude** /ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：大小；重要性；规模
- **英文释义**：the great size or importance of something
- **例句**：
  - The magnitude of the earthquake was measured at 7.5 on the Richter scale.
  - 地震的震级在里氏标度上为7.5级。
  - Few people understood the magnitude of the problem at first.
  - 起初很少有人了解问题的严重性。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ itude → 大的程度 → 大小/重要性

### 7. **wholesome** /ˈhəʊlsəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：健康的；有益的；有益身心的
- **英文释义**：good for your health or well-being; morally good
- **例句**：
  - Children need wholesome food to grow up healthy.
  - 孩子们需要健康的食物才能健康成长。
  - The show provides wholesome entertainment for the whole family.
  - 这个节目为全家提供有益身心的娱乐。
- **助记**：whole（完整的/全部的）+ some → 使人完整/健全的 → 健康的

### 8. **tentative** /ˈtentətɪv/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：试探性的；暂定的；犹豫的
- **英文释义**：not certain or fixed; done without confidence
- **例句**：
  - We made a tentative plan to meet next weekend, but it's not confirmed yet.
  - 我们暂定下个周末见面，但还没有确认。
  - She took a tentative step onto the icy road.
  - 她试探性地踏上了结冰的路面。
- **助记**：tent（尝试）+ ative → 尝试性的 → 试探性的；副词：tentatively

### 9. **nominally** /ˈnɒmɪnəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：名义上；表面上
- **英文释义**：in name only; officially but not in reality
- **例句**：
  - He is nominally the head of the department, but his assistant does most of the work.
  - 他名义上是部门负责人，但大部分工作是助理做的。
  - The country is nominally independent but heavily influenced by its neighbor.
  - 这个国家名义上独立，但受到邻国的强烈影响。
- **助记**：nominal（名义的）+ ly → 名义上地；形容词：nominal（名义上的）

### 10. **envisage** /ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：设想；预见；展望
- **英文释义**：to imagine or expect something to happen in the future
- **例句**：
  - It's hard to envisage a world without the internet.
  - 很难想象没有互联网的世界。
  - The architect envisaged a modern building surrounded by green spaces.
  - 建筑师设想了一座被绿地环绕的现代建筑。
- **助记**：en（使）+ visage（面容/景象）→ 使景象出现在眼前 → 设想

### 高一词汇

### 11. **equivocal** /ɪˈkwɪvəkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：模棱两可的；含糊的
- **英文释义**：open to more than one interpretation; ambiguous
- **例句**：
  - His answer was equivocal — we still don't know if he agrees or not.
  - 他的回答模棱两可——我们仍然不知道他是否同意。
  - The test results were equivocal and required further analysis.
  - 检测结果含糊不清，需要进一步分析。
- **助记**：equi（相等）+ vocal（声音）→ 两边说的一样多 → 模棱两可的；反义词：unequivocal（明确的）

### 12. **expedient** /ɪkˈspiːdiənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：权宜的；方便的；权宜之计
- **英文释义**：useful or convenient but possibly improper or immoral
- **例句**：
  - The government chose the politically expedient option rather than the right one.
  - 政府选择了政治上权宜之计而非正确的方案。
  - Using shortcuts might be expedient, but it doesn't lead to long-term success.
  - 走捷径可能很方便，但不会带来长期成功。
- **助记**：ex（出）+ ped（脚）+ ient → 用脚快速走出 → 方便快捷的（权宜的）

### 13. **promulgate** /ˈprɒmlɡeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：颁布；宣传；传播
- **英文释义**：to make a new law or idea known to everyone
- **例句**：
  - The government promulgated new regulations on environmental protection.
  - 政府颁布了新的环境保护法规。
  - The organization works to promulgate the ideals of peace and equality.
  - 该组织致力于宣传和平与平等的理想。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ mulg（公众）+ ate → 向公众传达 → 颁布；名词：promulgation（颁布）

### 14. **anomalous** /əˈnɒmələs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：异常的；反常的
- **英文释义**：different from what is usual or expected; irregular
- **例句**：
  - The anomalous weather pattern caused unexpected flooding.
  - 反常的天气模式导致了意外的洪水。
  - The scientist noticed anomalous data in the experiment results.
  - 科学家注意到实验结果中的异常数据。
- **助记**：a（不）+ nomal（正常）+ ous → 不正常的 → 异常的；名词：anomaly（异常现象）

### 15. **connotation** /ˌkɒnəˈteɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：内涵；含义；暗示
- **英文释义**：an idea or feeling that a word suggests in addition to its literal meaning
- **例句**：
  - The word "home" has positive connotations of warmth and comfort.
  - "家"这个词有温暖和舒适的积极内涵。
  - Different words can carry very different connotations even if they mean the same thing.
  - 不同的词即使意思相同也可能带有截然不同的内涵。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ not（标记）+ ation → 一起带有的标记 → 附加含义/内涵
