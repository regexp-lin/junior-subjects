## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **admonish** /ədˈmɒnɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：告诫；劝告；责备
- **英文释义**：to warn or reprimand someone firmly; to advise earnestly
- **例句**：
  - The teacher admonished the students for talking during the exam.
  - 老师告诫学生不要在考试时说话。
  - His mother admonished him to be more careful with his money.
  - 母亲劝告他要更谨慎地管理钱财。
- **助记**：ad（加强）+ mon（提醒）+ ish → 加强提醒 → 告诫；名词：admonition（告诫）

### 2. **bequeath** /bɪˈkwiːð/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：遗赠；遗留；传给
- **英文释义**：to leave property or possessions to someone by a will; to pass on
- **例句**：
  - He bequeathed his entire fortune to charity.
  - 他把全部财产遗赠给了慈善机构。
  - The ancient civilization bequeathed us many valuable artifacts.
  - 古代文明为我们留下了许多珍贵的文物。
- **助记**：be（使）+ queath（说）→ 通过遗嘱说出 → 遗赠；名词：bequest（遗赠物）

### 3. **capitulate** /kəˈpɪtʃuleɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：投降；屈服；让步
- **英文释义**：to cease resisting and surrender; to give in under agreed conditions
- **例句**：
  - The army was forced to capitulate after months of siege.
  - 军队在数月围困后被迫投降。
  - After hours of negotiation, the company finally capitulated to the workers' demands.
  - 经过数小时谈判，公司最终对工人的要求做出了让步。
- **助记**：capit（头）+ ulate → 低头 → 投降；名词：capitulation（投降）

### 4. **desiccate** /ˈdesɪkeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使干燥；使脱水；使干涸
- **英文释义**：to remove moisture from; to dry out completely
- **例句**：
  - The hot sun desiccated the soil, making it hard to grow crops.
  - 烈日使土壤干涸，难以种植作物。
  - Dried fruits are desiccated to preserve them for longer.
  - 干果经过脱水处理以延长保存时间。
- **助记**：de（完全）+ sicc（干）+ ate → 使完全变干 → 使干燥；形容词：desiccated（干燥的）

### 5. **elucidate** /ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：阐明；解释；说明
- **英文释义**：to make something clear; to explain in detail
- **例句**：
  - The professor elucidated the complex theory with simple examples.
  - 教授用简单的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。
  - Could you elucidate your point? I don't quite understand.
  - 你能解释一下你的观点吗？我不太明白。
- **助记**：e（出）+ lucid（清晰）+ ate → 使清晰出来 → 阐明；名词：elucidation（阐明）

### 6. **decree** /dɪˈkriː/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：法令；政令；判决
- **英文释义**：an official order issued by a legal authority; a formal decision
- **例句**：
  - The government issued a decree banning the sale of the product.
  - 政府颁布法令禁止销售该产品。
  - The court's decree required him to pay a large fine.
  - 法院的判决要求他支付巨额罚款。
- **助记**：de（向下）+ cree（区分）→ 自上而下区分 → 法令；也可作动词：颁布法令

### 7. **axiom** /ˈæksiəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：公理；格言；自明之理
- **英文释义**：a statement or proposition that is accepted as true without proof
- **例句**：
  - "Actions speak louder than words" is a well-known axiom.
  - "行动胜于言语"是一条众所周知的格言。
  - In geometry, we start with certain axioms and build proofs from them.
  - 在几何学中，我们从某些公理出发，并由此构建证明。
- **助记**：axi（价值）+ om → 被认为有价值/正确的 → 公理；形容词：axiomatic（公理的）

### 8. **pristine** /ˈprɪstiːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：原始的；崭新的；未受破坏的
- **英文释义**：in its original condition; unspoiled; spotlessly clean
- **例句**：
  - The pristine beaches attract thousands of tourists every year.
  - 原始的海滩每年吸引成千上万的游客。
  - She kept her room in pristine condition.
  - 她把房间保持得一尘不染。
- **助记**：prist（最初）+ ine → 最初状态的 → 原始的

### 9. **lucid** /ˈluːsɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：清晰的；易懂的；头脑清醒的
- **英文释义**：clearly expressed; easy to understand; rational and sane
- **例句**：
  - She gave a lucid explanation of the scientific concept.
  - 她对这一科学概念做了清晰的解释。
  - Despite his illness, he remained lucid and could answer questions.
  - 尽管生病，他仍然头脑清醒，能够回答问题。
- **助记**：luc（光）+ id → 有光的 → 清晰的；名词：lucidity（清晰）

### 10. **explicitly** /ɪkˈsplɪsɪtli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：明确地；清楚地；直截了当地
- **英文释义**：in a clear and detailed manner; leaving no room for doubt
- **例句**：
  - The rules explicitly state that phones are not allowed in the exam room.
  - 规则明确规定考场内不允许使用手机。
  - She explicitly told him she was not interested.
  - 她明确告诉他她不感兴趣。
- **助记**：explicit（明确的）+ ly → 明确地；形容词：explicit（明确的）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **recondite** /ˈrekəndaɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：深奥的；晦涩的；难解的
- **英文释义**：little known; abstruse; difficult to understand
- **例句**：
  - The professor's recondite lecture left most students confused.
  - 教授深奥的讲座让大多数学生感到困惑。
  - The book deals with recondite topics in philosophy.
  - 这本书涉及哲学中的晦涩话题。
- **助记**：re（再）+ cond（隐藏）+ ite → 一再隐藏的 → 深奥的

### 12. **loquacious** /ləˈkweɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：话多的；健谈的；喋喋不休的
- **英文释义**：tending to talk a great deal; talkative
- **例句**：
  - She is so loquacious that it's hard to get a word in.
  - 她太健谈了，很难插上话。
  - The loquacious tour guide shared endless stories about the city.
  - 这位健谈的导游讲述了无数关于这座城市的故事。
- **助记**：loqu（说）+acious（多…的）→ 说很多的 → 话多的；名词：loquacity（多话）

### 13. **truculent** /ˈtrʌkjələnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：好斗的；凶狠的；粗暴的
- **英文释义**：eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant
- **例句**：
  - The truculent teenager refused to follow any rules.
  - 这个好斗的少年拒绝遵守任何规则。
  - His truculent attitude made it difficult to reach an agreement.
  - 他粗暴的态度使达成协议变得困难。
- **助记**：truc（凶猛）+ ulent → 凶猛的 → 好斗的；名词：truculence（好斗）

### 14. **munificent** /mjuːˈnɪfɪsnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：慷慨的；大方的；丰厚的
- **英文释义**：very generous; larger or more lavish than usual
- **例句**：
  - The munificent donor gave millions to the hospital.
  - 这位慷慨的捐赠者向医院捐赠了数百万。
  - She received a munificent reward for her outstanding work.
  - 她因出色的工作获得了丰厚的奖励。
- **助记**：muni（礼物）+ fic（做）+ ent → 大量送礼的 → 慷慨的；名词：munificence（慷慨）

### 15. **meretricious** /ˌmerɪˈtrɪʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：华而不实的；俗艳的；虚假的
- **英文释义**：apparently attractive but having no real value; superficially plausible
- **例句**：
  - The meretricious decorations made the room look cheap.
  - 俗艳的装饰让房间看起来廉价。
  - His argument was meretricious and failed to convince the jury.
  - 他的论点华而不实，未能说服陪审团。
- **助记**：meretric（妓女）+ ious → 像妓女般表面吸引人的 → 华而不实的
