## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **invade** /ɪnˈveɪd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：入侵
- **英文释义**：to enter a country or area using military force in order to take control; to enter somewhere in large numbers
- **例句**：
  - The army invaded the enemy's territory at dawn.
  - 军队在黎明时分入侵了敌方领土。
  - Tourists invaded the small town during the summer festival.
  - 夏季节期间游客涌入这座小镇。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ vad（走）+ -e → 走进来 → 入侵

### 2. **invest** /ɪnˈvest/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：投资
- **英文释义**：to put money into something in order to make a profit; to spend time, effort, etc. on something
- **例句**：
  - He decided to invest in the stock market.
  - 他决定投资股市。
  - She has invested many years in her research.
  - 她在研究上投入了许多年。
- **助记**：in-（使）+ vest（穿衣）→ 原指"给穿上"→ 引申为投入（金钱、精力）→ 投资

### 3. **involve** /ɪnˈvɒlv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：涉及；包含
- **英文释义**：to include something as a necessary part; to cause someone to take part in something
- **例句**：
  - The job involves a lot of travel.
  - 这份工作涉及大量出差。
  - Don't involve me in your argument.
  - 别把我牵扯进你们的争论。
- **助记**：in-（进入）+ volv（卷、转）+ -e → 卷入其中 → 涉及；包含

### 4. **isolate** /ˈaɪsəleɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：隔离；使孤立
- **英文释义**：to separate someone or something from other people or things; to keep apart
- **例句**：
  - The sick patient was isolated to prevent the spread of infection.
  - 患病者被隔离以防止感染传播。
  - He felt isolated after moving to the new city.
  - 搬到新城市后他感到孤立无援。
- **助记**：isol（岛）+ -ate（使）→ 使成孤岛 → 隔离；使孤立

### 5. **issue** /ˈɪʃuː/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：问题；发行
- **英文释义**：an important subject or problem that people discuss; to officially make something available; to give out
- **例句**：
  - Climate change is a major issue facing the world today.
  - 气候变化是当今世界面临的重大问题。
  - The magazine issued a special edition last month.
  - 该杂志上个月发行了特刊。
- **助记**：ex-（出）+ it（走）→ 走出、发出 → 发行；引申为引起讨论的"问题"

### 6. **item** /ˈaɪtəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：项目；物品
- **英文释义**：a single thing in a list or collection; one of several things in a group
- **例句**：
  - Please check each item on the shopping list.
  - 请核对购物清单上的每一项。
  - There's one more item I need to discuss.
  - 我还有一项需要讨论。
- **助记**：来自拉丁语 item（同样地）→ 列表中的每一项 → 项目；物品

### 7. **ivory** /ˈaɪvəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：象牙
- **英文释义**：the hard white substance from the tusks of elephants, used to make decorative objects
- **例句**：
  - Ivory trading is now illegal in most countries.
  - 象牙贸易在大多数国家现已非法。
  - The piano keys were once made of ivory.
  - 钢琴琴键曾用象牙制成。
- **助记**：来自拉丁语 ebur（象牙）→ ivory

### 8. **jaw** /dʒɔː/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：下巴；颌
- **英文释义**：either of the two bones at the bottom of the face that hold the teeth; the lower part of the face
- **例句**：
  - He broke his jaw in the accident.
  - 他在事故中摔伤了颌骨。
  - Her jaw dropped when she heard the news.
  - 听到消息时她惊得下巴都掉了。
- **助记**：拟声词，可能与 chew（咀嚼）相关 → 颌；下巴

### 9. **jealous** /ˈdʒeləs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：嫉妒的
- **英文释义**：feeling unhappy because someone has something you want; feeling anger or unhappiness because you think someone you love prefers another person
- **例句**：
  - She was jealous of her sister's success.
  - 她嫉妒姐姐的成功。
  - He gets jealous when she talks to other boys.
  - 她和其他男生说话时他就会吃醋。
- **助记**： zeal（热情）+ -ous → 对某事物过分在意 → 嫉妒的

### 10. **journal** /ˈdʒɜːrnl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：日志；期刊
- **英文释义**：a diary or daily record of events; a magazine or newspaper that deals with a particular subject
- **例句**：
  - She keeps a journal to record her thoughts.
  - 她写日志记录自己的想法。
  - The article was published in a medical journal.
  - 这篇文章发表在医学期刊上。
- **助记**：journ（日）+ -al → 每日记录的 → 日志；期刊

### 高一词汇

### 11. **laconic** /ləˈkɒnɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：简洁的；言简意赅的
- **英文释义**：using very few words; brief and to the point
- **例句**：
  - He gave a laconic reply: "No comment."
  - 他简洁地答道："无可奉告。"
  - Her laconic style of writing is admired by many.
  - 她言简意赅的写作风格受到许多人推崇。
- **助记**：源自希腊 Laconia（拉科尼亚，斯巴达所在地区）→ 斯巴达人以寡言著称 → 简洁的；言简意赅的

### 12. **languid** /ˈlæŋɡwɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：无精打采的
- **英文释义**：showing little energy or interest; slow and relaxed
- **例句**：
  - The heat made everyone feel languid.
  - 炎热让大家感到无精打采。
  - She moved with a languid grace across the room.
  - 她无精打采却优雅地走过房间。
- **助记**：langu（松弛、虚弱）+ -id → 松弛无力的 → 无精打采的

### 13. **latent** /ˈleɪtnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：潜伏的；潜在的
- **英文释义**：existing but not yet active, developed, or visible; hidden
- **例句**：
  - The virus can remain latent in the body for years.
  - 病毒可在体内潜伏多年。
  - She has latent musical talent that was never developed.
  - 她有未被发掘的音乐天赋。
- **助记**：lat（隐藏）+ -ent → 被隐藏的 → 潜伏的；潜在的

### 14. **laudable** /ˈlɔːdəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：值得赞扬的
- **英文释义**：deserving praise, even if not wholly successful
- **例句**：
  - Her efforts to help the poor are laudable.
  - 她帮助穷人的努力值得赞扬。
  - It was a laudable attempt, even though it failed.
  - 虽然失败了，但这是一次值得赞扬的尝试。
- **助记**：laud（赞扬）+ -able（能…的）→ 值得赞扬的

### 15. **legitimate** /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmət/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：合法的；正当的
- **英文释义**：allowed by law; reasonable and acceptable; lawful
- **例句**：
  - The government is the legitimate authority of the country.
  - 政府是该国合法的权威机构。
  - She has a legitimate reason for being late.
  - 她迟到有正当理由。
- **助记**：leg（法律）+ -itim + -ate → 合乎法律的 → 合法的；正当的
