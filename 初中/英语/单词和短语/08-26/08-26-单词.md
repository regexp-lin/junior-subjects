## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **lawn** /lɔːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：草坪
- **英文释义**：an area of grass, especially one closely cut and looked after in a garden or park
- **例句**：
  - The children were playing on the lawn.
  - 孩子们在草坪上玩耍。
  - We need to mow the lawn this weekend.
  - 本周末我们得修剪草坪。
- **助记**：源自中世纪法语 launde（旷野、草地）→ 修剪整齐的草地 → 草坪

### 2. **league** /liːɡ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：联盟；联赛
- **英文释义**：a group of sports clubs that play against each other; an organization of people or countries that work together
- **例句**：
  - The team won the league championship last year.
  - 该队去年赢得了联赛冠军。
  - Several countries formed a league to promote peace.
  - 多个国家组成联盟以促进和平。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 ligare（捆绑）→ 绑在一起的组织 → 联盟；联赛

### 3. **lean** /liːn/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./adj.
- **中文释义**：倾斜；精瘦的
- **英文释义**：to bend or slope; to rest against something; (of a person) thin and fit, with little fat
- **例句**：
  - Don't lean against the wall—it's wet.
  - 别靠着墙，墙是湿的。
  - He has a lean, athletic build.
  - 他身材精瘦健壮。
- **助记**：古英语 hleonian（倾斜）→ 倾斜；lean meat（瘦肉）→ 精瘦的

### 4. **lecture** /ˈlektʃər/ `初三`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：讲座；训斥
- **英文释义**：a talk given to a group of people to teach them about a subject; to give a lecture; to scold or criticize someone angrily
- **例句**：
  - The professor gave a fascinating lecture on ancient history.
  - 教授做了一场关于古代史的精彩讲座。
  - My parents lectured me about my grades.
  - 父母因为我的成绩训斥了我。
- **助记**：lect（读、讲）+ -ure → 讲授 → 讲座；训斥

### 5. **legend** /ˈledʒənd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：传说；传奇人物
- **英文释义**：an old story that may or may not be true; a famous person who is admired by many people
- **例句**：
  - The legend of King Arthur has been passed down for centuries.
  - 亚瑟王的传说已流传数百年。
  - She is a legend in the world of tennis.
  - 她是网坛传奇人物。
- **助记**：leg（读）+ -end → 值得读的故事 → 传说；传奇人物

### 6. **leisure** /ˈleʒər/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：休闲
- **英文释义**：time when you are not working or studying; free time for relaxation or enjoyment
- **例句**：
  - What do you do in your leisure time?
  - 你休闲时做什么？
  - The park is a popular place for leisure activities.
  - 这个公园是热门的休闲活动场所。
- **助记**： leisir（许可）→ 被许可不用干活的时间 → 休闲

### 7. **lens** /lenz/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：镜头；透镜
- **英文释义**：a curved piece of glass or plastic that focuses or spreads light rays; the part of a camera that focuses light
- **例句**：
  - The photographer changed the lens for a close-up shot.
  - 摄影师换上镜头拍摄特写。
  - I need glasses with stronger lenses.
  - 我需要镜片度数更高的眼镜。
- **助记**：源自拉丁语 lens（扁豆）→ 透镜形状像扁豆 → 镜头；透镜

### 8. **liberal** /ˈlɪbərəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：自由的；开明的
- **英文释义**：willing to respect and accept behaviour or opinions different from your own; generous; relating to political liberalism
- **例句**：
  - She has liberal views on social issues.
  - 她对社会问题持开明观点。
  - The school encourages a liberal education.
  - 该校倡导通识教育。
- **助记**：liber（自由）+ -al → 自由的；开明的

### 9. **liberty** /ˈlɪbərti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：自由
- **英文释义**：the state of being free from restrictions; the freedom to live as you choose
- **例句**：
  - They fought for liberty and equality.
  - 他们为自由和平等而战。
  - You have the liberty to choose your own path.
  - 你有权选择自己的道路。
- **助记**：liber（自由）+ -ty → 自由

### 10. **likewise** /ˈlaɪkwaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：同样地
- **英文释义**：in the same way; similarly; also
- **例句**：
  - I'll do likewise if I get the chance.
  - 有机会我也会这样做。
  - "Nice to meet you." — "Likewise."
  - "很高兴见到你。" — "我也是。"
- **助记**：like（同样的）+ -wise（方式）→ 以同样的方式 → 同样地

### 高一词汇

### 11. **magnanimous** /mæɡˈnænɪməs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：宽宏大量的
- **英文释义**：very generous and forgiving, especially towards a rival or someone who has harmed you
- **例句**：
  - The magnanimous winner congratulated his opponent.
  - 宽宏大量的获胜者向对手表示祝贺。
  - She was magnanimous in victory and did not gloat.
  - 她胜不骄，表现得宽宏大量。
- **助记**：magn（大）+ anim（心）+ -ous → 心胸宽广 → 宽宏大量的

### 12. **malevolent** /məˈlevələnt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：恶意的
- **英文释义**：having or showing a wish to harm other people; evil
- **例句**：
  - She gave him a malevolent glare.
  - 她恶狠狠地瞪了他一眼。
  - The villain in the story is malevolent and cunning.
  - 故事中的反派邪恶而狡诈。
- **助记**：male（坏）+ vol（意愿）+ -ent → 怀着坏意愿的 → 恶意的

### 13. **mandate** /ˈmændeɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：命令；授权
- **英文释义**：an official order to do something; the authority given to an elected group to represent people; to order or require
- **例句**：
  - The government received a clear mandate from the voters.
  - 政府获得选民明确的授权。
  - The law mandates that all children attend school.
  - 法律规定所有儿童必须上学。
- **助记**：mand（命令）+ -ate → 命令；授权

### 14. **meander** /miˈændər/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：蜿蜒；漫步
- **英文释义**：to follow a winding course; to walk slowly without a definite aim
- **例句**：
  - The river meanders through the valley.
  - 河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。
  - We spent the afternoon meandering around the old town.
  - 我们一下午在老城里随意漫步。
- **助记**：源自希腊神话中的河流 Maiandros（蜿蜒曲折）→ 蜿蜒；漫步

### 15. **mediocre** /ˌmiːdiˈəʊkər/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：平庸的
- **英文释义**：of only average quality; not very good
- **例句**：
  - The film received mediocre reviews.
  - 这部电影获得平庸的评价。
  - He was a mediocre student who rarely stood out.
  - 他是个平庸的学生，很少出类拔萃。
- **助记**：medi（中间）+ ocr（山）→ 只在半山腰 → 平庸的
