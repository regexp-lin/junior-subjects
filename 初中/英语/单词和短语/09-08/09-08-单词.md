## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **polish** /ˈpɒlɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：擦亮；润色
- **英文释义**：to make something smooth and shiny by rubbing; to improve something
- **例句**：
  - She polished the silver until it shone.
  - 她把银器擦得闪闪发亮。
  - He polished his speech before the presentation.
  - 他在演讲前润色了发言稿。
- **助记**：pol（光滑）+ -ish → 使变光滑 → 擦亮；润色

### 2. **portion** /ˈpɔːʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：一部分；一份
- **英文释义**：a part of something; an amount of food for one person
- **例句**：
  - A large portion of the budget goes to salaries.
  - 预算的很大一部分用于工资。
  - The restaurant serves generous portions.
  - 这家餐厅的菜量很大。
- **助记**：port（分配）+ -ion → 被分配出来的部分 → 一部分

### 3. **portrait** /ˈpɔːtrət/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：肖像；描写
- **英文释义**：a painting, drawing, or photograph of a person; a description
- **例句**：
  - She painted a beautiful portrait of her grandmother.
  - 她画了一幅祖母的美丽肖像。
  - The book gives a vivid portrait of life in the 1800s.
  - 这本书生动描写了19世纪的生活。
- **助记**：por（向前）+ trait（描画）→ 描画出来的人像 → 肖像

### 4. **pose** /pəʊz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：摆姿势；构成（威胁）
- **英文释义**：to sit or stand in a particular position; to create a problem or danger
- **例句**：
  - She posed for a photograph.
  - 她摆好姿势拍照。
  - The situation poses a serious threat to safety.
  - 这种情况对安全构成了严重威胁。
- **助记**：pos（放置）+ -e → 把身体放置在某个位置 → 摆姿势

### 5. **possess** /pəˈzes/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：拥有；具有
- **英文释义**：to have or own something; to have a particular quality
- **例句**：
  - He possesses a great talent for painting.
  - 他具有出色的绘画天赋。
  - She possesses both intelligence and kindness.
  - 她兼具智慧和善良。
- **助记**：pos（能够）+ sess（坐）→ 能坐拥的 → 拥有

### 6. **potential** /pəˈtenʃl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj./n.
- **中文释义**：潜在的；潜力
- **英文释义**：possible but not yet actual; the ability to develop or achieve something
- **例句**：
  - She has the potential to become a great scientist.
  - 她有成为伟大科学家的潜力。
  - There is a potential risk of flooding.
  - 存在潜在的洪水风险。
- **助记**：pot（力量）+ -ential → 蕴藏的力量 → 潜力

### 7. **poverty** /ˈpɒvəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：贫穷；贫困
- **英文释义**：the state of being extremely poor
- **例句**：
  - Millions of people live in poverty.
  - 数百万人生活在贫困中。
  - Education is the key to ending poverty.
  - 教育是消除贫困的关键。
- **助记**：pov（贫穷）+ -erty → 贫穷的状态 → 贫困

### 8. **pray** /preɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：祈祷；祈求
- **英文释义**：to speak to God or a god; to hope very strongly for something
- **例句**：
  - She prayed for her family's health.
  - 她为家人的健康祈祷。
  - We can only pray that the situation improves.
  - 我们只能祈求情况好转。
- **助记**：pray 与 precari（恳求）同源 → 向神恳求 → 祈祷

### 9. **precede** /prɪˈsiːd/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：在……之前；先于
- **英文释义**：to come before something in time, order, or position
- **例句**：
  - A short introduction preceded the main speech.
  - 简短的介绍在主要演讲之前。
  - The war was preceded by years of tension.
  - 战争之前是多年的紧张局势。
- **助记**：pre（在前）+ cede（走）→ 走在前面 → 先于

### 10. **precious** /ˈpreʃəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：珍贵的；宝贵的
- **英文释义**：of great value; very important
- **例句**：
  - Time is precious—don't waste it.
  - 时间宝贵——别浪费。
  - Gold and diamonds are precious materials.
  - 黄金和钻石是珍贵的材料。
- **助记**：preci（价值）+ -ous → 有很高价值的 → 珍贵的

### 高一词汇

### 11. **revere** /rɪˈvɪə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：敬畏；崇敬
- **英文释义**：to feel deep respect and admiration for someone or something
- **例句**：
  - He is revered as a hero by the nation.
  - 他被全国人民当作英雄来崇敬。
  - She reveres her teacher for his wisdom.
  - 她因老师的智慧而崇敬他。
- **助记**：re（加强）+ vere（敬畏）→ 非常敬畏 → 崇敬

### 12. **rhetoric** /ˈretərɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：修辞；华丽的言辞
- **英文释义**：the art of using language effectively; language intended to persuade but may lack substance
- **例句**：
  - His speech was full of rhetoric but short on facts.
  - 他的演讲华丽辞藻很多但缺乏事实。
  - The study of rhetoric dates back to ancient Greece.
  - 修辞学的研究可追溯到古希腊。
- **助记**：rhet（说）+ -oric → 说话的艺术 → 修辞

### 13. **rigorous** /ˈrɪɡərəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：严格的；严谨的
- **英文释义**：very thorough and careful; demanding strict attention
- **例句**：
  - The experiment required rigorous testing.
  - 这个实验需要严格的测试。
  - She went through a rigorous training programme.
  - 她经历了严格的训练计划。
- **助记**：rigor（严格）+ -ous → 充满严格性的 → 严格的

### 14. **robust** /rəʊˈbʌst/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：强健的；坚固的
- **英文释义**：strong and healthy; able to withstand difficult conditions
- **例句**：
  - He has a robust constitution.
  - 他体格强健。
  - The company has a robust growth strategy.
  - 公司有一个稳健的增长策略。
- **助记**：rob（橡木）+ -ust → 像橡木一样坚固 → 强健的

### 15. **rudimentary** /ˌruːdɪˈmentri/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：基础的；初步的
- **英文释义**：involving or limited to basic principles; not fully developed
- **例句**：
  - He has only a rudimentary knowledge of French.
  - 他只掌握了法语的基础知识。
  - The equipment was rudimentary but functional.
  - 设备虽然简陋但能用。
- **助记**：rudi（粗糙的）+ ment（状态）+ -ary → 粗糙的初始状态 → 基础的
