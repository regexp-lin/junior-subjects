## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **migrate** /maɪˈɡreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：迁移；移居；迁徙
- **英文释义**：to move from one place to another, especially to find work or better living conditions
- **例句**：
  - Many birds migrate south during the winter months.
  - 许多鸟类在冬季月份向南迁徙。
  - Thousands of people migrated to the cities in search of better jobs.
  - 成千上万的人迁移到城市寻找更好的工作。
- **助记**：migr（移动）+ ate → 移动 → 迁移；名词：migration（迁移）

### 2. **astonish** /əˈstɒnɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使吃惊；使惊讶
- **英文释义**：to surprise someone very much
- **例句**：
  - The magician's performance astonished the entire audience.
  - 魔术师的表演让全场观众大吃一惊。
  - It astonished me that she could speak five languages.
  - 她能说五种语言，这让我很吃惊。
- **助记**：a + stonish（像被石头砸了一样）→ 大为吃惊；形容词：astonishing（令人惊讶的）

### 3. **compensate** /ˈkɒmpenseɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：补偿；弥补；赔偿
- **英文释义**：to give someone something to make up for loss, damage, or suffering
- **例句**：
  - The company compensated the workers for the overtime they worked.
  - 公司对工人加班进行了补偿。
  - Nothing can compensate for the loss of a loved one.
  - 什么都无法弥补失去亲人的痛苦。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ pens（称/支付）+ ate → 共同支付平衡 → 补偿；名词：compensation（补偿）

### 4. **retreat** /rɪˈtriːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：撤退；退缩；隐居处
- **英文释义**：to move back or away from a dangerous or unpleasant situation
- **例句**：
  - The army was forced to retreat after losing the battle.
  - 军队在战败后被迫撤退。
  - The mountain cabin served as a peaceful retreat from city life.
  - 山间小屋是远离城市生活的宁静隐居处。
- **助记**：re（回）+ treat（拉）→ 拉回来 → 撤退；反义词：advance（前进）

### 5. **commodity** /kəˈmɒdəti/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：商品；货物；有用的东西
- **英文释义**：a product that can be traded, bought, or sold
- **例句**：
  - Oil is one of the most valuable commodities in the world.
  - 石油是世界上最有价值的商品之一。
  - Fresh water has become a precious commodity in dry regions.
  - 淡水在干旱地区已成为珍贵的商品。
- **助记**：commod（方便）+ ity → 方便交易的东西 → 商品

### 6. **duration** /djuˈreɪʃn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：持续时间；期间
- **英文释义**：the length of time that something lasts
- **例句**：
  - The duration of the exam is two hours.
  - 考试持续时间为两个小时。
  - For the duration of the holiday, the school will be closed.
  - 假期期间学校将关闭。
- **助记**：dur（持续）+ ation → 持续的时间 → 持续时间；形容词：durable（耐用的）

### 7. **peculiar** /pɪˈkjuːliə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：奇怪的；独特的；特有的
- **英文释义**：strange or unusual; belonging only to a particular person, place, or thing
- **例句**：
  - There was a peculiar smell coming from the kitchen.
  - 厨房里传来一股奇怪的气味。
  - Each region has customs peculiar to itself.
  - 每个地区都有其特有的风俗。
- **助记**：peculi（特殊）+ ar → 特殊的 → 奇怪的/独特的；名词：peculiarity（特殊性）

### 8. **compulsory** /kəmˈpʌlsəri/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：强制的；义务的；必修的
- **英文释义**：required by law or a rule; obligatory
- **例句**：
  - Education is compulsory for all children aged 6 to 15 in China.
  - 在中国，6至15岁的儿童必须接受义务教育。
  - Wearing a seatbelt is compulsory when driving.
  - 开车时系安全带是强制的。
- **助记**：compuls（强迫）+ ory → 被强迫的 → 强制的；动词：compel（强迫）

### 9. **utterly** /ˈʌtəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：完全地；彻底地
- **英文释义**：completely; totally
- **例句**：
  - The plan was utterly destroyed by the unexpected storm.
  - 这个计划被突如其来的暴风雨彻底摧毁了。
  - She was utterly exhausted after the marathon.
  - 马拉松之后她彻底筋疲力尽。
- **助记**：utter（完全的）+ ly → 完全地；形容词：utter（完全的；彻底的）

### 10. **revise** /rɪˈvaɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：修改；复习；修订
- **英文释义**：to change or improve something; to study again for an exam
- **例句**：
  - The teacher asked us to revise our essays before submitting.
  - 老师让我们在提交前修改作文。
  - I need to revise all my notes for the final exam.
  - 我需要复习所有笔记为期末考试做准备。
- **助记**：re（再）+ vise（看）→ 再看一遍 → 修改/复习；名词：revision（修改；复习）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **indispensable** /ˌɪndɪˈspensəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不可或缺的；必不可少的
- **英文释义**：absolutely necessary; too important to be without
- **例句**：
  - Water is indispensable for all forms of life.
  - 水对所有生命形式都是不可或缺的。
  - A good dictionary is indispensable for language learners.
  - 一本好词典对语言学习者来说是必不可少的。
- **助记**：in（不）+ dispensable（可省去的）→ 不可省去的 → 不可或缺的

### 12. **repercussion** /ˌriːpəˈkʌʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：反响；影响；后果
- **英文释义**：an unintended consequence of an action or event, especially an unwelcome one
- **例句**：
  - The policy change had serious repercussions for the economy.
  - 政策变化对经济产生了严重的影响。
  - His careless words had repercussions that lasted for months.
  - 他的不慎言辞产生了持续数月的后果。
- **助记**：re（回）+ percuss（敲击）+ ion → 回击 → 反响/后果

### 13. **juxtapose** /ˌdʒʌkstəˈpəʊz/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：并列；并置；对比
- **英文释义**：to place two things close together for comparison or contrast
- **例句**：
  - The artist juxtaposed old and new elements in her painting.
  - 艺术家在她的画中将新旧元素并置对比。
  - The documentary juxtaposes wealth and poverty in the same city.
  - 这部纪录片在同一座城市中对比了富裕和贫穷。
- **助记**：juxta（旁边）+ pose（放置）→ 放在旁边 → 并列；名词：juxtaposition（并列）

### 14. **oblivious** /əˈblɪviəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：未察觉的；不知道的；健忘的
- **英文释义**：not aware of or paying attention to what is happening around you
- **例句**：
  - She was so focused on her phone that she was oblivious to the noise around her.
  - 她太专注于手机，对周围的噪音浑然不觉。
  - He seemed oblivious to the danger that lay ahead.
  - 他似乎没有意识到前方的危险。
- **助记**：ob（朝向）+ livi（遗忘）+ ous → 朝向遗忘的 → 未察觉的；名词：oblivion（遗忘）

### 15. **epitomize** /ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：是…的典范；集中体现
- **英文释义**：to be a perfect example of something
- **例句**：
  - Her dedication epitomizes the spirit of hard work.
  - 她的奉献精神集中体现了勤奋的精神。
  - This building epitomizes modern architecture at its finest.
  - 这座建筑是现代建筑的典范。
- **助记**：epitom（缩影）+ ize → 成为缩影 → 集中体现；名词：epitome（缩影；典范）
