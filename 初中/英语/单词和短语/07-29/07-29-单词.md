## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **compact** /kəmˈpækt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：紧凑的；小巧的
- **英文释义**：closely packed together; small and taking up little space
- **例句**：
  - The compact dictionary fits easily in my school bag.
  - 这本袖珍词典很容易放进书包。
  - They live in a compact apartment in the city center.
  - 他们住在市中心的一间紧凑公寓里。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ pact（压紧）→ 压在一起 → 紧凑的

### 2. **compose** /kəmˈpəʊz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：组成；创作（音乐、诗歌）
- **英文释义**：to make up or form; to write music or poetry
- **例句**：
  - The committee is composed of ten members.
  - 委员会由十名成员组成。
  - She enjoys composing songs in her spare time.
  - 她喜欢在业余时间创作歌曲。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ pose（放置）→ 放在一起组成 → 组成；创作

### 3. **compress** /kəmˈpres/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：压缩；浓缩
- **英文释义**：to press or squeeze something into a smaller space
- **例句**：
  - You can compress the file to save space on your computer.
  - 你可以压缩文件以节省电脑空间。
  - The teacher compressed two lessons into one class.
  - 老师把两课内容压缩成一节课讲完。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ press（压）→ 压在一起 → 压缩

### 4. **comprise** /kəmˈpraɪz/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：由……组成；包含
- **英文释义**：to consist of; to be made up of
- **例句**：
  - The team comprises fifteen players from different schools.
  - 队伍由来自不同学校的十五名队员组成。
  - The book comprises three parts: introduction, main content, and conclusion.
  - 本书包含三部分：引言、正文和结论。
- **助记**：com-（一起）+ prise（抓住）→ 一起抓住构成 → 由……组成

### 5. **condolence** /kənˈdəʊləns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：哀悼；吊唁
- **英文释义**：an expression of sympathy for someone who has lost a loved one
- **例句**：
  - Please accept my condolences on the loss of your grandfather.
  - 请接受我对你爷爷去世的哀悼。
  - The class sent a card of condolence to her family.
  - 全班给她的家人寄了一张吊唁卡片。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ dol（悲伤）+ -ence → 一起表达悲伤 → 哀悼

### 6. **confess** /kənˈfes/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：承认；坦白
- **英文释义**：to admit something, especially something wrong or embarrassing
- **例句**：
  - He finally confessed that he had broken the window.
  - 他终于承认是自己打碎了窗户。
  - I must confess I didn't finish the homework.
  - 我必须坦白我没做完作业。
- **助记**：con-（完全）+ fess（说）→ 完全说出来 → 承认；坦白

### 7. **conquest** /ˈkɒŋkwest/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：征服；战胜
- **英文释义**：the act of taking control of a country or defeating someone
- **例句**：
  - The conquest of the mountain was his greatest achievement.
  - 征服那座山是他最大的成就。
  - Reading that book was a conquest of my fear of long novels.
  - 读完那本书是我战胜对长篇小说的恐惧的胜利。
- **助记**：con-（完全）+ quest（寻求）→ 完全获得 → 征服

### 8. **conserve** /kənˈsɜːv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：保护；保存
- **英文释义**：to protect something from harm or loss; to use carefully
- **例句**：
  - We must conserve water during the dry season.
  - 旱季我们必须节约用水。
  - The government works to conserve endangered species.
  - 政府致力于保护濒危物种。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ serve（保持）→ 一起保持 → 保护；保存

### 9. **coral** /ˈkɒrəl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：珊瑚
- **英文释义**：a hard substance formed from the skeletons of tiny sea animals
- **例句**：
  - The Great Barrier Reef is made of coral.
  - 大堡礁由珊瑚构成。
  - We saw colorful coral when we went snorkeling.
  - 我们浮潜时看到了五颜六色的珊瑚。
- **助记**：coral → "科若"（谐音）→ 科学考察发现的珊瑚 → 珊瑚

### 10. **coupon** /ˈkuːpɒn/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：优惠券；礼券
- **英文释义**：a piece of paper that gives you a discount or free item
- **例句**：
  - I used a coupon to get 20% off my purchase.
  - 我用优惠券打了八折。
  - The magazine had a coupon for a free ice cream.
  - 杂志里有一张免费冰淇淋的礼券。
- **助记**：coupon → "酷碰"（谐音）→ 酷炫的碰运气省钱 → 优惠券

### 高一词汇

### 11. **connoisseur** /ˌkɒnəˈsɜː(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：鉴赏家；行家
- **英文释义**：a person who knows a lot about and enjoys something like art, food, or wine
- **例句**：
  - He is a connoisseur of classical music.
  - 他是古典音乐鉴赏家。
  - As a tea connoisseur, she can identify the origin of different teas.
  - 作为茶叶行家，她能辨认不同茶叶的产地。
- **助记**：来自法语 connaisseur（认识者）→ 认识精通的人 → 鉴赏家

### 12. **conscientious** /ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：认真的；勤恳的
- **英文释义**：taking care to do things carefully and correctly
- **例句**：
  - She is a conscientious student who always finishes her homework on time.
  - 她是个认真负责的学生，总是按时完成作业。
  - The conscientious worker never cut corners.
  - 那位勤恳的工人从不偷工减料。
- **助记**：conscience（良心）+ -ious → 凭良心做事 → 认真的

### 13. **conducive** /kənˈdjuːsɪv/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有益的；有利于……的
- **英文释义**：making a situation or outcome likely or possible
- **例句**：
  - A quiet environment is conducive to studying.
  - 安静的环境有利于学习。
  - The positive atmosphere was conducive to teamwork.
  - 积极的氛围有利于团队合作。
- **助记**：conduce（有助于）+ -ive → 有助于的 → 有益的

### 14. **constellation** /ˌkɒnstəˈleɪʃn/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：星座；群星
- **英文释义**：a group of stars that form a pattern in the sky
- **例句**：
  - Orion is one of the most famous constellations.
  - 猎户座是最著名的星座之一。
  - We learned to identify several constellations in astronomy class.
  - 我们在天文课上学了辨认几个星座。
- **助记**：con-（一起）+ stell（星星）+ -ation → 星星聚在一起 → 星座

### 15. **contrived** /kənˈtraɪvd/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不自然的；矫揉造作的
- **英文释义**：seeming false and not natural; too obviously planned
- **例句**：
  - The plot of the movie felt contrived and unrealistic.
  - 电影的情节显得矫揉造作、不真实。
  - Her smile seemed contrived rather than genuine.
  - 她的笑容看起来是做作的而非真诚的。
- **助记**：contrive（设计）+ -ed → 刻意设计出来的 → 矫揉造作的
