## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **struggle** /ˈstrʌɡl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：奋斗；挣扎；努力
- **英文释义**：to try very hard to do something difficult; to fight against someone or something
- **例句**：
  - He struggled to pass the math exam last semester.
  - 上学期他努力通过了数学考试。
  - Many families struggle with high living costs in big cities.
  - 许多家庭在大城市里与高生活成本作斗争。
- **助记**：stru（拉扯）+ ggle → 反复拉扯 → 挣扎、奋斗；联想"struggle for"（为...而奋斗）

### 2. **knowledge** /ˈnɒlɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：知识；学问；了解
- **英文释义**：information and understanding about a subject that you have learned
- **例句**：
  - Reading books is a great way to increase your knowledge.
  - 读书是增长知识的好方法。
  - She has a good knowledge of Chinese history.
  - 她对中国历史有很好的了解。
- **助记**：know（知道）+ ledge → 知道的东西 → 知识；注意 k 不发音

### 3. **increase** /ɪnˈkriːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：v./n.
- **中文释义**：v. 增加；增长；n. 增加量
- **英文释义**：to become or make something greater in amount, number, or degree
- **例句**：
  - The number of students learning English has increased rapidly.
  - 学习英语的学生人数迅速增加了。
  - There has been a sharp increase in online shopping this year.
  - 今年网上购物有了大幅增长。
- **助记**：in（向内）+ crease（生长）→ 向内生长 → 增加；反义词：decrease（减少）

### 4. **patient** /ˈpeɪʃnt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：耐心的；有耐性的
- **英文释义**：able to wait calmly for a long time or deal with difficulties without becoming angry
- **例句**：
  - A good teacher should be patient with every student.
  - 好老师应该对每个学生都有耐心。
  - Please be patient — the bus will come in a few minutes.
  - 请耐心等待——公交车几分钟后就来。
- **助记**：pati（忍受，来自 passion 的词根）+ ent（形容词后缀）→ 能忍受的 → 耐心的；名词形式：patience

### 5. **pressure** /ˈpreʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：压力；压强；紧迫感
- **英文释义**：the force produced when you press something; a feeling of stress caused by the need to do something
- **例句**：
  - Students are under a lot of pressure before the final exam.
  - 期末考试前学生们压力很大。
  - Don't put too much pressure on yourself — just do your best.
  - 不要给自己太多压力——尽力就好。
- **助记**：press（按压）+ ure（名词后缀）→ 按压的感觉 → 压力

### 6. **require** /rɪˈkwaɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：需要；要求
- **英文释义**：to need something; to officially demand that something must be done
- **例句**：
  - This job requires good communication skills.
  - 这份工作需要良好的沟通能力。
  - All students are required to wear school uniforms.
  - 所有学生被要求穿校服。
- **助记**：re（再次）+ quire（寻求）→ 反复寻求 → 要求；名词形式：requirement

### 7. **standard** /ˈstændəd/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：标准；水平；规范
- **英文释义**：a level of quality that is considered acceptable or desirable
- **例句**：
  - The school has high standards for students' behavior.
  - 学校对学生的行为有很高的标准。
  - Living standards have improved a lot in the past ten years.
  - 过去十年里生活水平提高了很多。
- **助记**：stand（站立）+ ard → 站立的标杆 → 标准

### 8. **nervous** /ˈnɜːvəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：紧张的；焦虑的；不安的
- **英文释义**：feeling worried or frightened about something that is happening or might happen
- **例句**：
  - I always feel nervous before giving a speech.
  - 我在演讲前总是感到紧张。
  - Don't be nervous — you've prepared well for the interview.
  - 别紧张——你已经为面试做好了充分准备。
- **助记**：nerve（神经）+ ous（形容词后缀）→ 神经紧绷的 → 紧张的

### 9. **properly** /ˈprɒpəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：恰当地；正确地；适当地
- **英文释义**：in a correct or suitable way
- **例句**：
  - Make sure you eat properly before the exam.
  - 考试前一定要好好吃饭。
  - If the machine doesn't work properly, ask the teacher for help.
  - 如果机器不能正常工作，向老师求助。
- **助记**：proper（恰当的）+ ly（副词后缀）→ 恰当地

### 10. **consider** /kənˈsɪdə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：考虑；认为；细想
- **英文释义**：to think about something carefully before making a decision
- **例句**：
  - You should consider all the choices before making a decision.
  - 你应该在做决定之前考虑所有的选择。
  - I consider him to be one of my best friends.
  - 我认为他是我最好的朋友之一。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ sider（星星）→ 一起看星星思考 → 考虑；名词形式：consideration

### 高一词汇

### 11. **appreciate** /əˈpriːʃieɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：感激；欣赏；理解
- **英文释义**：to feel grateful for something; to recognize the good qualities of something
- **例句**：
  - I really appreciate your help with my English homework.
  - 我非常感激你帮我做英语作业。
  - As you grow older, you will appreciate the beauty of classical music.
  - 随着年龄的增长，你会欣赏古典音乐的美。
- **助记**：ap（朝向）+ preci（价值，price 同源）+ ate → 认识到价值 → 感激、欣赏；名词：appreciation

### 12. **diverse** /daɪˈvɜːs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：多种多样的；不同的
- **英文释义**：including many different types of people or things
- **例句**：
  - China has a diverse culture with 56 ethnic groups.
  - 中国拥有 56 个民族的多元文化。
  - Students come from diverse backgrounds in this international school.
  - 这所国际学校的学生来自不同的背景。
- **助记**：di（分开）+ verse（转）→ 转向不同方向 → 多样的；名词：diversity

### 13. **significant** /sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：重要的；有意义的；显著的
- **英文释义**：important or large enough to have an effect or be noticed
- **例句**：
  - The invention of the Internet was a significant event in human history.
  - 互联网的发明是人类历史上一个重大事件。
  - There has been a significant improvement in air quality this year.
  - 今年空气质量有了显著改善。
- **助记**：sign（标记）+ ific + ant → 值得做标记的 → 重要的；名词：significance

### 14. **obstacle** /ˈɒbstəkl/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：障碍；阻碍；绊脚石
- **英文释义**：something that blocks your way or prevents you from doing something
- **例句**：
  - Lack of money is the biggest obstacle to his education.
  - 缺钱是他接受教育的最大障碍。
  - Don't let difficulties become obstacles to your dreams.
  - 不要让困难成为你实现梦想的绊脚石。
- **助记**：ob（对面）+ sta（站立）+ cle → 站在对面挡路的东西 → 障碍

### 15. **acquire** /əˈkwaɪə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：获得；习得；学到
- **英文释义**：to gain something by your own efforts, ability, or behavior
- **例句**：
  - Children acquire language skills naturally by listening and speaking.
  - 儿童通过听和说自然地习得语言技能。
  - She acquired a lot of experience during her trip abroad.
  - 她在国外旅行期间获得了很多经验。
- **助记**：ac（朝向）+ quire（寻求）→ 努力寻求 → 获得；名词：acquisition
