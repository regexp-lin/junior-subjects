## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **suppress** /səˈpres/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：压制；镇压；抑制
- **英文释义**：to prevent something from being seen or expressed; to put down by force
- **例句**：
  - He tried to suppress his laughter during the serious meeting.
  - 他在严肃的会议上努力抑制笑声。
  - The government suppressed the illegal activities quickly.
  - 政府迅速镇压了非法活动。
- **助记**：sup（在下面）+ press（压）→ 从下面压住 → 压制；名词：suppression（压制）

### 2. **reckon** /ˈrekən/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：认为；估计；计算
- **英文释义**：to think or believe; to calculate
- **例句**：
  - I reckon we'll arrive at the airport by 6 o'clock.
  - 我估计我们会在6点前到达机场。
  - She is reckoned to be one of the best students in the school.
  - 她被认为是学校最好的学生之一。
- **助记**：reckon 发音近"瑞肯"→ 在心里算一算 → 估计/认为

### 3. **conceal** /kənˈsiːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：隐藏；隐瞒；遮盖
- **英文释义**：to hide something or keep it secret
- **例句**：
  - He tried to conceal the truth from his parents.
  - 他试图对父母隐瞒真相。
  - The tall bushes concealed the entrance to the garden.
  - 高大的灌木丛遮住了花园的入口。
- **助记**：con（完全）+ ceal（隐藏）→ 完全隐藏 → 隐瞒；名词：concealment（隐藏）

### 4. **depict** /dɪˈpɪkt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：描绘；描述；刻画
- **英文释义**：to represent something in a picture or in words
- **例句**：
  - The painting depicts a beautiful sunset over the ocean.
  - 这幅画描绘了海上美丽的日落。
  - The novel depicts life in a small Chinese village in the 1950s.
  - 这部小说描述了20世纪50年代中国小村庄的生活。
- **助记**：de（加强）+ pict（画）→ 把…画出来 → 描绘；名词：depiction（描绘）

### 5. **quota** /ˈkwəʊtə/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：配额；限额；定量
- **英文释义**：an official limit on the amount of something that is allowed
- **例句**：
  - The country set a quota on the number of imports each year.
  - 该国对每年的进口数量设定了配额。
  - Each salesperson has a monthly quota to reach.
  - 每个销售人员都有一个月度销售目标。
- **助记**：quot（份额）+ a → 给出的一份 → 配额

### 6. **texture** /ˈtekstʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：质地；纹理；口感
- **英文释义**：the way a surface, substance, or fabric feels when you touch it
- **例句**：
  - The texture of silk is smooth and soft.
  - 丝绸的质地光滑柔软。
  - This cake has a light and fluffy texture.
  - 这个蛋糕口感轻盈蓬松。
- **助记**：text（编织）+ ure → 编织出来的样子 → 质地/纹理

### 7. **spontaneous** /spɒnˈteɪniəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：自发的；自然的；不由自主的
- **英文释义**：happening naturally without being planned or forced
- **例句**：
  - The audience burst into spontaneous applause.
  - 观众不由自主地鼓起掌来。
  - She is a spontaneous person who loves surprises.
  - 她是一个喜欢惊喜的自发随性的人。
- **助记**：spont（自愿）+ aneous → 自愿的 → 自发的；副词：spontaneously（自发地）

### 8. **adverse** /ˈædvɜːs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：不利的；有害的；相反的
- **英文释义**：having a negative or harmful effect
- **例句**：
  - Adverse weather conditions forced the cancellation of the outdoor event.
  - 不利的天气条件迫使户外活动取消。
  - The drug may have adverse side effects on some patients.
  - 这种药物可能对一些患者产生不良副作用。
- **助记**：ad（朝向）+ verse（转）→ 转向反面 → 不利的；名词：adversity（逆境）

### 9. **whereby** /weəˈbaɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：凭此；由此；借此
- **英文释义**：by which; by means of which
- **例句**：
  - They signed an agreement whereby both sides would share the profits equally.
  - 他们签署了一项双方平分利润的协议。
  - The school introduced a new system whereby students can choose their own courses.
  - 学校引入了一个学生可以自选课程的新系统。
- **助记**：where（哪里）+ by（通过）→ 通过此方式 → 凭此

### 10. **prosecute** /ˈprɒsɪkjuːt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：起诉；检举；进行
- **英文释义**：to officially charge someone with a crime in a court of law
- **例句**：
  - The company was prosecuted for polluting the river.
  - 该公司因污染河流而被起诉。
  - Anyone caught shoplifting will be prosecuted.
  - 任何被抓到在商店偷窃的人都将被起诉。
- **助记**：pro（向前）+ secute（追）→ 向前追究 → 起诉；名词：prosecution（起诉）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **ameliorate** /əˈmiːliəreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：改善；改进
- **英文释义**：to make something bad or unsatisfactory better
- **例句**：
  - The new policies were designed to ameliorate living conditions in rural areas.
  - 新政策旨在改善农村地区的生活条件。
  - Education can ameliorate the effects of poverty.
  - 教育可以改善贫穷的影响。
- **助记**：a + melior（更好）+ ate → 使更好 → 改善；名词：amelioration（改善）

### 12. **conundrum** /kəˈnʌndrəm/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：难题；困惑的问题
- **英文释义**：a confusing and difficult problem or question
- **例句**：
  - Climate change presents a conundrum for policymakers around the world.
  - 气候变化给全球政策制定者带来了一个难题。
  - The detective faced a conundrum — all suspects had solid alibis.
  - 侦探面临一个难题——所有嫌疑人都有可靠的不在场证明。
- **助记**：con（一起）+ undrum（谜）→ 把人绕在一起的谜 → 难题

### 13. **disseminate** /dɪˈsemɪneɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：传播；散布
- **英文释义**：to spread information, knowledge, or ideas widely
- **例句**：
  - The internet has made it easier to disseminate information quickly.
  - 互联网使快速传播信息变得更加容易。
  - The organization disseminates research findings to schools and hospitals.
  - 该组织向学校和医院传播研究成果。
- **助记**：dis（分开）+ semin（种子）+ ate → 把种子散播出去 → 传播；名词：dissemination（传播）

### 14. **ostensible** /ɒˈstensəbl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：表面上的；声称的
- **英文释义**：appearing to be true but not necessarily so; stated but possibly not real
- **例句**：
  - The ostensible reason for his visit was business, but he actually came to see friends.
  - 他来访的表面原因是公事，但实际上是来看朋友。
  - The ostensible purpose of the meeting was to discuss budgets.
  - 会议声称的目的是讨论预算。
- **助记**：ostens（展示）+ ible → 展示出来的（未必真实的）→ 表面上的；副词：ostensibly

### 15. **antithesis** /ænˈtɪθəsɪs/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：对立面；对比
- **英文释义**：the exact opposite of something; a contrast between two ideas
- **例句**：
  - His behavior is the antithesis of what a good leader should be.
  - 他的行为与一个好领导应有的表现截然相反。
  - Love and hate are often described as the antithesis of each other.
  - 爱与恨常被描述为彼此的对立面。
- **助记**：anti（反对）+ thesis（论点）→ 对立的论点 → 对立面
