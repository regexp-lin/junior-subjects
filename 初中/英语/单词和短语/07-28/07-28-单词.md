## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **beverage** /ˈbevərɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：饮料
- **英文释义**：a drink of any type
- **例句**：
  - Would you like a hot beverage? Coffee or tea?
  - 您要热饮吗？咖啡还是茶？
  - The vending machine sells various beverages.
  - 自动售货机出售各种饮料。
- **助记**：bever-（饮）+ -age（物）→ 饮用的东西 → 饮料

### 2. **bewilder** /bɪˈwɪldə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：使迷惑；使困惑
- **英文释义**：to confuse someone very much
- **例句**：
  - The complicated instructions bewildered the students.
  - 复杂的说明让学生们感到困惑。
  - I was bewildered by his sudden change of attitude.
  - 他态度的突然转变让我困惑不已。
- **助记**：be-（使）+ wilder（荒野）→ 使人如入荒野般迷失 → 使迷惑

### 3. **blossom** /ˈblɒsəm/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：开花；繁荣发展
- **英文释义**：to produce flowers; to develop in a successful way
- **例句**：
  - The cherry trees blossomed early this year.
  - 今年樱花开得早。
  - Her talent blossomed after she joined the drama club.
  - 加入戏剧社后，她的才华得到了绽放。
- **助记**：blossom 与 bloom 同源 → 花开；绽放

### 4. **brochure** /ˈbrəʊʃə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：小册子；宣传册
- **英文释义**：a small, thin book that gives information about something
- **例句**：
  - I picked up a brochure about the summer camp at the school fair.
  - 我在校园展会上拿了一本夏令营的宣传册。
  - The travel agency gave us brochures for several destinations.
  - 旅行社给了我们几个目的地的宣传册。
- **助记**：brochure → "不如舍"（谐音）→ 小册子不如舍去厚重 → 小册子

### 5. **buffet** /ˈbʌfeɪ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：自助餐
- **英文释义**：a meal at which people serve themselves from various dishes
- **例句**：
  - The hotel offers a breakfast buffet every morning.
  - 酒店每天早晨提供自助早餐。
  - We had a buffet at the class reunion.
  - 我们在同学聚会上吃了自助餐。
- **助记**：buffet 来自法语，原意"餐柜" → 自助餐

### 6. **capsule** /ˈkæpsjuːl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：胶囊；太空舱
- **英文释义**：a small container with medicine inside; a small spacecraft
- **例句**：
  - Take two capsules three times a day.
  - 每天三次，每次两粒胶囊。
  - The astronauts entered the space capsule before launch.
  - 宇航员在发射前进入太空舱。
- **助记**：capsule → cap-（盖子）+ -sule → 有盖的小容器 → 胶囊

### 7. **census** /ˈsensəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：人口普查；统计
- **英文释义**：an official count of the population
- **例句**：
  - The government conducts a census every ten years.
  - 政府每十年进行一次人口普查。
  - The census showed that the city's population had grown.
  - 普查显示城市人口增长了。
- **助记**：census → 来自拉丁语"清点" → 人口普查

### 8. **chaos** /ˈkeɪɒs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：混乱；无序
- **英文释义**：a state of complete confusion and disorder
- **例句**：
  - The classroom was in chaos after the teacher left.
  - 老师离开后教室里一片混乱。
  - The storm caused chaos on the roads.
  - 暴风雨导致道路一片混乱。
- **助记**：chaos → "开奥斯"（谐音）→ 开了一场混乱 → 混乱

### 9. **cheerful** /ˈtʃɪəfl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：快活的；开朗的
- **英文释义**：happy and positive; full of good spirits
- **例句**：
  - She has a cheerful personality that makes everyone smile.
  - 她性格开朗，能让每个人都笑起来。
  - The cheerful music lifted our spirits.
  - 欢快的音乐让我们精神焕发。
- **助记**：cheer（欢呼）+ -ful（充满）→ 充满欢呼的 → 快活的

### 10. **comic** /ˈkɒmɪk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：连环漫画；喜剧演员
- **英文释义**：a magazine or book with stories told in pictures; a comedian
- **例句**：
  - He loves reading comics in his spare time.
  - 他业余时间喜欢看连环漫画。
  - The comic made the audience laugh throughout the show.
  - 那位喜剧演员让观众整场笑声不断。
- **助记**：comic → com-（喜剧）+ -ic → 喜剧的；漫画

### 高一词汇

### 11. **catapult** /ˈkætəpʌlt/ `高一`

- **词性**：n./v.
- **中文释义**：弹弓；弹射器；猛投
- **英文释义**：a device for launching objects; to throw or launch suddenly
- **例句**：
  - The ancient army used catapults to attack the castle.
  - 古代军队用抛石机攻打城堡。
  - The news catapulted her to fame overnight.
  - 这则新闻使她一夜成名。
- **助记**：cata-（向下）+ pult（抛）→ 向下抛射 → 弹射器

### 12. **catastrophic** /ˌkætəˈstrɒfɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：灾难性的；毁灭性的
- **英文释义**：causing great damage or suffering
- **例句**：
  - The earthquake had catastrophic effects on the region.
  - 地震对该地区造成了灾难性影响。
  - Making such a mistake could be catastrophic for the project.
  - 犯这样的错误对项目可能是毁灭性的。
- **助记**：catastrophe（灾难）+ -ic → 灾难性的

### 13. **chronology** /krəˈnɒlədʒi/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：年表；时间顺序
- **英文释义**：the order in which events happened; a list of events in time order
- **例句**：
  - We need to understand the chronology of events to solve the mystery.
  - 我们需要理解事件的时间顺序才能解开谜题。
  - The history textbook presents events in chronological order.
  - 历史教科书按时间顺序呈现事件。
- **助记**：chrono-（时间）+ -logy（学科）→ 时间顺序；年表

### 14. **circumstantial** /ˌsɜːkəmˈstænʃl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：间接的；根据情况的
- **英文释义**：based on circumstances rather than direct evidence
- **例句**：
  - The police had only circumstantial evidence against the suspect.
  - 警方只有针对嫌疑人的间接证据。
  - The conclusion was based on circumstantial details.
  - 结论是基于间接证据得出的。
- **助记**：circumstance（情况）+ -ial → 与情况有关的 →  circumstantial

### 15. **colloquial** /kəˈləʊkwiəl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：口语的；通俗的
- **英文释义**：used in ordinary conversation rather than formal writing
- **例句**：
  - "Gonna" is a colloquial form of "going to."
  - "Gonna" 是 "going to" 的口语形式。
  - The teacher explained the difference between colloquial and formal English.
  - 老师解释了口语英语和书面英语的区别。
- **助记**：col-（一起）+ loqu（说）+ -ial → 一起说的 → 口语的
