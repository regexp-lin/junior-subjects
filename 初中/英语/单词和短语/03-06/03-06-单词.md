## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **achieve** /əˈtʃiːv/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：实现；达到；取得
- **英文释义**：to succeed in finishing something or reaching an aim, especially after a lot of effort
- **例句**：
  - She worked hard and finally achieved her dream of becoming a doctor.
  - 她努力学习，终于实现了成为医生的梦想。
  - If you want to achieve good grades, you need to study every day.
  - 如果你想取得好成绩，你需要每天学习。
- **助记**：a（朝向）+ chieve（来自 chief，首领）→ 朝着首领的目标前进 → 实现目标

### 2. **volunteer** /ˌvɒlənˈtɪə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：n. / v.
- **中文释义**：n. 志愿者；v. 自愿做，义务做
- **英文释义**：n. a person who does something willingly without being paid; v. to offer to do something without being forced
- **例句**：
  - Many volunteers helped clean up the park last weekend.
  - 上周末很多志愿者帮忙清理了公园。
  - He volunteered to teach English at the community center.
  - 他自愿在社区中心教英语。
- **助记**：volunt（意愿，来自 voluntary）+ eer（人）→ 有意愿的人 → 志愿者

### 3. **environment** /ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：环境；周围的事物
- **英文释义**：the conditions and surroundings in which people, animals, or plants live
- **例句**：
  - We should protect the environment by reducing waste.
  - 我们应该通过减少浪费来保护环境。
  - A good learning environment helps students concentrate better.
  - 良好的学习环境帮助学生更好地集中注意力。
- **助记**：environ（围绕）+ ment（名词后缀）→ 围绕在周围的事物 → 环境

### 4. **courage** /ˈkʌrɪdʒ/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：勇气；胆量
- **英文释义**：the ability to do something that frightens you; bravery
- **例句**：
  - It takes courage to speak in front of the whole school.
  - 在全校面前发言需要勇气。
  - The firefighter showed great courage during the rescue.
  - 消防员在救援中表现出了巨大的勇气。
- **助记**：cour（心，来自拉丁语 cor）+ age（名词后缀）→ 心中的力量 → 勇气；联想 encourage（鼓励）

### 5. **communicate** /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：交流；沟通；传达
- **英文释义**：to share information, ideas, or feelings with another person
- **例句**：
  - It's important to communicate with your parents about your feelings.
  - 与父母交流你的感受很重要。
  - We can communicate with friends around the world through the Internet.
  - 我们可以通过互联网和世界各地的朋友交流。
- **助记**：com（共同）+ muni（服务）+ cate（动词后缀）→ 共同交流服务 → 沟通；名词形式：communication

### 6. **separate** /ˈsepəreɪt/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj. / v.
- **中文释义**：adj. 分开的，单独的；v. 使分离，分开
- **英文释义**：adj. not joined or connected; v. to divide into parts or move apart
- **例句**：
  - Please put the recyclable waste in a separate bag.
  - 请把可回收垃圾放在一个单独的袋子里。
  - The teacher separated the two students who were talking in class.
  - 老师把上课说话的两个学生分开了。
- **助记**：se（分开）+ par（准备）+ ate → 分开准备 → 分离；注意发音：形容词 /ˈseprət/，动词 /ˈsepəreɪt/

### 7. **valuable** /ˈvæljuəbl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：有价值的；贵重的；宝贵的
- **英文释义**：worth a lot of money, or very useful and important
- **例句**：
  - The experience of studying abroad is very valuable.
  - 出国留学的经历非常宝贵。
  - Don't leave your valuable things on the bus.
  - 不要把贵重物品落在公交车上。
- **助记**：valu（价值，value）+ able（能...的）→ 有价值的

### 8. **gradually** /ˈɡrædʒuəli/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：逐渐地；逐步地
- **英文释义**：slowly over a period of time; little by little
- **例句**：
  - His English has gradually improved since he started reading every day.
  - 自从他开始每天阅读，他的英语逐渐提高了。
  - The sky gradually turned dark as the sun set.
  - 太阳落山时天空逐渐变暗了。
- **助记**：gradu（步，级别，如 grade 年级）+ ally（副词后缀）→ 一级一级地 → 逐渐地

### 9. **influence** /ˈɪnfluəns/ `初三`

- **词性**：n. / v.
- **中文释义**：n. 影响，影响力；v. 影响，对...起作用
- **英文释义**：n. the power to affect how someone thinks or behaves; v. to have an effect on someone or something
- **例句**：
  - Parents have a great influence on their children's behavior.
  - 父母对孩子的行为有很大的影响。
  - The weather can influence people's mood.
  - 天气会影响人们的心情。
- **助记**：in（进入）+ flu（流动）+ ence（名词后缀）→ 流入内心 → 影响；类比 flu（流感）也是"流入"

### 10. **purpose** /ˈpɜːpəs/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：目的；意图；用途
- **英文释义**：the reason for which something is done or made
- **例句**：
  - The purpose of this activity is to raise money for charity.
  - 这次活动的目的是为慈善筹款。
  - What is the purpose of your visit to Beijing?
  - 你来北京的目的是什么？
- **助记**：pur（向前，pro-的变体）+ pose（放置）→ 提前放好的方向 → 目的；固定短语 on purpose（故意地）

### 高一词汇

### 11. **persuade** /pəˈsweɪd/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：说服；劝说
- **英文释义**：to make someone agree to do something by giving them reasons
- **例句**：
  - I finally persuaded my parents to let me join the school trip.
  - 我终于说服父母让我参加学校旅行。
  - She tried to persuade him to give up smoking, but he refused.
  - 她试图劝说他戒烟，但他拒绝了。
- **助记**：per（彻底）+ suade（劝告）→ 彻底劝告 → 说服；注意与 advise（建议）的区别：persuade 强调"说服成功"

### 12. **identity** /aɪˈdentəti/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：身份；特征；同一性
- **英文释义**：who or what a person or thing is; the qualities that make someone different from others
- **例句**：
  - Please show your identity card when you check in at the hotel.
  - 入住酒店时请出示身份证。
  - Teenagers often struggle to find their own identity.
  - 青少年常常在寻找自我身份的过程中挣扎。
- **助记**：ident（相同，identify 的词根）+ ity（名词后缀）→ 用来确认"同一个人"的东西 → 身份

### 13. **fundamental** /ˌfʌndəˈmentl/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：基本的；根本的；基础的
- **英文释义**：forming the base or foundation; very important and necessary
- **例句**：
  - Reading is a fundamental skill for all students.
  - 阅读是所有学生的一项基本技能。
  - There are some fundamental differences between the two plans.
  - 这两个方案之间有一些根本性的区别。
- **助记**：fund（基础，foundation）+ a + ment + al → 基础的 → 根本的

### 14. **contribute** /kənˈtrɪbjuːt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：贡献；捐献；促成
- **英文释义**：to give something (money, help, ideas) in order to achieve something or help make it successful
- **例句**：
  - Everyone should contribute to protecting the environment.
  - 每个人都应该为保护环境作出贡献。
  - Many factors contributed to his success.
  - 许多因素促成了他的成功。
- **助记**：con（共同）+ tribute（给予）→ 共同给予 → 贡献；名词形式：contribution

### 15. **phenomenon** /fəˈnɒmɪnən/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：现象；奇迹；非凡的人（或事物）
- **英文释义**：something that exists and can be seen, felt, or experienced; a remarkable person or thing
- **例句**：
  - Global warming is a serious natural phenomenon.
  - 全球变暖是一种严重的自然现象。
  - The popularity of short videos is a cultural phenomenon.
  - 短视频的流行是一种文化现象。
- **助记**：pheno（显现）+ menon → 显现出来的事物 → 现象；复数形式：phenomena
