## 📖 单词详解

### 初三词汇

### 1. **engulf** /ɪnˈɡʌlf/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：吞没；包围
- **英文释义**：to surround or cover something completely, especially in a destructive way
- **例句**：
  - The flames engulfed the entire building within minutes.
  - 几分钟内火焰吞没了整栋大楼。
  - Darkness engulfed the room when the lights went out.
  - 灯灭后黑暗笼罩了整个房间。
- **助记**：en-（使）+ gulf（深渊）→ 使陷入深渊 → 吞没；包围

### 2. **grovel** /ˈɡrɒvl/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：卑躬屈膝；趴在地上
- **英文释义**：to lie or crawl on the ground with your face down, especially to show fear or respect
- **例句**：
  - The servant groveled before the king, begging for mercy.
  - 仆人匍匐在国王面前乞求宽恕。
  - He would never grovel to anyone, even for a promotion.
  - 他绝不会对任何人卑躬屈膝，哪怕是为了升职。
- **助记**：grovel → "跪服"（谐音）→ 跪着表示臣服 → 卑躬屈膝

### 3. **relish** /ˈrelɪʃ/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：享受；津津有味
- **英文释义**：to enjoy or get pleasure from something
- **例句**：
  - She relished every moment of the concert.
  - 她尽情享受着音乐会的每一刻。
  - He relished the challenge of solving difficult math problems.
  - 他津津有味地面对解难题的挑战。
- **助记**：relish → "瑞丽食"（谐音）→ 吃得很瑞丽（享受）→ 享受；津津有味

### 4. **barter** /ˈbɑːtə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：以物易物；交换
- **英文释义**：to exchange goods or services for other goods or services without using money
- **例句**：
  - In ancient times, people bartered wheat for cloth.
  - 古代人们用小麦换布料。
  - The children bartered their toys with each other at the fair.
  - 孩子们在集市上互相交换玩具。
- **助记**：barter → "吧特"（谐音）→ 拿东西吧，特别交换一下 → 以物易物

### 5. **knack** /næk/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：诀窍；技巧
- **英文释义**：a special skill or ability that you have naturally or can learn
- **例句**：
  - She has a knack for making friends wherever she goes.
  - 她走到哪儿都有交朋友的诀窍。
  - He has a real knack for fixing computers.
  - 他修电脑真有诀窍。
- **助记**：knack → "内克"（谐音）→ 内在克服困难的技巧 → 诀窍；技巧

### 6. **morsel** /ˈmɔːsl/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：一小块；少量
- **英文释义**：a very small piece or amount of food; a small piece of something
- **例句**：
  - She gave the dog a morsel of bread.
  - 她给了狗一小块面包。
  - He hadn't eaten a morsel since breakfast.
  - 早饭后他一口都没吃。
- **助记**：morsel → "猫酥"（谐音）→ 猫吃的一小口酥 → 一小块；少量

### 7. **crypt** /krɪpt/ `初三`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：地下室；墓穴
- **英文释义**：an underground room, especially one under a church, used for burying people
- **例句**：
  - The ancient crypt was discovered beneath the monastery.
  - 修道院地下发现了古代墓穴。
  - The crypt contained the remains of several kings.
  - 墓穴里安葬着几位国王的遗骸。
- **助记**：crypt → "可理普特"（谐音）→ 可以理葬的普通地下室 → 地下室；墓穴

### 8. **zealous** /ˈzeləs/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：热忱的；狂热的
- **英文释义**：having or showing strong and energetic support for a person, cause, or belief
- **例句**：
  - She is a zealous supporter of environmental protection.
  - 她是环保事业的热忱支持者。
  - The zealous fans waited for hours to see their idol.
  - 狂热的粉丝们等了几个小时就为见偶像一面。
- **助记**：zeal（热情）+ -ous → 充满热情的 → 热忱的；狂热的

### 9. **fickle** /ˈfɪkl/ `初三`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：善变的；反复无常的
- **英文释义**：changing frequently, especially in your loyalties or affections
- **例句**：
  - The weather in spring is fickle — sunny one day, rainy the next.
  - 春天的天气善变，今天晴明天雨。
  - She's too fickle to keep a boyfriend for long.
  - 她太善变了，男朋友处不长。
- **助记**：fickle → "飞扣"（谐音）→ 心思飞过来扣过去 → 善变的；反复无常的

### 10. **hereafter** /ˌhɪərˈɑːftə(r)/ `初三`

- **词性**：adv.
- **中文释义**：今后；此后
- **英文释义**：from this time onwards; in the future
- **例句**：
  - I promise to work harder hereafter.
  - 我承诺今后会更加努力。
  - Hereafter, all students must wear the school uniform.
  - 今后，所有学生必须穿校服。
- **助记**：here（这里）+ after（之后）→ 从这里之后 → 今后；此后

### 高一词汇

### 11. **sequester** /sɪˈkwestə(r)/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：隔离；扣押
- **英文释义**：to keep someone or something away from other people or things; to take something away from someone
- **例句**：
  - The jury was sequestered during the trial.
  - 庭审期间陪审团被隔离。
  - The court sequestered his property until the case was settled.
  - 法院扣押了他的财产直到案件了结。
- **助记**：sequester → "析快撕特"（谐音）→ 析出快速撕开隔离 → 隔离；扣押

### 12. **calibrate** /ˈkælɪbreɪt/ `高一`

- **词性**：v.
- **中文释义**：校准；调整
- **英文释义**：to mark or adjust a measuring instrument so that it gives accurate results
- **例句**：
  - The technician calibrated the scale before weighing.
  - 技术员在称重前校准了秤。
  - You need to calibrate your monitor for accurate colors.
  - 你需要校准显示器才能得到准确的颜色。
- **助记**：calibr-（caliber 口径）+ -ate → 按标准口径调整 → 校准；调整

### 13. **anachronistic** /əˌnækrəˈnɪstɪk/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：过时的；不合时代的
- **英文释义**：belonging to a different period of time; out of date
- **例句**：
  - Using a typewriter in the digital age seems anachronistic.
  - 在数字时代用打字机显得不合时宜。
  - The movie had anachronistic elements — phones in an ancient setting.
  - 这部电影有时代错乱之处——古代场景里出现了电话。
- **助记**：ana-（错误）+ chron（时间）+ -istic → 时间搞错的 → 过时的；不合时代的

### 14. **perspicuous** /pəˈspɪkjuəs/ `高一`

- **词性**：adj.
- **中文释义**：清晰的；明了的
- **英文释义**：clear and easy to understand
- **例句**：
  - The teacher gave a perspicuous explanation of the complex theory.
  - 老师把这个复杂理论解释得很清晰。
  - His perspicuous writing made the article easy to follow.
  - 他清晰的文笔让文章容易理解。
- **助记**：per-（透过）+ spic（看）+ -uous → 一眼能看透的 → 清晰的；明了的

### 15. **diatribe** /ˈdaɪətraɪb/ `高一`

- **词性**：n.
- **中文释义**：抨击；长篇大论的批评
- **英文释义**：an angry speech or piece of writing that strongly criticizes someone or something
- **例句**：
  - The blogger launched a diatribe against the new school policy.
  - 博主对新校规发起猛烈抨击。
  - His diatribe about lazy students lasted for twenty minutes.
  - 他对懒学生的长篇大论批评持续了二十分钟。
- **助记**：dia-（通过）+ tribe（ tribe 部落，联想"一通骂"）→ 通过言语攻击 → 抨击；长篇批评
